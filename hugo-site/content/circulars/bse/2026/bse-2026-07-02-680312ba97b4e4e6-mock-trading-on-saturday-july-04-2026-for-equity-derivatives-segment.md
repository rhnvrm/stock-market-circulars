## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Equity Derivatives segment on Saturday, July 04, 2026. The session allows trading members to test their trading applications, including third-party platforms and in-house systems using IML/ETI APIs, for various functionalities such as risk-reduction mode and trading halts. The new BOLT Pro TWS version 13.11 will also be released, with details to follow in a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session for Equity Derivatives segment scheduled for Saturday, July 04, 2026
- Members using third-party or in-house trading platforms via IML/ETI APIs can test functionalities including risk-reduction mode and trading halt
- New BOLT Pro TWS version 13.11 to be released; details via separate circular
- Members may choose to participate in Mock Trading Session or UAT environment to fulfill regulatory requirements per SEBI circular no. SEBI/HO/MRD1/DSAP/CIR/P/2020/234 dated November 24, 2020
- Reference to earlier Exchange Notice No. 20201201-22 dated December 1, 2020 regarding testing of trading and risk management software

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an operational notice implementing existing SEBI requirements for periodic testing of trading and risk management systems.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are requested to participate in the mock trading session or the UAT environment to fulfill regulatory testing requirements. Members should provide feedback on the mock trading session via the contact numbers/email provided by 4:30 pm on the session day.

## Important Dates

- Mock Trading Session: Saturday, July 04, 2026
- Log-in: 10:15 AM to 11:00 AM
- Pre-Open Session Order Entry: 11:00 AM to 11:07/08 AM
- Order Matching period: 11:08 AM to 11:12 AM
- Buffer period: 11:12 AM to 11:15 AM
- Continuous Trading: 11:15 AM to 03:30 PM
- Feedback deadline: 4:30 PM on session day

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, low-impact operational notice. It does not affect live trading, stock prices, or investor activity, but is relevant for trading members and technology vendors who need to test and validate their systems ahead of go-live, particularly with the new BOLT Pro TWS version 13.11.
