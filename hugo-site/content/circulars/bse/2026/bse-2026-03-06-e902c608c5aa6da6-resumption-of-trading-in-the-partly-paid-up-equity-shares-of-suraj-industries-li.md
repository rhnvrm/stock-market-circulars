## Summary

BSE has announced the resumption of trading in partly paid-up equity shares of Suraj Industries Limited (scrip code 890226). A total of 2,08,92,394 partly paid-up equity shares are available for trading following receipt of first/second call money by February 16, 2026.

## Key Points

- Total partly paid shares issued as on March 06, 2026: 2,99,25,394 shares
- Partly paid shares on which call money was received (as of February 16, 2026): 2,09,08,655 shares
- Shares available for trading on resumption: 2,08,92,394 shares
- Paid-up value per share: Rs. 5/- per share
- ISIN for tradable shares: IN9170U01035
- Lock-in period: Not Applicable
- Distinctive numbers range: From 18649681 To 39558335 (total 20,892,394 shares)
- Difference of 16,261 shares between call money received and tradable shares is due to one case where debit could not be executed owing to insufficient balance in the concerned account

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular pertains to the operational resumption of trading for partly paid-up shares following completion of call money collection.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should note the updated ISIN (IN9170U01035) for partly paid-up equity shares of Suraj Industries Limited available for trading
- No lock-in restrictions apply to these shares

## Important Dates

- Call money receipt cut-off date: February 16, 2026
- Trading resumption date: March 06, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This resumption enables holders of 2,08,92,394 partly paid-up equity shares of Suraj Industries Limited to trade their shares on BSE. The impact is confined to existing shareholders of Suraj Industries Limited who hold partly paid-up shares. A minor discrepancy of 16,261 shares (between call money received and shares admitted for trading) exists due to an account with insufficient balance, which does not affect the broader trading resumption.
