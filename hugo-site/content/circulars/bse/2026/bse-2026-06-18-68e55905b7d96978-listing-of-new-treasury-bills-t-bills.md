## Summary

BSE has notified that three new Treasury Bills (T-Bills) will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in the G Group - Debt Instruments segment with effect from June 19, 2026. The listed instruments include 91-day, 182-day, and 364-day T-Bills with maturity dates ranging from September 2026 to June 2027.

## Key Points

- Three new T-Bills listed under G Group - Debt Instruments effective June 19, 2026
- Instruments cover three tenors: 91-day, 182-day, and 364-day
- Market lot is 1 for all three securities
- Trading will be suspended two working days prior to the Maturity/Redemption Date (excluding Bank Holidays)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notice under the existing framework for Government Securities on BSE's debt segment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members must note that the above-listed securities will not be available for trading two (2) working days prior to the Maturity/Redemption Date (i.e., Maturity Date minus two Trading Days, excluding Bank Holidays). Members should update their systems accordingly to prevent trading in these securities during the restricted period.

## Important Dates

- **Listing effective date:** June 19, 2026
- **91TB17926 (ISIN: IN002026X115) maturity:** September 17, 2026
- **182T171226 (ISIN: IN002026Y113) maturity:** December 17, 2026
- **364TB17627 (ISIN: IN002026Z110) maturity:** June 17, 2027
