## Summary

BSE has announced the schedule for an Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for shares of PREMIUM CAPITAL MARKET AND INVESTMENTS LTD (PREMCAPM). The offer will be live from Friday, January 16, 2026 to Thursday, January 30, 2026. Trading Members and Custodian Members can participate through the iBBS platform during specified session timings.

## Key Points

- **Security:** PREMIUM CAPITAL MARKET AND INVESTMENTS LTD (Symbol: PREMCAPM, ISIN: INE555D01019, Scrip Code: 511660)
- **Offer Size:** Up to 17,04,000 Equity Shares
- **Offer Price:** Rs. 6.40 per share
- **Face Value:** Rs. 10.00
- **Market Lot:** 1
- **Opening Date:** Friday, January 16, 2026
- **Closing Date:** Thursday, January 30, 2026
- **Bid Offering Period:** 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM (January 16-30, 2026)
- **Give-up/Take-up Period:** 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM (January 16-29, 2026)
- **Last Day Give-up:** January 30, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Last Day Take-up:** January 30, 2026 (9:15 AM to 4:00 PM)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an operational notice for a specific takeover offer.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must ensure UCC of the Investor is registered and/or updated with correct PAN Number and Category in the Exchange UCC database
- Trading Members can bid only for Investors having shares in demat form
- Members must refer to Exchange Notices No. 20150930-1 (dated September 30, 2015) and No. 20170203-29 (dated February 3, 2017) for operational instructions for participation in OTB
- For Leased Line URL access settings, refer to Exchange Circular No. 20170503-12

## Important Dates

- **January 16, 2026:** Offer opening date; Bid offering, Give-up/Take-up begins (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **January 16-29, 2026:** Regular Give-up/Take-up period (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **January 30, 2026:** Offer closing date; Last day Give-up (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM); Last day Take-up (9:15 AM - 4:00 PM)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This is a company-specific takeover offer affecting only shareholders of PREMIUM CAPITAL MARKET AND INVESTMENTS LTD, a small-cap stock. The offer size is limited to 17.04 lakh shares at Rs. 6.40 per share, representing a relatively small transaction value with no broader market implications.

**Operational Impact:** Low to Medium for participating members - Trading Members and Custodian Members need to access iBBS/RTRMS platforms and ensure proper UCC registration for clients wishing to participate. Multiple bidding categories are available (FII, FI, MF, IC, Other QIBs, Company, Individual). Support contacts are provided for technical assistance with login IDs, password resets, and OTB module queries.
