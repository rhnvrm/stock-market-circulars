## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 35,69,50,000 new equity shares of Pulsar International Limited (Scrip Code: 512591) issued on a rights basis. These shares will be available for trading on the exchange from Tuesday, January 27, 2026. The shares were allotted on January 21, 2026, at par value of Rs. 1/- each and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 35,69,50,000 equity shares of Rs. 1/- each issued at par on rights basis
- Scrip Code: 512591
- ISIN: INE183U01022
- Distinctive Numbers: 71390001 to 428340000
- Date of Allotment: January 21, 2026
- Trading commencement: January 27, 2026
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the listing of these new securities for trading purposes from the specified date.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: January 21, 2026
- **Trading Commencement**: January 27, 2026 (Tuesday)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification with limited market impact, affecting only shareholders and traders of Pulsar International Limited. The rights issue increases the paid-up capital of the company, and the new shares will have the same rights and privileges as existing shares. No broader market or operational implications are expected.
