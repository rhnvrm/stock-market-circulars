## Summary

BSE announced the addition of ARITAS VINYL LIMITED (Exchange ticker: 544683) to the BSE SME IPO Index effective at the open of Tuesday, January 27, 2026. The stock is being listed on BSE on Friday, January 23, 2026.

## Key Points

- ARITAS VINYL LIMITED (ticker: 544683) listed on BSE effective January 23, 2026
- Stock will be added to BSE SME IPO Index on January 27, 2026
- Addition effective at market open on January 27, 2026
- Corporate action included in end-of-day file (*.SDE)
- This action follows previous Notice No. 20260122-19

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index composition update.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance requirements for market participants. Index composition change is informational for investors and index-tracking funds.

## Important Dates

- **January 23, 2026**: Listing date for ARITAS VINYL LIMITED on BSE
- **January 27, 2026**: Effective date for addition to BSE SME IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard index inclusion following a new SME listing. Impact limited to:
- Index tracking funds that replicate the BSE SME IPO Index may need to acquire shares
- Increased visibility for the newly listed company
- No impact on broader market operations or trading rules
