## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members that 495 new equity shares of Eureka Forbes Limited (Scrip Code: 543482, ISIN: INE0KCE01017) are listed and permitted to trade effective Wednesday, April 22, 2026. These shares were issued pursuant to a scheme and were previously kept in abeyance.

## Key Points

- 495 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each are being listed on BSE
- Shares were kept in abeyance under a scheme and are now released for trading
- ISIN: INE0KCE01017
- Distinctive Numbers: 4040476 to 4040970
- Shares are pari passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Trading permitted from April 22, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine operational listing notice under BSE's Listing Operations department.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of the exchange are notified to take note of the new securities and facilitate trading from the effective date. No additional compliance action is required from listed companies or investors beyond standard trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** April 21, 2026
- **Trading Effective Date:** April 22, 2026 (Wednesday)

## Impact Assessment

The impact of this listing is minimal. Only 495 equity shares are being added to the tradable pool, representing a negligible addition to the total equity of Eureka Forbes Limited. These shares were previously held in abeyance under a corporate scheme and their release is a routine administrative action. No significant price or liquidity impact is anticipated.
