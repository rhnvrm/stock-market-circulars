## Summary

BSE has notified that the equity shares of SMR Jewels Limited (Scrip Code: 544774), listed on the SME segment, will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment (M Group) effective Monday, June 22, 2026. This notice is in continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20260605-45 dated June 05, 2026.

## Key Points

- SMR Jewels Limited (Scrip Code: 544774) is being moved out of Trade for Trade (MT Group)
- The stock will shift to Rolling segment under M Group
- Change is effective from June 22, 2026
- This is a continuation of an earlier notice (20260605-45 dated June 05, 2026)
- Listed under the SME segment of BSE

## Regulatory Changes

The stock's trading classification is being changed from the Trade for Trade (MT Group) mechanism, where each trade must result in delivery and no netting is permitted, to the Rolling segment (M Group), which follows standard T+1 rolling settlement with netting of buy and sell positions.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members are informed of this change and should update their systems and client advisories accordingly. Members with open positions or pending transactions in SMR Jewels Limited should account for the segment change effective June 22, 2026. For further details, Trading Members may contact Mr. Anurag Jain at Tel. No. 022-2272 8822.

## Important Dates

- **June 08, 2026**: Notice date
- **June 22, 2026**: Effective date of transfer to Rolling segment (M Group)

## Impact Assessment

The transfer from Trade for Trade (MT Group) to Rolling segment (M Group) is generally a positive development for market participants as it eases trading restrictions. Under the MT Group, every trade requires compulsory delivery with no netting; under the M Group (Rolling segment), normal settlement with netting applies, improving liquidity and reducing settlement complexity. Traders and investors in SMR Jewels Limited should note the change in settlement mechanics effective June 22, 2026.
