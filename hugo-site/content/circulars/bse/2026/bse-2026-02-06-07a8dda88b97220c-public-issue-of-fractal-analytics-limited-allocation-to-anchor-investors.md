## Summary

BSE has announced the allocation of equity shares to anchor investors in the public issue of Fractal Analytics Limited. This represents the anchor investor portion of the IPO, which is allocated prior to the opening of the issue to the general public.

## Key Points

- Public issue of equity shares by Fractal Analytics Limited
- Allocation completed to anchor investors
- Anchor investment provides price discovery and institutional validation for the IPO
- Allocation details as per SEBI regulations for anchor investor participation

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard IPO process notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Anchor investors must comply with lock-in requirements as per SEBI ICDR Regulations
- Disclosure of allocation details as per regulatory requirements
- Trading members to note the anchor allocation for IPO processing

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: February 6, 2026
- Anchor investor allocation completed
- Public issue opening date to follow

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact. Anchor investor allocation is a positive indicator for the IPO as it demonstrates institutional interest in Fractal Analytics Limited. The allocation provides price discovery and typically indicates strong demand for the issue. Trading members and investors should note the anchor allocation ahead of the public offering period.
