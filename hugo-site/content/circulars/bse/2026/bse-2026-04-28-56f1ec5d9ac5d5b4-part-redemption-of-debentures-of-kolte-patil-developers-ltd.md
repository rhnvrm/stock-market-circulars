## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260428-49 informs trading members that Kolte-Patil Developers Ltd has fixed **02 May 2026** as the record date for part redemption of five series of Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs). Trading in all five debenture scrips will occur at a reduced face value effective **30 April 2026** (ex-date), in line with Settlement No. 621/2026-2027.

## Key Points

- Five NCD series of Kolte-Patil Developers Ltd are subject to part redemption under the same settlement reference (621/2026-2027).
- Record date for all five series: **02 May 2026**.
- Ex-date for all five series: **30 April 2026** — trading switches to reduced face value from this date.
- The debentures are privately placed (PVT) instruments listed on BSE's Debt segment.
- Reduced face values differ per series, ranging from Rs. 39,917 to Rs. 96,011 per debenture.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework introduced. This is a standard corporate action notification under BSE's Listing Compliance and Operations department, requiring adjustment to face value on the ex-date.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note and update systems to reflect reduced face values for the five scrips from 30 April 2026.
- All trades on or after the ex-date (30 April 2026) will be executed at the respective reduced face values.
