## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that ECap Equities Limited has fixed a record date for the redemption of its Non-Convertible Debentures (NCD). As a result, trading in the specified debenture series has been suspended with effect from March 18, 2026, one day prior to the record date of March 19, 2026.

## Key Points

- ECap Equities Limited has set March 19, 2026 as the record date for redemption of its NCDs.
- Trading in the debenture series EEL-ZC-03-4-26-PVT (ISIN: INE468N07623, BSE Code: 974635) is suspended from March 18, 2026.
- Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the suspension date onward.
- The circular reference number is DR-842/2025-2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational notice pursuant to existing BSE procedures for debenture redemptions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in EEL-ZC-03-4-26-PVT (ISIN: INE468N07623, BSE Code: 974635) with effect from March 18, 2026.
- Members are required to take note of the suspension and ensure no transactions are executed in this security from the stated date.

## Important Dates

- **March 13, 2026**: Circular issued by BSE.
- **March 18, 2026**: No dealings date — trading suspended from this date.
- **March 19, 2026**: Record date for redemption of debentures.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and traders of this specific NCD series (EEL-ZC-03-4-26-PVT). Trading members must ensure compliance by halting transactions in this security from March 18, 2026. The suspension is a routine pre-redemption measure and does not indicate any financial distress. Debenture holders will have their securities redeemed as per the terms on the record date.
