## Summary

BSE (Notice No. 20260608-31) has revised price bands for 14 equity scrips effective June 9, 2026. The revised bands range from 2% to 10%, replacing the previously applicable limits for these securities under the Department of Surveillance and Supervision (DOSS).

## Key Points

- Price bands revised for 14 scrips across various sectors effective June 9, 2026
- Two scrips (CL Educate Ltd and Innovassynth Technologies) face the tightest 2% band
- Four scrips (Agri-Tech India, Capitalnumbers Infotech, Kreon Finnancial Services, SVS Ventures) revised to 10% band
- Eight scrips revised to 5% band
- Issued by BSE Department of Surveillance and Supervision (DOSS)

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must adhere to the revised price bands for the listed scrips from June 9, 2026 onwards
- Orders outside the revised price band limits will not be accepted by the exchange
- Members may contact bse.surv@bseindia.com for clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 8, 2026
- **Effective Date:** June 9, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The revision of price bands restricts maximum permissible intraday price movement for the 14 affected scrips. The tightest 2% bands on CL Educate Ltd and Innovassynth Technologies indicate heightened surveillance concern for those securities. Trading members and investors holding positions in these scrips should be aware of the reduced trading range, which may affect liquidity and exit/entry strategies. The 10% band for four scrips represents a relatively wider allowance, suggesting moderate surveillance action for those companies.
