## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the rights issue of Shah Metacrop Limited has been revised for early closure. The issue will now close on Thursday, June 25, 2026, instead of the originally scheduled Friday, July 03, 2026. This notice is in continuation of earlier notices 20260624-6 and 20260609-51.

## Key Points

- Rights issue closure date advanced by approximately 8 days
- Original closure date: Friday, July 03, 2026
- Revised closure date: Thursday, June 25, 2026
- Continuation of prior notices: 20260624-6 and 20260609-51
- Issued by Mangesh Tayde, Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

The rights issue timeline for Shah Metacrop Limited has been modified. Trading members and eligible shareholders must take note of the revised closure date for participation in the rights issue.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must inform their clients of the revised closure date
- Eligible shareholders wishing to subscribe to the rights issue must do so on or before June 25, 2026
- No further extensions are indicated in this notice

## Important Dates

- **Revised Rights Issue Closure Date:** Thursday, June 25, 2026
- **Original Rights Issue Closure Date (superseded):** Friday, July 03, 2026
- **Notice Date:** June 25, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Shareholders and investors participating in Shah Metacrop Limited's rights issue have a significantly shortened window to subscribe. The early closure by approximately eight days may impact eligible shareholders who were planning to subscribe closer to the original deadline. Trading members should promptly communicate this change to their clients to avoid missed participation.
