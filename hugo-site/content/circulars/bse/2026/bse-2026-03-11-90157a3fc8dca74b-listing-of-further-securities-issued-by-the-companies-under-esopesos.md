## Summary

BSE has listed additional securities issued by 19 companies under their Employee Stock Option Plan (ESOP) or Employee Stock Option Scheme (ESOS) programs. These newly issued shares are admitted for trading on BSE with effect from Thursday, March 12, 2026. None of the newly listed shares are subject to any lock-in period.

## Key Points

- 19 companies have listed new securities under ESOP/ESOS schemes effective March 12, 2026.
- No lock-in restrictions apply to any of the newly listed shares.
- The largest issuances by volume are Ujjivan Small Finance Bank (1,818,422 shares), One 97 Communications/Paytm (217,265 shares), ICICI Bank (485,368 shares), and Paradeep Phosphates (201,605 shares).
- Shares span face values of ₹1, ₹2, ₹5, and ₹10 depending on the company.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification under existing ESOP/ESOS frameworks.

## Compliance Requirements

Market participants are requested to take note of the new securities and their ISIN/scrip codes for trading purposes. No additional compliance action is required.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** March 11, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** March 12, 2026 (Thursday)
