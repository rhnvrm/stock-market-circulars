## Summary

BSE has listed 1,985,000 new equity shares of Fundviser Capital (India) Limited (Scrip Code: 530197, ISIN: INE365H01014) with effect from May 14, 2026. The shares were issued at Rs. 122.50/- each (face value Rs. 10/- + premium Rs. 112.50/-) to Promoter and Non-Promoter on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. These shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 1,985,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 112.50/- (issue price Rs. 122.50/-)
- Allotment date: 23 March 2026
- Distribution numbers: 5,915,001 to 7,900,000
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Trading permitted on BSE from Thursday, May 14, 2026
- Issued to Promoter and Non-Promoter on preferential basis via warrant conversion

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice for newly issued securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to permit trading of these new securities from May 14, 2026. Lock-in restrictions apply to all allotted shares as detailed below.

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: 23 March 2026
- Trading effective from: 14 May 2026
- Lock-in expiry dates:
  - 1,012,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 5,915,001–6,927,000): locked until 30 November 2027
  - 138,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 6,927,001–7,065,000): locked until 30 November 2026
  - 585,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 7,065,001–7,650,000): locked until 29 November 2026
  - 250,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 7,650,001–7,900,000): locked until 30 November 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 1,985,000 new equity shares to the tradeable float of Fundviser Capital (India) Limited, subject to lock-in restrictions. The majority (1,012,000 shares) are locked in until November 2027, limiting immediate selling pressure. Only 835,000 shares become free after November 2026. The issue price of Rs. 122.50/- provides a reference point for market valuation. Impact is confined to existing shareholders and prospective investors in this small-cap scrip.
