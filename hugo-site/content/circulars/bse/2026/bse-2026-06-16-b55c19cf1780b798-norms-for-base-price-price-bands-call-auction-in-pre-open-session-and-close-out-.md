## Summary

BSE has forwarded SEBI circular HO/47/11/11(1)2026-MRD-POD3/I/13804/2026 dated June 15, 2026, which prescribes norms for base price determination, price bands, call auction in pre-open session, and close-out procedures specifically applicable to Exchange Traded Funds (ETFs).

## Key Points

- BSE Notice No. 20260616-23 directed to all Trading Members.
- References SEBI circular dated June 15, 2026 on ETF trading norms.
- Covers four key areas: base price norms, price band applicability, call auction in pre-open session, and close-out procedures for ETFs.
- The full SEBI circular is enclosed as an Annexure to the BSE notice (Attachment ID: 1781524158363).

## Regulatory Changes

SEBI has issued specific norms governing the trading framework for ETFs, addressing:
- **Base Price**: Rules for determining the base/reference price for ETF units.
- **Price Bands**: Circuit filter limits applicable to ETF trading.
- **Call Auction in Pre-Open Session**: ETF participation norms during the pre-open call auction window.
- **Close-Out Procedure**: Process to be followed for closing out positions in ETFs.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members are required to:
- Take note of SEBI circular HO/47/11/11(1)2026-MRD-POD3/I/13804/2026 and its provisions.
- Refer to the enclosed Annexure for detailed norms and ensure operations align with the updated ETF trading framework.

## Important Dates

- **SEBI Circular Date**: June 15, 2026
- **BSE Notice Date**: June 16, 2026
- Effective date of norms: as specified in the enclosed SEBI circular Annexure.

## Impact Assessment

This circular affects market participants trading in ETFs on BSE, including trading members, market makers, and institutional investors. The updated norms on price bands and call auction participation may alter pre-open session dynamics for ETFs, and the close-out procedure clarification reduces operational ambiguity. The impact is moderate and limited to ETF segment participants.
