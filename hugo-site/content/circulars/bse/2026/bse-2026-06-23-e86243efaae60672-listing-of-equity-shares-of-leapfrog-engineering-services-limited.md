## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members that the equity shares of Leapfrog Engineering Services Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company will trade under the symbol LESL on the SME segment.

## Key Points

- Leapfrog Engineering Services Limited is set to be listed on BSE's SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544797, Symbol: LESL, ISIN: INE0X6O01027
- The actual listing date and further security details will be communicated via a separate notice
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing admission notice.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are hereby informed of the forthcoming listing. No immediate compliance action is required until the formal listing date notice is issued.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: June 23, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced via a separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine SME listing notice with no immediate market impact. Trading in LESL shares cannot commence until BSE issues the follow-up notice confirming the listing date and full security details. The listing is limited to the SME segment, restricting its broader market relevance.
