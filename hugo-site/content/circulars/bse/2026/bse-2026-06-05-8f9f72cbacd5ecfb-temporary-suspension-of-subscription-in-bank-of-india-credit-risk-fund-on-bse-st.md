## Summary

BSE has issued an update (Notice No. 20260605-32) informing members that Bank of India Mutual Fund has communicated the temporary suspension of Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) and Systematic Withdrawal Plan (SWP) subscriptions under the **Bank of India Credit Risk Fund** on the BSE StAR MF platform, effective immediately. This notice serves as an addendum to the earlier Notice No. 20260525-27, which had addressed temporary suspension of subscriptions in the then-named BOI AXA Credit Risk Fund of BOI AXA Mutual Fund.

## Key Points

- SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) registrations under Bank of India Credit Risk Fund have been disabled on BSE StAR MF platform with immediate effect.
- SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) under Bank of India Credit Risk Fund have also been disabled on BSE StAR MF platform with immediate effect.
- This is an update/addendum to the earlier Notice No. 20260525-27 related to the same fund (previously known as BOI AXA Credit Risk Fund under BOI AXA Mutual Fund, now Bank of India Credit Risk Fund under Bank of India Mutual Fund).
- The notice cum addendum issued by Bank of India Mutual Fund is attached to this circular for reference.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework changes. This is an operational restriction communicated by the fund house (Bank of India Mutual Fund) to BSE, resulting in the disabling of SIP and SWP transaction types for the affected scheme on the BSE StAR MF platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- BSE trading members and distributors operating on the BSE StAR MF platform should note that new SIP and SWP registrations/transactions in the Bank of India Credit Risk Fund are not permitted until further notice.
- Members should refer to the attached notice cum addendum from Bank of India Mutual Fund for detailed scheme-level information.
- Members should inform their clients accordingly to avoid failed transactions.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 05 June 2026
- **Effective Date:** Immediate effect (from 05 June 2026)
- **Reference Notice:** No. 20260525-27 dated 25 May 2026 (original suspension notice)

## Impact Assessment

This restriction affects investors and distributors who transact through the BSE StAR MF platform in the Bank of India Credit Risk Fund. New SIP registrations and SWP instructions will not be processed for this scheme on the platform. Lump sum subscriptions are not explicitly mentioned as suspended in this notice. Investors with existing SIPs may also be impacted. The scope is limited to the BSE StAR MF platform; transactions through other platforms or directly with the AMC may or may not be affected depending on separate communications from Bank of India Mutual Fund.
