## Summary

BSE has issued a circular notifying the change in name of Galaxy Cloud Kitchens Ltd. The company's name has been updated on the exchange records, and all future references and trading will reflect the new name.

## Key Points

- BSE notifies the change in name of Galaxy Cloud Kitchens Ltd
- The name change is a corporate action that affects exchange records and trading symbols
- Members and market participants are informed to update their records accordingly
- The circular was issued on March 5, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

The company name change is recorded by BSE in compliance with corporate law requirements. Once a company legally changes its name through the Registrar of Companies (RoC), the stock exchange updates its records to reflect the new name.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and depository participants should update their records to reflect the new company name
- All future correspondence and documentation should use the updated name
- Investors holding shares in the company should note the name change for their portfolio records

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: March 5, 2026
- Effective date of name change: As per the circular details (refer to the PDF for the specific effective date)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative circular with low market impact. The name change does not affect the company's scrip code, ISIN, or trading operations. Shareholders' holdings remain unchanged. The primary impact is administrative — trading members, investors, and depository participants need to update their records to reflect the new company name. No financial or trading disruptions are expected.
