## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that ECOBOX INDUSTRIALS ASSET I PRIVATE LIMITED has fixed a Record Date of 10 March 2026 for the early redemption of its Non-Convertible Debentures. Trading in the specified debentures is prohibited from 10 March 2026 onwards.

## Key Points

- Trading members are advised not to deal in the debentures of ECOBOX INDUSTRIALS ASSET I PRIVATE LIMITED from 10 March 2026.
- The debenture series affected: EIAIPL-10.15%-RESET RATE-6-2-2, ISIN INE12W707013, Scrip Code 976482.
- Record Date for early redemption has been fixed as 10 March 2026.
- Notice reference: DR-836/2025-2026, Notice No. 20260305-22 dated 05 March 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a routine suspension notice issued by BSE pursuant to the company fixing a record date for redemption of its NCDs.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any buy or sell transactions in the debentures (ISIN: INE12W707013, Scrip Code: 976482) from 10 March 2026.
- Members should update their systems and client-facing platforms to reflect the trading suspension.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 05 March 2026
- **Record Date / No-Dealings From:** 10 March 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and prospective traders of this specific NCD series of ECOBOX INDUSTRIALS ASSET I PRIVATE LIMITED. Trading members must cease dealings from the record date. Existing debenture holders will receive early redemption proceeds as per the company's redemption schedule. No broader market impact is anticipated.
