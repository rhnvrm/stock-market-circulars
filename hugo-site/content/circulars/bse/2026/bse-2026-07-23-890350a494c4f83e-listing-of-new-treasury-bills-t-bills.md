## Summary

BSE has informed Trading Members that three new Treasury Bills (T-Bills) will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange under the G Group - Debt Instruments segment, effective July 24, 2026.

## Key Points

- Three new T-Bills are being listed: a 91-day, a 182-day, and a 364-day Treasury Bill
- Scrip code 805244, ISIN IN002026X164, symbol 91TB221026, maturing 22/10/2026
- Scrip code 805245, ISIN IN002026Y162, symbol 182T210127, maturing 21/01/2027
- Scrip code 805246, ISIN IN002026Z169, symbol 364TB22727, maturing 22/07/2027
- Market lot for all three instruments is 1
- These securities fall under the G Group - Debt Instruments category

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing notice issued under BSE's Debt segment listing department (DCS-Listing).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that the listed securities will not be available for trading two working days prior to their respective Maturity/Redemption dates (T-2 trading days, excluding bank holidays)
- Queries can be directed to BSE at 2272 8352/5753/8597

## Important Dates

- Effective date of listing: July 24, 2026
- Maturity date of 91-day T-Bill (91TB221026): October 22, 2026
- Maturity date of 182-day T-Bill (182T210127): January 21, 2027
- Maturity date of 364-day T-Bill (364TB22727): July 22, 2027

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard administrative listing notice for government Treasury Bills with no bearing on equity markets or listed companies. It is relevant only to debt segment trading members who need to be aware of the new instruments' trading eligibility windows and maturity-related trading restrictions.
