## Summary

BSE has announced adjustments to all Futures & Options contracts on Mphasis Ltd (Derivative Asset Code: MPHS, Equity Scrip Code: 526299) in the Equity Derivatives Segment. The adjustment is triggered by Mphasis Ltd's declaration of a Final Dividend of Rs. 62 per equity share for FY 2026-27, with July 08, 2026 set as the Record Date.

## Key Points

- Mphasis Ltd has declared a Final Dividend of Rs. 62/- per equity share for FY 2026-27.
- Record Date: July 08, 2026; Ex-date: July 08, 2026 (Wednesday).
- All F&O contract adjustments will be applied on July 07, 2026 (Tuesday, the day prior to ex-date).
- For Options contracts, Rs. 62/- will be deducted from all strike prices generated at end of day on July 07, 2026.
- For Futures contracts, the adjusted price = daily MTM settlement price of July 07, 2026 minus Rs. 62/-.
- Adjusted strike prices and futures base rates will be effective from July 08, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

Adjustments are being made in accordance with:
- BSE Exchange Notice No. 20180710-26 (Review of Adjustment of corporate actions for stock options).
- SEBI Master Circular No. SEBI/HO/MRD/DP/CIR/P/2016/135 dated December 16, 2016.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members of the Equity Derivatives Segment are informed to note the adjustments.
- No separate action is required from members; adjustments will be applied automatically by BSE.
- Members may contact their respective relationship managers for further details.

## Important Dates

| Adjustment Applied (F&O) | July 07, 2026 (Tuesday) |

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard corporate action adjustment for derivative contracts. Traders holding open Futures or Options positions in Mphasis Ltd (MPHS) will see their strike prices and futures base rates reduced by Rs. 62/- effective July 08, 2026. This ensures fair pricing continuity post-dividend and prevents artificial windfall or loss for derivative holders. The impact is limited to MPHS derivative market participants and does not affect the broader market.
