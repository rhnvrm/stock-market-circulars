## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in two series of non-convertible debentures (NCDs) issued by INDEL MONEY LIMITED effective January 28, 2026. The suspension is implemented due to the company fixing a record date for interest payment and redemption of the debentures. Trading members are advised not to deal in these securities from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Two NCD series of INDEL MONEY LIMITED affected by trading suspension
- IML-9.75%-13-2-26-NCD (ISIN: INE0BUS07AX7, Code: 939256) - suspended for redemption and interest payment
- IML-13-2-26-NCD (ISIN: INE0BUS07AW9, Code: 939258) - suspended for redemption only
- Record date fixed as January 28, 2026
- No dealings permitted from January 28, 2026
- Notice reference: DR-807/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action procedure for debenture redemption and interest payment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in the specified debentures from January 28, 2026
- Trading members should update their systems to reflect the trading suspension
- Members must inform clients holding these debentures about the suspension and redemption process

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 20, 2026
- **Record Date**: January 28, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Date**: January 28, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: High - Complete trading suspension prevents any secondary market transactions in these debentures. Investors holding these securities will not be able to trade them from the suspension date.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders as of the record date will receive their interest payments and principal redemption. Those attempting to trade after the suspension date will be unable to execute transactions.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems and communicate with affected clients regarding the suspension and upcoming redemption process.
