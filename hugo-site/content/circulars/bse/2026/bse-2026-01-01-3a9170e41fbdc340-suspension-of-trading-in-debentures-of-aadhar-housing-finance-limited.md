## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of Aadhar Housing Finance Limited effective January 8, 2026. The suspension is necessitated due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debenture from the no-dealings date.

## Key Points

- Company: Aadhar Housing Finance Limited
- Debenture ISIN: INE538L07205 (Code: 953266)
- Debenture Series: AHFL-9.6%-25-1-26-PVT
- Record Date: January 8, 2026
- No Dealings From: January 8, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- Circular Reference: DR-794/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action notification for debenture redemption and interest payment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from January 8, 2026
- Trading members should update their systems to reflect the trading suspension
- All pending orders or positions in these debentures should be reviewed and managed accordingly

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: January 1, 2026
- **Record Date**: January 8, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Date**: January 8, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low to medium. The suspension affects only one specific series of NCDs issued by Aadhar Housing Finance Limited. Debenture holders on record as of January 8, 2026 will receive their interest payment and principal redemption amount.

**Investor Impact**: Investors holding these debentures will receive redemption proceeds and final interest payment. Those wishing to exit their positions must do so before January 8, 2026.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must ensure their systems are updated to prevent inadvertent trading in these suspended debentures. The suspension is linked to a corporate action and is expected to be temporary until the redemption process is completed.
