## Summary

BSE has announced the non-competitive bidding facility for the Government of India's auction of three Treasury Bills on June 03, 2026, as notified by RBI on May 29, 2026. Trading members and direct investors can participate via the NCB-GSec module on BSE's iBBS web-based platform.

## Key Points

- Three T-Bills are available for non-competitive bidding: 91-day, 182-day, and 364-day maturities
- Bidding platform: https://ibbs.bseindia.com >> NCB-GSec module
- Minimum lot size is Rs. 100 for all three securities
- Minimum bid amount is Rs. 10,000 for all three securities
- This is in continuation of Exchange circular no. 20180423-42 dated April 26, 2018 on the launch of the non-competitive bidding facility

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice pursuant to RBI's press release dated May 29, 2026, announcing the GoI's T-Bill auction schedule.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the NCB-GSec module on BSE's iBBS system to place bids
- Members must submit bids by June 03, 2026, 8:00 am
- Direct investors must submit bids by June 02, 2026, 5:00 pm
- Bids must comply with the minimum lot (Rs. 100) and minimum amount (Rs. 10,000) requirements

## Important Dates

- **Bid Collection Start:** June 01, 2026, from 10:00 am (24-hour availability)
- **Bid Collection End (Direct Investors):** June 02, 2026, till 5:00 pm
- **Bid Collection End (Members):** June 03, 2026, till 8:00 am
- **Auction Date:** June 03, 2026
- **Settlement Date:** June 04, 2026
