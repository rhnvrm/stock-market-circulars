## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Mini Diamonds (India) Ltd. (Scrip Code: 523373) has fixed 16 June 2026 as the record date for a bonus issue. The company will issue one bonus equity share of Rs. 2/- each for every one equity share of Rs. 2/- held (1:1 ratio). Trading in the equity shares will be on an ex-bonus basis from 16 June 2026.

## Key Points

- Bonus ratio: 1:1 (one new share for every one share held)
- Face value of bonus shares: Rs. 2/- per share
- Record date: 16 June 2026
- Ex-bonus effective date: 16 June 2026
- Settlement number: DR-652/2026-2027
- Total shares to be allotted: 117,845,580 equity shares
- Allotment date: 17 June 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification pursuant to BSE listing regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure transactions in Mini Diamonds (India) Ltd. equity shares are processed on an ex-bonus basis effective 16 June 2026.
- Settlement of bonus shares will occur under settlement number DR-652/2026-2027.

## Important Dates

- **Record Date / Ex-Bonus Date:** 16 June 2026
- **Allotment Date:** 17 June 2026
- **Circular Date:** 11 June 2026

## Impact Assessment

Existing shareholders of Mini Diamonds (India) Ltd. as of the record date (16 June 2026) will receive one additional equity share for every share held, effectively doubling their holdings. The share price is expected to adjust proportionally on the ex-bonus date. A total of 117,845,580 new equity shares will be allotted on 17 June 2026, doubling the company's equity share capital. Impact is limited to existing shareholders and routine for market participants.
