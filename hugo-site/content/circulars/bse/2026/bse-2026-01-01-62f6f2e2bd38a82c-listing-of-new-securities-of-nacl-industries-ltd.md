## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 32,501,851 new equity shares of NACL Industries Ltd (Scrip Code: 524709) issued through a rights issue. The shares were allotted on December 31, 2025, and will commence trading on January 02, 2026. These shares were issued at Rs. 1/- face value with a premium of Rs. 75.70 per share and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 32,501,851 new equity shares listed
- Face value: Rs. 1/- per share
- Issue price: Rs. 76.70 per share (Rs. 1 + Rs. 75.70 premium)
- Issued on rights basis
- ISIN: INE295D01020
- Distinctive Numbers: 201511480 to 234013330
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard rights issue listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the new securities for trading purposes effective from the listing date.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 31, 2025
- **Listing Date**: January 02, 2026
- **Notice Date**: January 01, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action with limited market impact. The rights issue increases NACL Industries' equity capital base by approximately 32.5 million shares. Since shares rank pari-passu with existing shares, there are no special rights or restrictions. Impact is limited to NACL Industries shareholders and has no broader market implications.
