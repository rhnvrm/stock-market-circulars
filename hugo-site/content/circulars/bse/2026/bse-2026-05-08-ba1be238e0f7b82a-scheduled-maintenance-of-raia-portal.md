## Summary

BSE has notified all Investment Advisers (IAs) and Registered Advisers (RAs) that the RA/IA Portal will undergo scheduled maintenance starting Friday, May 8, 2026 at 5:30 PM and will remain unavailable through Sunday, May 10, 2026. Services are expected to resume on Monday, May 11, 2026.

## Key Points

- The RA/IA Portal will be offline from **Friday, May 8, 2026 at 5:30 PM**
- Downtime expected to last through the entire weekend (Saturday and Sunday)
- Portal services expected to resume on **Monday, May 11, 2026**
- All Investment Advisers are advised to plan their activities around the downtime
- No attachments were included with this notice

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine maintenance notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Investment Advisers should complete any pending portal activities before Friday, May 8, 2026 at 5:30 PM
- Plan all RA/IA Portal-dependent activities to avoid the maintenance window

## Important Dates

- **May 8, 2026 at 5:30 PM**: Portal goes offline
- **May 10, 2026 (Sunday)**: End of maintenance window
- **May 11, 2026 (Monday)**: Expected resumption of portal services

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited to Investment Advisers and Registered Advisers who use the BSE RA/IA Portal for their compliance and membership-related activities. The maintenance window is confined to a weekend, minimizing operational disruption. No impact on trading, market data, or broader market participants.
