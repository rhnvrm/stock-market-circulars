## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (No. 20260424-14) suspending trading in the debt securities of Rajgarh Transmission Ltd. effective Wednesday, April 29, 2026. This is a continuation of the earlier Notice No. 20250606-25 dated June 06, 2025. Trading Members are directed to take note of the suspension for Scrip Code 976760 (ISIN: INE0N1Z08013).

## Key Points

- Trading in Rajgarh Transmission Ltd. debt securities will be suspended from April 29, 2026.
- Affected instrument: Scrip Code **976760**, ISIN **INE0N1Z08013**.
- This notice is issued by BSE's DCS-Listing department under the Debt segment.
- This is an update to the earlier Notice No. 20250606-25 dated June 06, 2025.
- Trading Members may contact Ms. Rutuja Mhasade (+91 22 2272 8844, debt.listing@bseindia.com) or Hardik Bhuta (Assistant Vice President) for further details.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework introduced. This notice enforces a previously initiated suspension process for debt securities of Rajgarh Transmission Ltd., consistent with BSE's powers under its listing and trading regulations for debt instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must cease trading in Scrip Code 976760 (ISIN: INE0N1Z08013) with effect from April 29, 2026.
- Members should update their systems and notify relevant clients about the impending trading suspension.
- Any queries should be directed to the designated BSE officials before the suspension date.

## Important Dates

- **April 24, 2026**: Date of this notice (No. 20260424-14).
- **April 29, 2026 (Wednesday)**: Effective date of trading suspension for Scrip Code 976760 / ISIN INE0N1Z08013.
- **June 06, 2025**: Date of the original notice (No. 20250606-25) that this circular continues.

## Impact Assessment

The suspension directly affects holders of Rajgarh Transmission Ltd. debt securities and trading members active in the BSE debt segment. After April 29, 2026, no secondary market trades will be executable for this instrument on BSE. Investors holding these bonds will lose exchange-based liquidity, and any planned transactions must be completed before the suspension date. The action suggests an ongoing compliance or issuer-related issue that has not been resolved since the original June 2025 notice.
