## Summary

BSE has announced the cancellation of trading suspension for two companies - IND Renewable Energy Ltd (Scrip Code: 536709) and Rajesh Exports Ltd (Scrip Code: 531500) - effective February 12, 2026. This follows their compliance with provisions of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015. The notice is in continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20260112-12 dated January 12, 2026.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension lifted for two companies effective February 12, 2026
- IND Renewable Energy Ltd (Scrip Code: 536709) - suspension cancelled
- Rajesh Exports Ltd (Scrip Code: 531500) - suspension cancelled
- Companies have complied with SEBI LODR Regulations 2015
- All other provisions of the original suspension notice remain unchanged

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice reverses a previous trading suspension due to companies achieving compliance with existing SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015.

## Compliance Requirements

The two listed companies have successfully met the compliance requirements under SEBI LODR Regulations that were previously non-compliant, leading to the cancellation of their trading suspension.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** February 10, 2026
- **Effective Date:** February 12, 2026 - Trading suspension cancelled and normal trading resumes
- **Reference Notice:** Exchange Notice No. 20260112-12 dated January 12, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Positive Impact:** The cancellation of trading suspension is beneficial for shareholders and investors of both companies as it restores normal trading activity. This indicates that both IND Renewable Energy Ltd and Rajesh Exports Ltd have rectified their compliance issues and met the disclosure requirements under SEBI LODR Regulations. Market participants can resume normal trading in these securities from February 12, 2026. For queries, market participants can contact BSE at bse.soplodr@bseindia.com.
