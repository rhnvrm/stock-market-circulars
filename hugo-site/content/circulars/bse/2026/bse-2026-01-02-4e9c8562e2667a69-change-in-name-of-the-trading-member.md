## Summary

BSE has announced that trading member SKY COMMODITIES INDIA PRIVATE LIMITED (Member No. 6745) will change its name to GOPOCKET INVEST TECH PRIVATE LIMITED effective Monday, January 5, 2026. This is an administrative notification to inform all trading members of the exchange about the name change.

## Key Points

- Trading member number 6745 is changing its registered name
- Old name: SKY COMMODITIES INDIA PRIVATE LIMITED
- New name: GOPOCKET INVEST TECH PRIVATE LIMITED
- Member number remains unchanged: 6745
- Contact for clarifications: membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a purely administrative update regarding member name change.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should update their records to reflect the new name of member 6745. No specific compliance action required from other members.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** January 2, 2026
- **Effective Date:** Monday, January 5, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal impact. This is an administrative name change that does not affect trading operations, member rights, or obligations. The member number remains the same, ensuring continuity in all systems and processes. Other trading members need only update their internal records for reference purposes.
