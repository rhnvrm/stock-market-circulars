## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260522-43 informs trading members and custodians of an open offer by five acquirers to acquire up to 19,20,000 equity shares (25.60% of voting share capital) of Shantai Industries Limited at Rs. 21 per equity share. The offer is conducted through the BSE acquisition window mechanism under SEBI's Tender-Offer framework.

## Key Points

- Five acquirers collectively making the open offer: Radhe Dhokla Private Limited, Pandav Dishant Kanubhai, Nikunj Vijaybhai Prajapati, Pandav Jinesh Kanaiyalal, and Pandav Pradipkumar Vijaybhai
- Offer price: Rs. 21/- (Rupees Twenty One only) per fully paid-up equity share, payable in cash
- Target acquisition: up to 19,20,000 equity shares representing 25.60% of the voting share capital
- Face value of equity shares: Rs. 2.00 each
- The offer constitutes substantial acquisition of shares/voting rights accompanied with change in control
- Letter of Offer is attached to the notice for market participants' reference
- Offer details available on BSE website

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. The offer is made under the existing framework:
- SEBI circular no. CIR/CFD/POLICYCELL/1/2015 dated April 13, 2015
- SEBI circular no. CFD/DCR2/CIR/P/2016/131 dated December 09, 2016
- BSE notice no. 20170210-16 dated February 10, 2017
- BSE notice no. 20190424-35 dated April 24, 2019
- BSE notices 20200528-32, 20201102-43, and 20210825-62 (Revised Guidelines)
- SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 2011 – Regulations 3(1) and 4

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and custodians must note the acquisition window opening and facilitate client participation as per revised SEBI and BSE guidelines
- Market participants must follow the procedures outlined in SEBI circular CFD/DCR2/CIR/P/2016/131 and BSE notice 20170210-16 for tendering shares
- Public shareholders of Shantai Industries Limited wishing to participate must tender through the BSE acquisition window during the offer period

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: May 22, 2026
- Offer Opening Date: Tuesday, May 26, 2026
- Offer Closing Date: Tuesday, June 09, 2026
- SEBI Holiday (excluded from offer period): May 28, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This open offer is specific to public shareholders of Shantai Industries Limited. Shareholders holding equity shares of the company have the opportunity to tender their shares at Rs. 21 per share to the acquirers. The offer represents a change in control event (Regulations 3(1) and 4 of SEBI SAST Regulations), indicating the acquirers are seeking to gain substantial ownership and control of the target company. Market participants trading in Shantai Industries shares should monitor price movements around the offer period. The impact is confined to this specific scrip and its shareholders.
