## Summary

BSE has issued a revised schedule for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for shares of SANMITRA COMMERCIAL LIMITED (Symbol: ZSANMCOM, Scrip Code: 512062). The acquisition window is scheduled from Tuesday, January 6, 2026 to Tuesday, January 20, 2026. The offer is for up to 1,49,76,000 equity shares at an offer price of Rs. 15.00 per share.

## Key Points

- Security: SANMITRA COMMERCIAL LIMITED (ZSANMCOM)
- ISIN: INE896J01014
- Scrip Code: 512062
- Offer Size: Up to 1,49,76,000 equity shares
- Offer Price: Rs. 15.00 per share
- Face Value: Rs. 10.00
- Market Lot: 1
- Opening Date: Tuesday, January 6, 2026
- Closing Date: Tuesday, January 20, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a procedural notice for a specific takeover transaction.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members and Custodian Members must note the revised schedule
- Trading Members must ensure UCC of the Investor is registered and/or updated with correct PAN Number and Category in the Exchange UCC database
- Trading Members can only bid for Investors having shares in demat form
- Members and Custodians should note that if no securities are accepted, no End of Issue files will be generated in Extranet

## Important Dates

**Bid Offering Period:** January 6, 2026 to January 20, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)

**Give-up/Take-up:** January 6, 2026 to January 19, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)

**Give-up (Last Day):** January 20, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)

**Take-up (Last Day):** January 20, 2026 (9:15 AM to 4:00 PM)
