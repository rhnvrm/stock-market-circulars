## Summary

BSE Index Services Private Limited has announced the addition of Fractal Industries Limited (Exchange ticker: 544707) to the BSE SME IPO Index. The stock was listed on BSE effective February 24, 2026, and will be included in the index at the open of February 25, 2026.

## Key Points

- Fractal Industries Limited (ticker: 544707) listed on BSE on February 24, 2026
- The stock will be added to the BSE SME IPO Index effective at the open of February 25, 2026
- This action is included in the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE) for February 24, 2026
- Reference circular: Notice No. 20260223-23

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index rebalancing action by BSE Index Services Private Limited following the listing of a new SME IPO.

## Compliance Requirements

No direct compliance requirements for market participants. Index fund managers and ETF operators tracking the BSE SME IPO Index should update their portfolios to reflect the addition of Fractal Industries Limited (544707) effective February 25, 2026. Queries may be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **February 24, 2026**: Fractal Industries Limited listed on BSE; corporate action file (*.SDE) updated
- **February 25, 2026**: Fractal Industries Limited added to BSE SME IPO Index at market open

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited to participants benchmarking against or replicating the BSE SME IPO Index. The addition is a standard procedure following a new SME IPO listing and does not affect broader market operations or regulatory obligations for general market participants.
