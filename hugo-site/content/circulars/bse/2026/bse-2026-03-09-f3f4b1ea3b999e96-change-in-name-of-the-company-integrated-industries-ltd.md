## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Integrated Industries Ltd (Scrip Code 531889) has officially changed its company name to Nurture Well Industries Limited. The SCRIP ID and abbreviated name on the BOLT Plus System will be updated accordingly, effective March 13, 2026.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code 531889 previously trading as Integrated Industries Ltd will now be identified as Nurture Well Industries Limited.
- The new SCRIP ID on BOLT Plus System is **NWIL**.
- The new abbreviated name on BOLT Plus System is **NWIL**.
- Change is effective from **March 13, 2026**.
- Circular issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President - Listing Compliance & Operations.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory policy changes. This is a purely administrative update reflecting the company's legal name change on BSE's trading and settlement systems.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should update their internal systems and references to reflect the new company name (Nurture Well Industries Limited) and new SCRIP ID/abbreviated name (NWIL) for Scrip Code 531889.
- No action required by investors beyond noting the updated scrip identifier.

## Important Dates

- **March 09, 2026**: Circular issued.
- **March 13, 2026**: New name, SCRIP ID (NWIL), and abbreviated name (NWIL) become effective on BOLT Plus System.

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine name change notification. The scrip code (531889) remains unchanged, ensuring continuity of trading records. Trading members and market participants need only update their reference data for the new identifier NWIL. No changes to trading terms, lot sizes, or settlement procedures are involved.
