## Summary

BSE has admitted four trading members to additional segments on BSE Ltd. with only trading rights, effective 24 February 2026. Three members have been granted access to the Debt segment and one to the Equity Derivatives segment, subject to the Rules, Bye-laws, and Regulations of the Exchange.

## Key Points

- FRR Shares & Securities Ltd. (Member Code: 6207) admitted to the **Debt** segment
- Microcurves Trading Private Limited (Member Code: 6868) admitted to the **Debt** segment
- Wealth India Financial Services Pvt. Ltd. (Member Code: 6521) admitted to the **Debt** segment
- Composite Securities Ltd. (Member Code: 3145) admitted to the **Equity Derivatives** segment
- All admissions are for trading rights only on BSE Ltd.
- Members are bound by the Exchange's Rules, Bye-laws, and Regulations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine membership admission notice under existing BSE membership regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

Existing trading members have no new compliance obligations arising from this notice. Newly admitted members must comply with all applicable BSE Rules, Bye-laws, and Regulations for their respective segments.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 24 February 2026 (Notice No. 20260224-3)
- Admission is effective as per the date of the notice.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative circular with minimal market impact. The addition of three members to the Debt segment and one to the Equity Derivatives segment may marginally increase liquidity and participation in those segments. No disruption to existing trading members or market operations is expected. Queries can be directed to membership.ops@bseindia.com.
