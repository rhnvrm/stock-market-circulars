## Summary

BSE announces the allocation of equity shares to anchor investors for the public issue of MSAFE EQUIPMENTS LIMITED. The anchor investor allocation is a critical pre-IPO phase where qualified institutional buyers subscribe to shares one day before the issue opens to retail investors.

## Key Points

- Company: MSAFE EQUIPMENTS LIMITED
- Event: Anchor investor allocation for public issue (IPO)
- Exchange: BSE (Bombay Stock Exchange)
- Announcement Date: January 27, 2026
- This allocation precedes the opening of the public issue to retail investors

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard IPO process notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Anchor investors are Qualified Institutional Buyers (QIBs) as per SEBI regulations
- Anchor allocation occurs one day before the issue opening date
- Companies must disclose anchor investor allocation details as per SEBI (ICDR) Regulations

## Important Dates

- Anchor Investor Allocation Date: January 27, 2026
- Public issue opening date for retail investors: Expected to follow after anchor allocation

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - Anchor investor participation indicates institutional confidence in the IPO and helps in price discovery. Strong anchor allocation typically signals positive sentiment for the public issue.

**Company Impact:** High - Successful anchor allocation is crucial for the company's IPO process, providing initial capital commitment and validating the issue price.

**Investor Impact:** Medium - Retail investors can gauge institutional interest through anchor allocation results, which often influences subscription decisions when the public issue opens.
