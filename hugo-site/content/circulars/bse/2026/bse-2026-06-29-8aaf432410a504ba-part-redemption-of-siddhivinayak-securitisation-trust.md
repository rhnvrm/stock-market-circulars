## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Siddhivinayak Securitisation Trust has communicated a partial redemption of its Pass Through Certificates (PTCs). As a result, the face value of the PTC has been reduced to Rs. 9,791,300.51 per PTC, effective June 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- Siddhivinayak Securitisation Trust has carried out a partial redemption of its PTCs.
- The reduced face value per PTC is Rs. 9,791,300.51.
- Scrip: SST-7.80%-28-9-30-PTC | ISIN: INE2I7F15012 | BSE Code: 977125.
- The reduced face value is applicable for trading from June 30, 2026 onwards (DR-661/2026-2027).

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework introduced. This is an operational update pursuant to the trust's partial redemption schedule under the existing securitisation arrangement.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are required to take note of the revised face value and ensure that trades in SST-7.80%-28-9-30-PTC (BSE code: 977125, ISIN: INE2I7F15012) reflect the reduced face value of Rs. 9,791,300.51 per PTC from June 30, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 29, 2026
- **Effective Date of Reduced Face Value:** June 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This partial redemption reduces the outstanding principal per PTC for Siddhivinayak Securitisation Trust. Existing holders of these PTCs will see a corresponding reduction in the face value of their holdings. Market impact is limited to participants holding this specific securitisation instrument. Trading members must update their systems to reflect the new face value before trading commences on June 30, 2026.
