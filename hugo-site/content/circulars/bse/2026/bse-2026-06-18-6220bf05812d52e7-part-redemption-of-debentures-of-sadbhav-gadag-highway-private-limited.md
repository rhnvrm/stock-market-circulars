## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260618-6 informs trading members that Sadbhav Gadag Highway Private Limited has effected a partial redemption across three of its debenture series listed on the Debt Market Segment. The reduced face values are applicable from June 19, 2026 under DR-655/2026-2027.

## Key Points

- Three debenture series of Sadbhav Gadag Highway Private Limited have been partially redeemed.
- Reduced face values are effective from **June 19, 2026**.
- SGHPL-10%-RESET RATE-28-07-29 (INE04AP07073 / Scrip Code 976067): reduced face value of **Rs. 87,575 per debenture**.
- SGHPL-RR-31-07-29-PVT (INE04AP07040 / Scrip Code 977348): reduced face value of **Rs. 96,160 per debenture**.
- SGHPL-RR-29-07-29-PVT (INE04AP07057 / Scrip Code 977339): reduced face value of **Rs. 96,160 per debenture**.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework introduced. This is a standard corporate action notification under BSE's Listing Operations (Debt segment) procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must update their systems to reflect the reduced face values for the three debenture scrips effective June 19, 2026. Members are advised to take note of the revised face values before executing any trades in these instruments.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 18, 2026
- **Effective Date for Reduced Face Value:** June 19, 2026
- **Reference:** DR-655/2026-2027

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited to trading members active in the BSE Debt Market Segment who hold or trade the three Sadbhav Gadag Highway debenture series. The partial redemption reduces the outstanding principal per debenture, which must be reflected in trade valuation and settlement. No broader market or systemic impact is anticipated.
