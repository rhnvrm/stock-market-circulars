## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that Alka India Ltd (scrip code 530889) has officially changed its name to AUDROC Limited. The SCRIP ID and ABBREVIATED NAME on the BOLT Plus System will be updated accordingly, effective June 08, 2026.

## Key Points

- Existing name: Alka India Ltd
- New name: AUDROC Limited
- Scrip Code: 530889
- New SCRIP ID on BOLT Plus System: AUDROC
- New Abbreviated Name on BOLT Plus System: AUDROC
- Effective date: June 08, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

The BOLT Plus System identifiers (SCRIP ID and ABBREVIATED NAME) for scrip code 530889 will be changed from the existing Alka India Ltd identifiers to AUDROC to reflect the company's new legal name.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should update their records and systems to reflect the new company name and trading identifiers (AUDROC) for scrip code 530889 effective June 08, 2026.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: June 03, 2026
- Effective Date of Name Change on BOLT Plus System: June 08, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative update with minimal market impact. The scrip code (530889) remains unchanged; only the display name and abbreviated identifier on the BOLT Plus trading system are updated. Traders referencing the stock by its old name or symbol should update their records before June 08, 2026 to avoid confusion.
