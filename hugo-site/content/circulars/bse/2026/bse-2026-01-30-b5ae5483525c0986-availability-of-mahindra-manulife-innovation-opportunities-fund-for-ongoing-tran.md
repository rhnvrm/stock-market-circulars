## Summary

BSE has announced that Mahindra Manulife Innovation Opportunities Fund will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective February 02, 2026. The fund will also support systematic investment plans (SIP), systematic transfer plans (STP), and systematic withdrawal plans (SWP). This follows the earlier suspension referenced in Notice No. 20260108-40.

## Key Points

- Fund reopening date: February 02, 2026
- Available on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Both Regular and Direct plans available
- Three options per plan: Growth, IDCW Reinvestment, and IDCW Payout
- Total of 6 scheme variants with separate ISINs
- Facilities available: Subscription, Redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP

## Scheme Details

**Regular Plan Options:**
- Growth (ISIN: INF174V01CX8)
- IDCW Reinvestment (ISIN: INF174V01CY6)
- IDCW Payout (ISIN: INF174V01CZ3)

**Direct Plan Options:**
- Growth (ISIN: INF174V01DA4)
- IDCW Reinvestment (ISIN: INF174V01DB2)
- IDCW Payout (ISIN: INF174V01DC0)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding fund availability.

## Compliance Requirements

BSE members should update their systems to enable transactions for the specified scheme ISINs from February 02, 2026. Investors seeking to invest in or redeem from this fund can do so through the BSE StAR MF Platform starting from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** January 30, 2026
- **Effective Date:** February 02, 2026 - Fund reopens for all transactions

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal - This is a fund-specific operational update.

**Investor Impact:** Positive for existing and prospective investors of Mahindra Manulife Innovation Opportunities Fund who will regain the ability to make subscriptions, redemptions, and systematic transactions through the BSE platform.

**Operational Impact:** BSE members handling mutual fund transactions need to ensure their systems are configured to process orders for these six scheme variants starting February 02, 2026.
