## Summary

BSE's Surveillance department (DOSS) issued Notice No. 20260416-27 dated April 16, 2026, informing members and investors that clarifications have been received from Hindustan Copper Ltd and VA Tech Wabag Ltd. These clarifications were sought by the Exchange to verify the accuracy of news articles appearing in various newspapers, websites, and television channels.

## Key Points

- BSE proactively sought clarifications from two listed companies regarding news articles circulating in the media
- Hindustan Copper Ltd (Scrip Code: 513599) responded to a news article from The Hindu Business Line
- VA Tech Wabag Ltd (Scrip Code: 533269) responded to a news article from CNBC TV18
- Replies from both companies are attached to the notice for transparency to members and investors
- Notice issued by Deputy Vice President Sanjay Jain and Manager Shreya Ghose, Surveillance department

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this notice. It is an informational disclosure under BSE's standard surveillance and investor protection framework.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements are imposed on members or investors. The companies in question have already fulfilled their obligation by submitting clarifications to the Exchange.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** April 16, 2026
- No deadlines or effective dates applicable

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine surveillance disclosure with minimal market impact. The notice serves an investor protection purpose by ensuring accurate information is available regarding news circulating about Hindustan Copper Ltd and VA Tech Wabag Ltd. Investors holding or considering positions in these two stocks should review the attached company clarifications to assess any material developments referenced in the underlying news articles.
