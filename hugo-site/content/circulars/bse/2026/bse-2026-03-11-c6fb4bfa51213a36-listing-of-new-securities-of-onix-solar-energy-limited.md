## Summary

BSE has listed 4,576,305 new equity shares of ONIX SOLAR ENERGY LIMITED (Scrip Code: 513119) issued on a rights basis. These shares are available for trading on the Exchange with effect from Thursday, March 12, 2026.

## Key Points

- 4,576,305 equity shares of Rs. 10.00 each issued at a premium of Rs. 536.00 on rights basis
- ISIN: INE173M01012
- Distribution Numbers: 20493886 to 25070190
- Date of Allotment: March 9, 2026
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Scrip Code: 513119

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for newly issued securities pursuant to a rights issue.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed to permit trading of the newly listed securities from March 12, 2026. No additional compliance actions are required beyond standard trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** March 9, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** March 12, 2026 (Thursday)

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 4,576,305 new equity shares to the tradable float of ONIX SOLAR ENERGY LIMITED at an issue price of Rs. 546 (Rs. 10 face value + Rs. 536 premium). This rights issue expands the company's equity base and provides liquidity to rights shareholders. Market impact is moderate and localized to existing and prospective shareholders of the company.
