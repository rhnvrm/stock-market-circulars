BSE has published the Draft Offer Document (DRHP) for Technocrats Plasma Systems Limited's SME IPO, filed on December 31, 2025. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and is available on BSE SME website for public comments for a minimum period of 21 days.

## Key Points

- Company: Technocrats Plasma Systems Limited
- Filing Date: December 31, 2025
- Notice Date: January 01, 2026
- Document Type: Draft Offer Document (DRHP) for SME IPO
- Regulatory Compliance: Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Public Comment Period: 21 days from notice date
- Document Available At: www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Comments Email: equity.ipo@bseindia.com

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural notice for SME IPO draft offer document publication as per existing ICDR (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations.

- Public and stakeholders can submit comments on the draft offer document to equity.ipo@bseindia.com within 21 days from January 01, 2026

## Important Dates

- Notice Publication Date: January 01, 2026
- Public Comment Period Deadline: January 22, 2026 (21 days from notice date)

This notice has minimal market-wide impact as it is specific to the SME IPO process for Technocrats Plasma Systems Limited. The impact is limited to:
