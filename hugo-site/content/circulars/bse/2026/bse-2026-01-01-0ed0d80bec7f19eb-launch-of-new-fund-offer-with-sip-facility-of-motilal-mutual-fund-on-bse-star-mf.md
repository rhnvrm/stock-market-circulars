## Summary

MOTILAL MUTUAL FUND is launching MOTILAL OSWAL DIVERSIFIED EQUITY FLEXICAP PASSIVE FUND OF FUNDS as a New Fund Offer (NFO) with SIP facility on BSE StAR MF Platform. The NFO period runs from January 02, 2026 to January 15, 2026, with a tentative allotment date of January 21, 2026. Both Regular Plan and Direct Plan Growth options are available.

## Key Points

- NFO Period: January 02, 2026 to January 15, 2026
- Tentative Allotment Date: January 21, 2026
- Two scheme options available: Regular Plan - Growth (ISIN: INF247L01GM7) and Direct Plan - Growth (ISIN: INF247L01GL9)
- Scheme Category: EQUITY
- Extended trading available till midnight on January 15, 2026 (lumpsum purchases only)
- SIP/XSIP registration authentication required before 3:00 PM on NFO closure date
- Transactions without client-initiated funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 will be cancelled
- Pending transactions remain in system till 3:00 PM on last day or T+5, whichever is earlier

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds are available in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on January 21, 2026 for allotment
- Cheques and OTM payments acceptable during NFO period subject to receipt of clear funds in ICCL account by 6:00 PM on January 21, 2026
- Switches accepted during NFO period only if funds received on or before allotment date; funds received after allotment date will not be considered
- Transactions without client-initiated funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day will be cancelled with CC with N flag reported to RTA/AMC
- SIP/XSIP registrations must be authenticated by clients before 3:00 PM on NFO closure date (January 15, 2026)

## Important Dates

- **January 02, 2026**: NFO Start Date
- **January 15, 2026**: NFO End Date (extended till midnight for lumpsum purchases only)
- **January 15, 2026, 3:00 PM**: Deadline for SIP/XSIP registration authentication
- **January 21, 2026**: Tentative Allotment Date
- **January 21, 2026, 6:00 PM**: Deadline for clear funds in ICCL Account
- **T+1, 9:00 AM**: Deadline for client fund initiation (transactions without funds by this time will be cancelled)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal - This is a routine NFO launch for a new mutual fund scheme on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

**Operational Impact**: Low - Affects only BSE StAR MF Platform members and investors choosing to participate in this specific NFO. Members need to ensure proper fund settlement procedures and timelines are followed for successful allotment.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to investors interested in diversified equity flexicap passive fund of funds strategy offered by MOTILAL MUTUAL FUND.
