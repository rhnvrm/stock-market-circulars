## Summary

BSE observed significant price/volume movement in 15 listed scrips and sought clarifications from the respective companies. Replies have been received from 8 companies and are attached to the notice, while replies from the remaining 7 companies are still awaited.

## Key Points

- Companies that have submitted clarification replies:
  1. Aarnav Fashions Ltd (Scrip Code: 539562)
  2. Epigral Ltd (Scrip Code: 543332)
  3. Kaarya Facilities and Services Ltd (Scrip Code: 540756)
  4. Meghmani Organics Ltd (Scrip Code: 543331)
  5. Praruh Technologies Ltd (Scrip Code: 544538)
  6. Rander Corporation Ltd (Scrip Code: 531228)
  7. Sri Lotus Developers and Realty Ltd (Scrip Code: 544469)
  8. VJTF Eduservices Ltd (Scrip Code: 509026)
- Companies whose replies are still awaited:
  1. CMX Holdings Ltd (Scrip Code: 532217)
  2. Crazy Snacks Ltd (Scrip Code: 544807)
  3. Darjeeling Industries Ltd (Scrip Code: 539770)
  4. Gconnect Logitech and Supply Chain Ltd (Scrip Code: 544156)
  5. Kinetic Trust Ltd (Scrip Code: 531274)
  6. Mapro Industries Ltd (Scrip Code: 509762)
  7. Sujala Trading & Holdings Ltd (Scrip Code: 539117)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is a routine surveillance disclosure notice issued under BSE's Department of Surveillance (DOSS) to inform trading members and investors of company clarifications on unusual price/volume activity.

## Compliance Requirements

Companies with replies still awaited are expected to respond to the Exchange's clarification request. Trading members and investors are advised to take note of the disclosed replies before making trading decisions in these scrips.

## Important Dates

Notice issued: 22 July 2026. No further deadline specified for pending replies.

## Impact Assessment

This notice provides transparency on companies experiencing unusual trading activity, helping investors make informed decisions. The scrips listed may continue to see heightened scrutiny or surveillance action (e.g., ASM/GSM) if price/volume anomalies persist, particularly for companies yet to respond.
