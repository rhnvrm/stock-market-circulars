## Summary

BSE has revised the price bands for 17 scrips in the equity segment, effective March 10, 2026. The revised bands range from 2% to 20%, replacing existing levels as part of the exchange's ongoing surveillance measures.

## Key Points

- Price bands revised for 17 scrips effective March 10, 2026
- Revised bands range from 2% (most restrictive) to 20% (least restrictive)
- Circular issued under Notice No. 20260309-33 dated March 9, 2026
- Members may contact bse.surv@bseindia.com for clarifications

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must adhere to the revised price bands for all affected scrips from March 10, 2026 onwards
- Orders placed outside the revised price band limits will be rejected by the exchange system
- Members should update their trading systems and inform clients accordingly

## Important Dates

- **March 9, 2026**: Circular issued
- **March 10, 2026**: Revised price bands come into effect

## Impact Assessment

Traders and investors holding or intending to trade in these 17 scrips will face revised daily trading limits from March 10, 2026. Securities with tighter bands (2%) such as Beryl Securities, CDG Petchem, Citi Port Financial Services, and IB Infotech Enterprises will have the most restricted intraday movement. Arco Leasing Ltd, with a revised band of 20%, has the widest permitted movement. These revisions are typically triggered by surveillance observations and may indicate heightened volatility or risk concerns for the tighter-band scrips.
