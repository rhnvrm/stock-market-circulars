## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that two T-bills/Government Securities — a 182-day T-bill and a 364-day T-bill — will mature on their respective redemption dates. Accordingly, trading in these securities is suspended with effect from June 16, 2026.

## Key Points

- Two T-bills are maturing and trading in them is suspended from June 16, 2026
- Scrip Code 805158 (182TB18626, ISIN: IN002025Y388) — 182-day T-bill
- Scrip Code 805081 (364TB18626, ISIN: IN002025Z120) — 364-day T-bill
- Reference: DR-652/2026-2027
- Notice issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President – Listing Compliance & Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice triggered by the maturity of these government securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must not deal in the following securities from June 16, 2026 onwards:
- Scrip Code 805158 (182TB18626 | ISIN: IN002025Y388)
- Scrip Code 805081 (364TB18626 | ISIN: IN002025Z120)

## Important Dates

- **June 15, 2026**: Notice date
- **June 16, 2026**: Effective date of trading suspension (redemption date for both T-bills)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine administrative action following the scheduled maturity of two government T-bills. Trading members holding these securities should ensure no further transactions are executed from June 16, 2026. Holders will receive redemption proceeds on the maturity date per standard government securities settlement procedures.
