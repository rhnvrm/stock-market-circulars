## Summary

BSE has listed two new privately placed debt securities on its Debt Segment effective April 7, 2026. The securities are issued by Kosamattam Finance Limited and Kanakadurga Finance Limited, both traded exclusively in dematerialised form with a tick size of 1 paise.

## Key Points

- Two new debt instruments freshly listed on BSE Debt Segment via private placement
- Both securities tradeable only in dematerialised form
- Tick size for both securities: 1 paise
- Placement Memorandum available at BSE debt memorandum portal
- Trading members may contact BSE debt department for clarifications: 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice under BSE's debt market segment listing framework for privately placed instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form
- Refer to the Placement Memorandum on the BSE website for further details before trading

## Important Dates

**Kosamattam Finance Limited (Scrip Code: 977712)**
- Date of Allotment: April 6, 2026
- Interest Payment: Monthly from May 6, 2026 to January 6, 2029
- Date of Redemption: January 6, 2029

**Kanakadurga Finance Limited (Scrip Code: 977713)**
- Date of Allotment: April 2, 2026
- Interest Payment: Monthly from May 2, 2026 to May 2, 2031
- Date of Redemption: May 2, 2031
