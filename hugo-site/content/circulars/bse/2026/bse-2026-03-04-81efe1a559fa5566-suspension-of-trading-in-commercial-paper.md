## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260304-11 informing trading members that the commercial papers of Adani Enterprises Ltd (Scrip Code: 730359) and Marwadi Shares and Finance Limited (Scrip Code: 731023) are set to mature on March 5, 2026. Trading members are directed not to deal in these commercial papers with effect from March 5, 2026.

## Key Points

- Two commercial papers are reaching their redemption date on March 5, 2026
- Adani Enterprises Ltd CP: Scrip Code 730359, ISIN INE423A14YI2, identifier AEL-6-3-26-CP
- Marwadi Shares and Finance Limited CP: Scrip Code 731023, ISIN INE138I14016, identifier MSFL-6-3-26-CP
- Trading in both instruments is suspended effective March 5, 2026
- Reference: DR-833/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational notice under existing BSE debt segment rules requiring suspension of trading in commercial papers upon maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in Scrip Code 730359 (AEL-6-3-26-CP) from March 5, 2026
- Trading members must cease all dealings in Scrip Code 731023 (MSFL-6-3-26-CP) from March 5, 2026
- Members holding these instruments should prepare for redemption settlement on the maturity date

## Important Dates

- **March 4, 2026**: Notice issued by BSE
- **March 5, 2026**: Redemption date and effective date of trading suspension for both commercial papers

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited and routine. Both commercial papers are short-term debt instruments reaching natural maturity. The suspension affects only members holding or trading these specific CPs in the debt segment. Equity trading in Adani Enterprises and Marwadi Shares and Finance Limited remains unaffected. Holders will receive redemption proceeds as per standard settlement procedures.
