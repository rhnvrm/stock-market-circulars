## Summary

BSE has notified members that Shriram Mutual Fund has revised the face value of the Shriram Overnight Fund from Rs. 10 per unit to Rs. 1,000 per unit with effect from 30 May 2026. The scheme will be unavailable for transactions on the BSE StAR MF platform during a closure period of 27–29 May 2026, and will resume for transactions from 1 June 2026.

## Key Points

- Face value of Shriram Overnight Fund revised from **Rs. 10/- per unit** to **Rs. 1,000/- per unit**
- Change effective from **30 May 2026** on the BSE StAR MF platform
- Scheme suspended for transactions during closure period: **27 May 2026 to 29 May 2026** (two working days)
- Transactions will resume on BSE StAR MF platform from **1 June 2026**
- Change initiated by Shriram AMC via a notice cum addendum

## Regulatory Changes

The face value of Shriram Overnight Fund is being revised 100-fold — from Rs. 10 per unit to Rs. 1,000 per unit — as communicated by Shriram AMC. This is an AMC-level structural change to the scheme, effective on the BSE StAR MF platform from 30 May 2026.

## Compliance Requirements

- BSE members and distributors must note the temporary suspension of the scheme on BSE StAR MF platform from 27–29 May 2026
- No transactions (purchases, redemptions, switches) should be routed through BSE StAR MF for Shriram Overnight Fund during the closure period
- Systems or workflows referencing the face value of this scheme must be updated to reflect Rs. 1,000 per unit from 30 May 2026 onwards

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Circular Date | 27 May 2026 |
| Closure Period (no transactions) | 27 May 2026 – 29 May 2026 |
| Face Value Change Effective | 30 May 2026 |
| Resumption of Transactions on BSE StAR MF | 1 June 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

This change primarily affects distributors, investors, and platform operators transacting in Shriram Overnight Fund via BSE StAR MF. The 100x increase in face value (Rs. 10 → Rs. 1,000) is a common re-denomination seen in overnight/liquid funds to align with industry norms, and does not affect the underlying NAV or investor wealth. However, the 3-day transaction blackout (27–29 May 2026) requires members to plan redemptions or purchases accordingly. Automated SIP or bulk transaction systems referencing the old face value will need updates before 1 June 2026.
