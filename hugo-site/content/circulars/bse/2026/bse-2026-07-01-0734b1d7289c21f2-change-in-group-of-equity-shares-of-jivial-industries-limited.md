## Summary

BSE has issued a notice, in continuation of its earlier Notice No. 20260630-48 dated June 30, 2026, informing trading members that the equity shares of Jivial Industries Limited (Scrip Code: 544805) will be moved from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment (M Group), effective Wednesday, July 15, 2026.

## Key Points

- Jivial Industries Limited's shares (Scrip Code: 544805) currently trade in the Trade for Trade segment under MT Group.
- Effective July 15, 2026, the scrip will shift to the Rolling segment and be classified under M Group.
- This is a follow-up notice to BSE's earlier Notice No. 20260630-48 dated June 30, 2026.
- Trading members can contact Mr. Anurag Jain (Tel: 022-2272 8822) for further details.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced; this is an administrative reclassification of the trading segment/group for a specific SME-listed scrip under BSE's DCS-Listing department.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members dealing in Jivial Industries Limited shares should update their systems and processes to reflect the change from Trade for Trade (MT Group) to Rolling segment (M Group) settlement mechanics from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- June 30, 2026: Original Notice No. 20260630-48 issued.
- July 1, 2026: Current clarifying notice issued.
- July 15, 2026 (Wednesday): Effective date of segment/group change.

## Impact Assessment

The shift from Trade for Trade (which typically mandates delivery-based settlement without intraday netting) to the Rolling segment (M Group, allowing normal rolling settlement) generally eases trading restrictions and can improve liquidity for the scrip. Impact is limited to traders, investors, and members dealing in Jivial Industries Limited shares.
