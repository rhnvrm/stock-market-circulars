## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members that the equity shares of Fractal Industries Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the BSE SME segment. The scrip has been assigned code 544707 with symbol FIL and ISIN INE1W3Q01011. The specific listing date and further security details will be communicated through a subsequent notice.

## Key Points

- Fractal Industries Limited will be listed on BSE under the SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544707 | Symbol: FIL | ISIN: INE1W3Q01011
- The exact date of listing has not been specified; a separate notice will be issued
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this circular. It is a routine administrative notice announcing an upcoming new listing on the BSE SME platform.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the scrip details (code 544707, symbol FIL, ISIN INE1W3Q01011) and await the subsequent notice from BSE confirming the official listing date and full security details before initiating any trades.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: February 23, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced via a separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a preliminary listing notification with moderate market relevance. The actual market impact will be realised on the listing date, which is yet to be announced. Trading members and investors interested in Fractal Industries Limited should monitor BSE communications for the follow-up notice containing the listing date and complete security details. As an SME segment listing, the liquidity and trading volume are expected to be lower compared to mainboard listings.
