## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that ONIX SOLAR ENERGY LIMITED (BSE Code: 513119) has fixed 15 May 2026 as the record date for its rights issue. Trading in the company's equity shares must be conducted on an ex-rights basis effective from 15 May 2026 under the Rolling Settlement Segment (DR-631/2026-2027).

## Key Points

- Rights issue ratio: 8 (eight) new equity shares for every 17 (seventeen) existing equity shares held
- Face value of each rights equity share: Rs. 10/-
- Premium per share: Rs. 41/-
- Rights Issue Price: Rs. 51/- per share (face value + premium), payable on application
- Record Date: 15 May 2026
- Ex-Rights basis effective from: 15 May 2026 (Settlement No. DR-631/2026-2027)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notice under BSE listing compliance and operations guidelines.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all transactions in ONIX SOLAR ENERGY LIMITED equity shares are executed on an ex-rights basis from 15 May 2026 onwards.
- Members should update their systems to reflect the ex-rights status for settlement number DR-631/2026-2027.

## Important Dates

- **Record Date:** 15 May 2026
- **Ex-Rights Effective Date:** 15 May 2026
- **Notice Date:** 12 May 2026
- **Settlement Number:** DR-631/2026-2027

## Impact Assessment

This rights issue affects existing shareholders of ONIX SOLAR ENERGY LIMITED (BSE: 513119). Shareholders on record as of 15 May 2026 are entitled to subscribe to 8 new equity shares for every 17 held at Rs. 51 per share. The relatively modest premium (Rs. 41 on a Rs. 10 face value) and the standard ratio suggest a targeted capital raise. Market impact is limited to the company's existing shareholder base; no broader market or systemic implications are expected.
