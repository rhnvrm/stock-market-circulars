## Summary

BSE's Surveillance Department sought clarification from Tata Steel Ltd (Scrip Code: 500470) regarding a news article published on Business Standard. The exchange has confirmed receipt of the company's reply, notifying members and investors accordingly.

## Key Points

- BSE proactively sought clarification from Tata Steel Ltd to verify accuracy of a news article circulating in media.
- The article in question appeared on Business Standard (business-standard.com).
- Tata Steel Ltd has submitted its reply/clarification to the exchange.
- The notice is issued by BSE's Surveillance Department (DOSS) to inform market participants.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action is required from market participants. Members and investors are advised to refer to the company's clarification for accurate information regarding the news article.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 05 June 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine disclosure by BSE confirming receipt of a company clarification on a media report. Investors in Tata Steel Ltd (NSE: TATASTEEL, BSE: 500470) should review the attached company reply for details on the specific news item being addressed.
