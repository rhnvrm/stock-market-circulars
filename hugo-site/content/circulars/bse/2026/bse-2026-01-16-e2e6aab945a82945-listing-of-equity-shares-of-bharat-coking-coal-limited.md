BSE has informed trading members that equity shares of Bharat Coking Coal Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544678, symbol BHARATCOAL, and ISIN number INE05XR01022. The exact listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

- Company Name: Bharat Coking Coal Limited
- Scrip Code: 544678
- Trading Symbol: BHARATCOAL

- Specific listing date and security details pending separate notification
