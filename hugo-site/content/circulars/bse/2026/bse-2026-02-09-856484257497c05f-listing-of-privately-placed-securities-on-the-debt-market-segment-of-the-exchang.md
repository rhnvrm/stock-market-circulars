## Summary

BSE has listed commercial papers issued by five companies on its debt market segment effective February 9, 2026. The securities were issued on private placement basis and will trade in dematerialized form only. Trading members can contact the debt department for clarifications.

## Key Points

- Five commercial paper issuances from 360 ONE Alternates Asset Management Limited, Poonawalla Fincorp Limited, 360 ONE Prime Limited, KEC International Ltd., and 360 ONE WAM Limited
- All securities trade in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size is 1 paise for all listed securities
- Face value of Rs. 5,00,000 per commercial paper
- Tenure ranges from 43 days to 364 days
- Interest rates range from 7.15% to 8.40% p.a.
- All commercial papers are cumulative interest instruments

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Market lot size is 1 unit for all listed commercial papers

## Important Dates

- **Listing Effective Date**: February 9, 2026
- **Allotment Dates**: February 6, 2026 (four issuances) and February 9, 2026 (one issuance)
- **Redemption Dates**: Range from March 24, 2026 to February 5, 2027

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational circular announcing new debt instrument listings. The impact is limited to debt market participants interested in trading commercial papers. The listing provides additional short-term debt investment options with investment-grade ratings (A1+) from multiple issuers. No impact on equity markets or broader market operations.
