## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the 91-day Treasury Bill **91TB260226** (Scrip Code: 805148, ISIN: IN002025X356) is approaching its redemption date of February 26, 2026. Trading in this T-Bill is suspended with effect from **February 24, 2026** (DR-827/2025-2026).

## Key Points

- T-Bill 91TB260226 (Scrip Code 805148, ISIN IN002025X356) will mature on its scheduled redemption date of February 26, 2026.
- Trading members are directed not to deal in this T-Bill from February 24, 2026 onwards.
- This is a standard maturity-driven suspension under BSE's debt segment procedures.
- The circular references DR-827/2025-2026, the official demat redemption reference.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice is a routine operational instruction consistent with existing BSE procedures for T-Bill maturity and de-listing from the debt segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members** must cease all buying and selling activity in Scrip Code 805148 (ISIN IN002025X356) with effect from February 24, 2026.
- Members should ensure no new trades are executed in this instrument on or after the suspension date.
- Existing holders should note the instrument will be redeemed on the maturity date and settlement will proceed accordingly.

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Circular Date | February 23, 2026 |
| Trading Suspension Effective | February 24, 2026 |
| Redemption / Maturity Date | February 26, 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact, routine notice. The suspension applies solely to one 91-day T-Bill nearing maturity. No market-wide disruption is expected. Holders of this instrument will receive redemption proceeds on the maturity date as per standard RBI/BSE settlement procedures. Trading members should update their systems to block trading in Scrip Code 805148 from February 24, 2026.
