## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (No. 20260227-4) informing trading members that the equity shares of Accord Transformer & Switchgear Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course under the SME segment. The exact date of listing and further security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Accord Transformer & Switchgear Limited equity shares will be listed on BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: **544710**
- Trading Symbol: **ACCORDTS**
- ISIN: **INE132201018**
- Listing date and security details to be announced via a separate notice
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing admission notice for a new SME IPO company.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to note the upcoming listing of ACCORDTS on the SME segment
- Further operational details (lot size, price band, etc.) will be provided in a subsequent notice prior to the listing date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** February 27, 2026
- **Listing Date:** To be announced via separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This notice signals the imminent listing of Accord Transformer & Switchgear Limited on the BSE SME platform. The impact is limited to SME segment participants and investors who participated in the IPO. Trading members should monitor for the follow-up notice confirming the listing date and detailed security specifications before enabling trading in scrip code 544710.
