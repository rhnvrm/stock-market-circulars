## Summary

BSE issued a notice regarding significant price/volume movements observed in 19 listed scrips. As part of its surveillance process, the Exchange had written to these companies seeking clarification to ensure trading members and investors have the latest relevant information. Replies have been received and published for 12 companies, while responses from 7 companies remain awaited.

## Key Points

- BSE observed significant price/volume movement in 19 scrips in the recent past and sought clarification from each company.
- Replies received and attached (Replies1) from: Capital Trade Links Ltd (538476), Delta Corp Ltd (532848), Dhanvantri Jeevan Rekha Ltd (531043), Esha Media Research Ltd (531259), Indag Rubber Ltd (509162), Optimus Finance Ltd (531254), PC Jeweller Ltd (534809), Prospect Consumer Products Ltd (543814), Rishabh Instruments Ltd (543977), Sonata Software Ltd (532221), Veljan Denison Ltd (505232), and Vesuvius India Ltd (520113).
- Replies still awaited (Replies2) from: CMX Holdings Ltd (532217), Elango Industries Ltd (513452), Fine Line Circuits Ltd (517264), Marble City India Ltd (531281), Mayur Leather Products Ltd (531680), SC Agrotech Ltd (526081), and Sujala Trading & Holdings Ltd (539117).
- Notice issued under the Company related category, Equity segment, by BSE's Department of Surveillance (DOSS).
- Signed by Sanjay Jain (Deputy Vice President, Surveillance) and Shreya Ghose (Manager, Surveillance).

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a routine surveillance disclosure under BSE's ongoing price/volume monitoring framework.

## Compliance Requirements

Companies for which replies are awaited are expected to respond to BSE's clarification request. Trading members and investors are advised to review the attached replies for the latest available information on the flagged scrips.

## Important Dates

Notice dated 03 July 2026; no specific deadline mentioned for pending replies.

## Impact Assessment

This disclosure has limited direct market impact but signals continued surveillance attention on the listed scrips due to unusual price/volume activity. Investors in the flagged companies, particularly those with replies still pending, should monitor for further updates as unresolved clarifications can sometimes precede additional surveillance action such as ASM/GSM placement.
