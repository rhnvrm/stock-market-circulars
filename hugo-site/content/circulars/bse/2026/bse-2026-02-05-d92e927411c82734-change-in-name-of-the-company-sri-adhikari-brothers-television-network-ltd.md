## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs, through the Central Processing Centre, has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for Sri Adhikari Brothers Television Network Limited (CIN: L32200MH1994PLC083853). The company's name has been officially changed to Aqylon Nexus Limited with effect from January 23, 2026. This is a company limited by shares originally incorporated as Sri Adhikari Brothers Television Network Limited.

## Key Points

- **Old Name**: Sri Adhikari Brothers Television Network Limited
- **New Name**: Aqylon Nexus Limited
- **CIN**: L32200MH1994PLC083853
- **Effective Date**: January 23, 2026
- **Company Type**: Company limited by shares
- **Registered Office**: 6th Floor, Oberoi Chambers, Oberoi Complex, Next to Laxmi Industries Estate, Oshiwara New Link, Andheri West - 400053, Maharashtra, India
- **Approved by**: M. Yadubhushana Rao, Central Processing Centre
- **Certificate issued**: January 23, 2026 at 16:11:37 IST

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this circular. This is a notification of administrative change in the company's registered name as per the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014, Rule 29.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Dual Name Display Obligation**: The company must display its old name (Sri Adhikari Brothers Television Network Limited) along with the new name (Aqylon Nexus Limited) for a period of **two years** at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders
- All stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company and its directors on the MCA website at www.mca.gov.in/MCA21

## Important Dates

- **January 23, 2026**: Effective date of name change
- **January 23, 2028**: Expected end date for mandatory dual name display requirement (two years from change date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a standard corporate administrative action that does not alter the company's business operations, capital structure, or stakeholder rights and liabilities.

**Operational Impact**: The company will need to update all official documentation, letterheads, signage, and communications to reflect the new name while continuing to display the old name for the mandatory two-year period.

**Investor Impact**: No direct impact on shareholders' rights or holdings. The shares will trade under the new company name, and investors should update their records accordingly. Verification of company status can be done through the MCA portal.
