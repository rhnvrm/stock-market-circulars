## Summary

BSE has revised the schedule for transferring MODERN DIAGNOSTIC & RESEARCH CENTRE LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544673) equity shares from Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to Rolling segment (M Group). The transfer date is postponed by one day from Wednesday, January 21, 2026 to Thursday, January 22, 2026 due to a trading holiday on January 15, 2026 for Municipal Corporation Elections in Maharashtra.

## Key Points

- Company: MODERN DIAGNOSTIC & RESEARCH CENTRE LIMITED (SME IPO)
- Scrip Code: 544673
- Original transfer date: Wednesday, January 21, 2026
- Revised transfer date: Thursday, January 22, 2026
- Transfer: From MT Group (Trade for Trade segment) to M Group (Rolling segment)
- Reason for postponement: Trading holiday on January 15, 2026 for Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Elections
- This circular updates three previous notices: 20260106-16, 20260107-2, and 20260112-8

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a schedule adjustment to an existing planned group transfer.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members should:
- Note the revised transfer date of January 22, 2026
- Adjust their trading systems and processes accordingly
- Contact Mr. Anurag Jain (022-2272 8822) for further details

## Important Dates

- **January 13, 2026**: Notice date
- **January 15, 2026**: Trading holiday (Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Elections)
- **January 21, 2026**: Original transfer date (cancelled)
- **January 22, 2026**: Revised transfer date to Rolling segment (M Group)

## Impact Assessment

The one-day postponement has moderate operational impact on trading members who need to adjust their planning for the segment transfer. The change ensures proper scheduling around the election-related trading holiday. Market participants trading this SME IPO stock should be aware of the revised timeline for the shift from Trade for Trade to Rolling segment.
