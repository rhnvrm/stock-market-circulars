## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that NJ Mutual Fund will offer two New Fund Offer (NFO) schemes—NJ Value Fund and NJ Momentum Fund, both equity schemes—along with SIP facility on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The NFO period runs from July 10, 2026 to July 24, 2026, with a tentative allotment date of July 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- Two new equity NFO schemes launched: NJ Value Fund and NJ Momentum Fund
- Both schemes available with SIP facility on BSE StAR MF Platform
- NFO subscription window: July 10, 2026 to July 24, 2026
- Tentative allotment date: July 30, 2026
- Scheme plans and options detailed in Annexure I

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an operational notice regarding product availability on the BSE StAR MF platform, not a regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

- Transactions without client fund initiation by 9:00 AM on T+1 day (including during extended NFO period) will be cancelled, with CC flagged 'N' reported to RTA/AMC
- Transactions with initiated payment remain pending until 3:00 PM on the last fund payout day or T+5 day, whichever is earlier
- Clear funds must be available in the ICCL account by 6:00 PM on July 30, 2026, for allotment eligibility
- Cheque and OTM payments accepted during NFO period, subject to clear funds in ICCL account by 6:00 PM on July 30, 2026
- Switch transactions accepted during NFO period, subject to funds received before allotment date; switch-out funds received on/after allotment date will not be considered for allotment
- Lumpsum Purchase transactions accepted until midnight, July 24, 2026
- SIP/XSIP registrations must be client-authenticated by 3:00 PM on July 24, 2026 (NFO closure date)

## Important Dates

- NFO open: July 10, 2026
- NFO close (Lumpsum): midnight, July 24, 2026
- SIP/XSIP authentication deadline: 3:00 PM, July 24, 2026
- Tentative allotment date: July 30, 2026
- ICCL fund availability deadline: 6:00 PM, July 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice primarily relevant to BSE StAR MF trading members and distributors facilitating NJ Mutual Fund NFO transactions. It has no direct bearing on listed equities or broader market trading activity, and mainly requires operational compliance by members regarding fund settlement timelines and transaction processing rules.
