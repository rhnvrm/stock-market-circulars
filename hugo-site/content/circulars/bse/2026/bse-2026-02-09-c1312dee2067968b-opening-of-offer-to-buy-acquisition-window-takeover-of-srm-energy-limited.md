## Summary

Mr. Umesh Narpatchand Sanghvi and Mrs. Sapna Sanghvi (collectively the Acquirers) have initiated an open offer to acquire 23,55,600 fully paid-up equity shares of SRM Energy Limited, representing 26% of the voting share capital. The offer price is ₹4 per equity share (face value ₹10) payable in cash. This offer is made pursuant to Regulations 3(1) and (4) of SEBI (SAST) Regulations, 2011 for substantial acquisition of shares/voting rights accompanied with change in control and management of the target company.

## Key Points

- Acquirers: Mr. Umesh Narpatchand Sanghvi and Mrs. Sapna Sanghvi
- Target Company: SRM Energy Limited (CIN: L17100DL1985PLC303047)
- Offer Size: 23,55,600 equity shares (26% of voting share capital)
- Offer Price: ₹4 per fully paid-up equity share of face value ₹10
- Payment Mode: Cash
- Offer Type: Not conditional upon minimum level of acceptance per Regulation 19(1)
- Not a competing offer under Regulation 20 of SEBI (SAST) Regulations
- Tendered shares cannot be withdrawn once acceptance is submitted
- No statutory approvals currently required; subject to any future approvals if applicable

## Regulatory Framework

This open offer is made under Securities and Exchange Board of India (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 2011 as amended (SEBI SAST Regulations), specifically under Regulations 3(1) and (4) dealing with substantial acquisition of shares/voting rights with change in control and management.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public shareholders of SRM Energy Limited can tender their shares during the tendering period
- Shareholders must submit Form of Acceptance cum acknowledgement and Transfer Deed
- Once shares are tendered with requisite documents, acceptance cannot be withdrawn
- Acquirers may revise offer price and/or size before commencement of last working day before tendering period starts
- Any revisions or withdrawal would be announced in same newspapers as original Detailed Public Statement
