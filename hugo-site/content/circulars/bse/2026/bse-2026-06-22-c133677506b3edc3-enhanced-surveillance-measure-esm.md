## Summary

BSE has updated the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework effective June 23–24, 2026. Newly included securities (Annexure I) will attract a minimum 100% margin on all open positions as of June 23, 2026 and on new positions from June 24, 2026. Some securities are being downgraded to Stage I, while Stage II securities will trade under Trade-for-Trade with a 2% price band and Periodic Call Auction. Securities exiting the framework are listed in Annexure II.

## Key Points

- Securities in Annexure I meet the ESM inclusion criteria and will attract minimum 100% margin from June 24, 2026.
- Certain securities will move to a lower stage (Stage I) within the ESM framework effective June 23, 2026.
- All newly included ESM securities will shift from Rolling Settlement to Trade-for-Trade segment from June 24, 2026.
- Stage II securities will be placed under Trade-for-Trade with a 2% price band and Periodic Call Auction from June 23, 2026.
- Securities exiting the ESM framework from June 23, 2026 are listed in Annexure II.
- A consolidated list of all securities currently under ESM is provided in Annexure III.
- ESM classification is a market surveillance action and should not be construed as adverse action against the company.

## Regulatory Changes

This notice updates the ESM framework in continuation of prior notices: 20230602-44, 20230718-46, 20240809-42, 20240920-63, 20241004-65, and 20250725-61. Changes include addition of securities to ESM, stage downgrades to Stage I, and removals from the framework. ESM operates alongside all other prevailing surveillance measures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure minimum 100% margin is collected on open positions as of June 23, 2026 and all new positions from June 24, 2026 for newly included ESM securities.
- Trading members must route affected securities through the Trade-for-Trade segment from the respective effective dates.
- For Stage II securities, trading must comply with the 2% price band and Periodic Call Auction mechanism.
- Upon exit from ESM, the price band reverts to the pre-ESM applicable band, unless another surveillance measure's band takes precedence.
- Queries may be directed to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **June 22, 2026** — Notice date.
- **June 23, 2026** — Stage I downgrade effective; Stage II securities move to Trade-for-Trade with 2% price band and Periodic Call Auction; securities in Annexure II exit the ESM framework.
- **June 24, 2026** — Minimum 100% margin applies to newly included ESM securities (on open positions as of June 23 and all new positions); shift from Rolling Settlement to Trade-for-Trade effective.

## Impact Assessment

Affected securities face significantly tighter trading conditions: mandatory 100% margin increases capital requirements for traders, while the shift to Trade-for-Trade eliminates the ability to net off intraday positions. Stage II securities under Periodic Call Auction further restrict price discovery and liquidity. These measures collectively reduce speculative activity and may deter retail participation in the affected counters. The exit of securities from ESM (Annexure II) provides relief to those stocks, with price bands reverting to pre-ESM levels subject to no other overlapping surveillance framework.
