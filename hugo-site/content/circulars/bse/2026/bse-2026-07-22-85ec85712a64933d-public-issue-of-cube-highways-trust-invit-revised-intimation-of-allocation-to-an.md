## Summary

BSE has issued a notice conveying a revised intimation of allocation to anchor investors for the public issue of CUBE HIGHWAYS TRUST InvIT, dated July 22, 2026. This revised intimation supersedes the original anchor allocation notice (No. 20260721-32) issued the previous day, July 21, 2026.

## Key Points

- Notice relates to the ongoing public issue of CUBE HIGHWAYS TRUST InvIT (Infrastructure Investment Trust).
- A revised anchor investor allocation intimation letter has been issued in continuation of the earlier notice No. 20260721-32.
- The revised letter, "Revised_Anchor_Intimation_Letter_CHT," is attached to the exchange filing.
- Signed by Mangesh Tayde, Vice President, Listing Business Relationship, BSE.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a procedural/administrative update to a previously issued anchor investor allocation intimation.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action required from market participants beyond anchor investors reviewing the revised allocation details in the attached letter.

## Important Dates

- July 21, 2026: Original anchor allocation intimation notice (No. 20260721-32) issued.
- July 22, 2026: Revised anchor allocation intimation issued.

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This notice pertains specifically to the mechanics of anchor investor allocation within the CUBE HIGHWAYS TRUST InvIT public issue process and does not affect trading in any listed securities.
