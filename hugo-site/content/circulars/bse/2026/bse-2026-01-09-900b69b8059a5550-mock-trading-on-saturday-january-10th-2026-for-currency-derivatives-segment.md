## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Currency Derivatives segment on Saturday, January 10, 2026. The session is designed for trading members to test their trading applications, including those using third-party platforms, empaneled vendors, or in-house developed systems through ETI APIs. A new BOLT Pro TWS version 12.50 will be released, with details to be published separately. The mock trading is for testing and familiarization only and will not create any actual trading obligations or liabilities.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Saturday, January 10, 2026
- Available for Currency Derivatives and Cross Currency Derivatives segments
- New BOLT Pro TWS version 12.50 will be released
- Trading members can test various functionalities including exceptional market conditions
- No margin obligations, pay-in/pay-out obligations, or legal rights/liabilities will be created
- Feedback required by 3:00 PM on the same day

## Mock Trading Session Timings

| Session | From | To |
|---------|------|----|
| Log-in | 10:15 AM | 11:00 AM |
| Continuous Trading for Currency Derivatives | 11:00 AM | 02:00 PM |
| Continuous Trading for Cross Currency Derivatives | 11:00 AM | 02:00 PM |
| Trade Modification (Currency) | 02:10 PM | - |
| Trade Modification (Cross Currency) | 02:10 PM | - |

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are requested to participate actively in the mock trading session
- Members must provide feedback by 3:00 PM on January 10, 2026
- Contact BSE Tech Support (022-22728053 or bse.tech@bseindia.com) or BSE Helpdesk (022-45720400/600 & 022-69158500 or bsehelp@bseindia.com) for queries

## Important Dates

- **January 10, 2026**: Mock trading session
- **January 10, 2026 by 3:00 PM**: Feedback submission deadline

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: None. This is a mock trading session with no real market impact.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Provides opportunity for members to test their trading systems and familiarize themselves with the new BOLT Pro TWS version 12.50. Members using third-party platforms or in-house systems can validate their applications under simulated conditions, including exceptional market scenarios.

**Trading Impact**: No actual trades will be executed, and no margin, pay-in, or pay-out obligations will arise from the mock session.
