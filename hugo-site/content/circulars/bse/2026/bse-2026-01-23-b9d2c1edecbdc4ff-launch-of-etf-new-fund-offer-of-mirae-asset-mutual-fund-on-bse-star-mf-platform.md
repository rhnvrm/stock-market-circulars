## Summary

MIRAE ASSET Mutual Fund will launch a New Fund Offer (NFO) for MIRAE ASSET NIFTY INDIA INFRASTRUCTURE & LOGISTICS ETF on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The NFO period runs from January 27, 2025 to February 09, 2025, with a tentative allotment date of February 12, 2026. Both regular and direct plan variants will be available.

## Key Points

- Two scheme variants available: Regular Plan and Direct Plan, both under Equity category
- NFO extended till midnight on February 09, 2026 for lumpsum purchases only
- Transactions without client-initiated funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day will be cancelled
- Transactions with initiated payments remain pending until 3:00 PM on last day of NFO funds payout to AMC or T+5, whichever is earlier
- Cheques and OTM payments acceptable during NFO period subject to clear funds receipt
- Switch transactions accepted during NFO period subject to funds receipt on or before allotment date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard NFO launch announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds are available in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on February 11, 2026 for unit allotment
- Transactions without client-initiated funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day will be cancelled and reported to RTA/AMC with CC and N flag
- For switch transactions, funds must be received on or before NFO allotment date; late receipts will not be considered for allotment

## Important Dates

- **NFO Start Date**: January 27, 2025
- **NFO End Date**: February 09, 2025 (extended till midnight for lumpsum purchases)
- **Clear Funds Deadline**: February 11, 2026 by 6:00 PM
- **Tentative Allotment Date**: February 12, 2026
- **Payment Initiation Cutoff**: 9:00 AM on T+1 day
- **Pending Transaction Deadline**: 3:00 PM on last day of NFO payout or T+5, whichever is earlier

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact as it announces a routine NFO for a sector-specific ETF focused on infrastructure and logistics. The impact is limited to:

- Members and distributors on BSE StAR MF Platform who wish to offer this product
- Investors interested in gaining exposure to India's infrastructure and logistics sector through an ETF structure
- No impact on equity markets or existing securities trading
- Standard operational procedures apply with specific timelines for fund collection and allotment
