## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that under the Anchor Investor (AI) portion of the public issue of MILLWORKS TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED, 13,25,200 equity shares have been subscribed on July 13, 2026, at a price of Rs. 331 per equity share. This follows the company's official announcement dated July 13, 2026.

## Key Points

- Anchor investors have been allocated 13,25,200 equity shares of MILLWORKS TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED.
- The anchor allocation price was fixed at Rs. 331 per equity share.
- The allotment was completed and reported on July 13, 2026.
- The notice was issued by BSE's Listing Business Relationship department under the Company related category, Equity segment.
- An Anchor Investor Letter and the company's announcement are enclosed as attachments to the notice.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is an informational notice regarding anchor investor allocation in an ongoing public issue, not a regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific action is required from trading members beyond noting the anchor allocation details for reference in connection with the public issue.

## Important Dates

- July 13, 2026: Anchor investor shares subscribed and allotted; company announcement issued; BSE notice released.

## Impact Assessment

The anchor allocation at Rs. 331 per share provides an early price signal for MILLWORKS TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED's public issue and typically indicates institutional investor confidence ahead of the IPO opening for broader subscription. The direct market impact is limited to prospective investors evaluating the issue, with no immediate effect on existing listed securities or trading operations.
