## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 1,36,50,506 new units of Mindspace Business Parks REIT (Scrip Code: 543217) have been listed and are permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Tuesday, May 12, 2026. These units were allotted to the sponsor on a preferential basis at an issue price of Rs. 484.89/- per unit.

## Key Points

- 1,36,50,506 new units of Mindspace Business Parks REIT listed effective May 12, 2026
- Units allotted to sponsor on a preferential basis at Rs. 484.89/- per unit
- Distribution numbers: 648342977 to 661993482
- Date of allotment: May 7, 2026
- ISIN: INE0CCU25019
- New units rank pari-passu with existing units of the REIT
- Lock-in details for units as per Annexure I

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notice pursuant to a completed preferential allotment to the sponsor of the REIT.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to update their systems to reflect the new listed units from May 12, 2026
- Lock-in restrictions apply to allotted units as detailed in Annexure I; trading of locked-in units is restricted until the respective lock-in expiry dates

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** May 7, 2026
- **Effective Date for Trading:** May 12, 2026 (Tuesday)
- **Lock-in Expiry:** As per Annexure I (unit-wise details)

## Impact Assessment

The listing of approximately 1.36 crore new units on a preferential basis to the sponsor increases the total unit capital of Mindspace Business Parks REIT. Since the allotment is to the sponsor and subject to lock-in, the immediate impact on free-float and market liquidity is limited. The issue price of Rs. 484.89/- serves as a reference point for valuation. General unitholders are unaffected in terms of rights, as the new units rank pari-passu with existing units.
