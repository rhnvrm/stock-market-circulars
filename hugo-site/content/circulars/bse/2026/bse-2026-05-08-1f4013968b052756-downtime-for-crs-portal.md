## Summary

BSE has notified all members, including BSE Star MF Platform participants, that the CRS (Centralized Registration System) portal will be unavailable on May 9, 2026 from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM (10 hours) due to scheduled maintenance activity.

## Key Points

- CRS portal downtime: May 9, 2026, 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM
- Duration: 10 hours
- Reason: Scheduled maintenance activity
- Affects all BSE members and BSE Star MF Platform users
- No portal access or transactions possible during this window

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational maintenance notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must plan and complete any CRS portal activities before 8:00 AM on May 9, 2026
- Members should reschedule any CRS-dependent workflows to outside the downtime window

## Important Dates

- **May 9, 2026, 8:00 AM**: CRS portal downtime begins
- **May 9, 2026, 6:00 PM**: CRS portal expected to resume

## Impact Assessment

All BSE members and BSE Star MF Platform participants will be unable to access the CRS portal for a 10-hour window on May 9, 2026. Members should plan accordingly and avoid scheduling CRS-dependent tasks during this period. The impact is temporary and limited to a single business day.
