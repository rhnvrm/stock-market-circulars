## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing Trading Members, Self-Certified Syndicate Banks (SCSBs), and Intermediaries that Krishival Foods Limited has requested activation of the Call Money Module on BSE's IBBS (Internet Based Book Building Software) platform for collecting the First and Final Call money on its partly paid-up equity shares, in accordance with Exchange notice no. 20210104-37 dated January 4, 2021.

## Key Points

- Issuer: Krishival Foods Limited
- Type of Issue: Call Money Notice (First and Final Call)
- Security Type: Partly Paid Equity Shares
- Security Symbol: KRISHCM
- Market Lot: 1
- Number of Partly Paid-up Equity Shares: 33,33,160
- Call Money Amount Payable: Rs. 195.00 per equity share
- Registrar to the Issue: Purva Sharegistry (India) Private Limited (Contact: Ms. Deepali Dhuri, Tel: +91 2241343255, Email: support@purvashare.com)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this notice implements the existing framework under SEBI circular dated December 8, 2020, which mandates SCSBs and Trading Members with 3-in-1 type accounts to submit call money notice details collected during the issue period via the IBBS Call Money Module.

## Compliance Requirements

- SCSBs and Trading Members with 3-in-1 type accounts must submit details of call money notices collected from investors through the IBBS platform during the bidding period.
- Bidding must be conducted between 10:00 a.m. and 5:00 p.m. daily during the issue period.
- Queries related to regulatory/business rules should be directed to BSE Listing Sales (listing.sales@bseindia.com, 022-2272 8846/5063/8179); bidding-related queries to IBBS Operations (ibbs.ops@bseindia.com, 022-2272 8376/8436/8226).

## Important Dates

- Issue Opening Date: Tuesday, July 21, 2026
- Issue Closing Date: Tuesday, August 04, 2026
- Issue Period: 15 days
- Bidding Hours: 10:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. daily

## Impact Assessment

This notice primarily affects existing holders of Krishival Foods Limited's partly paid-up equity shares (symbol KRISHCM), who are required to pay the balance call money of Rs. 195 per share to convert their holdings into fully paid-up equity shares. SCSBs and trading members with 3-in-1 accounts need to ensure operational readiness on the IBBS platform to process these collections within the specified window. The broader market impact is limited, as this concerns a single company's capital structure completion event rather than systemic market operations.
