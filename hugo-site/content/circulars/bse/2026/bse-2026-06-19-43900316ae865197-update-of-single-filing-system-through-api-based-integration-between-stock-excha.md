## Summary

BSE has extended the single filing system through API-based integration between stock exchanges to include XBRL filings for additional Para B events under Regulation 30 of SEBI LODR 2015, effective June 20, 2026. Listed entities must submit XBRL filings within 24 hours of the corresponding PDF filing. Additionally, the standalone XBRL utility for the 'Awarding/bagging of orders' event is being discontinued and merged into the consolidated Para B utility.

## Key Points

- Single filing system extended to cover XBRL filings for multiple Para B of Part A of Schedule III events under SEBI LODR 2015, effective June 20, 2026.
- PDF filings will initially be accepted as compliance under Regulation 30; however, XBRL filing must follow within 24 hours.
- New XBRL utilities available at: Listing Center >> Listing Compliance >> XBRL >> XBRL utility.
- XBRL filings will also be available in iXBRL (human-readable) format at https://www.bseindia.com/corporates/ann.
- Standalone XBRL utility for 'Awarding/bagging/receiving/amendment/termination of orders not in normal course of business' is discontinued from June 20, 2026 and merged into the consolidated Para B events utility.
- Applies to equity and debt listed companies; REITs and InvITs are excluded from the new events.

## Regulatory Changes

- Extension of single filing system (per Exchange notice 20240930-60 dated September 30, 2024) to cover the following Para B events under Regulation 30 of SEBI LODR 2015:
  1. Commencement or postponement of commercial production/operations of any unit/division.
  2. Strategic, technical, manufacturing, or marketing tie-ups; new lines of business; closure of operations.
  3. Capacity addition or product launch.
  4. Awarding, bagging, amendment, or termination of orders/contracts not in normal course of business (now merged into unified utility).
  5. Binding agreements (loan agreements or others) and their revisions, amendments, or terminations.
  6. Disruption of operations due to natural calamity, force majeure, strikes, or lockouts.
  7. Changes arising from regulatory framework changes applicable to the listed entity.
  8. Pendency or outcome of material litigation/disputes.

## Compliance Requirements

- All listed entities (equity and debt) must:
  - Continue submitting PDF filings as before for Regulation 30 disclosures.
  - Additionally, submit XBRL filings within **24 hours** of the PDF filing for the above Para B events.
  - Use the updated XBRL utilities available at Listing Center from June 20, 2026.
  - Discontinue use of the standalone XBRL utility for the 'awarding/bagging orders' event; use the consolidated Para B utility instead.
- REITs and InvITs are **not** required to submit XBRL for the new events at this stage.

## Important Dates

- **June 20, 2026**: Effective date for extension of XBRL single filing system for new Para B events.
- **June 20, 2026**: Standalone XBRL utility for 'awarding/bagging orders' event discontinued; merged into Para B utility.
- Within **24 hours** of PDF filing: Deadline for submitting corresponding XBRL filing.

## Impact Assessment

This update primarily affects compliance officers and filing teams at all listed equity and debt companies. The expansion of XBRL-based single filing to additional Para B events streamlines multi-exchange disclosure by eliminating duplicate filings. However, it introduces a new 24-hour XBRL filing obligation post PDF submission, requiring entities to have updated XBRL utilities and processes in place by June 20, 2026. The consolidation of the 'orders' event into the Para B utility reduces fragmentation. Impact on investors is indirect — improved disclosure standardisation and faster cross-exchange visibility.
