## Summary

BSE has permitted trading in 5,97,60,558 new equity shares of Fusion Finance Limited (Scrip Code: 543652) with effect from Tuesday, January 6, 2026. These shares result from the conversion of partly paid shares issued on rights basis to fully paid up equity shares of Rs. 10/- each. The new shares are pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 5,97,60,558 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each listed
- Shares converted from partly paid to fully paid status
- Originally issued on rights basis
- ISIN: INE139R01012
- Distinctive Numbers: 6356224 to 107380109 and 53404334 to 107803637 (total range: 101023886 to 161207970)
- Shares have same rights as existing equity shares (pari-passu)
- Scrip Code: 543652

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are permitted to trade in these new securities starting from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 5, 2026
- **Trading Commencement Date**: January 6, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement for Fusion Finance Limited following the conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid shares. The listing increases the tradable equity base of the company. Impact is limited to shareholders and traders of Fusion Finance Limited with no broader market implications.
