## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 7,00,000 new equity shares of Retaggio Industries Limited (Scrip Code: 544391) are listed and permitted to trade effective Monday, July 13, 2026. These shares were issued on a preferential basis to a Non-Promoter allottee at an issue price of Rs. 26/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- plus premium of Rs. 16/-) pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 7,00,000 equity shares of face value Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 16/- (total issue price Rs. 26/-)
- Shares issued to a Non-Promoter allottee on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distinctive numbers: 17166161 to 17866160
- Date of allotment: 09/03/2026
- ISIN: INE0KWJ01014
- Lock-in applicable to all 7,00,000 shares until 20/02/2027

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing notification under BSE Listing Operations, not a new regulatory requirement.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific action required from trading members beyond updating systems to reflect the new tradable quantity from the effective date; the shares remain subject to lock-in until 20/02/2027 and cannot be traded during that period despite being listed.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: 09/03/2026
- Listing/Trading Effective Date: Monday, July 13, 2026
- Lock-in Expiry: 20/02/2027

## Impact Assessment

The impact is low given the small quantum (7,00,000 shares) relative to typical listed floats, and because the shares are under lock-in until February 2027, meaning they will not immediately affect free float or trading liquidity. The listing primarily formalizes the preferential allotment from warrant conversion for record-keeping and future tradability post lock-in.
