## Summary

Yajur Fibres Limited has filed its post-IPO shareholding pattern as on January 11, 2026, pursuant to Regulation 31(1)(a). The company is listed on the BSE SME segment with ISIN INE0CNJ01010. Total issued capital stands at 2,26,82,873 equity shares, with promoter holding at 69.49% (1,57,62,873 shares) and public holding at 30.51% (69,20,000 shares).

## Key Points

- Total equity shares: 2,26,82,873 fully paid up shares
- Promoter & Promoter Group: 15 shareholders holding 1,57,62,873 shares (69.49%)
- Public shareholders: 3,514 shareholders holding 69,20,000 shares (30.51%)
- All promoter shares are locked-in (100% of promoter holding)
- All shares are held in dematerialized form
- No partly paid shares, convertible securities, warrants, ESOPs, or depository receipts issued
- No promoter shares are pledged or under non-disposal undertaking
- Company is classified as SME with equity shares class
- Not a Public Sector Undertaking (PSU)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard shareholding pattern disclosure filing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Filed under Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015
- Post-IPO shareholding pattern disclosure requirement
- Promoter lock-in of 100% shares in compliance with SME IPO regulations

## Important Dates

- Shareholding pattern date: January 11, 2026
- Filing date: January 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Low impact routine disclosure. The shareholding structure shows compliance with minimum public shareholding norms for SME segment. Complete promoter lock-in indicates recent IPO listing with standard regulatory compliance. No encumbrances or concerns identified in the shareholding pattern. The filing provides transparency to investors about the ownership structure post-listing.
