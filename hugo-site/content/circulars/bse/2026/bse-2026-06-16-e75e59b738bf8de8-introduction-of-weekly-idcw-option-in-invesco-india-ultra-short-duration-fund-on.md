## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260616-30 informing members that Invesco Mutual Fund is introducing a Weekly Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal (IDCW) option under the Invesco India Ultra Short Duration Fund on the BSE StAR MF platform. The new option includes both Reinvestment and Payout facilities and is effective from 16 June 2026.

## Key Points

- Invesco Mutual Fund is launching a Weekly IDCW option under Invesco India Ultra Short Duration Fund.
- The option offers both Reinvestment and Payout facilities.
- The new option is available on the BSE StAR MF platform.
- Effective date: 16 June 2026.
- A Notice-cum-Addendum issued by Invesco Mutual Fund is attached to the circular for member reference.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced. This is a product-level addition by Invesco Mutual Fund, communicated to BSE members as an informational notice.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action is required of BSE members. Members dealing in Invesco mutual fund schemes should note the availability of the new Weekly IDCW option and update their systems and offerings accordingly to enable client transactions on BSE StAR MF.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** 16 June 2026 — Weekly IDCW option (Reinvestment & Payout) available on BSE StAR MF platform.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to investors in Invesco India Ultra Short Duration Fund who may now choose a Weekly IDCW plan with Reinvestment or Payout through the BSE StAR MF platform. This is a routine fund house product enhancement and does not affect broader market operations, trading rules, or compliance frameworks.
