## Summary

BSE has listed 1,39,49,323 equity shares of IndoStar Capital Finance Limited (Scrip Code: 541336) issued on preferential basis to promoters. The shares will be available for trading from January 30, 2026, with a lock-in period until August 29, 2027.

## Key Points

- Number of shares listed: 1,39,49,323 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each
- Issue type: Preferential allotment to promoters
- Issue price: Rs. 184/- (Rs. 10 face value + Rs. 174 premium)
- Trading commencement: January 30, 2026
- Distinctive numbers: 151886570 to 165835892
- Date of allotment: December 20, 2025
- ISIN: INE896L01010
- Ranking: Pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities listing. No specific compliance actions required beyond standard trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 20, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: January 30, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry**: August 29, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a preferential allotment to promoters with a lock-in period of approximately 32 months. The shares cannot be traded by promoters until August 2027, ensuring no immediate dilution effect on existing shareholders. This represents approximately 13.95 million shares added to the company's equity capital.
