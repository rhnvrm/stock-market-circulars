## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Bilaspur Pathrapali Road Pvt Ltd has formally changed its name to Bilaspur Pathrapali Road Limited. The change affects two debt-listed scrips and will be reflected on the BOLT Plus System effective June 12, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company name changes from **Bilaspur Pathrapali Road Pvt Ltd** to **Bilaspur Pathrapali Road Limited**
- Two debt scrips (codes 977216 and 977217) are affected
- SCRIP IDs and abbreviated names on BOLT Plus System remain unchanged (775BPRL35 and 785BPRL30 respectively)
- Change effective from June 12, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

The SCRIP ID and ABBREVIATED NAME for the company's debt-listed scrips on the BOLT Plus System will be updated to reflect the new legal name. The scrip identifiers themselves (775BPRL35, 785BPRL30) are retained without modification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the updated company name in their records. No additional action is required as the scrip identifiers on BOLT Plus remain the same.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 09, 2026
- **Effective Date of Name Change on BOLT Plus:** June 12, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal operational impact. This is a routine legal name change (conversion from private limited to limited company) for a debt-listed entity. The BOLT Plus scrip identifiers are unchanged, so trading workflows and references by scrip ID are unaffected. Trading members only need to update internal records that reference the full company name.
