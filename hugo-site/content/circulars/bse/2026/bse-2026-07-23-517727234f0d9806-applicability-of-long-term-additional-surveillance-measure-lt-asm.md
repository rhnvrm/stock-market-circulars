## Summary

BSE has issued an update to the Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LT-ASM) framework, referencing prior notices dating back to March 2018. The revised framework imposes 100% margins on certain securities, moves some securities to higher LT-ASM stages, applies lower price bands to others, transfers some securities to T/XT/MT/TS trading groups, and removes certain securities from the framework entirely. A consolidated list of securities currently under the framework is provided in Annexure III.

## Key Points

- 100% margin requirement applicable on all open positions as of July 27, 2026 and new positions from July 28, 2026 onwards, for securities in Annexure I (Part A)
- Securities moved to higher LT-ASM stages effective July 24, 2026, listed in Annexure I (Part B)
- Lower Price Band applicable w.e.f. July 24, 2026 for the same set of securities
- Securities in Annexure I (Part D) continue under LT-ASM but will be transferred to T / XT / MT / TS trading groups w.e.f. July 28, 2026
- Securities moving out of the LT-ASM framework w.e.f. July 24, 2026 are listed in Annexure II
- Shortlisting is based on XBRL submissions by listed companies and reviewed periodically
- The framework operates alongside other prevailing surveillance measures
- Inclusion under this framework is a market surveillance action, not an adverse judgment on the company

## Regulatory Changes

This notice implements the periodic review of the LT-ASM framework as established under Exchange notices dated March 21, 2018, October 27, 2018, December 4, 2020, August 9, 2024, and September 20, 2024, and the related June 4, 2021 notice covering group transfers. No new regulatory framework is introduced; this is an application of the existing framework to updated lists of securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must apply 100% margin on affected securities from the specified dates
- Members must observe the revised (lower) price bands for securities newly placed in the framework
- Members must account for group reclassification (T/XT/MT/TS) for affected securities from July 28, 2026
- Queries can be directed to bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- July 24, 2026: Lower price band applicable; securities move to higher LT-ASM stages; securities move out of LT-ASM framework
- July 27, 2026: Reference date for existing open positions subject to 100% margin
- July 28, 2026: 100% margin applicable on new positions; securities transferred to T/XT/MT/TS groups

## Impact Assessment

The measures increase trading costs and restrict price movement for securities newly added to or elevated within the LT-ASM framework, likely reducing liquidity and volatility for those specific stocks. Securities exiting the framework will see price bands reinstated to pre-framework levels, easing trading conditions. Overall market impact is limited to the specific securities named in the annexures rather than the broader market.
