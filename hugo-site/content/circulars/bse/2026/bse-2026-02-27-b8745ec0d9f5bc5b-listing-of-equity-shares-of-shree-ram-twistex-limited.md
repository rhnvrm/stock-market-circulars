## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the equity shares of Shree Ram Twistex Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange. The scrip has been assigned Scrip Code 544716 and symbol SRTL with ISIN INE19GK01015. The specific date of listing and further security details will be announced through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Equity shares of Shree Ram Twistex Limited are approved for listing on BSE.
- Scrip Code: 544716 | Symbol: SRTL | ISIN: INE19GK01015.
- The exact listing date has not yet been announced and will be communicated separately.
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this circular. It is a standard listing admission notice informing trading members of an upcoming new equity listing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should note the assigned scrip code (544716), symbol (SRTL), and ISIN (INE19GK01015) in preparation for the listing.
- Members should await the subsequent notice from BSE that will confirm the listing date and full security details before initiating any trading activity.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** February 27, 2026
- **Listing Date:** To be announced via a separate BSE notice.

## Impact Assessment

This is a preliminary listing announcement with low immediate market impact. Trading members and prospective investors in Shree Ram Twistex Limited should monitor BSE for the follow-up notice confirming the listing date. Once listed, the stock will be tradeable on the BSE Equity segment under scrip code 544716.
