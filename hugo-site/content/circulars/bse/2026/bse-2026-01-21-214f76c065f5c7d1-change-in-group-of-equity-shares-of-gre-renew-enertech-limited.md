## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of GRE RENEW ENERTECH LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544682), an SME IPO company, will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling settlement segment (M Group) effective February 04, 2026. This change follows the exchange's previous notice dated January 20, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: GRE RENEW ENERTECH LIMITED - SME IPO
- Scrip Code: 544682
- Current Segment: Trade for Trade segment (MT Group)
- New Segment: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective Date: Wednesday, February 04, 2026
- Notice Number: 20260121-4
- Contact: Mr. Nitinkumar Pujari, Tel: 022-2272 8398

## Regulatory Changes

The trading mechanism for GRE RENEW ENERTECH LIMITED will transition from Trade for Trade settlement to Rolling settlement. This represents a shift from a more restrictive trading framework (MT Group) to a standard rolling settlement framework (M Group), which typically allows for more flexible trading including intraday positions and normal delivery settlement cycles.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members are required to:
- Note the change in group classification effective February 04, 2026
- Update their systems to reflect the new group classification (M Group)
- Contact Mr. Nitinkumar Pujari on Tel. No. 022-2272 8398 for any further details or clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: Tuesday, January 20, 2026 (Initial Notice No. 20260120-47)
- **Current Notice Date**: Wednesday, January 21, 2026
- **Effective Date**: Wednesday, February 04, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This change positively impacts the liquidity and trading flexibility of GRE RENEW ENERTECH LIMITED shares. The move from Trade for Trade (MT Group) to Rolling settlement (M Group) removes trading restrictions, allowing for:
- Normal intraday trading
- Standard T+2 settlement cycle
- Increased market accessibility for retail and institutional investors
- Potential improvement in stock liquidity and price discovery

Trading members handling this scrip should prepare for the transition and inform their clients accordingly.
