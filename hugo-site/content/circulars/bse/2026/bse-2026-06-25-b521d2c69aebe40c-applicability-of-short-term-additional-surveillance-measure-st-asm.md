## Summary

BSE has notified the inclusion of securities under Short-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) Stage I and Stage II, effective June 30, 2026. Securities under Stage I will attract a margin of 50% (or existing margin, whichever is higher, capped at 100%), while Stage II securities will attract a flat 100% margin. The specific securities are listed in Annexure I attached to the notice.

## Key Points

- Securities satisfying criteria for ST-ASM Stage I inclusion are listed in Annexure I
- Securities satisfying criteria for ST-ASM Stage II inclusion are also listed in Annexure I
- ST-ASM Stage I margin: 50% or existing margin, whichever is higher (max 100%)
- ST-ASM Stage II margin: 100% or existing margin, whichever is higher (max 100%)
- Effective date: June 30, 2026 for all open positions as on June 29, 2026 and new positions from June 30, 2026
- ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures
- Shortlisting under ASM is a surveillance action and should not be construed as adverse action against the company

## Regulatory Changes

This notice is issued further to prior ASM framework revision notices: 20240920-71 (Sep 20, 2024), 20181027-1 (Oct 27, 2018), 20190719-33 (Jul 19, 2019), 20201204-56 (Dec 4, 2020), 20230925-49 (Sep 25, 2023), and 20240920-63 (Sep 20, 2024). The current notice applies the existing ASM framework provisions to newly shortlisted securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must apply the enhanced margin requirements on all open positions in affected securities as on June 29, 2026
- Members must collect the enhanced margin on all new positions in affected securities created from June 30, 2026 onwards
- The price band of any scrip exiting the ASM framework will be reinstated to its pre-ASM price band, unless it remains under another surveillance measure
- Members may contact BSE Surveillance at bse.surv@bseindia.com for clarifications

## Important Dates

- **June 25, 2026**: Notice date
- **June 29, 2026**: Reference date for open positions subject to new margin requirements
- **June 30, 2026**: Effective date for enhanced margin applicability (Stage I: 50%; Stage II: 100%)

## Impact Assessment

Securities included under ST-ASM Stage I face a minimum margin increase to 50%, while Stage II securities face a mandatory 100% margin requirement. This significantly raises the cost of holding or creating positions in affected scrips, likely reducing liquidity and trading volumes. Market participants with existing open positions in these securities as on June 29, 2026 must ensure sufficient margin is available before June 30, 2026 to avoid margin shortfall. The measure is purely surveillance-driven and does not reflect on the fundamental health of the listed companies.
