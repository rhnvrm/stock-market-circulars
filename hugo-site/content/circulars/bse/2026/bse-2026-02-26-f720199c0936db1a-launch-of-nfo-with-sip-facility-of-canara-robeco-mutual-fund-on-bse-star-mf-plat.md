## Summary

BSE has informed members that CANARA ROBECO Mutual Fund will launch the CANARA ROBECO BANKING AND FINANCIAL SERVICES FUND as a New Fund Offer (NFO) on the BSE StAR MF Platform from February 27, 2026 to March 13, 2026, with SIP facility. The fund is an equity scheme available in Regular and Direct plans across Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options. Tentative allotment date is March 20, 2026.

## Key Points

- NFO period: February 27, 2026 to March 13, 2026 on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Six scheme variants offered under Regular and Direct plans (Growth, IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment)
- SIP/XSIP registrations must be authenticated by clients before 3:00 PM on the NFO closure date (March 13, 2026)
- Transactions without client-initiated funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day will be cancelled; CC with N flag will be reported to RTA/AMC
- Transactions with initiated payments remain pending until 3:00 PM on the last fund payout day or T+5, whichever is earlier
- Extended NFO period till midnight on March 13, 2026 for lumpsum purchases only
- Switches accepted during NFO period only if funds received on or before the allotment date

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for NFO launch on BSE StAR MF Platform per existing mutual fund transaction processing rules.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clients initiate funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day to avoid cancellation
- Clear funds must be available in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on March 18, 2026 for allotment eligibility
- Cheques and OTM payments acceptable during NFO period subject to clear fund receipt by 6:00 PM on March 18, 2026
- SIP/XSIP authentication must be completed by 3:00 PM on March 13, 2026
- Switch-out funds received after the NFO allotment date will not be considered for allotment
