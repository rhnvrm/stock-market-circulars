## Summary

IIFL Finance Limited submitted a clarification to BSE regarding a news article titled "IIFL Finance Q3 Results: Stock tanks 15% despite sharp surge in Gold loans; Here's why" published on CNBCTV18.com on January 22, 2026. The company confirmed that it is not aware of any undisclosed information that could explain the stock price movement and that the price decline appears to be market sentiment driven. The company's Q3 FY2025-26 financial results were announced in compliance with SEBI LODR Regulations and are available in the public domain.

## Key Points

- Company confirms no undisclosed information or events that could explain the stock price movement
- Financial results for quarter and nine months ended December 31, 2025 were announced to stock exchanges in compliance with SEBI LODR Regulations
- Stock price movement appears to be market sentiment driven or factors beyond company's control
- No ongoing negotiations or events beyond those disclosed in Board Meeting outcome dated January 22, 2026
- No initiation of any new regulatory or legal proceedings against the company
- Company confirms compliance with Regulation 30 read with Schedule III of SEBI LODR Regulations, 2015

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Company confirms adherence to disclosure requirements under Regulation 30 of SEBI LODR Regulations, 2015
- Company commits to promptly inform stock exchanges in case of any further material developments
- Company to ensure continued adherence to regulatory guidelines

## Important Dates

- **January 22, 2026**: News article published on CNBCTV18.com
- **January 22, 2026**: Clarification submitted to BSE
- **January 22, 2026**: Board Meeting outcome disclosed (referenced in clarification)
- **December 31, 2025**: Quarter and nine months end for financial results

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: The clarification addresses a significant stock price decline of 15% following Q3 results announcement. While the company reported a surge in gold loans, the market reaction was negative, suggesting investor concerns about other aspects of the business. The company's statement that the movement is market sentiment driven indicates no fundamental undisclosed issues.

**Investor Impact**: Provides transparency to investors that there are no hidden material events or regulatory issues affecting the company. The clarification should help reduce speculation about undisclosed information.

**Regulatory Impact**: Demonstrates company's compliance with mandatory disclosure requirements under SEBI LODR Regulations when stock exchanges seek clarification on unusual price movements or news articles.
