## Summary

The Indian Clearing Corporation Limited (ICCL), under BSE Notice No. 20260416-5 dated 16 April 2026, has published the settlement schedule for the Offer to Buy through the Acquisition Window under Takeover regulations for Jolly Plastic Industries Limited. This notice is directed at clearing members and market participants involved in the open offer/takeover process for this company.

## Key Points

- Notice issued by ICCL under the Settlement/RMS category for the General segment
- Pertains to the Offer to Buy via Acquisition Window mechanism under SEBI Takeover Regulations
- Covers settlement obligations for transactions in Jolly Plastic Industries Limited during the acquisition window
- Clearing members are required to adhere to the specified settlement schedule for this open offer

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This notice operates within the existing SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations framework and ICCL's standard settlement procedures for acquisition window transactions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Clearing members and trading members participating in the Offer to Buy for Jolly Plastic Industries Limited must follow the settlement schedule as prescribed by ICCL
- Members must ensure timely delivery of securities and funds as per the settlement obligations outlined for the acquisition window period
- Participants should refer to the notice for specific pay-in and pay-out timelines applicable to this offer

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 16 April 2026
- Settlement dates: As per the schedule detailed in the notice (refer to source PDF for specific settlement day timelines)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has a limited but targeted market impact, primarily affecting clearing and trading members who are facilitating transactions in Jolly Plastic Industries Limited under the ongoing takeover open offer. The acquisition window mechanism ensures an orderly settlement of shares tendered during the takeover process. Investors and members involved in this specific offer must comply with the prescribed settlement schedule to avoid any settlement failures or penalties.
