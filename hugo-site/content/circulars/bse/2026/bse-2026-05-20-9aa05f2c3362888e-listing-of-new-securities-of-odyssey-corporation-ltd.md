## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 6,600,000 new equity shares of Odyssey Corporation Ltd. (Scrip Code: 531996) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Thursday, May 21, 2026. The shares were issued at Rs. 14/- per share (face value Rs. 5/- + premium Rs. 9/-) to promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 6,600,000 equity shares of Rs. 5/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 9/- (issue price Rs. 14/-) to promoters via preferential allotment
- Allotment was made on conversion of warrants
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution Numbers: 75048487 to 81648486
- ISIN: INE839E01023
- Entire allotment of 6,600,000 shares is subject to lock-in until November 30, 2027

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification under BSE's listing operations framework for newly issued securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to update their systems to reflect the new securities available for trading from May 21, 2026
- The lock-in restriction on all 6,600,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 75048487 to 81648486) must be observed until November 30, 2027; these shares cannot be traded or transferred until the lock-in period expires

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** February 3, 2026
- **Trading Effective From:** May 21, 2026 (Thursday)
- **Lock-in Expiry:** November 30, 2027

## Impact Assessment

This listing adds 6,600,000 new equity shares to the tradable float of Odyssey Corporation Ltd., though the entire allotment is locked in until November 2027, meaning no immediate increase in freely tradable shares. Market impact is minimal in the short term due to the lock-in. Existing shareholders should note the dilution from the preferential allotment to promoters at Rs. 14/- per share.
