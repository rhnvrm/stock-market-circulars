## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 2,500,000 new equity shares of Comfort Fincap Limited (Scrip Code: 535267, ISIN: INE274M01026) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Monday, June 8, 2026. The shares were issued on a preferential basis to Promoter and Non-Promoter shareholders pursuant to the conversion of warrants at an issue price of Rs. 9/- per share (face value Rs. 2/- at a premium of Rs. 7/-).

## Key Points

- 2,500,000 equity shares of Rs. 2/- face value issued at Rs. 9/- per share (Rs. 7/- premium)
- Issued to Promoter and Non-Promoter on preferential basis via warrant conversion
- Distribution numbers: 87,506,501 to 90,006,500
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Date of allotment: March 21, 2026
- Trading commences: June 8, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification under BSE's Listing Operations for newly allotted equity shares.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to update their systems to permit trading in the newly listed shares from June 8, 2026. The shares are subject to lock-in restrictions as detailed below and cannot be traded until respective lock-in periods expire.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** March 21, 2026
- **Trading Commencement:** June 8, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry – 1,500,000 shares (87,506,501–89,006,500):** December 15, 2027
- **Lock-in Expiry – 250,000 shares (89,006,501–89,256,500):** December 15, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry – 250,000 shares (89,256,501–89,506,500):** December 15, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry – 500,000 shares (89,506,501–90,006,500):** December 31, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 2,500,000 new equity shares to the tradeable float of Comfort Fincap Limited, though a significant portion (1,500,000 shares, 60%) remains locked in until December 2027. Only 1,000,000 shares (40%) will be free to trade from June 8, 2026, limiting immediate dilution impact. The preferential allotment via warrant conversion indicates a pre-planned capital raise. Market impact is expected to be limited given the company's small-cap status.
