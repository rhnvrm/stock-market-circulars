## Summary

Annapurna Finance Private Limited has announced part redemption of its Non-Convertible Debentures (ISIN: INE515Q07640, Code: 976927) with a record date of January 7, 2026. Trading members are informed that trading in these debentures will be conducted with a reduced face value effective from the same date.

## Key Points

- Security: AFPL-10.95%-22-07-27-PVT Non-Convertible Debentures
- ISIN: INE515Q07640
- Scrip Code: 976927
- Record Date: January 7, 2026
- Purpose: Part redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Reduced Face Value: Rs. 74,999.98 per debenture
- Effective Date: January 7, 2026
- Settlement Number: DR-793/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notification for debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the reduced face value for trading purposes
- All trades in these debentures from January 7, 2026 onwards will reflect the reduced face value of Rs. 74,999.98 per debenture
- Debenture holders on record as of January 7, 2026 will be eligible for interest payment and part redemption

## Important Dates

- **January 5, 2026**: Notice date
- **January 7, 2026**: Record date for interest payment and part redemption
- **January 7, 2026**: Effective date for trading with reduced face value (Settlement DR-793/2025-2026)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains specifically to holders of Annapurna Finance Private Limited's debentures. The part redemption is a routine corporate action in the debt securities segment. Debenture holders will receive partial redemption proceeds along with interest payment, while the remaining investment will continue with a reduced face value. Trading activity in this specific security may be affected temporarily as market participants adjust to the new face value.
