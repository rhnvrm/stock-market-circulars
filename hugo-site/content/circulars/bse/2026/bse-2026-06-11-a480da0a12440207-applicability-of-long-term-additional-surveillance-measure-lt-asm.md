## Summary

BSE has issued an update on the Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LT-ASM) framework, imposing 100% margins on securities listed in Annexure I (Part A) effective June 16, 2026. Additional changes include securities moving to higher LT-ASM stages, lower price bands effective June 12, 2026, group transfers to T/XT/MT/TS segments, and securities exiting the framework — all effective from June 12 or June 16, 2026.

## Key Points

- 100% margins will apply from June 16, 2026 on all open positions as of June 15, 2026 and on all new positions created from June 16, 2026 onwards (Annexure I, Part A).
- Securities are moving to higher stages within the LT-ASM framework effective June 12, 2026 (Annexure I, Part B).
- Lower Price Band is applicable from June 12, 2026 for securities in the LT-ASM framework.
- Certain securities will continue under LT-ASM but be transferred to T/XT/MT/TS Group effective June 16, 2026 (Annexure I, Part D).
- Securities moving out of the LT-ASM framework are listed in Annexure II, effective June 12, 2026.
- A consolidated list of all securities under the framework is provided in Annexure III.
- Shortlisting under LT-ASM is based on XBRL submissions and is purely for market surveillance — it should not be construed as adverse action against the concerned company.

## Regulatory Changes

This notice is issued under the revised LT-ASM framework established via Exchange notices: 20180321-46 (March 21, 2018), 20181027-1 (October 27, 2018), 20201204-56 (December 4, 2020), 20240809-46 (August 9, 2024), and 20240920-63 (September 20, 2024). The current circular updates the list of securities subject to LT-ASM and modifies margin, price band, and group classification rules for specific scrips.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must ensure 100% margin compliance for all open positions in affected securities by the close of June 15, 2026.
- New positions in affected securities from June 16, 2026 must be covered with 100% margins.
- Members should note revised group classifications (T/XT/MT/TS) for securities transferred under Annexure I (Part D) and adjust trading and settlement workflows accordingly.
- Members must observe the lower price band restrictions for applicable securities from June 12, 2026.
- For clarifications, members may contact BSE Surveillance at bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **June 12, 2026**: Securities moving to higher LT-ASM stages take effect; lower price band becomes applicable; securities exiting LT-ASM framework (Annexure II) take effect.
- **June 15, 2026**: Last date for existing open positions before 100% margin requirement kicks in.
- **June 16, 2026**: 100% margins applicable on all open positions as of June 15, 2026 and on new positions; group transfers to T/XT/MT/TS effective.

## Impact Assessment

The imposition of 100% margins significantly increases the cost of holding or creating positions in affected securities, likely reducing liquidity and trading volumes in those scrips. Securities moved to higher LT-ASM stages face more restrictive trading conditions. The lower price band limits downside trading flexibility. Transfers to T/XT/MT/TS groups alter settlement obligations for brokers and clients. Securities exiting the LT-ASM framework will have their previous price bands reinstated, unless subject to other surveillance measures. The actual breadth of impact depends on the number of securities in the attached Annexures, which are not detailed in the notice text itself.
