## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260227-29 informs trading members that 60,00,000 new equity shares of Titan Intech Limited (Scrip Code: 521005) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Monday, March 2, 2026. The shares were issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 60,00,000 equity shares of Re. 1/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 4.50 (issue price Rs. 5.50 per share)
- Issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis via conversion of warrants
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 828576883 to 834576882
- ISIN: INE807M01031
- Date of allotment: January 24, 2026
- Trading permitted from March 2, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification following a preferential allotment under existing SEBI regulations governing warrant conversion.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their systems to reflect the new securities available for trading from March 2, 2026
- All 60,00,000 shares are subject to lock-in until October 20, 2026 and must not be traded until that date

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** January 24, 2026
- **Trading Commencement:** March 2, 2026 (Monday)
- **Lock-in Expiry:** October 20, 2026 (for all 60,00,000 shares, Dist. Nos. 828576883 to 834576882)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. The addition of 60,00,000 shares represents a modest increase in the float for Titan Intech Limited (Scrip Code: 521005). All newly listed shares are under lock-in until October 20, 2026, meaning no immediate increase in tradeable supply. Market participants should note the lock-in expiry date as a potential future liquidity event.
