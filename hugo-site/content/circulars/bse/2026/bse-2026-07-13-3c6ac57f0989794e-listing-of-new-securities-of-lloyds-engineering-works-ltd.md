## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that they are permitted to trade in 14,25,192 new equity shares of Lloyds Engineering Works Ltd (Scrip Code: 539992) with effect from Tuesday, July 14, 2026. These shares arose from the conversion of partly paid-up rights shares to fully paid-up shares of Re. 1/- face value each, and rank pari-passu with the company's existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 14,25,192 equity shares of Lloyds Engineering Works Ltd become eligible for trading.
- Shares were issued on a rights basis and have now been converted from partly paid to fully paid-up status.
- Face value of each share is Re. 1/-.
- New shares carry ISIN INE093R01011, same as existing equity shares.
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company.
- Distinctive number ranges specified for the newly listed shares.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing permission notice issued under BSE's Listing Operations department.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must update systems to recognize the newly listed shares as tradeable from the effective date; no additional compliance action required from investors.

## Important Dates

- Effective date for trading: Tuesday, July 14, 2026
- Notice date: July 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing increases the tradeable float of Lloyds Engineering Works Ltd by converting previously partly paid rights shares into fully paid, freely tradeable equity. Impact is limited to shareholders and traders of this specific stock, with no broader market implications.
