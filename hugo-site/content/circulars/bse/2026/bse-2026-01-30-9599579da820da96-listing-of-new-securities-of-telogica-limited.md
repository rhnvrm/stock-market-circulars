## Summary

Telogica Limited (formerly Aishwarya Technologies and Telecom Limited) submitted distinctive numbers and lock-in details to BSE for new securities allotted between October 29, 2025 and November 4, 2025. The submission covers multiple tranches totaling 1,44,99,000 shares with lock-in periods extending until August 2026 and August 2027.

## Key Points

- Total of 1,44,99,000 new securities allotted across four different dates
- Allotment dates: October 29, 31, November 1, and November 4, 2025
- Lock-in periods: Primarily August 10, 2026, with some securities locked until August 10, 2027
- Distinctive number range: 33773033 to 65323032
- Submission made by Managing Director Srinivasa Rao Mandava (DIN: 03456187)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a compliance filing for listing new securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities are subject to mandatory lock-in periods as specified
- Allottees cannot trade locked-in securities until respective lock-in dates expire
- Company has fulfilled BSE listing requirements by submitting distinctive numbers and lock-in details

## Important Dates

- **October 29, 2025**: First allotment of 1,02,00,000 shares
- **October 31, 2025**: Second allotment of 30,62,754 shares
- **November 1, 2025**: Third allotment of 26,40,290 shares
- **November 4, 2025**: Fourth allotment of 81,46,956 shares
- **August 10, 2026**: Primary lock-in expiry date for most securities
- **August 10, 2027**: Extended lock-in expiry date for select securities

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is an administrative filing. The lock-in restrictions prevent immediate trading of these newly allotted securities, ensuring market stability. Only affects specific allottees of Telogica Limited who cannot trade their allocated shares until lock-in periods expire. No impact on existing shareholders or broader market operations.
