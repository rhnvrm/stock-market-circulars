## Summary

BSE has announced that BSE Indices will be calculated on February 1, 2026 (Sunday), which has been declared as a special trading day on account of the Union Budget 2026 presentation. Markets will remain open for regular trading hours on this day.

## Key Points

- BSE Indices will be calculated on Sunday, February 1, 2026
- Special trading day declared due to Union Budget 2026 presentation
- Markets will operate during regular trading hours
- This notice references earlier Notice No: 20260116-33
- Queries should be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational announcement for a special trading session.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should note the special trading session on February 1, 2026
- Normal trading operations will apply during regular hours on this Sunday

## Important Dates

- **February 1, 2026 (Sunday)**: Special trading session with regular trading hours
- **January 16, 2026**: Notice date

## Impact Assessment

This announcement affects all market participants as the exchange will operate on a Sunday to accommodate the Union Budget 2026 presentation. BSE Indices including the SENSEX will be calculated during regular trading hours. Trading members and investors should adjust their schedules accordingly for this special session.
