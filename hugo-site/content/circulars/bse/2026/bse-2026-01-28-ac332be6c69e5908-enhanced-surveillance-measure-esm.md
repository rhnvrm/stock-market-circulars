## Summary

BSE has issued an update to the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework effective January 29, 2026. Three new securities have been added to ESM Stage I: Amit Securities Ltd, Borana Weaves Ltd, and Citi Port Financial Services Ltd. CHPL Industries Ltd will be moved to a higher ESM stage. The circular includes a consolidated list of 30+ securities currently under ESM with their respective stages.

## Key Points

- 3 securities newly shortlisted under ESM Stage I effective January 29, 2026
- 1 security (CHPL Industries Ltd) moving to higher ESM stage
- No securities moving to lower ESM stage
- No securities moving out of ESM framework
- Consolidated list provided showing securities in ESM Stage I and Stage II
- ESM framework imposes additional surveillance and trading restrictions

## Securities Added to ESM (New)

### Stage I Additions:
1. **Amit Securities Ltd** (Scrip Code: 531557, ISIN: INE137E01014)
2. **Borana Weaves Ltd** (Scrip Code: 544404, ISIN: INE16SF01016) - As per NSE
3. **Citi Port Financial Services Ltd** (Scrip Code: 531235, ISIN: INE494D01011)

## Securities Moving to Higher ESM Stage

- **CHPL Industries Ltd** (Scrip Code: 539335, ISIN: INE714Q01014) - Moving to higher stage

## Securities Moving Out of ESM

- None (Nil)

## Consolidated ESM List (Partial)

The circular provides a consolidated list including:
- **Stage I Securities**: 22+ securities including Aayush Wellness Ltd, Adcon Capital Services Ltd, Adinath Textiles Ltd, Aerpace Industries Ltd, and others
- **Stage II Securities**: Including Aadi Industries Ltd, ABM Knowledgeware Ltd, Alps Industries Ltd, Arcee Industries Ltd, Ashnisha Industries Ltd, and Bandaram Pharma Packtech Ltd

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: January 29, 2026 - All ESM changes take effect

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure compliance with ESM framework requirements for listed securities
- Higher ESM stages typically involve stricter surveillance including price bands, trade-for-trade settlement, and additional margin requirements
- Market participants should review positions in affected securities
- Securities under ESM subject to enhanced disclosure and monitoring requirements

## Impact Assessment

**Trading Impact**: Securities under ESM face additional trading restrictions that may reduce liquidity and increase volatility. Stage movements indicate escalating or de-escalating surveillance concerns.

**Market Participants**: Investors and traders in these securities should be aware of potential additional margins, reduced leverage, and possible trade-for-trade settlement requirements depending on ESM stage.

**Regulatory Significance**: ESM framework is a preventive surveillance measure to protect investor interests and maintain market integrity for securities showing unusual price movements or other concerns.
