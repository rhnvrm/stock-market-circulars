## Summary

BSE announces the listing and admission to trading of four schemes under Arudha Hybrid Long-Short Fund managed by Bandhan AMC Limited, effective February 3, 2026. The fund is an interval investment strategy investing in equity and debt securities with limited short exposure through derivatives. All schemes are listed in the 'B' Group with trading permitted only in dematerialized form in unit market lots.

## Key Points

- Four schemes listed: Regular Plan-Growth, Regular Plan-Monthly IDCW, Direct Plan-Growth, and Direct Plan-Half yearly IDCW
- Face value: Rs. 10.00 per unit for all schemes
- Issue price: Rs. 10.00 per unit for all schemes
- Date of allotment: January 28, 2026
- Trading commences: February 3, 2026
- Market lot: 1 unit for all schemes
- Total units across all schemes: 38,368,431 units
- Investment Manager: Bandhan AMC Limited
- Registrar and Transfer Agent: Computer Age Management Services Limited (CAMS)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. The circular follows existing SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000, requiring mutual fund units to be traded only in dematerialized form.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all trades in these mutual fund units are executed in dematerialized form only
- Trades must be effected in unit market lots (1 unit)
- Trading members should note the respective scrip codes and ISIN numbers for each scheme

## Important Dates

- **January 28, 2026**: Date of allotment for all schemes
- **February 3, 2026**: Trading commences on BSE

## Impact Assessment

This listing has minimal market-wide impact and is primarily relevant to:

**Investors**: Provides liquidity options for Bandhan Mutual Fund investors through exchange trading platform

**Trading Members**: Need to update systems with new scrip codes and trading parameters for four new mutual fund schemes

**Fund House**: Enhances distribution and accessibility of Arudha Hybrid Long-Short Fund schemes

The listing follows standard procedures and does not affect broader market operations or regulatory frameworks.
