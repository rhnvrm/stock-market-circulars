## Summary

UTI Mutual Fund is launching UTI NIFTY500 SHARIAH INDEX FUND as a New Fund Offer (NFO) with SIP facility on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The NFO will be open from February 05, 2026, to February 18, 2026, with a tentative allotment date of February 23, 2026. The scheme will be available in both Regular and Direct plans with Growth option.

## Key Points

- NFO period: February 05-18, 2026
- Two plan options available: Regular Plan (ISIN: INF789F1AC96) and Direct Plan (ISIN: INF789F1AD04)
- Both plans offer Growth option under Equity category
- Tentative allotment date: February 23, 2026
- Extended trading until midnight on February 18, 2026, for lumpsum purchases only
- Clear funds must be available in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on February 23, 2026
- Transactions without client-initiated funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 will be cancelled
- Cheques and OTM payments accepted subject to clear funds receipt by allotment date
- Switches accepted during NFO period only if funds received before allotment date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

### For Members:
- Ensure client fund initiation by 9:00 AM on T+1 day to avoid transaction cancellation
- Report cancelled transactions with CC and N flag to RTA/AMC
- Maintain pending transactions until 3:00 PM on last day of fund payout or T+5, whichever is earlier
- Ensure clear funds availability in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on February 23, 2026
- Complete SIP/XSIP registration authentication by clients before 3:00 PM on NFO closure date (February 18, 2026)

### Payment Methods:
- Cheques accepted during NFO period
- OTM payments accepted during NFO period
- Both subject to receipt of clear funds in ICCL account by 6:00 PM on February 23, 2026

## Important Dates

- **NFO Opening Date**: February 05, 2026
- **NFO Closing Date**: February 18, 2026 (3:00 PM for regular transactions)
- **Extended Trading Period**: Until midnight on February 18, 2026 (lumpsum only)
- **SIP/XSIP Authentication Deadline**: 3:00 PM on February 18, 2026
- **Tentative Allotment Date**: February 23, 2026
- **Clear Funds Deadline**: 6:00 PM on February 23, 2026

### Operational Impact:
