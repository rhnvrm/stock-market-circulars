## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities issued on a private placement basis by five companies on its debt market segment effective January 8, 2026. The securities include issuances from Tata Housing Development Company (₹310 crore), ICICI Securities Primary Dealership (₹350 crore), Tata Steel (₹600 crore and ₹650 crore), Muthoottu Mini Financiers (₹10 crore), and Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited. All securities will trade in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- Five companies have listed commercial paper worth approximately ₹1,920 crore on BSE debt segment
- Securities trade only in dematerialized form with standard denomination of ₹5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for all securities is 1 paise
- Interest rates range from 6.00% p.a. (Tata Steel) to 9.65% p.a. (Muthoottu Mini Financiers)
- Maturity periods range from 41 days to 90 days from allotment date
- All securities carry A1+ credit ratings from agencies like CARE, CRISIL, ICRA, and IND
- Trading members can contact BSE debt department at specified phone numbers for clarifications

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notice for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all trades are conducted in dematerialized form only
- Trading must be in standard denomination of ₹5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Market lot size is 1 unit for all listed securities
- Trading members should use the specific ISIN numbers and scrip codes provided for each security
- Interest payments are cumulative and payable on redemption date

## Important Dates

- **Effective Listing Date**: January 8, 2026
- **Allotment Dates**: January 7-8, 2026 (varies by issuer)
- **Redemption Dates**:
  - February 17, 2026: Tata Steel (First Issuance)
  - February 25, 2026: Tata Housing Development Company
  - March 16, 2026: ICICI Securities Primary Dealership
  - March 25, 2026: Tata Steel (Second Issuance)
  - April 7, 2026: Muthoottu Mini Financiers

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine listing of privately placed commercial paper affecting only the debt market segment. The securities are institutional in nature with high minimum denomination requirements (₹5 lakhs), limiting participation to institutional and high-net-worth investors.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Trading members dealing in debt securities should update their systems with the new scrip codes and ISIN numbers. The securities follow standard BSE debt segment trading protocols.

**Issuer Profile**: The list includes highly rated issuers (all A1+ rated) including blue-chip companies like Tata Steel, indicating low credit risk. Interest rates are competitive and reflect current market conditions for short-term commercial paper.
