## Summary

BSE has informed Trading Members that Regency Fincorp Ltd has undergone part-redemption of two of its debenture series, resulting in reduced face values. The debentures will trade with the revised face value effective July 03, 2026.

## Key Points

- Debenture series RFL-14%-23-6-27-PVT (ISIN: INE964R07069, Scrip Code: 977676) has a reduced face value of Rs. 8,000 per debenture following part-redemption.
- Debenture series RFL-15%-1-1-28-PVT (ISIN: INE964R07085, Scrip Code: 977843) has a reduced face value of Rs. 94,737 per debenture following part-redemption.
- Notice reference: DR-664/2026-2027.
- No attachment accompanies this notice.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is an operational listing update reflecting part-redemption already effected by the issuer.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members must update their systems to reflect the reduced face values for the two ISINs and ensure trades are executed and settled at the revised face value from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 02, 2026
- Effective Date for Reduced Face Value Trading: July 03, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt-segment administrative notice with limited market-wide impact. It is relevant primarily to holders and traders of the specific Regency Fincorp Ltd debenture series named, who must account for the reduced face value in valuation and settlement from July 03, 2026 onward.
