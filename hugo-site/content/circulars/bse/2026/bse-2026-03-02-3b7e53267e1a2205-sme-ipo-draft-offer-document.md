## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document of Susan Electricals India Limited, filed on February 28, 2026, on the SME exchange website. The document contains all disclosures mandated under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and will remain available for public review for a minimum of 21 days from the notice date (March 2, 2026).

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Susan Electricals India Limited on February 28, 2026
- Document hosted on the BSE SME website for at least 21 days for public review
- Public comments invited and must be submitted within 21 days of the notice date
- Document complies with Schedule VI of the ICDR (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations
- Direct PDF link: https://www.bsesme.com/download/338840/SME_IPO%20InPrinciple/SUSAN_20260228205254.pdf

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This notice is a standard procedural disclosure under the ICDR Regulations requiring Draft Offer Documents for SME IPOs to be made publicly available for comment prior to the final offer.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members of the public and market participants may review the Draft Offer Document on the BSE SME website
- Comments, if any, must be submitted via email to **equity.ipo@bseindia.com** within 21 days from March 2, 2026
- No action required from brokers or trading members

## Important Dates

- **Document Filed:** February 28, 2026
- **Notice Date:** March 2, 2026
- **Public Comment Deadline:** March 23, 2026 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine SME IPO disclosure with no immediate market-wide impact. The notice is relevant to retail and institutional investors interested in participating in the upcoming IPO of Susan Electricals India Limited. The 21-day public comment window is a standard regulatory step before the final prospectus is approved, and the outcome may influence the final offer terms.
