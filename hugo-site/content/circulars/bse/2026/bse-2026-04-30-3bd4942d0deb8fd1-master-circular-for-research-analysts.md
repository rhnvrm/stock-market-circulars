## Summary

BSE has issued a Master Circular for Research Analysts (RAs) enlisted with the Exchange, consolidating all applicable notices and circulars issued up to March 31, 2026. The circular is intended to serve as a single-point reference for RAs to access all regulatory requirements applicable to them.

## Key Points

- Master Circular covers all notices and circulars issued by BSE to Research Analysts up to March 31, 2026
- A separate list of all circulars issued by the Exchange till March 31, 2026, is enclosed for ease of reference
- Multiple annexures are included: TRAI Directions, Standard Operating Procedure (SOP), CeFCoM guidelines, Deposit Requirements for RAs, Annexures 8A and 8B, and other annexures
- Contact for clarifications: ra.membership@bseindia.com
- Issued by Membership Compliance department

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This circular is a compilation of existing regulations and circulars applicable to Research Analysts, consolidating prior notices issued up to March 31, 2026.

## Compliance Requirements

- Enlisted Research Analysts must familiarise themselves with the Master Circular (MASTERCIRCULAR_RA_2026) as the authoritative reference for all applicable compliance obligations
- RAs should review all attached annexures including TRAI Directions, SOP, CeFCoM requirements, and deposit requirements
- Members seeking clarification should email ra.membership@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: April 30, 2026
- Coverage Period: All circulars issued up to March 31, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular primarily impacts Research Analysts (RAs) enlisted with BSE. It provides administrative convenience by consolidating regulatory requirements into a single document, reducing compliance complexity. No new obligations are introduced, but RAs should review the master circular to ensure they remain current with all applicable rules. Market operations are not directly affected.
