## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260304-11 informing trading members that the commercial papers of Adani Enterprises Ltd and Marwadi Shares and Finance Limited are maturing on their respective redemption dates. Trading in both instruments is suspended with effect from March 5, 2026.

## Key Points

- Two commercial papers are suspended from trading effective March 5, 2026
- Adani Enterprises Ltd (Scrip Code: 730359) — ISIN: INE423A14YI2, instrument: AEL-6-3-26-CP
- Marwadi Shares and Finance Limited (Scrip Code: 731023) — ISIN: INE138I14016, instrument: MSFL-6-3-26-CP
- Suspension is due to maturity/redemption on the designated redemption date
- Reference: DR-833/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice under existing BSE rules governing the suspension of debt instruments upon maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the commercial papers of Adani Enterprises Ltd (Scrip Code: 730359) and Marwadi Shares and Finance Limited (Scrip Code: 731023) from March 5, 2026 onwards.
- Members are advised to take note of the suspension and update their trading systems accordingly.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** March 4, 2026
- **Suspension Effective Date:** March 5, 2026
- **Redemption Date:** March 6, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice with limited market impact. The suspension affects only the two named commercial paper instruments. Holders of these instruments should note that redemption proceeds will be settled on the maturity date. No broader market disruption is expected. Trading members should ensure compliance to avoid any inadvertent transactions in these suspended instruments.
