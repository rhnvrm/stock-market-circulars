## Summary

BSE announces the listing of three new Treasury Bills (T-Bills) in the G GROUP - DEBT INSTRUMENTS segment, effective January 9, 2026. The T-Bills have varying maturity periods of 91 days, 182 days, and 364 days.

## Key Points

- Three T-Bills to be listed with scrip codes 805163, 805164, and 805165
- All securities have a market lot size of 1
- Securities will be unavailable for trading two working days prior to maturity date
- Trading members can contact BSE at 2272 8352/5753/8597 for clarifications

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the trading suspension rule: securities not available for trading two working days before maturity/redemption date (T-2)
- The two-day exclusion period does not include bank holidays

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date:** January 9, 2026
- **91-Day T-Bill (805163) Maturity:** April 9, 2026
- **182-Day T-Bill (805164) Maturity:** July 9, 2026
- **364-Day T-Bill (805165) Maturity:** January 7, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine administrative listing of government treasury bills. The listing provides additional short-term debt instruments for investors and enhances liquidity in the government securities market. No impact on equity markets or individual stocks.
