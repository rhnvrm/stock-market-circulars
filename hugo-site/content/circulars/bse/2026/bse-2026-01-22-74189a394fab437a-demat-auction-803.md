## Summary

BSE has published the demat auction list for Settlement No. 803 dated January 22, 2026. The auction includes 100 securities across various sectors where members have failed to deliver shares. Quantities range from 1 share to 9,715 shares depending on the security. This is a standard settlement mechanism to fulfill delivery obligations.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 803
- Date: January 22, 2026
- Total securities included: 100
- Largest quantity: RELIANCE POWER (9,715 shares)
- Other significant quantities: BHARAT ELECTRONICS (3,785 shares), JUBILANT INGREAVIA (3,750 shares), BHARAT COKING (2,650 shares)
- Includes major companies like Reliance Industries (576 shares), Lupin, Mahindra & Mahindra, UPL, Yes Bank, and Swiggy

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members with delivery obligations must participate in the demat auction
- Settlement must be completed as per BSE auction procedures
- Members are responsible for arranging securities or settling through auction mechanism

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: January 22, 2026
- Settlement Number: 803

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact on affected members and securities. The auction mechanism ensures settlement completion and market integrity. Members holding short positions in these securities will need to participate in the auction, potentially at premium prices. Largest impact on Reliance Power, Bharat Electronics, and Jubilant Ingreavia due to higher quantities. Routine market operation with limited broader market impact.
