## Summary

BSE has informed members that Mirae Asset Mutual Fund will launch the New Fund Offer (NFO) of the Mirae Asset Nifty200 Momentum 30 Plus 8-13 Yr G-Sec 75:25 Index Fund, along with SIP facility, on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The NFO will be open from July 10, 2026 to July 22, 2026, with a tentative allotment date of July 28, 2026.

## Key Points

- Scheme: Mirae Asset Nifty200 Momentum 30 Plus 8-13 Yr G-Sec 75:25 Index Fund (Equity category)
- Six plan variants offered: Regular Plan (Growth, IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment) and Direct Plan (Growth, IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment)
- ISINs range from INF769K01RD8 to INF769K01RI7 across the six variants
- NFO with SIP facility available on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Transactions without client fund initiation by 9:00 AM on T+1 day will be cancelled and reported to RTA/AMC with 'N' flag
- Pending transactions with initiated payment remain in the system until 3:00 PM on the last fund pay-out day or T+5, whichever is earlier
- Cheque and OTM payments accepted subject to clear funds by 6:00 PM on July 24, 2026
- Switch transactions accepted during NFO period, subject to receipt of funds prior to allotment date; switch-out funds received on/after allotment date not considered
- NFO remains open until midnight July 22, 2026 exclusively for lumpsum purchase transactions

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is an operational notice enabling a new fund offer on the BSE StAR MF distribution platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members/distributors must ensure client funds are initiated by the specified cut-off times to avoid transaction cancellation
- SIP/XSIP registrations must be authenticated by clients on or before 3:00 PM on July 22, 2026
- Clear funds must be available in the ICCL account by 6:00 PM on July 24, 2026 for allotment eligibility

## Important Dates

- NFO Period: July 10, 2026 to July 22, 2026
- SIP/XSIP authentication deadline: 3:00 PM, July 22, 2026
- Clear funds deadline in ICCL account: 6:00 PM, July 24, 2026
- Tentative Allotment Date: July 28, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice for mutual fund distributors and BSE StAR MF Platform members facilitating a new fund offer. It has no direct impact on listed equity securities or broader market trading and is relevant mainly to mutual fund transaction processing and compliance timelines for member intermediaries.
