## Summary

ICCL Ltd. has issued Notice No. 20260616-15 dated 16 June 2026 informing trading members of corporate actions applicable to securities in the Securities Lending and Borrowing (SLB) segment. The full list of affected securities and corresponding corporate action details are provided in the attached annexure.

## Key Points

- Notice issued by ICCL Ltd. (Indian Clearing Corporation Limited) on 16 June 2026.
- Pertains to corporate actions (such as dividends, bonuses, splits, or rights issues) impacting securities currently active in the SLB segment.
- Trading members are directed to refer to the annexure for the complete list of affected SLB securities and action details.
- Issued under the signature of Tushar Nimkar, Assistant Manager, ICCL.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This notice operationalises existing provisions for handling corporate actions within the SLB framework by informing participants of current events requiring position adjustments or settlements.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members participating in the SLB segment must review the attached annexure to identify securities in their SLB portfolios that are subject to corporate actions.
- Members should take necessary steps to manage open SLB positions in affected securities in accordance with the corporate action terms.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 16 June 2026
- Specific ex-dates or record dates for individual corporate actions will be listed in the annexure.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational communication for SLB segment participants. The actual market and operational impact depends on the specific securities and types of corporate actions listed in the annexure. Members with open lending or borrowing positions in the affected securities may need to adjust their positions or await settlement adjustments as prescribed by ICCL.
