## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260511-47 dated 11 May 2026 informs trading members that Suraj Industries Ltd. (Scrip Code: 526211) has forfeited 1,37,972 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each. These shares were partly paid-up and were forfeited on account of non-payment of allotment/call monies by the respective shareholders.

## Key Points

- Suraj Industries Ltd. (BSE Scrip Code: 526211) has forfeited 1,37,972 equity shares of face value Rs. 10/- each.
- The forfeited shares were partly paid-up and not fully subscribed/paid.
- Forfeiture is due to non-payment of allotment/call monies by the concerned shareholders.
- Distinctive numbers of the forfeited shares are listed in Annexure-I (attachment: Shares_DNR_SIL_Forfeiture).
- Notice issued by the Listing Operations Department of BSE.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notification under existing BSE listing and operations guidelines for forfeiture of partly paid-up equity shares.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed and should update their records accordingly.
- Members holding or dealing in partly paid-up shares of Suraj Industries Ltd. should verify the distinctive numbers against Annexure-I to identify affected shares.
- No further action required from trading members unless they are holding or managing positions in the affected shares.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 11 May 2026
- **Notice Number:** 20260511-47

## Impact Assessment

The forfeiture affects 1,37,972 partly paid-up equity shares of Suraj Industries Ltd. and is limited in market impact. Shareholders who held these partly paid shares and failed to pay allotment/call monies lose their entitlement and investment in these shares. The total paid-up equity capital of the company is reduced by the forfeited shares. Broader market impact is minimal as this is a routine corporate action confined to non-paying shareholders of a specific scrip.
