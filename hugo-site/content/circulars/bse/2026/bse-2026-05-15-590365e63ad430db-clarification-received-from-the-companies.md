## Summary

BSE's Surveillance department (DOSS) has observed significant price and/or volume movement in eight listed companies. The Exchange wrote to all eight companies seeking clarifications. As of Notice No. 20260515-20 dated 15 May 2026, four companies have submitted their replies (attached to the notice), while four others have not yet responded.

## Key Points

- BSE observed unusual price/volume activity in eight equity scrips and initiated clarification requests under its surveillance function.
- Replies have been received from GK Consultants Ltd (531758), Semac Construction Ltd (505368), Alkyl Amines Chemicals Ltd (506767), and Indo Amines Ltd (524648).
- Replies are still awaited from Anuroop Packaging Ltd (542865), Ken Financial Services Ltd (530547), Ritco Logistics Ltd (542383), and ZR2 Bioenergy Ltd (506640).
- The notice is issued jointly by the Deputy Vice President and Manager of BSE Surveillance.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This notice is part of BSE's ongoing market surveillance mechanism under which the Exchange monitors abnormal price/volume movements and seeks company-level disclosures to ensure informed trading by members and investors.

## Compliance Requirements

- The four companies that have not yet responded — Anuroop Packaging Ltd, Ken Financial Services Ltd, Ritco Logistics Ltd, and ZR2 Bioenergy Ltd — are required to submit their clarifications to BSE promptly.
- All listed companies must respond to BSE surveillance queries in a timely manner to ensure market transparency and investor protection.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 15 May 2026
- **Replies received by:** 15 May 2026 (for GK Consultants, Semac Construction, Alkyl Amines Chemicals, Indo Amines)
- **Replies pending as of:** 15 May 2026 (for Anuroop Packaging, Ken Financial Services, Ritco Logistics, ZR2 Bioenergy)

## Impact Assessment

Investors and trading members holding or tracking the eight scrips should be aware of BSE's heightened surveillance attention. The receipt of clarifications from four companies provides some disclosure comfort for those scrips, though the actual content of replies is in the attached documents. The four companies with pending replies remain under scrutiny, and further surveillance actions (such as ASM/GSM placement or trading restrictions) could follow if satisfactory explanations are not provided. Medium market impact given the routine nature of such surveillance notices, but individual stock impact may vary depending on the substance of the clarifications.
