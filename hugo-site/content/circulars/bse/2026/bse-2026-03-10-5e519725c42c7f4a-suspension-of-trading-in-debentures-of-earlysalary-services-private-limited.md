## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (DR-838/2025-2026) directing trading members to suspend dealings in the Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of EarlySalary Services Private Limited. The suspension is effective from 12 March 2026, coinciding with the record date set for redemption of debentures and payment of interest.

## Key Points

- Company: EarlySalary Services Private Limited
- Debenture Series: ESPL-10.9%-27-3-26-PVT
- ISIN: INE01YL07292 (BSE Code: 976055)
- Record Date: 12 March 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- No dealings permitted from: 12 March 2026
- Notice Reference: DR-838/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational notice under existing BSE framework for corporate actions on debt instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified EarlySalary Services NCDs (ISIN INE01YL07292, Code 976055) on or after 12 March 2026.
- Members are required to take note of the suspension and ensure compliance to avoid regulatory action.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 10 March 2026
- **Record Date:** 12 March 2026
- **No Dealings From:** 12 March 2026
- **Debenture Maturity (implied):** 27 March 2026 (per series name ESPL-10.9%-27-3-26-PVT)

## Impact Assessment

The suspension affects only holders and prospective traders of EarlySalary Services Private Limited's 10.9% NCDs maturing on 27 March 2026. Existing holders will receive redemption proceeds and final interest payment on the record date. The impact is limited to this specific debenture series and does not affect broader equity or debt market operations.
