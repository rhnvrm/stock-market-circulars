## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 9,34,887 new equity shares of Kerala Ayurveda Limited (Scrip Code: 530163) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Tuesday, April 21, 2026. The shares were issued at a premium on a preferential basis to Promoter and Non-Promoter categories and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 9,34,887 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at an issue price of Rs. 327.99/- (premium of Rs. 317.99/-)
- Issued on a preferential basis to Promoter and Non-Promoter shareholders
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE817B01025
- Distinctive numbers: 12060199 to 12995085
- Date of allotment: March 21, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification following a completed preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the listing and enable trading of these new securities from April 21, 2026. Lock-in restrictions must be observed as detailed below.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** March 21, 2026
- **Trading Commencement:** April 21, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (6,30,000 shares | Dist. Nos. 12060199–12690198):** October 30, 2027
- **Lock-in Expiry (3,04,887 shares | Dist. Nos. 12690199–12995085):** October 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. The listing adds approximately 9.35 lakh shares to the tradeable float of Kerala Ayurveda Limited, though a significant portion (6,30,000 shares) remains locked in until October 2027. The preferential allotment at Rs. 327.99/- per share signals capital-raising activity by the company. Overall impact is routine and confined to existing shareholders and traders of this scrip.
