## Summary

BSE has announced adjustments to Futures and Options contracts on Union Bank of India (Derivatives Asset Code: UBIL, Equity Scrip Code: 532477) following the company's declaration of a dividend of Rs. 5/- per equity share for FY 2026-27. The adjustments will be implemented on July 02, 2026, with the ex-date set as July 03, 2026 (Record Date).

## Key Points

- Union Bank of India has declared a dividend of Rs. 5/- per equity share for FY 2026-27
- Record Date fixed at July 03, 2026 (Friday)
- Ex-date is July 03, 2026; adjustments applied at end of July 02, 2026 (Thursday)
- All available Futures & Options contracts on UBIL will be adjusted
- Adjustment is in line with SEBI Master Circular No. SEBI/HO/MRD/DP/CIR/P/2016/135 dated December 16, 2016

## Regulatory Changes

The adjustment is made pursuant to Exchange Notice No. 20180710-26 (Review of Adjustment of corporate actions for stock options) and SEBI Master Circular No. SEBI/HO/MRD/DP/CIR/P/2016/135 dated December 16, 2016. No new regulatory framework is introduced; this is a standard corporate action adjustment procedure.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members of the Equity Derivatives Segment are informed to account for the adjusted strike prices and futures prices from July 03, 2026
- Members should contact their respective relationship managers for further details
- No separate action required from members beyond awareness of the adjusted contract parameters

## Important Dates

- **July 02, 2026 (Thursday):** Adjustment date — BSE applies adjustments to all F&O contracts at end of trading day
- **July 03, 2026 (Friday):** Ex-date and Record Date — adjusted strike prices and futures prices applicable for trading

## Impact Assessment

**Options Contracts:** Rs. 5/- will be deducted from all strike prices generated at the end of July 02, 2026. Adjusted strike prices become effective for trading from July 03, 2026.

**Futures Contracts:** The adjusted futures price will be the daily mark-to-market settlement price (reference rate) of each relevant futures contract on July 02, 2026, less Rs. 5/-. This adjusted rate serves as the base rate/previous close for trading on July 03, 2026.

The adjustment ensures that the dividend outflow is neutrally reflected in derivative contract pricing, preventing artificial arbitrage opportunities around the ex-date. Market participants holding open positions in UBIL F&O contracts should note the adjusted parameters when assessing their positions.
