## Summary

BSE has issued a notice updating the terms of two debt securities issued by Setco Auto Systems Private Limited. This is a continuation of Notice No. 20260513-3 dated May 13, 2026. The revised terms take effect from Wednesday, June 10, 2026, and include a partial waiver of the redemption premium for the period April 1, 2026 to May 31, 2026.

## Key Points

- Two scrip codes are affected: 975859 (ISIN: INE0I0R07151) and 973453 (ISIN: INE0I0R07169)
- Fixed interest rate remains unchanged at 5% per annum
- The Investor IRR (Redemption Premium) remains 18% per annum, but redemption premium for April 1, 2026 to May 31, 2026 is waived by the Debenture Holder
- Revised terms are effective from June 10, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

The redemption premium definition has been updated to explicitly carve out a waiver period. While the headline IRR remains 18% per annum, the debenture holder has agreed to waive the redemption premium accruing between April 1, 2026 and May 31, 2026. This amendment modifies the economic terms of the securities for trading members and debenture holders.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to note the revised terms for both scrip codes and update their records accordingly. Any trades executed on or after June 10, 2026 must reflect the revised terms. Members requiring further clarification may contact Ms. Pooja Trivedi at +91 22 2272 8844 or debt.listing@bseindia.com, or Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President.

## Important Dates

- **June 10, 2026**: Effective date for revised terms on both scrip codes
- **April 1, 2026 – May 31, 2026**: Period for which redemption premium is waived by the debenture holder
- **May 13, 2026**: Date of original notice (Notice No. 20260513-3) that this circular continues

## Impact Assessment

This update is limited in scope, affecting only two listed debt securities of Setco Auto Systems Private Limited. The waiver of redemption premium for two months reduces the effective yield for debenture holders for that period, which may have a minor pricing impact on secondary market trades. Trading members holding or trading these scrips should update their systems to reflect the amended redemption premium schedule prior to June 10, 2026.
