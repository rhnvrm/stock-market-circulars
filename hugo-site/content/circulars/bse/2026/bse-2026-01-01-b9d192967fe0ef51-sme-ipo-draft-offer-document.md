## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of AEGEUS TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED filed on December 31, 2025, on the BSE SME website for public review and comments. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and will remain accessible for at least 21 days for stakeholder feedback.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by AEGEUS TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED on December 31, 2025
- Document hosted on BSE SME website: www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Contains all disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available for public comments for minimum 21 days
- Comments to be submitted at equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard disclosure process under existing ICDR Regulations for SME IPOs.

## Compliance Requirements

- Interested parties may review the Draft Offer Document on BSE SME website
- Comments, if any, should be submitted within 21 days from January 01, 2026
- Comments to be sent to: equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Document Filing Date**: December 31, 2025
- **Notice Date**: January 01, 2026
- **Public Comment Period**: 21 days from January 01, 2026
- **Comment Deadline**: Approximately January 22, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal - affects only prospective investors interested in the upcoming SME IPO of AEGEUS TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED.

**Operational Impact**: Standard IPO process for SME segment. Public comment period allows stakeholders to provide feedback on the draft document before finalization.

**Investor Impact**: Provides transparency and opportunity for public input on the proposed IPO terms and disclosures for AEGEUS TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED.
