## Summary

BSE Clearing Ltd (BSECL) has issued a notice (No. 20260623-37) announcing the availability of its Trade API Plus service — an enhanced real-time trade data offering. The circular is categorised under Settlement/RMS and addressed to the general segment.

## Key Points

- BSECL has made available a new enhanced service called **Trade API Plus** for real-time trade data.
- The notice falls under the Settlement/RMS category and is issued by the BSECL department.
- No attachments were included with this circular.
- The service appears to be an upgrade or enhancement over an existing trade data API offering.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are mandated by this circular. This is an informational announcement regarding the availability of a new data service.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance obligations are imposed on market participants. Entities interested in subscribing to the Trade API Plus service should contact BSECL directly for onboarding details.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 23 June 2026
- No specific effective date or deadline is mentioned in the circular.

## Impact Assessment

This circular has low market impact as it is purely informational in nature. Brokers, trading members, and institutional participants who require real-time trade data feeds may benefit from this enhanced API service. It does not affect trading operations, margins, or settlement obligations for existing participants.
