## Summary

BSE has notified that 32,00,000 new equity shares of Eraaya Lifespaces Limited (Scrip Code: 531035) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Monday, March 9, 2026. These shares were issued at a premium of Rs. 80/- (issue price Rs. 81/-) to Promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 32,00,000 equity shares of Re. 1/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 80/- (issue price Rs. 81/-)
- Issued to Promoters on a preferential basis via conversion of warrants
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 203494161 to 206694160
- ISIN: INE432F01032
- Date of allotment: January 5, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for newly allotted securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to permit trading of these new securities from March 9, 2026
- All 32,00,000 shares are subject to lock-in until September 20, 2027, as they were issued to Promoters on a preferential basis

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** January 5, 2026
- **Trading Commencement:** March 9, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry:** September 20, 2027

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 32,00,000 shares to the tradable float of Eraaya Lifespaces Limited (Scrip Code: 531035), though all newly listed shares are under promoter lock-in until September 20, 2027 and will not be available for public trading during that period. Market impact is minimal given the lock-in restriction and the routine nature of this preferential allotment.
