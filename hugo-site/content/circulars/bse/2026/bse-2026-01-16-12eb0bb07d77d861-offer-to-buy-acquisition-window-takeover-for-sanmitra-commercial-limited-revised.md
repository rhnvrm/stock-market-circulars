## Summary

BSE has issued a revised schedule for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for SANMITRA COMMERCIAL LIMITED (Symbol: ZSANMCOM, Scrip Code: 512062). The offer is for up to 1,49,76,000 equity shares at Rs. 15.00 per share, scheduled from Tuesday, January 6, 2026 to Tuesday, January 20, 2026. Trading members and custodian members can participate through the iBBS platform and RTRMS systems.

## Key Points

- Offer size: Up to 1,49,76,000 equity shares of SANMITRA COMMERCIAL LIMITED
- Offer price: Rs. 15.00 per share (Face Value: Rs. 10.00)
- Security Symbol: ZSANMCOM (ISIN: INE896J01014, Scrip Code: 512062)
- Market lot: 1 share
- Opening date: January 6, 2026
- Closing date: January 20, 2026
- Bid offering period: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM (January 6-20, 2026)
- Give-up/Take-up period: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM (January 6-19, 2026)
- Final day give-up: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM (January 20, 2026)
- Final day take-up: 9:15 AM to 4:00 PM (January 20, 2026)
- Categories eligible: FII, Financial Institutions, Mutual Funds, Insurance Companies, Other QIBs, Other NIBs, Companies, and Individuals

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a revised schedule notification for an ongoing takeover offer.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure UCC of investors is registered/updated with correct PAN number and category in the Exchange UCC database
- Trading members can only bid for investors having shares in demat form
- Members and custodians must refer to Exchange Notices No. 20150930-1 (September 30, 2015) and 20170203-29 (February 3, 2017) for operational instructions
- If no securities are accepted, no End of Issue files will be generated in Extranet
- Access OTB module via iBBS platform (Internet: https://ibbs.bseindia.com/ or Leased Line: https://ibbsll.bseindia.com/)
- Access RTRMS (Internet: https://rtrms.bseindia.com/ or Leased Line: https://rtrmsll.bseindia.com/)
- Extranet access for files (Internet: https://member.bseindia.com/ or Leased Line: https://memberll.bseindia.com/)

## Important Dates

- **January 6, 2026**: Opening date for bid offering
- **January 6-19, 2026**: Give-up/Take-up period (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **January 6-20, 2026**: Bid offering period (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **January 20, 2026**: Closing date
  - Give-up (last day): 9:15 AM - 3:30 PM
  - Take-up (last day): 9:15 AM - 4:00 PM
