## Summary

BSE has revised the acquisition window (takeover) schedule for Esha Media Research Limited. The acquisition window will be extended from Tuesday, January 27, 2026, to Wednesday, January 28, 2026. This revision is made to exclude Monday, January 26, 2026 (SEBI Holiday) and Thursday, January 15, 2026 (Exchange Holiday due to Municipal Corporation Elections in Maharashtra).

## Key Points

- Acquisition window extended by one trading day for Esha Media Research Limited
- Revision issued in continuation to notice no. 20260109-23 dated January 09, 2026
- Extension necessitated by two holidays: SEBI Holiday and Exchange Holiday
- All market participants requested to note the revised schedule

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative adjustment to the previously announced acquisition window schedule.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants involved in the takeover of Esha Media Research Limited must note the revised acquisition window dates
- Ensure all takeover-related activities are conducted within the extended timeline

## Important Dates

- **Original Acquisition Window End Date**: Tuesday, January 27, 2026
- **Revised Acquisition Window End Date**: Wednesday, January 28, 2026
- **Excluded Date (SEBI Holiday)**: Monday, January 26, 2026
- **Excluded Date (Exchange Holiday)**: Thursday, January 15, 2026 (Municipal Corporation Elections in Maharashtra)
- **Notice Date**: January 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a procedural adjustment affecting only participants in the specific takeover of Esha Media Research Limited. The one-day extension accommodates holiday disruptions and ensures adequate time for completing acquisition procedures. No broader market implications or trading disruptions expected.
