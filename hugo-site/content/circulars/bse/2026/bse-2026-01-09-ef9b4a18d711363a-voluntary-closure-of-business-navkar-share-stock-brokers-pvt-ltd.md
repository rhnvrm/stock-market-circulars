## Summary

BSE has announced that Navkar Share & Stock Brokers Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 111) will cease business operations in the Currency derivative segment effective January 12, 2026, due to voluntary closure of business.

## Key Points

- Navkar Share & Stock Brokers Pvt. Ltd. is voluntarily closing its Currency derivative segment operations
- Member Number: 111
- Effective date: Monday, January 12, 2026
- Notice issued by: Amit Kadam, Assistant Vice President, Membership Department

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a member-specific business closure notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of this closure for their awareness. Members with queries should contact BSE at membership.ops@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 9, 2026
- **Effective Date**: January 12, 2026 (Monday)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine business closure notification for a single member firm in the Currency derivative segment. The impact is limited to the specific member and their clients. No broader market implications are expected.
