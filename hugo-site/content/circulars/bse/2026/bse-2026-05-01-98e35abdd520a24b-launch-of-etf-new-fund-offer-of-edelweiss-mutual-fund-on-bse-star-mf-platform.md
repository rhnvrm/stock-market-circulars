## Summary

BSE has informed its members that Edelweiss Mutual Fund will launch the EDELWEISS NIFTY NEXT 50 ETF as a New Fund Offer (NFO) on the BSE StAR MF Platform from May 4, 2026, to May 14, 2026. The NFO covers two schemes under the equity category, with a tentative allotment date of May 21, 2026.

## Key Points

- NFO window: May 4, 2026 to May 14, 2026 on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Two schemes offered: EDELWEISS NIFTY NEXT 50 ETF and EDELWEISS NIFTY NEXT 50 ETF - Direct Plan - Growth, both under the Equity category
- Tentative allotment date: May 21, 2026
- The NFO schemes remain extended till midnight on May 14, 2026, for lumpsum purchases
- Transactions where clients do not initiate funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day will be cancelled
- CC with 'N' flag will be reported to the RTA/AMC for cancelled transactions

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard NFO launch notice under the existing BSE StAR MF Platform operational framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clients initiate funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day for transactions not to be cancelled
- Clear funds must be available in the ICCL account by 6:00 PM on May 19, 2026, for allotment eligibility
- Cheque and OTM payments accepted during the NFO period only if clear funds reach ICCL by 6:00 PM on May 19, 2026
- Switch transactions accepted only if funds under the NFO scheme are received prior to the allotment date; switch-out funds received on or after the allotment date will not be considered
- Pending transactions remain in the system until 3:00 PM on the last day of funds pay-out to the AMC or T+5 day, whichever is earlier

## Important Dates

- **May 4, 2026**: NFO opens
- **May 14, 2026**: NFO closes (extended till midnight for lumpsum purchases)
- **May 19, 2026, 6:00 PM**: Deadline for clear funds in ICCL account for allotment eligibility
- **May 21, 2026**: Tentative allotment date

## Impact Assessment

This notice is operationally relevant for BSE members and mutual fund distributors using the BSE StAR MF Platform. Investors seeking exposure to the NIFTY NEXT 50 index via an ETF structure from Edelweiss can participate during the NFO window. The strict fund initiation and settlement timelines require members to proactively communicate cut-off times to clients to avoid transaction cancellations.
