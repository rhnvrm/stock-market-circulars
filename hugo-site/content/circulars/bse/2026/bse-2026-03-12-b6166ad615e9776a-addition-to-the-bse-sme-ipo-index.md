## Summary

Elfin Agro India Limited (BSE ticker: 544724) was listed on BSE on March 12, 2026. Effective at the open of March 13, 2026, it will be added to the BSE SME IPO Index. This is a routine index inclusion following the company's listing as part of the SME IPO segment.

## Key Points

- Elfin Agro India Limited (ticker: 544724) listed on BSE on March 12, 2026
- The stock will be added to the BSE SME IPO Index effective the open of March 13, 2026
- Reference notice: No. 20260311-44
- The corporate action is included in the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE) for March 12, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard index rebalancing action following a new SME IPO listing.

## Compliance Requirements

Index fund managers and ETF providers tracking the BSE SME IPO Index must update their portfolios to include Elfin Agro India Limited (544724) effective at the open of March 13, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **March 12, 2026**: Elfin Agro India Limited listed on BSE
- **March 13, 2026 (market open)**: Effective date of addition to BSE SME IPO Index

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. The addition is confined to the BSE SME IPO Index, which tracks newly listed SME IPO stocks. Impact is primarily relevant to index-tracking funds and ETFs benchmarked to this index. Queries can be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com.
