## Summary

BSE has released the trading and settlement programme for the Institutional Trading Platform (ITP) under the SME segment for April 2026 (FY 2026-27). The schedule covers settlement numbers ITP-002/26-27 through ITP-021/26-27, spanning trading dates from 01-04-2026 to 30-04-2026. Settlement follows a T+1 pay-in/pay-out cycle, with pay-in due by 10:00 a.m. and pay-out effected by 11:00 a.m.

## Key Points

- Settlement period covers 01-04-2026 to 30-04-2026 under FY 2026-27 numbering.
- Depository settlement numbers run from 2627002 to 2627021.
- Standard T+1 settlement cycle applies: pay-in at 10:00 a.m., pay-out by 11:00 a.m.
- Members must submit pay-in instructions to depositories/banks by 09:50 a.m. (10 minutes before pay-in deadline).
- Special dual-settlement arrangement applies on 02-04-2026: ITP-001/26-27 (trade date 30-03-2026) settles with pay-in at 10:00 a.m. and pay-out by 12:30 p.m.; ITP-002/26-27 (trade date 01-04-2026) settles with pay-in at 4:30 p.m. and pay-out by 9:30 p.m.
- Clearing handled by Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine periodic calendar publication.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members on the ITP-SME segment must adhere to the specified pay-in timings for each settlement.
- Pay-in instructions to depositories and banks must be submitted by 09:50 a.m. for standard settlements, and by 04:20 p.m. for the evening settlement of ITP-002/26-27 on 02-04-2026.

## Impact Assessment

This circular has medium operational impact for ITP-SME segment participants who must plan their settlement obligations for April 2026. The special back-to-back settlement on 02-04-2026 (marking the start of FY 2026-27) requires participants to manage two separate settlements on the same date with different timing windows. No impact on retail investors or broader market operations.
