## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document (DRHP) of **Navkkar Gold & Diamond Limited** on its SME website. The document was filed on February 28, 2026, and contains all disclosures as required under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations. It will remain available for public comments for a minimum of 21 days from the date of this notice (March 2, 2026).

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Navkkar Gold & Diamond Limited on February 28, 2026
- Document complies with Schedule VI of the ICDR (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations
- Hosted on BSE SME website for public viewing and comments
- Public comment window is open for at least 21 days from March 2, 2026
- Comments to be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This notice is issued in compliance with existing SEBI ICDR Regulations, which mandate public disclosure of the Draft Offer Document for a minimum of 21 days to allow stakeholder feedback prior to a formal IPO filing.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Public / Investors / Market Participants**: May review the Draft Offer Document on the BSE SME website and submit comments or objections to equity.ipo@bseindia.com within 21 days of March 2, 2026 (deadline: on or before March 23, 2026).
- **Navkkar Gold & Diamond Limited**: Must address any public comments received during the comment period before proceeding with the final offer document submission.

## Important Dates

- **DRHP Filing Date**: February 28, 2026
- **Notice Date**: March 2, 2026
- **Public Comment Deadline**: March 23, 2026 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine procedural notice with limited market impact. It signals an upcoming SME IPO by Navkkar Gold & Diamond Limited on the BSE SME platform. The broader market is unaffected. Investors interested in the gold and diamond sector may wish to review the DRHP for early insight into the company's financials and business model ahead of the formal IPO.
