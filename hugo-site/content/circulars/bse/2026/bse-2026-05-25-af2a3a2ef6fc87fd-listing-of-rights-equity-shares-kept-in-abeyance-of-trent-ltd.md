## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 740 rights equity shares of Trent Ltd (Scrip Code: 500251), which were allotted on April 22, 2026 but kept in abeyance, are now listed and permitted to trade with effect from Tuesday, May 26, 2026.

## Key Points

- 740 Rights Equity Shares of Re. 1/- each allotted on April 22, 2026 are now listed
- These shares were previously kept in abeyance
- ISIN: INE849A01020
- Distinctive Numbers: 355487462 to 355488201
- Shares are pari passu with existing equity shares of Trent Ltd
- Trading permitted from May 26, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification following resolution of shares previously kept in abeyance under the rights issue process.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to update their systems to reflect the new securities available for trading under Scrip Code 500251 from May 26, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **April 22, 2026**: Allotment date of the rights equity shares
- **May 26, 2026**: Effective date for listing and commencement of trading

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact given the very small quantity of 740 shares being listed. These shares are pari passu with existing equity shares, meaning no dilution-related pricing adjustments are required. The listing resolves the abeyance status for a small portion of Trent Ltd's rights issue allotment.
