## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document (DRHP) of **Decent Spinners Limited**, filed on June 16, 2026, on its SME platform in compliance with Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations. The document is open for public comments for a minimum of 21 days.

## Key Points

- Decent Spinners Limited has filed its Draft Offer Document with BSE under the SME IPO framework.
- The document is hosted on the BSE SME website at [www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx](http://www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx).
- Disclosures comply with Schedule VI of the ICDR (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations.
- Public comments are invited within 21 days from the date of this notice (by July 7, 2026).
- Comments must be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural notice under the SEBI ICDR Regulations requiring public display of the draft offer document for at least 21 days before an SME IPO proceeds.

## Compliance Requirements

- **General public / investors:** May review the draft offer document and submit comments to BSE at equity.ipo@bseindia.com within 21 days.
- **Decent Spinners Limited:** Must ensure all disclosures as required under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations are included in the filed document.

## Important Dates

- **Filing date:** June 16, 2026
- **Public comment deadline:** July 7, 2026 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal immediate market impact. It is a routine step in the SME IPO process, notifying the market that Decent Spinners Limited intends to list on the BSE SME platform. Impact is limited to prospective investors and market participants interested in this specific company's IPO.
