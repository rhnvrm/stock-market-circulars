## Summary

BSE has announced a trading holiday in the Mutual Fund Segment on Thursday, January 15, 2026, due to Municipal Corporation Elections in Maharashtra. This is a partial modification to the previously announced trading holiday calendar for 2026 issued on December 16, 2025.

## Key Points

- Trading holiday declared for Thursday, January 15, 2026
- Applies to Mutual Fund Segment only
- Exchange will remain closed on this day
- No trading will take place in the Mutual Fund Segment
- Modifies earlier notice number 20251216-22 dated December 16, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

Partial modification to the Exchange's Trading Holidays Calendar for Year 2026 previously declared in notice number 20251216-22.

## Compliance Requirements

Members should note that no trading activity will be possible in the Mutual Fund Segment on January 15, 2026 and plan their operations accordingly.

## Important Dates

- **January 15, 2026**: Trading holiday in Mutual Fund Segment
- **Reference Date**: December 16, 2025 - Original holiday calendar notice

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact as the closure affects only the Mutual Fund Segment for a single day. Market participants in the Mutual Fund Segment should plan transactions and settlements around this holiday. The closure is due to state-level elections and does not affect other trading segments.
