## Summary

BSE has issued an update regarding the debt securities of Mac Charles Hub Projects Pvt Ltd, continuing from prior notices dated December 18, 2025, March 10, 2026, and June 3, 2026. Three NCD scrip codes will be available for trading with revised terms on account of debt restructuring, effective Friday, June 19, 2026.

## Key Points

- Three NCD scrip codes of Mac Charles Hub Projects Pvt Ltd will trade under revised terms from June 19, 2026
- The revision is on account of debt restructuring
- Total NCDs affected: 44,000 (21,500 + 13,500 + 9,000)
- This notice is a continuation of a series of notices going back to December 2025
- Detailed revised terms are available in Annexure I of the notice

## Regulatory Changes

The terms of the following NCD scrips are being revised pursuant to debt restructuring:

| Scrip Code | ISIN | No. of NCDs |
|---|---|---|
| 977385 | INE09T907019 | 21,500 |
| 977616 | INE09T907027 | 13,500 |
| 977841 | INE09T907035 | 9,000 |

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are requested to note the revised terms as per Annexure I of the notice and update their records accordingly before trading commences on June 19, 2026. Members may contact Ms. Rutuja Mhasade (+91 22 2272 8597, debt.listing@bseindia.com) or Hardik Bhuta (Assistant Vice President) for further details.

## Important Dates

- **June 19, 2026**: Effective date for revised trading terms on all three NCD scrip codes
- Prior notices: December 18, 2025 (No. 20251218-39), March 10, 2026 (No. 20260310-26), June 3, 2026 (No. 20260603-16)

## Impact Assessment

The debt restructuring of Mac Charles Hub Projects Pvt Ltd impacts holders of approximately 44,000 NCDs across three ISIN codes. Bondholders should review the revised terms in Annexure I, as changes to coupon rates, maturity dates, or repayment schedules may apply. The broader market impact is limited, but existing NCD investors face material changes to their investment terms.
