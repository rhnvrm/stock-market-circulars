## Summary

BSE Ltd. has published a list of companies whose Registrar & Transfer Agent (RTA), Adroit Corporate Services Pvt. Ltd., is scheduled to visit BSE premises during March 2026. This is a routine operational notice facilitating physical investor service activities such as share transfers, transmissions, and related requests.

## Key Points

- Adroit Corporate Services Pvt. Ltd. is the RTA for over 50 BSE-listed companies covered in this circular.
- The visit schedule covers March 2026 at BSE Ltd. premises.
- Companies span diverse sectors including finance, steel, textiles, pharma, real estate, and electronics.
- Notable companies include Safari Industries India Ltd (523025), LKP Finance Ltd (507912), Scan Steels Ltd (511672), Arihant Superstructures Ltd (506194), and Securekloud Technologies Ltd (512161).
- This circular is informational in nature, enabling investors to plan physical service requests during the RTA's visit window.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine scheduling notice issued by BSE under its investor service facilitation framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- No new compliance obligations imposed on listed companies or investors.
- Investors of the listed companies who need physical RTA services (e.g., share transfer, name change, transmission) may coordinate with Adroit Corporate Services Pvt. Ltd. during the scheduled March 2026 visit period.

## Important Dates

- **Visit Period:** March 2026 (specific dates to be confirmed with Adroit Corporate Services Pvt. Ltd.)
- **Circular Date:** 2026-03-04

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard administrative notice relevant only to investors of the ~50+ companies serviced by Adroit Corporate Services Pvt. Ltd. who require physical RTA assistance. No trading, pricing, or regulatory implications for the broader market.
