## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260408-17 outlining the trading and settlement programme for the Institutional Trading Platform (ITP) under the SME segment for the month of May 2026 (01.05.2026 to 31.05.2026). The notice is issued by the ICCL department under the Settlement/RMS category.

## Key Points

- Notice covers the full trading and settlement schedule for the ITP-SME segment for May 2026.
- Issued by ICCL (Indian Clearing Corporation Limited) under the Settlement/RMS category.
- Attachment reference: ITP052026 contains the detailed programme schedule.
- Applicable segment: SME (Small and Medium Enterprises) Institutional Trading Platform.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced. This is a routine periodic notice publishing the monthly trading and settlement calendar for the ITP-SME segment.

## Compliance Requirements

Market participants operating on the ITP-SME segment should refer to the attached schedule (ITP052026) for all applicable trading and settlement dates in May 2026 and plan their operations accordingly.

## Important Dates

- Programme Period: 01.05.2026 to 31.05.2026
- Notice Date: 08 April 2026

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This is a standard administrative notice providing the monthly settlement calendar for the niche ITP-SME segment. It affects only institutional participants active on BSE's SME Institutional Trading Platform and involves no changes to trading rules or regulatory requirements.
