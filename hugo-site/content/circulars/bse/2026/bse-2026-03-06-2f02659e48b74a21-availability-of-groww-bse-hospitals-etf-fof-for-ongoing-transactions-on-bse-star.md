## Summary

GROWW BSE HOSPITALS ETF FOF is reopening for ongoing subscription and redemption, along with SIP, STP, and SWP facilities on the BSE StAR MF Platform with effect from March 09, 2026. This follows the earlier Notice No. 20260210-41.

## Key Points

- Effective date for reopening: March 09, 2026
- Both Regular and Direct Plans are available across Growth and IDCW (Payout and Reinvestment) options
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities will be available alongside standard subscription and redemption
- All six scheme variants fall under the Equity category

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding the availability of an existing mutual fund scheme on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of BSE operating on the StAR MF Platform should update their systems to enable transactions in the GROWW BSE HOSPITALS ETF FOF schemes from March 09, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **March 09, 2026**: Effective date for reopening of GROWW BSE HOSPITALS ETF FOF for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This notice enables mutual fund distributors and investors to transact in GROWW BSE HOSPITALS ETF FOF through the BSE StAR MF platform. The six scheme variants and their ISINs are:
