## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260624-3 informing trading members that equity shares of Diksha Polymers Limited (Scrip Code: 544798) will be moved from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment (M Group), effective Thursday, July 9, 2026. This is a continuation of the earlier notice (No. 20260623-31) dated June 23, 2026.

## Key Points

- Equity shares of Diksha Polymers Limited (Scrip Code: 544798) are being transferred out of the Trade for Trade (MT Group) segment.
- Trading will shift to the Rolling segment under M Group from July 9, 2026.
- This is an SME segment stock listed on BSE.
- The change was first communicated via Notice No. 20260623-31 on June 23, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

The settlement mechanism for Diksha Polymers Limited shares changes from Trade for Trade (T+1 gross settlement, no netting) under the MT Group to the normal Rolling segment under M Group, which allows netting of trades.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should update their systems and client communication to reflect the new segment classification for Diksha Polymers Limited (Scrip Code: 544798) effective July 9, 2026. Members may contact Mr. Anurag Jain at Tel. No. 022-2272 8822 for further details.

## Important Dates

- **June 23, 2026**: Initial notice issued (Notice No. 20260623-31).
- **June 24, 2026**: Confirmation notice issued (Notice No. 20260624-3).
- **July 9, 2026**: Effective date for transfer to Rolling segment (M Group).

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to traders and investors holding or transacting in Diksha Polymers Limited shares. Moving from Trade for Trade to Rolling segment generally eases trading conditions, as netting of buy and sell positions becomes permissible. This benefits active traders in the scrip by reducing settlement obligations. Broader market impact is negligible given the SME classification and single-stock scope.
