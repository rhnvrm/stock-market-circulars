## Summary

BSE has notified members that **Bajaj Finserv Low Duration Fund** will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption, along with SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities on the **BSE StAR MF Platform**, effective **February 23, 2026**. This notice references the earlier Notice No. 20260206-20.

## Key Points

- Bajaj Finserv Low Duration Fund is reopening for all ongoing transaction types: subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP.
- Effective date for availability on BSE StAR MF Platform: **February 23, 2026**.
- Six scheme variants are covered across Regular and Direct plans with three payout options each (IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment, and Growth).
- All six schemes are classified under the **DEBT** category.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced. This is an operational notice regarding the resumption of transactions for an existing mutual fund scheme on the BSE StAR MF platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members (distributors, brokers) transacting on BSE StAR MF must enable the listed schemes for client transactions from February 23, 2026 onwards.
- Ensure correct ISIN codes are used when processing transactions for each scheme variant.

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a **low** market impact, as it pertains to the operational availability of a single mutual fund family on the BSE StAR MF distribution platform. Investors and distributors dealing in Bajaj Finserv Low Duration Fund schemes can resume or initiate transactions (including SIP/STP/SWP) from February 23, 2026.
