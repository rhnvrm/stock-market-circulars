## Summary

BSE has notified the inclusion of certain securities under the Short-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) framework effective June 22, 2026. Securities meeting the criteria are placed under either Stage I (50% margin) or Stage II (100% margin). The list of affected scrips is provided in Annexure I attached to the notice.

## Key Points

- Securities satisfying ST-ASM Stage I criteria will attract a margin rate of 50% or the existing margin, whichever is higher (capped at 100%).
- Securities satisfying ST-ASM Stage II criteria will attract a margin rate of 100% or the existing margin, whichever is higher (capped at 100%).
- Enhanced margins apply to all open positions as on June 19, 2026 and all new positions created from June 22, 2026.
- The ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the exchanges.
- Shortlisting under ASM is purely a market surveillance action and should not be construed as an adverse action against the concerned company or entity.
- When a scrip exits the framework, its price band is reinstated to the band applicable before shortlisting, unless it remains under another surveillance measure.

## Regulatory Changes

This notice is issued further to earlier exchange notices on the ASM framework: 20240920-71 (Sep 20, 2024), 20181027-1 (Oct 27, 2018), 20190719-33 (Jul 19, 2019), 20201204-56 (Dec 4, 2020), 20230925-49 (Sep 25, 2023), and 20240920-63 (Sep 20, 2024). No new framework revisions are introduced; this is a periodic application of the existing ST-ASM provisions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure collection of the enhanced margin (50% for Stage I, 100% for Stage II) on all open positions in the shortlisted securities as on June 19, 2026.
- Enhanced margins must also be collected on new positions in these securities from June 22, 2026 onwards.
- Members may contact BSE Surveillance at bse.surv@bseindia.com for clarifications.

## Important Dates

- **June 19, 2026**: Reference date for open positions subject to enhanced margins.
- **June 22, 2026**: Effective date for applicability of ST-ASM margin rates (both Stage I and Stage II).

## Impact Assessment

Traders and investors holding or intending to take positions in the shortlisted securities will face significantly higher margin requirements, which may reduce leverage and liquidity in these scrips. Stage II securities at 100% margin effectively require full cash backing for positions. Market participants should review their portfolios against the Annexure I list and arrange additional collateral if needed before June 22, 2026. The measure is a standard BSE surveillance tool and does not reflect fundamental issues with the listed companies.
