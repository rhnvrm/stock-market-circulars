## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 36,62,702 new equity shares of Asian Energy Services Ltd (Scrip Code: 530355) have been listed and are permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Wednesday, July 1, 2026. The shares were issued at a premium of Rs. 325/- (issue price Rs. 335/-) to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 36,62,702 equity shares of Rs. 10/- face value issued at a premium of Rs. 325/- (total issue price Rs. 335/-)
- Issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis via conversion of warrants
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- ISIN: INE276G01015
- Distribution numbers: 44948299 to 48611000
- Date of allotment: May 5, 2026
- Trading permitted from July 1, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification under BSE's listing operations for newly allotted equity shares.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the listing and permit trading of these new securities from July 1, 2026. No additional compliance action is required from members beyond awareness of the new shares entering the tradeable pool.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** May 5, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** July 1, 2026
- **Lock-in Period Expiry:** December 31, 2026 (all 36,62,702 shares are under lock-in until this date)

## Impact Assessment

The addition of 36,62,702 shares to the tradeable float of Asian Energy Services Ltd represents a preferential allotment to non-promoters. However, since these shares are subject to lock-in until December 31, 2026, there is no immediate increase in free-float. Market impact is expected to be minimal in the short term. Post lock-in expiry, the shares could exert modest dilution pressure on existing shareholders.
