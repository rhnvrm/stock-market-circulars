## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Equity segment on Saturday, January 03, 2026. Trading members can participate to fulfill regulatory requirements under SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD1/DSAP/CIR/P/2020/234 dated November 24, 2020. Members may choose between the mock trading session or UAT environment for testing. There will be no new BOLT TWS release. Members using third-party platforms can test various functionalities including exceptional market conditions.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Saturday, January 03, 2026
- No new BOLT TWS software release
- Members can choose between mock trading or UAT environment for regulatory compliance
- Third-party trading platform users can test various functionalities
- Testing covers call auction sessions, risk-reduction mode, trading halt, block deals, and other features
- Feedback must be submitted by 4:30 PM on the same day

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This circular provides an opportunity for members to comply with existing SEBI requirements under circular no. SEBI/HO/MRD1/DSAP/CIR/P/2020/234 dated November 24, 2020 regarding testing of trading and risk management software.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should refer to Exchange Notice No. 20201201-22 dated December 1, 2020 regarding testing of software used in trading and risk management
- Members must fulfill regulatory requirements for software testing as per SEBI circular dated November 24, 2020
- Feedback for the mock trading session should be submitted by 4:30 PM on January 03, 2026

## Important Dates

- **January 03, 2026**: Mock trading session date
- **Feedback deadline**: 4:30 PM on January 03, 2026

**Market Impact**: None. This is a mock trading session with no actual market transactions.
