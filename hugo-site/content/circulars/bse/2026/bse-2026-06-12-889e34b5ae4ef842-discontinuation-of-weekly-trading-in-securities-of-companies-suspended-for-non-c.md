## Summary

BSE has announced the discontinuation of weekly trading restrictions on Ashiana Ispat Ltd (Scrip Code: 513401), effective June 30, 2026. The company had been subject to trading on a 'Trade for Trade' basis in the Z group, permitted only on the first trading day of every week, following suspension for non-compliance with Regulation 33 (submission of financial results) of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015 for two consecutive quarters (March 2025 and June 2025).

## Key Points

- The mandated six-month weekly trading period, as prescribed by SEBI Master Circular No. HO/49/14/14(7)2025-CFD-POD2/I/3762/2026, concludes on June 29, 2026.
- Trading in Ashiana Ispat Ltd securities on a 'Trade for Trade' basis in Z group on the first trading day of every week will be discontinued w.e.f. June 30, 2026.
- The restriction was originally triggered by non-compliance with Regulation 33 (financial results submission) for March 2025 and June 2025 quarters, as per Exchange Notice No. 20251112-40 dated November 12, 2025.
- This notice is issued by the Listing Compliance-Monitoring Department.

## Regulatory Changes

The six-month weekly trading window mandated under the SEBI Master Circular (last updated January 30, 2026; erstwhile Circular No. SEBI/HO/CFD/PoD2/CIR/P/0155 dated November 11, 2024) has elapsed, triggering the removal of the weekly trading restriction for the affected company.

## Compliance Requirements

- No new compliance action is required from the listed company under this notice.
- Market participants and trading members should note that normal trading restrictions applicable to Z group / suspended securities may still apply unless separately lifted by BSE.
- Investors with queries may contact BSE at bse.soplodr@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **June 29, 2026**: Last day of the mandated six-month weekly trading period.
- **June 30, 2026**: Weekly trading restriction (first trading day of every week, Trade for Trade, Z group) discontinued for Ashiana Ispat Ltd (513401).

## Impact Assessment

The discontinuation of the weekly trading restriction is a procedural step following the expiry of the SEBI-mandated six-month period. For investors holding Ashiana Ispat Ltd shares, this means the stock will no longer be confined to trading only on the first day of each week in the Trade for Trade Z group under this specific notice. However, the underlying suspension for LODR non-compliance may persist under separate regulatory action; investors should verify the company's current compliance and group classification before trading.
