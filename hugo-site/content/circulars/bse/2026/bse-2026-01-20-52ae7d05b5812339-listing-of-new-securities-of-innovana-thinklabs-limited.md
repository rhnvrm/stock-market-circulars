## Summary

BSE has listed 160,000 equity shares of Innovana Thinklabs Limited (Scrip Code: 544302) effective January 21, 2026. These shares were issued at a premium of Rs. 421/- per share to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. The shares are ranking pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 160,000 equity shares of face value Rs. 10/- each listed
- Issue price: Rs. 431/- per share (Rs. 10 face value + Rs. 421 premium)
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis
- Allotment pursuant to warrant conversion
- Distribution numbers: 20500001 to 20660000
- ISIN: INE403Y01018
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Trading commences: January 21, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification following SEBI preferential allotment regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the new securities for trading from January 21, 2026
- Lock-in restrictions apply as specified and must be enforced

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: October 13, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: January 21, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (150,000 shares)**: July 30, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (10,000 shares)**: July 29, 2026

## Lock-in Details

- 150,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 20500001 to 20650000): Locked-in until July 30, 2026
- 10,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 20650001 to 20660000): Locked-in until July 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The listing represents a small preferential allotment of 160,000 shares with most shares (150,000) under lock-in until July 2026. This is a routine corporate action for existing listed company Innovana Thinklabs Limited. Trading members should update their records for the new distribution numbers and ensure lock-in restrictions are properly enforced.
