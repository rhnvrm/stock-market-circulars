## Summary

BSE's Surveillance Department (DOSS) sought clarification from Rajesh Exports Ltd (Scrip Code: 531500) in response to news articles circulating on various newspapers, websites, and television channels, including CNBC TV18. The exchange has received the clarification from the company to inform members and investors about the accuracy of such reports.

## Key Points

- BSE Surveillance sought clarification from Rajesh Exports Ltd (Scrip Code: 531500)
- Clarification was sought in relation to news appearing on CNBC TV18 and potentially other media outlets
- The exchange has received the company's response
- Notice issued by Sanjay Jain (Deputy Vice President, Surveillance) and Shreya Ghose (Manager, Surveillance)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes associated with this notice.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action required for members or investors beyond noting the clarification provided by Rajesh Exports Ltd. Investors should refer to the company's official clarification for accurate information regarding the news articles in question.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 04 June 2026

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This is a routine surveillance measure by BSE to ensure transparency and accuracy of information available to investors. The clarification process helps address market speculation arising from media reports. No trading restrictions or penalties are imposed on Rajesh Exports Ltd as part of this notice.
