## Summary

BSE Limited, through Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd. (ICCL), has announced Demat Auction 614 for Rolling Settlement No. DR-614/2026-27. Trading members of the Exchange are invited to submit offers for purchase of securities through auction on April 22, 2026.

## Key Points

- Auction offer entry window: 2:00 PM to 2:45 PM on April 22, 2026
- Auction matching session: 2:45 PM to 3:00 PM
- Delivery statement and accepted auction offer reports available: 3:00 PM to 3:30 PM
- Money statement and final reports: 3:35 PM to 5:30 PM
- Auction pay-in scheduled for April 23, 2026 at 8:00 AM
- Offers must be submitted through trading terminals
- No corrections permitted for punching errors in securities code or rate
- Exchange reserves the right to accept or reject any offer without reason

## Regulatory Changes

Following implementation of the Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment, settlement is now processed through the Clearing Member of a Trading Member. The previous restriction on trading members offering securities in auction for settlements where they had short-delivered has been removed.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and Custodians must check their auction pay-in obligations for securities
- Securities pay-in must be executed in depositories within depository cut-off timings
- Trading members must refer to the cutoff rate in the "Auction Offer Entry Screen" before submitting offers
- Trading members must review notices related to Corporate Actions (Ex-Rights, Ex-Bonus, Ex-Split, etc.) issued by respective departments
- Extreme care required when entering securities codes and rates; no corrections allowed post-submission

## Important Dates

- **April 22, 2026**: Auction offer entry (2:00 PM – 2:45 PM), matching, and report generation
- **April 23, 2026**: Auction pay-in at 8:00 AM

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice with moderate impact limited to trading members and custodians involved in Rolling Settlement DR-614/2026-27. Members with short delivery positions must participate in the auction or arrange securities pay-in by the prescribed deadlines. The removal of restrictions on trading members offering in the same settlement where they short-delivered provides additional flexibility under the Uniform Membership structure.
