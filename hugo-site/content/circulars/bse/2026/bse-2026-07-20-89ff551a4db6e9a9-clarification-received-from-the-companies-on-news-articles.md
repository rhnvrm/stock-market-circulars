## Summary

BSE, as part of its routine surveillance to verify accuracy of news articles appearing in newspapers, websites, and television channels, sought clarifications from Pfizer Ltd (Scrip Code: 500680) and Rajesh Exports Ltd (Scrip Code: 531500) regarding news coverage about them. Pfizer Ltd has submitted its reply, which is attached to the notice, while the reply from Rajesh Exports Ltd is still awaited.

## Key Points

- BSE sought clarification from Pfizer Ltd and Rajesh Exports Ltd on news articles referencing them, published via The Hindu Business Line.
- Pfizer Ltd (Scrip Code: 500680) has submitted its clarification reply, attached to the notice.
- Reply from Rajesh Exports Ltd (Scrip Code: 531500) is pending as of the notice date.
- Notice issued by BSE's Department of Surveillance (DOSS), Equity segment.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine surveillance clarification notice, not a regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

Rajesh Exports Ltd is expected to submit its clarification reply to BSE regarding the referenced news article. No further action is required from investors or market participants.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 20 July 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard surveillance and disclosure procedure with minimal direct market impact. It informs investors that BSE has sought verification of news reports concerning these companies; the pending reply from Rajesh Exports Ltd may warrant monitoring for any follow-up disclosure, but no immediate trading or compliance implications arise from this notice alone.
