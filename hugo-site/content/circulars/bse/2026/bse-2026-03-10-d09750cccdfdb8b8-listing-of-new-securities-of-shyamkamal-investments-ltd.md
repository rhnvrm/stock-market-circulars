## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 1,29,15,000 new equity shares of Shyamkamal Investments Ltd (Scrip Code: 505515) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Wednesday, March 11, 2026. These shares were issued at par (Rs. 10/-) to non-promoters on a preferential basis.

## Key Points

- **Company:** Shyamkamal Investments Ltd (Scrip Code: 505515)
- **New Shares Listed:** 1,29,15,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each
- **Issue Type:** Preferential allotment to Non-Promoters at par
- **Effective Trading Date:** Wednesday, March 11, 2026
- **ISIN:** INE203N01015
- **Distribution Numbers:** 13498001 to 26413000
- **Date of Allotment:** December 12, 2025
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 10/- per share
- **Ranking:** Pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice pursuant to a preferential allotment already completed on December 12, 2025.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed and may facilitate trading in the newly listed shares from March 11, 2026.
- Lock-in restrictions apply to all 1,29,15,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 13498001 to 26413000) until **September 14, 2026**.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** December 12, 2025
- **Effective Listing / Trading Date:** March 11, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry Date:** September 14, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action with limited market impact. The newly listed shares are subject to lock-in until September 14, 2026, meaning they cannot be freely traded until that date. The preferential allotment to non-promoters increases the public float of Shyamkamal Investments Ltd over time. No broader market or systemic implications.
