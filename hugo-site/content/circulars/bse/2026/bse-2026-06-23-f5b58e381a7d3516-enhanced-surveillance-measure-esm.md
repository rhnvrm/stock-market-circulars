## Summary

BSE has updated the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework pursuant to prior notices dating back to June 2023. Securities listed in Annexure I are newly included under ESM and will attract a minimum 100% margin on all open and new positions from June 25, 2026. Certain securities are also being downgraded to Stage I, while others are being removed from the ESM framework entirely, all effective June 24–25, 2026.

## Key Points

- Securities in Annexure I are included under ESM and will attract minimum 100% margin w.e.f. June 25, 2026 on all open positions as on June 24, 2026 and new positions created from June 25, 2026.
- Select securities already under ESM will be moved to a lower stage (Stage I) w.e.f. June 24, 2026.
- All securities qualifying under ESM will be shifted from Rolling Settlement to Trade-for-Trade (TFT) segment w.e.f. June 25, 2026.
- Securities moving to Stage II will be placed under Trade-for-Trade with a 2% price band and subject to Periodic Call Auction w.e.f. June 24, 2026.
- Securities moving out of the ESM framework (Annexure II) will have their previous price bands reinstated w.e.f. June 24, 2026, unless covered by another surveillance measure.
- A consolidated list of all securities currently under ESM is available in Annexure III.
- ESM operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by exchanges.
- Inclusion under ESM is purely a market surveillance action and should not be construed as an adverse action against the concerned company.

## Regulatory Changes

This notice updates the ESM framework established under prior BSE notices (20230602-44, 20230718-46, 20240809-42, 20240920-63, 20241004-65, 20250725-61). It applies periodic review outcomes: new inclusions, stage downgrades to Stage I, and removals from the framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure collection of minimum 100% margin on all open positions in newly included ESM securities as on June 24, 2026, and on any new positions from June 25, 2026.
- Members must note the transition of affected securities from Rolling Settlement to Trade-for-Trade segment.
- Stage II securities must be traded under the Periodic Call Auction mechanism with a 2% price band.
- For clarifications, members may write to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **June 24, 2026**: Stage I downgrade effective; securities exit ESM framework; Stage II Trade-for-Trade with 2% price band and Periodic Call Auction begins.
- **June 25, 2026**: Minimum 100% margin requirement effective for newly included ESM securities; shift from Rolling Settlement to Trade-for-Trade segment effective.

## Impact Assessment

Affected securities face significantly tightened trading conditions: mandatory 100% upfront margin eliminates leveraged positions, the shift to Trade-for-Trade eliminates netting benefits, and Stage II securities face an additional 2% price band restriction via Periodic Call Auction. These measures collectively reduce liquidity and speculative activity in the identified scrips. Market participants holding positions in newly included securities must arrange additional margin by June 24, 2026 to avoid forced liquidation.
