## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for REMEDIUM LIFECARE LIMITED (CIN: L24100MH1988PLC343805). The company's name has been officially changed to SUPHA PHARMACHEM LIMITED with effect from December 15, 2025. The certificate was issued by the Central Processing Centre, ROC on December 15, 2025.

## Key Points

- **Old Name**: REMEDIUM LIFECARE LIMITED
- **New Name**: SUPHA PHARMACHEM LIMITED
- **CIN**: L24100MH1988PLC343805
- **Effective Date**: December 15, 2025
- **Original Incorporation Name**: ROXY ENGINEERS PRIVATE LIMITED
- **Certificate Issued By**: Ibson Shah, Central Processing Centre
- **Registered Address**: Office No.9, K Raheja Prime, Marol Industrial Estate, Behind Ravi Vihar Hotel, Sagbaug Road, Marol, Andheri East, Marol Naka, Mumbai, Mumbai-400059, Maharashtra, India

## Regulatory Changes

This is an administrative name change under the Companies Act. The certificate has been issued pursuant to rule 29 of the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014. The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company is **obligated to display the old name (REMEDIUM LIFECARE LIMITED) for a period of two years** along with its new name at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- All stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company and its directors on the MCA website at www.mca.gov.in/MCA21
- The company must update its name across all official documents, correspondence, and statutory filings

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date of Name Change**: December 15, 2025
- **Certificate Issue Date**: December 15, 2025
- **Dual Name Display Period**: Two years from December 15, 2025 (until December 15, 2027)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine administrative corporate action that does not affect the company's operations, financial position, or shareholder rights.

**Operational Impact**: The company will need to update all branding, documentation, and statutory records to reflect the new name while maintaining dual display for the mandatory two-year period.

**Stakeholder Impact**: No impact on rights and liabilities of stakeholders. Shareholders, creditors, and other stakeholders retain all existing rights and obligations under the new company name.
