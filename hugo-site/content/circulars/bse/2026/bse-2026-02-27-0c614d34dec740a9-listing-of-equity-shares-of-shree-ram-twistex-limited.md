## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260227-14 informing trading members that the equity shares of Shree Ram Twistex Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange. The exact listing date and further security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Shree Ram Twistex Limited equity shares are approved for listing on BSE
- Scrip Code: 544716
- Trading Symbol: SRTL
- ISIN: INE19GK01015
- Listing date to be announced via a separate notice
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing admission notice under BSE's listing procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the upcoming listing of SRTL and await the subsequent notice from BSE containing the confirmed listing date and detailed security information before initiating trades.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: February 27, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced via a separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine new listing notice with medium market impact. The listing of Shree Ram Twistex Limited (SRTL) will introduce a new equity security on BSE. Traders and investors should monitor the follow-up notice for the confirmed listing date and any specific trading conditions applicable at the time of listing.
