## Summary

BSE has informed market participants that 1,26,71,602 new equity shares of Coforge Limited (Scrip Code: 532541), issued pursuant to the Scheme of Amalgamation of Cigniti Technologies Limited with and into Coforge Limited, will be listed and permitted for trading with effect from Monday, July 13, 2026.

## Key Points

- 1,26,71,602 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each are being listed on BSE.
- These shares were issued to shareholders and creditors of Cigniti Technologies Limited (Transferor Company) as part of its amalgamation with Coforge Limited (Transferee Company).
- The new shares rank pari-passu with Coforge's existing equity shares.
- ISIN Number: INE591G01025.
- Distinctive Numbers range: 429999945 – 442671546.
- Date of Allotment: June 03, 2026.
- No additional attachments were provided with this notice.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an administrative listing approval finalizing a previously sanctioned scheme of amalgamation, not a new regulatory requirement.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific action required from market participants; brokers and depositories should ensure the new shares under the given ISIN and distinctive number range are correctly reflected in trading and settlement systems from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: June 03, 2026
- Notice Date: July 01, 2026 (as per notice signatures) / Notice No. dated July 10, 2026
- Effective Date of Listing and Trading: Monday, July 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing increases Coforge Limited's total outstanding equity share capital by 1,26,71,602 shares, completing the share issuance component of the Cigniti Technologies amalgamation. This event finalizes shareholder consolidation from the merger and may result in a marginal increase in free float and trading liquidity, but is not expected to significantly affect the stock price given it reflects an already-announced and approved corporate action.
