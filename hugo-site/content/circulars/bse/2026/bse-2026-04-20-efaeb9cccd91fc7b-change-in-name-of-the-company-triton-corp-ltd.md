## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Triton Corp Ltd (Scrip Code 523387) has informed the Exchange of a change in its company name. Effective April 24, 2026, the company will be renamed to HOMRE LIMITED, and its identifiers on the BOLT Plus trading system will be updated accordingly.

## Key Points

- Existing company name: Triton Corp Ltd
- New company name: HOMRE LIMITED
- Scrip Code remains unchanged: 523387
- Scrip ID on BOLT Plus System changes to: HOM
- Abbreviated name on BOLT Plus System changes to: HOM
- Effective date: April 24, 2026
- Segment: Equity
- Notice issued by: Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President – Listing Compliance & Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory rule changes. This is an administrative update reflecting the company's legal name change as communicated to BSE under listing obligations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should update their records to reflect the new name HOMRE LIMITED and new Scrip ID/Abbreviated Name (HOM) on the BOLT Plus System from April 24, 2026.
- No action required from investors beyond being aware of the name change when referencing Scrip Code 523387.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: April 20, 2026
- Effective Date of Name Change on BOLT Plus System: April 24, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine corporate name change. The scrip code (523387) remains the same, ensuring continuity for trading and settlement. Trading members using the BOLT Plus System should note the updated Scrip ID and Abbreviated Name (HOM) to avoid confusion from April 24, 2026 onwards.
