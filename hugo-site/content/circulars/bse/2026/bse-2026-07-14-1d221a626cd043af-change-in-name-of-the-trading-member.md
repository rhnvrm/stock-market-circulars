## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that SAAR Securities (India) Private Limited (Member No. 6833) will change its name to Tradepure Stock Broking Private Limited, effective Wednesday, 15th July 2026.

## Key Points

- Trading member SAAR Securities (India) Private Limited (Member No. 6833) is changing its registered name.
- The new name will be "Tradepure Stock Broking Private Limited".
- Member number 6833 remains unchanged.
- No attachments were provided with this notice.
- Queries can be directed to membership.ops@bseindia.com.

## Regulatory Changes

This is an administrative update to the trading member's registered name; no change to trading rules, membership status, or regulatory framework is involved.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members and market participants interacting with this member should update their records to reflect the new name, "Tradepure Stock Broking Private Limited", from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- Effective date of name change: Wednesday, 15th July 2026.
- Notice issued: 14 July 2026.

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact administrative notice affecting only the registered name of a single trading member. It has no bearing on stock prices, listed securities, or broader market operations, but counterparties should update internal records accordingly.
