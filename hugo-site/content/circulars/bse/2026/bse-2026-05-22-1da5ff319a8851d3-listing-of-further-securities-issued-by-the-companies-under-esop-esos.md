## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that additional equity shares issued by 11 companies under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP/ESOS) are listed and admitted for trading on the Exchange with effect from **Monday, May 25, 2026**. None of the newly listed shares are subject to lock-in restrictions.

## Key Points

- 11 companies have new ESOP/ESOS shares admitted to trading from May 25, 2026
- No lock-in period applies to any of the newly listed securities
- Largest issuance: Physicswallah Limited with 28,908,108 shares (ISIN INE0LP301011, Scrip 544609)
- Smallest issuance: Tata Elxsi Limited with 381 shares (ISIN INE670A01012, Scrip 500408)
- Face values range from ₹1 (Physicswallah) to ₹10 (RBL Bank, SBI Life, Shadowfax, Tata Elxsi)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification pursuant to ESOP/ESOS share issuances by listed companies.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are requested to take note of the newly listed securities and update their systems accordingly. No specific compliance action is required beyond awareness of the new share listings.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** May 22, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** Monday, May 25, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry:** Not applicable (NA) for all securities

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice with minimal market impact. The newly listed ESOP/ESOS shares represent employee compensation conversions and are standard corporate actions. The addition of shares is dilutive but marginal for most companies. Physicswallah's issuance of ~28.9 million shares is the largest in absolute terms but must be assessed relative to total outstanding shares to gauge dilution impact. No market disruption is expected.
