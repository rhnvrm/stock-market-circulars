## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members that Captain Polyplast Ltd (Scrip Code: 536974) is undergoing a group change from Group X to Group B, effective July 23, 2026.

## Key Points

- Captain Polyplast Ltd (Scrip Code 536974) is being moved from Group X to Group B.
- The change is effective from July 23, 2026.
- Trading members have been asked to take note of the reclassification.
- No attachments were provided with this notice.

## Regulatory Changes

This is a routine group reclassification carried out under BSE's Listing Operations department; it does not represent a new regulation but an administrative categorization change for the specified scrip.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members and brokers should update their systems to reflect the revised group classification for Captain Polyplast Ltd to ensure accurate trading, margin, and settlement treatment under Group B norms.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 22, 2026
- Effective Date of Group Change: July 23, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The move from Group X (typically comprising illiquid/restricted securities) to Group B indicates an improvement in the trading and settlement framework applicable to Captain Polyplast Ltd, potentially allowing for normal trading and settlement processes rather than trade-for-trade restrictions. The impact is specific to this scrip and its market participants, with no broader market implications.
