## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members that six Treasury Bills and Government Securities are maturing on their respective redemption dates. As a result, trading members are advised not to deal in these securities effective July 07, 2026.

## Key Points

- Six debt instruments identified for maturity and trading suspension
- Instruments include: 182TB90726 (Scrip Code 805164, ISIN IN002025Y404), 364TB90726 (Scrip Code 805090, ISIN IN002025Z153), 833GOI2026 (Scrip Code 800172, ISIN IN0020120039), 833GSJUL26 (Scrip Code 800796, ISIN IN000726P019), 91TB90726 (Scrip Code 805199, ISIN IN002026X016), and GS09JUL26 (Scrip Code 800795, ISIN IN000726C025)
- This action is referenced under DR-666/2026-2027
- Notice applies to the Debt segment under Listing Operations department

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a routine administrative notice implementing standard procedure for handling maturing government debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must cease dealing in the listed T-bills and government securities effective July 07, 2026, as these instruments will have matured and are no longer valid for trading.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 06, 2026
- Trading suspension effective: July 07, 2026 (redemption/maturity date)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, low-impact administrative notice affecting only debt segment trading members dealing in the specified T-bills and government securities. There is no impact on equity markets or listed company stocks. Trading members holding positions in these instruments should ensure settlement and redemption processes are completed prior to the effective date.
