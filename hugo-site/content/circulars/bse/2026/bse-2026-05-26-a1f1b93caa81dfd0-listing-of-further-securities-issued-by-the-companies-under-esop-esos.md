## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that further new securities issued by 16 companies under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP/ESOS) are listed and admitted for trading on the Exchange with effect from Wednesday, May 27, 2026. No lock-in period applies to any of the listed securities.

## Key Points

- 16 companies have new ESOP/ESOS shares admitted to trading from May 27, 2026
- No lock-in restrictions apply to any of the newly listed securities
- Wipro Limited has the largest allotment at 5,256,514 shares (face value ₹2)
- Anthem Biosciences Limited has the second-largest allotment at 1,481,250 shares (face value ₹2)
- HDFC Asset Management Company Limited allotted 184,952 shares (face value ₹5)
- Eicher Motors Limited allotted 183,326 shares (face value ₹1)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine operational notice under BSE's listing operations for employee stock option scheme issuances.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are requested to take note of the newly listed securities and update their systems accordingly. No specific compliance action is required from market participants beyond acknowledging the new securities available for trading.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** May 26, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** Wednesday, May 27, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notice with minimal market impact. The newly listed ESOP/ESOS shares represent employee compensation conversions and do not introduce significant new float except for Wipro (5.25 million shares) and Anthem Biosciences (1.48 million shares). None of the securities carry lock-in restrictions, so they are immediately tradable. The listings span diverse sectors including AMCs (HDFC AMC, Aditya Birla Sun Life AMC, UTI AMC), automotive (Eicher Motors, UNO Minda), IT (Wipro, Oracle Financial Services, NIIT, NIIT Learning Systems), and infrastructure (STL Networks, Clean Max, Genus Power).
