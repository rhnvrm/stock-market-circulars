## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the equity shares of Horizon Reclaim (India) Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange. The company is being listed on the SME segment. The specific listing date and further security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Equity shares of Horizon Reclaim (India) Limited are to be listed on BSE's SME segment.
- Scrip code assigned: **544794**; Symbol: **HORIZON**; ISIN: **INE1SEO01013**.
- The actual listing date has not yet been announced and will be informed via a separate notice.
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard new listing notification under BSE's DCS-Listing department.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the scrip details (code 544794, symbol HORIZON, ISIN INE1SEO01013) and await the subsequent notice confirming the listing date before initiating any dealings in the security.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 18, 2026
- **Listing Date:** To be announced via a separate notice.

## Impact Assessment

Limited market-wide impact as this is a single SME-segment listing. Relevant primarily to trading members and investors interested in Horizon Reclaim (India) Limited. No immediate trading action is required until the listing date notice is issued.
