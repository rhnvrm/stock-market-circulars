## Summary

BSE has informed trading members of the new ISIN number for Kotak Nifty PSU Bank ETF (Scrip Code: 590107) units, following a split/change in face value from Rs. 10/- to Re. 1/- per unit. This is a continuation of the earlier Exchange Notice No. 20260630-20 dated June 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- New ISIN for Kotak Nifty PSU Bank ETF: INF174KA1A86
- Change is due to split/change in face value of ETF units from Rs. 10/- to Re. 1/-
- The new ISIN will be effective for trades done on and from the ex-date
- Reference: DR-669/2026-2027

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory change; this is an operational update implementing a previously announced face value split via a new ISIN assignment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must update their systems to reflect the new ISIN (INF174KA1A86) for Kotak Nifty PSU Bank ETF units effective from the ex-date to ensure accurate trade processing and settlement.

## Important Dates

- Ex-Date: 10-07-2026
- Notice Date: 08 July 2026
- Prior reference notice: 20260630-20 dated 30 June 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact administrative/operational notice affecting only holders and traders of Kotak Nifty PSU Bank ETF units. The change in ISIN due to face value split does not affect the underlying value of investment but requires system and record updates by trading members and depositories for continuity of trading post ex-date.
