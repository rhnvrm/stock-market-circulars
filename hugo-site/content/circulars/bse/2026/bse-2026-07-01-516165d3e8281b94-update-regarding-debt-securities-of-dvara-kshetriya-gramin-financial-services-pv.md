## Summary

BSE has issued a notice in continuation to its earlier Exchange Notice No. 20250513-30 dated 13 May 2025, regarding the debt securities of Dvara Kshetriya Gramin Financial Services Private Limited. Trading Members are informed that the debt security bearing Scrip Code 976687 and ISIN INE179P08082 will be available for trading with revised terms, as detailed in the accompanying Annexure, with effect from Thursday, July 1, 2026.

## Key Points

- The notice pertains to debt securities of Dvara Kshetriya Gramin Financial Services Private Limited.
- Scrip Code: 976687; ISIN: INE179P08082.
- Revised terms for the security are detailed in an Annexure attached to the notice.
- This is a follow-up to a prior Exchange Notice (20250513-30) dated 13 May 2025.
- Revised terms take effect from July 1, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced; this is an operational update implementing revised terms for an existing listed debt security as detailed in the Annexure (not reproduced in the notice text).

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members dealing in this debt security must update their systems/records to reflect the revised terms effective July 1, 2026, and may contact BSE's Debt Listing department for clarifications.

## Important Dates

- 13 May 2025: Original Exchange Notice (20250513-30) issued.
- 1 July 2026: Revised terms become effective for trading.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to Trading Members and investors holding or dealing in this specific debt security (ISIN INE179P08082) of Dvara Kshetriya Gramin Financial Services Pvt Ltd. There is no broader market-wide impact expected.
