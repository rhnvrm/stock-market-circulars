## Summary

BSE has notified the inclusion of certain securities under Short-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) Stage I and Stage II. Enhanced margin requirements will apply from June 19, 2026 on all open positions as of June 18, 2026, and on all new positions created from June 19, 2026. The specific securities are listed in Annexure I attached to the notice.

## Key Points

- Securities in ST-ASM Stage I are subject to a minimum margin of 50% (or existing margin if higher), capped at a maximum of 100%.
- Securities in ST-ASM Stage II are subject to a minimum margin of 100% (or existing margin if higher), capped at 100%.
- Enhanced margins apply to open positions as on June 18, 2026 and all new positions from June 19, 2026.
- ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures.
- Shortlisting under ASM is purely for market surveillance purposes and is not an adverse action against the concerned company.
- When a scrip exits the framework, its price band reverts to the pre-ASM level unless it remains under another surveillance measure.

## Regulatory Changes

This notice is issued in continuation of prior BSE notices on the ASM framework:
- Notice 20240920-71 dated September 20, 2024
- Notice 20181027-1 dated October 27, 2018
- Notice 20190719-33 dated July 19, 2019
- Notice 20201204-56 dated December 4, 2020
- Notice 20230925-49 dated September 25, 2023
- Notice 20240920-63 dated September 20, 2024

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure the applicable margin rates (50% for ST-ASM Stage I; 100% for ST-ASM Stage II) are collected on all affected open and new positions.
- Members should review Annexure I to identify the specific securities under each stage.
- Margin obligations apply from June 19, 2026 on all open positions held as of June 18, 2026.
- For clarifications, members may contact BSE Surveillance at bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **June 17, 2026**: Notice date; Annexure I of affected securities published.
- **June 18, 2026**: Reference date for open positions subject to enhanced margins.
- **June 19, 2026**: Enhanced margin requirements become effective for ST-ASM Stage I and Stage II securities.

## Impact Assessment

The imposition of ST-ASM significantly increases the cost of holding or taking positions in the affected securities by mandating higher upfront margins (up to 100%). This is likely to reduce speculative activity and liquidity in these scrips in the short term. Investors with leveraged positions in the listed securities will need to either provide additional margin or reduce their exposure by June 19, 2026. The measure is part of BSE's ongoing market surveillance to curb excessive volatility and protect market integrity.
