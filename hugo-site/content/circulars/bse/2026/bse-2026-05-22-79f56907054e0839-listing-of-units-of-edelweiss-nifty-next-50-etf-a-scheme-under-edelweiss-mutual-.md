## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (No. 20260522-8) dated 22 May 2026 announcing the listing of units of Edelweiss Nifty Next 50 ETF, a scheme under Edelweiss Mutual Fund, on the Mutual Fund segment of BSE.

## Key Points

- Notice Number: 20260522-8
- Date of Notice: 22 May 2026
- Category: Company Related
- Segment: Mutual Fund
- Department: DCS-Listing
- The circular pertains to the listing of units of Edelweiss Nifty Next 50 ETF
- The ETF is a scheme under Edelweiss Mutual Fund
- No attachments were included with this notice

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this notice. It is a standard listing announcement for a new ETF scheme.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements are outlined in this notice for market participants beyond standard ETF trading and settlement procedures applicable to Mutual Fund segment securities.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 22 May 2026
- Listing effective as of the date of the notice (22 May 2026)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notice with low market impact. The listing of Edelweiss Nifty Next 50 ETF units on BSE provides investors with an additional exchange-traded vehicle to gain exposure to the Nifty Next 50 index. The impact is limited to participants interested in this specific ETF scheme and does not affect broader market operations or existing securities.
