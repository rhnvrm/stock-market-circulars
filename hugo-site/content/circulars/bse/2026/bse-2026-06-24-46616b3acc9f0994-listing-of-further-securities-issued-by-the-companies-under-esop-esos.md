## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted for trading new equity shares issued by 16 companies under their Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP/ESOS), effective Thursday, June 25, 2026. The total shares added span a range of face values (₹1, ₹2, and ₹10) and none carry any lock-in restrictions.

## Key Points

- 16 companies have new ESOP/ESOS shares admitted for trading from June 25, 2026
- No lock-in period applies to any of the newly listed shares
- Shares span face values of ₹1, ₹2, and ₹10 depending on the company
- Largest issuances by volume: Premier Energies Limited (957,142 shares), Nippon Life India Asset Management (515,730 shares), and Fedbank Financial Services (394,636 shares)
- Smallest issuances: PB Fintech Limited (1,800 shares) and IndoStar Capital Finance (2,900 shares)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification under existing ESOP/ESOS frameworks.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to take note of the new securities and their respective ISIN and scrip codes for trading purposes from June 25, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 24, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** June 25, 2026 (Thursday)
