## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Indsoya Ltd (Scrip Code 503639) is changing its name to Apollo Ingredients Limited effective May 15, 2026. The SCRIP ID and ABBREVIATED NAME on the BOLT Plus System will be updated accordingly to APOLLOIN.

## Key Points

- Existing company name: Indsoya Ltd
- New company name: Apollo Ingredients Limited
- Scrip Code: 503639 (unchanged)
- New SCRIP ID on BOLT Plus System: APOLLOIN
- New Abbreviated Name on BOLT Plus System: APOLLOIN
- Change effective date: May 15, 2026
- Segment: Equity

## Regulatory Changes

The company has obtained a Certificate of Incorporation reflecting the new name. BSE is updating the SCRIP ID and ABBREVIATED NAME in the BOLT Plus System to reflect the name change from Indsoya Ltd to Apollo Ingredients Limited.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to take note of the updated SCRIP ID (APOLLOIN) and ABBREVIATED NAME (APOLLOIN) for all trading and back-office operations from May 15, 2026 onwards. Any references to the old name or scrip identifiers should be updated in internal systems.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: May 11, 2026
- Effective Date of Name Change on BOLT Plus System: May 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative change. Trading members need to update their systems to reflect the new company name and BOLT Plus identifiers. The scrip code (503639) remains unchanged, minimising disruption. The change affects equity segment trading and any references to SCRIP ID or abbreviated name in trading terminals and back-office systems.
