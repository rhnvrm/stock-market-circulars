## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of ACCRETION NUTRAVEDA LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544694), an SME IPO listing, will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment (M Group) effective February 18, 2026. This follows the Exchange's previous notice dated February 3, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: ACCRETION NUTRAVEDA LIMITED (SME IPO)
- Scrip Code: 544694
- Current segment: Trade for Trade (MT Group)
- New segment: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective date: Wednesday, February 18, 2026
- Notice issued: February 4, 2026
- Previous notice reference: Notice No. 20260203-44 dated February 3, 2026
- Contact: Mr. Anurag Jain, Tel: 022-2272 8822

## Regulatory Changes

The stock will transition from Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to Rolling segment (M Group). This represents a shift from a more restrictive trading mechanism to normal rolling settlement, indicating the stock has completed its initial post-IPO stabilization period.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their systems to reflect the group change from MT to M Group
- Trading members should contact Mr. Anurag Jain for further operational details and clarifications
- Members need to ensure proper categorization and risk management parameters are updated for the new segment

## Important Dates

- **February 3, 2026**: Previous notice issued (Notice No. 20260203-44)
- **February 4, 2026**: Current notice issued
- **February 18, 2026**: Effective date for transfer to Rolling segment (M Group)

## Impact Assessment

**Trading Impact**: The transfer from Trade for Trade to Rolling segment will significantly improve liquidity and trading flexibility for ACCRETION NUTRAVEDA LIMITED shares. Investors will no longer face mandatory delivery requirements and can trade with intraday leverage.

**Market Participants**: Trading members and brokers need to update their systems and risk parameters. Retail and institutional investors will benefit from enhanced trading capabilities and reduced settlement restrictions.

**Liquidity Enhancement**: The move to M Group typically indicates the company has met stability criteria post-IPO, allowing for normal market-making activities and improved price discovery mechanisms.
