## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document of Zebion Infotech Limited, which was filed on March 07, 2026. The document has been made available on the BSE SME website in accordance with Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations for a mandatory public comment period of at least 21 days.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Zebion Infotech Limited on March 07, 2026
- Document is hosted on the BSE SME website: https://www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public comments are invited within 21 days from the date of this notice (March 09, 2026)
- The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This notice is in compliance with existing ICDR Regulations (Schedule VI) requirements for SME IPO draft offer document disclosures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Interested parties and public may review the Draft Offer Document on the BSE SME website
- Any comments or objections must be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com within 21 days from March 09, 2026
- Zebion Infotech Limited is required to address any public comments received during this period

## Important Dates

- **Document Filing Date:** March 07, 2026
- **Notice Date:** March 09, 2026
- **Public Comment Deadline:** March 30, 2026 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal broad market impact as it pertains solely to the SME IPO process for Zebion Infotech Limited. It is a procedural disclosure requirement enabling public scrutiny of the draft offer document before the IPO proceeds. Investors and analysts interested in the SME segment may review the document and submit comments to the exchange.
