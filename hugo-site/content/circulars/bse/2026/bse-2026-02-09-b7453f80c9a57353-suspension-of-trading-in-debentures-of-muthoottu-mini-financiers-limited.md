## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in specific non-convertible debentures of MUTHOOTTU MINI FINANCIERS LIMITED effective February 12, 2026. The suspension is due to the company setting a record date for redemption of debentures. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Company: MUTHOOTTU MINI FINANCIERS LIMITED
- ISIN: INE101Q07AP9
- Scrip Code: 975439
- Series: MMFL-10%-27-2-26-PVT
- Record Date: February 12, 2026
- No Dealings From: February 12, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures
- Notice Reference: DR-819/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures (INE101Q07AP9) from February 12, 2026
- Trading members are required to take note of the suspension
- Debenture holders of record as of February 12, 2026 will be eligible for redemption

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: February 9, 2026
- **Record Date**: February 12, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Date**: February 12, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This affects only a specific series of privately placed debentures of MUTHOOTTU MINI FINANCIERS LIMITED. The suspension is temporary and related to the redemption process.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Standard procedure for debenture redemption. Trading members should ensure compliance by avoiding transactions in the specified ISIN from the suspension date.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to holders of this specific debenture series who will receive redemption proceeds as per the terms of the instrument.
