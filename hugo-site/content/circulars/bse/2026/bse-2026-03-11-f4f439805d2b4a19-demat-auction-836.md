## Summary

Bombay Stock Exchange Ltd. Mumbai has issued the Demat Auction notice for Settlement No. 836, dated March 11, 2026. The auction covers short deliveries across 124 securities, requiring participants with outstanding obligations to settle through the auction mechanism.

## Key Points

- Settlement No. 836 dated March 11, 2026
- 124 scrips listed for demat auction
- Quantities range from 1 share (multiple scrips) to 245,520 shares (Deep Diamond, scrip 539559)
- Large auction quantities also include Rajnish Wellness (541601) at 50,000 shares and DSP Mutual Fund (544247) at 40,000 units
- Covers a broad cross-section of market segments including equity, ETFs, and other securities

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice under BSE's standard demat auction settlement process for handling short deliveries.

## Compliance Requirements

- Member brokers with short delivery obligations in the listed scrips must participate in the demat auction
- Buyers who did not receive deliveries will be settled through the auction at applicable close-out prices
- Members must ensure compliance with BSE auction settlement procedures and timelines

## Important Dates

- Auction Date: March 11, 2026
- Settlement No.: 836
