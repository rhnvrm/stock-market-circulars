## Summary

Andhra Pradesh Capital Region Development Authority (APCRDA) has announced part redemption of its bonds along with interest payment. The bonds (ISIN: INE01E708032, Scrip Code: 958178) will have their face value reduced to Rs. 100,000 per bond following the part redemption. Trading in the bonds will commence with the reduced face value from January 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- Part redemption of APCRDA bonds with 10.32% interest rate (maturity 16-8-26)
- ISIN: INE01E708032, BSE Scrip Code: 958178
- Record date set for February 1, 2026
- Reduced face value: Rs. 100,000 per bond
- Interest payment will be processed along with part redemption
- Trading with reduced face value effective from January 30, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action for bond redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the change in face value for settlement purposes
- Bonds will trade with reduced face value from the effective date
- Settlement reference: DR-809/2025-2026

## Important Dates

- **January 21, 2026**: Circular notice date
- **January 30, 2026**: Effective date for trading with reduced face value (Settlement No. DR-809/2025-2026)
- **February 1, 2026**: Record date for part redemption and interest payment

## Impact Assessment

**Bondholders**: Bondholders on record as of February 1, 2026 will receive partial redemption proceeds and interest payment. The face value reduction to Rs. 100,000 per bond indicates partial principal repayment.

**Market Operations**: Trading members must adjust their systems to reflect the reduced face value from January 30, 2026. This affects settlement and valuation calculations for these bonds.

**Liquidity**: No impact on trading operations; bonds continue to trade with adjusted face value reflecting the part redemption.
