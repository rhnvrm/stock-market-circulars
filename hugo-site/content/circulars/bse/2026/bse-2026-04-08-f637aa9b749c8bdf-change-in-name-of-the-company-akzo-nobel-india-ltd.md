## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (Notice No. 20260408-43) informing trading members that Akzo Nobel India Ltd (Scrip Code 500710) has communicated a change in its company name to JSW Dulux Limited. The SCRIP ID and abbreviated name on the BOLT Plus System will be updated accordingly effective April 15, 2026.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code 500710 will continue to remain the same
- Existing SCRIP ID 'AKZOINDIA' will be replaced by 'JSWDULUX' on BOLT Plus System
- Existing abbreviated name will be replaced by 'JSWDULUX' on BOLT Plus System
- The change takes effect from April 15, 2026
- Notice issued by Marian D'Souza, Assistant Vice President – Listing Compliance & Operations

## Regulatory Changes

The company's trading identifiers on the BSE BOLT Plus System will be updated:
- **Old Name:** Akzo Nobel India Ltd
- **New Name:** JSW Dulux Limited
- **Old SCRIP ID:** AKZOINDIA
- **New SCRIP ID:** JSWDULUX
- **New Abbreviated Name:** JSWDULUX

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members and market participants must update their internal systems, trading terminals, watchlists, and order management systems to reflect the new SCRIP ID (JSWDULUX) and abbreviated name for Scrip Code 500710 effective April 15, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** April 8, 2026
- **Effective Date:** April 15, 2026 — SCRIP ID and abbreviated name change on BOLT Plus System

## Impact Assessment

This is an operational change affecting how the stock is identified on BSE's BOLT Plus trading system. Traders, brokers, and automated trading systems referencing the old SCRIP ID 'AKZOINDIA' must update to 'JSWDULUX' before April 15, 2026 to avoid order entry errors. The Scrip Code 500710 remains unchanged, providing continuity for systems using numeric identifiers. The name change reflects a broader corporate rebranding, likely tied to a change in ownership or business partnership involving the JSW Group.
