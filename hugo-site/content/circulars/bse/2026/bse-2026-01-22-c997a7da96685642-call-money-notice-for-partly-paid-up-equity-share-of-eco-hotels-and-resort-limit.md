## Summary

BSE has activated the Call Money Module on its IBBS platform for Eco Hotels and Resort Limited to collect the first call money on partly paid-up equity shares. Trading Members, Self-Certified Syndicate Banks (SCSBs), and intermediaries can submit call money collection details during the 15-day issue period. A total of 1,28,76,808 partly paid-up equity shares require payment of Rs. 3.80 per share.

## Key Points

- First call on partly paid-up equity shares of Eco Hotels and Resort Limited (Symbol: ECOCM)
- Call money amount: Rs. 3.80 per partly paid-up equity share
- Total partly paid-up equity shares: 1,28,76,808 shares
- Market lot: 1 share
- Issue opens: January 27, 2026 (Tuesday)
- Issue closes: February 10, 2026 (Tuesday)
- Bidding hours: 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM daily
- Registrar: Bigshare Services Private Limited
- Process follows SEBI circular dated December 8, 2020 and BSE notice no. 20210104-37 dated January 4, 2021

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The process follows existing SEBI circular dated December 8, 2020 regarding call money collection procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members with 3-in-1 type accounts must submit call money collection details through the IBBS Call Money Module
- Self-Certified Syndicate Banks (SCSBs) must use the IBBS platform to submit collected call money details
- All submissions must be made during the bidding period (10:00 AM to 5:00 PM) within the issue period
- Intermediaries must note the activation of the Call Money Module and facilitate shareholder participation

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** January 22, 2026
- **Issue Opening Date:** January 27, 2026 (Tuesday)
- **Issue Closing Date:** February 10, 2026 (Tuesday)
- **Issue Period:** 15 days
- **Daily Bidding Window:** 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This is a company-specific corporate action affecting only existing shareholders of Eco Hotels and Resort Limited who hold partly paid-up equity shares.

**Operational Impact:** Medium for affected parties - Trading Members, SCSBs, and intermediaries handling this security need to facilitate the call money collection process through the IBBS platform.

**Shareholder Impact:** High for affected shareholders - Existing holders of partly paid-up equity shares must pay Rs. 3.80 per share to complete their payment obligations. Failure to pay may result in forfeiture of shares as per company regulations.
