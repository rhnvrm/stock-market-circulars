## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 5,80,000 new equity shares of Capfin India Limited (Scrip Code: 539198, ISIN: INE960C01013) have been listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Thursday, May 21, 2026. These shares were issued on a preferential basis to Promoters and Non-Promoters at an issue price of Rs. 32.88/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- + premium of Rs. 22.88/-).

## Key Points

- **Company:** Capfin India Limited | Scrip Code: 539198 | ISIN: INE960C01013
- **New Shares Listed:** 5,80,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- face value each
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 32.88/- per share (premium of Rs. 22.88/-)
- **Allotment Basis:** Preferential allotment to Promoters and Non-Promoters
- **Date of Allotment:** April 14, 2026
- **Distribution Numbers:** 45,53,531 to 51,33,530
- **Pari-passu:** New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- **Trading Effective From:** May 21, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notice pursuant to preferential allotment under applicable SEBI and BSE regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their systems to reflect the new securities available for trading from May 21, 2026.
- Lock-in restrictions must be observed as specified; shares under lock-in are not available for trading until the respective lock-in expiry dates.

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Date of Allotment | April 14, 2026 |
| Trading Commencement | May 21, 2026 |
| Lock-in Expiry (2,94,000 shares) | November 30, 2026 |
| Lock-in Expiry (1,70,000 shares) | November 30, 2026 |
| Lock-in Expiry (1,16,000 shares) | November 30, 2027 |

## Impact Assessment

**Lock-in Breakdown:**
- 1,16,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 45,53,531–46,69,530): Locked in until **November 30, 2027** (likely Promoter shares)
- 2,94,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 46,69,531–49,63,530): Locked in until **November 30, 2026**
- 1,70,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 49,63,531–51,33,530): Locked in until **November 30, 2026**

Only a portion of the 5.8 lakh newly listed shares will be immediately tradeable; the majority are subject to lock-in until late 2026 or 2027. The preferential allotment represents a capital raise at Rs. 32.88/- per share. Impact is confined to existing shareholders of Capfin India Limited and traders monitoring this scrip for dilution effects.
