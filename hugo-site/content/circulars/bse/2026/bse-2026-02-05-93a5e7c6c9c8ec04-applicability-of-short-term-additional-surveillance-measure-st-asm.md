## Summary

BSE has revised the list of securities under the Short Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) framework effective February 06, 2026. A total of 25 securities are being newly shortlisted in the ST-ASM framework with 5/15/30 days monitoring. One security (Hit Kit Global Solutions Ltd) will continue in ST-ASM but move to a higher stage, while 8 securities are exiting the ST-ASM framework. The framework applies enhanced surveillance to securities exhibiting unusual price movements or volatility.

## Key Points

- 25 securities newly shortlisted in Short Term 5/15/30 Days ASM Framework
- Effective date: February 06, 2026
- Hit Kit Global Solutions Ltd to move to higher stage ASM while continuing in ST-ASM
- 8 securities moving out of ST-ASM Framework
- List includes both main board and SME scrips (marked with @)
- Some securities marked with asterisk (*) are as per NSE coordination

## Stage Movement

**Moving to Lower Stage:** No securities identified for this category

## Regulatory Changes

The ST-ASM framework applies enhanced surveillance measures including:
- Reduced price bands
- Additional margin requirements
- Enhanced disclosure requirements
- Continuous monitoring for 5/15/30 day periods
- Potential progression to higher surveillance stages based on continued unusual activity

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure compliance with enhanced margin requirements for these securities
- Investors should be aware of reduced price bands and increased surveillance
- Brokers must inform clients about securities under ST-ASM framework
- All trades in these securities subject to additional monitoring and reporting

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** February 06, 2026
- Securities will be subject to ST-ASM framework from market opening on this date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** High - affects trading in 25 securities with enhanced restrictions potentially reducing liquidity and increasing trading costs through higher margins.

**Investor Impact:** Investors holding or trading these securities will face:
- Higher margin requirements
- Restricted price movements
- Reduced liquidity
- Need for enhanced due diligence

**Operational Impact:** Brokers and trading members must update systems to reflect new surveillance parameters and margin requirements for affected securities.

**Risk Management:** The ST-ASM framework is designed to protect investors from excessive volatility and potential market manipulation in securities showing unusual price movements.
