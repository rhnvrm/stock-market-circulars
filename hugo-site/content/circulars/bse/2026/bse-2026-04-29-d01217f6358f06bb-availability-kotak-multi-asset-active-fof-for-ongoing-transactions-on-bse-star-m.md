## Summary

BSE has informed members that the Kotak Multi Asset Active FOF will reopen for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP facility on the BSE StAR MF Platform with effect from April 30, 2026. This follows the earlier Notice No. 20260407-35 dated April 07, 2026.

## Key Points

- Kotak Multi Asset Active FOF reopens for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform from April 30, 2026
- Facility covers subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP
- Six scheme variants are available spanning Regular and Direct Plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- All six variants are classified under the Equity scheme category

| 6 | Kotak Multi Asset Active FOF - Direct Plan - IDCW Reinvestment | INF174KA1ZQ5 |

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notice regarding the resumption of transactions for an existing mutual fund scheme.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members. Members should update their systems to enable transactions in the listed Kotak Multi Asset Active FOF schemes on the BSE StAR MF Platform from April 30, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **April 30, 2026**: Effective date for reopening of Kotak Multi Asset Active FOF for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP on BSE StAR MF Platform
- **April 07, 2026**: Reference notice (Notice No. 20260407-35) that originally communicated the reopening

## Impact Assessment

Low operational impact. This notice affects mutual fund distributors and investors transacting through the BSE StAR MF Platform who wish to invest in or redeem Kotak Multi Asset Active FOF schemes. The reopening restores normal transaction access for this fund across six plan variants.
