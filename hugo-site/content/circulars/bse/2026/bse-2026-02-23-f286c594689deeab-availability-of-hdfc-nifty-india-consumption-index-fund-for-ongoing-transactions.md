## Summary

BSE has notified members that HDFC NIFTY INDIA CONSUMPTION INDEX FUND will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective February 24, 2026. This follows Notice No. 20260203-28 and reinstates full transactional access including Systematic Investment Plans (SIP), Systematic Transfer Plans (STP), and Systematic Withdrawal Plans (SWP).

## Key Points

- HDFC NIFTY INDIA CONSUMPTION INDEX FUND reopens for ongoing transactions from February 24, 2026
- Both Regular Plan and Direct Plan (Growth option) are available
- Full facility restored: subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Platform: BSE StAR MF
- Reference notice: BSE Notice No. 20260203-28

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational reinstatement of transactional availability for the fund on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of BSE StAR MF Platform should update their systems to enable transactions in the following schemes effective February 24, 2026:

| Scheme Name | Category | ISIN |
|---|---|---|
| HDFC NIFTY INDIA CONSUMPTION INDEX FUND - REGULAR PLAN - GROWTH | EQUITY | INF179KC1JZ3 |
| HDFC NIFTY INDIA CONSUMPTION INDEX FUND - DIRECT PLAN - GROWTH | EQUITY | INF179KC1KA4 |

## Important Dates

- **February 24, 2026**: Effective date for reopening of ongoing subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform

## Impact Assessment

Low operational impact. This notice is relevant only to BSE StAR MF Platform participants who facilitate transactions in HDFC mutual fund schemes. Investors in the HDFC NIFTY INDIA CONSUMPTION INDEX FUND will regain access to systematic and one-time transaction facilities through the BSE StAR MF channel. No broader market or regulatory impact anticipated.
