## Summary

BSE has issued a revised schedule for the Offer to Buy (Acquisition Window/Takeover) for OSCAR GLOBAL LTD. The offer allows acquisition of up to 8,58,000 equity shares at Rs. 12.15 per share through the Live OTB Session from January 2, 2026 to January 16, 2026. Trading members and custodian members can participate on behalf of investors holding shares in demat form.

## Key Points

- **Security**: OSCAR GLOBAL LTD (Symbol: OSCARGLO, Scrip Code: 530713)
- **ISIN**: INE473F01010
- **Offer Size**: Up to 8,58,000 equity shares
- **Offer Price**: Rs. 12.15 per share
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10.00
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Opening Date**: Friday, January 2, 2026
- **Closing Date**: Friday, January 16, 2026
- **Bid Offering Period**: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM (January 2-16, 2026)
- **Give-up/Take-up**: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM (January 2-14, 2026)
- **Last Day Give-up**: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM (January 16, 2026)
- **Last Day Take-up**: 9:15 AM to 4:00 PM (January 16, 2026)

## Regulatory Changes

This is a revised schedule for an existing takeover offer. No new regulatory changes are introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members**: Must ensure investor UCC is registered/updated with correct PAN Number and Category in Exchange UCC database
- **Eligible Participants**: Trading members can bid only for investors having shares in demat form
- **Platform Access**: Members must use iBBS platform (Internet: https://ibbs.bseindia.com/ or Leased Line: https://ibbsll.bseindia.com/) and RTRMS for participation
- **Reference Circulars**: Exchange Notices 20150930-1 (September 30, 2015) and 20170203-29 (February 3, 2017) for operational instructions
- **Bidding Categories**: FII, Financial Institutions, Mutual Funds, Insurance Companies, Other QIBs, Non-Institutional Buyers, Companies, and Individuals

## Important Dates

- **Offer Period**: January 2, 2026 to January 16, 2026
- **Give-up/Take-up Period**: January 2, 2026 to January 14, 2026
- **Last Day of Offer**: January 16, 2026
- **Bid Session Timing**: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM (daily)
- **Last Day Take-up Extended Timing**: 9:15 AM to 4:00 PM (January 16, 2026)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a company-specific takeover offer affecting only OSCAR GLOBAL LTD shareholders. The offer size of 8,58,000 shares is relatively small.

**Operational Impact**: Medium for participating members - Trading members and custodians must ensure proper UCC registration and use designated platforms (iBBS/RTRMS) for bid processing.

**Investor Impact**: Medium for OSCAR GLOBAL LTD shareholders - Investors holding shares in demat form can participate through their trading members to tender shares at the offer price of Rs. 12.15 per share.
