## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of three new Treasury Bills (T-Bills) on the Exchange under the G GROUP - DEBT INSTRUMENTS segment of the Debt Market, effective February 23, 2026. The bills include 91-day, 182-day, and 364-day tenors maturing in May 2026, August 2026, and February 2027 respectively.

## Key Points

- Three T-Bills to be listed effective February 23, 2026 under G GROUP - DEBT INSTRUMENTS
- Scrip 805181 (ISIN: IN002025X463): 91-day T-Bill, symbol 91TB220526, matures May 22, 2026
- Scrip 805182 (ISIN: IN002025Y461): 182-day T-Bill, symbol 182TB21826, matures August 21, 2026
- Scrip 805183 (ISIN: IN002025Z468): 364-day T-Bill, symbol 364TB19227, matures February 19, 2027
- Market lot for all three securities is 1
- Trading will be suspended two working days prior to each security's maturity/redemption date (T minus 2 trading days, excluding bank holidays)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice for newly issued government T-Bills on the BSE Debt Market segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the trading suspension rule: these securities will not be available for trading two working days before their respective maturity/redemption dates
- Members requiring clarification may contact BSE at 2272 8352 / 5753 / 8597

## Important Dates

- **Listing effective date:** February 23, 2026
- **91TB220526 maturity:** May 22, 2026 (trading halts ~May 20, 2026)
- **182TB21826 maturity:** August 21, 2026 (trading halts ~August 19, 2026)
- **364TB19227 maturity:** February 19, 2027 (trading halts ~February 17, 2027)
- **Notice date:** February 20, 2026 (issued by Hardik Bhuta, AVP, dated February 12, 2026)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine administrative listing of sovereign government securities (Treasury Bills) on the BSE debt platform. These instruments carry no credit risk and are standard short-term government borrowing instruments. The listing provides trading members with access to three additional liquid government securities across different maturity profiles (91, 182, and 364 days).
