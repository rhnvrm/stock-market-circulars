## Summary

GROWW Mutual Fund is launching GROWW SMALL CAP FUND as a New Fund Offer (NFO) with SIP facility on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The NFO period runs from January 08, 2026 to January 22, 2026, with six plan variants available under both Regular and Direct plans. The tentative allotment date is January 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- NFO period: January 08, 2026 to January 22, 2026
- Six scheme variants available: Regular Plan (Growth, IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment) and Direct Plan (Growth, IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment)
- All schemes categorized as EQUITY under Small Cap Fund category
- Tentative allotment date: January 30, 2026
- Extended trading available till midnight on January 22, 2026 for lumpsum purchases only
- Transactions without client fund initiation by 9:00 AM on T+1 will be cancelled
- Clear funds must be available in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on January 27, 2026
- Cheques and OTM payments accepted subject to clear funds receipt by deadline
- Switches accepted during NFO period subject to funds receipt before allotment date
- SIP/XSIP registration authentication required before 3:00 PM on NFO closure date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a new product launch announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure transactions for which clients have not initiated funds till 9:00 AM on T+1 day are cancelled with CC flagged with N flag reported to RTA/AMC
- Members must ensure clear funds are available in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on January 27, 2026 for allotment
- Members must complete SIP/XSIP registration authentication by 3:00 PM on January 22, 2026
- Pending transactions remain in system till 3:00 PM on last day of fund payout to AMC or T+5, whichever is earlier

## Important Dates

- **January 08, 2026**: NFO opening date
- **January 22, 2026**: NFO closing date (3:00 PM for regular transactions, midnight for extended lumpsum purchases)
- **January 22, 2026 by 3:00 PM**: SIP/XSIP registration authentication deadline
- **January 27, 2026 by 6:00 PM**: Clear funds deadline in ICCL Account
- **January 30, 2026**: Tentative allotment date

## Impact Assessment

Low impact announcement. This is a routine NFO launch that provides BSE StAR MF Platform members with a new investment product offering from GROWW Mutual Fund. The operational impact is limited to members who choose to participate in distributing this NFO to their clients. Standard transaction processing timelines and fund settlement procedures apply. No changes to existing market operations or trading rules.
