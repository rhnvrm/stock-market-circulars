## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the equity shares of Gold Coin Health Foods Ltd. will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange effective Friday, July 03, 2026, under the XT Group of Securities. Pursuant to SEBI circulars dated January 20, 2012 and April 11, 2023, the scrip will trade in the Trade-for-Trade segment for 10 trading days and will be part of the special pre-open session applicable to IPO and other specified categories of scrips. The listing follows a Scheme of Capital Reduction approved by the NCLT, Ahmedabad, vide order dated October 31, 2025.

## Key Points

- Equity shares of Gold Coin Health Foods Ltd. to be listed and admitted to dealings from July 03, 2026, in the XT Group of Securities.
- Scrip will be under Trade-for-Trade segment for 10 trading days.
- Scrip will be part of the special pre-open session for IPO and Other category scrips.
- Registered Office: A/16, 1st Floor, Vardan Tower, Pragatinagar, Naranpura, Ahmedabad, Gujarat, 380013.
- Securities: 15,00,450 fully paid-up equity shares of Rs. 10/- each (distinctive numbers 1 to 15,00,450).
- Scrip Code: 538542; Scrip ID on BOLT: GOLDCOINHF; ISIN: INE634J01027; Market Lot: 1.
- No lock-in applicable to the shares.
- Capital reduction carried out in two stages: face value reduced from Rs. 10/- to Rs. 5/- per share, then 2 shares of Rs. 5/- each consolidated into 1 share of Rs. 10/- each.
- Share capital changed from Rs. 1,50,04,500/- (30,00,900 shares of Rs. 5/- each) to 15,00,450 shares of Rs. 10/- each.

## Regulatory Changes

None directly; this is an implementation notice reflecting a previously NCLT-approved Scheme of Capital Reduction and applying existing SEBI circulars (CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012 and SEBI/HO/MRD-TPD1/CIR/P/2023/55 dated April 11, 2023) governing Trade-for-Trade and special pre-open session norms for newly listed/restructured scrips.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must recognize Gold Coin Health Foods Ltd. shares (Scrip Code 538542, ISIN INE634J01027) as newly listed in the XT Group from July 03, 2026, apply Trade-for-Trade settlement for the first 10 trading days, and route orders through the special pre-open session applicable to IPO/other category scrips as per the cited SEBI circulars.

## Important Dates

- October 31, 2025: NCLT Ahmedabad approved the Scheme of Capital Reduction.
- July 01, 2026: Notice date.
- July 03, 2026 (Friday): Effective date of listing and admission to dealings; start of Trade-for-Trade period (10 trading days).

## Impact Assessment

The notice is procedural and primarily affects trading members and existing shareholders of Gold Coin Health Foods Ltd. The Trade-for-Trade restriction for 10 days will limit intraday trading and require delivery-based settlement, potentially reducing short-term liquidity. The capital reduction (2:1 share consolidation with face value change) alters the per-share metrics but does not affect overall shareholder value proportionately. Market impact is expected to be limited given the small equity base (15,00,450 shares).
