## Summary

BSE Limited, through Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd. (ICCL), invites offers from trading members for the purchase of securities in Demat Auction 658 pertaining to Rolling Settlement No. DR-658/2026-27. The auction session is scheduled for June 25, 2026 with pay-in due on June 29, 2026.

## Key Points

- Auction offer entry window: 3:15 PM to 4:00 PM on June 25, 2026
- Auction matching: 4:00 PM to 4:15 PM
- Delivery statement and accepted auction offer reports: 4:15 PM to 4:45 PM
- Money statement and final reports: 4:50 PM to 6:45 PM
- Securities pay-in for auction: Monday, June 29, 2026 at 8:00 AM
- No corrections permitted after punching errors; the Exchange may accept or reject offers without assigning reasons
- Trading members must refer to the cutoff rate in the Auction Offer Entry Screen before submitting offers

## Regulatory Changes

Following implementation of the Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment, settlement is now processed through the Clearing Member of a Trading Member. The restriction previously preventing trading members from offering securities in auction for settlements in which they had short delivered has been removed.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and Custodians must check their auction pay-in obligations for securities and execute securities pay-in in depositories within depository cut-off timings.
- Trading members must exercise utmost care while entering securities codes and rates, as no corrections will be permitted post-submission.
- Members should refer to notices issued by the respective department regarding Corporate Actions (Ex-Rights, Ex-Bonus, Ex-Split, etc.) for relevant scrips.

## Important Dates

- **June 25, 2026**: Auction offer entry session (3:15 PM–4:00 PM); auction matching and reports through 6:45 PM
- **June 29, 2026**: Auction pay-in of securities at 8:00 AM

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice for trading and clearing members participating in BSE's demat auction process. It has no broad market impact and affects only members with short delivery obligations under Rolling Settlement DR-658/2026-27. The removal of the restriction on offering securities in auction for settlements with short delivery is a procedural update aligned with the Uniform Membership structure already in effect.
