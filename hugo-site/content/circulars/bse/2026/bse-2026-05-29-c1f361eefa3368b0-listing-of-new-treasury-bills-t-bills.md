## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that three new Treasury Bills (T-Bills) will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange under the G Group – Debt Instruments segment with effect from June 1, 2026. The issuances cover 91-day, 182-day, and 364-day tenors.

## Key Points

- Three T-Bills are being listed effective June 1, 2026 in the G Group – Debt Instruments segment.
- The T-Bills span three tenors: 91-day (matures August 28, 2026), 182-day (matures November 27, 2026), and 364-day (matures May 28, 2027).
- Trading in each security will be suspended two working days prior to the respective maturity/redemption date (i.e., Maturity Date minus two trading days, excluding bank holidays).
- Market lot for all three T-Bills is 1.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification under existing debt instrument listing norms.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must ensure they do not trade in these securities during the blackout period of two working days prior to the maturity/redemption date. For clarifications, members may contact BSE at 2272 8352 / 5753 / 8597.

## Important Dates

- **Listing effective date:** June 1, 2026
- **91-day T-Bill (805220 / 91TB28826) maturity:** August 28, 2026
- **182-day T-Bill (805221 / 182T271126) maturity:** November 27, 2026
- **364-day T-Bill (805222 / 364TB28527) maturity:** May 28, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard periodic listing of government-issued Treasury Bills. Debt segment participants and gilt traders will gain access to three new short-to-medium-term sovereign instruments from June 1, 2026. No change in trading rules or margin requirements is indicated.
