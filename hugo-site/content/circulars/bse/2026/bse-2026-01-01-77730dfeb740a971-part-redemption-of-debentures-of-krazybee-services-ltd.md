## Summary

KRAZYBEE SERVICES LTD has announced part redemption of its Non-Convertible Debentures (ISIN: INE07HK07775, Code: KSL-12.10%-20-10-26-PVT, Scrip Code: 976114) with a record date of January 5, 2026. Trading in these debentures will be conducted with a reduced face value effective from the same date.

## Key Points

- Company: KRAZYBEE SERVICES LTD
- ISIN: INE07HK07775
- Debenture Code: KSL-12.10%-20-10-26-PVT (976114)
- Purpose: Part redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Reduced face value: Rs. 37,500 per debenture
- Trading with reduced face value commences from effective date
- Settlement number: DR-791/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must:
- Note that trading in the company's debentures will be conducted with the reduced face value of Rs. 37,500 per debenture from the effective date
- Ensure systems reflect the reduced face value from January 5, 2026
- Process trades under settlement number DR-791/2025-2026

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 1, 2026
- **Record Date**: January 5, 2026
- **Effective Date for Reduced Face Value**: January 5, 2026
- **Settlement Number**: DR-791/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular affects only holders of KRAZYBEE SERVICES LTD debentures (INE07HK07775). The part redemption will result in a reduced face value of Rs. 37,500 per debenture. Trading members dealing in these specific debentures need to update their systems to reflect the new face value from January 5, 2026. The impact is limited to debenture holders and does not affect broader equity markets or other securities.
