## Summary

BSE has listed and permitted trading of 12,56,19,642 new equity shares of Enbee Trade & Finance Ltd. (Scrip Code: 512441) issued at par (Re.1/- each) on a rights basis. These shares are available for trading on the Exchange from Thursday, April 16, 2026.

## Key Points

- 12,56,19,642 equity shares of Re.1/- each issued at par on Rights Basis
- ISIN: INE993I01029
- Distribution Numbers: 571666671 to 697286312
- Date of Allotment: April 13, 2026
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Notice issued by Listing Operations Department

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing admission notice under BSE's Listing Operations for newly allotted rights issue shares.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed to note the availability of these new securities for trading. No specific compliance action is required from members beyond acknowledging the new securities in their trading systems.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** April 13, 2026
- **Trading Effective Date:** April 16, 2026 (Thursday)
- **Notice Date:** April 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This listing increases the total equity share capital of Enbee Trade & Finance Ltd. (Scrip Code: 512441). The new shares are pari-passu with existing shares, meaning they carry identical rights and entitlements. The rights issue expands the float for this scrip, which may have a modest dilutive impact on existing shareholders. Impact is limited to investors and trading members dealing in this specific scrip.
