## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260623-23 informs trading members that price bands have been revised for 30 equity scrips effective June 24, 2026. The revised bands range from 2% to 10%, tightening or adjusting trading limits for each listed scrip.

## Key Points

- Price band revisions are effective from **June 24, 2026**.
- 30 scrips are affected across sectors including finance, manufacturing, technology, and entertainment.
- Revised price bands are set at **2%**, **5%**, or **10%** depending on the scrip.
- Seven scrips receive the most restrictive 2% band: Aksh Optifibre Ltd, Mega Corporation Ltd, Sadhana Nitro Chem Ltd, Sancode Technologies Ltd, SGL Resources Ltd, Shyam Telecom Ltd.
- Five scrips are revised to 10%: Blue Cloud Softech Solutions Ltd, EMS Ltd, Invigorated Business Consulting Ltd, Prabhhans Industries Ltd, Sahara Maritime Ltd.
- The remaining scrips are revised to a 5% band.

## Regulatory Changes

The Department of Surveillance (DOSS) has administratively revised the daily price bands for the listed scrips. Price bands define the maximum permissible intraday price movement (upper and lower circuit limits) for a security.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must take note of the revised price band limits and update their systems accordingly before the start of trading on June 24, 2026.
- Orders placed outside the revised band will be rejected by the exchange's trading system.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 23, 2026
- **Effective Date:** June 24, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The revision of price bands to 2% for several scrips (Aksh Optifibre, Mega Corporation, Sadhana Nitro Chem, Sancode Technologies, SGL Resources, Shyam Telecom) represents a tightening of trading limits, typically applied as a surveillance measure to reduce volatility or curb speculative activity. Scrips moved to 10% bands may be experiencing increased regulatory comfort or relaxed surveillance. Traders and investors holding positions in these scrips should be aware of the revised daily movement ceilings effective June 24, 2026.
