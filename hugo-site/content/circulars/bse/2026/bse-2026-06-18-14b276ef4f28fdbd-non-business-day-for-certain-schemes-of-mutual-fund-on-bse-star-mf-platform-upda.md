## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing members that certain mutual fund schemes will observe Non-Business Days (NBD) during June 2026 on the BSE StAR MF platform. The exchange received communication from mutual funds confirming market closures for specific schemes on these dates.

## Key Points

- Certain mutual fund schemes will have Non-Business Days in June 2026 on the BSE StAR MF platform
- Communication received from multiple mutual funds regarding market closures for specific schemes
- A detailed list of affected schemes and NBD dates is provided in the attached annexure
- Notice issued by VP - Mutual Fund (Arunkumar Ganesan) and AVP - Mutual Fund (Mahesh Tare)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an operational update communicating Non-Business Day schedules as received from mutual fund houses for June 2026.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must not process the following transaction types for affected schemes on their designated Non-Business Days via BSE StAR MF:
  - Subscription
  - Redemption
  - Switch
  - SIP (Systematic Investment Plan)
  - Normal STP (Systematic Transfer Plan)
  - SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan)
  - AMC-STP
- Members should refer to the attached annexure for the complete list of affected schemes and their respective NBD dates

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 18 June 2026
- Applicable Period: June 2026 (specific dates per scheme as listed in annexure)

## Impact Assessment

Investors and distributors using the BSE StAR MF platform for the affected schemes will not be able to execute transactions on the designated Non-Business Days. This is a routine operational matter with limited broader market impact, affecting only specific schemes on specific dates. Members should consult the annexure to identify affected schemes and plan transactions accordingly to avoid disruption to investor servicing.
