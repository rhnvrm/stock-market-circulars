## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that several companies have fixed record dates for redemption of their Commercial Paper (CP). Trading members are advised not to deal in the CP instruments of these companies from the specified record/ex dates, as the instruments are being redeemed.

## Key Points

- The notice lists five Commercial Paper instruments across three issuers set for redemption.
- Ashoka Buildcon Limited (Scrip ID: ABL-14-7-26-CP, ISIN: INE442H14535, Scrip Code: 731487) - Record/Ex Date: 08/07/2026, Settlement DR: 667/2026-2027.
- Infina Finance Private Limited (Scrip ID: IFPL-13-7-26-CP, ISIN: INE879F14MU4, Scrip Code: 731462) - Record/Ex Date: 10/07/2026, Settlement DR: 669/2026-2027.
- Trust Investment Advisors Private Limited (Scrip ID: TIAPL-15-7-26-CP, ISIN: INE723X14MH4, Scrip Code: 731426) - Record/Ex Date: 14/07/2026, Settlement DR: 671/2026-2027.
- Infina Finance Private Limited (Scrip ID: IFPL-17-7-26-CP, ISIN: INE879F14MV2, Scrip Code: 731503) - Record/Ex Date: 16/07/2026, Settlement DR: 673/2026-2027.
- Infina Finance Private Limited (Scrip ID: IFPL-23-7-26-CP, ISIN: INE879F14MW0, Scrip Code: 731546) - Record/Ex Date: 22/07/2026, Settlement DR: 677/2026-2027.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a routine operational notice issued under BSE's Listing Operations department pertaining to corporate actions in the debt segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must not deal in the listed Commercial Paper instruments of the named companies from their respective record/ex dates onward, as these are being redeemed.
- Depository participants and settlement teams should ensure positions in the affected ISINs are reconciled ahead of the record dates for smooth redemption processing.

## Important Dates

- 08 July 2026: Record/Ex Date for Ashoka Buildcon Limited CP (INE442H14535).
- 10 July 2026: Record/Ex Date for Infina Finance Private Limited CP (INE879F14MU4).
- 14 July 2026: Record/Ex Date for Trust Investment Advisors Private Limited CP (INE723X14MH4).
- 16 July 2026: Record/Ex Date for Infina Finance Private Limited CP (INE879F14MV2).
- 22 July 2026: Record/Ex Date for Infina Finance Private Limited CP (INE879F14MW0).

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt-market administrative notice affecting Commercial Paper redemptions for three non-listed-equity issuers. It has no bearing on equity trading or listed stock prices and is primarily relevant to institutional debt market participants and trading members who deal in these specific CP instruments.
