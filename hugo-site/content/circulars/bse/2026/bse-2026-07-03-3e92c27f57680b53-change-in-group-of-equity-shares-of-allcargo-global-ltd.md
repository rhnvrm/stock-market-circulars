## Summary

BSE has notified market participants that the equity shares of Allcargo Global Ltd (BSE Scrip code: 544602) will be permitted for trading under the 'B' group with effect from Friday, July 17, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: Allcargo Global Ltd
- BSE Scrip Code: 544602
- New trading group: 'B' group
- Effective date: Friday, July 17, 2026
- No attachments accompanied this notice

## Regulatory Changes

This is a group reclassification under BSE's Listing Operations department, moving the scrip's trading group designation to 'B' group. Such reclassifications typically reflect changes in trading eligibility criteria such as market capitalization, trading volume, or compliance status.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action is required from investors or the company. Trading members and market participants should update their systems to reflect the new group classification for this scrip from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 3, 2026
- Effective Date of Group Change: Friday, July 17, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative reclassification affecting trading group designation for Allcargo Global Ltd shares. The change may affect trading rules such as circuit filters and settlement mechanisms applicable to the 'B' group, but is not expected to have a broader market impact beyond investors holding or trading this specific stock.
