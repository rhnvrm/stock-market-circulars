## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the commercial paper issued by Solar Defence and Aerospace Limited (Scrip Code: 730672, ISIN: INE02AL14023) under the identifier SDAL-26-2-26-CP will mature on February 25, 2026. Trading in this instrument is suspended effective February 25, 2026, as per notice DR-828/2025-2026.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of Solar Defence and Aerospace Limited (Scrip Code: 730672) is set to mature on February 25, 2026
- ISIN: INE02AL14023; Instrument: SDAL-26-2-26-CP
- Trading members are advised not to deal in this commercial paper from February 25, 2026 onwards
- Notice issued under reference DR-828/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational notice issued upon the scheduled redemption/maturity of a commercial paper instrument in the Debt segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in Scrip Code 730672 (SDAL-26-2-26-CP, ISIN: INE02AL14023) effective February 25, 2026
- Members should update their systems to reflect the suspension and ensure no new trades are executed in this instrument on or after the maturity date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** February 24, 2026
- **Maturity / Redemption Date:** February 25, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Effective:** February 25, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited and routine. This suspension affects only the single commercial paper instrument (SDAL-26-2-26-CP) of Solar Defence and Aerospace Limited in the BSE Debt segment. As the instrument is maturing as scheduled, this is a standard end-of-life action with no adverse implications for investors beyond the cessation of secondary market trading. Holders will receive redemption proceeds as per the terms of the instrument.
