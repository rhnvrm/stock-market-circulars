## Summary

BSE has listed 1,74,11,380 new equity shares of INNOVASSYNTH TECHNOLOGIES (INDIA) LIMITED (Scrip Code: 533315, ISIN: INE690J01011) issued on a rights basis. These shares are available for trading on the Exchange with effect from Friday, May 22, 2026.

## Key Points

- 1,74,11,380 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 30/- per share on a rights basis
- ISIN: INE690J01011
- Distribution Numbers: 75449317 to 92860696
- Date of Allotment: 19 May 2026
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular is a standard listing notification for newly issued securities following a rights offering.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed that the new securities are listed and permitted to trade. No additional compliance action is required beyond normal trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** 19 May 2026
- **Trading Commencement Date:** 22 May 2026 (Friday)

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 1,74,11,380 new equity shares to the tradable pool for INNOVASSYNTH TECHNOLOGIES (INDIA) LIMITED, increasing the company's total listed share capital. Existing shareholders who participated in the rights issue can now trade their allotted shares. The shares were issued at Rs. 40/- per share (Rs. 10 face value + Rs. 30 premium). Market impact is limited to the stock's liquidity and float adjustment.
