## Summary

BSE has announced the schedule for the Offer to Buy (Acquisition Window - Takeover) for shares of HARMONY CAPITAL SERVICES LIMITED (Scrip Code: 530055, Symbol: HRMNYCP). The offer will run from February 11, 2026 to February 25, 2026, targeting up to 31,52,994 equity shares at an offer price of Rs. 10.00 per share (matching the face value).

## Key Points

- **Security**: HARMONY CAPITAL SERVICES LIMITED (ISIN: INE264N01017)
- **Offer Size**: Up to 31,52,994 equity shares
- **Offer Price**: Rs. 10.00 per share
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10.00
- **Market Lot**: 1 share
- **Offer Period**: February 11-25, 2026
- **Bidding Platform**: iBBS and RTRMS accessible via internet and leased line
- **Eligible Investors**: Multiple categories including FII, FI, MF, IC, Other QIBs, Companies, and Individuals
- **Trading Members can only bid for investors holding shares in demat form**

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This circular provides operational details for a specific takeover offer in accordance with existing BSE procedures for Offer to Buy activities.

## Compliance Requirements

**For Trading Members and Custodian Members:**

1. Ensure investor UCC is registered/updated with correct PAN Number and Category in Exchange UCC database
2. Only bid for investors having shares in demat form
3. Follow operational instructions per Exchange Notices 20150930-1 (September 30, 2015) and 20170203-29 (February 3, 2017)
4. Access OTB module through designated URLs:
   - Internet: https://ibbs.bseindia.com/ or https://rtrms.bseindia.com/
   - Leased Line: https://ibbsll.bseindia.com/ or https://rtrmsll.bseindia.com/
5. Note: If no securities are accepted, no End of Issue files will be generated in Extranet

**Investor Categories for Bidding:**
- Foreign Institutional Buyer (FII)
- Financial Institution (FI)
- Mutual Fund (MF)
- Insurance Companies (IC)
- Other QIBs (OTH)
- Other NIB (NOH)
- Company (CO)
- Individual (IND)

## Important Dates

- **Offer Opening Date**: Wednesday, February 11, 2026
- **Offer Closing Date**: Wednesday, February 25, 2026
- **Bid Offering Period**: February 11-25, 2026 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Give-up/Take-up Period**: February 11-24, 2026 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Give-up (Last Day)**: February 25, 2026 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Take-up (Last Day)**: February 25, 2026 (9:15 AM - 4:00 PM)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a company-specific takeover offer affecting only shareholders of Harmony Capital Services Limited.

**Operational Impact**: Medium for participating brokers - Trading members and custodians need to ensure their systems and processes are ready for the offer period, including proper UCC registration and access to iBBS/RTRMS platforms.

**Investor Impact**: High for Harmony Capital shareholders - Existing shareholders have the opportunity to tender their shares at Rs. 10.00 per share during the 15-day offer window.

**Contact Support**: BSE has provided comprehensive contact details for technical support (iBBS login, password reset, RTRMS access) and operational queries through Trading Operations and Listing Sales teams.
