## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Electronic Gold Receipts (EGR) segment on Saturday, July 04, 2026. The session allows trading members, including those using third-party trading platforms or in-house systems via IML/ETI APIs, to test their trading applications for various functionalities including risk-reduction mode and trading halts. The new BOLT Pro TWS version 13.11 will also be released, with details to follow in a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for EGR segment on Saturday, July 04, 2026
- Members may participate in Mock Trading Session or UAT environment to fulfill regulatory testing requirements per SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD1/DSAP/CIR/P/2020/234 dated November 24, 2020
- Members using third-party or in-house trading systems via IML/ETI APIs can test functionalities including risk-reduction mode and trading halt
- New BOLT Pro TWS version 13.11 will be released, with details in a separate circular
- Trades from mock trading do not attract margin obligations or pay-in/pay-out obligations and create no rights or liabilities
- Members are requested to submit feedback on the mock trading session by 4:30 pm

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine operational notice referencing existing SEBI guidelines on software testing (SEBI circular no. SEBI/HO/MRD1/DSAP/CIR/P/2020/234 dated November 24, 2020) and BSE Notice No. 20201201-22 dated December 1, 2020.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should actively participate in the mock trading session to fulfill regulatory testing requirements for software used in trading and risk management
- Members should provide feedback on the mock trading session by 4:30 pm on the day of the session
- For queries, members may contact their Relationship Manager, BSE Tech Support (022-22728053; bse.tech@bseindia.com), or BSE Help Desk (022-45720400/600, 022-69158500; bsehelp@bseindia.com)

## Important Dates

- **Saturday, July 04, 2026**: Mock trading session for EGR segment
  - Log-in: 10:15 AM – 11:00 AM
  - Pre-Open Order Entry: 11:00 AM – 11:08 AM
  - Pre-Open Matching: 11:08 AM – 11:15 AM
  - Continuous Trading: 11:15 AM – 3:30 PM
  - Block Deal Window: 12:00 PM – 12:15 PM
  - Closing: 3:30 PM – 3:40 PM
  - Post-Closing: 3:40 PM – 3:50 PM
  - Feedback deadline: 4:30 PM

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, low-impact operational notice with no effect on live trading, listed securities, or market participants outside of the EGR trading member community. The mock session is purely for testing and familiarization purposes; no real financial obligations, margin requirements, or settlement obligations arise from it.
