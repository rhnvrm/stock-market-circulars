## Summary

BSE has published the list of Registrar and Transfer Agents (RTAs) scheduled to visit BSE Ltd. in January 2026. The circular provides details of companies serviced by Adroit Corporate Services Pvt. Ltd., enabling investors to access shareholder services during the RTA's visit to the exchange.

## Key Points

- Adroit Corporate Services Pvt. Ltd. is the featured RTA for January 2026
- The list includes 51+ companies serviced by Adroit Corporate
- Companies span multiple sectors including finance, manufacturing, real estate, and technology
- Investors can access RTA services at BSE premises during the scheduled visit
- Notable companies include Hind Rectifiers, LKP Finance, Safari Industries, and Securekloud Technologies

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance requirements. This is an informational circular for investor convenience.

**Investor Impact**: Low - provides convenient access to RTA services for shareholders of listed companies serviced by Adroit Corporate. Investors can resolve share transfer, dividend, and other shareholder-related queries during the RTA's visit.

**Market Impact**: None - purely operational/informational in nature with no effect on trading, pricing, or market operations.

**Operational Impact**: Facilitates better investor servicing by making RTAs accessible at the exchange premises.
