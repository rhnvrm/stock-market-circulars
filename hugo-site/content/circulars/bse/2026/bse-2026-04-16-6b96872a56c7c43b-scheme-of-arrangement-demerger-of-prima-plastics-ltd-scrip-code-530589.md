## Summary

BSE has issued a continuation notice (following Exchange Notice No. 20260413-53 dated 13 April 2026) regarding the Scheme of Arrangement (Demerger) of Prima Plastics Ltd. (Scrip Code: 530589). The key update clarifies that the company's equity shares will be transferred from the 'X' Group to the 'XT' Group (not the 'T' Group as may have been previously understood) with effect from 17th April 2026, which is also the Record Date fixed for the demerger.

## Key Points

- This notice is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20260413-53 dated 13 April 2026.
- Record Date for the demerger is 17th April 2026.
- Equity shares of Prima Plastics Ltd. (Scrip Code: 530589) will be transferred from 'X' Group to 'XT' Group effective 17 April 2026.
- The transfer is to 'XT' Group, not 'T' Group — this is a correction/clarification from the earlier notice.
- All other contents of the earlier notice remain unchanged.
- Issued pursuant to SEBI Circular No. SEBI/Cir/ISD/1/2010 dated November 2, 2010.

## Regulatory Changes

Pursuant to SEBI Circular No. SEBI/Cir/ISD/1/2010 dated 2 November 2010, equity shares of companies undergoing schemes of arrangement are moved to a special trading group. In this case, Prima Plastics Ltd. shares are being moved to the 'XT' Group, which is the designated group for such corporate action scenarios, correcting an earlier implication that shares would move to the 'T' Group.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the group reclassification from 'X' to 'XT' for Prima Plastics Ltd. shares effective 17 April 2026.
- Trading members should update their systems and inform clients accordingly regarding the group change.
- No attachment is required from trading members; this is an informational notice.

## Important Dates

- **13 April 2026**: Original Exchange Notice No. 20260413-53 issued, announcing record date and demerger details.
- **16 April 2026**: Current notice issued clarifying group transfer to 'XT' (not 'T').
- **17 April 2026**: Record Date for the demerger; effective date of group transfer from 'X' to 'XT'.

## Impact Assessment

This notice primarily impacts trading members and investors holding equity shares of Prima Plastics Ltd. (Scrip Code: 530589). The reclassification to the 'XT' Group signals an active corporate restructuring event and may affect liquidity and trading patterns temporarily. The correction from 'T' to 'XT' Group is operationally important for trading systems and risk frameworks that treat these groups differently. Shareholders should be aware of the record date of 17 April 2026 for eligibility in the demerger benefits.
