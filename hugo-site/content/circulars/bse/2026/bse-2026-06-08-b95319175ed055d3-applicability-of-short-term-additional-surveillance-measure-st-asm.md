## Summary

BSE has notified the inclusion of securities (listed in Annexure I) under Short Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) Stage I and Stage II, effective June 10, 2026. Securities under Stage I will attract a minimum 50% margin, while those under Stage II will attract a minimum 100% margin, applicable on both open positions as of June 9, 2026, and new positions created from June 10, 2026.

## Key Points

- Securities in Annexure I satisfying Stage I criteria: applicable margin shall be 50% or existing margin, whichever is higher (capped at 100%).
- Securities in Annexure I satisfying Stage II criteria: applicable margin shall be 100% or existing margin, whichever is higher (capped at 100%).
- ST-ASM operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by exchanges.
- Shortlisting under ASM is purely for market surveillance purposes and does not constitute an adverse action against the concerned company.
- Price band for scrips exiting the framework will be reinstated to the pre-ASM price band, unless the scrip remains under another surveillance measure.

## Regulatory Changes

This notice updates the ASM framework in reference to prior notices: 20240920-71 (Sep 20, 2024), 20181027-1 (Oct 27, 2018), 20190719-33 (Jul 19, 2019), 20201204-56 (Dec 4, 2020), 20230925-49 (Sep 25, 2023), and 20240920-63 (Sep 20, 2024). Securities meeting defined criteria for abnormal price/volume movement are placed under staged surveillance with escalating margin requirements.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure the applicable enhanced margin (50% for Stage I, 100% for Stage II) is collected on all open positions as of June 9, 2026, and on all new positions from June 10, 2026.
- Members must monitor the attached Annexure I for the list of affected securities.
- For clarifications, members may contact BSE at bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **June 9, 2026**: Reference date for open positions subject to enhanced margin.
- **June 10, 2026**: Effective date for enhanced margin requirements under ST-ASM Stage I and Stage II.

## Impact Assessment

Trading members and investors holding positions in the securities listed in Annexure I will face significantly increased margin requirements, potentially forcing reduction of open positions or infusion of additional capital. Stage II securities (100% margin) are especially restrictive, effectively requiring full cash backing for any position. This can reduce liquidity and increase trading costs for the affected scrips. The measure is temporary and surveillance-driven, with price bands reverting upon exit from the framework provided no other surveillance measure applies.
