## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members that the commercial paper of L&T Metro Rail (Hyderabad) Limited (Scrip Code: 731266, ISIN: INE128M14BD1) will mature on its Redemption Date. Trading in this instrument is suspended with effect from May 29, 2026.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper instrument: LTMRHL-01-06-26-CP (ISIN: INE128M14BD1)
- Scrip Code: 731266
- Issuer: L&T Metro Rail (Hyderabad) Limited
- Suspension effective from: May 29, 2026
- Reference: DR-640/2026-2027

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural notice triggered by the upcoming maturity of the commercial paper.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are advised not to deal in the aforesaid commercial paper of L&T Metro Rail (Hyderabad) Limited on or after May 29, 2026.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: May 27, 2026
- Suspension Effective Date: May 29, 2026 (Redemption Date)

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited to holders and traders of this specific commercial paper instrument. The suspension is a routine operational action aligned with the instrument's maturity, and no broader market or systemic impact is expected.
