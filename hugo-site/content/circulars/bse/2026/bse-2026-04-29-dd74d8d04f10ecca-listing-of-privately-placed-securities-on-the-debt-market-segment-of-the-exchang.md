## Summary

BSE has listed privately placed debt securities of Lucina Land Development Limited on the Debt Market Segment, effective April 29, 2026. The securities are admitted to trading under scrip code 977440 and ISIN INE0JZO07040, and will trade exclusively in dematerialised form.

## Key Points

- New debt securities of Lucina Land Development Limited are listed and admitted to trading on BSE Debt Segment effective April 29, 2026
- Securities issued on a private placement basis (further listing)
- Trading is permitted only in dematerialised form
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx
- Contact for clarifications: BSE Debt Department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine further listing notice under existing BSE Debt Segment framework for privately placed debt instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under the specified ISIN
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum on BSE website for further details

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** April 28, 2026
- **Listing Effective Date:** April 29, 2026
- **Interest Payment:** Monthly, from January 31, 2026 to January 30, 2029
- **Date of Redemption:** January 30, 2029

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative listing notice with minimal market impact. The securities are restricted to dematerialised trading and were issued via private placement, limiting broad retail market exposure. Key instrument details:
