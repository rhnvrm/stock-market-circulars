## Summary

BSE, as part of its surveillance measures and in line with the SEBI framework, has revised the list of scrips under the Trade-to-Trade (T2T) settlement segment. Securities meeting all three prescribed criteria (I, II, and III) listed in Annexure I will be transferred to the T/XT/MT/TS group effective June 11, 2026. Securities in Annexure II will continue in existing groups (T/XT/MT/TS/P/Z/ZP) but will be subject to a 5% price band.

## Key Points

- Securities satisfying Criteria I, II, and III (Annexure I) will be moved to T/XT/MT/TS Group from **June 11, 2026**.
- Securities in Annexure II remain in their current group (T/XT/MT/TS/P/Z/ZP) with a **5% price band**.
- Settlement will be on a **trade-to-trade basis** — no netting off of positions is permitted.
- A **100% VAR Margin** will be levied on these scrips as per Exchange Notice No. 20050805-12 dated August 5, 2005.
- Shortlisting is based on XBRL submissions by listed companies.
- The transfer is a **temporary surveillance measure** and does not constitute an adverse action against the company; it will be reviewed periodically.

## Regulatory Changes

The criteria for shifting securities to/from Trade-to-Trade settlement are decided in consultation with SEBI and applied uniformly across stock exchanges. The detailed criteria are available on the BSE website. This revision is a periodic review under the extant SEBI surveillance framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members** must take adequate precaution while trading in the affected securities.
- No netting off of positions will be allowed for T2T scrips.
- Trading members must ensure collection of 100% VAR Margin on these scrips.
- For clarifications, members may contact BSE Surveillance at **bse.surv@bseindia.com**.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 8, 2026
- **Effective Date:** June 11, 2026 (transfer of Annexure I securities to T/XT/MT/TS group)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine but operationally significant surveillance action. Affected securities will face stricter settlement conditions (trade-to-trade, no netting) and full VAR margin requirements, increasing the cost and risk management burden for trading members. The 5% price band on Annexure II securities further restricts intraday price movement. Market liquidity in the affected scrips may be reduced. The measure is temporary and subject to periodic review based on market conditions.
