## Summary

BSE has listed and permitted trading of 1,24,38,296 new equity shares of One Global Service Provider Limited (Scrip Code: 514330, ISIN: INE670O01013) effective March 04, 2026. These shares were issued pursuant to the Scheme of Amalgamation of Plus Care International Private Limited (Transferor Company) with One Global Service Provider Limited (Transferee Company) under Sections 230 to 232 of the Companies Act, 2013.

## Key Points

- 1,24,38,296 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each are newly listed on BSE for trading from March 04, 2026
- Shares issued under the Scheme of Amalgamation of Plus Care International Private Limited with One Global Service Provider Limited
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- ISIN: INE670O01013
- Distinctive numbers: 7321208 to 19759503
- Date of allotment: July 04, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular is issued under the standard listing process following court/NCLT-approved amalgamation under Sections 230 to 232 of the Companies Act, 2013.

## Compliance Requirements

Market participants are informed that the newly listed shares are available for trading. No specific compliance action is required from market participants beyond standard trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** July 04, 2025
- **Effective Trading Date:** March 04, 2026
- **Circular Date:** March 02, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action listing. The addition of 1,24,38,296 shares to the tradeable float of One Global Service Provider Limited (Scrip Code: 514330) may modestly increase liquidity for the scrip. Existing shareholders and new investors can trade these shares from March 04, 2026. The pari-passu ranking ensures no dilution of rights relative to existing shares.
