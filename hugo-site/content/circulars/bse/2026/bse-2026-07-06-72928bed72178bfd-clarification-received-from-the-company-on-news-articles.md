## Summary

The Exchange sought clarification from DCM Shriram Ltd (Scrip Code: 523367) regarding a news article that appeared on CNBC-TV18, as part of its routine process to verify news reports appearing in newspapers, websites, or television channels and to inform investors about their accuracy. The company's reply has been received and is attached to this notice.

## Key Points

- BSE, through its Surveillance Department (DOSS), sought clarification from DCM Shriram Ltd on a news article published/aired via CNBC-TV18.
- The company's reply to the clarification request is attached to the notice.
- This is a standard surveillance measure used by the Exchange to verify news accuracy and inform market participants.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine clarification notice, not a regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action is required from investors or market participants. DCM Shriram Ltd has already submitted its reply to the Exchange's clarification request.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 06 July 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is likely low, as this is a routine surveillance clarification rather than a disclosure of material non-public information or a regulatory action. Investors in DCM Shriram Ltd (Scrip Code: 523367) may wish to review the company's reply for context on the news article, but the notice itself does not indicate any adverse finding or corporate action.
