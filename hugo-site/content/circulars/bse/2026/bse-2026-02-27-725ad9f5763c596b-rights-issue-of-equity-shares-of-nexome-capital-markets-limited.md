## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Nexome Capital Markets Limited (BSE Code: 508905) has fixed March 5, 2026 as the record date for a rights issue of equity shares. Effective the same date, transactions in the company's equity shares must be conducted on an ex-rights basis under the Rolling Settlement segment.

## Key Points

- Company: Nexome Capital Markets Limited (BSE Code: 508905)
- Rights issue ratio: 1 new equity share for every 2 equity shares held
- Issue price: Rs. 75/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- + premium Rs. 65/-)
- Record date: March 5, 2026
- Ex-rights date: March 5, 2026 (Settlement No. DR-833/2025-2026)
- Segment: Dematerialised Securities â€“ Rolling Settlement

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine corporate action notification under BSE's standard listing compliance framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all transactions in Nexome Capital Markets Limited equity shares are executed on an **ex-rights basis** from March 5, 2026 onwards.
- Members should update their systems to reflect the ex-rights pricing adjustment effective DR-833/2025-2026.

## Important Dates

- **Record Date:** March 5, 2026
- **Ex-Rights Effective Date:** March 5, 2026
- **Settlement Number:** DR-833/2025-2026
- **Circular Date:** February 27, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This rights issue will dilute existing shareholding by up to 50% (1 new share per 2 held) if fully subscribed. The issue price of Rs. 75/- (face value Rs. 10/- + Rs. 65/- premium) reflects a premium over face value. Existing shareholders have the right to subscribe to maintain their proportional holding. Trading members must apply ex-rights adjustments from the record date, which may affect intraday pricing and margin calculations for this scrip. Impact is limited to holders and traders of this specific company's shares.
