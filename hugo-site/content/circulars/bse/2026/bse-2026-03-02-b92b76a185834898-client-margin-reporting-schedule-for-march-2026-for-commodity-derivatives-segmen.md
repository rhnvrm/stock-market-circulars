## Summary

The Indian Clearing Corporation Limited (ICCL) has published the client margin reporting schedule for the Commodity Derivatives Segment for March 2026. The circular specifies the last dates for margin reporting and corresponding penalty dates for each trade date from 02-Mar-2026 through 31-Mar-2026.

## Key Points

- Issued by Indian Clearing Corporation Limited (ICCL) under Notice No. 20260302-11.
- Covers all trade dates in the Commodity Derivatives Segment for March 2026.
- Each trade date has a designated last date for margin reporting and a penalty date.
- Reporting deadlines generally fall 5-7 trading days after the respective trade date.
- Penalty dates follow the last reporting date by one trading day.
- The schedule accounts for market holidays (e.g., no entries for certain dates such as 07-Mar and 08-Mar, suggesting weekend/holiday gaps).
- Signed by Manoj Panchal and Omkar Hatkar, Deputy Managers at ICCL.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is a routine monthly schedule circular issued in accordance with existing margin reporting obligations under ICCL/SEBI guidelines for commodity derivatives.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must submit client margin reports for each trade date by the specified last date of margin reporting.
- Failure to report by the deadline will attract penalties effective from the penalty date.
- Members should ensure their back-office and reporting systems are aligned with the published schedule.
