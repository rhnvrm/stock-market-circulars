## Summary

BSE has notified trading members and custodians that M/s. Smart Services Private Limited (the Acquirer) has launched an open offer to acquire up to 64,86,000 equity shares of Sharp India Limited (SIL), representing 25.00% of its fully paid-up equity and voting share capital, at a price of Rs. 10.00 per share payable in cash. The offer is made under SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 2011.

## Key Points

- Acquirer: M/s. Smart Services Private Limited
- Target Company: Sharp India Limited (SIL)
- Offer Price: Rs. 10.00 per equity share (face value Rs. 10 each), payable in cash
- Shares Offered For: Up to 64,86,000 equity shares (25.00% of fully paid-up equity and voting capital)
- Offer Period: Tuesday, June 16, 2026 to Tuesday, June 30, 2026 (excluding SEBI Holiday on Friday, June 26, 2026)
- Regulatory Basis: SEBI (SAST) Regulations 2011, Regulation 3(1) and Regulation 4 — substantial acquisition with change in control
- Letter of Offer is attached to the circular for participant reference
- Offer details available on BSE website: www.bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The offer is conducted under existing SEBI frameworks:
- SEBI circular CIR/CFD/POLICYCELL/1/2015 dated April 13, 2015
- SEBI circular CFD/DCR2/CIR/P/2016/131 dated December 9, 2016
- BSE notices 20170210-16, 20190424-35, 20200528-32, 20201102-43, and 20210825-62 covering revised guidelines for acquisition through stock exchange tender-offer mechanism

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and custodians must facilitate the acquisition window as per BSE's revised guidelines for tender-offer mechanisms
- Market participants must process buy/sell orders through the designated Offer to Buy acquisition window during the specified offer period
- Participants must adhere to SEBI (SAST) Regulations 2011 and the revised guidelines cited above

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: June 12, 2026
- Offer Opens: Tuesday, June 16, 2026
- Offer Closes: Tuesday, June 30, 2026
- Excluded Date (SEBI Holiday): Friday, June 26, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This open offer is stock-specific and directly affects existing public shareholders of Sharp India Limited. Shareholders tendering shares will receive Rs. 10.00 per share in cash. The offer targets 25% of the company's equity, indicating a change-of-control transaction. Broader market impact is limited; however, traders and arbitrageurs monitoring merger-arbitrage opportunities in the small-cap space may find this relevant. Trading members should configure systems to handle the acquisition window orders for SIL during the offer period.
