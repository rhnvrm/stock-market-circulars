## Summary

BSE issued Notice No. 20260522-47 informing trading members of the new ISIN number assigned to the equity shares of IB INFOTECH ENTERPRISES LTD (Scrip Code: 519463) following the sub-division of shares from face value Rs.10/- per share to Re.1/- per share. This notice is in continuation of Notice No. 20260518-38 dated May 18, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: IB INFOTECH ENTERPRISES LTD (Scrip Code: 519463)
- New ISIN: INE678B01039
- Reason for new ISIN: Sub-division of equity shares from face value Rs.10/- to Re.1/- per share
- The new ISIN is effective for trades executed on and from the ex-date
- DR reference: DR-638/2026-2027

## Regulatory Changes

The existing ISIN for IB INFOTECH ENTERPRISES LTD equity shares is replaced by the new ISIN INE678B01039 to reflect the changed face value of Re.1/- per share post sub-division.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must use the new ISIN INE678B01039 for all trades in IB INFOTECH ENTERPRISES LTD equity shares on and from the ex-date of 26 May 2026. All systems and records referencing the old ISIN should be updated accordingly.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 22 May 2026
- Continuation of Notice: 20260518-38 dated 18 May 2026
- Ex-Date (new ISIN effective): 26 May 2026

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited to trading members and shareholders of IB INFOTECH ENTERPRISES LTD. The ISIN change is a technical update consequent to the share sub-division and does not affect the economic value of holdings. Members must ensure back-office systems are updated with the new ISIN before the ex-date to avoid settlement issues.
