## Summary

BSE has listed 46,72,000 new equity shares of Magellanic Cloud Limited (Scrip Code: 538891) issued on a preferential basis to non-promoters. The shares are permitted to trade with effect from January 28, 2026. These shares are subject to a lock-in period until July 31, 2026.

## Key Points

- 46,72,000 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 73/-
- Issue price: Rs. 75/- per share
- Allotment date: November 11, 2025
- Trading commencement: January 28, 2026
- Distribution numbers: 584395121 to 589067120
- ISIN: INE613C01026
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard new securities listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities listing
- All 46,72,000 shares are subject to lock-in restrictions until July 31, 2026
- Shares cannot be transferred or sold before the lock-in expiry date

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 11, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: January 28, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry**: July 31, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine preferential allotment listing with minimal market impact. The addition of 46.72 lakh shares represents dilution for existing shareholders. The lock-in period until July 31, 2026 ensures these shares will not immediately add to market supply. Impact is limited to Magellanic Cloud Limited and its shareholders, with no broader market implications.
