## Summary

Indian Clearing Corporation Limited (ICCL) has issued the client margin reporting schedule for the Equity Derivatives Segment for March 2026. The circular specifies the last date for margin reporting and the corresponding penalty date for each trade date from 02-Mar-2026 through 30-Mar-2026.

## Key Points

- Margin reporting deadlines and penalty dates are defined for all Equity Derivatives trade dates in March 2026.
- Reporting windows extend into early April 2026, with the final penalty date being 09-Apr-2026 for trades on 30-Mar-2026.
- Members must report client margins within the specified window to avoid penalties.
- The circular is issued jointly by Manoj Panchal and Omkar Hatkar, both Deputy Managers at ICCL.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is a periodic operational schedule circular issued by ICCL for member compliance.

## Compliance Requirements

- All members trading in the Equity Derivatives Segment must report client margins by the "Last Date of Margin Reporting" for each respective trade date.
- Failure to report within the stipulated deadline will attract penalties effective from the "Penalty Date" listed for each trade date.

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a routine operational impact on all brokers and clearing members active in the Equity Derivatives Segment. Timely client margin reporting is a regulatory obligation; members must track each trade date's specific reporting deadline to avoid financial penalties. The schedule accounts for weekends and market holidays, particularly the end-of-month and early-April trading days.
