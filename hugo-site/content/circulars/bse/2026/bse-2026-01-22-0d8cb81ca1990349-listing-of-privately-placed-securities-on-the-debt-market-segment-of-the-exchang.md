## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial papers issued on a private placement basis on its debt market segment effective January 22, 2026. The listing includes fresh listings from 360 ONE Alternates Asset Management Limited, Adani Enterprises Ltd., Mirae Asset Sharekhan Financial Services Limited, and Godrej Housing Finance Limited, plus a further listing from Vivriti Capital Limited. All securities will trade in dematerialized form with a standard denomination of Rs.5 lakhs and tick size of 1 paise.

## Key Points

- Fresh listings: 4 commercial papers from 360 ONE Alternates (200 units), Adani Enterprises (200 units), Mirae Asset Sharekhan (1000 units), and Godrej Housing Finance (5000 units)
- Further listing: Vivriti Capital (200 units)
- All securities trade in dematerialized form only under respective ISIN numbers
- Standard denomination: Rs.5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Interest rates range from 6.9% p.a. (Godrej Housing Finance) to 8.75% p.a. (Vivriti Capital)
- Face value and paid-up value: Rs.500,000 per unit for all securities
- Credit ratings: A1+ from CARE, CRISIL, and/or ICRA

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading shall occur in standard denomination of Rs.5 lakhs and multiples
- Members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: January 22, 2026
- **Allotment Dates**: January 21-22, 2026
- **Redemption Dates**:
  - Godrej Housing Finance: March 13, 2026
  - Mirae Asset Sharekhan: April 15, 2026
  - Vivriti Capital: April 22, 2026
  - Adani Enterprises: August 25, 2026
  - 360 ONE Alternates: January 20, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Minimal impact on broader markets. This is a routine debt market listing notification affecting only debt segment trading members. The commercial papers listed are short-term debt instruments (3-12 months maturity) with investment-grade A1+ ratings, indicating low credit risk. Trading will be limited to institutional investors and qualified participants in the debt market segment.
