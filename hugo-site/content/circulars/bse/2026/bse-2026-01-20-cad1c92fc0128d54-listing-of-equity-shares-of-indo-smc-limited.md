## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of INDO SMC Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company will be listed on the SME segment with scrip code 544681, symbol INDOSMC, and ISIN INE0WKY01013. The specific listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: INDO SMC Limited
- Scrip Code: 544681
- Symbol: INDOSMC
- ISIN Number: INE0WKY01013
- Segment: SME
- Listing date to be announced separately

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed about the upcoming listing. No specific compliance actions required at this stage.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: January 20, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement for a single SME company. Impact is limited to trading members who will have access to trade the new security once listing is completed. The market-wide impact is minimal as this affects only one company in the SME segment.
