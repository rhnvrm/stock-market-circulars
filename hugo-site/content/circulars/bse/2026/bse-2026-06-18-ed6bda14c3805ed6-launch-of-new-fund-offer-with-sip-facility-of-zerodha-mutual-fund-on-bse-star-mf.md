## Summary

BSE has informed its members that Zerodha Mutual Fund will launch the **ZERODHA LIFE CYCLE FUND 2036** as a New Fund Offer (NFO) with SIP/XSIP facility on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The NFO opens on June 19, 2026, and closes on July 7, 2026, with a tentative allotment date of July 10, 2026.

## Key Points

- Scheme: ZERODHA LIFE CYCLE FUND 2036 - DIRECT PLAN - GROWTH
- Category: Equity
- ISIN: INF0R8F01224
- NFO Period: June 19, 2026 – July 7, 2026
- Platform: BSE StAR MF Platform
- Tentative Allotment Date: July 10, 2026
- Lumpsum purchases are accepted via the extended NFO window until midnight on July 7, 2026
- SIP/XSIP registrations must be client-authenticated by 3:00 PM on July 7, 2026 (NFO closure date)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced. This is an operational notice for BSE members facilitating mutual fund transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- Transactions where clients do not initiate funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day (including those placed during the extended NFO period) will be cancelled; CC with 'N' flag will be reported to the RTA/AMC.
- Transactions with client-initiated payment will remain pending until 3:00 PM on the last day of AMC funds pay-out, or T+5, whichever is earlier.
- Clear funds must be available in the ICCL account by 6:00 PM on July 10, 2026, for allotment eligibility.
- Cheque and OTM payments are accepted during the NFO period, subject to clear funds reaching the ICCL account by 6:00 PM on July 10, 2026.
- Switch transactions are accepted during the NFO period, but switch-out funds received on or after the allotment date will not be considered for allotment.

## Important Dates

| Event | Date / Time |
|---|---|
| NFO Opens | June 19, 2026 |
| NFO Closes (SIP/XSIP authentication deadline) | July 7, 2026, 3:00 PM |
| NFO Extended Window (Lumpsum only) | Until midnight July 7, 2026 |
| Clear funds deadline in ICCL account | July 10, 2026, 6:00 PM |
| Tentative Allotment Date | July 10, 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

This notice has low market impact and is operationally relevant only to BSE member brokers offering mutual fund transactions through the BSE StAR MF Platform. Retail investors subscribing to this scheme via member platforms should ensure timely fund initiation to avoid cancellation. No broader market or regulatory implications are expected.
