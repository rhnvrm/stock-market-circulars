## Summary

BSE Limited, through Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd (ICCL), has announced Demat Auction 654 for Rolling Settlement No. DR-654/2026-27. Trading members are invited to submit offers for purchase of securities via auction on June 19, 2026, with pay-in scheduled for June 22, 2026.

## Key Points

- Auction offer entry window: 2:00 PM to 2:45 PM on June 19, 2026
- Auction matching session: 2:45 PM to 3:00 PM
- Delivery statement and accepted offer reports: 3:00 PM to 3:30 PM
- Money statement and final reports: 3:35 PM to 5:30 PM
- Securities pay-in for auction: Monday, June 22, 2026 at 8:00 AM
- Under the Uniform Membership structure, settlement is processed through the Clearing Member of the Trading Member
- The restriction on trading members offering securities in auction for settlements where they had short deliveries has been removed

## Regulatory Changes

Following implementation of the Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment, the restriction on trading members offering securities in auction in the settlement in which they have short delivered such securities has been removed.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and custodians must check their auction pay-in obligations for securities
- Securities pay-in must be executed in depositories within depository cut-off timings
- Trading members must take utmost care while entering securities codes and rates; no corrections will be permitted after submission
- Members should refer to the cutoff rate on the Auction Offer Entry Screen before submitting offers
- Members should also consult notices related to Corporate Actions (Ex-Rights, Ex-Bonus, Ex-Split, etc.) for relevant securities

## Important Dates

- **June 19, 2026**: Auction offer entry (2:00 PM – 2:45 PM), matching, and report generation
- **June 22, 2026**: Auction pay-in at 8:00 AM

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice affecting trading members and custodians with obligations under Rolling Settlement DR-654/2026-27. Members with short deliveries in this settlement should participate in the auction within the specified time window and ensure timely securities pay-in by June 22, 2026. The removal of the short-delivery restriction on auction offers provides additional flexibility to affected trading members.
