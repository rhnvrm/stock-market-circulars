## Summary

BSE has announced that MSAFE EQUIPMENTS LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544695), an SME IPO stock, will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling settlement segment (M Group) effective Wednesday, February 18, 2026. This is a continuation of Exchange's Notice No. 20260203-45 dated February 03, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: MSAFE EQUIPMENTS LIMITED - SME IPO
- Scrip Code: 544695
- Current Group: MT Group (Trade for Trade segment)
- New Group: M Group (Rolling segment)
- Effective Date: Wednesday, February 18, 2026
- Notice Number: 20260204-6
- Contact Person: Mr. Anurag Jain (Tel: 022-2272 8822)

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will transition from Trade for Trade settlement mechanism to Rolling settlement mechanism, allowing for normal T+2 settlement cycle instead of delivery-based trading only.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should:
- Note the segment change effective February 18, 2026
- Update their trading systems to reflect the new group classification
- Contact Mr. Anurag Jain for any clarifications or further details

## Important Dates

- **February 03, 2026**: Previous notice (20260203-45) issued
- **February 04, 2026**: Current notice issued
- **February 18, 2026**: Effective date of segment transfer

## Impact Assessment

This change will improve liquidity and trading flexibility for MSAFE EQUIPMENTS LIMITED shares by moving from the restrictive Trade for Trade segment to the Rolling settlement segment. Investors will be able to trade the stock under normal market conditions with intraday trading possibilities, rather than compulsory delivery-based trading. This is a positive development typical for SME IPO stocks after initial listing period.
