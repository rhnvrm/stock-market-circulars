## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (No. 20260521-3) dated 21 May 2026 announcing the listing of units of the DSP Nifty FMCG ETF, a scheme under DSP Mutual Fund, on the Mutual Fund segment of the Exchange. This is administered by the DCS-Listing department under the Company Related category.

## Key Points

- BSE Notice No. 20260521-3 dated 21 May 2026
- DSP Nifty FMCG ETF units to be listed on BSE's Mutual Fund segment
- Scheme is part of DSP Mutual Fund
- The ETF tracks the Nifty FMCG Index, providing exposure to fast-moving consumer goods sector stocks
- Administered by BSE's DCS-Listing department

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notice under existing BSE listing framework for mutual fund ETF units.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements mentioned for market participants beyond standard trading procedures for listed ETF units on BSE's Mutual Fund segment.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 21 May 2026
- Listing Date: As per notice (effective from date of notice or as communicated separately by BSE/DSP Mutual Fund)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. The listing provides investors with an exchange-traded vehicle to gain passive exposure to the FMCG sector via the Nifty FMCG Index. This adds to the suite of thematic ETF options available on BSE and benefits retail and institutional investors seeking FMCG sector allocation without direct stock selection.
