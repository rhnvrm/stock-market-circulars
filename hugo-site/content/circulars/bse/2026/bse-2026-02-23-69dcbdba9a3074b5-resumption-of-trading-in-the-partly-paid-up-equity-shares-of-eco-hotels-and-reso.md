## Summary

Eco Hotels and Resorts Limited (BSE scrip code: 890225) has applied for resumption of trading in 88,54,060 partly paid-up equity shares on which the first call money of Rs. 3.80 per share (Rs. 2.50 against face value and Rs. 1.30 against premium) has been received. These shares carry a paid-up value of Rs. 5/- per share and are identified under ISIN IN9638N01028 with no lock-in period.

## Key Points

- Total partly paid shares originally issued (as on September 26, 2025): 1,28,76,808
- Partly paid shares on which first call money received: 88,68,167
- Partly paid shares eligible for trading on resumption: 88,54,060
- First call money received: Rs. 3.80 per share (Rs. 2.50 face value + Rs. 1.30 premium)
- Paid-up value per share available for trading: Rs. 5/-
- ISIN for trading shares: IN9638N01028
- No lock-in period applicable
- NSDL distinctive numbers: 51751136 to 55346877 (35,95,742 shares)
- CDSL distinctive numbers: 55352250 to 60610567 (52,58,318 shares)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This circular is an operational notice for resumption of trading pursuant to receipt of first call money on partly paid-up equity shares issued under a rights/public issue.

## Compliance Requirements

- BSE Listing Operations is notified to activate trading for the 88,54,060 partly paid-up equity shares under ISIN IN9638N01028.
- Depository participants (NSDL and CDSL) should ensure the respective distinctive number ranges are correctly mapped and available for market transactions.
- No action required from general investors beyond awareness of resumed tradability of these partly paid shares.

## Important Dates

- September 26, 2025: Date as of which total partly paid shares issued (1,28,76,808) were recorded
- February 23, 2026: Date of application for trading resumption submitted to BSE

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational update with medium market impact limited to existing holders of Eco Hotels and Resorts Limited's partly paid-up equity shares (ISIN IN9638N01028). Approximately 88,54,060 shares become tradeable following first call money collection, representing roughly 68.8% of the total partly paid shares issued. The remaining ~14,22,748 shares (on which first call money was not received) are excluded from this resumption. No lock-in constraints apply, so all eligible shares are freely tradeable upon resumption. The company is promoted by Eco Hotels UK PLC and is listed on BSE under scrip code 890225.
