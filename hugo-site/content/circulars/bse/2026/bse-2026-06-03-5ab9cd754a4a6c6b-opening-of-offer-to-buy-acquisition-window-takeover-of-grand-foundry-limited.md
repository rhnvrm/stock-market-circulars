## Summary

BSE has informed trading members and custodians that SAR Televenture Limited (Acquirer) has made an open offer to acquire up to 79,11,800 fully paid-up equity shares of Grand Foundry Limited (Target Company), representing 26.00% of total equity/voting share capital, at a price of Rs.2.50 per share payable in cash. The offer is being conducted through the BSE acquisition window mechanism under SEBI (SAST) Regulations, 2011.

## Key Points

- Acquirer: M/s SAR Televenture Limited
- Target Company: Grand Foundry Limited
- Offer price: Rs.2.50 per fully paid-up equity share (face value Rs.4.00 each)
- Shares sought: Up to 79,11,800 equity shares (26.00% of total equity/voting capital)
- Offer triggered under SEBI (SAST) Regulations, 2011 – Regulations 3(1) and 4
- Mechanism follows SEBI circular CIR/CFD/POLICYCELL/1/2015 (Apr 13, 2015) and CFD/DCR2/CIR/P/2016/131 (Dec 09, 2016)
- Letter of Offer attached to the BSE notice

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The offer follows existing framework under SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 2011 and the revised BSE guidelines for acquisition of shares through Stock Exchange pursuant to Tender-Offers (Exchange notices: 20170210-16, 20190424-35, 20200528-32, 20201102-43, 20210825-62).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members and Custodians must note the opening of the Offer to Buy acquisition window.
- Participants must follow the Revised Guidelines per SEBI circular CFD/DCR2/CIR/P/2016/131 dated December 09, 2016 and Exchange notice 20170210-16 dated February 10, 2017 and 20190424-35 dated April 24, 2019.
- Public shareholders of Grand Foundry Limited wishing to tender shares must do so through the BSE acquisition window during the offer period.
- Detailed offer information is available on the BSE website.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: June 03, 2026
- Offer Open Date: Tuesday, June 09, 2026
- Offer Close Date: Monday, June 22, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This open offer is significant for existing public shareholders of Grand Foundry Limited, who have the opportunity to tender their shares at Rs.2.50 per share — a price that may represent a premium or discount to the prevailing market price. The offer represents 26% of the total voting capital, which is the minimum threshold for a mandatory open offer under SEBI SAST Regulations. Broader market impact is limited to this specific company. Trading members with client holdings in Grand Foundry Limited should notify clients of the offer window and facilitate tendering as required.
