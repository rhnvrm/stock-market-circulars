## Summary

BSE has issued a notice to trading members informing them of the suspension of trading in the Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of Aditya Birla Real Estate Limited. The company has fixed April 17, 2026 as the record date for payment of interest and redemption on exercise of call option. Trading members have been advised not to deal in the specified debenture from April 17, 2026 onwards.

## Key Points

- Trading in the NCD of Aditya Birla Real Estate Limited (ABREL-8.05%-1-3-27-PVT) is suspended effective April 17, 2026
- Record date fixed: April 17, 2026
- Purpose: Payment of interest and redemption on exercise of call option
- Debenture code on BSE: DR-612/2026-2027
- ISIN: INE055A08078 (BSE scrip code: 975457)
- Notice issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President – Listing Compliance and Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard procedural notice under BSE's listing compliance framework requiring suspension of trading in debt securities ahead of a corporate action record date.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the debentures of Aditya Birla Real Estate Limited (ISIN: INE055A08078, scrip code: 975457) on or after April 17, 2026
- Members are required to take note of this suspension and ensure no transactions are executed in the specified instrument from the effective date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** April 15, 2026
- **Record Date:** April 17, 2026
- **No Dealings From:** April 17, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The trading suspension affects market participants holding or trading the specific NCD series ABREL-8.05%-1-3-27-PVT of Aditya Birla Real Estate Limited in the BSE debt segment. The call option exercise and simultaneous interest payment indicates an early redemption event for this debenture series. Impact is confined to the debt segment and is routine in nature; equity markets and other instruments of the company are unaffected by this notice.
