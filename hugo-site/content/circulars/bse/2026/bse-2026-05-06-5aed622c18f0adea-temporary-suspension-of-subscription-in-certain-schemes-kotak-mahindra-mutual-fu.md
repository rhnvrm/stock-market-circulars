## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260506-6 informing members of a temporary suspension of subscription activity in four specific Kotak Mahindra Mutual Fund overseas equity Fund-of-Fund (FOF) schemes on the BSE StAR MF Platform, effective May 6, 2026. This supersedes the earlier Notice No. 20260430-1 regarding resumption of subscriptions in Kotak Mahindra schemes.

## Key Points

- Lumpsum investments (fresh and additional purchases), switch-in transactions, and new SIP/XSIP and STP-in registrations have been disabled for the four affected schemes.
- Orders received after the cut-off time of 3:00 PM on May 5, 2026 will be rejected and refunded to investors once funds are received from AMC/RTA.
- Existing SIP/XSIP and STP registrations already on BSE StAR MF Platform will continue to be processed at the RTA/AMC end.
- Redemptions, switch-outs, STP-out, and SWP transactions remain permitted.

## Affected Schemes

- Kotak Global Emerging Market Overseas Equity Omni FOF
- Kotak Global Innovation Overseas Equity Omni FOF
- Kotak International REIT Overseas Equity Omni FOF
- Kotak Quality Overseas Equity Omni FOF

## Regulatory Changes

The suspension reverses the earlier resumption of subscriptions announced in Notice No. 20260430-1 dated April 30, 2026. BSE has implemented platform-level controls disabling new subscription and switch-in functionality for the listed schemes as requested by Kotak Mahindra AMC.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members (brokers/distributors) must not accept or process lumpsum, switch-in, or new systematic registration transactions for the four affected Kotak Mahindra schemes from May 6, 2026 onward.
- Any orders triggered to the Registrar & Transfer Agent (RTA) after May 5, 2026 at 3:00 PM will be rejected; members should inform investors accordingly.
- Members may continue to process redemptions, switch-outs, STP-out, and SWP for these schemes.

## Important Dates

- **May 5, 2026, 3:00 PM**: Cut-off time — orders received after this time will be rejected and refunded.
- **May 6, 2026**: Effective date of temporary suspension of subscriptions.

## Impact Assessment

The suspension affects new and additional investment flows into four Kotak Mahindra overseas equity FOF schemes on the BSE StAR MF platform. Investors seeking fresh exposure to these funds through lumpsum or systematic routes will be unable to do so until the suspension is lifted. Existing investors with active SIPs or STPs are unaffected as their registrations will continue to be processed. Exit options (redemption, switch-out, SWP) remain available, limiting downside risk for existing investors. The impact is moderate and confined to the BSE StAR MF channel for these specific schemes.
