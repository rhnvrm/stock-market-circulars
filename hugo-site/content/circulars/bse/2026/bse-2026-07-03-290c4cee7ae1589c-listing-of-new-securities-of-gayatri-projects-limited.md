## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 27,71,00,315 new equity shares of Rs. 2/- each of Gayatri Projects Limited (Scrip Code: 532767), issued at a premium of Rs. 8/- per share on a preferential basis to promoters and non-promoters, will be listed and admitted to trading on the Exchange effective Monday, July 6, 2026. These shares rank pari-passu with the existing equity shares of the company.

## Key Points

- Total new shares listed: 27,71,00,315 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each
- Issue price: Rs. 10/- per share (face value Rs. 2/- plus premium of Rs. 8/-)
- Allotment made to Promoters and Non-Promoters on a preferential basis
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distinctive numbers: 187198686 to 464299000
- ISIN: INE336H01023
- Allotment occurred in two tranches: 10,90,00,000 shares on 20/04/2026 and 16,81,00,315 shares on 22/04/2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is a routine listing approval notice for preferentially allotted shares under BSE Listing Operations.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must recognize the new distinctive number ranges and lock-in restrictions applicable to these shares when processing trades in Gayatri Projects Limited.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: 20/04/2026 (10,90,00,000 shares) and 22/04/2026 (16,81,00,315 shares)
- Listing and trading effective from: Monday, July 6, 2026
- Lock-in expiry dates: 08/01/2027 (for 10,90,00,000 and 7,52,40,515 shares) and 08/01/2028 (for 9,28,59,800 shares)

## Impact Assessment

The listing of these preferentially allotted shares increases the total equity base of Gayatri Projects Limited, which could have a dilutive effect on existing shareholders. However, a significant portion of the new shares are subject to lock-in until January 2027 or January 2028, limiting immediate free-float impact and potential selling pressure. Market participants should monitor the stock for any price adjustments related to this capital expansion.
