## Summary

BSE has published the list of securities subject to demat auction under Settlement No. 808 dated January 30, 2026. The auction covers 100 different scrips with varying quantities ranging from 1 share to 86,769 shares across multiple sectors including metals, infrastructure, technology, financial services, and commodities.

## Key Points

- Total of 100 scrips included in demat auction settlement 808
- Highest quantity: HINDUSTAN CO (86,769 shares)
- Other significant quantities: PARAS PETROF (18,980 shares), MANGAL CREDI (2,165 shares), BALU FORGE I (3,600 shares)
- Covers diverse sectors: metals, energy, technology, infrastructure, textiles, and financial services
- Settlement date: January 30, 2026
- Includes major stocks like Tata Motors, Tata Steel, Larsen & Toubro, Hindalco, Vedanta, Coal India, BHEL, and Grasim Industries

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine demat auction settlement notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members who have short positions in the listed securities must deliver the required quantities
- Participants must ensure availability of shares in demat form for auction settlement
- Settlement obligations must be fulfilled as per BSE auction settlement procedures

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: January 30, 2026
- Settlement Number: 808

## Impact Assessment

This demat auction affects trading members with outstanding delivery obligations in the specified securities. The auction mechanism ensures settlement completion by sourcing shares from the market when original sellers fail to deliver. Impact is limited to specific participants with short positions in these 100 scrips. Notable quantities in major stocks like Hindustan Copper (86,769 shares), Tata Steel (611 shares), and Hindustan Zinc (955 shares) indicate significant settlement failures that required auction intervention.
