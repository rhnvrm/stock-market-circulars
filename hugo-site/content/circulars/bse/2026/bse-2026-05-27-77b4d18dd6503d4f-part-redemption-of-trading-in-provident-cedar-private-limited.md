## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that Provident Cedar Private Limited has carried out a partial redemption of its debentures. As a result, the face value per debenture has been reduced to Rs. 2,19,119.59. The revised face value will be applicable for trading with effect from May 29, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: Provident Cedar Private Limited
- Scrip Code: 974311 (PCPL-17-4-28-PVT)
- ISIN: INE0NF907031
- Reduced Face Value per Debenture (post partial redemption): Rs. 2,19,119.59
- Effective date for trading with reduced face value: May 29, 2026
- Reference: DR-640/2026-2027

## Regulatory Changes

The face value of the debenture PCPL-17-4-28-PVT (ISIN: INE0NF907031) has been reduced from its previous value to Rs. 2,19,119.59 per debenture following partial redemption by the issuer company.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are required to take note of the revised face value and ensure all trades in the subject debenture (scrip code 974311) are conducted using the reduced face value of Rs. 2,19,119.59 per debenture with effect from May 29, 2026.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: May 27, 2026
- Effective Date for Trading at Reduced Face Value: May 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This notice has a limited, targeted impact affecting trading members dealing in Provident Cedar Private Limited debentures on the BSE Debt segment. The partial redemption reduces the outstanding principal per debenture, and trading members must update their systems to reflect the new face value of Rs. 2,19,119.59. There is no broader market impact as this pertains to a single privately placed debenture series.
