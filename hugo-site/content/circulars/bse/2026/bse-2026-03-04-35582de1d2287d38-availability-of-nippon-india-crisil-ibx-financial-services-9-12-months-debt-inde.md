## Summary

BSE has notified members (Notice No. 20260304-33) that the Nippon India CRISIL-IBX Financial Services 9-12 Months Debt Index Fund has been reopened for ongoing subscription and redemption, along with SIP, STP, and SWP facilities, on the BSE StAR MF Platform with effect from March 5, 2026. This follows an earlier notice (No. 20260220-41).

## Key Points

- The fund is now available for ongoing transactions including subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP on BSE StAR MF Platform.
- Effective date: March 5, 2026.
- Six scheme variants are available across Regular and Direct plans with Growth and IDCW options.
- Category: Debt (Short Duration / Index Fund).

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notice regarding platform availability of an existing mutual fund scheme.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Members of BSE StAR MF Platform may now process transactions for these schemes from March 5, 2026 onwards.

## Important Dates

- **March 5, 2026**: Effective date for reopening of the fund for ongoing subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This is a routine operational update enabling investors to transact in an existing Nippon India debt index fund through the BSE StAR MF Platform. It provides additional liquidity and accessibility for investors seeking short-duration debt index exposure aligned to the CRISIL-IBX Financial Services 9-12 Months benchmark.
