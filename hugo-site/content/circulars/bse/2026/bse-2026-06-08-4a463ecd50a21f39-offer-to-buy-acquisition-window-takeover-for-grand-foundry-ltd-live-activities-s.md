## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260608-23 announces the live activities schedule for the Offer to Buy (Acquisition Window – Takeover) for GRAND FOUNDRY LTD (Symbol: GFSTEELS, Scrip Code: 513343, ISIN: INE534A01028). The offer runs from Tuesday, 9th June 2026 to Monday, 22nd June 2026 at an offer price of Rs. 2.50 per share for up to 79,11,800 equity shares.

## Key Points

- **Security:** GRAND FOUNDRY LTD | Symbol: GFSTEELS | Scrip Code: 513343 | ISIN: INE534A01028
- **Offer Size:** Up to 79,11,800 equity shares
- **Offer Price:** Rs. 2.50 per share
- **Face Value:** Rs. 4.00
- **Market Lot:** 1
- **Opening Date:** Tuesday, 9th June 2026
- **Closing Date:** Monday, 22nd June 2026
- Bid Offering Period: 9th June to 22nd June 2026, 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.
- Give-up / Take-up Period: 9th June to 19th June 2026, 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.
- Give-up on last day (22nd June 2026): 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.
- Take-up on last day (22nd June 2026): 9:15 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.
- Investor categories include FII, FI, MF, IC, Other QIBs, NIB, Company, and Individual

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular operationalizes an existing takeover/open offer framework under SEBI Takeover Regulations via BSE's OTB (Offer to Buy) module.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must ensure UCC of investors is registered and updated with correct PAN Number and Category in the Exchange UCC database.
- Trading Members can bid only for investors holding shares in demat form.
- Members and Custodians should note that if no securities are accepted, no End of Issue files will be generated on the Extranet.
- For operational instructions, members should refer to Exchange Notices 20150930-1 (dated 30 September 2015) and 20170203-29 (dated 3 February 2017).
- Access platforms: iBBS (https://ibbs.bseindia.com/), RTRMS (https://rtrms.bseindia.com/), Extranet (https://member.bseindia.com/).
- For login/password issues, contact Trading Operations at 022-22728005 or user.trdops@bseindia.com.

## Impact Assessment

This notice has direct and high impact on existing shareholders of GRAND FOUNDRY LTD (GFSTEELS) who may wish to tender their shares in this takeover open offer at Rs. 2.50 per share. Trading members and custodians need to operationally prepare for bid submission via the iBBS platform. The offer price of Rs. 2.50 against a face value of Rs. 4.00 may indicate the stock is trading below its face value, which could be significant for investor decision-making. All eligible investor categories (FII, FI, MF, IC, QIBs, NIBs, corporates, and individuals) can participate.
