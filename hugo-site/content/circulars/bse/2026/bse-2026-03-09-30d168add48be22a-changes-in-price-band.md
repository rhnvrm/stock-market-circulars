## Summary

BSE has revised price bands for 17 equity scrips effective March 10, 2026. The revised bands range from 2% to 20%, replacing the previously applicable levels. Trading members should note the new limits before the market opens on March 10.

## Key Points

- Price bands revised for 17 scrips across the equity segment
- Effective date: March 10, 2026
- Revised bands range from 2% to 20%
- Changes are initiated by BSE Surveillance department
- Members may contact bse.surv@bseindia.com for clarifications

Price band percentages have been revised as follows:

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must adhere to the revised price bands for the listed scrips starting March 10, 2026
- Orders placed outside the new price band limits will be rejected by the exchange system
- Members should update their trading systems and alert clients holding positions in these scrips

## Important Dates

- **March 9, 2026**: Circular issued
- **March 10, 2026**: Revised price bands become effective

## Impact Assessment

The revision affects 17 scrips with varying implications. Scrips moved to tighter bands (2%) such as Beryl Securities, CDG Petchem, Citi Port Financial Services, and IB Infotech Enterprises will face significantly restricted intraday trading ranges, likely indicating heightened surveillance concern. Arco Leasing Ltd receives the widest band at 20%, suggesting relaxation of restrictions. The majority of scrips are set at 10% or 5%, representing moderate surveillance adjustments. Traders and investors holding positions in these scrips should be aware of the tightened or relaxed limits before the March 10 open.
