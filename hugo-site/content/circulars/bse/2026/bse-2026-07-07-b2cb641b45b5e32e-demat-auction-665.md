## Summary

BSE Limited has invited offers from trading members of the Exchange for purchase of securities in Auction in respect of Rolling Settlement No. DR-665/2026-27. This is a routine settlement/RMS notice governing the auction process for securities that were short-delivered in the settlement.

## Key Points

- Auction relates to Rolling Settlement No. DR-665/2026-27
- Auction offer entry window: 02:00 p.m. to 02:45 p.m. on Tuesday, July 07, 2026
- Auction matching: 02:45 p.m. to 03:00 p.m.
- Delivery statement and report of accepted auction offers: 03:00 p.m. to 03:30 p.m.
- Money statement and final reports: 03:35 p.m. to 05:30 p.m.
- Pay-in of the auction to be held on Wednesday, July 08, 2026 at 08:00 a.m.
- Under the Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment, settlement will be done through the Clearing Member of a Trading Member
- Restriction on trading members offering securities in auction in the settlement in which they short-delivered has been removed
- No corrections permitted for punching errors in securities code and rate; Exchange reserves right to accept/reject any offer without reason
- Trading members should refer to cutoff rates on the Auction Offer Entry Screen and check for Corporate Action notices (Ex-Rights/Ex-Bonus/Ex-Split etc.)

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine operational notice for a scheduled auction under existing settlement rules. The notice reiterates that, following implementation of the Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment, the restriction preventing a trading member from offering securities in an auction for a settlement in which it had short-delivered those securities has been removed.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and custodians must check their auction pay-in obligations for securities
- Securities pay-in must be executed in depositories within depository cut-off timings
- Trading members must exercise care while entering securities codes and rates, as no corrections are permitted after punching errors
- Members should refer to notices on Corporate Actions (Ex-Rights/Ex-Bonus/Ex-Split) affecting relevant scrips before submitting offers

## Important Dates

- Auction offer entry: 02:00 p.m. - 02:45 p.m., Tuesday, July 07, 2026
- Auction matching: 02:45 p.m. - 03:00 p.m., July 07, 2026
- Delivery statement/report: 03:00 p.m. - 03:30 p.m., July 07, 2026
- Money statement and final reports: 03:35 p.m. - 05:30 p.m., July 07, 2026
- Auction pay-in: 08:00 a.m., Wednesday, July 08, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, recurring settlement/RMS notice with limited market-wide impact. It affects only trading members and custodians involved in the specific rolling settlement (DR-665/2026-27) who need to participate in or fulfill obligations related to the auction of short-delivered securities. No direct impact on retail investors or the broader market is expected.
