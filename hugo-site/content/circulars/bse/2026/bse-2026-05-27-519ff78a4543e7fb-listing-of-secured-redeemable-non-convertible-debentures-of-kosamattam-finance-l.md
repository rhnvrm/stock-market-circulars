## Summary

BSE has admitted Secured Redeemable Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of Kosamattam Finance Limited to dealings on the Exchange under the **F GROUP** (debt segment), effective **Friday, May 29, 2026**. Trading members are hereby notified of the listing and associated operational details.

## Key Points

- Secured Redeemable Non-Convertible Debentures of Kosamattam Finance Limited are listed in the **F GROUP** from May 29, 2026.
- Detailed securities information is provided in Annexure I to the notice.
- Trading is permitted **only in dematerialized form**, per SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000.
- Market lot is **one bond** (unit market lot).
- Kosamattam Finance Limited's financial year ends on **31st March**.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. The listing follows existing SEBI guidelines mandating dematerialized trading for bonds.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all trades in these NCDs are executed exclusively in dematerialized form.
- Trades must be in unit market lots (one bond per lot).
- For clarifications, trading members may contact Kosamattam Finance Limited's Compliance Officer (Sreenath Palakkattillam, cs@kosamattam.com, +91 481 258 6506) or BSE's Mr. Hardik Bhuta (+91 22 2272 8814).

## Important Dates

- **May 27, 2026**: Notice date.
- **May 29, 2026**: Effective date of admission to dealings on BSE.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt securities listing with limited market impact. It provides liquidity to holders of Kosamattam Finance Limited NCDs on the BSE debt platform. The issuer is a Kerala-based NBFC (gold loan company) registered in Kottayam. RTA services are provided by KFIN Technologies Limited, Mumbai.
