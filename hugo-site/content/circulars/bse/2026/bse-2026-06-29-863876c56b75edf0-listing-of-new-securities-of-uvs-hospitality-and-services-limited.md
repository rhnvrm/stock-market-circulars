## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 14,90,000 new equity shares of UVS Hospitality and Services Limited (Scrip Code: 531652, ISIN: INE528C01018) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Tuesday, June 30, 2026. These shares were issued at Rs. 17/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- + premium of Rs. 7/-) to Non Promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 14,90,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 7/- (issue price Rs. 17/-) to Non Promoters on preferential basis
- Shares issued pursuant to conversion of warrants
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 38243101 to 39733100
- Allotment dates: 7,40,000 shares on 10/11/2025 and 7,50,000 shares on 11/11/2025
- All 14,90,000 new shares are subject to lock-in until 31/03/2027
- Trading permitted from June 30, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification under BSE's Listing Operations framework for newly issued securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to update their systems to reflect the new shares eligible for trading from June 30, 2026
- Market participants should note the lock-in restriction: all 14,90,000 shares (distribution numbers 38243101 to 39733100) are locked in until 31/03/2027 and cannot be traded until that date

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date 1:** 10 November 2025 — 7,40,000 shares allotted
- **Allotment Date 2:** 11 November 2025 — 7,50,000 shares allotted
- **Trading Commencement:** 30 June 2026 (Tuesday)
- **Lock-in Expiry:** 31 March 2027 — all 14,90,000 new shares become freely tradable

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 14,90,000 new equity shares to the tradable float of UVS Hospitality and Services Limited, representing a preferential allotment to non-promoters via warrant conversion. The issue price of Rs. 17/- (face value Rs. 10/- + Rs. 7/- premium) establishes the conversion rate for these warrants. Since all new shares carry a lock-in until March 31, 2027, there is no immediate increase in free-float supply. The dilutive impact on existing shareholders is moderate, and the effect on market price will depend on the company's total outstanding share capital relative to this issuance. Trading members should update their records accordingly ahead of the June 30, 2026 effective date.
