## Summary

BSE Limited, through ICCL (Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd.), has issued a notice inviting offers from trading members for the purchase of securities in Demat Auction 635, pertaining to Rolling Settlement No. DR-635/2026-27. The auction session is scheduled for Friday, May 22, 2026, with pay-in on Monday, May 25, 2026.

## Key Points

- Demat Auction 635 is for Rolling Settlement No. DR-635/2026-27 in the Equity segment
- Auction offer entry is open from 2:00 PM to 2:45 PM on May 22, 2026
- Auction matching takes place from 2:45 PM to 3:00 PM
- Delivery statements and accepted auction offer reports are available from 3:00 PM to 3:30 PM
- Money statements and final reports are available from 3:35 PM to 5:30 PM
- Settlement of clearing members is now applicable under the Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment
- The restriction on trading members offering securities in auctions for settlements in which they have short delivered has been removed
- No corrections will be permitted for punching errors in securities code or rate

## Regulatory Changes

- Under the Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment, settlement is now routed through the Clearing Member of a Trading Member
- The prior restriction on trading members participating in auctions for settlements where they had short deliveries has been lifted

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and custodians must check their auction pay-in obligations for securities
- Securities pay-in must be executed in depositories within depository cut-off timings on May 25, 2026
- Trading members must refer to the cutoff rate on the "Auction offer Entry Screen" before submitting offers
- Trading members should refer to notices on Corporate Actions (Ex-Rights, Ex-Bonus, Ex-Split, etc.) issued by respective departments
- Utmost care must be taken while entering securities code and rate as no corrections will be allowed

## Important Dates

- **May 22, 2026 (Friday):** Auction session — offer entry (2:00–2:45 PM), matching (2:45–3:00 PM), delivery statements (3:00–3:30 PM), final reports (3:35–5:30 PM)
- **May 25, 2026 (Monday):** Auction pay-in at 8:00 AM

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine demat auction notice affecting trading members and custodians with short delivery obligations in Rolling Settlement DR-635/2026-27. The removal of the restriction on offering securities in auctions for settlements with short deliveries is a notable operational update that provides greater flexibility to trading members. Members with auction obligations must ensure timely securities pay-in on May 25, 2026 to avoid penalties.
