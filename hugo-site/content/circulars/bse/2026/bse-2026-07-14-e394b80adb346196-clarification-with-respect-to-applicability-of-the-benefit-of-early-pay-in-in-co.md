## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (No. 20260714-6, dated July 14, 2026) forwarding a SEBI circular (Ref. No. HO/47/16/13(4)2026-MRD-POD1/I/14266/2026 dated June 19, 2026) that clarifies the applicability of the benefit of early pay-in of securities in the Commodity Derivatives Segment. The full SEBI circular is enclosed as Annexure_A. Members have been asked to note the contents and ensure compliance.

## Key Points

- SEBI has issued a clarification specifically addressing how the early pay-in benefit applies within the Commodity Derivatives Segment.
- BSE's Department of Surveillance and Supervision (DOSS) has circulated this clarification to trading members for awareness and compliance.
- The detailed clarification is contained in the attached Annexure_A (SEBI circular), not reproduced in the notice text itself.
- The notice falls under the Compliance category and applies broadly (General segment).

## Regulatory Changes

The underlying SEBI circular dated June 19, 2026 clarifies the scope and applicability of the early pay-in benefit in the Commodity Derivatives Segment. No changes are introduced by BSE itself; this notice is purely a pass-through communication of SEBI's clarification to members.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to review the enclosed SEBI circular (Annexure_A) and ensure their operations align with the clarified applicability of the early pay-in benefit in the Commodity Derivatives Segment. Queries can be directed to BSE's Member Oversight team via the contact numbers or email (Bse.inspection@bseindia.com) provided in the notice.

## Important Dates

- SEBI circular date: June 19, 2026
- BSE notice date: July 14, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a procedural/compliance notice targeted at trading members active in the Commodity Derivatives Segment. It does not directly affect listed stocks, trading in equities, or broader market operations, and carries low market impact. Members dealing in commodity derivatives should review the clarification to ensure correct application of the early pay-in benefit in their compliance processes.
