## Summary

BSE has issued a notice reminding Research Analysts (RAs) registered under SEBI (Research Analysts) Regulations, 2014 of the mandatory five-yearly renewal process for their registration. As per Regulation 11 read with Schedule II of the Regulations, RAs must pay renewal fees to both SEBI and BSE Limited (which is recognized by SEBI as the Research Analyst Administration and Supervisory Body - RAASB) within a window commencing three months prior to expiry of registration validity.

## Key Points

- Every registered RA must pay a renewal fee to SEBI every five years to keep registration valid.
- BSE, as the recognized RAASB under Regulation 14, also requires a separate renewal fee payment every five years.
- SEBI renewal fee challan is generated only after the BSE renewal fee payment is confirmed.
- Renewal fee payment window: three months prior to expiry of registration validity.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory change; this is a reiteration of existing renewal requirements under Regulation 11 read with Schedule II of the SEBI (Research Analysts) Regulations, 2014, and BSE's role as RAASB under Regulation 14.

## Compliance Requirements

**A. Renewal Fee Payable to SEBI**
- Individuals and Partnership Firms: ₹1,000
- Body Corporates, including LLPs: ₹5,000
(Taxes as applicable)

**B. Renewal Fee Payable to BSE Limited**
- Individuals and Partnership Firms: ₹9,000
- Body Corporates, including LLPs: ₹4,95,000
(Taxes as applicable)

**Renewal Fee Payment Process:**
1. Step 1: Pay applicable renewal fee to BSE Limited within the prescribed timeline.
2. Step 2: Upon confirmation of BSE fee payment, SEBI renewal fee challan is generated and shared with the RA.
3. Step 3: RA pays the prescribed SEBI renewal fee within the SEBI-specified timeline and submits proof of payment.

**Important Points:**
- SEBI challan generation is contingent on prior BSE fee payment confirmation.
- Delay in BSE fee payment delays the entire renewal process.
- Failure to pay SEBI renewal fee within timeline may result in non-renewal or cancellation of registration.
- Requests for renewal after expiry of registration validity will not be considered, regardless of reason.
- RAs should initiate the renewal process well in advance of expiry.

## Important Dates

Renewal fee payment window commences three months prior to expiry of registration validity; specific expiry dates vary by individual RA registration. No renewal requests will be entertained after expiry.

## Impact Assessment

This notice has no direct impact on listed securities or broader market trading. It affects only individual Research Analysts and RA firms registered with BSE/SEBI, who must ensure timely compliance with the two-step renewal fee payment process (BSE followed by SEBI) to avoid lapse or cancellation of their registration. For inquiries, RAs may contact ra.membership@bseindia.com.
