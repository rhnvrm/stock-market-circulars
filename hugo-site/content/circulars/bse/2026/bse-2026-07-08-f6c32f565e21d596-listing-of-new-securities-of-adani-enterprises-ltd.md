## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 5,20,29,136 new equity shares of Adani Enterprises Ltd. (Scrip Code: 512599, ISIN: INE423A01024), issued at Rs. 1/- each and allotted to Qualified Institutional Buyers (QIBs) pursuant to a Qualified Institutional Placement (QIP), have been listed and admitted for trading with effect from Thursday, July 9, 2026.

## Key Points

- Security: Adani Enterprises Ltd, Scrip Code 512599, ISIN INE423A01024
- 5,20,29,136 equity shares of Rs. 1/- each allotted to QIBs via Qualified Institutional Placement
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Issue Price: Rs. 2883/- per share
- Distinctive Numbers: 1356525481 to 1408554616
- Notice No. 20260708-34 issued by BSE Listing Operations department

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a standard listing approval notice under BSE Listing Operations.

## Compliance Requirements

No action required from trading members other than updating trading systems to reflect the newly listed shares from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: July 7, 2026
- Listing/Trading Effective Date: Thursday, July 9, 2026
- Notice Date: July 8, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing increases the free-float equity base of Adani Enterprises Ltd following the QIP allotment. While this is a routine procedural listing action, it marginally dilutes existing shareholding and adds to tradable supply at an issue price of Rs. 2883/- per share. Market impact is expected to be limited given QIP shares are typically absorbed by institutional investors already factored into recent price discovery.
