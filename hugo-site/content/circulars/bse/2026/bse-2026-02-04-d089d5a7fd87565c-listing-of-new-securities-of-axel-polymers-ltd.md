## Summary

Axel Polymers Ltd. (Scrip Code: 513642) has listed 24,84,444 new equity shares on BSE effective February 5, 2026. These shares were issued on a preferential basis to non-promoters at Rs. 45/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- with premium of Rs. 35/-). The shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares and are subject to lock-in until August 10, 2026.

## Key Points

- 24,84,444 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at premium of Rs. 35/-
- Issue price: Rs. 45/- per share
- Allotted to non-promoters on preferential basis
- Date of allotment: December 13, 2025
- Trading commences: February 5, 2026
- Distinctive numbers: 8516681 to 11001124
- ISIN: INE197C01012
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the listing of new securities
- Lock-in restrictions apply to all 24,84,444 shares until August 10, 2026
- Shares cannot be transferred or traded freely until lock-in period expires

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: December 13, 2025
- **Trading Start Date**: February 5, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry**: August 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing event with limited market impact. The preferential allotment increases the paid-up capital of Axel Polymers Ltd. by 24,84,444 shares. The lock-in period until August 10, 2026 ensures these shares will not create immediate selling pressure. Trading members should update their records to reflect the expanded share capital and distinctive number ranges.
