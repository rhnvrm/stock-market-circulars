## Summary

BSE has issued a notice advising all trading members to refrain from dealing in the partly paid equity shares of Eco Hotels and Resorts Limited (scrip code 890225) with effect from 29 May 2026. The restriction is in connection with a record date fixed by the company to identify partly paid shareholders eligible to pay the Third and Final Call on those shares.

## Key Points

- Trading members must not deal in partly paid equity shares of Eco Hotels and Resorts Limited (code 890225) from 29 May 2026
- Record date is 29 May 2026 for ascertaining shareholders eligible for the Third and Final Call
- Call amount: Rs. 3.80 per share (Rs. 2.50 towards Face Value + Rs. 1.30 towards Securities Premium)
- Settlement reference: DR-640/2026-2027
- Notice issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President – Listing Compliance and Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No permanent regulatory changes. This is a one-time trading restriction specific to the partly paid equity shares of Eco Hotels and Resorts Limited tied to a corporate action event.

## Compliance Requirements

- All trading members of BSE must cease dealings in the partly paid equity shares of Eco Hotels and Resorts Limited (scrip code 890225) effective 29 May 2026
- Members should note the settlement number DR-640/2026-2027 for reference

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 26 May 2026
- **Record Date:** 29 May 2026
- **No Dealings From:** 29 May 2026 (Settlement DR-640/2026-2027)

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited to trading members holding or transacting in the partly paid equity shares of Eco Hotels and Resorts Limited. The restriction applies only to the partly paid series of this scrip. Eligible shareholders will be required to pay the Third and Final Call of Rs. 3.80 per partly paid share, after which the shares will convert to fully paid equity shares. Market-wide impact is negligible given the narrow scope.
