## Summary

BSE has listed further securities issued by companies under Employee Stock Option Plan (ESOP) or Employee Stock Option Scheme (ESOS). The circular provides details of 9,960 shares across three tranches with varying lock-in provisions.

## Key Points

- Total of 9,960 shares listed across three separate ranges
- Two tranches (4,846 and 135 shares) have one-year lock-in period
- One tranche (4,979 shares) has no lock-in restrictions
- Share range covered: 51821983 to 51831942

## Lock-in Details

**Tranche 1:**
- Shares: 4,846
- Range: 51821983 to 51826828
- Lock-in: Yes
- Lock-in Period: 13-01-2026 to 12-01-2027

**Tranche 2:**
- Shares: 4,979
- Range: 51826829 to 51831807
- Lock-in: No

**Tranche 3:**
- Shares: 135
- Range: 51831808 to 51831942
- Lock-in: Yes
- Lock-in Period: 13-01-2026 to 12-01-2027

## Compliance Requirements

- Locked-in shares cannot be traded or transferred until the lock-in period expires
- Lock-in provisions apply from January 13, 2026 to January 12, 2027 for applicable tranches

## Important Dates

- Listing Date: January 22, 2026
- Lock-in Start Date: January 13, 2026
- Lock-in End Date: January 12, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine listing of employee stock options. The lock-in provisions on majority of shares (4,981 out of 9,960) ensure controlled release into the market over the next year.
