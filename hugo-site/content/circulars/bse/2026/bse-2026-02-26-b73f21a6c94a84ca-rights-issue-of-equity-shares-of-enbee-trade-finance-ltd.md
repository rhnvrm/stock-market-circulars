## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that ENBEE TRADE & FINANCE LTD. (Code: 512441) has fixed a record date of March 4, 2026 for its rights issue. Equity shares of the company will trade on an ex-rights basis effective from March 4, 2026 under settlement DR-832/2025-2026. The issue offers 21 new equity shares for every 10 shares held, at Re.1/- per share (at par).

## Key Points

- Company: ENBEE TRADE & FINANCE LTD. (BSE Code: 512441)
- Rights ratio: 21 new equity shares for every 10 equity shares held
- Issue price: Re.1/- per share (face value, issued at par)
- Record date: March 4, 2026
- Ex-rights effective date: March 4, 2026
- Settlement number: DR-832/2025-2026
- Full issue price of Re.1/- per share is payable at the time of application

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular is a standard corporate action notification in accordance with existing BSE listing and trading regulations governing rights issues.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all transactions in ENBEE TRADE & FINANCE LTD. equity shares are executed on an ex-rights basis from March 4, 2026 onwards.
- Members should note that the entire issue price of Re.1/- per share must be paid at the time of making the application; no deferred payment is permitted.
- Trading members are advised to update their systems and inform clients accordingly ahead of the record date.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** February 26, 2026
- **Record Date:** March 4, 2026
- **Ex-Rights Effective Date:** March 4, 2026
- **Settlement Reference:** DR-832/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

The rights issue will result in potential dilution of existing shareholding if shareholders do not subscribe. Shares will trade ex-rights from March 4, 2026, which may cause a price adjustment on that date. The ratio of 21:10 represents a significant issue size relative to existing capital, which could impact the share price and liquidity around the record date. Impact is limited to existing shareholders and traders in ENBEE TRADE & FINANCE LTD. (512441) and does not affect broader market operations.
