## Summary

BSE has notified members of a revision in the minimum application amount for Edelweiss Liquid Fund and Edelweiss Overnight Fund on the BSE StAR MF platform. Effective May 15, 2026, the minimum purchase amount (including minimum additional purchase and SIP/STP-In) has been increased from Rs. 100 to Rs. 1,000 (in multiples of Re. 1/- thereafter). The change has already been implemented on the BSE StAR MF platform.

## Key Points

- Minimum application amount revised upward from Rs. 100 to Rs. 1,000 for both schemes
- Applies to: Minimum Application Amount, Minimum Additional Purchase Amount, Systematic Investment Plan (SIP), and Systematic Transfer Plan-In (STP-In)
- Multiples remain the same: Re. 1/- thereafter
- Change mandated by Edelweiss AMC via a Notice-cum-Addendum
- Already implemented on the BSE StAR MF platform as of the notice date

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework introduced. This is an AMC-driven product parameter change communicated through BSE to its members. The Notice-cum-Addendum from Edelweiss AMC is attached to the circular for reference.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and distributors on the BSE StAR MF platform must ensure all new transactions for Edelweiss Liquid Fund and Edelweiss Overnight Fund meet the revised minimum of Rs. 1,000
- Existing SIPs and STPs below the revised threshold may need to be reviewed based on Edelweiss AMC's addendum terms

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** May 14, 2026
- **Effective Date:** May 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited and operational in nature. Investors or distributors initiating new purchases below Rs. 1,000 will be rejected on the BSE StAR MF platform. The tenfold increase in the minimum amount may marginally reduce retail participation in these schemes but is unlikely to affect existing investors with compliant SIPs. No broader market impact is anticipated.
