## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in specific non-convertible debentures (NCDs) of Krazybee Services Ltd effective January 15, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the no-dealings date.

## Key Points

- Company: Krazybee Services Ltd
- Debenture Series: KSL-11%-30-1-26-PVT
- ISIN: INE07HK07767
- Scrip Code: 975869
- Record Date: January 15, 2026
- No Dealings From: January 15, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Notice Reference: DR-799/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard procedural suspension aligned with corporate action requirements.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures (ISIN: INE07HK07767) from January 15, 2026 onwards
- Trading members are required to take note of the suspension and inform their clients holding these debentures
- No fresh positions can be created in these debentures after the specified date

## Important Dates

| Date | Event |
|------|-------|
| January 9, 2026 | Circular issue date |
| January 15, 2026 | Record date for redemption and interest payment |
| January 15, 2026 | Trading suspension effective date (no dealings) |

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - The suspension affects only a single series of privately placed debentures of Krazybee Services Ltd. This is a routine corporate action related to scheduled redemption and interest payment.

**Investor Impact:** Holders of these debentures will receive their redemption proceeds and interest payment as per the company's schedule. Trading is suspended to ensure proper settlement of the corporate action.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members need to update their systems to block trades in this specific ISIN and communicate the suspension to affected clients. The impact is limited to holders of this particular debenture series.
