## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation consequent upon the conversion of Niwas Housing Finance Private Limited to a public company. The company, originally incorporated on January 1, 2016 as Indostar Home Finance Private Limited, has been converted to a public company under Section 18 of the Companies Act, 2013, with approval from the Registrar of Companies (ROC), Central Processing Centre (CPC) via SRN AC0878110 dated December 31, 2025. The company's name has been changed to Niwas Housing Finance Limited effective December 31, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company originally incorporated as Indostar Home Finance Private Limited on January 1, 2016
- Corporate Identity Number (CIN): U65990MH2016PLC271587
- Converted from private to public company under Section 18 of the Companies Act, 2013
- New name: Niwas Housing Finance Limited (from Niwas Housing Finance Private Limited)
- Approval granted by ROC, CPC vide SRN AC0878110
- Certificate digitally signed by M. Yadubhushana Rao, Assistant Registrar of Companies
- Registered office: Unit No.305, 3rd Floor, Wing 2/E, Corporate Avenue, Andheri-Ghatkopar Link Road, Chakala, Mumbai City - 400093, Maharashtra

## Regulatory Changes

The company has undergone a statutory conversion from a private limited company to a public limited company in accordance with Section 18 of the Companies Act, 2013. This conversion requires approval from the Central Government (signified through the ROC) and results in changes to the company's constitutional structure, governance requirements, and regulatory compliance obligations.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance actions required from market participants or investors. The company will now be subject to public company regulations under the Companies Act, 2013, including enhanced disclosure requirements, board composition norms, and other statutory obligations applicable to public limited companies.

## Important Dates

- **January 1, 2016**: Original date of incorporation as Indostar Home Finance Private Limited
- **December 31, 2025**: Approval date (SRN AC0878110) and effective date of conversion to public company
- **December 31, 2025**: Name change from Niwas Housing Finance Private Limited to Niwas Housing Finance Limited
- **January 30, 2026**: Circular disclosure date

## Impact Assessment

This is an administrative and regulatory disclosure with minimal immediate market impact. The conversion from private to public company status indicates potential future plans for capital raising, public listing, or broader stakeholder participation. The name change reflects the company's new public company status. Market participants should note the updated company name and CIN for future correspondence and transactions. No immediate trading or investment action is required based on this disclosure.
