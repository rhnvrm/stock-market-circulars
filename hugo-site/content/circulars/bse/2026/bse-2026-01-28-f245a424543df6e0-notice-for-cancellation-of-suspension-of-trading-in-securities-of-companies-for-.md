## Summary

BSE has issued a notice canceling the trading suspension for Benara Bearings and Pistons Ltd (Scrip Code: 541178) effective January 30, 2026. This follows the company's compliance with provisions of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015, for which the suspension was originally imposed.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension will be cancelled for Benara Bearings and Pistons Ltd (Scrip Code: 541178)
- Effective date: January 30, 2026
- Suspension was originally imposed for non-compliance with SEBI LODR Regulations 2015
- This notice is in continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20251230-27 dated December 30, 2025
- Market participants are notified of the resumption of normal trading

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular represents the reversal of a previous suspension action following the company's return to compliance with existing SEBI LODR Regulations 2015.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants should update their systems to reflect the cancellation of suspension
- Normal trading operations can resume for scrip code 541178 from January 30, 2026
- No additional compliance actions required from market participants
- For clarifications, participants can contact BSE at bse.soplodr@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 28, 2026
- **Effective Date**: January 30, 2026 - Trading suspension cancellation becomes effective
- **Reference Notice**: December 30, 2025 - Original suspension notice (20251230-27)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited impact on broader market operations. Affects only shareholders and traders of Benara Bearings and Pistons Ltd.

**Operational Impact**: Positive development for the affected company. Trading can resume normally, restoring liquidity for shareholders and investors.

**Investor Impact**: Beneficial for existing shareholders who can now trade their holdings. Demonstrates the company's successful remediation of compliance issues.

**Regulatory Significance**: Indicates effective enforcement and compliance restoration mechanism under SEBI LODR framework.
