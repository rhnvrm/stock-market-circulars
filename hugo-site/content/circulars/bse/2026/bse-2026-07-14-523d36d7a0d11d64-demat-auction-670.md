## Summary

BSE Limited has issued a notice inviting offers from trading members of the Exchange for the purchase of securities in Auction in respect of Rolling Settlement No. DR-670/2026-27. This is a routine short-delivery auction process conducted through Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd.

## Key Points

- Auction offer entry window: 02:00 p.m. to 02:45 p.m. on Tuesday, July 14, 2026
- Auction matching: 02:45 p.m. to 03:00 p.m.
- Delivery statement and report of accepted auction offers: 03:00 p.m. to 03:30 p.m.
- Money statement and final reports: 03:35 p.m. to 05:30 p.m.
- Auction pay-in scheduled for Wednesday, July 15, 2026 at 08:00 a.m.
- Under the Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment, settlement will be done through the Clearing Member of a Trading Member
- The earlier restriction on trading members offering securities in auction for settlements where they had short-delivered such securities has been removed
- Trading members must exercise care while entering securities codes and rates, as punching errors will not be corrected
- The Exchange reserves the right to accept or reject any offer without assigning reasons

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this notice references the previously implemented Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment and reiterates the removal of restrictions on trading members offering securities in auction for settlements in which they had short-delivered such securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and Custodians must check their auction pay-in obligations for securities and execute securities pay-in within depository cut-off timings
- Trading members should refer to the cutoff rate on the Auction Offer Entry Screen before submitting offers
- Members should review notices on Corporate Actions (Ex-Rights/Ex-Bonus/Ex-Split etc.) affecting relevant scrips before placing auction offers

## Important Dates

- Auction offer entry, matching and reporting: Tuesday, July 14, 2026 (02:00 p.m. to 05:30 p.m.)
- Auction pay-in: Wednesday, July 15, 2026 at 08:00 a.m.

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard operational/administrative notice concerning the settlement auction mechanism for short-delivered securities under Rolling Settlement DR-670/2026-27. It affects only trading members and custodians involved in the auction process and carries no direct market-wide or stock-specific impact beyond routine settlement operations.
