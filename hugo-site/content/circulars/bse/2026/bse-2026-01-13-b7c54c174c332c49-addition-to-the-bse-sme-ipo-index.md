## Summary

GABION TECHNOLOGIES INDIA LIMITED (Exchange ticker: 544675) will be added to the BSE SME IPO Index effective at the open of Wednesday, January 14, 2026. The stock is being listed on BSE effective Tuesday, January 13, 2026.

## Key Points

- GABION TECHNOLOGIES INDIA LIMITED (ticker: 544675) to be added to BSE SME IPO Index
- Stock listing on BSE: January 13, 2026
- Index addition effective date: January 14, 2026 (at market open)
- Action included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- This follows reference to Notice No. 20260112-35

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index constituent update.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements. Market participants should note the index composition change for rebalancing and tracking purposes.

## Important Dates

- **January 13, 2026**: Stock listing on BSE
- **January 14, 2026**: Addition to BSE SME IPO Index (effective at market open)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine index addition with minimal market impact. Index tracking funds and ETFs benchmarked to the BSE SME IPO Index will need to adjust their portfolios to include GABION TECHNOLOGIES INDIA LIMITED. For queries, market participants can contact bseindex@bseindia.com.
