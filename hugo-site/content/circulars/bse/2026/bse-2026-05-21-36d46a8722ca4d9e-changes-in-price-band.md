## Summary

BSE's Department of Surveillance (DOSS) has revised the price bands for five equity scrips effective May 22, 2026. Three scrips have been tightened to a 2% band, indicating heightened surveillance concerns, while Autoline Industries moves to 5% and Candour Techtex to 10%.

## Key Points

- Price band revisions apply to five scrips across different band levels (2%, 5%, and 10%)
- Changes are effective from May 22, 2026
- Revisions are initiated by BSE Surveillance department (DOSS)
- No attachments accompany this notice
- Members may seek clarification at bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

The following price band revisions have been made:

| Scrip Code | Scrip Name | Revised Price Band |
|---|---|---|
| 532797 | Autoline Industries Ltd | 5% |
| 522292 | Candour Techtex Ltd | 10% |
| 543286 | Jetmall Spices and Masala Ltd | 2% |
| 532780 | Parsvnath Developers Ltd | 2% |
| 543970 | Shoora Designs Ltd | 2% |

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must adhere to the revised price bands for the listed scrips from May 22, 2026 onwards
- Orders placed outside the new price band limits will be rejected by the exchange trading system
- Members should update their risk management and order management systems accordingly

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** May 21, 2026
- **Effective Date:** May 22, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The tightening of price bands to 2% for Jetmall Spices, Parsvnath Developers, and Shoora Designs indicates BSE Surveillance is monitoring these scrips for unusual price movements or trading activity. A 2% band is among the most restrictive applied by BSE, significantly limiting intraday price movement. Candour Techtex's 10% band suggests a relatively relaxed restriction, possibly a step-down from a tighter prior band. Autoline Industries at 5% falls in the mid-range. Traders and investors in these scrips should expect constrained price discovery and potential liquidity reduction during the period the bands remain in effect.
