## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 58,00,000 new equity shares of Achyut Healthcare Limited (Scrip Code: 543499, ISIN: INE0K1401020) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Friday, May 22, 2026. These shares were issued at Rs. 6/- per share (face value Re. 1/- at a premium of Rs. 5/-) to Promoters and Non-Promoters on a preferential basis and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 58,00,000 equity shares of face value Re. 1/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 5/- (Issue Price: Rs. 6/-)
- Issued to Promoters and Non-Promoters on a preferential basis
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution Numbers: 235557001 to 241357000
- Date of Allotment: 23 March 2026
- Trading commences: Friday, 22 May 2026
- ISIN: INE0K1401020

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notice for newly allotted preferential shares.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities and may facilitate trading from May 22, 2026. Lock-in restrictions must be observed as specified below.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** 23 March 2026
- **Trading Effective From:** 22 May 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (38,00,000 shares):** 15 December 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (20,00,000 shares):** 15 December 2027

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing event with limited broad market impact. The 58,00,000 new shares increase the tradable float of Achyut Healthcare Limited. However, 20,00,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 235557001–237557000) are locked in until 15 December 2027 and 38,00,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 237557001–241357000) are locked in until 15 December 2026, limiting immediate supply-side pressure on the stock.
