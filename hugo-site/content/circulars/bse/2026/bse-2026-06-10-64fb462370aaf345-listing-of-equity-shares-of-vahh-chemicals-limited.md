## Summary

Vahh Chemicals Limited's equity shares will be listed and admitted to dealings on BSE under the 'MT' Group of Securities effective Thursday, June 11, 2026. The company conducted an SME IPO at an issue price of Rs. 60 per share (face value Rs. 10 + premium Rs. 50). The scrip will be in the Trade-for-Trade segment for 10 trading days as per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012.

## Key Points

- Scrip ID: VAHH | Scrip Code: 544778 | ISIN: INE0H3U01013
- Total securities: 8,305,868 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each, fully paid up
- Distinctive number range: 1 to 8,305,868
- Issue price: Rs. 60/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- + premium Rs. 50/-)
- Market lot: 2,000 equity shares
- Date of allotment: June 9, 2026
- Scrip will be part of Special Pre-open Session (SPOS) on June 11, 2026
- Trading only in dematerialised form as per SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The listing is governed by existing SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 (January 20, 2012), which mandates Trade-for-Trade segment for 10 trading days post-listing for SME scrips.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade this scrip only in dematerialised form
- Minimum trade size is 2,000 equity shares (one market lot); modifications require at least one month's prior market notice
- Lock-in details are as per Annexure I; shareholding pattern is as per Annexure II
- Corporate action for lock-in period has been marked in depository records

## Important Dates

- **June 9, 2026**: Date of allotment in the public issue
- **June 11, 2026**: Listing date; scrip admitted to dealings on BSE; SPOS participation
- **Trade-for-Trade period**: 10 trading days from June 11, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine SME IPO listing with limited broad market impact. Vahh Chemicals Limited is a small-cap company listed on the BSE SME platform. The Trade-for-Trade restriction for 10 days limits speculative activity in the initial period. Market maker is Mansi Share and Stock Broking Private Limited, and the registrar is KFin Technologies Limited. Impact is confined to IPO allottees, the market maker, and members dealing in SME securities.
