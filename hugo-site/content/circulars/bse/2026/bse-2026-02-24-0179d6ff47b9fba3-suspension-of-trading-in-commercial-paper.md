## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members that the Commercial Paper of Solar Defence and Aerospace Limited (Scrip Code: 730672, ISIN: INE02AL14023) will mature on February 25, 2026. Trading members are advised not to deal in this Commercial Paper from February 25, 2026 onwards.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper instrument SDAL-26-2-26-CP (ISIN: INE02AL14023) of Solar Defence and Aerospace Limited is set to mature on February 25, 2026.
- Scrip Code on BSE Debt Segment: 730672.
- Trading in this Commercial Paper is suspended with effect from February 25, 2026.
- Notice reference: DR-828/2025-2026.
- Issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President â€“ Listing Operations (CRD).

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice issued upon the scheduled maturity of a debt instrument under existing BSE rules governing the Debt Market Segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- All trading members of BSE must refrain from dealing in the Commercial Paper of Solar Defence and Aerospace Limited (Scrip Code: 730672, ISIN: INE02AL14023) with effect from February 25, 2026.
- Members should update their systems to reflect the suspension of this instrument.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** February 24, 2026
- **Suspension Effective Date:** February 25, 2026 (maturity/redemption date)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, low-impact notice. The suspension affects only the specific Commercial Paper instrument of Solar Defence and Aerospace Limited maturing on February 25, 2026. It has no broader market implications and is a standard administrative action that follows the scheduled redemption of a short-term debt instrument on the BSE Debt Segment.
