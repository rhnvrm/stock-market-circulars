## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) listed under Megraj Holdings Private Limited (Transferor Company) will be transferred to Green Gold Animation Private Limited (Transferee Company) with effect from Wednesday, June 10, 2026. The transfer is pursuant to an approved Scheme of Amalgamation between the two entities.

## Key Points

- Transfer effective date: Wednesday, June 10, 2026
- Transferor: Megraj Holdings Private Limited
- Transferee: Green Gold Animation Private Limited
- Security type: NCDs (Non-Convertible Debentures)
- Scrip code 974917 (ISIN: INE0NZ807013) is affected
- Scrip long name changes from `MPHL-7%-15-6-26-PVT` to `GAPL-7%-15-6-26-PVT`
- Abbreviated name and Scrip ID change from `7MHPL26` to `7GAPL26`

## Regulatory Changes

The scrip details for ISIN INE0NZ807013 are being updated on BSE systems to reflect the new transferee company (Green Gold Animation Private Limited) following the Scheme of Amalgamation. No change in ISIN or scrip code; only name and symbol identifiers are updated.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to note the updated scrip name, abbreviated name, and scrip ID effective June 10, 2026. Members should update their internal systems to reflect the new entity name (Green Gold Animation Private Limited) for this NCD.

## Important Dates

- **June 10, 2026**: Effective date of transfer of NCDs from Megraj Holdings Private Limited to Green Gold Animation Private Limited

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited to holders and trading members dealing in the specific NCD (scrip code 974917, ISIN INE0NZ807013). The security continues to exist with the same ISIN and scrip code but under the new issuer entity name. Members should ensure reference data is updated to avoid confusion in trade reporting and settlement. Contact: Abhishek Kadlak (+91 22 2272 8706, bse.schemes@bseindia.com).
