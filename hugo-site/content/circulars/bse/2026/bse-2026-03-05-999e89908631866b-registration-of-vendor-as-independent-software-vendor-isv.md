BSE has registered Mugenesys Software Private Limited (Vendor Code: 81) as an Independent Software Vendor (ISV) for providing Trading frontend services in the Equity Segment. Members are notified of this new registration per standard exchange communication.

No regulatory changes. This is a routine administrative registration of a new ISV by BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action required from members. Members may optionally engage with Mugenesys Software Private Limited for BSE-approved Trading frontend services in the Equity Segment.

- Notice Number: 20260305-31

Minimal market impact. This notice is informational, advising members of a newly registered ISV. Members seeking Trading frontend solutions for the Equity Segment now have an additional approved vendor option. The full list of registered ISVs is available at the BSE website.
