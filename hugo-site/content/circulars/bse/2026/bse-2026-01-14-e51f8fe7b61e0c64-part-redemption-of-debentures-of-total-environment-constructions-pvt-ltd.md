## Summary

BSE has notified that Total Environment Constructions Pvt Ltd has completed a part redemption of its debentures (ISIN: INE257Y07049, Scrip Code: 975621). Following this redemption, the face value of the debentures has been reduced to Rs. 92,857 per debenture. The debentures will trade with the new reduced face value starting January 16, 2026.

## Key Points

- Debenture ISIN: INE257Y07049 (TECPL-13.53%-31-3-29-PVT)
- Scrip Code: 975621
- New face value after part redemption: Rs. 92,857 per debenture
- Effective date for trading with reduced face value: January 16, 2026
- Notice reference: DR-800/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the reduced face value for the debentures
- All trades from January 16, 2026 onwards must reflect the new face value of Rs. 92,857 per debenture
- Trading members should update their systems accordingly

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 14, 2026
- **Effective Date**: January 16, 2026 - Debentures will trade with reduced face value

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action with limited market impact. It affects only holders of the specific debenture (ISIN: INE257Y07049) issued by Total Environment Constructions Pvt Ltd. The part redemption reduces the outstanding principal amount, and the face value adjustment reflects the proportional reduction. Trading members need to update their systems to reflect the new face value for accurate pricing and settlement.
