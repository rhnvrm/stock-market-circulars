## Summary

BSE has issued a revised schedule for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for P M TELELINNKS LIMITED (PMTELELIN). The offer will run from Tuesday, January 13, 2026 to Wednesday, January 28, 2026, with an offer price of Rs. 6.81 per share for up to 26,19,500 equity shares. Trading members and custodian members must follow the specified session timings and operational procedures for participation in the OTB module.

## Key Points

- Security: P M TELELINNKS LIMITED (Symbol: PMTELELIN, ISIN: INE092C01015, Scrip Code: 513403)
- Offer Size: Up to 26,19,500 equity shares
- Offer Price: Rs. 6.81 per share
- Face Value: Rs. 10.00
- Market Lot: 1
- Offer Period: January 13-28, 2026
- Bid Offering Session: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM daily
- Give-up/Take-up Period: January 12-27, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- Last day Give-up: January 28, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- Last day Take-up: January 28, 2026 (9:15 AM to 4:00 PM)

## Regulatory Changes

This is a revised schedule for an existing takeover offer. No new regulatory changes are introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure UCC of investors is registered/updated with correct PAN number and category in Exchange UCC database
- Trading members can only bid for investors having shares in demat form
- Members must refer to Exchange Notices 20150930-1 (September 30, 2015) and 20170203-29 (February 3, 2017) for operational instructions
- Access to OTB module available via iBBS platform (Internet: https://ibbs.bseindia.com/ or Leased Line: https://ibbsll.bseindia.com/)
- RTRMS platform access required (Internet: https://rtrms.bseindia.com/ or Leased Line: https://rtrmsll.bseindia.com/)
- Files on Extranet for acceptance of bids available after issue closure upon confirmation from Merchant Banker/RTA

- **Closing Date**: Wednesday, January 28, 2026
