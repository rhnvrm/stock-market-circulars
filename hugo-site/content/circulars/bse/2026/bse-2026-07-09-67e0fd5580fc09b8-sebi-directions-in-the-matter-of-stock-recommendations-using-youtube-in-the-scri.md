## Summary

BSE issued a notice to all trading members regarding SEBI's directions in the matter of stock recommendations made using YouTube in the scrip of Sadhna Broadcast Limited, concerning entity Varun Media Private Limited. This notice follows earlier Exchange notices dated November 01, 2023, May 29, 2025, and July 08, 2026. SEBI, via email dated July 09, 2026, has clarified that trading members should comply with the directions contained in the Final Order only.

## Key Points

- The notice pertains to unauthorized stock recommendations made via YouTube involving Varun Media Private Limited in the scrip of Sadhna Broadcast Limited.
- This is a continuation of prior Exchange notices: No. 20231101-12 (November 01, 2023), No. 20250529-47 (May 29, 2025), and No. 20260708-36 (July 08, 2026).
- SEBI has clarified via email dated July 09, 2026 that compliance is required only with the directions contained in the Final Order, not any confirmatory or interim orders.
- Attachments include the Confirmatory Order and Final Order relating to Sadhna Broadcast Limited.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory change; this is a clarificatory communication narrowing the compliance requirement to the Final Order issued by SEBI in this matter.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of the Exchange are advised to take note of SEBI's clarification and ensure compliance strictly with the directions contained in the Final Order in the matter of Varun Media Private Limited's stock recommendations concerning Sadhna Broadcast Limited.

## Important Dates

- November 01, 2023: Original Exchange Notice No. 20231101-12
- May 29, 2025: Exchange Notice No. 20250529-47
- July 08, 2026: Exchange Notice No. 20260708-36
- July 09, 2026: SEBI email clarifying compliance with Final Order; current notice issued

## Impact Assessment

This notice has limited direct market impact as it is a procedural clarification for trading members regarding compliance with an existing SEBI order. It does not introduce new trading restrictions or affect the listed status of Sadhna Broadcast Limited but reinforces regulatory oversight on unauthorized investment advice/recommendations disseminated via social media platforms like YouTube.
