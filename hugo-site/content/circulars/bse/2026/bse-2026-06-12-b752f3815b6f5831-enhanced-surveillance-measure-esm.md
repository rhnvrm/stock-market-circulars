## Summary

BSE has updated the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework under Notice No. 20260612-34 dated June 12, 2026. Securities listed in Annexure I have met the ESM inclusion criteria and will attract a minimum 100% margin on all open positions from June 16, 2026. Additionally, certain securities are being moved to a lower Stage (Stage I), while others shift to Stage II with a 2% price band and Periodic Call Auction. Securities exiting the ESM framework are listed in Annexure II.

## Key Points

- Securities in Annexure I will attract minimum 100% margin w.e.f. June 16, 2026 on all open positions as on June 15, 2026 and new positions created from June 16, 2026.
- Certain securities are being moved to a lower Stage (Stage I) under the ESM Framework w.e.f. June 15, 2026.
- Securities qualifying under ESM will be shifted from Rolling Settlement to Trade-for-Trade segment w.e.f. June 16, 2026.
- Securities shifting to Stage II will be subject to Trade-for-Trade with a 2% price band and Periodic Call Auction w.e.f. June 15, 2026.
- Securities moving out of the ESM Framework w.e.f. June 15, 2026 are listed in Annexure II.
- A consolidated list of all securities under the ESM framework is available in Annexure III.
- ESM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures.
- Shortlisting under ESM is purely for market surveillance purposes and should not be construed as an adverse action against the concerned company or entity.
- Upon exiting the framework, the price band of a scrip will revert to its pre-ESM price band, unless subject to another surveillance measure.

## Regulatory Changes

This notice references and updates the ESM framework established under prior notices: 20230602-44 (June 2, 2023), 20230718-46 (July 18, 2023), 20240809-42 (August 9, 2024), 20240920-63 (September 20, 2024), 20241004-65 (October 4, 2024), and 20250725-61 (July 25, 2025). No structural changes to the ESM framework are introduced; this is a periodic review updating the list of securities under the framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure minimum 100% margin is collected on all open positions in ESM-listed securities (Annexure I) as of June 15, 2026, and on all new positions from June 16, 2026.
- Members must adhere to Trade-for-Trade settlement obligations for securities shifting under ESM from June 16, 2026.
- For Stage II securities, members must comply with the 2% price band and Periodic Call Auction mechanism effective June 15, 2026.
- Members may direct clarification queries to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **June 15, 2026**: Effective date for securities moving to Stage I (lower stage), securities entering Stage II (Trade-for-Trade, 2% price band, Periodic Call Auction), and securities exiting the ESM framework (Annexure II).
- **June 16, 2026**: Effective date for 100% margin requirement on new ESM inclusions (Annexure I) and shift from Rolling Settlement to Trade-for-Trade segment for qualifying securities.

## Impact Assessment

The 100% margin requirement and mandatory Trade-for-Trade settlement significantly reduce leverage and liquidity for newly included securities, making short-term speculative trading considerably more expensive and restricted. Stage II securities face an additional constraint of a tight 2% price band with Periodic Call Auction, further limiting intraday price movement. Securities exiting the framework will see their pre-ESM price bands reinstated (unless covered by another surveillance measure), potentially improving liquidity. Market participants holding positions in affected securities as of June 15, 2026 must ensure adequate margin coverage before the June 16, 2026 effective date. The specific securities impacted are detailed in the attached Annexures I, II, and III of the original PDF notice.
