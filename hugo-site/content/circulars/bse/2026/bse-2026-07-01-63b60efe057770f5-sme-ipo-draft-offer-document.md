## Summary

BSE has notified that the Draft Offer Document of Unitec Fibres Limited, filed on June 29, 2026, containing disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations, has been made available on the Exchange's website for a period of at least 21 days to seek public comments ahead of its SME IPO.

## Key Points

- The Draft Offer Document pertains to Unitec Fibres Limited's proposed SME IPO.
- The document was filed on June 29, 2026.
- It is hosted on the BSE SME website for public review at www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx.
- The document will remain available for public comments for at least 21 days from the notice date.
- Public comments can be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is a disclosure notice made in compliance with Schedule VI of the SEBI ICDR (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Interested parties, investors, and stakeholders may review the Draft Offer Document and submit comments within the specified 21-day window.
- Comments should be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com within 21 days of the notice date.

## Important Dates

- Draft Offer Document filed: June 29, 2026
- Notice issued: July 1, 2026
- Public comment period: At least 21 days from the notice date (until on or after July 22, 2026)

## Impact Assessment

This is a procedural disclosure step in the SME IPO process for Unitec Fibres Limited, with limited immediate market impact. It signals an upcoming SME listing and provides a formal window for public scrutiny of the company's disclosures before further IPO processing.
