## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the equity shares of RECODE STUDIOS LIMITED will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange. The company is being listed on the SME segment under Scrip Code 544755, Symbol RECODE, and ISIN INE2B6701015. The precise date of listing and further security details will be communicated through a subsequent notice.

## Key Points

- RECODE STUDIOS LIMITED equity shares are approved for listing on BSE's SME segment.
- Scrip Code: 544755 | Symbol: RECODE | ISIN: INE2B6701015
- Actual listing date and security details to be announced separately.
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, DCS-Listing, BSE Limited.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this notice. It is a standard listing announcement under BSE's DCS-Listing department.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should take note of the upcoming listing and await the separate notice for the confirmed listing date and full security details before initiating any trades in RECODE STUDIOS LIMITED shares.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 11 May 2026
- **Listing Date:** To be announced via a separate notice.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notice for a new SME segment entrant. The impact is limited to trading members who will need to update their systems once the listing date is confirmed. Broader market impact is minimal given the SME classification of the company.
