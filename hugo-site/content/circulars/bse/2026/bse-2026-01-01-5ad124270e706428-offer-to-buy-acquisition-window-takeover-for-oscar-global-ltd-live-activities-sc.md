## Summary

BSE has announced the operational schedule for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for OSCAR GLOBAL LTD (OSCARGLO). The acquisition window will be open from January 2, 2026 to January 15, 2026. The offer is for up to 8,58,000 equity shares at Rs. 12.15 per share. Trading members and custodian members are provided with detailed session timings, bidding categories, and platform access URLs for participating in this OTB process.

## Key Points

- Offer size: Up to 8,58,000 equity shares of OSCAR GLOBAL LTD
- Offer price: Rs. 12.15 per share
- Face value: Rs. 10.00
- Security symbol: OSCARGLO (Scrip Code: 530713, ISIN: INE473F01010)
- Market lot: 1 share
- Bid offering period: January 2-15, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- Give-up/Take-up period: January 2-14, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- Last day give-up: January 15, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- Last day take-up: January 15, 2026 (9:15 AM to 4:00 PM)
- Platform access via iBBS, RTRMS, and Extranet (both Internet and Leased Line URLs provided)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This notice provides operational instructions for an upcoming takeover offer using BSE's existing OTB framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure UCC (Unique Client Code) of investors is registered and/or updated with correct PAN number and category in the Exchange UCC database
- Trading members can only bid for investors having shares in demat form
- Members must refer to Exchange Notices No. 20150930-1 (September 30, 2015) and 20170203-29 (February 3, 2017) for operational instructions for OTB participation
- Members and custodians should note that if no securities are accepted, no End of Issue files will be generated in Extranet

## Important Dates

- **January 2, 2026**: Offer opening date and start of bid offering period
- **January 2-14, 2026**: Give-up/Take-up period (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **January 15, 2026**: Offer closing date
  - Give-up (last day): 9:15 AM - 3:30 PM
  - Take-up (last day): 9:15 AM - 4:00 PM

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a company-specific takeover offer affecting only OSCAR GLOBAL LTD shareholders and does not impact broader market operations.

**Operational Impact**: Medium for participating members - Trading members and custodians need to ensure their systems and UCC databases are properly configured to facilitate client participation in the OTB process. The notice provides comprehensive contact information for technical support related to iBBS, RTRMS, and Extranet platform access.

**Investor Categories Eligible**: Foreign Institutional Buyers (FII), Financial Institutions (FI), Mutual Funds (MF), Insurance Companies (IC), Other QIBs (OTH), Non-Institutional Buyers (NOH), Companies (CO), and Individuals (IND).
