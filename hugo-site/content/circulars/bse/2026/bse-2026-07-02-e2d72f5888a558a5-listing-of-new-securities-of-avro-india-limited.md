## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 10,60,900 new equity shares of Avro India Limited (Scrip Code: 543512), issued at Re. 1/- face value with a premium of Rs. 11.72/- (issue price Rs. 12.72/-) to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants, are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Friday, July 3, 2026.

## Key Points

- Security: 10,60,900 equity shares of Re. 1/- each, issued to Non-Promoters on a preferential basis
- Basis of issue: Conversion of warrants
- Issue Price: Rs. 12.72/- (face value Re. 1/- plus premium of Rs. 11.72/-)
- Distinctive Numbers: 133110501 to 134171400
- ISIN: INE652Z01025
- Date of Allotment: 18/04/2026
- These shares rank pari-passu with the existing equity shares of the company
- Entire allotment (1,060,900 shares, Dist. Nos. 133110501 to 134171400) is under lock-in until 05/01/2027

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing approval notice under BSE Listing Operations for shares arising from warrant conversion on a preferential basis.

## Compliance Requirements

No action required from investors or trading members beyond updating systems to reflect the new listed quantity from July 3, 2026. The company must ensure lock-in restrictions on the allotted shares are maintained until the specified lock-in expiry date.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: April 18, 2026
- Listing/Trading Effective Date: July 3, 2026 (Friday)
- Lock-in Expiry: January 5, 2027

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate listing update reflecting conversion of warrants into equity by non-promoter allottees. The 10,60,900 new shares will marginally increase the total listed equity capital of Avro India Limited, but since the shares are locked in until January 2027, there is no immediate impact on free-float trading liquidity. The transaction reflects continued warrant-to-equity conversion activity but carries low near-term market impact.
