## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that new privately placed debt securities issued by Receivable Securitisation Trust 12A have been listed and admitted to trading on the BSE Debt Segment effective 17 July 2026. The notice details two Pass Through Certificate (PTC) instruments under fresh listing.

## Key Points

- Two new ISINs listed under Receivable Securitisation Trust 12A on a private placement basis.
- Instrument 1: Scrip Code 977971, Scrip ID 112RST1227, ISIN INE2XU715019, Detail Name RST12-11.26%-19-7-27-PTC, Quantity 900, Market Lot 1, Credit Rating ICRA A-(SO), Face/Paidup/Issue Price Rs. 100,000 each.
- Instrument 2: Scrip Code 977972, Scrip ID 127RST1227, ISIN INE2XU715027, Detail Name RST12-12.75%-19-7-27-PTC, Quantity 180, Market Lot 1, Credit Rating ICRA BBB-(SO), Face/Paidup/Issue Price Rs. 100,000 each.
- Both securities will trade only in dematerialised form.
- Tick size for these securities is 1 paise.
- Placement Memorandum available at bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx.
- Queries can be directed to the BSE Debt Department via the listed phone extensions.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a routine listing notification under existing BSE Debt Segment listing procedures for privately placed instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must ensure these securities are traded only in dematerialised form under the specified ISINs and adhere to the prescribed tick size of 1 paise. No additional compliance action required beyond standard trading protocols.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: 14 July 2026
- Listing/Effective Date: 17 July 2026
- Instrument 1 (INE2XU715019): Interest paid quarterly from 14 October 2026 to 19 July 2027; Redemption on 19 July 2027; Interest rate 11.26% p.a.
- Instrument 2 (INE2XU715027): No scheduled interim interest payment date specified (NA); Redemption on 19 July 2027; Interest rate 12.75% p.a.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt market listing notice with limited broader market impact. It primarily affects trading members and institutional investors dealing in privately placed debt instruments and pass-through certificates. The listing enables secondary market trading of these securitised debt instruments on BSE's Debt Segment, providing liquidity options for holders of Receivable Securitisation Trust 12A instruments.
