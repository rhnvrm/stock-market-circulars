## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that Anand Rathi Wealth Ltd (Scrip Code: 543415) has fixed the record date for a bonus issue. Shares will trade on an ex-bonus basis from June 3, 2026, with 83,020,634 new equity shares to be allotted on June 4, 2026.

## Key Points

- Bonus ratio: 1 bonus equity share of Rs. 5/- for every 1 existing equity share of Rs. 5/- held (1:1)
- Record date: June 3, 2026
- Ex-bonus effective date: June 3, 2026 (Settlement No. DR-643/2026-2027)
- Total bonus shares to be allotted: 83,020,634 equity shares
- Allotment date: June 4, 2026
- Reference: Company letter dated May 24, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification issued by BSE Listing Compliance and Operations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all transactions in Anand Rathi Wealth Ltd equity shares are processed on an ex-bonus basis from June 3, 2026 onwards.
- Settlement of trades must account for the bonus adjustment effective from Settlement No. DR-643/2026-2027.

## Important Dates

- **Record Date:** June 3, 2026
- **Ex-Bonus Date:** June 3, 2026
- **Bonus Share Allotment Date:** June 4, 2026
- **Notice Date:** May 25, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Existing shareholders of Anand Rathi Wealth Ltd as of the record date (June 3, 2026) will receive one additional equity share for every share held, doubling their shareholding. The share price is expected to adjust proportionally (approximately halved) on the ex-bonus date to reflect the increased share count. Total market capitalisation remains unchanged. Trading members must update their systems to reflect the ex-bonus pricing from June 3, 2026.
