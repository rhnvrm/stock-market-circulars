## Summary

Achyut Healthcare Limited is migrating from BSE SME Platform to BSE Mainboard Platform. As part of this process, the company has submitted a limited review report for its unaudited financial results for the half year ended September 30, 2025. The review was conducted by Doshi Doshi & Co, Chartered Accountants, in accordance with SRE 2410 standards and confirms compliance with SEBI Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements Regulations, 2015.

## Key Points

- Migration of Achyut Healthcare Limited from BSE SME Platform to BSE Mainboard Platform
- Limited review report covers half year ended September 30, 2025
- Review includes statement of unaudited financial results, statement of Assets and Liabilities, and cash flow statement
- Review conducted in accordance with Standard on Review Engagements (SRE) 2410
- Review performed by Doshi Doshi & Co, Chartered Accountants (Firm Registration No.: 153683W)
- No material misstatements identified in the financial disclosures
- Company located at 610 Colonade, B/H Iscon Temple, Ahmedabad - 380058

## Regulatory Changes

The migration from SME platform to Mainboard platform represents a significant regulatory change for Achyut Healthcare Limited. The company must now comply with the more stringent listing and disclosure requirements applicable to Mainboard-listed companies under SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015, Regulation 33.

## Compliance Requirements

- Submission of unaudited financial results reviewed by independent auditors
- Preparation of statement of Assets and Liabilities as on reporting date
- Preparation of cash flow statement for the review period
- Compliance with Regulation 33 of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015
- Financial statements must be prepared in accordance with applicable accounting standards and recognized accounting practices
- Board of Directors approval of financial statements required
- Disclosure of all information required under listing regulations

## Important Dates

- **September 30, 2025**: End of half year reporting period for unaudited financial results
- **October 16, 2025**: Date of limited review report by auditors
- **January 1, 2026**: BSE circular publication date

## Impact Assessment

**Company Impact**: The migration to Mainboard platform is a positive development for Achyut Healthcare Limited, indicating growth and maturity. However, it comes with increased compliance obligations and scrutiny.

**Investor Impact**: Mainboard listing typically provides better liquidity, wider investor base, and enhanced credibility. The clean limited review report provides assurance on financial disclosure quality.

**Market Impact**: The migration demonstrates the company's growth trajectory and ability to meet higher listing standards. The auditor's unqualified review conclusion indicates proper financial controls and transparent reporting.

**Operational Impact**: The company will need to maintain higher corporate governance standards and more frequent disclosures as per Mainboard requirements.
