## Summary

BSE has issued an update regarding the debt securities of Avigna Housing Private Limited in continuation of Exchange Notice No.20251111-12 dated November 11, 2025. Scrip Code 977283 (ISIN INE0QJD07018) will be available for trading with revised terms as specified in the attached Annexure, effective Tuesday, June 16, 2026.

## Key Points

- Continuation of Exchange Notice No.20251111-12 dated November 11, 2025
- Scrip Code 977283 with ISIN INE0QJD07018 will trade under revised terms from June 16, 2026
- Revised terms are detailed in the Annexure attached to the notice
- Trading Members may contact Ms. Pooja Trivedi (+91 22 2272 8844 / debt.listing@bseindia.com) or Hardik Bhuta (Assistant Vice President) for further details

## Regulatory Changes

The debt securities of Avigna Housing Private Limited (Scrip Code 977283, ISIN INE0QJD07018) will be available for trading with revised terms as outlined in the Annexure, superseding the terms previously notified under Exchange Notice No.20251111-12 dated November 11, 2025.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members must take note of the revised trading terms for Scrip Code 977283 (ISIN INE0QJD07018) and update their systems accordingly before the market opens on June 16, 2026. Members requiring clarification should contact the DCS-Listing department at debt.listing@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **June 15, 2026**: Notice issued by BSE
- **June 16, 2026 (Tuesday)**: Revised terms become effective for trading

## Impact Assessment

This notice has a limited and targeted impact, affecting only trading members who deal in the debt securities of Avigna Housing Private Limited. The change is operational in nature—updating the trading terms for a single scrip—and does not indicate a systemic regulatory shift. Holders of ISIN INE0QJD07018 and active traders in this instrument should review the Annexure to understand the specific revisions before June 16, 2026.
