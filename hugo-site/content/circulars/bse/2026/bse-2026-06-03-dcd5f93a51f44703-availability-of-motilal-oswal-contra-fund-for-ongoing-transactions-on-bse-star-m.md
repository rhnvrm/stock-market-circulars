## Summary

BSE has informed members that MOTILAL OSWAL CONTRA FUND will reopen for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP facility on the BSE StAR MF Platform with effect from June 4, 2026. This follows Notice No. 20260507-35 dated May 7, 2026, which had previously suspended the fund from new transactions.

## Key Points

- MOTILAL OSWAL CONTRA FUND reopens for transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform from June 4, 2026
- All transaction types are enabled: subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP (Systematic Investment Plan)
- Six scheme variants are covered across Regular and Direct plans
- All six schemes are classified under the Equity category
- Prior suspension was communicated via Notice No. 20260507-35 dated May 7, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This notice reinstates transaction availability following an earlier temporary suspension of the fund on BSE StAR MF.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members. Brokers and distributors using BSE StAR MF Platform may resume processing transactions in MOTILAL OSWAL CONTRA FUND schemes from June 4, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **June 4, 2026**: Effective date for reopening of MOTILAL OSWAL CONTRA FUND on BSE StAR MF Platform
- **May 7, 2026**: Original suspension notice (Notice No. 20260507-35)

## Impact Assessment

Low operational impact. Investors and distributors who were unable to transact in MOTILAL OSWAL CONTRA FUND on BSE StAR MF can resume activity from June 4, 2026. The six affected schemes and their ISINs are:
