## Summary

BSE has notified members about the launch of a New Fund Offer (NFO) for the INFINITY HYBRID LONG-SHORT FUND under Kotak Mahindra Mutual Fund's Specialized Investment Funds (SIF) umbrella on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The NFO opens June 15, 2026, and closes June 29, 2026, with SIP facility available. Six scheme variants are being offered across Regular and Direct plans with Growth and IDCW (Payout and Reinvestment) options.

## Key Points

- NFO period: June 15, 2026 to June 29, 2026 on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Six scheme variants offered: Regular Plan and Direct Plan, each with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- All schemes classified as DEBT type with ISINs INF174K30046, INF174K30053, INF174K30061, INF174K30012, INF174K30020, INF174K30038
- SIP/XSIP facility available alongside lumpsum purchases
- Lumpsum purchases extended till midnight on June 29, 2026 on BSE StAR MF Platform
- This notice supplements Notice No. 20260612-27 on introduction of Kotak Mahindra Mutual Fund SIF

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This circular operationalises the availability of Specialized Investment Funds (SIF) — a product category — on the BSE StAR MF Platform for Kotak Mahindra Mutual Fund.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clients initiate funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day; transactions without fund initiation will be cancelled and CC with 'N' flag reported to RTA/AMC
- Pending transactions (where payment initiated) remain in system until 3:00 PM on last day of funds pay-out to AMC or T+5, whichever is earlier
- Clear funds must be available in the ICCL account by 6:00 PM on July 3, 2026 for allotment eligibility
- Cheque and OTM payments accepted during NFO period subject to receipt of clear funds in ICCL account by 6:00 PM on July 3, 2026
- SIP/XSIP registrations must be authenticated by clients on or before 3:00 PM on June 29, 2026

## Important Dates

- **June 15, 2026**: NFO opens on BSE StAR MF Platform
- **June 29, 2026 (3:00 PM)**: Deadline for SIP/XSIP registration authentication by clients
- **June 29, 2026 (midnight)**: NFO closes; lumpsum purchases extended till midnight
- **July 3, 2026 (6:00 PM)**: Deadline for clear funds to be available in ICCL account
- **July 6, 2026**: Tentative allotment date for all six schemes

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice for BSE members and mutual fund distributors using the BSE StAR MF Platform. The NFO introduces Kotak Mahindra's first Specialized Investment Fund product — INFINITY HYBRID LONG-SHORT FUND — which employs a long-short strategy within the hybrid debt category. Members need to ensure proper fund collection timelines are followed. The SIF product category is relatively new, and this represents an early adoption by a major AMC, which may attract investor interest from sophisticated/high-net-worth investors eligible for SIFs. Operational impact is limited to members active in mutual fund distribution on the BSE StAR MF Platform.
