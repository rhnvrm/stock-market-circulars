## Summary

BSE has announced the listing and trading permission for 1,04,000 equity shares of Rex Sealing and Packing Industries Limited (Scrip Code: 543744) with effect from Wednesday, January 21, 2026. These shares were issued to Non-Promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 1,04,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 200/-
- Issue price: Rs. 210/- per share
- Shares issued to Non-Promoters on preferential basis
- Allotment pursuant to conversion of warrants
- Shares ranking pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE0MFT01014
- Distinctive Numbers: 2276001 to 2380000

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of the Exchange are informed to note the listing of these new securities and commence trading from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** October 20, 2025
- **Trading Start Date:** January 21, 2026
- **Lock-in Period Ends:** July 31, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing has minimal market impact as it involves a small quantity of shares (1,04,000) issued on preferential basis. All shares are subject to lock-in until July 31, 2026, preventing immediate trading activity. The shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company.
