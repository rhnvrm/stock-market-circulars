## Summary

BSE Clearing Limited (BSECL) has issued a notice updating members, custodians, PCMs, and banks on the revised list of banks eligible to be accepted as collateral providers, in accordance with Section 3.2 (Criteria for selection of Banks) of SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD/MRD-PoD-3/P/CIR/2024/65 dated 29 May 2024. This notice continues from BSECL's earlier circular 20260226-8 dated 26 February 2026 on norms for acceptable collaterals and exposure of Clearing Corporations.

## Key Points

- The notice provides an updated list of 35 banks currently eligible under SEBI's criteria for selection of banks for collateral purposes.
- Eligible banks include major public sector banks (SBI, Bank of Baroda, Bank of India, Punjab National Bank, Canara Bank, Union Bank of India, etc.), private sector banks (HDFC Bank, ICICI Bank, Axis Bank, Kotak Mahindra Bank, IndusInd Bank, IDFC First Bank, etc.), and foreign banks (Citibank, HSBC, Deutsche Bank, Barclays, JP Morgan Chase, MUFG Bank, Mizuho Bank, Standard Chartered, Sumitomo Mitsui Banking Corporation, Credit Agricole, DBS Bank).
- This is a continuation/update of the norms first circulated via BSECL Circular 20260226-8 dated 26 February 2026.
- No attachment was provided with this notice; the bank list is included directly in the circular text.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory change is introduced; this notice operationalizes and updates the list of banks eligible under existing SEBI norms (SEBI/HO/MRD/MRD-PoD-3/P/CIR/2024/65 dated 29-May-2024) governing acceptable collaterals and exposure norms for Clearing Corporations.

## Compliance Requirements

Members, Custodians, PCMs, and Banks are required to take note of the revised, current list of eligible banks as on the notice date (17 July 2026) for purposes of collateral placement and exposure management in accordance with SEBI's Section 3.2 criteria.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 17 July 2026
- Prior related circular: BSECL Circular 20260226-8 dated 26 February 2026
- Underlying SEBI circular: SEBI/HO/MRD/MRD-PoD-3/P/CIR/2024/65 dated 29 May 2024

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited direct market impact and is primarily an operational/administrative update for market intermediaries (members, custodians, PCMs, and banks) regarding which banks currently qualify as eligible collateral providers under SEBI's risk management framework. It does not affect trading, listing, or specific stocks, but ensures continued compliance with SEBI's collateral and exposure risk management norms by clearing members and custodians.
