## Summary

BSE has issued a revised schedule for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for P M TELELINNKS LIMITED. The offer is scheduled to run from Tuesday, January 13, 2026 to Wednesday, January 28, 2026. Trading Members and Custodian Members can facilitate bids for up to 26,19,500 equity shares at an offer price of Rs. 6.81 per share through the OTB module on iBBS and RTRMS platforms.

## Key Points

- **Security Details**: P M TELELINNKS LIMITED (Symbol: PMTELELIN, ISIN: INE092C01015, Scrip Code: 513403)
- **Offer Size**: Up to 26,19,500 equity shares
- **Offer Price**: Rs. 6.81 per share
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10.00
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Opening Date**: Tuesday, January 13, 2026
- **Closing Date**: Wednesday, January 28, 2026
- **Bid Offering Period**: January 13-28, 2026 from 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM
- **Give-up/Take-up**: January 12-27, 2026 from 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM
- **Last Day Give-up**: January 28, 2026 from 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM
- **Last Day Take-up**: January 28, 2026 from 9:15 AM to 4:00 PM

## Regulatory Changes

This is a revised schedule circular. No new regulatory changes are introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members**: Must ensure investor UCC is registered and/or updated with correct PAN Number and Category in the Exchange UCC database
- **Bidding Eligibility**: Trading Members can only bid for investors having shares in demat form
- **Categories for Bidding**: FII (Foreign Institutional Buyer), FI (Financial Institution), MF (Mutual Fund), IC (Insurance Companies), OTH (Other QIBs), NOH (Other), CO (Company), IND (Individual)
- **Platform Access**: Members must use iBBS platform (https://ibbs.bseindia.com/ or https://ibbsll.bseindia.com/) and RTRMS (https://rtrms.bseindia.com/ or https://rtrmsll.bseindia.com/)
- **File Access**: Extranet files for bid acceptance available after confirmation from Merchant Banker/RTA post-issue closure
- **Reference Circulars**: Members should refer to Exchange Notices No. 20150930-1 (September 30, 2015) and 20170203-29 (February 3, 2017) for operational instructions

## Important Dates

- **January 12, 2026**: Give-up/Take-up begins (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **January 13, 2026**: Offer opening date and bid offering period begins (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **January 27, 2026**: Last day for regular give-up/take-up (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **January 28, 2026**: Offer closing date
  - Give-up (last day): 9:15 AM - 3:30 PM
  - Take-up (last day): 9:15 AM - 4:00 PM
  - Bid offering: 9:15 AM - 3:30 PM

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a company-specific acquisition window that affects only shareholders of P M TELELINNKS LIMITED. The offer size of 26,19,500 shares represents a limited pool.

**Operational Impact**: Medium for participating members - Trading Members and Custodian Members need to ensure their systems and processes are configured correctly for the OTB module. They must verify investor UCC registration and ensure shares are in demat form.

**Investor Impact**: Low to Medium - Only affects shareholders of PMTELELIN who wish to participate in the offer. The offer price of Rs. 6.81 per share provides an exit opportunity for eligible shareholders during the specified window.

**Contact Support**: BSE has provided comprehensive contact details for technical support related to iBBS, RTRMS, and Extranet access, including specific points of contact for Trading Operations and Listing Sales teams.
