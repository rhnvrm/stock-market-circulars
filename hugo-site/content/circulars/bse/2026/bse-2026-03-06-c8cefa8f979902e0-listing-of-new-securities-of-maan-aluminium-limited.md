## Summary

BSE has notified the listing of new securities of Maan Aluminium Limited, providing distinctive numbers, quantities, and lock-in expiry dates for a total of 59,00,000 shares. The shares are subject to lock-in periods expiring either on 20/09/2026 or 20/09/2027 depending on the tranche.

## Key Points

- Total shares listed: 59,00,000 (5,900,000)
- Shares are assigned distinctive numbers ranging from 54084865 to 59984864
- Lock-in expiry dates are either 20/09/2026 or 20/09/2027
- The majority of shares (12,26,500) in a single tranche expire on 20/09/2027
- Depository credit confirmations are linked to each tranche with corresponding distinctive numbers

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification per BSE listing norms for new securities issued by Maan Aluminium Limited.

## Compliance Requirements

- Investors and depositories must note the lock-in expiry dates per tranche before initiating any transfers or transactions on the locked shares
- Depository participants should ensure credit confirmations align with the distinctive number ranges provided

## Important Dates

- **20/09/2026**: Lock-in expiry for the majority of tranches
- **20/09/2027**: Lock-in expiry for select tranches including the largest single tranche of 12,26,500 shares

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited broader market impact. It is relevant to existing and prospective shareholders of Maan Aluminium Limited who need to be aware of lock-in restrictions on the newly listed securities. Trading in these shares will be restricted until the respective lock-in expiry dates. The staggered lock-in expiry (2026 vs 2027) may influence secondary market liquidity for this stock.
