## Summary

AMBO AGRITEC LIMITED (Scrip Code: 543678) has listed 15,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 20/- (total issue price Rs. 30/-) on the BSE. These shares were allotted to promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. The securities will commence trading from Thursday, January 22, 2026.

## Key Points

- 15,00,000 equity shares of face value Rs. 10/- each issued
- Issue price: Rs. 30/- per share (including premium of Rs. 20/-)
- Allotted to promoters on preferential basis
- Issued pursuant to conversion of warrants
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE0N6V01016
- Distinctive Numbers: 15483161 to 16983160
- Date of allotment: July 17, 2025
- All shares subject to lock-in until August 31, 2027

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the listing of new securities
- Lock-in restrictions apply to all 15,00,000 shares until August 31, 2027
- Shares cannot be transferred or sold before the lock-in expiry date

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: July 17, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: January 22, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry**: August 31, 2027
- **Notice Date**: January 21, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine preferential allotment to promoters through warrant conversion. The impact on the market is minimal as:

- Shares are issued to existing promoters, not to public investors
- All shares are locked-in for approximately 1.5 years until August 2027
- The allotment represents a standard corporate action for fundraising
- No immediate dilution impact on trading liquidity due to lock-in restrictions
- The shares rank pari-passu with existing equity, maintaining uniform shareholder rights
