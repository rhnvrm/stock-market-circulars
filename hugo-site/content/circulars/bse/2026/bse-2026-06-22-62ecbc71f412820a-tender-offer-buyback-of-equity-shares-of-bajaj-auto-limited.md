## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that Bajaj Auto Limited (Scrip Code: 532977/132977) has fixed 24 June 2026 as the record date for its ongoing equity share buyback offer. The record date determines the entitlement and names of shareholders eligible to participate in the buyback through the tender offer mechanism.

## Key Points

- Bajaj Auto Limited has set 24 June 2026 as the record date for its tender offer buyback
- The buyback applies to dematerialised securities in the Rolling Settlement segment
- Ex-Entitlement date is also 24 June 2026
- Settlement number: DR-658/2026-2027
- Trading members are required to take note of the record date

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular is a procedural notice under existing SEBI buyback regulations, notifying the market of a company-specific corporate action event.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must take note of the record date and communicate relevant information to clients holding Bajaj Auto shares
- Shareholders who wish to participate in the buyback must ensure their holdings are in demat form and settled by the record date (24 June 2026)
- Members should update client entitlement records based on positions as of the record date

## Important Dates

- **Record Date / Ex-Entitlement Date:** 24 June 2026
- **Settlement Number:** DR-658/2026-2027
- **Notice Date:** 22 June 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has high direct impact on existing Bajaj Auto shareholders. Investors holding shares on the record date (24 June 2026) will be eligible to tender their shares in the buyback. Buybacks by large-cap companies like Bajaj Auto typically attract significant market interest, as they can provide an exit opportunity at a premium to market price. Trading members need to ensure client positions and entitlements are accurately recorded for settlement DR-658/2026-2027.
