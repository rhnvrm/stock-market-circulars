## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 6,21,82,965 new equity shares of Lloyds Engineering Works Ltd (Scrip Code: 539992) are permitted for trading effective Thursday, May 21, 2026. These shares arise from the conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up equity shares of Re. 1/- each, issued on a rights basis.

## Key Points

- 6,21,82,965 equity shares of Re. 1/- each are admitted for trading on BSE
- Shares result from conversion of partly paid rights shares to fully paid up
- ISIN: INE093R01011
- New shares are pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Two tranches of distinctive numbers assigned covering the full allotment

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard listing admission pursuant to completion of a rights issue conversion process.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed and permitted to trade in these new securities from May 21, 2026. No additional compliance action is required from members beyond standard trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** May 20, 2026
- **Trading Effective From:** Thursday, May 21, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds approximately 6.22 crore new fully paid equity shares to the tradeable float of Lloyds Engineering Works Ltd. Since these shares are pari-passu with existing shares and arise from a previously announced rights issue conversion, market impact is expected to be moderate — existing shareholders who converted their partly paid holdings gain liquidity, while the expanded share count could exert minor dilutive pressure on per-share metrics.
