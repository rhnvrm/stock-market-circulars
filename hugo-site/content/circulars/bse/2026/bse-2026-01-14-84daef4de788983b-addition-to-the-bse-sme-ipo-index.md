## Summary

YAJUR FIBRES LIMITED (Exchange ticker 544676) is being added to the BSE SME IPO Index effective January 16, 2026, following its listing on BSE on January 14, 2026. This is a routine index maintenance action following a new listing on the SME platform.

## Key Points

- YAJUR FIBRES LIMITED (ticker 544676) listed on BSE on Wednesday, January 14, 2026
- Stock will be added to BSE SME IPO Index effective at market open on Friday, January 16, 2026
- Action included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- Notice references prior Notice No. 20260113-25

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard index maintenance activity.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index fund managers tracking the BSE SME IPO Index may need to adjust portfolio compositions.

## Important Dates

- **January 14, 2026**: YAJUR FIBRES LIMITED listing date on BSE
- **January 16, 2026**: Effective date for addition to BSE SME IPO Index (at market open)

Minimal market impact. This is a routine index addition following a new SME listing. Primary impact is on:
- Index fund managers who track the BSE SME IPO Index and need to rebalance portfolios
- Investors in YAJUR FIBRES LIMITED who may see potential inflows from index-tracking funds
- No trading rule changes or operational impacts for brokers or market participants
