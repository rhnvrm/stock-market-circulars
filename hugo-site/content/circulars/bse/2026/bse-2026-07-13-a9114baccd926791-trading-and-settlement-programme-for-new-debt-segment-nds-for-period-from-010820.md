## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260713-13 dated 13 Jul 2026, announcing the trading and settlement programme for the New Debt Segment (NDS) for the period from 01.08.2026 to 31.08.2026. This is a routine operational notice issued by the Settlement/RMS department (BSECL) to inform market participants of trading and settlement dates applicable to the NDS segment for the upcoming month.

## Key Points

- Notice No. 20260713-13 issued by BSE on 13 Jul 2026
- Pertains to the New Debt Segment (NDS)
- Covers the trading and settlement schedule for the period 01.08.2026 to 31.08.2026
- Issued under the Settlement/RMS category by the BSECL department
- Detailed schedule is provided in the attachment "NDS082026"

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced; this is a periodic operational calendar update for the NDS segment.

## Compliance Requirements

Market participants, clearing members, and custodians trading in the NDS segment should align their trading and settlement operations with the published schedule for August 2026.

## Important Dates

Applicable period: 01 August 2026 to 31 August 2026. Specific trading and settlement dates are detailed in the attached NDS082026 schedule.

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact, routine administrative notice affecting operational planning for debt segment participants. It does not affect any specific listed stocks or securities and has no direct market-moving implications.
