## Summary

BSE has announced the availability of non-competitive bidding facility for trading members to participate in the RBI auction of Government of India dated securities scheduled for January 30, 2026. The auction will offer 6.48% GS 2035 (re-issue) worth Rs. 32,000 crore. Bidding will be conducted through BSE's iBBS web-based NCB-GSec module.

## Key Points

- Security offered: 6.48% GS 2035 (Re-issue)
- Issue size: Rs. 32,000 crore
- Minimum subscription: Rs. 10,000 in units of Rs. 100 and multiples thereof
- Maximum amount per bid: Rs. 2 crore
- Bidding platform: iBBS web-based system NCB-GSec module (https://ibbs.bseindia.com)
- Reference to RBI press release dated January 23, 2026
- Continuation of facility launched via circular 20180423-42 dated April 23, 2018

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular operationalizes the existing non-competitive bidding facility for the specific auction scheduled on January 30, 2026.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must submit bids through the NCB-GSec module of iBBS system
- Members must adhere to specified bidding timelines and amount limits
- Obligation and pay-in requirements to be fulfilled as per ICCL guidelines

## Important Dates

- **Bid Collection Start (24-hour availability)**: January 27, 2026 from 10:00 AM onwards
- **Bid Collection End (Direct Investors)**: January 29, 2026 till 5:00 PM
- **Bid Collection End (Members)**: January 30, 2026 till 8:00 AM
- **Auction Date**: January 30, 2026
- **Settlement Date**: February 2, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational circular with minimal market impact. It provides trading members and direct investors an opportunity to participate in government securities auction through the non-competitive route. The facility is specifically for participants who wish to invest in G-secs without competing on yield. Contact details provided for technical support (Trading Operations), bidding queries, and settlement-related issues through ICCL Operations.
