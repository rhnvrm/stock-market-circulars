## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Commodity Derivatives segment on Saturday, January 10th, 2026. The session is designed for trading members to test their trading applications, including third-party platforms and in-house systems using ETI APIs. A new BOLT Pro TWS version 12.50 will be released, with details to be published separately. The mock trades will not create any margin obligations, pay-in/pay-out obligations, rights, or liabilities.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Commodity Derivatives segment on Saturday, January 10, 2026
- Login window: 10:15 AM to 11:00 AM
- Continuous trading: 11:00 AM to 2:00 PM
- Trade modification: 2:00 PM to 2:10 PM
- New BOLT Pro TWS version 12.50 will be released (details in separate circular)
- Testing opportunity for various functionalities including risk-reduction mode, trading halt, and exceptional market conditions
- Mock trades will not attract any obligations or create rights and liabilities
- Members requested to provide feedback by 3:00 PM on the same day

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a technical testing session.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are encouraged to actively participate in the mock trading session
- Members must provide feedback by 3:00 PM on January 10, 2026
- Feedback should be submitted to BSE Tech Support (022-22728053, bse.tech@bseindia.com) or Help Desk (022-45720400/600 & 022-69158500, bsehelp@bseindia.com)
- Members can contact their respective Relationship Manager for queries

## Important Dates

- **January 10, 2026**: Mock trading session
  - 10:15 AM - 11:00 AM: Login window
  - 11:00 AM - 2:00 PM: Continuous trading
  - 2:00 PM - 2:10 PM: Trade modification
  - 3:00 PM: Feedback deadline

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: None. This is a mock trading session with no actual market transactions.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Provides testing opportunity for trading members using third-party platforms, empaneled vendors, or in-house systems. Members can familiarize themselves with the new BOLT Pro TWS version 12.50 and test various functionalities including exceptional market conditions. No margin, settlement, or other financial obligations will arise from this session.
