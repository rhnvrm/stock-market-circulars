## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of GABION TECHNOLOGIES INDIA LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544675), an SME IPO, will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment under M Group. This change will be effective from Thursday, January 29, 2026. This is a continuation of the Exchange's previous notice dated January 12, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: GABION TECHNOLOGIES INDIA LIMITED (SME IPO)
- Scrip Code: 544675
- Current segment: Trade for Trade segment (MT Group)
- New segment: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective date: Thursday, January 29, 2026
- Notice supersedes: BSE Notice No. 20260112-35 dated January 12, 2026
- Contact: Mr. Anurag Jain, Tel: 022-2272 8822

## Regulatory Changes

The trading mechanism for GABION TECHNOLOGIES INDIA LIMITED will change from Trade for Trade basis to Rolling settlement. This is a standard transition for newly listed SME companies after their IPO listing period, allowing for more liquid trading conditions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members should take note of the segment change effective January 29, 2026
- Trading Members may contact Mr. Anurag Jain for further clarification or details regarding this transition
- All dealings in the company's equity shares should be conducted under M Group from the effective date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 13, 2026
- **Previous Notice**: January 12, 2026 (Notice No. 20260112-35)
- **Effective Date**: January 29, 2026 (Thursday)

## Impact Assessment

This transition represents a positive development for GABION TECHNOLOGIES INDIA LIMITED as it moves from the restrictive Trade for Trade segment to the more liquid Rolling segment. The change will:

- Enable intraday trading opportunities that were not available under Trade for Trade
- Improve liquidity and price discovery for the stock
- Allow delivery-based and intraday positions under normal rolling settlement
- Facilitate easier entry and exit for traders and investors

The impact is primarily relevant to existing and potential shareholders of GABION TECHNOLOGIES INDIA LIMITED, as well as Trading Members handling transactions in this scrip.
