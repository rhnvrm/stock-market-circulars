## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260511-34 dated 11 May 2026 announcing the listing of equity shares of Bombay Super Hybrid Seeds Limited on the Exchange. The notice is issued by the DCS-Listing department under the Company Related category for the Equity segment.

## Key Points

- Notice No.: 20260511-34
- Notice Date: 11 May 2026
- Company: Bombay Super Hybrid Seeds Limited
- Segment: Equity
- Department: DCS-Listing
- Annexures attached as part of the listing documentation

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this notice. It is a standard listing admission notice for a new company joining the BSE equity segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company and its stakeholders must adhere to BSE listing obligations and disclosure requirements post-listing.
- Market participants should refer to the attached annexures for specific terms, issue details, and trading commencement information.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 11 May 2026
- Trading commencement date: To be confirmed via annexures attached to the notice.

## Impact Assessment

This listing introduces Bombay Super Hybrid Seeds Limited, an agricultural seeds company, to the BSE equity market. The impact is primarily relevant to retail and institutional investors interested in the agri-inputs sector. No broad market or systemic impact is anticipated. Investors should review the full annexures for allotment details, ISIN, and trading schedule.
