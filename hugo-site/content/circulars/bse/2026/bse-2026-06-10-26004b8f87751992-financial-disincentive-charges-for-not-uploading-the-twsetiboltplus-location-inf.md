## Summary

BSE has issued a financial disincentive notice to trading members who failed to register or incorrectly registered their TWS/ETI/BOLTPLUS terminal locations for the month of May 2026. A penalty of ₹1,000 per instance applies for orders placed from unregistered terminals, and the applicable charges will be debited to members' General Charges Accounts 60 days from the date of this notice.

## Key Points

- Financial disincentive of ₹1,000 per instance for orders placed from unregistered or incorrectly registered terminal locations
- The Financial Disincentive file for May 2026 has been released with filename LCS0526.xxxx, available in today's Equity Transaction Folder on Extranet
- Charges will be debited to the General Charges Account after 60 days from June 10, 2026 (i.e., around August 9, 2026)
- Members may seek waiver or review by emailing Waiver-Review@bseindia.com with subject line: "Waiver / review of Financial Disincentive_\<TM Name>_\<Clg No.>_LOCATION MISMATCH-MAY-2026"
- Waiver process details are outlined in Exchange Notice No. 20251008-67 dated October 08, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice enforces existing requirements under Exchange Notices:
- 20070531-8 (May 31, 2007)
- 20070828-28 (August 28, 2007)
- 20131204-9 (December 4, 2013)
- 20131213-26 (December 13, 2013)
- 20180925-4 (September 25, 2018)
- 20250924-54 (September 24, 2025)
- 20251223-22 (December 23, 2025)
- Penalty framework: Notice 20251010-20 (October 10, 2025) and 20260417-45 (April 17, 2026)

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all TWS/ETI/BOLTPLUS terminals are registered with BSE before placing orders
- Terminal location details must be uploaded to the Exchange prior to punching orders from any location
- Members with discrepancies in the LCS0526.xxxx file should review their terminal registrations immediately
- Members seeking waiver must follow the prescribed process and submit requests to Waiver-Review@bseindia.com within the stipulated period per Notice 20251008-67

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 10, 2026
- **Reference Period:** May 2026
- **Charge Debit Date:** On or around August 9, 2026 (60 days from notice date)
- **Waiver Request:** Must be submitted before charges are debited; refer to Notice 20251008-67 for specific timelines

## Impact Assessment

This circular has low broad market impact. It is an operational compliance enforcement action directed at trading members with unregistered terminal locations during May 2026. Affected members face a ₹1,000 per-instance charge, which could aggregate to significant amounts for members with multiple non-compliant terminals or high order volumes from unregistered locations. Members are advised to proactively download and review the LCS0526.xxxx file from Extranet and initiate waiver requests if applicable. No impact on listed securities or investor-facing operations.
