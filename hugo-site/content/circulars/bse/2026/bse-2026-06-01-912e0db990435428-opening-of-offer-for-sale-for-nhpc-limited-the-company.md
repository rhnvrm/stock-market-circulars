## Summary

The President of India, acting through the Ministry of Power (Government of India), is conducting an Offer for Sale (OFS) of up to 30,13,51,044 equity shares of NHPC Limited (3.00% of paid-up equity) through BSE and NSE. An oversubscription option allows the sale of an additional 30,13,51,044 shares (3.00%), bringing the potential total to 60,27,02,088 shares (6.00%). The floor price is Rs. 71 per share. Additionally, up to 45,20,265 shares may be offered to eligible employees of the Company.

## Key Points

- **Seller:** The President of India (Ministry of Power, Government of India)
- **Company:** NHPC Limited
- **Base Offer Size:** 30,13,51,044 equity shares (3.00% of paid-up equity)
- **Oversubscription Option:** Additional 30,13,51,044 equity shares (3.00%); total up to 60,27,02,088 shares (6.00%) if exercised
- **Employee Offer:** Up to 45,20,265 equity shares for eligible employees; individual limit of Rs. 5,00,000
- **Floor Price:** Rs. 71/- per equity share
- **Face Value:** Rs. 10/- per share
- **OFS Mechanism:** Separate designated window on BSE and NSE

## Regulatory Changes

The OFS is conducted under the Revised Guidelines for Bidding in OFS, referencing the following SEBI circulars:
- SEBI/HO/MRD/DOPI/CIR/P/2018/159 dated December 28, 2018 — Review of OFS through Stock Exchange Mechanism
- SEBI/HO/MRD/MRD-PoD-3/P/CIR/2023/10 dated January 10, 2023 — Comprehensive Framework on OFS
- SEBI/HO/MRD/MRD-PoD-3/P/CIR/2024/82 dated June 14, 2024 — Framework for OFS to Employees

BSE exchange notices referenced: 20190118-43, 20200701-27, 20230127-14, 20240701-19.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members and Custodians must refer to the revised OFS bidding guidelines cited in the notice.
- Non-Retail Investors may bid on T Day (June 02, 2026) only; they may carry forward unallotted bids to T+1 Day.
- Retail Investors and Eligible Employees may bid on T+1 Day (June 03, 2026).
- Employee participation is subject to approval from the competent authority per OFS Guidelines.
- All participants must adhere to the floor price of Rs. 71/- per share.

## Important Dates

- **T Day (Non-Retail Investors only):** June 02, 2026
- **T+1 Day (Retail Investors, Employees, and carry-forward Non-Retail bids):** June 03, 2026
- **Notice Date:** June 01, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a high-impact government divestment event for NHPC Limited. The sale of up to 6% of paid-up equity (60.27 crore shares) at a floor of Rs. 71 per share represents a large supply addition to the market. Institutional and retail investors should assess potential short-term price pressure around the OFS window. The employee offer provides an additional allocation pathway for NHPC staff. Market participants should monitor BSE's website (www.bseindia.com) for further details and updates on the offer.
