## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No 20260123-7 announcing the suspension of trading in debt securities of 12 companies effective January 27, 2025. The suspensions are due to redemption of securities or delisting applications filed by the respective companies. Trading members are instructed not to deal in these specific debt securities from the effective date.

## Key Points

- 12 debt securities from various issuers will be suspended from trading
- Suspension effective from January 27, 2025
- Reason: Redemption or delisting applications by companies
- Notice issued under reference DR-806/2025-2026
- Affects debt market segment only

1. EDELWEISS ASSET RECONSTRUCTION CO. LTD (ISIN: INE015L07204)
2. ROGER 08 2024 (ISIN: INE1GAM15010)

9. DHRUVA XXI 2023 (ISIN: INE0REI15027)

11. ASHNA REALTORS PRIVATE LIMITED (ISIN: INE542T11012)
12. TOTAL ENVIRONMENT CONSTRUCTIONS PRIVATE LIMITED (ISIN: INE257Y07031)

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debt securities from January 27, 2025
- Trading members are required to take note of this suspension
- All transactions in these securities must cease by the effective date

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: January 23, 2026
- Effective Date: January 27, 2025 (Trading suspension begins)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative action affecting the debt market segment. The suspension impacts holders and potential traders of these specific debt instruments. The redemption/delisting indicates normal lifecycle events for these securities. Market impact is limited to participants holding or trading these specific ISINs. No broader market disruption expected as these are isolated securities reaching maturity or being voluntarily delisted.
