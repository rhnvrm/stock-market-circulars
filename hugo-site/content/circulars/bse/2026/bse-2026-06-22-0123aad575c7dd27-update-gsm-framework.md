## Summary

BSE has issued an update to the Graded Surveillance Measure (GSM) Framework confirming that securities identified in Annexure III of Exchange Notice No. 20260306-35 (dated March 6, 2026) will be moved out of the GSM Framework effective June 23, 2026. The revised Group classification and Daily Price Band applicable to these securities post-exit are detailed in the accompanying Annexure.

## Key Points

- Securities in Annexure III of notice 20260306-35 dated March 6, 2026 are exiting the GSM Framework.
- Exit is effective from June 23, 2026.
- Revised Group and Daily Price Band assignments for the affected securities are provided in the attached Annexure (AnnexureGSMMoveout).
- Issued by BSE Surveillance Department (DOSS).
- Members may contact bse.surv@bseindia.com for clarifications.

## Regulatory Changes

The securities being moved out of the GSM Framework will no longer be subject to GSM-specific trading restrictions. Their applicable Group classification and Daily Price Band revert to those specified in the Annexure attached to this notice, effective June 23, 2026.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must apply the revised Group classification and Daily Price Band for affected securities from June 23, 2026.
- Members should refer to the attached AnnexureGSMMoveout for the complete list of securities and their revised trading parameters.

## Important Dates

- **June 22, 2026**: Notice issued.
- **June 23, 2026**: Effective date for exit from GSM Framework and application of revised Group and Daily Price Band.

## Impact Assessment

Securities exiting the GSM Framework regain standard trading conditions, including potentially wider price bands and removal of trade-for-trade settlement obligations associated with GSM stages. This is a positive development for the affected securities and their investors, as it signals improved compliance or surveillance standing. Market participants holding or trading these securities should update their systems to reflect the revised parameters from June 23, 2026.
