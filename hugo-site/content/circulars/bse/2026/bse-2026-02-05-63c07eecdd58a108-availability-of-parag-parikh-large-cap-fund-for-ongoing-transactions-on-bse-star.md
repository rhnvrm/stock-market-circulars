## Summary

BSE has announced that PARAG PARIKH LARGE CAP FUND will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective February 06, 2026. The fund will also offer SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities. This follows a previous suspension referenced in Notice No. 20260116-56.

## Key Points

- PARAG PARIKH LARGE CAP FUND reopening for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Effective date: February 06, 2026
- Six scheme variants available: Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Reinvestment, and IDCW Payout options
- All ISINs are in the Equity category
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities will be available
- References previous Notice No. 20260116-56 regarding suspension

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding fund availability on the trading platform.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Members are informed of the fund's availability for client transactions.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: February 05, 2026
- **Effective Date**: February 06, 2026 - Fund reopens for ongoing transactions

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine operational notice affecting only investors and distributors interested in PARAG PARIKH LARGE CAP FUND.

**Operational Impact**: BSE StAR MF Platform members can resume processing subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP transactions for this fund.

**Investor Impact**: Investors who were unable to transact in this fund during the suspension period can now resume their investment activities across all six scheme variants (Regular and Direct plans with Growth and IDCW options).
