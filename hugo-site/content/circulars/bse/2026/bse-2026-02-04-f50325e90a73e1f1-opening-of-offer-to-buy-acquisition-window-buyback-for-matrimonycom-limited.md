## Summary

Matrimony.com Limited has opened its buyback offer for up to 8,93,129 fully paid-up equity shares representing 4.14% of total paid-up equity share capital as on March 31, 2025. The buyback is through tender offer route at ₹655 per equity share for an aggregate amount not exceeding ₹5,850 lakhs (excluding transaction costs). The offer is made to all equity shareholders/beneficial owners as on the record date of January 30, 2026, on a proportionate basis.

## Key Points

- Buyback of up to 8,93,129 equity shares of face value ₹5 each
- Buyback price: ₹655 per equity share, payable in cash
- Maximum buyback size: ₹5,850 lakhs (excluding transaction costs)
- Represents 4.14% of total paid-up equity share capital as on March 31, 2025
- Record date: January 30, 2026
- Buyback method: Tender offer route on proportionate basis
- Two categories: Reserved category for small shareholders and General category for all other eligible shareholders
- Buyback size represents 24.68% of standalone and 24.81% of consolidated paid-up equity capital and free reserves as per March 31, 2025 financials

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a corporate action in compliance with existing regulations including:
- Securities and Exchange Board of India (Buy-Back of Securities) Regulations, 2018
- Companies Act, 2013 (Sections 68, 69, 70, 110)
- Companies (Share Capital and Debentures) Rules, 2014
- SEBI Listing Regulations, 2015

## Compliance Requirements

- Eligible shareholders as on record date (January 30, 2026) can participate in the buyback
- Shareholders must refer to the Letter of Offer for detailed participation procedures
- Separate categories maintained for small shareholders (reserved) and other shareholders (general)
- Conducted through tender offer on proportionate basis
- Manager to the Buyback: Saffron Capital Advisors Private Limited
- Registrar to the Buyback: KFin Technologies Limited

## Important Dates

- Record Date: January 30, 2026
- Reference date for financials: March 31, 2025
- Opening of buyback offer: February 4, 2026 (as per circular date)

## Impact Assessment

**High Impact on Shareholders**: This buyback offers shareholders an exit opportunity at ₹655 per share with proportionate allocation. Small shareholders have reserved quota ensuring participation.

**Capital Structure Impact**: The buyback of 4.14% equity shares will reduce the outstanding share capital and improve earnings per share metrics for remaining shareholders.

**Financial Impact**: The buyback size of ₹58.5 crore represents approximately 24.68-24.81% of the company's equity capital and free reserves, indicating substantial capital deployment for shareholder returns.

**Market Impact**: The tender offer process ensures fair and transparent price discovery while giving all eligible shareholders equal opportunity to participate on proportionate basis.
