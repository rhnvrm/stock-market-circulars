## Summary

BSE has issued its periodic update to the list of securities placed under the Additional Surveillance Measure (ASM) framework specific to companies undergoing the Insolvency Resolution Process (IRP) as per the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code (IBC). The update, effective July 03, 2026, adds newly qualifying securities (Annexure I), removes securities that no longer meet the criteria (Annexure II), and provides a consolidated list of all securities currently under the framework (Annexure III).

## Key Points

- Securities meeting IBC-IRP surveillance criteria are added to the ASM framework effective July 03, 2026 (Annexure I).
- Securities no longer meeting the criteria are removed from the framework effective the same date (Annexure II).
- A consolidated list of all securities currently under the framework is provided for reference (Annexure III).
- This framework operates in conjunction with other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the Exchange.
- Inclusion under this framework is purely a market surveillance action and should not be interpreted as an adverse view of the company or entity.
- Securities exiting the framework have their price band reinstated to the level applicable before shortlisting, unless subject to another surveillance framework, in which case that framework's price band applies.

## Regulatory Changes

This circular is a routine periodic update under the existing IBC-linked ASM framework, originally established via Exchange notices dated June 08, 2018, May 20, 2022, February 10, 2023, and September 05, 2025. No new regulatory framework is introduced; this is an application of the existing criteria to current data.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must apply the updated surveillance restrictions to securities newly added under Annexure I from July 03, 2026.
- Members should note the price band reinstatement for securities exiting under Annexure II, subject to other applicable surveillance frameworks.
- Queries can be directed to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **July 02, 2026**: Notice issued.
- **July 03, 2026**: Effective date for additions and removals under the IBC surveillance framework.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is confined to the specific securities listed in the annexures (not detailed in the notice text), which will face additional trading restrictions such as price bands upon inclusion, or relief from such restrictions upon removal. Market-wide impact is limited, but affected companies and their shareholders/traders should closely review the annexures for specific scrip-level implications.
