## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Minolta Finance Ltd (Scrip Code: 532164) has fixed 17 July 2026 as the Record Date for its Rights Issue of Equity Shares. Transactions in the company's equity shares will be done on an Ex-Rights basis with effect from 17 July 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: Minolta Finance Ltd. (Scrip Code: 532164)
- Rights entitlement: 4 (Four) equity shares of Re.1/- each for cash at a premium of Rs.0.20 per share for every 1 (One) equity share held
- Terms of payment: Full amount of issue price payable on application
- Settlement segment: Dematerialised Securities – Rolling Settlement Segment (Sett. No. DR-674/2026-2027)

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing operations notice regarding a corporate action.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must ensure transactions in Minolta Finance Ltd equity shares are processed on an Ex-Rights basis effective from the record date/ex-date of 17 July 2026.

## Important Dates

- Record Date: 17 July 2026
- Ex-Rights Basis effective from: 17 July 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to shareholders and traders of Minolta Finance Ltd. Existing shareholders become entitled to subscribe for additional shares at a fixed ratio and price, and the stock price will adjust downward on the ex-date to reflect the rights entitlement. No broader market or sector-wide impact is expected.
