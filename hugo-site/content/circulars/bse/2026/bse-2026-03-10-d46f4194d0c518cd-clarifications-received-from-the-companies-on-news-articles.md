## Summary

BSE has published clarifications from two listed companies — RailTel Corporation of India Ltd. (Scrip Code: 543265) and Uno Minda Limited (BSE Scrip: 532539) — in response to BSE Surveillance queries regarding news items published in financial media. RailTel clarified its MoU with GAIL for building a digital infrastructure backbone, confirming it is non-binding and not a material event. Uno Minda responded to a news report about the launch of an advanced trumpet horn range for the aftermarket segment.

## Key Points

- RailTel entered into a non-binding MoU with GAIL on 5th March 2026 for building a digital infrastructure backbone.
- RailTel confirmed no definitive terms have been finalized under the MoU.
- The MoU does not qualify as a material event under Regulation 30 of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015.
- RailTel stated price/volume movement in its shares was unrelated to the news and attributed to general market conditions.
- RailTel confirmed no undisclosed material information exists that could explain share price movement.
- Uno Minda responded to a March 9, 2026 ET Auto article titled "Uno Minda launches advanced trumpet horn range for aftermarket".
- Both companies affirm ongoing compliance with timely disclosure obligations under applicable SEBI regulations.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. These are standard clarification disclosures filed under Regulation 30 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015, in response to BSE Surveillance queries.

## Compliance Requirements

- Both companies have confirmed compliance with Regulation 30 of SEBI LODR regarding material event disclosures.
- No additional compliance action is required from investors or market participants.
- Companies reaffirm commitment to prompt disclosure of any future material events.

## Important Dates

- 5th March 2026: RailTel–GAIL MoU signed.
- 9th March 2026: BSE email query sent to RailTel; news articles published in The Hindu BusinessLine (RailTel) and ET Auto (Uno Minda).
- 9th March 2026: RailTel clarification letter dated.
- 10th March 2026: Uno Minda clarification letter dated; BSE circular published.

## Impact Assessment

Market impact is minimal. RailTel's MoU with GAIL is explicitly described as non-binding with no finalized terms, and the company has categorically stated it is not a material event under SEBI LODR. Share price movement was attributed to broader market conditions. Uno Minda's disclosure relates to a routine product launch in the aftermarket segment. Investors should note these clarifications as confirmations of regulatory compliance rather than indicators of significant corporate developments.
