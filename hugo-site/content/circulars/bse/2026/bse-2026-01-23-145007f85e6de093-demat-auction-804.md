## Summary

BSE has published the demat auction list for Settlement No. 804 dated January 23, 2026. The circular lists approximately 112 securities from various companies with their respective scrip codes and quantities to be auctioned in the demat auction settlement process.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 804
- Settlement Date: January 23, 2026
- Total securities listed: Approximately 112 scrips across various companies
- Quantities range from 1 share to 157,348 shares depending on the security
- Major quantities include: Harshil Agro (157,348 shares), Rajnish Wellness (130,727 shares), ACI Infocom (28,412 shares)
- Covers securities across multiple sectors including auto, banking, IT, energy, pharmaceuticals, and consumer goods

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced in this circular. This is a routine operational notice for demat auction settlement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and brokers involved in the listed securities must participate in the auction settlement process
- Settlement must be completed as per BSE's demat auction settlement procedures
- Concerned parties must ensure delivery/payment obligations for the listed securities and quantities

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: January 23, 2026
- Settlement Number: 804

## Impact Assessment

This circular has medium impact on specific market participants:

- **Direct Impact**: Brokers and trading members who have failed to deliver the listed securities must participate in the auction
- **Securities Affected**: 112 securities from various companies across multiple sectors
- **Market Impact**: Limited to auction participants; no broader market-wide implications
- **Operational Impact**: Standard settlement process for handling delivery failures in the demat system
- **Investor Impact**: Minimal direct impact on retail investors; primarily affects institutional settlement processes
