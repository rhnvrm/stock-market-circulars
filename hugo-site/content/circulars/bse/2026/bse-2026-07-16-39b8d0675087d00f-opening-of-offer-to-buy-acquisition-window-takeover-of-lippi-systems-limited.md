## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing market participants about the opening of an Open Offer (Acquisition Window mechanism) made by five acquirers - Vinesh Shivji Dholu, Jagdish Shivji Dholu, Shivji Karamrashi Dholu, Jagruti Vinesh Dholu, and Parul Jagdish Dholu - to purchase equity shares of LIPPI SYSTEMS LIMITED from public shareholders. The offer is priced at Rs.56.84 per fully paid-up equity share (face value Rs.10) and is being made pursuant to SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 2011.

## Key Points

- Acquirers: Vinesh Shivji Dholu (Acquirer-1), Jagdish Shivji Dholu (Acquirer-2), Shivji Karamrashi Dholu (Acquirer-3), Jagruti Vinesh Dholu (Acquirer-4), and Parul Jagdish Dholu (Acquirer-5)
- Target Company: LIPPI SYSTEMS LIMITED
- Offer price: Rs.56.84 per equity share, payable in cash
- Offer size: Up to 33,82,231 equity shares, representing the entire public shareholding (25.05% of the Expanded Share Capital)
- Face value of shares: Rs.10 each
- Mechanism: Acquisition Window mechanism for Tender Offers under Takeover regulations, per SEBI circulars dated April 13, 2015 and Dec 09, 2016
- Letter of Offer attached for market participants' reference

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this notice implements existing SEBI (SAST) Regulations, 2011 (Regulations 3(1) and 4) and the Mechanism for acquisition of shares through Stock Exchange pursuant to Tender-Offers, as previously established via SEBI circulars (April 13, 2015 and Dec 09, 2016) and subsequent BSE notices (Feb 10, 2017; April 24, 2019; May 28, 2020; Nov 02, 2020; Aug 25, 2021).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members and Custodians must process tenders through the Acquisition Window mechanism in accordance with the Revised Guidelines.
- Market participants should refer to the Letter of Offer and BSE website (www.bseindia.com) for full offer details and procedural requirements.

## Important Dates

- Offer opens: Monday, July 20, 2026
- Offer closes: Friday, July 31, 2026
- Notice issued: July 16, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The open offer directly affects public shareholders of LIPPI SYSTEMS LIMITED, who have the opportunity to tender up to 25.05% of the expanded share capital at a fixed cash price of Rs.56.84 per share during the offer window. Trading Members and Custodians need to ensure operational readiness to process tenders through the Acquisition Window during the specified period. Broader market impact is limited, as this is a stock-specific corporate action rather than a market-wide policy change.
