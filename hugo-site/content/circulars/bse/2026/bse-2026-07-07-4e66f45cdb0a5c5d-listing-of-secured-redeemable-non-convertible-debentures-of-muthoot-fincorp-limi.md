## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that Secured Redeemable Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of Muthoot Fincorp Limited will be admitted to dealings on the Exchange with effect from Wednesday, July 08, 2026, and placed in the "F Group" (Debt segment). Detailed securities information is provided in an annexure to the notice.

## Key Points

- Securities involved: Secured Redeemable Non-Convertible Debentures of Muthoot Fincorp Limited
- Listing category: F Group, Debt segment
- Effective date of dealings: July 08, 2026
- As per SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000, the bonds will trade only in dematerialized form
- Trades will be executed in unit market lot (one bond per lot)
- Muthoot Fincorp Limited's financial year ends March 31

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is an implementation of existing SEBI guidelines (February 16, 2000) mandating demat-only trading for these NCDs.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure trades in this scrip are executed in dematerialized form and in unit market lot (one bond)
- Queries on the notice can be directed to Muthoot Fincorp's Compliance Officer/Company Secretary (Mr. Sachu Sivas) or BSE contact (Mr. Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President)

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 07, 2026
- Effective date of dealings: Wednesday, July 08, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt-segment listing notice affecting bondholders and trading members dealing in Muthoot Fincorp Limited's NCDs. It has minimal impact on broader equity markets but is relevant for debt market participants and the company's creditors tracking secondary market liquidity for these instruments.
