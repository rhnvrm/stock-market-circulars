## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Madhusudan Securities Ltd (Scrip Code: 511000) has changed its name to MSL Global Limited. The scrip ID and abbreviated name on the BOLT Plus System will be updated accordingly effective July 9, 2026.

## Key Points

- Existing name: Madhusudan Securities Ltd
- New name: MSL Global Limited
- Scrip Code: 511000 (unchanged)
- New Scrip ID for BOLT Plus System: MSLGLOBAL
- New Abbreviated Name on BOLT Plus System: MSLGLOBAL
- Change is effective from July 9, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is a company-initiated name change communicated to the Exchange, resulting in an update to the scrip's identification details on the trading system.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should update their systems and records to reflect the new scrip ID and abbreviated name (MSLGLOBAL) for the company w.e.f. July 9, 2026. The scrip code (511000) remains unchanged.

## Important Dates

- Effective Date of Name Change on BOLT Plus System: July 9, 2026
- Notice Date: July 3, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative update with minimal market impact. Trading members and systems referencing the scrip by its abbreviated name/ID need to update to MSLGLOBAL from July 9, 2026 to avoid order routing or reference errors. No impact on shareholding, corporate structure, or financials.
