## Summary

Authum Investment & Infrastructure Limited (Scrip Code: 539177) has announced a bonus issue of 67,93,80,400 equity shares of Re. 1 each. The deemed date of allotment is January 14, 2026, with effective listing scheduled for January 15, 2026. The new shares will have distinctive numbers ranging from 169845101 to 849225500.

## Key Points

- Company: Authum Investment & Infrastructure Limited
- Scrip Code: 539177
- ISIN: INE206F01022
- Number of bonus shares: 67,93,80,400 equity shares of Re. 1 each
- Distinctive number range: 169845101 to 849225500
- Deemed date of allotment: January 14, 2026
- Effective listing date: January 15, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Existing shareholders will receive bonus shares as per the bonus ratio determined by the company. No action required from shareholders as the allotment will be credited automatically to their demat accounts.

## Important Dates

- **January 7, 2026**: Circular issued
- **January 14, 2026**: Deemed date of allotment
- **January 15, 2026**: Effective listing date of bonus shares

## Impact Assessment

The bonus issue will significantly increase the outstanding equity shares of Authum Investment & Infrastructure Limited by 67.94 crore shares. While this dilutes the share price proportionately, it does not affect the ownership percentage of existing shareholders. The bonus issue typically improves liquidity and makes shares more affordable for retail investors. Trading will commence for the new shares from January 15, 2026.
