## Summary

BSE has issued the client margin reporting schedule for the Electronic Gold Receipts segment for February 2026. The circular specifies the last date for margin reporting and penalty dates corresponding to each trade date throughout the month.

## Key Points

- Notice applies to Electronic Gold Receipts segment only
- Covers all trading days in February 2026
- Margin reporting deadline is typically 5 business days after trade date
- Penalty date is typically 1 business day after the margin reporting deadline
- Schedule extends into early March 2026 for trades executed at the end of February

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational schedule.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must report client margins by the specified last date for each trade date
- Failure to report by the deadline will result in penalties being applied on the penalty date
- Members trading in Electronic Gold Receipts segment must adhere to this schedule
