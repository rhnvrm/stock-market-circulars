## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in Commercial Paper issued by Julius Baer Capital (India) Private Limited (Scrip Code: 730495, ISIN: INE824H14SQ1) effective February 02, 2026, as the instrument is maturing on the redemption date. Trading members are advised not to deal in this Commercial Paper from the effective date.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Julius Baer Capital (India) Private Limited reaching maturity
- Scrip Code: 730495
- ISIN: INE824H14SQ1
- Trading suspension effective from February 02, 2026
- Notice Number: DR-811/2025-2026
- Category: Company related - Debt Segment

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for instrument maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in the specified Commercial Paper from February 02, 2026
- Members are required to take note of the suspension and ensure compliance

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: February 01, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date**: February 02, 2026
- **Redemption/Maturity Date**: February 02, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact as this is a routine suspension due to instrument maturity. Only affects holders and traders of this specific Commercial Paper. No broader market implications expected. Standard procedure for debt instruments reaching redemption date.
