## Summary

BSE Index Services Private Limited has announced the addition of two newly listed companies — Yaashvi Jewellers Limited and Harikanta Overseas Limited — to the BSE SME IPO Index. Both stocks were listed on BSE on June 02, 2026, and will be included in the index from the open of trading on June 03, 2026.

## Key Points

- Yaashvi Jewellers Limited (Exchange ticker: 544770) added to BSE SME IPO Index effective June 03, 2026.
- Harikanta Overseas Limited (Exchange ticker: 544769) added to BSE SME IPO Index effective June 03, 2026.
- Both companies were listed on BSE on June 02, 2026, per Notice Nos. 20260601-25 and 20260601-23 respectively.
- The index additions have been included in the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE) for June 02, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index composition update following new SME IPO listings on BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance actions required for market participants. Index-tracking funds and products referencing the BSE SME IPO Index should account for the addition of these two constituents from the open of June 03, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **June 02, 2026**: Listing date for Yaashvi Jewellers Limited and Harikanta Overseas Limited on BSE.
- **June 03, 2026**: Effective date for addition of both stocks to the BSE SME IPO Index (from market open).

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to index-linked products tracking the BSE SME IPO Index. Both additions are standard post-listing inclusions for SME IPOs and do not signal any broader market or regulatory change. Investors in SME IPO index funds or ETFs may see minor portfolio adjustments to reflect the new constituents.
