## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Simplex Castings Ltd (Scrip Code: 513472) has fixed 20 July 2026 as the Record Date for subdivision (stock split) of its equity shares. Each existing equity share of Rs.10 face value will be subdivided into five equity shares of Rs.2 face value each.

## Key Points

- Company: Simplex Castings Ltd (Scrip Code: 513472, ISIN: INE658D01011)
- Corporate action: Subdivision of one equity share of Rs.10/- face value into five equity shares of Rs.2/- each
- Record Date: 20 July 2026
- Effective date (w.e.f.): 20 July 2026
- Reference: DR-675/2026-2027

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulation; this is a company-specific corporate action notified by BSE Listing Operations department under the Corporate Actions category.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that the existing ISIN INE658D01011 (Rs.10/- paid-up) will not be valid for transactions on the Exchange on or after 20/07/2026.
- A new ISIN for the Rs.2/- paid-up shares will be announced separately by BSE in a subsequent notice.
- Depositories and trading systems need to update records to reflect the new share structure from the record date.

## Important Dates

- 06 July 2026: Notice issued
- 20 July 2026: Record Date and effective date for subdivision; old ISIN ceases to be valid for trading from this date

## Impact Assessment

The subdivision increases the number of outstanding shares fivefold while proportionately reducing the face value and market price per share, with no change in shareholders' overall investment value. This typically improves stock liquidity and affordability for retail investors. Trading members and depository participants must ensure systems are updated to handle the ISIN transition to avoid settlement issues around the record date.
