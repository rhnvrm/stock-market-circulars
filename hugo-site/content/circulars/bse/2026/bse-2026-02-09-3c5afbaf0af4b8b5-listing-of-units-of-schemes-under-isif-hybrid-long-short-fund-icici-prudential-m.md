## Summary

BSE has listed units of ICICI Prudential Mutual Fund's iSIF Hybrid Long-Short Fund effective February 10, 2026. Two schemes are listed: Growth (23.87 crore units, Scrip Code 544696) and Direct Plan Growth (19.76 crore units, Scrip Code 544697). Both schemes are interval investment strategy funds investing in equity and debt securities with limited short exposure through derivatives, admitted to the 'B' Group with face value Rs.10 per unit.

## Key Points

- Listing effective date: February 10, 2026
- Fund type: iSIF Hybrid Long-Short Fund (interval investment strategy)
- Investment strategy: Equity, debt securities with limited short exposure through derivatives
- Two schemes listed: Growth and Direct Plan Growth
- Both listed in 'B' Group with market lot of 1 unit
- Face value and issue price: Rs.10.00 per unit
- Date of allotment: February 4, 2026
- Units must be traded in dematerialized form only

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine mutual fund listing with minimal market impact. It provides investors with exchange-traded access to ICICI Prudential's interval hybrid fund that employs long-short strategies across equity and debt. The listing expands investment options for market participants seeking hybrid fund exposure with derivative strategies. No impact on equity markets or individual stocks.
