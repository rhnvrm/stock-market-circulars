## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that Mizuho Capsave Finance Private Limited has fixed 16 March 2026 as the record date for part redemption of non-convertible debentures and payment of interest on two NCD series. Trading in these debentures will be with reduced face value effective 16 March 2026 under Settlement No. DR-840/2025-2026.

## Key Points

- Two NCD series of Mizuho Capsave Finance Private Limited are subject to part redemption
- Record date for both series: 16 March 2026
- Purpose: Part redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Both series trade on the Debt segment of BSE
- Settlement number applicable: DR-840/2025-2026
- Effective date for reduced face value: 16 March 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a corporate action notification under existing BSE debt market operational framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that both NCD series will trade with reduced face value from 16 March 2026
- Members should update their systems to reflect the new face values post record date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 12 March 2026
- **Record Date:** 16 March 2026
- **Effective Date for Reduced Face Value:** 16 March 2026
- **Settlement:** DR-840/2025-2026
