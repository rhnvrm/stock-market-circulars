## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that Cybertech Systems and Software Ltd (BSE Code: 532173) has fixed **29 May 2026** as the record date to determine the entitlement and names of equity shareholders eligible to participate in its buyback offer under the Tender Offer route.

## Key Points

- Company: Cybertech Systems and Software Ltd (BSE Code: 532173)
- Record Date: 29 May 2026
- Ex-Entitlement Date: 29 May 2026
- Settlement Number: DR-640/2026-2027
- Segment: Dematerialised Securities – Rolling Settlement
- Purpose: Determination of eligible shareholders for the buyback offer

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification under BSE's listing compliance framework.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to take note of the record date and ex-entitlement date. Shareholders holding equity shares of Cybertech Systems and Software Ltd as of 29 May 2026 will be considered for participation eligibility in the buyback offer.

## Important Dates

- **Record Date:** 29 May 2026
- **Ex-Entitlement Date:** 29 May 2026
- **Settlement:** DR-640/2026-2027
- **Notice Date:** 26 May 2026

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited to existing shareholders of Cybertech Systems and Software Ltd. Shareholders holding shares on or before the record date of 29 May 2026 will be eligible to tender shares in the buyback. Trading members should update client records accordingly and communicate the buyback eligibility window to relevant clients holding scrip code 532173.
