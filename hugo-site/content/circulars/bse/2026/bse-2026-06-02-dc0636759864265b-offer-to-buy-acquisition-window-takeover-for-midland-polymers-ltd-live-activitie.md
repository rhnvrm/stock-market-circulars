## Summary

BSE has announced the live activities schedule for the Offer to Buy (OTB) – Acquisition Window under the Takeover framework for MIDLAND POLYMERS LTD (Symbol: MIDPOLY, Scrip Code: 531597). The offer opens on 3rd June 2026 and closes on 16th June 2026, with an offer price of Rs. 10.00 per share for up to 9,750,000 equity shares.

## Key Points

- Offer size: Up to 97,50,000 equity shares at Rs. 10.00 per share (face value Rs. 10.00)
- ISIN: INE046M01036; Scrip Code: 531597; Market lot: 1
- Bid Offering Period: 3rd June 2026 to 16th June 2026, 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.
- Give-up / Take-up period: 3rd June 2026 to 15th June 2026, 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.
- On the last day (16th June 2026): Give-up until 3:30 p.m.; Take-up extended to 4:00 p.m.
- Bidding categories: FII, FI, MF, IC, Other QIBs (OTH), Other NIB (NOH), Company (CO), Individual (IND)
- OTB platforms: iBBS (internet: https://ibbs.bseindia.com/) and RTRMS (internet: https://rtrms.bseindia.com/)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice operationalises the existing OTB – Acquisition Window (Takeover) framework as per Exchange Notices 20150930-1 and 20170203-29.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must ensure investors' UCC is registered and updated with the correct PAN Number and Category in the Exchange UCC database.
- Members can bid only for investors holding shares in demat form.
- Members and Custodians should note that if no securities are accepted, no End of Issue files will be generated on the Extranet.
- For login creation and password resets, contact Trading Operations at 022-22728005 or user.trdops@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **Opening Date:** Wednesday, 3rd June 2026
- **Closing Date:** Tuesday, 16th June 2026
- **Bid Offering Period:** 3rd June – 16th June 2026 (9:15 a.m. – 3:30 p.m.)
- **Give-up / Take-up:** 3rd June – 15th June 2026 (9:15 a.m. – 3:30 p.m.)
- **Last-day Give-up:** 16th June 2026 (9:15 a.m. – 3:30 p.m.)
- **Last-day Take-up:** 16th June 2026 (9:15 a.m. – 4:00 p.m.)

## Impact Assessment

This notice is primarily operational and relevant to trading members and custodians facilitating the takeover acquisition window for MIDLAND POLYMERS LTD. The offer price of Rs. 10.00 equals the face value, suggesting a distressed or regulatory-driven takeover scenario. The two-week window provides adequate time for members to arrange bids. Investors holding MIDPOLY shares in demat form should consult their trading member to participate. Broader market impact is limited given the company's scale.
