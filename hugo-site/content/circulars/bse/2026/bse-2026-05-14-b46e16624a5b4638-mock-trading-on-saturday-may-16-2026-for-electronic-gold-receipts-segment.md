## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Electronic Gold Receipts (EGR) segment on Saturday, May 16, 2026. Trading members using third-party trading platforms of empaneled vendors or in-house developed systems through ETI APIs are encouraged to participate and test their trading applications, including exceptional market condition scenarios such as risk-reduction mode and trading halt.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for EGR segment on May 16, 2026
- Accessible via ETI APIs for third-party and in-house developed trading systems
- No new BOLT TWS Release for this session
- Trades from mock session carry no margin, pay-in, or pay-out obligations and create no legal rights or liabilities
- Members are requested to provide feedback by 3:00 PM on the day of mock trading

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notice for a scheduled mock trading session.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members using third-party or in-house ETI API-based systems should participate to validate their system readiness
- Members must submit feedback on the mock trading session by 3:00 PM on May 16, 2026, via BSE Tech Support or BSE Helpdesk contacts

## Important Dates

- **May 16, 2026**: Mock trading session for EGR segment
- **Log-in**: 9:15 AM – 10:00 AM
- **Pre-Open Order Entry**: 10:00 AM – 10:08 AM
- **Pre-Open Matching**: 10:08 AM – 10:15 AM
- **Continuous Trading**: 10:15 AM – 2:00 PM
- **Block Deal Window**: 12:00 PM – 12:15 PM
- **Closing**: 2:00 PM – 2:10 PM
- **Post-Closing**: 2:10 PM – 2:20 PM
- **Feedback Deadline**: 3:00 PM on May 16, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine mock session intended solely for system testing and familiarization. No actual trades, margins, or settlement obligations will be generated. Members using ETI API-based platforms for EGR trading should use this opportunity to validate their systems under simulated market conditions including stress scenarios like risk-reduction mode and trading halts.
