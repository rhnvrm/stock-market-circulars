## Summary

BSE has listed 24,90,733 new equity shares of Suditi Industries Ltd. (Scrip Code: 521113, ISIN: INE691D01012), issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis at an issue price of Rs. 59.12/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- + premium Rs. 49.12/-). These shares are permitted to trade on BSE with effect from Tuesday, June 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- 24,90,733 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 49.12/-, totalling an issue price of Rs. 59.12/- per share
- Shares allotted to non-promoters on a preferential basis
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- ISIN: INE691D01012
- Trading permitted from June 30, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification for newly allotted equity shares.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed that the new securities are listed and permitted to trade from June 30, 2026. No specific compliance action is required from members beyond awareness of the new shares being available for trading.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment dates:** 24 March 2026 (6,95,100 shares), 27 March 2026 (4,33,100 shares), 30 March 2026 (8,22,733 shares), 31 March 2026 (5,39,800 shares)
- **Trading commencement:** June 30, 2026
- **Lock-in expiry:** December 31, 2026 (applicable to all 24,90,733 shares, Dist. Nos. 47116292 to 49607024)

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 24,90,733 shares to the tradable float of Suditi Industries Ltd. However, all newly listed shares are under lock-in until December 31, 2026, meaning they cannot be sold in the market until that date. The effective increase in freely tradable shares occurs only after the lock-in period expires. Market impact is limited given the lock-in restriction and the relatively small size of the issuance.
