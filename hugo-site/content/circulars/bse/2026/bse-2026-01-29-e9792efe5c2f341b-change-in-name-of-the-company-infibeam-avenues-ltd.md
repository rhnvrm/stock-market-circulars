## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation for the name change of Infibeam Avenues Limited (CIN: L64203GJ2010PLC061366) to AvenuesAI Limited, effective January 20, 2026. The certificate was issued by the Central Processing Centre, ROC on January 20, 2026. The company was originally incorporated as Infibeam Incorporation Limited.

## Key Points

- Company name changed from INFIBEAM AVENUES LIMITED to AVENUESAI LIMITED
- Effective date: January 20, 2026
- Corporate Identification Number (CIN): L64203GJ2010PLC061366
- Company type: Limited by shares
- Certificate issued by Assistant/Deputy/Registrar of Companies, Central Processing Centre
- Original incorporation name: Infibeam Incorporation Limited
- Registered address: 28th Floor, GIFT Two Building, Block No. 56, Road-5C, Zone-5, GIFT CITY, Gandhinagar-382355, Gujarat, India

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are involved. This is a corporate name change action processed under rule 29 of the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display the old name (Infibeam Avenues Limited) along with the new name (AvenuesAI Limited) for a period of two years at all places where the company is required to display its name under Section 12 of the Companies Act
- The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders
- All existing contracts, registrations, and legal documents remain valid under the new name

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date of Name Change**: January 20, 2026
- **Certificate Issue Date**: January 20, 2026
- **Old Name Display Requirement**: Two years from January 20, 2026 (until January 20, 2028)

## Impact Assessment

**Corporate Impact**: The name change reflects a strategic rebranding initiative, likely to emphasize the company's focus on AI technologies. The change from "Infibeam Avenues" to "AvenuesAI" signals a pivot or enhanced focus on artificial intelligence services.

**Market Impact**: Medium - While the name change does not affect the company's operations, financial position, or shareholder rights, it requires awareness among investors, traders, and market participants for proper identification. Stock ticker symbols may be updated by the exchange.

**Stakeholder Impact**: All stakeholders (shareholders, creditors, customers, vendors) should note the new legal name for correspondence and transactions. The CIN remains unchanged, ensuring continuity in regulatory records and filings.

**Operational Continuity**: No disruption to business operations. All existing contracts, licenses, and registrations continue under the new name without requiring fresh execution.
