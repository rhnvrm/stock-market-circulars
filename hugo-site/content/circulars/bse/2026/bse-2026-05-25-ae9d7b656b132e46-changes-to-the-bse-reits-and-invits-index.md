## Summary

BSE Index Services Private Ltd (formerly Asia Index Pvt. Ltd.) announced changes to the BSE REITS AND INVITS INDEX following a fast track IPO review. Raajmarg Infra Investment Trust (scrip code 544734) will be added to the index effective at the open of Tuesday, June 02, 2026.

## Key Points

- Raajmarg Infra Investment Trust (scrip code 544734) is being added to the BSE REITS AND INVITS INDEX.
- The addition is triggered by a fast track IPO review conducted for the index.
- The change takes effect at market open on Tuesday, June 02, 2026.
- The action is included in the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE) dated May 25, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory rule changes are involved. This is a constituent change to the BSE REITS AND INVITS INDEX driven by BSE Index Services' standard fast track IPO review process.

## Compliance Requirements

Index fund managers, ETF managers, and other investment products benchmarked to the BSE REITS AND INVITS INDEX should update their portfolios to reflect the new constituent ahead of or at the open on June 02, 2026. Queries may be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **May 25, 2026**: Announcement date; corporate action file (*.SDE) updated.
- **June 02, 2026**: Effective date — Raajmarg Infra Investment Trust added to BSE REITS AND INVITS INDEX at market open.

## Impact Assessment

The addition of Raajmarg Infra Investment Trust broadens the BSE REITS AND INVITS INDEX. Passive funds and ETFs tracking this index will need to rebalance accordingly. The impact is moderate for index-tracking vehicles but minimal for broader equity markets, as REITS/InvITs form a niche segment. Market participants benchmarked to this index should account for potential rebalancing-driven trading activity around the effective date.
