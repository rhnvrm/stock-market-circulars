## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the 182-day Treasury Bill (182TB17726, ISIN IN002025Y412, scrip code 805167) listed in the Debt segment is set to mature on its redemption date. Consequently, trading members are advised not to deal in this T-bill with effect from July 15, 2026.

## Key Points

- Security affected: 182TB17726 (182-Day Treasury Bill)
- ISIN: IN002025Y412
- Scrip Code: 805167
- Trading in this security will be suspended effective July 15, 2026
- Reference: DR-672/2026-2027
- Notice issued by Listing Operations Department, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a routine administrative notice implementing standard procedure for suspending trading in government securities/T-bills upon maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of the Exchange must not deal with the aforementioned T-bill from July 15, 2026 onwards. No other compliance action is required.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 14, 2026
- Trading Suspension Effective Date: July 15, 2026 (redemption/maturity date)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, low-impact notice limited to the debt/government securities segment. It affects only trading members dealing in this specific T-bill and has no bearing on equity markets or other listed securities. The suspension follows standard practice upon instrument maturity.
