## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in bonds of IDBI BANK LTD effective January 23, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption purposes. Trading members are advised to cease all dealings in the specified bond from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspended for IDBI BANK LTD bond with ISIN INE008A08V26 (Code: 953385)
- Bond series: IDBI-8.80%-9-2-26-PVT (8.80% coupon rate)
- Record date for redemption: January 23, 2026
- No dealings permitted from: January 23, 2026
- Notice number: DR-805/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard operational circular implementing existing bond redemption procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any transactions in the specified IDBI BANK bond (INE008A08V26) from January 23, 2026 onwards
- All market participants should update their trading systems to reflect the suspension
- Ensure client orders for this security are rejected from the suspension date

## Important Dates

- **January 20, 2026**: Circular issued
- **January 23, 2026**: Record date for bond redemption
- **January 23, 2026**: Trading suspension effective date (no dealings from this date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders and potential traders of this specific IDBI BANK bond series. The suspension is temporary and related to the redemption process, affecting only one debt instrument.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must ensure compliance systems are updated to prevent inadvertent trading in the suspended security. Bondholders as of the record date will be eligible for redemption proceeds.

**Investor Impact**: Investors holding this bond will receive redemption proceeds. No trading liquidity available from suspension date, which is standard for bonds approaching redemption.
