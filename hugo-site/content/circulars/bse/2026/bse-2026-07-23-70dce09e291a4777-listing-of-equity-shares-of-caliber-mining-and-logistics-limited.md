## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the equity shares of Caliber Mining and Logistics Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The scrip code, symbol, and ISIN have been assigned, though the exact listing date and further security details will be announced through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Equity shares of Caliber Mining and Logistics Limited are set to be listed on BSE.
- Scrip Code: 544833
- Symbol: CMLL
- ISIN: INE11XY01018
- No attachments were included with this notice.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a standard listing notification under BSE's DCS-Listing department, Company related category, Equity segment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should update their systems to recognize the new scrip code, symbol, and ISIN once trading commences. No other compliance action is required at this stage.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 23 July 2026
- Listing date: To be announced via a separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This notice formally introduces a new tradeable equity security to the exchange. Market impact is limited to participants tracking new listings or the specific scrip; there is no effect on existing listed securities or overall market operations.
