## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of three new Treasury Bills (T-Bills) on the Exchange under G GROUP - DEBT INSTRUMENTS, effective March 06, 2026. The securities span 91-day, 182-day, and 364-day tenors and will be available for dealings on the Debt segment.

## Key Points

- Three new T-Bills listed effective March 06, 2026 on BSE Debt segment under G GROUP - DEBT INSTRUMENTS
- Covers short-term (91-day), medium-term (182-day), and long-term (364-day) Treasury Bills
- Trading will be suspended two working days prior to each bill's maturity/redemption date (excluding bank holidays)
- Contact for clarifications: 2272 8352 / 5753 / 8597

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational listing notice.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must note that the listed T-Bills will not be available for trading two (2) working days prior to the Maturity/Redemption Date (Maturity Date minus two trading days, excluding Bank Holidays).

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This is a standard routine listing of government Treasury Bills issued by the RBI, providing trading members access to short, medium, and long-tenor sovereign debt instruments on BSE. No changes to trading rules or compliance frameworks are introduced.
