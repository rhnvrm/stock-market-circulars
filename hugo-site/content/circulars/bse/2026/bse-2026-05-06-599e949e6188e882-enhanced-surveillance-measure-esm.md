## Summary

BSE has issued updates to the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework (Notice No. 20260506-29). New securities meeting ESM criteria will attract a minimum 100% margin effective May 08, 2026, and will be shifted from Rolling Settlement to Trade-for-Trade settlement. Securities being downgraded to Stage I and those exiting the framework are also notified.

## Key Points

- New securities included under ESM (Annexure I) will attract minimum 100% margin on all open positions as on May 07, 2026 and new positions from May 08, 2026
- Securities being moved to lower Stage I under ESM Framework effective May 07, 2026 are listed in Annexure I
- Securities qualifying under ESM will shift from Rolling Settlement to Trade-for-Trade segment effective May 08, 2026
- Securities moving to Stage II will be placed under Trade-for-Trade with a 2% price band and Periodic Call Auction effective May 07, 2026
- Securities exiting the ESM Framework effective May 07, 2026 are listed in Annexure II
- A consolidated list of all securities under the ESM framework is provided in Annexure III
- ESM framework operates alongside all other prevailing surveillance measures

## Regulatory Changes

This notice updates the ESM framework with reference to prior notices dated June 02, 2023, July 18, 2023, August 09, 2024, September 20, 2024, October 04, 2024, and July 25, 2025. Securities are reclassified across ESM stages based on periodic review of market surveillance criteria.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure 100% margin collection on all open positions in newly included ESM securities as on May 07, 2026
- Trading members must account for Trade-for-Trade settlement for all ESM-listed securities from the effective dates
- Stage II securities must be traded within the 2% price band under Periodic Call Auction mechanism
- Price bands for securities exiting ESM will revert to pre-ESM levels unless the security is part of another surveillance measure, in which case that framework's price band prevails
- For clarifications, members may contact bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **May 07, 2026**: Stage I movement effective; Stage II Trade-for-Trade with 2% price band and Periodic Call Auction effective; ESM exits effective
- **May 08, 2026**: 100% margin requirement effective on open positions and new positions; Rolling Settlement to Trade-for-Trade shift effective for newly included securities

## Impact Assessment

The ESM framework update significantly restricts trading conditions for affected securities. The 100% margin requirement substantially increases the cost of holding or creating positions, likely reducing liquidity. The shift from Rolling Settlement to Trade-for-Trade eliminates netting benefits and increases settlement risk awareness. Stage II securities face further constraints with a tight 2% price band and Periodic Call Auction, which can limit price discovery and reduce trading volumes. Market participants with existing positions in affected securities must arrange additional margin by May 07, 2026. BSE has clarified that ESM inclusion is a surveillance action and not an adverse regulatory finding against the company.
