## Summary

BSE Commodity Exchange (BCX) has issued the Tender Period Margin Tracker for July 2026. This notice, issued by BSECL under the Settlement/RMS category, specifies the margin requirements that will be applicable during the tender period for commodity derivatives contracts expiring in July 2026. An annexure (Annexure_July) is attached with detailed margin schedules.

## Key Points

- Tender Period Margin Tracker is published for July 2026 commodity derivatives contracts.
- Issued by BSECL under the Settlement/RMS department.
- Applicable to the Commodity Derivatives segment on BSE Commodity Exchange (BCX).
- Detailed margin rates are provided in the attached Annexure_July.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is a routine monthly publication updating margin parameters for the upcoming tender period.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and participants in BCX commodity derivatives must ensure adequate margins are maintained as per the tracker during the tender period for July 2026 expiring contracts.
- Members should review the attached Annexure_July for contract-specific tender period margin rates.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 25 June 2026
- Applicable Period: July 2026 tender period for commodity derivatives contracts

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a moderate operational impact limited to members and clients holding positions in BCX commodity derivatives contracts due for expiry in July 2026. Elevated tender period margins are standard risk management practice to account for delivery risk as contracts approach expiry. Members should adjust collateral and margin funding accordingly ahead of the tender period.
