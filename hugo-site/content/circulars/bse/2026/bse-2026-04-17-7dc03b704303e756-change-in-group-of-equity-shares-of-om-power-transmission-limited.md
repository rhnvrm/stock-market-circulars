## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260417-14 informs trading members that the equity shares of Om Power Transmission Limited (Scrip Code: 544750) will be moved from the Trade for Trade segment (T Group) to the Rolling segment under B Group, effective Monday, May 04, 2026. This is a continuation of the earlier notice no. 20260416-40 dated April 16, 2026.

## Key Points

- Om Power Transmission Limited (Scrip Code: 544750) is being transferred from T Group (Trade for Trade) to B Group (Rolling segment).
- The transfer is effective from Monday, May 04, 2026.
- This notice is issued in continuation of BSE Notice No. 20260416-40 dated April 16, 2026.
- Trading members may contact Mr. Parag Jain at Tel. No. 022 2272 8685 for further details.

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares of Om Power Transmission Limited will no longer trade under the Trade for Trade (T Group) mechanism. They will shift to the Rolling segment under B Group, which follows standard rolling settlement (T+1) norms instead of mandatory delivery settlement applicable in the T Group.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should update their systems and client notifications to reflect the change in trading segment for Scrip Code 544750.
- No specific filing or compliance action is required from trading members beyond awareness and system updates.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** April 17, 2026
- **Effective Date:** Monday, May 04, 2026 — transfer from T Group to B Group (Rolling segment)
- **Prior Notice Reference:** BSE Notice No. 20260416-40 dated April 16, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The transfer from the Trade for Trade segment to the Rolling segment (B Group) is generally a positive development for the stock, as it improves liquidity by allowing intraday trading and standard rolling settlement. Investors and traders who were restricted under the T Group's mandatory delivery norms will have greater flexibility post-transition. The change may attract higher trading volumes and tighter bid-ask spreads for Om Power Transmission Limited shares starting May 04, 2026.
