## Summary

BSE has provisionally empanelled Bull8.AI Solutions Private Limited as an algorithmic trading solutions provider for trading members of the Exchange. The empanelment is effective immediately. Trading members can now engage with this vendor for algorithmic trading solutions, and the complete list of empanelled algo providers is available on the BSE website.

## Key Points

- Bull8.AI Solutions Private Limited has been provisionally empanelled with BSE
- The vendor will provide algorithmic trading solutions to trading members
- Empanelment is on a provisional basis
- Effective immediately from 06 January 2026
- Vendor contact: +91 7042753487
- Vendor address: Spectrum Metro Mall, Sector-D, Sector-75, Gautam Buddha Noida – 201301
- Updated list of all algo providers available on BSE website

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an administrative update regarding vendor empanelment.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. Trading members who wish to use algorithmic trading solutions may engage with the newly empanelled provider. Selection of algo providers remains at the discretion of trading members.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: 06 January 2026 (immediate effect)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is an informational circular that expands the choice of algo providers available to trading members.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Trading members who use or plan to use algorithmic trading solutions now have an additional vendor option. No changes required to existing operations or systems.

**Member Impact**: Optional. Trading members are not required to take any action unless they wish to engage with the newly empanelled algo provider.

**Additional Information**: The complete and updated list of all empanelled algo providers can be accessed at: https://www.bseindia.com/static/markets/Derivatives/DeriReports/market_information.html
