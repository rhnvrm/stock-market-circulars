## Summary

BSE has informed members that Motilal Oswal Mutual Fund has communicated a change in the settlement type of the Motilal Oswal Multi Asset Passive Fund of Fund on the BSE StAR MF platform, effective immediately. The settlement cycle has been shortened from T+3 to T+2.

## Key Points

- Scheme affected: Motilal Oswal Multi Asset Passive Fund of Fund
- Previous settlement type: T+3
- Revised settlement type: T+2
- Effective date: Immediate (from 24 June 2026)
- Platform: BSE StAR MF

## Regulatory Changes

The settlement cycle for the specified scheme has been revised from T+3 to T+2, aligning it with a faster settlement standard on the BSE StAR MF platform.

## Compliance Requirements

Members and distributors transacting in Motilal Oswal Multi Asset Passive Fund of Fund on the BSE StAR MF platform should note the revised settlement timeline (T+2) and update their operational and fund accounting processes accordingly.

## Important Dates

- Effective Date: 24 June 2026 (immediate effect)

## Impact Assessment

The change affects only one scheme — Motilal Oswal Multi Asset Passive Fund of Fund — and shortens the settlement window by one day (T+3 to T+2). This improves liquidity for investors and reduces counterparty risk. Operational impact is limited to members and back-office teams handling this specific scheme on the BSE StAR MF platform.
