## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260224-13 informs market participants that additional equity shares issued by seven companies under their Employee Stock Option Plan (ESOP) / Employee Stock Option Scheme (ESOS) are listed and admitted for trading on the BSE with effect from **Wednesday, February 25, 2026**. None of the newly listed shares are subject to any lock-in period.

## Key Points

- A total of 7 companies have had new ESOP/ESOS shares listed effective February 25, 2026.
- No lock-in restrictions apply to any of the newly listed shares.
- The largest issuance by volume is HDFC Bank Limited with 1,360,302 new shares.
- Xtglobal Infotech Limited issued 561,500 new shares (second largest by volume).
- All shares are admitted for trading from February 25, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice pursuant to existing ESOP/ESOS listing obligations under BSE regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action is required from market participants. The newly listed shares are immediately available for trading with no restrictions.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** February 24, 2026
- **Effective Date for Trading:** February 25, 2026 (Wednesday)
- **Lock-in Expiry:** Not Applicable (all shares are freely tradable)
