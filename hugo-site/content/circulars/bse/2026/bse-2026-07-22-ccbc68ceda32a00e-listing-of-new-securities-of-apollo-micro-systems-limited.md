## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 33,85,362 new equity shares of Apollo Micro Systems Limited (Scrip Code: 540879) are listed and permitted to trade with effect from Thursday, July 23, 2026. These shares were issued at a premium on a preferential basis to non-promoters, pursuant to conversion of warrants, and rank pari-passu with the existing equity shares of the company.

## Key Points

- 33,85,362 equity shares of face value Rs. 1/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 113/- per share (issue price Rs. 114/-).
- Shares allotted to non-promoters on a preferential basis, arising from conversion of warrants.
- Distinctive numbers: 357305441 to 360690802.
- Date of allotment: 08/06/2026.
- ISIN: INE713T01028.
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of Apollo Micro Systems Limited.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is a listing approval notice under BSE Listing Operations for securities arising from warrant conversion under a preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must recognize the new securities as listed and permitted to trade from the effective date; the shares are subject to a lock-in restriction as specified below.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: 08/06/2026
- Listing/Trading Effective Date: Thursday, July 23, 2026
- Lock-in expiry: 23/01/2027 (for all 33,85,362 shares, distinctive numbers 357305441 to 360690802)

## Impact Assessment

The listing modestly increases Apollo Micro Systems' outstanding equity share capital via warrant conversion, a routine corporate action. Since the shares are locked in until January 23, 2027, there is no immediate free-float dilution or selling pressure. Impact on trading and stock price is expected to be limited.
