## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted for trading further securities issued by six companies under their Employee Stock Option Plan (ESOP) / Employee Stock Option Scheme (ESOS) programs, effective Friday, March 6, 2026. A total of 571,238 new shares across six companies are now available for trading with no lock-in restrictions.

## Key Points

- Six companies have new ESOP/ESOS shares listed effective March 6, 2026
- None of the newly listed shares carry any lock-in restrictions (all marked NA)
- Aditya Birla Capital Limited (540691) lists the highest number of shares: 270,988 shares
- ICICI Bank Limited (532174) lists shares with face value of ₹2, the lowest among the listed companies
- IIFL Capital Services Limited (542773) and ICICI Bank Limited (532174) also have face value of ₹2 per share
- Notice issued by Marian Dsouza (Assistant Vice President) and Bhakti Wankhede (Deputy Manager), BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice for admission of ESOP/ESOS shares to trading.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance actions required from market participants. The notice is informational, advising all market participants to take note of the new securities admitted for trading.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** March 5, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** Friday, March 6, 2026
