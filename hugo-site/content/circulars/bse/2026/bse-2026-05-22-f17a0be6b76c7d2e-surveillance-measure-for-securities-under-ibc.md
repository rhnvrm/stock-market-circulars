## Summary

BSE has updated its Additional Surveillance Measure (ASM) framework for companies under the Insolvency Resolution Process (IRP) as per the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code (IBC). With effect from May 25, 2026, certain securities have been added to the IBC surveillance framework (Annexure I), certain securities have been removed (Annexure II), and a consolidated list of all securities currently under the framework is provided (Annexure III).

## Key Points

- Securities listed in Annexure I satisfy the criteria for inclusion under the Additional Surveillance Measure for IRP companies and will be placed under the IBC framework from May 25, 2026.
- Securities listed in Annexure II are being removed from the IBC framework effective May 25, 2026.
- A consolidated list of all securities currently under the framework is provided in Annexure III for ready reference.
- The IBC surveillance framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by exchanges.
- Shortlisting under the framework is purely for market surveillance purposes and should not be construed as adverse action against the concerned company.

## Regulatory Changes

This notice is issued in continuation of:
- Exchange notice no. 20180608-34 dated June 08, 2018
- Exchange notice no. 20220520-52 dated May 20, 2022
- Exchange notice no. 20230210-55 dated February 10, 2023
- Exchange notice no. 20250905-47 dated September 05, 2025

The IBC framework is periodically reviewed and the list of securities is updated accordingly.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants and members must take note of the updated list of securities under the IBC surveillance framework.
- Trading members must apply applicable price band restrictions for securities added to the framework from May 25, 2026.
- For clarifications, members may write to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **May 22, 2026**: Notice date
- **May 25, 2026**: Effective date for inclusion/exclusion of securities in the IBC surveillance framework

## Impact Assessment

Securities placed under the IBC framework are subject to additional surveillance measures including trade-for-trade settlement and restricted price bands, which limit intraday trading and increase settlement risk for market participants. Securities moving out of the framework will have their price bands reinstated to pre-shortlisting levels, subject to no other surveillance measures being applicable. The specific securities affected are detailed in the attached annexures; the consolidated list (Annexure III) should be consulted by all trading members to ensure compliance.
