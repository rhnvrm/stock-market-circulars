## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 4,00,000 equity shares of Kinetic Engineering Ltd (Scrip Code: 500240) issued to Non-Promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. The new shares will commence trading from January 2, 2026, and are subject to lock-in until July 29, 2026.

## Key Points

- 4,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at premium of Rs. 161/-
- Issue price: Rs. 171/- per share
- Allotted to Non-Promoters on preferential basis
- Issued pursuant to conversion of warrants
- Distinctive Numbers: 113519124 to 113919123
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE266B01017
- Date of allotment: September 24, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for new securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities listing
- All 4,00,000 shares are subject to lock-in restrictions until July 29, 2026
- Shares cannot be transferred or sold until lock-in period expires

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: September 24, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: January 2, 2026 (Friday)
- **Lock-in Expiry**: July 29, 2026
- **Notice Date**: January 1, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. The listing involves only 4,00,000 shares issued on preferential basis with lock-in restrictions until July 29, 2026, limiting immediate tradability.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members can facilitate transactions in these securities from January 2, 2026, though the lock-in period restricts transferability.

**Capital Structure Impact**: Addition of equity shares from warrant conversion increases the company's equity base, providing capital raised at Rs. 171/- per share (total consideration: Rs. 6.84 crores approximately).
