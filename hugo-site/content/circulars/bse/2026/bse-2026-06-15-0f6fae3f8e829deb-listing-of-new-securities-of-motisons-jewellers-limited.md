## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted 13,57,46,600 equity shares of Motisons Jewellers Limited (Scrip Code: 544053, ISIN: INE0FRK01020) for trading effective June 16, 2026. These shares were allotted to Qualified Institutional Buyers (QIBs) pursuant to a Qualified Institutional Placement (QIP) at an issue price of Rs. 11.05 per share.

## Key Points

- 13,57,46,600 equity shares of Re. 1/- each listed on BSE with effect from June 16, 2026
- Shares allotted to QIBs under a Qualified Institutional Placement (QIP)
- Issue price: Rs. 11.05 per share
- Date of allotment: June 11, 2026
- Distribution numbers: 1001760001 to 1137506600
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notice pursuant to an already completed QIP allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities and may contact Ms. Sahana Shetty (022 2272 8600) for any clarifications regarding this notice.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** June 11, 2026
- **Effective date for trading:** June 16, 2026 (Tuesday)
- **Notice date:** June 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The QIP adds approximately 13.57 crore new equity shares to the tradable float of Motisons Jewellers Limited, which may result in some dilution for existing shareholders. The pari-passu ranking means the new shares carry identical rights to existing shares. Trading members and investors should note the expanded share count when assessing the company's market capitalisation and liquidity from June 16, 2026 onwards.
