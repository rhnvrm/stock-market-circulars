## Summary

Hannah Joseph Hospital Limited has filed its shareholding pattern under Regulation 31(1)(a) as of January 30, 2026. The filing shows total issued equity of 22,698,363 shares with promoter and promoter group holding 68.83% (15,624,400 shares) through 6 shareholders, and public holding 31.17% (7,073,963 shares) through 622 shareholders. The company has declared no partly paid shares, convertible securities, warrants, ESOPs, depository receipts, locked-in shares, pledged shares, or shares under non-disposal undertaking.

## Key Points

- Total equity shares: 22,698,363 (all fully paid)
- Promoter & Promoter Group: 6 shareholders holding 15,624,400 shares (68.83%)
- Public shareholders: 622 shareholders holding 7,073,963 shares (31.17%)
- Dematerialized shares: 18,404,363 shares (81.08% of total)
- Physical shares: 4,294,000 shares (18.92% of total)
- No encumbrances on promoter shares (no pledge, NDU, or other encumbrances)
- No outstanding convertible securities or warrants

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are mentioned in this filing. This is a standard shareholding pattern disclosure as required under SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015.

## Compliance Requirements

This filing satisfies the listed entity's obligation to submit shareholding pattern under Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI LODR Regulations. No additional compliance actions are required from market participants.

## Important Dates

- Filing date: January 30, 2026
- Circular publication date: February 1, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine disclosure filing with no immediate market impact. The shareholding pattern shows stable promoter holding above the minimum threshold with no encumbrances, which is a positive indicator. The high proportion of dematerialized shares (81.08%) indicates good market infrastructure adoption. No trading restrictions, corporate actions, or material changes are indicated in this filing.
