## Summary

BSE (Notice No. 20260619-30) has revised the price bands for over 30 equity scrips effective June 22, 2026. The revisions affect a range of small and mid-cap companies, with new price bands set between 1% and 20% depending on the scrip.

## Key Points

- Price bands revised for 30+ BSE-listed equity scrips
- Effective date: June 22, 2026
- Revised bands range from 1% (e.g., Innovassynth Technologies) to 20% (e.g., Growington Ventures India Ltd)
- Covers companies across sectors including petrochemicals, textiles, agrochemicals, financial services, and infrastructure
- Notice issued by BSE's DOSS department under the Trading/Equity segment

## Regulatory Changes

Price bands for the listed scrips have been revised from their existing levels. The new bands restrict the maximum permissible intraday movement for these securities. Key revisions include:

- **1% band**: Innovassynth Technologies (India) Ltd (533315)
- **2% band**: Astonea Labs Ltd (544409), Filmcity Media Ltd (531486)
- **5% band**: Advance Petrochemicals (506947), Amerise Biosciences (531681), Aravali Securities & Finance (512344), Artson Ltd (522134), Atvo Enterprises (532090), Chiraharit Ltd (544561), Dhruva Capital Services (531237), Empower India (504351), Facor Alloys (532656), Fundviser Capital (530197), Gala Global Products (539228), Genus Prime Infra (532425), Gokak Textiles (532957), Hipolin Ltd (530853), HOMRE Ltd (523387), Ikoma Technologies (531997), JLA Infraville Shoppers (538765), Kuber Udyog (539408), Lexora Global (512345), Loyal Textile Mills (514036), Mayur Leather Products (531680)
- **10% band**: Alliance Integrated Metaliks (534064), Bhaskar Agrochemicals (524534), Desi Farms India (507984), Hybrid Financial Services (500262), Maruti Interior Products (543464)
- **20% band**: Growington Ventures India Ltd (539222)

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE must ensure that orders placed in the affected scrips adhere to the revised price bands from June 22, 2026 onwards. No action is required beyond system compliance with the updated limits.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: June 19, 2026
- **Effective Date**: June 22, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The revision of price bands is a regulatory measure typically applied to manage volatility in specific securities. Tighter bands (1%–5%) restrict intraday movement significantly, limiting speculative activity in the affected scrips. The 20% band for Growington Ventures India Ltd suggests a relaxation or adjustment for that security. Traders and investors holding or intending to trade in the listed scrips should note these limits to avoid order rejections. The impact is confined to the specific securities listed and does not affect broader market operations.
