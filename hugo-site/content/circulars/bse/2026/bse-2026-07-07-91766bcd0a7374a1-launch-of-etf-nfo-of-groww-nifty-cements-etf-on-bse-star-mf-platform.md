## Summary

BSE has notified members that GROWW Mutual Fund will launch the NFO of GROWW NIFTY CEMENTS ETF, along with its Direct Plan, on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The NFO runs from July 08, 2026 to July 22, 2026, with a tentative allotment date of July 29, 2026. The circular also details payment and fund pay-in instructions applicable to transactions placed under this NFO.

## Key Points

- GROWW Mutual Fund is launching two new schemes: GROWW NIFTY CEMENTS ETF and GROWW NIFTY CEMENTS ETF - DIRECT PLAN, both under the Equity scheme category.
- NFO subscription period: July 08, 2026 to July 22, 2026.
- Tentative allotment date for both schemes: July 29, 2026.
- The NFO period is extended till midnight on July 22, 2026, but only for lumpsum purchases on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an operational notice regarding a new fund offer, not a regulatory or policy change.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clients initiate funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day; otherwise transactions will be cancelled and reported to the RTA/AMC with an 'N' flag.
- Transactions with funds initiated by clients remain pending until 3:00 PM on the last day of the fund pay-out to the AMC, or T+5 day, whichever is earlier.
- Clear funds must be available in the ICCL account by 6:00 PM on July 23, 2026, for allotment eligibility.
- Cheque and OTM (One Time Mandate) payments are accepted during the NFO period, subject to clear funds reaching the ICCL account by the same deadline (6:00 PM, July 23, 2026).

## Important Dates

- NFO opens: July 08, 2026
- NFO closes (extended for lumpsum purchases): midnight, July 22, 2026
- Deadline for clear funds in ICCL account: 6:00 PM, July 23, 2026
- Tentative allotment date: July 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice for BSE StAR MF Platform members facilitating a new ETF offering by GROWW Mutual Fund. It has no direct impact on listed equities or stock prices but is relevant to mutual fund distributors and platform participants who must adhere to the specified fund pay-in timelines and cutoffs to ensure client transactions are processed and allotted correctly.
