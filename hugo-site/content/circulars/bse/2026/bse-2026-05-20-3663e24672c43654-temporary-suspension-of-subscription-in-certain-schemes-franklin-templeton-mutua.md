## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260520-27 informing members that Franklin Templeton Mutual Fund has temporarily suspended subscription-related transactions in two specific schemes — Franklin India Retirement Fund and Franklin Pension Plan — on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The suspension covers lumpsum investments, switch-in transactions, and new SIP/STP registrations, effective May 20, 2026. Redemptions, switch-outs, STP-Out, and SWP transactions remain permitted.

## Key Points

- Subscription suspension is effective from May 20, 2026, covering two schemes: Franklin India Retirement Fund and Franklin Pension Plan
- Lumpsum investments (fresh and additional purchases), switch-in transactions, and new SIP/XSIP and STP-in registrations have been disabled on BSE StAR MF platform
- Applications received after the cut-off time on May 19, 2026 will not be accepted or processed
- Existing SIP/XSIP registrations are paused and will not be triggered until further notice
- Redemption, Switch-out, STP-Out, and SWP transactions are still permitted
- Subscription orders triggered to the RTA after 3:00 PM on May 19, 2026 will be rejected and refunded

## Regulatory Changes

Franklin Templeton AMC requested BSE to disable subscription-side transaction capabilities for the affected schemes on the BSE StAR MF platform. This is an AMC-driven operational suspension, not a regulatory enforcement action. The platform changes have already been implemented as of the notice date.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members (trading members/distributors) must immediately cease submitting lumpsum, switch-in, or new SIP/STP orders for the two affected schemes
- Any orders already submitted after 3:00 PM on May 19, 2026 should be treated as rejected; refunds will follow upon receipt of funds from AMC/RTA
- Members should inform their clients about the suspension of existing SIP/XSIP registrations under these schemes
- Members should monitor for further communication from BSE/Franklin Templeton regarding resumption of subscriptions

## Important Dates

- **May 19, 2026, 3:00 PM**: Cut-off time — orders submitted after this time will be rejected
- **May 20, 2026**: Effective date of suspension for all subscription transactions in the affected schemes
- **Further notice**: Existing SIP/XSIP pauses will remain in effect until Franklin Templeton communicates resumption

## Impact Assessment

This suspension has a high operational impact on distributors and investors transacting through BSE StAR MF platform in these two Franklin Templeton retirement-focused schemes. Investors with active SIPs in Franklin India Retirement Fund or Franklin Pension Plan will see their installments paused indefinitely. Fresh investment flows into these schemes via BSE StAR MF are completely halted. The restriction to retirement and pension-oriented schemes may signal underlying fund-level stress or regulatory scrutiny, warranting close monitoring. Redemption liquidity is preserved, which limits downside risk for existing investors needing to exit.
