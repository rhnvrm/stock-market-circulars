## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Commodity Derivatives segment on Saturday, February 07, 2026. This session is designed for members to test their trading platforms and systems, including third-party applications and in-house developed systems. The mock trading is for testing and familiarization purposes only and will not create any actual trades, margin obligations, or settlement obligations.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Commodity Derivatives segment on February 07, 2026
- Login timing: 10:15 AM to 11:00 AM
- Continuous trading: 11:00 AM to 03:30 PM
- No new BOLT TWS Release will be implemented
- Members can alternatively use UAT environment to fulfill regulatory requirements
- Trading members using third-party platforms or in-house systems via IML/ETI APIs can test various functionalities
- Testing includes exceptional market conditions like risk-reduction mode and trading halt scenarios
- All mock trades will not attract any margin, pay-in, pay-out obligations or create rights/liabilities
- Feedback must be submitted by 4:30 PM on the same day

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This circular refers to existing compliance requirements:
- Exchange Notice No 20201201-22 dated December 1, 2020 regarding testing of software used in Trading and Risk Management
- SEBI circular no. SEBI/HO/MRD1/DSAP/CIR/P/2020/234 dated November 24, 2020 regarding regulatory testing requirements

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members may participate in either Mock Trading Session or UAT environment to fulfill regulatory requirements
- Members are encouraged to actively participate in the mock trading session
- Members must provide feedback by 4:30 PM on February 07, 2026
- Members using third-party or in-house trading systems should utilize this opportunity to test their applications

## Important Dates

- **February 07, 2026**: Mock trading session date
  - 10:15 AM - 11:00 AM: Login window
  - 11:00 AM - 03:30 PM: Continuous trading session
  - By 4:30 PM: Feedback submission deadline

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: None. This is a mock trading session with no real market transactions.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Optional participation for testing purposes only. Members can choose between mock trading or UAT environment for regulatory compliance.

**Member Impact**: Provides opportunity for trading members to test their systems, third-party platforms, and risk management tools in a simulated environment without any financial obligations or risks.
