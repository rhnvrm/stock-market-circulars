## Summary

BSE has published the list of securities subject to demat auction for Settlement No. 794 dated January 9, 2026. The auction covers 82 different scrips with varying quantities ranging from 1 share to 11,764 shares across multiple companies from various sectors.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 794
- Auction Date: January 9, 2026
- Total number of scrips involved: 82
- Covers securities from banking, IT, infrastructure, manufacturing, and other sectors
- Quantities range from as low as 1 share to as high as 11,764 shares
- Largest quantity: NEWTIME INFR (531959) - 11,764 shares
- Notable companies include: HDFC Bank, ITC, Wipro, Titan, Vedanta, Yes Bank, Union Bank, Federal Bank, Canara Bank, Bharat Heavy, Bharat Petro

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular. This is a routine operational notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Brokers and clearing members must settle their obligations for the listed securities
- Participants with shortages in the listed securities must participate in the demat auction or arrange delivery
- Settlement to be completed as per BSE's standard auction settlement procedures

## Important Dates

- **Settlement Number:** 794
- **Auction Date:** January 9, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - affects 82 different securities across various sectors

**Operational Impact:** Brokers and clearing members with short positions in these securities must participate in the auction or face penalty. The auction helps ensure smooth settlement of trades and delivery obligations.

**Affected Parties:** Trading members, clearing members, and investors with pending delivery obligations in the listed securities for settlement 794.

## Securities Under Auction

Complete list includes major companies such as ITC (250 shares), HDFC Bank (50 shares), Wipro (6 shares), Yes Bank (3 shares), Vedanta (100 shares), Union Bank (400 shares), and 76 other companies with quantities ranging from 1 to 11,764 shares.
