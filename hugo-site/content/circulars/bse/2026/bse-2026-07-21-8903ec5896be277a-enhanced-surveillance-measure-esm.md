## Summary

BSE has issued an update to its Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework, referencing a series of earlier notices dating back to June 2023. Securities listed in Annexure I have met the criteria for ESM inclusion and will attract a minimum 100% margin on all open and new positions from July 23, 2026. Some securities already under ESM will move down to a lower stage (Stage I), while others will exit the framework entirely per Annexure II. A consolidated list of all securities currently under ESM is provided in Annexure III.

## Key Points

- Securities in Annexure I have satisfied ESM inclusion criteria and will be shifted from Rolling Settlement to Trade-for-Trade segment effective July 23, 2026.
- Securities continuing under ESM but moving to a lower stage (Stage I) will do so effective July 22, 2026.
- Securities moving to Stage II will trade under Trade-for-Trade with a 2% price band and Periodic Call Auction w.e.f. July 22, 2026.
- Securities exiting the ESM framework (Annexure II) take effect July 22, 2026, with price bands reinstated to pre-ESM levels, subject to no other surveillance measure applying.
- ESM operates alongside all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the Exchange.
- Inclusion under ESM is a surveillance-based classification and is not to be construed as an adverse action against the concerned company.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced; this notice is a periodic update applying the existing ESM framework (as established via notices dated June 2, 2023, July 18, 2023, August 9, 2024, September 20, 2024, October 4, 2024, and July 25, 2025) to a fresh set of securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must apply minimum 100% margin on open and new positions in newly included ESM securities from July 23, 2026, and ensure affected securities are traded only in the Trade-for-Trade segment. Members with queries can contact bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- July 22, 2026: Securities move to lower ESM stage (Stage I); Stage II shift with 2% price band and Periodic Call Auction; securities exit ESM framework (Annexure II).
- July 23, 2026: 100% margin applies to open positions as of July 22, 2026 and new positions; newly included securities shift to Trade-for-Trade segment.

## Impact Assessment

Securities newly placed under ESM face significantly reduced trading liquidity due to 100% margin requirements and Trade-for-Trade restrictions, which eliminate intraday trading and increase capital costs for participants. Securities moving to Stage II face further constraints via tighter 2% price bands and periodic call auctions. Conversely, securities exiting the framework regain normal settlement and price band treatment, likely improving liquidity. Overall market impact is stock-specific and depends on the securities named in the annexures, which are not part of the notice text provided.
