## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document of Sham Foam Limited, filed on February 23, 2026, on the BSE SME website. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and is available for public review and comments for a minimum period of 21 days from the notice date.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Sham Foam Limited on February 23, 2026
- Document hosted on the BSE SME website in compliance with ICDR Regulations Schedule VI
- Public comments are invited within 21 days from February 24, 2026 (deadline: approximately March 17, 2026)
- Comments to be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Draft document available at: https://www.bsesme.com/download/314291/SME_IPO%20InPrinciple/ShamFoamDP_20260223212947.pdf

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural notice under the ICDR Regulations requiring public availability of SME IPO draft offer documents for at least 21 days prior to proceeding with the issue.

## Compliance Requirements

- Investors and public may review the Draft Offer Document and submit comments within 21 days to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- The company (Sham Foam Limited) must address any public comments received before proceeding with the IPO
- No compliance action required for existing listed companies or trading members

## Important Dates

- **Filing Date:** February 23, 2026
- **Notice Date:** February 24, 2026
- **Public Comment Deadline:** Approximately March 17, 2026 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a routine disclosure notice for an upcoming SME IPO by Sham Foam Limited. It is relevant primarily to prospective investors interested in the SME segment and analysts tracking the SME IPO pipeline. No impact on currently listed securities or ongoing trading operations.
