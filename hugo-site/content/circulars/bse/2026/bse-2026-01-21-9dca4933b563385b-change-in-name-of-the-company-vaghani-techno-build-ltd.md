## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for the company previously known as Vaghani Techno - Build Limited. The company name has been officially changed to Emrock Corporation Limited with effect from December 1, 2025. The certificate was issued by the Central Processing Centre, ROC.

## Key Points

- Original company name: Vaghani Techno - Build Limited
- New company name: Emrock Corporation Limited
- Corporate Identification Number (CIN): L41001GJ1994PLC168513
- Company type: Company limited by shares
- Certificate issued: December 1, 2025
- Issued by: M.Yadubhushana Rao, Assistant Registrar of Companies, Central Processing Centre
- Company was originally incorporated as Dhruv Makhan (India) Ltd on October 6, 1994
- Name changed to Vaghani Techno - Build Limited on October 10, 2006

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative notification of corporate name change approved under Section 21 of the Companies Act, 1956.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display the old name (Vaghani Techno - Build Limited) for a period of two years along with its new name (Emrock Corporation Limited) at all places where the company is required to display its name in terms of Section 12 of the Act
- All corporate documents, letterheads, and official communications must be updated to reflect the new name
- The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders

## Important Dates

- **December 1, 2025**: Effective date of name change from Vaghani Techno - Build Limited to Emrock Corporation Limited
- **Two-year period**: Duration for which old name must be displayed alongside new name

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative change with minimal market impact. The company's CIN, registered office address, and corporate structure remain unchanged. Stakeholders should update their records to reflect the new company name. The change does not affect existing contracts, rights, or obligations. Investors and market participants are advised to verify the latest status on the Ministry of Corporate Affairs website at www.mca.gov.in/MCA21.
