## Summary

BSE Limited has issued a notice (No. 20260227-14) informing trading members that the equity shares of Shree Ram Twistex Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange. The specific listing date and further security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Equity shares of Shree Ram Twistex Limited are approved for listing on BSE
- Scrip Code: 544716
- Trading Symbol: SRTL
- ISIN: INE19GK01015
- Exact listing date to be announced via a subsequent notice
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing admission notice for a new equity scrip on BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the scrip details and await the follow-up notice announcing the official listing date before initiating any dealings in the securities of Shree Ram Twistex Limited.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** February 27, 2026
- **Listing Date:** To be announced via a separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This notice signals the upcoming market debut of Shree Ram Twistex Limited on BSE. Trading members and investors should monitor for the follow-up listing date announcement. The listing will make the scrip (SRTL, code 544716) available for trading on BSE's equity segment. Impact is limited to participants interested in this specific company.
