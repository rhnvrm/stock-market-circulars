## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the equity shares of Jivial Industries Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange. The company is being listed on the SME segment. The exact date of listing and further details of the securities will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Jivial Industries Limited equity shares are approved for listing on BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544805
- Symbol: JIVIAL
- ISIN: INE0V3T01017
- Actual listing date to be announced via a separate notice

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice informing trading members of an upcoming equity listing on the SME platform.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should await the separate notice from BSE confirming the official listing date and security details before initiating trades in this scrip.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: June 30, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced via separate notice

## Impact Assessment

Minimal broader market impact. The listing is confined to the BSE SME segment and affects trading members and investors specifically interested in Jivial Industries Limited. Market participants should monitor BSE for the follow-up notice confirming the listing date.
