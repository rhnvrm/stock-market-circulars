## Summary

BSE has issued a revised notice (20260506-18) as a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20260402-44 dated April 02, 2026, pertaining to the part redemption of debentures of Adani Airport Holdings Limited. The exchange has now confirmed the actual reduced face value of the debentures, which will take effect from May 07, 2026.

## Key Points

- This is a revised/updated notice superseding the earlier notice dated April 02, 2026 (Notice No. 20260402-44)
- Scrip Code: 976065 | ISIN: INE0GCN07047 | Debenture Name: AAHL-9.35%-8-9-28-PVT
- Actual Reduced Face Value confirmed at **Rs. 66,483/- per Debenture**
- Debentures will be traded at the revised reduced face value effective **May 07, 2026**
- Reference: DR-625/2026-2027
- Notice issued under the Debt segment, Listing Operations department

## Regulatory Changes

The reduced face value of the debentures has been officially revised and confirmed by the company. Trading members must update their systems to reflect the new face value of Rs. 66,483 per debenture for scrip code 976065 of Adani Airport Holdings Limited.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members are advised to take note of the revised reduced face value.
- All trades in scrip code 976065 (AAHL-9.35%-8-9-28-PVT) must reflect the reduced face value of Rs. 66,483/- per debenture from May 07, 2026 onwards.
- Trading systems and back-office records should be updated accordingly before the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **April 02, 2026**: Original notice issued (Exchange Notice No. 20260402-44) regarding part redemption
- **May 06, 2026**: Revised notice issued confirming actual reduced face value
- **May 07, 2026**: Effective date from which debentures will be traded at the reduced face value of Rs. 66,483/-

## Impact Assessment

This notice impacts trading members and investors holding debentures of Adani Airport Holdings Limited (scrip code 976065) in the debt segment. The confirmed reduced face value of Rs. 66,483/- per debenture reflects the part redemption of the principal. The impact is limited to participants in this specific debenture instrument and is operational in nature — requiring system updates for trade settlement. No broader market disruption is anticipated.
