## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (No. 20260527-3) dated 27 May 2026 regarding the listing of units of **360 ONE MSCI India ETF**, a scheme under **360 ONE Mutual Fund**, on the Mutual Fund segment of BSE. This is a company-related notice processed by the DCS-Listing department.

## Key Points

- BSE Notice No. 20260527-3 dated 27 May 2026
- Units of 360 ONE MSCI India ETF are being listed on the BSE Mutual Fund segment
- The scheme is managed under 360 ONE Mutual Fund
- Processed by the DCS-Listing department
- No attachments included with this notice

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced. This is a standard listing notice for a new ETF scheme on BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements are outlined in this notice. Market participants and investors should refer to the scheme's offer documents and 360 ONE Mutual Fund's official communications for subscription, redemption, and trading details.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 27 May 2026
- **Listing Date:** 27 May 2026 (as per notice issuance)

## Impact Assessment

This listing provides investors with exchange-traded access to the 360 ONE MSCI India ETF on BSE's Mutual Fund segment. The MSCI India index tracks large- and mid-cap Indian equities, so this ETF offers passive exposure to the broader Indian equity market. Impact is limited to investors and distributors dealing in this specific ETF scheme; no broader market operations are affected.
