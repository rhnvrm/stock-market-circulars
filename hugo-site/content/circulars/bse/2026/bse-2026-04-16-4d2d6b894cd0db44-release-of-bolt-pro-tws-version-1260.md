## Summary

BSE has announced the release of BOLT Pro TWS version 12.60, which will go live on Monday, April 20, 2026. This is an **optional** upgrade applicable across Equity, Equity Derivatives, Currency Derivatives, Commodity Derivatives, and EGR segments. The release introduces one new feature: a toggle between old and new sizes in the Best Five Window.

## Key Points

- BOLT Pro TWS ver. 12.60 is an **optional** release — trading members are not mandated to upgrade.
- The new version is applicable across Equity, Equity Derivatives, Currency Derivatives, Commodity Derivatives, and EGR segments.
- New feature introduced: **Best Five Window – Facility to Switch Between Old and New Sizes**.
- Version available for download from the Exchange website and via the Auto-Download Interface for leased line users.
- BSE Helpdesk contact: 022-45720400/600 & 022-69158500 | bsehelp@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a technical software update to the BOLT Pro TWS trading terminal.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Optional**: Trading members may choose to upgrade at their discretion.
- Members using Leased Line Connections can upgrade by selecting ver. 12.60 in the TWS Version tab of the Auto-Download Interface on startup.
- Members may also download manually from the BSE website at http://www.bseindia.com/help/ by downloading `Update.zip`, unzipping it, and following the instructions in `How to use this files.doc`.

## Important Dates

| Date | Event |
|------|-------|
| April 17, 2026 (Friday, 7:00 PM onwards) | Version 12.60 available for download |
| April 18, 2026 (Saturday) | Version available for testing during mock trading session |
| April 20, 2026 (Monday) | Version goes live on production |

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine, optional software upgrade to BSE's BOLT Pro TWS trading terminal. The single feature addition (Best Five Window size toggle) is a UI/UX enhancement with no effect on trading strategies, market structure, or compliance obligations. Trading members who do not upgrade will continue to operate normally on their existing version.
