## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 18,33,334 new equity shares of iStreet Network Limited (Scrip Code: 524622, ISIN: INE532B01020) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Thursday, June 11, 2026. The shares were issued at Rs. 6/- each (face value Rs. 4/- + premium Rs. 2/-) to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- **Scrip Code:** 524622
- **ISIN:** INE532B01020
- **New shares:** 18,33,334 equity shares of Rs. 4/- each at a premium of Rs. 2/- (issue price Rs. 6/-)
- **Allotment basis:** Preferential allotment to non-promoters via warrant conversion
- **Distribution numbers:** 66466669 to 68300002
- **Date of allotment:** 07 March 2026
- **Trading effective from:** 11 June 2026
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification for newly allotted equity shares.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the listing and enable trading in these securities from June 11, 2026 onwards.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** 07 March 2026
- **Trading commences:** 11 June 2026
- **Lock-in expiry (1,66,667 shares, Dist. Nos. 66466669–66633335):** 14 January 2027
- **Lock-in expiry (16,66,667 shares, Dist. Nos. 66633336–68300002):** 15 January 2027

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The addition of 18,33,334 shares to the tradeable float is relatively small. The bulk of the new shares (16,66,667) are subject to lock-in until January 15, 2027, limiting near-term selling pressure. Only 1,66,667 shares become free earlier, on January 14, 2027. Existing shareholders may see marginal dilution consistent with the earlier warrant issuance.
