## Summary

BSE has informed members that the Nippon India CRISIL-IBX Financial Services 3-6 Months Debt Index Fund has been reopened for ongoing subscription and redemption, along with SIP, STP & SWP facilities on the BSE StAR MF Platform, effective March 16, 2026. This notice follows the earlier Notice No. 20260304-32.

## Key Points

- The fund is now available for ongoing transactions (subscription and redemption) from March 16, 2026
- SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities are also available
- Six scheme variants are available across Regular and Direct plans with Growth and IDCW options
- All six schemes fall under the DEBT category

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding the availability of existing fund schemes on the BSE StAR MF platform.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members. This is an informational notice to enable trading members to facilitate investor transactions in these schemes on the BSE StAR MF platform.

## Important Dates

- **March 16, 2026**: Effective date for reopening of the fund for ongoing subscription, redemption, SIP, STP & SWP on BSE StAR MF Platform

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This notice enables investors to transact in the Nippon India CRISIL-IBX Financial Services 3-6 Months Debt Index Fund through the BSE StAR MF platform. The fund targets short-duration debt instruments (3-6 months) indexed to the CRISIL-IBX Financial Services benchmark, providing a low-risk debt investment option. The availability of SIP, STP, and SWP facilities broadens accessibility for retail and systematic investors.
