## Summary

BSE has announced suspension of trading in non-convertible debentures of ECAP EQUITIES LIMITED (ISIN: INE468N07185, Code: ECE-GSEC-16-2-26-PVT/974112) effective February 2, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing the record date for redemption of these debentures on the same date. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from February 2, 2026 onwards.

## Key Points

- Company: ECAP EQUITIES LIMITED
- ISIN: INE468N07185
- Debenture Code: ECE-GSEC-16-2-26-PVT (974112)
- Record Date: February 2, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of Non-Convertible Debentures
- No Dealings From: February 2, 2026
- Notice Number: DR-811/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action procedure for debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures of ECAP EQUITIES LIMITED from February 2, 2026
- Trading members should update their systems to reflect the suspension
- Investors holding these debentures should note the redemption schedule

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** January 23, 2026
- **Record Date:** February 2, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Date:** February 2, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - affects only a single debenture series of one company

**Investor Impact:** Medium for existing debenture holders - they will receive redemption proceeds as per terms; no new positions can be taken from suspension date

**Operational Impact:** Minimal - standard corporate action requiring trading members to update their dealing systems to prevent trades in the suspended security
