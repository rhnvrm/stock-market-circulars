## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in non-convertible debentures of SATIN HOUSING FINANCE LIMITED (ISIN: INE02YC07018, Code: 975970) effective February 12, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for debenture redemption and interest payment. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the no-dealings date.

## Key Points

- Company: SATIN HOUSING FINANCE LIMITED
- Debenture Series: SHFL-10.90%-27-2-26-PVT
- ISIN: INE02YC07018
- BSE Code: 975970
- Record Date: February 12, 2026
- No Dealings From: February 12, 2026
- Circular Reference: DR-819/2025-2026
- Interest Rate: 10.90%

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action notification for debenture redemption and interest payment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debenture series from February 12, 2026
- Members are required to take note of the suspension and inform their clients accordingly
- Compliance with the no-dealings restriction is mandatory for all exchange participants

## Important Dates

- **February 10, 2026**: Circular issued
- **February 12, 2026**: Record date for redemption and interest payment
- **February 12, 2026**: Trading suspension effective (no dealings date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - affects single debenture series of SATIN HOUSING FINANCE LIMITED. Holders of this specific ISIN will be unable to trade from the suspension date.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to block trading in the specified security and communicate the suspension to affected clients. The suspension is for redemption purposes, indicating the debentures are reaching maturity, which is a routine corporate action.
