## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 87,86,362 new equity shares of Biocon Ltd. (Scrip Code: 532523), each of face value Rs. 5/-, issued at a premium of Rs. 371.41/- (issue price Rs. 376.41/-) to promoters and non-promoters on a preferential basis, are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Friday, July 24, 2026. These shares rank pari-passu with the company's existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- Security: 87,86,362 equity shares of Rs. 5/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 371.41/- to Promoters and Non-Promoters on a preferential basis
- Distinctive numbers: 1620907774 to 1629694135
- Date of allotment: 29/06/2026
- Issue price: Rs. 376.41/-
- ISIN: INE376G01013
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is a routine listing notice for preferentially allotted equity shares under BSE's Listing Operations department.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should update their systems to reflect the enhanced listed equity base of Biocon Ltd. and observe the applicable lock-in restrictions on the specified distinctive number ranges when handling these shares.

## Important Dates

- Date of allotment: 29 June 2026
- Listing and trading permission effective: 24 July 2026
- Lock-in expiry (33,07,719 shares, Dist. Nos. 1620907774–1624215492): 24/01/2027
- Lock-in expiry (57,119 shares, Dist. Nos. 1624215493–1624272611): 23/01/2028
- Lock-in expiry (33,85,718 shares, Dist. Nos. 1624272612–1627658329): 24/01/2027
- Lock-in expiry (20,35,806 shares, Dist. Nos. 1627658330–1629694135): 25/01/2027

## Impact Assessment

The listing increases Biocon Ltd.'s paid-up equity capital and free float, which modestly dilutes existing shareholders. As the shares were issued via preferential allotment to promoters and non-promoters rather than a public offering, there is no direct impact on market liquidity or price discovery on the listing date. A portion of the new shares carries lock-in restrictions extending into 2027 and 2028, limiting near-term sale by allottees.
