## Summary

NIWAS Housing Finance Private Limited (formerly Indostar Home Finance Private Limited) has been converted from a private company to a public company with concurrent name change to NIWAS Housing Finance Limited. The conversion was approved by the Registrar of Companies, Central Processing Centre (ROC, CPC) under Section 18 of the Companies Act, 2013, effective December 31, 2025.

## Key Points

- Original incorporation date: January 1, 2016 under Companies Act, 2013
- Original name: Indostar Home Finance Private Limited
- New name: NIWAS Housing Finance Limited
- Corporate Identity Number (CIN): U65990MH2016PLC271587
- Conversion approved via SRN AC0878110 dated December 31, 2025
- Approved by: M. Yadubhushana Rao, Assistant Registrar of Companies/Deputy Registrar of Companies/Registrar of Companies
- Registered office: Unit No.305, 3rd Floor, Wing 2/E, Corporate Avenue, Andheri-Ghatkopar Link Road, Chakala, Mumbai City-400093, Maharashtra

## Regulatory Changes

The company has undergone conversion from a private limited company to a public limited company under Section 18 of the Companies Act, 2013. This change in legal structure requires compliance with public company regulations including enhanced disclosure requirements, governance standards, and regulatory oversight.

## Compliance Requirements

- Stakeholders should note the new company name: NIWAS Housing Finance Limited
- All future correspondence and transactions should reference the new company name
- The company must comply with public company regulations under the Companies Act, 2013
- Updated CIN should be used in all regulatory filings: U65990MH2016PLC271587

## Important Dates

- **December 31, 2025**: Certificate of incorporation issued for conversion to public company and name change
- **December 31, 2025**: ROC approval granted (SRN AC0878110)
- **January 1, 2016**: Original incorporation date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: The conversion to a public company structure may indicate the company's preparation for broader capital market access or listing. Investors and counterparties should update their records with the new company name.

**Operational Impact**: The company's business activities in housing finance continue under the new name. The conversion to public company status brings additional regulatory obligations but enables greater access to capital markets.

**Stakeholder Impact**: All contracts, agreements, and legal documents referencing the old name should be updated. Banking relationships, customer accounts, and vendor agreements will need to reflect the new corporate identity.
