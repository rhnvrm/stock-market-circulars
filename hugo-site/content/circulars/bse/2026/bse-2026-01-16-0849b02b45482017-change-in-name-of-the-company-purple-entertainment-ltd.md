## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs, through its Central Processing Centre, has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for Purple Entertainment Limited. The company's name has been officially changed to Purple Agrotech Industries Limited with effect from November 4, 2025. This is an administrative corporate action that does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders.

## Key Points

- Company name changed from Purple Entertainment Limited to Purple Agrotech Industries Limited
- Corporate Identification Number (CIN): L65100GJ1974PLC084389
- Certificate issued by ROC Central Processing Centre, Manesar
- Company type: Company limited by shares
- Original incorporation name: Purple Entertainment Limited
- Certificate digitally signed by Mitika Raja, Assistant/Deputy/Registrar of Companies
- Registered office: 30-B, Third Floor, Ajanta Complex, Income Tax, Ashram Road, Ahmedabad - 380009, Gujarat

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate name change procedure completed in accordance with the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014, Rule 29.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display the old name (Purple Entertainment Limited) along with its new name for a period of two years at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- The name change does not affect any existing rights and liabilities of stakeholders
- All stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company and its directors on the Ministry's website www.mca.gov.in/MCA21

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date of Name Change**: November 4, 2025
- **Certificate Issuance Date**: November 4, 2025, 13:06:08 IST
- **Old Name Display Period**: Two years from November 4, 2025 (until November 4, 2027)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine administrative change that does not alter the company's business operations, financial position, or corporate structure.

**Operational Impact**: Low. The company must update its signage, letterheads, and documentation to reflect the new name while continuing to display the old name for the mandatory two-year period.

**Stakeholder Impact**: No impact on stakeholder rights and liabilities. All existing contracts, agreements, and legal obligations continue under the new name.

**Trading Impact**: Shareholders and market participants should note the new company name for identification purposes, though the scrip code and CIN remain unchanged.
