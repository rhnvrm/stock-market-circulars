- Offer for Sale (OFS) opened for East India Drums & Barrels Manufacturing Limited
- Special provisions for undersubscription acceptance included
- Circular provides settlement schedule and acquisition window details
- Standard BSE OFS framework and procedures apply
- Applicable to shareholders and potential investors of the company

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular applies existing BSE regulations for Offer for Sale and undersubscription acceptance procedures to East India Drums & Barrels Manufacturing Limited.
