## Summary

The Registrar of Companies, Gujarat, Dadra and Nagar Havelli has issued a Fresh Certificate of Incorporation consequent upon change of name for the company. The company, originally incorporated on June 6, 1994 as Devika Finstocks Private Limited under the Companies Act, 1956, and later known as Devika Proteins Limited, has changed its name to Dharti Proteins Limited effective July 22, 2011.

## Key Points

- **Corporate Identity Number**: L67120GJ1994PLC022199
- **Original Name**: Devika Finstocks Private Limited (at incorporation)
- **Previous Name**: Devika Proteins Limited
- **New Name**: Dharti Proteins Limited
- **Date of Original Incorporation**: June 6, 1994
- **Date of Name Change Certificate**: July 22, 2011
- **SRN**: B15369341
- **Registered Office**: 203, 2nd Floor, Abhijeet-1, Nr Mithakhali Circle, Ellisbridge, Ahmedabad - 380006, Gujarat, India

## Regulatory Changes

The name change was approved under Section 21 of the Companies Act, 1956, with the approval of the Central Government (Ministry of Corporate Affairs) signified in writing. The certificate was issued pursuant to Section 23(1) of the Companies Act, 1956, in accordance with Government of India, Department of Company Affairs Notification No. G.S.R 507 (E) dated June 24, 1985.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. This is a historical administrative record of a company name change that occurred in 2011.

## Important Dates

- **June 6, 1994**: Original incorporation as Devika Finstocks Private Limited
- **June 24, 1985**: Government notification reference (G.S.R 507 E)
