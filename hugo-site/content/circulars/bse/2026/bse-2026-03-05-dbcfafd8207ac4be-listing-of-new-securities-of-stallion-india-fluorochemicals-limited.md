## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 3,67,60,483 new equity shares of Stallion India Fluorochemicals Limited (Scrip Code: 544342) have been listed and are permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Friday, March 6, 2026. These shares were issued on a rights basis at Rs. 10/- face value with a premium of Rs. 89/- per share.

## Key Points

- 3,67,60,483 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 89/- on rights basis are now listed
- ISIN: INE0RYC01010
- Distribution Numbers: 79325255 to 116085737
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Scrip Code: 544342

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification under BSE's listing and disclosure requirements.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to update their systems to permit trading of the newly listed securities with effect from March 6, 2026.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: 02/03/2026
- Effective Date for Trading: 06/03/2026 (Friday, March 6, 2026)

## Impact Assessment

This listing increases the equity share capital of Stallion India Fluorochemicals Limited. The rights issue shares are pari-passu with existing shares, meaning they carry equal rights. The listing allows rights issue allottees to trade their newly allotted shares on BSE from March 6, 2026. Impact is limited to existing shareholders and active traders of this scrip.
