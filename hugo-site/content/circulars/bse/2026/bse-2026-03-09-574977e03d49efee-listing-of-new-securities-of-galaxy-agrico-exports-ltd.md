## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 1,31,59,102 new equity shares of Galaxy Agrico Exports Ltd. (Scrip Code: 531911) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Tuesday, March 10, 2026. These shares were issued on a rights basis at a face value of Rs. 10/- each with a premium of Rs. 25.87/-.

## Key Points

- 1,31,59,102 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 25.87/- on rights basis
- ISIN: INE803L01016
- Distribution Numbers: 5000021 to 18159122
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Trading effective from March 10, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to allow trading in the newly listed securities from the effective date. No additional compliance actions required beyond standard trading operations.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: March 5, 2026
- Trading Effective From: March 10, 2026 (Tuesday)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. The listing pertains to a rights issue for Galaxy Agrico Exports Ltd., a small-cap company. The newly issued shares are pari-passu with existing shares, meaning no change in shareholder rights or dividend entitlements relative to old shares. Existing shareholders who subscribed to the rights issue will be able to trade their new shares from March 10, 2026.
