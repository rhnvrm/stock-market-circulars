## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that SVCM Securities Private Limited (Clg. No. 6737) will no longer serve as the Market Maker for Khazanchi Jewellers Limited (Scrip Code: 543953). SW Capital Private Limited (Clg. No. 3070) has been appointed as the new Market Maker for the company, effective April 20, 2026.

## Key Points

- SVCM Securities Private Limited (Clg. No. 6737) ceases to be the Market Maker for Khazanchi Jewellers Limited (Scrip Code: 543953)
- SW Capital Private Limited (Clg. No. 3070) is the newly appointed Market Maker
- Khazanchi Jewellers Limited has entered into a formal agreement with SW Capital Private Limited
- SW Capital will assume all duties and obligations of Market Making for the scrip
- The change is applicable to the BSE SME segment

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an operational update reflecting a change in the designated Market Maker entity under existing BSE SME market-making framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- SW Capital Private Limited (Clg. No. 3070) must comply with all Market Maker duties and obligations as per BSE SME regulations from the effective date
- Trading members are informed to update their records accordingly

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** April 17, 2026
- **Effective Date:** April 20, 2026 (SW Capital Private Limited assumes Market Maker role)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine administrative change in market-making arrangements for a single SME scrip. Continuity of liquidity provision for Khazanchi Jewellers Limited (Scrip Code: 543953) is expected to be maintained under the new Market Maker, SW Capital Private Limited.
