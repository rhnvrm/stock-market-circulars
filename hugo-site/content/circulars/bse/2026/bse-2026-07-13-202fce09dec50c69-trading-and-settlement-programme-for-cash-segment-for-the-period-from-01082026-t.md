## Summary

BSE Clearing Ltd (BSECL) has issued the trading and settlement programme for the Cash Segment for the period from 01.08.2026 to 31.08.2026, covering both the T+1 and T+0 settlement cycles. The notice provides the settlement calendar attached as "CASH082026" for trading members to plan pay-in and pay-out schedules for the month.

## Key Points

- Notice pertains to the Cash Segment trading and settlement schedule for August 2026
- Covers both T+1 and T+0 settlement cycles
- Issued by BSE Clearing Ltd (BSECL) under the Settlement/RMS category
- Detailed settlement dates are provided in the attached annexure "CASH082026"

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine periodic publication of the settlement calendar and does not introduce any regulatory changes.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members and clearing members should refer to the attached settlement calendar to ensure timely pay-in and pay-out of funds and securities in accordance with the T+1 and T+0 cycles for the Cash Segment during August 2026.

## Important Dates

Applicable period: 01 August 2026 to 31 August 2026. Specific settlement dates are detailed in the attached "CASH082026" annexure.

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact as this is a standard operational notice providing the monthly settlement schedule. It primarily affects back-office and clearing operations of trading/clearing members rather than market pricing or specific securities.
