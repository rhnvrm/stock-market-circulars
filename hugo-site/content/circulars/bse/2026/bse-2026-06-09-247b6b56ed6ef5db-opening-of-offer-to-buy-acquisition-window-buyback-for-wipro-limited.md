## Summary

BSE has notified trading members and custodians of the opening of Wipro Limited's buyback offer via the tender offer route. Wipro will buy back up to 60,00,00,000 (60 crore) fully paid-up equity shares of face value Rs. 2/- each at Rs. 250 per share, for an aggregate consideration of Rs. 1,50,00,00,00,000 (Rs. 15,000 crore). BSE is the designated stock exchange for this buyback. The offer window is open from Thursday, June 11, 2026 to Wednesday, June 17, 2026.

## Key Points

- Wipro Limited is conducting a buyback of up to 60 crore equity shares (face value Rs. 2/- each) at Rs. 250 per share via the tender offer route.
- Aggregate buyback consideration: Rs. 15,000 crore.
- The buyback represents 5.72% of the total number of equity shares in the paid-up equity share capital.
- Buyback size is 24.99% of standalone and 19.99% of consolidated aggregate paid-up equity share capital and free reserves (as of March 31, 2026), both within the statutory 25% limit.
- Record date for eligible shareholders: Friday, June 5, 2026.
- BSE is the designated stock exchange for this buyback.
- Offer is governed by SEBI circular no. CFD/DCR2/CIR/P/2016/131 dated Dec 09, 2016 and BSE notice no. 20170210-16 dated Feb 10, 2017 and 20190424-35 dated April 24, 2019.
- Letter of Offer is attached to the circular for reference.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This circular operates under the existing SEBI mechanism for acquisition of shares through stock exchange pursuant to tender offers under Takeover, Buyback, and De-listing regulations, including SEBI circulars CIR/CFD/POLICYCELL/1/2015 (April 13, 2015) and CFD/DCR2/CIR/P/2016/131 (December 9, 2016), and BSE's revised guidelines issued via notices dated February 10, 2017; April 24, 2019; May 28, 2020; November 2, 2020; and August 25, 2021.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and custodians must note the opening of the acquisition window and facilitate client participation as per SEBI and BSE guidelines for tender offer buybacks.
- All market participants must refer to the attached Letter of Offer and the details available on the BSE website (www.bseindia.com).
- Participation must be in accordance with the revised SEBI and BSE guidelines for the tender offer mechanism.

## Important Dates

- **Record Date:** Friday, June 5, 2026 (eligibility date for shareholders)
- **Offer Opens:** Thursday, June 11, 2026
- **Offer Closes:** Wednesday, June 17, 2026
- **Circular Date:** June 9, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a high-impact corporate action for Wipro Limited (BSE ticker: WIPRO), one of India's largest IT companies. The buyback at Rs. 250 per share for an aggregate of Rs. 15,000 crore is significant in scale. Shareholders as of the June 5, 2026 record date are eligible to tender shares on a proportionate basis. The buyback price of Rs. 250 may represent a premium over the prevailing market price, creating arbitrage opportunities for eligible shareholders. Trading members and custodians must be prepared to handle client orders during the June 11–17 acquisition window. The buyback is within statutory limits under the Companies Act and SEBI Buyback Regulations, reducing equity share capital and potentially improving earnings per share metrics post-completion.
