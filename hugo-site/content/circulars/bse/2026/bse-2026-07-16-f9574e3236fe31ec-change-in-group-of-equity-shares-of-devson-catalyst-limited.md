## Summary

BSE has notified Trading Members that, in continuation of its earlier Notice No. 20260715-23 dated Wednesday, July 15, 2026, the equity shares of DEVSON CATALYST LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544823) will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment with effect from Thursday, July 30, 2026. As a result, dealings in the company's shares will shift to the M Group.

## Key Points

- Company: DEVSON CATALYST LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544823), listed on the SME segment.
- Trading group change: from MT Group (Trade for Trade) to M Group (Rolling segment).
- This notice is a continuation of BSE Notice No. 20260715-23 dated July 15, 2026.
- Trading Members may contact Mr. Anurag Jain (Tel: 022-2272 8822) for further details.
- Notice signed by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, DCS-Listing Department.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory change is introduced; this is an operational reclassification of the trading segment/group for the specified scrip under existing BSE SME segment rules.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members dealing in DEVSON CATALYST LIMITED shares should update their systems and processes to reflect the change from Trade for Trade (MT Group) settlement to normal Rolling settlement (M Group) from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- July 15, 2026: Original Notice No. 20260715-23 issued.
- July 16, 2026: Current confirmatory notice issued.
- July 30, 2026 (Thursday): Effective date of transfer to Rolling segment (M Group).

## Impact Assessment

The shift from Trade for Trade (MT Group) to Rolling segment (M Group) settlement typically eases trading restrictions, as Trade for Trade requires compulsory delivery-based settlement without intraday netting. Moving to the Rolling segment under M Group generally signals improved trading normalcy and liquidity conditions for the scrip, benefiting existing and prospective shareholders and traders of DEVSON CATALYST LIMITED.
