## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260520-37 informs trading members that CMS Info Systems Limited (BSE code: 543441) has fixed **May 22, 2026** as the record date for its equity share buyback offer. The record date is used to determine the entitlement and names of equity shareholders eligible to participate in the buyback.

## Key Points

- CMS Info Systems Limited (BSE: 543441) has announced a tender offer (buyback) of equity shares
- Record date fixed: **May 22, 2026**
- Ex-entitlement date: **May 22, 2026**
- Settlement number: **DR-636/2026-2027**
- Applicable segment: Dematerialised Securities – Rolling Settlement Segment
- Notice issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President – Listing Compliance and Operations, dated May 15, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification under existing BSE listing and buyback regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members of BSE are required to take note of the record date and ex-entitlement date
- Members must ensure eligible shareholders (those holding shares as of May 22, 2026) are properly informed of their entitlement to participate in the buyback offer

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Notice Date | May 20, 2026 |
| Notice Issued By BSE | May 15, 2026 |
| Record Date | May 22, 2026 |
| Ex-Entitlement Date | May 22, 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

This notice has a focused impact on shareholders of CMS Info Systems Limited. Investors holding shares of CMS Info Systems (BSE: 543441) as of the record date (May 22, 2026) will be eligible to participate in the buyback offer. Trading members should update their systems and notify eligible clients accordingly. There is no broader market or systemic impact from this notice.
