## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 3,50,00,000 new equity shares of SJ Corporation Limited (Scrip Code: 504398) have been listed and are permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Monday, April 27, 2026. These shares were issued at a premium on a preferential basis to Promoters and Non-Promoters.

## Key Points

- 3,50,00,000 equity shares of Re. 1/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 11/- (issue price Rs. 12/- per share)
- Issued to Promoters and Non-Promoters on a preferential basis
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 8355001 to 43355000
- Date of allotment: March 13, 2026
- ISIN: INE312B01027
- Trading permitted from April 27, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification pursuant to a completed preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to update their systems to reflect the new securities available for trading from April 27, 2026. Lock-in restrictions must be observed for the relevant tranches.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** March 13, 2026
- **Trading Commencement:** April 27, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (2,17,00,000 shares, Dist. Nos. 8355001–30055000):** October 31, 2027
- **Lock-in Expiry (1,33,00,000 shares, Dist. Nos. 30055001–43355000):** October 31, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing increases the tradable float of SJ Corporation Limited by 3.5 crore shares. However, a significant portion (2,17,00,000 shares) is locked in until October 2027, limiting immediate market impact. The remaining 1,33,00,000 shares have a shorter lock-in until October 2026. The preferential issue at Rs. 12/- may signal capital-raising activity by the company. Overall market impact is moderate and limited to shareholders and traders of this specific scrip.
