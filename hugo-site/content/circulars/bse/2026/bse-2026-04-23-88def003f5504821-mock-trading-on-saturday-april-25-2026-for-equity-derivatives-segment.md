## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Equity Derivatives segment on Saturday, April 25, 2026. The session is intended for trading members using third-party empaneled vendor platforms or in-house developed systems via ETI APIs to test their trading applications under various market conditions, including exceptional scenarios such as risk-reduction mode and trading halts.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session is scheduled for **Saturday, April 25, 2026** for the **Equity Derivatives segment**
- Open to trading members using third-party platforms (empaneled vendors) or in-house systems via ETI APIs
- No new BOLT TWS release will accompany this session
- Trades from mock trading carry **no margin obligations, no pay-in/pay-out obligations**, and create no rights or liabilities
- Members are requested to submit feedback by **4:00 PM** on the day of the session

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice for a system testing session.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members using ETI APIs (third-party or in-house) are encouraged to actively participate in the mock session
- Members should provide feedback on the mock trading session to BSE via the contact details provided:
  - **BSE Tech Support**: 022-22728053 | bse.tech@bseindia.com
  - **BSE Help Desk**: 022-45720400/600 & 022-69158500 | bsehelp@bseindia.com
- Feedback deadline: **4:00 PM on April 25, 2026**

## Important Dates

| Event | Date/Time |
|---|---|
| Mock Trading Session | Saturday, April 25, 2026 |
| Log-in | 09:15 AM – 10:00 AM |
| Pre-Open Order Entry | 10:00 AM – 10:07/08 AM (random stoppage in last 1 min) |
| Buffer Period | 10:08 AM – 10:12 AM |
| Order Matching Period | 10:12 AM – 10:15 AM |
| Continuous Trading | 10:15 AM – 03:00 PM |
| Feedback Deadline | 4:00 PM, April 25, 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact, informational notice. The mock trading session has no financial consequences — trades do not result in margin calls, settlement obligations, or legal liabilities. The session is relevant only to trading members actively using ETI API-based systems (third-party or in-house) for the Equity Derivatives segment. Participation is voluntary but encouraged to ensure system readiness and robustness.
