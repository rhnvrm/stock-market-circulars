## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Electronic Gold Receipts (EGR) segment on Saturday, June 06, 2026. The session is intended for trading members to test their trading applications, including third-party platforms and in-house systems via IML/ETI APIs, and to fulfil regulatory testing requirements under SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD1/DSAP/CIR/P/2020/234 dated November 24, 2020.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for EGR segment on Saturday, June 06, 2026
- Members may use this session or the UAT environment to meet regulatory testing requirements per SEBI circular no. SEBI/HO/MRD1/DSAP/CIR/P/2020/234
- Third-party platform users and in-house system developers using IML/ETI APIs can test functionalities including risk-reduction mode and trading halt scenarios
- No new BOLT TWS Release will be issued
- Trades from mock sessions carry no margin obligation, pay-in, or pay-out obligations, and create no rights or liabilities
- Members are requested to submit feedback by 4:30 PM on the day of the session

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. Members are directed to refer to Exchange Notice No. 20201201-22 dated December 1, 2020, and SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD1/DSAP/CIR/P/2020/234 dated November 24, 2020, regarding testing of software used in or related to trading and risk management.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must fulfil regulatory software testing requirements by participating either in this mock trading session or in the UAT environment
- Members using third-party empaneled vendor platforms or in-house systems via IML/ETI APIs should use this opportunity to validate various functionalities
- Feedback to be submitted to BSE by 4:30 PM on June 06, 2026, via contact numbers or email provided

## Important Dates

- **June 06, 2026 (Saturday):** Mock trading session for EGR segment
  - Log-in: 10:15 AM – 11:00 AM
  - Pre-Open Order Entry: 11:00 AM – 11:08 AM
  - Pre-Open Matching: 11:08 AM – 11:15 AM
  - Continuous Trading: 11:15 AM – 3:30 PM
  - Block Deal Window: 12:00 PM – 12:15 PM
  - Closing: 3:30 PM – 3:40 PM
  - Post-Closing: 3:40 PM – 3:50 PM
  - Feedback deadline: 4:30 PM

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine mock trading event for system testing and familiarisation purposes only. Participation is voluntary and trades executed during the session carry no financial obligations. The session primarily affects trading members who use the EGR segment and wish to validate their trading infrastructure ahead of live market operations.
