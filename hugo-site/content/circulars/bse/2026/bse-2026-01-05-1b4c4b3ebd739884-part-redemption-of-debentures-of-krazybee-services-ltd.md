## Summary

KRAZYBEE SERVICES LTD has announced part redemption of its Non-Convertible Debentures (ISIN: INE07HK07791, Code: 976345) along with interest payment. The record date for this corporate action is January 8, 2026, and trading in these debentures will commence with a reduced face value effective from the same date.

## Key Points

- Company: KRAZYBEE SERVICES LTD
- Security Code: 976345
- ISIN: INE07HK07791
- Corporate Action: Part Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- Reduced Face Value: Rs. 50,000 per Debenture (from Rs. 100,000)
- Reduction: 50% of original face value
- Settlement Number: DR-794/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action under existing debt securities regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must note the change in face value for trading purposes
- All trades executed on or after January 8, 2026 will reflect the reduced face value of Rs. 50,000 per debenture
- Members should update their systems to reflect the new face value for accurate settlement

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: January 8, 2026
- **Effective Date for Reduced Face Value Trading**: January 8, 2026
- **Settlement**: DR-794/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

**Debenture Holders**: Existing debenture holders will receive partial redemption of their investment along with accrued interest. The face value per debenture reduces by 50% from Rs. 100,000 to Rs. 50,000.

**Market Impact**: Limited market-wide impact as this affects only KRAZYBEE SERVICES LTD debenture holders. Trading liquidity may be affected temporarily as market participants adjust to the new face value.

**Trading Members**: Must ensure proper system updates to reflect the reduced face value to avoid settlement discrepancies.
