## Summary

BSE has notified trading members about the non-competitive bidding facility for the auction of GoI dated security — 6.94% GS 2036 — scheduled on June 05, 2026. Bidding is available via the NCB-GSec module on BSE's iBBS web-based system. This is in continuation of the facility launched via Exchange circular no. 20180423-42 dated April 23, 2018.

## Key Points

- Security: 6.94% GS 2036 (Symbol: 694GS36J26), type G-Sec, Re-issue
- Issue Size: Rs. 34,000 Cr
- Minimum subscription: 100 units; Minimum amount: Rs. 10,000
- Bidding platform: NCB-GSec module on https://ibbs.bseindia.com
- Bid collection opens June 02, 2026 from 10:00 am (24-hour availability)
- Settlement date: June 08, 2026
- RBI press release dated June 01, 2026 announced the auction

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This notice operates under the existing non-competitive bidding framework established by Exchange circular no. 20180423-42 (April 23, 2018).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the NCB-GSec module on the iBBS platform to place bids on behalf of clients.
- Members must ensure bids are submitted by June 05, 2026 till 8:00 am.
- Direct investors must submit bids by June 04, 2026 till 5:00 pm.
- For new user ID creation or password reset: contact Trading Operations at user.trdops@bseindia.com or 022-2272-8005/8215.
- For bidding-related queries: trading.ops@bseindia.com or 022-2272-8376/5626/8226.
- For obligation and pay-in queries: ICCL Operations at Collaterals@icclindia.com / settlements@icclindia.com or 022-2272-8468/8704/8264.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, time-sensitive operational notice for debt segment participants. Trading members and direct investors wishing to participate in the non-competitive auction for 6.94% GS 2036 must act within the narrow bidding window. The large issue size of Rs. 34,000 Cr reflects significant government borrowing activity. No market-wide disruption is expected; impact is limited to members and investors active in the government securities segment.
