## Summary

BSE's Surveillance department (DOSS) has issued Notice No. 20260513-22 revising the price bands for 8 equity segment securities effective May 14, 2026. The revised bands range from 2% to 10%, indicating tightened surveillance controls on these scrips.

## Key Points

- Price bands revised for 8 securities effective May 14, 2026
- Bands tightened to levels between 2% and 10%
- Issued by BSE Surveillance department under DOSS
- Affects equity segment trading members
- Contact: bse.surv@bseindia.com for clarifications

## Regulatory Changes

The following price band revisions have been mandated:

| Scrip Code | Scrip Name | Revised Price Band |
|------------|------------|-------------------|
| 540788 | Aspira Pathlab & Diagnostics Ltd | 10% |
| 512608 | Bhandari Hosiery Exports Ltd | 5% |
| 532640 | Cyber Media India Ltd | 5% |
| 504351 | Empower India Ltd | 2% |
| 521018 | Maral Overseas Ltd | 5% |
| 751113 | Shakti Press Ltd | 10% |
| 530697 | Zenlabs Ethica Ltd | 10% |
| 541400 | Zim Laboratories Ltd | 5% |

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE must ensure compliance with the revised price bands from May 14, 2026 onwards. Orders placed outside the revised price band limits will be rejected by the exchange system. No specific action is required from members beyond awareness of the new limits.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** May 13, 2026
- **Effective Date:** May 14, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The downward revision of price bands to 2%–10% signals heightened surveillance scrutiny on these securities. Empower India Ltd (504351) faces the most restrictive 2% band, severely limiting intraday price movement. Securities moved to lower bands typically experience reduced liquidity and wider bid-ask spreads. Traders and investors holding positions in these stocks should be aware of the tighter daily movement limits. The Surveillance department's action suggests these stocks may have exhibited abnormal price or volume behavior warranting closer monitoring.
