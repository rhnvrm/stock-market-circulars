## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in two series of Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) issued by Edelweiss Financial Services Ltd. effective January 13, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these securities from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Two NCD series affected: EFSL-8.95%-29-1-26-NCD (ISIN: INE532F07ET2, Code: 939186) and EFSL-29-1-26-NCD (ISIN: INE532F07EV8, Code: 939188)
- Record date set for January 13, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Trading suspension effective from January 13, 2026
- Notice reference: DR-797/2025-2026
- Segment: Debt market

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notification for debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from January 13, 2026
- Trading members are required to take note of the suspension and ensure compliance
- Investors holding these debentures on the record date will be eligible for interest payment and redemption proceeds

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 6, 2026
- **Record Date**: January 13, 2026
- **No Dealings From**: January 13, 2026
- **Redemption Date**: January 29, 2026 (based on NCD nomenclature)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - affects debenture holders and debt market participants dealing in Edelweiss Financial Services NCDs. The suspension is limited to two specific NCD series approaching maturity.

**Investor Impact**: Investors holding these debentures on the record date will receive their redemption proceeds and final interest payment. No trading will be possible from January 13, 2026 onwards.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update their systems and ensure no transactions are executed in these securities post-suspension date. Standard corporate action processing for debenture redemption.
