## Summary

BSE's Surveillance department (DOSS) observed significant price and volume movement in eleven listed companies and sought clarifications. This notice (20260610-24) reports that eight companies have submitted their replies, while three companies' responses are still pending.

## Key Points

- BSE wrote to eleven companies following unusual price/volume activity in their scrips
- Eight companies have responded; their replies are attached to the circular
- Three companies have not yet responded and their replies are awaited
- Issued by the Surveillance department on behalf of BSE Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an informational surveillance notice.

## Compliance Requirements

Companies that have received BSE queries regarding unusual price/volume movements are required to respond promptly with relevant clarifications to enable informed trading decisions by market participants. Replies from Elango Industries, Colinz Laboratories, and SRU Steels are still awaited.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 10 June 2026
- No specific deadlines mentioned for pending replies

## Impact Assessment

This notice primarily serves an investor protection and transparency function. The attached replies from eight companies may contain material information relevant to trading decisions in those scrips. Investors and trading members holding or considering positions in the three companies with pending replies (Elango Industries, Colinz Laboratories, SRU Steels) should remain alert for follow-up notices once clarifications are received.
