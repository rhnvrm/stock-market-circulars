## Summary

BSE has announced a corporate action for Securities Lending and Borrowing (SLB) securities. Kotak Mahindra Bank Ltd. will undergo a stock split with the record date and ex-date set for 14-January-2026. The SLB market will have a shut period from 13-Jan-2026 to 14-Jan-2026 to facilitate the corporate action adjustments.

## Key Points

- Security affected: Kotak Mahindra Bank Ltd.
- Corporate action type: Stock Split
- Book Closure/Record Date: 14-January-2026
- Ex-Date: 14-January-2026
- Shut Period: 13-Jan-2026 to 14-Jan-2026
- Affects all SLB positions in the security

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action notification for SLB securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- All SLB participants with positions in Kotak Mahindra Bank Ltd. must note the shut period
- No new SLB transactions in this security will be allowed during the shut period
- Existing positions will be adjusted according to the stock split ratio
- Market participants should plan their SLB strategies accounting for the two-day shut period

## Important Dates

- **13-January-2026**: SLB shut period begins
- **14-January-2026**: Book Closure/Record Date and Ex-Date for stock split; shut period ends

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: High - Kotak Mahindra Bank is a major banking stock with significant SLB activity. The shut period will temporarily halt all SLB transactions in this security.

**Operational Impact**: Medium - Participants need to adjust their SLB positions and strategies. Outstanding contracts will be modified based on the stock split ratio, which may affect margin requirements and position valuations.

**Liquidity Impact**: Temporary reduction in SLB liquidity for this specific security during the shut period. Participants looking to borrow or lend Kotak Mahindra Bank shares through SLB must complete transactions before 13-January-2026 or wait until after 14-January-2026.
