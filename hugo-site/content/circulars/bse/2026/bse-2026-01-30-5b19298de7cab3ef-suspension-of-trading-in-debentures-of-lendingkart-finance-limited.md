## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in specific debentures of Lendingkart Finance Limited (ISIN: INE090W07675) effective February 6, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing February 8, 2026 as the record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures during the suspension period.

## Key Points

- Trading suspended for LFL-11.33%-23-2-26-PVT debentures (Code: 975428)
- ISIN: INE090W07675
- No dealings permitted from February 6, 2026
- Record date: February 8, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Circular reference: DR-815/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action procedure for debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from February 6, 2026
- All trading members must take note of the suspension and ensure compliance
- No transactions should be executed in ISIN INE090W07675 during the suspension period

## Important Dates

- **January 30, 2026**: Circular issued
- **February 6, 2026**: No dealings commence (trading suspension begins)
- **February 8, 2026**: Record date for redemption and interest payment

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to debt market participants and holders of specific Lendingkart Finance debentures. The suspension prevents trading activity during the record date determination period.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders as of the record date will receive redemption proceeds and interest payment. Investors cannot trade these debentures from February 6, 2026.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to block trading in the specified ISIN and ensure no inadvertent transactions occur during the suspension period.
