## Summary

BSE Ltd. has published the schedule for Registrar and Transfer Agent (RTA) visits to its premises during February 2026. The circular lists companies whose share transfer and related investor services are handled by Adroit Corporate Services Pvt. Ltd., which will be visiting BSE during this period. This facility enables investors to meet RTA representatives in person for resolution of grievances related to share transfers, dematerialization, dividend payments, and other investor service matters.

## Key Points

- RTA visit schedule for February 2026 at BSE Ltd. premises
- Adroit Corporate Services Pvt. Ltd. is the featured RTA for this period
- List includes 52 companies serviced by Adroit Corporate
- Companies span various sectors including finance, manufacturing, real estate, textiles, and technology
- Investors can meet RTA representatives for grievance redressal and investor services

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance requirements. This is an informational circular for investor convenience.

## Important Dates

- **Visit Period:** February 2026
- **Circular Date:** January 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Negligible. This is a routine administrative arrangement.

**Operational Impact:** Low. Facilitates investor service delivery by enabling face-to-face meetings between investors and RTA representatives at BSE premises.

**Investor Impact:** Positive for investors holding shares in the listed companies, as it provides an additional channel for resolving share transfer, dematerialization, and dividend-related issues.

## Companies Covered by Adroit Corporate Services

The circular lists 52 companies whose investor services are managed by Adroit Corporate Services Pvt. Ltd., including:

- Antariksh Industries Ltd (501270)
- Anand Projects Ltd (501630)
- IndiaNivesh Ltd (501700)
- Marathon Nextgen Realty Ltd (503101)
- Hind Rectifiers Ltd (504036)
- Securekloud Technologies Ltd (512161)
- Safari Industries India Ltd (523025)
- And 45 other listed companies

Investors holding shares in these companies can visit BSE during February 2026 to meet Adroit Corporate representatives for assistance with investor services.
