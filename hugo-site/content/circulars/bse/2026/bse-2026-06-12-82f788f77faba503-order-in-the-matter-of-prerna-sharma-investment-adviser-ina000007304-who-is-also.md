## Summary

SEBI, vide order no. CGM/VS/WRO/WRO-DIV-4/61/2026 dated June 11, 2026, has cancelled the certificate of registration of Ms. Prerna Sharma as an Investment Adviser (SEBI Registration No. INA000007304), effective June 11, 2026. BSE has disseminated this notice (No. 20260612-23) to all Investment Advisers and investors for awareness.

## Key Points

- Ms. Prerna Sharma held dual registrations: Investment Adviser (INA000007304) and Research Analyst (INH000006819).
- SEBI's order no. CGM/VS/WRO/WRO-DIV-4/61/2026 cancelled her Investment Adviser registration with effect from June 11, 2026.
- The full order is available on the SEBI website (https://www.sebi.gov.in) and attached to this BSE notice.
- All Investment Advisers and investors are requested to take note of this cancellation.

## Regulatory Changes

SEBI has exercised its enforcement powers to cancel the registration of Ms. Prerna Sharma as an Investment Adviser. This follows SEBI's Western Regional Office (WRO) Division 4 proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

- Investment Advisers should note that Ms. Prerna Sharma is no longer a registered Investment Adviser as of June 11, 2026.
- Investors who were receiving advisory services from Ms. Prerna Sharma should seek services from a duly registered Investment Adviser.
- No one should engage or continue to engage her as an Investment Adviser after the effective date of cancellation.

## Important Dates

- **June 11, 2026**: Effective date of cancellation of Investment Adviser registration (INA000007304).
- **June 11, 2026**: Date of SEBI order no. CGM/VS/WRO/WRO-DIV-4/61/2026.
- **June 12, 2026**: Date of BSE notice dissemination (Notice No. 20260612-23).

## Impact Assessment

This enforcement action has limited systemic market impact but is significant for investors who engaged Ms. Prerna Sharma for investment advisory services. Such investors must now transition to other SEBI-registered Investment Advisers. The action underscores SEBI's continued enforcement of registration and conduct norms for Investment Advisers and Research Analysts.
