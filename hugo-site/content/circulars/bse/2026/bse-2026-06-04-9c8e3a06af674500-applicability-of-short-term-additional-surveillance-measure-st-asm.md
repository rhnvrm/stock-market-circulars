## Summary

BSE has identified securities satisfying criteria for inclusion under Short Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) at both Stage I and Stage II. Enhanced margin requirements become effective June 08, 2026, covering open positions as of June 05, 2026 and all new positions created from June 08, 2026 onwards. The affected securities are listed in Annexure I attached to the notice.

## Key Points

- Securities meeting ST-ASM Stage I criteria will attract a 50% margin rate or the existing applicable margin, whichever is higher, subject to a maximum cap of 100%.
- Securities meeting ST-ASM Stage II criteria will attract a 100% margin rate or the existing applicable margin, whichever is higher (effectively 100% in all cases).
- Both stages are effective from June 08, 2026, covering open positions as on June 05, 2026 and new positions from June 08, 2026.
- ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the Exchanges.
- Inclusion under ASM is purely for market surveillance purposes and does not constitute an adverse action against the concerned company.
- Upon exit from the framework, the price band of a scrip will be reinstated to its pre-ASM level, unless the scrip remains under another surveillance measure.

## Regulatory Changes

This notice is issued further to earlier ASM framework revisions:
- Exchange notice 20240920-71 dated September 20, 2024
- Exchange notice 20181027-1 dated October 27, 2018
- Exchange notice 20190719-33 dated July 19, 2019
- Exchange notice 20201204-56 dated December 04, 2020
- Exchange notice 20230925-49 dated September 25, 2023
- Exchange notice 20240920-63 dated September 20, 2024

No new framework changes are introduced; this notice applies the existing ST-ASM framework to newly qualifying securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure ST-ASM Stage I margin of 50% (or existing margin, whichever is higher, capped at 100%) is collected on affected securities from June 08, 2026.
- Trading members must ensure ST-ASM Stage II margin of 100% is collected on affected Stage II securities from June 08, 2026.
- Members should review Annexure I to identify the specific securities impacted and update their risk and margin systems accordingly.
- For clarifications, members may contact BSE Surveillance at bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **June 05, 2026**: Reference date for open positions subject to revised margin requirements.
- **June 08, 2026**: Effective date for revised ST-ASM margin rates on both open and new positions.

## Impact Assessment

The enhanced margin requirements under ST-ASM directly increase the cost of holding or initiating positions in the affected securities. Stage II securities effectively require 100% upfront margin, significantly restricting leveraged trading. This is intended to curb excessive speculation or volatility in the shortlisted scrips. Market participants with existing positions in these securities as of June 05, 2026 must ensure adequate margin is available before June 08, 2026 to avoid forced liquidation. The measure applies across all participant categories trading in the equity segment.
