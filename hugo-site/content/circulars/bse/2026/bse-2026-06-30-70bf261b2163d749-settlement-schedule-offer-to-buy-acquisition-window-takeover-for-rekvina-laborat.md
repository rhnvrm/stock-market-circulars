## Summary

BSE has issued the settlement calendar for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for Rekvina Laboratories Limited. The acquisition window will run from July 1, 2026 to July 14, 2026, with settlement scheduled for July 28, 2026 under Settlement No. 2026080.

## Key Points

- Settlement Type: Takeover (Settlement No. 2026080)
- Bidding window: July 1, 2026 to July 14, 2026
- Obligation download date: July 27, 2026
- Settlement date: July 28, 2026
- Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism must be used for demat share delivery via both CDSL and NSDL depositories
- Market Type designation in the depository system: "(Takeover)" with Settlement No. 2026080
- Members/Custodians are NOT required to upload any EPN file
- CDSL & NSDL early pay-in securities will be earmarked/blocked in clients' accounts
- Members should refer to MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for client-wise Stamp Duty charges

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. Members and Custodians are directed to refer to existing Notice No. 20200528-32 dated May 28, 2020, regarding settlement as per the Revised Guidelines of Mechanism for acquisition of shares through Stock Exchange.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members/Custodians must deliver demat shares exclusively through the Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism available in both depositories (CDSL and NSDL)
- Early pay-in of securities must be completed sufficiently in advance of prescribed timelines for closure of the BSE OTB Bidding window (for members placing bids) and confirmation window (for Custodians confirming bids)
- Use Market Type "(Takeover)" and Settlement No. 2026080 in the depository system
- No EPN file upload is required

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Bid Start Date | July 1, 2026 |
| Bid End Date | July 14, 2026 |

| Settlement Date | July 28, 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

This circular is primarily operational, affecting members and custodians participating in the Rekvina Laboratories Limited takeover offer. Participants must ensure early pay-in compliance before the BSE OTB platform deadlines to avoid bid rejection. The broader market impact is limited to parties involved in this specific acquisition.
