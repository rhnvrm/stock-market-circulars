## Summary

BSE has revised the trading timings for the Equity Derivatives segment in connection with the introduction of the Closing Auction Session (CAS) in the Equity Cash Segment. The key change is the extension of continuous trading end time from 3:30 PM to 3:40 PM, and the update of the VWAP calculation window from the last 30 minutes (3:00–3:30 PM) to 3:10–3:40 PM. These changes are effective Monday, August 3, 2026.

## Key Points

- Continuous trading session end time for Equity Derivatives is changed to **3:40 PM** (previously 3:30 PM)
- Trade Modification session remains until 4:15 PM
- Pre-Open session structure is retained: Order Entry (9:00–9:07/08 AM), Order Matching & Confirmation (9:08–9:12 AM), Buffer Period (9:12–9:15 AM)
- Log-in session: 7:50 AM to 9:00 AM
- VWAP for close price computation will now use the last 30 minutes from **3:10 PM to 3:40 PM**
- Close price methodology otherwise remains unchanged
- Mock trading session dates will be communicated via a separate circular

## Regulatory Changes

This circular follows SEBI circular No. SEBI/HO/47/11/11(3)2025-MRD-POD2/I/2765/2026 dated January 16, 2026, and BSE circular no. 20260119-17 dated January 19, 2026, which introduced the Closing Auction Session in the Equity Cash Segment. The current notice aligns Equity Derivatives trading timings with the new cash market structure.

## Compliance Requirements

- All trading members must update their systems to reflect the revised trading timings for the Equity Derivatives segment before August 3, 2026
- Members should participate in mock trading sessions (dates to be announced) to test readiness
- Risk and settlement systems must account for the updated VWAP window (3:10–3:40 PM) for close price calculations

## Important Dates

- **August 3, 2026 (Monday)**: Effective date for revised trading timings in Equity Derivatives segment
- Mock trading session dates: To be announced via a separate BSE circular

## Impact Assessment

The extension of continuous trading by 10 minutes (to 3:40 PM) in the Equity Derivatives segment aligns it with changes in the cash market introduced via the Closing Auction Session. This affects all participants in equity futures and options, requiring system and operational updates. The revised VWAP window (3:10–3:40 PM instead of 3:00–3:30 PM) will impact close price computation, daily mark-to-market settlements, and any strategies tied to end-of-day pricing. Brokers, proprietary desks, and algorithmic traders will need to update their cut-off timers and risk systems before the go-live date.
