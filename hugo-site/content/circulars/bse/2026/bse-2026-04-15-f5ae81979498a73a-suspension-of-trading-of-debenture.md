## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (No. 20260415-5) informing trading members that three debt securities issued by Kinara Capital Pvt Ltd will be suspended from trading effective April 16, 2026. The suspension is due to Redemption/Extinguishment or Application for Delisting made by the company.

## Key Points

- Three debentures of Kinara Capital Pvt Ltd are being suspended from trading on the BSE Debt segment.
- Suspension is effective from **April 16, 2026** (reference DR-611/2026-2027).
- Reason for suspension: Redemption/Extinguishment/Application for Delisting by the company.
- Trading members are advised not to deal in these securities from the effective date.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine operational notice under BSE's Listing Operations and Compliance framework for debt securities undergoing redemption or delisting.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must immediately cease dealing in the following debt securities from April 16, 2026:
  1. Scrip Code **975472** | ISIN: INE200W07381 | Kinara Capital Pvt Ltd
  2. Scrip Code **975668** | ISIN: INE200W07407 | Kinara Capital Pvt Ltd
  3. Scrip Code **976101** | ISIN: INE200W08124 | Kinara Capital Pvt Ltd
- All trading members are required to take note of this suspension.

## Important Dates

- **April 15, 2026**: Notice issued by BSE.
- **April 16, 2026**: Effective date of trading suspension for all three debentures.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and active traders of these three specific Kinara Capital debentures on the BSE Debt segment. As this is driven by redemption or extinguishment, affected investors will receive proceeds through the standard redemption process. There is no broader market or systemic impact anticipated.
