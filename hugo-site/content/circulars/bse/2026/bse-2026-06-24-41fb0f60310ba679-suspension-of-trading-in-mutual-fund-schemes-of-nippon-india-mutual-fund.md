## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that four schemes under Nippon India Fixed Horizon Fund - XLIII - Series 1 will mature on their respective Redemption Dates. Trading in all four schemes is suspended with effect from June 25, 2026, as part of the standard maturity process for close-ended mutual fund schemes.

## Key Points

- Four Nippon India Fixed Horizon Fund - XLIII - Series 1 schemes are maturing and trading is suspended from June 25, 2026.
- The suspension covers both Direct and Regular plan variants, as well as Growth and IDCW options.
- Reference: DR-659/2026-2027.
- No attachment was included with this notice.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice under existing BSE listing compliance procedures for maturing close-ended mutual fund schemes.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are advised **not to deal** in the following mutual fund schemes with effect from June 25, 2026:

| Scrip Code | ISIN | Scheme |
|---|---|---|
| 543342 | INF204KC1188 | Nippon India Fixed Horizon Fund - XLIII - Series 1 - Direct IDCW Plan |
| 543343 | INF204KC1170 | Nippon India Fixed Horizon Fund - XLIII - Series 1 - Direct Plan Growth Option |
| 543344 | INF204KC1162 | Nippon India Fixed Horizon Fund - XLIII - Series 1 - IDCW Plan |
| 543345 | INF204KC1154 | Nippon India Fixed Horizon Fund - XLIII - Series 1 - Growth Option |

## Important Dates

- **June 24, 2026**: Notice issued by BSE.
- **June 25, 2026**: Effective date of trading suspension for all four schemes.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to investors and trading members holding or dealing in these four specific close-ended fixed horizon fund schemes of Nippon India Mutual Fund. This is a routine end-of-life action for fixed maturity plans; unitholders will receive redemption proceeds as per the scheme terms. No broader market or systemic impact is expected.
