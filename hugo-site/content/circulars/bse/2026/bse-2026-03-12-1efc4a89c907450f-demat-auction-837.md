## Summary

BSE has published the list of securities for Demat Auction 837, dated 12 March 2026. The auction covers 86 scrips listed on the Bombay Stock Exchange, with quantities subject to auction settlement ranging from 1 share to 883,228 shares (SpiceJet).

## Key Points

- Settlement No. 837 dated 12 March 2026
- 86 scrips included across diverse sectors including pharma, infrastructure, energy, finance, and FMCG
- Largest auction quantity: SPICEJET LTD (Scrip Code 500285) — 883,228 shares
- Other notable quantities: EVEXIA LIFECARE (47,197 shares), FERTILIZERS (24,008 shares), ROYAL INDIA (122,593 shares), ADANI TOTAL (9,290 shares)
- Most scrips have small auction quantities (under 500 shares)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice for demat auction settlement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Brokers and members with short delivery positions in the listed scrips must participate in or be aware of the auction settlement process
- Members should reconcile their positions for all 86 listed scrips against their settlement obligations

## Important Dates

- Auction Settlement Date: 12 March 2026
- Settlement No.: 837

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine demat auction notice with moderate market impact. The large SpiceJet quantity (883,228 shares) could influence intraday price movement for that stock. ROYAL INDIA (122,593 shares) and EVEXIA LIFECARE (47,197 shares) are also notable. The majority of scrips have small quantities with minimal price impact expected. Market participants with positions in these scrips should monitor auction outcomes for settlement purposes.
