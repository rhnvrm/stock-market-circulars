## Summary

BSE has issued a notice forwarding a Gazette Notification received from SEBI concerning 23 individuals who have been officially declared as 'Terrorist' under the Fourth Schedule of the Unlawful Activities (Prevention) Act, 1967, pursuant to Section 35 of the Act. The Gazette Notification is enclosed as Annexure-1 for members' reference.

## Key Points

- SEBI shared a copy of the Gazette Notification with BSE regarding 23 individuals declared as 'Terrorist'.
- These individuals have been listed in the Fourth Schedule of the UA(P) Act, 1967 under Section 35.
- The notification is enclosed as Annexure-1 to the circular.
- Members are advised to take note for necessary actions and compliance.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory rule change; this is a notification of updates to the Fourth Schedule of the UA(P) Act, 1967, which lists individuals designated as terrorists under Section 35 of the Act.

## Compliance Requirements

Member brokers and trading members are advised to take note of the Gazette Notification and undertake necessary actions and compliances, which may include screening client and counterparty records against the updated list of designated individuals as part of anti-money laundering and counter-terrorism financing obligations.

## Important Dates

Notice issued: 20 July 2026 (Notice No. 20260720-11). No specific compliance deadline mentioned in the circular.

## Impact Assessment

This circular has no direct market or trading impact and does not pertain to any specific listed security. It is an administrative/compliance advisory requiring member firms to be aware of and act upon updated terrorist designations for regulatory (AML/CFT) compliance purposes.
