## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (No. 20260522-1) providing the live activities schedule for the Opening of Offer for Sale (OFS) for Central Bank of India on the equity segment. This notice is directed at trading members and market participants involved in the OFS process.

## Key Points

- Notice No.: 20260522-1, dated 22 May 2026
- Central Bank of India OFS is being conducted on BSE's equity segment
- Issued by the Trading Operations department under the Trading category
- The circular outlines the live activities schedule to guide brokers and participants during the OFS window
- No attachments are associated with this notice

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an operational notice detailing the schedule for an existing OFS mechanism.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members and market participants must adhere to the live activities schedule published by BSE Trading Operations for the Central Bank of India OFS. Members should ensure their systems and order management processes are aligned with the schedule timelines.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 22 May 2026
- OFS opening activities: as per the live activities schedule detailed by BSE (refer to Notice No. 20260522-1)

## Impact Assessment

This notice primarily impacts brokers, trading members, and investors looking to participate in the Central Bank of India OFS. The live activities schedule ensures orderly participation in the offer. Medium impact on the broader market; directly relevant to participants in this specific OFS.
