## Summary

BSE has listed five commercial papers issued on a private placement basis, admitting them to trading on the BSE Debt Segment effective June 3, 2026. The issuers are Sharekhan Limited, Network 18 Media & Investments Limited, Bajaj Finance Limited, Julius Baer Capital (India) Private Limited, and ICICI Home Finance Company Limited. All instruments carry top-tier short-term credit ratings (A1+) and mature in September 2026.

## Key Points

- All five CPs are traded in dematerialised form only, in standard denominations of ₹5 lakhs and multiples thereof, with a tick size of 1 paise.
- **Sharekhan Limited** (Scrip: 731794, ISIN: INE211H14AS5) – Qty 11,000 units, Issue Price ₹489,887.12, Rate 8.28% p.a., Redemption 02/09/2026. Rated CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+. Paying agent: HDFC Bank.
- **Network 18 Media & Investments Limited** (Scrip: 731797, ISIN: INE870H14XH3) – Qty 4,000 units, Issue Price ₹490,582.00, Rate 7.70% p.a., Redemption 02/09/2026. Rated CARE A1+, IND A1+. Paying agent: ICICI Bank.
- **Bajaj Finance Limited** (Scrip: 731798, ISIN: INE296A14H35) – Qty 21,500 units, Issue Price ₹490,318.50, Rate 7.92% p.a., Redemption 01/09/2026. Rated CRISIL A1+, IND A1+. Allotment date 02/06/2026. Paying agent: ICICI Bank.
- **Julius Baer Capital (India) Private Limited** (Scrip: 731795, ISIN: INE824H14UL8) – Qty 4,000 units, Issue Price ₹489,744.00, Rate 8.40% p.a., Redemption 02/09/2026. Rated CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+. Paying agent: HDFC Bank.
- **ICICI Home Finance Company Limited** (Scrip: 731796, ISIN: INE071G14HZ5) – Qty 2,100 units, Issue Price ₹491,062.50, Rate 7.30% p.a., Redemption 02/09/2026. Rated CARE A1+, ICRA A1+.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification under existing BSE Debt Segment framework for privately placed commercial papers.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities exclusively in dematerialised form under the respective ISINs.
- Transactions must be in the standard denomination of ₹5 lakhs and multiples thereof.
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE's Debt Department at 22728352 / 8597 / 8995 / 5753 / 8915.

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited to institutional and debt market participants trading on the BSE Debt Segment. These are standard 90-day commercial papers with top-tier A1+ ratings, representing low credit risk. The largest issuance by volume is Bajaj Finance (21,500 units), implying a face value of ₹1,075 crore. No equity market implications or changes to trading rules are involved. Routine operational communication for debt market participants.
