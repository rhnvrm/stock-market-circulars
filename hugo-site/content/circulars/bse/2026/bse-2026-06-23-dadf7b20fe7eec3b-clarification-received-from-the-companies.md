## Summary

BSE (Notice No. 20260623-20, dated 23 June 2026) has observed significant price/volume movement in 30 listed scrips and, in the interest of market transparency, wrote to the respective companies seeking clarification. Replies have been received from 21 companies and are attached to this notice; replies from the remaining 9 companies are still awaited.

## Key Points

- BSE's Surveillance department (DOSS) initiated the clarification process after detecting abnormal price/volume activity across 30 equity scrips.
- 21 companies have submitted their replies (attached as Replies1, Replies2, Replies3).
- 9 companies have not yet responded as of the notice date.
- This is a standard surveillance measure to ensure trading members and investors have access to the latest relevant information.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced by this notice. It is an operational surveillance action under BSE's existing market oversight framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- The 9 companies that have not yet replied are expected to submit their clarifications to BSE's Surveillance department promptly.
- Trading members and investors are advised to review the attached company replies before trading in the mentioned scrips.
- No specific action is mandated for investors at this stage beyond exercising informed caution.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 23 June 2026
- **Reply deadline for pending companies:** Not specified; implied urgency given exchange observation of price/volume movement.

## Impact Assessment

This circular signals heightened exchange scrutiny of 30 scrips due to unusual market activity. For the 21 companies that have responded, their clarifications are now publicly available, which may help stabilize market sentiment. The 9 companies with pending replies remain under watch, and further regulatory action (such as surveillance measures like ASM/GSM) cannot be ruled out if replies are not forthcoming. Investors and trading members should treat this as a caution signal and review company disclosures before taking positions in any of the listed scrips.
