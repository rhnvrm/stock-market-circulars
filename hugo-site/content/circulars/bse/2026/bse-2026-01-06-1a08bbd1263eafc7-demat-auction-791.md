## Summary

BSE has published the list of securities subject to demat auction under Settlement No. 791 dated January 6, 2026. The auction covers 88 scrips across various sectors, with quantities ranging from 1 share to 233,250 shares. This auction is a standard settlement mechanism used when sellers fail to deliver securities on the settlement date.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 791
- Auction Date: January 6, 2026
- Total number of scrips: 88 securities
- Largest quantity: YES BANK LTD (232,250 shares)
- Other significant quantities: WELCURE DRUG (50,000 shares), WELSPUN SPEC (10,000 shares), TOURISM FINA (18,255 shares)
- Includes major blue-chip stocks like RELIANCE IND, ICICI BANK, ADANI PORTS, HCL TECH, HINDALCO, ITC, NTPC

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard operational circular for demat auction settlement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Brokers and market participants must note the securities listed for auction
- Settlement obligations must be fulfilled through the auction mechanism
- Participants should ensure adequate funds/securities for auction participation

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: January 6, 2026
- Settlement Number: 791

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - The auction affects 88 securities across various market capitalizations. While routine, the inclusion of high-value stocks like YES BANK (233,250 shares) and major blue-chips indicates settlement failures that could impact specific trading members.

**Operational Impact:** Medium - Affected brokers must participate in the auction to close out their obligations. The auction mechanism helps maintain market integrity by ensuring trade settlement completion.

**Investor Impact:** Low to Medium - Direct impact limited to specific trades that failed to settle. Indirect impact on affected securities' liquidity during auction period.
