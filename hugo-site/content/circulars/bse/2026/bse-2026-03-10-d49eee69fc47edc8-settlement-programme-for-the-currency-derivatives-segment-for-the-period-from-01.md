## Summary

BSE has published the settlement programme for the Currency Derivatives segment for the period April 1, 2026 to April 30, 2026. The notice specifies daily settlement dates for all trade dates in April 2026 and highlights that the expiry and final settlement dates for April 2026 currency futures fall on April 28 and April 30, 2026, respectively.

## Key Points

- Settlement calendar covers trade dates from April 2, 2026 to April 30, 2026.
- Daily settlement dates are generally T+1 business days.
- April 28, 2026 is the expiry (last trading day) for April 2026 Currency Futures contracts.
- Final settlement date for April 2026 Currency Futures is April 30, 2026.
- The last trade date (April 30) settles on May 4, 2026.
- Issued by Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd. (ICCL).

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine operational notice publishing the monthly settlement calendar.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and clearing members in the Currency Derivatives segment must align their settlement and margin processes with the published schedule.
- Members should note the expiry date of April 28, 2026 for April 2026 currency futures and plan positions accordingly.
