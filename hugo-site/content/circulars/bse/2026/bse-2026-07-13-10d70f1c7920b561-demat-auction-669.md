## Summary

BSE Limited has invited offers from trading members for the purchase of securities in the Demat Auction (Auction No. 669) conducted in respect of Rolling Settlement No. DR-669/2026-27. This is a routine auction process to settle short deliveries in the settlement cycle.

## Key Points

- Notice No. 20260713-2 issued by BSE's Clearing and Settlement (BSECL) department under the Settlement/RMS category.
- Auction offers will be accepted on trading terminals on Monday, July 13, 2026.
- Auction offer entry window: 02:00 p.m. to 02:45 p.m.
- Auction matching: 02:45 p.m. to 03:00 p.m.
- Delivery statement and report of accepted auction offers: 03:00 p.m. to 03:30 p.m.
- Money statement and final reports: 03:35 p.m. to 05:30 p.m.
- Auction pay-in will be held on Tuesday, July 14, 2026 at 08:00 a.m.
- Settlement will be routed through the Clearing Member of a Trading Member, per the Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment.
- The prior restriction preventing trading members from offering securities in auction for settlements in which they themselves short-delivered has been removed.
- Trading members must exercise care while entering security codes and rates, as punching errors cannot be corrected after submission.
- The Exchange reserves the right to accept or reject any auction offer without providing reasons.
- Members are advised to refer to the cutoff rate on the Auction Offer Entry Screen and to check relevant corporate action notices (ex-Rights/ex-Bonus/ex-Split etc.) before submitting offers.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this notice reflects continued application of the Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment, under which settlement is routed through the Clearing Member of a Trading Member, and reiterates the removal of the earlier restriction on short-deliverers participating in auctions for the same settlement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and custodians must check their auction pay-in obligations for securities.
- Members must execute securities pay-in in depositories within the specified depository cut-off timings.
- Members should punch auction offers (security code and rate) accurately, as no corrections are permitted post-entry.

## Important Dates

- Auction offer entry: July 13, 2026, 02:00 p.m. – 02:45 p.m.
- Auction matching: July 13, 2026, 02:45 p.m. – 03:00 p.m.
- Delivery statement and accepted offers report: July 13, 2026, 03:00 p.m. – 03:30 p.m.
- Money statement and final reports: July 13, 2026, 03:35 p.m. – 05:30 p.m.
- Auction pay-in: July 14, 2026, 08:00 a.m.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, procedural settlement notice with limited market-wide impact. It is operationally relevant only to trading members and custodians with short-delivery obligations under Rolling Settlement DR-669/2026-27, who must participate in the auction process and meet pay-in deadlines to fulfill their settlement obligations.
