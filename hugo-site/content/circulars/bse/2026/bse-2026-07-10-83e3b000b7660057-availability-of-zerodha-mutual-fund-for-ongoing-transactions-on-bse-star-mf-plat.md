## Summary

BSE has notified members that ZERODHA Mutual Fund schemes, previously closed pursuant to notice no. 20260618-24 & 20260618-25 dated June 18, 2026, will reopen for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions, including SIP, STP and SWP facilities, on the BSE StAR MF Platform with effect from July 13, 2026.

## Key Points

- Two ZERODHA Mutual Fund schemes will resume ongoing transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform.
- Facilities being reinstated include subscriptions, redemptions, SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan).
- Scheme 1: ZERODHA LIFE CYCLE FUND 2036 - DIRECT PLAN - GROWTH (Equity Category), ISIN: INF0R8F01224.
- Scheme 2: ZERODHA LIFE CYCLE FUND 2041 - DIRECT PLAN - GROWTH (Equity Category), ISIN: INF0R8F01216.
- This follows a prior closure communicated via notice nos. 20260618-24 and 20260618-25 dated June 18, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is an operational update regarding transaction availability status for specific mutual fund schemes on the BSE StAR MF trading platform.

## Compliance Requirements

BSE members and market intermediaries dealing in these schemes should update their systems to reflect the reopening of ongoing transactions effective July 13, 2026, and inform relevant investors/distributors accordingly.

## Important Dates

- June 18, 2026: Original notices (20260618-24 & 20260618-25) regarding scheme closure.
- July 10, 2026: Current notice issued.
- July 13, 2026: Effective date for reopening of ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP transactions.

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal broader market impact, affecting only investors and distributors dealing in the two named ZERODHA mutual fund schemes on the BSE StAR MF Platform. It restores normal transaction capabilities for existing and prospective investors in these schemes, with no implications for listed equity trading or other market segments.
