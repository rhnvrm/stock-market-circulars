## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 3,67,60,483 new equity shares of Stallion India Fluorochemicals Limited (Scrip Code: 544342) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Friday, March 6, 2026. These shares were issued on a Rights basis at a face value of Rs. 10/- each at a premium of Rs. 89/- per share.

## Key Points

- 3,67,60,483 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 89/- (total issue price Rs. 99/- per share)
- ISIN: INE0RYC01010
- Distribution Numbers: 79325255 to 116085737
- Date of Allotment: February 2, 2026
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Trading effective from March 6, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification pursuant to a completed rights issue.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed to update their records and systems to reflect the new securities available for trading from March 6, 2026 under Scrip Code 544342.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: February 2, 2026
- Trading Commencement Date: March 6, 2026 (Friday)

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds approximately 3.68 crore new equity shares to the tradable float of Stallion India Fluorochemicals Limited. Since these shares are pari-passu with existing shares, no differentiation in rights or entitlements exists. The rights issue at Rs. 99/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- + premium Rs. 89/-) may influence the stock's market price upon commencement of trading. Impact is limited to existing and prospective shareholders of this specific company.
