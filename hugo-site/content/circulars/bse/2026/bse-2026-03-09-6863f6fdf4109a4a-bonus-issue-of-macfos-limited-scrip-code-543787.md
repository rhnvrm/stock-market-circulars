## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that MACFOS LIMITED (Scrip Code: 543787) has fixed March 10, 2026 as the record date for a bonus issue. The company will issue 1 bonus equity share of Rs. 10/- each for every 10 existing equity shares of Rs. 10/- each held. Shares will trade on an ex-bonus basis effective March 10, 2026 under settlement DR-836/2025-2026.

## Key Points

- Bonus ratio: 1 bonus equity share for every 10 existing equity shares held
- Face value of bonus shares: Rs. 10/- each
- Record date: March 10, 2026
- Ex-bonus effective date: March 10, 2026 (Settlement No. DR-836/2025-2026)
- Total bonus shares to be allotted: 941,682 equity shares
- Allotment date: March 11, 2026
- Market lot revised from 150 to 165 effective March 10, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification under BSE's listing compliance framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the ex-bonus effective date of March 10, 2026 and ensure all transactions in MACFOS LIMITED shares are executed accordingly.
- Trading members must update the revised market lot of 165 (from 150) for Scrip Code 543787 effective March 10, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **March 10, 2026**: Record date and ex-bonus effective date (Settlement DR-836/2025-2026)
- **March 11, 2026**: Allotment of 941,682 bonus equity shares
- **March 10, 2026**: Revised market lot of 165 becomes effective

## Impact Assessment

This bonus issue increases the total share count of MACFOS LIMITED by approximately 10%, which will proportionally reduce the share price on the ex-bonus date while keeping total shareholder value constant. The market lot change from 150 to 165 reflects the post-bonus adjusted lot size and trading members must update their systems accordingly. Impact is limited to existing shareholders and trading members dealing in this scrip.
