## Summary

BSE has informed Trading Members that it will launch additional contracts in the Commodity Derivatives Segment, in continuation of earlier exchange notices dated 10 Jul 2025 and 25 Sep 2025. The new contracts cover Gold Futures, Gold Options on Futures, Gold Mini Futures, Silver Futures, Silver Options on Futures (30KG), Silver Mini Futures and Silver Micro (KG) Futures, with launch dates spanning from September 2026 through mid-2027 as per a detailed contract launch calendar.

## Key Points

- Seven new commodity derivative products are being introduced: Gold Futures, Gold Options on Futures, Gold Mini Futures, Silver Futures, Silver Options on Futures (30KG), Silver Mini Futures, and Silver Micro (KG) Futures.
- Detailed contract specifications are provided as separate attachments for each product (Gold Futures, Gold Mini Futures, Gold Options on Futures, Silver Futures, Silver KG Futures, Silver Mini Futures, Silver Options on Futures).
- Gold Futures contracts will launch monthly from September 2026 through August 2027, each with an approximately four-month tenor to expiry.
- Gold Options on Futures contracts follow a similar monthly launch schedule, with underlying futures expiry and options expiry dates specified for each contract month.
- Gold Mini Futures Contracts begin launching from October 2026 onwards.
- This notice continues from and builds upon exchange notices no. 20250710-35 (10 Jul 2025) and 20250925-57 (25 Sep 2025).

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is an operational/product notice under the Trading Operations department introducing new tradable commodity derivative contracts as part of BSE's ongoing contract launch calendar.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members should review the detailed contract specifications for each product (attached separately) to understand lot sizes, tick sizes, margin requirements, and trading parameters before these contracts become available for trading, and update their systems/processes accordingly.

## Important Dates

- Gold Futures: monthly launches from September 07, 2026 (expiry January 05, 2027) through August 06, 2027 (expiry December 03, 2027).
- Gold Options on Futures: monthly launches from September 07, 2026 through August 06, 2027, with underlying futures expiry and options expiry dates aligned to the corresponding futures contract months.
- Gold Mini Futures Contract: launches beginning October 06, 2026 (expiry January 05, 2027), with subsequent monthly contracts.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine product expansion in BSE's Commodity Derivatives Segment, providing Trading Members and market participants additional avenues for trading and hedging gold and silver price risk. The impact is limited to commodity derivatives trading members and does not directly affect listed equity securities or corporate actions.
