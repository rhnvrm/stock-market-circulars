## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Vedanta Iron and Steel Limited (BSE Scrip Code: 544784) will be reclassified to the 'B' group for trading purposes, effective Tuesday, June 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- Vedanta Iron and Steel Limited (Scrip Code: 544784) is being moved to the 'B' group on BSE.
- The change takes effect from Tuesday, June 30, 2026.
- All market participants are informed to take note of this group reclassification.
- The circular was issued by the Listing Operations department.

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares of Vedanta Iron and Steel Limited will be traded under the 'B' group classification on BSE from June 30, 2026 onwards, replacing the previous group assignment.

## Compliance Requirements

Market participants, brokers, and investors trading in Vedanta Iron and Steel Limited (Scrip Code: 544784) should update their records and systems to reflect the new 'B' group classification effective from the stated date.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 15, 2026
- **Effective Date:** Tuesday, June 30, 2026 (trading under 'B' group begins)

## Impact Assessment

The reclassification to 'B' group may affect applicable circuit filters, margin requirements, and trading conditions for Vedanta Iron and Steel Limited shares. Investors and traders holding or intending to trade in this scrip should be aware of the change in group classification, as 'B' group stocks are subject to specific BSE trading norms distinct from other groups.
