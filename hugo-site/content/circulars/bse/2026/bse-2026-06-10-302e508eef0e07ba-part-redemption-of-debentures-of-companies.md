## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260610-39 informs trading members that 10 companies have fixed record dates for payment of interest and/or part redemption of their Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs). Trading in these debentures will take place at reduced face values from the respective ex-dates onwards.

## Key Points

- Affects 10 NCD issuers across debt segment with record dates of 13 June 2026 and 15 June 2026
- Trading in the listed debentures will reflect reduced face values from the ex-date
- Settlement references DR 650/2026-2027 and DR 651/2026-2027
- Includes infrastructure trusts (IndiGrid, Vertis), power utilities (UPPCL), Adani group entities, and Satin Housing Finance

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice under the Listing Operations – Debt segment for scheduled part redemptions of existing NCD issuances.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the reduced face values for the affected debentures effective from the respective ex-dates
- Settlement of trades in these debentures post ex-date must reflect the revised (reduced) face values

## Impact Assessment

This notice primarily impacts institutional investors and NCD holders in the debt segment. The part redemptions reduce the outstanding face value of each debenture, and trading systems must be updated to reflect the new face values from the ex-dates. Broader equity market impact is negligible. The presence of Adani group entities (Adani Green Energy UP, Prayatna Developers, Parampujya Solar) and IndiGrid Infrastructure Trust suggests routine amortisation of project finance debt.
