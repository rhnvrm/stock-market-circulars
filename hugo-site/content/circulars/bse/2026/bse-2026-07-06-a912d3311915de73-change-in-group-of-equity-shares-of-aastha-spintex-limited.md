## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that, in continuation of its earlier Notice No. 20260703-17 dated July 03, 2026, the equity shares of AASTHA SPINTEX LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544808) will be shifted from the Trade for Trade segment (T Group) to the Rolling segment, effective Monday, July 20, 2026. Consequently, dealings in the company's shares will move under the B Group.

## Key Points

- AASTHA SPINTEX LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544808) shares will transition from T Group (Trade for Trade) to Rolling segment.
- Post-transition, the shares will be classified under B Group.
- This follows and continues the directive from Notice No. 20260703-17 dated July 03, 2026.
- Trading members can contact Mr. Parag Jain (Tel: 022 2272 8685) for further details.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced; this is an operational reclassification of the trading segment/group for a specific scrip under existing BSE surveillance and settlement group norms.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must update their systems and processes to reflect the change in settlement group (from T to B) for AASTHA SPINTEX LIMITED effective the specified date, and ensure compliance with Rolling segment trading and settlement norms going forward.

## Important Dates

- July 03, 2026: Original Notice No. 20260703-17 issued.
- July 06, 2026: Current confirmatory notice issued.
- July 20, 2026 (Monday): Effective date of transition from T Group to Rolling segment/B Group.

## Impact Assessment

The shift from Trade for Trade (T Group) to Rolling segment (B Group) generally eases trading restrictions, as it removes the trade-for-trade delivery-based settlement requirement and allows normal rolling settlement (including intraday trading) for the stock. This is typically a positive development for liquidity and trading flexibility in the scrip, though the impact is limited to this individual company and its shareholders/traders.
