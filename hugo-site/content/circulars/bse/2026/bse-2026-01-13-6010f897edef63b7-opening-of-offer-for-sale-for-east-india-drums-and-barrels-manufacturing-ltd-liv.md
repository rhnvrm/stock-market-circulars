## Summary

BSE announces the schedule of activities for Offer for Sale (OFS) of shares of East India Drums and Barrels Manufacturing Ltd. The OFS involves 7,38,711 shares with a floor price of Rs 125 per share. Bidding for Institutional/Non-Institutional investors opens on January 14, 2026, while Retail investors can bid on January 16, 2026. The allocation follows price priority method with 10% reservation for retail investors.

## Key Points

- Total Offer Size: 7,38,711 shares of East India Drums and Barrels Manufacturing Ltd (Security Code: 523874, ISIN: INE191C01023)
- Floor Price: Rs 125.00 per share with tick size of Rs 0.05
- Face Value: Rs 10 per share, Market Lot: 1 share
- Retail Reservation: 73,871 shares (10% of issue size)
- Non-Retail Offer Size: 6,64,840 shares
- Allocation Methodology: Price Priority Method
- Bid Day for IC/MF/OTH/NII: January 14, 2026 (T Day)
- Bid Day for RI/RIC/EMP: January 16, 2026 (T+1 Day)
- Multiple bidding allowed across categories

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard OFS process notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members and Custodian Members must note the OFS schedule and timings
- Institutional Investors (MF/IC/OTHS) can bid on T Day (January 14) from 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM
- Non-Institutional Investors (NII) including retail investors with bid value above Rs 2 lakhs can bid on T Day
- Retail Investors (bid value up to Rs 2 lakhs) and Employees must bid on T+1 Day (January 16)
- 100% upfront margin required for all categories except 0% margin option for institutional investors
- Retail investors bidding in NII category not eligible for seller discount (if any)
- Access OFS module via iBBS platform (Internet: https://ibbs.bseindia.com/ or Leased Line: https://ibbsll.bseindia.com/)

## Important Dates

- **January 14, 2026 (T Day)**: Bid entry, modification and cancellation period for IC/MF/OTH/NII from 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM
- **January 16, 2026 (T+1 Day)**: 
  - Give up/Take up for institutional 0% margin bids until 7:30 AM
  - Bid entry, modification and cancellation for RI/RIC/EMP from 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM
  - Unallocated bidders from T Day can carry forward bids
- **January 19, 2026 (T+2 Day)**: Give up/Take up period until 7:30 AM
- Settlement: T+1 for IC/MF/OTHS/NII; T+2 for RI/RIC/EMP

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The OFS of 7,38,711 shares represents a significant secondary market offering that may impact the stock's trading dynamics and liquidity. The floor price of Rs 125 sets a benchmark for valuation.

**Investor Categories Affected**:
- Mutual Funds (MF), Insurance Companies (IC), and Other Institutional Investors can participate on T Day with optional 0% margin facility
- Non-Institutional Investors including HUF, Trusts, Body Corporates, PMS participants
- Retail Investors (individuals with bids up to Rs 2 lakhs) get 10% reservation and can bid at cut-off price on T+1 Day
- Employees can bid up to Rs 5 lakhs on T+1 Day

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must ensure system readiness for two-day bidding process with different investor categories on different days. The price priority allocation method ensures fair distribution based on bid prices rather than time priority.
