## Summary

BSE Limited, through Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd. (ICCL), has issued a notice inviting offers from trading members for the purchase of securities in Demat Auction 636, pertaining to Rolling Settlement No. DR-636/2026-27. The auction session is scheduled on Monday, May 25, 2026, with securities pay-in due on May 26, 2026.

## Key Points

- Auction offer entry window: 2:00 PM to 2:45 PM on May 25, 2026
- Auction matching: 2:45 PM to 3:00 PM on May 25, 2026
- Delivery statement and accepted offer reports: 3:00 PM to 3:30 PM on May 25, 2026
- Money statement and final reports: 3:35 PM to 5:30 PM on May 25, 2026
- Auction pay-in: May 26, 2026 at 8:00 AM
- Settlement will be processed through the Clearing Member of each Trading Member under the Uniform Membership structure
- Restriction on trading members offering securities in auctions for settlements where they had short deliveries has been removed

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The circular reiterates the existing provision that under the Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment, settlement is done through the Clearing Member of a Trading Member. It also notes that the earlier restriction on trading members offering securities in auctions for settlements where they had short delivered has been removed.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must check their auction pay-in obligations for securities.
- Securities pay-in must be executed in depositories within depository cut-off timings on May 26, 2026.
- Members and Custodians must verify their auction pay-in obligations.
- Trading members must exercise utmost care when entering securities codes and rates; no corrections will be permitted for punching errors.
- Trading members should refer to the cutoff rate on the Auction Offer Entry Screen before submitting offers.
- Members should also refer to notices on Corporate Actions (ex-rights, ex-bonus, ex-split, etc.) from the relevant department.

## Important Dates

- **May 25, 2026**: Auction session (offer entry, matching, and reports)
- **May 26, 2026 at 8:00 AM**: Auction pay-in

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational circular with limited market-wide impact. It affects only those trading members with short delivery obligations in Rolling Settlement DR-636/2026-27. The removal of the restriction on offering securities in auctions for settlements where members had short deliveries provides slightly greater flexibility to trading members but does not represent a significant operational change.
