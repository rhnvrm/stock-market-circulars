## Summary

BSE has announced the live activities schedule for the Offer to Buy (OTB) – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for SARDA PROTEINS LTD (Symbol: SRDAPRT, Scrip Code: 519242, ISIN: INE995U01011). The offer is open from Thursday, 11th June 2026 to Wednesday, 24th June 2026, at an offer price of Rs. 115.00 per share for up to 17,30,400 equity shares.

## Key Points

- **Security:** SARDA PROTEINS LTD | Symbol: SRDAPRT | Scrip Code: 519242 | ISIN: INE995U01011
- **Offer Size:** Up to 17,30,400 Equity Shares at Rs. 115.00 per share
- **Face Value:** Rs. 10.00 | Market Lot: 1
- **Bid Offering Period:** 11th June 2026 to 24th June 2026 (9:15 a.m. – 3:30 p.m.)
- **Give-up / Take-up:** 11th June 2026 to 23rd June 2026 (9:15 a.m. – 3:30 p.m.)
- **Give-up (last day):** 24th June 2026 (9:15 a.m. – 3:30 p.m.)
- **Take-up (last day):** 24th June 2026 (9:15 a.m. – 4:00 p.m.)
- OTB platform accessible via iBBS and RTRMS on internet and leased line

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This notice operationalizes an existing takeover/acquisition window mechanism under BSE's OTB framework, referencing earlier operational circulars (20150930-1 and 20170203-29).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must ensure the Investor's UCC is registered and updated with the correct PAN Number and Category in the Exchange UCC database.
- Members can bid only for investors holding shares in demat form.
- If no securities are accepted, no End of Issue files will be generated on Extranet.
- Members requiring new login IDs or password resets should contact Trading Operations at 022-22728005 or user.trdops@bseindia.com.

## Impact Assessment

This notice is relevant to trading members and custodians who have clients holding SARDA PROTEINS LTD shares (SRDAPRT) and wish to participate in the takeover open offer. The offer window is 10 trading days. Members must verify UCC and demat status prior to bidding. The offer involves up to 17,30,400 shares at Rs. 115 per share, implying a total deal size of approximately Rs. 19.9 crore. Impact is limited to participants in this specific scrip.
