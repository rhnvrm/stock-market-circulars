## Summary

BSE has listed additional securities issued by 9 companies under their Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP/ESOS). These shares are admitted for trading on the Exchange effective Friday, June 12, 2026. None of the newly listed shares carry a lock-in restriction.

## Key Points

- 9 companies have had new ESOP/ESOS shares listed effective June 12, 2026
- No lock-in period applies to any of the newly listed shares
- Total new shares range from 18,221 (Godrej Properties) to 285,407 (RBL Bank)
- All shares are listed on the Equity segment

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice for listing of further securities issued under existing employee stock option schemes.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to take note of the new ISIN ranges and scrip codes for the newly listed securities to ensure accurate order routing and settlement.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date for Trading:** Friday, June 12, 2026
- **Notice Date:** June 11, 2026
