## Summary

InfoBeans Technologies Limited (BSE Scrip Code: 543644) has communicated the details of its bonus equity share allotment to BSE's Listing Department. A total of 7,27,19,580 bonus equity shares will be allotted with a deemed date of allotment of 2nd March 2026, and the shares are scheduled to be listed effective 4th March 2026.

## Key Points

- **Company:** InfoBeans Technologies Limited
- **Scrip Code:** 543644
- **ISIN:** INE344S01016
- **Bonus Shares to be Allotted:** 7,27,19,580 equity shares
- **Distinctive Number Range:** 24455381 to 97174960
- **Deemed Date of Allotment:** 2nd March 2026
- **Effective Listing Date:** 4th March 2026
- Communication submitted by Surbhi Jain, Company Secretary and Compliance Officer

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this circular. It is a standard corporate action communication providing allotment-specific details as required under BSE listing obligations.

## Compliance Requirements

- BSE Listing Department is requested to take the allotment details on record.
- The company has fulfilled its disclosure obligation by furnishing distinctive numbers, ISIN, deemed allotment date, and effective listing date.

## Impact Assessment

The bonus issue will increase the total share count of InfoBeans Technologies Limited by 7,27,19,580 shares. Existing shareholders will receive bonus shares proportionally, diluting the per-share price while keeping overall market capitalisation unchanged at the time of listing. The listing of bonus shares on 4th March 2026 may cause short-term price adjustments. This is a routine corporate action with medium impact confined to existing shareholders and traders of INFOBEAN.
