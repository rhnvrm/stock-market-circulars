## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (Notice No. 20260310-4) informing trading members that Government Security 839RAJSDL26 (Scrip Code: 810957, ISIN: IN2920150355) is maturing on its redemption date. Trading members are advised to cease dealing in this security effective March 11, 2026.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code 810957 with ISIN IN2920150355 (839RAJSDL26) is maturing and trading is being suspended
- Trading suspension is effective from March 11, 2026
- This is a Rajasthan State Development Loan (SDL) maturing as per its scheduled redemption date
- Notice reference: DR-837/2025-2026
- Issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President – Listing Compliance & Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice pursuant to the maturity/redemption of a Government Security listed on BSE's Debt Market Segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in Scrip Code 810957 (IN2920150355 / 839RAJSDL26) from March 11, 2026 onwards
- Members should update their systems and alert their clients accordingly to avoid erroneous trades in this security

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** March 10, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date:** March 11, 2026 (Redemption Date)

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and traders of the specific Rajasthan SDL (839RAJSDL26). This is a routine end-of-life action for a maturing government security. Investors holding this security will receive redemption proceeds as per the terms of the instrument. No broader market disruption is expected.
