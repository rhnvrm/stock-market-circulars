## Summary

BSE has informed members that the Mirae Asset Nifty Metal ETF Fund of Fund (FOF), following its NFO period, has been reopened for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective February 27, 2026. SIP, STP, and SWP facilities are also available. This notice references the earlier NFO notice dated February 9, 2026 (Notice No. 20260209-11).

## Key Points

- Mirae Asset Nifty Metal ETF FOF is now available for ongoing subscription and redemption on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Effective date: February 27, 2026
- SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities are available
- Six scheme variants are available across Regular and Direct plans
- References prior NFO notice: Notice No. 20260209-11 dated February 9, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notice regarding the availability of a mutual fund scheme for ongoing transactions post-NFO closure.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members. BSE members and registered Mutual Fund Distributors (MFDs) on the BSE StAR MF Platform may now place ongoing subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP orders for the listed schemes.

## Important Dates

- **February 27, 2026**: Effective date for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform
- **February 9, 2026**: Original NFO notice date (Notice No. 20260209-11)

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This is a routine post-NFO availability notification for a sectoral ETF FOF focused on the Nifty Metal index. The six scheme variants (Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options) provide investors with standard flexibility. Availability on BSE StAR MF Platform enables distributors and investors to transact through the exchange's mutual fund infrastructure. No broad market or compliance implications.
