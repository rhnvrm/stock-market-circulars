## Summary

BSE Index Services Private Ltd (formerly Asia Index Pvt. Ltd.) has announced that Allcargo Global Limited (Scrip code: 544602), a spun-off scrip resulting from a scheme of arrangement for demerger, will be excluded from several BSE indices. The exclusion follows the previously established process (per Notice No. 20250403-46) for removing spun-off scrips from indices when the scrip does not hit the lower circuit on two consecutive trading sessions post-listing. Allcargo Global Limited did not hit the lower circuit on Friday, July 17, 2026 and Monday, July 20, 2026, triggering its removal.

## Key Points

- Allcargo Global Limited (Scrip code: 544602) will be dropped from multiple BSE indices effective prior to market open on Wednesday, July 22, 2026.
- Indices affected include: BSE SMALLCAP, BSE ALLCAP, BSE SERVICES, BSE 1000, BSE NEXT 500, BSE 250 MICROCAP, BSE 1000 MULTICAP EQUAL SIZE WEIGHTED (25%), BSE SMALLCAP 500, BSE SMALLCAP 500 ENHANCED VALUE 50, and BSE MIDSMALLCAP.
- The removal is based on a standardized post-demerger index exclusion process established via Notice No. 20250403-46.
- Trigger condition: the spun-off scrip did not hit the lower circuit on two consecutive trading sessions (July 17 and July 20, 2026).
- This action will be reflected in the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE) for the night of July 20, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory change; this is the application of an existing, previously announced BSE Index Services process for handling spun-off scrips following a demerger scheme of arrangement.

## Compliance Requirements

Index fund managers, ETF providers, and other passive investment vehicles tracking the affected BSE indices must rebalance their portfolios to remove Allcargo Global Limited by the effective date. Market participants with queries can contact bseindex@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- July 17, 2026: First session where Allcargo Global Limited did not hit the lower circuit.
- July 20, 2026: Second consecutive session confirming non-lower-circuit condition; notice issued.
- July 22, 2026: Effective date of exclusion from the listed BSE indices (prior to market open).

## Impact Assessment

The exclusion will trigger index-related selling pressure from passive funds and ETFs tracking the affected indices as they rebalance ahead of July 22, 2026. While mechanical and expected given the prior demerger process, the removal from ten distinct indices, including broad benchmarks like BSE ALLCAP and BSE 1000, may result in a noticeable, if temporary, supply-demand imbalance for the stock around the effective date.
