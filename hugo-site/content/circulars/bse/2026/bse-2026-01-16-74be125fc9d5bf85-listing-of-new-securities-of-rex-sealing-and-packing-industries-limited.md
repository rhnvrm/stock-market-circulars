## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 56,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each of Rex Sealing and Packing Industries Limited (Scrip Code: 543744) issued at a premium of Rs. 200/- to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion. These shares will be permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Monday, January 19, 2026.

## Key Points

- 56,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at premium of Rs. 200/- per share
- Issue price: Rs. 210/- per share
- Allotted to non-promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Distinctive Numbers: 2220001 to 2276000
- Date of allotment: September 26, 2025
- ISIN: INE0MFT01014
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Scrip Code: 543744

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must note the listing of these new securities for trading operations. The shares are subject to lock-in requirements as specified.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: September 26, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: January 19, 2026 (Monday)
- **Lock-in Expiry**: July 31, 2026 (all 56,000 shares with distinctive numbers 2220001 to 2276000)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notice with minimal market impact. The preferential allotment of 56,000 shares represents a relatively small issuance. The shares are subject to lock-in until July 31, 2026, limiting immediate liquidity impact. Trading members should update their systems to reflect the expanded share capital and lock-in restrictions for the specified distinctive numbers.
