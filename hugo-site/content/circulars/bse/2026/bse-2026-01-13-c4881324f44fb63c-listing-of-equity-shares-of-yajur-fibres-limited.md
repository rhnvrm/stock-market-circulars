## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that equity shares of Yajur Fibres Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the SME segment. The company has been assigned scrip code 544676, symbol YAJUR, and ISIN number INE0CNJ01010. The exact listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Yajur Fibres Limited
- Scrip Code: 544676
- Trading Symbol: YAJUR
- ISIN: INE0CNJ01010
- Segment: SME
- Listing date to be announced separately

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the scrip details and await the separate notice containing the listing date and complete security details before commencing trading activities.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: January 13, 2026
- Listing Date: To be notified through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine SME listing announcement with minimal immediate market impact. Trading members need to update their systems with the new scrip details. Investors interested in SME stocks should await the detailed listing notice for complete information.
