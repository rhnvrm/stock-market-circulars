## Summary

BSE has revised the price bands for 15 equity scrips effective March 05, 2026, as part of its surveillance function. The revised bands range from 2% to 20% and will govern the permissible trading range for these securities going forward.

## Key Points

- Price bands revised for 15 scrips effective March 05, 2026
- Bands revised to levels between 2% and 20%
- Action taken by BSE Surveillance department
- Notice No: 20260304-46
- Members may contact bse.surv@bseindia.com for clarifications

## Regulatory Changes

The following price band revisions have been made:

| Scrip Code | Scrip Name | Revised Price Band |
|------------|------------|--------------------|
| 531681 | Amerise Biosciences Ltd | 10% |
| 539946 | Bazel International Ltd | 5% |
| 542668 | Evans Electric Ltd | 10% |
| 533638 | Flexituff Ventures International Ltd | 5% |
| 540938 | Gujarat Hy-Spin Ltd | 10% |
| 523467 | Jai Mata Glass Ltd | 5% |
| 533482 | Kridhan Infra Ltd | 10% |
| 543460 | Quality RO Industries Ltd | 5% |
| 544456 | R M Drip and Sprinklers Systems Ltd | 5% |
| 751091 | Sadhana Nitrochem Ltd | 20% |
| 531794 | Seshachal Technologies Ltd | 2% |
| 526883 | Shikhar Consultants Ltd | 2% |
| 540914 | SRU Steels Ltd | 10% |
| 509953 | Trade Wings Ltd | 2% |
| 511726 | Vipul Ltd | 2% |

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must adhere to the revised price bands for the listed scrips from March 05, 2026 onwards
- Orders placed outside the revised price band limits will be rejected by the exchange system
- Members requiring clarification should contact BSE Surveillance at bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **March 04, 2026**: Circular issued
- **March 05, 2026**: Revised price bands become effective

## Impact Assessment

Traders and investors holding or trading in the 15 affected scrips will face revised daily price movement limits from March 05, 2026. Scrips with tighter bands (2%) such as Seshachal Technologies, Shikhar Consultants, Trade Wings, and Vipul Ltd will see more restricted intraday movement, likely reflecting heightened surveillance concern. Sadhana Nitrochem receives a wider 20% band, allowing greater price discovery. These changes are standard surveillance measures to manage volatility and protect market integrity.
