## Summary

BSE has notified that Choice Equity Broking Private Limited (Member No. 3299) will voluntarily cease business operations in the Currency derivative segment effective January 9, 2026. This is an administrative notification to inform all trading members of the exchange about the member's voluntary withdrawal from the currency derivatives segment.

## Key Points

- Choice Equity Broking Private Limited (Member No. 3299) is ceasing operations
- Cessation applies to Currency derivative segment only
- Effective date: Friday, January 9, 2026
- Reason: Voluntary closure of business
- Contact: membership.ops@bseindia.com for clarifications

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes applicable. This is a business closure notification.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for other trading members. This is an informational notice regarding a single member's voluntary cessation of business.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: January 9, 2026 - Choice Equity Broking Private Limited ceases operations in Currency derivative segment

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a voluntary closure of a single trading member in the currency derivatives segment. Other trading members are informed for awareness purposes only. No action required from other market participants.
