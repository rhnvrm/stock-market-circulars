## Summary

BSE has issued its periodic update on the Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LT-ASM) framework, referencing prior notices dated March 21, 2018, October 27, 2018, December 04, 2020, August 09, 2024, September 20, 2024, and June 04, 2021. The notice specifies securities newly placed under LT-ASM, securities moving to higher stages within the framework, securities exiting the framework, and securities being transferred to trade groups (T/XT/MT/TS) while remaining under LT-ASM. Detailed lists are provided in Annexures I (Parts A, B, D), II, and III attached to the circular.

## Key Points

- 100% margin shall apply from July 29, 2026 on all open positions as of July 28, 2026 and on new positions created from July 29, 2026 onwards, for securities in Annexure I (Part A).
- Securities moving to higher LT-ASM stages take effect July 27, 2026, per Annexure I (Part B).
- Lower price band applicable from July 27, 2026 for securities under the revised framework.
- Securities under Annexure I (Part D) shall continue in LT-ASM but be transferred to T/XT/MT/TS trade groups w.e.f. July 29, 2026.
- Securities exiting the LT-ASM framework w.e.f. July 27, 2026 are listed in Annexure II; their price bands revert to pre-shortlisting levels unless subject to another surveillance measure.
- A consolidated list of all securities currently under the LT-ASM framework is provided in Annexure III.
- Shortlisting is based on XBRL submissions by listed companies and is purely a surveillance measure, not an adverse action against any company.
- LT-ASM measures apply in conjunction with other prevailing surveillance frameworks.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced; this is a periodic application/review update under the existing LT-ASM framework established via prior BSE notices (2018, 2020, 2021, 2024).

## Compliance Requirements

Market participants and trading members must apply 100% margin requirements to affected securities as scheduled, observe revised price bands, and account for trade group transfers for impacted scrips. Queries can be directed to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- July 27, 2026: Higher LT-ASM stage moves and lower price band become effective; securities exit LT-ASM framework (Annexure II).
- July 28, 2026: Reference date for open positions subject to 100% margin.
- July 29, 2026: 100% margin applies to open/new positions; securities transferred to T/XT/MT/TS groups while remaining in LT-ASM.

## Impact Assessment

The update affects trading conditions—margin requirements, price bands, and trade group classification—for specific securities identified in the annexures, increasing trading costs and reducing leverage for affected stocks. Securities exiting the framework regain normal price bands, improving trading flexibility. Overall market impact is limited to the specific securities named in the annexures rather than the broader market.
