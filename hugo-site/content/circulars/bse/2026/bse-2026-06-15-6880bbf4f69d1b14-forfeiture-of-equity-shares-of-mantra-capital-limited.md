## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Mantra Capital Limited (Scrip Code: 511577) has forfeited 45,442 equity shares that were issued under a rights issue at a face value of Rs. 10/- each. These shares, bearing distinctive numbers 32054559 to 32100000, were partly paid-up and were forfeited due to non-payment of call money by the shareholders concerned.

## Key Points

- **Company:** Mantra Capital Limited (Scrip Code: 511577)
- **Shares Forfeited:** 45,442 equity shares
- **Face Value:** Rs. 10/- per share
- **Distinctive Nos.:** 32054559 to 32100000
- **Reason:** Non-payment of call money on partly paid-up shares issued under a rights issue
- **Notice No.:** 20260615-14

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a company-level corporate action communicated to the market via BSE's Listing Operations department.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the forfeiture. The forfeited shares will no longer be tradeable or outstanding. Members should update their records accordingly and ensure clients holding these shares (if any) are notified of the forfeiture status.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 15 June 2026

## Impact Assessment

The forfeiture of 45,442 partly paid-up shares has limited market impact given the small quantum relative to the company's total share capital. The action reduces the outstanding partly paid-up shares of Mantra Capital Limited. Shareholders who failed to pay the call money have lost both their shares and any amounts previously paid. Broader market or trading activity in the scrip is unlikely to be materially affected.
