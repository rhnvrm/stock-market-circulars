## Summary

SEDEMAC Mechatronics Limited (formerly SEDEMAC Mechatronics Private Limited), a Pune-based mechatronics company, finalised the allocation of **2,410,401 equity shares** to anchor investors on March 2, 2026, ahead of its Initial Public Offering (IPO). The Anchor Investor Allocation Price was set at **Rs. 1,352 per equity share** (face value Rs. 10, share premium Rs. 1,342). The allocation was approved by the IPO Committee in consultation with Book Running Lead Managers: ICICI Securities Limited, Avendus Capital Private Limited, and Axis Capital Limited.

## Key Points

- **Total anchor allocation:** 2,410,401 equity shares at Rs. 1,352 per share
- **Total anchor portion value:** Approximately Rs. 3,258.46 crore
- **Face value:** Rs. 10 per equity share; **Share premium:** Rs. 1,342 per share
- **Book Running Lead Managers:** ICICI Securities Limited, Avendus Capital Private Limited, Axis Capital Limited
- Allocation includes both domestic mutual funds and a foreign institutional investor (ADIA)
- Company registered office: Baner Road, Baner, Pune – 411045, Maharashtra

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This circular is a standard SEBI-mandated disclosure of anchor investor allocation prior to an IPO opening.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company has notified both BSE Limited (Dept. of Listing Operations) and NSE (MSD – Non-Continuous Markets Group) as required under SEBI ICDR Regulations.
- Anchor investors are subject to a lock-in period as prescribed under applicable SEBI regulations.

## Important Dates

- **Anchor Investor Allocation Date:** March 2, 2026
- IPO subscription dates to follow the anchor allocation day (specific open/close dates not stated in this circular)

## Impact Assessment

The strong anchor book with participation from marquee domestic funds (HDFC MF, SBI MF, ICICI Prudential, Nippon, Aditya Birla) and a global sovereign wealth fund (Abu Dhabi Investment Authority) signals robust institutional interest in SEDEMAC Mechatronics' IPO. The company operates in the automotive/mechatronics space and has manufacturing facilities in Pune's Chakan industrial area. The high anchor allocation price of Rs. 1,352 per share implies a significant premium valuation. Market impact is limited to the IPO segment; existing market participants are unaffected.
