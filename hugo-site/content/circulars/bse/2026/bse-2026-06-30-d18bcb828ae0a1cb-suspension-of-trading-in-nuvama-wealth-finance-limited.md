## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited has fixed 02 July 2026 as the record date for early redemption on exercise of a call option on its Non-Convertible Debentures (NCD). Trading in these debentures is suspended effective 02 July 2026.

## Key Points

- Trading members are advised **not to deal** in the specified Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited debentures from 02 July 2026.
- The suspension is triggered by the company fixing a record date for early redemption under a call option provision.
- The affected NCD is listed under BSE's Debt segment.
- Debenture series: NWFL-9.75%-17-1-28-PVT (Security Code: 976341, ISIN: INE918K07PU7).

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework introduced. This is a standard corporate action-driven trading suspension under existing BSE listing and operations guidelines for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in the NCD (ISIN: INE918K07PU7, Security Code: 976341) with effect from 02 July 2026.
- Members should inform their clients about the suspension and ensure no transactions are executed on or after the specified date.

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Record Date (Early Redemption / Call Option) | 02 July 2026 |
| No Dealings From | 02 July 2026 |
| Circular Date | 30 June 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

The suspension is limited to a single NCD series of Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited on the BSE Debt segment. Impact is confined to holders and active traders of this specific instrument. Post-redemption, the debenture will cease to exist upon completion of the early redemption process, removing it from the exchange's tradeable debt instruments. Investors holding this NCD should expect redemption proceeds as per the original terms of the call option.
