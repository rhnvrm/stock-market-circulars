## Summary

BSE has issued a circular regarding the change in name of SEQUENT SCIENTIFIC LIMITED. This is an administrative update to reflect the company's new name in exchange records and systems.

## Key Points

- Company name change for SEQUENT SCIENTIFIC LIMITED
- Administrative update for exchange records
- No impact on existing shareholdings or trading operations
- Stock symbol and ISIN may be updated accordingly

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a notification of a company name change as per corporate action.

## Compliance Requirements

- Exchange systems will be updated to reflect the new company name
- Trading members and market participants should note the name change for their records
- All references to the company should use the new name going forward

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: January 19, 2026
- Effective Date: To be specified in the detailed circular

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact administrative change. The company name change does not affect:
- Existing shareholdings
- Trading operations
- Market capitalization
- Investor rights or obligations

Market participants should update their internal systems and records to reflect the new company name.
