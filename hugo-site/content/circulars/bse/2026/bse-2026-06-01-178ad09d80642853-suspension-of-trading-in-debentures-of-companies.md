## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260601-43 directs trading members to refrain from dealing in the Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of Vistaar Financial Services Pvt Ltd and Avanse Financial Services Limited. Both companies have fixed record dates for the purpose of payment of interest or redemption of their respective privately placed NCDs.

## Key Points

- Trading members are advised NOT to deal in the specified debentures of both companies.
- Two privately placed NCD series are covered under this notice.
- Vistaar Financial Services Pvt Ltd (Scrip Code: 975745): ISIN INE016P07229, Record Date and Ex Date both on 03 Jun 2026, Settlement DR 643/2026-2027.
- Avanse Financial Services Limited (Scrip Code: 976082): ISIN INE087P07410, Record Date 07 Jun 2026, Ex Date 05 Jun 2026, Settlement DR 645/2026-2027.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is a standard operational notice issued by BSE Listing Operations under existing framework for NCD interest payment and redemption procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute trades in the debentures of Vistaar Financial Services Pvt Ltd (ISIN: INE016P07229) and Avanse Financial Services Limited (ISIN: INE087P07410) on or around their respective ex-dates.
- Members should note the settlement references (DR 643/2026-2027 and DR 645/2026-2027) for operational compliance.

## Important Dates

| Company | ISIN | Ex Date | Record Date | Settlement |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Vistaar Financial Services Pvt Ltd | INE016P07229 | 03 Jun 2026 | 03 Jun 2026 | DR 643/2026-2027 |
| Avanse Financial Services Limited | INE087P07410 | 05 Jun 2026 | 07 Jun 2026 | DR 645/2026-2027 |

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited to holders and prospective traders of these two specific privately placed NCD series. The suspension is temporary and tied to the record date mechanism for interest/redemption processing. There is no systemic or broad market impact. NCD holders will receive interest or redemption proceeds as applicable per the terms of each instrument.
