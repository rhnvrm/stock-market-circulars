## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Avasara Finance Ltd (Scrip Code: 511730) is changing its corporate name to **BYLD Capital Finance Limited**. The Scrip ID and Abbreviated Name on the BOLT Plus System will be updated to reflect the new name effective **June 15, 2026**.

## Key Points

- Existing company name: Avasara Finance Ltd
- New company name: BYLD Capital Finance Limited
- Scrip Code remains unchanged: **511730**
- New Scrip ID on BOLT Plus System: **BYLD**
- New Abbreviated Name on BOLT Plus System: **BYLD**
- Change effective date: June 15, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is an administrative update to the company's identifier on BSE's BOLT Plus trading system following the company's legal name change.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are advised to update their records and systems to reflect the new company name (BYLD Capital Finance Limited) and the updated Scrip ID/Abbreviated Name (BYLD) on BOLT Plus effective June 15, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **June 9, 2026**: Notice issued by BSE
- **June 15, 2026**: Effective date for name and identifier change on BOLT Plus System

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine name-change notification affecting only how the stock is identified on the BSE BOLT Plus trading system. The scrip code (511730) remains unchanged, so existing holdings, orders, and positions are unaffected. Trading members should ensure back-office and front-end systems are updated before June 15, 2026 to avoid confusion.
