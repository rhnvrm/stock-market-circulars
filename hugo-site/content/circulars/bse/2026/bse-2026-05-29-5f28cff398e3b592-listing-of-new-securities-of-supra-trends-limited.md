## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 1,01,20,000 new equity shares of Supra Trends Limited (Scrip Code: 511539, ISIN: INE533B01028) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Monday, June 1, 2026. The shares were issued at par (Rs. 10/- each) to Promoters and Non-Promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- **Scrip Code:** 511539
- **ISIN:** INE533B01028
- **Number of new shares:** 1,01,20,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each
- **Issue price:** Rs. 10/- per share (at par)
- **Date of allotment:** 20 April 2026
- **Trading effective from:** Monday, 1 June 2026
- **Distribution numbers:** 13537242 to 23657241
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative notice informing trading members of the listing of new securities issued pursuant to a preferential allotment on conversion of warrants.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the listing of these new securities and their permitted trading from June 1, 2026. The lock-in restrictions on portions of newly listed shares must be observed.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment date:** 20 April 2026
- **Trading commencement:** 1 June 2026
- **Lock-in expiry (33,70,000 shares, Dist. Nos. 13537242–16907241):** 31 December 2027
- **Lock-in expiry (67,50,000 shares, Dist. Nos. 16907241–23657241):** 31 December 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 1,01,20,000 new equity shares to the tradeable float of Supra Trends Limited starting June 1, 2026, though a significant portion remains under lock-in. Of the total shares, 33,70,000 shares are locked in until December 31, 2027, and 67,50,000 shares are locked in until December 31, 2026. Only the unlocked portion will be freely tradeable upon listing. The issuance represents dilution for existing shareholders, as shares were issued at par value (Rs. 10/-) to promoters and non-promoters via warrant conversion.
