## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for the company formerly known as Savani Financials Limited. The company's name has been officially changed to Mantra Capital Limited with effect from October 14, 2025. The company was originally incorporated as Savani Investment & Leasing Limited.

## Key Points

- **Old Name**: Savani Financials Limited
- **New Name**: Mantra Capital Limited
- **CIN**: L67120MH1983PLC031614
- **Effective Date**: October 14, 2025
- **Company Type**: Company limited by shares
- **Original Name**: Savani Investment & Leasing Limited (at incorporation)
- **Registered Office**: 602, Samarth Vaibhav Co-op Hsg Society Ltd., Off - Link Road, Lokhandwala Complex, Andheri (West), Mumbai - 400053, Maharashtra

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard administrative name change process completed under the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014, pursuant to rule 29.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display the old name (Savani Financials Limited) along with the new name (Mantra Capital Limited) for a period of two years at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders
- All stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company and its directors on the MCA website (www.mca.gov.in/MCA21)

## Important Dates

- **Certificate Issue Date**: October 14, 2025
- **Effective Date of Name Change**: October 14, 2025
- **Old Name Display Period**: Two years from October 14, 2025 (until October 14, 2027)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine corporate action involving a name change. Trading will continue under the new name with the same CIN.

**Operational Impact**: The company must update all statutory documents, letterheads, and public communications to reflect the new name while continuing to display the old name for the mandatory two-year period.

**Stakeholder Impact**: No impact on rights and liabilities of shareholders, creditors, or other stakeholders. All existing contracts, assets, and obligations remain valid under the new name.
