## Summary

BSE has updated the list of securities subject to Additional Surveillance Measure (ASM) for companies undergoing the Insolvency Resolution Process (IRP) under the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code (IBC). The changes are effective June 15, 2026. Securities newly meeting the IBC ASM criteria are listed in Annexure I, securities moving out of the framework are in Annexure II, and a consolidated list of all securities currently under the framework is in Annexure III.

## Key Points

- Securities listed in Annexure I are placed under the IBC ASM framework effective June 15, 2026.
- Securities listed in Annexure II are removed from the IBC ASM framework effective June 15, 2026.
- The IBC ASM framework operates alongside all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the exchanges.
- Shortlisting under this framework is purely for market surveillance purposes and does not constitute an adverse action against the concerned company.
- The framework is periodically reviewed.

## Regulatory Changes

This notice references and continues the framework established under prior Exchange notices: 20180608-34 (June 8, 2018), 20220520-52 (May 20, 2022), 20230210-55 (February 10, 2023), and 20250905-47 (September 5, 2025).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the updated list of securities under the IBC ASM framework.
- The IBC ASM framework applies in conjunction with any other surveillance measures already in force for a given security.
- For clarifications, members may write to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **June 15, 2026**: Effective date for securities moving into (Annexure I) and out of (Annexure II) the IBC ASM framework.

## Impact Assessment

Securities placed under the IBC ASM framework are subject to additional trading restrictions, typically including trade-for-trade settlement and applicable price bands. Securities exiting the framework will have their price bands reinstated to the levels applicable prior to their inclusion, provided they are not subject to any other surveillance framework. This update affects a defined set of securities undergoing insolvency proceedings and has limited broader market impact.
