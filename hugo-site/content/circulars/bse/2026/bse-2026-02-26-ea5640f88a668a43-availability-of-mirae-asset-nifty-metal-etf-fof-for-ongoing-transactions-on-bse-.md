## Summary

BSE has notified members that the Mirae Asset Nifty Metal ETF Fund of Funds (FOF), which was previously launched as an NFO, has been reopened for ongoing subscription and redemption on the BSE StAR MF Platform. This follows the earlier Notice No. 20260209-11. The scheme is available with SIP, STP, and SWP facilities effective February 27, 2026.

## Key Points

- Mirae Asset Nifty Metal ETF FOF NFO scheme reopened for ongoing transactions from February 27, 2026
- Available on BSE StAR MF Platform with SIP, STP, and SWP facilities
- Six plan variants available: Regular and Direct plans, each with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- Reference circular: Notice No. 20260209-11

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an operational update regarding the transition of a mutual fund scheme from NFO to ongoing subscription/redemption mode.

## Compliance Requirements

- BSE members and mutual fund distributors using BSE StAR MF Platform may now process ongoing subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP transactions for the listed Mirae Asset Nifty Metal ETF FOF schemes.
- Members should update their systems to reflect the availability of these six ISINs for ongoing transactions.

## Important Dates

- **February 27, 2026**: Effective date for ongoing subscription, redemption, and systematic transaction (SIP/STP/SWP) facilities on BSE StAR MF Platform

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This is a routine operational notice enabling ongoing transactions in a mutual fund FOF product after its NFO period. Primarily relevant to distributors, advisors, and investors interested in metal-sector ETF exposure through the Nifty Metal index via a fund-of-funds structure. No systemic or regulatory implications for the broader market.
