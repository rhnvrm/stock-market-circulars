## Summary

BSE has listed privately placed debt securities from Bajaj Finance Limited (1 series) and Adani Power Limited (4 series) on the debt market segment. The securities are now admitted to trading effective January 29, 2026. All securities will trade in dematerialized form with a tick size of 1 paise.

## Key Points

- Bajaj Finance Limited issued 1 debt series totaling Rs. 5,120 crores (512,000 units at Rs. 1 lakh face value)
- Adani Power Limited issued 4 debt series totaling Rs. 7,500 crores (750,000 units at Rs. 1 lakh face value)
- All securities allotted on January 27, 2026
- Trading only in dematerialized form under specified ISIN numbers
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Market lot: 1 unit for all securities
