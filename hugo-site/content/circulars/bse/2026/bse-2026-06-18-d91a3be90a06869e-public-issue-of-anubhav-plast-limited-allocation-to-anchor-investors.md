## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that Anchor Investors (AIs) have subscribed to 8,48,000 equity shares of Anubhav Plast Limited at Rs. 80/- per equity share as part of the company's ongoing public issue. The notice references an Anchor Allocation Letter issued by the company on June 18, 2026.

## Key Points

- 8,48,000 equity shares allotted to Anchor Investors in the public issue of Anubhav Plast Limited
- Issue price for the Anchor Investor portion: Rs. 80/- per equity share
- Allocation date: June 18, 2026
- Notice issued by Mangesh Tayde, Vice President, BSE
- Attachment: Company_Anchor_Allocation_Letter

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine informational notice regarding anchor investor allocation in an IPO.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action required from trading members. The notice is for information purposes only.

## Important Dates

- Anchor Investor allocation date: June 18, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. The anchor investor subscription at Rs. 80/- per share signals institutional interest in Anubhav Plast Limited's IPO and may influence retail investor sentiment ahead of the public issue opening. The allocation is routine and procedural.
