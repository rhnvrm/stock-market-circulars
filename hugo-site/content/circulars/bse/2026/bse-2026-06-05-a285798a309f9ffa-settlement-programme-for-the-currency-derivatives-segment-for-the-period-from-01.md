## Summary

ICCL (Indian Clearing Corporation Limited) has issued the settlement programme for the Currency Derivatives segment of BSE for the period from 01 July 2026 to 31 July 2026. The detailed schedule is provided in the attached document CDX072026.

## Key Points

- Settlement programme covers the Currency Derivatives segment for July 2026 (01.07.2026 to 31.07.2026)
- Issued by ICCL under the Settlement/RMS category
- Attachment CDX072026 contains the detailed day-wise settlement schedule
- Notice No: 20260605-11 dated 05 June 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine periodic issuance of the settlement calendar.

## Compliance Requirements

All participants in the Currency Derivatives segment must refer to the attached settlement programme (CDX072026) and ensure timely fulfillment of settlement obligations as per the prescribed schedule for July 2026.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: 05 June 2026
- Settlement Programme Period: 01 July 2026 to 31 July 2026

## Impact Assessment

Routine operational circular with low market impact. Participants in the Currency Derivatives segment should download the attachment CDX072026 to plan their settlement activities for July 2026. No changes to existing settlement mechanisms are indicated.
