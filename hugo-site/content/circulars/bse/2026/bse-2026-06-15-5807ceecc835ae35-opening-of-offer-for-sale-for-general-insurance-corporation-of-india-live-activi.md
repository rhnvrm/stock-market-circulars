## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260615-39 dated June 15, 2026, announcing the opening of the Offer for Sale (OFS) for General Insurance Corporation of India (GICRE) along with the live activities schedule governing the OFS process on the Exchange's equity segment.

## Key Points

- Notice issued under the Trading Operations department of BSE's equity segment.
- The notice pertains to the commencement of the Offer for Sale (OFS) for General Insurance Corporation of India.
- A live activities schedule has been published to guide participants through the OFS opening process.
- No attachments are associated with this notice; the schedule details are contained within the notice itself.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This notice operationalises the OFS mechanism under existing SEBI and BSE OFS framework guidelines.

## Compliance Requirements

- Brokers and trading members facilitating OFS bids must adhere to the live activities schedule published by BSE.
- Participants must ensure bids are placed within the designated timelines as per the schedule.
- Retail and non-retail investor categories should note any separate bidding windows applicable under the OFS structure.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 15, 2026
- **OFS Opening Date:** June 15, 2026 (as indicated by the notice date and live activities schedule)

## Impact Assessment

The OFS for General Insurance Corporation of India (GICRE), a government-owned reinsurer, provides an opportunity for investors to acquire shares via the exchange-based OFS mechanism. The live activities schedule is operationally significant for trading members and investors participating in the OFS. Market impact is moderate and confined to GICRE stock and OFS participants during the designated bidding window.
