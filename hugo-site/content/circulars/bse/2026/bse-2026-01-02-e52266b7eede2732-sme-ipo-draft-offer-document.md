## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of Craftech Paper Mills Limited, filed on December 31, 2025, for public comments. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and will remain accessible on the stock exchange website for at least 21 days. Market participants are invited to submit their comments to equity.ipo@bseindia.com.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Craftech Paper Mills Limited on December 31, 2025
- Document complies with Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website for public review
- 21-day comment period for stakeholders
- Comments to be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard procedure for SME IPO draft offer document publication as per ICDR Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- The draft offer document must remain available on the stock exchange website for at least 21 days
- Public comments are invited from all stakeholders
- Comments must be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com within the specified timeframe
- The company must address any comments received during this public review period

## Important Dates

- **December 31, 2025**: Draft Offer Document filed
- **January 2, 2026**: Notice issued and document made public
- **January 23, 2026**: Last date for public comments (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - This affects potential investors in the SME segment interested in Craftech Paper Mills Limited's upcoming IPO. The public comment period allows stakeholders to review and provide feedback on the offering.

**Investor Impact**: Medium - Prospective investors have 21 days to review the draft offer document and understand the company's financials, business model, and risk factors before the final offer document is published.

**Regulatory Impact**: Low - This is a standard procedural notification as part of the IPO process under ICDR Regulations, ensuring transparency and public participation in the IPO approval process.
