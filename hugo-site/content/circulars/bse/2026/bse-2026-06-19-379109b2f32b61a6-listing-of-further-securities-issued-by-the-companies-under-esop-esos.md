## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that further securities issued by 10 companies under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP/ESOS) are listed and admitted for trading on the Exchange with effect from Monday, June 22, 2026. None of the newly issued shares carry any lock-in restrictions.

## Key Points

- 10 companies have listed additional shares arising from ESOP/ESOS allotments.
- Effective date for trading: Monday, June 22, 2026.
- No lock-in period applies to any of the newly listed shares.
- The new shares are fungible with existing equity shares of the respective companies (same ISIN).

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification under BSE's existing framework for ESOP/ESOS share admissions.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should update their systems to reflect the new shares admitted for trading. No additional compliance action is required from members beyond standard trade processing.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 19, 2026
- **Effective Date for Trading:** June 22, 2026 (Monday)
