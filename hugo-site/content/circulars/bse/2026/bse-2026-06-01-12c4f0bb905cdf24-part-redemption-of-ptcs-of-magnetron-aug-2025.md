## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that MAGNETRON AUG 2025 has communicated reduced face values per Pass Through Certificate (PTC) following a partial redemption event. Two PTCs under the same series are affected, each carrying a distinct ISIN and revised face value. The reduced face values will be applicable from June 2, 2026.

## Key Points

- MAGNETRON AUG 2025 PTCs are subject to a partial redemption, reducing the face value per unit.
- Two ISINs are affected under the series MA2025-11.50%-28-07-28-PTC.
- Scrip Code 977078 (ISIN: INE2GPJ15012) will have a reduced face value of Rs. 4,301.62 per PTC.
- Scrip Code 977079 (ISIN: INE2GPJ15020) will have a reduced face value of Rs. 7,560.52 per PTC.
- Trading members are advised to update their records accordingly.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced. This notice implements a contractual partial redemption under existing PTC structures, adjusting the face value of specific debt instruments traded on BSE's debt segment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are required to take note of the revised reduced face values for both PTCs. Systems and records should reflect the updated face values for the affected scrip codes effective from June 2, 2026, as referenced under DR-642/2026-2027.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 1, 2026
- **Effective Date of Reduced Face Value:** June 2, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt market operational notice with limited market-wide impact. It affects holders and traders of two specific PTCs (scrip codes 977078 and 977079) under the MAGNETRON AUG 2025 series. The reduced face values reflect principal repayment passed through to PTC holders, which is a standard feature of pass-through certificate structures. No systemic risk or broader market disruption is expected.
