## Summary

BSE has published the demat auction list for Settlement No. 805 dated January 27, 2026. The auction includes securities from 89 scrips where short deliveries have occurred, requiring auction to complete settlement obligations. The quantities range from 1 share to 10,422 shares across various companies.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 805
- Auction Date: January 27, 2026
- Total scrips involved: 89 different securities
- Largest quantity: INDIAN INFOTECH (509051) - 10,422 shares
- Includes major stocks like KOTAK MAHINDRA (1,000 shares), PUNJAB NATIONAL BANK (3,000 shares), SUZLON ENERGY (1,000 shares)
- Covers diverse sectors including power, banking, automotive, technology, and manufacturing

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard auction settlement process as per existing BSE regulations for handling short deliveries.

## Compliance Requirements

- Brokers with short delivery positions must participate in the auction process
- Settlement must be completed through the auction mechanism for the listed quantities
- Buyers and sellers must settle their obligations as per BSE auction settlement rules

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: January 27, 2026
- Settlement Number: 805

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - Affects multiple scrips but quantities are relatively small for most stocks. Largest impact on INDIAN INFOTECH (10,422 shares), GARMENT MANTRA (7,113 shares), and REGIS INDUSTRIES (4,991 shares).

**Operational Impact:** Specific to brokers and shareholders involved in settlement 805 who failed to deliver securities on settlement date. May result in auction prices different from market prices, affecting settlement costs.

**Investor Impact:** Limited to those with open positions in the affected settlement; general market investors not directly impacted.
