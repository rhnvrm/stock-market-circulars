## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 1,54,280 new equity shares of Bharti Airtel Limited (Scrip Code: 532454, ISIN: INE397D01024) are permitted for trading effective Wednesday, April 29, 2026. These shares arise from the conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid-up shares of Rs. 5/- each, originally issued on a rights basis.

## Key Points

- 1,54,280 equity shares of Bharti Airtel Limited admitted to trading on BSE from April 29, 2026
- Shares are of face value Rs. 5/- each
- Originated from conversion of partly paid shares (rights issue) to fully paid-up shares
- ISIN: INE397D01024
- Distinctive numbers: 5748683775 to 5748838054
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice for newly converted equity shares.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed and permitted to trade in these new securities from the effective date. No additional compliance action required from members beyond normal trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** April 28, 2026
- **Trading Effective Date:** April 29, 2026 (Wednesday)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The addition of 1,54,280 shares is a small incremental increase to Bharti Airtel's existing large equity base. The shares are pari-passu with existing shares and result from a pre-announced rights issue conversion, so no pricing surprise is expected. Existing shareholders and traders are unaffected operationally.
