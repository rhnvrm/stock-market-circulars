## Summary

BSE Limited, through Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd. (ICCL), has issued a notice inviting trading members to submit auction offers for the purchase of securities under Rolling Settlement No. DR-626/2026-27. The auction is scheduled for May 11, 2026, with pay-in on May 12, 2026.

## Key Points

- Auction offer entry window: 2:00 PM to 2:45 PM on Monday, May 11, 2026
- Auction matching session: 2:45 PM to 3:00 PM
- Delivery statements and accepted auction offer reports available: 3:00 PM to 3:30 PM
- Money statements and final reports: 3:35 PM to 5:30 PM
- Pay-in of auction securities due by 8:00 AM on Tuesday, May 12, 2026
- Settlement will be routed through the Clearing Member of each Trading Member under the Uniform Membership structure
- The restriction on trading members offering securities in the same settlement in which they short-delivered has been removed

## Regulatory Changes

Following implementation of the Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment, settlement is now processed through the Clearing Member of a Trading Member. The prior restriction preventing trading members from offering securities in auction for settlements in which they had short delivered has been lifted.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and custodians must check their auction pay-in obligations for securities
- Securities pay-in must be executed in depositories within depository cut-off timings
- Trading members must refer to the cutoff rate on the "Auction Offer Entry Screen" before submitting offers
- Offers must reference any relevant Corporate Action notices (Ex-Rights, Ex-Bonus, Ex-Split, etc.) issued by the respective department
- No corrections will be permitted for punching errors in security code or rate; members must exercise utmost care

## Important Dates

- **May 11, 2026**: Auction offer entry (2:00 PM – 2:45 PM), matching, and report generation
- **May 12, 2026**: Auction securities pay-in deadline at 8:00 AM

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice affecting trading members and custodians with obligations under Rolling Settlement DR-626/2026-27. The removal of the restriction on offering securities in the same settlement where short delivery occurred slightly broadens participation eligibility. Members with short delivery positions should verify their pay-in obligations promptly to avoid penalties. No broad market impact is anticipated beyond the specific securities listed in the auction attachments (off626, ARR 626).
