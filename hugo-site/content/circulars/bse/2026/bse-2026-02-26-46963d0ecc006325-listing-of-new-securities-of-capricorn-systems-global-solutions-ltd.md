## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 2,39,76,000 new equity shares of Capricorn Systems Global Solutions Ltd. (Scrip Code: 512169, ISIN: INE968E01012), issued at par (Rs. 10/- each) on a rights basis, are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Friday, February 27, 2026.

## Key Points

- 2,39,76,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at par on rights basis are now listed
- Scrip Code: 512169 | ISIN: INE968E01012
- Distribution numbers: 3996001 to 27972000
- Date of Allotment: February 25, 2026
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification pursuant to a completed rights issue.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of the Exchange are informed to take note of the new securities available for trading. No specific compliance action is required beyond standard trading procedures for newly listed securities.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** February 25, 2026
- **Trading Commencement Date:** February 27, 2026 (Friday)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a routine listing of rights issue shares for a small-cap company (Capricorn Systems Global Solutions Ltd.). The new shares are pari-passu with existing shares, meaning no change in rights or entitlements. The addition of approximately 2.4 crore shares to the tradeable float may modestly increase liquidity for existing shareholders.
