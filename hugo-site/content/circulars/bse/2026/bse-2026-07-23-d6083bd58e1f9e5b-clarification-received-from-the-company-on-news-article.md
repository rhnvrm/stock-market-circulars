## Summary

BSE Ltd, as part of its routine surveillance process to verify news articles appearing in newspapers, websites, and television channels, sought clarification from Rajesh Exports Ltd (Scrip Code: 531500) regarding a news article published on The Hindu BusinessLine. As of this notice dated 23 July 2026, the company's reply is awaited.

## Key Points

- BSE sought clarification from Rajesh Exports Ltd on a news article to verify its accuracy for members and investors.
- The relevant news article was published on thehindubusinessline.com.
- Company reply is pending as of the notice date.
- No attachments were provided with this notice.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine surveillance clarification request under BSE's standard process for verifying market-relevant news reports.

## Compliance Requirements

Rajesh Exports Ltd is required to respond to the Exchange's clarification request regarding the news article in question.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 23 July 2026
- Company reply: Awaited (no deadline specified in notice)

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact procedural notice indicating that BSE has queried Rajesh Exports Ltd about a news report but has not yet received a response. Investors should monitor for the company's forthcoming clarification, as the underlying news article's content and the company's response could carry further market implications depending on their nature.
