## Summary

BSE has notified that 1,19,16,427 new equity shares of JK Paper Limited (Scrip Code: 532162, ISIN: INE789E01012) are listed and permitted for trading on the Exchange with effect from Tuesday, June 16, 2026. These shares were issued pursuant to a Composite Scheme of Arrangement involving JK Paper Limited as the Transferee Company and multiple transferor/resulting companies.

## Key Points

- 1,19,16,427 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each are newly listed for trading on BSE from June 16, 2026.
- Shares issued under a Composite Scheme of Arrangement where JK Paper Limited is the Transferee Company.
- ISIN: INE789E01012; Distribution Numbers: 178243586 – 190160012.
- Date of allotment was March 20, 2026.
- New shares rank pari-passu with the existing equity shares of JK Paper Limited.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an operational listing notice under existing BSE Listing Operations framework following approval of the scheme of arrangement.

## Compliance Requirements

Market participants are informed that the newly listed shares of JK Paper Limited are available for trading. No specific compliance action is required from participants beyond treating these shares as regular equity shares of JK Paper Limited.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** March 20, 2026
- **Trading Effective From:** June 16, 2026 (Tuesday)
- **Notice Date:** June 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds approximately 1.19 crore new equity shares to JK Paper Limited's tradeable float, arising from a multi-party composite scheme of arrangement. Companies involved as transferors/resulting party include JKPT Utility Packaging Solutions Private Limited (formerly Manipal Utility Packaging Solutions Private Limited), Securipax Packaging Private Limited, Horizon Packs Private Limited, Enviro Tech Ventures Limited, and PSV Agro Products Private Limited. Since the shares rank pari-passu with existing equity, there is a modest dilution effect on existing shareholders. Market impact is expected to be limited and orderly given the standard listing process.
