## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for the company previously known as Kapil Cotex Ltd (CIN: L21001MH1983PLC031114). The company's name has been officially changed to Skybiotech Healthcare Limited effective November 7, 2025. This certificate was issued by the Central Processing Centre, ROC and digitally signed by Sweety Kumar, Assistant/Deputy/Registrar of Companies.

## Key Points

- Old Name: KAPIL COTEX LTD
- New Name: SKYBIOTECH HEALTHCARE LIMITED
- Corporate Identification Number (CIN): L21001MH1983PLC031114
- Company Type: Company limited by shares
- Original Incorporation Name: KAPIL COTEX LTD
- Certificate Effective Date: November 7, 2025
- Issuing Authority: Central Processing Centre, Ministry of Corporate Affairs
- Authorized Signatory: Sweety Kumar

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this circular. This is a standard administrative notification of a company name change approved by the Ministry of Corporate Affairs under the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014, specifically pursuant to rule 29.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display the old name (Kapil Cotex Ltd) for a period of two years along with its new name (Skybiotech Healthcare Limited) at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders
- All stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company and its directors on the Ministry's website www.mca.gov.in/MCA21

## Important Dates

- **November 7, 2025**: Effective date of name change from Kapil Cotex Ltd to Skybiotech Healthcare Limited
- **November 7, 2025 to November 6, 2027**: Two-year period during which the company must display both old and new names

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is an administrative name change that does not affect the company's operations, financial standing, or shareholder rights. Trading will continue under the new name with the same CIN and corporate identity.

**Operational Impact**: Low. The company must update its branding, stationery, and displays to reflect the new name while maintaining dual display of both names for the mandated two-year period.

**Stakeholder Impact**: Informational only. Shareholders, creditors, and other stakeholders should note the new name for future correspondence and documentation. All existing rights, obligations, and liabilities remain unchanged.
