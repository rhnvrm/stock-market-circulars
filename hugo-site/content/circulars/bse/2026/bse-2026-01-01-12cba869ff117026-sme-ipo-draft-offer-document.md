## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of Technocrats Plasma Systems Limited on its SME platform for public comments. The document was filed on December 31, 2025 and contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations. Stakeholders can submit comments within 21 days from January 01, 2026.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Technocrats Plasma Systems Limited on December 31, 2025
- Document contains all disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website at www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public comments invited within 21 days from notice date
- Comments to be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard procedural notice for SME IPO draft offer document publication.

## Compliance Requirements

- Draft Offer Document to remain available on stock exchange website for at least 21 days
- Stakeholders can submit comments on the draft offer document to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Document complies with Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations disclosure requirements

## Important Dates

- **Draft Offer Document Filed**: December 31, 2025
- **Notice Date**: January 01, 2026
- **Public Comment Period**: 21 days from January 01, 2026 (deadline: January 21, 2026)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal market impact as it is a standard procedural step in the SME IPO process. The impact is limited to:

- Potential investors interested in Technocrats Plasma Systems Limited SME IPO
- Market participants who wish to provide feedback on the draft offer document
- No immediate trading or compliance impact on existing market participants

The public comment period allows stakeholders to review and provide input before the final offer document is issued.
