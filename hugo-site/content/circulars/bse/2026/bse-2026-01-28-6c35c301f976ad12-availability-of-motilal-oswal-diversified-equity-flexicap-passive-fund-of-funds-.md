## Summary

BSE has notified members that Motilal Oswal Diversified Equity Flexicap Passive Fund of Funds will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions along with SIP, STP and SWP facilities on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective January 29, 2026. This reopening applies to both Regular Plan - Growth and Direct Plan - Growth options of the scheme.

## Key Points

- Fund reopening for ongoing transactions from January 29, 2026
- Available on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Two plan options available: Regular Plan - Growth (ISIN: INF247L01GM7) and Direct Plan - Growth (ISIN: INF247L01GL9)
- All transaction facilities enabled: subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Scheme category: Equity (Fund of Funds)
- References previous Notice No. 20260101-31

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding fund availability.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. Members can resume processing transactions for this fund through the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: January 29, 2026 - Fund reopens for ongoing transactions
- **Notice Date**: January 28, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine operational notice affecting only investors and distributors interested in this specific mutual fund scheme. The reopening restores normal transaction capabilities for the fund after a temporary suspension period. No impact on equity markets or other mutual fund schemes.
