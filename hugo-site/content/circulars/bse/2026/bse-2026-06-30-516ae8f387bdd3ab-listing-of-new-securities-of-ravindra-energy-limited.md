## Summary

BSE has listed 1,98,54,940 new equity shares of Ravindra Energy Limited (Scrip Code: 504341) issued on a rights basis at Rs. 10/- face value and Rs. 91/- premium (total Rs. 101/- per share), effective for trading from July 1, 2026.

## Key Points

- 1,98,54,940 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 91/- per share on rights basis
- ISIN: INE206N01018
- Distribution Numbers: 181194464 to 201049403
- Date of Allotment: June 25, 2026
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification following completion of a rights issue.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed that the new securities are listed and permitted to trade on BSE with effect from July 1, 2026. No additional compliance action required from members beyond standard trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: June 25, 2026
- Trading Effective From: July 1, 2026 (Wednesday)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. The listing adds approximately 1.99 crore new equity shares of Ravindra Energy Limited (Scrip Code: 504341) to the tradeable float on BSE. These shares are pari-passu with existing shares, meaning no differentiation in rights or entitlements. Rights issue participants may begin trading their allotted shares from July 1, 2026.
