## Summary

BSE has notified revised trading terms for the debt securities of Pradhaan City K R Puram Private Limited (Scrip Code: 977087, ISIN: INE1ZL407013), effective Monday, June 15, 2026. This is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20250905-19 dated September 5, 2025. The revision involves strengthened security and additional corporate guarantees beyond what was previously in place.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code 977087 (ISIN: INE1ZL407013) will trade with revised terms from June 15, 2026
- Existing security remains unchanged; additional security includes pledge of Equity Shares and OCDs
- Existing guarantees remain unchanged; additional Corporate Guarantees provided by House of Pradhaan LLP and Assetz Private Limited
- This update follows Exchange Notice No. 20250905-19 dated September 5, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The revised terms introduce enhanced security and guarantee arrangements for the debt instrument:
- **Security (Annexure-A):** No change to existing security; additionally, pledge of Additional Equity Shares and OCDs has been added
- **Guarantees (Annexure-B):** No change to existing guarantees; additionally, Corporate Guarantees by House of Pradhaan LLP and Assetz Private Limited have been added

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members are required to note the revised terms for Scrip Code 977087 (ISIN: INE1ZL407013) and apply them for all trades executed from June 15, 2026 onwards. Members may contact Ms. Rutuja Mhasade at +91 22 2272 8597 or debt.listing@bseindia.com for further clarification.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 12, 2026
- **Effective Date:** Monday, June 15, 2026
- **Preceding Notice:** Exchange Notice No. 20250905-19 dated September 5, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The impact is moderate and targeted. Existing holders and prospective buyers of Pradhaan City K R Puram Private Limited's debt instrument (Scrip Code 977087) should note the strengthened security and guarantee structure, which may positively affect the credit profile of the security. The addition of corporate guarantees from House of Pradhaan LLP and Assetz Private Limited, along with the pledge of additional equity shares and OCDs, provides enhanced protection to debtholders. General market impact is limited given the single-scrip scope.
