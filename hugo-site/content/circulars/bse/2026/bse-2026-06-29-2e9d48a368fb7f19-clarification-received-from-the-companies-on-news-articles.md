## Summary

BSE's Surveillance department (DOSS) sought clarifications from Rajesh Exports Ltd and Tata Motors Passenger Vehicles Ltd regarding news articles published in various newspapers, websites, and television channels. Replies have been received from both companies and are attached to the notice for members and investors.

## Key Points

- BSE initiated clarification requests to verify accuracy of news articles circulating in media
- Rajesh Exports Ltd (Scrip Code: 531500) provided a reply referencing a Moneycontrol article
- Tata Motors Passenger Vehicles Ltd (Scrip Code: 500570) provided a reply referencing a Moneycontrol article
- Replies are attached to the notice for public information

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard surveillance-driven clarification notice.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. The companies have already submitted their clarifications to BSE as requested.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 29 June 2026

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This is a routine transparency measure by BSE's Surveillance department to ensure investors have accurate information about news circulating in the media. The clarifications are informational in nature and do not impose new obligations on investors or market participants.
