## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members that the equity shares of Omnitech Engineering Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange. The specific date of listing and further security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Omnitech Engineering Limited equity shares approved for listing on BSE
- Scrip Code: 544720
- Trading Symbol: OMNI
- ISIN: INE0UH301010
- Exact listing date to be announced via a separate notice
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing admission notice for a new equity scrip on BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should take note of the upcoming listing of Omnitech Engineering Limited and await the subsequent notice confirming the listing date and security details before initiating any dealings.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: March 4, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced via a separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This notice signals the imminent listing of Omnitech Engineering Limited (OMNI) on BSE. Trading members and investors should monitor for the follow-up notice confirming the effective listing date. The scrip will be available under Scrip Code 544720 in the Equity segment once listed.
