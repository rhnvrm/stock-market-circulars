## Summary

BSE has activated the Call Money Module on the IBBS (Internet Based Book Building Software) platform for Kati Patang Lifestyle Limited. This is a First and Final Call on 1,02,56,651 partly paid-up equity shares, with Rs. 10.00 payable per share. Trading Members, SCSBs, and intermediaries are requested to facilitate the collection of call money during the specified issue period.

## Key Points

- BSE activated the IBBS Call Money Module per Exchange Notice No. 20210104-37 dated January 4, 2021
- Security symbol: **PATANGFCM**; Market lot: 1
- Total partly paid-up equity shares under call: **1,02,56,651**
- Call money amount: **Rs. 10.00 per partly paid-up equity share** (First and Final Call)
- Issue period: **15 days** with bidding hours 10:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m.
- Registrar: MAS Services Limited, Delhi (SEBI Reg. No. INR000000049)
- Module available to SCSBs and Trading Members with 3-in-1 type accounts per SEBI circular dated December 8, 2020

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice operates under the existing framework established by:
- Exchange Notice No. 20210104-37 (January 4, 2021)
- SEBI circular dated December 8, 2020 governing the Call Money Module for SCSBs and Trading Members

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members** and **SCSBs** must submit call money notice details collected from investors through the IBBS platform during the issue period
- Only SCSBs and Trading Members with 3-in-1 type accounts are eligible to participate in the Call Money Module
- Queries on regulatory/business rules: contact Listing Sales at listing.sales@bseindia.com or 022-2272-8846/5063/8179
- Queries on IBBS platform: contact IBBS ops team at 022-2272-8376/8436

|---|---|

## Impact Assessment

This notice has a moderate, company-specific impact. Holders of partly paid-up equity shares of Kati Patang Lifestyle Limited must pay Rs. 10.00 per share during the April 10–24, 2026 window to fulfil their call money obligation. Failure to pay may result in forfeiture of partly paid shares per applicable company law provisions. Trading Members and SCSBs with eligible 3-in-1 accounts must ensure operational readiness on the IBBS platform before the opening date.
