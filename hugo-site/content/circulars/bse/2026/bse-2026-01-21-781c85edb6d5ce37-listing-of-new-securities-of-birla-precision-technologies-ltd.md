## Summary

BSE has listed 24,00,000 equity shares of Birla Precision Technologies Ltd (Scrip Code: 522105) with effect from January 22, 2026. These shares were issued to promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants at an issue price of Rs. 64 per share (face value Rs. 2 with premium of Rs. 62). The shares are subject to a lock-in period until July 31, 2027.

## Key Points

- 24,00,000 equity shares listed and permitted to trade from January 22, 2026
- Issue type: Preferential allotment to promoters pursuant to warrant conversion
- Face value: Rs. 2 per share, issued at premium of Rs. 62 per share
- Total issue price: Rs. 64 per share
- Distinctive Numbers: 65987638 to 68387637
- Date of allotment: September 29, 2025
- ISIN: INE372E01025
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for new securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the listing of new securities
- The securities are subject to lock-in restrictions as specified
- All 24,00,000 shares locked until July 31, 2027

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: September 29, 2025
- **Listing Date**: January 22, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry**: July 31, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Limited immediate market impact as all shares are subject to lock-in until July 2027. The preferential issue to promoters represents a capital raising exercise through warrant conversion. No immediate trading impact expected given the lock-in restrictions preventing sale of these shares in the near term.
