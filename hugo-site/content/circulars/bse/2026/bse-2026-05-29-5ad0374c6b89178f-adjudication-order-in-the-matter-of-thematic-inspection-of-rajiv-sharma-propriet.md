## Summary

SEBI has issued adjudication order no. Order/JS/YK/2026-27/32423 dated May 27, 2026, against Rajiv Sharma, Proprietor of M/s. Capital Life Market Research (SEBI Investment Adviser registration INA000001365, Research Analyst registration INH000022163), following a thematic inspection. BSE has published this notice to alert all Investment Advisers and investors.

## Key Points

- SEBI adjudication order reference: Order/JS/YK/2026-27/32423 dated May 27, 2026
- Entity penalised: Rajiv Sharma, Proprietor of M/s. Capital Life Market Research
- Investment Adviser registration: INA000001365
- Research Analyst registration: INH000022163
- Order arose from a thematic inspection of the entity's operations
- Full order text is attached to the BSE notice and available on the SEBI website

## Regulatory Changes

No systemic regulatory changes. This is an enforcement action specific to Rajiv Sharma and M/s. Capital Life Market Research arising from findings during a SEBI thematic inspection of investment advisers.

## Compliance Requirements

- All Investment Advisers are requested to take note of this adjudication order and ensure their own practices comply with SEBI regulations to avoid similar enforcement actions.
- Investors dealing with Rajiv Sharma or M/s. Capital Life Market Research should review the full order on the SEBI website for details of the findings and implications.

## Important Dates

- SEBI adjudication order date: May 27, 2026
- BSE notice date: May 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This enforcement action directly affects Rajiv Sharma and M/s. Capital Life Market Research (INA000001365 / INH000022163). There is no broad market or trading impact. However, it signals SEBI's continued focus on thematic inspections of investment advisers and research analysts, serving as a compliance reminder to all similarly registered entities. Investors subscribed to services of the affected entity should consult the full SEBI order for guidance on next steps.
