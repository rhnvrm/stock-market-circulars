## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members that the Rajasthan State Development Loan government security with scrip code 810957 (ISIN: IN2920150355, 839RAJSDL26) is maturing on its redemption date. Trading in this security is suspended with effect from March 11, 2026.

## Key Points

- Government security 839RAJSDL26 (Scrip Code: 810957, ISIN: IN2920150355) is maturing on its redemption date
- Trading in this security is suspended effective March 11, 2026
- Trading members are advised not to deal in this security from the suspension date
- Notice issued under DR-837/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice for the suspension of trading in a maturing government security under BSE's debt market segment procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all trading activity in scrip code 810957 (839RAJSDL26) from March 11, 2026
- Members should update their systems to reflect the suspension of this security

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** March 10, 2026
- **Suspension Effective Date:** March 11, 2026 (Redemption/Maturity Date)

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders of the specific Rajasthan State Development Loan security (839RAJSDL26). This is a standard lifecycle event — the security matures and is removed from trading. No broader market disruption is expected. Investors holding this security will receive redemption proceeds as per the terms of the instrument.
