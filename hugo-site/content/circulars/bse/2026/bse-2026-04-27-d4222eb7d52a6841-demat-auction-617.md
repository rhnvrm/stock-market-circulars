## Summary

BSE Limited (through ICCL - Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd.) has invited offers from trading members for purchase of securities in Demat Auction 617, corresponding to Rolling Settlement No. DR-617/2026-27. The auction session is scheduled for Monday, April 27, 2026, with pay-in on April 28, 2026.

## Key Points

- Auction is for Rolling Settlement No. DR-617/2026-27 in the Equity segment
- Trading members must submit auction offers via trading terminals within the specified session timings
- After Uniform Membership structure implementation, settlement is processed through the Clearing Member of the Trading Member
- The restriction on trading members offering securities in auctions for settlements where they had short deliveries has been removed
- No corrections will be permitted for punching errors in securities code or rate
- BSE reserves the right to accept or reject any offer without assigning reasons
- Trading members should refer to the cutoff rate on the "Auction Offer Entry Screen" before submitting offers
- Members should also check for corporate actions (Ex-Rights, Ex-Bonus, Ex-Split) in relevant securities

## Regulatory Changes

Under the Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment, settlement is now routed through the Clearing Member of a Trading Member. The previously applicable restriction on trading members offering securities in auctions for settlements in which they had short delivered has been removed.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must submit auction offers only during the designated offer entry session (2:00 PM to 2:45 PM on April 27, 2026)
- Members and Custodians must check their auction pay-in obligations and execute securities pay-in in depositories within depository cut-off timings on April 28, 2026
- Trading members must exercise utmost care while entering securities codes and rates, as no corrections will be allowed after submission
- Members should refer to notices on corporate actions before submitting auction offers

## Important Dates

- **April 27, 2026 (Monday):**
  - Auction Offer Entry Session: 2:00 PM – 2:45 PM
  - Auction Matching: 2:45 PM – 3:00 PM
  - Delivery Statement & Report of Accepted Offers: 3:00 PM – 3:30 PM
  - Money Statement & Final Reports: 3:35 PM – 5:30 PM
- **April 28, 2026 (Tuesday):** Securities Pay-in at 8:00 AM

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational circular affecting trading and clearing members who have short delivery obligations in Rolling Settlement DR-617/2026-27. The removal of the restriction on short-delivered members participating in auctions (under the Uniform Membership structure) provides greater flexibility. Members with auction pay-in obligations must ensure timely securities delivery through depositories on April 28, 2026 to avoid further settlement failures. No broad market-wide impact is anticipated beyond the specific counterparties involved in this settlement cycle.
