## Summary

BSE (Bombay Stock Exchange) has announced a demat auction for Settlement No. 834, dated March 9, 2026. The auction covers 76 securities from various listed companies, with quantities ranging from 1 to 27,111 shares per scrip.

## Key Points

- Settlement No. 834 demat auction conducted on March 9, 2026
- Total of 76 securities listed across various BSE-listed companies
- Quantities vary significantly — from 1 share (multiple scrips) to 27,111 shares (Franklin Industries, scrip code 540190)
- Large quantity scrips include: DSM Fresh Foods (544568) - 8,400 shares, SOFCOM Systems (538923) - 13,798 shares, SELLWIN Trading (538875) - 9,800 shares, DECCAN GOLD (512068) - 5,000 shares, LT Foods (532783) - 6,900 shares
- Covers a broad range of sectors including banking, pharma, infrastructure, FMCG, technology, and industrials

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a routine settlement auction notice under existing BSE demat auction procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants with short positions or failed deliveries in the listed securities must participate in or settle through the auction process
- Buyers and sellers in the listed scrips must comply with settlement obligations as per BSE's standard settlement procedures

## Important Dates

- Auction Date: March 9, 2026
- Settlement No.: 834

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine demat auction affecting 76 securities. The impact is limited to market participants with open or failed delivery positions in the listed scrips. Larger quantity auctions (such as Franklin Industries at 27,111 shares, SOFCOM Systems at 13,798 shares, and DSM Fresh Foods at 8,400 shares) may have a slightly more notable price impact on those individual stocks. Overall market impact is low; individual stock impact is medium for high-quantity auction scrips.
