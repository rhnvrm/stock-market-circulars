## Summary

BSE has observed significant price and/or volume movement in the scrips of several listed companies in recent trading sessions. As part of its surveillance process, the Exchange wrote to these companies seeking clarification to ensure trading members and investors have access to the latest relevant information. Replies have been received and disclosed for Savita Oil Technologies Ltd and JOJO Ltd, while responses are still awaited from eight other companies.

## Key Points

- BSE identified unusual price/volume movement in the scrips of multiple listed companies.
- Clarification replies received and attached for: Savita Oil Technologies Ltd (Scrip Code: 524667) and JOJO Ltd (Scrip Code: 531910).
- Reply awaited from: CMX Holdings Ltd (532217), Crazy Snacks Ltd (544807), Darjeeling Industries Ltd (539770), Gconnect Logitech and Supply Chain Ltd (544156), Kinetic Trust Ltd (531274), Mapro Industries Ltd (509762), Sujala Trading & Holdings Ltd (539117), and Dhanashree Electronics Ltd (542679).
- Issued by the Surveillance Department (DOSS) of BSE Ltd, signed by Sanjay Jain (DVP, Surveillance) and Shreya Ghose (Manager, Surveillance).

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine surveillance disclosure under BSE's standard practice of seeking clarification from companies exhibiting significant price/volume movement.

## Compliance Requirements

The eight companies for which replies are awaited are expected to respond to BSE's clarification request. Investors and trading members are advised to review the attached replies before making trading decisions in the concerned scrips.

## Important Dates

Notice dated 24 July 2026 (Notice No. 20260724-10).

## Impact Assessment

This notice has limited direct market impact but signals heightened surveillance attention on the listed scrips, particularly the eight companies yet to respond, which could face further scrutiny or potential inclusion in surveillance frameworks (e.g., ASM/GSM) if movements persist without adequate clarification.
