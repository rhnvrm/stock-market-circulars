## Summary

BSE has listed 50,048 new equity shares of Rex Sealing and Packing Industries Limited (Scrip Code: 543744, ISIN: INE0MFT01014) for trading effective Friday, June 12, 2026. The shares were issued at a premium of Rs. 200/- (issue price Rs. 210/-) on a preferential basis to non-promoters pursuant to the conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 50,048 equity shares of Rs. 10/- face value issued at a premium of Rs. 200/-, totalling an issue price of Rs. 210/- per share
- Issued on preferential basis to non-promoters via conversion of warrants
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 2534953 to 2585000
- All 50,048 shares are subject to lock-in until December 31, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notice pursuant to an already-completed preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed that these securities are now permitted for trading on BSE from June 12, 2026. No additional compliance action is required from trading members beyond standard trading protocols.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** April 17, 2026
- **Trading Commencement:** June 12, 2026 (Friday)
- **Lock-in Expiry:** December 31, 2026 (applicable to all 50,048 shares, distribution nos. 2534953–2585000)

## Impact Assessment

The listing is of limited market impact given the small size of the allotment (50,048 shares). The entire allotment is under lock-in until end of 2026, meaning no immediate selling pressure from these shares. Existing shareholders are unaffected as the new shares rank pari-passu with existing equity.
