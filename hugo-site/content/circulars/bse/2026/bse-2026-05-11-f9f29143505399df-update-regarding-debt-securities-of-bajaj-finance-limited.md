## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (20260511-21) informing trading members that two debt securities of Bajaj Finance Limited will be available for trading effective Tuesday, May 12, 2026. This is a continuation of earlier notice 20241029-49 dated October 29, 2024.

## Key Points

- Two Bajaj Finance Limited debt securities are being made available for trading on the BSE Debt Segment from May 12, 2026.
- ISIN INE296A07TE5 is mapped to Scrip Code 976076.
- ISIN INE296A07TF2 is mapped to Scrip Code 976091.
- This notice continues from an earlier notice dated October 29, 2024 (Notice No. 20241029-49).
- No attachments are included with this notice.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an operational update enabling trading access to previously listed debt instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the availability of these scrip codes for trading from May 12, 2026. Members requiring further details may contact:
- **Mr. Akshay Arolkar** — +91 22 2272 8352 — debt.listing@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **May 12, 2026 (Tuesday):** Effective date for trading availability of both securities.

## Impact Assessment

This update is relevant to institutional and retail participants active in the BSE Debt Segment who wish to trade Bajaj Finance debt instruments under the specified ISINs. The addition of these two scrip codes expands tradeable debt securities for Bajaj Finance. Impact is moderate for debt market participants specifically tracking Bajaj Finance fixed-income instruments.
