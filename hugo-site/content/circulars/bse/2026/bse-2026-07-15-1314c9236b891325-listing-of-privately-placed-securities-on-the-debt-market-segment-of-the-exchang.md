## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that new commercial papers (CPs) issued on a private placement basis by five issuers - Tata Steel Limited, Bharat Heavy Electricals Ltd (BHEL), Adani Ports and Special Economic Zone Ltd (APSEZ), Vivriti Capital Limited, and Adani Enterprises Ltd - have been listed and admitted to trading on the BSE Debt Segment with effect from 15 July 2026.

## Key Points

- All securities will trade in dematerialised form only, in standard denominations of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof, with a tick size of 1 paise.
- Tata Steel Limited: ISIN INE081A14HF9, Scrip Code 732089, Scrip ID TSL140726, quantity 16,000, face/paid-up value Rs. 5,00,000 each, issue price Rs. 4,97,322.50, allotment date 14/07/2026, interest rate 6.55% p.a., redemption on 13/08/2026, rated ICRA A1+/IND A1+, IPA HDFC Bank Limited.
- Bharat Heavy Electricals Ltd: ISIN INE257A14AJ2, Scrip Code 732090, Scrip ID BHEL15726, quantity 10,000, issue price Rs. 4,93,382.00, allotment date 15/07/2026, interest rate 6.80% p.a., redemption on 25/09/2026, rated CARE A1+/IND A1+, IPA ICICI Bank Limited.
- Adani Ports and Special Economic Zone Ltd: ISIN INE742F14RI8, Scrip Code 732091, Scrip ID APSEZ14726, quantity 5,000, issue price Rs. 4,91,544.00, allotment date 14/07/2026, interest rate 6.90% p.a., redemption on 13/10/2026, rated CARE A1+/CRISIL A1+, IPA HDFC Bank Limited.
- Vivriti Capital Limited: ISIN INE0P1414121, Scrip Code 732092, Scrip ID VCL140726, quantity 500, issue price Rs. 4,82,653.50, allotment date 14/07/2026, interest rate 9.37% p.a., redemption on 01/12/2026, rated CARE A1+, IPA The Federal Bank Ltd.
- Adani Enterprises Ltd: ISIN INE423A14ZJ7, Scrip Code 732093, Scrip ID AEL140726, quantity 500, issue price Rs. 4,88,186.00, allotment date 14/07/2026, interest rate 7.30% p.a., rated CARE A1+/ICRA A1+.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine administrative listing notice under DCS-Listing department procedures for admitting privately placed debt securities to trading.

## Compliance Requirements

No action required from investors or listed companies. Trading members should note the ISIN, scrip codes, and trading parameters (denomination, tick size) for the newly listed commercial papers. Queries can be directed to BSE's debt department at the numbers provided.

## Important Dates

- Effective listing date: 15 July 2026
- Allotment dates: 14/07/2026 (Tata Steel, APSEZ, Vivriti Capital, Adani Enterprises) and 15/07/2026 (BHEL)
- Redemption dates: 13/08/2026 (Tata Steel), 25/09/2026 (BHEL), 13/10/2026 (APSEZ), 01/12/2026 (Vivriti Capital)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine debt-market listing notice enabling trading in newly issued commercial papers by well-rated corporates. It does not affect equity trading, existing shareholders, or company fundamentals; relevance is limited to institutional debt market participants and trading members dealing in the BSE Debt Segment.
