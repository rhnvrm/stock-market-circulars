## Summary

BSE's Department of Surveillance (DOSS) has revised the price bands for 43 equity scrips, effective June 01, 2026. The revised bands range from 1% to 20%, indicating tightening or relaxation of daily price movement limits based on surveillance assessments.

## Key Points

- 43 scrips have price bands revised effective June 01, 2026
- Price bands revised to levels of 1%, 2%, 5%, 10%, or 20% depending on the scrip
- Revision is issued by the Surveillance department (DOSS)
- Members may seek clarification at bse.surv@bseindia.com
- Wanbury Ltd (524212) receives the widest band at 20%; Citurgia Biochemicals (506373) and Sharp India Ltd (523449) receive the tightest at 1%

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must note the revised price band limits for the affected scrips and ensure that all orders placed on or after June 01, 2026 comply with the new bands. No direct action is required from listed companies; this is an exchange-level trading parameter change.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** May 29, 2026
- **Effective Date:** June 01, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The revision of price bands by the Surveillance department typically signals a change in the risk profile or volatility assessment of the affected securities. Tighter bands (1%–2%) on scrips like Citurgia Biochemicals, Sharp India, Aspira Pathlab, Envair Electrodyne, Eureka Industries, Indus Finance, and Rekvina Laboratories indicate heightened surveillance concern and will significantly restrict intraday price movement. Broader bands (10%–20%), such as for Wanbury Ltd, indicate relaxation of prior restrictions, potentially improving liquidity. Traders and investors in these 43 scrips should adjust their strategies accordingly ahead of June 01, 2026.
