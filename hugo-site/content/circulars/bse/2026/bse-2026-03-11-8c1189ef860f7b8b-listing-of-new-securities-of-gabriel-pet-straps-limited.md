## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 67,708 new equity shares of Gabriel Pet Straps Limited (Scrip Code: 544108) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Thursday, March 12, 2026. The shares were issued on a preferential basis to promoters at an issue price of Rs. 256/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- each, at a premium of Rs. 246/-).

## Key Points

- 67,708 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 246/- (issue price Rs. 256/-)
- Shares allotted to promoters on a preferential basis
- Distribution numbers: 7412337 to 7480044
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE0QZF01012
- Effective trading date: March 12, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for newly allotted securities under the preferential allotment route.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to update their records to reflect the new securities available for trading from March 12, 2026.
- The allotted shares are subject to a lock-in period and cannot be traded until the lock-in expiry date.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** December 8, 2025
- **Effective Trading Date:** March 12, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry:** September 30, 2027 (all 67,708 shares, Dist. Nos. 7412337 to 7480044)

## Impact Assessment

The market impact is minimal. The 67,708 shares are entirely locked in until September 30, 2027, meaning no additional float is added to the market in the near term. The preferential allotment to promoters may marginally increase promoter shareholding percentage. Trading members should note the new ISIN and scrip details for reference purposes.
