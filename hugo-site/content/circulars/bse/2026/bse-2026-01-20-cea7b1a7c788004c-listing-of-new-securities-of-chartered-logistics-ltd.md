## Summary

Chartered Logistics Ltd (Scrip Code: 531977) has listed 35,00,000 new equity shares of Rs. 1/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 6.80/- on the BSE. These shares were allotted to Non-Promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants on November 9, 2025. Trading in these securities will commence from Wednesday, January 21, 2026. The new shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 35,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 1/- each issued at premium of Rs. 6.80/-
- Issue price: Rs. 7.80/- per share
- Allotted to Non-Promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Date of allotment: November 9, 2025
- Trading commencement: January 21, 2026
- ISIN: INE558F01026
- Distinguished numbers: 123640001 to 127140000
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Partial lock-in applicable on certain shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must note the listing of new securities and ensure compliance with lock-in restrictions:

- 5,00,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 12,36,40,001 to 12,41,40,000) locked-in until August 1, 2026
- 10,00,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 12,41,40,001 to 12,51,40,000) locked-in until July 31, 2026
- 20,00,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 12,51,40,001 to 12,71,40,000) locked-in until August 1, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: November 9, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: January 21, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (10,00,000 shares)**: July 31, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (25,00,000 shares)**: August 1, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This listing has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains to a single company's capital structure change. The preferential allotment increases the total equity base of Chartered Logistics Ltd by 35,00,000 shares. Lock-in restrictions on the entire allotment until July-August 2026 will prevent immediate liquidity impact. Trading members handling this scrip should update their records with the new share count and monitor lock-in compliance for restricted shares.
