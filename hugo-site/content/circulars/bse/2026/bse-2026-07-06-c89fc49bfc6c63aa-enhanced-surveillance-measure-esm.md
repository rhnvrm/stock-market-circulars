## Summary

BSE has issued its periodic update to the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework, referencing a series of prior notices dating back to June 2023. Securities listed in Annexure I have met the criteria for inclusion under ESM and will attract a minimum 100% margin on all open and new positions effective July 08, 2026. Some securities already under ESM will move down to a lower Stage I, while newly qualifying securities may be placed under Stage II with tighter restrictions. A separate set of securities is being removed from the framework per Annexure II, and a consolidated list of all securities currently under ESM is provided in Annexure III.

## Key Points

- Securities in Annexure I have satisfied ESM inclusion criteria and will attract minimum 100% margin on all open positions (as on July 07, 2026) and new positions created from July 08, 2026.
- Certain securities continuing under ESM will be moved to a lower Stage I effective July 07, 2026.
- Securities qualifying under ESM will be shifted from the Rolling Settlement segment to the Trade-for-Trade (T4T) segment effective July 08, 2026.
- Securities shifting to Stage II will be placed under Trade-for-Trade with a 2% price band and Periodic Call Auction mechanism effective July 07, 2026.
- Securities exiting the ESM framework (Annexure II) will have their price band reinstated to the level applicable before ESM inclusion, unless subject to another surveillance measure.
- A consolidated list of all securities currently under the ESM framework is provided in Annexure III.
- ESM measures apply in conjunction with any other prevailing surveillance measures on the same securities.
- Placement under ESM is characterized as a market surveillance action, not an adverse judgment on the company.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced; this is a routine periodic application of the existing ESM framework established via earlier circulars (notice nos. 20230602-44, 20230718-46, 20240809-42, 20240920-63, 20241004-65, and 20250725-61).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must apply minimum 100% margin on all open and new positions in securities newly placed under ESM, effective July 08, 2026.
- Members must ensure securities qualifying under ESM are traded only in the Trade-for-Trade segment from July 08, 2026.
- Members must apply the 2% price band and Periodic Call Auction mechanism for Stage II securities from July 07, 2026.
- Queries or clarifications should be directed to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- July 07, 2026: Stage movement to Stage I for continuing ESM securities; Stage II securities placed under 2% price band and Periodic Call Auction; securities exit ESM framework per Annexure II.
- July 08, 2026: Minimum 100% margin applies on open positions (as on July 07, 2026) and new positions; qualifying securities shift from Rolling Settlement to Trade-for-Trade segment.

## Impact Assessment

This circular directly affects trading conditions—margin requirements, settlement segment, and price bands—for the specific securities listed in the annexures. Investors and traders holding or dealing in these scrips will face higher margin costs and reduced liquidity due to Trade-for-Trade settlement and tighter price bands. Securities exiting the framework will see restored price bands and settlement flexibility, improving trading conditions. The broader market impact is limited since ESM targets a defined subset of securities based on surveillance criteria rather than the market as a whole.
