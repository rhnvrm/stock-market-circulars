## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Varthana Finance Private Limited has fixed 11 March 2026 as the record date for part redemption of its Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) and payment of interest. From this effective date, trading in these debentures will occur at a reduced face value of Rs. 25,000 per debenture.

## Key Points

- Company: Varthana Finance Private Limited
- Instrument: NCD series VFPL-11.5%-27-9-26-PVT (ISIN: INE125T07303, Scrip Code: 976059)
- Record Date: 11 March 2026
- Purpose: Part redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Reduced Face Value: Rs. 25,000 per debenture
- Effective Date for reduced face value trading: 11 March 2026
- Settlement Number: DR-837/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notice under existing BSE debt market listing norms requiring disclosure of record dates and reduced face value for partially redeemed debentures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that from 11 March 2026, trades in Varthana Finance NCDs (ISIN: INE125T07303) must be executed at the reduced face value of Rs. 25,000 per debenture.
- Members should update their systems to reflect the new face value effective from the settlement DR-837/2025-2026.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 02 March 2026
- **Record Date:** 11 March 2026
- **Effective Date for Reduced Face Value Trading:** 11 March 2026
- **Settlement Reference:** DR-837/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited market impact as it pertains to a single NCD series of a private limited company in the debt segment. Holders of VFPL-11.5%-27-9-26-PVT debentures will receive a partial principal repayment along with accrued interest on the record date. Post-redemption, the debenture face value will stand reduced to Rs. 25,000 per unit. Market impact is confined to the specific instrument and its holders; no broader equity or market-wide implications are expected.
