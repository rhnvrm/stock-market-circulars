## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that dealings in a specific Non-Convertible Debenture (NCD) series issued by Aditya Birla Sun Life Insurance Company Limited will be suspended from 09 July 2026. This follows the company fixing a Record Date of 09 July 2026 for early redemption of the debentures via exercise of a call option, along with payment of interest.

## Key Points

- Security affected: ABSLIC-7.45%-25-7-31-PVT NCD (ISIN: INE951F08028, Scrip Code: 973339)
- Debenture Reference (DR): 668/2026-2027
- Purpose: Early Redemption of Debentures (Exercise of Call Option) and Payment of Interest
- Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the 'No Dealings From' date
- Notice issued by Listing Compliance and Operations department, Debt segment

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine operational notice under BSE's Corporate Actions category for the Debt segment, not a regulatory policy change.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE must refrain from dealing in the specified NCDs of Aditya Birla Sun Life Insurance Company Limited (ISIN INE951F08028) starting 09 July 2026, in line with the Record Date fixed for redemption and interest payment.

## Important Dates

- Record Date: 09 July 2026
- No Dealings From: 09 July 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and traders of this specific NCD series in the debt segment. There is no equity market impact, and no listed equity stocks are affected. This is a standard pre-redemption trading suspension to facilitate accurate record-keeping for interest payment and call option settlement.
