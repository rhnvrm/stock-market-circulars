## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in non-convertible debentures of AADHAR HOUSING FINANCE LIMITED (ISIN: INE538L07205, Code: 953266) effective January 8, 2026. The suspension is imposed to facilitate redemption of debentures and payment of interest, with the record date set for January 8, 2026. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debenture series.

## Key Points

- Debenture series affected: AHFL-9.6%-25-1-26-PVT (INE538L07205)
- BSE scrip code: 953266
- Record date: January 8, 2026
- No dealings permitted from: January 8, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Circular reference: DR-794/2025-2026
- Issued by: Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President â€“ Listing Compliance and Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action notification for debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in the specified debenture (INE538L07205) from January 8, 2026
- Members should update their systems to reflect the trading suspension
- No new trades should be executed in this debenture series from the suspension date

## Important Dates

- **January 1, 2026**: Circular issued
- **January 8, 2026**: Record date for redemption and interest payment
- **January 8, 2026**: Trading suspension effective date (no dealings permitted)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This affects only holders of a specific debenture series of AADHAR HOUSING FINANCE LIMITED. The suspension is temporary and related to routine corporate actions (redemption and interest payment).

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must ensure compliance systems prevent trading in the suspended debenture. Investors holding this debenture will receive redemption proceeds and interest payment based on the record date. This is a standard debt market operational procedure with no broader market implications.
