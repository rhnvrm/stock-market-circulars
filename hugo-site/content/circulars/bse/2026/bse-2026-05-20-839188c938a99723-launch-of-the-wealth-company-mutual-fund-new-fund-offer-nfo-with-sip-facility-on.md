## Summary

BSE has notified members of the launch of The Wealth Company Large & Mid Cap Fund NFO on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The NFO subscription window runs from May 21, 2026 to June 4, 2026, covering six scheme variants under regular and direct plans. The tentative allotment date is June 10, 2026.

## Key Points

- NFO period: May 21, 2026 to June 4, 2026 on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Six scheme variants offered: Regular and Direct Plans each with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- Tentative allotment date: June 10, 2026
- Lumpsum purchases extended till midnight on June 4, 2026
- SIP/XSIP registrations must be authenticated by clients by 3:00 PM on June 4, 2026
- Clear funds must be available in the ICCL account by 6:00 PM on June 10, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an operational notice for members participating in mutual fund transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clients initiate funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day; transactions without fund initiation will be cancelled and CC with 'N' flag reported to RTA/AMC
- Pending transactions with initiated payments will remain in system until 3:00 PM on the last day of funds pay-out to AMC or T+5 day, whichever is earlier
- Clear funds must be in the ICCL account by 6:00 PM on June 10, 2026 for allotment eligibility
- Cheque and OTM payments accepted during NFO period subject to clear fund receipt by 6:00 PM on June 10, 2026
- Switch transactions accepted during NFO period only if funds received prior to allotment date; switch-out funds received on or after allotment date will not be considered
- SIP/XSIP registrations must be client-authenticated by 3:00 PM on June 4, 2026

## Important Dates

- **May 21, 2026**: NFO subscription opens
- **June 4, 2026 (3:00 PM)**: Deadline for SIP/XSIP registration authentication by clients
- **June 4, 2026 (midnight)**: NFO closure for lumpsum purchases
- **June 10, 2026 (6:00 PM)**: Deadline for clear funds in ICCL account; tentative allotment date

## Impact Assessment

This notice primarily impacts BSE StAR MF platform members (distributors, brokers) who facilitate mutual fund transactions. Investors in the large & mid cap equity category gain access to a new fund from The Wealth Company AMC. The operational requirements around fund initiation timelines, payment modes, and switch transaction rules are standard NFO procedures. No systemic market impact is anticipated.
