## Summary

BSE has announced the live activities schedule for the Offer to Buy (OTB) – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for AAA Technologies Ltd (Symbol: AAATECH, Scrip Code: 543671). The offer window runs from Thursday, 23rd April 2026 to Thursday, 7th May 2026 at an offer price of Rs. 101.00 per share, covering up to 33,34,968 equity shares.

## Key Points

- **Company:** AAA Technologies Ltd | Symbol: AAATECH | ISIN: INE0D0U01013 | Scrip Code: 543671
- **Offer Size:** Up to 33,34,968 equity shares
- **Face Value:** Rs. 10.00 | **Offer Price:** Rs. 101.00 per share
- **Offer Period:** 23rd April 2026 to 7th May 2026
- Live OTB session timings: 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. daily (final day Give-up closes at 4:00 p.m.)
- Bids accepted from investors holding shares in demat form only
- Members must ensure UCC of investors is registered with correct PAN and Category in Exchange UCC database

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice operationalises an existing OTB framework under Exchange Notices 20150930-1 (dated 30 Sep 2015) and 20170203-29 (dated 3 Feb 2017).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must verify that Investor UCCs are registered and updated with correct PAN and Category in the Exchange UCC database before placing bids.
- Bids can only be placed for investors holding shares in demat form.
- Members and Custodians should note that End of Issue files on Extranet will not be generated if no securities are accepted.
- For Leased Line URL access configuration, refer to Exchange Circular No. 20170503-12.

## Impact Assessment

This notice is operationally relevant to Trading Members and Custodian Members who represent clients holding shares in AAA Technologies Ltd (AAATECH). The takeover offer at Rs. 101.00 per share provides an exit opportunity for existing shareholders. Participation is restricted to eligible categories including FIIs, Financial Institutions, Mutual Funds, Insurance Companies, other QIBs, NIBs, Companies, and Individuals. Members should prepare client UCCs and demat holdings in advance of the 23rd April 2026 opening. No systemic or market-wide regulatory impact is expected.
