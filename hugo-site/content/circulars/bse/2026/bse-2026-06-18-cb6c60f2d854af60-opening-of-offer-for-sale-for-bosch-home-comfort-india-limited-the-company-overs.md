## Summary

BSE has issued notice no. 20260618-14 confirming that Bosch Global Software Technologies Private Limited, the seller in the Offer for Sale (OFS) for Bosch Home Comfort India Limited, has exercised the Oversubscription Option. The total offer size has been increased from the base of 1,963,994 equity shares to 2,166,628 equity shares, representing 7.97% of the total issued and paid-up equity share capital.

## Key Points

- Seller: Bosch Global Software Technologies Private Limited
- Base Offer Size: 1,963,994 equity shares (7.22% of total paid-up equity capital)
- Oversubscription Option exercised: 202,634 equity shares (0.75% of total paid-up equity capital)
- Total Offer Size after exercise: 2,166,628 equity shares (7.97% of total paid-up equity capital)
- T Day (June 18, 2026): Open for non-Retail Investors only
- T+1 Day (June 19, 2026): Open for Retail Investors and non-Retail Investors carrying forward un-allotted bids
- This notice is in continuation of Exchange notice no. 20260617-17 dated June 17, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notice informing the market of the exercise of a pre-disclosed oversubscription option within the existing OFS framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Participating non-Retail Investors on T Day (June 18, 2026) should note the revised total offer size of 2,166,628 shares
- Retail Investors and non-Retail Investors with un-allotted bids may participate on T+1 Day (June 19, 2026) via the designated OFS window on the Stock Exchanges

## Important Dates

- June 17, 2026: Original OFS notice issued (Exchange notice no. 20260617-17)
- June 18, 2026 (T Day): OFS open for non-Retail Investors; oversubscription option exercise letter dated
- June 19, 2026 (T+1 Day): OFS open for Retail Investors and non-Retail Investors carrying forward un-allotted bids

## Impact Assessment

The exercise of the oversubscription option increases the total shares on offer by approximately 10.3% above the base size (from 1,963,994 to 2,166,628 shares). This expands supply in the OFS window, which may moderate price pressure for participating investors. The impact is confined to the duration of the OFS (June 18–19, 2026) and primarily affects institutional and retail investors bidding through the designated OFS window. No broader market or regulatory impact is expected.
