## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of MONEYBOXX FINANCE LIMITED effective February 6, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the no-dealings date.

## Key Points

- Company: MONEYBOXX FINANCE LIMITED
- ISIN: INE296Q07050
- Security Code: 975415
- Debenture Series: MFL-13.15%-21-2-26-PVT
- Record Date: February 6, 2026
- No Dealings From: February 6, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures and payment of interest
- Notice Reference: DR-815/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notice for debenture redemption and interest payment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures of MONEYBOXX FINANCE LIMITED from February 6, 2026
- Trading members must ensure compliance with the no-dealings restriction
- Investors holding these debentures should note the record date for redemption and interest payment eligibility

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** January 30, 2026
- **Record Date:** February 6, 2026
- **No Dealings From:** February 6, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Limited to holders and traders of the specific debenture series (MFL-13.15%-21-2-26-PVT). The suspension is temporary and related to scheduled corporate action.

**Investor Impact:** Debenture holders as of the record date will be eligible for interest payment and redemption proceeds. No trading will be permitted from February 6, 2026.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members must update their systems to prevent dealings in the affected debenture from the specified date. Standard corporate action processing applies.
