## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document (DRHP) of Craftech Paper Mills Limited filed on December 31, 2025 on its SME website. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and will remain available for public comments for at least 21 days from the notice date.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Craftech Paper Mills Limited on December 31, 2025
- Document complies with Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website for minimum 21 days
- Public invited to submit comments on the draft offer document
- Comments can be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard procedural notice for SME IPO draft offer document publication.

## Compliance Requirements

- The Draft Offer Document is hosted on BSE SME website as per regulatory requirements
- Public comments on the draft offer document must be submitted within 21 days from January 02, 2026
- Comments should be sent to email: equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- The document will remain accessible for at least 21 days for public review

## Important Dates

- **December 31, 2025**: Draft Offer Document filed by Craftech Paper Mills Limited
- **January 02, 2026**: Notice date and start of public comment period
- **January 23, 2026**: Deadline for public comments (21 days from notice date)
