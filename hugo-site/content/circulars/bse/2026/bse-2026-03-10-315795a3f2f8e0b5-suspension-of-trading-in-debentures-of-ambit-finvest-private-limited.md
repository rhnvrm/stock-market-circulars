## Summary

BSE has issued a notice directing trading members not to deal in the Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of Ambit Finvest Private Limited, as the company has fixed a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. The suspension is effective from 12 March 2026.

## Key Points

- Trading in Ambit Finvest Private Limited's NCD series AFPL-9.50%-27-3-26-PVT (ISIN: INE985V07058, Code: 974741) is suspended from 12 March 2026
- Record date fixed: 12 March 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- Notice reference: DR-838/2025-2026
- All trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the specified date

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice under existing BSE framework for debt segment corporate actions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in AFPL-9.50%-27-3-26-PVT (INE985V07058, Code: 974741) from 12 March 2026
- Members must take note of the suspension and ensure no transactions are executed in this security on or after the record date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 10 March 2026
- **Record Date / No Dealings From:** 12 March 2026
- **Purpose on Record Date:** Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and traders of this specific NCD series of Ambit Finvest Private Limited. The debenture (9.50% coupon, maturing 27 March 2026) is approaching maturity, and the suspension is a routine pre-redemption measure. Investors holding these debentures will receive redemption proceeds and final interest on or around the record date. No broader market impact is anticipated.
