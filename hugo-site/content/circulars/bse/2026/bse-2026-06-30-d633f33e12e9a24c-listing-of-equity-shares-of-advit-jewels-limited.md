## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the equity shares of Advit Jewels Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The exact date of listing and further details of the securities will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Advit Jewels Limited equity shares to be listed on BSE
- Scrip Code: 544803
- Trading Symbol: RAMBHAJO
- ISIN: INE1SJO01012
- Listing date to be announced via a separate notice
- Notice issued by DCS-Listing department

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing admission notice issued under BSE's DCS-Listing department.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to take note of the upcoming listing. No immediate compliance action is required; members should await the subsequent notice for the confirmed listing date and security details.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: June 30, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced via a separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This notice marks the admission of Advit Jewels Limited (RAMBHAJO) to BSE equity dealings following its IPO. Trading members and investors tracking new listings should watch for the follow-up notice confirming the listing date. Until then, no trading in this scrip is possible on BSE.
