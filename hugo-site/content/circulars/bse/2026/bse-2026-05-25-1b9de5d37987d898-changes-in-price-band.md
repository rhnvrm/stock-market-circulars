## Summary

BSE's Surveillance Department (DOSS) has revised the price bands for 18 equity scrips, effective May 26, 2026. The revised bands range from 2% to 20%, replacing the previously applicable limits for each security.

## Key Points

- 18 scrips have had their price bands revised with effect from May 26, 2026.
- Revised price bands range from a low of 2% (Ampvolts Ltd, Shantidoot Infra Services Ltd) to a high of 20% (Recode Studios Ltd).
- The revision is issued by BSE's Surveillance department (DOSS) and applies to the Equity segment.
- Members may seek clarification by writing to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must observe the revised price bands for all 18 listed scrips from May 26, 2026. Orders placed outside the revised bands will be rejected by the exchange systems. Members requiring clarification should contact bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** May 25, 2026
- **Effective Date:** May 26, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The price band revisions restrict intraday price movements for these 18 scrips to the newly assigned limits. Securities assigned tighter bands (2–5%) will experience reduced volatility exposure, which may indicate heightened surveillance activity or illiquidity concerns for those counters. Recode Studios Ltd receives the widest band at 20%, suggesting a relaxation of prior restrictions. Sun Pharma Advanced Research Company Ltd, a relatively higher-profile stock, is also included with a 10% band revision. Trading members should update their risk and order management systems accordingly before the market opens on May 26, 2026.
