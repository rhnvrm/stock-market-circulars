## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 6,78,33,700 new equity shares of STARLINEPS ENTERPRISES LIMITED (Scrip Code: 540492), issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis, are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Thursday, July 2, 2026.

## Key Points

- 6,78,33,700 equity shares of Rs. 1/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 5/- (issue price Rs. 6/- per share) to Non-Promoters on a preferential basis.
- Distinctive numbers: 363132001 to 430965700.
- These shares rank pari-passu with the existing equity shares of the company.
- ISIN: INE594W01042.
- Date of allotment: 23/03/2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is a routine listing notice for shares already allotted on a preferential basis.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must recognize the new shares as listed and tradable from the effective date, subject to the lock-in restriction noted below.

## Important Dates

- Date of allotment: 23 March 2026
- Listing/trading effective date: Thursday, 2 July 2026
- Lock-in expiry: 7 January 2027

## Impact Assessment

The listing increases the tradable equity base of Starlineps Enterprises Limited, though the entire 6.78 crore share tranche remains under lock-in until January 7, 2027, meaning no immediate increase in free-float supply or trading liquidity. The preferential allotment reflects capital raised from non-promoter investors, which could be relevant for assessing future ownership dilution and float once the lock-in expires.
