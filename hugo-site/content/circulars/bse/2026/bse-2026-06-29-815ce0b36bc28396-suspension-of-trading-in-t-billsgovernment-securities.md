## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members that two government securities — a 364-day Treasury Bill and a Government Security dated July 2, 2026 — will mature on their respective redemption dates. Trading in both instruments is suspended effective June 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- Two securities are suspended from trading effective June 30, 2026, due to maturity.
- Scrip Code 805087 (364TB20726, ISIN: IN002025Z146) is a 364-day T-bill maturing on redemption date.
- Scrip Code 800357 (GS02JUL26C, ISIN: IN000726C017) is a Government Security maturing on July 2, 2026.
- Trading members are advised not to deal in these securities from the effective date.
- Reference: DR-661/2026-2027.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice issued upon the scheduled maturity of government debt instruments listed on BSE's Debt segment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must cease trading in the following securities with effect from June 30, 2026:
- Scrip Code 805087 — 364TB20726 (ISIN: IN002025Z146)
- Scrip Code 800357 — GS02JUL26C (ISIN: IN000726C017)

Members should ensure no new trades are executed in these instruments on or after the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **June 29, 2026**: Notice issued.
- **June 30, 2026**: Suspension of trading effective date for both securities.
- **July 2, 2026**: Scheduled redemption/maturity date for GS02JUL26C (Scrip Code 800357).

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited and routine. The suspension affects only holders or would-be traders of these two specific government debt instruments. As the suspension is triggered by scheduled maturity, there is no broader market disruption. Affected members should confirm redemption proceeds are credited through the standard RBI/clearing process.
