## Summary

ICCL (Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd.) has issued a revised settlement schedule for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for ADC India Communications Limited. The bidding window ran from 02/04/2026 to 17/04/2026, with revised obligation download and settlement dates pushed to 21/04/2026 and 22/04/2026 respectively. This notice supersedes the earlier settlement calendar referenced in Notice No. 20260330-18.

## Key Points

- Settlement Type: Takeover; Settlement No.: 2026026
- Bid period: 02/04/2026 to 17/04/2026
- Revised Obligation Download Date: 21/04/2026
- Revised Settlement Date: 22/04/2026
- Demat shares must be delivered exclusively via the Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism available through both depositories
- Members/Custodians must use Market Type "(Takeover)" and Settlement No. 2026026 in the depository system
- Stamp duty charges (client-wise) are available in the MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file

## Regulatory Changes

This circular revises the previously announced settlement calendar (Notice No. 20260330-18). Members and custodians should refer to Notice No. 20200528-32 dated May 28, 2020 for guidelines on the Mechanism for Acquisition of Shares through Stock Exchange.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and Custodians must deliver demat shares only through the Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism
- Correct Market Type "(Takeover)" and Settlement No. 2026026 must be used in depository system entries
- Members must consult the MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for client-wise stamp duty charges
- Any clarifications should be directed to relationship managers or the ICCL Help Desk (022-69158590 / 45720490 / 45720690; iccl.helpdesk@icclindia.com)

## Important Dates

- Bid Start Date: 02/04/2026
- Bid End Date: 17/04/2026
- Revised Obligation Download Date: 21/04/2026
- Revised Settlement Date: 22/04/2026

## Impact Assessment

This revision affects brokers, members, and custodians participating in the ADC India Communications Limited takeover acquisition process. The revised settlement timeline (obligation download: 21/04/2026; settlement: 22/04/2026) requires prompt action to ensure compliant early pay-in of demat shares. Failure to use the correct market type or settlement number in depository entries could result in settlement failures. Impact is limited to participants in this specific takeover transaction.
