## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members that NCL Seccolor Ltd, an Exclusively Listed Company (ELC), will be removed from the BSE Dissemination Board effective Wednesday, March 11, 2026. This action is taken pursuant to SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD/DSA/CIR/P/2016/110 dated October 10, 2016. Following the removal, shares of NCL Seccolor Ltd will no longer be available for trading on the Dissemination Board.

## Key Points

- NCL Seccolor Ltd (CIN: U21029TG1986PTC007033) will be removed from BSE's Dissemination Board effective March 11, 2026
- The company is registered at 10-3-162, 5th Floor, NCL Pearl, Sarojini Devi Road, Secunderabad, Telangana – 500 026
- After removal, the shares of NCL Seccolor Ltd will not be available on the Dissemination Board of the Exchange
- The action is taken in accordance with SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD/DSA/CIR/P/2016/110 dated October 10, 2016

## Regulatory Changes

This circular implements the provisions of SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD/DSA/CIR/P/2016/110 dated October 10, 2016, which governs the framework for exclusively listed companies on dissemination boards. No new regulatory changes are introduced; this is an enforcement action under the existing framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease trading in shares of NCL Seccolor Ltd on the Dissemination Board from March 11, 2026 onwards
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the company secretary M. Ravindra at companysecretary@nclbuildtek.com or BSE's Mr. Parag Jain (Manager, Listing Compliance & Operations) at direct.listing@bseindia.com or Ph: 022-2272-8685

## Important Dates

- **March 10, 2026**: Notice issued by BSE
- **March 11, 2026 (Wednesday)**: Effective date of removal of NCL Seccolor Ltd from BSE Dissemination Board

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to investors and trading members who hold or trade in shares of NCL Seccolor Ltd through the BSE Dissemination Board. Post removal, no transactions in these shares will be facilitated on the Exchange's Dissemination Board. This is a routine administrative action with no broader market impact. Existing shareholders should note the change and take appropriate steps regarding their holdings.
