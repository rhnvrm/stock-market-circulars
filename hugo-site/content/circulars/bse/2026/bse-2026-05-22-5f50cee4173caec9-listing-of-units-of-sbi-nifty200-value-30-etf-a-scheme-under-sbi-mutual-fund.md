## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (No. 20260522-30) dated 22 May 2026 regarding the listing of units of SBI Nifty200 Value 30 ETF, a scheme under SBI Mutual Fund, on the Mutual Fund segment of BSE.

## Key Points

- Notice issued by BSE's DCS-Listing department on 22 May 2026.
- The scheme being listed is SBI Nifty200 Value 30 ETF under SBI Mutual Fund.
- Listed on the Mutual Fund segment of BSE.
- No attachments provided with this notice.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced. This is a standard listing announcement enabling trading of the ETF units on BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements stated for market participants beyond standard procedures applicable to ETF unit trading on the BSE Mutual Fund segment.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 22 May 2026
- **Listing Date:** 22 May 2026 (implied by notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notice with limited market-wide impact. It enables investors to buy and sell units of SBI Nifty200 Value 30 ETF on BSE, providing liquidity for the ETF. The ETF tracks the Nifty200 Value 30 Index, which focuses on value-oriented stocks within the Nifty 200 universe. Impact is confined to investors interested in this specific ETF product.
