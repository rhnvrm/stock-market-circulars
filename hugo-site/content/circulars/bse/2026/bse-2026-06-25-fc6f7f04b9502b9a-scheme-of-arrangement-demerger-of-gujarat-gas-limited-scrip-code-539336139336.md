## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Gujarat Gas Limited (Scrip Code 539336/139336), now renamed Gujarat Energy Limited, has fixed **2 July 2026** as the record date for the demerger of its Gas Transmission Business Undertaking into GSPL Transmission Limited, pursuant to a Composite Scheme of Arrangement approved by the Ministry of Corporate Affairs, New Delhi. Shareholders of Gujarat Gas Limited will receive new equity shares of GSPL Transmission Limited in exchange.

## Key Points

- **Record Date & Ex-Entitlement Date:** 02 July 2026 (Settlement No. DR-663/2026-2027)
- **Share Entitlement Ratio:** 1 fully paid-up equity share of Rs. 10/- each of GSPL Transmission Limited for every 3 fully paid-up equity shares of Rs. 2/- each held in Gujarat Gas Limited
- Gujarat Gas Limited has been renamed to **Gujarat Energy Limited**; name change on BSE effective **01 July 2026**
- Trading in Gujarat Gas Limited equity shares will be transferred from **'A' Group to 'T' Group** with effect from 02 July 2026
- The scrip will participate in a **Special Pre-open Session (SPOS)** on 02 July 2026 per SEBI Circular CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012
- Equity shares of the resulting company, GSPL Transmission Limited, will be listed on BSE Limited subject to compliance with requisite formalities

## Regulatory Changes

- Per SEBI Circular No. SEBI/Cir/ISD/1/2010 dated 02 November 2010: Gujarat Gas Limited shares move from 'A' Group to 'T' Group (trade-for-trade segment) effective 02 July 2026
- Per SEBI Circular No. CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 dated 20 January 2012: scrip included in Call Auction in Pre-open Session (Special Pre-open Session / SPOS) on 02 July 2026
- Demerger sanctioned under Composite Scheme of Arrangement (for Amalgamation) approved by the Ministry of Corporate Affairs, New Delhi

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the group transfer of Gujarat Gas Limited (now Gujarat Energy Limited) from 'A' to 'T' Group effective 02 July 2026
- Trading members must account for the SPOS participation on 02 July 2026; refer to Exchange Notice No. 20120216-29 dated 16 February 2012 for SPOS details
- Trading members should note the company name change to Gujarat Energy Limited effective 01 July 2026 (refer Notice No. 20260624-18 dated 24 June 2026)
- Shareholders need to hold Gujarat Gas Limited shares as of the record date (02 July 2026) to be eligible for GSPL Transmission Limited shares

## Impact Assessment

This is a high-impact corporate action for holders of Gujarat Gas Limited (BSE: 539336/139336). The demerger separates the gas transmission business into a new entity (GSPL Transmission Limited), which will be listed on BSE. Shareholders receive 1 new share of GSPL Transmission Limited for every 3 Gujarat Gas shares held — creating value through the spin-off of the infrastructure business.

The simultaneous group change from 'A' to 'T' (trade-for-trade) segment means trades in Gujarat Energy Limited (formerly Gujarat Gas) will require delivery-based settlement from 02 July 2026, increasing settlement risk and potentially reducing liquidity temporarily. The SPOS on 02 July 2026 will facilitate price discovery post-restructuring. Arbitrageurs and institutional investors should factor in the T-group restrictions when planning positions around the record date.
