## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that dealings in Rights Entitlements (REs) for renunciation of Consecutive Commodities Limited will commence on the Exchange with effect from Thursday, July 02, 2026. The REs will be tradable in dematerialized form under scrip ID CCDL-RE, ISIN INE187R20011.

## Key Points

- Trading in Rights Entitlements (REs) of Consecutive Commodities Limited begins July 02, 2026
- Scrip Code: 751126; Scrip ID: CCDL-RE; ISIN: INE187R20011
- Market Lot: 1; Group: R
- REs will be settled on a T+1 Rolling Settlement basis, trade-for-trade
- REs are tradable only in dematerialized form
- Trading members must ensure clients do not mistake REs for ordinary equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory change; this is an operational notice implementing existing BSE circulars (notice no. 20200305-21 dated March 05, 2020 and notice no. 20200518-34 dated May 18, 2020) governing trading of Rights Entitlements.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must include a disclaimer in contract notes/bills for RE purchases, clarifying that purchase of REs only grants the right to apply for the rights issue or renounce the REs before issue closure
- Members must inform clients that unsubscribed and unrenounced REs will lapse and be extinguished after the Issue Closing Date
- Members should refer to prior BSE notices (20200305-21 and 20200518-34) for detailed operational guidance

## Important Dates

- Trading period for Renunciation of REs: Thursday, July 02, 2026 to Monday, July 20, 2026
- Rights Issue opens: July 02, 2026
- Rights Issue closes: July 24, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This notice is procedural and primarily impacts shareholders and traders of Consecutive Commodities Limited who are eligible to trade or renounce their rights entitlements. It has limited relevance to the broader market but is important for affected investors to act within the specified renunciation window to avoid lapse of their entitlements.
