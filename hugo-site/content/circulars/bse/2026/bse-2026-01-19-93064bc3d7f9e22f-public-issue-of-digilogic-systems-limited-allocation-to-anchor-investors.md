## Summary

BSE has announced the allocation of shares to anchor investors for the public issue of Digilogic Systems Limited. This represents the anchor book portion of the IPO, where qualified institutional buyers participate before the public offering opens.

## Key Points

- Digilogic Systems Limited has allocated shares to anchor investors as part of its IPO process
- Anchor investor allocation typically occurs one day before the IPO opens to the public
- The allocation provides institutional validation and price discovery for the public issue
- This is a standard step in the IPO listing process on BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard IPO process notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- BSE will publish the details of anchor investor allocation as per SEBI IPO guidelines
- The company must disclose anchor investor names, allocation amounts, and pricing details
- Information must be made available to public investors before the issue opens

## Important Dates

- Anchor investor allocation date: January 19, 2026
- Public issue opening date would follow shortly after anchor allocation

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - Anchor investor participation indicates institutional confidence in the IPO and helps establish price discovery for retail investors.

**Investor Impact**: Medium - Retail investors gain insights into institutional demand and pricing through anchor allocation details, helping inform their investment decisions.

**Operational Impact**: Low - Standard IPO process notification for market participants and investors tracking the Digilogic Systems Limited public issue.
