## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading for three Treasury Bills (T-Bills) that are scheduled to mature in February 2026. Trading Members are advised not to deal with these securities effective February 10, 2026.

## Key Points

- Three T-Bills will be suspended from trading due to upcoming maturity
- Suspension effective from February 10, 2026
- Notice issued under reference DR-817/2025-2026
- Trading Members must cease dealing in these specific T-Bills

## Affected Securities

| Scrip Code | ISIN | Particulars |
|------------|------|-------------|
| 805104 | IN002025Y206 | 182TB12226 |
| 805027 | IN002024Z446 | 364TB12226 |
| 805142 | IN002025X331 | 91TB120226 |

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational procedure for maturing government securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must not execute any trades in the three specified T-Bills from February 10, 2026
- Members should update their systems to reflect the trading suspension
- No new orders should be accepted for these securities

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: February 09, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date**: February 10, 2026
- **Redemption Date**: February 2026 (specific dates for each T-Bill maturity)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative action with limited market impact. The suspension affects only debt market participants holding or trading these specific T-Bills. Investors holding these securities will receive redemption proceeds upon maturity. The action is procedural and does not indicate any market disruption or regulatory concern.
