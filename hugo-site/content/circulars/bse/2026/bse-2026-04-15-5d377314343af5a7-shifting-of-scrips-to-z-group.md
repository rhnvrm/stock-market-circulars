## Summary

BSE has issued a notice that five scrips will be transferred to the 'Z' group with effect from April 27, 2026, due to non-compliance with Regulation 33 of the SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015 for two consecutive quarters — September 2025 and December 2025. The action is in accordance with SEBI Master Circular No. HO/49/14/14(7)2025-CFD-POD2/I/3762/2026 (last updated January 30, 2026) regarding penal actions for non-compliance.

## Key Points

- Five companies will be shifted to the 'Z' group effective **April 27, 2026** if they fail to comply by **April 22, 2026**.
- The non-compliance pertains to **Regulation 33 of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015** (submission of financial results) for Q2 and Q3 FY2025-26.
- An additional **28+ companies** already in the 'Z' group are also non-compliant for the same regulation and same quarters.
- The penal action is governed by SEBI's Standard Operating Procedure for suspension and revocation of trading of specified securities.
- Z group classification significantly restricts trading — shares can only be traded in the trade-to-trade (T2T) segment with no intraday positions allowed.

## Regulatory Changes

Action is taken under:
- **SEBI Master Circular No. HO/49/14/14(7)2025-CFD-POD2/I/3762/2026** issued July 11, 2023, last updated January 30, 2026 — Chapter VII(A): Penal Actions for Non-Compliance.
- **Regulation 33 of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015** — mandating timely submission of financial results by listed entities.

## Compliance Requirements

The five companies being shifted must submit their outstanding financial results for **September 2025 and December 2025 quarters** on or before **April 22, 2026** to avoid being moved to the 'Z' group. Failure to do so will result in automatic transfer to 'Z' group on April 27, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **April 15, 2026** — Notice date; circular issued by BSE.
- **April 22, 2026** — Deadline for the five companies to submit outstanding financial results and avoid Z group transfer.
- **April 27, 2026** — Effective date of Z group transfer for non-compliant companies.

## Impact Assessment

Transfer to the 'Z' group carries serious market consequences for the affected companies and their investors:

- **Trading restrictions**: Z group securities are traded only in the trade-to-trade segment, prohibiting intraday trading and netting of positions.
- **Liquidity risk**: Reduced market participation and increased price impact due to settlement obligations on every trade.
- **Investor sentiment**: Z group classification signals regulatory concern, often triggering sell-offs and reduced institutional interest.
- **Five actively listed companies** face imminent transfer if they do not file their September 2025 and December 2025 quarterly financial results by April 22, 2026.
- The **28+ companies already in Z group** for the same non-compliance underscore a broader systemic issue with financial result disclosure among smaller and mid-cap listed entities.
- Retail investors holding these scrips should be aware of the potential liquidity constraints and heightened regulatory scrutiny.
