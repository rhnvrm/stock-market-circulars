## Summary

BSE has issued an update to its Additional Surveillance Measure (ASM) framework applicable to companies undergoing Insolvency Resolution Process (IRP) under the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code (IBC). Pursuant to earlier notices dated June 08, 2018, May 20, 2022, February 10, 2023 and September 05, 2025, the Exchange has identified securities newly satisfying the IBC-related surveillance criteria (Annexure I) and securities exiting the framework (Annexure II), both effective from July 10, 2026. A consolidated list of all securities currently under the framework is provided in Annexure III.

## Key Points

- Securities listed in Annexure I have met the criteria for Additional Surveillance Measure under the IBC framework and will be placed under it effective July 10, 2026.
- Securities listed in Annexure II are moving out of the IBC surveillance framework effective July 10, 2026.
- Annexure III provides the consolidated, updated list of all securities currently under this framework.
- Securities under this framework are reviewed periodically.
- This surveillance measure operates in conjunction with all other applicable surveillance frameworks imposed by the Exchange.
- Placement under this framework is purely a market surveillance action and should not be interpreted as an adverse judgment on the company or entity.
- When a scrip exits the framework, its price band reverts to the band applicable prior to shortlisting, unless it remains subject to another surveillance framework, in which case that framework's price band prevails.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced; this is a periodic update under the existing IBC-related Additional Surveillance Measure framework established via prior Exchange notices dated June 08, 2018, May 20, 2022, February 10, 2023, and September 05, 2025.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must note the updated list of securities under the IBC surveillance framework and apply applicable trading restrictions and price bands accordingly. Queries can be directed to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **July 09, 2026**: Notice issued.
- **July 10, 2026**: Effective date for securities entering (Annexure I) and exiting (Annexure II) the IBC surveillance framework.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to the specific securities named in the annexures, which will face or be relieved of trading restrictions and price band adjustments. Market participants trading in IBC-affected companies should review the annexures to confirm applicable price bands and surveillance status. The overall market impact is low, as this is a routine, periodic administrative update rather than a new policy change.
