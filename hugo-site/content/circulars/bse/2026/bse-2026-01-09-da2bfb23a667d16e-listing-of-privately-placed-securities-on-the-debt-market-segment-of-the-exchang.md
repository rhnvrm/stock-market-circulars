## Summary

BSE has listed new privately placed commercial papers on its Debt Segment effective January 9, 2026. The circular details fresh listings of commercial papers from five companies (Birla Group Holdings Private Limited, IGH Holdings Private Limited, SG Finserve Limited, Julius Baer Capital India Private Limited, and Godrej Finance Limited) and mentions further listing of Reliance Retail Ventures Limited securities. All securities will trade in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- Five new commercial paper issues admitted to trading on BSE Debt Segment
- All securities trade in dematerialized form under specified ISIN numbers
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Total fresh listings involve 12,100 securities worth Rs. 605 crores (face value)
- Interest rates range from 6.45% to 7.40% p.a.
- Maturity periods range from 49 days (February 2026) to 364 days (January 2027)
