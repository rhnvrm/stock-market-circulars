## Summary

BSE has notified the listing and permission to trade for 9,37,96,508 new equity shares of COFORGE LIMITED (Scrip Code: 532541, ISIN: INE591G01025) issued on a preferential basis to non-promoters at an issue price of Rs. 1815.91 per share (face value Rs. 2 + premium Rs. 1813.91). Trading in these new securities commences on Monday, June 1, 2026.

## Key Points

- 9,37,96,508 equity shares of Rs. 2/- face value issued at a premium of Rs. 1813.91 per share to non-promoters on preferential basis
- Issue price: Rs. 1815.91 per share; aggregate consideration approximately Rs. 17,032 crore
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of COFORGE LIMITED
- Distribution numbers: 335850619 to 429647126
- Date of allotment: 23 April 2026
- Lock-in applicable on all 9,37,96,508 shares until 30 November 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification following completion of a preferential allotment under applicable SEBI and BSE regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to permit trading in the newly listed securities from June 1, 2026 onwards
- The lock-in restriction on all 9,37,96,508 shares must be observed until 30 November 2026; these shares are not freely tradeable until the lock-in expires

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** 23 April 2026
- **Effective Date for Trading:** 1 June 2026 (Monday)
- **Lock-in Expiry Date:** 30 November 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds approximately 9.37 crore shares to COFORGE LIMITED's tradeable equity base, representing a substantial preferential allotment. However, since all newly issued shares are subject to a lock-in until 30 November 2026, the immediate free-float impact is nil. Post lock-in expiry, the release of these shares into the market could exert selling pressure. Investors and traders should monitor the lock-in schedule closely. The aggregate issue size of ~Rs. 17,032 crore signals a significant capital-raising event, potentially for acquisitions, expansion, or debt reduction.
