## Summary

BSE has listed 34,41,417 equity shares of Ecoboard Industries Ltd. (Scrip Code: 523732, ISIN: INE866A01016), issued on a preferential basis to Promoters and Non-Promoters at an issue price of Rs. 33.50/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- + premium Rs. 23.50/-). These shares are permitted to trade on BSE with effect from Wednesday, May 27, 2026.

## Key Points

- **Total new shares listed:** 34,41,417 equity shares
- **Issue price:** Rs. 33.50/- per share (face value Rs. 10/-, premium Rs. 23.50/-)
- **Date of allotment:** January 10, 2026
- **Distribution numbers:** 22965324 to 26406740
- **ISIN:** INE866A01016
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Issued to both Promoters and Non-Promoters on a preferential basis

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice permitting newly allotted preferential shares to trade on the Exchange.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed that these securities are now listed and permitted to trade from May 27, 2026. Lock-in restrictions must be observed as per the details below — trading of locked-in shares is not permitted until the respective lock-in expiry dates.

## Important Dates

- **Date of allotment:** January 10, 2026
- **Trading commencement date:** May 27, 2026
- **Lock-in expiry (16,25,000 shares – dist. nos. 22965324 to 24590323):** November 30, 2027
- **Lock-in expiry (2,73,880 shares – dist. nos. 24590324 to 24814203):** November 30, 2026
- **Lock-in expiry (15,42,537 shares – dist. nos. 24814204 to 26406740):** November 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing of 34,41,417 new shares represents a capital expansion for Ecoboard Industries Ltd. via preferential allotment. Existing shareholders will experience dilution. The majority of the newly listed shares (approximately 91%) are subject to lock-in periods ranging from approximately 6 to 18 months from the trading commencement date, limiting near-term selling pressure. Only free shares (dist. nos. not under lock-in) will be freely tradeable from May 27, 2026. Market impact is expected to be minimal given the lock-in constraints on most of the allotted shares.
