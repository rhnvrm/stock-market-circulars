## Summary

BSE has listed 7,40,700 new equity shares of Ishan Dyes and Chemicals Limited (Scrip Code: 531109, ISIN: INE561M01018), issued to promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to the conversion of warrants. These shares are admitted for trading on the Exchange with effect from Monday, June 29, 2026.

## Key Points

- 7,40,700 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 53/- (issue price: Rs. 63/-)
- Issued to promoters on a preferential basis via conversion of warrants
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 26563698 to 27304397
- Date of allotment: March 28, 2026
- Effective trading date: June 29, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine corporate action circular informing trading members of newly listed securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed that the new securities are listed and permitted to trade from June 29, 2026. The newly issued shares are subject to a lock-in restriction until January 14, 2028.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** March 28, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** June 29, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry:** January 14, 2028

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 7,40,700 shares to the tradeable float of Ishan Dyes and Chemicals Limited, though all newly issued shares are under lock-in until January 2028 and will not be available for market trading in the near term. The issue was made to promoters at Rs. 63/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- plus premium of Rs. 53/-). Overall market impact is minimal given the small issue size and lock-in restriction.
