## Summary

BSE has published the list of securities under Demat Auction for Settlement No. 798 dated January 16, 2026. The auction covers 100 scrips across various sectors with quantities ranging from 1 share to 158,001 shares. This auction is conducted to complete delivery obligations where sellers have failed to deliver securities.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 798
- Date: January 16, 2026
- Total number of scrips: 100 securities listed
- Quantities range from 1 share (multiple scrips) to 158,001 shares (Tilak Ventures)
- Includes major stocks like Cipla (50 shares), Kotak Mahindra (320 shares), ONGC (20 shares), Tata Steel (5 shares)
- Also includes smaller companies and mutual fund units

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine operational circular for demat auction settlement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Brokers with shortages in the listed securities must participate in the auction
- Settlement must be completed as per BSE auction procedures
- Participants must ensure sufficient funds/securities for auction settlement
- Non-delivery obligations will be closed out through this auction mechanism

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: January 16, 2026
- Auction settlement to be completed as per BSE's standard auction timeline

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - The auction affects 100 securities with varying liquidity levels. High-quantity items like Tilak Ventures (158,001 shares) and Wardwizard (32,300 shares) may see price volatility during auction.

**Broker Impact:** Brokers with delivery shortages must arrange securities or face auction buy-in at potentially higher prices, impacting their operational costs.

**Investor Impact:** Clients whose brokers have failed to deliver may face delays in receiving securities until auction settlement is completed.
