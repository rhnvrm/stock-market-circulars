## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted for trading additional equity shares issued by 12 companies under their respective Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) or Employee Stock Ownership Schemes (ESOS). The new securities are effective for trading from **Thursday, February 26, 2026**. A combined total of approximately **1,673,075 new shares** have been listed across all 12 companies. None of the newly listed shares are under any lock-in period.

## Key Points

- 12 companies have listed fresh equity shares arising from ESOP/ESOS exercises
- All new securities are admitted for trading effective **February 26, 2026**
- No shares are subject to any lock-in restrictions
- Largest issuances by volume: Federal Bank (418,906 shares), ICICI Bank (369,765 shares), UNO Minda (364,806 shares)
- Smallest issuances by volume: Centum Electronics (4,833 shares), CreditAccess Grameen (11,675 shares)
- Face values vary: ₹1 (Marico), ₹2 (EPL, Federal Bank, ICICI Bank, Poonawalla Fincorp, UNO Minda), ₹10 (all others)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine administrative notice under existing ESOP/ESOS listing frameworks.

## Compliance Requirements

- All market participants are requested to note the updated ISIN-wise distribution number ranges for each company
- Trading systems should recognize the new share ranges as valid for settlement
- No specific compliance action required from investors or brokers beyond standard awareness
