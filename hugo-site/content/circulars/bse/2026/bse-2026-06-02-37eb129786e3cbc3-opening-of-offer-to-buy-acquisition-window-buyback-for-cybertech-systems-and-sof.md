## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members and custodians of the opening of an Offer to Buy (Acquisition Window) for the buyback of equity shares of Cybertech Systems and Software Limited. The buyback is conducted through the tender offer route using the stock exchange mechanism as per SEBI guidelines. The acquisition window is open from June 4, 2026 to June 10, 2026.

## Key Points

- Cybertech Systems and Software Limited proposes to buy back up to **8,50,000 (Eight Lakh Fifty Thousand)** fully paid-up equity shares
- **Face value:** Rs. 10/- per share
- **Buyback price:** Rs. 170/- per share (payable in cash)
- **Total buyback size:** Up to Rs. 14,45,00,000 (Rs. 14.45 Crores), excluding transaction costs
- Buyback represents **9.81%** of aggregate paid-up capital and free reserves (standalone) and **7.55%** (consolidated) as per FY ended March 31, 2026
- Buyback is within the statutory limit of 10% of total paid-up equity share capital and free reserves
- Offer made to equity shareholders/beneficial owners on a **proportionate basis** through tender offer route
- A Letter of Offer is attached to the notice

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The buyback is governed by existing SEBI circulars:
- SEBI circular no. CIR/CFD/POLICYCELL/1/2015 dated April 13, 2015
- SEBI circular no. CFD/DCR2/CIR/P/2016/131 dated December 09, 2016
- BSE notices: 20170210-16, 20190424-35, 20200528-32, 20201102-43, 20210825-62 (Revised Guidelines)

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and custodians must note and follow the revised guidelines for acquisition of shares through the stock exchange mechanism under Tender-Offers (Buyback)
- Market participants should refer to BSE website (www.bseindia.com) for full details of the Offer to Buy
- Participants must process tendering of shares as per SEBI/BSE guidelines for the buyback window

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Notice Date | June 2, 2026 |
| Record Date | Friday, May 29, 2026 |
| Acquisition Window Opens | Thursday, June 4, 2026 |
| Acquisition Window Closes | Wednesday, June 10, 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

This buyback affects existing equity shareholders and beneficial owners of Cybertech Systems and Software Limited as of the record date (May 29, 2026). Eligible shareholders can tender their shares at Rs. 170/- per share, which represents a premium over face value of Rs. 10/-. The buyback size of Rs. 14.45 Crores is within SEBI's 10% statutory threshold, limiting broader market impact. Trading members and custodians must facilitate the tender offer process during the June 4–10 acquisition window. Impact is limited to shareholders of this specific company.
