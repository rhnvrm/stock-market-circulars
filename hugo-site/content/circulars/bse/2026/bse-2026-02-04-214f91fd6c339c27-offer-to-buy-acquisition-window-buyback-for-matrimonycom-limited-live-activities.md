## Summary

BSE has announced the live activities schedule for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (BuyBack) of MATRIMONY.COM LIMITED shares. The offer will be conducted from Thursday, February 5, 2026 to Wednesday, February 11, 2026 through the iBBS platform. The company will buy back up to 8,93,129 equity shares at Rs. 655 per share.

## Key Points

- **Security Details**: MATRIMONY.COM LIMITED (Symbol: MATRIMONY, ISIN: INE866R01028, Scrip Code: 540704)
- **Offer Size**: Up to 8,93,129 equity shares
- **Offer Price**: Rs. 655 per share
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5.00
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Opening Date**: Thursday, February 5, 2026
- **Closing Date**: Wednesday, February 11, 2026
- **Session Timings**: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM (bidding), last day take-up extended to 4:00 PM

This buyback offer provides shareholders of MATRIMONY.COM LIMITED an opportunity to tender their shares at Rs. 655 per share. Trading members and custodians need to ensure system readiness and proper UCC registration for participating investors. The offer size of 8,93,129 shares represents the maximum buyback quantity, and participation is restricted to investors holding shares in demat form only.
