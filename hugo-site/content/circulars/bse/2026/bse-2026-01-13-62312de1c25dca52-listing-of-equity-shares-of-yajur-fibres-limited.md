## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Yajur Fibres Limited will be listed and admitted to trading on the Exchange's SME segment. The company has been assigned scrip code 544676 and symbol YAJUR with ISIN number INE0CNJ01010. The exact listing date and additional security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Yajur Fibres Limited
- Scrip Code: 544676
- Trading Symbol: YAJUR
- ISIN: INE0CNJ01010
- Segment: SME
- Listing date to be announced separately

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed to note the listing details for future trading operations once the listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: January 13, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement with minimal immediate market impact. Trading members should prepare for the upcoming listing of YAJUR shares on the SME platform. The actual market impact will depend on the company's business fundamentals and market conditions at the time of listing. No immediate action required until the listing date is formally announced.
