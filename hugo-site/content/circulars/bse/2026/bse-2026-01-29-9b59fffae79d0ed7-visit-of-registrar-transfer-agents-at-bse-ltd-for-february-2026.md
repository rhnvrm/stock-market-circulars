## Summary

BSE has published the schedule of Registrar and Transfer Agents (RTAs) visits to BSE Ltd. for February 2026. The circular contains a list of 51+ companies serviced by Adroit Corporate Services Pvt. Ltd., identifying their scrip codes and company names.

## Key Points

- RTA visits scheduled for February 2026 at BSE Ltd.
- Adroit Corporate Services Pvt. Ltd. is the RTA for all listed companies in this circular
- List includes 51+ companies across various sectors including manufacturing, finance, real estate, and technology
- Companies range from established entities like Hind Rectifiers Ltd (504036) to specialized firms like Securekloud Technologies Ltd (512161)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for listed companies or market participants. This is an informational circular regarding RTA visit schedules.

## Important Dates

- Visit Period: February 2026
- Specific dates not provided in the extracted content

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Negligible - This is an administrative circular with no direct market impact.

**Operational Impact:** Low - Investors and companies serviced by Adroit Corporate Services Pvt. Ltd. may benefit from knowing the RTA visit schedule for any shareholder service requirements.

**Stakeholder Impact:** The circular primarily serves as a reference for shareholders of the listed companies who may need to interact with their RTA during the scheduled visit period at BSE premises.
