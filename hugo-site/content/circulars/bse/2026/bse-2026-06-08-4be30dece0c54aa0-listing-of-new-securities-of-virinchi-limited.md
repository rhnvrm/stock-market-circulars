## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 8,50,000 new equity shares of Virinchi Limited (Scrip Code: 532372, ISIN: INE539B01017) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Tuesday, June 9, 2026. These shares were issued to promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants at an issue price of Rs. 28/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- + premium Rs. 18/-).

## Key Points

- 8,50,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 18/- (total issue price Rs. 28/-) to promoters via preferential allotment
- Allotment made pursuant to conversion of warrants
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 108796897 to 109646896
- Date of allotment: April 10, 2026
- Lock-in applicable on all 8,50,000 shares until January 31, 2028

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for newly allotted equity shares under a previously approved preferential issue.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to permit trading in these new securities from June 9, 2026. The shares are subject to lock-in and cannot be traded by the allottee (promoters) until January 31, 2028.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** April 10, 2026
- **Trading commences:** June 9, 2026 (Tuesday)
- **Lock-in expiry:** January 31, 2028

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The 8,50,000 shares issued to promoters are entirely under lock-in until January 2028, so no additional float is added to the market at this time. The listing is a procedural step following the warrant conversion. Existing shareholders are unaffected as new shares rank pari-passu.
