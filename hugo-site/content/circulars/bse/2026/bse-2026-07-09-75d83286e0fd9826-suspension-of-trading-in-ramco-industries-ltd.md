## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that Ramco Industries Ltd has fixed a Record Date for the purpose of payment of interest and early redemption of a specific series of its Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs). Trading members have been advised not to deal in these debentures around the record date.

## Key Points

- The suspension applies to Ramco Industries Ltd's Debt segment listing, specifically the NCD series RMIL-7.60%-7-2-28-PVT.
- Scrip Code: 976391; ISIN: INE614A07033.
- Purpose: Payment of Interest and Early Redemption of the Debentures.
- Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures as per the exchange notice.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a routine listing operations notice under BSE's Corporate Actions category for the Debt segment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must refrain from dealing in the specified NCD (ISIN INE614A07033) of Ramco Industries Ltd around the record date and ex-date to ensure accurate settlement of interest payment and early redemption.

## Important Dates

- Record Date / Ex Date: 23 July 2026
- Settlement Date (DR): 678/2026-2027 (settlement cycle reference)
- Notice Date: 09 July 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and traders of the specific NCD series (ISIN INE614A07033) of Ramco Industries Ltd. Trading suspension ensures clean record-keeping for interest payment and early redemption processing, with no impact on the company's equity shares or other listed instruments.
