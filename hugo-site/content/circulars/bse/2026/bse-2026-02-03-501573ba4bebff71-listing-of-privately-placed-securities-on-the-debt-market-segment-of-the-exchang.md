## Summary

BSE has listed commercial papers issued on private placement basis by Bajaj Finance Limited (Rs. 300 crore), Network 18 Media & Investments Limited (Rs. 300 crore), Poonawalla Fincorp Limited (Rs. 200 crore), Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited (Rs. 325 crore), and 360 ONE Alternates Asset Management Limited (Rs. 5 crore). All securities are admitted to trading on the BSE Debt Segment effective February 3, 2026.

## Key Points

- Fresh listings of commercial papers from 4 companies totaling Rs. 1,125 crore
- Further listing from 360 ONE Alternates for Rs. 5 crore
- All securities trade in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size is 1 paise
- Interest rates range from 7.47% to 8.45% p.a.
- Maturity periods range from 36 days to 365 days
- All commercial papers carry highest credit ratings (A1+)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading must be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- For clarifications, contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: February 3, 2026
- **Bajaj Finance CP Redemption**: May 4, 2026
- **Network 18 CP Redemption**: May 5, 2026
- **Poonawalla Fincorp CP Redemption**: February 2, 2027
- **Nuvama Wealth CP Redemption**: March 10, 2026
- **360 ONE Alternates CP Redemption**: January 19, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal - routine debt market listing activity

**Operational Impact**: Standard trading procedures apply with no changes to existing debt market operations

**Investor Impact**: Provides additional debt investment opportunities for institutional and qualified investors in short-term commercial papers from reputed financial institutions and corporates
