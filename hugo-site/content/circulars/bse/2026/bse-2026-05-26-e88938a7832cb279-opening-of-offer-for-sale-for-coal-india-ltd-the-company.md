## Summary

BSE has issued a notice regarding the Opening of Offer for Sale (OFS) for Coal India Limited (CIL), wherein the President of India (acting through the Ministry of Coal, Government of India) proposes to divest up to 6,16,27,283 equity shares representing 1.00% of the total paid-up equity share capital of the Company at a floor price of Rs. 412/- per share. The offer includes an oversubscription option for an additional 1.00% stake and a limited employee offer of 25,000 shares.

## Key Points

- **Seller:** President of India (Ministry of Coal, Government of India)
- **Company:** Coal India Limited (COALINDIA)
- **Shares on Offer:** Up to 6,16,27,283 equity shares (1.00% of total paid-up capital), face value Rs. 10/-
- **Oversubscription Option:** Additional 6,16,27,283 equity shares (1.00% of total paid-up capital)
- **Employee Offer:** Up to 25,000 equity shares; eligible employees may apply up to Rs. 5,00,000/-
- **Floor Price:** Rs. 412/- per equity share
- **Designated Stock Exchange:** BSE Limited
- **OFS is conducted on both BSE and NSE**

## Regulatory Changes

This OFS is conducted in accordance with SEBI circulars:
- SEBI/HO/MRD/DOPI/CIR/P/2018/159 dated December 28, 2018 – Review of OFS through Stock Exchange Mechanism
- SEBI/HO/MRD/MRD-PoD-3/P/CIR/2023/10 dated January 10, 2023 – Comprehensive Framework on OFS through Stock Exchange Mechanism
- SEBI/HO/MRD/MRD-PoD-3/P/CIR/2024/82 dated June 14, 2024 – Framework for OFS of Shares to Employees through Stock Exchange Mechanism

BSE internal guidelines referenced: Notices 20190118-43, 20200701-27, 20230127-14, and 20240701-19.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members and Custodians must follow BSE's revised OFS bidding guidelines referenced in the notice.
- Non-Retail Investors may bid on T Day (May 27, 2026) and optionally carry forward un-allotted bids to T+1 Day.
- Retail Investors and Employees may bid only on T+1 Day (May 29, 2026).
- Eligible Employees must apply within the Rs. 5,00,000/- limit per the OFS Guidelines and subject to approval from the competent authority.
- Market participants should monitor the BSE website (www.bseindia.com) for further details and dissemination of OFS information.

## Important Dates

## Impact Assessment

This OFS represents a notable government disinvestment event in Coal India Ltd, one of India's largest public sector undertakings. With up to 2% of the total paid-up equity capital available (base + oversubscription option), this offering is likely to generate significant market interest from both institutional and retail investors. The floor price of Rs. 412/- per share provides the market reference level. The two-day structure (T Day for non-retail, T+1 for retail and employees) is standard under the SEBI OFS framework. Investors holding or tracking COALINDIA should be aware of potential short-term price pressure typical during OFS events and monitor the BSE website for allotment details and final pricing information.
