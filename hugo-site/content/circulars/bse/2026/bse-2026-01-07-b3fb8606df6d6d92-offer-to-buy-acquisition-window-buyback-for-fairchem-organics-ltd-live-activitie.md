## Summary

BSE has announced the schedule for the Offer to Buy - Acquisition Window (Buyback) for FAIRCHEM ORGANICS LTD (Scrip Code: 543252, Symbol: FAIRCHEMOR, ISIN: INE0DNW01011). The buyback window will be open from Thursday, January 8, 2026, to Wednesday, January 14, 2026. The company will buy back up to 4,25,000 equity shares at an offer price of Rs.800 per share (face value Rs.10). Trading Members and Custodian Members can participate through the iBBS platform and RTRMS systems.

## Key Points

- Security: FAIRCHEM ORGANICS LTD (FAIRCHEMOR)
- Scrip Code: 543252
- ISIN: INE0DNW01011
- Offer Size: Up to 4,25,000 equity shares
- Offer Price: Rs.800 per share
- Face Value: Rs.10.00
- Market Lot: 1
- Opening Date: Thursday, January 8, 2026
- Closing Date: Wednesday, January 14, 2026
- Session Timings: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM (9:15 AM to 4:00 PM for take-up on last day)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This circular provides operational instructions for an existing Offer to Buy (OTB) framework as per Exchange Notices no. 20150930-1 (September 30, 2015) and 20170203-29 (February 3, 2017).

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members**: Must ensure UCC of the investor is registered and/or updated with correct PAN Number and Category in the Exchange UCC database
- **Eligible Participants**: Trading Members can bid only for investors having shares in demat form
- **Bidding Categories**: FII (Foreign Institutional Buyer), FI (Financial Institution), MF (Mutual Fund), IC (Insurance Companies), OTH (Other QIBs), NOH (Other), CO (Company), IND (Individual)
- **Platform Access**: Members must use iBBS platform (Internet: https://ibbs.bseindia.com/ or Leased Line: https://ibbsll.bseindia.com/) and RTRMS (Internet: https://rtrms.bseindia.com/ or Leased Line: https://rtrmsll.bseindia.com/)
- **File Access**: Extranet files for acceptance of bids available after issue closure upon confirmation from Merchant Banker/RTA

## Important Dates

- **Bid Offering Period**: January 8-14, 2026 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Bid Modification/Cancellation**: January 8-14, 2026 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Give-up/Take-up**: January 8-13, 2026 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Give-up (Last Day)**: January 14, 2026 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Take-up (Last Day)**: January 14, 2026 (9:15 AM - 4:00 PM)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The buyback is limited to 4,25,000 shares of FAIRCHEM ORGANICS LTD, representing a corporate action that provides exit opportunity to shareholders at Rs.800 per share. The offer price represents a premium opportunity for eligible shareholders.

**Operational Impact**: Trading Members and Custodian Members need to ensure their systems and UCC databases are updated to facilitate client participation. Members must have active iBBS and RTRMS platform access with proper login credentials.

**Investor Impact**: Shareholders holding FAIRCHEM ORGANICS LTD shares in demat form can participate through their trading members during the specified window. The buyback provides liquidity at a predetermined price for eligible investors across various categories (FII, FI, MF, IC, Individual, etc.).

**Contact Support**: BSE has provided detailed contact information for technical support - Trading Operations (022-22728005, user.trdops@bseindia.com) and specific personnel for OTB Module queries including Sandeep Pujari, Rushi Bhoir, Hormazdiar Mobedji, Mangesh Tayde, and Rinkesh Gaikwad.
