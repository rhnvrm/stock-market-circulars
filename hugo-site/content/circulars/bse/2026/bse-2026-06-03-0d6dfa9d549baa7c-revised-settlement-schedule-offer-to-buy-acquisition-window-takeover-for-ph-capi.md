## Summary

The Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd. (ICCL) has issued a revised settlement calendar for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) pertaining to P.H. Capital Limited (Settlement No. 2026052). The acquisition window was conducted from May 19, 2026 to June 2, 2026. The obligation download date has been revised to June 4, 2026, and the settlement date to June 5, 2026.

## Key Points

- Revised settlement calendar issued under Notice No. 20260603-6, superseding Notice No. 20260518-5
- Settlement Type: Takeover | Settlement No.: 2026052
- Bid period ran from May 19, 2026 to June 2, 2026
- Revised Obligation Download date: June 4, 2026
- Revised Settlement Date: June 5, 2026
- Demat shares must be delivered exclusively via the Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism, available through both depositories (NSDL and CDSL)
- Members/Custodians must use Market Type "(Takeover)" and Settlement No. 2026052 in the depository system
- Stamp duty charges (client-wise) available in the MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular revises previously announced settlement dates in accordance with the Revised Guidelines of Mechanism for acquisition of shares through Stock Exchange (refer Notice No. 20200528-32 dated May 28, 2020).

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and Custodians must deliver demat shares only through the Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism
- Use Market Type "(Takeover)" and Settlement No. 2026052 when entering details in the depository system
- Refer to the MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for client-wise Stamp Duty charges
- Follow the settlement framework established under Notice No. 20200528-32

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Bid Start Date | May 19, 2026 |
| Bid End Date | June 2, 2026 |
| Revised Obligation Download | June 4, 2026 |
| Revised Settlement Date | June 5, 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a targeted operational impact on Members and Custodians participating in the P.H. Capital Limited takeover acquisition window. The revised settlement dates (obligation download on June 4 and settlement on June 5, 2026) require prompt action from participants to ensure timely early pay-in of shares via the depository system. Failure to comply with the Early Pay-in mechanism or use of incorrect market type/settlement number in the depository could result in settlement failures. General market participants not involved in this specific takeover are unaffected.
