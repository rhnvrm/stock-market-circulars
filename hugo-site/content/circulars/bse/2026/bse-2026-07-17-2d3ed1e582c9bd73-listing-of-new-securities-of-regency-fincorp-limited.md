## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 58,62,879 new equity shares of Regency Fincorp Limited (Scrip Code: 540175), each of face value Rs. 10/-, issued at a premium of Rs. 12/- (issue price Rs. 22/-) to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants, are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Monday, July 20, 2026.

## Key Points

- 58,62,879 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each allotted at a premium of Rs. 12/- per share (issue price Rs. 22/-)
- Shares issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distinctive numbers: 80171072 to 86033950
- ISIN: INE964R01013
- Date of allotment: 21/05/2026

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a listing approval notice under standard BSE listing operations procedures for shares arising from preferential allotment/warrant conversion.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the listing for operational awareness. The allotted shares are subject to a lock-in until 31/01/2027 as per SEBI ICDR regulations governing preferential issues.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: 21/05/2026
- Listing/Trading effective from: Monday, July 20, 2026
- Lock-in expiry: 31/01/2027

## Impact Assessment

The listing marginally increases the free-float and total outstanding equity share capital of Regency Fincorp Limited. Since the shares are subject to lock-in until January 31, 2027, there is no immediate incremental tradable supply impact, and overall market impact is limited given the relatively small size of the allotment.
