## Summary

BSE Daily Bulletin No. 194/2025-2026 dated January 9, 2026, announcing book closure dates and record dates for various corporate actions across multiple companies. The bulletin primarily covers interest payment schedules for privately placed non-convertible debentures, along with notable actions including Havells India's interim dividend and Energy Infrastructure Trust's income distribution.

## Key Points

- Multiple record dates set for Achiievers Finance India Limited debenture interest payments on January 16, 2026 and February 2, 2026
- Auxilo Finserve Private Limited debenture interest payments scheduled for February 16 and 23, 2026
- Bharat Heavy Electricals Ltd. commercial paper redemption record date: January 14, 2026
- Havells India Limited interim dividend record date: January 23, 2026
- Energy Infrastructure Trust income distribution record date: January 9, 2026
- Grand Hills Developments Private Limited partial redemption and interest payment: January 16, 2026
- Ecobox Industrials Asset I Private Limited interest payment: January 16, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine operational bulletin.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies must ensure book closure/record date compliance for announced corporate actions
- Trading flags indicated (RD=1 or RD=0) determine trading eligibility on record dates
- Payment/allotment dates to be communicated separately by respective companies
- Investors holding securities on record dates are eligible for announced benefits

## Important Dates

- **January 9, 2026**: Record date for Energy Infrastructure Trust income distribution
- **January 14, 2026**: Record date for BHEL commercial paper redemption
- **January 16, 2026**: Record date for multiple Achiievers Finance debenture interest payments, Ecobox Industrials interest payment, and Grand Hills Developments interest/partial redemption
- **January 23, 2026**: Record date for Havells India interim dividend
- **February 2, 2026**: Record date for Achiievers Finance debenture interest payment
- **February 16, 2026**: Record date for Auxilo Finserve debenture interest payment
- **February 23, 2026**: Record date for Auxilo Finserve debenture interest payment

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine daily bulletin with standard corporate action announcements.

**Investor Impact**: Informational for investors holding the mentioned securities. Investors must hold securities on respective record dates to be eligible for dividends, interest payments, or redemptions.

**Operational Impact**: Administrative notification for market participants and depositories to process corporate actions on scheduled dates. The bulletin primarily affects debt security holders with multiple debenture interest payment schedules.

**Notable Equity Actions**: Havells India interim dividend is the most significant equity-related announcement for retail equity investors.
