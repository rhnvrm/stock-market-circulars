## Summary

BSE has issued a circular announcing the applicability of the Additional Surveillance Measure (ASM) for securities exhibiting unusual price movement. Seshachal Technologies Ltd (Scrip Code: 531794, ISIN: INE331F01028) has been newly shortlisted under ASM effective March 09, 2025. The consolidated ASM list now comprises 16 securities.

## Key Points

- Seshachal Technologies Ltd (531794) is newly added to the ASM framework effective March 09, 2025.
- The consolidated ASM list contains 16 securities across various sectors.
- ASM is applied to securities exhibiting unusual or abnormal price movements to protect investor interest.
- All 16 securities in the consolidated list are subject to enhanced surveillance conditions.

## Regulatory Changes

The ASM framework triggers additional trading conditions for shortlisted securities, which may include:
- Reduction in price bands
- Mandatory additional margins
- Periodic call auction mechanisms

The new addition (Seshachal Technologies Ltd) is subject to these measures from March 09, 2025 onwards.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure clients are informed of the ASM status of these securities.
- Brokers must apply applicable margin requirements for ASM-listed scrips.
- Investors holding or planning to trade in ASM securities should review the specific conditions imposed.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date for New Addition:** March 09, 2025 — Seshachal Technologies Ltd (531794) placed under ASM.
- **Circular Date:** March 06, 2026.
