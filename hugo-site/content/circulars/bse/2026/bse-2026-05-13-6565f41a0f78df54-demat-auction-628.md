## Summary

BSE Limited, through the Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd. (ICCL), has issued a notice inviting auction offers from trading members for the purchase of securities under Rolling Settlement No. DR-628/2026-27. The auction offer entry session is scheduled for May 13, 2026, with pay-in on May 14, 2026.

## Key Points

- Auction offer entry window: 2:00 PM to 2:45 PM on May 13, 2026
- Auction matching: 2:45 PM to 3:00 PM on May 13, 2026
- Delivery statement and accepted auction offer reports: 3:00 PM to 3:30 PM
- Money statement and final reports: 3:35 PM to 5:30 PM
- Auction pay-in: May 14, 2026 at 8:00 AM
- Settlement will be processed through the Clearing Member of the Trading Member, following the Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment
- The restriction on trading members offering securities in auction for settlements in which they had short delivered is now removed
- No corrections will be permitted for punching errors in securities code or rate

## Regulatory Changes

Following the implementation of the Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment, settlement for auction trades is now routed through the Clearing Member of a Trading Member. Additionally, the earlier restriction preventing trading members from offering securities in auction for settlements where they had short delivered has been lifted.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and custodians must check their auction pay-in obligations for securities
- Securities pay-in must be executed in depositories within depository cut-off timings
- Trading members must refer to the cutoff rate displayed on the "Auction Offer Entry Screen" before submitting offers
- Trading members must review notices issued for Corporate Actions (ex-rights, ex-bonus, ex-split, etc.) for relevant scrips before submitting offers
- Utmost care must be taken while entering securities codes and rates, as no corrections will be permitted after submission

## Important Dates

- **May 13, 2026**: Auction offer entry (2:00 PM – 2:45 PM), Matching (2:45 PM – 3:00 PM), Delivery statements (3:00 PM – 3:30 PM), Final reports (3:35 PM – 5:30 PM)
- **May 14, 2026**: Auction pay-in at 8:00 AM

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine demat auction notice applicable to trading members and custodians participating in Rolling Settlement DR-628/2026-27. The notice has moderate operational impact as it requires members to act within tight intraday windows. The removal of the restriction on short-delivered securities offering in auction may slightly expand participation eligibility. Members must ensure timely securities pay-in to avoid default. No broader market or systemic impact is expected.
