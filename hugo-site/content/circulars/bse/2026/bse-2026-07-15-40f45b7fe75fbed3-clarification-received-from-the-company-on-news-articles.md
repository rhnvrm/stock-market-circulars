## Summary

BSE, as part of its routine surveillance process to verify the accuracy of news articles appearing in newspapers, websites, and television channels, sought clarifications from listed companies about specific news reports. IDBI Bank Ltd (Scrip Code: 500116) has submitted its reply regarding a news article published on business-standard.com, which is attached to this notice. Rajesh Exports Ltd (Scrip Code: 531500) has not yet responded to a similar query concerning an article on thehindubusinessline.com.

## Key Points

- BSE sought clarification from IDBI Bank Ltd and Rajesh Exports Ltd on news articles concerning them.
- IDBI Bank Ltd has submitted its reply, which is attached to the notice.
- Reply from Rajesh Exports Ltd (Scrip Code: 531500) is still awaited.
- The exercise is intended to inform members and investors about the accuracy of circulating news reports.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine surveillance clarification notice, not a regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

Rajesh Exports Ltd is required to submit its clarification/reply to BSE regarding the referenced news article.

## Important Dates

Notice issued on 15 July 2026 (Notice No. 20260715-7). No specific deadline mentioned for Rajesh Exports Ltd's pending reply.

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard surveillance-related disclosure notice with limited market impact. It informs investors that IDBI Bank Ltd has clarified a news report about the company, while Rajesh Exports Ltd's clarification remains pending, which may warrant investor attention until resolved.
