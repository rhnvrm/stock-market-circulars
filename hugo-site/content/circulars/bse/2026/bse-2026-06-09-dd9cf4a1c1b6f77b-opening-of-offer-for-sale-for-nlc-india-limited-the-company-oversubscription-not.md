## Summary

The President of India, acting through the Ministry of Coal, has exercised the oversubscription option in the Offer for Sale (OFS) of NLC India Limited. This increases the total offer size from the base 2,77,32,732 equity shares (2.00%) to 4,15,99,098 equity shares (3.00% of total paid-up equity share capital). This notice is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20260608-44 dated June 08, 2026.

## Key Points

- **Seller:** The President of India (acting through the Ministry of Coal, Government of India)
- **Base Offer Size:** 2,77,32,732 equity shares representing 2.00% of paid-up equity share capital
- **Oversubscription Option Exercised:** Additional 1,38,66,366 equity shares (1.00% of paid-up equity)
- **Total Offer Size:** 4,15,99,098 equity shares representing 3.00% of total paid-up equity share capital
- **Retail Investor Reservation:** 41,59,911 equity shares (10% of total offer) reserved for Retail Investors on T+1 Day (June 10, 2026)
- **Employee Offer:** Up to 25,000 equity shares available to eligible employees; applications up to Rs. 5,00,000 permitted; first Rs. 2,00,000 prioritised for allocation
- **Face Value:** Rs. 10/- per share
- **Trading Platforms:** BSE Limited and National Stock Exchange of India Limited (NSE)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice formalises the exercise of the oversubscription option as provided for in the original OFS framework under Exchange Notice No. 20260608-44.

## Compliance Requirements

- Non-Retail Investors who bid on T Day (June 09, 2026) and wish to carry forward un-allotted bids may do so on T+1 Day (June 10, 2026)
- Retail Investors and Employees must submit bids on T+1 Day (June 10, 2026)
- Eligible employees applying under the Employee Offer may bid up to Rs. 5,00,000; however, only bids up to Rs. 2,00,000 will be considered for first-instance allocation

## Important Dates

- **June 09, 2026 (T Day):** OFS open for Non-Retail Investors
- **June 10, 2026 (T+1 Day):** OFS open for Retail Investors, Employees, and Non-Retail Investors carrying forward un-allotted bids
- **June 09, 2026:** Date of oversubscription notice and exercise of oversubscription option

## Impact Assessment

The exercise of the oversubscription option by the Government of India signals strong demand from non-retail investors on T Day, resulting in a 50% increase in the total offer size (from 2% to 3% of paid-up capital). This is a significant government disinvestment event for NLC India Limited, a public sector undertaking in the coal and power sector. The expanded offer increases the floating stock available in the market, which may have a modest dilutive effect. Retail investors and employees retain dedicated allocation windows on June 10, 2026, providing broader participation opportunities.
