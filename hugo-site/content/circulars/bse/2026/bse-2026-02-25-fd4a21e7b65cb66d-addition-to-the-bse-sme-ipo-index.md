## Summary

BSE Index Services Private Limited has announced the addition of Yashhtej Industries (India) Limited (Exchange Ticker: 544708) to the BSE SME IPO Index. The company was listed on BSE effective February 25, 2026, and will be included in the index at the open of trading on February 26, 2026.

## Key Points

- Yashhtej Industries (India) Limited (ticker: 544708) is being added to the BSE SME IPO Index.
- The company's listing on BSE is effective February 25, 2026.
- Index addition is effective at the open of Thursday, February 26, 2026.
- This action references prior Notice No. 20260224-17.
- The corporate action is included in the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE) dated February 25, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard index rebalancing action triggered by the new listing of an SME IPO on BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

- Index fund managers and ETF providers tracking the BSE SME IPO Index should update their portfolios to include Yashhtej Industries (India) Limited (544708) effective at the open of February 26, 2026.
- Clients with queries are directed to contact bseindex@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **February 25, 2026**: Listing of Yashhtej Industries (India) Limited on BSE.
- **February 26, 2026 (market open)**: Effective date of addition to BSE SME IPO Index.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to funds, ETFs, and portfolios that track or benchmark against the BSE SME IPO Index. Broader market impact is negligible. This is a routine constituent addition following the IPO listing of a small and medium enterprise on BSE.
