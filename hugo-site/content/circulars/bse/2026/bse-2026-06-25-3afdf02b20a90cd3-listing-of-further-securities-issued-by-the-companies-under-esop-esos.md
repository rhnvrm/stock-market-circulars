## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that additional equity shares issued by 21 companies under their Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP/ESOS) are listed and admitted for trading on the Exchange with effect from Monday, June 29, 2026. No lock-in period applies to any of the listed securities.

## Key Points

- 21 companies have had further securities listed under ESOP/ESOS schemes
- Effective trading date: June 29, 2026
- No lock-in period applies to any of the securities
- Largest issuances by share count: Indiabulls Limited (51,61,464 shares), AU Small Finance Bank (2,32,375 shares), Axis Bank (2,49,399 shares), and Anthem Biosciences (1,89,659 shares)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice for admission of new ESOP/ESOS securities to trading.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to take note of the newly listed securities and their respective ISIN codes, scrip codes, and share number ranges for settlement and trading purposes.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 25, 2026
- **Trading Effective From:** June 29, 2026 (Monday)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notice with low market impact. The additional shares represent employee stock option exercises across 21 companies and result in minor dilution to existing shareholders. Notable listings include ICICI Bank (1,10,884 shares, ISIN INE090A01021), Axis Bank (2,49,399 shares), AU Small Finance Bank (2,32,375 shares), and Computer Age Management Services (2,20,230 shares). All securities are free from lock-in, making them immediately tradeable from June 29, 2026.
