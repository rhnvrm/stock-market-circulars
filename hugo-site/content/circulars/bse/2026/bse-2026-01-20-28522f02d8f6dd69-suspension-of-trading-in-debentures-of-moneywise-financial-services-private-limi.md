## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in Non-Convertible Debentures of MONEYWISE FINANCIAL SERVICES PRIVATE LIMITED (ISIN: INE956L07076, Code: 974326) effective January 23, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Company: MONEYWISE FINANCIAL SERVICES PRIVATE LIMITED
- ISIN: INE956L07076
- BSE Code: 974326
- Security Type: Non-Convertible Debentures (9.50% coupon)
- Record Date: January 23, 2026
- No Dealings From: January 23, 2026
- Circular Reference: DR-805/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational circular for temporary trading suspension.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from January 23, 2026
- Trading members must note the suspension and ensure compliance with the no-dealings directive
- The suspension applies specifically to debenture series MFSPL-9.50%-5-2-26-PVT

## Important Dates

- **January 20, 2026**: Circular issued
- **January 23, 2026**: Record date for redemption and interest payment
- **January 23, 2026**: Trading suspension effective date

## Impact Assessment

The impact is minimal and localized to holders of this specific debenture series. This is a routine corporate action for debt securities approaching maturity. Trading suspension is temporary and relates to the redemption process and interest payment. The debentures belong to a private limited company with limited market participation. No broader market implications are expected.
