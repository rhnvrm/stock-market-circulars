## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that further new securities issued by 16 companies under their respective Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP/ESOS) are listed and admitted for trading on the Exchange with effect from Monday, June 15, 2026. None of the newly listed shares are subject to any lock-in period.

## Key Points

- 16 companies have listed additional shares arising from ESOP/ESOS exercises.
- Effective date for trading: Monday, June 15, 2026.
- No lock-in period applies to any of the newly issued shares across all 16 companies.
- The newly issued shares are incremental to existing outstanding share counts and represent routine dilution from employee stock option exercises.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice informing trading members of newly listed securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are requested to take note of the newly listed securities and their ISIN codes, scrip codes, and applicable share number ranges. No specific compliance action is required beyond awareness for trade execution.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 12, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** Monday, June 15, 2026
