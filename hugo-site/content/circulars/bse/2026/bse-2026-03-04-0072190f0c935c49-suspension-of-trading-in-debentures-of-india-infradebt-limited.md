## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (No. 20260304-24) directing trading members not to deal in the Non-Convertible Debentures of India Infradebt Limited with effect from 10 March 2026. The suspension is necessitated by the company fixing a Record Date for the purpose of redemption of debentures and payment of interest.

## Key Points

- Trading in debentures of India Infradebt Limited (series IIL-7.25%-25-3-26-PVT, ISIN: INE537P07596, Code: 973116) is suspended from 10 March 2026
- The Record Date is set as 10 March 2026 for both redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- The coupon rate on the debenture series is 7.25%
- Reference number for the circular is DR-836/2025-2026
- Issued under the Debt segment, Corporate Actions category

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice under existing BSE listing compliance procedures requiring suspension of trading ahead of a record date for corporate actions on debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any buy or sell transactions in the specified debenture series (INE537P07596 / Code 973116) from 10 March 2026 onwards
- Members should update their systems to block dealings in this security from the effective date

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** 04 March 2026
- **Record Date:** 10 March 2026
- **No Dealings From:** 10 March 2026
- **Purpose:** Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and traders of this specific NCD series. As the debenture is approaching its maturity/redemption date (25 March 2026 as suggested by the series name), this suspension is a standard pre-redemption measure. Investors holding this security will receive redemption proceeds and final interest payment on or around the record date. No broader market impact is expected.
