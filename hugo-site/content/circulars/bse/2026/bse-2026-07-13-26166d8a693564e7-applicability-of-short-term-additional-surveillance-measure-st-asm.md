## Summary

BSE has issued its periodic update on securities satisfying the criteria for inclusion under the Short Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM). Securities listed in the attached Annexure I have been placed under ST-ASM Stage I and Stage II, triggering higher margin requirements effective July 15, 2026. This follows a series of earlier exchange notices dating back to 2018 that established and revised the ASM framework.

## Key Points

- Securities satisfying ST-ASM Stage I criteria will attract a margin of 50% or the existing margin, whichever is higher, capped at a maximum of 100%.
- Securities satisfying ST-ASM Stage II criteria will attract a margin of 100% or the existing margin, whichever is higher, capped at a maximum of 100%.
- The specific list of affected securities is provided in Annexure I (attachment), not reproduced in the notice text.
- The ASM framework operates alongside all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the exchanges.
- Inclusion under ASM is purely a market surveillance action and should not be construed as adverse action against the concerned company or entity.
- Once a scrip moves out of the ASM framework, its price band reverts to the level applicable before shortlisting, unless it remains subject to another surveillance framework.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced; this notice applies the existing ST-ASM framework (established via notices 20181027-1, 20190719-33, 20201204-56, 20230925-49, 20240920-63, and 20240920-71) to a fresh set of securities identified as meeting the Stage I and Stage II inclusion criteria.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must apply the specified margin rates (50% for Stage I, 100% for Stage II securities, subject to the 100% cap) on all open positions as of July 14, 2026, and on new positions created from July 15, 2026.
- Members should refer to Annexure I for the specific list of affected securities and monitor them for compliance with the applicable margin requirements.
- Queries can be directed to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- July 13, 2026: Notice issued.
- July 14, 2026: Reference date for open positions subject to the new margin requirements.
- July 15, 2026: Effective date for revised margin rates on all open and new positions.

## Impact Assessment

The increased margin requirements (50%-100%) will raise the cost of trading and reduce leverage for market participants holding positions in the affected securities, likely dampening speculative activity and volumes in these scrips. Since the specific securities are named only in the attached Annexure (not included in the extracted text), the precise scope of impact cannot be determined from this notice alone; affected market participants should consult Annexure I directly.
