## Summary

BSE has listed 1,25,915 new equity shares of Sona BLW Precision Forgings Limited (Scrip Code: 543300, ISIN: INE073K01018) issued under the Sona Performance Share Plan 2025. These shares are permitted to trade effective Thursday, March 05, 2026, and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 1,25,915 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each (fully paid up) listed on BSE under Scrip Code 543300
- Issued under the Sona Performance Share Plan (ESPS) 2025
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 624313911 to 624439825
- Date of allotment: January 22, 2026
- ISIN: INE073K01018
- All 1,25,915 shares are subject to a one-year lock-in from the date of allotment

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice pursuant to an employee share performance plan allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed that these securities are listed and permitted for trading from March 05, 2026
- The newly issued shares under the ESPS are subject to a mandatory lock-in of minimum one year from the date of allotment (i.e., until January 22, 2027)

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** January 22, 2026
- **Effective Date for Trading:** March 05, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry:** January 22, 2027 (minimum one year from allotment)

## Impact Assessment

The listing is a routine administrative action following an employee share plan allotment. The quantity (1,25,915 shares) is small relative to the company's total equity base, and the mandatory one-year lock-in further limits any immediate market impact. No significant price or liquidity effect is anticipated.
