## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that several companies have fixed record dates for payment of interest and/or redemption of their Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) and Bonds. Trading members are advised not to deal in the debentures/bonds of these companies on and around the specified record/ex-dates.

## Key Points

- The notice lists specific ISINs of NCDs/Bonds becoming ex-interest/ex-redemption ahead of their record dates.
- Companies covered include Andhra Pradesh Capital Region Development Authority, ECL Finance Limited, Hinduja Leyland Finance Limited, Kotak Mahindra Investments Limited, Indel Money Limited, Manappuram Finance Limited, AMBIUM FINSERVE LIMITED, Kosamattam Finance Limited, Shriram Finance Limited, Kotak Mahindra Prime Limited, Housing & Urban Development Corporation Ltd. (HUDCO), ACHIIEVERS FINANCE INDIA LIMITED, and NTPC (listing truncated in source document).
- Record dates range from 30 July 2026 to 5 August 2026, with ex-dates coinciding with the record dates in each case.
- Settlement designations (DR series) for the listed instruments run through 2027.
- Trading members are directed to suspend dealing in the affected debt securities as per exchange practice around record dates.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a routine operational notice under BSE's Listing Operations/Corporate Actions process for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the listed Debentures/Bonds on the specified record dates and surrounding settlement window.
- Members should update internal systems to reflect the suspension for the relevant ISINs to avoid erroneous trades.

## Important Dates

- 30 July 2026: Record/Ex-date — Andhra Pradesh Capital Region Development Authority (INE01E708032)
- 3 August 2026: Record/Ex-date — ECL Finance Limited, Hinduja Leyland Finance Limited, Kotak Mahindra Investments Limited, Indel Money Limited
- 4 August 2026: Record/Ex-date — Manappuram Finance Limited, AMBIUM FINSERVE LIMITED
- 5 August 2026: Record/Ex-date — Kosamattam Finance Limited (two ISINs), Shriram Finance Limited (three ISINs), Kotak Mahindra Prime Limited, HUDCO, ACHIIEVERS FINANCE INDIA LIMITED

## Impact Assessment

This notice affects only holders and traders of the specific NCDs/Bonds listed, restricting trading in these debt instruments around their record dates for interest/redemption processing. There is no impact on equity markets or the broader trading segment; the effect is operational and limited to the debt segment participants holding these specific securities.
