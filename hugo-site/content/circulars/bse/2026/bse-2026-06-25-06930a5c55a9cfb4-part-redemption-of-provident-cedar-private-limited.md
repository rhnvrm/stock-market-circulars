## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that Provident Cedar Private Limited has completed a partial redemption of its listed debentures, resulting in a reduced face value of Rs. 191,966.87 per debenture. The revised face value will be applicable for trading from June 29, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: Provident Cedar Private Limited
- Scrip Code: PCPL-17-4-28-PVT (974311)
- ISIN: INE0NF907031
- Action: Partial redemption of debenture
- Revised/Reduced Face Value: Rs. 191,966.87 per debenture
- Effective trading date with reduced face value: June 29, 2026
- BSE Reference: DR-660/2026-2027

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework introduced. This is an operational notification pursuant to a corporate action (partial redemption) executed by the issuer.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to take note of the revised face value of the debenture (Rs. 191,966.87 per debenture) and ensure that trades in scrip PCPL-17-4-28-PVT (ISIN: INE0NF907031) reflect the updated face value from June 29, 2026 onwards.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: June 25, 2026
- Effective Date (trading with reduced face value): June 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited to holders and traders of Provident Cedar Private Limited debentures (ISIN: INE0NF907031). The partial redemption reduces the outstanding principal per debenture to Rs. 191,966.87. No broader market or systemic impact is expected. Trading members must update their systems to reflect the new face value before commencement of trading on June 29, 2026.
