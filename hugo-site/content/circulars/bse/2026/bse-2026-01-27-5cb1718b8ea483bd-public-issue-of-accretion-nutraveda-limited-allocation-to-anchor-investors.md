## Summary

BSE has issued a circular regarding the allocation of shares to anchor investors in the public issue of Accretion Nutraveda Limited. This notification is part of the IPO process where shares are allocated to qualified institutional buyers before the issue opens to the general public.

## Key Points

- Company: Accretion Nutraveda Limited
- Event: Allocation to Anchor Investors in Public Issue
- Exchange: BSE (Bombay Stock Exchange)
- Circular Date: January 27, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural notification for IPO anchor investor allocation.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants and investors should note the anchor investor allocation details
- The company must comply with SEBI regulations regarding anchor investor participation in IPOs
- Disclosure requirements as per SEBI (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations

## Important Dates

- Anchor Investor Allocation Date: January 27, 2026
- Public issue dates would follow after anchor allocation (specific dates to be confirmed in the prospectus)

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact for investors interested in the IPO of Accretion Nutraveda Limited. Anchor investor allocation is a standard step in the IPO process and indicates the issue is proceeding as planned. The allocation to anchor investors typically happens one day before the issue opens to the public and can provide price discovery signals to retail investors.
