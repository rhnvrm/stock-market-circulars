## Summary

BSE notifies that Galactico Corporate Services Ltd (Scrip Code: 542802) has fixed the record date for its rights issue of equity shares. The company will issue 23 equity shares of Re.1/- each at a premium of Re.0.75 per share for every 100 equity shares held. Trading on ex-rights basis will commence from January 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- Rights entitlement: 23 equity shares for every 100 shares held
- Face value: Re.1/- per share
- Premium: Re.0.75 per share
- Total issue price: Re.1.75 per share
- Full payment required at application time
- Scrip code: 542802
- Settlement number: DR-809/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification under existing framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must execute all transactions on ex-rights basis from January 30, 2026
- Shareholders on record as of January 31, 2026 will be eligible for rights entitlement
- Entire issue price of Re.1.75 must be paid at the time of application

## Important Dates

- **Ex-Rights Date**: January 30, 2026
- **Record Date**: January 31, 2026
- **Notice Date**: January 28, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - affects existing shareholders of Galactico Corporate Services Ltd who must decide whether to subscribe to rights issue.

**Operational Impact**: Standard corporate action requiring trading members to adjust systems for ex-rights trading. Ratio of 23:100 indicates a capital raise without significant dilution. Shareholders need to arrange funds if they wish to participate in the rights issue.
