## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members that the equity shares of Kratikal Tech Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company falls under the SME segment and has been assigned scrip code 544811, trading symbol KRATIKAL, and ISIN INE1L0M01019.

## Key Points

- Company: Kratikal Tech Limited
- Segment: SME
- Scrip Code: 544811
- Symbol: KRATIKAL
- ISIN: INE1L0M01019
- The date of listing and further details of the securities will be announced through a separate notice

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a standard listing admission notice under BSE's Department DCS-Listing.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the new scrip details for Kratikal Tech Limited in preparation for dealings once listing is confirmed. No immediate action is required until the formal listing date notice is issued.

## Important Dates

Notice Date: 06 July 2026. Exact listing date to be communicated via a subsequent BSE notice.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine procedural notice for a new SME listing and carries low systemic market impact. It is directly relevant to Kratikal Tech Limited, its promoters, and prospective investors who should watch for the follow-up notice confirming the listing date before trading begins.
