## Summary

BSE has notified that pursuant to an order by the Hon'ble Securities Appellate Tribunal (SAT), the delisting of Betala Global Securities Ltd. (Scrip Code: 531530) has been rescinded. As a result, the company's status will be changed from "Delisted" to "Suspended" with effect from May 25, 2026.

## Key Points

- The delisting of Betala Global Securities Ltd. (Scrip Code: 531530) stands rescinded by SAT order.
- Company status will change from **Delisted** to **Suspended** effective May 25, 2026.
- SEBI (Delisting of Equity Shares) Regulations, 2021 — specifically Regulation 34(1) and 34(2) — are no longer applicable to this company.
- The notice was issued by Netra Sahani (Deputy Vice President, Listing Compliance) and Manish Raval (Manager, Listing Compliance).

## Regulatory Changes

With the rescission of the delisting order, the provisions of Regulation 34(1) and Regulation 34(2) of the SEBI (Delisting of Equity Shares) Regulations, 2021 cease to apply to Betala Global Securities Ltd. The company transitions from a delisted entity back into the suspended category on BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants and intermediaries should update their records to reflect the revised status of Betala Global Securities Ltd. from "Delisted" to "Suspended".
- Obligations under Regulation 34(1) and 34(2) of the SEBI Delisting Regulations, 2021 are no longer applicable to this company.

## Important Dates

- **May 21, 2026**: BSE circular issued.
- **May 25, 2026**: Effective date for change in status from Delisted to Suspended.

## Impact Assessment

This change has moderate market impact. While the stock does not resume normal trading (it moves to suspended status rather than active trading), the rescission of delisting by the SAT is a legally significant event that reverses a prior regulatory action. Investors who held shares before the original delisting should note the status update. Trading in the stock remains halted under suspended status until further regulatory action permits resumption.
