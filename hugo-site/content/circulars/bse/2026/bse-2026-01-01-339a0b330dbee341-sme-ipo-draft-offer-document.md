## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Documents of two SME companies - Saffron Speciality Papers Limited and UHM Vacation Limited - on its website for public comments. Both documents were filed on December 31, 2025, and contain all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of SEBI (ICDR) Regulations, 2018. The documents will remain available for at least 21 days for public feedback.

## Key Points

- Two SME companies have filed Draft Offer Documents: Saffron Speciality Papers Limited and UHM Vacation Limited
- Both filings dated December 31, 2025
- Documents comply with Schedule VI of SEBI (ICDR) Regulations, 2018
- Draft documents available on BSE SME website for minimum 21 days
- Public comments solicited within 21 days from notice date
- Comments to be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard procedural notice for SME IPO draft document submissions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants and public invited to review draft offer documents and provide comments
- Comments must be submitted within 21 days from January 1, 2026 (i.e., by January 22, 2026)
- Comments to be sent to: equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Draft documents accessible at: www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx

## Important Dates

- **Filing Date**: December 31, 2025 (for both companies)
- **Notice Date**: January 1, 2026
- **Comment Deadline**: January 22, 2026 (21 days from notice date)
- **Document Availability**: Minimum 21 days from January 1, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine informational notice regarding SME IPO filings with minimal market impact. The circular serves to inform stakeholders about upcoming SME offerings and provides an opportunity for public feedback on the draft documents. No immediate action is required from existing market participants. The impact is limited to potential investors interested in these specific SME IPOs and the regulatory review process.
