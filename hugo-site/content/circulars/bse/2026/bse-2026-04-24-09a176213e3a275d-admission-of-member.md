## Summary

BSE Ltd. has admitted three new trading members effective 23rd April 2026. Two entities have been granted new membership, and one existing member has been granted an additional segment. The admission is subject to the Rules, Bye-laws, and Regulations of the Exchange.

## Key Points

- Three entities admitted as Trading Members of BSE Ltd. on 23rd April 2026
- Two new memberships granted (Betala Stock Broking Limited and Tradexa Private Limited)
- One additional segment membership granted to M D Overseas Pvt. Ltd.
- Newly admitted members have trading rights only on BSE Ltd.
- Tradexa Private Limited has the broadest segment access, covering all four major derivative and cash segments

## New Members Admitted

| Sr. No. | Name | Member No. | Segments | Membership Type |
|---------|------|------------|----------|-----------------|
| 1 | Betala Stock Broking Limited | 6986 | Cash / Equity Derivatives | New Membership |
| 2 | Tradexa Private Limited | 6987 | Cash / Equity Derivatives / Currency Derivatives / Commodity Derivatives | New Membership |
| 3 | M D Overseas Pvt. Ltd. | 4093 | Cash | Additional Segment Membership |

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine administrative notice under existing membership rules and regulations of BSE Ltd.

## Compliance Requirements

Existing trading members have no specific compliance obligations arising from this notice. For clarifications, trading members may contact: membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Admission Date:** 23rd April 2026
- **Notice Date:** 24th April 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The addition of two new trading members and one additional segment membership is a routine operational matter. Tradexa Private Limited's broad multi-segment access (cash, equity derivatives, currency derivatives, and commodity derivatives) may marginally increase liquidity across those segments. No impact on existing members' rights or obligations.
