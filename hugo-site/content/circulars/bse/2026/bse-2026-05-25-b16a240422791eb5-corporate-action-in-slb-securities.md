## Summary

ICCL Ltd. has issued Notice No. 20260525-59 dated 25 May 2026 informing trading members of corporate actions in the Securities Lending and Borrowing (SLB) segment. The specific details of affected securities and corresponding adjustments are provided in an attached annexure.

## Key Points

- Notice issued by ICCL Ltd. (Indian Clearing Corporation Limited) on 25 May 2026
- Pertains to the SLB (Securities Lending and Borrowing) segment
- Trading members are directed to refer to the annexure for a list of affected securities and corporate action details
- Issued by Assistant Manager Tushar Nimkar on behalf of ICCL Ltd.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is an operational notice communicating existing corporate action impacts on open SLB positions.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members participating in the SLB segment should review the attached annexure to identify affected securities and take appropriate action on any open lending or borrowing positions impacted by the stated corporate actions.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 25 May 2026
- Effective dates for individual corporate actions are specified in the annexure.

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited to participants with open positions in the SLB segment for the securities listed in the annexure. Corporate actions such as dividends, splits, or bonuses typically trigger adjustments to SLB contracts (e.g., price or quantity adjustments) to neutralise the economic effect on borrowers and lenders. Routine in nature; no broad market impact anticipated.
