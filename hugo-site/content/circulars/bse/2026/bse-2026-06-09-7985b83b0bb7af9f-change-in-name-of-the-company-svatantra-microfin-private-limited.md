## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that Svatantra Microfin Private Limited has changed its name to Svatantra Microfin Limited. The change affects five debt-listed scrips on the BOLT Plus system and takes effect from June 12, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company name changes from **Svatantra Microfin Private Limited** to **Svatantra Microfin Limited**
- Five debt scrips (codes: 973125, 974079, 974613, 975118, 975150) are affected
- The scrip IDs on BOLT Plus remain unchanged (129SM26, 1283SML26, 1240SML23, 1055SML26, 1010SML26)
- Only the abbreviated name in the BOLT Plus system is being updated to reflect the new company name
- Certificate of Incorporation is attached as supporting documentation

## Regulatory Changes

The SCRIP ID and ABBREVIATED NAME of the company and its debt-listed scrips in the BOLT Plus system will be updated to reflect the new company name: Svatantra Microfin Limited.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to take note of the name change and update their records accordingly. No specific action is required beyond awareness of the new company name and updated abbreviated name on BOLT Plus.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 09, 2026
- **Effective Date:** June 12, 2026 — new name and abbreviated name come into effect on BOLT Plus

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative update with minimal market impact. The scrip codes and scrip IDs remain unchanged, so trading continuity is unaffected. The change from "Private Limited" to "Limited" indicates the company has converted from a private to a public company structure. Debt market participants holding or trading Svatantra Microfin securities should update internal records to reflect the new legal name.
