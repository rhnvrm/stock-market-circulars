## Summary

BSE has announced the settlement programme for the Securities Lending and Borrowing (SLB) Reverse Leg Segment for the financial year 2026-27. The circular provides a comprehensive schedule of 12 monthly settlements, covering the period from April 2026 to March 2027. Each settlement includes specific obligation dates and corresponding pay-in/pay-out dates processed through the depository system.

## Key Points

- Settlement schedule covers 12 monthly cycles from April 2026 to March 2027
- Each settlement has a unique settlement number (SLR-001/26-27 through SLR-012/26-27)
- Corresponding depository settlement numbers range from 2627001 to 2627012
- Pay-in timing is fixed at 09:00 a.m. for all settlements
- Members must submit pay-in instructions to Depositories/banks by 08:50 a.m.
- Pay-out occurs on the day following the obligation date
- Notice issued by Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced in this circular. This is a routine announcement of the settlement calendar for the upcoming financial year.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must adhere to the published settlement schedule
- Pay-in instructions must be submitted to Depositories/banks latest by 08:50 a.m. on obligation dates
- Pay-in must be completed by 09:00 a.m. on the specified obligation dates
- Members must ensure timely compliance with monthly settlement obligations
