## Summary

BSE has informed members that ALPHAGREP MUTUAL FUND has been activated on the BSE StAR MF Platform with effect from July 02, 2026. NRI and Minor investor transactions are permitted under ALPHAGREP MUTUAL FUND schemes through the platform, subject to a restricted list of countries. Additionally, the ALPHAGREP MULTI ASSET ALLOCATION FUND - NFO scheme will be enabled for order routing from July 06, 2026.

## Key Points

- ALPHAGREP MUTUAL FUND activated on BSE StAR MF Platform effective July 02, 2026.
- NRI and Minor investor transactions are permitted under ALPHAGREP MUTUAL FUND schemes.
- NRI applications will not be accepted from: North Korea, Iran, Myanmar, Canada, and the United States of America.
- ALPHAGREP MULTI ASSET ALLOCATION FUND - NFO scheme to be enabled for order routing from July 06, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is an operational notice enabling a new AMC (ALPHAGREP MUTUAL FUND) for transaction processing on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

Members facilitating NRI transactions under ALPHAGREP MUTUAL FUND schemes must ensure applications are not accepted from investors based in North Korea, Iran, Myanmar, Canada, or the United States of America, per AMC instructions.

## Important Dates

- July 02, 2026: ALPHAGREP MUTUAL FUND activated on BSE StAR MF Platform.
- July 06, 2026: ALPHAGREP MULTI ASSET ALLOCATION FUND - NFO scheme enabled for order routing.

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact operational update expanding the range of mutual fund schemes and AMCs accessible via the BSE StAR MF Platform. It primarily concerns mutual fund distributors, RTAs, and members processing NRI/Minor investor transactions, with no direct bearing on listed equity trading or specific stock prices.
