## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Electronic Gold Receipts (EGR) segment on Saturday, May 30, 2026. The session will be conducted from both the primary site (PR) and disaster recovery site (DR). Trading members using third-party or in-house trading platforms via ETI APIs can use this session to test their systems under various conditions including exceptional market scenarios.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session for EGR segment on Saturday, May 30, 2026
- Sessions conducted from both Primary Site (PR) and Disaster Recovery Site (DR)
- Covers testing of risk-reduction mode, trading halt, and other exceptional market conditions
- No margin obligations, pay-in/pay-out obligations, or legal rights/liabilities arise from mock trades
- Members are encouraged to participate actively and submit feedback by 4:00 pm

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notice for a scheduled mock trading session.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members using third-party empaneled vendor platforms or in-house systems via ETI APIs are encouraged to participate
- Feedback must be submitted to BSE Tech Support or Help Desk by 4:00 pm on the day of the session
- Contact for TWS users: BSE Tech Support at 022-22728053 or bse.tech@bseindia.com
- General Help Desk: 022-45720400/600 & 022-69158500 or bsehelp@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **May 30, 2026 (Saturday):** Mock trading session for EGR segment
  - Log-in: 09:15 am – 10:00 am
  - Pre-Open (PR) Order Entry: 10:00 am – 10:08 am
  - Pre-Open (PR) Matching: 10:08 am – 10:15 am
  - Continuous Trading (PR): 10:15 am – 12:00 pm
  - Switchover to DR: 12:00 pm – 12:45 pm
  - Pre-Open (DR) Order Entry: 12:45 pm – 12:53 pm
  - Pre-Open (DR) Matching: 12:53 pm – 01:00 pm
  - Continuous Trading (DR): 01:00 pm – 02:45 pm
  - Closing: 02:45 pm – 02:55 pm
  - Post-Closing: 02:55 pm – 03:05 pm
- **Feedback deadline:** 4:00 pm on May 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This mock trading session has no financial or market impact. Trades executed during the session carry no margin obligations, pay-in/pay-out obligations, and create no rights or liabilities. The session is purely operational, aimed at allowing trading members to test and familiarize themselves with EGR segment trading systems, including DR switchover procedures. Impact is limited to trading members in the EGR segment.
