## Summary

BSE has circulated a notice informing all listed companies that the Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) has issued notification No. SEBI/LAD-NRO/GN/2026/312 dated July 10, 2026, introducing further amendments to the SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015. These are titled the Securities and Exchange Board of India (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) (Second Amendment) Regulations, 2026, and will come into force upon publication in the Official Gazette.

## Key Points

- SEBI notification No. SEBI/LAD-NRO/GN/2026/312 dated July 10, 2026 amends the LODR Regulations, 2015.
- The amendment is titled the SEBI (LODR) (Second Amendment) Regulations, 2026.
- The regulations come into force on the date of publication in the Official Gazette.
- BSE has attached the full notification (SEBI_LODR_SecondAmendment_Regulations_2026) for reference.
- All listed companies are required to take note and comply accordingly.

## Regulatory Changes

The circular itself does not detail the specific substantive changes to the LODR Regulations; it directs listed companies to refer to the attached SEBI notification for the full text of the amendment.

## Compliance Requirements

Listed companies must review the attached SEBI notification and ensure their listing compliance practices, disclosures, and related processes align with the amended provisions once the regulations take effect.

## Important Dates

- Notification date: July 10, 2026 (SEBI/LAD-NRO/GN/2026/312)
- BSE circular date: July 22, 2026
- Effective date: Date of publication in the Official Gazette (specific date not stated in this notice)

## Impact Assessment

This is a regulatory compliance update applicable across all listed companies rather than a specific corporate action. While it does not have immediate market or trading impact, companies should review the underlying SEBI notification promptly to identify any changes to disclosure obligations, listing requirements, or governance norms that may require procedural or documentation updates.
