## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading for three Treasury Bills (T-Bills) that are scheduled to mature on their respective redemption dates. Trading Members are advised not to deal with these T-Bills effective February 10, 2026.

## Key Points

- Three T-Bills will be suspended from trading
- Suspension is due to approaching maturity/redemption dates
- Trading Members must cease dealing in these securities from February 10, 2026
- Notice reference: DR-817/2025-2026

## T-Bills Affected

| Scrip Code | ISIN | Particulars |
|------------|------|-------------|
| 805104 | IN002025Y206 | 182TB12226 |
| 805027 | IN002024Z446 | 364TB12226 |
| 805142 | IN002025X331 | 91TB120226 |

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must not execute any trades in the specified T-Bills from the effective date
- Members should update their systems to reflect the trading suspension
- No new orders should be accepted for these securities

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: February 09, 2026
- **Effective Date**: February 10, 2026
- **Action Required**: Immediate cessation of trading in listed T-Bills

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative action that occurs when debt instruments reach maturity. The suspension prevents trading in securities that are about to be redeemed, ensuring orderly market operations. Impact is limited to holders of these specific T-Bills who should prepare for redemption rather than secondary market trading.
