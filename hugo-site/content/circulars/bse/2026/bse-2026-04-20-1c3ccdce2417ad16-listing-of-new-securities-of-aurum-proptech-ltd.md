## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 25,482 new equity shares of Aurum PropTech Ltd. (Scrip Code: 539289) are permitted for trading effective Tuesday, April 21, 2026. These shares arise from the conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up shares of Rs. 5/- each, issued on a rights basis.

## Key Points

- 25,482 equity shares of Aurum PropTech Ltd. admitted for trading on BSE from April 21, 2026
- Shares issued pursuant to conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up at Rs. 5/- each on rights basis
- ISIN: INE898S01029
- Distinctive Numbers: 7,28,25,567 to 7,28,51,048
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification under BSE's Listing Operations department.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed and permitted to trade in these new securities from the effective date. No additional compliance action required beyond normal trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: April 20, 2026
- Trading Effective Date: April 21, 2026 (Tuesday)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The addition of 25,482 shares is a small incremental increase to Aurum PropTech Ltd.'s equity capital, resulting from a pre-approved rights issue conversion. The shares are pari-passu with existing shares, so no dilution differential or priority difference applies.
