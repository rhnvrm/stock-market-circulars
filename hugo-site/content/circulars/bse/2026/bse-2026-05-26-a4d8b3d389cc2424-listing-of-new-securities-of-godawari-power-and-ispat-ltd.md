## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 13,61,000 new equity shares of Godawari Power and Ispat Ltd. (Scrip Code: 532734, ISIN: INE177H01039) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Wednesday, May 27, 2026. The shares were issued on a preferential basis to non-promoters pursuant to conversion of warrants at an issue price of Rs. 245/- per share.

## Key Points

- 13,61,000 equity shares of Re. 1/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 244/- (total issue price: Rs. 245/-)
- Issued to Non-Promoter on preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 671364911 to 672725910
- Date of allotment: April 20, 2026
- ISIN: INE177H01039
- All 13,61,000 newly listed shares are subject to lock-in until November 30, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification under BSE Listing Operations.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed that these securities are now listed and eligible for trading from May 27, 2026. The lock-in applicable to all 13,61,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 671364911 to 672725910) must be observed until November 30, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** April 20, 2026
- **Trading Effective Date:** May 27, 2026 (Wednesday)
- **Lock-in Expiry:** November 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing of preferentially allotted shares resulting from warrant conversion. The entire batch of 13,61,000 shares is under lock-in until November 30, 2026, meaning no additional float enters the market immediately. The shares rank pari-passu with existing equity, so there is no structural change to shareholder rights. Market impact is expected to be minimal in the near term given the lock-in restriction.
