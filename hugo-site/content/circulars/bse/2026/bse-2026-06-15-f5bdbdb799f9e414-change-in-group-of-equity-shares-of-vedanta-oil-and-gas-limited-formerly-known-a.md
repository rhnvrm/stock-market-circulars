## Summary

BSE has notified all market participants that the equity shares of Vedanta Oil and Gas Limited (formerly known as Malco Energy Limited), bearing BSE scrip code 544782, will be reclassified and permitted to trade under the 'B' group effective Tuesday, June 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: Vedanta Oil and Gas Limited (formerly Malco Energy Limited)
- BSE Scrip Code: 544782
- Change: Equity shares moved to 'B' group
- Effective Date: Tuesday, June 30, 2026
- Notice Number: 20260615-9

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares of Vedanta Oil and Gas Limited are being reclassified into the 'B' group on BSE. Group classification on BSE determines trading settlement conditions and eligibility criteria applicable to the scrip.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action is required from listed companies or investors. Market participants (brokers, traders) should update their systems to reflect the new group classification for scrip code 544782 effective June 30, 2026.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: June 15, 2026
- Effective Date of Group Change: Tuesday, June 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative reclassification. Moving to 'B' group may affect settlement norms and margin requirements applicable to trades in this scrip. The impact is limited to traders and investors holding or transacting in Vedanta Oil and Gas Limited shares. No broader market impact is anticipated.
