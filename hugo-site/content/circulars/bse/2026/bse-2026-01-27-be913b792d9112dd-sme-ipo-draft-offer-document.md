## Summary

BSE has announced the availability of the Draft Offer Document for Paluck Technologies Limited's SME IPO, filed on January 20, 2026. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and is available for public comments for a period of 21 days from January 27, 2026.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Paluck Technologies Limited on January 20, 2026
- Document available on BSE SME website at www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Contains disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Public comment period of 21 days from notice date
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard procedural notice as per ICDR Regulations for SME IPO draft document publication.

## Compliance Requirements

- The Draft Offer Document must be hosted on the stock exchange website for at least 21 days
- Public stakeholders can submit comments within 21 days from January 27, 2026
- Comments should be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Company must comply with all disclosures specified under Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations

## Important Dates

- **January 20, 2026**: Draft Offer Document filed
- **January 27, 2026**: Notice date and start of public comment period
- **February 17, 2026**: Last date for public comments (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal immediate market impact as it is a standard procedural announcement for an SME IPO process. The impact is limited to:

- **Potential Investors**: Opportunity to review the draft offer document and provide feedback before the final offer document is published
- **Paluck Technologies Limited**: Part of the mandatory regulatory process for SME IPO listing on BSE
- **Market Participants**: Informational notice about upcoming SME IPO opportunity

The public comment period allows stakeholders to raise concerns or seek clarifications before the IPO proceeds to the next stage.
