## Summary

BSE has published the schedule for Registrar and Transfer Agent (RTA) visits to its premises for January 2026. The circular provides a list of companies serviced by Adroit Corporate Services Pvt. Ltd., one of the RTAs scheduled to visit BSE during the month. This is a routine administrative arrangement to facilitate investor services and coordination between the exchange and RTAs.

## Key Points

- RTA visit schedule published for January 2026
- Adroit Corporate Services Pvt. Ltd. is the RTA featured in this circular
- List includes 52+ companies serviced by Adroit Corporate Services
- Companies span various sectors including manufacturing, finance, real estate, textiles, and technology
- Scrip codes range from 501270 to 524606

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for listed entities. This is an informational circular regarding RTA operational schedules.

## Important Dates

- **January 2026**: Month during which Adroit Corporate Services Pvt. Ltd. is scheduled to visit BSE premises

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Nil. This is a routine operational circular with no impact on trading or market operations.

**Investor Impact**: Low. The RTA visits facilitate investor grievance redressal and service coordination but do not directly affect shareholders.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Standard administrative arrangement between BSE and RTAs to ensure smooth investor services for the listed companies.
