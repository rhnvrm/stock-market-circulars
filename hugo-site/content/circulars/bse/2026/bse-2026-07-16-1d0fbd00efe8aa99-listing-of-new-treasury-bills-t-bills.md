## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that three new Treasury Bills (T-Bills) will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange under the G GROUP - DEBT INSTRUMENTS category, effective July 17, 2026.

## Key Points

- Three T-Bills will be listed: a 91-day, a 182-day, and a 364-day Treasury Bill.
- Scrip code 805241, ISIN IN002026X156, Symbol 91TB151026, Market Lot 1, Maturity Date 15/10/2026.
- Scrip code 805242, ISIN IN002026Y154, Symbol 182T140127, Market Lot 1, Maturity Date 14/01/2027.
- Scrip code 805243, ISIN IN002026Z151, Symbol 364TB15727, Market Lot 1, Maturity Date 15/07/2027.
- These securities will not be available for trading two working days prior to their respective maturity/redemption dates (Maturity Date minus two trading days, excluding bank holidays).

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an administrative listing notice under the Exchange's standard debt segment procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members dealing in the debt market segment should update their systems to recognize the new scrip codes, ISINs, and symbols, and observe the trading cut-off dates ahead of each T-Bill's maturity.

## Important Dates

- Listing effective from: July 17, 2026
- Maturity date of 91TB151026: October 15, 2026
- Maturity date of 182T140127: January 14, 2027
- Maturity date of 364TB15727: July 15, 2027
- Trading halts two working days before each respective maturity date

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, low-impact listing notice affecting only trading members active in the debt market segment. No impact on equity markets or listed companies; queries may be directed to the Exchange's listing department contacts provided.
