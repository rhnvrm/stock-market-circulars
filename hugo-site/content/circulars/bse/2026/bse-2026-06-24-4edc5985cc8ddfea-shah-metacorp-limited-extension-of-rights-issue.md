## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the Rights Issue of Shah Metacorp Limited has been extended. The issue, which was originally scheduled to close on Wednesday, June 24, 2026, has been extended to Friday, July 3, 2026. This notice is issued in continuation of BSE Notice No. 20260609-51.

## Key Points

- Rights Issue of Shah Metacorp Limited has been extended by approximately 9 days.
- Original closing date: Wednesday, June 24, 2026.
- New/extended closing date: Friday, July 3, 2026.
- This is a continuation of earlier notice no. 20260609-51.
- Notification issued by Mangesh Tayde, Vice President, BSE Listing Business Relationship Department.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular is an administrative update extending the timeline of an already-announced rights issue.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should note the revised closing date of July 3, 2026 for the Rights Issue.
- Eligible shareholders wishing to subscribe to the Rights Issue must do so before the extended deadline of July 3, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Original Issue Closing Date:** June 24, 2026
- **Extended Issue Closing Date:** July 3, 2026
- **Circular Date:** June 24, 2026
- **Reference Notice:** BSE Notice No. 20260609-51

## Impact Assessment

The extension provides additional time for eligible shareholders of Shah Metacorp Limited to subscribe to the rights issue. Trading members handling rights entitlements should update their systems and inform clients accordingly. The impact is limited to shareholders and trading members directly involved with Shah Metacorp Limited's rights issue.
