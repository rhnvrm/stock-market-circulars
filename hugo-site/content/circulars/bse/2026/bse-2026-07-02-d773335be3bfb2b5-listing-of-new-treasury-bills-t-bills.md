## Summary

BSE has informed Trading Members that three new Treasury Bills (T-Bills) will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange under the G GROUP - DEBT INSTRUMENTS segment, effective July 3, 2026.

## Key Points

- Three new T-Bills are being listed: a 91-day T-Bill (Scrip code 805235, ISIN IN002026X131, Symbol 91TB011026), a 182-day T-Bill (Scrip code 805236, ISIN IN002026Y139, Symbol 182T311226), and a 364-day T-Bill (Scrip code 805237, ISIN IN002026Z136, Symbol 364TB01727)
- All three securities have a market lot of 1
- Securities are listed in the G GROUP - DEBT INSTRUMENTS category
- Trading in each security will be halted two working days prior to its maturity/redemption date (Maturity Date minus two Trading Days, excluding Bank Holidays)
- Queries can be directed to BSE at 2272 8352/5753/8597

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing notification, not a regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members should update their systems to recognize the new scrip codes, ISINs, and symbols, and must note the trading cut-off (T-2 working days before maturity) for each instrument to avoid attempting trades in securities approaching redemption.

## Important Dates

- Listing effective date: July 3, 2026
- 91TB011026 maturity date: October 1, 2026
- 182T311226 maturity date: December 31, 2026
- 364TB01727 maturity date: July 1, 2027
- Trading halts 2 working days prior to each respective maturity date

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative listing of government Treasury Bills on BSE's debt segment. It has no direct impact on equity markets or listed companies. Debt market participants and trading members dealing in G-Sec/T-Bill instruments should update their trading systems accordingly and track the maturity-linked trading halt dates.
