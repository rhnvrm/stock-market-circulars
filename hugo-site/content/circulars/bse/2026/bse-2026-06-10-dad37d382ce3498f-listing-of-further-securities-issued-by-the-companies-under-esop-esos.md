## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that additional equity shares issued by 13 companies under their Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP/ESOS) are listed and admitted for trading on the Exchange effective Thursday, June 11, 2026. None of the newly listed shares carry any lock-in restriction.

## Key Points

- 13 companies have listed fresh ESOP/ESOS shares totalling over 1.6 million new securities across all issuers
- Effective date for trading: Thursday, June 11, 2026
- No lock-in period applies to any of the listed securities
- Companies span banking, insurance, energy, infrastructure, FMCG, and industrials sectors
- Largest allotment by share count: ESAF Small Finance Bank Ltd (275,284 shares at face value ₹10)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice under existing ESOP/ESOS listing framework.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to take note of the updated security ranges for the listed companies and update their systems accordingly for trading from June 11, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 10, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** June 11, 2026 (Thursday)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal broader market impact. Each allotment represents a small incremental increase in the floating supply of the respective securities. Detailed allotment ranges are as follows:
