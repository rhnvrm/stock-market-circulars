## Summary

BSE has updated the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework, announcing new inclusions attracting minimum 100% margin from June 1, 2026, downgrading certain securities to Stage I, shifting qualifying securities from Rolling Settlement to Trade-for-Trade, and releasing a list of securities exiting the ESM framework as of May 27, 2026.

## Key Points

- Securities in Annexure I will attract a minimum 100% margin on all open positions as on May 29, 2026, and on new positions created from June 1, 2026.
- Certain securities are being moved to a lower stage (Stage I) under the ESM Framework with effect from May 29, 2026.
- All securities qualifying under ESM will be shifted from the Rolling Settlement segment to the Trade-for-Trade (TFT) segment w.e.f. June 1, 2026.
- Securities moving to Stage II will be subject to Trade-for-Trade with a 2% price band and Periodic Call Auction w.e.f. May 29, 2026.
- Securities exiting the ESM Framework as of May 27, 2026 are listed in Annexure II.
- A consolidated list of all securities currently under ESM is provided in Annexure III.
- ESM operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the Exchanges.
- Shortlisting under ESM is purely for market surveillance purposes and should not be construed as an adverse action against the concerned company.

## Regulatory Changes

This notice references and builds upon prior ESM circulars:
- Notice No. 20230602-44 (June 2, 2023)
- Notice No. 20230718-46 (July 18, 2023)
- Notice No. 20240809-42 (August 9, 2024)
- Notice No. 20240920-63 (September 20, 2024)
- Notice No. 20241004-65 (October 4, 2024)
- Notice No. 20250725-61 (July 25, 2025)

The price band for securities exiting the ESM framework will revert to the band applicable before their inclusion, unless they remain subject to another surveillance measure.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure collection of minimum 100% margin on ESM-listed open positions as on May 29, 2026 and all new positions from June 1, 2026.
- Members must adhere to Trade-for-Trade settlement rules for all securities shifted into TFT under ESM.
- Stage II securities must be traded within the 2% price band and via Periodic Call Auction mechanism.
- Members may contact bse.surv@bseindia.com for any clarifications.

## Important Dates

- **May 27, 2026**: Securities in Annexure II exit the ESM framework; their prior price bands are reinstated.
- **May 29, 2026**: Stage I downgrades take effect; Stage II securities move to Trade-for-Trade with 2% price band and Periodic Call Auction; 100% margin applies to open positions as of this date.
- **June 1, 2026**: New ESM inclusions (Annexure I) shift to Trade-for-Trade segment; 100% margin applies to all new positions created from this date.

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a broad and significant market impact. Securities newly placed under ESM face severely restricted trading conditions — mandatory Trade-for-Trade settlement eliminates netting benefits, and the 100% margin requirement substantially increases the cost of holding positions. Stage II securities face additional constraints via 2% price bands and Periodic Call Auction, further limiting liquidity. Conversely, securities removed from the framework (Annexure II) regain normal price bands and settlement terms, improving their tradability. Market participants holding positions in affected securities should review their margin obligations and settlement procedures ahead of the effective dates.
