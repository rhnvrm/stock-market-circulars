## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation confirming that Deccan Bearings Ltd (CIN: L29130MH1985PLC035747) has officially changed its name to Satani Bearings Limited, effective January 5, 2026. The company is incorporated in Maharashtra and is limited by shares.

## Key Points

- Company name changed from **Deccan Bearings Ltd** to **Satani Bearings Limited**
- CIN remains unchanged: L29130MH1985PLC035747
- Certificate issued by Brijesh Kain, Central Processing Centre, MCA
- Company is registered as a company limited by shares
- Originally incorporated as Deccan Bearings Ltd
- Registered address: 136, B Wing Ansa Industrial Estate, Saki Vihar Road, Sakinaka, Andheri (East), Mumbai – 400072, Maharashtra

## Regulatory Changes

Pursuant to Rule 29 of the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014, the name change has been formally recorded. The company must display its old name (Deccan Bearings Ltd) alongside the new name (Satani Bearings Limited) for a period of two years at all places where a company is required to display its name, as per Section 12 of the Companies Act.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display the old name **Deccan Bearings Ltd** together with the new name **Satani Bearings Limited** for **two years** from the effective date, wherever the name is required to be displayed under Section 12 of the Companies Act.
- Stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company and its directors on the MCA website: [www.mca.gov.in/MCA21](http://www.mca.gov.in/MCA21).
- The name change does not affect the rights or liabilities of any stakeholders.

## Important Dates

- **January 5, 2026**: Effective date of name change (date of Certificate of Incorporation)
- **January 5, 2028**: End of mandatory two-year period for displaying the old name alongside the new name

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative corporate action with minimal market impact. The name change does not alter the company's legal structure, CIN, registered address, or the rights and liabilities of shareholders, creditors, or other stakeholders. Investors and market participants should update their records to reflect the new name — Satani Bearings Limited — while noting that all prior obligations and entitlements under Deccan Bearings Ltd remain intact.
