## Summary

BSE has published the Demat Auction list for Settlement No. 804 dated January 23, 2026. This circular lists securities and quantities available for auction due to delivery shortages in the demat settlement process. The auction includes shares from 112 different companies with quantities ranging from 1 share to over 157,000 shares.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 804
- Auction Date: January 23, 2026
- Total securities listed: 112 companies
- Largest quantity: Harshil Agro (157,348 shares)
- Other significant quantities: Rajnish Wellness (130,727 shares), ACI Infocom (28,412 shares)
- Includes major companies like Reliance Industries, ITC, Tata Motors, Bajaj Auto, and Adani Green
- Scrip codes and exact quantities provided for each security

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational circular for demat auction settlement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must participate in the demat auction for Settlement No. 804
- Buyers/sellers with delivery obligations must settle through the auction mechanism
- Standard BSE auction rules and timelines apply

## Important Dates

- Auction Date: January 23, 2026
- Settlement No: 804

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational circular with low market impact. The demat auction mechanism is a standard process for settling delivery shortages. While the list includes some major companies like Reliance Industries, ITC, and Tata Motors, the quantities involved are relatively small compared to their trading volumes. The auction primarily affects specific counterparties with settlement obligations rather than the broader market. Investors in the listed securities may experience minor price movements during the auction process.
