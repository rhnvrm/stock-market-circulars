## Summary

ICCL (Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd.) has published the settlement calendar for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) pertaining to ORTIN GLOBAL LIMITED. The bidding window opens on June 5, 2026, and closes on June 18, 2026, under Settlement No. 2026066. Obligations are downloadable on July 2, 2026, with final settlement on July 3, 2026.

## Key Points

- Settlement Type: Takeover; Settlement No. 2026066
- Bid Start Date: June 5, 2026; Bid End Date: June 18, 2026
- Obligation Download Date: July 2, 2026
- Settlement Date: July 3, 2026
- Demat shares must be delivered exclusively via the Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism available through both CDSL and NSDL depositories
- Market Type to be used in depository system: "(Takeover)"; Settlement No. 2026066
- CDSL & NSDL early pay-in securities will be earmarked/blocked in client accounts under the requisite market type and settlement number
- Members/Custodians are NOT required to upload any EPN file
- Members should refer to file MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo for client-wise Stamp Duty charges

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. Members/Custodians are directed to refer to Notice No. 20200528-32 dated May 28, 2020, regarding settlement as per Revised Guidelines of Mechanism for acquisition of shares through Stock Exchange.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and Custodians must ensure early pay-in of securities is reflected in BSE/ICCL systems before prescribed timelines for bid acceptance/confirmation on the BSE OTB platform
- Early pay-in must be completed sufficiently in advance of closure of the BSE OTB Bidding window (for members placing bids) and the confirmation window (for Custodians confirming bids)
- Use Market Type "(Takeover)" and Settlement No. 2026066 in all depository system entries

## Impact Assessment

This notice is operationally relevant to Members and Custodians participating in the takeover offer for ORTIN GLOBAL LIMITED. The use of the Early Pay-in mechanism is mandatory, and participants must coordinate depository entries ahead of OTB platform deadlines to avoid bid rejection. General market impact is low; impact is concentrated on entities involved in this specific acquisition window.
