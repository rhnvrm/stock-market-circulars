## Summary

The Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd. (ICCL) has published the trading and settlement programme for the Electronic Gold Receipt (EGR) segment on BSE for the period from 01 June 2026 to 30 June 2026. The notice (No. 20260526-1) provides settlement numbers, depository submission dates, pay-in/pay-out dates, and corresponding auction settlement details for 22 settlement cycles.

## Key Points

- Covers settlement numbers DR-045/2026-2027 through DR-066/2026-2027 (Sett. No. 2627045–2627066).
- Each regular settlement has a corresponding auction settlement (RA-series) with auction pay-in/pay-out one business day later.
- Regular pay-in deadline is 03:00 p.m.; pay-out of funds and securities is effected by 04:00 p.m.
- Auction pay-in deadline is 9:00 a.m.; members must submit instructions to depositories/banks by 8:50 a.m.
- Settlement DR-064/2026-2027 (trading date 26-06-2026) has pay-in on 29-06-2026, reflecting a weekend gap.
- Last two settlement cycles (DR-065 and DR-066) extend pay-out into July 2026 (01-07-2026 and 02-07-2026 respectively).

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational calendar notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members trading in the EGR segment must submit pay-in instructions to depositories/banks by 03:00 p.m. on the respective pay-in dates for regular settlements.
- For auction settlements, pay-in instructions must be submitted by 8:50 a.m. on the auction pay-in date.
- Members should plan settlement obligations in line with the published calendar, noting holiday-adjusted dates (e.g., weekends causing T+1 skips).

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine monthly reference document for clearing members and depositories participating in the EGR segment. No changes to settlement timelines or procedures are introduced. Members should note that the end-of-month settlements (DR-063 through DR-066) have adjusted pay-out dates due to weekends and the month-end, with the final cycle paying out on 02-07-2026.
