## Summary

BSE has published the list of securities to be auctioned under Settlement No. 793 dated January 8, 2026. This demat auction covers 100 different scrips with varying quantities, ranging from 1 share to 173,209 shares. The auction is conducted to fulfill delivery obligations when trading members fail to deliver securities on the settlement date.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 793
- Date: January 8, 2026
- Total number of scrips: 100
- Largest quantity: STANDARD CAPITAL LTD (511700) - 173,209 shares
- Other significant quantities: CWD Limited (22,500 shares), TAHMAR ENTERPRISES (10,543 shares), BALU FORGE INDUSTRIES (4,902 shares), SHINE FASHION (4,000 shares)
- Covers diverse sectors including auto, technology, banking, pharmaceuticals, metals, and specialty chemicals

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine settlement auction notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members with delivery obligations in the listed securities must participate in the auction process
- Members must settle their outstanding delivery obligations through the auction mechanism
- Auction participants must follow BSE's auction settlement procedures and timelines
- Payment and delivery obligations arising from the auction must be fulfilled as per BSE guidelines

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: January 8, 2026
- Settlement Number: 793
- Auction date and timing would be communicated separately as per BSE auction schedule

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - Affects liquidity and pricing for the 100 listed scrips during the auction period. Standard Capital Ltd faces the highest exposure with over 173,000 shares.

**Member Impact:** Direct impact on trading members who have failed to deliver securities in these scrips. Members will need to participate in the auction to close out their positions or face penalty.

**Investor Impact:** Minimal direct impact on retail investors. However, auction activity may cause temporary price volatility in the affected securities.

**Operational Impact:** Routine settlement process for handling delivery defaults. Part of BSE's regular market operations to ensure settlement discipline.
