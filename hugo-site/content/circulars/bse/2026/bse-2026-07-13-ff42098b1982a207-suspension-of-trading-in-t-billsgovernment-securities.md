## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that two Government Treasury Bills — 364TB16726 (Scrip Code 805093, ISIN IN002025Z161) and 91TB16726 (Scrip Code 805202, ISIN IN002026X024) — are maturing on their redemption date. Trading members are advised not to deal in these securities with effect from July 14, 2026, as per reference DR-671/2026-2027.

## Key Points

- Two T-bills reach maturity and will be suspended from trading
- Scrip Code 805093 (364TB16726, ISIN IN002025Z161)
- Scrip Code 805202 (91TB16726, ISIN IN002026X024)
- Trading suspension effective July 14, 2026
- Reference number: DR-671/2026-2027

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine operational notice regarding maturity of existing government debt instruments, not a new regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of the Exchange must not deal in the aforesaid T-bills/Government Securities from July 14, 2026 onward, as they have reached their redemption/maturity date.

## Important Dates

- **July 13, 2026**: Notice issued
- **July 14, 2026**: Effective date of trading suspension (redemption date) for both instruments

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard administrative notice affecting only the debt/G-Sec segment for two specific matured T-bills. No impact on equity markets or listed companies; relevant only to trading members holding or dealing in these specific government securities.
