## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 79,00,000 new equity shares of Motisons Jewellers Limited (Scrip Code: 544053, ISIN: INE0FRK01020) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Thursday, June 11, 2026. The shares were issued on a preferential basis to Non-Promoters pursuant to conversion of warrants at an issue price of Rs. 17/- per share (face value Re. 1/- plus premium of Rs. 16/-).

## Key Points

- 79,00,000 equity shares of Re. 1/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 16/- per share (issue price Rs. 17/-)
- Shares issued to Non-Promoters on a preferential basis via conversion of warrants
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution Numbers: 988460001 to 996360000
- Date of Allotment: February 10, 2026
- ISIN: INE0FRK01020

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification under BSE's Listing Operations department.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed that these new securities are admitted to trading. No specific compliance action is required from members beyond standard trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** February 10, 2026
- **Trading Effective From:** June 11, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry:** December 15, 2026 (applicable to all 79,00,000 shares, Dist. Nos. 988460001 to 996360000)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. The addition of 79,00,000 new equity shares increases the total share capital of Motisons Jewellers Limited. All newly listed shares are subject to lock-in until December 15, 2026, restricting their tradability until that date. The pari-passu ranking ensures no differential rights versus existing shareholders.
