## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 1,23,076 new equity shares of Sunita Tools Limited (Scrip Code: 544001) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Thursday, March 5, 2026. These shares were issued to promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 1,23,076 equity shares of face value Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 315/- (issue price Rs. 325/-)
- Issued to Promoters on a preferential basis via conversion of warrants
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 6110001 to 6233076
- ISIN: INE0Q1S01010
- Scrip Code: 544001

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for newly allotted securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the listing and permit trading in these securities from March 5, 2026. The newly listed shares are subject to lock-in restrictions applicable to promoter allotments.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** 20 October 2025
- **Trading Effective From:** 5 March 2026
- **Lock-in Period Expiry:** 30 September 2027 (for all 1,23,076 shares)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. The allotment represents a modest increase in Sunita Tools Limited's equity share capital. All newly listed shares are under promoter lock-in until September 30, 2027, meaning they will not be freely tradable in the near term. No immediate liquidity impact on the broader market.
