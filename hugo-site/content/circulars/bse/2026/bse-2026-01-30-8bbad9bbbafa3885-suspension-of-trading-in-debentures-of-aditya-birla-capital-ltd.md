## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in non-convertible debentures of Aditya Birla Capital Ltd (ISIN: INE860H07CL4, Code: 957542) effective February 6, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing the record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to ABCL 8.85% debentures maturing on February 23, 2026
- ISIN: INE860H07CL4, BSE Code: 957542
- Record date fixed as February 6, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- No dealings permitted from February 6, 2026 onwards
- Circular reference: DR-815/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard corporate action notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from February 6, 2026
- Trading members are required to take note of the suspension and inform their clients accordingly
- All dealing activities in ISIN INE860H07CL4 must cease from the no-dealings date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 30, 2026
- **Record Date**: February 6, 2026
- **No Dealings From**: February 6, 2026
- **Debenture Maturity**: February 23, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - affects holders and potential traders of this specific debenture series only. The suspension is temporary and routine for the redemption process.

**Operational Impact**: Low - standard procedure for debenture redemption. Trading members need to update their systems to block trades in the specified ISIN from February 6, 2026.

**Investor Impact**: Medium - existing debenture holders will receive redemption proceeds and final interest payment as per the record date. No new positions can be taken from the suspension date.
