## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing all market participants that the equity shares of Vedanta Aluminium Metal Limited (BSE Scrip Code: 544780) will be reclassified and permitted to trade under the 'B' group effective Tuesday, June 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- Vedanta Aluminium Metal Limited (BSE Scrip Code: 544780) is being moved to 'B' group for equity trading.
- The change is effective from Tuesday, June 30, 2026.
- The notice is issued by BSE's Listing Operations Department.

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares of Vedanta Aluminium Metal Limited will be reclassified under BSE's 'B' group category. The 'B' group on BSE typically includes companies that do not meet the criteria for the 'A' group and are subject to standard trading norms applicable to that segment.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action is required from investors or market participants. Brokers and trading members should update their systems to reflect the new group classification for Vedanta Aluminium Metal Limited (Scrip Code: 544780) ahead of the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 15, 2026
- **Effective Date:** Tuesday, June 30, 2026 (migration to 'B' group)

## Impact Assessment

The reclassification to the 'B' group is an administrative change by BSE and has minimal direct impact on shareholders or trading activity. It may affect the visibility and liquidity of the stock to some degree, as institutional investors and index trackers may have preferences tied to group classifications. Existing shareholders are not required to take any action.
