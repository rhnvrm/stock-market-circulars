## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 24,84,444 new equity shares of Axel Polymers Ltd. (Scrip Code: 513642) issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis. The shares will commence trading on February 5, 2026, at an issue price of Rs. 45/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- with a premium of Rs. 35/-). These shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares and are subject to a lock-in period until August 10, 2026.

## Key Points

- 24,84,444 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 35/-
- Total issue price: Rs. 45/- per share
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis
- Allotment date: December 13, 2025
- Trading commencement: February 5, 2026
- Distinctive numbers: 8516681 to 11001124
- ISIN: INE197C01012
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for new securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the listing of these new securities effective February 5, 2026
- The allotted shares are subject to lock-in restrictions until August 10, 2026
- All 24,84,444 shares (Dist. Nos. 8516681-11001124) are locked-in

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 13, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: February 5, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry**: August 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a company-specific preferential allotment. The issuance represents capital raising activity by Axel Polymers Ltd. to non-promoter investors. The lock-in period until August 2026 restricts immediate liquidity of these shares. Trading members should update their systems to reflect the increased share capital and distinctive number ranges.
