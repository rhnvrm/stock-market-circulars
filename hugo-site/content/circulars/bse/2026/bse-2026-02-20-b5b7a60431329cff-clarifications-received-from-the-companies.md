## Summary

BSE received and published clarifications from multiple listed companies in response to its email dated February 19, 2026 (Letter No. L/SURV/ONL/PV/SJ/2025-2026/3380), which sought explanations for recent price movements in their securities. The companies — Accretion Nutraveda Limited (Scrip Code: 544694), Associated Coaters Limited (Scrip Code: 544183), and BLB Limited (Scrip Code: 532290) — each affirmed full compliance with SEBI disclosure requirements and attributed the price movements to market conditions.

## Key Points

- BSE's surveillance team issued price-movement clarification requests to multiple companies via email dated February 19, 2026.
- Accretion Nutraveda Ltd (Scrip Code: 544694) confirmed all material and price-sensitive disclosures have been made promptly under Regulation 30 of SEBI (LODR) 2015; management denied any involvement in or knowledge of the price fluctuations.
- Associated Coaters Ltd (Scrip Code: 544183 / BSE Symbol: ASSOCIATED) similarly reiterated adherence to Regulation 30 and stated price movement is purely market-driven with no company control or knowledge of underlying reasons.
- BLB Ltd (Scrip Code: 532290) submitted a clarification on price movement (full text partially available).
- All companies committed to timely future disclosures should any material event arise.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The circular documents standard company responses under existing SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015, specifically Regulation 30 governing disclosure of material events.

## Compliance Requirements

- Listed companies must respond promptly to BSE surveillance queries regarding unusual price movements.
- Companies are required to continuously comply with Regulation 30 of SEBI (LODR) 2015 by disclosing all material and price-sensitive information to stock exchanges in a timely manner.
- Company Secretaries / Compliance Officers are responsible for submitting such clarifications on behalf of the company.

## Important Dates

- **February 19, 2026**: BSE issued price-movement clarification requests to the companies (Letter No. L/SURV/ONL/PV/SJ/2025-2026/3380).
- **February 20, 2026**: Accretion Nutraveda Ltd, Associated Coaters Ltd, and BLB Ltd submitted their respective clarification responses.

## Impact Assessment

This circular has low direct market impact. It reflects BSE's routine surveillance activity of querying companies during periods of abnormal price movements. No undisclosed material information, corporate actions, or regulatory violations were reported by any of the responding companies. Investors should note that the price movements in these securities (Scrip Codes: 544694, 544183, 532290) have been characterised by the respective managements as purely market-driven. The circular serves as a transparency mechanism and does not signal any imminent corporate event or regulatory action.
