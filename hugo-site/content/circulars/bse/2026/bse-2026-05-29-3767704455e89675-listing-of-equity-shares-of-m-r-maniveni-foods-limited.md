## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members that the equity shares of M.R. Maniveni Foods Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The scrip is assigned code 544768, symbol MANIVENI, and ISIN INE0YD301010. The exact listing date and further details of the securities will be communicated through a subsequent notice.

## Key Points

- M.R. Maniveni Foods Limited equity shares are approved for listing on BSE under the SME segment.
- Scrip Code: 544768 | Symbol: MANIVENI | ISIN: INE0YD301010
- The listing date has not yet been specified; a separate notice will provide that information.
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard pre-listing intimation notice to trading members.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the upcoming listing of MANIVENI on the BSE SME platform and await the subsequent notice for the confirmed listing date and full security details before initiating any trades.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 29 May 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced via a separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This notice has limited immediate market impact as it serves only as a preliminary intimation. The listing of M.R. Maniveni Foods Limited is confined to the BSE SME segment, which typically attracts a smaller investor base. Trading members should monitor for the follow-up notice containing the confirmed listing date and detailed security information.
