## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document (DRHP) of Horizon Reclaim (India) Limited, filed on March 06, 2026, on the BSE SME website. The document complies with Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and is available for public review for a minimum of 21 days. Stakeholders are invited to submit comments via email.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Horizon Reclaim (India) Limited on March 06, 2026
- Document made available on BSE SME website for at least 21 days from March 09, 2026
- Disclosures comply with Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations
- Public comments are solicited within 21 days of notice date
- DRHP hosted at BSE SME portal: https://www.bsesme.com/download/362363/SME_IPO%20InPrinciple/DRHP_HorizonReclaim_20260306223648.pdf

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard procedural notice under ICDR Regulations requiring public disclosure of SME IPO draft offer documents.

## Compliance Requirements

- Interested parties and public commenters must submit any comments on the Draft Offer Document within 21 days from March 09, 2026
- Comments to be sent to: equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Filing Date:** March 06, 2026
- **Notice Date:** March 09, 2026
- **Public Comment Deadline:** On or before March 30, 2026 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has limited broader market impact. It is specific to the upcoming SME IPO of Horizon Reclaim (India) Limited. Prospective investors and analysts interested in the SME segment may review the DRHP for investment evaluation. No existing listed securities or trading operations are affected.
