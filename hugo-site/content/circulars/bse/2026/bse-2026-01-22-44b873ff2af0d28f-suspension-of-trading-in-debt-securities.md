## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in debt securities of IL&FS Financial Services Limited effective January 22, 2026. The suspension is due to the maturity of the 8.90% private debt securities (ISIN: INE121H08073, Scrip Code: 953460) on January 27, 2026. Trading members are advised not to deal in these securities from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Debt security: IL&FS Financial Services Limited 8.90% bond
- ISIN: INE121H08073
- Scrip Code: 953460
- Maturity Date: January 27, 2026
- Trading suspension effective: January 22, 2026
- Circular Reference: DR-805/2025-2026
- Notice Number: 20260122-2

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for debt security maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified IL&FS Financial Services Limited debt securities from January 22, 2026 onwards
- Trading members must take note of the suspension and inform relevant stakeholders
- No new positions or dealings permitted in scrip code 953460

## Important Dates

- **January 22, 2026**: Trading suspension effective date
- **January 27, 2026**: Maturity date for the debt securities
- **Notice Date**: January 22, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders of this specific IL&FS Financial Services debt instrument. The suspension is a routine procedure preceding maturity and affects only this particular ISIN.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to prevent trading in scrip code 953460. Investors holding these securities should expect redemption at maturity as per the bond terms.
