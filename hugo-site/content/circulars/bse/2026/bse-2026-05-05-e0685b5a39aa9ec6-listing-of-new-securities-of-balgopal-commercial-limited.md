## Summary

BSE has listed 19,00,000 new equity shares of Balgopal Commercial Limited (Scrip Code: 539834) effective Wednesday, May 6, 2026. The shares were issued to promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to the conversion of warrants at an issue price of Rs. 60/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- with a premium of Rs. 50/-). These shares rank pari-passu with the existing equity shares of the company and are subject to a lock-in period until December 31, 2027.

## Key Points

- 19,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 50/- (total issue price: Rs. 60/- per share)
- Issued to promoters on preferential basis via conversion of warrants
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 19010001 to 20910000
- ISIN: INE119R01014
- Date of allotment: March 21, 2026
- Trading permitted effective May 6, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice for newly allotted securities following a preferential issue and warrant conversion.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to permit trading of the newly listed shares from May 6, 2026
- The entire 19,00,000 shares allotted to promoters are subject to lock-in and cannot be traded until December 31, 2027

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** March 21, 2026
- **Trading Effective Date:** May 6, 2026 (Wednesday)
- **Lock-in Expiry:** December 31, 2027 (applicable to all 19,00,000 shares, dist. nos. 19010001 to 20910000)

## Impact Assessment

This listing represents a promoter-level capital infusion via warrant conversion in Balgopal Commercial Limited, a small-cap equity scrip. The addition of 19,00,000 shares increases the total share count, which may dilute existing shareholders proportionally. However, since all newly listed shares are under lock-in until end of 2027, there is no immediate float impact or selling pressure from these shares. Market impact is minimal and confined to existing investors and trading members of this specific scrip.
