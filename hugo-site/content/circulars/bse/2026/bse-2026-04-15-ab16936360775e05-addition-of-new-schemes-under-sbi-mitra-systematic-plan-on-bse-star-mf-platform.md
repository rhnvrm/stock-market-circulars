## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260415-10 dated April 15, 2026, announcing the addition of new mutual fund schemes under the SBI MITRA Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The eligible schemes are detailed in the attached annexure titled 'Annexure_SBI_MITRA_SIP_Eligible_Schemes'.

## Key Points

- Notice No.: 20260415-10, dated April 15, 2026
- Category: Trading | Segment: Mutual Fund
- Department: Trading Operations
- New schemes have been added under the SBI MITRA Systematic Plan on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Annexure listing all eligible schemes is attached to the notice

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced. This is an operational update expanding the list of schemes available under the SBI MITRA SIP facility on BSE StAR MF.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements are mandated for market participants. Intermediaries and distributors using the BSE StAR MF Platform may refer to the attached annexure to update their systems and offerings with the newly added SBI MITRA SIP-eligible schemes.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: April 15, 2026
- Effective Date: Not explicitly stated; schemes are available as per the annexure from the notice date.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited and operational in nature. Investors and distributors using the BSE StAR MF Platform gain access to additional SBI MITRA Systematic Plan schemes, expanding investment options. There is no adverse market impact; this is a routine platform enhancement for mutual fund systematic investment.
