## Summary

BSE has issued the live activities schedule for the Offer to Buy (OTB) – Acquisition Window (Takeover) of shares of FABINO ENTERPRISES LIMITED (Symbol: FABINO, ISIN: INE0DRT01018, Scrip Code: 543444). The offer window runs from Monday, 29th June 2026 to Friday, 10th July 2026. Trading Members and Custodian Members are directed to follow the prescribed schedule and operational instructions.

## Key Points

- Offer size is up to 5,46,000 equity shares at an offer price of Rs. 10.00 per share (face value Rs. 20.00)
- Bid Offering Period: 29th June 2026 to 10th July 2026, 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM daily
- Give-up / Take-up Period: 29th June 2026 to 9th July 2026, 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM
- On the last day (10th July 2026): Give-up closes at 3:30 PM; Take-up closes at 4:00 PM
- Bidding open to categories: FII, FI, MF, IC, Other QIBs, Other NIB, Company, and Individual investors
- OTB module accessible via iBBS platform and RTRMS on both Internet and Leased Line URLs
- Trading Members must ensure UCC of investors is registered with correct PAN and category in the Exchange database
- Only shares held in demat form are eligible for bidding

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This notice operationalizes an existing Acquisition Window (Takeover) framework under SEBI Takeover Regulations, referencing prior Exchange Notices 20150930-1 and 20170203-29 for operational instructions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must verify that investor UCC records are updated with correct PAN numbers and investor categories in the BSE UCC database before submitting bids
- Members and Custodians should note that End of Issue files on the Extranet will only be generated if securities are accepted; no files will be generated if no securities are accepted
- Members requiring new login IDs or password resets for iBBS, RTRMS, or Extranet must contact BSE Trading Operations at 022-22728005 or user.trdops@bseindia.com

## Impact Assessment

This circular is operationally relevant to Trading Members, Custodian Members, and investors holding shares of FABINO ENTERPRISES LIMITED in demat form. The offer price of Rs. 10.00 per share is below the face value of Rs. 20.00, which is notable for participating shareholders evaluating the offer. The offer covers up to 5,46,000 equity shares. Members should ensure systems are configured to access iBBS and RTRMS platforms and that investor UCC details are current before the opening date of 29th June 2026.
