## Summary

BSE has published the details of anchor investor allocation for the public issue of GSP Crop Science Limited. The circular provides a list of anchor investors, the number of equity shares allocated to each, and the anchor investor allocation price, as required under SEBI regulations prior to the opening of the public issue.

## Key Points

- Anchor investor allocation was completed for the GSP Crop Science Limited IPO on March 13, 2026.
- The circular lists all anchor investors along with the number of equity shares allotted to each.
- Anchor investor shares are subject to a mandatory lock-in period of 30 days from the date of allotment in the public issue.
- The allocation price to anchor investors is the same as the IPO price (upper end of price band or fixed price).
- This disclosure is mandated by SEBI ICDR Regulations prior to the issue opening.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular is a standard disclosure under SEBI (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2018, which require issuers to disclose anchor investor allocations one day before the issue opens to the public.

## Compliance Requirements

- The issuer (GSP Crop Science Limited) and its Book Running Lead Managers (BRLMs) are required to publish anchor investor allocation details on the stock exchange platform before the issue opening date.
- Anchor investors are subject to a 30-day lock-in on their allotted shares from the date of allotment in the IPO.
- No further action required by trading members or investors beyond standard IPO application procedures.

## Important Dates

- Anchor investor allocation date: March 13, 2026
- Public issue open date: Expected March 14, 2026 (one business day after anchor allocation)
- Anchor investor lock-in expiry: 30 days from IPO allotment date

## Impact Assessment

The allocation of shares to anchor investors in the GSP Crop Science Limited IPO signals institutional confidence in the issue. Anchor investor participation is often viewed as a positive indicator for retail and non-institutional investors. The lock-in period ensures stability in the stock price immediately post-listing. Market impact is limited to participants in the GSP Crop Science IPO; broader market operations are unaffected.
