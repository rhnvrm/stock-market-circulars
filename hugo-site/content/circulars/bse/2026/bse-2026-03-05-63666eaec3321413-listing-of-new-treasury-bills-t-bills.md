## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that three new Treasury Bills (T-Bills) will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange under the G GROUP - DEBT INSTRUMENTS segment effective March 06, 2026. The T-Bills include 91-day, 182-day, and 364-day instruments.

## Key Points

- Three new T-Bills listed effective March 06, 2026 under G GROUP - DEBT INSTRUMENTS
- Scrip Code 805187 (ISIN: IN002025X489, Symbol: 91TB040626) matures on June 04, 2026
- Scrip Code 805188 (ISIN: IN002025Y487, Symbol: 182TB30926) matures on September 03, 2026
- Scrip Code 805189 (ISIN: IN002025Z484, Symbol: 364TB40327) matures on March 04, 2027
- Market lot for all three T-Bills is 1
- Trading will cease two working days prior to the respective Maturity/Redemption Date (excluding bank holidays)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification under existing G GROUP - DEBT INSTRUMENTS framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the trading cessation rule: securities will not be available for trading two (2) working days prior to the Maturity/Redemption Date (T minus 2 trading days, excluding bank holidays)
- For clarifications, members may contact BSE at 2272 8352 / 5753 / 8597

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date:** March 06, 2026
- **Maturity Date - 91TB040626 (805187):** June 04, 2026
- **Maturity Date - 182TB30926 (805188):** September 03, 2026
- **Maturity Date - 364TB40327 (805189):** March 04, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Routine government securities listing with minimal market impact. These T-Bills provide short- to medium-term investment options (91, 182, and 364 days) for trading members in the debt segment. No significant operational changes required; members should update their systems with the new scrip codes and ISIN numbers and configure trading halt triggers two working days before each maturity date.
