## Summary

BSE has listed 30,95,000 new equity shares of Indo Thai Securities Limited (Scrip Code: 533676, ISIN: INE337M01021) on the Exchange. These shares were issued on a preferential basis to Promoters and Non-Promoters pursuant to conversion of warrants, and are permitted to trade effective Monday, March 9, 2026.

## Key Points

- 30,95,000 equity shares of Rs. 1/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 36.60/-, making the issue price Rs. 37.60/- per share
- Shares allotted on preferential basis to Promoters and Non-Promoters via conversion of warrants
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution Numbers: 124923191 – 128018190
- Date of Allotment: 31 December 2025
- ISIN: INE337M01021

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notice pursuant to preferential allotment under warrant conversion.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed that these securities are now listed and permitted to trade. Lock-in restrictions apply to specified tranches and must be observed until respective lock-in expiry dates.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** 31 December 2025
- **Trading Commencement:** Monday, March 9, 2026
- **Lock-in Dates:**
  - 5,95,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 124923191–125518190): locked in until **15 September 2026**
  - 5,00,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 125518191–126018190): locked in until **15 September 2027**
  - 20,00,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 126018191–128018190): locked in until **15 September 2026**

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 30,95,000 shares to the tradeable float of Indo Thai Securities Limited from March 9, 2026, though a significant portion remains under lock-in. The preferential allotment at Rs. 37.60/- per share via warrant conversion is a company-level capital event with limited broader market impact. Investors should note the lock-in restrictions on approximately 30,95,000 shares until at least September 2026.
