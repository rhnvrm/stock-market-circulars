## Summary

BSE has issued an update regarding the debt securities of Triumph Composites Private Limited, continuing from Exchange Notice No. 20260429-3 dated April 29, 2026. The scrip (Code: 977750, ISIN: INE2I4608019) will be available for trading under revised terms with effect from Tuesday, May 26, 2026.

## Key Points

- This notice is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20260429-3 dated April 29, 2026.
- Scrip Code 977750 with ISIN INE2I4608019 will trade under revised terms from May 26, 2026.
- Trading members are advised to refer to Annexure I for the specific revised terms.
- Contact details provided for queries: Mr. Akash Shinde (+91 22 2272 8844) and Mr. Hardik Bhuta (Assistant Vice President) at debt.listing@bseindia.com.

## Regulatory Changes

The debt securities of Triumph Composites Private Limited will be subject to revised trading terms as detailed in Annexure I of this notice, effective May 26, 2026.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members dealing in Scrip Code 977750 (ISIN: INE2I4608019) must note and apply the revised terms from May 26, 2026 onwards. Members are encouraged to contact BSE's DCS-Listing department for clarification.

## Important Dates

- **April 29, 2026**: Original Exchange Notice No. 20260429-3 issued.
- **May 25, 2026**: This update notice issued.
- **May 26, 2026 (Tuesday)**: Revised trading terms become effective.

## Impact Assessment

The revision of trading terms for Triumph Composites Private Limited's debt securities (Scrip Code 977750) will directly impact trading members transacting in these instruments. Members must update their systems and processes to reflect the new terms before the market opens on May 26, 2026. The impact is limited to participants in this specific debt instrument.
