## Summary

Bombay Stock Exchange has published the demat auction notice for Settlement No. 807 dated January 29, 2026. The notice lists 145 securities across various companies that are available for auction to fulfill settlement obligations. The auction includes securities from diverse sectors including banking, energy, automobile, metals, technology, and manufacturing companies.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 807
- Auction Date: January 29, 2026
- Total number of securities listed: 145 scrips
- Quantities range from 1 share to 606,014 shares depending on the security
- Major securities include: Deep Diamond (606,014 shares), IDFC First Bank (18,550 shares), OMAX Autos (18,100 shares), Banganga Paper (12,512 shares)
- Covers multiple sectors: banking, metals, energy, automobiles, pharmaceuticals, technology, and consumer goods

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular. This is a standard operational notice for demat auction proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members with pending settlement obligations for the listed securities must participate in the auction
- Trading members must fulfill their delivery obligations for Settlement No. 807
- Participants must follow BSE's standard auction procedures and timelines
- Settlement must be completed as per BSE regulations

## Important Dates

- Auction Notice Date: January 29, 2026
- Settlement Number: 807
- Specific auction timing and settlement dates to be followed as per BSE's standard auction calendar

## Impact Assessment

This auction affects trading members who have failed to deliver securities for Settlement No. 807. The impact is medium as it involves 145 different securities, with significant quantities in some cases (particularly Deep Diamond with 606,014 shares). Members with outstanding obligations will need to participate in the auction to close out their positions. The auction ensures market integrity by completing pending settlements and may result in price volatility for the affected securities during the auction process.
