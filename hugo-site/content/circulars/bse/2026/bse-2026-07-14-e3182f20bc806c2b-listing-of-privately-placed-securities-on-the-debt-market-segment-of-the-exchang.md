## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that new commercial papers issued on a private placement basis by Vivriti Capital Limited, Navi Finserv Limited, InCred Financial Services Limited, and ECL Finance Limited have been listed and admitted to trading on the BSE Debt Segment effective July 14, 2026.

## Key Points

- Five commercial paper instruments listed across four issuers: Vivriti Capital Limited (two instruments), Navi Finserv Limited, InCred Financial Services Limited, and ECL Finance Limited.
- All securities will trade in dematerialised form under their respective ISIN numbers.
- Standard trading denomination is Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof, with a tick size of 1 paise.
- Vivriti Capital Limited: Scrip Code 732082 (ISIN INE0P1414105, Scrip ID VCL130726), Quantity 600, Issue Price Rs. 479,765.50, Rate of Interest 8.95% p.a., Redemption 01/01/2027, Credit Rating CARE A1+, IPA The Federal Bank Ltd.
- Vivriti Capital Limited: Scrip Code 732083 (ISIN INE0P1414113, Scrip ID VCL13726), Quantity 300, Issue Price Rs. 468,778.00, Rate of Interest 9.35% p.a., Redemption 30/03/2027, Credit Rating CARE A1+, IPA The Federal Bank Ltd.
- Navi Finserv Limited: Scrip Code 732084 (ISIN INE342T14GZ5, Scrip ID NFL130726), Quantity 900, Issue Price Rs. 478,695.00, Rate of Interest 9.50% p.a., Redemption 31/12/2026, Credit Rating CARE A1, IPA HDFC Bank Limited.
- InCred Financial Services Limited: Scrip Code 732085 (ISIN INE321N14489, Scrip ID IFSL130726), Quantity 500, Issue Price Rs. 482,244.00, Rate of Interest 8.90% p.a., Redemption 11/12/2026, Credit Rating ICRA A1+, IPA Bank of Maharashtra.
- ECL Finance Limited: Scrip Code 732086 (ISIN INE804I14YS5, Scrip ID EFL130726), Quantity 800, Issue Price Rs. 480,635.00, Credit Rating CRISIL A1+ / IVR A1+, Allotment Date 13/07/2026.
- All instruments have a face and paid-up value of Rs. 500,000 each, with actual/deemed date of allotment as 13/07/2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is a routine listing notification under BSE's DCS-Listing department for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under the specified ISINs, in standard denominations of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof, with a tick size of 1 paise. Queries can be directed to the BSE debt department at the listed contact numbers.

## Important Dates

- Actual/Deemed Date of Allotment: July 13, 2026
- Effective Listing Date: July 14, 2026
- Redemption Dates: December 11, 2026 (InCred); December 31, 2026 (Navi Finserv); January 1, 2027 (Vivriti Capital - VCL130726); March 30, 2027 (Vivriti Capital - VCL13726)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt market operational notice with minimal direct impact on equity markets or retail investors. It primarily affects institutional debt market participants and trading members dealing in commercial paper instruments of these NBFC issuers.
