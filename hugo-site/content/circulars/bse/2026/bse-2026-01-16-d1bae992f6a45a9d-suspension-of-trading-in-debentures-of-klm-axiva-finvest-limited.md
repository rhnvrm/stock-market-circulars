## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of KLM AXIVA FINVEST LIMITED effective January 22, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of its 9.85% NCDs maturing on February 6, 2026. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspended for KLM AXIVA FINVEST LIMITED NCDs (ISIN: INE01I507943, Scrip Code: 939669)
- Debenture series: KAFL-9.85%-6-2-26-NCD with 9.85% coupon rate
- Record date for redemption: January 22, 2026
- No dealings permitted from: January 22, 2026
- Notice number: DR-804/2025-2026
- Issued by: Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President â€“ Listing Compliance and Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine administrative action for debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from January 22, 2026
- All market participants must note the suspension and record date
- No new trades should be executed in ISIN INE01I507943 from the suspension date

## Important Dates

- **January 16, 2026**: Circular issued
- **January 22, 2026**: Record date for redemption
- **January 22, 2026**: Trading suspension effective from this date
- **February 6, 2026**: Debenture maturity date (based on instrument nomenclature)

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders of this specific debenture series of KLM AXIVA FINVEST LIMITED. The suspension is a standard procedure for facilitating redemption and does not indicate any adverse corporate action or financial distress. Market-wide impact is minimal as this affects only a single debt instrument. Debenture holders can expect redemption proceeds as per the terms of issuance after the record date is finalized.
