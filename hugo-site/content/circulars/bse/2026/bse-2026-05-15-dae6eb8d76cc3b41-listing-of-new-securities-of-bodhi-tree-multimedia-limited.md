## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260515-18 informs trading members that 1,200,000 new equity shares of Bodhi Tree Multimedia Limited (Scrip Code: 543767) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Monday, May 18, 2026. The shares were issued at Rs. 10/- per share (Rs. 1/- face value at a premium of Rs. 9/-) to non-promoters on a preferential basis and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 1,200,000 equity shares of Rs. 1/- face value issued at a premium of Rs. 9/- (issue price Rs. 10/-) to non-promoters on preferential basis
- Shares are pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 180497778 to 181697777
- ISIN: INE0EEJ01023
- Trading permitted from Monday, May 18, 2026
- Lock-in applies to all 1,200,000 shares until November 29, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification under BSE's Listing Operations framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities and may facilitate trading from May 18, 2026
- Lock-in restrictions must be observed: all 1,200,000 shares (dist. nos. 180497778 to 181697777) are locked in until November 29, 2026 and cannot be traded until that date

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** March 21, 2026
- **Listing / Trading Effective Date:** May 18, 2026 (Monday)
- **Lock-in Expiry Date:** November 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing of 1,200,000 preferentially allotted shares in Bodhi Tree Multimedia Limited represents a modest equity dilution event. Since all newly listed shares are subject to a lock-in until November 29, 2026, there is no immediate free-float addition. Market impact is minimal and confined to existing and prospective retail/institutional investors in this scrip. No systemic or broad market implications are anticipated.
