## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing all trading members that SSPL Wealth Private Limited (Member No. 6292) will be renamed to SSPL Securities Private Limited, effective Thursday, 4th June 2026.

## Key Points

- Trading member SSPL Wealth Private Limited (Member No. 6292) is changing its name to SSPL Securities Private Limited.
- The name change is effective from Thursday, 4th June 2026.
- The member number (6292) remains unchanged.
- Queries can be directed to membership.ops@bseindia.com.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative update reflecting the legal name change of a BSE trading member.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members and counterparties dealing with SSPL Wealth Private Limited should update their records to reflect the new name — SSPL Securities Private Limited — from the effective date. No action is required from the general market.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** Thursday, 4th June 2026 — name change takes effect.

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine administrative name change for a single trading member (Member No. 6292). Only entities directly transacting with or maintaining records for this member need to update their systems and documentation.
