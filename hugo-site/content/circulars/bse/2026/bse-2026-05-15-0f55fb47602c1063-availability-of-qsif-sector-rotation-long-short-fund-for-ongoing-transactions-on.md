## Summary

BSE has informed members that the QSIF Sector Rotation Long-Short Fund will reopen for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP facility on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective May 18, 2026. This follows Notice No. 20260426-1 dated April 26, 2026. Redemption and related outflow transactions are restricted to twice a week as per Quant AMC's communication.

## Key Points

- QSIF Sector Rotation Long-Short Fund reopens for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform from May 18, 2026
- Subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP facility all available
- Six scheme variants available across Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- Redemption, Switch-out, SWP, and STP out transactions accepted only on Tuesdays and Wednesdays
- If Tuesday or Wednesday is a non-business day, transactions processed on the next working day

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This notice operationalizes the earlier Notice No. 20260426-1 dated April 26, 2026 regarding the reopening of the fund.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and investors must note the restricted redemption/switch-out schedule (Tuesdays and Wednesdays only)
- Redemption payouts will be released by AMC/RTA as per payout TAT specified in the Scheme Master

## Important Dates

- **May 18, 2026**: Effective date for reopening of ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP on BSE StAR MF Platform

## Impact Assessment

Limited operational impact confined to investors and members transacting in the QSIF Sector Rotation Long-Short Fund on BSE StAR MF Platform. The twice-weekly restriction on redemptions and outflow transactions (Tuesdays and Wednesdays) is a notable liquidity constraint for investors in this Specialized Investment Fund (SIF) and should be factored into investment decisions.
