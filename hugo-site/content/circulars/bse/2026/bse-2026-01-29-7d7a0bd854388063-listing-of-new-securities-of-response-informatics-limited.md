## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 2,58,000 new equity shares of Response Informatics Limited (Scrip Code: 538273) effective from Friday, January 30, 2026. These shares were issued to promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to the conversion of warrants at an issue price of Rs. 78 per share. The shares are subject to a lock-in period until August 31, 2027.

## Key Points

- 2,58,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 68/-
- Issued to promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Date of allotment: November 28, 2025
- Issue price: Rs. 78 per share
- Distinctive Numbers: 8263403 to 8521402
- ISIN: INE401B01010
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Trading permitted from January 30, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for new securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should note the new securities in their systems
- The allotted shares are subject to lock-in restrictions and cannot be transferred until August 31, 2027

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: November 28, 2025
- **Trading Commencement Date**: January 30, 2026
- **Lock-in Period Expiry**: August 31, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low. This is a small preferential allotment to promoters representing a modest increase in share capital. With a lock-in period until August 31, 2027, these shares will not be available for trading in the near term, minimizing immediate liquidity impact.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal. Standard listing process with no special trading restrictions beyond the lock-in period. Trading members need to update their records with the new distinctive numbers and increased share capital.
