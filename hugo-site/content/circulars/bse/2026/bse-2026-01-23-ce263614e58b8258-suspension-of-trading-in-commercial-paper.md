## Summary

BSE has issued notice suspending trading in commercial paper of 11 companies effective from their respective record dates. The suspensions are due to scheduled redemptions of commercial paper between January 29, 2026 and February 5, 2026. Trading members are advised not to deal in these securities from the specified dates.

## Key Points

- 11 commercial paper instruments affected by trading suspension
- Suspensions range from January 29 to February 5, 2026
- Action taken due to record dates fixed for redemption purposes
- Trading members instructed not to deal in listed securities
- Notice categorized under Corporate Actions - Debt segment

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for corporate actions in the debt market segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute trades in the specified commercial paper from the respective "No Dealings From" dates
- Adherence to record date requirements for redemption processing
- Members must update their trading systems to reflect suspension status

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Limited to debt market participants holding these specific commercial paper instruments. No equity market impact.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members must ensure compliance systems prevent trading in suspended securities. Investors holding these instruments will receive redemption proceeds on maturity as per terms.

**Investor Impact:** Holders of these commercial papers should prepare for redemption proceeds. No trading liquidity available from suspension date until redemption completion.
