## Summary

BSE has issued a circular regarding the allocation of shares to anchor investors in the public issue of Kasturi Metal Composite Limited. This is part of the standard IPO process where qualified institutional buyers participate in the anchor investor portion before the issue opens to the public.

## Key Points

- Company: Kasturi Metal Composite Limited
- Type: Public Issue - Anchor Investor Allocation
- Exchange: BSE (Bombay Stock Exchange)
- Circular Date: January 23, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard disclosure as per SEBI IPO guidelines.

## Compliance Requirements

- Anchor investor allocation must be disclosed to the exchange
- Information must be made available to public investors before issue opening
- Compliance with SEBI (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: January 23, 2026
- Anchor investor allocation date would typically be one day before issue opening

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - Anchor investor participation provides price discovery and demonstrates institutional interest in the IPO, which can influence retail investor sentiment.

**Operational Impact:** Standard IPO process disclosure with no operational changes required for market participants.
