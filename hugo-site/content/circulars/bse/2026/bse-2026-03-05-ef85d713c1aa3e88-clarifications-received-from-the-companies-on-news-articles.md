## Summary

Bharat Forge Limited (BSE: 500493, NSE: BHARATFORG) has submitted a clarification to BSE and NSE in response to exchange queries regarding a news article published on www.cnbctv18.com dated March 05, 2026. The article related to North American Class 8 truck orders and broader market trends. The company confirms there are no undisclosed material developments and that all required disclosures under Regulation 30 of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015 have been made.

## Key Points

- The news article referenced is based on monthly industry-level data and broader market trends relating to North American Class 8 truck orders, not company-specific events.
- Bharat Forge clarifies there are no specific negotiations, events, or material developments requiring disclosure under Regulation 30 of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015.
- The company confirms it is not aware of any material information that has not been disclosed to stock exchanges that could explain price or volume movement in its shares.
- No regulatory or legal proceedings have been initiated or concluded beyond what has already been intimated to the exchanges.
- The news article has no material impact on the company's operations or financial position.
- The clarification was submitted by Company Secretary and Compliance Officer Tejaswini Chaudhari (Membership No.: A18907).

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This circular is a company-level clarification response to exchange surveillance queries under Regulation 30 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015.

## Compliance Requirements

- Bharat Forge has responded to BSE surveillance communication L/SURV/ONL/RV/SG/(2025-2026)/207 dated March 05, 2026.
- Bharat Forge has responded to NSE surveillance letter NSE/CM/Surveillance/16538 dated March 05, 2026.
- No additional compliance actions are required from market participants or other entities.

## Important Dates

- **March 05, 2026**: Date of CNBC TV18 news article and exchange surveillance queries.
- **March 05, 2026**: Date of Bharat Forge's clarification submission to BSE and NSE.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine clarificatory disclosure with no material market impact. The company explicitly states that the referenced news pertains to industry-level data and not any company-specific undisclosed event. Investors and market participants should note that Bharat Forge has confirmed compliance with all disclosure obligations under SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015. No changes to operations, financials, or market standing are implied.
