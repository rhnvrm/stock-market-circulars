## Summary

BSE has published the Demat Auction list for Settlement No. 838, dated 13 March 2026. The auction covers a wide range of securities across various sectors, with quantities ranging from 1 share to over 2,43,043 shares per scrip.

## Key Points

- Settlement No. 838 dated 13/03/2026
- Total of approximately 96 scrips listed for demat auction
- Quantities range from 1 share (multiple scrips) up to 2,43,043 shares (Standard Capital, scrip code 511700)
- Largest auction quantities: Standard Capital (511700) – 2,43,043; Rajnish Wellness (541601) – 1,98,494; Regaal Resources (544485) – 19,944; Variman Global (540570) – 30,924
- Notable scrips include Adani Power (533096, 1,000 shares), SpiceJet (500285, 4,100 shares), Yes Bank (532648, 2,100 shares), Vodafone Idea (532822, 30 shares)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice for demat auction settlement.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members and participants involved in the auction must adhere to BSE's standard demat auction settlement procedures for Settlement No. 838.

## Important Dates

- Auction Date / Settlement Date: 13 March 2026 (Settlement No. 838)

## Impact Assessment

Routine demat auction with no significant market-wide impact. Individual scrips listed have small auction quantities relative to typical traded volumes, with a few exceptions (Standard Capital at 2,43,043 shares, Rajnish Wellness at 1,98,494 shares). Participants holding short positions in the listed scrips for the relevant settlement may be subject to auction proceedings.
