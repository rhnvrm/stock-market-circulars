## Summary

ICCL Ltd. has issued Notice No. 20260417-15 dated April 17, 2026, informing trading members of corporate actions pertaining to securities in the Securities Lending and Borrowing (SLB) segment. The full details of the affected securities are provided in the attached annexure.

## Key Points

- Corporate actions have been announced for securities in the SLB segment.
- Trading members are directed to refer to the annexure for the list of affected securities and action details.
- Notice issued by Tushar Nimkar, Assistant Manager, ICCL Ltd.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This notice communicates operational updates resulting from corporate actions on SLB-eligible securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must review the annexed list of SLB securities subject to corporate actions.
- Members should adjust their SLB positions or strategies in line with the corporate action details provided in the annexure.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: April 17, 2026
- Effective dates for individual corporate actions are specified in the annexure.

## Impact Assessment

The notice affects trading members participating in the SLB segment. Corporate actions (such as dividends, splits, or mergers) on underlying securities can impact open SLB positions, requiring adjustments to lending/borrowing obligations. The full impact depends on the specific securities and actions listed in the annexure.
