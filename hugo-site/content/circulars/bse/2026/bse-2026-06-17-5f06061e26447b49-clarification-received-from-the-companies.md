## Summary

BSE's Department of Surveillance (DOSS) observed significant price/volume movements in 17 scrips and wrote to the respective companies seeking clarification. As of Notice No. 20260617-25 dated June 17, 2026, replies have been received from 14 companies, while responses are still awaited from 3 companies.

## Key Points

- BSE identified unusual price/volume activity in 17 listed scrips and sought clarifications to keep trading members and investors informed.
- Replies have been received from 14 companies and are attached to the notice (Replies1 and Replies2).
- 3 companies have not yet submitted their clarifications.
- This is a standard surveillance action under BSE's DOSS department with no trading suspension or additional surveillance measures announced in this notice.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced by this circular. It is an informational notice under BSE's ongoing market surveillance framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- The 3 companies for which replies are still awaited — Amrapali Industries Ltd (526241), Elango Industries Ltd (513452), and Grand Oak Canyons Distillery Ltd (523862) — are expected to submit their clarifications to BSE.
- Trading members and investors should review the attached replies from the 14 responding companies for relevant disclosures.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 17, 2026
- No specific deadline mentioned for pending replies.

## Impact Assessment

This notice has moderate informational impact. The 14 companies that have responded have provided clarifications that may contain material disclosures explaining recent price/volume movements. Investors in these scrips should review the attached replies. The 3 non-responding companies (Amrapali Industries, Elango Industries, Grand Oak Canyons Distillery) remain under scrutiny and may face follow-up surveillance action if clarifications are not received promptly.
