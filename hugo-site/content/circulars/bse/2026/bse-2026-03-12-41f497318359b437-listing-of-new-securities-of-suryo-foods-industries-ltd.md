## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 29,70,000 new equity shares of Suryo Foods & Industries Ltd. (Scrip Code: 519604), issued on a rights basis at Rs. 10/- face value with a premium of Rs. 10/-, are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Friday, March 13, 2026.

## Key Points

- 29,70,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 10/- on rights basis are now listed
- ISIN: INE565E01016
- Distribution Numbers: 3960001 to 6930000
- Date of Allotment: March 9, 2026
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Scrip Code: 519604

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification pursuant to a rights issue.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to update their systems to reflect the new shares available for trading from March 13, 2026. No additional compliance action is required beyond normal trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** March 9, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** March 13, 2026 (Friday)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. The listing of 29,70,000 rights issue shares increases the total float of Suryo Foods & Industries Ltd. (Scrip Code: 519604). The new shares are pari-passu with existing equity, meaning no differential rights. Existing shareholders who participated in the rights issue will be able to trade their allotted shares from March 13, 2026.
