## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members that the equity shares of SMR Jewels Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange. The company is being listed on the SME segment. The exact date of listing and further details of the securities will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- SMR Jewels Limited equity shares will be listed on BSE under the SME segment.
- Scrip Code assigned: **544774**
- Trading Symbol: **SMR**
- ISIN Number: **INE11XK01017**
- The specific listing date will be announced via a separate BSE notice.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing admission notice.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the upcoming listing and await the separate notice that will confirm the listing date and full details of the securities before initiating dealings.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 5, 2026
- **Listing Date:** To be announced via separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This notice has a limited market impact as it pertains to a new SME-segment listing. The stock is not yet available for trading and the listing date is pending. Retail and institutional investors interested in SMR Jewels Limited should monitor BSE for the follow-up notice confirming the listing date.
