## Summary

The Government of India, acting through the Ministry of Power (the Seller), has exercised the oversubscription option in the Offer for Sale (OFS) of NHPC Limited. This doubles the total offer size from 30,13,51,044 equity shares (3% of paid-up equity) to 60,27,02,088 equity shares (6% of paid-up equity). The notice continues from Exchange Notice No. 20260601-40 dated June 01, 2026.

## Key Points

- **Oversubscription Option Exercised:** The Seller has invoked the oversubscription option for an additional 30,13,51,044 equity shares (3% of total paid-up equity), on top of the base offer of 30,13,51,044 equity shares (3%).
- **Total Offer Size:** 60,27,02,088 equity shares, representing 6.00% of the total issued and paid-up equity share capital of NHPC Limited.
- **Retail Investor Reservation:** 6,02,70,210 equity shares (10% of total offer) are reserved for Retail Investors on T+1 Day (June 03, 2026), subject to valid bids.
- **Employee Offer Increased:** The employee offer is also doubled — up to 90,40,530 equity shares (increased from 45,20,265), with bids eligible up to Rs. 5,00,000 per employee; however, only bids up to Rs. 2,00,000 will be considered for first-priority allocation.
- **Trading Windows:** T Day (June 02, 2026) is for Non-Retail Investors only; T+1 Day (June 03, 2026) is open for Retail Investors, Employees, and Non-Retail Investors carrying forward un-allotted bids.
- **Face Value:** Rs. 10 per equity share.
- **Exchange Platform:** Designated windows on BSE Limited and NSE.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework introduced. This notice operates under existing OFS guidelines issued by SEBI and the exchange's designated OFS window mechanism. The oversubscription option exercise is a standard provision under the OFS framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Non-Retail Investors:** Must participate on T Day (June 02, 2026) via the designated BSE/NSE OFS window.
- **Retail Investors and Employees:** May participate on T+1 Day (June 03, 2026).
- **Employee Bidders:** Eligible employees must note the two-tier allocation limit — full bids up to Rs. 5,00,000 are accepted, but first-priority allocation is restricted to Rs. 2,00,000.
- **Brokers/Members:** Must ensure client orders are routed through the correct OFS segment and comply with updated offer size disclosures.

## Important Dates

- **T Day (Non-Retail Investors):** June 02, 2026
- **T+1 Day (Retail Investors, Employees, Non-Retail carryforward):** June 03, 2026
- **Notice Date:** June 02, 2026
- **Reference Notice:** BSE Notice No. 20260601-40 dated June 01, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The exercise of the oversubscription option significantly increases the supply of NHPC shares in the market — from 3% to 6% of total paid-up equity — which may exert short-term downward pressure on the share price. For long-term investors, this represents an opportunity to acquire a PSU utility stock at an OFS-determined price. The doubling of the employee quota enhances employee participation and alignment. The large quantum (over 60 crore shares) makes this one of the more substantial government divestments in the near term, with broader implications for government divestment targets and PSU sector sentiment.
