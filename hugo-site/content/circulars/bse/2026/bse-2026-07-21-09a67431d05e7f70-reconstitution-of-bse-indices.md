## Summary

BSE Index Services Private Ltd (formerly Asia Index Pvt. Ltd.) announced the reconstitution results for several BSE indices, effective at the open of trading on Tuesday, August 04, 2026. Vedanta Aluminium Metal Limited (Scrip Code 544780) will be added to the BSE ALLCAP, BSE LARGECAP, BSE LARGEMIDCAP, and BSE COMMODITIES indices. Additionally, several securities appearing on the latest GSM (Graded Surveillance Measure) list will be excluded from multiple indices, including Aqylon Nexus Limited, GVK Power & Infrastructure Limited, and BEML Land Assets Limited.

## Key Points

- Reconstitution is carried out as per the change in methodology notice no. 20251029-21.
- Vedanta Aluminium Metal Limited (Scrip Code 544780) added to BSE ALLCAP, BSE LARGECAP, BSE LARGEMIDCAP, and BSE COMMODITIES indices.
- Aqylon Nexus Limited (Scrip Code 530943) dropped from BSE SMALLCAP, BSE ALLCAP, BSE CONSUMER DISCRETIONARY, and BSE MIDSMALLCAP indices due to GSM listing.
- GVK Power & Infrastructure Limited (Scrip Code 532708) dropped from BSE ALLCAP, BSE UTILITIES, BSE MIDSMALLCAP, and BSE CPSE indices due to GSM listing.
- BEML Land Assets Limited (Scrip Code 543898) dropped from BSE SMALLCAP, BSE ALLCAP, BSE FINANCIAL SERVICES, and BSE MIDSMALLCAP indices due to GSM listing.
- All changes take effect at market open on August 4, 2026.
- Queries can be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory change is introduced by this notice; it implements a previously announced methodology change (notice no. 20251029-21) governing exclusion of GSM-listed securities from BSE indices.

## Compliance Requirements

Index fund managers, ETF providers, and other passive investment vehicles tracking the affected BSE indices (ALLCAP, LARGECAP, LARGEMIDCAP, COMMODITIES, SMALLCAP, CONSUMER DISCRETIONARY, MIDSMALLCAP, UTILITIES, CPSE, FINANCIAL SERVICES) must rebalance their portfolios to reflect the additions and exclusions effective August 4, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **July 21, 2026**: Notice issued announcing reconstitution results.
- **August 4, 2026**: Effective date for all index additions and exclusions (at market open).

## Impact Assessment

The reconstitution will generate passive fund flows into Vedanta Aluminium Metal Limited as index-tracking funds adjust holdings ahead of and on the effective date. Conversely, GSM-listed stocks (Aqylon Nexus, GVK Power & Infrastructure, BEML Land Assets) being dropped from multiple indices will likely see reduced passive fund demand and potential selling pressure from index-tracking vehicles. The impact is moderate and largely confined to passive investment strategies and index arbitrage activity rather than broader market movement.
