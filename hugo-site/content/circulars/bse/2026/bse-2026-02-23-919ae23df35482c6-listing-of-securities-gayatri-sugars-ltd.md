## Summary

BSE has listed 6,610,007 new equity shares of Gayatri Sugars Ltd. (Scrip Code: 532183, ISIN: INE622E01023) on the Exchange. These shares were issued at par (Rs. 10.00 each) to non-promoters on a preferential basis through conversion of warrants, allotted on January 11, 2025. Trading in these shares commences on Tuesday, February 24, 2026.

## Key Points

- **New Shares Listed:** 6,610,007 equity shares of Rs. 10.00/- each, issued at par
- **Issued To:** Non-promoters on a preferential basis via conversion of warrants
- **Ranking:** Pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- **Distinctive Numbers:** 67693634 to 74303640
- **Date of Allotment:** January 11, 2025
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 10.00/- per share
- **ISIN:** INE622E01023
- **Trading Effective From:** Tuesday, February 24, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification pursuant to an approved preferential allotment of equity shares upon conversion of warrants.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed that the new securities are listed and permitted to trade from February 24, 2026.
- The entire lot of 6,610,007 shares (Dist. Nos. 67693634 to 74303640) is under lock-in and cannot be traded until the lock-in period expires.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** January 11, 2025
- **Trading Commencement:** February 24, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry:** September 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The market impact of this listing is minimal. All 6,610,007 newly listed shares are subject to a lock-in period until September 15, 2026, meaning they cannot be traded in the open market until that date. The issuance represents a warrant conversion by non-promoters at par value (Rs. 10.00), which is a routine corporate action. Trading members should update their records to reflect the new share range for Gayatri Sugars Ltd.
