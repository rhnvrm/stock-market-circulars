## Summary

BSE has announced the listing and commencement of trading for 18,96,614 new equity shares of Thirumalai Chemicals Ltd. (Scrip Code: 500412) effective February 9, 2026. These shares were issued to promoters on a preferential basis at an issue price of Rs. 296 per share (face value Re. 1 with premium of Rs. 295). The shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares and are subject to lock-in until August 15, 2027.

## Key Points

- **Company**: Thirumalai Chemicals Ltd. (Scrip Code: 500412)
- **Number of Shares**: 18,96,614 equity shares of Re. 1/- each
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 296 per share (Re. 1 face value + Rs. 295 premium)
- **Allotment Type**: Preferential allotment to Promoters
- **Date of Allotment**: December 23, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: February 9, 2026 (Monday)
- **ISIN**: INE338A01024
- **Distinctive Numbers**: 118656161 to 120552774
- **Ranking**: Pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification following preferential allotment procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members**: Informed of the new securities available for trading from February 9, 2026
- **Lock-in Compliance**: All 18,96,614 shares (Dist. Nos. 118656161-120552774) are locked-in until August 15, 2027
- Shares must be tracked separately for lock-in compliance purposes

## Important Dates

- **December 23, 2025**: Date of allotment
- **February 6, 2026**: Notice date
- **February 9, 2026**: Trading commencement date
- **August 15, 2027**: Lock-in expiry date
