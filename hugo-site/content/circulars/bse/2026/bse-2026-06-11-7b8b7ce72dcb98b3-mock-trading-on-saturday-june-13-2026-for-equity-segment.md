## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Equity segment on Saturday, June 13, 2026. The session will be conducted from both the Primary Site (PR) and Disaster Recovery Site (DR) to allow trading members and third-party platform users to test trading applications, including exceptional market condition scenarios. There will be no new BOLT TWS Release associated with this mock session.

## Key Points

- Mock trading is scheduled for Saturday, June 13, 2026, covering both Primary Site (PR) and Disaster Recovery Site (DR).
- Third-party trading platform users may use this session to test their applications.
- Functionalities testable include various call auction sessions, risk-reduction mode, trading halt, and block deals.
- No new BOLT TWS Release will be issued.
- Trades from mock trading do not create any margin obligation, pay-in/pay-out obligation, or rights and liabilities.
- Members are requested to submit feedback by 5:00 pm on the same day.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a scheduled operational test session.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are encouraged to actively participate in the mock trading session.
- Members should submit feedback on the session to BSE via the provided contact numbers or email by 5:00 pm on June 13, 2026.
- Queries can be directed to the respective Relationship Manager, BSE Helpdesk, or BSE Tech Support team.

## Important Dates

- **Mock Trading Date:** Saturday, June 13, 2026
- **Login window:** 9:15 am – 9:45 am (PR)
- **Morning Block Deal Window (PR):** 9:45 am – 10:00 am
- **Pre-Open Order Entry (PR):** 10:00 am – 10:08 am (random stoppage in last 1 minute)
- **Pre-Open Matching (PR):** 10:08 am – 10:15 am
- **Continuous Trading T+1 (PR):** 10:15 am – 1:00 pm
- **Continuous Trading T+0 (PR):** 10:15 am – 12:30 pm
- **Auction for Settlement Details – Offer Entry & Matching (PR):** 11:20 am – 11:40 am
- **Switch Over to DR:** 1:00 pm – 1:45 pm
- **Pre-Open Order Entry (DR):** 1:45 pm – 1:53 pm (random stoppage in last 1 minute)
- **Pre-Open Matching (DR):** 1:53 pm – 2:00 pm
- **Continuous Trading T+1 (DR):** 2:00 pm – 3:45 pm
- **Continuous Trading T+0 (DR):** 2:00 pm – 3:15 pm
- **Afternoon Block Deal Window:** 3:00 pm – 3:15 pm
- **Auction for Settlement Details – Offer Entry & Matching (DR):** 2:30 pm – 3:00 pm
- **Closing:** 3:45 pm – 3:55 pm
- **Post-Closing:** 3:55 pm – 4:05 pm
- **Trade Modification T+1:** up to 4:15 pm
- **Trade Modification T+0:** up to 3:30 pm
- **Feedback deadline:** 5:00 pm on June 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This session has no financial or regulatory impact on market participants. It is a testing exercise with no obligations arising from resulting trades. The session is valuable for members wishing to validate their trading infrastructure, especially for edge-case scenarios such as call auctions, risk-reduction mode, and DR switchover procedures. Active participation is encouraged to ensure system readiness.
