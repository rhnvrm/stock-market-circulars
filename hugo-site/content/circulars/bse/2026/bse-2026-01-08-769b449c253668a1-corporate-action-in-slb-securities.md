## Summary

BSE has announced corporate actions in Securities Lending and Borrowing (SLB) securities for three companies. Authum Investment & Infrastructure Limited will undergo a bonus issue, while Godrej Consumer Products Ltd. and Angel One Limited have declared dividends. These corporate actions will result in foreclosure periods and shut periods for SLB transactions.

## Key Points

- Three companies have announced corporate actions affecting SLB securities
- Authum Investment & Infrastructure Limited: Bonus issue with ex-date January 13, 2026
- Godrej Consumer Products Ltd.: Dividend with ex-date January 30, 2026
- Angel One Limited: Dividend with ex-date January 21, 2026
- All corporate actions have specified shut periods during which SLB transactions will be restricted

## Corporate Actions Details

### Authum Investment & Infrastructure Limited
- **Action Type**: Bonus Issue
- **Record/Book Closure Date**: January 13, 2026
- **Ex-Date**: January 13, 2026
- **Foreclosure Date**: January 13, 2026
- **Shut Period**: January 12-13, 2026

### Godrej Consumer Products Ltd.
- **Action Type**: Dividend
- **Record/Book Closure Date**: January 30, 2026
- **Ex-Date**: January 30, 2026
- **Foreclosure Date**: Not Applicable
- **Shut Period**: January 29-30, 2026

### Angel One Limited
- **Action Type**: Dividend
- **Record/Book Closure Date**: January 21, 2026
- **Ex-Date**: January 21, 2026
- **Foreclosure Date**: Not Applicable
- **Shut Period**: January 20-21, 2026

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants with SLB positions in the affected securities must close or settle positions before the respective foreclosure dates
- No new SLB transactions will be permitted during the shut periods
- Existing SLB positions may need to be squared off or rolled over before the corporate action dates

## Important Dates

- **January 12-13, 2026**: Shut period for Authum Investment & Infrastructure Limited
- **January 13, 2026**: Ex-date and foreclosure for Authum bonus issue
- **January 20-21, 2026**: Shut period for Angel One Limited
- **January 21, 2026**: Ex-date for Angel One dividend
- **January 29-30, 2026**: Shut period for Godrej Consumer Products Ltd.
- **January 30, 2026**: Ex-date for Godrej Consumer Products dividend

## Impact Assessment

### Market Impact
- SLB participants must plan ahead to manage positions in these three securities
- Foreclosure requirements may lead to temporary reduction in available securities for lending
- Trading activity may be affected during shut periods as SLB transactions will be restricted

### Operational Impact
- Brokers and clearing members need to notify clients with SLB positions in these securities
- Systems must be updated to reflect shut periods and prevent new SLB transactions
- Settlement teams must ensure timely closure of positions before foreclosure dates
