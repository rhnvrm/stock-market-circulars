## Summary

BSE has announced the addition of Gabion Technologies India Limited (Exchange ticker 544675) to the BSE SME IPO Index. The company is being listed on BSE effective Tuesday, January 13, 2026, and will be added to the index at the open of Wednesday, January 14, 2026. This is a standard corporate action following the company's listing on the BSE SME platform.

## Key Points

- Gabion Technologies India Limited (ticker: 544675) listing on BSE effective January 13, 2026
- Addition to BSE SME IPO Index effective at market open on January 14, 2026
- Action included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- This follows Notice No. 20260112-35 issued previously
- Queries can be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index maintenance action following the listing of a new SME company.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index providers and fund managers tracking the BSE SME IPO Index should update their portfolios accordingly.

## Important Dates

- **January 13, 2026 (Tuesday)**: Gabion Technologies India Limited listing effective on BSE
- **January 14, 2026 (Wednesday)**: Addition to BSE SME IPO Index effective at market open

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low. This is a routine index addition following an SME listing. The impact is limited to investors and funds that track the BSE SME IPO Index specifically.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal. Index tracking funds will need to adjust their portfolios to include the new constituent, but this is standard index maintenance.

**Investor Impact**: Relevant only to investors tracking or benchmarked against the BSE SME IPO Index. The addition provides exposure to the newly listed Gabion Technologies India Limited for passive investors in this index.
