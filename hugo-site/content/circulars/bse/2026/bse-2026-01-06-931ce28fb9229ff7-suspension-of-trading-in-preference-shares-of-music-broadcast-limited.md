## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Music Broadcast Limited's 0.1% Non-Convertible Redeemable Preference Shares (ISIN: INE919I04010, Code: 717504) effective January 9, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption purposes.

## Key Points

- Trading Members are advised not to deal in the specified preference shares
- The suspension applies to MBL-0.1%-19-1-26-NCRPS series
- Notice reference: DR-795/2025-2026
- Circular issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President â€“ Listing Compliance and Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action procedure.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must cease all dealings in the specified preference shares from January 9, 2026
- No trading activity permitted in ISIN INE919I04010 (Code: 717504) from the suspension date

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: January 6, 2026
- **Record Date**: January 9, 2026
- **No Dealings From**: January 9, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This suspension affects only holders of Music Broadcast Limited's specific series of preference shares and has minimal market-wide impact. The suspension is a routine corporate action to facilitate redemption. Equity shareholders and other securities of Music Broadcast Limited are not affected. Trading Members dealing in debt/preference share segments should update their systems accordingly.
