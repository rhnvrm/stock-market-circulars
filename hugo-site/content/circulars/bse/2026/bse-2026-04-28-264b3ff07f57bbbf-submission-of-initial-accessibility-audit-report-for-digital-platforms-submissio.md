## Summary

BSE has issued a notice requiring all Investment Advisers to submit their Initial Accessibility audit report for digital platforms by April 30, 2026. This is in accordance with SEBI Circular No. SEBI/HO/ITD-1/ITD_VIAP/P/CIR/2025/131 dated September 25, 2025, which mandates compliance with the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016. Submission must be made through the IARA portal using existing login credentials.

## Key Points

- Investment Advisers must submit an Initial Accessibility audit report for digital platforms by April 30, 2026
- Submission is to be made via the IARA portal using existing login credentials
- This notice references multiple prior BSE circulars (20260325-61, 20251209-10, 20250829-32, 20250826-30, 20250807-12) on the same subject
- A User Manual with step-by-step guidance is attached to the notice
- Support is available at 022 22725854 / 5680 / 5359

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This notice reiterates and reinforces the compliance deadline established under SEBI Circular No. SEBI/HO/ITD-1/ITD_VIAP/P/CIR/2025/131 (September 25, 2025) relating to mandatory digital accessibility standards under the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016.

## Compliance Requirements

- All Investment Advisers registered with BSE must submit the Initial Accessibility audit report for their digital platforms
- Submission must be completed through the IARA portal
- Entities should use their existing login credentials for portal access
- Step-by-step guidance is available in the attached User Manual

## Important Dates

- **April 30, 2026**: Deadline for submission of Initial Accessibility audit report for digital platforms

## Impact Assessment

This notice has a low direct market impact but medium compliance significance for Investment Advisers. Failure to submit the audit report by the deadline may attract regulatory action from SEBI/BSE. The notice is administrative in nature, affecting only the Investment Adviser segment under BSE's Membership Compliance department. No trading, listing, or broader market operations are affected.
