## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260605-24 informs trading members that five commercial papers issued on a private placement basis by Nuvama Wealth Management Limited and Bajaj Financial Securities Limited have been listed and admitted to trading on the BSE Debt Segment effective June 5, 2026. All securities trade in dematerialised form in standard denominations of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples, with a tick size of 1 paise.

## Key Points

- Five commercial papers (CPs) are freshly listed on the BSE Debt Segment effective June 5, 2026
- Trading is permitted only in dematerialised form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof; tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent for all five CPs: ICICI Bank Ltd / ICICI Bank Limited
- All CPs carry top-tier short-term credit ratings (CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ or IND A1+)
- Face value and paid-up value for all CPs: Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Allotment date for all CPs: June 4, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification under BSE's debt market listing framework for privately placed securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form using the respective ISIN numbers
- All trades must be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the BSE Debt Department at: 22728352 / 8597 / 8995 / 5753 / 8915

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice with minimal market impact. The listing provides additional short-term debt instruments for institutional and eligible investors on the BSE Debt Segment. The CPs are of short tenure (1–2 months) with high credit quality (A1+ ratings). Bajaj Financial Securities has listed four CPs at 8.08% p.a. with varying maturities in August 2026, while Nuvama Wealth Management has listed one CP at 7.50% p.a. maturing in July 2026. The issue prices reflect prevailing market discount rates for these maturities.
