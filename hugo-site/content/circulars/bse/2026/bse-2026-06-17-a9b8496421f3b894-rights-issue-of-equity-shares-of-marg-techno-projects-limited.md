## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that MARG TECHNO PROJECTS LIMITED (BSE Code: 540254) has fixed 20 June 2026 as the record date for its rights issue of equity shares. Effective 19 June 2026, all transactions in the company's equity shares must be conducted on an ex-rights basis.

## Key Points

- Company: MARG TECHNO PROJECTS LIMITED (BSE Code: 540254)
- Rights issue ratio: 9 equity shares for every 2 equity shares held
- Issue price: Rs. 10/- per share (face value, cash at par)
- Record date: 20 June 2026
- Ex-rights date: 19 June 2026
- Settlement number: DR-655/2026-2027
- Segment: Dematerialised Securities – Rolling Settlement Segment

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification under existing BSE listing compliance and operations framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all transactions in MARG TECHNO PROJECTS LIMITED equity shares are executed on an ex-rights basis from 19 June 2026 onwards.
- Members should update their systems to reflect the ex-rights adjustment effective 19 June 2026.

## Important Dates

- **19 June 2026**: Ex-rights date — trades from this date will be without rights entitlement (Settlement No. DR-655/2026-2027)
- **20 June 2026**: Record date for determining eligible shareholders for the rights issue

## Impact Assessment

The rights issue carries a high dilution ratio of 9:2 (4.5x), meaning eligible shareholders as of the record date can subscribe to 4.5 times their existing holding at face value (Rs. 10/-). Existing shareholders who do not participate will face significant dilution of their ownership percentage. The ex-rights pricing adjustment will affect the market price of the scrip from 19 June 2026. Trading members should notify clients holding positions in scrip code 540254 about the upcoming ex-rights date to allow informed trading decisions.
