## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Gujarat Inject Kerala Ltd (Scrip Code: 524238) has fixed **08 July 2026** as the record date for the sub-division (stock split) of its equity shares. Each existing equity share of Rs.10/- face value will be split into ten equity shares of Re.1/- face value each, effective 08 July 2026.

## Key Points

- **Company:** Gujarat Inject Kerala Ltd (BSE Scrip Code: 524238)
- **Corporate Action:** Sub-division (stock split) of equity shares
- **Split Ratio:** 1:10 — one share of Rs.10/- subdivided into ten shares of Re.1/- each
- **Record Date:** 08 July 2026
- **Effective Date:** 08 July 2026
- **Segment:** Dematerialised Securities – Rolling Settlement Segment
- **DR Reference:** DR-667/2026-2027
- **Existing ISIN:** INE659F01014 (Rs.10/- paid-up) — will become invalid for exchange transactions on or after 08 July 2026
- **New ISIN:** To be announced via a separate notice

## Regulatory Changes

The face value of Gujarat Inject Kerala Ltd's equity shares will change from Rs.10/- to Re.1/- per share upon the record date. The existing ISIN INE659F01014 ceases to be valid for exchange transactions from 08 July 2026 onwards. A new ISIN for the Re.1/- paid-up shares will be communicated separately.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that the existing ISIN INE659F01014 cannot be used for transactions on the exchange on or after 08 July 2026.
- Members and depositories must update their systems to reflect the new face value (Re.1/-) and the new ISIN once announced.
- Members should inform their clients holding shares of Gujarat Inject Kerala Ltd about the change in ISIN and face value.

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard stock split (sub-division) corporate action. The total market capitalisation of the company remains unchanged; existing shareholders will hold ten times the number of shares at one-tenth the face value. The primary operational impact is the change of ISIN: the old ISIN INE659F01014 becomes invalid on 08 July 2026, and the new ISIN must be used for all subsequent trades. Trading members and depository participants need to ensure timely system updates to avoid settlement failures. The split may improve liquidity and affordability of shares for retail investors given the lower per-share price post-split.
