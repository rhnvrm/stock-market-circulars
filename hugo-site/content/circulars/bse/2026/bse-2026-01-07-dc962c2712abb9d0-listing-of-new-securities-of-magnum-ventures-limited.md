## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 20,00,000 new equity shares of MAGNUM VENTURES LIMITED (Scrip Code: 532896) issued on a preferential basis to promoters. These shares will be listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Thursday, January 8, 2026. The shares were allotted at a price of Rs. 30/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- plus premium of Rs. 20/-) and are subject to a lock-in period until July 29, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: MAGNUM VENTURES LIMITED (Scrip Code: 532896)
- Number of shares: 20,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each
- Issue price: Rs. 30/- per share (including premium of Rs. 20/-)
- Type of issue: Preferential allotment to Promoters
- Trading commencement date: January 8, 2026
- Distinctive numbers: 66411318 to 68411317
- ISIN: INE387I01016
- Ranking: Pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for newly issued securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed about the listing of new securities
- All 20,00,000 shares are subject to lock-in restrictions until July 29, 2026
- Lock-in applies to distinctive numbers 66411318 to 68411317

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: November 11, 2025
- **Trading Commencement Date**: January 8, 2026
- **Lock-in Period End Date**: July 29, 2026
- **Circular Notice Date**: January 7, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action with limited market impact. The preferential allotment to promoters increases the company's equity base by 20,00,000 shares. The lock-in period until July 29, 2026 ensures these shares cannot be traded immediately, preventing any sudden supply impact on the market. The shares rank pari-passu with existing shares, meaning they have equal rights. This listing notification is primarily informational for trading members and does not affect broader market operations or trading rules.
