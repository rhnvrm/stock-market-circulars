## Summary

BSE's Surveillance Department sought clarification from Schneider Electric Infrastructure Ltd (Scrip Code: 534139) regarding a news article published on Moneycontrol. The exchange initiated this query to verify the accuracy of the news and keep members and investors informed. The company's clarification has been received.

## Key Points

- BSE Notice No. 20260616-17 dated 16 June 2026
- Clarification sought from Schneider Electric Infrastructure Ltd (Scrip Code: 534139)
- News source in question: Moneycontrol (https://www.moneycontrol.com)
- Issued by BSE Surveillance Department (DOSS)
- Reply/attachment from the company has been received

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard surveillance-driven disclosure process.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements for market participants. The company has already responded to BSE's clarification request.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 16 June 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine clarification notice issued by BSE Surveillance to verify news accuracy and maintain transparency for investors. The clarification pertains only to Schneider Electric Infrastructure Ltd and does not impose any trading restrictions or regulatory obligations on other market participants.
