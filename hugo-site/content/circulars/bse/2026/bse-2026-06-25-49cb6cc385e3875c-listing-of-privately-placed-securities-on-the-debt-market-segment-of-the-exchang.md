## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260625-34 informs trading members that five commercial paper (CP) instruments issued on a private placement basis have been listed and admitted to trading on the BSE Debt Segment effective June 25, 2026. The issuers are Redington Limited (three CPs), KEC International Ltd. (one CP), and SBICAP Securities Limited (one CP). All securities are rated A1+ by at least two rating agencies, trade in standard denominations of Rs. 5 lakhs, and have a tick size of 1 paise.

## Key Points

- Five commercial papers freshly listed on BSE Debt Segment via private placement, effective June 25, 2026.
- All securities trade exclusively in dematerialised form; standard denomination is Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof; tick size is 1 paise.
- Redington Limited has three CPs listed: RL-22-09-26-CP (ISIN INE891D14C36, Qty 4,000, Issue Price Rs. 491,727, Rate 6.90% p.a., Redemption 22/09/2026), RL-24-09-26-CP (ISIN INE891D14C44, Qty 3,000, Issue Price Rs. 491,423.50, Rate 7.00% p.a., Redemption 24/09/2026), and RL-23-09-26-CP (ISIN INE891D14C51, Qty 4,000, Issue Price Rs. 491,516.50, Rate 7.00% p.a., Redemption 23/09/2026); IPA for all three is HDFC Bank Ltd; rated CRISIL A1+/ICRA A1+.
- KEC International Ltd. has one CP listed: KIL-23-9-26-CP (ISIN INE389H14MO3, Scrip Code 731992, Qty 2,000, Issue Price Rs. 491,183, Rate 7.28% p.a., Redemption 23/09/2026); IPA is Axis Bank Limited; rated CRISIL A1+/IND A1+.
- SBICAP Securities Limited has one CP listed: SSL-22-10-26-CP (ISIN INE212K14EM2, Scrip Code 731993, Qty 4,000, Issue Price Rs. 488,673.50, Rate 7.05% p.a., Allotment 24/06/2026, Redemption 22/10/2026); rated CRISIL A1+/ICRA A1+.
- All interest payments are cumulative and paid at maturity (on the respective redemption dates).

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational listing notice under existing BSE Debt Segment listing regulations for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure transactions in these CPs occur only in dematerialised form.
- Trades must be executed in standard denomination lots of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof.
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE's Debt Department at 22728352 / 8597 / 8995 / 5753 / 8915.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt market listing with minimal broader market impact. The instruments are short-tenor, high-quality (A1+ rated) money market instruments primarily of interest to institutional and wholesale debt market participants. The aggregate face value across all five CPs is approximately Rs. 850 crores (17,000 units × Rs. 5 lakhs each). No impact on equity trading or retail investors. Trading members active in the BSE Debt Segment should update their systems with the new scrip codes (731989–731993) and ISIN numbers to enable trading.
