## Summary

BSE has notified that Total Environment Constructions Pvt Ltd has completed a part redemption of its debentures (scrip code: 975621, ISIN: INE257Y07049). Following this redemption, the face value has been reduced to Rs. 92,857 per debenture. The debentures will trade with the revised reduced face value from January 16, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: Total Environment Constructions Pvt Ltd
- Debenture Series: TECPL-13.53%-31-3-29-PVT
- ISIN: INE257Y07049
- Scrip Code: 975621
- Reduced Face Value: Rs. 92,857 per debenture
- Purpose: Part Redemption of Debentures
- Trading with reduced face value commences: January 16, 2026
- Reference: DR-800/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a corporate action notice regarding part redemption of debentures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the reduced face value for trading purposes
- The debentures must be traded at the new face value of Rs. 92,857 per debenture from January 16, 2026 onwards
- All trading systems and records should be updated to reflect the revised face value

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: January 14, 2026
- Effective Date for Trading with Reduced Face Value: January 16, 2026
- Debenture Maturity: March 31, 2029

## Impact Assessment

**Debenture Holders**: Part redemption results in a reduced face value per debenture, providing partial liquidity to investors while maintaining their investment in the remaining principal amount.

**Trading Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to reflect the new face value of Rs. 92,857 per debenture to ensure accurate pricing and settlement.

**Market Impact**: Limited broader market impact as this is a specific corporate action affecting only holders of these particular debentures. The debt segment will continue normal operations with adjusted parameters for this security.
