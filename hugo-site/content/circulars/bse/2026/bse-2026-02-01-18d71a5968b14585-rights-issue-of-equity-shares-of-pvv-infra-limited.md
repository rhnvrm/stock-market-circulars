## Summary

PVV INFRA LIMITED (Scrip Code: 536659) has announced a rights issue of equity shares with a record date of February 5, 2026. The company is offering 6 equity shares of Rs.5 each for every 7 equity shares held by existing shareholders. Trading in the company's equity shares will be conducted on an ex-rights basis from February 5, 2026 (Settlement No. DR-814/2025-2026).

## Key Points

- Company: PVV INFRA LIMITED (Scrip Code: 536659)
- Rights ratio: 6 equity shares for every 7 equity shares held
- Face value: Rs.5 per share
- Issue price: Rs.5 per share at par
- Segment: Dematerialized Securities â€“ Rolling Settlement Segment
- Payment structure: Rs.1.25 per share on application, Rs.3.75 per share on first and final call

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE must:
- Execute all transactions in PVV INFRA LIMITED equity shares on an ex-rights basis from February 5, 2026
- Ensure proper settlement under Settlement No. DR-814/2025-2026
- Take note of the record date and payment terms for client servicing

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: February 5, 2026
- **Ex-Rights Date**: February 5, 2026
- **Settlement Number**: DR-814/2025-2026
- **Circular Date**: February 1, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Impact on Shareholders**: Existing shareholders as of the record date will be eligible to subscribe to the rights issue at par value. The rights entitlement ratio of 6:7 means shareholders can increase their holdings proportionally.

**Impact on Trading**: From February 5, 2026, all trading will be on ex-rights basis, meaning new buyers will not be entitled to participate in the rights issue. This may impact short-term price movements.

**Payment Terms**: The staggered payment structure (25% on application, 75% on call) provides flexibility to shareholders while reducing immediate capital outlay requirements.
