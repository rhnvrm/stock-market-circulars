## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that further new securities issued under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) / Employee Stock Option Schemes (ESOS) by 10 listed companies have been listed and admitted for trading with effect from Thursday, July 2, 2026.

## Key Points

- ArisInfra Solutions Limited (ISIN: INE0H9P01028) - 32,600 shares of face value ₹2
- AYM Syntex Limited (ISIN: INE193B01039) - 120,000 shares of face value ₹10
- India Shelter Finance Corporation Limited (ISIN: INE922K01024) - 69,456 shares of face value ₹5
- Jamna Auto Industries Limited (ISIN: INE039C01032) - 198,500 shares of face value ₹1
- Jeevan Scientific Technology Limited (ISIN: INE237B01018) - 161,250 shares of face value ₹10
- Max Estates Limited (ISIN: INE03EI01018) - 9,644 shares of face value ₹10
- Poonawalla Fincorp Limited (ISIN: INE511C01022) - 18,697 shares of face value ₹2
- Shadowfax Technologies Limited (ISIN: INE12UN01015) - 104,208 shares of face value ₹10
- Signatureglobal (India) Limited (ISIN: INE903U01023) - 175,335 shares of face value ₹1
- Tube Investments of India Limited (ISIN: INE974X01010) - 6,030 shares of face value ₹1

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a routine administrative listing action under existing BSE listing procedures for ESOP/ESOS-issued shares.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific action required from investors or trading members beyond noting the increase in listed and tradable share capital for the mentioned companies.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 1, 2026
- Effective Date of Listing: Thursday, July 2, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, low-impact listing update reflecting shares allotted to employees under ESOP/ESOS schemes across 10 companies. The additional shares represent a small incremental increase to each company's total outstanding share capital and do not materially affect trading, valuation, or market structure. No action is required from market participants beyond standard awareness of the marginally increased free-float/share count for these stocks.
