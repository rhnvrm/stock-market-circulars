## Summary

BSE has circulated a SEBI order (No. WTM/AS/WRO/32479/2026-27 dated July 09, 2026) concerning Zara Portal, Proprietor Mr. Anurag Jaiswal. The order restrains the Noticee from accessing the securities market and from buying, selling, or otherwise dealing in securities, directly or indirectly, or being associated with the securities market in any manner for a period of 1 year, effective from the date of completion of refund as directed in paragraph 29(a) of the SEBI order.

## Key Points

- SEBI has issued a market access restraint order against Zara Portal, Proprietor Mr. Anurag Jaiswal.
- The restraint period is 1 year, commencing from the date of completion of the refund directed under paragraph 29(a) of the order.
- The Noticee is prohibited from buying, selling, or dealing in securities, directly or indirectly, and from being associated with the securities market in any manner.
- If the Noticee holds any open positions in exchange-traded derivative contracts as of the order date, these must be closed out/squared off within 3 months from the order date or at contract expiry, whichever is earlier.
- Settlement of pay-in and pay-out obligations for transactions completed before close of trading on the order date is permitted.
- The order is effective from its date of issuance, July 09, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No broad regulatory or policy change; this is an individual enforcement action by SEBI against a specific market participant (Zara Portal, Proprietor Mr. Anurag Jaiswal) under order no. WTM/AS/WRO/32479/2026-27.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members of the Exchange must take note of the SEBI order and ensure compliance with its directions.
- Any open derivative positions held by the Noticee must be closed out or squared off within 3 months from the order date or at expiry, whichever is earlier.
- Trading members should facilitate settlement of pre-existing pay-in/pay-out obligations related to the Noticee's transactions prior to the order date.

## Important Dates

- Order Date: July 09, 2026
- Notice Date: July 09, 2026 (Notice No. 20260709-33)
- Derivative position close-out deadline: within 3 months from July 09, 2026, or at contract expiry, whichever is earlier
- Market restraint period: 1 year from the date of completion of refund as per paragraph 29(a) of the SEBI order

## Impact Assessment

The direct market impact is limited, as this order pertains to a single proprietorship (Zara Portal) rather than a listed company or broad market segment. However, trading members must ensure operational compliance regarding any open derivative positions linked to the Noticee and process related settlement obligations appropriately. This reflects SEBI's ongoing enforcement actions against unauthorized or non-compliant market participants.
