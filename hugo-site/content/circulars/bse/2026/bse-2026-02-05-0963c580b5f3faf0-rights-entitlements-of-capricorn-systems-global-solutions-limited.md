## Summary

BSE has announced the commencement of trading in Rights Entitlements (REs) for renunciation of CAPRICORN SYSTEMS GLOBAL SOLUTIONS LIMITED. Trading in REs will be permitted from Monday, February 09, 2026 to Thursday, February 12, 2026. The Rights Issue itself will be open from February 09, 2026 to February 17, 2026.

## Key Points

- Rights Entitlements trading begins February 09, 2026 and ends February 12, 2026
- Scrip Code: 751082, Scrip ID: CAPRI-RE, ISIN: INE968E20012
- Market Lot: 1, Group: R
- REs will be settled on T+1 Rolling Settlement basis
- Trading will be on trade-for-trade basis
- REs tradable in dematerialized form only
- Rights Issue open period: February 09, 2026 to February 17, 2026
- Unsubscribed or unrenounced REs will lapse after Issue Closing Date

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This notice follows existing framework for Rights Entitlements trading as per notices dated March 05, 2020 (20200305-21) and May 18, 2020 (20200518-34).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must inform all clients about commencement of REs trading
- Trading Members must mention the ISIN of the RE (INE968E20012) in client communications
- Trading Members must include prominent disclaimer in contract notes/bills for RE purchases
- Disclaimer must inform clients that REs give right to participate in Rights Issue or renounce before issue closes
- Disclaimer must warn that REs not subscribed or renounced will lapse after Issue Closing Date
- Trading Members must ensure clients do not mistake REs for ordinary shares

## Important Dates

- **February 09, 2026**: Rights Entitlements trading commences; Rights Issue opens
- **February 12, 2026**: Rights Entitlements trading for renunciation ends
- **February 17, 2026**: Rights Issue closes; deadline for subscription or renunciation

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - affects existing shareholders of CAPRICORN SYSTEMS GLOBAL SOLUTIONS LIMITED who can trade their rights entitlements in a 4-day window.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must implement specific disclosure and disclaimer requirements in client communications and contract notes for RE transactions.

**Investor Impact**: Shareholders have limited time (February 09-12, 2026) to renounce their rights entitlements through market trading if they do not wish to subscribe to the rights issue. Those who neither subscribe nor renounce will see their REs lapse.
