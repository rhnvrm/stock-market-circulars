## Summary

BSE has notified Trading Members that 123 CAPITALS (Member No. 6545) has converted its business structure from a partnership firm into a corporate entity named STOCKFIELD FINANCIAL SERVICES PRIVATE LIMITED (Member No. 6545). This conversion has been approved by SEBI, and the Trading Member's name will officially change effective Friday, 10th July, 2026.

## Key Points

- 123 CAPITALS (Member No. 6545) has converted from a partnership firm to a private limited company.
- The new corporate entity name is STOCKFIELD FINANCIAL SERVICES PRIVATE LIMITED.
- The Member Number (6545) remains unchanged.
- The conversion has received SEBI approval.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory requirements are introduced; this is a member-specific change reflecting the conversion of the trading member's legal entity structure, approved by SEBI under Membership Compliance norms.

## Compliance Requirements

Market participants and counterparties dealing with Member No. 6545 should update their records to reflect the new entity name, STOCKFIELD FINANCIAL SERVICES PRIVATE LIMITED, in place of 123 CAPITALS.

## Important Dates

- Effective Date: Friday, 10th July, 2026 — name change takes effect.

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact administrative notice affecting only the named trading member and its counterparties. There is no effect on listed securities, trading operations, or the broader market.
