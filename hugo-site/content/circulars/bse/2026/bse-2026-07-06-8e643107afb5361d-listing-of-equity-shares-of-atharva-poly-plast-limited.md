## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that the equity shares of Atharva Poly-Plast Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange effective Tuesday, July 7, 2026, under the 'MT' Group of Securities in the SME segment. Per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012, the scrip will trade in the Trade-for-Trade segment for the first 10 trading days.

## Key Points

- Company: Atharva Poly-Plast Limited, Registered Office: W-163A, S Block MIDC Bhosari, Pune - 411026, Maharashtra
- Scrip ID/Abbreviated Name on BOLT: ATHARVA; Scrip Code: 544812; ISIN: INE226801012
- Number of securities: 16,849,998 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each fully paid up (Distinctive Numbers 1 to 16,849,998)
- Market Lot: 2000 shares; trades will be executed only in dematerialised form
- Issue Price: Rs. 60/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- + premium Rs. 50/-)
- Date of Allotment in the public issue: July 3, 2026
- Scrip will be part of Special Pre-open Session (SPOS) on the listing day, July 7, 2026
- Market Maker: R. K. Stockholding Private Limited
- Registrar to the issue: MUFG INTIME India Private Limited (formerly Link Intime Private Limited)

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a listing notice implementing existing SEBI/BSE norms for SME IPO listings, including mandatory Trade-for-Trade treatment for new listings per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure trades in the scrip are executed only in dematerialised form and in minimum market lot of 2000 shares.
- The company has executed the necessary corporate action to mark the lock-in period in depository records as per Annexure I.
- Shareholding pattern is disclosed as per Annexure II.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: July 3, 2026
- Listing and commencement of trading: Tuesday, July 7, 2026
- Trade-for-Trade segment applicability: 10 trading days from listing date

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine SME listing notice with impact limited to shareholders, market makers, and trading members dealing in ATHARVA shares. The Trade-for-Trade restriction for 10 days will limit intraday trading and require delivery-based settlement, moderating early volatility typical of newly listed SME scrips.
