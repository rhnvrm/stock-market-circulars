## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 80,894,331 new equity shares of Aditya Birla Capital Ltd (Scrip Code: 540691), each of face value Rs. 10/-, issued to Promoters on a preferential basis at a premium of Rs. 346.02/- (issue price Rs. 356.02/- per share), are listed and permitted to trade with effect from Monday, July 6, 2026. These shares rank pari-passu with the existing equity shares of the company.

## Key Points

- New securities: 80,894,331 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each
- Issued to Promoters on a preferential allotment basis
- Issue price: Rs. 356.02/- per share (premium of Rs. 346.02/-)
- Distinctive numbers: 2621853065 to 2702747395
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE674K01013

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a listing approval notice under BSE Listing Operations for shares already allotted via preferential issue.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must recognize these shares as valid for trading from the effective date and observe the lock-in restriction on these shares until the specified date; no other member action required.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: June 23, 2026
- Listing/Trading Effective Date: Monday, July 6, 2026
- Lock-in expiry: January 11, 2028

## Impact Assessment

The listing increases Aditya Birla Capital's total equity share capital by 80,894,331 shares, but since these shares are subject to a lock-in until January 2028, there is no immediate impact on free float or trading liquidity. The preferential issue to promoters at a premium may signal promoter confidence but has limited near-term market impact given the lock-in restriction.
