## Summary

BSE, through ICCL (Indian Clearing Corporation Limited), has published the trading and settlement programme for the New Debt Segment (NDS) for the period from 01.07.2026 to 31.07.2026. This is a routine operational notice providing the scheduled trading and settlement calendar for NDS participants for the month of July 2026.

## Key Points

- Covers the trading and settlement schedule for the New Debt Segment (NDS) for July 2026.
- Issued by ICCL under the Settlement/RMS category.
- Attachment NDS072026 contains the detailed programme schedule.
- Applicable to all NDS segment participants for the specified period.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced. This is a standard periodic programme notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- NDS segment participants should refer to the attached schedule (NDS072026) for specific trading and settlement dates.
- Participants must adhere to the settlement obligations as per the published programme for the July 2026 period.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 05 June 2026
- Programme Period: 01.07.2026 to 31.07.2026

## Impact Assessment

Low impact. This is a routine monthly operational circular applicable only to New Debt Segment participants. It provides scheduling information for trading and settlement activities in July 2026 and does not introduce any new requirements or changes to existing rules.
