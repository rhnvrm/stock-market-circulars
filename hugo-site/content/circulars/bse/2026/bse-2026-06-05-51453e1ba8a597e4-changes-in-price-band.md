## Summary

BSE has revised the price bands for 27 equity scrips effective June 8, 2026, under Notice No. 20260605-35 issued by the Department of Surveillance and Supervision (DOSS). The revised bands range from 2% to 20%, indicating adjustments to trading limits based on surveillance requirements.

## Key Points

- 27 scrips affected with revised price bands effective June 8, 2026
- Price bands revised to 2% for 2 scrips (Covidh Technologies Ltd, MPIL Corporation Ltd) — the most restrictive level
- Price bands revised to 5% for 15 scrips
- Price bands revised to 10% for 7 scrips
- Price bands revised to 20% for 2 scrips (Rapid Multimodal Logistics Ltd, Vardhman Polytex Ltd) — the most permissive level among those revised
- Issued by Surveillance department; members may contact bse.surv@bseindia.com for clarifications

## Regulatory Changes

The price band limits for the listed scrips are being revised from their existing levels. Price bands restrict the maximum intraday movement (upper/lower circuit) permissible for a stock. Tighter bands (2%) indicate heightened surveillance concern, while wider bands (20%) reflect relaxation of restrictions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the revised price bands for the affected scrips and ensure order placement complies with the new limits from June 8, 2026 onwards
- No action required from listed companies; this is an exchange-level trading control measure

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 5, 2026
- **Effective Date:** June 8, 2026
