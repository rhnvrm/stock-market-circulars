## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 21,04,802 new equity shares of Bazel International Limited (Scrip Code: 539946, ISIN: INE217E01014) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Tuesday, June 9, 2026. The shares were issued at a premium of Rs. 32.12/- (face value Rs. 10/-) on a preferential basis to non-promoters, pursuant to the conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 21,04,802 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a total price of Rs. 42.12/- per share (Rs. 10/- face value + Rs. 32.12/- premium)
- Issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis via conversion of warrants
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution Nos.: 7710445 to 9815246
- Date of Allotment: 30 March 2026
- Trading commences: Tuesday, 9 June 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for newly allotted equity shares following corporate approval of a preferential warrant conversion.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to permit trading of these shares from June 9, 2026
- Lock-in restrictions must be observed as specified below

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** 30 March 2026
- **Trading Commencement:** 9 June 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (2,21,000 shares, Dist. Nos. 7710445–7931444):** 30 December 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (18,83,802 shares, Dist. Nos. 7931445–9815246):** 29 December 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds approximately 21.05 lakh shares to the tradeable float of Bazel International Limited, partially offset by lock-in restrictions on the full allotment until end-December 2026. Since all newly listed shares are under lock-in, there is no immediate increase in free-float liquidity. Market impact is minimal and confined to existing shareholders and prospective investors monitoring the company's equity dilution from the warrant conversion exercise.
