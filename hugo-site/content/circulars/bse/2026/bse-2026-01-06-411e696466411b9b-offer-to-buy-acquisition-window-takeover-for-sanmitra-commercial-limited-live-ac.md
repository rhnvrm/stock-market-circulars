## Summary

BSE has announced the schedule for Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) of shares of SANMITRA COMMERCIAL LIMITED (Scrip Code: 512062, Symbol: ZSANMCOM). The offer will be live from January 6, 2026 to January 19, 2026. Trading Members and Custodian Members can facilitate bids for up to 1,49,76,000 equity shares at an offer price of Rs. 15.00 per share through the iBBS platform and RTRMS system.

## Key Points

- Offer Size: Up to 1,49,76,000 equity shares
- Offer Price: Rs. 15.00 per share
- Face Value: Rs. 10.00 per share
- Market Lot: 1
- ISIN: INE896J01014
- Scrip Code: 512062
- Security Symbol: ZSANMCOM
- Bid offering period: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM daily
- Trading Members can bid for investors having shares in demat form only
- UCC of investors must be registered with correct PAN Number and Category

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This circular provides operational instructions for a specific takeover offer.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members and Custodian Members must ensure investor UCC is registered and updated with correct PAN Number and Category in Exchange UCC database
- Trading Members can only bid for investors having shares in demat form
- Members must access OTB module through specified URLs (iBBS platform, RTRMS, and Extranet)
- Members should refer to Exchange Notices 20150930-1 (September 30, 2015) and 20170203-29 (February 3, 2017) for operational instructions
- For Leased Line URL access settings, refer to Exchange Circular No. 20170503-12

## Important Dates

- **Opening Date:** Tuesday, January 6, 2026
- **Closing Date:** Monday, January 19, 2026
- **Bid Offering Period:** January 6, 2026 to January 19, 2026 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Give-up/Take-up Period:** January 6, 2026 to January 16, 2026 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Give-up (Last Day):** January 19, 2026 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Take-up (Last Day):** January 19, 2026 (9:15 AM - 4:00 PM)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This is a company-specific takeover offer affecting only SANMITRA COMMERCIAL LIMITED shareholders. No broader market implications.

**Operational Impact:** Medium for Trading Members and Custodians facilitating the offer. Members need to ensure proper system access (iBBS, RTRMS, Extranet) and investor UCC compliance.

**Investor Categories:** Multiple investor categories can participate including FII, Financial Institutions, Mutual Funds, Insurance Companies, Other QIBs, Companies, and Individuals.

**System Access:** Members must use specified URLs for Internet and Leased Line access. End of Issue files will only be generated on Extranet if securities are accepted after issue closure upon confirmation from Merchant Banker/RTA.

**Support Contacts:** BSE has provided comprehensive contact details for technical support including Trading Operations team members (Sandeep Pujari, Rushi Bhoir, Hormazdiar Mobedji) and Listing Sales team (Mangesh Tayde, Rinkesh Gaikwad) with phone numbers and email addresses.
