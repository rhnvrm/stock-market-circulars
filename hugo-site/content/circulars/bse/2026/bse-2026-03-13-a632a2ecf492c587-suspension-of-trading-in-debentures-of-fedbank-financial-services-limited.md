## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Fedbank Financial Services Limited has fixed a Record Date of March 20, 2026 for the redemption of a specific series of Non-Convertible Debentures. Trading in the said debentures is suspended from March 20, 2026 onwards. This is a routine corporate action related to the maturity and redemption of privately placed NCDs.

## Key Points

- Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures of Fedbank Financial Services Limited from March 20, 2026.
- The debenture series affected: **FFSL-GSEC32-4-4-26-PVT** with ISIN **INE007N07033** (BSE Code: 974516).
- Record Date for redemption is **March 20, 2026**.
- No dealings are permitted from March 20, 2026.
- Notice reference: DR-844/2025-2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular implements standard exchange procedure for suspending trading in debt securities approaching their redemption date.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in the debenture series FFSL-GSEC32-4-4-26-PVT (INE007N07033 / Code 974516) effective March 20, 2026.
- Members are required to take note of the suspension and ensure no trades are executed in the said security on or after the no-dealings date.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** March 13, 2026
- **Record Date for Redemption:** March 20, 2026
- **No Dealings From:** March 20, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and traders of the specific NCD series (FFSL-GSEC32-4-4-26-PVT). This is a standard end-of-life action for a privately placed debenture reaching its redemption date. Investors holding these debentures should expect redemption proceeds on or around the record date. No broader market or systemic impact is anticipated.
