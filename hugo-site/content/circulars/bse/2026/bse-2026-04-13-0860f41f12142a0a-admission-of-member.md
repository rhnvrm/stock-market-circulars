## Summary

BSE Ltd. has formally admitted five entities as Trading Members effective April 13, 2026. Three entities have been granted new memberships and two existing members have been granted access to additional segments. All admissions are subject to the Rules, Bye-laws, and Regulations of the Exchange.

## Key Points

- Five trading members admitted or granted additional segment access on BSE Ltd.
- Three new memberships granted to Genxpress Opp Private Limited, Kwikwiz Alpha Private Limited, and Auric Bullion and Jewellers.
- Two existing members — Nai Nath Commodities Private Limited and J.P.Morgan India Pvt. Ltd. — granted access to additional segments.
- Admitted members have trading rights only on BSE Ltd.
- Contact for clarifications: membership.ops@bseindia.com

|---------|-------------|------------|----------|------|

| 2 | Kwikwiz Alpha Private Limited | 6984 | Cash/Equity Derivatives | New Membership |
| 3 | Auric Bullion and Jewellers | 6985 | Cash/Commodity Derivatives | New Membership |
| 4 | Nai Nath Commodities Private Limited | 5177 | Equity Derivatives | Additional Segment |
| 5 | J.P.Morgan India Pvt. Ltd. | 324 | Equity Derivatives | Additional Segment |

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine administrative notice informing existing trading members of newly admitted members as required under Exchange rules.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action required for existing trading members. Newly admitted members must adhere to the Rules, Bye-laws, and Regulations of BSE Ltd. as applicable to their respective segments.

## Important Dates

- **Admission Date:** April 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard administrative circular notifying the market of new trading member admissions. The addition of J.P.Morgan India Pvt. Ltd. to Equity Derivatives is notable given the firm's institutional standing, but overall this notice does not affect trading conditions, compliance obligations, or market structure for existing participants.
