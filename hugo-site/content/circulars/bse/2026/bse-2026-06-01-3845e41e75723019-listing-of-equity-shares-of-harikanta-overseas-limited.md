## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that the equity shares of Harikanta Overseas Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange. The company is being listed on the SME segment. A separate notice will communicate the actual date of listing and further details of the securities.

## Key Points

- Harikanta Overseas Limited equity shares are approved for listing on BSE's SME segment
- Scrip Code: **544769**, Symbol: **HARIKANTA**, ISIN: **INE1UYW01010**
- Exact listing date will be announced via a separate notice
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing admission notice under BSE's DCS-Listing department.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should await the follow-up notice for the confirmed listing date and full security details before executing any trades in this scrip.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 1, 2026
- **Listing Date:** To be announced via separate notice

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a preliminary listing notice for an SME-segment company. The broader market is unaffected; only participants interested in SME IPO allotments or secondary market trading in Harikanta Overseas Limited need to monitor the subsequent listing date announcement.
