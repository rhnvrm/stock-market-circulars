## Summary

BSE has announced the resumption of trading in bonds of Power Finance Corporation Ltd (Scrip Code 974611) with effect from June 03, 2026. This follows the company's confirmation that the Put Option has not been exercised, allowing trading to resume normally.

## Key Points

- Trading in Power Finance Corporation Ltd bonds (Scrip Code 974611) will resume from June 03, 2026
- The Put Option associated with these bonds was NOT exercised by bondholders
- This notice continues from Exchange Notice No. 20260130-40 dated January 30, 2026
- Record Date for Payment of Interest and Redemption on Exercise of Put Option was February 05, 2026
- Reference number: DR-643/2026-2027

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notice confirming the reinstatement of normal trading status for the specified bond scrip.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members are requested to note that Scrip Code 974611 bonds will be available for trading from June 03, 2026. No specific compliance action is required from members beyond updating their systems to reflect the resumed trading status.

## Important Dates

- **Record Date (Put Option):** February 05, 2026
- **Trading Resumption Date:** June 03, 2026
- **Notice Date:** June 02, 2026
- **Reference Notice:** 20260130-40 dated January 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. The non-exercise of the Put Option means bondholders have elected to retain their holdings, and the bonds will continue to trade on the BSE Debt segment. This is a routine operational update relevant only to participants holding or trading Scrip Code 974611 bonds of Power Finance Corporation Ltd.
