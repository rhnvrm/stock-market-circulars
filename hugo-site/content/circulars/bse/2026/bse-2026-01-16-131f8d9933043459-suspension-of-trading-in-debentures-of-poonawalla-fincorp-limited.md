## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in specific non-convertible debentures of Poonawalla Fincorp Limited effective January 22, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing the record date for redemption of debentures with ISIN INE511C07805 (Code: 975372). Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Debenture series affected: PWFL-8.38%-6-2-26-PVT
- ISIN: INE511C07805
- BSE Code: 975372
- Record date for redemption: January 22, 2026
- No dealings permitted from: January 22, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of non-convertible debentures
- Notice reference: DR-804/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notification following established procedures for debenture redemption and trading suspension.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures of Poonawalla Fincorp Limited from January 22, 2026
- Trading members are required to take note of the suspension and ensure compliance with the no-dealing restriction
- The restriction applies specifically to debentures with ISIN INE511C07805

## Important Dates

- **January 16, 2026**: Circular issued
- **January 22, 2026**: Record date for redemption
- **January 22, 2026**: Trading suspension effective date (no dealings permitted from this date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The suspension affects only one specific series of non-convertible debentures issued by Poonawalla Fincorp Limited. Debenture holders will receive redemption proceeds as per the company's redemption schedule.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to prevent any trades in the specified debentures from January 22, 2026. Investors holding these debentures should be aware that they cannot trade them from the suspension date and should expect redemption proceeds based on the terms of the debentures.

**Investor Impact**: Holders of these specific debentures (ISIN: INE511C07805) will have their securities redeemed. This is a routine corporate action and investors whose names appear in the records on January 22, 2026 will be eligible for redemption proceeds.
