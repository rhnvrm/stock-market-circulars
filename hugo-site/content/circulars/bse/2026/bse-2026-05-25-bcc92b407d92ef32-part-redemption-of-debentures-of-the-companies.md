## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260525-5 informs trading members that four companies have fixed record dates for payment of interest and part redemption of Non-Convertible Debentures/Bonds. Trading in the respective debentures/bonds will be conducted at reduced face values from the specified ex-dates.

## Key Points

- Eight debenture series across four companies are undergoing part redemption
- Trading members must note reduced face values effective from respective ex-dates
- Record dates span from 26 May 2026 to 1 June 2026
- Settlements fall under DR 638, 640, and 641 of 2026-2027
- All instruments are privately placed NCDs/Bonds listed on the debt segment

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework introduced. This is a standard corporate action notification under BSE's listing operations for debt securities requiring adjustment of traded face values post partial redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to:
- Note the reduced face values for the listed debenture series from the respective ex-dates
- Ensure all trades in these instruments from the ex-date onward reflect the reduced face value
- Update records for settlement under the applicable DR numbers

## Impact Assessment

This notice impacts debt segment trading in eight privately placed NCD series. Trading members holding or trading these instruments must update their systems to reflect reduced face values from the respective ex-dates. The part redemptions reduce the outstanding principal on each debenture, which directly affects valuation and settlement amounts. FRIEZA 06 2025's debenture sees a significant reduction to Rs. 0.25/- per debenture, indicating a near-full redemption. Broader market impact is limited as these are privately placed instruments with restricted investor participation.
