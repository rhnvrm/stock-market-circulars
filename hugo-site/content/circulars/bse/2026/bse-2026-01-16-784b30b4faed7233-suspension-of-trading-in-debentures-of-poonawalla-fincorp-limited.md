## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in specific non-convertible debentures (NCDs) of Poonawalla Fincorp Limited effective January 22, 2026. The suspension is due to the company setting a record date for redemption of these debentures. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Company: Poonawalla Fincorp Limited
- Security affected: PWFL-8.38%-6-2-26-PVT (ISIN: INE511C07805, Code: 975372)
- Record date for redemption: January 22, 2026
- Trading suspension effective: January 22, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of non-convertible debentures
- Circular reference: DR-804/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational notice for corporate action processing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any transactions in the specified debentures from January 22, 2026
- Trading members should update their systems to reflect the trading suspension
- Debenture holders on record as of January 22, 2026 will be eligible for redemption

## Important Dates

- **January 16, 2026**: Notice issued by BSE
- **January 22, 2026**: Record date for redemption and effective date for trading suspension

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - affects only holders of specific PWFL debentures with ISIN INE511C07805. This is a routine corporate action related to scheduled debenture redemption.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - standard suspension procedure for debenture redemption. Trading members need to ensure compliance with no-dealing instruction from January 22, 2026.

**Investor Impact**: Investors holding these debentures as of the record date will receive redemption proceeds as per the terms of issuance. No trading opportunity available from suspension date onwards.
