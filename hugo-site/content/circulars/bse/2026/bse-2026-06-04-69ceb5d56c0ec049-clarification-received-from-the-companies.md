## Summary

BSE's Surveillance department (DOSS) observed significant price/volume movement in 10 scrips and wrote to the respective companies seeking clarifications. As of 04 June 2026, replies have been received from 6 companies, while responses from 4 companies are still awaited.

## Key Points

- BSE observed significant price/volume movement in 10 listed scrips and sought clarifications from the companies concerned
- 6 companies have submitted replies, which are attached to the notice
- 4 companies have not yet responded; replies are awaited
- Circular issued by BSE Surveillance department (DOSS) to ensure trading members and investors have the latest relevant information

## Companies That Have Replied

1. Axiscades Technologies Ltd (Scrip Code: 532395)
2. Electrotherm (India) Ltd (Scrip Code: 526608)
3. NIIT Ltd (Scrip Code: 500304)
4. PNB Gilts Ltd (Scrip Code: 532366)
5. Raj Television Network Ltd (Scrip Code: 532826)
6. Savita Oil Technologies Ltd (Scrip Code: 524667)

## Reply Awaited From

1. Elango Industries Ltd (Scrip Code: 513452)
2. Nexxus Petro Industries Ltd (Scrip Code: 544265)
3. York Exports Ltd (Scrip Code: 530675)
4. Colinz Laboratories Ltd (Scrip Code: 531210)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard surveillance disclosure notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- The 4 companies yet to respond (Elango Industries, Nexxus Petro Industries, York Exports, Colinz Laboratories) are expected to submit their clarifications to BSE at the earliest
- Trading members and investors are advised to review the attached replies from the 6 companies before making trading decisions in these scrips

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 04 June 2026
- No specific deadline mentioned for pending replies

## Impact Assessment

The circular signals heightened surveillance scrutiny on these 10 scrips due to unusual price or volume activity. Investors and trading members should exercise caution and review the company clarifications before trading. The 4 scrips awaiting replies (Elango Industries, Nexxus Petro, York Exports, Colinz Laboratories) remain under active surveillance and may be subject to further regulatory action if satisfactory responses are not received.
