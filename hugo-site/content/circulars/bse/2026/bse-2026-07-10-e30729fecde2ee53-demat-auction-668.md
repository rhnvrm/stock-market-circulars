## Summary

BSE Limited has invited offers from trading members of the Exchange for purchase of securities in Auction 668, pertaining to Rolling Settlement No. DR-668/2026-27. This is a routine settlement/RMS process to cover short deliveries in the settlement cycle, conducted by Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd.

## Key Points

- Auction No. 668 relates to Rolling Settlement DR-668/2026-27
- Auction offer entry window: 02:00 p.m. to 02:45 p.m. on Friday, July 10, 2026
- Auction matching: 02:45 p.m. to 03:00 p.m.
- Delivery statement and report of accepted auction offers: 03:00 p.m. to 03:30 p.m.
- Money statement and final reports: 03:35 p.m. to 05:30 p.m.
- Auction pay-in scheduled for Monday, July 13, 2026 at 08:00 a.m.
- Under the Uniform Membership structure in Equity Cash Segment, settlement is done through the Clearing Member of a Trading Member
- The prior restriction on trading members offering securities in auction for settlements in which they had short-delivered such securities has been removed
- No corrections permitted for punching errors in security code and rate; Exchange reserves right to accept/reject any offer
- Trading members advised to refer cutoff rates on the Auction Offer Entry Screen and check for Corporate Action notices (Ex-Rights/Ex-Bonus/Ex-Split) affecting relevant scrips

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a standard recurring auction process notice. It reiterates the existing removal of the restriction on trading members offering securities in auction for settlements where they had short-delivered, following the Uniform Membership structure implementation.

## Compliance Requirements

Members and Custodians must check their auction pay-in obligations for securities and execute securities pay-in within depository cut-off timings. Trading members must exercise care while punching security codes and rates, as no corrections are allowed post-entry.

## Important Dates

- Auction offer entry: July 10, 2026, 02:00 p.m. - 02:45 p.m.
- Auction matching: July 10, 2026, 02:45 p.m. - 03:00 p.m.
- Delivery statement/report: July 10, 2026, 03:00 p.m. - 03:30 p.m.
- Money statement/final reports: July 10, 2026, 03:35 p.m. - 05:30 p.m.
- Auction pay-in: Monday, July 13, 2026, 08:00 a.m.

## Impact Assessment

This notice has limited impact, confined to trading members and custodians participating in the short delivery auction process for Settlement DR-668/2026-27. It does not affect specific listed companies or broader market pricing, and represents routine clearing and settlement operations administered by Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd.
