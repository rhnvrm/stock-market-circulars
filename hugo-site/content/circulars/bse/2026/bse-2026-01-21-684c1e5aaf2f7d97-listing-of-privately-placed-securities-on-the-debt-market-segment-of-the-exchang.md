## Summary

BSE has listed commercial papers issued on private placement basis by five companies on its debt market segment effective January 21, 2026. The securities include commercial papers from Birla Group Holdings Private Limited, 360 ONE Alternates Asset Management Limited, Bajaj Financial Securities Limited, Samvardhana Motherson International Limited, and Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited. All securities will trade in dematerialized form with standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs.

## Key Points

- Five companies have listed commercial papers totaling 10,200 units on BSE debt segment
- All securities will trade only in dematerialized form under respective ISIN numbers
- Standard trading denomination is Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for all securities is 1 paise
- Interest rates range from 6.29% to 8% per annum
- Maturity periods vary from 10 days to 1 year
- All commercial papers have A1+ credit ratings from CRISIL, ICRA, CARE, or IND rating agencies

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Transactions must be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- All interest payments are cumulative and paid at redemption

- **Redemption Dates**: Range from January 30, 2026 to January 21, 2027 depending on security
