## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of Fly-Hi Maritime Travels Limited on its SME platform for public review and comments. The document was filed on December 31, 2025 and contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations. Stakeholders are invited to submit comments within 21 days from the notice date.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Fly-Hi Maritime Travels Limited on December 31, 2025
- Document contains all disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website (www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx) for minimum 21 days
- Public comments solicited within 21 days from January 01, 2026
- Comments to be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard procedural notice for SME IPO draft offer document disclosure.

## Compliance Requirements

- Stakeholders wishing to provide comments on the Draft Offer Document must submit them within 21 days from January 01, 2026
- Comments should be sent via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- The Draft Offer Document is accessible at www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx for review

## Important Dates

- **December 31, 2025**: Draft Offer Document filed by Fly-Hi Maritime Travels Limited
- **January 01, 2026**: Notice date and start of public comment period
- **January 22, 2026**: Deadline for submission of public comments (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has limited market-wide impact and is primarily relevant to:
- Potential investors interested in the Fly-Hi Maritime Travels Limited SME IPO
- Market participants and analysts tracking SME listings
- The company and its stakeholders

The public comment period allows stakeholders to review disclosures and provide feedback before the offer document is finalized, ensuring transparency in the IPO process.
