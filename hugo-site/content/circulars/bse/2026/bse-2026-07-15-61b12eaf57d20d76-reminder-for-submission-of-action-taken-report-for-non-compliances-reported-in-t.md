## Summary

BSE has issued a reminder to trading members regarding a prior notice (No. 20260608-21 dated June 8, 2026) requiring them to close non-compliance(s) identified in the Internal Audit Report for the half year ended March 31, 2026, and to submit an Action Taken Report (ATR) documenting the same.

## Key Points

- This is a follow-up reminder to Exchange Notice No. 20260608-21 dated June 8, 2026.
- Trading members must close all non-compliances flagged in their Internal Audit Report for the half year ended March 2026.
- The Action Taken Report must be submitted through the BEFS (BSE Electronic Filing System).
- Failure to close non-compliances or submit the ATR by the due date will result in prescribed action under Exchange Notice No. 20260417-45 dated April 17, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a reminder to comply with existing requirements under previously issued Exchange Notices No. 20260608-21 and No. 20260417-45.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must: (1) close all non-compliance(s) identified in the Internal Audit Report for the half year ended March 31, 2026, and (2) submit the Action Taken Report via BEFS confirming closure of these non-compliances.

## Important Dates

- Half year audit period ended: March 31, 2026
- Original notice: June 8, 2026 (Notice No. 20260608-21)
- Deadline for closure of non-compliances and ATR submission: July 31, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has no direct market or stock-price impact. It is an internal compliance obligation affecting BSE trading members only. Non-compliant members risk prescribed regulatory action (as per Notice No. 20260417-45) if they fail to close audit findings or submit the ATR by July 31, 2026.
