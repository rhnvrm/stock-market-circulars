## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Gabion Technologies India Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company will trade under the scrip code 544675 with symbol GTIL and ISIN INE1H1501013. The exact date of listing and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Gabion Technologies India Limited
- Scrip Code: 544675
- Symbol: GTIL
- ISIN: INE1H1501013
- Segment: SME
- Listing date to be announced separately

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard new listing announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the company details for future trading once the listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: January 12, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

Minimal immediate market impact. This is a routine listing announcement for an SME company. Trading will commence only after the listing date is formally notified through a subsequent circular. Trading members should prepare their systems to include the new scrip once the listing date is confirmed.
