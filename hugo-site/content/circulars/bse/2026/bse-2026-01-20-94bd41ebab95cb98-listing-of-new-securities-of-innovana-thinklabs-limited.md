## Summary

BSE has listed 160,000 equity shares of Innovana Thinklabs Limited (Scrip Code: 544302) issued on a preferential basis to non-promoters pursuant to warrant conversion. The shares are permitted for trading with effect from January 21, 2026. The shares were allotted on October 13, 2025, at an issue price of Rs. 431/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- plus premium of Rs. 421/-) and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 160,000 equity shares listed under ISIN INE403Y01018
- Issue price: Rs. 431/- per share (Rs. 10/- face value + Rs. 421/- premium)
- Allotted to non-promoters on preferential basis through warrant conversion
- Distribution numbers: 20500001 to 20660000
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Lock-in period applicable on 160,000 shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification following preferential allotment procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities listing. No specific compliance actions required beyond normal trading procedures for the listed securities.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: October 13, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: January 21, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (150,000 shares)**: July 30, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (10,000 shares)**: July 29, 2026

## Lock-in Details

- 150,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 20500001 to 20650000): Lock-in until July 30, 2026
- 10,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 20650001 to 20660000): Lock-in until July 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this involves a small preferential allotment for a single company. The 160,000 shares represent additional equity capital through warrant conversion. Lock-in restrictions on all shares provide stability during the lock-in period. Trading members can commence regular trading activities in these securities from January 21, 2026.
