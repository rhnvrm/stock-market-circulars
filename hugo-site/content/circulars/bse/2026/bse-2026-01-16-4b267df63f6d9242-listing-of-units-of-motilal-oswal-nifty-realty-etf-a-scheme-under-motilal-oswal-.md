## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of Motilal Oswal Nifty Realty ETF units, an open-ended scheme tracking the Nifty Realty TR Index. The ETF will commence trading on January 19, 2026, in the 'B' Group with scrip code 590150. A total of 720,000 units with a face value of Rs. 10 per unit will be available for trading in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- ETF Name: Motilal Oswal Nifty Realty ETF
- Listing Date: January 19, 2026
- Scrip Code: 590150
- ISIN: INF247L01CI4
- Total Units: 720,000
- Face Value: Rs. 10.00 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 84.96 per unit
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Date of Allotment: March 15, 2024
- Trading: Dematerialized form only
- Group: B

## Market Makers

Three authorized market makers have been designated:
1. East India Securities Ltd.
2. Motilal Oswal Financial Services Limited
3. Kanjalochana Finserve Private Limited
4. Irage Broking Services LLP

## Fund Details

- Fund: Motilal Oswal Fund
- Investment Manager: Motilal Oswal Asset Management Company Limited
- Registered Office: 10th Floor, Motilal Oswal Tower, Rahimtullah Sayani Road, Opp. Parel ST Depot, Prabhadevi, Mumbai-400025
- Tracking Index: Nifty Realty TR Index

## Registrar and Transfer Agent

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all trades are executed in dematerialized form only, as per SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000
- Trades will be effected in unit market lot (1 unit)

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: January 16, 2026
- Allotment Date: March 15, 2024
- Trading Commencement: January 19, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine ETF listing with minimal market-wide impact. The listing provides investors with a new instrument to gain exposure to the real estate sector through the Nifty Realty TR Index. The presence of four market makers ensures adequate liquidity for trading.
