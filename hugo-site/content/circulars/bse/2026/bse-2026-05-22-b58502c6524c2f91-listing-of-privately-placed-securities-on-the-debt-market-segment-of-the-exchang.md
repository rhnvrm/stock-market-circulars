## Summary

BSE has listed four new commercial papers (CPs) on its Debt Market Segment effective May 22, 2026. The securities were issued on a private placement basis by Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited, InCred Financial Services Limited, Bajaj Financial Securities Limited, and Mirae Asset Financial Services (India) Private Limited. All securities are to be traded exclusively in dematerialised form in standard denominations of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof, with a tick size of 1 paise.

## Key Points

- Four commercial papers freshly listed on BSE Debt Segment effective May 22, 2026
- All CPs issued on private placement basis with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Trading only in demat form; standard denomination Rs. 5 Lakhs; tick size 1 paise
- All allotment dates: May 21, 2026
- Redemption periods range from August to November 2026
- Interest rates range from 7.90% to 8.90% p.a. (cumulative)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice under the existing private placement framework for debt securities on BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under the respective ISIN numbers
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise applies to all listed securities
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE Debt Department: 22728352 / 8597 / 8995 / 5753 / 8915

## Important Dates

- **Listing effective date:** May 22, 2026
- **Allotment date (all CPs):** May 21, 2026
- **Redemption – Nuvama Wealth Finance CP (INE918K14DM6):** August 20, 2026
- **Redemption – InCred Financial Services CP (INE321N14463):** November 20, 2026
- **Redemption – Bajaj Financial Securities CP (INE01C314FQ0):** August 21, 2026
- **Redemption – Mirae Asset Financial Services CP (INE0JRU14479):** August 20, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt segment listing with limited broad market impact. The four CPs are short-term money market instruments with maturities between 3 and 6 months, carrying top-tier credit ratings (A1+ from CARE, CRISIL, ICRA, and/or IND). Relevant primarily to institutional debt market participants and trading members active on the BSE Debt Segment. No impact on equity markets.
