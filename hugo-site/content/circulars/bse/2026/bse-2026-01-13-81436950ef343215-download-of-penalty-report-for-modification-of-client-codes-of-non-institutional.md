## Summary

BSE has released the penalty report for client code modifications of non-institutional trades executed during December 2025. The report, named PM1225.Clg, is available for download in the Equity Transaction Folder on the Exchange's Extranet. This is a routine monthly report as per existing Exchange notices from 2011.

## Key Points

- Penalty report covers client code modifications for December 2025
- Report file name: PM1225.Clg
- Available in Equity Transaction Folder on BSE Extranet
- Applies to all segments (Equity, Derivatives, Currency, etc.)
- Members must save the file for future reference
- Report issued under Exchange Notice Nos. 20110729-24 (July 29, 2011) and 20110826-4 (August 26, 2011)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a routine report issued under existing framework established in 2011.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must download and save the penalty report from the Extranet
- Members should review penalties applicable to their client code modifications
- For queries or clarifications, members should contact BSE Trading Operations at 022-2272 8435 or 022-2272 5785, or email bse.reports@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** January 13, 2026
- **Reporting Period:** December 2025
- **File Download:** Available immediately in Extranet

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative circular with minimal market impact. It only affects trading members who modified client codes for non-institutional trades during December 2025 and have penalties applicable. The circular serves as an operational notice to ensure members are aware of and can access their penalty reports for record-keeping and payment purposes.
