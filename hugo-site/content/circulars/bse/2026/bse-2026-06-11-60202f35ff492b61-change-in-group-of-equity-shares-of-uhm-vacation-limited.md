## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the equity shares of UHM Vacation Limited (Scrip Code: 544779), listed on the SME segment, will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment, effective Thursday, June 25, 2026. Post-transfer, the shares will be dealt under the M Group.

## Key Points

- UHM Vacation Limited (Scrip Code: 544779) is moving from the MT Group (Trade for Trade) to the M Group (Rolling segment)
- This notice is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20260610-44 dated June 10, 2026
- The change is applicable to the SME segment
- Trading members may contact Mr. Anurag Jain at 022-2272 8822 for further details

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares of UHM Vacation Limited will no longer be traded on a trade-for-trade basis. Trading will move to the normal Rolling settlement mechanism under the M Group, which allows for netting of positions and standard T+1/T+2 settlement.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should update their systems and client records to reflect the group change for Scrip Code 544779 from MT Group to M Group, effective June 25, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 11, 2026
- **Effective Date:** Thursday, June 25, 2026 — transfer to Rolling segment (M Group) takes effect

## Impact Assessment

This change eases trading restrictions on UHM Vacation Limited shares. Moving from Trade for Trade (MT Group) to Rolling (M Group) means buyers and sellers can now net positions and benefit from standard rolling settlement, increasing liquidity and reducing settlement complexity for the scrip. Impact is limited to traders and investors holding or tracking this specific SME-listed company.
