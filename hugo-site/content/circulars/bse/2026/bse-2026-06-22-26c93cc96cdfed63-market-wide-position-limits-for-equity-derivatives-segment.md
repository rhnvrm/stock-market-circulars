## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (No. 20260622-35) updating the Market Wide Position Limits (MWPL) for stocks in the Equity Derivatives Segment. The applicable limits are detailed in Annexure-I attached to the circular. This is issued by the BSECL department under the Settlement/RMS category.

## Key Points

- MWPL specifies the maximum aggregate open interest permitted across all market participants for each stock in the equity derivatives segment.
- Updated limits are provided stock-wise in Annexure-I of the circular.
- Issued by the BSECL (BSE Clearing Limited) department.
- Applicable to the Derivatives segment.

## Regulatory Changes

No structural regulatory changes are introduced. This circular represents a periodic revision of MWPL thresholds in line with SEBI guidelines, reflecting changes in the underlying outstanding shares or floating stock of eligible companies.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and participants in the equity derivatives segment must ensure that their clients' open positions do not contribute to breaching the prescribed MWPL for any stock.
- Positions exceeding MWPL thresholds may attract restrictions on fresh position creation or compulsory close-out as per exchange rules.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 22 June 2026
- Effective period: As specified in Annexure-I (typically aligned with the current derivatives expiry cycle).

## Impact Assessment

This update affects all participants active in stock futures and options on BSE. Stocks approaching or at MWPL may see restrictions on fresh long or short positions, impacting hedging and speculative activity. Traders and risk managers should review Annexure-I to identify any stocks with tightened limits that may affect existing or planned positions.
