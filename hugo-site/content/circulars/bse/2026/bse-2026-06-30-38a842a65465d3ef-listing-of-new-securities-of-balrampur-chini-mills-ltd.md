## Summary

BSE has listed 93,16,771 new equity shares of Balrampur Chini Mills Ltd. (Scrip Code 500038, ISIN INE119A01028), issued at Rs. 483/- per share (face value Rs. 1/- at a premium of Rs. 482/-) to Promoter and Non-Promoter entities on a preferential basis. These shares are permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Wednesday, July 1, 2026.

## Key Points

- **Total new shares listed:** 93,16,771 equity shares of Rs. 1/- each
- **Issue price:** Rs. 483/- per share (face value Rs. 1/- + premium of Rs. 482/-)
- **Allotment basis:** Preferential allotment to Promoters and Non-Promoters
- **Distribution numbers:** 260649654 to 269966424
- **Date of allotment:** June 3, 2026
- **Trading effective from:** July 1, 2026
- **ISIN:** INE119A01028
- **Pari-passu ranking:** New shares rank equally with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notice informing trading members of newly listed securities under an existing preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the distribution numbers (260649654–269966424) for the new shares
- Members must observe lock-in restrictions: shares within the locked tranches are not freely tradeable until the respective lock-in expiry dates
- Settlement and delivery of locked-in shares must comply with applicable SEBI lock-in regulations for preferential allotments

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Date of allotment | June 3, 2026 |
| Listing / trading effective | July 1, 2026 |
| Lock-in expiry (53,20,907 shares) | December 31, 2026 |
| Lock-in expiry (39,95,864 shares) | December 31, 2027 |

## Impact Assessment

**Lock-in Breakdown:**
- 39,95,864 shares (Dist. Nos. 260649654–264645517): locked in until December 31, 2027 (longer lock-in, likely Promoter allotment)
- 53,20,907 shares (Dist. Nos. 264645518–269966424): locked in until December 31, 2026 (shorter lock-in, likely Non-Promoter allotment)

The new shares represent an addition to Balrampur Chini Mills' equity base via preferential allotment. The majority of shares carry lock-in restrictions, limiting immediate free-float impact. Trading members should update their systems to reflect the new distributable number range. The preferential issue price of Rs. 483/- per share provides a reference point for market valuation context.
