## Summary

BSE has listed commercial paper securities issued on private placement basis by six companies on its Debt Segment effective February 6, 2026. The securities include commercial papers from 360 ONE Prime Limited, Birla Group Holdings Private Limited, Pilani Investment And Industries Corporation Limited, Godrej Housing Finance Limited, Vivriti Capital Limited, and Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited. All securities carry A1+ credit ratings and will trade in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- Six companies have listed commercial paper on BSE Debt Segment
- All securities issued on private placement basis with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Trading in dematerialized form only under respective ISIN numbers
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Interest rates range from 7.25% to 8.90% per annum
- All securities carry A1+ credit rating from CRISIL, ICRA, or CARE
- Maturity periods range from 91 days to 364 days
