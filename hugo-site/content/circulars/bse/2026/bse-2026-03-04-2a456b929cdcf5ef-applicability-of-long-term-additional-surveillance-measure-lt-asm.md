## Summary

BSE has issued a circular on the applicability of the Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LT-ASM) framework effective March 05, 2026. Eleven securities have been newly shortlisted under the framework (Part A), one security has been moved to a higher ASM stage (Part B), none have been moved to a lower stage (Part C), and one security has been removed from the LT-ASM framework (Annexure II).

## Key Points

- **11 new securities** added to the Long Term ASM Framework effective March 05, 2026
- **1 security** (Elango Industries Ltd – 513452) moved to a higher Stage II ASM
- **No securities** moved to a lower ASM stage (Part C: Nil)
- **No securities** added directly to Stage IV LT-ASM (Part D: Nil)
- **1 security** (Capital Trust Ltd – 511505) moved out of the LT-ASM framework due to inclusion in ESM Framework (marked $)
- A consolidated list of all securities currently under LT-ASM is provided in Annexure III

## Regulatory Changes

- The LT-ASM framework is being updated per BSE's ongoing surveillance mandate.
- Part B update: Elango Industries Ltd (Scrip Code: 513452, ISIN: INE594D01018) moves to Stage II LT-ASM.
- Part D remains nil, meaning no direct Stage IV placements this cycle.
- Existing footnote notations apply: (*) NSE-basis inclusions, (^) Non-Promoter Holding basis, ($) ESM Framework inclusion.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and investors must note revised surveillance classifications for affected securities effective March 05, 2026.
- Securities under LT-ASM are subject to additional margin requirements, trade-to-trade settlement, or other restrictions depending on the ASM stage.
- Brokers must ensure client-level compliance with applicable trading conditions for all newly added and stage-changed securities.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** March 05, 2026 – All changes (new additions, stage movements, and exits) come into force.
- **Circular Date:** March 04, 2026
