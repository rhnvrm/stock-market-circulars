## Summary

BSE announces the allocation of shares to anchor investors for the public issue of DIGILOGIC SYSTEMS LIMITED. Anchor investor allocation is conducted prior to the issue opening date and helps in price discovery and demonstrates institutional confidence in the IPO.

## Key Points

- Company: DIGILOGIC SYSTEMS LIMITED
- Allocation made to qualified institutional buyers (QIBs) under the anchor investor category
- Anchor allocation conducted as per SEBI ICDR Regulations
- Allocation price determined for anchor investors
- Lock-in period applicable as per regulations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard procedural announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Anchor investors must comply with lock-in requirements as per SEBI regulations
- Disclosure requirements as per SEBI ICDR Regulations
- Allocation details made public for transparency

## Important Dates

- Allocation Date: January 19, 2026
- Issue opening date to follow after anchor allocation

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - Anchor investor participation indicates institutional confidence and helps establish price band for retail investors. The quality and quantity of anchor allocation often influences retail investor sentiment.

**Investor Impact:** Provides transparency on institutional participation and allocation price, helping retail investors make informed decisions during the main issue period.
