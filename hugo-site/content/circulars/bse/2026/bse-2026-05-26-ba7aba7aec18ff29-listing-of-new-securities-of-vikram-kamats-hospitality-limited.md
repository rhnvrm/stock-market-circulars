## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 9,09,325 new equity shares of Vikram Kamats Hospitality Limited (Scrip Code: 539659, ISIN: INE564S01019) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Wednesday, May 27, 2026. These shares were issued to promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants, at an issue price of Rs. 74/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- at a premium of Rs. 64/-).

## Key Points

- 9,09,325 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 64/- (issue price Rs. 74/-) to promoters via preferential allotment
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 17289866 to 18199190
- Date of allotment: April 8, 2026
- Trading permitted from May 27, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification for newly allotted securities following warrant conversion by promoters.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities being available for trading. Lock-in restrictions apply to all allotted shares as detailed below.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** April 8, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** May 27, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (2,58,784 shares):** November 29, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (2,64,000 shares):** November 29, 2027
- **Lock-in Expiry (3,86,541 shares):** November 30, 2027

## Impact Assessment

This listing adds 9,09,325 shares to the tradeable float of Vikram Kamats Hospitality Limited, though all shares are subject to promoter lock-in until late 2026 or 2027, meaning no immediate increase in free-float liquidity. The preferential allotment at Rs. 74/- per share via warrant conversion signals promoter commitment. Market impact is minimal given the lock-in constraints and the relatively small size of the issuance.
