## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document (DRHP) of **Twinkle Papers Limited**, filed on February 23, 2026, on the BSE SME website. The document contains all disclosures as required under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations. The public is invited to submit comments within 21 days of the notice date.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Twinkle Papers Limited on February 23, 2026
- Document hosted on BSE SME website: www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Available for public viewing for a minimum period of 21 days
- Complies with Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations disclosure requirements
- Notice issued by Nitinkumar Pujari, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard procedural notice under SEBI's ICDR (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, requiring public availability of the Draft Red Herring Prospectus (DRHP) before an SME IPO.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public and market participants may review the Draft Offer Document on the BSE SME website
- Comments, if any, must be submitted within **21 days from February 24, 2026** (deadline: approximately March 17, 2026)
- Comments to be sent to: **equity.ipo@bseindia.com**

## Important Dates

- **Filing Date:** February 23, 2026
- **Notice Date:** February 24, 2026
- **Public Comment Deadline:** 21 days from February 24, 2026 (on or before March 17, 2026)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal immediate market impact as Twinkle Papers Limited is a pre-listing SME entity. The circular is informational in nature, inviting public comments on the draft prospectus. Impact is limited to prospective investors and analysts evaluating the upcoming SME IPO. No changes to trading, surveillance, or existing listed securities are involved.
