## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in non-convertible debentures of Shriram Finance Limited (ISIN: INE721A07RG1, Code: 974379) effective February 6, 2026. The suspension is implemented due to the company fixing February 8, 2026 as the record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Debenture ISIN: INE721A07RG1 (Code: 974379)
- Record date: February 8, 2026
- Trading suspension starts: February 6, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and interest payment
- Notice reference: DR-815/2025-2026
- Category: Corporate Action - Debt Segment

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action procedure for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in the specified debentures from February 6, 2026
- No buy or sell transactions permitted for ISIN INE721A07RG1 during the suspension period
- Trading members must update their systems to reflect the trading suspension

## Important Dates

- **January 30, 2026**: Circular issued
- **February 6, 2026**: Trading suspension begins (no dealings permitted)
- **February 8, 2026**: Record date for redemption and interest payment

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited impact as suspension affects only specific debenture series of Shriram Finance Limited. Standard procedure for debt securities approaching redemption.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders as of record date (February 8, 2026) will receive redemption proceeds and interest payment. Investors wanting to exit must do so before February 6, 2026.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to ensure compliance systems prevent trading in the suspended debentures. Routine operational adjustment with minimal disruption.
