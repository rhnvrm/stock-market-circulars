## Summary

BSE has issued a notice reminding Investment Advisers of their obligation under the Ease of Doing Investment (EoDI) framework to disclose their registered name and SEBI registration number on Social Media Platforms (SMPs). The notice specifically requires Investment Advisers to promptly update their social media details whenever any changes occur.

## Key Points

- References point 26 of Exchange master circular No. 20260430-47 dated April 30, 2026 on EoDI disclosures
- Investment Advisers must display their registered name and SEBI registration number on all Social Media Platforms (SMPs)
- Any changes to social media details must be updated promptly on the respective platforms
- Issued by the Membership Department (Manager: Neeky Subramaniam)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice reiterates existing obligations under the EoDI framework established in master circular No. 20260430-47 dated April 30, 2026.

## Compliance Requirements

- Investment Advisers must ensure their registered name and SEBI registration number are disclosed on all Social Media Platforms they operate on
- Any changes to social media presence or details must be updated promptly without delay
- Ensure ongoing compliance with point 26 of master circular No. 20260430-47

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: June 2, 2026
- Reference Master Circular: No. 20260430-47 dated April 30, 2026
- No specific deadline mentioned; compliance is expected on an ongoing basis

## Impact Assessment

Low operational impact. This is a compliance reminder targeted at SEBI-registered Investment Advisers. No new obligations are introduced; entities already compliant with the master circular need only ensure timely updates when their social media details change. No market-wide or trading impact anticipated.
