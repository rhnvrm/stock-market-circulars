## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of Paluck Technologies Limited on its SME website for public comments. The document was filed on January 20, 2026 and contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations. Stakeholders have 21 days from the notice date to submit comments.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Paluck Technologies Limited on January 20, 2026
- Document contains all disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website: www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public comment period of 21 days from January 27, 2026
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural notice for SME IPO draft offer document filing as per existing ICDR Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Paluck Technologies Limited must host the Draft Offer Document for minimum 21 days
- Interested parties may submit comments within 21 days from January 27, 2026
- Comments should be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Company must address public comments as per SEBI ICDR regulations before proceeding with IPO

## Important Dates

- **January 20, 2026**: Draft Offer Document filed
- **January 27, 2026**: Notice date and start of public comment period
- **February 17, 2026**: Last date for public comments (21 days from notice)
- Minimum hosting period: 21 days
