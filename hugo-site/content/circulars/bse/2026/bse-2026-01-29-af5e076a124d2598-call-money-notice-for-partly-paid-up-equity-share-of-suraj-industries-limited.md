## Summary

BSE has activated the Call Money Module on its IBBS platform for Suraj Industries Limited's first call on partly paid-up equity shares. Shareholders holding 2,99,25,394 partly paid-up equity shares must pay Rs. 10 per share during the issue period from February 2-16, 2026. Trading members, SCSBs, and intermediaries can submit call money details through the IBBS platform during bidding hours (10:00 AM to 5:00 PM). The registrar for this issue is Beetal Financial & Computer Services Private Limited.

## Key Points

- First call on partly paid-up equity shares of Suraj Industries Limited
- Call money amount: Rs. 10 per partly paid-up equity share
- Total shares affected: 2,99,25,394 partly paid-up equity shares
- Security symbol: SURAJCM with market lot of 1
- Issue period: 15 days (February 2-16, 2026)
- Bidding hours: 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM daily
- Submissions through BSE's IBBS (Internet Based Book Building Software) platform
- SCSBs and trading members with 3-in-1 accounts can submit call money details

## Regulatory Changes

This notice operates in accordance with:
- BSE Exchange Notice No. 20210104-37 dated January 4, 2021
- SEBI circular dated December 8, 2020 regarding Call Money Module availability

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members**: Must note the activation of Call Money Module and facilitate call money payment process for clients
- **Self-Certified Syndicate Banks (SCSBs)**: Required to submit details of call money notices collected during the issue period through IBBS
- **Intermediaries**: Must be aware of the call money process and assist investors as needed
- **Shareholders of Suraj Industries Limited**: Must pay Rs. 10 per partly paid-up equity share during the specified period
- Platform access: Only SCSBs and trading members with 3-in-1 type accounts can submit details on the Call Money Module

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 29, 2026
- **Issue Opening Date**: Monday, February 2, 2026
- **Issue Closing Date**: Monday, February 16, 2026
- **Issue Period**: 15 days
- **Daily Bidding Period**: 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM

## Impact Assessment

**Shareholder Impact**: Shareholders of Suraj Industries Limited holding partly paid-up equity shares must arrange to pay Rs. 10 per share, representing a financial obligation on approximately 3 crore shares. Failure to respond to the call may result in forfeiture of shares as per company law provisions.

**Market Operations**: Limited impact on broader market operations as this is a company-specific corporate action. Trading members and SCSBs need to ensure their IBBS platform access is functional for the 15-day window.
