## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the equity shares of Devson Catalyst Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange effective Thursday, July 16, 2026, under the SME segment's 'MT' group of securities. Per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012, the scrip will trade in the Trade-for-Trade segment for the first 10 trading days.

## Key Points

- Company: Devson Catalyst Limited, registered office in Wadhwancity, Surendranagar, Gujarat
- Number of securities: 1,35,88,000 equity shares of Rs.10/- each, fully paid up (distinctive number range 1 to 13588000)
- Scrip ID/Abbreviated Name on BOLT: DEVSON; Scrip Code: 544823; ISIN: INE2KRP01017
- Market lot: 1200 shares (minimum trading lot); securities tradeable only in dematerialised form
- Issue price: Rs. 118/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- plus premium of Rs. 108/-)
- Date of allotment in the public issue: July 14, 2026
- Financial year end: March 31; shares rank pari passu
- Lock-in and shareholding pattern details provided in Annexure I and Annexure II respectively
- Market Maker: MNM Stock Broking Private Limited
- Registrar to the issue: MUFG Intime India Private Limited (formerly Link Intime India Private Limited)
- Scrip will be part of Special Pre-open Session (SPOS) on July 16, 2026, per BSE notice no. 20120216-29

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an operational listing notice implementing existing SEBI/BSE frameworks for SME IPO listings (SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 and SPOS notice 20120216-29).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure trades in this scrip are executed only in dematerialised form and in minimum market lots of 1200 shares.
- The company has completed corporate action with the depositories to mark the lock-in period on shares as per Annexure I.
- Trading members should refer to BSE's SPOS notice for participation requirements during the special pre-open session on the listing day.

## Important Dates

- July 14, 2026: Date of allotment in the public issue
- July 16, 2026 (Thursday): Effective date of listing and admission to dealings; also date of Special Pre-open Session (SPOS)
- 10 trading days from July 16, 2026: Trade-for-Trade segment applicability period

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine SME listing notice with limited broader market impact, relevant primarily to investors and market participants dealing in Devson Catalyst Limited shares. The Trade-for-Trade restriction for the initial 10 trading days is a standard safeguard for newly listed SME scrips, limiting speculative trading and requiring actual delivery for all trades during this period.
