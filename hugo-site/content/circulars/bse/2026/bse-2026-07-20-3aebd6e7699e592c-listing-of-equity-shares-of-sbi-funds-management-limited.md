## Summary

BSE has informed Trading Members that effective Tuesday, July 21, 2026, the equity shares of SBI Funds Management Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange under the 'A' Group of Securities. This follows the company's public issue, with allotment completed on July 18, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulation is introduced; this is an implementation notice under existing SEBI listing and dematerialisation guidelines. As per SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000, the securities will trade only in dematerialised form. Corporate action has been executed by the company to mark the lock-in period in depository records for shares held in demat form.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure the scrip is enabled for trading in dematerialised form only.
- The scrip will be part of the Special Pre-open Session (SPOS) on July 21, 2026, per Exchange notice no. 20120216-29 dated February 16, 2012, governing SPOS for IPOs and relisted scrips.
- Members must comply with lock-in restrictions as detailed in Annexure I and reference the shareholding pattern in Annexure II.

## Important Dates

- July 18, 2026: Date of allotment in the public issue
- July 20, 2026: Notice date
- July 21, 2026: Listing and commencement of dealings on BSE, including Special Pre-open Session (SPOS)

## Impact Assessment

This listing marks the market debut of SBI Funds Management Limited, a major asset management company, and enables secondary market trading of its shares from July 21, 2026. Trading members and investors gain access to a new large-cap 'A' Group scrip. The Special Pre-open Session mechanism will help in price discovery on the listing day, reducing volatility risk. Given the scale of the issue (over 2 billion shares) and the issue price of Rs. 574, this is a significant listing event for the AMC/mutual fund sector on the exchange.
