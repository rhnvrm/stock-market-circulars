## Summary

BSE has informed members that MIRAE ASSET Mutual Fund will launch the MIRAE ASSET BSE MIDCAP 150 MOMENTUM 30 ETF as a New Fund Offer (NFO) on the BSE StAR MF Platform, open from July 06, 2026 to July 13, 2026 (extended till midnight for lumpsum purchases on the BSE StAR MF Platform).

## Key Points

- Scheme: MIRAE ASSET BSE MIDCAP 150 MOMENTUM 30 ETF (Equity category) — tentative allotment date July 16, 2026
- Scheme: MIRAE ASSET BSE MIDCAP 150 MOMENTUM 30 ETF - DIRECT PLAN (Equity category) — tentative allotment date July 16, 2026
- Transactions where clients do not initiate funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day (including during the extended NFO period) will be cancelled and reported to RTA/AMC with an 'N' flag
- Transactions with funds initiated remain pending until 3:00 PM on the last day of funds pay-out to AMC, or T+5 day, whichever is earlier
- Cheque and OTM payments accepted during NFO period, subject to clear funds in the ICCL account by 6:00 PM on July 15, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine operational/product launch notice, not a regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure client funds are initiated by 9:00 AM on T+1 day to avoid cancellation
- Clear funds must be available in the ICCL account by 6:00 PM on July 15, 2026 for allotment eligibility
- Cheque/OTM payments must be processed within the specified fund clearance timeline

## Important Dates

- NFO Period: July 06, 2026 to July 13, 2026 (extended till midnight on July 13, 2026 for lumpsum purchases)
- Fund clearance deadline: 6:00 PM, July 15, 2026
- Tentative allotment date: July 16, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice for mutual fund distributors and members using the BSE StAR MF Platform. It has no direct impact on listed equity trading, stock prices, or broader market operations. Relevance is limited to mutual fund distributors, AMCs, and investors participating in this specific NFO.
