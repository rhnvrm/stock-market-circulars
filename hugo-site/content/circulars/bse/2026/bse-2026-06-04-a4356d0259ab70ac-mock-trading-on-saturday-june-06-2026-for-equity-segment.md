## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Equity segment on Saturday, June 06, 2026. Trading members may use this session to test their trading applications and fulfill regulatory requirements as mandated under SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD1/DSAP/CIR/P/2020/234 dated November 24, 2020.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Equity segment on Saturday, June 06, 2026
- Members may participate in the Mock Trading Session or UAT environment to meet regulatory testing requirements
- Third-party trading platform users can test functionalities including call auction sessions, risk-reduction mode, trading halt, and block deals
- No new BOLT TWS Release for this session
- Feedback from all participants required by 4:30 pm on the day of the session

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This session is conducted in compliance with the existing SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD1/DSAP/CIR/P/2020/234 dated November 24, 2020, and BSE Notice No. 20201201-22 dated December 1, 2020, regarding testing of software used in or related to Trading and Risk Management.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must participate either in the Mock Trading Session or the UAT environment to fulfill regulatory software testing requirements
- Feedback must be submitted to BSE by 4:30 pm on June 06, 2026
- Contact: BSE Tech Support at 022-22728053 for TWS users, third-party empaneled vendors, and in-house system developers
- Members may reach out to their Relationship Manager or BSE Helpdesk for queries

## Important Dates

- **June 06, 2026 (Saturday):** Mock Trading Session for Equity segment
- **June 06, 2026, by 4:30 pm:** Deadline for submitting feedback

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational event with no impact on regular market participants or listed securities. Only trading members and their technology teams are directly affected. Participation is voluntary but fulfills a regulatory testing obligation. No changes to normal trading operations are involved.
