## Summary

BSE has published the demat auction list for Settlement No. 788 dated January 1, 2026. This auction includes securities from 118 scrips where delivery shortages have occurred. The auction allows buyers who did not receive their securities to purchase them in the market to fulfill delivery obligations.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 788
- Date: January 1, 2026
- Total number of scrips: 118
- Quantities range from 1 share to 65,575 shares depending on the scrip
- Highest quantity: Rajnish Wellness Limited (65,575 shares)
- Notable large quantities include: Rama Steel Tubes (30,000), Sattva Sukun Infra (10,000), R&B Denims (7,501), Jupiter Infomedia (7,123)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard demat auction notice as part of BSE's clearing and settlement mechanism.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members with shortages must participate in the auction or arrange for securities delivery
- Buyers entitled to shares can bid in the auction to complete their purchases
- Settlement must be completed as per BSE auction settlement procedures

## Important Dates

- Auction Date: January 1, 2026
- Settlement No: 788
- Specific auction timings and settlement schedule to be followed as per BSE norms

## Impact Assessment

This demat auction affects 118 different securities across various sectors including banking, power, manufacturing, automobiles, pharmaceuticals, and technology. The auction mechanism ensures delivery completion and protects buyer interests when sellers fail to deliver shares. Impact is operational and limited to specific trades where delivery shortages occurred. Investors holding or trading these securities should be aware of potential price volatility during auction periods.
