## Summary

BSE has informed members that ZERODHA Mutual Fund has revised the minimum application amount for Systematic Investment Plans (SIP) under the Daily Frequency option for two of its schemes. The change lowers the minimum SIP amount from Rs. 100 to Rs. 10 (in multiples of Re. 1), effective immediately, and has been implemented on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Key Points

- Minimum SIP amount for Daily Frequency reduced from Rs. 100 to Rs. 10 for two Zerodha MF schemes.
- Both schemes now require Rs. 10 and multiples of Re. 1/- thereafter (previously Rs. 100 and multiples of Re. 1/-).
- Change is effective immediately upon issuance of this notice.
- The revision has already been implemented on the BSE StAR MF Platform.
- Based on a Notice-cum-Addendum issued by Zerodha AMC.

## Regulatory Changes

The minimum application amount for SIP (Daily Frequency) has been revised downward for the following schemes:

| Scheme Name | Previous Minimum | Revised Minimum |
|---|---|---|
| ZERODHA MULTI ASSET PASSIVE FOF | Rs. 100 and multiples of Re. 1/- | Rs. 10 and multiples of Re. 1/- |
| ZERODHA GOLD ETF FOF | Rs. 100 and multiples of Re. 1/- | Rs. 10 and multiples of Re. 1/- |

## Compliance Requirements

- Members/distributors transacting on BSE StAR MF Platform should note the revised minimum SIP amount for these two Zerodha schemes.
- No further action is required as the change has already been implemented on the platform.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** 26 May 2026 (immediate effect)

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact operational change that makes SIP investing more accessible by lowering the entry threshold for Daily SIPs in two Zerodha Mutual Fund schemes. It benefits small retail investors who wish to invest smaller amounts on a daily basis. There is no impact on existing SIP mandates or other fund operations.
