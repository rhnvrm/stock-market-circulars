## Summary

Indian Clearing Corporation Limited (ICCL) has published the client margin reporting schedule for the Commodity Derivatives Segment for March 2026. The schedule specifies the last date for margin reporting and the penalty date for each trade date from 02-03-2026 through 31-03-2026.

## Key Points

- Margin reporting window is typically 7–8 calendar days after the trade date.
- Penalty dates follow one day after the last date of margin reporting.
- Weekend and holiday gaps are accounted for in the schedule (e.g., trade date 06-03-2026 and 13-03-2026 share the same penalty date of 13-03-2026).
- The schedule extends into early April 2026 for trades executed in the last week of March 2026 (up to 09-04-2026).
- Circular issued under the Settlement/RMS category and applies to all members of the Commodity Derivatives Segment.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is a routine periodic schedule circular issued by ICCL for operational compliance.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members trading in the Commodity Derivatives Segment must submit client margin reports by the specified "Last Date of Margin Reporting" for each trade date.
- Failure to report by the deadline will result in penalties applied on the indicated "Penalty Date".
- Members should update their internal reporting workflows to align with this schedule for the entire month of March 2026.
