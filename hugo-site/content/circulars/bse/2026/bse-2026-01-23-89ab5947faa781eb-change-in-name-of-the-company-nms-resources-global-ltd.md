## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to a change of name for the company identified by CIN L74110DL1986PLC025457. The company's name has been officially changed from "NMS RESOURCES GLOBAL LIMITED" to "NMS GLOBAL LIMITED" with effect from October 31, 2023. The company remains a company limited by shares and was originally incorporated as "IFM IMPEX GLOBAL LIMITED".

## Key Points

- Official name change from NMS Resources Global Limited to NMS Global Limited
- Certificate issued by Registrar of Companies, ROC Delhi
- Corporate Identification Number (CIN): L74110DL1986PLC025457
- Company type: Company limited by shares
- Original incorporation name: IFM Impex Global Limited
- Certificate digitally signed by Banwari Sharma, Assistant/Deputy/Registrar of Companies
- Registered office location: H.No. 48, Hasanpur, I.P. Extension, East Delhi - 110092, Delhi, India

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a routine corporate name change processed under the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014, Rule 29.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display the old name (NMS Resources Global Limited) along with its new name (NMS Global Limited) for a period of two years at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- All company documents, letterheads, and official communications must be updated to reflect the new name
- Stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company on the Ministry of Corporate Affairs website (www.mca.gov.in/MCA21)

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date of Name Change:** October 31, 2023
- **Display Requirement Period:** Two years from October 31, 2023 (until October 30, 2025)
- **Certificate Issue Date:** October 31, 2023 at 13:20:36 IST

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This is an administrative change that does not affect the company's operations, financial position, or business activities.

**Shareholder Impact:** No impact on rights and liabilities of stakeholders. The name change does not affect ownership, shareholding patterns, or any existing contractual obligations.

**Operational Impact:** Limited to administrative updates required for displaying the dual names during the two-year transition period and updating official documentation and communications.

**Trading Impact:** Stock ticker and trading symbol may need to be updated on exchanges. Investors should be aware of the name change when searching for the company in trading systems.
