## Summary

BSE has announced the addition of Elfin Agro India Limited (Exchange ticker: 544724) to the BSE SME IPO Index. The stock was listed on BSE on March 12, 2026, and will be included in the index effective at the open of March 13, 2026.

## Key Points

- Elfin Agro India Limited (ticker: 544724) listed on BSE on March 12, 2026
- Stock will be added to the BSE SME IPO Index effective at market open on March 13, 2026
- This action is referenced from Notice No. 20260311-44
- The corporate action is included in the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE) dated March 12, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index rebalancing action following the listing of a new SME IPO on BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

No direct compliance requirements for market participants. Index-tracking funds and ETFs benchmarked to the BSE SME IPO Index should update their portfolios to reflect the addition of 544724 effective March 13, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **March 12, 2026**: Elfin Agro India Limited listed on BSE; corporate action file published
- **March 13, 2026**: Effective date for addition of 544724 to the BSE SME IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to the BSE SME IPO Index and any funds or products tracking it. Investors and fund managers with exposure to the BSE SME IPO Index should account for the inclusion of Elfin Agro India Limited in their portfolios from March 13, 2026.
