## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (No. 20260311-72) informing trading members that Pahal Financial Services Private Limited has fixed a record date of March 13, 2026, for redemption and interest payment on its Non-Convertible Debentures. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures effective March 13, 2026.

## Key Points

- Trading in NCDs of Pahal Financial Services Private Limited is suspended effective March 13, 2026
- The record date for redemption and interest payment is March 13, 2026
- Debenture series affected: PFSPL-14%-31-3-26-PVT (ISIN: INE514Q07338, Code: 975517)
- The debentures carry a 14% coupon rate with maturity on March 31, 2026
- Circular reference: DR-839/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice issued under existing BSE debt market rules requiring suspension of trading in debentures around the record date for corporate actions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the debentures of Pahal Financial Services Private Limited (ISIN: INE514Q07338, Scrip Code: 975517) from March 13, 2026 onwards
- Members are required to take note of the no-dealings date and ensure compliance

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** March 11, 2026
- **Record Date:** March 13, 2026
- **No Dealings From:** March 13, 2026
- **Debenture Maturity Date:** March 31, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and traders of this specific NCD series of Pahal Financial Services Private Limited. The suspension is a routine pre-redemption measure to ensure clean settlement of the final interest and principal repayment. Given the imminent maturity date of March 31, 2026, the trading suspension is expected and affects a narrow set of market participants in the debt segment.
