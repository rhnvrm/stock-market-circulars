## Summary

Accord Transformer and Switchgear Limited has filed its shareholding pattern with BSE under Regulation 31(1)(b) for the quarter ending February 26, 2026, in connection with the listing of its equity shares. The company has a total of 20,573,289 equity shares held by 530 shareholders.

## Key Points

- Total issued equity shares: 20,573,289
- Promoter & Promoter Group: 12,750,000 shares (61.97%), held by 2 shareholders
- Public shareholding: 7,823,289 shares (38.03%), held by 528 shareholders
- Shares held in dematerialised form: 16,595,289 (80.66% of total)
- No partly paid-up shares issued
- No convertible securities or warrants outstanding
- No depository receipts issued against shares
- Promoter shares are locked-in; no pledge or encumbrance on promoter shares
- Public category demat shares: 3,845,289 (49.15% of public holding)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine post-listing disclosure with no immediate market impact.
