## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that NEXOME CAPITAL MARKETS LIMITED (Scrip Code: 508905) has fixed 05 March 2026 as the record date for its rights issue. Equity shares of the company will trade on an ex-rights basis effective 05 March 2026 under Rolling Settlement DR-833/2025-2026.

## Key Points

- **Company:** NEXOME CAPITAL MARKETS LIMITED (BSE Code: 508905)
- **Rights Ratio:** 1 new equity share for every 2 existing equity shares held
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 75/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- + premium Rs. 65/-)
- **Record Date:** 05 March 2026
- **Ex-Rights Date:** 05 March 2026
- **Settlement Number:** DR-833/2025-2026
- **Segment:** Dematerialised Securities â€“ Rolling Settlement Segment

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine corporate action notification under BSE's listing compliance framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all transactions in NEXOME CAPITAL MARKETS LIMITED equity shares are executed on an **ex-rights basis** with effect from 05 March 2026.
- Members should update their systems to reflect the ex-rights adjustment from the specified record date and settlement number.

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a **medium** impact on existing shareholders of NEXOME CAPITAL MARKETS LIMITED. Shareholders holding shares as of the record date (05 March 2026) will be entitled to subscribe to 1 new share for every 2 shares held at Rs. 75/- per share. The ex-rights adjustment may result in a dilution-adjusted price correction in the scrip from the record date. Broader market impact is minimal given the company-specific nature of the action.
