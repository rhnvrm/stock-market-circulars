## Summary

The Indian Clearing Corporation Limited (ICCL) has issued the trading and settlement programme for the Basket Repo and Special Repo segment on BSE for the period from 01 July 2026 to 31 July 2026. Participants in this segment should refer to the attached schedule (REPO072026) for applicable trading and settlement dates.

## Key Points

- Issued by ICCL under the Settlement/RMS category for the General segment.
- Covers the full month of July 2026 (01.07.2026 to 31.07.2026).
- Attachment reference: REPO072026 contains the detailed schedule.
- Applicable to all participants in the Basket Repo and Special Repo segment on BSE.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced. This is a routine periodic notification of the settlement programme.

## Compliance Requirements

All participants in the Basket Repo and Special Repo segment must adhere to the trading and settlement dates specified in the attached programme (REPO072026) for July 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Programme Period:** 01 July 2026 to 31 July 2026
- **Notice Date:** 05 June 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard monthly operational notice enabling participants to plan their repo trading and settlement activities for July 2026. No changes to existing rules, margin requirements, or eligibility criteria are indicated.
