## Summary

BSE's Surveillance Department (DOSS) has sought clarification from GE Vernova T&D India Ltd (BSE Scrip Code: 522275) in response to news articles appearing across newspapers, websites, and television channels. The Exchange initiated this process to verify the accuracy of the reported news and to keep members and investors informed. As of this notice, the company's reply is still awaited.

## Key Points

- BSE's Surveillance Department issued Notice No. 20260505-23 dated 05 May 2026
- Clarification sought from GE Vernova T&D India Ltd (Scrip Code: 522275)
- The news article in question appeared on the Economic Times website
- The company's reply is awaited as of the notice date
- No attachment was included with this notice

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this notice. This is a standard surveillance inquiry under BSE's ongoing market monitoring process.

## Compliance Requirements

GE Vernova T&D India Ltd is required to respond to BSE's clarification request regarding the news article published on Economic Times. The Exchange expects a timely response to maintain transparency for investors and market participants.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 05 May 2026
- **Response Status:** Reply awaited (no deadline specified in the notice)

## Impact Assessment

The immediate market impact is low as this is a routine surveillance inquiry with no regulatory action taken yet. The notice signals that BSE is actively monitoring news related to GE Vernova T&D India Ltd (Scrip Code: 522275). Investors should watch for a follow-up notice once the company's clarification is received, which may provide further clarity on the news article in question. No trading restrictions or compliance actions have been imposed at this stage.
