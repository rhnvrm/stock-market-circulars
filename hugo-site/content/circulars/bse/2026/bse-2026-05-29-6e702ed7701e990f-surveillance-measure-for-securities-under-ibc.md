## Summary

BSE has issued an update to its Additional Surveillance Measure (ASM) framework for companies under the Insolvency Resolution Process (IRP) as per the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code (IBC). Effective June 1, 2026, certain securities have been added to and removed from the IBC surveillance framework. This notice references prior circulars from June 2018, May 2022, February 2023, and September 2025 establishing the framework.

## Key Points

- Securities listed in Annexure I have met the criteria for the IBC Additional Surveillance Measure framework, effective June 1, 2026.
- Securities listed in Annexure II are moving out of the IBC framework, also effective June 1, 2026.
- Annexure III provides the consolidated list of all securities currently under the IBC framework.
- Securities placed under this framework are subject to periodic review.
- The IBC framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the exchanges.
- Shortlisting under this framework is purely for market surveillance purposes and should not be construed as an adverse action against the concerned company.

## Regulatory Changes

The IBC surveillance framework is updated with new entrants (Annexure I) and exits (Annexure II) effective June 1, 2026. This is a continuation of the framework established under Exchange Notice No. 20180608-34 and subsequently updated via notices in 2022, 2023, and 2025.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the revised list of securities under the IBC framework from June 1, 2026.
- Members should refer to Annexures I, II, and III attached to the circular for the specific securities affected.
- For clarifications, members may contact BSE Surveillance at bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **June 1, 2026**: Effective date for additions and removals from the IBC surveillance framework.

## Impact Assessment

Securities moved into the IBC framework face additional surveillance restrictions, which may include trade-for-trade settlement and revised price bands. Securities moving out of the framework (Annexure II) will have their price bands reinstated to the levels applicable before shortlisting, provided the scrip is not part of any other surveillance framework. Market participants trading in IBC-listed scrips should review the consolidated Annexure III to ensure compliance with applicable restrictions.
