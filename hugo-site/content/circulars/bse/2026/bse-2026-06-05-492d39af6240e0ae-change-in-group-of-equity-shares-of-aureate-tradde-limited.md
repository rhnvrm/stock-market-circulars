## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that equity shares of Aureate Tradde Limited (SME IPO, Scrip Code: 544772) will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment (M Group), effective Friday, June 19, 2026. This notice is a continuation of BSE Notice No. 20260604-33 dated June 04, 2026.

## Key Points

- Aureate Tradde Limited (Scrip Code: 544772) currently trades in the Trade for Trade segment under MT Group
- Shares will migrate to the Rolling segment under M Group from June 19, 2026
- This is an SME segment stock that underwent an SME IPO
- Contact person: Mr. Anurag Jain, Tel. No. 022-2272 8822

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares of Aureate Tradde Limited will shift trading mechanism from Trade for Trade (MT Group) to the Rolling settlement (M Group). Under Trade for Trade, each trade must be settled individually and netting is not permitted. The move to Rolling segment allows normal market trading with netting of positions.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should update their systems and trading parameters to reflect the group change for Scrip Code 544772 effective June 19, 2026. Members with queries may contact Mr. Anurag Jain at 022-2272 8822.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 05, 2026
- **Effective Date:** Friday, June 19, 2026 (transfer from MT Group to M Group)
- **Previous Notice:** BSE Notice No. 20260604-33 dated June 04, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine post-IPO operational change. The transition from Trade for Trade (MT Group) to Rolling segment (M Group) typically occurs after the mandatory lock-in period following an SME IPO, allowing greater trading flexibility and liquidity for the stock. Impact is limited to trading members dealing in this specific SME scrip.
