## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (No. 20260304-19) informing trading members of the reduced face value applicable to the debentures of Edelweiss Rural & Corporate Services Ltd following a part redemption. This is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20251209-54. The debentures will trade at the updated reduced face value effective March 05, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: Edelweiss Rural & Corporate Services Ltd
- Scrip: EAVCL-9.90%-30-6-27-PVT
- ISIN: INE657N07613 (Scrip Code: 956710)
- Purpose: Part Redemption of Debentures
- Reduced Face Value: Rs. 9,16,650/- per Debenture
- Reference: DR-833/2025-2026
- Continuation of earlier notice No. 20251209-54

## Regulatory Changes

The face value of the debenture series EAVCL-9.90%-30-6-27-PVT (ISIN: INE657N07613) has been reduced to Rs. 9,16,650/- per debenture following a partial redemption by the issuer. Trading members must update their systems to reflect this revised face value.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are required to note the revised reduced face value of Rs. 9,16,650/- per debenture.
- All trades in this debenture series must reflect the updated face value from the effective date.
- Members should ensure their back-office and trading systems are updated accordingly.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: March 04, 2026
- Effective Date for trading at reduced face value: **March 05, 2026**

## Impact Assessment

This part redemption reduces the outstanding principal per debenture from the prior face value to Rs. 9,16,650/-. Existing holders of this privately placed debenture series will see their per-unit exposure reduced accordingly. The change affects settlement values and accrued interest calculations for this instrument. Broader market impact is limited given the privately placed nature of this debt instrument.
