## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that new privately placed debt securities issued by REC Limited have been listed and admitted to trading on the BSE Debt Segment effective July 20, 2026. The listing covers a fresh issuance of secured, rated non-convertible debentures under ISIN INE020B08GG7.

## Key Points

- Issuer: REC Limited
- Scrip Code: 977977; Scrip ID: 730RL31; Detail Name: RECL-7.30%-01-07-31-PVT
- ISIN: INE020B08GG7
- Quantity: 291,900 securities; Market Lot: 1
- Face Value, Paid-up Value and Issue Price: Rs. 100,000 each
- Credit Ratings: CRISIL AAA/Stable, ICRA AAA/Stable, IND AAA/Stable, CARE AAA/Stable
- Securities will trade only in dematerialised form
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing admission notice under BSE's DCS-Listing department for privately placed debt instruments, not a regulatory or policy change.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must note the security is tradable only in demat form under the specified ISIN. Members requiring clarification may contact BSE's debt department (contact numbers: 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915). Further issue details are available in the Placement Memorandum on BSE's debt market website.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: 17/07/2026
- Listing/Trading Effective Date: 20/07/2026
- Interest Payment: Annually, from 01/07/2027 to 01/07/2031
- Date of Redemption: 01/07/2031

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, low-impact administrative listing notice enabling secondary market trading of REC Limited's privately placed NCDs on the BSE Debt Segment. It carries a 7.30% annual coupon and AAA ratings across major agencies, indicating strong credit quality. The notice has no direct effect on REC Limited's equity shares or broader market trading and is primarily relevant to debt market participants and existing bondholders.
