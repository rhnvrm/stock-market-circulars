## Summary

BSE has announced shut periods for Securities Lending and Borrowing (SLB) operations for six companies undertaking dividend-related corporate actions. The shut periods will restrict SLB transactions during book closure/record date periods ranging from January 14-30, 2026. The affected companies include major large-cap stocks: DCM Shriram Limited, Havells India Limited, Siemens Energy India Limited, HCL Technologies Ltd, United Spirits Limited, and Tata Consultancy Services Ltd.

## Key Points

- Six companies have declared dividends with corresponding SLB shut periods
- Shut periods range from 2 to 5 days per company
- Ex-dividend dates fall between January 16-30, 2026
- SLB participants cannot initiate new transactions during shut periods for these securities
- Both TCS and HCL Technologies share the same shut period (January 14-17, 2026)
- Havells and Siemens Energy have shut periods of only 2 days each

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular implements existing SLB framework regulations regarding corporate action shut periods.

## Compliance Requirements

- SLB market participants must not execute new lending or borrowing transactions in the specified securities during their respective shut periods
- Existing SLB positions may be affected by the book closure periods
- Trading members must ensure their systems reflect these restrictions during the shut periods
- Participants should plan their SLB strategies accounting for these restrictions

## Important Dates

**HCL Technologies Ltd & Tata Consultancy Services Ltd:**
- Ex Date: January 16, 2026
- Record Date: January 17, 2026
- Shut Period: January 14-17, 2026

**Havells India Limited:**
- Ex Date: January 23, 2026
- Record Date: January 23, 2026
- Shut Period: January 22-23, 2026

**DCM Shriram Limited:**
- Ex Date: January 23, 2026
- Record Date: January 24, 2026
- Shut Period: January 22-24, 2026

**United Spirits Limited:**
- Ex Date: January 27, 2026
- Record Date: January 27, 2026
- Shut Period: January 23-27, 2026

**Siemens Energy India Limited:**
- Ex Date: January 30, 2026
- Record Date: January 30, 2026
- Shut Period: January 29-30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Moderate impact on SLB market liquidity for these six securities during their respective shut periods. The affected stocks are high-value, liquid blue-chip companies commonly used in lending and borrowing operations.

**Operational Impact:** SLB participants must adjust their lending/borrowing strategies and inventory management around these dates. The staggered shut periods across January 14-30 may require careful planning for portfolios containing multiple affected securities.

**Trading Impact:** No impact on regular equity trading; restrictions apply only to SLB segment. Investors can continue buying/selling these stocks normally in the cash market, subject to standard ex-dividend date rules.
