## Summary

BSE has issued a notice referencing SEBI Circular No. HO/(79)2026-MIRSD-PODMMC dated February 26, 2026, and earlier Exchange notices dated April 28, 2026, and June 02, 2026, requiring all registered Research Analysts to submit details of their websites, mobile applications, and social media handles used for research activities. The information will be published on the Exchange's website and used for whitelisting with Meta and Google. Submissions are due by July 28, 2026.

## Key Points

- All registered Research Analysts must report digital platform details (websites, mobile apps, social media handles) via a designated online form.
- Submitted information will be displayed on the BSE website for investors and stakeholders.
- Reported handles/platforms will be considered for whitelisting with Meta and Google.
- Unreported social media handles related to research services may not be whitelisted and could be liable for deletion/removal under applicable regulations.
- All fields in the form are mandatory; "NA" must be entered where not applicable.
- Multiple handles/URLs for the same platform must be listed comma-separated in the respective fields.
- Links must point to the main landing page/profile, not individual posts, reels, or stories.
- Research Analysts must verify and cross-check all links before submission and ensure details are complete, accurate, and current as of the submission date.
- Queries can be directed to BSE Membership department at 022 22725854 / 5680.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory change; this is an operational reminder/notice implementing SEBI Circular No. HO/(79)2026-MIRSD-PODMMC dated February 26, 2026, and prior BSE notices (20260428-47 and 20260602-34) regarding disclosure of digital platforms used by Research Analysts.

## Compliance Requirements

Registered Research Analysts must submit complete and accurate details of all websites, mobile applications, and social media handles used for research services through the specified BSE form, ensuring all fields are filled (using "NA" where not applicable) and that landing-page links are correctly provided.

## Important Dates

- Submission deadline: July 28, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This notice has no direct market or stock-price impact but is operationally significant for Research Analysts, who face the risk of their unreported social media handles or digital platforms being deemed non-compliant, potentially leading to removal or de-whitelisting from Meta and Google. Timely compliance is necessary to maintain uninterrupted investor-facing digital presence.
