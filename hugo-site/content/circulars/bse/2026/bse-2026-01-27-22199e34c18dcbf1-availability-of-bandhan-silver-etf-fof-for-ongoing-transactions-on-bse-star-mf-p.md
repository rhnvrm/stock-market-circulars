## Summary

BSE has notified that BANDHAN SILVER ETF FOF (Fund of Funds) will reopen for ongoing transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective January 28, 2026. Both Regular and Direct plan variants with Growth option will be available for subscription and redemption, along with systematic investment facilities (SIP, STP, and SWP).

## Key Points

- BANDHAN SILVER ETF FOF reopening for ongoing transactions after NFO period
- Two plan variants available: Regular Plan - Growth (INF194KB1KM1) and Direct Plan - Growth (INF194KB1KN9)
- All systematic facilities enabled: SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan)
- Trading available through BSE StAR MF Platform only
- Scheme type classified as Equity category
- Notice references previous circular No 20260109-42 regarding initial launch

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notice regarding mutual fund platform availability.

## Compliance Requirements

BSE members and intermediaries should update their systems to enable transactions for BANDHAN SILVER ETF FOF on the BSE StAR MF Platform from the specified date.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** January 27, 2026
- **Effective Date:** January 28, 2026 - Ongoing subscription and redemption begins

## Impact Assessment

Limited operational impact. This notice affects only investors and distributors dealing with BANDHAN SILVER ETF FOF on BSE's mutual fund platform. The availability of systematic investment facilities provides convenience for retail investors seeking silver exposure through the fund of funds structure. No impact on equity markets or broader market operations.
