## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Commercial Paper issued by Solar Industries India Limited (Scrip Code: 730539, ISIN: INE343H14204) effective February 06, 2026, due to the instrument reaching its redemption date. This is a standard procedure for maturing commercial paper.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Solar Industries India Limited to mature on redemption date
- Scrip Code: 730539
- ISIN: INE343H14204
- Trading suspension effective from February 06, 2026
- Notice reference: DR-815/2025-2026
- Issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President â€“ Listing Compliance and Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice for commercial paper maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must not deal in the aforementioned Commercial Paper with effect from February 06, 2026
- Trading Members are advised to take note of this suspension and ensure compliance

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: February 05, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date**: February 06, 2026
- **Redemption Date**: On or around February 06, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative action with minimal market impact. The suspension affects only a single commercial paper instrument issued by Solar Industries India Limited. Trading members dealing in this specific debt instrument need to cease trading from February 06, 2026. This does not affect the company's equity shares or other debt instruments. The impact is limited to holders and traders of this particular commercial paper as it approaches maturity and redemption.
