## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that certain securities (listed in the attached Annexure) will be moved to higher stages of the Graded Surveillance Measure (GSM) framework effective June 11, 2026. The affected securities will be subject to enhanced surveillance actions depending on their assigned GSM stage, ranging from increased margin requirements to restricted weekly trading with Additional Surveillance Deposit (ASD) obligations.

## Key Points

- Securities listed in the Annexure will move to higher GSM stages from June 11, 2026
- Four GSM stages with progressively stricter restrictions are in effect
- Stage I: 100% applicable margin rate and 5% (or lower) price band
- Stage II: Trade-for-trade with 5% price band and 50% ASD on trade value (buyers)
- Stage III: Trade-for-trade, weekly trading (Mondays only), and 100% ASD on trade value (buyers)
- Stage IV: Same as Stage III with an additional restriction of no upward price movement
- No netting off permitted; all settlements on trade-to-trade basis
- Trading members advised to exercise adequate precautions when dealing in these securities

## Regulatory Changes

This notice is issued with reference to the GSM framework originally published on February 23, 2017 (Notice No. 20170223-44) and subsequently updated via notices dated March 3, 2017, July 20, 2018, November 29, 2019, and November 17, 2023. The current circular implements the movement of specific securities to higher GSM stages as a periodic review action under that framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note and implement the prescribed surveillance actions for all securities listed in the Annexure from June 11, 2026
- Buyers in Stage II securities must deposit ASD of 50% of trade value
- Buyers in Stage III and Stage IV securities must deposit ASD of 100% of trade value
- No netting off of positions is permitted; settlement is strictly trade-to-trade
- Members should refer to the GSM FAQ at the BSE website or contact bse.surv@bseindia.com for clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 10, 2026
- **Effective Date:** June 11, 2026 (securities move to higher GSM stages)
- Weekly trading for Stage III and IV securities: Every Monday or the 1st trading day of the week

## Impact Assessment

The movement of securities into higher GSM stages significantly restricts trading activity and increases the cost of participation for buyers through mandatory ASD requirements. Stage III and Stage IV restrictions, which limit trading to once a week and require 100% ASD, effectively reduce liquidity in the affected securities. Stage IV's prohibition on upward price movement further constrains price discovery. Investors and traders holding or planning to trade these securities should review the Annexure and reassess their positions given the heightened settlement obligations and restricted trading windows.
