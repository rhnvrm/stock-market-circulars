## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Bhakti Gems and Jewellery Ltd has changed its corporate name. The company's SCRIP ID and abbreviated name on the BOLT Plus System will be updated to reflect the new name on the effective date specified in the circular.

## Key Points

- Company: Bhakti Gems and Jewellery Ltd
- Type: Corporate name change notification
- The scrip code remains unchanged
- SCRIP ID and abbreviated name on BOLT Plus System will be updated to reflect the new name
- Trading members should update their records accordingly

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a corporate name change communicated by the company to the Exchange. BSE is updating the SCRIP ID and abbreviated name on its BOLT Plus trading system to reflect the new name.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should update their records and systems to reflect the new company name and updated SCRIP ID/abbreviated name on the BOLT Plus System effective from the notified date.

## Important Dates

- **March 09, 2026**: Notice issued by BSE
- Effective date for name change on BOLT Plus System: as specified in the circular PDF

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The name change is purely administrative. The scrip code remains unchanged, so existing orders, holdings, and positions are unaffected. Trading members need to note the updated SCRIP ID for BOLT Plus System transactions from the effective date onwards.
