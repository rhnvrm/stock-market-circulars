## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Deepak Builders and Engineers India Ltd (Scrip Code: 544276) has fixed June 19, 2026 as the record date for sub-division of its equity shares. Each existing equity share of Rs. 10/- face value will be split into 10 equity shares of Re. 1/- face value each, effective the same date.

## Key Points

- Sub-division ratio: 1 share of Rs. 10/- → 10 shares of Re. 1/- each
- Record date: June 19, 2026
- Effective date for new face value: June 19, 2026
- Internal reference: DR-655/2026-2027
- Old ISIN INE0OPA01019 (Rs. 10/- paid up) will be invalid for exchange transactions on or after June 19, 2026
- New ISIN for Re. 1/- paid up shares will be communicated via a separate notice

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework introduced. This is a standard corporate action notification under BSE Listing Operations, Category: Corporate Actions, Segment: Equity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease using ISIN INE0OPA01019 for transactions on or after June 19, 2026
- Members should await the separate BSE notice announcing the new ISIN for Re. 1/- paid-up shares before executing post-record-date trades
- Back-office and settlement systems should be updated to reflect the revised face value and new ISIN once announced

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Notice Date | June 11, 2026 |
| Record Date | June 19, 2026 |
| Old ISIN invalid from | June 19, 2026 |
| New ISIN effective | June 19, 2026 (number TBA) |

## Impact Assessment

The split increases share affordability and liquidity by reducing the face value from Rs. 10/- to Re. 1/- (10:1 ratio), which typically broadens retail investor participation. Trading members face an operational impact requiring ISIN migration in their systems before the record date. The old ISIN INE0OPA01019 will be deactivated on the exchange on June 19, 2026; failure to transition to the new ISIN will result in transaction failures. The new ISIN will be separately notified, so members should monitor BSE circulars closely in the days preceding the record date.
