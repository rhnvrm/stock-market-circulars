## Summary

BSE has announced the allocation of shares to anchor investors for the public issue of Hannah Joseph Hospital Limited. Anchor investor bidding is conducted one day prior to the issue opening for public subscription and helps establish institutional confidence in the offering.

## Key Points

- Hannah Joseph Hospital Limited has completed anchor investor allocation for its public issue
- Anchor allocation typically occurs one day before the IPO opens for retail investors
- Anchor investors are qualified institutional buyers (QIBs) who commit to the issue upfront
- The allocation details including number of shares and allocation price have been disclosed
- This is part of the IPO listing process on BSE

## Regulatory Changes

Not applicable - this is a company-specific listing announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Information disclosure requirement as per SEBI IPO regulations
- Anchor investors are subject to lock-in period as per SEBI guidelines
- Transparency in allocation process to anchor investors

## Important Dates

- Anchor Investor Allocation Date: January 21, 2026
- IPO public bidding likely to open shortly after anchor allocation
- Specific issue opening and closing dates to be confirmed in the prospectus

**Market Impact**: Medium - Anchor investor allocation demonstrates institutional confidence in the IPO and helps price discovery. Successful anchor allocation often indicates positive market sentiment for the issue.
