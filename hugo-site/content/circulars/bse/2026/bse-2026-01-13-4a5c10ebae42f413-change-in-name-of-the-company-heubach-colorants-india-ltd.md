## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for the company previously known as Heubach Colorants India Limited. The company's name has been officially changed to Sudarshan Colorants India Limited with effect from December 12, 2025. The certificate was issued by the Central Processing Centre, ROC and digitally signed by Assistant Registrar Ibson Shah.

## Key Points

- Former name: Heubach Colorants India Limited
- New name: Sudarshan Colorants India Limited
- CIN: L24110MH1956PLC010806
- Company type: Limited by shares
- Original incorporation name: Colour-Chem Limited
- Certificate issued by: Central Processing Centre, ROC
- Approved and signed by: Ibson Shah, Assistant Registrar of Companies

## Regulatory Changes

This is a formal name change under the Companies Act, 2014, processed through the Ministry of Corporate Affairs. The change has been approved pursuant to rule 29 of the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display the old name (Heubach Colorants India Limited) along with the new name (Sudarshan Colorants India Limited) for a period of two years at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- All stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company and its directors on the MCA website www.mca.gov.in/MCA21
- Registered office address: Rupa Renaissance, B Wing, 25th Floor, D-33, MIDC Road, TTC Industrial Area Juinagar, Navi Mumbai, Thane- 400705, Maharashtra

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date of Name Change**: December 12, 2025
- **Certificate Issue Date**: December 12, 2025 (digitally signed at 10:07:14 IST)
- **Dual Name Display Period**: 2 years from December 12, 2025 (until December 12, 2027)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The name change will require investors and market participants to update their records and recognize the new corporate identity. Trading symbol and ISIN may need to be updated by the exchange.

**Operational Impact**: Low - The certificate explicitly states that this name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders. The company's CIN, legal status, and shareholding structure remain unchanged.

**Stakeholder Impact**: Medium - Shareholders, creditors, and other stakeholders need to be aware of the name change for correspondence and legal documentation. The mandatory two-year dual-name display period helps ease the transition.
