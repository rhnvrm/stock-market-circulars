## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in a specific non-convertible debenture (NCD) of 360 ONE PRIME LIMITED (ISIN: INE248U07FP9, Code: 976054) effective February 12, 2026. The suspension is due to the company exercising its call option for early redemption with a record date of February 12, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: 360 ONE PRIME LIMITED
- Debenture Code: OPL-Nifty 50-20-9-34-PVT
- ISIN: INE248U07FP9 (976054)
- Record Date: February 12, 2026
- Purpose: Early Redemption on Exercise of Call Option
- No Dealings From: February 12, 2026
- Notice Number: DR-819/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action notification for debenture suspension.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debenture from February 12, 2026
- Trading members must take note of the suspension and communicate to their clients
- No trading or dealings permitted in the affected security from the no-dealings date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** February 10, 2026
- **Record Date:** February 12, 2026
- **No Dealings From:** February 12, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders of this specific debenture issue. Investors holding this NCD will have their holdings redeemed early as per the call option exercise by the issuer. Trading members should inform affected clients about the suspension and upcoming redemption. This is a routine corporate action with no broader market implications.
