## Summary

BSE has informed members that the GROWW NIFTY SMALLCAP 250 MOMENTUM QUALITY 100 INDEX FUND will reopen for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP facility on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective June 19, 2026, pursuant to Notice No. 20260527-43 dated May 27, 2026.

## Key Points

- The fund reopens for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform from June 19, 2026
- Six scheme variants are available across Regular and Direct Plans
- Plans include Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- All six scheme variants are categorised under Equity
- The fund tracks the NIFTY SMALLCAP 250 MOMENTUM QUALITY 100 Index

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notice regarding the availability of an existing mutual fund scheme for ongoing transactions.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members beyond noting the availability of the scheme on the BSE StAR MF Platform for processing client transactions from June 19, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **June 19, 2026**: GROWW NIFTY SMALLCAP 250 MOMENTUM QUALITY 100 INDEX FUND reopens for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform
