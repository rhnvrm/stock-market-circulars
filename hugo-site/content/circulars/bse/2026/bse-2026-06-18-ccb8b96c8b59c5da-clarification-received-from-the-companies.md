## Summary

BSE's Surveillance department (DOSS) issued Notice No. 20260618-17 noting significant price/volume movement in 12 scrips. The Exchange had written to these companies seeking relevant information for trading members and investors. As of June 18, 2026, replies have been received from 9 companies while 3 companies' responses are still awaited.

## Key Points

- BSE observed abnormal price/volume movement in 12 listed scrips
- Exchange proactively sought clarifications to ensure informed trading
- 9 companies submitted replies (attached to the circular)
- 3 companies have not yet responded
- Issued by BSE Surveillance (DOSS) — signed by Sanjay Jain (DVP) and Shreya Ghose (Manager)

## Companies That Have Replied

1. CIAN Agro Industries & Infrastructure Ltd (Scrip Code: 519477)
2. EMS Ltd (Scrip Code: 543983)
3. Gujarat Poly Electronics Ltd (Scrip Code: 517288)
4. IDBI Bank Ltd (Scrip Code: 500116)
5. Kirloskar Industries Ltd (Scrip Code: 500243)
6. Max Heights Infrastructure Ltd (Scrip Code: 534338)
7. Nurture Well Industries Ltd (Scrip Code: 531889)
8. The Anup Engineering Ltd (Scrip Code: 542460)
9. UCO Bank (Scrip Code: 532505)

## Reply Awaited From

1. Amrapali Industries Ltd (Scrip Code: 526241)
2. Elango Industries Ltd (Scrip Code: 513452)
3. Grand Oak Canyons Distillery Ltd (Scrip Code: 523862)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a disclosure/surveillance notice under BSE's market oversight framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies with pending replies (Amrapali Industries, Elango Industries, Grand Oak Canyons Distillery) are expected to submit their clarifications to BSE Surveillance at the earliest.
- Trading members and investors should refer to the attached replies from the 9 responding companies before making trading decisions in these scrips.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: June 18, 2026
- No specific deadline stated for pending replies, but prompt response is implied by surveillance protocols.

## Impact Assessment

This is an informational surveillance notice. The price/volume movement in these scrips warrants investor caution until company clarifications are reviewed. The inclusion of PSU banks IDBI Bank (500116) and UCO Bank (532505) alongside smaller-cap names suggests a broad-based surveillance sweep rather than a targeted action. No trading restrictions are imposed by this notice itself.
