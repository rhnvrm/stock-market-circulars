## Summary

Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd. (ICCL), in reference to SEBI Circular No. MRD/DoP/SE/Dep/Cir-14/2007 dated December 20, 2007, has notified the stock-wise Market Wide Position Limits (MWPL) and the position limits applicable to Clearing Members (CM), Institutional Investors and Clients under the Securities Lending and Borrowing Scheme (SLBS) segment for the month of July 2026. The detailed stock-wise limits are provided in Annexure I attached to the notice.

## Key Points

- ICCL is an approved intermediary under the Securities Lending Scheme, 1997, operating the broad framework of the SLB Scheme.
- The notice specifies stock-wise MWPL applicable for July 2026.
- Position limits are separately defined for Clearing Members (CM), Institutional Investors and Clients under the SLB segment.
- Detailed stock-wise data is contained in Annexure I (not reproduced in the notice text).
- Queries can be directed to ICCL's risk monitoring team via email or the listed contact numbers.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory change is introduced; this is a routine monthly update issued under the existing SEBI framework for SLBS (SEBI Circular No. MRD/DoP/SE/Dep/Cir-14/2007 dated December 20, 2007).

## Compliance Requirements

Members and participants trading in the SLB segment must adhere to the stock-wise MWPL and position limits specified in Annexure I for July 2026 when undertaking securities lending and borrowing transactions.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 1, 2026
- Applicability: For the month of July 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, recurring monthly notice that updates position limits for the SLB segment. It has limited standalone market impact but is operationally important for clearing members and institutional participants to ensure compliance with position limits and avoid breaches during the SLB trading period.
