## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Currency Derivatives segment on Saturday, July 18, 2026. The session allows trading members using third-party trading platforms of empaneled vendors or in-house developed systems via ETI APIs to test their trading applications, including under exceptional market conditions. The new BOLT Pro TWS version 13.25 will also be released, with details to be published via a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Currency Derivatives and Cross Currency Derivatives segments on July 18, 2026.
- Two mock trading sessions will be conducted during the day (Session 1 and Session 2).
- New BOLT Pro TWS version 13.25 will be released; details to follow in a separate circular.
- Trades from mock trading do not attract margin, pay-in/pay-out obligations, and create no rights or liabilities.
- Members are requested to actively participate and submit feedback by 6:30 PM.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is an operational/testing notice, not a regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

No mandatory compliance action; participation in mock trading is voluntary but encouraged. Members are requested to provide feedback on the mock trading session via the contact numbers/email provided by 6:30 PM on the day of the session.

## Important Dates

- **July 18, 2026**: Mock trading session date.
  - Session 1: Log-in 08:30 AM–09:00 AM; Continuous Trading (Currency & Cross Currency Derivatives) 09:00 AM–12:25 PM; Trade Modification at 12:35 PM.
  - Session 2: Log-in 02:45 PM–03:00 PM; Continuous Trading (Currency & Cross Currency Derivatives) 03:00 PM–05:30 PM; Trade Modification at 05:40 PM.
- Feedback deadline: 6:30 PM on July 18, 2026.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, low-impact operational notice intended for system testing and familiarization by trading members. It has no direct market or financial impact, and no listed securities are affected. It is primarily relevant to trading members and vendors involved in Currency Derivatives trading infrastructure.
