## Summary

BSE has issued a notice regarding the suspension of trading in specific non-convertible debentures of POONAWALLA FINCORP LIMITED. The company has fixed a record date of January 8, 2026 for the redemption of debentures bearing ISIN INE511C07771 (Code: 974566, Series: PFL-GSEC-23-1-26-PVT). Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures effective from the record date.

## Key Points

- Debenture ISIN: INE511C07771
- Debenture Code: 974566
- Series: PFL-GSEC-23-1-26-PVT
- Record Date: January 8, 2026
- No Dealings From: January 8, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures
- Notice Reference: DR-794/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in the specified debentures from January 8, 2026
- Trading members are required to take note of the suspension and inform their clients accordingly
- No transactions should be executed in the affected debenture series from the specified date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 1, 2026
- **Record Date**: January 8, 2026
- **Suspension Effective Date**: January 8, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders of the specific debenture series (PFL-GSEC-23-1-26-PVT) of POONAWALLA FINCORP LIMITED. This is a routine corporate action for debenture redemption and does not affect equity shares or other debt instruments of the company. Debenture holders on record as of January 8, 2026 will receive their redemption proceeds as per the terms of issue. No broader market impact is expected as this affects a single privately placed debenture series.
