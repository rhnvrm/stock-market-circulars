## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of Lendingkart Finance Limited (ISIN: INE090W08038, Code: 975718) effective January 13, 2026. The suspension is due to the company exercising its call option for redemption along with interest payment, with the record date set for January 13, 2026.

## Key Points

- Trading Members advised not to deal in specified debentures from January 13, 2026
- Debenture: LFL-16%-20-5-26-PVT (16% coupon rate)
- ISIN: INE090W08038
- Scrip Code: 975718
- Circular Reference: DR-787/2025-2026
- Notice issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President â€“ Listing Compliance and Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard trading suspension pursuant to corporate action procedures for debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must cease all dealings in the specified debentures from January 13, 2026
- Members should update their systems to reflect the trading suspension
- No new positions should be created in these debentures

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 8, 2026
- **Record Date**: January 13, 2026
- **No Dealings From**: January 13, 2026
- **Purpose Date**: Redemption on Exercise of Call Option and Payment of Interest on January 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders and traders of this specific debenture series. The suspension prevents further trading activity, ensuring orderly redemption process.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders on record date (January 13, 2026) will receive redemption proceeds and final interest payment. No trading possible from suspension date.

**Operational Impact**: Brokers and trading members must update systems to block trading in this ISIN. Back-office teams need to process redemption settlements for clients holding these debentures.
