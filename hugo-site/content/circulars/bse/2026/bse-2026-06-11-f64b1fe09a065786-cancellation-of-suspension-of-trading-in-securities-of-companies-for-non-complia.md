## Summary

BSE has cancelled the proposed suspension of trading in the securities of Pro Fin Capital Services Ltd (Scrip Code: 511557), effective June 15, 2026. This reverses the earlier Exchange Notice No. 20260513-14 dated May 13, 2026, which had announced suspension for non-compliance with SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015 — specifically Regulation 33 (Submission of Financial Results) — for two consecutive quarters (September 2025 and December 2025).

## Key Points

- The cancellation of suspension applies to Pro Fin Capital Services Ltd (Scrip Code: 511557).
- The original suspension was triggered by non-compliance with Regulation 33 of SEBI LODR Regulations, 2015 (non-submission of financial results for Q2 FY26 and Q3 FY26).
- BSE has decided not to proceed with the suspension effective June 15, 2026.
- All other provisions of the original notice (20260513-14 dated May 13, 2026) remain unchanged.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This notice supersedes the suspension action announced for Pro Fin Capital Services Ltd under Notice No. 20260513-14, specifically lifting the intended trading suspension for that company while keeping all other aspects of the original notice intact.

## Compliance Requirements

Market participants are requested to take note of this cancellation. No additional compliance actions are required from the company or participants as a result of this notice beyond acknowledging the restoration of normal trading status for the specified scrip.

## Important Dates

- **June 11, 2026**: Date of this cancellation notice.
- **June 15, 2026**: Effective date from which the suspension will NOT be imposed for Pro Fin Capital Services Ltd.
- **May 13, 2026**: Date of original suspension notice (20260513-14) being partially superseded.

## Impact Assessment

This cancellation is beneficial for shareholders and market participants holding or trading in Pro Fin Capital Services Ltd (511557), as it prevents an imminent trading halt. The stock will remain tradeable on BSE without interruption from June 15, 2026 onwards. The reversal suggests the company may have taken remedial steps toward compliance or that BSE has decided to grant relief. Overall market impact is limited given this is a single company-specific action.
