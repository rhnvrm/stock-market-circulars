## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 16,84,24,217 new equity shares of Sumeet Industries Ltd. (Scrip Code: 514211), issued on a rights basis, are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Friday, July 24, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: Sumeet Industries Ltd.
- Scrip Code: 514211
- ISIN: INE235C01036
- Number of shares: 16,84,24,217 equity shares
- Face value: Rs. 2/- per share
- Premium: Rs. 9.86/- per share
- Issue basis: Rights issue
- Distinctive numbers: 526325681 to 694749897
- Date of Allotment: 22/07/2026
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a routine listing notification following completion of a rights issue allotment, issued under BSE's Listing Operations department.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must recognize the newly listed shares as tradable and pari-passu with existing equity shares from the effective date. No specific action is required from investors beyond standard settlement procedures.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: July 22, 2026
- Listing and trading effective from: July 24, 2026 (Friday)
- Notice Date: July 23, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing increases the total outstanding share count and free float of Sumeet Industries Ltd. following the rights issue, diluting existing shareholding proportionally while providing existing shareholders with newly allotted shares ranking equally with old shares. Market impact is limited to routine trading adjustments given this is a standard post-rights-issue listing procedure.
