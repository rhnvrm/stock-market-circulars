## Summary

MIRAE ASSET Mutual Fund is launching the MIRAE ASSET NIFTY INDIA INFRASTRUCTURE & LOGISTICS ETF as a New Fund Offer (NFO) on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The NFO period runs from January 27, 2025 to February 09, 2025, with a tentative allotment date of February 12, 2026. Both regular and direct plan options are available in the equity category.

## Key Points

- NFO period: January 27, 2025 to February 09, 2025
- Two scheme options available: Regular Plan and Direct Plan (both equity category)
- Tentative allotment date: February 12, 2026
- Extended trading hours available until midnight on February 09, 2026 for lumpsum purchases only
- Transactions without client-initiated funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 will be cancelled
- Clear funds must be available in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on February 11, 2026
- Cheques and OTM payments accepted during NFO period subject to fund clearance deadline
- Switch transactions accepted during NFO period subject to fund receipt before allotment date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard NFO launch notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure client-initiated funds are received by 9:00 AM on T+1 day to avoid cancellation
- Pending transactions (with initiated payment) will remain in system until 3:00 PM on the last day of fund payout to AMC or T+5, whichever is earlier
- CC with N flag will be reported to RTA/AMC for cancelled transactions
- Clear funds must be available in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on February 11, 2026 for allotment
- Switch transactions require funds to be received on or before allotment date; late receipts will not be considered for allotment

## Important Dates

- **January 27, 2025**: NFO period starts
- **February 09, 2025**: NFO period ends (extended till midnight for lumpsum purchases)
- **February 11, 2026, 6:00 PM**: Deadline for clear funds in ICCL Account
- **February 12, 2026**: Tentative allotment date
- **T+1, 9:00 AM**: Deadline for client-initiated funds to avoid cancellation

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market-wide impact as it announces a routine NFO launch for a sector-specific ETF focused on infrastructure and logistics. The impact is limited to:

- Members of BSE StAR MF Platform who wish to participate in the NFO
- Investors interested in infrastructure and logistics sector exposure through passive ETF investment
- No impact on existing listed securities or market operations
- Standard operational procedures apply with specific fund payment timelines for NFO participants
