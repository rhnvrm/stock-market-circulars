## Summary

BSE observed significant price/volume movements in the scrips of 22 listed companies and had written to them seeking clarification under its surveillance process. Replies have been received and published for 14 companies, while responses from 8 companies remain awaited.

## Key Points

- BSE's Department of Surveillance Support (DOSS) sought clarifications from companies showing unusual price/volume activity.
- Replies received from: Gujarat Natural Resources Ltd, Gulf Oil Lubricants India Ltd, Incap Ltd, Laxmipati Engineering Works Ltd, LIC Housing Finance Ltd, Mahalaxmi Rubtech Ltd, Mohite Industries Ltd, Quess Corp Ltd, Saksoft Ltd, SKM Egg Products Export (India) Ltd, SMS Pharmaceuticals Ltd, UHM Vacation Ltd, Ujjivan Small Finance Bank Ltd, and Vedanta Iron And Steel Ltd.
- Replies still awaited from: CMX Holdings Ltd, Dhanvantri Jeevan Rekha Ltd, Elango Industries Ltd, Grand Oak Canyons Distillery Ltd, Marble City India Ltd, Mayur Leather Products Ltd, SC Agrotech Ltd, and Sujala Trading & Holdings Ltd.
- The clarification replies themselves are contained in the attached documents (Replies1, Replies2), not detailed within the notice text.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is a routine surveillance disclosure under BSE's standard practice of seeking clarifications for abnormal price/volume movement.

## Compliance Requirements

Companies listed under "reply awaited" are expected to submit their clarifications to BSE; investors and trading members should note that pending confirmations may indicate incomplete information regarding these scrips' price/volume activity.

## Important Dates

Notice dated 01 July 2026 (Notice No. 20260701-20). No specific deadlines mentioned for pending replies.

## Impact Assessment

The disclosure provides transparency on price/volume anomalies for the named scrips, helping investors and trading members factor in company-provided context. Stocks with pending replies (e.g., CMX Holdings, Dhanvantri Jeevan Rekha, Elango Industries, Grand Oak Canyons Distillery, Marble City India, Mayur Leather Products, SC Agrotech, Sujala Trading & Holdings) may warrant closer monitoring given the lack of clarification, while those with responses provide added information to assess the underlying cause of price/volume movements.
