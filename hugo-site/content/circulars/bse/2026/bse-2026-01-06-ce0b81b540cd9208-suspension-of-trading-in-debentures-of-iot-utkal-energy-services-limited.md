## Summary

BSE has announced suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures of IOT UTKAL ENERGY SERVICES LIMITED (ISIN: INE310L07860, Code: 950210) effective January 13, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Debenture ISIN: INE310L07860 (Code: 1008IOT26A, BSE Code: 950210)
- Record date fixed: January 13, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- No dealings permitted from: January 13, 2026
- Reference number: DR-797/2025-2026
- Notice issued by: Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President â€“ Listing Compliance and Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard operational suspension due to corporate action.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from January 13, 2026
- All exchange members must take note of the suspension and update their systems accordingly
- Ensure client communications reflect the trading suspension to prevent transaction attempts

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 6, 2026
- **Record Date**: January 13, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Effective**: January 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

High impact on debt market participants holding or trading IOT UTKAL ENERGY SERVICES LIMITED debentures. Investors will be unable to trade these securities from the record date. This is a temporary suspension related to the redemption process. Debenture holders on record as of January 13, 2026 will be eligible for interest payment and redemption proceeds. Market liquidity for this instrument will be completely frozen from the suspension date.
