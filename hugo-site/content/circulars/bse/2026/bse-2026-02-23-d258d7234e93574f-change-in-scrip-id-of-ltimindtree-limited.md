## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the Scrip ID of LTIMindtree Limited (Scrip Code: 540005) in the BOLT Plus trading system will be changed from **LTIM** to **LTM** with effect from February 27, 2026. This change was made at the request of LTIMindtree Limited.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code 540005 (LTIMindtree Limited) will have its BOLT Plus Scrip ID updated from `LTIM` to `LTM`.
- The change is effective from **February 27, 2026**.
- The change was initiated at the request of the company.
- All trading members must update their systems and workflows to use the new Scrip ID.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are involved. This is an administrative update to the identifier used within BSE's BOLT Plus trading system.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must take note of the new Scrip ID `LTM` for LTIMindtree Limited and update any internal systems, order templates, or algorithmic trading configurations accordingly prior to February 27, 2026.
- For clarifications, members may contact:
  - Mr. Tushar Shetty: +91 22 2272 8634 | Listing.crd@bseindia.com
  - Ms. Asmita Chavan: +91 22 2272 8266 | Listing.crd@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **February 23, 2026**: Notice issued.
- **February 27, 2026**: New Scrip ID `LTM` becomes effective in BOLT Plus system.

## Impact Assessment

This is a purely administrative change with minimal market impact. Traders and algorithmic systems that reference LTIMindtree by its BOLT Plus Scrip ID (`LTIM`) must update to the new ID (`LTM`) before the effective date to avoid order entry errors. There is no change to the Scrip Code (540005), ISIN, or any financial attributes of the security.
