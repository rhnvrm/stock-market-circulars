## Summary

BSE announces the listing of three new Treasury Bills (T-Bills) in the Debt Market segment under G Group effective January 30, 2026. The T-Bills have varying maturity periods of 91 days, 182 days, and 364 days with market lot size of 1 unit each.

## Key Points

- Three T-Bills to be listed on BSE Debt Market segment from January 30, 2026
- All securities classified under G GROUP - DEBT INSTRUMENTS
- Market lot size: 1 unit for all three instruments
- Trading will be suspended two working days before maturity date
- Scrip codes: 805172 (91-day), 805173 (182-day), 805174 (364-day)

## Listed Securities Details

| Scrip Code | ISIN | Symbol | Maturity Date | Tenor |
|------------|------|--------|---------------|-------|
| 805172 | IN002025X430 | 91TB300426 | April 30, 2026 | 91 days |
| 805173 | IN002025Y438 | 182TB30726 | July 30, 2026 | 182 days |
| 805174 | IN002025Z435 | 364TB28127 | January 28, 2027 | 364 days |

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification for new government securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that these securities will not be available for trading two working days prior to maturity/redemption date
- Trading suspension calculation: Maturity Date (T) minus two Trading Days (excluding Bank Holidays)
- No additional compliance obligations for trading members

## Important Dates

- **Listing Effective Date:** January 30, 2026
- **91-day T-Bill Maturity:** April 30, 2026 (trading stops April 28, 2026)
- **182-day T-Bill Maturity:** July 30, 2026 (trading stops July 28, 2026)
- **364-day T-Bill Maturity:** January 28, 2027 (trading stops January 26, 2027)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard administrative listing of government securities that expands available debt instruments for trading. The T-Bills provide short to medium-term investment options for debt market participants. Trading members should update their systems with the new scrip codes and symbols. Contact BSE at 2272 8352/5753/8597 for clarifications.
