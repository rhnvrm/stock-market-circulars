## Summary

BSE has announced the discontinuation of trading and voluntary delisting of debentures of Shreshta Infra Projects Private Limited (Scrip Code: 975332). The company has complied with all formalities under Chapter VIA of the SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015, and trading will cease effective June 03, 2026.

## Key Points

- Debentures of Shreshta Infra Projects Private Limited (Scrip Code: 975332) are being voluntarily delisted from BSE.
- Trading in these debentures will be discontinued with effect from Wednesday, June 03, 2026.
- The delisting is pursuant to Chapter VIA of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015.
- The company has completed all required formalities for voluntary delisting.
- Trading members may contact the debt department for clarifications at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915.

## Regulatory Changes

The delisting is carried out under Chapter VIA of the SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015, as amended. No new regulatory changes are introduced by this circular; it implements existing delisting provisions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are directed to take note of the discontinuation of trading in Scrip Code 975332.
- No further trades should be executed in these debentures on or after June 03, 2026.
- Members requiring clarification should contact BSE's debt department directly.

## Important Dates

- **June 02, 2026**: Circular issuance date.
- **June 03, 2026**: Effective date of trading discontinuation and delisting of debentures (Scrip Code: 975332).

## Impact Assessment

The delisting affects holders and traders of debentures of Shreshta Infra Projects Private Limited only. As a voluntary delisting of a private limited company's debt instrument, the market-wide impact is limited. Existing debenture holders should note that secondary market liquidity via BSE will no longer be available from June 03, 2026, and should plan accordingly for any redemption or settlement needs.
