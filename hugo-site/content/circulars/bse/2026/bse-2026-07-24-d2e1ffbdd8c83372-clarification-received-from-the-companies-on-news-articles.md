## Summary

BSE's Surveillance Department (DOSS) sought clarifications from companies whose names appeared in news articles across newspapers, websites, and television channels, as part of its process of verifying media reports and informing members/investors about their accuracy. Petronet LNG Ltd (Scrip Code: 532522) has submitted its reply, which is attached to the notice. Rajesh Exports Ltd (Scrip Code: 531500) has not yet responded, and its reply remains awaited.

## Key Points

- BSE routinely seeks clarification from companies on news articles to verify accuracy for members and investors.
- Petronet LNG Ltd (532522): reply received regarding a Moneycontrol news article; attached to the notice.
- Rajesh Exports Ltd (531500): reply awaited regarding a Hindu BusinessLine news article.
- Notice issued by BSE's Surveillance team (Deputy Vice President Sanjay Jain and Manager Shreya Ghose).

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine surveillance clarification notice, not a regulatory or policy change.

## Compliance Requirements

Rajesh Exports Ltd is expected to submit its clarification reply to BSE regarding the news article referenced. No action is required from other market participants beyond noting the disclosure.

## Important Dates

Notice dated 24 July 2026. No specific deadline is mentioned for Rajesh Exports Ltd's pending reply.

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact, routine disclosure/surveillance measure aimed at market transparency. It does not indicate any confirmed material development but signals that investors and traders should watch for follow-up clarification from Rajesh Exports Ltd and review the attached reply from Petronet LNG Ltd for context on the reported news.
