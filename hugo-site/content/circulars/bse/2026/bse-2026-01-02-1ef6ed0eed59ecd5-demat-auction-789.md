## Summary

BSE has published the list of securities subject to demat auction under Settlement No. 789 dated January 2, 2026. The auction covers 98 different scrips with varying quantities ranging from 1 share to 49,900 shares across multiple sectors including power, banking, infrastructure, pharmaceuticals, technology, and manufacturing.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 789
- Settlement Date: January 2, 2026
- Total number of scrips: 98
- Largest quantity: EXCEL REALTY (49,900 shares)
- Other significant quantities: BABA ARTS LTD (8,935 shares), SAMBHV STEEL (23,534 shares), SWOJAS FOODS (5,000 shares), BAJAJ HOUSING (3,000 shares)
- Sectors affected: Power, Banking, Real Estate, Pharmaceuticals, Technology, Manufacturing, Metals, Infrastructure

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational circular for demat auction settlement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and participants must settle their obligations for the listed securities as per the auction mechanism
- Settlement must be completed as per BSE's prescribed timelines for Settlement No. 789
- Concerned parties must ensure availability of securities or funds for auction settlement

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: January 2, 2026
- Settlement Number: 789

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational circular with minimal market impact. The demat auction affects specific quantities of shares where delivery obligations were not met in regular settlement. The impact is limited to:

- Participants who failed to deliver securities in the regular settlement cycle
- The specific scrips and quantities listed for auction
- No broad market implications expected
- Standard settlement mechanism for ensuring contract fulfillment
