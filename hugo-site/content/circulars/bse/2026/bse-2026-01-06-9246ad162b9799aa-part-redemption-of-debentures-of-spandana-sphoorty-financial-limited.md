## Summary

SPANDANA SPHOORTY FINANCIAL LIMITED has fixed the record date for payment of interest and part redemption of its non-convertible debentures (ISIN: INE572J07745, Code: 974793). Trading in these debentures will commence with reduced face value from January 9, 2026.

## Key Points

- Record date set for January 9, 2026
- Purpose: Part redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Security affected: SSFL-11.10%-24-4-26-PVT (ISIN: INE572J07745)
- Reduced face value: Rs. 14,290 per debenture
- Effective date for reduced face value: January 9, 2026
- Settlement number: DR-795/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the reduced face value effective from January 9, 2026
- All trades from the effective date onwards will reflect the reduced face value of Rs. 14,290 per debenture
- Debenture holders on record as of January 9, 2026 will be eligible for interest payment and part redemption

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: January 9, 2026
- **Effective Date for Reduced Face Value**: January 9, 2026
- **Notice Issue Date**: January 6, 2026 (Notice No. 20260106-7)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders of SPANDANA SPHOORTY FINANCIAL LIMITED debentures (ISIN: INE572J07745). The part redemption reduces the outstanding principal amount, with the face value being reduced to Rs. 14,290 per debenture.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update their systems to reflect the reduced face value from January 9, 2026. This is a routine debt market operation with no broader market implications beyond the specific security holders.
