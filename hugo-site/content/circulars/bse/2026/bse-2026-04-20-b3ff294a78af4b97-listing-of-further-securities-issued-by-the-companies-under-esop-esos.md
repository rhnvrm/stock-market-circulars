## Summary

BSE has listed further new securities issued under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP/ESOS) by six companies. These securities are admitted for trading on the Exchange effective Tuesday, April 21, 2026. None of the newly listed shares are subject to any lock-in period.

## Key Points

- Six companies have had additional ESOP/ESOS shares listed for trading from April 21, 2026
- No lock-in restrictions apply to any of the newly listed securities
- Total new shares listed across all six companies: approximately 3,194,739 shares
- ICICI Bank has the largest addition with 3,001,435 new shares (ISIN: INE090A01021)
- Reliance Industries added 66,088 shares (ISIN: INE002A01018)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice for listing of employee stock option shares.

## Compliance Requirements

Market participants are requested to take note of the new securities and their respective ISIN and scrip codes for trading purposes. No additional compliance action is required.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** April 20, 2026
- **Trading Effective Date:** April 21, 2026 (Tuesday)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. The newly listed ESOP/ESOS shares represent a small fraction of each company's total outstanding shares. Details of newly listed securities:
