## Summary

BSE has informed trading members of the new ISIN number allotted to Pondy Oxides & Chemicals Ltd (Scrip Code: 532626) following the sub-division (stock split) of its equity shares from a face value of Rs.5/- to Rs.2/- per share. This is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20260709-30 dated July 09, 2026.

## Key Points

- New ISIN allotted: INE063E01061
- Reason: Sub-division of equity shares from face value Rs.5/- to Rs.2/-
- Applicable to: Pondy Oxides & Chemicals Ltd (Scrip Code: 532626)
- Reference: DR- 676/2026-2027
- This notice supersedes/continues from Exchange Notice No. 20260709-30 dated July 09, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is an operational update assigning the new ISIN consequent to the previously approved sub-division of equity shares.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must update their systems to recognize the new ISIN INE063E01061 for trades in Pondy Oxides & Chemicals Ltd shares executed on and from the ex-date.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 20, 2026
- Ex-Date (new ISIN effective for trades): July 21, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to operational and back-office processes for trading members and depository participants, who need to ensure the new ISIN is correctly mapped for settlement of trades from the ex-date onward. Shareholders will see their holdings reflected under the new ISIN with a proportionally adjusted share count due to the sub-division, but overall economic value remains unaffected.
