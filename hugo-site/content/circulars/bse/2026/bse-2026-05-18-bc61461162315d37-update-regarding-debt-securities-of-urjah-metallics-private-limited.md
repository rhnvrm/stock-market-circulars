## Summary

BSE has issued an update (Notice No. 20260518-10) regarding the debt securities of Urjah Metallics Private Limited. Following the receipt of final call money from debenture holders, the ISIN for Scrip Code 977447 will change and the paid-up value will be revised upward to Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture, effective 19 May 2026.

## Key Points

- This notice is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20260109-24 dated 09 January 2026
- Urjah Metallics Private Limited has received final call money from its debenture holders
- The existing ISIN INE0U4E07015 will be replaced by new ISIN INE0U4E07023 effective 19 May 2026
- The paid-up value will increase from Rs. 77,143.00 per debenture to Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture
- Scrip Code 977447 is associated with this change

## Regulatory Changes

- ISIN for the listed debt securities of Urjah Metallics Private Limited changes from INE0U4E07015 to INE0U4E07023
- Paid-up value revised from Rs. 77,143.00 per debenture to Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture upon receipt of final call money

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members are required to note the new ISIN (INE0U4E07023) and updated paid-up value for Scrip Code 977447
- All systems and records referencing the old ISIN INE0U4E07015 must be updated to reflect the new ISIN INE0U4E07023

## Important Dates

- **18 May 2026**: Date of this notice
- **19 May 2026 (Tuesday)**: Effective date for the new ISIN and revised paid-up value
- **09 January 2026**: Date of original Exchange Notice No. 20260109-24 (referenced as continuation)

## Impact Assessment

This change is limited in scope to trading members and debenture holders of Urjah Metallics Private Limited. The transition to a new ISIN and the increase in paid-up value from Rs. 77,143 to Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture reflects full payment of call money. Trading members must ensure their systems are updated before market open on 19 May 2026 to avoid settlement or trading issues. The impact is operational and confined to participants holding or trading these specific debt securities.
