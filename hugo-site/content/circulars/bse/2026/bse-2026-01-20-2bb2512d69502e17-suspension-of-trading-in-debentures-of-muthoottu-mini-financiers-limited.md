## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of Muthoottu Mini Financiers Limited effective January 28, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing the record date for redemption of debentures, and trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the record date.

## Key Points

- Trading in debentures of Muthoottu Mini Financiers Limited (ISIN: INE101Q07AX3, Code: 939903) will be suspended
- The debenture series is MMFL-8.50%-12-2-26-NCD with 8.50% interest rate
- Record date for redemption is set as January 28, 2026
- No dealings permitted from January 28, 2026 onwards
- Notice issued under reference number DR-807/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notice for debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in the specified debentures from January 28, 2026
- Trading members must note the suspension and inform their clients accordingly
- No buy or sell transactions should be executed for ISIN INE101Q07AX3 from the specified date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 20, 2026
- **Record Date**: January 28, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Date**: January 28, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and potential traders of this specific debenture series (MMFL-8.50%-12-2-26-NCD). Existing debenture holders will receive redemption proceeds as per the terms of issuance. The suspension is temporary and relates solely to the redemption process. Other securities of Muthoottu Mini Financiers Limited, if any, are not affected by this notice. This is a routine corporate action with minimal market-wide impact.
