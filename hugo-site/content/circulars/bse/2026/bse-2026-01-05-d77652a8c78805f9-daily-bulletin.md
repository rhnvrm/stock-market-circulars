## Summary

BSE Daily Bulletin No. 190/2025-2026 dated January 05, 2026 announces record dates for various corporate actions including dividend payments, commercial paper redemptions, and non-convertible debenture (NCD) interest payments and partial redemptions across multiple issuers.

## Key Points

- Bulletin covers record dates spanning from January 2026 to March 2026
- Multiple commercial paper redemptions scheduled for 360 ONE WAM LIMITED between February 2-24, 2026
- Several private placement NCD partial redemptions and interest payments scheduled for January 2026
- Aurionpro Solutions Ltd. announces third interim dividend with record date February 10, 2026
- Bajaj Finance Limited has commercial paper redemptions in March 2026
- All securities will have trading flag 1 on their respective record dates

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this bulletin.

## Compliance Requirements

- Investors must hold securities on the specified record dates to be eligible for respective corporate actions
- Trading flag 1 will be applicable on record dates for all listed securities
- Issuers must process payments/redemptions as per the scheduled dates

## Important Dates

**January 2026:**
- January 7: ANNAPURNA FINANCE PRIVATE LIMITED - NCD partial redemption and interest payment
- January 10: Ambit Finvest Private Limited - NCD interest and partial redemption
- January 12: AVANTI FINANCE PRIVATE LIMITED - NCD interest payment
- January 15: AVANTI FINANCE PRIVATE LIMITED and AVIGNA HOUSING PRIVATE LIMITED - NCD interest payments
- January 16: Akara Capital Advisors Private Limited - NCD interest and partial redemption; AVANTI FINANCE PRIVATE LIMITED (2 series) - NCD interest payments

**February 2026:**
- February 2-24: Multiple 360 ONE WAM LIMITED commercial paper redemptions (8 series)
- February 10: Aurionpro Solutions Ltd. - Third interim dividend record date

**March 2026:**
- March 4, 11: Bajaj Finance Limited - Commercial paper redemptions

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - affects multiple debt and equity securities with scheduled redemptions and payments

**Investor Impact:** Investors holding these securities on record dates will receive dividends, interest payments, or principal redemptions as applicable. Those trading around record dates should note the ex-date implications.

**Issuer Impact:** Listed companies and private placement issuers must ensure timely processing of payments and redemptions to maintain compliance and investor confidence.
