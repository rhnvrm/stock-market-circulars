## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that equity shares of MSAFE EQUIPMENTS LIMITED will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544695 and symbol MSAFE with ISIN INE2B5L01011.

## Key Points

- Company Name: MSAFE EQUIPMENTS LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544695
- Trading Symbol: MSAFE
- ISIN: INE2B5L01011
- Segment: SME
- Listing date to be informed through separate notice

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the scrip details for future trading once the listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: February 03, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard new listing announcement on the SME segment. The impact is limited to the specific company and its stakeholders. The actual listing date and security details will be communicated separately, allowing trading members to prepare for trading commencement.
