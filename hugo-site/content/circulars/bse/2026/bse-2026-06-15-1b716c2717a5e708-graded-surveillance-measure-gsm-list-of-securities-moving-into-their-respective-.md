## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that certain securities (listed in the attached Annexure) will be moved to higher stages of the Graded Surveillance Measure (GSM) framework effective June 16, 2026. The move triggers enhanced surveillance actions depending on the stage assigned, ranging from higher margin requirements to restricted weekly trading.

## Key Points

- Securities will be moved to their respective higher GSM stages from June 16, 2026.
- Settlement for affected securities will be on a trade-to-trade basis; no netting off is permitted.
- Four GSM stages are defined with progressively restrictive surveillance actions.
- Additional Surveillance Deposit (ASD) is applicable from Stage II onwards, payable by buyers.
- Trading members are advised to take adequate precautions while dealing in these securities.

## Regulatory Changes

The GSM framework referenced in this notice was originally published via Exchange notice no. 20170223-44 (February 23, 2017), with subsequent amendments on March 3, 2017; July 20, 2018; November 29, 2019; and November 17, 2023. This notice operationalises the latest stage migrations under that framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Stage I:** Applicable margin rate of 100%; price band of 5% or lower as applicable.
- **Stage II:** Trade-for-trade settlement; price band of 5% or lower; ASD of 50% of trade value to be deposited by buyers.
- **Stage III:** Trade-for-trade settlement; price band of 5% or lower; trading permitted once a week (every Monday / 1st trading day of the week); ASD of 100% of trade value to be deposited by buyers.
- **Stage IV:** Trade-for-trade settlement; price band of 5% or lower; trading permitted once a week (every Monday / 1st trading day of the week); ASD of 100% of trade value to be deposited by buyers; no upward price movement permitted.
- Members must ensure no netting off is done for trades in GSM securities.
- Members may refer to the BSE FAQ on GSM or contact bse.surv@bseindia.com for clarifications.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 15, 2026
- **Effective Date:** June 16, 2026 (securities move to higher GSM stages)

## Impact Assessment

Affected securities will face significantly tighter trading conditions — higher margins, restricted settlement, and in upper stages, weekly-only trading with no upward price movement. This limits liquidity and increases the cost of trading for buyers via ASD requirements. Retail and institutional participants holding or looking to trade these securities should review the Annexure and plan accordingly. The overall market impact is limited to the specific securities listed in the annexure.
