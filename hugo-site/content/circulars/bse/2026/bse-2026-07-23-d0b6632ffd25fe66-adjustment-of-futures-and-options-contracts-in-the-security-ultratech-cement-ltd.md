## Summary

BSE has informed trading members of the Equity Derivatives Segment that Futures & Options contracts on the underlying scrip UltraTech Cement Ltd (Derivative Asset Code – ULCL, Equity Scrip Code – 532538) will be adjusted to account for a Final Dividend of Rs. 240/- per equity share declared by the company for FY 2026-27. The record date for the dividend is July 30, 2026, with the ex-date being Thursday, July 30, 2026. Adjustments will be made by the Exchange on Wednesday, July 29, 2026.

## Key Points

- UltraTech Cement Ltd has fixed July 30, 2026 as the Record Date for payment of a Final Dividend of Rs. 240/- per equity share for FY 2026-27.
- The Exchange will adjust all available Futures & Options contracts on ULCL on Wednesday, July 29, 2026.
- Ex-date for the corporate action is Thursday, July 30, 2026.
- Adjustment is made in pursuance of Exchange notice 20180710-26 and SEBI Master Circular No. SEBI/HO/MRD/DP/CIR/P/2016/135 dated December 16, 2016.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is an operational adjustment applying existing SEBI/BSE guidelines on corporate action adjustments for stock derivatives.

## Compliance Requirements

- Options Contracts: The full dividend value of Rs. 240/- will be deducted from all strike prices generated at the end of July 29, 2026. Adjusted strike prices will be available for trading from July 30, 2026.
- Futures Contracts: The adjusted futures price will be the reference rate (daily mark-to-market settlement price on July 29, 2026) less the dividend amount of Rs. 240/-. This adjusted rate will serve as the base rate/previous close for trading on July 30, 2026.
- Trading members should contact their respective relationship manager for further details.

## Important Dates

- July 29, 2026 (Wednesday): Exchange makes the necessary contract adjustments; reference rate is based on this day's mark-to-market settlement price.
- July 30, 2026 (Thursday): Ex-date and Record Date; adjusted strike prices and futures base rate become effective for trading.

## Impact Assessment

The adjustment ensures that F&O contract prices for UltraTech Cement Ltd accurately reflect the dividend payout, preventing distortion in option strike prices and futures valuations. Market impact is limited to derivative traders and market makers holding positions in ULCL contracts, who need to account for the price adjustment in their trading and risk models around July 29-30, 2026.
