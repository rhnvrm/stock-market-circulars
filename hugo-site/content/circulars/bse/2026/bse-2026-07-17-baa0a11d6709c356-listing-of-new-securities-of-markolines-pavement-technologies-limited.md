## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 1,62,800 new equity shares of Markolines Pavement Technologies Limited (Scrip Code: 543364), issued at a premium of Rs. 155 per share to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants, are listed and permitted to trade with effect from Monday, July 20, 2026. The new shares rank pari-passu with the existing equity shares of the company.

## Key Points

- 1,62,800 equity shares of face value Rs. 10/- each allotted at an issue price of Rs. 165/- (premium of Rs. 155/-)
- Shares issued to Non-Promoters on a preferential basis upon conversion of warrants
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE0FW001016
- Allotments made in three tranches: 22,800 shares (30/01/2026), 40,000 shares (27/02/2026), and 1,00,000 shares (05/03/2026)
- All 1,62,800 shares are under lock-in until 20/01/2027

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a standard listing notification following preferential allotment and warrant conversion under existing SEBI ICDR regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

No action required from trading members or investors beyond updating trading systems to reflect the increased share count effective the listing date.

## Important Dates

- Allotment dates: January 30, 2026; February 27, 2026; March 5, 2026
- Listing/trading effective date: Monday, July 20, 2026
- Lock-in expiry: January 20, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The new shares are subject to lock-in until January 20, 2027, so they will not be immediately available for trading, limiting any near-term supply overhang or dilution effect on the stock.
