## Summary

BSE has listed 7,19,999 bonus equity shares of Gretex Corporate Services Limited (Scrip Code: 543324, ISIN: INE199P01028) effective Wednesday, June 3, 2026. The shares were allotted as bonus shares in a 9:10 ratio (9 bonus shares for every 10 held) reserved on 8,00,000 equity shares. These new shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company.

## Key Points

- **Total bonus shares listed:** 7,19,999 equity shares
- **Allotment ratio:** 9 bonus shares for every 10 equity shares held
- **Scrip Code:** 543324
- **ISIN:** INE199P01028
- **Distinctive numbers:** 23439348 to 24159346
- **Trading effective from:** Wednesday, June 3, 2026
- **Ranking:** Pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification pursuant to bonus share allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed that these new securities are listed and permitted to trade from June 3, 2026. Members must note the lock-in restrictions on specific tranches before executing trades on behalf of clients holding locked shares.

## Important Dates

- **Listing/Trading effective date:** June 3, 2026
- **Lock-in expiry — 05 Dec 2026:** Applies to 85,013 shares (DN: 23439348–23524360), 94,986 shares (DN: 23524361–23619346), and 6,480 shares (DN: 24152867–24159346) — totalling 1,86,479 shares
- **Lock-in expiry — 05 Dec 2027:** Applies to 2,97,000 shares (DN: 23619347–23916346) and 2,36,520 shares (DN: 23916347–24152866) — totalling 5,33,520 shares

## Impact Assessment

The listing increases the total tradeable equity share count of Gretex Corporate Services Limited, though a significant portion (approximately 74% or 5,33,520 shares) remains locked until December 2027. The freely tradeable portion from June 3, 2026 is limited to shares outside lock-in restrictions. Market impact is minimal as bonus issues are non-dilutive in value terms; the share price typically adjusts downward proportionally on the ex-bonus date. Existing shareholders' proportional ownership remains unchanged.
