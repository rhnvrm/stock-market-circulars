## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (No. 20260310-4) informing trading members that Government Security **839RAJSDL26** (Scrip Code: 810957, ISIN: IN2920150355) is maturing on its redemption date. Accordingly, trading in this security is suspended with effect from **March 11, 2026**, under reference DR-837/2025-2026.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code **810957** (ISIN: IN2920150355), identified as **839RAJSDL26**, is a Rajasthan State Development Loan maturing on its redemption date.
- Trading members are advised **not to deal** in this security from March 11, 2026 onwards.
- The notice is issued under reference **DR-837/2025-2026**.
- Circular issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President – Listing Compliance & Operations.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework changes. This is a routine operational notice under BSE's standard process for suspending trading in maturing government/state development loan securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must **cease all trading activity** in Scrip Code 810957 (839RAJSDL26) effective March 11, 2026.
- Members should update their systems and alert their clients holding or intending to trade this security.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** March 10, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Effective:** March 11, 2026 (redemption/maturity date)

## Impact Assessment

This suspension is a standard end-of-life action for a maturing Rajasthan State Development Loan. The impact is limited to existing holders of this specific security who will no longer be able to trade it on BSE's debt segment. Redemption proceeds will be settled as per normal maturity procedures. No broader market or systemic impact is anticipated.
