## Summary

Hannah Joseph Hospital Limited has filed its shareholding pattern as on January 30, 2026, under Regulation 31(1)(a). The total share capital consists of 22,698,363 equity shares held by 628 shareholders. Promoter and Promoter Group hold 68.83% while Public shareholders hold 31.17%. The company has confirmed no partly paid shares, convertible securities, warrants, outstanding ESOPs, depository receipts, locked-in shares, pledged shares, or shares under non-disposal undertaking.

## Key Points

- Total equity shares: 22,698,363 fully paid-up shares
- Promoter & Promoter Group: 6 shareholders holding 15,624,400 shares (68.83%)
- Public shareholding: 622 shareholders holding 7,073,963 shares (31.17%)
- Dematerialized shares: 18,404,363 shares (81.08% of total)
- No partly paid shares, convertible securities, warrants, or ESOPs outstanding
- No shares are locked-in, pledged, or under non-disposal undertaking
- No shares held against depository receipts
- No equity shares with differential voting rights
- No significant beneficial owner identified

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this disclosure.

## Compliance Requirements

This is a routine compliance filing under SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015, Regulation 31(1)(a), requiring listed entities to submit shareholding patterns on a periodic basis.

## Important Dates

- Shareholding pattern date: January 30, 2026
- Filing date: February 1, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard regulatory disclosure with no immediate market impact. The shareholding structure shows stable promoter holding above minimum public shareholding requirements. The high dematerialization rate (81.08%) indicates good market infrastructure participation. No encumbrances on promoter shares suggest financial stability.
