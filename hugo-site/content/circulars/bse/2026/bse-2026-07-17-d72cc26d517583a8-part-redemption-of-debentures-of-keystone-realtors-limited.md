## Summary

BSE has issued a notice in continuation of its earlier Exchange Notice No. 20260319-3 dated March 19, 2026, regarding the part redemption of debentures of Keystone Realtors Limited (scrip code: 977174). The Company has now informed the Exchange that it has revised the reduced face value of the debentures to Rs. 85,074.63 per debenture. Trading Members are advised that the debentures will trade with this revised reduced face value effective July 20, 2026.

## Key Points

- Notice is a follow-up to Exchange Notice No. 20260319-3 dated March 19, 2026, on part redemption of debentures.
- Company: Keystone Realtors Limited.
- Debenture: KRL-9.5%-29-9-28-PVT, ISIN INE263M07109, Scrip Code 977174.
- Revised (reduced) face value: Rs. 85,074.63 per debenture.
- Reference: DR-675/2026-2027.
- Signed by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President, Listing Compliance and Operations.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory change; this is an operational update revising the previously communicated reduced face value for the part-redeemed debentures.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members are required to take note of the revised reduced face value and ensure the debentures are traded accordingly from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- March 19, 2026: Original notice (20260319-3) on part redemption of debentures issued.
- July 17, 2026: Current notice revising the reduced face value.
- July 20, 2026: Effective date from which debentures will trade at the revised reduced face value of Rs. 85,074.63 per debenture.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to trading members and investors dealing in this specific NCD (KRL-9.5%-29-9-28-PVT) of Keystone Realtors Limited on BSE's debt segment. The revision ensures accurate pricing reflecting the part-redemption already undertaken, with no broader market-wide implications.
