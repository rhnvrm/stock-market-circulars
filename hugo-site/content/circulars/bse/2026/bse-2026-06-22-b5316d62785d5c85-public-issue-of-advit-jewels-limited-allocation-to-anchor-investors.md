## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 35,88,700 equity shares have been allocated to Anchor Investors (AIs) in the public issue of Advit Jewels Limited at a price of Rs. 138 per equity share on June 22, 2026.

## Key Points

- 35,88,700 equity shares allocated to Anchor Investors
- Issue price for anchor allocation: Rs. 138 per equity share
- Allocation date: June 22, 2026
- Notice issued by BSE Listing Business Relationship Department
- Company announcement enclosed as attachment

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural notification for anchor investor allocation in an IPO.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the anchor investor allocation as part of the pre-IPO process. No specific compliance actions required from members beyond awareness of the allocation details.

## Important Dates

- Anchor Investor Allocation Date: June 22, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This allocation sets the anchor price at Rs. 138 per equity share for the Advit Jewels Limited IPO. Anchor investor participation signals institutional interest in the issue. The IPO is expected to open for general subscription shortly after this anchor allocation. Market participants can use the anchor price as a reference for evaluating the issue's attractiveness.
