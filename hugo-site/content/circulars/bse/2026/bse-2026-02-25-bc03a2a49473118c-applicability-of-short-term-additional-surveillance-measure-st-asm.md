## Summary

BSE has issued an update to the Short Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) framework, effective February 26, 2026. Six new securities have been added to the ST-ASM 5/15/30 Days framework, one security has been moved to a lower stage, and ten securities have been removed from the framework entirely. No securities were moved to a higher stage in this update.

## Key Points

- 6 securities newly shortlisted in ST-ASM Framework (Stage I) effective February 26, 2026
- 0 securities moved to a higher ASM stage
- 1 security (Aritas Vinyl Ltd, 544683) moved to a lower ASM stage
- 10 securities removed from the ST-ASM Framework effective February 26, 2026
- KRN Heat Exchanger And Refrigeration Ltd (544263) shortlisted as per NSE
- Vanta Bioscience Ltd (540729) and Marc Loire Fashions Ltd (544437) are SME scrips

## Regulatory Changes

The ST-ASM framework applies enhanced surveillance in the form of 5/15/30-day trading restrictions on shortlisted securities. Securities are staged (Stage I and above) based on continued surveillance criteria. Additions, stage changes, and removals are periodically reviewed and published by BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the revised ST-ASM list effective February 26, 2026
- Applicable margin and trading restrictions as per the ST-ASM framework stage apply to newly added securities
- Securities moving out of ST-ASM revert to standard trading conditions unless covered by another surveillance framework (LT-ASM, GSM, ESM, Trade for Trade, or Pledge Framework)

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** February 25, 2026
- **Effective Date:** February 26, 2026
