## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260611-16 announces the listing and admission to dealings of equity shares of Vedanta Iron And Steel Limited (VISL) effective Monday, June 15, 2026. The shares are allotted pursuant to a Composite Scheme of Arrangement involving the demerger of various undertakings of Vedanta Limited (VL), approved by the NCLT Mumbai Bench on December 16, 2025. The scrip will be classified under the T Group and placed in the Trade-for-Trade segment for 10 trading days.

## Key Points

- **Listing Date:** June 15, 2026 (Monday)
- **Scrip Code:** 544784 | **Scrip ID (BOLT):** VISL | **ISIN:** INE1CLE01013
- **Group:** T (Trade-for-Trade for first 10 trading days)
- **Total Shares Listed:** 3,91,03,88,057 equity shares of Re. 1/- each, fully paid
- **Distinctive Numbers:** 1 to 3,910,388,057
- **Face Value:** Re. 1/- per share
- **Market Lot:** 1
- VISL will participate in the Special Pre-Open Session (SPOS) for IPO and Other category scrips as per SEBI Circulars CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012, CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 (January 20, 2012) and SEBI/HO/MRD-TPD1/CIR/P/2023/55 (April 11, 2023)
- For SPOS clarifications, contact BSE Helpdesk: 022-6136 3100/3171

## Regulatory Changes

- Listing governed under SEBI Circular No. CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 (January 20, 2012) and SEBI/HO/MRD-TPD1/CIR/P/2023/55 (April 11, 2023) mandating Trade-for-Trade segment for new listings
- Shares classified under T Group per BSE grouping norms for newly listed securities
- Special Pre-Open Session (SPOS) applicable as per SEBI guidelines for IPO/relisted scrips (BSE Notice 20120216-29)

## Compliance Requirements

- All market participants must note the listing effective June 15, 2026
- Trading in VISL shares will be in Trade-for-Trade (T+1 delivery-based settlement) for 10 trading days from listing
- Participants must follow SPOS procedures for price discovery on listing day
- Company Secretary & Compliance Officer: Ms. Tina Lakhani (Tel: +91 22 6643 4500, Email: visl.cs@vedanta.co.in)

## Impact Assessment

The listing of Vedanta Iron And Steel Limited marks a significant corporate restructuring event, adding approximately 3.91 billion new equity shares to BSE. As a demerger from Vedanta Limited, existing VL shareholders receive VISL shares, creating immediate trading interest. The T Group and Trade-for-Trade classification for 10 days limits speculative intraday trading, ensuring delivery-based settlement and reducing volatility risk during the price discovery phase. The SPOS mechanism on listing day will allow for orderly price discovery. Market participants holding VISL shares must plan for mandatory delivery obligations during the Trade-for-Trade window. The company is headquartered in Mumbai (C-103, Atul Projects, Corporate Avenue, New Link, Chakala MIDC, Mumbai – 400 093).
