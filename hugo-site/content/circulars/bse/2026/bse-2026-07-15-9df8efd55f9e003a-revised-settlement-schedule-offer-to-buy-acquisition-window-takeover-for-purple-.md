## Summary

BSE (via Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd.) issued a revised settlement schedule for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for Purple Finance Limited. This revises the settlement calendar earlier communicated in Notice No. 20260630-27 for the takeover window that was conducted from 01/07/2026 to 14/07/2026.

## Key Points

- Revised settlement schedule issued for Takeover Settlement No. 2026078.
- Bid window ran from 01/07/2026 to 14/07/2026.
- Revised obligation download date: 16/07/2026.
- Revised settlement date: 17/07/2026.
- Demat shares must be delivered only via the Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism, available in both depositories.
- Members/Custodians must use Market Type "(Takeover)" and Settlement No. 2026078 in the depository system.
- Members/Custodians should refer to Notice No. 20200528-32 dated May 28, 2020 for guidelines on acquisition of shares through the Stock Exchange mechanism.
- Client-wise Stamp Duty charges are available in the MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is an operational revision to a previously announced settlement calendar under the existing takeover offer-to-buy mechanism.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members/Custodians must ensure demat shares are delivered exclusively through Early Pay-in of Shares in the depository system, tagging the correct Market Type and Settlement Number.
- Members/Custodians should consult referenced notices for full procedural compliance and stamp duty details.
- Queries to be directed to ICCL Help Desk (022-69158590/45720490/45720690, iccl.helpdesk@icclindia.com).

## Important Dates

- Bid Start Date: 01/07/2026
- Bid End Date: 14/07/2026
- Revised Obligation Download: 16/07/2026
- Revised Settlement Date: 17/07/2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational/administrative revision affecting settlement timelines for the Purple Finance Limited takeover acquisition window. It primarily impacts Members and Custodians handling the settlement process and has minimal direct impact on general market participants or trading activity.
