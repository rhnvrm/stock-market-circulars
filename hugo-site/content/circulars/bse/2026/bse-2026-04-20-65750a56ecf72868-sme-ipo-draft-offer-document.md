## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document (DRHP) of JEE Pumps Limited, filed with the SME Exchange, on the BSE SME website. The document is hosted for a minimum of 21 days as required under Schedule VI of the SEBI (ICDR) Regulations, 2018, inviting public comments.

## Key Points

- JEE Pumps Limited has filed its Draft Offer Document with BSE SME Exchange on 19 April 2026.
- The document is available on the BSE SME website: www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public comments are invited within 21 days from the date of this notice (i.e., by 11 May 2026).
- Comments can be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- The notice was issued under Notice No. 20260420-14 by the DCS-Listing department.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. The process follows existing requirements under Schedule VI of the SEBI (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2018.

## Compliance Requirements

- Interested parties and public investors may review the Draft Offer Document on the BSE SME website.
- Any comments or observations must be submitted within 21 days of this notice to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Date of Filing by Company:** 19 April 2026
- **Notice Date:** 20 April 2026
- **Public Comment Deadline:** 11 May 2026 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine procedural notice with limited market-wide impact. It affects prospective investors and stakeholders interested in the upcoming SME IPO of JEE Pumps Limited. No immediate impact on existing listed securities or trading operations.
