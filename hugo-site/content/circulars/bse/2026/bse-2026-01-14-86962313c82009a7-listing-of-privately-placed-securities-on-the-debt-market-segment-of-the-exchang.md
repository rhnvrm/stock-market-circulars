## Summary

BSE announced the listing of commercial papers issued on private placement basis by five companies on its debt market segment effective January 14, 2026. The listings include fresh issuances from SEIL Energy India Limited, Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited, and Kotak Mahindra Investments Limited, plus further issuances from Julius Baer Capital (India) Private Limited and Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited.

## Key Points

- All securities will be traded only in dematerialised form under respective ISIN numbers
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for securities is 1 paise
- Total of 3 fresh listings and 2 further listings announced
- All commercial papers carry CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings
- Face value and paid-up value for all securities: Rs. 500,000 per unit

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative notification for debt market participants. The listing of these commercial papers provides additional short-term debt instruments for institutional investors. All securities carry highest credit ratings (CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+), indicating strong creditworthiness. The tenures range from 7 days to 146 days, catering to various short-term liquidity needs of investors. Impact is limited to debt market participants and does not affect equity markets.
