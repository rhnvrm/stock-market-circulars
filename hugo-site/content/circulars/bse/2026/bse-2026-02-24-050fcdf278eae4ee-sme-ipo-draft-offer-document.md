## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260224-2 informing that Twinkle Papers Limited has filed its Draft Offer Document (DRHP) on February 23, 2026 under the SME IPO segment. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and is available on the BSE SME website for public review and comments for a minimum period of 21 days.

## Key Points

- Twinkle Papers Limited has filed its Draft Offer Document on February 23, 2026
- The document complies with Schedule VI of the ICDR (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations
- Draft Offer Document is hosted on the BSE SME website: www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public comments are invited within 21 days from the date of the notice (i.e., by March 17, 2026)
- Comments to be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This notice is issued in compliance with existing ICDR Regulations requiring public disclosure of Draft Offer Documents for SME IPOs.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants, analysts, and the public may review the Draft Offer Document on the BSE SME portal
- Any comments or observations on the Draft Offer Document must be submitted within 21 days from February 24, 2026
- Comments to be sent to: equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Draft Offer Document Filed:** February 23, 2026
- **Notice Date:** February 24, 2026
- **Public Comment Deadline:** March 17, 2026 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine procedural notice with low immediate market impact. It signals an upcoming SME IPO by Twinkle Papers Limited on the BSE SME platform. The notice is relevant primarily to potential investors, analysts, and stakeholders interested in the SME IPO pipeline. No impact on existing listed securities or trading operations.
