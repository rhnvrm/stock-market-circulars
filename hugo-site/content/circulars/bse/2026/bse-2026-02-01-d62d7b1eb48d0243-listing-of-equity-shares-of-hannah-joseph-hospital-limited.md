## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that the equity shares of Hannah Joseph Hospital Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544687, symbol HANNAH, and ISIN number INE0JVH01012. The exact listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Hannah Joseph Hospital Limited equity shares to be listed on BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544687
- Symbol: HANNAH
- ISIN: INE0JVH01012
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the upcoming listing and should await the separate notice for listing date and security details before commencing trading activities.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: February 1, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement for an SME company with limited immediate market impact. Trading members need to be aware of the new scrip code and symbol for future trading. The actual market impact will depend on the company's business profile, offering size, and investor interest, which will be clarified when the listing date and security details are announced.
