## Summary

BSE Ltd. has issued this circular to inform investors and market participants about the scheduled visits of Registrar & Transfer Agents (RTAs) at BSE Ltd. during March 2026. The circular provides a detailed list of companies and their associated RTAs, enabling investors to visit the respective RTA representatives at BSE for resolving transfer, demat, and other investor service-related queries.

## Key Points

- Adroit Corporate Services Pvt. Ltd. is listed as an RTA visiting BSE Ltd. in March 2026.
- The circular covers a large number of listed companies serviced by Adroit Corporate Services, including well-known names such as Safari Industries India Ltd (523025), Arihant Superstructures Ltd (506194), LKP Finance Ltd (507912), Scan Steels Ltd (511672), and Securekloud Technologies Ltd (512161).
- Investors in any of the listed companies can approach the respective RTA during their scheduled visit at BSE for grievance redressal or service requests.
- The list is provided in Excel format, organized by RTA name with scrip codes and company long names.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this circular. It is an operational and administrative notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- No specific compliance requirements for listed companies or brokers.
- Investors wishing to avail RTA services in person are advised to visit BSE Ltd. during the scheduled RTA visit dates in March 2026.
- Companies and their RTAs should ensure availability and readiness during the scheduled visit period.

## Important Dates

- Applicable period: March 2026
- Circular date: 2026-03-04
- Specific visit dates within March 2026 are to be confirmed per the detailed schedule (attached Excel file).

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market or trading impact. It is a routine investor-service facilitation notice that helps retail investors connect with their company's RTA for transfer-related services, share certificates, dividend queries, or demat requests. The companies listed span a wide range of sectors including steel, finance, textiles, pharma, and real estate. No price-sensitive information is contained in this circular.
