## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that equity shares of Defrail Technologies Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544677, symbol DEFRAIL, and ISIN INE0ZE201010. The exact listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Defrail Technologies Limited to be listed on BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544677
- Symbol: DEFRAIL
- ISIN: INE0ZE201010
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Security details to be provided in subsequent notice

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the scrip code, symbol, and ISIN for the upcoming listing. Members must await the separate notice containing the listing date and security details before commencing trading.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: January 16, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This announcement has limited market-wide impact as it pertains to a single SME company listing. The impact is primarily relevant to investors and trading members interested in Defrail Technologies Limited. The SME segment listing expands investment opportunities in the small and medium enterprise space but does not affect broader market operations or existing securities.
