## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document of FX MULTITECH LIMITED filed on December 31, 2025, containing all disclosures specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations. The document is available on the stock exchange website for a minimum period of 21 days to solicit public comments.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by FX MULTITECH LIMITED on December 31, 2025
- Document complies with Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website for public review
- Public comment period of at least 21 days from notice date
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard procedural notice under existing ICDR Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public can review the Draft Offer Document at www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Any comments on the Draft Offer Document should be submitted within 21 days from January 01, 2026
- Comments to be sent to: equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Draft Offer Document Filing Date**: December 31, 2025
- **Notice Date**: January 01, 2026
- **Public Comment Deadline**: January 22, 2026 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal market impact as it is a routine procedural disclosure for an SME IPO draft offer document. It is relevant primarily for potential investors interested in FX MULTITECH LIMITED's upcoming SME IPO and stakeholders wishing to provide feedback during the public comment period. No immediate action required for existing market participants.
