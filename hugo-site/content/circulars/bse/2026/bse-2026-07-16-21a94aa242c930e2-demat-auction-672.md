## Summary

BSE Limited has invited offers from trading members for the purchase of securities in Auction 672, pertaining to Rolling Settlement No. DR-672/2026-27. This is a routine settlement process notice covering auction offer entry, matching, delivery, and pay-in schedules.

## Key Points

- Notice No. 20260716-2 issued by BSE Clearing (BSECL) under Settlement/RMS category, General segment.
- Auction offer entry window: 02:00 p.m. to 02:45 p.m. on Thursday, July 16, 2026.
- Auction matching: 02:45 p.m. to 03:00 p.m.
- Delivery statement and report of accepted auction offers: 03:00 p.m. to 03:30 p.m.
- Money statement and final reports: 03:35 p.m. to 05:30 p.m.
- Pay-in of the auction will be held on Friday, July 17, 2026 at 08:00 a.m.
- Members and custodians must check auction pay-in obligations for securities and execute securities pay-in in depositories within depository cut-off timings.
- Following implementation of the Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment, settlement will be done through the Clearing Member of a Trading Member.
- The restriction on trading members offering securities in auction in a settlement where they have short-delivered such securities has been removed.
- Trading members must exercise care while punching securities code and rate; no corrections are permitted for punching errors.
- The Exchange reserves the right to accept or reject any offer without assigning reasons.
- Trading members should refer to the cutoff rate on the Auction Offer Entry Screen and account for Corporate Action notices (Ex-Rights/Ex-Bonus/Ex-Split, etc.) issued for the relevant securities.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this notice reflects a previously implemented policy update removing the restriction on trading members offering securities in auction in the settlement in which they had short-delivered such securities, following the Uniform Membership structure implementation in the Equity Cash Segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and custodians must submit auction offers within the specified time windows via trading terminals.
- Accurate entry of securities code and rate is required, as punching errors cannot be corrected.
- Members must ensure timely securities pay-in through depositories within depository cut-off timings.
- Settlement will be routed through the Clearing Member of the respective Trading Member.

## Important Dates

- Auction offer entry: July 16, 2026, 02:00 p.m. - 02:45 p.m.
- Auction matching: July 16, 2026, 02:45 p.m. - 03:00 p.m.
- Delivery statement and reports: July 16, 2026, 03:00 p.m. - 03:30 p.m.
- Money statement and final reports: July 16, 2026, 03:35 p.m. - 05:30 p.m.
- Auction pay-in: July 17, 2026, 08:00 a.m.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, procedural market operations notice with limited impact confined to trading members and custodians participating in the auction settlement process for Rolling Settlement DR-672/2026-27. It does not directly affect any specific listed company's stock price or broader market sentiment, though it is operationally significant for members with short-delivery obligations in this settlement cycle.
