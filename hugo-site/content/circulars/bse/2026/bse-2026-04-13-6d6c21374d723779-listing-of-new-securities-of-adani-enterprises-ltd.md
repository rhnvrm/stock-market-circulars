## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260413-22 dated 13 April 2026 regarding the listing of new securities of Adani Enterprises Ltd. on the equity segment. This is an administrative notice from the Listing Operations department.

## Key Points

- Notice No.: 20260413-22
- Date: 13 April 2026
- Company: Adani Enterprises Ltd.
- Segment: Equity
- Department: Listing Operations
- Category: Company Related
- No attachments accompany this notice

## Regulatory Changes

No specific regulatory changes indicated. This is a standard listing notice for newly issued securities of Adani Enterprises Ltd.

## Compliance Requirements

Market participants, brokers, and traders should note the admission of new securities of Adani Enterprises Ltd. for trading on the BSE equity segment and update their systems accordingly.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 13 April 2026
- Listing effective as per the notice date; specific trading commencement date to be confirmed from the full circular.

## Impact Assessment

The listing of new securities of Adani Enterprises Ltd. (NSE/BSE ticker: ADANIENT) on the equity segment increases the float available for trading. As Adani Enterprises is a large-cap conglomerate, new securities listing may have moderate impact on share price and trading volumes depending on the nature and size of the issuance (e.g., rights issue, preferential allotment, or bonus shares). Investors and traders should refer to the full circular for specific details on the number and nature of new securities being listed.
