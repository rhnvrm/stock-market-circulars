## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 1,76,20,550 new equity shares of Lloyds Metals and Energy Limited (Scrip Code: 512455) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Wednesday, June 10, 2026. These shares were issued at a premium of Rs. 739/- (issue price Rs. 740/-) per share to non-promoters on a preferential basis, pursuant to the conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 1,76,20,550 equity shares of face value Rs. 1/- each listed effective June 10, 2026
- Issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis via conversion of warrants
- Issue price: Rs. 740/- per share (face value Rs. 1/- + premium Rs. 739/-)
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Date of allotment: March 13, 2026
- ISIN: INE281B01032
- Distribution numbers: 545164539 to 562785088
- All 1,76,20,550 shares are subject to lock-in until January 5, 2027

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This notice is a standard listing notification for securities already allotted, now permitted to trade on BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members may deal in these securities from June 10, 2026 onwards
- The lock-in restriction on all 1,76,20,550 shares must be observed until January 5, 2027; these shares cannot be transferred or traded during the lock-in period

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** March 13, 2026
- **Listing / Trading Effective Date:** June 10, 2026 (Wednesday)
- **Lock-in Expiry Date:** January 5, 2027

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds approximately 1.76 crore equity shares to the BSE-listed capital of Lloyds Metals and Energy Limited. Since all newly listed shares are under lock-in until January 5, 2027, there is no immediate increase in tradable float. The preferential allotment at Rs. 740/- per share to non-promoters through warrant conversion indicates prior fundraising activity completed in March 2026. Market impact at the time of listing is expected to be limited given the lock-in, but the dilution becomes relevant post January 2027 when the shares are released.
