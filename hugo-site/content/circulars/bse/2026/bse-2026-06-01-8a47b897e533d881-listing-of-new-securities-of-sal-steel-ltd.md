## Summary

BSE has listed 48,00,000 new equity shares of S.A.L. Steel Ltd. (Scrip Code: 532604, ISIN: INE658G01014) issued to a promoter on a preferential basis through conversion of warrants. The shares are permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Tuesday, June 2, 2026, though they remain under lock-in until December 15, 2027.

## Key Points

- 48,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 15/- (issue price Rs. 25/-) to a promoter via warrant conversion
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 104216701 to 109016700
- Date of allotment: December 28, 2025
- Effective date for trading on BSE: June 2, 2026
- All 48,00,000 shares are under lock-in until December 15, 2027

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice following a preferential allotment under SEBI regulations governing conversion of warrants.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed that these securities are now listed and permitted to trade. The lock-in restriction must be observed — the entire allotment of 48,00,000 shares (dist. nos. 104216701 to 109016700) is locked in until December 15, 2027 and cannot be freely traded by the promoter during this period.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** December 28, 2025
- **Effective Trading Date:** June 2, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry:** December 15, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Market impact is minimal. The entire allotment is subject to promoter lock-in until December 2027, meaning no additional floating stock enters the market. The preferential allotment increases total paid-up equity capital of S.A.L. Steel Ltd. by 48,00,000 shares at Rs. 25/- per share (Rs. 12 crore).
