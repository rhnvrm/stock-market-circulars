## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Mitcon Credentia Trusteeship Services Ltd has communicated the reduced face value on partial redemption of Pass-through Certificates (PTCs) issued under TRINITY TRUST SEP 2025. The PTCs will trade with the new reduced face value effective May 25, 2026.

## Key Points

- Partial redemption of PTCs for TRINITY TRUST SEP 2025 has been processed
- Reduced face value is Rs. 4,132.44 per PTC
- Security: TTS25-11.75%-21-9-27-PTC (Scrip Code: 977166, ISIN: INE2JU715017)
- Trustee: Mitcon Credentia Trusteeship Services Ltd
- Reference: DR-637/2026-2027

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notice communicating the updated face value of existing debt securities following a scheduled partial redemption event.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are required to take note of the revised reduced face value of Rs. 4,132.44 per PTC for TRINITY TRUST SEP 2025 PTCs and ensure trades are executed reflecting the updated face value from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** May 22, 2026
- **Effective Date:** May 25, 2026 (PTCs to be traded with reduced face value from this date)

## Impact Assessment

This partial redemption reduces the face value of each PTC in TRINITY TRUST SEP 2025 to Rs. 4,132.44, which will be reflected in the trading price from May 25, 2026. Impact is limited to holders and traders of this specific debt instrument (Scrip Code 977166). The change is routine in nature for pass-through certificate structures where principal is periodically returned to investors.
