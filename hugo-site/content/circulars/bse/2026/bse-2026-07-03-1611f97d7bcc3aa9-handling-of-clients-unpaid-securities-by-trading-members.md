## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260703-20 dated July 03, 2026, drawing the attention of trading members to a SEBI circular (ref. no. HO/38/11/(9)2026-MIRSD-POD/I/15382/2026) dated July 03, 2026, regarding the "Handling of Client's Unpaid Securities by Trading Members." A copy of the SEBI circular is enclosed as Annexure_A. Members are required to take note of the contents and ensure compliance.

## Key Points

- BSE forwards SEBI circular ref. no. HO/38/11/(9)2026-MIRSD-POD/I/15382/2026 dated July 03, 2026 on handling of client's unpaid securities by trading members.
- The full SEBI circular is attached as Annexure_1/Annexure_A.
- Trading members are directed to review and comply with the SEBI circular's requirements.
- Issued by BSE's Department of Surveillance & Supervision (DOSS), Member Oversight function.

## Regulatory Changes

The notice itself does not detail the specific regulatory changes; these are contained in the referenced SEBI circular (HO/38/11/(9)2026-MIRSD-POD/I/15382/2026) attached as an annexure, which prescribes the framework for handling of client's unpaid securities by trading members.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must review the enclosed SEBI circular and ensure their internal processes for handling client's unpaid securities align with the prescribed requirements. Queries or clarifications can be directed to BSE's Member Oversight team via the provided contact numbers (022-69158540, 022-45720640, 022-45720440) or email (Bse.inspection@bseindia.com).

## Important Dates

- Notice/Circular Date: July 03, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a compliance-oriented circular applicable to all BSE trading members, requiring adherence to SEBI's framework on client's unpaid securities. It does not directly impact specific stocks or trading activity but reinforces regulatory obligations around client asset handling, which is relevant to operational and compliance risk management for member firms.
