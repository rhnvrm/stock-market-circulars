## Summary

BSE Limited, through Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd. (ICCL), has announced Demat Auction 650 for Rolling Settlement No. DR-650/2026-27. Trading members are invited to submit offers on trading terminals on June 15, 2026 for purchase of securities in auction.

## Key Points

- Auction offer entry window: 2:00 PM to 2:45 PM on June 15, 2026
- Auction matching session: 2:45 PM to 3:00 PM
- Delivery statement and accepted auction offer reports: 3:00 PM to 3:30 PM
- Money statement and final reports: 3:35 PM to 5:30 PM
- Auction pay-in scheduled for Tuesday, June 16, 2026 at 8:00 AM
- Members and Custodians must execute securities pay-in in depositories within depository cut-off timings
- No corrections will be permitted for punching errors in security codes or rates
- The Exchange reserves the right to accept or reject any offer without assigning reasons

## Regulatory Changes

Following implementation of Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment, settlement is now routed through the Clearing Member of a Trading Member. The earlier restriction on trading members offering securities in auction within the same settlement in which they have short-delivered has been removed.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must submit auction offers only through trading terminals during the designated entry window (2:00 PM to 2:45 PM)
- Members and Custodians are required to check their auction pay-in obligations for securities and complete securities pay-in in depositories within depository cut-off timings on June 16, 2026
- Trading members must exercise utmost care when entering security codes and rates, as no corrections will be permitted after submission
- Members should refer to the cutoff rate displayed on the Auction Offer Entry Screen before submitting offers
- Members should consult notices issued regarding Corporate Actions (Ex-Rights, Ex-Bonus, Ex-Split, etc.) for relevant scrips before submitting offers

## Important Dates

- **June 15, 2026**: Auction offer entry (2:00 PM – 2:45 PM), Matching (2:45 PM – 3:00 PM), Delivery statements (3:00 PM – 3:30 PM), Final reports (3:35 PM – 5:30 PM)
- **June 16, 2026**: Auction pay-in at 8:00 AM

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine demat auction notice with limited market-wide impact. It directly concerns trading members who have short-delivered securities in Rolling Settlement DR-650/2026-27 and those who wish to participate by offering securities for purchase. The removal of the restriction allowing short-delivering members to now offer in the same settlement auction (under the Uniform Membership structure) slightly broadens auction participation eligibility.
