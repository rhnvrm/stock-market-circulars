## Summary

BSE has published the Demat Auction list for Settlement No. 831 dated March 4, 2026. This auction covers securities that were short-delivered in the normal settlement cycle. A total of approximately 87 scrips are listed for auction with varying quantities.

## Key Points

- Settlement No. 831 auction held on March 4, 2026
- Covers short deliveries from the normal market settlement
- 87 scrips listed spanning large-cap, mid-cap, and small-cap companies
- Notable quantities include EVEXIA LIFECARE (83,932 shares), SYLPH INDUSTRIES (217,789 shares), and VIRAM SUVARNKAR (14,744 shares)
- Large-cap stocks such as Adani Enterprises (228), HDFC Bank (1), Hero MotoCorp (150), Maruti Suzuki (158), and Reliance Industries (1) are also included

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants with short delivery positions in the listed scrips must participate in or be aware of the auction process
- Buyers of auctioned securities will receive shares at auction prices; any shortfall is settled in cash at close-out rates
- Brokers must inform their clients of any positions subject to auction

## Important Dates

- Auction Date: March 4, 2026
- Settlement No.: 831

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a routine settlement mechanism to address short deliveries. The auction affects small quantities of shares across a broad set of scrips. Notably, SYLPH INDUSTRIES (217,789 shares) and EVEXIA LIFECARE (83,932 shares) have relatively large quantities under auction, which may have minor price impact for those specific securities. Overall, no systemic or broad market implications.
