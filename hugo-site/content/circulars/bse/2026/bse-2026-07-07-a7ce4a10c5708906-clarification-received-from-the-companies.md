## Summary

BSE observed significant price/volume movement in 20 listed scrips in the recent past and, as part of its surveillance process, wrote to the respective companies seeking clarification. Replies have been received from 16 companies and are attached to the notice, while responses from 4 companies remain awaited.

## Key Points

- Companies that have submitted replies (16): Mazda Ltd (523792), Credent Global Finance Ltd (539598), Elango Industries Ltd (513452), MPS Ltd (532440), Mayur Leather Products Ltd (531680), Platinum Industries Ltd (544134), Savita Oil Technologies Ltd (524667), TeleCanor Global Ltd (530595), Nurture Well Industries Ltd (531889), Spectrum Electrical Industries Ltd (544386), IKIO Technologies Ltd (543923), Swan Corp Ltd (503310), Tuticorin Alkali Chemicals and Fertilizers Ltd (506808), TAAL Tech Ltd (539956), Amir Chand Jagdish Kumar (Exports) Ltd (544743), Bandaram Pharma Packtech Ltd (524602)
- Companies whose replies are still awaited (4): CMX Holdings Ltd (532217), LE Lavoir Ltd (539814), SC Agrotech Ltd (526081), Sujala Trading & Holdings Ltd (539117)
- The clarifications were sought to ensure trading members and investors have the latest relevant information about these scrips
- Attachments containing the actual replies (Replies1 to Replies4) are provided with the notice

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a routine surveillance disclosure under BSE's monitoring of price/volume movements.

## Compliance Requirements

Companies with pending replies (CMX Holdings, LE Lavoir, SC Agrotech, Sujala Trading & Holdings) are expected to respond to the Exchange's clarification request in due course.

## Important Dates

Notice dated 07 July 2026.

## Impact Assessment

Investors and trading members in the listed scrips should review the attached company clarifications for material information that may explain recent price/volume volatility. The four companies with pending replies warrant continued monitoring as unexplained price/volume movement without clarification can indicate elevated risk or potential surveillance action.
