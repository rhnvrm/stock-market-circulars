## Summary

BSE has informed members that AXIS Mutual Fund will offer the AXIS NIFTY50 Equal Weight Index Fund as a New Fund Offer (NFO) with SIP facility on the BSE StAR MF Platform from July 03, 2026 to July 17, 2026. The scheme is available in Regular and Direct Plan (Growth) options, both categorized under Equity.

## Key Points

- Scheme: AXIS NIFTY50 Equal Weight Index Fund - Regular Plan - Growth (ISIN: INF846KA1556) and Direct Plan - Growth (ISIN: INF846KA1564)
- Scheme Category: Equity
- Tentative allotment date for both plans: July 23, 2026
- NFO available for subscription with SIP facility on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Transactions without funds initiated by 9:00 AM on T+1 day (including extended NFO period transactions) will be cancelled, with CC flagged N reported to RTA/AMC
- Transactions with initiated payment remain pending until 3:00 PM on the last day of funds pay-out or T+5 day, whichever is earlier
- Cheque and OTM payments accepted during NFO period, subject to clear funds by 6:00 PM on July 23, 2026
- Switch transactions accepted during NFO period, subject to funds received before allotment date; switch-out funds received on/after allotment date will not be considered for allotment

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is an operational notice regarding a new scheme launch and associated transaction handling instructions on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must ensure clear funds are available in the ICCL account by 6:00 PM on July 23, 2026 for allotment eligibility. Members must monitor fund initiation timelines to avoid transaction cancellation, and ensure SIP/XSIP registrations are authenticated by clients before the specified deadline.

## Important Dates

- NFO Period: July 03, 2026 to July 17, 2026
- Lumpsum purchase extension: till midnight July 17, 2026
- SIP/XSIP authentication deadline: 3:00 PM on July 17, 2026
- Tentative allotment date: July 23, 2026
- Clear funds deadline: 6:00 PM on July 23, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This notice has operational relevance primarily for mutual fund distributors and BSE StAR MF Platform members handling transactions for the new AXIS index fund scheme. It does not directly affect listed equity securities or broader trading activity, and carries low market impact.
