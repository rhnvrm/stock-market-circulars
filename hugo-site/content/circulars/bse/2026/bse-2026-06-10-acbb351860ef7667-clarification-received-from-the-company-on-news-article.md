## Summary

BSE's Surveillance Department (DOSS) sought clarification from Coromandel International Ltd (Scrip Code: 506395) in response to news articles appearing across newspapers, websites, and television channels. The Exchange received the company's reply as part of its standard process to verify media reports and keep members and investors informed about accuracy.

## Key Points

- BSE proactively monitors news coverage of listed companies and seeks clarifications to protect investor interests.
- Coromandel International Ltd (Scrip Code: 506395) was asked to clarify news appearing on The Hindu (thehindu.com).
- The company's clarification/reply has been received and is attached to the notice.
- The notice is issued by the Surveillance Department under Deputy Vice President Sanjay Jain and Manager Shreya Ghose.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine surveillance and investor information measure.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance obligations. The company has already responded with its clarification. Members and investors are advised to review the attached reply for accurate information on the underlying news.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 10 June 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard BSE surveillance procedure to verify media reports and disseminate accurate information. The outcome depends on the content of Coromandel International's clarification, which investors should review directly.
