## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that a new privately placed debt security issued by HDB Financial Services Limited has been listed and admitted to trading on the Debt Segment of the Exchange, effective July 24, 2026.

## Key Points

- Security is listed in dematerialised form only, under the specified ISIN.
- Tick size for the security is 1 paise.
- Trading members can refer to the Placement Memorandum on BSE's debt market data page for further details.
- Issuing company: HDB Financial Services Limited (Scrip Code: 977860, Scrip ID: 823HFSL29).
- Instrument detail name: HFSL-8.2301%-5-7-29-PVT, ISIN: INE756I07FQ4.
- Quantity: 40,000 units; Market Lot: 1.
- Credit Rating: CARE AAA/Stable, CRISIL AAA/Stable.
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000; Paid-up Value: Rs. 1,00,000; Issue Price: Rs. 1,01,191.20.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing notification under existing debt segment listing procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should update systems to recognize the new ISIN for trading and settlement, and may contact BSE's debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: 23/07/2026
- Listing/Effective Date: 24/07/2026
- Interest Payment Dates: Annually from 05/07/2027 to 05/07/2029
- Date of Redemption: 05/07/2029

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative listing notice with minimal broader market impact. It primarily affects trading members and investors dealing in HDB Financial Services' privately placed debt instruments, enabling dematerialised trading in the new ISIN on BSE's Debt Segment.
