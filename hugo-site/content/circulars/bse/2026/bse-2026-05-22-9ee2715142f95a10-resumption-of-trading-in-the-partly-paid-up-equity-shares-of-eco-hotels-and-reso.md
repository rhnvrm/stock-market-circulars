## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260522-46 informs trading members that trading in 85,45,990 partly paid-up equity shares of Eco Hotels and Resorts Limited (scrip code 890225) will resume effective 27th May 2026 (DR-839/2026-2027). The resumption follows the receipt of Second Call Money and is a continuation of the earlier Exchange Notice No. 20260317-9 dated 17th March 2026.

## Key Points

- Trading in 85,45,990 partly paid-up equity shares of Eco Hotels and Resorts Limited resumes w.e.f. 27th May 2026
- New ISIN assigned: IN9638N01036
- Market lot: 1
- Shares are Face Value Rs. 10/- each, issued on a Rights basis
- Shares are partly paid up to Rs. 7.50 per share
- Second Call Money received: Rs. 3.80 per partly paid equity share, comprising Rs. 2.50 per share toward Face Value and Rs. 1.30 toward Securities Premium
- A Synopsis (SynopsisEcoHotels270526) containing details of partly paid shares available for trading is attached to the notice

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice operationalises the previously communicated reinstatement of trading following the corporate action (second call money receipt) under the ongoing partly paid shares framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are required to take note of the resumption of trading effective 27th May 2026
- Members should update their systems with the new ISIN (IN9638N01036) for the partly paid shares
- Members should refer to the attached Synopsis (SynopsisEcoHotels270526) for full details of the partly paid shares available for trading

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 22nd May 2026
- **Trading Resumption Date:** 27th May 2026
- **Reference Notice:** BSE Notice No. 20260317-9 dated 17th March 2026

## Impact Assessment

This notice has a medium operational impact on trading members dealing in Eco Hotels and Resorts Limited partly paid-up shares. Members must ensure their trading systems recognise the new ISIN (IN9638N01036) from 27th May 2026. The resumption reflects a normal corporate action milestone (second call money collection) and does not indicate any regulatory concern. Market impact is limited to participants in this specific scrip.
