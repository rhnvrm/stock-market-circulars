## Summary

BSE Trading Operations has issued a notice (No. 20260608-46) detailing the live activities schedule for the opening of the Offer for Sale (OFS) for NLC India Limited. This circular is directed at trading members and market participants involved in the OFS process on the equity segment.

## Key Points

- Notice issued by BSE Department of Trading Operations on 08 June 2026
- Pertains to the Offer for Sale (OFS) opening for NLC India Limited (NLCINDIA)
- Provides the live activities schedule to guide market participants through the OFS process
- Segment: Equity; Category: Trading
- No attachments included with this notice

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced. This is an operational circular providing activity timelines for the NLC India OFS event.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members and market participants intending to participate in the NLC India Limited OFS are required to follow the live activities schedule as specified. Members should ensure systems and order management processes are aligned with the announced timelines.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 08 June 2026
- OFS Opening: 08 June 2026 (as indicated by the live activities schedule notice)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a moderate operational impact for trading members and investors participating in the NLC India OFS. The live activities schedule ensures orderly execution of the offer, and adherence to the schedule is important for brokers and their clients bidding in the OFS. NLC India Limited (NLCINDIA) is a government-owned entity, and OFS events typically attract institutional and retail investor participation. No broader market disruption is anticipated.
