## Summary

BSE received clarifications from three listed companies - Emmvee Photovoltaic Power Limited (Scrip Code: 544608), Indian Metals & Ferro Alloys Limited (Stock Code: 533047), and Manaksia Aluminium Company Limited (Scrip Code: 539045) - in response to BSE Surveillance Department's queries regarding increased volume/price movements in their securities. All three companies confirmed compliance with SEBI LODR Regulation 30, stated no undisclosed material information, and attributed the volume/price behavior to market-driven conditions.

## Key Points

- Emmvee Photovoltaic Power Limited responded to BSE letter L/SURV/ONL/PV/APJ/2025-2026/846 dated January 07, 2026
- Indian Metals & Ferro Alloys Limited responded to BSE letter L/SURV/ONL/PV/APJ/2025-2026/848 dated January 7, 2026
- All companies confirmed full compliance with Regulation 30 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015
- No material information or events withheld that could impact price/volume behavior
- Companies attribute volume/price movements to market conditions and market-driven forces
- All companies confirmed no pending compliance requirements

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies are required to respond to BSE surveillance queries regarding unusual volume/price movements
- Must confirm compliance with SEBI LODR Regulation 30 regarding disclosure of material events
- Need to clarify whether any undisclosed information exists that may impact securities behavior

## Important Dates

- January 07, 2026: Date of clarifications submitted by all three companies

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine surveillance and disclosure exercise with minimal market impact. The clarifications confirm that the observed volume/price movements in these securities are market-driven rather than triggered by undisclosed material events. This provides transparency to investors and confirms regulatory compliance by the listed entities. No corrective actions or concerns identified.
