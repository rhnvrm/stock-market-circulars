## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that three new Treasury Bills (T-Bills) will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange under the G GROUP - DEBT INSTRUMENTS segment effective May 4, 2026. The newly listed instruments cover 91-day, 182-day, and 364-day tenors.

## Key Points

- Three new T-Bills listed effective May 4, 2026 on BSE's G GROUP - DEBT INSTRUMENTS segment
- Scrip codes: 805208, 805209, and 805210
- Market lot for all three instruments is 1
- Trading will be suspended two working days prior to each instrument's maturity/redemption date (excluding bank holidays)
- Contact numbers for clarifications: 2272 8352 / 5753 / 8597

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notice for newly issued government Treasury Bills.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must note that the listed securities will not be available for trading two working days prior to the Maturity/Redemption Date (Maturity Date minus two trading days, excluding bank holidays).

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice with minimal market impact. The listing of these three T-Bills provides additional short-term government debt instruments for trading members and investors on BSE's debt segment. The instruments span 91-day, 182-day, and 364-day maturities, offering liquidity across multiple short-term horizons. No action is required from trading members beyond noting the listing date and the pre-maturity trading blackout period.
