## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that, in continuation of its earlier Notice No. 20260722-34 dated July 22, 2026, the equity shares of Sotefin Bharat Limited (Scrip Code: 544832) will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment with effect from Thursday, August 06, 2026. Consequently, dealings in the company's shares will be shifted to the M Group.

## Key Points

- Scrip: Sotefin Bharat Limited, Scrip Code 544832
- Segment change: Trade for Trade (MT Group) → Rolling segment
- New trading group: M Group
- This is a follow-up to BSE Notice No. 20260722-34 dated July 22, 2026
- Trading members can contact Mr. Anurag Jain (Tel: 022-2272 8822) for further details

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced; this is an administrative reclassification of the security's trading segment under BSE's existing SME segment rules, moving it out of the more restrictive Trade for Trade mechanism into the Rolling settlement segment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must update their systems to reflect the change in trading group from MT Group to M Group for this scrip effective the stated date. No additional compliance filing is required from the company itself as per this notice.

## Important Dates

- July 22, 2026: Original Notice No. 20260722-34 issued
- July 23, 2026: Current confirmation notice issued
- August 06, 2026 (Thursday): Effective date of segment transfer to Rolling segment / M Group

## Impact Assessment

The move from Trade for Trade to the Rolling segment typically indicates reduced settlement restrictions, allowing intraday trading and squaring-off positions, which was not permitted under Trade for Trade. This is generally viewed as a positive operational development for liquidity and trading flexibility in the stock, though impact is limited to this specific SME-listed company and its existing shareholders/traders rather than the broader market.
