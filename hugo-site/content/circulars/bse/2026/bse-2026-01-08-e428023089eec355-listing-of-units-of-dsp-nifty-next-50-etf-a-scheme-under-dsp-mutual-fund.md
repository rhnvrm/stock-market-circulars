## Summary

BSE announces the listing of DSP Nifty Next 50 ETF units effective January 9, 2026. The open-ended scheme tracks the Nifty Next 50 Index with 922,004 units allotted on January 5, 2026 at Rs. 70.41 per unit. Trading will be conducted exclusively in dematerialized form in the 'B' Group with a market lot of 1 unit. Thirteen market makers have been appointed to provide liquidity.

## Key Points

- **Scrip Code**: 544674
- **ISIN**: INF740KA1XU7
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10.00 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 70.41 per unit
- **Total Units**: 922,004 units
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Group**: B
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only (as per SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000)
- **BOLT System ID**: NEXT50ADD
- **Investment Manager**: DSP Asset Managers Private Limited
- **Registrar**: CAMS (Computer Age Management Services Limited)

## Market Makers

Thirteen market makers appointed:
1. Kanjalochana Finserve Pvt Ltd
2. Share India Securities Ltd

4. Choice Equity Broking Private Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. Standard listing follows SEBI guidelines requiring mutual fund units to be traded exclusively in dematerialized form.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all trades are executed in dematerialized form only
- Trades must be effected in unit market lot (1 unit minimum)
- Market makers must provide liquidity as per their obligations

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: January 5, 2026
- **Listing Date**: January 9, 2026 (Friday)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine ETF listing that provides investors with another passive investment vehicle tracking the Nifty Next 50 Index. The presence of 13 market makers ensures adequate liquidity for trading. Impact is limited to mutual fund segment participants and investors interested in mid-cap equity exposure through ETF route.
