## Summary

BSE announces a demat auction for Settlement No. 803 dated January 22, 2026. The circular lists 102 scrips across various sectors where securities will be auctioned due to delivery shortfalls. Quantities range from 1 share to 9,715 shares depending on the scrip. Major quantities include Reliance Power (9,715 shares), Bharat Heavy Electricals (3,785 shares), Jubilant Ingrevia (3,750 shares), Reliance Industries (576 shares), and BHARAT COKING (2,650 shares).

## Key Points

- Settlement number: 803
- Auction date: January 22, 2026
- Total scrips covered: 102 securities
- Highest quantity: Reliance Power - 9,715 shares
- Sectors affected: Diversified including pharmaceuticals, engineering, finance, power, metals, technology, and consumer goods
- Notable large quantity auctions: BHEL (3,785), Jubilant Ingrevia (3,750), IFCI (2,800), RNIT AI Solutions (2,200), KPI Green Energy (1,740), Hindustan Copper (1,656)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard operational auction notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members with delivery obligations in Settlement 803 must participate in the auction process
- Members must ensure adequate funds/securities for auction settlement
- Participants must comply with BSE's auction mechanism and timelines
- Clearing members responsible for their clients' delivery shortfalls must arrange securities through the auction

## Important Dates

- Settlement No.: 803
- Circular Date: January 22, 2026
- Auction execution date: To be conducted as per BSE's standard auction timeline for the settlement

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - Affects 102 scrips with varying liquidity levels. Large quantity auctions in liquid stocks like Reliance Industries, BHEL, and Reliance Power may see price volatility during auction execution.

**Operational Impact:** Medium - Brokers with delivery shortfalls must arrange securities through auction, potentially at unfavorable prices. Penalty costs will apply for failed deliveries.

**Participant Impact:** Trading members with open positions in these scrips for Settlement 803 face potential auction buy-in costs and penalties if they cannot fulfill delivery obligations through normal means.

**Systemic Risk:** Low - Standard auction mechanism to ensure settlement integrity and delivery completion.
