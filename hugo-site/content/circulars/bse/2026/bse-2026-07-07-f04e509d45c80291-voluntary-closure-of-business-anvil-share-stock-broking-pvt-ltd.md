## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that Anvil Share & Stock Broking Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 32) has been deactivated in the Cash and Equity Derivative segments effective Tuesday, July 07, 2026, following the member's voluntary closure of business.

## Key Points

- Anvil Share & Stock Broking Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 32) has ceased operations voluntarily.
- Deactivation applies to both Cash and Equity Derivative segments.
- Deactivation is effective from July 07, 2026.
- The notice was issued by the Membership Department, signed by Amit Kadam, Assistant Vice President.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is an administrative notice regarding member deactivation, not a change in regulation.

## Compliance Requirements

No action is required from other trading members or investors. Queries related to the notice can be directed to membership.ops@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- July 07, 2026: Effective date of deactivation of Anvil Share & Stock Broking Pvt. Ltd. in Cash and Equity Derivative segments.

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal market impact as it pertains solely to the administrative deactivation of a single trading member due to voluntary business closure. There is no effect on listed securities, trading operations, or other market participants.
