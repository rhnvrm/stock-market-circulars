## Summary

BSE Clearing Limited (BSECL) has announced the go-live of a Pledge Release API that enables direct system-level integration between BSECL and the Depositories for processing the release of pledged securities. This notice, issued under the Settlement/RMS category, pertains to general market segment operations and was released by the BSECL department with no attachments.

## Key Points

- BSECL has implemented a new API-based mechanism for pledge release processing.
- The API establishes direct connectivity between BSE Clearing Limited and Depositories (NSDL/CDSL).
- This is an operational/technical enhancement aimed at streamlining the pledge release workflow.
- The notice does not specify particular stocks or securities affected.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory requirement is introduced; this is an operational go-live of a system integration (API) between BSECL and Depositories to support existing pledge/margin frameworks.

## Compliance Requirements

Clearing members, depository participants, and custodians dealing with pledged securities should ensure their systems and processes are aligned with the new API-based pledge release mechanism. No specific action is detailed for retail investors.

## Important Dates

Go-live effective as per Notice No. 20260713-1 dated 13 July 2026.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is primarily operational, improving efficiency and turnaround time for releasing pledged securities via automated API integration between BSECL and depositories. There is no direct market-moving impact or effect on specific listed stocks, though it may benefit collateral management processes for clearing members and depository participants.
