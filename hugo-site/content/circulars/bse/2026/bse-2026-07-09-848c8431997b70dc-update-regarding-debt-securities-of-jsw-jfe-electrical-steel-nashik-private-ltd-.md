## Summary

BSE has issued a notice in continuation of its earlier Exchange Notice No. 20250429-27 dated April 29, 2025, regarding the debt securities of JSW JFE Electrical Steel Nashik Private Ltd (formerly known as JSQUARE Electrical Steel Nashik Private Limited). Trading Members have been informed that the debt security bearing Scrip Code 976360 and ISIN INE1EY607018 will be suspended from trading on account of debt restructuring, as detailed in the accompanying Annexure, with effect from Tuesday, July 14, 2026.

## Key Points

- The notice pertains to debt securities of JSW JFE Electrical Steel Nashik Private Ltd, formerly JSQUARE Electrical Steel Nashik Private Limited.
- Scrip Code 976360 (ISIN INE1EY607018) will be suspended from trading due to debt restructuring.
- The suspension follows and continues from Exchange Notice No. 20250429-27 dated April 29, 2025.
- Further details of the restructuring are provided in the Annexure attached to the notice.
- Trading Members may contact Ms. Bhairavi Bondale (+91 22 2272 8597, debt.listing@bseindia.com) for further information.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework changes; this is an administrative action by BSE's DCS-Listing department to suspend trading in a specific debt security as part of an ongoing debt restructuring process for the issuer.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members are required to note the suspension and cease trading in Scrip Code 976360 (ISIN INE1EY607018) effective the specified date. Members should refer to the Annexure for details of the debt restructuring and contact the listed BSE officials for clarifications if needed.

## Important Dates

- Original Notice Reference: April 29, 2025 (Notice No. 20250429-27)
- Notice Date: July 09, 2026
- Trading Suspension Effective Date: Tuesday, July 14, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The suspension directly affects investors and Trading Members holding or dealing in this specific debt instrument (Scrip Code 976360) of JSW JFE Electrical Steel Nashik Private Ltd, as they will be unable to trade the security on the exchange after July 14, 2026. The broader market impact is limited, as this concerns a single privately placed debt security undergoing restructuring rather than a market-wide measure.
