## Summary

BSE has listed 4,20,00,000 (4.20 crore) new equity shares of LCC Infotech Ltd. (Scrip Code: 532019, ISIN: INE938A01021), issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis at an issue price of Rs. 4.55 per share (face value Rs. 2/- plus premium of Rs. 2.55/-). These shares are permitted to trade on BSE with effect from June 16, 2026.

## Key Points

- 4,20,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 2.55/- per share (total issue price: Rs. 4.55/-)
- Issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 126593351 to 168593350
- Date of allotment: March 6, 2026
- All 4,20,00,000 shares are subject to lock-in until December 20, 2027

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative listing notice under BSE Listing Operations.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed that the newly listed shares (Dist. Nos. 126593351 to 168593350) are available for trading from June 16, 2026. Members should note the lock-in restriction on all 4,20,00,000 shares until December 20, 2027, meaning these shares cannot be sold or transferred until the lock-in period expires.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** March 6, 2026
- **Listing / Trading Effective Date:** June 16, 2026 (Tuesday)
- **Lock-in Expiry Date:** December 20, 2027

## Impact Assessment

The listing represents a preferential allotment to non-promoters, expanding the total share capital of LCC Infotech Ltd. The issue price of Rs. 4.55/- provides context for the valuation at the time of allotment. The lock-in period until December 2027 means these shares will not add to tradeable float immediately, limiting near-term dilution impact. Overall market impact is minimal given the small-cap nature of the company.
