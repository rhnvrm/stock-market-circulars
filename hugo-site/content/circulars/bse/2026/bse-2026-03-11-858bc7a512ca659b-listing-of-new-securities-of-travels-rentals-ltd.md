## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that new equity shares of Travels & Rentals Ltd. (Scrip Code: 544242) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Thursday, March 12, 2026. The shares were issued on a rights basis at a premium.

## Key Points

- 1,01,52,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 5/- on rights basis are now listed
- Trading permitted from March 12, 2026
- ISIN: INE0SG001011
- Distribution numbers: 11202686 to 21354685
- Date of allotment: March 9, 2026
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification under existing BSE listing procedures for rights issue securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed to note the listing and enable trading in the new securities of Travels & Rentals Ltd. from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** March 9, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** March 12, 2026 (Thursday)

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 1,01,52,000 new equity shares to the tradeable float of Travels & Rentals Ltd. on BSE. Since these shares are pari-passu with existing shares, there is no differential treatment. The rights issue increases the company's share capital and may have a minor dilutive effect on existing shareholders. Impact is limited to investors and traders of this specific scrip.
