## Summary

BSE has announced the schedule for Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for Harmony Capital Services Limited. The offer will run from February 11, 2026, to February 25, 2026, with an offer price of Rs. 10.00 per share (face value Rs. 10.00). The acquirer is seeking up to 31,52,994 equity shares through the exchange platform.

## Key Points

- **Security**: Harmony Capital Services Limited (Symbol: HRMNYCP, ISIN: INE264N01017, Scrip Code: 530055)
- **Offer Size**: Up to 31,52,994 equity shares
- **Offer Price**: Rs. 10.00 per share
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10.00
- **Market Lot**: 1 share
- **Offer Period**: February 11-25, 2026
- **Platform**: iBBS and RTRMS systems accessible via internet and leased line
- Trading Members can only bid for investors holding shares in demat form
- UCC registration must be updated with correct PAN and category details

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This circular provides operational instructions for an upcoming corporate action following existing OTB framework as per Exchange Notices 20150930-1 (September 30, 2015) and 20170203-29 (February 3, 2017).

## Compliance Requirements

**Trading Members and Custodian Members must:**

1. Ensure investor UCC is registered/updated with correct PAN number and category in Exchange UCC database
2. Access OTB module through designated platforms (iBBS/RTRMS)
3. Submit bids only for investors with shares in demat form
4. Monitor Extranet for acceptance confirmation files after issue closure
5. Contact Trading Operations for login ID creation and password resets using specified contacts

**Investor Categories for Bidding:**
- Foreign Institutional Buyer (FII)
- Financial Institution (FI)
- Mutual Fund (MF)
- Insurance Companies (IC)
- Other QIBs (OTH)
- Other NIB (NOH)
- Company (CO)
- Individual (IND)
