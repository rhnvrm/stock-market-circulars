## Summary

5PAISA CAPITAL LIMITED (BSE Code: 540776) has announced a Rights Issue of equity shares, with the Record Date set for 17 March 2026. Trading members are informed that transactions in the company's equity shares must be conducted on an Ex-Rights basis effective from 17 March 2026 under Settlement No. DR-841/2025-2026.

## Key Points

- Company: 5PAISA CAPITAL LIMITED (BSE Scrip Code: 540776)
- Rights Issue ratio: 1 (ONE) equity share for every 2 (TWO) equity shares held
- Face value of new shares: Rs. 10/- each
- Premium: Rs. 290/- per share
- Issue price: Rs. 300/- per share, payable fully on application
- Record Date: 17 March 2026
- Ex-Rights trading effective from: 17 March 2026 (Settlement No. DR-841/2025-2026)
- Segment: Dematerialised Securities – Rolling Settlement

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular is a standard corporate action notification under BSE's listing compliance framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all transactions in 5PAISA CAPITAL LIMITED equity shares are executed on an Ex-Rights basis from 17 March 2026 onwards.
- Members must update their systems to reflect the ex-rights adjustment effective the Record Date.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** 13 March 2026
- **Record Date:** 17 March 2026
- **Ex-Rights Effective Date:** 17 March 2026
- **Settlement Number:** DR-841/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

This corporate action affects existing shareholders of 5PAISA CAPITAL LIMITED, who are entitled to subscribe to 1 new share for every 2 shares held at Rs. 300/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- + premium Rs. 290/-). Traders and investors must account for the ex-rights adjustment in pricing from 17 March 2026. The rights issue will increase the company's share capital upon completion. Impact is limited to holders and traders of this specific scrip.
