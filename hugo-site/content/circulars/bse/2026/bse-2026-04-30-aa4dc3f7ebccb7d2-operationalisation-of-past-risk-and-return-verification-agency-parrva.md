## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260430-45 dated April 30, 2026, forwarding a SEBI circular (Ref: HO/38/14/(4)2026-MIRSD-POD/I/10557/2026 dated April 29, 2026) regarding the operationalisation of the Past Risk and Return Verification Agency (PaRRVA). All Research Analysts are directed to take note of the circular.

## Key Points

- SEBI has issued a circular on the operationalisation of PaRRVA (Past Risk and Return Verification Agency)
- The SEBI circular is dated April 29, 2026, with reference number HO/38/14/(4)2026-MIRSD-POD/I/10557/2026
- BSE has forwarded the SEBI circular as an annexure to all Research Analysts
- The notice is issued by the Membership Compliance Department under the Research Analysts segment

## Regulatory Changes

SEBI has operationalised PaRRVA, an agency responsible for verifying past risk and return claims made by Research Analysts. This represents a new oversight mechanism for claims related to historical performance data published by Research Analysts.

## Compliance Requirements

- All Research Analysts registered with BSE are required to take note of the SEBI circular on PaRRVA
- Research Analysts should review the enclosed SEBI circular (Annexure) for detailed compliance obligations related to PaRRVA's operationalisation
- Compliance with PaRRVA verification requirements as specified in the SEBI circular is mandatory

## Important Dates

- SEBI Circular Date: April 29, 2026
- BSE Notice Date: April 30, 2026
- Specific effective dates for PaRRVA compliance are detailed in the attached SEBI circular (Annexure)

## Impact Assessment

This circular primarily impacts registered Research Analysts, who will be subject to verification of their past risk and return disclosures by PaRRVA. The establishment of this agency signals increased regulatory scrutiny over historical performance claims made by Research Analysts, aimed at protecting investors from misleading or unverified past performance data. No direct impact on trading operations or listed companies is indicated.
