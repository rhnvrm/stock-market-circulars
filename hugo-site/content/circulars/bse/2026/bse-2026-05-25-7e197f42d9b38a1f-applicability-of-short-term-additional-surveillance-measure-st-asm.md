## Summary

BSE has notified the inclusion of certain securities under the Short Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) framework under both Stage I and Stage II, effective May 27, 2026. This is part of BSE's ongoing market surveillance program aimed at curbing abnormal price movements and protecting investor interests.

## Key Points

- Securities in Annexure I have satisfied criteria for inclusion under ST-ASM Stage I with a 50% margin requirement (or existing margin, whichever is higher), capped at 100%.
- Separate securities in Annexure I have also satisfied criteria for ST-ASM Stage II with a 100% margin requirement (or existing margin, whichever is higher), capped at 100%.
- The enhanced margin requirements apply to all open positions as on May 26, 2026, and all new positions created from May 27, 2026.
- The ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the exchanges.
- Inclusion under ASM is purely a market surveillance action and should not be construed as an adverse action against the concerned company or entity.

## Regulatory Changes

This notice is issued further to prior ASM framework revision notices:
- Notice 20240920-71 dated September 20, 2024
- Notice 20181027-1 dated October 27, 2018
- Notice 20190719-33 dated July 19, 2019
- Notice 20201204-56 dated December 4, 2020
- Notice 20230925-49 dated September 25, 2023
- Notice 20240920-63 dated September 20, 2024

No new framework changes are introduced; this circular applies the existing ST-ASM provisions to newly qualifying securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members (brokers) must apply enhanced margin rates to affected securities from May 27, 2026.
- **ST-ASM Stage I:** Collect margin of 50% or existing applicable margin, whichever is higher (max cap: 100%).
- **ST-ASM Stage II:** Collect margin of 100% or existing applicable margin, whichever is higher (max cap: 100%).
- Members should refer to Annexure I (attached to the notice) for the full list of affected securities.
- For clarifications, members may write to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **May 25, 2026:** Notice issued.
- **May 26, 2026:** Reference date for open positions subject to enhanced margins.
- **May 27, 2026:** Enhanced margin requirements become effective for both ST-ASM Stage I and Stage II securities.

## Impact Assessment

Traders and investors holding or planning to trade in the affected securities will face significantly higher margin requirements from May 27, 2026. ST-ASM Stage II securities face the maximum 100% margin obligation, which may reduce liquidity and deter speculative activity in those scrips. When a scrip exits the ASM framework, its price band will revert to the pre-ASM level, provided it is not under any other surveillance measure. The measure is targeted at curbing volatility and unusual price behaviour and is not indicative of any fundamental adverse development at the company level.
