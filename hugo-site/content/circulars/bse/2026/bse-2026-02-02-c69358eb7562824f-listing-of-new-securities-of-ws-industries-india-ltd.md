## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 99,43,125 new equity shares of W.S. Industries (India) Ltd. (Scrip Code: 504220) issued to Non Promoters on a preferential basis. The shares will commence trading on February 3, 2026, and are subject to lock-in until August 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- 99,43,125 equity shares of face value Rs. 10/- each issued at premium of Rs. 90/-
- Issue price: Rs. 100/- per share
- Allotment date: January 2, 2026
- Trading commencement: February 3, 2026
- Shares issued to Non Promoters on preferential basis
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distinctive Numbers: 65918750 to 75861874
- ISIN: INE100D01014

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the listing of new securities effective February 3, 2026
- Lock-in restrictions apply to all 99,43,125 shares until August 30, 2026
- Shares are subject to standard preferential allotment regulations

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: January 2, 2026
- **Trading Commencement**: February 3, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry**: August 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a routine preferential allotment for W.S. Industries (India) Ltd. representing additional equity capital raised from Non Promoters. The shares are subject to 8-month lock-in period as per regulations, preventing immediate selling pressure. Trading members should update their systems to reflect the increased share capital and new distinctive number ranges.
