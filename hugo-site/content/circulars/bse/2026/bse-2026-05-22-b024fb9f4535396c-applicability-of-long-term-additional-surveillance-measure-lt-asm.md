## Summary

BSE has issued a notice regarding the applicability of the Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LT-ASM) framework. Effective May 27, 2026, 100% margins will be imposed on all open positions as of May 26, 2026 and on new positions created from May 27, 2026 onwards for securities listed in Annexure I (Part A). Lower Price Bands will apply from May 25, 2026 for securities moved to higher LT-ASM stages. Securities in Annexure I (Part D) will be transferred to the T/XT/MT/TS Group from May 27, 2026, while securities in Annexure II will exit the LT-ASM framework from May 25, 2026.

## Key Points

- 100% margins applicable from May 27, 2026 on all open positions (as of May 26, 2026) and new positions for securities in Annexure I (Part A)
- Securities moved to higher LT-ASM stages effective May 25, 2026 are listed in Annexure I (Part B)
- Lower Price Band applicable for stage-upgraded securities from May 25, 2026
- Securities in Annexure I (Part D) to be transferred to T/XT/MT/TS Group from May 27, 2026 but continue under LT-ASM framework
- Securities exiting LT-ASM framework from May 25, 2026 are listed in Annexure II
- Consolidated list of all securities currently under LT-ASM framework provided in Annexure III
- Shortlisting is based solely on market surveillance and not an adverse action against any company
- Price bands reinstated for exiting securities, unless under another surveillance measure
- Shortlisting is based on XBRL submissions by the respective listed companies

## Regulatory Changes

This notice is issued pursuant to the LT-ASM framework established under BSE notice no. 20180321-46 (March 21, 2018) and revised under subsequent notices including:
- Notice 20181027-1 (October 27, 2018)
- Notice 20201204-56 (December 04, 2020)
- Notice 20240809-46 (August 09, 2024)
- Notice 20240920-63 (September 20, 2024)

The transfer of securities to T/XT/MT/TS Group while continuing in LT-ASM is governed by notice no. 20210604-41 (June 04, 2021).

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must ensure 100% margin collection on open and new positions in Annexure I (Part A) securities from May 27, 2026
- Brokers and trading members must apply the lower price band for securities moved to higher LT-ASM stages from May 25, 2026
- Members should monitor the consolidated Annexure III for the complete current list of LT-ASM securities
- For clarifications, members may contact bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **May 25, 2026**: Lower Price Band effective for securities moving to higher LT-ASM stages (Annexure I, Part B); Securities exit LT-ASM framework (Annexure II)
- **May 26, 2026**: Reference date for existing open positions subject to 100% margin
- **May 27, 2026**: 100% margins effective on open positions and new positions (Annexure I, Part A); Transfer of securities to T/XT/MT/TS Group (Annexure I, Part D)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has a significant impact on market participants trading in the affected securities. The 100% margin requirement substantially increases the capital required to maintain or open positions in LT-ASM-listed securities, reducing leverage and liquidity. The lower price band further restricts downside trading activity. Securities transferred to T/XT/MT/TS groups face altered trading conditions. Conversely, securities exiting the LT-ASM framework will have their original price bands restored (subject to no other surveillance measure), potentially easing trading restrictions. The LT-ASM framework is a key regulatory tool for BSE to manage risk around securities showing unusual long-term price or volume patterns.
