## Summary

BSE Index Services Private Limited has announced that Caliber Mining and Logistics Limited (Exchange ticker: 544833), which was listed on BSE effective Friday, July 24, 2026, will be added to the BSE IPO Index. The addition takes effect at the market open on Tuesday, July 28, 2026. This follows the exchange's earlier Notice No. 20260723-27 regarding the listing.

## Key Points

- Caliber Mining and Logistics Limited listed on BSE on July 24, 2026 (Exchange ticker: 544833)
- The stock will be added to the BSE IPO Index effective the open of trading on July 28, 2026
- This corporate action is included in the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE) for the notice date
- Queries can be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is a routine index maintenance action by BSE Index Services Private Limited related to a new listing.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action required from listed companies or market participants. Index fund managers and ETF providers tracking the BSE IPO Index should update their portfolios to reflect the addition effective July 28, 2026.

## Important Dates

- July 24, 2026: Listing of Caliber Mining and Logistics Limited on BSE
- July 28, 2026: Effective date of addition to the BSE IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited primarily to passive funds and ETFs benchmarked to the BSE IPO Index, which will need to rebalance holdings to include Caliber Mining and Logistics Limited. Broader market impact is minimal as this is a standard procedural update following a new listing.
