## Summary

BSE Limited has informed trading members that further new securities issued under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) / Employee Stock Option Schemes (ESOS) by 11 listed companies have been listed and admitted for trading on the Exchange, effective Friday, July 3, 2026.

## Key Points

- 11 companies have new shares issued under ESOP/ESOS schemes listed for trading
- Companies involved: Axis Bank Limited, Cyient DLM Limited, Ideaforge Technology Limited, Kirloskar Pneumatic Co. Limited, Lodha Developers Limited, Milkfood Limited, Nava Limited, NIIT Learning Systems Limited, Onward Technologies Limited, South Indian Bank Limited, and Suven Life Sciences Limited
- Share quantities range from 1,218,000 shares (Milkfood) to 5,000 shares (Kirloskar Pneumatic)
- No securities are under lock-in as per the notice
- No attachments accompanied this notice

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine administrative listing notice under existing BSE listing operations procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to take note of the listing of these additional securities for trading purposes. No further action is required from investors or other market participants.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 2, 2026
- Effective listing/trading date: Friday, July 3, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine ESOP/ESOS-related listing notice with minimal market impact. The additional shares represent employee stock option exercises being formally listed for trading, resulting in minor incremental increases to free float and share capital for the respective companies. No trading restrictions or lock-in periods apply to these newly listed shares.
