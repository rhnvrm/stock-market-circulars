## Summary

BSE has announced the live activities schedule for the Offer to Buy (OTB) – Acquisition Window for the delisting of PIYUSH LIMITED (Scrip Code: 999944, ISIN: INE10W701018). The offer window opens on Tuesday, 12th May 2026 and closes on Monday, 18th May 2026. The offer price is Rs. 668.00 per share for up to 10,490 equity shares. The scrip is only listed at the Calcutta Stock Exchange.

## Key Points

- **Security:** PIYUSH LIMITED (Symbol: PIYUSH, Scrip Code: 999944, ISIN: INE10W701018)
- **Offer Price:** Rs. 668.00 per share
- **Offer Size:** Up to 10,490 equity shares
- **Face Value:** Rs. 10.00
- **Market Lot:** 1
- **Opening Date:** Tuesday, 12th May 2026
- **Closing Date:** Monday, 18th May 2026
- **Listing:** Only listed at Calcutta Stock Exchange
- **OTB Platform:** iBBS (https://ibbs.bseindia.com/)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular operates under existing OTB framework per Exchange Notices 20150930-1 (September 30, 2015) and 20170203-29 (February 3, 2017).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must ensure the Unique Client Code (UCC) of investors is registered and/or updated with the correct PAN Number and Category in the Exchange UCC database.
- Trading Members can only bid for investors holding shares in demat form.
- Members and Custodians must note that if no securities are accepted, no End of Issue files will be generated in Extranet.
- Extranet files for bid acceptance will be available only after confirmation from the Merchant Banker/RTA post issue closure.

## Impact Assessment

This circular directly impacts shareholders of PIYUSH LIMITED who wish to tender shares in the delisting offer. Trading Members and Custodians on BSE must act within the defined window (12–18 May 2026) to facilitate client participation. The offer price of Rs. 668.00 is the fixed acquisition price. Eligible bidding categories include FII, FI, MF, IC, OTH (Other QIBs), NOH (Other NIB), CO (Company), and IND (Individual).
