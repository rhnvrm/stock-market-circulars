## Summary

BSE's Surveillance department (DOSS) sought clarification from IDBI Bank Ltd (Scrip Code: 500116) regarding news articles appearing in various newspapers, websites, and television channels. The purpose was to verify the accuracy of the reported information and to keep members and investors informed. IDBI Bank has submitted its reply, which is attached to this notice (Notice No. 20260427-17, dated 27 April 2026).

## Key Points

- BSE Surveillance initiated a clarification request to IDBI Bank Ltd (Scrip Code: 500116) based on news coverage appearing across media platforms, including Moneycontrol.
- IDBI Bank has responded to the Exchange's query; the reply is attached to this circular.
- The notice was issued by Deputy Vice President Sanjay Jain and Manager Shreya Ghose from BSE Surveillance.
- The circular falls under the equity segment and is categorised as company-related.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this circular. It is a routine surveillance and investor protection measure.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements are imposed on market participants. The circular is informational, intended to provide transparency to members and investors regarding the news article in question.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 27 April 2026
- **Notice Number:** 20260427-17

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact routine disclosure. BSE regularly issues such clarification notices as part of its surveillance function to ensure price discovery is based on accurate information. The attached reply from IDBI Bank would contain the substantive detail; the notice itself does not indicate any adverse finding or regulatory action against the company.
