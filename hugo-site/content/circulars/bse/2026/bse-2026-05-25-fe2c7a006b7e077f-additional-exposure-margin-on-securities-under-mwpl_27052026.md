## Summary

ICCL (Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd.) has notified members that additional exposure margin of 15% will be levied in the equity derivatives segment on securities where the top 10 clients collectively account for more than 20% of the Market Wide Position Limit (MWPL). This is a continuation of the framework established by BSE Notice dated March 18, 2019. The specific securities subject to this margin are listed in the attached Annexure.

## Key Points

- Additional exposure margin of **15%** will apply in the equity derivatives segment.
- Applies to securities where the top 10 clients account for more than **20% of MWPL**.
- Where additional surveillance margin (ASM) is also applicable, the **higher** of additional exposure margin or additional surveillance margin will be levied.
- Scrips are identified based on **3-month rolling data** and reviewed on a **monthly basis**.
- The specific scrips subject to this margin are listed in the Annexure to the notice.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced; this notice continues the existing MWPL-based additional exposure margin framework established under BSE Notice dated March 18, 2019. The list of affected securities has been updated for the May–June 2026 review cycle.

## Compliance Requirements

- All members trading in equity derivatives must apply the additional exposure margin of 15% on the scrips specified in the Annexure.
- Where ASM is also applicable on any of those scrips, members must levy whichever margin rate is higher.
- Members should refer to the Annexure for the complete list of applicable securities.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** May 29, 2026 — immediately following the expiry of May 2026 contracts.
- **Applicable Until:** June 2026 expiry.

## Impact Assessment

This is a periodic, routine margin update affecting derivatives traders in specific securities that have high concentration risk among top clients relative to MWPL. The impact is limited to members with open or new positions in the securities listed in the Annexure. Members must ensure their risk systems are updated to apply the revised margin rates from May 29, 2026. For queries, members may contact ICCL at risk.iccl@icclindia.com or +91-22-22725186/5059/8699.
