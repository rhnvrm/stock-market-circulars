## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260505-39 notifying the inclusion of securities under the Short-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) framework. Securities satisfying the criteria have been placed under either ST-ASM Stage I (50% margin) or ST-ASM Stage II (100% margin), effective May 07, 2026. The list of affected securities is provided in the attached Annexure I.

## Key Points

- Securities included under ST-ASM Stage I will attract a margin of 50% or existing margin, whichever is higher (capped at 100%).
- Securities included under ST-ASM Stage II will attract a margin of 100% or existing margin, whichever is higher (capped at 100%).
- Enhanced margins apply to all open positions as on May 06, 2026 and all new positions created from May 07, 2026.
- ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures.
- Shortlisting under ASM is purely for market surveillance purposes and should not be construed as an adverse action against the concerned company or entity.
- Upon exit from the framework, the price band of the scrip will be reinstated to the level applicable before shortlisting, provided the scrip is not under any other surveillance measure.

## Regulatory Changes

This notice is issued in continuation of prior ASM framework revisions referenced in Exchange notices: 20240920-71 (Sep 20, 2024), 20181027-1 (Oct 27, 2018), 20190719-33 (Jul 19, 2019), 20201204-56 (Dec 4, 2020), 20230925-49 (Sep 25, 2023), and 20240920-63 (Sep 20, 2024). No new framework changes are introduced; this is an application of the existing ST-ASM criteria to newly qualifying securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure applicable margin rates (50% for Stage I, 100% for Stage II) are collected on all open and new positions in shortlisted securities from May 07, 2026.
- Members may refer to Annexure I (attached to the notice PDF) for the complete list of securities under each stage.
- For clarifications, members may contact BSE Surveillance at bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** May 05, 2026
- **Reference Date for Open Positions:** May 06, 2026 (end of day)
- **Effective Date for Enhanced Margins:** May 07, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The enhanced margin requirements under ST-ASM Stage I and Stage II significantly increase the cost of holding or creating positions in the shortlisted securities. Stage II securities face the maximum 100% margin, effectively requiring traders to have full capital coverage for positions. This is likely to reduce liquidity and trading volumes in affected scrips. The measure is a regulatory tool to curb excessive speculation or unusual price/volume activity and does not reflect on the fundamental standing of the companies involved.
