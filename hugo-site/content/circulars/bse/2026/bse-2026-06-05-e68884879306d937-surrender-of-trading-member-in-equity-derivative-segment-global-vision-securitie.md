## Summary

BSE has approved the surrender of trading membership of Global Vision Securities Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 6853) in the Equity derivative segment, effective June 5, 2026. Trading members requiring clarification may contact BSE's Membership department via email.

## Key Points

- Global Vision Securities Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 6853) has surrendered its trading membership in the Equity derivative segment.
- The surrender has been formally approved by BSE.
- Effective date: June 5, 2026.
- For queries, trading members may contact membership.ops@bseindia.com.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative action reflecting the exit of a specific trading member from the Equity derivative segment.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance action required from other trading members. Members with open positions or pending settlements with Global Vision Securities Pvt. Ltd. should ensure orderly resolution through the Exchange's standard processes.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** June 5, 2026 — surrender of trading membership in Equity derivative segment takes effect.

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited to Global Vision Securities Pvt. Ltd. and its existing clients in the Equity derivative segment. No broader market or systemic implications. Other trading members are unaffected. Clients of the surrendering member should transition to alternate trading members for future equity derivative transactions.
