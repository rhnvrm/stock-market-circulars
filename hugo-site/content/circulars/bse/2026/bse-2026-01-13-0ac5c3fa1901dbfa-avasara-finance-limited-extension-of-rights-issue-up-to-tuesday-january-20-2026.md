## Summary

BSE has announced the extension of the rights issue period for Avasara Finance Limited. The closing date for the rights issue has been extended to Tuesday, January 20, 2026, providing additional time for shareholders to participate in the offering.

## Key Points

- Rights issue closing date extended to January 20, 2026
- Extension applies to Avasara Finance Limited
- Shareholders have additional time to exercise their rights

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a procedural extension of an ongoing corporate action.

## Compliance Requirements

- Shareholders wishing to participate must submit applications by the extended deadline of January 20, 2026
- All rights issue procedures and terms remain as previously disclosed

## Important Dates

- **Extended Closing Date**: Tuesday, January 20, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This extension provides existing shareholders of Avasara Finance Limited with additional time to evaluate and participate in the rights issue. The extension may indicate lower-than-expected subscription rates or administrative considerations. Shareholders should review the rights issue terms and make informed decisions within the extended timeline.
