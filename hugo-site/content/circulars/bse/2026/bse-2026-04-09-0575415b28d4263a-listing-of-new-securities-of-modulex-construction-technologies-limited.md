## Summary

BSE has listed 18,68,512 new equity shares of Modulex Construction Technologies Limited (Scrip Code: 504273, ISIN: INE064R01012) for trading effective Friday, April 10, 2026. The shares were issued on a preferential basis at Rs. 25/- each (face value Rs. 10/- + premium Rs. 15/-) to Promoter and Non-Promoter categories and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 18,68,512 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 15/- (issue price Rs. 25/-)
- Issued to Promoter and Non-Promoter on a preferential basis
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 69219537 to 71088048
- Date of allotment: 05 February 2026
- Trading effective from: 10 April 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification under BSE's Listing Operations framework for newly allotted preferential shares.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to note the new securities and permit trading from April 10, 2026
- Lock-in restrictions must be observed for the shares under lock-in until the specified dates

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** 05 February 2026
- **Trading Commencement:** 10 April 2026 (Friday)
- **Lock-in Expiry (13,00,540 shares):** 30 November 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (5,67,972 shares):** 30 November 2027

## Impact Assessment

The listing results in a modest equity dilution for existing shareholders of Modulex Construction Technologies Limited. The preferential allotment at Rs. 25/- per share raises capital for the company. A significant portion of the newly issued shares (18,68,512 total) are under lock-in: 5,67,972 shares locked until November 2027 and 13,00,540 shares locked until November 2026, limiting near-term selling pressure from allottees. Market impact is expected to be limited given the lock-in provisions.
