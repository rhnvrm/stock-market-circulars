## Summary

BSE has announced the schedule for an Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for SANMITRA COMMERCIAL LIMITED shares. The offer is for up to 1,49,76,000 equity shares at Rs. 15.00 per share, scheduled from January 6, 2026 to January 19, 2026. Trading Members and Custodian Members can participate through the iBBS and RTRMS platforms during specified session timings.

## Key Points

- **Security Name**: SANMITRA COMMERCIAL LIMITED
- **Security Symbol**: ZSANMCOM
- **ISIN**: INE896J01014
- **Scrip Code**: 512062
- **Offer Size**: Up to 1,49,76,000 Equity Shares
- **Offer Price**: Rs. 15.00 per share
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10.00
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Opening Date**: Tuesday, January 6, 2026
- **Closing Date**: Monday, January 19, 2026
- Bid Offering Period: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM daily (January 6-19, 2026)
- Give-up/Take-up Period: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM (January 6-16, 2026)
- Give-up on last day: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM (January 19, 2026)
- Take-up on last day: 9:15 AM to 4:00 PM (January 19, 2026)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular provides operational instructions for a specific corporate action under existing OTB framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members and Custodian Members** must:
  - Ensure investor UCC is registered/updated with correct PAN Number and Category in Exchange UCC database
  - Only bid for investors having shares in demat form
  - Refer to Exchange Notices 20150930-1 (September 30, 2015) and 20170203-29 (February 3, 2017) for operational instructions
  - Access OTB Module through designated URLs:
    - iBBS platform: https://ibbs.bseindia.com/ (Internet) or https://ibbsll.bseindia.com/ (Leased Line)
    - RTRMS: https://rtrms.bseindia.com/ (Internet) or https://rtrmsll.bseindia.com/ (Leased Line)
    - Extranet: https://member.bseindia.com/ (Internet) or https://memberll.bseindia.com/ (Leased Line)
  - Note: Files on Extranet for bid acceptance available only after Exchange receives confirmation from Merchant Banker/RTA post-issue closure
  - Note: If no securities are accepted, no End of Issue files will be generated in Extranet

## Important Dates

- **January 6, 2026**: Offer opening date; Bid Offering Period begins
- **January 6-16, 2026**: Give-up/Take-up period (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **January 6-19, 2026**: Bid Offering Period (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **January 19, 2026**: Offer closing date
  - Give-up on last day: 9:15 AM - 3:30 PM
  - Take-up on last day: 9:15 AM - 4:00 PM

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a company-specific takeover offer affecting only SANMITRA COMMERCIAL LIMITED shareholders.

**Operational Impact**: Medium for participating members - Trading Members and Custodians need to ensure proper UCC registration and use designated platforms (iBBS, RTRMS, Extranet) for bid processing.

**Investor Categories**: The offer is open to multiple investor categories including FII, FI, Mutual Funds, Insurance Companies, Other QIBs, Companies, and Individuals.

**Contact Information**: BSE has provided detailed contact points for technical support related to iBBS login, password reset, and OTB module queries (Trading Operations: 022-22728005, user.trdops@bseindia.com).
