## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of Chemmanur Credits and Investments Limited effective January 29, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to CCIL-10.75%-13-2-26-NCD (ISIN: INE051307AZ7, Code: 939687)
- Record date fixed for January 29, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- No dealings permitted from January 29, 2026 onwards
- Notice issued under reference DR-808/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational circular for suspension of trading due to corporate action.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from January 29, 2026
- Members must take note of the suspension and inform their clients holding these debentures
- Members should update their trading systems to prevent inadvertent trading

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 21, 2026
- **Record Date**: January 29, 2026
- **No Dealings From**: January 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders will receive their redemption proceeds and final interest payment based on holdings as of the record date. Investors cannot trade these securities from January 29, 2026.

**Market Impact**: Trading liquidity in these specific NCDs will cease from the suspension date. This is a routine corporate action with no broader market implications.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must ensure compliance with the suspension and update client communications regarding redemption proceeds.
