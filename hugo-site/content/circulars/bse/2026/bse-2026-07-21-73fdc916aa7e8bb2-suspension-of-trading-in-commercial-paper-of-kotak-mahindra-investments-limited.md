## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members that Kotak Mahindra Investments Limited has fixed a record date for the redemption of its Commercial Paper. Trading members have been advised not to deal in this Commercial Paper from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Issuer: Kotak Mahindra Investments Limited
- Scrip Code: 732105
- Scrip ID: KMIL-23-07-26-CP
- ISIN: INE975F14C90
- Trading members advised not to deal in this Commercial Paper due to upcoming redemption
- Segment: Debt
- Category: Corporate Actions

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine operational notice related to a scheduled debt redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of the Exchange must refrain from dealing in the Commercial Paper of Kotak Mahindra Investments Limited (Scrip Code 732105, ISIN INE975F14C90) from the Book Closure/Record Date to the Ex Date.

## Important Dates

- BC/RD to Ex Date: 22 July 2026
- Settlement Number: 677/2026-2027

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and traders of this specific Commercial Paper instrument issued by Kotak Mahindra Investments Limited. It does not affect the equity shares of Kotak Mahindra group companies or broader market operations. This is a standard pre-redemption trading suspension applicable only to the debt segment instrument specified.
