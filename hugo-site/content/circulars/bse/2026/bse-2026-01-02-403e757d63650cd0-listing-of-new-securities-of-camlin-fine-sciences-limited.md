## Summary

Camlin Fine Sciences Limited (Scrip Code: 532834) has listed 41,06,181 new equity shares of Re.1/- each on BSE with effect from Monday, January 5, 2026. These shares were issued at a premium of Rs.246.69 to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to a share swap arrangement.

## Key Points

- 41,06,181 equity shares of Re.1/- each issued
- Issue price: Rs.247.69 per share (Re.1 face value + Rs.246.69 premium)
- Allotted to non-promoters on preferential basis pursuant to share swap
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distinctive Numbers: 187980433 to 192086613
- ISIN: INE052I01032
- Date of allotment: November 30, 2025
- All 41,06,181 shares subject to lock-in until July 4, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must note the listing and commencement of trading for these securities from January 5, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: November 30, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: Monday, January 5, 2026
- **Lock-in Period Ends**: July 4, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard listing notification with minimal market impact. The preferential issue increases the equity base by 41.06 lakh shares. The lock-in period until July 4, 2026 ensures these shares cannot be traded for approximately 7 months from the date of allotment, limiting immediate supply impact on the secondary market.
