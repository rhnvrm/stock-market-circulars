## Summary

BSE Daily Bulletin No. 224/2025-2026 (dated 23 February 2026) publishes the official Book-Closure/Record Date schedule for a range of securities. Highlights include CIE Automotive India Limited's final dividend of Rs. 7.00 per share (70%) with a record date of 22 April 2026, ECap Equities Limited's early NCD redemption on 27 February 2026, and numerous Commercial Paper redemptions and NCD interest payments across Birla Group Holdings, Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited, Akara Capital Advisors, and others.

## Key Points

- CIE Automotive India Limited (Scrip Code 532756) has declared a Final Dividend of Rs. 7.0000 per share (70%) with Record Date 22 April 2026.
- ECap Equities Limited (975500) has an early NCD redemption on 27 February 2026.
- Akara Capital Advisors Private Limited (977052) has part-redemption of NCD and interest payment with Record Date 06 March 2026.
- Balkrishna Industries Ltd. (730703) has a Commercial Paper redemption with Record Date 27 February 2026.
- Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited has four separate NCD interest payment record dates between 05 March and 11 March 2026.
- Birla Group Holdings Private Limited has seven CP redemption record dates spanning 04 March to 20 March 2026.
- India Infrastructure Finance Company Limited (961707) has a bond interest payment with Record Date 13 March 2026.
- ICICI Home Finance Company Limited (730896) has a CP redemption with Record Date 26 March 2026.
- A.K. Capital Services Ltd. (729270) has a CP redemption with Record Date 05 March 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This bulletin is a routine periodic publication of scheduled corporate actions as per existing BSE listing and disclosure requirements.

## Compliance Requirements

- Investors holding equity shares of CIE Automotive India Limited must ensure shares are held in their demat/physical account on or before the Record Date of 22 April 2026 to be eligible for the final dividend.
- Holders of CP, NCD, and Bond instruments issued by the listed entities should note the respective record dates to qualify for redemption proceeds or interest payments.
- Registrars and Transfer Agents (RTAs) must update beneficiary records as of each stated record date.
