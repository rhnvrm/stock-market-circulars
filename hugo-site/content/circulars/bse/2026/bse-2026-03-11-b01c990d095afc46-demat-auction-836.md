## Summary

BSE has published the list of securities scheduled for Demat Auction 836, dated 11 March 2026. This auction covers shares across a broad range of listed companies where delivery obligations were not met during regular settlement. The auction facilitates settlement by sourcing shares on behalf of buyers who did not receive delivery.

## Key Points

- Settlement No. 836 dated 11/03/2026
- Over 100 securities listed across multiple sectors are included in the auction
- Quantities range from 1 share (e.g., Antony Waste, BODHI TREE M, Dixon Techno) to large lots such as Rajnish Wellness (50,000 shares) and Deep Diamond (245,520 shares)
- Both large-cap names (Dabur India, Tata Power, Tata Steel, Lupin, TVS Motor, Siemens, Hindustan Zinc) and small/mid-cap companies are included
- DSP Mutual Fund units (40,000 units) and other financial instruments are also part of the auction

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a routine operational circular issued under existing BSE settlement and auction framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members with auction obligations must participate in the auction process as per BSE guidelines
- Sellers who fail to deliver securities are liable for auction proceeds and associated penalties
- Buyers will receive either the auctioned shares or close-out at applicable rates if auction fails

## Important Dates

- Auction Date: 11 March 2026
- Settlement No.: 836

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine demat auction circular with limited market-wide impact. Individual stocks listed may see minor price pressure or volume activity around the auction date. High-quantity auctions such as Deep Diamond (245,520 shares), Rajnish Wellness (50,000 shares), Royal India (5,000 shares), JM Financial (3,000 shares), and Mangal Compufix (3,000 shares) may have slightly higher impact on those specific securities. Large-cap stocks like Dabur, Tata Power, Tata Steel, and Lupin appear in small quantities and are unlikely to be materially affected.
