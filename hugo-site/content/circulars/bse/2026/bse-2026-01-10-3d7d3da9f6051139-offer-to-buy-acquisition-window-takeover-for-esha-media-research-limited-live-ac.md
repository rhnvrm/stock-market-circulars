## Summary

BSE has announced the schedule for an Offer to Buy (Acquisition Window - Takeover) for ESHA MEDIA RESEARCH LIMITED (Symbol: ESHAMEDIA, ISIN: INE328F01016, Scrip Code: 531259). The offer will be live from January 13, 2026 to January 27, 2026, targeting up to 22,89,802 equity shares at an offer price of Rs. 15.00 per share with a face value of Rs. 10.00.

## Key Points

- **Offer Size**: Up to 22,89,802 equity shares
- **Offer Price**: Rs. 15.00 per share
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10.00
- **Market Lot**: 1 share
- **Opening Date**: Monday, January 13, 2026
- **Closing Date**: Friday, January 27, 2026
- **Bid Offering Period**: January 13-27, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Give-up/Take-up Period**: January 13-23, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Last Day Give-up**: January 27, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Last Day Take-up**: January 27, 2026 (9:15 AM to 4:00 PM)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This circular provides operational instructions for a specific corporate action.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members and Custodians** must ensure UCC of investors is registered/updated with correct PAN Number and Category in the Exchange UCC database
- Trading Members can only bid for investors having shares in demat form
- Members must refer to Exchange Notices 20150930-1 (September 30, 2015) and 20170203-29 (February 3, 2017) for operational instructions
- Access to OTB Module available via iBBS platform (Internet: https://ibbs.bseindia.com/ or Leased Line: https://ibbsll.bseindia.com/)
- RTRMS access (Internet: https://rtrms.bseindia.com/ or Leased Line: https://rtrmsll.bseindia.com/)
- Extranet access for files (Internet: https://member.bseindia.com/ or Leased Line: https://memberll.bseindia.com/)

## Important Dates

- **January 13, 2026**: Opening date for bid offering and give-up/take-up
- **January 23, 2026**: Last day for regular give-up/take-up period
- **January 27, 2026**: Closing date for offer, last day for give-up (until 3:30 PM) and take-up (until 4:00 PM)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a company-specific takeover activity affecting only ESHA MEDIA RESEARCH LIMITED shareholders.

**Operational Impact**: Medium for participating trading members and custodians who need to ensure proper UCC registration and facilitate bidding for eligible investors.

**Investor Categories Eligible**: Foreign Institutional Investors (FII), Financial Institutions (FI), Mutual Funds (MF), Insurance Companies (IC), Other QIBs (OTH), Non-Institutional Investors (NOH), Companies (CO), and Individuals (IND).
