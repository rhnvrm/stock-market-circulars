## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of Motilal Oswal Silver ETF units effective January 19, 2026. The ETF is an open-ended scheme that replicates/tracks the Silver ETF Total Return Index. A total of 465,000 units will be available for trading in dematerialized form only, with a market lot of 1 unit. Three market makers have been appointed to provide liquidity.

## Key Points

- **Fund Name**: Motilal Oswal Silver ETF
- **Investment Manager**: Motilal Oswal Asset Management Company Limited
- **Number of Units**: 465,000 units
- **Scrip Code**: 590147
- **ISIN**: INF247L01FZ1
- **Trading Symbol**: MOSILVER
- **Group**: E
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10.00 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 272.95 per unit
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Date of Allotment**: August 11, 2025
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only (as per SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification following existing SEBI guidelines for mutual fund units trading in dematerialized form.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all trades in Motilal Oswal Silver ETF units are executed in dematerialized form only
- Trades will be executed in unit market lot (minimum 1 unit)
- Members should familiarize themselves with the scrip details and trading parameters

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 16, 2026
- **Date of Allotment**: August 11, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date**: January 19, 2026 (Monday)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine ETF listing that adds another investment instrument for silver exposure. The listing expands investment options for market participants interested in precious metals.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Trading members need to update their systems with the new scrip details. Three market makers (East India Securities Ltd., Motilal Oswal Financial Services Limited, and Kanjalochana Finserve Private Limited) will ensure adequate liquidity.

**Investor Impact**: Provides investors with a new avenue to gain exposure to silver prices through an exchange-traded fund format with demat trading convenience.
