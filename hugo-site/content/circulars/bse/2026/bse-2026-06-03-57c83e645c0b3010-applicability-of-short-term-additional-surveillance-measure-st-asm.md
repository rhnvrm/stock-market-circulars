## Summary

BSE has notified the applicability of Short Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) for securities that have satisfied the criteria for inclusion under ST-ASM Stage I and Stage II. Enhanced margin requirements will be effective from June 05, 2026, covering open positions as on June 04, 2026 and all new positions created from June 05, 2026. The specific securities are listed in Annexure I attached to the notice.

## Key Points

- Securities have been shortlisted for ST-ASM Stage I and Stage II based on market surveillance criteria
- Stage I: Applicable margin rate shall be 50% or existing margin, whichever is higher (capped at 100%)
- Stage II: Applicable margin rate shall be 100% or existing margin, whichever is higher (capped at 100%)
- Enhanced margins apply to all open positions as on June 04, 2026 and new positions from June 05, 2026
- ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures
- Shortlisting under ASM is purely for market surveillance purposes and should not be construed as adverse action against the company
- Upon exit from the framework, price band will be reinstated to pre-ASM levels, unless another surveillance measure applies

## Regulatory Changes

This notice is issued further to prior ASM framework circulars: Notice No. 20240920-71 (Sep 20, 2024), 20181027-1 (Oct 27, 2018), 20190719-33 (Jul 19, 2019), 20201204-56 (Dec 04, 2020), 20230925-49 (Sep 25, 2023), and 20240920-63 (Sep 20, 2024). No new framework changes are introduced; this is an operational application of the existing ST-ASM framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure collection of enhanced margins (50% for Stage I, 100% for Stage II) on affected securities
- Members must apply revised margin rates on all open positions as on June 04, 2026
- All new positions in shortlisted securities from June 05, 2026 must attract the applicable ST-ASM margin rates
- For clarifications, members may contact bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **June 03, 2026**: Notice date and publication of ST-ASM inclusion list
- **June 04, 2026**: Reference date for open positions subject to enhanced margins
- **June 05, 2026**: Effective date for ST-ASM Stage I and Stage II margin requirements

## Impact Assessment

Traders and investors holding positions in the shortlisted securities will face significantly higher margin requirements starting June 05, 2026. Stage II securities are subject to the maximum 100% margin, meaning full value must be blocked as collateral, which can substantially reduce leveraged positions. Stage I securities face at least 50% margin. This may lead to forced position reduction for margin-constrained participants. The measure is temporary and reversible upon satisfying exit criteria, after which the pre-ASM price band is restored.
