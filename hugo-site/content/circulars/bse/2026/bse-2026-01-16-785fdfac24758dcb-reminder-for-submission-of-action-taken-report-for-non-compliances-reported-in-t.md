## Summary

BSE has issued a reminder to trading members regarding the submission of Action Taken Reports (ATR) for non-compliances identified in their Internal Audit Reports for the half year ended September 30, 2025. Members must close all reported non-compliances and submit their compliance status through BEFS by January 31, 2026. Failure to comply will result in monetary penalties and disciplinary actions as per existing Exchange guidelines.

## Key Points

- Reminder follows earlier Exchange notices dated December 2, 2025 (Notice No. 20251202-5) and December 12, 2025 (Notice No. 20251212-10)
- Trading members must close non-compliances reported in Internal Audit Report for half year ended September 30, 2025
- Submission must be made through BSE Electronic Filing System (BEFS)
- Non-compliance or late submission will trigger monetary penalties and disciplinary actions
- Penalties will be imposed in accordance with Exchange Notice No. 20251010-20 dated October 10, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular serves as a reminder of existing compliance obligations established in previous Exchange notices.

## Compliance Requirements

### Who Must Comply
- All BSE trading members who have non-compliances reported in their Internal Audit Reports for H1 FY2025-26

### Required Actions
1. Review non-compliances identified in Internal Audit Report for half year ended September 30, 2025
2. Close all outstanding non-compliance observations
3. Prepare and submit Action Taken Report showing compliance status
4. Submit ATR through BSE Electronic Filing System (BEFS) on or before the deadline

### Submission Method
- Electronic submission through BEFS (BSE Electronic Filing System)

## Important Dates

- **Reference Period**: Half year ended September 30, 2025
- **First Notice Date**: December 2, 2025 (Notice No. 20251202-5)
- **Second Notice Date**: December 12, 2025 (Notice No. 20251212-10)
- **Current Reminder Date**: January 16, 2026
- **Submission Deadline**: January 31, 2026

## Impact Assessment

### Operational Impact
- Trading members with outstanding audit non-compliances must prioritize closure and reporting activities
- Requires coordination between compliance, internal audit, and operational teams to address identified issues
- Members must allocate resources to complete remediation within the 15-day window from reminder date

### Financial Impact
- Members failing to submit ATR or close observations by deadline will face monetary penalties
- Penalty structure follows Exchange Notice No. 20251010-20 dated October 10, 2025
- Potential disciplinary actions may affect member standing and operational permissions

### Compliance Risk
- High risk for members with unresolved audit findings
- Non-submission triggers automatic penalty process
- Repeated non-compliance may lead to escalated disciplinary measures
- Members should prioritize this submission to maintain good standing with the Exchange
