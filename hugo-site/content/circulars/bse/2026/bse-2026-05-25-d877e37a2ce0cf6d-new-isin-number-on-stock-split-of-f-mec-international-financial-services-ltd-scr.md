## Summary

BSE has assigned a new ISIN number to F MEC International Financial Services Ltd (Scrip Code: 539552) following the sub-division of its equity shares from a face value of Rs.10/- to Rs.2/- per share. This notice is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20260512-38 dated May 12, 2026.

## Key Points

- New ISIN: **INE108T01021** assigned to F MEC International Financial Services Ltd (539552)
- Sub-division of equity shares from Rs.10/- face value to Rs.2/- face value
- The new ISIN is effective for trades done on and from the ex-date: **26-May-2026**
- DR reference: 638/2026-2027
- This notice continues from Exchange Notice No. 20260512-38 dated May 12, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

A new ISIN (INE108T01021) replaces the previous ISIN for F MEC International Financial Services Ltd equity shares following the stock split. Trading members must use the new ISIN for all transactions on and after the ex-date.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members of BSE must update their systems with the new ISIN (INE108T01021) for F MEC International Financial Services Ltd (Scrip Code: 539552)
- All trades executed on or after 26-May-2026 must reference the new ISIN for the sub-divided shares of Rs.2/- each

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 25-May-2026
- **Ex-Date (Effective Date for new ISIN):** 26-May-2026
- **Prior notice:** Exchange Notice No. 20260512-38 dated 12-May-2026

## Impact Assessment

The stock split reduces the face value of each equity share from Rs.10/- to Rs.2/-, resulting in a 5x increase in the number of shares outstanding. The new ISIN INE108T01021 must be used by all trading members from the ex-date onwards. This is a routine corporate action with operational impact limited to updating ISIN references in trading and depository systems. Investors holding shares in demat form will see their holdings automatically updated by the depositories.
