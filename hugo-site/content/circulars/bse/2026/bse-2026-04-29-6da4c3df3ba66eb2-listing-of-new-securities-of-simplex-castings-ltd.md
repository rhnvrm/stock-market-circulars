## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260429-20 informs trading members that 4,25,104 new equity shares of Simplex Castings Ltd. (Scrip Code: 513472, ISIN: INE658D01011) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Thursday, April 30, 2026. The shares were issued on a preferential basis to Non-Promoters at an issue price of Rs. 494.00/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- at a premium of Rs. 484/-).

## Key Points

- 4,25,104 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 484/- per share to Non-Promoters on a preferential basis
- Issue price: Rs. 494.00/- per share
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 7882944 to 8308047
- ISIN: INE658D01011
- All 4,25,104 shares are subject to lock-in until November 30, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification under BSE's Listing Operations for newly allotted securities admitted to trading.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities and must update their records accordingly
- The lock-in restriction on all 4,25,104 shares (Dist. Nos. 7882944 to 8308047) must be observed until November 30, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** March 28, 2026
- **Effective Date for Trading:** April 30, 2026 (Thursday)
- **Lock-in Expiry:** November 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 4,25,104 equity shares to the tradeable float of Simplex Castings Ltd. (Scrip Code 513472), representing a preferential allotment to Non-Promoters. However, all newly listed shares are locked in until November 30, 2026, meaning they cannot be sold during this period, limiting any immediate dilution impact on the open market. Trading members should note the lock-in restriction when processing transactions involving these specific share certificate numbers.
