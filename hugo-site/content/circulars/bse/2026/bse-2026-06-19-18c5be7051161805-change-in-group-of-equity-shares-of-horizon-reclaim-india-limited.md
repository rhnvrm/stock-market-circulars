## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that equity shares of Horizon Reclaim (India) Limited (Scrip Code: 544794), currently traded in the Trade for Trade segment under MT Group, will be shifted to the Rolling segment under M Group effective Monday, July 06, 2026. This follows Notice No. 20260618-28 dated June 18, 2026.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 544794 (Horizon Reclaim (India) Limited)
- Segment: SME
- Transfer from: Trade for Trade segment (MT Group)
- Transfer to: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective date: Monday, July 06, 2026
- This notice is a continuation of BSE Notice No. 20260618-28 dated June 18, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares of Horizon Reclaim (India) Limited will transition out of the Trade for Trade (TFT) settlement mechanism under MT Group and move to normal Rolling settlement under M Group. In the TFT segment, trades must result in delivery and cannot be squared off intraday; the shift to Rolling settlement restores standard intraday and delivery trading flexibility.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should update their systems and client records to reflect the new segment classification (M Group) effective July 06, 2026. Members with queries may contact Mr. Anurag Jain at BSE on Tel. No. 022-2272 8822.

## Important Dates

- **June 18, 2026**: Original notice (No. 20260618-28) issued
- **June 19, 2026**: This continuation notice issued
- **July 06, 2026 (Monday)**: Effective date for transfer to Rolling segment (M Group)

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to traders and investors holding or dealing in Horizon Reclaim (India) Limited shares. Moving from Trade for Trade to Rolling segment generally eases trading restrictions, as intraday squaring off becomes permissible and normal margin-based trading resumes. This is a positive development for market liquidity in the scrip.
