## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that ALTIUS TELECOM INFRASTRUCTURE TRUST has fixed a record date of 04 June 2026 for redemption of its Non-Convertible Debenture (ATIT-8.40%-18-12-26-CP) on exercise of a call option, along with payment of interest. Trading in the debenture is suspended from that date.

## Key Points

- Suspension of trading applicable to NCD: **ATIT-8.40%-18-12-26-CP** (ISIN: INE0BWS08019, Code: 975310)
- Record date fixed: **04 June 2026**
- Purpose: Redemption of NCD on exercise of Call Option and Payment of Interest
- Debenture series identifier: DR-644/2026-2027
- Trading members are directed not to deal in the debentures from 04 June 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice under BSE's Listing Compliance and Operations framework requiring cessation of secondary market trading in the specified NCD ahead of its record date.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease dealing in the ALTIUS TELECOM INFRASTRUCTURE TRUST NCD (INE0BWS08019 / Code 975310) from 04 June 2026.
- Members should update their systems to reflect the trading suspension for this ISIN.

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to secondary market participants holding or intending to trade the specific NCD (ATIT-8.40%-18-12-26-CP). From 04 June 2026, no transactions will be permitted on the exchange for this instrument. Debenture holders will receive redemption proceeds and accrued interest per the terms of the call option exercise. This is a standard end-of-life action for the NCD series and does not affect equity or other instruments of ALTIUS TELECOM INFRASTRUCTURE TRUST.
