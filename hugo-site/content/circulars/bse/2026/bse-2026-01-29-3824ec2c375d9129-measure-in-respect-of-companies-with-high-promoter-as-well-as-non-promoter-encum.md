## Summary

BSE has updated its surveillance framework for companies with high promoter and non-promoter encumbrance as per Regulation 28(3) of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulation 2011. Forbes & Company Ltd (Scrip Code: 502865, ISIN: INE518A01013) has been added to this surveillance measure effective January 30, 2026. The consolidated framework now includes 14 securities. No securities are being removed from the framework.

## Key Points

- Forbes & Company Ltd newly included in high encumbrance surveillance measure
- Measure applies to companies with high promoter as well as non-promoter encumbrance
- Framework based on SEBI (SAST) Regulation 2011, Regulation 28(3)
- No securities moving out of the framework in this update
- Total of 14 securities currently under this surveillance measure

## Regulatory Changes

This circular implements surveillance measures under SEBI (SAST) Regulation 2011, specifically Regulation 28(3), which deals with encumbrance of shares. The framework aims to monitor companies where significant shareholding (both promoter and non-promoter) is encumbered, indicating potential financial stress or pledging of shares.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants should note the inclusion of Forbes & Company Ltd in this surveillance framework
- Trading members and investors should exercise appropriate due diligence when dealing with securities under this measure
- Companies under this framework are subject to enhanced monitoring by the exchange
- Securities in this framework may face additional scrutiny and potential trading restrictions

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: January 30, 2026
- **Circular Date**: January 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited market-wide impact as only one security is being added. However, specific impact on Forbes & Company Ltd as it comes under enhanced surveillance.

**Trading Impact**: Securities under this framework may experience:
- Increased investor caution
- Potential liquidity concerns
- Enhanced regulatory oversight
- Possible price volatility due to surveillance designation

**Investor Impact**: Investors holding Forbes & Company Ltd should be aware of the high encumbrance status, which indicates significant pledging of shares by both promoters and non-promoters. This may signal financial stress and warrants careful monitoring.

**Consolidated Framework**: The framework now covers 14 securities across various sectors, including infrastructure, manufacturing, financial services, and paints industries.
