## Summary

BSE has listed 14,10,000 new equity shares of Shemaroo Entertainment Limited (Scrip Code: 538685) issued to Promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of loan. These shares are permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Monday, May 11, 2026.

## Key Points

- 14,10,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 100/- (issue price Rs. 110/-)
- Issued to Promoters on a preferential basis via conversion of loan
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 27348587 to 28758586
- ISIN: INE363M01019
- All 14,10,000 shares are subject to lock-in until 29/11/2027

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notice under BSE's Listing Operations for newly issued securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed that the new securities are listed and permitted to trade from May 11, 2026
- Lock-in restrictions apply to all 14,10,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 27348587–28758586) until November 29, 2027; these shares cannot be traded during the lock-in period

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** 27 March 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** Monday, 11 May 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry:** 29 November 2027

## Impact Assessment

The listing increases the total issued share capital of Shemaroo Entertainment Limited by 14,10,000 shares. Since these shares are allotted to Promoters via loan conversion at Rs. 110/- per share and are locked in until November 2027, there is no immediate free-float impact. Market participants should note the potential dilution effect on existing shareholders and the future unlock event in late 2027, which could increase supply in the secondary market.
