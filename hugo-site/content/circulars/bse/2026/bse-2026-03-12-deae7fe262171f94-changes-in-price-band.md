## Summary

BSE has revised the price bands for 11 scrips effective March 13, 2026. The revisions range from 2% to 20% and cover companies across diverse sectors including healthcare, gas, textiles, financial services, energy, media, and metals.

## Key Points

- Price bands revised for 11 scrips effective March 13, 2026
- Band levels vary: 2% (Shukra Bullions), 5% (Amwill Health Care, Jindal Poly Films, Venus Remedies), 10% (DCM Financial Services, Gita Renewable Energy, Mahamaya Steel, Picturehouse Media, Usha Martin Education), 20% (Bhagawati Gas, Bhandari Hosiery Exports)
- Action taken by BSE Surveillance department
- Members may contact bse.surv@bseindia.com for clarifications

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must adhere to the revised price bands for the listed scrips from March 13, 2026 onwards
- Orders placed outside the revised price band limits will not be accepted by the exchange
- Members requiring clarification should write to bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** March 12, 2026
- **Effective Date:** March 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The revised price bands will limit daily price movement for the affected scrips. Tighter bands (2%-5%) on Shukra Bullions, Amwill Health Care, Jindal Poly Films, and Venus Remedies indicate heightened surveillance concern, potentially restricting volatility. Wider bands (20%) for Bhagawati Gas and Bhandari Hosiery allow greater price discovery. Trading members must update order management systems to reflect the new limits before market open on March 13, 2026.
