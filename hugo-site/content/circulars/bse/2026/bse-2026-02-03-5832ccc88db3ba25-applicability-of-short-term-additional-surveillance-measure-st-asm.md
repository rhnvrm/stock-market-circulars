## Summary

BSE has announced changes to the Short Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) framework effective February 04, 2026. The circular includes three annexures: Annexure I lists 12 securities being shortlisted in the ST-ASM framework, Annexure II lists 5 securities moving out of the framework, and Annexure III provides a consolidated list of all securities currently under ST-ASM with their respective stages (Stage I or Stage II).

## Key Points

- 12 securities shortlisted in Short Term 5/15/30 Days ASM Framework effective February 04, 2026
- 5 securities moving out of ST-ASM Framework effective February 04, 2026
- No securities moving to higher or lower ASM stages within the framework
- Framework includes both main board and SME securities
- Securities are categorized into Stage I and Stage II surveillance measures
- Consolidated list includes 9 securities actively under ST-ASM surveillance

## Regulatory Changes

No changes to the ST-ASM framework structure itself. The circular implements routine surveillance adjustments by adding and removing securities based on their trading patterns and risk profiles.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must comply with ST-ASM requirements for all listed securities
- Securities marked with @ are SME scrips under ST-ASM framework
- Securities marked with * are as per NSE classification
- Securities marked with ~ are T+0 scrips shortlisted as per parent company
- Different markers indicate reason for exit: LT-ASM (*), Trade for Trade (**), GSM (#), Pledge Framework (^), or ESM ($)

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** February 04, 2026
- **Circular Issue Date:** February 03, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - affects 17 securities with changes in surveillance framework, potentially impacting liquidity and trading patterns for these securities.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members dealing in these securities need to adjust their surveillance compliance measures. Securities entering ST-ASM may face additional margin requirements and trading restrictions under Stage I or Stage II measures.

**Investor Impact:** Investors holding or trading these securities should be aware of enhanced surveillance measures which may include price bands, margin increases, or trading frequency restrictions depending on the ASM stage.
