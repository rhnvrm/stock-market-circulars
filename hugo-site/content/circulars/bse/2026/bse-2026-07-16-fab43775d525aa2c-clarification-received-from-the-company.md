## Summary

BSE observed significant price/volume movement in a set of listed scrips and had written to the respective companies seeking clarification under its surveillance process. This notice discloses that five companies have submitted replies (attached to the notice), while replies from nine other companies remain awaited as of the notice date.

## Key Points

- Companies that have replied to BSE's query: Carysil Ltd (524091), Kronox Lab Sciences Ltd (544187), SI Capital & Financial Services Ltd (530907), WEP Solutions Ltd (532373), JITF Infralogistics Ltd (540311).
- Companies from which replies are still awaited: Archean Chemical Industries Ltd (543657), Gconnect Logitech and Supply Chain Ltd (544156), MKP Mobility Ltd (521244), Darjeeling Industries Ltd (539770), CMX Holdings Ltd (532217), Mapro Industries Ltd (509762), Sujala Trading & Holdings Ltd (539117), VJTF Eduservices Ltd (509026), Kinetic Trust Ltd (531274).
- The exercise is intended to ensure trading members and investors have access to the latest relevant information amid unusual trading activity.
- Replies from the responding companies are attached to the original notice on BSE's platform.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine surveillance disclosure under BSE's standard practice of seeking clarifications on abnormal price/volume movements.

## Compliance Requirements

Companies listed under "reply awaited" are expected to respond to BSE's clarification request. Investors and trading members should treat pending clarifications as a caution flag until responses are received and reviewed.

## Important Dates

Notice dated 16 July 2026 (Notice No. 20260716-17).

## Impact Assessment

The disclosure itself does not indicate any specific adverse or positive development for the named companies, but signals that these scrips experienced unusual price/volume activity warranting exchange scrutiny. Investors should monitor for the actual clarification content (attached replies) and watch for further exchange action, such as placement under surveillance frameworks (ASM/GSM), if replies are unsatisfactory or remain pending.
