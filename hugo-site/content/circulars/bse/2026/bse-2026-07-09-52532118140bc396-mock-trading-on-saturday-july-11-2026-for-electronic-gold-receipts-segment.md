## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Electronic Gold Receipts (EGR) segment on Saturday, July 11, 2026. Trading members using third-party trading platforms of empaneled vendors or in-house developed systems through ETI APIs can use this session to test their trading applications for various functionalities, including exceptional market conditions such as risk-reduction mode and trading halt. The notice also mentions that a new BOLT Pro TWS version 13.20 will be released, with details to be published via a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for the EGR segment on July 11, 2026.
- Open to trading members using empaneled vendor platforms or in-house systems via ETI APIs.
- Session allows testing of functionalities like risk-reduction mode and trading halt.
- New BOLT Pro TWS version 13.20 to be released; details to follow in a separate circular.
- Trades from mock trading do not attract margin obligations, pay-in/pay-out obligations, and do not create rights or liabilities.
- Members are requested to actively participate and provide feedback by 3:00 pm on the mock trading day.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is an operational notice for a scheduled mock trading session, not a regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are encouraged (not mandated) to participate in the mock trading session.
- Members should provide feedback on the mock trading session via the contact numbers/email provided (BSE Tech Support: 022-22728053, bse.tech@bseindia.com; Help Desk: 022-45720400/600, 022-69158500, bsehelp@bseindia.com) by 3:00 pm on the session day.

## Important Dates

- **Mock Trading Session Date**: Saturday, July 11, 2026
- **Trading Session Timings**:
  - Log-in: 09:15 am – 10:00 am
  - Pre-Open Order Entry: 10:00 am – 10:30 am
  - Pre-Open Matching: 10:30 am – 10:38 am
  - Continuous Trading: 10:38 am – 02:00 pm
  - Block Deal Window: 12:00 pm – 12:15 pm
  - Closing: 02:00 pm – 02:10 pm
  - Post-Closing: 02:10 pm – 02:20 pm
- **Feedback Deadline**: 3:00 pm on July 11, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, low-impact operational notice affecting only trading members who wish to test their trading systems ahead of live operations. There is no impact on listed securities, investors, or market pricing, as mock trades carry no financial obligations or legal effect.
