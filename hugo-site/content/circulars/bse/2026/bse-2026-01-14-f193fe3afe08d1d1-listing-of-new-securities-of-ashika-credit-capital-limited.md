## Summary

BSE has listed 65,34,507 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each of Ashika Credit Capital Limited (Scrip Code: 543766) effective January 16, 2026. These shares were issued pursuant to the scheme of amalgamation between Yaduka Financial Services Limited (Transferor Company) and Ashika Credit Capital Limited (Transferee Company) under sections 230-232 of the Companies Act, 2013. The newly listed shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 65,34,507 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each listed on BSE
- Shares issued under scheme of amalgamation between Yaduka Financial Services Limited and Ashika Credit Capital Limited
- Listing and trading effective from January 16, 2026
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN Number: INE094B01013
- Distinctive Numbers: 38196639 to 44731145
- Date of Allotment: December 1, 2025
- Scrip Code: 543766

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification following a corporate amalgamation approved under the Companies Act, 2013.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. This is an informational notice regarding the listing of new securities.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** December 1, 2025
- **Notice Date:** January 14, 2026
- **Listing Effective Date:** January 16, 2026 (Friday)

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This is a routine corporate action following a completed amalgamation. The listing increases the total outstanding equity shares of Ashika Credit Capital Limited by 65,34,507 shares. No trading restrictions apply. Market participants can trade these shares alongside existing shares from January 16, 2026. Clarifications can be obtained by contacting BSE at 022-2272 5271/5878.
