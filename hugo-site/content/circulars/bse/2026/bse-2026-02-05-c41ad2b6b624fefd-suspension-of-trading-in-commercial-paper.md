## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Commercial Paper issued by Solar Industries India Limited (Scrip Code: 730539, ISIN: INE343H14204) effective February 06, 2026, due to the instrument reaching its redemption date. This is a standard procedure for maturing commercial paper instruments.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Solar Industries India Limited to be suspended from trading
- Scrip Code: 730539
- ISIN: INE343H14204
- Suspension effective from February 06, 2026
- Reason: Maturity on redemption date
- Notice Number: 20260205-3
- Reference: DR-815/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for maturing debt instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must not deal in the aforementioned Commercial Paper effective February 06, 2026
- Trading Members are advised to take note of the suspension and update their systems accordingly

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: February 05, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Date**: February 06, 2026
- **Redemption Date**: February 09, 2026 (implied from standard T+3 settlement)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine suspension affecting a single commercial paper instrument reaching maturity. Such suspensions are standard practice in debt markets and do not indicate any issues with the issuer or broader market concerns.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Trading members need to ensure their systems reflect the suspension, but this is a routine operational update. Investors holding this commercial paper will receive redemption proceeds as per the original terms.
