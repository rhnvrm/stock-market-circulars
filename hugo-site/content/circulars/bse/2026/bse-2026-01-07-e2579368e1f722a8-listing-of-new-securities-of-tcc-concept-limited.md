## Summary

TCC Concept Limited (Scrip Code: 512038) has listed 1,06,10,690 new equity shares of Rs. 10/- each on BSE, effective from Thursday, January 8, 2026. These shares were issued at a premium of Rs. 547.94/- to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to a share swap arrangement. The shares are subject to lock-in until July 14, 2026.

## Key Points

- 1,06,10,690 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued
- Issue price: Rs. 557.94/- per share (Rs. 10/- face value + Rs. 547.94/- premium)
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis through share swap
- Distribution numbers: 35672509 to 46283198
- Date of allotment: December 8, 2025
- ISIN: INE887D01016
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification under existing SEBI guidelines for preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the listing of new securities
- Lock-in restrictions apply to all 1,06,10,690 shares until July 14, 2026
- Shares cannot be transferred or sold during the lock-in period

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 8, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: January 8, 2026
- **Lock-in Period Expiry**: July 14, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a routine preferential allotment listing for a single company. The shares are subject to a 7-month lock-in period, preventing immediate trading by allottees. Trading members should update their systems to reflect the increased equity capital and new distribution numbers for TCC Concept Limited.
