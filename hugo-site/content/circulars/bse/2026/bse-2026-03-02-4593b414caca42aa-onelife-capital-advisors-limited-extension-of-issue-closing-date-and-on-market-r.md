## Summary

Onelife Capital Advisors Limited (BSE: 533632, NSE: ONELIFECAP) has announced an extension of its ongoing Rights Issue closing date and the last date for On-Market Renunciation. The Right Issue Committee, at its meeting held on March 02, 2026, approved extending the issue closing date from March 06, 2026 to March 16, 2026, providing eligible equity shareholders additional time to apply.

## Key Points

- Rights Issue closing date extended by 10 days, from **March 06, 2026** to **March 16, 2026**
- Last date for On-Market Renunciation extended from **March 02, 2026** to **March 10, 2026**
- Last date for receiving requests for Application Form and Rights Entitlement Letter also extended to **March 10, 2026**
- Issue Opening Date remains unchanged: **February 23, 2026**
- No changes to terms and conditions of the Letter of Offer (LOF), Abridged Letter of Offer (ALOF), or Application Form
- Addendum to the LOF will be published on the Company's website, Registrar's website (KFintech), BSE, and NSE websites
- Right Issue Committee meeting was held on March 02, 2026 (1:45 PM to 2:15 PM)

## Regulatory Changes

This circular is issued pursuant to Regulation 30 and other applicable provisions of the SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015. The extension is a procedural change to the Rights Issue timeline and does not introduce any new regulatory requirements.

## Compliance Requirements

- Eligible equity shareholders must apply or renounce their rights entitlements within the revised timeline
- The company is required to publish an addendum to the LOF reflecting the revised closing date and post-issue activity timelines on all relevant portals
- BSE and NSE are requested to disseminate this information on their portals

## Important Dates

| Event | Old Timeline | Revised Timeline |
|---|---|---|
| Issue Opening Date | Monday, February 23, 2026 | Monday, February 23, 2026 (unchanged) |
| Last Date for On-Market Renunciation | Monday, March 02, 2026 | Tuesday, March 10, 2026 |
| Last Date for Application Form / RE Letter Requests | Monday, March 02, 2026 | Tuesday, March 10, 2026 |
| Issue Closing Date | Friday, March 06, 2026 | Monday, March 16, 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

The extension benefits existing eligible equity shareholders of Onelife Capital Advisors Limited by providing an additional 10-day window to apply for the Rights Issue or renounce their entitlements on the market. There is no change to the financial terms of the issue. Post-issue activity timelines will shift accordingly. The market impact is limited to shareholders of Onelife Capital Advisors Limited (Scrip Code: 533632 on BSE; ONELIFECAP on NSE).
