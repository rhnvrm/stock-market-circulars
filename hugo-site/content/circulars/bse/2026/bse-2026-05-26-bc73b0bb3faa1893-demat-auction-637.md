## Summary

BSE Limited, through Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd. (ICCL), has issued a notice inviting offers from trading members for purchase of securities in Demat Auction 637, pertaining to Rolling Settlement No. DR-637/2026-27. The auction will be conducted on May 26, 2026 with pay-in scheduled for May 27, 2026.

## Key Points

- Auction offer entry window: 2:00 PM to 2:45 PM on May 26, 2026
- Auction matching: 2:45 PM to 3:00 PM on May 26, 2026
- Delivery statement and accepted offer reports: 3:00 PM to 3:30 PM
- Money statement and final reports: 3:35 PM to 5:30 PM
- Securities pay-in (auction): May 27, 2026 at 8:00 AM
- Members and custodians must check auction pay-in obligations and execute securities pay-in within depository cut-off timings
- No corrections permitted for punching errors in securities code or rate
- Exchange reserves right to accept or reject any offer without reason

## Regulatory Changes

The notice reiterates that following implementation of the Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment, settlement is processed through the Clearing Member of a Trading Member. The earlier restriction on trading members offering securities in auction for settlements in which they had short delivered has been removed.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must enter auction offers via trading terminals during the specified session window
- Members and custodians must verify auction pay-in obligations and complete securities pay-in within depository cut-off timings on May 27, 2026
- Trading members should refer to the cutoff rate on the "Auction Offer Entry Screen" before submitting offers
- Trading members must review notices related to Corporate Actions (Ex-Rights, Ex-Bonus, Ex-Split, etc.) before participating

## Important Dates

- **May 26, 2026**: Auction offer entry (2:00 PM – 2:45 PM), Matching (2:45 PM – 3:00 PM), Delivery/Money statements (3:00 PM – 5:30 PM)
- **May 27, 2026**: Auction pay-in at 8:00 AM

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice applicable to trading members and custodians participating in the Equity Cash Segment. Impact is limited to members with short delivery obligations in Rolling Settlement DR-637/2026-27. No broader market impact is anticipated.
