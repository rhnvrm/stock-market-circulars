## Summary

Indian Clearing Corporation Limited (ICCL) has issued the client margin reporting schedule for the Equity Derivatives segment for March 2026 (Notice No. 20260302-9). The schedule specifies the last date for margin reporting and the corresponding penalty date for each trade date during the month.

## Key Points

- Issued by ICCL under the Settlement/RMS category for the Equity Derivatives segment.
- Covers trade dates from 02-Mar-2026 through 30-Mar-2026.
- Reporting deadlines extend into early April 2026 for late-month trade dates.
- Failure to report by the last date of margin reporting will attract penalties on the specified penalty date.
- Signed by Manoj Panchal and Omkar Hatkar, Deputy Managers at ICCL.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is a routine monthly schedule circular issued to inform members of their reporting obligations under existing margin reporting framework for the Equity Derivatives segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members (brokers) must submit client margin reports for each trade date by the corresponding last date of margin reporting.
- Non-compliance by the reporting deadline will result in penalties applied on the penalty date.
- Members should ensure timely and accurate margin reporting for all Equity Derivatives trades executed during March 2026.

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a medium operational impact on trading members and brokers participating in the Equity Derivatives segment. Members must plan their back-office and compliance workflows to meet the specified reporting deadlines. Late reporting will result in financial penalties. There is no impact on trading rules, margin rates, or market structure. The schedule accounts for weekends and market holidays, with some trade dates having reporting windows of approximately 5-7 trading days.
