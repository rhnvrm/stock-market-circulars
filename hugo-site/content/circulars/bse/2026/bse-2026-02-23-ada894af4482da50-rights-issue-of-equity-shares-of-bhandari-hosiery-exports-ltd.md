## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Bhandari Hosiery Exports Ltd (Scrip Code: 512608) has fixed **25 February 2026** as the record date for its rights issue of equity shares. Transactions in the company's equity shares must be conducted on an ex-rights basis effective from 25 February 2026 under settlement DR-828/2025-2026.

## Key Points

- **Company:** Bhandari Hosiery Exports Ltd (BSE Code: 512608)
- **Record Date:** 25 February 2026
- **Rights Entitlement:** 4 (four) new equity shares for every 5 (five) equity shares held
- **Face Value:** Re. 1/- per share
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 2.56 per share (face value Re. 1/- + premium Re. 1.56)
- **Full amount of Rs. 2.56 is payable on application**
- **Ex-Rights Date:** 25 February 2026, Settlement No. DR-828/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular is a standard corporate action notification under BSE's listing compliance framework, requiring trading members to apply ex-rights treatment to transactions in the scrip from the specified date.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all transactions in Bhandari Hosiery Exports Ltd equity shares (Code: 512608) are executed on an **ex-rights basis** with effect from **25 February 2026**.
- Members should update their systems to reflect the ex-rights adjustment in line with Settlement No. DR-828/2025-2026.

## Impact Assessment

The rights issue is a moderate corporate action affecting existing shareholders of Bhandari Hosiery Exports Ltd. The ratio of 4 shares for every 5 held at Rs. 2.56 represents a significant dilutive event for non-participating shareholders. The full issue price being payable on application (no partly paid structure) simplifies the settlement. Market impact is limited to this specific scrip; trading members must be prepared for price adjustment on the ex-rights date (25 February 2026).
