## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for S R Industries Ltd. The company's name has been officially changed to Arur Footwear Limited with effect from September 12, 2025. The certificate was issued by the Central Processing Centre, Registrar of Companies.

## Key Points

- Company CIN: L29246PB1989PLC009531
- Old Name: S R Industries Ltd
- New Name: Arur Footwear Limited
- Effective Date: September 12, 2025
- Company Type: Company limited by shares
- Original Incorporation Name: S R Industries Ltd
- Certificate issued by: Central Processing Centre, ROC
- Approved by: Sabari Raj, Assistant/Deputy/Registrar of Companies

## Regulatory Changes

The name change is a corporate action approved by the Ministry of Corporate Affairs under the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014, specifically pursuant to rule 29.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company is obligated to display the old name (S R Industries Ltd) for a period of two years along with its new name (Arur Footwear Limited) at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- All stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company and its directors on the MCA website: www.mca.gov.in/MCA21
- The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders

## Important Dates

- **September 12, 2025**: Effective date of name change from S R Industries Ltd to Arur Footwear Limited
- **September 12, 2027**: End of two-year period for mandatory display of old name alongside new name

## Impact Assessment

**Corporate Impact**: This is a routine administrative corporate action with minimal operational impact. The company retains its Corporate Identification Number (CIN) and all existing rights, obligations, and liabilities remain unchanged.

**Stakeholder Impact**: Shareholders, creditors, and other stakeholders should note the new company name for all future correspondence and transactions. The company's registered address remains at E-217, Industrial Area, Phase 8B, Mohali-160071, Punjab.

**Market Impact**: The name change has negligible market impact as it is an administrative change. Trading operations will continue under the new name, and investors should be aware of the name change to avoid confusion in tracking the security.
