## Summary

BSE announces the listing of three new Treasury Bills (T-Bills) with effect from January 19, 2026. The T-Bills include 91-day, 182-day, and 364-day instruments that will be available for trading in the G GROUP - DEBT INSTRUMENTS category.

## Key Points

- Three Treasury Bills to be listed on BSE debt segment from January 19, 2026
- All T-Bills have market lot of 1 unit
- Securities will be unavailable for trading two working days prior to maturity/redemption date
- Trading halts at T-2 (excluding bank holidays)

## Treasury Bills Details

**91-Day T-Bill:**
- Scrip Code: 805166
- ISIN: IN002025X414
- Symbol: 91TB170426
- Maturity Date: April 17, 2026

**182-Day T-Bill:**
- Scrip Code: 805167
- ISIN: IN002025Y412
- Symbol: 182TB17726
- Maturity Date: July 17, 2026

**364-Day T-Bill:**
- Scrip Code: 805168
- ISIN: IN002025Z419
- Symbol: 364TB15127
- Maturity Date: January 15, 2027

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the T-2 trading halt rule before maturity
- Members requiring clarification can contact BSE on 2272 8352/5753/8597

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** January 19, 2026
- **91-Day Maturity:** April 17, 2026
- **182-Day Maturity:** July 17, 2026
- **364-Day Maturity:** January 15, 2027
- **Notice Issue Date:** January 8, 2026 (Notice Date: January 16, 2026)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing of government treasury bills with minimal market impact. These short-term debt instruments provide trading opportunities for debt market participants and institutional investors. The listing expands the available government securities for trading on BSE's debt platform.
