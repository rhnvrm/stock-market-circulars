## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 57 new equity shares of UPL Ltd (Scrip Code: 512070, ISIN: INE628A01036) are permitted for trading effective Friday, June 5, 2026. These shares arise from the conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up shares of Rs. 2/- each on a rights basis.

## Key Points

- 57 new equity shares of UPL Ltd admitted for trading from June 5, 2026
- Shares issued pursuant to conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up (face value Rs. 2/- each) on rights basis
- ISIN: INE628A01036 (same as existing shares)
- New shares are pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distinctive numbers assigned: 890707351–890707400 (50 shares), 890734697–890734701 (5 shares), 890789252–890789253 (2 shares)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification under BSE's listing operations.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed and permitted to trade in these newly listed securities from the effective date. No additional compliance action required from members.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 4, 2026
- **Trading Effective Date:** Friday, June 5, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The addition of 57 shares is negligible relative to UPL Ltd's total equity capital. This is a routine administrative listing following conversion of partly paid rights shares to fully paid status.
