## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial papers (CPs) on its Debt Market Segment under Notice No. 20260311-32, effective March 11, 2026. These securities were issued on a private placement basis and allotted on March 10, 2026. Six issuers are covered: Bajaj Financial Securities Limited, Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited, SBICAP Securities Limited, Manappuram Finance Limited, Julius Baer Capital (India) Private Limited, and JSW Energy Limited. All securities are to be traded exclusively in dematerialised form.

## Key Points

- All listed CPs are traded in dematerialised form only under their respective ISIN numbers
- Standard denomination for trading is Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for all securities is 1 paise
- All CPs were allotted on March 10, 2026
- Face value and paid-up value for all CPs is Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Credit ratings are high-grade (A1+) across all issuers from CRISIL, CARE, ICRA, and IND rating agencies
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the BSE Debt Department: 22728352 / 8597 / 8995 / 5753 / 8915

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine operational listing notice under existing BSE Debt Segment listing framework for privately placed securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form
- Trades must be in standard denominations of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be observed for all listed CPs
