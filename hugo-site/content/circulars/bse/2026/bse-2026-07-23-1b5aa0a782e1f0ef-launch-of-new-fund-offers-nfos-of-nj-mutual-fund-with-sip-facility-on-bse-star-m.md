## Summary

BSE has issued an update to its earlier Notice No. 20260709-47 dated July 09, 2026, regarding the launch of NJ Value Fund and NJ Momentum Fund (New Fund Offer schemes) with SIP facility on the BSE StAR MF Platform. NJ AMC has revised the clear fund acceptance date for these NFO schemes from July 30, 2026 to July 29, 2026.

## Key Points

- The revision applies to NJ Value Fund and NJ Momentum Fund, both NFO schemes offered with SIP facility on BSE StAR MF Platform.
- Clear fund acceptance date moved earlier, from July 30, 2026 to July 29, 2026.
- Tentative allotment date for both schemes remains July 30, 2026.
- Clear funds must be available in the ICCL account by 6:00 PM on July 29, 2026 to be eligible for allotment.
- Cheque and OTM (One Time Mandate) payment modes are accepted during the NFO period, subject to clear funds reaching the ICCL account by the same deadline.
- The NFO schemes remain open until midnight on July 24, 2026 for Lumpsum Purchase transactions and SIP/XSIP registrations.
- SIP/XSIP registrations must be authenticated by clients on or before midnight on July 24, 2026 (the NFO closure date).

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory requirement is introduced; this is a scheme-specific operational revision to a previously announced NFO timeline communicated via Exchange Notice 20260709-47.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure client funds are cleared and credited to the ICCL account by 6:00 PM on July 29, 2026 for allotment eligibility.
- Members must ensure SIP/XSIP registrations are authenticated by clients before the midnight July 24, 2026 NFO closure deadline.
- Members processing Cheque/OTM payments must track clearance timelines against the revised deadline.

## Important Dates

- July 24, 2026 (midnight): NFO closure for Lumpsum Purchase transactions and SIP/XSIP registrations.
- July 29, 2026 (6:00 PM): Revised clear fund acceptance deadline in ICCL account (previously July 30, 2026).
- July 30, 2026: Tentative allotment date for both schemes.

## Impact Assessment

This is a minor operational/administrative update relevant primarily to BSE StAR MF trading members and investors participating in the NJ Value Fund and NJ Momentum Fund NFOs. It does not affect listed equity trading, market indices, or broader compliance frameworks, and carries low market impact.
