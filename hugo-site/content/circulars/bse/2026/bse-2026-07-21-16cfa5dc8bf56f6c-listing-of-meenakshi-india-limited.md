## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the equity shares of Meenakshi India Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange effective Wednesday, July 22, 2026, and placed in the 'T' Group of Securities.

## Key Points

- Company: Meenakshi India Limited
- Scrip Code: 544831
- Abbreviated Name on BOLT System: MEEIND
- ISIN: INE208H01016
- Market Lot: 1 (One) Share
- Number of Shares: 11,250,000 equity shares of Rs. 10 each, fully paid up
- Distinctive Numbers: 1 to 11,250,000
- Registered Office: Whites Road, Royapettah, Chennai, Tamil Nadu 600014
- Registrar to the Issue: Cameo Corporate Services Limited, Chennai
- Securities will trade only in dematerialised form as per SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000
- Physical share certificates have been enfaced with non-transferability; demat shares carry a lock-in marked in depository records
- All shares rank pari-passu, including dividend entitlement

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a standard listing admission notice under existing SEBI/BSE listing norms for newly issued equity shares.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade the scrip only in dematerialised form and in unit market lot of 1 share.
- Physical certificates are non-transferable per company confirmation.
- Queries may be directed to the company's Compliance Officer or BSE's Listing Department (Mr. Parag Jain, Manager; Mr. Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President).

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 21, 2026
- Listing and Trading Commencement: Wednesday, July 22, 2026
- Audited financials enclosed for quarter/year ended March 31, 2026
- Annual Report enclosed for FY 2024-25

## Impact Assessment

This notice enables trading in Meenakshi India Limited shares (MEEIND) starting July 22, 2026. Impact is primarily relevant to investors and brokers dealing in this specific scrip; it does not affect broader market operations or other listed securities.
