## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 34,77,98,677 new equity shares of Easy Trip Planners Limited (Scrip Code: 543272), issued at a premium of Rs. 8.19 per share to non-promoters on a preferential basis, are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Thursday, July 2, 2026. These shares rank pari-passu with the company's existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 34,77,98,677 equity shares of face value Re. 1/- each issued at an issue price of Rs. 9.19/- (including premium of Rs. 8.19/-) per share
- Shares allotted to non-promoters on a preferential basis
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distinctive numbers of the new shares: 3636853189 to 3984651865
- ISIN: INE07O001026

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing approval notice, not a regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific action required from trading members or investors beyond noting the listing and applicable lock-in restrictions on the new shares.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: 26/05/2026
- Listing/Trading Effective Date: 02/07/2026 (Thursday)
- Lock-in Expiry: 25/01/2027

## Impact Assessment

The listing formalizes admission of preferentially allotted shares to trading, but all 34,77,98,677 shares remain under lock-in until January 25, 2027, so there is no immediate increase in tradable free-float or market supply. The preferential issuance to non-promoters may have longer-term implications for shareholding pattern and dilution once the lock-in lapses.
