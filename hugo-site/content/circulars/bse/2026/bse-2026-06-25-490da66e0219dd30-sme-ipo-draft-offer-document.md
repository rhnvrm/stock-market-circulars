## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of Jivkar Seeds Limited on the SME website. The document was filed on June 24, 2026, and contains all disclosures required under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations. The public is invited to submit comments within 21 days from the date of notice.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Jivkar Seeds Limited on June 24, 2026
- Document hosted on the BSE SME website for a minimum of 21 days
- Public comments are invited via email at equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine disclosure under Schedule VI of the SEBI (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations (ICDR Regulations).

## Compliance Requirements

- Interested parties and the public may review the Draft Offer Document at: https://www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Any comments on the Draft Offer Document must be submitted within 21 days from June 25, 2026, to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Filing Date:** June 24, 2026
- **Notice Date:** June 25, 2026
- **Public Comment Deadline:** July 16, 2026 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has limited broader market impact. It is relevant to retail and institutional investors interested in participating in the Jivkar Seeds Limited SME IPO. The 21-day public comment window is a standard regulatory step before the IPO proceeds to the next stage of approval.
