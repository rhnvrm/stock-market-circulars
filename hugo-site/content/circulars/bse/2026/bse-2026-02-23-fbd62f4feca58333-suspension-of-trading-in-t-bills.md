## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260223-7 informing trading members that the 91-day Treasury Bill with Scrip Code 805148 (ISIN: IN002025X356, Particulars: 91TB260226) is maturing on its redemption date. Accordingly, trading in this T-Bill is suspended with effect from February 24, 2026 (DR-827/2025-2026).

## Key Points

- T-Bill 91TB260226 (Scrip Code: 805148, ISIN: IN002025X356) is maturing on its redemption date
- Trading in the instrument is suspended effective February 24, 2026
- The notice applies to all trading members of BSE's Debt Segment
- Reference circular number: DR-827/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice issued upon maturity of a government Treasury Bill instrument in the Debt Segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in Scrip Code 805148 (91TB260226) from February 24, 2026 onwards
- Members should update their systems to reflect the suspension of this instrument

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** February 23, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Effective:** February 24, 2026 (Redemption/Maturity Date)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine administrative action triggered by the natural maturity of a 91-day Treasury Bill. Only holders of Scrip Code 805148 are directly affected. No broader market or systemic implications.
