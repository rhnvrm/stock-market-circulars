## Summary

ICICI Prudential Mutual Fund's two Special Investment Fund (SIF) long-short schemes will reopen for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform with effect from June 10, 2026, pursuant to BSE Notice No. 20260518-35 dated May 18, 2026. Redemption and switch-out transactions under these SIF schemes are subject to specific transaction frequency restrictions set by the AMC/RTA.

## Key Points

- Two SIF schemes reopened for ongoing transactions: **ISIF Active Asset Allocator Long-Short Fund** and **ISIF Equity Long-Short Fund**
- Both schemes will be available for subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP facility from **June 10, 2026**
- Redemption and switch-out transactions for ISIF Active Asset Allocator Long-Short Fund (Regular and Direct – Growth) are permitted **twice a week: Monday and Wednesday only**
- Redemption and switch-out transactions for ISIF Equity Long-Short Fund (Regular and Direct – Growth) are permitted **daily**
- If Monday or Wednesday falls on a non-business day, redemptions for the Active Asset Allocator fund will be processed on the next working/business day
- NAV applicability and redemption payouts will follow AMC/RTA processing timelines as per scheme master

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced. This notice implements the reopening of SIF schemes per the earlier BSE Notice No. 20260518-35 (May 18, 2026), which had presumably suspended or restricted these schemes from ongoing transactions.

## Compliance Requirements

- BSE StAR MF platform members must note the restricted transaction frequency for ISIF Active Asset Allocator Long-Short Fund (Monday and Wednesday only for redemptions and switch-outs)
- Members should align order entry and client instruction timelines accordingly
- Redemption payouts will be governed by the payout TAT in the scheme master; members should communicate this to investors

## Important Dates

- **June 10, 2026**: Effective date for reopening of ICICI Prudential SIF schemes for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP on BSE StAR MF Platform
- **May 18, 2026**: Original notice (No. 20260518-35) referenced as the basis for this reopening

## Impact Assessment

This circular has moderate operational impact for distributors and investors using the BSE StAR MF platform to transact in ICICI Prudential SIF schemes. The non-daily redemption frequency for the Active Asset Allocator Long-Short Fund (twice weekly) is a notable liquidity constraint compared to standard equity mutual funds and requires investor awareness. The Equity Long-Short Fund allows daily redemptions, making it more liquid. Overall market impact is low as these are niche SIF (alternative investment-style) products with a limited investor base.
