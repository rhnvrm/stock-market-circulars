## Summary

BSE has listed 15,099,200 fully paid equity shares of Modern Diagnostic & Research Centre Limited in dematerialized form. The shares are subject to various lock-in periods, with the earliest lock-in expiring on February 3, 2026, and the latest extending to January 20, 2029.

## Key Points

- Total securities listed: 15,099,200 equity shares
- All securities are fully paid and in demat form
- Securities are distributed across 11 distinct ranges with different lock-in periods
- Lock-in periods range from February 3, 2026 to January 20, 2029
- Initial lock-in date for most tranches: December 30, 2025

## Securities Details

### Distribution of Shares by Lock-in Period:

- **577,600 shares**: Lock-in until February 3, 2026 (Distinctive Nos. 11,000,001 to 11,577,600)
- **584,000 shares**: Lock-in until April 4, 2026 (Distinctive Nos. 11,577,601 to 12,161,600)
- **2,937,600 shares**: No lock-in - FREE (Distinctive Nos. 1 to 875,000 and 12,161,601 to 15,099,200)
- **Multiple tranches**: Lock-in periods of January 20, 2027, January 20, 2028, and January 20, 2029

## Important Dates

- **December 30, 2025**: Initial lock-in date for majority of tranches
- **February 3, 2026**: Lock-in expiry for 577,600 shares
- **April 4, 2026**: Lock-in expiry for 584,000 shares
- **January 20, 2027**: Lock-in expiry for multiple tranches (3,370,000 shares total)
- **January 20, 2028**: Lock-in expiry for multiple tranches (7,020,000 shares total)
- **January 20, 2029**: Lock-in expiry for 2,220,000 shares

## Compliance Requirements

- All listed securities must remain in dematerialized form
- Lock-in restrictions must be observed as per the specified dates
- Trading in locked-in securities is restricted until respective lock-in expiry dates

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard listing announcement with minimal market impact. The staggered lock-in periods will result in gradual release of shares into the market over approximately 3 years, which helps prevent sudden supply shocks. The availability of 2,937,600 free shares (approximately 19.5% of total) provides immediate liquidity for trading.
