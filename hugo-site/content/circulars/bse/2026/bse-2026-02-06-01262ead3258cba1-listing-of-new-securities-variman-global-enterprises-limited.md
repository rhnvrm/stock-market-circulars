## Summary

BSE has listed 5,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 1/- each of Variman Global Enterprises Limited (Scrip Code: 540570) issued at a premium of Rs. 9.50/- per share to non-promoters on a preferential basis. The shares will be available for trading from Monday, February 9, 2026, and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 5,00,000 equity shares of face value Rs. 1/- each issued at Rs. 10.50/- per share
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE717F01028
- Distinctive Numbers: 194581001 to 195081000
- Date of Allotment: December 8, 2025
- Trading permitted from February 9, 2026
- All shares subject to lock-in until August 15, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for preferentially allotted securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities listing. The allotted shares are subject to mandatory lock-in requirements as per SEBI regulations for preferential issues.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 8, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: February 9, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry**: August 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. The preferential issue represents a small capital raise of Rs. 52.5 lakhs (5,00,000 shares at Rs. 10.50). The lock-in period until August 15, 2026, prevents immediate sale by allottees, limiting supply-side pressure. This is a routine corporate action with no material impact on trading dynamics or market participants.
