## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Choice Equity Broking Private Limited (Member No. 3299) will voluntarily cease business operations in the Currency derivative segment effective January 9, 2026. This is an administrative closure initiated by the member firm.

## Key Points

- Choice Equity Broking Private Limited (Member No. 3299) is voluntarily closing business
- Cessation applies specifically to Currency derivative segment only
- Effective date: Friday, January 9, 2026
- Member firms can contact membership.ops@bseindia.com for clarifications

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a voluntary business closure by a member firm.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for other trading members. This is an informational notice about one member's voluntary exit from the currency derivatives segment.

## Important Dates

- **January 8, 2026**: Notice date
- **January 9, 2026**: Effective date of cessation of operations in Currency derivative segment

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This affects only one member firm's operations in the currency derivatives segment. Trading members are informed for awareness purposes. Clients of Choice Equity Broking Private Limited may need to transition their currency derivative positions to other brokers before the effective date.
