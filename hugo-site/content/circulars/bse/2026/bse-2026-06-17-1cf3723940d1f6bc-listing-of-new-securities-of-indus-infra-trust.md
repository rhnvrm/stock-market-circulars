## Summary

BSE has listed 14,28,57,142 units of Indus Infra Trust (Scrip Code: 544137, ISIN: INE0NHL23019) at Rs. 119/- per unit, allotted to Qualified Institutional Buyers (QIBs) under a Qualified Institutional Placement. The units are admitted for trading on BSE with effect from Thursday, June 18, 2026.

## Key Points

- **Scrip Code:** 544137
- **ISIN:** INE0NHL23019
- **Units Allotted:** 14,28,57,142 units at Rs. 119/- each
- **Allotment Type:** Qualified Institutional Placement (QIP) to QIBs
- **Distribution Numbers:** 468148690 to 611005831
- **New units rank pari-passu** with existing units of the trust

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice following a QIP allotment by Indus Infra Trust.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new listing and may contact Ms. Mayuri Visaria (Tel: 022-22728901) for any clarifications regarding this notice.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** June 16, 2026
- **Trading Commencement:** June 18, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds approximately 14.28 crore new units to the tradeable float of Indus Infra Trust at an issue price of Rs. 119/-. Since these units rank pari-passu with existing units, there is potential for near-term dilution pressure. The QIP proceeds strengthen the trust's capital base for infrastructure investment activities. Market impact will depend on existing unitholders' response to the increased supply.
