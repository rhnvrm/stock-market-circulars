## Summary

BSE has listed 1,01,52,000 new equity shares of Travels & Rentals Ltd. (Scrip Code: 544242) on its Exchange. These shares, issued on a rights basis at Rs. 10/- face value with a premium of Rs. 5/-, are permitted for trading effective Thursday, March 12, 2026.

## Key Points

- **Company:** Travels & Rentals Ltd. (Scrip Code: 544242)
- **Number of Shares:** 1,01,52,000 equity shares
- **Face Value:** Rs. 10/- per share
- **Issue Premium:** Rs. 5/- per share (total issue price Rs. 15/-)
- **Issue Type:** Rights Issue
- **ISIN:** INE0SG001011
- **Distribution Numbers:** 11202686 to 21354685
- **Date of Allotment:** March 09, 2026
- These new shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice pursuant to a completed rights issue.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members of BSE are informed and permitted to trade the new securities from the effective date.
- No additional compliance action required beyond standard trading procedures for newly listed securities.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** March 09, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** March 12, 2026 (Thursday)

## Impact Assessment

The listing increases the tradeable float of Travels & Rentals Ltd. by 1,01,52,000 equity shares. As a rights issue, existing shareholders had the opportunity to subscribe, maintaining proportional ownership. The new shares are pari-passu with existing shares, so no differential rights or dilution concerns beyond normal rights-issue dilution apply. Impact is limited to existing shareholders and traders in Scrip Code 544242.
