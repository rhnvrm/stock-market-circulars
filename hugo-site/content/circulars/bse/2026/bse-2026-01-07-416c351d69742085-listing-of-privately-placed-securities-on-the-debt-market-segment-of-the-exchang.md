## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of privately placed debt securities from two companies - Ambium Finserve Limited and Lucina Land Development Limited - on the BSE Debt Segment effective January 7, 2026. The securities will be traded in dematerialized form only with a tick size of 1 paise.

## Key Points

- **Ambium Finserve Limited**: 30,000 debentures with 11.16% p.a. interest rate, face value Rs. 10,000, maturing on January 19, 2028 (ISIN: INE0RU307221, Scrip Code: 977439, Rating: CARE BBB-/Stable)
- **Lucina Land Development Limited**: 21,000 debentures with 13% p.a. interest rate, face value Rs. 100,000, maturing on January 30, 2029 (ISIN: INE0JZO07040, Scrip Code: 977440, Rating: IVR A-(CE)/Stable)
- All securities will be traded only in dematerialized form
- Market lot size is 1 for both securities
- Tick size is 1 paise
- Placement memoranda available on BSE website

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for further details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date of Listing**: January 7, 2026
- **Ambium Finserve Limited**:
  - Date of Allotment: January 6, 2026
  - Interest Payment: Monthly from February 19, 2026 to January 19, 2028
  - Redemption Date: January 19, 2028
- **Lucina Land Development Limited**:
  - Date of Allotment: January 5, 2026
  - Interest Payment: Monthly from January 31, 2026 to January 30, 2029
  - Redemption Date: January 30, 2029

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement with minimal market impact. The listing adds new debt instruments to the BSE Debt Segment for trading members interested in fixed-income securities. The impact is limited to participants in the debt market segment and does not affect equity trading or broader market operations. The securities are privately placed and represent standard corporate debt issuances with moderate credit ratings.
