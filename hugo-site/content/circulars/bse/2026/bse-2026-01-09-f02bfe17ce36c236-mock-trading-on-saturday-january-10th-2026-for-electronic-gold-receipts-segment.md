## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Electronic Gold Receipts (EGR) segment on Saturday, January 10th, 2026. The session is designed for trading members to test their trading applications, including third-party platforms from empaneled vendors and in-house developed systems using ETI APIs. A new BOLT Pro TWS version 12.50 will be released, with details to be published separately. The mock session will cover various functionalities including risk-reduction mode, trading halt, and exceptional market conditions. All trades executed during this session will not create any rights, liabilities, margin obligations, or settlement requirements.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Electronic Gold Receipts segment on Saturday, January 10th, 2026
- New BOLT Pro TWS version 12.50 to be released (details in separate circular)
- Trading members can test third-party platforms, empaneled vendor systems, or in-house ETI API-based applications
- Testing will include various functionalities: risk-reduction mode, trading halt, and exceptional market conditions
- Mock trades will not attract any margin obligations, pay-in/pay-out requirements, or create rights and liabilities
- Members requested to provide feedback by 3:00 PM on the same day
- Active participation from all members is encouraged

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational activity for system testing and familiarization purposes.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are encouraged to actively participate in the mock trading session
- Members must submit feedback for the mock trading session by 3:00 PM on January 10th, 2026
- Contact BSE Helpdesk (022-45720400/600 & 022-69158500; bsehelp@bseindia.com) or BSE Tech Support (022-22728053; bse.tech@bseindia.com) for queries or clarifications
- Members should coordinate with their respective Relationship Manager for any assistance

## Important Dates

**January 10th, 2026 - Mock Trading Session Schedule:**

- **Log-in:** 10:15 AM - 11:00 AM
- **Pre-Open Order Entry Period:** 11:00 AM - 11:08 AM
- **Matching Period:** 11:08 AM - 11:15 AM
- **Continuous Trading:** 11:15 AM - 02:00 PM
- **Block Deal Window:** 12:00 PM - 12:15 PM
- **Closing:** 02:00 PM - 02:10 PM
- **Post-Closing:** 02:10 PM - 02:20 PM
- **Feedback Submission Deadline:** 3:00 PM

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** None. This is a mock trading session with no real market transactions or obligations.

**Operational Impact:** Low. Provides an opportunity for trading members to:
- Test and familiarize themselves with the new BOLT Pro TWS version 12.50
- Validate their trading applications and systems
- Test third-party platforms and in-house developed ETI API systems
- Practice handling exceptional market conditions, risk-reduction mode, and trading halts
- Ensure readiness of trading infrastructure

**Member Impact:** Voluntary participation with no financial obligations or settlement requirements. Members can use this session for system testing and training purposes without any risk exposure.
