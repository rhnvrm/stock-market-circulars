## Summary

BSE Limited, through Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd., has issued a demat auction notice inviting offers from trading members for the purchase of securities under Rolling Settlement No. DR-659/2026-27. The auction session is scheduled for Monday, June 29, 2026, with pay-in on Tuesday, June 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- Auction offer entry window: 2:00 PM to 2:45 PM on June 29, 2026
- Auction matching: 2:45 PM to 3:00 PM
- Delivery statement and report of accepted auction offers: 3:00 PM to 3:30 PM
- Money statement and final reports: 3:35 PM to 5:30 PM
- Securities pay-in for auction: June 30, 2026 at 8:00 AM
- No corrections permitted for punching errors in securities code or rate
- The Exchange reserves the right to accept or reject any offer without reason

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. The notice reiterates that under the Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment, settlement is done through the Clearing Member of a Trading Member. The earlier restriction on trading members offering securities in auction for settlements in which they had short delivered has been removed.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and Custodians must check their auction pay-in obligations for securities
- Execute securities pay-in in depositories within depository cut-off timings
- Trading members must refer to the cutoff rate in the "Auction Offer Entry Screen" before placing offers
- Trading members should refer to notices on Corporate Actions (Ex-Rights, Ex-Bonus, Ex-Split, etc.) issued by respective departments

## Important Dates

- **June 29, 2026 (Monday):** Auction offer entry and matching sessions
- **June 30, 2026 (Tuesday), 8:00 AM:** Securities pay-in for the auction

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice applicable to trading members and custodians with obligations under Rolling Settlement DR-659/2026-27. The impact is limited to participants with short delivery positions in the relevant settlement cycle. No broader market or regulatory impact is anticipated.
