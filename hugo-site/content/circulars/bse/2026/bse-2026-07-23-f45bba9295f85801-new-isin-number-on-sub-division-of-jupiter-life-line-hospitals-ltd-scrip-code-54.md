## Summary

BSE has notified trading members of the new ISIN number to be used for the equity shares of Jupiter Life Line Hospitals Ltd (Scrip Code: 543980) following the sub-division (stock split) of the company's equity shares from a face value of Rs.10 each to Rs.2 each. This notice is a continuation of the earlier Exchange Notice No. 20260721-5 dated July 21, 2026.

## Key Points

- New ISIN assigned: INE682M01020 for the sub-divided equity shares of Jupiter Life Line Hospitals Ltd.
- The sub-division is from face value Rs.10/- per share to Rs.2/- per share.
- The new ISIN will be effective for trades done on and from the ex-date, July 24, 2026.
- Reference: DR-679/2026-2027.
- This notice supersedes/continues the prior notice (20260721-5) dated July 21, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory change; this is an operational/administrative notice implementing the previously announced stock split by updating the ISIN used for settlement and trading systems.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members and depositories must update their systems to reflect the new ISIN (INE682M01020) for Jupiter Life Line Hospitals Ltd shares for all trades executed on or after the ex-date.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 23, 2026
- Ex-Date (new ISIN effective from): July 24, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is operational rather than market-moving — it ensures continuity of trading and settlement for Jupiter Life Line Hospitals Ltd shares post sub-division. Trading members must ensure correct ISIN mapping to avoid settlement errors starting July 24, 2026.
