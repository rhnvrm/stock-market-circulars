## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document (DRHP) of Anand Seamless Limited, filed on February 27, 2026, on the Stock Exchange website. The document contains all disclosures as required under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and is open for public comments for a minimum of 21 days from the date of notice (March 2, 2026).

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Anand Seamless Limited on February 27, 2026
- Document hosted on the BSE SME website: https://www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public comment window open for at least 21 days from March 2, 2026
- Disclosures comply with Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations
- Comments to be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural notice under the SEBI ICDR Regulations requiring public disclosure of SME IPO draft offer documents and solicitation of public comments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members of the public, investors, and stakeholders are invited to review the Draft Offer Document and submit comments within 21 days from March 2, 2026 (deadline: on or before March 23, 2026)
- Comments must be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Filing Date:** February 27, 2026
- **Notice Date:** March 2, 2026
- **Public Comment Deadline:** On or before March 23, 2026 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal broad market impact. It is relevant to prospective investors, analysts, and stakeholders interested in the upcoming SME IPO of Anand Seamless Limited. The 21-day public comment period is a standard regulatory requirement under ICDR Regulations before the IPO can proceed, providing transparency and investor protection at the SME listing stage.
