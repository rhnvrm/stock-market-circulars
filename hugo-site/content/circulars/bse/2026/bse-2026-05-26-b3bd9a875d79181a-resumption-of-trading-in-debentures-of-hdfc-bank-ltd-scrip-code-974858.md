## Summary

BSE has announced the resumption of trading in debentures of HDFC Bank Ltd with Scrip Code 974858, effective May 27, 2026. Trading had been suspended following a record date of May 9, 2026, set for redemption on exercise of a Put Option. Since the Put Option was not fully exercised by debenture holders, HDFC Bank requested BSE to resume normal trading in the instrument.

## Key Points

- Trading in HDFC Bank debentures (Scrip Code 974858) will resume from May 27, 2026
- The resumption follows the record date of May 9, 2026, which was set for redemption on exercise of the Put Option
- HDFC Bank informed BSE that the Put Option was not fully exercised by debenture holders
- Reference notice: BSE Notice No. 20260505-40 dated May 5, 2026 (original suspension notice)
- Designated reference: DR-639/2026-2027

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This notice reinstates normal trading status for a previously suspended debt security following the partial non-exercise of a Put Option.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members are requested to note the resumption of trading in Scrip Code 974858 of HDFC Bank Ltd effective May 27, 2026, and update their systems accordingly.

## Important Dates

- **May 9, 2026**: Record date for redemption on exercise of Put Option (as per original notice 20260505-40)
- **May 26, 2026**: Date of this notice
- **May 27, 2026**: Effective date for resumption of trading in Scrip Code 974858

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. The resumption of trading provides liquidity to debenture holders who did not exercise the Put Option. The debt segment of BSE is directly affected; equity markets remain unimpacted. Trading members dealing in HDFC Bank debt instruments should update their systems to reflect the trading resumption.
