## Summary

BSE has notified that trading member JAGRUTI SECURITIES LTD. (Member No. 294) will undergo a name change to "JAGRUTI SECURITIES PRIVATE LIMITED" effective January 30, 2026. This is an administrative update with no impact on trading operations.

## Key Points

- Trading member name changing from JAGRUTI SECURITIES LTD. to JAGRUTI SECURITIES PRIVATE LIMITED
- Member Number 294 remains unchanged
- Change is purely administrative in nature
- Contact: membership.ops@bseindia.com for clarifications

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative name change reflecting a corporate restructuring from a public limited company to a private limited company.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance action required from other trading members. This is an informational notice only.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: Friday, January 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

No market or operational impact. The name change is administrative and does not affect the trading member's operations, member number, or any existing relationships with other market participants.
