## Summary

ICCL (Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd.) has published the settlement calendar for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for Restaurant Brands Asia Limited. The bidding window runs from June 8, 2026 to June 19, 2026, with obligations downloadable on July 3, 2026 and final settlement on July 6, 2026 under Settlement No. 2026065.

## Key Points

- Settlement Type: Takeover; Settlement No. 2026065
- Bidding period: June 8, 2026 – June 19, 2026
- Obligation download date: July 3, 2026
- Settlement date: July 6, 2026
- Demat shares must be delivered exclusively via the Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism available on both CDSL and NSDL depositories
- Market Type to use in depository system: "(Takeover)" with Settlement No. 2026065
- CDSL and NSDL early pay-in securities will be earmarked/blocked in client accounts under the requisite market type and settlement number
- Members and Custodians are NOT required to upload any EPN file
- Members should refer to the MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for client-wise Stamp Duty charges

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. Participants are directed to refer to Notice No. 20200528-32 dated May 28, 2020, regarding settlement as per Revised Guidelines of Mechanism for Acquisition of Shares through Stock Exchange.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and Custodians must complete early pay-in of securities in the depository system sufficiently in advance of the prescribed timelines for closure of the BSE OTB Bidding window and Custodian confirmation window
- Early pay-in effect must be reflected in BSE/ICCL systems before prescribed bid acceptance/confirmation timelines
- Use Market Type "(Takeover)" and Settlement No. 2026065 in depository system entries
- No EPN file upload required for this settlement

## Impact Assessment

This notice is operationally relevant to stock exchange members and custodians participating in the Restaurant Brands Asia Limited takeover acquisition window. The early pay-in requirement via CDSL/NSDL is mandatory, and participants must plan their depository operations to meet bid window deadlines. The settlement follows standard takeover acquisition window procedures. For queries, members may contact ICCL Help Desk at 022-69158590/45720490/45720690 or iccl.helpdesk@icclindia.com.
