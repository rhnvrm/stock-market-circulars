## Summary

BSE has issued an update to the Additional Surveillance Measure (ASM) framework applicable to companies undergoing the Insolvency Resolution Process (IRP) under the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code (IBC). This notice references prior circulars dated June 08, 2018, May 20, 2022, February 10, 2023, and September 05, 2025, and communicates the latest periodic review of securities meeting or ceasing to meet the criteria under this framework, effective July 14, 2026.

## Key Points

- Securities listed in Annexure I have satisfied the criteria for the IBC Additional Surveillance Measure and will be placed under the framework effective July 14, 2026.
- Securities listed in Annexure II are moving out of the IBC framework effective the same date.
- Annexure III provides a consolidated list of all securities currently under the framework.
- Securities under the framework are reviewed periodically.
- This surveillance framework operates in conjunction with other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the Exchange.
- Placement under this framework is purely a market surveillance action and should not be construed as adverse action against the company or entity.
- Price bands for securities exiting the framework will revert to their previous applicable price bands, unless the security remains subject to another surveillance framework, in which case that framework's price band will prevail.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced; this is a periodic update applying the existing IBC-linked Additional Surveillance Measure criteria (originally established via notices from 2018, 2022, 2023, and 2025) to a revised list of securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must note the updated inclusion (Annexure I) and exclusion (Annexure II) lists and adjust trading/monitoring practices accordingly. Queries can be directed to bse.surv@bseindia.com. The specific company/security names are contained in the annexures, which were not included in the extracted text.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** July 14, 2026 — for both securities entering and exiting the IBC surveillance framework.
- **Notice Date:** July 13, 2026.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to the specific securities named in the annexures, which face continued or new trading restrictions (or relief from restrictions) under the ASM/IBC framework, including potential price band changes. Since the annexure details (company names) are not present in the extracted text, the overall market impact is assessed as medium, pending identification of the affected securities.
