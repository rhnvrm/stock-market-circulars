## Summary

BSE Notice 20260420-15 informs trading members and custodians of an Offer for Sale (OFS) by the promoter group of String Metaverse Limited. The sellers — Spacenet Enterprises India Limited, Yaralagadda Venkat Subba Rao, Lanka Divyabharathi, and Pendurthi Annapurna — propose to sell up to 38,10,000 equity shares (3.27% of total paid-up equity) at a floor price of Rs.66 per share through BSE's designated OFS window on April 21–22, 2026.

## Key Points

- Sellers are promoter group entities/individuals of String Metaverse Limited
- Up to 38,10,000 equity shares (face value Rs.10 each) on offer, representing 3.27% of total paid-up equity
- Floor price set at Rs.66 per equity share
- T Day (April 21, 2026): Open for Non-Retail Investors only
- T+1 Day (April 22, 2026): Open for Retail Investors and Non-Retail Investors carrying forward un-allotted bids from T Day
- OFS conducted through BSE's separate designated window
- Purpose of sale: achieving minimum public shareholding requirements

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The OFS is conducted under the existing framework referenced in prior SEBI circulars:
- SEBI/HO/MRD/DOPI/CIR/P/2018/159 dated December 28, 2018 (Review of OFS mechanism)
- SEBI/HO/MRD/MRD-PoD-3/P/CIR/2023/10 dated January 10, 2023 (Comprehensive OFS Framework)
- SEBI/HO/MRD/MRD-PoD3/P/CIR/2024/82 dated June 14, 2024 (OFS to Employees Framework)

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and custodians must refer to BSE notice nos. 20190118-43, 20200701-27, 20230127-14, and 20240701-19 for revised OFS bidding guidelines
- All market participants must adhere to the designated OFS window for bidding
- Retail and non-retail investor participation must follow applicable SEBI OFS framework rules

## Important Dates

- **April 20, 2026**: Notice issued
- **April 21, 2026 (T Day)**: OFS open for Non-Retail Investors
- **April 22, 2026 (T+1 Day)**: OFS open for Retail Investors and Non-Retail Investors carrying forward un-allotted bids

## Impact Assessment

This OFS is a routine promoter divestment to comply with SEBI's minimum public shareholding norms. The offer size of 3.27% of paid-up capital at a floor price of Rs.66 per share has moderate market impact, primarily affecting investors interested in String Metaverse Limited. Retail investors can participate on T+1 Day, providing broader access. Trading members and custodians need to ensure client orders are routed through BSE's designated OFS window within the stipulated schedule.
