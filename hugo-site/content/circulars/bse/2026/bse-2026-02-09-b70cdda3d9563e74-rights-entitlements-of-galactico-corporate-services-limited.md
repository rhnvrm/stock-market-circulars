## Summary

BSE has authorized trading in Rights Entitlements (REs) for GALACTICO CORPORATE SERVICES LIMITED, enabling shareholders to renounce their rights from February 11-17, 2026. The Rights Issue will remain open from February 11-23, 2026. REs will be settled on T+1 rolling settlement basis, trade-for-trade, and are tradable only in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- Company: GALACTICO CORPORATE SERVICES LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 751083
- Scrip ID: GALACT-RE
- ISIN: INE906Y20010
- Market Lot: 1
- Group: R
- Settlement: T+1 Rolling Settlement, trade-for-trade basis
- REs tradable only in dematerialized form
- Unsubscribed and un-renounced REs will lapse after issue closing date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for rights entitlement trading following established BSE procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

**For Trading Members:**
- Inform all clients about commencement of RE trading with proper ISIN identification
- Include prominent disclaimer in contract notes/bills for RE purchases clarifying:
  - REs only provide right to participate in Rights Issue or renounce before closure
  - REs are not ordinary shares of the company
  - Un-renounced/unsubscribed REs will lapse after Issue Closing Date
- Refer to notice 20200305-21 (March 05, 2020) and notice 20200518-34 (May 18, 2020) for additional guidelines

**For Investors:**
- REs must be held in demat form for trading
- Apply with requisite money or renounce before February 23, 2026 to avoid lapse

## Important Dates

- **February 9, 2026**: Circular issuance date
- **February 11, 2026**: RE trading commences; Rights Issue opens
- **February 11-17, 2026**: Renunciation trading period for REs
- **February 23, 2026**: Rights Issue closes; last date to subscribe or renounce
- **After February 23, 2026**: Un-renounced/unsubscribed REs will lapse and be extinguished

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - affects only shareholders and potential investors of GALACTICO CORPORATE SERVICES LIMITED.

**Operational Impact:** Medium for trading members - requires client communication, disclaimer inclusion in contract notes, and adherence to T+1 settlement procedures for RE trades.

**Investor Impact:** Medium for existing shareholders - provides opportunity to either participate in rights issue, renounce entitlements for trading, or allow them to lapse. Critical action required within specified timeframes to avoid loss of entitlements.
