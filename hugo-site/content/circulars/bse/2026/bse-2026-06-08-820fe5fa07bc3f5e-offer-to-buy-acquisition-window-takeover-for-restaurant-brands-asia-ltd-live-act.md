## Summary

BSE has issued a live activities schedule for the Offer to Buy (OTB) – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for **Restaurant Brands Asia Ltd (RBA)**. Trading Members and Custodian Members are required to note the bidding session timings and operational details for the offer period running from **8th June 2026 to 19th June 2026**.

## Key Points

- **Security:** Restaurant Brands Asia Ltd | Symbol: RBA | ISIN: INE07T201019 | Scrip Code: 543248
- **Offer Size:** Up to 20,80,61,717 equity shares
- **Offer Price:** Rs. 70.00 per share
- **Face Value:** Rs. 10.00
- **Market Lot:** 1
- **Opening Date:** Monday, 8th June 2026
- **Closing Date:** Friday, 19th June 2026
- Bidding is open to multiple investor categories: FII, FI, MF, IC, Other QIBs, Other NIBs, Companies, and Individuals
- OTB module accessible via iBBS and RTRMS platforms on both Internet and Leased Line

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This notice operationalises an existing takeover offer under SEBI's acquisition/takeover framework, referencing Exchange Notices 20150930-1 and 20170203-29 for participation instructions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must ensure the Unique Client Code (UCC) of each investor is registered and updated with the correct PAN number and investor category in the Exchange UCC database.
- Only demat-form shares are eligible for bidding.
- If no securities are accepted, no End of Issue files will be generated on the Extranet.
- Members requiring new login IDs or password resets for iBBS, RTRMS, or Extranet must contact BSE Trading Operations at 022-22728005 or user.trdops@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

| Activity | Period | Timings |
|---|---|---|
| Bid Offering Period | 8th June – 19th June 2026 | 9:15 a.m. – 3:30 p.m. |
| Give-up / Take-up | 8th June – 18th June 2026 | 9:15 a.m. – 3:30 p.m. |
| Give-up (last day) | 19th June 2026 | 9:15 a.m. – 3:30 p.m. |
| Take-up (last day) | 19th June 2026 | 9:15 a.m. – 4:00 p.m. |

## Impact Assessment

This offer represents a significant corporate event for Restaurant Brands Asia Ltd shareholders. With an offer size of up to ~20.8 crore shares at Rs. 70.00 per share, the total potential acquisition value is approximately Rs. 1,456 crore. Eligible shareholders holding RBA shares in demat form can tender through registered Trading Members across all investor categories. Trading members and custodians must ensure operational readiness on the iBBS and RTRMS platforms for the 12-day offer window. The extended take-up window on the final day (until 4:00 p.m.) provides slightly more flexibility for last-day custodian operations.
