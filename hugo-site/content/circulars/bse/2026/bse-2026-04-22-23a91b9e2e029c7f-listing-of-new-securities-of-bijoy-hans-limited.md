## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260422-35 informs trading members that 4,05,21,836 new equity shares of Bijoy Hans Limited (Scrip Code: 524723) have been listed and are permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Thursday, April 23, 2026. The shares were issued at a premium on a preferential basis to promoters and non-promoters.

## Key Points

- 4,05,21,836 equity shares of Rs. 10/- face value each issued at a premium of Rs. 2.50/- (issue price Rs. 12.50/-)
- Shares allotted on preferential basis to promoters and non-promoters
- Distinctive numbers range: 7500022 to 48021857
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Lock-in details are provided as a separate attachment (Distinctive_Range_LockinPeriod)
- ISIN: INE491D01017

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notice under BSE's Listing Operations department for newly allotted securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to note the new securities available for trading from April 23, 2026
- Lock-in restrictions apply to a subset of shares as per the attachment; trading members must refer to the lock-in schedule before executing trades on behalf of clients holding these shares

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** March 4, 2026
- **Trading Commencement Date:** April 23, 2026 (Thursday)
- **Notice Date:** April 22, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds approximately 40.5 million new equity shares to the tradeable float of Bijoy Hans Limited at an issue price of Rs. 12.50 per share. The influx of new shares could affect short-term price and liquidity dynamics. Lock-in restrictions on promoter portions will limit immediate selling pressure from that segment. The pari-passu ranking means no preferential dividend or voting rights distinction from existing shares.
