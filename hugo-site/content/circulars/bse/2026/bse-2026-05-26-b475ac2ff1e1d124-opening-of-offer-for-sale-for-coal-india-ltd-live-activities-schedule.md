## Summary

BSE Trading Operations has issued a notice (No. 20260526-32) announcing the live activities schedule for the opening of the Offer for Sale (OFS) for Coal India Ltd on the equity segment. This notice provides operational guidance to market participants for the OFS session.

## Key Points

- Notice issued by BSE Trading Operations department on 26 May 2026
- Pertains to the Offer for Sale (OFS) for Coal India Ltd (COALINDIA)
- Covers the live activities schedule for the OFS opening session
- Applicable to the Equity segment
- No attachments included with this notice

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an operational notice providing the schedule of live activities related to the Coal India Ltd OFS session.

## Compliance Requirements

Brokers, trading members, and market participants are required to:
- Take note of the live activities schedule for the OFS session
- Ensure systems and operations are aligned with the announced schedule
- Facilitate client orders for the OFS within the designated session timings

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 26 May 2026
- **OFS Opening:** 26 May 2026 (as per the live activities schedule)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has high market impact as Coal India Ltd is a major public sector undertaking with significant retail and institutional investor participation. The OFS opening provides an opportunity for investors to acquire shares, and the live activities schedule is essential for trading members to plan their operations and manage client orders efficiently during the OFS window.
