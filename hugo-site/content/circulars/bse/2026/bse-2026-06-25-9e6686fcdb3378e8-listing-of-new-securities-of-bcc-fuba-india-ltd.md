## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 44,32,220 new equity shares of BCC Fuba India Ltd (Scrip Code: 517246) are permitted for trading effective Monday, June 29, 2026. These shares arise from the conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up equity shares of Rs. 10/- each, issued on a rights basis.

## Key Points

- 44,32,220 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each are admitted for trading on BSE from June 29, 2026.
- Shares result from conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up, issued on rights basis.
- ISIN: INE788D01016.
- Distinctive numbers range: 15310051 to 19742270.
- New shares are pari-passu with the existing equity shares of BCC Fuba India Ltd.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification under BSE's Listing Operations department.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the admission of these new securities and may trade in them from the effective date. No additional compliance action is required beyond standard trading norms.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 25, 2026
- **Trading Effective From:** Monday, June 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This listing has a limited and localised impact, affecting only existing shareholders of BCC Fuba India Ltd who converted their partly paid shares. The new shares are pari-passu with existing equity, so there is no dilution in rights beyond what was already factored in at the time of the rights issue. Market impact is minimal.
