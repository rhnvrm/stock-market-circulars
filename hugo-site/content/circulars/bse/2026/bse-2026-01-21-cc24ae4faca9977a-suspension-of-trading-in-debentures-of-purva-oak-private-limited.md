## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in debentures of PURVA OAK PRIVATE LIMITED effective January 27, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for early redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the no-dealings date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspended for PURVA OAK PRIVATE LIMITED debentures (ISIN: INE0SRE07015, Code: 975840)
- Debenture series: POPL-12.50%-RESET RATE-19-11-2
- Record date set for January 27, 2026
- No dealings permitted from January 27, 2026
- Purpose: Early redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Notice number: DR-806/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notification for debenture suspension due to redemption activity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from January 27, 2026
- Trading members are required to take note of this suspension
- All market participants must comply with the no-dealings restriction

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 21, 2026
- **Record Date**: January 27, 2026
- **No Dealings From**: January 27, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The suspension affects holders and potential traders of PURVA OAK PRIVATE LIMITED's 12.50% reset rate debentures. Investors holding these debentures will receive their interest payment and principal redemption based on holdings as of the record date. The trading halt prevents any transactions in these instruments from the specified date, ensuring proper settlement for the corporate action. Impact is limited to this specific debt instrument and does not affect broader market operations or other securities.
