## Summary

BSE has announced the schedule of activities for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for NETLINK SOLUTIONS INDIA LIMITED. The offer will run from Wednesday, January 28, 2026 to Tuesday, February 10, 2026. The acquirer is offering to purchase up to 6,57,599 equity shares at Rs. 201.00 per share.

## Key Points

- **Security**: NETLINK SOLUTIONS INDIA LIMITED (Symbol: NETLINK, ISIN: INE040F01033, Scrip Code: 509040)
- **Offer Size**: Up to 6,57,599 equity shares
- **Offer Price**: Rs. 201.00 per share
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10.00
- **Market Lot**: 1 share
- **Offer Period**: January 28, 2026 to February 10, 2026
- **Bid Offering Timings**: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM (daily during offer period)
- Trading members can only bid for investors having shares in demat form
- UCC of investors must be registered with correct PAN and category

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard takeover offer process following existing OTB framework outlined in Exchange Notices 20150930-1 (September 30, 2015) and 20170203-29 (February 3, 2017).

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members and Custodians**: Must ensure investor UCC is registered/updated with correct PAN number and category in Exchange UCC database
- **Trading Members**: Can only bid for investors holding shares in demat form
- **Bidding Categories**: FII (Foreign Institutional Buyer), FI (Financial Institution), MF (Mutual Fund), IC (Insurance Companies), OTH (Other QIBs), NOH (Other), CO (Company), IND (Individual)
- Members must access OTB module through iBBS platform (Internet: https://ibbs.bseindia.com/ or Leased Line: https://ibbsll.bseindia.com/)
- RTRMS access required (Internet: https://rtrms.bseindia.com/ or Leased Line: https://rtrmsll.bseindia.com/)

## Important Dates

- **Opening Date**: Wednesday, January 28, 2026
- **Closing Date**: Tuesday, February 10, 2026
- **Bid Offering Period**: January 28, 2026 to February 10, 2026 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Give-up/Take-up Period**: January 28, 2026 to February 9, 2026 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Give-up (Last Day)**: February 10, 2026 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Take-up (Last Day)**: February 10, 2026 (9:15 AM - 4:00 PM)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a company-specific takeover offer affecting only NETLINK SOLUTIONS INDIA LIMITED shareholders.

**Operational Impact**: Medium for trading members - Members handling NETLINK shares need to facilitate bid submissions for their clients during the offer period. Requires operational coordination for UCC verification and demat account confirmation.

**Investor Impact**: High for NETLINK shareholders - Shareholders have opportunity to tender their shares at Rs. 201.00 per share during the 14-day offer window. Decision to participate depends on individual assessment of offer price versus current market price and future prospects.

**Contact Points**: BSE has provided detailed contact information for technical support (iBBS, RTRMS, Extranet access) and operational queries through Trading Operations and Listing Sales teams.
