## Summary

BSE has announced the Live Activities Schedule for the Offer to Buy (OTB) – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for Kesoram Industries Ltd (Scrip Code: 502937, Symbol: KESORAMIND, ISIN: INE087A01019). The offer is open from 25th February 2026 to 12th March 2026, with an offer price of Rs. 5.48 per share for up to 8,07,72,600 equity shares.

## Key Points

- **Security:** Kesoram Industries Ltd | Symbol: KESORAMIND | ISIN: INE087A01019 | Scrip Code: 502937
- **Offer Size:** Up to 8,07,72,600 equity shares
- **Offer Price:** Rs. 5.48 per share
- **Face Value:** Rs. 10.00 per share
- **Market Lot:** 1
- **Opening Date:** Thursday, 25th February 2026
- **Closing Date:** Thursday, 12th March 2026
- **Session Timings:** 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. (Bid Offering Period); Take-up on last day extended to 4:00 p.m.
- Bidding categories include FII, FI, MF, IC, Other QIBs, NIB, Company, and Individual investors
- OTB module accessible via iBBS and RTRMS platforms on both Internet and Leased Line

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular operationalises the takeover acquisition window under existing OTB framework. Members are referred to Exchange Notices 20150930-1 (dated 30 Sep 2015) and 20170203-29 (dated 3 Feb 2017) for operational instructions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must ensure UCC (Unique Client Code) of investors is registered and/or updated with the correct PAN Number and Category in the Exchange UCC database.
- Trading Members can only bid for investors holding shares in demat form.
- Members and Custodians should note that if no securities are accepted, no End of Issue files will be generated on the Extranet.
- Extranet files for acceptance of bids will be available only after the Exchange receives confirmation from the Merchant Banker/RTA post issue closure.

## Impact Assessment

This takeover acquisition window has significant market impact for Kesoram Industries Ltd shareholders and trading participants. The offer targets up to 8.07 crore shares at Rs. 5.48/share, representing a sizable open market acquisition. Trading members and custodians across all investor categories (FII, MF, Insurance, Individuals, etc.) are eligible to participate. The extended 16-day window provides ample opportunity for demat shareholders to tender their shares. Operational readiness on iBBS and RTRMS platforms is essential for seamless participation.
