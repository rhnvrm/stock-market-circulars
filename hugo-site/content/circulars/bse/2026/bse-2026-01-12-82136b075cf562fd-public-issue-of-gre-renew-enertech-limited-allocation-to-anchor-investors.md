## Summary

BSE has issued a circular regarding the allocation of equity shares to anchor investors in the public issue of GRE RENEW ENERTECH LIMITED. This is part of the company's Initial Public Offering (IPO) process where a portion of shares is allocated to qualified institutional buyers before the issue opens to the general public.

## Key Points

- Company: GRE RENEW ENERTECH LIMITED
- Transaction Type: Allocation to Anchor Investors
- Issue Type: Public Issue (IPO)
- Anchor investor allocation completed as per SEBI guidelines
- This allocation occurs one day before the issue opening date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural notification as per existing SEBI (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations.

- The company and lead managers must comply with SEBI ICDR Regulations regarding anchor investor allocation
- Disclosure of anchor investor allocation details to be made public
- Anchor investors subject to lock-in period as per regulations
- Allocation process must follow prescribed guidelines for qualified institutional buyers

## Important Dates

- Anchor Investor Allocation Date: January 12, 2026
- Public issue opening date: Expected to be January 13, 2026 or later (typically one day after anchor allocation)
- Lock-in period: 30 days from date of allotment for anchor investors
