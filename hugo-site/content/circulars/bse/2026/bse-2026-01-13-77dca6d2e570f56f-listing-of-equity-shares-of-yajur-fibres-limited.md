## Summary

Yajur Fibres Limited has filed its post-IPO shareholding pattern as on January 11, 2026, under Regulation 31(1)(a). The company is an SME listed entity with scrip code YAJUR and ISIN INE0CNJ01010. Total equity shares held are 22,682,873, with promoters holding 15,762,873 shares (69.49%) and public shareholders holding 6,920,000 shares (30.51%).

## Key Points

- Total equity shares: 22,682,873 fully paid-up shares
- Promoter & Promoter Group holding: 15,762,873 shares (69.49%)
- Public shareholding: 6,920,000 shares (30.51%)
- Number of promoter shareholders: 15
- Number of public shareholders: 3,514
- All promoter shares (100%) are under lock-in
- No partly paid-up shares, convertible securities, warrants, or ESOPs issued
- No pledged shares or non-disposal undertakings
- Not a Public Sector Undertaking (PSU)
- All shares held in dematerialized form

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard shareholding pattern disclosure.

## Compliance Requirements

- Shareholding pattern filed under Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI LODR Regulations
- SME company compliance with post-IPO disclosure requirements
- Promoter shares subject to lock-in provisions (100% of promoter holding)

## Important Dates

- Report Date: January 11, 2026
- Filing Date: January 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine disclosure with minimal market impact. The shareholding pattern shows standard post-IPO structure for an SME company with appropriate promoter lock-in. The 69.49% promoter holding provides stability while maintaining sufficient public float of 30.51% for trading liquidity. No concerns regarding encumbrances or convertible securities that could affect future shareholding dynamics.
