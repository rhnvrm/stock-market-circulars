## Summary

BSE has notified the listing of 76,00,000 (7.6 million) new equity shares of Apex Capital And Finance Limited (Scrip Code: 541133) on the Exchange with effect from Thursday, June 25, 2026. The shares were issued at Rs. 125/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- at a premium of Rs. 115/-) to Promoters and Non-Promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 76,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 115/- (issue price Rs. 125/-)
- Shares issued to Promoter and Non-Promoter category on a preferential basis via conversion of warrants
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- ISIN: INE758W01019
- Distribution Numbers: 5919979 to 13519978
- Date of Allotment: May 11, 2026
- Permitted to trade effective June 25, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification for new securities issued under a preferential allotment pursuant to warrant conversion.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed that these new securities are listed and permitted to trade from June 25, 2026. Lock-in restrictions apply to a majority of the allotted shares and must be observed.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** May 11, 2026
- **Trading commencement:** June 25, 2026
- **Lock-in expiry (55,48,000 shares; Dist. Nos. 5919979–11467978):** February 28, 2028
- **Lock-in expiry (2,82,079 shares; Dist. Nos. 11467979–11750057):** February 28, 2027
- **Lock-in expiry (2,82,290 shares; Dist. Nos. 11750058–12032347):** February 28, 2028
- **Lock-in expiry (14,87,631 shares; Dist. Nos. 12032348–13519978):** February 28, 2027

## Impact Assessment

This listing increases the tradeable float of Apex Capital And Finance Limited by 76,00,000 shares. However, the majority of shares (approximately 73.17 lakh out of 76 lakh) are subject to lock-in until February 2027 or February 2028, limiting near-term liquidity impact. The preferential allotment at Rs. 125/- per share via warrant conversion signals prior fundraising activity by the company. Impact is limited to existing shareholders and is a routine corporate action with no broader market implications.
