## Summary

BSE has permitted trading of Kotak Nifty Alpha 50 ETF units effective February 10, 2026, in the B Group category under the permitted securities framework. The open-ended scheme will have 15,41,26,753 units available for trading with a face value of Rs. 10 per unit and market lot of 1. Four market makers have been designated to ensure liquidity.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: ALPHA, listed in B Group
- Total Units: 15,41,26,753 units
- Face Value: Rs. 10 per unit
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- ISIN: INF174KA1IA5
- Investment Manager: Kotak Mahindra Asset Management Company Ltd
- Registered Office: 2nd Floor, 12-BKC, Plot No. C-12, G-Block, Bandra Kurla Complex, Bandra East, Mumbai - 400 051
- Registrar: Computer Age Management Services Limited (CAMS), Chennai

## Market Makers

Four designated market makers for liquidity:
1. Kotak Securities Limited
2. Parwati Capital
3. Kanjalochana Finserve Private Limited
4. Motilal Oswal Financial Services Limited

## Regulatory Framework

Trading permitted under BSE's provisions for "permitted securities category" which allows dealings in securities listed on other exchanges.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: February 9, 2026
- Trading Commencement: February 10, 2026 (Tuesday)
