## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in commercial papers of 22 companies effective from their respective record dates (January 22-26, 2026) due to scheduled redemptions. Trading members are advised not to deal in these commercial papers from the specified no-dealings dates.

## Key Points

- 22 commercial papers suspended for trading due to redemption
- Record dates: January 22, 23, 24, and 26, 2026
- No dealings allowed from respective record dates
- Affects major financial institutions including ICICI Securities, Bajaj Finance, Poonawalla Fincorp, Kotak Mahindra Prime, and Nuvama entities
- Notice numbers: DR-804/2025-2026 and DR-805/2025-2026

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified commercial papers from their respective no-dealings dates
- All transactions must cease before the record date for redemption
- Members should update their systems to reflect trading suspension

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 19, 2026
- **No Dealings Start**: January 22, 2026 (for 11 CPs) and January 23, 2026 (for 11 CPs)
- **Record Dates**: January 22, 23, 24, and 26, 2026
- **Purpose**: Redemption of Commercial Paper

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action affecting the debt market segment. The suspension impacts commercial papers from major NBFCs and financial services companies. Trading members holding these instruments should plan redemption settlement. No impact on equity markets. The staggered record dates allow for systematic processing of multiple redemptions across different issuers.
