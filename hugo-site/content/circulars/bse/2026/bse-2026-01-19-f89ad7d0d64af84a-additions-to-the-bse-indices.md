## Summary

BSE announces the addition of two newly listed companies to its indices. Bharat Coking Coal Limited (ticker: 544678) will be added to the BSE IPO Index effective January 21, 2026, following its listing on January 19, 2026. Defrail Technologies Limited (ticker: 544677) will be added to the BSE SME IPO Index effective January 20, 2026, after its listing on the SME platform on January 19, 2026.

## Key Points

- Bharat Coking Coal Limited (544678) listed on BSE on January 19, 2026
- Will be added to BSE IPO Index at market open on January 21, 2026
- Defrail Technologies Limited (544677) listed on BSE SME platform on January 19, 2026
- Will be added to BSE SME IPO Index at market open on January 20, 2026
- Changes included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index maintenance action following new listings.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index-tracking funds and portfolios may need to adjust holdings to reflect the index composition changes.

## Important Dates

- **January 19, 2026**: Listing date for both Bharat Coking Coal Limited and Defrail Technologies Limited
- **January 20, 2026**: Defrail Technologies Limited added to BSE SME IPO Index (at market open)
- **January 21, 2026**: Bharat Coking Coal Limited added to BSE IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. These are routine index additions following new listings. Index funds tracking BSE IPO and BSE SME IPO indices will need to acquire shares of the respective companies. The additions reflect the normal functioning of BSE's index maintenance process and support market visibility for newly listed companies.
