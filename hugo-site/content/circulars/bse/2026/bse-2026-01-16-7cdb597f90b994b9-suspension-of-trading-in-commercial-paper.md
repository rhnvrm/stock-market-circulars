## Summary

BSE has issued a notice suspending trading in Commercial Paper issued by Kotak Mahindra Investments Limited (Scrip Code: 730922, ISIN: INE975F14C82) effective from January 19, 2026, as the instrument is scheduled to mature on January 20, 2026.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper issuer: Kotak Mahindra Investments Limited
- Scrip Code: 730922
- ISIN: INE975F14C82
- Trading suspension effective date: January 19, 2026
- Redemption/maturity date: January 20, 2026
- Notice reference: DR-801/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational notice for instrument maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must cease all trading activities in the specified Commercial Paper from January 19, 2026
- Members are advised not to accept or execute any orders for this instrument
- Acknowledgment and compliance with the suspension directive is mandatory

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 16, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Date**: January 19, 2026
- **Redemption Date**: January 20, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative action with minimal market impact. The suspension is procedural, occurring one trading day before the instrument's scheduled redemption. Trading Members holding positions should ensure settlement processes are aligned with the maturity schedule. No broader implications for the debt market or issuer creditworthiness.
