## Summary

BSE has issued an update to its Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LT-ASM) framework, referencing prior notices dating back to March 2018 and subsequent revisions through September 2024. The notice specifies securities being newly placed under LT-ASM, securities moving to higher stages within the framework, securities moving out of the framework, and securities being transferred to T/XT/MT/TS trading groups while continuing under LT-ASM. Full lists are provided in Annexures I (Parts A, B, D), II, and III.

## Key Points

- 100% margin shall apply on all open positions as on July 06, 2026 and new positions created from July 07, 2026 onwards, for securities newly placed under LT-ASM (Annexure I, Part A)
- Securities moving to higher stages of the LT-ASM framework take effect July 03, 2026 (Annexure I, Part B)
- Lower Price Band shall be applicable w.e.f. July 03, 2026 for the above securities
- Securities continuing in LT-ASM but being transferred to T/XT/MT/TS Group effective July 07, 2026 are listed in Annexure I (Part D)
- Securities moving out of the LT-ASM framework w.e.f. July 03, 2026 are listed in Annexure II
- A consolidated list of all securities currently under the LT-ASM framework is provided in Annexure III
- Securities under the framework are reviewed periodically
- Shortlisting is based on XBRL submissions by listed companies and is intended solely for market surveillance, not as an adverse action against any company

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced; this is a periodic update/review under the existing LT-ASM framework established via BSE notices dated March 21, 2018, October 27, 2018, December 04, 2020, June 04, 2021, August 09, 2024, and September 20, 2024.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must apply 100% margin on open and new positions in securities newly added to LT-ASM as specified
- Trading members must observe the revised (lower) price bands applicable to affected securities
- Members should note the transfer of certain LT-ASM securities to T/XT/MT/TS trading groups
- Queries may be directed to bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- July 03, 2026: Lower price band effective; securities move to higher LT-ASM stages; securities move out of LT-ASM framework
- July 06, 2026: Reference date for existing open positions subject to 100% margin
- July 07, 2026: 100% margin applicable on open and new positions; securities transferred to T/XT/MT/TS Group

## Impact Assessment

Securities newly placed under LT-ASM or moved to higher stages face materially reduced trading liquidity due to 100% margin requirements and tighter price bands, which can suppress speculative trading and increase capital costs for market participants holding these positions. Conversely, securities exiting the framework will see their price bands reinstated to pre-LT-ASM levels, potentially restoring normal trading activity, subject to no other surveillance measure applying. Overall market impact is stock-specific and depends on the individual securities named in the annexures, which were not itemized in the extracted text.
