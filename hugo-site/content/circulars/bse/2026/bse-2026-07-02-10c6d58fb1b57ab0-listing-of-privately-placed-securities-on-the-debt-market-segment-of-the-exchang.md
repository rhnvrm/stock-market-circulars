## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that new securities issued on a private placement basis have been listed and admitted to trading on the BSE Debt Segment effective 02 July 2026. The listing covers commercial papers (CPs) issued by Time Technoplast Limited, Nuvama Wealth Management Limited, Tata Steel Limited, and ICICI Home Finance Company Limited, along with privately placed debt securities including an issuance by Ariel Trust 2026.

## Key Points

- Time Technoplast Limited: Commercial Paper (ISIN INE508G14JC1), face value Rs. 5,00,000, quantity 1,500, issue price Rs. 4,81,397, interest rate 7.75% p.a., redemption on 31/12/2026, IPA IDBI Bank Ltd.
- Nuvama Wealth Management Limited: Commercial Paper (ISIN INE531F14GA5), face value Rs. 5,00,000, quantity 700, issue price Rs. 4,92,480, interest rate 7.85% p.a., redemption on 10/09/2026, IPA ICICI Bank Ltd.
- Tata Steel Limited: Commercial Paper (ISIN INE081A14HE2), face value Rs. 5,00,000, quantity 16,000, issue price Rs. 4,91,826.50, interest rate 6.74% p.a., redemption on 29/09/2026, IPA HDFC Bank Limited.
- ICICI Home Finance Company Limited: Commercial Paper (ISIN INE071G14IA6), face value Rs. 5,00,000, quantity 6,000, issue price Rs. 4,67,105.50, interest rate 7.20% p.a., redemption on 24/06/2027, IPA ICICI Bank Limited.
- Ariel Trust 2026: Privately placed debt security listing, quantity 497,028,840 (details truncated in source notice).
- All securities will trade only in dematerialised form under the specified ISIN numbers.
- Commercial papers will trade in standard denominations of Rs. 5 lakh and multiples thereof, with a tick size of 1 paise.
- Debt securities under the private placement route also carry a tick size of 1 paise; further details are available via BSE's Placement Memorandum portal.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine administrative listing notice under BSE's Debt Market Segment rules for privately placed instruments; no new regulatory changes are introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must note the applicable ISIN numbers, trading denominations, and tick sizes for each newly listed instrument. For debt securities issued under private placement, members should refer to the Placement Memorandum on BSE's website for further details. Queries can be directed to BSE's Debt Department.

## Important Dates

- Effective listing date: 02 July 2026
- Time Technoplast CP: allotment 02/07/2026, redemption 31/12/2026
- Nuvama Wealth Management CP: allotment 01/07/2026, redemption 10/09/2026
- Tata Steel CP: allotment 01/07/2026, redemption 29/09/2026
- ICICI Home Finance CP: allotment 02/07/2026, redemption 24/06/2027

## Impact Assessment

This notice is procedural and primarily relevant to debt market participants and trading members dealing in commercial paper and privately placed debt instruments. It does not directly affect the equity trading or share price of the listed companies (Time Technoplast, Nuvama Wealth Management, Tata Steel, ICICI Home Finance) but confirms their access to short-term and structured debt funding markets, which can be a minor positive indicator of ongoing liquidity management and creditworthiness (reflected in A1+ ratings from CRISIL, ICRA, CARE, and IND).
