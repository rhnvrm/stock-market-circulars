## Summary

BSE has communicated a SEBI order (No. QJA/MN/NRO/NRO-DIV-III/32419/2026-27 dated May 26, 2026) debarring Mr. Nishaan Singh, Proprietor of Sanbun Investments, from accessing the securities market. This is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20240130-61 dated January 30, 2024.

## Key Points

- Mr. Nishaan Singh (Proprietor of Sanbun Investments) is debarred from accessing the securities market, directly or indirectly, for 3 months from May 26, 2026, or until compliance with directions at paragraph 99 (a), (g), and (h) of the SEBI order, whichever is later.
- The noticee is prohibited from buying, selling, or otherwise dealing in securities in any manner whatsoever during the debarment period.
- If the noticee holds open positions in exchange-traded derivative contracts as of the order date, they may close out/square off such positions within 3 months or at contract expiry, whichever is earlier.
- Settlement of pay-in and pay-out obligations for transactions executed before the close of trading on the order date is permitted.
- The order is effective from May 26, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action pursuant to an existing SEBI investigation.

## Compliance Requirements

- All trading members must take note of the debarment order and ensure Mr. Nishaan Singh / Sanbun Investments is not permitted to trade on the exchange.
- Trading members must block the entity from accessing the securities market directly or indirectly.
- Members must allow only the permitted activities: squaring off open derivative positions within the specified window and settling pre-order transactions.

## Important Dates

- **Order Date:** May 26, 2026
- **BSE Notice Date:** May 27, 2026
- **Debarment Period:** 3 months from May 26, 2026 (i.e., until August 26, 2026), or until compliance with SEBI directions, whichever is later.
- **Derivative Position Close-out Deadline:** Within 3 months of the order date or at contract expiry, whichever is earlier.

## Impact Assessment

This order directly impacts Mr. Nishaan Singh and Sanbun Investments, restricting their securities market access. The broader market impact is limited as this is an enforcement action against a single individual/proprietorship. Trading members are required to operationally enforce the debarment and monitor for any attempted circumvention. The case is a continuation of a prior 2024 notice, suggesting an ongoing regulatory investigation.
