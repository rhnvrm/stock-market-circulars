## Summary

BSE announces the addition of two newly listed companies to its indices. Shadowfax Technologies Limited (ticker: 544685), listed on January 28, 2026, will be added to the BSE IPO index effective January 30, 2026. Digilogic Systems Limited (ticker: 544684), listed on the SME platform on January 28, 2026, will be added to the BSE SME IPO index effective January 29, 2026.

## Key Points

- Shadowfax Technologies Limited (544685) added to BSE IPO index effective at open on January 30, 2026
- Digilogic Systems Limited (544684) added to BSE SME IPO index effective at open on January 29, 2026
- Both companies listed on BSE on January 28, 2026
- Changes included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- Reference notices: 20260127-50 (Shadowfax) and 20260127-49 (Digilogic)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index composition update following new listings.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance requirements for market participants. Index tracking funds and passive investment strategies may need to adjust portfolios to reflect the new index composition.

## Important Dates

- **January 28, 2026**: Listing date for both Shadowfax Technologies Limited and Digilogic Systems Limited
- **January 29, 2026**: Digilogic Systems Limited added to BSE SME IPO index (effective at open)
- **January 30, 2026**: Shadowfax Technologies Limited added to BSE IPO index (effective at open)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. Routine index additions following IPO listings. Index funds tracking BSE IPO and BSE SME IPO indices will need to include these securities in their portfolios. The additions reflect normal market operations and expansion of the listed universe on BSE.
