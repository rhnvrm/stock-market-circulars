## Summary

BSE's Surveillance department issued Notice No. 20260223-26 on 23 February 2026, seeking clarification from IDFC First Bank Ltd (Scrip Code: 539437) regarding news articles published across newspapers, websites, and television channels. The exchange sought this clarification to verify the accuracy of the news and to keep members and investors informed.

## Key Points

- BSE Surveillance proactively sought clarification from IDFC First Bank Ltd (Scrip Code: 539437) regarding circulating news articles.
- The news article in question was sourced from The Economic Times (economictimes.indiatimes.com).
- The company's reply has been received and is attached to the notice.
- Notice issued by Deputy Vice Presidents Sanjay Jain and Soumil Shah of BSE Surveillance.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this circular. It is a standard surveillance action by BSE to ensure price/news accuracy and market integrity.

## Compliance Requirements

- IDFC First Bank Ltd was required to respond to BSE's clarification request regarding the news article.
- The company's reply is attached and accessible via the notice.
- No additional compliance action is required from other listed entities or market participants.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 23 February 2026
- **Notice Number:** 20260223-26
