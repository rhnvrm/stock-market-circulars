## Summary

Aditya Birla Sun Life Mutual Fund is launching the ADITYA BIRLA SUN LIFE BSE TOP 10 BANKS ETF as a New Fund Offer (NFO) on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The NFO is open from February 23 to February 25, 2026, with a tentative allotment date of February 27, 2026. Both a regular plan and a direct plan are being offered under the equity category.

## Key Points

- NFO period: February 23, 2026 to February 25, 2026 on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Two schemes offered: ADITYA BIRLA SUN LIFE BSE TOP 10 BANKS ETF and its Direct Plan, both under the Equity category
- Tentative allotment date for both schemes: February 27, 2026
- Lumpsum purchases extended till midnight on February 25, 2026 on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Transactions where the client has not initiated funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 will be cancelled with a CC with N flag reported to RTA/AMC
- Pending transactions (with initiated payment) remain in system till 3:00 PM on the last day of funds payout to AMC or T+5, whichever is earlier
- Cheques and OTM payments are acceptable during the NFO period subject to clear funds reaching ICCL account

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard NFO launch notification under existing BSE StAR MF operational framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clients initiate fund transfers by 9:00 AM on T+1 to avoid transaction cancellation
- Clear funds must be available in the ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on February 27, 2026, to qualify for unit allotment
- Cheque and OTM payment modes are permissible only if clear funds are credited to ICCL by the 6:00 PM deadline on February 27, 2026

## Important Dates

- **February 23, 2026**: NFO opens
- **February 25, 2026**: NFO closes (lumpsum purchases extended till midnight)
- **February 27, 2026**: Tentative allotment date; clear funds must be in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM
- **T+1 by 9:00 AM**: Deadline for clients to initiate fund transfers to avoid cancellation
- **T+5 or last payout day by 3:00 PM**: Outer limit for pending transactions with initiated payments

## Impact Assessment

This circular is operationally relevant to BSE members and distributors facilitating mutual fund transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform. Investors in banking-sector equity ETFs have a new instrument focused on the top 10 banks.
