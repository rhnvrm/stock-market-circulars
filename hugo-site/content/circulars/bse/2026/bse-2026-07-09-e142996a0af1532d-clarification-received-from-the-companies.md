## Summary

BSE observed significant price/volume movement in the scrips of eleven listed companies in the recent past and had written to them seeking clarification. The Exchange has received and attached replies from five companies, while replies from the remaining six companies are still awaited.

## Key Points

- Companies that have replied (clarifications attached):
  1. D.P. Abhushan Ltd (Scrip Code: 544161)
  2. LE Lavoir Ltd (Scrip Code: 539814)
  3. Saraswati Commercial India Ltd (Scrip Code: 512020)
  4. Tirupati Starch & Chemicals Ltd (Scrip Code: 524582)
  5. Vaibhav Global Ltd (Scrip Code: 532156)
- Companies whose replies are awaited:
  1. KPT Industries Ltd (Scrip Code: 505299)
  2. CMX Holdings Ltd (Scrip Code: 532217)
  3. Mapro Industries Ltd (Scrip Code: 509762)
  4. SC Agrotech Ltd (Scrip Code: 526081)
  5. Sujala Trading & Holdings Ltd (Scrip Code: 539117)
  6. VJTF Eduservices Ltd (Scrip Code: 509026)
- Issued by BSE's Surveillance Department (DOSS) to inform trading members and investors of the latest relevant information on these scrips.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine surveillance disclosure, not a regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

Companies with pending replies are expected to respond to the Exchange's clarification request. Trading members and investors should refer to the attached replies (Replies1, Replies2) for details on the price/volume movement in the listed scrips.

## Important Dates

Notice dated 09 July 2026 (Notice No. 20260709-37).

## Impact Assessment

Low-to-medium market impact. The disclosure serves an investor-protection and transparency function by making company clarifications on unusual price/volume activity publicly available, but does not itself impose trading restrictions or new obligations.
