## Summary

BSE has notified members that TATA MUTUAL FUND will offer the TATA MULTI SECTOR PASSIVE FOF as a New Fund Offer (NFO) with SIP facility on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The NFO subscription window runs from June 22, 2026 to July 6, 2026, covering six scheme variants across Regular and Direct plans. Tentative allotment date is July 9, 2026.

## Key Points

- NFO open period: June 22, 2026 to July 6, 2026 on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Six scheme variants offered under TATA MULTI SECTOR PASSIVE FOF (Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options)
- Tentative allotment date: July 9, 2026
- Clear funds must be available in the ICCL account by 6:00 PM on July 9, 2026
- NFO schemes extended till midnight on July 6, 2026 for lumpsum purchases only
- SIP/XSIP registrations must be authenticated by clients on or before 3:00 PM on July 6, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notice informing members of a new NFO availability on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- Transactions where clients do not initiate funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day (including those during extended NFO period) will be cancelled; CC with 'N' flag must be reported to RTA/AMC.
- Transactions with client-initiated payment remain pending until 3:00 PM on the last day of funds pay-out to AMC or T+5 day, whichever is earlier.
- Cheque and OTM payments accepted during NFO period only if clear funds are received in ICCL account by 6:00 PM on July 9, 2026.
- Switch transactions accepted during NFO period subject to receipt of funds prior to allotment date; switch-out funds received on or after allotment date will not be considered.
- Members must ensure SIP/XSIP client authentication is completed by 3:00 PM on July 6, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **June 22, 2026**: NFO subscription opens
- **July 6, 2026 (3:00 PM)**: Deadline for SIP/XSIP registration authentication by clients
- **July 6, 2026 (midnight)**: NFO closes for lumpsum purchases on BSE StAR MF Platform
- **July 9, 2026 (6:00 PM)**: Deadline for clear funds to be available in ICCL account
- **July 9, 2026**: Tentative allotment date for all six schemes

## Impact Assessment

This notice is operationally relevant for BSE members and distributors participating in mutual fund transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform. Members handling TATA AMC NFO subscriptions must adhere to fund initiation timelines (T+1 by 9:00 AM) and ensure clear funds are received before the allotment date. The NFO covers six equity scheme variants with both lumpsum and SIP/XSIP options, broadening investment choices for clients seeking passive multi-sector fund-of-fund exposure.
