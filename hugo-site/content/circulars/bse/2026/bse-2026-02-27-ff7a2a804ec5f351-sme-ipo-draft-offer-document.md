## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of Futurex Management Solutions Limited, filed on February 25, 2026, on the Stock Exchange website. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and is open for public comments for a minimum period of 21 days from the date of notice.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Futurex Management Solutions Limited on February 25, 2026
- Document hosted on the BSE SME website in compliance with Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Public comments invited within 21 days from February 27, 2026 (deadline: March 20, 2026)
- Comments to be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Document accessible via BSE SME portal

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular is issued in compliance with existing ICDR (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, specifically Schedule VI, which mandates public availability of draft offer documents for SME IPOs.

## Compliance Requirements

- The Draft Offer Document is made available on the Stock Exchange website for at least 21 days as required under ICDR Regulations
- Interested parties and the public are requested to submit any comments on the draft offer document within 21 days from the date of notice to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Futurex Management Solutions Limited is required to address public comments received during the review period

## Important Dates

- **Filing Date:** February 25, 2026
- **Notice Date:** February 27, 2026
- **Public Comment Deadline:** March 20, 2026 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited broader market impact as it pertains to a single SME company's IPO process. It is relevant to retail and institutional investors interested in participating in the upcoming IPO of Futurex Management Solutions Limited. The 21-day public comment window provides an opportunity for market participants to review disclosures and flag any concerns before the IPO proceeds. No immediate trading or compliance action is required from existing market participants.
