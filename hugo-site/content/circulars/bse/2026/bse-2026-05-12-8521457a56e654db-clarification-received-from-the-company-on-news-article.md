## Summary

BSE's Surveillance department sought clarification from Vodafone Idea Ltd (Scrip Code: 532822) regarding a news article published on BusinessToday.in. This is a routine disclosure measure undertaken by the Exchange to verify news circulating in media and to keep members and investors informed about the accuracy of such reports.

## Key Points

- BSE Notice No. 20260512-22, dated 12 May 2026
- Clarification sought from Vodafone Idea Ltd (Scrip Code: 532822) concerning a news article on https://www.businesstoday.in
- Notice issued by BSE Surveillance department (DOSS)
- Signed by Sanjay Jain (Deputy Vice President, Surveillance) and Shreya Ghose (Manager, Surveillance)
- A reply/attachment from the company is referenced in the notice

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this notice.

## Compliance Requirements

Vodafone Idea Ltd was required to provide a clarification to BSE regarding the referenced news article. The company's reply is attached to the notice.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 12 May 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact, routine surveillance action. The Exchange periodically seeks such clarifications to maintain market integrity and protect investor interests. The outcome depends on the content of Vodafone Idea's response; no trading restrictions or penalties are indicated by this notice alone.
