## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of PRACHAY CAPITAL LIMITED effective January 30, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption on exercise of put option and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspended for debentures with ISIN: INE0IID07710 (Code: 976847)
- Debenture series: PCL-14%-14-8-29-UL-PVT
- Record date set for January 30, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption on exercise of put option and payment of interest
- No dealings permitted from January 30, 2026
- Notice reference: DR-809/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a corporate action notification requiring temporary trading suspension.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in the specified debentures from January 30, 2026
- Trading members must note the suspension and inform relevant stakeholders
- No buy or sell transactions permitted in the affected debenture series

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 21, 2026
- **Record Date**: January 30, 2026
- **No Dealings From**: January 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: High - Complete trading suspension affects liquidity and investor ability to transact in these debentures. Holders of the debentures exercising put options will have their holdings redeemed.

**Investor Impact**: Investors holding these debentures cannot trade them from the suspension date. Those eligible can exercise put options for redemption and will receive interest payments as per the record date.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to block trades in the specified ISIN and communicate the suspension to clients holding or interested in these securities.
