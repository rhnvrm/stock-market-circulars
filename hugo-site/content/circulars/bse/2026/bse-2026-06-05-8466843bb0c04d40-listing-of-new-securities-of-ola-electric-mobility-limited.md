## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 21,75,78,428 new equity shares of Ola Electric Mobility Limited (Scrip Code: 544225, ISIN: INE0LXG01040) are listed and admitted for trading on the Exchange with effect from Monday, June 8, 2026. These shares were allotted to Qualified Institutional Buyers (QIBs) pursuant to a Qualified Institutional Placement (QIP) at an issue price of Rs. 35.86 per share.

## Key Points

- 21,75,78,428 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each allotted via Qualified Institutional Placement (QIP) to QIBs
- Issue price: Rs. 35.86 per share
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Date of allotment: June 4, 2026
- Effective date for trading: Monday, June 8, 2026
- Distribution numbers: 4410829886 to 4628408313
- Scrip Code: 544225 | ISIN: INE0LXG01040

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice for newly allotted securities under a QIP.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members may contact Mr. Karan Shah at Tel. No. 022 2272 8987 for any clarifications regarding this notice.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** June 4, 2026
- **Effective Date for Trading:** Monday, June 8, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing of approximately 21.76 crore new equity shares represents a notable dilution event for existing shareholders of Ola Electric Mobility Limited. These QIP shares, issued at Rs. 35.86 per share, are now freely tradeable on BSE from June 8, 2026. The QIP indicates institutional interest in the company's growth trajectory. Market participants should factor in the increased share count when assessing valuations and per-share metrics.
