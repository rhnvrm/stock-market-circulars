## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Equity segment on Saturday, April 18, 2026, operating from both the Primary Site (PR) and Disaster Recovery Site (DR). The session provides trading members and third-party platform users an opportunity to test trading applications across various functionalities. A new BOLT Pro TWS version 12.60 will also be released, with details to follow via a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session for Equity segment on April 18, 2026 (Saturday)
- Sessions run from both Primary Site (PR) and Disaster Recovery Site (DR)
- Third-party trading platform users may also participate to test their applications
- Functionalities covered include: call auction sessions, risk-reduction mode, trading halt, block deals, etc.
- New BOLT Pro TWS version 12.60 to be released; details via separate circular
- Trades from mock session carry no margin, pay-in/pay-out obligations, or legal rights/liabilities
- Members requested to submit feedback by 3:00 pm on the day

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a scheduled operational/testing event.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are requested to actively participate in the mock trading session
- Members must submit feedback on the session by 3:00 pm on April 18, 2026, via provided contact numbers or email
- Queries should be directed to respective Relationship Managers

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine mock trading exercise with no financial or legal impact on participants. Trades executed during the session do not create margin obligations, pay-in/pay-out obligations, or any rights and liabilities. The session is relevant for trading members who wish to test system readiness, especially with the upcoming BOLT Pro TWS 12.60 release. DR site switchover testing adds operational resilience value for members managing contingency workflows.
