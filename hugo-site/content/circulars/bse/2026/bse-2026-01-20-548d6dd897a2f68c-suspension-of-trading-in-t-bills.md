## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in T-Bill with SCRIP CODE 805133 (ISIN IN002025X307, 91TB220126) effective from January 21, 2026, due to its maturity on January 22, 2026. Trading members are advised not to deal with this T-Bill from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- T-Bill SCRIP CODE: 805133
- ISIN: IN002025X307
- Particulars: 91TB220126
- Maturity/Redemption Date: January 22, 2026
- Trading Suspension Effective Date: January 21, 2026
- Notice Number: 20260120-3
- Circular Reference: DR-803/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational procedure for securities approaching maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal with the specified T-Bill (805133) from January 21, 2026
- Members are required to take note of the suspension and ensure compliance
- No new trades should be executed in this instrument after the suspension date

## Important Dates

- **January 20, 2026**: Circular issued
- **January 21, 2026**: Trading suspension effective date
- **January 22, 2026**: T-Bill maturity/redemption date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine suspension affecting a single T-Bill approaching maturity. Such suspensions are standard practice to prevent trading in instruments about to be redeemed.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Trading members simply need to ensure no trades are executed in this specific T-Bill from January 21, 2026. The instrument will be redeemed the following day, returning principal to holders.

**Investor Impact**: Low - Investors holding this T-Bill will receive redemption proceeds on the maturity date. No action required beyond normal redemption processing.
