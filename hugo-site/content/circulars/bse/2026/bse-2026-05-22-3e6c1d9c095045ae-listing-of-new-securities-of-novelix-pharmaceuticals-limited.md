## Summary

BSE has listed 23,20,000 new equity shares of Novelix Pharmaceuticals Limited (Scrip Code: 536565) issued on a preferential basis to Promoters and Non-Promoters at an issue price of Rs. 60/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- + premium Rs. 50/-). These shares are permitted to trade on BSE with effect from Monday, May 25, 2026.

## Key Points

- 23,20,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 50/- (total price Rs. 60/-) on preferential basis
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 17615001 to 19935000
- ISIN: INE314I01036
- Date of allotment: February 12, 2026
- Trading commences: May 25, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notice pursuant to a completed preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the new securities and their applicable lock-in periods before facilitating any transactions.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** February 12, 2026
- **Trading Effective From:** May 25, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (3,25,000 shares — dist. nos. 17615001–17940000):** November 30, 2027
- **Lock-in Expiry (19,95,000 shares — dist. nos. 17940001–19935000):** November 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. The addition of 23,20,000 shares increases the tradeable float of Novelix Pharmaceuticals Limited, though the bulk of newly listed shares (19,95,000) remain under lock-in until November 2026 and a smaller tranche (3,25,000) until November 2027. Only shares outside the lock-in period are freely tradeable from May 25, 2026.
