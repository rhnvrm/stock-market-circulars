## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that new commercial paper (CP) securities issued on a private placement basis by four issuers - 360 ONE Alternates Asset Management Limited (two tranches), Time Technoplast Limited, Bajaj Financial Securities Limited, and Indian Oil Corporation Ltd. - have been listed and admitted to trading on the BSE Debt Segment effective 10 July 2026.

## Key Points

- All securities are commercial papers with a face value and paid-up value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, traded only in dematerialised form.
- Trading will occur in standard denominations of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof, with a tick size of 1 paise.
- **360 ONE Alternates Asset Management Limited**: Two CP issuances - 360OAAML27 (ISIN INE0TQI14525, 100 units, 7.45% p.a., maturing 05/04/2027, rated CARE A1+) and 360OAAML26 (ISIN INE0TQI14533, 100 units, 7.20% p.a., maturing 08/10/2026, rated CARE A1+). Issuing and paying agent: HDFC Bank Limited.
- **Time Technoplast Limited**: CP TTL10726 (ISIN INE508G14JE7, 1000 units, 7.75% p.a., maturing 08/01/2027, rated CRISIL A1+/ICRA A1+). Issuing and paying agent: IDBI Bank Ltd.
- **Bajaj Financial Securities Limited**: CP BFSL9726 (ISIN INE01C314GJ3, 3000 units, 6.95% p.a., maturing 26/10/2026, rated CRISIL A1+/IND A1+). Issuing and paying agent: ICICI Bank Limited.
- **Indian Oil Corporation Ltd.**: CP IOCL10726 (ISIN INE242A14YZ0, 20,000 units, 6.43% p.a., maturing 08/09/2026, rated ICRA A1+/IND A1+).

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing notification under BSE's Debt Market Segment procedures for privately placed securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must ensure trades in these securities are conducted only in dematerialised form, in standard denominations of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof, with the specified tick size of 1 paise. Queries can be directed to BSE's debt department.

## Important Dates

- Effective listing date: 10 July 2026
- 360OAAML27: Allotment 09/07/2026, Redemption 05/04/2027
- 360OAAML26: Allotment 09/07/2026, Redemption 08/10/2026
- TTL10726: Allotment 10/07/2026, Redemption 08/01/2027
- BFSL9726: Allotment 09/07/2026, Redemption 26/10/2026
- IOCL10726: Allotment 10/07/2026, Redemption 08/09/2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact, routine administrative listing notice affecting the debt market segment only. It enables trading members to trade these newly listed commercial papers but does not affect equity markets, broader trading operations, or retail investors directly. Relevant primarily to institutional debt market participants and the issuing companies' treasury/finance functions.
