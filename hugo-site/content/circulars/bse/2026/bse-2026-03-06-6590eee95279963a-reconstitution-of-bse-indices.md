## Summary

BSE has announced the reconstitution of the BSE ALLCAP index, effective March 23, 2026. The rebalancing involves the addition of over 35 securities and the deletion of 19 securities from the index. This reconstitution will require index-tracking funds and ETFs to adjust their portfolios accordingly.

## Key Points

- Effective date for all changes: **March 23, 2026**
- Index affected: **BSE ALLCAP**
- Number of securities being added: 35+
- Number of securities being dropped: 19
- Notable additions include Physicswallah Ltd., Pine Labs Ltd., National Securities Depository Ltd., JSW Cement Ltd., Bluestone Jewellery And Lifestyle Ltd., and Canara HSBC Life Insurance Company Ltd.
- Notable deletions include Reliance Infrastructure Ltd., Rajesh Exports Ltd., and Advait Energy Transitions Ltd.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework introduced. This is a periodic index reconstitution exercise carried out by BSE as part of standard index maintenance procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Fund managers and portfolio managers tracking the BSE ALLCAP index must rebalance their portfolios to reflect the new index composition by March 23, 2026.
- Index ETF providers and mutual funds benchmarked to BSE ALLCAP must update their holdings accordingly.
- Market participants should note the additions and deletions to anticipate potential liquidity changes in the affected securities.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** March 6, 2026
- **Effective Date of Reconstitution:** March 23, 2026
