## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260225-12, continuing from Exchange Notice No. 20260218-36 dated February 18, 2026, informing trading members of Scrip ID changes for 8 debt listed securities of Muthoottu Mini Financiers (now renamed to MUTHOOT MCRED LIMITED) on the BOLT Plus trading system. The changes are effective from February 26, 2026.

## Key Points

- 8 debt securities of Muthoottu Mini Financiers will have updated Scrip IDs on the BOLT Plus system effective February 26, 2026.
- The company name changes from **Muthoottu Mini Financiers** to **MUTHOOT MCRED LIMITED** across all affected scrips.
- This is a continuation of an earlier notice (No. 20260218-36 dated February 18, 2026).
- Trading members must use the new Scrip IDs when transacting on the BOLT Plus system from the effective date.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced. This is an operational update to reflect the company's name change in the trading system identifiers.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members** are required to update their systems and references to use the new Scrip IDs for all 8 affected debt securities on the BOLT Plus system from February 26, 2026 onwards.
- Old Scrip IDs should no longer be used after February 25, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **February 18, 2026** – Original notice (No. 20260218-36) issued announcing the name/Scrip ID change.
- **February 25, 2026** – Date of this continuation notice (No. 20260225-12).
- **February 26, 2026** – Effective date for new Scrip IDs on BOLT Plus system.
