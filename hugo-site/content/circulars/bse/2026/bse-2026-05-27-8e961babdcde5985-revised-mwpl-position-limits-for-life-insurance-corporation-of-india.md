## Summary

ICCL (Indian Clearing Corporation Limited) has revised the Market Wide Position Limits (MWPL) for Life Insurance Corporation of India (BSE scrip code: 543526) in stock derivatives contracts. This revision is issued in pursuance of notice No. 20260321-1 dated March 23, 2026, and partially modifies circular No. 20231229-46 dated December 29, 2023. The updated limits are effective from May 28, 2026.

## Key Points

- MWPL revised for LIC (scrip code 543526) in stock derivatives segment
- Applicable to Stock Brokers, Foreign Portfolio Investors (FPIs) – Category I & II, and Mutual Funds (MFs)
- Table header updated as per SEBI circular
- Issued by ICCL Chief Risk Officer Sushant Majhi

## Regulatory Changes

Partial modification of circular No. 20231229-46 (December 29, 2023) in pursuance of notice No. 20260321-1 (March 23, 2026). The position limit table header has been revised in accordance with a SEBI circular.

## Compliance Requirements

All members and participants must adhere to the revised MWPL for LIC derivatives contracts. The updated limits are:

| Scrip Code | Scrip Name | Market Wide Limit (No. of Shares) | Client/NRI/Scheme of MF | TM Proprietary | TM (Proprietary+Client)/FPI (Cat I)/MF |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 543526 | Life Insurance Corporation of India | 6,64,12,476 | 66,40,200 | 1,32,81,800 | 1,99,23,400 |

For clarifications, members may contact:

- Mr. Rahul Vishwakarma: 2272 5186
- Email: risk.monitoring@icclindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** May 28, 2026
- **Notice Date:** May 28, 2026
- **Signed:** May 27, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This revision affects all market participants holding or intending to take positions in LIC stock derivatives on BSE. Traders exceeding the new limits must reduce their positions to comply. The market-wide limit of 6,64,12,476 shares sets an aggregate cap across all participants, while per-entity sub-limits govern individual broker, FPI, and MF exposure. Participants with concentrated LIC derivatives positions should review and adjust accordingly before the May 28, 2026 effective date.
