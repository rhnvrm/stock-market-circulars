## Summary

BSE has notified all trading members that SVCM Securities Private Limited (Member No. 6737) will voluntarily cease business in the Cash segment effective Friday, June 05, 2026. The notice was issued by the Membership & Compliance department.

## Key Points

- SVCM Securities Private Limited (Member No. 6737) is voluntarily closing its business.
- Operations in the Cash segment will cease from June 05, 2026.
- Notice issued by Amit Kadam, Assistant Vice President, Membership.
- For clarifications, trading members may contact membership.ops@bseindia.com.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a membership cessation notice under BSE's standard procedures for voluntary closure.

## Compliance Requirements

- Existing clients of SVCM Securities Private Limited should make alternative arrangements with another SEBI-registered broker before June 05, 2026.
- Counterparties and trading members with open positions or pending transactions involving SVCM Securities should settle them prior to the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **June 04, 2026**: Notice issued.
- **June 05, 2026 (Friday)**: Effective date of cessation of business in the Cash segment.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited and localised. The voluntary closure of a single trading member (SVCM Securities Private Limited, Member No. 6737) in the Cash segment does not affect broader market operations. Clients of this broker need to transfer their accounts and positions to another registered member before the effective date. No systemic or market-wide disruption is anticipated.
