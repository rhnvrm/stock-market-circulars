## Summary

BSE Limited, through Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd. (ICCL), has issued a notice inviting auction offers from trading members for the purchase of securities under Rolling Settlement No. DR-611/2026-27. The auction is scheduled for April 17, 2026, with pay-in on April 20, 2026.

## Key Points

- Auction offer entry session: 2:00 PM to 2:45 PM on April 17, 2026
- Auction matching session: 2:45 PM to 3:00 PM
- Delivery statement and accepted auction offer reports: 3:00 PM to 3:30 PM
- Money statement and final reports: 3:35 PM to 5:30 PM
- Pay-in of auction securities scheduled for Monday, April 20, 2026 at 8:00 AM
- Settlement will be processed through the Clearing Member of each Trading Member following Uniform Membership structure implementation
- The restriction on trading members offering securities in auction for settlements where they had short deliveries has been removed

## Regulatory Changes

Following the implementation of the Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment, settlement is now done through the Clearing Member of a Trading Member. The prior restriction preventing trading members from offering securities in auction for settlements in which they had short deliveries has been lifted.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and custodians must check their auction pay-in obligations for securities
- Securities pay-in must be executed in depositories within depository cut-off timings on April 20, 2026
- Trading members must refer to the cutoff rate on the Auction Offer Entry Screen before submitting offers
- Trading members must exercise utmost care when entering securities codes and rates — no corrections will be permitted after punching errors
- Members should refer to notices regarding corporate actions (Ex-Rights, Ex-Bonus, Ex-Split, etc.) issued by respective departments

## Important Dates

- **April 17, 2026**: Auction offer entry (2:00 PM – 2:45 PM), matching (2:45 PM – 3:00 PM), and report generation (3:00 PM – 5:30 PM)
- **April 20, 2026**: Auction pay-in deadline at 8:00 AM

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice applicable to trading members and custodians participating in BSE's equity cash segment. The removal of the restriction on short-delivering members participating in auctions increases market liquidity in the auction process. Members with outstanding short delivery positions in settlement DR-611/2026-27 should ensure timely securities pay-in to avoid penalties. No broad market impact is expected beyond participants in this specific settlement cycle.
