## Summary

The President of India, acting through the Ministry of Railways, as promoter-seller of Indian Railway Finance Corporation (IRFC), has exercised the oversubscription option in the ongoing Offer for Sale (OFS). This expands the total offer size from the base of 13,06,85,060 shares (1%) to 243,087,100 equity shares (1.86% of total paid-up equity share capital), to be sold via BSE and NSE designated OFS windows.

## Key Points

- **Seller:** The President of India (Ministry of Railways, Government of India), Promoter of IRFC
- **Base Offer Size:** 13,06,85,060 equity shares (1% of total paid-up equity share capital), face value Rs. 10 each
- **Oversubscription Option Exercised:** Up to 112,402,040 additional equity shares (0.86% of total paid-up equity share capital)
- **Total Offer Size:** 243,087,100 equity shares (1.86% of total paid-up equity share capital)
- **Retail Investor Reservation:** 24,308,711 equity shares (10% of total offer) reserved for Retail Investors on T+1 Day
- **Employee Offer:** Up to 25,000 equity shares (0.0002% of paid-up capital) offered to eligible employees of IRFC
- This notice is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20260623-34 dated June 23, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular exercises an oversubscription option as permitted under the OFS Guidelines issued by SEBI, consistent with the terms disclosed in the original OFS notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Brokers/Trading Members:** Must facilitate OFS bids through the designated BSE and NSE OFS windows on the applicable trading days
- **Retail Investors:** May participate on T+1 Day (June 25, 2026), including carrying forward un-allotted bids from T Day
- **Non-Retail Investors:** May bid on T Day (June 24, 2026) and optionally carry forward un-allotted bids to T+1 Day
- **Eligible Employees:** May participate subject to terms and conditions in the OFS Guidelines
- Valid bids must be received for the retail reservation tranche to be allocated

## Important Dates

- **T Day (June 24, 2026):** OFS open for Non-Retail Investors only
- **T+1 Day (June 25, 2026):** OFS open for Retail Investors; Non-Retail Investors with un-allotted bids from T Day may also carry forward
- **Notice Date:** June 24, 2026
- **Reference Notice:** Exchange Notice No. 20260623-34 dated June 23, 2026 (original OFS notice)

## Impact Assessment

The exercise of the oversubscription option is a significant development, increasing the government's divestment quantum in IRFC by 86% above the base offer size. The total sale of 243,087,100 shares at market price represents a substantial liquidity event for IRFC on the secondary market. Short-term selling pressure on IRFC shares is likely given the expanded supply. Retail investors have dedicated access on June 25 with 10% reservation, and a small employee tranche adds inclusivity. The OFS structure via stock exchange designated windows ensures transparency and price discovery in line with SEBI OFS Guidelines.
