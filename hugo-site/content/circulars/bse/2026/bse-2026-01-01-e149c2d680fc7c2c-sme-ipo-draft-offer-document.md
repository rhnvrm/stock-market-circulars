## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of Fly-Hi Maritime Travels Limited filed on December 31, 2025, on its SME platform for public comments. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and will be available for at least 21 days for stakeholder feedback.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Fly-Hi Maritime Travels Limited on December 31, 2025
- Document complies with Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations disclosures
- Available on BSE SME website at www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public comments invited within 21 days from notice date
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard procedural notification for draft offer document availability.

## Compliance Requirements

- Stakeholders wishing to provide comments must submit them within 21 days from January 01, 2026
- Comments should be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Draft Offer Document accessible on BSE SME website for review

## Important Dates

- **December 31, 2025**: Draft Offer Document filed
- **January 01, 2026**: Notice date and start of public comment period
- **January 22, 2026**: Last date for submitting public comments (21 days from notice)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine procedural notice for an SME IPO draft offer document. The impact is limited to potential investors and stakeholders interested in Fly-Hi Maritime Travels Limited. The 21-day public comment period is a standard regulatory requirement under ICDR Regulations for transparency and stakeholder participation in the IPO process.
