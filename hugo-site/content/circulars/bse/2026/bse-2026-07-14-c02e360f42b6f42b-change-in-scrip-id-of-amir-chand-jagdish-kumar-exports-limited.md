## Summary

BSE has notified trading members of a change in the Scrip ID of Amir Chand Jagdish Kumar (Exports) Limited in the BOLT Plus System, effective from July 20, 2026. The Scrip ID will change from "AMIRCHAND" to "AEROPLANE" while the Scrip Code (544743) remains unchanged.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code 544743 remains unchanged.
- Old Scrip ID in BOLT Plus System: AMIRCHAND.
- New Scrip ID in BOLT Plus System: AEROPLANE.
- Change applies to Amir Chand Jagdish Kumar (Exports) Limited.
- Trading members are requested to take note of the change.

## Regulatory Changes

This is an administrative update by BSE to the trading system identifier (Scrip ID) for the company, not a regulatory or compliance change.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should update their systems and records to reflect the new Scrip ID "AEROPLANE" for orders and trades in this security from the effective date. No action required from investors regarding shareholding, which remains unaffected.

## Important Dates

- Effective Date: July 20, 2026 (change in Scrip ID takes effect in BOLT Plus System).

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact administrative change affecting only the trading identifier used in BSE's BOLT Plus System. It does not affect the company's shareholding structure, ISIN, or fundamental listing status. Trading members must ensure their systems recognize the new Scrip ID to avoid order placement errors post effective date.
