## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 19,20,000 new equity shares of NEXOME CAPITAL MARKETS LIMITED (Scrip Code: 508905, ISIN: INE641A01013) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Thursday, May 21, 2026. The shares were issued at a premium of Rs. 54/- (Issue Price: Rs. 64/-) to Promoters and Non-Promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 19,20,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- face value issued at Rs. 64/- (Rs. 54/- premium) via preferential allotment
- Shares issued to Promoters and Non-Promoters through conversion of warrants
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 8815501 to 10735500
- Date of allotment: April 20, 2026
- ISIN: INE641A01013

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification under BSE's Listing Operations department.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to permit trading of the newly listed securities from May 21, 2026. Lock-in restrictions apply to portions of the allotted shares as detailed below.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** April 20, 2026
- **Trading Effective From:** May 21, 2026
- **Lock-in Period 1:** 3,88,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 8815501–9203500) locked until December 31, 2027
- **Lock-in Period 2:** 7,70,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 9203501–9973500) locked until December 31, 2026
- **Lock-in Period 3:** 7,62,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 9973501–10735500) locked until December 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing increases the tradable share count of NEXOME CAPITAL MARKETS LIMITED by 19,20,000 shares, though a significant portion (19,20,000 shares total) remains under lock-in through end of 2026 or 2027. Market impact is limited to existing shareholders and active traders of this scrip. No broader market or systemic implications.
