## Summary

BSE has listed five commercial papers (CPs) on the Debt Market Segment effective June 1, 2026. The securities were issued on a private placement basis by Infina Finance Private Limited, Vertis Infrastructure Trust (two tranches), Tata Steel Limited, and SBICAP Securities Limited. All CPs are allotted as of May 29, 2026, and mature in August 2026.

## Key Points

- Trading is permitted only in dematerialised form under the respective ISIN numbers.
- Standard denomination is Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof; tick size is 1 paise.
- All securities are rated A1+ by at least one major rating agency (CRISIL and/or ICRA and/or IND).
- Interest is cumulative, payable on the redemption date.
- Queries should be directed to BSE Debt Department: 22728352 / 8597 / 8995 / 5753 / 8915.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must ensure trades in these securities occur only in dematerialised form, in the standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof.

## Impact Assessment

Minimal broader market impact. This is a standard operational listing of short-term debt instruments (commercial papers) on BSE's Debt Segment. The instruments are high-quality (A1+ rated) with short tenors of approximately 90 days. Tata Steel's issuance of 40,000 units (face value Rs. 500,000 each) is the largest tranche by quantity. These listings are relevant primarily to institutional debt market participants.
