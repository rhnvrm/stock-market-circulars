## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures of ECAP EQUITIES LIMITED (ISIN: INE468N07185, Code: 974112) effective February 2, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of these debentures. Trading members are advised not to deal in these securities from the no-dealings date.

## Key Points

- Company: ECAP EQUITIES LIMITED
- Security: ECE-GSEC-16-2-26-PVT (Non-Convertible Debentures)
- ISIN: INE468N07185
- BSE Code: 974112
- Record Date: February 2, 2026
- No Dealings From: February 2, 2026
- Circular Reference: DR-811/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard procedural suspension pursuant to debt instrument redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in the specified debentures from February 2, 2026
- Members must take note of the suspension and ensure compliance
- No transactions should be executed in ISIN INE468N07185 from the specified date

## Important Dates

- **January 23, 2026**: Circular notice date
- **February 2, 2026**: Record date for redemption
- **February 2, 2026**: No dealings effective date

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as this involves a single privately placed debt security. The suspension is a routine administrative action accompanying the redemption process. Only holders and potential traders of this specific debenture series are affected. No broader debt market implications.
