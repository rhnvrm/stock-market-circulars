## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that equity shares of KASTURI METAL COMPOSITE LIMITED will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company will be listed on the SME segment with scrip code 544688, symbol KASTURI, and ISIN number INE0T1301023. The specific date of listing and detailed security information will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Kasturi Metal Composite Limited
- Scrip Code: 544688
- Trading Symbol: KASTURI
- ISIN Number: INE0T1301023
- Segment: SME
- Listing status: To be listed in due course
- Exact listing date to be announced separately

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the scrip details for the upcoming listing. No immediate compliance actions required until the actual listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: February 2, 2026
- Listing Date: To be informed through separate notice

This is a routine listing announcement for a new SME company. The impact is limited to market participants interested in the SME segment. The actual trading will commence only after the listing date is formally announced through a subsequent circular. No immediate operational changes required for trading members.
