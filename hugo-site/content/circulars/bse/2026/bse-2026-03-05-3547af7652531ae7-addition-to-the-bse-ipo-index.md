## Summary

BSE has announced that Omnitech Engineering Limited (Exchange ticker: 544720) will be added to the BSE IPO Index effective at the open of Monday, March 09, 2026. The company was listed on BSE on March 05, 2026, following Notice No. 20260304-44.

## Key Points

- Omnitech Engineering Limited (ticker: 544720) listed on BSE on March 05, 2026
- Stock will be added to the BSE IPO Index effective at market open on March 09, 2026
- The corporate action is included in the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE) dated March 05, 2026
- Queries can be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard index rebalancing action to include a newly listed IPO stock in the BSE IPO Index.

## Compliance Requirements

Index fund managers and ETF providers tracking the BSE IPO Index should update their portfolios to include Omnitech Engineering Limited (544720) effective at the open of March 09, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **March 05, 2026**: Omnitech Engineering Limited listed on BSE; corporate action file (*.SDE) released
- **March 09, 2026**: Effective date for addition of 544720 to the BSE IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a routine addition to the BSE IPO Index for a newly listed company. Investors and fund managers tracking or benchmarking against the BSE IPO Index will need to account for this addition from March 09, 2026. No impact on other indices or broader market operations.
