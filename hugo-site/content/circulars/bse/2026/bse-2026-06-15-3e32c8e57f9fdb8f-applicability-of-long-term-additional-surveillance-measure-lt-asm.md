## Summary

BSE has issued a notice regarding the applicability of Long Term Additional Surveillance Measures (LT-ASM) on certain equity securities. Effective June 18, 2026, 100% margins will be imposed on all open positions as of June 17, 2026 and on new positions created from June 18, 2026 onwards. Securities are also being moved to higher LT-ASM stages and some are exiting the framework, both effective June 16, 2026.

## Key Points

- 100% margins applicable from June 18, 2026 on open positions as on June 17, 2026 and all new positions thereafter (Annexure I, Part A)
- Securities moving to higher stages of the LT-ASM framework effective June 16, 2026 (Annexure I, Part B)
- Lower Price Band imposed effective June 16, 2026 for securities placed under the framework
- Certain securities to continue in LT-ASM but be transferred to T / XT / MT / TS Group effective June 18, 2026 (Annexure I, Part D)
- Securities exiting the LT-ASM framework effective June 16, 2026 listed in Annexure II
- Consolidated list of all securities currently under LT-ASM framework provided in Annexure III
- Shortlisting is based on XBRL submissions by listed companies and is solely a market surveillance action
- Price band for securities exiting the framework will revert to the band applicable before LT-ASM placement (unless subject to another surveillance measure)

## Regulatory Changes

This notice is issued in continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20180321-46 (March 21, 2018) and subsequent notices, including revisions to the LT-ASM framework via notices dated October 27, 2018, December 04, 2020, August 09, 2024, and September 20, 2024. The revised LT-ASM framework governs margin requirements and price band restrictions for shortlisted securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must maintain 100% margins on all open positions in affected securities as of June 17, 2026, and on new positions from June 18, 2026
- Brokers and members must adhere to lower price bands for securities placed under the framework effective June 16, 2026
- LT-ASM operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures; members must ensure compliance with all applicable restrictions simultaneously
- Members with queries may contact BSE Surveillance at bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **June 16, 2026**: Securities moved to higher LT-ASM stages take effect; lower price band applies; securities exiting the framework do so effective this date
- **June 17, 2026**: Reference date for open positions subject to 100% margin requirement
- **June 18, 2026**: 100% margins effective on open positions as of June 17, 2026 and all new positions; transfer of certain securities to T/XT/MT/TS Group effective this date

## Impact Assessment

The imposition of 100% margins substantially increases the cost of trading and holding positions in affected securities, reducing leverage and liquidity. Securities moved to higher LT-ASM stages face tighter price bands, further constraining trading activity. The transfer of certain securities to T/XT/MT/TS groups imposes trade-for-trade settlement, eliminating netting benefits. Securities exiting the framework will see margins and price bands normalised, potentially improving their trading conditions. This notice does not reflect any adverse regulatory or legal action against the listed companies; shortlisting is purely surveillance-driven based on XBRL data.
