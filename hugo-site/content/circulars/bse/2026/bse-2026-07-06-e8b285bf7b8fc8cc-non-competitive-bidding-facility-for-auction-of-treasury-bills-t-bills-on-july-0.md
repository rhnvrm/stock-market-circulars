## Summary

BSE has issued a notice enabling non-competitive bidding through its NCB-GSec module (on the iBBS web-based system) for the auction of three Treasury Bills to be conducted by RBI on July 08, 2026. This follows RBI's press release dated July 03, 2026 announcing the Government of India's plan to sell three T-Bills via auction, and is in continuation of Exchange circular no. 20180423-42 dated April 26, 2018 regarding the launch of the non-competitive bidding facility for Government Securities auctions.

## Key Points

- Non-competitive bidding facility available via the NCB-GSec module on BSE's iBBS platform (https://ibbs.bseindia.com)
- Three securities on offer: 91 Day T-Bill (091T071026), 182 Day T-Bill (182T060127), and 364 Day T-Bill (364T080727)
- Issue sizes: Rs. 9,000 Cr (91-day), Rs. 8,000 Cr (182-day), Rs. 7,000 Cr (364-day); all new issuances
- Minimum lot and multiples: Rs. 100; Minimum amount: Rs. 10,000
- Maximum bid amounts: Rs. 450 Cr (91-day), Rs. 400 Cr (182-day), Rs. 350 Cr (364-day)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is an operational notice implementing the existing non-competitive bidding framework established under Exchange circular no. 20180423-42 dated April 26, 2018, pursuant to RBI's July 03, 2026 press release on the T-Bill auction.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members and direct investors participating in the non-competitive bidding process must submit bids within the specified windows via the NCB-GSec module. Queries on user ID creation/password reset, bidding, or obligation/pay-in should be directed to BSE Trading Operations or ICCL Operations teams as per the contact details provided.

## Important Dates

- Bid Collection Start Date: July 06, 2026, from 10:00 am onwards (24 hours availability)
- Bid Collection End Date (Direct Investors): July 07, 2026, till 5:00 pm
- Bid Collection End Date (Members): July 08, 2026, till 8:00 am
- Auction Date: July 08, 2026
- Settlement Date: July 09, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative/operational notice with low market impact, relevant primarily to debt market participants, trading members, and direct investors engaging in Government Securities auctions. It does not affect equity markets or listed companies.
