## Summary

BSE has announced the Live Activities Schedule for the Offer to Buy (OTB) – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for AVI PRODUCTS INDIA LIMITED (Symbol: APIL, Scrip Code: 523896, ISIN: INE316O01021). The offer window runs from Wednesday, 13th May 2026 to Tuesday, 26th May 2026 at an offer price of Rs. 33.00 per share for up to 8,59,769 equity shares.

## Key Points

- **Offer Price:** Rs. 33.00 per share
- **Offer Size:** Up to 8,59,769 equity shares
- **Face Value:** Rs. 10.00
- **Market Lot:** 1
- **Opening Date:** Wednesday, 13th May 2026
- **Closing Date:** Tuesday, 26th May 2026
- **Bid Offering Period:** 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM daily (13th May to 26th May 2026)
- **Give-up (last day):** 13th May to 25th May 2026, 9:15 AM – 3:30 PM
- **Take-up (last day – 26th May):** 9:15 AM – 4:00 PM
- Accessible via iBBS platform at https://ibbs.bseindia.com/ (Internet) and https://ibbsll.bseindia.com/ (Leased Line)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice operationalises the existing OTB framework under Exchange Notices 20150930-1 (dated 30 September 2015) and 20170203-29 (dated 3 February 2017) for participation in the Acquisition Window.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must ensure the Unique Client Code (UCC) of each investor is registered and/or updated with the correct PAN Number and Category in the Exchange UCC database before placing bids.
- Bids can only be placed for investors holding shares in demat form.
- If no securities are accepted, no End of Issue files will be generated on the Extranet.
- Members and Custodians should use designated contacts for new login IDs or password resets for iBBS, RTRMS, and Extranet systems (contact: Trading Operations, 022-22728005, user.trdops@bseindia.com).

## Impact Assessment

This notice is operationally relevant to Trading Members and Custodian Members of BSE who hold or manage client positions in AVI PRODUCTS INDIA LIMITED (APIL). The offer price of Rs. 33.00 against a face value of Rs. 10.00 represents a premium. Eligible bidding categories include FII, Financial Institutions, Mutual Funds, Insurance Companies, Other QIBs, Other NIB, Companies, and Individuals. Broader market impact is limited given the small offer size and single-company scope.
