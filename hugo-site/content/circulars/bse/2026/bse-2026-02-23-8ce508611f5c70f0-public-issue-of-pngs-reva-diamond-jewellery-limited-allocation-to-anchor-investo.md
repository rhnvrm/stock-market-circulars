## Summary

BSE has issued a circular providing details of the allocation of shares to anchor investors in the public issue (IPO) of PNGS REVA DIAMOND JEWELLERY LIMITED. Anchor investor allocations precede the public subscription window and are a key component of the IPO process under SEBI regulations.

## Key Points

- The circular pertains to the public issue of equity shares of PNGS REVA DIAMOND JEWELLERY LIMITED.
- Details of anchor investor allocation, including names of anchor investors, number of shares allotted, and allotment price, are disclosed.
- Anchor investor allocation is made one day prior to the opening of the public issue.
- The allotment to anchor investors is subject to a lock-in period as prescribed by SEBI (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations.
- This disclosure is mandatory under SEBI ICDR Regulations and BSE listing requirements.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced by this circular. It is a routine disclosure under the existing SEBI (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2018, which mandate disclosure of anchor investor allocation details prior to the public issue opening.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company (PNGS REVA DIAMOND JEWELLERY LIMITED) and its lead managers are required to ensure accurate and timely disclosure of anchor investor allocation details on the stock exchange platform.
- Anchor investors are subject to a lock-in on 50% of their allocation for 90 days and the remaining 50% for 30 days from the date of allotment, as per SEBI norms.
- Market participants should note the anchor investor price as a reference for the IPO price band.

## Important Dates

- **Anchor Investor Allocation Date:** 2026-02-23 (one trading day before public issue opening)
- **Public Issue Opening:** Expected on or after 2026-02-24 (to be confirmed in the prospectus)
- **Lock-in Period (50% of allocation):** 30 days from the date of allotment
- **Lock-in Period (remaining 50%):** 90 days from the date of allotment

## Impact Assessment

This circular is of moderate significance for market participants interested in the PNGS REVA DIAMOND JEWELLERY LIMITED IPO. The anchor investor allocation provides a confidence signal for retail and institutional investors. Successful anchor allocation at the issue price indicates institutional interest in the company. The circular has limited broader market impact but is directly relevant to investors participating in or tracking this IPO.
