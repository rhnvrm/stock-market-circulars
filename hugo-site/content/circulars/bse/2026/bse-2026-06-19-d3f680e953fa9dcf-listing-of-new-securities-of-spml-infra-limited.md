## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 42,44,844 new equity shares of Rs. 2/- each of SPML Infra Limited (Scrip Code: 500402) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Monday, June 22, 2026. These shares were issued at a premium of Rs. 213/- (issue price Rs. 215/-) to Promoters and Non-Promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 42,44,844 equity shares of Rs. 2/- face value issued at a premium of Rs. 213/- (total issue price: Rs. 215/-)
- Issued to Promoters and Non-Promoters on preferential basis via warrant conversion
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution Nos.: 79671336 to 83916179
- ISIN: INE937A01023
- Date of Allotment: April 22, 2026
- Trading permitted from June 22, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice under existing BSE listing regulations for newly allotted securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the listing of these new securities and update their records accordingly. Lock-in restrictions apply to specified shares and must be observed:

- 23,59,833 shares (Dist. Nos. 79671335–82031168): locked in until December 30, 2027
- 18,85,011 shares (Dist. Nos. 82031169–83916179): locked in until December 30, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** April 22, 2026
- **Listing/Trading Effective Date:** June 22, 2026 (Monday)
- **Lock-in Expiry (partial):** December 30, 2026 (18,85,011 shares)
- **Lock-in Expiry (partial):** December 30, 2027 (23,59,833 shares)

## Impact Assessment

The addition of 42,44,844 shares to the tradable pool (subject to lock-in restrictions) will increase the total share capital of SPML Infra Limited. The preferential allotment at Rs. 215/- per share indicates the price at which promoters and other allottees acquired shares via warrant conversion. Free float impact is limited in the near term due to lock-in restrictions on all newly listed shares until at least December 2026. Existing shareholders face dilution from this fresh issuance.
