## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the equity shares of Waterways Leisure Tourism Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in the 'B' Group of Securities effective Wednesday, July 1, 2026. The company operates under the brand Cordelia Cruises and its scrip will trade under the symbol CORDELIA (Scrip Code: 544802).

## Key Points

- **Listing Date**: Wednesday, July 1, 2026
- **Group**: B Group of Securities
- **Scrip ID / Abbreviated Name**: CORDELIA
- **Scrip Code**: 544802
- **ISIN**: INE0LZF01013
- **Total Securities Listed**: 72,394,543 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each, fully paid up
- **Distinctive Number Range**: 1 to 72,394,543
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 808/- per share (Face Value Rs. 10/- + Premium Rs. 798/-)
- **Date of Allotment**: June 29, 2026
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Pari Passu**: Yes
- **Financial Year End**: March 31
- Registrar: MUFG Intime India Private Limited (formerly Link Intime India Private Limited)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The listing complies with existing SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000, which mandate trading only in dematerialised (demat) form.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that shares of Waterways Leisure Tourism Limited can only be traded in dematerialised form per SEBI guidelines.
- The scrip will be part of the **Special Pre-open Session (SPOS)** on July 1, 2026. Members are referred to Exchange Notice No. 20120216-29 dated February 16, 2012 for details on SPOS for IPOs and relisted scrips.
- Lock-in details are as per Annexure I; shareholding pattern as per Annexure II.
- Corporate action for lock-in period marking in depository records has been executed for shares in demat form.

## Important Dates

- **June 29, 2026**: Date of allotment in the public issue
- **July 1, 2026**: Listing and commencement of trading on BSE (including SPOS)

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard IPO listing notice. Waterways Leisure Tourism Limited (Cordelia Cruises) enters the BSE with 72.4 million shares at an issue price of Rs. 808, implying a listing market cap of approximately Rs. 5,849 crore. The scrip falls under the 'B' group, indicating it does not qualify for the more liquid 'A' group at listing. Trading members and IPO allottees should be aware of the Special Pre-open Session on listing day, which determines the opening price through a price discovery mechanism. Lock-in restrictions on promoter and anchor investor holdings apply as detailed in Annexure I.
