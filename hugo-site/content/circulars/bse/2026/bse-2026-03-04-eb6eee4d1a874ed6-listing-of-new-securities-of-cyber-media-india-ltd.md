## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 49,53,415 new equity shares of Cyber Media India Ltd. (Scrip Code: 532640) are permitted for trading effective Thursday, March 05, 2026. These shares arise from the conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up equity shares of Re. 1/- each, issued on a rights basis.

## Key Points

- 49,53,415 equity shares of Re. 1/- each are being listed following conversion from partly paid to fully paid up status
- Shares were originally issued on a rights basis
- ISIN: INE278G01037
- Distinctive numbers: 15667243 to 20620657
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification following completion of the rights issue conversion process.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are permitted and expected to facilitate trading in these newly listed securities from the effective date. No additional compliance actions are required beyond normal trading operations.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** March 04, 2026
- **Trading Effective Date:** March 05, 2026 (Thursday)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. The listing adds 49,53,415 shares to the tradeable float of Cyber Media India Ltd., representing a conversion of previously issued partly paid rights shares to fully paid status. The pari-passu ranking with existing shares means no dilution of rights for existing fully paid shareholders beyond what was already anticipated at the time of the rights issue.
