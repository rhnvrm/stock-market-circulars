## Summary

BSE has notified Trading Members that the price bands applicable to 17 specified scrips have been revised from their existing levels, effective July 08, 2026. The revisions adjust the permissible daily price movement (circuit filter) percentage for each scrip individually.

## Key Points

- 17 scrips have had their price bands revised as part of BSE's periodic surveillance review.
- Revised price bands range from 2% to 20% depending on the scrip.
- Notable revisions: Consecutive Commodities Limited to 20%; Ghushine Fintrrade Ocean Ltd, Kaushalya Infrastructure Development Corporation Ltd, Pearl Green Clubs and Resorts Ltd, and Saraswati Commercial India Ltd to 10%; several others to 2% or 5%.
- The circular carries no attachment; details are provided directly in the notice table.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members must update their systems to reflect the revised price bands for the listed scrips before trading commences on the effective date. Queries can be directed to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- Effective date of revised price bands: July 08, 2026
- Notice date: July 07, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to trading members and investors holding or trading these specific scrips, most of which appear to be small-cap/SME securities already subject to enhanced surveillance. Widened price bands (e.g., to 20% or 10%) may increase intraday volatility potential for affected stocks, while tightened bands reduce it. No broad market impact is expected.
