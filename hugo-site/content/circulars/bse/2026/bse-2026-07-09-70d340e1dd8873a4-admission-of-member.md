## Summary

BSE Ltd. has informed its Trading Members that it has admitted Sonawala Global Equities Private Limited as a new Trading Member with Cash/Equity Derivatives segment rights, and has granted additional segment memberships to five existing members, expanding their trading rights into Equity Derivatives, Debt, EGR (Electronic Gold Receipts) and Commodity Derivatives segments.

## Key Points

- Sonawala Global Equities Private Limited (Member No. 6993) admitted as a new Trading Member for Cash/Equity Derivatives segment.
- Fortune Fiscal Ltd. (Member No. 3091) granted additional segment membership for Equity Derivatives.
- NDA Securities Ltd. (Member No. 3082) granted additional segment membership for the Debt segment.
- Shree Balaji Multi Commodities Private Limited (Member No. 6723) granted additional segment membership for the EGR (Electronic Gold Receipts) segment.
- NX Block Trades Private Limited (Member No. 6848) granted additional segment membership for Commodity Derivatives.
- Market Hub Stock Broking Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 6107) granted additional segment membership for Commodity Derivatives and EGR segments.
- All admissions are subject to the Rules, Bye-laws and Regulations of the Exchange.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is a routine administrative notification of membership admissions and segment expansions under the Membership Department's existing rules.

## Compliance Requirements

Newly admitted and upgraded Trading Members must operate strictly within the terms and subject to the Rules, Bye-laws and Regulations of BSE Ltd. for their respective segments. Members requiring clarification may contact membership.ops@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

Notice issued and effective 09 July 2026.

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal market impact as it pertains solely to internal membership administration at BSE, formalizing trading rights for one new member and expanded segment access for five existing members. It does not affect listed securities, trading in specific stocks, or broader market operations.
