## Summary

BSE, through ICCL (Indian Clearing Corporation Limited), has issued the trading and settlement programme for the New Debt Segment (NDS) covering the period from 01 June 2026 to 30 June 2026. This is a routine monthly notice outlining the scheduled trading and settlement dates for participants in the NDS.

## Key Points

- Covers the full month of June 2026 (01.06.2026 to 30.06.2026)
- Issued by ICCL under the Settlement/RMS category
- Applicable to the New Debt Segment (NDS) of BSE
- Attachment reference: NDS062026 (detailed schedule document)
- Notice Number: 20260509-4, dated 09 May 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard periodic operational notice providing the trading and settlement calendar.

## Compliance Requirements

NDS segment participants should refer to the attached schedule (NDS062026) to plan their trading and settlement activities for June 2026. Adherence to the published settlement dates is mandatory for all clearing members and trading members operating in the New Debt Segment.

## Important Dates

- Programme Period: 01 June 2026 to 30 June 2026
- Notice Date: 09 May 2026
- Detailed holiday and settlement schedule available in attachment NDS062026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine administrative notice. NDS segment participants — primarily institutional investors and debt market intermediaries — should update their operational calendars for June 2026 based on the detailed schedule in the attachment. No changes to market structure or rules are implied.
