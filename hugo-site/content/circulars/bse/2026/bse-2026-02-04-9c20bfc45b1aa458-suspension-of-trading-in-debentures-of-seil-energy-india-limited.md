## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in the Non-Convertible Debentures of SEIL Energy India Limited effective February 6, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for early redemption of debentures on exercise of call option and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these securities.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension effective from February 6, 2026
- Applies to 8.45% SEIL-18-6-29-PVT debentures (ISIN: INE460M07010, Code: 975744)
- Suspension due to early redemption on exercise of call option
- Interest payment also scheduled on record date
- Trading members instructed not to deal in these debentures

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a company-specific corporate action requiring trading suspension as per standard procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from February 6, 2026
- Trading members must take note of the suspension and inform their clients
- No dealings permitted in ISIN INE460M07010 (Code: 975744)

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: February 4, 2026
- **Record Date**: February 6, 2026
- **No Dealings From**: February 6, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Debt Market Impact**: High impact for holders of SEIL Energy India Limited debentures as trading will be completely suspended. Investors holding these securities will not be able to trade them from the suspension date.

**Liquidity Impact**: Complete loss of liquidity for this specific debenture series. Holders on record date will receive interest payment and principal redemption proceeds.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders on record date will benefit from early redemption proceeds and interest payment. Those wishing to exit positions must do so before February 6, 2026.
