## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of equity shares of GABION TECHNOLOGIES INDIA LIMITED totaling 1,35,75,360 shares. The securities are listed in demat form with different lock-in periods based on security type (Pre-Lock-in, Anchor, and IPO categories).

## Key Points

- Total securities listed: 1,35,75,360 equity shares
- All securities in demat form
- Pre-lock-in securities: 99,75,360 shares with lock-in from 02-01-2026 to varying end dates (2027-2029)
- Anchor investor securities: 10,22,400 shares with lock-in from 10-01-2026 to February-April 2026
- IPO securities: 25,77,600 shares with no lock-in period

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Lock-in restrictions apply to pre-lock-in and anchor investor securities as per specified dates
- Pre-lock-in securities divided into three tranches with lock-in periods of 1, 2, and 3 years respectively
- Anchor investor securities have short-term lock-in of approximately 1-3 months

## Important Dates

- **Pre-Lock-in Start Date:** 02-01-2026
- **Pre-Lock-in End Dates:** 
  - First tranche (36,30,894 shares): 10-02-2027
  - Second tranche (36,29,394 shares): 10-02-2028
  - Third tranche (27,15,072 shares): 10-02-2029
- **Anchor Lock-in Period:** 10-01-2026 to 07-02-2026 (5,10,400 shares) and 10-01-2026 to 08-04-2026 (5,12,000 shares)
- **IPO Securities:** No lock-in (25,77,600 shares)

## Impact Assessment

This listing enables trading of Gabion Technologies India Limited equity shares on BSE. The structured lock-in periods indicate a typical IPO arrangement with promoter/pre-IPO investor lock-ins (1-3 years), anchor investor lock-ins (1-3 months), and freely tradeable IPO shares. The majority of shares (73.5%) are under long-term lock-in, suggesting limited immediate float. Impact is specific to investors and stakeholders of Gabion Technologies with minimal broader market implications.
