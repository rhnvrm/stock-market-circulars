## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (20260527-10) informing trading members that the 7% privately placed debentures of Ramasigns Industries Limited (Scrip Code: 973907, ISIN: INE650D08013) are set to mature on May 31, 2026. Trading in these debt securities is suspended effective May 29, 2026.

## Key Points

- Debt securities of Ramasigns Industries Limited (RMIL-7%-31-5-26-PVT) are maturing on May 31, 2026
- Scrip Code: 973907 | ISIN: INE650D08013
- Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from May 29, 2026
- Reference circular: DR-640/2025-2026
- Notice issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President – Listing Compliance and Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice pursuant to the maturity of a listed debt instrument.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must cease dealing in the debentures of Ramasigns Industries Limited (ISIN: INE650D08013) with effect from May 29, 2026. No further transactions should be initiated in this scrip on or after that date.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** May 27, 2026
- **Suspension Effective Date:** May 29, 2026
- **Maturity Date:** May 31, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This is a standard end-of-life notice for a single privately placed debenture (7% fixed rate) issued by Ramasigns Industries Limited. The suspension is a procedural step ahead of the instrument's maturity. Holders of these debentures should expect redemption proceeds as per the terms of the instrument on the maturity date of May 31, 2026.
