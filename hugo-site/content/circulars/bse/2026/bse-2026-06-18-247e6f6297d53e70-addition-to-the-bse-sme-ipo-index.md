## Summary

Susan Electricals India Limited (Exchange ticker: 544793) was listed on BSE on June 18, 2026. Effective at the open of Friday, June 19, 2026, the stock will be added to the BSE SME IPO Index.

## Key Points

- Stock: Susan Electricals India Limited (ticker: 544793)
- Listed on BSE effective June 18, 2026
- Addition to BSE SME IPO Index effective June 19, 2026 (market open)
- Action included in the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE) on June 18, 2026
- Reference to prior Notice No. 20260617-35

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard index rebalancing action following a new SME IPO listing.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index tracking funds and ETFs referencing the BSE SME IPO Index should update their portfolios to reflect the addition effective June 19, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **June 18, 2026**: Listing of Susan Electricals India Limited on BSE
- **June 19, 2026**: Effective date for addition to BSE SME IPO Index (from market open)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a routine addition of a newly listed SME stock to the BSE SME IPO Index. Passive funds tracking the index will need to include this stock from June 19, 2026. No broader market or regulatory implications.
