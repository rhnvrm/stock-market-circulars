## Summary

BSE has notified the applicability of Short Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) for select securities that have satisfied the criteria for inclusion under Stage I and Stage II of the ST-ASM framework. Enhanced margin requirements will be effective from May 26, 2026, applying to all open positions as of May 25, 2026 and new positions created from May 26, 2026. The specific list of securities is provided in Annexure I attached to the notice.

## Key Points

- Securities satisfying ST-ASM Stage I criteria will attract a margin of 50% or existing margin (whichever is higher), subject to a maximum cap of 100%.
- Securities satisfying ST-ASM Stage II criteria will attract a margin of 100% or existing margin (whichever is higher), subject to a maximum cap of 100%.
- Margins apply to all open positions as on May 25, 2026 and all new positions from May 26, 2026.
- The ST-ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the Exchanges.
- Shortlisting under ASM is purely a market surveillance action and should not be construed as adverse action against the concerned company or entity.
- On exit from the framework, the Price Band of a scrip will be reinstated to the band applicable before shortlisting, unless the scrip is under another surveillance measure.

## Regulatory Changes

This notice references the revised ASM framework as established through prior Exchange notices: 20240920-71 (September 20, 2024), 20181027-1 (October 27, 2018), 20190719-33 (July 19, 2019), 20201204-56 (December 4, 2020), 20230925-49 (September 25, 2023), and 20240920-63 (September 20, 2024). No new framework changes are introduced; this is an application notice under the existing ST-ASM framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure applicable margins (50% for Stage I, 100% for Stage II) are collected on all affected securities from May 26, 2026.
- Margins apply to open positions held as of end-of-day May 25, 2026 as well as any new positions initiated from May 26, 2026 onwards.
- Members with queries or clarifications may contact BSE Surveillance at bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **May 22, 2026**: Notice date.
- **May 25, 2026**: Reference date for existing open positions subject to new margin requirements.
- **May 26, 2026**: Effective date for enhanced margin requirements under ST-ASM Stage I and Stage II.

## Impact Assessment

Traders and investors holding positions in securities listed under Annexure I will face significantly higher margin requirements — up to 100% of position value — effective May 26, 2026. This increases the cost of holding or creating positions in these scrips and may reduce liquidity. Stage II securities face the most restrictive treatment with mandatory 100% margin. The measure is designed to curb speculative activity or abnormal price/volume movements in the flagged securities. Market participants should review the Annexure I list and assess exposure ahead of the May 26 effective date.
