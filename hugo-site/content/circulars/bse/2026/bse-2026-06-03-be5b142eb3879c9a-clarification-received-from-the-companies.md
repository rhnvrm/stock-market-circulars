## Summary

BSE's Surveillance department (DOSS) observed significant price and volume movement in eight listed scrips. The Exchange wrote to the respective companies seeking clarification to ensure trading members and investors have access to the latest relevant information. As of this notice, four companies have submitted replies (attached), while four others have yet to respond.

## Key Points

- BSE observed unusual price/volume movement in eight equity scrips and sought clarifications from the companies
- Replies received from: Alkyl Amines Chemicals Ltd (506767), Balaji Amines Ltd (530999), Sonata Software Ltd (532221), and Sterlite Technologies Ltd (532374)
- Replies still awaited from: Elango Industries Ltd (513452), Nexxus Petro Industries Ltd (544265), York Exports Ltd (530675), and Colinz Laboratories Ltd (531210)
- Clarification documents from the responding companies are attached to this notice

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard surveillance action under BSE's market monitoring framework to ensure timely dissemination of material information.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies that have not yet responded — Elango Industries, Nexxus Petro Industries, York Exports, and Colinz Laboratories — are expected to submit their clarifications to BSE promptly
- Investors and trading members should review the attached replies from the four responding companies before trading in these scrips

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 03 June 2026
- No specific deadline mentioned for pending replies; companies are expected to respond as soon as possible

## Impact Assessment

The circular is informational and primarily relevant to investors holding positions in the eight named scrips. The receipt of clarifications from four companies may help resolve uncertainty around their recent price/volume movements. The four companies with pending replies remain under scrutiny, and investors should exercise caution until clarifications are received. No trading restrictions have been imposed as part of this notice.
