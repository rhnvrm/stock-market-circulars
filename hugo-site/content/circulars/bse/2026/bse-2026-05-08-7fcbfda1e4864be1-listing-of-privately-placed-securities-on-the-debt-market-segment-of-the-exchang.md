## Summary

BSE has listed commercial papers (CPs) issued on a private placement basis by five companies on the BSE Debt Market Segment, effective 08 May 2026. All securities are available for trading in dematerialised form in standard denominations of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof, with a tick size of 1 paise.

## Key Points

- Five commercial papers admitted to trading on BSE Debt Segment effective 08 May 2026
- All CPs have a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each and were allotted on 07 May 2026
- Trading is exclusively in dematerialised form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples; tick size: 1 paise
- Queries to be directed to BSE Debt Department: 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification under existing BSE Debt Market framework for privately placed securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form
- Trades must be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members to refer to respective ISIN numbers for trading

## Important Dates

- **Listing/Trading effective date:** 08 May 2026
- **Allotment date (all CPs):** 07 May 2026
- **Redemption – SBFC Finance CP:** 24 September 2026
- **Redemption – Navi Finserv CP:** 06 August 2026
- **Redemption – Nuvama Clearing Services CP:** 06 August 2026
- **Redemption – Fedbank Financial Services CP:** 06 August 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notice with no broad market impact. The listing enables trading members to trade these privately placed commercial papers on the BSE Debt Segment. All CPs carry top-tier short-term credit ratings (A1+), indicating low credit risk. The interest rates range from 7.45% to 9.62% p.a., reflecting varying credit spreads among issuers. Impact is limited to institutional participants active in the debt segment.
