## Summary

EDELWEISS Mutual Fund is launching EDELWEISS FINANCIAL SERVICES FUND as a New Fund Offer (NFO) with SIP facility on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The NFO period runs from January 27, 2026 to February 10, 2026, with a tentative allotment date of February 17, 2026. The scheme offers six plan variants covering Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options.

## Key Points

- NFO period: January 27, 2026 to February 10, 2026
- Scheme category: Equity
- Six plan variants available: Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- Tentative allotment date: February 17, 2026
- Extended trading available till midnight on February 10, 2026 for lumpsum purchases only
- Transactions without client-initiated funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 will be cancelled
- Clear funds must be available in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on February 13, 2026
- Cheques and OTM payments accepted during NFO period subject to fund clearance
- SIP/XSIP registration authentication required before 3:00 PM on NFO closure date
- Switches accepted during NFO period subject to receipt of funds before allotment date

## Important Dates

- **NFO Start Date**: January 27, 2026
- **NFO Closure Date**: February 10, 2026
- **Extended Period for Lumpsum**: Till midnight on February 10, 2026
- **SIP/XSIP Authentication Deadline**: 3:00 PM on February 10, 2026
- **Clear Funds Deadline**: 6:00 PM on February 13, 2026
- **Tentative Allotment Date**: February 17, 2026

## Operational Requirements

- Transactions for which clients have not initiated funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day (including extended period transactions) will be cancelled and reported to RTA/AMC with CC and N flag
- Transactions with initiated payments remain pending until 3:00 PM on the last day of fund payout to AMC or T+5, whichever is earlier
- Clear funds must be available in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on February 13, 2026 for allotment
- Cheques and OTM payments are acceptable subject to receipt of clear funds by the deadline
- Switch transactions accepted only if funds received before allotment date; switches received on or after allotment date will not be considered

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine NFO launch on the BSE StAR MF Platform expanding investment options for mutual fund investors. The launch provides access to a sector-focused equity fund targeting financial services. Impact is limited to mutual fund distributors and investors interested in the financial services sector through EDELWEISS Mutual Fund schemes.
