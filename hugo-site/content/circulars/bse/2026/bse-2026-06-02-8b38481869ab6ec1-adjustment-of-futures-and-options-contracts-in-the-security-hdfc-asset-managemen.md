## Summary

BSE has announced adjustments to all Futures and Options contracts on HDFC Asset Management Company Ltd (Derivative Asset Code: HAMC, Equity Scrip Code: 541729) in the Equity Derivatives Segment. The adjustment is triggered by the company's declaration of a Final Dividend of Rs. 54.00 per equity share for FY 2026-27, with June 05, 2026 fixed as the Record Date.

## Key Points

- HDFC AMC (HAMC) has declared a Final Dividend of Rs. 54.00/- per equity share for the financial year 2026-27.
- Record Date is set as June 05, 2026; Ex-date is June 05, 2026 (Friday).
- BSE will carry out all contract adjustments on Thursday, June 04, 2026 (end of day).
- For Options: Rs. 54.00 will be deducted from all existing strike prices; adjusted strikes effective from June 05, 2026.
- For Futures: Adjusted futures price = Daily MTM settlement price on June 04, 2026 minus Rs. 54.00; this adjusted rate serves as the base/previous close for June 05, 2026 trading.
- Adjustments are in line with Exchange Notice 20180710-26 and SEBI Master Circular SEBI/HO/MRD/DP/CIR/P/2016/135 dated December 16, 2016.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. The adjustment follows the established BSE/SEBI framework for corporate action-driven F&O contract modifications as outlined in SEBI Master Circular SEBI/HO/MRD/DP/CIR/P/2016/135 and BSE Notice 20180710-26.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members in the Equity Derivatives Segment should note the adjusted strike prices and futures base rates effective June 05, 2026.
- Members should update their risk and margin systems to reflect the adjusted contract parameters from June 05, 2026.
- Members with open positions in HAMC derivatives should account for the Rs. 54.00 price adjustment in their position management.
- For further details, members are requested to contact their respective BSE relationship managers.

## Important Dates

- **June 04, 2026 (Thursday):** BSE carries out F&O contract adjustments at end of day; reference rate (MTM settlement price) used for futures adjustment.
- **June 05, 2026 (Friday):** Ex-date and Record Date for dividend; adjusted strike prices and futures base rates are effective for trading.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, well-defined adjustment affecting all open Futures and Options positions on HDFC AMC (HAMC). The Rs. 54.00 per share dividend deduction from strike prices and futures settlement prices is moderately significant given HDFC AMC's active derivatives market. Traders and hedgers holding HAMC F&O positions should review their strategies as the adjusted prices may affect delta, premium levels, and hedge ratios. No disruption to trading continuity is expected; adjusted contracts will be available normally from June 05, 2026.
