## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Bluegod Entertainment Limited (Scrip Code: 539175) has fixed March 6, 2026 as the record date for its rights issue. Transactions in the company's equity shares must be conducted on an ex-rights basis effective from March 6, 2026 under Settlement No. DR-834/2025-2026.

## Key Points

- Rights issue ratio: 9 (nine) new equity shares for every 47 (forty-seven) equity shares held
- Issue price: Rs. 3/- per share (face value Re. 1/- each at a premium of Rs. 2/- per share)
- Entire issue price of Rs. 3/- per share is payable in full on application
- Record date: March 6, 2026
- Ex-rights effective date: March 6, 2026 (Settlement No. DR-834/2025-2026)
- Segment: Dematerialised Securities – Rolling Settlement Segment

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification under BSE's listing compliance framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all transactions in Bluegod Entertainment Limited (Code: 539175) equity shares are executed on an ex-rights basis from March 6, 2026 onwards.
- Members should update their systems to reflect the ex-rights adjustment effective from the specified settlement date.

## Important Dates

- **Record Date:** March 6, 2026
- **Ex-Rights Effective Date:** March 6, 2026
- **Settlement Number:** DR-834/2025-2026
- **Notice Date:** March 4, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The rights issue will dilute existing shareholding with a ratio of 9:47. The issue price of Rs. 3/- (at a premium of Rs. 2/- over face value of Re. 1/-) is payable entirely on application. Existing shareholders as of the record date will be entitled to subscribe to the rights shares. Trading members and investors holding the stock must account for the ex-rights adjustment in their positions from March 6, 2026. Market impact is limited to existing shareholders of this single scrip.
