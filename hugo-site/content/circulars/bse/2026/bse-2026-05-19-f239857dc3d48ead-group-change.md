## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members that JMG Corporation Ltd (scrip code 523712) will undergo a group change from Group T to Group XT, effective May 20, 2026. Trading in the company's shares is currently under suspension.

## Key Points

- JMG Corporation Ltd (scrip code 523712) is being moved from Group T to Group XT
- The group change is effective May 20, 2026
- Trading in JMG Corporation Ltd shares is currently under suspension
- Notice issued by the Listing Compliance & Operations department

## Regulatory Changes

The company's classification on BSE is changing from Group T (trade-to-trade segment) to Group XT, which typically indicates further surveillance or restricted trading status.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to note and update their records accordingly. No specific action is required from listed companies or investors beyond awareness of the group reclassification.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** May 19, 2026
- **Effective Date:** May 20, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The reclassification to Group XT signals a more restrictive trading category for JMG Corporation Ltd. Since trading is already suspended, the immediate market impact is limited. However, investors and members holding or tracking this scrip should be aware of the changed group status, which may affect future trading terms once the suspension is lifted.
