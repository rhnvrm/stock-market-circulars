## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 20,00,000 new equity shares of Som Distilleries & Breweries Ltd. (Scrip Code: 507514) have been listed and are permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Friday, March 13, 2026. These shares were issued on a preferential basis to promoters at an issue price of Rs. 112/- per share (face value Rs. 2/- + premium Rs. 110/-).

## Key Points

- 20,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 110/-, totalling Rs. 112/- per share
- Shares issued to Promoters on a preferential basis
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 205901313 to 207901312
- ISIN: INE480C01038
- Trading effective from March 13, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification pursuant to a preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to note the new securities available for trading from March 13, 2026
- Lock-in restriction applies to all 20,00,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 205901313 to 207901312) until September 28, 2027

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** April 25, 2025
- **Trading Commencement:** March 13, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry:** September 28, 2027

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 20,00,000 equity shares to the tradable pool of Som Distilleries & Breweries Ltd., though the entire allotment is under lock-in until September 2027 and will not be available for trading by promoters in the near term. Market impact is minimal as these are promoter-held, locked-in shares. The preferential allotment at Rs. 112/- per share reflects the capital-raising activity undertaken in April 2025.
