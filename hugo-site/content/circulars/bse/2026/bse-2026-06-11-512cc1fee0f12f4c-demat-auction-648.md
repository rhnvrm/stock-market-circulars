## Summary

BSE Limited, through Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd (ICCL), has announced Demat Auction 648 for Rolling Settlement No. DR-648/2026-27. Trading members are invited to submit offers for purchase of securities with auction offer entry scheduled on Thursday, June 11, 2026, and securities pay-in on Friday, June 12, 2026.

## Key Points

- Auction offer entry window: 2:00 PM to 2:45 PM on June 11, 2026
- Auction matching: 2:45 PM to 3:00 PM on June 11, 2026
- Delivery statement and accepted offer reports: 3:00 PM to 3:30 PM on June 11, 2026
- Money statement and final reports: 3:35 PM to 5:30 PM on June 11, 2026
- Auction pay-in (securities): Friday, June 12, 2026 at 8:00 AM
- Settlement restriction on members who short-delivered has been removed following implementation of Uniform Membership structure

## Regulatory Changes

Following implementation of the Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment, settlement is now processed through the Clearing Member of a Trading Member. The restriction previously preventing trading members from offering securities in auction for settlements in which they had short-delivered has been removed.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and Custodians must check their auction pay-in obligations for securities and execute securities pay-in in depositories within depository cut-off timings.
- Trading members must exercise utmost care when entering securities codes and rates; no corrections will be permitted for punching errors.
- Trading members should refer to the cutoff rate displayed on the "Auction offer Entry Screen" when submitting offers.
- Members should refer to notices issued regarding Corporate Actions (ex-rights, ex-bonus, ex-split, etc.) for relevant scrips.

## Important Dates

- **June 11, 2026**: Auction offer entry (2:00 PM – 2:45 PM), Matching (2:45 PM – 3:00 PM), Reports (3:00 PM – 5:30 PM)
- **June 12, 2026**: Securities pay-in at 8:00 AM

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice applicable to trading and clearing members with short delivery positions in Rolling Settlement DR-648/2026-27. Market impact is limited to affected members who must fulfill auction obligations. The removal of the short-delivery restriction broadens participation in the auction, potentially improving price discovery and settlement efficiency.
