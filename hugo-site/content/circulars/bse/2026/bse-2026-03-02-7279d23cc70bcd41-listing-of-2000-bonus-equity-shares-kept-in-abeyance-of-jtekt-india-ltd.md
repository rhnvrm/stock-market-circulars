## Summary

BSE has listed 2,000 bonus equity shares of Jtekt India Ltd. (Scrip Code: 520057) that were previously kept in abeyance. These shares are permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Wednesday, March 04, 2026.

## Key Points

- 2,000 Bonus Equity Shares of Re. 1/- each, previously held in abeyance, are now listed on BSE
- Scrip Code: 520057 (Jtekt India Ltd.)
- ISIN: INE643A01035
- Distinctive Numbers: 198681033 to 198683032
- Trading commences on Wednesday, March 04, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a routine administrative action releasing bonus shares that were previously held in abeyance pending resolution of underlying eligibility or corporate action disputes.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed that these securities are now available for trading under Scrip Code 520057 from March 04, 2026. No additional compliance action is required from trading members beyond standard trade handling.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** March 02, 2026
- **Trading Commencement Date:** Wednesday, March 04, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is minimal given the very small quantity of 2,000 shares being released. These shares were held in abeyance as part of a prior bonus issue of Jtekt India Ltd. and their release has no material effect on the company's overall share capital or market liquidity. Trading members should update their records to reflect the new distinctive number range (198681033–198683032) associated with ISIN INE643A01035.
