## Summary

BSE Limited has invited offers from trading members of the Exchange for the purchase of securities in Auction, in respect of Rolling Settlement No. DR-664/2026-27. This is a routine demat auction conducted for securities that were short-delivered in the settlement, allowing trading members to fulfil their delivery obligations through the auction mechanism.

## Key Points

- Auction offer entry, matching, and reporting will take place on Monday, July 06, 2026.
- Auction offer entry window: 02:00 p.m. to 02:45 p.m.
- Auction matching: 02:45 p.m. to 03:00 p.m.
- Delivery statement and report of accepted auction offers: 03:00 p.m. to 03:30 p.m.
- Money statement and final reports: 03:35 p.m. to 05:30 p.m.
- Pay-in of the auction will be held on Tuesday, July 07, 2026 at 08:00 a.m.
- Settlement will be routed through the Clearing Member of a Trading Member under the Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment.
- The earlier restriction preventing trading members from offering securities in an auction for a settlement in which they had short-delivered those same securities has been removed.
- No corrections will be permitted for punching errors in securities code or rate; the Exchange reserves the right to accept or reject any offer without assigning reasons.
- Trading members are advised to refer to the cutoff rate on the Auction Offer Entry Screen and to check for related Corporate Action notices (Ex-Rights/Ex-Bonus/Ex-Split, etc.) before submitting offers.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced; this notice reflects the continued application of the Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment, under which the restriction on trading members offering securities in auctions where they had short-delivered those securities has been removed.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and custodians must check their auction pay-in obligations for securities and execute securities pay-in within depository cut-off timings.
- Members must exercise care while punching securities codes and rates, as corrections are not permitted post-entry.
- Members should cross-check applicable Corporate Action notices for affected scrips prior to placing auction offers.

## Important Dates

- July 06, 2026: Auction offer entry, matching, and reporting (02:00 p.m. – 05:30 p.m.)
- July 07, 2026: Auction pay-in at 08:00 a.m.

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard operational/settlement notice affecting only trading members and custodians involved in Rolling Settlement DR-664/2026-27. It does not directly impact listed companies, retail investors, or broader market pricing, and carries low market-wide significance beyond ensuring orderly settlement of short-delivered securities.
