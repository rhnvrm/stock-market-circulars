## Summary

BSE has notified trading members and custodians of an open offer (takeover) for Restaurant Brands Asia Limited. The acquirers — Lenexis Foodworks Private Limited, Aayush Agrawal Trust, Inspira Foodworks Private Limited, and Mr. Aayush Madhusudan Agrawal — along with PAC Inspira Agro Trading LLC, are offering to purchase up to 26% of the company's expanded voting share capital at INR 70.39 per share. The offer is conducted through BSE's acquisition window mechanism per SEBI's tender-offer framework.

## Key Points

- Acquirers: Lenexis Foodworks Private Limited (Acquirer 1), Aayush Agrawal Trust (Acquirer 2), Inspira Foodworks Private Limited (Acquirer 3), Mr. Aayush Madhusudan Agrawal (Acquirer 4)
- Person Acting in Concert (PAC): Inspira Agro Trading LLC
- Target Company: Restaurant Brands Asia Limited
- Offer price: INR 70.00 per equity share plus applicable interest of INR 0.39, totalling INR 70.39 per share
- Maximum shares sought: 20,80,61,717 equity shares (26% of expanded voting share capital)
- Face value of shares: INR 10.00 each
- Offer conducted via BSE acquisition window under SEBI tender-offer mechanism

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The offer is conducted under existing frameworks:
- SEBI circular CIR/CFD/POLICYCELL/1/2015 dated April 13, 2015
- SEBI circular CFD/DCR2/CIR/P/2016/131 dated December 9, 2016
- BSE revised guidelines (notices: 20170210-16, 20190424-35, 20200528-32, 20201102-43, 20210825-62)
- SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 2011 — Regulations 3(1), 4, 18(11), and 18(11A)

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and custodians must facilitate participation of public shareholders in the offer through the BSE acquisition window
- The offer must comply with SEBI (SAST) Regulations, 2011 and BSE's revised tender-offer guidelines
- Offer details are available on the BSE website (www.bseindia.com)
- A SEBI extension letter dated May 11, 2026 granted an extension to file the Letter of Offer (LOF), which is attached to this notice

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: June 4, 2026
- Offer Opens: Monday, June 8, 2026
- Offer Closes: Friday, June 19, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This open offer has high market impact as it targets 26% of Restaurant Brands Asia Limited's expanded voting share capital. Public shareholders have an opportunity to tender their shares at a premium offer price of INR 70.39 per share. Trading in RBA shares may see elevated activity during the offer window (June 8–19, 2026). Market participants should note that the offer is conducted exclusively through BSE's acquisition window mechanism, and all transactions must adhere to SEBI's revised tender-offer guidelines.
