## Summary

Yaashvi Jewellers Limited, a jewellery company based in Jaipur, Rajasthan, is being listed on the BSE SME platform effective Tuesday, June 2, 2026. The shares will trade under the 'MT' Group of Securities and will be placed in the Trade-for-Trade (TFT) segment for the first 10 trading days as per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012.

## Key Points

- **Scrip Code:** 544770 | **Scrip ID:** YAASHVI | **ISIN:** INE1T6L01010
- Total shares listed: 1,76,21,524 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each, fully paid up
- Issue price: Rs. 83/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- + premium Rs. 73/-)
- Distinctive number range: 1 to 1,76,21,524
- Market lot: 1,600 equity shares
- Date of allotment in public issue: May 29, 2026
- Pari Passu: Yes
- Financial year end: March 31
- Shares will be traded exclusively in dematerialised form
- The scrip will participate in the Special Pre-open Session (SPOS) on June 2, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The listing follows standard BSE SME IPO procedures under existing SEBI guidelines, including the mandatory 10-day Trade-for-Trade segment requirement per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 and dematerialisation-only trading per SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade this scrip only in dematerialised form
- Trades must be executed in minimum market lot of 1,600 equity shares; this may be modified by the Exchange with at least one month's prior market notice
- Lock-in details as per Annexure I must be observed
- Shareholding pattern per Annexure II must be noted
- Market Maker obligations are assigned to Shreni Shares Limited (SEBI Reg. No.: INZ000268538)

## Important Dates

- **May 29, 2026:** Date of allotment in the public issue
- **June 2, 2026:** Effective date of listing and commencement of trading on BSE SME platform
- **June 2, 2026:** Special Pre-open Session (SPOS) for this scrip
- **10 trading days from June 2, 2026:** Duration of Trade-for-Trade segment restriction

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard SME IPO listing with limited broader market impact. Retail and institutional investors who participated in the IPO at Rs. 83 per share can trade from June 2, 2026. The mandatory Trade-for-Trade mechanism for the first 10 sessions restricts speculative intraday trading, as each trade must result in delivery. Market making responsibilities rest with Shreni Shares Limited, ensuring minimum liquidity. The small market lot size of 1,600 shares targets SME-segment investors. Registrar services are provided by Bigshare Services Private Limited.
