## Summary

BSE has notified the listing and admission for trading of further securities issued by seven companies under their respective Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) or Employee Stock Option Schemes (ESOS). These securities are effective for trading from Friday, April 10, 2026. None of the newly listed shares are subject to any lock-in period.

## Key Points

- Seven companies have listed additional shares arising from ESOP/ESOS exercises
- Effective trading date: Friday, April 10, 2026
- None of the newly issued shares carry any lock-in restrictions (all marked NA)
- Total new shares listed span a range of face values: ₹1, ₹2, and ₹10
- Largest issuance by volume: Ujjivan Small Finance Bank with 1,181,559 new shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced. This is a standard operational notice under BSE's listing framework for securities issued pursuant to employee stock option plans.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action is required from market participants. The notice is for information purposes, informing participants that the listed shares are now available for trading on the exchange.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** April 9, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** Friday, April 10, 2026
