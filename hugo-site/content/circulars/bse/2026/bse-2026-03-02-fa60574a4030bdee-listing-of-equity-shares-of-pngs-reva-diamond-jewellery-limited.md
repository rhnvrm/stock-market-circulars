## Summary

BSE has issued this circular regarding the listing and commencement of trading of equity shares of PNGS Reva Diamond Jewellery Limited on the BSE SME platform pursuant to its Initial Public Offering (IPO). The circular provides details about the listing date, trading parameters, and ISIN information for the newly listed company.

## Key Points

- Equity shares of PNGS Reva Diamond Jewellery Limited are being listed on BSE SME platform
- The listing is pursuant to the company's IPO
- Trading in the shares is set to commence effective from the listing date
- The circular specifies the ISIN, scrip code, and market lot details for trading
- A corresponding listing circular has also been issued by NSE for the same company

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a routine listing circular issued under existing BSE listing regulations and SEBI (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations governing IPO listings on the SME platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are required to note the listing details and enable trading in the security from the effective date
- Members must ensure client orders are placed within applicable price bands
- Depository participants should update their systems with the new ISIN for the listed security

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** March 2, 2026
- **Listing / Trading Commencement Date:** March 3, 2026 (effective next trading session following the circular)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a medium impact limited to investors who participated in the PNGS Reva Diamond Jewellery Limited IPO and market participants interested in the SME segment. The listing enables price discovery and provides liquidity to IPO allottees. As an SME listing, broader market impact is minimal. Trading members and depository participants need to update their systems to accommodate the new security.
