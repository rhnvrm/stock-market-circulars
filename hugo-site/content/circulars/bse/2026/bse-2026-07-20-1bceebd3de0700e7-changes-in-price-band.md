## Summary

BSE has notified trading members of revisions to the price bands applicable to nine scrips, effective from July 21, 2026. The revised price bands range from 2% to 20% depending on the scrip, reflecting changes in the surveillance-based volatility limits set by the Exchange.

## Key Points

- Nine scrips have had their price bands revised by BSE's Surveillance Department (DOSS).
- Revised price bands (effective July 21, 2026):
  - Anirit Ventures Ltd (530705): 2%
  - Atharva Poly-Plast Ltd (544812): 10%
  - Epsom Properties Ltd (531155): 2%
  - HOMRE Ltd (523387): 2%
  - Kratikal Tech Ltd (544811): 20%
  - NCL Research & Financial Services Ltd (530557): 10%
  - Sampark India Logistics Ltd (544810): 20%
  - Saptak Chem And Business Ltd (506906): 5%
  - Seemax Resources Ltd (544813): 10%
- Notice issued by BSE's Surveillance team (Sanjay Jain, Deputy Vice President; Arpita Joshi, Manager).

## Regulatory Changes

The price band (daily circuit filter) percentage applicable to each of the listed scrips has been changed from its existing level to the new percentage specified in the notice, under BSE's ongoing price band review/surveillance process.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must update their systems to reflect the revised price bands for these scrips from the effective date. No other compliance filing is required; queries can be directed to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 20, 2026
- Effective Date of Revised Price Bands: July 21, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The revision alters the permissible intraday price movement (circuit limits) for the nine affected scrips, which can influence trading strategies, liquidity, and volatility management for market participants holding or trading these securities. The impact is scrip-specific and does not affect the broader market.
