## Summary

BSE has informed members that Axis Mutual Fund has implemented subscription restrictions for AXIS GOLD FUND on the BSE StAR MF Platform, effective June 8, 2026. Fresh/additional purchases via lump sum and switch-in transactions are capped at ₹10,00,000 per PAN per month. Redemptions and outflows remain unrestricted.

## Key Points

- Fresh/additional purchases (lump sum mode) and switch-in transactions in AXIS GOLD FUND are limited to ₹10,00,000 per PAN per month
- The restriction applies to transactions received after the 3:00 PM cut-off on June 8, 2026
- Redemption, switch-out, and STP-out transactions are permitted without any amount restriction
- Subscription orders above ₹10,00,000 triggered to the RTA on or after 3:00 PM on June 8, 2026 will be rejected and refunded
- Refunds will be processed after funds are received from AMC/RTA

## Regulatory Changes

Axis Mutual Fund issued a Notice-cum-Addendum imposing a monthly subscription cap of ₹10,00,000 per PAN for AXIS GOLD FUND. BSE has implemented these changes on the BSE StAR MF Platform in accordance with the AMC communication.

## Compliance Requirements

- BSE members and distributors must ensure that subscription orders for AXIS GOLD FUND do not exceed ₹10,00,000 per PAN per month for lump sum purchases and switch-ins
- Orders exceeding the limit submitted after the cut-off will be rejected; members should inform investors of the cap to avoid failed transactions and refund delays

## Important Dates

- **June 8, 2026, 3:00 PM**: Effective date and time for the subscription restriction; orders above ₹10,00,000 received after this cut-off will be rejected

## Impact Assessment

This restriction primarily affects investors seeking to make large lump sum investments or switch-ins into AXIS GOLD FUND exceeding ₹10 lakh per month per PAN. Gold fund inflows may be moderated, likely in response to excess liquidity or portfolio rebalancing needs at the AMC level. Outflows (redemptions, switch-outs, STP-outs) are unaffected, so existing investors retain full exit flexibility. Operational impact on BSE StAR MF Platform participants is moderate, requiring order validation checks against the per-PAN monthly cap.
