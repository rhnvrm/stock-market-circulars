## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260608-18 informs trading members that 13,000 new equity shares of Apollo Micro Systems Limited (Scrip Code: 540879) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Tuesday, June 9, 2026. The shares were issued to a Non-Promoter on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants at an issue price of Rs. 114/- per share (face value Rs. 1/- at a premium of Rs. 113/-).

## Key Points

- 13,000 equity shares of Rs. 1/- face value issued at a premium of Rs. 113/- per share
- Issued to Non-Promoter on a preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 357292441 to 357305440
- Issue price: Rs. 114/- per share
- ISIN: INE713T01028
- Lock-in applicable on all 13,000 shares until December 10, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notice informing trading members of newly allotted equity shares now available for trading.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are notified that the 13,000 new equity shares are listed and permitted to trade on BSE effective June 9, 2026. The entire allotment of 13,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 357292441 to 357305440) is subject to a lock-in period and cannot be freely traded until December 10, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** May 12, 2026
- **Trading Commencement:** June 9, 2026 (Tuesday)
- **Lock-in Expiry:** December 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The 13,000 newly listed shares represent a small incremental addition to Apollo Micro Systems Limited's total share capital. The lock-in period extending to December 10, 2026 prevents the allottee from selling these shares in the near term, further limiting any potential downward price pressure. This is a standard post-preferential-allotment listing notification.
