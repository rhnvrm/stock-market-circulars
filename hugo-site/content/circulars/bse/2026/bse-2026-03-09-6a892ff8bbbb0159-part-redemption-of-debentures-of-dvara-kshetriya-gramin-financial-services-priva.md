## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that DVARA KSHETRIYA GRAMIN FINANCIAL SERVICES PRIVATE LIMITED has fixed a record date of 12 March 2026 for the part redemption of its Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) and payment of interest. Trading in these debentures will be conducted at a reduced face value from the effective date.

## Key Points

- Company: DVARA KSHETRIYA GRAMIN FINANCIAL SERVICES PRIVATE LIMITED
- Debenture Series: DKGFSPL-11%-27-12-26-PVT (ISIN: INE179P07548, Scrip Code: 976048)
- Record Date: 12 March 2026
- Purpose: Part Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- Reduced Face Value: Rs. 3,750 per Debenture
- Effective Date: 12 March 2026
- Settlement Number: DR-838/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification under BSE's debt segment procedures for part redemption events.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that debentures of DVARA KSHETRIYA GRAMIN FINANCIAL SERVICES PRIVATE LIMITED will trade with a reduced face value of Rs. 3,750 per debenture effective from 12 March 2026.
- Members should update their systems to reflect the reduced face value for settlement purposes under DR-838/2025-2026.

## Important Dates

- Record Date: 12 March 2026
- Effective Date for Reduced Face Value Trading: 12 March 2026
- Notice Date: 09 March 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action with limited market impact, affecting only holders of the DKGFSPL-11%-27-12-26-PVT NCD series (ISIN: INE179P07548). The reduction in face value reflects the partial principal repayment made to debenture holders. Broader market impact is negligible as this concerns a privately placed debt instrument of a private limited NBFC.
