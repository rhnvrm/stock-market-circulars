## Summary

BSE has notified members that, pursuant to its earlier Notice No. 20260630-50 dated June 30, 2026, the CHOICE OVERNIGHT FUND will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions, along with SIP, STP and SWP facilities, on the BSE StAR MF Platform with effect from July 06, 2026.

## Key Points

- CHOICE OVERNIGHT FUND transactions resume on the BSE StAR MF Platform from July 06, 2026.
- Facility covers subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP and SWP.
- Four scheme options are available under the fund, spanning Regular and Direct plans with Growth and Daily IDCW Reinvestment options:
  - CHOICE OVERNIGHT FUND - Regular Plan - Growth (ISIN: INF2KCX01095)
  - CHOICE OVERNIGHT FUND - Regular Plan - Daily IDCW Reinvestment (ISIN: INF2KCX01079)
  - CHOICE OVERNIGHT FUND - Direct Plan - Growth (ISIN: INF2KCX01087)
  - CHOICE OVERNIGHT FUND - Direct Plan - Daily IDCW Reinvestment (ISIN: INF2KCX01061)
- All four schemes fall under the Overnight fund category.
- Notice issued by Arunkumar Ganesan (VP - Mutual Fund) and Mahesh Tare (AVP - Mutual Fund).

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is an operational update implementing the reopening referenced in prior Notice No. 20260630-50 dated June 30, 2026.

## Compliance Requirements

BSE StAR MF Platform members and distributors dealing in CHOICE OVERNIGHT FUND should ensure their systems and order routing reflect the reopened status for transactions from the effective date, using the correct scheme and ISIN codes listed above.

## Important Dates

- June 30, 2026: Original notice (20260630-50) referenced.
- July 03, 2026: Current notice issued.
- July 06, 2026: Effective date for reopening of ongoing transactions (subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, SWP).

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, low-impact operational notice limited to mutual fund distributors and investors transacting in the CHOICE OVERNIGHT FUND via the BSE StAR MF Platform. It has no bearing on listed equities or broader market operations.
