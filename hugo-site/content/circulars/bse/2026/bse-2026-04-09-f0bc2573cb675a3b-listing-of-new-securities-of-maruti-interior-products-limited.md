## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 4,53,00,000 new equity shares of Maruti Interior Products Limited (Scrip Code: 543464) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Friday, April 10, 2026. The shares were issued on a rights basis at Rs. 10/- par value each.

## Key Points

- 4,53,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued on rights basis are now listed on BSE
- Scrip Code: 543464 | ISIN: INE0JSJ01014
- Distribution numbers: 15100001 to 60400000
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Trading effective from Friday, April 10, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification following completion of a rights issue.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed to note the listing and enable trading in these new securities from the effective date. No additional compliance action is required from members beyond standard trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** April 07, 2026
- **Trading Effective From:** April 10, 2026 (Friday)
- **Notice Date:** April 09, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing increases the total tradeable equity share count of Maruti Interior Products Limited by 4,53,00,000 shares. Since these shares are pari-passu with existing shares, they carry identical rights. The rights issue increases float and may have a short-term dilutive effect on existing shareholders. Impact is limited to investors and traders in this specific scrip.
