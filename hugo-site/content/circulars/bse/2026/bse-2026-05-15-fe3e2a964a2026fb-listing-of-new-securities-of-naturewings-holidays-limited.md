## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 3,98,400 new equity shares of Naturewings Holidays Limited (Scrip Code: 544245) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Monday, May 18, 2026. These shares were issued on a preferential basis to non-promoters at an issue price of Rs. 75/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- plus premium of Rs. 65/-).

## Key Points

- **Scrip Code:** 544245
- **ISIN:** INE0N4701016
- **Number of shares listed:** 3,98,400 equity shares
- **Face value:** Rs. 10/- each, issued at a premium of Rs. 65/- (total issue price Rs. 75/-)
- **Allotment type:** Preferential basis to non-promoters
- **Distribution numbers:** 3160401 to 3558800
- **Date of allotment:** January 6, 2026
- **Trading effective from:** Monday, May 18, 2026
- **Shares rank pari-passu** with existing equity shares of the company

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification under BSE's Listing Operations framework for newly allotted securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to permit trading in these new securities from May 18, 2026.
- The entire allotment of 3,98,400 shares is subject to a **lock-in period until November 30, 2026**; these shares cannot be sold or transferred until the lock-in expires.

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Date of Allotment | January 6, 2026 |
| Trading Commences | May 18, 2026 |
| Lock-in Expiry | November 30, 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine preferential allotment listing for Naturewings Holidays Limited, a relatively small company. The addition of 3,98,400 shares represents a modest capital expansion. The lock-in restriction through November 30, 2026 limits near-term selling pressure from these newly listed shares. Impact is confined to existing and prospective shareholders of this specific company with no broader market implications.
