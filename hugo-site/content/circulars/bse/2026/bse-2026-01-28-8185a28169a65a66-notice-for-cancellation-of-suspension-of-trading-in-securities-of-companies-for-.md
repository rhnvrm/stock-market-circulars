## Summary

BSE has issued a notice canceling the trading suspension for Benara Bearings and Pistons Ltd (Scrip Code: 541178). The suspension, which was imposed due to non-compliance with SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015, will be lifted effective January 30, 2026. This follows the company's compliance with the required regulatory provisions.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension for Benara Bearings and Pistons Ltd (Scrip Code: 541178) will be canceled
- The cancellation is in continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20251230-27 dated December 30, 2025
- Company has achieved compliance with SEBI LODR Regulations, 2015
- Market participants can resume trading activities in this security from the effective date

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular represents the reversal of a previous suspension order following the company's achievement of compliance with existing SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015.

## Compliance Requirements

- No action required from market participants
- Market participants should note that normal trading will resume for the specified scrip
- For clarifications, market participants can contact BSE at bse.soplodr@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** January 28, 2026
- **Effective Date of Suspension Cancellation:** January 30, 2026
- **Reference Notice:** Exchange Notice No. 20251230-27 dated December 30, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - Affects only one company with relatively small market presence.

**Operational Impact:** Positive for the company and its shareholders as trading activity can resume. The suspension cancellation indicates that Benara Bearings and Pistons Ltd has rectified its non-compliance issues and met all regulatory requirements under SEBI LODR Regulations.

**Investor Impact:** Beneficial for existing shareholders who will regain liquidity in their holdings. The company's return to normal trading status may restore investor confidence.
