## Summary

BSE has listed 14,50,000 new equity shares of Cranex Ltd. (Scrip Code: 522001, ISIN: INE608B01010) arising from a preferential allotment through conversion of warrants. The shares are permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Monday, June 29, 2026. Shares were issued at Rs. 102/- each (face value Rs. 10/- at a premium of Rs. 92/-) to both promoter and non-promoter shareholders.

## Key Points

- 14,50,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 92/- (Issue Price: Rs. 102/-)
- Allotment made to Promoter and Non-Promoter on a preferential basis via conversion of warrants
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 6570001 to 8020000
- Date of Allotment: April 24, 2026
- Trading permitted from June 29, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for newly allotted equity shares following warrant conversion under preferential allotment norms.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed that the new securities are listed and may be traded from June 29, 2026. Lock-in restrictions must be observed for the respective tranches as detailed below.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** April 24, 2026
- **Trading Effective From:** June 29, 2026 (Monday)
- **Lock-in Expiry (7,50,000 shares – Dist. Nos. 6570001–7320000):** December 30, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (3,00,000 shares – Dist. Nos. 7320001–7620000):** December 31, 2027
- **Lock-in Expiry (4,00,000 shares – Dist. Nos. 7620001–8020000):** December 30, 2027

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 14,50,000 shares to the tradeable float of Cranex Ltd., though a majority are subject to lock-in periods extending to end of 2026 and 2027. Only shares beyond the lock-in period will be freely tradeable from June 29, 2026. The preferential allotment at Rs. 102/- per share via warrant conversion signals capital raising activity by the company. The pari-passu ranking means no differential rights compared to existing shareholders.
