## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 1,15,36,824 new equity shares of Solara Active Pharma Sciences Ltd. (Scrip Code: 541540, ISIN: INE624Z01016) are permitted for trading effective Friday, May 29, 2026. These shares arise from the conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up at Rs. 112.50 each, issued on a rights basis.

## Key Points

- **Company:** Solara Active Pharma Sciences Ltd. (Scrip Code: 541540)
- **ISIN:** INE624Z01016
- **New shares listed:** 1,15,36,824 equity shares
- **Nature of shares:** Converted from partly paid to fully paid up at Rs. 112.50 each, issued on rights basis
- **Trading effective from:** Friday, May 29, 2026
- **Distinctive number ranges:** 10050807; 36005268 to 46056074; 1486017; 46066743 to 47552759
- Shares are pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice under BSE's Listing Operations framework following completion of a rights issue conversion.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are permitted to trade in these new securities from May 29, 2026 onwards. No additional compliance action is required from members beyond standard trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- **May 29, 2026:** Effective date for trading in new equity shares of Solara Active Pharma Sciences Ltd.

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 1,15,36,824 new fully paid equity shares of SOLARA to the tradeable float on BSE, increasing overall liquidity. Since these shares are pari-passu with existing equity, there is no differential treatment. Shareholders who held partly paid rights shares are now able to freely trade their holdings. The impact is moderate as it represents a supply increase to the market following conclusion of the rights issue process.
