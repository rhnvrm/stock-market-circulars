## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for Darjeeling Ropeway Co Ltd. The company name has been officially changed to Darjeeling Industries Limited with effect from December 26, 2025. This is an administrative change that does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders.

## Key Points

- Company name changed from DARJEELING ROPEWAY CO LTD to DARJEELING INDUSTRIIES LIMITED
- Corporate Identification Number (CIN): L45202MH1936PLC294011
- Company type: Company limited by shares
- Certificate issued by Central Processing Centre, Ministry of Corporate Affairs
- Registered office: 104, Floor-1, Shreeji Darshan, Tata Road No. 2, Roxy Cinema Opera House, Girgaon, Mumbai, Mumbai City-400004, Maharashtra
- Certificate digitally signed by M. Yadubhushana Rao, Assistant/Deputy/Registrar of Companies

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes involved. This is a standard administrative procedure for company name change under the Companies Act, 2013 and Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014, Rule 29.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display the old name (DARJEELING ROPEWAY CO LTD) for a period of two years along with its new name at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act, 2013
- All stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company on the Ministry of Corporate Affairs website www.mca.gov.in/MCA21
- The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date of Name Change**: December 26, 2025
- **Certificate Issue Date**: December 26, 2025 (11:30:23 IST)
- **Old Name Display Requirement**: Two years from effective date (until December 26, 2027)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine corporate administrative action.

**Operational Impact**: No impact on company operations, business activities, or financial obligations.

**Stakeholder Impact**: No impact on rights and liabilities of shareholders, creditors, or other stakeholders. The company continues with the same CIN and legal entity status.

**Trading Impact**: Investors and market participants should note the new name for identification purposes. The company's stock symbol may be updated to reflect the new name.
