## Summary

Motilal Oswal Asset Management Company Limited has announced a one-day extension of the New Fund Offer (NFO) period for the Motilal Oswal Nifty Diversified Equity Flexicap Passive FOF scheme. The closing date has been extended from January 15, 2026 to January 16, 2026, with the re-opening date adjusted to January 29, 2026. All other terms and conditions of the scheme remain unchanged.

## Key Points

- NFO closing date extended from January 15, 2026 to January 16, 2026
- Re-opening date adjusted to January 29, 2026
- Scheme: Motilal Oswal Nifty Diversified Equity Flexicap Passive FOF
- All other terms and conditions remain unchanged
- Notice cum addendum forms integral part of Scheme Information Document and Key Information Memorandum
- Available on BSE StAR MF Platform

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative update regarding the NFO timeline.

## Compliance Requirements

- Investors are requested to take note of the revised closure and re-opening dates
- The notice cum addendum forms an integral part of the Scheme Information Document and Key Information Memorandum
- Standard mutual fund investment disclosures apply: "Mutual fund investments are subject to market risks, read all scheme related documents carefully"

## Important Dates

- **Original NFO Closure Date**: January 15, 2026
- **Extended NFO Closure Date**: January 16, 2026
- **Re-opening Date**: January 29, 2026
- **Circular Date**: January 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact of this circular is minimal as it only involves a one-day extension of the NFO period. This provides investors with an additional day to subscribe to the fund offer. The extension does not affect existing market operations or trading activities. The change is purely administrative and benefits potential investors who may need additional time to complete their subscription process. No impact on stock markets or listed securities.
