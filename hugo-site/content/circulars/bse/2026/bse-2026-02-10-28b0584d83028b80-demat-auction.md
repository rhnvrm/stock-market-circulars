## Summary

BSE has published the demat auction schedule for Settlement No. 816 dated February 10, 2026. The circular lists 155+ securities across various scrip codes along with the quantities scheduled for auction. This auction is part of the settlement mechanism for securities where delivery obligations were not met in the normal settlement process.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 816
- Settlement Date: February 10, 2026
- Total securities listed: 155+ scrips across multiple companies
- Quantities range from 1 unit to 80,730 units depending on the security
- Includes major stocks like State Bank of India (3,684 units), Larsen & Toubro, Hero MotoCorp, Vedanta, and others
- Also includes smaller companies and specialty securities
- Covers diverse sectors including banking, manufacturing, technology, energy, and finance

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine operational circular for auction settlement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members with short delivery positions in the listed securities must participate in the demat auction
- Members must ensure adequate funds/securities are available for auction settlement
- Settlement obligations must be fulfilled as per BSE auction mechanisms
- Members should note the specific quantities for each scrip code listed

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: February 10, 2026 (Settlement No. 816)
- Auction execution date would follow BSE's standard auction timeline post this circular

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - Affects specific securities where delivery shortfalls occurred. The auction mechanism helps complete settlement obligations.

**Trading Member Impact:** Medium to High - Trading members with short positions in these securities must participate in the auction, potentially at higher prices than market rates.

**Investor Impact:** Low to Medium - Investors expecting delivery of these securities will receive them through the auction process. Some price impact possible during auction execution.

**Operational Impact:** This is a standard settlement process ensuring market integrity and completion of delivery obligations for the listed securities.
