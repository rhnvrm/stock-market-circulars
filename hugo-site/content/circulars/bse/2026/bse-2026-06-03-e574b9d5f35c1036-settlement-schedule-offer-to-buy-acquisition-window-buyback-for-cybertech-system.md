## Summary

ICCL (Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd.) has published the settlement calendar for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Buyback) for Cybertech Systems and Software Limited. The buyback window will be open from June 4, 2026 to June 10, 2026, with settlement scheduled for June 17, 2026 under Settlement No. 2026062.

## Key Points

- Buyback acquisition window runs from **04/06/2026 to 10/06/2026**
- Settlement Number: **2026062** (Settlement Type: Buyback)
- Obligation download date: **16/06/2026**; Settlement date: **17/06/2026**
- Demat shares must be delivered exclusively via the Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism available on both CDSL and NSDL
- Members/Custodians must use Market Type "(Buyback)" and Settlement No. 2026062 in the depository system
- CDSL & NSDL early pay-in securities will be earmarked/blocked in client accounts under the requisite market type and settlement number
- No EPN file upload is required from Members & Custodians
- Members should refer to the MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for client-wise Stamp Duty charges

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This notice references existing guidelines per Notice No. 20200528-32 dated May 28, 2020, regarding settlement as per Revised Guidelines of Mechanism for Acquisition of Shares through Stock Exchange.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and Custodians must ensure early pay-in of securities is reflected in BSE/ICCL systems **before** the prescribed timelines for accepting/confirming bids on the BSE OTB platform
- Early pay-in must be completed sufficiently in advance of the closure of the BSE OTB Bidding window (for members placing bids) and the Confirmation window (for Custodians confirming bids)
- Use the correct Market Type "(Buyback)" and Settlement No. 2026062 when submitting to the depository system
- Refer to MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for Stamp Duty details per client

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Bid Start Date | 04 June 2026 |
| Bid End Date | 10 June 2026 |
| Obligation Download | 16 June 2026 |
| Settlement Date | 17 June 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a moderate operational impact on stock brokers, members, and custodians participating in the Cybertech Systems and Software Limited buyback. Participants must act promptly to complete early pay-in before BSE OTB window deadlines to avoid bid rejection. The settlement follows a standard buyback acquisition window process with no unusual conditions. Shareholders of Cybertech Systems and Software Limited tendering shares in the buyback are directly affected by these operational timelines.
