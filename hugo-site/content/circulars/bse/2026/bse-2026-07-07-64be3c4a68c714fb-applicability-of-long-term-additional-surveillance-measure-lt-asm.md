## Summary

BSE has issued its periodic update on the Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LT-ASM) framework, referencing prior notices dated March 21, 2018, October 27, 2018, December 04, 2020, August 09, 2024, September 20, 2024, and June 04, 2021. The notice specifies securities newly placed under LT-ASM, securities moving to higher stages within the framework, securities moving out of the framework, and securities being transferred to T/XT/MT/TS trading groups while remaining under LT-ASM. A consolidated list of all securities currently under the framework is provided as Annexure III.

## Key Points

- 100% margin shall apply on all open positions as of July 09, 2026 and on new positions created from July 10, 2026 onwards, for securities in Annexure I (Part A).
- Securities moving to higher LT-ASM stages are listed in Annexure I (Part B), effective July 08, 2026.
- Lower Price Band applies effective July 08, 2026 for the securities covered under Part A/B.
- Securities under the June 04, 2021 framework (Annexure I Part D) will remain in LT-ASM but be transferred to T/XT/MT/TS trading groups effective July 10, 2026.
- Securities exiting the LT-ASM framework effective July 08, 2026 are listed in Annexure II; their price bands will revert to pre-shortlisting levels, unless subject to another surveillance measure.
- Shortlisting under this framework is based on XBRL submissions by listed companies and is not to be construed as adverse action against any company.
- Securities under the framework are reviewed periodically.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced; this notice is a periodic operational update applying the existing revised LT-ASM framework (as amended via notices in 2018, 2020, 2021, and 2024) to a fresh list of securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must apply 100% margin on affected securities from July 10, 2026 (based on open positions as of July 09, 2026) and on new positions thereafter.
- Members must apply the revised lower price bands from July 08, 2026 for applicable securities.
- Members must ensure securities moving into T/XT/MT/TS groups are handled per the applicable trading group rules from July 10, 2026.
- Queries can be directed to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- July 08, 2026: Lower price band effective; list of securities moving to higher LT-ASM stages and list of securities exiting LT-ASM effective.
- July 09, 2026: Reference date for open positions subject to 100% margin.
- July 10, 2026: 100% margin effective on open and new positions; securities transferred to T/XT/MT/TS groups.

## Impact Assessment

The measures increase trading costs and reduce trading flexibility (via 100% margin and tighter price bands) for the specific securities named in the annexures, likely dampening speculative trading and volatility in those scrips. Securities exiting the framework benefit from restored normal price bands, improving trading conditions. Overall market-wide impact is limited since the measure targets a defined subset of surveilled securities rather than broad market segments.
