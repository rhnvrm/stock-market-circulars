BSE has announced that the Draft Offer Document of LARISA HOTELS AND RESORTS LIMITED, filed on December 31, 2025, is now available on the BSE SME website for public comments. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and will remain available for at least 21 days for stakeholder feedback.

- Draft Offer Document filed by LARISA HOTELS AND RESORTS LIMITED on December 31, 2025
- Document contains all disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website (www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx) for minimum 21 days
- Public comments solicited via email at equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Nitinkumar Pujari, Assistant Vice President
- Notice Number: 20260105-17
- Segment: SME
- Category: Company related
