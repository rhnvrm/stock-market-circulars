## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that AlphaGrep Mutual Fund will launch the AlphaGrep Flexi Cap Fund as a New Fund Offer (NFO) with SIP facility on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The NFO will be open from July 21, 2026, to August 4, 2026, with a tentative allotment date of August 11, 2026.

## Key Points

- Scheme: AlphaGrep Flexi Cap Fund (Equity category), offered in Regular and Direct plans, each with Growth, IDCW Reinvestment, and IDCW Payout options
- Six ISINs listed covering Regular Plan (Growth: INF2VOA01103, IDCW Reinvestment: INF2VOA01111, IDCW Payout: INF2VOA01129) and Direct Plan (Growth: INF2VOA01079, IDCW Reinvestment: INF2VOA01087, IDCW Payout: INF2VOA01095)
- Tentative allotment date for all options: August 11, 2026
- NFO period with SIP facility: July 21, 2026 to August 4, 2026
- Lumpsum purchases extended till midnight on August 4, 2026; SIP/XSIP registrations must be authenticated by clients on or before 3:00 PM on August 4, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an operational notice regarding a new scheme launch rather than a regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

- Transactions for which clients do not initiate funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day (including during the extended NFO period) will be cancelled, and CC with 'N' flag reported to the RTA/AMC
- Transactions with client-initiated payment remain pending until 3:00 PM on the last day of funds pay-out to the AMC, or T+5 day, whichever is earlier
- Clear funds must be available in the ICCL account by 6:00 PM on August 11, 2026, for allotment
- Cheque and OTM payments accepted during the NFO period, subject to receipt of clear funds in the ICCL account by 6:00 PM on August 11, 2026

## Important Dates

- NFO/SIP period: July 21, 2026 – August 4, 2026
- Lumpsum purchase extension: till midnight, August 4, 2026
- SIP/XSIP authentication deadline: 3:00 PM, August 4, 2026
- Tentative allotment date: August 11, 2026
- Clear funds deadline in ICCL account: 6:00 PM, August 11, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine mutual fund product launch notice with operational relevance limited to trading members and distributors handling transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform. It has no direct impact on listed securities, market pricing, or broader trading activity.
