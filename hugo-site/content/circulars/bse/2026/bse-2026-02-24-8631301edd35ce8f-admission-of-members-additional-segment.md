## Summary

BSE has admitted four trading members to additional segments effective February 24, 2026. Three members have been granted trading rights in the Debt segment, and one member has been admitted to the Equity Derivatives segment. These admissions are subject to BSE's Rules, Bye-laws, and Regulations.

## Key Points

- FRR Shares & Securities Ltd. (Member Code: 6207) admitted to the Debt segment
- Microcurves Trading Private Limited (Member Code: 6868) admitted to the Debt segment
- Wealth India Financial Services Pvt. Ltd. (Member Code: 6521) admitted to the Debt segment
- Composite Securities Ltd. (Member Code: 3145) admitted to the Equity Derivatives segment
- Admissions are for trading rights only on BSE Ltd.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine administrative notice under BSE's membership admission process.

## Compliance Requirements

Existing trading members requiring clarification on this notice may contact BSE Membership Operations at membership.ops@bseindia.com. The newly admitted members must comply with BSE Rules, Bye-laws, and Regulations applicable to their respective segments.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: February 24, 2026 (Notice No. 20260224-3)
- Effective Date: February 24, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard operational notice expanding segment access for four existing BSE members. It slightly increases the participant base in the Debt and Equity Derivatives segments, which may marginally improve liquidity in those segments. No action is required from existing trading members.
