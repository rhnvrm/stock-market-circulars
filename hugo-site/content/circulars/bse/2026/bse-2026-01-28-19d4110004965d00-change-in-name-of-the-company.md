## Summary

The Registrar of Companies, Gujarat, Dadra and Nagar Havelli has issued a Fresh Certificate of Incorporation consequent upon change of name. The company originally incorporated as DEVIKA FINSTOCKS PRIVATE LIMITED on June 6, 1994 under the Companies Act, 1956, changed its name to DEVIKA PROTEINS LIMITED, and has now been renamed to DHARTI PROTEINS LIMITED effective July 22, 2011.

## Key Points

- Corporate Identity Number (CIN): L67120GJ1994PLC022199
- Original incorporation name: DEVIKA FINSTOCKS PRIVATE LIMITED (June 6, 1994)
- Previous name: DEVIKA PROTEINS LIMITED
- New name: DHARTI PROTEINS LIMITED
- Name change approved under Section 21 of the Companies Act, 1956
- Certificate issued pursuant to Section 23(1) of the Companies Act, 1956
- Government of India approval via Notification No. G.S.R 507 (E) dated June 24, 1985
- Service Request Number (SRN): B15369341
- Registered office: 203, 2nd Floor, Abhijeet-1, Nr Mithakhali Circle, Ellisbridge, Ahmedabad - 380006, Gujarat

## Regulatory Changes

The name change was conducted in compliance with:
- Section 21 of the Companies Act, 1956 (resolution requirements)
- Section 23(1) of the Companies Act, 1956 (certificate issuance)
- Central Government approval under relevant notifications

## Compliance Requirements

- Company passed necessary resolution for name change
- Obtained Central Government approval in writing
- Fresh Certificate of Incorporation issued by Registrar of Companies
- Certificate digitally signed under Companies (Electronic Filing and Authentication of Documents) Rules, 2006, Rule 5(2)

## Important Dates

- **Original Incorporation Date**: June 6, 1994
- **Government Notification Date**: June 24, 1985 (G.S.R 507 E)
- **Name Change Effective Date**: July 22, 2011
- **Certificate Issue Date**: July 22, 2011

This is a historical document from 2011 being circulated for record purposes. The name change from DEVIKA PROTEINS LIMITED to DHARTI PROTEINS LIMITED has no immediate market impact as it occurred over a decade ago.
