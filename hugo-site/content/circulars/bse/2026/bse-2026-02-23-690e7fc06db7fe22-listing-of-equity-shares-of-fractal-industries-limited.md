## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members that equity shares of Fractal Industries Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the BSE SME segment. The scrip details have been provided, but the exact date of listing will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Fractal Industries Limited is being listed on the BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544707
- Symbol: FIL
- ISIN: INE1W3Q01011
- The listing date will be announced via a separate notice
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard new listing notification under BSE's SME platform admission process.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are advised to take note of the forthcoming listing of Fractal Industries Limited and await the separate notice that will confirm the listing date and security details before initiating any transactions.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: February 23, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced via separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a moderate market impact limited to the BSE SME segment. The listing of Fractal Industries Limited (FIL) opens trading opportunities for investors in the SME space. The absence of a confirmed listing date means traders should monitor BSE announcements closely for the follow-up notice before placing orders.
