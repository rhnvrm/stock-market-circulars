## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 71,16,572 new equity shares of Kati Patang Lifestyle Ltd (Scrip Code: 531126) are permitted for trading effective Friday, May 22, 2026. These shares arise from the conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up equity shares of Rs. 10/- each, issued on a rights basis.

## Key Points

- 71,16,572 equity shares of Kati Patang Lifestyle Ltd admitted for trading on BSE
- Shares result from conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up (Rs. 10/- each) issued on rights basis
- ISIN: INE237C01016
- Distinctive Numbers: 35898281 to 43014852
- New shares are pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Scrip Code: 531126

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification following completion of the rights issue conversion process.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed and permitted to trade in these newly listed securities from the effective date. No additional compliance action is required from members beyond standard trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: May 21, 2026
- Trading Effective Date: May 22, 2026 (Friday)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. The listing adds 71,16,572 new equity shares to the tradeable float of Kati Patang Lifestyle Ltd, which may marginally increase liquidity for this scrip. The shares are pari-passu with existing equity, so no dilution of rights or preferential treatment applies. Impact is contained to existing shareholders and traders in this specific scrip.
