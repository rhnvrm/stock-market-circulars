## Summary

BSE has listed three privately placed debt instruments issued by Aditya Birla Capital Ltd (ABCL) on the BSE Debt Segment, effective March 12, 2026. All securities were allotted on March 10, 2026, are tradeable only in dematerialised form, and carry a tick size of 1 paise.

## Key Points

- Three ABCL debt instruments admitted to trading on BSE Debt Segment from March 12, 2026
- All securities issued on a private placement basis and tradeable in demat form only
- Tick size: 1 paise; Market lot: 1 for all three instruments
- Credit ratings of AAA (CRISIL and/or ICRA) for all securities
- For queries, trading members may contact BSE Debt Department: 22728352 / 8597 / 8995 / 5753 / 8915

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a routine further listing notice for privately placed debt instruments under the existing BSE Debt Segment framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum on the BSE website for further details
- Placement memoranda available at: https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** March 10, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** March 12, 2026
- **Redemption Dates:** September 15, 2028 (Scrip 976974) | December 21, 2028 (Scrip 958487) | October 3, 2031 (Scrip 973520)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a routine further listing of privately placed debt instruments by a AAA-rated issuer (Aditya Birla Capital Ltd). The listing adds liquidity options for institutional holders of these instruments on the BSE Debt Segment. No action required by equity market participants. Trading members dealing in debt instruments should update their systems with the new scrip codes and ISINs.
