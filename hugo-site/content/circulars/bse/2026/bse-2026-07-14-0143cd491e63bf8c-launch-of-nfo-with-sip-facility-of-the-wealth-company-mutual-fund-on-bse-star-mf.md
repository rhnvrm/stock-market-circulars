## Summary

BSE has notified members that The Wealth Company Mutual Fund will launch the NFO for The Wealth Company Mid Cap Fund, along with SIP facility, on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The NFO will run from July 15, 2026, to July 29, 2026, with a tentative allotment date of August 4, 2026, across six scheme variants (Regular/Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options).

## Key Points

- Scheme: The Wealth Company Mid Cap Fund (Equity category), offered in six variants across Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options.
- ISINs: INF2F0001628 (Regular-Growth), INF2F0001636 (Regular-IDCW Payout), INF2F0001644 (Regular-IDCW Reinvestment), INF2F0001651 (Direct-Growth), INF2F0001669 (Direct-IDCW Payout), INF2F0001677 (Direct-IDCW Reinvestment).
- Tentative allotment date for all variants: August 4, 2026.
- Transactions where clients fail to initiate funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day (including those during the extended NFO period) will be cancelled and reported to RTA/AMC with an 'N' flag.
- Transactions with funds initiated remain pending until 3:00 PM on the last fund pay-out day to AMC or T+5 day, whichever is earlier.
- Cheque and OTM payments are accepted during the NFO period, subject to clear funds reaching the ICCL account by 6:00 PM on August 4, 2026.
- Switch transactions are accepted during the NFO period, but switch-out funds received on/after the allotment date will not be considered for allotment.
- The NFO period is extended till midnight on July 29, 2026, only for lumpsum purchases on BSE StAR MF; SIP/XSIP registrations must be authenticated by clients by 3:00 PM on July 29, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is an operational notice under the Trading Operations department regarding a new fund offer facilitation on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure client funds are initiated by 9:00 AM on T+1 day to avoid transaction cancellation.
- Members must ensure clear funds are available in the ICCL account by 6:00 PM on August 4, 2026, for cheque, OTM, and lumpsum transactions to be eligible for allotment.
- SIP/XSIP registrations must be authenticated by clients on or before 3:00 PM on July 29, 2026.
- Members handling switch transactions must monitor timing of switch-out fund receipt relative to the allotment date.

## Important Dates

- NFO Open Date: July 15, 2026
- NFO Close Date: July 29, 2026 (lumpsum purchases extended till midnight; SIP/XSIP authentication deadline 3:00 PM)
- Tentative Allotment Date: August 4, 2026
- Fund pay-in deadline: 6:00 PM on August 4, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational/procedural circular relevant to BSE StAR MF trading members facilitating mutual fund transactions. It has no direct bearing on listed equity securities or broader market trading activity, and primarily requires operational compliance from members regarding fund pay-in timelines and transaction processing for the new scheme.
