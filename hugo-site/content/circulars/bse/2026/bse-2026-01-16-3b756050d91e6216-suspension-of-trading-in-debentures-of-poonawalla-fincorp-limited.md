## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in specific non-convertible debentures issued by Poonawalla Fincorp Limited effective January 22, 2026. The suspension is implemented due to the company fixing the record date for redemption of the debentures. Trading members are instructed not to deal in the specified debenture series from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspended for debenture series PWFL-8.38%-6-2-26-PVT (Scrip Code: 975372)
- ISIN: INE511C07805
- Record date for redemption: January 22, 2026
- No dealings permitted from: January 22, 2026
- Circular reference: DR-804/2025-2026
- Notice issued by: Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President â€“ Listing Compliance and Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine corporate action notice for debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all trading activities in the specified debenture series (ISIN: INE511C07805) from January 22, 2026
- Trading members are required to take note of the suspension and ensure compliance
- No dealing in the affected debentures is permitted from the specified date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 16, 2026
- **Record Date**: January 22, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Date**: January 22, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders and traders of the specific debenture series PWFL-8.38%-6-2-26-PVT. The suspension is temporary and related to the redemption process.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to block trading in this debenture series from January 22, 2026. Debenture holders will receive redemption proceeds as per the company's redemption terms.

**Investor Impact**: Investors holding these debentures will have their investments redeemed on the scheduled date. Secondary market liquidity ceases from January 22, 2026, but this is expected as part of the redemption process.
