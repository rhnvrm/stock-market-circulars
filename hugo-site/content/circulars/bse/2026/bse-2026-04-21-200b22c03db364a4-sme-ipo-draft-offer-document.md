## Summary

BSE has published a notice informing stakeholders that the Draft Offer Document (DRHP) of Home Zone Rubber Solutions Limited has been filed on April 21, 2026, and is now available on the BSE SME website. The document complies with Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and is open for public comments for a minimum period of 21 days from the date of the notice.

## Key Points

- Home Zone Rubber Solutions Limited has filed its Draft Red Herring Prospectus (DRHP) with BSE on April 21, 2026
- The DRHP contains all disclosures as required under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations
- The document is hosted on the BSE SME website (bsesme.com) for public access
- Public comments are invited within 21 days from April 21, 2026 (deadline: May 12, 2026)
- Comments must be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural notice under existing ICDR Regulations (Schedule VI) governing SME IPO draft offer document disclosures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Any interested party wishing to comment on the Draft Offer Document must submit comments to equity.ipo@bseindia.com within 21 days of the notice date
- No compliance obligations for existing listed companies or general market participants

## Important Dates

- **Filing Date:** April 21, 2026
- **Public Comment Deadline:** May 12, 2026 (21 days from April 21, 2026)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal broader market impact. It pertains solely to the IPO process of Home Zone Rubber Solutions Limited on the SME platform. Investors and analysts interested in participating in or commenting on this SME IPO should review the DRHP on bsesme.com and submit feedback before the deadline. No impact on existing listed securities or trading operations.
