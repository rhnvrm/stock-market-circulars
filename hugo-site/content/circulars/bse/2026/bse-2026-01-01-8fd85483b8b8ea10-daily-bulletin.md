## Summary

BSE Daily Bulletin No. 188/2025-2026 dated January 1, 2026, announces book closure and record dates for various corporate actions. The bulletin primarily covers privately placed non-convertible debentures (NCDs) with scheduled interest payments and redemptions (full or partial) between January and March 2026.

## Key Points

- Bulletin covers record dates for NCD redemptions and interest payments
- Multiple companies listed with actions scheduled between January 5, 2026 and March 30, 2026
- All securities mentioned are privately placed non-convertible debentures
- Trading flag set to '1' for all listed securities
- Actions include full redemptions, partial redemptions, and interest payments

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this bulletin.

## Compliance Requirements

Investors holding the specified NCDs must verify their holdings as of the respective record dates to be eligible for:
- Interest payments
- Full or partial redemption proceeds
- Combined interest and redemption payments

This is a routine informational bulletin with minimal market impact. It serves as a reference for debt security holders to track upcoming corporate actions on their investments. The bulletin provides transparency on scheduled NCD redemptions and interest payments, allowing investors to plan for cash flows from their debt investments.
