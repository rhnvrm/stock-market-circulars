## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 89,89,344 equity shares of Trejhara Solutions Limited (Scrip Code: 542233) effective January 30, 2026. These shares were issued pursuant to a scheme of amalgamation between LP Logistics Plus Chemical SCM Private Limited (Transferor Company) and Trejhara Solutions Limited (Transferee Company) under sections 230-232 of the Companies Act, 2013. The newly listed shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 89,89,344 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each approved for listing
- Shares issued under amalgamation scheme between LP Logistics Plus Chemical SCM Private Limited and Trejhara Solutions Limited
- Scrip Code: 542233
- ISIN Number: INE00CA01015
- Distinctive Numbers: 14516299 to 23505642
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Trading permitted from January 30, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification following completion of an amalgamation scheme approved under the Companies Act, 2013.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance actions required for market participants. This is an informational circular regarding the listing of new securities resulting from a corporate restructuring.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: November 11, 2025
- **Listing and Trading Commencement**: January 30, 2026 (Friday)
- **Circular Date**: January 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The listing increases the paid-up capital of Trejhara Solutions Limited by approximately 89.89 lakh shares as a result of the merger with LP Logistics Plus Chemical SCM Private Limited. The shares are ranking pari-passu with existing shares, ensuring equal treatment for all shareholders. This is a routine post-merger listing with no immediate operational or trading implications for market participants.
