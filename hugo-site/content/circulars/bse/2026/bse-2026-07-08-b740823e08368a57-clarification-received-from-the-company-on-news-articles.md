## Summary

BSE Ltd, as part of its routine surveillance process, sought clarification from listed companies regarding news articles appearing in newspapers, websites, and television channels concerning them. This notice reports the status of clarification requests sent to two companies: Ather Energy Ltd, which has submitted its reply, and Rajesh Exports Ltd, whose reply is still awaited.

## Key Points

- BSE verifies news articles about listed companies to inform investors and members about the accuracy of media reports.
- Ather Energy Ltd (Scrip Code: 544397) has replied to BSE's clarification request regarding a news article published on moneycontrol.com. The reply is attached to the notice.
- Rajesh Exports Ltd (Scrip Code: 531500) has not yet responded to BSE's clarification request regarding a news article published on thehindubusinessline.com.
- The notice was issued by BSE's Department of Surveillance (DOSS), signed by Sanjay Jain (Deputy Vice President, Surveillance) and Shreya Ghose (Manager, Surveillance).

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine surveillance disclosure notice, not a regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

- Rajesh Exports Ltd is expected to submit its clarification reply to BSE regarding the referenced news article.
- Investors and market participants should refer to the attached reply from Ather Energy Ltd for verified information regarding the news coverage.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 08 July 2026
- No specific deadline mentioned for Rajesh Exports Ltd's pending reply.

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact, procedural surveillance notice. It does not indicate any confirmed adverse development for either company but flags that media reports about them are under exchange scrutiny. Investors in Ather Energy Ltd and Rajesh Exports Ltd should monitor for the underlying news articles and any follow-up company disclosures, as unconfirmed media reports can occasionally precede material announcements or price volatility.
