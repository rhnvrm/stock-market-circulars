## Summary

BSE has issued an update regarding the debt securities of IndiGrid Infrastructure Trust, in continuation of earlier notices dated 01 Mar 2023, 22 Feb 2024, and 26 Jun 2024. Trading Members are informed that four specific debt securities (identified by Scrip Code and ISIN) will become available for trading with revised terms as per Annexure I, on account of debt restructuring, effective Friday, July 03, 2026.

## Key Points

- This is a continuation notice referencing three prior Exchange notices: No. 20230301-38 (dated 01 Mar 2023), No. 20240222-3 (dated 22 Feb 2024), and No. 20240626-33 (dated 26 Jun 2024)
- Four debt securities of IndiGrid Infrastructure Trust are affected:
  - Scrip Code 974630, ISIN INE219X07363
  - Scrip Code 975399, ISIN INE219X07439
  - Scrip Code 975400, ISIN INE219X07447
  - Scrip Code 975765, ISIN INE219X07454
- These securities will be available for trading with revised terms as per Annexure I due to debt restructuring
- Trading Members can contact Ms. Pooja Trivedi (+91 22 2272 8844, debt.listing@bseindia.com) for further details

## Regulatory Changes

The revised terms applicable to the listed debt securities are detailed in Annexure I (attached to the original notice), reflecting changes arising from a debt restructuring exercise undertaken by IndiGrid Infrastructure Trust.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members dealing in these ISINs must update their systems and trading practices to reflect the revised terms effective from the stated date. Members should refer to Annexure I for the specific revised terms and contact the Exchange's Debt Listing team for clarifications.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 02 Jul 2026
- Effective Date for revised terms: Friday, July 03, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and traders of the four specified IndiGrid Infrastructure Trust debt securities (ISINs INE219X07363, INE219X07439, INE219X07447, INE219X07454). Market participants trading these instruments need to be aware of the revised terms taking effect from July 3, 2026, arising from the underlying debt restructuring. Broader equity/debt market impact is minimal given the narrow scope of affected instruments.
