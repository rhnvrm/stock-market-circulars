## Summary

BSE has notified trading members and custodians of the opening of an Offer to Buy (Buyback) for Onward Technologies Limited via the tender offer route through the stock exchange mechanism. The company proposes to buy back up to 5,48,780 fully paid-up equity shares at Rs.328 per share, for a total consideration not exceeding Rs.18 crores, from eligible shareholders as of the record date of May 18, 2026.

## Key Points

- Buyback of up to 5,48,780 equity shares (face value Rs.10 each), representing 2.41% of total outstanding equity shares as on March 31, 2026
- Buyback price: Rs.328 per equity share, payable in cash
- Maximum aggregate consideration: Rs.18 crores (excluding transaction costs)
- Offer conducted via tender offer route through BSE stock exchange mechanism
- Buyback offer size represents 8.53% (standalone) and 7.49% (consolidated) of paid-up equity share capital and free reserves as on March 31, 2026
- Buyback is within the statutory limit of 10% under Section 68(2)(c) of the Companies Act, approved through the board approval route
- Eligible shareholders are those holding shares as on the record date: Monday, May 18, 2026
- Letter of Offer attached to the notice for reference

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The buyback is conducted in accordance with existing SEBI mechanisms:
- SEBI circular no. CIR/CFD/POLICYCELL/1/2015 dated April 13, 2015
- SEBI circular no. CFD/DCR2/CIR/P/2016/131 dated December 09, 2016
- BSE notices: 20170210-16, 20190424-35, 20200528-32, 20201102-43, 20210825-62

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and custodians must follow the revised guidelines for acquisition of shares through the stock exchange pursuant to tender offers under Buyback Regulations
- Market participants should refer to the Letter of Offer attached and available on the BSE website (www.bseindia.com) for full offer details
- Participants must adhere to the tender offer procedures as per SEBI circular CFD/DCR2/CIR/P/2016/131 and BSE notice 20170210-16

## Important Dates

- Record Date: Monday, May 18, 2026 (eligibility date for shareholders)
- Buyback Offer Opens: Friday, May 22, 2026
- Buyback Offer Closes: Friday, May 29, 2026
- Note: May 28, 2026 is excluded as it is a SEBI Holiday

## Impact Assessment

This buyback is company-specific and directly impacts existing shareholders of Onward Technologies Limited who held shares as of May 18, 2026. The buyback represents 2.41% of outstanding shares at a price of Rs.328 per share, offering shareholders a potential exit or partial liquidity opportunity. The offer size of Rs.18 crores is moderate and within statutory limits under board approval route, indicating no extraordinary capital restructuring. Broader market impact is limited; however, trading members and custodians handling client accounts with ONWARDTEC positions should ensure timely communication and participation facilitation for eligible clients during the offer window (May 22–29, 2026, excluding May 28).
