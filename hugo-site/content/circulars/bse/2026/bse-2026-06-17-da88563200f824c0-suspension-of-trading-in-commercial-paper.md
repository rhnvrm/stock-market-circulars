## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members that the commercial paper of SBICAP Securities Limited (Scrip Code: 731670, ISIN: INE212K14DZ6) will mature on its redemption date. Accordingly, trading in this instrument is suspended with effect from June 18, 2026.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper SBICAP-19-06-26-CP (ISIN: INE212K14DZ6) issued by SBICAP Securities Limited is maturing.
- Scrip Code: 731670 on the BSE Debt segment.
- Trading members are directed not to deal in this commercial paper from June 18, 2026.
- Notice reference: DR-654/2026-2027.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice issued upon maturity of a debt instrument.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must cease all dealings in the commercial paper of SBICAP Securities Limited (ISIN: INE212K14DZ6) with effect from June 18, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **June 17, 2026**: Notice issued.
- **June 18, 2026**: Suspension of trading effective; commercial paper matures on the redemption date.

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited and routine. This notice affects only holders or prospective traders of SBICAP Securities Limited's specific commercial paper tranche maturing on June 19, 2026. No broader market or systemic impact is anticipated.
