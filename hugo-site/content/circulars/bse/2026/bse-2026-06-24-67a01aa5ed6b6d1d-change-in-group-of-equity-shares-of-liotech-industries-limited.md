## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the equity shares of LIOTECH INDUSTRIES LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544796) will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment under M Group, effective Thursday, July 9, 2026. This is a continuation of the Exchange's earlier Notice No. 20260623-33 dated June 23, 2026.

## Key Points

- LIOTECH INDUSTRIES LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544796) is being moved from Trade for Trade (MT Group) to Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective date of transfer: Thursday, July 09, 2026
- This notice continues from BSE Notice No. 20260623-33 dated June 23, 2026
- The company is listed on the SME segment
- Contact: Mr. Anurag Jain, Tel. No. 022-2272 8822 for further details

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares of LIOTECH INDUSTRIES LIMITED will shift from Trade for Trade settlement (MT Group) to normal Rolling settlement (M Group). In Trade for Trade, each trade results in mandatory delivery with no netting; under Rolling settlement, normal netting and settlement cycles apply.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members are informed of the upcoming segment change and should update their systems and inform clients accordingly ahead of July 9, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **June 23, 2026**: Original notice issued (Notice No. 20260623-33)
- **June 24, 2026**: This continuation notice issued
- **July 09, 2026**: Effective date of transfer from MT Group (Trade for Trade) to M Group (Rolling segment)

## Impact Assessment

The shift from Trade for Trade to Rolling segment generally eases trading restrictions on the stock. Under Trade for Trade, investors must deliver shares and funds for each trade without netting, which is more restrictive. Moving to Rolling segment under M Group allows normal T+1 rolling settlement with netting, potentially improving liquidity and trading activity in LIOTECH INDUSTRIES LIMITED shares. This affects traders and investors holding or planning to trade this SME-listed stock.
