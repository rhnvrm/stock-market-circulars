## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 7,50,000 new equity shares of Containe Technologies Limited (Scrip Code: 543606) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Friday, May 29, 2026. The shares were issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to the conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 7,50,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- face value each, issued at a premium of Rs. 76/- per share (total issue price Rs. 86/-)
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis via conversion of warrants
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- ISIN: INE0M8901010
- Distribution numbers: 6244001 to 6994000

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice informing trading members of the addition of new securities to the tradable universe on BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the listing and enable trading in these securities from the effective date. No specific compliance action is required from members beyond standard procedures.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** April 9, 2026
- **Effective Date of Trading:** May 29, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry:** November 30, 2026 (all 7,50,000 shares, dist. nos. 6244001–6994000)

## Impact Assessment

This listing introduces 7,50,000 new equity shares of Containe Technologies Limited to BSE. The shares are subject to a lock-in period until November 30, 2026, meaning they cannot be traded until that date. Impact on the broader market is negligible; effect is limited to existing shareholders and traders of this scrip. The preferential allotment via warrant conversion increases the company's total paid-up equity capital.
