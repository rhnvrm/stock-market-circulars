## Summary

BSE's Surveillance Department (DOSS) issued Notice No. 20260507-19 dated May 7, 2026, informing members and investors that a clarification has been received from KEI Industries Ltd (Scrip Code: 517569) in response to BSE's query about news articles circulating in various newspapers, websites, and television channels.

## Key Points

- BSE Surveillance sought clarification from KEI Industries Ltd (Scrip Code: 517569) regarding news articles published on NDTV Profit (https://www.ndtvprofit.com)
- The clarification has been received from the company
- The notice is issued to inform members and investors about the accuracy of news articles concerning the company
- Issued by the Surveillance Department (DOSS) of BSE Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this notice.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements are imposed on market participants. This is an informational notice. Investors and members are advised to refer to the company's clarification for accurate information regarding the news articles in question.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: May 7, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine surveillance disclosure with minimal market impact. The clarification from KEI Industries Ltd is intended to ensure transparency and accuracy of information available to investors. No trading restrictions or penalties are involved. Investors tracking KEI Industries Ltd should review the company's official clarification for details on the underlying news articles.
