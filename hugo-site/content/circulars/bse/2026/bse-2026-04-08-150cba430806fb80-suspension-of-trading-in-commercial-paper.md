## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260408-7 directs trading members to refrain from dealing in the commercial papers of nine companies that have fixed record dates for redemption. The suspensions are effective on or before each company's respective record date, spanning April 9 to April 29, 2026.

## Key Points

- Trading members are advised not to deal in commercial papers of the listed companies from their respective ex-dates onward.
- Nine companies are covered across various record dates in April 2026.
- All affected instruments are in the Debt segment under the Listing Operations department.
- Settlement references are under the 607/2026-2027 to 620/2026-2027 series.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural notice under existing BSE rules governing suspension of trading in debt instruments approaching redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any buy or sell trades in the listed commercial papers on or after the respective ex-dates.
- Members should update their systems to block trading in the specified ISINs from the indicated ex-dates.

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited to debt segment participants holding or trading these specific commercial paper instruments. As these are short-term money market instruments approaching maturity, the suspension is a routine precautionary measure to prevent trading after the redemption record date is fixed. Infina Finance Private Limited is notably affected across three separate CP issuances (scrip codes 730929, 730953, 730644) with staggered redemption dates.
