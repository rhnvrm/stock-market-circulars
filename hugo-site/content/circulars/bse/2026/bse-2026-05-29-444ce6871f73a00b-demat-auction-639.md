## Summary

BSE Limited, through the Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd. (ICCL), has announced Demat Auction 639 for Rolling Settlement No. DR-639/2026-27. Trading members are invited to submit auction offers for securities on May 29, 2026, with pay-in scheduled for June 1, 2026.

## Key Points

- Auction offer entry window: 2:00 PM to 2:45 PM on May 29, 2026
- Auction matching: 2:45 PM to 3:00 PM
- Delivery statement and accepted auction offer reports: 3:00 PM to 3:30 PM
- Money statement and final reports: 3:35 PM to 5:30 PM
- Offers are accepted on trading terminals
- Pay-in of auction securities will be held on Monday, June 1, 2026 at 8:00 AM
- Under the Uniform Membership structure in Equity Cash Segment, settlement is done through the Clearing Member of a Trading Member
- The restriction on trading members offering securities in auction for settlements where they have short-delivered has been removed

## Regulatory Changes

Following implementation of the Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment, settlement is now routed through the Clearing Member of a Trading Member. The prior restriction preventing trading members from offering securities in auction for settlements where they had short-delivered those securities has been lifted.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and custodians must check their auction pay-in obligations for securities.
- Securities pay-in must be executed in depositories within depository cut-off timings.
- Trading members must take utmost care when entering securities codes and rates; no corrections will be permitted after punching errors.
- Members should refer to the cutoff rate on the Auction Offer Entry Screen before submitting offers.
- Members should refer to notices on Corporate Actions (Ex-Rights, Ex-Bonus, Ex-Split, etc.) issued by the respective departments.

## Important Dates

- **May 29, 2026**: Auction offer entry (2:00 PM–2:45 PM), matching (2:45 PM–3:00 PM), delivery statement (3:00 PM–3:30 PM), money statement (3:35 PM–5:30 PM)
- **June 1, 2026**: Auction securities pay-in at 8:00 AM

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice affecting trading members and custodians with obligations under Rolling Settlement DR-639/2026-27. The removal of the restriction on short-delivering members participating in the auction may slightly broaden the pool of auction participants. No broad market impact is expected; impact is limited to members with specific short-delivery positions in this settlement cycle.
