## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 11,797,736 new equity shares of ONIX SOLAR ENERGY LIMITED (Scrip Code: 513119, ISIN: INE173M01012) issued on a rights basis are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Friday, June 5, 2026. The shares were allotted on June 2, 2026, at Rs. 10/- face value with a premium of Rs. 41/- (total issue price Rs. 51/-).

## Key Points

- 11,797,736 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 41/- per share on a rights basis
- ISIN: INE173M01012; Distribution Numbers: 25070191 to 36867926
- Date of Allotment: June 2, 2026
- Trading permitted with effect from June 5, 2026
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational notice admitting rights-issue shares to trading pursuant to allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should update their systems to reflect the new security details (ISIN INE173M01012, distribution numbers 25070191–36867926) and ensure the shares are available for trading from June 5, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** June 2, 2026
- **Effective date for trading:** June 5, 2026 (Friday)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine post-rights-issue listing notice with low market impact. The newly listed shares (approximately 11.8 million) will increase the tradable float of ONIX SOLAR ENERGY LIMITED and are fungible with existing shares. No disruption to normal trading is expected.
