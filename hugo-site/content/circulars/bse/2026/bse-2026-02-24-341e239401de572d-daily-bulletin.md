## Summary

BSE Daily Bulletin No. 225/2025-2026 dated February 24, 2026 lists book closure and record dates for various securities across multiple companies. The bulletin covers corporate actions including commercial paper redemptions, NCD interest payments and partial redemptions, an equity rights issue, and an equity call payment, with record dates ranging from February 27, 2026 to June 16, 2026.

## Key Points

- Bulletin covers record dates (RD) for multiple debt and equity securities
- One corporate action is marked as **Cancelled**: ECap Equities Limited (975500) NCD interest payment scheduled for 12/03/2026
- Kosamattam Finance Limited has the highest number of entries across multiple NCD series (scrip codes 936620, 936776, 936991, 937205) with staggered interest payment dates in April, May, and June 2026
- ANNVRRIDHHI VENTURES LIMITED (890229) has the nearest upcoming record date at 27/02/2026 for a First Call of Rs. 2.50 per equity share
- HealthCare Global Enterprises Limited (539787) has a Rights Issue record date set for 02/03/2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine informational bulletin.

- Investors holding securities of listed companies should note the record dates to be eligible for corporate benefits

- Shareholders of ANNVRRIDHHI VENTURES LIMITED must ensure shares are held by 27/02/2026 to be eligible for the First Call payment
