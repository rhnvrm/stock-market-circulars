## Summary

BSE has published an updated list of 31 securities that are currently under the Add-on Price Band Framework. This framework applies enhanced surveillance measures to certain securities, typically restricting price movements and trading activity. The circular provides the complete list with scrip codes, ISIN numbers, and company names for market participants' reference.

## Key Points

- 31 securities are currently under the Add-on Price Band Framework
- List includes scrip code, ISIN number, and company name for each security
- Framework applies enhanced surveillance and trading restrictions
- Securities span various sectors including infrastructure, technology, manufacturing, and finance
- Notable inclusions: TAPARIA TOOLS LTD., AUTORIDERS INTERNATIONAL LTD., AXENTRA CORP LIMITED

## Regulatory Changes

This is an updated list reflecting the current status of securities under the existing Add-on Price Band Framework. No new regulatory changes are announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must be aware of the enhanced surveillance measures applicable to these securities
- Trading in these securities is subject to add-on price band restrictions
- Investors and brokers should exercise appropriate caution when dealing in these securities

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: February 01, 2026
- Effective Date: Immediate (as of circular publication)

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to the 31 securities listed in the framework. Trading volumes and liquidity in these stocks may be constrained due to price band restrictions. Market participants dealing in these specific securities need to account for the enhanced surveillance measures. The broader market remains unaffected. This is a periodic update for informational purposes to ensure market transparency.
