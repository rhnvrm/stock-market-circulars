## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260423-11 informing trading members that the debt securities of Logix Buildtech Pvt Ltd under Scrip Code 960193 will be available for trading with revised terms effective Friday, April 24, 2026. The revision involves a change in ISIN, scrip ID, scrip name, and maturity date.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code 960193 will trade under revised terms from April 24, 2026
- The ISIN changes from INE806U07017 to INE806U07033
- Scrip ID changes from 4LBPL23 to 12LBPL26
- Scrip Long Name changes from LBPL-4%-4-11-23-PVT to LBPL-12%-31-10-26-PVT
- Maturity date revised from November 4, 2023 to October 31, 2026
- The coupon rate changes from 4% to 12%

## Regulatory Changes

The debt security under Scrip Code 960193 is being updated to reflect new instrument terms. The old ISIN (INE806U07017) is replaced by a new ISIN (INE806U07033), indicating this is effectively a new debt instrument replacing the matured one.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must take note of the revised scrip details before trading on or after April 24, 2026
- Members should update their systems to reflect the new ISIN (INE806U07033) and scrip ID (12LBPL26)
- For queries, members may contact Ms. Bhairavi Bondale at +91 22 2272 8844 or debt.listing@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **April 23, 2026**: Notice issued
- **April 24, 2026**: Revised terms effective for trading
- **October 31, 2026**: New maturity date of the revised debt security

## Impact Assessment

This update is limited in scope, affecting only traders and holders of Logix Buildtech Pvt Ltd debt securities. The change from a matured 4% instrument to a new 12% instrument with a 2026 maturity suggests a restructuring or rollover of the company's debt. Trading members dealing in BSE debt segment should update their systems accordingly to avoid settlement issues from April 24, 2026 onward.
