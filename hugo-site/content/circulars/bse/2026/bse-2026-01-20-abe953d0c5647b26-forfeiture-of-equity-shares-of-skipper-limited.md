## Summary

SKIPPER LIMITED (Scrip Code: 538562) has forfeited 33,198 equity shares of Re. 1/- each that were partly paid-up, due to non-payment of allotment/call monies by shareholders. The forfeited shares have distinctive numbers ranging from 112904036 to 112937233.

## Key Points

- Company: Skipper Limited (Scrip Code: 538562)
- Number of shares forfeited: 33,198 equity shares
- Face value: Re. 1/- each
- Reason: Non-payment of allotment/call monies
- Share status: Partly paid-up shares
- Distinctive numbers: 112904036 to 112937233

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

This is an informational notice to trading members. No specific compliance actions required from market participants.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: January 20, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action with minimal market impact. The forfeiture affects a relatively small number of partly paid-up shares (33,198 shares) due to shareholders' failure to pay the remaining amount due. This reduces the total outstanding share count of Skipper Limited marginally and has no significant operational or trading implications for the broader market or other shareholders.
