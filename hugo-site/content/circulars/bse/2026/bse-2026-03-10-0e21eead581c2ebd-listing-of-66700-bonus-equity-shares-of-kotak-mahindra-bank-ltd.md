## Summary

BSE has listed 66,700 bonus equity shares of Kotak Mahindra Bank Ltd (Scrip Code: 500247, ISIN: INE237A01036) for trading effective Wednesday, March 11, 2026. These shares were originally allotted on January 29, 2026 but were kept in abeyance and are now permitted to trade on the Exchange.

## Key Points

- 66,700 bonus equity shares of Re. 1/- each are now listed on BSE
- Shares were allotted on January 29, 2026 but had been kept in abeyance
- Trading permitted from Wednesday, March 11, 2026
- Scrip Code: 500247
- ISIN: INE237A01036
- Distribution Numbers: 9946390306 to 9946457005

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice for bonus shares previously held in abeyance.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed of the listing and may trade these securities from the effective date. No specific compliance action required beyond awareness of the new tradable securities.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** January 29, 2026
- **Trading Effective Date:** Wednesday, March 11, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The 66,700 shares represent a very small addition to Kotak Mahindra Bank's existing share capital. These were bonus shares previously kept in abeyance, meaning they were already accounted for in the bonus issue announcement. Their release for trading is a routine administrative action with negligible effect on the stock's liquidity or price.
