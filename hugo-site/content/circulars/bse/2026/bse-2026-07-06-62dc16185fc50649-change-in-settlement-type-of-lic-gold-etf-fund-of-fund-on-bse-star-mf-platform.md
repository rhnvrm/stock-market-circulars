## Summary

BSE has informed its Trading Members that, based on communication received from LIC Mutual Fund, the settlement type for the LIC Gold ETF Fund of Fund scheme on the BSE StAR MF platform has been revised from T+3 to T+2, with immediate effect from the notice date of 06 July 2026.

## Key Points

- The notice pertains to the Trading and Mutual Fund segment of the BSE StAR MF platform.
- Scheme affected: LIC Gold ETF Fund of Fund.
- Settlement type changed from T+3 (current) to T+2 (revised).
- The change is effective immediately upon issuance of the notice.
- No attachments were included with this notice.
- Signed off by Arunkumar Ganesan (VP - Mutual Fund) and Mahesh Tare (AVP - Mutual Fund).

## Regulatory Changes

The settlement cycle for the LIC Gold ETF Fund of Fund scheme has been shortened by one day, moving from a T+3 settlement to a T+2 settlement on the BSE StAR MF platform, as communicated by LIC Mutual Fund.

## Compliance Requirements

Members and participants transacting in the LIC Gold ETF Fund of Fund scheme via the BSE StAR MF platform must update their internal systems and processes to reflect the revised T+2 settlement cycle and ensure timely settlement of transactions accordingly.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 06 July 2026
- Effective Date: Immediate (06 July 2026)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational update affecting only the LIC Gold ETF Fund of Fund scheme on the BSE StAR MF platform. The impact is limited to distributors and members dealing in this specific scheme, who need to align their settlement processes with the shortened T+2 cycle. There is no broader impact on the equity market or other listed securities.
