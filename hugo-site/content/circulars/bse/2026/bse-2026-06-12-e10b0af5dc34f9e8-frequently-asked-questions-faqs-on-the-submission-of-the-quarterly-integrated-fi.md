## Summary

BSE has issued a set of Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) to assist all listed entities in understanding and complying with the submission requirements of the Quarterly Integrated Filing – Governance. This filing was formerly known as the Corporate Governance Report. The FAQs are provided for reference and guidance and are enclosed as Annexure 1 to the notice.

## Key Points

- BSE has released FAQs on the Quarterly Integrated Filing – Governance (formerly Corporate Governance Report).
- The FAQs are intended to clarify submission requirements and provide guidance to listed entities.
- All listed entities are directed to take note of the FAQs and comply accordingly.
- The circular is issued by the Listing Compliance and Operations department.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. The circular provides clarificatory FAQs on an existing filing requirement — the Quarterly Integrated Filing – Governance — which replaced the erstwhile Corporate Governance Report.

## Compliance Requirements

- All listed entities must review the annexed FAQs (Annexure 1) on the Quarterly Integrated Filing – Governance.
- Entities must ensure submissions are made in accordance with the guidance provided in the FAQs.
- Compliance is mandatory for all listed companies under the Equity segment.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 12 June 2026
- No specific deadline is mentioned beyond the existing quarterly filing schedule.

## Impact Assessment

This is a guidance circular with medium operational impact. It does not introduce new obligations but clarifies existing requirements for the Quarterly Integrated Filing – Governance. Listed companies that were uncertain about submission procedures will benefit from the FAQs, reducing the risk of non-compliance due to procedural ambiguity. No direct market or trading impact is expected.
