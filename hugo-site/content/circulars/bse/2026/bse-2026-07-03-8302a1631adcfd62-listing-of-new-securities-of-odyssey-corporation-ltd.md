## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 78,50,000 new equity shares of Odyssey Corporation Ltd. (Scrip Code: 531996) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Monday, July 6, 2026. These shares were issued to promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- Company: Odyssey Corporation Ltd. (Scrip Code - 531996)
- 78,50,000 equity shares of Rs. 5/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 9/- per share to Promoters on a preferential basis, pursuant to conversion of warrants
- Issue Price: Rs. 14/- per share
- ISIN: INE839E01023
- Distinctive Numbers: 81648487 to 89498486
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Date of Allotment: 02/05/2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is a standard listing notice approving trading of newly allotted preferential shares following warrant conversion.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must recognize the new equity shares (Distinctive Nos. 81648487 to 89498486) as eligible for trading from the effective date. The allotted shares are subject to a lock-in period as specified below and cannot be traded until the lock-in expires.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: May 2, 2026
- Effective Date of Listing/Trading: Monday, July 6, 2026
- Lock-in Expiry: January 31, 2028 (applicable to all 78,50,000 shares, Dist. Nos. 81648487 to 89498486)

## Impact Assessment

The listing increases Odyssey Corporation Ltd.'s total equity share capital through a preferential allotment to promoters, which may modestly dilute existing non-promoter shareholding but also signals increased promoter commitment via warrant conversion. As the new shares are locked-in until January 31, 2028, there is no immediate free-float or trading supply impact. Market impact is limited to shareholders tracking capital structure and promoter holding changes.
