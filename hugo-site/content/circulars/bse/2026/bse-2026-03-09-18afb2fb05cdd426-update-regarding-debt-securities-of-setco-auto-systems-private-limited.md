## Summary

BSE has issued a continuation notice (Notice No. 20260309-25) reaffirming the suspension of two debt securities of Setco Auto Systems Private Limited. This follows the original suspension notice dated January 30, 2026 (Notice No. 20260130-21). The securities will remain suspended until further notice.

## Key Points

- Continuation of suspension originally announced on January 30, 2026
- Two scrip codes are affected: 973453 (ISIN: INE0I0R07110) and 975859 (ISIN: INE0I0R07102)
- Trading Members are instructed that both scrip codes will remain suspended until further notice
- Contact details provided for further queries: Ms. Pooja Trivedi and Hardik Bhuta (Assistant Vice President) at debt.listing@bseindia.com or +91 22 2272 8844

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice reaffirms the existing suspension status of the two debt securities under the debt segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must refrain from trading in scrip codes 973453 and 975859 until further notice from BSE
- Members should monitor BSE notices for updates on the suspension status of these securities

## Important Dates

- **January 30, 2026**: Original suspension notice (20260130-21) issued
- **March 9, 2026**: Continuation notice issued reaffirming suspension
- **Until further notice**: Suspension remains in effect indefinitely

## Impact Assessment

The continued suspension of these two debt instruments restricts trading activity for holders of Setco Auto Systems Private Limited's debt securities. The impact is limited to investors and trading members dealing specifically in these privately placed debt securities. No timeline for lifting the suspension has been provided, creating uncertainty for existing holders. Members requiring clarification should contact BSE's debt listing team directly.
