## Summary

BSE has issued the settlement programme for the Futures & Options (F&O) segment covering the period from 01 April 2026 to 30 April 2026. The circular, issued by Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd. (ICCL), provides the trade date-to-daily settlement date mapping for all trading days in April 2026.

## Key Points

- Settlement programme covers the F&O segment for the entire month of April 2026 (01.04.2026 to 30.04.2026).
- Daily settlement dates are typically T+1 business days after the corresponding trade date.
- The last trade date of April (30.04.2026) settles on 04.05.2026, reflecting the settlement lag into May 2026.
- Trading holiday gaps are reflected in the schedule (e.g., 03–05 April and 14 April are not listed as trade dates, indicating exchange holidays).
- Issued by Tajindersingh Virdi, Assistant Vice President, for Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd. (ICCL).

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice providing the scheduled settlement calendar for the F&O segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and clearing members participating in the F&O segment must adhere to the published settlement dates for daily mark-to-market and final settlements.
- Participants should ensure funds and securities are available by the respective daily settlement dates.
