## Summary

ICCL (Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd.) has issued a revised settlement calendar for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) pertaining to Glittek Granites Limited. This revision updates the obligation download and settlement dates for Settlement No. 2026053, originally referenced in Notice No. 20260518-11.

## Key Points

- Settlement Type: Takeover (Settlement No. 2026053)
- The bid window ran from 19/05/2026 to 02/06/2026
- Revised Obligation Download Date: 08/06/2026
- Revised Settlement Date: 09/06/2026
- Demat shares must be delivered exclusively via the Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism available through both depositories
- Members/Custodians must use Market Type "(Takeover)" and Settlement No. 2026053 in the depository system

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework introduced. This notice revises the settlement schedule previously announced under Notice No. 20260518-11, in line with the Revised Guidelines of Mechanism for Acquisition of Shares through Stock Exchange (originally issued via Notice No. 20200528-32 dated May 28, 2020).

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and Custodians must deliver demat shares only through the Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism
- Correct Market Type "(Takeover)" and Settlement No. 2026053 must be specified in all depository system entries
- Members should refer to the MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for client-wise Stamp Duty charges

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Bid Start Date | 19/05/2026 |
| Bid End Date | 02/06/2026 |
| Revised Obligation Download | 08/06/2026 |
| Revised Settlement Date | 09/06/2026 |

## Impact Assessment

This revision has a moderate operational impact limited to members and custodians directly participating in the Glittek Granites takeover offer. The updated settlement date (09/06/2026) and obligation download date (08/06/2026) require timely action by involved parties to ensure compliance. No broader market or systemic impact is expected. Members are advised to contact the ICCL Help Desk (022-69158590 / 45720490 / 45720690 or iccl.helpdesk@icclindia.com) for clarifications.
