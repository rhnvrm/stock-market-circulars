## Summary

BSE Ltd. has informed market participants that 14,65,048 new equity shares of Triveni Engineering & Industries Limited (Scrip Code: 532356), issued pursuant to a Composite Scheme of Arrangement, are listed and permitted for trading on the Exchange with effect from Friday, July 24, 2026.

## Key Points

- The Composite Scheme of Arrangement involves Triveni Engineering & Industries Limited (Amalgamated Company / Demerged Company), Sir Shadilal Enterprises Limited (Amalgamating Company), and Triveni Power Transmission Limited (Resulting Company), along with their respective shareholders and creditors.
- 14,65,048 new equity shares of Re. 1/- each are being listed.
- The new shares rank pari-passu with the existing equity shares of the Company.
- ISIN Number: INE256C01024.
- Distinctive Numbers: 257945111 – 259410158.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is an implementation notice for the listing of shares arising from a previously sanctioned Composite Scheme of Arrangement (amalgamation and demerger).

## Compliance Requirements

No specific action required from market participants; the Exchange has completed its listing approval process for the new shares.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: June 09, 2026
- Listing and Trading Effective Date: July 24, 2026 (Friday)
- Notice Date: July 23, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing increases the total tradable equity share count of Triveni Engineering & Industries Limited, reflecting completion of the amalgamation of Sir Shadilal Enterprises Limited into the Company and the associated demerger into Triveni Power Transmission Limited. As the new shares rank pari-passu with existing shares, the impact on existing shareholders is limited to a marginal increase in outstanding share count; the event is primarily procedural in nature for the market at large.
