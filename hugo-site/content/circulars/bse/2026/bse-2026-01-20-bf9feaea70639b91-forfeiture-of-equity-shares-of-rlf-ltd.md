## Summary

BSE has issued a circular notifying the forfeiture of equity shares of RLF Ltd. The circular lists multiple shareholders whose shares have been forfeited, with specific folio number ranges (STDN to ENDN) and corresponding share quantities.

## Key Points

- Share forfeiture action initiated for RLF Ltd.
- Multiple shareholders affected across various folio numbers
- Each affected shareholder has 100 shares forfeited across multiple folio ranges
- Folio numbers range from 1081901 to 1429200 series
- Total affected shareholders include: Jitendra R Touganj, Neelam C Jain, Rakesh M Mittal, Sita Devi, Anil Bhushan Jain, Dharam Pal Jerath, Anita Agrawal, Archna Duggal, D S Negi, Sonu Khanna, Manreet Kaur, Manpuneet Singh, Jagjit Kaur, Man Mohan Singh, D S Gulati, T S Gulati, Subhash Dua, Mahaveer Chand, Sonia Dutta, Suresh Jain, Goutham Chowdhary, R Veena Ranka, R Ramesh Kumar, Uttam Jain, Usha Rani Bajaj, Bhushan Kumar Kukreja, Lalit Kumar Rastogi, Raj Kumari, Ved Parkash, Praveen Kumar Sekhri, Naresh Kumar Sekhri, Madhu Bala Sekhri, Raj Rani Sekhri, Mohinder Kumar Sekhri, Mahinder Singh, and Rajesh Kumar Gupta

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a corporate action notice regarding share forfeiture.

## Compliance Requirements

- Affected shareholders should verify their shareholding status
- RLF Ltd. must update its share register reflecting the forfeited shares
- Depositories and registrars should update records accordingly

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: January 20, 2026
- Effective Date: January 20, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The forfeiture affects specific shareholders of RLF Ltd. and will result in a reduction of the company's issued share capital. This is typically due to non-payment of calls or other corporate action reasons. The impact is limited to the affected shareholders and does not have broader market implications. The company's capital structure will be adjusted accordingly.
