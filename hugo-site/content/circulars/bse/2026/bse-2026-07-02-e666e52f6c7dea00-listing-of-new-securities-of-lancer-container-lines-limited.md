## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 10,28,69,409 new equity shares of Lancer Container Lines Limited (Scrip Code: 539841) issued on a preferential basis to non-promoters are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Friday, July 3, 2026.

## Key Points

- Lancer Container Lines Limited (Scrip Code 539841) has new equity shares listed for trading from July 3, 2026.
- 10,28,69,409 equity shares of face value Rs. 5/- each were issued at a premium of Rs. 14.77/- each to non-promoters on a preferential basis.
- Issue price was Rs. 19.77/- per share.
- ISIN for the securities: INE359U01028.
- The new shares rank pari-passu with the existing equity shares of the company.
- Distinctive numbers range from 250394310 to 353263718.
- Date of allotment: 19/02/2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is an implementation notice under existing listing regulations for preferential issue of shares.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must recognize the new shares as listed and tradable from the effective date, subject to applicable lock-in restrictions on the allotted shares.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: 19 February 2026
- Listing/Trading Effective Date: 3 July 2026
- Lock-in Expiry: 15 January 2027

## Impact Assessment

The listing increases the tradable equity base of Lancer Container Lines Limited following the preferential allotment, though the newly listed shares remain under lock-in until January 15, 2027, limiting immediate float impact. Existing shareholders and market participants tracking the stock should note the dilution from the additional 10.29 crore shares now recognized as listed, pari-passu with existing equity.
