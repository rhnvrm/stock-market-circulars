## Summary

BSE has issued an update on securities under the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework effective January 28, 2026. The circular shows one security (Thrive Future Habitats Ltd, Scrip Code: 523120, ISIN: INE628D01014) will be moved to a higher ESM stage. No new securities are being added to ESM, and no securities are being removed from the framework or moved to lower ESM stages.

## Key Points

- One security moving to higher ESM stage: Thrive Future Habitats Ltd (Scrip Code: 523120)
- No new securities shortlisted for ESM framework
- No securities moving to lower ESM stages
- No securities exiting the ESM framework
- Changes effective from January 28, 2026
- Consolidated list includes 32+ securities across ESM Stage I and Stage II

## Regulatory Changes

The Enhanced Surveillance Measure framework continues with updated staging for affected securities. Securities under ESM are subject to additional surveillance criteria including:

- Trade-for-trade settlement
- Price bands restrictions
- Additional margin requirements
- Enhanced disclosure obligations

Stage progression indicates continued surveillance concerns for the affected security.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must adhere to ESM framework requirements for all listed securities
- Securities in higher ESM stages face stricter trading restrictions
- Investors should be aware of additional surveillance measures on these securities
- Market participants must comply with trade-for-trade settlement for ESM securities

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: January 28, 2026 - ESM stage changes become applicable

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited, as only one security is being moved to a higher surveillance stage. However, this may affect liquidity and trading interest in Thrive Future Habitats Ltd.

**Investor Impact**: Investors holding or trading Thrive Future Habitats Ltd should note the increased surveillance stage, which typically indicates regulatory concerns about price volatility, volume patterns, or corporate governance issues.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update their surveillance systems to reflect the new ESM classification effective January 28, 2026.

The consolidated list shows a significant number of securities (32+ listed) remain under various ESM stages, indicating continued regulatory focus on market surveillance and investor protection.
