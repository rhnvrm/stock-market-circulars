## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of Lendingkart Finance Limited (ISIN: INE090W07675, Code: 975428) effective February 6, 2026. The suspension is implemented due to the company's scheduled redemption of debentures and interest payment with a record date of February 8, 2026. Trading members are strictly advised not to deal in these debentures during the suspension period.

## Key Points

- Debenture series: LFL-11.33%-23-2-26-PVT (11.33% coupon rate)
- ISIN: INE090W07675
- BSE Code: 975428
- Record date for redemption and interest payment: February 8, 2026
- No dealings permitted from: February 6, 2026
- Circular reference: DR-815/2025-2026
- Issued by: Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President â€“ Listing Compliance and Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notification implementing existing procedures for debenture redemption and record date fixing.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members**: Must not execute any trades in the specified debentures (INE090W07675) from February 6, 2026 onwards
- **Brokers**: Should update their systems to prevent trading in these debentures during the suspension period
- **Investors**: Cannot buy or sell these debentures through the exchange from the suspension date

## Important Dates

- **January 30, 2026**: Circular issued
- **February 6, 2026**: Trading suspension begins (no dealings permitted)
- **February 8, 2026**: Record date for redemption and interest payment

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders of this specific debenture series of Lendingkart Finance Limited. The suspension is temporary and procedural, related to the maturity and redemption process.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders will receive their redemption proceeds and final interest payment based on the February 8, 2026 record date. No new investors can enter positions after February 5, 2026.

**Operational Impact**: Trading systems must be updated to block transactions in this security. Settlement of any pending trades executed before February 6, 2026 will proceed normally.
