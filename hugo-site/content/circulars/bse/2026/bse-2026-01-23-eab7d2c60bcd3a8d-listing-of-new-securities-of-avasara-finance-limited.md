## Summary

BSE has approved the listing and trading of 1,00,01,800 new equity shares of Avasara Finance Limited (Scrip Code: 511730) issued on a rights basis. The shares will be available for trading from January 27, 2026, and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 1,00,01,800 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at par on rights basis
- Scrip Code: 511730
- ISIN: INE759D01017
- Distinctive Numbers: 5000901 to 15002700
- Date of Allotment: January 21, 2026
- Trading commencement: January 27, 2026
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for rights issue shares.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to update their systems to reflect the new securities and facilitate trading from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** January 21, 2026
- **Trading Commencement:** January 27, 2026 (Tuesday)
- **Notice Date:** January 23, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This listing increases the total issued equity capital of Avasara Finance Limited by 1,00,01,800 shares. The rights issue provides existing shareholders with the opportunity to maintain their proportional ownership. The impact is limited to shareholders and investors of Avasara Finance Limited with no broader market implications.
