## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that GOLDIAM INTERNATIONAL LTD (Scrip Code: 526729) has fixed July 10, 2026 as the record date for its bonus share issue. The company's shares will trade on an ex-bonus basis effective the same date. As per the company's letter dated July 02, 2026, 37,639,281 equity shares are expected to be allotted as bonus shares on July 13, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: GOLDIAM INTERNATIONAL LTD (Scrip Code: 526729)
- Bonus ratio: 1 (ONE) equity share of Rs. 2/- each for every 3 (THREE) equity shares of Rs. 2/- each held
- Record date: July 10, 2026
- Ex-bonus basis effective from: July 10, 2026
- Settlement number: DR-669/2026-2027
- Total shares to be allotted: 37,639,281 equity shares (as on July 13, 2026)
- Reference: Company's letter dated July 02, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is a routine notification of a corporate action (bonus issue) under BSE's Listing Operations department.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must ensure trades in GOLDIAM INTERNATIONAL LTD shares are settled on an ex-bonus basis from July 10, 2026 onwards. Depository participants and registrars must process the bonus share credit to eligible shareholders as of the record date.

## Important Dates

- Record Date: July 10, 2026
- Ex-Bonus Trading Effective Date: July 10, 2026
- Expected Bonus Share Allotment Date: July 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The bonus issue will increase the company's outstanding equity share count and proportionally adjust the share price downward on the ex-bonus date, without affecting overall shareholder value. Existing shareholders as of the record date will receive additional shares in a 1:3 ratio, which may improve stock liquidity going forward.
