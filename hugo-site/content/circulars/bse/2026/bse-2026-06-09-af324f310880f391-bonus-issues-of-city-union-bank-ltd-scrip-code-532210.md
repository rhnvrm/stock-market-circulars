## Summary

City Union Bank Ltd (BSE Scrip Code: 532210) has announced a bonus issue with a record date of June 12, 2026. The company will issue 1 bonus equity share of Rs. 1/- face value for every 3 equity shares held. Shares will trade on an ex-bonus basis from June 12, 2026 under Settlement No. DR-650/2026-2027.

## Key Points

- Bonus ratio: 1 (ONE) bonus equity share for every 3 (THREE) equity shares held
- Face value of bonus shares: Rs. 1/- each
- Record date: June 12, 2026
- Ex-bonus effective date: June 12, 2026 (Settlement No. DR-650/2026-2027)
- Total bonus shares to be allotted: 247,696,809 equity shares
- Allotment date: June 15, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification under BSE Listing Operations.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of the Exchange must note that transactions in CITY UNION BANK LTD equity shares will be on an ex-bonus basis effective June 12, 2026. Settlement and clearing obligations should reflect the adjusted price accordingly from that date.

## Important Dates

- **June 12, 2026**: Record date and ex-bonus effective date (Settlement No. DR-650/2026-2027)
- **June 15, 2026**: Allotment of 247,696,809 bonus equity shares

## Impact Assessment

The 1:3 bonus issue will increase City Union Bank's outstanding equity share count by approximately 33.3%, proportionally reducing the share price on the ex-bonus date. Existing shareholders retain the same economic value but with a higher number of shares. The allotment of ~247.7 million new shares will enhance liquidity in the scrip. No impact on non-holders; trading members must ensure proper settlement adjustments from June 12, 2026.
