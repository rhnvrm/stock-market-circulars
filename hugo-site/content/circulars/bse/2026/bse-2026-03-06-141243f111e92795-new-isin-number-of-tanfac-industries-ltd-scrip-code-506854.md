## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260306-2 as a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20260304-6 dated March 04, 2026, informing trading members of the new ISIN number assigned to TANFAC INDUSTRIES LTD (Scrip Code: 506854) following the sub-division of its equity shares from Rs.10/- to Rs.5/- per share.

## Key Points

- New ISIN number assigned: **INE639B01023**
- Company: TANFAC INDUSTRIES LTD (Scrip Code: 506854)
- Sub-division: Equity shares split from face value Rs.10/- to Rs.5/- per share
- The new ISIN is effective for all trades on and from the ex-date: **09-03-2026**
- This notice references DR-835/2025-2026
- Issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President – Listing Compliance and Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular implements a previously approved corporate action (stock sub-division) by assigning a new ISIN to the sub-divided equity shares of TANFAC INDUSTRIES LTD.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their systems to use the new ISIN **INE639B01023** for all trades in TANFAC INDUSTRIES LTD equity shares of Rs.5/- face value.
- All trades on or after the ex-date (09-03-2026) must reference the new ISIN.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 06 March 2026
- **Ex-Date (Effective Date for New ISIN):** 09 March 2026
- **Reference Notice:** Exchange Notice No. 20260304-6 dated 04 March 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard operational update following a stock sub-division corporate action. Trading members and market participants holding or trading TANFAC INDUSTRIES LTD shares must ensure the new ISIN (INE639B01023) is used from the ex-date of 09-03-2026. The sub-division doubles the number of shares at half the face value, which may increase liquidity and retail participation. No broader market impact is anticipated.
