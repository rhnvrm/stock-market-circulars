## Summary

BSE has issued a notice announcing the opening of the Offer to Buy (Acquisition Window) for the buyback of equity shares of TeamLease Services Limited under the Tender Offer route using the Stock Exchange Mechanism. The Letter of Offer has been made available to trading members and custodians, and the buyback offer window runs from Thursday, July 9, 2026, to Wednesday, July 15, 2026.

## Key Points

- TeamLease Services Limited proposes to buy back up to 14,87,500 (Fourteen Lakh Eighty-Seven Thousand Five Hundred) fully paid-up equity shares of face value Rs. 10 each.
- The aggregate buyback amount will not exceed Rs. 238 crores.
- The buyback price is fixed at Rs. 1,600 per equity share, payable in cash.
- The buyback will be executed on a proportionate basis through the Tender Offer route via the Stock Exchange Mechanism.
- The buyback size represents 24.96% (standalone) and 22.85% (consolidated) of the aggregate paid-up capital and free reserves as per FY26 audited financials, within the statutory limit of 25%.
- The shares proposed to be bought back represent 8.87% of the total paid-up equity share capital as on Wednesday, May 20, 2026.
- The notice references SEBI circulars CIR/CFD/POLICYCELL/1/2015 (April 13, 2015) and CFD/DCR2/CIR/P/2016/131 (December 9, 2016), along with subsequent BSE notices revising the mechanism for acquisition of shares through stock exchanges for tender offers under takeovers, buybacks, and delisting.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced; the notice operationalizes the buyback under existing SEBI Buyback Regulations and the revised BSE guidelines for the Acquisition Window mechanism (per notices dated Feb 10, 2017, April 24, 2019, May 28, 2020, Nov 2, 2020, and Aug 25, 2021).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and custodians must process bids/orders in accordance with the Revised Guidelines under BSE notice no. 20170210-16 and subsequent updates.
- Market participants should refer to the attached Letter of Offer and the details published on the BSE website (www.bseindia.com) for further compliance and procedural requirements.

## Important Dates

- Record date for eligibility: Wednesday, May 20, 2026 (shareholding basis for buyback entitlement percentage).
- Buyback offer period: Thursday, July 9, 2026, to Wednesday, July 15, 2026.
- Notice issued: July 8, 2026.

## Impact Assessment

This buyback offer is a significant corporate action for TeamLease Services Limited shareholders, providing an exit opportunity at a fixed price of Rs. 1,600 per share for up to 8.87% of outstanding equity. Market participants, particularly arbitrageurs and existing shareholders, should note the tender window dates to participate. The buyback, being within statutory limits, reflects the company's capital allocation strategy and may have a modestly positive signaling effect on the stock, while also reducing outstanding share count and free float post-buyback.
