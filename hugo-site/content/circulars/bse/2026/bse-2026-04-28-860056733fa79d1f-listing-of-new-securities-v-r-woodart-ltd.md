## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 1,39,90,000 new equity shares of V R Woodart Ltd (Scrip Code: 523888, ISIN: INE317D01014) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Wednesday, April 29, 2026. The shares were issued on a preferential basis to Promoter and Non-Promoter at an issue price of Rs. 22/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- with a premium of Rs. 12/-).

## Key Points

- **Company:** V R Woodart Ltd
- **Scrip Code:** 523888
- **ISIN:** INE317D01014
- **New Shares Listed:** 1,39,90,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 22/- per share (Rs. 10/- face value + Rs. 12/- premium)
- **Allotment Type:** Preferential basis to Promoter and Non-Promoter
- **Allotment Date:** November 11, 2025
- **Distribution Numbers:** 14891807 to 28881806
- **Pari-passu:** New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- **Effective Trading Date:** April 29, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice pursuant to a preferential allotment already approved and completed.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the new share listing effective April 29, 2026
- Lock-in restrictions must be observed as per the details below; locked-in shares are not freely tradable until the respective lock-in expiry dates

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** November 11, 2025
- **Effective Listing/Trading Date:** April 29, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (Tranche 1 – 28,88,180 shares, dist. 1,48,91,807–1,77,79,986):** November 30, 2027
- **Lock-in Expiry (Tranche 2 – 33,21,820 shares, dist. 1,77,79,987–2,11,01,806):** November 30, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (Tranche 3 – 28,88,181 shares, dist. 2,11,01,807–2,39,89,987):** November 30, 2027
- **Lock-in Expiry (Tranche 4 – 48,91,819 shares, dist. 2,39,89,988–2,88,81,806):** November 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing increases the total tradable equity base of V R Woodart Ltd, though a significant portion remains subject to lock-in until November 2026 and November 2027. The preferential allotment at Rs. 22/- per share expands promoter and non-promoter holdings. Impact is limited to existing shareholders and traders active in this scrip; broader market impact is negligible given the small-cap nature of the company.
