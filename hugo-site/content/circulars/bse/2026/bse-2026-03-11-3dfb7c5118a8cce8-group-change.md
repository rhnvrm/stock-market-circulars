## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Artemis Electricals and Projects Limited (scrip code 542670) will undergo a group change from Group X to Group B effective March 12, 2026. This reclassification impacts the trading and surveillance category applicable to the stock.

## Key Points

- Artemis Electricals and Projects Limited (scrip code 542670) is moving from Group X to Group B.
- The group change is effective from March 12, 2026.
- Notice issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President – Listing Compliance & Operations.
- Notice No: 20260311-22, dated March 11, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

The stock is being reclassified from Group X (a restricted/surveillance group typically for illiquid or non-compliant securities) to Group B (a standard equity group), indicating an improvement in its classification status on BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to note and apply the updated group classification for Artemis Electricals and Projects Limited (scrip code 542670) in their trading systems from March 12, 2026 onwards.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** March 11, 2026
- **Effective Date of Group Change:** March 12, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The reclassification from Group X to Group B may result in changes to trading conditions for the stock, including potential adjustments to price bands, margin requirements, and settlement obligations. Investors and traders holding or monitoring scrip code 542670 should be aware of the updated group status as it may affect order placement and risk parameters from March 12, 2026.
