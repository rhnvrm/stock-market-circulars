## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (No. 20260413-28) updating trading members about revised terms for debt securities of Magnite Developers Private Limited. This is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20250220-28 dated February 20, 2025. The revised terms take effect from Wednesday, April 15, 2026.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code 974290 with ISIN INE0M1Q07015 will trade with revised terms effective April 15, 2026.
- This notice is a continuation of the earlier notice dated February 20, 2025 (Notice No. 20250220-28).
- An Annexure containing the specific revised terms is attached to the original notice.
- Trading members are advised to review the revised terms before the effective date.

## Regulatory Changes

The debt securities of Magnite Developers Private Limited will be available for trading under revised terms as detailed in the Annexure attached to the official notice. The specific nature of the revision (e.g., coupon rate, maturity, redemption terms) is contained in the Annexure.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the revised terms for Scrip Code 974290 (ISIN INE0M1Q07015) before trading commences on April 15, 2026.
- Members should review the Annexure for full details of the revised terms.
- For clarifications, members may contact Ms. Pooja Trivedi or Hardik Bhuta (Assistant Vice President) at +91 22 2272 8844 or debt.listing@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** April 13, 2026
- **Effective Date:** Wednesday, April 15, 2026 (revised terms become applicable for trading)
- **Reference Notice:** 20250220-28 dated February 20, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This notice has a moderate and targeted impact, affecting trading members and investors holding or intending to trade in the debt securities of Magnite Developers Private Limited (Scrip Code 974290). The revision of terms may affect pricing, yield, or redemption conditions. Members must ensure their systems and orders reflect the updated terms from April 15, 2026 onwards to avoid any discrepancies in trade settlements.
