## Summary

BSE has provisionally empanelled Stratzy Fintech Private Limited as an Algorithmic Trading solutions provider for trading members of the Exchange. This circular is effective immediately and the updated list of Algo Providers is available on the BSE website.

## Key Points

- Stratzy Fintech Private Limited has been provisionally empanelled with BSE to provide Algorithmic Trading solutions to trading members.
- Vendor address: Flat No 1501, Center Point, Mulund East, Mumbai – 400081.
- Contact: +91 8291113955.
- The circular is applicable with immediate effect from 06 March 2026.
- The full updated list of empanelled Algo Providers is published on the BSE website.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine empanelment notification updating the list of approved Algo Providers on the Exchange.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action is required from trading members. Members may optionally engage Stratzy Fintech Private Limited for algorithmic trading solutions if they choose to do so.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** 06 March 2026 (immediate effect)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This notice expands the pool of provisionally empanelled Algo Providers available to BSE trading members. Trading members seeking algorithmic trading solutions now have an additional vendor option. No operational changes are mandated.
