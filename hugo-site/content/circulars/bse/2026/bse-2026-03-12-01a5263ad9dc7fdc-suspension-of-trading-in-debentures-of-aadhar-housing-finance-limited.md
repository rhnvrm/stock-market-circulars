## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (No. 20260312-36) directing trading members not to deal in the 9.55% Non-Convertible Debentures of Aadhar Housing Finance Limited (ISIN: INE538L07270, Scrip Code: 953764) effective 16 March 2026. The suspension is in connection with the company's fixed record date for redemption of the debentures and payment of interest.

## Key Points

- Debenture series: AHFL-9.55%-31-3-26-PVT (ISIN: INE538L07270, Scrip Code: 953764)
- Record date fixed: 16 March 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- No dealings permitted from: 16 March 2026
- Notice reference: DR-840/2025-2026
- Issued by: Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President – Listing Compliance and Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice under BSE's debt segment procedures requiring trading members to halt dealings in the specified debenture series around the record date.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not buy or sell the specified NCD (INE538L07270 / Scrip Code 953764) on or after 16 March 2026.
- Members are required to take note of the suspension and ensure no transactions are executed in this debenture series from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 12 March 2026
- **Record Date:** 16 March 2026
- **No Dealings From:** 16 March 2026
- **Debenture Maturity:** 31 March 2026 (as indicated by series name AHFL-9.55%-31-3-26-PVT)

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and traders of this specific NCD series. Trading members must cease dealing in scrip code 953764 from 16 March 2026. Debenture holders will receive redemption proceeds and final interest payment on or around the record date. The broader market impact is minimal as this is a routine end-of-lifecycle corporate action for a single debenture series maturing on 31 March 2026.
