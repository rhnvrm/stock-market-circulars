## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260616-9 directing trading members to suspend dealings in Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of Weisshorn Realty Private Limited. The company has fixed 18 June 2026 as the Record Date for redemption of NCDs on exercise of a Put Option and payment of interest, effective from 18 June 2026.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to debentures of Weisshorn Realty Private Limited under DR-654/2026-2027
- ISIN: INE0OKC08011 (BSE scrip code 974625), series WRPL-18%-24-2-28-PVT
- No dealings permitted from 18 June 2026 onwards
- Record Date for redemption and interest payment is 18 June 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of NCD on Exercise of Put Option and Payment of Interest

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework introduced. This is a standard operational notice under BSE's Listing Compliance and Operations procedures governing corporate action-related trading suspensions in the Debt segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified NCDs (ISIN INE0OKC08011, scrip code 974625) from 18 June 2026
- Members should update their systems and alert clients holding this instrument about the suspension

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 16 June 2026
- **Record Date:** 18 June 2026
- **No Dealings From:** 18 June 2026

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited to holders of Weisshorn Realty Private Limited's NCDs under the specific series WRPL-18%-24-2-28-PVT. The suspension is a standard pre-redemption measure and does not signal any distress; it facilitates an orderly put option exercise and interest payout. Broader market impact is negligible given the private nature of the debenture.
