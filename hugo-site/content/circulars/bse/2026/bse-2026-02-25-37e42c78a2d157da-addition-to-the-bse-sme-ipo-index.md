## Summary

BSE Index Services Private Limited has announced the addition of Yashhtej Industries (India) Limited (Exchange Ticker: 544708) to the BSE SME IPO Index. The company was listed on BSE on February 25, 2026, and its inclusion in the index is effective at the open of February 26, 2026.

## Key Points

- Yashhtej Industries (India) Limited (ticker: 544708) is being added to the BSE SME IPO Index.
- The stock was listed on BSE effective February 25, 2026, per Notice No. 20260224-17.
- Index inclusion is effective at the open of Thursday, February 26, 2026.
- The corporate action has been included in the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE) dated February 25, 2026.
- Queries may be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index composition update following a new SME IPO listing on BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

- Index-tracking funds and ETFs referencing the BSE SME IPO Index should update their portfolios to include Yashhtej Industries (India) Limited (544708) effective at the open of February 26, 2026.
- Clients and data vendors should incorporate the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE) to reflect this change.

## Important Dates

- **February 25, 2026**: Listing of Yashhtej Industries (India) Limited on BSE.
- **February 26, 2026 (market open)**: Effective date of addition to the BSE SME IPO Index.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to index-linked products tracking the BSE SME IPO Index. Passive funds and ETFs benchmarked to this index will need to add exposure to Yashhtej Industries (India) Limited before the open of February 26, 2026. No broader market or regulatory impact is anticipated from this routine rebalancing action.
