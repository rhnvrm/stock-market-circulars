## Summary

BSE's Index Cell has announced changes to the index methodology for two equity indices — BSE BANKEX and BSE Focused IT Index — effective 29 May 2026. The primary change tightens the constituent weighting caps in BSE BANKEX, reducing the individual stock cap from 20% to 19% and the aggregate top-3 stock cap from 45% to 43%. Market coverage, liquidity criteria, and buffer rules remain unchanged.

## Key Points

- Individual constituent weight cap in BSE BANKEX reduced from **20% to 19%**
- Aggregate weight cap for top 3 stocks reduced from **45% to 43%**
- Aggregate weight cap for top 5 stocks remains unchanged at **60%**
- Excess weight continues to be distributed proportionally across remaining index stocks
- Descending weight structure (top to bottom) is maintained
- Market coverage methodology (minimum 90% float-adjusted market cap), liquidity criteria (minimum 90% trading frequency over preceding six months), and buffer rules (2% inclusion/exclusion buffer) are **unchanged**
- Minimum constituent count of 14 is preserved; preference given to derivative stocks when filling minimum count

## Regulatory Changes

The revised constituent weighting rules for BSE BANKEX are:

| Parameter | Existing | New |
|---|---|---|
| Individual constituent weight cap | 20% | 19% |
| Aggregate weight — top 3 stocks | 45% | 43% |
| Aggregate weight — top 5 stocks | 60% | 60% (no change) |
| Excess weight distribution | Proportional | Proportional (no change) |
| Descending weight structure | Maintained | Maintained (no change) |

No changes are specified for the BSE Focused IT Index methodology beyond what is noted in the notice header.

## Compliance Requirements

- Index fund managers, ETF providers, and passive investment vehicles tracking BSE BANKEX or BSE Focused IT Index should review and update their rebalancing models to reflect the new weighting caps ahead of the effective date.
- Entities using these indices as benchmarks should update benchmark documentation and disclosures accordingly.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 13 April 2026
- **Effective Date:** 29 May 2026 (next quarterly rebalance at which new methodology will be applied)

## Impact Assessment

The tightening of weighting caps in BSE BANKEX will marginally reduce concentration risk by limiting the influence of the largest banking constituents. The reduction of the individual cap from 20% to 19% and the top-3 aggregate from 45% to 43% will redistribute weight slightly toward mid-tier banking stocks within the index. This is a low-disruption change but will require rebalancing by index-linked products (ETFs, index funds) at the next quarterly review on or around 29 May 2026. The impact is primarily operational for fund managers rather than systemic for market participants broadly.
