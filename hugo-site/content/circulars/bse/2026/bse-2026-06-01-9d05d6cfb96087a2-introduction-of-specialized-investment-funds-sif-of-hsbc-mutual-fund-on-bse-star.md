## Summary

BSE has announced the activation of HSBC Mutual Fund's Specialized Investment Funds (SIF) on the BSE StAR MF Platform with effect from June 1, 2026, pursuant to SEBI's Regulatory Framework for Specialized Investment Funds (SEBI circular SEBI/HO/IMD/IMD-PoD-1/P/CIR/2025/26 dated February 27, 2025). Transactions for NRI and Minor investors are permitted under HSBC MUTUAL FUND_SIF schemes through the platform, subject to country-level restrictions.

## Key Points

- HSBC MUTUAL FUND_SIF activated on BSE StAR MF Platform from **June 1, 2026**
- NRI and Minor investor transactions permitted under HSBC MUTUAL FUND_SIF schemes
- NRI applications are **not accepted** from investors in: United States of America, Ukraine, Canada, Syria, Cuba, Russia, Iran, Pakistan, Korea (Democratic People's Republic Of), and Belarus
- The **REDHEX HYBRID LONG-SHORT FUND SIF - NFO** scheme will be activated for order routing from **June 2, 2026**
- Three annexures accompany the notice: Regulatory Framework (Annexure I), Minimum Investment Threshold Monitoring (Annexure II), and Detailed Transaction Acceptance Process (Annexure III)
- Members may contact the Mutual Fund Helpdesk at 022-45720450 / 022-45720650 on business days between 8:00 a.m. and 7:00 p.m.

## Regulatory Changes

This circular implements SEBI's Regulatory Framework for Specialized Investment Funds (SIF) under SEBI/HO/IMD/IMD-PoD-1/P/CIR/2025/26 dated February 27, 2025. SIFs are a new category of investment vehicle, and HSBC Mutual Fund is among the first AMCs to go live on BSE StAR MF under this framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must adhere to the country-level NRI investment restrictions listed and not process applications from investors in the ten restricted countries
- Members should refer to Annexure III for the detailed process for transaction acceptance under SIF on the BSE StAR MF Platform
- Members must monitor the Minimum Investment Threshold requirements as outlined in Annexure II
- Queries or clarifications should be routed through the BSE CRS portal at https://bsecrs.bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **June 1, 2026**: HSBC MUTUAL FUND_SIF activated on BSE StAR MF Platform
- **June 2, 2026**: REDHEX HYBRID LONG-SHORT FUND SIF - NFO scheme activated for order routing

## Impact Assessment

This notice expands the product suite available on the BSE StAR MF Platform by introducing a new SIF category from HSBC AMC. Members and distributors participating in mutual fund transactions via BSE StAR MF will gain access to HSBC's SIF schemes, including the new NFO. The NRI country restriction list limits distribution for cross-border investors, specifically barring applications from ten high-risk jurisdictions. Operational impact is moderate, requiring members to update transaction workflows and eligibility checks for SIF scheme orders.
