## Summary

BSE has informed members that Nippon India Mutual Fund will be offering the **Nippon India CRISIL-IBX Financial Services 3-6 Months Debt Index Fund** as a New Fund Offer (NFO) with SIP facility on the BSE StAR MF Platform from **March 05, 2026 to March 09, 2026**. The fund covers six scheme variants across Regular and Direct plans with Growth and IDCW options. Tentative allotment is scheduled for March 12, 2026.

## Key Points

- NFO period: March 05, 2026 to March 09, 2026 on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Six scheme variants offered across Regular and Direct plans (Growth, IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment)
- ISINs: INF204KC1GU9, INF204KC1GV7, INF204KC1GW5, INF204KC1GX3, INF204KC1GY1, INF204KC1GZ8
- Tentative allotment date: March 12, 2026
- Lumpsum purchases extended till midnight on March 09, 2026
- SIP/XSIP registration authentication must be completed before 3:00 PM on NFO closure date
- Transactions where client has not initiated funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day will be cancelled
- Pending transactions (funds initiated) remain in system till 3:00 PM on the last payout day or T+5, whichever is earlier

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for an NFO launch.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure client funds are initiated before 9:00 AM on T+1 day to avoid cancellation
- Clear funds must be available in ICCL Account by **6:00 PM on March 12, 2026** for unit allotment
- Cheques and OTM payments accepted during NFO period subject to receipt of clear funds in ICCL account by 6:00 PM on March 12, 2026
- SIP/XSIP authentication must be completed before 3:00 PM on March 09, 2026
- Switch transactions must have funds received before the allotment date to be eligible; funds received on or after allotment date will not be considered

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| NFO Open Date | March 05, 2026 |
| NFO Close Date | March 09, 2026 |

| SIP/XSIP Authentication Deadline | 3:00 PM, March 09, 2026 |
| Tentative Allotment Date | March 12, 2026 |
| Clear Funds Deadline in ICCL | 6:00 PM, March 12, 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notification of limited market impact. It affects BSE StAR MF platform members and their clients looking to invest in a short-duration debt index fund tracking CRISIL-IBX Financial Services 3-6 Months segment. No broader market or equity segment impact expected. Members should ensure operational compliance with fund transfer and authentication timelines to avoid transaction cancellations.
