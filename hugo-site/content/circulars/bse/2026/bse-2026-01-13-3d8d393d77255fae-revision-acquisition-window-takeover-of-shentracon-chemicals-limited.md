## Summary

BSE has revised the acquisition window (takeover) period for SHENTRACON CHEMICALS LIMITED. The window, originally scheduled to end on Friday, January 23, 2026, has been extended to Tuesday, January 27, 2026. The extension excludes January 26, 2026 (SEBI Holiday) and January 15, 2026 (Thursday - Exchange Holiday due to Municipal Corporation Elections in Maharashtra 2026).

## Key Points

- Acquisition window for SHENTRACON CHEMICALS LIMITED extended by 4 calendar days
- Extension is a continuation of previous notice no. 20260107-37 dated January 07, 2026
- Two holidays excluded from the extended period: January 15 and January 26, 2026
- Notice applies to all market participants in the equity segment

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a procedural revision to the previously announced acquisition window timeline.

## Compliance Requirements

- All market participants must note the revised acquisition window closing date
- Trading related to the takeover must comply with the extended timeline
- Participants should refer to the original notice no. 20260107-37 for complete takeover details

## Important Dates

- **Original Closing Date:** Friday, January 23, 2026
- **Revised Closing Date:** Tuesday, January 27, 2026
- **Excluded Holiday 1:** Thursday, January 15, 2026 (Exchange Holiday - Municipal Corporation Elections in Maharashtra)
- **Excluded Holiday 2:** Sunday, January 26, 2026 (SEBI Holiday)
- **Notice Date:** January 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The extension provides additional time for shareholders to participate in the acquisition window for SHENTRACON CHEMICALS LIMITED. The impact is limited to stakeholders of this specific company and does not affect broader market operations. The exclusion of holidays ensures adequate trading days are available for the takeover process.
