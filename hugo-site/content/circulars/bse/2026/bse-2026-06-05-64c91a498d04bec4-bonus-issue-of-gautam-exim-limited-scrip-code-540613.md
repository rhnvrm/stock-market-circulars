## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Gautam Exim Limited (Scrip Code: 540613) has fixed a record date for a bonus issue of equity shares. Equity shares will trade on an ex-bonus basis from June 10, 2026. A total of 1,84,86,000 equity shares are to be allotted.

## Key Points

- Bonus ratio: 3 bonus equity shares of Rs.5/- each for every 1 equity share of Rs.5/- each held
- Record date: June 10, 2026
- Ex-bonus effective date: June 10, 2026
- Settlement reference: DR-648/2026-2027
- Market lot revised from 750 to 3000, effective June 10, 2026
- Total bonus shares to be allotted: 1,84,86,000 equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

The market lot for Gautam Exim Limited (Scrip Code: 540613) will change from 750 to 3000 with effect from June 10, 2026, reflecting the 3:1 bonus ratio.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to:
- Note the ex-bonus effective date of June 10, 2026 and apply appropriate price adjustments
- Update the revised market lot (3000) for Gautam Exim Limited (540613) in trading systems from June 10, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Record Date:** June 10, 2026
- **Ex-Bonus Date:** June 10, 2026 (Settlement DR-648/2026-2027)
- **Allotment Date:** June 11, 2026
- **Market Lot Change Effective:** June 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Existing shareholders of Gautam Exim Limited as of the record date (June 10, 2026) will receive 3 additional equity shares for every 1 share held, tripling their holding count. The share price is expected to adjust proportionally on the ex-bonus date. The market lot increase from 750 to 3000 reflects the post-bonus share count and will affect minimum tradeable quantities. Total new shares of 1,84,86,000 will increase the company's equity base fourfold.
