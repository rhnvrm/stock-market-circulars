## Summary

BSE has published Notice No. 20260615-33 informing that the Draft Offer Document of **Utazzo Services Limited**, filed on June 12, 2026, is now available on the BSE website. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and is open for public comments for a minimum period of 21 days from the date of notice.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Utazzo Services Limited on June 12, 2026
- Available on the BSE SME website for at least 21 days for public review
- Complies with Schedule VI of the ICDR (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations
- Public comments are invited within 21 days from June 15, 2026 (deadline: approximately July 6, 2026)
- Comments to be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Draft document hosted at: https://www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural notice under SEBI ICDR Regulations requiring public availability of the Draft Red Herring Prospectus (DRHP) before an SME IPO proceeds.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members of the public, investors, and stakeholders may review the Draft Offer Document on the BSE SME website
- Any comments or objections must be submitted within 21 days from June 15, 2026 to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 15, 2026
- **Draft Offer Document Filing Date:** June 12, 2026
- **Public Comment Deadline:** On or before July 6, 2026 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal immediate market impact. It is a standard procedural step in the SME IPO process for Utazzo Services Limited, which is not yet listed on BSE. Market participants interested in the upcoming SME IPO may review the draft document and submit feedback. No changes to existing trading, settlement, or compliance obligations are involved.
