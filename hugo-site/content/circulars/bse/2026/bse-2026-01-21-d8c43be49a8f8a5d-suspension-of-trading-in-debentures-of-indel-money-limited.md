## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in the Non-Convertible Debentures of INDEL MONEY LIMITED (ISIN: INE0BUS07BJ4, Code: IML-11%-07-2-26-PVT, Scrip Code: 976160) effective January 23, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest.

## Key Points

- Trading Members advised not to deal in the specified debentures from January 23, 2026
- Record date for both redemption and interest payment: January 23, 2026
- Debenture ISIN: INE0BUS07BJ4 (IML-11%-07-2-26-PVT)
- Scrip Code: 976160
- Notice reference: DR-805/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action notice for debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must refrain from dealing in the specified INDEL MONEY LIMITED debentures from January 23, 2026
- All trading activity in this instrument must cease on the specified date
- Members required to note the suspension and inform their clients accordingly

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 21, 2026
- **Record Date**: January 23, 2026
- **No Dealings From**: January 23, 2026
- **Purpose**: Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders of this specific debenture instrument. The suspension is procedural to facilitate the redemption process and interest payment.

**Investor Impact**: Medium - Debenture holders will receive redemption proceeds and accrued interest as per the terms. No further trading opportunity available post-suspension date.

**Operational Impact**: Trading Members must update their systems to block trading in this instrument and notify affected clients holding these debentures about the impending redemption.
