## Summary

BSE has issued the live activities schedule for the Offer to Buy (OTB) – Acquisition Window for the buyback of shares of Sarla Performance Fibers Ltd (symbol: SARLAPOLY, scrip code: 526885). The buyback window is open from Thursday, 21st May 2026 to Wednesday, 27th May 2026, with bidding sessions from 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM daily. The offer is for up to 40,00,000 equity shares at Rs. 110.00 per share.

## Key Points

- **Security:** Sarla Performance Fibers Ltd | Symbol: SARLAPOLY | ISIN: INE453D01025 | Scrip Code: 526885
- **Offer Size:** Up to 40,00,000 equity shares
- **Offer Price:** Rs. 110.00 per share
- **Face Value:** Rs. 1.00
- **Market Lot:** 1
- **Opening Date:** Thursday, 21st May 2026
- **Closing Date:** Wednesday, 27th May 2026
- Bidding is open to multiple investor categories including FIIs, FIs, Mutual Funds, Insurance Companies, Other QIBs, NIBs, Companies, and Individuals
- Only demat shares are eligible for bidding

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular operationalises the existing OTB framework as per Exchange Notices 20150930-1 (dated 30 September 2015) and 20170203-29 (dated 3 February 2017).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must ensure the Unique Client Code (UCC) of each investor is registered and/or updated with the correct PAN Number and investor Category in the Exchange UCC database before placing bids.
- Members and Custodians should note that no End of Issue files will be generated on Extranet if no securities are accepted.
- Extranet files for acceptance of bids will only be available upon confirmation of acceptance from the Merchant Banker/RTA after issue closure.
- For Leased Line URL access settings, members should refer to Exchange Circular No. 20170503-12.

## Impact Assessment

This circular is operationally relevant to trading members and custodians who have clients holding Sarla Performance Fibers Ltd shares and wish to participate in the buyback. The buyback offer price of Rs. 110.00 per share may represent a premium or discount depending on the prevailing market price, making participation decisions important for investors. The offer window is short (5 trading days), requiring prompt action from eligible participants. Systems access via iBBS and RTRMS platforms must be confirmed in advance; members needing new login IDs or password resets should contact BSE Trading Operations at 022-22728005 or user.trdops@bseindia.com.
