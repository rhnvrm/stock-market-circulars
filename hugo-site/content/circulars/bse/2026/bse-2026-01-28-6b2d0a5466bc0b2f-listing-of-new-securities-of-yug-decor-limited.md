## Summary

BSE has listed 53,94,075 new equity shares of Yug Decor Limited (Scrip Code: 540550) issued through a rights offering at par value of Rs. 10 per share. The shares are permitted for trading effective January 29, 2026, and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares. Additionally, the market lot for the company has been revised from 3450 shares to 5175 shares.

## Key Points

- **Number of Shares Listed**: 53,94,075 equity shares
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10 per share
- **Issue Price**: At par (Rs. 10)
- **Issue Type**: Rights basis
- **ISIN**: INE796W01019
- **Distinctive Numbers**: 10789270 to 16183344
- **Allotment Date**: January 27, 2026
- **Trading Effective Date**: January 29, 2026
- **Market Lot Change**: From 3450 shares to 5175 shares
- **Ranking**: Pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard listing notification following rights issue allotment procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the revised market lot of 5175 shares (previously 3450 shares) effective January 29, 2026
- Trading systems should be updated to reflect the new market lot before trading commences
- Members should ensure proper handling of the new ISIN and distinctive number range

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: January 27, 2026
- **Listing Date**: January 29, 2026
- **Market Lot Change Effective Date**: January 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine rights issue listing for a single company. The addition of approximately 53.94 lakh shares will increase the float of Yug Decor Limited.

**Operational Impact**: Low to Medium for trading members - The market lot change from 3450 to 5175 shares (50% increase) requires system updates and trader awareness to avoid order placement errors. Members must ensure their trading systems are configured correctly before January 29, 2026.

**Liquidity Impact**: The new shares rank equally with existing shares and may improve overall liquidity in the scrip, though the impact depends on trading volumes post-listing.
