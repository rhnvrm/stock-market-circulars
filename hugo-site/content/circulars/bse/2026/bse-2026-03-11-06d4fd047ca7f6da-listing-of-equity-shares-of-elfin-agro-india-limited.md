## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the equity shares of Elfin Agro India Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange. The company will trade on the SME segment under scrip code 544724 and symbol ELFIN. The specific listing date and further security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Elfin Agro India Limited equity shares are approved for listing on BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544724
- Trading Symbol: ELFIN
- ISIN: INE1FEW01013
- Exact listing date to be announced via a separate notice
- Notice issued by Nitinkumar Pujari, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing admission notice under BSE's SME platform framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should note the upcoming admission of Elfin Agro India Limited shares to dealings
- Members should await the follow-up notice confirming the exact listing date and security details before initiating trades

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: March 11, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced via a separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This notice signals the imminent listing of Elfin Agro India Limited on the BSE SME segment. Impact is limited to market participants specifically interested in the SME segment and this company. Trading members should prepare systems to accommodate the new scrip (544724 / ELFIN / INE1FEW01013) once the listing date is confirmed.
