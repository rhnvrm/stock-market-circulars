## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 25,27,500 new equity shares of Kilburn Engineering Limited (Scrip Code - 522101), issued at a premium of Rs. 415/- each (issue price Rs. 425/- per share of Rs. 10/- face value) to Promoter and Non-Promoter allottees on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants, are listed and permitted to trade effective Monday, July 20, 2026.

## Key Points

- Security: Kilburn Engineering Limited, Scrip Code 522101, ISIN INE338F01015
- Total new shares listed: 25,27,500 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each
- Issue price: Rs. 425/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- plus premium of Rs. 415/-)
- Shares issued to Promoter and Non-Promoters on preferential basis via conversion of warrants
- Distinctive numbers: 53472859 to 56000358
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Allotment occurred in two tranches: 11/05/2026 and 16/05/2026

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing notification under BSE Listing Operations following preferential allotment and warrant conversion.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific action required from trading members beyond recognizing the new shares as tradable and pari-passu with existing equity from the effective listing date. Allottee shares are subject to lock-in as detailed below.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment (Tranche 1): 11/05/2026 — 3,10,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 53472859-53782858), lock-in up to 31/01/2027
- Date of Allotment (Tranche 2): 11/05/2026 — 3,00,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 53782859-54082858), lock-in up to 31/01/2028
- Date of Allotment (Tranche 3): 11/05/2026 — 3,82,500 shares (Dist. Nos. 54082859-54465358), lock-in up to 31/01/2027
- Date of Allotment (Tranche 4): 16/05/2026 — 15,35,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 54465359-56000358), lock-in up to 31/01/2027
- Listing and trading effective date: Monday, July 20, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing increases Kilburn Engineering Limited's outstanding equity share capital by 25,27,500 shares, arising from conversion of previously issued warrants to promoters and non-promoters. Most shares carry lock-in restrictions until January 2027 or January 2028, limiting immediate free-float dilution. The event is a standard corporate action with low market-wide significance but relevant to existing shareholders tracking dilution and promoter shareholding changes.
