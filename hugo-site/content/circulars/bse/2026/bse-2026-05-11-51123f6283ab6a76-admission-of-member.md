## Summary

BSE has admitted two new Trading Members effective 08 May 2026. Both entities have been granted trading rights on BSE Ltd. subject to the Exchange's Rules, Bye-laws, and Regulations.

## Key Points

- One Centurion Ventures Private Limited (Member No. 6988) admitted for the Execution Only Platform (EOP) segment.
- Chronon Tradetech LLP (Member No. 6989) admitted for the Cash/Equity Derivatives segments.
- Both admissions are classified as New Membership.
- Effective date of admission: 08 May 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine membership admission notice.

## Compliance Requirements

Existing Trading Members have no direct compliance obligations arising from this notice. For clarifications, members may contact membership.ops@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **08 May 2026**: Effective date of admission for both new Trading Members.

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. Two new participants have been added to the BSE trading ecosystem — one in the Execution Only Platform segment and one in Cash/Equity Derivatives. This is a standard administrative notice with no bearing on existing members' operations or compliance obligations.
