## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the equity shares of Merritronix Limited (Scrip Code: 544773) will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment (M Group), effective Monday, June 22, 2026. This follows the earlier Notice No. 20260605-37 dated June 05, 2026.

## Key Points

- Merritronix Limited (Scrip Code: 544773) shares currently trade in the Trade for Trade (MT Group) segment
- Shares will move to the Rolling segment under M Group from June 22, 2026
- This is a continuation of BSE Notice No. 20260605-37 dated June 05, 2026
- Applicable to SME segment

## Regulatory Changes

The scrip is being moved out of the Trade for Trade (MT Group) mechanism, which requires compulsory delivery on every trade, into the normal Rolling settlement (M Group), where standard netting and settlement rules apply.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must update their systems and client communications to reflect the change in trading group for Merritronix Limited (Scrip Code: 544773) effective June 22, 2026. For further details, members may contact Mr. Anurag Jain at Tel. No. 022-2272 8822.

## Important Dates

- **June 08, 2026**: Notice issued
- **June 22, 2026**: Effective date — transfer from MT Group (Trade for Trade) to M Group (Rolling segment)

## Impact Assessment

This change relaxes the trading restrictions on Merritronix Limited shares. Moving from Trade for Trade to Rolling settlement allows netting of trades, potentially increasing liquidity and reducing settlement obligations for trading members. Investors and traders holding or transacting in this scrip will experience standard rolling settlement terms from June 22, 2026 onwards.
