## Summary

BSE announces the addition of Modern Diagnostic & Research Centre Limited (Exchange ticker: 544673) to the BSE SME IPO Index. The stock is being listed on BSE on January 07, 2026, and will be added to the index effective at the open of trading on January 08, 2026. This action is part of routine index maintenance following the company's listing.

## Key Points

- Modern Diagnostic & Research Centre Limited (544673) listed on BSE on January 07, 2026
- Stock will be added to BSE SME IPO Index effective January 08, 2026
- Addition references Notice No. 20260106-16
- Action included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- Queries to be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine index composition update.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements. Index participants and investors tracking the BSE SME IPO Index should note the composition change for portfolio adjustments.

## Important Dates

- **January 07, 2026**: Listing date for Modern Diagnostic & Research Centre Limited on BSE
- **January 08, 2026**: Effective date for index addition (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard index inclusion following a new SME IPO listing. Index funds and ETFs tracking the BSE SME IPO Index will need to acquire shares of Modern Diagnostic & Research Centre Limited. The inclusion may provide additional liquidity and visibility for the newly listed stock among index investors.
