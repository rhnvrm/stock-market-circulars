## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 498,125 additional equity shares issued by 9 companies under their Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and Employee Stock Option Schemes (ESOS). These securities will be listed and admitted for trading on the exchange with effect from Wednesday, January 14, 2026. None of the issued shares are subject to lock-in restrictions.

## Key Points

- Total of 9 companies issuing further securities under ESOP/ESOS schemes
- Largest issuance: Niva Bupa Health Insurance (88,000 shares)
- Second largest: Wipro Limited (74,568 shares)
- All shares have no lock-in period (NA)
- Face values range from ₹1 to ₹10 per share
- Trading effective date: January 14, 2026
- Notice Number: 20260113-19

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. The issuance represents employee compensation through stock options, a routine corporate action. The share quantities are relatively small compared to the outstanding shares of these companies.

**Liquidity Impact**: Negligible incremental impact on trading volumes. The additional shares may provide minor liquidity enhancement.

**Shareholder Dilution**: Minor dilution for existing shareholders across all 9 companies. The impact is distributed and not concentrated in any single entity.

**Operational Impact**: None for market participants beyond updating trading systems with new share counts and distinctive numbers.
