## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that KRChoksey Shares & Securities Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 385) will voluntarily cease its operations in the Currency Derivatives segment with effect from Monday, February 23, 2026.

## Key Points

- KRChoksey Shares & Securities Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 385) is voluntarily closing its business in the Currency Derivatives segment.
- The cessation of business is effective from Monday, February 23, 2026.
- This is a voluntary closure initiated by the member, not a regulatory action.
- Trading members with queries may contact BSE Membership Department at membership.ops@bseindia.com.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative notice regarding a member's voluntary withdrawal from the Currency Derivatives segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and counterparties dealing with KRChoksey Shares & Securities Pvt. Ltd. in the Currency Derivatives segment should make alternative arrangements prior to February 23, 2026.
- For clarifications, contact BSE Membership Department: membership.ops@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **February 23, 2026**: Effective date of cessation of business in Currency Derivatives segment for KRChoksey Shares & Securities Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 385).

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This notice affects only KRChoksey Shares & Securities Pvt. Ltd.'s participation in the BSE Currency Derivatives segment. Clients and counterparties of this member should be aware and transition their activity accordingly before the effective date. No broader market or systemic implications are anticipated.
