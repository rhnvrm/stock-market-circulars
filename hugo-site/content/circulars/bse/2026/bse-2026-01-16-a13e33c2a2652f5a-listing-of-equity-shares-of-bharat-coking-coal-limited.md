## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that equity shares of Bharat Coking Coal Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544678, trading symbol BHARATCOAL, and ISIN number INE05XR01022. The exact listing date and complete security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Bharat Coking Coal Limited equity shares to be listed on BSE
- Scrip Code: 544678
- Trading Symbol: BHARATCOAL
- ISIN Number: INE05XR01022
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes specified in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the security details for Bharat Coking Coal Limited and await further notice regarding the listing date and complete security specifications.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 16, 2026
- **Listing Date**: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This listing will add a new coal sector company to the BSE equity segment, providing investors with an additional investment option in the coal and energy sector. The impact is moderate as it expands market opportunities, though the specific listing date remains pending. Trading members and investors should watch for the follow-up notice containing the final listing date and complete security details.
