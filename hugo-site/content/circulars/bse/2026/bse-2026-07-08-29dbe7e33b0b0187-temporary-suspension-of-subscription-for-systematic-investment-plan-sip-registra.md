## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (No. 20260708-14 dated July 08, 2026) informing members about the temporary suspension of Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) subscriptions under three PGIM India Mutual Fund schemes, effective July 09, 2026. This follows an earlier notice (No. 20260605-41 dated June 05, 2026) regarding restrictions on SIP registrations for these schemes, and implements changes as per a Notice-cum-Addendum issued by PGIM India Mutual Fund, already reflected on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Key Points

- Temporary suspension of SIP investments applies to three PGIM India Mutual Fund schemes:
  - PGIM India Global Equity Opportunities Fund of Fund
  - PGIM India Emerging Markets Equity Fund of Fund
  - PGIM India Global Select Real Estate Securities Fund of Fund
- Fresh SIP registrations will not be accepted after the cut-off time on July 08, 2026.
- Existing SIP registrations made prior to the effective date will continue to be processed at the AMC/RTA end without change.
- Redemption, Switch-out, and STP-out transactions remain permitted without any amount restriction.
- Changes have already been implemented on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory change; this is an operational advisory from BSE relaying instructions from PGIM India Mutual Fund's Notice-cum-Addendum regarding SIP subscription suspension in specific overseas/real estate-focused fund-of-fund schemes.

## Compliance Requirements

BSE trading members dealing in mutual fund units via the BSE StAR MF Platform must:

- Stop accepting fresh SIP registrations for the named schemes after the cut-off time on July 08, 2026.
- Continue processing existing SIP registrations without alteration.
- Continue to permit redemption, switch-out, and STP-out transactions without restriction.
- Refer to the attached Notice-cum-Addendum from PGIM India Mutual Fund for further details.

## Important Dates

- June 05, 2026: Original notice (20260605-41) on SIP restrictions issued.
- July 08, 2026: Cut-off date for fresh SIP registrations; date of this notice.
- July 09, 2026: Effective date of temporary SIP subscription suspension.

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact operational notice limited to SIP subscription processing for three specific PGIM India fund-of-fund schemes on the BSE StAR MF Platform. It does not affect listed equity trading, existing SIP investors, or redemption/switch-out flexibility. Impact is confined to prospective SIP investors in these particular schemes and to mutual fund distributors/members who must update their systems to reject fresh SIP registrations accordingly.
