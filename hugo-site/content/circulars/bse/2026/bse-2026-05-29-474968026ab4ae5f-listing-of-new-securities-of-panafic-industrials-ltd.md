## Summary

BSE has listed 41,06,25,000 new equity shares of Panafic Industrials Ltd. (Scrip Code: 538860, ISIN: INE655P01029) issued at par (Rs. 1/- each) on a rights basis. These shares are available for trading on BSE with effect from Monday, June 1, 2026.

## Key Points

- 41,06,25,000 equity shares of Rs. 1/- each issued at par on rights basis are now listed on BSE.
- Scrip Code: 538860 | ISIN: INE655P01029
- Distribution Numbers: 82125001 to 492750000
- Date of Allotment: May 26, 2026
- Trading commences: June 1, 2026 (Monday)
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice informing trading members of the availability of new securities for trading.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed to update their systems to permit trading in these newly listed securities from June 1, 2026 onwards under Scrip Code 538860.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** May 26, 2026
- **Effective Date for Trading:** June 1, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing event for rights issue shares. The addition of approximately 41.06 crore new shares will increase the total share capital of Panafic Industrials Ltd. and may cause minor dilution to existing shareholders. The shares are pari-passu with existing equity, so no preferential rights or ranking differences apply. Market impact is expected to be minimal and localised to this scrip.
