## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Shadowfax Technologies Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544685, symbol SHADOWFAX, and ISIN number INE12UN01015. The exact date of listing and complete security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Shadowfax Technologies Limited
- Scrip Code: 544685
- Symbol: SHADOWFAX
- ISIN Number: INE12UN01015
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Security details will be provided in subsequent notice

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of the Exchange are informed to note the upcoming listing of Shadowfax Technologies Limited equity shares for future trading activities.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: January 27, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This listing will add a new equity security to BSE's trading platform. Trading members should prepare for the commencement of trading in SHADOWFAX shares once the listing date is formally announced. The listing represents the company's entry into the public equity markets and will provide investors with a new investment opportunity in the logistics and delivery services sector.
