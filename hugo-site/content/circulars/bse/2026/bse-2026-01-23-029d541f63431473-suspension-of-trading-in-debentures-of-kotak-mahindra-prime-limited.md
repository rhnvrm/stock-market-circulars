## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of KOTAK MAHINDRA PRIME LIMITED effective February 5, 2026. The suspension is due to the company setting a record date for redemption of specific debentures. Trading members are advised not to deal in the mentioned debentures from the effective date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspended for KOTAK MAHINDRA PRIME LIMITED debentures (ISIN: INE916DA7RW2, Code: 974322)
- Debenture series: KMPL-7.9240%-20-2-26-PVT with 7.924% coupon rate
- Purpose: Redemption of Non-Convertible Debentures
- Circular reference: DR-814/2025-2026
- Issued by: Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President â€“ Listing Compliance and Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notice for debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from February 5, 2026
- All exchange members are required to take note of the suspension
- Members must ensure compliance with the no-dealings restriction from the specified date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** January 23, 2026
- **Record Date:** February 5, 2026
- **No Dealings From:** February 5, 2026
- **Redemption Date:** February 5, 2026 (implied)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - affects only a specific debenture series of KOTAK MAHINDRA PRIME LIMITED

**Investor Impact:** Debenture holders will receive redemption proceeds as per the terms. No trading opportunity available from the record date.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members must update their systems to prevent trading in the specified security from February 5, 2026. Standard operational procedure for maturing debt instruments.

**Liquidity Impact:** Minimal - debentures approaching maturity typically have low trading volumes. The suspension is temporary and tied to the redemption process.
