## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Bluegod Entertainment Ltd (Scrip Code: 539175) has informed the Exchange about a change in its company name. The SCRIP ID and Abbreviated Name for the BOLT Plus System will be updated accordingly, effective July 17, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company Bluegod Entertainment Ltd is changing its name to Starbeam Ventures Limited.
- Scrip Code 539175 remains unchanged.
- New Scrip ID for BOLT Plus System: STARBEAM
- New Abbreviated Name on BOLT Plus System: STARBEAM
- Supporting document: Certificate of Incorporation (Bluegod).

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is an administrative update to the company's registered name and associated trading identifiers following submission of the Certificate of Incorporation reflecting the new name.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should update their internal systems and records to reflect the new SCRIP ID and abbreviated name (STARBEAM) for Scrip Code 539175 from the effective date to avoid transaction or reporting mismatches.

## Important Dates

- Effective Date of Name Change: July 17, 2026
- Notice Date: July 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact administrative notice. There is no change to the company's scrip code, shareholding structure, or business operations—only the display name and abbreviated identifiers on the BOLT Plus trading system are updated. Trading members and investors should note the new identifiers to ensure accurate order placement and record-keeping post-effective date.
