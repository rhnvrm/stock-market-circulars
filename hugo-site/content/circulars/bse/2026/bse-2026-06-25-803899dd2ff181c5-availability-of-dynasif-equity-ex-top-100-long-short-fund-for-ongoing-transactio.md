## Summary

BSE has informed members that the DYNASIF Equity Ex-Top 100 Long-Short Fund, a Specialized Investment Fund (SIF) scheme of 360 ONE Mutual Fund, will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP facility on the BSE StAR MF Platform with effect from June 29, 2026. This follows the earlier Notice No. 20260604-25 dated June 04, 2026.

## Key Points

- Six plan variants of DYNASIF Equity Ex-Top 100 Long-Short Fund are being made available for ongoing transactions.
- The fund falls under the Equity SIF category.
- Both Regular and Direct Plans are available, each with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options.
- Ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP enrollments will be enabled from June 29, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This notice operationalises the reopening of a previously notified SIF scheme for ongoing transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

Members (stock exchange participants and distributors) operating on the BSE StAR MF Platform should ensure their systems are updated to process transactions for the six scheme variants listed below:

| 2 | DYNASIF Equity Ex-Top 100 Long-Short Fund - Regular Plan - IDCW Payout | INF579M30141 |
| 3 | DYNASIF Equity Ex-Top 100 Long-Short Fund - Regular Plan - IDCW Reinvestment | INF579M30158 |
| 4 | DYNASIF Equity Ex-Top 100 Long-Short Fund - Direct Plan - Growth | INF579M30166 |
| 5 | DYNASIF Equity Ex-Top 100 Long-Short Fund - Direct Plan - IDCW Payout | INF579M30174 |
| 6 | DYNASIF Equity Ex-Top 100 Long-Short Fund - Direct Plan - IDCW Reinvestment | INF579M30182 |

## Important Dates

- **June 29, 2026**: Effective date for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP in all six plan variants on BSE StAR MF Platform.
- **June 04, 2026**: Original notice (No. 20260604-25) referencing the SIF scheme introduction.

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact operational notice relevant to distributors, registered investment advisers, and investors seeking exposure to the DYNASIF Equity Ex-Top 100 Long-Short Fund through BSE StAR MF. The fund's equity long-short strategy targeting ex-top 100 securities may appeal to investors seeking differentiated SIF exposure. No market-wide or systemic impact is anticipated.
