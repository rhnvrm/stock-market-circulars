## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Equity Derivatives segment on Saturday, January 3, 2026. This session allows trading members to test their trading platforms, third-party systems, and in-house developed systems. Members can participate in either the mock trading session or UAT environment to fulfill regulatory requirements under SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD1/DSAP/CIR/P/2020/234 dated November 24, 2020. There will be no new BOLT TWS Release for this session.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Equity Derivatives segment on Saturday, January 3, 2026
- Members can test trading applications for various functionalities including exceptional market conditions, risk-reduction mode, and trading halt
- Participation is optional - members may choose mock trading or UAT environment for regulatory compliance
- Trading members using third-party platforms or in-house systems via IML/ETI APIs can utilize this opportunity
- No new BOLT TWS Release will be implemented
- Feedback must be submitted by 4:30 PM on the same day
- Reference to Exchange Notice No 20201201-22 dated December 1, 2020 regarding testing of trading and risk management software

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This mock trading session allows members to comply with existing SEBI requirements under circular no. SEBI/HO/MRD1/DSAP/CIR/P/2020/234 dated November 24, 2020.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should participate in either mock trading session or UAT environment to fulfill regulatory testing requirements
- Members using third-party trading platforms or in-house developed systems should test their applications during the session
- All members are requested to provide feedback by 4:30 PM on January 3, 2026
- Members should refer to Exchange Notice No 20201201-22 dated December 1, 2020 for testing guidelines

## Important Dates

- **January 3, 2026**: Mock trading session for Equity Derivatives segment
  - Login: 10:15 AM to 11:15 AM
  - Continuous Trading: 11:15 AM to 3:30 PM
  - Feedback deadline: 4:30 PM

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: None. This is a mock trading session with no impact on live trading or market operations.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Optional participation for members to test their systems and ensure readiness. No mandatory system changes or upgrades required as there is no new BOLT TWS Release.

**Member Impact**: Low. Provides an opportunity for voluntary testing of trading platforms and fulfillment of regulatory requirements in a controlled environment.
