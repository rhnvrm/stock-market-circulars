## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of three new Treasury Bills (T-Bills) in the Debt Instruments segment (G Group) effective January 19, 2026. The T-Bills have varying maturity periods of 91 days, 182 days, and 364 days. Trading will be suspended two working days prior to each maturity/redemption date.

## Key Points

- Three new T-Bills to be listed on BSE from January 19, 2026
- All T-Bills listed under G Group - Debt Instruments
- Market lot size is 1 for all three securities
- Trading to cease two working days before maturity dates (excluding bank holidays)
- Notice issued on January 16, 2026 (Notice No: 20260116-40)

## New Treasury Bills Details

### 91-Day T-Bill
- Scrip Code: 805166
- ISIN: IN002025X414
- Symbol: 91TB170426
- Maturity Date: April 17, 2026

### 182-Day T-Bill
- Scrip Code: 805167
- ISIN: IN002025Y412
- Symbol: 182TB17726
- Maturity Date: July 17, 2026

### 364-Day T-Bill
- Scrip Code: 805168
- ISIN: IN002025Z419
- Symbol: 364TB15127
- Maturity Date: January 15, 2027

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the listing of these securities in the debt segment
- Members must ensure trading is halted two working days before respective maturity dates
- For clarifications, trading members should contact BSE at 2272 8352/5753/8597

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: January 19, 2026
- **Trading Suspension for 91-Day T-Bill**: April 15, 2026 (T-2)
- **Maturity - 91-Day T-Bill**: April 17, 2026
- **Trading Suspension for 182-Day T-Bill**: July 15, 2026 (T-2)
- **Maturity - 182-Day T-Bill**: July 17, 2026
- **Trading Suspension for 364-Day T-Bill**: January 13, 2027 (T-2)
- **Maturity - 364-Day T-Bill**: January 15, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine administrative listing of government securities in the debt segment.

**Operational Impact**: Limited to debt segment trading members who deal in government securities. No impact on equity trading operations.

**Investor Impact**: Provides additional short-term investment options for debt market participants seeking government-backed securities with varying maturity periods from 3 months to 1 year.

**Liquidity**: T-Bills are highly liquid instruments backed by the government, suitable for treasury management and short-term investments.
