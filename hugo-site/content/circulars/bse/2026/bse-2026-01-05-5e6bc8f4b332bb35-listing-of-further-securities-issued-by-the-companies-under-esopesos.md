## Summary

BSE has announced the listing and admission for trading of further securities issued by 6 companies under their Employee Stock Option Plan (ESOP) and Employee Stock Option Scheme (ESOS). These securities will be available for trading effective Tuesday, January 6, 2026. The circular covers a total of 2,117,579 shares across the companies with no lock-in restrictions.

## Key Points

- 6 companies have issued further securities under ESOP/ESOS schemes
- Total shares listed: 2,117,579 shares across all companies
- Effective date for trading: January 6, 2026
- No lock-in period applicable for any of the listed securities
- Face values range from ₹1 to ₹10 per share
- Largest issuance: Indiqube Spaces Limited with 1,953,646 shares (face value ₹1)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification for ESOP/ESOS shares.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must note the listing of these securities
- Standard trading procedures apply from January 6, 2026
- For clarifications, contact BSE at 022-2272-5878

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 5, 2026
- **Trading Commencement**: January 6, 2026 (Tuesday)
- **Lock-in Period**: Not Applicable (NA) for all companies

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine administrative listing of ESOP/ESOS shares representing employee stock option exercises. The share counts represent minor dilution relative to the existing equity base of these companies.

**Liquidity Impact**: Negligible increase in floating stock. With no lock-in restrictions, these shares are immediately tradeable, but volumes are relatively small.

**Investor Impact**: Existing shareholders may experience minimal dilution. The largest impact is on Indiqube Spaces Limited with ~1.95 million shares, followed by Avantel Limited with ~497,000 shares.
