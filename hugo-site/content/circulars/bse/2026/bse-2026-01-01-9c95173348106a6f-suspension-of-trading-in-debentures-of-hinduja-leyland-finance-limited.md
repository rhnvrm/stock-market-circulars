## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in specific debentures of Hinduja Leyland Finance Limited effective January 12, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Company: Hinduja Leyland Finance Limited
- ISIN: INE146O07482
- Scrip Code: 974568
- Series: HLFL-GSEC-26-1-26-PVT
- Record Date: January 12, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- No dealings permitted from: January 12, 2026
- Notice Number: 20260101-50
- Reference: DR-796/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification for debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures (ISIN: INE146O07482) from January 12, 2026
- Trading members are required to take note of the suspension and inform their clients accordingly
- All dealing activities in the mentioned debenture series must cease from the specified date

## Important Dates

- **January 1, 2026**: Circular issued
- **January 12, 2026**: Record date for redemption and interest payment
- **January 12, 2026**: Trading suspension begins (no dealings permitted)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders of the specific debenture series (HLFL-GSEC-26-1-26-PVT). This is a routine corporate action that affects only the debt segment.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders as of the record date (January 12, 2026) will receive interest payment and redemption proceeds. No trading liquidity will be available from the suspension date.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to block trading in this specific ISIN and inform clients holding these debentures about the redemption and interest payment schedule.
