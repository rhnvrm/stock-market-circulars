## Summary

BSE has announced the exclusion of Sayaji Industries Limited from the Periodic Call Auction System (PCAS) mechanism effective from Tuesday, January 06, 2026. This follows the earlier notice dated October 05, 2017 relating to the listing of the company.

## Key Points

- Sayaji Industries Limited (Scrip Code: 540728, ISIN: INE327G01024) will be excluded from PCAS mechanism
- Effective date: January 06, 2026
- This is a follow-up to the listing notice issued on October 05, 2017
- Trading members have been notified of this change

## Regulatory Changes

The securities of Sayaji Industries Limited will no longer be traded under the Periodic Call Auction System mechanism. The stock will return to normal trading mode.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of the exchange are informed to take note of this exclusion and adjust their trading activities accordingly from the effective date. For clarifications, members can contact Mr. Parag Jain, Manager - Listing Compliance & Operations at 022-2272 8685 or email direct.listing@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: January 05, 2026
- Effective Date: January 06, 2026 (Tuesday)

## Impact Assessment

This is a positive development for Sayaji Industries Limited as exclusion from PCAS typically indicates improved liquidity or compliance with normal trading requirements. The impact is limited to this specific stock and represents a return to regular trading mechanisms, which may improve trading flexibility for market participants.
