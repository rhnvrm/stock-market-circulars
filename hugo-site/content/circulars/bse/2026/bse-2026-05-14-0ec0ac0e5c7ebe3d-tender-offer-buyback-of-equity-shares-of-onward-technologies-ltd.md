## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260514-46 informing trading members that Onward Technologies Ltd (BSE Code: 517536) has fixed **18 May 2026** as the record date for its equity shares buyback (tender offer). Shareholders on record as of this date will be eligible to participate in the buyback.

## Key Points

- Onward Technologies Ltd (BSE Code: 517536) has announced a buyback of equity shares via the tender offer route.
- Record date is set as **18 May 2026** in the Dematerialised Securities – Rolling Settlement Segment.
- The record date determines both the entitlement quantum and the names of eligible shareholders.
- Settlement reference: DR-632/2026-2027.
- Ex-entitlement date is also **18 May 2026**.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This notice is a standard corporate action communication under BSE's Listing Compliance and Operations framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are required to take note of the record date and communicate it to relevant clients holding Onward Technologies Ltd shares.
- Shareholders wishing to participate in the buyback must ensure their holdings are in dematerialised form prior to the record date.

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Notice Date | 14 May 2026 |
| Record Date / Ex-Entitlement Date | 18 May 2026 |
| Settlement No. | DR-632/2026-2027 |

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a moderate, stock-specific impact. Existing shareholders of Onward Technologies Ltd (517536) need to hold shares in demat form on or before 18 May 2026 to be eligible for the buyback offer. The buyback may provide an exit opportunity at a premium for eligible shareholders. No broader market or systemic impact is expected.
