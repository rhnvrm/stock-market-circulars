## Summary

BSE has announced the live activities schedule for the Offer to Buy (OTB) – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for ORTIN GLOBAL LTD (Symbol: ORTINGLOBE, ISIN: INE749B01020, Scrip Code: 539287). The offer window runs from Friday, 5th June 2026 to Thursday, 18th June 2026 at an offer price of Rs. 14.65 per share for up to 21,14,162 equity shares.

## Key Points

- **Security:** ORTIN GLOBAL LTD (ORTINGLOBE), ISIN: INE749B01020, Scrip Code: 539287
- **Offer Size:** Up to 21,14,162 equity shares
- **Offer Price:** Rs. 14.65 per share
- **Face Value:** Rs. 10.00
- **Market Lot:** 1
- **Opening Date:** Friday, 5th June 2026
- **Closing Date:** Thursday, 18th June 2026
- Bid offering and modifications/cancellations are permitted from 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. throughout the offer period
- Give-up/Take-up sessions run 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. (give-up) and 9:15 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. (take-up) on the last day (18th June 2026)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a procedural/operational notice providing the schedule of activities for the OTB window in line with existing OTB framework (refer Exchange Notices 20150930-1 and 20170203-29 for operational instructions).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must ensure the Unique Client Code (UCC) of each investor is registered and updated with the correct PAN Number and Category in the Exchange UCC database before placing bids.
- Members and Custodians may only bid for investors holding shares in demat form.
- Members and Custodians should note that no End of Issue files will be generated on the Extranet if no securities are accepted.
- Extranet files for bid acceptance will be available only after confirmation of acceptance is received from the Merchant Banker/RTA post issue closure.

## Impact Assessment

This notice is primarily operational, directed at BSE trading and custodian members. Investors in ORTIN GLOBAL LTD (ORTINGLOBE) who wish to tender shares under the takeover offer must do so through their brokers via the iBBS platform during the specified window. The offer is open across multiple investor categories (FII, FI, MF, IC, OTH, NIB, CO, IND). The offer price of Rs. 14.65 provides a reference exit price for existing shareholders during the two-week window.
