## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document of Paluck Technologies Limited for its SME IPO, filed on January 20, 2026. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and is available for public review and comments for a period of 21 days.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Paluck Technologies Limited on January 20, 2026
- Document available on BSE SME website for at least 21 days
- Contains all mandatory disclosures under Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Public comments invited within 21 days from notice date
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural notice as per existing ICDR (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public and stakeholders may review the Draft Offer Document on www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Comments, if any, should be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com within 21 days
- Company must maintain document availability for minimum 21-day period

## Important Dates

- **Document Filing Date**: January 20, 2026
- **Notice Date**: January 27, 2026
- **Comment Submission Deadline**: February 17, 2026 (21 days from notice date)

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a pre-IPO procedural notice for an SME segment company. No immediate trading or market operations impact.

**Investor Impact**: Low to Medium - Relevant only for potential investors interested in the upcoming Paluck Technologies Limited SME IPO. Provides opportunity for due diligence and comment submission.
