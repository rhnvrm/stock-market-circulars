## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of Motilal Oswal Nifty 500 ETF units effective Monday, January 19, 2026. The ETF is an open-ended scheme that tracks the Nifty 500 Total Return Index and will trade in the 'B' Group. The scheme has 14.3 million units with a face value of Rs. 10 per unit and issue price of Rs. 23.88 per unit. Three market makers have been appointed to provide liquidity.

## Key Points

- ETF tracks Nifty 500 Total Return Index
- Total units: 14,300,000 units
- Scrip Code: 590153, ISIN: INF247L01BU1
- BOLT System ID: MONIFTY500
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Face Value: Rs. 10.00 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 23.88 per unit
- Date of Allotment: September 29, 2023
- Units will only be traded in dematerialized form
- Listed in 'B' Group

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Standard SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000 apply requiring mutual fund units to be traded only in dematerialized form.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all trades are executed in dematerialized form only
- Trades will be in unit market lot (i.e., one unit)
- Market makers must comply with their obligations for providing liquidity

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 16, 2026
- **Original Allotment Date**: September 29, 2023
- **Listing Effective Date**: Monday, January 19, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine listing notification for an ETF that will provide investors with exposure to the Nifty 500 Total Return Index. The presence of three market makers (East India Securities Ltd., Motilal Oswal Financial Services Limited, and Kanjalochana Finserve Private Limited) ensures adequate liquidity for trading.
