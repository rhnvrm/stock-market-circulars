## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document (DRHP) of Fly-Hi Maritime Travels Limited, filed on December 31, 2025, containing all disclosures specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations. The document is available on BSE SME website for public comments for a minimum period of 21 days.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Fly-Hi Maritime Travels Limited on December 31, 2025
- Document contains all disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website for at least 21 days for public comments
- Document accessible at www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard disclosure notice as per existing ICDR Regulations requirements.

## Compliance Requirements

- Stakeholders and public may review the Draft Offer Document
- Comments, if any, must be submitted within 21 days from January 01, 2026
- Comments to be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Draft Offer Document Filing Date**: December 31, 2025
- **Notice Date**: January 01, 2026
- **Public Comment Period**: 21 days from January 01, 2026
- **Comment Submission Deadline**: Approximately January 22, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a standard procedural notice for an SME IPO. The circular primarily affects:
- Potential investors interested in Fly-Hi Maritime Travels Limited IPO
- Market participants seeking to provide feedback on the offer document
- SME segment listing pipeline

No immediate trading or compliance impact on existing listed securities.
