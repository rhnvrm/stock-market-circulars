## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (Notice No. 20260522-9) informing its members that certain mutual fund schemes will observe Non-Business Days (NBD) during May 2026 on the BSE StAR MF platform. This follows communication received from the respective mutual funds regarding market closures for those schemes.

## Key Points

- Certain mutual fund schemes will have designated Non-Business Days in May 2026.
- On NBD dates, the following transaction types will not be permitted: Subscription, Redemption, Switch, SIP, Normal STP, SWP, and AMC-STP.
- The specific schemes and their corresponding NBD dates are listed in Annexure 1 attached to the circular.
- The restriction applies exclusively to the BSE StAR MF platform.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an operational update based on communications received from mutual fund houses regarding their scheme-specific Non-Business Days.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and distributors must ensure that no transactions (Subscription/Redemption/Switch/SIP/Normal STP/SWP/AMC-STP) are processed for the affected schemes on their respective NBD dates through the BSE StAR MF platform.
- Members should refer to Annexure 1 for the complete list of affected schemes and applicable dates.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 22 May 2026
- Applicable Period: May 2026 (specific NBD dates as per Annexure 1)

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited and routine. Only specific mutual fund schemes are affected on their designated Non-Business Days. Investors and distributors using BSE StAR MF for the listed schemes will need to plan transactions around the NBD dates. No broader market or systemic impact expected.
