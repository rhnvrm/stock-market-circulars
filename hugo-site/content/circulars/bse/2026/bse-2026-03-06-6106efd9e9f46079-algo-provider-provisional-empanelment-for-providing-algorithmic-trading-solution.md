## Summary

BSE has provisionally empanelled Stratzy Fintech Private Limited as an Algorithmic Trading Solutions provider for trading members of the Exchange. This circular is issued under Notice No. 20260306-5 and is effective immediately.

## Key Points

- Stratzy Fintech Private Limited has been granted provisional empanelment as an Algo Provider with BSE.
- The empanelment is effective with immediate effect from 06 March 2026.
- Stratzy Fintech is located at Flat No 1501, Center Point, Mulund East, Mumbai – 400081, and can be contacted at +91 8291113955.
- The updated list of all empanelled Algo Providers is available on the BSE website.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced. This is an administrative update to the list of provisionally empanelled Algo Providers permitted to offer algorithmic trading solutions to BSE trading members.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members who wish to engage Stratzy Fintech Private Limited for algorithmic trading solutions may now do so, as the vendor is provisionally empanelled. Members should refer to the BSE website for the complete and current list of empanelled Algo Providers before onboarding any vendor.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** 06 March 2026 (immediate effect)

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This notice expands the pool of provisionally empanelled algo solution providers available to BSE trading members by one vendor. It does not alter trading rules, market structure, or compliance obligations. Trading members seeking algorithmic trading infrastructure have an additional vendor option to evaluate.
