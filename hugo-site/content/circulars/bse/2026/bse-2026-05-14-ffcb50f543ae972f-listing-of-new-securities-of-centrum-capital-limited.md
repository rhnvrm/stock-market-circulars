## Summary

BSE has listed 2,72,50,000 new equity shares of Centrum Capital Limited (Scrip Code: 501150) effective Friday, May 15, 2026. These shares were issued at Rs. 2852/- per share (face value Rs. 1/- at a premium of Rs. 2851/-) to promoters on a preferential basis, pursuant to conversion of warrants allotted earlier.

## Key Points

- 2,72,50,000 equity shares of Rs. 1/- face value issued at Rs. 2852/- per share to promoters
- Shares issued on a preferential basis via conversion of warrants
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 459579195 to 486829194
- ISIN: INE660C01027
- All 2,72,50,000 shares are under lock-in until 21 November 2027

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification under BSE's listing operations for securities issued through preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed that the new securities are listed and permitted to trade from May 15, 2026
- Lock-in restriction applies to all 2,72,50,000 shares (dist. nos. 459579195–486829194) until 21/11/2027; these shares cannot be traded during the lock-in period

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** 27 March 2026
- **Effective Date for Trading:** 15 May 2026 (Friday)
- **Lock-in Expiry:** 21 November 2027

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds approximately 2.72 crore shares to Centrum Capital's equity base, representing a capital raise of approximately Rs. 777 crore at the issue price. Since all newly listed shares are locked in until November 2027, there is no immediate increase in free-float. The preferential allotment to promoters increases promoter holding, which may be viewed positively as a signal of promoter confidence. Existing shareholders face dilution from this issuance.
