## Summary

BSE's Surveillance department (DOSS) sought clarification from Hindustan Zinc Ltd (Scrip Code: 500188) regarding news articles published on MoneyControl. The company has provided its clarification to the Exchange. This is a routine measure undertaken by BSE to verify news accuracy and keep members and investors informed.

## Key Points

- BSE Notice No. 20260608-26 dated 08 June 2026
- Hindustan Zinc Ltd (Scrip Code: 500188) was asked to clarify news articles appearing on MoneyControl
- The company has responded with its clarification
- Issued by BSE Surveillance department (DOSS)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this notice.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance obligations for market participants. The company has fulfilled its disclosure obligation by providing the clarification to BSE.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 08 June 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard BSE surveillance action to ensure price-sensitive or material news is confirmed or denied by the listed company. Investors should refer to the company's clarification (attached as 'Reply' in the original notice) for the substance of the response regarding the MoneyControl news article.
