## Summary

BSE has informed all market participants that Rajvir Industries Ltd (Scrip Code: 532665) will be transferred to the 'Z' group effective July 06, 2026, due to non-compliance with Regulation 27(2) of the SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015 for two consecutive quarters (December 2025 and March 2026). Since the company is already in the Z group, trades will continue to be settled on a Trade for Trade basis.

## Key Points

- Rajvir Industries Ltd (Scrip: 532665) formally moved to Z group effective July 06, 2026
- Non-compliance pertains to Regulation 27(2) — failure to submit the Corporate Governance Report for Q3 FY26 (December 2025) and Q4 FY26 (March 2026)
- Company was already placed in Z group prior to this notice; this circular confirms continuation
- Settlement mode remains Trade for Trade (no netting benefit; each trade settled individually)
- Action taken pursuant to SEBI Master Circular No. HO/49/14/14(7)2025-CFD-POD2/I/3762/2026 (issued July 11, 2023, last updated January 30, 2026), Chapter VII(A) — Penal Actions for Non-Compliance

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework introduced. Action is taken under the existing SEBI Master Circular on penal actions for non-compliance with SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015 and the Standard Operating Procedure for suspension and revocation of trading of specified securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Rajvir Industries Ltd must submit the overdue Corporate Governance Reports for December 2025 and March 2026 quarters under Regulation 27(2) of SEBI LODR Regulations to seek revocation from Z group
- Market participants and investors should note the Trade for Trade settlement applicable to scrip 532665
- Queries may be directed to bse.soplodr@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 24, 2026
- **Effective Date of Z Group Transfer:** July 06, 2026
- **Non-compliant Quarters:** December 2025 (Q3 FY26) and March 2026 (Q4 FY26)

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to investors holding or trading shares of Rajvir Industries Ltd (BSE: 532665). Under Trade for Trade settlement, buy and sell positions cannot be netted, increasing the cash and delivery obligation for traders. No short selling or intraday squaring off is permitted. Since the company was already in Z group before this circular, there is no incremental change in trading conditions — this notice serves as a formal regulatory update confirming the ongoing status. Broader market impact is negligible.
