## Summary

BSE has issued a notice in continuation of its earlier Notice No. 20260720-17 dated July 20, 2026, informing trading members that the equity shares of Alpine Texworld Limited (Scrip Code: 544825) will be moved from the Trade for Trade segment (T Group) to the Rolling segment, and will be classified under B Group, with effect from Tuesday, August 4, 2026.

## Key Points

- Scrip: Alpine Texworld Limited, Scrip Code 544825
- Trading shifts from Trade for Trade (T Group) to Rolling segment
- Post-transition, the shares will be dealt under B Group
- Change is effective from Tuesday, August 4, 2026
- This notice is issued in continuation of Notice No. 20260720-17 dated July 20, 2026
- Trading members can contact Mr. Parag Jain (Tel: 022 2272 8685) for further details

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is an administrative reclassification of the trading segment and group for the specified scrip under existing BSE listing and trading segment rules.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should update their systems and trading protocols to reflect that Alpine Texworld Limited will trade under the Rolling segment (B Group) instead of Trade for Trade (T Group) from the effective date. No filings are required from listed companies or investors.

## Important Dates

- July 20, 2026: Original Notice No. 20260720-17 issued
- July 21, 2026: Current clarificatory notice issued
- August 4, 2026 (Tuesday): Effective date of transition to Rolling segment, B Group

## Impact Assessment

The move from Trade for Trade to the Rolling segment typically indicates normalization of trading conditions for the scrip, allowing intraday trading and netting off of positions, which were previously restricted under the T Group settlement mechanism. This is generally viewed positively for liquidity and trading flexibility in the stock, though it affects only Alpine Texworld Limited and has limited broader market impact.
