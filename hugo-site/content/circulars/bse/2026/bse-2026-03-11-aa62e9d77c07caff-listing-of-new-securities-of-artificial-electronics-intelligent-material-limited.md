## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 9,50,000 new equity shares of Artificial Electronics Intelligent Material Limited (Scrip Code: 526443, ISIN: INE072B01027) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Thursday, March 12, 2026. These shares were issued at a premium of Rs. 30/- (issue price Rs. 40/-) to Promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 9,50,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 30/- (total issue price Rs. 40/- per share)
- Shares allotted to Promoters on a preferential basis via conversion of warrants
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 26719591 to 27669590
- Date of allotment: January 30, 2026
- ISIN: INE072B01027

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification for newly allotted securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to permit trading in these new securities from March 12, 2026
- Lock-in restrictions apply: all 9,50,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 26719591 to 27669590) are locked in until September 30, 2027

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** January 30, 2026
- **Listing/Trading effective date:** March 12, 2026
- **Lock-in expiry:** September 30, 2027

## Impact Assessment

This listing adds 9,50,000 equity shares to the tradeable float of Artificial Electronics Intelligent Material Limited, though the shares are subject to lock-in until September 30, 2027 and therefore cannot be traded by the allottees until that date. The preferential allotment to promoters via warrant conversion may indicate capital-raising activity. Overall market impact is minimal given the small size and lock-in restriction.
