## Summary

Rajnandini Fashion India Limited (BSE ticker: 544771) is listed on BSE effective June 03, 2026. Effective at the open of June 04, 2026, the stock will be added to the BSE SME IPO Index. This action is included in the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE) on June 03, 2026.

## Key Points

- Rajnandini Fashion India Limited (ticker: 544771) lists on BSE on June 03, 2026.
- The stock is added to the BSE SME IPO Index effective June 04, 2026 at market open.
- The index change is referenced under Notice No. 20260602-37.
- The corporate action is reflected in the end-of-day SDE file dated June 03, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard index reconstitution following a new SME IPO listing on BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance action required from brokers, investors, or listed entities. Index-tracking fund managers and ETF providers tracking the BSE SME IPO Index should update their portfolios to include 544771 (Rajnandini Fashion India Limited) effective June 04, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **June 03, 2026**: Rajnandini Fashion India Limited listed on BSE; SDE corporate action file updated.
- **June 04, 2026**: Stock added to BSE SME IPO Index at market open.

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. The addition is a routine administrative update to the BSE SME IPO Index following the company's listing. Passive funds and ETFs tracking the BSE SME IPO Index will need to incorporate this constituent. No impact on broader market operations or other listed entities.
