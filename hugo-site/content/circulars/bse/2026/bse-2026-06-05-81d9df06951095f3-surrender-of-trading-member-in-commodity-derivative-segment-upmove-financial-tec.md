## Summary

BSE has approved the surrender of trading membership of Upmove Financial Technologies Services (Member No. 6694) in the Commodity derivative segment, effective June 5, 2026.

## Key Points

- Upmove Financial Technologies Services (Member No. 6694) has surrendered its trading membership in the Commodity derivative segment.
- The surrender has been approved by BSE effective June 5, 2026.
- Trading members with clarifications may contact BSE Membership department at membership.ops@bseindia.com.

## Regulatory Changes

No broader regulatory changes. This is an administrative action specific to Upmove Financial Technologies Services.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance action required from other trading members. Members seeking clarification may contact BSE at membership.ops@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** June 5, 2026 — Surrender of trading membership approved.

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. The surrender affects only Upmove Financial Technologies Services and its clients in the Commodity derivative segment. Other trading members are unaffected. Clients of the surrendered member should make alternative arrangements for their commodity derivative trading.
