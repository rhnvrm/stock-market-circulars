## Summary

BSE has announced the schedule for an Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for shares of Premium Capital Market and Investments Ltd (PREMCAPM). The offer window will be live from Friday, January 16, 2026 to Thursday, January 30, 2026. The acquirer is offering to purchase up to 17,04,000 equity shares at Rs. 6.40 per share through the iBBS platform.

## Key Points

- **Security**: Premium Capital Market and Investments Ltd (Symbol: PREMCAPM, Scrip Code: 511660)
- **ISIN**: INE555D01019
- **Offer Size**: Up to 17,04,000 equity shares
- **Offer Price**: Rs. 6.40 per share
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10.00
- **Market Lot**: 1 share
- **Offer Period**: January 16, 2026 to January 30, 2026
- **Bid Offering Period Timing**: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM (daily)
- **Give-up/Take-up Period**: January 16 to January 29, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Last Day Give-up**: January 30, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Last Day Take-up**: January 30, 2026 (9:15 AM to 4:00 PM)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular follows existing OTB framework as per previous Exchange Notices no. 20150930-1 (September 30, 2015) and 20170203-29 (February 3, 2017).

## Compliance Requirements

### For Trading Members and Custodian Members:

1. **UCC Registration**: Ensure investor UCC is registered and/or updated with correct PAN Number and Category in Exchange UCC database
2. **Eligibility**: Trading Members can bid only for investors holding shares in demat form
3. **Platform Access**: Use iBBS platform (https://ibbs.bseindia.com/ for internet or https://ibbsll.bseindia.com/ for leased line) and RTRMS (https://rtrms.bseindia.com/)
4. **File Access**: Access Extranet (https://member.bseindia.com/) for acceptance of bids files after issue closure
5. **Note**: If no securities are accepted, no End of Issue files will be generated in Extranet

### Investor Categories for Bidding:

- Foreign Institutional Buyer (FII)
- Financial Institution (FI)
- Mutual Fund (MF)
- Insurance Companies (IC)
- Other QIBs (OTH)
- Other Non-Institutional Buyer (NOH)
- Company (CO)
- Individual (IND)

## Important Dates

- **Opening Date**: Friday, January 16, 2026
- **Closing Date**: Thursday, January 30, 2026
- **Give-up/Take-up Period**: January 16-29, 2026
- **Last Day for Give-up**: January 30, 2026
- **Last Day for Take-up**: January 30, 2026 (extended till 4:00 PM)

## Impact Assessment

### Market Impact: Low

This is a company-specific takeover offer with limited market-wide implications. The offer targets a relatively small number of shares (17,04,000 equity shares) of a single company.

### Operational Impact: Medium (for participating members)

Trading members and custodians handling shareholders of Premium Capital Market and Investments Ltd need to:
- Ensure proper UCC registration and updates
- Coordinate with clients holding PREMCAPM shares
- Access iBBS and RTRMS platforms for bidding and order management
- Monitor the offer window timeline for client participation
