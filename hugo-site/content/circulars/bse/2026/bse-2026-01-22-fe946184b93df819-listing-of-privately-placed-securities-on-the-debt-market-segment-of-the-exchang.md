## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities issued on a private placement basis on the debt market segment effective January 22, 2026. The listing includes fresh commercial paper from 360 ONE Alternates Asset Management Limited, Adani Enterprises Ltd., Mirae Asset Sharekhan Financial Services Limited, and Godrej Housing Finance Limited, along with further listings from Vivriti Capital Limited and Birla Group Holdings Private Limited. All securities will trade in dematerialized form with a standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof.

## Key Points

- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form under specified ISIN numbers
- Trading denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- All commercial papers have face value of Rs. 500,000 each
- Interest rates range from 6.9% to 8.75% per annum
- Redemption periods vary from March 2026 to January 2027
- Credit ratings: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+, and CRISIL A1+

This circular has minimal market impact as it pertains to routine listing of privately placed commercial paper on the debt segment. It does not affect equity trading or broader market operations. Trading members dealing in debt securities should note the new ISINs and trading parameters.
