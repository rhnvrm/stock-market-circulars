## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that Ravindra Energy Limited (Code: 504341) has fixed 08 June 2026 as the record date for its rights issue. With effect from that date, all transactions in the company's equity shares must be conducted on an ex-rights basis.

## Key Points

- Rights issue ratio: 1 new equity share for every 9 equity shares held
- Issue price: Rs 101 per share (face value Rs 10 + premium Rs 91)
- Record date: 08 June 2026
- Ex-rights date: 08 June 2026 (Settlement No. DR-646/2026-2027)
- Segment: Dematerialised Securities – Rolling Settlement
- Exchange code: 504341

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification under BSE's Listing Compliance and Operations framework.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must ensure all transactions in Ravindra Energy Limited equity shares (Code: 504341) are executed on an ex-rights basis with effect from 08 June 2026. Members should update their systems to reflect the ex-rights adjustment from that settlement date.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 04 June 2026
- **Record Date:** 08 June 2026
- **Ex-Rights Effective Date:** 08 June 2026 (Settlement No. DR-646/2026-2027)

## Impact Assessment

This rights issue will dilute existing shareholding by approximately 11.1% (1 new share per 9 existing shares). The issue price of Rs 101 per share (face value Rs 10 + premium Rs 91) determines the theoretical ex-rights price. Existing shareholders as of the record date will be eligible to subscribe; those who do not subscribe may see dilution. Trading members must flag the ex-rights adjustment in their systems from 08 June 2026.
