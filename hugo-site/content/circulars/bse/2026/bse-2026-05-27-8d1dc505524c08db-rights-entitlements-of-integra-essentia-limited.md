## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that dealings in Rights Entitlements (REs) for renunciation of INTEGRA ESSENTIA LIMITED will commence on Friday, May 29, 2026. The REs are tradable in dematerialized form only and will be settled on a T+1 rolling settlement basis on a trade-for-trade basis.

## Key Points

- Rights Entitlements (REs) for INTEGRA ESSENTIA LIMITED will be available for trading for renunciation from May 29, 2026 to June 05, 2026
- Scrip Code: 751118; Scrip ID: ESSEN-RE3; ISIN: INE418N20043
- Market Lot: 1; Group: R
- Settlement on T+1 Rolling Settlement basis, trade-for-trade
- REs are tradable in dematerialized form only
- Rights Issue open period: May 29, 2026 to June 10, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice follows the framework established under BSE notice no. 20200305-21 (March 05, 2020) and notice no. 20200518-34 (May 18, 2020) governing trading in Rights Entitlements.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must inform all clients about the commencement of trading in REs, specifying the ISIN (INE418N20043)
- Clients must be clearly informed that REs are not ordinary shares of INTEGRA ESSENTIA LIMITED
- Contract notes/bills for RE purchases must prominently include a disclaimer stating that purchasing REs only grants the right to participate in the Rights Issue or renounce before the issue closes
- REs that are neither subscribed (with requisite application money) nor renounced on or before the Issue Closing Date (June 10, 2026) will lapse and be extinguished

## Important Dates

- **May 29, 2026**: Commencement of RE trading for renunciation; Rights Issue opens
- **June 05, 2026**: Last day for trading in Rights Entitlements for renunciation
- **June 10, 2026**: Rights Issue closes; unrenounced and unsubscribed REs lapse after this date

## Impact Assessment

This circular is of moderate impact, limited to existing shareholders of INTEGRA ESSENTIA LIMITED and investors looking to participate in or renounce the rights issue. Trading members dealing in this scrip must ensure proper client communication and disclaimer inclusion in contract notes. The trade-for-trade settlement mechanism reduces speculative trading risk but may limit liquidity in the REs.
