## Summary

BSE has announced a demat auction for Settlement No. 837 dated 12 March 2026. The auction covers securities from approximately 86 companies where delivery obligations were not fulfilled, requiring BSE to conduct an auction to source the shares and complete settlement.

## Key Points

- Settlement No. 837 dated 12 March 2026
- Approximately 86 scrips listed for auction across diverse sectors
- Largest auction quantity: SPICEJET LTD (500285) — 883,228 shares
- Second largest: ROYAL INDIA (512047) — 122,593 shares
- Other notable quantities: EVEXIA LIFECARE (524444) — 47,197 shares; FERTILIZERS (590024) — 24,008 shares; ADANI TOTAL GAS (542066) — 9,290 shares
- Auction covers companies across sectors including aviation, energy, pharmaceuticals, infrastructure, finance, and FMCG

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice under BSE's settlement and auction framework for handling short/non-delivery of securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants with short delivery positions in the listed scrips are subject to auction settlement
- Auction participants must comply with BSE auction procedures and timelines
- Settlement of auction obligations must be completed as per BSE settlement calendar

## Important Dates

- Auction Date: 12 March 2026
- Settlement No.: 837

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine demat auction notice. Impact is generally limited to the specific securities listed and their respective market participants with open short positions. However, scrips with large auction quantities — particularly SPICEJET (883,228 shares) — may experience short-term price volatility due to forced buying in the auction. Investors holding these securities should be aware of potential price movements around the auction settlement date.
