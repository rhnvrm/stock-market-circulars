## Summary

BSE has listed 33,444 new equity shares of W.S. Industries (India) Ltd. (Scrip Code: 504220, ISIN: INE100D01014) issued to Non-Promoters on a preferential basis through conversion of warrants. These shares are permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Thursday, May 14, 2026.

## Key Points

- 33,444 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 139.50/- (issue price Rs. 149.50/-)
- Issued to Non-Promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants
- Distribution numbers: 75861875 to 75895318
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Lock-in applies on all 33,444 shares until November 29, 2027

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification under BSE's listing operations for newly issued securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed that the new securities are listed and available for trading from May 14, 2026. The allotted shares are subject to a lock-in period and cannot be traded by the allottees until November 29, 2027.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** March 4, 2026
- **Trading Commencement:** May 14, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry:** November 29, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The allotment of 33,444 shares is a small addition to W.S. Industries (India) Ltd.'s equity base. The full lock-in on these shares until November 2027 limits any near-term selling pressure. Relevant primarily to existing shareholders and trading members dealing in Scrip Code 504220.
