## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the equity shares of Hexagon Nutrition Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange. The exact date of listing will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Hexagon Nutrition Limited equity shares will be listed on BSE
- Scrip Code: 544785
- Symbol: HEXAGON
- ISIN: INE0JUI01012
- Listing date and full security details to be announced via a separate notice

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing admission notice issued by BSE's DCS-Listing department.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to take note of the upcoming listing. No immediate action is required; members should await the subsequent notice confirming the exact listing date and detailed security information.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: June 11, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced via a separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This listing notice is of medium market impact, relevant primarily to investors and traders tracking Hexagon Nutrition Limited. The scrip will become available for trading on BSE once the listing date is formally notified. No immediate trading activity is triggered by this notice.
