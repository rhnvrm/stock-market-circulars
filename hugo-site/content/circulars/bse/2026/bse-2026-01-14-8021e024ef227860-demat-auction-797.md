## Summary

BSE has announced a demat auction for Settlement No. 797 dated January 14, 2026. The auction involves 85 scrips spanning multiple sectors with quantities ranging from 1 share to 5,990,413 shares. This auction is part of the regular settlement process for outstanding securities.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 797
- Auction Date: January 14, 2026
- Total Scrips Involved: 85 companies
- Largest Quantity: Tilak Ventures (503663) with 5,990,413 shares
- Other High Volume Scrips: Padam Cotton (531395) with 11,092 shares, Sunshine Capital (539574) with 83,138 shares, Mehai Technology (540730) with 40,100 shares
- Includes companies across sectors: Pharmaceuticals, Technology, Steel, Finance, Real Estate, Textiles, and others

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard settlement auction notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members holding short positions in the listed scrips must participate in the demat auction process
- Settlement obligations must be fulfilled as per BSE settlement calendar
- Auction participants must comply with standard auction procedures and timelines

## Important Dates

- **Auction Date**: January 14, 2026
- **Settlement No.**: 797

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - affects 85 scrips with one notably large position (Tilak Ventures).

**Operational Impact**: Brokers and clearing members with outstanding delivery obligations in these scrips must participate in the auction to close their positions. The high volume in Tilak Ventures (5.99 million shares) may lead to significant price movement during auction.

**Investor Impact**: Shareholders of the listed companies may see temporary price volatility during the auction settlement process, particularly for scrips with larger quantities under auction.
