## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted for trading 22,98,850 new equity shares of Craftsman Automation Ltd. (Scrip Code: 543276, ISIN: INE00LO01017) with effect from Monday, June 22, 2026. These shares were allotted to Qualified Institutional Buyers (QIBs) pursuant to a Qualified Institutional Placement (QIP) at an issue price of Rs. 8700/- per share.

## Key Points

- 22,98,850 equity shares of Rs. 5/- face value each are newly listed on BSE
- Shares allotted to QIBs via Qualified Institutional Placement (QIP)
- Issue price: Rs. 8700/- per share
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 23855584 to 26154433
- Trading effective from June 22, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notice pursuant to a completed QIP allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities and may contact Mr. Karan Shah (Tel: 022 2272 8987) for any clarifications regarding this notice.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** June 18, 2026
- **Effective Date for Trading:** June 22, 2026 (Monday)

## Impact Assessment

The listing of ~22.99 lakh new equity shares at Rs. 8700/- implies a capital raise of approximately Rs. 1,998 crore (~Rs. 2,000 crore) through this QIP. Existing shareholders face equity dilution as these pari-passu shares become tradable from June 22, 2026. The high issue price reflects the current market valuation of Craftsman Automation. No immediate trading restrictions or compliance burdens are placed on members beyond awareness of the new share availability.
