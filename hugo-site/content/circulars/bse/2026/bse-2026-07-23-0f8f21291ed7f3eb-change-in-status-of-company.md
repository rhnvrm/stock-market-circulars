## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing that, pursuant to orders passed by the Hon'ble Securities Appellate Tribunal (SAT), the delisting of Trinity Tradelink Ltd (Scrip Code: 512417) and Newever Trade Wings Ltd (Scrip Code: 536644) stands rescinded. Consequently, the status of these companies will change from "Delisted" to "Suspended" effective July 27, 2026.

## Key Points

- The delisting of Trinity Tradelink Ltd (Scrip Code 512417) has been rescinded pursuant to SAT orders.
- The delisting of Newever Trade Wings Ltd (Scrip Code 536644) has been rescinded pursuant to SAT orders.
- Status of both companies changes from "Delisted" to "Suspended" with effect from July 27, 2026.
- As a result, Regulation 24(1) and Regulation 24(2) of the SEBI (Delisting of Equity Shares) Regulations, 2009 will no longer apply to these companies.

## Regulatory Changes

The change is driven by orders issued by the Securities Appellate Tribunal, which have overturned or set aside the earlier delisting of the two companies. As a result of this rescission, the applicability of specific delisting regulations (Regulation 24(1) and 24(2) of the SEBI Delisting Regulations, 2009) ceases for these entities, and their listing status reverts to "Suspended" rather than remaining "Delisted."

## Compliance Requirements

No direct action is required from other market participants. BSE and the Listing Compliance department will update the official status of the two scrips in their systems effective the stated date. Shareholders and stakeholders of these companies should note the status change for compliance and disclosure tracking purposes.

## Important Dates

- July 23, 2026: Notice issued by BSE.
- July 27, 2026: Effective date for change in status of the companies from "Delisted" to "Suspended."

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to shareholders, potential investors, and stakeholders of Trinity Tradelink Ltd and Newever Trade Wings Ltd. Moving from "Delisted" to "Suspended" status does not restore trading but changes the regulatory categorization, potentially affecting future re-listing or trading resumption processes for these specific scrips. There is no broader market-wide impact from this notice.
