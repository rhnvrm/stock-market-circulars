## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 29,50,000 new equity shares of Umiya Tubes Limited (Scrip Code: 539798) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Monday, April 20, 2026. These shares were allotted on a preferential basis to Non-Promoters at an issue price of Rs. 23/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- + premium Rs. 13/-).

## Key Points

- 29,50,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 13/- (total issue price Rs. 23/-)
- Allotted on a preferential basis to Non-Promoters
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE173U01015
- Distribution numbers: 10006668 to 12956667

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for newly allotted securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the listing and permitted trading of these new securities from April 20, 2026. All 29,50,000 shares are subject to lock-in restrictions until November 30, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** December 22, 2025
- **Effective Trading Date:** April 20, 2026 (Monday)
- **Lock-in Expiry:** November 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing of 29,50,000 preferential shares increases the tradable equity base of Umiya Tubes Limited. However, the entire allotted quantity is under lock-in until November 30, 2026, meaning no immediate selling pressure from these shares. Market impact is expected to be minimal given the lock-in restriction and the company's small-cap profile.
