## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 14,765 new equity shares of Fusion Finance Limited (Scrip Code: 543652) resulting from the conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up shares of Rs. 10/- each that were issued on a rights basis. These shares will be available for trading from February 05, 2026.

## Key Points

- 14,765 equity shares converted from partly paid to fully paid up
- Face value: Rs. 10/- per share
- Scrip Code: 543652
- ISIN: INE139R01012
- Distinctive Numbers: 161351031 to 161365795
- Shares are pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Originally issued on rights basis

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are permitted to trade in these new securities effective from the commencement date. No specific compliance actions required beyond standard trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: February 04, 2026
- **Trading Commencement Date**: February 05, 2026 (Thursday)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. This is a routine administrative listing of a small number of shares (14,765) arising from conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up status. The shares have pari-passu rights with existing equity shares, meaning they carry the same voting and dividend rights. Trading members can commence trading these securities from February 05, 2026.
