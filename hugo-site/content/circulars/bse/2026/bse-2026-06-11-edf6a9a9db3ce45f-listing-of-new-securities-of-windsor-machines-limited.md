## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 7,00,000 new equity shares of Windsor Machines Limited (Scrip Code 522029, ISIN INE052A01021) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Friday, June 12, 2026. The shares were allotted to promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants at an issue price of Rs. 191.85 per share.

## Key Points

- 7,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 2/- face value issued at a premium of Rs. 189.85/-, totalling Rs. 191.85/- per share
- Allotted to Promoters on a preferential basis through conversion of warrants
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 87822202 to 88522201
- Date of allotment: March 27, 2026
- Trading permitted from June 12, 2026
- Entire allotment of 7,00,000 shares is locked in until December 31, 2027

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification for newly allotted securities under an existing preferential/warrant conversion arrangement.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed that these securities are now listed and available for trading from June 12, 2026. The full allotment of 7,00,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 87822202 to 88522201) is subject to lock-in restrictions and cannot be traded or transferred until the lock-in period expires on December 31, 2027.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** March 27, 2026
- **Trading effective from:** June 12, 2026
- **Lock-in expiry:** December 31, 2027

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine preferential allotment of 7,00,000 equity shares to promoters arising from warrant conversion. Since the entire allotment is subject to a lock-in until December 31, 2027, there is no immediate addition to the free float. Minimal market impact is expected. The issue price of Rs. 191.85/- per share reflects the warrant conversion price agreed at the time of warrant issuance.
