## Summary

BSE has issued a notice regarding the suspension of trading in specific bonds of REC LIMITED. The company has fixed January 16, 2026 as the record date for redemption of its 5.94% bonds maturing on January 31, 2026. Trading members are advised not to deal in these bonds effective from the record date.

## Key Points

- Company: REC LIMITED
- Bond Code: 960409
- ISIN: INE020B08DK6
- Bond Details: RECL-5.94%-31-1-26-PVT
- Record Date: January 16, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of Bonds
- No Dealings From: January 16, 2026
- Notice Number: DR-800/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action related to bond redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified bonds from January 16, 2026
- All market participants should take note of the trading suspension
- Members should update their systems to reflect the trading halt

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 9, 2026
- **Record Date**: January 16, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Date**: January 16, 2026
- **Bond Maturity Date**: January 31, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The suspension affects holders and potential traders of REC LIMITED's 5.94% bonds. This is a routine corporate action with limited market-wide impact, primarily affecting:

- Bond holders who need to be on record as of January 16, 2026 to receive redemption proceeds
- Trading members dealing in this specific bond series
- Debt segment participants with positions in REC LIMITED bonds

The suspension is temporary and related to the upcoming redemption on January 31, 2026. No broader market implications expected.
