## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260608-41 informing trading members that 17 companies have fixed 11 June 2026 as the Record Date for redemption of their respective Commercial Papers. Trading members are advised not to deal in the Commercial Papers of these companies effective from that date.

## Key Points

- All affected Commercial Papers have a Record Date and Ex-Date of **11 June 2026**
- Settlement is under DR 649/2026-2027
- Trading members must cease dealing in these Commercial Papers from the specified ex-date
- The circular covers 17 companies across the financial services sector
- No attachments accompany this notice

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice under the Debt segment, Listing Operations department, consistent with standard procedure for CP redemptions.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members of BSE are required to:
- Refrain from dealing in the Commercial Papers of all listed companies from their respective ex-dates (11 June 2026)
- Ensure no new trades are executed in these instruments on or after the ex-date

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt market notice with minimal broader market impact. The suspension affects only specific short-term commercial paper instruments issued by 17 financial and corporate entities, all maturing/redeeming on 11 June 2026. Debt segment trading members dealing in these CPs must ensure compliance. Equity markets and retail investors are not affected. The companies involved are primarily financial institutions and NBFCs.
