## Summary

BSE has issued a circular notifying a change in the name of a listed company. The name change has been officially certified by the Office of the Registrar of Companies under the Ministry of Corporate Affairs, Government of India. The order is digitally signed by M. Nagaraju, dated February 10, 2026.

## Key Points

- A listed company has undergone an official change of name.
- The change is certified by the Registrar of Companies under the Ministry of Corporate Affairs.
- The official order is digitally signed by M. Nagaraju on 2026-02-10.
- BSE members and market participants are advised to take note of the updated company name for trading and compliance purposes.

## Regulatory Changes

The company's registered name has been changed as per the order issued by the Registrar of Companies. This change is binding on all exchanges, depositories, and market participants.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and trading participants must update their records to reflect the new company name.
- Depositories and registrars should update ISIN-linked records accordingly.
- All future filings, disclosures, and communications must use the new name.

## Important Dates

- Date of RoC Order: 2026-02-10
- BSE Circular Date: 2026-03-04

## Impact Assessment

The name change has moderate operational impact as it requires updates across trading systems, depository records, and investor-facing platforms. The underlying business and securities remain unaffected. Investors holding shares of the company will continue to hold the same securities under the revised name.
