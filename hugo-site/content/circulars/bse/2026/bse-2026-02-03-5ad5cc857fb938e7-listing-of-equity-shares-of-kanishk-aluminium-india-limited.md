## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of KANISHK ALUMINIUM INDIA LIMITED will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company will be listed on the SME segment with scrip code 544693, symbol KANISHK, and ISIN number INE163401016. The specific date of listing and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: KANISHK ALUMINIUM INDIA LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544693
- Symbol: KANISHK
- ISIN Number: INE163401016
- Segment: SME
- Listing date to be informed separately
- Security details to be provided in a separate notice

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

Trading members are informed to take note of the upcoming listing. No specific compliance actions required at this stage.

This is a routine listing announcement with minimal market impact. The listing will add one more SME security to the BSE platform. Specific trading details and the actual listing date will be communicated separately, allowing market participants to prepare for trading activities.
