## Summary

BSE has issued a notice identifying securities that have met the criteria for inclusion under the Short Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM), under both Stage I and Stage II. This action is part of the Exchange's ongoing surveillance framework and results in enhanced margin requirements on the identified securities, effective July 08, 2026.

## Key Points

- Securities listed in Annexure I have satisfied criteria for inclusion under ST-ASM Stage I.
- Securities listed in Annexure I have also satisfied criteria for inclusion under ST-ASM Stage II.
- ST-ASM Stage I: Applicable margin shall be 50% or existing margin, whichever is higher, capped at a maximum of 100%.
- ST-ASM Stage II: Applicable margin shall be 100% or existing margin, whichever is higher, capped at a maximum of 100%.
- The ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the Exchange.
- Inclusion under ASM is purely a market surveillance measure and should not be construed as an adverse action against the concerned company or entity.
- When a scrip moves out of the ASM framework, its price band will be reinstated to the level applicable prior to shortlisting, unless it remains subject to another surveillance framework.

## Regulatory Changes

This notice is issued pursuant to and in continuation of earlier Exchange notices dated September 20, 2024 (20240920-71), October 27, 2018 (20181027-1), July 19, 2019 (20190719-33), December 4, 2020 (20201204-56), September 25, 2023 (20230925-49), and September 20, 2024 (20240920-63), all pertaining to the ASM framework revisions. No new regulatory framework is introduced; this is an application of the existing ST-ASM criteria to newly identified securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must apply the specified margin rates (50% for Stage I, 100% for Stage II, or existing margin if higher, capped at 100%) on all open positions as of July 07, 2026, and on new positions created from July 08, 2026, for securities listed in Annexure I.
- Members should refer to the attached Annexure_STASM for the specific list of affected securities.
- Queries regarding the notice may be directed to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- July 07, 2026: Reference date for open positions subject to the new margin requirements.
- July 08, 2026: Effective date for revised margin rates under ST-ASM Stage I and Stage II.

## Impact Assessment

The increased margin requirements (up to 100% for Stage II securities) will raise the cost of trading and holding positions in the affected scrips, likely reducing speculative activity and liquidity in these securities. While the measure is limited to specific securities identified through surveillance criteria rather than the broader market, it can materially affect trading volumes and price behavior for the impacted stocks. Investors and traders holding or planning to trade the listed securities should account for higher margin outlays starting July 08, 2026.
