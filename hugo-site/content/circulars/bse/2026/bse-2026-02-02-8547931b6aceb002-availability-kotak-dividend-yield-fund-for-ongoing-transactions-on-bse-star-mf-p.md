## Summary

BSE has announced that KOTAK DIVIDEND YIELD FUND will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions along with SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective February 03, 2026. This follows a previous notice (20260102-22) regarding the fund's closure. Six variants of the scheme across Regular and Direct plans with different payout options will be available.

## Key Points

- KOTAK DIVIDEND YIELD FUND reopening for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Effective date: February 03, 2026
- Six scheme variants available: Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Reinvestment, and IDCW Payout options
- All schemes fall under EQUITY category
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities will be available
- This circular follows reference to Notice No. 20260102-22

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding fund availability.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members. This is an informational notice regarding mutual fund availability on the platform.

## Important Dates

- **February 03, 2026**: Effective date for reopening of KOTAK DIVIDEND YIELD FUND for ongoing transactions

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine operational matter affecting only investors and distributors of KOTAK DIVIDEND YIELD FUND.

**Investor Impact**: Positive for existing and prospective investors of KOTAK DIVIDEND YIELD FUND who can now resume subscriptions, redemptions, and systematic transaction facilities through BSE StAR MF Platform.

**Operational Impact**: BSE StAR MF Platform members can process transactions for all six variants of the fund starting February 03, 2026.
