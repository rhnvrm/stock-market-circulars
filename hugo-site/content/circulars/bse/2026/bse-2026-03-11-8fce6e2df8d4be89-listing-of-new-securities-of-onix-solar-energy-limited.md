## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 4,576,305 new equity shares of ONIX SOLAR ENERGY LIMITED (Scrip Code: 513119) issued on a rights basis are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Thursday, March 12, 2026. The shares were allotted on March 9, 2026 and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 4,576,305 equity shares of Rs. 10.00 each issued at a premium of Rs. 536.00 on a rights basis
- ISIN: INE173M01012
- Distribution numbers: 20493886 to 25070190
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Effective trading date: March 12, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for newly issued rights shares.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to update their systems to permit trading in the newly listed securities from March 12, 2026 under Scrip Code 513119 and ISIN INE173M01012.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** March 9, 2026
- **Trading Commencement Date:** March 12, 2026 (Thursday)

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 4,576,305 new equity shares to the tradeable float of ONIX SOLAR ENERGY LIMITED. Issued at a total price of Rs. 546 per share (Rs. 10 face value + Rs. 536 premium), the rights issue increases the company's equity base. These shares are pari-passu with existing shares, meaning no differential rights. Impact is limited to existing shareholders who participated in the rights issue and traders in this scrip.
