## Summary

BSE has notified members that Mirae Asset Mutual Fund will launch the MIRAE ASSET NIFTY200 MOMENTUM 30 PLUS 8-13 YR G-SEC 50:50 ETF as a New Fund Offer (NFO) on the BSE StAR MF Platform, open from July 10, 2026 to July 22, 2026. The notice includes scheme details and operational instructions for members regarding fund pay-in, allotment, and payment modes.

## Key Points

- NFO covers two scheme variants: MIRAE ASSET NIFTY200 MOMENTUM 30 PLUS 8-13 YR G-SEC 50:50 ETF and its Direct Plan, both categorized under Equity.
- Tentative allotment date for both schemes is July 27, 2026.
- Transactions where clients do not initiate funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day (including during the extended NFO period) will be cancelled, with a Contract Confirmation (CC) 'N' flag reported to the RTA/AMC.
- Transactions with initiated payment remain pending in the system until 3:00 PM on the last day of funds pay-out to the AMC, or T+5 day, whichever is earlier.
- Cheque and OTM (One Time Mandate) payments are accepted during the NFO period, subject to clear funds reaching the ICCL account by 6:00 PM on July 24, 2026.
- Switch transactions are accepted during the NFO period, subject to funds being received under the NFO scheme prior to the allotment date; switch-out funds received on or after the allotment date will not be considered for allotment.
- The NFO remains open until midnight on July 22, 2026 for Lumpsum Purchase transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is an operational notice regarding a new fund offer launch and associated transaction processing rules on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must ensure client funds are initiated and cleared within the specified timelines (9:00 AM T+1 for fund initiation; 6:00 PM on July 24, 2026 for clear funds in the ICCL account) to avoid transaction cancellation and to ensure eligibility for allotment. Members must also correctly report CC flags to the RTA/AMC for transactions that fail the funding deadline.

## Important Dates

- NFO Period: July 10, 2026 to July 22, 2026 (open until midnight on July 22, 2026 for Lumpsum Purchase)
- Clear funds deadline in ICCL account: 6:00 PM, July 24, 2026
- Tentative Allotment Date: July 27, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, low-impact operational notice primarily affecting mutual fund distributors and members participating in the BSE StAR MF Platform. It does not directly affect listed equity trading, stock prices, or broader market operations, but members handling this NFO must adhere to the specified payment and allotment timelines to ensure smooth processing for investors.
