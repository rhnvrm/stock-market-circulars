## Summary

BSE has issued a reminder notice directing all Research Analysts to submit their final accessibility audit report for digital platforms, along with details of remediation of audit findings and compliance confirmation, through the IARA portal by July 31, 2026. This requirement stems from SEBI Circular No. SEBI/HO/ITD-1/ITD_VIAP/P/CIR/2025/131 dated September 25, 2025, which mandates compliance with the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016 and rules made thereunder.

## Key Points

- Research Analysts must submit the Final Accessibility Audit Report for their digital platforms (websites, apps, etc.) to BSE.
- Submission must also include details of remediation of findings identified during the audit.
- Analysts must confirm compliance with the accessibility requirements for their digital platforms.
- Submission is to be made via the IARA portal using existing login credentials.
- A User Manual (User_Manual_RA) has been attached with step-by-step navigation and submission instructions.
- Support contact provided: 022 22725854 / 5680 / 5359.
- This notice references a chain of prior circulars dated April 28, 2026, March 25, 2026, December 9, 2025, August 29, 2025, August 26, 2025, and August 7, 2025 on the same subject.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory change is introduced; this is a follow-up/reminder notice reiterating compliance obligations under the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, as operationalized through SEBI Circular No. SEBI/HO/ITD-1/ITD_VIAP/P/CIR/2025/131 dated September 25, 2025.

## Compliance Requirements

- All Research Analysts registered with BSE must submit the final accessibility audit report for their digital platforms.
- Remediation actions taken on audit findings must be documented and submitted.
- Confirmation of compliance with accessibility norms for digital platforms is required.
- Submission must be completed through the IARA portal.

## Important Dates

- Submission Due Date: July 31, 2026
- Notice issued: July 8, 2026 (Notice No. 20260708-12)
- Underlying SEBI circular: September 25, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is an operational compliance matter affecting Research Analysts registered with BSE rather than listed companies or traded securities, so there is no direct market or stock price impact. However, non-compliance by Research Analysts could result in regulatory action or membership-related consequences from BSE/SEBI, making timely submission important for affected intermediaries.
