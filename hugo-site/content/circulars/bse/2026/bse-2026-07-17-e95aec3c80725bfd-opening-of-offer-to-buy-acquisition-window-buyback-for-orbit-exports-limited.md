## Summary

BSE has notified market participants that Orbit Exports Limited will conduct a buyback of up to 11,04,000 fully paid-up equity shares (face value Rs.10 each), representing 4.16% of the company's total paid-up equity share capital, through the Tender Offer mechanism via the Stock Exchange Acquisition Window. The buyback price is Rs.250 per equity share, for an aggregate amount not exceeding Rs.27.60 crore, excluding transaction costs. BSE is the Designated Stock Exchange for this offer.

## Key Points

- Buyback size: up to 11,04,000 equity shares (4.16% of paid-up equity capital)
- Buyback price: Rs.250 per fully paid-up equity share of face value Rs.10
- Aggregate buyback amount: not exceeding Rs.27,60,00,000 (excluding transaction costs)
- Buyback represents 9.88% (standalone) and 9.16% (consolidated) of paid-up equity capital and free reserves as per FY2025-26 audited financials, within the statutory 10% limit
- Mechanism: Tender Offer process through the Stock Exchange Acquisition Window, per SEBI circulars dated April 13, 2015 and Dec 09, 2016, and related BSE notices
- BSE is the Designated Stock Exchange for the offer
- Letter of Offer has been circulated to market participants along with this notice

## Regulatory Changes

None. This notice implements the existing SEBI/BSE framework for tender-offer buybacks (SEBI circular CIR/CFD/POLICYCELL/1/2015 and CFD/DCR2/CIR/P/2016/131, along with BSE notices 20170210-16, 20190424-35, 20200528-32, 20201102-43, and 20210825-62).

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members and custodians must process buyback tenders through the Acquisition Window in accordance with the Revised Guidelines for Mechanism for Acquisition of Shares through Stock Exchange, referring to the Letter of Offer and further details available on the BSE website.

## Important Dates

- Record Date for eligibility: Wednesday, July 15, 2026
- Buyback offer opens: Tuesday, July 21, 2026
- Buyback offer closes: Monday, July 27, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The buyback offers eligible shareholders of Orbit Exports Limited an exit opportunity at Rs.250 per share on a proportionate basis. Trading members and custodians need to ensure operational readiness to process tenders through the Acquisition Window during the offer period. Impact is limited to shareholders of this specific company and the market intermediaries handling the transaction.
