## Summary

BSE, through ICCL (Indian Clearing Corporation Limited), has issued the settlement schedule for the Offer to Buy under the Acquisition Window (Takeover) for Indo Borax and Chemicals Limited. This notice (No. 20260409-4) outlines the settlement procedures applicable to market participants involved in this specific takeover transaction.

## Key Points

- Notice issued by ICCL under the Settlement/RMS category on 09 April 2026
- Pertains to the Offer to Buy through the Acquisition Window mechanism for Indo Borax and Chemicals Limited
- Applicable to the General segment
- No attachments included with this notice; full schedule details are contained within the circular PDF

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This circular operationalizes existing SEBI Takeover Regulations by providing the specific settlement schedule for the Indo Borax and Chemicals Limited acquisition window.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and clearing members participating in the Offer to Buy for Indo Borax and Chemicals Limited must adhere to the settlement schedule outlined in this circular
- Participants should refer to the attached PDF for specific settlement dates, obligations, and procedural requirements
- ICCL members involved in the takeover transaction must ensure timely compliance with settlement obligations

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 09 April 2026
- Specific settlement dates for the acquisition window are detailed in the circular PDF (20260409-4)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a targeted impact on trading and clearing members directly involved in the takeover bid for Indo Borax and Chemicals Limited. It ensures orderly settlement of transactions under the Acquisition Window mechanism. The broader market impact is limited, as this pertains to a single company's takeover process. Participants in the offer must closely follow the prescribed schedule to avoid settlement failures or penalties.
