## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that new equity shares of Tata Consumer Products Limited (Scrip Code: 500800, ISIN: INE192A01025) are listed and permitted to trade with effect from Wednesday, June 03, 2026. These shares were issued pursuant to a Scheme and had been kept in abeyance.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 500800; ISIN: INE192A01025
- Securities: Equity Shares of Re. 1/- each issued pursuant to a Scheme (previously kept in abeyance)
- Total shares listed: 584 (103 + 189 + 292)
- Distinctive number ranges: 632585947–632586049 (103 shares), 632537391–632537579 (189 shares)
- Shares are pari passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Effective date for trading: June 03, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an administrative listing notice under BSE's Listing Operations department.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed and permitted to trade in these newly listed securities from the effective date. No additional compliance action is required beyond normal trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: June 02, 2026
- Effective Date for Trading: June 03, 2026 (Wednesday)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The number of shares being listed (584 shares) is negligible relative to Tata Consumer Products Limited's total outstanding equity. This is a routine administrative action to permit trading in shares that were withheld pending resolution of a Scheme-related matter.
