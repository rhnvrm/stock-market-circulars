## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for the company formerly known as Tavernier Resources Limited. The company's name has been officially changed to Eforu Entertainment Limited with effect from October 27, 2025. The company was originally incorporated as Dewan Industries Limited.

## Key Points

- Old Name: Tavernier Resources Limited
- New Name: Eforu Entertainment Limited
- Corporate Identification Number (CIN): L93290MH1994PLC193901
- Effective Date: October 27, 2025
- Certificate issued by Central Processing Centre, ROC
- Company type: Company limited by shares
- Original name at incorporation: Dewan Industries Limited
- Registered office: Level 4, A-Wing, Dynasty Business Park, Andheri Kurla Road, Andheri East, Mumbai-400059, Maharashtra

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate name change procedure completed under the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014, Rule 29.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display the old name (Tavernier Resources Limited) along with the new name (Eforu Entertainment Limited) for a period of two years at all places where the company is required to display its name under Section 12 of the Companies Act
- This requirement applies to all official documents, signage, and communications
- The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders

## Important Dates

- **October 27, 2025**: Effective date of name change and certificate issuance
- **October 27, 2027**: End of two-year period for mandatory dual name display requirement

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative corporate action with minimal market impact. The name change does not affect:
- The company's CIN
- Rights and liabilities of stakeholders
- Legal obligations or contracts
- Trading operations (subject to exchange notification)

Stakeholders should note the new company name for future correspondence and verify the company's latest status on the Ministry of Corporate Affairs website at www.mca.gov.in/MCA21.
