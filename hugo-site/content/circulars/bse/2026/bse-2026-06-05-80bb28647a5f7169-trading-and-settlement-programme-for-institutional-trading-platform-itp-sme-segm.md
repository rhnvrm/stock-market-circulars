## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260605-8, issued on 5 June 2026 by the Indian Clearing Corporation Limited (ICCL), publishes the trading and settlement programme for the Institutional Trading Platform (ITP) - SME Segment for the period from 1 July 2026 to 31 July 2026. The schedule is available as attachment ITP072026.

## Key Points

- Notice issued by ICCL under the Settlement/RMS category for the SME segment.
- Covers trading and settlement dates for the ITP-SME segment for the full month of July 2026.
- Attachment ITP072026 contains the detailed schedule.
- Applicable to all market participants operating in the Institutional Trading Platform for SME securities.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced. This is a routine periodic notification of the settlement calendar.

## Compliance Requirements

Market participants and clearing members active in the ITP-SME segment should refer to the attached schedule (ITP072026) to plan their trading and settlement obligations for July 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Schedule Period:** 01 July 2026 to 31 July 2026
- **Notice Date:** 05 June 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard monthly operational notice providing visibility into upcoming settlement dates for the ITP-SME segment. Participants should update their internal systems and settlement workflows to reflect the July 2026 schedule.
