## Summary

BSE has announced Demat Auction Settlement No. 795 dated January 12, 2026. The circular lists 103 securities across various companies that are available for demat auction with their respective quantities. This is a routine settlement mechanism for securities where physical delivery obligations need to be met through auction.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 795
- Date: January 12, 2026
- Total securities listed: 103 scrips
- Quantities range from 1 share to 9,999 shares depending on the scrip
- Includes major stocks like ITC (95 shares), TATA STEEL (100 shares), HINDALCO (1 share), HCL TECH (20 shares)
- Also includes banking stocks like DCB BANK (50 shares), YES BANK (125 shares), PUNJAB NATIONAL BANK (15 shares)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard operational circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members with outstanding delivery obligations for the listed securities must participate in the demat auction
- Settlement must be completed as per BSE auction settlement procedures
- Members should ensure availability of securities for auction or arrange for purchase

## Important Dates

- Auction Date: January 12, 2026
- Settlement No: 795

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact operational circular. Demat auctions are routine settlement mechanisms that occur regularly at BSE. The quantities involved are relatively small for most scrips, suggesting minimal market impact. Only members with specific delivery obligations for these securities need to take action. The auction helps maintain orderly settlement and delivery of securities in the cash market segment.
