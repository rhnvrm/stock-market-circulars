## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (No. 20260514-18) dated 14 May 2026 informing members and stakeholders that Growth Securities Private Limited has applied for the surrender of its BSE membership. This is an administrative membership compliance matter handled by the Membership Compliance Department.

## Key Points

- Growth Securities Private Limited has formally applied to surrender its BSE membership
- The notice is categorized under 'Others' in the General segment
- Issued by BSE's Membership Compliance Department
- No attachments were included with this notice

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this notice. It is a procedural notification of a membership surrender application.

## Compliance Requirements

Members and counterparties dealing with Growth Securities Private Limited should take note of this application. Any open positions, obligations, or business relationships with this entity may need to be reviewed in light of the pending membership surrender.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 14 May 2026
- No specific deadlines mentioned in the notice

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to parties directly transacting with Growth Securities Private Limited. The broader market is not affected. Counterparties should review any outstanding settlements or obligations with this member pending the conclusion of the surrender process.
