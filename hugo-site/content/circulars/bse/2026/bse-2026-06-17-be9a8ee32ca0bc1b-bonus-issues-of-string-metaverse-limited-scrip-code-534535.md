## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that String Metaverse Limited (Scrip Code: 534535) has fixed **19 June 2026** as the record date for a bonus issue. Equity shares of the company will trade on an ex-bonus basis effective 19 June 2026 (Settlement No. DR-655/2026-2027).

## Key Points

- Bonus ratio: **2 (Two) bonus equity shares of Re. 1/- each for every 9 (Nine) equity shares of Re. 1/- each** held as on the record date.
- Record date: **19 June 2026**
- Ex-bonus effective date: **19 June 2026** (Settlement No. DR-655/2026-2027)
- Total bonus shares to be allotted: **55,588,884 equity shares** on **22 June 2026**, as per the company's letter dated 8 June 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification issued under BSE listing compliance and operations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their systems to reflect ex-bonus pricing from 19 June 2026.
- Shareholders holding equity shares as on the record date (19 June 2026) will be eligible for the bonus shares.

## Impact Assessment

The 2:9 bonus issue will increase the total share count of String Metaverse Limited by approximately 22.2%, leading to a proportional adjustment in the share price on the ex-bonus date. Existing shareholders will receive 2 additional shares for every 9 held at no cost, maintaining their proportional ownership. Market liquidity may improve modestly post-allotment due to the increased float of approximately 55.59 million new shares.
