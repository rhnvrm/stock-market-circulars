## Summary

BSE has notified members that JM Financial Mutual Fund is launching the JM Multi Asset Allocation Fund as an NFO (New Fund Offer) on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The NFO opens June 24, 2026 and closes July 08, 2026, with SIP/XSIP facility available. Six scheme variants are offered across Regular and Direct plans with Growth and IDCW (Payout and Reinvestment) options.

## Key Points

- NFO subscription window: June 24, 2026 to July 08, 2026 on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Six scheme variants available — Regular and Direct plans, each with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- Tentative allotment date: July 14, 2026; clear funds must be in ICCL account by 6:00 PM on that date
- Transactions without client-initiated funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day will be cancelled
- SIP/XSIP registrations must be authenticated by clients on or before 3:00 PM on July 08, 2026
- NFO schemes remain extended till midnight on July 08, 2026 for Lumpsum purchases only

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an operational notice announcing the availability of a new mutual fund NFO on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clients initiate fund payment by 9:00 AM on T+1 day to avoid transaction cancellation
- Pending transactions (with initiated payment) will remain in system until 3:00 PM on the last funds pay-out day or T+5, whichever is earlier
- Cheque and OTM payments accepted during NFO period, subject to clear funds in ICCL account by 6:00 PM on July 14, 2026
- Switch-out funds received on or after the allotment date will not be considered for allotment
- SIP/XSIP client authentication must be completed by 3:00 PM on July 08, 2026

## Important Dates

- **June 24, 2026**: NFO subscription opens
- **July 08, 2026**: NFO subscription closes (midnight for Lumpsum; 3:00 PM for SIP/XSIP authentication)
- **July 14, 2026**: Tentative allotment date; clear funds must be in ICCL account by 6:00 PM

## Impact Assessment

This notice is relevant to BSE members and distributors operating on the BSE StAR MF Platform who wish to facilitate client investments in JM Multi Asset Allocation Fund. The multi-asset category offers exposure across equity, debt, and other asset classes. Members need to adhere to the payment and SIP authentication timelines to ensure valid allotment for their clients. No broader market impact anticipated; routine NFO operational instructions.
