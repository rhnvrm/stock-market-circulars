## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that DVARA KSHETRIYA GRAMIN FINANCIAL SERVICES PRIVATE LIMITED has fixed 12 March 2026 as the record date for part redemption of its Non-Convertible Debentures and payment of interest. Trading in these debentures will continue with a reduced face value effective from the same date.

## Key Points

- Company: DVARA KSHETRIYA GRAMIN FINANCIAL SERVICES PRIVATE LIMITED
- Instrument: DKGFSPL-11%-27-12-26-PVT (ISIN: INE179P07548, Scrip Code: 976048)
- Record Date: 12 March 2026
- Purpose: Part Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- Reduced Face Value: Rs. 3,750/- per Debenture
- Effective Date for Reduced Face Value: 12 March 2026
- Settlement Reference: DR-838/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an operational notice under existing corporate action and debt market settlement frameworks.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that from 12 March 2026, trading in the specified debentures will be conducted at the reduced face value of Rs. 3,750/- per debenture.
- Members should update their systems and inform clients holding these NCDs accordingly.

## Important Dates

- **Record Date:** 12 March 2026
- **Effective Date for Reduced Face Value:** 12 March 2026
- **Notice Date:** 09 March 2026

## Impact Assessment

This part redemption affects holders of DVARA KSHETRIYA GRAMIN FINANCIAL SERVICES PRIVATE LIMITED NCDs (11%, maturing 27 December 2026). The face value will be reduced by Rs. 3,750/- per debenture effective 12 March 2026, alongside interest payment. Impact is limited to investors holding this specific privately placed instrument; broader market impact is minimal.
