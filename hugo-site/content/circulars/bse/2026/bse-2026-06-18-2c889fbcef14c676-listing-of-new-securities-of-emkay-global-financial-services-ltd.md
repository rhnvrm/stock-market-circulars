## Summary

BSE has listed 7,93,400 new equity shares of Emkay Global Financial Services Ltd (Scrip Code: 532737, ISIN: INE296H01011) issued to promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to the conversion of warrants. The shares are ranked pari-passu with existing equity shares and are permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Friday, June 19, 2026.

## Key Points

- 7,93,400 equity shares of face value Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 229.50/- (issue price Rs. 239.50/-)
- Shares issued to promoters on a preferential basis via conversion of warrants
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 26336732 to 27130131
- Trading permitted from June 19, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice for securities already allotted under a preferential issue.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed that these securities are now listed and may be traded on the Exchange from the effective date. No additional compliance action is required from members beyond standard trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment (Tranche 1):** 30 April 2026 — 3,78,300 shares (Dist. Nos. 26336732 to 26715031)
- **Date of Allotment (Tranche 2):** 12 May 2026 — 4,15,100 shares (Dist. Nos. 26715032 to 27130131)
- **Trading effective from:** 19 June 2026
- **Lock-in expiry (Tranche 1):** 18 December 2027
- **Lock-in expiry (Tranche 2):** 22 December 2027

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 7,93,400 shares to the tradable float of Emkay Global Financial Services Ltd, representing a modest increase in total share capital. However, all newly listed shares are subject to promoter lock-in until December 2027, meaning they will not be available for market trading in the near term. Market impact is expected to be minimal. The issue price of Rs. 239.50/- per share reflects the warrant conversion terms agreed with the promoters.
