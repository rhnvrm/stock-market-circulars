## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the equity shares of Seemax Resources Limited (Scrip Code: 544813) will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment, effective Tuesday, July 21, 2026. This is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20260706-30 dated Monday, July 06, 2026. Following the transfer, dealings in the company's equity shares will be shifted to M Group.

## Key Points

- Scrip: Seemax Resources Limited, Scrip Code 544813, listed on the SME segment
- Shares currently trade in Trade for Trade segment under MT Group
- Shares will move to Rolling segment under M Group effective July 21, 2026
- This notice follows up on an earlier Exchange Notice No. 20260706-30 dated July 06, 2026
- Queries can be directed to Mr. Anurag Jain (Tel: 022-2272 8822)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework changes; this is an operational update reclassifying the trading group/segment for a specific SME-listed security in accordance with prior Exchange notice.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must update their systems and processes to reflect the change in trading segment and group for Seemax Resources Limited effective the transition date. No other filing or compliance action is required from the company itself as per this notice.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 07, 2026
- Prior related Notice: July 06, 2026 (No. 20260706-30)
- Effective Date of Segment Change: Tuesday, July 21, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The shift from Trade for Trade (MT Group) to Rolling settlement (M Group) generally indicates improved trading liquidity conditions and eases settlement mechanics for the stock, moving it from a more restrictive trade-for-trade regime to normal rolling settlement. This is a positive operational development for shareholders and traders of Seemax Resources Limited, though the overall market impact is limited to this single SME-listed company.
