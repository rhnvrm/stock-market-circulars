## Summary

BSE has announced the non-competitive bidding facility for the State Government Securities (SDL) auction scheduled on May 26, 2026, in continuation of the RBI press release dated May 22, 2026. Trading members can participate through the NCB-GSec module on BSE's iBBS platform. The auction covers 13 SDL securities across multiple Indian states.

## Key Points

- Bidding is available via the NCB-GSec module at https://ibbs.bseindia.com
- Bid collection begins May 25, 2026 from 10:00 am (24-hour availability for direct investors until 5:00 pm)
- Member bid submission closes May 26, 2026 at 8:00 am
- Auction Date: May 26, 2026; Settlement Date: May 27, 2026
- Minimum subscription amount is Rs. 10,000 for all securities; face value unit is Rs. 100
- This is in continuation of Exchange circular no. 20191122-28 (November 22, 2019) on the launch of the non-competitive bidding facility for SDLs

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice issued under the existing non-competitive bidding framework established via circular 20191122-28.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the NCB-GSec module on iBBS to submit bids on behalf of clients
- Members must ensure bids are submitted before May 26, 2026 at 8:00 am
- Direct investors must submit by May 25, 2026 at 5:00 pm
- Minimum bid amount: Rs. 10,000; bids must be in multiples of Rs. 100

## Impact Assessment

This circular is operationally relevant to debt market participants and trading members who facilitate client participation in SDL auctions. The auction covers 13 securities from 8 state issuers (Andhra Pradesh, Bihar, Chhattisgarh, Gujarat, Kerala, Madhya Pradesh, Uttar Pradesh, Uttarakhand) with aggregate issue sizes ranging from Rs. 500 Cr to Rs. 1,800 Cr per security, and a combined total exceeding Rs. 13,250 Cr. Both new issuances and re-issues are included, offering investment opportunities across maturities from 2034 to 2049. The bidding window is tight, particularly for members, who must act before 8:00 am on auction day.
