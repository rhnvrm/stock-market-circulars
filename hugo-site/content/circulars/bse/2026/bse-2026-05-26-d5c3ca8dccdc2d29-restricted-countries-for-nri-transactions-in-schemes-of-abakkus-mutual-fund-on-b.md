## Summary

BSE has notified members of a revised communication from ABAKKUS Mutual Fund regarding the restriction of NRI applications for their schemes on the BSE StAR MF Platform. Effective immediately, NRI investors from Canada will not be accepted for transactions in ABAKKUS Mutual Fund schemes per AMC instruction.

## Key Points

- This notice revises the earlier BSE Notice No. 20251204-44, which introduced ABAKKUS Mutual Fund on the BSE StAR MF Platform.
- ABAKKUS Mutual Fund has directed BSE to restrict NRI applications from investors belonging to Canada with immediate effect.
- Only one country is currently on the restricted list: Canada.
- The restriction applies to all schemes of ABAKKUS Mutual Fund transacted through the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Regulatory Changes

AMC (ABAKKUS Mutual Fund) has issued a revised instruction to BSE to block NRI investment applications originating from Canada. This updates the original onboarding terms established under Notice No. 20251204-44.

## Compliance Requirements

- BSE members and distributors must ensure that NRI applications from Canadian-resident investors are not processed for ABAKKUS Mutual Fund schemes on the BSE StAR MF Platform.
- The restriction is applicable with immediate effect from the date of this notice (26 May 2026).

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** 26 May 2026 (immediate effect)

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited in scope, affecting only NRI investors residing in Canada who wish to invest in ABAKKUS Mutual Fund schemes via the BSE StAR MF Platform. There is no broader market disruption. Members should update their application processing workflows to reject such transactions to remain compliant with the AMC's revised instructions.
