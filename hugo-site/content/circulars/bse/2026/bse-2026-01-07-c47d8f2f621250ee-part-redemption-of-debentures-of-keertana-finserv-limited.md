## Summary

KEERTANA FINSERV LIMITED has announced part redemption of its non-convertible debentures (ISIN: INE0NES07170, Code: 976653, Series: KFL-11.30%-30-01-27-PVT). Trading in these debentures will be conducted with a reduced face value effective January 15, 2026, following the record date set for the same day.

## Key Points

- Debenture series: KFL-11.30%-30-01-27-PVT (ISIN: INE0NES07170)
- Scrip code: 976653
- Record date for part redemption: January 15, 2026
- Reduced face value: Rs. 57,142.87 per debenture
- Trading with reduced face value effective from: January 15, 2026
- Settlement number: DR-799/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that trading in the debentures will be conducted with the reduced face value of Rs. 57,142.87 per debenture from the effective date
- Members should update their systems to reflect the reduced face value for settlement purposes

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: January 15, 2026
- **Effective Date for Reduced Face Value**: January 15, 2026
- **Settlement Number**: DR-799/2025-2026
- **Circular Issue Date**: January 07, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This part redemption affects holders of KEERTANA FINSERV LIMITED's non-convertible debentures (Series KFL-11.30%-30-01-27-PVT). The operational impact is limited to updating trading systems to reflect the reduced face value. Market impact is minimal as this is a private placement debenture with limited secondary market activity. Debenture holders as of the record date will receive partial redemption, and subsequent trading will reflect the reduced principal amount.
