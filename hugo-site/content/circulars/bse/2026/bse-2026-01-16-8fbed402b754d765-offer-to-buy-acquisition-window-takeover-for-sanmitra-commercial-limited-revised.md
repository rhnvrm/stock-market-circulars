## Summary

BSE has issued a revised live activities schedule for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) of SANMITRA COMMERCIAL LIMITED (ZSANMCOM). The offer is for up to 1,49,76,000 equity shares at Rs. 15.00 per share, running from January 6, 2026 to January 20, 2026. Trading Members and Custodian Members can facilitate bids through the iBBS and RTRMS platforms during specified session timings.

## Key Points

- Security: SANMITRA COMMERCIAL LIMITED (Symbol: ZSANMCOM, ISIN: INE896J01014, Scrip Code: 512062)
- Offer Size: Up to 1,49,76,000 equity shares
- Offer Price: Rs. 15.00 per share
- Face Value: Rs. 10.00
- Market Lot: 1 share
- Offer Period: January 6, 2026 to January 20, 2026
- Bid offering session: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM daily
- Give-up/Take-up available from January 6-19, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- Last day give-up: January 20, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- Last day take-up: January 20, 2026 (9:15 AM to 4:00 PM)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a revised schedule notification for an existing acquisition window process.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must ensure investor UCC is registered/updated with correct PAN number and category in Exchange UCC database
- Members can only bid for investors having shares in demat form
- Members must refer to Exchange Notices 20150930-1 (September 30, 2015) and 20170203-29 (February 3, 2017) for operational instructions
- Access OTB module via iBBS platform (Internet: https://ibbs.bseindia.com/ or Leased Line: https://ibbsll.bseindia.com/)
- Access RTRMS platform (Internet: https://rtrms.bseindia.com/ or Leased Line: https://rtrmsll.bseindia.com/)
- Files on Extranet for bid acceptance available after Exchange receives confirmation from Merchant Banker/RTA post-issue closure

## Important Dates

- **Opening Date**: Tuesday, January 6, 2026
- **Bid Offering Period**: January 6-20, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Give-up/Take-up Period**: January 6-19, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Last Day Give-up**: January 20, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Last Day Take-up**: January 20, 2026 (9:15 AM to 4:00 PM)
- **Closing Date**: Tuesday, January 20, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited market impact as it pertains only to shareholders of SANMITRA COMMERCIAL LIMITED who wish to participate in the takeover offer. The revised schedule provides updated timings and operational details for Trading Members and Custodian Members facilitating the acquisition window. Investor categories eligible include FII, FI, Mutual Funds, Insurance Companies, Other QIBs, Other NIBs, Companies, and Individuals. No securities acceptance will result in no End of Issue files being generated in Extranet.
