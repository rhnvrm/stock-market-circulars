## Summary

BSE has notified that 8,10,000 new equity shares of Mizzen Ventures Limited (Scrip Code: 531537) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Monday, June 22, 2026. The shares were issued to Promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 8,10,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 42/- (total issue price Rs. 52/-)
- Shares issued to Promoters on a preferential basis via warrant conversion
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE681K01026
- Distribution numbers: 21185001 to 21995000
- Date of Allotment: 30 March 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for newly allotted equity shares following preferential warrant conversion.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed of the new securities and their tradability from June 22, 2026. The newly listed shares carry a lock-in restriction applicable to the Promoters.

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: 30 March 2026
- Trading Effective From: Monday, 22 June 2026
- Lock-in Period: Distribution Nos. 21185001–21995000 locked in until 31 December 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 8,10,000 shares to the tradeable float of Mizzen Ventures Limited; however, all newly issued shares are under promoter lock-in until December 31, 2026, so there is no immediate increase in freely tradeable supply. Market impact is minimal and confined to existing shareholders of this scrip.
