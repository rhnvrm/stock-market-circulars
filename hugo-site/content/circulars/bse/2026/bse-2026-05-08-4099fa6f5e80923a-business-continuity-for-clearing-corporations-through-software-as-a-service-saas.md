## Summary

BSE's Indian Clearing Corporation Limited (ICCL) has issued a notice regarding a mock session to test business continuity arrangements for clearing corporations operating through Software as a Service (SaaS) mode. This is a preparedness exercise for ICCL members.

## Key Points

- Notice issued by ICCL under the Settlement/RMS category for the General segment
- Mock session designed to test business continuity for clearing corporations via SaaS mode
- Participation is intended for members of ICCL
- No attachments accompanying this notice

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an operational drill/mock session notice.

## Compliance Requirements

ICCL members are expected to participate in the mock session to validate their readiness for business continuity scenarios under SaaS-based clearing infrastructure. Specific participation instructions would be communicated separately.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 08 May 2026
- Mock session date/time: Not specified in the available content (refer to full PDF or subsequent communication from ICCL)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a scheduled mock/drill exercise to ensure operational resilience of clearing infrastructure. Only ICCL members involved in the mock session will be affected during the test window. No trading or settlement disruptions expected for the broader market.
