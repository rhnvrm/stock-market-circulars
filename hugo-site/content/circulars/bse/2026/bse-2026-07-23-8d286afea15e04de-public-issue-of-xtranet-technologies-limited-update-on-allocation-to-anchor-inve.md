## Summary

BSE has issued a notice forwarding an update on the allocation to anchor investors for the public issue of Xtranet Technologies Limited. This update, dated July 23, 2026, is in continuation of an earlier notice (No. 20260722-15) and was submitted by the company along with a supporting attachment (Anchor_1).

## Key Points

- The notice pertains to the ongoing public issue (IPO) of Xtranet Technologies Limited.
- It provides an updated disclosure on the allocation made to anchor investors.
- This follows up on the company's earlier anchor investor allocation notice dated July 22, 2026 (Notice No. 20260722-15).
- The notice was issued by BSE's Listing Business Relationship department and signed by Mangesh Tayde, Vice President.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine disclosure update forwarded by the exchange as part of the IPO anchor allocation process, not a regulatory or policy change.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific action is required from market participants. The notice is informational, disclosing updated anchor investor allocation details for the company's public issue as required under SEBI ICDR regulations.

## Important Dates

- July 22, 2026: Original anchor investor allocation notice (No. 20260722-15) issued.
- July 23, 2026: Updated anchor investor allocation notice issued.

## Impact Assessment

The update is primarily relevant to investors tracking the Xtranet Technologies Limited IPO, particularly institutional and anchor investors, as it reflects finalized allocation details ahead of listing. It has limited broader market impact but is a routine and necessary step in the IPO process, offering transparency on anchor book allocation.
