## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in debt securities of AVIOM India Housing Finance Private Limited (Scrip Code: 975877, ISIN: INE0E2307229) effective January 29, 2026. The suspension is due to the maturity of the 14% debt security on January 31, 2026.

## Key Points

- Debt security: AVIOM India Housing Finance Private Limited 14% bond
- ISIN: INE0E2307229
- Scrip Code: 975877
- Maturity Date: January 31, 2026
- Trading suspension effective: January 29, 2026
- Notice Reference: DR-808/2025-2026
- Notice Number: 20260128-9

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for debt security maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debt securities of AVIOM India Housing Finance Private Limited from January 29, 2026 onwards
- All trading members are required to take note of this suspension

## Important Dates

- **January 28, 2026**: Notice issued
- **January 29, 2026**: Trading suspension effective date
- **January 31, 2026**: Debt security maturity date

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action with minimal market impact. The suspension affects a single debt instrument of a private limited company approaching its scheduled maturity date. Trading members holding or dealing in these securities should complete any necessary transactions before the January 29, 2026 suspension date. No broader market implications expected.
