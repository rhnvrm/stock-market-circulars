## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members that the equity shares of Sedemac Mechatronics Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Bombay Stock Exchange. The exact date of listing and further security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Sedemac Mechatronics Limited equity shares are approved for listing on BSE
- Scrip Code: 544723
- Trading Symbol: SEDEMAC
- ISIN: INE00XB01019
- Exact listing date to be announced via a separate notice
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing admission notice under BSE's listing procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the upcoming listing of SEDEMAC and await a follow-up notice from BSE confirming the official listing date and detailed security information before initiating trades.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: March 10, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced via separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This circular signals the imminent listing of Sedemac Mechatronics Limited on BSE's equity segment. Impact is limited to market participants interested in new listings, particularly in the mechatronics/engineering sector. Trading members should monitor for the follow-up notice confirming the exact listing date and trading commencement details.
