## Summary

BSE has invoked the alternate trading venue mechanism for the Equity segment, making NSE exclusive scrips available for trading on BSE from 12:40 PM on January 10, 2026. This follows the earlier exchange notice 20260109-19 dated January 9, 2026.

## Key Points

- Alternate trading venue invoked for Equity segment
- NSE exclusive scrips now available for trading on BSE
- Trading begins from 12:40 PM on January 10, 2026
- References prior notice 20260109-19 dated January 9, 2026
- Notice issued by Head of Trading Operations and VP Trading Operations

## Regulatory Changes

Activation of the alternate trading venue mechanism allows scrips that are typically exclusive to NSE to be traded on BSE's platform, ensuring market continuity and providing an alternate venue for equity trading.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must:
- Note the availability of NSE exclusive scrips on BSE platform
- Ensure readiness to trade these scrips from the specified time
- Follow standard trading procedures for equity segment transactions

## Important Dates

- **January 9, 2026**: Original notice (20260109-19) issued
- **January 10, 2026**: Current notice issued
- **January 10, 2026, 12:40 PM**: NSE exclusive scrips available for trading on BSE

## Impact Assessment

The invocation of the alternate trading venue has significant market impact by ensuring trading continuity for NSE exclusive scrips. This mechanism provides market participants with an alternate platform for equity trading, maintaining market liquidity and operational resilience during potential disruptions to the primary trading venue.
