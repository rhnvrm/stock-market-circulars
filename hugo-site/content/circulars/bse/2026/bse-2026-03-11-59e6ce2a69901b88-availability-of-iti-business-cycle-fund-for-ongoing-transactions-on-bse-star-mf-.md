## Summary

BSE has informed its members that ITI Business Cycle Fund will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption, along with SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities on the BSE StAR MF Platform, effective March 12, 2026. This is a follow-up to Notice No. 20260212-38.

## Key Points

- ITI Business Cycle Fund reopens for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform from March 12, 2026
- Both Regular and Direct plans are available across Growth and IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal) variants
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities are also being made available
- Six scheme variants are covered under this notice

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notice regarding the availability of mutual fund schemes on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members beyond being aware of the fund's availability for transactions from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** March 12, 2026 — ITI Business Cycle Fund reopens for ongoing subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This notice restores normal transactional access to ITI Business Cycle Fund schemes on the BSE StAR MF Platform for investors and intermediaries. Members and distributors using the platform can resume processing subscription, redemption, and systematic transactions for these equity fund schemes from March 12, 2026.
