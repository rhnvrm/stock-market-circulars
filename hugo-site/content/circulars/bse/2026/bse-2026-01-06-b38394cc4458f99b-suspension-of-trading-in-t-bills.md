## Summary

BSE has issued a notice suspending trading in T-Bill 759CG2026 (Scrip Code: 800242, ISIN: IN0020150093) effective January 7, 2026, as the instrument is maturing on its redemption date. Trading members are advised not to deal with this T-Bill from the suspension date onwards.

## Key Points

- T-Bill 759CG2026 with ISIN IN0020150093 and Scrip Code 800242 is maturing
- Trading suspension effective from January 7, 2026
- Notice issued under reference DR-793/2025-2026
- All trading members must cease dealing in this instrument from the suspension date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine administrative action related to instrument maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in T-Bill 759CG2026 from January 7, 2026
- Members should update their systems to reflect the trading suspension
- Existing holdings will be settled as per normal maturity procedures

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 6, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date**: January 7, 2026
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: On or around January 7, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a single T-Bill reaching maturity, affecting only specific holders of this instrument.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members holding or intending to trade this specific T-Bill must adjust their portfolios accordingly. Redemption proceeds will be processed as per standard maturity procedures.

**Investor Impact**: Holders of T-Bill 759CG2026 will receive redemption proceeds on maturity date. No trading opportunity available from suspension date.
