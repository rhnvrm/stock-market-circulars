## Summary

BSE has announced the schedule for an Offer to Buy (Acquisition Window - Takeover) for shares of SHREE DIGVIJAY CEMENT COMPANY LTD. The offer will be conducted through BSE's iBBS platform from January 5, 2026 to January 16, 2026. The offer size is up to 3,85,43,837 equity shares at an offer price of Rs. 92.20 per share. Trading members and custodian members can participate through the designated BSE platforms during specified session timings.

## Key Points

- **Security Details**: SHREE DIGVIJAY CEMENT COMPANY LTD (Symbol: SHREDIGCEM, ISIN: INE232A01011, Scrip Code: 502180)
- **Offer Size**: Up to 3,85,43,837 equity shares
- **Offer Price**: Rs. 92.20 per share
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10.00
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Offer Period**: January 5, 2026 to January 16, 2026
- **Bid Offering Period**: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM (Jan 5-16, 2026)
- **Give-up/Take-up Period**: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM (Jan 5-15, 2026)
- **Last Day Give-up**: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM (Jan 16, 2026)
- **Last Day Take-up**: 9:15 AM to 4:00 PM (Jan 16, 2026)
- **Eligible Categories**: FII, FI, MF, IC, Other QIBs, Other NIB, Company, Individual

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular follows existing OTB operational framework as per Exchange Notices 20150930-1 (September 30, 2015) and 20170203-29 (February 3, 2017).

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members**: Must ensure investor UCC is registered/updated with correct PAN Number and Category in Exchange UCC database
- **Bidding Eligibility**: Trading members can only bid for investors having shares in demat form
- **Platform Access**: Members must use iBBS platform (Internet: https://ibbs.bseindia.com/ or Leased Line: https://ibbsll.bseindia.com/) and RTRMS (Internet: https://rtrms.bseindia.com/ or Leased Line: https://rtrmsll.bseindia.com/)
- **File Downloads**: Extranet files for bid acceptance available after Exchange receives confirmation from Merchant Banker/RTA post-issue closure
- **No Securities Accepted**: If no securities are accepted, no End of Issue files will be generated in Extranet
- **Leased Line Configuration**: Refer to Exchange Circular 20170503-12 for leased line URL access settings

## Important Dates

- **Opening Date**: Monday, January 5, 2026
- **Closing Date**: Friday, January 16, 2026
- **Bid Offering Period**: January 5-16, 2026 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Give-up/Take-up Period**: January 5-15, 2026 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Last Day Give-up**: January 16, 2026 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Last Day Take-up**: January 16, 2026 (9:15 AM - 4:00 PM)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - affects shareholders of SHREE DIGVIJAY CEMENT COMPANY LTD who wish to participate in the takeover offer. The offer size of 3.85+ crore shares represents a significant portion of shareholding.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members and custodian members need to ensure their systems and UCCs are properly configured for OTB participation. Investors holding shares in demat form can tender their shares through their trading members during the 10-day offer window.
