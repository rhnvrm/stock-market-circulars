## Summary

BSE has issued a notice announcing the opening of an Offer to Buy through the Acquisition Window mechanism for a takeover of ARCO LEASING LIMITED. Mr. Jitesh Kothari (Acquirer 1) and Mr. Atul Ramshankar Jaiswal (Acquirer 2), collectively the Acquirers, are making an open offer to public shareholders of the Target Company to acquire up to 27,74,970 fully paid-up equity shares (25.57% of the fully paid-up equity and voting share capital) at a price of Rs. 10.00 per equity share, payable in cash. The offer is made pursuant to Regulations 3(1) and 4 of the SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 2011, for substantial acquisition of equity shares and voting share capital accompanied with a change in control and management of the Target Company.

## Key Points

- Target Company: ARCO LEASING LIMITED
- Acquirers: Mr. Jitesh Kothari (Acquirer 1) and Mr. Atul Ramshankar Jaiswal (Acquirer 2)
- Offer price: Rs. 10.00 per equity share (cash)
- Shares to be acquired: up to 27,74,970 equity shares (face value Rs. 10.00 each)
- Stake represented: 25.57% of fully paid-up equity and voting share capital
- Mechanism: Acquisition Window for Tender Offers under Takeover, Buy Back and Delisting, per SEBI circulars CIR/CFD/POLICYCELL/1/2015 (April 13, 2015) and CFD/DCR2/CIR/P/2016/131 (Dec 09, 2016), and BSE notices dated Feb 10, 2017, April 24, 2019, May 28, 2020, Nov 02, 2020 and Aug 25, 2021
- Trigger: Regulations 3(1) and 4 of SEBI (SAST) Regulations, 2011, involving change in control and management
- Letter of Offer attached for market participants' review

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced; the notice implements the existing Revised Guidelines for the Mechanism for Acquisition of Shares through the Stock Exchange pursuant to Tender-Offers under Takeovers, Buy Back and Delisting, as previously issued by SEBI and BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members and Custodians must process the tender offer for ARCOLEA shares in accordance with the Revised Guidelines under SEBI circular CFD/DCR2/CIR/P/2016/131 and BSE notices no. 20170210-16 and 20190424-35. Market participants should refer to the attached Letter of Offer and details available on the BSE website for procedural compliance.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 02, 2026
- Offer Period: Friday, July 03, 2026 to Thursday, July 16, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The open offer represents a substantial acquisition of shares accompanied by a change in control and management of Arco Leasing Limited, which is significant for existing shareholders deciding whether to tender shares and for market participants tracking corporate control changes. The impact is limited in scope to this specific company and its shareholders, with standard operational implications for trading members and custodians facilitating the tender process through the Acquisition Window during the specified offer period.
