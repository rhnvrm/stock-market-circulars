## Summary

BSE has issued a notice under the Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LT-ASM) framework, imposing enhanced surveillance conditions on multiple securities effective May 26–29, 2026. This includes 100% margin requirements, lower price bands, and movement of securities across LT-ASM stages or out of the framework entirely.

## Key Points

- 100% margins will be applicable from May 29, 2026 on all open positions as of May 27, 2026 and all new positions created from May 29, 2026 onwards (Annexure I, Part A).
- Securities are being moved to higher stages within the LT-ASM framework effective May 26, 2026 (Annexure I, Part B).
- Lower Price Band will be applicable from May 26, 2026 for securities placed under the framework.
- Certain securities in LT-ASM will be transferred to T / XT / MT / TS Group effective May 29, 2026 (Annexure I, Part D).
- Securities moving out of the LT-ASM framework effective May 26, 2026 are listed in Annexure II.
- A consolidated list of all securities currently under the LT-ASM framework is provided in Annexure III.
- Shortlisting is based on XBRL submissions by listed companies and should not be construed as adverse action against the company.

## Regulatory Changes

This notice is issued with reference to the revised LT-ASM framework as updated through Exchange notices dated March 21, 2018; October 27, 2018; December 04, 2020; August 09, 2024; September 20, 2024; and June 04, 2021. The framework continues to evolve with periodic reviews of placed securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must maintain 100% margins on affected securities for open positions as of May 27, 2026 and all new positions from May 29, 2026.
- Trading members must adhere to applicable price band restrictions (Lower Price Band) from May 26, 2026 for securities entering or escalating within the framework.
- For securities moving out of the framework, the price band is reinstated to the pre-shortlisting level, unless the scrip is part of another surveillance measure.
- For clarifications, members may write to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **May 26, 2026**: Securities moved to higher LT-ASM stages (Annexure I Part B) and Lower Price Band becomes effective; securities exit LT-ASM framework (Annexure II).
- **May 27, 2026**: Reference date for open positions subject to 100% margins.
- **May 29, 2026**: 100% margins become effective (Annexure I Part A); securities transferred to T / XT / MT / TS Group (Annexure I Part D).

## Impact Assessment

The LT-ASM framework significantly restricts trading activity for affected securities. The 100% margin requirement substantially raises the capital needed to hold or create positions, reducing leveraged participation. The lower price band limits downside movement, reducing liquidity. Securities moved to T / XT / MT / TS group face further trade restrictions. Collectively, these measures reduce speculative trading in flagged scrips and may impact price discovery and liquidity for affected stocks.
