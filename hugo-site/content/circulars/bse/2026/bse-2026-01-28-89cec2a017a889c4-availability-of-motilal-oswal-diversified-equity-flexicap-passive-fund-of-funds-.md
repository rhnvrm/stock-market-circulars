## Summary

BSE has announced that MOTILAL OSWAL DIVERSIFIED EQUITY FLEXICAP PASSIVE FUND OF FUNDS will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform effective January 29, 2026. This follows previous Notice No. 20260101-31 and includes both Regular and Direct plan options with GROWTH variants. The reopening also enables SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities.

## Key Points

- Fund reopening effective from January 29, 2026
- Two scheme variants available: Regular Plan (ISIN: INF247L01GM7) and Direct Plan (ISIN: INF247L01GL9)
- Both schemes fall under EQUITY category
- All transaction facilities restored: subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Available on BSE StAR MF Platform
- References previous Notice No. 20260101-31

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding the resumption of transactions for specific mutual fund schemes.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members. This is an informational notice about fund availability.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 28, 2026
- **Effective Date**: January 29, 2026 - Reopening for ongoing transactions including SIP, STP, and SWP facilities

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This notice affects only investors interested in Motilal Oswal's specific fund of funds scheme.

**Operational Impact**: Low. BSE members and distributors can now process transactions for these schemes through BSE StAR MF Platform. Investors who were waiting to transact in these schemes can now resume their investment activities.

**Investor Impact**: Positive for existing and prospective investors in these specific schemes, as they regain access to full transaction capabilities including systematic investment options.
