## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 91,887 new equity shares of Ugro Capital Ltd (Scrip Code: 511742, ISIN: INE583D01011) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Monday, May 25, 2026. These shares were issued to Non-Promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to the conversion of Compulsory Convertible Debentures (CCDs).

## Key Points

- 91,887 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 175/- (issue price Rs. 185/-)
- Issued to Non-Promoters on a preferential basis via CCD conversion
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 155196437 to 155288323
- Date of allotment: February 11, 2026
- Trading permitted from May 25, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification pursuant to conversion of CCDs into equity shares.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to update their records. These shares are subject to a lock-in period and cannot be freely traded until the lock-in expires.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** February 11, 2026
- **Listing / Trading Effective Date:** May 25, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry:** August 1, 2026 (for all 91,887 shares, Dist. Nos. 155196437 to 155288323)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The issuance of 91,887 shares represents a small incremental addition to Ugro Capital Ltd's share capital. The shares are subject to lock-in until August 1, 2026, limiting near-term trading liquidity of this tranche. No disruption to existing shareholders or trading operations is anticipated.
