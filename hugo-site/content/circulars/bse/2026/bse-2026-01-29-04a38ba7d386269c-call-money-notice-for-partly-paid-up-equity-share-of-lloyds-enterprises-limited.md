## Summary

BSE has activated the Call Money Module on its IBBS platform for Lloyds Enterprises Limited to collect the first and final call money on partly paid-up equity shares. The issue opens on February 2, 2026 and closes on February 16, 2026. Shareholders holding partly paid-up equity shares must pay Rs. 19.50 per share during the 15-day issue period. The registrar for the issue is Bigshare Services Private Limited.

## Key Points

- First and final call on 25,44,25,324 partly paid-up equity shares of Lloyds Enterprises Limited
- Call money amount: Rs. 19.50 per partly paid-up equity share
- Market lot: 1 share
- Bidding hours: 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM during the issue period
- Self-Certified Syndicate Banks (SCSBs) and Trading Members with 3-in-1 accounts can submit call money details
- Process follows SEBI circular dated December 8, 2020 and BSE notice no. 20210104-37 dated January 4, 2021

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice implements existing framework established by:
- BSE Exchange notice no. 20210104-37 dated January 4, 2021
- SEBI circular dated December 8, 2020 regarding Call Money Module procedures

## Compliance Requirements

**For Shareholders:**
- Holders of partly paid-up equity shares must pay Rs. 19.50 per share during the issue period
- Payment must be made through SCSBs or Trading Members with 3-in-1 accounts

**For Trading Members and SCSBs:**
- Must submit details of call money notices collected during the issue period through the IBBS platform
- Need to ensure proper processing of shareholder payments

**For Intermediaries:**
- Must note the activation of Call Money Module and assist in the process as required

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** January 29, 2026
- **Issue Opening Date:** Monday, February 2, 2026
- **Issue Closing Date:** Monday, February 16, 2026
- **Issue Period:** 15 days
- **Bidding Hours:** 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM on each trading day

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - affects specific shareholders of Lloyds Enterprises Limited holding partly paid-up shares

**Operational Impact:** Shareholders must arrange payment of Rs. 19.50 per share, representing the final call on partly paid-up equity shares. Total call money to be collected: approximately Rs. 49.61 crores (25,44,25,324 shares Ã— Rs. 19.50)
