## Summary

BSE has published the list of securities to be auctioned in Settlement No. 812 dated February 5, 2026. The auction includes 140 scrips with varying quantities ranging from 1 share to 60,000 shares across multiple sectors.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 812
- Settlement Date: February 5, 2026
- Total number of scrips: 140 (70 pairs listed in the circular)
- Quantities range from 1 share (multiple scrips) to 60,000 shares (STEEL AUTHORITY)
- Covers diverse sectors including financial services, power, infrastructure, metals, textiles, and technology

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard auction settlement notice.

- Participants involved in the auction settlement must ensure delivery of securities as per BSE auction settlement procedures
- Settlement participants must comply with the specified quantities and settlement timelines

## Important Dates

- **Settlement Date:** February 5, 2026
- **Settlement Number:** 812

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine demat auction settlement with limited market-wide impact. The auction is designed to resolve delivery failures and ensure settlement integrity. While it includes major stocks like Reliance Industries (25 shares), Bajaj Finance (20 shares), and HCL Tech (4,591 shares), the quantities are relatively small compared to daily trading volumes. The highest quantity is for STEEL AUTHORITY at 60,000 shares. Individual stocks included in the auction may experience minor price movements, but overall market impact is expected to be minimal.
