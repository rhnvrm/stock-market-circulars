## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that Anchor Investors (AIs) have subscribed to 12,74,000 equity shares in the public issue of Atharva Poly-Plast Limited at a price of Rs. 60/- per equity share. The allocation took place on June 29, 2026, as part of the company's initial public offering process.

## Key Points

- 12,74,000 equity shares allocated to Anchor Investors in the public issue of Atharva Poly-Plast Limited
- Anchor investor price set at Rs. 60/- per equity share
- Allocation completed on June 29, 2026
- Notice issued by Listing Business Relationship department under Notice No. 20260629-45
- Company's Anchor Intimation Letter enclosed as attachment

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard disclosure pursuant to SEBI requirements for public issues mandating disclosure of anchor investor allocations prior to the public issue opening.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the anchor investor allocation as part of standard IPO process disclosures. No specific compliance action required from trading members; the notice serves as an informational circular.

## Important Dates

- **Anchor Investor Allocation Date:** June 29, 2026
- **Announcement Date:** June 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine IPO-related disclosure with minimal broader market impact. The anchor investor allocation of 12,74,000 shares at Rs. 60/- signals institutional interest in Atharva Poly-Plast Limited's public issue. The anchor investor tranche is typically a precursor to the public subscription window opening. Impact is limited to prospective retail and institutional investors considering participation in this specific IPO.
