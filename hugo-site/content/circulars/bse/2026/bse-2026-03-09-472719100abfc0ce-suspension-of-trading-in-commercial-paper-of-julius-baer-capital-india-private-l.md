## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members that the commercial paper of Julius Baer Capital (India) Private Limited (Scrip Code: 730732, ISIN: INE824H14TA3) will mature on its redemption date and trading in the same is suspended effective March 10, 2026.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of Julius Baer Capital (India) Private Limited (JBCIPL-11-03-26-CP) is maturing as per its scheduled redemption date.
- Scrip Code: 730732; ISIN: INE824H14TA3.
- Trading members are advised not to deal in this commercial paper from March 10, 2026 onwards.
- Reference: DR-836/2025-2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice regarding the maturity and consequent suspension of trading in a specific debt instrument.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in the commercial paper of Julius Baer Capital (India) Private Limited (Scrip Code: 730732, ISIN: INE824H14TA3) with effect from March 10, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **March 09, 2026**: Date of circular issuance.
- **March 10, 2026**: Effective date of suspension of trading in the commercial paper (redemption date).
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: March 11, 2026 (as indicated by the instrument name JBCIPL-11-03-26-CP).

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited and routine. This notice affects only one specific commercial paper instrument (Scrip Code: 730732) of Julius Baer Capital (India) Private Limited. Trading members are required to stop dealing in this instrument from March 10, 2026, ahead of its maturity. No broader market or systemic impact is anticipated.
