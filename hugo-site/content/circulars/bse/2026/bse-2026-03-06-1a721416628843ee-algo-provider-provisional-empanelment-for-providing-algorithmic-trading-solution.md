## Summary

BSE has provisionally empanelled Stratzy Fintech Private Limited as an Algo Provider authorised to offer Algorithmic Trading Solutions to BSE trading members. The circular is issued under Notice No. 20260306-5 and takes effect immediately.

## Key Points

- Stratzy Fintech Private Limited has been granted provisional empanelment by BSE to provide Algorithmic Trading Solutions.
- Vendor address: Flat No 1501, Center Point, Mulund East, Mumbai – 400081.
- Contact: +91 8291113955.
- The updated list of all empanelled Algo Providers is available on the BSE website.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This circular updates the list of provisionally empanelled Algo Providers in line with existing BSE guidelines for algorithmic trading solutions.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members wishing to use Stratzy Fintech Private Limited's algorithmic trading solutions may now engage with the vendor under the existing framework for empanelled Algo Providers. No new compliance obligations are imposed by this circular.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** 06 March 2026 (immediate effect)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is an administrative update expanding the pool of approved Algo Providers available to BSE trading members. Trading members gain an additional vendor option (Stratzy Fintech Private Limited) for algorithmic trading solutions, but no mandatory action is required.
