## Summary

BSE has admitted Algoquant Global Securities Private Limited as a new Trading Member with Member Number 6975. The entity has been granted trading rights in the Cash segment under a New Membership arrangement, subject to BSE's Rules, Bye-laws and Regulations.

## Key Points

- New trading member admitted: Algoquant Global Securities Private Limited
- Member Number: 6975
- Segment: Cash
- Membership Type: New Membership
- Trading rights limited to BSE Ltd. only
- Admission subject to Exchange Rules, Bye-laws and Regulations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine membership admission notification.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for existing trading members. The new member must comply with all applicable BSE Rules, Bye-laws and Regulations.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: January 2, 2026
- Notice Number: 20260102-16

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative notification with minimal market impact. The admission of a new trading member expands the participant base but does not affect trading operations, market structure, or existing members' obligations. Trading members requiring clarification can contact membership.ops@bseindia.com.
