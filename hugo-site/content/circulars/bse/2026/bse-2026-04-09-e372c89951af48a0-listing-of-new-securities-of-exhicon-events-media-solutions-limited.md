## Summary

BSE has listed 3,76,000 new equity shares of Exhicon Events Media Solutions Limited (Scrip Code: 543895, ISIN: INE0O8901016) effective Friday, April 10, 2026. The shares were issued at a premium of Rs. 305/- (issue price Rs. 315/- per share of face value Rs. 10/-) to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 3,76,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each listed with effect from April 10, 2026
- Issued at a premium of Rs. 305/- per share; issue price Rs. 315/-
- Issued to Non-Promoters on a preferential basis via conversion of warrants
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 22966001 to 23342000
- All 3,76,000 shares are subject to lock-in until October 29, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notice pursuant to a preferential allotment already approved and allotted.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed that the new securities are permitted to trade from April 10, 2026
- Lock-in restrictions apply: all 3,76,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 22966001–23342000) are locked in until October 29, 2026 and cannot be traded until that date

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** December 17, 2025
- **Listing/Trading Effective Date:** April 10, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry Date:** October 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing of these shares increases the tradeable float of Exhicon Events Media Solutions Limited marginally. However, since all newly listed shares are under lock-in until October 29, 2026, there is no immediate increase in free-float. Market impact is expected to be minimal. The preferential allotment at Rs. 315/- per share reflects the company's capital-raising activity through warrant conversion, which was completed in December 2025.
