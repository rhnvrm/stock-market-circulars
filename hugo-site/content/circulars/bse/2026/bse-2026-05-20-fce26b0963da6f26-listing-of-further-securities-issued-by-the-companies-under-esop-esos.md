## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that further new securities issued by 14 companies under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP/ESOS) are listed and admitted for trading on the exchange with effect from Thursday, May 21, 2026. The issuances represent routine dilution from employee compensation schemes across sectors including banking, finance, energy, and manufacturing.

## Key Points

- 14 companies have listed new ESOP/ESOS shares effective May 21, 2026
- HDFC Bank has the largest issuance at 796,290 shares (face value ₹1)
- Anand Rathi Share and Stock Brokers issued 301,050 shares with a lock-in until April 13, 2027
- Arman Financial Services issued 1,000 shares with a lock-in until May 11, 2027
- Suzlon Energy issued 195,000 shares (face value ₹2)
- Two companies (Anand Rathi and Arman Financial Services) have lock-in restrictions on newly listed shares
- All other issuances carry no lock-in (NA)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice under existing ESOP/ESOS listing framework.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are requested to take note of the newly listed securities and update their systems accordingly. No additional compliance action is required from market participants beyond acknowledgment.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date for Trading:** May 21, 2026 (Thursday)
- **Lock-in Expiry – Anand Rathi Share And Stock Brokers Limited:** April 13, 2027
- **Lock-in Expiry – Arman Financial Services Limited:** May 11, 2027
