## Summary

BSE has listed 33,01,886 new equity shares of KRN Heat Exchanger And Refrigeration Limited (Scrip Code: 544263, ISIN: INE0Q3J01015) allotted to Qualified Institutional Buyers (QIBs) under a Qualified Institutional Placement (QIP) at an issue price of Rs. 1060/- per share. These shares are admitted for trading on the Exchange with effect from Thursday, June 4, 2026.

## Key Points

- 33,01,886 equity shares of face value Rs. 10/- each allotted to QIBs under QIP
- Issue price: Rs. 1060/- per share
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Scrip Code: 544263 | ISIN: INE0Q3J01015
- Distribution range: Folio Nos. 62156601 to 65458486

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice for newly issued securities pursuant to a completed QIP.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members may contact Ms. Akshata Mhatre at Tel. No. 022 2272 5042 for any clarifications regarding this notice.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** June 1, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** June 4, 2026 (Thursday)

## Impact Assessment

The listing of approximately 33 lakh new shares at Rs. 1060/- represents a capital raise of roughly Rs. 350 crore via QIP. The new shares are pari-passu with existing equity, resulting in dilution for existing shareholders. Trading members should update their records to reflect the revised share capital of KRN Heat Exchanger And Refrigeration Limited effective June 4, 2026.
