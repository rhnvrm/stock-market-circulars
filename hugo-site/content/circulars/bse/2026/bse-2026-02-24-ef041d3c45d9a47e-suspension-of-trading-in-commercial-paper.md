## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members that the Commercial Paper (CP) of Solar Defence and Aerospace Limited (Scrip Code: 730672, ISIN: INE02AL14023) will mature on February 25, 2026. Trading in this commercial paper is suspended effective February 25, 2026, under reference DR-828/2025-2026.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper SDAL-26-2-26-CP of Solar Defence and Aerospace Limited is set to mature on February 25, 2026.
- Scrip Code: 730672; ISIN: INE02AL14023.
- Trading members are advised not to deal in this CP from February 25, 2026 onwards.
- Notice issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President â€“ Listing Operations (CRD).

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine administrative action pursuant to the maturity/redemption of a commercial paper instrument on the BSE Debt Segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all trading activity in the Commercial Paper (ISIN: INE02AL14023, Scrip Code: 730672) of Solar Defence and Aerospace Limited with effect from February 25, 2026.
- Members should update their systems and alert their clients accordingly to avoid any inadvertent transactions.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** February 24, 2026
- **Suspension / Redemption Date:** February 25, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited and routine. This circular affects only trading members and investors holding the specific commercial paper (SDAL-26-2-26-CP) of Solar Defence and Aerospace Limited. Upon maturity, holders will receive redemption proceeds. There is no broader market or systemic impact.
