## Summary

Bombay Stock Exchange has published the demat auction list for Settlement No. 807 dated January 29, 2026. The auction includes 145 scrips with varying quantities available for auction, ranging from single shares to thousands of shares across different companies.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 807
- Auction Date: January 29, 2026
- Total number of scrips: 145
- Highest quantity: Deep Diamond (606,014 shares)
- Lowest quantity: Multiple scrips with 1 share each
- Covers diverse sectors including banking, automobile, energy, manufacturing, and technology

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard operational auction notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must participate in the demat auction for the listed securities
- Settlement must be completed as per BSE auction settlement procedures
- Members holding short positions in the listed scrips must fulfill obligations through auction

## Important Dates

- Auction Date: January 29, 2026
- Settlement No: 807

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - Demat auctions are routine settlement mechanisms to cover short deliveries. The quantities involved are relatively small for most scrips and represent normal market operations.

**Trading Impact**: Minimal - Only affects members with outstanding delivery obligations in the listed securities. The auction mechanism ensures orderly settlement without disrupting regular trading.

**Notable Quantities**: Deep Diamond (606,014 shares), OMAX Autos (18,100 shares), IDFC First Bank (18,550 shares), and Banganga Paper (12,512 shares) have the highest auction quantities, which may indicate significant delivery failures in these specific securities.
