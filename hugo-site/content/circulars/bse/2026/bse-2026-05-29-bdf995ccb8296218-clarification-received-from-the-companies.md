## Summary

BSE's Surveillance Department (DOSS) issued Notice No. 20260529-25 on 29 May 2026 regarding significant price/volume movement observed in 12 listed scrips. The Exchange had sought clarifications from these companies to ensure trading members and investors have access to the latest relevant information. Replies have been received from 7 companies and are attached to the notice; replies from the remaining 5 companies are still awaited.

## Key Points

- BSE observed significant price/volume movement in 12 listed companies and sought clarifications
- Clarifications have been received from 7 companies and are attached to the circular
- 5 companies have not yet responded to the Exchange's query
- The notice is issued by the Surveillance department to ensure market transparency

## Companies That Replied

1. Artificial Electronics Intelligent Material Ltd (Scrip Code: 526443)
2. Global Ocean Logistics India Ltd (Scrip Code: 544665)
3. Granules India Ltd (Scrip Code: 532482)
4. New Delhi Television Ltd (Scrip Code: 532529)
5. Sportking India Ltd (Scrip Code: 539221)
6. Usha Martin Ltd (Scrip Code: 517146)
7. ZR2 Bioenergy Ltd (Scrip Code: 506640)

## Companies with Reply Awaited

1. Cargotrans Maritime Ltd (Scrip Code: 543618)
2. Elango Industries Ltd (Scrip Code: 513452)
3. Nexxus Petro Industries Ltd (Scrip Code: 544265)
4. Spice Lounge Food Works Ltd (Scrip Code: 539895)
5. York Exports Ltd (Scrip Code: 530675)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an informational notice from BSE Surveillance as part of standard market oversight procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies that have not yet responded (Cargotrans Maritime, Elango Industries, Nexxus Petro Industries, Spice Lounge Food Works, York Exports) are expected to submit their clarifications to the Exchange promptly.
- Trading members and investors are advised to review the attached replies from the 7 companies that have responded before making investment decisions in these scrips.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 29 May 2026
- No specific deadline mentioned for pending replies

## Impact Assessment

This notice is part of BSE's standard surveillance mechanism to maintain market integrity. Investors holding or considering positions in the 12 mentioned scrips should review the clarifications provided by companies. The absence of a reply from 5 companies may indicate continued uncertainty around the price/volume movements observed in those scrips. No trading restrictions have been imposed as part of this notice.
