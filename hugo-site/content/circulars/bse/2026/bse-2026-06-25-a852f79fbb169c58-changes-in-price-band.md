## Summary

BSE's Surveillance Department (DOSS) has revised price bands for 30 equity scrips effective June 29, 2026. The new bands range from 2% to 20%, replacing the previously applicable limits for each scrip. Trading members are advised to take note of the revised limits before market open on that date.

## Key Points

- Price bands revised for 30 scrips across the equity segment effective June 29, 2026.
- Revised bands are set at 2%, 5%, 10%, or 20% depending on the scrip.
- Action initiated by BSE Surveillance Department (DOSS) under Notice No. 20260625-45.
- Six scrips are placed under the narrowest 2% band: Citizen Infoline Ltd, Filtron Engineers Ltd, Neelkanth Rockminerals Ltd, Ramchandra Leasing & Finance Ltd, Seshachal Technologies Ltd, and Zim Laboratories Ltd.
- Three scrips receive the widest 20% band: Dev Labtech Venture Ltd, Hexagon Nutrition Ltd, and Innovative Ideals and Services (India) Ltd.
- Clarifications can be directed to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Regulatory Changes

Price bands for the listed scrips are being reset from their existing levels to new percentages as part of BSE's ongoing surveillance process. This is a standard regulatory tool used to control excess volatility and protect market integrity in specific securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must:
- Update order management systems to reflect the revised price bands for each of the 30 scrips before June 29, 2026.
- Ensure client orders placed on or after June 29, 2026 comply with the new band limits.
- Contact bse.surv@bseindia.com for any clarifications prior to the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 25, 2026
- **Effective Date:** June 29, 2026 (revised price bands apply from market open)

## Impact Assessment

The revision affects 30 scrips, predominantly small and micro-cap companies. Tightening to 2% bands for six scrips (e.g., Citizen Infoline, Filtron Engineers, Zim Laboratories) signals heightened surveillance concern and will significantly restrict intraday price movement, potentially reducing liquidity. Widening to 20% for three scrips (Dev Labtech, Hexagon Nutrition, Innovative Ideals) relaxes constraints and may allow greater price discovery. Traders and investors holding positions in any of these 30 scrips should review their strategies ahead of June 29, 2026.
