## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (No. 20260224-11) informing trading members that the commercial paper of Solar Defence and Aerospace Limited (scrip code 730672), identified as SDAL-26-2-26-CP with ISIN INE02AL14023, is set to mature on February 25, 2026. Trading members are directed not to deal in this commercial paper effective February 25, 2026.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper scrip code **730672** (SDAL-26-2-26-CP) of **Solar Defence and Aerospace Limited** will reach its redemption date on **February 25, 2026**.
- ISIN: **INE02AL14023**
- Trading in this commercial paper is suspended with effect from **February 25, 2026**.
- Reference number: DR-828/2025-2026.
- Notice issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President â€“ Listing Operations (CRD).

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard administrative action triggered by the maturity of a commercial paper instrument.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must **not deal** in the commercial paper of Solar Defence and Aerospace Limited (ISIN: INE02AL14023, Scrip Code: 730672) from February 25, 2026 onwards.
- Members should update their systems and advise clients accordingly to avoid any erroneous transactions in this instrument.

## Important Dates

- **February 24, 2026**: Notice issued.
- **February 25, 2026**: Redemption/maturity date of the commercial paper; trading suspension takes effect.

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact. It pertains solely to the scheduled maturity of a single commercial paper instrument (SDAL-26-2-26-CP) issued by Solar Defence and Aerospace Limited. Upon maturity, the instrument ceases to trade as a matter of course. Investors holding this paper will receive redemption proceeds as per the terms of the instrument. No broader market or operational disruption is expected.
