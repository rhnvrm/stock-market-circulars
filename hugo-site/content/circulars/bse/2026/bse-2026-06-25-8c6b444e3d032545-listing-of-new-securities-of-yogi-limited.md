## Summary

BSE has listed 18,40,000 new equity shares of YOGI LIMITED (Scrip Code: 511702, ISIN: INE290E01011) issued to Promoters on a preferential basis through conversion of warrants. These shares are permitted for trading on the Exchange effective Monday, June 29, 2026.

## Key Points

- 18,40,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 22/- (issue price Rs. 32/- per share)
- Shares allotted to Promoters on a preferential basis via warrant conversion
- Distribution numbers: 43160001 to 45000000
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- All 18,40,000 shares are subject to lock-in until December 27, 2027

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice permitting newly allotted preferential shares to be traded on the BSE Equity segment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed that these securities are now listed and available for trading. The lock-in restriction applies to all 18,40,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 43160001 to 45000000) until December 27, 2027, meaning the Promoters cannot sell or transfer these shares before that date.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** April 2, 2026
- **Trading Effective From:** June 29, 2026 (Monday)
- **Lock-in Expiry:** December 27, 2027

## Impact Assessment

This listing adds 18,40,000 shares to the tradable float of YOGI LIMITED; however, since all newly issued shares are under promoter lock-in until December 2027, there is no immediate increase in freely tradable supply. Market impact is minimal in the short term.
