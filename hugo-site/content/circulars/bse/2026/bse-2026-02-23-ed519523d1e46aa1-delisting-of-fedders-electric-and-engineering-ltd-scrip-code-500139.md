## Summary

BSE has announced the delisting of Fedders Electric and Engineering Ltd (Scrip Code: 500139) from the Exchange effective Monday, March 02, 2026. The delisting follows the approval of a Resolution Plan by the Hon'ble National Company Law Tribunal (NCLT), Allahabad Bench, vide its order dated October 16, 2025. The company has confirmed compliance with all NCLT requirements for delisting.

## Key Points

- Fedders Electric and Engineering Ltd (Scrip Code: 500139) is being delisted from BSE.
- Delisting is pursuant to a Resolution Plan approved by NCLT, Allahabad Bench, on October 16, 2025.
- The company has confirmed full compliance with the NCLT delisting order requirements.
- Effective date of delisting from BSE records: **Monday, March 02, 2026**.
- Trading Members are requested to take note of the delisting.

## Regulatory Changes

The delisting is mandated under a court-approved Resolution Plan by the NCLT under the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code (IBC) framework. This is a compulsory/regulatory delisting, not a voluntary one initiated by the company's shareholders.

- **October 16, 2025**: NCLT Allahabad Bench approved the Resolution Plan.

- **March 02, 2026**: Effective date of delisting from BSE records.

## Impact Assessment

This delisting has a high impact on existing shareholders and traders holding positions in Fedders Electric and Engineering Ltd (Scrip Code: 500139). Post March 02, 2026, the shares will no longer be tradable on BSE. Investors holding the scrip should be aware that liquidity via the exchange will cease. The NCLT-driven nature of this delisting indicates the company underwent insolvency proceedings, which may have implications for shareholder recovery depending on the terms of the Resolution Plan.
