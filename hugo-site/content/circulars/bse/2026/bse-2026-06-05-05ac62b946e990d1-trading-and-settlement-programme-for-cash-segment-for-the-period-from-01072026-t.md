## Summary

BSE (via ICCL) has released the trading and settlement programme for the Cash (Equity) segment for the period from 01 July 2026 to 31 July 2026. The schedule covers both the T+1 and T+0 settlement cycles applicable to equity trades.

## Key Points

- Published by ICCL (Indian Clearing Corporation Limited), the clearing arm of BSE.
- Covers the full month of July 2026 (01.07.2026 to 31.07.2026).
- Applicable to the Cash (Equity) segment on BSE.
- Details trading days and corresponding settlement dates under T+1 and T+0 cycles.
- Attachment reference: CASH072026.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a routine periodic notice publishing the settlement calendar for the upcoming month.

## Compliance Requirements

- Brokers, trading members, and clearing members should refer to the attached schedule (CASH072026) to plan funds and securities obligations for July 2026.
- Pay-in and pay-out obligations must be met on the settlement dates specified in the programme.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Period:** 01 July 2026 to 31 July 2026
- **Notice Date:** 05 June 2026
- Specific trading and settlement dates are detailed in the attachment CASH072026.

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard operational notice with moderate impact on market participants. All equity segment participants — brokers, custodians, and clearing members — must align their settlement processes with the published schedule. The inclusion of both T+1 and T+0 cycles reflects the dual-settlement framework currently operative on BSE for eligible equity securities.
