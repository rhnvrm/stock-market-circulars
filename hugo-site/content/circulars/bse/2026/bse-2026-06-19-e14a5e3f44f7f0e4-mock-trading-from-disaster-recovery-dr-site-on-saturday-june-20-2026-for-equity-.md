## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session from its Disaster Recovery (DR) site for the Equity Derivatives segment on Saturday, June 20, 2026. The session is intended to test system readiness and help trading members familiarise themselves with DR operations. Trades executed during the session carry no margin, pay-in, pay-out obligations or legal rights and liabilities.

## Key Points

- Mock trading will be conducted from BSE's DR site specifically for the Equity Derivatives segment.
- Trading members using third-party platforms (empanelled vendors) or in-house ETI API-based systems can use this session to test functionalities including risk-reduction mode and trading halt scenarios.
- Participation fulfils regulatory testing requirements per SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD1/DSAP/CIR/P/2020/234 dated November 24, 2020, and BSE Notice No. 20201201-22 dated December 1, 2020.
- Members may choose between the Mock Trading Session or UAT environment to meet their regulatory obligations.
- Mock trades will not create any margin obligations, pay-in/pay-out obligations, or rights and liabilities.
- Members are requested to submit feedback by 1:00 PM on the day of the session.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This notice operationalises existing requirements under SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD1/DSAP/CIR/P/2020/234 (November 24, 2020) regarding testing of software used in or related to Trading and Risk Management.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must participate either in the Mock Trading Session or the UAT environment to satisfy their periodic DR testing obligations.
- Members using third-party or in-house ETI API trading systems should test all relevant functionalities during the session.
- Feedback must be submitted to BSE by 1:00 PM on June 20, 2026, via the contact numbers or email IDs provided.

## Important Dates

- **June 20, 2026 (Saturday):** Mock Trading Session from DR site for Equity Derivatives segment.
- **Feedback deadline:** 1:00 PM on June 20, 2026.

**Session Timings:**

| Trading Session       | From      | To           |

| Log-in                | 08:15 AM  | 09:00 AM     |

## Impact Assessment

Operational impact is limited to Saturday, June 20, 2026. There is no financial or regulatory impact on market participants beyond the voluntary/mandatory participation in the DR mock session. No actual trades, margins, or settlement obligations will be generated. The session is purely for testing and system familiarisation.
