## Summary

BSE Limited, through Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd (ICCL), has issued a notice inviting trading members to submit offers for the purchase of securities under Demat Auction 605, pertaining to Rolling Settlement No. DR-605/2026-27. The auction session is scheduled for April 8, 2026, with pay-in due on April 9, 2026.

## Key Points

- Auction offer entry window: 2:00 PM to 2:45 PM on April 8, 2026
- Auction matching session: 2:45 PM to 3:00 PM on April 8, 2026
- Delivery statement and accepted auction offer reports: 3:00 PM to 3:30 PM on April 8, 2026
- Money statement and final reports: 3:35 PM to 5:30 PM on April 8, 2026
- Auction pay-in (securities): April 9, 2026 at 8:00 AM
- Settlement is executed through the Clearing Member of a Trading Member under the Uniform Membership structure
- The previous restriction on trading members offering securities in auctions for settlements where they had short delivered has been removed
- No corrections will be permitted for punching errors in securities code or rate

## Regulatory Changes

Following the implementation of the Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment, settlement is now done through the Clearing Member of a Trading Member. Additionally, the restriction that previously prevented trading members from offering securities in an auction for a settlement in which they had short delivered has been removed.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must submit auction offers via trading terminals within the prescribed session timings
- Members and Custodians must check their auction pay-in obligations and execute securities pay-in in depositories within depository cut-off timings
- Trading members must refer to the cutoff rate on the "Auction Offer Entry Screen" before submitting offers
- Trading members should review notices on Corporate Actions (Ex-Rights, Ex-Bonus, Ex-Split, etc.) issued by the respective departments
- Utmost care must be taken while entering securities code and rate; no corrections will be allowed post-submission

## Important Dates

- **April 8, 2026**: Auction session (offer entry, matching, delivery statements, money statements)
- **April 9, 2026, 8:00 AM**: Auction pay-in (securities) deadline

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice affecting trading members and custodians with obligations under Rolling Settlement DR-605/2026-27. The removal of the restriction on short-delivering members participating in the auction increases flexibility for trading members. Members with short delivery positions should ensure timely securities pay-in to avoid penalties. The operational window is tight, requiring prompt action on April 8-9, 2026.
