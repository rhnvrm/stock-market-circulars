## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (No. 20260310-50) directing trading members not to deal in the Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of Dishman Carbogen Amcis Limited effective March 12, 2026. The suspension is in advance of the record date set for redemption on exercise of put option.

## Key Points

- Trading in debentures of Dishman Carbogen Amcis Limited is suspended from March 12, 2026
- The specific debenture series affected: DCAL-10.04%-15-7-26-PVT (ISIN: INE385W07034, Scrip Code: 975834)
- Record date for redemption on exercise of put option is March 12, 2026
- Reference number: DR-838/2025-2026
- Notice issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President – Listing Compliance and Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice under existing BSE framework for debt segment corporate actions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in DCAL-10.04%-15-7-26-PVT debentures (INE385W07034 / Scrip Code 975834) from March 12, 2026 onwards
- All members are required to take note of the suspension and ensure compliance

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** March 10, 2026
- **Record Date / No Dealings From:** March 12, 2026
- **Purpose:** Redemption on Exercise of Put Option

## Impact Assessment

The trading suspension is limited to one specific NCD series of Dishman Carbogen Amcis Limited. Impact is confined to trading members and investors holding or intending to trade in this debenture. Existing holders should be aware that the instrument is being redeemed on the put option exercise date, and no secondary market transactions will be permitted from the record date.
