## Summary

BSE has listed 3,77,15,000 new equity shares of Tilaknagar Industries Ltd (Scrip Code: 507205) for trading effective Thursday, March 5, 2026. These shares were issued to Non Promoters on a preferential basis through conversion of warrants at an issue price of Rs. 382 per share (face value Rs. 10 + premium Rs. 372).

## Key Points

- **Quantity:** 3,77,15,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 382/- per share (Rs. 10 face value + Rs. 372 premium)
- **Issued to:** Non Promoters on a preferential basis
- **Mechanism:** Conversion of warrants
- **Ranking:** Pari-passu with existing equity shares
- **Distribution Numbers:** 208193751 to 245908750
- **ISIN:** INE133E01013
- **Date of Allotment:** November 20, 2025
- **Trading effective from:** March 5, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice pursuant to a preferential allotment under existing SEBI regulations governing warrant conversion and preferential issue norms.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed that the new securities are listed and permitted to trade from March 5, 2026.
- The entire allotment of 3,77,15,000 shares is subject to a lock-in period and cannot be traded until after September 4, 2026.

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Date of Allotment | November 20, 2025 |
| Trading Commencement | March 5, 2026 |
| Lock-in Expiry | September 4, 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 3,77,15,000 shares to the tradable float of Tilaknagar Industries Ltd, representing potential dilution for existing shareholders. However, since the entire new allotment is under lock-in until September 4, 2026, there will be no immediate increase in free-float or selling pressure until that date. The preferential allotment to Non Promoters at Rs. 382/- per share via warrant conversion signals institutional or strategic investor participation. Market impact post lock-in expiry will depend on prevailing stock price relative to the issue price.
