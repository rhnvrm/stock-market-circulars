## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 7,00,000 new equity shares of SEPC Limited (Scrip Code: 532945) will be available for trading from Friday, June 05, 2026. These shares arise from the conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up, issued on a rights basis at a face value of Re. 10/- each.

## Key Points

- 7,00,000 equity shares of SEPC Limited admitted for trading effective June 05, 2026
- Shares result from conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up (rights basis)
- Face value: Re. 10/- per share
- ISIN: INE964H01014
- Scrip Code: 532945
- Distinctive numbers: 1707680718 to 1708380717
- New shares are pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice for newly eligible shares following completion of the partly paid conversion process.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are permitted to trade in these new securities from June 05, 2026 onwards. No specific compliance action is required beyond normal trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 04, 2026
- **Trading Commencement:** June 05, 2026 (Friday)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The addition of 7,00,000 shares represents a small incremental increase in the tradable float of SEPC Limited. Since the new shares are pari-passu with existing equity, there is no differential pricing or rights associated with them. This is a routine operational listing following the rights issue conversion process.
