## Summary

KEERTANA FINSERV LIMITED has announced part redemption of its non-convertible debentures (ISIN: INE0NES07170, Code: 976653, Series: KFL-11.30%-30-01-27-PVT). Trading members are informed that trading in these debentures will commence with reduced face value from January 15, 2026.

## Key Points

- Part redemption of non-convertible debentures of KEERTANA FINSERV LIMITED
- ISIN: INE0NES07170
- Debenture Code: 976653
- Series: KFL-11.30%-30-01-27-PVT
- Reduced face value: Rs. 57,142.87 per debenture
- Record date: January 15, 2026
- Effective date: January 15, 2026
- Settlement number: DR-799/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that trading in the debentures will be conducted with reduced face value effective from January 15, 2026
- All trading systems and records must reflect the new reduced face value of Rs. 57,142.87 per debenture from the effective date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** January 7, 2026
- **Record Date:** January 15, 2026
- **Effective Date for Reduced Face Value:** January 15, 2026
- **Settlement:** DR-799/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action with limited market impact. The part redemption affects only holders of the specific debenture series (KFL-11.30%-30-01-27-PVT) of KEERTANA FINSERV LIMITED. The reduced face value will impact the trading and valuation of these debentures going forward. Trading members dealing with this security need to update their systems to reflect the new face value from the effective date.
