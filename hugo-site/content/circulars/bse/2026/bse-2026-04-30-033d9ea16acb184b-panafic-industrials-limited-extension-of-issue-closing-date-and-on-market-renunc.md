## Summary

BSE has notified trading members of an extension to the ongoing Rights Issue of Panafic Industrials Limited (continuation of Notice No. 20260422-10). The Issue Closing Date has been extended by two weeks from May 8, 2026 to May 22, 2026, and the Last Date for On-Market Renunciation of Rights Entitlements has correspondingly been extended from May 4, 2026 to May 18, 2026.

## Key Points

- Rights Issue closing date extended by 14 days, from Friday, May 8, 2026 to Friday, May 22, 2026
- Last date for On-Market Renunciation of Rights Entitlements extended from Monday, May 4, 2026 to Monday, May 18, 2026
- This notice supersedes timeline information in the earlier Notice No. 20260422-10
- Issue Opening Date remains unchanged at Friday, April 24, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular revises the operational timeline of an existing Rights Issue under BSE's Listing and Business Relationship framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must communicate the revised Rights Issue timelines to eligible shareholders of Panafic Industrials Limited
- Shareholders wishing to renounce their Rights Entitlements on-market must do so on or before Monday, May 18, 2026
- Shareholders must subscribe or renounce their rights on or before the new Issue Closing Date of Friday, May 22, 2026

## Important Dates

| Event | Original Date | Revised Date |
|---|---|---|
| Issue Opening Date | Friday, April 24, 2026 | Friday, April 24, 2026 (unchanged) |
| Last Date for On-Market Renunciation | Monday, May 4, 2026 | Monday, May 18, 2026 |
| Issue Closing Date | Friday, May 8, 2026 | Friday, May 22, 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

The extension provides eligible shareholders of Panafic Industrials Limited additional time (14 days) to decide whether to subscribe to or renounce their Rights Entitlements. This is relevant only to existing shareholders of the company and does not affect broader market operations. Trading members should update their records and inform affected clients of the revised schedule to avoid lapsed entitlements.
