## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of three new Treasury Bills (T-Bills) in the Debt Instruments segment (G Group) effective January 30, 2026. The T-Bills have tenors of 91 days, 182 days, and 364 days with maturity dates in April 2026, July 2026, and January 2027 respectively.

## Key Points

- Three T-Bills to be listed on January 30, 2026 in G Group - Debt Instruments
- 91-day T-Bill (ISIN: IN002025X430, Symbol: 91TB300426) maturing April 30, 2026
- 182-day T-Bill (ISIN: IN002025Y438, Symbol: 182TB30726) maturing July 30, 2026
- 364-day T-Bill (ISIN: IN002025Z435, Symbol: 364TB28127) maturing January 28, 2027
- All securities have a market lot size of 1
- Trading will not be available two working days prior to maturity/redemption date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for new government securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the trading restriction: securities will not be available for trading two working days before maturity date (T-2)
- T-2 calculation excludes bank holidays
- Trading members requiring clarification can contact BSE at 2272 8352/5753/8597

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date:** January 30, 2026
- **Maturity Dates:**
  - 91TB300426: April 30, 2026
  - 182TB30726: July 30, 2026
  - 364TB28127: January 28, 2027
- **Trading Cessation:** Two working days before respective maturity dates

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine administrative notification for listing of standard government treasury bills. Trading members should update their systems with the new scrip codes and ISIN numbers. The T-2 trading restriction before maturity is a standard operational requirement for debt instruments to facilitate settlement.
