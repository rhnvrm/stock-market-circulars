## Summary

BSE has notified members that GROWW ARBITRAGE FUND will reopen for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) facility on the BSE StAR MF Platform with effect from April 29, 2026. This follows the earlier suspension notice (Notice No. 20260407-34 dated April 07, 2026). Six scheme variants under both regular and direct plans are covered.

## Key Points

- GROWW ARBITRAGE FUND reopens for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform from April 29, 2026
- Reopening covers subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP facility
- Six scheme variants are available: Regular Plan (Growth, IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment) and Direct Plan (Growth, IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment)
- Fund was previously suspended per Notice No. 20260407-34 dated April 07, 2026
- All schemes are categorised under Equity segment

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational reinstatement of a previously suspended mutual fund on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. BSE members dealing in mutual funds on the StAR MF Platform should update their systems to reflect the availability of these schemes from April 29, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **April 07, 2026**: Original suspension notice issued (Notice No. 20260407-34)
- **April 28, 2026**: This notice issued (Notice No. 20260428-32)
- **April 29, 2026**: GROWW ARBITRAGE FUND reopens for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP on BSE StAR MF Platform

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This notice affects investors and distributors transacting in GROWW ARBITRAGE FUND via the BSE StAR MF Platform, restoring normal access for subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP registrations. The fund had been temporarily unavailable following the April 07 suspension notice; its reinstatement represents a return to normal operations with no broader market or regulatory implications.
