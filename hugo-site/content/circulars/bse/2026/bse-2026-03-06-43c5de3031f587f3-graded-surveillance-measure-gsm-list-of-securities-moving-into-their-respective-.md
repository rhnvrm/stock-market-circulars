## Summary

BSE has announced the movement of 2 securities into GSM Stage III under the Graded Surveillance Measure (GSM) framework as of March 6, 2026. The affected companies are Binny Mills Ltd and ECS Biztech Ltd. GSM Stage III imposes heightened trading restrictions on these securities.

## Key Points

- Binny Mills Ltd (Security Code: 535620, ISIN: INE160L01011) moved to GSM Stage III
- ECS Biztech Ltd (Security Code: 540063, ISIN: INE925Q01024) moved to GSM Stage III
- Securities marked (#) move to a lower GSM stage due to inclusion in the ESM (Enhanced Surveillance Measure) Framework
- Securities marked ($) move to a lower GSM stage due to inclusion in the IBC (Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code) Framework

## Regulatory Changes

The GSM framework is a surveillance mechanism introduced by BSE to protect investor interest by placing securities under enhanced monitoring and trading restrictions. Stage III represents a significant level of restriction within the GSM hierarchy, typically involving trade-to-trade settlement and margin requirements.

## Compliance Requirements

- Traders and investors holding or intending to trade in these securities must comply with the enhanced trading conditions applicable to GSM Stage III
- Members should note the revised settlement and margin requirements for these securities
- Transactions in GSM Stage III securities are subject to trade-to-trade settlement

## Important Dates

- Effective Date: March 6, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Investors and traders in Binny Mills Ltd and ECS Biztech Ltd will face significant trading restrictions under GSM Stage III, including mandatory trade-to-trade settlement and higher margin requirements. Liquidity in these securities is likely to decrease substantially. Retail investors should exercise caution and review their positions in these securities given the heightened regulatory scrutiny.
