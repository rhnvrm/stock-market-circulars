## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of AKARA CAPITAL ADVISORS PRIVATE LIMITED effective February 4, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the effective date.

## Key Points

- Debenture affected: ACAPL-12.5%-19-2-26-PVT (ISIN: INE08XP07241, Code: 975926)
- Record date: February 4, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- No dealings permitted from: February 4, 2026
- Circular reference: DR-813/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from February 4, 2026
- All trading members are required to take note of the suspension
- Members should inform their clients holding or interested in these debentures about the trading suspension

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 30, 2026
- **Record Date**: February 4, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Date**: February 4, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - affects only holders of specific private debentures of AKARA CAPITAL ADVISORS PRIVATE LIMITED

**Investor Impact**: Medium for debenture holders - those holding these NCDs will receive redemption proceeds and interest payment, but cannot trade from the record date

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - standard suspension procedure for corporate action processing in debt securities segment
