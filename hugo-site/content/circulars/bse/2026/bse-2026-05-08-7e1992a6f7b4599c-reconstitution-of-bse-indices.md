## Summary

BSE's Index Cell has issued a notice (No. 20260508-12) dated 08 May 2026 announcing the reconstitution of BSE Indices. This periodic reconstitution involves additions, deletions, or reweighting of constituent stocks across one or more BSE equity indices.

## Key Points

- Notice issued by BSE Index Cell under the Equity segment
- Reconstitution applies to BSE Indices (specific indices and changes detailed in the attached PDF)
- No attachment was directly available in the notice text; full details are in the referenced PDF
- Periodic index reconstitutions are routine corporate actions by BSE to maintain index relevance and representativeness

## Regulatory Changes

Index composition changes as determined by BSE's Index Maintenance Sub-Committee. Specific additions, deletions, and weight adjustments are outlined in the accompanying PDF document.

## Compliance Requirements

- Index fund managers and ETF providers tracking affected BSE indices must rebalance their portfolios in line with the new index composition
- Market participants holding index-linked derivatives or structured products should review their exposure
- Custodians and portfolio managers should update benchmark compositions accordingly

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 08 May 2026
- Effective date of reconstitution: To be confirmed per the attached PDF details

## Impact Assessment

Index reconstitutions have moderate market impact. Stocks added to indices typically experience buying pressure from passive funds, while deleted stocks may face selling pressure. The impact is concentrated around the effective date of reconstitution. Market participants with index-linked mandates must act to avoid tracking error.
