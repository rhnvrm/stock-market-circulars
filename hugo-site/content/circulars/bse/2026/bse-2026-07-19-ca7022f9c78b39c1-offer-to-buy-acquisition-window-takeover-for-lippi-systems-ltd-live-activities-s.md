## Summary

BSE has issued the schedule of activities for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) of shares of LIPPI SYSTEMS LTD. The offer window is live from Monday, 20th July 2026 to Friday, 31st July 2026, covering an offer size of up to 33,82,231 equity shares at a price of Rs. 56.84 per share.

## Key Points

- Security: LIPPI SYSTEMS LTD (Symbol: LIPPISYS, ISIN: INE845B01018, Scrip Code: 526604)
- Offer Size: Up to 33,82,231 equity shares
- Market Lot: 1; Face Value: Rs. 10.00 per share
- Offer Price: Rs. 56.84 per share
- Bidding categories include FII, FI, MF, Insurance Companies, Other QIBs, Other NIB, Company, and Individual
- Trading and Custodian Members must access the offer via iBBS, RTRMS, and Extranet platforms (internet and leased line URLs provided)
- UCC of investors must be registered/updated with correct PAN and category before bidding

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this notice operationalizes the previously approved Offer to Buy under the Acquisition Window (Takeover) mechanism, referencing prior operational instructions in Exchange Notices No. 20150930-1 (dated 30 Sept 2015) and 20170203-29 (dated 3 Feb 2017).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must ensure UCC registration/updation with correct PAN and investor category in the Exchange UCC database.
- Trading Members may bid only for investors holding shares in demat form.
- Members and Custodians must note that no End of Issue files will be generated on Extranet if no securities are accepted.
- Members/Custodians should use listed contacts for iBBS/RTRMS login creation and password resets.

## Important Dates

- Opening Date: Monday, 20th July 2026
- Closing Date: Friday, 31st July 2026
- Bid Offering Period: 20th July 2026 to 31st July 2026, 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.
- Give-up/Take-up Period: 20th July 2026 to 30th July 2026, 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.
- Give-up (last day of offer): 31st July 2026, 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.
- Take-up (last day of offer): 31st July 2026, 9:15 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.

## Impact Assessment

The notice is operationally significant for Trading Members and Custodian Members handling bids for LIPPI SYSTEMS LTD shares under the takeover offer, requiring adherence to specific session timings and UCC compliance. Market impact is limited to shareholders and market participants dealing in LIPPISYS, with no broader market-wide implications.
