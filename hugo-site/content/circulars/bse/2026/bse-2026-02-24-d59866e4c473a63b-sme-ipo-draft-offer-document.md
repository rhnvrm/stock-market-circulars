## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document (DRHP) of Twinkle Papers Limited, filed on February 23, 2026, on the BSE SME platform. The document is made available for public review for a minimum of 21 days in compliance with Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations. Stakeholders are invited to submit comments via email.

## Key Points

- Twinkle Papers Limited has filed its Draft Offer Document with BSE for an SME IPO.
- The document was filed on February 23, 2026, and the notice was issued on February 24, 2026.
- The Draft Offer Document is hosted on the BSE SME website: www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public comments are invited within 21 days from the date of notice.
- The notice was issued by Nitinkumar Pujari, Assistant Vice President, BSE.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this circular. The filing is made in accordance with existing requirements under Schedule VI of the SEBI (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations (ICDR Regulations).

## Compliance Requirements

- Interested parties and the public may review the Draft Offer Document on the BSE SME website.
- Comments, if any, must be submitted within 21 days from February 24, 2026 (i.e., by March 17, 2026).
- Comments should be sent to: equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Filing Date:** February 23, 2026
- **Notice Date:** February 24, 2026
- **Public Comment Deadline:** March 17, 2026 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited broader market impact and is specific to prospective investors and stakeholders of Twinkle Papers Limited. It represents a standard procedural step in the SME IPO process. Market participants interested in the SME segment should review the Draft Offer Document for investment consideration and may submit comments before the deadline.
