## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that Mach Conferences and Events Ltd (scrip code 544248) has officially changed its name to **Mach Travel Solutions Limited**. The change will be reflected in the BOLT Plus System effective **June 03, 2026**. The Scrip ID and abbreviated name (MACHLTD) remain unchanged.

## Key Points

- Company scrip code 544248 is undergoing a name change from Mach Conferences and Events Ltd to Mach Travel Solutions Limited.
- The Scrip ID on BOLT Plus System remains **MACHLTD**.
- The abbreviated name on BOLT Plus System remains **MACHLTD**.
- Effective date of the change in the exchange system: **June 03, 2026**.
- A Certificate of Incorporation (certificate_of_corporation_mach) has been submitted as an attachment.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory rule changes. This is an administrative update to reflect the company's new legal name as per its Certificate of Incorporation.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed for awareness purposes. No specific action is required from trading members, as the Scrip ID and abbreviated name on BOLT Plus System remain the same (MACHLTD).

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** May 27, 2026
- **Effective Date of Name Change on BOLT Plus System:** June 03, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. Since both the Scrip ID and the abbreviated name (MACHLTD) remain unchanged, trading operations are unaffected. Investors and trading members should note the updated legal name of the company for documentation and compliance purposes.
