## Summary

Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd. (ICCL), the approved intermediary under the Securities Lending Scheme, 1997, has published the stock-wise Market Wide Position Limits (MWPL) and participant-level position limits applicable under the Securities Lending and Borrowing Scheme (SLBS) for the month of June 2026. The limits cover Clearing Members (CM), Institutional Investors, and Clients, and are detailed in Annexure I of the notice.

## Key Points

- ICCL has issued MWPL and position limits for all stocks traded under SLBS for June 2026.
- Limits are specified for three participant categories: Clearing Members (CM), Institutional Investors, and Clients.
- The notice references the governing framework established under SEBI Circular No. MRD/DoP/SE/Dep/Cir-14/2007 dated December 20, 2007.
- Stock-wise limits are provided in Annexure I (attached to the notice).
- Issued by Sushant Majhi, Chief Risk Officer, ICCL.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is a routine monthly issuance of position limits consistent with the existing SLBS framework established under SEBI Circular No. MRD/DoP/SE/Dep/Cir-14/2007.

## Compliance Requirements

- All Members and Participants must adhere to the MWPL and participant-level position limits specified in Annexure I for the month of June 2026.
- Clearing Members must ensure their clients and institutional investors remain within the prescribed limits throughout the month.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 29 May 2026
- **Applicable Period:** June 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice with low market impact. Position limits are updated monthly and serve as standard risk management guardrails for the SLBS segment. Members and participants should review Annexure I to confirm their exposure remains within the updated limits for June 2026. No trading disruptions or structural changes are expected.
