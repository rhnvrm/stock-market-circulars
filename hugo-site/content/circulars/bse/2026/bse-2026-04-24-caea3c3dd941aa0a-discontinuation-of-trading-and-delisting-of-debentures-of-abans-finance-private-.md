## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the debentures of Abans Finance Private Limited (Scrip Code: 974454) will be voluntarily delisted from the Exchange. The company has completed all required formalities under Chapter VIA of the SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015. Trading in these debentures will be discontinued effective Monday, April 27, 2026.

## Key Points

- Abans Finance Private Limited (Scrip Code: 974454) has fulfilled all formalities for voluntary delisting of its debentures
- Trading in the debentures will be discontinued from April 27, 2026
- The delisting is governed by Chapter VIA of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015
- Trading members are requested to take note of the discontinuation

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The delisting is executed in compliance with the existing framework under Chapter VIA of the SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015, as amended.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease trading in Abans Finance Private Limited debentures (Scrip Code: 974454) from April 27, 2026
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the BSE Debt Department at: 22728352 / 8597 / 8995 / 5753 / 8915

## Important Dates

- **April 24, 2026**: Notice issued by BSE
- **April 27, 2026 (Monday)**: Last date of trading; debentures discontinued from trading on this date

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and traders of Abans Finance Private Limited debentures listed on BSE's Debt Segment. After April 27, 2026, no further trading will be possible in these instruments on the Exchange. Existing debenture holders should be aware of the delisting and plan accordingly. This is a routine voluntary delisting action with no broader market implications.
