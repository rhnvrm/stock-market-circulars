## Summary

OXYZO FINANCIAL SERVICES LTD has informed BSE of a revised reduced face value for its debentures (ISIN: INE04VS07388, Scrip Code: 976577) following a part redemption. The new reduced face value is Rs. 66,666.67 per debenture for the OFSL-9.75%-27-3-27-PVT series. Trading members are required to trade these debentures at the revised reduced face value effective January 21, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: OXYZO FINANCIAL SERVICES LTD
- Debenture Series: OFSL-9.75%-27-3-27-PVT
- ISIN: INE04VS07388
- Scrip Code: 976577
- Purpose: Part Redemption of Debentures
- Revised Reduced Face Value: Rs. 66,666.67 per Debenture
- Effective Date: January 21, 2026
- Circular Reference: DR-803/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action notification for part redemption of privately placed debentures with revised face value.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must trade the debentures at the revised reduced face value of Rs. 66,666.67 per debenture
- All trading members of BSE are required to take note of this change
- The revised face value must be applied to all trades from the effective date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 20, 2026
- **Effective Date for Trading at Revised Face Value**: January 21, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a medium impact limited to holders and traders of OXYZO FINANCIAL SERVICES LTD debentures (ISIN: INE04VS07388). The part redemption results in a reduced face value, which affects:

- Debenture holders who will receive partial redemption proceeds
- Trading members who must update their systems to reflect the new face value
- Future interest calculations and redemption proceeds will be based on the reduced face value of Rs. 66,666.67

The change is operational in nature and affects a specific debt instrument rather than having broader market implications.
