## Summary

BSE has notified members that OLD BRIDGE FLEXI CAP FUND has been reopened for ongoing subscription and redemption, along with SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities on the BSE StAR MF Platform, effective March 04, 2026. This notice follows the earlier Notice No. 20260212-36.

## Key Points

- OLD BRIDGE FLEXI CAP FUND is now available for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform from March 04, 2026.
- Both Regular and Direct plan variants are available.
- Three payout/reinvestment options are available: Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment.
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities are enabled along with subscription and redemption.
- This is a follow-up to Notice No. 20260212-36, indicating the fund was previously suspended from new transactions.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an operational reinstatement of transaction facilities for an existing mutual fund scheme on the BSE StAR MF platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and distributors on BSE StAR MF Platform may now process ongoing transactions for the listed OLD BRIDGE FLEXI CAP FUND schemes.
- No specific compliance action required; this is an availability notice.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** March 04, 2026 — Fund reopened for ongoing subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP on BSE StAR MF Platform.
- **Reference Notice:** 20260212-36 (original suspension/closure notice dated February 12, 2026).
