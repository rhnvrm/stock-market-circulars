## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of bonus equity shares of Cupid Limited (Scrip Code: 530843, ISIN: INE509F01029). The bonus shares carry distinctive numbers 26,89,32,141 to 34,46,60,700, representing 7,57,28,560 new bonus shares. The total post-bonus paid-up capital stands at 1,07,57,28,560 shares.

## Key Points

- Company: Cupid Limited (manufacturer and exporter of male/female condoms, water-based lubricants, and IVD kits)
- Scrip Code: 530843
- ISIN: INE509F01029
- Distinctive numbers of bonus shares: 26,89,32,141 to 34,46,60,700
- Total post-bonus shares: 1,07,57,28,560
- Company Secretary: Saurabh Karmase (FCS 11349)
- CIN: L25193MH1993PLC070846

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification to BSE regarding the listing of newly allotted bonus equity shares.

## Compliance Requirements

- Cupid Limited has submitted the required annexure to BSE detailing distinctive numbers and allotment details.
- The Company Secretary has certified and submitted the filing on behalf of the company.

## Important Dates

- Deemed Date of Allotment: 10th March 2026
- Effective Listing Date on BSE: 11th March 2026

## Impact Assessment

Existing shareholders of Cupid Limited will receive bonus equity shares credited to their demat accounts. Trading in the bonus shares will commence from 11th March 2026. The bonus issue increases the total outstanding share count, which may result in a proportional adjustment to the share price. Impact is limited to existing shareholders and does not affect the broader market.
