## Summary

BSE has notified trading members of record dates fixed by several companies for payment of interest and/or redemption of their Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) and bonds. Trading members are advised not to deal in these specific debt securities as trading will be suspended ahead of the respective record dates.

## Key Points

- Trading members must not deal in the listed NCDs/bonds as per the schedule below
- IFCI Ltd: two private placement NCDs (ISIN INE039A09NM1, INE039A09NL3), record date 16/07/2026
- SMC Global Securities Limited: two NCDs (ISIN INE103C07025, INE103C07033), record date 22/07/2026
- Chemmanur Credits and Investments Limited: two NCDs (ISIN INE051307BA8, INE051307BE0), record date 29/07/2026
- Power Finance Corporation Ltd: private placement bond (ISIN INE134E08II2), record date 29/07/2026
- ECap Equities Limited: private placement NCD (ISIN INE468N07BJ0), record date 29/07/2026
- Muthoot Mcred Limited: zero-coupon NCD (ISIN INE101Q07904), record date 30/07/2026
- Bajaj Finance Limited: bond 875BFL26B (ISIN INE296A08797), record date 30/07/2026
- JM Financial Credit Solutions Limited: private placement NCD (ISIN INE651J07978), record date 30/07/2026
- SK Finance Limited: private placement NCD (ISIN INE124N07721), record date 30/07/2026
- Midland Microfin Limited: private placement NCD (ISIN INE884Q07756), record date 30/07/2026
- Prachay Capital Limited: private placement NCD (ISIN INE0IID07710), record date 30/07/2026
- Aadhar Housing Finance Limited: private placement NCD (ISIN INE883F07314), record date 31/07/2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is a routine listing operations notice under BSE's standard process for suspending trading in debt securities around record dates for interest/redemption payments.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE must refrain from dealing in the specified ISINs on and around the settlement dates indicated (DR settlement numbers referenced range from 673/2026 to 684/2026). No action is required from retail investors beyond awareness that these securities will not trade during the suspension window.

## Important Dates

- 16 July 2026: Record date/ex-date for IFCI Ltd NCDs
- 22 July 2026: Record date/ex-date for SMC Global Securities Limited NCDs
- 29 July 2026: Record date/ex-date for Chemmanur Credits and Investments Limited, Power Finance Corporation Ltd, and ECap Equities Limited securities
- 30 July 2026: Record date/ex-date for Muthoot Mcred Limited, Bajaj Finance Limited, JM Financial Credit Solutions Limited, SK Finance Limited, Midland Microfin Limited, and Prachay Capital Limited securities
- 31 July 2026: Record date/ex-date for Aadhar Housing Finance Limited security

## Impact Assessment

This notice has limited market-wide impact as it pertains only to trading in specific debt instruments (NCDs/bonds) of the named issuers, not their equity shares. Bondholders and debt-market participants in these specific ISINs should be aware of the suspension to avoid failed trades around the record/ex-dates. No impact on equity market trading or broader indices.
