## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session in the Equity segment on Saturday, July 18, 2026, conducted from the PR (Primary/Disaster Recovery) site. The session is being held pursuant to earlier circulars (20260319-21 and 20260610-41) covering the introduction of the Closing Auction Session (CAS) and related Standard Operating Procedures. Two mock sessions are planned: one to test CAS-related changes, and another to test general trading functionality without CAS changes, in preparation for live trading resuming on Monday, July 20, 2026.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session 1: Tests changes related to the Closing Auction Session (CAS), including reference price calculation, order entry for limit/market orders, and order matching/confirmation.
- Mock trading session 2: Tests standard trading functionality without CAS changes, to prepare for live trading resumption.
- Session 1 runs from log-in at 08:15 am through post-closing at 12:45 pm, including Pre-Open, Continuous Trading (T+1 and T+0), Special Pre-Open for IPO/re-listed scrips, Periodic Call Auction, Block Deal windows, and the Closing Auction Session.
- Session 2 begins with log-in at 02:30 pm, followed by a Morning Block Deal window and Pre-Open period (details partially provided in source notice).
- No attachments were provided with this notice.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced by this notice; it references and operationalizes prior circulars 20260319-21 and 20260610-41 concerning the Closing Auction Session (CAS) and associated Standard Operating Procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members and market participants are expected to participate in the mock trading sessions to test system readiness, particularly for CAS-related functionality, ahead of live trading resuming on July 20, 2026. No specific compliance filings are required.

## Important Dates

- Mock trading session date: Saturday, July 18, 2026
- Session 1 timing: 08:15 am to 12:45 pm
- Session 2 timing: begins 02:30 pm
- Live trading resumes: Monday, July 20, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, low-impact operational notice affecting only system testing on a non-trading Saturday. It has no bearing on listed securities, stock prices, or live market activity, but ensures readiness of exchange infrastructure ahead of the CAS rollout in live trading.
