## Summary

BSE has announced the schedule for an Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for NETLINK SOLUTIONS INDIA LIMITED (Security Symbol: NETLINK, ISIN: INE040F01033, Scrip Code: 509040). The offer will run from Wednesday, January 28, 2026 to Tuesday, February 10, 2026. The acquirer is offering Rs. 201.00 per share for up to 6,57,599 equity shares with a face value of Rs. 10.00 and market lot of 1.

## Key Points

- **Security**: NETLINK SOLUTIONS INDIA LIMITED (NETLINK)
- **Offer Size**: Up to 6,57,599 equity shares
- **Offer Price**: Rs. 201.00 per share
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10.00
- **Opening Date**: Wednesday, January 28, 2026
- **Closing Date**: Tuesday, February 10, 2026
- **Bid Offering Period**: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM (January 28 - February 10, 2026)
- Trading members can bid for investors having shares in demat form
- UCC of the investor must be registered with correct PAN Number and Category

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This circular provides operational instructions for an acquisition window under existing takeover regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

### For Trading Members and Custodian Members:

1. Ensure investor UCC is registered and/or updated with correct PAN Number and Category in the Exchange UCC database
2. Can only bid for investors having shares in demat form
3. Follow operational instructions per Exchange Notices 20150930-1 (September 30, 2015) and 20170203-29 (February 3, 2017)
4. Access OTB Module via:
   - iBBS platform: https://ibbs.bseindia.com/ (Internet) or https://ibbsll.bseindia.com/ (Leased Line)
   - RTRMS: https://rtrms.bseindia.com/ (Internet) or https://rtrmsll.bseindia.com/ (Leased Line)
   - Extranet: https://member.bseindia.com/ (Internet) or https://memberll.bseindia.com/ (Leased Line)

### Bidding Categories:
- Foreign Institutional Buyer (FII)
- Financial Institution (FI)
- Mutual Fund (MF)
- Insurance Companies (IC)
- Other QIBs (OTH)
- Other NIB (NOH)
- Company (CO)
- Individual (IND)

## Important Dates

- **Bid Offering Period**: January 28, 2026 to February 10, 2026 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Give-up/Take-up Period**: January 28, 2026 to February 9, 2026 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Give-up (Last Day)**: February 10, 2026 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Take-up (Last Day)**: February 10, 2026 (9:15 AM - 4:00 PM)
- **Extranet Files Availability**: After issue closure, upon confirmation from Merchant Banker/RTA
