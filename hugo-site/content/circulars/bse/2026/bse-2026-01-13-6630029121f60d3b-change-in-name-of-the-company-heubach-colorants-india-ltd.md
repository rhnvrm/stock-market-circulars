## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for Heubach Colorants India Limited. The company's name has been officially changed to Sudarshan Colorants India Limited, effective December 12, 2025. The certificate was issued by the Central Processing Centre, ROC under CIN L24110MH1956PLC010806.

## Key Points

- Company name changed from Heubach Colorants India Limited to Sudarshan Colorants India Limited
- Effective date: December 12, 2025
- CIN remains unchanged: L24110MH1956PLC010806
- Company originally incorporated as Colour-Chem Limited
- Company remains limited by shares
- Certificate digitally signed by Assistant/Deputy/Registrar of Companies

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a corporate action pursuant to the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014, specifically rule 29.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display the old name (Heubach Colorants India Limited) along with the new name for a period of two years at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- The name change does not affect existing rights and liabilities of stakeholders
- All stakeholders are advised to verify the latest company status on MCA portal www.mca.gov.in/MCA21

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date of Name Change:** December 12, 2025
- **Certificate Issue Date:** December 12, 2025, 10:07:14 IST
- **Old Name Display Period:** Two years from December 12, 2025 (until December 12, 2027)

## Impact Assessment

**Corporate Impact:** The name change represents a corporate rebranding from Heubach Colorants to Sudarshan Colorants. This requires updates to all corporate documents, letterheads, signage, and digital presence.

**Shareholder Impact:** No impact on shareholding structure, rights, or liabilities. CIN remains unchanged, ensuring continuity of corporate identity for regulatory purposes.

**Market Impact:** Minimal operational impact. The company's registered office remains at Rupa Renaissance, B Wing, 25th Floor, Juinagar, Navi Mumbai. Trading symbol and ISIN may require updates by stock exchanges.

**Compliance Burden:** Company must maintain dual name display for two years, requiring updates to all statutory communications and public-facing materials.
