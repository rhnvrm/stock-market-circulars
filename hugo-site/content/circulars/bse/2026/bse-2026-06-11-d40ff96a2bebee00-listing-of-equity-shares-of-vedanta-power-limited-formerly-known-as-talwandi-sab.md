## Summary

BSE has notified all market participants that Vedanta Power Limited (formerly known as Talwandi Sabo Power Limited), a demerged entity from Vedanta Limited, will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange effective Monday, June 15, 2026. The scrip will be placed in the T Group (Trade-for-Trade segment) for 10 trading days and will be subject to a special pre-open session (SPOS).

## Key Points

- Listing effective date: June 15, 2026 on BSE
- Scrip Code: 544781; Scrip ID / Abbreviated name on BOLT: VEDPOWER
- ISIN: INE694L01019
- Group: T (Trade-for-Trade for 10 trading days)
- Total securities: 3,91,03,88,057 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each, fully paid
- Distinctive numbers: 1 – 3,91,03,88,057
- Market Lot: 1; Face Value: Rs. 10/- each
- Shares allotted pursuant to Composite Scheme of Arrangement
- Special Pre-Open Session (SPOS) applicable as per SEBI Circular nos. CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012, CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 (January 20, 2012) and SEBI/HO/MRD-TPD1/CIR/P/2023/55 (April 11, 2023)
- Helpdesk for SPOS clarifications: 022-6136 3100/3171

## Regulatory Changes

The listing is governed by SEBI Circular No. CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012 and SEBI/HO/MRD-TPD1/CIR/P/2023/55 dated April 11, 2023, mandating Trade-for-Trade segment placement for 10 trading days and inclusion in the Special Pre-Open Session for IPO and Other category scrips.

## Compliance Requirements

- All market participants must note the listing effective June 15, 2026 and transact accordingly
- Trading in the scrip is restricted to Trade-for-Trade for 10 trading days from listing
- Market participants must comply with SPOS rules as per BSE Notice no. 20120216-29
- Participants seeking SPOS clarifications should contact the Exchange Helpdesk

## Important Dates

- NCLT Approval Date: December 16, 2025
- Appointed Date: May 01, 2026
- Effective Date: May 01, 2026
- Date of Allotment: May 04, 2026
- Listing Date on BSE: June 15, 2026
- Trade-for-Trade period: 10 trading days from June 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing of Vedanta Power Limited represents a significant new entrant to BSE, with approximately 3.91 billion equity shares issued 1:1 to Vedanta Limited shareholders as part of a broader Composite Scheme of Arrangement that also demerged Vedanta Aluminum Metal Limited and other undertakings. The Trade-for-Trade placement and SPOS requirements introduce short-term trading constraints, limiting speculative activity during the initial 10 trading days. Investors in Vedanta Limited (VL) who received VPL shares in the demerger ratio of 1:1 should take note of the new scrip details for portfolio tracking. The demerger, approved by NCLT Mumbai Bench, restructures the Merchant Power Undertaking into a standalone listed entity, potentially unlocking value for shareholders.
