## Summary

BSE has listed commercial paper issued by six companies on private placement basis on the BSE Debt Segment effective February 6, 2026. The securities will trade in dematerialized form with a standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and tick size of 1 paise.

## Key Points

- Six companies have listed commercial paper totaling 19,200 units (Rs. 9,600 crores)
- All securities rated A1+ by CRISIL/ICRA/CARE
- Trading permitted only in dematerialized form
- Standard market lot size: 1 unit
- Minimum trading denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Interest rates range from 7.25% to 8.90% p.a.
- Maturity periods range from 91 days to 364 days

No regulatory changes. Standard listing procedures applied.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Trades must be executed in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Minimum tick size of 1 paise must be observed
- For clarifications, trading members should contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: February 6, 2026 (trading commences)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - Routine listing of debt instruments on BSE Debt Segment with no impact on equity markets.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Standard trading procedures apply. Trading members have established processes for debt segment trading in dematerialized form.

**Liquidity**: Commercial paper provides short-term funding options for issuers and investment opportunities for institutional investors in the debt market.
