## Summary

BSE has listed 2,37,50,000 new equity shares of Virtual Global Education Limited (Scrip Code: 534741) issued at par (Re. 1/- each) to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. Trading in these shares is permitted on the Exchange with effect from Wednesday, April 22, 2026.

## Key Points

- 2,37,50,000 equity shares of Re. 1/- each listed on BSE
- Shares issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis via warrant conversion
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- ISIN: INE247C01023
- Distribution numbers: 542413699 to 566163698
- Issue price: Re. 1/- per share (at par)
- All 2,37,50,000 shares are subject to lock-in until November 29, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notice under BSE's Listing Operations department for newly allotted securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed that the new securities are now listed and permitted to trade from April 22, 2026
- Lock-in restrictions apply to all 2,37,50,000 shares (distribution nos. 542413699–566163698) until November 29, 2026; these shares cannot be traded until the lock-in period expires

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** February 6, 2026
- **Listing/Trading effective date:** April 22, 2026 (Wednesday)
- **Lock-in expiry:** November 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing of these preferential allotment shares increases the total share count of Virtual Global Education Limited, resulting in equity dilution for existing shareholders. The addition of 2,37,50,000 shares at Re. 1/- (issue price) represents a relatively small capital infusion. Since all new shares are under lock-in until November 29, 2026, there is no immediate float impact on market liquidity. Trading members should note the distribution number range for settlement and depository purposes.
