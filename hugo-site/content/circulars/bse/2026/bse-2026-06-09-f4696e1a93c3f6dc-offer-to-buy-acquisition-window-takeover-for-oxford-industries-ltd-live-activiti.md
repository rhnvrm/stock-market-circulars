## Summary

BSE has issued a live activities schedule for the Offer to Buy (OTB) – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for Oxford Industries Ltd (Symbol: OXFORDIN, Scrip Code: 514414, ISIN: INE114D01015). The offer window runs from Friday, 12th June 2026 to Thursday, 25th June 2026, with an offer price of Rs. 5.00 per share for up to 15,45,271 equity shares.

## Key Points

- **Security:** Oxford Industries Ltd | Symbol: OXFORDIN | Scrip Code: 514414 | ISIN: INE114D01015
- **Offer Size:** Up to 15,45,271 equity shares
- **Offer Price:** Rs. 5.00 per share
- **Face Value:** Rs. 10.00
- **Market Lot:** 1
- **Opening Date:** Friday, 12th June 2026
- **Closing Date:** Thursday, 25th June 2026
- Bid Offering Period runs 12th–25th June 2026 from 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.
- Give-up / Take-up sessions run 12th–24th June 2026 from 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.
- On the last day (25th June 2026), Give-up closes at 3:30 p.m. and Take-up closes at 4:00 p.m.
- Eligible bidding categories: FII, FI, MF, IC, Other QIBs, Other NIB, Company, Individual

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an operational notice implementing existing OTB framework under Exchange Notices 20150930-1 and 20170203-29.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must ensure the Unique Client Code (UCC) of each investor is registered and updated with the correct PAN Number and Category in the Exchange UCC database.
- Only investors holding shares in demat form are eligible for bidding.
- Members and Custodians should note that no End of Issue files will be generated on the Extranet if no securities are accepted.
- Extranet files for bid acceptance will be available only after confirmation from the Merchant Banker/RTA post-issue closure.
- For Leased Line access settings, refer to Exchange Circular No. 20170503-12.

## Impact Assessment

This notice is relevant to trading members and custodians participating in the takeover acquisition window for Oxford Industries Ltd. The offer is limited in scope, covering up to 15,45,271 shares at Rs. 5.00 per share. Members must ensure UCC compliance before submitting bids. The OTB module is accessible via iBBS and RTRMS platforms on both Internet and Leased Line connections. Overall market impact is low; operational impact is medium for members involved in this specific takeover offer.
