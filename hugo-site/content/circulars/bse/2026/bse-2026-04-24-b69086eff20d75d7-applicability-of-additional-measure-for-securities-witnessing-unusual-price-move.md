## Summary

BSE has conducted a review under the Additional Measure (AMS) framework for securities witnessing unusual price movement, pursuant to Exchange circular no. 20251107-56 dated November 07, 2025. Securities satisfying the inclusion criteria will be placed under the measure effective April 27, 2026, while certain securities will simultaneously be removed from the framework.

## Key Points

- Review conducted under the AMS framework established via circular 20251107-56 (November 07, 2025)
- Annexure I lists securities newly included under the Additional Measure w.e.f. April 27, 2026
- Annexure II lists securities moving out of the framework w.e.f. April 27, 2026
- Annexure III provides a consolidated list of all securities currently under the framework
- Contact for clarifications: bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory policy changes introduced. This is a periodic review update under the existing Additional Measure framework for securities exhibiting unusual price movement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the updated list of securities under the Additional Measure framework
- Members should refer to the attached Annexures (I, II, and III) for the complete and current list of affected securities
- Any clarifications should be directed to bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **April 24, 2026**: Circular issued
- **April 27, 2026**: Effective date for inclusion of new securities (Annexure I) and removal of securities (Annexure II) under the Additional Measure framework

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a moderate impact on the specific securities identified in the Annexures. Securities placed under the Additional Measure may face additional trading restrictions or surveillance scrutiny due to unusual price movements. Market participants holding or trading in affected securities should review the Annexures carefully. The broader market impact is limited as this is a routine surveillance action targeting select securities.
