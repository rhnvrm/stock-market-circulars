## Summary

BSE has notified its trading members of a scheme name change for ITI Mutual Fund on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The change is based on communication received from ITI Mutual Fund (ITI AMC) and takes effect from July 10, 2026. Two debt schemes are being renamed, with no other changes to scheme features indicated.

## Key Points

- ITI Mutual Fund has revised the names of two of its schemes available on the BSE StAR MF Platform.
- "ITI Dynamic Bond Fund" is renamed to "ITI Dynamic Term Fund".
- "ITI Ultra Short Duration Fund" is renamed to "ITI Ultra Short Term Fund".
- The change is effective July 10, 2026.
- A Notice-cum-Addendum issued by ITI AMC is attached to the circular for further details.
- Notice issued jointly by BSE's VP - Mutual Fund and AVP - Mutual Fund.

## Regulatory Changes

This is a scheme name change communicated by the fund house (ITI AMC) and disseminated by BSE for platform members; it does not appear to reflect a change in SEBI regulation, only an administrative update to scheme naming on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

BSE StAR MF Platform members and distributors should update their systems, order entry screens, and client communications to reflect the revised scheme names effective July 10, 2026, to avoid transaction errors or investor confusion. Members should refer to the attached Notice-cum-Addendum from ITI AMC for complete details of the change.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 9, 2026
- Effective Date of Scheme Name Change: July 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to operational and administrative updates for BSE StAR MF Platform members dealing in ITI Mutual Fund schemes. There is no indication of any change to the investment objective, portfolio composition, NAV, or unit holder rights of the affected schemes, so investor impact is minimal beyond recognizing the new scheme names in statements and transactions.
