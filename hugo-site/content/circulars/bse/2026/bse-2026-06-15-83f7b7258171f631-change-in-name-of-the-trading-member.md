## Summary

BSE has notified all trading members that Magnum Equity Services Ltd (Member No. 3159) will be renamed to Magnum Equity Services Private Limited effective Tuesday, 16th June 2026. This is an administrative membership update issued by the Membership Compliance department.

## Key Points

- Trading member Magnum Equity Services Ltd (Member No. 3159) is changing its name
- New name: Magnum Equity Services Private Limited
- Effective date: Tuesday, 16th June 2026
- For clarifications, trading members may contact: membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine administrative name change for a BSE trading member, reflecting a conversion of the entity type from a limited company to a private limited company.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members dealing with Magnum Equity Services Ltd (Member No. 3159) should update their records to reflect the new name — Magnum Equity Services Private Limited — effective 16th June 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** Tuesday, 16th June 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard administrative change affecting only the registered name of a single trading member. It does not affect trading operations, memberships, or market participants beyond the need to update counterparty records.
