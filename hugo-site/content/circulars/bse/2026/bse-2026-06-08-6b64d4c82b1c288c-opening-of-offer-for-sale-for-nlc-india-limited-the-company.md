## Summary

The President of India, acting through the Ministry of Coal, Government of India, is conducting an Offer for Sale (OFS) of equity shares of NLC India Limited via the BSE and NSE OFS mechanism. The base offer size is up to 2,77,32,732 equity shares (2.00% of paid-up capital) at a floor price of Rs. 303 per share. BSE is the Designated Stock Exchange for this OFS.

## Key Points

- **Seller:** The President of India (Ministry of Coal), Government of India
- **Base Offer Size:** Up to 2,77,32,732 equity shares (2.00% of paid-up equity capital)
- **Oversubscription Option:** Additional 1,38,66,366 equity shares (1.00%), bringing the total to 4,15,99,098 shares (3.00%) if exercised
- **Floor Price:** Rs. 303/- per equity share
- **Face Value:** Rs. 10/- per equity share
- **Employee Offer:** Up to 25,000 equity shares for eligible employees; each eligible employee may apply up to Rs. 5,00,000
- **Designated Stock Exchange:** BSE Limited
- **Bidding Exchanges:** BSE and NSE via a separate, designated OFS window

## Regulatory Changes

This OFS is conducted in accordance with existing SEBI frameworks:
- SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD/DOPI/CIR/P/2018/159 dated December 28, 2018 – Review of OFS through Stock Exchange Mechanism
- SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD/MRD-PoD-3/P/CIR/2023/10 dated January 10, 2023 – Comprehensive Framework on OFS
- SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD/MRD-PoD-3/P/CIR/2024/82 dated June 14, 2024 – Framework for OFS to Employees

Trading members and custodians are referred to BSE notice nos. 20190118-43, 20200701-27, 20230127-14, and 20240701-19 for revised OFS bidding guidelines.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and custodians must follow the revised OFS bidding guidelines as referenced in the prior BSE notices
- Non-retail investors may bid only on T Day (June 09, 2026); they may carry forward unallotted bids to T+1 Day
- Retail investors and employees may bid on T+1 Day (June 10, 2026)
- Eligible employees applying under the Employee Offer must comply with the OFS Guidelines and applicable approval from the competent authority
- Market participants should monitor BSE Website (www.bseindia.com) for further dissemination of OFS details

## Important Dates

- **T Day (Non-Retail Investors only):** June 09, 2026
- **T+1 Day (Retail Investors, Employees, and Non-Retail carry-forward):** June 10, 2026
- **Notice Date:** June 08, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a significant government disinvestment event in NLC India Limited, a public sector undertaking under the Ministry of Coal. The OFS represents up to 3.00% of total paid-up equity if the oversubscription option is exercised, which could result in meaningful secondary market supply pressure around the bidding dates. The floor price of Rs. 303/- per share will act as a reference price point for market participants. The two-day OFS window (June 09–10, 2026) will require trading members and custodians to be operationally prepared for order routing via the designated OFS window on both BSE and NSE. Retail investors and employees have a dedicated participation window on T+1 Day, broadening access.
