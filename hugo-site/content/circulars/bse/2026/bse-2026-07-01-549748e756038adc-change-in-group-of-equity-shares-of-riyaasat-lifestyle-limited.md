## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that, in continuation of its earlier Notice No. 20260630-60 dated June 30, 2026, the equity shares of RIYAASAT LIFESTYLE LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544804), currently listed in the SME segment, will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment, effective Wednesday, July 15, 2026. As part of this transition, dealings in the company's equity shares will be shifted to the M Group.

## Key Points

- Scrip: RIYAASAT LIFESTYLE LIMITED, Scrip Code 544804, SME segment.
- Trading will move from Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to Rolling segment.
- Equity shares will be reclassified under M Group.
- This notice is a continuation of Notice No. 20260630-60 dated June 30, 2026.
- Query contact: Mr. Anurag Jain, Tel. 022-2272 8822.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework change; this is an operational reclassification of the trading group/segment for a specific scrip under existing BSE SME segment rules.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must update their systems and trading practices to reflect the scrip's move to the Rolling segment and M Group from the effective date. No action required from retail investors beyond awareness of the changed settlement type (rolling settlement instead of trade-for-trade).

## Important Dates

- June 30, 2026: Original Notice No. 20260630-60 issued.
- July 1, 2026: Current clarifying notice issued.
- July 15, 2026 (Wednesday): Effective date of transfer to Rolling segment (M Group).

## Impact Assessment

The shift from Trade for Trade (MT Group) to Rolling settlement (M Group) typically indicates improved trading normalcy for the scrip, allowing intraday trading and netting off of positions, which were previously restricted under trade-for-trade rules. This is generally viewed as a positive operational development for liquidity and trading flexibility in RIYAASAT LIFESTYLE LIMITED shares, though impact is limited to this specific SME-listed company.
