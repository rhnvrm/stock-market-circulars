## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (20260610-4) informing trading members that two T-bills/Government Securities are scheduled to mature on their respective redemption dates, and trading in these instruments will be suspended effective June 11, 2026 (DR-649/2026-2027).

## Key Points

- Trading in two government securities is suspended from June 11, 2026 due to imminent maturity
- Scrip Code 810580 (807RJ2026, ISIN: IN2920160032) is affected
- Scrip Code 800355 (GS15JUN26C, ISIN: IN000626C050) is affected
- Trading members are advised not to deal in these securities from the effective date

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a standard operational action triggered by the maturity of listed government securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must cease dealing in the following securities with effect from June 11, 2026:

| Scrip Code | Particulars | ISIN |
|---|---|---|
| 810580 | 807RJ2026 | IN2920160032 |
| 800355 | GS15JUN26C | IN000626C050 |

## Important Dates

- **June 10, 2026**: Circular issued
- **June 11, 2026**: Suspension of trading effective (DR-649/2026-2027)

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited to holders and traders of the two specific government securities listed. This is a routine lifecycle event — the securities are maturing and are therefore being suspended from trading. No broader market or systemic impact is expected.
