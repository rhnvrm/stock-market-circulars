## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that Consecutive Commodities Limited's ongoing Rights Issue has been extended. In continuation of notice no. 20260701-13, the Issue Closing Date has been pushed from Friday, July 24, 2026 to Friday, July 31, 2026. Correspondingly, the last date for On-Market Renunciation of Rights Entitlements has been extended from Monday, July 20, 2026 to Monday, July 27, 2026.

## Key Points

- Rights issue of Consecutive Commodities Limited has been extended by one week.
- Issue Opening Date remains unchanged at Thursday, July 02, 2026.
- Last date for On-Market Renunciation extended from July 20, 2026 to July 27, 2026.
- Issue Closing Date extended from July 24, 2026 to July 31, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is an administrative extension of previously announced rights issue timelines communicated via BSE notice.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should update their systems and inform clients of the revised timelines for on-market renunciation and rights issue subscription for Consecutive Commodities Limited.

## Important Dates

- Issue Opening Date: Thursday, July 02, 2026
- Last date for On-Market Renunciation: Monday, July 27, 2026 (revised from July 20, 2026)
- Issue Closing Date: Friday, July 31, 2026 (revised from July 24, 2026)

## Impact Assessment

The extension provides additional time for eligible shareholders to renounce or subscribe to their rights entitlements in Consecutive Commodities Limited. Impact is limited to the company's shareholders and trading members handling the renunciation window; no broader market impact expected.
