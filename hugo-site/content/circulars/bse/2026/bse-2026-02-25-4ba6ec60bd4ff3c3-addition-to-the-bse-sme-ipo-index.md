## Summary

BSE Index Services Private Limited has announced the addition of Yashhtej Industries (India) Limited (Exchange ticker: 544708) to the BSE SME IPO Index. The stock was listed on BSE effective February 25, 2026, and will be included in the index at the open of February 26, 2026. This follows the company's prior listing notice (Notice No. 20260224-17).

## Key Points

- Yashhtej Industries (India) Limited (ticker: 544708) is added to the BSE SME IPO Index.
- The stock was listed on BSE effective Wednesday, February 25, 2026.
- Index inclusion is effective at the open of Thursday, February 26, 2026.
- The corporate action has been included in the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE) for February 25, 2026.
- Queries to be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced. This is a standard index rebalancing action triggered by a new SME IPO listing on BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

- Index fund managers and ETF providers tracking the BSE SME IPO Index must update their portfolios to include Yashhtej Industries (India) Limited (544708) effective at market open on February 26, 2026.
- Market participants should process the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE) dated February 25, 2026, to capture this addition.

## Important Dates

- **February 25, 2026**: Listing of Yashhtej Industries (India) Limited on BSE; end-of-day SDE file updated.
- **February 26, 2026**: Effective date for addition to the BSE SME IPO Index (at market open).

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited and routine in nature. The addition affects only index-tracking products and portfolios benchmarked against the BSE SME IPO Index. Given the SME segment context, the stock's weighting in the index is expected to be small. No broader market or systemic impact is anticipated.
