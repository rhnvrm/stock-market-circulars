## Summary

BSE's Surveillance Department (DOSS) issued Notice No. 20260622-15 dated 22 June 2026 to inform members and investors that it has sought clarifications from listed companies regarding news articles appearing in newspapers, websites, and television channels. A reply has been received from Yatra Online Ltd, while a reply from Eraaya Lifespaces Ltd is still awaited.

## Key Points

- BSE Surveillance sought clarifications from two companies regarding media news articles to verify accuracy and inform investors.
- **Yatra Online Ltd** (Scrip Code: 543992): Clarification sought in connection with a news article on CNBC TV18 Hindi (https://hindi.cnbctv18.com). Reply has been received and is attached to the notice.
- **Eraaya Lifespaces Ltd** (Scrip Code: 531035): Clarification sought in connection with a news article on Moneycontrol (https://www.moneycontrol.com). Reply is still awaited.
- Notice issued by Deputy Vice President Sanjay Jain and Manager Shreya Ghose from the Surveillance Department.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard surveillance and transparency measure by BSE to verify media reports about listed companies.

## Compliance Requirements

- Eraaya Lifespaces Ltd (Scrip Code: 531035) is expected to submit its clarification/reply to BSE Surveillance regarding the Moneycontrol news article.
- Members and investors should refer to the attached reply from Yatra Online Ltd for the company's official clarification.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 22 June 2026
- No specific deadline mentioned for Eraaya Lifespaces Ltd's pending reply.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine surveillance notice with low market impact. BSE periodically seeks clarifications from companies to ensure transparency and protect investor interests. The reply from Yatra Online Ltd is already available for review. Investors in Eraaya Lifespaces Ltd (531035) should monitor for the pending clarification, as the underlying news article on Moneycontrol may have prompted market speculation.
