## Summary

SEBI has issued a final order (No. QJA/BS/MIRSD/MIRSD-SEC-5/32425/2026-27) dated May 27, 2026, against Mr. Amit Guruh Sachdeva, a SEBI-registered Research Analyst bearing registration number INH100005190. The order arises from an inspection conducted by SEBI. BSE has communicated this order to all Research Analysts and investors via Notice No. 20260529-3.

## Key Points

- SEBI passed a final order against Mr. Amit Guruh Sachdeva, Research Analyst, SEBI Reg. No. INH100005190.
- Order reference: QJA/BS/MIRSD/MIRSD-SEC-5/32425/2026-27, dated May 27, 2026.
- The order is consequent to an inspection carried out by SEBI under the MIRSD (Market Intermediaries Regulation and Supervision Department).
- The full order is attached to the BSE notice and is also published on the SEBI website (https://www.sebi.gov.in).
- BSE has advised all Research Analysts and investors to take note of the order.

## Regulatory Changes

No broad regulatory changes are introduced. This is an enforcement action specific to an individual Research Analyst.

## Compliance Requirements

- All Research Analysts are directed to take note of this order and ensure adherence to SEBI regulations applicable to Research Analysts.
- Investors who may have engaged with or relied upon research services from Mr. Amit Guruh Sachdeva (INH100005190) should review the SEBI order for details of any adverse findings.

## Important Dates

- **May 27, 2026**: SEBI final order date (Order No. QJA/BS/MIRSD/MIRSD-SEC-5/32425/2026-27).
- **May 29, 2026**: BSE Notice date communicating the order to market participants.

## Impact Assessment

This enforcement action is limited to Mr. Amit Guruh Sachdeva as an individual Research Analyst and does not directly impact broader market operations. However, it serves as a regulatory signal to other Research Analysts regarding SEBI's active inspection and enforcement of compliance standards under the Research Analyst regulations. Investors who subscribed to or acted upon research from this analyst should review the order details on the SEBI website.
