## Summary

BSE has issued a notice suspending trading in two debt securities effective June 22, 2026. The suspension applies to debentures issued by Santladevi Resorts Pvt Ltd (ISIN: INE11LD07014) and Bond Basket 01 2024 (ISIN: INE0TN615018), due to redemption or application for delisting made by the respective companies.

## Key Points

- Trading in the specified debt securities will be suspended from June 22, 2026
- Suspension is triggered by redemption or application for delisting by the issuing companies
- Two securities are affected: Santladevi Resorts Pvt Ltd and Bond Basket 01 2024
- Trading members are advised not to deal in these securities from the effective date
- Reference: DR-656/2026-2027

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced. This notice enforces existing listing and trading regulations applicable to debt securities undergoing redemption or delisting.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all transactions in the suspended debt securities with effect from June 22, 2026
- Members are required to take note of the suspension and ensure no deals are executed in these scrips post the effective date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 19, 2026
- **Suspension Effective Date:** June 22, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The suspension directly affects holders and traders of the two identified debt securities. Investors holding these debentures will no longer be able to trade them on BSE from June 22, 2026. The impact is limited to the specific securities listed and does not have broader market implications. Holders should consult the respective companies for information on redemption proceeds or delisting procedures.
