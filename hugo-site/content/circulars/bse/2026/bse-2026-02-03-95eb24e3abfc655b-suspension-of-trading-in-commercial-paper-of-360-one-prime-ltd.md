## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in a specific Commercial Paper issued by 360 ONE PRIME LTD (Scrip Code: 360OPL-16-06-26-CP, ISIN: INE248U14RD6, Series Code: 729020). The suspension is effective from February 04, 2025, following the company's decision to buyback the commercial paper. Trading members are advised not to deal in this instrument.

## Key Points

- Company: 360 ONE PRIME LTD
- Scrip Code: 360OPL-16-06-26-CP
- ISIN: INE248U14RD6
- Series Code: 729020
- Reason: Buyback of Commercial Paper
- Trading suspension effective: February 04, 2025
- Circular Reference: DR-813/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard operational action following a corporate decision to buyback commercial paper.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified commercial paper (ISIN: INE248U14RD6) from February 04, 2025 onwards
- All trading members are required to take note of this suspension and update their systems accordingly
- Members should ensure compliance with the trading restriction to avoid regulatory violations

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: February 03, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date**: February 04, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low to Medium - The suspension affects only holders of this specific commercial paper instrument. Investors holding this paper will need to participate in the buyback process rather than trading on the secondary market.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update their systems to prevent inadvertent trading in the suspended instrument. The buyback provides an exit route for current holders, limiting negative impact on liquidity concerns.
