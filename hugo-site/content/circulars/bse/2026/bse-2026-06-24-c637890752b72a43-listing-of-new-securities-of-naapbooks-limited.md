## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 4,46,400 new equity shares of Naapbooks Limited (Scrip Code: 543351, ISIN: INE0GOA01018) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Thursday, June 25, 2026. The shares were issued to Non-Promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants at an issue price of Rs. 61/- per share.

## Key Points

- 4,46,400 equity shares of face value Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 51/- (total issue price Rs. 61/-)
- Issued to Non-Promoters on preferential basis via conversion of warrants
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 10834201 to 11280600
- All 4,46,400 shares are subject to lock-in until January 15, 2027

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification for securities already allotted.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed that these securities are now available for trading on the Exchange from June 25, 2026. The lock-in restriction (until January 15, 2027) applies to all 4,46,400 shares in distribution range 10834201 to 11280600.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** May 11, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** June 25, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry:** January 15, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a standard listing of preferentially allotted shares following warrant conversion for Naapbooks Limited, a small-cap company. The entire newly listed tranche is subject to lock-in until January 2027, meaning these shares will not be immediately available for trading by the allottees, reducing short-term supply pressure.
