## Summary

BSE has informed members that Edelweiss Gold ETF FOF will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption, along with SIP, STP & SWP facility, on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective March 06, 2026. This follows a prior notice (No. 20260211-39) regarding the scheme.

## Key Points

- Edelweiss Gold ETF FOF reopens for ongoing transactions from March 06, 2026
- Available on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Facilities available: Subscription, Redemption, SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan)
- Two scheme variants available: Regular Plan - Growth and Direct Plan - Growth

## Scheme Details

| Sr. No | Scheme Name | Scheme Category | ISIN |
|--------|-------------|-----------------|------|
| 1 | Edelweiss Gold ETF FOF - Regular Plan - Growth | DEBT | INF754K01WQ2 |
| 2 | Edelweiss Gold ETF FOF - Direct Plan - Growth | DEBT | INF754K01WP4 |

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notice regarding the reopening of an existing mutual fund scheme for ongoing transactions.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of BSE operating on the BSE StAR MF Platform should take note of the availability of these schemes for processing investor transactions from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** March 06, 2026 — Edelweiss Gold ETF FOF reopens for ongoing subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP transactions.

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This notice is relevant primarily to mutual fund distributors and investors using the BSE StAR MF Platform who wish to invest in or redeem from Edelweiss Gold ETF FOF. The reopening restores normal transaction availability for the scheme's two plan variants.
