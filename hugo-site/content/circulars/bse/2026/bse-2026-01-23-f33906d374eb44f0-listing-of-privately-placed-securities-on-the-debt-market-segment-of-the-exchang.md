## Summary

BSE has listed commercial papers issued on a private placement basis by six companies on its debt market segment effective January 23, 2026. The securities are listed in dematerialized form with face value of Rs.5 lakhs each, with interest rates ranging from 7.5% to 10.45% p.a. and maturity periods varying from 3 months to 1 year. The instruments carry credit ratings of A1+ from CRISIL, ICRA, or CARE.

## Key Points

- Six companies have listed commercial papers totaling 10,900 instruments worth Rs.545 crores
- All securities will trade only in dematerialized form under specified ISIN numbers
- Standard denomination is Rs.5 lakhs with tick size of 1 paise
- Allotment date for all instruments is January 22, 2026
- Interest payment structure is cumulative at maturity
- Trading members can contact debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing announcement for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs.5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be followed for all transactions

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: January 22, 2026 (all issuers)
- **Effective Trading Date**: January 23, 2026
- **Redemption Dates**:
  - Infina Finance: April 20, 2026
  - 360 ONE Alternates: April 22, 2026
  - Krazybee Services: October 21, 2026
  - Muthoot Fincorp: January 21, 2027
  - 360 ONE WAM: January 21, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine administrative listing announcement affecting only the debt market segment. The listing adds liquidity options for institutional investors holding these commercial papers.

**Issuer Details**:

1. **Infina Finance Private Limited** - 5,000 CPs at 7.95% p.a., 3-month tenure (CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+)
2. **Muthoot Fincorp Limited** - 4,000 CPs at 8.83% p.a., 1-year tenure (CRISIL A1+)
3. **360 ONE WAM Limited** - 800 CPs at 8.40% p.a., 1-year tenure (CARE A1+, ICRA A1+)
4. **Krazybee Services Ltd** - 1,000 CPs at 10.45% p.a., 9-month tenure (CRISIL A1+)
5. **360 ONE Alternates Asset Management** - 100 CPs at 7.5% p.a., 3-month tenure (CARE A1+)
6. **Time Technoplast Limited** - Details truncated in source

**Operational Impact**: Debt segment trading members gain additional instruments for trading. No impact on equity markets or retail investors.
