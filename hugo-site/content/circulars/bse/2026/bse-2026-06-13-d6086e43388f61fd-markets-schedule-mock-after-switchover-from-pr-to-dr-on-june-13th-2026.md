## Summary

BSE has initiated a switchover of its trading systems from the Primary (PR) location to the Disaster Recovery (DR) location on June 13, 2026, in continuation of Notice Nos. 20260611-8, 9, 10, 11, and 12 dated June 11, 2026. A mock trading session will be conducted at the DR location with revised timings across all segments, with Pre-Open session commencing at 1:45 PM.

## Key Points

- Trading systems have been switched from PR to DR location effective June 13, 2026
- Pre-Open session for all segments (Equity, Equity Derivatives, Currency Derivatives, Commodity Derivatives, EGR) starts at 1:45 PM
- Continuous trading for most segments runs from 2:00 PM to 3:45 PM
- This is a Mock Trading session at the DR location
- Equity T+1 continuous trading: 2:00 PM to 3:45 PM; T+0: 2:00 PM to 3:15 PM
- Equity Derivatives switchover window: 1:00 PM to 1:45 PM, then continuous trading 2:00 PM to 3:45 PM

## Regulatory Changes

No permanent regulatory changes. This circular details temporary revised trading session timings applicable only for the DR Mock Trading session on June 13, 2026, as part of the PR-to-DR switchover exercise.

## Compliance Requirements

- All trading members must note the revised session timings for the DR Mock Trading session
- Members should align their systems and operations to the DR location schedule
- Trade Modification deadlines: Equity T+1 by 4:15 PM; Equity T+1 (alternate) by 3:30 PM; Equity Derivatives by 3:00 PM; Currency Derivatives by 4:00 PM

## Important Dates

- **June 13, 2026**: PR to DR switchover date; DR Mock Trading session
- **1:00 PM**: Equity Derivatives switchover to DR begins
- **1:45 PM**: Pre-Open Order Entry starts for all segments
- **2:00 PM**: Continuous Trading begins (Equity, Equity Derivatives)
- **3:45 PM**: Continuous Trading closes for most segments
- **4:15 PM**: Final Trade Modification deadline (Equity T+1)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has high operational impact as it affects all trading participants across every segment on BSE for the entirety of June 13, 2026. The DR switchover compresses the trading day — sessions start approximately 1.75 hours later than normal (1:45 PM vs typical 9:00 AM). All members, brokers, and algorithmic trading systems must be reconfigured for DR connectivity and adjusted session windows. The mock nature of the session limits financial risk, but operational readiness for DR infrastructure is critical for all market participants.
