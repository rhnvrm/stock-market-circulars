## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (No. 20260409-2) updating trading members about revised terms for the debt securities of IKAT Exports Private Limited. This is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20250417-11 dated April 17, 2025. The revised terms are effective from Friday, April 9, 2026.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code **974537** with ISIN **INE0OFR08019** is available for trading with revised terms effective April 9, 2026.
- This notice is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20250417-11 dated April 17, 2025.
- The revision applies to the Debt segment under the DCS-Listing department.
- Revised terms are detailed in Annexure I attached to the notice.

## Regulatory Changes

The terms governing the listed debt securities of IKAT Exports Private Limited have been revised. Specific changes are outlined in Annexure I of the original notice. Trading members must refer to the annexure for the exact revised terms before trading.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must acknowledge and apply the revised terms for Scrip Code 974537 (ISIN INE0OFR08019) when executing trades on or after April 9, 2026.
- Members requiring clarification should contact the designated BSE officials.

## Important Dates

- **April 17, 2025**: Original notice (No. 20250417-11) issued.
- **April 9, 2026**: Revised terms become effective for trading.

## Impact Assessment

This update has a limited market impact as it pertains to a single debt instrument of a private limited company. Trading members active in the debt segment dealing with IKAT Exports Private Limited securities need to update their systems to reflect the revised terms. General market participants are unlikely to be affected.
