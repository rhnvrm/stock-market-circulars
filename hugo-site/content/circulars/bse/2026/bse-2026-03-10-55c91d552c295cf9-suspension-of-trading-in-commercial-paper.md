## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260310-7 informing trading members that 24 companies have fixed 12 March 2026 as the record date for redemption of their respective Commercial Papers. Trading members are advised not to deal in the commercial papers of these companies with effect from 12 March 2026, under circular DR-838/2025-2026.

## Key Points

- 24 companies have fixed 12 March 2026 as the record date for Commercial Paper redemption
- Trading in the commercial papers of all listed companies is suspended from 12 March 2026
- Suspension applies to the Debt Segment of BSE
- Reference circular: DR-838/2025-2026
- Notice issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President – Listing Compliance and Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice under existing BSE framework (DR-838/2025-2026) for suspension of trading in commercial papers ahead of redemption record dates.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must not deal in the commercial papers of the 24 listed companies effective 12 March 2026
- Members must ensure no transactions are executed in these instruments on or after the record date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 10 March 2026
- **Record Date / No Dealings From:** 12 March 2026
- **Purpose:** Redemption of Commercial Paper for all 24 companies
