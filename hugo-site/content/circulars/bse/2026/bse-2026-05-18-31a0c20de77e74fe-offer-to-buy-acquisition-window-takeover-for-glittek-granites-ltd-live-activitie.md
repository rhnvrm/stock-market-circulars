## Summary

BSE has announced the Offer to Buy (OTB) – Acquisition Window (Takeover) schedule for GLITTEK GRANITES LTD (Symbol: GLITTEKG, Scrip Code: 513528, ISIN: INE741B01027). The offer opens on Tuesday, 19th May 2026 and closes on Tuesday, 2nd June 2026. The offer price is Rs. 12.65 per share for up to 67,50,000 equity shares with a face value of Rs. 5.00.

## Key Points

- **Security:** GLITTEK GRANITES LTD | Symbol: GLITTEKG | Scrip Code: 513528 | ISIN: INE741B01027
- **Offer Size:** Up to 67,50,000 equity shares
- **Offer Price:** Rs. 12.65 per share
- **Face Value:** Rs. 5.00
- **Market Lot:** 1
- **Bid Offering Period:** 19th May 2026 to 2nd June 2026 (9:15 AM – 3:30 PM)
- **Give-up (last day):** 19th May 2026 to 1st June 2026 (9:15 AM – 3:30 PM)
- **Take-up (last day):** 2nd June 2026 (9:15 AM – 3:30 PM)
- **Take-up extended session on 2nd June 2026:** 9:15 AM – 4:00 PM
- Bidding categories include FII, FI, MF, IC, OTH (QIBs), NOH (NIB), CO (Company), and IND (Individual)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice operationalizes an existing OTB mechanism under exchange rules. Trading members should refer to Exchange Notice No. 20150930-1 (dated 30 September 2015) and No. 20170203-29 (dated 3 February 2017) for operational instructions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must ensure the UCC (Unique Client Code) of each investor is registered and updated with the correct PAN Number and Category in the Exchange UCC database.
- Members can only bid for investors holding shares in demat form.
- Members and Custodians should note that if no securities are accepted, no End of Issue files will be generated on the Extranet.
- Extranet files for bid acceptance will be available only after confirmation of acceptance is received from the Merchant Banker/RTA post issue closure.
- For Leased Line URL access settings, refer to Exchange Circular No. 20170503-12.
