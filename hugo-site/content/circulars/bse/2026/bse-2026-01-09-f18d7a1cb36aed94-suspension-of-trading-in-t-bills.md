## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in three Treasury Bills (T-Bills) that are approaching their redemption dates. Trading Members are advised not to deal in these specified T-Bills with effect from January 12, 2026. This is a routine operational measure for maturing government securities.

## Key Points

- Three T-Bills will mature on their respective redemption dates
- Trading suspension effective from January 12, 2026
- Notice issued under DR-796/2025-2026
- Applies to Debt segment only
- Trading Members must cease dealings in these securities from the specified date

## T-Bills Affected

| Scrip Code | ISIN | Particulars |
|------------|------|-------------|
| 805015 | IN002024Z404 | 364TB15126 |
| 805092 | IN002025Y164 | 182TB15126 |
| 805130 | IN002025X299 | 91TB150126 |

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for maturing securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must not deal in the specified T-Bills from January 12, 2026
- Trading Members should update their systems to reflect the trading suspension
- Ensure no new trades are executed in these securities after the suspension date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 9, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date**: January 12, 2026
- **Redemption Date**: On or after January 12, 2026 (as per maturity schedule)

## Impact Assessment

The impact of this circular is minimal and routine. It affects only Trading Members in the Debt segment who hold or trade the three specified T-Bills. This is a standard operational procedure for maturing government securities to prevent trading in instruments that are about to be redeemed. Market participants holding these T-Bills will receive redemption proceeds as per the normal settlement process. No broader market impact is anticipated.
