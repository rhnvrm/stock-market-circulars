## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260409-16 informs trading members that ICICI Prudential Mutual Fund has communicated the maturity record dates for six plans under its Fixed Maturity Plan-Series 88 - 1226 Days Plan F. Trading members are directed not to deal in these plans effective immediately.

## Key Points

- Six ICICI Prudential Fixed Maturity Plan-Series 88 (1226 Days Plan F) units are suspended from trading due to upcoming maturity.
- Record date and ex-date for all six plans: **April 13, 2026**.
- Settlement DR reference: **609/2026-2027**.
- Plans affected cover Growth, Quarterly IDCW Payout, Half Yearly IDCW Payout, and their respective Direct Plan variants.
- Notice issued by Marian D'Souza, Assistant Vice President – Listing Compliance and Operations.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework introduced. This is a standard operational notice under existing BSE listing and mutual fund maturity procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must immediately cease dealing in the six listed mutual fund scrip codes (543693–543698).
- No further transactions should be executed in these plans on or after the suspension date.
