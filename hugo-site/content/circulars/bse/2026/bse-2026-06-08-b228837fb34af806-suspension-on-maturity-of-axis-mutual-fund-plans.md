## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260608-35 informs trading members that Axis Mutual Fund has fixed June 10, 2026 as the Record Date for redemption of eight Fixed Maturity Plan (FMP) schemes across Series 129 (108-day) and Series 130 (92-day). Trading members are advised not to deal in these schemes from the ex-date onwards.

## Key Points

- Eight Axis FMP schemes are suspended from trading effective June 10, 2026 (ex-date and record date coincide).
- Plans span two series: Series 129 (108 Days) with four variants and Series 130 (92 Days) with four variants.
- Settlement number applicable: 648/2026-2027.
- All eight schemes carry distinct ISINs and BSE scrip codes.
- The suspension is triggered by the maturity/redemption lifecycle of these closed-ended mutual fund plans.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice implementing the trading suspension that accompanies the scheduled maturity of fixed-tenor mutual fund plans under existing BSE listing and operations rules.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease dealing in the listed eight FMP schemes on and from June 10, 2026.
- Members should communicate this restriction to their clients holding or intending to trade in these schemes.

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Notice Date | June 8, 2026 |
| Ex-Date | June 10, 2026 |
| Record Date | June 10, 2026 |
| Settlement Reference | 648/2026-2027 |

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited to existing unit-holders of the eight listed Axis FMP schemes. As these are closed-ended fixed maturity plans nearing their natural maturity, secondary market activity is typically low. Holders will receive redemption proceeds as per the fund's terms. No broader market or systemic impact is expected. The affected schemes are:

- **Series 129 (108 Days):** Direct IDCW Payout (INF846KA1465), Direct Growth (INF846KA1440), Regular Growth (INF846KA1432), Regular IDCW Payout (INF846KA1457)
- **Series 130 (92 Days):** INF846KA1507, INF846KA1481, INF846KA1473, INF846KA1499
