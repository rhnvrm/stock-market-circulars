## Summary

BSE has announced that the Mahindra Manulife Innovation Opportunities Fund will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective February 02, 2026. The fund will also offer SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities. This notice supersedes previous communication (Notice No. 20260108-40) and covers six plan variants of the equity scheme.

## Key Points

- Fund reopening date: February 02, 2026
- Platform: BSE StAR MF (Mutual Fund)
- Available facilities: Subscription, Redemption, SIP, STP, SWP
- Scheme category: Equity
- Total variants available: 6 (3 Regular Plans + 3 Direct Plans)
- Plan options include: Growth, IDCW Reinvestment, and IDCW Payout

## Scheme Details

The following six variants of Mahindra Manulife Innovation Opportunities Fund will be available:

**Regular Plans:**
1. Growth (ISIN: INF174V01CX8)
2. IDCW Reinvestment (ISIN: INF174V01CY6)
3. IDCW Payout (ISIN: INF174V01CZ3)

**Direct Plans:**
1. Growth (ISIN: INF174V01DA4)
2. IDCW Reinvestment (ISIN: INF174V01DB2)
3. IDCW Payout (ISIN: INF174V01DC0)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding scheme availability.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements. Members of BSE StAR MF Platform should ensure their systems are updated to enable transactions in the specified schemes from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** January 30, 2026
- **Effective Date:** February 02, 2026 - Scheme reopens for all transactions

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This is a routine operational matter affecting only investors interested in the Mahindra Manulife Innovation Opportunities Fund.

**Investor Impact:** Positive for existing and potential investors who will regain access to subscription, redemption, and systematic transaction facilities for this equity scheme.

**Operational Impact:** BSE StAR MF Platform members need to ensure the scheme is enabled for transactions from February 02, 2026.
