## Summary

BSE has notified Trading Members and Custodian Members of the schedule of activities for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Buyback) of shares of Noble Polymers Ltd, to be conducted from Friday, 10th July 2026 to Thursday, 23rd July 2026 on the Exchange.

## Key Points

- Security Name: NOBLE POLYMERS LTD
- Security Symbol: NOBPOL
- ISIN: INE203Q01026
- Scrip Code: 539200
- Offer Size: Up to 22,76,406 Equity Shares
- Market Lot: 1
- Face Value: Rs. 5.00 per share
- Offer Price: Rs. 5.00 per share
- Bidding categories include FII, FI, MF, IC, Other QIBs, Other NIB, Company, and Individual investors
- OTB Module accessible via iBBS platform (Internet and Leased Line URLs) and RTRMS

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this circular reiterates operational instructions from earlier Exchange Notices no. 20150930-1 dated September 30, 2015, and 20170203-29 dated February 3, 2017, regarding participation in the Offer to Buy (OTB) mechanism.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must ensure the UCC of investors is registered and/or updated with correct PAN number and category in the Exchange UCC database.
- Trading Members can bid for investors holding shares in demat form.
- Members and Custodians should note that if no securities are accepted, no End of Issue files will be generated on the Extranet.
- Members and Custodians should use provided contacts for new login IDs and password resets on iBBS, RTRMS, and Extranet systems.

## Important Dates

- Bid Offering Period: 10th July 2026 to 23rd July 2026, 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.
- Bid Modifications/Cancellation: 10th July 2026 to 23rd July 2026, 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.
- Give-up/Take-up: 10th July 2026 to 22nd July 2026, 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.
- Give-up (last day of offer): 23rd July 2026, 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.
- Take-up (last day of offer): 23rd July 2026, 9:15 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.

## Impact Assessment

This is an operational/procedural notice primarily relevant to Trading and Custodian Members facilitating investor participation in the Noble Polymers Ltd buyback via the Acquisition Window. It does not represent a market-wide regulatory change but affects shareholders of Noble Polymers Ltd who wish to tender shares in the buyback and members responsible for processing bids.
