## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 9,10,476 new equity shares of Virinchi Limited (Scrip Code: 532372), each with a face value of Rs. 10/-, are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Monday, July 20, 2026. These shares were issued to a Non-Promoter on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants, at an issue price of Rs. 28.00/- (including a premium of Rs. 18/- per share).

## Key Points

- 9,10,476 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 18/- per share to a Non-Promoter entity.
- Shares issued on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants.
- Issue price fixed at Rs. 28.00/- per share.
- Distinctive numbers: 109646897 to 110557372.
- ISIN: INE539B01017.
- New shares rank pari-passu with the existing equity shares of the company.
- Entire allotment of 9,10,476 shares is under lock-in until 31/01/2027.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing notification under BSE's Listing Operations department confirming admission of newly allotted securities to trading.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should update their systems to reflect the additional listed quantity of Virinchi Limited shares. No specific action is required from the general investing public beyond awareness of the lock-in on the newly listed shares.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: 15/05/2026
- Listing/Trading Effective Date: Monday, July 20, 2026
- Lock-in Expiry: 31/01/2027

## Impact Assessment

The listing represents a minor increase in Virinchi Limited's outstanding equity base via a preferential allotment. Since the newly listed shares are under lock-in until January 31, 2027, there is no immediate impact on free float or trading liquidity. The transaction reflects warrant holder conversion activity and has limited near-term market impact for existing shareholders.
