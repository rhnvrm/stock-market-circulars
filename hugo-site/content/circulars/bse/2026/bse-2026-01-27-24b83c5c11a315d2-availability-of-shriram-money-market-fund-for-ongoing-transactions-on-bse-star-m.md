## Summary

BSE announces the reopening of SHRIRAM MONEY MARKET FUND for ongoing transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The fund will be available for subscription, redemption, and systematic facilities (SIP, STP, SWP) starting January 28, 2026. Both Regular Plan and Direct Plan Growth options will be available.

## Key Points

- SHRIRAM MONEY MARKET FUND reopening for ongoing transactions
- Available facilities: subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Two plan options available: Regular Plan - Growth (ISIN: INF680P01489) and Direct Plan - Growth (ISIN: INF680P01471)
- Both plans categorized as Debt schemes
- This notice references previous Notice No 20260116-58

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is an operational update regarding fund availability.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements. Members can facilitate transactions in the specified schemes through BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** January 28, 2026 - SHRIRAM MONEY MARKET FUND available for ongoing transactions
- **Notice Date:** January 27, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This is a routine operational notice affecting a single mutual fund scheme.

**Investor Impact:** Low to medium for existing or potential investors in SHRIRAM MONEY MARKET FUND who can now resume or initiate transactions.

**Operational Impact:** BSE StAR MF Platform members can now process transactions for this money market fund, restoring full service availability for this specific scheme.
