## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260611-48 informs trading members that ten companies have fixed 15 June 2026 as the record date for payment of interest and/or part redemption of their non-convertible debentures (NCDs). Trading in these debentures will be conducted with reduced face values effective from the ex-date of 15 June 2026.

## Key Points

- Record date and ex-date for all listed debentures: **15 June 2026**
- Settlement DR reference: **651/2026-2027** for all scrips
- Trading will occur at **reduced face value** from the ex-date onward
- Affects both private and InvIT/trust category debentures
- Ten scrip codes across distinct issuers are covered in this notice

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced. This is a standard corporate action notification under BSE listing and debt segment operations, requiring trading systems to reflect reduced face values post record date.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their systems to reflect the reduced face values effective 15 June 2026
- Trades in the listed debentures from the ex-date must be executed at the reduced face value as specified per debenture

## Impact Assessment

This notice has a medium operational impact confined to the BSE debt segment. Trading members holding or trading in these NCDs must reflect the reduced face values from 15 June 2026. Issuers span infrastructure (Gawar Waranga Highways, Interise Trust, Anzen India Energy Yield Plus Trust), financial services (Varthana Finance, Namdev Finvest, Namra Finance, Spandana Sphoorty), and diversified sectors (Magnum Ventures). The simultaneous record date across all scrips suggests a coordinated settlement cycle. No broad market impact is expected given the private/restricted nature of most debenture series.
