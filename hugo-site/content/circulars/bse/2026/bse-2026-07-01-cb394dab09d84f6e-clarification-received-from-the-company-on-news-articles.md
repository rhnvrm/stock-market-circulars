## Summary

BSE issued a notice regarding clarifications sought from companies in connection with news articles appearing in newspapers, websites, or television channels. This is part of the Exchange's routine surveillance process to verify the accuracy of such news and inform members/investors. Maruti Suzuki India Ltd (Scrip Code: 532500) has submitted its reply regarding a news article from moneycontrol.com, which is attached to the notice. Zee Entertainment Enterprises Ltd (Scrip Code: 505537) has not yet responded to a query concerning a news article from economictimes.indiatimes.com.

## Key Points

- BSE sought clarification from two companies on news articles reported in the media.
- Maruti Suzuki India Ltd (Scrip Code: 532500) has replied to BSE's query regarding a moneycontrol.com article; the reply is attached to the notice.
- Zee Entertainment Enterprises Ltd (Scrip Code: 505537) has not yet replied to BSE's query regarding an economictimes.indiatimes.com article.
- The notice was issued by the Surveillance Department (DOSS) of BSE, signed by Sanjay Jain (Deputy Vice President) and Shreya Ghose (Manager).

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this notice. It is a routine surveillance communication under BSE's standard practice of seeking clarifications on market-moving news reports.

## Compliance Requirements

- Zee Entertainment Enterprises Ltd is required to submit its response to BSE's clarification request regarding the referenced news article.
- Investors and members should refer to the attached reply from Maruti Suzuki India Ltd for details on the clarification provided.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 01 July 2026
- No specific deadline is mentioned for Zee Entertainment Enterprises Ltd's pending reply.

## Impact Assessment

This notice has limited direct market impact as it is a standard verification exercise conducted by the Exchange. However, market participants may monitor Maruti Suzuki's clarification for context on the underlying news report, and the pending status of Zee Entertainment's reply may draw continued investor attention until a response is filed.
