## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that four SBI Fixed Maturity Plan (FMP) Series 76 mutual fund schemes will mature on their redemption date and are therefore being suspended from trading effective May 26, 2026. The schemes had a tenure of 1221 days and cover both Regular and Direct plan variants in Growth and IDCW Payout options.

## Key Points

- Four SBI FMP Series 76 (1221 Days) schemes are maturing on their redemption date
- Trading members are advised not to deal in any of these schemes from May 26, 2026
- Affected scrip codes: 543755, 543756, 543757, 543758
- Schemes span Regular Plan and Direct Plan variants, each offering Growth and IDCW Payout options
- Reference notice: DR-052/2026-2027

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine operational notification pursuant to the scheduled maturity of fixed maturity plan schemes.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members of BSE must immediately cease dealing in the following SBI Mutual Fund schemes effective May 26, 2026:

| Scrip Code | Scheme | ISIN |
|---|---|---|
| 543755 | SBI FMP Series 76 (1221 Days) - Regular Plan - Growth | INF200KA17U0 |
| 543756 | SBI FMP Series 76 (1221 Days) - Regular Plan - IDCW Payout | INF200KA18U8 |
| 543757 | SBI FMP Series 76 (1221 Days) - Direct Plan - Growth | INF200KA19U6 |
| 543758 | SBI FMP Series 76 (1221 Days) - Direct Plan - IDCW Payout | INF200KA10V3 |

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** May 25, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Effective:** May 26, 2026 (Redemption Date)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational action with minimal market impact. The suspension affects only four variants of a single SBI Fixed Maturity Plan series that has completed its scheduled 1221-day tenure. Existing unitholders will receive redemption proceeds as per the scheme's maturity terms. No broader market disruption is expected.
