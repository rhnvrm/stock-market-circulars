## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 1,70,747 new equity shares of Sona BLW Precision Forgings Limited (Scrip Code: 543300) issued under the 'Sona Performance Share Plan 2025' are listed and permitted to trade effective Thursday, June 04, 2026. The shares are subject to a minimum one-year lock-in from the date of allotment.

## Key Points

- 1,70,747 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each (fully paid up) listed on BSE effective June 04, 2026
- Shares issued under the 'Sona BLW Precision Forgings Limited Sona Performance Share Plan 2025' (ESPS)
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution Nos.: 624450526 to 624621272
- ISIN: INE073K01018
- Date of Allotment: May 05, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for newly allotted employee share plan securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to permit trading in these new securities from June 04, 2026
- All 1,70,747 shares are subject to a minimum lock-in period of one year from the date of allotment (i.e., locked in until at least May 05, 2027)

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** May 05, 2026
- **Listing / Trading Effective Date:** June 04, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (minimum):** May 05, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The addition of 1,70,747 shares represents a small incremental increase to the total issued share capital of Sona BLW Precision Forgings Limited. The one-year lock-in period further limits any near-term supply effect on the secondary market. This is a routine corporate action pursuant to the company's employee share performance plan.
