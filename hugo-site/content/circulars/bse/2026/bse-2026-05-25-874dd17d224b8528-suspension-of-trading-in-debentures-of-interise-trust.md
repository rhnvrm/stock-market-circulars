## Summary

BSE has issued a notice directing trading members not to deal in the Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of INTERISE TRUST with effect from 29 May 2026. The suspension is necessitated by the company fixing a record date for early redemption of the NCD and payment of interest under the exercise of a call option.

## Key Points

- Trading in INTERISE TRUST NCDs (ISIN: INE790Z07053, Code: 974904) is suspended from 29 May 2026
- Record date fixed: 29 May 2026
- Purpose: Early Redemption of NCD and Payment of Interest (Exercise of Call Option)
- Debenture series: INT-7.85%(Reset Rate)-31-3-40-DR-640/2026-2027
- Notice issued by Marian D'Souza, Assistant Vice President – Listing Compliance and Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework introduced. This notice is a standard operational directive under existing BSE listing and trading regulations requiring suspension of trading ahead of a corporate action record date.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any buy or sell transactions in INTERISE TRUST debentures (ISIN: INE790Z07053, Scrip Code: 974904) from 29 May 2026 onwards
- Members are advised to notify their clients holding or intending to trade in this security

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 25 May 2026
- **Record Date:** 29 May 2026
- **No Dealings From:** 29 May 2026

## Impact Assessment

The suspension is limited to a single NCD series of INTERISE TRUST and represents a routine corporate action-driven trading halt. Existing debenture holders will receive early redemption proceeds along with accrued interest upon exercise of the call option. Market impact is narrow, confined to holders of this specific debt instrument.
