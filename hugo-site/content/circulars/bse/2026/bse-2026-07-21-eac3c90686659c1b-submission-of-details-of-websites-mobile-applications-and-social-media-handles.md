## Summary

BSE has issued a notice referencing SEBI Circular No. HO/(79)2026-MIRSD-PODMMC dated February 26, 2026, and earlier Exchange notices dated April 28, 2026, and June 02, 2026, requiring all registered Investment Advisers to report details of the websites, mobile applications, and social media handles used for their advisory activities. This information will be published on the Exchange's website and used for whitelisting purposes with Meta and Google.

## Key Points

- All registered Investment Advisers (IAs) must submit details of their digital platforms via a designated online form.
- Submitted information will be displayed on BSE's website for investor and stakeholder benefit.
- Reported websites, apps, and social media handles will be considered for whitelisting with Meta and Google.
- Unreported social media handles related to advisory activities may not be whitelisted and could be removed/deleted per regulatory requirements.
- All fields in the submission form are mandatory; "NA" should be entered where not applicable.
- For multiple handles on a single platform, names and URLs should be listed comma-separated in the respective fields.
- Only main landing page/profile links should be submitted, not links to individual posts, reels, or stories.
- IAs must verify and cross-check all links before submission and ensure information is complete, accurate, and current as of the submission date.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory change is introduced; this notice reiterates and operationalizes compliance under existing SEBI Circular No. HO/(79)2026-MIRSD-PODMMC dated February 26, 2026, and prior BSE notices dated April 28, 2026, and June 02, 2026, concerning disclosure of digital platforms used by Investment Advisers.

## Compliance Requirements

- Registered Investment Advisers must fill out and submit the prescribed form detailing all websites, mobile applications, and social media handles used for advisory activities.
- All mandatory fields must be completed accurately, with "NA" used where a field does not apply.
- Multiple handles/URLs for the same platform must be comma-separated.
- Only main profile/landing page links are acceptable, not links to specific content.
- IAs must verify link accuracy before submission.
- Queries can be directed to BSE Membership Compliance at 022 22725854 / 5680.

## Important Dates

- SEBI Circular reference date: February 26, 2026
- Prior Exchange Notices: April 28, 2026 and June 02, 2026
- Submission deadline: July 28, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a procedural compliance notice affecting only SEBI-registered Investment Advisers on BSE, with no direct impact on listed securities, trading activity, or investors at large. Non-compliant IAs risk their unreported social media handles being excluded from whitelisting and potentially removed from platforms, which could affect their digital outreach and client engagement, but this carries no broader market or stock-specific implications.
