## Summary

BSE has listed 2,50,000 new equity shares of Valiant Communications Limited (Scrip Code: 526775, ISIN: INE760B01019) issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis through conversion of warrants. These shares are permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Thursday, June 11, 2026.

## Key Points

- 2,50,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 758/- (issue price Rs. 768/-) per share
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 12864231 to 13114230
- Date of allotment: May 7, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification following a preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed that these securities are now listed and permitted to trade. The entire lot of 2,50,000 shares carries a lock-in restriction until December 20, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** May 7, 2026
- **Trading Commencement:** June 11, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry:** December 20, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. The addition of 2,50,000 shares to the tradable float of Valiant Communications Limited is a routine post-allotment listing. The full allotment is under lock-in until December 20, 2026, so no immediate increase in tradable supply is expected.
