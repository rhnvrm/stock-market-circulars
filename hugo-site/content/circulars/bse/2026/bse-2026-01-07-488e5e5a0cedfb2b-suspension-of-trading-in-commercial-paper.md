## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in commercial papers issued by 17 companies with record dates fixed between January 12-16, 2026 for redemption purposes. Trading members are advised not to deal in these commercial papers from their respective no-dealings dates. The circular affects major financial institutions and corporate entities including Adani Enterprises, Bajaj Finance, Bajaj Housing Finance, Axis Finance, ICICI Securities, Tata Power, and others.

## Key Points

- 17 commercial papers suspended for trading due to redemption record dates
- Record dates range from January 12, 2026 to January 16, 2026
- No dealings permitted from respective record dates
- Issuers include major financial services companies and corporates
- Notice Number: 20260107-7 with DR references from DR-796 to DR-800/2025-2026
- Covers both NBFC and corporate issuers across various sectors

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification for commercial paper redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the listed commercial papers from their respective no-dealings dates
- Compliance required for all commercial papers listed with their specific ISIN codes
- Trading suspension effective from record date for each instrument

## Important Dates

- **January 12, 2026**: Record date and no dealings from date for Adani Enterprises Ltd. CP (INE423A14YK8)
- **January 14, 2026**: Record date for 6 commercial papers including Axis Finance, Bajaj Finance, Julius Baer Capital, Navi Finserv, Bajaj Housing Finance, and Bajaj Financial Securities
- **January 15, 2026**: Record date for 6 commercial papers including Julius Baer Capital, ICICI Securities, Tata Power, Infina Finance, Bajaj Finance, and Bajaj Financial Securities
- **January 16, 2026**: Record date for 3 commercial papers including IndoStar Capital Finance, Birla Group Holdings, and Bajaj Finance

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low to medium - Routine redemption process for commercial papers with temporary trading suspension. No significant market disruption expected as this is a standard corporate action.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to prevent trades in affected commercial papers from specified dates. Affects debt market segment operations temporarily.

**Investor Impact**: Investors holding these commercial papers will receive redemption proceeds as per the terms. No trading opportunity available from record dates, but this is expected for maturing instruments.

**Liquidity Impact**: Temporary removal of trading facility for 17 commercial papers, but impact limited as instruments are approaching maturity and redemption.
