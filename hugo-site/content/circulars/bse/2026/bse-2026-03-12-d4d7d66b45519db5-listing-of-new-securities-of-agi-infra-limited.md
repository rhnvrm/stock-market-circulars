## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260312-21 informs trading members that 28,30,188 new equity shares of AGI Infra Limited (Scrip Code: 539042, ISIN: INE976R01033) have been listed and admitted for trading on the Exchange with effect from Friday, March 13, 2026. These shares were allotted to Qualified Institutional Buyers (QIBs) pursuant to a Qualified Institutional Placement (QIP).

## Key Points

- **Company:** AGI Infra Limited (Scrip Code: 539042, ISIN: INE976R01033)
- **New Shares Listed:** 28,30,188 equity shares of Re. 1/- each
- **Allotment Type:** Qualified Institutional Placement (QIP) to QIBs
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 265/- per share
- **Distinctive Numbers:** 122167201 to 124997388
- **Ranking:** Pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- **Trading Effective Date:** Friday, March 13, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice for newly allotted securities under an existing QIP framework.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to update their records to reflect the newly listed securities. For clarifications, trading members may contact:
- **Contact:** Vaidehi Phatak
- **Tel:** 022 2272 8646
- **Signed by:** Marian D'souza, Assistant Vice President

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** March 10, 2026
- **Trading Commencement:** March 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing of 28.30 lakh new shares via QIP increases the total equity share capital of AGI Infra Limited. At the issue price of Rs. 265/-, this QIP raises approximately Rs. 75 crore. The new shares rank pari-passu with existing shares, meaning existing shareholders face marginal dilution. The market impact is moderate and localized to AGI Infra Limited's stock.
