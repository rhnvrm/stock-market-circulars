## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in two series of non-convertible debentures issued by Kosamattam Finance Limited effective January 15, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to two debenture series: INE403Q08134 (Code: 936448) and INE403Q08142 (Code: 936450)
- Record date set for January 15, 2026
- Purpose includes both redemption of debentures and payment of interest for first series
- Purpose is redemption only for second series
- No dealings permitted from January 15, 2026
- Notice reference: DR-799/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine corporate action notice for debenture redemption and interest payment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures of Kosamattam Finance Limited from January 15, 2026
- Trading members should take note of the suspension and communicate to clients holding or intending to trade these securities

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** January 8, 2026
- **Record Date:** January 15, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Date:** January 15, 2026
- **Actual Notice Issued:** January 6, 2025 (as per signatory date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - affects liquidity in two specific debenture series of Kosamattam Finance Limited. Holders of these debentures will be unable to trade from the suspension date.

**Investor Impact:** Investors holding these debentures should be aware they cannot exit positions after January 14, 2026. Those eligible as per record date will receive redemption proceeds and interest payment.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members must update their systems to block trading in ISINs INE403Q08134 and INE403Q08142 from January 15, 2026.
