## Summary

BSE has notified a revision of price bands applicable to 15 listed scrips, effective from July 16, 2026. The price band percentages have been changed from their existing levels as part of the Exchange's ongoing surveillance measures to manage volatility and trading risk in these securities.

## Key Points

- 15 scrips have had their price bands revised, with new limits ranging from 2% to 20%.
- Notable revisions include Hipolin Ltd and VL E-Governance & IT Solutions Ltd, both tightened to a 2% band.
- CSM Technologies Ltd, Gujarat Energy Ltd, and Prime Focus Ltd have wider bands set at 20%.
- The revision takes effect from July 16, 2026 across all listed scrips mentioned.
- Queries can be directed to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must update their trading systems to reflect the revised price bands for these scrips ahead of the effective date, ensuring order entry complies with the new circuit limits.

## Important Dates

- Effective Date: July 16, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The price band adjustments will directly affect the maximum permissible intraday price movement for the 15 scrips listed. Tighter bands (e.g., 2% for Hipolin and VL E-Governance) indicate heightened surveillance concern and reduced volatility tolerance, while wider bands (e.g., 20% for CSM Technologies, Gujarat Energy, and Prime Focus) allow greater price movement. Investors and traders in these scrips should adjust their trading strategies accordingly, particularly around order placement near circuit limits.
