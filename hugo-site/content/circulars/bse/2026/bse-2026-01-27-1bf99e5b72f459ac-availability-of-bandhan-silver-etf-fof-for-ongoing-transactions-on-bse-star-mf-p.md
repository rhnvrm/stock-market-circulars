## Summary

BSE has announced that BANDHAN SILVER ETF FOF (Fund of Funds) will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective January 28, 2026. The scheme will also offer SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities.

## Key Points

- BANDHAN SILVER ETF FOF reopening for ongoing transactions from January 28, 2026
- Available on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Both Regular Plan and Direct Plan Growth options available
- Full facility support including SIP, STP, and SWP
- Two scheme options: Regular Plan (ISIN: INF194KB1KM1) and Direct Plan (ISIN: INF194KB1KN9)
- This notice references previous Notice No 20260109-42

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding the availability of a mutual fund scheme.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements. Members can facilitate transactions in the specified schemes through the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** January 27, 2026
- **Effective Date:** January 28, 2026 - Scheme reopens for ongoing transactions

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This is a routine operational notice affecting only participants interested in investing in silver-focused ETF fund of funds.

**Investor Impact:** Positive for investors seeking exposure to silver through fund of funds structure. Provides access to ongoing investment and redemption options with systematic transaction facilities.

**Operational Impact:** BSE StAR MF Platform members can now process subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP transactions for BANDHAN SILVER ETF FOF schemes.
