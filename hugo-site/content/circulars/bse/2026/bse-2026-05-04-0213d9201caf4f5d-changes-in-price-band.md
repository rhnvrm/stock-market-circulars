## Summary

BSE's Department of Surveillance and Supervision (DOSS) has revised the price bands for 23 equity scrips effective May 05, 2026. The revised bands range from 2% to 10%, reflecting surveillance assessments of trading activity in these securities.

## Key Points

- Price bands revised for 23 scrips across BSE Equity segment effective May 05, 2026
- Revised bands are set at 2% (4 scrips), 5% (15 scrips), or 10% (4 scrips)
- Issued by BSE Surveillance department (DOSS) under Notice No. 20260504-30
- Members may seek clarification at bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

Price band revisions restrict the daily permissible trading range for the affected scrips. Scrips with a 2% band face the tightest restriction, indicating heightened surveillance concern, while 10% bands represent a relatively relaxed limit.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must observe the revised price bands for the listed scrips starting May 05, 2026. Orders placed outside the applicable price band will be rejected by the exchange trading system.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** May 04, 2026
- **Effective Date:** May 05, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The revision tightens trading limits for 4 scrips to a 2% band (Finelistings Technologies, Universus Photo Imagings, Worth Investment & Trading, Zelio E Mobility), suggesting elevated surveillance concern for these securities. The 15 scrips moved to a 5% band and 4 scrips to a 10% band represent standard surveillance adjustments. Traders and investors holding positions in these securities should factor the revised intraday price limits into their trading strategies effective May 05, 2026.
