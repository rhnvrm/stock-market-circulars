## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 27,778 new equity shares of Rose Merc. Limited (Scrip Code: 512115) are listed and permitted to trade with effect from Monday, July 20, 2026. These shares were issued at a premium of Rs. 80/- per share (Rs. 10/- face value) to non-promoters on a preferential basis, pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- Company: Rose Merc. Limited (Scrip Code: 512115)
- Security Details: 27,778 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 80/- to Non-Promoters on a preferential basis, pursuant to conversion of warrants
- Issue Price: Rs. 90/- per share
- ISIN: INE649C01012
- Distinctive Numbers: 6216408 to 6244185
- Date of Allotment: 12/05/2026
- These shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing notice for new securities arising from warrant conversion.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific action required from trading members beyond updating systems to reflect the newly listed and tradeable shares. The shares under lock-in (all 27,778 shares) cannot be traded until the lock-in period expires.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: May 12, 2026
- Listing/Trading Effective Date: Monday, July 20, 2026
- Lock-in Expiry: March 30, 2027

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, low-impact listing event. The quantity of new shares (27,778) is minimal and issued on a preferential basis to non-promoters following warrant conversion. All shares are under lock-in until March 30, 2027, meaning no immediate float increase or trading impact is expected. Overall market impact is negligible.
