## Summary

BSE has notified the opening of an Offer to Buy (Acquisition Window) for the buyback of equity shares of Cyient Limited through the tender offer route. The company proposes to repurchase up to 64,00,000 (64 lakh) fully paid-up equity shares at INR 1,125 per share, aggregating up to INR 720 crore. The offer is open to all eligible shareholders except promoters, promoter group members, and persons in control.

## Key Points

- **Company:** Cyient Limited
- **Buyback Quantity:** Up to 64,00,000 (Sixty Four Lakh) equity shares of face value INR 5/- each
- **Buyback Price:** INR 1,125/- per equity share (payable in cash)
- **Total Buyback Size:** Up to INR 720,00,00,000 (INR 720 Crore)
- **Buyback as % of Paid-up Capital:** Up to 5.76% of existing paid-up equity share capital (standalone basis as on 31 March 2026)
- **Buyback Offer Size as % of Net Worth:** 20.31% (standalone) and 14.09% (consolidated) — within the statutory 25% limit under Regulation 4(i) of the Buyback Regulations
- **Method:** Tender Offer route via BSE Acquisition Window
- **Eligible Shareholders:** All equity shareholders as on Record Date, excluding promoters, promoter group, and persons in control
- **Record Date:** 17 June 2026
- **Offer Period:** Tuesday, 23 June 2026 to Tuesday, 30 June 2026 (excluding SEBI Holiday on Friday, 26 June 2026)
- The offer is governed by SEBI circular no. CIR/CFD/POLICYCELL/1/2015 (April 13, 2015) and CFD/DCR2/CIR/P/2016/131 (December 9, 2016), and BSE notices including 20170210-16, 20190424-35, 20200528-32, 20201102-43, and 20210825-62

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. The buyback mechanism operates under existing SEBI regulations for acquisition of shares through the stock exchange via tender offers, as revised through multiple SEBI and BSE circulars since 2015.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members and Custodians** must note the opening of the acquisition window and facilitate eligible client participation in the buyback tender offer.
- Market participants must adhere to the Revised Guidelines per SEBI circular CFD/DCR2/CIR/P/2016/131 (December 9, 2016) and BSE notice 20170210-16 (February 10, 2017) and 20190424-35 (April 24, 2019).
- Eligible shareholders (excluding promoters, promoter group, and persons in control) must submit their bids through the BSE Acquisition Window within the offer period.
- Full details are available on the BSE website at www.bseindia.com.

## Impact Assessment

This buyback is significant for Cyient Limited shareholders. At INR 1,125 per share, the offer represents a premium opportunity for eligible retail and institutional shareholders to tender their shares. The buyback size of INR 720 crore (representing ~5.76% of paid-up capital) is substantial. Small shareholders receive a reservation benefit on a proportionate basis. The offer window is short (6 trading days, with one holiday excluded), requiring prompt action from eligible shareholders. Trading members and custodians must ensure proper facilitation of client orders through the BSE acquisition window mechanism.
