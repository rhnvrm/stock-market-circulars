## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document of Susan Electricals India Limited, which was filed on February 28, 2026. The document, made available on the BSE SME website, contains all disclosures as required under Schedule VI of the ICDR (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations. The public is invited to submit comments within 21 days of the notice date.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Susan Electricals India Limited on February 28, 2026
- Document hosted on BSE SME website in compliance with ICDR Regulations Schedule VI
- Public comments invited for a minimum period of 21 days from March 2, 2026
- Comments to be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice is issued in accordance with the existing ICDR Regulations requiring public disclosure of draft offer documents for a minimum of 21 days before proceeding with an SME IPO.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members of the public, investors, and market participants may review the Draft Offer Document on the BSE SME website
- Any comments or objections must be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com within 21 days from March 2, 2026 (deadline: March 23, 2026)
- No specific compliance obligations imposed on trading members or brokers by this circular

## Important Dates

- **February 28, 2026**: Draft Offer Document filed by Susan Electricals India Limited
- **March 2, 2026**: BSE notice date; public comment period begins
- **March 23, 2026**: Deadline for submitting public comments (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal broader market impact. It is a standard procedural notice for an SME IPO draft offer document. The impact is limited to prospective investors and stakeholders interested in Susan Electricals India Limited's upcoming IPO on the BSE SME platform. No trading disruptions or compliance burden on existing market participants is expected.
