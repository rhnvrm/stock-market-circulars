## Summary

BSE's Surveillance Department has sought clarification from Yatra Online Ltd (Scrip Code: 543992) regarding a news article that appeared on CNBC TV18 Hindi. The exchange initiated this query to verify the accuracy of the news and keep members and investors informed. As of the notice date, a reply from the company is still awaited.

## Key Points

- BSE Surveillance has requested clarification from Yatra Online Ltd (Scrip Code: 543992) on a news article published on CNBC TV18 Hindi.
- The exchange seeks to verify the accuracy of news appearing in newspapers, websites, and television channels.
- Reply from Yatra Online Ltd is awaited at the time of this notice.
- Notice issued by BSE Surveillance Department under Notice No. 20260619-21.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes have been introduced. This is a standard surveillance query to verify news accuracy.

## Compliance Requirements

Yatra Online Ltd is expected to provide a formal clarification to BSE regarding the referenced news article on CNBC TV18 Hindi. Members and investors should await the company's response before acting on the news.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 19 June 2026
- Reply deadline: Not specified; reply is currently awaited.

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This is a routine surveillance measure by BSE to ensure transparency and accuracy of market-sensitive information. No trading restrictions have been imposed on Yatra Online Ltd. Investors should monitor for the company's clarification response, which may have follow-on implications depending on the nature of the news article.
