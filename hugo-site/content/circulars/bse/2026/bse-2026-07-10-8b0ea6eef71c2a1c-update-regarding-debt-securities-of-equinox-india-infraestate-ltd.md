## Summary

BSE has issued a notice regarding debt securities of Equinox India Infraestate Ltd, informing Trading Members that the securities bearing Scrip Codes 977672 (ISIN INE741X07093) and 977168 (ISIN INE741X07101) will be available for trading with revised terms on account of debt restructuring, as detailed in the accompanying Annexure, with effect from Monday, July 13, 2026. This notice is issued in continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20260227-26 dated 27 Feb 2026 and Notice No. 20260630-37 dated 30 Jun 2026.

## Key Points

- Debt securities of Equinox India Infraestate Ltd will trade with revised terms from July 13, 2026.
- Scrip Code 977672, ISIN INE741X07093 is affected.
- Scrip Code 977168, ISIN INE741X07101 is affected.
- Revised terms are on account of debt restructuring, details in Annexure.
- This is a continuation of prior notices dated 27 Feb 2026 and 30 Jun 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is an operational update implementing previously announced debt restructuring terms for the specified ISINs.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members should update their systems to reflect the revised terms for the specified scrip codes and ISINs from the effective date, and may contact BSE's Debt Listing team (Ms. Bhairavi Bondale, +91 22 2272 8597, debt.listing@bseindia.com) for further details.

## Important Dates

- 27 Feb 2026: Original Exchange Notice No. 20260227-26 regarding the debt securities.
- 30 Jun 2026: Follow-up Notice No. 20260630-37.
- 13 Jul 2026 (Monday): Revised terms become effective for trading.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and traders of Equinox India Infraestate Ltd's specific debt securities (ISINs INE741X07093 and INE741X07101). Trading Members need to ensure accurate reflection of the revised terms in their systems ahead of the effective date to avoid settlement or valuation discrepancies.
