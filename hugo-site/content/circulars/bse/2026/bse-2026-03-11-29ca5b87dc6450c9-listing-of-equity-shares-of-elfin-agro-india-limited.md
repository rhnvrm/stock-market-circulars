## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the equity shares of Elfin Agro India Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course under the SME segment. The specific listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Elfin Agro India Limited equity shares approved for listing on BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544724
- Symbol: ELFIN
- ISIN: INE1FEW01013
- Exact listing date to be announced via a separate notice
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard new listing admission notice for the BSE SME platform.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should monitor for the follow-up notice from BSE that will confirm the exact listing date and full security details before initiating trades in ELFIN shares.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: March 11, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced via separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This circular signals the imminent listing of Elfin Agro India Limited on the BSE SME segment. Impact is moderate and limited to market participants interested in SME segment stocks. Trading members and retail investors should await the follow-up notice for the confirmed listing date before planning any transactions in this scrip.
