## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260515-4 informs trading members that Kolte-Patil Developers Ltd has set 18 May 2026 as the record date for part redemption of five series of its privately placed Non-Convertible Debentures. From the ex-date (18 May 2026), all five scrips will trade at reduced face values as specified below. Settlement reference is DR 632/2026-2027 for all series.

## Key Points

- Five NCD series of Kolte-Patil Developers Ltd are undergoing part redemption simultaneously.
- Record date and ex-date are both 18 May 2026 for all five series.
- Trading will continue post-redemption with reduced face values per debenture.
- All five series share settlement number DR 632/2026-2027.
- Notice issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President – Listing Compliance and Operations.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced. This is an operational corporate action notice under BSE's Listing Operations procedures requiring debenture scrips to reflect reduced face values after partial redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their systems to reflect the reduced face values effective 18 May 2026.
- Trades executed on or after the ex-date must be settled at the revised (reduced) face values.
- Members should communicate the change to clients holding these debenture scrips.

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Notice Date | 15 May 2026 |
| Record Date (all 5 series) | 18 May 2026 |
| Ex-Date (all 5 series) | 18 May 2026 |
