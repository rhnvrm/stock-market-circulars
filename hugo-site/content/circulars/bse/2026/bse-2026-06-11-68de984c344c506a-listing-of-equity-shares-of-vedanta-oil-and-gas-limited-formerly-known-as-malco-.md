## Summary

Vedanta Oil and Gas Limited (formerly known as Malco Energy Limited), a demerged entity from Vedanta Limited, will be listed and admitted to dealings on BSE effective **Monday, June 15, 2026**, under the T Group of Securities. The listing follows NCLT-approved Composite Scheme of Arrangement. The scrip will trade in the Trade-for-Trade segment for 10 trading days and will be part of the Special Pre-Open Session (SPOS) for IPO and Other category scrips.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: **544782**, Scrip ID: **VOGL**, ISIN: **INE704J01044**
- Total securities listed: **3,91,03,88,057 equity shares** of Re. 1/- each, allotted pursuant to the Scheme of Arrangement
- Distinctive numbers: 1 to 3910388057
- Listed under **T Group** (Trade-for-Trade) for 10 trading days per SEBI Circulars CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 and SEBI/HO/MRD-TPD1/CIR/P/2023/55
- Market Lot: 1; Face Value: Re. 1/- fully paid
- Company registered at 1st Floor, C Wing, Unit 103, Corporate Avenue, Atul Projects, Chakala, Andheri (East), Mumbai – 400093
- Company Secretary & Compliance Officer: Ms. Shivangi Dhanuka; Email: vogl.sect@cairnindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

The listing is governed by:
- SEBI Circular CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 & CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012 (Special Pre-Open Session)
- SEBI Circular SEBI/HO/MRD-TPD1/CIR/P/2023/55 dated April 11, 2023
- BSE Notice 20120216-29 on Enabling Special Pre-Open Session for IPOs & Relisted Scrips

The scrip is placed in the Trade-for-Trade segment, meaning each trade results in compulsory delivery with no netting or offsetting allowed for the first 10 trading days.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must note the SPOS applicability for VOGL on listing day (June 15, 2026)
- All trades in VOGL will be on a Trade-for-Trade basis for the first 10 trading days from listing
- For SPOS clarifications, participants may contact BSE Helpdesk at 022-6136 3100/3171
- Lock-in: Not Applicable

## Impact Assessment

**High impact** event. Vedanta Limited's Composite Scheme of Arrangement resulted in multiple demergers:
1. **Aluminum Undertaking** → Vedanta Aluminium Metal Limited (VAML) — to be listed on BSE and NSE
2. **Merchant Power Undertaking** → Vedanta Power Limited (formerly Talwandi Sabo Power Limited) — listed separately
3. **Oil and Gas Undertaking** → Vedanta Oil and Gas Limited (formerly Malco Energy Limited) — this listing

All existing Vedanta Limited shareholders who received VOGL shares (1:1 ratio under the scheme) can begin trading from June 15, 2026. The Trade-for-Trade restriction for the first 10 days means higher settlement obligations and potential liquidity constraints during the initial trading period. SPOS applicability on day one will help in price discovery.
