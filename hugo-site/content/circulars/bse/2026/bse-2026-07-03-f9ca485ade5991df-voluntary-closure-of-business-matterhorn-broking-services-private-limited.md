## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that Matterhorn Broking Services Private Limited (Member No. 6942) will voluntarily cease to do business in the Cash and Equity Derivative segments with effect from Monday, July 06, 2026, due to voluntary closure of business.

## Key Points

- Matterhorn Broking Services Private Limited (Member No. 6942) is voluntarily closing its business.
- The member will stop trading operations in the Cash and Equity Derivative segments.
- The closure takes effect from Monday, July 06, 2026.
- No attachments were provided with this notice.
- Queries regarding the notice may be directed to membership.ops@bseindia.com.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a member-specific administrative notice issued under the Membership Compliance department rather than a change to regulations or exchange rules.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members and market participants dealing with Matterhorn Broking Services Private Limited should note the cessation of the member's trading activities and take necessary steps to settle or transfer any pending obligations before the effective date. No action is required from the general investing public or listed companies.

## Important Dates

- Effective date of closure: Monday, July 06, 2026
- Notice date: July 03, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to Matterhorn Broking Services Private Limited, its clients, and counterparties who traded through this member. There is no broader impact on market operations, other trading members, or listed securities. Clients of this member should ensure their accounts and open positions are appropriately transitioned before the closure date.
