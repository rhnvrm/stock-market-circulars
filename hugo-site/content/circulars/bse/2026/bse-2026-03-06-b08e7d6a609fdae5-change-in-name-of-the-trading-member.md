## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that Fortune Capital Services Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 6524) will officially change its name to Flattrade Broking Private Limited, effective Monday, March 9, 2026.

## Key Points

- Trading member Fortune Capital Services Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 6524) is changing its name
- New name: Flattrade Broking Private Limited
- Effective date: Monday, March 9, 2026
- For clarifications, trading members may email: membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative update reflecting the legal name change of a BSE trading member.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action required from other trading members. Members dealing with or referencing Member No. 6524 should update their records to reflect the new name — Flattrade Broking Private Limited — from March 9, 2026 onwards.

## Important Dates

- **March 9, 2026**: Name change takes effect

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a purely administrative change affecting only the registered name of a single trading member (No. 6524). No operational, trading, or regulatory implications for the broader market or other participants.
