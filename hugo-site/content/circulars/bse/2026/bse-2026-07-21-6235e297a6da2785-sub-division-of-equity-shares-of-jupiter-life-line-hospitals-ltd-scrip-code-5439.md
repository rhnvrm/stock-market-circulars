## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that Jupiter Life Line Hospitals Ltd (Scrip Code: 543980) has fixed 24 July 2026 as the Record Date for sub-division of its equity shares. Each existing equity share of face value Rs.10/- will be sub-divided into five equity shares of face value Rs.2/- each, effective 24 July 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: Jupiter Life Line Hospitals Ltd (Scrip Code: 543980)
- Corporate action: Sub-division of one equity share of Rs.10/- face value into five equity shares of Rs.2/- each
- Record Date: 24 July 2026
- Effective date (w.e.f.): 24 July 2026
- Reference: DR-679/2026-2027
- Existing ISIN INE682M01012 (Rs.10/- paid up) will cease to be valid for exchange transactions on or after 24 July 2026
- A new ISIN for the Rs.2/- paid-up shares will be notified separately

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is a corporate action notice implementing the company's approved share sub-division.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update systems to reflect the sub-divided shares and the new face value from 24 July 2026
- The old ISIN (INE682M01012) must not be used for transactions on or after 24 July 2026
- Market participants should watch for the separate notice announcing the new ISIN for the Rs.2/- paid-up shares

## Important Dates

- Record Date: 24 July 2026
- Effective date of sub-division: 24 July 2026
- Old ISIN invalid for trading: on or after 24 July 2026

## Impact Assessment

The sub-division increases the number of outstanding shares fivefold while proportionally reducing the face value and price per share, with no change in shareholders' overall equity value. Impact is limited to Jupiter Life Line Hospitals Ltd shareholders and market systems requiring an ISIN update; no broader market-wide effect is expected.
