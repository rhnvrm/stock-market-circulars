## Summary

BSE issued surveillance queries to Laxmi Dental Limited (scrip code 544339), Hindustan Tin Works Limited, and Capri Global Capital Limited regarding unusual volume movements in their securities on January 21, 2026. All three companies responded that they have no unpublished price sensitive information pending disclosure and that the volume movements are purely market-driven. The companies confirmed compliance with Regulation 30 of SEBI LODR Regulations, 2015.

## Key Points

- **Laxmi Dental Limited** (BSE: 544339, NSE: LAXMIDENTL) received BSE query ref L/SURV/ONL/PV/APJ/2025-2026/876 on January 21, 2026 at 01:03 PM
- **Hindustan Tin Works Limited** received BSE query ref L/SURV/ONL/PV/AJI/2025-2026/3298 on January 20, 2026
- **Capri Global Capital Limited** (BSE: 531595) received BSE query ref L/SURV/ONL/PV/APJ/2025-2026/875 on January 21, 2026
- All companies confirmed no unpublished price sensitive information exists
- All companies affirmed compliance with SEBI LODR Regulation 30 disclosure requirements
- Price/volume movements attributed to normal market behavior

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies must respond to BSE surveillance queries regarding unusual price/volume movements
- Companies must confirm whether any unpublished price sensitive information exists under Regulation 30 of SEBI LODR Regulations, 2015
- Responses must be made available on company websites

## Important Dates

- **January 20, 2026**: Hindustan Tin Works received surveillance query
- **January 21, 2026**: Laxmi Dental and Capri Global received surveillance queries and responded

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. These are routine surveillance clarifications confirming no material developments.

**Investor Impact**: The clarifications provide transparency that volume movements are market-driven without any underlying corporate developments.

**Operational Impact**: No operational changes for the listed companies.
