## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that the equity shares of Omnitech Engineering Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange. The scrip has been assigned code 544720 with symbol OMNI and ISIN INE0UH301010. The exact date of listing and further security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Omnitech Engineering Limited equity shares will be listed on BSE in due course
- Scrip Code: 544720
- Trading Symbol: OMNI
- ISIN: INE0UH301010
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited
- Separate notice will follow with the confirmed listing date and security details

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard new listing admission notice under BSE's listing framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should note the scrip code (544720), symbol (OMNI), and ISIN (INE0UH301010) for Omnitech Engineering Limited
- Members should await the follow-up notice for the confirmed listing date before initiating dealings

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: March 4, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced via separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine new listing announcement. Trading members and investors should note the scrip identifiers for future reference. The actual market impact will depend on the final listing date, issue price, and market conditions at the time of listing. No immediate trading action is required until the listing date is officially confirmed.
