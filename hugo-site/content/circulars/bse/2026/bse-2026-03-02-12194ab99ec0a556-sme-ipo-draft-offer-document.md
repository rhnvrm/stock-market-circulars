## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document (DRHP) of Anand Seamless Limited, filed on February 27, 2026, on the SME exchange website. The document contains all disclosures as required under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and is open for public comments for a minimum period of 21 days from the notice date.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Anand Seamless Limited on February 27, 2026
- Document hosted on the BSE SME website: https://www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public comment window is open for at least 21 days from March 02, 2026
- Comments must be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. The publication is in compliance with existing ICDR Regulations requiring Draft Offer Documents to be made available on the exchange website for a minimum of 21 days for public review and comment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants, investors, and stakeholders who wish to submit comments on the Draft Offer Document of Anand Seamless Limited must do so within 21 days of March 02, 2026
- Comments to be emailed to: equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Filing Date:** February 27, 2026
- **Notice Date:** March 02, 2026
- **Public Comment Deadline:** On or before March 23, 2026 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited broader market impact as it pertains to the SME IPO process for a single company, Anand Seamless Limited. It is a procedural disclosure step in the SME IPO pipeline. Investors and analysts interested in the company's forthcoming IPO should review the Draft Offer Document and submit any comments by the deadline.
