## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in debentures of INDEL MONEY LIMITED (IML-11%-07-2-26-PVT, ISIN: INE0BUS07BJ4, Code: 976160) effective January 23, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to debenture series IML-11%-07-2-26-PVT (ISIN: INE0BUS07BJ4)
- Debenture code: 976160
- Record date set for January 23, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- No dealings permitted from January 23, 2026
- Circular reference: DR-805/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard suspension procedure following the company's decision to set a record date for debenture redemption and interest payment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all trading activity in the specified debentures from January 23, 2026
- Members must ensure no deals are executed in debentures with ISIN INE0BUS07BJ4
- Immediate compliance required with no grace period

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 21, 2026
- **Record Date**: January 23, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date**: January 23, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This suspension has a high impact on debt market participants holding or trading these specific debentures. Investors holding these instruments will not be able to exit their positions through market trading from the suspension date. The redemption and interest payment process will proceed as per the company's schedule. Trading members must update their systems and inform clients holding these debentures about the suspension to avoid any attempted transactions that would be rejected.
