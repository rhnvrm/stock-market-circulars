## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for TAAL ENTERPRISES LIMITED (CIN: L74110KA2014PLC176836). The company's name has been officially changed to TAAL TECH LIMITED with effect from November 4, 2025. This is an administrative change that does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders.

## Key Points

- Company's new name: TAAL TECH LIMITED
- Previous name: TAAL ENTERPRISES LIMITED
- Corporate Identification Number (CIN): L74110KA2014PLC176836
- Certificate issued by: Central Processing Centre, ROC
- Effective date of name change: November 4, 2025
- Company type: Company limited by shares
- Certificate digitally signed by Sweety Kumar, Assistant/Deputy/Registrar of Companies

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate name change procedure conducted under the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014, Rule 29.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display both the old name (TAAL ENTERPRISES LIMITED) and new name (TAAL TECH LIMITED) for a period of two years at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- The company must update its name on all official documents, letterheads, and communications
- Registered office address: AKR Tech Park, 3rd Floor, C Block, Sy # 112 Krishna Reddy Industrial Area, 7th Mile Hosur Road, Singasandra, Bangalore South, Bangalore- 560068, Karnataka, India

## Important Dates

- **November 4, 2025**: Effective date of name change from TAAL ENTERPRISES LIMITED to TAAL TECH LIMITED
- **November 4, 2027**: End of mandatory two-year period for displaying both old and new names

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. Name changes are routine corporate actions that do not affect the fundamental business operations or financial position of the company.

**Operational Impact**: Low. The company will need to update branding, stationery, and legal documents. The name change does not affect existing contracts, agreements, or stakeholder rights and liabilities.

**Stakeholder Impact**: No material impact on shareholders, creditors, or other stakeholders. All rights and obligations continue unchanged under the new company name. Stakeholders are advised to verify the latest company status on the MCA website (www.mca.gov.in/MCA21).
