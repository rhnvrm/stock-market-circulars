## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260304-13 informs trading members that 19 companies have fixed record dates for redemption of their Commercial Papers. Trading members are advised not to deal in the listed Commercial Papers from the respective no-dealings dates (March 6, 9, or 10, 2026).

## Key Points

- 19 companies have fixed record dates for Commercial Paper redemption across three dates: March 6, 9, and 10, 2026
- Trading members must cease dealings in each CP from its respective record date
- Circular covers notices DR-834/2025-2026, DR-835/2025-2026, and DR-836/2025-2026
- Issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President – Listing Compliance and Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice under existing BSE debt market framework for Commercial Paper redemptions.

- **06/03/2026**: No dealings from this date for Standard Chartered Securities CP (SCSL-9-3-26-CP)
- **09/03/2026**: No dealings from this date for Standard Chartered Securities CP (SCSL-10-3-26-CP) and Tata Realty CP (TRIL-10-3-26-CP)

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact confined to debt market segment participants trading in these specific Commercial Paper instruments. This is a standard redemption process with no broader equity market implications. Affected trading members must ensure compliance with the no-dealings advisory from the respective dates to avoid regulatory violations. The concentration of 16 out of 19 redemptions on March 10, 2026 (DR-836/2025-2026) is operationally notable but routine.
