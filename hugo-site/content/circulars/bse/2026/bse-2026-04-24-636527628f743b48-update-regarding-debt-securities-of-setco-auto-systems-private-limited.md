## Summary

BSE has issued a continuation notice (Notice No. 20260424-23) regarding the suspension of trading in debt securities of Setco Auto Systems Private Limited. This follows the earlier notice No. 20260330-9 dated 30 March 2026. Two NCD scrip codes remain suspended from trading until further notice due to an ongoing debt restructuring process.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension on two NCDs of Setco Auto Systems Private Limited remains in effect
- Suspension is due to debt restructuring and will continue till further notice
- ISIN INE0I0R07144 (Scrip Code 975859): 975 NCDs with face value ₹1,00,000 each
- ISIN INE0I0R07136 (Scrip Code 973453): 2,150 NCDs with face value ₹10,00,000 each
- This is a continuation of the suspension first announced on 30 March 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice reaffirms and extends the existing trading suspension on the specified NCDs pending completion of the restructuring process.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must ensure no trades are executed in Scrip Codes 975859 and 973453
- Members may contact BSE DCS-Listing department for further clarifications
- Contact: Ms. Bhairavi Bondale, +91 22 2272 8844, debt.listing@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- Original suspension notice: 30 March 2026 (Notice No. 20260330-9)
- Current update notice date: 24 April 2026
- Suspension end date: Till further notice (no defined date)

## Impact Assessment

Holders of these NCDs are unable to trade their securities on BSE until the restructuring is resolved, resulting in illiquidity. The two suspended instruments together represent 975 NCDs (₹1 lakh face value) and 2,150 NCDs (₹10 lakh face value), indicating significant debt exposure under restructuring. Market participants should monitor for a subsequent BSE notice announcing resumption of trading.
