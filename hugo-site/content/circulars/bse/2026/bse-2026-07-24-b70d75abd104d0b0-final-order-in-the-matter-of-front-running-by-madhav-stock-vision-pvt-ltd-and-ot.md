## Summary

SEBI has passed a final order (No. WTM/AS/ISD/ISD-SEC-4/32507/2026-27 dated July 24, 2026) in the matter of front running by Madhav Stock Vision Pvt. Ltd. and other noticees. BSE has notified all trading members of the exchange about the order and the restrictions imposed on the noticees.

## Key Points

- Noticee 1 (Madhav Stock Vision Pvt. Ltd.) is restrained from accessing the securities market and prohibited from buying, selling, or dealing in securities, directly or indirectly, in its proprietary account, for one year.
- Noticees 2 to 6 are restrained from accessing the securities market and prohibited from buying, selling, or dealing in securities, directly or indirectly, for one year.
- Noticees with open positions in exchange-traded derivative contracts as on the date of the order may close out/square off such positions within 3 months from the date of the order, or at expiry of the contracts, whichever is earlier.
- Noticees are permitted to settle pay-in and pay-out obligations for transactions that took place before close of trading on the date of the order.

## Regulatory Changes

This is an enforcement action under SEBI's final order following an investigation into front-running activities; it does not introduce new regulatory requirements but enforces existing securities market conduct rules.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are advised to take note of the order and ensure compliance with the restrictions imposed on the named noticees.
- Noticees must restrict market access and trading activity as directed, except for the limited close-out and settlement provisions specified.

## Important Dates

- Order date: July 24, 2026
- Order effective from: July 24, 2026
- Market restriction period: One year from the effective date
- Close-out window for open derivative positions: 3 months from the date of the order, or contract expiry, whichever is earlier

## Impact Assessment

The order directly impacts Madhav Stock Vision Pvt. Ltd. and five other noticees by barring them from market access for one year, which is a significant enforcement outcome for those entities. The broader market impact is limited, as this is an entity-specific enforcement action rather than a market-wide regulatory change. Trading members are required to be aware of the restricted entities to ensure compliance with the order.
