## Summary

BSE has announced corporate actions for six securities in the Securities Lending and Borrowing (SLB) segment. All actions are dividend-related with specific record dates, ex-dates, and shut periods scheduled for January 2026. During the shut periods, these securities will have restrictions on SLB transactions.

## Key Points

- Six major companies announce dividend corporate actions
- All actions involve dividend distributions with defined record dates
- Shut periods ranging from 2 to 5 days during which SLB transactions are restricted
- Ex-dates set one to two days before record dates
- Affects major blue-chip stocks across IT, FMCG, industrial, and energy sectors

## Securities and Shut Periods

### DCM Shriram Limited
- **Record Date**: January 24, 2026
- **Ex-Date**: January 23, 2026
- **Shut Period**: January 22-24, 2026 (3 days)

### Havells India Limited
- **Record Date**: January 23, 2026
- **Ex-Date**: January 23, 2026
- **Shut Period**: January 22-23, 2026 (2 days)

### Siemens Energy India Limited
- **Record Date**: January 30, 2026
- **Ex-Date**: January 30, 2026
- **Shut Period**: January 29-30, 2026 (2 days)

### HCL Technologies Ltd
- **Record Date**: January 17, 2026
- **Ex-Date**: January 16, 2026
- **Shut Period**: January 14-17, 2026 (4 days)

### United Spirits Limited
- **Record Date**: January 27, 2026
- **Ex-Date**: January 27, 2026
- **Shut Period**: January 23-27, 2026 (5 days)

### Tata Consultancy Services Ltd
- **Record Date**: January 17, 2026
- **Ex-Date**: January 16, 2026
- **Shut Period**: January 14-17, 2026 (4 days)

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must not engage in SLB transactions for these securities during the specified shut periods
- Foreclosure of existing SLB positions may be required before shut periods begin
- Investors should settle any SLB obligations before the foreclosure dates
- Brokers and clearing members must ensure compliance with shut period restrictions

## Important Dates

- **January 14-17, 2026**: Shut period for HCL Technologies and TCS
- **January 22-24, 2026**: Shut period for DCM Shriram
- **January 22-23, 2026**: Shut period for Havells India
- **January 23-27, 2026**: Shut period for United Spirits
- **January 29-30, 2026**: Shut period for Siemens Energy India

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Moderate impact on SLB market liquidity for these six securities during their respective shut periods. Investors utilizing securities lending and borrowing strategies will need to plan around these restrictions.

**Operational Impact**: Trading desks and risk management teams must adjust SLB positions and strategies to comply with shut period restrictions. Portfolio managers holding these stocks should note the ex-dividend dates for dividend entitlement.

**Investor Impact**: Shareholders on record as of the record dates will be entitled to receive dividends. Those engaged in SLB transactions must close positions before shut periods or risk foreclosure.
