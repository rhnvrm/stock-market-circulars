## Summary

BSE has listed privately placed debt securities of GMR Hyderabad International Airport Limited (GHIAL) on its debt market segment effective January 23, 2026. The securities carry a 7.60% reset rate per annum with quarterly interest payments and mature on January 22, 2041.

## Key Points

- Issuer: GMR Hyderabad International Airport Limited
- Quantity: 210,000 units
- Face Value: Rs. 100,000 per unit
- Interest Rate: 7.60% per annum (reset rate)
- Credit Rating: CRISIL AA+/Positive, ICRA AA+/Positive
- Date of Allotment: January 22, 2026
- Maturity Date: January 22, 2041
- Interest Payment: Quarterly, starting March 31, 2026
- ISIN: INE802J07043
- Scrip Code: 977480
- Scrip ID: RRGHIAL41
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading: Dematerialised form only
- Tick Size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification for privately placed debt instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE802J07043
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for further details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: January 22, 2026
- **Listing Date**: January 23, 2026
- **First Interest Payment**: March 31, 2026
- **Redemption Date**: January 22, 2041
- **Interest Payment Frequency**: Quarterly from March 31, 2026 to January 22, 2041

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine listing of privately placed debt securities that adds liquidity to the debt market segment for GMR Hyderabad International Airport Limited bonds.

**Investor Impact**: Low. Affects only investors and trading members dealing in debt securities of GMR Hyderabad Airport. The high credit rating (AA+) indicates strong creditworthiness.

**Operational Impact**: No significant operational changes required. Trading members can now trade these securities on BSE debt platform following standard debt trading procedures with 1 paise tick size.
