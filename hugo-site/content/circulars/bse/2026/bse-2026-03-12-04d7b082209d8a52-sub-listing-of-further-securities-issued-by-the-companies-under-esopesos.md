## Summary

BSE has notified market participants that further new securities issued by five companies under their Employee Stock Option Plan (ESOP) / Employee Stock Option Scheme (ESOS) are listed and admitted for trading on the Exchange effective Friday, March 13, 2026. None of the newly listed shares carry any lock-in restrictions.

## Key Points

- 5 companies have had additional ESOP/ESOS shares listed on BSE effective March 13, 2026
- No lock-in period applies to any of the newly listed securities
- Total shares listed range from 906 (UTI AMC) to 100,000,000 (Billionbrains Garage Ventures)
- Companies span insurance, entertainment, technology, and asset management sectors

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification under existing ESOP/ESOS framework.

## Compliance Requirements

Market participants are requested to take note of the new securities and their ISIN/scrip code details for trading purposes.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** Friday, March 13, 2026 — new securities admitted for trading on BSE
- **Notice Date:** March 12, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. These are routine ESOP/ESOS allotments resulting in marginal dilution for each company. Details of newly listed securities:
