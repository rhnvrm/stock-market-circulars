## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document (DRHP) of Zebion Infotech Limited, filed on March 07, 2026, on the BSE SME website. The document contains all disclosures as required under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and is available for public review and comments for a minimum period of 21 days.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document of Zebion Infotech Limited filed on March 07, 2026
- Document complies with Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website: https://www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public comments invited within 21 days from the date of notice (March 09, 2026)
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard procedural filing under the ICDR (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, Schedule VI.

## Compliance Requirements

- Investors and public stakeholders may review the Draft Offer Document on the BSE SME website
- Any comments or objections must be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com within 21 days from March 09, 2026
- No action required from existing market participants or listed entities

## Important Dates

- **Filing Date:** March 07, 2026
- **Notice Date:** March 09, 2026
- **Public Comment Deadline:** March 30, 2026 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market-wide impact. It pertains solely to the pre-IPO regulatory process for Zebion Infotech Limited on the BSE SME platform. The document is made available as part of standard SEBI-mandated transparency requirements before an SME IPO proceeds. Potential investors in the SME segment may wish to review the offer document during the comment period.
