## Summary

BSE Clearing Corporation Limited (BSECL) has issued a notice revising the timings for collateral addition, release, and allocation processes. This change falls under the Settlement/RMS (Risk Management System) category and applies to the General segment.

## Key Points

- Notice issued by BSECL under Settlement/RMS category
- Revision pertains to timing windows for collateral addition, release, and allocation
- Applies to the General trading segment
- No attachment provided with the notice; details communicated via the notice text itself

## Regulatory Changes

The circular revises the operational timings governing when trading members and clearing members can add collateral, release collateral, and have collateral allocated within BSE's clearing and settlement framework.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members, clearing members, and custodians should update their internal operational schedules and systems to align with the revised collateral timing windows to ensure timely compliance with margin and collateral obligations.

## Important Dates

Effective from 22 Jul 2026 (notice date), per Notice No. 20260722-8.

## Impact Assessment

This is an operational/administrative change affecting back-office and risk management processes for trading and clearing members. It does not directly impact listed securities, stock prices, or trading of specific instruments, but requires operational adjustment by market intermediaries to avoid margin shortfalls or settlement delays.
