## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Kedia Construction Co. Ltd (BSE: 508993) has fixed **29 June 2026** as the record date for reduction of equity share capital pursuant to a Scheme of Arrangement and Amalgamation approved by the Hon'ble National Company Law Tribunal (NCLT), Mumbai Bench. Trading members are directed not to deal in the company's equity shares effective 29 June 2026.

## Key Points

- Record date for share capital reduction set at **29 June 2026**
- Trading/dealings in equity shares of Kedia Construction suspended effective **29 June 2026** (DR-660/2026-2027)
- Share capital reduced from Rs. 1,50,00,000/- to Rs. 30,00,000/- — an **80% reduction** in total paid-up capital
- Number of shares remains unchanged at **30,00,000 (Thirty Lakh)** equity shares
- Face value reduced from **Rs. 5/- per share to Re. 1/- per share**
- Action is pursuant to NCLT, Mumbai Bench order under a Scheme of Arrangement and Amalgamation

## Regulatory Changes

The reduction is approved under a Scheme of Arrangement and Amalgamation sanctioned by the National Company Law Tribunal (NCLT), Mumbai Bench. The issued, subscribed, and paid-up equity share capital of the company stands reduced from Rs. 1,50,00,000/- divided into 30,00,000 fully paid-up equity shares of Rs. 5/- each, to Rs. 30,00,000/- divided into 30,00,000 equity shares of Re. 1/- each.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading members** are advised not to deal in the equity shares of Kedia Construction Co. Ltd (508993) effective 29 June 2026
- Members must comply with the no-dealings directive under DR-660/2026-2027
- All members should update client records to reflect the revised face value of Re. 1/- per share post record date

## Impact Assessment

This capital reduction has a **high impact** on existing shareholders and market participants:

- The face value of each equity share is reduced by 80% (from Rs. 5/- to Re. 1/-), which will affect book value calculations, dividend per share metrics, and regulatory capital ratios
- The total paid-up capital drops from Rs. 1.50 Crore to Rs. 30 Lakhs, reflecting the restructuring under the NCLT-approved scheme
- Trading in the stock is suspended from 29 June 2026; trading members must not execute any transactions in the scrip on or after that date
- Shareholders will hold the same number of shares post-reduction, but at a significantly lower face value — any scheme-related adjustments (e.g., amalgamation component) should be reviewed for further corporate action notifications
