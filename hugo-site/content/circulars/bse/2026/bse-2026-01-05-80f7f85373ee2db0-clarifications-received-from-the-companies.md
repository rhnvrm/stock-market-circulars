## Summary

BSE circular dated January 5, 2026, containing clarifications from two listed companies - Technopack Polymers Limited (Scrip Code: 543656) and Trent Limited (Scrip Code: 500251) - in response to exchange queries regarding significant movements in their share prices and trading volumes. Both companies confirmed compliance with disclosure obligations and stated that no undisclosed material information exists that could explain the market movements.

## Key Points

- Technopack Polymers Limited responded to BSE query dated January 2, 2026 (Ref: L/SURV/ONL/PV/AJ/2025-2026/3266) regarding significant price movement
- Trent Limited responded to BSE query dated January 5, 2026 (Ref: L/SURV/ONL/PV/APJ/2025-2026/836) regarding significant volume movement
- Both companies confirmed regular compliance with Regulation 30 of SEBI LODR 2015
- Both companies stated all material information has been disclosed to stock exchanges in a timely manner
- No pending announcements or undisclosed information that could affect share price/volume
- Both companies attribute the movements to market-driven forces beyond management's control

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

This circular demonstrates routine surveillance mechanism compliance:
- Listed companies must respond to exchange queries regarding unusual price/volume movements
- Companies must confirm adherence to continuous disclosure obligations under Regulation 30 of SEBI LODR 2015
- Companies must disclose if any undisclosed material information exists

## Important Dates

- January 5, 2026: BSE query sent to Trent Limited (12:44 PM)

**Market Impact**: Low - These are standard surveillance clarifications with no new material information disclosed. Both companies confirmed that price/volume movements are market-driven with no corporate actions or undisclosed developments.
