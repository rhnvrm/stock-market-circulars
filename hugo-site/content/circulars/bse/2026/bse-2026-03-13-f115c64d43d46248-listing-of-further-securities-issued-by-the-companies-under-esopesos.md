## Summary

BSE has notified all market participants that further new securities issued by 12 companies under their Employee Stock Option Schemes (ESOP/ESOS) are listed and admitted for trading on the Exchange with effect from Monday, March 16, 2026. None of the newly listed shares carry any lock-in period.

## Key Points

- 12 companies have listed additional shares arising from ESOP/ESOS exercises
- All new securities are effective for trading from March 16, 2026
- No lock-in period applies to any of the newly listed shares
- Total new shares range from 1,397 (Godrej Properties) to 223,525 (RBL Bank)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine administrative listing notification under BSE's standard ESOP/ESOS listing process.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action is required from market participants. The notice is for informational purposes to update participants on newly tradeable securities.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** March 13, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** Monday, March 16, 2026

## Impact Assessment
