## Summary

BSE notifies trading members that SEBI turnover fees for June 2026 across Cash, Derivatives, Currency Derivatives, Commodity Derivatives, Interest Rate Derivatives, New Debt, Repo, and EGR segments will attract GST at 18% (effective July 18, 2022). The combined fee plus GST will be debited from members' settlement (valan) or Exchange dues accounts on July 3, 2026.

## Key Points

- SEBI turnover fee is applicable to all major segments: Cash, Derivatives, Currency Derivatives, Commodity Derivatives, Interest Rate Derivatives, New Debt, Repo, and EGR.
- GST at 18% applies on top of the turnover fee, effective July 18, 2022.
- Debit will occur on the 3rd working day of the next month — July 3, 2026.
- Turnover data and fee breakdowns will be available in EXTRANET (current date > EQ > Transaction) on or before July 2, 2026.
- GST at 18% is payable on the amount in column no. 26 of the TF2A file.
- Invoices for June 2026 will be available in EXTRANET under: Periodic files > 2026 > ZIP file named SEBIJUN2026.Member_Code.
- Fee will be debited separately from the settlement account with an appropriate narration.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This notice reaffirms the existing framework under which SEBI turnover fees attract 18% GST (applicable since July 18, 2022), as referenced in Exchange Notice No. 20260529-1.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure sufficient funds are available in their settlement (valan) account or Exchange dues account before July 3, 2026.
- Members should download and review turnover fee files (TF2A through TF2J series) from EXTRANET on or before July 2, 2026.
- GST liability should be verified from column 26 of the TF2A file.
- Invoices must be downloaded from the specified EXTRANET path for record-keeping.

## Important Dates

- **On or before July 2, 2026**: Turnover data and invoices available in EXTRANET.
- **July 3, 2026**: SEBI turnover fees plus 18% GST debited from members' settlement/Exchange dues accounts.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine monthly operational notice with no market-wide impact. It affects trading members who must maintain adequate funds in their settlement accounts. The fee structure and GST rate remain unchanged from prior months. Members should verify their individual turnover figures using the TF2A–TF2J file series to anticipate the debit amount.
