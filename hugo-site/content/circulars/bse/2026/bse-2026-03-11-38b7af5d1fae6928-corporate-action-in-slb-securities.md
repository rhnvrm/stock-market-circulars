## Summary

BSE has notified corporate actions applicable to Securities Lending and Borrowing (SLB) segment participants for three securities. eClerx Services Limited is undergoing a bonus issue, while RailTel Corporation of India Limited and Indian Railway Finance Corporation are paying dividends. All actions share a record/ex date of March 13, 2026, with a shut period starting March 12, 2026.

## Key Points

- eClerx Services Limited will have a **bonus issue** with record date and ex date both on March 13, 2026, and a foreclosure date of March 13, 2026
- RailTel Corporation of India Limited will pay a **dividend** with record date and ex date on March 13, 2026
- Indian Railway Finance Corporation will pay a **dividend** with record date and ex date on March 13, 2026
- Shut period for all three securities runs from March 12, 2026 to March 13, 2026
- No foreclosure date applicable for the two dividend actions

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular applies existing SLB framework rules for handling corporate actions within the Securities Lending and Borrowing segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- SLB participants holding positions in ECLERX, RAILTEL, or IRFC must account for the shut period from March 12–13, 2026
- Lenders and borrowers in the SLB segment should ensure positions are settled or adjusted in accordance with BSE SLB corporate action procedures
- For eClerx, borrowers must be aware of the foreclosure date of March 13, 2026, which may trigger mandatory recall/return of securities

## Important Dates

| Security | Corporate Action | Ex Date | Record Date | Foreclosure Date | Shut Period |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| eClerx Services Limited | Bonus Issue | 13-Mar-2026 | 13-Mar-2026 | 13-Mar-2026 | 12-Mar-2026 to 13-Mar-2026 |
| RailTel Corporation of India Limited | Dividend | 13-Mar-2026 | 13-Mar-2026 | N/A | 12-Mar-2026 to 13-Mar-2026 |
| Indian Railway Finance Corporation | Dividend | 13-Mar-2026 | 13-Mar-2026 | N/A | 12-Mar-2026 to 13-Mar-2026 |

## Impact Assessment

The circular has a **medium impact** on SLB market participants. The bonus issue for eClerx Services Limited is particularly significant as it involves a foreclosure date, meaning outstanding SLB contracts may be forcibly closed. Dividend actions for RailTel and IRFC require appropriate dividend adjustment handling in the SLB segment. Participants with open SLB positions in these three securities must act before the shut period commences on March 12, 2026. Broader market impact is limited as this affects only SLB segment participants in these specific securities.
