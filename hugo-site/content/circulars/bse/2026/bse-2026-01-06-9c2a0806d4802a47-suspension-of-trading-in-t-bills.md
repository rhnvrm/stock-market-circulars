## Summary

BSE has issued a notice suspending trading in Treasury Bill 759CG2026 (Scrip Code: 800242, ISIN: IN0020150093) effective January 7, 2026, as the instrument is scheduled to mature on the redemption date. Trading members are advised to refrain from dealing in this T-Bill from the specified date.

## Key Points

- T-Bill 759CG2026 with ISIN IN0020150093 will mature on the redemption date
- Scrip Code: 800242
- Trading suspension effective from January 7, 2026
- Trading members must not deal with this T-Bill from the suspension date
- Notice reference: DR-793/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard procedural notice for security maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all trading activity in T-Bill 759CG2026 (Scrip Code: 800242) from January 7, 2026
- Members should update their trading systems to reflect the suspension
- No new deals should be executed in the mentioned T-Bill

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 6, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date**: January 7, 2026
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: As scheduled (specific date not mentioned in circular)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low. This is a routine administrative action affecting a single Treasury Bill approaching maturity. T-Bills are short-term government securities and their maturity is a planned event. The suspension affects only this specific instrument and is a standard market practice to prevent trading in securities close to redemption. No broader market disruption expected.
