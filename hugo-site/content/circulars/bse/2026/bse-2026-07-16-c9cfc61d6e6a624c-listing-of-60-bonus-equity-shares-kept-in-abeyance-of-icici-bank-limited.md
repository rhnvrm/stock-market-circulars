## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 60 bonus equity shares of ICICI Bank Limited (Scrip Code: 532174), which were previously kept in abeyance, have been listed and are permitted to trade with effect from Friday, July 17, 2026.

## Key Points

- Security: 60 Bonus Equity Shares of Rs. 2/- each, previously kept in abeyance
- ISIN: INE090A01021
- Scrip Code: 532174
- Distinctive numbers span three ranges: 45 shares (6412514909–6412514953), 5 shares (6412331084–6412331088), and 10 shares (6412331089–6412331098)
- Notice signed by Janardhan Wagle, Deputy Vice President, Listing Operations

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is an operational listing notice, not a regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

No action required from investors or trading members beyond standard trading procedures; the shares become tradable from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- Effective listing and trading date: Friday, July 17, 2026
- Notice date: July 16, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact given the very small quantity (60 shares) involved. This is a routine administrative listing of bonus shares that were held in abeyance, likely due to pending resolution of ownership or legal disputes on the original shareholding. No effect on ICICI Bank's overall share price, liquidity, or trading patterns is expected.
