## Summary

BSE has announced the delisting of Rolta India Ltd. (Scrip Code 500366) effective Monday, January 19, 2026. The delisting follows a Resolution Plan approved by the National Company Law Tribunal (NCLT), Mumbai Bench-I via order dated December 15, 2025. The company has confirmed compliance with all NCLT requirements for delisting.

## Key Points

- Rolta India Ltd. (Scrip Code 500366) will be delisted from BSE exchange records
- Delisting is pursuant to NCLT-approved Resolution Plan
- Company has confirmed compliance with NCLT order dated December 15, 2025
- Trading members must take note of this delisting action

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework changes. This is an implementation of existing delisting provisions under NCLT resolution proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must acknowledge and update their records regarding the delisting
- No trading will be permitted in Rolta India Ltd. shares post delisting date
- Trading members can contact Ms. Vaidehi Phatak (Tel: 022-2272 8646) for clarifications

## Important Dates

- **December 15, 2025**: NCLT order approving Resolution Plan
- **January 16, 2026**: BSE notice date
- **January 19, 2026**: Effective delisting date from BSE exchange records

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: High - Complete removal of trading facility for Rolta India Ltd. shares on BSE. Existing shareholders will be affected as per the NCLT-approved Resolution Plan terms.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update systems and remove the scrip from tradable securities. All pending orders and positions must be settled before the delisting date.

**Investor Impact**: Shareholders of Rolta India Ltd. will no longer be able to trade shares on BSE platform post January 19, 2026. Their rights and entitlements will be governed by the NCLT Resolution Plan.
