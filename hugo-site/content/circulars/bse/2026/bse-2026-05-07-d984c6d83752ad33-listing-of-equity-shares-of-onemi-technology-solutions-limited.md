## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members that the equity shares of OnEMI Technology Solutions Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange. The exact listing date and further security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- OnEMI Technology Solutions Limited equity shares approved for listing on BSE
- Scrip Code: 544754
- Trading Symbol: KISSHT
- ISIN: INE12F801023
- Actual listing date to be announced via a separate notice
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing admission notice under BSE's DCS-Listing department for the Equity segment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should take note of the upcoming listing of OnEMI Technology Solutions Limited shares. Members must await the subsequent notice from BSE for the confirmed listing date and full security details before initiating any dealings in this scrip.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: May 7, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced via separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This notice signals the imminent listing of OnEMI Technology Solutions Limited (brand name KISSHT, a fintech/buy-now-pay-later platform) on BSE. Market participants and investors interested in this company should monitor BSE for the follow-up notice confirming the exact listing date. The impact is moderate — it opens a new publicly traded equity on the exchange, expanding investment options in the fintech sector.
