## Summary

BSE has informed members that Kotak Mahindra Mutual Fund has introduced Specialized Investment Funds (SIF) in accordance with SEBI's Regulatory Framework circular dated February 27, 2025. Kotak Mahindra MUTUAL FUND_SIF has been activated on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective June 12, 2026, with the NFO scheme INFINITY HYBRID LONG-SHORT FUND SIF enabled for order routing from June 15, 2026.

## Key Points

- Kotak Mahindra MUTUAL FUND_SIF activated on BSE StAR MF Platform with effect from **June 12, 2026**.
- Transactions for NRI and Minor investors are permitted under Kotak Mahindra MUTUAL FUND_SIF schemes.
- NRI applications are **not accepted** from investors based in **USA** and **Canada**.
- INFINITY HYBRID LONG-SHORT FUND SIF – NFO scheme enabled for order routing from **June 15, 2026**.
- Three annexures provided: Regulatory Framework (I), Monitoring of Minimum Investment Threshold (II), and Detailed Transaction Process on BSE StAR MF Platform (III).

## Regulatory Changes

This activation is pursuant to SEBI circular **SEBI/HO/IMD/IMD-PoD-1/P/CIR/2025/26 dated February 27, 2025**, which established the Regulatory Framework for Specialized Investment Funds (SIF). The SIF category represents a new product type introduced by SEBI, sitting between mutual funds and portfolio management services, targeting sophisticated investors.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must follow the detailed transaction acceptance process outlined in **Annexure III** for processing SIF transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform.
- Members must ensure NRI applications from USA and Canada are not accepted under Kotak Mahindra MUTUAL FUND_SIF schemes.
- Members should monitor minimum investment thresholds as detailed in **Annexure II**.
- For queries, members may contact the Mutual Fund Helpdesk at **022-45720450 / 022-45720650** on business days between 8:00 a.m. and 7:00 p.m., or via the CRS portal.

## Impact Assessment

This notice primarily impacts mutual fund distributors, registered members, and investment advisers operating on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The introduction of SIF by Kotak Mahindra expands the product suite available for eligible investors on the platform. The restriction on NRI investors from the USA and Canada aligns with standard FEMA/FATCA compliance norms applicable to such jurisdictions. No impact on equity, derivatives, or debt markets is anticipated. Members transacting on BSE StAR MF must update their systems to accommodate the new SIF transaction workflow per Annexure III before June 15, 2026.
