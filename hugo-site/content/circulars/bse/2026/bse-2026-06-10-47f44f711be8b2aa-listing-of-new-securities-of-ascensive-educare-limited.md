## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 50,00,000 (50 lakh) new equity shares of Ascensive Educare Limited (Scrip Code: 543443) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Thursday, June 11, 2026. These shares were issued on a preferential basis to Promoters pursuant to the conversion of warrants at an issue price of Rs. 3.32/- per share (face value Re. 1/- plus premium of Rs. 2.32/-).

## Key Points

- 50,00,000 equity shares of Re. 1/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 2.32/- (total issue price Rs. 3.32/-) to Promoters on a preferential basis
- Shares issued pursuant to conversion of warrants
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 40851161 to 45851160
- ISIN: INE0E4I01027
- Entire lot of 50,00,000 shares subject to lock-in until December 10, 2027

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice informing trading members of the commencement of trading in newly listed securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the listing and permit trading in these securities from June 11, 2026 onwards. The lock-in restriction on all 50,00,000 shares must be observed until December 10, 2027.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** September 4, 2024
- **Trading Commencement Date:** June 11, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry Date:** December 10, 2027

## Impact Assessment

This listing adds 50 lakh shares to the tradeable float of Ascensive Educare Limited (Scrip Code 543443), though the entire allotment is under lock-in until December 2027 and thus not immediately tradeable by the allottees. The preferential allotment to Promoters via warrant conversion at Rs. 3.32/- per share results in minor equity dilution for existing shareholders. Overall market impact is minimal given the lock-in period and the size of the issuance.
