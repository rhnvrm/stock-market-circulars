## Summary

BSE has listed six new commercial papers (CPs) issued on a private placement basis on its Debt Segment, effective February 27, 2026 (Notice No. 20260227-28). All securities are traded exclusively in dematerialised form in standard denominations of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof, with a tick size of 1 paise.

## Key Points

- All six instruments are Commercial Papers issued at a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, traded only in demat form
- Standard denomination for trading: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof; tick size: 1 paise
- Contact for clarifications: BSE Debt Department at 22728352 / 8597 / 8995 / 5753 / 8915
- Signed by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification under existing BSE Debt Segment framework for privately placed securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all transactions in these CPs are conducted only in dematerialised form
- Trading must be in standard denominations of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members should note the respective ISIN numbers for each instrument before executing trades
