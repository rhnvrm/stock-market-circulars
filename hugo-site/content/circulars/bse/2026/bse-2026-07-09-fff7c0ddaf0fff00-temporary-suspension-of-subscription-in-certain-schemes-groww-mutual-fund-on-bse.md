## Summary

BSE has informed members that GROWW Mutual Fund has requested a temporary suspension of subscriptions in certain schemes on the BSE StAR MF Platform. Effective July 10, 2026, lumpsum investments (fresh/additional purchases), switch-in transactions, and new systematic registrations (SIP/XSIP and STP-in) under the affected GROWW AMC schemes have been disabled on the platform.

## Key Points

- Lumpsum investments (fresh and additional purchases), switch-in transactions, and new systematic registrations (SIP/XSIP and STP-in) under specific GROWW Mutual Fund schemes (listed in the Annexure) have been disabled on BSE StAR MF.
- Applications received after the applicable cut-off time on July 09, 2026, will not be accepted or processed.
- The suspension takes effect from close of business on July 09, 2026.
- Existing SIP/STP-IN registrations made prior to the effective date will continue to be processed by the AMC/RTA without change.
- Redemption, Switch-out, STP-out, and SWP transactions remain permitted.
- Details of the affected schemes are provided in the Annexure and the Notice-cum-Addendum issued by GROWW AMC, attached to the notice.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is an operational platform update made by BSE at the request of GROWW Mutual Fund (AMC) to temporarily halt fresh inflows into select schemes via the StAR MF platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members/distributors must stop accepting lumpsum purchase, switch-in, and new SIP/STP-in applications for the affected GROWW schemes after the July 09, 2026 cut-off.
- Members should continue processing redemptions, switch-outs, STP-outs, and SWPs as normal.
- Existing SIP/STP-IN mandates registered before the effective date will continue uninterrupted at the AMC/RTA level.
- Members should refer to the attached Annexure for the specific list of affected schemes.

## Important Dates

- **July 09, 2026 (close of business)**: Suspension of subscriptions, switch-ins, and new SIP/STP-in registrations takes effect for affected schemes.
- **July 10, 2026**: Effective date communicated by GROWW Mutual Fund for the suspension.

## Impact Assessment

The restriction is limited to specific GROWW Mutual Fund schemes and affects only fresh subscription-related transactions on the BSE StAR MF platform; redemptions and switch-outs are unaffected, minimizing disruption for existing investors. Mutual fund distributors and investors intending to make fresh investments, switches, or start new systematic plans in the affected schemes will need to route through alternate channels or wait until the suspension is lifted. There is no direct impact on listed equity trading or broader market operations.
