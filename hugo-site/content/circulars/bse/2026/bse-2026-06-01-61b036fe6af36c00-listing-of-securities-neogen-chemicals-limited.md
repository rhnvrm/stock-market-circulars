## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 10,00,000 new equity shares of Neogen Chemicals Limited (Scrip Code: 542665) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Tuesday, June 2, 2026. These shares were issued to promoters on a preferential basis at an issue price of Rs. 1610/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- with a premium of Rs. 1600/-) and are subject to a lock-in period until December 1, 2027.

## Key Points

- 10,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 1600/- (issue price Rs. 1610/-) to promoters on a preferential basis
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 26381675 to 27381674
- ISIN: INE136S01016
- Entire allotment of 10,00,000 shares is under lock-in until December 1, 2027
- Trading permitted from June 2, 2026 (subject to lock-in restrictions)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for newly allotted securities following a preferential issue by Neogen Chemicals Limited.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new listing and must update their systems accordingly
- The lock-in on the entire allotment (26381675 to 27381674) must be observed until December 1, 2027
- No transfers or trades of locked-in shares are permitted during the lock-in period

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** April 18, 2026
- **Effective Date for Trading:** June 2, 2026 (Tuesday)
- **Lock-in Expiry:** December 1, 2027

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 10,00,000 new equity shares to the tradeable capital base of Neogen Chemicals Limited. However, since the entire allotment is subject to a lock-in until December 1, 2027, there is no immediate addition to the free-float. The preferential issue to promoters at Rs. 1610/- per share raises the promoter holding and may signal confidence in the company's growth prospects. Market impact is expected to be minimal in the short term given the lock-in constraints.
