## Summary

BSE has informed members that THE WEALTH COMPANY LARGE & MID CAP FUND will reopen for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP facility on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective June 12, 2026. This follows the earlier suspension communicated via Notice No. 20260520-32 dated May 20, 2026.

## Key Points

- Six scheme variants across Regular and Direct plans are being made available for ongoing transactions.
- Transactions supported include subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) facility.
- All six schemes are categorised as Equity (Large & Mid Cap Fund).
- The reopening is effective from June 12, 2026 on BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This notice restores normal transaction availability following the temporary suspension notified on May 20, 2026.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of the BSE StAR MF Platform may resume processing subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP transactions for the listed schemes from June 12, 2026 onwards.

## Important Dates

- **June 12, 2026**: Effective date for reopening of ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform.
- **May 20, 2026**: Date of the prior notice (No. 20260520-32) that suspended transactions.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational update with limited market impact. Investors and distributors using BSE StAR MF Platform will regain access to transact in all six variants of THE WEALTH COMPANY LARGE & MID CAP FUND. The scheme details are as follows:
