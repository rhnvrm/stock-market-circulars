## Summary

BSE has announced that BANDHAN GOLD ETF FOF (Fund of Fund) will reopen for ongoing transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective January 28, 2026. The scheme will be available for subscription, redemption, and systematic transaction facilities (SIP, STP, SWP) for both Regular and Direct plans.

## Key Points

- BANDHAN GOLD ETF FOF reopening for ongoing transactions from January 28, 2026
- Available on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Facilities available: Subscription, Redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Two plan options: Regular Plan - Growth and Direct Plan - Growth
- ISIN for Regular Plan: INF194KB1KK5
- ISIN for Direct Plan: INF194KB1KL3
- Scheme type: Equity (Gold ETF Fund of Fund)
- Reference to previous notice: 20260109-39

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding the availability of a mutual fund scheme for transactions.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. This is an informational notice for investors and distributors interested in transacting in BANDHAN GOLD ETF FOF through the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** January 27, 2026
- **Effective Date:** January 28, 2026 - Reopening of BANDHAN GOLD ETF FOF for ongoing transactions

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This is a routine operational announcement affecting a single mutual fund scheme. It provides transaction access to investors interested in gold ETF exposure through a fund of fund structure.

**Investor Impact:** Positive for investors seeking gold exposure through mutual funds, as it enables ongoing investments and redemptions along with systematic transaction facilities on the BSE platform.

**Operational Impact:** Minimal - Standard mutual fund transaction processing through existing BSE StAR MF Platform infrastructure.
