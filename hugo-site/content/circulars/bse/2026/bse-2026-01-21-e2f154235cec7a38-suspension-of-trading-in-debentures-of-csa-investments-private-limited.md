## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in 12% Non-Convertible Debentures of CSA INVESTMENTS PRIVATE LIMITED (ISIN: INE0LWH07062, Code: 975908) effective January 28, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing the record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to CSAIPL-12%-12-2-26-PVT debentures
- ISIN: INE0LWH07062, BSE Code: 975908
- No dealings permitted from January 28, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Notice number: DR-807/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notification for trading suspension due to record date fixing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in the specified debentures from January 28, 2026
- No buy or sell orders should be accepted for these debentures
- Members must ensure compliance with the trading suspension directive
- All pending orders should be reviewed and cancelled if necessary

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 21, 2026
- **Record Date**: January 28, 2026
- **No Dealings From**: January 28, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: High - Complete trading halt affects liquidity and existing positions in these debentures.

**Investor Impact**: High - Investors holding these debentures cannot trade them from the suspension date. Only those holding debentures as of the record date will be eligible for interest payment and redemption proceeds.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to block trading in these securities and communicate the suspension to clients holding or interested in these debentures.
