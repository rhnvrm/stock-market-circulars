## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of units of Axis Fixed Maturity Plan - Series 129 (108 Days) Direct IDCW Payout, a close-ended debt scheme under Axis Mutual Fund, effective Monday, March 2, 2026. The scheme is classified under Group F on BSE with scrip code 544712.

## Key Points

- Units listed under Group F on BSE effective March 2, 2026
- Scrip Code: 544712; Scrip ID on BOLT: AXISFMP129
- Total units: 39,989 at face value and issue price of Rs. 10.00 per unit
- ISIN: INF846KA1465
- Date of allotment: February 24, 2026
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Trading permitted only in dematerialized form per SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000
- Investment Manager: Axis Asset Management Company Limited
- Registrar and Transfer Agent: KFin Technologies Limited, Mumbai

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. Listing is conducted in accordance with existing SEBI guidelines mandating dematerialized trading for mutual fund units.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade units only in dematerialized form
- Trades to be executed in unit market lot of one unit
- Members may contact Parag Jain (Manager, Listing Operations) at BSE (022-2272 8685 / mf.listing@bseindia.com) or Darshan Kapadia (Compliance Officer, Axis AMC) for clarifications

## Important Dates

- **February 24, 2026**: Date of allotment of units
- **March 2, 2026**: Effective date of listing and commencement of trading on BSE

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing of a small close-ended debt mutual fund scheme with 39,989 units. The scheme carries relatively low interest rate risk and moderate credit risk. Market impact is minimal given the limited unit count and the nature of the instrument as a fixed maturity plan targeting a specific investment horizon of 108 days.
