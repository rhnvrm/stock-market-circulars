## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the debt security IKFL-9.95%-25-7-26-PVT (ISIN INE859C07170, Scrip Code 975853) issued by IKF Finance Limited will mature on its maturity date. Consequently, trading members are advised not to deal in this debt security with effect from July 23, 2026.

## Key Points

- The debt security IKFL-9.95%-25-7-26-PVT of IKF Finance Limited is maturing.
- ISIN: INE859C07170; Scrip Code: 975853.
- Trading members are advised not to deal in the security from July 23, 2026 onwards.
- Reference: DR-678/2025-2026.
- Issued under the authority of Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President – Listing Compliance and Operations.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a routine operational notice regarding suspension of trading due to security maturity, in line with standard BSE debt segment procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must cease dealing in the specified debt security (ISIN INE859C07170) effective July 23, 2026, and ensure no further trades are executed in this instrument after the maturity date.

## Important Dates

- Maturity Date: July 22, 2026
- Trading Suspension Effective: July 23, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains to a single debt security from IKF Finance Limited reaching its scheduled maturity. It is a standard administrative action in the debt segment with no implications for other securities or broader market operations.
