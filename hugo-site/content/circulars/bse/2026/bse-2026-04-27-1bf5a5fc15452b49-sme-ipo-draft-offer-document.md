## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document (DRHP) of **Maharaja & Speedex India Limited**, filed on April 24, 2026, on the BSE SME website. The document contains all disclosures as required under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and is open for public comments for a minimum period of 21 days from the date of the notice.

## Key Points

- The Draft Offer Document was filed by Maharaja & Speedex India Limited on April 24, 2026.
- The document is hosted on the BSE SME website in compliance with ICDR Regulations (Schedule VI).
- Public comments are invited within 21 days from April 27, 2026 (deadline: approximately May 18, 2026).
- Comments should be submitted to: equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- The DRHP is accessible at the BSE SME portal.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural notice under the SEBI (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, requiring public display of the draft offer document for at least 21 days.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Public / Investors / Analysts:** May review the Draft Offer Document and submit comments to equity.ipo@bseindia.com within 21 days of the notice date.
- **Maharaja & Speedex India Limited:** Must address any public comments received before proceeding with the IPO.

| Event | Date |

## Impact Assessment

This notice has a narrow, company-specific impact limited to Maharaja & Speedex India Limited's upcoming SME IPO. There is no broader market or operational impact. Investors and analysts interested in participating in or commenting on this SME IPO should review the DRHP within the stipulated 21-day window.
