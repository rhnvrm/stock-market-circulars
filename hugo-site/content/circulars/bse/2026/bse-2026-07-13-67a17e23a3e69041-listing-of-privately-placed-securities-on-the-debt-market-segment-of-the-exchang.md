## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that new commercial paper securities issued on a private placement basis by five companies have been listed and admitted to trading on the BSE Debt Segment effective July 13, 2026. The securities will trade in dematerialised form in standard denominations of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof, with a tick size of 1 paise.

## Key Points

- Five companies have fresh commercial paper listings: Navi Finserv Limited, UGRO Capital Limited, SG Finserve Limited, Titan Company Limited, and Electrosteel Castings Ltd.
- Navi Finserv Limited: ISIN INE342T14GY8, Scrip Code 732076, 200 units of Rs. 5,00,000 face value, CARE A1 rating, 10% p.a. interest, allotted 10/07/2026, redemption 07/12/2026.
- UGRO Capital Limited: ISIN INE583D14956, Scrip Code 732077, 400 units, IND A1+ rating, 9% p.a. interest, allotted 10/07/2026, redemption 06/01/2027.
- SG Finserve Limited: ISIN INE618R14042, Scrip Code 732078, 600 units, ICRA A1+ rating, 7.30% p.a. interest, allotted 10/07/2026, redemption 31/08/2026.
- Titan Company Limited: ISIN INE280A14583, Scrip Code 732074, 16,500 units, CARE A1+ rating, 6.75% p.a. interest, allotted 10/07/2026, redemption 08/10/2026.
- Electrosteel Castings Ltd.: ISIN INE086A14899, Scrip Code 732075, 2,000 units, CRISIL A1+/IND A1+ rating, 6.55% p.a. interest, allotted 10/07/2026, redemption 08/10/2026.
- All securities trade only in dematerialised form under their respective ISIN numbers.
- Trading standard denomination is Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof, with a tick size of 1 paise.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a routine listing notification under BSE's DCS-Listing department for the Debt Segment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must note the trading parameters (denomination, tick size, dematerialised form) for these newly listed commercial papers. Queries can be directed to the BSE debt department at the listed contact numbers.

## Important Dates

- Deemed date of allotment (all securities): July 10, 2026
- Effective listing date: July 13, 2026
- Redemption dates: Navi Finserv - December 7, 2026; UGRO Capital - January 6, 2027; SG Finserve - August 31, 2026; Titan Company - October 8, 2026; Electrosteel Castings - October 8, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative listing notice with limited direct market impact. It confirms admission of new short-term debt instruments (commercial papers) to trading, primarily relevant to debt market participants and trading members dealing in these specific ISINs. Titan Company Limited's large issuance (16,500 units, ~Rs. 825 crore face value) is notable given its scale relative to the other issuers, but as a commercial paper listing it reflects normal treasury/working capital financing rather than a material corporate event for equity investors.
