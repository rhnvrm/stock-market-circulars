## Summary

BSE Limited has issued a notice informing trading members that the equity shares of Elfin Agro India Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the BSE SME segment. The exact date of listing and further security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Elfin Agro India Limited equity shares are to be listed on the BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544724
- Symbol: ELFIN
- ISIN: INE1FEW01013
- The specific listing date will be announced via a separate notice
- Notice issued by Nitinkumar Pujari, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing admission notice under BSE SME segment rules.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to prepare for the admission of Elfin Agro India Limited shares to dealings. No immediate compliance action is required until the listing date notice is issued.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: March 11, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced via a separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine new listing announcement for a small/medium enterprise. Market impact is limited to participants interested in the SME segment. The actual trading activity will commence only after the listing date is formally announced in a subsequent notice.
