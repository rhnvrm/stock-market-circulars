## Summary

BSE has informed members that GROWW BSE HOSPITALS ETF FOF will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption, including SIP, STP & SWP facilities, on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective March 09, 2026. This follows the earlier Notice No. 20260210-41.

## Key Points

- GROWW BSE HOSPITALS ETF FOF reopens for ongoing transactions from March 09, 2026
- Available on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Facilities include: subscription, redemption, SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan)
- Six scheme variants available across Regular and Direct plans with Growth and IDCW options

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notice regarding the availability of an existing mutual fund scheme for ongoing transactions.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members. Members and distributors should update their systems to reflect the availability of these schemes for ongoing transactions from March 09, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **March 09, 2026**: Effective date for reopening of GROWW BSE HOSPITALS ETF FOF for ongoing subscription, redemption, and SIP/STP/SWP transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This notice enables investors to transact in GROWW BSE HOSPITALS ETF FOF schemes through the BSE StAR MF Platform following a period of suspension (NFO or temporary closure). Affects distributors and investors using the BSE StAR MF Platform for this specific fund family.
