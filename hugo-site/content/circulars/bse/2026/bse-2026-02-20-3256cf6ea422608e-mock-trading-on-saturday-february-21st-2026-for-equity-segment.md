## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Equity segment on Saturday, February 21, 2026. Trading members using third-party trading platforms are encouraged to use this session to test their trading applications across various functionalities including exceptional market conditions. There will be no new BOLT TWS release associated with this session.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Saturday, February 21, 2026 for the Equity segment
- Third-party trading platform users can test application functionalities during the session
- Functionalities covered include: call auction sessions, risk-reduction mode, trading halt, block deals
- No new BOLT TWS Release will accompany this session
- Members are requested to submit feedback by 3:00 PM on the day of the mock session

## Impact Assessment

This circular has no market or financial impact as it pertains solely to a mock (simulated) trading session. The session is intended to ensure trading member systems are operationally ready and tested for scenarios such as auction mechanisms, risk-reduction mode, and block deal windows. Participation is voluntary but strongly encouraged to maintain system reliability and readiness.
