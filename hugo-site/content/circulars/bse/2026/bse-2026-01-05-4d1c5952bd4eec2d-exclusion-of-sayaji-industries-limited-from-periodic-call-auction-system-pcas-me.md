## Summary

BSE has announced that Sayaji Industries Limited (Scrip Code: 540728, ISIN: INE327G01024) will be excluded from the Periodic Call Auction System (PCAS) mechanism effective from January 06, 2026. This follows the company's listing notification from October 05, 2017.

## Key Points

- Sayaji Industries Limited to be excluded from PCAS mechanism
- Effective date: Tuesday, January 06, 2026
- Scrip Code: 540728
- ISIN Number: INE327G01024
- Notice references original listing notice 20171005-43 dated October 05, 2017

## Regulatory Changes

The securities of Sayaji Industries Limited will transition from PCAS mechanism to regular trading framework. This exclusion typically indicates that the stock has met the criteria for normal trading and no longer requires the periodic call auction mechanism.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members of the Exchange must:
- Note the exclusion of Sayaji Industries Limited from PCAS effective January 06, 2026
- Adjust their trading systems and processes accordingly
- Contact Mr. Parag Jain (Manager - Listing Compliance & Operations) at 022-2272 8685 or direct.listing@bseindia.com for clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** January 05, 2026
- **Effective Date:** January 06, 2026 (Tuesday)
- **Original Listing Notice:** October 05, 2017 (Notice No. 20171005-43)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Positive for Sayaji Industries Limited shareholders as exclusion from PCAS indicates improved liquidity and trading conditions. The stock will be available for normal continuous trading instead of periodic call auctions.

**Operational Impact:** Minimal impact on trading members who will need to update their systems to reflect normal trading parameters for this security. The change enables better price discovery and increased trading flexibility for market participants.
