## Summary

BSE's surveillance department sought clarifications from three listed companies — Himadri Speciality Chemical Ltd (BSE: 500184), Lloyds Metals and Energy Ltd (BSE: 512455), and Mazagon Dock Shipbuilders Ltd — regarding unusual increases in trading volumes. All companies responded on 06 March 2026 confirming full compliance with SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015 and the absence of any undisclosed or price-sensitive information.

## Key Points

- BSE surveillance team issued reference no. L/SURV/ONL/PV/APJ/2025-2026/954 to Himadri Speciality Chemical and L/SURV/ONL/PV/APJ/2025-2026/956 to Lloyds Metals and Energy on 06 March 2026.
- Himadri Speciality Chemical Ltd confirmed no undisclosed/price-sensitive information or impending announcement/corporate action.
- Lloyds Metals and Energy Ltd stated the volume movement is purely market-driven and attributable to prevailing market conditions; management has no knowledge of reasons for the increase.
- All companies reaffirmed continued compliance with Regulation 30 of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015 for timely dissemination of UPSI.
- Mazagon Dock Shipbuilders Ltd was also included in the circular (full response content partially unreadable due to OCR issues in source PDF).

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This circular is a dissemination of company responses to BSE surveillance queries under existing SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015.

## Compliance Requirements

- No new compliance requirements for the market.
- The responding companies have reaffirmed ongoing compliance with Regulation 30 of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015 regarding timely disclosure of material events and UPSI.

## Important Dates

- 06 March 2026: BSE surveillance letters issued and company clarifications submitted.

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. These are standard surveillance-driven clarification responses. No corporate actions, earnings surprises, or regulatory actions are indicated. Investors should note that the volume spikes in HIMADRI, LLOYDMETAL, and MAZDOCK scrips have been flagged by BSE surveillance but no material information was found to be withheld by the companies. Volume movements are attributed to broader market conditions.
