## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document for Paluck Technologies Limited's SME IPO, filed on January 20, 2026. The document contains all disclosures as required under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and is available on the BSE SME website for public comments for a period of 21 days.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Paluck Technologies Limited on January 20, 2026
- Document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website for minimum 21 days for public comments
- Comments can be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com within 21 days from notice date
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard notification as per ICDR Regulations for SME IPO draft offer documents.

## Compliance Requirements

- The Draft Offer Document is made available for public scrutiny as per regulatory requirements
- Stakeholders and public can review and submit comments within 21 days from January 27, 2026
- Comments should be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Document available at: www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx

## Important Dates

- **Draft Filing Date**: January 20, 2026
- **Notice Date**: January 27, 2026
- **Public Comment Period**: 21 days from January 27, 2026 (until February 17, 2026)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine notification for an SME segment IPO draft document.

**Investor Impact**: Low to Medium for potential SME IPO investors - Those interested in investing in Paluck Technologies Limited should review the draft offer document and may submit comments during the 21-day period.

**Operational Impact**: Informational only - Standard regulatory disclosure process for SME IPOs with no immediate operational changes required.
