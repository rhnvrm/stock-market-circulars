## Summary

Authum Investment & Infrastructure Limited has submitted an undertaking to BSE for the listing of 67,93,80,400 bonus equity shares issued under Chapter XI of SEBI (ICDR) Regulations, 2018. The company confirms that bonus shares will be credited to the depository system by 12 PM on January 14, 2026, and will be available for trading from the beginning of day on January 16, 2026.

## Key Points

- Total bonus shares to be listed: 67,93,80,400 equity shares
- Issued in compliance with Chapter XI of SEBI (ICDR) Regulations, 2018
- Depository credit deadline: 12 PM on January 14, 2026 (record date next working day)
- Trading commencement: Beginning of day (BOD) on January 16, 2026 (Friday)
- Submitted by Company Secretary and Compliance Officer Dipyanti Jaiswar

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard undertaking for bonus share listing as per existing SEBI (ICDR) Regulations, 2018.

## Compliance Requirements

- Company must credit bonus shares to depository system by specified deadline of 12 PM on January 14, 2026
- Shares must be made available for trading from BOD on January 16, 2026
- Formal undertaking to be submitted to BSE Department of Corporate Services

## Important Dates

- **January 14, 2026 (by 12 PM)**: Bonus shares to be credited in depository system (next working day of record date)
- **January 16, 2026 (Friday, BOD)**: Bonus shares available for trading

## Impact Assessment

This bonus issue represents a significant capital restructuring for Authum Investment & Infrastructure Limited with nearly 68 crore bonus shares being issued. Existing shareholders will see their shareholding increase proportionately based on the bonus ratio. The two-day gap between depository credit and trading availability ensures smooth settlement and reduces operational risk. Trading volume may increase post-listing as the expanded share base improves liquidity.
