## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that they are permitted to trade in new equity shares of AvenuesAI Ltd. (Scrip Code: 539807) with effect from Thursday, July 23, 2026. The new securities comprise 1,68,825 equity shares of Re.1/- each, issued pursuant to the conversion of partly paid rights shares into fully paid-up shares.

## Key Points

- 1,68,825 equity shares of Re.1/- each have been listed following conversion of partly paid rights shares to fully paid-up shares.
- ISIN for the securities: INE483S01020.
- Distinctive numbers: 1,204 shares (3001112874–3001114077) and 1,67,621 shares (3471499497–3471667117).
- These new shares rank pari-passu with the existing equity shares of the company.
- Notice issued and signed by Janardhan Wagle, Deputy Vice President, BSE.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an administrative listing notice permitting trading in newly issued fully paid-up equity shares under the existing ISIN.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should update their systems to recognize the additional fully paid-up shares as tradable and ensure the distinctive number ranges are reflected correctly in back-office records.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 22, 2026
- Effective date for trading in new securities: Thursday, July 23, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is minimal and operational in nature. The listing adds a relatively small number of shares (1,68,825) arising from conversion of partly paid rights shares, which increases the fully paid-up equity base marginally but does not represent a fresh capital raise or corporate action with market-moving significance. Trading members need only ensure accurate recognition of the new fully paid-up shares from the effective date.
