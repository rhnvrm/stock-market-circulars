## Summary

Indian Clearing Corporation Limited (ICCL) has issued the client margin reporting schedule for July 2026 for the Electronic Gold Receipts (EGR) segment. The notice specifies trade dates, corresponding last dates for margin reporting, and penalty dates for all trading days in July 2026.

## Key Points

- Covers all 23 trading days in July 2026 for the Electronic Gold Receipts segment.
- Each trade date has a corresponding last date for margin reporting, typically T+5 working days.
- Penalty dates follow the reporting deadline, typically the next working day after the last reporting date.
- Late reporting beyond the last date will attract penalties on the designated penalty date.
- Issued by ICCL on behalf of BSE for the EGR segment.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a routine monthly operational schedule issued by ICCL for margin compliance in the Electronic Gold Receipts segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members trading in the Electronic Gold Receipts segment must report client margins by the specified last date for each trade date.
- Failure to report within the deadline will result in penalties levied on the respective penalty date.
- Members should ensure timely submission of margin data for all 23 trading days listed in July 2026.
