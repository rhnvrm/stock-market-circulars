## Summary

BSE's Surveillance department (DOSS) sought clarifications from Piramal Pharma Ltd and Eraaya Lifespaces Ltd regarding news articles appearing in various newspapers, websites, and television channels. Replies from both companies have been received and are attached to the notice.

## Key Points

- BSE Notice No. 20260623-18 dated 23 June 2026
- Clarification sought to verify news accuracy and inform members/investors
- Piramal Pharma Ltd (Scrip Code: 543635): clarification relates to a news article on NDTV Profit
- Eraaya Lifespaces Ltd (Scrip Code: 531035): clarification relates to a news article on Moneycontrol
- Replies from both companies are attached to the notice

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine surveillance disclosure notice.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. Companies have already responded with their clarifications as attached to this notice.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 23 June 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard BSE surveillance measure to ensure transparency and accuracy of information available to market participants. Investors in Piramal Pharma Ltd and Eraaya Lifespaces Ltd should review the attached company clarifications for specific details regarding the news articles in question.
