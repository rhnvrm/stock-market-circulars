## Summary

BSE observed significant price/volume movement in 14 listed scrips and sought clarifications from the respective companies. Replies have been received from 7 companies and are attached to the notice, while replies from 7 other companies remain awaited.

## Key Points

- Exchange wrote to companies with notable recent price/volume movement to ensure trading members and investors have access to relevant information.
- Companies that have replied: Alpine Housing Development Corporation Ltd (526519), eClerx Services Ltd (532927), Him Teknoforge Ltd (505712), Mrs. Bectors Food Specialities Ltd (543253), Seemax Resources Ltd (544813), Tarc Ltd (543249), Wakefit Innovations Ltd (544642).
- Companies whose replies are still awaited: CMX Holdings Ltd (532217), Crazy Snacks Ltd (544807), Darjeeling Industries Ltd (539770), Gconnect Logitech and Supply Chain Ltd (544156), Kinetic Trust Ltd (531274), Mapro Industries Ltd (509762), Sujala Trading & Holdings Ltd (539117).
- Actual reply content is contained in the attachments referenced by the notice, not reproduced in the notice text itself.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine surveillance disclosure notice, not a regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

Companies listed as "reply awaited" are expected to submit their clarification to the Exchange; investors and trading members should review the attached replies for the companies that have responded.

## Important Dates

Notice dated 23 July 2026.

## Impact Assessment

Low-to-medium market impact. This disclosure primarily serves investor awareness and surveillance transparency for the specific scrips named; it does not itself alter trading status but may precede further surveillance action (e.g., ASM/GSM placement) for companies that fail to respond or whose clarifications raise concerns.
