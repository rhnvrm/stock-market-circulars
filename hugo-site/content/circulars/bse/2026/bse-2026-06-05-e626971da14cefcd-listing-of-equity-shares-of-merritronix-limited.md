## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members that the equity shares of Merritronix Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The scrip is assigned to the SME segment. The specific listing date and further security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Merritronix Limited equity shares are to be listed on BSE's SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544773 | Symbol: MRTX | ISIN: INE1RQS01010
- Listing date has not yet been announced; a separate notice will follow
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, DCS-Listing, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard pre-listing intimation notice under BSE's DCS-Listing department.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the scrip details and await the subsequent notice from BSE confirming the listing date and any additional trading parameters before executing transactions in this scrip.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: June 5, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced via a separate BSE notice

## Impact Assessment

Limited broader market impact. This notice pertains solely to a new SME-segment scrip (Merritronix Limited). Relevant primarily to market participants interested in SME listings. Trading members should monitor BSE for the follow-up notice confirming the effective listing date.
