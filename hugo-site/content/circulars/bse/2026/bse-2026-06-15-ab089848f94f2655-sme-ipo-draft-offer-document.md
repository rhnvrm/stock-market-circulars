## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document of Cloud Interiors Limited, filed on June 11, 2026, on the BSE SME website. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and is available for public review for a minimum of 21 days. Stakeholders are invited to submit comments within this period.

## Key Points

- Cloud Interiors Limited filed its Draft Offer Document on June 11, 2026
- The document is hosted on the BSE SME website under the SME IPO section
- Public comments are invited within 21 days from the date of notice (i.e., by July 6, 2026)
- The document complies with Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations
- Comments should be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard disclosure pursuant to SEBI (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, requiring the Draft Offer Document to be made publicly available for at least 21 days prior to filing the final offer document.

## Compliance Requirements

- Interested parties and public may review the Draft Offer Document at: https://www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Any comments on the draft document must be submitted within 21 days from June 15, 2026
- Comments to be sent to: equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Draft Offer Document Filed:** June 11, 2026
- **Notice Date:** June 15, 2026
- **Public Comment Deadline:** On or before July 6, 2026 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal immediate market impact as Cloud Interiors Limited is a pre-listing SME company. The publication of the Draft Offer Document is a mandatory regulatory step under ICDR Regulations before the company can proceed with its SME IPO on BSE. Investors interested in the upcoming IPO should review the document and submit comments if any during the stipulated period.
