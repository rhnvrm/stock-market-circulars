## Summary

BSE has listed 6,46,674 new equity shares of Black Box Limited (Scrip Code: 500463) for trading on the Exchange with effect from Thursday, April 9, 2026. The shares were issued on a preferential basis to Non-Promoters pursuant to conversion of warrants at an issue price of Rs. 417/- per share (face value Rs. 2/- + premium of Rs. 415/-).

## Key Points

- **Company:** Black Box Limited (Scrip Code: 500463)
- **New Shares Listed:** 6,46,674 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 417/- per share (face value Rs. 2/- + premium Rs. 415/-)
- **Issued To:** Non-Promoters on a preferential basis
- **Purpose:** Conversion of warrants
- **ISIN:** INE676A01027
- **Distribution Numbers:** 170490723 to 171137396
- **Pari-passu:** New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notice for newly issued securities following a preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed that the new securities are listed and permitted to trade from April 9, 2026.
- Lock-in applies to all 6,46,674 shares (Dist. Nos. 170490723–171137396) until **October 20, 2026**.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** February 28, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** April 9, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry:** October 20, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing of 6,46,674 new shares represents a modest expansion of Black Box Limited's share capital. Since the shares were issued to Non-Promoters via warrant conversion at Rs. 417/-, the dilution effect is limited. All new shares are under lock-in until October 20, 2026, which restricts near-term selling pressure from these allottees. Existing shareholders should be aware of the dilution, while traders should note the increase in total tradeable float post lock-in expiry.
