## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 2,76,000 new equity shares of Kaarya Facilities and Services Limited (Scrip Code: 540756) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Thursday, March 5, 2026. The shares were issued on a preferential basis to non-promoters at an issue price of Rs. 13.09 per share.

## Key Points

- 2,76,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 3.09/- to non-promoters on a preferential basis
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 9350259 to 9626258
- Date of allotment: December 13, 2025
- Issue price: Rs. 13.09 per share
- ISIN: INE282Y01016
- All 2,76,000 shares are subject to lock-in until September 7, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification pursuant to a completed preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the lock-in restriction on all 2,76,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 9350259 to 9626258) until September 7, 2026
- These shares cannot be traded until the lock-in period expires on September 7, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** December 13, 2025
- **Trading Commencement Date:** March 5, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry Date:** September 7, 2026 (applicable to all 2,76,000 shares)

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 2,76,000 equity shares to the tradeable float of Kaarya Facilities and Services Limited (Scrip Code: 540756), though the entire allotment remains under lock-in until September 7, 2026. There is no immediate increase in free-float liquidity. Market impact is minimal given the small size of the allotment and the lock-in restriction.
