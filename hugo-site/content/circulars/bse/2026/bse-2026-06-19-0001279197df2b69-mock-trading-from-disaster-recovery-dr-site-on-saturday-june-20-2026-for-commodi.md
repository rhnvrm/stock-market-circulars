## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session from its Disaster Recovery (DR) site for the Commodity Derivatives segment on Saturday, June 20, 2026. The session runs from 8:15 AM to 12:00 PM. Participation is voluntary and trades will not create any margin, pay-in, or pay-out obligations.

## Key Points

- Mock trading will be conducted from BSE's DR site specifically for the Commodity Derivatives segment.
- Trading members using third-party platforms (empaneled vendors) or in-house systems via ETI APIs are encouraged to use this session to test their applications.
- Members may alternatively participate in the UAT environment to fulfil regulatory requirements per SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD1/DSAP/CIR/P/2020/234 dated November 24, 2020.
- Feedback from all members must be submitted by 1:00 PM on the day of the session.
- Trades executed during mock trading carry no legal, financial, or margin obligations.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This circular references the existing SEBI circular no. SEBI/HO/MRD1/DSAP/CIR/P/2020/234 (November 24, 2020) and Exchange Notice No. 20201201-22 (December 1, 2020) regarding testing of software used in or related to Trading and Risk Management.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are encouraged (not mandated) to participate in the mock trading session or the UAT environment to meet their regulatory software-testing obligations.
- Members must provide feedback on the mock trading session by 1:00 PM on June 20, 2026, to the contact numbers/email IDs provided.

## Important Dates

- **June 20, 2026 (Saturday):** Mock Trading Session
  - Log-in: 8:15 AM – 9:00 AM
  - Continuous Trading: 9:00 AM – 12:00 PM
  - Feedback deadline: 1:00 PM

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine operational drill to test DR site readiness for the Commodity Derivatives segment. No trades will result in actual obligations. Members using third-party or in-house trading systems benefit from the opportunity to validate their systems under simulated market conditions.
