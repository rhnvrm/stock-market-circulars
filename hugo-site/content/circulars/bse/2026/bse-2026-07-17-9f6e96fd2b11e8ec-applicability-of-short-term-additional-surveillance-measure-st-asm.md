## Summary

BSE has issued a notice identifying securities that satisfy the criteria for inclusion under the Short-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) Stage I and Stage II, as per the attached Annexure I. This action follows the ASM framework established under prior Exchange notices dated September 20, 2024, October 27, 2018, July 19, 2019, December 4, 2020, September 25, 2023, and September 20, 2024.

## Key Points

- Securities listed in Annexure I have met the criteria for ST-ASM Stage I inclusion.
- Securities listed in Annexure I have also met the criteria for ST-ASM Stage II inclusion.
- ST-ASM Stage I securities: applicable margin rate is 50% or the existing margin, whichever is higher, capped at a maximum of 100%.
- ST-ASM Stage II securities: applicable margin rate is 100% or the existing margin, whichever is higher, capped at a maximum of 100%.
- These margin requirements apply to all open positions as of July 20, 2026, and to new positions created from July 21, 2026 onward.
- The ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the Exchange.
- Inclusion under ASM is purely a market surveillance measure and should not be construed as an adverse action against the concerned company or entity.
- When a scrip exits the ASM framework, its price band reverts to the band applicable before shortlisting, unless it remains subject to another surveillance framework, in which case that framework's price band prevails.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced; this notice applies the existing ST-ASM framework (as revised via prior circulars) to a fresh set of securities identified as meeting the Stage I and Stage II criteria.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must apply the specified margin rates (50% for Stage I, 100% for Stage II, subject to a 100% cap) to all open and new positions in the identified securities.
- Members must ensure compliance from the effective date and monitor the securities listed in Annexure I.
- Queries or clarifications should be directed to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 17, 2026
- Margin measures effective: July 21, 2026
- Applicable to open positions as on July 20, 2026, and new positions from July 21, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The increased margin requirements (50%-100%) will raise the cost of trading and reduce leverage for market participants holding or initiating positions in the identified securities, likely dampening trading volumes and volatility in these names. Since specific securities are listed only in the attached Annexure (not included in the provided text), the precise scope of impacted stocks cannot be determined from this notice alone. The measure is a standard surveillance action rather than a punitive one, but it may still affect short-term liquidity and price behavior in the affected securities.
