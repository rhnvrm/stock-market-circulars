## Summary

BSE has announced the complete schedule for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) of NETLINK SOLUTIONS INDIA LIMITED. The offer is for up to 6,57,599 equity shares at an offer price of Rs. 201.00 per share. The bidding window will remain open from Wednesday, January 28, 2026 to Tuesday, February 10, 2026. Trading members and custodian members can participate through the iBBS and RTRMS platforms during specified session timings.

## Key Points

- **Security**: NETLINK SOLUTIONS INDIA LIMITED (Symbol: NETLINK, Scrip Code: 509040)
- **ISIN**: INE040F01033
- **Offer Size**: Up to 6,57,599 equity shares
- **Offer Price**: Rs. 201.00 per share
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10.00
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Offer Period**: January 28, 2026 to February 10, 2026
- **Bid Offering Period Timings**: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM
- **Give-up/Take-up Period**: January 28 to February 9, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Last Day Give-up**: February 10, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Last Day Take-up**: February 10, 2026 (9:15 AM to 4:00 PM - extended by 30 minutes)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular follows existing OTB operational guidelines as per Exchange Notices No. 20150930-1 (September 30, 2015) and No. 20170203-29 (February 3, 2017).

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members**: Must ensure investor UCC is registered/updated with correct PAN Number and Category in the Exchange UCC database
- **Eligibility**: Trading members can only bid for investors having shares in demat form
- **Platform Access**: Members must use iBBS platform (Internet: https://ibbs.bseindia.com/ or Leased Line: https://ibbsll.bseindia.com/) for bidding
- **RTRMS Access**: Available at https://rtrms.bseindia.com/ (Internet) or https://rtrmsll.bseindia.com/ (Leased Line)
- **File Access**: Extranet files for acceptance of bids will be available after issue closure upon confirmation from Merchant Banker/RTA
- **Categories for Bidding**: FII (Foreign Institutional Buyer), FI (Financial Institution), MF (Mutual Fund), IC (Insurance Companies), OTH (Other QIBs), NOH (Other NIB), CO (Company), IND (Individual)

## Important Dates

- **Opening Date**: Wednesday, January 28, 2026
- **Closing Date**: Tuesday, February 10, 2026
- **Bid Offering Period**: January 28 - February 10, 2026 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Give-up/Take-up Period**: January 28 - February 9, 2026 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Last Day for Give-up**: February 10, 2026 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Last Day for Take-up**: February 10, 2026 (9:15 AM - 4:00 PM)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a company-specific takeover offer affecting only NETLINK SOLUTIONS INDIA LIMITED shareholders. The offer size of 6,57,599 shares represents a targeted acquisition and will not have broader market implications.

**Operational Impact**: Medium for participating members - Trading members and custodians handling NETLINK shares will need to facilitate client participation through the iBBS/RTRMS platforms during the 14-day offer period. Members must ensure proper UCC registration and demat requirements are met.

**Investor Impact**: High for NETLINK shareholders - Existing shareholders have an opportunity to tender their shares at Rs. 201.00 per share during the offer period. Investors should evaluate the offer price against current market price and their investment objectives.
