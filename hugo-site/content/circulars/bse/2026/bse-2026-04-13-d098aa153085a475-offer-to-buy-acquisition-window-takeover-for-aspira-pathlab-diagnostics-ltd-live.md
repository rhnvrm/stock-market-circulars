## Summary

BSE has announced the live activities schedule for the Offer to Buy (OTB) – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for **ASPIRA PATHLAB & DIAGNOSTICS LTD** (Symbol: ASPIRA, Scrip Code: 540788, ISIN: INE500C01017). The offer window is open from Wednesday, 15th April 2026 to Tuesday, 28th April 2026, with an offer price of Rs. 55.00 per share for up to 26,76,180 equity shares.

## Key Points

- **Security:** ASPIRA PATHLAB & DIAGNOSTICS LTD (ASPIRA), Scrip Code 540788, ISIN INE500C01017
- **Offer Size:** Up to 26,76,180 equity shares
- **Offer Price:** Rs. 55.00 per share
- **Face Value:** Rs. 10.00
- **Market Lot:** 1
- **Opening Date:** Wednesday, 15th April 2026
- **Closing Date:** Tuesday, 28th April 2026
- Live OTB session bid offering runs 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. daily
- Give-up/Take-up modifications allowed from 15th–27th April 2026 (9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.)
- Last day give-up (28th April): 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.; last day take-up: 9:15 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.
- Modification and cancellation of bids: **Not Allowed**

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This notice operationalises the existing OTB – Acquisition Window (Takeover) framework as per Exchange Notices 20150930-1 (dated 30 September 2015) and 20170203-29 (dated 3 February 2017).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must ensure UCC of investors is registered and/or updated with the correct PAN Number and Category in the Exchange UCC database.
- Trading Members may only bid for investors holding shares in demat form.
- If no securities are accepted, no End of Issue files will be generated in the Extranet.
- Members and Custodians should use the iBBS platform (https://ibbs.bseindia.com/) and RTRMS platform (https://rtrms.bseindia.com/) for bid submission.
- Leased Line access available via https://ibbsll.bseindia.com/ and https://rtrmsll.bseindia.com/.
- Extranet file access for bid acceptance confirmation available at https://member.bseindia.com/ (Internet) or https://memberll.bseindia.com/ (Leased Line).

## Impact Assessment

This circular directly impacts shareholders and trading/custodian members dealing in ASPIRA PATHLAB & DIAGNOSTICS LTD (Scrip Code: 540788). The takeover offer at Rs. 55.00 per share for up to 26,76,180 shares represents a significant liquidity event for existing shareholders. Trading members must ensure operational readiness on the iBBS and RTRMS platforms for the two-week offer window. Eligible investor categories include FII, FI, MF, IC, OTH, NOH, CO, and IND. Members should take note of the strict no-modification/cancellation policy and the differing session end times on the final day.
