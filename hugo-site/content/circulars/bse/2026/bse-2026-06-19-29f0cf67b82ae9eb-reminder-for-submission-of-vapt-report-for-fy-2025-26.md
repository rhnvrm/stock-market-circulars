## Summary

BSE has issued a reminder to all trading members for submission of their Vulnerability Assessment and Penetration Testing (VAPT) audit reports for FY 2025-26. Reports must be submitted electronically via the BEFS portal within the prescribed deadlines to avoid penal or disciplinary action. Members who are also registered with NSE are required to submit only to NSE under the technology-based sharing mechanism notified in September 2025.

## Key Points

- Reminder references earlier Exchange Notice No. 20260514-27 dated May 14, 2026 on VAPT report submission for FY 2025-26.
- Two submission tracks exist depending on the category of the member: half-yearly (for QSBs and CII/Protected Systems) and yearly (for Self-certification REs, Small/Mid-size REs, and Qualified REs not categorised as QSBs).
- VAPT audits must be conducted through CERT-In empanelled auditors.
- Reports must be submitted after approval from the respective IT Committee.
- Members registered with both BSE and NSE need to submit their VAPT report to NSE only, per the common submission framework announced September 29, 2025.
- User manuals for the VAPT audit module are enclosed (Annexure A for Members, Annexure B for Auditors).

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice reiterates existing requirements and timelines from Exchange Notice No. 20260514-27 (May 14, 2026) and reinforces the cross-exchange sharing mechanism from Exchange Notice No. 20250929-81 (September 29, 2025).

## Compliance Requirements

- All trading members must conduct VAPT through a CERT-In empanelled auditor.
- Reports require IT Committee approval before submission.
- Submission must be made electronically through the BEFS portal.
- Members registered with NSE must submit VAPT reports to NSE only; members not registered with NSE must continue submitting directly to BSE.
- Failure to submit by due dates may result in penal or disciplinary action.

## Important Dates

| Requirement | Applicable Members | Due Date |
|---|---|---|
| Half-yearly VAPT (Oct 2025 – Mar 2026) — Conduct & submit via CERT-In auditor | QSBs and entities identified as Protected Systems / CII by NCIIPC | June 30, 2026 |
| Yearly VAPT (Apr 2025 – Mar 2026) — Conduct via CERT-In auditor | Self-certification REs, Small/Mid-size REs, Qualified REs (non-QSB) | June 30, 2026 |
| Yearly VAPT — Report submission after IT Committee approval | Self-certification REs, Small/Mid-size REs, Qualified REs (non-QSB) | July 31, 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

This is an operational compliance notice affecting all BSE trading members. The immediate impact is administrative — members must ensure their VAPT audits are complete, IT Committee-approved, and uploaded to BEFS (or NSE for dual-registered members) before the deadlines. Non-compliance risks penal or disciplinary action from BSE. No impact on trading operations, securities, or market structure.
