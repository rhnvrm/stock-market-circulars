## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Covidh Technologies Ltd (Scrip Code: 534920) has informed the Exchange of a change in its company name. Effective June 16, 2026, the company will be renamed to iSERA Lifesciences Limited, and the corresponding SCRIP ID and ABBREVIATED NAME on the BOLT Plus System will be updated accordingly.

## Key Points

- Existing company name: Covidh Technologies Ltd
- New company name: iSERA Lifesciences Limited
- Scrip Code: 534920 (unchanged)
- New SCRIP ID on BOLT Plus System: ISERA
- New Abbreviated Name on BOLT Plus System: ISERA
- Effective date: June 16, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory policy changes. This circular is an operational notification informing trading members of the updated identifiers for the renamed company on BSE's BOLT Plus trading system.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should update their systems to reflect the new company name (iSERA Lifesciences Limited) and the new SCRIP ID/Abbreviated Name (ISERA) effective June 16, 2026. No additional compliance action is required beyond awareness of the updated identifiers.

## Important Dates

- **June 10, 2026**: Notice issued by BSE
- **June 16, 2026**: Effective date of name change on BOLT Plus System

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine administrative update affecting only the display identifiers for a single company (Scrip Code 534920) on the BOLT Plus trading system. Traders and investors holding or tracking this scrip should note the updated symbol ISERA when referencing the company post June 16, 2026.
