## Summary

BSE has listed 32,75,000 new equity shares of Raaj Medisafe India Limited (Scrip Code: 524502) issued on a preferential basis to non-promoters. These shares are permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Wednesday, May 6, 2026.

## Key Points

- 32,75,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- face value issued at a premium of Rs. 45/- (issue price Rs. 55/- per share)
- Shares issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE548H01015
- Distribution numbers: 13212613 to 16487612
- Allotment date: 26 March 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice for newly allotted securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed that these securities are now listed and permitted to trade. No additional compliance action is required beyond standard trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** 26 March 2026
- **Trading Effective Date:** 6 May 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry:** 31 December 2026 (applicable to all 32,75,000 shares, Dist. Nos. 13212613–16487612)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. The listing adds 32,75,000 shares to the tradeable float for Raaj Medisafe India Limited, though the entire allotment is under lock-in until 31 December 2026, meaning no immediate increase in freely tradeable shares. Existing shareholders face minor dilution from this preferential allotment.
