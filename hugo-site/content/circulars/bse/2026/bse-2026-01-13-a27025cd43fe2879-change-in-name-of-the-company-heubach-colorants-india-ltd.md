## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for the company with CIN L24110MH1956PLC010806. The company name has been officially changed from HEUBACH COLORANTS INDIA LIMITED to SUDARSHAN COLORANTS INDIA LIMITED, effective December 12, 2025. The certificate was issued by the Central Processing Centre, ROC.

## Key Points

- Company name changed from Heubach Colorants India Limited to Sudarshan Colorants India Limited
- Effective date: December 12, 2025
- Corporate Identification Number (CIN): L24110MH1956PLC010806
- Company was originally incorporated as Colour-Chem Limited
- Certificate digitally signed by Assistant/Deputy/Registrar of Companies
- Name change does not affect rights and liabilities of stakeholders

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a routine administrative update reflecting a company's name change approved by the Ministry of Corporate Affairs under the Companies Act, 2013 and Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014.

## Compliance Requirements

- Company must display the old name (Heubach Colorants India Limited) for a period of two years along with its new name at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- All stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company and its directors on the Ministry website www.mca.gov.in/MCA21
- Company must update all legal documents, letterheads, signage, and communications to reflect the new name

## Important Dates

- **December 12, 2025**: Effective date of name change
- **December 12, 2027**: End of mandatory two-year period for displaying old name alongside new name
- **January 13, 2026**: Date of BSE circular notification

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. Name changes are routine corporate actions that do not affect the company's operations, financial position, or shareholder rights. Trading symbol may be updated by the exchange.

**Operational Impact**: Low. The company will need to update all corporate documents, stationery, signage, and digital assets. The two-year transition period allows for gradual implementation.

**Stakeholder Impact**: No impact on rights and liabilities of shareholders, creditors, or other stakeholders. All existing contracts, agreements, and legal obligations continue under the new name.
