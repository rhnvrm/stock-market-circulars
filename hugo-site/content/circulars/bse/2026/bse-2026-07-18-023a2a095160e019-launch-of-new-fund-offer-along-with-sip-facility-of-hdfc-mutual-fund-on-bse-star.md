## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that HDFC Mutual Fund will launch a New Fund Offer (NFO) for HDFC NIFTY METAL ETF FOF, along with SIP facility, on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The NFO will be open from July 20, 2026 to August 3, 2026, with a tentative allotment date of August 6, 2026, for both Regular and Direct Growth plans.

## Key Points

- Scheme: HDFC NIFTY METAL ETF FOF, offered in Regular Plan - Growth (ISIN INF179KC1KR8) and Direct Plan - Growth (ISIN INF179KC1KS6), both under the Equity scheme category.
- NFO along with SIP facility available on BSE StAR MF Platform from Jul 20, 2026 to Aug 03, 2026.
- Tentative allotment date for both schemes is Aug 06, 2026.
- Transactions where clients do not initiate funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day will be cancelled, and a Cancel/Confirm ('CC') record with 'N' flag will be reported to the RTA/AMC.
- Transactions with initiated payment remain pending until 3:00 PM on the last day of funds pay-out to the AMC, or T+5 day, whichever is earlier.
- Cheque and OTM payments are accepted during the NFO period, subject to clear funds in the ICCL account by 6:00 PM on Aug 06, 2026.
- Switch transactions are accepted during the NFO period, but switch-out funds received on or after the allotment date will not be considered for allotment.
- The NFO remains open until midnight on Aug 03, 2026, exclusively for Lumpsum Purchase transactions.
- SIP/XSIP registrations must be authenticated by clients on or before 3:00 PM on Aug 03, 2026 (NFO closure date).

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an operational notice enabling a new scheme launch on the existing BSE StAR MF Platform infrastructure, not a change to regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure clients initiate fund payments by 9:00 AM on T+1 day to avoid transaction cancellation.
- Members must ensure clear funds are available in the ICCL account by 6:00 PM on Aug 06, 2026, for allotment eligibility, including for cheque/OTM payments.
- Members handling switch transactions must ensure funds under the NFO scheme are received prior to the allotment date.
- SIP/XSIP registrations must be authenticated by clients by 3:00 PM on Aug 03, 2026.

## Important Dates

- NFO and SIP facility open: Jul 20, 2026
- NFO closure for Lumpsum Purchase: midnight, Aug 03, 2026
- SIP/XSIP authentication deadline: 3:00 PM, Aug 03, 2026
- Tentative allotment date: Aug 06, 2026
- Clear funds deadline in ICCL account: 6:00 PM, Aug 06, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, low-impact operational notice for BSE StAR MF Platform members and mutual fund distributors facilitating the launch of a new ETF-based fund of fund scheme. It does not affect listed equity securities, trading halts, or broader market operations, but requires operational compliance from members handling MF transactions for this specific scheme.
