## Summary

BSE, in continuation of its earlier Notice No. 20260703-23 dated July 03, 2026, has informed trading members that the equity shares of Adon Agro Commodities Limited (Scrip Code: 544809) will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment, effective Monday, July 20, 2026. Consequently, dealings in the company's shares will shift to the M Group.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code 544809 (Adon Agro Commodities Limited) is moving out of Trade for Trade (MT Group).
- The stock will be reclassified under the Rolling segment, specifically the M Group.
- This change follows and finalizes an earlier notice (20260703-23) issued on July 03, 2026.
- The company is listed in the SME segment.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulation is introduced; this is an administrative reclassification of the trading group/segment for a specific SME-listed security under BSE's listing department (DCS-Listing).

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must update their systems to reflect the change in settlement group for this scrip from July 20, 2026 onward. Queries can be directed to Mr. Anurag Jain (Tel: 022-2272 8822).

## Important Dates

- July 03, 2026: Original notice (20260703-23) issued.
- July 06, 2026: Confirmation notice issued.
- July 20, 2026 (Monday): Effective date of transfer from Trade for Trade (MT Group) to Rolling segment (M Group).

## Impact Assessment

The shift from Trade for Trade to the Rolling segment typically indicates improved trading liquidity/normalization for the stock, allowing net settlement rather than trade-for-trade (gross) settlement. Impact is confined to shareholders, traders, and brokers dealing in this specific SME stock, with no broader market implications.
