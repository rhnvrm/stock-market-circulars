## Summary

BSE has prohibited trading in the securities of Polo Hotels Limited (Scrip Code: 526687) effective from Tuesday, January 27, 2026. This interim measure follows the company's failure to respond to show cause notices and its non-existence at the registered office address during exchange site visits.

## Key Points

- Polo Hotels Limited (526687) failed to provide any response to BSE show cause notices
- Company found to be non-existent at its registered office address during BSE site visits
- Trading in all securities of the company will be prohibited/suspended
- Suspension is an interim measure pending further action
- Trading members and investors advised to take note

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is an enforcement action under existing BSE listing compliance regulations for companies that fail to maintain proper communication and registered office requirements.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in Polo Hotels Limited securities from January 27, 2026
- Investors holding positions in this security should be aware that trading will not be possible during the suspension period
- The company must respond to show cause notices and establish verifiable presence at registered office to potentially lift the suspension

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 23, 2026
- **Effective Date**: January 27, 2026 (Tuesday) - Trading prohibition begins

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: High - Complete halt in trading activity eliminates liquidity for existing shareholders. Investors cannot exit positions through the exchange.

**Investor Impact**: Severe - Shareholders unable to trade their holdings; potential signal of serious corporate governance issues or company insolvency.

**Compliance Impact**: Indicates serious lapses in basic listing obligations including maintenance of registered office and communication with the exchange. May lead to delisting proceedings if issues remain unresolved.
