## Summary

BSE has announced the live activities schedule for the Offer to Buy (OTB) – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for **Indo Borax and Chemicals Ltd** (Scrip Code: 524342, Symbol: INDOBORAX, ISIN: INE803D01021). The offer window is open from **Friday, 10th April 2026 to Friday, 24th April 2026**, with an offer price of **Rs. 256.30 per share** for up to **83,43,400 equity shares**.

## Key Points

- Security: Indo Borax and Chemicals Ltd | Symbol: INDOBORAX | Scrip Code: 524342 | ISIN: INE803D01021
- Offer size: Up to 83,43,400 equity shares (face value Rs. 1.00)
- Offer price: Rs. 256.30 per share
- Opening date: Friday, 10th April 2026
- Closing date: Friday, 24th April 2026
- Live OTB session timings: 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. daily
- Give-up (last day of offer): 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. on 23rd April 2026
- Take-up (last day of offer): 9:15 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. on 24th April 2026
- Bidding categories include: FII, FI, MF, IC, OTH (Other QIBs), NOH (Other NIB), CO (Company), IND (Individual)
- OTB platform accessible via iBBS: https://ibbs.bseindia.com/ (internet) or https://ibbsll.bseindia.com/ (leased line)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice operates under the framework established by Exchange Notices 20150930-1 (September 30, 2015) and 20170203-29 (February 3, 2017) for OTB operational instructions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must ensure investor UCC is registered and/or updated with correct PAN Number and Category in the Exchange UCC database.
- Trading Members may only bid for investors holding shares in demat form.
- Members and Custodians must note that if no securities are accepted, no End of Issue files will be generated on the Extranet.
- Extranet files for bid acceptance will be available only after the Exchange receives confirmation from the Merchant Banker/RTA post issue closure.
- For leased line URL configuration, members must refer to Exchange Circular No. 20170503-12.

## Impact Assessment

This circular has **high direct market impact** on Indo Borax and Chemicals Ltd (INDOBORAX). The takeover acquisition window allows shareholders across all investor categories (retail, institutional, FII, MF, etc.) to tender up to 83,43,400 shares at a fixed price of Rs. 256.30. Trading members and custodians must operationally prepare their OTB platform access (iBBS/RTRMS), verify client UCC data, and manage bids within the defined session windows.
