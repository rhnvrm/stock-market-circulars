## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that new equity shares of RBL Bank Limited (Scrip Code: 540065) are listed and permitted to trade with effect from Tuesday, July 14, 2026. The shares were issued to promoters on a preferential basis and rank pari-passu with the existing equity shares of the company.

## Key Points

- 92,91,34,820 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 270/- per share to promoters on a preferential basis.
- Issue price: Rs. 280/- per share.
- ISIN: INE976G01028.
- Distinctive numbers: 619423214 to 1548558033.
- Date of allotment: 18/06/2026.
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of RBL Bank Limited.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is a routine listing notification for shares allotted via preferential issue.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to note the listing and permit trading of the new securities from the effective date. No further action is required from investors.

## Important Dates

- Date of allotment: 18/06/2026
- Listing and trading effective from: Tuesday, July 14, 2026
- Lock-in expiry for 61,94,23,214 shares (Dist. Nos. 619423214 to 1238846427): 18/06/2031
- Lock-in expiry for 30,97,11,606 shares (Dist. Nos. 1238846428 to 1548558033): 16/01/2027

## Impact Assessment

The preferential allotment significantly increases RBL Bank's outstanding equity share capital and strengthens promoter shareholding. A majority of the new shares (61.94 crore) are locked in for five years until June 2031, while a smaller tranche (30.97 crore) is locked in until January 2027, limiting near-term supply overhang despite the large issuance.
