## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that String Metaverse Ltd (Scrip Code: 534535) has fixed **April 24, 2026** as the record date for sub-division of its equity shares. The existing equity shares of Rs.10/- each will be sub-divided into ten equity shares of Re.1/- each. The sub-divided paid-up value will also be effective from April 24, 2026 (Reference: DR-617/2026-2027).

## Key Points

- String Metaverse Ltd (Scrip Code: 534535) is undertaking a 10:1 stock split
- Record date fixed: **April 24, 2026**
- Existing share face value: Rs.10/- per share
- New share face value: Re.1/- per share (10 shares issued for every 1 existing share)
- Existing ISIN **INE958L01026** (Rs.10/- paid up) will become invalid for exchange transactions on or after April 24, 2026
- A new ISIN for Re.1/- paid-up shares will be communicated via a separate notice
- Applicable segment: Dematerialised Securities – Rolling Settlement Segment

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework introduced. This is a corporate action under existing BSE listing and settlement regulations. The change in ISIN is a procedural requirement following the sub-division.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading members** must note the record date and ensure transactions from April 24, 2026 onwards use the new ISIN (to be announced separately)
- **Depositories and participants** must update ISIN mapping once the new ISIN is notified
- **Shareholders** holding in demat form will automatically receive sub-divided shares; no action required from them prior to the record date

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Notice Date | April 07, 2026 |
| Record Date | April 24, 2026 |
| Sub-divided shares effective | April 24, 2026 |
| Old ISIN (INE958L01026) invalidated | April 24, 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

- **Market Liquidity**: A 10:1 split typically improves share liquidity by reducing the per-share price, making it more accessible to retail investors.
- **Price Adjustment**: The share price will be adjusted proportionally on the ex-date; traders should update price targets and stop-loss levels accordingly.
- **ISIN Transition**: There is an operational risk during the ISIN transition period. Trading members must await the new ISIN notice before executing post-record-date trades.
- **Index/Portfolio Impact**: Portfolios and systems referencing the old ISIN (INE958L01026) must be updated to the new ISIN once announced to avoid settlement failures.
