## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in non-convertible debentures of IOT UTKAL ENERGY SERVICES LIMITED (Code: 1008IOT26A, ISIN: INE310L07860, Scrip Code: 950210) effective January 13, 2026. The suspension is implemented due to the company fixing a record date for payment of interest and redemption of debentures. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Company: IOT UTKAL ENERGY SERVICES LIMITED
- Debenture Code: 1008IOT26A
- ISIN: INE310L07860
- Scrip Code: 950210
- Record Date: January 13, 2026
- No dealings permitted from: January 13, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Circular Reference: DR-797/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational measure applied when a company fixes a record date for corporate actions involving debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from January 13, 2026
- All market participants must take note of the trading suspension
- No fresh positions or transactions should be initiated in the affected debenture series

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** January 6, 2026
- **Record Date:** January 13, 2026
- **No Dealings From:** January 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** High - Complete trading suspension affects all debenture holders' ability to buy or sell the security. Investors holding these debentures will face liquidity constraints from the no-dealings date.

**Investor Impact:** Debenture holders should be aware that they cannot exit their positions from January 13, 2026. Only investors holding the debentures on the record date will be eligible for interest payment and redemption proceeds.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members must update their systems to block trading in this specific ISIN and inform clients holding these debentures about the suspension.
