## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that under the Anchor Investors (AIs) portion of the public issue of Sotefin Bharat Limited, 13,68,000 equity shares were subscribed on July 15, 2026 at a price of Rs. 187 per equity share. A copy of the company's announcement dated July 15, 2026 was enclosed with the notice for reference.

## Key Points

- 13,68,000 equity shares allocated to Anchor Investors in Sotefin Bharat Limited's public issue
- Allocation price fixed at Rs. 187 per equity share
- Subscription/allocation date: July 15, 2026
- Notice issued by BSE's Listing Business Relationship department under Notice No. 20260715-26

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine disclosure notice regarding anchor investor allocation, not a regulatory or policy change.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific action required from trading members beyond being informed of the anchor allocation ahead of the public issue.

## Important Dates

- July 15, 2026: Anchor Investor allocation date and notice issue date

## Impact Assessment

The anchor investor allocation is an early indicator of institutional demand for the Sotefin Bharat Limited IPO and typically precedes the opening of the issue to other investor categories. The allocation price of Rs. 187 per share may serve as a reference point for retail and other investors evaluating the issue. Impact is confined to prospective investors and market participants tracking this specific IPO, with no broader market-wide implications.
