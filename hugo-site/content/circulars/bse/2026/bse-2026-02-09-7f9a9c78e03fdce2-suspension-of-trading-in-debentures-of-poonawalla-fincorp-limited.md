## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in non-convertible debentures of Poonawalla Fincorp Limited effective February 12, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of specific debentures. Trading members are advised not to deal in the mentioned debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspended for debentures with ISIN: INE511C07789 (Code: 974627)
- Debenture series: PFL-REPO RATE-27-2-26-PVT
- Record date for redemption: February 12, 2026
- No dealings allowed from: February 12, 2026
- Notice reference: DR-819/2025-2026
- Issued by: Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President â€“ Listing Compliance and Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational notice for trading suspension related to corporate action.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in the specified debentures from February 12, 2026
- Trading members must note the suspension and ensure compliance with the no-dealing restriction
- Ensure client communications reflect the trading suspension for affected debenture holders

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: February 9, 2026
- **Record Date**: February 12, 2026
- **Trading Suspension From**: February 12, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and traders of the specific Poonawalla Fincorp debenture series (INE511C07789). This is a routine corporate action with no broader market implications. Debenture holders on the record date will receive redemption proceeds as per the terms of the instrument. Market participants should adjust their portfolios and trading strategies accordingly for this specific security.
