BSE has issued Notice No. 20260423-20 dated April 23, 2026, pertaining to the filing of a Draft Offer Document for an SME IPO. The notice is issued by the DCS-Listing department under the SME segment and is categorised as a company-related circular.

- Notice Number: 20260423-20
- Issued by the DCS-Listing department for the SME segment
- Subject relates to an SME IPO Draft Offer Document submission
- No attachments are included with this notice

No regulatory changes are introduced by this notice. It is a procedural communication related to the IPO listing process on BSE's SME platform.

The relevant SME company is expected to comply with BSE's SME IPO listing requirements and ensure the Draft Offer Document meets SEBI and BSE guidelines for SME public offerings.
