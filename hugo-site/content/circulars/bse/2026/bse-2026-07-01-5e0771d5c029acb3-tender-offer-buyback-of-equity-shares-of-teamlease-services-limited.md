## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that TeamLease Services Limited has fixed 03 July 2026 as the Record Date for determining shareholder entitlement and eligibility to participate in the company's equity share buyback offer.

## Key Points

- Company: TeamLease Services Limited (Scrip Code: 539658, DR No: 664/2026-2027)
- Record Date fixed: 03 July 2026
- Purpose: To determine the entitlement and names of equity shareholders eligible to participate in the buyback offer
- Applies to dematerialised securities under the Rolling Settlement segment
- Ex-entitlement basis from date and settlement number: 03/07/2026

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a procedural notice under existing buyback regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are requested to take note of the record date and ensure trades are settled appropriately to reflect ex-entitlement status from 03 July 2026 onward.

## Important Dates

- Record Date: 03 July 2026
- Ex-entitlement basis date: 03 July 2026

## Impact Assessment

Shareholders holding TeamLease Services Limited shares as of the record date will be eligible to participate in the buyback offer. Trading members must account for the ex-entitlement adjustment from the record date onward. The impact is specific to TeamLease shareholders and does not have broader market implications.
