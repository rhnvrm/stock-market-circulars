## Summary

BSE has issued a notice under the Settlement/RMS category addressing the confirmation of Fixed Deposit Receipts (FDRs) that are held in electronic (dematerialized) form. This is a routine operational notice issued by BSE Clearing (BSECL) relevant to members and participants who submit FDRs as part of their collateral or margin requirements.

## Key Points

- Notice No. 20260707-4 dated 07 Jul 2026 pertains to confirmation procedures for FDRs held in electronic form.
- Falls under the Settlement/RMS category within the General segment.
- Issued by BSE Clearing Corporation Limited (BSECL).
- No attachment was provided with this notice, indicating it may reference an existing process or circular rather than introducing new requirements.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are specified in the available notice text. The notice appears to relate to operational confirmation of electronic FDRs already accepted as collateral.

## Compliance Requirements

Clearing members and trading members using FDRs as collateral should ensure their electronic FDR records are confirmed and up to date per BSECL's Settlement/RMS processes. Members should refer to BSE's official notice board for any linked documentation or procedural steps, since no attachment was available in this notice.

## Important Dates

Notice issued and effective 07 Jul 2026.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine risk management and settlement operations notice with no direct impact on listed securities or trading activity. It primarily concerns back-office collateral verification processes for clearing/trading members and carries low market significance.
