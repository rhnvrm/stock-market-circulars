## Summary

The Indian Clearing Corporation Limited (ICCL) has issued the client margin reporting schedule for March 2026 for the Equity Derivatives segment. Members are required to submit client margin reports by the specified last dates for each trade date, failing which penalty dates apply.

## Key Points

- Schedule covers trade dates from 02-Mar-2026 through 30-Mar-2026.
- Reporting deadlines extend into early April 2026, with the last penalty date falling on 09-Apr-2026.
- Each trade date has a corresponding last date for margin reporting (typically T+6 to T+8 working days) and a penalty date one day after.
- Issued by Manoj Panchal and Omkar Hatkar, Deputy Managers at ICCL, on behalf of ICCL.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is a routine monthly schedule notice under existing client margin reporting norms for the Equity Derivatives segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must submit client margin reports for each trade date by the corresponding last date of margin reporting listed in the schedule.
- Failure to report by the last date will attract penalties effective from the penalty date.
- Members should ensure their back-office and reporting systems are aligned with the published schedule for the entire month of March 2026.

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a moderate operational impact on broker-members and clearing members participating in the Equity Derivatives segment. Timely compliance with the reporting schedule is mandatory to avoid penalties. No impact on stock prices or broader market structure. Members with automated reporting workflows should verify their systems account for the March 2026 schedule, particularly around month-end and any market holidays.
