## Summary

BSE sought clarifications from four listed companies regarding news articles that appeared in various newspapers, websites, and television channels, as part of its routine surveillance process to verify accuracy of media reports and inform members/investors. Replies have been received from two companies, while responses from the other two are still awaited.

## Key Points

- BSE routinely seeks clarification from companies when news articles about them appear in media, to verify accuracy for members and investors.
- Borosil Renewables Ltd (Scrip Code: 502219) has replied to a query regarding a Moneycontrol news article.
- NIBE Ltd (Scrip Code: 535136) has replied to a query regarding a The Hindu BusinessLine news article.
- Premier Explosives Ltd (Scrip Code: 526247) has not yet replied to a query regarding an Economic Times news article.
- Rajesh Exports Ltd (Scrip Code: 531500) has not yet replied to a query regarding a The Hindu BusinessLine news article.
- Replies received are attached to the notice on the BSE platform.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine surveillance/disclosure notice, not a regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

Premier Explosives Ltd and Rajesh Exports Ltd are expected to submit their clarifications to BSE regarding the referenced news articles. No specific deadline is mentioned in the notice.

## Important Dates

Notice Date: 09 July 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact, routine disclosure/surveillance notice. It does not indicate any confirmed material event but flags that media speculation exists around these four companies. Investors may monitor for the pending replies from Premier Explosives Ltd and Rajesh Exports Ltd, and review the attached replies from Borosil Renewables Ltd and NIBE Ltd for context on the underlying news articles.
