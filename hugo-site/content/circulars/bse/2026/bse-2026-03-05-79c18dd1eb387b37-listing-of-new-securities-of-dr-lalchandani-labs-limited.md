## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 43,04,000 new equity shares of Dr Lalchandani Labs Limited (Scrip Code: 541299) have been listed and are permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Friday, March 6, 2026. These shares were issued at par on a rights basis.

## Key Points

- 43,04,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at par on rights basis are now listed on BSE
- ISIN: INE871Z01013
- Distribution Numbers: 4333069 to 8637068
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Trading permitted from March 6, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for newly allotted rights issue shares.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to enable trading in the newly listed shares from the effective date. No additional compliance action is required beyond normal trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: 02 March 2026
- Trading Effective Date: 06 March 2026 (Friday)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. The listing adds 43,04,000 new equity shares of Dr Lalchandani Labs Limited to the tradeable float on BSE. As a rights issue, shares were offered to existing shareholders at par (Rs. 10/-). The addition is routine and company-specific with no broader market implications.
