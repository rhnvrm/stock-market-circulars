## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Akara Capital Advisors Private Limited has fixed a record date of 06 March 2026 for the purpose of payment of interest and part redemption of its Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs). Trading in the debentures will occur at a reduced face value effective from the same date.

## Key Points

- Company: Akara Capital Advisors Private Limited
- Debenture Series: ACAPL-13.30%-21-02-27-PVT
- ISIN: INE08XP07332 (Scrip Code: 977052)
- Record Date: 06 March 2026
- Purpose: Part Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- Reduced Face Value: Rs. 41,666.83 per Debenture
- Effective Date of Reduced Face Value: 06 March 2026
- Settlement Number: DR-834/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification under BSE's debt segment listing compliance framework. The debenture will trade at a reduced face value post the record date.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that debentures of Akara Capital Advisors Private Limited (ISIN: INE08XP07332) will trade with a reduced face value of Rs. 41,666.83 per debenture effective 06 March 2026.
- Members should update their systems to reflect the revised face value from the effective date onward.
- Settlement to be processed under Settlement No. DR-834/2025-2026.

## Important Dates

- **Record Date:** 06 March 2026
- **Effective Date for Reduced Face Value:** 06 March 2026
- **Circular Date:** 23 February 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and trading members dealing in the specific NCD series (ACAPL-13.30%-21-02-27-PVT, ISIN INE08XP07332). This is a privately placed debt instrument and its part redemption is a routine corporate action. The reduced face value of Rs. 41,666.83 per debenture will apply from 06 March 2026. No broader market impact is expected.
