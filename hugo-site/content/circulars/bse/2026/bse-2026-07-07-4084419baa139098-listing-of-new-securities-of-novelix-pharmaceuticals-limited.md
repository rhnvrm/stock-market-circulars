## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 28,10,000 new equity shares of Novelix Pharmaceuticals Limited (Scrip Code: 536565) are listed and permitted to trade effective Wednesday, July 8, 2026. These shares were issued at a premium of Rs. 10/- per share (issue price Rs. 20/-, face value Rs. 10/-) to Promoter and Non-Promoter allottees on a preferential basis, pursuant to the conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 28,10,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 10/- (issue price Rs. 20/-) allotted on a preferential basis following conversion of warrants.
- Distinctive numbers of the new shares: 21085001 to 23895000.
- New shares rank pari-passu with the existing equity shares of the company.
- ISIN: INE314I01036.
- Date of allotment: 29/05/2026.
- New securities listed and permitted to trade with effect from July 8, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing notice under BSE Listing Operations for shares arising from preferential allotment/warrant conversion.

## Compliance Requirements

No action required from trading members beyond recognizing the new shares as tradeable from the effective date; company already complied with allotment and lock-in disclosure requirements.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: 29/05/2026
- Listing/Trading effective date: Wednesday, July 8, 2026
- Lock-in expiry: 11,50,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 22745001–23445000) locked in until 31/01/2027; 16,60,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 21085001–22745000) locked in until 31/01/2028

## Impact Assessment

Low market-wide impact as this is a routine listing of preferential shares from warrant conversion. Moderate impact for existing shareholders of Novelix Pharmaceuticals due to incremental dilution, though a significant portion of the new shares remain under lock-in until 2027-2028, limiting near-term free-float supply increase.
