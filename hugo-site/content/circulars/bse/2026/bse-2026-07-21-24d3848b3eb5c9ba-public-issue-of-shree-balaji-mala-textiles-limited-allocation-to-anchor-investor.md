## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that under the Anchor Investor (AI) portion of the public issue of Shree Balaji (Mala) Textiles Limited, 7,64,000 equity shares were subscribed on July 21, 2026 at a price of Rs. 70 per equity share. This intimation is based on the company's announcement dated July 21, 2026.

## Key Points

- Anchor Investors subscribed to 7,64,000 equity shares of Shree Balaji (Mala) Textiles Limited
- Subscription price fixed at Rs. 70/- per equity share
- Notice issued under Category: Company related, Segment: Equity, Department: Listing Business Relationship
- Circular signed by Mangesh Tayde, Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine informational notice, not a regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action required from trading members beyond being informed of the anchor investor allocation ahead of the public issue.

## Important Dates

- Anchor Investor allocation date: July 21, 2026
- Notice date: July 21, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This anchor allocation signals institutional investor interest in the Shree Balaji (Mala) Textiles Limited IPO ahead of its listing, which can be a positive sentiment indicator for the issue. However, the direct market impact is limited to investors and subscribers tracking this specific IPO, with no broader implications for the wider equity market.
