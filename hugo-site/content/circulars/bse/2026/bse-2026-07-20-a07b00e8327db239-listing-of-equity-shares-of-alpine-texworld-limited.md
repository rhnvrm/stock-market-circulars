## Summary

BSE Limited has notified trading members that the equity shares of Alpine Texworld Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The notice provides the company's scrip code, trading symbol, and ISIN, with the date of listing and further securities details to follow in a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company: ALPINE TEXWORLD LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544825
- Symbol: ALPINETEX
- ISIN: INE1JCQ01037
- Equity shares will be listed and admitted to dealings on BSE in due course
- The date of listing and further security details will be announced via a separate notice

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an administrative listing notification issued under BSE's DCS-Listing department.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should update their systems and records to recognize the new scrip code, symbol, and ISIN for Alpine Texworld Limited ahead of the listing date.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 20 July 2026
- Date of listing: To be announced separately

## Impact Assessment

This notice marks the formal admission of Alpine Texworld Limited's equity shares for trading on BSE, enabling market participants to trade the stock once listing is confirmed. Impact is limited to the new listing and does not affect existing listed securities or broader market operations.
