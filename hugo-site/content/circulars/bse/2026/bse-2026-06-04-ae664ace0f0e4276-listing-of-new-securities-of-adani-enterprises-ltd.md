## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 766 new equity shares of Adani Enterprises Ltd. (Scrip Code: 512599) are permitted for trading effective Friday, June 05, 2026. These shares arise from the conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid shares of face value Rs. 1/- each, issued on a rights basis.

## Key Points

- 766 equity shares of Rs. 1/- each are now eligible for trading on BSE from June 05, 2026.
- Shares arise from conversion of partly paid rights shares to fully paid.
- ISIN: INE423A01024.
- Distinctive numbers: 1212444339 to 1212445089 (751 shares) and 1347245234 to 1347245248 (15 shares).
- New shares are pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard admission-to-trading notice following conversion of partly paid shares.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are permitted to trade in these new securities from the effective date. No additional compliance action is required beyond normal trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** Friday, June 05, 2026 — trading in new securities commences.

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The addition of 766 shares to the tradable float of Adani Enterprises Ltd. is negligible relative to the company's total outstanding share capital. This is a routine operational listing arising from a rights issue conversion.
