## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in debentures of JM Financial Asset Reconstruction Company Ltd (ISIN: INE265J07506, Scrip Code: 974582) effective January 16, 2026. The suspension is implemented due to the company fixing the record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest on the same date.

## Key Points

- Trading Members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from January 16, 2026
- Debenture ISIN: INE265J07506 (JMFARCL-10.21%-2-2-26-PVT)
- Scrip Code: 974582
- Record date set for January 16, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Circular Reference: DR-800/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural suspension in accordance with corporate action requirements.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must refrain from executing any trades in the specified debentures from January 16, 2026 onwards
- Trading Members are required to take note of this suspension and communicate to relevant stakeholders
- No dealings permitted in JMFARCL debentures (INE265J07506) from the suspension date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 9, 2026
- **Record Date**: January 16, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Date**: January 16, 2026 (No dealings from this date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - affects only specific debenture holders of JM Financial Asset Reconstruction Company Ltd.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - routine corporate action suspension. Trading Members need to ensure compliance with the no-dealings directive.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to debenture holders who will receive redemption proceeds and interest payment. Investors holding these debentures should be aware that trading will not be possible from January 16, 2026.

**Segment**: Debt Market Segment only
