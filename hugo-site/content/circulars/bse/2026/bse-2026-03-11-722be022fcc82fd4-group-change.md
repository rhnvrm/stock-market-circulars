## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Artemis Electricals and Projects Limited (Scrip Code: 542670) will undergo a group change from Group X to Group B, effective March 12, 2026. This reclassification impacts how the stock is traded and surveilled on the exchange.

## Key Points

- Artemis Electricals and Projects Limited (Scrip Code: 542670) is being moved from Group X to Group B
- The group change is effective from March 12, 2026
- Notice issued on March 11, 2026 (Notice No. 20260311-22)
- Issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President – Listing Compliance & Operations

## Regulatory Changes

The reclassification from Group X to Group B represents a change in the company's trading segment classification on BSE. Group X typically denotes companies under an exclusive listing platform or with specific surveillance restrictions, while Group B represents a broader general trading group, indicating an improvement in the company's listing status.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their systems and records to reflect the new group classification for Scrip Code 542670 effective March 12, 2026
- Any trading strategies or risk parameters tied to Group X classification should be revised accordingly

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** March 11, 2026
- **Effective Date of Group Change:** March 12, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The move from Group X to Group B may affect applicable price bands, margin requirements, and trading restrictions for Artemis Electricals and Projects Limited. Investors and trading members should review their exposure and trading parameters for this scrip ahead of the effective date. A shift out of Group X generally signals improved compliance or liquidity standing, which could positively influence market sentiment for the stock.
