## Summary

BSE has published Notice No. 20260609-42 informing market participants that the Draft Offer Document (DRHP) of **A N P Technologies Limited**, filed on June 08, 2026, is now available on the BSE SME website. The document has been made public for at least 21 days to invite comments, in accordance with Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by A N P Technologies Limited on June 08, 2026
- Document hosted on the BSE SME website for a minimum period of 21 days
- Public comments are invited within 21 days from the notice date (i.e., by June 30, 2026)
- The document complies with Schedule VI of the ICDR (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural notice under SEBI ICDR Regulations requiring public availability of the Draft Offer Document before an SME IPO proceeds.

## Compliance Requirements

- Interested parties may review the Draft Offer Document at: https://www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Any comments on the Draft Offer Document must be submitted within **21 days** from June 9, 2026
- Comments to be sent to: **equity.ipo@bseindia.com**

## Important Dates

- **Document Filed:** June 08, 2026
- **Notice Date:** June 09, 2026
- **Public Comment Deadline:** June 30, 2026 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal immediate market impact. It is a routine disclosure step in the SME IPO process for A N P Technologies Limited, an unlisted company. Existing market participants and investors are unaffected unless they wish to submit comments on the draft offer document. The IPO has not yet been approved or scheduled; this is an early-stage regulatory requirement.
