## Summary

BSE has announced adjustments to all Futures and Options contracts on Tech Mahindra Ltd (Derivative Asset Code: TECM, Equity Scrip Code: 532755) in the Equity Derivatives Segment. The adjustments are necessitated by the company's declaration of a Final Dividend of Rs. 36.00 per equity share for FY 2026-27, with July 03, 2026 fixed as the Record Date.

## Key Points

- Tech Mahindra Ltd has declared a Final Dividend of Rs. 36.00 per equity share for FY 2026-27
- Record Date: July 03, 2026
- Ex-date: July 03, 2026 (Friday)
- Adjustment effective date: July 02, 2026 (Thursday)
- All available Futures and Options contracts on TECM will be adjusted
- Adjustments are in accordance with SEBI Master Circular SEBI/HO/MRD/DP/CIR/P/2016/135 dated December 16, 2016

## Regulatory Changes

Adjustments are made pursuant to BSE Exchange Notice No. 20180710-26 (Review of Adjustment of corporate actions for stock options) and SEBI Master Circular No. SEBI/HO/MRD/DP/CIR/P/2016/135 dated December 16, 2016.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members of the Equity Derivatives Segment are informed of the upcoming adjustments
- Members are requested to contact their respective relationship managers for further details
- No specific compliance action required from members beyond awareness of adjusted contract terms

## Important Dates

- **July 02, 2026 (Thursday):** Adjustment date — strike prices and futures prices will be adjusted at end of day
- **July 03, 2026 (Friday):** Ex-date and Record Date — adjusted prices applicable for trading from this date

## Impact Assessment

**Options Contracts:** All strike prices for TECM options will be reduced by Rs. 36.00 at end of day on July 02, 2026. Adjusted strike prices will be available for trading from July 03, 2026 onwards.

**Futures Contracts:** The adjusted futures price for each contract will be calculated as the daily mark-to-market settlement price on July 02, 2026 minus Rs. 36.00. This adjusted rate will serve as the base rate/previous close for trading on July 03, 2026.

Traders holding open TECM derivatives positions should factor in this adjustment when managing their positions around the ex-date.
