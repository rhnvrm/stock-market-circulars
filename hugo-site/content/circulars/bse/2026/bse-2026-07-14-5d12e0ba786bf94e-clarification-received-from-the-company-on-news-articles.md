## Summary

BSE Ltd, as part of its surveillance function, sought clarification from four listed companies regarding news articles that appeared in various newspapers, websites, and television channels. This is a standard exercise to verify the accuracy of such news reports for the benefit of members and investors. Replies have been received from two companies, while two others have yet to respond.

## Key Points

- Shadowfax Technologies Ltd (Scrip Code: 544685) has submitted a reply regarding a news article that appeared on moneycontrol.com.
- Gensol Engineering Ltd (Scrip Code: 542851) has submitted a reply regarding a news article that appeared on bfsi.economictimes.indiatimes.com.
- Rajesh Exports Ltd (Scrip Code: 531500) has not yet replied to a news article that appeared on thehindubusinessline.com.
- IDBI Bank Ltd (Scrip Code: 500116) has not yet replied to a news article that appeared on business-standard.com.
- The actual replies from Shadowfax Technologies and Gensol Engineering are attached to the notice but not detailed in the notice text itself.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine surveillance communication under BSE's standard procedure for verifying market-moving or noteworthy news articles.

## Compliance Requirements

Rajesh Exports Ltd and IDBI Bank Ltd are expected to submit their clarifications to BSE regarding the referenced news articles; companies are generally required to respond to exchange queries of this nature in a timely manner.

## Important Dates

Notice dated 14 July 2026 (Notice No. 20260714-13). No specific deadline is mentioned for the pending replies.

## Impact Assessment

This notice has limited direct market impact as it is a procedural surveillance update rather than a disclosure of material corporate information. However, investors in Shadowfax Technologies, Gensol Engineering, Rajesh Exports, and IDBI Bank may wish to review the underlying news articles and any attached company replies for context, particularly given Gensol Engineering's history of regulatory scrutiny.
