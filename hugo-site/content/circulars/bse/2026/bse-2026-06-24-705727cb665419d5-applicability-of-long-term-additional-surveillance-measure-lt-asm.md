## Summary

BSE has issued a notice regarding the applicability of Long Term Additional Surveillance Measures (LT-ASM) on certain securities. Effective June 30, 2026, 100% margins will be imposed on all open positions as on June 29, 2026 and all new positions created from June 30, 2026 onwards. Additionally, securities are being moved to higher stages of the LT-ASM framework and some are being transferred to T/XT/MT/TS groups.

## Key Points

- 100% margins applicable from June 30, 2026 on open and new positions in securities listed under Annexure I (Part A)
- Securities in Annexure I (Part B) are moved to higher stages of LT-ASM framework effective June 25, 2026
- Lower Price Band applicable on relevant securities from June 25, 2026
- Securities in Annexure I (Part D) will be transferred to T/XT/MT/TS Group effective June 30, 2026
- Securities moving out of LT-ASM Framework from June 25, 2026 are listed in Annexure II
- Consolidated list of all securities under the framework provided in Annexure III
- Shortlisting is based on XBRL submissions by listed companies and is for surveillance purposes only
- Price bands for securities exiting the framework revert to pre-shortlisting levels, subject to no other surveillance measure being applicable

## Regulatory Changes

This notice is issued with reference to:
- Exchange notice no. 20180321-46 dated March 21, 2018 (original LT-ASM framework)
- Exchange notice no. 20181027-1 dated October 27, 2018
- Exchange notice no. 20201204-56 dated December 04, 2020
- Exchange notice no. 20240809-46 dated August 09, 2024
- Exchange notice no. 20240920-63 dated September 20, 2024 (latest revision of LT-ASM framework)
- Exchange notice no. 20210604-41 dated June 04, 2021 (transfer to T/XT/MT/TS Group provisions)

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must ensure 100% margins are collected/maintained on positions in LT-ASM securities effective June 30, 2026
- Brokers and members must update systems to reflect group transfers to T/XT/MT/TS effective June 30, 2026
- Lower price band restrictions must be enforced from June 25, 2026 for applicable securities
- For clarifications, members may write to bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **June 25, 2026**: Securities moved to higher LT-ASM stages take effect; lower price band applies; securities exit LT-ASM framework
- **June 29, 2026**: Reference date for open positions subject to 100% margin from June 30
- **June 30, 2026**: 100% margins applicable on all open and new positions; securities transferred to T/XT/MT/TS Group

## Impact Assessment

The imposition of 100% margins will significantly increase the cost of holding positions in affected securities, likely reducing liquidity and trading volumes. The transfer of securities to T/XT/MT/TS groups further restricts trading to trade-for-trade settlement, eliminating netting benefits and increasing settlement obligations for traders. The lower price band limits downside movement but also constrains normal market activity. These measures are designed to protect investors from highly volatile or potentially manipulated securities, though they can also impair price discovery and exit options for existing holders.
