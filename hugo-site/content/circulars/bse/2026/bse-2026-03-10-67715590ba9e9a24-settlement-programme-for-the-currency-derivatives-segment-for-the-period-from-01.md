## Summary

BSE has issued the settlement programme for the Currency Derivatives Segment for the period from April 1, 2026 to April 30, 2026. The notice, issued by Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd. (ICCL), provides a detailed schedule of trade dates and their corresponding daily settlement dates. A special note is included regarding the expiry and final settlement of April 2026 currency future contracts.

## Key Points

- The settlement programme covers the period from April 2, 2026 to April 30, 2026 (trading days within the month).
- Each trade date is mapped to a specific daily settlement date, typically one to two business days after the trade date.
- The expiry date (last trading day) for April 2026 Currency Future contracts is April 28, 2026.
- The final settlement date for April 2026 Currency Future contracts is April 30, 2026.
- The settlement date for trades on April 30, 2026 falls on May 4, 2026.
- Notice issued by Tajindersingh Virdi, Assistant Vice President, Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced. This is a routine operational schedule circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and participants in the Currency Derivatives Segment must adhere to the published trade date and settlement date schedule.
- Participants must ensure sufficient funds/margins are available by the respective daily settlement dates.
- Members should note the special final settlement date of April 30, 2026 for April 2026 currency future contracts expiring on April 28, 2026.
