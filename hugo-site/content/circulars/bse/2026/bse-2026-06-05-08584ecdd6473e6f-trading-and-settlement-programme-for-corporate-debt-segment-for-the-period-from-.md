## Summary

BSE, through ICCL (Indian Clearing Corporation Limited), has issued the trading and settlement programme for the Corporate Debt segment covering the period from 01 July 2026 to 31 July 2026. This notice (No. 20260605-9) provides the settlement schedule that market participants must follow for debt securities traded on BSE's Corporate Debt platform.

## Key Points

- Settlement/RMS category circular issued by ICCL for the Debt segment
- Covers the full calendar month of July 2026 (01.07.2026 to 31.07.2026)
- Attachment ICDM072026 contains the detailed day-wise settlement schedule
- Applicable to all participants trading in the Corporate Debt segment on BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine monthly operational notice providing the settlement programme calendar.

## Compliance Requirements

- All trading members and clearing members participating in the Corporate Debt segment must adhere to the settlement dates and obligations specified in the attached schedule (ICDM072026)
- Participants should plan pay-in and pay-out of securities and funds in accordance with the published settlement calendar

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** 05 June 2026
- **Programme Period:** 01 July 2026 to 31 July 2026
- Specific settlement dates and holidays are detailed in attachment ICDM072026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has medium operational impact for participants active in the Corporate Debt segment. It provides essential scheduling information for settlement of corporate bond trades for the month of July 2026. Participants must refer to the attached settlement programme (ICDM072026) to plan their obligations. No market-wide disruption or policy change is involved.
