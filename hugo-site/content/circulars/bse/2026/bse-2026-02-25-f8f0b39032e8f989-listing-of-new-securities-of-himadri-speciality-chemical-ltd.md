## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 24,000 new equity shares of Himadri Speciality Chemical Ltd (Scrip Code: 500184) have been listed and are permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Thursday, February 26, 2026. These shares were issued to Non Promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to the conversion of warrants at an issue price of Rs. 316/- per share.

## Key Points

- 24,000 equity shares of face value Re. 1/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 315/- (total issue price Rs. 316/-)
- Allotted to Non Promoters on a preferential basis via conversion of warrants
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 504494951 to 504518950
- ISIN: INE019C01026
- Trading permitted from February 26, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for newly allotted securities following completion of a preferential warrant conversion process.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities available for trading from February 26, 2026
- Lock-in restriction applies: all 24,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 504494951 to 504518950) are locked in until **August 30, 2026** and cannot be traded until that date

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** November 10, 2025
- **Trading Effective From:** February 26, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry:** August 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The market impact of this listing is minimal. The allotment of 24,000 shares represents a very small addition to the existing float of Himadri Speciality Chemical Ltd. Furthermore, all newly listed shares are subject to a lock-in period until August 30, 2026, meaning they will not be available for trading in the near term. No immediate supply pressure is expected on the stock.
