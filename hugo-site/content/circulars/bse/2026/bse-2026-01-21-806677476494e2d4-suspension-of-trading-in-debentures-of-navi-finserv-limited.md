## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of NAVI FINSERV LIMITED effective January 29, 2026. The suspension is implemented due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are strictly advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Debenture ISIN: INE342T07528 (Code: 975914)
- Debenture Series: NFL-10.40%-13-2-26-PVT (10.40% coupon rate)
- Record Date: January 29, 2026
- No Dealings From: January 29, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- Notice Reference: DR-808/2025-2026
- Issuer Authority: Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President â€“ Listing Compliance and Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action notice implementing existing trading suspension protocols for debenture redemption activities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all trading activities in the specified NAVI FINSERV LIMITED debentures from January 29, 2026
- Members must update their systems to reflect the trading suspension
- No buy or sell orders should be accepted for ISIN INE342T07528 from the specified date
- Trading members are required to inform their clients holding these debentures about the suspension

## Important Dates

- **January 21, 2026**: Notice issued
- **January 29, 2026**: Record date for interest payment and redemption
- **January 29, 2026**: Trading suspension effective date (no dealings from this date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders of this specific debenture series. The suspension is temporary and related to scheduled corporate action (redemption and interest payment).

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders as of the record date will receive their interest payment and redemption proceeds. No trading liquidity will be available from January 29, 2026 onwards.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must ensure compliance with the no-dealing directive and update their trading systems accordingly to prevent inadvertent transactions in the suspended security.
