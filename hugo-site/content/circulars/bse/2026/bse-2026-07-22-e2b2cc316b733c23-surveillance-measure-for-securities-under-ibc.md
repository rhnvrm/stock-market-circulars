## Summary

BSE has issued an update to its Additional Surveillance Measure (ASM) framework applicable to companies undergoing Insolvency Resolution Process (IRP) under the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code (IBC). This is a periodic review under the framework first introduced via notice no. 20180608-34 dated June 08, 2018, and subsequently updated via notices dated May 20, 2022, February 10, 2023, and September 05, 2025. New securities satisfying the IBC-ASM criteria have been added to the surveillance list, other securities have been removed, and a consolidated list of all securities currently under the framework has been provided, all effective July 23, 2026.

## Key Points

- Securities listed in Annexure I have newly satisfied the criteria for the Additional Surveillance Measure relating to IBC-based Insolvency Resolution Process, effective July 23, 2026.
- Securities listed in Annexure II are moving out of the IBC ASM framework, effective July 23, 2026.
- Annexure III provides the consolidated list of all securities currently under the IBC ASM framework.
- This surveillance framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the Exchange.
- Inclusion under this framework is purely a market surveillance measure and should not be construed as an adverse action against the concerned company or entity.
- For securities exiting the framework, the price band will revert to the band applicable prior to being shortlisted, unless the scrip remains subject to another surveillance framework, in which case that framework's price band will prevail.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced; this is a periodic (routine) application of the existing IBC-linked ASM criteria established under prior BSE notices (June 2018, May 2022, February 2023, and September 2025).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the revised list of securities under the IBC ASM framework and apply applicable trading restrictions/price bands accordingly.
- Members are directed to refer to the attached annexures (I, II, and III) for the specific securities added, removed, and currently under the framework.
- Queries or clarifications may be directed to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- Effective date for additions and removals from the IBC ASM framework: July 23, 2026.
- Notice issued: July 22, 2026.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is company-specific: securities newly added to the IBC ASM framework will face additional surveillance measures such as tighter price bands and trading restrictions, potentially reducing liquidity and increasing volatility for those stocks. Securities removed from the framework will have their price bands reinstated to pre-ASM levels (unless subject to another surveillance measure), which may improve trading flexibility. As the specific companies are named only in the PDF annexures and not in the notice text, the overall market impact is assessed as medium, being a recurring compliance-driven update rather than a broad policy shift.
