## Summary

BSE has notified members that certain mutual fund schemes will observe Non-Business Days (NBD) on the BSE StAR MF Platform during July 2026, as communicated by the respective Mutual Funds. On these dates, several transaction types will be suspended for the affected schemes.

## Key Points

- BSE has received communication from Mutual Funds designating specific dates in July 2026 as Non-Business Days for certain schemes.
- The following transaction types will NOT be permitted under affected schemes on NBD dates via BSE StAR MF platform: Subscription, Redemption, Switch, SIP, Normal STP, SWP, and AMC-STP.
- The specific schemes and their respective NBD dates are listed in Annexure 1 attached to the notice.
- This is a platform-level restriction applicable only to transactions routed through BSE StAR MF.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This notice implements existing NBD provisions as communicated by individual Mutual Funds for July 2026.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and distributors must ensure no Subscription, Redemption, Switch, SIP, Normal STP, SWP, or AMC-STP orders are placed for the listed schemes on their designated NBD dates via BSE StAR MF.
- Members should refer to Annexure 1 for the complete list of affected schemes and corresponding NBD dates.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 30 June 2026
- **Effective Period:** July 2026 (specific NBD dates as per Annexure 1)

## Impact Assessment

Investors and distributors transacting in the affected mutual fund schemes through BSE StAR MF will be unable to place Subscription, Redemption, Switch, SIP, Normal STP, SWP, or AMC-STP requests on the notified Non-Business Days. Impact is scheme-specific and limited to the BSE StAR MF platform channel; transactions through other channels (e.g., AMC websites, NSE MF) may remain available subject to those platforms' own NBD schedules.
