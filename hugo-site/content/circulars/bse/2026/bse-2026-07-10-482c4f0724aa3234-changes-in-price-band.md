## Summary

BSE has notified Trading Members that the applicable price bands for 29 specified scrips have been revised from their existing levels, effective July 13, 2026. The revised price band limits range from 2% to 20% depending on the security.

## Key Points

- 29 scrips across various sectors have had their price bands revised.
- New price band percentages range from as tight as 2% (Incon Engineers Ltd, RSC International Ltd) to as wide as 20% (Anubhav Plast Ltd, Dc Infotech and Communication Ltd, Shankara Building Products Ltd).
- Revised bands apply uniformly to all listed trading members dealing in these scrips.
- Queries regarding the revision can be directed to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Regulatory Changes

This is a routine surveillance-driven adjustment of daily price bands (circuit filters) under BSE's Department of Surveillance (DOSS), applicable to the Equity segment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must update their systems to reflect the new price band limits for the listed scrips effective July 13, 2026, and ensure orders comply with the revised circuit limits.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 10, 2026
- Effective Date: July 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The revision affects intraday trading volatility limits for the 29 scrips involved, including notable names such as Shankara Building Products Ltd, CL Educate Ltd, and Softtech Engineers Ltd. Tighter bands (2-5%) reduce daily price movement flexibility for several small-cap and mid-cap stocks, likely reflecting surveillance concerns over volatility, while wider bands (10-20%) are applied to select scrips. Market impact is limited to trading mechanics rather than fundamentals.
