## Summary

BSE has notified that trading in partly paid equity shares of Cyber Media (India) Ltd. (Scrip Code 890223) will be suspended from January 14, 2026. The company has fixed January 14, 2026, as the Record Date to identify shareholders eligible to receive the First and Final Call Notice requiring payment of Rs. 7.90 per partly paid share.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies only to partly paid equity shares (Scrip Code 890223)
- Record Date: January 14, 2026
- No dealings from: January 14, 2026 (Settlement No. DR-798/2025-2026)
- First and Final Call amount: Rs. 7.90 per partly paid share
- Call amount breakdown: Rs. 5.00 towards Face Value + Rs. 2.90 towards Premium
- Trading members must not deal in partly paid shares from the specified date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action procedure for partly paid shares.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in partly paid equity shares of Cyber Media (India) Ltd. from January 14, 2026
- Trading members are required to take note of this suspension and communicate to clients
- Shareholders holding partly paid shares must be prepared to pay the First and Final Call amount as per the company's notice

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 9, 2026
- **Record Date**: January 14, 2026
- **No Dealings From**: January 14, 2026
- **Settlement Number**: DR-798/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

**Trading Impact**: Complete suspension of trading in partly paid equity shares of Cyber Media (India) Ltd. Shareholders holding these shares cannot trade them from January 14, 2026, until further notice.

**Shareholder Impact**: Holders of partly paid shares must pay Rs. 7.90 per share (First and Final Call) to convert them to fully paid shares. Failure to pay may result in forfeiture of shares as per company law provisions.

**Market Operations**: Trading members must update their systems and inform clients holding partly paid shares about the suspension and payment obligation.
