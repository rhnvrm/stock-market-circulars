## Summary

BSE has informed its members that Nippon India Mutual Fund has rolled over Nippon India Fixed Maturity Plan - XLIII - Series 1. The maturity date of the scheme has been revised from June 29, 2026, to July 25, 2031, extending the scheme's tenure by approximately five years. This communication is in reference to earlier notices (No. 20210813-2 and 20250701-64) issued at the time of the fund's New Fund Offer launch on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Key Points

- Nippon India Fixed Maturity Plan - XLIII - Series 1 has been rolled over on the BSE StAR MF Platform.
- Original maturity date: June 29, 2026.
- Revised maturity date: July 25, 2031.
- The rollover extends the scheme's tenure by approximately 5 years and 1 month.
- A notice cum addendum has been issued by Nippon India AMC with further details.
- BSE members are directed to refer to the attached addendum for complete information.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this circular. The rollover is a scheme-level action by Nippon India AMC, communicated to BSE members as an operational update on the StAR MF Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- BSE members trading or facilitating transactions in Nippon India Fixed Maturity Plan - XLIII - Series 1 on the StAR MF Platform should take note of the revised maturity date.
- Members are advised to refer to the notice cum addendum issued by Nippon India AMC for any investor-level disclosures or action requirements.

## Important Dates

- **Original Maturity Date:** June 29, 2026
- **Revised Maturity Date:** July 25, 2031
- **Notice Date:** June 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to existing unit holders of Nippon India Fixed Maturity Plan - XLIII - Series 1. Investors who expected redemption on June 29, 2026, will now have their funds locked in until July 25, 2031. There is no broader market impact. Members transacting on BSE StAR MF Platform should update their records to reflect the new maturity date.
