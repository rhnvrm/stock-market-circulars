## Summary

BSE has issued a notice cancelling the previously announced suspension of trading in securities of Benara Bearings and Pistons Ltd (Scrip Code: 541178) and Shree Securities Ltd (Scrip Code: 538975). The suspension cancellation is effective March 13, 2026, in continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20260210-29 dated February 10, 2026.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension for two companies has been cancelled effective March 13, 2026
- Benara Bearings and Pistons Ltd (Scrip Code: 541178) will not be suspended
- Shree Securities Ltd (Scrip Code: 538975) will not be suspended
- This notice is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20260210-29 dated February 10, 2026
- All other provisions of the original notice remain unchanged

## Regulatory Changes

The original suspension was imposed for non-compliance with provisions of certain Regulations under SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015. The cancellation implies these companies have since addressed the relevant compliance requirements.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants are requested to take note of the restoration of trading for the two listed companies
- All other provisions of the original Exchange Notice No. 20260210-29 dated February 10, 2026 remain in effect
- For further clarification, market participants may contact bse.soplodr@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- Original suspension notice: February 10, 2026 (Exchange Notice No. 20260210-29)
- This cancellation notice date: March 11, 2026
- Effective date of suspension cancellation: March 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Investors and traders holding or intending to trade in Benara Bearings and Pistons Ltd (541178) and Shree Securities Ltd (538975) will be able to resume normal trading activities from March 13, 2026. The lifting of suspension indicates these companies have resolved their SEBI LODR compliance issues. Market liquidity for these scrips will be restored, benefiting existing shareholders who were unable to exit positions during the suspension period.
