## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 92,19,900 new equity shares of PVV Infra Ltd. (Scrip Code: 536659) are permitted for trading with effect from Wednesday, June 17, 2026. These shares arise from the conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up equity shares of Rs. 5/- each, issued on a rights basis.

## Key Points

- 92,19,900 equity shares of PVV Infra Ltd. are admitted for trading on BSE from June 17, 2026.
- Shares result from conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up of Rs. 5/- face value, issued on rights basis.
- ISIN: INE428B01021
- Distinctive Numbers: 115108333 to 124328232
- New shares are pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice admitting newly converted fully paid equity shares to trading on BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed they may trade in these new securities from June 17, 2026. No separate compliance action is required beyond standard trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 16, 2026
- **Trading Effective Date:** June 17, 2026 (Wednesday)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a routine corporate action enabling trading in new equity shares following the conversion of partly paid rights shares to fully paid up. Existing shareholders who held partly paid shares under the rights issue will now have fully paid, tradeable shares. The new shares rank pari-passu with existing equity, so there is no dilution of rights for existing fully paid shareholders beyond what was already factored in when the rights issue was announced.
