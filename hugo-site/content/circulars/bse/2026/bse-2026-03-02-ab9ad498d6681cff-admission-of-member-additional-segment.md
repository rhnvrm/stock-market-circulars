## Summary

BSE has admitted Adinath Stock Broking Pvt. Ltd. (Member Code: 3175) as a Trading Member on BSE Ltd. with trading rights in the Commodity Derivatives segment. The admission is subject to the Rules, Bye-laws, and Regulations of the Exchange.

## Key Points

- Adinath Stock Broking Pvt. Ltd. has been granted trading rights (only) on BSE Ltd.
- The member is admitted under the Commodity Derivatives segment.
- Member Code assigned: 3175.
- Admission is governed by BSE's Rules, Bye-laws, and Regulations.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine membership admission notice.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance obligations for existing trading members. Members with queries may contact BSE Membership Operations at membership.ops@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- Admission effective: 02 March 2026.

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard administrative notice informing existing trading members of a new entrant in the Commodity Derivatives segment. No changes to trading rules, margin requirements, or operational procedures are involved.
