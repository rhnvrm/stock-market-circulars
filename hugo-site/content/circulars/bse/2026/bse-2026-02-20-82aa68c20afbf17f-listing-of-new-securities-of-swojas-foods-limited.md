## Summary

BSE has listed 21,00,000 new equity shares of Swojas Foods Limited (Scrip Code: 530217) effective Monday, February 23, 2026. The shares were issued to Non-Promoters on a preferential basis at Rs. 16.50/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- plus premium of Rs. 6.50/-) pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 21,00,000 equity shares listed on BSE effective February 23, 2026
- Shares issued to Non-Promoters via preferential allotment on warrant conversion
- Issue price: Rs. 16.50/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- + premium Rs. 6.50/-)
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE295B01016
- Distribution numbers: 30962651 to 33062650
- Date of allotment: January 21, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice pursuant to a completed preferential allotment under SEBI regulations governing warrant conversion.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed that these securities are now permitted to trade on BSE from February 23, 2026
- All 21,00,000 shares are subject to lock-in until September 15, 2026 and cannot be traded until that date

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** January 21, 2026
- **Trading Commencement:** February 23, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry:** September 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing increases the total share count of Swojas Foods Limited by 21,00,000 shares, representing a dilution of the existing shareholder base. Since all newly listed shares are under lock-in until September 15, 2026, there is no immediate sell-side pressure from the allottees. Market impact is limited to existing shareholders of Swojas Foods Limited (Scrip Code: 530217).
