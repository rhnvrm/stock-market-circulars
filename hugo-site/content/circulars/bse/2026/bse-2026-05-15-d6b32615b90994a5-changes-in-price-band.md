## Summary

BSE's Department of Stock Surveillance (DOSS) has revised the price bands for 35 equity scrips effective May 18, 2026. Trading members are notified that daily price movement limits for these securities have been tightened or adjusted, ranging from 1% to 10%, as part of ongoing surveillance actions.

## Key Points

- 35 scrips across equity segment have revised price bands effective May 18, 2026
- Price bands revised to varying levels: 1% (1 scrip), 2% (4 scrips), 5% (27 scrips), and 10% (3 scrips)
- Action initiated by BSE Surveillance (DOSS) department
- No attachments to the notice; complete details provided within the circular
- Members with clarifications may contact bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all orders in the above-listed scrips comply with the revised price band limits from May 18, 2026
- Orders placed outside the revised price band will be rejected by the exchange trading system
- Members should update their systems and inform clients trading in these scrips accordingly
- For clarifications, contact BSE Surveillance at bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** May 15, 2026
- **Effective Date:** May 18, 2026 (Monday)

## Impact Assessment

This price band revision affects 35 equity scrips and restricts their daily price movement to the newly assigned band limits. Scrips assigned a 1% or 2% band (Shantai Industries, Gala Global Products, Ikoma Technologies, Natura Hue Chem, True Green Bio Energy) face the most restrictive limits, indicating heightened surveillance concern. The 10% band assigned to Aerpace Industries, B-Right Realestate, and Pajson Agro India represents a relatively relaxed surveillance measure. Investors and traders holding positions in these scrips should be aware of intraday price movement restrictions starting May 18, 2026. Tighter bands typically signal BSE's intent to curb unusual price volatility or suspected manipulative trading in these securities.
