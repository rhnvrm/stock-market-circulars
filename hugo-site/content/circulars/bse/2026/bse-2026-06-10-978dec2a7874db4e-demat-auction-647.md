## Summary

BSE Limited, through ICCL (Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd.), has announced Demat Auction 647 for Rolling Settlement No. DR-647/2026-27. Trading members are invited to submit auction offers for the purchase of securities that were short-delivered in the settlement.

## Key Points

- Auction offer entry window: 2:00 PM to 2:45 PM on Wednesday, June 10, 2026
- Auction matching session: 2:45 PM to 3:00 PM
- Delivery statement and accepted auction offer reports: 3:00 PM to 3:30 PM
- Money statement and final reports: 3:35 PM to 5:30 PM
- Securities pay-in (auction settlement): Thursday, June 11, 2026 at 8:00 AM
- Settlement is processed through the Clearing Member of each Trading Member under the Uniform Membership structure
- Restriction on trading members offering securities in auctions for settlements where they have short-delivered has been removed

## Regulatory Changes

Following the implementation of the Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment, settlement for auction trades is now routed through the Clearing Member of the Trading Member. The earlier restriction preventing trading members from offering securities in auction for settlements in which they had short-delivered has been lifted.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and Custodians must check their auction pay-in obligations for securities
- Securities pay-in must be executed in depositories within depository cut-off timings
- Trading members must exercise utmost care while entering securities codes and rates; no corrections will be permitted for punching errors
- Trading members should refer to the cutoff rate shown on the "Auction offer Entry Screen" before submitting offers
- Members should review Corporate Action notices (Ex-Rights, Ex-Bonus, Ex-Split, etc.) issued by respective departments

## Important Dates

- **June 10, 2026**: Auction offer entry (2:00 PM – 2:45 PM), Matching (2:45 PM – 3:00 PM), Reports (3:00 PM – 5:30 PM)
- **June 11, 2026**: Auction securities pay-in at 8:00 AM

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice affecting trading members with short-delivery positions in Rolling Settlement DR-647/2026-27. The removal of the restriction on offering securities in the same settlement where a short delivery occurred provides greater flexibility to trading members in meeting auction obligations. The impact is limited to participants with open auction liabilities in this specific settlement cycle.
