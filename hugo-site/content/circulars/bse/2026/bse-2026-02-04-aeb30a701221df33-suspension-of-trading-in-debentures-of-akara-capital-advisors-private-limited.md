## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in Non-Convertible Debentures of AKARA CAPITAL ADVISORS PRIVATE LIMITED (ISIN: INE08XP07258, Code: 975943) effective February 6, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing February 7, 2026 as the record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension for AKARA CAPITAL ADVISORS PRIVATE LIMITED debentures (10.01% interest rate, issued 2022)
- ISIN: INE08XP07258, BSE Code: 975943
- No dealings permitted from February 6, 2026 onwards
- Record date set for February 7, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Circular reference: DR-815/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard procedural suspension pursuant to corporate action requirements for determining eligible debenture holders for interest payment and redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from February 6, 2026
- Members must ensure compliance with the trading suspension directive
- All trading systems should reflect the suspension status for the affected security

## Important Dates

- **February 4, 2026**: Notice issued
- **February 6, 2026**: Trading suspension effective (no dealings from this date)
- **February 7, 2026**: Record date for determining eligible debenture holders for redemption and interest payment

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders of this specific private company debenture. No broader market implications.

**Investor Impact**: High for existing debenture holders who need to hold positions through record date to be eligible for interest payment and redemption proceeds. Trading liquidity eliminated from February 6, 2026.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update systems to block trades in this security and inform clients holding these debentures about the suspension and upcoming corporate action.
