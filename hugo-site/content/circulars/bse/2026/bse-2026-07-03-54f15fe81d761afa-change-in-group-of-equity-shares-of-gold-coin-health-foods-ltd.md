## Summary

BSE has notified market participants that the equity shares of Gold Coin Health Foods Ltd. (BSE Scrip code: 538542) will be moved to the 'X' group for trading, effective Friday, July 17, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: Gold Coin Health Foods Ltd.
- BSE Scrip Code: 538542
- Trading in the company's equity shares shall be permitted under the 'X' group.
- Change is effective from Friday, July 17, 2026.
- Notice issued by BSE Listing Operations Department, signed by Marian Dsouza (Assistant Vice President) and Abhishek Kadlak (Deputy Manager).

## Regulatory Changes

The scrip's trading group classification is being changed to 'X' group, a segment on BSE where trading may be subject to specific settlement conditions such as trade-for-trade settlement, which typically restricts intraday trading and requires delivery-based settlement only.

## Compliance Requirements

Market participants, brokers, and trading members dealing in Gold Coin Health Foods Ltd. shares must update their systems and trading practices to reflect the new group classification from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 3, 2026
- Effective Date of Group Change: Friday, July 17, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The reclassification to 'X' group generally signals enhanced surveillance or trade-for-trade settlement requirements, which can reduce trading liquidity and restrict speculative/intraday trading in the stock. Shareholders and traders of Gold Coin Health Foods Ltd. should be aware of potential changes in trading behavior and settlement obligations once the change takes effect.
