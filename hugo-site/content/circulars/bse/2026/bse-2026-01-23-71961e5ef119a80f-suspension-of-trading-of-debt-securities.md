## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in debt securities of 12 companies effective January 27, 2025. The suspension is due to redemption or delisting applications submitted by these companies. Trading members are instructed not to deal in these specified debt securities.

## Key Points

- 12 debt securities across various issuers will be suspended from trading
- Reason for suspension: Redemption or delisting applications by companies
- Notice Number: 20260123-7 (DR-806/2025-2026)
- Trading members must cease all dealings in these securities

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the listed debt securities effective from the suspension date
- All market participants must take note of this circular
- No trading or transactions permitted in these securities post-suspension

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 23, 2026
- **Effective Date**: January 27, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The suspension affects debt market participants holding or trading these specific securities. Investors holding these instruments should note that secondary market liquidity will cease from the effective date. The action is procedural, relating to natural lifecycle events (redemption) or issuer-initiated delisting. Limited broader market impact as these are specific debt instruments undergoing corporate actions.
