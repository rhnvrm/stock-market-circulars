## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that Upmove Financial Technologies Services (Member No. 6694) has converted its partnership firm into a corporate entity. The new corporate name is 1FC Securities Pvt. Ltd., retaining the same member number (6694). This conversion has been approved by SEBI.

## Key Points

- Upmove Financial Technologies Services (Member No. 6694) has converted from a partnership firm to a corporate entity.
- The new name is **1FC Securities Pvt. Ltd.** (Member No. 6694).
- SEBI has approved the conversion.
- The member number (6694) remains unchanged.

## Regulatory Changes

The trading member's registered name on BSE stands changed from Upmove Financial Technologies Services to 1FC Securities Pvt. Ltd. The change reflects the legal restructuring of the entity from a partnership firm to a private limited company.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members and market participants are advised to update their records to reflect the new name of Member No. 6694. No additional compliance action is required from other trading members.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** Tuesday, 16th June 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative name change with minimal market impact. It affects only the identity of one trading member (No. 6694) and does not alter trading rights, obligations, or operations. Counterparties dealing with this member should update internal records accordingly.
