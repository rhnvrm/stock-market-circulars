## Summary

BSE has issued a Call Money Notice for the partly paid-up equity shares of Yarn Syndicate Limited, activating the Call Money Module on the IBBS (Internet Based Book Building Software) platform. This is a First and Final Call, inviting shareholders and intermediaries to submit call money payments between June 8 and June 22, 2026, at Rs. 13.50 per partly paid-up equity share.

## Key Points

- Yarn Syndicate Limited has requested BSE to activate the Call Money Module of the IBBS platform for its First and Final Call on partly paid-up equity shares.
- The activation is in accordance with Exchange Notice No. 20210104-37 dated January 4, 2021, and SEBI circular dated December 8, 2020.
- Security symbol for the call money issue is **YARNCM** with a market lot of 1.
- Total partly paid-up equity shares involved: **1,80,00,000** (1.8 crore shares).
- Call money amount payable per partly paid-up equity share: **Rs. 13.50**.
- Self-Certified Syndicate Banks (SCSBs) and Trading Members with 3-in-1 type accounts are eligible to submit call money details via the IBBS platform.
- Registrar to the Issue: CAMEO CORPORATE SERVICES LIMITED, Chennai.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. The notice operates under the existing framework established by SEBI's circular dated December 8, 2020, and BSE Exchange Notice No. 20210104-37 dated January 4, 2021, governing call money notices for partly paid-up equity shares via the IBBS platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members, SCSBs, and Intermediaries** must note the activation of the Call Money Module and facilitate submission of call money details during the issue period.
- Only SCSBs and Trading Members with 3-in-1 type accounts are authorized to submit call money notice details on the IBBS platform.
- For regulatory/business rule queries: contact Listing Sales at listing.sales@bseindia.com or 022-2272 8846/5063/8179.
- For IBBS bidding-related queries: contact ibbs.ops@bseindia.com or 022-2272 8376/8436/8226.
- For new user ID creation or password reset: contact user.trdops@bseindia.com or 022-2272 8005/8215.

## Impact Assessment

This notice has a **medium** impact, primarily affecting holders of partly paid-up equity shares of Yarn Syndicate Limited (symbol: YARNCM) and the intermediaries (Trading Members and SCSBs) who facilitate the call money collection. The First and Final Call at Rs. 13.50 per share across 1.8 crore partly paid-up shares implies a total call money collection of approximately Rs. 24.3 crore. Shareholders who do not pay the call money by the closing date risk forfeiture of their partly paid-up shares per applicable regulations. There is no broader market-wide impact.
