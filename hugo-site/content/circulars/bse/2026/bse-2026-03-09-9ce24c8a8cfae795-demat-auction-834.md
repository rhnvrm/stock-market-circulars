## Summary

BSE has published the Demat Auction list for Settlement No. 834 dated 09/03/2026. The auction covers 76 securities (scrips) across a wide range of companies listed on BSE. Quantities range from as low as 1 share (multiple scrips) to 27,111 shares (Franklin Ind, scrip code 540190).

## Key Points

- Settlement No.: 834
- Date: 09/03/2026
- Total scrips listed for auction: 76
- Largest quantity: Franklin Ind (540190) — 27,111 shares
- Other significant quantities: Sofcom Systems (538923) — 13,798 shares; Leading Leas (540360) — 18,402 shares; Sellwin Trad (538875) — 9,800 shares; DSM Fresh Fo (544568) — 8,400 shares; LT Foods Ltd (532783) — 6,900 shares; Deccan Gold (512068) — 5,000 shares
- Many scrips have very small quantities (1–10 shares), indicating minor short-delivery positions

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for a scheduled demat auction.

## Compliance Requirements

- Clearing members and brokers with short delivery positions in the listed scrips must participate in or be aware of the auction process.
- Members must ensure settlement obligations are met in accordance with BSE auction procedures.

## Important Dates

- Auction Settlement Date: 09/03/2026 (Settlement No. 834)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine demat auction notice. Impact is limited to brokers and clearing members with open short-delivery positions in the 76 listed scrips. The auction ensures settlement finality for outstanding delivery failures. Investors holding positions in the listed scrips should be aware of potential price impacts during auction, particularly for illiquid scrips or those with larger auction quantities such as Franklin Ind, Sofcom Systems, and Leading Leas.
