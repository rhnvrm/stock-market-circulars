## Summary

BSE has issued a revised schedule for the Offer to Buy (Takeover) for OSCAR GLOBAL LTD through the Acquisition Window mechanism. The offer seeks to acquire up to 8,58,000 equity shares at Rs. 12.15 per share. The offer period runs from Friday, January 2, 2026 to Friday, January 16, 2026. Trading members and custodian members can facilitate investor participation through the iBBS platform and RTRMS systems.

## Key Points

- **Security Details**: OSCAR GLOBAL LTD (Symbol: OSCARGLO, Scrip Code: 530713, ISIN: INE473F01010)
- **Offer Size**: Up to 8,58,000 equity shares
- **Offer Price**: Rs. 12.15 per share
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10.00
- **Market Lot**: 1 share
- **Offer Period**: January 2-16, 2026
- **Bid Offering Period**: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM daily (January 2-16, 2026)
- **Give-up/Take-up Period**: January 2-14, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Last Day Give-up**: January 16, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Last Day Take-up**: January 16, 2026 (9:15 AM to 4:00 PM)

## Regulatory Changes

This is a revised schedule notice for an existing takeover offer. No new regulatory changes are introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

### For Trading Members and Custodians:

1. **UCC Registration**: Ensure investor UCC is registered/updated with correct PAN Number and Category in Exchange UCC database
2. **Demat Requirement**: Can only bid for investors having shares in demat form
3. **Platform Access**: Use iBBS platform (Internet: https://ibbs.bseindia.com/ or Leased Line: https://ibbsll.bseindia.com/)
4. **RTRMS Access**: Available at https://rtrms.bseindia.com/ (Internet) or https://rtrmsll.bseindia.com/ (Leased Line)
5. **File Download**: Access Extranet (https://member.bseindia.com/ or https://memberll.bseindia.com/) for acceptance files after issue closure
6. **Reference Circulars**: Follow operational instructions per Exchange Notices 20150930-1 (Sept 30, 2015) and 20170203-29 (Feb 3, 2017)
