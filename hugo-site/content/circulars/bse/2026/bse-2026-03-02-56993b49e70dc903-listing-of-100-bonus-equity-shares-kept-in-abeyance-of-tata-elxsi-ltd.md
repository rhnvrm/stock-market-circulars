## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 100 bonus equity shares of Tata Elxsi Ltd (Scrip Code: 500408, ISIN: INE670A01012), previously kept in abeyance, are now listed and permitted to trade on the exchange with effect from Wednesday, March 04, 2026.

## Key Points

- 100 bonus equity shares of Tata Elxsi Ltd (face value Rs. 10/- each) kept in abeyance are now listed on BSE
- Scrip Code: 500408; ISIN: INE670A01012
- Distinctive numbers assigned: 31165821 to 31165920
- Trading permitted from Wednesday, March 04, 2026
- Notice issued under Notice No. 20260302-31 dated 02 Mar 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice for the admission of previously withheld bonus shares to trading.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities and may facilitate trading in these shares from the effective date. No specific compliance action is required beyond awareness of the new distinctive number range.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** March 02, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** Wednesday, March 04, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The quantity involved (100 shares) is negligible relative to Tata Elxsi Ltd's total outstanding equity. This is a standard housekeeping action to release bonus shares that were previously held in abeyance, likely due to pending legal or procedural matters. No material price or liquidity effect is expected.
