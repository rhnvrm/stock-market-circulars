## Summary

BSE Limited has invited offers from trading members for the purchase of securities in Auction No. 676, pertaining to Rolling Settlement No. DR-676/2026-27. This is a routine auction conducted by the Exchange (through Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd.) to settle short-delivered securities from the relevant settlement cycle.

## Key Points

- Auction offers are invited from trading members for purchase of securities short-delivered in Rolling Settlement DR-676/2026-27.
- Offers will be accepted on trading terminals on Wednesday, July 22, 2026.
- Auction offer entry window: 02:00 p.m. to 02:45 p.m.
- Auction matching window: 02:45 p.m. to 03:00 p.m.
- Delivery statement and report of accepted auction offers: 03:00 p.m. to 03:30 p.m.
- Money statement and final reports: 03:35 p.m. to 05:30 p.m.
- Pay-in of auction securities is scheduled for Thursday, July 23, 2026 at 08:00 a.m.
- Members and custodians must check auction pay-in obligations and execute securities pay-in within depository cut-off timings.
- Following the Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment, settlement will be routed through the Clearing Member of a Trading Member.
- The prior restriction preventing trading members from offering securities in an auction for a settlement in which they themselves short-delivered those securities has been removed.
- Trading members must exercise care while entering security codes and rates, as punching errors cannot be corrected once submitted.
- The Exchange reserves the right to accept or reject any offer without assigning a reason.
- Members are advised to refer to the cutoff rate on the Auction Offer Entry Screen and to check for any corporate action notices (ex-rights, ex-bonus, ex-split, etc.) affecting the relevant securities.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory change is introduced by this notice; it references the existing removal of the restriction on trading members offering securities in an auction for settlements in which they short-delivered those securities, implemented following the Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and custodians must verify their auction pay-in obligations for securities.
- Securities pay-in must be executed in depositories within the specified depository cut-off timings.
- Members must accurately enter security codes and rates during the auction offer entry window, as no corrections are permitted post-entry.
- Members should cross-check applicable corporate action notices before placing auction offers.

## Important Dates

- Auction offer entry: July 22, 2026, 02:00 p.m. - 02:45 p.m.
- Auction matching: July 22, 2026, 02:45 p.m. - 03:00 p.m.
- Delivery statement/report of accepted offers: July 22, 2026, 03:00 p.m. - 03:30 p.m.
- Money statement and final reports: July 22, 2026, 03:35 p.m. - 05:30 p.m.
- Auction pay-in: July 23, 2026, 08:00 a.m.

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard, recurring operational notice related to the settlement auction mechanism for short-delivered securities and does not indicate any company-specific or market-wide event. Impact is limited to trading and clearing members involved in the DR-676/2026-27 settlement cycle, who must adhere to the specified timelines for auction participation and pay-in.
