## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted for trading the further new securities issued by six companies under their respective Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP/ESOS). These securities are effective for trading from Friday, February 27, 2026. No lock-in period applies to any of the listed securities.

## Key Points

- Six companies have new ESOP/ESOS shares listed on BSE effective February 27, 2026
- Total new shares across all companies: approximately 1,408,233 shares
- No lock-in restrictions apply to any of the newly listed securities
- Notice issued under BSE Notice No. 20260226-15
- Issued by Marian Dsouza (Assistant Vice President) and Bhakti Wankhede (Deputy Manager) on behalf of BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification under existing ESOP/ESOS framework for employee stock option exercises.

## Compliance Requirements

- All market participants are required to take note of the newly listed securities
- Trading systems should reflect the updated share counts and distribution number ranges for each scrip from February 27, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** February 26, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** Friday, February 27, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry:** Not applicable (NA) for all securities
