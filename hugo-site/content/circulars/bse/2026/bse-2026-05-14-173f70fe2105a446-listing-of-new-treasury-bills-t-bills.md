## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that three new Treasury Bills (T-Bills) will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in the G GROUP - DEBT INSTRUMENTS segment with effect from May 15, 2026. The T-Bills carry maturities of 91 days, 182 days, and 364 days.

## Key Points

- Three new T-Bills listed effective May 15, 2026 under G GROUP - DEBT INSTRUMENTS
- Scrip codes: 805214, 805215, and 805216
- Market lot for all three securities is 1
- Trading will be suspended two working days prior to each security's Maturity/Redemption Date (excluding Bank Holidays)
- Contact for clarifications: 2272 8352 / 5753 / 8597

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification under the existing debt instruments framework.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must note that the listed T-Bills will not be available for trading two (2) working days prior to the Maturity/Redemption Date (i.e., Maturity Date minus two trading days, excluding Bank Holidays).

## Important Dates

- **Listing effective date:** May 15, 2026
- **805214 (91TB13826) maturity:** August 13, 2026 — trading suspended from ~August 11, 2026
- **805215 (182T121126) maturity:** November 12, 2026 — trading suspended from ~November 10, 2026
- **805216 (364TB13527) maturity:** May 13, 2027 — trading suspended from ~May 11, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Routine addition of government-issued short-term debt instruments to the BSE debt segment. These T-Bills provide trading members and investors with access to risk-free sovereign instruments across 91-day, 182-day, and 364-day tenors. Market impact is minimal as T-Bill listings are a regular occurrence; the primary operational note is the two-day pre-maturity trading suspension.
