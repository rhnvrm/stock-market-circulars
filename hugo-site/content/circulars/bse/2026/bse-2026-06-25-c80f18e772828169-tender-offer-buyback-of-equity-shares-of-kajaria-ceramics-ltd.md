## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Kajaria Ceramics Ltd (BSE Code: 500233/100233) has fixed 29 June 2026 as the record date for its equity share buyback offer. The record date will be used to determine the entitlement and names of equity shareholders eligible to participate in the buyback.

## Key Points

- Kajaria Ceramics Ltd has announced a tender offer (buyback) of its equity shares
- Record date fixed: **29 June 2026**
- Ex-entitlement date: **29 June 2026** (Rolling Settlement Segment)
- Settlement number: DR-660/2026-2027
- BSE codes: 500233 / 100233
- Segment: Dematerialised Securities – Rolling Settlement Segment

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notification under existing buyback regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are required to take note of the record date and communicate it to relevant clients holding Kajaria Ceramics Ltd shares
- Shareholders must hold shares in demat form as of the record date (29 June 2026) to be eligible for the buyback offer

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Record Date | 29 June 2026 |
| Ex-Entitlement Date | 29 June 2026 |
| Settlement No. | DR-660/2026-2027 |

## Impact Assessment

This notice is relevant to existing shareholders of Kajaria Ceramics Ltd who wish to participate in the buyback offer. Shareholders on record as of 29 June 2026 will be eligible to tender their shares. The buyback provides an exit opportunity for shareholders at a price typically at a premium to market, and may marginally support the stock price in the short term. Trading members should ensure clients are informed of the record date to allow them to position accordingly.
