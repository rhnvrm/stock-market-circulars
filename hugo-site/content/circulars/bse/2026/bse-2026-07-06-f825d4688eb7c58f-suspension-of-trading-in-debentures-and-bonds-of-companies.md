## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that several companies have fixed record dates for payment of interest and/or redemption on their Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) and Bonds. Trading members are advised not to deal in the debentures/bonds of these companies on the specified ex-dates, ahead of the record dates.

## Key Points

- The notice lists 14 specific ISINs across 12 companies where trading in debentures/bonds is to be suspended.
- Companies affected: Avanti Finance Private Limited, Achiievers Finance India Limited, Sammaan Capital Limited, Axis Finance Limited (two ISINs), Muthoot Microfin Limited, West Bengal State Electricity Distribution Company Ltd, Kosamattam Finance Limited (two ISINs), KLM Axiva Finvest Limited (two ISINs), IndoStar Capital Finance Limited, UGRO Capital Limited, Shriram Finance Limited, and Truhome Finance Limited.
- Each entry specifies the scrip code, scrip ID, ISIN number, record date, ex-date, and settlement DR (designated reference) number.
- Record dates/ex-dates for this batch range from 15 July 2026 to 26 July 2026.
- No attachment was provided with this notice; details are contained in the notice table itself.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a routine operational notice under BSE's Debt segment Listing Operations, implementing standard suspension-of-trading procedures ahead of interest/redemption record dates for NCDs and bonds.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members of the Exchange must not deal in the listed Debentures/Bonds during the suspension window around each security's record date/ex-date.
- Members should update their systems to reflect the suspension for the specific ISINs listed to avoid non-compliant trades.

## Important Dates

- 15 July 2026: Record date/ex-date for Avanti Finance Private Limited (INE0BNQ07139).
- 16 July 2026: Record date/ex-date for Achiievers Finance India Limited (INE065507AP7).
- 17 July 2026: Record date/ex-date for Sammaan Capital Limited (INE148I07FJ9), Axis Finance Limited (INE891K07846), and Muthoot Microfin Limited (INE046W07263).
- 20 July 2026: Record date/ex-date for West Bengal State Electricity Distribution Company Ltd (INE411L07031).
- 21 July 2026: Record date/ex-date for Axis Finance Limited (INE891K08034).
- 22 July 2026: Record date/ex-date for Kosamattam Finance Limited (INE403Q07ES5).
- 23 July 2026: Record date/ex-date for KLM Axiva Finvest Limited (INE01I507968 and INE01I507950), IndoStar Capital Finance Limited (INE896L07934), and UGRO Capital Limited (INE583D07547).
- 24 July 2026: Ex-date for Kosamattam Finance Limited (INE403Q07CU5, record date 26 July 2026), Shriram Finance Limited (INE721A07LD1), and Truhome Finance Limited (INE432R07315).

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt-market operational notice affecting a limited set of NCD/bond ISINs, primarily impacting bondholders and debt-segment trading members who must avoid trading these instruments around their record dates. There is no direct impact on equity markets or the broader listed companies' share prices, as the suspension applies only to specific debt instruments for administrative processing of interest payments and redemptions.
