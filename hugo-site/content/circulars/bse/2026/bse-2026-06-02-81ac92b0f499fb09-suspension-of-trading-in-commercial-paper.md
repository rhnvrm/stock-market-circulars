## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the commercial paper of Sharekhan Limited (Scrip Code: 731257, ISIN: INE211H14AM8, Particulars: SL-4-6-26-CP) will mature on its redemption date. Trading members are advised not to deal in this commercial paper with effect from June 03, 2026.

## Key Points

- Sharekhan Limited's commercial paper (ISIN: INE211H14AM8) is maturing on its redemption date.
- Trading in scrip code 731257 (SL-4-6-26-CP) is suspended effective June 03, 2026.
- Reference number: DR-643/2026-2027.
- Notice issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President – Listing Compliance & Operations.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice pursuant to the maturity of a commercial paper instrument listed on the BSE debt segment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must cease all dealings in Sharekhan Limited's commercial paper (Scrip Code: 731257, ISIN: INE211H14AM8) from June 03, 2026 onwards.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 02, 2026
- **Suspension Effective Date:** June 03, 2026 (Redemption/Maturity Date)

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact, routine notice. The suspension is a standard consequence of commercial paper maturity and does not reflect any adverse corporate event. Only trading members holding or dealing in this specific commercial paper instrument are affected.
