## Summary

BSE has notified all trading members that O3 Securities Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 3280) will be renamed to **Buglerock Capital Private Limited** effective Wednesday, 24th June 2026.

## Key Points

- Trading member O3 Securities Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 3280) is changing its name to Buglerock Capital Private Limited.
- The name change is effective from 24th June 2026.
- The member number (3280) remains unchanged.
- Queries may be directed to membership.ops@bseindia.com.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative update to the trading member's registered name on BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

Other trading members should update their records to reflect the new name of Member No. 3280. No action required from investors or listed companies.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** Wednesday, 24th June 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine name change for a single trading member. Existing memberships, trading rights, and client accounts under Member No. 3280 continue unaffected under the new entity name.
