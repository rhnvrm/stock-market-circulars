## Summary

STAR HOUSING FINANCE LIMITED has announced part redemption of its Non-Convertible Debentures (ISIN: INE526R07017, Code: 975161) with a record date of February 4, 2026. The debentures carry a coupon rate of 13.10% and mature on April 19, 2027. Trading in these debentures will commence with reduced face value effective February 4, 2026.

## Key Points

- Part redemption of 13.10% Non-Convertible Debentures (NCD) series
- ISIN: INE526R07017, BSE Code: 975161
- Combined event: Part redemption and interest payment
- New face value: Rs. 33,333.34 per debenture (post-redemption)
- Trading with reduced face value begins February 4, 2026
- Settlement number: DR-813/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action under existing debenture redemption regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the reduced face value for trading purposes
- All trades executed on or after February 4, 2026 will be at the reduced face value of Rs. 33,333.34
- Debenture holders on record as of February 4, 2026 will be eligible for interest payment and part redemption

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 29, 2026
- **Record Date**: February 4, 2026
- **Effective Date for Reduced Face Value**: February 4, 2026
- **Settlement Number**: DR-813/2025-2026
- **Debenture Maturity**: April 19, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**For Debenture Holders**: Positive impact as they receive partial principal repayment along with interest payment. The reduced face value reflects the partial redemption, reducing outstanding principal exposure.

**For Trading Members**: Operational impact requiring system updates to reflect the new face value of Rs. 33,333.34 per debenture for all transactions from February 4, 2026 onwards.

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders of this specific privately placed debenture series. No broader market implications. The part redemption improves the issuer's debt profile while providing liquidity to investors.
