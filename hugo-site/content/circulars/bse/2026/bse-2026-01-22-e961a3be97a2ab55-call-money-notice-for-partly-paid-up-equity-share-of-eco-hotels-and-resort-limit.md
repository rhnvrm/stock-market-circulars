## Summary

BSE has activated the Call Money Module of its IBBS platform for Eco Hotels and Resort Limited to collect the first call money on 1,28,76,808 partly paid-up equity shares. Trading Members, Self-Certified Syndicate Banks (SCSBs), and intermediaries can submit call money collection details through the platform. The call money amount is Rs. 3.80 per partly paid-up equity share.

## Key Points

- First call on partly paid-up equity shares of Eco Hotels and Resort Limited (symbol: ECOCM)
- Total partly paid-up shares affected: 1,28,76,808 shares
- Call money amount: Rs. 3.80 per share
- Market lot: 1 share
- Bidding hours: 10:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. during issue period
- Issue period duration: 15 days
- Registrar: Bigshare Services Private Limited (SEBI Reg: INR000001385)
- Call Money Module available to SCSBs and Trading Members with 3-in-1 accounts
- Process follows Exchange notice no. 20210104-37 dated January 4, 2021 and SEBI circular dated December 8, 2020

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The circular implements existing framework under:
- BSE Exchange notice no. 20210104-37 dated January 4, 2021
- SEBI circular dated December 8, 2020 regarding call money notices

## Compliance Requirements

**For Trading Members and SCSBs:**
- Submit details of call money notices collected during the issue period through IBBS Call Money Module
- Only Trading Members with 3-in-1 type accounts can access the module
- Ensure submissions made during bidding period (10:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m.)

**For Shareholders:**
- Partly paid-up equity shareholders must pay Rs. 3.80 per share as first call money
- Payment must be made through authorized channels during the issue period

- **Issue Opening Date:** Tuesday, January 27, 2026
