## Summary

BSE has listed and permitted trading of 52,54,700 new equity shares of Candour Techtex Limited (Scrip Code: 522292) on the Exchange effective Monday, June 1, 2026. These shares were issued on a preferential basis to non-promoters at Rs. 125/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- with a premium of Rs. 115/-).

## Key Points

- 52,54,700 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 115/- (issue price Rs. 125/-) to non-promoters on a preferential basis
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 19156739 to 24411438
- ISIN: INE713D01055
- Date of allotment: February 16, 2026
- Trading permitted from June 1, 2026
- All 52,54,700 shares are under lock-in until December 31, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification following a preferential allotment approved under applicable SEBI regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to update their systems to reflect the new securities under Scrip Code 522292 and ISIN INE713D01055
- Lock-in restrictions must be observed for all 52,54,700 shares (dist. nos. 19156739–24411438) until December 31, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** February 16, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** June 1, 2026 (Monday)
- **Lock-in Expiry:** December 31, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal broader market impact. The listing adds 52,54,700 new shares to the tradable float of Candour Techtex Limited; however, these shares are fully locked in until December 31, 2026, meaning no additional supply will enter the market in the near term. The preferential allotment was made to non-promoters at Rs. 125/-, which may serve as a reference price point for market participants.
