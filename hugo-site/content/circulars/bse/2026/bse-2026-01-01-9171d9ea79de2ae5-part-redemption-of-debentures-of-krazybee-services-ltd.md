## Summary

BSE has notified that KRAZYBEE SERVICES LTD (Code: KSL-12.10%-20-10-26-PVT, ISIN: INE07HK07775, Scrip Code: 976114) has fixed a record date for part redemption of non-convertible debentures and payment of interest. Trading in the debentures will commence with reduced face value from January 5, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: KRAZYBEE SERVICES LTD
- ISIN: INE07HK07775
- Scrip Code: 976114
- Record Date: January 5, 2026
- Purpose: Part redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Reduced Face Value: Rs. 37,500 per debenture
- Effective Date: January 5, 2026
- Settlement Number: DR-791/2025-2026
- Notice Number: 20260101-42

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action involving part redemption of debentures as per existing regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that trading in KRAZYBEE SERVICES LTD debentures will be conducted with reduced face value of Rs. 37,500 per debenture
- The reduced face value becomes effective from January 5, 2026
- Trading members should update their systems to reflect the new face value for settlement DR-791/2025-2026

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** January 1, 2026
- **Record Date:** January 5, 2026
- **Effective Date for Reduced Face Value:** January 5, 2026
- **Settlement Number:** DR-791/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

**Debenture Holders:** Holders of KRAZYBEE SERVICES LTD debentures as on the record date (January 5, 2026) will receive partial redemption proceeds and interest payment. The face value reduction to Rs. 37,500 per debenture indicates a partial return of principal.

**Trading Impact:** From January 5, 2026, all trades in these debentures will be executed and settled at the reduced face value of Rs. 37,500. Trading members must adjust their systems accordingly to ensure accurate pricing and settlement.

**Market Segment:** This action is specific to the debt segment and affects only holders of this particular series of non-convertible debentures. No impact on equity markets or other debt instruments.
