## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (Notice No. 20260304-7) informing trading members that the equity shares of Omnitech Engineering Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange. The specific date of listing and further security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Equity shares of Omnitech Engineering Limited are approved for listing on BSE
- Scrip Code assigned: **544720**
- Trading Symbol: **OMNI**
- ISIN: **INE0UH301010**
- Exact listing date to be announced via a separate notice
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing admission notice under BSE's listing framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should note the scrip code (544720) and symbol (OMNI) for reference
- Members should await the follow-up notice from BSE confirming the actual listing date and security details before initiating any trades

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** Wednesday, March 4, 2026
- **Listing Date:** To be announced via a separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This notice signals the imminent listing of Omnitech Engineering Limited on BSE's equity segment. Trading members and investors should watch for the follow-up circular that will confirm the listing date and full security details. The stock will trade under symbol OMNI with ISIN INE0UH301010 once listed.
