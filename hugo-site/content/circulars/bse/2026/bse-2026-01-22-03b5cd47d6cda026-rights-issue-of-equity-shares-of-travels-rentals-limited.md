## Summary

TRAVELS & RENTALS LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544242) has announced a rights issue with record date set for January 28, 2026. The company is offering 1 equity share of Rs.10/- each for every 1 equity share held, at a total issue price of Rs.15/- per share (including premium of Rs.5/-). Trading on ex-rights basis will commence from January 28, 2026 (Settlement No. DR-807/2025-2026).

## Key Points

- Rights ratio: 1:1 (One new equity share for every one existing equity share)
- Face value: Rs.10/- per share
- Premium: Rs.5/- per share
- Total issue price: Rs.15/- per share
- Payment: Full issue price payable at application
- Security type: Dematerialised securities in rolling settlement segment
- Scrip Code: 544242

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a corporate action notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must execute all transactions in the equity shares on ex-rights basis from January 28, 2026
- Full issue price of Rs.15/- per share must be paid at the time of application
- Trading members are required to note and comply with the ex-rights trading requirements

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: January 28, 2026
- **Ex-Rights Date**: January 28, 2026
- **Settlement Number**: DR-807/2025-2026
- **Circular Date**: January 22, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The 1:1 rights ratio represents significant capital raising activity. Existing shareholders will need to decide whether to subscribe to maintain their proportional ownership.

**Shareholder Impact**: Shareholders on record as of January 28, 2026 will be eligible to participate in the rights issue. Non-participation will result in dilution of ownership percentage.

**Trading Impact**: Stock will trade ex-rights from January 28, 2026, which may result in price adjustment reflecting the rights entitlement separation.
