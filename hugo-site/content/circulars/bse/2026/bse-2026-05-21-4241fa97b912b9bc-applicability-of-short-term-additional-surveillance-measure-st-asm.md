## Summary

BSE has identified securities satisfying criteria for inclusion under Short Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) Stage I and Stage II. Enhanced margin requirements will apply from May 25, 2026, covering all open positions as of May 22, 2026, and new positions created from May 25, 2026. The specific securities are listed in Annexure I attached to the notice.

## Key Points

- Securities meeting ST-ASM Stage I criteria will attract a margin rate of 50% or existing margin, whichever is higher, capped at a maximum of 100%.
- Securities meeting ST-ASM Stage II criteria will attract a margin rate of 100% or existing margin, whichever is higher, capped at 100%.
- The enhanced margin applies to all open positions as on May 22, 2026, and all new positions from May 25, 2026.
- ST-ASM is applied in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures.
- Inclusion under ASM is purely a market surveillance action and should not be construed as adverse action against the concerned company.
- Upon exit from the framework, a scrip's price band will be reinstated to its pre-ASM level, unless it remains under another surveillance measure.

## Regulatory Changes

This notice is issued in continuation of prior ASM framework revisions under Exchange notices:
- 20240920-71 (September 20, 2024)
- 20181027-1 (October 27, 2018)
- 20190719-33 (July 19, 2019)
- 20201204-56 (December 4, 2020)
- 20230925-49 (September 25, 2023)
- 20240920-63 (September 20, 2024)

No new framework changes are introduced; this notice applies the existing ST-ASM framework to newly shortlisted securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure collection of applicable enhanced margins (50% for Stage I, 100% for Stage II) on affected securities from May 25, 2026.
- Members must cover open positions as of May 22, 2026, with the revised margin rates.
- Members should refer to Annexure I for the full list of shortlisted securities.
- For clarifications, members may contact BSE Surveillance at bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **May 21, 2026**: Notice issued.
- **May 22, 2026**: Reference date for open positions subject to enhanced margin.
- **May 25, 2026**: Enhanced margin requirements come into effect for both Stage I and Stage II securities.

## Impact Assessment

Traders and investors holding positions in the shortlisted securities will face significantly higher margin obligations. Stage II securities requiring 100% margin effectively restrict leveraged trading entirely. This may reduce liquidity and increase volatility in affected scrips as participants deleverage or exit positions before the effective date. The measure is a standard surveillance tool aimed at curbing excessive speculation; the specific securities affected are detailed in the attached Annexure I and are not identified in the notice text itself.
