## Summary

BSE has announced the introduction of daily Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) facility for THE WEALTH COMPANY MUTUAL FUND on the BSE StAR MF platform. This enhancement allows investors to make daily investments in eligible schemes of THE WEALTH COMPANY MUTUAL FUND through the BSE mutual fund trading platform.

## Key Points

- Daily SIP facility introduced for THE WEALTH COMPANY MUTUAL FUND
- Available on BSE StAR MF platform
- Provides investors with more frequent investment options
- Enhances flexibility in systematic investment planning
- Applicable to eligible schemes of the mutual fund

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational enhancement to the BSE StAR MF platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- Mutual fund distributors and intermediaries should update their systems to support daily SIP transactions
- Investors can opt for daily SIP frequency when placing SIP orders for THE WEALTH COMPANY MUTUAL FUND schemes
- Standard SIP rules and regulations apply

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: February 2, 2026
- Implementation: Effective immediately

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This is a platform enhancement that affects only THE WEALTH COMPANY MUTUAL FUND investors.

**Operational Impact:** Minimal - The daily SIP facility provides additional investment flexibility for retail investors who prefer more frequent contributions. This may attract investors seeking greater control over their investment timing and cash flow management.

**Investor Benefits:** Investors can now make daily contributions instead of being limited to monthly, quarterly, or other frequencies, allowing for better rupee cost averaging and cash flow alignment.
