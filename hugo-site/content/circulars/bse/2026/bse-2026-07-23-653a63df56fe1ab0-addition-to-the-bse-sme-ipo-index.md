## Summary

BSE Index Services Private Limited has announced that Sotefin Bharat Limited (Exchange ticker: 544832), which listed on BSE on July 23, 2026, will be added to the BSE SME IPO Index effective from the market open on July 24, 2026. This follows the company's earlier listing notification (Notice No. 20260722-34).

## Key Points

- Sotefin Bharat Limited listed on BSE on Thursday, July 23, 2026
- The stock (ticker: 544832) will be added to the BSE SME IPO Index effective at market open on Friday, July 24, 2026
- This index action will be reflected in the same night's end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- The notice references and follows up on prior Notice No. 20260722-34

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is a routine index constituent update by BSE Index Services Private Limited following a new SME listing.

## Compliance Requirements

No direct compliance action required from market participants. Index fund managers and ETF providers tracking the BSE SME IPO Index should update their portfolios to reflect the new constituent effective July 24, 2026. Queries can be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- July 23, 2026: Sotefin Bharat Limited listed on BSE
- July 24, 2026: Effective date of addition to the BSE SME IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact, routine index maintenance action affecting passive funds and index trackers benchmarked to the BSE SME IPO Index. There is no direct impact on broader market trading, compliance obligations, or investors outside of those tracking this specific SME index.
