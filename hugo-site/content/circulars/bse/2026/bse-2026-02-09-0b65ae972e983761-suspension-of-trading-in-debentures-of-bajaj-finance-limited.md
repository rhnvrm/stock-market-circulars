## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in specific non-convertible debentures of Bajaj Finance Limited (Code: BFL-8%-27-2-26-PVT, ISIN: INE296A07SJ6, Scrip Code: 974761) effective February 12, 2026. The suspension is implemented due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest.

## Key Points

- Trading suspended for Bajaj Finance debenture series BFL-8%-27-2-26-PVT
- ISIN: INE296A07SJ6, Scrip Code: 974761
- Record date set for February 12, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- No dealings permitted from February 12, 2026
- Circular reference: DR-819/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework changes. This is a standard operational suspension procedure mandated when companies fix record dates for debt instrument redemption and interest payment events.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from February 12, 2026
- All pending orders for the specified ISIN must be cancelled
- Trading members must inform their clients holding these debentures about the suspension
- Settlement obligations for trades executed before suspension date must be completed as per normal settlement cycle

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: February 9, 2026
- **Record Date**: February 12, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date**: February 12, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: High - Complete trading halt affects liquidity for holders of these specific debentures. Investors cannot exit positions from the suspension date.

**Investor Impact**: High - Debenture holders will receive redemption proceeds and final interest payment based on holdings as of record date. No trading flexibility available from suspension date.

**Operational Impact**: Medium - Trading members need to update their systems to block trades in the specified ISIN and communicate with affected clients.

**Liquidity Impact**: High - Zero liquidity for this specific debenture series from February 12, 2026 until redemption is completed.
