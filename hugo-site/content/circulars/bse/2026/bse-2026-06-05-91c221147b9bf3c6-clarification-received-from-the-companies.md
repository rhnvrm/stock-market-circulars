## Summary

BSE's Surveillance Department (DOSS) observed significant price/volume movements in nine scrips and sought clarifications from the respective companies. Six companies have submitted their replies, which are attached to this notice. Three companies have yet to respond.

## Key Points

- BSE wrote to nine companies after observing significant price/volume movement in their scrips
- Six companies have responded; replies are attached to the notice for investor reference
- Three companies have not yet submitted their replies
- Notice issued by the Surveillance Department (DOSS) to ensure trading members and investors have latest relevant information

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced. This is an informational disclosure under BSE's surveillance framework to maintain market transparency.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies that have not yet responded — Elango Industries Ltd (513452), York Exports Ltd (530675), and Colinz Laboratories Ltd (531210) — are expected to submit their clarifications to BSE
- Trading members and investors are advised to review the attached company replies before trading in the affected scrips

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 05 June 2026
- No specific deadline mentioned for pending replies
