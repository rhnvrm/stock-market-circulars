## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Arunis Abode Ltd (scrip code 526935) has changed its name to Kalind Limited. Effective March 10, 2026, the SCRIP ID and abbreviated name on the BOLT Plus System will be updated accordingly.

## Key Points

- Scrip code 526935 will undergo a name change from **Arunis Abode Ltd** to **Kalind Limited**
- The SCRIP ID on BOLT Plus System changes from existing value to **KALIND**
- The abbreviated name on BOLT Plus System changes to **KALIND**
- Change is effective from **March 10, 2026**
- Notice issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President - Listing Compliance and Operations

## Regulatory Changes

The company has formally notified BSE of its name change. BSE is passing on this information to all trading members so they can update their systems and records to reflect the new company name and identifiers.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their records to reflect the new company name: **Kalind Limited**
- All references to the scrip on BOLT Plus System should use the new SCRIP ID and abbreviated name: **KALIND**
- No action required from investors beyond noting the name change; the scrip code (526935) remains unchanged

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** March 4, 2026
- **Effective Date:** March 10, 2026 (name and BOLT Plus System identifiers updated)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate name-change notification with moderate operational impact limited to trading systems and member records. The scrip code (526935) remains unchanged, ensuring continuity of trading. Trading members need to update references in their systems before March 10, 2026 to avoid confusion. Investors holding shares are unaffected beyond the name change.
