## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 89,13,93,612 new equity shares of Tilak Ventures Limited (Scrip Code: 503663) issued through a rights offering. The shares will commence trading on BSE from January 16, 2026, and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- Company: Tilak Ventures Limited (Scrip Code: 503663)
- Number of shares: 89,13,93,612 equity shares of Re. 1/- each
- Issue type: Rights issue at par value
- ISIN: INE026L01022
- Distinctive Numbers: 445696807 to 1337090418
- Allotment date: January 12, 2026
- Trading commencement: January 16, 2026 (Friday)
- Rights: Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for rights issue shares.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the listing of these new securities for trading purposes from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: January 12, 2026
- **Trading Commencement Date**: January 16, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a company-specific listing event with limited market impact. The rights issue significantly increases the equity base of Tilak Ventures Limited. Existing shareholders who participated in the rights offering will see their holdings reflected post-listing. The shares will trade under the existing scrip code 503663 and ISIN INE026L01022.
