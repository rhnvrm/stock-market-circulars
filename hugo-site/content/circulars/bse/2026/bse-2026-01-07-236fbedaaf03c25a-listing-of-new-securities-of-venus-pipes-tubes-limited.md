## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 1,20,000 new equity shares of Venus Pipes & Tubes Limited (Scrip Code: 543528) effective from Thursday, January 8, 2026. These shares were issued at a premium of Rs. 1,690/- on a face value of Rs. 10/- to Non-Promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to the conversion of warrants. The shares will rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- **Company**: Venus Pipes & Tubes Limited
- **Scrip Code**: 543528
- **Number of Shares**: 1,20,000 equity shares
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10/- per share
- **Premium**: Rs. 1,690/- per share
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 1,700/- per share
- **Allottees**: Non-Promoters
- **Basis**: Preferential allotment pursuant to warrant conversion
- **ISIN**: INE0JA001018
- **Distinctive Numbers**: 20596111 to 20716110
- **Date of Allotment**: October 7, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: January 8, 2026
- **Ranking**: Pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for new securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to enable trading in these new securities from January 8, 2026
- The shares are subject to lock-in requirements as specified below
- All 1,20,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 20596111 to 20716110) are locked-in until July 31, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: October 7, 2025
- **Trading Commencement Date**: January 8, 2026
- **Lock-in Period Ends**: July 31, 2026
- **Notice Date**: January 7, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low. The listing of 1.2 lakh shares represents a relatively small addition to the existing equity base. The shares are locked-in until July 31, 2026, preventing immediate selling pressure.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal operational impact for trading members. Standard listing procedures apply. The shares rank pari-passu with existing equity, maintaining uniformity in shareholder rights.

**Dilution Effect**: Limited dilution given the modest size of the preferential allotment. Existing shareholders may experience minor dilution in their ownership percentage.
