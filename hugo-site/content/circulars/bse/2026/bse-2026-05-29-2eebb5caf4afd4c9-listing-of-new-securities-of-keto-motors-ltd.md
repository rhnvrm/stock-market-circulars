## Summary

BSE has notified all market participants that 5,60,47,800 new equity shares of Keto Motors Ltd (Scrip Code: 537392, ISIN: INE392H01026) are listed and permitted for trading on the Exchange with effect from Monday, June 1, 2026. These shares were issued pursuant to the Scheme of Amalgamation of Keto Motors Private Limited (Transferor Company) with Keto Motors Limited (Transferee Company), under a Resolution Plan approved per the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code, 2016, as per the NCLT Hyderabad Bench order dated June 12, 2025.

## Key Points

- 5,60,47,800 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each are newly listed for Keto Motors Ltd (Scrip Code: 537392)
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- ISIN: INE392H01026
- Distribution numbers: 14386673 to 70434472
- Date of allotment: March 31, 2026
- Trading permitted from: June 1, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a listing action arising from a previously approved NCLT Hyderabad Bench order dated June 12, 2025, under the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code, 2016.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance actions required from market participants. The listing is informational — brokers and trading members should update their systems to reflect the new share count and tradability of these securities from June 1, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** March 31, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** June 1, 2026 (Monday)
- **Notice Date:** May 29, 2026
- **NCLT Order Date:** June 12, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited to existing and prospective shareholders of Keto Motors Ltd. The addition of 5.6 crore new shares could affect the stock's float and liquidity. Since these shares rank pari-passu with existing equity, no preferential rights or pricing differentials apply. The amalgamation under IBC reflects a resolution of insolvency for the transferor entity (Keto Motors Private Limited), and this listing completes the corporate restructuring process.
