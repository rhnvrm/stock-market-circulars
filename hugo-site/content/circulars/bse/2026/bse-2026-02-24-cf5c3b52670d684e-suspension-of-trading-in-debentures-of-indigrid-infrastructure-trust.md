## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (No. 20260224-49) informing trading members that INDIGRID INFRASTRUCTURE TRUST has fixed a Record Date of February 27, 2026 for early redemption and interest payment of its Non-Convertible Debentures (IGT-8.9922%-14-2-29-PVT-InvIT). Trading members are instructed not to deal in these debentures effective from February 27, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: INDIGRID INFRASTRUCTURE TRUST
- Debenture Series: IGT-8.9922%-14-2-29-PVT-InvIT
- ISIN: INE219X07025 (Scrip Code: 958599)
- Record Date: February 27, 2026
- Purpose: Early Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- No dealings permitted from: February 27, 2026
- Reference: DR-830/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework changes. This is a standard operational notice under existing BSE debt segment regulations requiring suspension of trading in debentures ahead of a corporate action (early redemption and interest payment).

## Compliance Requirements

- All trading members of BSE are required to immediately cease dealings in the specified debentures (INE219X07025 / 958599) from February 27, 2026.
- Members must take note of the no-dealing restriction and ensure their systems and clients are informed accordingly.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** February 24, 2026
- **Record Date:** February 27, 2026
- **No Dealings From:** February 27, 2026
- **Purpose on Record Date:** Early Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest

## Impact Assessment

Trading in INDIGRID INFRASTRUCTURE TRUST's debentures (IGT-8.9922%-14-2-29-PVT-InvIT) will be suspended from February 27, 2026. Existing holders of these debentures will receive early redemption proceeds along with accrued interest as of the record date. The early redemption signals a reduction in INDIGRID's outstanding debt obligations under this series. Market participants holding or intending to trade these debentures must act before the suspension date.
