## Summary

BSE has listed privately placed debt securities of Muthoot MCred Limited on the BSE Debt Segment effective May 21, 2026. The securities (Scrip Code 977801, ISIN INE101Q07CA7) carry a 9.30% per annum coupon, were allotted on May 20, 2026, and mature on November 20, 2028.

## Key Points

- **Issuer:** Muthoot MCred Limited
- **Scrip Code:** 977801 | **Scrip ID:** 930MML2028
- **ISIN:** INE101Q07CA7
- **Quantity Listed:** 3,00,000 units
- **Face Value / Issue Price:** Rs. 10,000 per unit
- **Coupon Rate:** 9.30% p.a., paid monthly
- **Credit Rating:** ICRA A Stable
- **Market Lot:** 1 | **Tick Size:** 1 paise
- Trading permitted only in dematerialised form

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification under BSE's debt market segment admission process for privately placed instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities exclusively in dematerialised form.
- For further details on the placement, members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at: https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx
- Queries may be directed to the BSE Debt Department at 22728352 / 8597 / 8995 / 5753 / 8915.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** May 20, 2026
- **Listing / Trading Effective:** May 21, 2026
- **First Interest Payment:** July 10, 2026
- **Interest Payment Schedule:** Monthly from July 10, 2026 to November 20, 2028
- **Date of Redemption:** November 20, 2028

## Impact Assessment

This listing has a narrow market impact, affecting only institutional and professional participants in the BSE Debt Segment who trade Muthoot MCred Limited's NCDs. The ICRA A Stable rating indicates investment-grade creditworthiness. The monthly interest payment structure and fixed November 2028 maturity provide clear cash-flow visibility for debt investors. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations is anticipated.
