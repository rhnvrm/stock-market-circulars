## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of ABANS FINANCE PRIVATE LIMITED effective January 19, 2026. The suspension is due to the company setting a record date for debenture redemption and interest payment. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the no-dealings date.

## Key Points

- Company: ABANS FINANCE PRIVATE LIMITED
- Debenture Code: AFPL-Nifty50Index-3-2-26-PVT
- ISIN: INE00ZD07603 (Security Code: 974349)
- Record Date: January 19, 2026
- No Dealings From: January 19, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- Circular Reference: DR-801/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action notification for debenture redemption and interest payment proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in the specified debentures from January 19, 2026
- Trading members must ensure no trades are executed in the affected debenture securities (INE00ZD07603)
- Members must note the record date and suspension details for operational compliance

## Important Dates

- **January 14, 2026**: Circular issue date
- **January 19, 2026**: Record date for redemption and interest payment
- **January 19, 2026**: Trading suspension effective date (no dealings permitted)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The suspension affects a specific private company's debt instrument. Holders of these debentures will not be able to trade them from January 19, 2026.

**Operational Impact**: Medium - Trading members must update their systems to prevent trades in the specified debenture. The suspension facilitates the corporate action process for redemption and interest payment.

**Investor Impact**: High for affected debenture holders - Investors holding these debentures will lose trading liquidity from the suspension date but will receive redemption proceeds and interest as per the company's schedule.
