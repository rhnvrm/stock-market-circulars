## Summary

Emiac Technologies Limited (Scrip Code: 544747, ISIN: INE1OSW01015) will be listed and admitted to dealings on BSE effective Monday, April 13, 2026, under the 'MT' Group of Securities in the SME segment. The scrip will trade in the Trade-for-Trade segment for 10 trading days as per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012.

## Key Points

- **Listing Date:** Monday, April 13, 2026
- **Scrip ID / Code:** EMIAC / 544747
- **ISIN:** INE1OSW01015
- **Total Securities:** 12,243,149 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each, fully paid up (Distinctive Nos. 1 to 12,243,149)
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 98/- per share (Face Value Rs. 10/- + Premium Rs. 88/-)
- **Market Lot:** 1,200 equity shares
- **Group:** MT Group (SME segment)
- **Trade-for-Trade segment** applicable for first 10 trading days
- **Special Pre-open Session (SPOS)** scheduled on April 13, 2026
- Shares tradeable only in dematerialised form
- Date of Allotment in the Public Issue: April 9, 2026
- Financial Year ending: March 31

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The listing is governed by existing SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012, and SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000, mandating demat-only trading for securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade the scrip in minimum market lot of 1,200 equity shares (subject to modification by the Exchange with at least one month's prior notice).
- All trades must be conducted in dematerialised form only.
- Trading members should refer to Exchange notice no. 20120216-29 dated February 16, 2012, for SPOS details.
- Lock-in period details are as per Annexure I; shareholding pattern as per Annexure II. Corporate action for lock-in marking in depository records has been executed.

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard SME IPO listing with limited broader market impact. The scrip is restricted to Trade-for-Trade for the first 10 trading days, reducing speculative intraday activity. The minimum lot size of 1,200 shares at an issue price of Rs. 98 implies a minimum trade value of approximately Rs. 1,17,600, typical for SME listings. Market Maker is Shreni Shares Limited and Registrar is Bigshare Services Private Limited. Investors and trading members dealing in this scrip should note the demat-only requirement and SPOS participation rules on listing day.
