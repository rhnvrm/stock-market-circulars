## Summary

BSE Daily Bulletin No. 197/2025-2026 dated January 14, 2026, provides information on book closure dates and record dates for various corporate actions. The bulletin includes details for dividend payments, redemption of non-convertible debentures (NCDs), and interest payments across multiple securities.

## Key Points

- Accelya Solutions India Limited has set February 6, 2025 as the record date for interim dividend payment on equity shares
- Multiple NCDs issued by Abans Finance Private Limited, Akara Capital Advisors Private Limited, and Ambium Finserve Limited have upcoming interest payment and redemption dates
- Abans Finance Private Limited NCD redemption and interest payment scheduled for January 19, 2026
- Akara Capital Advisors Private Limited has multiple NCD series with record dates ranging from February 1-6, 2026
- Ambium Finserve Limited has scheduled multiple interest payment dates extending through January 2028
- All securities with record dates will have trading flag set to 1

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this bulletin.

## Compliance Requirements

- Investors must hold securities on the specified record dates to be eligible for respective corporate actions
- Securities will trade with appropriate flags on the record dates indicated
- Market participants should note the trading restrictions on record dates for affected securities

## Important Dates

- **January 19, 2026**: Record date for Abans Finance Private Limited NCD redemption and interest payment
- **February 1, 2026**: Record dates for two Akara Capital Advisors NCD series (codes 977126, 977127)
- **February 2, 2026**: Record dates for three Akara Capital Advisors NCD series (codes 976112, 977298, 977383)
- **February 4, 2026**: Record date for Akara Capital Advisors NCD (code 975926)
- **February 6, 2025**: Record date for Accelya Solutions India Limited interim dividend (Note: appears to be past date, likely data entry issue)
- **February 6, 2026**: Record date for Akara Capital Advisors NCD (code 977052)
- **August 4, 2026 - January 4, 2028**: Multiple record dates for Ambium Finserve Limited NCD interest payments and redemption

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine daily bulletin with standard corporate action disclosures. Impact is limited to specific security holders:

- **Equity investors**: Accelya Solutions India Limited shareholders on record date will receive interim dividend
- **Debt investors**: NCD holders of Abans Finance, Akara Capital Advisors, and Ambium Finserve will receive scheduled interest payments and/or principal redemptions based on their holdings on respective record dates
- **Market operations**: Minimal impact, standard trading flag adjustments on record dates
- **Liquidity**: No significant market-wide impact expected from these routine corporate actions
