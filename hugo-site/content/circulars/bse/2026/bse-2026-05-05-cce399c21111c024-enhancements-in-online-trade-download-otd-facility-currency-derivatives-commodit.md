## Summary

BSE has extended the enhanced Online Trade Download (OTD) application to Currency Derivatives, Commodity Derivatives, and EGR segments. This follows the earlier rollout in the Equity and Equity Derivatives segments (circulars 20260206-37 and 20260213-36). The new version is now live in production and accessible to all trading members via leased line or internet connectivity.

## Key Points

- The enhanced OTD application previously deployed for Equity and Equity Derivatives segments is now extended to Currency Derivatives, Commodity Derivatives, and EGR segments.
- The new version is available in production as of May 05, 2026.
- Trading members may choose leased line or internet connectivity based on their preference.
- The existing OTD version continues to operate without any changes; the new version is an addition, not a replacement.
- Members must contact the BSE helpdesk to download and enable the new version along with connectivity details.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a technology infrastructure enhancement by BSE to extend an upgraded OTD facility to additional market segments.

## Compliance Requirements

- No mandatory compliance action required.
- Members wishing to use the new OTD version should contact BSE helpdesk at 022-45720400/600 or 022-69158500, or email bsehelp@bseindia.com.
- Adoption of the new version is optional; the existing OTD version remains available.

## Important Dates

- **May 05, 2026**: New OTD version made available in production for Currency Derivatives, Commodity Derivatives, and EGR segments.

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited to operational convenience for trading members active in Currency Derivatives, Commodity Derivatives, and EGR segments. The enhancement offers an improved trade download facility without disrupting existing workflows. Since the legacy OTD version continues unchanged, members face no forced migration and can transition at their own pace.
