## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members that India Infradebt Limited has fixed a record date of March 10, 2026 for redemption and interest payment on its 7.25% Non-Convertible Debentures (series IIL-7.25%-25-3-26-PVT). Trading members are directed not to deal in these debentures effective March 10, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: India Infradebt Limited
- Debenture Series: IIL-7.25%-25-3-26-PVT
- ISIN: INE537P07596 (Code: 973116)
- Record Date: March 10, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- No dealings permitted from: March 10, 2026
- Notice Reference: DR-836/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notice under BSE's debt segment operational procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any buy or sell transactions in the specified debenture (INE537P07596 / Code 973116) from March 10, 2026 onwards.
- Members are required to take note of and comply with the trading suspension as directed by BSE Listing Compliance and Operations.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: March 04, 2026
- Record Date: March 10, 2026
- No Dealings From: March 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This suspension is limited in scope, affecting only holders and traders of India Infradebt Limited's 7.25% NCD series maturing March 25, 2026. The debenture is approaching its maturity date, and the suspension is a routine pre-redemption measure to ensure clean settlement of final interest and principal repayment. Market impact is minimal and confined to participants in this specific debt instrument.
