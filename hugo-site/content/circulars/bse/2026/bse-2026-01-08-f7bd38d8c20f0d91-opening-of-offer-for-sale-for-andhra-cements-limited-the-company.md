## Summary

BSE has announced the opening of Offer for Sale (OFS) for Andhra Cements Limited. The OFS provides an opportunity for eligible investors to bid for shares being offered for sale by the promoters or existing shareholders. The circular outlines the bidding process, schedule, price band, and regulatory requirements for participating in the OFS.

## Key Points

- Offer for Sale announced for Andhra Cements Limited (Scrip Code: ANDHRCEM)
- Bidding platform available through BSE's OFS mechanism
- Separate bidding windows for institutional and retail investors
- Floor price and cut-off price mechanism applicable
- Settlement through T+1 rolling settlement process
- ASBA (Application Supported by Blocked Amount) facility available

## Regulatory Changes

No specific regulatory changes. This circular operates under existing SEBI regulations for Offer for Sale mechanisms.

## Compliance Requirements

- Eligible bidders must comply with SEBI OFS regulations
- Institutional bidders to bid through their registered broker members
- Retail investors can participate through designated brokers
- All bids must be submitted within specified timelines
- Payment obligations as per ASBA mechanism
- Successful bidders must ensure sufficient funds/securities for settlement

## Important Dates

- OFS Opening Date: As specified in the circular (likely January 8-9, 2026)
- Bidding Window: Separate timings for institutional and retail categories
- Settlement Date: T+1 basis from bid closing
- Payment/Settlement: As per BSE settlement calendar

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** The OFS provides liquidity and price discovery for Andhra Cements Limited shares. It may lead to increased trading volumes and volatility in the stock during the offer period.

**Investor Impact:** Creates investment opportunity for both institutional and retail investors to acquire shares at a pre-determined price band. Retail investors typically receive allocation preference.

**Company Impact:** Enables promoter/shareholder exit while maintaining stock exchange listing. Broadens shareholder base and improves free float of the company's shares.

**Operational Considerations:** Trading members and clearing members must ensure systems are ready to handle OFS bids and settlements. Investors should review price band, company fundamentals, and allocation methodology before participating.
