## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 12,58,918 new equity shares of TANFAC Industries Ltd (Scrip Code: 506854, ISIN: INE639B01023) issued under a Qualified Institutional Placement (QIP) are listed and admitted for trading on the Exchange with effect from Tuesday, June 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- 12,58,918 equity shares of Rs. 5/- each allotted to Qualified Institutional Buyers (QIBs) via QIP are now listed on BSE
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Issue price: Rs. 1985.83/- per share
- Distribution numbers: 19950001 to 21208918
- Contact for clarifications: Ms. Prachi Babadi, Tel: 022-22728035

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing admission notice following a completed QIP allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to update their systems to recognise the newly listed shares under Scrip Code 506854 (ISIN: INE639B01023) for trading from June 30, 2026.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: June 25, 2026
- Effective date of listing and trading: June 30, 2026 (Tuesday)

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds approximately 12.59 lakh new equity shares to TANFAC Industries Ltd's tradable float. At the QIP issue price of Rs. 1985.83, this represents a capital raise of approximately Rs. 250 crore. The dilution may have a moderate impact on existing shareholders, while the QIP price serves as a near-term price reference point for the stock.
