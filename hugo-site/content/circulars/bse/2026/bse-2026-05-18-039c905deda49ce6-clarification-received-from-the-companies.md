## Summary

BSE's Surveillance department issued Notice No. 20260518-23 dated 18 May 2026 informing trading members and investors that clarifications have been received from five companies that had exhibited significant price/volume movement. The Exchange had sought explanations from these companies to ensure market participants have the latest relevant information. One company, ZR2 Bioenergy Ltd, has not yet submitted its reply.

## Key Points

- BSE observed significant price/volume movement in six scrips and sought clarifications from the respective companies.
- Replies have been received from five companies: Anuroop Packaging Ltd (542865), Ken Financial Services Ltd (530547), Ritco Logistics Ltd (542383), Safety Controls & Devices Ltd (544746), and TeleCanor Global Ltd (530595).
- Reply is still awaited from ZR2 Bioenergy Ltd (Security Code: 506640).
- The attached replies from the five companies are available with the notice.
- Notice issued by the Surveillance department under Deputy Vice President Sanjay Jain and Manager Shreya Ghose.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice is part of BSE's ongoing surveillance mechanism to maintain market integrity and ensure timely disclosure of material information.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and investors are advised to review the clarifications submitted by the five companies before making investment decisions in these scrips.
- ZR2 Bioenergy Ltd (506640) is required to submit its reply to BSE's query at the earliest.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 18 May 2026
- Reply pending from ZR2 Bioenergy Ltd — no deadline specified in the notice.

## Impact Assessment

The receipt of clarifications from five companies partially resolves uncertainty around the observed price/volume movements in those scrips. Investors in Anuroop Packaging, Ken Financial Services, Ritco Logistics, Safety Controls & Devices, and TeleCanor Global can refer to the attached replies for context. ZR2 Bioenergy Ltd (506640) remains under scrutiny with its response still pending, which may sustain cautious sentiment around that scrip until a clarification is provided.
