## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for Savani Financials Limited. The company's name has been officially changed to Mantra Capital Limited with effect from October 14, 2025. The company was originally incorporated as Savani Investment & Leasing Limited.

## Key Points

- Old name: SAVANI FINANCIALS LIMITED
- New name: MANTRA CAPITAL LIMITED
- Corporate Identification Number (CIN): L67120MH1983PLC031614
- Certificate issued by: Central Processing Centre, Ministry of Corporate Affairs
- Original incorporation name: SAVANI INVESTMENT & LEASING LIMITED
- Registered office: 602, Samarth Vaibhav Co-op Hsg Society Ltd., Off - Link Road, Lokhandwala Complex, Andheri (West), Mumbai - 400053, Maharashtra
- Certificate digitally signed by Assistant/Deputy/Registrar of Companies - Sweety Kumar

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard administrative name change process under the Companies Act, 2013.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display the old name (Savani Financials Limited) along with its new name (Mantra Capital Limited) for a period of two years at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders
- All existing obligations and legal standing of the company remain unchanged

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date of Name Change**: October 14, 2025
- **Certificate Issue Date**: October 14, 2025
- **Display Requirement Period**: Two years from October 14, 2025 (until October 14, 2027)

## Impact Assessment

This is a purely administrative change with minimal market impact. The name change does not affect:
- Company operations or business activities
- Rights and liabilities of shareholders and other stakeholders
- Trading status or listing on stock exchanges
- Legal standing or contractual obligations

Stakeholders can verify the company's latest status and view public documents on the Ministry of Corporate Affairs website at www.mca.gov.in/MCA21.
