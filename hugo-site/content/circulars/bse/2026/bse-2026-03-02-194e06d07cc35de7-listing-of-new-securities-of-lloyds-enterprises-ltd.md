## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 22,02,95,320 new equity shares of Lloyds Enterprises Ltd. (Scrip Code: 512463) are available for trading effective Wednesday, March 4, 2026. These shares arise from the conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up shares of Re. 1/- each, issued on a rights basis.

## Key Points

- Total new securities: 22,02,95,320 equity shares of Re. 1/- each
- These shares result from conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up on rights basis
- ISIN: INE080I01025
- Scrip Code: 512463
- New shares are pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Two tranches of distinctive numbers assigned

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification under BSE's listing and trading admission procedures for newly issued securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are permitted and informed to trade in these new securities from the effective date
- No additional compliance actions required from trading members beyond normal trading procedures

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: March 2, 2026
- Trading Effective Date: Wednesday, March 4, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds approximately 220.3 million new equity shares to the tradeable float of Lloyds Enterprises Ltd. The shares carry the same rights as existing equity shares (pari-passu), so no dilution in per-share rights occurs relative to the rights issue terms. Distinctive numbers span two ranges: 1272126622–1403230610 (13,11,03,989 shares) and 1404612073–1493803403 (8,91,91,331 shares). Market impact is moderate, reflecting an increase in available liquidity for the scrip.
