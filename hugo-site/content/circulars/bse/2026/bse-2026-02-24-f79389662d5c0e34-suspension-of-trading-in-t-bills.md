## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (No. 20260224-10) informing trading members that two Treasury Bills — 182TB27226 (Scrip Code 805110) and 364TB27226 (Scrip Code 805033) — are maturing on their respective redemption dates. Trading members are directed to cease dealing in these T-Bills with effect from February 25, 2026.

## Key Points

- Two T-Bills are scheduled for maturity and will be suspended from trading effective February 25, 2026
- Scrip Code 805110 (ISIN: IN002025Y222) — 182-day T-Bill: 182TB27226
- Scrip Code 805033 (ISIN: IN002024Z461) — 364-day T-Bill: 364TB27226
- Notice reference: DR-828/2025-2026
- Issued by: Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President – Listing Compliance & Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a routine operational action under existing BSE debt market guidelines for T-Bills reaching maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in Scrip Code 805110 (IN002025Y222 / 182TB27226) from February 25, 2026 onwards
- Trading members must not deal in Scrip Code 805033 (IN002024Z461 / 364TB27226) from February 25, 2026 onwards
- Members are advised to take note and ensure compliance immediately

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** February 24, 2026
- **Suspension Effective Date:** February 25, 2026 (trading halt for both T-Bills)
- **Redemption Date:** February 25, 2026 (maturity date for both instruments)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, low-impact notice affecting only holders and traders of these two specific T-Bills. The suspension is a standard end-of-life procedure for maturing government securities. Market participants holding these instruments should prepare for redemption processing. No broader market impact is anticipated.
