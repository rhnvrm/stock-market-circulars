## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading for a specific debenture series (AHFL-7.05%-24-2-26-PVT, ISIN: INE883F07256, Code: 973820) issued by AADHAR HOUSING FINANCE LIMITED. The suspension is effective from February 9, 2026, coinciding with the record date set for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in this debenture from the specified date.

## Key Points

- AADHAR HOUSING FINANCE LIMITED debenture series AHFL-7.05%-24-2-26-PVT suspended from trading
- ISIN: INE883F07256, BSE Code: 973820
- Record date: February 9, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- No dealings permitted from February 9, 2026
- Notice number: DR-816/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action notification for debenture redemption and interest payment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debenture (INE883F07256) from February 9, 2026
- Members should update their systems to reflect the trading suspension
- Clients holding these debentures should be informed about the suspension and upcoming redemption

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 30, 2026
- **Record Date**: February 9, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Date**: February 9, 2026 (no dealings from this date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low to medium - affects only holders and traders of this specific debenture series. The suspension is temporary and related to the natural maturity/redemption process.

**Operational Impact**: Medium for debt market participants - trading members need to ensure compliance with the no-dealing instruction and update their systems accordingly. Investors holding this debenture will receive redemption proceeds and final interest payment as per the record date.

**Liquidity Impact**: Limited - the debenture will not be tradable from the suspension date, but this is expected as part of the redemption process. Holders will receive cash proceeds upon redemption.
