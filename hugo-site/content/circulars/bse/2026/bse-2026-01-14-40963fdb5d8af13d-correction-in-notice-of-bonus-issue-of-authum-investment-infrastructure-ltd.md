## Summary

Authum Investment & Infrastructure Limited has issued a corrected undertaking for the listing of 67,93,80,400 bonus equity shares under Chapter XI of SEBI (ICDR) Regulations, 2018. The bonus shares will be credited to the depository system by 12 PM on Wednesday, January 14, 2026 (next working day after record date), and will be available for trading from the beginning of day on Friday, January 16, 2026.

## Key Points

- Total bonus shares to be listed: 67,93,80,400 equity shares
- Issuance under Chapter XI of SEBI (ICDR) Regulations, 2018
- Credit to depository system: Latest by 12 PM on January 14, 2026 (Wednesday)
- Trading availability: Beginning of day (BOD) on January 16, 2026 (Friday)
- This is a corrected notice updating the original bonus issue announcement

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This circular provides corrected timelines for compliance with existing SEBI (ICDR) Regulations, 2018 requirements for bonus share issuance.

## Compliance Requirements

- Company must credit bonus shares to depository system by 12 PM on January 14, 2026
- Shares must be made available for trading from BOD January 16, 2026
- Undertaking submitted by Company Secretary and Compliance Officer as per regulatory requirements

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: Prior to January 14, 2026
- **Depository Credit Deadline**: Wednesday, January 14, 2026 by 12:00 PM
- **Trading Commencement**: Friday, January 16, 2026 (Beginning of Day)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - affects shareholders of Authum Investment & Infrastructure Limited who are entitled to bonus shares based on the record date. The corrected timeline provides clarity on when shares will be credited and available for trading.

**Operational Impact**: Shareholders and brokers need to note the revised credit and trading timelines. The bonus shares will significantly increase the total equity base of the company (67.94 crore shares), which may impact share price and liquidity upon commencement of trading.
