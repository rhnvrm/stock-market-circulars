## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 100 bonus equity shares of Tata Elxsi Ltd (Scrip Code: 500408, ISIN: INE670A01012) that were previously kept in abeyance are now listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Wednesday, March 04, 2026.

## Key Points

- 100 Bonus Equity Shares of Rs. 10/- each, previously held in abeyance, are now listed on BSE.
- Scrip Code: 500408 | ISIN: INE670A01012
- Distinctive Numbers: 31165821 to 31165920 (100 shares total)
- Trading permitted from Wednesday, March 04, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard administrative listing notice for bonus shares that were earlier withheld from trading (kept in abeyance) and are now cleared for listing.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed and expected to note the listing of these securities and update their systems accordingly to facilitate trading from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** March 02, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** Wednesday, March 04, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. Only 100 shares are being listed, representing a negligible addition to Tata Elxsi's existing share capital. This is a routine administrative action to release bonus shares previously kept in abeyance, with no material effect on price discovery or liquidity.
