## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (No. 20260310-51) informing trading members that EarlySalary Services Private Limited has fixed a Record Date of 12 March 2026 for the redemption and interest payment of its Non-Convertible Debentures. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from 12 March 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: EarlySalary Services Private Limited
- Debenture Series: ESPL-10.9%-27-3-26-PVT
- ISIN: INE01YL07292 (BSE Code: 976055)
- Record Date: 12 March 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- No dealings permitted from: 12 March 2026
- Notice Reference: DR-838/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice under BSE's debt segment procedures for suspension of trading ahead of a corporate action (redemption and interest payment).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in EarlySalary Services Private Limited debentures (ISIN: INE01YL07292, Code: 976055) from 12 March 2026 onwards.
- Members should update their systems to reflect the trading suspension effective on the record date.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 10 March 2026
- Record Date: 12 March 2026
- No Dealings From: 12 March 2026

## Impact Assessment

This notice affects holders and traders of EarlySalary Services Private Limited's NCD series ESPL-10.9%-27-3-26-PVT. The suspension is limited in scope, applying only to this specific debenture series in the BSE debt segment. The redemption on the record date indicates the instrument is reaching maturity, with investors receiving principal and final interest payment. Market impact is low given the private placement nature of the instrument.
