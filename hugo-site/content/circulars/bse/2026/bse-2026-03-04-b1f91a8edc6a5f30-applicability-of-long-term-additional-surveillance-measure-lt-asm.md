## Summary

BSE has issued an update to the Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LT-ASM) framework effective March 05, 2026. Eleven new securities have been shortlisted for inclusion in the LT-ASM framework (Part A), one security (Elango Industries Ltd) has been moved to a higher ASM Stage II (Part B), no securities are being moved to a lower stage (Part C), no securities are being added directly to Stage IV (Part D), and one security (Capital Trust Ltd) is being removed from the LT-ASM framework due to inclusion in the ESM framework (Annexure II).

## Key Points

- **11 new securities** added to Long Term ASM framework w.e.f. March 05, 2026
- **1 security** (Elango Industries Ltd, Scrip Code 513452) moved to higher ASM Stage II
- **No securities** moved to lower ASM stages
- **No securities** added directly to Stage IV LT-ASM
- **1 security** (Capital Trust Ltd, Scrip Code 511505) removed from LT-ASM due to inclusion in ESM framework
- Consolidated LT-ASM list contains a large number of securities across multiple ASM stages

## Regulatory Changes

The LT-ASM framework is being updated as part of BSE's ongoing surveillance mechanism to monitor securities exhibiting abnormal price/volume behavior over a longer time horizon. Securities placed under LT-ASM are subject to enhanced scrutiny, including restrictions on price movement and mandatory trade-for-trade settlement in certain stages.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Brokers and trading members** must update their systems to reflect the revised LT-ASM list effective March 05, 2026
- **Investors** holding securities newly added to LT-ASM should be aware of applicable trading restrictions and margin requirements
- **Companies** placed under LT-ASM should note that their securities will be subject to enhanced surveillance conditions

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** March 05, 2026 — all changes (additions, stage movements, and removals) take effect
