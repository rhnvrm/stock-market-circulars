## Summary

BSE has notified that Sayaji Industries Limited (Scrip Code: 540728) will be excluded from the Periodic Call Auction System (PCAS) mechanism effective January 06, 2026. This follows the company's listing notice dated October 05, 2017.

## Key Points

- Company: Sayaji Industries Limited
- ISIN: INE327G01024
- Scrip Code: 540728
- Action: Exclusion from PCAS mechanism
- Notice references previous listing notice 20171005-43 dated October 05, 2017
- Trading members can contact BSE Listing Compliance & Operations for clarifications

## Regulatory Changes

The securities of Sayaji Industries Limited will no longer be traded under the Periodic Call Auction System (PCAS) mechanism and will likely move to regular trading sessions.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE must note the change in trading mechanism for Sayaji Industries Limited securities and adjust their trading systems accordingly.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 05, 2026
- **Effective Date**: January 06, 2026 (Tuesday)
- **Reference Notice**: October 05, 2017 (original listing notice)

## Impact Assessment

This change has minimal market impact and affects only trading members dealing with Sayaji Industries Limited securities. The exclusion from PCAS typically indicates improved liquidity or trading characteristics that no longer require the periodic call auction mechanism. Trading members should update their systems to reflect the new trading mechanism for this scrip.
