## Summary

BSE has announced that Navkar Share & Stock Brokers Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 111) will voluntarily cease business operations in the Currency derivative segment effective January 12, 2026. This is an administrative notice informing trading members about the voluntary closure.

## Key Points

- Navkar Share & Stock Brokers Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 111) is voluntarily closing operations
- Closure applies specifically to the Currency derivative segment
- The cessation is due to voluntary business closure decision
- Trading members have been notified of this change

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative notification about a member's voluntary business closure.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for other trading members. For queries or clarifications, members should contact membership.ops@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 9, 2026
- **Effective Date**: January 12, 2026 (Monday) - Navkar Share & Stock Brokers Pvt. Ltd. will cease currency derivative operations

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a voluntary closure by a single trading member affecting only their currency derivatives operations. Other market participants and segments remain unaffected. The closure is administrative in nature and does not indicate any systemic issues or regulatory concerns.
