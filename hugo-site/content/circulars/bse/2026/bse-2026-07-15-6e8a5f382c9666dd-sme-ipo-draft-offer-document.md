## Summary

BSE has notified that the Draft Offer Document of Omara Ventures India Limited, filed on July 14, 2026, has been made available on the SME segment website. The document, containing all disclosures required under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations, will be hosted for a minimum period of 21 days to allow for public comments.

## Key Points

- Company: Omara Ventures India Limited (SME segment)
- Draft Offer Document filed on July 14, 2026
- Document contains disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations
- Hosted on the SME IPO DRHP page: https://www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public comments invited within 21 days from the date of the notice
- Comments to be emailed to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, DCS-Listing department

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a procedural disclosure under existing ICDR (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations governing SME IPO draft offer documents.

## Compliance Requirements

- Interested parties/public must submit comments on the Draft Offer Document within 21 days of the notice date (by around August 5, 2026) to equity.ipo@bseindia.com.
- No action required from listed companies or trading members; this is an informational hosting notice.

## Important Dates

- Draft Offer Document filed: July 14, 2026
- Notice date: July 15, 2026
- Public comment window: 21 days from notice date (minimum hosting period)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has limited immediate market impact and is relevant primarily to prospective investors, merchant bankers, and stakeholders tracking the SME IPO pipeline for Omara Ventures India Limited. It represents a routine regulatory disclosure step in the SME IPO process rather than a trading or compliance event affecting existing listed securities.
