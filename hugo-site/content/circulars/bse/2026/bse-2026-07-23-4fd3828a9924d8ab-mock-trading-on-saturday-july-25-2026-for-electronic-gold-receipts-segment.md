## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Electronic Gold Receipts (EGR) segment on Saturday, July 25, 2026, to be conducted from both the disaster recovery (DR) site and the primary (PR) site. Trading members using third-party trading platforms of empaneled vendors or in-house developed systems through ETI APIs can use this session to test their trading applications, including exceptional market condition functionalities such as risk-reduction mode and trading halts. There will be no new BOLT TWS Release for this exercise.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session for EGR segment on Saturday, July 25, 2026, from DR and PR sites.
- Enables testing of third-party vendor platforms and in-house systems via ETI APIs.
- Tests functionalities including risk-reduction mode and trading halt under exceptional market conditions.
- No new BOLT TWS Release accompanies this session.
- Trades from mock session carry no margin, pay-in/pay-out obligations, and create no rights or liabilities.
- Members are requested to actively participate and submit feedback by 3:00 pm on the session day.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is an operational/testing notice and does not introduce any regulatory or policy change.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members, particularly those using third-party empaneled vendor platforms or in-house ETI API-based systems, are encouraged to participate in the mock session and submit feedback to BSE Tech Support (bse.tech@bseindia.com, 022-22728053) or the Help Desk (bsehelp@bseindia.com, 022-45720400/600, 022-69158500) by 3:00 pm on the day of the session.

## Important Dates

- Mock trading session: Saturday, July 25, 2026
- DR site session: Log-in 07:30 am–08:00 am; Pre-Open order entry 08:00–08:08 am; Matching 08:08–08:15 am; Continuous trading 08:15 am–10:45 am; Block deal window 09:45–10:00 am
- Switchover to PR site: 12:30 pm–01:00 pm
- PR site session: Pre-Open order entry 01:00–01:08 pm; Matching 01:08–01:15 pm; Continuous trading 01:15–02:00 pm; Trade modification until 02:10 pm
- Feedback deadline: 3:00 pm on July 25, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, low-impact operational notice affecting only trading members and vendors participating in system testing. No effect on listed securities, market pricing, or investor obligations, as mock trades do not create margin, settlement, or legal obligations.
