## Summary

BSE has listed 1,21,00,000 new equity shares of Websol Energy System Limited (Scrip Code: 517498, ISIN: INE855C01023) effective Thursday, May 21, 2026. The shares were issued at a premium of Rs. 52/- per share (issue price Rs. 53/-) to the promoter group on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 1,21,00,000 equity shares of Re. 1/- each issued to promoter group on preferential allotment
- Issue price: Rs. 53/- per share (face value Re. 1/- + premium of Rs. 52/-)
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 42,20,63,471 to 43,41,63,470
- Date of allotment: 13 March 2026
- Trading permitted from 21 May 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notice pursuant to preferential allotment under existing SEBI regulations governing warrant conversions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed that the new securities are listed and permitted to trade on BSE from May 21, 2026
- All 1,21,00,000 shares are subject to lock-in restrictions and cannot be freely traded by the promoter group until the lock-in period expires

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** 13 March 2026
- **Trading Effective Date:** 21 May 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry Date:** 29 November 2027 (all 1,21,00,000 shares, dist. nos. 422063471–434163470)

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds approximately 1.21 crore shares to the total equity base of Websol Energy System Limited. Since the entire allotment is under lock-in until November 2027, there is no immediate increase in free-float or selling pressure in the near term. The preferential allotment to the promoter group increases promoter shareholding and may reflect the company's fundraising or capital restructuring strategy. Market impact is expected to be minimal given the lock-in constraint.
