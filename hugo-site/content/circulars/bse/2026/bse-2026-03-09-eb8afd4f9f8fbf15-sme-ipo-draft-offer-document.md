## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document of Zebion Infotech Limited, filed on March 07, 2026, on the SME platform. The document contains all disclosures as required under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and is available for public review and comment for a minimum of 21 days.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Zebion Infotech Limited on March 07, 2026
- Document hosted on the BSE SME website for at least 21 days
- Public comments are invited from interested parties
- Draft Offer Document complies with Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard disclosure requirement under Schedule VI of the ICDR (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Interested parties and the public may review the Draft Offer Document on the BSE SME website: https://www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Comments, if any, must be submitted within 21 days from March 09, 2026
- Comments to be sent to: equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- Draft Offer Document filing date: March 07, 2026
- Notice date: March 09, 2026
- Public comment deadline: March 30, 2026 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited broad market impact. It is relevant to potential investors, analysts, and stakeholders interested in the upcoming SME IPO of Zebion Infotech Limited. The 21-day public comment window allows market participants to flag any concerns regarding the disclosures before the IPO proceeds.
