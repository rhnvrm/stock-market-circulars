## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members that the equity shares of Clean Max Enviro Energy Solutions Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange. The exact date of listing will be communicated via a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Clean Max Enviro Energy Solutions Limited is being listed on BSE under Scrip Code **544717** and Symbol **CLEANMAX**
- ISIN assigned: **INE647U01026**
- The listing date has not yet been specified and will be announced through a separate notice
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification under BSE's standard procedures for admitting new securities to dealings.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are hereby informed of the upcoming listing and should prepare their systems accordingly
- Further details on trading parameters, lot size, and price band will be provided in the subsequent listing notice

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** February 27, 2026
- **Listing Date:** To be announced via a separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a moderate market impact as it signals the imminent listing of Clean Max Enviro Energy Solutions Limited, a company operating in the clean/renewable energy sector. Investors holding IPO allotments should await the follow-up notice confirming the exact listing date. Trading members should ensure readiness for admitting this scrip to dealings upon the official listing date announcement.
