## Summary

BSE has updated the list of securities under the encumbrance surveillance measure pursuant to Regulation 28(3) of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 2011. Effective March 05, 2026, **Shalimar Paints Ltd** (Scrip Code: 509874, ISIN: INE849C01026) has been added to the framework. No securities are moving out of the framework in this update. The consolidated list now comprises 11 securities.

## Key Points

- **Shalimar Paints Ltd** (Scrip Code: 509874, ISIN: INE849C01026) is newly added to the encumbrance measure effective March 05, 2026.
- No securities are being removed from the framework in this update (Annexure II lists Nil exits).
- The consolidated list (Annexure III) now contains **11 securities**.
- The measure applies to companies with high Promoter as well as non-Promoter encumbrance as per Reg. 28(3) of SEBI (SAST) Regulations 2011.
- Securities marked (*) are based on NSE data.

## Regulatory Changes

This circular is issued under **Regulation 28(3) of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 2011**, which mandates surveillance actions on companies where promoter and/or non-promoter encumbrance levels are deemed high. The update revises the shortlisted securities list as of March 05, 2026.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and investors should note the inclusion of Shalimar Paints Ltd in this framework.
- Companies in the consolidated list may be subject to additional disclosures, trading restrictions, or margin requirements as applicable under the encumbrance surveillance framework.
- Market participants should review their exposure to all 11 listed securities.
- Securities exiting due to inclusion in LT ASM, ESM, or TT frameworks are governed by those respective frameworks' rules.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** March 05, 2026 — Shalimar Paints Ltd enters the encumbrance measure framework.
- **Circular Date:** March 04, 2026.
