## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that equity shares of Aritas Vinyl Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544683, symbol ARITAS, and ISIN number INE1D8001016. The exact date of listing and detailed security information will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Aritas Vinyl Limited equity shares to be listed on BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544683
- Trading Symbol: ARITAS
- ISIN: INE1D8001016
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the scrip details for Aritas Vinyl Limited and await the separate notice for the actual listing date and trading commencement.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: January 22, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

Minimal market impact. This is a routine SME listing announcement affecting only those interested in this specific company. No immediate action required by trading members until the listing date is announced.
