## Summary

BSE has listed 1,44,36,764 new equity shares of EPUJA SPIRITECH LIMITED (Scrip Code: 532092, ISIN: INE807D01030) for trading effective Wednesday, June 3, 2026. The shares were issued at Rs. 3.40/- each (face value Re. 1/- at a premium of Rs. 2.40/-) to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- Total new shares listed: 1,44,36,764 equity shares of Re. 1/- each
- Issue price: Rs. 3.40/- per share (face value Re. 1/- + premium Rs. 2.40/-)
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis via warrant conversion
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE807D01030
- Two tranches of allotment: 64,77,451 shares (11 Sep 2025) and 79,59,313 shares (22 Sep 2025)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for newly allotted securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed that these securities are now permitted to trade. The entire lot of 1,44,36,764 shares is subject to lock-in until 30 December 2026.

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date 1: 11 September 2025 — 64,77,451 shares (Dist. Nos. 89046690–95524140)
- Allotment Date 2: 22 September 2025 — 79,59,313 shares (Dist. Nos. 95524141–103483453)
- Trading Commencement: 3 June 2026
- Lock-in Expiry: 30 December 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. The listing adds approximately 1.44 crore shares to the tradeable float of EPUJA SPIRITECH LIMITED; however, all newly listed shares are under lock-in until 30 December 2026, so no immediate increase in tradeable supply. The preferential allotment at Rs. 3.40/- per share to non-promoters via warrant conversion is a completed corporate action with no further compliance obligations for trading members beyond awareness of the new securities.
