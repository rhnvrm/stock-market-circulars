## Summary

BSE has announced the schedule of activities for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) of SHREE DIGVIJAY CEMENT COMPANY LTD (SHREDIGCEM, Scrip Code: 502180, ISIN: INE232A01011). The offer will run from Monday, January 5, 2026 to Friday, January 16, 2026. The acquirer is offering Rs. 92.20 per share for up to 3,85,43,837 equity shares with a face value of Rs. 10.00 and market lot of 1.

## Key Points

- **Security Details**: SHREE DIGVIJAY CEMENT COMPANY LTD (Symbol: SHREDIGCEM, Scrip Code: 502180, ISIN: INE232A01011)
- **Offer Size**: Up to 3,85,43,837 equity shares
- **Offer Price**: Rs. 92.20 per share
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10.00
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Offer Period**: January 5, 2026 to January 16, 2026
- **Bid Offering Period**: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM (January 5-16, 2026)
- **Trading Members can bid for investors having shares in demat form only**
- **Eligible Categories**: FII, Financial Institutions, Mutual Funds, Insurance Companies, Other QIBs, Other NIBs, Companies, and Individuals

## Session Timings

- **Bid Offering Period**: January 5-16, 2026 | 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM
- **Give-up/Take-up**: January 5-15, 2026 | 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM
- **Give-up (last day)**: January 16, 2026 | 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM
- **Take-up (last day)**: January 16, 2026 | 9:15 AM to 4:00 PM

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members and Custodian Members must ensure UCC of the investor is registered and/or updated with correct PAN Number and Category in the Exchange UCC database
- Trading Members can only bid for investors having shares in demat form
- Files on Extranet for acceptance of bids will be available only after Exchange receives confirmation from Merchant Banker/RTA after issue closure
- If no securities are accepted, no End of Issue files will be generated in Extranet

## Impact Assessment

This takeover offer provides an exit opportunity for shareholders of SHREE DIGVIJAY CEMENT COMPANY LTD at Rs. 92.20 per share. The 10-day offer window allows sufficient time for eligible investors to participate through their trading members.
