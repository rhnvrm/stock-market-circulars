## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 31,25,000 new equity shares of Eraaya Lifespaces Limited (Scrip Code: 531035, ISIN: INE432F01032) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Wednesday, May 27, 2026. The shares were issued to promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to the conversion of warrants at an issue price of Rs. 81/- per share (face value Rs. 1/- + premium Rs. 80/-).

## Key Points

- 31,25,000 equity shares of Rs. 1/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 80/- per share (issue price Rs. 81/-)
- Issued to Promoters on a preferential basis via conversion of warrants
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 206694161 to 209819160
- ISIN: INE432F01032
- All 31,25,000 shares are subject to lock-in until November 30, 2027

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification under BSE's listing operations for newly issued securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the listing and lock-in details. The locked-in shares (distribution numbers 206694161 to 209819160) cannot be traded until November 30, 2027.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: April 23, 2026
- Effective trading date: May 27, 2026
- Lock-in expiry: November 30, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. The new shares represent a preferential allotment to promoters and are fully locked in until November 2027, so no immediate increase in freely tradable float. This is a routine corporate action following warrant conversion by the promoter group of Eraaya Lifespaces Limited.
