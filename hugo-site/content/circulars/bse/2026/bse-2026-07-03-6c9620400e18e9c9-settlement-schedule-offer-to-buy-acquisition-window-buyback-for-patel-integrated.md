## Summary

BSE Clearing (ICCL) has issued the settlement calendar for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Buyback) of Patel Integrated Logistics Limited. The bidding window runs from 06/07/2026 to 10/07/2026, with obligation download on 16/07/2026 and settlement on 17/07/2026 under Settlement No. 2026083.

## Key Points

- Settlement Type: Buyback; Settlement No. 2026083
- Bid Start Date: 06/07/2026; Bid End Date: 10/07/2026
- Obligation Download: 16/07/2026
- Settlement Date: 17/07/2026
- Demat shares must be delivered only via the Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism, available in both depositories (CDSL & NSDL)
- Market Type “(Buyback)” and Settlement No. 2026083 must be used by Members/Custodians in the depository system
- CDSL & NSDL early pay-in securities will be earmarked/blocked in the client's account under the requisite market type and settlement number
- Members/Custodians are NOT required to upload any EPN file
- Members should refer to the MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for client-wise stamp duty charges
- Reference to Notice No. 20200528-32 dated May 28, 2020, regarding settlement per Revised Guidelines for Mechanism for Acquisition of Shares through Stock Exchange

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this notice operationalizes existing BSE/ICCL guidelines for settlement of acquisition window (buyback) transactions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Participants must ensure early pay-in of securities is reflected in BSE/ICCL systems before prescribed timelines for bid acceptance/confirmation on the BSE OTB platform
- Early pay-in must be completed in the depository system well in advance of the closure of the OTB bidding window and the custodian confirmation window
- Members/Custodians should direct queries to ICCL Help Desk (022-69158590/45720490/45720690, iccl.helpdesk@icclindia.com)

## Important Dates

- Bid Start Date: 06/07/2026
- Bid End Date: 10/07/2026
- Obligation Download: 16/07/2026
- Settlement Date: 17/07/2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a procedural/operational notice for Members and Custodians facilitating the buyback of Patel Integrated Logistics Limited shares through the Acquisition Window. It does not alter buyback terms but ensures orderly settlement; impact is confined to operational compliance for market intermediaries and shareholders tendering shares.
