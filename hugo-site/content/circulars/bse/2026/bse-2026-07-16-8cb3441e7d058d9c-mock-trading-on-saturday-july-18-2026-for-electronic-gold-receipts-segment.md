## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Electronic Gold Receipts (EGR) segment on Saturday, July 18, 2026. The session allows trading members using third-party or in-house ETI API-based trading systems to test their applications, including under exceptional market conditions such as risk-reduction mode and trading halts. A new BOLT Pro TWS version 13.25 will also be released, with details to follow in a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for EGR segment on July 18, 2026
- Two mock trading sessions will be conducted: Session 1 (morning) and Session 2 (afternoon)
- Trading members can test functionalities including risk-reduction mode and trading halts
- New BOLT Pro TWS version 13.25 to be released (details via separate circular)
- Feedback from members requested by 6:30 pm on the day of the mock session

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is an operational notice for system testing purposes.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are encouraged to actively participate in both mock trading sessions and provide feedback to BSE Tech Support or Help Desk by 6:30 pm on the day of the session.

## Important Dates

- **July 18, 2026**: Mock trading sessions
  - Session 1: Log-in 08:30 am–09:00 am; Pre-Open Order Entry 09:00 am–09:07 am; Matching 09:07 am–09:15 am; Continuous Trading 09:15 am–12:25 pm; Block Deal Window 11:45 am–12:00 pm; Trade Modification 12:25 pm–12:35 pm
  - Session 2: Log-in 02:45 pm–03:00 pm; Pre-Open Order Entry 03:00 pm–03:38 pm; Matching 03:38 pm–03:45 pm; Continuous Trading 03:45 pm–05:30 pm; Block Deal Window 04:00 pm–04:15 pm; Trade Modification 05:30 pm–05:40 pm

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, low-impact operational notice. Trades resulting from the mock session do not attract margin obligations, pay-in/pay-out requirements, or create any rights and liabilities. The session primarily benefits trading members and vendors by allowing them to validate system readiness, including for the upcoming BOLT Pro TWS version 13.25 release.
