## Summary

BSE has released the trading and settlement programme for the Mutual Fund segment covering the period February 1-28, 2026. The circular details settlement numbers, dates, and payout schedules for mutual fund subscriptions (both liquid and non-liquid schemes) and redemptions. Settlement numbers range from 2526207 to 2526226.

## Key Points

- Non-liquid mutual fund subscriptions have T+1 payout cycle (units paid out next business day)
- Liquid mutual fund subscriptions have same-day (T+0) payout
- Funds payout to AMCs for non-liquid transactions occurs before 3:00 PM on T day
- NFO (New Fund Offer) scheme payouts happen on T day based on funds confirmation received by ICCL by 6:00 PM
- Redemption settlements vary by scheme TAT (T+1 to T+10)
- Total of 20 settlement dates scheduled for February 2026
- Settlement number 2526220 (February 19, 2026) appears only in redemption schedule

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine operational schedule.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must adhere to specified settlement dates and timelines
- Funds must be paid in according to the schedule for subscriptions
- Securities must be paid in by specified dates for redemptions
- Investors should verify scheme-specific TAT from scheme master on BSE Star MF Platform for redemption payouts

## Important Dates

- **Programme Period**: February 1-28, 2026
- **First Settlement**: February 2, 2026 (Settlement No. 2526207)
- **Last Settlement**: February 27, 2026 (Settlement No. 2526226)
- **No trading days**: February 7, 8, 14, 15, 21, 22, 28, 2026
- **Funds Payout Deadline**: Before 3:00 PM on T day (non-liquid)
- **NFO Funds Confirmation**: By 6:00 PM on T day

## Impact Assessment

**Operational Impact**: Medium - Provides essential schedule for mutual fund trading operations on BSE Star MF platform. All participants including brokers, AMCs, and investors need to align their operations with these dates.

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine monthly schedule with no changes to existing settlement processes or timelines.

**Participant Action Required**: Market participants should update their systems and calendars with the February 2026 settlement schedule to ensure timely processing of subscription and redemption orders.
