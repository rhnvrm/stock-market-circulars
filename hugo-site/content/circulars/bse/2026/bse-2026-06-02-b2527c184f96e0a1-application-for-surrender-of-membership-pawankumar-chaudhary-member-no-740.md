## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members that Pawankumar Chaudhary (Member No. 740) has applied to surrender their membership of the Exchange, pursuant to BSE Rules, Bye-laws, and Regulations. Any trading member wishing to raise an objection must do so in writing within 15 days of this notice.

## Key Points

- Pawankumar Chaudhary, holding BSE Member No. 740, has applied for surrender of membership.
- The surrender application is being processed in accordance with BSE Rules, Bye-laws, and Regulations.
- Trading members who have objections must submit them in writing to the Membership Department.
- The objection deadline is on or before June 16, 2026 (15 days from notice date).
- For clarifications, contact the Membership Department at membership.ops@bseindia.com.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine administrative notice under existing BSE membership framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members with objections to the surrender of Pawankumar Chaudhary's membership must submit written objections to the BSE Membership Department.
- Objections must be received on or before June 16, 2026.
- Contact: membership.ops@bseindia.com for any clarifications.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 2, 2026
- **Objection Deadline:** June 16, 2026 (15 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal market impact. It is a standard administrative process for the voluntary exit of a single trading member (Member No. 740). No disruption to trading operations or market participants is anticipated. Only entities with a direct interest in objecting to this membership surrender are affected.
