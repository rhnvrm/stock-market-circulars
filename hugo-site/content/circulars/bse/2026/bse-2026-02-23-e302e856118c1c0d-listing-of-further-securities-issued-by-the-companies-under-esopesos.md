## Summary

BSE has notified all market participants that additional equity shares issued by 16 companies under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and Employee Stock Ownership Schemes (ESOS) are listed and admitted for trading on the Exchange effective Tuesday, February 24, 2026. None of the newly listed shares are subject to any lock-in period.

## Key Points

- 16 companies have new ESOP/ESOS shares admitted for trading from February 24, 2026
- Bajaj Finserv Limited has the largest allotment with 27,36,768 shares (INE918I01026, Scrip: 532978)
- Wipro Limited allotted 1,36,919 shares (INE075A01022, Scrip: 507685)
- Motilal Oswal Financial Services allotted 5,15,126 shares (INE338I01027, Scrip: 532892)
- Birlasoft Limited allotted 8,16,103 shares (INE836A01035, Scrip: 532400)
- All newly issued shares have no lock-in period (NA)
- Notice issued under Category: Company Related, Segment: Equity

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice pursuant to existing SEBI regulations governing listing of shares issued under employee benefit schemes.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants are requested to update their records to reflect the new share distribution numbers for each company
- Trading systems should recognize the expanded ISIN ranges for affected companies effective February 24, 2026
- No action required from retail investors or general market participants

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** February 23, 2026
- **Effective Date for Trading:** Tuesday, February 24, 2026
- **Lock-in expiry:** Not applicable (all shares are freely tradable from listing date)
