## Summary

BSE has issued its periodic update to the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework, referencing a series of prior notices dating back to June 2023. Securities meeting the ESM inclusion criteria (detailed in Annexure I) will attract a minimum 100% margin on all open and new positions effective July 09, 2026. Certain securities already under ESM will move to a lower stage (Stage I), while others will move up to Stage II with tighter price bands and periodic call auctions. Some securities will exit the framework entirely effective July 08, 2026 (Annexure II), and a consolidated list of all securities currently under ESM is provided in Annexure III.

## Key Points

- Securities newly qualifying under ESM criteria will attract minimum 100% margin on all open positions (as of July 08, 2026) and new positions from July 09, 2026.
- Securities continuing under ESM may be moved to Stage I (lower stage) effective July 08, 2026.
- Securities moving to Stage II will be placed under Trade-for-Trade with a 2% price band and Periodic Call Auction, effective July 08, 2026.
- All ESM-qualifying securities will shift from Rolling Settlement to Trade-for-Trade segment effective July 09, 2026.
- Securities exiting ESM (Annexure II) will have their price band reinstated to pre-ESM levels, unless subject to another surveillance framework.
- ESM operates in conjunction with other prevailing surveillance measures and is purely a market surveillance mechanism, not an adverse action against the company.
- Specific security names are listed in Annexures I, II, and III, which were not included in the extracted notice text.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced; this notice applies the existing ESM framework (established via prior circulars from June 2023 through July 2025) to an updated list of securities based on periodic review criteria.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must apply minimum 100% margin on all open and new positions in newly included ESM securities from the effective dates.
- Members must ensure affected securities are transacted only in the Trade-for-Trade segment, not Rolling Settlement.
- Members should monitor Stage II securities for the applicable 2% price band and Periodic Call Auction mechanism.
- Queries can be directed to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **July 08, 2026**: Stage movements (to Stage I), Stage II Trade-for-Trade with 2% price band and Periodic Call Auction, and exits from ESM framework take effect.
- **July 09, 2026**: 100% margin requirement and shift to Trade-for-Trade segment take effect for newly qualifying ESM securities.

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a direct and significant impact on the specific securities named in the annexures, subjecting them to substantially higher margin requirements, reduced price bands, and Trade-for-Trade settlement, which typically reduces liquidity and trading activity in those stocks. For the broader market, this is a routine periodic surveillance update rather than a systemic event, consistent with BSE's ongoing ESM monitoring process.
