## Summary

BSE Daily Bulletin No. 232/2025-2026 dated 06 March 2026 provides the scheduled book closure, record dates, payment dates, and purposes for various listed securities. The bulletin primarily covers multiple privately placed Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of Achiievers Finance India Limited with record dates in March and April 2026.

## Key Points

- Bulletin No. 232/2025-2026 issued for 06 March 2026
- Multiple NCD series of Achiievers Finance India Limited have record dates set for 16/03/2026
- One NCD series (Code 959891) has a record date of 01/04/2026 for redemption
- One NCD series (Code 977406) has a record date of 06/04/2026 for interest payment
- Purposes include: Payment of Interest, Redemption of NCD, and combined Redemption & Payment of Interest
- All listed instruments are Privately Placed Non-Convertible Debentures (Priv. placed Non Conv Deb)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine informational bulletin.

## Compliance Requirements

- Holders of the listed NCD series should note their respective record dates to be eligible for interest payments or redemption proceeds
- Depository participants and registrars should ensure updated beneficiary records ahead of each record date
