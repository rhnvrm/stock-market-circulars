## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that pursuant to the Composite Scheme of Arrangement (for Demerger) approved by the Hon'ble National Company Law Tribunal, Allahabad Bench, Triveni Engineering & Industries Ltd (Scrip Code 532356/132356) has fixed 22 July 2026 as the Record Date for determining shareholder entitlements arising from the demerger of its Gears and Defence business segments (PTB Undertaking) into Triveni Power Transmission Limited.

## Key Points

- The Scheme provides for demerger of the PTB Undertaking, comprising the Gears and Defence business segments, of Triveni Engineering & Industries Ltd (Demerged Company) into Triveni Power Transmission Limited (Resulting Company).
- Upon the Scheme becoming effective, Triveni Power Transmission Limited will issue and allot equity shares to shareholders of Triveni Engineering & Industries Ltd in the ratio: for every 3 equity shares of face value Re.1/- each held in Triveni Engineering & Industries Ltd, shareholders will receive 1 equity share of face value Rs.2/- each of Triveni Power Transmission Limited.
- Entitlement is automatic — shareholders receive shares without any application, act, or deed.
- The Scheme was approved by the NCLT, Allahabad Bench, governing the arrangement between Triveni Engineering & Industries Ltd and its respective shareholders and creditors, and Triveni Power Transmission Limited and its respective shareholders and creditors.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this notice implements the NCLT-approved Composite Scheme of Arrangement (Demerger) under existing SEBI framework, including reference to SEBI Circular No. SEBI/Cir/ISD/1/2010 dated 2 November 2010 concerning corporate action processing.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members and depository participants must process the demerger corporate action per the record date, ensuring shareholder entitlements as of the record date are correctly captured for allotment of Triveni Power Transmission Limited shares. No action is required from shareholders to receive entitlements.

## Important Dates

- Record Date: 22 July 2026
- Ex-Entitlement Date: 22 July 2026
- Settlement Number: DR-677/2026-2027

## Impact Assessment

This is a high-impact corporate action for shareholders of Triveni Engineering & Industries Ltd, resulting in a structural change to the company (demerger of Gears and Defence business segments) and creation of new listed entitlements in Triveni Power Transmission Limited. The stock is likely to see price adjustment around the ex-entitlement date to reflect the value transferred to the demerged business. Trading members should ensure accurate entitlement computation and client communication ahead of the record date.
