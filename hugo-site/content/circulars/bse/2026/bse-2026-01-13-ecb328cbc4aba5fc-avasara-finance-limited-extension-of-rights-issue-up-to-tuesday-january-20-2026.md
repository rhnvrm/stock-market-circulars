## Summary

BSE has issued a circular regarding the extension of the Rights Issue for Avasara Finance Limited. The closing date for the Rights Issue has been extended to Tuesday, January 20, 2026, providing additional time for eligible shareholders to subscribe to the issue.

## Key Points

- Rights Issue of Avasara Finance Limited has been extended
- New closing date: Tuesday, January 20, 2026
- Extension provides additional time for shareholders to participate in the rights offering
- Applicable to existing eligible shareholders of Avasara Finance Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an administrative extension of an ongoing rights issue timeline.

## Compliance Requirements

- Shareholders interested in subscribing must do so before the extended closing date of January 20, 2026
- Trading Members and Clearing Members should note the revised timeline
- All procedures and terms of the original rights issue remain applicable with only the timeline modified

## Important Dates

- **Extended Closing Date**: Tuesday, January 20, 2026
- **Circular Date**: January 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The extension provides additional time for shareholders to evaluate and participate in the rights offering, potentially improving subscription rates.

**Investor Impact**: Positive for shareholders who require additional time to arrange funds or complete subscription formalities. The extension indicates flexibility in the capital raising process.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - This is a timeline adjustment that extends the subscription window, affecting mainly the administrative processes of brokers and depositories handling rights entitlements.
