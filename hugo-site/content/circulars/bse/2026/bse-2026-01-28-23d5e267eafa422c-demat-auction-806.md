## Summary

BSE has released the list of securities and quantities for Demat Auction under Settlement No. 806 dated January 28, 2026. The auction includes 176 different scrips across various sectors with quantities ranging from 1 share to 71,475 shares. This is a standard settlement auction to fulfill delivery obligations where physical securities need to be auctioned in demat form.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 806
- Auction Date: January 28, 2026
- Total number of scrips: 176 securities
- Highest quantity: VODAFONE IDEA (71,475 shares)
- Wide range of securities including large-cap, mid-cap, and small-cap stocks
- Includes companies from diverse sectors: banking, steel, telecom, pharma, auto, energy, and others
- Notable large quantities include: PNB (24,000), ADIFRL (24,800), SWIGGY (5,210), TATA MOTORS (11,200), TATA STEEL (5,665)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine operational circular for demat auction settlement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members with outstanding delivery obligations must participate in the auction
- Members must ensure adequate funds/securities for auction settlement
- Auction will be conducted as per BSE's standard demat auction procedures
- Settlement obligations must be fulfilled for the listed scrips and quantities

## Important Dates

- Auction Date: January 28, 2026
- Settlement No.: 806

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - Affects 176 securities with varying quantities. Large quantities in popular stocks like VODAFONE IDEA (71,475), PNB (24,000), ADIFRL (24,800), TATA MOTORS (11,200), and SWIGGY (5,210) may see localized price movements during auction.

**Operational Impact:** Medium - Brokers with open positions in these scrips must ensure auction participation and settlement. Affects delivery obligations across diverse sectors including banking, telecom, automotive, steel, and pharmaceuticals.

**Investor Impact:** Low to Medium - Retail investors generally not directly affected unless they have pending delivery obligations through their brokers. May cause temporary volatility in affected scrips during auction hours.
