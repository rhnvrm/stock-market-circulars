## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the equity shares of Laser Power & Infra Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange, in the 'B' Group of Securities, effective Thursday, July 16, 2026. This follows the company's recent public issue, with allotment completed on July 14, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: Laser Power & Infra Limited, registered office in Kolkata, West Bengal
- Total securities listed: 140,368,342 equity shares of Rs. 5/- each, fully paid up (distinctive number range 1 to 140,368,342)
- Scrip ID / Abbreviated Name on BOLT: LASERPOWER
- Scrip Code: 544822
- ISIN: INE17IR01028
- Market Lot: 1
- Issue Price: Rs. 214/- per share (face value Rs. 5/- + premium Rs. 209/-)
- Date of allotment in public issue: July 14, 2026
- Securities will be traded only in dematerialised form, per SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000
- Registrar to the issue: MUFG Intime India Private Limited (SEBI registration number INR000004058)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is an operational listing notice implementing the outcome of the company's public issue and allotment process under existing SEBI/BSE listing norms.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure trading in LASERPOWER shares occurs only in dematerialised form.
- The company has executed the necessary corporate action to mark the lock-in period in depository records, as detailed in Annexure I.
- Shareholding pattern is disclosed in Annexure II.
- Company contact for clarifications: Debendra Banthiya, Company Secretary and Compliance Officer.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: July 14, 2026
- Listing and commencement of dealings: Thursday, July 16, 2026
- Special Pre-open Session (SPOS) for the scrip: Thursday, July 16, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard new listing notification enabling trading in Laser Power & Infra Limited shares. Impact is primarily relevant to investors who participated in the IPO and trading members preparing for the scrip's market debut, including participation in the Special Pre-open Session mechanism designed for price discovery in newly listed/relisted scrips. No broader market-wide impact is expected.
