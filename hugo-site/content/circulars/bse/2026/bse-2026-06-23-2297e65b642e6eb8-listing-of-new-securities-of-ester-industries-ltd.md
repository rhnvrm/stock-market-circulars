## Summary

BSE has listed 67,08,851 new equity shares of Ester Industries Ltd. (Scrip Code: 500136, ISIN: INE778B01029), issued on a preferential basis to Promoters and Non-Promoters pursuant to conversion of warrants. The shares are tradable on the Exchange with effect from Wednesday, June 24, 2026, and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- **Quantity:** 67,08,851 equity shares of Rs. 5/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 153/- (Issue Price: Rs. 158/-)
- **Basis of Issue:** Preferential allotment to Promoters and Non-Promoters via conversion of warrants
- **Date of Allotment:** May 12, 2026
- **Distribution Numbers:** 107808842 to 114517692
- **Trading effective from:** June 24, 2026
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification under BSE's Listing Operations procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of the Exchange are informed that these new securities are now eligible for trading. Lock-in restrictions must be observed for the applicable share tranches as per SEBI preferential allotment regulations.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** May 12, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** June 24, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (37,97,468 shares — Dist. Nos. 107808842–111606309):** January 17, 2028
- **Lock-in Expiry (29,11,383 shares — Dist. Nos. 111606310–114517692):** January 17, 2027

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds approximately 67.09 lakh new equity shares to the tradable float of Ester Industries Ltd. (Scrip Code: 500136). However, the immediate free-float impact is limited as all new shares carry lock-in periods (until January 2027 and January 2028), meaning they cannot be traded during the lock-in window. Post lock-in expiry, additional supply of shares will enter the market, which could have a dilutive effect on existing shareholders. The preferential allotment at Rs. 158/- per share reflects the company's fundraising through warrant conversion.
