## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the commercial paper issued by Sharekhan Limited (Scrip Code: 731178, ISIN: INE211H14AL0, particulars: SL-26-5-26-CP) will mature on its redemption date of May 25, 2026. Trading members are directed to cease dealing in this instrument effective from that date.

## Key Points

- Sharekhan Limited commercial paper (ISIN: INE211H14AL0) reaches its redemption/maturity date on May 25, 2026
- Scrip Code: 731178; Particulars: SL-26-5-26-CP
- All trading members must stop dealing in this commercial paper from May 25, 2026
- Reference: DR-637/2026-2027

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice triggered by the natural maturity of a debt instrument.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE must not execute any trades in the Sharekhan Limited commercial paper (INE211H14AL0) on or after May 25, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** May 22, 2026
- **Redemption / Maturity Date:** May 25, 2026 (trading suspension effective from this date)

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited to participants holding or trading the specific Sharekhan Limited commercial paper. This is a routine end-of-life notice for a single debt instrument with no broader market implications. Holders should ensure redemption proceeds are processed as per normal debt settlement procedures.
