## Summary

Cupid Limited (Scrip Code: 530843, ISIN: INE509F01029) has announced a bonus issue of equity shares. The bonus shares carry distinctive numbers 26,89,32,141 to 34,46,60,700, with a total of 10,757,28,560 shares involved. The shares are set to be listed on BSE effective 11th March 2026.

## Key Points

- Bonus equity shares issued by Cupid Limited (Scrip Code: 530843)
- Distinctive numbers: 26,89,32,141 to 34,46,60,700
- Total shares: 1,07,57,28,560
- ISIN: INE509F01029
- Deemed date of allotment: 10th March 2026
- Effective listing date: 11th March 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard bonus issue notification in accordance with BSE listing obligations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Company Secretary and Compliance Officer (Saurabh Karmase, FCS 11349) has confirmed the allotment details.
- Cupid Limited must ensure the bonus shares are duly credited to eligible shareholders' demat accounts prior to the listing date.

## Important Dates

- **Deemed Date of Allotment:** 10th March 2026
- **Effective Listing Date:** 11th March 2026

## Impact Assessment

The bonus issue will expand Cupid Limited's equity share capital, which may affect the share price proportionally on the ex-bonus date. Existing shareholders will receive additional shares at no cost. Market liquidity for the scrip may improve post-listing of bonus shares. No broader market or systemic impact is anticipated.
