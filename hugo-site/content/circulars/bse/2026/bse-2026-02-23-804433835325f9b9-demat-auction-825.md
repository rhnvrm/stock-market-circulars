## Summary

BSE has published the Demat Auction list for Settlement No. 825 dated 23rd February 2026. This circular lists securities where short deliveries have occurred and the quantities to be auctioned to fulfill settlement obligations. Over 130 scrips across various market segments are included in this auction.

## Key Points

- Settlement No. 825 demat auction held on 23/02/2026
- Over 130 scrips listed with shortfall quantities requiring auction settlement
- Quantities range from as low as 1 share (e.g., ABBOTT INDIA, AXIS BANK) to large lots such as NOVARTIS INDIA (4031), Vistar Amar (4002), MRC AGROTECH (3000), BGIL FILMS (3602)
- Both large-cap (RELIANCE, HDFC BANK, INFOSYS, SBI, L&T) and small/mid-cap stocks are included
- Covers equity shares across BSE-listed companies

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a routine operational settlement notice under existing BSE settlement and auction framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Clearing members and brokers with short delivery positions in the listed scrips must participate in or be subject to the demat auction process
- Members should reconcile their positions against the listed quantities
- Auction proceeds and close-out prices will be applied as per BSE settlement rules

## Important Dates

- Auction Date: 23/02/2026
- Settlement No.: 825

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine demat auction settlement notice. Impact is primarily on brokers and clearing members who have short delivery positions in the listed securities. End investors in the affected scrips may experience delayed or auction-based settlement for the specified quantities. Notable large-quantity auctions include NOVARTIS INDIA (4031 shares), Vistar Amar (4002 shares), BGIL FILMS (3602 shares), and MRC AGROTECH (3000 shares), which may see localized price impact.
