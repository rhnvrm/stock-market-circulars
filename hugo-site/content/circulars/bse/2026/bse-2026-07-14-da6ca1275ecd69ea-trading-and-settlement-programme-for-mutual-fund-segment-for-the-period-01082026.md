## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260714-2, dated 14 July 2026, issued by BSECL under the Settlement/RMS category, announces the Trading and Settlement Programme for the Mutual Fund segment for the period 01.08.2026 to 31.08.2026. The notice specifies settlement numbers, transaction/settlement dates, funds pay-in dates, and pay-out/securities pay-in dates for Mutual Fund subscription (excluding liquid), liquid fund subscription, and mutual fund redemption transactions.

## Key Points

- Settlement numbers 2627084 through 2627104 cover the trading days from 03-08-2026 to 31-08-2026.
- For Non-Liquid MF subscriptions, funds pay-in occurs on the settlement date (T day) and units pay-out generally occurs on T+1 day.
- For Liquid MF subscriptions, both funds pay-in and units pay-out occur on the same settlement date (T day).
- For MF Redemptions, securities pay-in occurs on the transaction date (T day).
- Funds pay-out to AMCs for non-liquid transactions is processed before 3:00 PM on T day.
- Funds pay-out to AMCs for NFO (New Fund Offer) scheme transactions is based on funds confirmation received by BSECL by 6:00 PM on T day.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced; this is a routine periodic publication of the settlement calendar for the Mutual Fund segment as per existing BSE StAR MF operational procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

Asset Management Companies (AMCs), Registrar and Transfer Agents (RTAs), and trading members dealing in the BSE Mutual Fund segment must adhere to the specified pay-in and pay-out timelines for subscription and redemption transactions to ensure timely settlement.

## Important Dates

- Settlement period covered: 01.08.2026 to 31.08.2026
- Settlement cycle begins with settlement no. 2627084 on 03-08-2026 and ends with settlement no. 2627104 on 31-08-2026
- Non-liquid MF funds pay-out to AMCs: before 3:00 PM on T day
- NFO scheme funds pay-out to AMCs: based on confirmation received by 6:00 PM on T day

## Impact Assessment

This circular is a routine operational announcement affecting back-office settlement processing for AMCs, RTAs, and BSE StAR MF platform participants. It does not affect listed equity trading, stock prices, or investor-facing market activity. Its impact is confined to operational and treasury planning for mutual fund transaction processing in August 2026.
