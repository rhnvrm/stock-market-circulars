## Summary

BSE has notified the listing and commencement of trading of 62,27,14,719 new equity shares of Gujarat Energy Limited (formerly Gujarat Gas Limited, Scrip Code: 539336) effective June 18, 2026. These shares were issued pursuant to a Scheme of Amalgamation and Arrangement involving Gujarat State Petroleum Corporation Limited (GSPC), Gujarat State Petronet Limited (GSPL), GSPC Energy Limited (GEL), Gujarat Gas Limited (GGL), and GSPL Transmission Limited (GTL).

## Key Points

- 62,27,14,719 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each are listed and permitted for trading from June 18, 2026
- Shares issued under a Scheme of Amalgamation and Arrangement involving GSPC, GSPL, GEL, GGL (Transferee/Demerged Company), and GTL (Resulting Company)
- ISIN: INE844O01030; Distribution Numbers: 688390126 – 1311104844
- Date of Allotment: May 16, 2026
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of Gujarat Energy Limited
- 37,28,73,995 equity shares held by GSPL in Gujarat Energy Limited have been cancelled due to cross-holding elimination

## Regulatory Changes

The company has undergone a name change from Gujarat Gas Limited to Gujarat Energy Limited as part of the scheme. The Scrip Code (539336) remains unchanged. The new ISIN INE844O01030 applies to the newly issued shares under this scheme.

## Compliance Requirements

All market participants are informed to update their records to reflect the new share capital and the company's changed name. The cancellation of 37,28,73,995 cross-held shares by GSPL must be factored into post-scheme shareholding calculations.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** May 16, 2026
- **Listing / Trading Effective Date:** June 18, 2026 (Thursday)
- **Circular Date:** June 17, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a high-impact event for Gujarat Energy Limited (formerly Gujarat Gas Limited) and its shareholders. The scheme consolidates major Gujarat government-owned energy entities — GSPC, GSPL, and GEL — into the restructured Gujarat Energy Limited, significantly altering the company's share capital and ownership structure. Over 62 crore new shares enter the market on June 18, 2026, which may affect price discovery and liquidity. The simultaneous cancellation of ~37 crore cross-held shares partially offsets the dilution effect. Investors should reassess their positions in GUJGASLTD in light of the new corporate structure.
