## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Exclusive Securities Ltd (Member No. 6369) will voluntarily cease to carry on business in the Cash and Equity Derivative segments with effect from Monday, July 20, 2026, due to voluntary closure of business.

## Key Points

- Exclusive Securities Ltd, Member No. 6369, is voluntarily closing its business.
- The member will cease operations in the Cash segment and Equity Derivative segment.
- Effective date of cessation is July 20, 2026.
- No attachments were provided with this notice.
- Queries can be directed to the Membership Department at membership.ops@bseindia.com.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is an administrative notice regarding a single member's voluntary exit from business, not a regulatory or policy change.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members and market participants dealing with Exclusive Securities Ltd should note the cessation of its trading activities and make necessary arrangements for any pending transactions or client accounts prior to the effective date. Any clarifications should be directed to the Membership Department via email.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 17, 2026
- Effective Date of Cessation: Monday, July 20, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to Exclusive Securities Ltd's clients and counterparties, who will need to transition their accounts or open positions before the cessation date. There is no broader market-wide impact, as this pertains to the voluntary closure of a single trading member's business.
