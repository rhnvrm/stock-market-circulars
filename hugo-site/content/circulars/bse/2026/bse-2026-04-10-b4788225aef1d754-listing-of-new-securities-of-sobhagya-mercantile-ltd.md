## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 3,00,000 new equity shares of Sobhagya Mercantile Ltd. (Scrip Code: 512014, ISIN: INE754D01018) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Monday, April 13, 2026. The shares were issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 3,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- face value each issued at a premium of Rs. 664.49/- (issue price Rs. 674.49/-)
- Issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis via conversion of warrants
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 8400001 to 8700000
- Date of allotment: March 11, 2026
- Trading permitted from April 13, 2026
- Full lot of 3,00,000 shares subject to lock-in until October 31, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notice under BSE's listing operations framework for new securities issued through preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to note the new securities and their trading eligibility from April 13, 2026
- The locked-in shares (dist. nos. 8400001 to 8700000) are not available for trading until October 31, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** March 11, 2026
- **Trading Commencement:** April 13, 2026 (Monday)
- **Lock-in Expiry:** October 31, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine preferential allotment listing for Sobhagya Mercantile Ltd., a small-cap listed company. The issuance of 3,00,000 shares at Rs. 674.49/- per share represents a modest capital raise. The entire allotted tranche is under lock-in until October 31, 2026, limiting near-term dilution impact on free-float. Broader market impact is negligible.
