## Summary

BSE has announced that the Draft Offer Document of Kumar Cotton Mills Ltd, filed on June 30, 2026, containing disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations, has been made available on the Exchange's website for a minimum period of 21 days to seek public comments.

## Key Points

- The Draft Offer Document pertains to the SME IPO of Kumar Cotton Mills Ltd.
- The document was filed with BSE on June 30, 2026.
- The document is hosted on the SME website: https://www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public comments are invited within 21 days from the date of the notice.
- Comments can be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice signed by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a procedural disclosure under Schedule VI of the SEBI ICDR Regulations for SME IPO draft offer documents.

## Compliance Requirements

Interested parties and the public are requested to submit comments on the Draft Offer Document within 21 days from the notice date to equity.ipo@bseindia.com. The company must address any comments received as part of the IPO listing process.

## Important Dates

- Draft Offer Document filed: June 30, 2026
- Notice date: July 1, 2026
- Public comment period: 21 days from notice date (until approximately July 22, 2026)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, low-impact regulatory disclosure related to the SME IPO process for Kumar Cotton Mills Ltd. It does not affect existing listed securities or broader market trading, but is relevant to prospective investors evaluating the upcoming SME IPO.
