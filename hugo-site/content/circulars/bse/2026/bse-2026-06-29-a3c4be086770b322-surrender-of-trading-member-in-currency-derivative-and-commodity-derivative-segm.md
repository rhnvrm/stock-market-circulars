## Summary

BSE has approved the surrender of trading membership of KRCHOKSEY SHARES & SECURITIES PVT. LTD. (Member No. 385) in the Currency Derivative and Commodity Derivative segments, effective June 29, 2026.

## Key Points

- Trading member KRCHOKSEY SHARES & SECURITIES PVT. LTD. (Member No. 385) has surrendered its membership in the Currency Derivative and Commodity Derivative segments.
- The surrender has been approved by BSE effective June 29, 2026.
- The notice is issued by the Membership Department under the General segment.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative notice confirming approval of a membership surrender by an individual trading member.

## Compliance Requirements

No direct compliance requirements for other trading members. Members with queries are advised to contact the Membership Department at membership.ops@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** June 29, 2026 — surrender of trading membership approved.

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This is a routine membership administrative matter affecting only KRCHOKSEY SHARES & SECURITIES PVT. LTD. (Member No. 385). Other trading members in the Currency Derivative and Commodity Derivative segments are not affected.
