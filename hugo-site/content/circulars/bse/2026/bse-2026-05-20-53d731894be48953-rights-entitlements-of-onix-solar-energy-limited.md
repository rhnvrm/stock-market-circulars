## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that dealings in Rights Entitlements (REs) for renunciation of ONIX SOLAR ENERGY LIMITED will commence on the Exchange from Monday, May 25, 2026. The REs are tradable in dematerialized form only and will be settled on a T+1 Rolling Settlement basis on a trade-for-trade basis.

## Key Points

- Rights Entitlements (REs) for ONIX SOLAR ENERGY LIMITED will be available for trading/renunciation from May 25 to May 26, 2026
- Scrip Code: 751117; Scrip ID: ONIX-RE1; ISIN: INE173M20020
- Market Lot: 1; Group: R
- Settlement is on T+1 Rolling basis, trade-for-trade
- REs are tradable in dematerialized form only
- REs that are neither subscribed nor renounced by the Issue Closing Date will lapse and be extinguished

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice operationalizes existing Rights Entitlement trading framework as established under BSE notice no. 20200305-21 (March 5, 2020) and notice no. 20200518-34 (May 18, 2020).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must inform all clients about commencement of trading in REs, mentioning the ISIN (INE173M20020)
- Trading members must clarify that REs shall not be mistaken for ordinary shares of ONIX SOLAR ENERGY LIMITED
- Contract notes/bills for RE purchases must prominently include a disclaimer stating that purchase of REs only gives the right to participate in the Rights Issue or renounce the REs before issue closure
- Clients must be informed that unsubscribed and unrenounced REs will lapse after the Issue Closing Date

## Important Dates

- **May 25, 2026**: Commencement of RE trading/renunciation period; Rights Issue opens
- **May 26, 2026**: Last day for RE renunciation trading
- **June 1, 2026**: Rights Issue closes; unrenounced and unsubscribed REs lapse after this date

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice with limited market-wide impact. It primarily affects holders of ONIX SOLAR ENERGY LIMITED rights entitlements and trading members dealing in this scrip. The short two-day renunciation window (May 25-26) means rights holders must act promptly to either subscribe or renounce their entitlements. Trading members have specific disclosure obligations to clients purchasing REs to ensure informed participation in the Rights Issue.
