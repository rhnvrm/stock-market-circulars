## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 3,42,72,605 new equity shares of Galactico Corporate Services Limited (Scrip Code: 542802) issued on a Rights Basis at Re.1/- each (at a premium of Rs.0.75/-) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Friday, February 27, 2026. The newly issued shares carry ISIN INE906Y01028 and rank pari-passu with the existing equity shares of the company.

## Key Points

- **Company:** Galactico Corporate Services Limited (Scrip Code: 542802)
- **Number of Shares:** 3,42,72,605 equity shares
- **Face Value:** Re.1/- per share
- **Issue Premium:** Rs.0.75/- per share (total issue price: Rs.1.75/-)
- **Issue Type:** Rights Basis
- **ISIN:** INE906Y01028
- **Distribution Numbers:** 149011331 to 183283935
- **Date of Allotment:** February 24, 2026
- **Trading Effective From:** Friday, February 27, 2026
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification pursuant to a completed rights issue.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed that these new securities are now available for trading on the Exchange from February 27, 2026. Members should update their systems to reflect the new share count and ISIN for Galactico Corporate Services Limited.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** February 24, 2026
- **Listing / Trading Effective Date:** February 27, 2026 (Friday)

## Impact Assessment

This listing increases the total share count of Galactico Corporate Services Limited by 3,42,72,605 shares. The rights issue was priced at Re.1/- face value plus Rs.0.75/- premium (Rs.1.75/- total). The impact is limited to existing shareholders and traders of this scrip; there is no broader market or regulatory impact. The addition of new shares may affect near-term liquidity and share price dynamics for this specific counter.
