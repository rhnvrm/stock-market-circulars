## Summary

BSE has updated the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework under Notice No. 20260526-22. New securities meeting ESM criteria will attract a minimum 100% margin requirement effective May 29, 2026, and will be shifted from Rolling Settlement to Trade-for-Trade segment. Some securities are being moved to lower Stage I of the ESM framework, while others are exiting the framework entirely.

## Key Points

- Securities newly included under ESM (Annexure I) shall attract minimum 100% margin on all open positions as on May 27, 2026 and new positions from May 29, 2026.
- Securities moving to lower Stage I under ESM framework are effective May 27, 2026.
- Newly qualifying ESM securities will shift from Rolling Settlement to Trade-for-Trade segment effective May 29, 2026.
- Securities shifting to Stage II will be under Trade for Trade with a 2% price band and Periodic Call Auction effective May 27, 2026.
- Securities exiting the ESM framework (Annexure II) are effective May 27, 2026.
- A consolidated list of all securities under ESM framework is provided in Annexure III.

## Regulatory Changes

This notice references and continues the ESM framework established under prior notices: 20230602-44 (June 2, 2023), 20230718-46 (July 18, 2023), 20240809-42 (August 9, 2024), 20240920-63 (September 20, 2024), 20241004-65 (October 4, 2024), and 20250725-61 (July 25, 2025). The current update applies stage transitions and margin changes to specific securities as per the criteria defined in those earlier notices.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Members/Brokers**: Must ensure minimum 100% margin collection on ESM-listed securities for all open and new positions from the specified effective dates.
- **Members**: Must note the shift of qualifying securities from Rolling Settlement to Trade-for-Trade settlement.
- **Market Participants**: ESM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the Exchanges.
- For clarifications, members may write to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **May 27, 2026**: Stage I downgrade effective; securities exiting ESM framework effective; Stage II securities move to Trade-for-Trade with 2% price band and Periodic Call Auction.
- **May 29, 2026**: Minimum 100% margin requirement effective on open positions (as on May 27, 2026) and new positions; shift from Rolling Settlement to Trade-for-Trade segment effective.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine periodic update to the ESM framework affecting multiple equity securities listed on BSE. The 100% margin requirement significantly increases the cost of holding or taking new positions in affected securities, reducing speculative activity. The shift to Trade-for-Trade eliminates netting benefits and increases settlement risk for traders. Securities exiting the framework will have their prior price bands reinstated, unless subject to another surveillance measure. The shortlisting is purely for market surveillance purposes and is not to be construed as adverse action against the concerned companies.
