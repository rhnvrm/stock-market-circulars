## Summary

Motilal Oswal Asset Management Company Limited has announced a one-day extension for the New Fund Offer (NFO) period of the Motilal Oswal Nifty Diversified Equity Flexicap Passive FOF scheme. The closing date has been extended from January 15, 2026 to January 16, 2026, with the re-opening date consequently shifted to January 29, 2026. All other terms and conditions of the scheme remain unchanged.

## Key Points

- NFO closing date extended from January 15, 2026 to January 16, 2026
- Re-opening date revised to January 29, 2026
- Scheme: Motilal Oswal Nifty Diversified Equity Flexicap Passive FOF
- All other terms and conditions remain unchanged
- Notice cum addendum forms integral part of Scheme Information Document and Key Information Memorandum

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative extension of the NFO period for the mutual fund scheme.

## Compliance Requirements

- Investors should take note of the revised NFO closing date
- The notice cum addendum forms an integral part of the Scheme Information Document and Key Information Memorandum
- Standard mutual fund disclaimer applies: investments are subject to market risks, read all scheme related documents carefully

## Important Dates

- **Original NFO Closing Date**: January 15, 2026
- **Extended NFO Closing Date**: January 16, 2026
- **Re-opening Date**: January 29, 2026
- **Circular Date**: January 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a minor administrative extension with minimal market impact. The one-day extension of the NFO period provides potential investors an additional day to subscribe to the Motilal Oswal Nifty Diversified Equity Flexicap Passive FOF scheme on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The extension is likely operational in nature and does not indicate any material changes to the fund structure or investment strategy. Impact is limited to prospective investors in this specific mutual fund scheme.
