## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the equity shares of Turtlemint Fintech Solutions Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in the 'B' Group of Securities effective Monday, June 29, 2026. The company completed its IPO with allotment on June 24, 2026 at an issue price of Rs. 152 per share (face value Rs. 1, premium Rs. 151).

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 544799 | Scrip ID: TURTLEMINT | ISIN: INE0OC301013
- Total securities: 294,478,906 equity shares of Rs. 1/- each, fully paid up
- Distinctive number range: 1 to 59200 and 60245 to 294,479,950
- Listed in 'B' Group of Securities; market lot is 1
- Trading only permitted in dematerialised form as per SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000
- Scrip will be part of the Special Pre-open Session (SPOS) on June 29, 2026
- Registrar to the issue: KFin Technologies Limited, Mumbai

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular follows standard SEBI guidelines for IPO listings, including mandatory dematerialised trading and Special Pre-open Session participation for newly listed scrips.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all trades in TURTLEMINT are executed in dematerialised form only
- Trading members should refer to Exchange notice no. 20120216-29 dated February 16, 2012 for SPOS procedures
- Lock-in details are specified in Annexure I; shareholding pattern in Annexure II
- Corporate action for lock-in period has been executed in depositories records for demat shares

## Important Dates

- **June 24, 2026**: Date of allotment in public issue
- **June 25, 2026**: Notice date
- **June 29, 2026 (Monday)**: Effective date of listing and commencement of trading; also SPOS date

## Impact Assessment

The listing of Turtlemint Fintech Solutions Limited introduces a new fintech equity scrip to BSE's 'B' Group. With ~294 million shares at an issue price of Rs. 152, the implied market capitalisation at issue price is approximately Rs. 44,761 crore. Trading members and investors holding allotted shares should note the SPOS participation on listing day and ensure holdings are in demat form. The listing has no systemic impact on broader market operations.
