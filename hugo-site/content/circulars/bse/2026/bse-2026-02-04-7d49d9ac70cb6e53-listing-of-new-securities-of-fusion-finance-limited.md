## Summary

BSE has permitted trading in 14,765 new equity shares of Fusion Finance Limited (Scrip Code: 543652) resulting from the conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up shares of Rs. 10/- each issued on rights basis. Trading will commence from February 05, 2026. These shares carry the same rights as existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 14,765 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each face value
- Shares resulted from conversion of partly paid to fully paid up
- Original issuance was on rights basis
- ISIN: INE139R01012
- Distinctive Numbers: 161351031 to 161365795
- Shares are pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Company: Fusion Finance Limited
- Scrip Code: 543652

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are authorized to trade in these newly listed securities following standard trading protocols.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: February 04, 2026
- **Trading Commencement Date**: February 05, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine administrative listing notification for a small quantity of shares (14,765 shares) resulting from conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid. The shares have the same rights as existing equity shares and represent a corporate action completion rather than a new capital raise.
