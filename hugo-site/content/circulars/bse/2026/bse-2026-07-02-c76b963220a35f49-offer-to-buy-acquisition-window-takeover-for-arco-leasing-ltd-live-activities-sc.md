## Summary

BSE has notified Trading Members and Custodian Members of the schedule of activities for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) of shares of ARCO LEASING LTD. The offer window is live from Friday, 3rd July 2026 to Thursday, 16th July 2026.

## Key Points

- Security Name: ARCO LEASING LTD; Security Symbol: ZARCOLEA; ISIN: INE955S01019; Scrip Code: 511038
- Offer Size: Up to 27,74,970 equity shares; Market Lot: 1; Face Value: Rs. 10.00 per share
- Offer Price: Rs. 10.00 per share
- Bid Offering Period: 3rd July 2026 to 16th July 2026, 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.
- Give-up/Take-up Period: 3rd July 2026 to 15th July 2026, 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.
- On the last day of the offer (16th July 2026): Give-up from 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.; Take-up from 9:15 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.
- Bidding categories include FII, FI, MF, Insurance Companies, Other QIBs, Other NIB, Company, and Individual
- Access via iBBS platform (Internet: ibbs.bseindia.com; Leased Line: ibbsll.bseindia.com), RTRMS, and Extranet for Files
- Files on Extranet for acceptance of bids will be available only after confirmation of acceptance is received from the Merchant Banker/RTA post issue closure
- Trading Members must ensure UCC of investors is correctly registered/updated with PAN and Category in the Exchange UCC database
- Bids permitted only for investors holding shares in demat form
- If no securities are accepted, no End of Issue files will be generated in Extranet

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this circular operationalizes existing OTB (Offer to Buy) procedures per Exchange Notices No. 20150930-1 dated September 30, 2015 and No. 20170203-29 dated February 3, 2017.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members and Custodian Members must ensure UCC registration accuracy (PAN and Category) for all participating investors
- Bids can only be placed for investors holding shares in demat form
- Members/Custodians should monitor Extranet for End of Issue files, noting these will not be generated if no securities are accepted
- Use designated iBBS, RTRMS, and Extranet URLs (Internet or Leased Line as applicable) for participation

## Important Dates

- Offer Opening Date: Friday, 3rd July 2026
- Offer Closing Date: Thursday, 16th July 2026
- Give-up/Take-up Period: 3rd July 2026 to 15th July 2026
- Final Give-up: 16th July 2026 (9:15 a.m. – 3:30 p.m.)
- Final Take-up: 16th July 2026 (9:15 a.m. – 4:00 p.m.)

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to Trading Members, Custodian Members, and investors participating in the takeover offer for ARCO LEASING LTD (ZARCOLEA). It requires operational readiness for bid submission and give-up/take-up processes within the specified window but does not have broader market-wide implications.
