## Summary

BSE has issued a notice in continuation of multiple prior notices (dated 20 Jun 2025, 13 May 2025, 27 Mar 2025, 08 Nov 2024, and 17 Sep 2021) regarding debt securities of IndiGrid Infrastructure Trust. Trading Members are informed that six specified scrip codes/ISINs will be available for trading with revised terms as per Annexure I, effective Thursday, July 09, 2026, on account of debt restructuring.

## Key Points

- Update pertains to debt securities of IndiGrid Infrastructure Trust following debt restructuring.
- Six Scrip Codes with corresponding ISINs will trade with revised terms from July 9, 2026:
  - Scrip Code 976811 — ISIN INE219X07538
  - Scrip Code 976810 — ISIN INE219X07520
  - Scrip Code 976689 — ISIN INE219X07512
  - Scrip Code 976556 — ISIN INE219X07488
  - Scrip Code 958219 — ISIN INE219X07462
  - Scrip Code 973450 — ISIN INE219X07306
- Revised terms are detailed in Annexure I (attachment to the notice).
- This notice continues a series of prior notices tracking developments on these debt securities.

## Regulatory Changes

Revised terms for the listed debt securities are being implemented as per Annexure I due to debt restructuring undertaken by IndiGrid Infrastructure Trust. Specific details of the revised terms (such as coupon, maturity, or other structural changes) are contained in the Annexure, not reproduced in the notice text.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members are required to note the revised terms applicable to the specified ISINs and update their systems/records accordingly ahead of the effective date. Queries can be directed to BSE's Debt Listing officials.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 08 Jul 2026
- Effective Date for revised trading terms: Thursday, 09 Jul 2026
- Prior related notices: 20 Jun 2025, 13 May 2025, 27 Mar 2025, 08 Nov 2024, 17 Sep 2021

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and trading members dealing in the six specified IndiGrid Infrastructure Trust debt ISINs, whose trading terms will change due to debt restructuring. Market-wide impact is minimal as this concerns a specific set of debt instruments, but affected market participants should review Annexure I for the revised terms to ensure accurate trading and settlement.
