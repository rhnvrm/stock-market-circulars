## Summary

BSE is introducing a Business Unified Portal to aggregate all day-to-day operational activities required by Trading Members for enabling trading platforms and terminals into a single, unified platform. The portal goes live on Monday, March 16, 2026, and is accessible via the Member SSO URL.

## Key Points

- New portal accessible via Member SSO at https://membersso.bseindia.com/login under the 'Unified Portal' option
- Consolidates activities currently spread across BEFS, UCC, and OMRS portals
- Existing portals will continue to function during a transition period
- A separate circular will announce the discontinuation date for existing portals
- Activities are segregated under Exchange terminal and non-Exchange terminal categories

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory requirements are introduced. This is an operational/infrastructure change consolidating existing facilities into a single portal. BSE will issue a separate circular to announce when the existing portals (BEFS, UCC, OMRS) will be discontinued.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members must:
- Familiarise themselves with the new Unified Portal accessible via Member SSO
- Use existing login credentials to access the portal
- Continue using existing portals during the transition period until further notice
- For ETI/IBT/STWT/Algo Approval queries: contact Trading.app@bseindia.com or 022-22728640 / 022-22725116
- For Trader ID Creation, Pro Trading Requests, Throttle Limit Management, Location ID Registration, DMA Activation/Deactivation: contact User.trdops@bseindia.com or 022-22728005 / 022-22728215

## Important Dates

- **March 16, 2026**: Business Unified Portal goes live
- Transition period: Existing portals (BEFS, UCC, OMRS) remain active until a separate discontinuation circular is issued

## Impact Assessment

This change affects all BSE Trading Members who use operational portals for terminal approvals and management. The unified portal improves usability and operational efficiency by consolidating the following activities:

- Apply for ETI / IBT / STWT / DMA / SOR / Algo approval
- Trader ID creation requests and confirmation
- Dealer Registration (Location ID)
- Throttle Limit Management (Equity Derivatives)
- Proprietary (PRO) Trading Activation requests and confirmation
- DMA Activation / De-Activation

Impact is moderate — operational improvement with no immediate disruption as legacy portals remain available during transition. Members should plan to migrate workflows to the new portal ahead of the eventual shutdown of legacy systems.
