## Summary

BSE's Surveillance Department (DOSS) issued this notice to inform members and investors that it had sought clarifications from three listed companies regarding news articles appearing in various media outlets. Replies have been received from Pine Labs Ltd and IIFL Capital Services Ltd, while a reply is still awaited from Amara Raja Energy & Mobility Ltd.

## Key Points

- BSE sought clarifications to verify accuracy of news articles appearing in newspapers, websites, and television channels.
- Pine Labs Ltd (Scrip Code: 544606) replied regarding an article from The Hindu Business Line.
- IIFL Capital Services Ltd (Scrip Code: 542773) replied regarding an article from NDTV Profit.
- Amara Raja Energy & Mobility Ltd (Scrip Code: 500008) has not yet responded to a query related to a Moneycontrol article.
- The notice was issued by the Surveillance Department, signed by Deputy Vice President Sanjay Jain and Manager Shreya Ghose.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Amara Raja Energy & Mobility Ltd (Scrip Code: 500008) is expected to submit a clarification to BSE regarding the news article published on Moneycontrol.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 22 April 2026
- No specific deadline mentioned for the pending reply from Amara Raja Energy & Mobility Ltd.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine surveillance notice with minimal market impact. Clarifications from Pine Labs and IIFL Capital have already been received and attached. The pending response from Amara Raja Energy & Mobility Ltd may be of interest to investors in that company pending its submission.
