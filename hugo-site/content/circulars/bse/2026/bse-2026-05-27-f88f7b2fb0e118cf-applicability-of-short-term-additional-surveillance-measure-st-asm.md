## Summary

BSE has identified securities satisfying criteria for inclusion under Short Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) at Stage I and Stage II levels. Enhanced margin requirements will apply from June 01, 2026, covering both open positions as on May 29, 2026 and all new positions created from June 01, 2026 onwards. The action is purely a market surveillance measure and should not be construed as adverse action against any company.

## Key Points

- Securities in Annexure I placed under ST-ASM Stage I with 50% margin (or existing margin, whichever is higher), capped at 100%
- Securities in Annexure I placed under ST-ASM Stage II with 100% margin (or existing margin, whichever is higher), capped at 100%
- Effective date: June 01, 2026 for open positions as on May 29, 2026 and new positions from June 01, 2026
- ST-ASM operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures
- Shortlisting is purely for market surveillance purposes — not an adverse action against the company
- Price band of scrips exiting the framework will revert to pre-ASM applicable price band (unless under another surveillance measure)

## Regulatory Changes

This notice is issued further to the ASM framework revisions under BSE notices:
- 20240920-71 (September 20, 2024)
- 20181027-1 (October 27, 2018)
- 20190719-33 (July 19, 2019)
- 20201204-56 (December 4, 2020)
- 20230925-49 (September 25, 2023)
- 20240920-63 (September 20, 2024)

No new regulatory framework is introduced; this is an application of the existing ST-ASM framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must note and apply the enhanced margin requirements for securities listed in Annexure I from June 01, 2026
- ST-ASM Stage I: Collect minimum 50% margin (or existing margin, whichever is higher), subject to a maximum cap of 100%
- ST-ASM Stage II: Collect 100% margin (or existing margin, whichever is higher), subject to a maximum cap of 100%
- Apply margin requirements to both open positions as on May 29, 2026 and new positions from June 01, 2026
- For clarifications, members may contact bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **May 27, 2026**: Notice date; circular issued
- **May 29, 2026**: Reference date for open positions subject to enhanced margins
- **June 01, 2026**: Effective date for enhanced margin requirements under ST-ASM Stage I and Stage II

## Impact Assessment

Trading members and investors holding or planning positions in the securities listed in Annexure I will face significantly higher margin obligations from June 01, 2026. Stage I securities face at least 50% margin and Stage II securities face 100% margin, substantially increasing the capital required to maintain or open positions. This may reduce liquidity and trading volumes in the affected scrips. The measure is a standard BSE surveillance tool and does not indicate fundamental issues with the listed companies, though it may create short-term price pressure due to forced position unwinding or reduced participation.
