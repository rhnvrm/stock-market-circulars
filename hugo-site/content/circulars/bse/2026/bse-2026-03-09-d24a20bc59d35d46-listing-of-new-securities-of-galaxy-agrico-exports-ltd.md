## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260309-26 informs trading members that 1,31,59,102 new equity shares of Galaxy Agrico Exports Ltd. (Scrip Code 531911) issued on a rights basis are listed and permitted to trade on BSE with effect from Tuesday, March 10, 2026.

## Key Points

- 1,31,59,102 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 25.87/- on rights basis are now listed on BSE
- ISIN: INE803L01016
- Distribution Numbers: 5000021 to 18159122
- Date of Allotment: March 5, 2026
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Trading effective from March 10, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for newly allotted rights issue shares.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to update their systems to permit trading of the new securities from March 10, 2026
- No additional compliance actions required for members beyond standard trading procedures

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: March 5, 2026
- Trading effective date: March 10, 2026 (Tuesday)

## Impact Assessment

The listing of 1,31,59,102 new equity shares increases the total share count of Galaxy Agrico Exports Ltd., which may have a dilutive effect on existing shareholders. The shares were issued at Rs. 35.87/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- + premium Rs. 25.87/-). The new shares are fully fungible with existing shares and rank pari-passu. Market impact is expected to be limited to existing shareholders and active traders in this scrip.
