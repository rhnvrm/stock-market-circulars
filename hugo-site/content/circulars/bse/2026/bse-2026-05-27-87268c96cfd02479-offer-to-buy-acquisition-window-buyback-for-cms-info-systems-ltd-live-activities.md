## Summary

BSE has issued a notice to trading members and custodian members regarding the Offer to Buy (OTB) – Acquisition Window (Buyback) for CMS INFO SYSTEMS LTD (CMSINFO). The buyback window is open from Friday, 29 May 2026 to Friday, 4 June 2026 at an offer price of Rs. 340 per share, with a total offer size of up to 49,39,126 equity shares.

## Key Points

- **Security:** CMS INFO SYSTEMS LTD | Symbol: CMSINFO | ISIN: INE925R01014 | Scrip Code: 543441
- **Offer Price:** Rs. 340.00 per share
- **Offer Size:** Up to 49,39,126 equity shares
- **Face Value:** Rs. 10.00 | Market Lot: 1
- **Opening Date:** Friday, 29 May 2026
- **Closing Date:** Friday, 4 June 2026
- **Session Timings:** 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. for most sessions; Take-up on last day extended to 4:00 p.m.
- Bids can only be placed for shares held in demat form

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular communicates the operational schedule for an already-announced buyback under the existing OTB Acquisition Window framework. Members are referred to Exchange Notices 20150930-1 and 20170203-29 for operational instructions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure that the Unique Client Code (UCC) of each investor is registered and updated with the correct PAN Number and Category in the Exchange UCC database before submitting bids.
- Only demat holdings are eligible for participation in this buyback.
- Members and Custodians should note that no End of Issue files will be generated on the Extranet if no securities are accepted.
- For login/password issues, members should contact Trading Operations at 022-22728005 or user.trdops@bseindia.com.

## Impact Assessment

This circular is operationally relevant to trading members and custodians facilitating client participation in the CMS INFO SYSTEMS LTD buyback. The offer at Rs. 340 per share for up to ~49.4 lakh shares provides an exit opportunity for eligible shareholders across multiple investor categories including FII, FI, MF, IC, individuals, and companies. Impact is moderate and limited to members and investors directly holding CMSINFO shares who may wish to tender in the buyback.
