## Summary

BSE has notified non-business days for select mutual fund schemes from Invesco, DSP, Kotak, and Nippon India on the BSE StAR MF Platform during March 2026. These closures are primarily for overseas equity funds of funds and debt schemes, corresponding to holidays in the respective underlying markets.

## Key Points

- Invesco India funds (Pan European, Global Equity Income, Global Consumer Trends, EQQQ Nasdaq-100 ETFs FoF) will be closed on **March 19, 2026**.
- DSP Value Fund will be closed on **March 17, 2026**.
- All DSP Debt Schemes (including DSP Liquidity Fund, Overnight Fund) and most DSP overseas FoFs will be closed on **March 19, 2026**; DSP Gold ETF FoF, Multi Asset Allocation Fund, Silver ETF FoF, and Equity Savings Fund remain open on that date.
- Kotak international/overseas funds (Global Emerging Market, US Specific Equity, Global Innovation, International REIT) will be closed on **March 3, March 6, March 26, and March 31, 2026**.
- Nippon India Japan Equity Fund will be closed on **March 20, 2026**.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notification under existing mutual fund transaction guidelines for BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- Distributors and investors must note that purchase, redemption, and switch transactions for the listed schemes will **not be processed** on the specified non-business days.
- Transactions submitted on non-business days will typically be processed on the next applicable business day, subject to each fund house's terms.

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited and routine. Investors and distributors in the affected overseas and debt mutual fund schemes should plan transactions accordingly to avoid processing delays. The closures align with international market holidays and are a normal feature of funds-of-funds investing in global markets. No structural or regulatory market impact is anticipated.
