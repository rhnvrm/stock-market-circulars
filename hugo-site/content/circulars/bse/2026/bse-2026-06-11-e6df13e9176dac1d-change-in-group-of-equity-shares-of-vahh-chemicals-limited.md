## Summary

BSE has notified that the equity shares of Vahh Chemicals Limited (Scrip Code: 544778), listed on the SME segment, will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment under M Group, effective Thursday, June 25, 2026. This is a continuation of Notice No. 20260610-34 dated June 10, 2026.

## Key Points

- Vahh Chemicals Limited (Scrip Code: 544778) will move from Trade for Trade (MT Group) to Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective date: Thursday, June 25, 2026
- This notice is a follow-up to Exchange Notice No. 20260610-34 dated June 10, 2026
- Listed on the SME segment of BSE
- Contact: Mr. Anurag Jain, Tel. No. 022-2272 8822 for further details

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares of Vahh Chemicals Limited will no longer be subject to the Trade for Trade (MT Group) restrictions. Once transferred, the shares will trade under the Rolling segment (M Group), allowing normal rolling settlement rather than mandatory delivery-based settlement.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members are required to note the change in trading segment for Vahh Chemicals Limited effective June 25, 2026. No specific action is required from trading members other than updating their trading and back-office systems to reflect the new segment classification.

## Important Dates

- **June 10, 2026**: Original notice issued (Notice No. 20260610-34)
- **June 11, 2026**: This follow-up notice issued (Notice No. 20260611-2)
- **June 25, 2026**: Effective date of transfer from MT Group to M Group (Rolling segment)

## Impact Assessment

This change benefits traders and investors in Vahh Chemicals Limited by moving the scrip from the more restrictive Trade for Trade (MT Group) mechanism — where trades must result in actual delivery and netting is not permitted — to the Rolling segment (M Group), which allows normal T+1 rolling settlement with netting. This generally indicates improved liquidity and reduced settlement risk for the scrip, and may increase trading activity. The impact is limited to participants trading in this specific SME-listed company.
