## Summary

BSE has announced changes to multiple BSE indices effective Tuesday, June 09, 2026, arising from the merger of Wim Plast Ltd (ticker: 526586) with Cello World Ltd (ticker: 544012) under a Scheme of Arrangement. Wim Plast Ltd will be dropped from four indices on the effective date.

## Key Points

- Wim Plast Ltd (526586) is merging with Cello World Ltd (544012), effective June 09, 2026.
- No new securities are being added to any index as part of this change.
- The corporate action file (*.SDE) has been updated as of end-of-day on June 04, 2026.
- Queries can be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com.

## Regulatory Changes

The following index constituents changes will take effect on June 09, 2026:

| Index | Action | Ticker | Stock Name |
|---|---|---|---|
| BSE SMALLCAP | DROP | 526586 | Wim Plast Ltd. |
| BSE ALLCAP | DROP | 526586 | Wim Plast Ltd. |
| BSE CONSUMER DISCRETIONARY | DROP | 526586 | Wim Plast Ltd. |
| BSE MIDSMALLCAP | DROP | 526586 | Wim Plast Ltd. |

## Compliance Requirements

Index fund managers, ETF managers, and portfolio managers tracking the affected indices (BSE SMALLCAP, BSE ALLCAP, BSE CONSUMER DISCRETIONARY, BSE MIDSMALLCAP) should rebalance their portfolios to remove Wim Plast Ltd prior to or on the effective date of June 09, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **June 04, 2026**: Notice issued; end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE) updated.
- **June 09, 2026**: Effective date of merger and index constituent changes.

## Impact Assessment

The removal of Wim Plast Ltd from four BSE indices is a direct consequence of its merger with Cello World Ltd. Investors and funds benchmarked to or tracking BSE SMALLCAP, BSE ALLCAP, BSE CONSUMER DISCRETIONARY, and BSE MIDSMALLCAP will need to adjust their holdings accordingly. The impact is moderate and localized to these four indices and holders of Wim Plast Ltd within index-tracking strategies.
