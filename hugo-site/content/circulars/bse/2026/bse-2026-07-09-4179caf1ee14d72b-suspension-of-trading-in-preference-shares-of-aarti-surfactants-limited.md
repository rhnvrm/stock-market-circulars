## Summary

BSE has notified Trading Members that dealings in the Non-Convertible Redeemable Preference Shares (NCRPS) of Aarti Surfactants Limited (Scrip Code: 700135, ISIN: INE09EO04017) will be suspended from 21 July 2026, following the Board's approval of full redemption of these shares.

## Key Points

- The Board of Directors of Aarti Surfactants Limited has approved redemption of 10,82,387 fully paid-up NCRPS of face value Rs. 10 each.
- Redemption price is Rs. 167.70 per NCRPS (Rs. 10 face value + Rs. 157.70 premium), plus an annualized return of 4% on the redemption price for a seven-year period, as per the terms governing the preference shares.
- Record Date for determining eligible preference shareholders is 21 July 2026.
- No dealings in the preference shares will be permitted from 21 July 2026 onward.
- Payment date for the redemption proceeds is fixed as Wednesday, 5 August 2026.
- Circular reference: DR-676/2026-2027; Notice No. 20260709-25.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine corporate action notice implementing a Board-approved redemption schedule, not a change in regulation.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members of BSE must not deal in the Preference shares of Aarti Surfactants Limited (Scrip Code: 700135) on or after 21 July 2026.

## Important Dates

- **21 July 2026** — Record Date; no dealings permitted from this date.
- **5 August 2026** — Redemption payment date.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders of Aarti Surfactants Limited's NCRPS, a narrow debt-segment instrument. Trading Members must ensure no transactions occur in this instrument after the record date. There is no bearing on the company's equity shares or on the broader market.
