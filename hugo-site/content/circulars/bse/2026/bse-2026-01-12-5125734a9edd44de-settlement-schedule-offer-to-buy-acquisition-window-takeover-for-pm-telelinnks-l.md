## Summary

BSE has announced the settlement schedule for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for P.M TELELINNKS LIMITED. The bidding window will be open from January 13, 2026 to January 27, 2026, with obligation download on February 9, 2026 and final settlement on February 10, 2026. This is Settlement No. 2026005 under the Takeover category.

## Key Points

- Bidding period: January 13, 2026 to January 27, 2026
- Settlement Number: 2026005
- Settlement Type: Takeover
- Demat shares must be delivered only through Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism
- Available in both depositories (CDSL and NSDL)
- Market Type "Takeover" and Settlement No. 2026005 to be used by Members/Custodians in depository system
- Securities will be earmarked/blocked in clients' accounts
- No EPN file upload required by Members & Custodians

## Regulatory Changes

Members/Custodians should refer to Notice No. 20200528-32 dated May 28, 2020 regarding settlement as per Revised Guidelines of Mechanism for acquisition of shares through Stock Exchange.

## Compliance Requirements

- Participants must ensure early pay-in of securities is reflected in BSE/ICCL systems before prescribed timelines for accepting/confirming bids
- Early pay-in process must be completed sufficiently in advance before closure of BSE OTB Bidding window
- Members must refer to MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for client-wise Stamp Duty charges
- Members/Custodians should contact relationship managers or ICCL Help Desk for clarifications (Contact: 022-69158590/45720490/45720690, Email: iccl.helpdesk@icclindia.com)

## Important Dates

- **Bid Start Date**: January 13, 2026
- **Bid End Date**: January 27, 2026
- **Obligation Download**: February 9, 2026
- **Settlement Date**: February 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular impacts shareholders of P.M TELELINNKS LIMITED who wish to participate in the takeover acquisition window. The settlement schedule provides a clear timeline for the acquisition process. Members and custodians handling client positions in this stock need to ensure proper early pay-in procedures are followed within the specified timelines. The impact is limited to participants in this specific takeover transaction and does not affect broader market operations.
