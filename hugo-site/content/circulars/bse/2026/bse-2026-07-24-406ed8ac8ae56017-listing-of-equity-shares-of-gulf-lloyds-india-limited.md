## Summary

BSE Limited has notified trading members that the equity shares of GULF LLOYDS (INDIA) LIMITED will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in the SME segment. The exact listing date and further security details will be announced in a subsequent notice.

## Key Points

- Company: GULF LLOYDS (INDIA) LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544834
- Symbol: GULFLLOYDS
- ISIN: INE1WDC01012
- Segment: SME
- No attachments accompanied this notice

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is an administrative listing notice under the Company-related category, Department DCS-Listing.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the scrip code, symbol, and ISIN for GULF LLOYDS (INDIA) LIMITED in preparation for trading once dealings commence. No specific action is required until the listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 24 July 2026
- Listing date: To be announced via separate notice

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to market participants tracking new SME listings and the specific stock. Trading members should update reference data (scrip code, symbol, ISIN) ahead of the formal listing to ensure readiness for dealings once the security becomes tradable.
