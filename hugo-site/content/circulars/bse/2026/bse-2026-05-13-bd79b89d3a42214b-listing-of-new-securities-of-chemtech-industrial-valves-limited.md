## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 5,00,000 new equity shares of Chemtech Industrial Valves Limited (Scrip Code: 537326, ISIN: INE212P01011) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Thursday, May 14, 2026. These shares were issued to Promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to the conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 5,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 190/- (issue price Rs. 200/-)
- Issued to Promoters on preferential basis via conversion of warrants
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 17937334 to 18437333
- Trading permitted from May 14, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification under BSE's Listing Operations department.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities and may trade them from May 14, 2026 onwards. No additional compliance action required from trading members.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** March 27, 2026
- **Trading Effective Date:** May 14, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry:** December 15, 2027 (all 5,00,000 shares, Dist. Nos. 17937334 to 18437333 are under lock-in)

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 5,00,000 shares to the tradable float for Chemtech Industrial Valves Limited; however, all newly listed shares are subject to lock-in until December 15, 2027, meaning no immediate increase in free-float. Market impact is minimal and confined to this single scrip.
