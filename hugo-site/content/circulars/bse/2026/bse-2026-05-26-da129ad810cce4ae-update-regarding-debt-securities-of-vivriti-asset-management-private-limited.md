## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the NCD scrip (Scrip Code 975094, ISIN INE0BXI07010) currently listed under Vivriti Asset Management Private Limited (VAMPL) will be transferred to Vivriti Funds Private Limited (VFPL) with effect from Friday, May 29, 2026. The transfer is pursuant to a Composite Scheme of Arrangement under Sections 230–232 of the Companies Act, 2013.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code 975094 (ISIN INE0BXI07010) will move from Vivriti Asset Management Private Limited to Vivriti Funds Private Limited effective May 29, 2026.
- The scrip long name changes from **VAMPL-11%-15-1-27-PVT** to **VFPL-11%-15-1-27-PVT**.
- The abbreviated name and Scrip ID change from **11VAMPL27** to **11VFPL27**.
- The scheme involves multiple entities: Vivriti Capital Limited (Demerged/Amalgamated Company), Hari And Company Investments Madras Private Limited (Resulting Company 1), Vivriti Next Private Limited, Vivriti Asset Management Private Limited (Amalgamating Company), and Vivriti Funds Private Limited (Resulting Company 2).
- Contact for queries: Bhakti Wankhede, +91 22 2272 5271, bse.schemes@bseindia.com.

## Regulatory Changes

The transfer is effected under the Composite Scheme of Arrangement sanctioned pursuant to Sections 230 to 232 of the Companies Act, 2013. The scrip identity (name, abbreviation, and Scrip ID) is being updated to reflect the new issuing entity, Vivriti Funds Private Limited.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to note the updated scrip details effective May 29, 2026, and ensure their systems reflect the new scrip long name (VFPL-11%-15-1-27-PVT), abbreviated name (11VFPL27), and Scrip ID (11VFPL27) for the same ISIN INE0BXI07010.

## Important Dates

- **May 29, 2026 (Friday):** Effective date of transfer of scrip from Vivriti Asset Management Private Limited to Vivriti Funds Private Limited.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders of and traders in ISIN INE0BXI07010, a privately placed NCD. The ISIN remains unchanged, so settlement continuity is maintained; however, trading members must update scrip name and ID references in their systems to avoid operational discrepancies. The restructuring reflects a broader corporate demerger involving Vivriti Capital and its group entities.
