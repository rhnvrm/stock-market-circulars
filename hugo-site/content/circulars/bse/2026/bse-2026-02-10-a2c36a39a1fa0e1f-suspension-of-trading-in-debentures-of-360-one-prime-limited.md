## Summary

BSE has issued a trading suspension notice for Non-Convertible Debentures of 360 ONE PRIME LIMITED (Code: 975450, ISIN: INE248U07FA1) effective February 13, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the record date.

## Key Points

- Company: 360 ONE PRIME LIMITED
- Debenture ISIN: INE248U07FA1 (360OPL-9.3%-28-2-26-PVT)
- BSE Code: 975450
- Interest Rate: 9.3%
- Record Date: February 13, 2026
- No Dealings From: February 13, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- Notice Number: DR-820/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action notification for debenture redemption as per existing regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in the specified debentures from February 13, 2026
- Trading members must inform their clients holding these debentures about the suspension
- No buy or sell orders should be accepted for INE248U07FA1 from the specified date

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: February 10, 2026
- **Record Date**: February 13, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Date**: February 13, 2026
- **Maturity Date**: February 28, 2026 (as indicated by debenture name)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: High - Complete trading suspension for these debentures, eliminating secondary market liquidity. Holders cannot exit positions from February 13, 2026 onwards.

**Investor Impact**: High - Debenture holders must hold until redemption. No trading opportunity available from record date. Interest payment and principal redemption will proceed as scheduled.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update systems to block orders for this ISIN and communicate with affected clients holding these securities.
