## Summary

Bombay Stock Exchange Ltd., Mumbai has issued a Demat Auction notice for Settlement No. 836, dated March 11, 2026. The auction lists 124 scrips across a wide variety of companies, with quantities to be auctioned ranging from 1 share to 245,520 shares.

## Key Points

- Settlement No. 836 auction conducted by BSE on March 11, 2026
- 124 securities listed across diverse sectors including pharma, technology, finance, FMCG, infrastructure, and more
- Quantities range from as low as 1 share (multiple scrips) to 245,520 shares (Deep Diamond, scrip code 539559)
- Notable large-quantity auctions include: Rajnish Wellness (541601) – 50,000 shares; DSP Mutual Fund (544247) – 40,000 shares; Tilak Ventures (503663) – 42,493 shares; PRISMX GLOBAL (501314) – 14,158 shares
- Both large-cap and small-cap companies are represented

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard settlement auction conducted under existing SEBI and BSE auction settlement framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants with short delivery obligations in the listed scrips must participate or ensure settlement through the auction mechanism
- Buyers in the auction must comply with applicable payment and delivery timelines as per BSE settlement rules
- Members should note their obligations under the BSE Bye-Laws and SEBI regulations for auction settlements

## Important Dates

- **Auction Date:** March 11, 2026
- **Settlement No.:** 836

## Impact Assessment

This demat auction affects a broad cross-section of listed securities. Participants with short delivery positions in any of the 124 listed scrips will be subject to auction buy-in at potentially higher prices, impacting their settlement costs. Scrips with very large auction quantities — such as Deep Diamond (245,520 shares), Rajnish Wellness (50,000 shares), Tilak Ventures (42,493 shares), and DSP Mutual Fund ETF (40,000 units) — may see temporary price pressure or volatility. Overall market impact is moderate and routine in nature.
