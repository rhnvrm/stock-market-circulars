## Summary

BSE has announced that trading in Rights Entitlements (REs) for renunciation of STEELCO GUJARAT LIMITED will commence on Monday, May 25, 2026. The REs will be tradable on the Exchange under scrip code 751116 (Scrip ID: STEEL-RE, ISIN: INE629B20016) and will be settled on a T+1 Rolling Settlement basis on a trade-for-trade basis. The Rights Issue itself is open from May 25 to June 4, 2026.

## Key Points

- Trading in Rights Entitlements (REs) for STEELCO GUJARAT LIMITED begins May 25, 2026
- Trading period for renunciation: Monday, May 25, 2026 to Friday, May 29, 2026
- Scrip Code: 751116 | Scrip ID: STEEL-RE | ISIN: INE629B20016
- Market Lot: 1 | Group: R
- Settlement on T+1 Rolling Settlement basis, trade-for-trade
- REs are tradable in dematerialized form only
- REs not subscribed or renounced by the Issue Closing Date will lapse and be extinguished

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice operationalizes existing rights entitlement trading framework per BSE notices 20200305-21 (March 5, 2020) and 20200518-34 (May 18, 2020).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must inform all clients about the commencement of trading in REs, specifying the ISIN (INE629B20016)
- Trading members must clarify to clients that REs must not be mistaken for ordinary shares of the company
- Contract notes/bills for RE purchases must prominently include a disclaimer stating that purchase of REs only grants the right to participate in the Rights Issue or renounce the REs before the issue closes
- REs must be held and traded in dematerialized form only

## Important Dates

- **May 25, 2026**: Commencement of trading in Rights Entitlements; Rights Issue opens
- **May 29, 2026**: Last day for trading/renunciation of Rights Entitlements
- **June 4, 2026**: Rights Issue closes; unsubscribed/unrenounced REs lapse and are extinguished

## Impact Assessment

This circular is relevant to existing shareholders of STEELCO GUJARAT LIMITED and trading members on BSE. Shareholders holding REs may either participate in the Rights Issue by applying with requisite application money or renounce their entitlements by selling REs on the exchange during the trading window (May 25–29, 2026). The trade-for-trade settlement and T+1 basis limit speculative activity. REs that are neither subscribed nor renounced will be extinguished after June 4, 2026, resulting in a loss of value for holders who take no action.
