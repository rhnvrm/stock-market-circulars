## Summary

BSE Trading Operations has notified trading members of revised strike price intervals applicable to stock options in the Equity Derivatives segment. The updated intervals will come into effect from **July 01, 2026**, and are detailed in the Annexure attached to Notice No. 20260619-20.

## Key Points

- Updated strike price intervals for stock options are effective from July 01, 2026.
- The revised intervals are provided on a per-scrip basis in the attached Annexure.
- Trading members are advised to review the Annexure to understand applicable intervals for individual underlying scrips.
- For clarifications, trading members should contact their respective BSE Relationship Managers.

## Regulatory Changes

BSE is revising the strike price intervals for stock options across underlying scrips in the Equity Derivatives segment. Such periodic revisions align strike price spacing with changes in stock prices, liquidity, and market conditions to ensure a well-structured options chain.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note and incorporate the revised strike price intervals into their trading systems and strategies before July 01, 2026.
- Members requiring clarification on specific scrips should proactively contact their Relationship Managers at BSE.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 19, 2026
- **Effective Date:** July 01, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This update primarily affects derivatives trading members and proprietary desks involved in stock options. Revised strike price intervals may alter the granularity of available options strikes, impacting hedging strategies, covered calls, and other structured positions. Systems that auto-generate or display options chains will need to be updated to reflect the new intervals before the effective date. No immediate market disruption is expected as this is a routine operational adjustment.
