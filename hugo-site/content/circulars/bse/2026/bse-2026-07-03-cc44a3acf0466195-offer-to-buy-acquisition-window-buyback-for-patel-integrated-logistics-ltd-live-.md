## Summary

BSE has issued the Live Activities Schedule for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Buyback) of shares of Patel Integrated Logistics Ltd (PATINTLOG). The buyback window runs from Monday, 6th July 2026 to Friday, 10th July 2026, with the Exchange detailing session timings for bid offering, modification/cancellation, and give-up/take-up activities for Trading and Custodian Members.

## Key Points

- Security: PATEL INTEGRATED LOGISTICS LTD (Symbol: PATINTLOG, ISIN: INE529D01014, Scrip Code: 526381)
- Offer size: Up to 54,00,000 equity shares, Market Lot: 1, Face Value: Rs. 10.00
- Offer Price: Rs. 20.00 per share
- Bid Offering Period: 6th–10th July 2026, 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. daily
- Bid Modifications/Cancellation: 6th–10th July 2026, 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.
- Give-up/Take-up: 6th–9th July 2026, 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.
- Last day (10th July 2026): Give-up until 3:30 p.m.; Take-up until 4:00 p.m.
- Bidding categories include FII, FI, MF, IC, Other QIBs, Other NIB, Company, and Individual investors
- Access via iBBS, RTRMS, and Extranet platforms (internet and leased line URLs provided)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is an operational notice referencing existing Exchange Notices no. 20150930-1 (30 Sept 2015) and 20170203-29 (3 Feb 2017) for OTB operational instructions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must ensure the UCC of each investor is registered/updated with correct PAN and Category in the Exchange UCC database.
- Trading Members can bid only for investors holding shares in demat form.
- Members and Custodians should note that no End of Issue files will be generated in Extranet if no securities are accepted.
- Files on the Extranet for bid acceptance will be available only after confirmation of acceptance is received from the Merchant Banker/RTA post issue closure.

## Important Dates

- Opening Date: Monday, 6th July 2026
- Closing Date: Friday, 10th July 2026
- Give-up cutoff (final day): 10th July 2026, 3:30 p.m.
- Take-up cutoff (final day): 10th July 2026, 4:00 p.m.

## Impact Assessment

This is a procedural/operational circular for Trading and Custodian Members facilitating participation in the PATINTLOG buyback via the Acquisition Window mechanism. It has limited broader market impact but is important for shareholders and intermediaries directly involved in tendering shares for this specific buyback offer.
