## Summary

BSE issued a circular containing clarifications from two Adani Group companies - Adani Green Energy Limited and Adani Ports and Special Economic Zone Limited - in response to media reports published on January 27, 2026. Both companies provided clarifications under Regulation 30(11) of SEBI Listing Regulations.

## Key Points

- Adani Green Energy clarified media reports about targeting 7 GWh BESS in Gujarat with capex of Rs. 40,000 crore in FY27
- The company referenced its prior disclosures from November 11, 2025 and November 13, 2025 about leading implementation of Adani's first BESS project
- Adani Green is on track to deploy one of the world's largest single location BESS in coming months
- The company intends to enhance BESS capacities potentially more than 2x current year's capacity in subsequent financial year
- Adani Ports responded to clarification request regarding media report on economictimes.indiatimes.com (content truncated in source)
- Both companies confirmed compliance with Regulation 30 disclosure requirements

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This circular involves routine clarifications under existing Regulation 30(11) of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies must respond to stock exchange queries regarding media reports under Regulation 30(11)
- Companies must make necessary disclosures in compliance with Regulation 30 of SEBI Listing Regulations
- Clarifications must be provided when stock exchanges seek verification of rumours or media reports

## Important Dates

- **January 27, 2026**: Date of media reports and stock exchange clarification requests
- **January 27, 2026**: Date of company responses
- **November 11, 2025**: Adani Green's original press release about BESS project
- **November 13, 2025**: Adani Green's subsequent communication about BESS implementation

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - These are clarifications on previously disclosed information rather than new material developments. Adani Green is reiterating its existing plans for BESS deployment.

**Operational Impact**: Low - No changes to operations or previously announced plans. Companies are confirming their ongoing projects and compliance with disclosure norms.

**Investor Impact**: Low - Clarifications provide confirmation of existing strategies but do not materially alter investment thesis. The BESS expansion plans were already in public domain through prior disclosures.
