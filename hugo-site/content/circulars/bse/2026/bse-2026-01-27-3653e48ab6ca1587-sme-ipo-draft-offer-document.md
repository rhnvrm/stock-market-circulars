## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of Paluck Technologies Limited on its SME website for public comments. The document was filed on January 20, 2026 and contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations. Stakeholders have 21 days from the notice date to submit comments.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Paluck Technologies Limited on January 20, 2026
- Document contains disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website at www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public comment period: 21 days from January 27, 2026
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural notice for SME IPO draft offer document publication as per existing ICDR Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- The Draft Offer Document must be hosted on the Stock Exchange website for at least 21 days
- Public stakeholders may review and submit comments on the draft document
- Comments should be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com within the 21-day period

## Important Dates

- **January 20, 2026**: Draft Offer Document filed by Paluck Technologies Limited
- **January 27, 2026**: Notice date and start of public comment period
- **February 17, 2026**: Last date for submitting public comments (21 days from notice date)
