## Summary

BSE has listed 527,500 equity shares of Suyog Telematics Limited (Scrip Code: 537259) issued on a preferential basis to promoters pursuant to conversion of warrants. The shares are permitted to trade with effect from Friday, January 30, 2026, and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- Number of shares: 527,500 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each
- Issue price: Rs. 570/- per share (Rs. 10/- face value + Rs. 560/- premium)
- Allotment basis: Preferential allotment to promoters
- Issuance reason: Conversion of warrants
- Distribution numbers: 11189621 to 11717120
- Date of allotment: October 10, 2025
- ISIN: INE442P01014
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification for new securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the listing and should ensure proper trading arrangements for the new securities from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: October 10, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: January 30, 2026 (Friday)
- **Lock-in Period Expiry**: July 30, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low. The preferential allotment of 527,500 shares represents a relatively small issue to promoters only, with no immediate free float impact due to the lock-in period until July 2027.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal. Standard listing procedure for warrant conversion with no special trading arrangements required.

**Lock-in Details**: All 527,500 shares (distribution numbers 11189621-11717120) are subject to lock-in until July 30, 2027, preventing any near-term dilution in the tradable float.
