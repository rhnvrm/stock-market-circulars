## Summary

BSE has announced the addition of three newly listed companies to the BSE SME IPO Index effective February 05, 2026. The companies - Kanishk Aluminium India Limited (544693), Accretion Nutraveda Limited (544694), and MSafe Equipments Limited (544695) - were listed on BSE on February 04, 2026, and will be included in the index from the opening of the next trading day.

## Key Points

- Three companies being added to BSE SME IPO Index: Kanishk Aluminium India Limited, Accretion Nutraveda Limited, and MSafe Equipments Limited
- Companies listed on BSE effective February 04, 2026
- Index addition effective at market open on February 05, 2026
- Exchange tickers: 544693, 544694, and 544695 respectively
- Corporate action changes included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- Referenced previous listing notices: 20260203-43, 20260203-44, and 20260203-45

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index rebalancing action following the listing of new SME companies.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index constituents are automatically updated in trading systems through the corporate action file. Queries can be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **February 04, 2026**: Listing date for all three companies on BSE
- **February 05, 2026**: Effective date for index addition (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine administrative action for SME IPO listings. The BSE SME IPO Index tracks newly listed SME companies, and periodic additions are expected as new companies complete their IPOs.

**Operational Impact**: Index fund managers and ETFs tracking the BSE SME IPO Index will need to rebalance portfolios to include these three stocks. The impact is limited to SME-focused investment products.

**Investor Impact**: Investors tracking or benchmarking against the BSE SME IPO Index should note the inclusion of these three companies in index calculations from February 05, 2026 onwards.
