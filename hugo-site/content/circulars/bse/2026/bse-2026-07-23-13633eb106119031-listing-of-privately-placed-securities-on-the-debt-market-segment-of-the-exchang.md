## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that new securities issued on a private placement basis have been listed and admitted to trading on the BSE Debt Segment effective 23 July 2026. The listings comprise commercial papers (CPs) issued by Bajaj Finance Limited, Bajaj Financial Securities Limited, 360 ONE Alternates Asset Management Limited, and Poonawalla Fincorp Limited (two separate issues).

## Key Points

- All securities will trade only in dematerialised form under their respective ISINs.
- Trading will occur in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof.
- Tick size for these securities is 1 paise.
- Fresh listings include:
  - Bajaj Finance Limited (Scrip Code 732137, ISIN INE296A14I42) – Rs. 500,000 face value CP, quantity 2,000, rated CRISIL A1+/ICRA A1+, redemption on 21/10/2026.
  - Bajaj Financial Securities Limited (Scrip Code 732136, ISIN INE01C314GO3) – Rs. 500,000 face value CP, quantity 500, rated CRISIL A1+/IND A1+, redemption on 26/11/2026.
  - 360 ONE Alternates Asset Management Limited (Scrip Code 732138, ISIN INE0TQI14541) – Rs. 500,000 face value CP, quantity 100, rated CARE A1+, redemption on 22/10/2026.
  - Poonawalla Fincorp Limited (Scrip Code 732140, ISIN INE511C14ZT4) – Rs. 500,000 face value CP, quantity 5,000, rated CARE A1+/CRISIL A1+, redemption on 12/10/2026.
  - Poonawalla Fincorp Limited (Scrip Code 732142, ISIN INE511C14ZS6) – Rs. 500,000 face value CP, quantity 10,000, rated CARE A1+/CRISIL A1+, redemption on 19/10/2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is a routine administrative listing notice under BSE's Debt Segment (DCS-Listing department).

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the denomination, tick size, and ISIN-based dematerialised trading requirements for these instruments. Queries can be directed to the BSE debt department at the numbers provided (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915).

## Important Dates

- Actual/Deemed date of allotment for all CPs: 22 July 2026.
- Listing effective date: 23 July 2026.
- Redemption dates range from 12 October 2026 to 26 November 2026 depending on the specific CP.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification affecting debt market participants and trading members dealing in commercial papers. It has no direct impact on equity markets or listed stock prices, and is procedural in nature for BSE's Debt Segment operations.
