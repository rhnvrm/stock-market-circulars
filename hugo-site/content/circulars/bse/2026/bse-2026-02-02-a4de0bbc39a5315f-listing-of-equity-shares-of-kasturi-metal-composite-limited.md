## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that equity shares of Kasturi Metal Composite Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544688, symbol KASTURI, and ISIN No. INE0T1301023. The exact date of listing and detailed security information will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Kasturi Metal Composite Limited
- Scrip Code: 544688
- Symbol: KASTURI
- ISIN Number: INE0T1301023
- Segment: SME
- Listing date to be announced separately

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the scrip details for the upcoming listing. No immediate action required until the listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: February 2, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement with minimal market impact. The listing will add one more SME stock to the BSE platform. Trading members and investors should await the separate notice containing the actual listing date and complete security details before any trading activity can commence.
