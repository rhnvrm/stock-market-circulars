## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 13,37,49,957 new equity shares of Garment Mantra Lifestyle Ltd. (Scrip Code: 539216) are permitted for trading effective Thursday, June 04, 2026. These shares arise from the conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up shares of Re. 1/- each, issued on a rights basis.

## Key Points

- 13,37,49,957 new equity shares admitted to trading from June 04, 2026
- Shares result from conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up (Re. 1/- each) issued on rights basis
- ISIN: INE653S01028
- Distinctive Numbers: 444460076 to 578210032
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Scrip Code: 539216

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification following the completion of a rights issue and conversion of partly paid equity shares to fully paid up status.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed and permitted to trade in the newly listed securities from the effective date. No additional compliance action is required from members beyond standard trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 03, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** Thursday, June 04, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This listing expands the tradeable float of Garment Mantra Lifestyle Ltd. by 13,37,49,957 equity shares. Since the new shares are pari-passu with existing shares and arise from a rights conversion (already subscribed by existing shareholders), the dilution impact on existing fully paid shareholders is limited. Market impact is expected to be minimal as these shares were previously issued on a partly paid basis.
