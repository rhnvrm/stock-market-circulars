## Summary

BSE has announced the opening of Offer for Sale (OFS) for East India Drums & Barrels Manufacturing Limited. This circular addresses the undersubscription acceptance mechanism for the offering, allowing investors to participate in the OFS process.

## Key Points

- Offer for Sale (OFS) opened for East India Drums & Barrels Manufacturing Limited
- Circular includes provisions for undersubscription acceptance
- Mechanism allows for acceptance of undersubscribed portions of the offering
- Standard OFS procedures and guidelines apply

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular implements existing OFS framework with undersubscription acceptance provisions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Eligible investors must follow prescribed bidding procedures
- Undersubscription acceptance process must be completed as per circular guidelines
- All participants must comply with standard OFS regulations and timelines
- Bidding members must ensure compliance with BSE OFS platform requirements

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: January 14, 2026
- OFS opening date and bidding schedule as specified in the detailed circular
- Settlement dates to follow standard T+2 cycle post-OFS closure

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - The OFS provides exit opportunity for existing shareholders and entry opportunity for institutional and retail investors in East India Drums & Barrels Manufacturing Limited.

**Investor Impact:** Investors gain opportunity to acquire shares through the OFS mechanism. Undersubscription acceptance provisions ensure flexibility in case of lower-than-expected demand.

**Company Impact:** Facilitates share distribution and price discovery for East India Drums & Barrels Manufacturing Limited while maintaining orderly market conditions.
