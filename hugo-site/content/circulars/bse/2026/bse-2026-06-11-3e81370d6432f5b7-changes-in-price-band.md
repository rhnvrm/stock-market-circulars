## Summary

BSE has revised the price bands for 22 equity scrips effective June 12, 2026, under Notice No. 20260611-42 issued by the Surveillance department. The revisions affect scrips across various sectors, with new price bands set at 5%, 10%, or 20% depending on the security.

## Key Points

- Price band revisions are effective from June 12, 2026
- 22 scrips are affected across various sectors
- Revised price bands range from 5% to 20%
- Notice issued by BSE Surveillance department (DOSS)
- Members may seek clarification at bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must ensure all orders and trades in the above-mentioned scrips comply with the revised price bands effective June 12, 2026. Systems and risk management frameworks should be updated to reflect the new limits.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 11, 2026
- **Effective Date:** June 12, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The majority of affected scrips (16 out of 22) have been assigned a 5% price band, indicating heightened surveillance concern or volatility control measures. Four scrips (AA Plus Tradelink, Genesys International, Getalong Enterprise, Ravelcare) have been assigned a 10% band, while Autofurnish Ltd has been assigned a wider 20% band. Vertex Securities Ltd appears twice under different scrip codes (531950 and 890234), both revised to 5%. Tighter price bands restrict intraday price movement and may reduce liquidity in these securities, affecting traders who rely on wider price movements.
