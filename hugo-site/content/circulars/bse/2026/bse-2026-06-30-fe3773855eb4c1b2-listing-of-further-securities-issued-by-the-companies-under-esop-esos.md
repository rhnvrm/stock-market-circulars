## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that additional equity shares issued by 16 companies under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP/ESOS) are listed and admitted for trading on the Exchange effective **Wednesday, July 1, 2026**. None of the newly listed shares are subject to any lock-in period.

## Key Points

- 16 companies have new ESOP/ESOS shares admitted for trading from July 1, 2026
- No lock-in restrictions apply to any of the listed securities
- Shares span a variety of sectors including pharmaceuticals, banking, technology, infrastructure, and fintech
- Federal Bank has the largest allotment with 450,675 new shares; Laurus Labs follows with 393,001 shares
- SPML Infra and Moschip Technologies also have significant allotments of 233,744 and 250,630 shares respectively

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this circular. It is a routine operational notice for the listing of employee stock option shares.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action is required from trading members or market participants beyond noting the new share listings and updating records accordingly.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 30, 2026
- **Effective Date for Trading:** July 1, 2026 (Wednesday)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notice with minimal market impact. The newly listed ESOP/ESOS shares increase the float for each of the 16 companies by small amounts relative to their existing share counts. No price adjustment or special trading arrangements are required. The largest additions in absolute share count are for Federal Bank (450,675 shares), Laurus Labs (393,001 shares), and Intellect Design Arena (164,940 shares), though these remain a negligible fraction of their total outstanding shares.
