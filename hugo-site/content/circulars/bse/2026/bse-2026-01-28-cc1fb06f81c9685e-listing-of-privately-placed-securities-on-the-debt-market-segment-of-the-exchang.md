## Summary

BSE has listed commercial papers issued by five companies on private placement basis on its debt market segment effective January 28, 2026. The securities will trade in dematerialized form only with a standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof, with a tick size of 1 paise.

## Key Points

- Five companies have listed commercial papers totaling 11,800 units
- Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited: 2,500 units at 8.60% p.a., maturing January 27, 2027
- Time Technoplast Limited: 200 units at 7.65% p.a., maturing August 25, 2026
- 360 ONE Prime Limited: 5,000 units at 8.40% p.a., maturing January 25, 2027
- Bajaj Finance Limited: 4,000 units at 7.20% p.a., maturing June 15, 2026
- Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited: 100 units at 7.40% p.a., maturing March 25, 2026
- All securities carry CRISIL A1+ and/or ICRA A1+ credit ratings
- Trading restricted to dematerialized form with standard lot size of Rs. 5 lakhs

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under specified ISIN numbers
- Minimum trading denomination is Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size is 1 paise for all listed commercial papers
- Market lot size is 1 unit for all securities

## Important Dates

- Listing effective date: January 28, 2026
- Allotment dates: January 27-28, 2026
- Redemption dates range from March 25, 2026 to January 27, 2027
- Interest payment: Cumulative at redemption for all securities

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative notice with minimal market impact. The listing provides additional short-term debt instruments for institutional investors and adds liquidity to the BSE debt segment. The commercial papers have tenures ranging from 56 to 364 days with interest rates between 7.20% to 8.60% p.a., reflecting current money market conditions. No impact on equity markets or retail investors.
