## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 1,34,680 new equity shares of Shri Gang Industries & Allied Products Limited (Scrip Code: 523309) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Friday, July 24, 2026. The shares were issued to promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- New securities: 1,34,680 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each, issued at a premium of Rs. 89/- (issue price Rs. 99/-)
- Allotment made to promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants
- These shares rank pari-passu with the existing equity shares of the company
- Distinctive numbers: 19980001 to 20114680
- ISIN: INE241V01018
- Scrip Code: 523309

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing notice under BSE Listing Operations.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the listing for operational awareness; no specific action required beyond recognizing the new shares as pari-passu with existing equity from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: 06 June 2026
- Listing/Trading Effective Date: Friday, 24 July 2026
- Lock-in period: up to 31 January 2028 for the allotted 1,34,680 shares

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to Shri Gang Industries & Allied Products Limited shareholders and trading members. The allotment increases the company's paid-up equity base marginally through a preferential warrant conversion to promoters, with a lock-in until January 2028 restricting immediate sale. No broader market or sectoral impact is expected.
