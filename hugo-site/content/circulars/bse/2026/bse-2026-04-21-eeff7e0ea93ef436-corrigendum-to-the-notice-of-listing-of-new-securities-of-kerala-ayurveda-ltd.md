## Summary

BSE has issued a corrigendum to clarify that Notice No. 20260420-21 dated April 20, 2026, pertaining to Kerala Ayurveda Ltd. (Scrip Code: 530163), was issued in duplicate due to a technical error in the system. Trading members are advised to ignore that duplicate notice. The original Notice No. 20260420-18 dated April 20, 2026 remains fully valid and unchanged.

## Key Points

- Notice No. 20260420-21 (dated April 20, 2026) was erroneously issued as a duplicate and should be disregarded
- The original Notice No. 20260420-18 (dated April 20, 2026) for listing of new securities of Kerala Ayurveda Ltd. remains valid
- Scrip Code for Kerala Ayurveda Ltd. is 530163
- No changes to the substantive content of the original listing notice

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is purely an administrative correction to retract a duplicate notice issued due to a system error.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should disregard Notice No. 20260420-21 and refer only to Notice No. 20260420-18 for details regarding the listing of new securities of Kerala Ayurveda Ltd.

## Important Dates

- April 20, 2026: Original Notice No. 20260420-18 issued (remains valid)
- April 20, 2026: Duplicate Notice No. 20260420-21 issued (to be ignored)
- April 21, 2026: This corrigendum issued

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This corrigendum is an administrative action to void a duplicate notice caused by a technical system error. The underlying listing of new securities of Kerala Ayurveda Ltd. proceeds as per the original notice. No trading or compliance actions are required beyond disregarding the duplicate notice.
