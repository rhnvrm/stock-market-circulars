## Summary

BSE's Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd. (ICCL) has issued the settlement calendar for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) mechanism for LIPPI SYSTEMS LIMITED. The takeover offer bidding window runs from 20 July 2026 to 31 July 2026, under Settlement No. 2026089, with obligation download on 13 August 2026 and final settlement on 14 August 2026.

## Key Points

- Settlement Type: Takeover; Settlement No.: 2026089
- Bid Start Date: 20/07/2026; Bid End Date: 31/07/2026
- Obligation Download: 13/08/2026
- Settlement Date: 14/08/2026
- Demat shares must be delivered only through the Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism, available in both CDSL and NSDL depositories
- Members/Custodians must use Market Type "(Takeover)" and Settlement No. 2026089 in the depository system
- Reference to Notice No. 20200528-32 dated 28 May 2020 for revised guidelines on mechanism for acquisition of shares through the Stock Exchange
- CDSL & NSDL early pay-in securities will be earmarked/blocked in the client's account under the requisite market type and settlement number
- Members & Custodians are NOT required to upload any EPN file
- Members should refer to the MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for client-wise stamp duty charges

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this notice operationalizes the existing acquisition window (takeover) mechanism as per the framework established under Notice No. 20200528-32 dated 28 May 2020.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and Custodians must ensure early pay-in of securities is reflected in BSE/ICCL systems before the prescribed timelines for accepting/confirming bids on the BSE OTB platform
- Early pay-in of securities in the depository system must be completed well in advance of the closure of the BSE OTB bidding window and the custodian confirmation window
- Correct usage of Market Type "(Takeover)" and Settlement No. 2026089 is mandatory in depository system entries
- For queries, Members and Custodians may contact ICCL Help Desk at 022-69158590/45720490/45720690 or iccl.helpdesk@icclindia.com

## Important Dates

- Bid Start Date: 20 July 2026
- Bid End Date: 31 July 2026
- Obligation Download: 13 August 2026
- Settlement Date: 14 August 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational/settlement notice relevant primarily to BSE trading members and custodians participating in the Lippi Systems Limited takeover offer via the Acquisition Window. It has limited broader market impact but is important for accurate and timely settlement compliance by participating intermediaries and shareholders tendering shares in the offer.
