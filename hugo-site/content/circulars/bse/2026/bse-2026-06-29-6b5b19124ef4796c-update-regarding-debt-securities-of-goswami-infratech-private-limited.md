## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in the debt securities of Goswami Infratech Private Limited with effect from Wednesday, July 02, 2026. This notice is a continuation of Exchange Notice 20260519-8 dated May 19, 2026, and the suspension is on account of debt restructuring.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code **974946** with ISIN **INE219O07370** will be suspended from trading effective July 02, 2026.
- The suspension is due to debt restructuring as detailed in Annexure I of the original notice.
- This is a follow-up to Exchange Notice 20260519-8 dated May 19, 2026.
- Trading members are advised to take note of the suspension before the effective date.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced. The suspension is applied under existing BSE provisions governing debt securities when an issuer undergoes debt restructuring.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease trading in Scrip Code 974946 (ISIN: INE219O07370) from July 02, 2026 onwards.
- Members with queries may contact Mr. Akash Shinde at +91 22 2272 8597 or email debt.listing@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **May 19, 2026**: Original Exchange Notice 20260519-8 issued regarding debt restructuring.
- **June 29, 2026**: This update notice issued.
- **July 02, 2026 (Wednesday)**: Effective date of trading suspension for Scrip Code 974946 / ISIN INE219O07370.

## Impact Assessment

Holders and traders of Goswami Infratech Private Limited's debt securities (Scrip Code 974946) will be unable to trade these instruments on BSE from July 02, 2026. The suspension signals active debt restructuring, which may affect recovery prospects for debt security holders. Market participants exposed to this scrip should reassess their positions and monitor further announcements from the exchange regarding the restructuring outcome.
