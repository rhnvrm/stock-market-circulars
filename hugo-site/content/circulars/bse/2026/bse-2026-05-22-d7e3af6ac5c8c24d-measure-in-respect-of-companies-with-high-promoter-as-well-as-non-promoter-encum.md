## Summary

BSE has issued an update under the encumbrance-based surveillance measure as per Regulation 28(3) of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulation 2011. This circular is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20220131-43 dated January 31, 2022. It updates the list of securities subject to a minimum 75% margin requirement in the Equity and Equity Derivatives segments.

## Key Points

- Securities in **Annexure I** have met the criteria for inclusion under the encumbrance measure and will attract a **minimum 75% margin** in Equity and Equity Derivatives segments effective **May 27, 2026**.
- The 75% margin applies to all open positions as on **May 26, 2026**, and all new positions created from **May 27, 2026**.
- Securities in **Annexure II** are eligible to **exit the framework** effective **May 25, 2026**.
- A consolidated list of all securities currently under the framework is provided in **Annexure III**.
- This measure is applied in conjunction with all other prevailing measures imposed by the Exchanges and is subject to periodic review.
- Inclusion in this measure should not be construed as an adverse action against the concerned company/entity.

## Regulatory Changes

This is a periodic update to an existing surveillance framework established under SEBI (SAST) Regulation 2011, Regulation 28(3). No new regulatory framework has been introduced; the circular updates the list of securities under the existing encumbrance-based margin measure.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members** must take note of the updated securities lists (Annexures I, II, and III).
- Members must ensure collection of **minimum 75% margin** on all qualifying positions in securities listed in Annexure I from May 27, 2026.
- For clarifications, members may write to **bse.surv@bseindia.com**.

## Important Dates

- **May 25, 2026**: Annexure II securities exit the encumbrance framework.
- **May 26, 2026**: Reference date for open positions that will attract 75% margin from May 27, 2026.
- **May 27, 2026**: Minimum 75% margin becomes effective for Annexure I securities.

## Impact Assessment

Trading members and market participants holding positions in securities listed under Annexure I will face significantly elevated margin requirements (minimum 75%), increasing the cost of maintaining or initiating positions. This may result in reduced liquidity and trading activity in the affected securities. Securities exiting the framework (Annexure II) will revert to standard margin norms, potentially improving tradability. The measure targets companies where promoter and/or non-promoter encumbrance is high, signalling potential financial stress or pledging risk, and is designed as a protective measure for market integrity.
