## Summary

BSE has notified members that pursuant to Notice No. 20260414-3 dated April 14, 2026, two schemes of THE WEALTH COMPANY MUTUAL FUND — WSIF Equity Ex-Top 100 Long-Short Fund and WSIF Equity Long-Short Fund — will reopen for ongoing transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective May 11, 2026.

## Key Points

- Both schemes are classified under the Equity category.
- Reopening applies to ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) facility.
- Effective date for reopening: May 11, 2026.
- Detailed plans and options for each scheme are provided in the attached Annexure.
- This notice follows an earlier Notice No. 20260414-3 dated April 14, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an operational reinstatement of transaction availability for two previously paused mutual fund schemes on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- BSE members dealing in mutual funds should update their systems to enable transactions in WSIF Equity Ex-Top 100 Long-Short Fund and WSIF Equity Long-Short Fund from May 11, 2026.
- Members should refer to the Annexure for the full list of plans and options under each scheme.

## Important Dates

- **May 11, 2026**: Effective date for reopening of both schemes for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP on BSE StAR MF Platform.
- **April 14, 2026**: Original notice date (Notice No. 20260414-3) referenced as the basis for this update.

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited to mutual fund distributors and investors transacting through the BSE StAR MF Platform. The reopening of these two equity long-short fund schemes provides renewed access to investors who wish to subscribe, redeem, or set up SIPs. No broader market or systemic impact is anticipated.
