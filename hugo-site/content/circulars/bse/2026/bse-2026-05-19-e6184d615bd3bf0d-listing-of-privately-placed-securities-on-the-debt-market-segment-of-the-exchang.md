## Summary

BSE has listed four new Commercial Papers (CPs) on its Debt Market Segment, issued on a private placement basis and admitted to trading effective May 19, 2026. The securities are issued by Navi Finserv Limited, Infina Finance Private Limited, L&T Metro Rail (Hyderabad) Limited, and 360 ONE Alternates Asset Management Limited. All securities trade in dematerialized form in standard denominations of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof, with a tick size of 1 paise.

## Key Points

- Four Commercial Papers freshly listed on BSE Debt Segment effective May 19, 2026
- All allotted on May 18, 2026 on private placement basis
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples; tick size: 1 paise
- Trading only in dematerialized form under respective ISINs
- Rates range from 7.05% p.a. (L&T Metro Rail) to 10.10% p.a. (Navi Finserv)
- Redemption dates range from June 22, 2026 to November 13, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification under BSE's Debt Market Segment framework for privately placed securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Transactions must be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the BSE Debt Department: 22728352 / 8597 / 8995 / 5753 / 8915
