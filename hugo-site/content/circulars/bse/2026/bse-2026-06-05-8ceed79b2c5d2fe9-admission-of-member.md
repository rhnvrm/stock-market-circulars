## Summary

BSE has admitted two new entities as Trading Members of BSE Ltd. with effect from 05 June 2026. Both admissions are classified as New Membership, granting only trading rights on BSE Ltd. subject to its Rules, Bye-laws, and Regulations.

## Key Points

- Probulls Stockbroking Private Limited admitted as Member No. 6990 with trading rights in the Cash/Equity Derivatives segment
- Powerup Money Fintech Private Limited admitted as Member No. 6991 with trading rights in the Cash segment
- Both memberships are categorised as New Membership (not a transfer or upgrade of existing membership)
- Effective date of admission: 05 June 2026
- Members with queries may contact: membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine membership admission notice under BSE's existing Rules, Bye-laws, and Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for existing trading members. Existing members are simply being informed of the new participants admitted to the exchange.

## Important Dates

- **Admission Date:** 05 June 2026 — effective date for both new trading members

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The admission of two new trading members is a routine exchange operation. Probulls Stockbroking will participate in both Cash/Equity and Derivatives segments, while Powerup Money Fintech will operate only in the Cash segment. This slightly increases the pool of registered intermediaries on BSE but has no direct impact on existing members or market operations.
