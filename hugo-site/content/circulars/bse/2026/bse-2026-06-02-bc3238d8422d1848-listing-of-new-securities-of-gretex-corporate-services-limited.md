## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 8,00,000 new equity shares of Gretex Corporate Services Limited (Scrip Code: 543324, ISIN: INE199P01028) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Wednesday, June 3, 2026. The shares were issued at Rs. 379/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- + premium of Rs. 369/-) to promoters and non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 8,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at Rs. 379/- per share (premium Rs. 369/-)
- Issued on preferential basis via conversion of warrants to promoters and non-promoters
- Distinctive numbers: 22639348 to 23439347
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Trading effective from June 3, 2026
- ISIN: INE199P01028

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification under BSE's listing operations for newly allotted securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to permit trading in these securities from June 3, 2026. Lock-in restrictions apply to all 8,00,000 shares as detailed below; trading is restricted for locked-in shares until the respective lock-in expiry dates.

## Impact Assessment

This listing increases the total issued and paid-up share capital of Gretex Corporate Services Limited by 8,00,000 shares. The preferential allotment was executed in tranches between January 13 and January 29, 2026. All newly listed shares are subject to lock-in, limiting immediate secondary market supply. The impact on broader markets is negligible; effect is limited to existing and prospective shareholders of Gretex Corporate Services Limited (BSE: 543324).
