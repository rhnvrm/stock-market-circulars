## Summary

BSE has prohibited/suspended trading in securities of two companies — Sellwin Traders Ltd. (scrip code 538875) and AVI Polymers Ltd (scrip code 539288) — effective Thursday, June 4, 2026. The action follows a site visit by the Exchange during which neither company was found to be present/operational at their respective registered office addresses.

## Key Points

- Trading in Sellwin Traders Ltd. (538875) is prohibited/suspended with effect from June 4, 2026.
- Trading in AVI Polymers Ltd (539288) is prohibited/suspended with effect from June 4, 2026.
- The suspension is an **interim measure** following Exchange site visits that found both companies absent from their registered office addresses.
- Trading members and investors are requested to take note of the restrictions.

## Regulatory Changes

No permanent regulatory changes. The suspension is described as an **interim measure** pending further investigation or resolution. BSE retains discretion to lift or extend the suspension based on compliance by the companies.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members**: Must not facilitate trading in the securities of the two suspended companies from June 4, 2026 onwards.
- **Investors**: Should be aware that these securities are not tradable until further notice; any open positions or pending orders should be managed accordingly.
- **Affected Companies**: To have the suspension lifted, the companies would typically need to demonstrate their physical presence at registered offices or provide satisfactory clarification to BSE Listing Compliance.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: June 3, 2026
- **Effective Date of Suspension**: June 4, 2026 (Thursday)

## Impact Assessment

Investors holding shares in Sellwin Traders Ltd. or AVI Polymers Ltd. face immediate trading illiquidity — they cannot buy or sell these securities on BSE until the suspension is lifted. The interim nature of the suspension suggests BSE may reinstate trading if the companies demonstrate compliance with listing norms, but the timeline is uncertain. The absence of companies from registered offices is a serious listing compliance violation that could escalate to permanent delisting if not resolved.
