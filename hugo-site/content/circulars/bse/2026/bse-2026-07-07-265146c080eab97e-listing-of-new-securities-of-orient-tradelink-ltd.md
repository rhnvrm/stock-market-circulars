## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 34,53,588 new equity shares of Orient Tradelink Ltd. (Scrip Code - 531512), issued at a premium of Rs. 12/- per share on a preferential basis to Non-Promoters, are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Wednesday, July 8, 2026. These shares rank pari-passu with the existing equity shares of the company.

## Key Points

- Security: 34,53,588 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 12/- to Non-Promoters on a preferential basis.
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of Orient Tradelink Ltd.
- Shares were allotted across multiple dates between 07/01/2026 and 20/01/2026 in tranches ranging from 50,000 to 4,22,953 shares, with distinctive numbers from 35232001 to 38685588.
- Issue Price: Rs. 22/- per share.
- ISIN: INE681D01039.
- All 34,53,588 shares are subject to lock-in until 28/02/2027.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a standard listing notification under BSE's Listing Operations department for shares issued via preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must recognize the additional listed quantity of Orient Tradelink Ltd. shares for trading purposes; the entire allotted quantity remains under lock-in until 28/02/2027 and is not available for trading during this period.

## Important Dates

- Allotment dates: 07/01/2026 through 20/01/2026 (multiple tranches).
- Listing/trading effective date: Wednesday, July 8, 2026.
- Lock-in expiry: 28/02/2027.

## Impact Assessment

The listing increases the total tradable equity base of Orient Tradelink Ltd., though the newly listed shares are locked in until February 2027 and will not immediately affect free-float liquidity. The preferential allotment to non-promoters may have implications for shareholding pattern and dilution, relevant to existing shareholders and market participants tracking the stock.
