## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that three new Treasury Bills (T-Bills) will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in the G GROUP - DEBT INSTRUMENTS segment with effect from June 12, 2026. The listing covers all three standard T-Bill tenors: 91-day, 182-day, and 364-day.

## Key Points

- Three T-Bills listed across 91-day, 182-day, and 364-day tenors
- Effective date: June 12, 2026
- Listed under G GROUP - DEBT INSTRUMENTS
- Market lot for all three securities is 1
- Trading is restricted during the last two working days prior to each T-Bill's maturity/redemption date (T minus 2 trading days, excluding bank holidays)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification under existing BSE debt market framework.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must note that the above-listed securities shall not be available for trading two (2) working days prior to the Maturity/Redemption Date (i.e., Maturity Date (T) minus two Trading Days, not being Bank Holidays). Members should update their systems accordingly to prevent trading during the restricted window.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational listing of government-issued Treasury Bills. The addition of these three T-Bills expands the available short-term government securities on BSE's debt segment, providing trading members and investors with standard liquidity instruments across 91-day, 182-day, and 364-day horizons. Impact is limited to debt market participants; no equity market impact is anticipated.
