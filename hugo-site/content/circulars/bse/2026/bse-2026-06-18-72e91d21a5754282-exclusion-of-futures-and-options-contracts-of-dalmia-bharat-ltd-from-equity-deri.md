## Summary

BSE has announced the exclusion of Futures and Options contracts for Dalmia Bharat Ltd (Derivatives Asset Code: DABL, Equity Scrip Code: 542216) from the Equity Derivatives segment. This follows SEBI Circular SEBI/HO/MRD/MRD-PoD-2/P/CIR/2024/116 dated August 30, 2024 on eligibility criteria and BSE Notice 20240902-3 dated September 2, 2024 on review of eligibility criteria for entry/exit of stocks in the derivatives segment.

## Key Points

- Dalmia Bharat Ltd F&O contracts will be discontinued from trading in the Equity Derivatives segment effective **August 28, 2026**.
- No new expiry month contracts will be generated for this security.
- Existing unexpired contracts for June 2026, July 2026, and August 2026 will continue to trade until their respective expiry dates.
- The exclusion is based on SEBI's eligibility criteria review framework for stocks in the derivatives segment.

## Regulatory Changes

This action is taken pursuant to SEBI Circular Ref. No. SEBI/HO/MRD/MRD-PoD-2/P/CIR/2024/116 dated August 30, 2024, which revised the eligibility criteria for stocks to be included in or excluded from the equity derivatives segment. BSE Notice 20240902-3 (September 2, 2024) had outlined the review process for entry/exit of stocks.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that no new positions can be initiated in Dalmia Bharat Ltd F&O contracts beyond the existing expiry months.
- Members should inform their clients about the discontinuation and advise them to manage open positions accordingly before August 28, 2026.
- No new expiry month contracts will be made available; all activity must conclude by the August 27, 2026 expiry.

## Important Dates

- **June 25, 2026** — Expiry of June 2026 contracts
- **July 30, 2026** — Expiry of July 2026 contracts
- **August 27, 2026** — Expiry of August 2026 contracts (final expiry)
- **August 28, 2026** — Effective date of discontinuation; no contracts available for trading from this date

## Impact Assessment

Traders and investors holding open F&O positions in Dalmia Bharat Ltd (DABL) need to plan their exit or rollover strategy before the respective expiry dates. After August 28, 2026, no derivatives exposure in this stock will be possible on BSE. The impact is moderate, primarily affecting market participants who use DABL derivatives for hedging or speculative purposes. Spot market trading in the equity shares (Scrip Code: 542216) remains unaffected.
