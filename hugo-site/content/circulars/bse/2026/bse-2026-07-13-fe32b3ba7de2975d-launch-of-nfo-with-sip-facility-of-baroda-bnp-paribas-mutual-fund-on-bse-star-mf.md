## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that Baroda BNP Paribas Mutual Fund will launch the Baroda BNP Paribas Services Fund as a New Fund Offer (NFO) with SIP facility on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The NFO will be open from July 14, 2026 to July 28, 2026, with a tentative allotment date of August 3, 2026, across six scheme variants (Regular/Direct Plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options).

## Key Points

- Scheme: Baroda BNP Paribas Services Fund (Equity category)
- Six variants offered: Regular Plan (Growth, IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment) and Direct Plan (Growth, IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment)
- ISINs range from INF251K01VU3 to INF251K01VZ2
- Tentative allotment date for all variants: August 3, 2026
- Transactions with funds not initiated by 9:00 AM on T+1 day will be cancelled and reported to RTA/AMC with 'N' flag
- Transactions with funds initiated will remain pending until 3:00 PM on the last day of fund pay-out or T+5 day, whichever is earlier
- Cheque and OTM payments accepted during NFO period, subject to clear funds in ICCL account by 6:00 PM on July 31, 2026
- Switch transactions accepted during NFO period, but switch-out funds received on/after allotment date won't be considered for allotment
- NFO extended till midnight July 28, 2026 for lumpsum purchases only; SIP/XSIP registrations must be authenticated by clients by 3:00 PM on July 28, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an operational/procedural notice regarding a scheme launch, not a regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure clear funds are available in the ICCL account by 6:00 PM on July 31, 2026 for allotment eligibility
- Members must monitor client fund initiation deadlines to avoid transaction cancellation
- SIP/XSIP registrations require client authentication by 3:00 PM on the NFO closure date

## Important Dates

- NFO Open: July 14, 2026
- NFO Close (Lumpsum, extended): Midnight, July 28, 2026
- SIP/XSIP authentication deadline: 3:00 PM, July 28, 2026
- Clear funds deadline in ICCL account: 6:00 PM, July 31, 2026
- Tentative Allotment Date: August 3, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, low-impact operational circular relevant primarily to BSE trading members handling mutual fund transactions on the StAR MF Platform. It does not affect listed equity trading, market surveillance, or broader market operations, and has no direct implications for specific stocks.
