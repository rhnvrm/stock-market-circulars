## Summary

BSE has listed 1,52,68,950 new equity shares of Lloyds Metals and Energy Limited (Scrip Code: 512455, ISIN: INE281B01032) issued to Promoter and Non-Promoter shareholders on a preferential basis through conversion of warrants. These shares are permitted to trade on BSE with effect from Thursday, March 12, 2026.

## Key Points

- 1,52,68,950 equity shares of Rs. 1/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 739/- (issue price Rs. 740/-) on a preferential basis
- Shares issued to both Promoter and Non-Promoter categories via conversion of warrants
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 529090089 to 544359038
- Date of allotment: December 31, 2025
- Trading commences: March 12, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular is a routine listing notification under BSE's equity segment for new securities admitted to trading.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to permit trading of the newly listed shares from March 12, 2026
- Lock-in restrictions must be observed for the specified tranches of shares as detailed below

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** December 31, 2025
- **Trading Effective From:** March 12, 2026
- **Lock-in upto September 30, 2027:** 1,50,00,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 529090089 to 544090088) — Promoter tranche
- **Lock-in upto September 30, 2026:** 2,68,950 shares (Dist. Nos. 544090089 to 544359038) — Non-Promoter tranche

## Impact Assessment

The listing results in a dilution of existing shareholding due to the addition of over 1.52 crore new equity shares at Rs. 740/- per share. The majority of shares (1.50 crore) are locked in until September 2027, limiting near-term selling pressure from promoters. The smaller Non-Promoter tranche of 2,68,950 shares has a shorter lock-in until September 2026. Overall market impact is moderate, with limited free float addition in the short term due to lock-in constraints.
