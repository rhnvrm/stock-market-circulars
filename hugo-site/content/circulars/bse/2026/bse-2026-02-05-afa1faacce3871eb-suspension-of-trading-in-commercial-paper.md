## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260205-3 suspending trading in Commercial Paper issued by Solar Industries India Limited (Scrip Code: 730539, ISIN: INE343H14204) effective February 06, 2026. The suspension is due to the instrument reaching its redemption/maturity date. Trading Members are advised to cease dealing in this Commercial Paper.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Solar Industries India Limited to be suspended from trading
- Scrip Code: 730539
- ISIN: INE343H14204
- Suspension effective from: February 06, 2026
- Reason: Maturity on redemption date
- Notice Reference: DR-815/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for commercial paper maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must not deal in the aforementioned Commercial Paper from February 06, 2026
- Trading Members are required to take note of this suspension and update their systems accordingly
- No trading or transactions permitted in this specific debt instrument after the effective date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: February 05, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date**: February 06, 2026
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: February 09, 2026 (implied as T+2 from suspension date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine administrative action related to the natural maturity of a debt instrument. The suspension affects only holders and traders of this specific Commercial Paper.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Standard procedure for maturing debt securities. Trading Members need to ensure this instrument is removed from their trading systems.

**Investor Impact**: Low - Investors holding this Commercial Paper will receive redemption proceeds on the maturity date as per original terms. No trading impact for equity shares of Solar Industries India Limited.
