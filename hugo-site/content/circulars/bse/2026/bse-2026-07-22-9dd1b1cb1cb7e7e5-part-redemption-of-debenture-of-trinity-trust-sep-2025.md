## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that MITCON Credentia Trusteeship Services Ltd. has notified a reduced face value on partial redemption of Pass-Through Certificates (PTCs) issued under TRINITY TRUST SEP 2025. The reduced face value will apply for trading purposes with effect from July 23, 2026.

## Key Points

- Instrument: TRINITY TRUST SEP 2025 (TTS25-11.75%-21-9-27-PTC)
- ISIN: INE2JU715017
- Scrip Code: 977166
- Purpose: Part Redemption of PTCs
- Reduced Face Value: Rs. 2851.78 per debenture
- Trustee: MITCON Credentia Trusteeship Services Ltd.
- Reference: DR-678/2026-2027

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is an operational notice reflecting a corporate action (partial redemption) already effected by the issuer/trustee.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to take note of the reduced face value and ensure the security is traded accordingly from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 22, 2026
- Effective Date for trading at reduced face value: July 23, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and traders of the TRINITY TRUST SEP 2025 PTC instrument in the debt segment. The reduced face value reflects a partial principal repayment already made to investors, and trading systems/members must update reference data accordingly to avoid mispricing. No broader market or equity impact is expected.
