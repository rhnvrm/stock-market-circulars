## Summary

Total Environment Constructions Pvt Ltd has informed BSE about the part redemption of its debentures (ISIN: INE257Y07049, Scrip Code: 975621). The debentures will trade with a reduced face value of Rs. 92,857 per debenture effective January 16, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: Total Environment Constructions Pvt Ltd
- Debenture: TECPL-13.53%-31-3-29-PVT
- ISIN: INE257Y07049
- Scrip Code: 975621
- New reduced face value: Rs. 92,857 per debenture
- Effective date for trading with reduced face value: January 16, 2026
- Notice reference: DR-800/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a corporate action notification regarding part redemption of debentures.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are required to take note of the reduced face value and ensure that the debentures are traded with the revised face value of Rs. 92,857 per debenture from January 16, 2026 onwards.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 14, 2026
- **Effective Date for Trading with Reduced Face Value**: January 16, 2026
- **Debenture Maturity Date**: March 31, 2029 (as per debenture name)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action with limited market impact. The part redemption affects only the holders of this specific debenture series. Trading members need to update their systems to reflect the new face value. The impact is confined to debt market participants dealing with this particular instrument, with no broader market implications.
