## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260312-34 informs trading members that Achiievers Finance India Limited has fixed 16 March 2026 as the Record Date for redemption and/or interest payment on two Non-Convertible Debentures. Trading members are directed not to deal in these debentures with effect from 16 March 2026.

## Key Points

- Two NCD instruments of Achiievers Finance India Limited are suspended from trading effective 16 March 2026.
- AFIL-25-3-26 (ISIN: INE065507AJ0, Code: 975612) — suspended for Redemption of Debentures.
- AFIL-12%-25-3-26 (ISIN: INE065507AG6, Code: 975616) — suspended for Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest.
- Both suspensions are under DR-840/2025-2026.
- Notice issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President – Listing Compliance and Operations.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice under existing BSE debt market regulations requiring suspension of trading ahead of record dates for corporate actions on debentures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the two listed debenture instruments (ISIN: INE065507AJ0 and INE065507AG6) from 16 March 2026 onwards.
- Members should update their systems to block trading in these securities on and after the record date.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 12 March 2026
- **Record Date / No Dealings From:** 16 March 2026

## Impact Assessment

The suspension affects only holders and potential traders of these two specific NCD instruments. Impact is limited in scope — routine for debt market operations. Investors holding these debentures can expect redemption proceeds and/or interest payments around the record date of 16 March 2026. No broader market impact anticipated.
