## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that new securities issued on a private placement basis have been listed and admitted to trading on the BSE Debt Segment effective July 7, 2026. The annexure details the listing of Commercial Papers from four issuers: Bharat Petroleum Corporation Ltd. (two CP issues), Indian Oil Corporation Ltd., Time Technoplast Limited, and Sharekhan Limited.

## Key Points

- Commercial Papers (CPs) issued by Bharat Petroleum Corpn. Ltd., Indian Oil Corporation Ltd., Time Technoplast Limited, and Sharekhan Limited have been listed on the BSE Debt Segment.
- Securities will be traded only in dematerialised form under the specified ISIN numbers.
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof.
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise.
- BPCL CP (Scrip Code 732043, ISIN INE029A14BV8): Quantity 60,000, Face Value Rs. 500,000, Issue Price Rs. 493,360.50, Rate of Interest 6.14% p.a., allotted 06/07/2026, redemption 24/09/2026, rated CARE A1+/CRISIL A1+.
- BPCL CP (Scrip Code 732044, ISIN INE029A14BW6): Quantity 70,000, Face Value Rs. 500,000, Issue Price Rs. 497,690.00, Rate of Interest 6.05% p.a., allotted 07/07/2026, redemption 04/08/2026, rated CARE A1+/CRISIL A1+.
- IOCL CP (Scrip Code 732046, ISIN INE242A14YX5): Quantity 54,500, Face Value Rs. 500,000, Issue Price Rs. 493,308.00, Rate of Interest 6.11% p.a., allotted 06/07/2026, redemption 25/09/2026, rated ICRA A1+/IND A1+.
- Time Technoplast CP (Scrip Code 732047, ISIN INE508G14JD9): Quantity 160, Face Value Rs. 500,000, Issue Price Rs. 481,697.50, Rate of Interest 7.62% p.a., allotted 07/07/2026, redemption 05/01/2027, rated CRISIL A1+/ICRA A1+.
- Sharekhan Limited CP (Scrip Code 732049, ISIN INE211H14AW7): Quantity 8,000, Face Value Rs. 500,000, Issue Price Rs. 492,490.81, Rate of Interest 7.14% p.a., allotted 06/07/2026, redemption 22/09/2026, rated CRISIL A1+/ICRA A1+.
- Issuing and Paying Agents are State Bank of India (for BPCL and IOCL issues) and IDBI Bank Ltd. (for Time Technoplast issue).

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is a routine administrative notice confirming the listing of privately placed debt securities on the Exchange's Debt Segment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the standard trading denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof, the 1 paise tick size, and that settlement/dematerialised trading applies under the respective ISINs. Queries can be directed to the BSE Debt Department at the listed contact numbers.

## Important Dates

- Listing effective: July 7, 2026
- BPCL CP (BPCL60726): Allotment 06/07/2026, Redemption 24/09/2026
- BPCL CP (BPCL70726): Allotment 07/07/2026, Redemption 04/08/2026
- IOCL CP (IOCL6726): Allotment 06/07/2026, Redemption 25/09/2026
- Time Technoplast CP (TTL7726): Allotment 07/07/2026, Redemption 05/01/2027
- Sharekhan CP (SL6726): Allotment 06/07/2026, Redemption 22/09/2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notice for privately placed commercial papers on the BSE Debt Segment. It has minimal impact on equity market participants but is relevant for debt market/fixed income investors and trading members dealing in these specific CP instruments issued by BPCL, IOCL, Time Technoplast, and Sharekhan.
