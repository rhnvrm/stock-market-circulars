## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for TAAL ENTERPRISES LIMITED (CIN: L74110KA2014PLC176836). The company name has been officially changed to TAAL TECH LIMITED with effect from November 4, 2025. This is an administrative change that does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders.

## Key Points

- Original company name: TAAL ENTERPRISES LIMITED
- New company name: TAAL TECH LIMITED
- Corporate Identification Number (CIN): L74110KA2014PLC176836
- Certificate issued by: Central Processing Centre, ROC
- Approved by: Sweety Kumar, Assistant/Deputy/Registrar of Companies
- Company type: Company limited by shares
- Registered address: AKR Tech Park, 3rd Floor, C Block, Sy # 112 Krishna Reddy Industrial Area, 7th Mile Hosur Road, Singasandra, Bangalore South, Bangalore-560068, Karnataka, India

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard name change procedure under the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014, specifically pursuant to Rule 29.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display both the old name (TAAL ENTERPRISES LIMITED) and new name (TAAL TECH LIMITED) for a period of two years at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- This dual display requirement is mandatory and applies to all locations where company name display is legally required
- All stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company and its directors on the MCA website: www.mca.gov.in/MCA21

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date of Name Change**: November 4, 2025
- **Certificate Issue Date**: November 4, 2025
- **Old Name Display Period**: Two years from November 4, 2025 (until November 4, 2027)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is an administrative name change that does not affect the company's operations, business activities, or financial position.

**Operational Impact**: Low. The company must update stationery, signage, and all official communications to reflect the new name while also displaying the old name for two years.

**Stakeholder Impact**: None to rights and liabilities. The certificate explicitly states that the name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of any stakeholders. All existing contracts, agreements, and obligations remain valid under the new name.
