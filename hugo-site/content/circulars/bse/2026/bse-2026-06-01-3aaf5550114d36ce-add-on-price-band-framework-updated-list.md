## Summary

BSE has updated the list of securities identified for placement under the Add-on Price Band Framework, pursuant to Exchange circular no. 20210811-6 dated August 11, 2021. A periodic review has been carried out by the Surveillance department and the revised list of securities is provided in Annexure I attached to this notice.

## Key Points

- Periodic review conducted by BSE Surveillance (DOSS) to identify securities for the Add-on Price Band Framework.
- The updated list of securities is attached as Annexure I to Notice No. 20260601-31.
- This action is in continuation of the framework established via circular no. 20210811-6 dated August 11, 2021.
- Members may direct clarifications to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced. This circular represents a routine update to the existing Add-on Price Band Framework originally established in August 2021. Securities identified through the review process are added to or removed from the restricted list as applicable.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the updated list of securities placed under the Add-on Price Band Framework.
- Trades in the identified securities will be subject to the additional price band restrictions as specified in the framework.
- Members should refer to Annexure I for the complete and current list of affected scrips.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 01, 2026
- **Effective Date:** June 01, 2026 (applicable as per the framework terms)

## Impact Assessment

Securities included in the Add-on Price Band Framework are subject to tighter price movement limits beyond the standard circuit filters, aimed at curbing excessive volatility or potential manipulation. Traders and investors holding or intending to trade in the listed scrips may face restricted intraday price movement. The impact is limited to the specific securities enumerated in Annexure I and does not affect the broader equity market. This is a standard surveillance measure with moderate operational impact on affected securities.
