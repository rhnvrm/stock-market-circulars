## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 49,53,415 new equity shares of Cyber Media India Ltd. (Scrip Code: 532640) are permitted for trading effective Thursday, March 05, 2026. These shares arise from the conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up equity shares of Re. 1/- each, issued on a rights basis.

## Key Points

- **Company:** Cyber Media India Ltd. (Scrip Code: 532640)
- **Number of New Shares:** 49,53,415 equity shares
- **Nature:** Conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up, issued on rights basis
- **Face Value:** Re. 1/- each
- **ISIN:** INE278G01037
- **Distinctive Numbers:** 15667243 to 20620657
- **Pari-passu Status:** These shares rank equally with existing equity shares of the company

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for new securities arising from a rights issue conversion.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are permitted to trade in these new securities from the effective date.
- Members should update their systems to reflect the new distinctive numbers (15667243 to 20620657) under ISIN INE278G01037.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** March 04, 2026
- **Trading Effective Date:** Thursday, March 05, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The addition of 49,53,415 equity shares increases the total tradeable equity of Cyber Media India Ltd. on BSE. As these shares are pari-passu with existing shares and arise from a rights issue conversion, the dilution impact is already reflected in prior corporate action announcements. Market impact is expected to be moderate, primarily affecting holders of the partly paid shares who can now trade fully paid securities.
