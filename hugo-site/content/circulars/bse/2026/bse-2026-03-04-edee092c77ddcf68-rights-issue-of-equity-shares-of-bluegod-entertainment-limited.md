## Summary

BLUEGOD ENTERTAINMENT LIMITED (BSE Code: 539175) has announced a Rights Issue of equity shares with a Record Date of 06 March 2026. Trading in the company's equity shares must be conducted on an Ex-Rights basis effective 06/03/2026 under Rolling Settlement DR-834/2025-2026.

## Key Points

- Rights Issue ratio: 9 equity shares for every 47 equity shares held
- Face value: Re.1/- per share
- Issue price: Rs.3/- per share (Re.1/- face value + Rs.2/- premium)
- Full issue price of Rs.3/- per share is payable on application
- Ex-Rights date and Record Date are both 06/03/2026
- Settlement Number: DR-834/2025-2026
- Applies to Dematerialised Securities in the Rolling Settlement Segment

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notification in accordance with BSE listing compliance procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must ensure all transactions in BLUEGOD ENTERTAINMENT LIMITED equity shares are executed on an Ex-Rights basis with effect from 06/03/2026
- Members must take note of the record date and settlement number for trade processing purposes

## Important Dates

- **Record Date:** 06 March 2026
- **Ex-Rights Basis Effective From:** 06 March 2026
- **Settlement Number:** DR-834/2025-2026
- **Circular Date:** 04 March 2026

## Impact Assessment

Existing shareholders of BLUEGOD ENTERTAINMENT LIMITED as of the record date (06/03/2026) are entitled to subscribe to 9 new equity shares for every 47 shares held at Rs.3/- per share. Trades executed on or after 06/03/2026 will be on an ex-rights basis, meaning buyers of shares on or after this date will not be entitled to the rights entitlement. The full subscription amount is payable upfront on application. Impact is limited to existing shareholders and trading members handling this scrip.
