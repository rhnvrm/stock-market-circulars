## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading for three Treasury Bills (T-Bills) that are maturing on their respective redemption dates. Trading Members are advised to cease dealing with these specific T-Bills effective January 06, 2026.

## Key Points

- Three T-Bills to be suspended from trading due to maturity
- Suspension effective from January 06, 2026
- Notice issued under reference DR-792/2025-2026
- Trading Members must not deal with the specified T-Bills after the suspension date

## T-Bills Details

**Suspended T-Bills:**

1. **182TB80126**
   - Scrip Code: 805089
   - ISIN: IN002025Y156

2. **364TB80126**
   - Scrip Code: 805012
   - ISIN: IN002024Z396

3. **91TB080126**
   - Scrip Code: 805127
   - ISIN: IN002025X281

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must cease all trading activities in the mentioned T-Bills
- Members should update their systems to reflect the trading suspension
- No new positions should be opened in these securities from January 06, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** January 05, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date:** January 06, 2026
- **Redemption Date:** As per respective T-Bill maturity schedules

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice with limited market impact. The suspension is standard procedure for maturing debt securities. Trading Members holding positions in these T-Bills should be aware of the redemption process and ensure settlement of their positions. The impact is limited to specific T-Bill holders and does not affect broader market operations.
