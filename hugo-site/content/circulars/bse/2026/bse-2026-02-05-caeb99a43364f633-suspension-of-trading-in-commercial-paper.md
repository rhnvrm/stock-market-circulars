## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Commercial Paper issued by Solar Industries India Limited (Scrip Code: 730539, ISIN: INE343H14204) effective February 6, 2026, as the instrument is reaching its maturity/redemption date. Trading members are advised not to deal in this Commercial Paper from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Solar Industries India Limited to be suspended from trading
- Scrip Code: 730539
- ISIN: INE343H14204
- Suspension effective date: February 6, 2026
- Reason: Maturity on redemption date
- Notice Number: 20260205-3
- Document Reference: DR-815/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard procedural action for maturing debt instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the aforementioned Commercial Paper of Solar Industries India Limited from February 6, 2026
- Trading members are required to take note of this suspension and ensure compliance

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: February 5, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date**: February 6, 2026
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: February 9, 2026 (inferred from CP naming convention SIIL-9-2-26-CP)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine administrative action affecting only holders of this specific Commercial Paper instrument.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Standard maturity procedure for debt instruments. Only affects trading members and investors holding this particular Commercial Paper.

**Segment Affected**: Debt market segment only.

**Investor Action**: Holders of this Commercial Paper will receive redemption proceeds on the maturity date as per the instrument terms.
