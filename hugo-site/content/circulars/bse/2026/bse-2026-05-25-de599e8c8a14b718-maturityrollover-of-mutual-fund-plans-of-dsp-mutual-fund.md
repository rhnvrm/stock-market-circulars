## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that DSP Mutual Fund has fixed the record date for the maturity/rollover of DSP FMP - Series 268 - 1281 Days under five plan variants. The record date and ex-date are both set for 26 May 2026. Trading members are advised not to deal in these mutual fund plans.

## Key Points

- Five DSP FMP Series 268 - 1281 Days scheme variants are reaching maturity/rollover
- Record date and ex-date for all five plans: 26 May 2026
- Settlement DR: 638/2026-2027 for all plans
- Trading members must refrain from dealing in these plans as per the notice
- Notice issued by Marian Dsouza, AVP – Listing Compliance and Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine corporate action notification for the scheduled maturity/rollover of fixed maturity plans.

Trading members of BSE are advised not to deal in the following DSP Mutual Fund plans from the specified dates:

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 25 May 2026
- **Record Date / Ex Date:** 26 May 2026 (all five plans)
- **Settlement DR:** 638/2026-2027

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited to existing unitholders of these five DSP FMP Series 268 - 1281 Days plans. As fixed maturity plans reaching their scheduled tenure, this is an expected lifecycle event. Trading members must observe the trading restriction from the ex-date. No broader market or systemic impact is anticipated.
