## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 12,85,71,428 new equity shares of Restaurant Brands Asia Limited (Scrip Code: 543248), each of face value Rs. 10, issued at a premium of Rs. 60 to promoters on a preferential basis, are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Thursday, July 16, 2026. These shares rank pari-passu with the company's existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 543248 (Restaurant Brands Asia Limited)
- Number of shares listed: 12,85,71,428 equity shares
- Face value: Rs. 10 per share
- Issue price: Rs. 70.00 per share (premium of Rs. 60/share)
- Allotment basis: Preferential allotment to Promoters
- Distinctive numbers: 582876288 to 711447715
- ISIN: INE07T201019
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is a routine listing approval notice issued by BSE's Listing Operations Department under the Company-related category for the Equity segment.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific action required from trading members or investors beyond updating trading systems to reflect the enlarged equity base of the company effective the listing date.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: 02/06/2026
- Listing and trading effective date: Thursday, July 16, 2026
- Lock-in expiry for the newly allotted shares: 16/01/2028

## Impact Assessment

The listing increases Restaurant Brands Asia Limited's total equity share capital through a preferential issue to promoters, which may modestly increase promoter shareholding and could have a marginal dilutive effect on existing public shareholders' percentage holding. Since the shares are locked in until January 16, 2028, there is no immediate free-float or trading supply impact. Market impact is limited to updated equity base calculations for the stock.
