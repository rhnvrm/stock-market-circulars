## Summary

OM Power Transmission Limited (BSE ticker: 544750) was listed on BSE on April 17, 2026, and will be added to the BSE IPO Index effective at the open of Tuesday, April 21, 2026. This follows Notice No. 20260416-40 and is a standard index inclusion for newly listed IPO stocks.

## Key Points

- OM Power Transmission Limited (ticker: 544750) listed on BSE on April 17, 2026
- Stock to be added to the BSE IPO Index effective April 21, 2026 (market open)
- Corporate action included in the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE) for April 17, 2026
- Queries to be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard index composition update by BSE Index Services Private Ltd.

## Compliance Requirements

No direct compliance requirements for market participants. Index-tracking funds and ETFs benchmarked to the BSE IPO Index should update their portfolios to include 544750 by the open of April 21, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **April 17, 2026**: Listing of OM Power Transmission Limited on BSE; corporate action file updated
- **April 21, 2026**: Effective date for addition of 544750 to the BSE IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. The addition is a routine index rebalancing triggered by a new IPO listing. Passive funds and ETFs tracking the BSE IPO Index will need to incorporate this stock. No broader regulatory or operational impact on the wider market is anticipated.
