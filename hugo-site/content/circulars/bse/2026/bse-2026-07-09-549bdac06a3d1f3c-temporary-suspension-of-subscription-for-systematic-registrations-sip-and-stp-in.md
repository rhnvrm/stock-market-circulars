## Summary

BSE has issued an addendum to its earlier Notice No. 20260608-9 dated June 08, 2026, informing members that Franklin Templeton Mutual Fund has temporarily suspended subscriptions through Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) and Systematic Transfer Plan (STP-In) for two of its schemes, effective July 09, 2026. This change has been implemented on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Key Points

- Franklin Templeton Mutual Fund has temporarily suspended SIP and STP-In investments for select schemes.
- Affected schemes: Franklin U.S. Opportunities Equity Active Fund of Funds and Franklin Asian Equity Fund.
- Fresh SIP/STP-In registrations will not be accepted after the cut-off time on July 08, 2026.
- Existing SIP/XSIP and STP-In registrations made prior to the effective date will continue to be processed by the AMC/RTA without change.
- Redemption, Switch-out, and STP-out transactions remain permitted without any amount restriction.
- The suspension has been implemented on the BSE StAR MF Platform per the Notice-cum-Addendum issued by Franklin Templeton AMC.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory (SEBI/exchange) rule change; this is an operational update reflecting the AMC's own decision to suspend systematic subscriptions in specific schemes, communicated via exchange notice for member compliance.

## Compliance Requirements

BSE StAR MF Platform members and distributors must:
- Stop accepting fresh SIP and STP-In registrations for the two named schemes after the July 08, 2026 cut-off.
- Continue processing existing systematic registrations without alteration.
- Ensure redemption, switch-out, and STP-out transactions are processed normally without amount restrictions.
- Refer to the attached Notice-cum-Addendum from Franklin Templeton AMC for full details.

## Important Dates

- June 08, 2026: Original notice (20260608-9) regarding temporary suspension.
- July 08, 2026: Cut-off after which fresh SIP/STP-In registrations are not accepted.
- July 09, 2026: Effective date of suspension on BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to distributors, RTAs, and investors dealing in the two specified Franklin Templeton schemes on the BSE StAR MF platform. Existing systematic investment commitments continue uninterrupted, and investors retain full redemption/switch-out flexibility, minimizing disruption. No impact on listed equities or broader trading segments.
