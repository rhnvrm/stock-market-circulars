## Summary

ICCL Ltd. has issued Notice No. 20260513-27 dated 13 May 2026, informing trading members of corporate actions applicable to securities in the Securities Lending and Borrowing (SLB) segment. The specific details of affected securities and the nature of the corporate actions are contained in the attached annexure.

## Key Points

- Notice issued by ICCL Ltd. (Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd.) on 13 May 2026
- Pertains to the SLB (Securities Lending and Borrowing) segment
- Trading members are directed to refer to the annexure for the list of affected securities and corporate action details
- Issued under the Corporate Actions category by the ICCL department

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This notice is an operational communication regarding the processing of corporate actions within the existing SLB framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members participating in the SLB segment must review the attached annexure for details of the corporate actions
- Members should account for any adjustments to open SLB positions resulting from the corporate actions described in the annexure

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 13 May 2026
- Specific effective dates for corporate actions are listed in the annexure (not reproduced in the notice body)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has a moderate operational impact on trading members active in the SLB segment. Corporate actions such as dividends, splits, rights issues, or mergers can affect open lending/borrowing positions and require corresponding adjustments. Members with open SLB positions in the affected securities should review the annexure promptly to manage position risk and settlement obligations.
