## Summary

BSE has issued a notice announcing the opening of an Offer to Buy through the Acquisition Window (Takeover) mechanism for Rikhav Securities Limited. The open offer is made by Acquirers M/s. B.D. Lakhani, M/s. B.N. Lakhani, M/s. H.D. Lakhani, and M/s. N.D. Lakhani, together with Persons Acting in Concert (PACs) Mr. Hitesh Himmatlal Lakhani, Mr. Deep Hitesh Lakhani, Mrs. Vaishali Rajendra Shah, and Mrs. Bharati Hitesh Lakhani, to the public shareholders of Rikhav Securities Limited (Target Company).

## Key Points

- Offer price: Rs. 47.75 (Rupees Forty Seven and Seventy Five Paisa) per fully paid-up equity share/voting right, determined under Regulation 8(2) of the SEBI (SAST) Regulations.
- Offer size: up to 99,55,920 fully paid-up equity shares of face value Rs. 5/- each, representing 26% of the total voting share capital of the Target Company on a fully diluted basis.
- The offer is made pursuant to Regulations 3(2) and 3(3) of the SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations.
- If tendered shares exceed the offer size, acceptance will be on a proportionate basis in consultation with the Manager to the Offer, ensuring no non-marketable lots and no acceptance below the minimum marketable lot.
- The mechanism follows SEBI circulars CIR/CFD/POLICYCELL/1/2015 (April 13, 2015) and CFD/DCR2/CIR/P/2016/131 (Dec 9, 2016), along with subsequent BSE guideline notices.
- Letter of Offer is attached to the notice for market participants' review.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; the notice operationalizes existing SEBI SAST Regulations and the Exchange's revised guidelines for acquisition of shares via the Stock Exchange Acquisition Window mechanism for tender-offer based takeovers.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members and Custodians must note the offer mechanics and ensure tendering of shares by eligible public shareholders is processed per the Revised Guidelines under the Acquisition Window, including proportionate acceptance rules and marketable lot requirements.

## Important Dates

- Offer period: July 24, 2026 (Friday) to August 06, 2026 (Thursday)
- Assumed payment/consideration date: August 20, 2026 (Thursday)
- Notice date: July 22, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The open offer directly affects public shareholders of Rikhav Securities Limited, providing an exit opportunity at a fixed price for up to 26% of the company's voting share capital. Trading Members and Custodians need to be prepared to facilitate tendering through the Acquisition Window during the offer period. Broader market impact is limited, as this is a company-specific takeover-related corporate action rather than a systemic or regulatory change.
