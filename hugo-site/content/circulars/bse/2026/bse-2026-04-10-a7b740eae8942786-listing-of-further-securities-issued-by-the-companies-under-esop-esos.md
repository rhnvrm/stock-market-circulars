## Summary

BSE has notified that 28,482 further equity shares of Dev Information Technology Limited (Scrip Code: 543462, ISIN: INE060X01034) issued under the Employee Stock Option Plan (ESOP) / Employee Stock Option Scheme (ESOS) are listed and admitted for trading on BSE with effect from Monday, April 13, 2026.

## Key Points

- 28,482 new equity shares of Dev Information Technology Limited are listed for trading effective April 13, 2026.
- ISIN: INE060X01034 | Scrip Code: 543462
- Face value of each share: Rs. 2
- Distribution certificate numbers: 56335233 to 56363714
- No lock-in period applies to these shares (Lock-in: NA)
- Issued under ESOP/ESOS scheme

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification under BSE's standard process for ESOP/ESOS-issued securities.

## Compliance Requirements

All market participants are required to take note of the new securities listed for trading. No additional compliance action is required from general market participants.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** April 10, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** Monday, April 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The allotment of 28,482 shares is a small addition to Dev Information Technology Limited's existing share capital. As there is no lock-in, the shares are immediately tradable from April 13, 2026. This is a standard corporate action arising from employee stock options and does not affect broader market operations or other listed companies.
