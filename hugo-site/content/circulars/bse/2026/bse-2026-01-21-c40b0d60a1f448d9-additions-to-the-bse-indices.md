## Summary

BSE announces the addition of four newly listed companies to its indices. AMAGI MEDIA LABS LIMITED (ticker 544679) will be added to the BSE IPO index effective January 23, 2026. Three SME companies - NARMADESH BRASS INDUSTRIES LIMITED (544680), INDO SMC LIMITED (544681), and GRE RENEW ENERTECH LIMITED (544682) - will be added to the BSE SME IPO index effective January 22, 2026.

## Key Points

- AMAGI MEDIA LABS LIMITED listed on BSE on January 21, 2026
- AMAGI MEDIA LABS to be added to BSE IPO index at market open on January 23, 2026
- Three SME companies listed on BSE SME platform on January 21, 2026
- NARMADESH BRASS INDUSTRIES, INDO SMC, and GRE RENEW ENERTECH to be added to BSE SME IPO index at market open on January 22, 2026
- Changes included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- Queries can be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index maintenance activity following new listings.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements. Market participants tracking these indices should note the additions for portfolio rebalancing purposes.

## Important Dates

- **January 21, 2026**: Listing date for all four companies on BSE
- **January 22, 2026**: Three SME companies added to BSE SME IPO index at market open
- **January 23, 2026**: AMAGI MEDIA LABS added to BSE IPO index at market open

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine administrative action reflecting the inclusion of newly listed companies into their respective benchmark indices. Index funds and ETFs tracking the BSE IPO and BSE SME IPO indices will need to acquire these securities as part of their rebalancing activities. The staggered effective dates (January 22 for SME stocks, January 23 for AMAGI MEDIA LABS) allow for orderly index maintenance.
