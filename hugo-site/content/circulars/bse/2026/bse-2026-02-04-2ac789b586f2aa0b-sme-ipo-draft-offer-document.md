## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of Panchatv Bharat Limited filed on February 02, 2026 on its SME website for public comments. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and will remain available for at least 21 days.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Panchatv Bharat Limited on February 02, 2026
- Document contains all disclosures required under Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website: www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public comments invited within 21 days from notice date
- Comments to be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard notification as per existing ICDR Regulations requirements for draft offer documents.

## Compliance Requirements

- Investors and market participants may review the Draft Offer Document on BSE SME website
- Comments, if any, must be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com within 21 days from February 04, 2026
- Company must host the Draft Offer Document for minimum 21-day public comment period

## Important Dates

- **Draft Offer Document Filing Date**: February 02, 2026
- **Notice Date**: February 04, 2026
- **Public Comment Period**: 21 days from February 04, 2026 (deadline: February 25, 2026)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to SME segment investors interested in Panchatv Bharat Limited IPO. Standard procedural notification with no immediate trading impact.

**Stakeholder Impact**: Affects potential investors, merchant bankers, and the issuer company. Provides opportunity for public scrutiny before final offer document.

**Operational Impact**: Standard IPO process continuation. Comments received may lead to modifications in final offer document before IPO launch.
