## Summary

CWD Limited (Scrip Code: 543378) has submitted details to BSE regarding its proposed bonus share issue. The company will allot 1,97,07,776 bonus equity shares with distinctive numbers ranging from 43,94,957 to 2,41,02,732. The deemed date of allotment is January 5, 2026, with effective listing scheduled for January 6, 2026. The submission is made in compliance with SEBI LODR Regulations, 2015 and SEBI Circular No. SEBI/HO/CFD/PoD/2024/122 dated September 16, 2024, subject to receipt of in-principle approval from BSE Limited.

## Key Points

- Total bonus shares to be allotted: 1,97,07,776 equity shares
- Distinctive number range: 43,94,957 to 2,41,02,732
- ISIN: INE0H8H01019
- Bonus issue subject to in-principle approval from BSE Limited
- Submission made pursuant to record date fixed for bonus share issue

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. The circular is a compliance submission under existing SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015, read with SEBI Circular No. SEBI/HO/CFD/PoD/2024/122 dated September 16, 2024.

## Compliance Requirements

- CWD Limited has submitted requisite details of bonus issue as per regulatory requirements
- Company confirms information furnished is true, correct, and complete
- Submission made in accordance with approvals granted by Board of Directors
- Compliance with SEBI LODR Regulations and applicable SEBI circulars maintained

## Important Dates

- **Deemed Date of Allotment**: January 5, 2026
- **Effective Listing Date**: January 6, 2026
- **Circular Date**: January 1, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Shareholder Impact**: Existing shareholders will receive bonus shares as per the approved ratio, increasing their total shareholding without additional investment. The total number of outstanding shares will increase by 1,97,07,776 equity shares.

**Market Impact**: Limited immediate market impact expected. Bonus issues typically result in proportional reduction in share price while maintaining overall market capitalization. Trading will commence for bonus shares from the effective listing date of January 6, 2026.

**Liquidity Impact**: Increased share count may improve liquidity in the scrip, making it more accessible to retail investors due to lower per-share price post-bonus.

**Corporate Action**: This is a positive corporate action indicating company's confidence and desire to reward shareholders while improving share liquidity.
