## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of GRE RENEW ENERTECH LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544682), currently trading in the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group), will be transferred to the Rolling segment and shifted to M Group effective February 04, 2026. This follows the company's SME IPO listing.

## Key Points

- Company: GRE RENEW ENERTECH LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544682
- Segment: SME
- Current Group: MT Group (Trade for Trade segment)
- New Group: M Group (Rolling segment)
- Effective Date: Wednesday, February 04, 2026
- This is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20260120-47 dated January 20, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

The trading mechanism for GRE RENEW ENERTECH LIMITED will transition from Trade for Trade (T2T) settlement to Rolling settlement. In T2T, every trade results in delivery with no intraday trading allowed, while rolling settlement permits normal intraday trading and T+1 settlement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must prepare systems for the segment change
- Orders and positions should be adjusted for the new trading mechanism
- Trading members can contact Mr. Nitinkumar Pujari (Tel: 022-2272 8398) for further details

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: January 21, 2026
- Effective Date: February 04, 2026
- Previous Notice Reference: January 20, 2026 (Notice No. 20260120-47)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** The transition from T2T to rolling settlement typically indicates improved compliance or reduced surveillance concerns. This change will enhance liquidity and trading flexibility for GRE RENEW ENERTECH LIMITED shares, allowing intraday trading and potentially attracting more investor participation. The move is positive for existing shareholders and the company's market profile.
