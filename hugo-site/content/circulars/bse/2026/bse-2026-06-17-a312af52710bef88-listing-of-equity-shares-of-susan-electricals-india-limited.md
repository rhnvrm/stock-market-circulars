## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the equity shares of Susan Electricals India Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange effective Thursday, June 18, 2026, under the 'MT' Group of Securities. In accordance with SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012, the scrip will trade in the Trade-for-Trade segment for the first 10 trading days.

## Key Points

- **Listing Date:** June 18, 2026 (Thursday)
- **Scrip Code:** 544793 | **Scrip ID:** SUSAN
- **ISIN:** INE2L0V01019
- **Group:** MT (SME segment)
- **Total Shares:** 2,03,30,795 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each, fully paid up
- **Distinctive Numbers:** 1 to 20330795
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 127/- per share (Face Value Rs. 10/- + Premium Rs. 117/-)
- **Market Lot:** 1,000 equity shares
- **Date of Allotment:** June 16, 2026
- Trading will be in dematerialised form only
- The scrip will participate in the Special Pre-open Session (SPOS) on June 18, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The listing follows existing SEBI guidelines:
- SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 (January 20, 2012): Trade-for-Trade segment for 10 trading days
- SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000: Mandatory dematerialised trading
- Exchange notice 20120216-29 (February 16, 2012): Special Pre-open Session for IPOs & Relisted Scrips

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade this scrip **only in dematerialised form**
- Trades must be executed in minimum market lots of **1,000 equity shares**
- Any change to the market lot will be communicated with at least one month's prior notice
- Trading members should note the Trade-for-Trade restriction for the first 10 trading days from listing
- Lock-in details are as per Annexure I; Shareholding Pattern is as per Annexure II attached to the notice

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|

| Listing & Trading Commencement | June 18, 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard SME IPO listing on BSE. The listing of Susan Electricals India Limited (a New Delhi-based company) at Rs. 127/- per share introduces a new SME scrip to the market. The 10-day Trade-for-Trade restriction limits speculative activity in the initial period, ensuring genuine delivery-based trades. The minimum market lot of 1,000 shares restricts retail participation to a minimum investment of Rs. 1,27,000/-. Market Maker Mansi Share and Stock Broking Private Limited is designated to provide liquidity for the scrip as per SME listing norms. Impact is limited to trading members operating in the SME segment and investors who participated in the IPO.
