## Summary

BSE has published the schedule for Registrar and Transfer Agent (RTA) visits to its offices during February 2026. This circular lists companies whose share registry is maintained by Adroit Corporate Services Pvt. Ltd. The RTA representatives will be available at BSE premises to address investor grievances and provide registry-related services.

## Key Points

- RTA visit schedule for February 2026 at BSE offices
- Adroit Corporate Services Pvt. Ltd. is the featured RTA
- List includes 51+ companies serviced by Adroit Corporate
- Companies span multiple sectors including finance, infrastructure, manufacturing, textiles, and technology
- Investors can meet RTA representatives for share transfer, dematerialization, dividend, and other registry matters

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine administrative notification.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance requirements for listed entities. This is an informational circular for investor benefit.

## Important Dates

- Visit Period: February 2026 (specific dates to be communicated separately or available on BSE website)

## Impact Assessment

**Investor Impact:** Positive - provides convenient access to RTA services at BSE premises for grievance resolution

**Market Impact:** Nil - purely administrative in nature

**Operational Impact:** Facilitates investor servicing and complaint resolution for shareholders of the 51+ listed companies maintained by Adroit Corporate Services

## Companies Covered

The circular covers companies including Antariksh Industries, Anand Projects, IndiaNivesh, Marathon Nextgen Realty, Hind Rectifiers, Permanent Magnets, Flomic Global Logistics, Maharashtra Corporation, Arihant Superstructures, LKP Finance, Securekloud Technologies, Safari Industries India, and approximately 40+ other listed entities serviced by Adroit Corporate Services Pvt. Ltd.
