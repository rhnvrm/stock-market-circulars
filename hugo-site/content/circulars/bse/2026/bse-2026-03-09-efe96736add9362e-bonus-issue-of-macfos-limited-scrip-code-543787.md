## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that MACFOS LIMITED (Scrip Code: 543787) has fixed **March 10, 2026** as the record date for a bonus issue of equity shares. Trading in the scrip will be on an ex-bonus basis from March 10, 2026 under Settlement No. DR-836/2025-2026. A total of 941,682 bonus equity shares are to be allotted on March 11, 2026.

## Key Points

- Bonus ratio: **1 bonus equity share of Rs. 10/- for every 10 existing equity shares of Rs. 10/- held**
- Record date: **March 10, 2026**
- Ex-bonus effective date: **March 10, 2026** (Settlement No. DR-836/2025-2026)
- Total bonus shares to be allotted: **941,682 equity shares** on March 11, 2026
- Market lot revised from **150 to 165** effective March 10, 2026
- Segment: Dematerialised Securities – Rolling Settlement

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification in accordance with BSE listing and trading norms for bonus issues.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the ex-bonus trading effective date of March 10, 2026 and update their systems accordingly.
- The revised market lot of **165** (up from 150) for Scrip Code 543787 must be applied from March 10, 2026.
- Members should refer to the company's letter dated March 7, 2026 for further details on share allotment.

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Record Date | March 10, 2026 |
| Ex-Bonus Effective Date | March 10, 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

This bonus issue increases the share count by 10% for existing shareholders, which will proportionally reduce the share price on the ex-bonus date. The market lot revision from 150 to 165 reflects the post-bonus adjusted lot size. Impact is moderate and limited to holders of MACFOS LIMITED shares and trading members handling transactions in this scrip. No broader market or systemic impact is anticipated.
