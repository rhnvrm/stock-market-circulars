## Summary

BSE has issued a notice, in continuation of its earlier notice dated January 31, 2022, identifying securities that meet the criteria for high Promoter and non-Promoter 'Encumbrance' under Regulation 28(3) of the SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 2011. Securities newly meeting the criteria will attract a minimum 75% margin in the Equity and Equity Derivatives segments, while some previously flagged securities are eligible to move out of the framework.

## Key Points

- Securities listed in Annexure I have satisfied the inclusion criteria under the high encumbrance measure.
- These securities will attract a minimum 75% margin in the Equity and Equity Derivatives segment effective July 16, 2026.
- The margin applies to all open positions as of July 15, 2026, and to new positions created from July 16, 2026 onward.
- Securities listed in Annexure II are eligible to exit the framework effective July 14, 2026.
- A consolidated list of all securities currently under the framework is provided in Annexure III.
- This measure operates in conjunction with other surveillance measures already in force and will be subject to periodic review.
- Inclusion in this list should not be construed as an adverse action against the concerned company or entity.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced; this is an operational update applying the existing framework established under Exchange notice no. 20220131-43 (January 31, 2022), pursuant to Regulation 28(3) of SEBI (SAST) Regulations, 2011, which governs promoter and non-promoter share encumbrance disclosures.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members must apply the minimum 75% margin requirement to all identified securities in Equity and Equity Derivatives segments, covering both existing open positions and new positions from the effective date. Members should refer to Annexures I, II, and III for the specific list of securities added, removed, and currently included in the framework. Queries can be directed to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- July 14, 2026: Securities in Annexure II move out of the framework.
- July 15, 2026: Reference date for open positions subject to the new margin.
- July 16, 2026: Minimum 75% margin becomes effective for securities in Annexure I, applying to open positions as of July 15 and new positions from this date.

## Impact Assessment

The increased margin requirement (minimum 75%) will raise the cost of trading and reduce leverage available for the affected securities, likely dampening trading volumes and liquidity in these names. This measure is targeted at specific companies with high promoter/non-promoter share encumbrance and does not have broad market-wide implications, though it may signal elevated risk perception for the flagged entities.
