## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the commercial paper issued by UGRO Capital Limited (Scrip Code: 731388, ISIN: INE583D14840) is maturing on June 18, 2026. Accordingly, trading in this instrument has been suspended effective June 17, 2026.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of UGRO Capital Limited (UCL-18-06-26-CP) will mature on June 18, 2026.
- Scrip Code: 731388; ISIN: INE583D14840.
- Trading in the above commercial paper is prohibited from June 17, 2026 onwards.
- Reference number: DR-653/2026-2027.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a routine suspension tied to the natural maturity of the commercial paper instrument.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are advised not to deal in the commercial paper of UGRO Capital Limited (ISIN: INE583D14840) with effect from June 17, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **June 16, 2026**: Notice issued by BSE.
- **June 17, 2026**: Suspension of trading effective from this date.
- **June 18, 2026**: Redemption/maturity date of the commercial paper.

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited to holders and potential traders of this specific commercial paper instrument. The suspension is a standard pre-maturity action ensuring orderly winding down of the instrument. No broader market or systemic impact is expected.
