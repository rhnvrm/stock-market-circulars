## Summary

BSE has notified members of a revision in the minimum application amount for Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) in Daily Frequency under Motilal Oswal Active Momentum Fund, communicated by Motilal Oswal Mutual Fund via a Notice-cum-Addendum. The revised minimum SIP installment amount has been implemented on the BSE StAR MF Platform with immediate effect.

## Key Points

- The change pertains to the SIP Minimum Installment Amount for Daily Frequency SIP under Motilal Oswal Active Momentum Fund.
- Existing minimum application amount: Rs. 100/- and multiples of Re. 1/- thereafter.
- Revised minimum application amount: Rs. 21/- and multiples of Re. 1/- thereafter.
- The change has been duly implemented on the BSE StAR MF Platform as per the Notice-cum-Addendum issued by Motilal Oswal AMC.
- Notice signed by Arunkumar Ganesan (VP - Mutual Fund) and Mahesh Tare (AVP - Mutual Fund).

## Regulatory Changes

This is an operational/product change communicated by the AMC (Motilal Oswal Mutual Fund) and passed through by BSE for implementation on its StAR MF distribution platform; it is not a SEBI or BSE regulatory policy change.

## Compliance Requirements

BSE StAR MF members, distributors, and platforms transacting in Motilal Oswal Active Momentum Fund SIPs should update their systems/processes to reflect the revised minimum Daily SIP installment amount of Rs. 21/- (and multiples of Re. 1/- thereafter) instead of the earlier Rs. 100/-.

## Important Dates

Effective immediately from the date of the notice, 01 Jul 2026.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to investors and distributors dealing in Motilal Oswal Active Momentum Fund's Daily Frequency SIP, lowering the entry threshold for daily SIP investments and potentially increasing accessibility for smaller investors. There is no impact on listed equity securities or broader market operations.
