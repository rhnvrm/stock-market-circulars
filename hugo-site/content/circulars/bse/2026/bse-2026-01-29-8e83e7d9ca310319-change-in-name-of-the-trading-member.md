## Summary

BSE has notified all trading members about the name change of trading member JAGRUTI SECURITIES LTD. (Member No. 294). The entity will be renamed to "JAGRUTI SECURITIES PRIVATE LIMITED" effective Friday, January 30, 2026. This is an administrative change related to the corporate structure of the trading member.

## Key Points

- Trading Member: JAGRUTI SECURITIES LTD. (Member No. 294)
- New Name: JAGRUTI SECURITIES PRIVATE LIMITED
- Effective Date: January 30, 2026
- Notice Number: 20260129-2
- Contact for clarifications: membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a corporate name change from a public limited company structure to a private limited company structure.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance actions required from other trading members. This is an informational notice to update records and systems with the new entity name effective from the specified date.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 29, 2026
- **Effective Date**: January 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal to no impact on market operations or other trading members. This is a routine administrative change reflecting the corporate restructuring of the trading member entity. Trading member number (294) remains unchanged, ensuring continuity of operations and identification in BSE systems.
