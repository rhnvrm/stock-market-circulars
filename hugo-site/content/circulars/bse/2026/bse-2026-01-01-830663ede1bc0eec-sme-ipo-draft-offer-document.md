## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of Fly-Hi Maritime Travels Limited filed on December 31, 2025. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and is hosted on the SME website for public comments for a minimum period of 21 days.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Fly-Hi Maritime Travels Limited on December 31, 2025
- Document contains all disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website at www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public comments invited for 21 days from notice date
- Comments to be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a procedural notice in compliance with existing ICDR Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public and stakeholders can review the Draft Offer Document and submit comments within 21 days from January 01, 2026
- Comments should be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Document must remain available on the exchange website for at least 21 days as per regulatory requirements

## Important Dates

- **Draft Offer Document Filing Date:** December 31, 2025
- **Notice Date:** January 01, 2026
- **Public Comment Period:** 21 days from January 01, 2026 (ending approximately January 22, 2026)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal market impact and is primarily informational. It follows standard regulatory procedure for SME IPOs where draft offer documents are made publicly available for comments before finalization. The impact is limited to prospective investors and stakeholders interested in the Fly-Hi Maritime Travels Limited IPO. No immediate action required by existing market participants or listed entities.
