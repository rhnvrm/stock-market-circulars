## Summary

BSE has listed 333,700 new equity shares of Kanpur Plastipack Limited (Scrip Code: 507779) issued on preferential basis to promoters. The shares will be available for trading from January 20, 2026, with a lock-in period until July 31, 2027.

## Key Points

- 333,700 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 192.61/-
- Issue price: Rs. 202.61/- per share
- Allotted to promoters on preferential basis
- Shares ranking pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distinctive Numbers: 23240840 to 23574539
- ISIN: INE694E01014

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the new securities are available for trading from January 20, 2026, subject to lock-in restrictions.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: November 1, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: January 20, 2026
- **Lock-in Period Ends**: July 31, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as the shares are part of a preferential allotment to promoters with a lock-in period extending to July 2027. The shares will not be available for immediate trading by the allottees, ensuring no dilution pressure on existing shareholders in the near term.
