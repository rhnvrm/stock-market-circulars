## Summary

BSE has issued a notice to all trading members providing an update on the SAT (Securities Appellate Tribunal) order concerning front-running by Ashok Maheshwari and others in respect of Darshan Bakul Shah. This is a continuation of earlier Exchange notices dated April 27, 2026 (No. 20260427-33) and July 13, 2026 (No. 20260713-18). SAT had earlier ordered, via its July 3, 2026 order, that the impugned order remain stayed for Noticee No. 2 subject to deposit of 100% of the penalty amount within four weeks, with the deposited amount to be placed in an interest-bearing account by SEBI. SEBI has now confirmed via email dated July 15, 2026 that Mr. Darshan Bakul Shah has paid the penalty amount of Rs. 25,00,000.

## Key Points

- Matter relates to alleged front-running by Ashok Maheshwari and others in respect of Darshan Bakul Shah.
- SAT order dated July 3, 2026 stayed the impugned order for Noticee No. 2, conditional on 100% penalty deposit within four weeks.
- Deposited penalty amount to be held in an interest-bearing account by SEBI pending further proceedings.
- SEBI confirmed via email dated July 15, 2026 that Darshan Bakul Shah paid Rs. 25,00,000 penalty.
- Notice issued in continuation of prior Exchange notices dated April 27, 2026 and July 13, 2026.
- Signed by Poonam Pisat (Assistant Vice President, Investigation) and Vinayak Bhalavi (Deputy Manager, Investigation).

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a procedural update confirming compliance with a prior SAT stay order conditioned on penalty payment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are advised to take note of the penalty payment confirmation and ensure compliance with related record-keeping and monitoring requirements as applicable to front-running surveillance matters.

## Important Dates

- April 27, 2026: Original Exchange Notice No. 20260427-33.
- July 3, 2026: SAT order staying the impugned order subject to penalty deposit.
- July 13, 2026: Exchange Notice No. 20260713-18 (continuation).
- July 15, 2026: SEBI email confirming penalty payment of Rs. 25,00,000 by Darshan Bakul Shah; current BSE notice issued.

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal direct market impact as it pertains to an individual enforcement/compliance matter rather than a listed company or security. It serves as an informational and record-keeping update for trading members regarding ongoing SEBI/SAT proceedings on front-running, with no immediate trading, listing, or disclosure implications for the broader market.
