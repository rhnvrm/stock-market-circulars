## Summary

BSE has updated the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework under Notice No. 20260608-33. New securities listed in Annexure I will attract a minimum 100% margin requirement effective June 10, 2026, on all open positions as on June 09, 2026 and on new positions created thereafter. Qualifying securities will also be shifted from Rolling Settlement to Trade-for-Trade segment from June 10, 2026.

## Key Points

- Securities in Annexure I will attract minimum 100% margin w.e.f. June 10, 2026 on all open and new positions
- Securities qualifying under ESM will move from Rolling Settlement to Trade-for-Trade segment w.e.f. June 10, 2026
- Some securities will be moved to a lower Stage (Stage I) under ESM framework w.e.f. June 09, 2026
- Securities shifting to Stage II will be under Trade for Trade with a 2% price band and Periodic Call Auction w.e.f. June 09, 2026
- Securities exiting the ESM framework are listed in Annexure II, effective June 09, 2026
- A consolidated list of all securities under the ESM framework is provided in Annexure III
- Placement under ESM is purely for market surveillance and should not be construed as adverse action against the company
- Price bands for securities exiting ESM will revert to pre-ESM levels, unless subject to another surveillance measure

## Regulatory Changes

This notice references and updates the ESM framework established under prior notices:
- Notice no. 20230602-44 dated June 02, 2023
- Notice no. 20230718-46 dated July 18, 2023
- Notice no. 20240809-42 dated August 09, 2024
- Notice no. 20240920-63 dated September 20, 2024
- Notice no. 20241004-65 dated October 04, 2024
- Notice no. 20250725-61 dated July 25, 2025

No structural change to the ESM framework is introduced; this is a periodic update to the list of securities under ESM.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Brokers/Members**: Must ensure minimum 100% margin is collected on all ESM-shortlisted securities for open positions as on June 09, 2026 and new positions from June 10, 2026
- **Brokers/Members**: Must comply with Trade-for-Trade settlement rules for all newly included securities from June 10, 2026
- **Brokers/Members**: For Stage II securities, adhere to the 2% price band and Periodic Call Auction mechanism from June 09, 2026
- For clarifications, members may write to bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

| Date | Event |
|------|-------|
| June 08, 2026 | Notice issued |
| June 09, 2026 | Stage I downgrade for qualifying securities; Stage II Trade-for-Trade with 2% price band effective; ESM exits effective |
| June 10, 2026 | 100% margin requirement effective on open (as of June 09) and new positions; Rolling Settlement to Trade-for-Trade shift effective |

## Impact Assessment

The inclusion of securities under ESM significantly restricts their tradability:
- **Liquidity impact**: Trade-for-Trade settlement eliminates netting benefits, reducing trading activity in affected securities
- **Cost impact**: 100% margin requirement sharply increases the capital required to hold or create positions, discouraging speculative activity
- **Price impact**: Stage II securities face a restrictive 2% price band with Periodic Call Auction, further limiting price discovery
- **Positive signals**: Securities exiting ESM revert to normal price bands and settlement, restoring liquidity for those counters
- ESM operates in conjunction with other surveillance measures (ASM, GSM) and does not constitute any regulatory finding against the listed company
