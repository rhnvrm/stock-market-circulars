## Summary

BSE has issued an update to the Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LT-ASM) framework, referencing prior notices dated March 21, 2018, October 27, 2018, December 04, 2020, August 09, 2024, September 20, 2024, and June 04, 2021. The notice specifies securities newly placed under LT-ASM (Annexure I Part A), securities moving to higher LT-ASM stages (Annexure I Part B), securities under a related surveillance measure to be transferred to T/XT/MT/TS trading groups (Annexure I Part D), securities moving out of the LT-ASM framework (Annexure II), and a consolidated list of all securities currently under the framework (Annexure III).

## Key Points

- 100% margin shall apply on all open positions as of July 10, 2026, and on new positions created from July 13, 2026 onwards, for securities listed in Annexure I (Part A).
- Securities moving to higher LT-ASM stages take effect July 09, 2026 (Annexure I Part B).
- Lower price band becomes applicable w.e.f. July 09, 2026 for securities under the framework.
- Securities under the related surveillance measure (per notice dated June 04, 2021) will continue in LT-ASM but be transferred to the T / XT / MT / TS trading group w.e.f. July 13, 2026 (Annexure I Part D).
- Securities moving out of the LT-ASM framework w.e.f. July 09, 2026 are listed in Annexure II; their price bands will revert to pre-shortlisting levels, unless subject to another surveillance measure.
- A consolidated list of all securities currently under the LT-ASM framework is provided in Annexure III.
- Shortlisting/review is based on periodic assessment and XBRL submissions by listed companies, and is explicitly stated to not be construed as adverse action against any company.
- This framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the Exchange.

## Regulatory Changes

This notice is an operational update applying the existing LT-ASM framework (established via notices from 2018, 2020, 2021, and 2024) to a revised list of securities — adding some, escalating others to higher stages, transferring some to restricted trading groups, and removing others.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must apply 100% margin on the specified securities from the effective dates and adhere to the revised price bands and trading group classifications. Queries can be directed to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- July 09, 2026: Lower price band effective; securities move to higher LT-ASM stages; securities move out of LT-ASM framework.
- July 10, 2026: Reference date for open positions subject to 100% margin.
- July 13, 2026: 100% margin effective on open and new positions; securities transferred to T/XT/MT/TS trading group.

## Impact Assessment

Securities newly added to or escalated within the LT-ASM framework face materially higher margin requirements (100%) and tighter price bands, likely reducing trading liquidity and increasing cost of carry for affected positions. Securities transferred to T/XT/MT/TS groups face further trading restrictions. Conversely, securities exiting the framework see restored price bands and reduced restrictions, a positive development for those scrips. The specific securities affected are listed in the annexures, which are not included in the notice text.
