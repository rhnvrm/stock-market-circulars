## Summary

BSE's Department of Surveillance (DOSS) has revised the price bands for 16 equity scrips effective May 12, 2026. The revised bands range from 2% to 20%, reflecting updated surveillance assessments for each scrip.

## Key Points

- Price bands revised for 16 scrips effective May 12, 2026
- Revised bands range from 2% (most restrictive) to 20% (least restrictive)
- Action taken by BSE Surveillance department under Notice No. 20260511-42
- Members may contact bse.surv@bseindia.com for clarifications

## Regulatory Changes

Price band limits have been changed from existing levels to the following revised levels:

|---|---|---|
| 544694 | Accretion Nutraveda Ltd | 5 |
| 506947 | Advance Petrochemicals Ltd | 2 |
| 544450 | Chembond Chemicals Ltd | 5 |
| 530871 | Chembond Material Technologies Ltd | 5 |
| 540821 | Crystal Business System Ltd | 10 |
| 504000 | Elpro International Ltd | 10 |
| 530853 | Hipolin Ltd | 2 |
| 523387 | HOMRE Ltd | 2 |
| 544465 | Laxmi India Finance Ltd | 5 |
| 514036 | Loyal Textile Mills Ltd | 2 |
| 532649 | Nectar Lifesciences Ltd | 5 |
| 544559 | Om Metallogic Ltd | 5 |
| 533202 | Satchmo Holdings Ltd | 5 |
| 751113 | Shakti Press Ltd | 20 |
| 543535 | We Win Ltd | 10 |
| 512345 | Yash Trading & Finance Ltd | 2 |

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must note the revised price bands for the above scrips and ensure all orders placed from May 12, 2026 onwards comply with the new limits. No action is required beyond awareness and system adjustments for order placement.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** May 11, 2026
- **Effective Date:** May 12, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Four scrips (Advance Petrochemicals, Hipolin, HOMRE, Loyal Textile Mills, Yash Trading & Finance) have been placed under tighter 2% bands, significantly restricting daily price movement and liquidity. Seven scrips are set to a 5% band, three to 10%, and Shakti Press Ltd to a wider 20% band. Traders and investors holding positions in these scrips should be aware of the revised limits as orders outside the new bands will be rejected by the exchange system.
