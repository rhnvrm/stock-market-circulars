## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that further new securities issued by 13 companies under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP/ESOS) are listed and admitted for trading on the Exchange effective Thursday, May 14, 2026. None of the newly listed shares carry any lock-in restrictions.

## Key Points

- 13 companies have listed additional shares arising from ESOP/ESOS exercises
- All newly listed securities are effective for trading from May 14, 2026
- No lock-in period applies to any of the listed securities
- Total new shares span across large-cap (ICICI Bank, Tech Mahindra) and mid/small-cap companies
- ICICI Bank has the largest single allotment at 887,886 shares; Shadowfax Technologies follows with 622,743 shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice informing trading members of newly listed ESOP/ESOS securities.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance actions required from trading members or market participants beyond taking note of the new securities admitted for trading.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** May 13, 2026
- **Trading Effective Date:** May 14, 2026 (Thursday)
