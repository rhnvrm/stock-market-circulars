## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 21,90,635 new equity shares of SEPC LIMITED (Scrip Code: 532945), issued at a premium pursuant to conversion of Compulsorily Convertible Debentures (CCDs) on a preferential basis to non-promoters, are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Monday, July 27, 2026.

## Key Points

- 21,90,635 equity shares of face value Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 1.96/- per share
- Shares issued to Non-Promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of Compulsorily Convertible Debentures (CCD)
- Issue price: Rs. 11.96/- per share
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distinctive numbers: 1943322757 to 1945513391
- Date of allotment: 06/01/2026
- ISIN: INE964H01014

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing approval notice under BSE Listing Operations.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific action required from trading members or investors; the notice is informational, confirming admission of the new shares to trading.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: January 6, 2026
- Listing and trading effective date: Monday, July 27, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing represents a minor incremental increase in SEPC Limited's outstanding equity share capital due to conversion of CCDs held by non-promoters. As the new shares rank pari-passu with existing shares and the quantity is relatively small, the impact on overall float and price is expected to be limited. Trading members should update their systems to reflect the increased share count from the effective date.
