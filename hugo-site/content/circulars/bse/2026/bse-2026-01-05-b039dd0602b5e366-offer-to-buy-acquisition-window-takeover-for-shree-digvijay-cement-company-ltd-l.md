## Summary

BSE has announced the schedule for an Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for shares of SHREE DIGVIJAY CEMENT COMPANY LTD (SHREDIGCEM, Scrip Code: 502180, ISIN: INE232A01011). The offer will be live from Monday, January 5, 2026 to Friday, January 16, 2026. The offer size is up to 3,85,43,837 equity shares at an offer price of Rs. 92.20 per share with a face value of Rs. 10.00.

## Key Points

- **Security**: SHREE DIGVIJAY CEMENT COMPANY LTD (SHREDIGCEM)
- **Scrip Code**: 502180
- **ISIN**: INE232A01011
- **Offer Size**: Up to 3,85,43,837 equity shares
- **Offer Price**: Rs. 92.20 per share
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10.00
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Bid Offering Period**: January 5-16, 2026 from 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM
- **Give-up/Take-up Period**: January 5-15, 2026 from 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM
- **Last Day Give-up**: January 16, 2026 from 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM
- **Last Day Take-up**: January 16, 2026 from 9:15 AM to 4:00 PM

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular follows existing OTB framework as per Exchange Notices 20150930-1 (September 30, 2015) and 20170203-29 (February 3, 2017).

## Compliance Requirements

**Trading Members and Custodian Members must:**

1. Ensure investor UCC is registered and/or updated with correct PAN Number and Category in Exchange UCC database
2. Only bid for investors having shares in demat form
3. Use designated platforms for bidding:
   - iBBS platform: https://ibbs.bseindia.com/ (Internet) or https://ibbsll.bseindia.com/ (Leased Line)
   - RTRMS: https://rtrms.bseindia.com/ (Internet) or https://rtrmsll.bseindia.com/ (Leased Line)
   - Extranet: https://member.bseindia.com/ (Internet) or https://memberll.bseindia.com/ (Leased Line)
4. Note that Extranet files for bid acceptance will only be available after Exchange receives confirmation from Merchant Banker/RTA post-issue closure
5. Note that no End of Issue files will be generated if no securities are accepted

**Eligible Investor Categories:**
- Foreign Institutional Buyer (FII)
- Financial Institution (FI)
- Mutual Fund (MF)
- Insurance Companies (IC)
- Other QIBs (OTH)
- Other NIB (NOH)
- Company (CO)
- Individual (IND)
