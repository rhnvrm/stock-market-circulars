## Summary

BSE has notified that, in continuation of its earlier Notice No. 20260706-34 dated Monday, July 06, 2026, the equity shares of KRATIKAL TECH LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544811) will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment with effect from Tuesday, July 21, 2026. Consequently, dealings in the company's shares will shift to the M Group.

## Key Points

- Company: KRATIKAL TECH LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544811
- Segment: SME
- Current Group: MT (Trade for Trade)
- New Group: M (Rolling segment)
- Effective Date: Tuesday, July 21, 2026
- This notice follows and confirms the earlier Notice No. 20260706-34 dated July 06, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

The trading mechanism for KRATIKAL TECH LIMITED shares changes from Trade for Trade (where each trade must result in delivery, with no intraday squaring off or netting) to the Rolling segment (which permits normal trading, including intraday trading and netting off of positions).

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members must update their systems to reflect the change in group classification effective July 21, 2026. Queries can be directed to Mr. Anurag Jain at Tel. No. 022-2272 8822.

## Important Dates

- July 06, 2026: Original Notice No. 20260706-34 issued
- July 07, 2026: Current confirmatory notice issued
- July 21, 2026 (Tuesday): Effective date of transfer to Rolling segment (M Group)

## Impact Assessment

The shift from Trade for Trade to Rolling segment is generally a positive development for shareholders and traders of KRATIKAL TECH LIMITED, as it removes the delivery-based trading restriction and allows for more flexible, liquid trading including intraday transactions. This move typically follows a period of surveillance or settlement stability review. The impact is limited to this specific SME-listed scrip and does not have broader market implications.
