## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Electronic Gold Receipts (EGR) segment on Saturday, May 23, 2026. Trading members using third-party platforms from empaneled vendors or in-house systems via ETI APIs can use this session to test their trading applications, including exceptional market conditions such as risk-reduction mode and trading halts. There will be no new BOLT TWS Release.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for EGR segment on May 23, 2026
- Open to members using third-party empaneled vendor platforms or in-house ETI API-based systems
- No new BOLT TWS Release associated with this session
- Trades from mock trading carry no margin, pay-in, or pay-out obligations
- No rights or liabilities created from mock trades
- Members requested to provide feedback by 1:00 PM on the day

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notice for a scheduled mock trading session.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are encouraged to actively participate in the mock trading session
- Members must submit feedback on the session by 01:00 PM on May 23, 2026
- For queries, members should contact their Relationship Manager, BSE Helpdesk (022-45720400/600 & 022-69158500; bsehelp@bseindia.com), or BSE Tech Support (022-22728053; bse.tech@bseindia.com)

## Important Dates

- **May 23, 2026 (Saturday):** Mock trading session for EGR segment
  - Log-in: 09:15 AM – 10:00 AM
  - Pre-Open Order Entry: 10:00 AM – 10:08 AM
  - Pre-Open Matching: 10:08 AM – 10:15 AM
  - Continuous Trading: 10:15 AM – 12:00 PM
  - Block Deal Window: 11:00 AM – 11:15 AM
  - Closing: 12:00 PM – 12:10 PM
- **Feedback deadline:** 01:00 PM on May 23, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine operational exercise for the EGR segment. Participation is voluntary but encouraged. No financial obligations arise from trades executed during the mock session, and no real market positions are created. The session helps trading members validate system readiness and familiarize themselves with platform functionalities including risk-reduction mode and trading halt scenarios.
