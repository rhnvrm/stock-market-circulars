## Summary

BSE's Surveillance department issued Notice No. 20260511-26 dated 11 May 2026, informing members and investors that it has sought clarification from Vodafone Idea Ltd (Scrip Code: 532822) regarding a news article published on businesstoday.in. The exchange has not yet received a reply from the company.

## Key Points

- BSE proactively monitors news articles across newspapers, websites, and television channels to verify their accuracy.
- Clarification was sought from Vodafone Idea Ltd (Scrip Code: 532822) in connection with an article on businesstoday.in.
- As of the notice date, the company's reply is still awaited.
- The notice was issued by the Surveillance department under the DOSS segment.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Vodafone Idea Ltd is expected to respond to BSE's clarification request. Members and investors are advised to await the company's official response before acting on the news article in question.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 11 May 2026
- Response deadline: Not specified; reply from Vodafone Idea Ltd is pending.

## Impact Assessment

This notice reflects BSE's ongoing surveillance activity to maintain market integrity. The pending clarification from Vodafone Idea Ltd (a major telecom company) may be of interest to investors holding or tracking IDEA shares. Until the clarification is received and published, investors should exercise caution regarding the news article referenced on businesstoday.in.
