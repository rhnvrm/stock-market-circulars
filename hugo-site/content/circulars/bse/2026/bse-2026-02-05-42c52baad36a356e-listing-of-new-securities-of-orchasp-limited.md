## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 2,68,75,000 new equity shares of Orchasp Limited (Scrip Code: 532271) issued on a preferential basis to promoters. The shares will be listed and permitted to trade on the exchange from Friday, February 6, 2026. These shares are subject to a lock-in period until September 30, 2027.

## Key Points

- 2,68,75,000 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 1.20/-
- Issue price: Rs. 3.20/- per share
- Allotment date: December 18, 2025
- Shares issued to promoters on preferential basis
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE215B01022
- Distinctive Numbers: 319550367 to 346425366

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should note the listing of new securities effective February 6, 2026
- All 2,68,75,000 shares are locked-in until September 30, 2027
- Shares cannot be transferred or traded by allottees during the lock-in period

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 18, 2025
- **Trading Commencement Date**: February 6, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry Date**: September 30, 2027

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine preferential allotment to promoters with minimal market impact. The shares represent additional capital raised by Orchasp Limited and are subject to standard lock-in requirements for preferential issues to promoters. Trading members should be aware of the increased share capital and lock-in restrictions when processing transactions.
