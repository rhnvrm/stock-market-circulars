## Summary

BSE has announced special trading session timings for the Securities Lending and Borrowing (SLB) segment on Sunday, February 01, 2026, coinciding with the presentation of Union Budget 2026. The trading session will commence earlier than usual with login starting at 7:00 AM and continuous trading running from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM.

## Key Points

- Special trading session scheduled for Sunday, February 01, 2026
- Session coincides with Union Budget 2026 presentation
- Login window: 7:00 AM to 9:00 AM
- Continuous trading: 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM
- Applies to Securities Lending and Borrowing (SLB) segment only
- Notice issued by Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd.

## Regulatory Changes

No permanent regulatory changes. This is a one-time operational adjustment for the Union Budget day.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure system readiness for early login at 7:00 AM on February 01, 2026
- Participants should be prepared for extended trading hours until 5:00 PM on a Sunday
- Normal SLB trading protocols and settlement procedures remain applicable

## Important Dates

- **February 01, 2026 (Sunday)**: Special SLB trading session
  - Login: 7:00 AM - 9:00 AM
  - Continuous Trading: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM

## Impact Assessment

This circular has moderate operational impact on SLB trading members who will need to staff and operate on a Sunday with modified timings. The early start time (7:00 AM login) requires advance preparation. Market participants engaged in securities lending and borrowing activities should plan accordingly for the Union Budget day. Other trading segments are not affected by this notice.
