## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Mercury Ev-Tech Ltd (scrip code 531357) will be reclassified from Group XT to Group T with effect from June 02, 2026. This is a routine administrative change issued by the Listing Compliance & Operations department.

## Key Points

- Mercury Ev-Tech Ltd (scrip code 531357) is being moved from Group XT to Group T
- The change is effective June 02, 2026
- Notice issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President – Listing Compliance & Operations
- Trading members are advised to take note of this reclassification

## Regulatory Changes

The scrip is transitioning from Group XT (trade-for-trade segment for securities under surveillance or with specific listing conditions) to Group T (trade-for-trade segment). Both groups operate on a trade-for-trade basis, but the XT group typically denotes newly listed or specially categorized securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must update their systems to reflect the new group classification for Mercury Ev-Tech Ltd (531357) effective June 02, 2026. No specific action is required from investors, but they should be aware of the change in group classification.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: June 01, 2026
- Effective Date: June 02, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The reclassification from XT to T group is a minor administrative change. Both XT and T groups operate under trade-for-trade settlement, so the practical trading impact is minimal. This type of change typically indicates that the scrip has met certain conditions to exit the XT category. Market impact is expected to be negligible.
