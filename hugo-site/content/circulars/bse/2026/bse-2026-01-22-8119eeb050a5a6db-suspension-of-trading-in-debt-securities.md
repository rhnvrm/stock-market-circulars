## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in debt securities of IL&FS Financial Services Limited (Scrip Code: 953460, ISIN: INE121H08073) effective January 22, 2026. The suspension is due to the securities maturing on January 27, 2026. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debt securities from the effective date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspended for IL&FS Financial Services Limited 8.90% privately placed debt securities
- Scrip Code: 953460
- ISIN: INE121H08073
- Maturity Date: January 27, 2026
- Effective Date of Suspension: January 22, 2026
- Notice Reference: DR-805/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational notice for securities reaching maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debt securities from January 22, 2026
- Members should update their systems to reflect the trading suspension
- All pending orders for these securities should be cancelled

## Important Dates

- **January 22, 2026**: Effective date of trading suspension
- **January 27, 2026**: Maturity date of debt securities

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a routine suspension related to debt maturity. Only affects holders and potential traders of IL&FS Financial Services Limited's specific debt instrument (8.90% bonds). The suspension prevents trading in securities that are scheduled to mature within days, ensuring orderly settlement and preventing potential settlement issues.
