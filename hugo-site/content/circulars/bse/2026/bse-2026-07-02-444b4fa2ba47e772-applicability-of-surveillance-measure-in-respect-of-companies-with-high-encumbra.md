## Summary

BSE has issued a notice under its continuing surveillance framework addressing securities with high promoter 'Encumbrance' as defined under Regulation 28(3) of SEBI (SAST) Regulations, 2011. This is a continuation of earlier Exchange notices dated October 24, 2019 and April 17, 2020. Securities meeting the encumbrance criteria will face a minimum 35% margin requirement in the Equity and Equity Derivatives segments, while other previously listed securities have been found eligible for exclusion from the framework.

## Key Points

- Securities listed in Annexure I have met the criteria for inclusion under the high-encumbrance surveillance framework.
- These securities will attract a minimum 35% margin in the Equity and Equity Derivatives segments.
- The margin applies to all open positions as on July 06, 2026 and new positions created from July 07, 2026 onward.
- Securities listed in Annexure II are eligible to move out of the framework effective July 03, 2026.
- Annexure III provides a consolidated list of all securities currently under the framework.
- This measure operates alongside all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the Exchange.
- Inclusion in this framework is purely a surveillance action and should not be construed as an adverse view on the company.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced; this notice applies the existing high-encumbrance surveillance measure (per Reg. 28(3) of SEBI SAST Regulations, 2011) to an updated list of securities, per the periodic review process established in prior notices (20191024-30 and 20200417-31).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must apply the minimum 35% margin requirement to securities in Annexure I for open and new positions as specified.
- Companies wishing to contest their inclusion in the framework must submit a representation to the Exchange by 5:00 PM on July 03, 2026.
- Queries can be directed to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- July 03, 2026: Deadline for company representations regarding inclusion; also effective date for securities exiting the framework (Annexure II).
- July 06, 2026: Reference date for open positions subject to the new margin.
- July 07, 2026: Effective date for the minimum 35% margin on Annexure I securities and all new positions.

## Impact Assessment

The increased margin requirement (minimum 35%) raises the cost of trading and holding positions in the affected securities, likely reducing liquidity and increasing hedging costs for market participants in these names. Investors and traders holding positions in securities listed in Annexure I should reassess exposure ahead of the July 07, 2026 effective date, while those in Annexure II securities benefit from reduced margin burden starting July 03, 2026.
