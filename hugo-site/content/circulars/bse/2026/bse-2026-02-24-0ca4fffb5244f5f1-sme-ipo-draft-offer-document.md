## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document (DRHP) of **Twinkle Papers Limited**, filed on February 23, 2026, on the BSE SME website. The document is available for public review for a minimum of 21 days in accordance with Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Twinkle Papers Limited on February 23, 2026
- Document hosted on the BSE SME website: [www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx](http://www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx)
- Public comments are invited within 21 days from the date of this notice (by March 17, 2026)
- Disclosures comply with Schedule VI of the SEBI ICDR Regulations
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural notice under the existing SEBI ICDR (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, specifically Schedule VI, which mandates public availability of the draft offer document.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Investors and public:** May review the Draft Offer Document on the BSE SME website and submit comments within 21 days to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- **Twinkle Papers Limited:** Must ensure all disclosures in the DRHP meet Schedule VI requirements of ICDR Regulations

## Important Dates

- **Filing Date:** February 23, 2026
- **Notice Date:** February 24, 2026
- **Public Comment Deadline:** March 17, 2026 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal broad market impact. It is a routine SME IPO process notification limited to Twinkle Papers Limited. Relevant stakeholders include prospective investors in the SME segment who may wish to review the draft offer document and provide comments before the IPO proceeds to the next regulatory stage.
