## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of Twinkle Papers Limited, filed on February 23, 2026, on the BSE SME website. The document contains all disclosures as required under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations. The public is invited to submit comments within 21 days from the date of this notice.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Twinkle Papers Limited on February 23, 2026
- Document is available on the BSE SME website: www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Document complies with Schedule VI of the ICDR (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations
- Public comments are invited for a minimum period of 21 days from February 24, 2026
- Comments must be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This notice is in accordance with existing ICDR Regulations requiring public availability of draft offer documents for SME IPOs.

## Compliance Requirements

- The Draft Offer Document will remain hosted on the BSE SME website for at least 21 days
- Interested parties and investors may review the document and submit comments to equity.ipo@bseindia.com within 21 days of this notice (by approximately March 17, 2026)

## Important Dates

- **Filing Date:** February 23, 2026
- **Notice Date:** February 24, 2026
- **Public Comment Deadline:** On or before March 17, 2026 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal immediate market impact. It is a standard procedural disclosure for an SME IPO draft offer document. Impact is limited to potential investors and analysts interested in Twinkle Papers Limited. No existing listed securities or trading operations are affected.
