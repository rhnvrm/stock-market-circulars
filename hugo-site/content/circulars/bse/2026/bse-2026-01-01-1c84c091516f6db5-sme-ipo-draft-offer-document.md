## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document of FX MULTITECH LIMITED for public comments. The document was filed on December 31, 2025 and contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations. The document will be available on the stock exchange website for at least 21 days to allow stakeholders to submit comments.

## Key Points

- Company: FX MULTITECH LIMITED
- Document filing date: December 31, 2025
- Notice date: January 01, 2026
- Public comment period: 21 days from notice date
- Document complies with Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website: www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Comments to be sent to: equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by: Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural notification for an SME IPO draft offer document as per existing ICDR Regulations requirements.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public and stakeholders can review the Draft Offer Document on BSE SME website
- Comments, if any, must be submitted within 21 days from January 01, 2026
- Comments should be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- The document must remain available on the exchange website for minimum 21 days as per regulatory requirements

## Important Dates

- Document Filed: December 31, 2025
- Notice Date: January 01, 2026
- Comment Period Deadline: January 22, 2026 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine procedural notification with minimal market impact. It affects only potential investors interested in the FX MULTITECH LIMITED SME IPO. The public comment period is a standard regulatory requirement to ensure transparency and allow stakeholder feedback before the final offer document is issued. No immediate trading or operational impact on existing market participants.
