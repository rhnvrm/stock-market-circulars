## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted for trading the further new securities issued by six companies under their Employee Stock Option Plan (ESOP) / Employee Stock Option Scheme (ESOS). These securities are effective from Monday, April 27, 2026. None of the newly listed shares carry any lock-in period.

## Key Points

- Six companies have had ESOP/ESOS-allotted shares listed on BSE effective April 27, 2026.
- A total of approximately 2.2 million new shares are being listed across all six companies.
- HDFC Bank accounts for the largest allotment at 1,784,256 shares (Face Value ₹1).
- No shares are subject to lock-in restrictions.
- Issued under BSE Notice No. 20260424-7 dated April 24, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notice under existing ESOP/ESOS frameworks.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action is required from market participants. The notice is for information purposes to inform all market participants of the addition of these securities to the tradable universe on BSE.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** April 24, 2026
- **Trading Effective From:** Monday, April 27, 2026
