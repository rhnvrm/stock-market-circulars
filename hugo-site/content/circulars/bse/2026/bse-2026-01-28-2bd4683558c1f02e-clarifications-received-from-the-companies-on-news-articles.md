## Summary

BSE issued a circular containing clarifications from two listed companies - Adani Energy Solutions Ltd and Hindustan Petroleum Corporation Ltd - regarding news articles published about their business activities. Both companies confirmed that the reported contracts/agreements are in the ordinary course of business and do not require disclosure under SEBI Listing Regulations.

## Key Points

- Adani Energy Solutions Ltd clarified news about securing hybrid power mandate from Asahi India Glass
- Contract signed on January 7, 2026 for hybrid power supply and management with Asahi India Glass
- Adani confirmed the contract is in ordinary course of business (transmission and distribution of energy)
- No material impact on company operations
- Hindustan Petroleum Corporation Ltd provided clarification on news article from CNBCTV18 dated January 27, 2026
- HPCL news relates to partnership with Castrol India for re-refined base oil ecosystem development
- HPCL signed non-binding Memorandum (document appears truncated)
- Both companies confirmed no disclosure required under Regulation 30 of SEBI Listing Regulations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies must provide clarifications when BSE requests information about news articles
- Companies must assess whether contracts fall under disclosure requirements of Schedule III, Part A, Para B, Item 4 of SEBI Listing Regulations
- Disclosure required only for contracts not in ordinary course of business
- Companies must continue monitoring for events requiring disclosure under Regulation 30

## Important Dates

- January 27, 2026: News articles published about both companies
- January 27, 2026: BSE email requesting clarification (ref: L/SURV/ONL/RV/SG/ (2025-2026)/ 155)
- January 28, 2026: Circular issued with company clarifications
- January 7, 2026: Adani Energy Solutions signed contract with Asahi India Glass

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. Both clarifications confirm business-as-usual activities with no material operational or financial impact.

**Investor Impact:** Low. The clarifications provide transparency that the reported news items do not constitute material events requiring separate disclosure.

**Operational Impact:** None. Both contracts/agreements are part of ordinary business operations for the respective companies.

**Compliance Impact:** Demonstrates proper adherence to BSE surveillance requirements for clarifying market news and proper application of materiality thresholds under SEBI Listing Regulations.
