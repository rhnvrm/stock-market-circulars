## Summary

BSE has updated the Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LT-ASM) framework. Securities listed in Annexure I (Part A) will attract 100% margins effective June 19, 2026. Securities in Annexure I (Part B) are moved to higher stages of the LT-ASM framework, and a Lower Price Band becomes applicable from June 17, 2026. Securities in Annexure I (Part D) will continue in LT-ASM but be transferred to T/XT/MT/TS Groups from June 19, 2026. Securities in Annexure II exit the LT-ASM framework from June 17, 2026.

## Key Points

- 100% margins applicable from June 19, 2026 on all open positions as of June 18, 2026 and new positions thereafter (Annexure I, Part A)
- Securities moved to higher LT-ASM stages effective June 17, 2026 (Annexure I, Part B)
- Lower Price Band imposed effective June 17, 2026 for securities in higher stages
- Certain securities transferred to T/XT/MT/TS Group effective June 19, 2026 while remaining in LT-ASM (Annexure I, Part D)
- Securities moving out of LT-ASM framework effective June 17, 2026 listed in Annexure II
- Consolidated list of all securities under the framework provided in Annexure III
- Shortlisting is solely for surveillance purposes and not an adverse action against the company

## Regulatory Changes

This notice references the revised LT-ASM framework as per Exchange notices: 20180321-46 (March 21, 2018), 20181027-1 (October 27, 2018), 20201204-56 (December 4, 2020), 20240809-46 (August 9, 2024), and 20240920-63 (September 20, 2024). The current update applies the revised framework to new and existing securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must ensure 100% margins are maintained on open positions in affected securities from June 19, 2026
- Price bands for securities exiting the framework revert to pre-LT-ASM levels, unless the security is under another surveillance measure
- Members may contact bse.surv@bseindia.com for clarifications

## Important Dates

- **June 17, 2026**: Lower Price Band effective; securities move to higher LT-ASM stages; securities exit LT-ASM framework (Annexure II)
- **June 18, 2026**: Reference date for existing open positions subject to 100% margin
- **June 19, 2026**: 100% margin requirement effective for open and new positions; group transfers (T/XT/MT/TS) effective

## Impact Assessment

The imposition of 100% margins significantly increases the cost of holding positions in affected securities, likely reducing liquidity and trading volumes. Securities moved to higher LT-ASM stages face tighter price bands, further constraining price discovery. Group transfers to T/XT/MT/TS may affect settlement mechanisms and eligible investor categories. The framework applies in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures, potentially compounding restrictions on certain securities.
