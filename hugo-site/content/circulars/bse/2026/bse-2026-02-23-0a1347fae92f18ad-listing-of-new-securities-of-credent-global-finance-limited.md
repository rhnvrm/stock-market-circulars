## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 1,00,00,000 (1 crore) equity shares of Credent Global Finance Limited (Scrip Code: 539598, ISIN: INE727C01024) are listed and admitted for trading effective February 24, 2026. These shares were allotted to Qualified Institutional Buyers (QIBs) pursuant to a Qualified Institutional Placement (QIP) at an issue price of Rs. 30/- per share.

## Key Points

- 1,00,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 2/- face value each are newly listed on BSE
- Shares allotted to QIBs under Qualified Institutional Placement (QIP)
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Issue price: Rs. 30/- per share
- Distribution numbers: 51461231 to 61461230
- ISIN: INE727C01024 | Scrip Code: 539598

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notice under BSE's procedures for admitting newly allotted securities for trading.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new listing and may contact Mr. Gaurav Bajare (Tel: 022 2272 8291) for any clarifications regarding this notice.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** February 19, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** February 24, 2026 (Tuesday)
- **Notice Date:** February 23, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The addition of 1 crore new equity shares via QIP increases the total share supply of Credent Global Finance Limited on the exchange. The shares are priced at Rs. 30/- per share, which serves as a reference point for market participants. As QIP shares rank pari-passu with existing shares, there is no differential in rights or dividends. Traders should note the expanded float when assessing liquidity and price movements for scrip code 539598.
