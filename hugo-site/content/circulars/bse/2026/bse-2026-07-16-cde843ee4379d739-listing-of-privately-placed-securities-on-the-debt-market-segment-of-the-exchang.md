## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that several new securities issued on a private placement basis have been listed and admitted to trading on the BSE Debt Segment effective 16 July 2026. The listings comprise fresh and further issuances of Commercial Papers (CPs) by ICICI Home Finance Company Limited, Kotak Mahindra Prime Limited, InCred Financial Services Limited, Poonawalla Fincorp Limited, and Bajaj Financial Securities Limited.

## Key Points

- All listed securities trade only in dematerialised form under their respective ISINs.
- Trading takes place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof, with a tick size of 1 paise.
- Fresh Listings:
  - ICICI Home Finance Company Limited — ISIN INE071G14IC2, Scrip ID IHFC16726, 5,000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 face value, 6.70% p.a., allotted 16/07/2026, redemption 24/09/2026, rated CARE A1+/ICRA A1+.
  - Kotak Mahindra Prime Limited — ISIN INE916D146X5, Scrip ID KMPL160726, 3,000 units, 7.10% p.a., allotted 16/07/2026, redemption 05/10/2026, rated CRISIL A1+/ICRA A1+.
  - InCred Financial Services Limited — ISIN INE321N14497, Scrip ID IFSL150726, 600 units, 8.55% p.a., allotted 15/07/2026, redemption 30/12/2026, rated ICRA A1+.
- Further Listings:
  - Poonawalla Fincorp Limited — ISIN INE511C14ZH9, Scrip ID PFL28126, 2,000 units, 7.49% p.a., allotted 15/07/2026, redemption 28/01/2027, rated CARE A1+/CRISIL A1+.
  - Bajaj Financial Securities Limited — ISIN INE01C314FK3, Scrip ID BFSL13526, 7,000 units, allotted 15/07/2026, rated CRISIL A1+/IND A1+ (rate of interest and redemption details truncated in source).

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing notice under BSE's DCS-Listing department for privately placed debt instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action is required from trading members beyond awareness of the new ISINs and trading parameters. Members requiring clarification may contact BSE's debt department at the listed phone numbers.

## Important Dates

- Effective listing date: 16 July 2026
- Allotment dates: 15–16 July 2026 (varies by issuer)
- Redemption dates: range from 24 September 2026 to 28 January 2027 depending on the instrument

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact, routine administrative circular confined to the BSE Debt Segment. It enables trading in newly issued commercial papers by well-rated NBFCs and financial institutions but has no direct bearing on equity markets, listed company operations, or broader market sentiment. Relevant primarily to debt market participants and trading members dealing in commercial paper instruments.
