## Summary

BSE has listed commercial papers issued on private placement basis by five companies on its debt market segment effective January 30, 2026. The securities include issues from 360 ONE Alternates Asset Management Limited (2 issues), Bajaj Finance Limited, Pilani Investment and Industries Corporation Limited, Gera Developments Private Limited, and Vivriti Capital Limited. All securities will trade in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- Total of 5 commercial paper issues listed on BSE Debt Segment
- Trading in dematerialized form only under specified ISIN numbers
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for securities is 1 paise
- Interest rates range from 6.95% to 8.10% p.a.
- Maturity periods vary from 3 months to 12 months
- All issues rated A1+ by CARE, CRISIL, or ICRA

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Trading to be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Contact debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915 for clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** January 30, 2026 (trading admitted)
- **360 ONE Alternates (Issue 1):** Allotment: Jan 29, 2026 | Redemption: Apr 29, 2026
- **360 ONE Alternates (Issue 2):** Allotment: Jan 29, 2026 | Redemption: Jul 29, 2026
- **Bajaj Finance:** Allotment: Jan 29, 2026 | Redemption: Apr 30, 2026
- **Pilani Investment:** Allotment: Jan 30, 2026 | Redemption: Jan 29, 2027
- **Gera Developments:** Allotment: Jan 29, 2026 | Redemption: Jul 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This is a routine listing of debt securities on the debt market segment and does not affect equity trading or broader market operations.

**Operational Impact:** None for equity market participants. Only affects debt segment trading members who may trade these commercial papers.

**Investor Impact:** Provides additional investment opportunities in highly-rated (A1+) short-term debt instruments from reputed issuers including Bajaj Finance Limited.
