## Summary

BSE has issued a revised schedule for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for SANMITRA COMMERCIAL LIMITED (Symbol: ZSANMCOM, Scrip Code: 512062). The offer period runs from January 6, 2026 to January 20, 2026, with an offer size of up to 1,49,76,000 equity shares at Rs. 15.00 per share. Trading Members and Custodian Members are requested to note the revised live activities schedule and operational procedures.

## Key Points

- Security: SANMITRA COMMERCIAL LIMITED (ZSANMCOM, ISIN: INE896J01014)
- Offer size: Up to 1,49,76,000 equity shares
- Offer price: Rs. 15.00 per share (Face Value: Rs. 10.00)
- Market lot: 1 share
- Offer period: January 6, 2026 to January 20, 2026
- Trading Members can bid for investors having shares in demat form
- UCC of investors must be registered with correct PAN Number and Category

## Regulatory Changes

This is a revised schedule notice. No new regulatory changes are introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members and Custodian Members must note the revised schedule
- Members must ensure investor UCC is registered/updated with correct PAN Number and Category in Exchange UCC database
- Trading Members can only bid for investors with shares in demat form
- Members must refer to Exchange Notices 20150930-1 (September 30, 2015) and 20170203-29 (February 3, 2017) for operational instructions
- If no securities are accepted, no End of Issue files will be generated in Extranet

## Important Dates

- **Opening Date**: Tuesday, January 6, 2026
- **Closing Date**: Tuesday, January 20, 2026

- **Give-up/Take-up Period**: January 6-19, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
