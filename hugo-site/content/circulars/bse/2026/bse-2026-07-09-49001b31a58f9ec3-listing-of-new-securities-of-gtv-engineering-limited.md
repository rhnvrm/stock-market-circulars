## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 39,42,046 new equity shares of GTV Engineering Limited (Scrip Code: 539479), issued to promoters on a preferential basis, are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Friday, July 10, 2026.

## Key Points

- 39,42,046 equity shares of Rs. 2/- face value each issued at a premium of Rs. 57.65/- per share to promoters on a preferential basis.
- Issue price: Rs. 59.65/- per share.
- ISIN: INE910R01024.
- Distinctive numbers: 46858321 to 50800366.
- Date of allotment: 04/06/2026.
- These shares rank pari-passu with the existing equity shares of the company.
- Entire allotment of 3,942,046 shares is under lock-in until 10/02/2028.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing notice for shares already allotted via preferential issue.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should update their systems to recognize the newly listed shares as tradable from the effective date, noting that these specific distinctive number ranges remain under lock-in and are not freely tradable until the lock-in expiry date.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: 04 June 2026
- Listing/Trading Effective Date: 10 July 2026
- Lock-in Expiry: 10 February 2028

## Impact Assessment

The listing increases GTV Engineering Limited's total issued equity share capital, marginally diluting existing shareholders. Since all new shares are under lock-in until February 2028, there is no immediate increase in free-float trading volume or selling pressure. The impact is company-specific with negligible broader market significance.
