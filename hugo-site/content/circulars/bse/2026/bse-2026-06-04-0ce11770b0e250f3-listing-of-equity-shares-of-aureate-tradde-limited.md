## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members that the equity shares of Aureate Tradde Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange. The specific date of listing and further details of the securities will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Aureate Tradde Limited's equity shares are approved for listing on BSE under the SME segment.
- Scrip Code assigned: **544772**
- Trading Symbol: **Aureate**
- ISIN Number: **INE1KVL01010**
- The listing date has not yet been announced and will be notified separately.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing admission notice.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should take note of the upcoming listing and await the subsequent notice detailing the exact listing date and security specifications before initiating trades.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 4, 2026
- **Listing Date:** To be announced via a separate notice.

## Impact Assessment

This notice has limited market-wide impact as it pertains solely to the admission of a new SME-segment company onto BSE. Investors and trading members interested in Aureate Tradde Limited should monitor for the follow-up notice confirming the listing date and trading commencement details.
