## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to the change of name of Purple Entertainment Limited to Purple Agrotech Industries Limited, effective November 4, 2025. The certificate was issued by the Central Processing Centre, ROC, and digitally signed by Assistant/Deputy/Registrar of Companies Mitika Raja. The company's Corporate Identification Number (CIN) L65100GJ1974PLC084389 remains unchanged.

## Key Points

- Company name changed from Purple Entertainment Limited to Purple Agrotech Industries Limited
- Effective date of name change: November 4, 2025
- Corporate Identification Number (CIN): L65100GJ1974PLC084389 (unchanged)
- Company type: Limited by shares
- Certificate issued by Central Processing Centre, ROC Manesar
- Digital signature by Mitika Raja on November 4, 2025 at 13:06:08 IST
- Registered office address: 30-B, Third Floor, Ajanta Complex, Income Tax, Ashram Road, Ahmedabad-380009, Gujarat, India

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate name change procedure conducted under the Companies Act, 2013 and Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014, specifically pursuant to Rule 29.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display the old name (Purple Entertainment Limited) along with its new name (Purple Agrotech Industries Limited) for a period of two years at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders
- All stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company and its directors on the Ministry of Corporate Affairs website: www.mca.gov.in/MCA21

## Important Dates

- **November 4, 2025**: Effective date of name change from Purple Entertainment Limited to Purple Agrotech Industries Limited
- **November 4, 2025**: Date of certificate issuance (digitally signed at 13:06:08 IST)
- **Two-year period**: Company must display both old and new names during this period

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. Name changes are administrative in nature and do not affect the company's operations, assets, liabilities, or shareholder rights.

**Operational Impact**: Low. The company will need to update all statutory records, letterheads, websites, and official communications to reflect the new name while continuing to display the old name for two years.

**Stakeholder Impact**: Negligible. All existing contracts, agreements, and obligations remain valid and enforceable under the new name. Shareholders retain their ownership stakes without any dilution or change.

**Trading Impact**: Investors should note the new company name when trading shares. The stock symbol may be updated by the exchange in due course.
