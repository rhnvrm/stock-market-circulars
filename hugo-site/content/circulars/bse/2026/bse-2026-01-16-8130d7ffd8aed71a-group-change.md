## Summary

BSE has announced that Halder Venture Limited will undergo a group change from XT group to T group effective January 19, 2026. Trading members are requested to take note of this change.

## Key Points

- Company: Halder Venture Limited
- Scrip Code: 539854
- Current Group: XT
- New Group: T
- Effective Date: January 19, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard group migration within BSE's trading segment classification system.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are requested to note the group change and update their systems accordingly before the effective date.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: January 16, 2026
- Effective Date: January 19, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative change affecting a single company. The move from XT group to T group typically indicates a change in trading parameters or surveillance category. Trading members should update their systems to reflect the new group classification, but minimal market impact is expected.
