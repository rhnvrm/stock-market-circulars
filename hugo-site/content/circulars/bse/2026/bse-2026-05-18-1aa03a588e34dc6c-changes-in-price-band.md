## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260518-31, issued on May 18, 2026, revises the price bands for 29+ equity scrips effective May 19, 2026. The revision is issued by the Department of Stock Surveillance (DOSS) and applies to the Equity segment. The majority of scrips are revised to a 5% price band, two scrips (Callista Industries Ltd and Kartik Investments Trust Ltd) are set to 2%, and Dynamic Cables Ltd is revised to 10%.

## Key Points

- Price band revisions effective from **May 19, 2026**
- 29+ scrips affected across various sectors
- Most scrips revised to **5% price band** (tighter trading range)
- Callista Industries Ltd (539335) and Kartik Investments Trust Ltd (501151) revised to **2% price band** (most restrictive)
- Dynamic Cables Ltd (540795) revised to **10% price band** (relatively wider)
- Issued by Department of Stock Surveillance (DOSS), indicating surveillance-driven action
- No attachments; full list communicated via the notice itself

## Regulatory Changes

Price bands are circuit filters that limit the maximum intraday price movement of a scrip. Revisions to lower bands (e.g., from 20% to 5%) restrict volatility and are typically applied under BSE's surveillance framework (including ESM/GSM measures). The revised bands replace the existing levels for each listed scrip.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members**: Must note the revised price bands and ensure order placement complies with the new limits from May 19, 2026 onwards
- **Risk Management Systems**: Firms should update their systems to reflect the new circuit filter levels for affected scrips
- No action required from listed companies directly

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: May 18, 2026
- **Effective Date**: May 19, 2026
