## Summary

BSE has issued an update (Notice No. 20260521-2) in continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20260427-16 dated April 27, 2026, regarding the revision of terms for two privately placed debt securities of Equinox India Infraestate Ltd. The revisions include changes to ISIN codes, scrip names, scrip IDs, and maturity dates, effective Friday, May 22, 2026.

## Key Points

- Two debt scrips of Equinox India Infraestate Ltd are being revised with new ISINs and extended maturity dates.
- Scrip Code 977168 (30,500 securities): ISIN changes from INE741X07077 to INE741X07101; maturity extended from 31/08/2030 to 31/03/2031.
- Scrip Code 977672 (29,000 securities): ISIN changes from INE741X07085 to INE741X07093; maturity extended from 31/08/2030 to 31/03/2031.
- Both scrips carry a coupon rate of 13.50% and are privately placed.
- Changes are effective from May 22, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

| Scrip Code | Old ISIN | New ISIN | Old Scrip ID | New Scrip ID | Old Maturity | New Maturity |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 977168 | INE741X07077 | INE741X07101 | 1350EIL30 | 1350EIL31 | 31/08/2030 | 31/03/2031 |
| 977672 | INE741X07085 | INE741X07093 | 1350EIIL30 | 1350EIIL31 | 31/08/2030 | 31/03/2031 |

The scrip long names are also updated to reflect the new maturity dates:
- 977168: EIL-13.50%-31-08-30-PVT → EIL-13.50%-31-03-31-PVT
- 977672: EIIL-13.50%-31-08-30-PVT → EIIL-13.50%-31-03-31-PVT

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must note the revised ISIN codes and updated scrip details for both securities.
- All back-office and trading systems should be updated to reflect the new ISINs, scrip IDs, and maturity dates before trading commences on May 22, 2026.
- Members should refer to earlier Exchange Notice No. 20260427-16 dated April 27, 2026 for background context.

## Important Dates

- **April 27, 2026**: Original Exchange Notice No. 20260427-16 issued.
- **May 21, 2026**: This update notice issued.
- **May 22, 2026 (Friday)**: Effective date for revised terms — scrips available for trading with new ISIN codes and maturity dates.

## Impact Assessment

This revision impacts trading members who deal in the privately placed debt securities of Equinox India Infraestate Ltd. The extension of maturity dates from August 2030 to March 2031 for both scrips indicates a restructuring of the debt terms. Members holding or trading these securities must update their records with new ISINs to avoid settlement failures. The change affects a combined 59,500 listed securities across the two scrips.
