## Summary

BSE has listed 2,000 bonus equity shares of Jtekt India Ltd. (Scrip Code: 520057) that were previously kept in abeyance. These shares are admitted to trading with effect from Wednesday, March 4, 2026.

## Key Points

- 2,000 bonus equity shares of Re. 1/- each, previously kept in abeyance, are now listed on BSE
- Applicable to Jtekt India Ltd. with Scrip Code: 520057
- ISIN: INE643A01035
- Distinctive Numbers assigned: 198681033 to 198683032
- Trading in these shares commences from March 4, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard administrative action releasing bonus shares that were held in abeyance pending resolution of underlying entitlement issues.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed to note the listing of these securities and update their records accordingly. No specific compliance action is required beyond awareness of the new securities being available for trading.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** March 2, 2026
- **Trading Commencement Date:** March 4, 2026 (Wednesday)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The quantity of 2,000 shares is very small relative to the total outstanding equity of Jtekt India Ltd. This is a routine resolution of previously suspended bonus share allotments and is unlikely to cause any material price or liquidity movement in the scrip.
