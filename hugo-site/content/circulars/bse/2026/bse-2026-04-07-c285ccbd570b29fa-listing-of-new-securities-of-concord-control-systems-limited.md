BSE has issued Notice No. 20260407-12 dated 07 April 2026 regarding the listing of new securities of Concord Control Systems Limited on the equity segment. The notice was issued by the Listing Operations department under the company-related category.

- Notice No.: 20260407-12
- Date: 07 April 2026
- Category: Company related
- Segment: Equity
- Department: Listing Operations
- Subject: Listing of New Securities of Concord Control Systems Limited
- No attachments are included with this notice

No specific regulatory changes are outlined in this notice. It is an operational listing notice issued by BSE's Listing Operations department.

Concord Control Systems Limited and its relevant stakeholders should take note of the new securities being listed on the BSE equity segment. Market participants should update their records accordingly.

- Notice Date: 07 April 2026
- Listing effective as per the notice issuance on 07 April 2026

This notice pertains specifically to Concord Control Systems Limited and signals the admission of new securities to trading on the BSE equity segment. The impact is limited to existing and prospective investors in this company. No broader market impact is anticipated.
