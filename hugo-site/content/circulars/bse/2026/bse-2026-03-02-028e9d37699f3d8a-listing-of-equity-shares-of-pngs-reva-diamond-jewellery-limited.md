## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members that the equity shares of PNGS Reva Diamond Jewellery Limited are to be listed and admitted to dealings on BSE. The scrip has been assigned code 544718 and symbol PNGSREVA. The specific listing date and further details of the securities will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Equity shares of PNGS Reva Diamond Jewellery Limited will be listed on BSE
- Scrip Code: 544718
- Symbol: PNGSREVA
- ISIN: INE1RDG01013
- Exact listing date to be announced via a separate notice
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing admission notice under BSE's listing procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are advised to take note of the upcoming listing of PNGS Reva Diamond Jewellery Limited and await the subsequent notice for the confirmed listing date and full security details before initiating trades.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** Monday, March 2, 2026
- **Listing Date:** To be announced via a separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This notice signals the imminent listing of PNGS Reva Diamond Jewellery Limited on BSE's equity segment. The impact is primarily relevant to IPO allottees and investors tracking the stock. Trading members should monitor for the follow-up notice confirming the listing date to prepare for the commencement of trading in PNGSREVA (ISIN: INE1RDG01013).
