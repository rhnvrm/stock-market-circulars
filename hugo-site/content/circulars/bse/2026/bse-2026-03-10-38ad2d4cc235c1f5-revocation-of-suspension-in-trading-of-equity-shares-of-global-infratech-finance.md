## Summary

BSE has issued a circular revoking the suspension in trading of equity shares of Global Infratech & Finance Limited (Scrip Code: 531463). This means trading in the company's shares, which was previously suspended, is now permitted to resume on BSE.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension in equity shares of Global Infratech & Finance Limited (Scrip Code: 531463) has been revoked by BSE.
- The company is engaged in investments in shares and securities and is currently operational.
- Registered office is at A-401, Pearl Arcade, Opp. P. K. Jewellers, Dawood Baug Lane, Off. J P Road, Andheri (W), Mumbai – 400 058.
- Corporate/communication address is at 7/23 Kirti Nagar Industrial Area, Near Underpass, Delhi-110015.
- Managing Director and Chairman: Mr. Mohit Bajaj.
- Company Secretary & Compliance Officer: Dhaval Bajaj (Email: asianlakcfl@gmail.com).
- Company website: www.globalinfrafin.in.

## Regulatory Changes

The prior suspension order on trading of Global Infratech & Finance Limited equity shares stands revoked. No new regulatory framework is introduced; this circular restores the normal trading status of the scrip.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants and brokers may resume trading in the equity shares of Global Infratech & Finance Limited (Scrip Code: 531463) on BSE.
- The company is expected to maintain continued compliance with BSE listing obligations to avoid future suspension.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: 2026-03-10
- Trading resumption is effective as per the date of this circular (2026-03-10).

## Impact Assessment

The revocation of suspension allows existing shareholders and market participants to trade in Global Infratech & Finance Limited shares on BSE. This is a positive development for shareholders who were unable to exit or enter positions during the suspension period. The impact is limited to investors holding or intending to trade this specific scrip. No broader market or systemic impact is anticipated given the company's small size and niche focus on share and securities investments.
