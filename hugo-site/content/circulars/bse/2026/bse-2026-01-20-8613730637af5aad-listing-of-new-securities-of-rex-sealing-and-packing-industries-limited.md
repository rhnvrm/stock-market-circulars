## Summary

BSE has listed 1,04,000 new equity shares of Rex Sealing and Packing Industries Limited (Scrip Code: 543744) issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. The shares will commence trading from Wednesday, January 21, 2026.

## Key Points

- 1,04,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 200/-
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distinctive Numbers: 2276001 to 2380000
- Date of allotment: October 20, 2025
- Issue price: Rs. 210/- per share
- ISIN: INE0MFT01014
- All 1,04,000 shares subject to lock-in until July 31, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification following warrant conversion and preferential allotment procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed of the new securities listing and should update their systems accordingly for trading commencing January 21, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** October 20, 2025
- **Trading Commencement:** January 21, 2026
- **Lock-in Period Ends:** July 31, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine listing of preferential shares issued to non-promoters following warrant conversion. The relatively small number of shares (1,04,000) and lock-in restrictions until July 31, 2026 limit immediate trading impact. The shares are pari-passu with existing equity, ensuring no structural changes to shareholder rights.
