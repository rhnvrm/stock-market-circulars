## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that several new securities issued on a private placement basis have been listed and admitted to trading on the BSE Debt Segment, effective July 9, 2026. The notice covers fresh and further listings of Commercial Papers (CPs) by Bajaj Finance Limited, OXYZO Financial Services Limited, Dalmia Cement (Bharat) Limited, and Bajaj Financial Securities Limited, as well as the listing of privately placed Debt Securities (Non-Convertible Debentures), including a fresh listing by Jio Credit (details truncated in source).

## Key Points

- New Commercial Papers listed under BSE Debt Segment effective today, tradable only in dematerialised form under specified ISINs.
- Trading in these CPs will occur in standard denominations of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof, with a tick size of 1 paise.
- Fresh CP listings: Bajaj Finance Limited (ISIN INE296A14I18, Scrip Code 732059), OXYZO Financial Services Limited (ISIN INE04VS14368, Scrip Code 732060), and Dalmia Cement (Bharat) Limited (ISIN INE755K14FS4, Scrip Code 732061).
- Further listing: Bajaj Financial Securities Limited (ISIN INE01C314FQ0, Scrip Code 731720).
- A separate section lists Privately Placed Debt Instruments (NCDs), traded in dematerialised form with a tick size of 1 paise; placement memoranda are available on the BSE debt market data portal.
- Trading members with queries may contact the BSE debt department at the listed extension numbers.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a standard administrative listing notice reflecting admission of new privately placed debt instruments to trading on the BSE Debt Segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure trades in the listed Commercial Papers occur in the specified standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof.
- Debt securities must be traded only in dematerialised form under the specified ISINs.
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum on the BSE debt market data page for further details on the NCD issuances.

## Important Dates

- Listing effective date: July 9, 2026
- Deemed date of allotment for the Commercial Papers: July 8, 2026
- Redemption dates vary by instrument: Bajaj Finance CP (BFL8726) – October 5, 2026; OXYZO CP (OFSL80726) – July 7, 2027; Dalmia Cement CP (DCBL080726) – September 21, 2026; Bajaj Financial Securities CP (BFSL21526) – August 21, 2026.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice for the debt/fixed-income market segment with limited direct impact on equity trading or broader market sentiment. It is primarily relevant to debt market participants, institutional investors, and trading members dealing in commercial papers and privately placed NCDs of the named issuers, including Bajaj Finance, which also has actively traded listed equity.
