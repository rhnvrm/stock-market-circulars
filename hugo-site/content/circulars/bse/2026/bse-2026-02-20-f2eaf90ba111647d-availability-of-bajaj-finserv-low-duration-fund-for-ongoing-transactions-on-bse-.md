## Summary

BSE has informed members that Bajaj Finserv Low Duration Fund will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption, along with SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities on the BSE StAR MF Platform, effective February 23, 2026. This notice references the earlier Notice No. 20260206-20.

## Key Points

- Bajaj Finserv Low Duration Fund reopens for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform from February 23, 2026
- All transaction types enabled: subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Six scheme variants available across Regular and Direct plans
- All schemes are classified under the DEBT category

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular operationalizes the reopening of an existing mutual fund scheme for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform, following the prior notice dated February 6, 2026 (Notice No. 20260206-20).

## Compliance Requirements

- BSE members and mutual fund distributors using the BSE StAR MF Platform should update their systems to reflect the availability of all six Bajaj Finserv Low Duration Fund variants from February 23, 2026
- Ensure correct ISIN mapping for each plan/option combination before processing transactions

## Important Dates

- **February 20, 2026**: Notice issued (Notice No. 20260220-42)
- **February 23, 2026**: Effective date for reopening of ongoing subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice with low market impact. It enables investors and distributors to transact in Bajaj Finserv Low Duration Fund through the BSE StAR MF Platform. The fund is a debt category product (low duration), primarily targeting conservative investors seeking short-term fixed-income exposure. The availability of both Regular and Direct plans with Growth and IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal) options provides flexibility for different investor profiles. No impact on equity markets or broader market operations.
