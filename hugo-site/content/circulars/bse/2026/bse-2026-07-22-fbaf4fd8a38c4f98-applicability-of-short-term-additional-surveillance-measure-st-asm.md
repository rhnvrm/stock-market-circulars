## Summary

BSE has notified that certain securities, listed in the attached Annexure I, have met the criteria for inclusion under the Short-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) framework. Securities have been categorized into ST-ASM Stage I and Stage II, each carrying different margin requirements, effective July 24, 2026. This action follows a series of prior exchange notices dating back to 2018 that established and revised the ASM framework.

## Key Points

- Securities meeting ST-ASM Stage I criteria will attract a margin of 50% or the existing margin, whichever is higher, capped at a maximum of 100%.
- Securities meeting ST-ASM Stage II criteria will attract a margin of 100% or the existing margin, whichever is higher, capped at a maximum of 100%.
- Margins apply to all open positions as on July 23, 2026, and to new positions created from July 24, 2026 onward.
- The ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the Exchange.
- Inclusion under ASM is based solely on market surveillance criteria and should not be interpreted as an adverse action against the concerned company or entity.
- When a scrip exits the ASM framework, its price band will revert to the level applicable before inclusion, unless it remains subject to another surveillance framework, in which case that framework's price band will apply.
- The specific list of securities affected is provided in the attached Annexure I, which is not included in the notice text.

## Regulatory Changes

This notice implements the ST-ASM framework as previously established and revised under exchange notices 20181027-1, 20190719-33, 20201204-56, 20230925-49, 20240920-63, and 20240920-71. No new regulatory framework is introduced; this is a routine application of the existing surveillance criteria to a fresh set of securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must apply the specified margin rates (50% for Stage I, 100% for Stage II) to all open and new positions in the identified securities from July 24, 2026.
- Members should refer to Annexure I for the specific list of affected securities.
- Queries regarding the notice can be directed to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- Notice date: July 22, 2026
- Margin applicability: On all open positions as on July 23, 2026, and new positions created from July 24, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Securities placed under ST-ASM Stage I and Stage II will face significantly higher margin requirements, which typically reduces trading volumes and liquidity in these stocks as leveraged and speculative positions become more costly. This can lead to increased price volatility or a slowdown in trading activity for the affected securities. Since the specific securities are named only in the attached annexure, market participants holding positions in potentially volatile or high-risk mid/small-cap stocks should verify the annexure directly to assess exposure.
