## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for Savani Financials Limited. The company's name has been officially changed to Mantra Capital Limited with effect from October 14, 2025. The certificate was issued by the Central Processing Centre, ROC.

## Key Points

- Old name: SAVANI FINANCIALS LIMITED
- New name: MANTRA CAPITAL LIMITED
- Effective date: October 14, 2025
- Corporate Identification Number (CIN): L67120MH1983PLC031614
- Company type: Limited by shares
- Original incorporation name: SAVANI INVESTMENT & LEASING LIMITED
- Certificate digitally signed by Sweety Kumar, Assistant/Deputy/Registrar of Companies

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate name change process completed under the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014, specifically pursuant to rule 29.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display the old name (Savani Financials Limited) for a period of two years along with its new name at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- All company documentation, letterheads, signage, and official communications must be updated to reflect the new name
- The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders

## Important Dates

- **October 14, 2025**: Effective date of name change
- **October 14, 2027**: End of mandatory two-year period for displaying old name alongside new name

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. Name changes are administrative corporate actions that do not affect the fundamental business operations, financial position, or shareholder equity.

**Operational Impact**: The company will need to update all branding, documentation, and legal paperwork. Trading symbol may be updated by the exchange.

**Stakeholder Impact**: No impact on shareholder rights, liabilities, or holdings. All existing contracts, agreements, and obligations continue under the new name.
