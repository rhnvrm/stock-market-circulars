## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members of fixed record dates for redemption of Commercial Paper issued by several companies. Trading members are advised not to deal in these Commercial Paper instruments as they approach their record and settlement dates.

## Key Points

- Notice applies to the Debt segment under the Listing Operations department.
- Companies affected include Kotak Mahindra Prime Limited (two CP series), Axis Finance Limited, Capri Global Capital Limited, Network18 Media & Investments Limited (two CP series), Mirae Asset Financial Services (India) Private Limited, Bajaj Housing Finance Limited, 360 ONE Alternates Asset Management Limited (two CP series), 360 ONE Prime Limited, Bajaj Financial Securities Limited (two CP series), Tata Realty and Infrastructure Limited, Bajaj Finance Limited, and Fedbank Financial Services Limited.
- Each entry specifies the Scrip Code, Company Name, Scrip ID, ISIN, Record Date, Ex Date, and Settlement DR (deliverable) reference.
- Record/Ex dates for the listed instruments range from 23 July 2026 to 4 August 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a routine operational notice under existing BSE debt segment trading rules regarding Commercial Paper redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must refrain from dealing in the listed Commercial Paper instruments from their respective record/ex dates as redemption proceeds and the instruments approach maturity.

## Important Dates

- 23 July 2026: Record/Ex Date for Kotak Mahindra Prime Limited (KMPL-24-07-26-CP, ISIN INE916D146W7)
- 30 July 2026: Record/Ex Date for Axis Finance Limited (AFL-31-7-26-CP, ISIN INE891K14QB0)
- 3 August 2026: Record/Ex Date for Capri Global Capital Limited, Network18 Media & Investments Limited, Mirae Asset Financial Services (India) Private Limited, Bajaj Housing Finance Limited, 360 ONE Alternates Asset Management Limited, 360 ONE Prime Limited, and Bajaj Financial Securities Limited (multiple CP series maturing 03/08/2026)
- 4 August 2026: Record/Ex Date for Kotak Mahindra Prime Limited, Network18 Media & Investments Limited, 360 ONE Alternates Asset Management Limited, Tata Realty and Infrastructure Limited, Bajaj Finance Limited, Fedbank Financial Services Limited, and Bajaj Financial Securities Limited (multiple CP series maturing 04/08/2026)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, low-impact administrative notice limited to the debt/Commercial Paper segment. It affects trading members' ability to deal in specific short-term debt instruments nearing redemption but has no bearing on equity markets, listed stock prices, or broader market operations.
