## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the equity shares of Atharva Poly-Plast Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange's SME segment in due course. The exact date of listing will be announced via a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company: Atharva Poly-Plast Limited
- Scrip Code: 544812
- Symbol: ATHARVA
- ISIN: INE226801012
- Segment: SME
- Category: Company related, DCS-Listing department
- No attachments accompanied the notice

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a standard listing admission notice under BSE's DCS-Listing department procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should update their systems to recognize the new scrip code, symbol, and ISIN once trading commences. No specific action is required from investors until the listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 06 July 2026
- Date of listing: To be announced via a separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This notice formalizes the upcoming listing of Atharva Poly-Plast Limited's equity shares on BSE's SME platform, enabling trading once the listing date is confirmed. Impact is primarily limited to the company, its shareholders, and SME segment market participants, with minimal broader market significance.
