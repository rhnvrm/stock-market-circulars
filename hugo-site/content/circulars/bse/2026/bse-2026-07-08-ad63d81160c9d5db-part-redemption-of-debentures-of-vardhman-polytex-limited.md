## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that Vardhman Polytex Ltd. has fixed a record date for part redemption of its Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs). Consequent to the part redemption, the debentures will trade with a reduced face value effective 09 July 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: Vardhman Polytex Ltd.
- Scrip Code: 977714
- Scrip ID: VPL-18%-31-3-31-PVT
- ISIN: INE835A07018
- Settlement Number: 668/2026-2027
- Reduced face value: Rs. 53,333.33 per debenture

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine operational notice regarding a scheduled part redemption event on an existing NCD issue.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must ensure trades in the debenture from the ex-date are executed and settled based on the reduced face value of Rs. 53,333.33 per debenture.

## Important Dates

- Record Date: 09 July 2026
- Ex Date: 09 July 2026
- Settlement Number: 668/2026-2027

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and traders of this specific NCD (INE835A07018). Trading members need to adjust systems and settlement processes to reflect the reduced face value from the ex-date to avoid pricing or settlement errors. No broader market or equity impact is expected.
