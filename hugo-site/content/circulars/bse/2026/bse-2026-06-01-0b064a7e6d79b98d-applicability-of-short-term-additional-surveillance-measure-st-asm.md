## Summary

BSE has notified the inclusion of certain securities under Short Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) Stage I and Stage II. Securities under Stage I will attract a minimum margin of 50% (capped at 100%), while those under Stage II will attract a minimum margin of 100%, both effective June 3, 2026, applicable on open positions as of June 2, 2026 and new positions thereafter.

## Key Points

- Securities in Annexure I have satisfied criteria for inclusion under ST-ASM Stage I with applicable margin of 50% or existing margin, whichever is higher (max 100%).
- Securities in Annexure I have also satisfied criteria for inclusion under ST-ASM Stage II with applicable margin of 100% or existing margin, whichever is higher (max 100%).
- ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures.
- Inclusion under ASM is purely a market surveillance action and should not be construed as adverse action against the concerned company.
- On exit from the framework, the price band applicable before shortlisting will be reinstated, unless the scrip is under another surveillance measure.

## Regulatory Changes

This notice is issued further to Exchange notices: 20240920-71 (Sep 20, 2024), 20181027-1 (Oct 27, 2018), 20190719-33 (Jul 19, 2019), 20201204-56 (Dec 4, 2020), 20230925-49 (Sep 25, 2023), and 20240920-63 (Sep 20, 2024) regarding revision of the ASM framework. The current circular implements fresh inclusions under both Stage I and Stage II categories.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure collection of enhanced margins (50% for Stage I, 100% for Stage II) on all open positions as of June 2, 2026.
- Enhanced margins apply to all new positions created from June 3, 2026 onward.
- Members may contact BSE Surveillance at bse.surv@bseindia.com for clarifications.

## Important Dates

- **June 2, 2026**: Reference date for open positions subject to enhanced margins.
- **June 3, 2026**: Effective date for enhanced margin requirements under ST-ASM Stage I and Stage II.

## Impact Assessment

Traders and investors holding or planning positions in the affected securities will face significantly higher margin requirements, increasing the cost of leveraged positions. Stage II securities requiring 100% margin are effectively restricted to fully funded trades, limiting speculative activity. This may lead to reduced liquidity and higher volatility in the affected scrips. The framework is a temporary surveillance measure and does not reflect fundamental issues with the listed companies.
