## Summary

BSE has listed further securities issued under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP/ESOS) by four companies, effective February 6, 2026. The securities include shares from ICICI Prudential Life Insurance (224,000 shares), Larsen & Toubro (30,657 shares), RBL Bank (341,431 shares), and Wipro (154,549 shares). None of the newly listed shares are subject to lock-in restrictions.

## Key Points

- Four companies listing additional securities under ESOP/ESOS schemes
- Total of 750,637 new shares across all four companies
- All securities admitted for trading from February 6, 2026
- No lock-in period applicable to any of the listed securities
- Notice issued on February 5, 2026 by BSE

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. The additional shares represent routine ESOP exercises and constitute a small percentage of outstanding shares for these large-cap companies.

**Trading Impact**: The securities are immediately available for trading without restrictions, providing liquidity to employees exercising their stock options.

**Dilution Impact**: Negligible dilution given the relatively small number of shares compared to the market capitalization and float of these companies.
