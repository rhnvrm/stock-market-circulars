## Summary

BSE has notified the applicability of the Short Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) for securities listed in the attached Annexure I. Securities meeting the criteria are placed under either ST-ASM Stage I (50% margin requirement) or ST-ASM Stage II (100% margin requirement), effective July 1, 2026. The measure applies to all open positions as on June 30, 2026, and all new positions created from July 1, 2026.

## Key Points

- Securities in ST-ASM Stage I will attract a margin rate of 50% or existing margin, whichever is higher, capped at a maximum of 100%.
- Securities in ST-ASM Stage II will attract a margin rate of 100% or existing margin, whichever is higher, capped at 100%.
- Both stages are effective from July 1, 2026, covering open positions as on June 30, 2026, and new positions from July 1, 2026.
- The ST-ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the exchanges.
- Shortlisting under ASM is purely a surveillance action and should not be construed as an adverse action against the concerned company or entity.
- Upon exit from the framework, the price band of the scrip will be reinstated to the pre-ASM level, provided the scrip is not part of any other surveillance measure.

## Regulatory Changes

This notice is issued further to Exchange notices 20240920-71, 20181027-1, 20190719-33, 20201204-56, 20230925-49, and 20240920-63 concerning revisions to the ASM framework. No new framework changes are introduced; this circular applies the existing ST-ASM framework to newly shortlisted securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure that the applicable margin rates (50% for Stage I, 100% for Stage II) are collected from clients on all affected securities.
- Margin requirements apply to open positions as on June 30, 2026, and all new positions created from July 1, 2026.
- Members should refer to the attached Annexure I to identify the specific securities covered under each stage.
- For clarifications, members may contact BSE Surveillance at bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **June 29, 2026**: Notice date; securities shortlisted under ST-ASM Stage I and Stage II announced.
- **June 30, 2026**: Reference date for open positions to which new margin requirements apply.
- **July 1, 2026**: Effective date for new margin rates under ST-ASM Stage I and Stage II.

## Impact Assessment

Market participants holding positions in the shortlisted securities will face significantly higher margin requirements from July 1, 2026. Stage II securities require 100% margin, which may force position reductions for traders unable to meet the enhanced collateral requirements. This can lead to increased selling pressure and potential liquidity tightening in the affected scrips. The measure is designed to curb speculative activity and protect market integrity. The specific securities are listed in Annexure I (not reproduced in the notice text), so members must review the attachment to identify affected holdings.
