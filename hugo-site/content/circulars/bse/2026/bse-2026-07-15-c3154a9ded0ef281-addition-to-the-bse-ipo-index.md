## Summary

BSE Index Services Private Limited has announced that Kusumgar Limited (Exchange ticker: 544821), which was listed on BSE effective July 15, 2026, will be added to the BSE IPO Index. The addition takes effect at the open of trading on July 17, 2026, as referenced in Notice No. 20260714-25.

## Key Points

- Kusumgar Limited (ticker 544821) listed on BSE on Wednesday, July 15, 2026
- The stock will be added to the BSE IPO Index effective the open of Friday, July 17, 2026
- This action will be included in the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE) for the notice date
- Queries can be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

This is a routine index maintenance action by BSE Index Services Private Limited (formerly Asia Index Pvt. Ltd.), a wholly owned subsidiary of BSE Ltd, and does not constitute a regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action is required from listed companies or investors. Index fund managers and products tracking the BSE IPO Index should account for the addition of Kusumgar Limited in their portfolio rebalancing.

## Important Dates

- July 15, 2026: Kusumgar Limited listed on BSE
- July 17, 2026: Effective date for addition of Kusumgar Limited (544821) to the BSE IPO Index

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited primarily to passive funds and index-tracking products benchmarked to the BSE IPO Index, which will need to include Kusumgar Limited in their holdings from July 17, 2026. The broader market impact is minimal, as this is a standard procedural update following a new listing.
