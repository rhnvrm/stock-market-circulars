## Summary

BSE, as part of its routine surveillance process, sought clarification from Rajesh Exports Ltd (Scrip Code: 531500) regarding a news article that appeared on The Hindu Business Line. As of this notice, the company's reply is awaited.

## Key Points

- BSE verifies news articles appearing in newspapers, websites, and television channels to inform investors about their accuracy.
- Clarification was sought from Rajesh Exports Ltd (Scrip Code: 531500) concerning a report on thehindubusinessline.com.
- No attachment was provided with this notice.
- The company's response is currently pending.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine surveillance clarification request, not a regulatory or policy change.

## Compliance Requirements

Rajesh Exports Ltd is required to respond to BSE's clarification request regarding the news article in question.

## Important Dates

Notice issued: 22 July 2026. No deadline specified for the company's reply in the notice.

## Impact Assessment

This notice has limited immediate market impact as it merely records that a clarification has been sought and a reply is awaited. Investors should monitor for the company's official response, which could confirm or refute the news article and potentially affect investor sentiment toward the stock.
