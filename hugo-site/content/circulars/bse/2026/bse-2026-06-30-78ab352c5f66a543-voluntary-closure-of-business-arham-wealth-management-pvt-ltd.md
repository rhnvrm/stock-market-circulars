## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Arham Wealth Management Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 6565) will voluntarily cease business in the Currency Derivatives segment effective Wednesday, July 1, 2026.

## Key Points

- Arham Wealth Management Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 6565) is voluntarily closing its business in the Currency Derivatives segment.
- The cessation of business is effective from Wednesday, July 1, 2026.
- The notice is issued by the Membership Compliance Department of BSE.
- Contact for clarifications: membership.ops@bseindia.com.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative notice regarding the voluntary exit of a trading member from the Currency Derivatives segment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members and counterparties who have dealings with Arham Wealth Management Pvt. Ltd. in the Currency Derivatives segment should take note of the cessation of business effective July 1, 2026, and make appropriate arrangements.

## Important Dates

- **July 1, 2026**: Effective date of voluntary closure of Arham Wealth Management Pvt. Ltd.'s business in the Currency Derivatives segment.

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited to counterparties and clients of Arham Wealth Management Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 6565) in the Currency Derivatives segment. Broader market operations are unaffected. Affected parties should contact the BSE Membership Department at membership.ops@bseindia.com for further clarifications.
