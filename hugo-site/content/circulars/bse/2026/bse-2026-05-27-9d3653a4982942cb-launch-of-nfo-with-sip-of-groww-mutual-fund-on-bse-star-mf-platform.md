## Summary

BSE has notified members that GROWW Mutual Fund will launch the GROWW NIFTY SMALLCAP 250 MOMENTUM QUALITY 100 INDEX FUND as a New Fund Offer (NFO) with SIP facility on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The NFO subscription window opens May 29, 2026, and closes June 12, 2026, with a tentative allotment date of June 19, 2026.

## Key Points

- NFO period runs from May 29, 2026 to June 12, 2026 on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Six scheme variants available: Regular and Direct plans, each with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- ISIN codes assigned: INF666M01PD6, INF666M01PE4, INF666M01PF1 (Regular plans); INF666M01PA2, INF666M01PB0, INF666M01PC8 (Direct plans)
- Tentative allotment date: June 19, 2026
- Lumpsum purchases extended till midnight on June 12, 2026; SIP/XSIP registrations must be authenticated by 3:00 PM on June 12, 2026
- Fund category: Equity (Index Fund tracking NIFTY SMALLCAP 250 MOMENTUM QUALITY 100 Index)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is an operational notice for an NFO availability on the BSE StAR MF Platform under existing mutual fund trading frameworks.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure client funds are initiated by 9:00 AM on T+1 day; transactions without timely fund initiation will be cancelled and reported to RTA/AMC with a 'N' flag
- Transactions with initiated payments remain pending until 3:00 PM on the last AMC funds pay-out day or T+5, whichever is earlier
- Clear funds must be available in the ICCL account by 6:00 PM on June 16, 2026 for allotment eligibility
- Cheque and OTM payments accepted only if clear funds are received in ICCL account by 6:00 PM on June 16, 2026
- Switch transactions accepted during NFO period only if funds are received prior to allotment date; switch-out funds received on or after allotment date are ineligible

## Important Dates

- **May 29, 2026**: NFO subscription opens
- **June 12, 2026**: NFO closure date; SIP/XSIP authentication deadline at 3:00 PM; Lumpsum purchases accepted till midnight
- **June 16, 2026**: Clear funds deadline in ICCL account by 6:00 PM for allotment eligibility
- **June 19, 2026**: Tentative allotment date for all six scheme variants

## Impact Assessment

Low operational impact, limited to BSE StAR MF Platform members (distributors, brokers) facilitating transactions in GROWW Mutual Fund schemes. Investors and members must adhere to the payment and authentication timelines to avoid transaction cancellations. No broader market or regulatory impact anticipated.
