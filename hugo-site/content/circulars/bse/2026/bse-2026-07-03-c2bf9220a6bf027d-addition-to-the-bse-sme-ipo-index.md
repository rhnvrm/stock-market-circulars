## Summary

BSE Index Services Private Limited has announced the addition of Crazy Snacks Limited (Exchange ticker: 544807) to the BSE SME IPO Index. This follows the company's listing on BSE, effective Friday, July 3, 2026, pursuant to Notice No. 20260702-44. The stock will be added to the index effective at the open of trading on Monday, July 6, 2026.

## Key Points

- Crazy Snacks Limited listed on BSE effective July 3, 2026
- Stock (ticker 544807) to be added to the BSE SME IPO Index
- Addition takes effect at market open on July 6, 2026
- Action will be reflected in the corporate action file (*.SDE) for the notice date
- Queries can be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine index maintenance action following a new SME listing, not a regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action required from market participants. Index fund managers and passive investors tracking the BSE SME IPO Index should update their portfolios to reflect the addition effective July 6, 2026.

## Important Dates

- July 3, 2026: Listing of Crazy Snacks Limited on BSE
- July 6, 2026: Effective date of addition to the BSE SME IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

Low market-wide impact. This is a standard index inclusion following an SME IPO listing, primarily relevant to index-tracking funds and investors monitoring the BSE SME IPO Index composition. No impact on trading rules, settlement, or broader market operations.
