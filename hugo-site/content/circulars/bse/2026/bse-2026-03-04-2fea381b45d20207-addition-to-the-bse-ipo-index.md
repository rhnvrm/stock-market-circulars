## Summary

BSE Index Services Private Limited has announced the addition of PNGS Reva Diamond Jewellery Limited (Exchange ticker: 544718) to the BSE IPO Index. The stock was listed on BSE on March 04, 2026, and will be included in the index effective at the open of March 06, 2026.

## Key Points

- PNGS Reva Diamond Jewellery Limited (ticker: 544718) listed on BSE on Wednesday, March 04, 2026
- The stock will be added to the BSE IPO Index effective at market open on Friday, March 06, 2026
- This action follows Notice No. 20260302-41 which referenced the upcoming listing
- The corporate action has been included in the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE) on March 04, 2026
- Contact for queries: bseindex@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index rebalancing action by BSE Index Services Private Limited to reflect the new listing on the BSE IPO Index.

## Compliance Requirements

No direct compliance requirements for market participants. Index-tracking fund managers and ETF operators tracking the BSE IPO Index should update their portfolios to include PNGS Reva Diamond Jewellery Limited (544718) effective March 06, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **March 04, 2026**: PNGS Reva Diamond Jewellery Limited listed on BSE; corporate action file updated
- **March 06, 2026**: Effective date for addition of 544718 to the BSE IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine index addition with limited broad market impact. The primary impact is on funds and investment products that track or benchmark against the BSE IPO Index, which will need to rebalance to include PNGS Reva Diamond Jewellery Limited. Individual equity investors are not directly affected unless they hold BSE IPO Index-linked instruments. The stock's inclusion may attract additional passive fund buying on or before March 06, 2026.
