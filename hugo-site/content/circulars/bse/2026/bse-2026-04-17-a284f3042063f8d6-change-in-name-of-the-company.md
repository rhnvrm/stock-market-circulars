## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that Nibe Ordnance and Maritime Ltd (scrip code 512091) has changed its corporate name to Global Defence Industries Limited. The SCRIP ID and ABBREVIATED NAME on the BOLT Plus System will be updated accordingly effective April 22, 2026.

## Key Points

- Scrip code 512091 remains unchanged
- Old name: Nibe Ordnance and Maritime Ltd
- New name: Global Defence Industries Limited
- New SCRIP ID on BOLT Plus System: GDIL
- New Abbreviated Name on BOLT Plus System: GDIL
- Change is effective April 22, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

The SCRIP ID and ABBREVIATED NAME used in the BOLT Plus trading system will be changed from the existing identifiers to GDIL to reflect the company's new legal name.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must update their records and systems to reflect the new company name and scrip identifiers (GDIL) for scrip code 512091 effective April 22, 2026. Orders and references using old identifiers should be transitioned accordingly.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: April 17, 2026
- Effective Date of Name/ID Change: April 22, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate name change with moderate operational impact on trading members who use BOLT Plus System. Traders must update watchlists, algos, and internal references from the old scrip ID to GDIL. No change to scrip code 512091 or the underlying equity itself; market impact is expected to be low beyond the administrative update.
