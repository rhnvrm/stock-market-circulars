## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260311-45 informing trading members that PAN-India Drugs & Chemicals Limited, an Exclusively Listed Company (ELC), will be removed from the BSE Dissemination Board effective Thursday, March 12, 2026. The removal is pursuant to SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD/DSA/CIR/P/2016/110 dated October 10, 2016, and is triggered by the company exercising an exit option.

## Key Points

- PAN-India Drugs & Chemicals Limited (CIN: U24232MP1992PLC007026) will be removed from the BSE Dissemination Board effective March 12, 2026
- Reason for removal is exercise of Exit Option by the company
- Post removal, shares of the company will no longer be available on the Dissemination Board of BSE
- Registered address: Plot No. 323, Sector (E), Industrial Area, Sanwer Road, Indore - 452006

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. The action is carried out in accordance with the existing SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD/DSA/CIR/P/2016/110 dated October 10, 2016, which governs the framework for exclusively listed companies on the Dissemination Board.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are advised to take note of the removal and cease any dissemination board transactions for PAN-India Drugs & Chemicals Limited from March 12, 2026
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the company (Mr. Ashish Jayant Shah, Director; panindia1992@gmail.com) or BSE (Mr. Parag Jain, Manager - Listing Compliance & Operations; Ph: 022-2272 8685; direct.listing@bseindia.com)

## Important Dates

- **March 11, 2026**: Notice issued by BSE
- **March 12, 2026 (Thursday)**: Effective date of removal from the BSE Dissemination Board

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited and isolated to shareholders of PAN-India Drugs & Chemicals Limited who had access to the Dissemination Board for trading. Following the removal, shares of this company will no longer be tradable on the BSE Dissemination Board. The broader market and trading community are unaffected. This is a routine administrative action with no systemic implications.
