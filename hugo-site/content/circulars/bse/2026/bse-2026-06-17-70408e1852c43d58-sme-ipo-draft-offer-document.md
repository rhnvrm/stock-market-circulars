## Summary

BSE has published a notice making the Draft Offer Document of **S. K. Offset Limited** available on the BSE SME website. The document was filed on June 16, 2026, and contains all disclosures as required under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations. It will remain available for public comments for a minimum of 21 days from the date of the notice.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by S. K. Offset Limited on June 16, 2026
- Document available on the BSE SME website: https://www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public comments invited within 21 days from June 17, 2026 (deadline: on or before July 8, 2026)
- Comments to be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Document complies with Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural notice under SEBI's ICDR (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations requiring public availability of the Draft Red Herring Prospectus (DRHP) for SME IPOs.

## Compliance Requirements

- Investors, analysts, and the public may review the Draft Offer Document on the BSE SME portal
- Any comments or objections must be submitted within 21 days to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- No action required from listed entities or market participants unless they wish to submit comments

## Important Dates

- **Filing Date:** June 16, 2026
- **Notice Date:** June 17, 2026
- **Public Comment Deadline:** July 8, 2026 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a routine SME IPO disclosure notice for S. K. Offset Limited, a single company seeking to list on the BSE SME platform. The notice invites public scrutiny of the draft offer document before the IPO proceeds, which is a standard regulatory safeguard under ICDR Regulations. No trading or compliance obligations are imposed on existing market participants.
