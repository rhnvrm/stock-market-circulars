## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260603-33 informs trading members that 16 companies have fixed record dates for the redemption of their Commercial Papers (CPs). Trading members are advised not to deal in the commercial papers of the listed companies from their respective ex-dates onward.

## Key Points

- 16 companies have set record dates for CP redemption between 05 June 2026 and 09 June 2026
- Trading members must cease dealing in the affected CPs from their respective ex-dates
- All suspended CPs are in the Debt segment under Listing Operations
- Settlement DRs are in the 645/2026-2027 to 647/2026-2027 range
- Issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President - Listing Compliance and Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard operational notice under BSE's existing listing and trading rules requiring suspension of debt instrument trading ahead of redemption record dates.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the commercial papers listed below from their respective ex-dates
- Members should update their systems to block these CPs from the applicable ex-dates

## Impact Assessment

Impact is confined to debt segment participants holding or trading these specific commercial paper instruments. This is a routine redemption-cycle action with no broader equity market implications. The bulk of suspensions (14 of 16 CPs) are effective 09 June 2026, with one each on 05 June and 08 June. Investors holding these CPs will receive redemption proceeds on the scheduled settlement dates under DR series 645-647/2026-2027.
