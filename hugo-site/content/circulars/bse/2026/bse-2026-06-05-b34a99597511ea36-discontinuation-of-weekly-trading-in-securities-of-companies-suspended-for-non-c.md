## Summary

BSE has announced the discontinuation of weekly Trade-for-Trade (Z group) trading in the securities of Vasudhagama Enterprises Ltd (Scrip Code: 539291) with effect from June 23, 2026. This follows the completion of the mandated six-month period during which the company's shares were permitted to trade only on the first trading day of every week, as a consequence of non-compliance with SEBI LODR Regulation 27(2).

## Key Points

- Trading in Vasudhagama Enterprises Ltd (539291) on a weekly Trade-for-Trade basis in the Z group will be **discontinued w.e.f. June 23, 2026**.
- The six-month weekly trading window, mandated under SEBI's Master Circular, is set to expire on **June 22, 2026**.
- The suspension was originally imposed vide Exchange Notice No. 20251029-4 dated October 29, 2025, for non-compliance with Regulation 27(2) of SEBI LODR 2015 (Corporate Governance Compliance report) for **two consecutive quarters — March 2025 and June 2025**.
- The applicable regulatory framework is SEBI Master Circular No. HO/49/14/14(7)2025-CFD-POD2/I/3762/2026, originally issued July 11, 2023 and last updated January 30, 2026 (erstwhile Circular No. SEBI/HO/CFD/PoD2/CIR/P/0155 dated November 11, 2024).

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced. This notice applies the existing SEBI Master Circular provision that mandates six months of restricted weekly trading for companies suspended for LODR non-compliance, after which the weekly trading window is formally discontinued.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Vasudhagama Enterprises Ltd** must ensure ongoing compliance with Regulation 27(2) of SEBI LODR 2015 (quarterly Corporate Governance Compliance report submission) to avoid re-imposition of trading restrictions.
- Brokers and trading members should note the cessation of weekly trading sessions for scrip 539291 after June 22, 2026.
- For clarifications, stakeholders may contact BSE at **bse.soplodr@bseindia.com**.

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Original suspension notice (Notice No. 20251029-4) | October 29, 2025 |
| Six-month weekly trading window expires | June 22, 2026 |
| Weekly Trade-for-Trade trading discontinued | June 23, 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

The discontinuation of the weekly trading window effectively removes the only available avenue for trading in Vasudhagama Enterprises Ltd shares on BSE, since full suspension remains in force. This is a procedural step completing the regulatory lifecycle of the restriction rather than a new adverse action. Market impact is minimal given the company's small size and the prior restriction already in place. Investors holding shares in the scrip should be aware that no trading will be possible after June 22, 2026 until the underlying suspension is formally lifted.
