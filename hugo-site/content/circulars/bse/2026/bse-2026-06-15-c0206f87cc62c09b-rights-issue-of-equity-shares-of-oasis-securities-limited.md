## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260615-37 informs trading members that OASIS SECURITIES LIMITED (BSE Code: 512489) has fixed a record date for a rights issue of equity shares. The company will offer 3 new equity shares of Re.1/- face value at a premium of Rs.9/- per share (total Rs.10/- per share) for every 2 equity shares currently held. Transactions in the company's equity shares must be conducted on an ex-rights basis from 18 June 2026 onwards.

## Key Points

- OASIS SECURITIES LIMITED (BSE Code: 512489) has announced a rights issue of equity shares.
- Rights ratio: 3 new shares for every 2 existing shares held.
- Issue price: Rs.10/- per share (Re.1/- face value + Rs.9/- premium).
- Full application amount of Rs.10/- per share is payable upfront on application.
- Record Date: 18 June 2026.
- Ex-Rights Date: 18 June 2026 (Settlement No. DR-654/2026-2027).
- Segment: Dematerialised Securities – Rolling Settlement Segment.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is a standard corporate action notification under BSE's listing compliance and operations framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all transactions in the equity shares of OASIS SECURITIES LIMITED are executed on an **ex-rights basis** with effect from 18 June 2026.
- Members must take note of the settlement number DR-654/2026-2027 for reference in their operations.

## Impact Assessment

This rights issue has a moderate impact on existing shareholders of OASIS SECURITIES LIMITED. The 3:2 rights ratio (150% entitlement) is dilutive; the share price is expected to adjust downward to reflect the ex-rights value from 18 June 2026. The full issue price of Rs.10/- is payable on application, requiring existing shareholders to commit capital to maintain their proportional stake. Broader market impact is negligible given the company's small-cap profile.
