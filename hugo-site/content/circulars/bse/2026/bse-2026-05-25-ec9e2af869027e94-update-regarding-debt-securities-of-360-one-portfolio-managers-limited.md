## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (continuing from Notice No. 20240807-21 dated August 07, 2024) informing trading members that debt securities of 360 One Portfolio Managers Limited under Scrip Code 975883 (ISIN INE196P07237) have undergone a part redemption by quantity. Following this redemption, 1,250 units remain available for trading.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 975883
- ISIN: INE196P07237
- Issuer: 360 One Portfolio Managers Limited
- Redemption type: Part Redemption by Quantity
- Existing Quantity prior to redemption: 3,550 units
- Redeemed Quantity: 2,300 units
- Net Quantity available for trading post-redemption: 1,250 units

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory rule changes. This is an operational update reflecting a reduction in the outstanding quantity of the listed debt security due to partial redemption by the issuer.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to note the updated outstanding quantity (1,250 units) for ISIN INE196P07237 and ensure their systems reflect the revised tradeable quantity. Members with queries may contact:

- **Officer:** Mr. Akshay Arolkar
- **Phone:** +91 22 2272 8352
- **Email:** debt.listing@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** May 25, 2026
- **Original Notice:** 20240807-21 dated August 07, 2024

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. The part redemption reduces the tradeable quantity of ISIN INE196P07237 from 3,550 to 1,250 units. This is a routine corporate action for debt securities and affects only holders or prospective traders of this specific scrip. Liquidity in this instrument will be reduced proportionally given the smaller outstanding float.
