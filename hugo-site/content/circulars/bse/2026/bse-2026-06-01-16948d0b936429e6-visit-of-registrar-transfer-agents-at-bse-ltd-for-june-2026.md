## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that representatives of various Registrar and Transfer Agents (RTAs) will visit the Department of Investor Services (DIS) at BSE's Cama Building, Dalal Street, Mumbai, during June 8–12, 2026. Members and investors with complaints against client companies of these RTAs may approach the respective RTA representatives during the scheduled hours.

## Key Points

- RTA visits scheduled from June 8 (Monday) to June 12 (Friday), 2026
- Location: Department of Investor Services, 1st Floor, Cama Building, Dalal Street, Mumbai – 400001
- Ten RTAs are scheduled across five days
- Investors with grievances against RTA client companies may approach representatives during their respective time slots
- Contact officers: Mr. Akshay Kulte (2272 8415) and Mr. Anjappa Kadmur (2272 5770)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance obligations imposed on trading members. Members are encouraged to inform investors about this facility for grievance redressal.
