## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 5,43,531 new equity shares of Power & Instrumentation (Gujarat) Limited (Scrip Code: 543912, ISIN: INE557Z01018) have been listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Tuesday, May 26, 2026. These shares were issued on a preferential basis to Promoters and Non-Promoters pursuant to the conversion of warrants at an issue price of Rs. 83.75/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- + premium Rs. 73.75/-).

## Key Points

- 5,43,531 new equity shares listed effective May 26, 2026
- Issued at Rs. 83.75/- per share (Rs. 10/- face value + Rs. 73.75/- premium) via preferential allotment on conversion of warrants
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 20325370 to 20868900
- Date of allotment: March 18, 2026
- ISIN: INE557Z01018

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification following completion of a preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to take note of the new securities being available for trading from May 26, 2026. Lock-in restrictions apply to the allotted shares as detailed below.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** March 18, 2026
- **Trading Commencement:** May 26, 2026 (Tuesday)
- **Lock-in expiry (2,15,000 shares — Dist. Nos. 20325370 to 20540369):** December 1, 2027
- **Lock-in expiry (3,28,531 shares — Dist. Nos. 20540370 to 20868900):** December 1, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The addition of 5,43,531 shares represents a modest increase in the tradeable float of Power & Instrumentation (Gujarat) Limited. The majority of the newly issued shares (both tranches) are subject to lock-in periods extending to December 2026 and December 2027 respectively, limiting immediate selling pressure. Impact is confined to existing and prospective shareholders of this company and has no broader market implications.
