## Summary

BSE Clearing Corporation (BSECL) has issued the settlement programme for the Futures & Options (F&O) segment for the period from 1 August 2026 to 31 August 2026, under Notice No. 20260713-17 dated 13 July 2026. The notice specifies the settlement schedule (referenced as attachment FNO082026) that market participants and clearing members must follow for the month.

## Key Points

- Notice pertains to the Derivatives segment, issued by BSECL (BSE Clearing Corporation)
- Category: Settlement/RMS (Risk Management System)
- Covers the settlement calendar for F&O trades executed during August 2026
- Attachment FNO082026 contains the detailed day-wise settlement schedule

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced; this is a routine periodic publication of the settlement calendar for the F&O segment.

## Compliance Requirements

Clearing members, trading members, and market participants dealing in the F&O segment should align their back-office and funds/securities pay-in/pay-out processes with the published settlement schedule for August 2026.

## Important Dates

Settlement programme period: 01 August 2026 to 31 August 2026. Detailed day-wise dates are provided in the attachment (FNO082026).

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational circular with low market impact. It carries medium operational relevance for clearing members and trading participants who need to plan settlement obligations, margin funding, and pay-in/pay-out schedules for the derivatives segment during the specified period.
