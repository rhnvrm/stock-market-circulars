## Summary

BSE has announced the settlement schedule for Matrimony.com Limited's buyback through the Acquisition Window mechanism. The bidding window will be open from February 5-11, 2026, with obligation download on February 17, 2026, and final settlement on February 18, 2026. Settlement number 2026011 has been assigned for this buyback.

## Key Points

- Bidding period: February 5-11, 2026
- Settlement number: 2026011
- Obligation download date: February 17, 2026
- Final settlement date: February 18, 2026
- Demat shares must be delivered through Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism only
- Market Type "(Buyback)" and settlement number must be specified in depository systems
- Securities will be earmarked/blocked in client accounts automatically
- No EPN file upload required by members and custodians

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular implements existing guidelines per Notice No.20200528-32 dated May 28, 2020 regarding the Revised Guidelines of Mechanism for acquisition of shares through Stock Exchange.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Members/Custodians**: Must use Early Pay-in mechanism through CDSL or NSDL for demat share delivery
- **Members/Custodians**: Must ensure Market Type "(Buyback)" and Settlement No. 2026011 are used in depository systems
- **Members/Custodians**: Must complete early pay-in process sufficiently in advance before BSE OTB bidding window closure
- **Members/Custodians**: Must ensure early pay-in effect is reflected in BSE/ICCL systems before prescribed timelines for bid acceptance/confirmation
- **Members**: Should refer to MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for client-wise stamp duty charges
- **Note**: CDSL & NSDL early pay-in securities will be automatically earmarked/blocked - no EPN file upload required

## Important Dates

- **February 5, 2026**: Bid start date
- **February 11, 2026**: Bid end date
- **February 17, 2026**: Obligation download date
- **February 18, 2026**: Settlement date

## Impact Assessment

**Shareholder Impact**: Shareholders of Matrimony.com Limited wishing to participate in the buyback must ensure their shares are delivered through the early pay-in mechanism during the specified bidding window.

**Member/Custodian Impact**: Brokers and custodians handling client participation must ensure timely completion of early pay-in processes and proper use of settlement identifiers in depository systems. The automatic earmarking feature simplifies the process by eliminating EPN file requirements.

**Operational Impact**: Standard buyback settlement procedure with clear timelines. The 7-day bidding window provides adequate time for participation, and the early pay-in requirement ensures settlement certainty.
