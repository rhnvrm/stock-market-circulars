## Summary

BSE has prohibited and suspended trading in the equity shares of G G Engineering Ltd. (BSE Scrip Code: 540614) effective Friday, June 12, 2026. The action follows a site visit by the Exchange during which the company was found not to be in existence at its registered office address. The suspension is described as an interim measure.

## Key Points

- Trading in securities of G G Engineering Ltd. (Scrip Code: 540614) is prohibited/suspended from June 12, 2026.
- During a BSE site visit, the company was not found at its registered office address.
- The suspension is an interim measure pending further action.
- All trading members and investors are directed to take note of the suspension.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced. The action is taken under BSE's existing powers to suspend trading in listed securities where a company is found non-existent or unresponsive at its registered address, as a protective interim measure.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members:** Must not execute any trades in the securities of G G Engineering Ltd. from June 12, 2026 onwards until further notice.
- **Investors:** Should take note of the suspension and refrain from placing orders in this scrip.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 11, 2026
- **Suspension Effective Date:** Friday, June 12, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Existing shareholders of G G Engineering Ltd. will be unable to sell or buy the scrip on the BSE platform from June 12, 2026. The inability to locate the company at its registered office is a serious red flag suggesting potential shell company activity, corporate fraud, or abandonment. Investors holding this scrip face significant liquidity risk as no exit route is available during the suspension period. The interim nature of the suspension suggests the Exchange may take further action, including potential delisting, depending on the outcome of its investigation.
