## Summary

BSE, in continuation of its earlier notice dated August 6, 2025, has informed Trading Members that the debt securities of KEELZ FACILITY MANAGEMENT SERVICES PRIVATE LIMITED, bearing Scrip Code 976986 and ISIN INE1QV007010, will be available for trading with revised terms effective Thursday, July 16, 2026. The revision follows a debt restructuring exercise, with details specified in an accompanying Annexure.

## Key Points

- Notice is a follow-up to Exchange Notice No. 20250806-31 dated 06 Aug 2025 concerning the same debt securities.
- Scrip Code: 976986; ISIN: INE1QV007010.
- The debt securities will trade with revised terms due to a debt restructuring.
- Revised terms are detailed in the Annexure attached to the notice (not reproduced in full here).
- Effective date for trading with revised terms: Thursday, July 16, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced; this is an administrative/operational update reflecting a debt restructuring already undertaken by the issuer, now being implemented on the exchange trading system.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members are required to take note of the revised trading terms for the specified ISIN and update their systems/records accordingly. Queries can be directed to BSE's Debt Listing contact (Ms. Bhairavi Bondale / Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President) at debt.listing@bseindia.com or +91 22 2272 8597.

## Important Dates

- Original notice: 06 August 2025 (Notice No. 20250806-31)
- Current notice date: 15 July 2026
- Effective date for revised trading terms: 16 July 2026 (Thursday)

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and traders of this specific debt security (ISIN INE1QV007010) of Keelz Facility Management Services Pvt Ltd. It reflects the operational outcome of a debt restructuring, which may alter coupon rates, maturity, or other terms — investors and trading members holding this security should review the Annexure for specific changes to pricing, yield, or repayment terms. No broader market-wide impact is expected.
