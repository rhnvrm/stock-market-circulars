## Summary

Anubhav Plast Limited's equity shares will be listed and admitted to dealings on BSE under the 'MT' Group of Securities effective Monday, June 29, 2026. The scrip will be placed in the Trade-for-Trade segment for 10 trading days as per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012.

## Key Points

- **Listing Date:** June 29, 2026 (Monday)
- **Scrip Code:** 544800 | **Scrip ID:** ANUBHAV | **ISIN:** INE1O7201010
- **Total Securities:** 1,10,00,000 Equity Shares of Rs. 10/- each, fully paid up
- **Distinctive Number Range:** 1 to 11,000,000
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 80/- per share (Face Value Rs. 10/- + Premium Rs. 70/-)
- **Market Lot:** 1,600 equity shares
- **Group:** MT Group of Securities
- **Segment:** SME
- Trading will be in dematerialised form only
- Special Pre-open Session (SPOS) applicable on June 29, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The listing follows standard SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000 (dematerialised trading only) and SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 (Trade-for-Trade segment for new listings).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade this scrip only in dematerialised form.
- All trades must be in the minimum market lot of 1,600 equity shares or multiples thereof until modified by the Exchange with at least one month's prior notice.
- Trading members must participate in the Special Pre-open Session (SPOS) on June 29, 2026 (refer Exchange notice no. 20120216-29 dated February 16, 2012 for SPOS procedures).
- Lock-in details as per Annexure I and shareholding pattern as per Annexure II of the circular.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** June 24, 2026
- **Listing Effective Date:** June 29, 2026
- **Trade-for-Trade Period:** 10 trading days from June 29, 2026
- **SPOS Date:** June 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard SME IPO listing on BSE. Impact is limited to trading members dealing in SME securities and investors who participated in the Anubhav Plast Limited IPO. The Trade-for-Trade restriction for the first 10 trading days means no intraday trading is permitted — all trades must result in delivery, which may reduce speculative activity and liquidity initially. The Market Maker is CapitalSquare Advisors Private Limited, which is responsible for providing liquidity in this scrip post-listing.
