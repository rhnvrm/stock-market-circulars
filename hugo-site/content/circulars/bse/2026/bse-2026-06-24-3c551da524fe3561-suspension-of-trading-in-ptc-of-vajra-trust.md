## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing all trading members that Axis Trustee Services Limited has communicated the early redemption of Pass-through Certificates (PTCs) issued by Vajra 012 Trust. As a result, trading in the specified PTC is suspended with effect from June 25, 2026.

## Key Points

- Axis Trustee Services Limited notified BSE of the early redemption of PTCs of Vajra 012 Trust.
- The affected security is: **VAJRA 12-9.25%-20-10-28-PTC** (ISIN: INE10VT15014, Scrip Code: 975906).
- Trading members are advised **not to deal** in the above-mentioned PTC from June 25, 2026 onwards.
- Reference: DR-659/2026-2027.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced. This notice implements the suspension of trading in a specific debt security following an early redemption event as communicated by the trustee.

## Compliance Requirements

- All trading members must immediately cease buying or selling VAJRA 12-9.25%-20-10-28-PTC (Scrip Code: 975906, ISIN: INE10VT15014) effective June 25, 2026.
- Trading members must take note of this notice and ensure compliance within their systems and operations.

## Important Dates

- **June 24, 2026**: Date of notice issuance.
- **June 25, 2026**: Effective date of trading suspension in the PTC.

## Impact Assessment

The early redemption and consequent trading suspension directly impacts holders and traders of Vajra 012 Trust PTCs. Any open positions or pending orders in Scrip Code 975906 must be settled or cancelled before the suspension takes effect. Market participants holding these PTCs should coordinate with Axis Trustee Services Limited regarding the redemption process and proceeds.
