## Summary

BSE has informed its members that THE WEALTH COMPANY AMC has revised the minimum and additional application amount for purchase, switch, and minimum redemption amount in The Wealth Company Arbitrage Fund and The Wealth Company Liquid Fund on the BSE StAR MF platform. The revised amount takes effect immediately.

## Key Points

- Both The Wealth Company Arbitrage Fund and The Wealth Company Liquid Fund are affected.
- The minimum/additional application amount for purchase and switch has been reduced from Rs. 1,000 to Rs. 100 (in multiples of Rs. 1/- thereafter).
- The change applies to transactions on the BSE StAR MF platform with immediate effect.
- A Notice-cum-Addendum issued by THE WEALTH COMPANY AMC is attached to the circular for reference.

## Regulatory Changes

THE WEALTH COMPANY AMC issued a Notice-cum-Addendum revising the minimum application thresholds:

| Scheme | Existing Min/Additional Amount | Revised Min/Additional Amount |
|---|---|---|
| The Wealth Company Arbitrage Fund | Rs. 1,000 and multiples of Rs. 1/- | Rs. 100 and multiples of Rs. 1/- |
| The Wealth Company Liquid Fund | Rs. 1,000 and multiples of Rs. 1/- | Rs. 100 and multiples of Rs. 1/- |

## Compliance Requirements

- BSE members and distributors operating on the BSE StAR MF platform must take note of the revised minimum application amounts.
- Members are advised to ensure necessary system/process updates at their end to reflect the new thresholds for these two schemes.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** Immediate (from 18 June 2026).

## Impact Assessment

The reduction in minimum application amount from Rs. 1,000 to Rs. 100 lowers the entry barrier for retail investors in both The Wealth Company Arbitrage Fund and The Wealth Company Liquid Fund. This is a routine AMC-level administrative change with limited broader market impact. Distributors and members on the BSE StAR MF platform need to update their systems accordingly to accept lower-value transactions for these schemes.
