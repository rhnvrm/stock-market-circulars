## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 37,735 new equity shares of Cyber Media India Ltd (Scrip Code: 532640) are approved for trading effective Thursday, April 30, 2026. These shares arise from the conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up shares of Rs. 10/- each, issued on a rights basis.

## Key Points

- 37,735 equity shares of Cyber Media India Ltd admitted for trading on BSE
- Shares result from conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up at Rs. 10/- each on rights basis
- ISIN: INE278G01037
- Distinctive numbers: 20620658 to 20658392
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification under BSE's listing operations for newly issued securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are permitted to trade in these new securities from April 30, 2026. No specific compliance action is required from members beyond standard trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: April 29, 2026
- Trading commencement date: April 30, 2026 (Thursday)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The addition of 37,735 shares is a small incremental increase to the existing float of Cyber Media India Ltd. The shares are pari-passu with existing equity, so no change in shareholder rights or economic entitlements occurs. Relevant primarily to existing rights shareholders who converted their partly paid shares.
