## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in commercial papers issued by 9 companies effective from January 6-8, 2026. The suspension is due to companies fixing record dates for commercial paper redemption. Trading members are advised not to deal in these specific commercial paper instruments from the respective no-dealings dates.

## Key Points

- 9 commercial paper instruments affected across 8 distinct issuers
- Trading suspension dates range from January 6-8, 2026
- Record dates coincide with no-dealings dates for all instruments
- Applies to debt segment only
- Trading members must comply with suspension directives

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notification for commercial paper redemption procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any deals in the listed commercial papers from respective no-dealings dates
- Compliance with record date requirements for redemption purposes
- Adherence to DR (Debt Registry) notifications: DR-792/2025-2026, DR-793/2025-2026, and DR-794/2025-2026

## Important Dates

**January 6, 2026:**
- Julius Baer Capital (India) Private Limited (INE824H14SM0) - Record Date & No Dealings From
- Adani Enterprises Ltd. (INE423A14YJ0) - Record Date & No Dealings From

**January 7, 2026:**
- ARKA FINCAP LIMITED (INE03W114666) - Record Date & No Dealings From
- Axis Finance Limited (INE891K14PP2) - Record Date & No Dealings From

**January 8, 2026:**
- Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited (INE523L14AJ4) - Record Date & No Dealings From
- VIVRITI CAPITAL LIMITED (INE01HV14953) - Record Date & No Dealings From
- Tata Housing Development Company Limited (INE582L14IY2) - Record Date & No Dealings From

- Bajaj Financial Securities Limited (INE01C314DP7) - Record Date & No Dealings From

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - affects debt market participants dealing in commercial papers. No impact on equity markets.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members must update systems to block trading in specified ISINs. Temporary liquidity impact for holders of these commercial papers during suspension period.

**Investor Impact:** Investors holding these commercial papers should be aware of upcoming redemptions. No new positions can be taken from the respective no-dealings dates.

**Sectoral Distribution:** Affects diverse sectors including financial services (Julius Baer, Axis Finance, Nuvama, Vivriti Capital, Bajaj Financial), conglomerates (Adani Enterprises), housing finance (Tata Housing), and technology distribution (Redington).
