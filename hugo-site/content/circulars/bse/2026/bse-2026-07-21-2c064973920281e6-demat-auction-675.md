## Summary

BSE Limited, through Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd., has invited offers from trading members for purchase of securities in Demat Auction 675, conducted in respect of Rolling Settlement No. DR-675/2026-27. The auction process, pay-in schedule, and related procedural guidelines are detailed in the notice.

## Key Points

- Auction offers are invited from trading members for securities under Rolling Settlement DR-675/2026-27.
- Auction offer entry window: 02:00 p.m. to 02:45 p.m. on Tuesday, July 21, 2026.
- Auction matching: 02:45 p.m. to 03:00 p.m.
- Delivery statement and report of accepted auction offers: 03:00 p.m. to 03:30 p.m.
- Money statement and final reports: 03:35 p.m. to 05:30 p.m.
- Auction pay-in is scheduled for Wednesday, July 22, 2026 at 08:00 a.m.
- Members and custodians must check their auction pay-in obligations and execute securities pay-in within depository cut-off timings.
- Under the Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment, settlement will be done through the Clearing Member of a Trading Member.
- The prior restriction on trading members offering securities in auction for settlements where they had short-delivered such securities has been removed.
- Trading members are cautioned to exercise care while punching security codes and rates, as no corrections are permitted after punching errors.
- The Exchange reserves the right to accept or reject any offer without assigning reasons.
- Members are advised to refer to the cutoff rate on the Auction Offer Entry Screen and to notices on Corporate Actions (Ex-Rights/Ex-Bonus/Ex-Split etc.) affecting relevant securities.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a routine operational notice implementing existing Uniform Membership structure provisions for settlement through Clearing Members and removal of a prior restriction on offering short-delivered securities in auction.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members and custodians must verify auction pay-in obligations for securities and complete securities pay-in within depository cut-off timings. Members must exercise caution while entering security codes and rates during the offer entry window, as punching errors cannot be corrected after submission.

## Important Dates

- Auction offer entry: 02:00 p.m. – 02:45 p.m., Tuesday, July 21, 2026
- Auction matching: 02:45 p.m. – 03:00 p.m., July 21, 2026
- Delivery statement/report of accepted offers: 03:00 p.m. – 03:30 p.m., July 21, 2026
- Money statement and final reports: 03:35 p.m. – 05:30 p.m., July 21, 2026
- Auction pay-in: 08:00 a.m., Wednesday, July 22, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, procedural market-operations notice affecting trading members and custodians involved in the settlement auction process. It does not pertain to any specific listed company or carry broader market impact, but non-compliance with pay-in timelines could result in penalties for affected members.
