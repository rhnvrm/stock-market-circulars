## Summary

Kinara Capital Private Limited has informed BSE about the revised actual reduced face value of its debentures following a part redemption. The debentures (ISIN: INE200W07381, Scrip Code: 975472) with a coupon rate of 13% maturing on December 6, 2027, will now trade with a reduced face value of Rs. 89,135.69 per debenture.

## Key Points

- Company: Kinara Capital Private Limited
- Debenture ISIN: INE200W07381
- Scrip Code: 975472
- Coupon Rate: 13%
- Maturity Date: December 6, 2027
- Revised Reduced Face Value: Rs. 89,135.69 per debenture
- Nature: Privately placed debentures
- Notice Reference: DR-813/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine corporate action notification regarding part redemption of debentures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must take note of the revised reduced face value
- All trading in these debentures must reflect the new reduced face value from the effective date
- Trading members should update their systems and records accordingly

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: February 3, 2026
- **Effective Date**: February 4, 2026 - Debentures will trade with the revised reduced face value

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This notification affects only holders of specific privately placed debentures of Kinara Capital Private Limited. The part redemption is a standard corporate action that reduces the outstanding principal amount.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Trading members need to update the face value in their systems. The debentures will continue trading with the revised face value, which reflects the partial return of principal to debenture holders.

**Investor Impact**: Neutral to positive for debenture holders - Part redemption returns capital to investors while maintaining the coupon rate on the remaining outstanding amount until maturity.
