## Summary

BSE has listed commercial paper issued on a private placement basis by five companies on the debt market segment effective January 29, 2026. The securities include fresh listings from Julius Baer Capital (India) Private Limited, Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited, Bajaj Finance Limited, Poonawalla Fincorp Limited, and Birla Group Holdings Private Limited, along with a further listing from Embassy Office (details incomplete in source).

## Key Points

- All securities will be traded only in dematerialized form under specified ISIN numbers
- Trading denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Total quantity listed: 34,700 units across five issuers (face value Rs. 500,000 each)
- All commercial papers carry CRISIL A1+ and/or ICRA A1+ ratings
- Interest rates range from 7.55% to 7.99% p.a.
- Redemption periods range from April 2026 to January 2027

- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only

- **Market Lot**: 1 unit

- **Interest Payment**: Cumulative (paid at redemption)

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the specified ISIN numbers for trading these securities
- All transactions must be in dematerialized form
- Minimum trading denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs must be maintained

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational circular for debt market participants. The listing expands the commercial paper options available for institutional investors and debt traders. The securities carry top-tier credit ratings (A1+) and represent short to medium-term debt instruments from established financial and corporate entities. Impact is limited to debt market segment with no effect on equity trading or retail investors.
