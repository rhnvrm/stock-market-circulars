## Summary

Skybridge Interactive LLP (Acquirer) has announced an open offer to acquire up to 1,32,92,000 fully paid-up equity shares of BABA ARTS LIMITED (BAL), representing 25.32% of the fully paid-up equity and voting share capital, at a price of Rs. 6.00 per share payable in cash. The offer is made pursuant to SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 2011 and involves a change in control and management of the Target Company.

## Key Points

- Acquirer: Skybridge Interactive LLP
- Target Company: BABA ARTS LIMITED (BAL)
- Offer Price: Rs. 6.00 per equity share (face value Rs. 1.00 each)
- Shares to be acquired: Up to 1,32,92,000 equity shares (25.32% of fully paid-up equity and voting share capital)
- Offer period: Tuesday, June 09, 2026 to Monday, June 18, 2026
- Offer is through the BSE Acquisition Window mechanism per SEBI Tender-Offer guidelines
- Letter of Offer is attached to the notice for market participant reference

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. The offer is conducted under the existing framework established by SEBI circular no. CIR/CFD/POLICYCELL/1/2015 (April 13, 2015) and CFD/DCR2/CIR/P/2016/131 (December 9, 2016), along with BSE notices 20170210-16, 20190424-35, 20200528-32, 20201102-43, and 20210825-62 governing the mechanism for acquisition of shares through stock exchanges pursuant to tender offers under takeovers.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members and Custodians must note the open offer and facilitate participation by public shareholders of BABA ARTS LIMITED through the BSE Acquisition Window.
- The offer must be conducted per the Revised Guidelines of SEBI circular no. CFD/DCR2/CIR/P/2016/131 dated December 09, 2016 and Exchange notice no. 20170210-16 dated February 10, 2017 and 20190424-35 dated April 24, 2019.
- Market participants should refer to the BSE website for full details of the Offer to Buy.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: June 04, 2026
- Offer Opening Date: Tuesday, June 09, 2026
- Offer Closing Date: Monday, June 18, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This open offer directly impacts public shareholders of BABA ARTS LIMITED, who have the opportunity to tender their shares at Rs. 6.00 per share during the offer window (June 9–18, 2026). The offer involves a change in control and management of BAL, making it significant for existing investors. Trading members and custodians need to be prepared to facilitate tendering for eligible shareholders. The offer price of Rs. 6.00 per share for a Rs. 1.00 face value equity share sets the benchmark for shareholder decision-making during the offer period.
