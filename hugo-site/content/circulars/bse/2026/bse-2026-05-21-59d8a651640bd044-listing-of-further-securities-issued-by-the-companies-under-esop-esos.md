## Summary

BSE has listed additional securities issued by 12 companies under their Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) or Employee Stock Ownership Schemes (ESOS). These new shares are admitted for trading on the Exchange with effect from Friday, May 22, 2026. None of the newly listed shares carry any lock-in restriction.

## Key Points

- 12 companies have had new ESOP/ESOS shares listed on BSE effective May 22, 2026
- A total of approximately 830,297 new shares across all companies are now tradable
- No shares are subject to any lock-in period (all marked NA)
- Shares span face values of ₹1, ₹2, ₹5, and ₹10
- Distribution numbers (Dist Nos.) are provided for each company for reference

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this notice. This is a routine operational circular informing trading members of newly listed ESOP/ESOS shares.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to take note of the newly listed securities and update their records accordingly. No additional compliance actions are required from market participants.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** May 21, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** Friday, May 22, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry:** Not applicable (no lock-in for any of the listed securities)
