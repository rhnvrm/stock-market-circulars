## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 22,47,000 new equity shares of Hilltone Software and Gases Ltd. (Scrip Code: 544308) effective January 29, 2026. These shares were issued on a preferential basis to non-promoters at Rs. 44/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- plus premium of Rs. 34/-) and are subject to lock-in until August 16, 2026.

## Key Points

- Total shares listed: 22,47,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each
- Issue price: Rs. 44/- per share (including premium of Rs. 34/-)
- Allotment date: November 29, 2025
- Trading commencement: January 29, 2026
- Distinctive numbers: 10930801 to 13177800
- ISIN: INE168C01013
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification following SEBI guidelines for preferential issue.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to update their systems for the newly listed securities
- Lock-in restrictions must be enforced until August 16, 2026 for all 22,47,000 shares
- Shares are subject to preferential allotment regulations and lock-in provisions

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 29, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: January 29, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry**: August 16, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine listing affecting a single scrip with moderate share count.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Standard listing process requiring only system updates by trading members to reflect the new securities in their trading platforms.

**Investor Impact**: The preferential issue increases the company's equity base by 22,47,000 shares. The lock-in period until August 16, 2026 ensures these shares cannot be traded freely for approximately 7 months from the listing date, providing price stability during this period.
