## Summary

BSE has informed members that Canara Mutual Fund has communicated a change in scheme name on the BSE StAR MF Platform, effective April 23, 2026. The scheme formerly known as Canara Robeco Consumer Trends Fund will be renamed to Canara Robeco Consumption Fund.

## Key Points

- Canara Robeco Consumer Trends Fund is renamed to Canara Robeco Consumption Fund.
- The change is effective from April 23, 2026 on the BSE StAR MF Platform.
- The notice cum addendum issued by Canara AMC is attached to the circular.
- No structural or investment mandate changes are indicated; this is a name change only.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative update reflecting the revised scheme name as communicated by Canara AMC to BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

Members and participants on BSE StAR MF Platform should update their records and systems to reflect the new scheme name — Canara Robeco Consumption Fund — effective April 23, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **April 22, 2026**: Notice issued by BSE.
- **April 23, 2026**: Effective date of scheme name change on BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a purely administrative name change for one Canara Robeco mutual fund scheme on the BSE StAR MF Platform. Investors and distributors should note the updated scheme name for future transactions and record-keeping, but there is no change to the fund's structure, investment objective, or management.
