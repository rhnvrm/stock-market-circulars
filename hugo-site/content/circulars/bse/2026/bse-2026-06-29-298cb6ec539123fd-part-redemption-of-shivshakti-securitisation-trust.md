## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Shivshakti Securitisation Trust has communicated the reduced face value per Pass Through Certificate (PTC) following a partial redemption. The PTCs under scrip code 977121 (ISIN: INE2I7G15010) will trade at the revised reduced face value effective June 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: Shivshakti Securitisation Trust
- Instrument: SST-7.73%-28-9-29-PTC (Scrip Code: 977121, ISIN: INE2I7G15010)
- Purpose: Part Redemption of PTCs
- Reduced Face Value: Rs. 9,767,671.12 per PTC
- Effective Date: June 30, 2026
- Reference: DR-661/2026-2027

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notification under BSE's Listing Operations (Dept.), requiring the exchange to update the traded face value of the PTC following a partial redemption event.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are required to take note of the revised reduced face value of Rs. 9,767,671.12 per PTC for Shivshakti Securitisation Trust (Scrip Code: 977121) and ensure all trading and settlement activities reflect this updated face value from June 30, 2026 onwards.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: June 29, 2026
- Effective Date for Revised Face Value: June 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This notice has a narrow, instrument-specific impact, affecting only holders and traders of the Shivshakti Securitisation Trust PTC (Scrip Code: 977121). The reduced face value reflects cash returned to investors through partial redemption of principal. No broader market or systemic impact is expected. Trading members must update their systems to reflect the new face value of Rs. 9,767,671.12 per PTC before market open on June 30, 2026.
