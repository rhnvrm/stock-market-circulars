## Summary

BSE has issued instructions requiring trading members to submit the status of readiness and compliance with accessibility requirements for their investor-facing digital platforms via the BEFS portal. The submission must be completed by March 31, 2026, and covers platform-wise accessibility details under the Rights of Persons with Disabilities framework.

## Key Points

- Trading members must log into the BEFS portal and navigate to: Rights of Persons with Disabilities – Digital platform wise accessibility
- Details must be submitted for all relevant digital platforms (website, mobile app, etc.) individually
- Each platform's accessibility details must be saved before proceeding to the next
- Final submission is made via a SUBMIT button; no changes are permitted after submission
- The process is irreversible once the SUBMIT button is clicked and confirmed

## Regulatory Changes

This circular operationalizes compliance requirements under the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, specifically relating to accessibility of investor-facing digital platforms maintained by trading members.

## Compliance Requirements

- Login to BEFS portal
- Navigate to 'Rights of Persons with Disabilities – Digital platform wise accessibility'
- Fill in accessibility readiness details for each digital platform (website, mobile app, and any other applicable platforms)
- Save details for each platform separately
- Click SUBMIT to make the final, irrevocable submission to the Exchange
- Note: Details must be submitted for all applicable digital platforms, even if multiple platforms are maintained

## Important Dates

- **Submission Deadline: March 31, 2026** – All trading members must complete their submission on the BEFS portal by this date

## Impact Assessment

This circular affects all BSE trading members that operate investor-facing digital platforms (websites, mobile apps, etc.). It is an administrative compliance requirement with no direct market or trading impact. Non-submission by the deadline may result in regulatory action. The irreversible nature of the final submission requires members to ensure all platform details are complete and accurate before clicking SUBMIT.
