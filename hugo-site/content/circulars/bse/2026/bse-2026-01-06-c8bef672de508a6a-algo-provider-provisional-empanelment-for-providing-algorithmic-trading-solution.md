## Summary

BSE has provisionally empanelled Bull8.AI Solutions Private Limited as an algorithmic trading solutions provider. Trading members can now utilize this vendor's services for algorithmic trading. The empanelment is effective immediately.

## Key Points

- Bull8.AI Solutions Private Limited has been provisionally empanelled with BSE
- The vendor will provide algorithmic trading solutions to trading members
- Contact: +91 7042753487
- Office: Spectrum Metro Mall, Sector-D, Sector-75, Gautam Buddha Noida – 201301
- Complete list of algo providers available on BSE website

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an addition to the existing list of approved algorithmic trading solution providers.

## Compliance Requirements

No mandatory compliance requirements. Trading members seeking algorithmic trading solutions may choose to engage with the newly empanelled provider.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: January 6, 2026 (immediate effect)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact. It expands the choice of algorithmic trading solution providers available to BSE trading members. The provisional empanelment provides trading members with an additional vendor option for implementing algorithmic trading strategies. The updated list of all algo providers is maintained at: https://www.bseindia.com/static/markets/Derivatives/DeriReports/market_information.html
