## Summary

BSE has informed members about the launch of a New Fund Offer (NFO) for **ICICI Prudential Multi-Asset Active FOF** on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The NFO opens on June 30, 2026, and closes on July 14, 2026, with SIP/XSIP facility available. Two plan variants are offered — Regular Plan (Growth) and Direct Plan (Growth) — both under the Fund of Funds (FOF) category.

## Key Points

- NFO period: June 30, 2026 to July 14, 2026 on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Two schemes available: ICICI Prudential Multi-Asset Active FOF – Regular Plan (ISIN: INF109K1A542) and Direct Plan (ISIN: INF109K1A559)
- Tentative allotment date: July 17, 2026
- SIP/XSIP registrations must be authenticated by clients on or before 3:00 PM on July 14, 2026
- NFO extended till midnight on July 14, 2026, for lumpsum purchases only
- Cheque and OTM payments accepted subject to clear funds in ICCL account by 6:00 PM on July 16, 2026
- Switch transactions accepted during NFO period subject to receipt of funds prior to allotment date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice informing members of a new fund offer availability on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure client fund initiation by 9:00 AM on T+1 day; transactions without fund initiation will be cancelled and CC with 'N' flag reported to RTA/AMC
- Transactions with pending payments remain in the system until 3:00 PM on the last day of funds pay-out to AMC or T+5 day, whichever is earlier
- Switch-out funds received on or after the allotment date (July 17, 2026) shall not be considered for allotment
- Clear funds must be in the ICCL account by 6:00 PM on July 16, 2026, for allotment eligibility

## Important Dates

| Date | Event |
|------|-------|
| June 30, 2026 | NFO opens |
| July 14, 2026 (3:00 PM) | SIP/XSIP registration deadline |
| July 14, 2026 (midnight) | NFO closes (lumpsum purchases) |
| July 16, 2026 (6:00 PM) | Clear funds deadline in ICCL account |
| July 17, 2026 | Tentative allotment date |

## Impact Assessment

This notice has limited market impact as it pertains to a new fund offer on the BSE StAR MF Platform. It is operationally relevant to mutual fund distributors, members, and investors interested in the ICICI Prudential Multi-Asset Active FOF. No changes to trading rules, surveillance measures, or broader market operations are involved. Members should update their systems to accept transactions for the two new ISINs (INF109K1A542 and INF109K1A559) during the NFO window.
