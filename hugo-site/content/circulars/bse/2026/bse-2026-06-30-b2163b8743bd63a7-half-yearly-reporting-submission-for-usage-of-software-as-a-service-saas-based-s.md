## Summary

BSE has issued a notice reminding all trading members to submit their half yearly compliance report on the usage of Software as a Service (SaaS) based solutions and applications for the period January 2026 to June 2026. The submission is due by July 31, 2026, and must be made exclusively through the BSE Electronic Filing System (BEFS).

## Key Points

- Compliance is mandated under SEBI Circular No. SEBI/HO/MIRSD2/DOR/CIR/P/2020/221 dated November 03, 2020, and Exchange Notice No. 20201127-7 dated November 27, 2020.
- Reporting period: January 2026 to June 2026.
- Submission must be made in electronic form only via BEFS at https://befs.bseindia.com.
- Members registered with both BSE and NSE must submit their SaaS report to NSE only, per the technology-based sharing mechanism introduced via Exchange Notice No. 20250929-81 dated September 29, 2025.
- Exclusive BSE members must continue submitting their reports to BSE through the BEFS portal.
- Submission guidelines are provided in Annexure 1 attached to the circular.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This notice reaffirms the existing half-yearly SaaS reporting obligation under the SEBI advisory on SaaS-based solutions for financial sector organizations. The cross-exchange sharing mechanism (members registered with both BSE and NSE submitting only to NSE) was previously established via Exchange Notice No. 20250929-81.

## Compliance Requirements

- All trading members must review their usage of SaaS-based solutions and applications for the period January 2026 to June 2026.
- Exclusive BSE members must submit the SaaS compliance report through the BEFS portal (https://befs.bseindia.com) in electronic form.
- Members registered with both BSE and NSE are exempt from BSE submission and must file with NSE only.
- Refer to Annexure 1 for detailed guidelines on completing the submission.

## Important Dates

- **Reporting Period**: January 2026 to June 2026
- **Submission Deadline**: July 31, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative compliance notice with no direct market or operational impact. It affects only trading members who use SaaS solutions and are registered exclusively with BSE. Members also registered with NSE face no additional burden as they are directed to submit only to NSE. Non-compliance may attract regulatory scrutiny from BSE's Member Oversight department.
