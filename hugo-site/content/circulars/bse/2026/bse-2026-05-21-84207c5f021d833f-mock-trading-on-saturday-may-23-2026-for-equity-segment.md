## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Equity segment on Saturday, May 23, 2026. Trading members using third-party trading platforms are encouraged to use this session to test their applications under various functionalities and exceptional market conditions. There will be no new BOLT TWS release associated with this session.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Equity segment on Saturday, May 23, 2026
- Third-party trading platform users can test various functionalities including call auction sessions, risk-reduction mode, trading halt, and block deals
- No new BOLT TWS release will be issued
- Feedback must be submitted by 01:00 pm on the day of the session
- Members can contact BSE Tech Support or Help Desk for queries

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine mock trading exercise for system testing and member preparedness.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are encouraged (not mandated) to participate and test their trading applications
- Members are requested to submit feedback by 01:00 pm on May 23, 2026
- Contact BSE Tech Support at 022-22728053 or bse.tech@bseindia.com for TWS-related queries
- Contact BSE Help Desk at 022-45720400/600 or 022-69158500 or bsehelp@bseindia.com for general queries

## Important Dates

- **Mock Trading Date:** Saturday, May 23, 2026
- **Feedback Deadline:** 01:00 pm on May 23, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational exercise with no impact on live markets, listed securities, or investor portfolios. The session is limited to Saturday and is intended solely for system testing by trading members. Participation is voluntary and the session provides an opportunity for members to validate their trading infrastructure against various simulated market scenarios.
