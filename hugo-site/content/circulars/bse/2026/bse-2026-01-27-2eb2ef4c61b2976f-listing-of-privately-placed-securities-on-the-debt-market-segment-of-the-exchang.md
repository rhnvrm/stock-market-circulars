## Summary

BSE has listed commercial papers issued on a private placement basis by six companies on its debt market segment, effective January 27, 2026. The listed securities include CPs from Navi Finserv Limited, Birla Group Holdings Private Limited, Adani Enterprises Ltd., L&T Metro Rail (Hyderabad) Limited, Julius Baer Capital (India) Private Limited, and Fedbank Financial Services Limited. All securities will trade in dematerialized form with standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof.

## Key Points

- Six companies have listed commercial papers on BSE debt segment effective January 27, 2026
- Trading will be in dematerialized form only under specified ISIN numbers
- Standard trading denomination is Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for all securities is 1 paise
- Interest rates range from 7.40% to 9.40% per annum
- Maturity periods range from approximately 3 months to 12 months
- All issuances carry A1+ credit ratings from CARE, CRISIL, ICRA, or IND
