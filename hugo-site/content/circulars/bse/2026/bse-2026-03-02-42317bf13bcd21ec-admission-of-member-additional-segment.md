## Summary

BSE Ltd. has admitted Adinath Stock Broking Pvt. Ltd. (Member Code: 3175) as a Trading Member with trading rights in the Commodity Derivatives segment. This admission is subject to the Rules, Bye-laws, and Regulations of the Exchange.

## Key Points

- Adinath Stock Broking Pvt. Ltd. (Member Code: 3175) has been granted admission as a Trading Member on BSE Ltd.
- The member is admitted with trading rights only (no additional rights beyond trading).
- The admission is specifically for the Commodity Derivatives segment.
- The admission is governed by BSE's Rules, Bye-laws, and Regulations.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this notice. This is a routine membership admission under existing Exchange rules.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members requiring clarification on the matter may contact BSE at membership.ops@bseindia.com.
- The newly admitted member must operate in compliance with BSE's Rules, Bye-laws, and Regulations applicable to the Commodity Derivatives segment.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** 02 March 2026 (date of Notice No. 20260302-26)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative notice with minimal market impact. The admission of a single trading member to the Commodity Derivatives segment slightly expands market participation. Existing trading members and investors in Commodity Derivatives are not directly affected. No changes to trading rules, margins, or compliance obligations arise from this notice.
