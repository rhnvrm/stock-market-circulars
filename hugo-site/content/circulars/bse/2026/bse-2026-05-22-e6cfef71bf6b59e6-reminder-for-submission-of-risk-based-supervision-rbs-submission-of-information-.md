## Summary

BSE has issued a reminder to all active members to submit Risk Based Supervision (RBS) data for the period April 1, 2025 – March 31, 2026. The submission deadline is May 31, 2026, and must be made electronically through the BSE Electronic Filing System (BEFS).

## Key Points

- This is a reminder referencing original Exchange notice no. 20260417-25 dated April 17, 2026.
- RBS data submission is mandatory for all active Members of the Exchange — defined as those who executed at least one trade during the period April 1, 2025 – March 31, 2026.
- Submission must be made electronically via BEFS (BSE Electronic Filing System).
- Non-compliance will attract penal charges and/or disciplinary action as prescribed in Exchange notice no. 20260417-45 dated April 17, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice is a reminder for an existing mandatory compliance requirement under the Risk Based Supervision framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- All active BSE members (those with at least one trade in FY 2025-26) must submit RBS information/data for the period April 1, 2025 – March 31, 2026.
- Submission must be completed electronically through BEFS on or before the due date.
- Members should refer to Exchange notice no. 20260417-45 dated April 17, 2026 for details on penal charges applicable for non-submission.

## Important Dates

- **Submission Deadline:** May 31, 2026
- **Data Period Covered:** April 1, 2025 – March 31, 2026
- **Original Notice Date:** April 17, 2026 (notice no. 20260417-25)

## Impact Assessment

This circular affects all active BSE member brokers who traded during FY 2025-26. Failure to comply by May 31, 2026 may result in financial penalties and disciplinary action. There is no direct impact on market trading, securities prices, or investors. Members requiring clarification may contact BSE's Member Oversight department at 022-69158540, 022-45720640, or 022-45720440, or email bse.inspection@bseindia.com.
