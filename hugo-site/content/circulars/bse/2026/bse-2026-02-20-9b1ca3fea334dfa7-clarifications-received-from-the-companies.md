## Summary

BSE received clarification letters from multiple listed companies in response to surveillance queries (Letter No. L/SURV/ONL/PV/SJ/2025-2026/3380, dated February 19, 2026) regarding unusual price movements in their securities. Companies responding include Accretion Nutraveda Limited (Scrip Code: 544694), Associated Coaters Limited (Scrip Code: 544183), and BLB Limited (Scrip Code: 532290), among others. All responding companies affirm full compliance with disclosure norms and attribute price movements to market forces.

## Key Points

- BSE issued surveillance query emails on February 19, 2026 to companies exhibiting unusual price movements
- All responding companies assert compliance with Regulation 30 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015
- Companies state no material price-sensitive information has been withheld
- All companies attribute price movements to market-driven conditions beyond management control
- Companies commit to timely disclosure of any future material events
- Accretion Nutraveda Limited (formerly Accretion Nutraveda Private Limited) — Scrip Code 544694, Ahmedabad, Gujarat
- Associated Coaters Limited (formerly Associated Coaters Private Limited) — Scrip Code 544183, BSE Symbol: ASSOCIATED, Kolkata
- BLB Limited — Scrip Code 532290

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This circular documents company responses to BSE's standard surveillance mechanism under the existing SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015 framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- No new compliance requirements imposed on market participants
- Companies are reminded of their ongoing obligations under Regulation 30 of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015 to promptly disclose all material events and price-sensitive information
- Companies must continue to inform BSE of any future material events as and when they occur

## Important Dates

- **February 19, 2026**: BSE issued surveillance query letters to the companies
- **February 20, 2026**: Companies submitted clarification replies to BSE

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. These are standard surveillance-driven clarification disclosures. The companies' responses confirm no undisclosed material developments, suggesting the price movements were speculative or sentiment-driven rather than fundamentals-based. Investors in Accretion Nutraveda Ltd (544694), Associated Coaters Ltd (ASSOCIATED/544183), and BLB Ltd (532290) should note that management has disclaimed knowledge of specific reasons for price fluctuations. No trading restrictions or regulatory actions were announced as part of this disclosure.
