## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that further securities issued by 15 companies under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP/ESOS) have been listed and admitted for trading on the Exchange with effect from **Friday, May 29, 2026**. None of the newly listed shares carry any lock-in restriction.

## Key Points

- 15 companies have listed new ESOP/ESOS shares effective May 29, 2026
- No lock-in period applies to any of the listed securities
- Largest issuance: Shadowfax Technologies Limited — 766,530 shares (ISIN: INE12UN01015)
- Second largest: Nippon Life India Asset Management Limited — 193,469 shares (ISIN: INE298J01013)
- ICICI Bank issues 253,442 new shares at face value ₹2 per share
- All shares are immediately eligible for trading upon listing

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced. This is a standard operational listing notice under BSE's Listing Operations department.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to take note of the new ISIN distribution ranges for each company to correctly identify the newly admitted shares in trading systems. No additional compliance action is required from market participants.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** May 27, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** May 29, 2026 (Friday)
