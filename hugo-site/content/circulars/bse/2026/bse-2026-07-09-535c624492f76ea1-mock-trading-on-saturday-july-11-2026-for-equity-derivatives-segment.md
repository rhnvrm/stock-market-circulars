## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Equity Derivatives segment on Saturday, July 11, 2026. The session allows trading members using third-party platforms, in-house systems, or ETI APIs to test their trading applications, including functionality under exceptional market conditions such as risk-reduction mode and trading halts. The new BOLT Pro TWS version 13.20 will also be released, with details to follow in a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Equity Derivatives segment on Saturday, July 11, 2026
- Trading members can test third-party vendor platforms, in-house systems, and ETI API-based systems
- Testing covers functionalities including risk-reduction mode and trading halt under exceptional market conditions
- New BOLT Pro TWS version 13.20 to be released, with details in a separate circular
- Additional mock sessions planned for July 18, July 25, and August 1, 2026, related to Closing Auction Session (CAS) changes for Stock Futures, per circulars 20260319-21 and 20260610-41
- Trades from mock sessions carry no margin, pay-in/pay-out obligations, or rights and liabilities
- Members requested to submit feedback on the mock session by 3:00 PM on the session day

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is an operational notice implementing prior circulars (20260319-21 and 20260610-41) regarding introduction of Closing Auction Session (CAS) for Stock Futures.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are requested to actively participate in the mock trading session and provide feedback via phone or email (bse.tech@bseindia.com or bsehelp@bseindia.com) by 3:00 PM on the session day. No mandatory compliance action beyond participation and feedback.

## Important Dates

- July 11, 2026 (Saturday): Mock trading session for Equity Derivatives segment, sessions from 9:15 AM (log-in) to 2:00 PM (continuous trading close); feedback due by 3:00 PM
- July 18, 2026: Additional mock trading session (CAS-related, timings to be announced)
- July 25, 2026: Additional mock trading session (CAS-related, timings to be announced)
- August 1, 2026: Additional mock trading session (CAS-related, timings to be announced)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine system testing exercise with no effect on live trading, pricing, or investor obligations. Relevant primarily to trading members and vendors who must ensure system readiness ahead of the BOLT Pro TWS version 13.20 rollout and upcoming CAS-related changes to Stock Futures trading.
