## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the ongoing Rights Issue of MARG Techno Projects Limited has been extended. The Issue Closing Date has been pushed from Monday, July 06, 2026 to Tuesday, July 28, 2026, and the Last date for On-Market Renunciation of Rights Entitlements has correspondingly been extended from Wednesday, July 01, 2026 to Wednesday, July 22, 2026. This notice is issued in continuation of an earlier notice no. 20260624-39.

## Key Points

- The Rights Issue of MARG Techno Projects Limited has been extended by 22 days.
- Issue Opening Date remains unchanged at Monday, June 29, 2026.
- Last date for On-Market Renunciation extended to Wednesday, July 22, 2026.
- Issue Closing Date extended to Tuesday, July 28, 2026.
- This notice supersedes/continues from prior notice no. 20260624-39.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory change; this is an administrative extension of previously announced rights issue timelines by the exchange on behalf of the issuer.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members and market participants dealing in the Rights Entitlements (RE) of MARG Techno Projects Limited must align their trading and renunciation activities with the revised timelines. No specific filing action is required from trading members beyond adherence to the updated dates.

## Important Dates

- Issue Opening Date: Monday, June 29, 2026 (unchanged)
- Last date for On-Market Renunciation: Wednesday, July 22, 2026 (revised from July 01, 2026)
- Issue Closing Date: Tuesday, July 28, 2026 (revised from July 06, 2026)

## Impact Assessment

The extension provides shareholders and investors additional time to trade Rights Entitlements on-market and to apply for the rights issue. Market participants should update their systems and trading calendars accordingly to avoid missing the revised renunciation and closing dates. Impact is limited to MARG Techno Projects Limited's securities and does not affect broader market operations.
