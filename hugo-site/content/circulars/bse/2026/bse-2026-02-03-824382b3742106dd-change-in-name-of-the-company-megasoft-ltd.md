## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for MEGASOFT LIMITED (CIN: L24100TN1999PLC042730). The company's name has been officially changed to SIGMA ADVANCED SYSTEMS LIMITED with effect from January 12, 2026. This certificate was approved and digitally signed by the Central Processing Centre, ROC.

## Key Points

- Company original name: MEGASOFT LIMITED
- New company name: SIGMA ADVANCED SYSTEMS LIMITED
- Corporate Identification Number (CIN): L24100TN1999PLC042730
- Company type: Company limited by shares
- Certificate issued by: Central Processing Centre, ROC
- Approved by: Shorya Chak, Assistant/Deputy/Registrar of Companies
- Registered address: No. 85, Kutchery Road, Mylapore, Chennai-600004, Tamil Nadu, India

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative corporate action reflecting a company name change approved by the Ministry of Corporate Affairs under the Companies Act, 2014.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display the old name (MEGASOFT LIMITED) for a period of two years along with its new name (SIGMA ADVANCED SYSTEMS LIMITED) at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders
- All stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company and its directors on the MCA website: www.mca.gov.in/MCA21

## Important Dates

- **January 12, 2026**: Effective date of name change from MEGASOFT LIMITED to SIGMA ADVANCED SYSTEMS LIMITED
- **February 3, 2026**: Date of BSE circular notification
- **January 12, 2028**: End of two-year period for mandatory display of old name alongside new name

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine corporate administrative action that does not affect the company's operations, financial position, or shareholder rights.

**Operational Impact**: The company must update all official documentation, letterheads, signage, and communications to reflect the new name while continuing to display the old name for two years. Trading symbol and ISIN may be updated by the exchange in due course.

**Stakeholder Impact**: No impact on rights and liabilities of shareholders, creditors, or other stakeholders. The CIN remains unchanged, ensuring continuity of corporate identity for regulatory and compliance purposes.
