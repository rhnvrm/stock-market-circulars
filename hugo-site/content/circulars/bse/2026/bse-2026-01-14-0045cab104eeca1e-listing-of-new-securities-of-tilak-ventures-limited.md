## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 89,13,93,612 new equity shares of Tilak Ventures Limited (Scrip Code: 503663) issued on a rights basis. These shares will be available for trading on the Exchange from Friday, January 16, 2026. The shares were allotted on January 12, 2026, at par value of Re. 1/- each and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- Company: Tilak Ventures Limited (Scrip Code: 503663)
- Number of new shares: 89,13,93,612 equity shares
- Face value: Re. 1/- per share
- Issue type: Rights issue at par
- ISIN: INE026L01022
- Distinctive Numbers: 445696807 to 1337090418
- Date of allotment: January 12, 2026
- Trading commencement: January 16, 2026
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the listing of these new securities for trading purposes. No specific compliance actions required beyond normal trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- **January 12, 2026**: Date of allotment
- **January 14, 2026**: Notice date
- **January 16, 2026**: Trading commencement date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The addition of approximately 89.14 crore new equity shares represents a substantial increase in the equity base of Tilak Ventures Limited, which will likely impact the liquidity and trading volumes of the stock.

**Investor Impact**: Medium - Existing shareholders who participated in the rights issue will see their holdings reflected in trading accounts. The increased share base may affect earnings per share calculations and market capitalization.

**Operational Impact**: Low - Standard listing process with no unusual requirements for brokers or trading members.
