## Summary

BSE has issued a circular regarding the allocation of shares to anchor investors in the public issue of Fractal Analytics Limited. This allocation represents the first stage of the IPO process where institutional investors are allotted shares at a discovered price before the issue opens to the general public.

## Key Points

- Fractal Analytics Limited has completed anchor investor allocation for its public issue
- Anchor investors are allocated shares one day prior to the issue opening
- This allocation helps in price discovery and demonstrates institutional confidence
- The circular provides details of the allocation made to anchor investors

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- BSE members and market participants to take note of the anchor investor allocation
- Information to be disseminated to clients and stakeholders as per regulatory requirements

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: February 06, 2026
- Anchor investor allocation completed as per the circular date

## Impact Assessment

The anchor investor allocation is a positive development for the upcoming IPO of Fractal Analytics Limited. It demonstrates institutional interest and helps establish a reference price for the public issue. This information is relevant for investors planning to participate in the IPO and provides insights into institutional demand for the company's shares.
