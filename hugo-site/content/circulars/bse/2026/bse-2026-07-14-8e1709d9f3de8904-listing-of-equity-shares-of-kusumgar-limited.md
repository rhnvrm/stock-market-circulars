## Summary

BSE Limited has notified trading members that the equity shares of Kusumgar Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been allotted Scrip Code 544821, trading symbol KUSUMGAR, and ISIN INE0ISX01025.

## Key Points

- Equity shares of Kusumgar Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on BSE.
- Scrip Code: 544821
- Trading Symbol: KUSUMGAR
- ISIN: INE0ISX01025
- The exact date of listing and further security details will be announced through a separate notice.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is an administrative listing notice under the Company-related category, Equity segment, issued by the DCS-Listing department.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action is required from trading members at this stage; they are informed for awareness ahead of the formal listing.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 14 July 2026
- Date of listing: To be announced via a separate notice

## Impact Assessment

The notice formalizes the impending listing of Kusumgar Limited's equity shares on BSE, enabling trading members to prepare for dealings once the listing date is confirmed. Market impact is currently limited as no trading has commenced; impact will materialize once the listing date and trading details are announced.
