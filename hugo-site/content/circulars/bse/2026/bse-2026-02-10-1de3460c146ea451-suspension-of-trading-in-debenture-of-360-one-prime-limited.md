## Summary

360 ONE PRIME LIMITED has set February 12, 2026 as the record date for early redemption on exercise of call option for its non-convertible debenture (ISIN: INE248U07FP9, Code: 976054). Trading members are advised not to deal in this debenture effective February 12, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: 360 ONE PRIME LIMITED
- Debenture ISIN: INE248U07FP9
- Debenture Code: OPL-Nifty 50-20-9-34-PVT (976054)
- Record Date: February 12, 2026
- Purpose: Early Redemption on Exercise of Call Option
- No Dealings From: February 12, 2026
- Notice Reference: DR-819/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debenture from February 12, 2026
- Trading members are requested to take note of the suspension

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: February 10, 2026
- **Record Date**: February 12, 2026
- **No Dealings From**: February 12, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this affects only a single privately placed debenture series of 360 ONE PRIME LIMITED. The suspension is temporary and relates to the issuer exercising its call option for early redemption. Bondholders of this specific ISIN will be affected, but broader market operations remain unaffected.
