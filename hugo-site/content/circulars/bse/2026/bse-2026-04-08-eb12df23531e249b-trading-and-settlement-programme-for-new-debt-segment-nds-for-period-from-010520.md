## Summary

BSE, through ICCL (Indian Clearing Corporation Limited), has published the trading and settlement programme for the New Debt Segment (NDS) covering the period from 01 May 2026 to 31 May 2026. This notice (No. 20260408-16) outlines the scheduled trading and settlement dates applicable to NDS participants for the upcoming month.

## Key Points

- Notice No.: 20260408-16, issued on 08 April 2026
- Covers the New Debt Segment (NDS) trading and settlement calendar for May 2026
- Administered by ICCL under the Settlement/RMS category
- Attachment NDS052026 contains the detailed programme schedule
- Programme period: 01 May 2026 to 31 May 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced. This is a routine periodic notification of the trading and settlement schedule for the NDS.

## Compliance Requirements

- NDS segment participants must adhere to the trading and settlement dates specified in the attachment NDS052026
- Clearing members and trading members operating in the New Debt Segment should plan their operations in accordance with the published schedule

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 08 April 2026
- Programme Period: 01 May 2026 to 31 May 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a medium operational impact for participants in the New Debt Segment, as it defines the settlement windows they must comply with throughout May 2026. There is no broader market-wide impact. Participants should download and review attachment NDS052026 for the complete day-wise trading and settlement schedule.
