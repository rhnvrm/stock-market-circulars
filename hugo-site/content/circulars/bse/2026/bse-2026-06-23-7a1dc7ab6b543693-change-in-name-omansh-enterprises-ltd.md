## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that Omansh Enterprises Ltd (Scrip Code: 538537) has notified the Exchange of its change in corporate name to **Pipan Oils Limited**. The SCRIP ID and abbreviated name on the BOLT Plus System will be updated accordingly, effective June 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- Existing company: Omansh Enterprises Ltd (Scrip Code: 538537)
- New company name: Pipan Oils Limited
- New SCRIP ID on BOLT Plus System: **PIPAN**
- New abbreviated name on BOLT Plus System: **PIPAN**
- Change is applicable to the Equity segment
- Notice issued by Marian D'Souza, Assistant Vice President – Listing Compliance & Operations

## Regulatory Changes

The scrip identification details on the BSE BOLT Plus System will be updated from the existing OMANSH identifiers to PIPAN, reflecting the company's new legal name. A Certificate of Incorporation has been attached as supporting documentation.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must update their records and systems to reflect the new scrip ID (PIPAN) and abbreviated name (PIPAN) for Scrip Code 538537 effective June 30, 2026. Orders and references post the effective date should use the new identifiers.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 23, 2026
- **Effective Date of Name Change on BOLT Plus System:** June 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate name change with moderate operational impact on trading members who maintain scrip-specific records or automated systems referencing the old SCRIP ID (OMANSH). Investors holding shares in Omansh Enterprises Ltd will continue to hold the same shares under the new name Pipan Oils Limited with no change in scrip code (538537). Members should ensure their back-office and trading systems are updated before June 30, 2026 to avoid any order-routing issues.
