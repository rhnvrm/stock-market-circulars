## Summary

BSE Index Services Private Limited has announced a rebalancing of the BSE SAATVIK 100 Index. Effective at the open of trading on Monday, June 22, 2026, three securities will be added and nine securities will be removed from the index.

## Key Points

- 3 securities added to BSE SAATVIK 100: Tata Motors Ltd (544569), ICICI Prudential Asset Management Company Ltd (544658), and Tata Capital Ltd (544574).
- 9 securities removed from BSE SAATVIK 100: Indian Overseas Bank (532388), Oracle Financial Services Software Ltd (532466), Oil India Ltd (533106), Indian Bank (532814), Dixon Technologies (India) Ltd (540699), Vodafone Idea Ltd (532822), LG Electronics India Ltd (544576), Adani Total Gas Ltd (542066), and Rail Vikas Nigam Ltd (542649).
- Changes are included in the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE) effective June 17, 2026.
- Queries can be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory rule changes. This is a periodic index composition rebalancing by BSE Index Services Private Limited, a wholly owned subsidiary of BSE Ltd.

## Compliance Requirements

Index fund managers, ETF managers, and other market participants whose portfolios are benchmarked to or replicate the BSE SAATVIK 100 Index must update their holdings to reflect the new composition by the open of trading on June 22, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **June 17, 2026**: Notice issued; changes included in end-of-day corporate action file.
- **June 22, 2026 (Monday)**: Changes effective at market open.

## Impact Assessment

The rebalancing involves a net reduction of 6 constituents (3 adds vs 9 drops), suggesting a structural adjustment rather than routine rotation. Passive funds and ETFs tracking BSE SAATVIK 100 will need to execute trades before the open on June 22. Securities being added may see buying pressure, while those being dropped may face selling pressure in the days leading up to the effective date. The change spans multiple sectors including banking, technology, energy, consumer electronics, and telecom.
