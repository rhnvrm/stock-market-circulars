## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of KOSAMATTAM FINANCE LIMITED effective January 22, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption purposes. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspended for KOSAMATTAM FINANCE LIMITED NCDs (ISIN: INE403Q07EN6, Code: 939637)
- Record date for redemption set as January 22, 2026
- No dealings permitted from January 22, 2026 onwards
- Applies to debt segment securities
- Notice reference: DR-804/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational notice for debenture redemption process.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified KOSAMATTAM FINANCE LIMITED debentures from January 22, 2026
- Trading members should note the suspension and update their systems accordingly
- Members must ensure compliance with the no-dealing instruction for ISIN INE403Q07EN6

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** January 16, 2026
- **Record Date:** January 22, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date:** January 22, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and traders of KOSAMATTAM FINANCE LIMITED's specific NCD series (KFL-6-2-26-NCD). This is a routine corporate action for debenture redemption with no broader market implications. Investors holding these debentures will have their holdings redeemed as per the company's redemption schedule. The suspension ensures orderly processing of the redemption without trading interference.
