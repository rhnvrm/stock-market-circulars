## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (Notice No. 20260505-7) informing trading members that T-bill 182TB08526 (Scrip Code: 805140, ISIN: IN002025Y321) is set to mature on its Redemption Date. Trading in this government security is suspended with effect from May 06, 2026 (DR-624/2026-2027).

## Key Points

- Scrip Code **805140** (182TB08526, ISIN: IN002025Y321) will reach its redemption date and is suspended from trading.
- Suspension is effective **May 06, 2026**.
- The circular applies to all Trading Members of BSE in the Debt segment.
- Reference number for this suspension: DR-624/2026-2027.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced. This is a standard operational action triggered by the maturity of the underlying government security under existing BSE listing and operations procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must **not deal** in Scrip Code 805140 (182TB08526) on or after May 06, 2026.
- Members should update their systems and alert desks to block or flag this scrip from the effective date.

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Notice Date | May 05, 2026 |
| Trading Suspension Effective | May 06, 2026 |
| Redemption Date | May 08, 2026 (implied by 182-day T-bill series) |

## Impact Assessment

This suspension has a **limited, targeted impact** confined to members and investors holding or actively trading Scrip Code 805140. As a routine redemption event for a Treasury Bill, no broader market disruption is expected. Members with open positions or pending orders in this scrip must ensure all activity is wound down before May 06, 2026 to avoid settlement issues.
