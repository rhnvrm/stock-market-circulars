## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (No. 20260618-9) informing trading members that two NCD scrips of Edelweiss Asset Reconstruction Company Limited will be available for trading with revised terms effective Friday, June 19, 2026, due to debt restructuring. This is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20260212-31 dated February 12, 2026.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code 954527 (ISIN: INE015L07428): Originally 7,505 NCDs listed; 891 repurchased by the company; 6,614 NCDs remain as balance
- Scrip Code 954394 (ISIN: INE015L07279): Originally 500 NCDs listed; 370 repurchased by the company; 130 NCDs remain as balance
- Revised terms take effect from June 19, 2026
- Both scrips will continue to be available for trading with updated outstanding NCD counts

## Regulatory Changes

The outstanding NCD quantities for both scrips have been revised downward following partial repurchase by Edelweiss Asset Reconstruction Company Limited as part of a debt restructuring exercise. Trading members must note the updated balance NCD counts for these scrips.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to note the revised NCD balances for Scrip Codes 954527 and 954394 and update their records accordingly. For further details, members may contact:
- Mr. Akshay Arolkar: +91 22 2272 8352 / debt.listing@bseindia.com
- Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President

## Important Dates

- **June 18, 2026**: Notice issued
- **June 19, 2026 (Friday)**: Revised terms become effective for trading in both scrips
- **February 12, 2026**: Original notice (No. 20260212-31) issued regarding these debt securities

## Impact Assessment

The debt restructuring has significantly reduced the tradeable NCD quantity for both scrips. Scrip 954394 (INE015L07279) is particularly affected, with only 130 NCDs remaining out of 500 (a 74% reduction), which may result in very thin liquidity. Scrip 954527 (INE015L07428) retains 6,614 of 7,505 NCDs (approximately 88% remaining), with relatively better liquidity. Trading members holding or dealing in these instruments should take note of the revised outstanding quantities when executing trades from June 19, 2026.
