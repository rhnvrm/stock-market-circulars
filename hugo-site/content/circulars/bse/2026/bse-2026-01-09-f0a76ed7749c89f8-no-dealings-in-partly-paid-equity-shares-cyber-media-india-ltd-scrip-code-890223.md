## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Cyber Media (India) Ltd. (Scrip Code 890223) has fixed January 14, 2026, as the record date to ascertain holders of partly paid-up equity shares eligible to receive the First and Final Call Notice. Trading members are advised not to deal in the partly paid equity shares from January 14, 2026 (Settlement No. DR-798/2025-2026).

## Key Points

- Company: Cyber Media (India) Ltd.
- Scrip Code: 890223 (Partly Paid-up Equity Shares)
- Record Date: January 14, 2026
- No Dealings From: January 14, 2026
- Settlement Number: DR-798/2025-2026
- Call Amount: Rs. 7.90 per partly paid share (Rs. 5/- towards face value + Rs. 2.90 towards premium)
- Purpose: First and Final Call on partly paid shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action related to call payment on partly paid equity shares.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any deals in partly paid equity shares (Scrip Code 890223) of Cyber Media (India) Ltd. from January 14, 2026
- Trading members are required to take note of this restriction
- Shareholders holding partly paid shares as of the record date will be eligible to receive the call notice

## Important Dates

- **January 9, 2026**: Circular issue date
- **January 14, 2026**: Record date for call notice and commencement of no dealings period
- **Settlement No. DR-798/2025-2026**: Applicable settlement

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - affects only partly paid equity shares of a single company

**Investor Impact**: Medium for partly paid shareholders - they must pay the First and Final Call of Rs. 7.90 per share to convert to fully paid shares

**Operational Impact**: Low - standard trading restriction for corporate action processing

**Liquidity Impact**: Minimal - trading restriction applies only to the partly paid share class, not regular equity shares
