## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (No. 20260522-29) dated 22 May 2026 announcing the listing of units of SBI Nifty Smallcap 250 ETF, a scheme under SBI Mutual Fund, on the BSE Mutual Fund segment. This is a standard listing notice issued by the DCS-Listing department under the Company Related category.

## Key Points

- Notice No.: 20260522-29
- Date of Notice: 22 May 2026
- Scheme: SBI Nifty Smallcap 250 ETF
- Fund House: SBI Mutual Fund
- Segment: Mutual Fund
- Department: DCS-Listing
- No attachments included with this notice

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this notice. It is a routine operational listing announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements are outlined in this circular. Market participants and investors may trade the newly listed ETF units through the BSE Mutual Fund segment upon listing.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 22 May 2026
- Listing effective as of the notice date (22 May 2026)

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact routine notice. The listing of SBI Nifty Smallcap 250 ETF units provides investors with exchange-traded access to the Nifty Smallcap 250 index through SBI Mutual Fund on BSE. No disruption to existing trading or settlement operations is expected.
