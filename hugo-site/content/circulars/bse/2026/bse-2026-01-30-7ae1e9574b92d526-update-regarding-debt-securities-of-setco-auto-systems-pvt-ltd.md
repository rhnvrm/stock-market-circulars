## Summary

BSE has issued an update regarding debt securities of Setco Auto Systems Pvt. Ltd, announcing revised terms and new ISIN codes for certain scrips. This follows the earlier Exchange Notice No. 20251128-32 dated November 28, 2025. Two scrip codes (975859 and 973453) will continue trading with revised terms including new ISINs and extended maturity dates effective February 1, 2026.

## Key Points

- Continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20251128-32 dated November 28, 2025
- Two scrip codes affected: 975859 and 973453
- New ISINs assigned for revised debt instruments
- Extended maturity dates from December 1, 2025 to January 31, 2026
- Changes effective from Sunday, February 1, 2026
- Trading members can contact BSE Debt Listing officials for clarifications

## Regulatory Changes

**ISIN and Term Revisions:**

**Scrip Code 975859:**
- Old ISIN: INE0I0R07078
- New ISIN: INE0I0R07102
- Quantity: 975
- Old Maturity Date: December 1, 2025
- New Maturity Date: January 31, 2026

**Scrip Code 973453:**
- Old ISIN: INE0I0R07086
- New ISIN: INE0I0R07110
- Quantity: 2,150
- Old Maturity Date: December 1, 2025
- New Maturity Date: January 31, 2026

**Continuing Trading ISINs:**
- Scrip Code 975859: ISIN INE0I0R07045
- Scrip Code 973453: ISIN INE0I0R07052

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the revised terms for the specified debt securities
- Systems should be updated to reflect new ISIN codes and maturity dates
- Trading members should contact BSE officials for any clarifications needed

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** January 30, 2026
- **Effective Date:** February 1, 2026 (Sunday)
- **New Maturity Date:** January 31, 2026 (for both revised ISINs)
- **Previous Maturity Date:** December 1, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a limited-scope administrative update affecting only holders and traders of specific debt securities issued by Setco Auto Systems Pvt. Ltd. The maturity extension of two months (from December 1, 2025 to January 31, 2026) indicates a restructuring of the debt obligations. The impact is confined to investors holding these particular debt instruments totaling 975 and 2,150 quantities respectively. Trading members need to update their systems with the new ISIN codes to ensure proper settlement and tracking.
