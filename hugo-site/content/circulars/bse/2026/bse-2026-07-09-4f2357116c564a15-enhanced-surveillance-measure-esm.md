## Summary

BSE has issued an update to its Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework, referencing a series of prior notices dating back to June 2023. Securities listed in Annexure I have met the criteria for ESM inclusion and will be subject to a minimum 100% margin effective July 13, 2026. Some securities already under ESM will move down to a lower stage (Stage I) effective July 10, 2026. Other securities are being removed from the ESM framework altogether per Annexure II, while Annexure III provides the consolidated list of all securities currently under the framework.

## Key Points

- Securities in Annexure I have satisfied ESM inclusion criteria and will attract minimum 100% margin on all open positions as of July 10, 2026 and new positions from July 13, 2026.
- Securities continuing under ESM but moving to a lower Stage I will do so effective July 10, 2026 (also listed in Annexure I).
- A consolidated list of all securities currently under the ESM framework is provided in Annexure III.
- Securities newly qualifying for ESM will shift from the Rolling Settlement segment to the Trade-for-Trade segment effective July 13, 2026.
- Securities moving to Stage II will be placed under Trade-for-Trade with a 2% price band and Periodic Call Auction effective July 10, 2026.
- Securities exiting the ESM framework (Annexure II) do so effective July 10, 2026, with their price band reinstated to pre-ESM levels, subject to no other surveillance measure applying.
- ESM inclusion is purely a market surveillance mechanism and is not to be construed as an adverse action against the concerned company.
- ESM operates in conjunction with all other applicable surveillance measures.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced; this notice is a periodic update applying the existing ESM framework (established via prior notices from June 2023 through July 2025) to a revised list of securities, including new inclusions, stage changes, and exclusions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must apply minimum 100% margin on ESM-qualifying securities per Annexure I from the specified effective dates.
- Members must recognize the shift of qualifying securities from Rolling Settlement to Trade-for-Trade segment.
- Members must apply the revised price band (2%) and Periodic Call Auction mechanism for securities moving to Stage II.
- Queries should be directed to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- July 10, 2026: Stage I migration for continuing ESM securities; Stage II securities move to Trade-for-Trade with 2% price band and Periodic Call Auction; exclusions per Annexure II take effect.
- July 13, 2026: Minimum 100% margin applies to new ESM inclusions (Annexure I) on open and new positions; affected securities shift to Trade-for-Trade segment.

## Impact Assessment

This notice has a high trading impact for the specific securities named in the annexures (not detailed in the notice text), as it materially changes margin requirements, settlement segment, and price band mechanics for those stocks. Increased margin requirements and Trade-for-Trade restrictions typically reduce liquidity and trading activity in affected names, while securities exiting the framework should see normalized trading conditions. The overall market impact is contained to the specific securities listed in the annexures rather than the broader market.
