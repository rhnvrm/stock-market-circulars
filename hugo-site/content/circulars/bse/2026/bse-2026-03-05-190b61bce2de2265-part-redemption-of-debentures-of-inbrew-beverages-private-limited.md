## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Inbrew Beverages Private Limited has fixed March 9, 2026 as the record date for part redemption of its non-convertible debentures and payment of interest. Trading in these debentures will be conducted with a reduced face value effective from the same date under settlement number DR-835/2025-2026.

## Key Points

- Company: Inbrew Beverages Private Limited
- Scrip Code: 974276
- ISIN: INE696R07059
- Debenture Series: IBPLD-13.25%-25-9-26-PVT
- Record Date: March 9, 2026
- Purpose: Part Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- Reduced Face Value: Rs. 748,175.18 per Debenture
- Effective Date for Reduced Face Value: March 9, 2026
- Settlement Number: DR-835/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a routine corporate action notification under BSE's debt segment operational framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that debentures of Inbrew Beverages Private Limited will trade with the reduced face value of Rs. 748,175.18 per debenture with effect from March 9, 2026.
- Members should update their systems to reflect the revised face value from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Record Date:** March 9, 2026
- **Effective Date for Reduced Face Value:** March 9, 2026
- **Settlement Number:** DR-835/2025-2026
- **Notice Date:** March 5, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt corporate action impacting holders of Inbrew Beverages Private Limited's privately placed NCDs. The part redemption reduces the face value of each debenture to Rs. 748,175.18. Impact is limited to the specific debenture series (ISIN INE696R07059) and relevant trading members dealing in the debt segment. No broader market impact is expected.
