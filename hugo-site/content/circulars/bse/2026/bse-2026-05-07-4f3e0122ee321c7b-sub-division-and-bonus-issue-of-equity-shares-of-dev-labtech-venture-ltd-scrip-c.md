## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that DEV LABTECH VENTURE LTD (Scrip Code: 543848) has fixed 15 May 2026 as the record date for a simultaneous sub-division of equity shares and a 1:1 bonus issue. Each existing Rs.10/- equity share will be split into two Rs.5/- shares, and one additional bonus share of Rs.5/- will be issued for every Rs.5/- share held post-split. The existing ISIN INE0NIJ01017 will cease to be valid for exchange transactions on or after 15 May 2026.

## Key Points

- Record date for both corporate actions: 15 May 2026
- Sub-division ratio: 1 share of Rs.10/- face value → 2 shares of Rs.5/- face value
- Bonus issue ratio: 1 bonus share of Rs.5/- for every 1 share of Rs.5/- held (1:1)
- Net effect: each pre-split share of Rs.10/- becomes 4 shares of Rs.5/- (2 from split × 2 from bonus)
- Total bonus shares to be allotted: 2,37,26,278 equity shares, allotment date 18 May 2026
- Market lot revised from 1,000 to 4,000 effective 15 May 2026
- Existing ISIN INE0NIJ01017 (Rs.10/- paid-up) invalid for exchange transactions from 15 May 2026
- New ISIN for Rs.5/- paid-up shares to be communicated by a separate notice
- Notice reference: DR-631/2026-2027

## Regulatory Changes

The market lot for DEV LABTECH VENTURE LTD (543848) is revised from 1,000 to 4,000 shares effective 15 May 2026, reflecting the 4x increase in share count resulting from the combined sub-division and bonus issue.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease using ISIN INE0NIJ01017 for exchange transactions on or after 15 May 2026
- Members must update systems to reflect the revised market lot of 4,000 shares from 15 May 2026
- Members should monitor BSE for the separate notice announcing the new ISIN for Rs.5/- paid-up shares
- Settlement and depository systems must account for the new share structure effective the record date

## Important Dates

- **07 May 2026**: Notice date; company letter reference date
- **15 May 2026**: Record date for sub-division and bonus issue; revised market lot effective; existing ISIN INE0NIJ01017 invalid for exchange transactions
- **18 May 2026**: Allotment of 2,37,26,278 bonus equity shares
- **TBD**: Separate BSE notice to announce new ISIN for Rs.5/- paid-up shares

## Impact Assessment

This is a high-impact corporate action affecting all shareholders and trading members dealing in DEV LABTECH VENTURE LTD. The combined sub-division and bonus issue quadruples the number of shares outstanding, reducing the face value from Rs.10/- to Rs.5/- per share and theoretically adjusting the market price proportionately. The market lot increase from 1,000 to 4,000 reflects this 4x multiplication. Traders and clearing members must update their systems before 15 May 2026 to avoid settlement failures. The invalidation of the existing ISIN requires immediate action from depositories and members to transition to the new ISIN once announced.
