## Summary

BSE has issued an update (Notice No. 20260513-3) continuing from Exchange Notice No. 20260424-23 dated April 24, 2026, regarding debt securities of Setco Auto Systems Private Limited (SASPL). Two scrip codes (975859 and 973453) will be available for trading with revised terms effective Thursday, May 14, 2026. The key change involves new ISINs and extended maturity dates from March 31, 2026 to May 31, 2026.

## Key Points

- Two debt securities of Setco Auto Systems Private Limited have been revised with new ISINs and extended maturity dates.
- Scrip Code 975859 (old ISIN INE0I0R07144) transitions to new ISIN INE0I0R07151.
- Scrip Code 973453 (old ISIN INE0I0R07136) transitions to new ISIN INE0I0R07169.
- Both securities have their maturity date extended from 31/03/2026 to 31/05/2026.
- Scrip long names updated from SASPL-5%-31-3-26-PVT to SASPL-5%-31-5-26-PVT for both.
- This notice follows and continues Exchange Notice No. 20260424-23 dated April 24, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

| Scrip Code | Old ISIN | New ISIN | Old Maturity | New Maturity | Old Name | New Name |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 975859 | INE0I0R07144 | INE0I0R07151 | 31/03/2026 | 31/05/2026 | SASPL-5%-31-3-26-PVT | SASPL-5%-31-5-26-PVT |
| 973453 | INE0I0R07136 | INE0I0R07169 | 31/03/2026 | 31/05/2026 | SASPL-5%-31-3-26-PVT | SASPL-5%-31-5-26-PVT |

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must update their records to reflect the new ISINs (INE0I0R07151 and INE0I0R07169) replacing the old ISINs.
- Trades from May 14, 2026 onwards must reference the new ISIN codes and revised scrip long names.
- Members should ensure their systems reflect the extended maturity dates.
- For queries, Trading Members may contact Ms. Bhairavi Bondale at +91 22 2272 8844 or debt.listing@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **April 24, 2026**: Original notice (Exchange Notice No. 20260424-23) issued.
- **May 13, 2026**: This update notice issued.
- **May 14, 2026 (Thursday)**: Effective date for trading with revised terms and new ISINs.
- **May 31, 2026**: New maturity date for both revised debt securities.

## Impact Assessment

This update affects holders and traders of two specific debt securities of Setco Auto Systems Private Limited, a private limited company. The maturity extension by two months (March to May 2026) alongside ISIN changes requires Trading Members to update their systems and records. The 5% coupon rate remains unchanged. The impact is confined to participants in the BSE Debt Segment holding or trading these specific scrips. Members who have not yet updated following the April 24 notice must act before May 14, 2026 to ensure continuity of trading.
