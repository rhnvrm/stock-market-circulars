## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) issued by MUTHOOTTU MINI FINANCIERS LIMITED effective February 12, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of its 10% debentures maturing on February 27, 2026. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the no-dealings date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspended for MMFL-10%-27-2-26-PVT debentures (ISIN: INE101Q07AP9, Code: 975439)
- Record date for redemption: February 12, 2026
- No dealings permitted from: February 12, 2026
- Circular reference: DR-819/2025-2026
- Issued by: Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President â€“ Listing Compliance and Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational circular implementing existing redemption procedures for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified MUTHOOTTU MINI FINANCIERS LIMITED debentures from February 12, 2026
- Trading members must inform their clients about the trading suspension
- Debenture holders as of record date will be eligible for redemption proceeds

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** February 9, 2026
- **Record Date:** February 12, 2026
- **No Dealings From:** February 12, 2026
- **Debenture Maturity:** February 27, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low to Medium - affects only holders of specific MUTHOOTTU MINI FINANCIERS LIMITED debentures. No impact on equity markets or broader debt market operations.

**Investor Impact:** Medium - investors holding these debentures cannot trade them from February 12, 2026. Holders as of record date will receive redemption proceeds as per debenture terms.

**Operational Impact:** Routine suspension procedure for debenture redemption. Trading members need to update their systems to block trading in the specified ISIN from the no-dealings date.
