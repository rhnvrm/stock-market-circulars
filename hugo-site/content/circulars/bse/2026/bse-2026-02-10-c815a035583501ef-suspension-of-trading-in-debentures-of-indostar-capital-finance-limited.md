## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in specific non-convertible debentures (ISIN: INE896L07959, Code: 975444) of INDOSTAR CAPITAL FINANCE LIMITED effective February 13, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for interest payment and redemption of the debentures. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Company: INDOSTAR CAPITAL FINANCE LIMITED
- Debenture ISIN: INE896L07959
- Debenture Code: 975444
- Series: ICFL-9.95%-28-2-26-PVT
- Record Date: February 13, 2026
- No Dealings From: February 13, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- Notice Reference: DR-820/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures of INDOSTAR CAPITAL FINANCE LIMITED from February 13, 2026
- Trading members are required to take note of the suspension and ensure compliance
- No new positions or trades should be executed in the affected debenture series

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: February 10, 2026
- **Record Date**: February 13, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Effective From**: February 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders and traders of this specific debenture series. The suspension affects liquidity for this particular non-convertible debenture (9.95% coupon) but is a standard procedure for record date determination.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to block trading in this security from February 13, 2026. Investors holding these debentures will receive interest payment and redemption proceeds as per the record date.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders as of the record date will be entitled to receive the final interest payment and redemption amount. No trading opportunity available after February 13, 2026.
