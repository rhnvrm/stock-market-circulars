## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of SMR Jewels Limited (Scrip Code: 544774, Scrip ID: SMR) will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange effective Monday, June 8, 2026, under the 'MT' Group of Securities on the SME platform. As per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012, the scrip will trade in the Trade-for-Trade segment for the first 10 trading days.

## Key Points

- **Listing Date:** Monday, June 8, 2026
- **Scrip Code:** 544774 | **Scrip ID:** SMR
- **ISIN:** INE11XK01017
- **Total Securities:** 18,653,743 Equity Shares of Rs. 10/- each, fully paid up
- **Distinctive Number Range:** 1 to 18,653,743
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 128/- per share (Face Value Rs. 10/- + Premium Rs. 118/-)
- **Market Lot:** 1,000 equity shares
- **Date of Allotment:** June 4, 2026
- **Group:** MT Group (SME Segment)
- **Trade-for-Trade segment** for first 10 trading days per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012
- Trading only in dematerialised form as per SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000
- SMR Jewels will be part of the **Special Pre-open Session (SPOS)** on June 8, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. The listing is subject to existing SEBI guidelines:
- Trade-for-Trade segment applicability per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 (January 20, 2012)
- Mandatory dematerialised trading per SEBI guidelines (February 16, 2000)
- Special Pre-open Session applicable per BSE Notice No. 20120216-29 (February 16, 2012)

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade SMR Jewels shares only in demat form
- Trades must be executed in minimum market lots of 1,000 equity shares
- Trading members should note Trade-for-Trade segment restrictions for the first 10 trading days
- Lock-in details as per Annexure I; Shareholding Pattern as per Annexure II
- Corporate actions for lock-in period have been executed in depository records

## Important Dates

- **June 4, 2026:** Date of allotment in public issue
- **June 5, 2026:** Notice date
- **June 8, 2026:** Listing date and commencement of trading (effective Monday); also SPOS date
- **~June 23, 2026:** End of Trade-for-Trade segment period (approximately 10 trading days from listing)

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard SME IPO listing with moderate market impact. The scrip is restricted to the Trade-for-Trade segment for 10 trading days, meaning no intraday trading is permitted and each trade must result in delivery. The market lot of 1,000 shares at Rs. 128/- implies a minimum trade value of approximately Rs. 1,28,000/-. The Market Maker is Rikhav Securities Limited, providing liquidity support as required under SME listing norms. The Registrar is Purva Shareregistry (India) Private Limited. The listing is relevant primarily to trading members dealing in SME securities and investors who participated in the IPO.
