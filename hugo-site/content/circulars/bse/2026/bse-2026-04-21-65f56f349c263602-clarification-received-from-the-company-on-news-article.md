## Summary

BSE's Surveillance Department issued Notice No. 20260421-17 dated April 21, 2026, informing members and investors that the Exchange had sought clarification from Deccan Gold Mines Ltd (Scrip Code: 512068) regarding news articles published in various newspapers, websites, and television channels. The company's reply has been received and attached to the notice.

## Key Points

- BSE Surveillance Department proactively sought clarification from Deccan Gold Mines Ltd (Scrip Code: 512068) in response to news articles appearing in various media outlets.
- The news article in question originated from the Economic Times (economictimes.indiatimes.com).
- The company's clarification/reply has been received and is attached to the notice for transparency.
- Notice issued under the Equity segment, Company Related category, by the DOSS Department.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this notice.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. This is an informational notice providing the company's clarification to members and investors.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: April 21, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine surveillance disclosure with minimal market impact. The notice is limited to Deccan Gold Mines Ltd (Scrip Code: 512068) and serves to keep investors informed about the accuracy of media reports concerning the company. No broader market or operational implications are expected.
