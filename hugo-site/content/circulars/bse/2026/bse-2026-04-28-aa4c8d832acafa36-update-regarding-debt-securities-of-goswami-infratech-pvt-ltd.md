## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (No. 20260428-36) informing trading members that debt securities of Goswami Infratech Pvt Ltd will be suspended from trading effective Monday, May 4, 2026, due to restructuring. This is a continuation of the earlier Notice No. 20241226-2 dated December 26, 2024.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code **974946** with ISIN **INE219O07362** will be suspended from trading on BSE's Debt Market Segment.
- Suspension is effective from **Monday, May 4, 2026**.
- Reason for suspension: **restructuring** of the debt securities.
- This notice is a continuation of Notice No. 20241226-2 (December 26, 2024), indicating the matter has been ongoing.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework introduced. This is an operational action under BSE's authority to suspend trading in debt securities undergoing restructuring, consistent with existing listing and trading rules for the Debt Market Segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members** must note the suspension and refrain from executing trades in Scrip Code 974946 (ISIN INE219O07362) on or after May 4, 2026.
- Members with client positions or pending orders in this scrip should take appropriate action prior to the suspension date.
- For queries, trading members may contact:
  - **Mr. Akash Shinde** – +91 22 2272 8844 | debt.listing@bseindia.com
  - **Hardik Bhuta**, Assistant Vice President, DCS-Listing

## Impact Assessment

This suspension directly affects investors and trading members holding or trading Goswami Infratech Pvt Ltd's debt securities (ISIN INE219O07362). Once suspended, no trades can be executed on BSE's Debt Market Segment, reducing liquidity to zero for this instrument. Bondholders will need to explore off-market transactions or await resolution of the restructuring process. The prior notice from December 2024 suggests this restructuring has been in progress for several months.
