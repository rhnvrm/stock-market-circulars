## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260225-45 informing trading members of the new ISIN number assigned to the sub-divided equity shares of Varvee Global Limited (Scrip Code: 514274). This notice is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20260223-20 dated February 23, 2026. The sub-division reduces the face value of each equity share from Rs.10/- to Rs.5/-.

## Key Points

- New ISIN for Varvee Global Limited (Scrip Code: 514274) is **INE273D01027**
- Sub-division of equity shares from face value Rs.10/- to Rs.5/- per share
- New ISIN is effective for all trades on and from the ex-date: **March 2, 2026**
- This notice continues from Exchange Notice No. 20260223-20 dated February 23, 2026
- DR reference: 831/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework introduced. This notice implements a corporate action (share sub-division) already approved and previously announced, updating the ISIN identifier to reflect the new share denomination.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their systems to use the new ISIN **INE273D01027** for all trades in Varvee Global Limited equity shares on and from March 2, 2026
- All trades referencing the old ISIN should be migrated to the new ISIN from the ex-date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** February 25, 2026
- **Reference Notice Date:** February 23, 2026 (Notice No. 20260223-20)
- **Ex-Date (Effective Date for New ISIN):** March 2, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational update following a share sub-division corporate action. Trading members and depository participants must ensure their systems recognize and process the new ISIN (INE273D01027) from the ex-date. Existing shareholders will have their holdings reflected under the new ISIN with the adjusted face value of Rs.5/- per share. No change in the company's overall market capitalization is expected solely from this sub-division.
