## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Currency Derivatives segment on Saturday, May 30, 2026. The session will run from both the primary site (PR) and the disaster recovery site (DR), giving trading members an opportunity to test their systems under simulated market conditions including exceptional scenarios.

## Key Points

- Mock trading covers Currency Derivatives and Cross Currency Derivatives
- Sessions run from both primary site (PR) and disaster recovery (DR) site
- Trading members using third-party platforms (ETI APIs) or in-house systems may participate
- Trades have no margin, pay-in, or pay-out obligations and create no rights or liabilities
- Members are requested to submit feedback by 4:00 pm on the day

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational test exercise.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are encouraged to actively participate in the mock session
- Feedback to be provided by 4:00 pm on May 30, 2026, via contact numbers or email to BSE Tech Support or Help Desk

## Important Dates

- **Mock Trading Date:** Saturday, May 30, 2026
- **Log-in:** 09:15 am – 10:00 am
- **Continuous Trading – Currency Derivatives (PR):** 10:00 am – 12:00 pm
- **Continuous Trading – Cross Currency Derivatives (PR):** 10:00 am – 12:00 pm
- **Switchover to DR:** 12:00 pm – 12:45 pm
- **Continuous Trading – Currency Derivatives (DR):** 12:45 pm – 02:45 pm
- **Continuous Trading – Cross Currency Derivatives (DR):** 12:45 pm – 02:45 pm
- **Trade Modification (Currency):** 03:00 pm
- **Trade Modification (Cross Currency):** 03:00 pm
- **Feedback Deadline:** 4:00 pm on May 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This mock trading session has no real market impact. It is purely a testing exercise for trading members to validate their systems and ETI API integrations under simulated conditions, including switchover from the primary to the disaster recovery site. No financial obligations arise from trades executed during this session.
