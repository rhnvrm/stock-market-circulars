## Summary

BSE has listed 2,49,30,765 new equity shares of MUFIN GREEN FINANCE LIMITED (Scrip Code: 542774, ISIN: INE08KJ01020), issued to Non-Promoters on a preferential basis at an issue price of Rs. 98/- per share (face value Re. 1/- at a premium of Rs. 97/-). These shares are permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Friday, June 19, 2026.

## Key Points

- **Company:** MUFIN GREEN FINANCE LIMITED (Scrip Code: 542774)
- **ISIN:** INE08KJ01020
- **New Shares:** 2,49,30,765 equity shares of Re. 1/- each
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 98/- per share (Re. 1 face value + Rs. 97 premium)
- **Issued To:** Non-Promoters on a preferential basis
- **Pari-passu:** Ranking pari-passu with existing equity shares
- **Distribution Numbers:** 173231424 to 198162188
- **Trading effective:** June 19, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice for newly allotted securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed that the new securities are listed and permitted for trading from June 19, 2026. The shares carry a lock-in restriction that must be observed by holders.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** March 4, 2026
- **Trading Effective Date:** June 19, 2026 (Friday)
- **Lock-in Expiry:** December 22, 2026 (for all 2,49,30,765 shares, Dist. Nos. 173231424 to 198162188)

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds approximately 2.49 crore new equity shares to the tradeable float of MUFIN GREEN FINANCE LIMITED. Since these shares are issued to Non-Promoters and are subject to lock-in until December 22, 2026, near-term free-float impact is limited. The preferential allotment at Rs. 98/- per share indicates the company raised capital from outside the promoter group. Market impact is expected to be minimal given the lock-in period.
