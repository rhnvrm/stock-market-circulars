## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the price bands for 14 specified scrips have been revised from their existing levels, effective July 14, 2026. The revisions, issued under Notice No. 20260713-35 by the Department of Surveillance (DOSS), apply to a mix of small-cap and thinly traded securities and set new percentage price bands ranging from 2% to 10%.

## Key Points

- Price bands revised for 14 scrips effective July 14, 2026.
- New price bands range from 2% to 10% depending on the scrip.
- Scrips affected: Acceleratebs India Ltd (543938) - 5%, BFL Asset Finvest Ltd (539662) - 10%, Exato Technologies Ltd (544626) - 10%, GSB Finance Ltd (511543) - 5%, Indo Credit Capital Ltd (526887) - 2%, Jindal Leasefin Ltd (539947) - 2%, Kahan Packaging Ltd (543979) - 10%, Kaizen Agro Infrabuild Ltd (538833) - 10%, Liotech Industries Ltd (544796) - 5%, Peninsula Land Ltd (503031) - 2%, PPAP Automotive Ltd (532934) - 10%, Shardul Securities Ltd (512393) - 10%, Shree Steel Wire Ropes Ltd (513488) - 10%, UHM Vacation Ltd (544779) - 5%.
- Queries can be directed to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Regulatory Changes

This is a routine surveillance-driven adjustment of daily price bands (circuit filters) for the listed scrips, applied by BSE's Surveillance Department under its standard market monitoring framework.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must update their systems to reflect the revised price bands for the affected scrips and ensure orders comply with the new circuit limits from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- Effective Date: July 14, 2026
- Notice Date: July 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The revision affects intraday price volatility limits for 14 scrips, most of which are small-cap or less liquid names. Tighter or wider price bands can constrain or expand daily trading ranges, impacting short-term trading strategies and liquidity for holders of these specific securities. Overall market impact is limited given the narrow scope of scrips involved.
