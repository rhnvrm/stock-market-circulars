## Summary

BSE has shortlisted Nutricircle Ltd (Scrip Code: 530219, ISIN: INE536C01029) under Additional Surveillance Measure effective January 05, 2025, due to unusual price movement. The consolidated list now includes 12 securities subject to this enhanced monitoring framework designed to curb excessive speculation and protect investor interests.

## Key Points

- Nutricircle Ltd newly added to Additional Surveillance Measure framework
- Total of 12 securities currently under this surveillance measure
- Measure targets securities exhibiting unusual price movements
- Framework aims to protect investor interests and maintain market integrity

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework introduced. This circular applies existing Additional Surveillance Measure provisions to securities showing abnormal price behavior.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure compliance with Additional Surveillance Measure requirements for affected securities
- Enhanced monitoring and reporting obligations apply to transactions in these 12 securities
- Members should inform clients about surveillance status of these securities

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** January 05, 2025 - Additional Surveillance Measure applicable to Nutricircle Ltd

## Impact Assessment

**Trading Impact:** Securities under Additional Surveillance Measure typically face restrictions such as higher margin requirements, trade-for-trade settlement, or periodic call auctions to dampen speculative activity.
