## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 67,708 new equity shares of Gabriel Pet Straps Limited (Scrip Code: 544108) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Thursday, March 12, 2026. These shares were issued on a preferential basis to promoters at a premium.

## Key Points

- 67,708 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 246/- (issue price Rs. 256/-) to promoters on preferential basis
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 7412337 to 7480044
- ISIN: INE0QZF01012
- Trading permitted from March 12, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for newly allotted securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities available for trading from March 12, 2026
- The entire allotment of 67,708 shares is subject to lock-in and cannot be traded until the lock-in period expires

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** December 8, 2025
- **Trading Effective Date:** March 12, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry:** September 30, 2027 (for all 67,708 shares, Dist. Nos. 7412337 to 7480044)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine preferential allotment listing with minimal market impact. The entire lot of 67,708 shares is locked in until September 30, 2027, meaning no immediate supply pressure on the stock. The issue price of Rs. 256/- (face value Rs. 10/- + premium Rs. 246/-) reflects the valuation agreed at the time of allotment in December 2025.
