## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that FREDUN PHARMACEUTICALS LIMITED (Scrip Code: 539730) has fixed 16 July 2026 as the record date for its bonus issue. Shares will trade on an ex-bonus basis effective the same date, 16 July 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: FREDUN PHARMACEUTICALS LIMITED (Scrip Code 539730)
- Bonus ratio: 2 (TWO) new equity shares of Rs. 10/- each for every 1 (ONE) existing equity share of Rs. 10/- each held
- Record date: 16 July 2026
- Ex-bonus date: 16 July 2026
- Notice/DR reference: DR-673/2026-2027
- Total shares to be allotted: 11,025,380 equity shares, as on 17 July 2026, per the company's letter dated 14 July 2026

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine corporate action notification under BSE Listing Operations, not a regulatory or policy change.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must update systems to reflect the ex-bonus basis trading from 16 July 2026 and ensure settlement and entitlement processing aligns with the record date.

## Important Dates

- Record Date: 16 July 2026
- Ex-Bonus Date: 16 July 2026
- Allotment Date: 17 July 2026

## Impact Assessment

The bonus issue will increase Fredun Pharmaceuticals' outstanding share count by 11,025,380 shares, with the stock price adjusting downward proportionally on the ex-bonus date to reflect the 2:1 bonus ratio. Existing shareholders as of the record date will receive additional shares without any cash outlay, while overall market capitalization remains unchanged. Impact is limited to this specific stock and its shareholders.
