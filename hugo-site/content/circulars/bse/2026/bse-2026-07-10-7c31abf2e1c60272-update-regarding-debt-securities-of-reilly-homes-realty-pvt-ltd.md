## Summary

BSE has issued a notice, in continuation of its earlier Exchange Notice No. 20240920-23 dated 20 September 2024, informing Trading Members that trading in a specific debt security of Reilly Homes Realty Pvt Ltd (RHPL) will be suspended on account of debt restructuring, effective Wednesday, July 15, 2026.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code 976021 (ISIN: INE0YTX08017) of Reilly Homes Realty Pvt Ltd will be suspended from trading.
- The suspension is on account of debt restructuring, as detailed in the Annexure to the notice.
- This update follows a prior Exchange Notice No. 20240920-23 dated 20 September 2024 regarding the same debt securities.
- Trading Members can contact Ms. Rutuja Mhasade (+91 22 2272 8597, debt.listing@bseindia.com) for further details.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework changes; this is an operational notice implementing suspension of trading for a specific debt security due to an ongoing debt restructuring process.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members must take note of the suspension and cease trading in the specified ISIN from the effective date. No other filing or compliance action is required by market participants.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 10, 2026
- Effective Date of Suspension: Wednesday, July 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The suspension affects only holders and prospective traders of the specific debt security (ISIN INE0YTX08017) issued by Reilly Homes Realty Pvt Ltd. It reflects the company's ongoing debt restructuring process and limits liquidity for bondholders from the effective date, though it does not have a broader market-wide impact.
