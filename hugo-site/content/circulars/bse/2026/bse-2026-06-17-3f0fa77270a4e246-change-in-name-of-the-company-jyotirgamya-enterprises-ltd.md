## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Atma Industries Limited (Scrip Code 539246) has changed its name to Jyotirgamya Enterprises Ltd. The SCRIP ID and abbreviated name on BOLT Plus System will remain ATMAIND, effective June 23, 2026.

## Key Points

- Existing company name: Atma Industries Limited
- New company name: Jyotirgamya Enterprises Ltd
- Scrip Code: 539246
- BOLT Plus SCRIP ID: ATMAIND (unchanged)
- Abbreviated name on BOLT Plus: ATMAIND (unchanged)
- Effective date: June 23, 2026
- Segment: Debt

## Regulatory Changes

The company's display name on exchange systems will be updated from ATMA INDUSTRIES LIMITED to Jyotirgamya Enterprises Ltd. No changes are being made to the SCRIP ID or abbreviated name used in trading systems.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should update their internal records and client-facing materials to reflect the new company name. No action is required with respect to BOLT Plus trading system identifiers, as these remain unchanged.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: June 17, 2026
- Effective Date of Name Change on Exchange: June 23, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine administrative name change with no effect on trading identifiers, ISIN, or scrip code. Investors holding securities of this company are unaffected operationally; only the display name changes in exchange records.
