## Summary

BSE has announced that Aarnav Fashions Limited (Scrip Code: 539562) will undergo a group change from Group X to Group B effective February 25, 2026. Trading members are advised to take note of this reclassification.

## Key Points

- Aarnav Fashions Limited (Scrip Code: 539562) is being moved from Group X to Group B
- The change is effective from February 25, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

The company is being reclassified from Group X (a restricted/surveillance group typically for companies with compliance issues or low liquidity) to Group B (a broader general trading group), signifying an improvement in the company's listing compliance status.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their systems and records to reflect the new group classification for Aarnav Fashions Limited (Scrip Code: 539562) effective February 25, 2026
- No specific action required from investors, but brokers must ensure correct group designation is applied in trading terminals

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** February 24, 2026
- **Effective Date of Group Change:** February 25, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The reclassification from Group X to Group B is generally a positive development for Aarnav Fashions Limited. Group X securities typically carry trading restrictions and heightened scrutiny, while Group B securities have more standard trading conditions. This move may result in improved liquidity, broader investor participation, and potentially reduced trading costs for the scrip. Existing holders and prospective investors should be aware of the changed trading dynamics post-reclassification.
