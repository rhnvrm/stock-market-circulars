## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document of Futurex Management Solutions Limited, filed on February 25, 2026, on the BSE SME website. The document is made available for a minimum period of 21 days in accordance with Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations, inviting public comments.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Futurex Management Solutions Limited on February 25, 2026
- Document is hosted on the BSE SME website for public review
- Public comments are invited within 21 days from the date of notice (i.e., by March 20, 2026)
- Document complies with all disclosures specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural notice under the SEBI ICDR (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, requiring public availability of draft offer documents before SME IPO proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members of the public, investors, and market participants may review the Draft Offer Document on the BSE SME website
- Any comments or objections must be submitted within 21 days from February 27, 2026 to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Futurex Management Solutions Limited is required to address any comments received during the public comment period

## Important Dates

- **Draft Offer Document Filing Date:** February 25, 2026
- **Notice Date:** February 27, 2026
- **Public Comment Deadline:** March 20, 2026 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal broad market impact. It is a routine procedural step in the SME IPO process for Futurex Management Solutions Limited. Prospective investors interested in the upcoming SME IPO should review the Draft Offer Document at the BSE SME website and submit any comments before the deadline. No trading or compliance obligations are imposed on existing market participants.
