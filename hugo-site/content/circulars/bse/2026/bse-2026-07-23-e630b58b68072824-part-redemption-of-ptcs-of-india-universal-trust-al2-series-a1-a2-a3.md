## Summary

BSE has notified trading members of the partial redemption of Pass-Through Certificates (PTCs) issued under India Universal Trust AL2 - Series A1, A2 and A3. The reduced face value per PTC for each series has been communicated by the trust, and these securities will trade at the revised face value from July 24, 2026.

## Key Points

- Notice pertains to the Debt segment, Listing Operations department, Corporate Actions category.
- Three PTC series are affected: Series A1 (ISIN INE1CBK15011, Scrip Code 976206), Series A2 (ISIN INE1CBK15029, Scrip Code 976207), and Series A3 (ISIN INE1CBK15037, Scrip Code 976208).
- Reduced face values post partial redemption:
  - Series A1 (IUTAL2-8.03%-21-11-26-PTC): Rs. 238,925.01 per PTC
  - Series A2 (IUTAL2-8.09%-21-11-27-PTC): Rs. 6,078,139.51 per PTC
  - Series A3 (IUTAL2-8.17%-21-11-30-PTC): Rs. 9,194,183.32 per PTC
- Reference number: DR-679/2026-2027.
- No attachment was provided with this notice.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an operational notification of a corporate action (partial redemption) rather than a regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must update their systems to reflect the reduced face values for the three PTC series and ensure trades executed on or after the effective date use the revised face value per PTC.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 23, 2026
- Effective Date for trading at reduced face value: July 24, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt-market operational update limited to holders and traders of India Universal Trust AL2 PTCs. It has no bearing on equity markets or listed company stocks and requires only administrative acknowledgment by trading members dealing in these debt instruments.
