## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members that LTIMindtree Ltd will be renamed to LTM Limited in the equity derivatives segment effective April 17, 2026. The scrip code (540005) and symbol (LTM) remain unchanged, and no other parameters are affected.

## Key Points

- The underlying scrip name in the equity derivatives segment will change from **LTIMindtree Ltd** to **LTM Limited** effective April 17, 2026.
- Scrip Code: **540005** (unchanged)
- Symbol: **LTM** (unchanged)
- All other trading parameters for the scrip remain the same.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative update reflecting a corporate name change for LTIMindtree Ltd (NSE/BSE symbol: LTM) to LTM Limited, effective in the derivatives segment from April 17, 2026.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are advised to update their internal records and systems to reflect the new underlying scrip name (LTM Limited) for scrip code 540005 in the equity derivatives segment.

## Important Dates

- **April 17, 2026**: Effective date for the name change in the equity derivatives segment.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is minimal. This is a cosmetic/administrative name change only. The scrip code, trading symbol, and all other contract parameters remain unchanged. Trading members need to update references to the underlying company name in their systems but no other action is required.
