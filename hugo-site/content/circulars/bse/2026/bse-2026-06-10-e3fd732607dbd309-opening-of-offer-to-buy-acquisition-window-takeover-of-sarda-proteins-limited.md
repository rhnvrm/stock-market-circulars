## Summary

BSE has notified trading members and custodians of an open offer by ONIX RENEWABLE LIMITED (Acquirer) along with three Persons Acting in Concert (PACs) — DIVYESHKUMAR MANSUKHBHAI SAVALIYA, PIYUSH MANSUKHBHAI SAVALIYA, and NIKHIL HARESHBHAI SAVALIYA — to acquire equity shares of SARDA PROTEINS LIMITED (SPL) from public shareholders. The offer is made under the SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 2011 and involves a change in control and management of the target company.

## Key Points

- Acquirer: ONIX RENEWABLE LIMITED, along with three PACs
- Target company: SARDA PROTEINS LIMITED (SPL)
- Offer price: Rs. 115.00 per fully paid-up equity share (face value Rs. 10 each)
- Shares to be acquired: Up to 17,30,400 equity shares, representing 19.28% of the voting share capital
- Offer triggered under Regulations 3(1) & 4 of SEBI (SAST) Regulations, 2011 — substantial acquisition with change in control
- Offer to Buy window: June 11, 2026 (Thursday) to June 24, 2026 (Wednesday)
- Letter of Offer is attached to the notice
- Offer is conducted via the BSE acquisition window mechanism per SEBI circular CIR/CFD/POLICYCELL/1/2015 and CFD/DCR2/CIR/P/2016/131

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The offer operates under the established SEBI mechanism for acquisition of shares through the Stock Exchange pursuant to Tender-Offers under Takeovers, as revised by Exchange notices 20170210-16, 20190424-35, 20200528-32, 20201102-43, and 20210825-62.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and custodians must facilitate client participation in the offer to buy window as per the Revised Guidelines of SEBI circular CFD/DCR2/CIR/P/2016/131 dated December 9, 2016
- Market participants should refer to BSE website (www.bseindia.com) for full details of the Offer to Buy
- Participants must follow Exchange notice 20170210-16 (Feb 10, 2017) and 20190424-35 (April 24, 2019) revised guidelines

## Important Dates

- Notice date: June 10, 2026
- Offer to Buy window opens: June 11, 2026 (Thursday)
- Offer to Buy window closes: June 24, 2026 (Wednesday)

## Impact Assessment

This open offer directly impacts public shareholders of SARDA PROTEINS LIMITED, who are being offered Rs. 115 per share for up to 19.28% of the company's voting capital. The transaction represents a change in control and management, making it a significant corporate event. Shareholders holding SPL equity must evaluate whether to tender their shares at the offer price during the June 11–24, 2026 window. Trading members and custodians have operational obligations to support client participation through the BSE acquisition window mechanism.
