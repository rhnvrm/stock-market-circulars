## Summary

BSE has revised the acquisition window (takeover) period for P.M. TELELINNKS LIMITED. The acquisition window will now be extended from Tuesday, January 27, 2026, to Wednesday, January 28, 2026. This revision is necessary to exclude Monday, January 26, 2026 (SEBI Holiday) and Thursday, January 15, 2026 (Exchange Holiday due to Municipal Corporation Elections in Maharashtra).

## Key Points

- Acquisition window extended by one day to accommodate holidays
- Original notice referenced: 20260109-14 dated January 09, 2026
- Monday, January 26, 2026 excluded (SEBI Holiday)
- Thursday, January 15, 2026 excluded (Exchange Holiday - Municipal Corporation Elections in Maharashtra)
- New acquisition window dates: Tuesday, January 27, 2026 to Wednesday, January 28, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a procedural adjustment to the previously announced acquisition window timeline.

## Compliance Requirements

All market participants involved in the takeover of P.M. TELELINNKS LIMITED must:
- Note the revised acquisition window dates
- Plan their participation accordingly for the extended period
- Follow standard takeover procedures during the revised window

## Important Dates

- **January 13, 2026**: Notice issued
- **January 15, 2026**: Exchange Holiday (Municipal Corporation Elections in Maharashtra) - Excluded
- **January 26, 2026**: SEBI Holiday - Excluded
- **January 27, 2026**: Acquisition window start date (Tuesday)
- **January 28, 2026**: Acquisition window end date (Wednesday)

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to participants in the P.M. TELELINNKS LIMITED takeover process. The one-day extension ensures adequate trading opportunity despite intervening holidays. This is a routine procedural adjustment with minimal market-wide impact, affecting only stakeholders directly involved in this specific acquisition.
