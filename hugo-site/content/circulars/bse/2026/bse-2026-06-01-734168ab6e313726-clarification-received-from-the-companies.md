## Summary

BSE's Surveillance department (DOSS) observed significant price and volume movements in eight scrips and sought clarifications from the respective companies. As of June 1, 2026, four companies have submitted their replies, while four others are yet to respond.

## Key Points

- BSE identified significant price/volume movement in eight listed companies and wrote to each seeking relevant information for market participants.
- Replies have been received from Cargotrans Maritime Ltd (543618), Abram Food Ltd (544422), Adani Total Gas Ltd (542066), and Astec Lifesciences Ltd (533138); attached to this notice.
- Replies are still awaited from Elango Industries Ltd (513452), Nexxus Petro Industries Ltd (544265), Spice Lounge Food Works Ltd (539895), and York Exports Ltd (530675).
- The notice is issued by the Surveillance department under the signatures of Deputy Vice President Sanjay Jain and Manager Shreya Ghose.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice is part of ongoing exchange-level surveillance activity to ensure investors and trading members have access to the latest relevant company information.

## Compliance Requirements

- The four companies that have not yet responded — Elango Industries, Nexxus Petro Industries, Spice Lounge Food Works, and York Exports — are expected to submit clarifications to BSE promptly.
- Trading members and investors should refer to the attached replies from the four responding companies before trading in these scrips.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 1, 2026
- Replies from Cargotrans Maritime, Abram Food, Adani Total Gas, and Astec Lifesciences are already attached as of this date.
- No specific deadline mentioned for pending replies from the remaining four companies.

## Impact Assessment

The notice reflects active surveillance monitoring of unusual trading activity across eight scrips spanning diverse sectors (maritime logistics, food, energy, agrochemicals, and others). Investors trading in these stocks should review the attached company clarifications. The four scrips still awaiting replies (Elango Industries, Nexxus Petro Industries, Spice Lounge Food Works, York Exports) remain under scrutiny, which may indicate continued regulatory attention and potential follow-up actions if responses are not received.
