## Summary

BSE has issued an update to its earlier notice (No. 20260612-29 dated June 12, 2026) regarding the revised nomination framework for demat accounts and mutual fund folios. This follows SEBI Circular No. SEBI/HO/OIAE/OIAE_IAD-3/P/CIR/2026/12676 dated May 29, 2026, which prescribes revised nomination norms effective September 1, 2026. Members trading on the BSE StAR MF platform are advised to update their systems to reflect the revised field requirements for nominee details under the UCC Creation and Nomination Modification structures.

## Key Points

- Investors may nominate up to three nominees.
- Where nomination is opted for, the nominee's name and relationship with the investor are mandatory fields.
- If a nominee is a minor, the minor's date of birth is mandatory.
- Additional nominee details (address, mobile number, email ID, percentage share, and guardian details for minors) remain optional, subject to applicable validations.
- Based on inputs from Registrar & Transfer Agents (RTAs), these optional fields will be reflected in the UCC Creation and Nomination Modification structures on BSE StAR MF.
- Five annexures accompany the notice: SEBI circular, Client Master Bulk Upload (183-field format), 56-field Bulk Download/Upload format, UCC API (183 fields) nomination validation changes, and Nomination API (56 fields) nomination validation changes.
- For non-demat transactions requiring new folio creation at the AMC/RTA end, nominee-related details captured at the UCC level will be forwarded to RTAs for incorporation into folio records.
- Members must ensure nominee details are accurate, complete, and verified prior to order placement, as incorrect or incomplete information may cause RTA rejection of transactions.

## Regulatory Changes

SEBI Circular No. SEBI/HO/OIAE/OIAE_IAD-3/P/CIR/2026/12676 (dated May 29, 2026) revises the nomination framework for demat accounts and mutual fund folios, allowing up to three nominees with specified mandatory and optional fields, as detailed above.

## Compliance Requirements

Members, including those using third-party vendor solutions or in-house trading applications, must update their systems and operational processes to align with the revised UCC Creation and Nomination Modification field structures on BSE StAR MF ahead of the effective date. Nominee data must be validated and complete before order placement to avoid RTA-driven transaction rejections.

## Important Dates

- May 29, 2026: SEBI Circular No. SEBI/HO/OIAE/OIAE_IAD-3/P/CIR/2026/12676 issued.
- June 12, 2026: BSE's original notice (20260612-29) on the modified nomination norms.
- September 1, 2026: Revised nomination norms become applicable.
- July 16, 2026: This update notice issued, with revised formats and API validation annexures.

## Impact Assessment

This is an operational/compliance update primarily affecting BSE StAR MF trading members, RTAs, and vendors who must adapt UCC and nomination-related systems before the September 1, 2026 deadline. There is no direct impact on listed stocks or trading activity, but non-compliance could disrupt mutual fund order processing for affected members.
