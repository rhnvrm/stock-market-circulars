## Summary

BSE's Surveillance Department (DOSS) issued Notice No. 20260520-14 on 20 May 2026 regarding significant price/volume movements observed in four listed companies. The Exchange had written to these companies to ensure trading members and investors have the latest relevant information. Clarifications have been received from two companies, while replies remain awaited from two others.

## Key Points

- BSE observed significant price/volume movement in four scrips and initiated inquiries
- Inspirisys Solutions Ltd (Scrip Code: 532774) has submitted its reply
- Brainbees Solutions Ltd (Scrip Code: 544226) has submitted its reply
- ZR2 Bioenergy Ltd (Security Code: 506640) has not yet responded
- Accord Transformer & Switchgear Ltd (Scrip Code: 544710) has not yet responded
- Replies received are attached to the notice as referenced attachments

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This notice is part of BSE's ongoing market surveillance framework to ensure timely and transparent dissemination of material information to investors and trading members.

## Compliance Requirements

- Inspirisys Solutions Ltd and Brainbees Solutions Ltd have fulfilled the disclosure requirement by submitting clarifications.
- ZR2 Bioenergy Ltd and Accord Transformer & Switchgear Ltd are expected to submit their replies to BSE at the earliest.
- Trading members and investors are advised to review the attached clarification documents before trading in the mentioned scrips.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 20 May 2026
- **Reply deadline for pending companies:** Not specified; replies are described as "awaited"

## Impact Assessment

For Inspirisys Solutions (532774) and Brainbees Solutions (544226), the receipt of clarifications provides market participants with relevant information to make informed trading decisions, potentially reducing speculative volatility. For ZR2 Bioenergy (506640) and Accord Transformer & Switchgear (544710), the absence of a reply may sustain information uncertainty, and these scrips could remain under heightened surveillance scrutiny until clarifications are provided. Investors should exercise caution when trading in the non-responding scrips.
