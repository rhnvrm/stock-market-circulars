## Summary

BSE has issued a notice regarding the allocation of equity shares to anchor investors in the public issue (IPO) of OMNITECH ENGINEERING LIMITED. This disclosure is made pursuant to SEBI regulations requiring exchanges to publish anchor investor allotment details prior to the opening of public subscription.

## Key Points

- OMNITECH ENGINEERING LIMITED is proceeding with its public issue (IPO) on BSE.
- Equity shares have been allocated to identified anchor investors at the anchor investor price.
- The anchor investor allocation is completed one day before the IPO subscription opens.
- Anchor investors are subject to a mandatory lock-in period on their allotted shares.
- The circular provides the list of anchor investors, number of shares allotted, and the anchor allotment price.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This notice is issued in compliance with SEBI (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2018, and SEBI circular on anchor investor framework, which mandates disclosure of anchor investor allocation details on the stock exchange platform before the issue opening date.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company and its lead managers are required to ensure anchor investor allotment details are disclosed to BSE prior to the IPO subscription opening.
- Anchor investors are subject to a lock-in period of 30 days from the date of allotment for 50% of shares allotted, and 90 days for the remaining 50%, as per SEBI norms.
- No action is required from retail or non-institutional investors at this stage.

## Important Dates

- **Anchor Investor Allocation Date:** 2026-02-24
- **IPO Subscription Opening:** Expected 2026-02-25 (one day after anchor allocation, per standard SEBI timelines)
- **Lock-in Period (50% of anchor allocation):** 30 days from date of allotment
- **Lock-in Period (remaining 50%):** 90 days from date of allotment

## Impact Assessment

The anchor investor allocation signals institutional interest in OMNITECH ENGINEERING LIMITED's IPO. Successful anchor allocation at the issue price lends credibility to the offering and may positively influence retail and non-institutional investor sentiment. The disclosure is routine in nature and does not affect existing listed securities or broader market operations. Impact is confined to participants in the OMNITECH ENGINEERING LIMITED IPO.
