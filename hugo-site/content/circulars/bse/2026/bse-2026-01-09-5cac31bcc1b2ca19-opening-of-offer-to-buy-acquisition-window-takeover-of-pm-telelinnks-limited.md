## Summary

BSE has announced the opening of an Offer to Buy through an Acquisition Window (Takeover) for P.M. Telelinnks Limited. A Letter of Offer will be sent to public shareholders of the company. Shareholders are advised to consult their stockbroker, investment consultant, or the Manager/Registrar to the Offer for clarifications regarding the action to be taken. Shareholders who have recently sold their equity shares should hand over the Letter of Offer and accompanying forms to their buyers.

## Key Points

- Letter of Offer will be sent to all public shareholders of P.M. Telelinnks Limited
- Document is marked as important and requires immediate attention
- Shareholders can consult stockbrokers, investment consultants, or the Manager/Registrar for clarifications
- Recent sellers of equity shares should forward the Letter of Offer to buyers

## Regulatory Changes

Not applicable - this is a notification of a takeover offer process rather than a regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public shareholders should review the Letter of Offer when received
- Shareholders requiring clarifications should contact their stockbroker, investment consultant, or the Manager/Registrar to the Open Offer
- Shareholders who recently sold equity shares must hand over the Letter of Offer and accompanying forms to their buyers

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: January 9, 2026
- Specific offer period dates to be communicated in the detailed Letter of Offer

## Impact Assessment

This takeover offer has high impact on P.M. Telelinnks Limited shareholders as it provides an exit opportunity and may result in change of control. Shareholders need to carefully evaluate the offer terms in the Letter of Offer to make informed decisions about tendering their shares. The acquisition window mechanism allows public shareholders to participate in the takeover process at the offer price.
