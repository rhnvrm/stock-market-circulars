## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing market participants, trading members, and custodians about the opening of the Offer to Buy under the Acquisition Window (Buyback) mechanism for KAJARIA CERAMICS LIMITED. The buyback is being conducted via the Tender Offer route using the Stock Exchange Mechanism, as per SEBI's revised guidelines. The offer window runs from Friday, July 03, 2026 to Thursday, July 09, 2026.

## Key Points

- Kajaria Ceramics Limited proposes to buy back up to 21,50,000 (21.5 lakh) fully paid-up equity shares of face value Re. 1/- each.
- This represents 1.35% of the total paid-up equity share capital of the company as on April 30, 2026.
- Buyback price: Rs. 1,380/- per equity share, payable in cash.
- Maximum aggregate buyback amount: Rs. 296.70 Crores, excluding transaction costs.
- Eligible shareholders are Equity Shareholders as on the Record Date, Monday, June 29, 2026, participating on a proportionate basis.
- The buyback is conducted through the Tender Offer route using the Stock Exchange Mechanism, per SEBI circulars CIR/CFD/POLICYCELL/1/2015 (April 13, 2015) and CFD/DCR2/CIR/P/2016/131 (Dec 09, 2016), and subsequent BSE notices.
- Letter of Offer is attached to the notice for shareholder reference.
- Buyback size represents 10.27% (standalone) and 9.87% (consolidated) of the aggregate paid-up capital and free reserves for FY ended March 31, 2026, within the statutory 25% limit under the Companies Act and SEBI Buyback Regulations.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this notice operationalizes existing SEBI and BSE guidelines governing tender-offer buybacks through the Acquisition Window mechanism.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and custodians must facilitate tendering of shares by eligible shareholders through the Acquisition Window during the specified offer period.
- Participants should refer to the Letter of Offer and BSE website (www.bseindia.com) for detailed offer mechanics, timelines, and settlement procedures.

## Important Dates

- Record Date: Monday, June 29, 2026
- Buyback Offer Period: Friday, July 03, 2026 to Thursday, July 09, 2026
- Notice Date: July 02, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The buyback offer provides eligible shareholders of Kajaria Ceramics an opportunity to tender shares at a premium price of Rs. 1,380 per share during the offer window. Trading members and custodians need to ensure operational readiness to process tenders through the Acquisition Window mechanism during July 3-9, 2026. The impact is contained to Kajaria Ceramics shareholders and does not have broader market-wide implications.
