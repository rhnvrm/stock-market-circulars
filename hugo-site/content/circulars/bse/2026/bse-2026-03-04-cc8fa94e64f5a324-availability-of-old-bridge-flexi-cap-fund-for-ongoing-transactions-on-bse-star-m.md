## Summary

BSE has informed members that OLD BRIDGE FLEXI CAP FUND has been reopened for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions, including SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities, on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective March 04, 2026. This follows an earlier notice (No. 20260212-36).

## Key Points

- OLD BRIDGE FLEXI CAP FUND is now available for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform from March 04, 2026.
- Both Regular and Direct plan variants are available across Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options.
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities are enabled along with standard subscription and redemption.
- Six scheme variants in total are now accessible on the platform.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational reinstatement of transaction availability for an existing mutual fund scheme.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members. Members and distributors using BSE StAR MF Platform may now process transactions in these schemes as per standard platform procedures.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** March 04, 2026 — Schemes reopened for ongoing transactions.
- **Reference Notice:** BSE Notice No. 20260212-36 (original closure/suspension notice).

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This notice restores transaction access for six variants of OLD BRIDGE FLEXI CAP FUND on the BSE StAR MF Platform.
