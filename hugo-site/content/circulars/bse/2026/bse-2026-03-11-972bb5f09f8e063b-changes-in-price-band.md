## Summary

BSE has revised the price bands for 11 listed scrips effective March 12, 2026, under Notice No. 20260311-38. The revisions are part of BSE's ongoing surveillance measures and affect equity segment securities with new bands ranging from 2% to 10%.

## Key Points

- Price band revisions effective from **March 12, 2026**
- 11 scrips affected across the equity segment
- Revised bands range from 2% (S&S Power Switchgear Ltd) to 10% (Identixweb Ltd, Svaraj Trading & Agencies Ltd, Yashhtej Industries (India) Ltd)
- Action taken by BSE Surveillance department
- Members may contact bse.surv@bseindia.com for clarifications

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the revised price bands and ensure compliance from March 12, 2026
- Orders placed in these scrips must fall within the newly revised price band limits
- Members requiring clarification should contact BSE Surveillance at bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** March 11, 2026
- **Effective Date:** March 12, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The price band revisions tighten or adjust trading limits for 11 scrips, which will restrict daily price movement to the specified percentage. S&S Power Switchgear Ltd faces the most restrictive band at 2%, indicating heightened surveillance concern. The 10% band assigned to Identixweb Ltd, Svaraj Trading & Agencies Ltd, and Yashhtej Industries (India) Ltd suggests relatively more flexibility. These changes primarily affect retail and intraday traders in these securities, potentially reducing volatility but also limiting price discovery in the short term.
