## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members that the HDFC NIFTY AUTO INDEX FUND will reopen for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions, including SIP, STP and SWP facilities, on the BSE StAR MF Platform with effect from July 13, 2026. This follows an earlier notice (No. 20260619-25 dated June 19, 2026) that had presumably placed the scheme under a temporary suspension of ongoing transactions.

## Key Points

- The HDFC NIFTY AUTO INDEX FUND will resume ongoing transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform from July 13, 2026.
- Facilities being restored include ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan).
- Two scheme variants are covered: HDFC NIFTY AUTO INDEX FUND - Regular Plan - Growth (ISIN: INF179KC1KP2) and HDFC NIFTY AUTO INDEX FUND - Direct Plan - Growth (ISIN: INF179KC1KQ0).
- The notice references and follows up on an earlier notice no. 20260619-25 dated June 19, 2026.
- Issued by BSE's Trading Operations department, signed by Arunkumar Ganesan (VP - Mutual Fund) and Mahesh Tare (AVP - Mutual Fund).

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an operational/administrative notice regarding platform availability of a specific mutual fund scheme, not a regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

BSE StAR MF Platform members and registered distributors should update their systems and client-facing platforms to reflect the reopening of ongoing transaction facilities for the HDFC NIFTY AUTO INDEX FUND (both Regular and Direct Growth plans) effective July 13, 2026, and ensure orders are processed accordingly from that date.

## Important Dates

- June 19, 2026: Original notice (No. 20260619-25) referenced in this circular.
- July 10, 2026: Date of this notice.
- July 13, 2026: Effective date for reopening of ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP and SWP facilities.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, low-impact operational notice relevant primarily to mutual fund distributors and investors transacting in the HDFC NIFTY AUTO INDEX FUND via the BSE StAR MF Platform. It does not affect listed equity securities, broader market trading, or require action from listed companies.
