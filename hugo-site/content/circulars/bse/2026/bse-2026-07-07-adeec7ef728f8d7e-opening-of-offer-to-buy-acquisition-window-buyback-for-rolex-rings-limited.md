## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing Trading Members and Custodians about the opening of the Offer to Buy through the Acquisition Window mechanism for the buyback of equity shares of Rolex Rings Limited. The buyback will be conducted via the Tender Offer route as per SEBI's mechanism for acquisition of shares through stock exchanges pursuant to takeovers, buybacks, and delisting.

## Key Points

- Rolex Rings Limited proposes to buy back up to 10,000,000 (1 crore) fully paid-up equity shares of face value Re. 1 each
- The buyback represents 3.67% of the total number of equity shares in the company's paid-up equity capital
- Buyback price is fixed at Rs. 180.00 per equity share, payable in cash
- Aggregate consideration for the buyback is up to Rs. 1,800.00 million, excluding transaction costs
- The buyback route is the Tender Offer mechanism via the stock exchange Acquisition Window
- BSE is the Designated Stock Exchange for this offer
- Buyback size represents 20.43% of the aggregate fully paid-up equity share capital and free reserves as per audited financials as on March 31, 2025, within the statutory 25% limit under the shareholder approval route
- Letter of Offer has been attached and further offer details are available on the BSE website

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; the offer follows SEBI circular no. CIR/CFD/POLICYCELL/1/2015 dated April 13, 2015, and no. CFD/DCR2/CIR/P/2016/131 dated December 9, 2016, along with BSE's revised guideline notices (20170210-16, 20190424-35, 20200528-32, 20201102-43, and 20210825-62) governing acquisition of shares via stock exchange under tender-offer buybacks.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members and Custodians must adhere to the Revised Guidelines of the Mechanism for acquisition of shares through the Stock Exchange for this Tender Offer, ensuring eligible shareholder participation is processed per the Acquisition Window framework. Market participants should refer to the BSE website for full offer details and the attached Letter of Offer.

## Important Dates

- Record Date: Friday, July 03, 2026
- Board meeting recommending the buyback: Thursday, April 23, 2026
- Offer Opening Date: Thursday, July 09, 2026
- Offer Closing Date: Wednesday, July 15, 2026
- Notice issued/published: July 07, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The buyback offer is a corporate action of moderate significance for Rolex Rings Limited shareholders, providing an exit opportunity for eligible shareholders at a defined price of Rs. 180 per share. Trading Members and Custodians need to be operationally ready to process tender offer bids during the acquisition window from July 9 to July 15, 2026. The impact on broader market trading activity is limited, confined mainly to Rolex Rings Limited's stock and its eligible shareholder base as of the record date.
