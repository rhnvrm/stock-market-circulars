## Summary

BSE Clearing Corporation (ICCL) has issued a revised settlement schedule for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) pertaining to REKVINA LABORATORIES LIMITED. This revises the earlier settlement calendar issued vide Notice No. 20260630-35 for the takeover window that was conducted from 01/07/2026 to 14/07/2026.

## Key Points

- The revised schedule applies to Settlement No. 2026080 under the Takeover settlement type.
- Bid period covered: 01/07/2026 to 14/07/2026.
- Revised Obligation Download date: 22/07/2026.
- Revised Settlement Date: 23/07/2026.
- Demat shares must be delivered only through the Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism, available in both depositories.
- Members/Custodians must use Market Type "(Takeover)" and Settlement No. 2026080 in the depository system.
- Members/Custodians are directed to refer to Notice No. 20200528-32 (dated May 28, 2020) for guidelines on the mechanism for acquisition of shares through the Stock Exchange.
- Client-wise Stamp Duty charges are available in the MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a revision of a previously announced settlement calendar (originally under Notice No. 20260630-35) for an existing Takeover offer window.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and Custodians must deliver demat shares only via Early Pay-in of Shares in either depository.
- Correct usage of Market Type "(Takeover)" and Settlement No. 2026080 is mandatory in depository system entries.
- Members should refer to the MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for client-wise stamp duty charges and ensure compliance with related stamp duty obligations.
- For clarifications, Members and Custodians may contact their relationship managers or the ICCL Help Desk (022-69158590/45720490/45720690, iccl.helpdesk@icclindia.com).

## Important Dates

- Bid Start Date: 01/07/2026
- Bid End Date: 14/07/2026
- Revised Obligation Download: 22/07/2026
- Revised Settlement Date: 23/07/2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to operational/settlement processes for Members and Custodians involved in the REKVINA LABORATORIES LIMITED takeover offer window. There is no change to offer pricing, terms, or eligibility; the notice solely defers/revises the settlement obligation download and settlement dates, requiring administrative attention from clearing members but posing minimal market-wide impact.
