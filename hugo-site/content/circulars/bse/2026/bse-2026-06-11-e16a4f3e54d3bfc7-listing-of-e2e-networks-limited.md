## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260611-23 dated 11 June 2026 announcing the listing of equity shares of E2E Networks Limited on the BSE equity segment. The notice is issued by the DCS-Listing department and is classified as a company-related circular.

## Key Points

- BSE Notice No. 20260611-23 dated 11 June 2026
- E2E Networks Limited equity shares are being listed on BSE
- Issued by the DCS-Listing department under the Equity segment
- Annexures are attached to the notice for detailed listing information

## Regulatory Changes

No broader regulatory changes are introduced. This is a standard listing admission notice specific to E2E Networks Limited.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants and brokers should note the admission of E2E Networks Limited securities for trading on BSE
- Trading members must update their systems to reflect the newly listed security
- Investors should refer to the attached annexures for ISIN, scrip code, and other listing details

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 11 June 2026
- Listing effective date: As specified in the attached annexures (refer to the PDF for exact trading commencement date)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a medium impact limited to traders and investors in E2E Networks Limited. The listing enables BSE-based trading in the company's equity shares, expanding liquidity and access for investors. No systemic or market-wide impact is anticipated.
