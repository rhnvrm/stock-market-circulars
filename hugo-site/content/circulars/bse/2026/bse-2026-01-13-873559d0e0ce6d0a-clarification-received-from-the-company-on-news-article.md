## Summary

Larsen & Toubro Limited issued a clarification in response to a news article published on Moneycontrol.com dated January 13, 2026, which reported that Kuwait was discussing the cancellation of oil project tenders worth $8.7 billion, causing L&T shares to fall 3%. The company clarified that the projects referred to in the media report were not part of L&T's existing order book. The company also stated it cannot comment on the status of tenders or commercial decisions of its clients.

## Key Points

- Media report suggested Kuwait was discussing cancellation of oil project tenders worth $8.7 billion
- L&T shares fell 3% following the news report
- Company clarified the referenced projects were NOT part of L&T's order book
- L&T stated it cannot comment on client tender status or commercial decisions
- Clarification issued to both BSE (Stock Code: 500510) and NSE (Stock Code: LT)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

This is a company clarification in response to stock exchange queries regarding media reports, as required under listing obligations and disclosure requirements.

## Important Dates

- **January 13, 2026**: Date of media article publication
- **January 13, 2026**: Date of stock exchange query
- **January 13, 2026**: Date of company clarification

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low to Medium - The clarification addresses market speculation that caused a 3% decline in L&T shares. By confirming the projects were not in the order book, the company mitigates concerns about potential order book cancellations.

**Operational Impact**: None - Since the projects were not part of L&T's order book, there is no direct operational or financial impact on the company.

**Investor Sentiment**: The clarification is positive for investor sentiment as it removes uncertainty about potential order cancellations and revenue impact.
