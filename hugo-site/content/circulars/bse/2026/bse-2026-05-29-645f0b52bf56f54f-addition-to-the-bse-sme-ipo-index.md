## Summary

BSE Index Services has announced the addition of AUTOFURNISH LIMITED (Exchange ticker: 544767) to the BSE SME IPO Index. The stock was listed on BSE effective May 29, 2026, and will be included in the index from the open of trading on Monday, June 01, 2026.

## Key Points

- AUTOFURNISH LIMITED (ticker: 544767) listed on BSE on May 29, 2026 per Notice No. 20260527-47
- The stock will be added to the BSE SME IPO Index effective June 01, 2026 (market open)
- The corporate action is included in the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE) for May 29, 2026
- Queries can be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index constituent update following a new SME IPO listing.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index-tracking funds and ETFs benchmarked to the BSE SME IPO Index should update their portfolios to include AUTOFURNISH LIMITED (544767) effective June 01, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **May 29, 2026**: AUTOFURNISH LIMITED listed on BSE; end-of-day corporate action file updated
- **June 01, 2026**: Effective date for addition to BSE SME IPO Index (market open)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market-wide impact. The addition affects only the BSE SME IPO Index, which tracks newly listed SME IPO stocks. Passive funds or portfolios benchmarked to this index will need to incorporate AUTOFURNISH LIMITED. The stock is a small-cap SME listing, so broader market impact is minimal.
