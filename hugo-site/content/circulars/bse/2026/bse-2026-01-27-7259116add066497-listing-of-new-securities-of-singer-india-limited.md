## Summary

Singer India Limited (Scrip Code: 505729) has listed 5,63,578 new equity shares of Rs. 2/- each on BSE, effective from January 28, 2026. These shares were issued at a premium of Rs. 77.59/- per share (total issue price Rs. 79.59/-) to non-promoters on a preferential basis. The allotment date was December 29, 2025, and the shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 5,63,578 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each listed on BSE
- Issue price: Rs. 79.59/- per share (Rs. 2/- face value + Rs. 77.59/- premium)
- Allotted to non-promoters on preferential basis
- Allotment date: December 29, 2025
- Trading commences: January 28, 2026
- Distinctive Numbers: 61655540 to 62219117
- ISIN: INE638A01035
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the listing of these new securities for trading purposes starting January 28, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 29, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: January 28, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry**: August 9, 2026

## Lock-in Details

All 5,63,578 shares (Distinctive Numbers 61655540 to 62219117) are subject to lock-in until August 9, 2026.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine preferential allotment with minimal market impact. The shares represent a small equity dilution and are locked-in for approximately 7 months from the trading commencement date, preventing immediate selling pressure. The preferential issue to non-promoters may be for strategic purposes or fundraising, but the quantum is relatively modest.
