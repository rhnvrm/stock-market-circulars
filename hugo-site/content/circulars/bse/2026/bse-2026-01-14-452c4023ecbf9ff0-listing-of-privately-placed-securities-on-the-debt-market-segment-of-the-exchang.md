## Summary

BSE has listed privately placed debt securities of Aditya Birla Capital Ltd on the debt market segment effective January 14, 2026. Two tranches of non-convertible debentures have been admitted to trading following allotment on January 12, 2026.

## Key Points

- Two debt securities of Aditya Birla Capital Ltd listed on BSE Debt Segment
- First security: 20,400 NCDs with 7.60% p.a. interest rate, maturing July 20, 2035 (Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000)
- Second security: 500 NCDs with 7.24% p.a. interest rate, maturing February 18, 2031 (Face Value: Rs. 10,00,000)
- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Tick size set at 1 paise
- Both securities carry AAA credit ratings from multiple agencies
- Date of allotment: January 12, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing announcement for privately placed debt instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Securities to be traded under specified ISIN numbers (INE674K07051 and INE860H07HE8)
- Trading members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915
- Market lot for both securities is 1 unit

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** January 12, 2026
- **Listing Date:** January 14, 2026
- **First Interest Payment (7.60% NCD):** July 23, 2026
- **First Interest Payment (7.24% NCD):** February 18, 2022 (likely February 18, 2027 for annual payment)
- **Redemption Date (7.60% NCD):** July 20, 2035
- **Redemption Date (7.24% NCD):** February 18, 2031
- **Interest Payment Frequency:** Annually for both securities

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This is a further listing of privately placed debt securities that will trade among institutional investors in the debt market segment. The listing does not affect equity markets or retail investors.

**Issuer Impact:** Provides Aditya Birla Capital Ltd with long-term debt funding (9-year and 14-year tenures) at competitive rates (7.24% and 7.60% p.a.) with AAA credit ratings from CRISIL, ICRA, and India Ratings.

**Investor Impact:** Limited to qualified institutional buyers who participated in the private placement. Securities offer fixed-income returns with high credit quality (AAA ratings) and put/call options as per disclosure documents.
