## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (No. 20260601-41) advising all trading members that TIL Limited has fixed a record date of 03 June 2026 to determine shareholders of partly paid equity shares who are eligible to pay the first and final call money. Trading members are instructed not to deal in the partly paid equity shares of TIL Limited from 03 June 2026 onwards in connection with this corporate action.

## Key Points

- TIL Limited (Scrip Code: 890237) has fixed a record date for its partly paid equity shares.
- Trading members must not deal in the partly paid equity shares of TIL Ltd from **03 June 2026**.
- The settlement reference is **DR-643/2026-2027**.
- The first and final call money is **Rs. 41.25 per partly paid share**.
- The purpose of the record date is to ascertain shareholders eligible to pay the call money.

## Regulatory Changes

No permanent regulatory changes. This is a one-time operational instruction tied to TIL Limited's corporate action of raising first and final call money on its partly paid equity shares.

## Compliance Requirements

- All trading members of BSE must refrain from dealing in the partly paid equity shares of TIL Limited (Scrip Code: 890237) from 03 June 2026.
- Members must take note of the no-dealings restriction and ensure their systems and clients are informed accordingly.

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |

## Impact Assessment

This notice has a limited and targeted impact, affecting only traders and investors holding partly paid equity shares of TIL Limited (Scrip Code: 890237). Trading in these partly paid shares will be halted from 03 June 2026 to allow the company to finalize the list of eligible shareholders for the first and final call money of Rs. 41.25 per share. Fully paid equity shares of TIL Limited are unaffected. The restriction is short-term and linked specifically to the record date settlement process.
