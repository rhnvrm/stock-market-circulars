## Summary

BSE has notified the schedule of activities for the buyback offer of P M TELELINNKS LIMITED (scrip code 513403) through the Acquisition Window mechanism. The offer will be conducted from Tuesday, January 13, 2026, to Tuesday, January 27, 2026, for up to 26,19,500 equity shares at Rs. 6.81 per share. Trading members and custodian members can participate through the iBBS platform and RTRMS system during specified session timings.

## Key Points

- **Security Details**: P M TELELINNKS LIMITED (Symbol: PMTELELIN, ISIN: INE092C01015, Scrip Code: 513403)
- **Offer Size**: Up to 26,19,500 equity shares
- **Offer Price**: Rs. 6.81 per share
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10.00
- **Market Lot**: 1 share
- **Bid Offering Period**: January 13-27, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Give-up/Take-up Period**: January 12-23, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Last Day Give-up**: January 27, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Last Day Take-up**: January 27, 2026 (9:15 AM to 4:00 PM)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This circular provides operational guidelines for a specific buyback transaction using existing OTB framework established under Exchange Notices 20150930-1 and 20170203-29.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members**: Must ensure investor UCC is registered/updated with correct PAN Number and Category in Exchange UCC database
- **Eligibility**: Trading members can only bid for investors holding shares in demat form
- **Platform Access**: Members must use iBBS (Internet: https://ibbs.bseindia.com/ or Leased Line: https://ibbsll.bseindia.com/) and RTRMS platforms
- **Investor Categories**: Bids accepted from FII, FI, MF, IC, OTH (Other QIBs), NOH (Other NIB), CO (Company), and IND (Individual)
- **File Access**: Extranet files for bid acceptance available only after Exchange receives confirmation from Merchant Banker/RTA post-issue closure
- **No Acceptance Scenario**: If no securities are accepted, no End of Issue files will be generated in Extranet

## Important Dates

- **January 12, 2026**: Give-up/Take-up period begins (9:15 AM)
- **January 13, 2026**: Bid offering period opens (9:15 AM)
- **January 23, 2026**: Give-up/Take-up period ends (3:30 PM)
- **January 27, 2026**: Last day for bid offering (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **January 27, 2026**: Last day for give-up (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **January 27, 2026**: Last day for take-up (9:15 AM - 4:00 PM extended)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal - This is a company-specific buyback affecting only P M TELELINNKS LIMITED shareholders. The offer size of 26,19,500 shares at Rs. 6.81 per share represents a modest transaction value.

**Operational Impact**: Low - Routine buyback process using established OTB infrastructure. Trading members familiar with the Acquisition Window mechanism from previous transactions. Standard operational procedures apply.

**Stakeholder Impact**: Relevant primarily to existing shareholders of PMTELELIN who wish to participate in the buyback offer and trading members facilitating their participation. Contact details provided for technical support on iBBS, RTRMS, and Extranet systems.
