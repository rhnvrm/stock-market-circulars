## Summary

BSE has notified Trading Members that the equity shares of SHREE RAM TWISTEX LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544716) will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (T Group) to the Rolling segment, where they will be classified under B Group, effective Tuesday, March 17, 2026. This is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20260227-42 dated February 27, 2026.

## Key Points

- Equity shares of SHREE RAM TWISTEX LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544716) to move from T Group (Trade for Trade) to B Group (Rolling segment)
- Effective date: Tuesday, March 17, 2026
- Continuation of prior notice: BSE Notice No. 20260227-42 dated February 27, 2026
- Contact person: Mr. Parag Jain, Tel. No. 022 2272 8685

## Regulatory Changes

The scrip will exit the Trade for Trade (T Group) settlement mechanism, where each trade must result in delivery and payment, and will move to the standard Rolling settlement (B Group) where netting of trades is permitted.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must update their systems and client-facing information to reflect the segment change effective March 17, 2026.
- Members should communicate the change to relevant clients holding or trading in SHREE RAM TWISTEX LIMITED shares.

## Important Dates

- **March 17, 2026**: Effective date for transfer from T Group to B Group (Rolling segment)
- **February 27, 2026**: Original notice date (BSE Notice No. 20260227-42)
- **March 2, 2026**: Date of this follow-up notice

## Impact Assessment

The move from T Group to B Group improves liquidity and trading flexibility for SHREE RAM TWISTEX LIMITED shares. In T Group, each trade requires mandatory delivery, which restricts intraday trading. Under B Group Rolling settlement, normal netting and intraday trading will be permissible. This is generally a positive development for retail and institutional participants trading this scrip.
