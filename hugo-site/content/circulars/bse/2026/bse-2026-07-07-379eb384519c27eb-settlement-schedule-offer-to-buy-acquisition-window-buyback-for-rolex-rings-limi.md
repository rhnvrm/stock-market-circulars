## Summary

BSE Clearing Corporation (ICCL) has issued the settlement calendar for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Buyback) for Rolex Rings Limited. The buyback window will be conducted from July 9, 2026 to July 15, 2026, with obligation download on July 21, 2026 and final settlement on July 22, 2026.

## Key Points

- Settlement Type: Buyback
- Settlement Number: 2026085
- Bid Start Date: 09/07/2026
- Bid End Date: 15/07/2026
- Obligation Download: 21/07/2026
- Settlement Date: 22/07/2026
- Demat shares must be delivered only through the Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism, available in both depositories (CDSL & NSDL)
- Market Type "(Buyback)" and Settlement No. 2026085 must be used by Members/Custodians in the depository system
- Members/Custodians should refer to Notice No. 20200528-32 dated May 28, 2020, regarding settlement per Revised Guidelines of Mechanism for acquisition of shares through Stock Exchange
- CDSL & NSDL early pay-in securities will be earmarked/blocked in the client's account under the requisite market type and settlement number
- Members and Custodians are NOT required to upload any EPN file
- Members should refer to the MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for client-wise Stamp Duty charges

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this notice operationalizes existing buyback acquisition window guidelines under Notice No. 20200528-32 dated May 28, 2020.

## Compliance Requirements

- Participants must ensure early pay-in of securities is reflected in BSE/ICCL systems before prescribed timelines for bid acceptance/confirmation on the BSE OTB platform
- Early pay-in of securities in the depository system must be completed sufficiently in advance of the closure of the BSE OTB bidding window and the custodian confirmation window
- Members/Custodians must use correct Market Type and Settlement Number in the depository system
- For queries, Members/Custodians may contact the ICCL Help Desk at 022-69158590/45720490/45720690 or iccl.helpdesk@icclindia.com

## Important Dates

- Bid Start Date: July 9, 2026
- Bid End Date: July 15, 2026
- Obligation Download: July 21, 2026
- Settlement Date: July 22, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a procedural/operational notice governing the settlement mechanics of the ongoing Rolex Rings Limited buyback via the stock exchange acquisition window. It primarily affects trading members, custodians, and depository participants involved in processing buyback tenders, ensuring smooth settlement of shares tendered under the buyback. It does not carry direct market-moving significance beyond confirming the buyback process timeline for shareholders participating in the offer.
