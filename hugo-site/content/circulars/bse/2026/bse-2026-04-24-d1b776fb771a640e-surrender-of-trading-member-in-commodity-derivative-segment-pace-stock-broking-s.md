## Summary

BSE has approved the surrender of trading membership of Pace Stock Broking Services Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 3013) in the Commodity derivative segment, effective April 23, 2026. This is an administrative notice to all trading members of the exchange.

## Key Points

- Pace Stock Broking Services Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 3013) has surrendered its trading membership in the Commodity derivative segment.
- The surrender has been approved by BSE effective April 23, 2026.
- The notice was issued by the Membership Department under the General segment.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine administrative action involving the exit of a trading member from the Commodity derivative segment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members requiring clarification on this notice may contact BSE's Membership Department at membership.ops@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date of Surrender:** April 23, 2026
- **Notice Date:** April 24, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to Pace Stock Broking Services Pvt. Ltd. and its clients in the Commodity derivative segment. Existing clients of the member would need to transfer their accounts or positions to another trading member. No broader market impact is anticipated.
