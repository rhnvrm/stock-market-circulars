## Summary

Mehul Telecom Limited (BSE ticker: 544751) was listed on BSE on April 24, 2026. Effective at the open of Monday, April 27, 2026, it will be added to the BSE SME IPO Index. This notice references the original listing notice (Notice No. 20260423-27) and confirms the corporate action has been included in the end-of-day corporate action file.

## Key Points

- Mehul Telecom Limited (ticker: 544751) listed on BSE on April 24, 2026
- Stock to be added to the BSE SME IPO Index effective April 27, 2026 (market open)
- Corporate action included in tonight's end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- Queries to be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index rebalancing action following a new SME IPO listing.

## Compliance Requirements

No direct compliance requirements for market participants. Index fund managers and ETF providers tracking the BSE SME IPO Index should update their portfolios to include Mehul Telecom Limited (544751) before market open on April 27, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **April 24, 2026**: Mehul Telecom Limited listed on BSE
- **April 27, 2026**: Effective date of addition to BSE SME IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. The addition is a standard index inclusion following a new SME IPO listing. It primarily affects passive investment products and index-tracking funds benchmarked against the BSE SME IPO Index. No broader market or regulatory implications.
