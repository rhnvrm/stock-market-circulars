## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that additional equity shares issued under ESOP/ESOS by four companies — ICICI Bank Limited, InterGlobe Aviation Limited, Solara Active Pharma Sciences Limited, and Tata Consumer Products Limited — have been listed and admitted for trading with effect from Thursday, July 09, 2026.

## Key Points

- ICICI Bank Limited (ISIN INE090A01021, Scrip Code 532174): 237,850 shares of face value ₹2 listed, distinctive numbers 7173615745 to 7173853594.
- InterGlobe Aviation Limited (ISIN INE646L01027, Scrip Code 539448): 9,099 shares of face value ₹10 listed, distinctive numbers 386657499 to 386666597.
- Solara Active Pharma Sciences Limited (ISIN INE624Z01016, Scrip Code 541540): 70,000 shares of face value ₹10 listed, distinctive numbers 48176013 to 48246012.
- Tata Consumer Products Limited (ISIN INE192A01025, Scrip Code 500800): 6,690 shares of face value ₹1 listed, distinctive numbers 989645269 to 989651958.
- None of the newly listed shares are under lock-in.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine listing approval under existing ESOP/ESOS scheme provisions.

## Compliance Requirements

No action required from investors or trading members beyond noting the updated share capital and listing status of the respective companies.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 08, 2026
- Effective listing/trading date for new shares: Thursday, July 09, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listed quantities are small relative to the total outstanding shares of each company, resulting in negligible dilution and no significant market impact. This is a standard administrative listing update reflecting employee stock option exercises.
