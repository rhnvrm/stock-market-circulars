## Summary

BSE has notified trading members of revised price bands for 29 equity scrips effective May 25, 2026. The majority of affected securities are being moved to a 5% price band, with some assigned 10% and others 20% bands, indicating varying levels of surveillance or volatility management.

## Key Points

- Price bands revised for 29 equity scrips effective May 25, 2026
- 21 scrips assigned a 5% price band (most restrictive)
- 4 scrips assigned a 10% price band
- 4 scrips assigned a 20% price band
- Applies to trading members on BSE Equity segment

## Regulatory Changes

Price bands (circuit filters) for the listed scrips have been revised from their existing levels. These changes limit the maximum permissible intraday price movement for the affected securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must ensure orders placed in the affected scrips from May 25, 2026 onward comply with the revised price band limits. Orders outside the new bands will be rejected by the exchange system.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** May 22, 2026
- **Effective Date:** May 25, 2026
