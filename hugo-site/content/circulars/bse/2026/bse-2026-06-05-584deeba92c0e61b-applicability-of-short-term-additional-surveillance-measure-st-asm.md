## Summary

BSE has identified securities satisfying the criteria for inclusion under Short Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) Stage I and Stage II. Enhanced margin requirements will apply from June 9, 2026, on both open positions as of June 8, 2026, and new positions created from June 9, 2026. Specific securities are listed in the attached Annexure I.

## Key Points

- Securities in ST-ASM Stage I will attract a minimum margin of 50% or the existing applicable margin, whichever is higher, capped at 100%.
- Securities in ST-ASM Stage II will attract a minimum margin of 100% or the existing applicable margin, whichever is higher, capped at 100%.
- Both stages are effective from June 9, 2026, covering open positions as of June 8, 2026, and all new positions from June 9, 2026.
- The ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by exchanges.
- Shortlisting under ASM is a market surveillance action and should not be construed as an adverse action against the concerned company or entity.
- On exit from the framework, the price band of the scrip will be reinstated to the pre-ASM level, unless the scrip remains under another surveillance measure.

## Regulatory Changes

This notice is issued further to earlier Exchange notices establishing and revising the ASM framework:
- Notice 20240920-71 dated September 20, 2024
- Notice 20181027-1 dated October 27, 2018
- Notice 20190719-33 dated July 19, 2019
- Notice 20201204-56 dated December 4, 2020
- Notice 20230925-49 dated September 25, 2023
- Notice 20240920-63 dated September 20, 2024

No new framework changes are introduced; this notice applies the existing ST-ASM framework to newly qualifying securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure applicable margin rates are collected for ST-ASM Stage I securities: 50% or existing margin, whichever is higher (max 100%).
- Trading members must ensure applicable margin rates are collected for ST-ASM Stage II securities: 100% or existing margin, whichever is higher.
- Members should refer to Annexure I (attached to the original circular) for the complete list of affected securities.
- For clarifications, members may contact BSE at bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **June 8, 2026**: Reference date for open positions subject to new margin requirements.
- **June 9, 2026**: Effective date for enhanced margin requirements on open positions (as of June 8) and all new positions.

## Impact Assessment

Market participants holding positions in the securities listed under ST-ASM Stage I will face a minimum 50% margin requirement, and those under Stage II will face a mandatory 100% margin, effective June 9, 2026. This significantly increases the cost of carrying or initiating positions in affected securities, which may lead to position unwinding and reduced liquidity in those stocks. The measure is a standard surveillance tool aimed at curbing speculative or abnormal price/volume activity and does not reflect any fundamental adverse finding about the listed companies.
