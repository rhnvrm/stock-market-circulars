## Summary

BSE has published the securities list for Demat Auction No. 824 (Settlement No. 824), dated 20 February 2026. This auction covers 47 scrips where delivery obligations were not met, requiring the exchange to conduct an auction to fulfil outstanding settlement obligations on behalf of defaulting sellers.

## Key Points

- Demat Auction No. 824 relates to Settlement No. 824 dated 20/02/2026
- Total of 47 scrips listed across diverse sectors including energy, banking, FMCG, technology, and infrastructure
- Quantities range from 1 share (e.g., ITC, ONGC) to 2,668 shares (Gokul Refoils)
- Auction is conducted by BSE to settle failed deliveries on behalf of short sellers
- Buyers in the original settlement will receive securities through the auction process

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice under BSE's settlement framework for handling failed deliveries.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members with short delivery obligations in the listed scrips are subject to auction proceedings
- Auction participants must comply with BSE's demat auction procedures and timelines
- Close-out rates apply if auction quantities remain unmatched

## Important Dates

- **Settlement Date:** 20 February 2026
- **Auction Reference:** Settlement No. 824
- **Publication Date:** 23 February 2026

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This is a routine demat auction covering 47 scrips with generally small quantities. The largest quantity is 2,668 shares of Gokul Refoils, followed by 1,001 shares of Vistar Amar and 1,000 shares of MOIL Ltd. Affected buyers will receive their securities through the auction mechanism. No broader market or regulatory implications.
