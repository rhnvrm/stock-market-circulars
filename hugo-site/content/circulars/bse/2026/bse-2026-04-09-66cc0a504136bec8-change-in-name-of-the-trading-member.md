## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260409-9 informing all trading members that the trading member CROSSEAS CAPITAL SERVICES PVT. LTD. (Member No. 3037) will officially change its name to **CROSSEAS CAPITAL MARKETS PRIVATE LIMITED** with effect from Friday, 10th April 2026.

## Key Points

- Trading member CROSSEAS CAPITAL SERVICES PVT. LTD. is changing its name to CROSSEAS CAPITAL MARKETS PRIVATE LIMITED.
- The member number (3037) remains unchanged.
- The name change is effective from 10th April 2026.
- Notice issued by the Membership Compliance Department of BSE.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced. This is a purely administrative update reflecting a name change of an existing exchange member. The member's registration and membership number remain the same.

## Compliance Requirements

- All trading members are advised to update their records to reflect the new name of Member No. 3037.
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE Membership Department at membership.ops@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 09 April 2026
- **Effective Date:** 10 April 2026 (Friday)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal market impact. It is a routine administrative name change affecting a single trading member. Counterparties and other trading members dealing with Member No. 3037 should update their internal records with the new legal entity name. No changes to trading access, settlement obligations, or market operations are expected.
