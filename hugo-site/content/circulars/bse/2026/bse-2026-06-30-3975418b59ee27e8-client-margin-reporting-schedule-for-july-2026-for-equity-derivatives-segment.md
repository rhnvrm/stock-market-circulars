## Summary

Indian Clearing Corporation Limited (ICCL) has published the client margin reporting schedule for July 2026 for the Equity Derivatives segment. The notice specifies, for each trade date in July 2026, the last date by which margin reporting must be completed and the corresponding penalty date if reporting is missed.

## Key Points

- The schedule covers all trading days from 01-07-2026 to 31-07-2026 in the Equity Derivatives segment.
- Each trade date has a corresponding last date for margin reporting (typically 5 trading days later) and a penalty date (the next trading day after the reporting deadline).
- Late reporting beyond the last date will attract penalties effective from the penalty date.
- The schedule is issued by ICCL on behalf of BSE.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is a routine monthly schedule update for client margin reporting obligations under existing SEBI and exchange margin reporting frameworks.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure client margin data is reported by the last date of margin reporting for each respective trade date.
- Failure to report by the deadline will result in penalties levied from the penalty date onwards.
- Members should plan their back-office and reporting workflows in line with the published schedule, particularly for trade dates where reporting deadlines fall near month-end or extend into August 2026.

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a routine operational impact on all trading members active in the Equity Derivatives segment. Members must adhere to the prescribed reporting schedule to avoid penalties. The schedule extends into early August 2026 for trades executed at the end of July, requiring members to maintain reporting discipline through the month-end. No market-wide or structural changes are implied.
