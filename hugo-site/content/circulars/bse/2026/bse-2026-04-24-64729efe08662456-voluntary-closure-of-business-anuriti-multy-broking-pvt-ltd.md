## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Anuriti Multy Broking Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 6497) will voluntarily cease its brokerage business in the Cash and Equity Derivative segments effective Monday, April 27, 2026.

## Key Points

- Anuriti Multy Broking Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 6497) is voluntarily closing its business on BSE.
- Cessation applies to both the Cash segment and the Equity Derivatives segment.
- Effective date of closure: Monday, April 27, 2026.
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE Membership Department at membership.ops@bseindia.com.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice is an administrative communication regarding the exit of a trading member from the exchange.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and clients of Anuriti Multy Broking Pvt. Ltd. should take note of the closure and make alternate arrangements before April 27, 2026.
- Any queries or clarifications regarding this notice should be directed to membership.ops@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **April 27, 2026**: Effective date of voluntary closure of business for Anuriti Multy Broking Pvt. Ltd. in Cash and Equity Derivative segments.

## Impact Assessment

The closure is limited to a single trading member (Member No. 6497) and is voluntary in nature. The direct market impact is low. However, existing clients of this broker will need to transfer their accounts and open positions to another registered member before the effective date. Counterparties and clearing members dealing with Anuriti Multy Broking should ensure all obligations are settled prior to April 27, 2026.
