## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in debt securities of 12 companies effective January 27, 2025. The suspension is due to redemption of securities or delisting applications filed by the companies. Trading members are advised not to deal in these specified debt securities.

## Key Points

- 12 debt securities from various issuers will be suspended from trading
- Suspension effective from January 27, 2025
- Reason: Redemption of securities or delisting applications
- Trading members must cease dealing in these securities
- Notice Number: DR-806/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notice for suspension of trading in debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the listed debt securities from January 27, 2025
- Trading members are required to take note of the suspension and update their systems accordingly
- All trading activity in these securities must cease on the effective date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** January 23, 2026
- **Effective Date:** January 27, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - affects debt market participants holding or trading these specific securities. The suspension is procedural due to redemption/delisting.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members must update their systems to block trading in these 12 debt securities. Investors holding these securities should be aware of the suspension and redemption/delisting process.
