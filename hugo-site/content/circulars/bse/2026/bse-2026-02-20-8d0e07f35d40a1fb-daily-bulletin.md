## Summary

BSE Daily Bulletin No. 223/2025-2026 dated 20 February 2026 publishes book-closure and record date notices for a range of listed companies and debt instruments. The bulletin covers commercial paper redemptions, non-convertible debenture interest payments, an interim dividend, an InvIT income distribution, and a rights issue.

## Key Points

- **360 ONE WAM LIMITED** has five commercial paper (CP) redemption record dates: 02 Mar, 04 Mar, 09 Mar, 20 Mar, and 24 Mar 2026 (scrip codes 730869, 730704, 730769, 730842, 730867).
- **Adani Airport Holdings Limited** (scrip 976065) has a record date of 23 Feb 2026 for part-redemption of privately-placed non-convertible bonds and interest payment.
- **Altius Telecom Infrastructure Trust** (scrip 543225) has a record date of 02 Mar 2026 for InvIT income distribution.
- **AROHAN FINANCIAL SERVICES LTD.** (scrips 959043 & 973901) has a record date of 16 Mar 2026 for interest payment on privately-placed NCDs.
- **Bajaj Financial Securities Limited** has six CP redemption record dates spanning 01 Apr 2026 to 29 Jun 2026 (scrips 731094, 731079, 731098, 731116, 731128, 731119).
- **Bhandari Hosiery Exports Ltd.** (scrip 512608) has a record date of 25 Feb 2026 for a rights issue of equity shares.
- **Bharat Heavy Electricals Ltd. (BHEL)** (scrip 730853) has a CP redemption record date of 24 Mar 2026.
- **Dhunseri Ventures Limited** (scrip 523736) has a record date of 27 Feb 2026 with dividend payment on 06 Mar 2026 for an interim dividend of Rs. 3.50 per share (35%).
- **Finnable Credit Private Limited** (scrip 977558) has multiple NCD interest payment record dates on 11 May 2026, 11 Aug 2026, and 11 Jun 2027 (content truncated in source).

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This bulletin is a routine publication of corporate action record dates pursuant to existing SEBI and BSE listing obligations.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Investors/shareholders** holding the relevant securities on the specified record dates will be eligible for the respective corporate actions (dividend, redemption, interest, or rights entitlement).
- **Registrars and Transfer Agents (RTAs)** must update beneficiary records as of each record date.
- **Brokers and depository participants** should communicate upcoming record dates to clients holding the listed scrips.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine informational bulletin with no systemic market impact. Investors in the listed equity scrips (notably Dhunseri Ventures for dividend and Bhandari Hosiery for rights issue) should note the near-term record dates of 25–27 February 2026. Debt instrument holders in 360 ONE WAM, Adani Airport Holdings, Arohan Financial, Bajaj Financial Securities, BHEL, and Finnable Credit should plan for upcoming redemption and interest inflows. The InvIT distribution from Altius Telecom Infrastructure Trust is relevant to InvIT unit holders.
