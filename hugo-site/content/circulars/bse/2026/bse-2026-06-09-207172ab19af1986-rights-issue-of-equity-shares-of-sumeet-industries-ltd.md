## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Sumeet Industries Ltd (BSE Code: 514211) has fixed 12 June 2026 as the record date for a rights issue. All transactions in the company's equity shares must be executed on an ex-rights basis effective from the same date.

## Key Points

- Rights issue ratio: 8 (eight) equity shares for every 25 (twenty-five) equity shares held
- Issue price: Rs 2 face value per share plus a premium of Rs 9.86 per share (total Rs 11.86 per share)
- Record date: 12 June 2026
- Ex-rights date: 12 June 2026
- Settlement reference: DR-650/2026-2027
- Segment: Dematerialised Securities – Rolling Settlement

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This notice implements an existing provision requiring BSE to inform trading members of ex-rights dates for listed companies conducting rights issues.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are required to ensure all transactions in Sumeet Industries Ltd equity shares (BSE Code: 514211) are carried out on an ex-rights basis with effect from 12 June 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Record Date:** 12 June 2026
- **Ex-Rights Date:** 12 June 2026
- **Settlement Reference:** DR-650/2026-2027

## Impact Assessment

Existing shareholders of Sumeet Industries Ltd as of the record date (12 June 2026) will be entitled to subscribe to 8 new equity shares for every 25 shares held at an issue price of Rs 11.86 per share. The simultaneous ex-rights and record date means the market price will adjust to reflect the rights entitlement from 12 June 2026. Impact is moderate and limited to existing shareholders and active traders in this stock.
