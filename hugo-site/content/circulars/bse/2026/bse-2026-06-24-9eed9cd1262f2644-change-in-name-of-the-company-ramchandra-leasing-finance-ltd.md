## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that Ramchandra Leasing & Finance Ltd (Scrip Code: 538540) has notified the Exchange of a change in its corporate name. The company will be renamed **RAAMA Finance Limited** effective July 01, 2026. The Scrip ID and abbreviated name on the BOLT Plus System will remain unchanged as **RAAMA**.

## Key Points

- Existing company name: Ramchandra Leasing & Finance Ltd
- New company name: RAAMA Finance Limited
- Scrip Code: 538540
- Scrip ID on BOLT Plus System: RAAMA (no change)
- Abbreviated name on BOLT Plus System: RAAMA (no change)
- Issued by: Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President – Listing Compliance & Operations

## Regulatory Changes

The company name change is a corporate action notified to BSE under the Listing Operations department. The Exchange is updating its records to reflect the new legal name. No changes are being made to the scrip code, ISIN, or trading symbol.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to note the name change in their records. No changes to trading procedures are required, as the Scrip ID and abbreviated name on BOLT Plus System remain the same (RAAMA).

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: June 24, 2026
- Effective Date: July 01, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is minimal. Since the Scrip ID and abbreviated name on BOLT Plus System remain unchanged as RAAMA, there is no disruption to trading operations. Trading members and investors only need to update their internal records to reflect the new legal name, RAAMA Finance Limited.
