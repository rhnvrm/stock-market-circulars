## Summary

BSE has notified trading members of a revised list of scrips available for transactions in the Securities Lending and Borrowing (SLB) segment, effective July 08, 2026. This update is issued in reference to SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/18/2013 dated May 30, 2013, concerning the review of the SLB Framework. The complete revised list is provided as an annexure to the notice.

## Key Points

- BSE Clearing Corporation Limited (BSECL) has revised the list of scrips eligible for the SLB segment.
- The revision is made under the framework established by SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/18/2013 dated May 30, 2013.
- The revised list takes effect from July 08, 2026.
- Trading members should refer to the attached Annexure_SLB for the complete list of eligible securities.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced; this is a periodic update to the list of scrips eligible for SLB transactions under the existing SEBI SLB Framework review mechanism (CIR/MRD/DP/18/2013).

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members dealing in the SLB segment must refer to the updated Annexure_SLB list and ensure that SLB transactions from July 08, 2026 onward are conducted only in scrips included in the revised list.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 07, 2026
- Effective Date of Revised SLB Scrip List: July 08, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, low-impact operational update affecting only market participants active in the Securities Lending and Borrowing segment. It does not affect general equity trading, listing status, or corporate actions for any specific stock. Members should verify scrip eligibility before initiating SLB transactions post-effective date to avoid failed or non-compliant trades.
