## Summary

BSE has listed and permitted trading of 4,45,000 new equity shares of Hittco Tools Ltd (Scrip Code: 531661, ISIN: INE863C01019) with effect from Monday, June 8, 2026. The shares were issued on a preferential basis to Promoter and Non-Promoter at an issue price of Rs. 13.92 per share (face value Rs. 10 + premium Rs. 3.92).

## Key Points

- 4,45,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 3.92/- per share
- Issued on a preferential basis to Promoter and Non-Promoter
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution Numbers: 6729148 to 7174147
- Date of Allotment: March 28, 2026
- Issue Price: Rs. 13.92/- per share
- ISIN: INE863C01019
- All 4,45,000 shares are subject to lock-in until December 30, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for newly allotted equity shares under a preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed that the new securities are listed and permitted to trade from June 8, 2026
- Lock-in restriction applies: all 4,45,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 6729148 to 7174147) are locked in until December 30, 2026 and cannot be traded during this period

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: March 28, 2026
- Effective Date for Trading: June 8, 2026 (Monday)
- Lock-in Expiry: December 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a routine admission of preferentially allotted shares for a small-cap scrip. The entire allotment is under lock-in through end of 2026, so no immediate increase in freely tradable float. Trading members should update their records for Scrip Code 531661 to reflect the expanded share capital.
