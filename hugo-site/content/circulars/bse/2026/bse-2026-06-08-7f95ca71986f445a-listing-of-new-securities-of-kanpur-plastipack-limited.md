## Summary

BSE has listed 4,66,500 new equity shares of Kanpur Plastipack Limited (Scrip Code: 507779, ISIN: INE694E01014) for trading effective Tuesday, June 9, 2026. The shares were issued at a premium on a preferential basis to Promoter and Non-Promoter shareholders pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 4,66,500 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 120/- (issue price Rs. 130/- per share)
- Issued to Promoter and Non-Promoter on preferential basis via warrant conversion
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 24026540 to 24493039
- Date of allotment: 23 March 2026
- Trading permitted from 9 June 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice for newly allotted securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to permit trading in the newly listed securities from June 9, 2026 onwards.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: 23 March 2026
- Trading commencement: 9 June 2026
- Lock-in periods:
  - 2,96,500 shares (Dist. Nos. 24026540–24323039): locked in until 30 December 2027
  - 1,00,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 24323040–24423039): locked in until 30 December 2026
  - 28,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 24423040–24451039): locked in until 31 December 2026
  - 42,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 24451040–24493039): locked in until 30 December 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a routine listing of preferentially allotted shares following warrant conversion. The majority of the new shares (2,96,500 out of 4,66,500) carry a longer lock-in until end of 2027, restricting near-term tradeable float. The remaining shares are locked until end of 2026, further limiting immediate supply. Overall effect on the scrip's liquidity is minimal in the short term.
