## Summary

BSE has notified that certain securities (listed in the attached Annexure I) have met the criteria for inclusion under the Short-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM), under both Stage I and Stage II categories. This follows the ASM framework established through a series of earlier Exchange notices dated September 20, 2024, October 27, 2018, July 19, 2019, December 4, 2020, September 25, 2023, and September 20, 2024.

## Key Points

- Securities meeting ST-ASM Stage I criteria will attract a margin of 50% or the existing margin, whichever is higher, capped at a maximum of 100%.
- Securities meeting ST-ASM Stage II criteria will attract a margin of 100% or the existing margin, whichever is higher, capped at a maximum of 100%.
- These margin requirements apply to all open positions as on July 03, 2026, and to new positions created from July 06, 2026 onward.
- The ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the Exchange.
- Inclusion under ASM is purely a market surveillance measure and should not be interpreted as an adverse action against the concerned company or entity.
- When a scrip exits the ASM framework, its price band will be reinstated to the level applicable before shortlisting, unless it remains subject to another surveillance framework, in which case that framework's price band will prevail.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced; this notice applies the existing ST-ASM framework (as revised via prior circulars) to a fresh set of securities identified as meeting Stage I and Stage II criteria.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must apply the specified higher margin rates (50% for Stage I, 100% for Stage II securities) on all open and new positions in the affected securities from the effective date. Members with queries can contact bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- Notice date: July 02, 2026
- Margin applicability: effective July 06, 2026, on open positions as on July 03, 2026 and new positions created from July 06, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The increased margin requirements will raise the cost of trading in the affected securities, likely reducing speculative activity and liquidity in these names. Since the specific securities are listed only in the annexure (not reproduced in this notice), traders and members should review Annexure I directly to identify affected stocks and adjust their positions and risk management accordingly.
