## Summary

BSE has notified all trading members about the name change of trading member SKY COMMODITIES INDIA PRIVATE LIMITED (Member No. 6745) to GOPOCKET INVEST TECH PRIVATE LIMITED, effective from Monday, January 5, 2026.

## Key Points

- Trading member number remains unchanged: 6745
- Old name: SKY COMMODITIES INDIA PRIVATE LIMITED
- New name: GOPOCKET INVEST TECH PRIVATE LIMITED
- Effective date: Monday, January 5, 2026
- Notice issued by Amit Kadam, Assistant Vice President - Membership

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative update regarding corporate name change of a trading member.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should:
- Update their records to reflect the new name of the trading member
- Contact membership.ops@bseindia.com for any clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** January 2, 2026
- **Effective Date:** January 5, 2026 (Monday)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal impact. This is a routine administrative change affecting only the corporate name of one trading member. The member number remains the same, and there is no impact on trading operations, market participants, or investors. Trading members need only update their internal records for reference purposes.
