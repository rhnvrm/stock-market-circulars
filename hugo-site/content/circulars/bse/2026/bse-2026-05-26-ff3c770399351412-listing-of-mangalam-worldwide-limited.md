## Summary

BSE has issued a listing notice (Notice No. 20260526-17) dated 26 May 2026 announcing the listing of securities of Mangalam Worldwide Limited on the BSE equity segment. The notice is issued by the Department of Corporate Services – Listing (DCS-Listing) and is accompanied by three annexures containing detailed listing information.

## Key Points

- BSE Notice No. 20260526-17 dated 26 May 2026 pertains to the listing of Mangalam Worldwide Limited.
- The securities are listed on the Equity segment of BSE.
- The notice is issued by the DCS-Listing department under the Company Related category.
- Three annexures (Annexure I, Annexure II, Annexure III) accompany the notice, providing detailed particulars of the listed securities.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced. This is a standard listing notice confirming the admission of Mangalam Worldwide Limited's securities to trading on BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants, brokers, and investors should note the listing of Mangalam Worldwide Limited's securities and update their records accordingly.
- Trading members should refer to the annexures for ISIN, scrip code, and other technical details required for trading.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 26 May 2026
- **Listing Date:** 26 May 2026 (as indicated by the notice date and same-day listing convention)

## Impact Assessment

The listing of Mangalam Worldwide Limited introduces a new equity security to BSE's trading platform. Impact is localised to investors and traders interested in this company. Market-wide impact is low; company-specific impact is significant for shareholders and IPO allottees who can now trade their holdings on the exchange.
