## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 860 new equity shares of Navin Fluorine International Ltd. (Scrip Code: 532504, ISIN: INE048G01026) are permitted for trading with effect from Wednesday, June 24, 2026. These shares arise from the conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up equity shares of Rs. 2/- each, issued on a rights basis.

## Key Points

- 860 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each are admitted to trading from June 24, 2026
- Shares issued pursuant to conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up on rights basis
- ISIN: INE048G01026 (same as existing equity shares)
- Scrip Code: 532504
- New shares are pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distinctive numbers span multiple tranches across various certificate ranges

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification for newly issued shares following conversion of partly paid rights entitlements to fully paid equity shares.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are permitted to trade in the 860 new equity shares from June 24, 2026. No additional compliance action is required beyond normal trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 23, 2026
- **Trading Effective Date:** Wednesday, June 24, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The 860 additional shares represent a very small addition to the existing equity share capital of Navin Fluorine International Ltd. The shares are pari-passu with existing shares and carry the same ISIN, so no separate tracking or trading adjustments are needed by market participants.
