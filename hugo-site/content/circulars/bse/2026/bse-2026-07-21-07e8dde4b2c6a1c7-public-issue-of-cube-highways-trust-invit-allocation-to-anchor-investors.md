## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that, under the Anchor Investors (AIs) portion of the Public Issue of CUBE HIGHWAYS TRUST InvIT, 11,10,19,565 units were subscribed on July 21, 2026 at a price of Rs. 152 per unit. A copy of the company's announcement dated July 21, 2026 was enclosed with the notice.

## Key Points

- Anchor Investor portion of CUBE HIGHWAYS TRUST InvIT public issue was subscribed for 11,10,19,565 units.
- Allocation price to Anchor Investors was Rs. 152 per unit.
- Subscription occurred on July 21, 2026, one day ahead of the public issue opening for Anchor Investors as per standard practice.
- Notice issued by BSE Listing Business Relationship department, signed by Mangesh Tayde, Vice President.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an informational notice regarding anchor investor allocation in an ongoing public issue.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action required from trading members; the notice is for informational purposes to keep members apprised of anchor allocation details ahead of the public issue.

## Important Dates

- July 21, 2026: Anchor Investor allocation date and units subscribed.

## Impact Assessment

The successful anchor allocation signals investor demand ahead of the CUBE HIGHWAYS TRUST InvIT public issue and may serve as a sentiment indicator for the broader issue subscription. Impact is limited to prospective investors in the InvIT and does not affect any currently listed securities.
