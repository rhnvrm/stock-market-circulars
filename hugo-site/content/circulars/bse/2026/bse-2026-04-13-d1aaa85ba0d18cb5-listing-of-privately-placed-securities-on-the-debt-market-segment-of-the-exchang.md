## Summary

BSE has listed five new Commercial Papers (CPs) issued on a private placement basis on its Debt Market Segment, effective April 13, 2026. These CPs are now admitted to trading under the BSE Debt Segment. All securities trade only in dematerialised form with a standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof, and a tick size of 1 paise.

## Key Points

- Five new Commercial Papers are admitted to trading on the BSE Debt Segment effective April 13, 2026.
- All CPs are traded in dematerialised form only, in denominations of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof.
- Tick size for all securities is 1 paise.
- Trading members may contact the Debt Department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915 for clarifications.
- Signed by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, DCS-Listing.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational listing notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all transactions in these securities are executed only in dematerialised form.
- Trading must be in standard denominations of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof.
