## Summary

BSE has announced the complete schedule for the Offer to Buy (Acquisition Window/Takeover) for OSCAR GLOBAL LTD. The offer will be live from January 2, 2026 to January 15, 2026, allowing investors to tender up to 8,58,000 equity shares at Rs. 12.15 per share through the iBBS platform.

## Key Points

- **Security**: OSCAR GLOBAL LTD (Symbol: OSCARGLO, ISIN: INE473F01010, Scrip Code: 530713)
- **Offer Size**: Up to 8,58,000 equity shares
- **Offer Price**: Rs. 12.15 per share
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10.00
- **Market Lot**: 1 share
- **Trading Members can bid for investors having shares in demat form only**
- **UCC registration/update with correct PAN and category is mandatory**
- **Multiple access platforms available**: iBBS, RTRMS (both Internet and Leased Line URLs provided)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The circular follows existing operational frameworks established under Exchange Notices 20150930-1 (September 30, 2015) and 20170203-29 (February 3, 2017) for OTB participation.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members and Custodian Members** must ensure investor UCC is registered/updated with correct PAN number and category in the Exchange UCC database
- Only investors with shares in **demat form** are eligible to participate
- Members must use designated BSE platforms (iBBS/RTRMS) for bid submission
- **Session Timings** must be strictly adhered to:
  - Bid Offering Period: January 2-15, 2026 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
  - Give-up/Take-up: January 2-14, 2026 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
  - Give-up (last day): January 15, 2026 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
  - Take-up (last day): January 15, 2026 (9:15 AM - 4:00 PM)

## Important Dates

- **Opening Date**: Friday, January 2, 2026
- **Bid Offering Period**: January 2-15, 2026 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Give-up/Take-up Period**: January 2-14, 2026 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Last Day - Give-up**: January 15, 2026 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Last Day - Take-up**: January 15, 2026 (9:15 AM - 4:00 PM extended)
- **Closing Date**: Thursday, January 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited, as the offer targets a specific stock (OSCAR GLOBAL LTD) with a defined acquisition size of 8.58 lakh shares. This is a structured takeover process affecting only shareholders of OSCARGLO.

**Operational Impact**: Moderate for Trading Members and Custodian Members who have clients holding OSCARGLO shares. They need to ensure:
- Platform access (iBBS/RTRMS) is functional
- Client UCC records are updated
- Clients are informed about the takeover opportunity
- Technical support contacts are available for login/password issues

**Investor Categories Eligible**: FII, Financial Institutions, Mutual Funds, Insurance Companies, Other QIBs, Other NIB, Companies, and Individuals can participate through their respective trading members.

**Technical Note**: If no securities are accepted, no End of Issue files will be generated in Extranet. Acceptance confirmation files will only be available after issue closure once confirmation is received from Merchant Banker/RTA.
