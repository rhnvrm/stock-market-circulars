## Summary

BSE has approved the listing and trading of 4,74,65,031 equity shares of Innovassynth Technologies (India) Limited (formerly known as Innovassynth Investments Limited, Scrip Code: 533315). These shares were issued pursuant to a scheme of merger by absorption under sections 230-232 of the Companies Act, 2013. Trading will commence on January 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- 4,74,65,031 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each to be listed
- Shares issued under merger scheme: Innovassynth Technologies (India) Limited merged with Innovassynth Investments Limited
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE690J01011
- Distinctive Numbers: 27984286 to 75449316
- Allotment Date: December 23, 2025
- Trading Start Date: January 30, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification following completion of a merger scheme approved under the Companies Act, 2013.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants should note the addition of 4.75 crore shares to the tradable float
- The company has completed merger compliance under sections 230-232 of the Companies Act, 2013
- All listing requirements have been fulfilled by the company

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 23, 2025
- **Notice Date**: January 29, 2026
- **Trading Commencement**: January 30, 2026 (Friday)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: The listing of 4.75 crore additional shares represents a significant increase in the tradable share base of Innovassynth Technologies. This may impact liquidity and shareholding patterns. The shares rank equally with existing shares, ensuring no differential rights.

**Corporate Impact**: Reflects completion of the merger between Innovassynth Technologies (India) Limited and Innovassynth Investments Limited, consolidating operations under a single listed entity. The company has transitioned from its previous name (Innovassynth Investments Limited) to its current name.

**Investor Impact**: Existing and potential investors should be aware of the expanded share capital and adjust their analysis of market capitalization and per-share metrics accordingly.
