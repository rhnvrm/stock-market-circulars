## Summary

ICCL Ltd. has issued Notice No. 20260421-36 dated 21 April 2026 informing trading members of corporate actions in the Securities Lending and Borrowing (SLB) segment. The specific details of the corporate actions are provided in an attached annexure.

## Key Points

- Notice issued by ICCL Ltd. (Indian Clearing Corporation Limited) on 21 April 2026
- Pertains to the SLB (Securities Lending and Borrowing) segment
- Corporate action details are contained in the attached annexure
- Trading members are required to review the annexure for affected securities

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is an operational notice communicating pending corporate actions that affect positions or obligations in the SLB segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must review the attached annexure for the list of securities undergoing corporate actions in the SLB segment
- Members should assess the impact on their existing SLB positions and take appropriate action as per ICCL guidelines

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 21 April 2026
- Specific ex-dates or record dates for the corporate actions are detailed in the annexure (not reproduced in the notice body)

## Impact Assessment

Trading members with open positions in the affected SLB securities may face adjustments to their lending/borrowing obligations due to corporate actions such as dividends, splits, or rights issues. The actual market impact depends on the specific securities and corporate actions listed in the annexure.
