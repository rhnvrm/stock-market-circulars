## Summary

BSE has announced the reconstitution of multiple sectoral indices effective March 23, 2026. The affected indices are BSE Commodities, BSE Consumer Discretionary, BSE Energy, and BSE Financial Services. The reconstitution involves additions of newly listed and eligible companies and removal of stocks that no longer meet index criteria.

## Key Points

- Effective date for all index changes: **March 23, 2026**
- BSE Commodities sees 6 additions and 4 deletions
- BSE Consumer Discretionary sees 17 additions and 3 deletions
- BSE Energy has no changes (no additions or deletions)
- BSE Financial Services sees multiple additions including Piramal Finance Ltd., Pine Labs Ltd., and National Securities Depository Ltd., with 1 deletion
- Many additions are recently listed companies (scrip codes in 544xxx range)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework introduced. This is a periodic index reconstitution exercise carried out by BSE as part of its standard index maintenance methodology.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Index fund managers and ETF providers** tracking affected BSE indices must rebalance their portfolios to reflect the new constituents effective March 23, 2026.
- **Market participants** holding positions in affected securities should be aware of potential price impact due to index-driven buying/selling around the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Announcement Date:** March 13, 2026
- **Effective Date:** March 23, 2026
