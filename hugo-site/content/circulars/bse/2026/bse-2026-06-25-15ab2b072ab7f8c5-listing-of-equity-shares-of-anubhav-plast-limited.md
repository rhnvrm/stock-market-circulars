## Summary

BSE has issued a notice to trading members informing them that the equity shares of Anubhav Plast Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company is being listed on the SME segment. The exact date of listing and further details of the securities will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Anubhav Plast Limited equity shares are approved for listing on the BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544800, Symbol: ANUBHAV, ISIN: INE1O7201010
- Actual listing date and security details to be announced via a subsequent notice
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing admission notice.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the scrip details (Code: 544800, Symbol: ANUBHAV, ISIN: INE1O7201010) and await the follow-up notice confirming the listing date before initiating trades.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: June 25, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced via a separate notice

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact at this stage as the notice only confirms approval for listing; the actual commencement of trading is subject to a forthcoming separate notice. Relevant primarily to investors and trading members tracking the Anubhav Plast Limited IPO on the SME platform.
