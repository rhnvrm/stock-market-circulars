## Summary

BSE has issued a revised bonus issue notice for Trent Ltd [Lakme Ltd] (Scrip Code 500251 / 100251), continuing from Exchange Notice No. 20260529-45 dated May 29, 2026. The company has submitted a revised annexure confirming that 17,77,24,420 equity shares will be allotted on June 5, 2026. The record date remains June 4, 2026.

## Key Points

- This notice is in continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20260529-45 dated May 29, 2026
- Trent Ltd [Lakme Ltd] has submitted a revised annexure with updated allotment details
- 17,77,24,420 equity shares will be allotted as bonus shares on June 5, 2026
- Record date remains unchanged at June 4, 2026
- All other terms of the original notice 20260529-45 remain unchanged
- Applicable to both Scrip Code 500251 (Trent Ltd) and 100251 (Lakme Ltd)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a factual update to a previously issued corporate action notice, providing revised allotment figures submitted by the company.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are required to take note of the revised allotment details
- No other action is required beyond acknowledgment of the revised figures
- The revised company letter dated June 3, 2026 is attached as a reference

## Important Dates

- **Record Date:** June 4, 2026
- **Allotment Date:** June 5, 2026
- **Original Notice Date:** May 29, 2026 (Notice No. 20260529-45)
- **Revised Notice Date:** June 3, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The revision clarifies the exact number of bonus shares (17,77,24,420 equity shares) to be allotted for the Trent Ltd / Lakme Ltd bonus issue. Trading members and shareholders should update their records accordingly. The record date of June 4, 2026 is unchanged, so no changes to ex-date or trading eligibility are anticipated. The allotment will occur on June 5, 2026, one day after the record date.
