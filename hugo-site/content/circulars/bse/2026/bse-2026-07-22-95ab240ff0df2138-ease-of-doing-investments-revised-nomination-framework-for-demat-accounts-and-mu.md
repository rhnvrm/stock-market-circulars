## Summary

BSE has issued a follow-up to its earlier Notice No. 20260716-27 dated July 16, 2026, on the revised nomination framework for demat accounts and mutual fund folios. This update specifies the validation requirements and prescribed forms (Annexure A for opt-in, Annexure B for opt-out) that members must use when facilitating investor nomination choices through online or physical modes, in line with the underlying SEBI circular.

## Key Points

- Nomination facility (opt-in or opt-out) must be offered to investors via online or physical mode.
- For online opt-in: nomination details per Annexure A must be displayed to the investor before submission.
- For physical opt-in: a duly completed and signed Annexure A must be obtained from the investor.
- For online opt-out: nomination declaration details per Annexure B must be displayed to the investor before submission.
- For physical opt-out: a duly completed and signed Annexure B must be obtained from the investor.
- Annexure A (Nomination Opt-in) and Annexure B (Nomination Opt-out) forms are attached to the notice.
- Members using third-party vendor solutions or in-house trading applications must implement all necessary system, process, operational, and compliance arrangements well ahead of the implementation date.

## Regulatory Changes

This notice operationalizes the SEBI-mandated revised nomination framework referenced in BSE Notice No. 20260716-27 (July 16, 2026), prescribing standardized forms and validation workflows for nomination opt-in/opt-out across demat accounts and mutual fund folios.

## Compliance Requirements

- Regulated entities must capture and maintain records of investors' nomination opt-in/opt-out choices.
- Records must be preserved and made available to the RTA, AMC, Regulator, or other authorized authority upon request or at prescribed intervals.
- Members must ensure their trading applications and vendor systems support the prescribed online display and physical form requirements.
- Queries on BSE StAR Mutual Fund Operations can be directed to the Mutual Fund Helpdesk (022-45720450/650, 022-69158550, available 8:00 a.m.–7:00 p.m. on business days) or via the CRS Portal.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 22, 2026
- References prior Notice No. 20260716-27 dated July 16, 2026
- No specific implementation deadline stated; members directed to complete system readiness "well in advance" of the prescribed implementation date

## Impact Assessment

This is an operational/compliance circular affecting trading members' systems and processes for investor nomination management. It does not directly affect security prices or trading activity but requires member firms and their technology vendors to update onboarding and recordkeeping workflows to remain compliant with SEBI's nomination framework.
