## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that three Commercial Paper (CP) instruments issued by Aditya Birla Lifestyle Brands Limited and PCBL Chemical Limited have fixed record dates for redemption. Trading members are advised not to deal in these Commercial Papers from the specified record/ex dates onward.

## Key Points

- Aditya Birla Lifestyle Brands Limited CP (Scrip Code 731491, ISIN INE14LE14079, Scrip ID ABLB-16-7-26-CP) — Record Date and Ex Date: 15/07/2026, Settlement DR 672/2026-2027
- PCBL Chemical Limited CP (Scrip Code 731542, ISIN INE602A14497, Scrip ID PCL-22-7-26-CP) — Record Date and Ex Date: 21/07/2026, Settlement DR 676/2026-2027
- Aditya Birla Lifestyle Brands Limited CP (Scrip Code 731548, ISIN INE14LE14087, Scrip ID ABLBL-23-07-26-CP) — Record Date and Ex Date: 22/07/2026, Settlement DR 677/2026-2027

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a routine operational notice under Listing Operations regarding redemption-related trading suspension of specific debt instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must not deal in the listed Commercial Paper ISINs from their respective record/ex dates, as these instruments are due for redemption.

## Important Dates

- 15 Jul 2026: Record/Ex Date for Aditya Birla Lifestyle Brands Limited CP (INE14LE14079)
- 21 Jul 2026: Record/Ex Date for PCBL Chemical Limited CP (INE602A14497)
- 22 Jul 2026: Record/Ex Date for Aditya Birla Lifestyle Brands Limited CP (INE14LE14087)

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to institutional and debt-market participants holding these specific Commercial Paper instruments approaching redemption. There is no effect on equity trading or broader market activity.
