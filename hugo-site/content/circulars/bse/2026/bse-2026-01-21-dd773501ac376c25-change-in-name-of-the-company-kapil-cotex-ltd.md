## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for the company previously known as KAPIL COTEX LTD. The company name has been officially changed to SKYBIOTECH HEALTHCARE LIMITED effective November 7, 2025. The certificate was issued by the Central Processing Centre, ROC.

## Key Points

- Company CIN: L21001MH1983PLC031114
- Old Name: KAPIL COTEX LTD
- New Name: SKYBIOTECH HEALTHCARE LIMITED
- Effective Date: November 7, 2025
- Certificate issued by: Sweety Kumar, Central Processing Centre
- Company Type: Company limited by shares
- Originally incorporated as: KAPIL COTEX LTD

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes applicable. This is an administrative name change under the Companies Act, 2014.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display the old name (KAPIL COTEX LTD) along with the new name (SKYBIOTECH HEALTHCARE LIMITED) for a period of two years at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- All stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company and its directors on the MCA website: www.mca.gov.in/MCA21
- The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date of Name Change**: November 7, 2025
- **Dual Name Display Period**: Two years from November 7, 2025 (until November 7, 2027)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine administrative change that does not affect the company's operations, financial position, or shareholder rights.

**Operational Impact**: The company will need to update all official documentation, letterheads, signage, and communications to reflect the new name while maintaining dual display for the mandated two-year period.

**Shareholder Impact**: No impact on shareholder rights or liabilities. The CIN remains unchanged, ensuring continuity of corporate identity.
