## Summary

BSE Limited has issued Notice No. 20260717-6 inviting offers from trading members of the Exchange for the purchase of securities in Auction (ARR 673), in respect of Rolling Settlement No. DR-673/2026-27. This is a routine settlement/RMS notice covering securities that were short-delivered and now need to be sourced through the auction mechanism.

## Key Points

- Auction reference: ARR 673, pertaining to Rolling Settlement No. DR-673/2026-27
- Offers accepted on trading terminals on Friday, July 17, 2026
- Auction offer entry window: 02:00 p.m. to 02:45 p.m.
- Auction matching window: 02:45 p.m. to 03:00 p.m.
- Delivery statement and report of accepted auction offers: 03:00 p.m. to 03:30 p.m.
- Money statement and final reports: 03:35 p.m. to 05:30 p.m.
- Under the Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment, settlement will be done through the Clearing Member of a Trading Member
- The earlier restriction preventing trading members from offering securities in auction in the settlement where they short-delivered such securities has been removed
- No corrections permitted for punching errors in security code or rate; Exchange reserves the right to accept/reject any offer without assigning reasons
- Trading members advised to refer to the cut-off rate on the Auction Offer Entry Screen and to check for related Corporate Action notices (Ex-Rights/Ex-Bonus/Ex-Split, etc.)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this notice reflects the already-implemented Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment, under which settlement is routed through the Clearing Member of a Trading Member, and confirms removal of the earlier restriction on offering short-delivered securities in the same settlement's auction.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members and Custodians must check their auction pay-in obligations for securities and execute securities pay-in in depositories within the depository cut-off timings
- Trading members must exercise care while punching security codes and rates, as no corrections are permitted post-entry
- Members should cross-check relevant Corporate Action notices before placing auction offers

## Important Dates

- July 17, 2026: Auction offer entry (2:00–2:45 p.m.), matching (2:45–3:00 p.m.), delivery statement (3:00–3:30 p.m.), money statement and final reports (3:35–5:30 p.m.)
- July 20, 2026, 8:00 a.m.: Auction pay-in

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard, recurring settlement/RMS operational notice affecting trading members and custodians involved in the auction process for short-delivered securities under Rolling Settlement DR-673/2026-27. It does not name specific listed securities or companies and carries low market-wide impact, being relevant primarily to back-office and clearing operations of trading members and custodians participating in the auction.
