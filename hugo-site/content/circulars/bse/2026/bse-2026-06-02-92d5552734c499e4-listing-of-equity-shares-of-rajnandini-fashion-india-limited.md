## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the equity shares of Rajnandini Fashion India Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange. The company is being listed under the SME segment. The exact listing date and further security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Rajnandini Fashion India Limited equity shares to be listed on BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544771
- Trading Symbol: RFIL
- ISIN: INE1GM501015
- Listing date and full security details to be notified via a separate circular

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing admission notice for an SME IPO on BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to take note of the upcoming listing. No immediate compliance action is required until the separate notice confirming the listing date is issued.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: June 2, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced via a separate notice

## Impact Assessment

Limited market-wide impact. The listing pertains to a single SME-segment company. Trading members should await the follow-up notice for the confirmed listing date and full trading details before executing any transactions in RFIL (Scrip Code: 544771, ISIN: INE1GM501015).
