## Summary

BSE's Surveillance department (DOSS) has revised the price bands for 17 equity scrips effective May 27, 2026. The revised bands range from 2% to 10%, reflecting changes from their existing levels as part of routine surveillance measures.

## Key Points

- Price band revisions apply to 17 scrips across diverse sectors effective May 27, 2026
- Bands revised to either 2%, 5%, or 10% from their current levels
- Six scrips revised to 2% band (tighter restriction): Asia Capital Ltd, Gaudium IVF and Women Health Ltd, IB Infotech Enterprises Ltd, Mukat Pipes Ltd, Sarthak Global Ltd, Subhash Silk Mills Ltd
- Seven scrips revised to 10% band: Cargotrans Maritime Ltd, Evans Electric Ltd, GE Vernova T&D India Ltd, Hitech Corporation Ltd, Modison Ltd, Radico Khaitan Ltd, United Foodbrands Ltd
- Four scrips revised to 5% band: Chandra Bhagat Pharma Ltd, TPI India Ltd, Yaan Enterprises Ltd, York Exports Ltd
- Queries to be directed to bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

Price bands have been revised from existing levels for the listed scrips. Price bands limit the maximum permissible movement (up or down) in a stock's price during a trading session. Tighter bands (2%) indicate heightened surveillance concern, while wider bands (10%) indicate relaxation toward normal trading conditions.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must ensure all orders for the affected scrips comply with the revised price band limits effective May 27, 2026. Orders placed outside the new band will be rejected by the exchange trading system automatically.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** May 26, 2026
- **Effective Date:** May 27, 2026
