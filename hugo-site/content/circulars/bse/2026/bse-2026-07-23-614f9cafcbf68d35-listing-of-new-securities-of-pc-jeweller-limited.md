## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 7,90,45,290 equity shares of PC Jeweller Limited (Scrip Code: 534809), issued at a premium on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants, are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Friday, July 24, 2026.

## Key Points

- 7,90,45,290 equity shares of Re. 1/- each face value issued at a premium of Rs. 4.62/- each.
- Shares allotted to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants.
- Distinctive numbers range from 8569557886 to 8648603175.
- Issue price was Rs. 5.62 per share.
- ISIN: INE785M01021.
- New shares rank pari-passu with the existing equity shares of the company.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine listing notification under BSE's Listing Operations department.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific action required from trading members other than updating systems to reflect the newly listed shares from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: March 31, 2026
- Effective Date of Listing: July 24, 2026
- Lock-in Period Ends: January 29, 2027

## Impact Assessment

The listing increases the free-float-eligible share count of PC Jeweller Limited once the lock-in expires, though the newly issued shares remain locked in until January 29, 2027, limiting immediate trading impact. The preferential allotment via warrant conversion reflects capital raised from non-promoter investors, which may be of interest to shareholders tracking dilution and ownership changes.
