## Summary

BSE has announced the transfer of YAJUR FIBRES LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544676) equity shares from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment, with corresponding shift to M Group. This change follows the company's SME IPO listing and will be effective from January 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- YAJUR FIBRES LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544676) is a recent SME IPO listing
- Shares currently trading in Trade for Trade segment (MT Group)
- Transfer to Rolling segment with M Group classification
- Change follows Exchange's Notice No. 20260113-25 dated January 13, 2026
- Contact person: Mr. Anurag Jain, Tel: 022-2272 8822

## Regulatory Changes

The trading mechanism for YAJUR FIBRES LIMITED will shift from Trade for Trade settlement (which requires 100% upfront delivery) to Rolling settlement, allowing for more flexible trading conditions. The group classification changes from MT Group to M Group.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members should:
- Note the segment and group change for YAJUR FIBRES LIMITED
- Update their systems and trading platforms accordingly
- Contact Mr. Anurag Jain for clarifications or additional details
- Inform clients about the change in trading mechanism

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** January 14, 2026
- **Previous Notice:** January 13, 2026 (Notice No. 20260113-25)
- **Effective Date:** January 30, 2026 (Friday)

## Impact Assessment

This change will enhance trading liquidity and flexibility for YAJUR FIBRES LIMITED shares by moving from the restrictive Trade for Trade segment to the Rolling settlement mechanism. Investors will benefit from intraday trading opportunities and reduced settlement requirements. The transition from MT Group to M Group indicates the stock's graduation to regular trading status following its SME IPO listing period.
