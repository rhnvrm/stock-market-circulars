## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that trading in the Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of SK Finance Limited will be suspended from March 18, 2026, coinciding with the record date fixed for redemption and interest payment on the 9.25% debentures.

## Key Points

- Trading in SK Finance Limited's NCD series SKFL-9.25%-2-4-26-PVT (ISIN: INE124N07689, BSE Code: 975579) is suspended effective March 18, 2026.
- The suspension applies to both redemption of debentures and payment of interest.
- Notice reference: DR-842/2025-2026.
- Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the specified date.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice under BSE's listing compliance framework requiring suspension of trading on the record date for corporate actions on debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in SKFL-9.25%-2-4-26-PVT (ISIN: INE124N07689, Code: 975579) from March 18, 2026.
- Members are expected to take immediate note of the notice and ensure no transactions are executed in the said debentures on or after the no-dealings date.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** March 13, 2026
- **Record Date:** March 18, 2026
- **No Dealings From:** March 18, 2026
- **Purpose:** Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and traders of this specific NCD series of SK Finance Limited. The suspension is routine and time-bound, triggered by the upcoming redemption date of April 2, 2026 (as implied by the series name SKFL-9.25%-2-4-26). Investors holding these debentures will receive redemption proceeds and accrued interest as of the record date. No broader market impact is anticipated.
