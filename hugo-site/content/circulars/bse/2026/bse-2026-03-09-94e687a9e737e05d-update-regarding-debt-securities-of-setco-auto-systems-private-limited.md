## Summary

BSE has issued a continuation notice (Notice No. 20260309-25) regarding the suspension of debt securities of Setco Auto Systems Private Limited. In continuation of earlier Notice No. 20260130-21 dated January 30, 2026, trading members are informed that two scrip codes will remain suspended until further notice.

## Key Points

- Continuation of suspension originally notified on January 30, 2026 (Notice No. 20260130-21)
- Two debt securities of Setco Auto Systems Private Limited remain suspended
- Scrip Code 973453 (ISIN: INE0I0R07110) is suspended
- Scrip Code 975859 (ISIN: INE0I0R07102) is suspended
- Suspension is indefinite — "till further Notice"

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice extends/reaffirms the suspension status of the two debt securities originally communicated on January 30, 2026.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not facilitate trading in the suspended scrip codes (973453 and 975859)
- Members holding or dealing in these securities should note the continued suspension
- For queries, members may contact Ms. Pooja Trivedi at +91 22 2272 8844 or debt.listing@bseindia.com, or Hardik Bhuta (Assistant Vice President)

## Important Dates

- Original suspension notice: January 30, 2026 (Notice No. 20260130-21)
- Current update notice date: March 9, 2026
- End of suspension: Not specified — suspended "till further Notice"

## Impact Assessment

Trading in both debt securities of Setco Auto Systems Private Limited (a private limited company) remains halted on BSE's debt market segment. This impacts trading members and investors holding these instruments, as no secondary market transactions are permitted. The indefinite nature of the suspension suggests unresolved issues with the issuer. Market impact is limited given these are privately placed debt instruments of a single company.
