## Summary

BSE has issued a prohibition/suspension of trading in the securities of Pradhin Limited (Scrip Code: 530095) as an interim measure, effective March 12, 2026. The action was taken due to the company's failure to respond to a show cause notice and confirmed non-existence at its registered office address during a site visit conducted by the Exchange.

## Key Points

- Trading in securities of Pradhin Limited (Scrip Code: 530095) is prohibited/suspended effective March 12, 2026
- The company failed to provide any response to BSE's show cause notice
- A site visit by the Exchange confirmed the company does not exist at its registered office address
- The suspension is described as an interim measure
- All trading members and investors are notified to take note of this action

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework changes. This is an enforcement action under BSE's existing listing compliance authority to suspend trading in securities of non-compliant companies that are unresponsive and unverifiable at their registered addresses.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must immediately cease facilitating trades in Pradhin Limited (Scrip Code: 530095) from March 12, 2026
- Investors holding or intending to trade in this scrip must note the suspension and refrain from placing orders
- No further action is specified for the company in this notice, though the interim nature suggests further proceedings may follow

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** March 11, 2026
- **Suspension Effective Date:** March 12, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This suspension has a high impact on existing investors in Pradhin Limited, who will be unable to trade their holdings on BSE from March 12, 2026 onwards. The confirmation of non-existence at the registered office raises serious concerns about the company's operational status and corporate governance. Investors may face significant liquidity risk as a result of this suspension. The interim nature of the measure suggests the possibility of further regulatory action, including potential delisting proceedings.
