## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that further equity shares issued by eight companies under their Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) / Employee Stock Option Schemes (ESOS) have been listed and admitted for trading with effect from Wednesday, July 15, 2026.

## Key Points

- Abans Financial Services Limited (ISIN INE00ZE01026, Scrip Code 543712): 46,919 shares of face value ₹2, distinctive numbers 50792841 to 50839759
- CARE Ratings Limited (ISIN INE752H01013, Scrip Code 534804): 26,733 shares of face value ₹10, distinctive numbers 30289613 to 30316345
- Coforge Limited (ISIN INE591G01025, Scrip Code 532541): 19,265 shares of face value ₹2, distinctive numbers 442671547 to 442690811
- KFin Technologies Limited (ISIN INE138Y01010, Scrip Code 543720): 18,657 shares of face value ₹10, distinctive numbers 187825485 to 187844141
- Prime Focus Limited (ISIN INE367G01038, Scrip Code 532748): 500,000 shares of face value ₹1, distinctive numbers 775990548 to 776490547
- Suzlon Energy Limited (ISIN INE040H01021, Scrip Code 532667): 1,015,428 shares of face value ₹2, distinctive numbers 14050991552 to 14052006979
- Tejas Networks Limited (ISIN INE010J01012, Scrip Code 540595): 123,042 shares of face value ₹10, distinctive numbers 210578871 to 210701912
- Tracxn Technologies Limited (ISIN INE0HMF01019, Scrip Code 543638): 22,142 shares of face value ₹1, distinctive numbers 107963114 to 107985255

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a routine listing approval under existing ESOP/ESOS listing procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to take note of the listing and admission of these additional shares for trading purposes. No further action is required from investors or listed companies beyond standard post-allotment listing compliance.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 14, 2026
- Effective Listing Date: Wednesday, July 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative listing of employee stock option shares across eight companies. The impact on market capitalization and share price is minimal given the relatively small quantities involved (ranging from ~18,657 to ~1,015,428 shares) compared to each company's total outstanding shares. No trading restrictions or price adjustments are indicated.
