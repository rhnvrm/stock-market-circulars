## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (No. 20260429-7) informing trading members that Sovereign Gold Bond 2019 Series (Scrip Code: 800287, ISIN: IN0020180033) is scheduled to mature on its Redemption Date. Accordingly, trading in this security has been suspended with effect from April 30, 2026, under reference DR-621/2026-2027.

## Key Points

- Trading in Sovereign Gold Bond 2019 Series is suspended from April 30, 2026
- Scrip Code: 800287; ISIN: IN0020180033
- Suspension is due to the security reaching its Redemption Date
- All trading members are advised not to deal in this security from the effective date
- Reference: DR-621/2026-2027

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice pursuant to the maturity of a listed government security on the Debt Segment of BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must cease all dealings in Sovereign Gold Bond 2019 Series (Scrip Code: 800287 / ISIN: IN0020180033) with effect from April 30, 2026
- Members are required to take note of this suspension and ensure no trades are executed in this scrip on or after the effective date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** April 29, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Effective From:** April 30, 2026 (Redemption Date)

## Impact Assessment

This suspension affects only holders and traders of Sovereign Gold Bond 2019 Series on the BSE Debt Segment. The impact is limited and expected, as the bond has reached maturity. Investors holding this bond will receive redemption proceeds as per the scheduled terms. No broader market impact is anticipated.
