## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260625-48 informs Trading Members that Patel Integrated Logistics Ltd (BSE Code: 526381) has fixed June 30, 2026 as the Record Date to determine the entitlement and names of equity shareholders eligible to participate in the company's buyback offer via the tender offer route in the Rolling Settlement (Dematerialised) segment.

## Key Points

- Company: Patel Integrated Logistics Ltd (BSE Code: 526381)
- Record Date: June 30, 2026
- Ex-Entitlement Date: June 30, 2026
- Settlement Number: DR-661/2026-2027
- Segment: Equity – Dematerialised Securities, Rolling Settlement
- Purpose: Determine entitlement and names of equity shareholders eligible to participate in the buyback offer

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notification under existing buyback regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must take note of the record date and ex-entitlement date for the buyback offer.
- Eligible shareholders must hold shares on record as of June 30, 2026 to qualify for participation in the buyback.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 25, 2026
- **Record Date:** June 30, 2026
- **Ex-Entitlement Date:** June 30, 2026
- **Settlement No.:** DR-661/2026-2027

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to existing shareholders of Patel Integrated Logistics Ltd. Shareholders holding shares on the record date (June 30, 2026) will be eligible to tender shares in the buyback. Trading Members should update client records and ensure eligible shareholders are informed about the buyback participation window. No broader market or systemic impact is expected.
