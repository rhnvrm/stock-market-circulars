## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (No. 20260302-21) informing trading members that the equity shares of PNGS Reva Diamond Jewellery Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange. The exact listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Equity shares of PNGS Reva Diamond Jewellery Limited are approved for listing on BSE
- Scrip Code: 544718
- Trading Symbol: PNGSREVA
- ISIN: INE1RDG01013
- Exact listing date to be announced via a separate notice
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing admission notice under BSE's listing framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should note the upcoming listing of PNGSREVA and prepare accordingly
- Members should await the subsequent notice from BSE for the confirmed listing date and full security details before initiating trades

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: March 2, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced via a separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This notice signals the imminent listing of PNGS Reva Diamond Jewellery Limited on BSE. Market participants interested in the diamond jewellery sector should monitor BSE announcements for the confirmed listing date. The listing will make the scrip (PNGSREVA, code 544718) available for trading on the Exchange's equity segment.
