## Summary

Indian Clearing Corporation Limited (ICCL) has published the client margin reporting schedule for July 2026 for the Commodity Derivatives Segment. The notice specifies the last date for margin reporting and the corresponding penalty date for each trade date in July 2026.

## Key Points

- Schedule covers all trading days in July 2026 (01-Jul to 31-Jul 2026) for the Commodity Derivatives Segment.
- Margin reporting window is typically 5 trading days after the trade date.
- Penalty is levied one trading day after the last date of margin reporting if compliance is missed.
- The schedule extends into early August 2026 for trades executed at month-end.
- Issued by ICCL (Indian Clearing Corporation Limited) on behalf of BSE.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a routine monthly schedule notice under existing client margin reporting requirements for the Commodity Derivatives Segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must report client margins for each trade date by the specified last date of margin reporting.
- Failure to report by the deadline will attract penalties on the penalty date.
- Members trading in Commodity Derivatives are required to adhere strictly to this schedule.
