## Summary

BSE's Surveillance department has revised price bands for 30 equity scrips, effective March 16, 2026. The revisions cover a range of price band levels — 2%, 5%, 10%, and 20% — applied based on surveillance assessments of individual securities. Trading members are advised to take note of the new limits before market open on March 16, 2026.

## Key Points

- Price bands revised for 30 scrips across BSE equity segment
- Effective date: March 16, 2026
- Revised bands range from 2% (tightest) to 20% (widest)
- Two scrips assigned the most restrictive 2% band: Maruti Interior Products Ltd (543464) and Take Solutions Ltd (532890)
- Two scrips assigned a 20% band: Bharatrohan Airborne Innovations Ltd (544535) and Gaudium IVF and Women Health Ltd (544709)
- Circular issued by BSE Surveillance department (Sanjay Jain, Deputy Vice President; Arpita Joshi, Manager)

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure orders in the above scrips are placed within the newly revised price band limits from March 16, 2026 onwards
- Members requiring clarification should contact BSE Surveillance at bse.surv@bseindia.com
- No action required from listed companies; this is a trading-level restriction

## Important Dates

- **March 13, 2026**: Circular issued
- **March 16, 2026**: Revised price bands come into effect

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine but operationally significant surveillance action. Tighter price bands (2% and 5%) on several scrips will limit intraday price movement, which may reduce liquidity and increase impact cost for traders in these securities. The 2% band on Maruti Interior Products Ltd and Take Solutions Ltd suggests elevated surveillance concern for potential price manipulation or abnormal volatility. The 20% band assigned to Bharatrohan Airborne Innovations Ltd and Gaudium IVF and Women Health Ltd likely reflects their relatively recent listing or lower liquidity profile. Overall market impact is low given the small-cap nature of the affected scrips.
