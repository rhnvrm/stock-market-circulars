## Summary

BSE has announced that Alka India Ltd (Scrip Code: 530889) will be removed from Stage 3 of the Graded Surveillance Measure (GSM) framework effective January 29, 2026. This action follows an NCLT-approved resolution plan dated February 7, 2025. Simultaneously, the trading suspension on the company's securities will be revoked, and the stock will participate in the Special Pre-open Session (SPOS) for IPOs and relisted scrips.

## Key Points

- Alka India Ltd (530889) to be moved out of GSM Stage 3 framework
- Trading suspension on the securities will be revoked
- Action based on NCLT-approved resolution plan dated February 7, 2025
- Stock will be part of Special Pre-open Session (SPOS) for IPOs and relisted scrips
- Related to previous exchange notices dated May 6, 2022 (20220506-12) and February 14, 2024 (20240214-11)

## Regulatory Changes

The company will transition out of the stringent Stage 3 surveillance framework under SEBI's shell company directions. Stage 3 typically represents the highest level of surveillance with trading restrictions. The removal indicates successful completion of resolution proceedings under insolvency framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the removal from GSM Stage 3
- Participants should be aware of SPOS applicability for the scrip
- Trading members should refer to exchange notice 20120216-29 for SPOS operational details
- All market participants and the company are required to take note of these changes

## Important Dates

- **NCLT Order Date**: February 7, 2025
- **Circular Date**: January 23, 2026
- **Effective Date**: January 29, 2026 (Thursday) - GSM Stage 3 removal and trading suspension revocation

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Positive for Alka India Ltd shareholders as trading restrictions will be lifted. The stock will regain liquidity after being under surveillance framework. The SPOS mechanism will help establish fair price discovery during the initial trading session post-relisting.

**Investor Impact**: Existing shareholders will be able to trade freely. New investors will have access to the stock through normal trading channels. The SPOS session will provide a controlled environment for price discovery.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update their systems to reflect the changed status. The company's successful resolution plan indicates operational restructuring and potential business revival.
