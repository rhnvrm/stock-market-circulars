## Summary

BSE has listed five commercial papers (CPs) issued on a private placement basis on its Debt Market Segment, effective June 29, 2026. The securities are issued by SEIL Energy India Limited, Nexus Select Trust, DSP Finance Private Limited, Tata Power Co. Ltd, and OXYZO Financial Services Limited. All securities are traded in dematerialised form with a standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and a tick size of 1 paise.

## Key Points

- All five CPs are admitted to trading on BSE Debt Segment effective June 29, 2026
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Trading is exclusively in dematerialised form
- All CPs were issued via ICICI Bank Limited as the Issuing and Paying Agent
- Credit ratings range from ICRA A1+ to CRISIL A1+/IND A1+, indicating high short-term credit quality
- Maturities range from September 2026 to December 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notice informing trading members of newly admitted debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these CPs only in dematerialised form
- Transactions must be in standard denominations of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Trading members requiring clarification may contact the BSE Debt Department at: 22728352 / 8597 / 8995 / 5753 / 8915

## Important Dates

- **Listing / Trading Effective Date:** June 29, 2026
- **SEIL Energy CP Redemption:** September 10, 2026
- **Nexus Select Trust CP Redemption:** September 24, 2026
- **DSP Finance CP Redemption:** September 22, 2026
- **Tata Power CP Redemption:** September 23, 2026
- **OXYZO Financial CP Redemption:** December 22, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal broad market impact as it pertains solely to the BSE Debt Segment and involves privately placed commercial papers targeting institutional participants. All five issuers carry top-tier short-term credit ratings (A1+), signalling low credit risk. The largest issuance by quantity is Tata Power Co. Ltd with 10,000 units. OXYZO Financial Services stands out with a higher coupon rate of 9.30% p.a. and a longer tenor to December 2026, compared to the other issuers at 6.80–7.20% maturing in September 2026. Debt segment trading members should update their systems to reflect the new scrip codes and ISINs.
