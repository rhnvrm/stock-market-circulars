## Summary

AKARA CAPITAL ADVISORS PRIVATE LIMITED has announced part redemption of its non-convertible debentures along with interest payment. The record date has been fixed as February 6, 2026, and trading in the debentures will commence with reduced face value from the same date. The debenture face value will be reduced to Rs. 50,000.13 per debenture.

## Key Points

- Company: AKARA CAPITAL ADVISORS PRIVATE LIMITED
- Debenture: ACAPL-13.30%-21-02-27-PVT
- ISIN: INE08XP07332
- BSE Code: 977052
- Record Date: February 6, 2026
- Purpose: Part redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Reduced Face Value: Rs. 50,000.13 per debenture
- Trading with reduced face value begins: February 6, 2026
- Settlement Number: DR-815/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification for debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that trading in the company's debentures will be conducted with reduced face value effective February 6, 2026
- All trading and settlement systems should reflect the new face value from the effective date
- Trading members should update their records accordingly for the settlement DR-815/2025-2026

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: February 6, 2026 - Eligibility date for part redemption and interest payment
- **Effective Date**: February 6, 2026 - Trading commences with reduced face value
- **Settlement Number**: DR-815/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action with minimal market impact. The part redemption affects privately placed non-convertible debentures of a private limited company with limited trading activity on the debt segment. Debenture holders will receive part payment and interest, with the remaining debenture value reduced to Rs. 50,000.13 per unit. Trading members dealing in these securities need to update their systems to reflect the reduced face value from the effective date.
