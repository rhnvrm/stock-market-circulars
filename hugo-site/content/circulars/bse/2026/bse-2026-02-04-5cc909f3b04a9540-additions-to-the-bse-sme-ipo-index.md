## Summary

BSE announced the addition of three newly listed SME companies to the BSE SME IPO Index effective at the open of Thursday, February 05, 2026. The companies are Kanishk Aluminium India Limited (ticker: 544693), Accretion Nutraveda Limited (ticker: 544694), and Msafe Equipments Limited (ticker: 544695), all of which were listed on BSE on Wednesday, February 04, 2026.

## Key Points

- Three new SME companies added to BSE SME IPO Index
- Kanishk Aluminium India Limited (Exchange ticker: 544693)
- Accretion Nutraveda Limited (Exchange ticker: 544694)
- Msafe Equipments Limited (Exchange ticker: 544695)
- All three stocks listed on BSE on February 04, 2026
- Index inclusion effective at market open on February 05, 2026
- Changes included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index rebalancing action.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index-tracking funds and ETFs may need to adjust their portfolios to reflect the new index composition.

## Important Dates

- **February 04, 2026**: Listing date for all three companies on BSE
- **February 05, 2026**: Effective date for index inclusion (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

The addition of these three SME stocks to the BSE SME IPO Index is a routine administrative action with minimal market impact. Index-tracking funds and passive investors in the BSE SME IPO Index will need to acquire these stocks in proportion to their index weightings. The impact is limited to the SME segment and is not expected to affect broader market operations or trading patterns.
