## Summary

BSE has issued a circular disclosing the allocation of equity shares to anchor investors in the public issue (IPO) of Rajputana Stainless Limited. Anchor investor allocations are made one day prior to the IPO subscription opening and reflect institutional interest in the offering.

## Key Points

- Rajputana Stainless Limited is conducting a public issue of equity shares on BSE.
- Shares have been allocated to anchor investors at the anchor investor price as determined by the company and book running lead managers.
- The circular provides a list of anchor investors, the number of shares allocated to each, and the aggregate allocation details.
- This allocation is a mandatory pre-IPO disclosure required under SEBI regulations.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular is a standard disclosure under SEBI (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2018, which mandate disclosure of anchor investor allocations before the IPO opens for public subscription.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company and its book running lead managers are required to disclose anchor investor allocation details to the stock exchange prior to the IPO subscription period.
- Anchor investors are subject to a lock-in period of 30 days from the date of allotment for 50% of shares, and 90 days for the remaining 50%, as per SEBI norms.

## Important Dates

- **Anchor Investor Allocation Date:** March 6, 2026
- IPO subscription is expected to open the following business day(s) after this allocation.

## Impact Assessment

This circular is relevant to retail and institutional investors tracking the Rajputana Stainless Limited IPO. Anchor investor participation and pricing signal institutional confidence in the issue. No broader market or systemic impact is expected. Investors should review the anchor investor list and allocation price as part of their IPO evaluation.
