## Summary

BSE has published Notice No. 20260302-2 announcing that the Draft Offer Document of **Navkkar Gold & Diamond Limited**, filed on February 28, 2026, is now available on the BSE SME website. The document has been made public in accordance with Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations, inviting public comments for a minimum period of 21 days.

## Key Points

- The Draft Offer Document (DRHP) of Navkkar Gold & Diamond Limited was filed on February 28, 2026.
- The document is hosted on the BSE SME website for public access.
- It contains all disclosures as mandated under Schedule VI of the ICDR (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations.
- Public comments are invited for a period of at least 21 days from the date of the notice (March 2, 2026).
- Comments must be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this notice. It is a procedural filing under the existing ICDR Regulations framework governing SME IPOs.

## Compliance Requirements

- **General Public / Market Participants**: May review the Draft Offer Document on the BSE SME website and submit any comments or objections to equity.ipo@bseindia.com within 21 days.
- **No action required** by brokers, trading members, or listed companies.

## Important Dates

- **Filing Date**: February 28, 2026
- **Notice Date**: March 2, 2026
- **Public Comment Deadline**: On or before March 23, 2026 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine pre-IPO disclosure notice for a company on the SME segment. The impact is limited to prospective investors and analysts interested in Navkkar Gold & Diamond Limited's upcoming IPO. There is no immediate impact on existing listed securities, trading operations, or broader market participants.
