## Summary

BSE has listed 20,99,750 new equity shares of Rama Petrochemicals Ltd. (Scrip Code: 500358), issued to promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. These shares are permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Monday, June 1, 2026, and rank pari-passu with the existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 20,99,750 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at par to promoters on a preferential basis
- Shares issued pursuant to conversion of warrants
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 13320601 to 15420350
- ISIN: INE783A01013
- All 20,99,750 new shares are subject to lock-in until 30 December 2027

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification under BSE Listing Operations for newly allotted securities following a preferential issue.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities and their eligibility to trade from June 1, 2026
- The lock-in restriction on all 20,99,750 shares must be observed until 30/12/2027; these shares are not freely tradeable during the lock-in period

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** 13 April 2026
- **Trading permitted from:** 1 June 2026 (Monday)
- **Lock-in expiry:** 30 December 2027

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds approximately 20.99 lakh shares to the tradeable pool of Rama Petrochemicals Ltd., representing a dilutive preferential allotment to promoters via warrant conversion at the face value of Rs. 10/-. However, since all newly issued shares are locked in until December 2027, there is no immediate increase in free-float or trading volume. The primary market impact is the equity dilution for existing shareholders. Traders and investors in scrip code 500358 should note the expanded total share count.
