## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 9,00,000 new equity shares of Atal Realtech Limited (Scrip Code: 543911) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Tuesday, March 10, 2026. These shares were issued at a premium on a preferential basis to the Promoter pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 9,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each face value issued at a premium of Rs. 14/- (issue price Rs. 16/-) to Promoter on a preferential basis
- Shares issued pursuant to conversion of warrants
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution Numbers: 122996251 to 123896250
- ISIN: INE0ALR01029
- Date of Allotment: October 10, 2025
- Trading effective from: March 10, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification for newly allotted securities under an existing preferential allotment framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to note the listing and permit trading in the new securities from March 10, 2026
- All 9,00,000 shares are subject to lock-in; trading members should be aware of the lock-in restrictions

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** October 10, 2025
- **Trading Commencement:** March 10, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry:** September 30, 2027 (for all 9,00,000 shares, Dist. Nos. 122996251–123896250)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing event with minimal broader market impact. The 9,00,000 shares are entirely under lock-in until September 30, 2027, so there is no immediate increase in free-float or trading supply. The issue price of Rs. 16/- (face value Rs. 2/- + premium Rs. 14/-) reflects the preferential allotment terms agreed at the time of warrant issuance. Impact is confined to existing shareholders and the Promoter of Atal Realtech Limited.
