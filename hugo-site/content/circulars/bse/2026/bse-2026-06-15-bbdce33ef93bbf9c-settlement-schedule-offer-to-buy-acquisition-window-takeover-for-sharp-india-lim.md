## Summary

ICCL (Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd.) has published the settlement schedule for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for Sharp India Limited. The bidding window runs from June 16, 2026 to June 30, 2026, with final settlement on July 6, 2026. Members and custodians must use the early pay-in mechanism via both depositories (CDSL and NSDL) to deliver demat shares.

## Key Points

- Settlement Type: Takeover | Settlement No.: 2026073
- Demat shares must be delivered exclusively through the Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism available in both CDSL and NSDL
- Members/Custodians must use Market Type "(Takeover)" and Settlement No. 2026073 in the depository system
- CDSL & NSDL early pay-in securities will be earmarked/blocked in clients' accounts under the requisite market type and settlement number
- Members and Custodians are NOT required to upload any EPN file
- Members should refer to file MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo for client-wise Stamp Duty charges
- Reference Notice No. 20200528-32 (May 28, 2020) for Revised Guidelines on acquisition of shares through Stock Exchange

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice operationalizes the existing acquisition window framework under the Revised Guidelines for Mechanism for Acquisition of Shares through Stock Exchange (per Notice No. 20200528-32).

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and Custodians must ensure early pay-in of securities is reflected in BSE/ICCL systems before the prescribed timelines for accepting/confirming bids on the BSE OTB platform
- Early pay-in must be completed sufficiently in advance of closure of the BSE OTB Bidding window (for members placing bids) and the confirmation window (for custodians confirming bids)
- No EPN file upload is required
- Stamp duty charges should be verified via the MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Bid Start Date | June 16, 2026 |
| Bid End Date | June 30, 2026 |
| Obligation Download | July 3, 2026 |
| Settlement Date | July 6, 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

This notice affects members and custodians participating in the Sharp India Limited takeover offer. Impact is limited to participants in this specific acquisition window. The early pay-in requirement is a standard procedural mandate that reduces counterparty risk. Participants failing to complete early pay-in before the BSE OTB Bidding/Confirmation window closes risk bid rejection. No broader market or systemic impact is anticipated.
