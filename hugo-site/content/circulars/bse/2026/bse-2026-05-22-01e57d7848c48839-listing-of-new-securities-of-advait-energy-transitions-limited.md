## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 5,630 new equity shares of Advait Energy Transitions Limited (Scrip Code: 543230, ISIN: INE0ALI01010) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Monday, May 25, 2026. The shares were issued at a premium of Rs. 1766/- (issue price Rs. 1776/-) to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 5,630 equity shares of face value Rs. 10/- each listed on BSE effective May 25, 2026
- Shares issued at a premium of Rs. 1766/- per share; total issue price Rs. 1776/-
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis via conversion of warrants
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 10937382 to 10943011
- ISIN: INE0ALI01010

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification pursuant to an earlier allotment on preferential basis.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities available for trading from May 25, 2026
- All 5,630 shares are subject to a lock-in period and cannot be traded freely until January 30, 2027

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** March 4, 2026
- **Listing / Trading Effective Date:** May 25, 2026 (Monday)
- **Lock-in Expiry Date:** January 30, 2027

## Impact Assessment

The listing involves a very small number of shares (5,630) relative to the company's total equity base, and all newly listed shares carry a lock-in until January 30, 2027, further limiting immediate market impact. This is a routine administrative listing action with low significance for broader market participants.
