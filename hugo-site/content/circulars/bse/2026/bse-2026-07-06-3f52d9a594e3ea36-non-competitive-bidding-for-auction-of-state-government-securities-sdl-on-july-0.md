## Summary

BSE has issued a notice referencing RBI's July 03, 2026 press release announcing an auction of State Government Securities (SDL) on July 07, 2026. Trading members and direct investors can participate in the non-competitive bidding process through the NCB-GSec module of BSE's iBBS web-based system, in line with BSE circular no. 20191122-28 dated November 22, 2019.

## Key Points

- Bidding is conducted via https://ibbs.bseindia.com under the NCB-GSec module
- Securities on offer include new and re-issued SDLs from Bihar, Chhattisgarh, Himachal Pradesh, Jammu & Kashmir, Jharkhand, Kerala, Madhya Pradesh, Manipur, Sikkim, Telangana, Uttar Pradesh, and Uttarakhand
- Individual issue sizes range from Rs. 2 crore (SK SDL 2039) to Rs. 20 crore (7.90% MP 2056)
- Minimum subscription size is Rs. 100, in multiples of Rs. 10,000, subject to the maximum amount specified per security

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory change; this is an operational notice implementing the non-competitive bidding facility established under BSE circular 20191122-28 (November 22, 2019), pursuant to RBI's July 03, 2026 auction announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure client bids are submitted within the specified windows via the NCB-GSec module
- Direct investors must place bids by 5:00 pm on July 06, 2026
- Members must place bids by 8:00 am on July 07, 2026

## Important Dates

- Bid Collection Start: July 06, 2026, 10:00 am (24-hour availability)
- Bid Collection End (Direct Investors): July 06, 2026, 5:00 pm
- Bid Collection End (Members): July 07, 2026, 8:00 am
- Auction Date: July 07, 2026
- Settlement Date: July 08, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice affecting only trading members and investors participating in the SDL non-competitive bidding auction. It has no bearing on listed equities or broader market trading and carries low market impact.
