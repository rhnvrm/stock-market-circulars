## Summary

BSE has listed 6,74,30,883 new equity shares of Poonawalla Fincorp Limited (Scrip Code: 524000, ISIN: INE511C01022) allotted to Qualified Institutional Buyers (QIBs) under a Qualified Institutional Placement (QIP). The shares are admitted for trading effective Thursday, April 16, 2026.

## Key Points

- 6,74,30,883 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each listed on BSE
- Shares allotted to QIBs pursuant to a Qualified Institutional Placement (QIP)
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Issue price: Rs. 370.75 per share
- Distribution numbers: 812817659 to 880248541
- Date of allotment: April 13, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice following completion of a QIP under SEBI regulations governing institutional placements.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the new share distribution range (812817659 to 880248541) for settlement and reconciliation purposes.
- Queries may be directed to Mr. Tejas Tandel at Tel. 022 2272 8721.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** April 13, 2026
- **Trading Commencement:** April 16, 2026 (Thursday)
- **Notice Date:** April 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing of approximately 6.74 crore new equity shares via QIP represents a capital raise for Poonawalla Fincorp Limited at Rs. 370.75 per share, implying a total raise of approximately Rs. 2,500 crore. The dilution from new shares may exert modest short-term pressure on the stock price. However, QIP proceeds typically strengthen the capital base of NBFCs, supporting growth and regulatory capital requirements. The pari-passu ranking ensures no preference distinction from existing shareholders.
