## Summary

BSE has announced changes to several of its indices following the merger of JB Chemicals & Pharmaceuticals Ltd (506943) into Torrent Pharmaceuticals Limited (500420), effective Friday, July 17, 2026. As a result of this Scheme of Arrangement, JB Chemicals & Pharmaceuticals will be removed from multiple BSE indices, with various replacement stocks added in cascading adjustments across index tiers.

## Key Points

- JB Chemicals & Pharmaceuticals Ltd (506943) is merging into Torrent Pharmaceuticals Limited (500420), effective July 17, 2026.
- The merger triggers a series of ADD/DROP changes across seven BSE indices to maintain index composition rules.
- Index changes cascade: as JB Chemicals is dropped from higher-tier indices, replacement stocks are pulled up from lower-tier indices, triggering further downstream adjustments.

## Regulatory Changes

Index constituent changes effective July 17, 2026:

- **BSE 500**: DROP JB Chemicals & Pharmaceuticals Ltd (506943); ADD Grindwell Norton Ltd. (506076)
- **BSE 250 SMALLCAP INDEX**: DROP JB Chemicals & Pharmaceuticals Ltd (506943); ADD Grindwell Norton Ltd. (506076)
- **BSE 400 MIDSMALLCAP INDEX**: DROP JB Chemicals & Pharmaceuticals Ltd (506943); ADD Grindwell Norton Ltd. (506076)
- **BSE 500 EQUAL WEIGHT**: DROP JB Chemicals & Pharmaceuticals Ltd (506943); ADD Grindwell Norton Ltd. (506076)
- **BSE 1000**: DROP JB Chemicals & Pharmaceuticals Ltd (506943); ADD Knowledge Marine & Engineering Ltd. (543273)
- **BSE 1000 MULTICAP EQUAL SIZE WEIGHTED (25%)**: DROP JB Chemicals & Pharmaceuticals Ltd (506943); ADD Knowledge Marine & Engineering Ltd. (543273)
- **BSE Next 250 MicroCap**: DROP Knowledge Marine & Engineering Ltd. (543273); ADD Shreeji Shipping Global Ltd. (544490)
- **BSE Next 500**: DROP Grindwell Norton Ltd. (506076); ADD Knowledge Marine & Engineering Ltd. (543273)
- **BSE SMALLCAP 500**: DROP JB Chemicals & Pharmaceuticals Ltd (506943); ADD Shreeji Shipping Global Ltd. (544490)
- **BSE 250 MicroCap**: DROP Grindwell Norton Ltd. (506076); ADD Shreeji Shipping Global Ltd. (544490)

## Compliance Requirements

No direct action is required from investors or listed companies. Index fund managers, ETF providers, and passive investment vehicles tracking the affected BSE indices will need to rebalance their portfolios in line with the announced changes effective July 17, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **July 17, 2026**: Effective date for the merger of JB Chemicals & Pharmaceuticals into Torrent Pharmaceuticals, and for all associated index constituent changes.

## Impact Assessment

The index reconstitution will affect passive fund flows tied to the seven listed BSE indices, with stocks being added or dropped experiencing potential trading volume and price impact around the effective date due to rebalancing by index-tracking funds. The changes reflect standard index maintenance procedures following a corporate merger and do not indicate any regulatory concern.
