## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Maha Rashtra Apex Corporation Ltd. (Scrip Code: 523384) has forfeited 58,204 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each. These shares were partly paid-up and were forfeited due to non-payment of allotment/call monies by the respective shareholders.

## Key Points

- Company: Maha Rashtra Apex Corporation Ltd. (Scrip Code: 523384)
- Number of shares forfeited: 58,204 equity shares
- Face value: Rs. 10/- per share
- Reason for forfeiture: Non-payment of allotment/call monies on partly paid-up shares
- Distinctive numbers of forfeited shares are provided in Annexure-I of the notice

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notification under BSE Listing Operations.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed for record and reference purposes. No specific compliance action is required from trading members. The forfeiture of shares is a corporate action by the company in accordance with applicable company law provisions for non-payment of call money.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 12 May 2026
- Notice Number: 20260512-14

## Impact Assessment

The forfeiture affects a limited number of partly paid-up shares (58,204) and is a routine corporate action with minimal market impact. The affected shares will be cancelled and may be re-issued by the company. Existing fully paid-up shareholders are not directly impacted. The overall float and trading of the scrip on BSE remains largely unaffected given the small quantum of shares involved.
