## Summary

BSE has notified Trading Members and Custodian Members of the schedule of activities for the Offer to Buy - Acquisition Window (Buyback) of shares of ROLEX RINGS LTD. The buyback offer runs from Thursday, 9th July 2026 to Wednesday, 15th July 2026, with an offer price of Rs. 180 per share for up to 1,00,00,000 equity shares.

## Key Points

- Security: ROLEX RINGS LTD (Symbol: ROLEXRINGS, ISIN: INE645S01024, Scrip Code: 543325)
- Offer size: Up to 1,00,00,000 equity shares
- Market lot: 1; Face value: Rs. 1.00
- Offer price: Rs. 180.00 per share
- Bid offering, modification/cancellation period: 9th July 2026 to 15th July 2026, 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.
- Give-up/take-up period: 9th July 2026 to 8th July 2026, 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.
- On last day (15th July 2026): Give-up till 3:30 p.m., Take-up till 4:00 p.m.
- Bidding categories include FII, FI, MF, IC, Other QIBs, Other NIB, Company, and Individual
- Access via iBBS platform (Internet/Leased Line), RTRMS, and Extranet for file exchange

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is an operational notice referencing existing Exchange Notices no. 20150930-1 (dated 30 September 2015) and 20170203-29 (dated 3 February 2017) for OTB operational instructions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must ensure the UCC of investors is registered/updated with correct PAN and Category in the Exchange UCC database.
- Trading Members can bid only for investors holding shares in demat form.
- Members and Custodians should note that if no securities are accepted, no End of Issue files will be generated in Extranet.

## Important Dates

- Offer opening: Thursday, 9th July 2026
- Offer closing: Wednesday, 15th July 2026
- Give-up (last day): 15th July 2026, 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.
- Take-up (last day): 15th July 2026, 9:15 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.

## Impact Assessment

This notice is procedural, informing Trading and Custodian Members of the operational timelines to facilitate the ROLEX RINGS LTD buyback via the Acquisition Window mechanism. It has limited direct market impact but is important for members and eligible shareholders participating in the buyback to adhere to bidding, modification, and settlement timelines.
