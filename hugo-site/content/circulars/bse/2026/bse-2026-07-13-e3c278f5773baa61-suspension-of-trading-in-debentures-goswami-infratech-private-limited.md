## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that GOSWAMI INFRATECH PRIVATE LIMITED has fixed 15 July 2026 as the Record Date for Voluntary Early Redemption of its Non-Convertible Debenture (NCD) GIPL-ZC-30-6-26-PVT (security code 974946). No dealings in this debenture will occur from 15 July 2026. Trading in the debenture is already under suspension, as previously communicated via Exchange Notice No. 20260629-25 dated 29 June 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: Goswami Infratech Private Limited
- Instrument: Non-Convertible Debenture (NCD) — GIPL-ZC-30-6-26-PVT
- Security Code: 974946
- Notice reference: DR-672/2026-2027
- Purpose: Early Redemption of NCD
- Record Date: 15 July 2026
- No Dealings From: 15 July 2026
- Trading in the debenture remains under suspension per earlier Exchange Notice No. 20260629-25 dated 29 June 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is an operational/administrative notice implementing the previously announced suspension and confirming the record date for early redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must take note that dealings in the specified debenture are prohibited from the record date onward and should not process any trades in this security.

## Important Dates

- Record Date: 15 July 2026
- No Dealings From: 15 July 2026
- Prior suspension notice: 29 June 2026 (Notice No. 20260629-25)

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders of this specific NCD issued by an unlisted private company. There is no effect on listed equity markets or broader trading activity; this is a routine administrative update related to debt redemption processing.
