## Summary

BSE's Surveillance department (DOSS) sought clarifications from two listed companies regarding news articles published in various media outlets. Hampton Sky Realty Ltd has submitted its reply, while Coromandel International Ltd's response is still awaited.

## Key Points

- BSE proactively sought clarifications to verify accuracy of news articles and keep members/investors informed.
- Hampton Sky Realty Ltd (Scrip Code: 526407) responded to a news article from moneycontrol.com.
- Coromandel International Ltd (Scrip Code: 506395) has not yet replied regarding a news article from thehindu.com.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Coromandel International Ltd is expected to submit its clarification to BSE in response to the news article referenced from thehindu.com.
- Listed companies are required to respond promptly to BSE queries under surveillance obligations.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 09 June 2026
- No specific deadline mentioned for Coromandel International's pending reply.

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact anticipated. This is a routine surveillance action to ensure transparency and accuracy of information available to investors. Hampton Sky Realty's reply has already been received; Coromandel International's pending response may result in follow-up action if not submitted promptly.
