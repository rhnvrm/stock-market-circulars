## Summary

BSE has informed members that Kotak Mahindra Mutual Fund has revised the minimum redemption amount for six of its schemes traded on the BSE StAR MF platform. The revision lowers the minimum redemption threshold significantly, effective June 3, 2026.

## Key Points

- Minimum redemption amount reduced from Rs. 1,000 to Rs. 100 for six Kotak Mahindra schemes
- Redemption multiples revised from Re. 100 to Re. 10
- Six schemes are affected: Kotak Income Plus Arbitrage Omni FOF, Kotak Flexicap Fund, Kotak Large & Midcap Fund, Kotak Nifty Next 50 Index Fund, Kotak Business Cycle Fund, and Kotak Contra Fund
- Notice-cum-Addendum issued by Kotak Mahindra AMC is attached to the circular

## Regulatory Changes

The minimum redemption parameters for the listed schemes on BSE StAR MF have been updated as follows:

| Parameter | Existing | Revised |
|---|---|---|
| Minimum Redemption Amount | Rs. 1,000 | Rs. 100 |
| Multiples Thereafter | Re. 100 | Re. 10 |

## Compliance Requirements

Exchange members operating on the BSE StAR MF platform should update their systems to reflect the revised minimum redemption amounts for the six affected Kotak Mahindra schemes.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** June 3, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a minor operational change that benefits investors by lowering the threshold for redemptions in these six Kotak Mahindra schemes. The reduction from Rs. 1,000 to Rs. 100 allows retail investors to redeem smaller amounts, improving liquidity flexibility. No adverse market impact is anticipated; the change is investor-friendly and administrative in nature.
