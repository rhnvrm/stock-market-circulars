## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of ARMAN FINANCIAL SERVICES LIMITED with effect from January 16, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Company: ARMAN FINANCIAL SERVICES LIMITED
- ISIN: INE109C07097
- Scrip Code: 975626
- Debenture Series: AFSL-10.20%-25-1-26-PVT
- Record Date: January 16, 2026
- No Dealings From: January 16, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- Notice Reference: DR-800/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational measure in line with existing market practices for handling corporate actions on debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures (ISIN: INE109C07097) from January 16, 2026
- Members must ensure compliance with the no-dealings restriction to avoid settlement issues
- Trading members should update their systems to reflect the trading suspension

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: January 9, 2026
- **Record Date**: January 16, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Effective From**: January 16, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - affects only holders of this specific debenture series. The suspension is temporary and routine for record date processing.

**Investor Impact**: Low - existing debenture holders will receive their redemption proceeds and interest as per the terms. No new trading activity will be permitted from the record date.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - standard procedure for debt market participants. Trading members need to ensure their systems prevent execution of orders for this ISIN from January 16, 2026.
