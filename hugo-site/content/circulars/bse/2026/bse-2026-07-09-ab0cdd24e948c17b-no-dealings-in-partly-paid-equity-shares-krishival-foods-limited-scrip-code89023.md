## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that a record date of 13 July 2026 has been fixed for Krishival Foods Limited (Scrip Code: 890232) to determine holders of partly paid-up rights equity shares eligible to receive the First and Final Call Notice for payment of call money. Trading members are directed not to deal in the partly paid equity shares of the company from the record date.

## Key Points

- Company: Krishival Foods Limited
- Scrip Code: 890232
- Reference: DR-670/2026-2027
- Record Date: 13 July 2026
- Purpose: To ascertain shareholders of partly paid shares eligible to pay the First and Final Call
- Call amount: Rs. 195 per share (Rs. 6.50 towards face value and Rs. 188.50 towards share premium)
- No dealings in partly paid equity shares from 13 July 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a standard BSE notice implementing corporate action processing for partly paid rights shares under existing listing operations procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must refrain from dealing in the partly paid equity shares of Krishival Foods Limited effective from the record date of 13 July 2026, and take note of the settlement number associated with the no-dealings period.

## Important Dates

- Record Date / No Dealings From: 13 July 2026
- Notice Date: 09 July 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders of partly paid-up rights equity shares of Krishival Foods Limited and trading members handling this specific scrip. It does not affect the company's fully paid equity shares or broader market trading activity. The notice ensures orderly identification of shareholders liable for the First and Final Call payment of Rs. 195 per partly paid share.
