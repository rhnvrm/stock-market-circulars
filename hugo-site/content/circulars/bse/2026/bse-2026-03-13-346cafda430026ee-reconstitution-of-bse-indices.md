## Summary

BSE Index Services Private Ltd (formerly Asia Index Pvt. Ltd.) has announced the reconstitution of the BSE SMALLCAP 500 index. Four securities will be dropped and four new securities will be added, effective at the open of Monday, March 23, 2026.

## Key Points

- BSE SMALLCAP 500 index is being reconstituted with effect from March 23, 2026
- Four stocks are being removed from the index
- Four new stocks are being added to the index
- Changes have been included in the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE) on March 13, 2026
- Queries can be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory rule changes; this is a routine periodic index reconstitution carried out by BSE Index Services Private Ltd.

## Compliance Requirements

- Index fund managers, ETF managers, and passive funds tracking the BSE SMALLCAP 500 must rebalance their portfolios to reflect the new composition by the open of March 23, 2026.
- Clients should refer to the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE) released on March 13, 2026 for technical implementation details.

## Important Dates

- **March 13, 2026**: Announcement date; end-of-day corporate action file updated
- **March 23, 2026**: Effective date — changes take effect at market open
