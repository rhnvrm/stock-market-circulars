## Summary

BSE has issued a notice outlining the live activities schedule for the opening of the Offer for Sale (OFS) for NHPC Ltd. This notice falls under the Trading Operations department and is categorised under the Trading and Equity segments.

## Key Points

- Notice issued by BSE Trading Operations department on 01 June 2026
- Pertains to the opening of the Offer for Sale (OFS) for NHPC Ltd
- Covers the live activities schedule for OFS participants
- Categorised under Equity segment, Trading category
- No attachments accompanying this notice

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced. This is an operational notice providing the activity timeline for the NHPC OFS opening.

## Compliance Requirements

Market participants (brokers, investors) intending to participate in the NHPC OFS should refer to this notice and the live activities schedule to ensure timely submission of bids and adherence to the OFS process.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 01 June 2026
- OFS Opening: 01 June 2026 (as indicated by the notice date and subject)

## Impact Assessment

This notice is operationally significant for investors and brokers participating in the NHPC OFS. The live activities schedule governs the sequencing of OFS-related actions on the exchange platform. Impact is moderate and limited to participants in the NHPC OFS; broader market impact is minimal.
