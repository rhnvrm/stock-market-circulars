## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members that the PTCs (Pass Through Certificates) of INDIA UNIVERSAL TRUST AL1 have been redeemed by the trustee, Catalyst Trusteeship Limited. As a result, trading in these PTCs is suspended with effect from June 25, 2026.

## Key Points

- Catalyst Trusteeship Limited confirmed the redemption of PTCs of INDIA UNIVERSAL TRUST AL1.
- Scrip: IUTA1-8.02%-20-9-26-PTC | ISIN: INE16J715019 | Scrip Code: 976050.
- Trading in the aforesaid PTCs is suspended from June 25, 2026.
- Reference: DR-659/2026-2027.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This notice implements the standard process of suspending trading in redeemed debt securities under BSE Listing Operations guidelines.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are advised not to deal in IUTA1-8.02%-20-9-26-PTC (ISIN: INE16J715019, scrip code 976050) from June 25, 2026 onwards. Members must ensure no buy or sell orders are placed for this instrument after the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **June 24, 2026**: Notice issued by BSE.
- **June 25, 2026**: Suspension of trading effective date.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to current holders and trading members dealing in INDIA UNIVERSAL TRUST AL1 PTCs. Since the PTCs have been fully redeemed, the suspension is a procedural step to prevent any further trading in these securities. Investors holding these PTCs would have already received their redemption proceeds from the trustee.
