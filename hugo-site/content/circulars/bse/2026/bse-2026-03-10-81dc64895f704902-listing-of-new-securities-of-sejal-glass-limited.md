## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 13,00,000 new equity shares of Sejal Glass Limited (Scrip Code: 532993) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Wednesday, March 11, 2026. The shares were issued on a preferential basis to Promoter and Non-Promoter categories at an issue price of Rs. 555/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- each, premium Rs. 545/-).

## Key Points

- 13,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 545/- (issue price Rs. 555/-)
- Shares issued on a preferential basis to Promoter and Non-Promoter
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 10100001 to 11400000
- Date of allotment: 17/12/2025
- ISIN: INE955I01044
- Trading permitted from March 11, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification pursuant to preferential allotment of equity shares.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new listing and may facilitate trading in these securities from March 11, 2026. Lock-in restrictions must be observed for the applicable tranches as detailed below.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** December 17, 2025
- **Trading Commencement:** March 11, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (5,00,000 shares — Dist. Nos. 1,01,00,001 to 1,06,00,000):** September 14, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (4,00,000 shares — Dist. Nos. 1,06,00,001 to 1,10,00,000):** September 14, 2027
- **Lock-in Expiry (4,00,000 shares — Dist. Nos. 1,10,00,001 to 1,14,00,000):** September 14, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 13,00,000 new equity shares to the tradable float of Sejal Glass Limited from March 11, 2026, though 13,00,000 shares are subject to lock-in until September 2026 or September 2027 depending on the tranche. The effective freely tradable addition is limited until lock-in periods expire. Impact is company-specific and low for broader market participants.
