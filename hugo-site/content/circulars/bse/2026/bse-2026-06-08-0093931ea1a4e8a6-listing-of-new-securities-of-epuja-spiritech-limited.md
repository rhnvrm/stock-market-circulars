## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 95,98,434 new equity shares of EPUJA SPIRITECH LIMITED (Scrip Code: 532092, ISIN: INE807D01030) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Tuesday, June 9, 2026. These shares were issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to the conversion of warrants at an issue price of Rs. 3.40/- per share.

## Key Points

- 95,98,434 equity shares of Rs. 1/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 2.40/- per share
- Issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis via conversion of warrants
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 103483454 to 113081887
- ISIN: INE807D01030
- Entire allotment is subject to lock-in until January 31, 2027

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification pursuant to an approved preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the new securities and their lock-in status. The 95,98,434 shares (Dist. Nos. 103483454 to 113081887) are locked in until January 31, 2027 and cannot be traded until the lock-in period expires.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** September 30, 2025
- **Trading Commencement:** June 9, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry:** January 31, 2027

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds approximately 95.98 lakh new shares to the tradeable universe of EPUJA SPIRITECH LIMITED, though all newly listed shares are under lock-in until January 31, 2027. There is no immediate increase in free-float. The issue price of Rs. 3.40/- per share (face value Rs. 1/- + premium Rs. 2.40/-) serves as a reference point for market pricing. Impact is limited to existing shareholders and prospective investors of this specific scrip.
