## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 45,00,000 new equity shares of North Eastern Carrying Corporation Limited (Scrip Code: 534615, ISIN: INE553C01016) have been listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Monday, June 29, 2026. These shares were issued to Promoters on a preferential basis at an issue price of Rs. 15.81/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- with a premium of Rs. 5.81/-) and are subject to a lock-in period until January 31, 2028.

## Key Points

- 45,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 5.81/- per share to Promoters on a preferential basis
- Issue price: Rs. 15.81/- per share
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution Numbers: 100000001 to 104500000
- Date of Allotment: June 6, 2026
- ISIN: INE553C01016
- Lock-in applies to all 45,00,000 shares until January 31, 2028

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notice pursuant to a completed preferential allotment to promoters.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to update their systems to reflect the new shares available for trading from June 29, 2026. The locked-in shares (100000001 to 104500000) are not available for trading until the lock-in expiry on January 31, 2028.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** June 6, 2026
- **Listing / Trading Effective Date:** June 29, 2026 (Monday)
- **Lock-in Expiry Date:** January 31, 2028

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine preferential allotment listing with limited market impact. The 45,00,000 shares are locked in until January 2028, meaning they will not be available for open market trading in the near term. The allotment increases promoter holding and the total share capital of the company. Existing shareholders should note the dilution effect. No immediate trading disruption is expected.
