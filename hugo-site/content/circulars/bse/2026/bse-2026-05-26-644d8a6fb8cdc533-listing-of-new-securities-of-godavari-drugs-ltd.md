## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 25,96,935 new equity shares of Godavari Drugs Ltd (Scrip Code: 530317, ISIN: INE362C01012) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Wednesday, May 27, 2026. These shares were issued on a preferential basis to Promoters and Non-Promoters at an issue price of Rs. 89/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- at a premium of Rs. 79/-).

## Key Points

- 25,96,935 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 79/- (issue price Rs. 89/-) on a preferential basis
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 7530501 to 10127435
- Date of allotment: March 18, 2026
- Trading permitted from May 27, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification following completion of a preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities and their respective lock-in periods. No specific compliance action is required beyond awareness of lock-in restrictions on the newly listed shares.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** March 18, 2026
- **Listing / Trading Effective Date:** May 27, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (1,12,360 shares, Dist. Nos. 7530501–7642860):** November 30, 2027
- **Lock-in Expiry (24,84,575 shares, Dist. Nos. 7642860–10127435):** November 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing of 25,96,935 new equity shares increases the total share capital of Godavari Drugs Ltd, resulting in dilution for existing shareholders. The bulk of the newly issued shares (24,84,575) are subject to a lock-in until November 30, 2026, and the remaining 1,12,360 shares are locked in until November 30, 2027, limiting their immediate tradability. Overall market impact is moderate given the company's small-cap nature and the lock-in constraints on the new shares.
