## Summary

BSE has announced the non-competitive bidding window for the Government of India (GoI) dated securities auction scheduled for June 19, 2026. The auction covers four securities and is conducted via the NCB-GSec module on BSE's iBBS web-based platform. This notice is in continuation of Exchange circular no. 20180423-42 (April 23, 2018) which launched the non-competitive bidding facility for RBI government securities auctions.

## Key Points

- Four GoI dated securities (G-Secs) are up for auction on June 19, 2026, via the RBI-announced sale
- Bidding is available through the NCB-GSec module at https://ibbs.bseindia.com
- Bid collection opens June 16, 2026 from 10:00 am (24-hour availability)
- Minimum subscription unit is 100 units; minimum bid amount is Rs. 10,000 per security
- Maximum bid amount for each security is Rs. 2 crore
- Settlement date is June 22, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This notice operates under the existing non-competitive bidding framework established by Exchange circular no. 20180423-42 dated April 23, 2018, and the RBI press release dated June 15, 2026.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice relevant to trading members and direct investors seeking to participate in the non-competitive segment of the RBI's G-sec auction. The four securities span maturities from 2029 to 2056, covering short-to-ultra-long-duration government bonds including a sovereign green bond (SGrB 2056). Combined issue size is Rs. 32,000 crore. Participation requires action before June 19, 2026 at 8:00 am for members, making this time-sensitive for eligible participants.
