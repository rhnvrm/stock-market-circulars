## Summary

BSE has issued a notice suspending trading in the debt securities of Taquito Lease Operators Private Limited with effect from April 29, 2026. The suspension is due to the maturity of the debentures on their scheduled maturity date. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the effective date.

## Key Points

- Trading in debentures of Taquito Lease Operators Private Limited is suspended effective April 29, 2026
- The suspension is triggered by the maturity of the debt instrument
- Scrip Code: 957924 | ISIN: INE496Z07024
- Security name: TLOPL-9.5%-3-5-26-PVT (9.5% debenture maturing May 3, 2026)
- Reference: DR-620/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice pursuant to the maturity schedule of the listed debt security.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in debentures with ISIN INE496Z07024 (Scrip Code: 957924) from April 29, 2026 onwards
- All members are required to take note of this suspension and ensure compliance

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** April 28, 2026
- **Suspension Effective Date:** April 29, 2026
- **Maturity Date:** May 3, 2026 (as indicated by the security name TLOPL-9.5%-3-5-26-PVT)

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and traders of this specific debenture. The suspension is a routine action consequent to the securities reaching their maturity date. Debenture holders should expect redemption proceeds as per the terms of the instrument. No broader market or systemic impact is anticipated.
