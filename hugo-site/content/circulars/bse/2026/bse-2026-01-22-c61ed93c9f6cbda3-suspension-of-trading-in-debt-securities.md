## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in debt securities of IL&FS Financial Services Limited (Scrip Code: 953460, ISIN: INE121H08073) effective January 22, 2026. The suspension is due to the scheduled maturity of the 8.90% debt securities on January 27, 2026. Trading members are advised not to deal in these securities.

## Key Points

- Debt securities affected: IL&FS Financial Services Limited 8.90% bonds
- ISIN: INE121H08073
- Scrip Code: 953460
- Maturity Date: January 27, 2026
- Trading suspension effective: January 22, 2026
- Notice Number: 20260122-2
- Reference: DR-805/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural suspension prior to debt maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any deals in the specified IL&FS Financial Services Limited debt securities
- Members should update their systems to reflect the trading suspension
- No new orders should be accepted for these securities

## Important Dates

- **January 22, 2026**: Trading suspension effective date
- **January 27, 2026**: Maturity date of debt securities

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action with minimal market impact. The suspension affects only one specific debt security issue and is a standard procedure before maturity. Investors holding these securities will receive their principal and final interest payment on the maturity date. No broader market implications expected.
