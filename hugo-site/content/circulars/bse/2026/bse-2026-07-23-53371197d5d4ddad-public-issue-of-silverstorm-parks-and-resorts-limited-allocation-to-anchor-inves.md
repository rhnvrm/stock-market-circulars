## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that under the Anchor Investor (AI) portion of the public issue of Silverstorm Parks and Resorts Limited, 16,52,000 equity shares were subscribed on July 23, 2026 at a price of Rs. 133 per equity share. The notice encloses the company's announcement dated July 23, 2026 confirming the anchor allocation.

## Key Points

- 16,52,000 equity shares allocated to Anchor Investors in the Silverstorm Parks and Resorts Limited IPO
- Anchor allocation price fixed at Rs. 133 per equity share
- Subscription confirmed on July 23, 2026
- Notice issued by BSE Listing Business Relationship department, signed by Mangesh Tayde, Vice President

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a disclosure notice, not a regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

No action required from trading members beyond being informed of the anchor allocation ahead of the public issue.

## Important Dates

- July 23, 2026: Anchor investor shares subscribed and company announcement issued

## Impact Assessment

The anchor allocation signals institutional investor interest ahead of the Silverstorm Parks and Resorts Limited public issue and may serve as a sentiment indicator for retail investors evaluating the IPO. Market-wide impact is limited as this pertains to a single company's listing process.
