## Summary

BSE has listed 5,00,000 new equity shares of VARIMAN GLOBAL ENTERPRISES LIMITED (Scrip Code: 540570) issued on a preferential basis to non-promoters. The shares will be available for trading from Monday, February 9, 2026, and are subject to lock-in restrictions until August 15, 2026.

## Key Points

- 5,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 1/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 9.50/-
- Total issue price: Rs. 10.50 per share
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis
- Shares ranking pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distinctive Numbers: 194581001 to 195081000
- Date of Allotment: December 8, 2025
- ISIN: INE717F01028
- Scrip Code: 540570

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed about the new securities listing
- Lock-in restrictions must be observed until August 15, 2026
- All 5,00,000 shares under distinctive numbers 194581001 to 195081000 are subject to lock-in

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 8, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: February 9, 2026 (Monday)
- **Lock-in Expiry**: August 15, 2026
- **Notice Date**: February 6, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine preferential allotment with minimal market impact. The addition of 5,00,000 shares represents dilution for existing shareholders, but the lock-in period until August 15, 2026 prevents immediate selling pressure. The shares are issued to non-promoters at Rs. 10.50 per share, indicating the company's capital raising efforts. Trading members can facilitate transactions in these securities from February 9, 2026, subject to lock-in compliance.
