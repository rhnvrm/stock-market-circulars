## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that three schemes of Trust Mutual Fund's Fixed Maturity Plan Series II (1196 Days) are maturing as on their redemption date. Consequently, trading members are advised not to deal in these schemes with effect from July 08, 2026, per notice DR-667/2026-2027.

## Key Points

- The notice covers three scrip codes of Trust Mutual Fund's Fixed Maturity Plan Series II (1196 Days):
  - 543880: Direct Plan - Growth (ISIN INF0GCD01651)
  - 543881: Regular Plan - Growth (ISIN INF0GCD01636)
  - 543882: Regular Plan - IDCW Payout (ISIN INF0GCD01644)
- These are closed-ended debt (Fixed Maturity Plan) schemes reaching their scheduled maturity/redemption date.
- Trading members are directed to cease dealing in these schemes from the effective date.
- Reference: DR-667/2026-2027, issued by Marian DSouza, Assistant Vice President - Listing Compliance and Operations.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a routine administrative notice implementing the pre-scheduled maturity terms of a closed-ended fund scheme under existing SEBI mutual fund regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE must stop dealing in the three listed Trust Mutual Fund scrip codes (543880, 543881, 543882) with effect from July 08, 2026.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 07, 2026
- Trading suspension effective from: July 08, 2026 (schemes' redemption/maturity date)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This affects a small number of unit holders in three specific closed-ended Fixed Maturity Plan schemes of Trust Mutual Fund that have reached their natural maturity. There is no impact on equity markets, listed companies, or other market participants. Unit holders in these schemes should expect redemption proceeds per the scheme's maturity terms.
