## Summary

BSE has approved the listing and trading of 3,03,030 new equity shares of Som Distilleries & Breweries Ltd. (Scrip Code: 507514) effective from January 27, 2026. These shares were issued to promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to the conversion of warrants at an issue price of Rs. 110 per share. The shares are subject to a lock-in period until July 28, 2027.

## Key Points

- 3,03,030 equity shares of face value Rs. 2 each issued at a premium of Rs. 108
- Shares issued to promoters on preferential basis through warrant conversion
- Distribution numbers: 196503583 to 196806612
- Date of allotment: December 27, 2024
- Issue price: Rs. 110 per share
- ISIN: INE480C01038
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the new securities listing for Som Distilleries & Breweries Ltd.
- The newly listed shares are subject to lock-in restrictions and cannot be freely traded until the lock-in period expires

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 23, 2026
- **Date of Allotment**: December 27, 2024
- **Trading Commencement Date**: January 27, 2026 (Tuesday)
- **Lock-in Period Ends**: July 28, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal immediate impact as all shares are locked-in until July 2027 and cannot be traded in the open market. The allotment represents approximately 3.03 lakh shares issued to promoters.

**Operational Impact**: Routine listing procedure with no changes to trading operations. Trading members need to update their systems to reflect the increased share capital and new distribution numbers for Som Distilleries & Breweries Ltd.
