## Summary

BSE Limited, through the Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd. (ICCL), has issued a notice inviting offers from trading members for the purchase of securities in Demat Auction 652, pertaining to Rolling Settlement No. DR-652/2026-27. The auction session is scheduled for June 17, 2026, with pay-in on June 18, 2026.

## Key Points

- Auction offer entry window: 2:00 PM to 2:45 PM on June 17, 2026
- Auction matching session: 2:45 PM to 3:00 PM
- Delivery statement and accepted auction offer reports: 3:00 PM to 3:30 PM
- Money statement and final reports: 3:35 PM to 5:30 PM
- Auction pay-in: June 18, 2026 at 8:00 AM
- No corrections permitted for punching errors; members must exercise utmost care with security codes and rates
- The Exchange reserves the right to accept or reject any offer without assigning a reason
- Members should refer to the cutoff rate on the Auction Offer Entry Screen before submitting offers

## Regulatory Changes

Following implementation of the Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment, settlement is now routed through the Clearing Member of a Trading Member. The earlier restriction preventing trading members from offering securities in an auction for a settlement in which they had short-delivered has been removed.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and custodians must check their auction pay-in obligations for securities.
- Securities pay-in must be executed in depositories within depository cut-off timings on June 18, 2026.
- Members should refer to notices issued by respective departments regarding Corporate Actions (ex-rights, ex-bonus, ex-split, etc.) before placing auction offers.

## Important Dates

- **June 17, 2026**: Auction session (offer entry, matching, and initial reports)
- **June 18, 2026 at 8:00 AM**: Auction pay-in (securities delivery)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice applicable to trading members and custodians participating in Rolling Settlement DR-652/2026-27. The primary impact is on members with short delivery positions who must participate in the auction process. The removal of the restriction on offering securities in the same settlement where short delivery occurred provides additional flexibility to trading members under the updated Uniform Membership structure.
