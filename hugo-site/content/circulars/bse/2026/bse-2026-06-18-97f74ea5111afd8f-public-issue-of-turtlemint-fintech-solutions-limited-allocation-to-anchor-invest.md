## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 2,61,31,680 equity shares have been subscribed by Anchor Investors (AIs) in the public issue of Turtlemint Fintech Solutions Limited at a price of Rs. 152 per equity share on June 18, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: Turtlemint Fintech Solutions Limited
- Anchor Investor allocation: 2,61,31,680 equity shares
- Issue price for Anchor Investors: Rs. 152 per equity share
- Allocation date: June 18, 2026
- Notice issued by BSE's Listing Business Relationship Department

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine procedural notice informing trading members of completed anchor investor allocation ahead of the public subscription period.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed for awareness purposes. No specific compliance actions are required of members in response to this notice. The enclosed company announcement (Anchor_letter) provides further details for reference.

## Important Dates

- Anchor Investor allocation date: June 18, 2026
- Public issue subscription period: to be announced separately by the company

## Impact Assessment

The anchor investor allocation of over 2.61 crore shares at Rs. 152 per share establishes a price benchmark for the Turtlemint Fintech Solutions IPO. A successful anchor book (subscribed at the issue price) signals institutional confidence and may positively influence retail and non-institutional investor sentiment during the public subscription window. The total anchor allocation value is approximately Rs. 397.2 crore. Impact is limited to participants in this specific IPO and does not affect broader market operations.
