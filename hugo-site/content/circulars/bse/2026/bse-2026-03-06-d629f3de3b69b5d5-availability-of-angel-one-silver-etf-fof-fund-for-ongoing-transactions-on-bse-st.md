## Summary

BSE has informed members that ANGEL ONE SILVER ETF FOF FUND is reopening for ongoing subscription and redemption, along with SIP, STP, and SWP facilities on the BSE StAR MF Platform, effective March 06, 2026. This follows the earlier Notice No. 20260206-19.

## Key Points

- Angel One Silver ETF FOF Fund is available for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform from March 06, 2026.
- Both Regular Plan and Direct Plan (Growth options) are available.
- Facilities available include subscription, redemption, SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan).
- Both scheme plans are categorized under DEBT.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notice regarding fund availability on the platform.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of BSE StAR MF Platform may process ongoing transactions in Angel One Silver ETF FOF Fund schemes from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** March 06, 2026 — Fund reopens for ongoing subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP transactions.
