## Summary

BSE has notified that Halder Venture Limited will undergo a group change from XT Group to T Group effective January 19, 2026. This is a standard administrative change in the trading classification of the security.

## Key Points

- Company: Halder Venture Limited
- Scrip Code: 539854
- Current Group: XT (Trade-to-Trade)
- New Group: T (Trade-to-Trade)
- Effective Date: January 19, 2026
- Notice Number: 20260116-21
- Segment: Equity

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a reclassification of the trading group for the specific security.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are requested to note the group change
- Update internal systems to reflect the new group classification
- Ensure proper settlement and trading procedures for T Group from the effective date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 16, 2026
- **Effective Date**: January 19, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is an administrative group change for a single company. The change from XT to T group may affect trading and settlement procedures for this particular scrip. Trading members dealing with Halder Venture Limited should update their systems accordingly before the effective date.
