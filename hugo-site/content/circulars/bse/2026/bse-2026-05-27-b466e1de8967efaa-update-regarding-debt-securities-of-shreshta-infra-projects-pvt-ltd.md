## Summary

BSE has issued a notice that the Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of Shreshta Infra Projects Pvt Ltd (Scrip Code: 975332, ISIN: INE0CKK08019) will be suspended from trading effective Monday, June 01, 2026, due to the voluntary delisting of these NCDs. This is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20250130-34 dated January 30, 2025.

## Key Points

- Trading in Shreshta Infra Projects Pvt Ltd NCDs will be suspended from June 01, 2026
- Reason for suspension: voluntary delisting of NCDs
- Scrip Code: 975332
- ISIN: INE0CKK08019
- Total NCDs: 35,000 units; Face Value: ₹1,00,000 per NCD; Total Issue Size: ₹350 Crore
- This notice is a follow-up to Exchange Notice No. 20250130-34 dated January 30, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The NCD scrip (975332 / INE0CKK08019) of Shreshta Infra Projects Pvt Ltd is being removed from the tradable securities list on BSE's Debt Market Segment via voluntary delisting.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members are required to take note of the trading suspension effective June 01, 2026, and ensure no trades are executed in this scrip on or after that date. Members may contact Mr. Akash Shinde at +91 22 2272 8352 or debt.listing@bseindia.com for further clarifications.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** May 27, 2026
- **Suspension Effective Date:** Monday, June 01, 2026
- **Original Reference Notice:** 20250130-34 dated January 30, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The suspension affects holders and traders of Shreshta Infra Projects Pvt Ltd NCDs on BSE's Debt Market Segment. With an issue size of ₹350 Crore (35,000 NCDs at ₹1,00,000 face value each), the impact is limited to investors holding this specific ISIN. Post-suspension, secondary market liquidity in these NCDs on BSE will cease. Investors should plan their positions accordingly before June 01, 2026.
