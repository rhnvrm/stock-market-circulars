## Summary

The Indian Clearing Corporation Limited (ICCL) has published the client margin reporting schedule for June 2026 for the Commodity Derivatives (BCX) segment. The schedule specifies the last date for margin reporting and the corresponding penalty date for each trade date in June 2026.

## Key Points

- Covers all trading days in June 2026 from 01-06-2026 to 30-06-2026.
- Each trade date has a corresponding last date for margin reporting (typically 7 calendar days later) and a penalty date (the following business day).
- Applies specifically to the Commodity Derivatives (BCX) segment.
- Issued by ICCL on behalf of BSE.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine monthly schedule circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members of the Commodity Derivatives segment must submit client margin reports by the specified last date for each trade date.
- Failure to report by the last date will result in penalties levied on the penalty date.
- Members should ensure timely margin reporting to avoid penalties.
