## Summary

SEBI has issued interim order no. WTM/KV/CFID/CFID-SEC6/32431/2026-27 dated June 03, 2026, restraining Noticee No. 2 from buying, selling, or dealing in securities of Rajesh Exports Limited, either directly or indirectly, in any manner whatsoever, until further orders. BSE has notified all trading members to take note and ensure compliance.

## Key Points

- SEBI order no. WTM/KV/CFID/CFID-SEC6/32431/2026-27 issued on June 03, 2026
- Noticee No. 2 is restrained from all transactions (buy/sell/deal) in securities of Rajesh Exports Limited
- Restriction applies both directly and indirectly, in any manner whatsoever
- Order remains in effect until further orders from SEBI
- Full order copy is attached to the notice and available on SEBI's website (www.sebi.gov.in)

## Regulatory Changes

This is an interim regulatory enforcement action by SEBI under its investigation authority (CFID - Commodity and Financial Intermediaries Department). The order imposes a trading prohibition on a specific entity (Noticee No. 2) with respect to Rajesh Exports Limited securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- All trading members of BSE must take note of the SEBI interim order
- Trading members must ensure that the restrained party (Noticee No. 2) does not execute any transactions in Rajesh Exports Limited securities
- Members are advised to review the full order available on the SEBI website for complete details on the identity of Noticee No. 2 and any additional directions

## Important Dates

- **Order Date:** June 03, 2026
- **Effective Date:** June 03, 2026 (immediate effect from date of order)
- **Expiry:** Until further orders from SEBI

## Impact Assessment

This interim order signals an active SEBI investigation into Rajesh Exports Limited. The restraint on Noticee No. 2 restricts a specific entity from participating in the securities of this company. Trading members must exercise caution and conduct due diligence to ensure they do not inadvertently facilitate trades on behalf of the restrained party. The broader market impact depends on the identity and trading volume of Noticee No. 2, which is detailed in the full order. Such interim orders can signal potential adverse findings and may affect investor sentiment around Rajesh Exports Limited.
