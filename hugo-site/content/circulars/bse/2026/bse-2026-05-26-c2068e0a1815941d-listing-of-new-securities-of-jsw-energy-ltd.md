## Summary

BSE has admitted 7,61,90,476 equity shares of JSW Energy Ltd (Scrip Code: 533148, ISIN: INE121E01018) for trading effective May 27, 2026. These shares were allotted to Qualified Institutional Buyers (QIBs) pursuant to a Qualified Institutional Placement (QIP) at an issue price of Rs. 525/- per share.

## Key Points

- 7,61,90,476 new equity shares of face value Rs. 10/- each listed on BSE
- Shares allotted to QIBs via Qualified Institutional Placement (QIP)
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Issue price: Rs. 525/- per share
- Distribution numbers: 1757292261 to 1833482736
- Trading commences: Wednesday, May 27, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notice following completion of a QIP.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to update their systems to recognize the newly listed securities under Scrip Code 533148 effective May 27, 2026. For clarifications, trading members may contact Ms. Hetika Chandni at Tel. No. 022 2272 8772.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** May 25, 2026
- **Effective Date of Listing/Trading:** May 27, 2026 (Wednesday)

## Impact Assessment

The QIP results in a meaningful dilution of approximately 7.62 crore new shares for JSW Energy Ltd, raising capital at Rs. 525/- per share. The shares are pari-passu with existing equity, so no differential rights are created. Market impact is moderate given the size of the issuance; institutional investors (QIBs) are the allottees, limiting immediate retail market disruption. Trading members should note the new distribution number range for settlement purposes.
