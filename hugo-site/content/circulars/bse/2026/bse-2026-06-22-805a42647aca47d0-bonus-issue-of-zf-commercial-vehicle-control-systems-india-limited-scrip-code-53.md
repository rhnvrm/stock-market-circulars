## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that ZF Commercial Vehicle Control Systems India Limited (Scrip Code: 533023 / 133023) has fixed **24 June 2026** as the record date for a bonus issue. Shares will trade on an ex-bonus basis starting 24 June 2026 (Settlement No. DR-658/2026-2027). Approximately 94,837,920 equity shares will be allotted on 25 June 2026.

## Key Points

- **Bonus ratio:** 5 equity shares of Rs. 5/- each for every 1 equity share of Rs. 5/- held.
- **Record date:** 24 June 2026.
- **Ex-bonus date:** 24 June 2026 (Settlement No. DR-658/2026-2027).
- **Allotment date:** 25 June 2026 — approximately 94,837,920 new equity shares to be allotted.
- **Scrip codes:** 533023 (equity) / 133023.
- Notice issued by Assistant Vice President, Listing Compliance and Operations, Marian Dsouza.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory rule changes. This is a standard corporate action notification under BSE's listing compliance framework, requiring the exchange to inform trading members of the ex-bonus date and settlement details.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure client positions and orders reflect the ex-bonus adjustment from 24 June 2026 onwards.
- Settlement obligations for trades up to the record date must account for bonus entitlement eligibility.
- Members should refer to the attached company undertaking (Undertaking_ZF) and the company's letter dated 18 June 2026 for further details on share allotment.

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Circular Date | 22 June 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

The 5:1 bonus ratio is highly dilutive — the share count will increase sixfold from existing levels. The market price is expected to adjust proportionately on the ex-bonus date (24 June 2026). Existing shareholders on record as of 24 June 2026 will receive five additional shares for each share held. With ~94.8 million new shares being allotted, liquidity in the stock is expected to improve significantly post-allotment. Traders holding short positions or derivatives exposure should account for the price adjustment on the ex-date.
