## Summary

BSE has notified that certain securities, as listed in the attached Annexure I, have met the criteria for inclusion under the Short-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) framework. Securities have been placed under ST-ASM Stage I and Stage II, with corresponding increases in applicable margin requirements effective July 16, 2026. This action follows the ASM framework established under prior Exchange notices dated September 20, 2024, October 27, 2018, July 19, 2019, December 4, 2020, and September 25, 2023.

## Key Points

- Securities listed in Annexure I have satisfied criteria for ST-ASM Stage I inclusion.
- Securities listed in Annexure I have also satisfied criteria for ST-ASM Stage II inclusion.
- ST-ASM Stage I: applicable margin rate is 50% or existing margin, whichever is higher, capped at a maximum of 100%.
- ST-ASM Stage II: applicable margin rate is 100% or existing margin, whichever is higher, capped at a maximum of 100%.
- Margins apply to all open positions as on July 15, 2026 and new positions created from July 16, 2026 onward.
- ASM measures operate in conjunction with other prevailing surveillance measures.
- Inclusion under ASM is a market surveillance action and not to be construed as an adverse action against the concerned company or entity.
- Price bands for scrips exiting the ASM framework will revert to their pre-shortlisting levels, unless the scrip remains subject to another surveillance framework.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced; this notice is an operational update applying the existing ST-ASM framework (as revised under prior circulars) to a fresh set of securities identified per periodic review criteria.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must apply the revised margin rates (50% for Stage I, 100% for Stage II securities) on all open and new positions in the identified securities from the effective date. Members with queries may contact BSE Surveillance at bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 14, 2026
- Margin applicability: Effective July 16, 2026, covering open positions as on July 15, 2026 and new positions from July 16, 2026.

## Impact Assessment

The increased margin requirements (50%-100%) will raise the cost of trading and reduce leverage for market participants holding or trading the affected securities, likely dampening speculative activity and volatility in these names. Since the specific securities are listed only in the unattached Annexure I, the direct company-level impact cannot be assessed from this notice alone. The measure is procedural and recurring in nature, applied uniformly under BSE's standard surveillance framework.
