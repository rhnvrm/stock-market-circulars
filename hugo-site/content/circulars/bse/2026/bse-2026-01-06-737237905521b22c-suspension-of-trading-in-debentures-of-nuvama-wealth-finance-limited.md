## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in Non-Convertible Debentures of NUVAMA WEALTH FINANCE LIMITED (ISIN: INE918K07IX6, Code: 974081) effective January 9, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing the record date for redemption of these debentures. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to NUVAMA WEALTH FINANCE LIMITED debentures with ISIN INE918K07IX6
- Debenture code: NWFL-NIFTY 50 INDEX-27-1-26-PV (974081)
- Purpose: Redemption of Non-Convertible Debentures
- Trading members instructed to cease dealing in these securities
- Notice issued under DR-795/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action notice for debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from January 9, 2026
- Trading members must take note of the suspension and communicate to clients holding these securities
- All pending orders and positions should be managed before the suspension date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 6, 2026
- **Record Date**: January 9, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Date**: January 9, 2026 (No dealings from this date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders of this specific debenture series. Trading suspension is temporary and tied to the redemption process.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders will have their securities redeemed as per the terms. No trading allowed from the record date, preventing any last-minute transactions.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to ensure systems are updated to block trades in this ISIN and inform clients holding these debentures about the redemption process and trading suspension.
