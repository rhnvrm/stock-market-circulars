## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Navkar Share & Stock Brokers Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 111) will cease operations in the Currency derivative segment effective January 12, 2026, due to voluntary closure of business.

## Key Points

- Navkar Share & Stock Brokers Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 111) is voluntarily closing business
- Cessation applies to Currency derivative segment only
- Effective date: Monday, January 12, 2026
- Trading members have been notified of the closure

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a notification of a member's voluntary business closure.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for other members. This is an informational notice regarding one member's exit from the currency derivatives segment.

## Important Dates

- **January 9, 2026**: Notice date
- **January 12, 2026**: Effective date of business cessation in currency derivatives segment

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. This represents a single trading member's voluntary exit from the currency derivatives segment. Other market participants and trading operations remain unaffected. For clarifications, members can contact membership.ops@bseindia.com.
