## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of SHREE RAM TWISTEX LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544716) will be moved from the Trade for Trade (T Group) segment to the Rolling segment, where they will be classified under B Group. This change is effective from Tuesday, March 17, 2026, and is a continuation of an earlier notice dated February 27, 2026.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 544716 (SHREE RAM TWISTEX LIMITED)
- Transfer from Trade for Trade segment (T Group) to Rolling segment (B Group)
- Effective date: Tuesday, March 17, 2026
- Continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20260227-42 dated February 27, 2026
- Contact: Mr. Parag Jain, Tel. No. 022 2272 8685

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares of SHREE RAM TWISTEX LIMITED will no longer be traded under the Trade for Trade (T Group) settlement mechanism. From March 17, 2026, the shares will move to the Rolling settlement under B Group, allowing standard netting and carry-forward trading.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members should take note of the segment change and update their systems accordingly before the effective date.
- Members should inform their clients about the change in trading group, which may affect margin and settlement obligations.
- For queries, Trading Members may contact Mr. Parag Jain at Tel. No. 022 2272 8685.

## Important Dates

- **February 27, 2026**: Original notice (No. 20260227-42) issued regarding the group change
- **March 2, 2026**: Current notice date confirming the transfer details
- **March 17, 2026 (Tuesday)**: Effective date of transfer from T Group to B Group (Rolling segment)

## Impact Assessment

This change improves trading flexibility for SHREE RAM TWISTEX LIMITED shares. Moving from the Trade for Trade (T Group) to B Group under Rolling settlement means traders will no longer be required to deliver shares on a gross basis; netting of buy and sell positions will be permitted. This typically increases liquidity and reduces settlement burden for active traders. Investors should be aware that the stricter surveillance conditions associated with T Group trading will no longer apply from the effective date.
