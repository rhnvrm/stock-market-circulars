## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 62,89,308 new equity shares of IDEAFORGE TECHNOLOGY LIMITED (Scrip Code: 543932, ISIN: INE349Y01013), allotted to Qualified Institutional Buyers (QIBs) pursuant to a Qualified Institutional Placement (QIP), are listed and admitted for trading on the Exchange with effect from Tuesday, July 14, 2026.

## Key Points

- 62,89,308 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each allotted to QIBs under the QIP.
- These shares rank pari-passu with the existing equity shares of the company.
- Date of allotment: July 10, 2026.
- Issue price: Rs. 795/- per share.
- Distinctive numbers: 43396290 to 49685597.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing notice under BSE Listing Operations.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific action required from trading members beyond noting the new listing for trading purposes. Queries can be directed to Mr. Dhananjay Apte (Tel: 022-2272 8918).

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: July 10, 2026
- Listing/Trading Effective Date: July 14, 2026 (Tuesday)
- Notice Date: July 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market-wide impact. The listing marginally increases the free-float/tradable equity base of Ideaforge Technology Limited following the QIP allotment. Trading members should update systems to reflect the new distinctive number range for the additional shares.
