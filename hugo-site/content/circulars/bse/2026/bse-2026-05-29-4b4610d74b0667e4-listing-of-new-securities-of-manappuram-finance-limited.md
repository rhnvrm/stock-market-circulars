## Summary

BSE has listed 9,29,01,373 new equity shares of Manappuram Finance Limited (Scrip Code: 531213) issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis. These shares are permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Monday, June 1, 2026.

## Key Points

- 9,29,01,373 equity shares of face value Rs. 2/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 234/- per share (issue price Rs. 236/-)
- Issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 846434730 to 939336102
- ISIN: INE522D01027
- All newly listed shares are subject to a lock-in until November 30, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notice following a completed preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities and their eligibility to trade from June 1, 2026
- Lock-in restrictions must be observed: all 9,29,01,373 shares (Dist. Nos. 846434730 to 939336102) are locked in until November 30, 2026 and cannot be traded during this period

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** March 27, 2026
- **Trading Commencement:** June 1, 2026 (Monday)
- **Lock-in Expiry:** November 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds approximately 9.29 crore new equity shares to Manappuram Finance Limited's outstanding equity. However, since all newly listed shares are under lock-in until November 30, 2026, there is no immediate increase in free-float. Market impact is expected to be moderate; the preferential allotment signals capital raising from non-promoter investors. Post lock-in expiry, a potential increase in selling pressure from non-promoter allottees should be monitored.
