## Summary

BSE has notified trading members and custodians of an open offer under the SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 2011 for Purple Finance Limited. The acquirers — Allied Commodities Private Limited and Mr. Sandeep Jindal — along with four Persons Acting in Concert (PACs), are offering to purchase up to 1,76,48,152 equity shares (26% of the Emerging Voting Capital) at Rs. 55.00 per share payable in cash.

## Key Points

- **Acquirers:** Allied Commodities Private Limited (Acquirer 1) and Mr. Sandeep Jindal (Acquirer 2)
- **PACs:** Intellect Stock Broking Limited (PAC 1), Intellect Money Finvest Private Limited (PAC 2), Mr. Amitabh Chaturvedi (PAC 3), and AC Enterprises Private Limited (PAC 4)
- **Target Company:** Purple Finance Limited
- **Offer Price:** Rs. 55.00 per equity share (face value Rs. 10.00 each), payable in cash
- **Shares Targeted:** Up to 1,76,48,152 fully paid-up equity shares representing 26% of the Emerging Voting Capital
- **Regulatory Basis:** SEBI (SAST) Regulations 2011, Regulations 3 and 4
- **Letter of Offer:** Attached to the notice for perusal

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This offer is conducted under the existing SEBI mechanism for acquisition of shares through the Stock Exchange pursuant to Tender-Offers under Takeover, as per:
- SEBI Circular No. CIR/CFD/POLICYCELL/1/2015 dated April 13, 2015
- SEBI Circular No. CFD/DCR2/CIR/P/2016/131 dated December 09, 2016
- BSE Revised Guidelines (Notices: 20170210-16, 20190424-35, 20200528-32, 20201102-43, 20210825-62)

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and custodians must facilitate eligible public shareholders of Purple Finance Limited in tendering shares through the Acquisition Window mechanism on BSE.
- Participants must follow the Revised Guidelines of SEBI Circular No. CFD/DCR2/CIR/P/2016/131 and relevant BSE notices.
- Details of the Offer to Buy are available on the BSE website.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 29, 2026
- **Offer Opens:** Wednesday, July 01, 2026
- **Offer Closes:** Tuesday, July 14, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Public shareholders of Purple Finance Limited are directly impacted and must evaluate whether to tender their shares at the offer price of Rs. 55.00 during the two-week window. The offer targets 26% of the Emerging Voting Capital, which represents a significant stake acquisition and could lead to a change in control. Market participants should note the acquisition window dates and ensure client orders are placed accordingly within the BSE Acquisition Window mechanism.
