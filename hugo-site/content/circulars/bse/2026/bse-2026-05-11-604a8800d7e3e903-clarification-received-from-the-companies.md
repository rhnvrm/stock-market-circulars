## Summary

BSE's Surveillance department (DOSS) issued Notice No. 20260511-22 on 11 May 2026 regarding significant price/volume movement observed in 12 listed companies. The Exchange had written to these companies seeking relevant information to keep trading members and investors informed. Eight companies have submitted their replies (attached to the notice), while four companies' replies are still awaited.

## Key Points

- BSE observed significant price/volume movement in 12 scrips and initiated clarification requests under its surveillance mandate
- Eight companies have responded with replies attached to the notice
- Four companies have not yet responded and their replies are still awaited
- The notice was issued by the Surveillance department (DOSS) and signed by Deputy Vice President Sanjay Jain and Manager Shreya Ghosh

## Companies That Submitted Replies

- Autoline Industries Ltd (BSE Security Code: 532797)
- DMR Engineering Ltd (BSE Security Code: 543410)
- Gconnect Logitech and Supply Chain Ltd (BSE Security Code: 544156)
- Godrej Industries Ltd (BSE Security Code: 500164)
- Tejas Networks Ltd (BSE Security Code: 540595)
- Titan Intech Ltd (BSE Security Code: 521005)
- Universal Cables Ltd (BSE Security Code: 504212)
- Vindhya Telelinks Ltd (BSE Security Code: 517015)

## Companies With Replies Awaited

- Adhata Global Ltd (BSE Security Code: 531286)
- Milgrey Finance & Investments Ltd (BSE Security Code: 511018)
- Takyon Networks Ltd (BSE Security Code: 544471)
- ZR2 Bioenergy Ltd (BSE Security Code: 506640)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard surveillance-driven disclosure notice under BSE's existing framework for monitoring unusual price/volume activity in listed securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies that have received BSE queries regarding unusual price/volume movement are required to submit timely clarifications to the Exchange
- Adhata Global Ltd, Milgrey Finance & Investments Ltd, Takyon Networks Ltd, and ZR2 Bioenergy Ltd must submit their pending replies to BSE
- Trading members and investors should review the attached company replies before transacting in these scrips

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 11 May 2026
- No specific deadline mentioned for pending replies; companies with awaited replies should respond promptly to avoid further surveillance action

## Impact Assessment

This notice has a moderate impact on the 12 named companies and their investors. For the eight companies that have responded, the attached clarifications may help explain recent price/volume movements and could influence investor sentiment positively or negatively depending on the content. For the four companies with replies still awaited, continued non-response may attract additional scrutiny, potential surveillance measures, or trading restrictions. Investors in these scrips should review the company clarifications carefully before making trading decisions.
