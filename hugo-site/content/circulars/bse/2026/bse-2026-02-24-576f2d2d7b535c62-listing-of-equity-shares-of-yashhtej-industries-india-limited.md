## Summary

BSE Limited has notified trading members that the equity shares of Yashhtej Industries (India) Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the BSE SME segment. The specific listing date and full security details will be communicated through a subsequent separate notice.

## Key Points

- Yashhtej Industries (India) Limited equity shares are approved for listing on BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544708
- Trading Symbol: YASHHTEJ
- ISIN: INE1O6P01016
- Exact listing date to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Nitinkumar Pujari, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing admission notice under BSE SME segment rules.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the upcoming listing of YASHHTEJ (Scrip Code: 544708) and await the follow-up notice for the confirmed listing date and detailed security information before initiating trades.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** February 24, 2026
- **Listing Date:** To be announced via a separate notice

## Impact Assessment

Limited immediate market impact as this is a preliminary listing intimation for a new SME scrip. The actual market impact will be assessable only after the listing date notice is issued and trading commences. Trading members dealing in the SME segment should monitor BSE for the follow-up circular confirming the listing date.
