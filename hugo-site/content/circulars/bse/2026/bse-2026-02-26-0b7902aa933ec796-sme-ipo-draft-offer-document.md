## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document of Lorven International Limited, filed on February 25, 2026, in compliance with Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations. The document is hosted on the BSE SME website for a minimum period of 21 days to invite public comments.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Lorven International Limited on February 25, 2026
- Document hosted on BSE SME website for a minimum of 21 days from the notice date (February 26, 2026)
- Public comments are invited and must be submitted within the 21-day window
- Comments to be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Draft Prospectus available at the BSE SME website: https://www.bsesme.com/download/315269/SME_IPO%20InPrinciple/Draft_Prospectus_Lorven_20260225202720.pdf

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The disclosure is made pursuant to existing requirements under Schedule VI of the SEBI (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations (ICDR Regulations).

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants, investors, and other stakeholders wishing to submit comments on the Draft Offer Document must do so within 21 days from February 26, 2026
- Comments must be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Draft Offer Document Filing Date:** February 25, 2026
- **BSE Notice Date:** February 26, 2026
- **Public Comment Deadline:** On or before March 19, 2026 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This circular is of moderate impact, limited to participants in the SME segment. It signals the upcoming IPO of Lorven International Limited on the BSE SME platform. Prospective investors and analysts should review the Draft Prospectus and submit any comments within the stipulated timeframe. No broader market or trading operations are affected.
