## Summary

BSE has authorized trading members to trade in 636 new equity shares of Grasim Industries Ltd (Scrip Code: 500300) effective January 30, 2026. These shares result from the conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid shares of Rs. 2/- each, originally issued on a rights basis. The new shares carry the same rights and privileges as existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 636 new equity shares of Grasim Industries Ltd approved for trading
- Shares arise from conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid shares
- Face value: Rs. 2/- per share
- Original issuance: Rights basis
- Scrip Code: 500300
- ISIN: INE047A01021
- Distinctive Numbers: 680459639 to 680460274
- New shares are pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for additional securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are permitted to trade in these securities through normal trading channels
- No special compliance requirements beyond standard equity trading procedures

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** January 29, 2026
- **Trading Commencement Date:** January 30, 2026 (Friday)

## Impact Assessment

The impact of this listing is minimal. The addition of 636 equity shares represents a negligible increase to Grasim Industries' outstanding share capital. This is a routine administrative action completing the conversion process of partly paid shares issued under a rights offering. Trading members can execute orders in these securities without any restrictions, and the pari-passu status ensures these shares have identical rights to existing shares. No material effect on market operations or stock price is anticipated.
