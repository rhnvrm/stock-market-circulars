## Summary

BSE Ltd. has admitted three entities as Trading Members effective 11th June 2026. The admissions include one new membership and two additional segment memberships across equity derivatives and debt segments.

## Key Points

- Blue Sapphire Global Markets LLP (Member No. 6992) admitted as a new trading member in Cash/Equity and Derivatives segments
- G. Das Capital Markets Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 768) granted additional segment membership for Equity Derivatives
- Angel One Limited (Member No. 612) granted additional segment membership for the Debt segment
- All memberships are subject to the Rules, Bye-laws and Regulations of the Exchange

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine administrative notice informing existing trading members of new admissions.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance action required from existing trading members. The notice is purely informational. Queries may be directed to membership.ops@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- Admission effective date: 11th June 2026
- Notice date: 12th June 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The addition of new trading members, particularly Angel One Limited's expansion into the Debt segment, marginally increases liquidity providers in those segments. No operational changes are required from existing members.
