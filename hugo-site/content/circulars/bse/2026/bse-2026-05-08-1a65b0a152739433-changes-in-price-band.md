## Summary

BSE (Notice No. 20260508-35) has revised price bands for 26 equity scrips effective May 11, 2026. The revisions cover a range of band levels — 1%, 2%, 5%, 10%, and 20% — applied individually based on each security's surveillance classification. Trading members must note the updated limits before the market opens on May 11, 2026.

## Key Points

- Price bands revised for 26 scrips across BSE Equity segment, effective May 11, 2026
- Band levels revised to: 1% (1 scrip), 2% (2 scrips), 5% (11 scrips), 10% (8 scrips), and 20% (4 scrips)
- Action initiated by the Department of Surveillance & Supervision (DOSS)
- No attachments; all details contained within the notice
- Members may seek clarification at bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their systems to reflect the revised price bands for all 26 listed scrips before the start of trading on May 11, 2026
- Orders placed outside the revised band limits will be rejected by the exchange trading system
- Members with queries should contact BSE Surveillance at bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** May 8, 2026
- **Effective Date:** May 11, 2026 (all revised price bands come into force)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine surveillance action by BSE's DOSS department. The tightening of price bands (e.g., to 1% or 2%) for certain scrips such as Hindusthan Insulators & Industries Ltd and Aksh Optifibre Ltd suggests heightened scrutiny or unusual price movement concerns. Scrips moved to 20% bands (HRS Aluglaze, Mehul Telecom, Omnitech Engineering, Vivid Mercantile) may be experiencing relaxation of prior restrictions. Traders holding positions in these 26 securities should review order strategies to avoid rejections under the new band limits effective May 11.
