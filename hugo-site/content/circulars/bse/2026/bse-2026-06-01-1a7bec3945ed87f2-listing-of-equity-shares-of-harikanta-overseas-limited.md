## Summary

Harikanta Overseas Limited's equity shares will be listed and admitted to dealings on BSE effective Tuesday, June 2, 2026, under the 'MT' Group of Securities in the SME segment. The scrip will be placed in the Trade-for-Trade segment for 10 trading days in accordance with SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012.

## Key Points

- **Scrip Code:** 544769 | **ISIN:** INE1UYW01010 | **Scrip ID:** HARIKANTA
- **Total Securities:** 9,865,740 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each, fully paid up
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 91/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- + premium Rs. 81/-)
- **Market Lot:** 1,200 equity shares
- **Date of Allotment:** May 29, 2026
- Trading will be in dematerialised form only
- The scrip will be part of the Special Pre-open Session (SPOS) on June 2, 2026
- Market Maker: Aftertrade Broking Private Limited (SEBI Reg. No.: INZ000155638)
- Registrar: Bigshare Services Private Limited (SEBI Reg. No.: INR000001385)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The listing is governed by existing SEBI guidelines including:
- SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 (January 20, 2012) mandating Trade-for-Trade segment for 10 days post-listing
- SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000 requiring trading exclusively in dematerialised form
- SEBI notice 20120216-29 governing Special Pre-open Sessions for IPOs and relisted scrips

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade this scrip only in dematerialised form
- All trades must be in the minimum market lot of 1,200 equity shares
- Members must participate in the Special Pre-open Session (SPOS) on June 2, 2026, before regular trading commences
- Lock-in details as per Annexure I and shareholding pattern as per Annexure II must be observed
- Corporate actions for lock-in period marking in depository records have been executed by the company

## Important Dates

- **Listing Effective Date:** Tuesday, June 2, 2026
- **SPOS Date:** Tuesday, June 2, 2026
- **Trade-for-Trade Period:** 10 trading days from June 2, 2026
- **Date of Allotment in Public Issue:** May 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard SME IPO listing with moderate market impact. The Trade-for-Trade restriction for 10 trading days limits speculative activity and ensures delivery-based settlement, reducing short-term volatility risk. The minimum market lot of 1,200 shares at an issue price of Rs. 91/- implies a minimum trade value of approximately Rs. 1,09,200, which filters retail participation and targets institutional and high-net-worth investors. The listing is confined to the BSE SME platform and does not affect broader market indices or trading operations.
