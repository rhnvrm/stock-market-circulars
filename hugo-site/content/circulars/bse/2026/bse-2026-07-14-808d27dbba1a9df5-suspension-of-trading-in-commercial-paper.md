## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that three companies have fixed record dates for redemption of their Commercial Paper (CP) instruments. Trading members are advised not to deal in the CP of these companies from the specified dates as the instruments approach maturity/redemption.

## Key Points

- ECL Finance Limited (Scrip Code: 731519, Scrip ID: EFL-21-7-26-CP, ISIN: INE804I14YN6) — Record/Ex Date: 20 July 2026, Settlement Number: 675/2026-2027
- BOBCARD Limited (Scrip Code: 731529, Scrip ID: BL-21-7-26-CP, ISIN: INE027214936) — Record/Ex Date: 20 July 2026, Settlement Number: 675/2026-2027
- Home Credit India Finance Private Limited (Scrip Code: 731555, Scrip ID: HCIFPL-24-07-26-CP, ISIN: INE172V14091) — Record/Ex Date: 23 July 2026, Settlement Number: 678/2026-2027
- Trading members are advised not to deal in these Commercial Papers on and from the respective record/ex dates.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is a routine operational notice under Listing Operations/Corporate Actions for debt segment instruments approaching redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of the Exchange must refrain from dealing in the listed Commercial Paper scrips from the specified record/ex dates onward, in line with standard redemption processing for debt instruments.

## Important Dates

- 20 July 2026: Record/Ex Date and trading suspension for ECL Finance Limited (EFL-21-7-26-CP) and BOBCARD Limited (BL-21-7-26-CP)
- 23 July 2026: Record/Ex Date and trading suspension for Home Credit India Finance Private Limited (HCIFPL-24-07-26-CP)

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and traders of these specific Commercial Paper instruments in the debt segment. Trading suspension ahead of redemption is a standard, low-risk administrative process that does not affect equity markets or broader trading activity.
