## Summary

BSE has notified trading members and custodians of the opening of an Offer to Buy (Acquisition Window) for Zydus Lifesciences Limited's buyback of equity shares through the tender offer route. The buyback is priced at INR 1,260 per share for an aggregate amount not exceeding INR 1,100 crore, open from June 4 to June 10, 2026.

## Key Points

- Zydus Lifesciences Limited proposes to buyback up to 87,30,158 (eighty-seven lakh thirty thousand one hundred and fifty-eight) fully paid-up equity shares of face value INR 1/- each
- Buyback price: INR 1,260 per equity share, payable in cash
- Maximum aggregate buyback size: INR 1,100,00,00,000 (INR 1,100 crore), excluding transaction costs
- Shares to be bought back represent 0.87% of the total paid-up share capital
- Buyback size represents 5.16% (standalone) and 3.84% (consolidated) of aggregate paid-up equity capital and free reserves as at March 31, 2026
- Buyback is within the statutory 10% board approval route limit under Section 68 of the Companies Act and Regulation 5 of the Buyback Regulations
- Offer is on a proportionate basis from equity shareholders/beneficial owners as on the Record Date (May 29, 2026)
- Letter of Offer is attached to the notice

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This offer is governed by existing SEBI circulars:
- SEBI circular CIR/CFD/POLICYCELL/1/2015 dated April 13, 2015
- SEBI circular CFD/DCR2/CIR/P/2016/131 dated December 9, 2016
- BSE notices: 20170210-16, 20190424-35, 20200528-32, 20201102-43, 20210825-62 (Revised Guidelines for acquisition through Stock Exchange under Tender Offers)

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and custodians must facilitate the tender offer for eligible shareholders
- Offer details and participation guidelines are available on the BSE website (www.bseindia.com)
- All market participants must adhere to the Revised Guidelines per SEBI circular CFD/DCR2/CIR/P/2016/131 and BSE notice 20170210-16 and 20190424-35

## Important Dates

- **Record Date:** May 29, 2026 (eligibility date for shareholders)
- **Offer Opens:** Thursday, June 4, 2026
- **Offer Closes:** Wednesday, June 10, 2026
- **Notice Date:** June 2, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a significant corporate action for Zydus Lifesciences Limited shareholders. Eligible shareholders as on May 29, 2026 can tender their shares at a premium price of INR 1,260 per share through the BSE acquisition window. The total buyback of up to INR 1,100 crore is material but within SEBI's board approval route limits. Trading members and custodians must ensure their systems are ready to process tender orders between June 4–10, 2026. Market participants should note the short offer window of only seven days.
