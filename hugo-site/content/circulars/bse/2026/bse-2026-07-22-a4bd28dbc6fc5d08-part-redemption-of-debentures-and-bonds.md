## Summary

BSE has notified trading members of record dates fixed by seven companies for the purpose of payment of interest and/or part redemption of their non-convertible debentures (NCDs). Following the record dates, the debentures will trade at a reduced face value from the specified ex-dates.

## Key Points

- Seven NCD series across seven issuers have fixed record dates for interest payment/part redemption.
- U.P. Power Corporation Limited (UPPCL-8.97%-15-2-27-PVT, ISIN INE540P07103): Record date 30/07/2026, Ex-date 30/07/2026, reduced face value Rs. 500,000/- per debenture.
- FRIEZA 06 2025 (FRIEZA-9.90%-17-12-26-PTC, ISIN INE26SJ15017): Record date 01/08/2026, Ex-date 31/07/2026, reduced face value Rs. 0.1532 per debenture.
- Akara Capital Advisors Private Limited (ACAPL-12.50%-17-01-27-PVT, ISIN INE08XP07449): Record date 02/08/2026, Ex-date 31/07/2026, reduced face value Rs. 3846.16 per debenture.
- Samunnati Finance Private Limited (SFPL-12.50%-27-9-26-PVT, ISIN INE551U07332): Record date 12/08/2026, Ex-date 12/08/2026, reduced face value Rs. 4166.59 per debenture.
- InCred Financial Services Limited (IFSL-9.90%-25-11-27-PVT, ISIN INE321N07475): Record date 13/08/2026, Ex-date 13/08/2026, reduced face value Rs. 41,666.69 per debenture.
- Aptus Finance India Private Limited (AFIPL-8.95%-RESET RATE-28-2-29, ISIN INE04MH07042): Record date 13/08/2026, Ex-date 13/08/2026, reduced face value Rs. 62,500/- per debenture.
- Greaves Finance Limited (GFL-10.50%-29-9-27-PVT, ISIN INE1QWF07014): Record date 13/08/2026, Ex-date 13/08/2026, reduced face value Rs. 54,163/- per debenture.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a routine operational notice under existing listing and debt settlement rules for corporate actions on NCDs.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must ensure that trades in the listed debentures reflect the reduced face value effective from the respective ex-dates, and settle transactions per the settlement dates/DR (deal reference) periods indicated for each ISIN.

## Important Dates

- 30/07/2026: Record date and ex-date for UPPCL NCD (INE540P07103).
- 31/07/2026: Ex-date for FRIEZA and Akara Capital NCDs.
- 01/08/2026: Record date for FRIEZA NCD (INE26SJ15017).
- 02/08/2026: Record date for Akara Capital NCD (INE08XP07449).
- 12/08/2026: Record date and ex-date for Samunnati Finance NCD (INE551U07332).
- 13/08/2026: Record date and ex-date for InCred Financial Services, Aptus Finance India, and Greaves Finance NCDs.

## Impact Assessment

This notice has limited market-wide impact, being confined to the debt segment and specific bondholders/trading members dealing in these particular NCD ISINs. It ensures accurate settlement and pricing of debentures following partial redemption, but does not affect equity markets or broader trading activity.
