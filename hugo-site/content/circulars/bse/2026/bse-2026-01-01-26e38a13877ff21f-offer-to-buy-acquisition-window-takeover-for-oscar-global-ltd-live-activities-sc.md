## Summary

BSE has notified trading members and custodian members about the schedule for the Offer to Buy (Acquisition Window/Takeover) for Oscar Global Ltd (OSCARGLO, Scrip Code: 530713). The offer will run from January 2, 2026 to January 15, 2026, offering to acquire up to 8,58,000 equity shares at Rs. 12.15 per share. The circular provides detailed session timings, bidding categories, platform access URLs, and contact information for trading operations.

## Key Points

- **Security**: Oscar Global Ltd (Symbol: OSCARGLO, ISIN: INE473F01010, Scrip Code: 530713)
- **Offer Size**: Up to 8,58,000 equity shares
- **Offer Price**: Rs. 12.15 per share
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10.00
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Opening Date**: Friday, January 2, 2026
- **Closing Date**: Thursday, January 15, 2026
- **Bid Offering Period**: January 2-15, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Give-up/Take-up Period**: January 2-14, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Last Day Give-up**: January 15, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Last Day Take-up**: January 15, 2026 (9:15 AM to 4:00 PM)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This circular provides operational instructions for a specific corporate action.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure investor UCC is registered/updated with correct PAN number and category in the Exchange UCC database
- Trading members can only bid for investors having shares in demat form
- Members and custodians must use designated iBBS and RTRMS platforms for bidding
- Members must refer to Exchange Notices 20150930-1 (September 30, 2015) and 20170203-29 (February 3, 2017) for operational instructions
- Members and custodians to note that if no securities are accepted, no End of Issue files will be generated in Extranet

## Important Dates

- **January 2, 2026**: Offer opens; bid offering and give-up/take-up period begins
- **January 14, 2026**: Last day for regular give-up/take-up (until 3:30 PM)
- **January 15, 2026**: Offer closes; last day give-up (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM); last day take-up extended until 4:00 PM

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a company-specific takeover offer affecting only Oscar Global Ltd shareholders. The offer size of 8,58,000 shares is relatively small.

**Operational Impact**: Medium for participating members - Trading members and custodians handling Oscar Global Ltd clients will need to process bids through the iBBS/RTRMS platforms during the specified period.

**Investor Categories Eligible**: Foreign Institutional Investors (FII), Financial Institutions (FI), Mutual Funds (MF), Insurance Companies (IC), Other QIBs, Non-Institutional Buyers, Companies, and Individuals.

**Platform Access**: Multiple access methods provided (Internet and Leased Line) for iBBS, RTRMS, and Extranet platforms. Contact details provided for trading operations support and password resets.
