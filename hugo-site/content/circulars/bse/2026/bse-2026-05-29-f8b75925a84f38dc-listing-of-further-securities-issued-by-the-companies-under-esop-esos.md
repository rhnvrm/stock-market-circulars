## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that further new securities issued by 22 companies under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP/ESOS) are listed and admitted for trading on the Exchange effective Monday, June 01, 2026. None of the newly listed shares are subject to any lock-in period.

## Key Points

- 22 companies have new ESOP/ESOS shares listed effective June 01, 2026
- No lock-in restrictions apply to any of the newly listed securities
- Largest allotment by volume: Meesho Limited (52,48,913 shares, ISIN INE0VDM01015)
- Largest allotment among traditional listed firms: Infosys Limited (7,01,328 shares, ISIN INE009A01021)
- Face values range from ₹1 to ₹10 across the listed companies

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice informing trading members of newly listable ESOP/ESOS securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are requested to take note of the new securities and their respective ISIN codes, scrip codes, share counts, and distribution number ranges as listed in the notice.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** May 29, 2026
- **Effective Date for Trading:** Monday, June 01, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing of ESOP/ESOS shares is a routine corporate action with low market impact. The incremental share additions are small relative to each company's total outstanding shares. Meesho Limited has the largest absolute addition at ~52.5 lakh shares. No lock-in periods apply, so shares are immediately tradeable. Market participants should update their records for the new distribution number ranges for each affected scrip.
