## Summary

BSE has permitted trading in 14,765 new equity shares of Fusion Finance Limited (Scrip Code: 543652) arising from conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up shares of Rs. 10/- each issued on rights basis. Trading is permitted with effect from Thursday, February 05, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: Fusion Finance Limited
- Scrip Code: 543652
- Number of new shares: 14,765 equity shares
- Face value: Rs. 10/- each
- Origin: Conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up shares issued on rights basis
- ISIN: INE139R01012
- Distinctive Numbers: 161351031 to 161365795
- Status: Pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification for new securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are permitted to trade in these new securities from the effective date
- The new shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: February 04, 2026
- **Trading Commencement Date**: February 05, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine corporate action involving conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid shares. The small quantity of 14,765 shares represents a routine rights issue conversion and is unlikely to have significant impact on trading volumes or stock price. Trading members can commence trading in these securities from February 05, 2026.
