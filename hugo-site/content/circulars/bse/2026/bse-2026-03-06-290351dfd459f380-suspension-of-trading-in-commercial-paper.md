## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members that the commercial paper of Julius Baer Capital (India) Private Limited (Scrip Code: 730733, ISIN: INE824H14SZ2) is set to mature on March 9, 2026. Accordingly, trading in this commercial paper is suspended with effect from March 9, 2026.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code 730733 (JBCPL-10-3-26-CP) will mature on March 9, 2026
- ISIN: INE824H14SZ2
- Trading members are advised not to deal in this commercial paper from March 9, 2026 onwards
- Reference: DR-835/2025-2026
- Notice issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President – Listing Operations (CRD)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for the maturity and consequent suspension of trading in a commercial paper instrument.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must cease all dealings in the commercial paper of Julius Baer Capital (India) Private Limited (Scrip Code: 730733, ISIN: INE824H14SZ2) with effect from March 9, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** March 6, 2026
- **Suspension Effective Date (Maturity Date):** March 9, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to trading members and investors holding or intending to trade in this specific commercial paper. As this is a routine maturity event, there is no broader market or systemic impact. Holders of the instrument should expect settlement/redemption as per the maturity terms.
