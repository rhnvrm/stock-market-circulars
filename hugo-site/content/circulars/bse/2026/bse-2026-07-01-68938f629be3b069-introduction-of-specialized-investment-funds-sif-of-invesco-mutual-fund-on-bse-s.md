## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that, pursuant to SEBI's regulatory framework for Specialized Investment Funds (SIF) under circular SEBI/HO/IMD/IMD-PoD-1/P/CIR/2025/26 dated February 27, 2025, INVESCO Mutual Fund's Specialized Investment Funds have been made available on the BSE StAR MF Platform. INVESCO MUTUAL FUND_SIF has been activated with effect from July 1, 2026, and the Summit Equity Long-Short Fund SIF - NFO scheme will be activated for order routing from July 2, 2026.

## Key Points

- INVESCO MUTUAL FUND_SIF activated on BSE StAR MF Platform effective July 1, 2026
- Summit Equity Long-Short Fund SIF - NFO scheme activated for order routing effective July 2, 2026
- NRI and Minor investor transactions are permitted under INVESCO MUTUAL FUND_SIF schemes via BSE StAR MF
- NRI applications are not accepted from investors belonging to: Democratic Republic of Korea, Iran, Myanmar, United States of America, and Canada
- Detailed transaction acceptance process for SIF provided in Annexure III
- Annexure I covers the regulatory framework for SIF; Annexure II covers monitoring of minimum investment threshold under SIF

## Regulatory Changes

This notice operationalizes SEBI's Regulatory Framework for Specialized Investment Funds (SEBI/HO/IMD/IMD-PoD-1/P/CIR/2025/26 dated February 27, 2025) by enabling SIF scheme trading through the BSE StAR MF Platform for INVESCO Mutual Fund.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must adhere to the detailed transaction acceptance process for SIF as outlined in Annexure III
- Members must monitor and comply with minimum investment threshold requirements per Annexure II
- Members must restrict NRI applications from investors in the listed non-acceptance countries
- Queries may be raised via the CRS portal (https://bsecrs.bseindia.com) or the Mutual Fund Helpdesk (022-45720450 / 022-45720650, 8:00 a.m. to 7:00 p.m. on business days)

## Important Dates

- July 1, 2026: INVESCO MUTUAL FUND_SIF activated on BSE StAR MF Platform
- July 2, 2026: Summit Equity Long-Short Fund SIF - NFO scheme activated for order routing

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational/product enablement notice for mutual fund distributors and trading members using the BSE StAR MF Platform. It expands available mutual fund product offerings (SIF category) but has no direct bearing on listed equity securities or broader market trading activity.
