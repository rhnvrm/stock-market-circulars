## Summary

BSE has updated the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework effective January 16, 2026. Four new securities are being added to ESM Stage I, one security (PH Capital Ltd) is being moved to a higher ESM stage, and no securities are moving out of the framework. The circular includes a consolidated list of 28+ securities currently under ESM surveillance across different stages.

## Key Points

- 4 new securities shortlisted for ESM Stage I framework
- 1 security (PH Capital Ltd) moving to higher ESM stage
- No securities moving to lower ESM stage or exiting ESM framework
- Changes effective from January 16, 2026
- Consolidated list includes securities in ESM Stage I and Stage II

## Securities Added to ESM Stage I

**New Additions (Effective Jan 16, 2026):**

1. **Global Ocean Logistics India Ltd** (544665, INE1ITY01011) - SME Scrip
2. **HCKK Ventures Ltd** (539224, INE345Q01017)
3. **Mangalam Industrial Finance Ltd** (537800, INE717C01025)
4. **Rukmani Devi Garg Agro Impex Ltd** (544552, INE0S5T01017) - SME Scrip

## Securities Moving to Higher ESM Stage

**Stage Migration (Upward):**

- **PH Capital Ltd** (500143, INE160F01013) - Moving to higher ESM stage

## Securities Moving to Lower ESM Stage

- Nil

## Securities Exiting ESM Framework

- Nil

## Impact Assessment

**Trading Impact:**
- Securities under ESM framework face enhanced surveillance and may have trade-to-trade settlement
- Investors in the 4 newly added securities should be aware of increased monitoring and potential trading restrictions
- PH Capital Ltd shareholders face stricter surveillance as the security moves to a higher ESM stage

**Market Implications:**
- ESM framework aims to alert investors about securities with unusual price movements or market behavior
- Enhanced surveillance can impact liquidity and trading volumes
- Securities remain under ESM until they meet exit criteria as per BSE regulations
