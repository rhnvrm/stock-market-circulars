## Summary

BSE has issued an update to its Additional Surveillance Measure (ASM) framework applicable to companies undergoing the Insolvency Resolution Process (IRP) under the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code (IBC). Certain securities have been newly added to this surveillance framework (Annexure I), while others have been removed (Annexure II), with both changes effective July 02, 2026. A consolidated list of all securities currently under the framework is provided in Annexure III.

## Key Points

- Securities meeting the IBC-related surveillance criteria are placed under the Additional Surveillance Measure effective July 02, 2026 (Annexure I).
- Securities that no longer meet the criteria are being moved out of the framework effective July 02, 2026 (Annexure II).
- A consolidated list of all securities currently under the framework is attached as Annexure III.
- This framework operates alongside all other surveillance measures imposed by the Exchange from time to time.
- Inclusion under this framework is purely a market surveillance measure and should not be interpreted as an adverse action against the company.
- Securities exiting the framework will have their price band reinstated to the level applicable before shortlisting, unless they remain subject to another surveillance framework, in which case that framework's price band applies.

## Regulatory Changes

This notice updates the ongoing Additional Surveillance Measure framework for IBC-related companies, first introduced via Exchange notice no. 20180608-34 (June 08, 2018) and subsequently updated via notices dated May 20, 2022, February 10, 2023, and September 05, 2025. No new regulatory framework is introduced; this is a periodic review update to the list of covered securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must note the updated list of securities under the IBC surveillance framework and apply corresponding trading restrictions/price band adjustments. Queries can be directed to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **July 02, 2026**: Effective date for both the addition of new securities (Annexure I) and removal of securities (Annexure II) from the IBC surveillance framework.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to the specific securities listed in the annexures (not identified in the extracted text), which will see changes in trading restrictions and price bands. Securities newly added face constrained price bands and heightened surveillance, while those exiting revert to normal price band conditions unless covered by another surveillance measure. This is a routine, periodic administrative update with no broad market-wide effect.
