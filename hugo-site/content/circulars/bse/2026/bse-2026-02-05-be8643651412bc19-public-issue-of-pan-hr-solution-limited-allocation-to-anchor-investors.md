## Summary

PAN HR Solution Limited has finalized allocation of 5,37,600 equity shares to anchor investors at ₹78 per share (face value ₹10, premium ₹68) on February 05, 2026. The total amount raised from anchor investors is ₹4,19,32,800. Three institutional investors participated in the anchor book, with no applications received from domestic mutual funds.

## Key Points

- Total anchor allocation: 5,37,600 equity shares at ₹78 per share
- Face value: ₹10 per share with premium of ₹68 per share
- Total funds raised from anchors: ₹4.19 crore
- Three anchor investors participated: Rajasthan Global Private Limited (26.19%), LRSD Securities Private Limited (25.89%), and INTI Capital VCC - INTI Capital I (47.92%)
- No applications received from domestic mutual funds
- Board meeting held on February 05, 2026 to finalize allocation

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a standard anchor investor allocation disclosure as per SEBI IPO regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Disclosure of anchor investor allocation details to stock exchange
- Public dissemination of anchor allocation information before issue opening
- Compliance with SEBI ICDR Regulations for anchor investor participation

## Important Dates

- **February 05, 2026**: Board meeting date for anchor allocation finalization
- **February 05, 2026**: Circular issue date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The successful anchor allocation at ₹78 per share provides a price discovery benchmark for the upcoming public issue. The participation of three institutional investors demonstrates institutional interest, though absence of mutual fund participation is notable.

**Investor Impact**: The anchor allocation represents 100% subscription of the anchor portion, which typically indicates positive sentiment. Retail and other investors can use the anchor price as reference for the main issue bidding.

**Operational Impact**: Low - Standard IPO process milestone with no operational changes required for market participants.
