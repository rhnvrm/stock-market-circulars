## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that further new securities issued under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) / Employee Stock Option Schemes (ESOS) by 10 listed companies will be listed and admitted for trading with effect from Friday, July 24, 2026.

## Key Points

- Ten companies have new ESOP/ESOS shares listed: AAVAS Financiers Limited (2,244 shares), AXIS Bank Limited (116,524 shares), Clean Max Enviro Energy Solutions Limited (9,220 shares), CreditAccess Grameen Limited (24,375 shares), Five-Star Business Finance Limited (8,180 shares), HDFC Bank Limited (3,815,292 shares), Kirloskar Oil Engines Limited (126,354 shares), Restaurant Brands Asia Limited (271,147 shares), RMC Switchgears Limited (5,020 shares), and Tata Motors Limited (211,346 shares)
- Each company's shares carry distinctive number ranges as specified in the notice
- No shares are listed under lock-in for any of the companies (marked NA)
- Face values vary by company, ranging from ₹1 to ₹10 per share

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine administrative listing notice under existing ESOP/ESOS listing procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to take note of the listing for trading purposes; no specific action is mandated beyond acknowledgment.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 23, 2026
- Effective listing date for trading: Friday, July 24, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, low-impact administrative notice reflecting standard ESOP/ESOS share allotments across 10 companies. The incremental share quantities are small relative to the overall issued capital of these companies (particularly for large-cap names like HDFC Bank and Axis Bank), and are expected to have negligible impact on stock price or trading volumes.
