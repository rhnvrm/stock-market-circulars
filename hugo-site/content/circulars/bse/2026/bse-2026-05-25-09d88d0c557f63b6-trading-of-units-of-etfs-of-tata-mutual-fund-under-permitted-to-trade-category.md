## Summary

BSE has issued a notice permitting the trading of units of Exchange Traded Funds (ETFs) of Tata Mutual Fund under the Permitted to Trade category on the BSE Mutual Fund segment. This is an operational listing notice facilitating secondary market trading of these ETF units on BSE.

## Key Points

- ETF units of Tata Mutual Fund are admitted under BSE's Permitted to Trade category.
- Trading is applicable in the Mutual Fund segment of BSE.
- Notice issued by the DCS-Listing department under Company Related category.
- No attachments were included with this notice.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice enabling secondary market trading access for Tata Mutual Fund ETF units on BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

Market participants (brokers, traders, investors) may trade the specified Tata Mutual Fund ETF units on BSE under the Permitted to Trade category. Standard mutual fund trading rules and settlement norms apply.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 25 May 2026
- Trading commencement: As per the effective date communicated via the notice (specific date not available in provided content).

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This notice expands the trading venue availability for Tata Mutual Fund ETF investors by allowing secondary market transactions on BSE. It benefits investors seeking liquidity and price discovery on BSE's platform for these specific ETF schemes.
