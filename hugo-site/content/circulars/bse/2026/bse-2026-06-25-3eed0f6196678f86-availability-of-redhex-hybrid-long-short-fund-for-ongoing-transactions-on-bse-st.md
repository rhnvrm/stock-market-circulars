## Summary

BSE has informed members that HSBC Mutual Fund has reopened the REDHEX Hybrid Long Short Fund (a Specialized Investment Fund / SIF Scheme) for ongoing transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform, effective June 25, 2026. This follows Notice No. 20260601-26 dated June 01, 2026. The scheme is now available for subscriptions, redemptions, SIP registrations, and SWP registrations.

## Key Points

- REDHEX Hybrid Long Short Fund (SIF Scheme) by HSBC Mutual Fund is reopened for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform from June 25, 2026.
- The scheme supports: ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) registrations, and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) registrations.
- Redemption and SWP transactions will be processed **once a week, every Monday**.
- Full list of schemes with plans and options is available in Annexure 1 of the notice.
- Redemption Processing Setup as maintained in RTA records is provided in Annexure 2.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice implements the reopening of an existing SIF scheme for ongoing transactions, consistent with the earlier Notice No. 20260601-26 dated June 01, 2026.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members (brokers/distributors) are requested to note the weekly redemption and SWP processing cycle (every Monday) and inform their clients accordingly.
- Members should refer to Annexure 1 for the detailed scheme, plan, and option list and Annexure 2 for the Redemption Processing Setup.

## Important Dates

- **June 25, 2026**: Effective date for reopening of REDHEX Hybrid Long Short Fund for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform.
- **Every Monday**: Scheduled processing day for redemption and SWP transactions.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational update with limited market-wide impact. It is relevant to BSE members and distributors who facilitate transactions in HSBC Mutual Fund schemes on the BSE StAR MF Platform. Investors in the REDHEX Hybrid Long Short Fund should note that redemption and SWP requests will only be processed on Mondays, which may affect liquidity planning compared to daily-processed schemes.
