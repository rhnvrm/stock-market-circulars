## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that the suspension in trading of equity shares of Mahesh Developers Ltd (Scrip Code: 542677) will be revoked with effect from July 22, 2026. Trading in the company's securities will resume in the "XT" group, pursuant to SEBI Master Circular No. HO/49/14/14(7)2025-CFD-POD2/I/3762/2026 dated January 30, 2026 (erstwhile SEBI Circular No. SEBI/HO/CFD/CMD/CIR/P/2020/12 dated January 22, 2020).

## Key Points

- Scrip Code 542677 (Mahesh Developers Ltd) suspension revoked effective July 22, 2026
- Trading will resume in the "XT" group
- A company profile is attached as Annexure I
- The Information Memorandum will be available on BSE's website under Corporates → Corporate Filings → Information Memorandum → Revocation
- The scrip will be part of the Special Pre-open Session (SPOS) for IPOs & Relisted Scrips - Relist session on July 22, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; action is taken under existing SEBI Master Circular No. HO/49/14/14(7)2025-CFD-POD2/I/3762/2026 dated January 30, 2026 governing revocation of suspension in trading.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the resumption of trading and the applicability of the Special Pre-open Session (SPOS) mechanism for the relisted scrip, referring to BSE notice no. 20120216-29 on SPOS for IPOs & Relisted Scrips.

## Important Dates

- July 14, 2026: Notice issued
- July 22, 2026: Suspension revoked; trading resumes; SPOS relist session held

## Impact Assessment

The revocation allows shareholders of Mahesh Developers Ltd to trade their shares again after a period of suspension, restoring liquidity for this specific scrip. The impact is limited to the company and its shareholders/trading members dealing in this scrip, with no broader market-wide implications.
