## Summary

BSE has announced that BSE Indices will be calculated on February 01, 2026 (Sunday), which has been declared as a special trading day by the exchange on account of Union Budget 2026. Markets will remain open for regular trading hours during this special session.

## Key Points

- Special trading session scheduled for February 01, 2026 (Sunday)
- BSE Indices will be calculated during this session
- Regular trading hours will be maintained
- Special session declared on account of Union Budget 2026
- This notice references Notice No: 20260116-33
- Queries can be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational announcement for a special trading session.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should note the special trading session on February 01, 2026
- Participants should be prepared for regular trading hours on the specified Sunday
- All index calculation and related operations will proceed as on a regular trading day

## Important Dates

- **February 01, 2026 (Sunday)**: Special trading session for BSE Indices
- **Notice Date**: January 16, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This special trading session is scheduled to accommodate trading activity around the Union Budget 2026 presentation. All market participants, including trading members, institutional investors, and retail traders, should plan for active trading on what would normally be a non-trading day. Regular trading hours indicate full market functionality during the session.
