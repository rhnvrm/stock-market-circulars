## Summary

ICCL (Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd.) has published the settlement calendar for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for Jauss Polymers Limited. The bidding window runs from April 22, 2026 to May 6, 2026, with obligations downloaded on May 11, 2026 and settlement completed on May 12, 2026. Settlement number is 2026040 under the Takeover market type.

## Key Points

- Settlement Type: Takeover | Settlement No.: 2026040
- Bid Start Date: 22/04/2026 | Bid End Date: 06/05/2026
- Obligation Download Date: 11/05/2026
- Settlement Date: 12/05/2026
- Demat shares must be delivered exclusively via the Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism (available on both CDSL and NSDL)
- Market Type "(Takeover)" and Settlement No. 2026040 must be used in the depository system
- Members are NOT required to upload any EPN file
- CDSL & NSDL early pay-in securities will be earmarked/blocked in clients' accounts under the specified market type and settlement number

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. Members are referred to Notice No. 20200528-32 dated May 28, 2020, regarding settlement as per Revised Guidelines of Mechanism for acquisition of shares through Stock Exchange.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members/Custodians must use Market Type "(Takeover)" and Settlement No. 2026040 in the depository system
- Early pay-in of securities must be completed sufficiently in advance of the BSE OTB Bidding window closure
- Effect of early pay-in must be reflected in BSE/ICCL systems before prescribed timelines for bid acceptance/confirmation
- Members should refer to the MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for client-wise Stamp Duty charges

## Important Dates

## Impact Assessment

This circular affects brokers, members, and custodians participating in the takeover acquisition window for Jauss Polymers Limited. Operational impact is limited to entities facilitating client participation in this specific open offer. Members must ensure timely early pay-in of demat shares to avoid bid rejection. No broader market impact is anticipated beyond the participants in this offer.
