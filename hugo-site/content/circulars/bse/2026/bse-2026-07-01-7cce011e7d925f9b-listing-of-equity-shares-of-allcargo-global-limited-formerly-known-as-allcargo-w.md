## Summary

BSE has informed market participants that equity shares of Allcargo Global Limited (formerly Allcargo Worldwide Limited, originally incorporated as Allcargo Ecu Limited) will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange effective Friday, July 03, 2026. The shares will be placed in the 'T' Group of Securities and will trade in the Trade-for-Trade segment for the first 10 trading days, in line with SEBI circulars dated January 20, 2012 and April 11, 2023. The listing follows a Composite Scheme of Arrangement approved by the NCLT Mumbai Bench.

## Key Points

- Allcargo Global Limited (AGL) shares list on BSE from July 03, 2026.
- Scrip Code: 544602; Scrip ID: AGL; ISIN: INE1YPB01014.
- Placed in 'T' Group of Securities; Trade-for-Trade segment for 10 trading days.
- Total securities: 98,27,82,096 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each (Distinctive numbers 1 to 982782096).
- Face value Rs. 2/- each, fully paid; Market lot: 1 share.
- No lock-in applicable.
- Scrip will be part of the Special Pre-Open Session (SPOS) applicable to IPO and other newly listed scrips.
- Shares allotted pursuant to a Composite Scheme of Arrangement involving Allcargo Logistics Limited (demerged company), transferring the International Supply Chain/Multimodal Transport Business (ISC/MTO) undertaking to Allcargo Global Limited (previously an unlisted wholly-owned subsidiary).
- Scheme also involved amalgamation of Allcargo Supply Chain Private Limited and Gati Express & Supply Chain Private Limited into Allcargo Gati Limited (BSE/NSE listed).

## Regulatory Changes

Listing and trading arrangements follow SEBI Circular No. CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012, and SEBI/HO/MRD-TPD1/CIR/P/2023/55 dated April 11, 2023, mandating Trade-for-Trade settlement for newly listed/relisted scrips for an initial period, along with Special Pre-Open Session (SPOS) procedures under CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 and CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012.

## Compliance Requirements

Market participants and trading members must ensure orders in AGL are processed under Trade-for-Trade norms for the first 10 trading sessions and must account for the scrip's inclusion in the Special Pre-Open Session. Queries on SPOS can be directed to the BSE Helpdesk (022-6136 3100/3171).

## Important Dates

- NCLT Mumbai Bench order approving Composite Scheme of Arrangement: October 10, 2025
- Appointed Date: October 01, 2023
- Effective Date: November 01, 2025
- Date of Allotment: November 15, 2025
- Listing Effective Date: July 03, 2026 (Friday)
- Trade-for-Trade period: 10 trading days from listing

## Impact Assessment

The listing enables shareholders who received AGL shares under the demerger scheme to trade their holdings. The Trade-for-Trade restriction and 'T' Group classification will limit intraday trading and increase settlement rigor initially, which may affect near-term liquidity and price discovery for the stock. The event is a corporate restructuring outcome for the broader Allcargo group rather than a new capital-raising listing, limiting broader market impact.
