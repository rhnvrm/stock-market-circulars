## Summary

Kasturi Metal Composite Limited (Exchange ticker: 544688) will be added to the BSE SME IPO Index effective at the open of Wednesday, February 4, 2026, following its listing on BSE on Tuesday, February 3, 2026.

## Key Points

- Kasturi Metal Composite Limited is being listed on BSE effective February 3, 2026
- The stock will be added to the BSE SME IPO Index on February 4, 2026
- Exchange ticker: 544688
- This action has been included in the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- This follows reference to Notice No. 20260202-35

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index constituent addition following a new SME listing.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. This is an informational notice regarding index composition changes.

## Important Dates

- **February 3, 2026**: Listing date for Kasturi Metal Composite Limited on BSE
- **February 4, 2026**: Effective date for addition to BSE SME IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine addition of a newly listed SME stock to the BSE SME IPO Index. Index funds and ETFs tracking the BSE SME IPO Index may need to adjust their portfolios to include this stock. For queries, market participants can contact bseindex@bseindia.com.
