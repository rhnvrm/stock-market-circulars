## Summary

Gujarat Gas Limited (Exchange Ticker: GUJGASLTD, 539336) is demerging its Transmission Business into a newly formed entity, GSPL Transmission Limited (GSPLTRANS), effective from the start of trading on Thursday, July 02, 2026. As part of the scheme, existing shareholders of Gujarat Gas Limited will receive 3 equity shares of GSPL Transmission Limited for every 1 equity share held. GSPL Transmission Limited is expected to list on BSE at a future date.

## Key Points

- Gujarat Gas Limited (GUJGASLTD) will demerge its Transmission Business into GSPL Transmission Limited (GSPLTRANS) effective July 02, 2026.
- Share swap ratio: 3 equity shares of GSPL Transmission Limited for every 1 equity share of Gujarat Gas Limited.
- A Special Pre-Open Session (SPOS) will be held on July 02, 2026 to enable price discovery of GUJGASLTD post-demerger.
- GSPL Transmission Limited will be added to 16 BSE Indices at zero price, effective July 02, 2026 (after close of July 01, 2026), without divisor adjustment.
- GSPL Transmission Limited's listing on BSE is expected at a future date to be announced separately.

## Regulatory Changes

As part of the scheme of arrangement, GSPL Transmission Limited will be incorporated into 16 BSE Indices without divisor adjustment. The addition is at zero price after the close of trading on Wednesday, July 01, 2026.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants and index fund managers tracking the affected BSE Indices must account for the addition of GSPL Transmission Limited at zero price from July 02, 2026.
- Investors holding Gujarat Gas Limited should be aware of the share entitlement (3:1) under the demerger scheme.
- For queries, participants may contact bseindex@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **July 01, 2026 (Close of trading):** GSPL Transmission Limited added to 16 BSE Indices at zero price.
- **July 02, 2026 (Start of trading):** Demerger effective date; Special Pre-Open Session (SPOS) held for price discovery of GUJGASLTD post-demerger.
- **Future date (TBA):** Expected listing of GSPL Transmission Limited equity shares on BSE.

## Impact Assessment

This demerger has broad market impact across 16 BSE Indices including BSE 500, BSE PSU, BSE MIDCAP, BSE ALLCAP, BSE ENERGY, BSE 250 SMALLCAP INDEX, BSE 400 MIDSMALLCAP INDEX, BSE POWER & ENERGY, BSE 1000, BSE 1000 MULTICAP EQUAL SIZE WEIGHTED (25%), BSE 500 EQUAL WEIGHT, BSE SMALLCAP 500, BSE SMALLCAP 500 LOW VOLATILITY 50, BSE LARGEMIDCAP, BSE MIDSMALLCAP, and BSE 500 SHARIAH. Index funds and ETFs tracking these benchmarks will need to incorporate the new constituent. The Special Pre-Open Session ensures orderly price discovery for Gujarat Gas Limited post-demerger, reducing potential volatility at market open on July 02, 2026.
