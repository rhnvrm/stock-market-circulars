## Summary

Fairchem Organics Limited (Scrip Code: 543252) has fixed January 5, 2026 as the Record Date to determine the entitlement and names of equity shareholders eligible to participate in the company's buyback offer. The ex-entitlement date is also January 5, 2026 under settlement number DR-791/2025-2026.

## Key Points

- Company: Fairchem Organics Limited (Scrip Code: 543252)
- Record Date: January 5, 2026
- Purpose: Determine shareholders eligible for buyback participation
- Ex-Entitlement Date: January 5, 2026
- Settlement Number: DR-791/2025-2026
- Segment: Dematerialised Securities – Rolling Settlement
- Circular issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President – Listing Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must take note of the record date and ex-entitlement date
- Only shareholders holding shares as of the record date (January 5, 2026) will be eligible to participate in the buyback offer
- Shares will trade ex-entitlement from January 5, 2026

## Important Dates

- **January 1, 2026**: Notice date
- **January 5, 2026**: Record date for buyback eligibility
- **January 5, 2026**: Ex-entitlement basis from date

## Impact Assessment

**For Shareholders**: Shareholders holding Fairchem Organics shares as of the record date will be eligible to participate in the buyback offer. This may provide an exit opportunity at potentially favorable prices.

**For Trading Members**: Need to ensure proper handling of trades and client communications regarding the ex-entitlement date to avoid disputes.

**Market Impact**: Limited to Fairchem Organics stock. Expected price movement around the record date and buyback execution period as shareholders evaluate participation in the tender offer.
