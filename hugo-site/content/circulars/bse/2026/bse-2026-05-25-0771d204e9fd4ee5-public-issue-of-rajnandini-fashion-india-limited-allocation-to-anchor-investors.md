## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that under the Anchor Investor (AI) portion of the public issue of Rajnandini Fashion India Limited, 8,18,000 equity shares have been subscribed on May 25, 2026, at a price of Rs. 63 per equity share.

## Key Points

- 8,18,000 equity shares allocated to Anchor Investors
- Issue price for Anchor Investor allocation: Rs. 63 per equity share
- Allocation date: May 25, 2026
- Notice issued by Mangesh Tayde, Vice President, BSE
- Announcement copy issued by the company is enclosed with the notice

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural notice informing trading members of anchor investor allocation ahead of the public issue opening.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to take note of the anchor investor allocation details for Rajnandini Fashion India Limited's public issue. No specific compliance actions are required from trading members beyond awareness.

## Important Dates

- Anchor Investor allocation date: May 25, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The anchor investor allocation at Rs. 63 per share signals the IPO price band and investor interest for Rajnandini Fashion India Limited. The subscription of 8,18,000 shares by anchor investors is a precursor to the public issue opening. Impact is limited to prospective retail and institutional investors considering participation in this IPO. No broader market or regulatory impact is anticipated.
