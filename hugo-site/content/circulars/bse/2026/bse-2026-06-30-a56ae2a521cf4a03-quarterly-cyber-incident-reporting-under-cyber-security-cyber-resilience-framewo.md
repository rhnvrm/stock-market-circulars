## Summary

BSE has issued a notice requiring all trading members (Regulated Entities) to submit their Quarterly Cyber Incident Report (QCIR) for the quarter ending June 30, 2026, through the BEFS member portal on or before July 15, 2026. This is in line with the SEBI Cyber Security & Cyber Resilience Framework circular dated August 20, 2024, and the corresponding BSE exchange circular dated August 22, 2024.

## Key Points

- Quarterly Cyber Incident Reports for Q ending June 30, 2026 must be submitted by July 15, 2026.
- Submission path: `https://befs.bseindia.com` → Cyber Incident Report → Quarterly Incident Reporting.
- Members registered with both BSE and NSE must submit their QCIR to NSE only (per Exchange Notice 20250929-81 dated September 29, 2025).
- Exclusive BSE members must continue submitting through the BEFS portal.
- For Immediate Cyber Incident reporting, members should follow the SOP in Exchange circular 20250108-42 dated January 08, 2025.
- Non-submission or delayed submission will attract disciplinary action per Exchange Notice 20260417-45 dated April 17, 2026.
- Submission guidelines are provided in Annexure 1 of this notice.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This notice is a reminder and operational update under the existing SEBI Cyber Security & Cyber Resilience Framework (SEBI/HO/ITD-1/ITD_CSC_EXT/P/CIR/2024/113 dated August 20, 2024). A technology-based sharing mechanism (Exchange Notice 20250929-81) now routes dual-registered NSE/BSE members to submit solely to NSE, reducing duplication.

## Compliance Requirements

- All trading members (Regulated Entities) must file the Quarterly Cyber Incident Report for Q2 FY2026 (quarter ending June 30, 2026).
- BSE-exclusive members: submit via BEFS portal under the path specified.
- Dual-registered members (BSE + NSE): submit to NSE only.
- Members must refer to Exchange circular 20250108-42 for Immediate Cyber Incident reporting procedures.
- For XBRL-related issues, contact: +91 9316749660 / bse.xbrl@bseindia.com.
- For process-related queries: 022-45720640 / 022-69158540 / 022-45720440 / mscopr@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **Quarter End:** June 30, 2026
- **Submission Deadline:** July 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard periodic compliance obligation with no direct market or price impact. Trading members that fail to submit on time face disciplinary action from BSE as outlined in Exchange Notice 20260417-45. The dual-registration carve-out (NSE-only submission for members on both exchanges) simplifies reporting overhead for those members and reduces duplicate submissions across exchanges.
