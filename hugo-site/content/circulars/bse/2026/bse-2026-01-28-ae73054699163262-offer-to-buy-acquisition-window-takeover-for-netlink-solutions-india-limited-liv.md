## Summary

BSE has announced the schedule for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for NETLINK SOLUTIONS INDIA LIMITED (Scrip Code: 509040, ISIN: INE040F01033). The offer will be conducted from Wednesday, January 28, 2026 to Tuesday, February 10, 2026 through the iBBS platform. The acquirer is offering to purchase up to 6,57,599 equity shares at Rs. 201.00 per share.

## Key Points

- **Security Name**: NETLINK SOLUTIONS INDIA LIMITED
- **Scrip Code**: 509040
- **ISIN**: INE040F01033
- **Offer Size**: Up to 6,57,599 equity shares
- **Offer Price**: Rs. 201.00 per share
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10.00
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Trading Period**: January 28, 2026 to February 10, 2026
- **Bid Offering Period**: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM (January 28 to February 10, 2026)
- Trading members can bid only for investors having shares in demat form
- UCC of investors must be registered with correct PAN and category

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard takeover offer process following existing OTB framework as outlined in Exchange Notices no. 20150930-1 dated September 30, 2015 and 20170203-29 dated February 3, 2017.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members and Custodian Members** must ensure investor UCC is registered with correct PAN number and category in Exchange UCC database
- **Eligible Participants**: Foreign Institutional Buyers (FII), Financial Institutions (FI), Mutual Funds (MF), Insurance Companies (IC), Other QIBs, Non-Institutional Buyers (NOH), Companies (CO), and Individuals (IND)
- **Platform Access**: iBBS platform (Internet: https://ibbs.bseindia.com/ or Leased Line: https://ibbsll.bseindia.com/)
- **RTRMS Access**: Internet (https://rtrms.bseindia.com/) or Leased Line (https://rtrmsll.bseindia.com/)
- Members can only bid for investors holding shares in demat form
- Files for acceptance of bids will be available on Extranet after issue closure and confirmation from Merchant Banker/RTA

## Important Dates

- **Offer Opening Date**: Wednesday, January 28, 2026
- **Offer Closing Date**: Tuesday, February 10, 2026
- **Bid Offering Period**: January 28, 2026 to February 10, 2026 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Give-up/Take-up Period**: January 28, 2026 to February 9, 2026 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Give-up (Last Day)**: February 10, 2026 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Take-up (Last Day)**: February 10, 2026 (9:15 AM - 4:00 PM)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a company-specific takeover offer with limited broader market implications. The offer is for up to 6,57,599 shares of a single mid/small-cap company.

**Operational Impact**: Medium for participating trading members - Members need to ensure proper UCC registration, access to iBBS/RTRMS platforms, and familiarity with OTB procedures. Contact details provided for technical support.

**Shareholder Impact**: High for Netlink Solutions shareholders - Existing shareholders have an opportunity to tender their shares at Rs. 201.00 per share during the 14-day offer period through their trading members.

**Process**: The offer follows standard OTB framework with clear session timings, bidding categories, and platform accessibility guidelines.
