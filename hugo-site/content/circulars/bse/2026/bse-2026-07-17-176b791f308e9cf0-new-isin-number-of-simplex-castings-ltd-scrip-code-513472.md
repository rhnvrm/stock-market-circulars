## Summary

BSE has informed trading members of the new ISIN number allotted to SIMPLEX CASTINGS LTD (Scrip Code: 513472) following the sub-division of its equity shares from face value Rs.10/- to Rs.2/- each. This is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20260706-6 dated July 06, 2026.

## Key Points

- New ISIN for SIMPLEX CASTINGS LTD: INE658D01029
- Reason for change: Sub-division of equity shares from Rs.10/- face value to Rs.2/- face value
- This notice supersedes/continues the earlier notice dated July 06, 2026 regarding the same corporate action
- Reference: DR- 675/2026-2027

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory change; this is an operational update assigning the new ISIN consequent to the previously approved stock split.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must update their systems to reflect the new ISIN (INE658D01029) for SIMPLEX CASTINGS LTD for all trades executed on and after the ex-date.

## Important Dates

- Ex-Date: July 20, 2026 (new ISIN effective for trades done on and from this date)
- Notice Date: July 17, 2026
- Original corporate action notice: July 06, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is operational rather than financial — trading members and depository participants need to ensure correct ISIN mapping for settlement of SIMPLEX CASTINGS LTD shares post sub-division. Shareholders' holdings will reflect the new Rs.2/- face value shares under the new ISIN from the ex-date.
