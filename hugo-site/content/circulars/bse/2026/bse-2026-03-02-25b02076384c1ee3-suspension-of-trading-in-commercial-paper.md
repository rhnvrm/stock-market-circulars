## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (No. 20260302-52) directing trading members to suspend dealings in the commercial papers of 17 companies effective 09 March 2026, coinciding with their record date for redemption. This is a standard pre-redemption trading suspension under DR-835/2025-2026.

## Key Points

- Trading members must not deal in the listed commercial papers from 09 March 2026 onwards.
- All 17 companies have set 09 March 2026 as the record date for redemption of their respective commercial papers.
- The suspension applies to all transactions in the Debt segment of BSE.
- The directive is issued under reference DR-835/2025-2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is a routine operational notice requiring temporary suspension of trading in specific commercial paper instruments ahead of their maturity/redemption dates.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in the specified commercial paper instruments effective 09 March 2026.
- Members holding positions in these instruments should plan accordingly prior to the suspension date.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 02 March 2026
- **Record Date / No Dealings From:** 09 March 2026 (for all 17 companies)
