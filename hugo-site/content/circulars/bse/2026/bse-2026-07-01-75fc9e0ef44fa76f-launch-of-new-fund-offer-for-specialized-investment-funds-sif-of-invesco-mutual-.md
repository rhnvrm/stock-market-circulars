## Summary

BSE has notified members that Invesco Mutual Fund will launch the New Fund Offer (NFO) for Summit Equity Long-Short Fund (SIF), along with SIP facility, on the BSE StAR MF Platform from July 02, 2026, to July 16, 2026. This follows BSE's earlier notice (20260701-17) introducing Invesco's Specialized Investment Funds (SIF) on the platform.

## Key Points

- Scheme: SUMMIT EQUITY LONG-SHORT FUND (SIF), offered in Regular and Direct plans, each with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options (6 scheme variants total).
- Scheme category: Equity.
- ISINs range from INF205K30014 to INF205K30063 across the six variants.
- Tentative allotment date for all variants: July 22, 2026.
- Transactions where clients do not initiate funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day will be cancelled and reported to RTA/AMC with 'N' flag.
- Transactions with initiated payment remain pending until 3:00 PM on the last day of funds pay-out to the AMC, or T+5 day, whichever is earlier.
- Cheque and OTM payments are accepted during the NFO period, subject to clear funds reaching the ICCL account by 6:00 PM on July 22, 2026.
- NFO extended till midnight July 16, 2026, only for Lumpsum purchases; SIP/XSIP registrations must be authenticated by clients by 3:00 PM on July 16, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory change; this is an operational/procedural notice implementing the earlier SIF introduction notice (20260701-17) for a specific fund launch.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure client transactions comply with fund pay-in timelines to avoid cancellation with 'N' flag.
- Clear funds must be available in the ICCL account by 6:00 PM on July 22, 2026, for allotment eligibility.
- SIP/XSIP registrations require client authentication by 3:00 PM on July 16, 2026.

## Important Dates

- NFO Period: July 02, 2026 to July 16, 2026 (extended till midnight July 16, 2026 for Lumpsum purchases only).
- SIP/XSIP authentication deadline: 3:00 PM, July 16, 2026.
- Tentative allotment date: July 22, 2026.
- Clear funds deadline in ICCL account: 6:00 PM, July 22, 2026.

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact; this is a routine operational notice for mutual fund distributors and trading members regarding processing of a new equity long-short SIF scheme on the BSE StAR MF platform. No direct effect on listed equity securities or broader market trading.
