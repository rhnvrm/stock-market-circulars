## Summary

BSE has notified members that ICICI Prudential Mutual Fund is launching the **ICICI Prudential Balanced Hybrid Fund** as a New Fund Offer (NFO) on the BSE StAR MF Platform from June 30, 2026 to July 14, 2026. The fund offers six scheme variants across Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Reinvestment, and IDCW Payout options. The tentative allotment date is July 17, 2026.

## Key Points

- NFO subscription window: June 30, 2026 to July 14, 2026
- Six scheme variants offered under Equity category (Regular and Direct plans, each with Growth, IDCW Reinvestment, IDCW Payout options)
- Tentative allotment date: July 17, 2026
- Clear funds must be in ICCL account by 6:00 PM on July 16, 2026
- SIP/XSIP registrations must be authenticated by clients on or before 3:00 PM on July 14, 2026
- Lumpsum purchases extended till midnight July 14, 2026 on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Cheque and OTM payments accepted during NFO period subject to fund clearance conditions

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notice for the launch of a new mutual fund scheme on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clients initiate funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day; failing which transactions shall be cancelled and CC with 'N' flag reported to RTA/AMC
- Transactions with initiated payments remain pending until 3:00 PM on the last day of funds pay-out to AMC or T+5 day, whichever is earlier
- Switch-out funds received on or after the allotment date (July 17, 2026) shall not be considered for allotment
- Cheque and OTM payments must result in clear funds in ICCL account by 6:00 PM on July 16, 2026 to be eligible for allotment
