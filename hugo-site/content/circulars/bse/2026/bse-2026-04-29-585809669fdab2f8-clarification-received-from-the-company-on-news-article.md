## Summary

BSE's Surveillance Department (DOSS) sought clarification from Ideaforge Technology Ltd (Scrip Code: 543932) regarding a news article published on BusinessWorld (businessworld.in). The company has submitted its reply, which is attached to this notice for the information of members and investors.

## Key Points

- BSE proactively monitors news articles across newspapers, websites, and television channels that may affect listed companies
- Ideaforge Technology Ltd (Scrip Code: 543932) was asked to clarify a news article appearing on BusinessWorld
- The company's clarification/reply has been received and is attached to this notice
- Notice issued by BSE Surveillance Department (DOSS)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this notice.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements for market participants. This is an informational notice providing investors and members access to the company's clarification on a media report.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 29 April 2026
- Notice Number: 20260429-15

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. This is a standard BSE surveillance action where the exchange seeks clarification from a listed company on news reports to ensure transparency and accurate information for investors. The attached reply from Ideaforge Technology Ltd serves to inform the market about the company's official stance on the referenced news article.
