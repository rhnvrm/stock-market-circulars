## Summary

BSE has issued the schedule of activities for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Buyback) of shares of TEAMLEASE SERVICES LTD. The buyback window is open from Thursday, 9th July 2026 to Wednesday, 15th July 2026. Trading Members and Custodian Members are advised to note the bidding, modification, give-up, and take-up timelines for participation via the iBBS platform.

## Key Points

- Security: TEAMLEASE SERVICES LTD (Symbol: TEAMLEASE, ISIN: INE985S01024, Scrip Code: 539658)
- Offer Size: Up to 14,87,500 equity shares
- Market Lot: 1
- Face Value: Rs. 10.00 per share
- Offer Price: Rs. 1600.00 per share
- Offer Period: 9th July 2026 to 15th July 2026
- Bidding categories include FII, FI, MF, IC, Other QIBs, Other NIB, Company, and Individual investors
- Access provided via iBBS, RTRMS, and Extranet platforms (internet and leased line URLs specified)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is an operational notice implementing the buyback under existing Exchange circulars (Notice nos. 20150930-1 dated September 30, 2015 and 20170203-29 dated February 3, 2017) governing OTB (Offer to Buy) participation procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must ensure the UCC (Unique Client Code) of investors is registered and/or updated with correct PAN number and category in the Exchange UCC database.
- Trading Members can bid only for investors holding shares in demat form.
- Members and Custodians must note that if no securities are accepted, no End of Issue files will be generated on the Extranet.
- Extranet files for bid acceptance become available only after confirmation of acceptance is received from the Merchant Banker/RTA post issue closure.

## Important Dates

- Bid Offering Period: 9th July 2026 to 15th July 2026, 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.
- Bid Modifications/Cancellation: 9th July 2026 to 15th July 2026, 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.
- Give-up/Take-up: 9th July 2026 to 14th July 2026, 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.
- Give-up (last day of offer): 15th July 2026, 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.
- Take-up (last day of offer): 15th July 2026, 9:15 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.

## Impact Assessment

The notice has limited direct market impact beyond facilitating an orderly buyback process for TEAMLEASE SERVICES LTD shares. It is primarily operational, affecting Trading Members' and Custodian Members' systems and processes for bid submission, modification, and settlement. Shareholders tendering shares should be aware of the offer price (Rs. 1600.00) and closing date (15th July 2026) to participate within the window.
