## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260226-3 advises trading members to suspend dealings in commercial papers of 18 companies that have fixed record dates for redemption. Ten companies have set 05/03/2026 as the record date (covered under DR-833/2025-2026) and eight companies have set 06/03/2026 as the record date (covered under DR-834/2025-2026).

## Key Points

- Trading members must not deal in the listed commercial papers from their respective record dates
- 10 companies with record date 05/03/2026 fall under circular DR-833/2025-2026
- 8 companies with record date 06/03/2026 fall under circular DR-834/2025-2026
- All suspensions are for the purpose of Redemption of Commercial Paper
- Notice issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President â€“ Listing Compliance And Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational notice under existing commercial paper redemption framework (DR-833/2025-2026 and DR-834/2025-2026).

## Important Dates

- **26/02/2026**: Notice issued by BSE
- **05/03/2026**: Record date and no-dealings start date for 10 companies (DR-833/2025-2026)
- **06/03/2026**: Record date and no-dealings start date for 8 companies (DR-834/2025-2026)

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited to holders and traders of the specific commercial paper instruments listed. Equity markets and other debt instruments of these companies are unaffected. Holders of these CPs should note that redemption proceeds will be processed on the respective record dates. Trading members dealing in the debt segment must update their systems to block trades in these 18 instruments from their respective restriction dates.
