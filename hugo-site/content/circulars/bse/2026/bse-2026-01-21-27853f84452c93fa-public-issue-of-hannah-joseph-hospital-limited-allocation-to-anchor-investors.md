## Summary

BSE has announced the allocation details to anchor investors for the public issue (IPO) of Hannah Joseph Hospital Limited. This circular provides information about the anchor portion allocation, which precedes the general public offering.

## Key Points

- Company: Hannah Joseph Hospital Limited
- Issue Type: Public Issue (IPO)
- Anchor investor allocation completed
- Allocation details disclosed as per SEBI regulations
- Price band and allocation quantum available in the detailed circular

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard disclosure as per SEBI (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations for anchor investor allocation in public issues.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants to take note of anchor allocation for price discovery
- Details disclosed as per SEBI ICDR Regulations requirements
- Information relevant for retail and institutional investors participating in the IPO

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: January 21, 2026
- Anchor allocation date: As specified in the circular
- Public issue dates: To be confirmed in the prospectus

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - Anchor investor allocation indicates institutional interest in the IPO and helps in price discovery before the public offering opens. This information is valuable for retail investors evaluating participation in the public issue.

**Investor Impact:** The anchor allocation provides insight into institutional demand and pricing, which can guide investment decisions for the upcoming public portion of the IPO.
