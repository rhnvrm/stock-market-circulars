## Summary

BSE has approved the listing and trading of 5,27,500 equity shares of Suyog Telematics Limited (Scrip Code: 537259) with effect from January 30, 2026. These shares were issued to promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants at an issue price of Rs. 570 per share (face value Rs. 10 plus premium of Rs. 560).

## Key Points

- 5,27,500 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at premium of Rs. 560/-
- Issued to promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Date of allotment: October 10, 2025
- Issue price: Rs. 570 per share
- Distinctive numbers: 11189621 to 11717120
- ISIN: INE442P01014
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- All shares subject to lock-in until July 30, 2027

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities available for trading
- Lock-in restrictions must be observed: all 5,27,500 shares (Dist. Nos. 11189621-11717120) are locked-in until July 30, 2027

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: October 10, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: January 30, 2026
- **Lock-in Period Ends**: July 30, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Low impact event. This is a routine preferential allotment listing for a single company. The shares represent a relatively small addition to the company's equity capital and are subject to a lock-in period until July 2027, limiting immediate trading volume impact. Market participants should note the lock-in restrictions when considering liquidity in this security.
