## Summary

BSE has listed five new commercial paper securities issued on private placement basis by Godrej Housing Finance Limited, Standard Chartered Securities (India) Limited, Bajaj Housing Finance Limited, Birla Group Holdings Private Limited, and Julius Baer Capital (India) Private Limited. These securities are admitted to trading on the BSE Debt Segment effective February 10, 2026.

## Key Points

- Five commercial paper instruments listed on BSE Debt Segment
- All securities issued on private placement basis with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Trading in dematerialized form only under respective ISIN numbers
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Interest rates range from 7.09% to 7.85% per annum
- All securities carry CRISIL A1+ and/or ICRA A1+/IND A1+ credit ratings

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof must be maintained
- Trading must adhere to tick size of 1 paise

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: February 10, 2026
- **Allotment Dates**: February 9-10, 2026
- **Redemption Dates**: May 8, 2026 to February 10, 2027 (varying by security)

This is a routine operational notice with minimal market impact. The listing provides institutional investors and trading members access to short-term debt instruments from reputed financial institutions and corporate entities.
