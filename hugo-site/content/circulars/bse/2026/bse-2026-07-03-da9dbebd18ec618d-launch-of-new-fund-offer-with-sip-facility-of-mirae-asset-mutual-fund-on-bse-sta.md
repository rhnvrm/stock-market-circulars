## Summary

BSE has notified members that Mirae Asset Mutual Fund will launch a New Fund Offer (NFO) with SIP facility for the Mirae Asset BSE Midcap 150 Momentum 30 ETF FOF on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The NFO will be open from July 6, 2026, to July 20, 2026, with a tentative allotment date of July 23, 2026, for both Regular and Direct Plan (Growth) options.

## Key Points

- Scheme: Mirae Asset BSE Midcap 150 Momentum 30 ETF FOF, offered in Regular Plan (ISIN: INF769K01QW0) and Direct Plan (ISIN: INF769K01QX8), both Growth options.
- NFO period: July 6, 2026 to July 20, 2026.
- Tentative allotment date: July 23, 2026.
- Transactions where clients fail to initiate funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day will be cancelled and reported to RTA/AMC with an 'N' flag.
- Transactions with initiated payments remain pending until 3:00 PM on the last day of funds pay-out or T+5 day, whichever is earlier.
- Cheque and OTM payments accepted during the NFO period subject to clear funds in the ICCL account by 6:00 PM on July 22, 2026.
- Switch transactions accepted during the NFO period, subject to receipt of funds prior to the allotment date; switch-out funds received on/after allotment date will not be considered.
- Lumpsum purchases extended till midnight on July 20, 2026; SIP/XSIP registrations must be authenticated by clients by 3:00 PM on July 20, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an operational/product notice, not a regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

- BSE StAR MF Platform members must ensure clear funds are credited to the ICCL account by 6:00 PM on July 22, 2026, for allotment eligibility.
- Members must ensure client payment initiation deadlines (9:00 AM T+1) are communicated and adhered to, to avoid transaction cancellation.
- SIP/XSIP registration authentication must be completed by clients by 3:00 PM on July 20, 2026.

## Important Dates

- NFO Open: July 6, 2026
- NFO Close (Lumpsum, extended): Midnight, July 20, 2026
- SIP/XSIP authentication deadline: 3:00 PM, July 20, 2026
- Clear funds deadline in ICCL account: 6:00 PM, July 22, 2026
- Tentative Allotment Date: July 23, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine product launch notice affecting BSE StAR MF Platform members and mutual fund distributors handling Mirae Asset transactions. It has no direct bearing on listed equity trading or share prices, and primarily requires operational awareness among members regarding payment and registration deadlines for the NFO.
