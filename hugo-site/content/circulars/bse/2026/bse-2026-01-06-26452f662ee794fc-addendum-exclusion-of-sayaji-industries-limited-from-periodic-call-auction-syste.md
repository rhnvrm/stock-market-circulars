## Summary

This is an addendum to BSE notice 20260105-10 dated January 5, 2026. BSE clarifies that Sayaji Industries Limited (Scrip Code: 540728, ISIN: INE327G01024) will be excluded from the Periodic Call Auction System (PCAS) mechanism effective from Wednesday, January 7, 2026.

## Key Points

- Addendum to previous notice 20260105-10 dated January 5, 2026
- Company affected: Sayaji Industries Limited
- ISIN Number: INE327G01024
- Scrip Code: 540728
- The security will be excluded from PCAS mechanism
- Notice Number: 20260106-2
- Category: Company related (Equity segment)

## Regulatory Changes

Sayaji Industries Limited will no longer trade under the Periodic Call Auction System (PCAS) mechanism. The company will move to regular trading mechanism.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note this change and adjust their trading systems accordingly. For clarifications, members may contact:

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 6, 2026
- **Effective Date**: Wednesday, January 7, 2026
- **Reference Notice**: 20260105-10 dated January 5, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This change has minimal market impact as it affects only one company. The exclusion from PCAS typically indicates improved liquidity or trading activity in the scrip, allowing it to trade in the normal market segment. Trading members dealing with Sayaji Industries Limited should update their systems to reflect this change from January 7, 2026.
