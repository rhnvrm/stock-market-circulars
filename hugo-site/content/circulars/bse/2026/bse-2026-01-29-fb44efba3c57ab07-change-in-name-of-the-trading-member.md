## Summary

BSE has notified all trading members about the name change of trading member JAGRUTI SECURITIES LTD. (Member No. 294) to "JAGRUTI SECURITIES PRIVATE LIMITED" effective from Friday, January 30, 2026. This is an administrative change in the corporate name structure of the trading member.

## Key Points

- Trading Member: Jagruti Securities Ltd. (Member No. 294)
- Old Name: JAGRUTI SECURITIES LTD.
- New Name: JAGRUTI SECURITIES PRIVATE LIMITED
- Effective Date: January 30, 2026 (Friday)
- Contact for clarifications: membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a corporate name modification from a public limited company designation to a private limited company designation.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should:
- Update their records with the new name of the trading member
- Ensure proper identification in any dealings with Jagruti Securities from January 30, 2026 onwards
- Contact membership.ops@bseindia.com for any clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 29, 2026
- **Effective Date**: January 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a purely administrative change with minimal market impact. The trading member number (294) remains unchanged, and there is no impact on trading operations, client accounts, or market activities. Other trading members only need to update their internal records to reflect the new corporate name structure.
