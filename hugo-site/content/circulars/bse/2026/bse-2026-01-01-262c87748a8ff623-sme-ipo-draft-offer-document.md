## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document of DHANWEL HYBRID SEEDS LIMITED filed on December 31, 2025. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and is available on the BSE SME website for public comments for a period of at least 21 days.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by DHANWEL HYBRID SEEDS LIMITED on December 31, 2025
- Document contains all disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website at www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public comments invited within 21 days from notice date (January 01, 2026)
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural notice for SME IPO draft offer document publication as per ICDR Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public and stakeholders may review the Draft Offer Document on BSE SME website
- Comments, if any, should be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com within 21 days
- The company must maintain the document available for at least 21 days for public review

## Important Dates

- **Document Filing Date**: December 31, 2025
- **Notice Date**: January 01, 2026
- **Comment Period**: 21 days from January 01, 2026 (deadline: January 22, 2026)

This notice has limited market impact as it pertains to a single SME company's IPO draft offer document. The impact is confined to:

- Potential investors interested in DHANWEL HYBRID SEEDS LIMITED SME IPO
