## Summary

BSE has listed 44,40,000 equity shares of Re. 1/- each of ERAAYA LIFESPACES LIMITED (Scrip Code: 531035) issued at a premium of Rs. 80/- to Promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. The new securities are permitted to trade with effect from Tuesday, February 3, 2026. The shares are ranking pari-passu with the old equity shares of the company and are subject to lock-in until August 15, 2027.

## Key Points

- 44,40,000 equity shares of Re. 1/- each listed
- Issue price: Rs. 81/- per share (Re. 1/- face value + Rs. 80/- premium)
- Allotment to Promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Date of Allotment: December 29, 2025
- Trading commences: February 3, 2026
- Distinctive Numbers: 194894161 to 199334160
- ISIN: INE432F01032
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed about the listing of new securities
- The allotted shares are subject to mandatory lock-in provisions as per preferential allotment regulations

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: December 29, 2025
- **Trading Commencement Date**: February 3, 2026
- **Lock-in Period Ends**: August 15, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine listing of preferentially allotted shares to promoters. The shares are subject to lock-in until August 15, 2027, meaning they cannot be traded immediately by the allottees.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Trading members need to be aware of the new securities for trading purposes. The lock-in restrictions prevent immediate liquidity impact from these shares.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to existing and potential investors of ERAAYA LIFESPACES LIMITED. The preferential allotment represents capital raising by the company through warrant conversion, indicating promoter commitment.
