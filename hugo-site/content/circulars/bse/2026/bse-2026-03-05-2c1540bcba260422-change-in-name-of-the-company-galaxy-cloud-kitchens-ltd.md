## Summary

BSE has issued a circular notifying market participants of a change in the name of Galaxy Cloud Kitchens Ltd, a company listed on the Exchange. The circular informs trading members and investors about the updated company name and the effective date of the change.

## Key Points

- The company Galaxy Cloud Kitchens Ltd has changed its name as notified to BSE.
- The new name is effective from the date specified in the circular.
- The scrip code and ISIN of the company remain unchanged.
- Trading members and market participants are advised to take note of the updated company name for all future references and transactions.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this circular. It is an administrative notification to update records to reflect the new company name as approved by the Registrar of Companies (RoC).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are required to update their internal systems and records with the new company name.
- Depository participants and registrars should update company name records accordingly.
- Investors holding shares of the company should note that their holdings remain unaffected; only the name displayed will change.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** March 5, 2026
- **Effective Date of Name Change:** As specified in the circular (refer to the original PDF for the exact date).

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact. It is a routine administrative update reflecting the company's legal name change. Existing shareholders' holdings, the scrip code, and ISIN remain unchanged. Trading activity is not expected to be materially affected by this name change.
