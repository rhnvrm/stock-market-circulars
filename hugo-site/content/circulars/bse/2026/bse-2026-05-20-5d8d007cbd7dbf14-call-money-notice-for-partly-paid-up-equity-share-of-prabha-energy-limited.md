## Summary

BSE has issued a Call Money Notice activating the Call Money Module on its IBBS (Internet Based Book Building Software) platform for Prabha Energy Limited. This is the First Call on Partly Paid-up Equity Shares (symbol: PRABHAPPCM), with a call money amount of Rs. 47.52 per share. The issue runs for 15 days from May 26 to June 9, 2026.

## Key Points

- Prabha Energy Limited has requested BSE to activate the Call Money Module of IBBS for its First Call on Partly Paid-up Equity Shares
- Security symbol: PRABHAPPCM; Market lot: 1
- Total partly paid-up equity shares: 96,67,258
- Call Money Amount payable per share: Rs. 47.52
- Bidding window: 10:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. daily during issue period
- Registrar: MUFG Intime India Private Limited (SEBI Reg No: INR000004058)
- Activated in accordance with Exchange Notice No. 20210104-37 dated January 4, 2021

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice operates under the framework established by SEBI circular dated December 8, 2020, and BSE Exchange Notice No. 20210104-37 dated January 4, 2021, governing the Call Money Module on IBBS.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members and Self-Certified Syndicate Banks (SCSBs) must use the IBBS Call Money Module to submit call money details collected during the issue period
- Only SCSBs and Trading Members with 3-in-1 type accounts are eligible to submit details via the Call Money Module
- Members must adhere to the bidding window of 10:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. throughout the issue period
- For regulatory/business rule queries: contact Listing Sales at listing.sales@bseindia.com or 022-2272 8846/5063/8179
- For IBBS bidding queries: contact ibbs.ops@bseindia.com or 022-2272 8376/8436/8226
- For new user ID creation or password reset: contact user.trdops@bseindia.com or 022-2272 8005/8215

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** May 20, 2026
- **Issue Opening Date:** Tuesday, May 26, 2026
- **Issue Closing Date:** Tuesday, June 9, 2026
- **Issue Period:** 15 days
- **Daily Bidding Window:** 10:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m.

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a targeted impact on Trading Members, SCSBs, and holders of Prabha Energy Limited's partly paid-up equity shares. Shareholders holding 96,67,258 partly paid-up shares are required to pay Rs. 47.52 per share as the First Call amount. The operational window of 15 days provides adequate time for compliance. Broader market impact is limited, but non-compliance by existing partly paid-up shareholders could result in forfeiture of shares per applicable regulations.
