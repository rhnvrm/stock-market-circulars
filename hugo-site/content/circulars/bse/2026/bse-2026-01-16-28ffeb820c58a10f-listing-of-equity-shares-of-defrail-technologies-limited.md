## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Defrail Technologies Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company will trade on the SME segment with scrip code 544677, symbol DEFRAIL, and ISIN INE0ZE201010. The specific listing date and detailed security information will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Defrail Technologies Limited to be listed on BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544677
- Trading Symbol: DEFRAIL
- ISIN: INE0ZE201010
- Exact listing date to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the upcoming listing. No specific compliance actions required at this stage. Further details including the listing date will be provided in a subsequent notice.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 16, 2026
- **Listing Date**: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This announcement has minimal impact on existing market operations. It represents a routine addition to the SME segment, providing trading members advance notice of the new security. The listing will expand investment opportunities in the SME space for interested investors. Market participants should await the follow-up notice for specific trading commencement details and security specifications.
