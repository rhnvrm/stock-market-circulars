## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document of Futurex Management Solutions Limited, which was filed on February 25, 2026. The document is hosted on the BSE SME website for a minimum of 21 days in compliance with Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations, inviting public comments.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Futurex Management Solutions Limited on February 25, 2026
- Document is available on BSE SME website for at least 21 days from the notice date (February 27, 2026)
- Public comments are invited and must be submitted within 21 days of the notice date
- Comments to be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Document hosted at: https://www.bsesme.com/download/348290/SME_IPO%20InPrinciple/FuturexManagementSolutionsLimited_20260225233325.pdf

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard disclosure requirement under Schedule VI of the SEBI (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations (ICDR Regulations).

## Compliance Requirements

- Members of the public and market participants wishing to submit comments on the Draft Offer Document must do so within 21 days of February 27, 2026
- Comments must be sent via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Draft Offer Document Filing Date:** February 25, 2026
- **Notice Date:** February 27, 2026
- **Public Comment Deadline:** On or before March 20, 2026 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal broader market impact. It pertains solely to the upcoming SME IPO of Futurex Management Solutions Limited, an unlisted company. The primary relevance is to prospective investors, analysts, and stakeholders who may wish to review the draft prospectus and submit public comments before the IPO proceeds further. No trading or operational disruption is expected for existing market participants.
