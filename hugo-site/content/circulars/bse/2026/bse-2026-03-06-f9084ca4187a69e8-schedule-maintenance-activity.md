## Summary

BSE has notified members of a scheduled maintenance activity on the BSE STAR MF platform (v2.bsestarmf.in) on Saturday, March 7, 2026, from 3:00 PM to 12:00 AM (midnight). During this window, both web portal and API interface access to the platform will be unavailable.

## Key Points

- Maintenance window: Saturday, March 7, 2026, 3:00 PM to 12:00 AM
- Affected URL: https://v2.bsestarmf.in
- Both web portal and API interfaces will be inaccessible during the maintenance period
- Members are advised to inform their technical teams and vendors to plan activities accordingly

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational maintenance notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must notify their technical teams and vendors about the maintenance window
- Plan mutual fund transactions and system activities to avoid the maintenance period (3:00 PM – 12:00 AM on March 7, 2026)

## Important Dates

- **March 7, 2026, 3:00 PM – 12:00 AM**: Scheduled maintenance window; BSE STAR MF platform unavailable

## Impact Assessment

All members and their clients who rely on the BSE STAR MF platform for mutual fund order placement, redemptions, or data access via web portal or API will experience a service outage during the maintenance window. Since the downtime falls on a Saturday (a non-trading day for equities), the impact on regular market operations is limited. However, members with automated systems or scheduled API jobs should reschedule those activities outside the maintenance window.
