## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260622-3 informing Trading Members that three T-bills/Government Securities are maturing on their respective redemption dates. Trading in these instruments must stop effective June 23, 2026, as the securities reach end-of-life under DR-657/2026-2027.

## Key Points

- Three T-bill instruments — spanning 91-day, 182-day, and 364-day tenors — are set to mature
- Trading Members are directed not to deal in these securities from June 23, 2026
- The suspension is consequential to the redemption dates fixed by the issuing authority
- No attachment or further documentation is associated with this notice

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced. The suspension follows standard BSE procedures for debt instrument maturity under the Listing Operations department.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members must immediately cease all trading activity in the three listed T-bill scrip codes (805084, 805161, 805196) effective June 23, 2026. Members should update their systems to block these ISINs from further transactions.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 22, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Effective:** June 23, 2026
- **Reference:** DR-657/2026-2027

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited and routine. The three instruments affected are:

| Scrip Code | Particulars | ISIN |
|---|---|---|
| 805084 | 364TB25626 | IN002025Z138 |
| 805161 | 182TB26626 | IN002025Y396 |
| 805196 | 91TB260626 | IN002025X513 |

This is a standard end-of-tenor action with no broader market implications. Holders of these instruments will receive redemption proceeds as per the terms set by the issuer.
