## Summary

BSE Limited, through Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd. (ICCL), has issued a notice inviting auction offers from trading members for the purchase of securities under Rolling Settlement No. DR-643/2026-27. The auction is scheduled for June 4, 2026, with pay-in on June 5, 2026.

## Key Points

- Auction offer entry window: 2:00 PM to 2:45 PM on June 4, 2026
- Auction matching: 2:45 PM to 3:00 PM on June 4, 2026
- Delivery statement and accepted offer reports: 3:00 PM to 3:30 PM on June 4, 2026
- Money statement and final reports: 3:35 PM to 5:30 PM on June 4, 2026
- Pay-in of auction securities: June 5, 2026 at 8:00 AM
- No corrections permitted for punching errors in securities code or rate
- Under the Uniform Membership structure, settlement is processed through the Clearing Member of a Trading Member
- Restriction on trading members offering securities in auctions (for settlements in which they had short deliveries) has been removed

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. The notice reiterates the existing Uniform Membership structure for Equity Cash Segment settlements, where settlement flows through the Clearing Member of a Trading Member.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and custodians must check their auction pay-in obligations for securities
- Securities pay-in must be executed in depositories within depository cut-off timings on June 5, 2026
- Trading members must refer to the cutoff rate on the Auction Offer Entry Screen before submitting offers
- Trading members should refer to notices regarding Corporate Actions (Ex-Rights, Ex-Bonus, Ex-Split, etc.) issued by the respective departments
- Utmost care must be taken while entering securities codes and rates, as no corrections will be allowed post-submission

## Important Dates

- **June 4, 2026**: Auction session (2:00 PM – 5:30 PM)
- **June 5, 2026**: Auction pay-in at 8:00 AM

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice applicable to trading members of BSE with obligations under Rolling Settlement DR-643/2026-27. The impact is limited to members who have short-delivered securities in this settlement cycle. The removal of the restriction on offering securities in auction for the same settlement in which short delivery occurred provides operational flexibility to trading members under the Uniform Membership structure.
