## Summary

BSE has announced the settlement calendar for the Offer to Buy through Acquisition Window (Takeover) for PREMIUM CAPITAL MARKET AND INVESTMENTS LIMITED. The bidding window will be open from January 16, 2026 to January 30, 2026, with settlement scheduled for February 13, 2026. This is Settlement No. 2026008.

## Key Points

- Bidding Period: January 16, 2026 to January 30, 2026
- Settlement Number: 2026008
- Settlement Type: Takeover
- Obligation Download Date: February 12, 2026
- Settlement Date: February 13, 2026
- Demat shares must be delivered through Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism only
- Market Type "(Takeover)" and settlement No. 2026008 to be used in depository system

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular follows the Revised Guidelines of Mechanism for acquisition of shares through Stock Exchange as per Notice No.20200528-32 dated May 28, 2020.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Members/Custodians**: Must deliver demat shares only through Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism available in both depositories
- **Participants**: Must ensure early pay-in securities are reflected in BSE/ICCL systems before prescribed timelines for bid acceptance/confirmation
- **Process Completion**: Early pay-in must be completed sufficiently in advance before closure of BSE OTB Bidding window and confirmation window
- **CDSL & NSDL**: Early pay-in securities will be earmarked/blocked in clients' accounts with requisite market type and settlement number
- **No EPN File Required**: Members and Custodians are NOT required to upload any EPN file
- **Stamp Duty**: Members should refer to MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for client-wise Stamp Duty charges

## Important Dates

- **January 14, 2026**: Circular issued
- **January 16, 2026**: Bid start date
- **January 30, 2026**: Bid end date
- **February 12, 2026**: Obligation download date
- **February 13, 2026**: Settlement date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a company-specific takeover offer affecting only shareholders and participants involved in the acquisition of PREMIUM CAPITAL MARKET AND INVESTMENTS LIMITED.

**Operational Impact**: Low to Medium for participating members - Requires adherence to early pay-in mechanisms and proper use of settlement numbers in depository systems. Members must coordinate timing to ensure early pay-in reflects before bidding window closure.
