## Summary

BSE has notified members of a scheduled maintenance activity on the BSE Star MF platform (v2.bsestarmf.in) on Saturday, March 7, 2026, from 3:00 PM to 12:00 AM midnight (9 hours). During this window, both the web portal and API interfaces will be inaccessible.

## Key Points

- Maintenance window: Saturday, March 7, 2026, 03:00 PM to 12:00 AM (midnight)
- Affected URL: https://v2.bsestarmf.in
- Both web portal access and API interfaces will be unavailable
- Members should inform their technical teams and vendors in advance
- Notice issued by Arunkumar Ganesan (VP - Mutual Fund) and Mahesh Tare (AVP - Mutual Fund)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational/infrastructure maintenance notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must inform their technical teams and vendors about the scheduled downtime
- Plan mutual fund transaction activities to avoid the maintenance window (3:00 PM – 12:00 AM on March 7, 2026)
- Ensure any automated or API-based integrations are accounted for during this period

## Important Dates

- **Maintenance Date:** Saturday, March 7, 2026
- **Downtime Start:** 3:00 PM IST
- **Downtime End:** 12:00 AM IST (midnight)

## Impact Assessment

All members using the BSE Star MF platform for mutual fund transactions will be unable to access the platform via web or API during the 9-hour maintenance window on Saturday evening. Members with automated systems or vendors relying on API integrations should plan accordingly. Saturday activity on mutual fund platforms is generally lower than weekdays, moderating the overall impact.
