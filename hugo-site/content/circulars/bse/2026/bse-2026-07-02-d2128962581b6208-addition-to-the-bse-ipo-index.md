## Summary

BSE Index Services Private Limited has announced that CSM Technologies Limited (Exchange ticker: 544806), which listed on BSE on July 2, 2026, will be added to the BSE IPO Index effective at the open of trading on Monday, July 6, 2026. This follows the company's earlier listing announcement in Notice No. 20260701-23.

## Key Points

- CSM Technologies Limited (ticker 544806) listed on BSE effective Thursday, July 2, 2026
- The stock will be added to the BSE IPO Index effective the open of Monday, July 6, 2026
- This addition will be reflected in the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE) for the notice date
- Queries can be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an index maintenance action following the standard process for including newly listed IPO stocks in the BSE IPO Index.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action required from listed companies or market participants. Index fund managers and ETF providers tracking the BSE IPO Index should adjust portfolios to reflect the addition effective the stated date.

## Important Dates

- July 2, 2026: CSM Technologies Limited listed on BSE
- July 6, 2026: Effective date of addition to the BSE IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This is a routine, procedural index update reflecting the recent listing of CSM Technologies Limited. Passive funds and ETFs benchmarked to the BSE IPO Index will need to rebalance holdings to include the new constituent, which may generate modest incremental demand for the stock around the effective date, but the overall market impact is minimal.
