## Summary

AQUAPHARM CHEMICAL LTD has announced the partial redemption of its debentures (ISIN: INE0SYQ07015, Scrip Code: 975354) with a reduced face value of Rs. 85,000 per debenture. The debentures will trade at this reduced face value starting January 09, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: AQUAPHARM CHEMICAL LTD
- Debenture ISIN: INE0SYQ07015
- Scrip Code: 975354
- Instrument: ACL-8.90%-29-1-29-PVT
- Reduced Face Value: Rs. 85,000 per debenture
- Circular Reference: DR-795/2025-2026
- Notice Number: 20260108-20

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action involving partial redemption of debentures as per the terms of the debt instrument.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must note the revised face value for the debentures
- All trades in these debentures must reflect the reduced face value of Rs. 85,000 per debenture
- Trading Members are required to update their systems to reflect the reduced face value

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** January 08, 2026
- **Effective Date for Trading at Reduced Face Value:** January 09, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Debenture Holders:** Partial redemption results in a reduced face value per debenture, indicating that a portion of the principal has been returned to debenture holders.

**Trading Impact:** The reduced face value will affect trading calculations, including accrued interest and settlement values. Trading members must ensure their systems are updated to reflect the new face value.

**Market Segment:** Impact is limited to the debt segment and specifically affects holders and traders of AQUAPHARM CHEMICAL LTD debentures with ISIN INE0SYQ07015.
