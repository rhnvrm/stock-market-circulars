## Summary

BSE has listed 269,402 new equity shares of Diamines & Chemicals Ltd. (Scrip Code: 500120, ISIN: INE591D01014) issued at Rs. 552/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- + premium of Rs. 542/-) to Promoter and Non-Promoter shareholders on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. These shares are permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Friday, June 12, 2026.

## Key Points

- 269,402 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 542/- (issue price Rs. 552/-)
- Issued to Promoter and Non-Promoter on preferential basis via warrant conversion
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 9,783,991 to 10,053,392
- Date of allotment: March 24, 2026
- Trading commences: June 12, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification for newly allotted securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed that the new securities are listed and may be traded from June 12, 2026. Lock-in restrictions apply to substantially all new shares and must be observed.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** March 24, 2026
- **Trading Effective From:** June 12, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (263,602 shares, Dist. Nos. 9,783,991–10,047,592):** December 14, 2027
- **Lock-in Expiry (5,800 shares, Dist. Nos. 10,047,593–10,053,392):** December 14, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minor dilutive impact on existing shareholders of Diamines & Chemicals Ltd. due to addition of 269,402 shares via preferential allotment. The bulk of new shares (263,602) are locked in until December 2027, limiting immediate selling pressure. Only 5,800 shares have a shorter lock-in expiring December 2026. Overall market impact is negligible given the small issue size.
