## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that ENBEE TRADE & FINANCE LTD. (Scrip Code: 512441) has fixed 04 March 2026 as the record date for a rights issue. The company is offering 21 equity shares of Re.1/- each for every 10 equity shares held. Trades in the scrip must be conducted on an ex-rights basis effective from 04/03/2026 under Settlement No. DR-832/2025-2026.

## Key Points

- Company: ENBEE TRADE & FINANCE LTD. (BSE Code: 512441)
- Rights ratio: 21 new equity shares for every 10 existing equity shares held
- Face value: Re.1/- per share
- Issue price: Re.1/- per share (at par)
- Full issue price payable at the time of making the application
- Segment: Dematerialised Securities â€“ Rolling Settlement
- Settlement Number: DR-832/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification under BSE's listing compliance framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all transactions in ENBEE TRADE & FINANCE LTD. equity shares are executed on an ex-rights basis with effect from 04/03/2026.
- Members must note that the full issue price of Re.1/- per share is payable upfront at the time of application.

## Important Dates

- **Record Date:** 04 March 2026
- **Ex-Rights Date:** 04 March 2026
- **Settlement:** DR-832/2025-2026
- **Circular Date:** 26 February 2026

## Impact Assessment

This rights issue has a dilutive impact on existing shareholders who do not subscribe. The ratio of 21:10 is significantly dilutive, more than doubling the share count for subscribing shareholders. Trading members and investors holding the scrip should be aware of the ex-rights adjustment effective 04/03/2026. The impact is limited to holders of ENBEE TRADE & FINANCE LTD. shares and does not affect broader market operations.
