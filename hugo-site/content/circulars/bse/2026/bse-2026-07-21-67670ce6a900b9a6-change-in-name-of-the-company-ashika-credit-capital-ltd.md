## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Ashika Credit Capital Ltd has informed the Exchange about a change in its company name to Ashika Global Securities Limited. The SCRIP ID and ABBREVIATED NAME on the BOLT Plus System will remain ASHIKAG, with the change effective from July 27, 2026.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code 543766 changes company name from "Ashika Credit Capital Ltd" to "Ashika Global Securities Limited"
- Scrip ID for BOLT Plus System remains "ASHIKAG"
- Abbreviated name on BOLT Plus System remains "ASHIKAG"
- Change is based on the Certificate of Incorporation (COI) submitted by the company

## Regulatory Changes

This is an administrative update reflecting the company's name change as approved via its Certificate of Incorporation. No change to scrip code or trading identifiers is involved.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should update their internal records to reflect the new company name effective July 27, 2026. No action is required from investors as the scrip ID and abbreviated name remain unchanged.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 21, 2026
- Effective Date of Name Change: July 27, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact administrative notification. Since the scrip code (543766) and trading symbol (ASHIKAG) remain unchanged, there is no disruption to trading, settlement, or investor holdings. The change is purely cosmetic, reflecting the company's updated legal name in Exchange records.
