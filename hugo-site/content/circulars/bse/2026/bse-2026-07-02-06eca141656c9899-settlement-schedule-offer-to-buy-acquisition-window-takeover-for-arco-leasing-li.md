## Summary

BSE's Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd. (ICCL) has issued the settlement calendar for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for Arco Leasing Limited. The acquisition window bidding will run from July 3, 2026 to July 16, 2026, under Settlement No. 2026082, with obligation download on July 29, 2026 and final settlement on July 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- Settlement Type: Takeover; Settlement No. 2026082
- Bid Start Date: 03/07/2026; Bid End Date: 16/07/2026
- Obligation Download: 29/07/2026
- Settlement Date: 30/07/2026
- Demat shares must be delivered only through the Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism, available via both depositories (CDSL and NSDL)
- Market Type "(Takeover)" and Settlement No. 2026082 must be used by Members/Custodians in the depository system
- Members/Custodians must refer to Notice No. 20200528-32 dated May 28, 2020 for revised guidelines on the mechanism for acquisition of shares through the Stock Exchange
- No EPN (Early Pay-in Notification) file upload is required by Members and Custodians
- CDSL & NSDL early pay-in securities will be earmarked/blocked in the client's account under the requisite market type and settlement number
- Members should refer to the MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for client-wise stamp duty charges

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced; this notice operationalizes the previously established framework (per Notice No. 20200528-32 dated May 28, 2020) governing acquisition of shares through the stock exchange mechanism for takeover offers.

## Compliance Requirements

- Participants must ensure early pay-in of securities is reflected in BSE/ICCL systems before the prescribed timelines for bid acceptance/confirmation on the OTB platform
- Early pay-in of securities in the depository system must be completed sufficiently in advance of the bidding window closure and custodian confirmation window
- Members and Custodians should use the correct Market Type and Settlement Number when processing shares in the depository system
- For queries, Members/Custodians may contact their relationship managers or the ICCL Help Desk (022-69158590/45720490/45720690, iccl.helpdesk@icclindia.com)

## Important Dates

- Bid Start Date: July 3, 2026
- Bid End Date: July 16, 2026
- Obligation Download: July 29, 2026
- Settlement Date: July 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational/procedural notice targeted at Trading Members and Custodians participating in the Arco Leasing Limited takeover acquisition window. It does not represent a new corporate action announcement or carry direct price-sensitive implications for retail investors, but is relevant for compliance and settlement processing by market intermediaries involved in the offer.
