## Summary

BSE has announced the availability of non-competitive bidding facility for the Government of India's Treasury Bills auction scheduled on February 25, 2026. Three T-Bills — 91-day, 182-day, and 364-day — are available for bidding through the NCB-GSec module on BSE's iBBS platform. The aggregate issue size is Rs. 34,000 crore.

## Key Points

- Government of India announced sale of three Treasury Bills via auction on February 25, 2026, as per RBI press release dated February 20, 2026.
- Bidding is accessible through the NCB-GSec module at https://ibbs.bseindia.com.
- 91-day T-Bill (091T270526): Issue size Rs. 14,000 crore; max individual bid Rs. 700 crore.
- 182-day T-Bill (182T260826): Issue size Rs. 12,000 crore; max individual bid Rs. 600 crore.
- 364-day T-Bill (364T250227): Issue size Rs. 8,000 crore; max individual bid Rs. 400 crore.
- All three T-Bills are new issuances (not reissues).
- Minimum lot size is 100 units; minimum bid amount is Rs. 10,000 for all three securities.
- This circular is in continuation of Exchange circular no. 20180423-42 dated April 26, 2018.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an operational notice under the existing non-competitive bidding framework established via circular 20180423-42 (April 26, 2018).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members wishing to participate must submit bids through the NCB-GSec module on the iBBS platform.
- Members must ensure bids are submitted before February 25, 2026, 8:00 AM (member deadline).
- Direct investors must submit bids by February 24, 2026, 5:00 PM.
- Contact Trading Operations (trading.ops@bseindia.com / 022-2272 8376/5626/8226) for bidding-related queries.
- Contact ICCL Operations (settlements@icclindia.com / 022-2272 8468/8704/8264) for obligation and pay-in queries.

## Important Dates

| Event | Date / Time |
|---|---|
| Bid Collection Start | February 23, 2026, 10:00 AM (24-hour availability) |
| Bid Collection End (Direct Investors) | February 24, 2026, 5:00 PM |
| Bid Collection End (Members) | February 25, 2026, 8:00 AM |
| Auction Date | February 25, 2026 |
| Settlement Date | February 26, 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

This notice affects trading members and direct investors participating in the Government Securities debt market. The aggregate issuance of Rs. 34,000 crore across three T-Bill tenors (91-day, 182-day, 364-day) is a routine government borrowing activity. Equity market participants are not directly impacted. Members should ensure timely bid submission via the iBBS platform to avoid missing the auction window.
