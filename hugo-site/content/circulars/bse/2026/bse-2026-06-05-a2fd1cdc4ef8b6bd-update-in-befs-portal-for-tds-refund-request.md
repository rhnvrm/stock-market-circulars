## Summary

BSE has updated the BEFS (BSE Electronic Filing System) Portal to enhance the TDS refund request process for its members. Effective June 9, 2026, members can track their refund application status and payment details via a new 'Status Report (TDS Refund Request)' tab. Mandatory information required for submission has also been reduced. Critically, from June 9, 2026, TDS refund requests for general charges will exclusively be accepted through the BEFS Portal — submissions via any other mode will not be considered.

## Key Points

- Since April 10, 2021, members have been required to upload TDS certificates on the BEFS Portal to claim refunds for general charges billing.
- A new 'Status Report (TDS Refund Request)' tab is now available within the 'TDS Refund Request' section for tracking application and payment status.
- Mandatory information required to submit a refund application has been reduced.
- From June 9, 2026, BEFS Portal is the only accepted channel for TDS refund requests on general charges; other modes will be rejected.
- TDS refunds will be credited to the member's bank account within 15–20 days of receipt of a complete request or clarification on income verification, whichever is later.
- For expedited processing, members are advised to upload invoice-wise workings in Excel format under the 'TDS Working File' tab.
- For payments other than general charges made on a gross basis, a separate refund request must be raised with the respective department.

## Regulatory Changes

- TDS refund requests for general charges will be accepted exclusively through the BEFS Portal effective June 9, 2026.
- A new status tracking feature ('Status Report (TDS Refund Request)') has been introduced in the portal.
- Mandatory submission fields for refund applications have been streamlined.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must:
- Submit all TDS refund requests for general charges solely via the BEFS Portal from June 9, 2026 onward.
- Upload TDS certificates that are digitally signed, generated from the TRACES portal, and provided in PDF format.
- Provide: TDS Certificate Number, TDS Amount (as per certificate), TDS Certificate copy, TDS Refund Claimed Amount (cannot exceed certificate amount), and invoice-wise workings in Excel under the 'TDS Working File' tab.
- For non-general-charge payments made on a gross basis, raise a separate refund request with the relevant department.

## Important Dates

- **April 10, 2021**: BEFS Portal became mandatory for TDS certificate uploads for general charges refunds.
- **June 9, 2026**: Enhanced portal goes live with status tracking; BEFS Portal becomes the sole accepted channel for TDS refund requests on general charges.

## Impact Assessment

This change primarily affects BSE trading members who claim TDS refunds on general charges. The process is now fully digital and portal-based, eliminating physical or email submissions. The addition of real-time status tracking and reduced mandatory fields improves operational efficiency. Members must ensure they are registered and able to access the BEFS Portal before June 9, 2026, or risk having their refund requests rejected. The 15–20 day refund credit timeline remains unchanged.
