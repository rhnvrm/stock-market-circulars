## Summary

BSE has listed 48,69,933 new equity shares of Celebrity Fashions Ltd. (Scrip Code: 532695, ISIN: INE185H01016) issued at a premium of Rs. 0.31/- per share (issue price Rs. 10.31/-) to Promoters and Non-Promoters on a preferential basis. These shares are ranked pari-passu with existing equity shares and are permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Thursday, May 21, 2026.

## Key Points

- 48,69,933 equity shares of face value Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 0.31/- (total issue price Rs. 10.31/-)
- Shares allotted to both Promoters and Non-Promoters on a preferential basis
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distinctive numbers range: 5,96,78,414 to 6,45,48,346
- Date of allotment: March 21, 2026
- Trading permitted from May 21, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification under BSE's Listing Operations framework for newly allotted securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities and their tradability from May 21, 2026
- Lock-in restrictions must be observed for the specified tranches until their respective expiry dates

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** March 21, 2026
- **Trading Effective From:** May 21, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (14,54,898 shares; Dist. Nos. 59678414–61133311):** November 30, 2027
- **Lock-in Expiry (34,15,035 shares; Dist. Nos. 61133312–64548346):** November 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing increases Celebrity Fashions Ltd.'s total equity share count, resulting in dilution for existing shareholders. The preferential allotment at Rs. 10.31/- per share (minimal premium of Rs. 0.31 over face value) may signal a fundraise at near-book value. Lock-in on approximately 99.99% of the newly issued shares (all 48,69,933 shares fall within the lock-in ranges) restricts immediate secondary market float, limiting short-term price pressure from new allottees selling. Traders should note the shares become freely tradable in tranches from November 2026 and November 2027.
