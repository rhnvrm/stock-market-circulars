## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260423-27 dated April 23, 2026, announcing the listing of equity shares of Mehul Telecom Limited on the SME segment of BSE. The notice is issued by the DCS-Listing department under the Company Related category.

## Key Points

- Equity shares of Mehul Telecom Limited are being listed on the BSE SME platform
- Notice issued by the DCS-Listing department on April 23, 2026
- Notice Number: 20260423-27
- Segment: SME (Small and Medium Enterprises)
- Annexures are attached to the notice for additional listing details

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notice under existing BSE SME listing framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Mehul Telecom Limited is required to comply with all BSE SME listing obligations post-listing
- The company must adhere to continuous disclosure and compliance requirements applicable to SME-listed entities
- Market participants and brokers should update their systems to reflect the new listing

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: April 23, 2026
- Listing Date: As specified in the attached annexures (refer to the PDF for exact trading commencement date)

## Impact Assessment

This listing expands the BSE SME segment with a new entrant in the telecom space. The impact is limited to investors participating in the SME platform and those specifically interested in Mehul Telecom Limited. Market-wide impact is low; company-specific impact is moderate as it marks the beginning of the company's publicly traded status on BSE.
