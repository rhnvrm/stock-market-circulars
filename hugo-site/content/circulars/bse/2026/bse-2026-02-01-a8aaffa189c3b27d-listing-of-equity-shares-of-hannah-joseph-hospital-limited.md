## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Hannah Joseph Hospital Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company will be listed on the SME segment with scrip code 544687, symbol HANNAH, and ISIN number INE0JVH01012. The exact listing date and further security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Hannah Joseph Hospital Limited
- Scrip Code: 544687
- Trading Symbol: HANNAH
- ISIN: INE0JVH01012
- Segment: SME
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Further security details will be provided in a subsequent notice

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of the Exchange are informed to note the scrip details for future trading purposes once the listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: February 1, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement for a new SME company. The impact is limited to the specific company and interested investors. No broader market implications. Trading members should await the separate notice for the actual listing date and complete security details before commencing trading activities.
