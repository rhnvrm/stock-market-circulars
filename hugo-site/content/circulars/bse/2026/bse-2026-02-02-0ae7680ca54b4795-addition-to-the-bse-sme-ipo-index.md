## Summary

BSE announced the addition of Hannah Joseph Hospital Limited (Exchange ticker 544687) to the BSE SME IPO Index effective February 3, 2026. The company is being listed on BSE on February 2, 2026, and will be included in the index at the open of trading on the following day.

## Key Points

- Hannah Joseph Hospital Limited (ticker: 544687) listed on BSE on February 2, 2026
- Stock will be added to BSE SME IPO Index effective February 3, 2026
- Addition will be reflected in the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- Reference made to Notice No. 20260201-4 for additional context

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index composition update.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index composition change is automatically implemented.

## Important Dates

- **February 2, 2026**: Hannah Joseph Hospital Limited listing date on BSE
- **February 3, 2026**: Effective date for addition to BSE SME IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard index maintenance action with minimal market impact. The addition affects the BSE SME IPO Index composition and may result in portfolio rebalancing for index-tracking funds. Market participants tracking this index should note the change for replication purposes. For queries, BSE directs clients to contact bseindex@bseindia.com.
