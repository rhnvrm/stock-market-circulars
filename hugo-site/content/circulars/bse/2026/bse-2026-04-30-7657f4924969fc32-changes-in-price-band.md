## Summary

BSE's Department of Surveillance (DOSS) has revised price bands for 21 equity scrips, effective May 4, 2026. The revised bands range from 2% to 20%, replacing the existing levels for each scrip. This is a routine surveillance action aimed at curbing excessive volatility or speculative activity in select securities.

## Key Points

- Price bands revised for 21 scrips across equity segment, effective May 4, 2026.
- Revised bands: 2% (2 scrips), 5% (12 scrips), 10% (2 scrips), 20% (5 scrips).
- Action taken by BSE Surveillance department (DOSS).
- Members may seek clarification at bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Regulatory Changes

Price bands are revised by BSE Surveillance as a measure to control abnormal price movements. A tightened band (e.g., 2% or 5%) restricts daily price movement, while a wider band (e.g., 20%) allows greater fluctuation. These revisions supersede previously applicable bands for the listed scrips.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must note and apply the revised price bands for the affected scrips starting May 4, 2026. No action is required beyond awareness; the exchange systems will enforce the new limits automatically. Members with queries may contact bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** April 30, 2026
- **Effective Date:** May 4, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Traders and investors holding positions in any of the 21 affected scrips should be aware of the new daily price movement limits. Scrips assigned a 2% band (Emrock Corporation, iStreet Network) face the most restrictive limits, potentially reducing liquidity. The 5 scrips assigned a 20% band (Clinitech Laboratory, Om Power Transmission, Prima Plastics, Transgene Biotek, and Clinitech Laboratory) have relatively wider movement permitted. Overall market impact is limited to these specific securities; broader indices are unaffected.
