## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 10,01,78,890 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each of ACME Solar Holdings Ltd (Scrip Code: 544283, ISIN: INE622W01025) have been listed and admitted for trading effective Monday, June 08, 2026. These shares were allotted to Qualified Institutional Buyers (QIBs) pursuant to a Qualified Institutional Placement (QIP) at an issue price of Rs. 279.50 per share.

## Key Points

- **Company:** ACME Solar Holdings Ltd (Scrip Code: 544283, ISIN: INE622W01025)
- **New Shares Listed:** 10,01,78,890 equity shares of Rs. 2/- face value each
- **Allotment Type:** Qualified Institutional Placement (QIP) to QIBs
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 279.50 per share
- **Effective Trading Date:** Monday, June 08, 2026
- **Date of Allotment:** June 04, 2026
- **Distribution Numbers:** 605971805 to 706150694
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice under BSE's Listing Operations department for newly allotted equity shares following a QIP.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to update their systems to reflect the new securities available for trading from June 08, 2026. For clarifications, members may contact Ms. Prachi Babadi at Tel. No. 022-22728035.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** June 04, 2026
- **Effective Date for Trading:** June 08, 2026 (Monday)

## Impact Assessment

The QIP adds approximately 10.02 crore new shares to the tradeable float of ACME Solar Holdings Ltd at Rs. 279.50 per share, representing a capital raise of approximately Rs. 2,800 crore. The dilution effect is relevant to existing shareholders. Since the shares are pari-passu with existing equity, no differential rights are created. The listing broadens institutional participation and may influence near-term price discovery given the known QIP issue price acting as a reference point.
