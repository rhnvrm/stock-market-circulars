## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in specific non-convertible debentures of POONAWALLA FINCORP LIMITED effective January 22, 2026. The suspension is due to the company setting a record date for debenture redemption. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the no-dealings date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspended for POONAWALLA FINCORP LIMITED debentures (ISIN: INE511C07805, Code: 975372)
- Debenture series: PWFL-8.38%-6-2-26-PVT
- Record date for redemption: January 22, 2026
- No dealings permitted from: January 22, 2026
- Notice reference: DR-804/2025-2026
- Issued by: Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President â€“ Listing Compliance and Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action notification for debenture redemption proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must immediately cease all dealing activities in the specified debentures from January 22, 2026
- Members should update their systems to reflect the trading suspension
- No buy or sell orders should be accepted for ISIN INE511C07805 from the specified date

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: January 16, 2026
- **Record Date**: January 22, 2026
- **No Dealings From**: January 22, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited impact as this affects only a specific series of non-convertible debentures of POONAWALLA FINCORP LIMITED. The suspension is temporary and related to scheduled redemption.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders whose names appear in the records as of January 22, 2026 will be eligible for redemption. No secondary market trading will be possible from the suspension date.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to ensure their systems prevent any trades in the specified debenture series from January 22, 2026 onwards.
