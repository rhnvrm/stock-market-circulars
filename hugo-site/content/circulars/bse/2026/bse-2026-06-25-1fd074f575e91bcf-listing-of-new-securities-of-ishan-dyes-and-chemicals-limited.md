## Summary

BSE has listed 4,23,280 new equity shares of Ishan Dyes and Chemicals Limited (Scrip Code: 531109, ISIN: INE561M01018) issued to promoters on a preferential basis through conversion of warrants. The shares are permitted to trade on the Exchange from Monday, June 29, 2026, and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 4,23,280 equity shares of Rs. 10/- face value issued at a premium of Rs. 53/- each (issue price Rs. 63/-)
- Issued to promoters on preferential basis via warrant conversion
- Trading commences June 29, 2026
- Distribution numbers: 26140418 to 26563697
- Date of allotment: March 21, 2026
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- All 4,23,280 shares are subject to lock-in until January 14, 2028

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notice under BSE's Listing Operations.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed that the new securities are listed and tradeable from June 29, 2026. The locked-in shares (distribution numbers 26140418–26563697) cannot be traded until January 14, 2028.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** March 21, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** June 29, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry:** January 14, 2028

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The entire new tranche of 4,23,280 shares is locked in until January 2028, meaning no additional float enters the market immediately. The preferential allotment at Rs. 63/- per share represents a fixed price point for promoter acquisition via warrant conversion. Trading members should update their records for the new distribution number range.
