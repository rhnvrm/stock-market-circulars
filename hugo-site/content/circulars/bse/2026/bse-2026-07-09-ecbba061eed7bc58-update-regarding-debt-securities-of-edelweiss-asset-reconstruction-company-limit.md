## Summary

BSE has issued a follow-up notice to its earlier communication dated February 12, 2026 (Notice No. 20260212-31) regarding the debt securities of Edelweiss Asset Reconstruction Company Limited. Trading Members are informed that the Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) bearing Scrip Code 954527 and ISIN INE015L07428 will be available for trading with revised terms, effective Friday, July 10, 2026, on account of a debt restructuring exercise undertaken by the company.

## Key Points

- This is a continuation notice referencing the original Exchange Notice No. 20260212-31 dated February 12, 2026, concerning debt restructuring of Edelweiss Asset Reconstruction Company Limited.
- Scrip Code: 954527; ISIN: INE015L07428.
- Number of NCDs originally listed: 6,614.
- Number of NCDs repurchased by the company: 928.
- Balance NCDs available post-restructuring: 5,686.
- The revised terms take effect from Friday, July 10, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced; this notice operationalizes the debt restructuring previously approved and communicated via Notice No. 20260212-31, updating the tradable quantity of NCDs under the given ISIN following partial repurchase by the company.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members are required to note the revised terms and updated balance NCD quantity for the specified ISIN before resuming trading. Queries can be directed to Mr. Akshay Arolkar or Hardik Bhuta (Assistant Vice President), DCS-Listing Department, via debt.listing@bseindia.com or +91 22 2272 8352.

## Important Dates

- February 12, 2026: Original notice (20260212-31) regarding debt restructuring.
- July 9, 2026: Current notice date.
- July 10, 2026 (Friday): Effective date for trading resumption with revised terms.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and traders of this specific NCD issuance (ISIN INE015L07428) of Edelweiss Asset Reconstruction Company Limited. The balance of tradable NCDs has been reduced from 6,614 to 5,686 following the company's repurchase of 928 NCDs as part of its debt restructuring. There is no broader market-wide impact, as this pertains to a single debt instrument in the debt segment.
