## Summary

BSE has announced the settlement schedule for the buyback of shares of FAIRCHEM ORGANICS LIMITED through the Offer to Buy - Acquisition Window mechanism. The buyback bidding window will operate from January 8-14, 2026, with settlement number 2026003. Members and custodians must deliver demat shares through the Early Pay-in mechanism, with obligations download on January 20, 2026 and final settlement on January 21, 2026.

## Key Points

- Settlement Type: Buyback with Settlement No. 2026003
- Bidding period: January 8-14, 2026
- Obligation download: January 20, 2026
- Settlement date: January 21, 2026
- Demat shares must be delivered only through Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism available in both depositories
- Market Type "Buyback" and Settlement No. 2026003 must be used by Members/Custodians in depository system
- CDSL & NSDL early pay-in securities will be earmarked/blocked in clients' accounts
- No EPN file upload required by Members & Custodians
- Client-wise stamp duty charges available in MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular implements existing buyback framework as per Revised Guidelines of Mechanism for acquisition of shares through Stock Exchange referenced in Notice No.20200528-32 dated May 28, 2020.

## Compliance Requirements

**For Members/Custodians:**
- Complete early pay-in of securities in depository system sufficiently in advance before BSE OTB Bidding window closure
- Ensure effect of early pay-in is reflected in BSE/ICCL systems before prescribed timelines for accepting/confirming bids
- Use correct Market Type "Buyback" and Settlement No. 2026003 in depository system
- Securities will be automatically earmarked/blocked in requisite market type and settlement number
- Review MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for client-wise stamp duty charges

## Important Dates

- **January 8, 2026**: Bid start date
- **January 14, 2026**: Bid end date
- **January 20, 2026**: Obligation download date
- **January 21, 2026**: Settlement date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - Limited to shareholders of FAIRCHEM ORGANICS LIMITED who wish to participate in the buyback offer.

**Operational Impact:** Medium - Members and custodians handling FAIRCHEM shares must ensure timely early pay-in of securities through depository system and coordinate with the BSE OTB platform timeline requirements. The automatic earmarking/blocking mechanism simplifies the process compared to manual EPN file uploads.
