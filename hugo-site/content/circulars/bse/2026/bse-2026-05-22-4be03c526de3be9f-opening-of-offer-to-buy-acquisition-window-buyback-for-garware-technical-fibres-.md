## Summary

BSE has notified the opening of the Offer to Buy (Acquisition Window) for the buyback of equity shares of Garware Technical Fibres Limited through the tender offer mechanism. The buyback is open to all eligible shareholders as on the record date of May 20, 2026, on a proportionate basis.

## Key Points

- Buyback quantity: Up to 16,17,500 (Sixteen Lakh Seventeen Thousand Five Hundred) fully paid-up equity shares
- Face value: INR 10/- per share
- Buyback price: INR 680/- per share (cash)
- Maximum aggregate buyback amount: INR 110,00,00,000/- (INR 110 Crore), excluding transaction costs
- Shares represent 1.63% of total paid-up equity share capital
- Buyback size is 9.46% (standalone) and 9.35% (consolidated) of aggregate paid-up equity capital and free reserves as of March 31, 2025
- Buyback is within the statutory 10% limit under Section 68(2) of the Companies Act and Regulation 5(i)(b) of SEBI Buyback Regulations

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This offer operates under existing SEBI circular no. CIR/CFD/POLICYCELL/1/2015 (April 13, 2015), circular no. CFD/DCR2/CIR/P/2016/131 (December 9, 2016), and BSE notices including 20170210-16, 20190424-35, 20200528-32, 20201102-43, and 20210825-62 governing the mechanism for share acquisition through stock exchange tender offers.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members and Custodians must note the offer window dates and facilitate eligible shareholder participation
- Participation is restricted to shareholders on record as of the record date (May 20, 2026)
- Offer must be conducted per SEBI's revised guidelines on acquisition through stock exchange tender offer mechanism
- Letter of Offer is attached for reference; full details available on the BSE website

## Important Dates

- **Record Date:** Wednesday, May 20, 2026
- **Offer Window Opens:** Tuesday, May 26, 2026
- **Offer Window Closes:** Tuesday, June 2, 2026
- **Holiday Exclusion:** Thursday, May 28, 2026 (SEBI Holiday — excluded from offer window)
- **Notice Date:** May 22, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a company-specific corporate action relevant to existing shareholders of Garware Technical Fibres Limited. The buyback at INR 680/- per share may provide an exit or partial liquidity opportunity for eligible shareholders at a defined price. The offer size (1.63% of equity) and total outlay (INR 110 crore) are relatively modest in scale. Trading members and custodians must be operationally prepared to process tender instructions within the defined window, noting the exclusion of May 28, 2026 as a SEBI holiday.
