## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260227-3 informing trading members that equity shares of Kiaasa Retail Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the BSE SME segment. The precise listing date and further securities details will be communicated via a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Kiaasa Retail Limited is being listed on the BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code assigned: **544711**
- Trading Symbol: **KIAASA**
- ISIN: **INE1C3F01018**
- The exact listing date has not yet been announced and will be notified separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing admission notice under BSE SME compliance guidelines.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to prepare for dealings in KIAASA equity shares once the listing date is officially notified
- Members should await the subsequent notice confirming the listing date and full security details before initiating trades

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** February 27, 2026
- **Listing Date:** To be announced via a separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a new SME IPO listing on BSE. The immediate market impact is limited to the SME segment. Investors and trading members interested in small and medium enterprise stocks should monitor for the follow-up notice confirming the listing date. The scrip (KIAASA, Code: 544711) will be tradeable on the SME platform once officially listed.
