## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the trade modification timing in the Equity Segment has been extended by 15 minutes, moving the deadline from 4:00 PM to 4:15 PM. This change takes effect from June 1, 2026.

## Key Points

- Trade modification timing in the Equity Segment revised from 04:00 PM to 04:15 PM
- Applicable to all trading members operating in the Equity Segment
- Notice issued by the Trading Operations department

## Regulatory Changes

The trade modification window in the Equity Segment is extended by 15 minutes, providing trading members additional time to modify trades after market close.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should update their internal workflows and back-office systems to reflect the new trade modification deadline of 4:15 PM in the Equity Segment.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** June 1, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a minor operational change that slightly extends the post-market trade modification window. It benefits trading members by providing an additional 15 minutes to process and modify trades, reducing the risk of time-bound errors. Impact on market operations is minimal.
