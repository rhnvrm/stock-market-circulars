## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document (DRHP) of Anand Seamless Limited, filed on February 27, 2026, on the SME exchange website. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and is available for public review and comments for a minimum period of 21 days from the date of notice.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document of Anand Seamless Limited is now publicly available on BSE SME website
- Filed on February 27, 2026, under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations
- Document hosted at the BSE SME public issues portal: https://www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public comments are invited within 21 days from the notice date (March 02, 2026)
- Comments to be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice is in compliance with existing ICDR Regulations requiring Draft Offer Documents to be made publicly available for a minimum of 21 days to invite stakeholder comments before proceeding with the IPO.

## Compliance Requirements

- Interested parties and the public may review the Draft Offer Document on the BSE SME website
- Any comments or objections must be submitted within 21 days of March 02, 2026 (deadline: approximately March 23, 2026)
- Comments to be sent to: equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Filing Date:** February 27, 2026
- **Notice Date:** March 02, 2026
- **Public Comment Deadline:** March 23, 2026 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited immediate market impact as it is a standard procedural disclosure for an upcoming SME IPO. It signals that Anand Seamless Limited is in the early stages of its public listing process on the BSE SME platform. Investors and analysts interested in the SME IPO pipeline should review the Draft Offer Document for investment assessment. No trading or compliance action is required from existing market participants.
