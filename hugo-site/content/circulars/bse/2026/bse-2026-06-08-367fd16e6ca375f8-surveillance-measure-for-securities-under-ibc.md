## Summary

BSE has updated the Additional Surveillance Measure (ASM) framework for companies undergoing Insolvency Resolution Process (IRP) under the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code (IBC). Securities meeting the criteria (listed in Annexure I) are brought under the framework, while certain securities (Annexure II) are moved out, both effective June 09, 2026. A consolidated list of all securities currently under the framework is provided in Annexure III.

## Key Points

- Securities in Annexure I have satisfied criteria for IBC-related Additional Surveillance Measure, effective June 09, 2026.
- Securities in Annexure II are moving out of the IBC framework effective June 09, 2026.
- Annexure III provides the full consolidated list of securities currently under the IBC surveillance framework.
- Securities under this framework are subject to periodic review.
- This framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the exchanges.
- Shortlisting under this framework is purely for market surveillance purposes and should not be construed as adverse action against any company.

## Regulatory Changes

This notice continues the framework established under:
- Exchange notice no. 20180608-34 dated June 08, 2018
- Exchange notice no. 20220520-52 dated May 20, 2022
- Exchange notice no. 20230210-55 dated February 10, 2023
- Exchange notice no. 20250905-47 dated September 05, 2025

No new regulatory framework is introduced; this is a periodic update to the existing IBC surveillance list.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants and members must take note of securities added to and removed from the IBC surveillance framework.
- Trading in securities under this framework is subject to applicable surveillance conditions (e.g., price bands, trade-for-trade settlement).
- For clarifications, members may write to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **June 08, 2026**: Notice date.
- **June 09, 2026**: Effective date for addition of new securities to, and removal of securities from, the IBC surveillance framework.

## Impact Assessment

Securities added to the IBC framework will be subject to additional surveillance conditions including modified price bands. Securities exiting the framework will have their price bands reinstated to pre-shortlisting levels, provided they are not subject to any other surveillance measure. The impact is limited to the specific scrips listed in the annexures and does not affect broader market operations. Investors and traders in the affected scrips should review the annexures carefully to understand applicable trading restrictions.
