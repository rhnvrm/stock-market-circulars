## Summary

BSE has listed further new securities issued by five companies under their respective Employee Stock Option Plan (ESOP) or Employee Stock Option Scheme (ESOS) programs. These securities are admitted for trading on the Exchange with effect from Friday, March 13, 2026. None of the newly listed shares are subject to any lock-in period.

## Key Points

- Five companies have had additional ESOP/ESOS shares listed on BSE effective March 13, 2026
- Balaji Telefilms Limited (532382): 51,997 shares (face value ₹2), dist. nos. 141759845–141811841
- Billionbrains Garage Ventures Limited (544603): 100,000,000 shares (face value ₹2), dist. nos. 6173596632–6273596631
- ICICI Prudential Life Insurance Company Limited (540133): 130,830 shares (face value ₹10), dist. nos. 1449049004–1449179833
- Tata Elxsi Limited (500408): 911 shares (face value ₹10), dist. nos. 62295773–62296683
- UTI Asset Management Company Limited (543238): 906 shares (face value ₹10), dist. nos. 128519131–128520036
- No lock-in period applies to any of the listed securities

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice informing market participants of newly listed ESOP/ESOS securities.

## Compliance Requirements

All market participants are requested to take note of the newly listed securities and their respective ISIN codes, scrip codes, and distribution number ranges for reference in trading and settlement operations.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** March 12, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** Friday, March 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is minimal and routine. The largest tranche is Billionbrains Garage Ventures Limited at 100 million shares, which could have a marginal dilutive effect. The other four companies have very small share additions (ranging from 906 to 130,830 shares), representing negligible dilution. No lock-in restrictions apply, meaning all shares are immediately tradeable. Market participants should update their records with the new distribution number ranges.
