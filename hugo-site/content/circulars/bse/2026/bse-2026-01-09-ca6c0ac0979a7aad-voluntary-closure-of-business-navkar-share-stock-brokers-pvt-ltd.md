## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Navkar Share & Stock Brokers Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 111) will cease operations in the Currency derivative segment effective January 12, 2026, due to voluntary closure of business.

## Key Points

- Navkar Share & Stock Brokers Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 111) is voluntarily closing its business
- Operations will cease in the Currency derivative segment only
- Effective date: Monday, January 12, 2026
- Trading members have been informed of this closure

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a member-specific business closure notification.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for other trading members. For clarifications, members may contact membership.ops@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **January 9, 2026**: Notice date
- **January 12, 2026**: Effective date of cessation of business in currency derivative segment

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a single member's voluntary closure in the currency derivatives segment. Other trading members and market operations remain unaffected. Clients of Navkar Share & Stock Brokers may need to transfer their currency derivative positions to other brokers before the closure date.
