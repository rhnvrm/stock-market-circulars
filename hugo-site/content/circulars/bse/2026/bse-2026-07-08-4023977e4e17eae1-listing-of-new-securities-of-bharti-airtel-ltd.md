## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 14,67,61,335 new equity shares of Bharti Airtel Ltd. (Scrip Code - 532454), each of face value Rs. 5/-, issued at a premium of Rs. 1918/- per share to promoters on a preferential basis, are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Thursday, July 9, 2026.

## Key Points

- 14,67,61,335 equity shares of Rs. 5/- each allotted to Promoters on a preferential basis
- Issue price: Rs. 1923/- per share (face value Rs. 5/- + premium Rs. 1918/-)
- Distinctive numbers: 6094392982 to 6241154316
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- ISIN: INE397D01024
- Shares are subject to lock-in until 09/01/2028

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a standard listing notification under BSE's Listing Operations department.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the listing for operational awareness; no specific compliance action is required beyond recognizing the lock-in status of these shares in trading systems.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: 23/06/2026
- Listing and trading effective from: 09/07/2026 (Thursday, July 9, 2026)
- Lock-in period ends: 09/01/2028

## Impact Assessment

The listing increases Bharti Airtel's outstanding equity share capital, but since the shares are locked-in until January 2028, there is no immediate increase in tradable float or market supply. The preferential allotment to promoters may signal continued promoter confidence in the company. Market impact is expected to be low to moderate, primarily relevant for shareholding pattern and dilution calculations rather than immediate trading dynamics.
