## Summary

BSE has notified market participants that further new securities issued by 7 companies under their Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP/ESOS) are listed and admitted for trading on BSE with effect from Monday, March 9, 2026. None of the newly listed shares are subject to any lock-in period.

## Key Points

- 7 companies have had additional ESOP/ESOS shares listed effective March 9, 2026
- No lock-in restrictions apply to any of the newly listed securities
- Total new shares listed span across equity segment
- All shares are admitted for trading without any restrictions

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification pursuant to ESOP/ESOS share allotments made by the respective companies.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. The notice is informational, advising all market participants to take note of the newly listed securities available for trading.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** March 6, 2026
- **Trading Effective From:** Monday, March 9, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry:** Not Applicable (no lock-in for any of the securities)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. These are routine ESOP/ESOS-related listings representing relatively small additions to the outstanding share capital of each company. The largest addition is L&T Finance with 7,98,240 shares, while InterGlobe Aviation has the smallest addition of just 200 shares. No trading restrictions apply, and the shares are immediately available for normal market transactions from March 9, 2026.
