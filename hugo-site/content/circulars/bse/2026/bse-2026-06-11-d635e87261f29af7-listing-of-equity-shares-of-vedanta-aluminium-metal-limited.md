## Summary

BSE has notified all market participants that the equity shares of Vedanta Aluminium Metal Limited (VAML) will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange effective Monday, June 15, 2026, under the T Group of Securities. The listing is pursuant to a NCLT-approved Composite Scheme of Arrangement involving the demerger of Vedanta Limited's Aluminium Undertaking into VAML. The scrip will remain in the Trade-for-Trade segment for 10 trading days from the date of listing.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 544780 | Scrip ID: VAML | ISIN: INE1CDF01017
- Total shares listed: 3,91,03,88,057 equity shares of Re. 1/- each (distinctive numbers 1–3910388057)
- Listed under T Group (Trade-for-Trade) for first 10 trading days per SEBI Circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 and SEBI/HO/MRD-TPD1/CIR/P/2023/55
- VAML will be part of the Special Pre-Open Session (SPOS) for IPO and Other category scrips
- Market lot: 1 | Face value: Re. 1/- fully paid
- Registered office: C-103 Atul Projects, Corporate Avenue New Link, Chakala MIDC, Mumbai – 400093
- Company Secretary & Compliance Officer: Ms. Dashmeet Rana | Email: vaml.sect@vedanta.co.in

## Regulatory Changes

The listing is governed by SEBI Circular No. CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012, and SEBI/HO/MRD-TPD1/CIR/P/2023/55 dated April 11, 2023, which mandate Trade-for-Trade settlement for new listings of this nature. The Special Pre-Open Session framework applies as per CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 and CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must note VAML's inclusion in the Trade-for-Trade segment for 10 trading days from June 15, 2026
- Participants must observe Special Pre-Open Session procedures for VAML as per the Exchange's Notice No. 20120216-29
- For SPOS clarifications, participants may contact the Helpdesk at 022-6136 3100/3171
- Lock-in details: NA

## Important Dates

- NCLT Approval Date: December 16, 2025
- Appointed Date: May 01, 2026
- Effective Date: May 01, 2026
- Date of Allotment: May 04, 2026
- Listing Date on BSE: June 15, 2026
- Trade-for-Trade period: 10 trading days from June 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a high-impact listing event. Vedanta Aluminium Metal Limited represents the Aluminium Undertaking demerged from Vedanta Limited under an NCLT-approved Composite Scheme of Arrangement. All existing shareholders of Vedanta Limited received VAML shares in a 1:1 ratio, creating a large shareholder base immediately upon listing. The Trade-for-Trade restriction for 10 days limits speculative activity and requires mandatory delivery settlement, which may constrain liquidity in the initial trading period. The special pre-open session on listing day will establish the opening price through a call auction mechanism. Vedanta Limited shareholders and institutional investors holding VAML should prepare for price discovery on June 15, 2026.
