## Summary

BSE has issued a revised schedule for the Offer to Buy (Acquisition Window/Takeover) for OSCAR GLOBAL LTD (Symbol: OSCARGLO, Scrip Code: 530713). The offer is for up to 8,58,000 equity shares at Rs. 12.15 per share. The offer period runs from January 2, 2026 to January 16, 2026. Trading members and custodian members can participate through the iBBS and RTRMS platforms during specified session timings.

## Key Points

- **Company:** OSCAR GLOBAL LTD (OSCARGLO)
- **ISIN:** INE473F01010, Scrip Code: 530713
- **Offer Size:** Up to 8,58,000 equity shares
- **Offer Price:** Rs. 12.15 per share
- **Face Value:** Rs. 10.00
- **Market Lot:** 1
- **Offer Period:** January 2, 2026 to January 16, 2026
- **Bid Offering Session:** 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM daily
- **Trading Members must ensure UCC registration with correct PAN and category**
- **Only demat shares eligible for bidding**

## Session Timings

**Bid Offering Period:** January 2-16, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)

**Give-up/Take-up:** January 2-14, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)

**Give-up (Last Day):** January 16, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)

**Take-up (Last Day):** January 16, 2026 (9:15 AM to 4:00 PM)

This revised schedule is specific to OSCAR GLOBAL LTD and primarily affects existing shareholders who may wish to tender their shares in the takeover offer.
