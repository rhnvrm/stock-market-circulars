## Summary

BSE has listed commercial papers issued by five companies on a private placement basis on its debt market segment effective January 14, 2026. The securities include fresh listings from SEIL Energy India Limited, Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited, and Kotak Mahindra Investments Limited, plus further listings from Julius Baer Capital (India) Private Limited and Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited. All commercial papers carry CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ ratings.

## Key Points

- All commercial papers will trade only in dematerialized form under specified ISIN numbers
- Standard trading denomination is Rs.5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for securities is 1 paise
- Face value for all instruments is Rs.500,000 per unit
- Interest rates range from 6.26% to 7.6% per annum
- Maturity periods vary from 7 days to 146 days
- All issuances are cumulative interest instruments

- Trading members must note that securities trade only in dematerialized form
- Standard market lot is 1 unit per the specifications
- Trading must be conducted in denominations of Rs.5 lakhs and multiples

- **Effective Trading Date**: January 14, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains to routine debt market operations. The listing provides institutional investors and debt market participants with additional short-term investment instruments rated A1+ by both major credit rating agencies. The commercial papers offer tenures ranging from ultra-short (7 days) to short-term (146 days) with competitive interest rates reflecting current money market conditions. Impact is limited to debt segment participants and does not affect equity markets or retail investors.
