## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that T-Bill 91TB060226 (ISIN: IN002025X323, Scrip Code: 805139) will mature on its redemption date and trading in this instrument will be suspended effective February 4, 2026. This is a standard procedure for maturing treasury bills under notice DR-813/2025-2026.

## Key Points

- T-Bill 91TB060226 with ISIN IN002025X323 and Scrip Code 805139 is maturing
- Trading suspension effective from February 4, 2026
- Trading members advised not to deal in this T-Bill from the suspension date
- Notice issued under reference DR-813/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice for instrument maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute trades in the specified T-Bill (Scrip Code 805139) from February 4, 2026 onwards
- Members should update their systems to reflect the trading suspension
- Ensure clients are informed about the maturity and trading restrictions

## Important Dates

- **February 3, 2026**: Notice date
- **February 4, 2026**: Trading suspension effective date
- **February 6, 2026**: Implied redemption/maturity date (91-day T-Bill)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This affects only a single T-Bill instrument nearing maturity. Standard market operation with no broader market implications.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Trading members need to update systems to block trading in this specific scrip code. Routine administrative action for debt market operations.
