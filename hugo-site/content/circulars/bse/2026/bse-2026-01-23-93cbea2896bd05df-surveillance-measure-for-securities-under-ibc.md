## Summary

BSE has issued updated surveillance measures for securities of companies under the Insolvency Resolution Process (IRP) as per the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code (IBC). Effective January 27, 2026, Radhagobind Commercial Ltd (Scrip Code: 539673, ISIN: INE792P01020) has been placed under Stage 1 Additional Surveillance Measure. No securities are moving between stages or being excluded from surveillance during this period.

## Key Points

- One security added to IBC Stage 1 surveillance: Radhagobind Commercial Ltd
- No securities in Stage 0 (Receipt of corporate announcement/disclosure)
- No securities moving from Stage I to Stage II
- No securities moving from Stage II to Stage I
- No securities excluded from IBC surveillance framework
- Changes effective from January 27, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

This circular implements the BSE's framework for Additional Surveillance Measures (ASM) for companies undergoing Insolvency Resolution Process under the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code. The framework has multiple stages:

- **Stage 0**: Receipt of corporate announcement/disclosure from the company
- **Stage 1**: Securities shortlisted in ASM for Companies relating to IRP as per IBC
- **Stage 2**: Enhanced surveillance for companies in advanced stages of insolvency proceedings

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must be aware of securities under IBC surveillance framework
- Enhanced due diligence required for securities under ASM
- Investors should note the insolvency status when trading these securities
- Price bands and trading restrictions may apply to securities under surveillance

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: January 23, 2026
- **Effective Date**: January 27, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited. Only one security is affected in this update, resulting in minimal immediate market disruption.

**Trading Impact**: Radhagobind Commercial Ltd will be subject to Additional Surveillance Measures, which may include price bands, trading restrictions, or enhanced reporting requirements. Liquidity in this security may be affected.

**Investor Impact**: Investors holding or trading Radhagobind Commercial Ltd should be aware of the company's status under IBC proceedings and the associated risks of insolvency.
