## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members that the equity shares of Kiaasa Retail Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange. The company will be listed under the SME segment. The specific date of listing and further security details will be communicated through a subsequent notice.

## Key Points

- Kiaasa Retail Limited equity shares are approved for listing on BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544711
- Symbol: KIAASA
- ISIN: INE1C3F01018
- Exact listing date to be announced via a separate notice
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this circular. This is a standard listing admission notice under BSE's SME compliance framework.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to take note of the upcoming listing of Kiaasa Retail Limited. Members should await the subsequent notice for the confirmed listing date and security-specific details before initiating any dealings.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** February 27, 2026
- **Listing Date:** To be announced via a separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited immediate market impact as it is a preliminary listing announcement without a confirmed trading date. The listing is confined to the SME segment, which typically attracts a narrower investor base. Market participants and trading members should monitor for the follow-up circular confirming the listing date and additional details for KIAASA (scrip code 544711, ISIN INE1C3F01018).
