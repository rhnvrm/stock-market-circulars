## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that additional securities issued under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and Employee Stock Option Schemes (ESOS) by 16 listed companies will be listed and admitted for trading with effect from Tuesday, July 14, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine administrative listing notice for shares arising from exercise of employee stock options, not a regulatory or policy change.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific action is required from trading members or investors. The Exchange has completed the listing formalities; the new shares are automatically added to the respective companies' listed capital and become available for trading.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 13, 2026
- Effective listing/trading date: Tuesday, July 14, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, low-impact administrative notice reflecting incremental share issuances under employee stock option schemes across 16 companies. The additional shares represent a small proportion of each company's total outstanding equity and typically cause negligible dilution. No trading restrictions, lock-ins, or special conditions apply (all companies show 'NA' under lock-in status), so the shares are freely tradable from the effective date.
