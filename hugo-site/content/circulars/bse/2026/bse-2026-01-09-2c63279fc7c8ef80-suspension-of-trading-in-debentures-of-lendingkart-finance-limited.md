## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of Lendingkart Finance Limited (Series: LFL-30-1-26-PVT, ISIN: INE090W07667, Code: 975356) effective January 15, 2026. The suspension is implemented to facilitate the redemption of debentures and payment of interest, with the record date fixed as January 15, 2026. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Company: Lendingkart Finance Limited
- ISIN: INE090W07667
- BSE Code: 975356
- Series: LFL-30-1-26-PVT
- Record Date: January 15, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- No Dealings From: January 15, 2026
- Notice Reference: DR-799/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard operational notice regarding trading suspension in specific debt instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures of Lendingkart Finance Limited from January 15, 2026
- Trading members are required to take note of the suspension and ensure compliance
- All transactions must cease on the specified date to facilitate the redemption and interest payment process

## Important Dates

- **January 9, 2026**: Notice issued by BSE
- **January 15, 2026**: Record date for redemption and interest payment; trading suspension effective from this date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited and specific to debenture holders of Lendingkart Finance Limited. The suspension is procedural and temporary, affecting only one series of NCDs.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders will receive their redemption proceeds and interest payment. Investors cannot trade these debentures from the record date onwards.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to ensure their systems and processes prevent trading in the specified ISIN from January 15, 2026. This is a routine operational requirement for debt market participants when redemption and interest payment events occur.
