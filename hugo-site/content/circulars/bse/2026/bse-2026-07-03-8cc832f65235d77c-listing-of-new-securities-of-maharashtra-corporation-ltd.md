## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 58,100 equity shares of Maharashtra Corporation Ltd. (Scrip Code: 505523), issued pursuant to conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up shares of Re.1/- each on a rights basis, are permitted to be traded with effect from Monday, July 6, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: Maharashtra Corporation Ltd. (Scrip Code: 505523)
- Quantity: 58,100 equity shares of Re.1/- each
- Nature: Conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up shares issued on rights basis
- ISIN: INE272E01027
- Distinctive Numbers: 179695136 - 179753235
- These new shares rank pari-passu with the existing equity shares of the company
- Notice signed by Janardhan Wagle, Deputy Vice President

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing permission notice, not a regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must recognize the newly fully-paid shares as tradable and pari-passu with existing equity shares from the effective date; no separate compliance action is required for market participants.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 3, 2026
- Effective trading date: Monday, July 6, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is minimal given the small quantum of shares (58,100) involved relative to the company's overall equity base. This action simply completes the process of converting previously partly-paid rights shares to fully-paid status, increasing the tradable float marginally. No material effect on price discovery or market operations is expected.
