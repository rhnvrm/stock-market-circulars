## Summary

BSE has notified that the Draft Offer Document of Complete Sports and Management India Limited, filed on June 30, 2026 under the SME IPO route, has been hosted on the SME website. The document, containing disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the SEBI ICDR Regulations, will remain available for public comments for a period of at least 21 days from the date of the notice.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Complete Sports and Management India Limited on June 30, 2026.
- Filed under the SME IPO segment, DCS-Listing department.
- Document contains disclosures as required under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations.
- Document is hosted at: bsesme.com/download/306417/SME_IPO InPrinciple/DRHP_CSML_20260630235110.pdf
- Public comments invited within 21 days from the date of notice.
- Comments to be shared via email at equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is a routine disclosure notice under the SEBI (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations for SME IPOs, requiring draft offer documents to be hosted publicly for comment before listing approval.

## Compliance Requirements

- Interested parties/public may review the Draft Offer Document and submit comments within the specified 21-day window.
- No compliance action required from existing listed companies or market participants.

## Important Dates

- Draft Offer Document filed: June 30, 2026
- Notice date: July 1, 2026
- Public comment period: 21 days from notice date (until approximately July 22, 2026)

## Impact Assessment

This notice pertains to a pre-IPO SME company and has no direct impact on currently listed securities or trading. It is procedural, aimed at ensuring public disclosure and feedback ahead of a potential SME listing on BSE.
