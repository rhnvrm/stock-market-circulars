## Summary

Mangalam Worldwide Ltd (Scrip Code: 544764) has fixed **July 10, 2026** as the record date for sub-division of its equity shares. Each existing equity share of Rs.10/- face value will be subdivided into 10 equity shares of Re.1/- each. The existing ISIN INE0JYY01011 will cease to be valid for exchange transactions on or after July 10, 2026, and a new ISIN for Re.1/- paid-up shares will be notified separately.

## Key Points

- Record date for the share sub-division is **July 10, 2026**
- Split ratio: 1 equity share of Rs.10/- face value → 10 equity shares of Re.1/- face value
- Applicable segment: Dematerialised Securities – Rolling Settlement Segment
- Existing ISIN **INE0JYY01011** (Rs.10/- paid up) will not be valid for transactions on or after July 10, 2026
- New ISIN for Re.1/- paid-up shares will be communicated via a separate notice
- Reference number: DR-669/2026-2027

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action (stock split) executed under existing listing and compliance frameworks. The notice is issued under BSE Notice No. 20260630-19 by the Listing Operations department.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that the existing ISIN INE0JYY01011 will be invalid for exchange transactions from July 10, 2026 onwards
- Members should update their systems to reflect the new face value (Re.1/-) effective July 10, 2026
- Await BSE's separate notice announcing the new ISIN for Re.1/- paid-up shares before processing transactions post-record date

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Notice Date | June 30, 2026 |
| Record Date | July 10, 2026 |
| New face value effective | July 10, 2026 |
| Old ISIN (INE0JYY01011) validity ends | July 9, 2026 (last valid trading day) |

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine stock split (1:10) corporate action. Shareholders will hold ten times the number of shares at one-tenth the face value, with no change in overall shareholding value at the time of split. Trading members and depository participants need to update their records and systems to reflect the new ISIN and face value effective July 10, 2026. Market liquidity for the scrip may improve post-split due to the reduced per-share price. The primary operational risk is using the old ISIN after the record date, which BSE has explicitly flagged as invalid.
