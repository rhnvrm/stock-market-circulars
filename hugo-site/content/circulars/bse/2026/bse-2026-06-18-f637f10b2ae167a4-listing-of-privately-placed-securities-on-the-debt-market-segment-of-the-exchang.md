## Summary

BSE has listed five new Commercial Papers (CPs) issued on a private placement basis on the BSE Debt Segment effective June 18, 2026. The issuers are Muthoot Fincorp Limited, SBICAP Securities Limited, Navi Finserv Limited, Tata Power Co. Ltd, and Bajaj Financial Securities Limited. All securities are to be traded in dematerialised form in standard denominations of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof, with a tick size of 1 paise.

## Key Points

- Five Commercial Papers freshly listed on the BSE Debt Segment effective June 18, 2026
- All CPs have a face value and paid-up value of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Trading permitted only in demat form in standard denominations of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Interest payment is cumulative on the date of redemption for all issuances
- Credit ratings range from CARE A1 to CRISIL A1+ / ICRA A1+ / IND A1+

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notice under BSE's debt market segment admission process for privately placed securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all transactions in these securities occur in dematerialised form only
- Trading must be conducted in standard denominations of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the BSE Debt Department at 22728352 / 8597 / 8995 / 5753 / 8915

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice with minimal broader market impact. The five CPs collectively represent a combined issuance of approximately Rs. 15,600 crore in face value (31,340 units × Rs. 5 Lakhs). All instruments carry high short-term credit ratings (A1 / A1+), indicating strong creditworthiness. Redemptions are scheduled between September 15–21, 2026, making these 90-day instruments. Impact is limited to institutional debt market participants and trading members active in the BSE Debt Segment.
