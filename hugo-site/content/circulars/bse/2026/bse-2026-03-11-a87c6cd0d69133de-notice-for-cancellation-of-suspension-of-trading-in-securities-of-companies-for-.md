## Summary

BSE has issued a notice cancelling the suspension of trading in securities of two companies that were previously suspended for non-compliance with certain provisions of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015. The suspension cancellation is effective March 13, 2026, and is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20260210-29 dated February 10, 2026.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension is cancelled for two companies effective March 13, 2026
- This notice continues from Exchange Notice No. 20260210-29 dated February 10, 2026
- Companies affected: Benara Bearings and Pistons Ltd (Scrip Code: 541178) and Shree Securities Ltd (Scrip Code: 538975)
- All other provisions of the original suspension notice remain unchanged

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This notice modifies the earlier suspension notice (20260210-29) by exempting two specific securities from the suspension that was imposed for non-compliance with SEBI LODR Regulations, 2015.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants are requested to take note of the cancellation of suspension for the listed scrips
- All other provisions of the original notice No. 20260210-29 dated February 10, 2026 remain in force
- For clarifications, market participants may contact BSE at bse.soplodr@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **February 10, 2026**: Original suspension notice (No. 20260210-29) issued
- **March 11, 2026**: Current cancellation notice issued
- **March 13, 2026**: Effective date for cancellation of suspension (trading resumes)

## Impact Assessment

Investors and traders holding or tracking Benara Bearings and Pistons Ltd (541178) and Shree Securities Ltd (538975) will be able to resume normal trading in these securities from March 13, 2026. The cancellation suggests these companies have addressed the LODR non-compliance issues that led to the original suspension. Market impact is limited to participants with exposure to these two specific scrips.
