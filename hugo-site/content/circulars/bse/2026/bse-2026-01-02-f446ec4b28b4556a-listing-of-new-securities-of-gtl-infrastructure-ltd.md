## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted for trading 2,60,409 equity shares of GTL Infrastructure Ltd. (Scrip Code: 532775) with effect from Monday, January 05, 2026. These shares were issued at par value of Rs. 10/- each pursuant to the conversion of Foreign Currency Convertible Bonds (FCCBs) of the Company.

## Key Points

- Total shares listed: 2,60,409 equity shares
- Face value: Rs. 10/- per share
- Issue price: At par (Rs. 10/-)
- Origin: Conversion of Foreign Currency Convertible Bonds (FCCBs)
- Scrip Code: 532775
- ISIN: INE221H01019
- Ranking: Pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distinctive Numbers: 4370345347 to 4370605755

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for shares arising from FCCB conversion.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of the Exchange are informed to take note of the new securities being available for trading from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** January 02, 2026
- **Date of Allotment:** November 09, 2017
- **Effective Trading Date:** Monday, January 05, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. The listing represents a small number of shares (approximately 2.6 lakh) issued from a historical FCCB conversion that occurred in 2017. The shares rank pari-passu with existing equity, meaning they carry the same rights and privileges. This is primarily an administrative listing with no dilution concerns for current shareholders as the conversion occurred years ago.
