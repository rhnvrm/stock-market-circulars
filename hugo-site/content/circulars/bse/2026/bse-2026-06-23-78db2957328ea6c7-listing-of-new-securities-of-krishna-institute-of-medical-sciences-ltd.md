## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 1,98,67,549 equity shares of Krishna Institute of Medical Sciences Ltd (Scrip Code: 543308, ISIN: INE967H01025) are listed and admitted for trading effective Wednesday, June 24, 2026. These shares were allotted to Qualified Institutional Buyers (QIBs) pursuant to a Qualified Institutional Placement (QIP) at an issue price of Rs. 755 per share.

## Key Points

- 1,98,67,549 equity shares of Rs. 2/- face value each are being listed on BSE
- Shares allotted to QIBs under a Qualified Institutional Placement (QIP)
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Issue price: Rs. 755/- per share
- Date of allotment: June 19, 2026
- Distribution numbers: 400138936 to 420006484
- Effective trading date: June 24, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notice following completion of a QIP allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to update their systems to reflect the newly listed securities of Krishna Institute of Medical Sciences Ltd under Scrip Code 543308. For clarifications, members may contact Ms. Prachi Babadi at Tel. 022-22728035.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** June 19, 2026
- **Effective Listing/Trading Date:** June 24, 2026 (Wednesday)

## Impact Assessment

The QIP listing adds approximately 1.99 crore new equity shares to the tradeable float of KIMS on BSE at an issue price of Rs. 755, implying a capital raise of approximately Rs. 1,499 crore. The new shares are pari-passu with existing shares and will be fully tradeable from June 24, 2026. This is a dilutive event for existing shareholders but reflects institutional investor confidence at the QIP pricing level. Trading members should note the expanded share count when handling orders in KIMS scrip.
