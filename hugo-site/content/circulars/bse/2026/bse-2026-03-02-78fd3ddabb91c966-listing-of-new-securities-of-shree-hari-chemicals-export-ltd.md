## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 6,05,494 new equity shares of Shree Hari Chemicals Export Ltd. (Scrip Code: 524336) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Wednesday, March 4, 2026. These shares were issued to Promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to the conversion of Compulsory Convertible Debentures (CCDs).

## Key Points

- 6,05,494 equity shares of face value Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 69/- per share (total issue price Rs. 79/-)
- Shares issued to Promoters on a preferential basis via conversion of CCDs
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 4986141 to 5591634
- ISIN: INE065E01017
- All 6,05,494 shares are subject to a lock-in period until June 4, 2027

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification in accordance with BSE listing requirements for newly allotted securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities and their eligibility for trading from March 4, 2026
- Lock-in restriction applies: the 6,05,494 shares (Dist. Nos. 4986141–5591634) cannot be traded until June 4, 2027

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** December 4, 2025
- **Effective Trading Date:** March 4, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry:** June 4, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. The allotment represents a relatively small preferential issue to promoters via CCD conversion. Since all newly listed shares are under lock-in until June 2027, there is no immediate increase in free-float or selling pressure in the market. Existing shareholders experience marginal dilution at Rs. 79/- per share.
