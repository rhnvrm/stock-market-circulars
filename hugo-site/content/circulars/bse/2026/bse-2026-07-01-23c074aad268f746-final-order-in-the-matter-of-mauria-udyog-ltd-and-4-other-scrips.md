## Summary

BSE has issued a notice to all trading members regarding a SEBI final order dated June 30, 2026 (Order No. WTM/AS/IVD-2/ID16/32460/2026-27) in the matter of Mauria Udyog Ltd. and 4 other scrips. This is a continuation of earlier Exchange notices dated April 12, 2024 and June 19, 2023. The order restrains multiple noticees from accessing the securities market and from buying, selling, or otherwise dealing in securities, directly or indirectly, for varying periods.

## Key Points

- SEBI order restrains noticees from accessing the securities market and dealing in securities directly or indirectly, or being associated with the securities market in any manner.
- Restraint periods vary by noticee: 7 years (Noticee 1), 6 years (Noticees 3 to 7), 5 years (Noticees 8-11, 145, 152, 167, and 203-225), and 4 years (remaining noticees except Noticees 2, 46, 69, 127 and 199).
- The period of restraint will be adjusted against any period already undergone under the prior Interim Order.
- Noticees with open positions in exchange-traded derivative contracts as of the order date may close out/square off such positions within 3 months from the date of the order.
- Noticees are permitted to settle pay-in and pay-out obligations for transactions that took place before close of trading on the order date.
- The order is effective from its date of issuance (June 30, 2026).

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework changes; this is an enforcement action under existing SEBI powers, continuing from an earlier interim order dated in 2023/2024 in the same matter.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must take note of the restrained noticees and ensure no securities market access or dealing is facilitated for them.
- Trading members should ensure compliance in respect of any open derivative positions held by restrained noticees, allowing closure/square-off within the permitted 3-month window.
- Members must facilitate settlement of pre-order pay-in/pay-out obligations for the restrained entities as applicable.

## Important Dates

- SEBI Order Date: June 30, 2026
- Order Effective Date: June 30, 2026 (date of the order)
- Deadline to close out/square off open derivative positions: within 3 months from June 30, 2026 (by approximately September 30, 2026)
- Related prior notices: April 12, 2024 (Notice No. 20240412-1) and June 19, 2023 (Notice No. 20230619-58)

## Impact Assessment

The order has a direct and significant impact on the specific noticees named in the SEBI order, who face multi-year market restraints. For the broader market and trading members, the impact is primarily operational and compliance-related — ensuring restrained entities are not permitted to trade and that any legacy derivative positions are wound down within the stipulated timeframe. No broad market-wide trading disruption is expected.
