## Summary

BSE has issued this notice as a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20260601-36 dated June 1, 2026, regarding the SAT order in the matter of M/s Veerkrupa Jewellers Limited involving NNM Securities Private Limited and Miker Financial Consultants Private Limited. The Securities Appellate Tribunal (SAT) granted a stay of debarment and recovery proceedings, conditional on the deposit of 50% of the penalty amount. SEBI has since confirmed receipt of ₹15,00,000 from the Noticees.

## Key Points

- SAT order dated June 24, 2026 granted stay of debarment and recovery proceedings against the Noticees
- Stay is conditional on deposit of 50% of the penalty amount within four weeks from the order date
- SEBI confirmed via email dated June 29, 2026 that ₹15,00,000 has been received from NNM Securities Private Limited and Miker Financial Consultants Private Limited
- This is a follow-up to BSE Notice No. 20260601-36 dated June 1, 2026
- All trading members are advised to take note and ensure compliance

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice updates the status of an existing enforcement action, confirming that the SAT-imposed condition for stay of debarment has been satisfied with the receipt of the partial penalty amount by SEBI.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are advised to take note of the SAT order and SEBI's confirmation of penalty receipt. No direct compliance action is required from trading members beyond awareness of the status update in this matter.

## Important Dates

- June 1, 2026: Original BSE Notice No. 20260601-36 issued
- June 24, 2026: SAT order granting conditional stay of debarment and recovery proceedings
- June 29, 2026: SEBI confirmed receipt of ₹15,00,000 from the Noticees
- Within four weeks of June 24, 2026 (by approximately July 22, 2026): Deadline for 50% penalty deposit as per SAT order

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to the specific parties involved — NNM Securities Private Limited and Miker Financial Consultants Private Limited. The SAT stay, now supported by the confirmed penalty deposit, provides interim relief to these entities from debarment and recovery proceedings. General trading members are not directly affected but should remain aware of the ongoing regulatory proceedings in the Veerkrupa Jewellers matter.
