## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 4,85,94,390 new equity shares of KS Smart Technologies Limited (Scrip Code: 516038, ISIN: INE737E01011) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Wednesday, June 17, 2026. The shares were allotted on March 31, 2026, on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- **3,45,94,390 equity shares** at Rs. 10/- each (at par) issued to **Promoters** via preferential allotment on conversion of warrants
- **1,40,00,000 equity shares** at Rs. 20/- each (Rs. 10/- face value + Rs. 10/- premium) issued to **Non-Promoters** via preferential allotment on conversion of warrants
- Total new shares listed: **4,85,94,390**
- Distribution numbers: 115475611 to 164070000
- All new shares rank **pari-passu** with the existing equity shares of the company

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for securities already allotted on March 31, 2026.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to permit trading in these newly listed securities from June 17, 2026 onwards
- Lock-in restrictions must be observed for the allotted shares as detailed below

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** March 31, 2026
- **Effective date of listing/trading:** June 17, 2026
- **Lock-in until:** February 15, 2027 — for 3,45,94,390 promoter shares (Dist. Nos. 115475611 to 150070000)
- **Lock-in until:** February 14, 2027 — for 1,40,00,000 non-promoter shares (Dist. Nos. 150070001 to 164070000)

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds approximately 4.86 crore shares to the tradeable equity base of KS Smart Technologies Limited, representing a material increase in share capital. However, the majority of shares (3,45,94,390 promoter shares) are locked in until February 15, 2027, and the non-promoter shares until February 14, 2027, limiting immediate float impact. Existing shareholders face dilution from this preferential allotment. The event is operationally routine for trading members.
