## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures of Lendingkart Finance Limited (ISIN: INE090W08038, Code: 975718) effective January 13, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing the record date for redemption on exercise of call option and payment of interest. Trading members are explicitly advised not to deal in these debentures from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Trading in Lendingkart Finance Limited debentures (LFL-16%-20-5-26-PVT) to be suspended
- ISIN: INE090W08038
- BSE Code: 975718
- Record date fixed: January 13, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption on exercise of call option and payment of interest
- No dealings permitted from January 13, 2026
- Circular reference: DR-787/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a corporate action notification regarding temporary trading suspension for specific debentures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from January 13, 2026
- All trading members are required to take note of this suspension
- Trading activities in these debentures must cease as per the notice

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 8, 2026
- **Record Date**: January 13, 2026
- **No Dealings From**: January 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: High - Complete trading suspension affects liquidity for holders of these specific debentures. Investors holding these securities will not be able to trade them from the record date.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to prevent any transactions in these debentures. The suspension affects the 16% coupon debentures maturing on May 20, 2026.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders should note that redemption on exercise of call option will be processed based on holdings as of the record date. Interest payment will also be processed for eligible holders.
