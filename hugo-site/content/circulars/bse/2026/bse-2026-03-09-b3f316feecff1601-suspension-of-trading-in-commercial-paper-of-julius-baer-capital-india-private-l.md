## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members that the commercial paper of Julius Baer Capital (India) Private Limited (Scrip Code: 730732, ISIN: INE824H14TA3) is set to mature on March 11, 2026. Trading in this commercial paper is suspended effective March 10, 2026, ahead of its redemption date.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of Julius Baer Capital (India) Private Limited (Scrip Code: 730732) is maturing on its redemption date of March 11, 2026.
- ISIN: INE824H14TA3, identified as JBCIPL-11-03-26-CP.
- Trading members are advised not to deal in this commercial paper with effect from March 10, 2026.
- Notice reference: DR-836/2025-2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice pursuant to the scheduled maturity of a commercial paper instrument listed on the BSE Debt Market Segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in Scrip Code 730732 (ISIN: INE824H14TA3) from March 10, 2026 onwards.
- Members should update their systems to reflect the suspension of this commercial paper.

## Important Dates

- **March 9, 2026**: Notice issued by BSE.
- **March 10, 2026**: Effective date of trading suspension.
- **March 11, 2026**: Scheduled redemption/maturity date of the commercial paper.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited and routine. This suspension affects only one commercial paper instrument (JBCIPL-11-03-26-CP) in the debt segment. Trading members holding or dealing in this instrument must ensure no transactions are executed from March 10, 2026. The maturity event is expected and poses no systemic risk to the broader market.
