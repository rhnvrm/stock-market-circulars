## Summary

BSE has notified the listing and permission to trade of 34,31,446 new equity shares of Ratnaveer Precision Engineering Limited (Scrip Code 543978) with effect from Monday, June 29, 2026. These shares were issued at Rs. 159.25 per share (face value Rs. 10/- plus a premium of Rs. 149.25) to Promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 34,31,446 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each listed and permitted to trade from June 29, 2026
- Shares issued at issue price of Rs. 159.25/- (Rs. 10 face value + Rs. 149.25 premium)
- Issued to Promoters on a preferential basis via conversion of warrants
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution Numbers: 67995236 to 71426681
- ISIN: INE05CZ01011
- Date of allotment: May 12, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification for newly issued securities pursuant to an already completed preferential allotment and warrant conversion.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed that the new securities are listed and permitted to trade from June 29, 2026. All applicable trading rules and lock-in restrictions must be observed.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** May 12, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** June 29, 2026 (Monday)
- **Lock-in Expiry:** February 11, 2028

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 34,31,446 new equity shares to Ratnaveer Precision Engineering Limited's share capital. However, all newly issued shares are subject to a lock-in period until February 11, 2028, meaning they cannot be traded until that date. As a result, there is no immediate impact on the company's free float or secondary market liquidity. The preferential allotment at Rs. 159.25 per share to Promoters reflects confidence in the company at that valuation level.
