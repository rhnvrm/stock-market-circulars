## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the units of Citius TransNet Investment Trust will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange. The trust has been assigned scrip code 544753, trading symbol CITIUSINVT, and ISIN INE2Q7823014. The specific listing date and further security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Citius TransNet Investment Trust units are to be listed on BSE under the Equity segment.
- Scrip Code: 544753
- Symbol: CITIUSINVT
- ISIN: INE2Q7823014
- Listing date to be announced via a separate notice.
- Notice issued by the DCS-Listing department, signed by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing admission notice for a new investment trust.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to take note of the forthcoming listing of Citius TransNet Investment Trust units and await the separate notice that will specify the exact listing date and security details before commencing dealings.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: April 28, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced via a separate notice.

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a moderate market impact as it introduces a new investment trust to BSE's equity segment, expanding the available investment instruments. Trading members should monitor for the follow-up notice confirming the listing date. Investors interested in infrastructure or transnet-related investment trusts may find this listing relevant for portfolio consideration.
