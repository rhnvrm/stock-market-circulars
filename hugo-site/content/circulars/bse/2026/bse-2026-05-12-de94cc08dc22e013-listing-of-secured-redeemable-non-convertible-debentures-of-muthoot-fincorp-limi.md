## Summary

BSE has admitted Secured Redeemable Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of Muthoot Fincorp Limited (Tranche III) for trading on the Exchange effective Wednesday, May 13, 2026. The securities will trade under the 'F GROUP' in the Debt segment, exclusively in dematerialized form with a unit market lot of one bond.

## Key Points

- Muthoot Fincorp Limited's Secured Redeemable NCDs (Tranche III) are listed on BSE's Debt segment under F GROUP
- Trading commences on May 13, 2026
- All trades must be in dematerialized form as per SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000
- Market lot size is one bond (unit market lot)
- Company's financial year ends on March 31
- Registrar and Share Transfer Agent: Integrated Registry Management Services Private Limited, Chennai

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The listing follows existing SEBI guidelines (February 16, 2000) mandating that bonds be traded only in dematerialized form.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must ensure all trades in this scrip are executed in dematerialized form only
- Trades must be in unit market lots (one bond per lot)
- Trading Members may contact Hardik Bhuta at BSE (022-22728814) or Sachu Sivas at Muthoot Fincorp (sachu.sivas@muthoot.com, +914714911621) for clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** May 12, 2026
- **Trading Commencement Date:** May 13, 2026 (Wednesday)

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard debt listing notice with limited broad market impact. It provides debt market participants and investors an opportunity to trade Muthoot Fincorp's Tranche III NCDs on the BSE platform. Muthoot Fincorp Limited, headquartered in Trivandrum, Kerala, is part of the Muthoot Group and is active in the NBFC/financial services sector. The listing enhances liquidity for NCD holders and expands the company's publicly tradable debt instruments on the exchange.
