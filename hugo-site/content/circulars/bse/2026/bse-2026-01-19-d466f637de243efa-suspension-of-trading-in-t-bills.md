## Summary

BSE has issued a notice suspending trading in two Treasury Bills (T-Bills) that are maturing on their redemption date. Trading members are advised not to deal in these securities effective from January 20, 2026.

## Key Points

- Two T-Bills will be suspended from trading due to maturity
- Scrip Code 805095 (ISIN: IN002025Y172) - 182TB22126
- Scrip Code 805018 (ISIN: IN002024Z412) - 364TB22126
- Trading suspension effective from January 20, 2026
- Notice reference: DR-802/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural notice for T-Bill maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease dealing in the mentioned T-Bills from January 20, 2026
- Members should update their systems to reflect the trading suspension
- No new trades should be executed in these securities

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 19, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Date**: January 20, 2026
- **Redemption Date**: On or around January 20, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine maturity event affecting only two T-Bill instruments in the debt segment.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Trading members need to ensure their systems are updated to prevent trading in these maturing securities. This is a standard operational procedure that occurs regularly with maturing debt instruments.

**Investor Impact**: No significant impact on investors as T-Bills are reaching natural maturity and will be redeemed according to their terms.
