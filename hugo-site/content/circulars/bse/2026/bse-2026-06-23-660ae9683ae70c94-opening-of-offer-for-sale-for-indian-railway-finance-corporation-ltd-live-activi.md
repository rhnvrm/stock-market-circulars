## Summary

BSE issued Notice No. 20260623-36 on 23 June 2026 announcing the live activities schedule for the opening of the Offer for Sale (OFS) of shares of Indian Railway Finance Corporation Ltd (IRFC). This notice is issued by the Trading Operations department under the Equity segment.

## Key Points

- The Offer for Sale (OFS) for Indian Railway Finance Corporation Ltd (IRFC) is being opened on BSE.
- A detailed Live Activities Schedule has been published (attachment: Live_Activity_Schedule_IRFC_LTD_Offer_For_Sale) outlining the operational timeline and activity sequence for the OFS.
- The notice falls under the Trading category and Equity segment, issued by the Trading Operations department.
- IRFC is a government-backed NBFC (Non-Banking Financial Company) that finances Indian Railways' capital expenditure.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice is operational in nature, providing the activity schedule for the OFS mechanism as per existing SEBI OFS guidelines.

## Compliance Requirements

- Stock brokers and trading members must follow the live activities schedule for the IRFC OFS as published in the attached document.
- Participants must adhere to the specified timelines for order entry, modification, and cancellation windows during the OFS.
- Retail and non-retail investors should be routed through the appropriate bidding windows as per BSE OFS operational guidelines.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 23 June 2026
- **OFS Opening:** As per the Live Activities Schedule attached to Notice No. 20260623-36 (refer to the PDF for specific session timings).

## Impact Assessment

This OFS is a high-impact market event given IRFC's status as a major PSU listed on Indian exchanges. The live activities schedule is critical for trading members to plan order flow and ensure timely participation. Retail investors bidding at the cut-off price and non-retail (institutional) investors placing price bids must align operations with the published schedule. Any deviation from the schedule timings may result in missed participation windows.
