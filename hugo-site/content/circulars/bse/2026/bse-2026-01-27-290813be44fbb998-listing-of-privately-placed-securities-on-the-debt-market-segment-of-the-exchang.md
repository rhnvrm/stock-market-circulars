## Summary

BSE has listed commercial papers issued by six companies on its debt market segment effective January 27, 2026. The securities were issued on private placement basis and will trade in dematerialized form only. All commercial papers have standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs with tick size of 1 paise.

## Key Points

- Six issuers listed commercial papers totaling 17,214 securities
- All securities trade in demat form only under designated ISIN numbers
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5,00,000 and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Interest rates range from 7.40% to 9.40% p.a.
- Tenure ranges from 77 days to 365 days
