## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members that the equity shares of Elfin Agro India Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange. The company will trade under Scrip Code 544724, Symbol ELFIN, on the SME segment. The specific listing date will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Elfin Agro India Limited equity shares are approved for listing on BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544724 | Symbol: ELFIN | ISIN: INE1FEW01013
- Exact listing date and security details to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Nitinkumar Pujari, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing admission notice under BSE's standard listing procedures for SME companies.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should note the upcoming listing of ELFIN on the SME segment
- Members should await the subsequent notice for the confirmed listing date and full security details before initiating trades

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** March 11, 2026
- **Listing Date:** To be announced via a separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a preliminary listing notice for a new SME scrip. Impact is limited to market participants interested in the SME segment and investors tracking Elfin Agro India Limited. Trading in the shares cannot commence until the formal listing date notice is issued. The listing expands the SME segment universe on BSE.
