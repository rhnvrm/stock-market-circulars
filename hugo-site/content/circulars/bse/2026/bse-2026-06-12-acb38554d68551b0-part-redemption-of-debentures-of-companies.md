## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Kolte-Patil Developers Ltd, Mahaveer Finance India Limited, and Renew Solar Energy (Jharkhand Three) Private Limited have fixed 15 June 2026 as the record date for part redemption of their non-convertible debentures (NCDs). From the ex-date onwards, the respective debentures will trade at reduced face values as specified below.

## Key Points

- Seven NCD instruments across three companies are undergoing part redemption
- Record date and ex-date for all instruments: 15 June 2026
- All instruments are under settlement DR 651/2026-2027
- Trading in these debentures will continue but at reduced face values effective from the ex-date
- All instruments are privately placed (PVT series)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notice informing the market of part redemption events and the resulting reduced face values for affected NCD instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the reduced face values for all seven instruments effective from 15 June 2026
- Members should update their systems to reflect the new reduced face values for trading purposes
- Interest / part redemption payments will be made to eligible debenture holders as of the record date

## Important Dates

- **Record Date:** 15 June 2026 (for all seven instruments)
- **Ex-Date:** 15 June 2026 (for all seven instruments)
- **Settlement:** DR 651/2026-2027
