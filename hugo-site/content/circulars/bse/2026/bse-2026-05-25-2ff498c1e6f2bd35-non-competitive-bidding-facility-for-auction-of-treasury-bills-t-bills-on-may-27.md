## Summary

BSE has announced the non-competitive bidding facility for the Government of India's auction of three Treasury Bills on May 27, 2026. Trading members can participate through the NCB-GSec module of BSE's iBBS web-based system. This is in continuation of Exchange circular no. 20180423-42 dated April 26, 2018.

## Key Points

- GoI will sell three Treasury Bills (91-day, 182-day, 364-day) via RBI auction on May 27, 2026
- Non-competitive bidding available through BSE's iBBS platform at https://ibbs.bseindia.com >> NCB-GSec module
- Bidding opens May 25, 2026 from 10:00 am with 24-hour availability
- Minimum lot size is Rs. 100 for all three T-Bills
- Minimum bid amount is Rs. 10,000 for all three T-Bills

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This notice operates under the framework established by Exchange circular no. 20180423-42 (April 26, 2018) on the non-competitive bidding facility for Government Securities auctions conducted by RBI.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the NCB-GSec module on the iBBS platform to submit bids
- Members must submit bids by May 27, 2026 at 8:00 am (deadline for members)
- Direct investors must submit bids by May 26, 2026 at 5:00 pm
- Contact Trading Operations for user ID creation, password reset, or bidding queries
- Contact ICCL Operations for obligation, pay-in, and settlement-related queries

## Important Dates

| Event | Date/Time |
|---|---|

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice for debt market participants. Trading members seeking to participate in the GoI T-Bill auction have a narrow window to submit bids through BSE's NCB-GSec system. The total issue size across all three bills is Rs. 24,000 crore, reflecting standard weekly government borrowing activity. Settlement occurs on May 29, 2026 (T+2). Impact is limited to members and direct investors active in the primary debt market.
