## Summary

BSE has issued an update to the Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LT-ASM) framework. Effective June 10, 2026, 100% margins will be applicable on all open positions as of June 9, 2026 and on new positions created thereafter. Securities moving to higher LT-ASM stages and a lower price band will be applicable from June 8, 2026. Additionally, certain securities will continue under LT-ASM but be transferred to the T/XT/MT/TS group effective June 10, 2026. Specific securities exiting the framework are also identified.

## Key Points

- 100% margins imposed on securities under LT-ASM framework w.e.f. June 10, 2026 (covering open positions as of June 9, 2026 and all new positions)
- Securities moved to higher stages of the LT-ASM framework w.e.f. June 8, 2026 (Annexure I, Part B)
- Lower Price Band applicable w.e.f. June 8, 2026 for newly included securities
- Certain securities to continue in LT-ASM but transferred to T/XT/MT/TS Group w.e.f. June 10, 2026 (Annexure I, Part D)
- Securities moving out of the LT-ASM framework w.e.f. June 8, 2026 listed in Annexure II
- Consolidated list of all securities under the framework available in Annexure III
- Shortlisting is based on XBRL submissions by listed companies and should not be construed as adverse action against the company

## Regulatory Changes

This notice is issued under the revised LT-ASM framework referenced in Exchange notices: 20180321-46, 20181027-1, 20201204-56, 20240809-46, and 20240920-63. The LT-ASM framework revision includes periodic review of securities placed under surveillance. The price band of scrips exiting the framework will revert to the applicable price band prior to shortlisting, unless subject to another surveillance measure.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must ensure 100% margins are maintained on LT-ASM securities from June 10, 2026
- Members should refer to the attached Annexures (I through III) for the specific list of securities affected
- The LT-ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures
- For clarifications, members may write to bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **June 8, 2026**: Securities moved to higher LT-ASM stages take effect; Lower Price Band applicable; Securities exiting LT-ASM framework (Annexure II) effective
- **June 9, 2026**: Reference date for existing open positions subject to 100% margin
- **June 10, 2026**: 100% margins applicable on open positions and new positions; Securities transferred to T/XT/MT/TS Group effective

## Impact Assessment

The imposition of 100% margins under LT-ASM significantly increases the cost of holding and trading in affected securities, effectively restricting speculative activity and reducing liquidity. The transfer of certain securities to the T/XT/MT/TS trade-for-trade group further limits intraday trading. Lower price bands cap downside movement on a daily basis. These measures collectively aim to curb unusual price movements and protect investors in volatile or potentially manipulated securities. Traders and investors holding positions in listed LT-ASM securities must arrange for additional margins by June 9, 2026 to avoid forced liquidation.
