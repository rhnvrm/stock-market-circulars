## Summary

BSE has published the trading and settlement programme for its Mutual Fund segment covering the period February 1-28, 2026. The circular provides detailed settlement schedules for mutual fund subscriptions (both liquid and non-liquid schemes) and redemptions, including settlement numbers, transaction dates, funds pay-in dates, and units/securities payout dates.

## Key Points

- Settlement schedule covers 20 settlement cycles (SETM_NO 2526207 to 2526226) for February 2026
- Non-liquid MF subscriptions follow T+1 payout cycle (units credited next business day)
- Liquid MF subscriptions follow T+0 payout cycle (same day settlement)
- Funds payout to AMCs for non-liquid transactions happens before 3:00 PM on T day
- NFO (New Fund Offering) scheme payouts occur on T day based on funds confirmation received by ICCL till 6:00 PM
- Redemption settlement dates align with transaction dates, with actual payout based on scheme-specific TAT (T1 to T10)
- Settlement cycle accounts for market holidays on February 7, 14, 19, 26, 2026 (no settlement numbers assigned)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine operational schedule announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must adhere to the published settlement schedule for mutual fund transactions
- AMCs must process funds received before 3:00 PM on T day for non-liquid schemes
- For NFO schemes, funds confirmation must be provided to ICCL by 6:00 PM on T day
- Investors should verify redemption TAT for specific schemes using the scheme master on BSE Star MF Platform

## Important Dates

- **Settlement Period**: February 1-28, 2026
- **First Settlement**: February 2, 2026 (SETM_NO 2526207)
- **Last Settlement**: February 27, 2026 (SETM_NO 2526226)
- **Market Holidays** (no settlements): February 7, 14, 19, 26, 2026
- **Non-Liquid Funds Payout**: Before 3:00 PM on transaction day
- **NFO Funds Confirmation Deadline**: 6:00 PM on transaction day

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a standard monthly operational schedule that ensures orderly settlement of mutual fund transactions on BSE Star MF platform.

**Operational Impact**: Market participants, AMCs, and investors can plan their mutual fund transactions for February 2026 based on the published schedule. The schedule maintains consistency with standard settlement practices (T+1 for non-liquid, T+0 for liquid schemes).

**Investor Impact**: Investors can reference this schedule to understand when their subscription units will be credited or redemption proceeds will be paid out, factoring in scheme-specific TAT timelines available on the BSE Star MF Platform.
