## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (No. 20260224-7) informing trading members that the equity shares of Yashhtej Industries (India) Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the BSE SME platform. The specific listing date and full security details are to be communicated through a subsequent notice.

## Key Points

- Equity shares of Yashhtej Industries (India) Limited are approved for listing on BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code assigned: **544708**
- Trading Symbol: **YASHHTEJ**
- ISIN Number: **INE1O6P01016**
- Actual listing date and security details will be communicated via a separate notice
- Notice issued by Nitinkumar Pujari, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing admission notice for a new SME IPO on the BSE SME platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are advised to note the scrip details and await the separate notice confirming the listing date before initiating trades.
- Members should update their systems with the assigned Scrip Code (544708), Symbol (YASHHTEJ), and ISIN (INE1O6P01016) in preparation for trading.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** February 24, 2026
- **Listing Date:** To be announced via a separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This notice marks the preliminary step in the BSE SME listing process for Yashhtej Industries (India) Limited. The impact is moderate and limited to participants in the SME segment. Investors and traders should monitor for the follow-up notice confirming the exact listing date and detailed security information before making any trading decisions. No immediate market disruption is expected.
