## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Kinara Capital Private Limited has fixed a record date of 10 March 2026 for the redemption and interest payment of its Non-Convertible Debentures (KCPL-13.74%-25-3-26-PVT, scrip code 973862, ISIN INE200W07415). Trading in these debentures is suspended with effect from 10 March 2026.

## Key Points

- Trading in Kinara Capital Private Limited debentures (KCPL-13.74%-25-3-26-PVT) is suspended from 10 March 2026
- ISIN: INE200W07415, Scrip Code: 973862
- Record date fixed at 10 March 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- Notice reference: DR-836/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice issued under existing BSE framework for debt segment corporate actions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures (INE200W07415 / scrip code 973862) from 10 March 2026 onwards
- Members must take note of the no-dealings directive and ensure compliance

## Important Dates

- **Record Date:** 10 March 2026
- **No Dealings From:** 10 March 2026
- **Notice Date:** 09 March 2026

## Impact Assessment

This suspension is limited in scope, affecting only one privately placed NCD series of Kinara Capital Private Limited. Holders of these debentures will receive redemption proceeds and accrued interest as of the record date. Market impact is minimal given the private placement nature of the instrument and the routine nature of the action.
