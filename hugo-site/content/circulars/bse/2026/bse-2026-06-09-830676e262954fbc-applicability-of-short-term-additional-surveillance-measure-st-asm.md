## Summary

BSE has notified the inclusion of securities under the Short Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) framework. Securities included in ST-ASM Stage I will attract a minimum margin of 50% (capped at 100%), while those in ST-ASM Stage II will attract a mandatory 100% margin, both effective June 11, 2026.

## Key Points

- Securities satisfying ST-ASM Stage I criteria: applicable margin rate is 50% or existing margin, whichever is higher (maximum capped at 100%).
- Securities satisfying ST-ASM Stage II criteria: applicable margin rate is 100% or existing margin, whichever is higher (maximum capped at 100%).
- Enhanced margins apply to all open positions as on June 10, 2026 and all new positions created from June 11, 2026.
- The full list of shortlisted securities is provided in the attached Annexure I.
- ASM inclusion is a surveillance measure and should not be construed as adverse action against the listed company.
- Upon exit from the framework, the price band of the scrip reverts to the band applicable before inclusion, unless covered under another surveillance measure.

## Regulatory Changes

This notice is issued in continuation of earlier ASM framework revision circulars: 20240920-71 (Sep 20, 2024), 20181027-1 (Oct 27, 2018), 20190719-33 (Jul 19, 2019), 20201204-56 (Dec 4, 2020), 20230925-49 (Sep 25, 2023), and 20240920-63 (Sep 20, 2024). The current circular applies the existing ST-ASM framework to newly shortlisted securities without structural changes to the framework itself.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must collect enhanced margins (50% for Stage I; 100% for Stage II) on all open and new positions in shortlisted securities from June 11, 2026.
- Members should refer to the attached Annexure I for the complete list of securities under each stage.
- For clarifications, members may contact BSE Surveillance at bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **June 10, 2026**: Reference date for open positions subject to enhanced margins.
- **June 11, 2026**: Effective date for enhanced margin requirements under ST-ASM Stage I and Stage II.

## Impact Assessment

Market participants holding or trading in the shortlisted securities will face significantly higher margin requirements, increasing the cost of carrying positions. Stage II securities are subject to the maximum 100% margin, effectively requiring full cash backing for positions. This may reduce liquidity and trading volumes in the affected scrips. The measure is part of BSE's ongoing market surveillance to address abnormal price or volume activity, and does not reflect any fundamental adverse finding against the companies involved.
