## Summary

Recode Studios Limited (BSE ticker: 544755) is being listed on BSE on May 12, 2026, and will be added to the BSE SME IPO Index effective at the open of Tuesday, May 13, 2026. This follows Notice No. 20260511-16 and is part of the standard index inclusion process for newly listed SME IPO stocks.

## Key Points

- Recode Studios Limited (ticker: 544755) listed on BSE effective May 12, 2026
- Stock added to BSE SME IPO Index effective at open of May 13, 2026
- The corporate action is included in the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE) dated May 12, 2026
- Queries should be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index reconstitution notice reflecting the addition of a newly listed SME IPO stock to the BSE SME IPO Index.

## Compliance Requirements

No direct compliance requirements for listed companies or investors. Index-tracking funds and products referencing the BSE SME IPO Index should update their portfolios to reflect the addition of Recode Studios Limited (544755) effective at the open of May 13, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **May 12, 2026**: Recode Studios Limited listed on BSE; corporate action file published
- **May 13, 2026 (market open)**: Effective date for addition of 544755 to BSE SME IPO Index

## Impact Assessment

The addition of Recode Studios Limited to the BSE SME IPO Index is a routine post-listing index event. Impact is primarily relevant to passive investment vehicles and structured products tracking the BSE SME IPO Index, which may need to rebalance to include the new constituent. Broader market impact is limited given the SME segment scope.
