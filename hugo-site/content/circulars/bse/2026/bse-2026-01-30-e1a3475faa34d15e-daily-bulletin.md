## Summary

BSE Daily Bulletin No. 207/2025-2026 dated January 30, 2026, provides comprehensive information on upcoming book closure dates and record dates for various corporate actions. The bulletin primarily covers debt securities including Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) and Commercial Papers, with actions such as redemptions and interest payments scheduled between February and March 2026.

## Key Points

- Bulletin covers corporate actions for multiple debt securities across various issuers
- Primary actions include NCD redemptions, interest payments, and commercial paper redemptions
- Record dates span from February 2026 to January 2036 for various securities
- Trading flag set to 1 (trading suspended) for all listed securities on their respective record dates
- Major issuers include Aditya Birla Capital Ltd, Bajaj Finance Limited, 360 ONE Prime Limited, and Aditya Birla Real Estate Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes announced. This is a routine informational bulletin disclosing scheduled corporate actions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Investors must hold securities in their demat accounts on the specified record dates to be eligible for respective corporate actions
- Trading will be suspended (flag 1) on record dates for the affected securities
- Depository participants and brokers must ensure proper recording of beneficial ownership on record dates
- Payment agents must process redemptions and interest payments to eligible holders post-record date

## Important Dates

**February 2026:**
- February 9, 2026: CP redemption - Aditya Birla Real Estate Limited
- February 17, 2026: CP redemption - Aditya Birla Real Estate Limited  
- February 18, 2026: NCD redemptions and interest payments - Aditya Birla Capital Ltd
- February 20, 2026: Interest payment - Aditya Birla Capital Ltd
- February 23, 2026: Interest payment and NCD redemptions - Aditya Birla Capital Ltd

**March 2026:**
- March 2, 2026: NCD redemptions and interest payments - Aditya Birla Capital Ltd
- March 5, 2026: NCD redemption - Aditya Birla Capital Ltd
- March 6, 2026: Interest payment - Aditya Birla Capital Ltd
- March 9, 2026: Interest payment - Aditya Birla Capital Ltd
- March 17, 2026: NCD redemption - 360 ONE Prime Limited

**Long-term dates:**
- Multiple interest payment and partial redemption dates for Bajaj Finance Limited NCDs extending from January 2028 to January 2036

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low to Medium - Routine corporate actions affecting debt securities. Trading suspension on record dates is standard procedure and has minimal market disruption.

**Investor Impact:** Medium - Important for debt security holders to note record dates for claiming redemption proceeds and interest payments. Missing record dates could result in delayed payments.

**Operational Impact:** Low - Standard operational procedures for registrars, depository participants, and paying agents. Multiple simultaneous record dates for Aditya Birla Capital securities may require coordinated processing.

**Liquidity Impact:** Temporary - Brief trading suspensions on specific securities on record dates will temporarily affect liquidity but will resume normal trading thereafter.
