## Summary

BSE has informed members that Motilal Oswal Mutual Fund is launching the **MOTILAL OSWAL BSE CLEAN ENVIRONMENT INDEX FUND** as a New Fund Offer (NFO) on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The NFO will be open from June 5, 2026 to June 19, 2026, with SIP/XSIP facility available. The tentative allotment date is June 25, 2026.

## Key Points

- NFO period: June 5, 2026 to June 19, 2026
- Two schemes available: Regular Plan - Growth (ISIN: INF247L01HD4) and Direct Plan - Growth (ISIN: INF247L01HC6)
- Both schemes are equity category with tentative allotment date of June 25, 2026
- Lumpsum purchases extended till midnight on June 19, 2026
- SIP/XSIP registrations must be authenticated by clients on or before 3:00 PM on June 19, 2026
- Transactions where clients do not initiate funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day shall be cancelled with CC 'N' flag reported to RTA/AMC
- Pending transactions remain in system until 3:00 PM on the last day of funds pay-out to AMC or T+5 day, whichever is earlier

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an operational notice for member information regarding an NFO launch.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure client funds are initiated by 9:00 AM on T+1 day to avoid transaction cancellation
- Cheque and OTM payments accepted during NFO period subject to receipt of clear funds in ICCL account by 6:00 PM on June 25, 2026
- Switch transactions accepted during NFO period subject to receipt of funds prior to allotment date; switch-out funds received on or after allotment date shall not be considered for allotment
- SIP/XSIP client authentication must be completed by 3:00 PM on June 19, 2026
- Clear funds must be available in the ICCL account by 6:00 PM on June 25, 2026 for allotment eligibility

## Important Dates

| Event | Date/Deadline |
|---|---|
| NFO Opens | June 5, 2026 |
| NFO Closes | June 19, 2026 |
| SIP/XSIP Authentication Deadline | 3:00 PM, June 19, 2026 |
| Lumpsum Extended Period Ends | Midnight, June 19, 2026 |
| Clear Funds Deadline in ICCL | 6:00 PM, June 25, 2026 |
| Tentative Allotment Date | June 25, 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

This NFO launch is a routine operational event on the BSE StAR MF Platform. Members facilitating client investments in the Motilal Oswal BSE Clean Environment Index Fund must adhere to the specified transaction, payment, and authentication deadlines. The fund targets clean environment-themed equity index exposure and may attract ESG-oriented investors. Operational risk lies in ensuring timely fund initiation to avoid transaction cancellations.
