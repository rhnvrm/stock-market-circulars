## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 5,31,29,400 new equity shares of Omega Interactive Technologies Ltd (Scrip Code: 511644, ISIN: INE113B01037) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Wednesday, May 27, 2026. The shares were issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to the conversion of warrants at an issue price of Rs. 10.35/- per share (face value Re. 1/- plus premium of Rs. 9.35/-).

## Key Points

- **Quantity:** 5,31,29,400 equity shares of Re. 1/- each
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 10.35/- per share (premium of Rs. 9.35/-)
- **Issued To:** Non-Promoters on preferential basis via conversion of warrants
- **Ranking:** Pari-passu with existing equity shares
- **Distribution Numbers:** 25912491 to 79041890
- **ISIN:** INE113B01037
- **Trading commences:** Wednesday, May 27, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification following allotment of securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to update their systems to enable trading of the newly listed securities from May 27, 2026. No additional compliance action required from members beyond standard procedures for newly listed securities.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** April 15, 2026
- **Trading Effective Date:** May 27, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry:** November 30, 2026 (all 5,31,29,400 shares locked until this date)

## Impact Assessment

This is a company-specific listing event with limited broader market impact. The addition of 5,31,29,400 shares represents a dilution of the existing equity base of Omega Interactive Technologies Ltd. The entire tranche is subject to lock-in until November 30, 2026, meaning these shares will not be available for trading in the secondary market until that date despite being listed. Market impact is expected to be minimal.
