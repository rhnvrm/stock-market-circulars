## Summary

BSE announces the commencement of trading for Kotak Nifty Alpha 50 ETF units from February 10, 2026 in the B Group under the permitted securities category. The ETF is managed by Kotak Mahindra Asset Management Company Ltd with 15.41 crore units available for trading.

## Key Points

- Trading commences on Tuesday, February 10, 2026
- Scrip Code: ALPHA, ISIN: INF174KA1IA5
- Total Units: 15,41,26,753 with face value of Rs. 10 per unit
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Listed in B Group category
- Four designated Market Makers: Kotak Securities Limited, Parwati Capital, Kanjalochana Finserve Private Limited, and Motilal Oswal Financial Services Limited
- Registrar: Computer Age Management Services Limited (CAMS)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing under existing BSE Rules, Byelaws and Regulations permitting dealings in securities listed on other exchanges under the "permitted securities category".

## Compliance Requirements

No additional compliance requirements for trading members. Standard trading procedures apply for B Group securities.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** February 9, 2026
- **Trading Commencement Date:** February 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine ETF listing providing investors additional access to a Nifty Alpha 50 tracking instrument through BSE platform. The presence of four market makers ensures adequate liquidity support for the ETF.
