## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the commercial paper of Julius Baer Capital (India) Private Limited (Scrip Code: 730733, ISIN: INE824H14SZ2) will mature on its maturity date of March 09, 2026. Trading in this instrument is suspended with effect from March 09, 2026.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of Julius Baer Capital (India) Private Limited (JBCPL-10-3-26-CP) is set to mature
- Scrip Code: 730733; ISIN: INE824H14SZ2
- Trading members are advised not to deal in this commercial paper from March 09, 2026
- Reference: DR-835/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice regarding the maturity and consequent suspension of trading in a specific commercial paper instrument.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE must cease dealing in the commercial paper of Julius Baer Capital (India) Private Limited (Scrip Code: 730733, ISIN: INE824H14SZ2) with effect from March 09, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **March 06, 2026**: Date of circular issuance
- **March 09, 2026**: Effective date of trading suspension (maturity date of the commercial paper)

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited and routine. This suspension affects only one specific commercial paper instrument in the debt segment. Trading members dealing in this instrument must ensure no transactions are executed from March 09, 2026 onwards. No broader market or systemic impact is anticipated.
