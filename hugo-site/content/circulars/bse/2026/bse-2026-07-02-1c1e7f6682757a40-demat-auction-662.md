## Summary

BSE Limited has invited offers from trading members of the Exchange for purchase of securities in Auction (Demat Auction 662) in respect of Rolling Settlement No. DR-662/2026-27. This is a routine settlement process to handle short-delivered securities through the auction mechanism.

## Key Points

- Auction offers will be accepted on trading terminals on Thursday, July 02, 2026
- Auction offer entry window: 02:00 p.m. to 02:45 p.m.
- Auction matching: 02:45 p.m. to 03:00 p.m.
- Delivery statement and report of accepted auction offers: 03:00 p.m. to 03:30 p.m.
- Money statement and final reports: 03:35 p.m. to 05:30 p.m.
- Pay-in of the auction to be held on Friday, July 03, 2026 at 08:00 a.m.
- Settlement will be done through the Clearing Member of a Trading Member under the Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment
- The restriction on trading members offering securities in auction in the settlement where they had short delivered such securities has been removed
- No corrections will be permitted for punching errors in securities code and rate
- Exchange reserves the right to accept or reject any offer without assigning any reason

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this notice operationalizes the existing auction mechanism under the Uniform Membership structure, confirming that the prior restriction on trading members offering securities in auction for settlements where they had short-delivered has been removed.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and custodians must check their auction pay-in obligations for securities and execute securities pay-in within depository cut-off timings
- Trading members should exercise care while punching securities codes and rates, as no corrections are permitted post-entry
- Members should refer to the cutoff rate on the Auction Offer Entry Screen before submitting offers
- Members should review related Corporate Actions notices (Ex-Rights/Ex-Bonus/Ex-Split) for affected securities before placing auction offers

## Important Dates

- Auction offer entry, matching, and reporting: Thursday, July 02, 2026 (02:00 p.m. – 05:30 p.m.)
- Auction pay-in: Friday, July 03, 2026 at 08:00 a.m.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, recurring settlement/RMS notice concerning the auction process for short-delivered securities under Rolling Settlement DR-662/2026-27. It primarily affects trading members, clearing members, and custodians operationally, with no direct impact on specific listed companies or broader market sentiment.
