## Summary

BSE has issued the live activities schedule for the Offer to Buy (OTB) – Acquisition Window (Buyback) for Garware Technical Fibres Ltd (symbol: GARFIBRES, scrip code: 509557, ISIN: INE276A01018). The buyback offer is open from 29th May 2026 to 2nd June 2026, with bid offering sessions running from 26th May 2026, at an offer price of Rs. 680 per share for up to 16,17,500 equity shares.

## Key Points

- **Security:** Garware Technical Fibres Ltd | Symbol: GARFIBRES | ISIN: INE276A01018 | Scrip Code: 509557
- **Offer Size:** Up to 16,17,500 equity shares (face value Rs. 10 each)
- **Offer Price:** Rs. 680.00 per share
- **Offer Opening Date:** Tuesday, 29th May 2026
- **Offer Closing Date:** Tuesday, 2nd June 2026
- **Bid Offering / Modification / Cancellation Period:** 26th May 2026 to 2nd June 2026, 9:15 AM – 3:30 PM
- **Give-up / Take-up:** 26th May 2026 to 1st June 2026, 9:15 AM – 3:30 PM
- **Give-up (last day):** 2nd June 2026, 9:15 AM – 3:30 PM
- **Take-up (last day):** 2nd June 2026, 9:15 AM – 4:00 PM
- Bidding is available across investor categories: FII, FI, MF, IC, Other QIBs, NIB, Company, Individual
- Only shares held in demat form are eligible for bidding

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice references existing operational instructions under Exchange Notices 20150930-1 (dated 30 September 2015) and 20170203-29 (dated 3 February 2017) for OTB participation.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must ensure that the UCC (Unique Client Code) of investors is registered and updated with the correct PAN number and investor category in the BSE UCC database.
- Only demat-held shares are eligible; members may bid on behalf of eligible investors.
- Members and Custodians should note that End of Issue files on the Extranet will only be generated if securities are accepted.
- Login credentials for iBBS and RTRMS platforms should be managed via the Trading Operations contact (022-22728005 / user.trdops@bseindia.com).

## Impact Assessment

This circular is operationally relevant to trading members and custodians participating in the buyback of Garware Technical Fibres Ltd shares. The offer size of up to 16.17 lakh shares at Rs. 680 per share implies a maximum buyback outlay of approximately Rs. 110 crore. The notice is procedural and poses no systemic market risk; impact is limited to participants in this specific OTB session and holders of GARFIBRES shares seeking to tender in the buyback.
