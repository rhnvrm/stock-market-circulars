## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 10,30,145 new equity shares of ECO HOTELS AND RESORTS LIMITED (Scrip Code: 514402, ISIN: INE638N01012) have been listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Tuesday, February 24, 2026. The shares were issued on a preferential basis to non-promoters at an issue price of Rs. 34.50/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- with a premium of Rs. 24.50/-).

## Key Points

- 10,30,145 equity shares of Rs. 10/- face value issued at a premium of Rs. 24.50/- (issue price Rs. 34.50/-)
- Shares issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 64627944 to 65658088
- ISIN: INE638N01012
- All 10,30,145 shares are subject to lock-in until August 25, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification under BSE's procedures for newly issued securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities available for trading from February 24, 2026
- Lock-in restrictions must be observed: all 10,30,145 shares (Dist. Nos. 64627944 to 65658088) are locked in until August 25, 2026 and cannot be traded until that date

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** July 22, 2025
- **Effective Trading Date:** February 24, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry:** August 25, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This listing increases the total tradeable equity share capital of ECO HOTELS AND RESORTS LIMITED by 10,30,145 shares. However, since all newly listed shares are under lock-in until August 25, 2026, there is no immediate increase in free-float. Market impact is minimal and confined to investors tracking this specific scrip (Code: 514402).
