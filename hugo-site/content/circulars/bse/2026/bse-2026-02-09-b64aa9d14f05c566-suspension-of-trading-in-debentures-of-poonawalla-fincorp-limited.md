## Summary

BSE has issued a notice suspending trading in Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of POONAWALLA FINCORP LIMITED effective February 12, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption purposes. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the no-dealings date.

## Key Points

- Company: POONAWALLA FINCORP LIMITED
- Debenture ISIN: INE511C07789
- Series Code: PFL-REPO RATE-27-2-26-PVT (974627)
- Record Date: February 12, 2026
- No Dealings From: February 12, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures
- Notice Number: 20260209-34
- Reference: DR-819/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action procedure for debt securities redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any deals in the specified debentures from February 12, 2026
- Trading members must take note of the suspension and update their trading systems accordingly
- Members should communicate the suspension to relevant clients holding or interested in these debentures

## Important Dates

- **February 9, 2026**: Circular issued by BSE
- **February 12, 2026**: Record date for redemption
- **February 12, 2026**: Trading suspension effective date (no dealings from this date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - affects only holders and potential traders of this specific debenture series. The suspension is procedural and temporary, related to the redemption process.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update their systems to prevent inadvertent trading in the suspended debenture. Existing holders will receive redemption proceeds as per the company's redemption terms.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders will have their securities redeemed as per the terms. No trading opportunity available from the record date onwards for this series.
