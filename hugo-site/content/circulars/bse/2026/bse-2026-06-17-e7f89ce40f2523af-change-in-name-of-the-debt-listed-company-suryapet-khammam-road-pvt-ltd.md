## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that SURYAPET KHAMMAM ROAD Pvt Ltd, a debt-listed company, has changed its name to SURYAPET KHAMMAM ROAD LIMITED. Consequently, the SCRIP ID and abbreviated name for its two listed debt securities on the BOLT Plus System will be updated effective June 23, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company name changes from SURYAPET KHAMMAM ROAD Pvt Ltd to SURYAPET KHAMMAM ROAD LIMITED
- Two debt securities (scrip codes 976131 and 976132) are affected
- SCRIP IDs on BOLT Plus System updated to reflect the new abbreviated name
- Change effective from June 23, 2026
- Notice issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President – Listing Compliance & Operations

## Regulatory Changes

The SCRIP ID and abbreviated name on the BOLT Plus System will be updated for both listed debt securities of the company:

| Scrip Code | Existing Name | New Name | BOLT Plus Scrip ID |
|---|---|---|---|
| 976131 | SURYAPET KHAMMAM ROAD Pvt Ltd | SURYAPET KHAMMAM ROAD LIMITED | 808SKRL35 |
| 976132 | SURYAPET KHAMMAM ROAD Pvt Ltd | SURYAPET KHAMMAM ROAD LIMITED | 828SKRL31 |

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members dealing in the debt securities of this company should update their internal systems and records to reflect the new company name (SURYAPET KHAMMAM ROAD LIMITED) and associated BOLT Plus system identifiers effective June 23, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **June 17, 2026**: Notice issued by BSE
- **June 23, 2026**: Effective date of name change on BOLT Plus System

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited and administrative in nature. Only the company name, SCRIP ID, and abbreviated name on the BOLT Plus System are changing. The underlying debt securities, scrip codes, ISINs, and trading conditions remain unchanged. Trading members and investors in these debt instruments should note the updated identifiers to avoid confusion when executing transactions post June 23, 2026.
