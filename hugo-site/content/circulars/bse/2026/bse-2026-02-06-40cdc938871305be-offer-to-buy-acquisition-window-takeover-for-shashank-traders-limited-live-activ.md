## Summary

BSE has notified trading members and custodian members about the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for SHASHANK TRADERS LIMITED. The offer is for up to 15,46,900 equity shares at Rs. 30.00 per share and will be conducted from February 9, 2026, to February 23, 2026, through BSE's iBBS platform and RTRMS system.

## Key Points

- **Security Name**: SHASHANK TRADERS LIMITED
- **Security Symbol**: SHASHANK
- **ISIN**: INE508R01018
- **Scrip Code**: 540221
- **Offer Size**: Up to 15,46,900 equity shares
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10.00
- **Offer Price**: Rs. 30.00 per share
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Bid offering period**: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM daily during offer period
- **Categories eligible**: FII, Financial Institution, Mutual Fund, Insurance Companies, Other QIBs, Non-Institutional Buyers, Company, Individual

## Session Timings

- **Bid Offering Period**: February 9-23, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Give-up/Take-up**: February 9-20, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Give-up (last day)**: February 23, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Take-up (last day)**: February 23, 2026 (9:15 AM to 4:00 PM)

## Platform Access URLs

- **iBBS Internet**: https://ibbs.bseindia.com/
- **iBBS Leased Line**: https://ibbsll.bseindia.com/
- **RTRMS Internet**: https://rtrms.bseindia.com/
- **RTRMS Leased Line**: https://rtrmsll.bseindia.com/
- **Extranet Internet**: https://member.bseindia.com/
- **Extranet Leased Line**: https://memberll.bseindia.com/

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure investor UCC is registered and/or updated with correct PAN number and category in the Exchange UCC database
- Trading members can only bid for investors having shares in demat form
- Members should refer to Exchange Notices 20150930-1 (September 30, 2015) and 20170203-29 (February 3, 2017) for operational instructions
- For leased line URL access settings, refer to Exchange Circular 20170503-12
- Files on Extranet for acceptance of bids available only after confirmation from Merchant Banker/RTA post-issue closure
- No End of Issue files will be generated if no securities are accepted

## Important Dates

- **Opening Date**: Monday, February 9, 2026
- **Closing Date**: Monday, February 23, 2026
- **Give-up/Take-up Period**: February 9-20, 2026
- **Last Day for Give-up**: February 23, 2026
- **Last Day for Take-up**: February 23, 2026 (extended to 4:00 PM)

## Impact Assessment

This is a company-specific takeover offer with limited market-wide impact. The offer affects only shareholders of SHASHANK TRADERS LIMITED and trading members facilitating the acquisition. The 15-day offer window provides adequate time for eligible shareholders to participate through their respective trading members using BSE's designated platforms.
