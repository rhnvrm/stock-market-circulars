## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that, in continuation of its earlier Notice No. 20260701-23 dated July 01, 2026, the equity shares of CSM Technologies Limited (Scrip Code: 544806) will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (T Group) to the Rolling segment with effect from Thursday, July 16, 2026. Consequently, dealings in the company's equity shares will be shifted to the B Group.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code 544806 (CSM Technologies Limited) shall move out of Trade for Trade (T Group) segment.
- The stock will be reclassified under the Rolling segment, specifically the B Group.
- This change follows an earlier notice (No. 20260701-23) dated July 01, 2026.
- Trading members can contact Mr. Parag Jain (Tel: 022 2272 8685) for further details.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced; this is a segment/group reclassification under existing BSE trading segment rules, moving the scrip from compulsory Trade for Trade settlement to normal Rolling settlement.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must update their systems to reflect the change in trading segment and group classification for CSM Technologies Limited shares effective the specified date. No specific action is required from shareholders.

## Important Dates

- July 01, 2026: Original notice (20260701-23) issued.
- July 02, 2026: Current confirmatory notice issued.
- July 16, 2026 (Thursday): Effective date of transfer from T Group (Trade for Trade) to B Group (Rolling segment).

## Impact Assessment

The shift from Trade for Trade to Rolling segment typically indicates improved liquidity conditions or reduced surveillance concerns, allowing for regular intraday trading and squaring-off of positions, which was not permitted under Trade for Trade settlement. This is generally viewed as a positive development for trading flexibility in the stock, though it does not directly affect the company's fundamentals or valuation.
