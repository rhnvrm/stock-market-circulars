## Summary

BSE has issued a notice under its encumbrance surveillance framework (in continuation of Notice No. 20220131-43 dated January 31, 2022) requiring a minimum 75% margin on securities where promoter and/or non-promoter encumbrance is high, as defined under Regulation 28(3) of the SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations 2011. The measure covers both the Equity and Equity Derivatives segments.

## Key Points

- Securities listed in **Annexure I** have met the inclusion criteria and will attract a minimum **75% margin** in the Equity and Equity Derivatives segments effective **June 3, 2026**, on all open positions as on June 2, 2026, and all new positions from June 3, 2026.
- Securities listed in **Annexure II** are eligible to exit the framework effective **June 1, 2026**.
- A consolidated list of all securities currently under the framework is provided in **Annexure III**.
- This measure applies in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by exchanges.
- The framework is subject to periodic review.
- Inclusion in this measure should **not** be interpreted as an adverse regulatory action against the concerned company.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced. This is a continuation of the existing encumbrance-based surveillance measure established under Notice No. 20220131-43 (January 31, 2022), implemented pursuant to Regulation 28(3) of the SEBI (SAST) Regulations 2011, which requires disclosure and regulatory action when promoter or non-promoter encumbrance on shares crosses prescribed thresholds.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members** must ensure that the minimum 75% margin is collected on all applicable open and new positions in securities listed under Annexure I.
- Members should refer to Annexure III for the complete current list of securities under this surveillance framework.
- For clarifications, members may contact BSE Surveillance at **bse.surv@bseindia.com**.

## Important Dates

| Date | Event |
|------|-------|
| May 29, 2026 | Notice issued |
| June 1, 2026 | Annexure II securities eligible to exit the framework |
| June 2, 2026 | Reference date for open positions subject to new margin requirement |
| June 3, 2026 | 75% margin requirement effective for Annexure I securities |

## Impact Assessment

This is a targeted surveillance action affecting a specific set of securities with high promoter or non-promoter share encumbrance. The elevated 75% margin requirement increases the cost of holding positions (both long and short) in affected securities, which may reduce liquidity and trading volumes in those counters. Investors and traders with existing positions in Annexure I securities must ensure adequate margin is available by the close of June 2, 2026, to avoid margin shortfalls. Securities exiting the framework (Annexure II) will revert to standard margin norms from June 1, 2026, potentially improving their liquidity.
