## Summary

BSE has issued a notice applying Long Term Additional Surveillance Measures (LT-ASM) on select securities effective from late May and early June 2026. The framework imposes 100% margins, lower price bands, and group transfers on shortlisted securities. Securities are also being moved between LT-ASM stages or exiting the framework as part of periodic review.

## Key Points

- 100% margins will be applicable from June 1, 2026 on all open positions as of May 29, 2026 and new positions created from June 1, 2026 onwards (Annexure I, Part A)
- Securities moved to higher LT-ASM stages effective May 27, 2026 (Annexure I, Part B)
- Lower Price Band applicable on stage-upgraded securities effective May 27, 2026
- Certain securities will remain in LT-ASM but be transferred to T / XT / MT / TS Group effective June 1, 2026 (Annexure I, Part D)
- Securities moving out of LT-ASM Framework effective May 27, 2026 are listed in Annexure II
- Consolidated list of all securities under the framework is in Annexure III
- Shortlisting is based on XBRL submissions by listed companies and is a surveillance action, not an adverse action against the company
- LT-ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures

## Regulatory Changes

This notice is issued in reference to Exchange notices:
- 20180321-46 (March 21, 2018) — original LT-ASM framework
- 20181027-1 (October 27, 2018) — revision
- 20201204-56 (December 4, 2020) — revision
- 20240809-46 (August 9, 2024) — revision
- 20240920-63 (September 20, 2024) — revision
- 20210604-41 (June 4, 2021) — group transfer provisions

The revised LT-ASM framework stages securities based on market surveillance criteria derived from XBRL filings.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must maintain 100% margins on open positions in LT-ASM-listed securities as of May 29, 2026, starting June 1, 2026
- All new positions in these securities from June 1, 2026 will also require 100% margins
- Members should refer to the attached annexures for the complete list of affected securities
- For clarifications, members may write to bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

| Date | Event |
|------|-------|
| May 27, 2026 | Securities moved to higher LT-ASM stages take effect; Lower Price Band applies; Securities exit LT-ASM framework |
| May 29, 2026 | Reference date for open positions subject to 100% margin |
| June 1, 2026 | 100% margins effective on open and new positions; Group transfers (T/XT/MT/TS) effective |

## Impact Assessment

**High impact on affected securities.** The imposition of 100% margins substantially increases the cost of holding or creating positions in shortlisted securities, which typically leads to reduced liquidity and higher volatility for those scrips. Lower price bands restrict downside movement and group transfers to T/XT/MT/TS groups further limit trading flexibility (e.g., trade-for-trade settlement). Securities exiting the framework will have their previous price bands reinstated unless subject to another surveillance measure. The action is purely surveillance-driven based on XBRL data and does not reflect a regulatory or legal action against the underlying companies.
