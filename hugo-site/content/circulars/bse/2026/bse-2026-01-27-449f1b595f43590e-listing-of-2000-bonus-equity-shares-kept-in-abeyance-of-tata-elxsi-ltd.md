## Summary

BSE has listed 2,000 bonus equity shares of Tata Elxsi Ltd (Scrip Code: 500408) that were previously kept in abeyance. These shares are now permitted for trading effective Wednesday, January 28, 2025.

## Key Points

- 2,000 bonus equity shares of face value Rs. 10/- each listed
- Shares were previously kept in abeyance
- ISIN: INE670A01012
- Distinctive Numbers: 31163221 to 31165220
- Trading commences January 28, 2025
- Scrip Code: 500408

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification for shares that were held in abeyance.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the listing and permit trading in these securities from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 27, 2026
- **Trading Commencement**: Wednesday, January 28, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The listing of 2,000 bonus shares represents a very small quantity relative to Tata Elxsi's total share capital. This administrative action releases shares from abeyance for normal trading but does not affect overall market operations or valuation significantly.
