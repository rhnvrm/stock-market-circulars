## Summary

BSE Limited has announced that equity shares of Shadowfax Technologies Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544685, trading symbol SHADOWFAX, and ISIN number INE12UN01015. The exact date of listing and detailed security information will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Shadowfax Technologies Limited equity shares to be listed on BSE
- Scrip Code: 544685
- Trading Symbol: SHADOWFAX
- ISIN Number: INE12UN01015
- Specific listing date to be announced via separate notice
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard new listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed to note the security details for upcoming trading activities once the listing becomes effective.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: January 27, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This circular has medium impact as it introduces a new security to the BSE equity segment. Trading members and investors interested in logistics and technology sectors should monitor for the subsequent notice announcing the actual listing date and security details. The addition expands investment opportunities in the delivery and logistics technology space.
