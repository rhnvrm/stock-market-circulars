## Summary

BSE has issued an update to Notice No. 20260527-46 (dated May 27, 2026) regarding the change in face value of Shriram Overnight Fund on the BSE StAR MF platform. Shriram AMC has completed the face value conversion process, and revised units are now being allotted under a new ISIN at the AMC/RTA level. Members must immediately use the revised ISIN for all transactions on the BSE StAR MF platform.

## Key Points

- Shriram AMC has completed the face value conversion process for Shriram Overnight Fund.
- Revised units will be allotted under a new ISIN at the AMC/RTA end.
- A Notice-cum-Addendum issued by Shriram AMC, along with an Annexure containing the new ISIN details, is attached to the circular.
- All transactions on the BSE StAR MF platform for this fund must now be executed using the revised ISIN.

## Regulatory Changes

This circular supersedes the operational details from Notice No. 20260527-46 (May 27, 2026). The face value conversion is now complete, and the new ISIN replaces the old one for Shriram Overnight Fund on the BSE StAR MF platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must update their systems and workflows to use the new ISIN for Shriram Overnight Fund.
- All transactions on the BSE StAR MF platform for Shriram Overnight Fund must be executed with the revised ISIN details with immediate effect.
- Members should refer to the attached Addendum and Annexure from Shriram AMC for the specific new ISIN.

## Important Dates

- **May 27, 2026**: Original notice (No. 20260527-46) issued regarding the planned face value change.
- **June 17, 2026**: Update issued confirming completion of face value conversion; new ISIN effective immediately.

## Impact Assessment

Members and distributors transacting in Shriram Overnight Fund on the BSE StAR MF platform are directly affected and must update transaction systems to reflect the new ISIN without delay. Failure to do so may result in failed or rejected transactions. The impact is limited in scope to Shriram Overnight Fund and does not affect other schemes or market segments.
