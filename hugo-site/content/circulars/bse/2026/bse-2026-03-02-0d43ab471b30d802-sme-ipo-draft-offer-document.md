## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document of Susan Electricals India Limited, filed on February 28, 2026, on the BSE SME website. The document is available for public review for a minimum period of 21 days as required under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations. Stakeholders are invited to submit comments via email.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Susan Electricals India Limited on February 28, 2026
- Document hosted on BSE SME website in compliance with Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations
- Public comment period: 21 days from the date of notice (March 2, 2026)
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Direct PDF link available on the BSE SME portal

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice is issued in routine compliance with Schedule VI of the SEBI (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, which mandates public availability of draft offer documents for a minimum of 21 days prior to IPO proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants, analysts, and the public may review the Draft Offer Document on the BSE SME website
- Any comments or objections must be submitted within 21 days from March 2, 2026 (deadline: approximately March 23, 2026)
- Comments to be sent to: equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Document Filing Date:** February 28, 2026
- **Notice Date:** March 2, 2026
- **Public Comment Deadline:** March 23, 2026 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a moderate, targeted impact relevant to investors and market participants in the SME segment. The disclosure of Susan Electricals India Limited's Draft Offer Document signals an upcoming SME IPO on BSE. The 21-day public comment window is standard procedure and does not indicate any immediate market action. Investors interested in SME IPOs should review the document and submit any comments before the deadline.
