## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 71,944 new equity shares of BLACK BOX LIMITED (Scrip Code: 500463) with effect from January 29, 2026. These shares were issued at Rs. 417/- per share (face value Rs. 2/- with premium of Rs. 415/-) to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. The shares are subject to lock-in until August 5, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: BLACK BOX LIMITED (Scrip Code: 500463)
- Number of shares: 71,944 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each
- Issue price: Rs. 417/- per share (Rs. 2/- face value + Rs. 415/- premium)
- Allotment date: December 17, 2025
- Trading commencement: January 29, 2026
- ISIN: INE676A01027
- Distinctive numbers: 170281101 to 170353044
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis
- Purpose: Conversion of warrants
- Ranking: Pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for new securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities listing
- Shares are subject to mandatory lock-in provisions
- All 71,944 shares (Dist. Nos. 170281101-170353044) are locked-in until August 5, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 17, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: January 29, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry**: August 5, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The listing involves a relatively small number of shares (71,944) from warrant conversion. The shares are locked-in until August 5, 2026, preventing immediate selling pressure. This is a routine corporate action with no significant impact on trading operations or market participants.
