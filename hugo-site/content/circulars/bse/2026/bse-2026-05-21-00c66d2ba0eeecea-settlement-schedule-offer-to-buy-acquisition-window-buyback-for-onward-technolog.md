## Summary

ICCL (Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd.) has announced the settlement schedule for the Offer to Buy (OTB) Acquisition Window Buyback for Onward Technologies Limited. The buyback bidding window runs from May 22, 2026 to May 29, 2026, with obligations to be downloaded on June 4, 2026 and final settlement on June 5, 2026.

## Key Points

- Settlement No. 2026055 has been assigned for this buyback transaction
- Bidding period: May 22, 2026 to May 29, 2026
- Demat shares must be delivered exclusively via the Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism available on both CDSL and NSDL depositories
- Members/Custodians must use Market Type "(Buyback)" and Settlement No. 2026055 in the depository system
- CDSL and NSDL early pay-in securities will be earmarked/blocked in clients' accounts under the requisite market type and settlement number
- Members and Custodians are NOT required to upload any EPN file
- Members should refer to MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for client-wise Stamp Duty charges

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. Members/Custodians are directed to refer to Notice No. 20200528-32 dated May 28, 2020 regarding settlement as per Revised Guidelines of Mechanism for acquisition of shares through Stock Exchange.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and Custodians must complete the early pay-in of securities in the Depository system sufficiently in advance before the closure of the BSE OTB Bidding window
- Early pay-in must be reflected in BSE/ICCL systems before prescribed timelines for accepting/confirmation of Bids on the BSE OTB platform
- Custodians must confirm bids within the prescribed confirmation window
- Use Settlement No. 2026055 and Market Type "(Buyback)" for all depository system entries

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Bid Start Date | May 22, 2026 |
| Bid End Date | May 29, 2026 |
| Obligation Download | June 4, 2026 |
| Settlement Date | June 5, 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

This circular directly impacts brokers, members, and custodians participating in the Onward Technologies Limited buyback. Participants must ensure timely early pay-in of shares through CDSL or NSDL to avoid rejection of bids. The buyback window is open for approximately one week, and failure to comply with early pay-in timelines could result in bid non-confirmation. For clarifications, participants may contact the ICCL Help Desk at 022-69158590/45720490/45720690 or iccl.helpdesk@icclindia.com.
