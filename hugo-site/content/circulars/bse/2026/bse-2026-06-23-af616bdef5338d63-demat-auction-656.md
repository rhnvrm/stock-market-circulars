## Summary

BSE Limited (through Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd.) has issued a notice inviting auction offers from trading members for the purchase of securities under Rolling Settlement No. DR-656/2026-27. The auction session is scheduled for June 23, 2026, with pay-in obligations due on June 24, 2026.

## Key Points

- Auction offer entry window: 2:00 PM to 2:45 PM on June 23, 2026
- Auction matching: 2:45 PM to 3:00 PM
- Delivery statement and accepted auction offer reports: 3:00 PM to 3:30 PM
- Money statement and final reports: 3:35 PM to 5:30 PM
- Securities pay-in (auction): June 24, 2026 at 8:00 AM
- Members and custodians must execute securities pay-in in depositories within depository cut-off timings
- Under the Uniform Membership structure in Equity Cash Segment, settlement is processed through the Clearing Member of a Trading Member
- The restriction on trading members offering securities in auction for settlements in which they have short-delivered has been removed

## Regulatory Changes

Following implementation of the Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment, settlement is now routed through the Clearing Member of a Trading Member. The earlier restriction prohibiting trading members from offering securities in auction for the same settlement in which they short-delivered has been lifted.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must submit auction offers only through trading terminals within the specified session window
- Members and custodians are required to verify their auction pay-in obligations for securities and complete pay-in within depository cut-off timings
- Trading members must exercise caution when entering security codes and rates; no corrections will be permitted after entry
- Members should refer to the cutoff rate on the "Auction Offer Entry Screen" before placing offers
- Members should monitor corporate action notices (Ex-Rights, Ex-Bonus, Ex-Split, etc.) issued by the respective departments for relevant scrips

## Important Dates

- **June 23, 2026**: Auction offer entry (2:00 PM – 2:45 PM), matching (2:45 PM – 3:00 PM), and reports (3:00 PM – 5:30 PM)
- **June 24, 2026**: Auction securities pay-in at 8:00 AM

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice applicable to trading members and custodians participating in BSE's rolling settlement auction mechanism. Impact is limited to members with short delivery positions in DR-656/2026-27. No broad market disruption is expected. The removal of the restriction on offering securities in the same settlement where a member short-delivered provides slightly more flexibility to trading members.
