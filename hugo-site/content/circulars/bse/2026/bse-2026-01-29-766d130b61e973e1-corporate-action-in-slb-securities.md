## Summary

BSE has announced shut periods for Securities Lending and Borrowing (SLB) mechanism due to upcoming corporate actions in 10 securities. The shut periods are scheduled between February 1-6, 2026, covering 9 dividend declarations and 1 spin-off event. During these periods, SLB transactions will be restricted to prevent settlement complications arising from corporate actions.

## Key Points

- 10 securities will have SLB shut periods in early February 2026
- 9 companies declaring dividends: Symphony, Quess Corp, LT Foods, CRIZAC, Vaibhav Global, Cochin Shipyard, Balkrishna Industries, MAS Financial Services, and Garden Reach Shipbuilders
- 1 company undergoing spin-off: Genus Power Infrastructures (with foreclosure date)
- All shut periods are 2-day periods preceding the record date
- Ex-dates align with record dates for all corporate actions

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice regarding SLB mechanism restrictions during corporate action periods.

## Compliance Requirements

- SLB participants must not initiate new lending/borrowing transactions in the affected securities during shut periods
- Existing SLB positions should be squared off or rolled over before the shut period begins
- For Genus Power Infrastructures, foreclosure is mandatory on February 6, 2026
- Market participants must ensure settlement obligations are met before shut periods commence

## Important Dates

**February 1-2, 2026:**
- LT Foods Limited - Dividend (Shut: Feb 1-2, Record: Feb 2, Ex: Feb 2)
- Balkrishna Industries - Dividend (Shut: Feb 1-2, Record: Feb 2, Ex: Feb 2)

**February 2-3, 2026:**
- Symphony Limited - Dividend (Shut: Feb 2-3, Record: Feb 3, Ex: Feb 3)
- Vaibhav Global - Dividend (Shut: Feb 2-3, Record: Feb 3, Ex: Feb 3)
- Cochin Shipyard Limited - Dividend (Shut: Feb 2-3, Record: Feb 3, Ex: Feb 3)
- Garden Reach Shipbuilders - Dividend (Shut: Feb 2-3, Record: Feb 3, Ex: Feb 3)

**February 3-4, 2026:**
- CRIZAC LIMITED - Dividend (Shut: Feb 3-4, Record: Feb 4, Ex: Feb 4)
- MAS Financial Services - Dividend (Shut: Feb 3-4, Record: Feb 4, Ex: Feb 4)

**February 5-6, 2026:**
- Quess Corp Limited - Dividend (Shut: Feb 5-6, Record: Feb 6, Ex: Feb 6)
- Genus Power Infrastructures - Spin Off (Shut: Feb 5-6, Record: Feb 6, Ex: Feb 6, Foreclosure: Feb 6)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - Affects liquidity in SLB market for 10 securities during respective shut periods. Participants with open positions need to plan ahead.

**Operational Impact:** SLB participants must adjust their lending/borrowing strategies and ensure positions are appropriately managed before shut periods. The Genus Power spin-off requires mandatory foreclosure, which may impact participants with outstanding positions.

**Trading Impact:** Regular equity trading continues unaffected. Only SLB mechanism is restricted during specified periods.
