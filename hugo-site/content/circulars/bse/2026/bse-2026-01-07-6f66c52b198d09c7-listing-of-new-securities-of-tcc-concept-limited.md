## Summary

BSE has listed 76,112 new equity shares of TCC Concept Limited (Scrip Code: 512038) issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to share swap. These shares will be permitted to trade on the exchange from January 8, 2026, and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 76,112 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at premium of Rs. 547.94/-
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis via share swap
- Distribution numbers: 47451950 to 47528061
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE887D01016
- All shares subject to lock-in period

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities listing
- Lock-in restrictions apply to all allotted shares until July 14, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** December 11, 2025
- **Trading Commencement:** January 8, 2026
- **Lock-in Period Ends:** July 14, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. The allotment represents a small number of shares (76,112) issued via share swap arrangement. All shares are subject to a 7-month lock-in period until July 14, 2026, preventing immediate trading. The issue price of Rs. 557.94/- includes a significant premium of Rs. 547.94/- over face value of Rs. 10/-.
