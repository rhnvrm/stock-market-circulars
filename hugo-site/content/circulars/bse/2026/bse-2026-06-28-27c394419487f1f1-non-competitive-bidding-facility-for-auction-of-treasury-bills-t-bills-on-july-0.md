## Summary

BSE has announced the non-competitive bidding facility for three Government of India Treasury Bills to be auctioned by RBI on July 01, 2026. Trading members and direct investors can participate through the NCB-GSec module on BSE's iBBS web-based platform.

## Key Points

- Three T-Bills are available for non-competitive bidding: 91-day, 182-day, and 364-day maturities.
- Bidding is conducted via the NCB-GSec module at https://ibbs.bseindia.com.
- Bid collection opens June 29, 2026 from 10:00 am (24-hour availability).
- This is in continuation of Exchange circular no. 20180423-42 dated April 26, 2018, which launched the non-competitive bidding facility for Government Securities auctions.
- RBI press release dated June 25, 2026 announced the original auction.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This notice is a routine operational communication under the existing non-competitive bidding framework established in 2018.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the NCB-GSec module on iBBS to place bids.
- Members must submit bids by July 01, 2026, 8:00 am.
- Direct investors must submit bids by June 30, 2026, 5:00 pm.
- Minimum lot size is Rs. 100 and bids must be in multiples thereof.

## Important Dates

| Event | Date/Time |

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine scheduled auction notice with low market impact. It provides debt segment participants an opportunity to invest in short-term Government of India Treasury Bills through BSE's non-competitive bidding mechanism. No changes to trading rules or regulations are introduced. The total issue size across all three T-Bills is Rs. 24,000 crore.
