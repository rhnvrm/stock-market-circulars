## Summary

BSE has issued a Master Circular for the Equity Segment consolidating all relevant notices and circulars issued by the Trading Operations Department. This serves as a single reference point for members to access all applicable guidelines for the Equity Segment.

## Key Points

- Consolidates all previously issued notices and circulars for the Equity Segment into one document
- Prepared by the Trading Operations Department
- Includes two attachments: the main Equity Master Circular and Part III EOD File Formats for Equity Segment
- Issued under Notice No. 20260430-18 dated 30 April 2026

## Regulatory Changes

This circular is a compilation document and does not introduce new regulations. It consolidates existing notices and circulars issued by BSE's Trading Operations Department for the Equity Segment into a single accessible reference.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members are expected to refer to this Master Circular as the primary reference for all Equity Segment trading operations
- All applicable notices and circulars for the Equity Segment are now accessible at one place via this document
- Members should review the attached EOD File Formats document for end-of-day file format specifications

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 30 April 2026
- Effective: Immediately upon issuance

## Impact Assessment

This Master Circular has broad operational impact on all BSE members participating in the Equity Segment. By consolidating all trading operation guidelines into a single document, it simplifies compliance and reference for members. The inclusion of EOD file format specifications is particularly relevant for back-office and technology teams managing settlement and reporting processes.
