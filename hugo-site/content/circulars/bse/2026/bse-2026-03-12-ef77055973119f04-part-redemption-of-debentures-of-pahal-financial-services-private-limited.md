## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Pahal Financial Services Private Limited has fixed a record date for part redemption of its Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) along with payment of interest. Trading in these debentures will occur with a reduced face value effective 16 March 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: Pahal Financial Services Private Limited
- Debenture Series: PFSPL-14%-30-4-26-PVT (ISIN: INE514Q07346, Code: 975642)
- Record Date: 16 March 2026
- Purpose: Part Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- Reduced Face Value: Rs. 4,166.82 per debenture
- Effective Date: 16 March 2026
- Settlement: DR-840/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notice under BSE's debt segment listing compliance framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that from 16 March 2026, trading in the debentures of Pahal Financial Services Private Limited will be conducted with the reduced face value of Rs. 4,166.82 per debenture.
- Members should update their systems to reflect the new reduced face value effective the stated date and settlement number.

## Important Dates

- **Record Date:** 16 March 2026
- **Effective Date for Reduced Face Value:** 16 March 2026
- **Settlement Number:** DR-840/2025-2026
- **Notice Date:** 12 March 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt corporate action with limited market impact. It affects only holders of the specific NCD series (ISIN INE514Q07346) of Pahal Financial Services Private Limited. The reduction in face value by Rs. 4,166.82 per debenture reflects a partial principal repayment alongside an interest payment. Trading members dealing in this instrument must ensure updated face value is applied from 16 March 2026.
