## Summary

BSE has updated the list of securities under the High Promoter Encumbrance Surveillance Measure pursuant to SEBI (SAST) Regulation 2011, Rule 28(3). Securities in Annexure I will attract a minimum 35% margin in both the Equity and Equity Derivatives segments effective June 5, 2026, covering open positions as of June 4, 2026 and all new positions thereafter. Securities in Annexure II exit the framework from June 3, 2026.

## Key Points

- Securities in Annexure I are newly included and will attract a minimum **35% margin** in Equity and Equity Derivatives segments from **June 5, 2026**, applicable to all open positions as on June 4, 2026 and new positions from June 5, 2026.
- Securities in Annexure II are eligible to exit the framework effective **June 3, 2026**.
- Annexure III provides the consolidated list of all securities currently under the framework.
- This measure operates alongside all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the exchanges and is subject to periodic review.
- Inclusion is solely for market surveillance purposes and does not constitute an adverse action against the concerned company or entity.

## Regulatory Changes

This notice continues the framework established under Exchange Notice No. 20191024-30 (October 24, 2019) and 20200417-31 (April 17, 2020). The updated security lists reflect the latest periodic review of promoter encumbrance disclosures under Reg. 28(3) of the SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 2011.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members** must apply a minimum 35% margin on all equity and equity derivatives positions in Annexure I securities from June 5, 2026.
- **Companies** wishing to contest their inclusion in the framework must submit representations to BSE by **5:00 PM on June 3, 2026**.
- Members may refer to the referenced Exchange notices or contact BSE at bse.surv@bseindia.com for clarifications.

## Important Dates

| Date | Event |
|------|-------|
| June 3, 2026 | Annexure II securities exit framework; deadline for company representations (5:00 PM) |
| June 4, 2026 | Reference date for existing open positions subject to the new 35% margin |
| June 5, 2026 | 35% margin requirement becomes effective for Annexure I securities |

## Impact Assessment

Trading members and investors holding positions in newly included securities (Annexure I) will face increased margin requirements of at least 35% in both equity and equity derivatives segments starting June 5, 2026. This may constrain leveraged positions and increase the cost of holding or creating fresh positions in affected stocks. Securities exiting the framework (Annexure II) will revert to standard margin norms from June 3, 2026, potentially reducing the cost of trading those securities. The measure is routine and does not signal regulatory action against the companies involved.
