## Summary

BSE has issued a notice, in continuation of its earlier Notice No. 20260706-28 dated July 06, 2026, informing trading members that the equity shares of Sampark India Logistics Limited (Scrip Code: 544810) will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment with effect from Tuesday, July 21, 2026. The company's shares will accordingly be dealt under the M Group.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code 544810 (Sampark India Logistics Limited) is being moved out of the Trade for Trade (MT Group) segment.
- The stock will be shifted to the Rolling segment under M Group.
- This is a follow-up to Notice No. 20260706-28 dated July 06, 2026.
- Segment falls under SME category, Department: DCS-Listing.
- Trading members can contact Mr. Anurag Jain (Tel: 022-2272 8822) for further details.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced; this is an administrative reclassification of the trading segment/group for a specific SME-listed company, executed under existing BSE surveillance and settlement group norms.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must update their systems to reflect the change in settlement/trading group for this scrip from MT Group to M Group starting the effective date, and ensure trades are processed under the Rolling segment rules going forward.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 07, 2026
- Prior related notice: July 06, 2026 (Notice No. 20260706-28)
- Effective date of segment change: Tuesday, July 21, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The shift from Trade for Trade to Rolling segment generally indicates improved liquidity or normalization of trading conditions for the stock, allowing standard rolling settlement instead of the more restrictive trade-for-trade mechanism. This is relevant for shareholders, traders, and brokers dealing in Sampark India Logistics Limited shares, though the impact is confined to this single SME-listed company.
