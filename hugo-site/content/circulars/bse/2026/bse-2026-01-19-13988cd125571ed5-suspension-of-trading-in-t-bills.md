## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in two Treasury Bills (T-Bills) effective January 20, 2026, as these securities will mature on their redemption date. Trading members are instructed not to deal with these T-Bills from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Two T-Bills are being suspended from trading due to maturity
- SCRIP CODE 805095 (ISIN: IN002025Y172) - 182TB22126
- SCRIP CODE 805018 (ISIN: IN002024Z412) - 364TB22126
- Trading suspension effective from January 20, 2026
- Notice reference: DR-802/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational procedure for maturing debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in the specified T-Bills from January 20, 2026
- Members must ensure no new trades are executed in SCRIP CODE 805095 and 805018
- Acknowledge and comply with notice DR-802/2025-2026

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 19, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Date**: January 20, 2026
- **Redemption Date**: As per maturity schedule

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine maturity-related suspension affecting only two T-Bill instruments in the debt segment. Trading members holding positions in these securities should complete their transactions before the suspension date or hold until redemption. No broader market implications expected.
