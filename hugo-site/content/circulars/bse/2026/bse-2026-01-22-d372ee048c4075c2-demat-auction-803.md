## Summary

BSE has announced a demat auction for Settlement No. 803 dated January 22, 2026. The auction covers 100 scrips with specified quantities ranging from 1 share to 9,715 shares across various companies including major names like Reliance Industries, Bharat Electronics, IFCI, and YES Bank.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 803
- Date: January 22, 2026
- Total scrips covered: 100 companies
- Auction type: Demat auction for delivery obligations
- Quantities range from 1 share (multiple scrips) to 9,715 shares (Reliance Power)
- Notable quantities include: Reliance Power (9,715 shares), Bharat Electronics (3,785 shares), Jubilant Ingrevia (3,750 shares), IFCI (2,800 shares), Bharat Coking (2,650 shares)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard operational circular for demat auction proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members with outstanding delivery obligations for the listed scrips in Settlement No. 803 must participate in the demat auction
- Delivery of securities as per the quantities specified in the auction list
- Settlement of obligations as per BSE auction procedures and timelines

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: January 22, 2026
- Settlement Number: 803

## Impact Assessment

This demat auction affects members who have failed to deliver securities for Settlement No. 803. The auction mechanism ensures settlement completion and market integrity. Impact is localized to members with delivery shortfalls in the specified scrips. The wide range of companies affected (100 scrips) indicates standard market activity with delivery obligations across multiple sectors including financial services, industrial manufacturing, energy, and technology.
