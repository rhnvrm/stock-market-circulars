## Summary

BSE's Surveillance Department (DOSS) has issued Notice No. 20260625-17 dated 25 June 2026, informing members and investors that the Exchange has sought clarification from two companies regarding news articles appearing in various newspapers, websites, and television channels. As of the notice date, replies from both companies are still awaited.

## Key Points

- BSE proactively sought clarifications to verify accuracy of news articles circulating in media
- Two companies have been asked to respond: Rajesh Exports Ltd and Tata Motors Passenger Vehicles Ltd
- Both companies sourced from MoneyControl articles
- Replies from both companies are pending as of notice date
- Notice issued by BSE Surveillance Department (DOSS)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine surveillance action by BSE to ensure market transparency and accurate information dissemination to investors.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Rajesh Exports Ltd (Scrip Code: 531500)**: Required to submit clarification to BSE regarding news article on MoneyControl
- **Tata Motors Passenger Vehicles Ltd (Scrip Code: 500570)**: Required to submit clarification to BSE regarding news article on MoneyControl
- Both companies are expected to provide timely responses to the Exchange's queries

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: 25 June 2026
- **Reply Status**: Awaited from both companies as of 25 June 2026

## Impact Assessment

This notice signals that BSE's surveillance team has identified potentially market-sensitive news articles relating to Rajesh Exports Ltd and Tata Motors Passenger Vehicles Ltd. Until clarifications are received and published, investors should exercise caution regarding unverified news about these companies. The outcome of the clarifications could have varying market impact depending on the nature of the news articles in question. Tata Motors, being a large-cap stock, may attract wider investor attention pending the clarification.
