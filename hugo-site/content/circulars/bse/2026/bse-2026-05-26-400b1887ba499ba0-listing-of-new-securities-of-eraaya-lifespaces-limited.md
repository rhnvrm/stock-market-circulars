## Summary

BSE has listed 44,75,000 new equity shares of Eraaya Lifespaces Limited (Scrip Code: 531035, ISIN: INE432F01032) issued to promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. These shares are permitted to trade on BSE with effect from Wednesday, May 27, 2026. The shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company.

## Key Points

- 44,75,000 equity shares of Rs. 1/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 80/- (issue price Rs. 81/-) to promoters
- Issued on preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants
- Trading permitted from May 27, 2026
- All 44,75,000 newly listed shares are under lock-in until November 30, 2027
- Two tranches allotted: 32,00,000 shares on April 28, 2026 and 12,75,000 shares on April 29, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice informing trading members of new securities admitted to trading on BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to permit trading in these securities from May 27, 2026. Lock-in restrictions must be observed — all 44,75,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 209819161 to 214294160) are locked in and cannot be traded until November 30, 2027.

## Important Dates

- **April 28, 2026**: Allotment of 32,00,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 209819161–213019160)
- **April 29, 2026**: Allotment of 12,75,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 213019161–214294160)
- **May 27, 2026**: Effective date for trading of new securities on BSE
- **November 30, 2027**: Lock-in expiry for all 44,75,000 newly listed shares

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 44,75,000 shares to the BSE-tradable float of Eraaya Lifespaces Limited; however, since all newly issued shares are under promoter lock-in until November 30, 2027, there is no immediate increase in free-float. The preferential allotment at Rs. 81/- per share (face value Rs. 1/- at a premium of Rs. 80/-) represents a capital raise from the promoter group via warrant conversion. Market impact is limited in the near term due to the lock-in period.
