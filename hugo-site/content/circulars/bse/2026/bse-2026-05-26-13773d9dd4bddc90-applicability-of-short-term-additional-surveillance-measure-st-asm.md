## Summary

BSE has notified the inclusion of certain securities under the Short Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) under both Stage I and Stage II frameworks. Enhanced margin requirements will be effective from May 29, 2026, applicable on all open positions as on May 27, 2026, and all new positions created from May 29, 2026 onwards. The specific securities are listed in the attached Annexure I.

## Key Points

- Securities in **ST-ASM Stage I**: Applicable margin raised to 50% or existing margin, whichever is higher, subject to a maximum cap of 100%.
- Securities in **ST-ASM Stage II**: Applicable margin raised to 100% or existing margin, whichever is higher, subject to a maximum cap of 100%.
- Enhanced margins apply to open positions as on May 27, 2026, and new positions from May 29, 2026.
- ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures.
- Shortlisting under ASM is purely a market surveillance action and should not be construed as an adverse action against the concerned company or entity.
- Upon exit from the ASM framework, the price band of a scrip will be reinstated to the level applicable before it was shortlisted, provided it is not under any other surveillance measure.

## Regulatory Changes

This notice is issued further to prior Exchange notices including:

No new framework revisions are introduced; this circular applies the existing ST-ASM framework to newly shortlisted securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members**: Must ensure collection of enhanced margins (50% for Stage I, 100% for Stage II) from clients holding or creating positions in the notified securities.
- **Market Participants**: Must note that ASM is in addition to all other existing surveillance measures; the most restrictive measure prevails.
- For clarifications, members may contact BSE Surveillance at **bse.surv@bseindia.com**.

## Impact Assessment

Traders and investors holding positions in the securities listed in Annexure I will face significantly higher margin requirements from May 29, 2026. Stage II securities will require 100% margin, effectively requiring full cash cover for positions. This may lead to forced position liquidation for market participants unable to meet the enhanced margin calls, potentially increasing selling pressure on affected scrips. The measure is designed to curb speculative activity and enhance market integrity. Price bands for exiting securities will revert to pre-ASM levels only if no other surveillance measure is concurrently applicable.
