## Summary

BSE published clarifications from two listed companies - Mukat Pipes Limited (Stock Code: 523832) and Antelopus Selan Energy Limited (formerly Selan Exploration Technology Limited) - in response to exchange queries regarding significant price movements in their securities. Both companies confirmed compliance with SEBI (LODR) Regulations 2015 and stated there is no undisclosed price-sensitive information or pending corporate actions. The companies attributed the price movements to market-driven forces beyond their control.

## Key Points

- Mukat Pipes Limited responded on January 20, 2026 to BSE's clarification request regarding significant share price movement
- Antelopus Selan Energy Limited responded to BSE's email dated January 19, 2026 (Ref. No. L/SURV/ONL/PV/AJ/2025-2026/3295)
- Both companies confirmed full compliance with SEBI (LODR) Regulations 2015
- No undisclosed price-sensitive information exists at either company
- No impending announcements or corporate actions requiring disclosure
- Companies continue to promptly inform exchanges of all material events as required
- Price movements attributed to market conditions and market-driven factors

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies reaffirmed their ongoing commitment to comply with SEBI (LODR) Regulations 2015
- Continued prompt disclosure of all material events, information, and actions to stock exchanges
- Timely submission of price-sensitive information under Regulation 30 of SEBI (LODR) Regulations 2015

## Important Dates

- January 19, 2026: BSE sent clarification request to Antelopus Selan Energy Limited
- January 20, 2026: Mukat Pipes Limited submitted clarification response
- January 20, 2026: Antelopus Selan Energy Limited submitted clarification response

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal to none. These are standard surveillance clarifications that confirm no material undisclosed information exists. The circular provides transparency that recent price movements in both stocks are market-driven rather than based on corporate developments.

**Investor Impact:** Neutral. Investors are informed that no corporate actions or material events are pending that would warrant the observed price movements. This helps prevent speculation about undisclosed developments.

**Operational Impact:** None. Both companies are operating normally with no changes to their disclosure practices or corporate governance processes.
