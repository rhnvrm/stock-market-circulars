## Summary

BSE has issued a follow-up notice to its earlier Notice No. 20260630-15 dated June 30, 2026, informing trading members of the new ISIN number allotted to Gujarat Inject Kerala Ltd (Scrip Code: 524238) following the sub-division of its equity shares from a face value of Rs.10/- to Re.1/- per share.

## Key Points

- The new ISIN for Gujarat Inject Kerala Ltd's equity shares (post sub-division) is INE659F01022.
- The change relates to sub-division of equity shares from face value Rs.10/- to Re.1/- each.
- This notice is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20260630-15 dated June 30, 2026, which originally announced the sub-division.
- The new ISIN will apply to trades executed on and from the ex-date.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory change; this is an operational/administrative update implementing a previously approved corporate action (sub-division of shares) by assigning the corresponding new ISIN.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must update their systems to reflect the new ISIN (INE659F01022) for Gujarat Inject Kerala Ltd's equity shares for all trades executed on or after the ex-date. Reference number DR-667/2026-2027 should be noted for record-keeping.

## Important Dates

- Ex-Date: 08-07-2026 (new ISIN becomes effective for trades from this date)
- Notice Date: 07 July 2026
- Original corporate action notice: 20260630-15 dated June 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to Gujarat Inject Kerala Ltd and its trading members/shareholders. Trading systems and back-office processes must correctly map the old and new ISINs to ensure accurate settlement and record-keeping post ex-date. No broader market impact is expected.
