## Summary

BSE has issued a notice suspending trading in Treasury Bill scrip code 805133 (ISIN: IN002025X307, Security: 91TB220126) effective January 21, 2026, as the instrument will mature on January 22, 2026. Trading members are advised not to deal in this T-Bill from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- T-Bill scrip code 805133 (91TB220126) with ISIN IN002025X307 to be suspended from trading
- Suspension effective from January 21, 2026
- Reason: Instrument maturity on redemption date
- Notice reference: DR-803/2025-2026
- Category: Corporate Actions - Debt Segment

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for instrument maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the mentioned T-Bill (scrip code 805133) from January 21, 2026 onwards
- Members should update their trading systems to reflect the suspension
- Ensure client communications regarding unavailability of this instrument for trading

## Important Dates

- **January 20, 2026**: Notice issued
- **January 21, 2026**: Trading suspension effective date
- **January 22, 2026**: T-Bill maturity/redemption date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine suspension for a single maturing T-Bill instrument. Treasury Bills have defined maturity dates and such suspensions are scheduled events. No broader market implications.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Trading members need to ensure their systems reflect the suspension, but this is standard procedure for maturing debt instruments. Investors holding the T-Bill will receive redemption proceeds as per normal settlement process.
