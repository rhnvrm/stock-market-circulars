## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 55,64,283 new equity shares of Raconteur Global Resources Limited (Scrip Code: 541703) are listed and permitted to trade on BSE with effect from Wednesday, May 13, 2026. These shares were issued on a preferential basis to Non-Promoters at an issue price of Rs. 14/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- at a premium of Rs. 4/-).

## Key Points

- 55,64,283 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 4/- per share
- Shares issued to Non-Promoters on a preferential basis
- Issue price: Rs. 14/- per share
- ISIN: INE00WY01013
- Distribution numbers: 5213562 to 10777844
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Lock-in details as per Annexure I

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice for newly allotted securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the listing of these new securities and update their records accordingly. Lock-in restrictions apply as detailed in Annexure I of the notice.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: March 18, 2026
- Trading commencement date: May 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing increases the tradable share count of Raconteur Global Resources Limited (541703) by 55,64,283 shares. The shares were issued at Rs. 14/- each on a preferential basis to Non-Promoters, which may result in minor dilution for existing shareholders. Impact is limited to existing holders of this scrip and is routine in nature.
