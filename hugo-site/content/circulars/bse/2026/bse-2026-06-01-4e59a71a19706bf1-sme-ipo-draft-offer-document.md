## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document of Amtech Esters Limited, filed on May 31, 2026, on the BSE SME website. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and is available for public comments for a minimum period of 21 days.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Amtech Esters Limited on May 31, 2026
- Document is available on the BSE SME website for at least 21 days
- Disclosures comply with Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations
- Public comments are invited within 21 days from June 1, 2026
- Comments to be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard procedural notice under the ICDR Regulations requiring public display of the Draft Offer Document prior to an SME IPO.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members of the public and stakeholders may review the Draft Offer Document at https://www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Any comments or objections must be submitted within 21 days of the notice date (by approximately June 22, 2026) to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Filing Date:** May 31, 2026
- **Notice Date:** June 1, 2026
- **Public Comment Deadline:** On or before June 22, 2026 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a routine disclosure step in the SME IPO process for Amtech Esters Limited. It affects only prospective investors and stakeholders interested in this specific company's upcoming public issue. No trading or operational changes are involved.
