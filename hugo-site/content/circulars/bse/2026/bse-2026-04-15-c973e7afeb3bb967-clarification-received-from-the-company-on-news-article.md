## Summary

BSE's Surveillance department (DOSS) issued Notice No. 20260415-12 dated 15 April 2026, informing members and investors that it had sought a clarification from Physicswallah Ltd (Scrip Code: 544609) regarding news articles circulating in newspapers, websites, and television channels. The company has submitted its reply, which is attached to the notice.

## Key Points

- BSE Surveillance department proactively sought clarification from Physicswallah Ltd in response to news articles appearing across various media outlets.
- The news article referenced originates from Business Standard (https://www.business-standard.com).
- Physicswallah Ltd (Scrip Code: 544609) has provided its reply, which is attached to the notice.
- The notice is issued to keep members and investors informed about the accuracy of media reports.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this notice.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements are imposed. This is an informational disclosure initiated by BSE's Surveillance department under its standard practice of verifying media reports that may affect market sentiment or investor decisions.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 15 April 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited. This is a routine surveillance-driven clarification notice with no trading restrictions, penalties, or structural changes. Investors in Physicswallah Ltd (Scrip Code: 544609) should review the attached company reply for context on the specific news article referenced. No broader market or operational impact is anticipated.
