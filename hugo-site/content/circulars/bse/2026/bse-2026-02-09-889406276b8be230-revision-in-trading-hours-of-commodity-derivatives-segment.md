## Summary

BSE has announced a revision in trading hours for the Commodity Derivatives Segment effective March 09, 2026. This change is necessitated by the adjustment in US daylight saving timings. The continuous trading session for internationally referenceable non-agricultural commodities will now end at 11:30 PM, with trade modification timings at 11:45 PM.

## Key Points

- Revised trading hours effective from March 09, 2026
- Change triggered by US daylight saving time adjustment
- Applies to internationally referenceable non-agricultural commodities
- Continuous trading: 9:00 AM to 11:30 PM
- Trade modification window: 11:45 PM
- References previous notice 20250929-55 dated September 29, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

This circular updates the trading hours framework for commodity derivatives trading, specifically for internationally referenceable non-agricultural commodities. The revision aligns BSE's trading hours with international market timings following the US daylight saving time change.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the revised trading hours
- Systems and operations must be adjusted to accommodate the new timings from March 09, 2026
- Trading members should contact their respective Relationship Manager for clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: February 09, 2026
- **Effective Date**: March 09, 2026
- **Reference Notice**: 20250929-55 dated September 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The revision has moderate operational impact on trading members dealing in commodity derivatives. Members will need to adjust their trading strategies, risk management systems, and operational schedules to accommodate the modified hours. The change ensures continued synchronization with international commodity markets following the US daylight saving time adjustment. No impact on equities or other segments.
