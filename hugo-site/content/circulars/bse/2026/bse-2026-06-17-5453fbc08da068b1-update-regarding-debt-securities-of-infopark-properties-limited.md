## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members that the debt securities of Infopark Properties Limited (Scrip Code 974042, ISIN INE0KZX08021) will be available for trading with revised terms effective June 18, 2026. This notice is in continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20220711-25 dated July 11, 2022.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code 974042 with ISIN INE0KZX08021 will trade under revised terms from June 18, 2026
- This update continues from Exchange Notice No. 20220711-25 dated July 11, 2022
- Revised terms are detailed in the Annexure attached to the notice
- Contact: Mr. Akash Shinde (+91 22 2272 8844, debt.listing@bseindia.com) or Hardik Bhuta (Assistant Vice President)

## Regulatory Changes

Revised trading terms for Infopark Properties Limited debt securities (Scrip Code 974042, ISIN INE0KZX08021) as specified in the Annexure.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must update their systems to reflect the revised terms for Scrip Code 974042 (ISIN INE0KZX08021) before trading commences on June 18, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **June 17, 2026**: Notice issued
- **June 18, 2026 (Thursday)**: Revised terms become effective for trading

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative update with limited market impact. Only trading members dealing in Infopark Properties Limited debt securities (Scrip Code 974042) need to take note and update trading parameters accordingly. The change is isolated to a single debt instrument.
