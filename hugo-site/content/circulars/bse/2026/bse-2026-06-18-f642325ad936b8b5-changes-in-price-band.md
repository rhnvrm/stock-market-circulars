## Summary

BSE has revised the price bands for 24 equity scrips effective June 19, 2026. The revisions set price bands ranging from 2% to 20%, with the majority of scrips moved to a 5% band, indicating heightened surveillance activity on these securities.

## Key Points

- 24 scrips have price band revisions effective June 19, 2026
- Most scrips (15 of 24) revised to a 5% price band
- Three scrips — Crystal Business System Ltd (540821), Daikaffil Chemicals India Ltd (530825), Kesoram Industries Ltd (502937), and Vishnu Prakash R Punglia Ltd (543974) — moved to a restrictive 2% band
- Gujarat Poly Electronics Ltd (517288) and SHAH METACORP Ltd (751121) revised to 10%
- Aureate Tradde Ltd (544772) revised to 20%
- Issued by BSE Surveillance Department (DOSS)

## Regulatory Changes

The price band for each listed scrip has been revised from its existing level to the new percentage as specified. Price bands limit the maximum intraday price movement permissible for a security on the exchange.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must note the revised price bands for the affected scrips and ensure all orders placed from June 19, 2026 onwards comply with the new limits. Members requiring clarification may contact bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 18, 2026
- **Effective Date:** June 19, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Scrips moved to a 2% band (Crystal Business System, Daikaffil Chemicals, Kesoram Industries, Vishnu Prakash R Punglia) face the most restrictive trading constraints, limiting intraday price discovery and potentially reducing liquidity. The 5% band revisions represent standard surveillance tightening for small and mid-cap stocks showing irregular price or volume movements. Aureate Tradde Ltd's revision to 20% may indicate a relaxation of a previously tighter band. Traders and investors holding positions in any of these 24 scrips should factor in the new daily price movement limits when planning trades from June 19, 2026.
