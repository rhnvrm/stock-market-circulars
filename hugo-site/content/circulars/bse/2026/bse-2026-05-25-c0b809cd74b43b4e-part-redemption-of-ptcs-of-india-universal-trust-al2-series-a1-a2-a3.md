## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that Catalyst Trusteeship Ltd has notified the reduced face values per PTC (Pass Through Certificate) following partial redemption of India Universal Trust AL2 - Series A1, A2, and A3. The revised face values will be effective from May 26, 2026 (DR-638/2025-2026).

## Key Points

- Catalyst Trusteeship Ltd is the trustee that has communicated the reduced face values post partial redemption
- Three series of PTCs are affected: Series A1, A2, and A3
- The PTCs will trade with the new reduced face values effective May 26, 2026
- This is part of the scheduled amortization of these asset-backed securities

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an operational notice updating the traded face values of existing debt instruments following a contractual partial redemption event.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to take note of the revised reduced face values for the affected PTCs and ensure trades from May 26, 2026 onwards reflect the updated face values per PTC.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** May 25, 2026
- **Effective Date for Revised Face Values:** May 26, 2026
- Reference: DR-638/2025-2026
