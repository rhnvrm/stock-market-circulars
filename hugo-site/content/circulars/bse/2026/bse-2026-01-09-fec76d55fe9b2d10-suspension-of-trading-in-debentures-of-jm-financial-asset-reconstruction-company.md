## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in non-convertible debentures (NCD) of JM Financial Asset Reconstruction Company Ltd effective January 16, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Company: JM Financial Asset Reconstruction Company Ltd (Code: JMFARCL)
- ISIN: INE265J07506 (974582)
- Debenture type: 10.21% Non-Convertible Debentures
- Record date: January 16, 2026
- No dealings from: January 16, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Notice reference: DR-800/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notice for debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures (INE265J07506) from January 16, 2026
- Members are required to take note of the suspension and inform their clients accordingly
- All trading activity in these debentures must cease on the specified date

## Important Dates

- **January 9, 2026**: Circular issued
- **January 16, 2026**: Record date for redemption and interest payment
- **January 16, 2026**: Trading suspension effective date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine corporate action affecting a single debt instrument. The suspension is temporary and limited to debenture holders of JM Financial Asset Reconstruction Company Ltd.

**Investor Impact**: Low to medium for debenture holders - Investors holding these debentures will receive their redemption amount and final interest payment. No trading will be possible from the record date.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Standard procedure for debenture redemption that trading members regularly handle. No system changes or special procedures required beyond blocking trades in the specified ISIN.
