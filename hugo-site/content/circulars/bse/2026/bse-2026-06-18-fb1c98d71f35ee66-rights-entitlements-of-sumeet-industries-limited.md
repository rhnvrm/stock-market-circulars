## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that dealings in Rights Entitlements (REs) for renunciation of Sumeet Industries Limited will commence on Monday, June 22, 2026. The REs are tradable in dematerialised form only and will be settled on a T+1 rolling settlement basis on a trade-for-trade basis.

## Key Points

- Rights Entitlements (REs) for Sumeet Industries Limited will be available for trading on BSE from June 22, 2026.
- Scrip Code: 751123; Scrip ID: SUMEET-RE; ISIN: INE235C20010; Market Lot: 1; Group: R.
- Trading period for renunciation runs from June 22, 2026 to July 15, 2026.
- The underlying Rights Issue is open from June 22, 2026 to July 20, 2026.
- Settlement is on T+1 rolling basis, trade-for-trade.
- REs are tradable in dematerialised form only.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The notice references existing guidelines from BSE notice no. 20200305-21 (March 5, 2020) and notice no. 20200518-34 (May 18, 2020) for further operational details on RE trading.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must inform all clients about the commencement of RE trading, specifying the ISIN (INE235C20010).
- Members must clarify that REs should not be mistaken for ordinary shares of Sumeet Industries Limited.
- Contract notes/bills must include a prominent disclaimer stating that purchase of REs only confers the right to participate in the Rights Issue or renounce the REs before issue closure.
- REs neither subscribed nor renounced by the Issue Closing Date (July 20, 2026) will lapse and be extinguished.

## Important Dates

- **June 22, 2026**: Commencement of RE trading on BSE; Rights Issue opens.
- **July 15, 2026**: Last date for trading/renunciation of Rights Entitlements.
- **July 20, 2026**: Rights Issue closes; unsubscribed/unrenounced REs lapse after this date.

## Impact Assessment

This notice is relevant to existing shareholders of Sumeet Industries Limited who received Rights Entitlements and to trading members facilitating RE transactions. The trade-for-trade settlement and T+1 basis limit speculative activity. Shareholders must act before July 20, 2026 to either subscribe to the rights issue or renounce their entitlements; failure to do so results in the REs lapsing with no residual value.
