## Summary

Bharat Forge Limited (BSE: 500493, NSE: BHARATFORG) submitted a clarification to BSE and NSE on March 5, 2026, in response to exchange queries regarding a news item published on cnbctv18.com relating to North American Class 8 truck orders. The company confirms there are no specific undisclosed negotiations, material events, or regulatory proceedings that require disclosure under Regulation 30 of the SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015.

## Key Points

- The news article referenced industry-level data and broader market trends on North American Class 8 truck orders, not company-specific developments.
- Bharat Forge confirms no specific negotiations or material developments are currently underway requiring disclosure under Regulation 30 of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015.
- The company is not aware of any undisclosed material information that could explain price or volume movement in its shares.
- No new regulatory or legal proceedings have been initiated beyond what has already been disclosed to exchanges.
- The article has no material impact on the company's operations or financial position.
- The company asserts it has been making timely disclosures of all material events as required under applicable regulations.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a clarification filing with no new regulatory changes.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. The company reaffirms its ongoing compliance with Regulation 30 of the SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015 regarding timely disclosure of material events.

## Important Dates

- **March 5, 2026**: Date of CNBC TV18 news article, exchange queries (BSE email L/SURV/ONL/RV/SG/2025-2026/207 and NSE letter NSE/CM/Surveillance/16538), and this clarification filing.

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The clarification is routine in nature, confirming that the referenced news pertains to industry-wide data rather than any company-specific undisclosed event. No material impact on Bharat Forge's operations, financials, or share price is anticipated from this filing.
