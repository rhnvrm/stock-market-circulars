## Summary

BSE has announced adjustments to all Futures and Options contracts on ITC Ltd (Derivative Asset Code: ITCL, Equity Scrip Code: 500875) in the Equity Derivatives Segment. The adjustments are necessitated by ITC Ltd's declaration of a final dividend of Rs. 8.00 per equity share for FY 2026-27, with May 27, 2026 fixed as the Record Date.

## Key Points

- ITC Ltd has declared a final dividend of Rs. 8.00 per equity share for FY 2026-27
- Record Date: May 27, 2026
- Ex-date: May 27, 2026 (Wednesday)
- Adjustment execution date: May 26, 2026 (Tuesday)
- All available Futures & Options contracts on ITCL will be adjusted
- Adjustment is in accordance with Exchange Notice No. 20180710-26 and SEBI Master Circular SEBI/HO/MRD/DP/CIR/P/2016/135 dated December 16, 2016

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. Adjustments are carried out under the existing framework established by SEBI Master Circular No. SEBI/HO/MRD/DP/CIR/P/2016/135 (December 16, 2016) and BSE Exchange Notice No. 20180710-26 on review of adjustment of corporate actions for stock options.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members of the Equity Derivatives Segment are informed of the upcoming contract adjustments
- Members should update their systems to reflect adjusted strike prices and futures prices effective May 27, 2026
- Members with queries are requested to contact their respective BSE Relationship Manager

## Important Dates

- **May 26, 2026 (Tuesday):** Exchange executes adjustments; adjusted strike prices and futures prices computed at end of day
- **May 27, 2026 (Wednesday):** Ex-date and Record Date; adjusted prices available for trading from market open

## Impact Assessment

**Options Contracts:** Rs. 8.00 will be deducted from all strike prices generated at the close of May 26, 2026. Adjusted strike prices will be effective for trading from May 27, 2026 onwards.

**Futures Contracts:** The adjusted futures price will be calculated as the daily mark-to-market (MTM) settlement price of each relevant futures contract on May 26, 2026, minus the dividend amount of Rs. 8.00. This adjusted rate will serve as the base rate/previous close for trading on May 27, 2026.

The adjustment is a standard, exchange-mandated process to ensure F&O contract prices reflect the dividend outflow, preventing arbitrage opportunities between the cash and derivatives markets around the ex-date. Traders holding positions in ITC F&O contracts should factor in these adjustments when managing their portfolios around May 26–27, 2026.
