## Summary

BSE has notified that BANDHAN GOLD ETF FOF (Fund of Funds) will be available for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform from January 28, 2026. The scheme will support Systematic Investment Plan (SIP), Systematic Transfer Plan (STP), and Systematic Withdrawal Plan (SWP) facilities.

## Key Points

- BANDHAN GOLD ETF FOF reopening for ongoing transactions after previous closure
- Both Regular Plan and Direct Plan Growth options will be available
- Full transaction facilities including SIP, STP, and SWP will be enabled
- Scheme type is Equity with two ISIN options available
- References previous Notice No 20260109-39 regarding initial closure

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding fund availability.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must note the availability of the scheme for processing investor transactions
- Transactions can be processed through BSE StAR MF Platform from the effective date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 27, 2026
- **Effective Date**: January 28, 2026 - Scheme reopens for ongoing transactions

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine operational notice affecting a single mutual fund scheme.

**Investor Impact**: Low to medium for existing and potential investors in BANDHAN GOLD ETF FOF. Investors can now make fresh subscriptions, redemptions, and set up systematic plans.

**Operational Impact**: BSE StAR MF Platform members need to enable transaction processing for the two scheme plans (Regular and Direct) with ISINs INF194KB1KK5 and INF194KB1KL3 respectively.

## Scheme Details

1. **BANDHAN GOLD ETF FOF - REGULAR PLAN - GROWTH**
   - ISIN: INF194KB1KK5
   - Scheme Type: Equity

2. **BANDHAN GOLD ETF FOF - DIRECT PLAN - GROWTH**
   - ISIN: INF194KB1KL3
   - Scheme Type: Equity
