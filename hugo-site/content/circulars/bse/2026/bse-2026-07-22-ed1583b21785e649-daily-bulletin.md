- Notice No. 20260722-38 dated 22 July 2026 issued by BSE's Listing Operations Department under the Company related category, General segment.
- The circular attaches the Daily Bulletin file (bu220726) for 22nd July, 2026.

No specific compliance action is required from listed companies, members, or market participants as a direct result of this notice. The Daily Bulletin serves as a reference document for market data.
