## Summary

BSE has issued an update to its earlier notice (No. 20260527-40 dated May 27, 2026) concerning the FATCA/UBO file structure and validations on the BSE StAR MF platform. Based on inputs from Registrar and Transfer Agents (RTAs), additional tax descriptions have been added under the Non-Profit Organization (NPO) category, and RTAs will begin enforcing FATCA/UBO/NPO validations for all client categories from September 1, 2026, across the UI, Bulk File Upload Utility, and APIs.

## Key Points

- New tax descriptions added under the NPO category for FATCA module updates: Society, Trust, Foreign Contribution (Regulation) Act (FCRA), Non-Governmental Organization (NGO), and Section 25/Section 8 Company.
- RTAs will implement FATCA/UBO/NPO validations for all categories of clients maintained in their records effective September 1, 2026.
- Changes apply uniformly across the User Interface (UI), Bulk File Upload Utility, and APIs.
- For non-demat transactions requiring new folio creation/allotment at the AMC/RTA end, FATCA/UBO/NPO details captured at UCC creation or subsequent modification must be reported to the RTA's central team along with transaction details.
- Members must ensure client FATCA/UBO/NPO information is accurate, complete, and verified before order placement; incorrect, incomplete, inconsistent, or missing data may cause RTA rejection of transactions.
- Detailed process notes are provided in Annexure I (FATCA File Structure with Applicable Masters and Codes) and Annexure II (Process Notes on RTA Validation of FATCA/UBO/NPO).
- Members using third-party vendor solutions or in-house trading applications must complete necessary system development and modifications before the implementation date.

## Regulatory Changes

The FATCA/UBO file structure on the BSE StAR MF platform is revised to include expanded NPO tax category descriptions (Society, Trust, FCRA, NGO, Section 25/Section 8 Company). RTAs will additionally enforce FATCA/UBO/NPO validation checks across all client categories, extending beyond the scope previously communicated in Notice No. 20260527-40.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must update and verify FATCA/UBO/NPO details for all clients prior to order placement.
- For non-demat transactions involving new folio creation, FATCA/UBO/NPO details at the time of UCC creation/modification must be transmitted to the RTA's central team for onward processing by the AMC.
- Members relying on third-party vendors or in-house applications must complete system changes to align with the revised file structure and validation rules before September 1, 2026.
- Queries can be directed to the Mutual Fund Helpdesk (022-45720450/650 or 022-69158550, 8:00 a.m.–7:00 p.m. on business days) or via the CRS Portal (https://bsecrs.bseindia.com).

## Important Dates

- **May 27, 2026**: Original notice (20260527-40) on FATCA/UBO file structure and validations issued.
- **July 17, 2026**: Current update notice issued with revised NPO tax descriptions.
- **September 1, 2026**: RTA validations for FATCA/UBO/NPO requirements become effective for all client categories.

## Impact Assessment

This is an operational compliance update primarily affecting BSE StAR MF trading members, RTAs, and AMCs rather than listed securities or stock prices. Members must undertake system and process changes to ensure accurate FATCA/UBO/NPO data capture before the September 1, 2026 deadline, or risk transaction rejections by RTAs. The impact is confined to mutual fund operational workflows and does not affect equity market trading or listed stocks.
