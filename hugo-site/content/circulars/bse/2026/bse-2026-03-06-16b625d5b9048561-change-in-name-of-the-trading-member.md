## Summary

BSE has notified all trading members that Fortune Capital Services Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 6524) will officially change its name to Flattrade Broking Private Limited, effective Monday, 9th March 2026.

## Key Points

- Trading member Fortune Capital Services Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 6524) is changing its name
- New name: Flattrade Broking Private Limited
- Effective date: Monday, 9th March 2026
- For clarifications, trading members may contact membership.ops@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Amit Kadam, Assistant Vice President, Membership

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a purely administrative update reflecting a legal name change of a registered trading member.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action required from other trading members. Internal systems referencing Member No. 6524 should be updated to reflect the new entity name — Flattrade Broking Private Limited — from 9th March 2026 onwards.

## Important Dates

- **9th March 2026 (Monday):** Name change takes effect

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine administrative name change affecting only Member No. 6524. Other trading members and market participants interacting with this member should note the updated name for reference and record-keeping purposes.
