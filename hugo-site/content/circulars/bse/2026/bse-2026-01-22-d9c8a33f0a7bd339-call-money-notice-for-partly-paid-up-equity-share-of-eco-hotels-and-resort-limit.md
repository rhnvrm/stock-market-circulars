## Summary

BSE has activated the Call Money Module on its IBBS platform for Eco Hotels and Resort Limited to facilitate the first call on partly paid-up equity shares. Shareholders holding 1,28,76,808 partly paid-up equity shares (security symbol: ECOCM) are required to pay Rs. 3.80 per share. The issue opens on January 27, 2026, and closes on February 10, 2026, with a bidding period from 10:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. daily. The module is available to Self-Certified Syndicate Banks (SCSBs) and Trading Members with 3-in-1 type accounts to submit call money details.

## Key Points

- First call on partly paid-up equity shares of Eco Hotels and Resort Limited
- Total shares subject to call: 1,28,76,808 partly paid-up equity shares
- Call money amount: Rs. 3.80 per share
- Security symbol: ECOCM with market lot of 1
- Issue period: 15 days (January 27 - February 10, 2026)
- Bidding hours: 10:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. on each trading day
- Registrar: Bigshare Services Private Limited
- Process follows Exchange notice no. 20210104-37 dated January 4, 2021 and SEBI circular dated December 8, 2020

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice implements existing provisions under Exchange notice no. 20210104-37 (January 4, 2021) and SEBI circular dated December 8, 2020 regarding call money procedures for partly paid-up shares.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members and SCSBs**: Must use the Call Money Module on IBBS platform to submit details of call money notices collected during the issue period
- **Eligible Participants**: Only Self-Certified Syndicate Banks (SCSBs) and Trading Members with 3-in-1 type accounts can access the module
- **Shareholders**: Holders of partly paid-up equity shares must pay Rs. 3.80 per share during the specified period

## Important Dates

- **Issue Period Duration**: 15 days
- **Daily Bidding Window**: 10:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m.

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a company-specific corporate action affecting only existing shareholders of Eco Hotels and Resort Limited who hold partly paid-up equity shares.

**Operational Impact**: Medium for affected stakeholders - Trading Members, SCSBs, and intermediaries need to facilitate the call money collection process through the IBBS platform. Shareholders must arrange funds to meet the call obligation of Rs. 3.80 per share.
