## Summary

BSE has revised the effective date for trading in equity shares of Shankara Buildpro Limited (Scrip Code 544517) under 'B' group from the originally scheduled date to January 27, 2026. This revision is necessitated by the declaration of January 15, 2026 as a trading holiday on account of Municipal Corporation Elections in Maharashtra.

## Key Points

- Shankara Buildpro Limited (Scrip Code: 544517) group classification change revised
- Trading under 'B' group now effective from Tuesday, January 27, 2026
- Revision due to trading holiday on January 15, 2026 for Municipal Corporation Elections
- This is a continuation of earlier notices dated January 12, 2026 (Notice No. 20260112-8) and January 09, 2026 (Notice No. 20260109-9)
- All market participants are informed of this schedule change

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular updates the implementation timeline for a previously announced group classification change due to an intervening trading holiday.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must note the revised effective date of January 27, 2026 for trading Shankara Buildpro Limited shares under 'B' group
- Trading systems and processes should be updated to reflect the postponed group change date
- Participants should refer to earlier notices (20260112-8 and 20260109-9) for original group change details

## Important Dates

- **January 15, 2026**: Trading holiday on account of Municipal Corporation Elections in Maharashtra
- **January 27, 2026**: Revised effective date for trading Shankara Buildpro Limited equity shares under 'B' group

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to single stock (Shankara Buildpro Limited). The postponement provides additional time for market participants to prepare for the group classification change.

**Operational Impact**: Market participants need to update their systems with the revised timeline. The delay does not materially affect market operations but requires notification updates.

**Trading Impact**: Investors and traders in Shankara Buildpro Limited shares should be aware of the postponed group change effective date when planning their trading strategies.
