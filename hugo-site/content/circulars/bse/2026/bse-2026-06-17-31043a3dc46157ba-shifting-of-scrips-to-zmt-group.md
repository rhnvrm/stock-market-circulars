## Summary

BSE has announced the transfer of three listed scrips to the 'Z' group with effect from **June 29, 2026**, pursuant to SEBI's Master Circular on penal actions for non-compliance with SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015. The action is triggered by failure to submit the Reconciliation of Share Capital Audit Report (Regulation 76 of SEBI Depositories & Participants Regulations, 2018) for two consecutive quarters — December 2025 and March 2026. A compliance window until **June 23, 2026** is available to avoid the transfer.

## Key Points

- Three scrips are being transferred to 'Z' group effective June 29, 2026 due to non-compliance with Regulation 76 for Q3 and Q4 FY2025-26.
- Companies have until **June 23, 2026** to comply and avoid the group transfer.
- Trades executed in 'Z' group are settled on a **Trade-for-Trade (T2T) basis**, meaning no netting of positions.
- An additional 28 companies, already in the 'Z'/'MT' group, are also non-compliant for the same regulation and same quarters.
- Action is taken under SEBI Master Circular No. HO/49/14/14(7)2025-CFD-POD2/I/3762/2026 (originally issued July 11, 2023, last updated January 30, 2026).

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This notice is an enforcement action under the existing SEBI Master Circular (Chapter VII(A) — Penal Actions for Non-Compliance) and the Standard Operating Procedure for suspension and revocation of trading of specified securities of listed entities.

## Compliance Requirements

**Regulation 76** of SEBI (Depositories & Participants) Regulations, 2018 requires listed companies to submit a Reconciliation of Share Capital Audit Report on a quarterly basis. The three scrips below have failed to submit this report for two consecutive quarters:

| Scrip Code | Company Name |
|---|---|
| 539982 | Advance Syntex Ltd |
| 539402 | Naksh Precious Metals Ltd |
| 540955 | Tasty Dairy Specialities Ltd |

## Important Dates

- **June 23, 2026**: Deadline for the three scrips to achieve compliance and avoid group transfer.
- **June 29, 2026**: Effective date of transfer to 'Z' group if compliance is not achieved by June 23, 2026.

## Impact Assessment

Investors in the three scrips being transferred (Advance Syntex Ltd, Naksh Precious Metals Ltd, Tasty Dairy Specialities Ltd) will face significantly reduced liquidity as 'Z' group trades are settled on a **Trade-for-Trade basis** — intraday netting is not permitted, increasing settlement risk and reducing trading flexibility. This action signals ongoing governance and compliance concerns at these companies. The 28 companies already in 'Z'/'MT' group face continued trading restrictions.
