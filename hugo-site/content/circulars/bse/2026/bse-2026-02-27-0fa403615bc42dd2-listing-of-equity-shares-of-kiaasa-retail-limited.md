## Summary

BSE Limited has informed trading members that the equity shares of **Kiaasa Retail Limited** will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course under the SME segment. This notice serves as a preliminary intimation; a separate notice will provide the exact listing date and security details.

## Key Points

- Kiaasa Retail Limited equity shares are approved for listing on the BSE SME segment.
- Scrip Code: **544711**
- Trading Symbol: **KIAASA**
- ISIN: **INE1C3F01018**
- The specific listing date and security details will be communicated through a subsequent notice.
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this circular. It is a standard listing intimation under BSE's compliance framework for SME IPOs.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are hereby informed and should update their systems to accommodate the new scrip once the listing date is officially announced.
- Members should await the follow-up notice for the confirmed listing date and full security details before initiating any dealings.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** February 27, 2026
- **Listing Date:** To be announced via a separate notice.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative notice with limited market-wide impact. The listing is confined to the BSE SME segment, which caters to small and medium enterprises. Market participants interested in Kiaasa Retail Limited should monitor BSE announcements for the confirmed listing date. No immediate action is required by trading members beyond awareness of the upcoming listing.
