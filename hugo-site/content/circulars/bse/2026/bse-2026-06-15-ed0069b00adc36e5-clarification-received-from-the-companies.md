## Summary

BSE's Surveillance department (DOSS) issued Notice No. 20260615-24 on 15 June 2026, noting significant price/volume movement in 19 scrips. The Exchange had written to these companies seeking clarifications to ensure trading members and investors have access to the latest relevant information. Replies have been received from 13 companies; 6 companies are yet to respond.

## Key Points

- BSE observed significant price/volume movement in 19 listed scrips and sought clarifications from the respective companies.
- 13 companies have submitted their replies (attached to the notice as Replies1, Replies2, and Replies3).
- 6 companies have not yet responded; their replies are awaited.
- The notice is issued under the Equity segment by the Department of Surveillance and Supervision (DOSS).

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is a routine surveillance disclosure notice requiring companies to explain unusual price or volume activity in their shares.

## Compliance Requirements

- The 6 companies awaiting reply are expected to submit their clarifications to BSE at the earliest.
- Trading members and investors in the mentioned scrips should review the attached company replies for material disclosures before making investment decisions.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 15 June 2026
- No specific deadline is stated for pending replies, but the Exchange's standard practice expects prompt responses.
