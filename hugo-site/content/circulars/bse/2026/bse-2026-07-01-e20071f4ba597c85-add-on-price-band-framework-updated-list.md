## Summary

BSE has issued an updated list of securities identified for inclusion under the Add-on Price Band Framework, pursuant to its earlier circular no. 20210811-6 dated August 11, 2021. The Exchange's Surveillance Department conducted a periodic review to identify securities meeting the criteria for this framework, with the updated list provided as Annexure I to the notice.

## Key Points

- This circular is a follow-up to BSE circular no. 20210811-6 dated August 11, 2021, which originally introduced the Add-on Price Band Framework.
- The Exchange's Surveillance Department (DOSS) has completed a fresh review to identify securities for placement under this framework.
- The updated list of identified securities is contained in Annexure I attached to the notice.
- Members with queries can contact the Exchange at bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced; this is a periodic update to the list of securities covered under the existing Add-on Price Band Framework established in 2021.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should review Annexure I to identify which securities have been newly added to or retained in the Add-on Price Band Framework and ensure trading systems and client communications reflect the applicable price band restrictions for these securities.

## Important Dates

Notice issued and effective July 01, 2026 (Notice No. 20260701-36).

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to the specific securities named in Annexure I, which will be subject to additional price band restrictions to curb excessive volatility. Traders and investors holding or trading these securities should note the tighter price movement limits. The broader market impact is minimal, as this is a routine surveillance measure rather than a systemic policy change.
