## Summary

BSE has published the schedule and company list for Registrar & Transfer Agent (RTA) visits to BSE Ltd. during March 2026. The circular provides a detailed mapping of BSE-listed companies to their respective RTAs, enabling investors to identify the appropriate RTA for resolving share transfer-related grievances. Adroit Corporate Services Pvt. Ltd. is listed as the RTA for a large number of companies in this circular.

## Key Points

- BSE has scheduled RTA visits to its premises for March 2026 to facilitate investor grievance redressal.
- Adroit Corporate Services Pvt. Ltd. serves as the RTA for over 50 BSE-listed companies included in this circular.
- The list covers companies across diverse sectors including finance, steel, textiles, pharmaceuticals, chemicals, and electronics.
- Investors with pending transfer requests or grievances can contact the respective RTA during the scheduled visit period.
- Companies listed under Adroit Corporate include: Antariksh Industries, Anand Projects, IndiaNivesh, Marathon Nextgen Realty, Hind Rectifiers, Permanent Magnets, Flomic Global Logistics, Safari Industries India, Securekloud Technologies, Scan Steels, LKP Finance, Bajaj Steel Industries, Arihant Superstructures, and others.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this circular. It is a routine operational notice for scheduling RTA visits.

## Compliance Requirements

- No new compliance obligations for listed companies.
- Investors holding shares in companies serviced by the visiting RTAs should utilize the visit window to resolve pending transfer requests, name changes, or other folio-related issues.
- RTAs are expected to attend BSE premises as per the schedule to address investor grievances in person.

## Important Dates

- **Month:** March 2026
- **Circular Date:** March 4, 2026
- Specific visit dates within March 2026 are governed by the schedule communicated separately by BSE to the respective RTAs.

## Impact Assessment

This circular has no direct market or trading impact. It is an administrative and investor-service oriented notice. Retail investors with unresolved transfer grievances in companies managed by Adroit Corporate Services Pvt. Ltd. may benefit from the scheduled visits. No action is required from brokers, trading members, or listed companies as a result of this circular.
