## Summary

BSE has announced a mock trading session for the Equity Derivatives segment on Saturday, May 23, 2026. The session is intended for trading members to test their third-party or in-house trading platforms via ETI APIs, including testing of exceptional market conditions such as risk-reduction mode and trading halt scenarios. Trades from this session carry no financial obligations.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Saturday, May 23, 2026, for the Equity Derivatives segment
- Open to trading members using third-party empaneled vendor platforms or in-house systems via ETI APIs
- No new BOLT TWS release will accompany this session
- Trades will not create margin obligations, pay-in/pay-out obligations, or any rights and liabilities
- Members are requested to submit feedback by 01:00 PM on the day of the session

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a scheduled operational/testing activity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are encouraged to participate actively in the mock trading session
- Members should submit feedback on the session to BSE Tech Support or BSE Helpdesk by 01:00 PM on May 23, 2026
- Contact for TWS/IML users: BSE Tech Support — 022-22728053 / bse.tech@bseindia.com
- Contact for general queries: BSE Helpdesk — 022-45720400/600 & 022-69158500 / bsehelp@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **May 23, 2026 (Saturday):** Mock trading session for Equity Derivatives segment
- **01:00 PM, May 23, 2026:** Deadline for submitting session feedback

| Session | From | To |

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine system-testing exercise with no financial consequences for participants. It provides an opportunity for trading members to validate their platforms against live-like conditions including edge cases such as risk-reduction mode and trading halts. Participation is voluntary but encouraged to ensure system readiness.
