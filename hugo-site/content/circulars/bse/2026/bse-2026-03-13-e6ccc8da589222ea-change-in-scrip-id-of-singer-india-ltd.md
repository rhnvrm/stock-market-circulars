## Summary

BSE has notified trading members of a change in the Scrip ID of Singer India Ltd (Scrip Code: 505729) in the BOLT Plus trading system. The scrip ID will change from **SINGER** to **SINGERIND**, effective March 18, 2026.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code 505729 (Singer India Ltd) will have its BOLT Plus Scrip ID changed from `SINGER` to `SINGERIND`
- The change is effective from **March 18, 2026**
- This is an administrative/system-level change and does not affect the company's fundamentals
- Trading members must update their systems to reflect the new Scrip ID

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a purely administrative update to the scrip identifier used within BSE's BOLT Plus trading system.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are required to take note of the new Scrip ID (`SINGERIND`) for Singer India Ltd
- Members should update internal systems, order management platforms, and references accordingly before March 18, 2026
- For clarifications, members may contact:
  - Mr. Tushar Shetty: +91 22 2272 8634 | Listing.crd@bseindia.com
  - Ms. Asmita Chavan: +91 22 2272 8266 | Listing.crd@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **March 13, 2026**: Notice issued
- **March 18, 2026**: New Scrip ID `SINGERIND` becomes effective in BOLT Plus system

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine scrip ID rename in BSE's BOLT Plus system. Trading members using automated systems or algorithms referencing the old scrip ID `SINGER` must update their configurations prior to March 18, 2026 to avoid order entry issues. No impact on the company's equity, shareholders, or financial position.
