## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in Non-Convertible Debentures of MANBA FINANCE LIMITED (ISIN: INE939X07119, Code: 975425) effective February 13, 2026. The suspension is implemented due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Debenture: MANBA FINANCE LIMITED - 13.25% - 28-2-26 - PVT
- ISIN: INE939X07119
- BSE Code: 975425
- Record Date: February 13, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- No Dealings From: February 13, 2026
- Notice Reference: DR-820/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action notification requiring trading suspension in accordance with existing debt market regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from February 13, 2026
- Members must ensure systems are updated to prevent dealing in INE939X07119
- Compliance with the corporate action timeline for redemption and interest payment

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: February 10, 2026
- **Record Date**: February 13, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Date**: February 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: High - Complete trading suspension affects liquidity and market access for this security. Debenture holders will not be able to trade the security from the suspension date.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems and inform clients holding these debentures about the trading suspension. The debentures are approaching maturity (28-2-26) and will be redeemed, with final interest payment to holders on record as of February 13, 2026.

**Investor Impact**: Holders on record date will receive redemption proceeds and final interest payment. No further trading opportunity available after suspension begins.
