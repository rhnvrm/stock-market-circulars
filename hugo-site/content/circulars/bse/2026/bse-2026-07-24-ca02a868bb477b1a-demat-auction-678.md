## Summary

BSE Limited (through Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd.) has invited offers from trading members for the purchase of securities in the Demat Auction pertaining to Rolling Settlement No. DR-678/2026-27. Offers will be accepted on trading terminals on Friday, July 24, 2026, with auction matching, delivery statements, and final money reports scheduled the same day. The auction pay-in is scheduled for Monday, July 27, 2026.

## Key Points

- Auction offer entry window: 02:00 p.m. to 02:45 p.m. on July 24, 2026
- Auction matching: 02:45 p.m. to 03:00 p.m.
- Delivery statement and report of accepted auction offers: 03:00 p.m. to 03:30 p.m.
- Money statement and final reports: 03:35 p.m. to 05:30 p.m.
- Auction pay-in: Monday, July 27, 2026 at 08:00 a.m.
- Settlement will be routed through the Clearing Member of a Trading Member, per the Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment
- The prior restriction preventing trading members from offering securities in auction for a settlement in which they had short-delivered those securities has been removed
- No corrections permitted for punching errors in security code or rate; Exchange reserves right to accept or reject any offer without reason

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this notice reiterates the existing removal of the restriction on trading members offering securities in auction in settlements where they had short-delivered such securities, following implementation of the Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and Custodians must check their auction pay-in obligations for securities and execute securities pay-in within depository cut-off timings
- Trading members must exercise care while entering security codes and rates, as no corrections are permitted post-entry
- Members should refer to the cutoff rate on the Auction Offer Entry Screen and account for any Corporate Action notices (Ex-Rights/Ex-Bonus/Ex-Split etc.) affecting the relevant securities

## Important Dates

- July 24, 2026: Auction offer entry, matching, and reporting
- July 27, 2026: Auction pay-in (08:00 a.m.)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, recurring operational notice related to the settlement auction process for short-delivered securities under Rolling Settlement DR-678/2026-27. It primarily affects trading members and custodians involved in the settlement cycle and has no direct impact on specific listed stocks or broader market sentiment.
