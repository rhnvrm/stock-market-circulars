## Summary

BSE has issued a notice regarding the applicability of Long Term Additional Surveillance Measures (LT-ASM) on select securities. Effective June 17, 2026, 100% margins will be imposed on all open positions as on June 16, 2026 and new positions created thereafter. Additionally, securities moving to higher stages of the LT-ASM framework and changes in lower price bands will take effect from June 15, 2026. Some securities are also being transferred out of LT-ASM to their respective T/XT/MT/TS groups.

## Key Points

- 100% margins applicable from June 17, 2026 on all open positions as on June 16, 2026 and new positions created from June 17, 2026 onwards (Annexure I, Part A)
- Securities moving to higher stages of LT-ASM Framework effective June 15, 2026 (Annexure I, Part B)
- Lower Price Band applicable from June 15, 2026 for securities in higher LT-ASM stages
- Certain securities to continue in LT-ASM but transferred to T/XT/MT/TS Group effective June 17, 2026 (Annexure I, Part D)
- Securities moving out of LT-ASM Framework effective June 15, 2026 listed in Annexure II
- Consolidated list of all securities under the Framework provided in Annexure III
- Shortlisting is purely for market surveillance purposes and not an adverse action against the company

## Regulatory Changes

This notice references and operates under the revised LT-ASM framework established through multiple prior notices:
- Exchange notice no. 20180321-46 dated March 21, 2018 (original framework)
- Exchange notice no. 20181027-1 dated October 27, 2018
- Exchange notice no. 20201204-56 dated December 04, 2020
- Exchange notice no. 20240809-46 dated August 09, 2024
- Exchange notice no. 20240920-63 dated September 20, 2024 (latest revision)
- Exchange notice no. 20210604-41 dated June 04, 2021 (transfer provisions)

Price bands for securities exiting the framework will be reinstated to pre-shortlisting levels, unless the security remains under another surveillance measure.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must maintain 100% margins on positions in securities listed under Annexure I (Part A) from June 17, 2026
- Members should review the attached annexures to identify all affected securities
- LT-ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by exchanges
- Shortlisting is based on XBRL submissions by listed companies
- Members with clarifications may contact bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **June 15, 2026**: Effective date for securities moving to higher LT-ASM stages (Annexure I, Part B), lower price band applicability, and securities moving out of LT-ASM (Annexure II)
- **June 16, 2026**: Reference date for open positions subject to 100% margin requirement
- **June 17, 2026**: Effective date for 100% margin requirement on open and new positions; transfer of securities to T/XT/MT/TS groups

## Impact Assessment

The imposition of 100% margins significantly increases the cost of holding or creating positions in affected securities, reducing leverage and potentially forcing liquidation of existing positions that cannot meet the margin requirement. Market liquidity for these securities is likely to decline. Securities moving to higher LT-ASM stages face progressively stricter restrictions, while those exiting the framework will see restored price bands. The measure affects retail and institutional participants holding positions in the listed securities and is intended to curb speculative activity identified through BSE's market surveillance function.
