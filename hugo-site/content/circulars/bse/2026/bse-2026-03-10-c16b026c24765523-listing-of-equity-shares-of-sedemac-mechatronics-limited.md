## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that the equity shares of Sedemac Mechatronics Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange. The scrip has been assigned code 544723 with symbol SEDEMAC and ISIN INE00XB01019. The specific listing date and further security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Sedemac Mechatronics Limited equity shares are approved for listing on BSE
- Scrip Code: 544723
- Symbol: SEDEMAC
- ISIN: INE00XB01019
- Exact listing date to be announced via a separate notice
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine new listing admission notice under BSE's standard listing process.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should monitor BSE for the follow-up notice confirming the exact listing date and full security details before initiating any trades in SEDEMAC shares.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: March 10, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced via a separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This notice signals the imminent listing of Sedemac Mechatronics Limited on BSE's equity segment. Trading members and investors interested in the mechatronics sector should watch for the subsequent notice confirming the listing date. The listing will provide a public trading avenue for SEDEMAC shares (ISIN INE00XB01019).
