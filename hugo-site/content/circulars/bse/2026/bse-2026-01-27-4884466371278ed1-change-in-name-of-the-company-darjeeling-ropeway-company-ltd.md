## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for Darjeeling Ropeway Co Ltd. The company's name has been officially changed to Darjeeling Industriies Limited with effect from December 26, 2025. The certificate was issued by the Central Processing Centre, ROC and digitally signed on December 26, 2025.

## Key Points

- Original company name: DARJEELING ROPEWAY CO LTD
- New company name: DARJEELING INDUSTRIIES LIMITED
- Corporate Identification Number (CIN): L45202MH1936PLC294011
- Effective date of name change: December 26, 2025
- Certificate issued by: M. Yadubhushana Rao, Assistant/Deputy/Registrar of Companies, Central Processing Centre
- Company type: Company limited by shares
- Originally incorporated as: DARJEELING ROPEWAY CO LTD

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a corporate name change under the Companies Act.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display the old name (Darjeeling Ropeway Co Ltd) along with the new name (Darjeeling Industriies Limited) for a period of two years at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders
- All stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company and its directors on the MCA website www.mca.gov.in/MCA21

## Important Dates

- **December 26, 2025**: Effective date of name change and certificate issuance
- **December 26, 2027**: End of mandatory two-year period for displaying old name alongside new name

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is an administrative name change with no impact on business operations, shareholding structure, or financial position.

**Operational Impact**: Low. The company must update its corporate communications, letterheads, signage, and display both names for two years as required.

**Stakeholder Impact**: No impact on rights and liabilities of stakeholders. All existing contracts, agreements, and obligations remain valid under the new name.
