## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 1,77,00,000 new equity shares of Jolly Plastic Industries Ltd. (Scrip Code: 507968, ISIN: INE289M01016) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Monday, May 25, 2026. The shares were issued at par (Rs. 10/- each) to non-promoters via preferential allotment.

## Key Points

- 1,77,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at par to non-promoters on a preferential basis
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 6676401 to 24376400
- Date of allotment: April 3, 2026
- Issue price: Rs. 10/- per share
- ISIN: INE289M01016

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification for new securities admitted to trading on BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed that these shares are now eligible for trading. The entire allotment of 1,77,00,000 shares is subject to lock-in restrictions and cannot be traded until the lock-in expiry date.

## Important Dates

- Date of allotment: April 3, 2026
- Trading effective date: May 25, 2026
- Lock-in expiry: November 28, 2026 (for all 1,77,00,000 shares, dist. nos. 6676401 to 24376400)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a standard preferential allotment listing for a small-cap company. The entire new issuance is locked in until November 28, 2026, so no immediate selling pressure is expected. The new shares increase the total equity base of Jolly Plastic Industries Ltd. and are pari-passu with existing shares.
