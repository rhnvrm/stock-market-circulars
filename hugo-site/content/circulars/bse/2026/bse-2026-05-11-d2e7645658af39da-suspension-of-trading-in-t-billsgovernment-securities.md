## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members that three T-bills/Government Securities will mature on their respective redemption dates and trading in these instruments is suspended with effect from May 12, 2026. This is a routine action under DR-628/2026-2027.

## Key Points

- Three T-bills/Government Securities are maturing and trading is suspended effective May 12, 2026.
- Trading members are advised not to deal in these securities from the effective date.
- The notice is issued under DR-628/2026-2027.
- No attachments were included with this notice.

## Securities Affected

| Scrip Code | Particulars | ISIN |
|------------|-------------|------|
| 805143 | 182TB14526 | IN002025Y339 |
| 805066 | 364TB14526 | IN002025Z070 |
| 805178 | 91TB140526 | IN002025X455 |

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This notice enforces the standard market procedure of suspending trading in government securities upon maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must immediately cease dealing in scrip codes 805143, 805066, and 805178 from May 12, 2026 onwards.
- Members holding or transacting in these instruments should take note and adjust their positions accordingly.

## Important Dates

- **May 11, 2026**: Notice issued by BSE.
- **May 12, 2026**: Effective date of trading suspension for all three T-bills/Government Securities.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and active traders of these three specific debt instruments. As these are maturing T-bills, the suspension is expected and orderly. No broader market disruption is anticipated. Members with open positions or pending settlements in these securities should ensure compliance before May 12, 2026.
