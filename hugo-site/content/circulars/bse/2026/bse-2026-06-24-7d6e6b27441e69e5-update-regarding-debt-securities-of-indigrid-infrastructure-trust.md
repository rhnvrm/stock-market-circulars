## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that three debt securities of IndiGrid Infrastructure Trust will be available for trading with revised terms effective June 25, 2026, following a debt restructuring. This notice continues from Exchange Notice No. 20250327-48 (March 27, 2025) and Notice No. 20251226-15 (December 26, 2025).

## Key Points

- Three scrip codes with revised trading terms effective June 25, 2026
- Revision is on account of debt restructuring of IndiGrid Infrastructure Trust
- Revised terms are detailed in Annexure I of the notice
- Continuation of a process initiated in March 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The following securities will trade under revised terms per Annexure I:

| Scrip Code | ISIN |
|------------|------|
| 976557 | INE219X07470 |
| 976558 | INE219X07496 |
| 976972 | INE219X07579 |

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members are requested to note the revised trading terms for the above scrip codes and update their systems accordingly before June 25, 2026. Members may contact Ms. Pooja Trivedi at +91 22 2272 8844 or debt.listing@bseindia.com for further details.

## Important Dates

- **June 24, 2026**: Notice issued
- **June 25, 2026 (Thursday)**: Revised terms become effective for trading

## Impact Assessment

This notice directly affects holders and traders of the three specified IndiGrid Infrastructure Trust debt securities. The debt restructuring changes the trading terms of these instruments, which may affect pricing, yield expectations, and settlement conditions. Trading members dealing in these securities must update their systems to reflect the revised terms before the effective date. The impact is limited to participants in these specific debt instruments and does not affect broader equity markets.
