## Summary

BSE has provisionally empanelled Quick Algoplus Private Limited as an Algorithmic Trading solutions provider for trading members of the Exchange. This empanelment is effective immediately as of 21 May 2026.

## Key Points

- Quick Algoplus Private Limited has been granted provisional empanelment status by BSE.
- The vendor is based in Indore, Madhya Pradesh (TT Nagar Pipliya Kumar, DDU Nagar, Indore – 452010).
- Contact number for the vendor: +91 8357000107.
- The empanelment is effective immediately.
- The updated list of all empanelled Algo Providers is available on the BSE website under the derivatives/market-information section.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine vendor empanelment update to the existing list of approved Algorithmic Trading solution providers on BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are advised to take note of the new empanelled vendor. Members intending to use algorithmic trading solutions may now engage Quick Algoplus Private Limited as their service provider. No mandatory action is required from existing trading members.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** 21 May 2026 (immediate effect)

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This notice expands the pool of approved Algo providers available to BSE trading members by one additional vendor. It does not affect trading rules, margins, surveillance, or listing requirements. Trading members seeking algorithmic trading solutions now have an additional provisionally empanelled vendor option.
