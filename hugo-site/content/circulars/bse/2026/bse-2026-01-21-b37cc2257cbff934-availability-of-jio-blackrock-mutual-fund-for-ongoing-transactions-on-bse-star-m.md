## Summary

BSE has announced that JioBlackrock Low Duration Fund and JioBlackrock Short Duration Fund will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective January 22, 2026. Both schemes will support SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities.

## Key Points

- Two JioBlackrock debt fund schemes reopening for transactions
- Direct Plan - Growth options available for both schemes
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities will be enabled
- Applies to BSE StAR MF Platform
- Reference to previous Notice No. 20260107-18

## Scheme Details

| Sr. No. | Scheme Name | Scheme Category | ISIN |
|---------|-------------|-----------------|------|
| 1 | JIOBLACKROCK LOW DURATION FUND - DIRECT PLAN - GROWTH | DEBT | INF22M001150 |
| 2 | JIOBLACKROCK SHORT DURATION FUND - DIRECT PLAN - GROWTH | DEBT | INF22M001143 |

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding the resumption of transactions for specific mutual fund schemes.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements. Members can facilitate transactions in these schemes through the BSE StAR MF Platform from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: January 22, 2026 - Schemes reopen for ongoing subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP transactions
- **Notice Date**: January 21, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine operational notice affecting only two specific debt mutual fund schemes.

**Investor Impact**: Positive for investors interested in JioBlackrock debt funds, as it provides access to low duration and short duration investment options with systematic transaction facilities.

**Operational Impact**: BSE StAR MF Platform members can now process transactions for these schemes, expanding the available mutual fund offerings on the platform.
