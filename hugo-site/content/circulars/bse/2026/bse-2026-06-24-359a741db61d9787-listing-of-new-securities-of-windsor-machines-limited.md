## Summary

BSE has listed 27,80,000 new equity shares of Windsor Machines Limited (Scrip Code: 522029, ISIN: INE052A01021) effective Thursday, June 25, 2026. The shares were issued at a premium of Rs. 189.85/- (issue price Rs. 191.85/- per share of face value Rs. 2/-) to Promoters on a preferential basis, pursuant to the conversion of warrants. All newly listed shares are subject to a lock-in period through December 31, 2027.

## Key Points

- 27,80,000 equity shares of Rs. 2/- face value listed and permitted to trade effective June 25, 2026
- Issued to Promoters on a preferential basis via conversion of warrants
- Issue price: Rs. 191.85/- per share (face value Rs. 2/- + premium Rs. 189.85/-)
- Distribution numbers: 88522202 to 91302201
- Date of allotment: April 13, 2026
- ISIN: INE052A01021
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Entire 27,80,000 shares are under lock-in until December 31, 2027

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notice pursuant to a completed preferential allotment and warrant conversion under applicable SEBI regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed that the new securities are now admitted to trading. The lock-in restriction on all 27,80,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 88522202 to 91302201) must be observed until December 31, 2027; these shares cannot be sold or transferred by the Promoters during this period.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** April 13, 2026
- **Listing / Trading Effective Date:** June 25, 2026 (Thursday)
- **Lock-in Expiry Date:** December 31, 2027

## Impact Assessment

The addition of 27,80,000 shares increases Windsor Machines Limited's equity capital, resulting in modest dilution for existing shareholders. However, since the entire new tranche is locked in until December 31, 2027, there is no immediate increase in free-float or selling pressure. Market impact is limited in the near term; the unlock event in late 2027 may be a watch point for investors holding the stock.
