## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260311-46 informing trading members of the new ISIN number assigned to Hindusthan Urban Infrastructure Ltd (Scrip Code: 539984) following a sub-division of its equity shares. This is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20260219-7 dated February 19, 2026. The new ISIN replaces the old one for all trades executed on or after the Ex-Date of March 13, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: Hindusthan Urban Infrastructure Ltd (Scrip Code: 539984)
- New ISIN: INE799B01025
- Reason: Sub-division of equity shares from face value Rs.10/- per share to Rs.2/- per share
- The new ISIN is effective for trades on and from Ex-Date: March 13, 2026
- Reference DR number: DR-839/2025-2026
- This notice is a continuation of earlier Notice No. 20260219-7 dated February 19, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced. This circular implements a corporate action (share sub-division) by assigning a new ISIN to the sub-divided equity shares, as required under standard BSE/SEBI procedures for corporate actions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the new ISIN number INE799B01025 for all trades in Hindusthan Urban Infrastructure Ltd equity shares on and from March 13, 2026.
- Members should update their systems and records to reflect the new ISIN prior to the Ex-Date.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: March 11, 2026
- Ex-Date (effective date for new ISIN): March 13, 2026
- Earlier related notice: February 19, 2026 (Notice No. 20260219-7)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational update affecting trading members and investors holding shares of Hindusthan Urban Infrastructure Ltd. The share sub-division from Rs.10 to Rs.2 increases the number of shares outstanding by a factor of 5, potentially improving liquidity and retail accessibility. Trading members must ensure systems are updated with the new ISIN (INE799B01025) before March 13, 2026 to avoid settlement issues. The impact is limited to this single scrip and is a standard corporate action process.
