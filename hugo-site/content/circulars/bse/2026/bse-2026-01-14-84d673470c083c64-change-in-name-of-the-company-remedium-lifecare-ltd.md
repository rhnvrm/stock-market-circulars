## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for Remedium Lifecare Limited. The company's name has been officially changed to Supha Pharmachem Limited with effect from December 15, 2025. The certificate was issued by the Central Processing Centre, ROC on December 15, 2025.

## Key Points

- Previous company name: REMEDIUM LIFECARE LIMITED
- New company name: SUPHA PHARMACHEM LIMITED
- Corporate Identification Number (CIN): L24100MH1988PLC343805
- Company type: Company limited by shares
- Original incorporation name: ROXY ENGINEERS PRIVATE LIMITED
- Certificate issued by: Ibson Shah, Central Processing Centre
- Registered office location: Office No.9, K Raheja Prime, Marol Industrial Estate, Behind Ravi Vihar Hotel, Sagbaug Road, Marol, Andheri East, Mumbai-400059, Maharashtra

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard name change procedure under the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014, Rule 29.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display the old name (REMEDIUM LIFECARE LIMITED) alongside the new name (SUPHA PHARMACHEM LIMITED) for a period of two years at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders
- All legal obligations and contracts continue under the new name

## Important Dates

- **December 15, 2025**: Effective date of name change
- **December 15, 2027**: End of mandatory dual-name display period (two years from effective date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is an administrative change with no impact on the company's operations, financial position, or business activities.

**Stakeholder Impact**: No impact on stakeholder rights and liabilities. All existing contracts, agreements, and legal obligations remain valid under the new name.

**Operational Impact**: The company must update all statutory documents, letterheads, signage, and communications to reflect the new name while maintaining dual-name display for two years as required by law.
