## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading for debt securities of Triumph Composites Private Limited with effect from Wednesday, July 02, 2026. This notice is a continuation of Exchange Notice 20260525-39 dated May 25, 2026, and the suspension is being imposed on account of debt restructuring.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code **977750** (ISIN: **INE2I4608019**) will be suspended from trading effective July 02, 2026.
- The suspension is due to ongoing debt restructuring of Triumph Composites Private Limited.
- This notice continues from Exchange Notice 20260525-39 dated May 25, 2026.
- Trading Members can contact Mr. Akash Shinde (+91 22 2272 8597) or Hardik Bhuta (Assistant Vice President) at debt.listing@bseindia.com for further details.

## Regulatory Changes

The debt security bearing Scrip Code 977750 and ISIN INE2I4608019 will be removed from active trading on BSE's Debt segment as of July 02, 2026 pending resolution of the debt restructuring process.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must note the suspension effective July 02, 2026 and refrain from executing trades in Scrip Code 977750.
- Any open positions or pending settlements in this scrip should be managed prior to the suspension date.

## Important Dates

- **May 25, 2026**: Original Exchange Notice 20260525-39 issued.
- **June 29, 2026**: Current notice issued.
- **July 02, 2026 (Wednesday)**: Effective date of trading suspension for Scrip Code 977750 / ISIN INE2I4608019.

## Impact Assessment

Holders of Triumph Composites Private Limited debt securities (INE2I4608019) will be unable to exit positions on BSE after July 02, 2026. The suspension due to debt restructuring signals financial stress at the issuer level, and investors should assess their exposure. Liquidity for this instrument will effectively cease on the exchange from the effective date.
