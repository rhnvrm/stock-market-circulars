## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that Supra Pacific Financial Services Limited (Scrip Code: 540168) has fixed 12 March 2026 as the record date for its rights issue. Trading in the equity shares must be conducted on an ex-rights basis from 12 March 2026 under the Rolling Settlement Segment.

## Key Points

- Record date for the rights issue is **12 March 2026**
- Rights ratio: **32 new equity shares for every 49 equity shares held**
- Issue price: **Rs 10 per share (face value) + Rs 13 premium = Rs 23 per share**
- Settlement reference: **DR-838/2025-2026**
- Shares are in dematerialised form under the Rolling Settlement Segment
- Trading members must transact in the scrip on an ex-rights basis from 12 March 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a routine corporate action notification under BSE's listing compliance framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all transactions in Supra Pacific Financial Services Limited (540168) equity shares are executed on an **ex-rights basis** effective 12 March 2026.
- Members should update their systems and inform clients accordingly prior to the record date.

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Notice Date | 10 March 2026 |
| Record Date | 12 March 2026 |
| Ex-Rights Effective Date | 12 March 2026 |
| Settlement No. | DR-838/2025-2026 |

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a moderate, company-specific impact. Existing shareholders of Supra Pacific Financial Services Limited as of the record date (12 March 2026) will be entitled to subscribe to 32 new equity shares for every 49 shares held at Rs 23 per share (Rs 10 face value + Rs 13 premium). The ex-rights adjustment will affect the share price from 12 March 2026. Trading members need to ensure proper client communication and order handling around the ex-rights date.
