## Summary

BSE Limited (through Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd.) has issued a notice inviting offers from trading members of the Exchange for the purchase of securities in Auction 677, corresponding to Rolling Settlement No. DR-677/2026-27. The auction process, including offer entry, matching, and reporting, is scheduled for Thursday, July 23, 2026, with pay-in due on Friday, July 24, 2026.

## Key Points

- Notice No. 20260723-3 relates to Demat Auction 677 under the Settlement/RMS category, General segment, issued by BSECL.
- Auction offer entry window: 02:00 p.m. to 02:45 p.m.
- Auction matching window: 02:45 p.m. to 03:00 p.m.
- Delivery statement and report of accepted auction offers: 03:00 p.m. to 03:30 p.m.
- Money statement and final reports: 03:35 p.m. to 05:30 p.m.
- Pay-in of the auction will be held on Friday, July 24, 2026 at 08:00 a.m.
- Following implementation of the Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment, settlement will be done through the Clearing Member of a Trading Member.
- The prior restriction preventing trading members from offering securities in auction in a settlement where they have short-delivered such securities has been removed.
- Trading members must exercise care while punching securities code and rate, as punching errors cannot be corrected; the Exchange reserves the right to accept or reject any offer without assigning reasons.
- Members should refer to the cutoff rate on the Auction Offer Entry Screen and to notices on Corporate Actions (Ex-Rights/Ex-Bonus/Ex-Split, etc.) for the relevant securities.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced; the notice reiterates the operational effect of the previously implemented Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment, under which settlement flows through the Clearing Member of a Trading Member, and confirms removal of the restriction on trading members offering securities in auction for settlements in which they had short-delivered those securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and custodians must check their auction pay-in obligations for securities and execute securities pay-in within depository cut-off timings.
- Trading members must ensure accuracy when entering securities codes and rates during the auction offer entry window, as no corrections are permitted after punching.
- Members should review relevant corporate action notices (rights, bonus, split, etc.) before submitting auction offers.

## Important Dates

- Auction offer entry, matching, and reporting: Thursday, July 23, 2026 (02:00 p.m. – 05:30 p.m.)
- Auction pay-in: Friday, July 24, 2026, 08:00 a.m.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, procedural notice governing the mechanics of a scheduled demat auction for short-delivered securities under Rolling Settlement DR-677/2026-27. It primarily affects trading members and custodians who must adhere to the specified timelines for offer entry and pay-in; it has no direct or material impact on listed companies, retail investors, or broader market pricing.
