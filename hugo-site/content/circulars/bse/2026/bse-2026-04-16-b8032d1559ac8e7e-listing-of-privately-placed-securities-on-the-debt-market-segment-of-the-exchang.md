## Summary

BSE has notified trading members of the fresh listing of five Commercial Papers (CPs) issued on a private placement basis on the BSE Debt Market Segment, effective April 16, 2026. The CPs are from GIC Housing Finance Ltd., Truhome Finance Limited, DSP Finance Private Limited, Dhampur Sugar Mills Ltd., and Tata Steel Limited. All securities are available for trading only in dematerialised form in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof, with a tick size of 1 paise.

## Key Points

- Five new Commercial Papers admitted to trading on the BSE Debt Segment effective April 16, 2026.
- All CPs were issued on a private placement basis with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each.
- Trading is permitted only in dematerialised form in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples.
- Tick size for all listed securities is 1 paise.
- Allotment date for all five CPs is April 15, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification under BSE's Debt Capital Markets framework for privately placed Commercial Papers.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form.
- All transactions must be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof.
- Members requiring clarifications may contact the BSE Debt Department at 22728352 / 8597 / 8995 / 5753 / 8915.
