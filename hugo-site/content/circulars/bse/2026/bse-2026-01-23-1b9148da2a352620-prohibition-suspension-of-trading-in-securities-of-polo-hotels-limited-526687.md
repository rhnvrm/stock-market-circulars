## Summary

BSE has announced the prohibition and suspension of trading in securities of Polo Hotels Limited (Scrip Code: 526687) effective from Tuesday, January 27, 2026. This interim measure follows the company's failure to respond to show cause notices and its confirmed non-existence at the registered office address during site visits conducted by the Exchange.

## Key Points

- Trading in Polo Hotels Limited (526687) is prohibited/suspended as an interim measure
- Company has not responded to show cause notices issued by BSE
- Site visits by the Exchange confirmed the company is non-existent at its registered office address
- Suspension applies to all securities of Polo Hotels Limited in the equity segment
- Trading members and investors are advised to take note of this suspension

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular implements existing enforcement provisions for non-compliant companies that cannot be located at their registered addresses.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in Polo Hotels Limited securities from January 27, 2026
- All market participants must comply with the trading prohibition
- Investors holding positions in this security should be aware of the suspension

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 23, 2026
- **Effective Date of Suspension**: Tuesday, January 27, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: High - Complete trading halt prevents any buy/sell transactions in the security. Existing shareholders will be unable to exit positions until the suspension is lifted.

**Investor Impact**: High - Investors holding Polo Hotels Limited securities face liquidity risk as they cannot trade the stock. This raises serious concerns about the company's operational status and corporate governance.

**Regulatory Significance**: This action demonstrates BSE's enforcement of listing compliance requirements and protection of market integrity when companies fail to maintain proper communication channels or physical presence at registered addresses.
