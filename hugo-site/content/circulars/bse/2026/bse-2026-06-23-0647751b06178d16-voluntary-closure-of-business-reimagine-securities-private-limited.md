## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Reimagine Securities Private Limited (Member No. 6941) will voluntarily cease business operations in the Cash and Equity Derivative segments effective Wednesday, June 24, 2026.

## Key Points

- Reimagine Securities Private Limited (Member No. 6941) is voluntarily closing its business.
- Cessation of trading activity applies to both Cash and Equity Derivative segments.
- Effective date: Wednesday, June 24, 2026.
- Issued by the BSE Membership Compliance Department.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a membership cessation notice under existing exchange bye-laws governing voluntary closure of trading member businesses.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and market participants are informed to update their records accordingly.
- Clients of Reimagine Securities Private Limited should contact their broker or the exchange for assistance in transferring accounts or positions.
- For clarifications, contact BSE Membership Operations at membership.ops@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **June 24, 2026**: Effective date of cessation of business for Reimagine Securities Private Limited in Cash and Equity Derivative segments.

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited to clients and counterparties of Reimagine Securities Private Limited (Member No. 6941). No broader market or systemic impact is expected. Affected clients should make arrangements to transfer open positions or accounts to another registered trading member before the effective date.
