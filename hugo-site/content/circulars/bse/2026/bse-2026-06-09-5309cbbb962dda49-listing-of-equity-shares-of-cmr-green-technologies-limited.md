## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members that the equity shares of CMR Green Technologies Limited are to be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange. The exact date of listing and further details of the securities will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- CMR Green Technologies Limited equity shares will be listed on BSE in due course
- Scrip Code: 544777
- Trading Symbol: CMRGREEN
- ISIN: INE00WV01027
- The specific listing date will be announced via a separate notice

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing admission notice issued by BSE's DCS-Listing department.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are hereby informed of the upcoming listing. No immediate action is required; members should await the follow-up notice specifying the exact listing date and additional securities details.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: June 9, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced via separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This notice signals the imminent commencement of trading in CMR Green Technologies Limited on BSE. Market participants and investors should monitor for the follow-up notice confirming the listing date. The impact is moderate and limited to parties with interest in this specific scrip.
