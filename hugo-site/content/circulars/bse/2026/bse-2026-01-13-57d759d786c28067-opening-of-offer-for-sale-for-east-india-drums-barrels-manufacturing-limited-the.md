## Summary

BSE has announced the opening of Offer for Sale (OFS) for East India Drums & Barrels Manufacturing Limited. The OFS allows existing shareholders to sell their shares through the exchange platform, providing liquidity and enabling public participation in the company's equity.

## Key Points

- Offer for Sale opened for East India Drums & Barrels Manufacturing Limited
- OFS mechanism enables existing shareholders to divest their holdings through the exchange
- Transaction will be conducted on BSE's trading platform
- Provides opportunity for retail and institutional investors to participate

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard OFS announcement following existing SEBI guidelines for Offer for Sale mechanism.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members to facilitate OFS orders from eligible investors
- Existing shareholders eligible to participate in the OFS must follow the prescribed bidding process
- All participants must comply with standard OFS trading regulations and settlement procedures
- Bidding to be conducted as per BSE's OFS guidelines

## Important Dates

- OFS Opening Date: January 13, 2026 (as indicated by circular date)
- Specific bidding dates and closing date to be confirmed in the detailed OFS notice

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - The OFS provides liquidity for existing shareholders and investment opportunity for new investors in East India Drums & Barrels Manufacturing Limited.

**Trading Impact:** Limited to the specific scrip. Expected increased trading activity in the company's shares during the OFS period.

**Investor Impact:** Opportunity for retail and institutional investors to acquire shares at the OFS price. Existing shareholders can use this mechanism for partial divestment.
