## Summary

BSE has issued a circular announcing the sub-division (stock split) and bonus issue of equity shares of Silver Touch Technologies Ltd (Scrip Code: 543525). The circular provides details on the applicable record date, split/bonus ratio, revised face value, and adjustments to be made for derivatives and other positions.

## Key Points

- Silver Touch Technologies Ltd (Scrip Code: 543525) has announced a sub-division of equity shares along with a bonus issue.
- The record date for the corporate action has been fixed as notified by the company.
- Post sub-division, the face value of equity shares will be revised accordingly.
- Bonus shares will be issued to eligible shareholders as of the record date.
- BSE will make necessary adjustments to open positions in derivatives contracts (if any) on account of the corporate action.
- Ex-date trading adjustments will apply from the relevant date.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. The circular is an operational notice for market participants regarding an upcoming corporate action and associated exchange-level adjustments.
