## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260224-7 informing trading members that the equity shares of Yashhtej Industries (India) Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the BSE SME segment. The specific listing date and further details of the securities will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Yashhtej Industries (India) Limited is being listed on the BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code assigned: 544708
- Trading Symbol: YASHHTEJ
- ISIN: INE1O6P01016
- Exact listing date to be announced via a separate notice
- Notice issued by Nitinkumar Pujari, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard new listing admission notice under BSE's SME segment framework.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the forthcoming listing and await a separate notice from BSE confirming the exact date of listing and full details of the securities before commencing trading.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: February 24, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced via a separate BSE notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative notice for a new SME segment listing. Market impact is limited at this stage as the actual listing date has not been specified. Trading members should monitor for the follow-up notice confirming the listing date and security details. The stock will trade under the symbol YASHHTEJ (Scrip Code: 544708) once listed.
