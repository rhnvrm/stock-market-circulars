## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures of ARKA FINCAP LIMITED effective January 21, 2026. The suspension is implemented because the company has fixed the record date for payment of interest and redemption of debentures. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the record date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspended for ARKA FINCAP LIMITED debentures (ISIN: INE03W107165, Code: 974525)
- Suspension effective from January 21, 2026
- Reason: Record date fixed for interest payment and redemption
- Trading members advised not to deal in these debentures
- Notice issued under DR-803/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action requiring temporary trading suspension.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease dealing in ARKA FINCAP LIMITED debentures (INE03W107165) from January 21, 2026
- Trading members should note the suspension and update their trading systems accordingly
- Members must comply with the no-dealing restriction for the specified security

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** January 16, 2026
- **Record Date:** January 21, 2026
- **No Dealings From:** January 21, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and traders of ARKA FINCAP LIMITED debentures. The suspension is temporary and related to a standard corporate action (interest payment and redemption). Trading members dealing in debt securities need to update their systems to prevent trades in this specific debenture. Debenture holders whose names appear on the record date will be eligible for interest payment and redemption as per the company's terms.
