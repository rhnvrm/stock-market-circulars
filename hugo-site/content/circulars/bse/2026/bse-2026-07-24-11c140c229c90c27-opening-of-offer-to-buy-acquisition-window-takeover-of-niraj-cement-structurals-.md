## Summary

BSE has issued a notice announcing the opening of an open offer (Acquisition Window/Takeover mechanism) made by Mr. Gulshankumar Vijaykumar Chopra (the Acquirer) to the public shareholders of Niraj Cement Structurals Limited (the Target Company). The Acquirer proposes to purchase up to 1,55,20,529 fully paid-up equity shares of face value Rs. 10 each, representing 26.00% of the Target Company's fully paid-up equity and voting share capital, at a price of Rs. 29 per share payable in cash. The offer is made pursuant to Regulation 3(1) of the SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 2011.

## Key Points

- Acquirer: Mr. Gulshankumar Vijaykumar Chopra
- Target Company: Niraj Cement Structurals Limited
- Offer price: Rs. 29/- per fully paid-up equity share (cash)
- Shares sought: up to 1,55,20,529 equity shares (face value Rs. 10 each)
- Stake sought: 26.00% of the Target Company's fully paid-up equity and voting share capital
- BSE is the Designated Stock Exchange for this offer
- Offer executed through the Acquisition Window (Tender-Offer) mechanism per SEBI circulars CIR/CFD/POLICYCELL/1/2015 (13 Apr 2015) and CFD/DCR2/CIR/P/2016/131 (09 Dec 2016), as revised by BSE notices 20170210-16, 20190424-35, 20200528-32, 20201102-43 and 20210825-62
- Letter of Offer attached to the notice for market participants' reference

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; the notice implements existing SEBI (SAST) Regulations, 2011 and previously issued SEBI/BSE circulars governing the Acquisition Window mechanism for tender-offers under takeovers, buybacks and delisting.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members and Custodians must process the offer in accordance with the Revised Guidelines for the Acquisition Window mechanism as set out in the referenced SEBI and BSE circulars.
- Market participants should refer to the attached Letter of Offer and BSE's website for full details of the offer before facilitating tenders.

## Important Dates

- Notice date: 24 July 2026
- Offer opens: Friday, 31 July 2026
- Offer closes: Thursday, 13 August 2026

## Impact Assessment

The offer affects public shareholders of Niraj Cement Structurals Limited, who may tender up to 26.00% of the company's equity at Rs. 29 per share during the offer window. Trading Members and Custodians handling orders in this scrip need to be aware of the Acquisition Window process and timelines to facilitate client participation. Broader market impact is limited, as this is a company-specific takeover offer processed through the exchange's standard tender-offer mechanism.
