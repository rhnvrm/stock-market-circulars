## Summary

BSE has updated the Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LT-ASM) framework, imposing 100% margins on specified securities effective June 03, 2026. Additionally, securities are being moved to higher LT-ASM stages, lower price bands are being applied, and certain securities are being transferred to T/XT/MT/TS groups or exited from the LT-ASM framework, all effective from June 01–03, 2026.

## Key Points

- 100% margins will be applicable from June 03, 2026 on all open positions as on June 02, 2026, and on all new positions created from June 03, 2026 onwards (Annexure I, Part A)
- Securities are being moved to their respective higher stages under the LT-ASM framework effective June 01, 2026 (Annexure I, Part B)
- Lower Price Band will be applicable for affected securities effective June 01, 2026
- Certain securities will continue in the LT-ASM framework but will be transferred to T/XT/MT/TS Group effective June 03, 2026 (Annexure I, Part D)
- Securities moving out of the LT-ASM framework effective June 01, 2026 are listed in Annexure II
- A consolidated list of all securities currently under the LT-ASM framework is provided in Annexure III
- Shortlisting is based on XBRL submissions by listed companies and should not be construed as an adverse action against the company

## Regulatory Changes

This notice is issued under the revised LT-ASM framework, referencing prior notices including the original framework notice (20180321-46, March 21, 2018) and subsequent revisions up to September 2024 (20240920-63). The current update enforces higher-stage transitions and group transfers as part of the periodic review mechanism built into the framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must ensure 100% margin availability for positions in LT-ASM securities listed in Annexure I (Part A) before June 03, 2026
- Brokers and members must update their systems to apply the lower price bands for affected securities from June 01, 2026
- Members should communicate the group transfer (to T/XT/MT/TS) for securities in Annexure I (Part D) to their clients ahead of June 03, 2026
- For clarifications, members may write to bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **June 01, 2026**: Higher-stage LT-ASM transitions take effect; lower price bands apply; securities exit the LT-ASM framework (Annexure II)
- **June 02, 2026**: Reference date for open positions subject to 100% margin requirement
- **June 03, 2026**: 100% margins become applicable on open positions and new positions; securities transferred to T/XT/MT/TS Group

## Impact Assessment

The 100% margin requirement significantly increases the capital needed to hold or create new positions in the affected securities, likely reducing liquidity and trading volumes in these scrips. The transfer of certain securities to the T/XT/MT/TS group imposes trade-for-trade settlement, eliminating netting benefits and increasing settlement risk awareness for traders. The lower price band further restricts downside trading flexibility. Overall, this is a high-impact update for market participants trading in the listed securities, requiring immediate action to meet margin and operational compliance requirements.
