## Summary

BSE has announced the addition of two newly listed companies to their respective IPO indices. BHARAT COKING COAL LIMITED (ticker: 544678) will be added to the BSE IPO index effective January 21, 2026, following its listing on January 19, 2026. DEFRAIL TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED (ticker: 544677) will be added to the BSE SME IPO index effective January 20, 2026, following its SME platform listing on January 19, 2026.

## Key Points

- BHARAT COKING COAL LIMITED (544678) listed on BSE main board on January 19, 2026
- Addition to BSE IPO index effective at open on January 21, 2026
- DEFRAIL TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED (544677) listed on BSE SME platform on January 19, 2026
- Addition to BSE SME IPO index effective at open on January 20, 2026
- Both actions included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- References prior notices 20260116-39 and 20260116-41

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard index reconstitution following new IPO listings.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index additions are administrative actions managed by BSE Index Services.

## Important Dates

- **January 19, 2026**: Listing date for both BHARAT COKING COAL LIMITED and DEFRAIL TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED
- **January 20, 2026**: DEFRAIL TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED added to BSE SME IPO index (at open)
- **January 21, 2026**: BHARAT COKING COAL LIMITED added to BSE IPO index (at open)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. These are routine index additions for newly listed IPO companies to their respective benchmark indices. The additions follow standard BSE procedures for incorporating new listings into IPO tracking indices. Index funds and ETFs tracking these indices may need to adjust holdings accordingly. No impact on trading parameters or market operations.
