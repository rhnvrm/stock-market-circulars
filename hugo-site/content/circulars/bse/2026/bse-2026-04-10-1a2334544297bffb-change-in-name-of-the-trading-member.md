## Summary

BSE has notified all trading members that the registered name of trading member Gogia Capital Services Ltd. (Member No. 3006) will officially change to **Gogia Capital Growth Limited** effective Monday, 13th April 2026.

## Key Points

- Trading member Gogia Capital Services Ltd. (Member No. 3006) is being renamed to Gogia Capital Growth Limited
- The name change is effective from Monday, 13th April 2026
- The member number (3006) remains unchanged
- No attachments were included with this notice
- For clarifications, trading members may email membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative update to reflect the revised legal name of an existing BSE trading member.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members transacting with or referencing Gogia Capital Services Ltd. should update their records to reflect the new name — Gogia Capital Growth Limited — from 13th April 2026 onwards. The member number (3006) remains the same and can be used for identification.

## Important Dates

- **10th April 2026**: Notice issued by BSE
- **13th April 2026 (Monday)**: Effective date of name change from Gogia Capital Services Ltd. to Gogia Capital Growth Limited

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal market impact. It is a routine administrative name change affecting a single trading member. Other trading members and market participants dealing with this entity should update internal records and documentation to reflect the new name. No trading restrictions or operational disruptions are expected.
