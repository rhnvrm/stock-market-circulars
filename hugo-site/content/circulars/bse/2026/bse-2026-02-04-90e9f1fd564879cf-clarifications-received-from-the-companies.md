## Summary

BSE sought clarifications from Pace Digitek Limited (Scrip: 544550), Sahara Maritime Limited (Scrip: 544056), and Welspun Living Limited regarding significant increases in trading volume and price movements of their shares. All three companies confirmed compliance with SEBI LODR Regulations 2015, stated there is no undisclosed price-sensitive information or impending announcements, and attributed the movements to market-driven conditions beyond their control.

## Key Points

- **Pace Digitek Limited**: Clarified volume increase is market-driven with no undisclosed information requiring disclosure under Regulation 30
- **Sahara Maritime Limited**: Confirmed no undisclosed price-sensitive information or impending announcement influencing share price
- **Welspun Living Limited**: Response included but content truncated in source document
- All companies affirmed ongoing compliance with SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015
- Companies confirmed they will continue to make timely disclosures as required

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This circular documents routine surveillance responses.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies confirmed compliance with Regulation 30 of SEBI LODR Regulations 2015
- Ongoing obligation to disclose material events and price-sensitive information promptly
- Companies committed to continue making all required disclosures

## Important Dates

- **February 4, 2026**: Date of clarifications from Pace Digitek and Sahara Maritime
- **February 3, 2026**: BSE request to Sahara Maritime (Reference: L/SURV/ONL/PV/AJ/2025-2026/3320)
- **February 4, 2026**: BSE request to Pace Digitek (Reference: L/SURV/ONL/PV/APJ/2025-2026/900)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low. These are standard surveillance responses with no new material information disclosed. The clarifications confirm that recent price and volume movements are market-driven rather than based on company-specific undisclosed developments.

**Investor Impact**: Neutral. Investors are informed that there is no pending announcement or undisclosed information affecting these stocks. The movements reflect normal market dynamics.

**Operational Impact**: None. Companies continue normal operations with no changes indicated.
