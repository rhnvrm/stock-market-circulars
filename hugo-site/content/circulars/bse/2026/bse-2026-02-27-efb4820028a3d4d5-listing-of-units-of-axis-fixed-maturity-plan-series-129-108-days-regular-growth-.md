## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of units of Axis Fixed Maturity Plan - Series 129 (108 Days) Regular Growth, a close-ended debt scheme under Axis Mutual Fund, effective Monday, March 2, 2026. The units are admitted to dealings on BSE under the 'F' Group with Scrip Code 544714.

## Key Points

- Scheme is a close-ended debt scheme with relatively low interest rate risk and moderate credit risk
- Total units listed: 74,299,461
- Scrip Code: 544714; ISIN: INF846KA1432
- Scrip ID on BOLT System: AXISRGG129
- Face value and issue price: Rs. 10.00 per unit
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Trading is permitted only in dematerialized form as per SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000
- Registrar and Transfer Agent: KFin Technologies Limited, Mumbai

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. Units must be traded in dematerialized form per existing SEBI guidelines issued on February 16, 2000.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all trades in this scrip are conducted in dematerialized form only
- Trades must be in unit market lot (1 unit minimum)
- Members may contact Mr. Parag Jain (Manager, Listing Operations) at BSE (022-2272 8685 / mf.listing@bseindia.com) or Mr. Darshan Kapadia (Compliance Officer, Axis AMC) for clarifications

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: February 24, 2026
- Listing effective date: March 2, 2026
- Notice date: February 27, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine mutual fund listing with limited broader market impact. It affects trading members and investors interested in this specific close-ended FMP series. The scheme has a short tenure of 108 days and is categorized under the 'F' Group, indicating a fixed maturity product. Investors should note the demat-only trading requirement.
