## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in non-convertible debentures of DISHMAN CARBOGEN AMCIS LTD (ISIN: INE385W07034, Code: 975834) effective January 29, 2026. The suspension is due to the record date fixed for redemption on exercise of put option and payment of interest on the debentures.

## Key Points

- Company: DISHMAN CARBOGEN AMCIS LTD
- Debenture Code: DCAL-10.04%-15-7-26-PVT
- ISIN: INE385W07034
- BSE Code: 975834
- Record Date: January 29, 2026
- Trading Suspension Date: January 29, 2026
- Circular Reference: DR-808/2025-2026
- Trading Members advised not to deal in these debentures

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard operational suspension triggered by corporate action events.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must not deal in the specified debentures of DISHMAN CARBOGEN AMCIS LTD from January 29, 2026
- Trading Members must take note of the suspension and inform their clients accordingly
- No transactions should be executed in the suspended debenture series

## Important Dates

- **January 28, 2026**: Circular issued
- **January 29, 2026**: Record date for put option redemption and interest payment
- **January 29, 2026**: Trading suspension effective from this date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - Trading suspension affects liquidity for holders of this specific debenture series but does not impact the equity shares or other debt instruments of the company.

**Investor Impact**: High for debenture holders - Investors holding these debentures cannot trade them on the exchange from the suspension date. Those exercising put option or entitled to interest will be determined based on the January 29, 2026 record date.

**Operational Impact**: Low - Standard corporate action procedure for debt securities with limited broader market implications.
