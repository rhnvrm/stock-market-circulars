## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Orissa Bengal Carrier Limited (Scrip Code: 541206) has informed the Exchange of a change in its company name to OBCL Limited. The SCRIP ID and Abbreviated Name on the BOLT Plus System will be updated accordingly, effective July 17, 2026.

## Key Points

- Existing name: Orissa Bengal Carrier Ltd
- New name: OBCL Limited
- Scrip Code: 541206 (unchanged)
- Scrip ID for BOLT Plus System: OBCL (no change)
- Abbreviated name on BOLT Plus System: OBCL (no change)
- Change effective from July 17, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

This is an administrative update to reflect the company's new legal name as informed to the Exchange. The Certificate of Incorporation reflecting the name change was submitted as an attachment (COI_Orissa_bengal_carrier_ltd).

## Compliance Requirements

No action is required from investors or trading members beyond noting the updated company name in records and systems. Trading members should update their internal systems to reflect the new company name from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- Effective date of name change: July 17, 2026
- Notice date: July 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact, purely administrative change with no effect on trading, scrip ID, or shareholder rights. The scrip ID and abbreviated name on the BOLT Plus System remain unchanged, minimizing any operational disruption for market participants.
