## Summary

BSE Daily Bulletin No. 200/2025-2026 dated January 20, 2026, announces record dates and book closure dates for various corporate actions across multiple securities. Key highlights include DCM Shriram Limited's second interim dividend of Rs. 3.60 per share (180%), Dr. Lalchandani Labs Limited's rights issue, and multiple interest payment and redemption schedules for Andhra Pradesh Capital Region Development Authority and EarlySalary Services Private Limited debt instruments.

## Key Points

- DCM Shriram Limited (Code: 523367) announces record date of January 24, 2026 for second interim dividend of Rs. 3.60 per share (180%)
- Dr. Lalchandani Labs Limited (Code: 541299) sets record date of January 27, 2026 for rights issue of equity shares
- Andhra Pradesh Capital Region Development Authority has multiple record dates on February 1, 2026 for partial NCD redemption and interest payments across three debt instruments (Codes: 958178, 958179, 958180)
- EarlySalary Services Private Limited has extensive schedule of interest payments and NCD redemptions throughout 2026 and into 2027 across multiple debt series (Codes: 976147, 976935, 976976)
- All listed securities will have trading flag set to 1 on respective record dates

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine informational bulletin.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must note the record dates for entitlement to dividends, interest payments, and participation in corporate actions
- Trading members should ensure proper marking of ex-dates in their systems
- Investors must hold securities as of the record date to be eligible for announced benefits

## Important Dates

**Equity Securities:**
- January 24, 2026: Record date for DCM Shriram Limited dividend
- January 27, 2026: Record date for Dr. Lalchandani Labs Limited rights issue

**Debt Securities (Andhra Pradesh CRDA):**
- February 1, 2026: Record date for interest payments and partial redemption

**Debt Securities (EarlySalary Services):**
- January 21, 2026 to January 21, 2027: Multiple interest payment and redemption dates scheduled across various NCD series
- Key dates include: January 23, 2026, February 13, 2026, February 20, 2026, March 15, 2026, March 21, 2026, March 23, 2026, April 21-22, 2026, May 23, 2026, June 20-22, 2026, August 21, 2026, December 21, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low to Medium - Routine bulletin affecting specific securities holders

**Investor Impact:** Medium - Important for shareholders and debenture holders to track entitlement dates for dividends, interest, and corporate actions

**Operational Impact:** Low - Standard corporate action processing by depositories and trading members

- DCM Shriram shareholders must hold shares until January 24, 2026 to receive 180% interim dividend
