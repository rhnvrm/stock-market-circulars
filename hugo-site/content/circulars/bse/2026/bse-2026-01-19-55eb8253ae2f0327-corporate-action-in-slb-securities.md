## Summary

BSE notifies shut periods for Securities Lending and Borrowing (SLB) in six securities undergoing dividend corporate actions. The shut periods range from January 19-20, 2026 to January 28-29, 2026, during which SLB transactions will be suspended for the respective securities.

## Key Points

- Six companies have declared dividends with corresponding SLB shut periods
- Zensar Technologies: Ex-date and record date January 29, 2026, shut period January 28-29, 2026
- Wipro: Ex-date and record date January 27, 2026, shut period January 23-27, 2026
- NLC India Limited: Ex-date and record date January 20, 2026, shut period January 19-20, 2026
- Bank of Maharashtra: Ex-date and record date January 20, 2026, shut period January 19-20, 2026
- Central Bank of India: Ex-date and record date January 23, 2026, shut period January 22-23, 2026
- Angel One Limited: Ex-date and record date January 21, 2026, shut period January 20-21, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants engaged in Securities Lending and Borrowing must note the shut periods for these securities
- No new SLB positions can be created during the specified shut periods
- Existing positions should be managed considering the shut period restrictions

## Important Dates

- **January 19-20, 2026**: SLB shut period for NLC India Limited and Bank of Maharashtra
- **January 20-21, 2026**: SLB shut period for Angel One Limited
- **January 22-23, 2026**: SLB shut period for Central Bank of India
- **January 23-27, 2026**: SLB shut period for Wipro
- **January 28-29, 2026**: SLB shut period for Zensar Technologies

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This circular affects only participants in the Securities Lending and Borrowing segment. The shut periods are standard procedure during corporate actions and are limited to 1-5 days per security. Regular equity trading remains unaffected. Market participants have adequate advance notice to adjust their SLB positions if needed.
