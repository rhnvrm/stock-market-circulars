## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document of Dhanwel Hybrid Seeds Limited, filed on December 31, 2025, for public review and comments. The document contains all disclosures specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and will be available on the stock exchange website for at least 21 days. Stakeholders are invited to submit comments to BSE within this period.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Dhanwel Hybrid Seeds Limited on December 31, 2025
- Document contains all disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website for public review for minimum 21 days
- Public comments invited within 21 days from notice date
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Document accessible at www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard procedural notice as per existing ICDR Regulations requirements for SME IPOs.

## Compliance Requirements

- Stakeholders and interested parties can review the Draft Offer Document
- Comments, if any, must be submitted to BSE at equity.ipo@bseindia.com within 21 days from January 01, 2026
- Company must keep the document available on exchange website for at least 21 days

## Important Dates

- **Draft Filing Date**: December 31, 2025
- **Notice Date**: January 01, 2026
- **Comment Deadline**: January 22, 2026 (21 days from notice date)
- **Document Availability**: Minimum 21 days from January 01, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine disclosure for an SME IPO process. Impacts only potential investors and stakeholders interested in Dhanwel Hybrid Seeds Limited.

**Investor Impact**: Informational - Provides transparency and opportunity for public feedback on the proposed SME listing. Investors interested in this IPO can review detailed disclosures and raise concerns if any.
