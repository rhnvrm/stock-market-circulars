## Summary

BSE has published the Demat Auction notice for **Settlement No. 828** dated **February 26, 2026**. The auction lists 89 securities (identified by scrip code and name) along with their respective quantities available for auction. This is a standard market operations circular issued by BSE under its demat auction settlement process.

## Key Points

- Settlement No. 828 dated 26/02/2026
- Total of **89 securities** listed for demat auction
- Quantities range from 1 unit (e.g., 3M India, Bajaj Steel, Vodafone Idea) to 37,000 units (Viram Suvarna) and 28,800 units (SBL Infratech)
- Covers securities across diverse sectors including IT, FMCG, metals, energy, pharma, infrastructure, and financial services
- Large-quantity securities include: Viram Suvarna (37,000), SBL Infratech (28,800), EMPOWER India (14,136), Himatsingka (6,043), and Dilip Buildcon (5,000)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a routine settlement/auction notice under BSE's existing demat auction framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Brokers/Members**: Must participate in the demat auction as required for settlement of outstanding deliveries
- **Investors**: Holders of the listed securities should be aware of potential auction-related price impact
- Members must ensure timely delivery of securities listed in the auction to avoid short delivery penalties

## Important Dates

- **Auction Date**: February 26, 2026
- **Settlement Number**: 828

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine demat auction notice with medium operational impact for market participants:

- **Brokers/Trading Members**: Must comply with auction settlement obligations for all 89 listed securities
- **High-quantity securities** (Viram Suvarna at 37,000 shares, SBL Infratech at 28,800 shares, EMPOWER India at 14,136 shares) may see localized price volatility during the auction process
- **Marquee stocks** included: Reliance Industries (125 shares), ITC (1,600), Tata Consultancy Services (5), Hindustan Unilever (600), Hindustan Zinc (500), Bank of Maharashtra (1,350), and Life Insurance Corporation (100)
- Overall market impact is low; this is standard settlement infrastructure activity
- Affected parties are primarily those with delivery obligations in the listed scrips for Settlement No. 828
