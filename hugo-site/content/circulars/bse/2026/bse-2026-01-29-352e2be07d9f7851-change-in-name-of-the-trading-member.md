## Summary

BSE has issued a notice regarding the name change of trading member JAGRUTI SECURITIES LTD. (Member No. 294) to "JAGRUTI SECURITIES PRIVATE LIMITED". The change will be effective from Friday, January 30, 2026. This is a routine administrative update with no impact on trading operations or market participants.

## Key Points

- Trading member JAGRUTI SECURITIES LTD. (Member No. 294) will undergo a name change
- New name: JAGRUTI SECURITIES PRIVATE LIMITED
- Member number remains unchanged: 294
- Change reflects conversion from public limited to private limited company structure
- No operational changes or trading disruptions expected

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative name change notification as per BSE membership records.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should update their internal records to reflect the new name
- No action required from other market participants
- For clarifications, members can email: membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** January 29, 2026
- **Effective Date:** January 30, 2026 (Friday)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** None. This is a routine administrative change.

**Operational Impact:** Minimal. The trading member's operations, member number, and trading rights remain unchanged. Only the legal name of the entity is being updated in BSE records.

**Stakeholder Impact:** No impact on clients or counterparties. The member number (294) remains the same for identification purposes.
