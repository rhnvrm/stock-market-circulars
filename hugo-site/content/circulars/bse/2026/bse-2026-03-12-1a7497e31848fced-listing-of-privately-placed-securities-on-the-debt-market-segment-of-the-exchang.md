## Summary

BSE has listed three privately placed debt securities issued by Aditya Birla Capital Ltd (ABCL) on its Debt Segment, effective March 12, 2026. All securities were allotted on March 10, 2026, and are admitted to trading in dematerialised form only. The instruments carry AAA credit ratings from both CRISIL and ICRA.

## Key Points

- Three NCDs of Aditya Birla Capital Ltd admitted to BSE Debt Segment via further listing
- All securities trade exclusively in dematerialised form with a tick size of 1 paise
- Market lot is 1 for all three instruments
- All three ISINs allotted on March 10, 2026, listed effective March 12, 2026
- Credit ratings: CRISIL AAA/STABLE and/or ICRA AAA/STABLE for all instruments
- Put/Call options as per respective Disclosure Documents

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification under BSE's private placement debt listing framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under the respective ISIN numbers
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE Debt Department at 22728352 / 8597 / 8995 / 5753 / 8915
- Placement Memorandum available at: https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx
