## Summary

BSE has announced the settlement calendar for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for NETLINK SOLUTIONS (INDIA) LIMITED. The takeover bidding window will operate from January 28, 2026 to February 10, 2026, with settlement number 2026009. Obligation download and settlement dates are scheduled for February 16 and 17, 2026 respectively.

## Key Points

- Takeover bidding period: January 28, 2026 to February 10, 2026
- Settlement Number: 2026009
- Obligation Download Date: February 16, 2026
- Settlement Date: February 17, 2026
- Demat shares must be delivered through Early Pay-in mechanism only
- Market Type designation: "(Takeover)" to be used in depository systems
- Members must refer to Notice No.20200528-32 dated May 28, 2020 for revised acquisition guidelines

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular implements existing takeover regulations as per the Revised Guidelines of Mechanism for acquisition of shares through Stock Exchange.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members/Custodians must deliver demat shares through Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism available in both depositories
- Market Type "(Takeover)" and settlement number 2026009 must be specified in depository system
- Early pay-in securities will be automatically earmarked/blocked in client accounts with requisite market type and settlement number
- No EPN file upload required by Members and Custodians
- Members must ensure early pay-in effect is reflected in BSE/ICCL systems before prescribed timelines for bid acceptance/confirmation
- Early pay-in process must be completed sufficiently in advance before BSE OTB Bidding window closure
- Members should refer to MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for client-wise stamp duty charges

## Important Dates

- **January 28, 2026**: Bid Start Date
- **February 10, 2026**: Bid End Date
- **February 16, 2026**: Obligation Download Date
- **February 17, 2026**: Settlement Date

## Impact Assessment

This takeover offer impacts shareholders of Netlink Solutions (India) Limited who wish to participate in the acquisition window. Trading members and custodians handling transactions for this stock must ensure compliance with early pay-in requirements and prescribed timelines. The 14-day bidding window provides adequate time for shareholders to participate. Non-compliance with early pay-in procedures or missing prescribed timelines could result in bid rejection.
