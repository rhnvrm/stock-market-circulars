## Summary

BSE has notified members that JioBlackRock Large Cap Fund will be reopened for ongoing transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform with effect from April 16, 2026. This follows Notice No. 20260323-52 dated March 23, 2026. The fund will be available for subscriptions, redemptions, and systematic facility transactions (SIP, STP, SWP).

## Key Points

- JioBlackRock Large Cap Fund is being reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions on BSE StAR MF Platform.
- Systematic Investment Plan (SIP), Systematic Transfer Plan (STP), and Systematic Withdrawal Plan (SWP) facilities will also be available.
- The reopening is pursuant to an earlier notice (No. 20260323-52 dated March 23, 2026), indicating the fund was previously closed for ongoing transactions.
- Only the Direct Plan – Growth option is listed (ISIN: INF22M001176) under the Equity scheme category.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notification enabling transaction processing for an existing scheme on the BSE StAR MF platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members (stock brokers and mutual fund distributors) operating on BSE StAR MF should update their systems to enable transactions in JioBlackRock Large Cap Fund – Direct Plan – Growth (ISIN: INF22M001176) from April 16, 2026.
- No specific compliance action is required beyond enabling the scheme for client transactions.

## Important Dates

- **April 15, 2026**: Notice issued.
- **April 16, 2026**: JioBlackRock Large Cap Fund available for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP on BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Impact Assessment

This notice has moderate operational impact for mutual fund distributors and investors using the BSE StAR MF platform. Investors previously unable to transact in JioBlackRock Large Cap Fund through this platform will gain access starting April 16, 2026. The impact is limited to participants in this specific fund and platform; broader market impact is minimal.
