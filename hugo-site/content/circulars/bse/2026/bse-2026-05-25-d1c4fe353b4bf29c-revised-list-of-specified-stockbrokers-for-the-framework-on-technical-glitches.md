## Summary

BSE has revised the list of 'Specified Stockbrokers' under SEBI's framework for addressing technical glitches in stock brokers' electronic trading systems (SEBI circular dated January 09, 2026). The top 20 stockbrokers by IBT/STWT client count with more than 10,000 registered clients (excluding closed accounts) as of March 31 of the previous financial year qualify as Specified Stockbrokers. Five new members have been added and four have been excluded based on the revised criteria.

## Key Points

- Criteria: Top 20 stockbrokers by IBT/STWT clients with more than 10,000 registered clients (excluding closed accounts) as of March 31 of the preceding financial year
- 5 new stockbrokers added to the Specified Stockbroker list
- 4 stockbrokers removed from the list for no longer meeting the criteria
- Newly added members must comply with enhanced obligations within two months (by July 25, 2026)
- Excluded members may voluntarily continue adhering to enhanced obligations
- The revised consolidated list is provided in Annexure-A

## Regulatory Changes

This notice implements the revised framework from SEBI circular SEBI/HO/38/44/12(1)2026-MIRSD-TPD1 dated January 09, 2026, and BSE notice 20260109-70 dated January 09, 2026, on 'Review of framework to address Technical Glitch in Stock Brokers Electronic Trading Systems'.

## Compliance Requirements

**Newly Added Specified Stockbrokers (Table-1):**
1. Ashika Stock Services Limited
2. MarketWolf Securities Pvt Ltd
3. Moneywise Finvest Limited
4. PhonePe Wealth Broking Private Limited
5. Share India Securities Limited

These members must comply with the enhanced obligations and responsibilities outlined in the SEBI circular within two months from the date of this notice.

**Excluded Stockbrokers (Table-2):**
1. Indiabulls Securities Limited
2. Profitmart Securities Pvt. Ltd.
3. Ventura Securities Ltd.
4. Zebu Share and Wealth Managements Pvt. Ltd.

Excluded members may continue to adhere to enhanced obligations on a voluntary basis.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** May 25, 2026
- **Compliance Deadline for Newly Added Members:** July 25, 2026 (two months from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

The circular directly affects nine stockbrokers — five newly designated members face mandatory enhanced compliance obligations within two months, including upgraded systems and reporting requirements related to technical glitch prevention and management. The four excluded members no longer carry mandatory obligations but may voluntarily maintain the higher standards. Broader market impact is limited; this is an administrative revision of a compliance framework applicable to a specific segment of large-volume brokers.
