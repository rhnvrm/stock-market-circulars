## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that effective Tuesday, July 21, 2026, the equity shares of Millworks Technologies Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange under the 'MT' Group of Securities on the SME segment. Per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012, the scrip will trade in the Trade-for-Trade segment for the first 10 trading days.

## Key Points

- Company: Millworks Technologies Limited, registered in Bangalore, Karnataka
- Number of securities: 17,614,755 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each, fully paid up
- Distinctive number range: 1 to 17,614,755
- Scrip ID/Abbreviated Name on BOLT System: MILLWORKS
- Scrip Code: 544826
- ISIN: INE1SRC01010
- Market Lot: 400 shares
- Issue Price: Rs. 331/- per share (Face Value Rs. 10/- + premium Rs. 321/-)
- Trading permitted only in dematerialised form per SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000
- Market Maker: Pace Stock Broking Services Private Limited
- Registrar to the issue: Purva Sharegistry (India) Pvt. Ltd.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an operational listing notice implementing SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012 regarding Trade-for-Trade segment applicability for newly listed SME scrips.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure trades in this scrip are executed only in dematerialised form and in minimum market lot of 400 equity shares.
- The company has executed necessary corporate action for lock-in period marking in depository records as per Annexure I.
- Shareholding pattern is detailed in Annexure II.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment in public issue: July 17, 2026
- Special Pre-open Session (SPOS): Thursday, July 16, 2026
- Listing and commencement of dealings: Tuesday, July 21, 2026
- Trade-for-Trade segment applicable for 10 trading days from listing date

## Impact Assessment

This listing notice affects trading members and investors dealing in Millworks Technologies Limited shares. The Trade-for-Trade restriction for 10 trading days limits intraday trading flexibility and requires actual delivery, which is a standard safeguard for newly listed SME scrips to curb speculative trading. Broader market impact is minimal given the small SME issue size.
