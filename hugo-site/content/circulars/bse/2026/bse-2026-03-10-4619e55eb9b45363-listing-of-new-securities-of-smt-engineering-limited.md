## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 1,550,000 new equity shares of SMT Engineering Limited (Scrip Code: 538563) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Wednesday, March 11, 2026. The shares were issued on a preferential basis to non-promoters at a premium.

## Key Points

- 1,550,000 equity shares of Rs. 10.00/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 215.00/- (issue price Rs. 225.00/-)
- Shares issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 16520001 to 18070000
- ISIN: INE673E01018

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification pursuant to preferential allotment of equity shares.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to note the listing effective March 11, 2026
- Lock-in applies to all 1,550,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 16520001 to 18070000) until September 20, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** February 10, 2026
- **Trading Commencement:** March 11, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry:** September 20, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. The listing adds 1,550,000 shares to the tradeable float of SMT Engineering Limited, though all newly listed shares are subject to lock-in until September 20, 2026, meaning no immediate increase in free float. Existing shareholders are unaffected as new shares rank pari-passu.
