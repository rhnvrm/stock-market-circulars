## Summary

BSE has announced the schedule for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for NETLINK SOLUTIONS INDIA LIMITED (Security Symbol: NETLINK, ISIN: INE040F01033, Scrip Code: 509040). The offer period runs from Wednesday, January 28, 2026 to Tuesday, February 10, 2026. The acquirer is offering Rs. 201.00 per share for up to 6,57,599 equity shares with a face value of Rs. 10.00 and market lot of 1.

## Key Points

- Offer Size: Up to 6,57,599 equity shares
- Offer Price: Rs. 201.00 per share
- Face Value: Rs. 10.00
- Market Lot: 1 share
- Opening Date: Wednesday, January 28, 2026
- Closing Date: Tuesday, February 10, 2026
- Bid Offering Period: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM (January 28 to February 10, 2026)
- Trading members can bid for investors having shares in demat form only
- UCC of investors must be registered with correct PAN number and category

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard takeover offer process following existing BSE OTB (Offer to Buy) framework as per Exchange Notices no. 20150930-1 dated September 30, 2015 and 20170203-29 dated February 3, 2017.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members and Custodian Members must ensure investor UCC is registered and updated with correct PAN Number and Category in the Exchange UCC database
- Trading Members can only bid for investors having shares in demat form
- Members must access iBBS platform via Internet (https://ibbs.bseindia.com/) or Leased Line (https://ibbsll.bseindia.com/)
- RTRMS platform access required via Internet (https://rtrms.bseindia.com/) or Leased Line (https://rtrmsll.bseindia.com/)
- Extranet access for files via Internet (https://member.bseindia.com/) or Leased Line (https://memberll.bseindia.com/)
- End of Issue files on Extranet will only be generated if securities are accepted

## Important Dates

- **Offer Opening Date**: Wednesday, January 28, 2026 at 9:15 AM
- **Bid Offering Period**: January 28, 2026 to February 10, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Give-up / Take-up Period**: January 28, 2026 to February 9, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Give-up (Last Day)**: February 10, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Take-up (Last Day)**: February 10, 2026 (9:15 AM to 4:00 PM - extended by 30 minutes)
- **Offer Closing Date**: Tuesday, February 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a company-specific takeover offer affecting only NETLINK SOLUTIONS INDIA LIMITED. The offer size is relatively small at 6.57 lakh shares.

**Operational Impact**: Medium for Trading Members and Custodians who need to facilitate bids for their clients holding NETLINK shares. They must ensure proper UCC registration, demat holdings verification, and access to iBBS/RTRMS platforms.

**Investor Categories Eligible**: Foreign Institutional Investors (FII), Financial Institutions (FI), Mutual Funds (MF), Insurance Companies (IC), Other QIBs (OTH), Non-Institutional Buyers (NOH), Companies (CO), and Individuals (IND).

**Technical Requirements**: Members need active login credentials for iBBS, RTRMS, and Extranet systems. Support contacts provided for user creation and password resets through Trading Operations (022-22728005, user.trdops@bseindia.com).
