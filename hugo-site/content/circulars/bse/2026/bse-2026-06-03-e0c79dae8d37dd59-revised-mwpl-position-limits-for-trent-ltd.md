## Summary

ICCL (Indian Clearing Corporation Limited) has revised the Market Wide Position Limits (MWPL) for stock derivatives contracts on Trent Ltd. (BSE Scrip Code: 500251), effective June 04, 2026. This revision is issued in pursuance of notice No. 20260321-1 dated March 21, 2026, and partially modifies circular no. 20231229-46 dated December 29, 2023.

## Key Points

- MWPL for Trent Ltd. revised to **50,385,730 shares**
- Mutual Fund scheme-wise limit set at **5,038,500 shares**
- Trading Member (TM) Proprietary limit set at **10,077,000 shares**
- Client / NRI / TM (Proprietary + Client) / FPI (Category I) / MF limit set at **15,115,650 shares**
- Applicable to all Members and Participants trading stock derivatives on BSE
- Issued by Chief Risk Officer, ICCL on June 03, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

The revised MWPL supersedes the earlier limits for Trent Ltd. as specified under circular no. 20231229-46 dated December 29, 2023, in partial modification thereof. The update reflects changes in the total outstanding shares or floating stock of Trent Ltd., which determine MWPL calculations under SEBI norms.

## Compliance Requirements

- All Stock Brokers, Foreign Portfolio Investors (FPIs) — Category I & II, and Mutual Funds (MFs) must ensure their open positions in Trent Ltd. derivatives do not exceed the newly revised limits.
- Trading Members must monitor proprietary and client positions against the updated thresholds.
- Breaches of MWPL may attract regulatory action including forced position closure.

## Important Dates

- **June 03, 2026** — Notice issued by ICCL
- **June 04, 2026** — Revised MWPL limits become effective

## Impact Assessment

The revised MWPL directly affects participants holding or building positions in Trent Ltd. futures and options contracts. Traders, arbitrageurs, and institutional investors (FPIs and MFs) with significant exposure to Trent Ltd. derivatives must review and potentially rebalance their positions before June 04, 2026. The new limits may expand or restrict capacity depending on the direction of revision relative to prior limits.
