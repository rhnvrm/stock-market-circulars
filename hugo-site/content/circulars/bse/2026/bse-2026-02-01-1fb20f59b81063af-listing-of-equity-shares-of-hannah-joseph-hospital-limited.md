## Summary

Hannah Joseph Hospital Limited has filed its shareholding pattern under Regulation 31(1)(a) as of January 30, 2026. The company has 628 total shareholders with 22,698,363 equity shares. Promoter and Promoter Group holds 68.83% (15,624,400 shares held by 6 shareholders), while Public holds 31.17% (7,073,963 shares held by 622 shareholders). The company has declared no partly paid-up shares, convertible securities, warrants, ESOPs, depository receipts, locked-in shares, pledged shares, or shares under non-disposal undertaking.

## Key Points

- Total equity shares: 22,698,363
- Promoter & Promoter Group shareholding: 68.83% (15,624,400 shares)
- Number of promoter shareholders: 6
- Public shareholding: 31.17% (7,073,963 shares)
- Number of public shareholders: 622
- Dematerialized shares: 18,404,363 (81.08% of total shares)
- Filing date: January 30, 2026
- No partly paid-up shares, convertible securities, warrants, or ESOPs outstanding
- No shares in locked-in, pledged, or under non-disposal undertaking

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this filing.

## Compliance Requirements

This is a standard shareholding pattern disclosure filed under Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015. The company has submitted the required declarations regarding various categories of securities and encumbrances.

## Important Dates

- Filing Date: January 30, 2026
- Report Period: As of January 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine disclosure with no material impact on market operations or trading. The shareholding pattern shows a stable promoter holding at 68.83% with no encumbrances, pledges, or lock-ins reported. The majority of shares (81.08%) are held in dematerialized form, indicating good liquidity potential. No significant changes or concerns are highlighted in this filing.
