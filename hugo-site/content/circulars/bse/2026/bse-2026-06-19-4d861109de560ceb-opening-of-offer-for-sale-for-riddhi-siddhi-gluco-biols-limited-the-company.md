## Summary

Vital Connections LLP, a promoter group entity of Riddhi Siddhi Gluco Biols Limited (RSGBL), is conducting an Offer for Sale (OFS) through BSE's designated OFS window. The promoter proposes to sell up to 8,23,422 equity shares of face value Rs. 10 each, representing 11.55% of the total issued and paid-up equity share capital, at a floor price of Rs. 730 per share. The offer is being made to achieve minimum public shareholding requirements.

## Key Points

- Seller: Vital Connections LLP (promoter group shareholder of RSGBL)
- Offer size: Up to 8,23,422 equity shares (11.55% of total paid-up capital)
- Face value: Rs. 10 per share
- Floor price: Rs. 730 per share
- T Day (non-Retail Investors only): June 22, 2026
- T+1 Day (Retail Investors + non-Retail carryforward): June 23, 2026
- Purpose: To achieve minimum public shareholding in the Company
- Shares held in dematerialized form

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The OFS is being conducted in accordance with existing SEBI and BSE OFS guidelines, including:
- SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD/DOPI/CIR/P/2018/159 dated December 28, 2018 (Review of OFS through Stock Exchange Mechanism)
- SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD/MRD-PoD-3/P/CIR/2023/10 dated January 10, 2023 (Comprehensive Framework on OFS)
- SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD/MRD-PoD-3/P/CIR/2024/82 dated June 14, 2024 (Framework for OFS to Employees)
- BSE notices: 20190118-43, 20200701-27, 20230127-14, 20240701-19

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members and Custodians must refer to the revised OFS bidding guidelines referenced in prior BSE notices.
- Market participants should note that BSE will disseminate offer details on the BSE website (www.bseindia.com).
- Bids from non-Retail Investors must be placed on T Day (June 22, 2026); unallotted bids may be carried forward to T+1 Day.
- Retail Investors may participate on T+1 Day (June 23, 2026).

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: June 19, 2026
- T Day (non-Retail Investors): June 22, 2026
- T+1 Day (Retail Investors & carryforward non-Retail): June 23, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This OFS is limited in scope to Riddhi Siddhi Gluco Biols Limited and its existing shareholders. The sale of 11.55% promoter stake is aimed at meeting SEBI's minimum public shareholding norms. At the floor price of Rs. 730 per share, the maximum offer size is approximately Rs. 60.1 crore. Market participants and retail investors interested in RSGBL should monitor BSE's OFS window on the respective dates. No broader market or systemic impact is expected.
