## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document (DRHP) of Zebion Infotech Limited, filed on March 07, 2026, on the SME website. The document is available for public review for a minimum of 21 days in compliance with Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations. Public stakeholders are invited to submit comments via email.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document of Zebion Infotech Limited filed on March 07, 2026
- Document is hosted on the BSE SME website for at least 21 days for public review
- Disclosures comply with Schedule VI of the ICDR (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations
- Public comments are invited and must be submitted within 21 days from the date of this notice (March 09, 2026)
- Comments to be sent to: equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice is issued in accordance with existing ICDR Regulations, specifically Schedule VI, which mandates public availability of draft offer documents for SME IPOs.

## Compliance Requirements

- Investors and public stakeholders wishing to comment on the Draft Offer Document must submit their comments to equity.ipo@bseindia.com within 21 days from March 09, 2026 (deadline: approximately March 30, 2026)
- The Draft Offer Document is accessible on the BSE SME website at: https://www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx

## Important Dates

- **Document Filing Date:** March 07, 2026
- **Notice Date:** March 09, 2026
- **Public Comment Deadline:** March 30, 2026 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal broad market impact. It is a procedural notice relevant primarily to prospective investors, analysts, and stakeholders interested in the upcoming SME IPO of Zebion Infotech Limited. The public comment period allows for transparency and regulatory scrutiny before the IPO proceeds. No immediate trading or operational impact on existing market participants.
