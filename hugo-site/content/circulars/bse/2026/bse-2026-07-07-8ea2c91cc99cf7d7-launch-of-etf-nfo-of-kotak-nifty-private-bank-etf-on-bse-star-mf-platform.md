## Summary

BSE has notified members that Kotak Mahindra Mutual Fund will launch the Kotak Nifty Private Bank ETF as a New Fund Offer (NFO) on the BSE StAR MF Platform from July 08, 2026, to July 15, 2026 (extended till midnight for lumpsum purchases). The notice outlines the schemes on offer, tentative allotment date, and payment/settlement instructions members must follow while processing transactions under this NFO.

## Key Points

- Two schemes are being offered: Kotak Nifty Private Bank ETF and Kotak Nifty Private Bank ETF - Direct Plan, both under the Equity category.
- Tentative allotment date for both schemes is July 20, 2026.
- NFO subscription window: July 08, 2026 to July 15, 2026, with the window extended till midnight on July 15, 2026 only for lumpsum purchases on the BSE StAR MF Platform.
- Transactions for which clients do not initiate funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day will be cancelled and reported to RTA/AMC with an 'N' flag.
- Transactions with initiated payment remain pending until 3:00 PM on the last day of funds pay-out to the AMC, or T+5 day, whichever is earlier.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is an operational/product notice regarding a new ETF launch on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds are available in the ICCL account by 6:00 PM on July 17, 2026, for allotment eligibility.
- Cheque and OTM payments are accepted during the NFO period, subject to clear funds reaching the ICCL account by the same deadline (6:00 PM, July 17, 2026).
- Switch transactions are accepted during the NFO period, provided funds under the NFO scheme are received prior to the allotment date; switch-out funds received on or after the allotment date will not be considered for allotment.
- Members must monitor client fund initiation timelines to avoid transaction cancellations.

## Important Dates

- NFO Period: July 08, 2026 – July 15, 2026 (extended till midnight July 15, 2026 for lumpsum purchases)
- Clear funds deadline in ICCL account: 6:00 PM, July 17, 2026
- Tentative Allotment Date: July 20, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This notice has limited market-wide impact and primarily concerns operational compliance for BSE StAR MF Platform members handling this specific NFO. It does not affect listed equity trading, pricing, or broader market operations, but timely adherence to fund pay-in deadlines is necessary to ensure client allotments are processed without cancellation.
