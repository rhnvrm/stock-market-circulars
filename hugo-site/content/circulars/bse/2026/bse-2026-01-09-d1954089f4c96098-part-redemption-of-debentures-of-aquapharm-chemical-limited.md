## Summary

AQUAPHARM CHEMICAL LIMITED has fixed January 13, 2026 as the Record Date for payment of interest and part redemption of its Non-Convertible Debentures (ISIN: INE0SYQ07015, Code: 975354). Trading in the debentures will commence with reduced face value from the effective date.

## Key Points

- Company: AQUAPHARM CHEMICAL LIMITED
- ISIN: INE0SYQ07015
- BSE Code: 975354 (ACL-8.90%-29-1-29-PVT)
- Record Date: January 13, 2026
- Purpose: Part Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- Reduced Face Value: Rs. 70,000 per Debenture
- Effective Date: January 13, 2026
- Settlement Number: DR-797/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must note that trading in AQUAPHARM CHEMICAL LIMITED debentures will be conducted with reduced face value of Rs. 70,000 per debenture effective from January 13, 2026
- All trading and settlement systems must reflect the reduced face value from the effective date
- Members should update their records and inform clients holding these debentures

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 09, 2026
- **Record Date**: January 13, 2026
- **Effective Date of Reduced Face Value**: January 13, 2026
- **Settlement**: DR-797/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

This corporate action has moderate impact on debenture holders and trading members:

- **Debenture Holders**: Will receive partial redemption proceeds and interest payment for securities held as of Record Date
- **Trading Impact**: Post January 13, 2026, all trades will be executed at the reduced face value of Rs. 70,000 per debenture
- **Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update their systems and client records to reflect the reduced face value
- **Market Segment**: Affects debt market segment participants dealing with AQUAPHARM CHEMICAL LIMITED debentures
