## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260407-16 informing trading members that 240 bonus equity shares of Rs. 2/- each of ICICI Bank Limited (Scrip Code: 532174, ISIN: INE090A01021), which were previously kept in abeyance, are now listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Wednesday, April 08, 2026.

## Key Points

- 240 bonus equity shares of ICICI Bank Limited (Scrip Code: 532174) are now listed on BSE
- Shares have face value of Rs. 2/- each
- ISIN: INE090A01021
- These shares were previously kept in abeyance (withheld from distribution pending resolution of disputes or other issues)
- Trading permitted from April 08, 2026
- Shares distributed across 8 distinctive number ranges totalling 240 shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine administrative listing notice.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for trading members beyond standard procedures for trading newly listed securities.

- **Effective Trading Date:** April 08, 2026 (Wednesday)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The 240 shares represent an extremely small quantity relative to ICICI Bank's total outstanding equity. This is a routine release of bonus shares previously held in abeyance, likely due to resolution of shareholder disputes or documentation issues. No material effect on ICICI Bank's share price or trading volumes is expected.
