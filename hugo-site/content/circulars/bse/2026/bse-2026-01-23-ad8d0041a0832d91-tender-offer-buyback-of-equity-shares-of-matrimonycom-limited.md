## Summary

BSE has notified that MATRIMONY.COM LIMITED (scrip code: 540704) has fixed 30th January 2026 as the record date to determine the entitlement and names of equity shareholders eligible to participate in the company's buyback offer. The ex-entitlement date is also 30th January 2026 (Settlement No. DR-809/2025-2026).

## Key Points

- Company: MATRIMONY.COM LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 540704
- Record Date: 30th January 2026
- Ex-Entitlement Date: 30th January 2026
- Settlement Number: DR-809/2025-2026
- Segment: Dematerialised Securities - Rolling Settlement Segment
- Purpose: Tender offer (buyback) of equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members of BSE are required to take note of the record date and ex-entitlement date
- Only shareholders holding shares on the record date (30/01/2026) will be eligible to participate in the buyback offer
- Shares will trade on ex-entitlement basis from 30/01/2026

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: 23rd January 2026
- **Record Date**: 30th January 2026
- **Ex-Entitlement Date**: 30th January 2026
- **Settlement Number**: DR-809/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium. The buyback announcement typically has positive sentiment for the stock as it signals management confidence and provides an exit opportunity at potentially premium prices for participating shareholders. Trading will be on ex-entitlement basis from 30/01/2026.

**Shareholder Impact**: Shareholders holding equity shares on the record date will be eligible to tender their shares in the buyback offer. This is a voluntary corporate action where shareholders can choose to participate or retain their holdings.

**Trading Impact**: From 30/01/2026, shares will trade ex-entitlement, meaning buyers after this date will not be eligible for the buyback offer.
