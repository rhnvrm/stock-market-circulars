## Summary

BSE has approved the surrender of trading membership of Todi Securities Private Limited (Member No. 6136) in the Currency Derivative segment with effect from March 12, 2026. This is an administrative notice informing all trading members of the exchange about this membership change.

## Key Points

- Todi Securities Private Limited (Member No. 6136) has surrendered its trading membership in the Currency Derivative segment.
- The surrender has been approved by BSE effective March 12, 2026.
- Trading members with queries may contact BSE Membership Department at membership.ops@bseindia.com.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard administrative action relating to the exit of a trading member from the Currency Derivative segment.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance requirements for other trading members. Members seeking clarification may contact BSE Membership Department via email at membership.ops@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** March 12, 2026 — Surrender of trading membership of Todi Securities Private Limited in the Currency Derivative segment.

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The surrender affects only Todi Securities Private Limited's ability to trade in the Currency Derivative segment on BSE. Other trading members and the broader market are unaffected. Clients of Todi Securities in this segment would need to transfer their positions or accounts to another trading member.
