## Summary

BSE has revised the record date for redemption of Commercial Paper for five companies from January 14, 2026 to January 13, 2026. Consequently, the suspension/no dealing date has been advanced to January 13, 2026. This circular updates Exchange Notice No. 20260107-7 dated January 07, 2025.

## Key Points

- Record date revised from January 14, 2026 to January 13, 2026
- No dealing date advanced to January 13, 2026
- Affects five companies: Bajaj Housing Finance Ltd, Bajaj Finance Ltd, Axis Finance Ltd, Navi Finserv Ltd, and Bajaj Financial Securities Ltd
- Purpose: Redemption of Commercial Paper
- Reference: DR-797/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative revision to previously announced suspension dates.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must note the revised suspension date of January 13, 2026
- No dealing permitted in the specified Commercial Paper ISINs from January 13, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Revised Record Date**: January 13, 2026 (previously January 14, 2026)
- **Revised No Dealings From**: January 13, 2026 (previously January 14, 2026)
- **Notice Date**: January 12, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a one-day advancement of the suspension date. Trading Members holding positions in these Commercial Paper instruments should adjust their redemption timelines accordingly. The change affects debt segment trading only and relates to scheduled redemption activities.
