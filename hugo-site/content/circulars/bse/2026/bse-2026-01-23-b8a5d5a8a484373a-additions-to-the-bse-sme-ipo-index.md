## Summary

BSE announces the addition of Aritas Vinyl Limited (Exchange ticker: 544683) to the BSE SME IPO Index effective January 27, 2026. The stock is being listed on BSE on January 23, 2026, with the index inclusion taking effect at the open of trading on January 27, 2026.

## Key Points

- Aritas Vinyl Limited (ticker: 544683) listed on BSE on January 23, 2026
- Stock will be added to BSE SME IPO Index effective at market open on January 27, 2026
- This action references prior Notice No. 20260122-19
- Corporate action included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- Queries can be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index constituent update following the listing of a new SME company.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index tracking funds and participants monitoring the BSE SME IPO Index should note the change in index composition.

## Important Dates

- **January 23, 2026**: Aritas Vinyl Limited listing on BSE
- **January 27, 2026**: Effective date for addition to BSE SME IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard index maintenance action affecting a single SME stock. Primary impact is on index tracking strategies and funds that replicate the BSE SME IPO Index, which will need to adjust portfolios to reflect the new constituent. The addition follows the established process for including newly listed SME IPO companies in the relevant index.
