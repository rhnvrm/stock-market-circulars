## Summary

BSE's Surveillance department (DOSS) observed significant price and/or volume movement in 18 listed scrips and wrote to the respective companies seeking clarifications. As of June 19, 2026, replies have been received and attached from 12 companies, while responses are still awaited from 6 companies.

## Key Points

- BSE initiated clarification requests due to unusual price/volume activity in 18 equity scrips.
- Replies received from 12 companies are attached to this notice (Replies1 and Replies2).
- 6 companies have not yet responded and their replies are awaited.
- Notice issued by the Surveillance department under DOSS.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard surveillance disclosure notice under BSE's investor protection framework to ensure trading members and investors have the latest relevant information.

## Compliance Requirements

- The 6 companies with pending replies (Gowra Leasing & Finance Ltd, Harikanta Overseas Ltd, Amrapali Industries Ltd, Elango Industries Ltd, Grand Oak Canyons Distillery Ltd, Balaji Telefilms Ltd) are expected to submit clarifications to BSE promptly.
- Trading members and investors should review the attached replies from the 12 responding companies before taking positions in these scrips.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 19, 2026
- Replies from 12 companies attached as of this date; no explicit deadline stated for pending replies.
