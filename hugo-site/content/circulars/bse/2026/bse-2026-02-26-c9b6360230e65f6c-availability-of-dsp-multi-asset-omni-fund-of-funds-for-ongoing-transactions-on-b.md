## Summary

BSE has informed members that DSP Multi Asset Omni Fund of Funds will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption, along with SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facility on the BSE StAR MF Platform with effect from February 27, 2026. This notice references the earlier Notice No. 20260204-13.

## Key Points

- DSP Multi Asset Omni Fund of Funds reopens for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform from February 27, 2026
- Both Regular Plan and Direct Plan variants are available
- Three sub-options available per plan: Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities are enabled along with regular subscription and redemption
- All six scheme variants are categorised under the Equity segment

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notice regarding the resumption of ongoing transaction facility for an existing mutual fund scheme on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members beyond processing transactions in the listed scheme variants through the BSE StAR MF Platform as per standard operating procedures.

## Important Dates

- **February 27, 2026**: Effective date for reopening of ongoing subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP transactions for DSP Multi Asset Omni Fund of Funds on BSE StAR MF Platform

## Impact Assessment

This notice has limited market impact and is relevant primarily to distributors and investors using the BSE StAR MF Platform for transacting in DSP mutual fund schemes. The six available scheme variants and their ISINs are:
