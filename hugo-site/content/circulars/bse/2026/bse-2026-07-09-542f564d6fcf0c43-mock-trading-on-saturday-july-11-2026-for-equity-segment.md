## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Equity segment on Saturday, July 11, 2026. The session allows trading members, including those using third-party trading platforms, to test their trading applications across various functionalities such as call auction sessions, risk-reduction mode, trading halts, and block deals. The new BOLT Pro TWS version 13.20 will also be released, with details to follow in a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Equity segment on July 11, 2026
- Trading members using third-party platforms can test application functionalities during this session
- Functionalities to be tested include call auction sessions, risk-reduction mode, trading halts, and block deals
- New BOLT Pro TWS version 13.20 to be released, with details in a separate circular
- Additional mock trading sessions scheduled for Closing Auction Session (CAS) testing on July 18, July 25, and August 1, 2026, per earlier circulars 20260319-21 and 20260610-41
- Trades from mock sessions do not attract margin, pay-in/pay-out obligations, or create rights and liabilities
- Members requested to provide feedback on the mock trading session by 3:00 pm on the day

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is an operational notice regarding scheduled testing activity.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are encouraged to actively participate in the mock trading session and submit feedback on system performance and features to BSE by 3:00 pm on the session day.

## Important Dates

- July 11, 2026: Mock trading session for Equity segment
- July 18, 2026: Mock trading session for Closing Auction Session (CAS) testing
- July 25, 2026: Mock trading session for Closing Auction Session (CAS) testing
- August 1, 2026: Mock trading session for Closing Auction Session (CAS) testing

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a test session with no real financial obligations, margin requirements, or settlement implications. The session primarily serves to help trading members validate their trading systems and familiarize themselves with exchange functionalities ahead of the BOLT Pro TWS version 13.20 rollout and upcoming Closing Auction Session implementation.
