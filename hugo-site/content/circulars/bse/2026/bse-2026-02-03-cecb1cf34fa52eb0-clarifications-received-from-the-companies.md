## Summary

BSE issued a circular containing clarifications from two companies - Atul Auto Limited and Ceeta Industries Limited - in response to surveillance queries about significant movements in their securities. Both companies confirmed compliance with SEBI LODR Regulations and stated that the movements were purely market-driven with no pending material information.

## Key Points

- Atul Auto Limited (BSE Script: 531795) responded to surveillance query about increase in trading volume
- Ceeta Industries Limited (CIN: L15100KA1984PLC021494) responded to query about significant price movement
- Both companies confirmed full compliance with Regulation 30 of SEBI LODR Regulations, 2015
- Companies stated all material events and price-sensitive information have been disclosed to the exchange
- No pending information or announcements that may affect price behavior
- Share price/volume fluctuations attributed entirely to market conditions

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies must continue to comply with Regulation 30 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015
- Prompt disclosure of all material events and price-sensitive information to the exchange
- Response to surveillance queries regarding unusual price or volume movements

## Important Dates

- February 03, 2026: Date of clarifications from both companies
- February 02, 2026: Date of BSE surveillance query to Ceeta Industries (Ref: L/SURV/ONL/PV/AJ/2025-2026/3316)
- February 03, 2026: Date of BSE surveillance query to Atul Auto (Ref: L/SURV/ONL/PV/APJ/2025-2026/897)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine surveillance and disclosure circular with minimal market impact. Both companies have confirmed that recent price/volume movements are market-driven with no undisclosed material information. The circular reinforces the ongoing surveillance mechanism of BSE to monitor unusual trading activity and ensure timely disclosure compliance. No action required from market participants.
