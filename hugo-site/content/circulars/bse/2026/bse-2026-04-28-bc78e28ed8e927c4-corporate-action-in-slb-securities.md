## Summary

ICCL Ltd. has issued Notice No. 20260428-11 dated 28 April 2026 informing trading members of corporate actions affecting securities in the Securities Lending and Borrowing (SLB) segment. The specific securities and action details are provided in the annexure attached to the notice.

## Key Points

- Notice issued by ICCL Ltd. (Indian Clearing Corporation Limited) under the SLB segment.
- Trading members are required to refer to the attached annexure for the list of securities affected by corporate actions.
- The notice falls under the Corporate Actions category and pertains to the SLB segment.
- Issued by Tushar Nimkar, Assistant Manager, ICCL Ltd.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is an operational notice communicating existing corporate action adjustments applicable to SLB positions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must review the annexure for the specific securities impacted by corporate actions in the SLB segment.
- Members should make necessary adjustments to their SLB positions in accordance with the corporate action details provided in the annexure.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 28 April 2026
- Specific effective dates for individual corporate actions are detailed in the attached annexure.

## Impact Assessment

This notice has a moderate operational impact on trading members with open positions in the SLB segment for the affected securities. Corporate actions such as dividends, splits, or mergers can require adjustment of SLB contracts, and members must act on the annexure details to ensure their positions are correctly managed. The broader market impact is limited to participants actively engaged in the SLB segment.
