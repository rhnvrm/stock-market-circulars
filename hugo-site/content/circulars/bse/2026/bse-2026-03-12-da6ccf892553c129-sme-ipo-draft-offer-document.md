## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document (DRHP) of Devson Catalyst Limited, filed on March 11, 2026, on the SME platform. The document is available for public viewing and comments for at least 21 days, as mandated under Schedule VI of the SEBI ICDR Regulations.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Devson Catalyst Limited on March 11, 2026
- Document hosted on BSE SME website: www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public comments invited within 21 days from the date of notice (by approximately April 2, 2026)
- Complies with all disclosure requirements under Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This notice is a standard procedural requirement under SEBI ICDR Regulations, Schedule VI, for SME IPO draft offer documents.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public and stakeholders may submit comments on the Draft Offer Document within 21 days from March 12, 2026
- Comments to be sent to: equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Document must remain available on the BSE SME website for at least 21 days

## Important Dates

- Filing Date: March 11, 2026
- Notice Date: March 12, 2026
- Public Comment Deadline: On or before approximately April 2, 2026 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a routine disclosure notice for an SME IPO by Devson Catalyst Limited. It is relevant primarily to investors interested in the SME segment and those wishing to review or comment on the company's IPO plans. No immediate trading or compliance obligations arise for existing market participants.
