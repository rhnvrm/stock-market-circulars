None. This is a routine listing notification under existing ESOP/ESOS listing procedures.

## Impact Assessment

The additional shares represent employee stock option allotments and are a routine, small-scale increase in listed share capital for each company. The quantities are marginal relative to each company's outstanding share base, resulting in negligible dilution and no material market impact. This is a standard operational listing update rather than a market-moving event.
