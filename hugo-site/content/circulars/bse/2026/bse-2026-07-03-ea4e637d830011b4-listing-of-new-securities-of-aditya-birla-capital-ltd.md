## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 3,14,58,905 new equity shares of Aditya Birla Capital Ltd (Scrip Code: 540691), each of face value Rs. 10/-, issued at a premium of Rs. 346.02/- on a preferential basis to promoters and non-promoters, are listed and permitted to trade with effect from Monday, July 6, 2026.

## Key Points

- 3,14,58,905 equity shares issued at a premium of Rs. 346.02/- (issue price Rs. 356.02/-) to promoters and non-promoters on preferential basis.
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company.
- Distinctive numbers: 2702747396 to 2734206300.
- Date of allotment: 23/06/2026.
- ISIN: INE674K01013.
- Shares are subject to lock-in as detailed below.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a listing/trading permission notice under standard preferential issue procedures, not a regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must recognize the new distinctive number range as valid for trading from the effective date.
- Lock-in restrictions apply to specific tranches of shares and must be tracked accordingly:
  - 56,17,661 shares (Dist. Nos. 2702747396–2708365056): locked in until 11/01/2028.
  - 2,58,41,244 shares (Dist. Nos. 2708365057–2734206300): locked in until 11/01/2027.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: 23/06/2026
- Listing/Trading Effective Date: Monday, July 6, 2026
- Lock-in expiry (Tranche 1 – 56,17,661 shares): 11/01/2028
- Lock-in expiry (Tranche 2 – 2,58,41,244 shares): 11/01/2027

## Impact Assessment

This listing increases the total tradable equity base of Aditya Birla Capital Ltd, though the bulk of the newly issued shares remain under lock-in until 2027-2028, limiting immediate free-float impact. The preferential issue to promoters and non-promoters may be relevant for shareholders tracking promoter holding changes and future dilution, but the notice itself is procedural and carries low immediate market risk.
