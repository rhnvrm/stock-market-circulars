## Summary

BSE announces the listing of three new Treasury Bills (T-Bills) effective January 30, 2026, in the G Group - Debt Instruments segment. The T-Bills have tenors of 91 days, 182 days, and 364 days with maturity dates ranging from April 2026 to January 2027.

## Key Points

- Three T-Bills to be listed on January 30, 2026
- Scrip code 805172 (ISIN: IN002025X430) - 91TB300426 maturing April 30, 2026
- Scrip code 805173 (ISIN: IN002025Y438) - 182TB30726 maturing July 30, 2026
- Scrip code 805174 (ISIN: IN002025Z435) - 364TB28127 maturing January 28, 2027
- All T-Bills have a market lot size of 1
- Trading will be suspended two working days prior to maturity date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for government securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the trading suspension rule: securities will not be available for trading two working days before the maturity/redemption date (T-2 trading days, excluding bank holidays)
- Trading members should update their systems to reflect the new scrip codes and ISINs

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: January 30, 2026
- **Maturity Dates**:
  - 91-day T-Bill: April 30, 2026
  - 182-day T-Bill: July 30, 2026
  - 364-day T-Bill: January 28, 2027
- **Trading Suspension**: Two working days before each respective maturity date

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine administrative action for listing government Treasury Bills. These instruments provide short-term investment options for debt market participants and contribute to liquidity in the government securities market. The listing affects only debt market traders and institutional investors dealing in government securities.
