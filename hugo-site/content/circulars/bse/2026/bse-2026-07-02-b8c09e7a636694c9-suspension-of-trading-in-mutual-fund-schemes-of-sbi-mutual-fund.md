## Summary

BSE has notified Trading Members that four Mutual Fund schemes of SBI Mutual Fund under the SBI Fixed Maturity Plan (FMP) - Series 59 (1618 Days) will mature pursuant to the redemption date fixed by the fund house. Consequently, trading in these schemes will be suspended effective July 3, 2026.

## Key Points

- The notice covers four SBI FMP Series 59 (1618 Days) scheme variants: Regular Plan-Growth, Regular Plan-IDCW Payout, Direct Plan-Growth, and Direct Plan-IDCW Payout.
- Scrip codes affected: 543455, 543456, 543457, and 543459.
- ISINs affected: INF200KA17D6, INF200KA18D4, INF200KA19D2, and INF200KA10E9.
- The schemes are maturing as per the redemption date fixed by SBI Mutual Fund.
- Reference number: DR-664/2026-2027.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an operational notice implementing scheme maturity/redemption, not a new regulation.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members of the Exchange are advised not to deal in the aforesaid Mutual Fund schemes of SBI Mutual Fund with effect from July 3, 2026.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 2, 2026
- Trading suspension effective: July 3, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and traders of the specific SBI FMP Series 59 (1618 Days) schemes, which are closed-ended debt instruments reaching maturity. There is no impact on equity markets or other listed securities. Investors in these schemes will receive redemption proceeds as per the fund's maturity process.
