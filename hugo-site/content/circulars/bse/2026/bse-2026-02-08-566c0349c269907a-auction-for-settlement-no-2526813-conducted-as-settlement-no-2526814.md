## Summary

BSE has issued a partial modification to the February 2026 settlement calendar for the Equity Cash Segment. The auction for settlement no. 2526813 (trade date: February 4, 2026) will now be conducted as settlement no. 2526814 with a revised auction trade date of February 9, 2026 and auction settlement date of February 10, 2026.

## Key Points

- Auction for settlement 2526813 rescheduled to be conducted as settlement 2526814
- Members must submit pay-in instructions to Depositories/Banks by 7:50 AM
- Pay-in time for auction at Clearing Corporation is 8:00 AM on February 10, 2026
- All shortages in C group and trade-to-trade segments (T, P, Z groups) will be directly closed out
- Scrips where no-delivery period has been abolished will have shortages directly closed out

## Regulatory Changes

This notice modifies the previously published settlement calendar notice no. 20260120-37 dated January 20, 2026. The change affects the auction schedule timing for settlement 2526813.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must adhere to the revised auction schedule
- Pay-in instructions must be submitted to Depositories/Banks latest by 7:50 AM on auction day
- Members must ensure timely settlement of auction obligations
- Direct close-out provisions apply as per notice 20190529-43 for specified categories

## Important Dates

- **Trade Date (Original Settlement 2526813):** February 4, 2026
- **Custodian Confirmation:** February 5, 2026
- **Settlement Date:** February 5, 2026
- **Auction Trade Date (Settlement 2526814):** February 9, 2026
- **Auction Settlement Date:** February 10, 2026
- **Pay-in Deadline:** 7:50 AM on February 10, 2026
- **Auction Pay-in Time at CC:** 8:00 AM on February 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Members with delivery shortages in settlement 2526813 need to prepare for the revised auction timeline. The rescheduling provides additional time but requires strict adherence to the 7:50 AM pay-in deadline. Members dealing in C group securities and trade-to-trade segments should note the direct close-out provisions to avoid penalties. The modification ensures orderly settlement while accommodating calendar adjustments for February 2026.
