## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities issued on a private placement basis by multiple companies on its debt market segment, effective January 19, 2026. The securities will trade in dematerialized form with a standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and tick size of 1 paise.

## Key Points

- Five commercial paper instruments listed from four companies: Poonawalla Fincorp Limited (2 issues), Navi Finserv Limited (2 issues), and Infina Finance Private Limited (1 issue)
- All securities trade only in dematerialized form under specified ISIN numbers
- Standard trading denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Interest rates range from 7.10% to 9.85% per annum
- Tenures range from 3 months to 6 months
- Contact: BSE Debt Department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915 for clarifications

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Trading members should use the specified scrip codes and ISIN numbers for transactions

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** January 19, 2026
- **Allotment Date (all issues):** January 16, 2026
- **Redemption Dates:**
  - April 16-17, 2026 (Poonawalla Fincorp PFL-17-4-26-CP, Navi Finserv NFL-16-4-26-CP, Infina Finance IFPL-17-4-26-CP)
  - June 11, 2026 (Poonawalla Fincorp PFL-11-6-26-CP)
  - July 16, 2026 (Navi Finserv NFL-16-7-26-CP)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This is a routine listing of debt securities on the debt segment.

**Trading Impact:** Limited to debt market participants. These commercial papers provide short-term investment options for institutional investors.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members dealing in debt securities need to update their systems with new scrip codes and ISIN numbers.
