## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260414-1 dated 14 April 2026 announcing the launch of SBI Mutual Fund New Fund Offers (NFOs) on the BSE StAR MF platform. The NFOs come with a Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) facility, enabling investors to subscribe through periodic investments. Detailed scheme-specific information is provided in the attached annexure.

## Key Points

- SBI Mutual Fund is launching new schemes (NFOs) on the BSE StAR MF platform.
- SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) facility is available for these NFOs.
- The notice is issued by the Trading Operations department under the Mutual Fund segment.
- Full scheme details, including scheme names, subscription dates, and other parameters, are provided in the attached Annexure.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced. This is an operational notice informing market participants of new mutual fund products being made available for subscription on BSE StAR MF.

## Compliance Requirements

- Registered mutual fund distributors and stock brokers on BSE StAR MF may facilitate subscriptions in the newly launched SBI MF NFOs.
- Participants should refer to the Annexure for scheme-specific details, including NFO open/close dates and minimum subscription amounts.
- SIP registrations should be processed as per existing BSE StAR MF operational guidelines.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 14 April 2026
- NFO subscription period dates: Refer to the attached Annexure for scheme-specific open and close dates.

## Impact Assessment

This notice has a moderate operational impact on mutual fund distributors and brokers registered on the BSE StAR MF platform. Investors interested in SBI Mutual Fund schemes will have access to these NFOs with the added convenience of SIP-based investments. The availability of SIP facility broadens accessibility for retail investors. No broad market or systemic impact is expected.
