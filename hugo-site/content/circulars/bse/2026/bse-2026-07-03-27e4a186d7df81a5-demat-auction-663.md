## Summary

BSE Limited has invited offers from trading members of the Exchange for the purchase of securities in Auction with respect to Rolling Settlement No. DR-663/2026-27. This is a routine settlement/RMS notice detailing the auction schedule, timings, and procedural requirements for trading members and custodians.

## Key Points

- Auction relates to Rolling Settlement No. DR-663/2026-27
- Offers accepted on trading terminals on Friday, July 3, 2026
- Auction offer entry window: 2:00 p.m. to 2:45 p.m.
- Auction matching window: 2:45 p.m. to 3:00 p.m.
- Delivery statement and report of accepted auction offers: 3:00 p.m. to 3:30 p.m.
- Money statement and final reports: 3:35 p.m. to 5:30 p.m.
- Pay-in of the auction scheduled for Monday, July 6, 2026 at 8:00 a.m.
- Settlement now occurs through the Clearing Member of a Trading Member under the Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment
- Restriction on trading members offering securities in auction for settlements where they short-delivered such securities has been removed
- No corrections permitted for punching errors in securities code and rate; Exchange reserves right to accept or reject any offer without assigning reason
- Trading members advised to refer to cutoff rates on the Auction Offer Entry Screen and check for Corporate Action notices (Ex-Rights/Ex-Bonus/Ex-Split etc.)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this notice reiterates the removal of the prior restriction preventing trading members from offering securities in auction within the same settlement in which they had short-delivered such securities, following implementation of the Uniform Membership structure.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and custodians must check their auction pay-in obligations for securities and execute securities pay-in within depository cut-off timings
- Trading members must exercise care while entering securities codes and rates, as no corrections are allowed post-entry
- Trading members should verify cutoff rates and any related corporate action notices before submitting auction offers

## Important Dates

- Auction offer entry, matching, and reporting: Friday, July 3, 2026 (2:00 p.m. – 5:30 p.m.)
- Auction pay-in: Monday, July 6, 2026 at 8:00 a.m.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice affecting trading members and custodians participating in the auction mechanism for short-delivered securities under Rolling Settlement DR-663/2026-27. It has no direct impact on specific listed stocks or broader market sentiment, serving primarily as a procedural and scheduling communication for settlement operations.
