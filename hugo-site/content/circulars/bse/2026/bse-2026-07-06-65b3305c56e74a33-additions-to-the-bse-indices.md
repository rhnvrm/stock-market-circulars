## Summary

BSE Index Services has announced the addition of two newly listed companies to their respective BSE indices, following their recent IPO listings on the exchange. Aastha Spintex Limited (ticker: 544808) will be added to the BSE IPO index, while Adon Agro Commodities Limited (ticker: 544809) will be added to the BSE SME IPO index.

## Key Points

- Aastha Spintex Limited (Exchange ticker - 544808) was listed on BSE effective Monday, July 06, 2026, per earlier Notice No: 20260703-17
- Aastha Spintex Limited will be added to the BSE IPO index effective the open of Wednesday, July 08, 2026
- Adon Agro Commodities Limited (Exchange ticker - 544809) was listed on the SME platform of BSE effective Monday, July 06, 2026, per earlier Notice No: 20260703-23
- Adon Agro Commodities Limited will be added to the BSE SME IPO index effective the open of Tuesday, July 07, 2026
- Both actions will be included in the respective night's end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is a routine index maintenance action reflecting standard inclusion of newly listed IPO stocks into applicable indices.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action required from market participants. Index fund managers and passive trackers of BSE IPO and BSE SME IPO indices should update portfolio compositions accordingly. Queries can be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- July 06, 2026: Aastha Spintex Limited and Adon Agro Commodities Limited listed on BSE
- July 07, 2026: Adon Agro Commodities Limited added to BSE SME IPO index (effective at open)
- July 08, 2026: Aastha Spintex Limited added to BSE IPO index (effective at open)

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact, routine administrative update reflecting the standard process of adding newly listed securities to relevant BSE indices. The action primarily affects index funds and ETFs that track the BSE IPO and BSE SME IPO indices, requiring minor portfolio rebalancing. No material impact on broader market trading or investor decision-making is expected.
