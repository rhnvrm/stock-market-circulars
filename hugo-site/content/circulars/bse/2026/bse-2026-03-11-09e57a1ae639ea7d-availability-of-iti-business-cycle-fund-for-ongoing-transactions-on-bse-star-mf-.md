## Summary

BSE has informed its members that ITI Business Cycle Fund will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption, along with SIP, STP, and SWP facility on the BSE StAR MF Platform, effective March 12, 2026. This notice follows the earlier Notice No. 20260212-38.

## Key Points

- ITI Business Cycle Fund reopens for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform from March 12, 2026.
- Facilities available: subscription, redemption, SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan).
- Six scheme variants are made available across Regular and Direct plans with Growth and IDCW (Payout and Reinvestment) options.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding the availability of a mutual fund scheme on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members beyond processing transactions in the listed schemes as per standard BSE StAR MF platform procedures.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** March 12, 2026 — ITI Business Cycle Fund reopens for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This notice affects distributors and investors using the BSE StAR MF Platform who wish to transact in ITI Business Cycle Fund schemes. The six available schemes (Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options) cover ISINs INF00XX01DJ5 through INF00XX01DO5. No disruption to existing holdings; this simply restores transactional access.
