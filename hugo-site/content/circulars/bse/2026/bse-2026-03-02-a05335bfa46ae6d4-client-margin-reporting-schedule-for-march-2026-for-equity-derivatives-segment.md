## Summary

Indian Clearing Corporation Limited (ICCL) has issued the client margin reporting schedule for March 2026 for the Equity Derivatives Segment. The circular specifies the last dates for margin reporting and corresponding penalty dates for each trade date during the month of March 2026, extending into early April 2026.

## Key Points

- The schedule covers trade dates from 02-Mar-2026 through 30-Mar-2026.
- Each trade date has a designated last date for margin reporting (typically 5 trading days later) and a subsequent penalty date.
- Penalty dates fall one trading day after the last date of margin reporting.
- The schedule accounts for market holidays, with some gaps in trading dates (e.g., 26-Mar-2026 appears to be a holiday).
- Issued on behalf of ICCL by Deputy Managers Manoj Panchal and Omkar Hatkar.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is a routine monthly schedule notification in accordance with existing SEBI and ICCL margin reporting framework for the Equity Derivatives Segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members of the Equity Derivatives Segment must submit client margin reports by the specified last date of margin reporting for each trade date.
- Failure to report by the last date will result in penalties applied on the designated penalty date.
- Members should update their internal compliance calendars with the dates provided in this circular.

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a moderate operational impact on trading members and clearing members participating in the Equity Derivatives Segment.
