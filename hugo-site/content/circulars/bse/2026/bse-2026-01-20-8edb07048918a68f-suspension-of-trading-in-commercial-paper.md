## Summary

BSE has issued a trading suspension notice for commercial papers of 25 companies due to upcoming redemptions. Trading members are advised not to deal in these commercial papers from their respective record dates, which range from January 22 to January 30, 2026. The notice covers major financial institutions and corporates including L&T Metro Rail, Bajaj Finance, Tata Power, JSW Energy, Supreme Industries, and various other entities.

## Key Points

- 25 commercial papers will be suspended from trading due to redemption
- Record dates span from January 22, 2026 to January 30, 2026
- Trading suspension coincides with respective record dates for each instrument
- Notice categorized under Corporate Actions in the Debt segment
- Multiple reference numbers: DR-804 through DR-809 for fiscal year 2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard procedural notice for commercial paper redemptions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified commercial papers from their respective "No Dealings From" dates
- Members must ensure compliance with trading suspension from the record dates mentioned
- All trading activity in the listed commercial papers must cease from the specified dates
