## Summary

BSE has issued notice that **Advance Syntex Ltd (539982)** and **Tasty Dairy Specialities Ltd (540955)** will be shifted to the 'Z' group effective **June 29, 2026**, unless they achieve compliance by **June 23, 2026**. The non-compliance relates to Regulation 31 of the SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015 — specifically failure to submit shareholding patterns for two consecutive quarters (December 2025 and March 2026). An additional 28 companies already in the 'Z' group are also noted as non-compliant under the same regulation.

## Key Points

- Two scrips face Z-group transfer: Advance Syntex Ltd (539982) and Tasty Dairy Specialities Ltd (540955)
- Non-compliance: Regulation 31 — Submission of Shareholding Pattern — for Q3 (Dec 2025) and Q4 (Mar 2026)
- Companies must comply by **June 23, 2026** to avoid Z-group classification
- Transfer to Z group takes effect **June 29, 2026** if compliance is not achieved
- 28 additional companies are already in Z group for the same non-compliance
- Dharan Infra-EPC Ltd and Golden Tobacco Ltd are non-compliant at NSE but compliant at BSE; included per SEBI Master Circular requirements
- Trades in Z group scrips are settled on a **Trade for Trade (TfT) basis**
- Action is pursuant to SEBI Master Circular No. HO/49/14/14(7)2025-CFD-POD2/I/3762/2026 (originally issued July 11, 2023, last updated January 30, 2026)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced. This notice enforces existing penal actions under SEBI Master Circular Chapter VII(A) — Penal Actions for Non-Compliance with SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015, specifically Regulation 31 (Shareholding Pattern submission).

## Compliance Requirements

- **Advance Syntex Ltd (539982)** and **Tasty Dairy Specialities Ltd (540955)** must file shareholding patterns for December 2025 and March 2026 quarters with BSE **on or before June 23, 2026** to avoid Z-group transfer.
- All listed entities must comply with Regulation 31 (quarterly shareholding pattern submission) to avoid similar penal action.
- Companies already in Z group should work toward compliance to seek revocation of Z-group classification per the Standard Operating Procedure for suspension and revocation of trading.

## Important Dates

| Date | Event |
|------|-------|
| June 23, 2026 | Compliance deadline for Advance Syntex Ltd and Tasty Dairy Specialities Ltd |
| June 29, 2026 | Effective date of Z-group transfer for non-compliant scrips |

## Impact Assessment

**For investors in Advance Syntex Ltd and Tasty Dairy Specialities Ltd:** If companies fail to comply by June 23, 2026, trades will shift to trade-for-trade settlement starting June 29, 2026, eliminating netting benefits and increasing transaction costs. This may trigger selling pressure ahead of the deadline.

**For investors in the 28 already-Z-group companies:** No change in trading conditions; the notice confirms their continued non-compliant status.

**Broader market impact:** The notice underscores SEBI's ongoing enforcement of LODR disclosure norms. Companies in the Z group face reduced liquidity and investor confidence due to trade-for-trade settlement restrictions.
