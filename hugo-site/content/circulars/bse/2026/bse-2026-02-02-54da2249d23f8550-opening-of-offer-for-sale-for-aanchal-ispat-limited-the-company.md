## Summary

BSE has announced the opening of Offer for Sale (OFS) for Aanchal Ispat Limited (ISIN: INE322R01022). The seller is Mukesh Goel, who is part of the promoter and promoter group. The OFS will be conducted over two trading days with separate sessions for Non-Retail and Retail Investors. BSE Ltd has been declared as the designated stock exchange, with NSE Clearing Limited as the designated clearing corporation.

## Key Points

- Seller: Mukesh Goel (Promoter category)
- Company: Aanchal Ispat Limited
- ISIN: INE322R01022
- Designated Stock Exchange: BSE Ltd
- Clearing Corporation: NSE Clearing Limited
- Two-day bidding process with separate sessions for investor categories
- Non-Retail Investors can carry forward un-allotted bids to T+1 Day
- Notice issued pursuant to SEBI OFS Circulars and LODR Regulations 2015

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard OFS process conducted in accordance with existing SEBI OFS Circulars and paragraph 5(b) requirements for announcing seller's intention to undertake the offer.

## Compliance Requirements

- Prospective investors and brokers must read the entire Offer Notice dated February 02, 2026 along with SEBI OFS Circulars before participating
- Orders to be placed on the designated stock exchange (BSE)
- Investors must adhere to category-specific bidding timelines
- Non-Retail Investors must indicate willingness to carry forward bids if choosing T+1 Day allocation option

## Important Dates

- **T Day (February 03, 2026)**: Non-Retail Investors bidding session
  - Timing: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM IST
  - Only Non-Retail Investors allowed to place bids
  - Option to carry forward un-allotted bids to T+1 Day for unsubscribed Retail Category portion

- **T+1 Day (February 04, 2026)**: Retail Investors and carry-forward bids
  - Retail Investors can place bids
  - Non-Retail Investors who chose to carry forward un-allotted bids can participate

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The OFS by a promoter will impact the shareholding pattern of Aanchal Ispat Limited and may affect stock liquidity and price discovery during the offer period.

**Investor Impact**: The two-day structure provides opportunities for both investor categories, with Non-Retail Investors having priority access on T Day and option to participate in unsubscribed Retail portion on T+1 Day.

**Trading Impact**: Separate OFS window will operate during standard trading hours without disrupting normal market operations. The carry-forward mechanism for Non-Retail Investors provides flexibility in allocation.

**Operational Impact**: Investors and brokers need to plan participation based on investor category and understand the carry-forward mechanism for optimal allocation chances.
