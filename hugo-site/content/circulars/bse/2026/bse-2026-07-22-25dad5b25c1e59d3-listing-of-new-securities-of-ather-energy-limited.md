## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 1,08,15,307 equity shares of Rs. 1/- each of Ather Energy Limited (Scrip Code: 544397, ISIN: INE0LEZ01016), allotted to Qualified Institutional Buyers (QIBs) pursuant to a Qualified Institutional Placement (QIP), have been listed and admitted for trading on the Exchange with effect from Thursday, July 23, 2026.

## Key Points

- 1,08,15,307 new equity shares of Ather Energy Limited allotted to QIBs via QIP
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Date of allotment: July 20, 2026
- Issue price: Rs. 1202/- per share
- Distinctive numbers: 383310003 to 394125309
- Scrip Code: 544397; ISIN: INE0LEZ01016

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing/admission notice under BSE Listing Operations.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific action required from trading members beyond noting the additional listed shares for trading purposes. Queries may be directed to Mr. Karan Shah (Tel: 022 2272 8987).

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: July 20, 2026
- Listing/Trading Effective Date: July 23, 2026 (Thursday)

## Impact Assessment

The listing increases the free-float and total outstanding equity share capital of Ather Energy Limited following its QIP. This is a routine corporate action with limited immediate market impact, though it marginally increases share supply and may have a modest dilutive effect on existing shareholders.
