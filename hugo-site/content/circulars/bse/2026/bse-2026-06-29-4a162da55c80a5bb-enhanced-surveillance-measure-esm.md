## Summary

BSE has updated the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework under Notice No. 20260629-29. Securities newly added to ESM will attract a minimum 100% margin on all open positions as of June 30, 2026, and all new positions from July 1, 2026. Affected securities will move from Rolling Settlement to Trade-for-Trade segment. Some securities are being downgraded to Stage I, others are exiting the framework entirely.

## Key Points

- New ESM inclusions (Annexure I) attract minimum 100% margin effective July 1, 2026
- Securities under ESM shifted from Rolling Settlement to Trade-for-Trade segment w.e.f. July 1, 2026
- Securities moving to Stage II placed under Trade-for-Trade with 2% price band and Periodic Call Auction w.e.f. June 30, 2026
- Some securities downgraded to Stage I (lower stage) under ESM w.e.f. June 30, 2026
- Securities exiting ESM framework listed in Annexure II, effective June 30, 2026
- Consolidated list of all securities under ESM framework provided in Annexure III
- ESM classification is purely a market surveillance measure and not an adverse action against the company

## Regulatory Changes

This notice builds on prior ESM circulars (20230602-44, 20230718-46, 20240809-42, 20240920-63, 20241004-65, 20250725-61) and updates the list of securities subject to ESM. The framework requires periodic review of all shortlisted securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure minimum 100% margin is collected on all open positions in newly added ESM securities from June 30, 2026 (for open positions) and July 1, 2026 (for new positions)
- Trading in ESM securities must comply with Trade-for-Trade settlement rules
- Stage II securities must observe the 2% price band and Periodic Call Auction mechanism
- Price band of securities exiting ESM reverts to the band applicable before ESM inclusion, unless the security is under another surveillance measure

## Important Dates

- **June 30, 2026**: Stage I downgrade effective; Stage II price band (2%) and Periodic Call Auction effective; securities exit ESM framework (Annexure II)
- **July 1, 2026**: Minimum 100% margin effective for new ESM inclusions on new positions; shift to Trade-for-Trade settlement effective

## Impact Assessment

Affected securities face significantly reduced liquidity due to the Trade-for-Trade settlement requirement and heightened margin obligations (100% minimum). Stage II securities are further constrained by the 2% price band and Periodic Call Auction, limiting intraday price discovery. Market participants holding or trading these securities should expect increased capital requirements and reduced flexibility. The ESM is a surveillance tool only and carries no implication of regulatory action against the issuing company.
