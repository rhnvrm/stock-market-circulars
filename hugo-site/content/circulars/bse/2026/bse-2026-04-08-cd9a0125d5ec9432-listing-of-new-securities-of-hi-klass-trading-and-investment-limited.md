## Summary

BSE has listed 64,58,500 new equity shares of Hi-Klass Trading and Investment Limited (Scrip Code: 542332, ISIN: INE302R01024) effective Thursday, April 9, 2026. The shares were issued at Rs. 25/- each (face value Rs. 5/- with a premium of Rs. 20/-) to Promoters and Non-Promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 64,58,500 equity shares of Rs. 5/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 20/- (issue price Rs. 25/-) via preferential allotment
- Shares allotted on conversion of warrants; rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 20596401 to 27054900
- Date of allotment: 11 February 2026
- Trading permitted from April 9, 2026 on BSE
- ISIN: INE302R01024

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice under BSE's Listing Operations framework for newly allotted securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed that the new securities are listed and permitted to trade. Lock-in restrictions apply to specific tranches as detailed below and must be observed by depositories and trading members.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** 11 February 2026
- **Trading commencement:** Thursday, 9 April 2026
- **Lock-in expiry (15,00,000 shares; dist. nos. 20596401–22096400):** 30 October 2027
- **Lock-in expiry (49,58,500 shares; dist. nos. 22096401–27054900):** 30 October 2026

## Impact Assessment

This listing increases the tradable equity base of Hi-Klass Trading and Investment Limited. The majority of newly listed shares (49,58,500) are locked in until October 2026, limiting immediate free-float impact. The promoter tranche (15,00,000 shares) carries a longer lock-in until October 2027. Overall market impact is minimal given the company-specific and routine nature of this preferential allotment.
