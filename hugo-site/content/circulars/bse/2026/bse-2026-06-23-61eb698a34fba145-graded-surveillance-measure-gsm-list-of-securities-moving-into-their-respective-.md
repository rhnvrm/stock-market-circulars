## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260623-22 informing trading members that certain securities (listed in the attached Annexure) will be moved to higher stages of the Graded Surveillance Measure (GSM) framework with effect from June 24, 2026. These securities will attract progressively stricter surveillance actions depending on their assigned GSM stage (I through IV).

## Key Points

- Securities in the Annexure will move to higher GSM stages effective June 24, 2026
- Four GSM stages with escalating restrictions apply: Stage I through Stage IV
- Stage I: 100% applicable margin rate with a price band of 5% or lower
- Stage II: Trade-for-trade settlement with 5% price band and Additional Surveillance Deposit (ASD) of 50% of trade value by buyers
- Stage III: Trade-for-trade, 5% price band, weekly trading (Mondays/first trading day only), and ASD of 100% of trade value by buyers
- Stage IV: Same as Stage III with an additional restriction of no upward price movement
- No netting off is allowed; settlement on trade-to-trade basis
- Trading members are urged to take adequate precautions when trading these securities

## Regulatory Changes

This notice is issued with reference to the GSM Framework first published on February 23, 2017 (Exchange notice no. 20170223-44) and subsequently updated via notices dated March 3, 2017; July 20, 2018; November 29, 2019; and November 17, 2023. Securities are being escalated to higher stages within the existing GSM framework rather than introducing new regulatory provisions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the updated GSM stage for each affected security as listed in the Annexure
- Buyers in Stage II securities must deposit ASD of 50% of trade value
- Buyers in Stage III and Stage IV securities must deposit ASD of 100% of trade value
- All trades in affected securities must be settled on a trade-to-trade basis with no netting off permitted
- Members should review the GSM FAQ at http://www.bseindia.com/markets/equity/EQReports/graded_surveil_measure.aspx?expandable=6
- Clarifications can be directed to bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 23, 2026
- **Effective Date:** June 24, 2026 (securities move to new GSM stages)

## Impact Assessment

Affected securities will face significantly reduced liquidity and heightened trading friction. Stage III and IV securities are particularly impacted, restricted to once-weekly trading on Mondays, requiring full trade value as Additional Surveillance Deposit, and in Stage IV, prohibited from any upward price movement. These measures are designed to protect investors from speculative or manipulated securities. Trading members and investors holding or considering positions in these securities should exercise caution and review the full Annexure for the complete list of affected scrips.
