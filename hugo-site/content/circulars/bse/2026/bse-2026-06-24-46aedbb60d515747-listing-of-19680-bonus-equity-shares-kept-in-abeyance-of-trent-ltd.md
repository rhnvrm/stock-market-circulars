## Summary

BSE has listed 19,680 bonus equity shares of Trent Ltd. (Scrip Code: 500251, ISIN: INE849A01020) that were previously kept in abeyance. These shares are permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Thursday, June 25, 2026.

## Key Points

- 19,680 bonus equity shares of Rs. 1/- each kept in abeyance are now listed on BSE
- Scrip Code: 500251; ISIN: INE849A01020
- Distribution range: 533212622 to 533232301
- Effective trading date: Thursday, June 25, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative notice enabling previously withheld bonus shares to be admitted to trading.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities and may facilitate trading in these shares from June 25, 2026 onward.

## Important Dates

- **June 25, 2026**: Effective date for trading of newly listed bonus shares

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The quantity of 19,680 shares is very small relative to Trent Ltd.'s total share capital. This is a housekeeping action resolving previously abeyanced bonus shares, with no significant effect on price discovery or liquidity.
