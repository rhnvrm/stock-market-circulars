## Summary

BSE has announced the introduction of Daily Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) facility for THE WEALTH COMPANY MUTUAL FUND on the BSE StAR MF (Mutual Fund) platform. This enhancement allows investors to make daily SIP investments in schemes offered by THE WEALTH COMPANY MUTUAL FUND.

## Key Points

- Daily SIP facility introduced for THE WEALTH COMPANY MUTUAL FUND
- Available on BSE StAR MF platform
- Provides investors with more flexibility in investment frequency
- Routine platform enhancement for mutual fund trading

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational enhancement to the BSE StAR MF platform.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements. This is an informational circular about platform feature enhancement.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: February 2, 2026
- Effective Date: To be confirmed in the detailed circular

## Impact Assessment

Low impact circular. This is a routine operational update that enhances investor convenience by providing daily SIP option for THE WEALTH COMPANY MUTUAL FUND schemes on the BSE platform. The change primarily benefits retail investors seeking more frequent investment options but does not affect market operations or create compliance obligations.
