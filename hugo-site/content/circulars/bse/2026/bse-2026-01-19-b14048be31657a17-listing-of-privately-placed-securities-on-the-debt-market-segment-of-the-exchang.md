## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued on private placement basis by multiple companies on the Debt Segment effective January 19, 2026. The securities will trade in dematerialized form with a standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof. Five Commercial Papers from four companies have been listed with varying maturity periods ranging from April to July 2026.

## Key Points

- Fresh listing of 5 Commercial Papers from Poonawalla Fincorp Limited (2), Navi Finserv Limited (2), Infina Finance Private Limited (1), and Tata Housing Development Company Limited
- All securities will trade only in dematerialized form under specified ISIN numbers
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Interest rates range from 7.10% to 9.85% per annum
- All Commercial Papers follow cumulative interest payment structure

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low. This is a routine administrative notification regarding fresh listings of privately placed Commercial Papers. The listing provides additional short-term debt instruments for institutional investors and treasury operations.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal. Trading members dealing in debt securities need to update their systems with new scrip codes and ISIN numbers. Standard trading parameters apply.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to institutional investors and high-net-worth individuals who participate in debt market trading with minimum investment of Rs. 5 lakhs per unit. The Commercial Papers offer short-term investment options (3-6 months maturity) with interest rates between 7.10% to 9.85% depending on issuer credit rating.
