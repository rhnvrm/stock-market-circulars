## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the debt securities of Paranjape Spaces and Services Private Limited (PSSPL-15%-07-07-26-PVT, ISIN INE0LLO07012, Scrip Code 974589) are maturing on the scheduled maturity date. Trading members have been advised not to deal in these debentures with effect from July 06, 2026, under reference DR-665/2025-2026.

## Key Points

- Security: PSSPL-15%-07-07-26-PVT debentures issued by Paranjape Spaces and Services Private Limited
- ISIN: INE0LLO07012
- Scrip Code: 974589
- Trading in this debt security is being suspended as it approaches maturity
- Reference number: DR-665/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine administrative notice related to the natural maturity of a debt instrument, not a new regulatory rule.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of the Exchange must not deal in the aforesaid debentures of Paranjape Spaces and Services Private Limited with effect from July 06, 2026.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 03, 2026
- Trading restriction effective from: July 06, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and potential traders of this specific privately placed debt security. As the instrument is maturing, this is a standard administrative action and does not indicate any distress or default. No broader market or equity impact is expected.
