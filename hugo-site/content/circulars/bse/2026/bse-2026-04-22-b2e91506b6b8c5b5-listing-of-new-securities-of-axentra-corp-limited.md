## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 1,00,00,000 new equity shares of Axentra Corp Limited (Scrip Code: 511634) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from April 23, 2026. These shares were issued on a preferential basis to non-promoters at an issue price of Rs. 20/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- with a premium of Rs. 10/-).

## Key Points

- 1,00,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 10/- per share
- Issue price: Rs. 20/- per share
- Allotted to non-promoters on a preferential basis
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 9700001 to 19700000
- ISIN: INE919M01026
- Trading effective from Thursday, April 23, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice for newly allotted preferential shares.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed to note the listing of these new securities and permit trading from April 23, 2026. The allottees (non-promoters) are subject to a lock-in period and cannot trade the shares until the lock-in expiry.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** February 25, 2026
- **Trading Commencement:** April 23, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry:** October 31, 2026 (for all 1,00,00,000 shares, dist. nos. 9700001 to 19700000)

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 1 crore new equity shares to the tradeable universe of Axentra Corp Limited, increasing the total share count and potentially diluting existing shareholders. The shares are under lock-in until October 31, 2026, so no immediate selling pressure from these allottees is expected. Impact is limited to shareholders and traders of Axentra Corp Limited (Scrip Code 511634).
