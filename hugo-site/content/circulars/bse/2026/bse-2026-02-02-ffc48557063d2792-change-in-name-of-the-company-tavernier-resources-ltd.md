## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for the company formerly known as Tavernier Resources Limited. The company's name has been officially changed to Eforu Entertainment Limited with effect from October 27, 2025. The company was originally incorporated as Dewan Industries Limited and remains a company limited by shares with CIN L93290MH1994PLC193901.

## Key Points

- Former name: Tavernier Resources Limited
- New name: Eforu Entertainment Limited
- Effective date of name change: October 27, 2025
- CIN: L93290MH1994PLC193901
- Original incorporation name: Dewan Industries Limited
- Certificate issued by: Central Processing Centre, ROC
- Approved by: Sweety Kumar, Assistant/Deputy/Registrar of Companies
- Registered office: Level 4, A-Wing, Dynasty Business Park, Andheri Kurla Road, Andheri East, J.B. Nagar, Mumbai, Mumbai-400059, Maharashtra, India

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are involved. This is a standard corporate name change procedure under the Companies Act, 2014, processed through the Ministry of Corporate Affairs' Central Processing Centre.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display the old name (Tavernier Resources Limited) along with its new name (Eforu Entertainment Limited) for a period of two years at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders
- All legal obligations and contracts remain valid under the new name
- Stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status and view public documents on www.mca.gov.in/MCA21

## Important Dates

- **October 27, 2025**: Effective date of name change and certificate issuance
- **October 27, 2027**: End of mandatory two-year period for displaying old name alongside new name

## Impact Assessment

This name change has minimal operational and market impact. It is an administrative corporate action that does not affect:
- The company's legal status or CIN
- Rights and liabilities of stakeholders
- Existing contracts, agreements, or obligations
- Share ownership or trading

The primary impact is cosmetic, requiring updates to company stationery, documentation, and branding materials. Shareholders and creditors retain all existing rights under the new name.
