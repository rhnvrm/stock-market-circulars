## Summary

Zerodha Broking Ltd. (BSE Member No. 6498) has announced the voluntary closure of its business in the Currency Derivative segment on BSE. The cessation of operations will be effective from Friday, May 22, 2026.

## Key Points

- Zerodha Broking Ltd. (Member No. 6498) is voluntarily ceasing business in the Currency Derivative segment on BSE.
- The closure is effective from Friday, May 22, 2026.
- The closure is voluntary in nature and limited to the Currency Derivative segment.
- Trading Members of the Exchange have been officially notified via Notice No. 20260521-15.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this notice. This is an operational notification regarding a member's voluntary business decision to exit the Currency Derivative segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and clients of Zerodha who operate in the Currency Derivative segment on BSE should make alternative arrangements before May 22, 2026.
- For clarifications, members may contact BSE Membership Department at: membership.ops@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **May 22, 2026 (Friday):** Effective date of cessation of Zerodha Broking Ltd.'s business in the Currency Derivative segment on BSE.

## Impact Assessment

This closure is limited to Zerodha's participation in the BSE Currency Derivative segment. Clients of Zerodha who trade currency derivatives through BSE will need to either shift to another broker or use an alternate exchange. The broader market impact is low as currency derivative trading on BSE involves multiple other members. Zerodha may continue operations in other segments (equity, equity derivatives, etc.) on BSE and NSE unless separately notified.
