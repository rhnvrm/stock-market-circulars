## Summary

BSE has published the demat auction list for Settlement No. 795 dated January 12, 2026. This circular lists 103 securities across various sectors that are available for auction to cover short deliveries and settlement obligations. The quantities range from single shares to several thousand shares depending on the security.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 795
- Auction Date: January 12, 2026
- Total Securities Listed: 103 scrips
- Quantity Range: 1 to 9,999 shares per scrip
- Highest Quantity: JAIPRAKASH P (9999 shares), VODAFONE IDE (5800 shares), Jonjua Overs (4286 shares)
- Includes securities from banking, pharma, technology, metals, and various other sectors

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine operational circular for settlement auction process.

## Compliance Requirements

- Brokers and clearing members must participate in the auction to fulfill their short delivery obligations
- Securities listed are available for bidding through the demat auction mechanism
- Participants must follow BSE's auction settlement procedures and timelines
- Failed delivery positions must be closed out through this auction process

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: January 12, 2026
- Settlement Number: 795
- Auction execution as per BSE's standard settlement calendar

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - Affects trading members with short positions in the listed securities. The auction mechanism helps maintain market integrity by ensuring settlement of trades.

**Operational Impact:** Brokers with failed deliveries in these 103 securities must participate in the auction. Large quantities in securities like JAIPRAKASH P (9,999 shares), VODAFONE IDE (5,800 shares), and Indian Energ (2,033 shares) may see price volatility during auction.

**Investor Impact:** Investors holding these securities may experience temporary price movements due to auction buying. Settlement failures indicate liquidity constraints or delivery issues in these specific scrips.
