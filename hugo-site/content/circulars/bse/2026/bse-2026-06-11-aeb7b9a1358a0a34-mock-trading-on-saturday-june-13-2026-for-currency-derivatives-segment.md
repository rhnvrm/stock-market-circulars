## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Currency Derivatives segment on Saturday, June 13, 2026. The session will be conducted from both the primary site (PR) and disaster recovery site (DR), allowing trading members to test their platforms and ETI API integrations under simulated market conditions.

## Key Points

- Mock trading covers both Currency Derivatives and Cross Currency Derivatives
- Session runs from primary site (PR) in the morning, switching to disaster recovery site (DR) in the afternoon
- Trading members using third-party empaneled vendor platforms or in-house systems via ETI APIs are encouraged to participate
- No new BOLT TWS Release scheduled
- Trades from mock session carry no margin obligations, pay-in/pay-out obligations, or legal rights and liabilities
- Members are requested to submit feedback by 5:00 pm on June 13, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational exercise for system testing and familiarization.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are encouraged (but not mandated) to actively participate in the mock session
- Feedback should be submitted to BSE Tech Support or Help Desk by 5:00 pm on June 13, 2026
- Contact for queries: BSE Tech Support — 022-22728053 / bse.tech@bseindia.com; Help Desk — 022-45720400/600 & 022-69158500 / bsehelp@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Mock Trading Date:** Saturday, June 13, 2026
- **Log-in:** 09:15 am – 10:00 am
- **Continuous Trading – Currency Derivatives (PR):** 10:00 am – 01:00 pm
- **Continuous Trading – Cross Currency Derivatives (PR):** 10:00 am – 01:00 pm
- **Switchover to DR:** 01:00 pm – 01:45 pm
- **Continuous Trading – Currency Derivatives (DR):** 01:45 pm – 03:45 pm
- **Continuous Trading – Cross Currency Derivatives (DR):** 01:45 pm – 03:45 pm
- **Trade Modification (Currency):** 04:00 pm
- **Trade Modification (Cross Currency):** 04:00 pm
- **Feedback Deadline:** 5:00 pm, June 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a voluntary testing exercise with no financial obligations arising from mock trades. The session provides an opportunity for trading members to validate their Currency and Cross Currency Derivatives trading systems, including ETI API integrations, under simulated primary and disaster recovery site conditions. No changes to live trading rules or obligations.
