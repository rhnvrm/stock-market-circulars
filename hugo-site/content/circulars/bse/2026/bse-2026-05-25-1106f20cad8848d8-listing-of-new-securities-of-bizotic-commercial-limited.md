## Summary

BSE has listed 2,64,000 new equity shares of Bizotic Commercial Limited (Scrip Code: 543926) issued to promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. These shares are permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Tuesday, May 26, 2026.

## Key Points

- 2,64,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 280/- (issue price Rs. 290/-) to promoters on preferential basis
- Shares allotted pursuant to conversion of warrants
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 9378001 to 9642000
- ISIN: INE0OJ401014
- All 2,64,000 shares are subject to lock-in until December 30, 2027

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice pursuant to an approved preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed that the new securities are listed and available for trading from May 26, 2026. The lock-in restriction on all 2,64,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 9378001 to 9642000) must be observed until December 30, 2027.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** April 2, 2026
- **Trading effective from:** May 26, 2026
- **Lock-in expiry:** December 30, 2027

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing of a small block of promoter shares issued via warrant conversion. The shares carry a lock-in until end of 2027, so there is no near-term float addition. Market impact is minimal given the limited share count and lock-in restriction.
