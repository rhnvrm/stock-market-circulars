## Summary

BSE has issued a public notice informing trading members that Credit Suisse Securities India Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 497) has formally applied to surrender its membership of the Exchange. The notice is issued in accordance with the Exchange's Rules, Bye-laws, and Regulations, providing an opportunity for any trading member to raise objections within the stipulated timeframe.

## Key Points

- Credit Suisse Securities India Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 497) has applied to surrender its BSE membership.
- The application is being processed in accordance with BSE's Rules, Bye-laws, and Regulations.
- Trading members may raise objections in writing to the Membership Department.
- Objections must be submitted within 15 days from the date of the notice.
- Contact for clarifications: membership.ops@bseindia.com.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this notice. It is a procedural notice under existing BSE membership rules governing the voluntary surrender of exchange membership.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members who wish to object to the surrender must submit written objections to the BSE Membership Department on or before **March 18, 2026**.
- Objections must be formal and in writing; verbal or informal objections will not be considered.
- For queries, members may contact the Membership Department at membership.ops@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** March 4, 2026
- **Objection Deadline:** March 18, 2026 (15 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal broader market impact. It relates solely to the administrative exit of Credit Suisse Securities India Pvt. Ltd. from BSE membership. Counterparties or trading members with pending dealings or disputes involving Member No. 497 should review their positions and file objections if warranted before the deadline.
