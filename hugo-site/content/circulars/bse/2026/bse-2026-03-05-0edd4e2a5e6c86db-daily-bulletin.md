## Summary

BSE Daily Bulletin No. 231/2025-2026 dated March 5, 2026 lists upcoming book closure and record dates for various companies. The bulletin covers corporate actions including dividend payments, NCD/bond interest payments and partial redemptions, commercial paper redemptions, and a rights issue, scheduled between March 9 and April 13, 2026.

## Key Points

- Balmer Lawrie & Co. Ltd. (523319) has a record date of March 11, 2026 for an interim dividend of Rs. 4.25 per share (42.5%).
- BCC Fuba India Ltd. (517246) has a record date of March 11, 2026 for a rights issue of equity shares.
- Adani Enterprises Ltd. (729636) has a record date of March 12, 2026 for redemption of commercial papers.
- Balkrishna Industries Ltd. (730801) has a record date of March 12, 2026 for redemption of commercial papers.
- Damodar Valley Corporation (972826) has a record date of March 9, 2026 for payment of interest and partial redemption of bonds.
- Bilaspur Pathrapali Road Private Limited has record dates of March 13, 2026 for NCD partial redemption and interest payment.
- Multiple Auxilo Finserve Private Limited NCD series have record dates in April 2026 for interest payments.
- Multiple Dishman Carbogen Amcis Limited NCD series have record dates in March–April 2026 for NCD redemption and interest payments.
- Adani Airport Holdings Limited (977544) has a record date of March 16, 2026 for interest payment on privately placed NCDs.
- Choice Finserv Private Limited (977413) has a record date of March 11, 2026 for NCD interest payment.
- Axis Finance Limited (975899) has a record date of April 10, 2026 for NCD interest payment.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine informational bulletin.

## Compliance Requirements

- Investors and depository participants should note the record dates to ensure proper entitlement to dividends, interest payments, or redemption proceeds.
- Companies must ensure timely processing of corporate actions on or before the stated payment/allotment dates.

## Impact Assessment

This bulletin has moderate impact on holders of the listed debt and equity securities. Equity investors in Balmer Lawrie & Co. should note the interim dividend record date of March 11, 2026. BCC Fuba India shareholders should be aware of the rights issue record date.
