## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document (DRHP) of **Navkkar Gold & Diamond Limited**, filed on February 28, 2026, on the BSE SME website. The document contains all disclosures as required under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations. Public comments are invited for a minimum period of 21 days from the date of this notice.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document of Navkkar Gold & Diamond Limited is now publicly available on the BSE SME website
- The document was filed on February 28, 2026
- Disclosures comply with Schedule VI of the SEBI ICDR Regulations
- Public comments are solicited within 21 days from March 2, 2026 (deadline: on or before March 23, 2026)
- Comments to be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice is issued in compliance with existing SEBI ICDR Regulations requiring public availability of draft offer documents for SME IPOs for a minimum of 21 days prior to filing with SEBI.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Investors / Public**: May review the Draft Offer Document on the BSE SME website and submit comments, if any, to equity.ipo@bseindia.com within 21 days of this notice
- **Navkkar Gold & Diamond Limited**: Must ensure the Draft Offer Document remains publicly accessible for the stipulated period
- **BSE**: Hosting obligation fulfilled by publishing the DRHP on the SME platform

## Important Dates

- **Filing Date**: February 28, 2026
- **Notice Date**: March 2, 2026
- **Public Comment Deadline**: On or before March 23, 2026 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine procedural disclosure with limited immediate market impact. The notice is relevant to retail and institutional investors interested in the SME segment, particularly those tracking upcoming IPOs in the gold and diamond industry. Navkkar Gold & Diamond Limited's DRHP availability signals a potential SME IPO listing on BSE in the near term, subject to regulatory approvals.
