## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the equity shares of UHM Vacation Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange effective Thursday, June 11, 2026, under the 'MT' Group of Securities on the SME platform. The scrip will be placed in the Trade-for-Trade segment for 10 trading days as per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012.

## Key Points

- **Scrip Code:** 544779 | **Scrip ID (BOLT):** UHMVL
- **ISIN:** INE0YZ901011
- **Total Shares Listed:** 66,45,836 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each, fully paid up
- **Distinctive Numbers:** 1 to 66,45,836
- **Market Lot:** 800 equity shares
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 166/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- + premium Rs. 156/-)
- **Date of Allotment:** June 09, 2026
- Trading is permitted only in dematerialised form per SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000
- Special Pre-open Session (SPOS) will be held on June 11, 2026 for this scrip
- Lock-in details and shareholding pattern are provided in Annexure I and Annexure II respectively

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The listing is governed by existing SEBI framework including:
- SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 (January 20, 2012) mandating Trade-for-Trade segment for 10 trading days post-listing
- SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000 requiring dematerialised trading only
- Exchange notice 20120216-29 (February 16, 2012) governing Special Pre-open Session for IPOs

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade UHMVL only in demat form; physical settlement is not permitted
- Trades must be executed in minimum market lot of 800 equity shares; the Exchange may revise this with at least one month's prior notice
- Members should note the Trade-for-Trade restriction applies for the first 10 trading days from listing
- Lock-in periods as marked in depository records must be respected; corporate action has been executed by the company

## Important Dates

- **June 09, 2026:** Date of allotment in the public issue
- **June 11, 2026:** Effective date of listing and commencement of trading on BSE SME platform
- **June 11, 2026:** Special Pre-open Session (SPOS) for UHMVL
- **10 trading days from June 11, 2026:** Duration of Trade-for-Trade segment restriction

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine SME IPO listing with limited market-wide impact. The listing is restricted to the SME platform under 'MT' Group, and the Trade-for-Trade mechanism for the initial 10 days limits speculative activity by requiring mandatory delivery on both sides of every trade. The issue price of Rs. 166/- implies a market lot value of Rs. 1,32,800 per lot (800 shares), which is relevant for retail participant planning. Market Maker is GIRIRAJ STOCK BROKING PRIVATE LIMITED, providing liquidity support as required under SME listing norms. The Registrar is MUFG INTIME INDIA PRIVATE LIMITED. Impact is confined to traders interested in the SME segment and specifically in UHM Vacation Limited.
