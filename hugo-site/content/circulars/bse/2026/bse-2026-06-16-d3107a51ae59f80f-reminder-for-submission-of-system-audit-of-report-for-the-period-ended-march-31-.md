## Summary

BSE has issued a reminder to all trading members to submit the System Audit Report for the period ended March 31, 2026. This follows earlier notices dated April 22, 2026 (Notice No. 20260422-34) and May 11, 2026 (Notice No. 20260511-23). Submission must be completed electronically through the BEFS portal by June 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- Deadline for submission: June 30, 2026
- Submission must be made electronically via the BEFS portal
- User manuals for members (Annexure A) and auditors (Annexure B) are enclosed with the notice
- Failure to submit by the due date may result in penal or disciplinary action

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a reminder circular referencing existing requirements under Exchange Notice No. 20260422-34 (April 22, 2026) and Notice No. 20260511-23 (May 11, 2026).

## Compliance Requirements

- All trading members must submit the System Audit Report for the period ended March 31, 2026
- Submission must be done electronically through the BEFS portal
- Must be completed on or before June 30, 2026
- Members and auditors should refer to the enclosed user manuals (Annexure A and Annexure B respectively) for guidance on using the system audit module

## Important Dates

- **June 30, 2026**: Deadline for submission of System Audit Report for period ended March 31, 2026 via the BEFS portal

## Impact Assessment

This circular affects all trading members of BSE. Non-compliance with the submission deadline may attract penal or disciplinary action from the exchange. The circular has no direct market or price impact but is operationally significant for member firms' compliance and oversight functions.
