## Summary

BSE Index Services Pvt. Ltd. (BISPL) will switch its SFTP server to the Disaster Recovery (DR) site on Saturday, April 18, 2026, from 10:00 AM to 2:00 PM IST as part of its Business Continuity Planning (BCP) preparedness drill. All BISPL clients who access end-of-day (EOD) downloadable files via SFTP are requested to connect to the DR SFTP server during this window.

## Key Points

- BISPL SFTP service will run from the DR site on April 18, 2026 (Saturday), 10:00 AM – 2:00 PM IST
- DR SFTP Server IP: **103.47.198.38**, External Port: **TCP 3313**
- April 18, 2026 is a non-trading day; no new EOD files will be generated
- EOD files available on the DR SFTP server will be from the previous trading day, April 17, 2026
- Clients are urged to proactively participate in the DR test to verify connectivity and file download capability
- For issues, contact BSE Helpdesk at 022-2272-8500 or BISPL Team at 022-2272-5872 / 022-2272-8131, or email bseindex@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this notice. This is an operational drill mandated by BCP/DR readiness requirements.

## Compliance Requirements

- BISPL SFTP clients must attempt to connect to the DR SFTP server (IP: 103.47.198.38, Port: TCP 3313) during the scheduled window on April 18, 2026
- Clients should verify they can successfully download files from the DR site
- Any connectivity issues must be reported to the BSE Helpdesk or BISPL Team during the mock session

## Important Dates

- **April 18, 2026 (Saturday)**: DR Mock Session — 10:00 AM to 2:00 PM IST
- **April 17, 2026**: Reference date for EOD files available on the DR SFTP server during the drill

## Impact Assessment

Impact is minimal and limited to BISPL SFTP clients (index data consumers). Since the drill falls on a non-trading Saturday, there is no disruption to live market operations or trading activity. The exercise is a standard infrastructure resilience test. Clients who do not participate may face unpreparedness in an actual DR scenario but face no immediate operational consequences from non-participation on this date.
