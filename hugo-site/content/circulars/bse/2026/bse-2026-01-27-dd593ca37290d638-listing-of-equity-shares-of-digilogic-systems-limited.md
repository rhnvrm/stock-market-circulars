## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that equity shares of DIGILOGIC SYSTEMS LIMITED will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company will be listed on the SME segment with scrip code 544684, symbol DIGILOGIC, and ISIN INE1OOT01028. The exact listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: DIGILOGIC SYSTEMS LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544684
- Trading Symbol: DIGILOGIC
- ISIN: INE1OOT01028
- Segment: SME
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the security details and await further notice regarding the actual listing date and trading commencement.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: January 27, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard listing announcement with minimal immediate market impact. The circular serves as an informational notice to trading members about an upcoming SME listing. Trading will commence only after a separate notice is issued with the specific listing date and additional security details. No immediate action is required from market participants beyond noting the security identifiers for future reference.
