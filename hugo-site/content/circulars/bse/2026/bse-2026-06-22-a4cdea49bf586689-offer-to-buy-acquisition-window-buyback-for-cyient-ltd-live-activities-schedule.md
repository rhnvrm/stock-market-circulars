## Summary

BSE has announced the live activities schedule for the Offer to Buy (OTB) – Acquisition Window Buyback for CYIENT LTD (Scrip Code: 532175, Symbol: CYIENT, ISIN: INE136B01020). The buyback is open from Tuesday, 23rd June 2026 to Tuesday, 30th June 2026, at an offer price of Rs. 1125.00 per share, for up to 64,00,000 equity shares.

## Key Points

- **Security:** CYIENT LTD | Symbol: CYIENT | ISIN: INE136B01020 | Scrip Code: 532175
- **Offer Price:** Rs. 1125.00 per share
- **Offer Size:** Up to 64,00,000 equity shares
- **Face Value:** Rs. 5.00
- **Market Lot:** 1
- **Opening Date:** Tuesday, 23rd June 2026
- **Closing Date:** Tuesday, 30th June 2026
- **Session Timings:** 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. (Bid Offering, Modifications/Cancellation, Give-up); Take-up on the last day (30th June) extends to 4:00 p.m.
- Bidding is available across multiple investor categories: FII, FI, MF, IC, Other QIBs, NIB, Company, and Individual
- OTB module accessible via iBBS and RTRMS platforms (internet and leased line URLs provided)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice operationalizes the existing OTB framework as per Exchange Notices 20150930-1 (September 30, 2015) and 20170203-29 (February 3, 2017).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must ensure the Unique Client Code (UCC) of each investor is registered and/or updated with the correct PAN Number and Category in the Exchange UCC database.
- Trading Members may only bid for investors holding shares in demat form.
- Members and Custodians should note that if no securities are accepted, no End of Issue files will be generated on the Extranet.
- For leased line access settings, refer to Exchange Circular No. 20170503-12.

## Impact Assessment

This is an operational notice affecting trading members and custodian members of BSE who wish to participate in CYIENT LTD's buyback via the Acquisition Window. The buyback at Rs. 1125.00 per share may be of interest to institutional and individual shareholders holding CYIENT shares. Members must ensure compliance with UCC registration requirements before placing bids. The notice has no broader market or regulatory impact beyond participants in this specific buyback offer.
