## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session on Saturday, July 18, 2026, for the Equity Derivatives segment. Two separate mock sessions will be conducted: Session 1 to test changes related to the Closing Auction Session (CAS) introduced via circulars 20260319-21 and 20260610-41, and Session 2 to test general trading functionalities without CAS changes, ahead of the resumption of live trading on Monday, July 20, 2026.

## Key Points

- Two mock trading sessions scheduled on July 18, 2026 in the Equity Derivatives segment.
- Session 1 tests CAS-related changes; Session 2 tests standard trading functionality for live trading readiness.
- New BOLT Pro TWS version 13.25 will be released, with details to be published in a separate circular.
- Trades executed in Session 1 will not carry over or be visible in Session 2.
- Third-party trading application vendors and in-house system developers are encouraged to use this session to test CAS functionality.
- Mock trades do not attract margin obligations, pay-in/pay-out obligations, and create no rights or liabilities.
- Members are requested to submit feedback on the mock trading session by 1:00 pm on the day of the session.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an operational/testing notice implementing procedures previously introduced via circular no. 20260319-21 (Closing Auction Session) and 20260610-41 (Standard Operating Procedures).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members, particularly those using third-party trading applications (TWS vendors and in-house developers), should participate in the mock session to validate CAS functionality.
- Members should provide feedback on the mock trading session by 1:00 pm on July 18, 2026 via the contact numbers/email provided (BSE Tech Support: 022-22728053, bse.tech@bseindia.com; Help Desk: 022-45720400/600, 022-69158500, bsehelp@bseindia.com).

## Important Dates

- Mock Trading Session 1: July 18, 2026 — Log-in 08:30 am–09:00 am; Pre-Open 09:00 am–09:15 am; Continuous Trading 09:15 am–12:25 pm; Stock future price band update from 10:40 am.
- Mock Trading Session 2: July 18, 2026 — Log-in 02:45 pm–03:00 pm; Pre-Open 03:00 pm–03:45 pm; Continuous Trading 03:45 pm–05:30 pm.
- Feedback deadline: 1:00 pm on July 18, 2026.
- Live trading resumes: Monday, July 20, 2026.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, low-impact operational notice affecting trading members and technology vendors who must validate system readiness for the newly introduced Closing Auction Session ahead of live trading. No financial, margin, or settlement impact arises from the mock sessions themselves.
