## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260227-4 informing trading members that equity shares of Accord Transformer & Switchgear Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course under the SME segment. The specific listing date and further securities details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company: Accord Transformer & Switchgear Limited
- Scrip Code: 544710
- Symbol: ACCORDTS
- ISIN: INE132201018
- Segment: SME (Small and Medium Enterprises)
- Listing date to be announced via a separate notice
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing admission notice under BSE SME segment rules.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the upcoming listing of ACCORDTS and prepare for trading once the listing date is formally communicated. Members should monitor BSE for a follow-up notice with the exact listing date and securities details.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: February 27, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced via a separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This listing introduces a new SME-segment equity to BSE. Impact is limited to market participants interested in the SME IPO space and those tracking Accord Transformer & Switchgear Limited. Broader market impact is low. Investors should await the follow-up notice confirming the exact listing date before initiating any trades.
