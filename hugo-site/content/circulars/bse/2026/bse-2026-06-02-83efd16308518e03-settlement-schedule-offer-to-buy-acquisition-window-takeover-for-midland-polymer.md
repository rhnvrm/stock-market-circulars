## Summary

The Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd. (ICCL) has published the settlement calendar for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for Midland Polymers Ltd. The acquisition window will be open from June 3, 2026 to June 16, 2026, with final settlement on June 22, 2026.

## Key Points

- Settlement Type: Takeover (Settlement No. 2026061)
- Bidding window open from June 3, 2026 to June 16, 2026
- Obligation download date: June 19, 2026
- Final settlement date: June 22, 2026
- Demat shares must be delivered exclusively via the Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism available through both depositories (CDSL and NSDL)
- Members/Custodians must use Market Type "(Takeover)" and Settlement No. 2026061 in the depository system
- No EPN file upload is required from Members or Custodians
- CDSL & NSDL early pay-in securities will be earmarked/blocked in clients' accounts under the requisite market type and settlement number

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. Members are directed to refer to Notice No. 20200528-32 dated May 28, 2020 regarding settlement as per the Revised Guidelines of Mechanism for acquisition of shares through Stock Exchange.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and Custodians must complete early pay-in of securities in the Depository system sufficiently in advance of the prescribed BSE OTB Bidding window closure timelines
- Early pay-in effect must be reflected in BSE/ICCL systems before the prescribed timelines for accepting/confirming bids on the BSE OTB platform
- Members should refer to the MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for client-wise Stamp Duty charges
- No EPN file upload required

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Bid Start Date | June 3, 2026 |
| Bid End Date | June 16, 2026 |
| Obligation Download | June 19, 2026 |
| Settlement Date | June 22, 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

This notice is operationally relevant to stock brokers (Members) and Custodians participating in the takeover offer for Midland Polymers Ltd. The impact is limited to those entities involved in facilitating client participation in this specific acquisition window. There is no broader market-wide impact. Participants must ensure timely early pay-in of shares to avoid bid rejection due to timing issues on the BSE OTB platform.
