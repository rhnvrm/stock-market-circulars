## Summary

BSE has issued a notice suspending trading in T-Bill 759CG2026 (Scrip Code: 800242, ISIN: IN0020150093) effective January 7, 2026, as the instrument is maturing on its redemption date. Trading members are advised not to deal in this T-Bill from the effective date.

## Key Points

- T-Bill 759CG2026 with Scrip Code 800242 will mature on redemption date
- ISIN: IN0020150093
- Trading suspension effective from January 7, 2026
- Notice issued under reference DR-793/2025-2026
- Trading members instructed to cease dealing in this instrument

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for instrument maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in T-Bill 759CG2026 (Scrip Code: 800242) from January 7, 2026 onwards
- Members must update their trading systems to reflect the suspension
- Ensure clients are informed about the trading suspension

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 6, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date**: January 7, 2026
- **T-Bill Maturity**: Redemption date (specific date not mentioned in circular)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low. This is a routine administrative action affecting a single T-Bill instrument reaching maturity. Only holders of this specific T-Bill are impacted.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal. Standard end-of-life process for debt instruments. Trading members need to update systems to prevent trades in this scrip code.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to holders of T-Bill 759CG2026 who will receive redemption proceeds as per standard maturity procedures.
