## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 68,75,92,710 new equity shares of Integra Essentia Limited (Scrip Code 535958) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Tuesday, June 16, 2026. The shares were issued on a rights basis at a face value of Re. 1/- each at a premium of Re. 0.45/-.

## Key Points

- **Company:** Integra Essentia Limited (Scrip Code: 535958)
- **New Shares Listed:** 68,75,92,710 equity shares of Re. 1/- each
- **Issue Price:** Re. 1/- face value + Re. 0.45/- premium (total Re. 1.45/- per share)
- **Basis of Issue:** Rights issue
- **ISIN:** INE418N01035
- **Distinctive Numbers:** 1067690545 to 1755283254
- **Date of Allotment:** June 11, 2026
- **Trading Commencement:** June 16, 2026
- The new shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notice under BSE's Listing Operations department.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to update their systems to reflect the listing of the new securities under ISIN INE418N01035 effective June 16, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** June 11, 2026
- **Trading Commencement Date:** June 16, 2026 (Tuesday)

## Impact Assessment

The listing of these rights issue shares represents a significant increase in the share count of Integra Essentia Limited. The issuance at Re. 1.45/- per share (face value Re. 1/- + premium Re. 0.45/-) on a rights basis reflects a capital-raising exercise by the company. The new shares are pari-passu with existing shares, so no differential rights are introduced. Market impact is limited to existing shareholders who participated in the rights issue; trading in these shares begins June 16, 2026.
