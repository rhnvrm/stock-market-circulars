## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in debt securities of 12 companies effective January 27, 2025. The suspension is due to redemption of securities or delisting applications made by the respective companies. Trading members are advised to cease all dealings in these specified debt securities.

## Key Points

- 12 debt securities across various trusts and companies to be suspended
- Suspension reason: Redemption or delisting applications
- Trading members must not deal in these securities from the effective date
- Notice issued under reference DR-806/2025-2026

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the listed debt securities

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** January 23, 2026
- **Effective Date:** January 27, 2025 (Note: Date appears to be January 27, 2026 based on notice date)

## Impact Assessment

The suspension affects holders and potential traders of these specific debt securities. Impact is limited to debt market segment and specific to the 12 securities listed. Investors holding these securities should expect no trading activity post-suspension date. The action is routine for securities undergoing redemption or delisting processes.
