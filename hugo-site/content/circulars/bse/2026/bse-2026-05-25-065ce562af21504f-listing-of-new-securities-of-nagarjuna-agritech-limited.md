## Summary

BSE has listed 2,12,86,020 new equity shares of Nagarjuna Agritech Limited (Scrip Code: 531832, ISIN: INE793H01017) issued on a preferential basis to promoter and non-promoter shareholders. The shares are permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Tuesday, May 26, 2026. The shares were allotted on March 20, 2026 at an issue price of Rs. 68/- each (face value Rs. 10/- + premium of Rs. 58/-).

## Key Points

- **Total new shares listed:** 2,12,86,020 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each
- **Issue price:** Rs. 68/- per share (Rs. 10/- face value + Rs. 58/- premium)
- **Issued to:** Promoter and Non-Promoter on a preferential basis
- **Date of allotment:** March 20, 2026
- **Effective trading date:** May 26, 2026
- **Distribution numbers:** 9369101 to 30655120
- **Pari-passu ranking:** These shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice pursuant to a completed preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed of the new securities and permitted to facilitate trading from May 26, 2026. The lock-in restrictions must be observed for the applicable tranches.

## Important Dates

- **Date of allotment:** March 20, 2026
- **Trading permitted from:** May 26, 2026
- **Lock-in expiry (Tranche 1):** January 31, 2027 — 1,56,18,030 shares (Dist. Nos. 14212376–29830405)
- **Lock-in expiry (Tranche 2):** January 31, 2027 — 5,77,215 shares (Dist. Nos. 30077906–30655120)
- **Lock-in expiry (Tranche 3):** January 31, 2028 — 48,43,275 shares (Dist. Nos. 9369101–14212375)
- **Lock-in expiry (Tranche 4):** January 31, 2028 — 2,47,500 shares (Dist. Nos. 29830406–30077905)

## Impact Assessment

The listing of approximately 2.13 crore new equity shares represents a meaningful addition to the tradeable float of Nagarjuna Agritech Limited. However, the bulk of shares are subject to lock-in until January 2027 or January 2028, limiting immediate selling pressure. The preferential allotment at Rs. 68/- provides a reference price point for market participants. Existing shareholders should note the dilution effect from this new issuance.
