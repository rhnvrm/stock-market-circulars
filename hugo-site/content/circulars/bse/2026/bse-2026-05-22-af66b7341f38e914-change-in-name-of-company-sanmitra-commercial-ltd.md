## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that Sanmitra Commercial Ltd (Scrip Code: 512062) has officially changed its name to Tandhan Industries Limited. The SCRIP ID and abbreviated name on the BOLT Plus System will be updated accordingly effective May 29, 2026.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code 512062 previously traded as Sanmitra Commercial Ltd.
- New company name: Tandhan Industries Limited.
- New SCRIP ID on BOLT Plus System: TANDHANIN.
- New Abbreviated Name on BOLT Plus System: TANDHANIN.
- Change is effective from May 29, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory rule changes. This is an administrative update to reflect the company's legally approved name change via a fresh Certificate of Incorporation.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should update their records and systems to reflect the new company name, SCRIP ID (TANDHANIN), and abbreviated name (TANDHANIN) for Scrip Code 512062 ahead of the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** May 22, 2026
- **Effective Date of Name Change on BOLT Plus System:** May 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a purely administrative change affecting only the display name and symbol identifier for a single equity scrip. Traders holding or tracking Scrip Code 512062 should note the updated identifiers to avoid confusion in order entry and reporting.
