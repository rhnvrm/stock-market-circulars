## Summary

BSE notifies the listing and admission to trading of Motilal Oswal Nifty Smallcap 250 ETF units effective January 19, 2026. The ETF is an open-ended scheme tracking the Nifty Smallcap 250 TR Index with 4,200,000 units listed in the B Group. Trading will be conducted in dematerialized form only with a market lot of 1 unit.

## Key Points

- ETF Name: Motilal Oswal Nifty Smallcap 250 ETF
- Fund Manager: Motilal Oswal Asset Management Company Limited
- Scrip Code: 590148
- ISIN: INF247L01CH6
- BOLT System ID: MOSMALL250
- Number of Units: 4,200,000
- Face Value: Rs. 10.00 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 16.34 per unit
- Date of Allotment: March 15, 2024
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Group: B
- Three designated market makers: East India Securities Ltd., Motilal Oswal Financial Services Limited, and Kanjalochana Finserve Private Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This notice implements existing SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000, requiring mutual fund units to trade only in dematerialized form.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all trades in this ETF are executed in dematerialized form only
- Trades must be effected in unit market lot (1 unit minimum)
- Trading members should note the designated market makers for liquidity provision

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 16, 2026
- **Allotment Date**: March 15, 2024
- **Trading Commencement Date**: January 19, 2026 (Monday)

## Impact Assessment

Routine operational notice with minimal market impact. The listing expands investment options in the smallcap segment through a passively managed ETF tracking the Nifty Smallcap 250 TR Index. Impact is limited to mutual fund segment participants and investors seeking exposure to small-cap equities. The presence of three market makers ensures adequate liquidity. No immediate action required from trading members beyond system updates for the new scrip.
