## Summary

Aureate Tradde Limited's equity shares will be listed and admitted to dealings on BSE effective Friday, June 5, 2026, under the 'MT' Group of Securities on the SME segment. As per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012, the scrip will remain in the Trade-for-Trade segment for the first 10 trading days.

## Key Points

- **Scrip Code:** 544772 | **Scrip ID:** AUREATE | **ISIN:** INE1KVL01010
- **Total Securities:** 1,29,85,736 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each, fully paid up
- **Distinctive Number Range:** 1 to 12985736
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 70/- per share (Face Value Rs. 10/- + Premium Rs. 60/-)
- **Market Lot:** 2,000 equity shares
- **Date of Allotment:** June 3, 2026
- **Listing Date:** June 5, 2026 (Friday)
- **Trade-for-Trade Segment:** Applicable for 10 trading days from listing
- **Special Pre-open Session (SPOS):** Applicable on June 5, 2026
- Trading permitted only in dematerialised form

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. The listing is governed by existing SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 (dated January 20, 2012) mandating Trade-for-Trade for the first 10 trading days and SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000 requiring trading in dematerialised form only.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade this scrip only in dematerialised form.
- Trades must be executed in minimum market lot of 2,000 equity shares.
- Trading members should refer to Exchange notice no. 20120216-29 for SPOS operational details.
- Lock-in details as per Annexure I and shareholding pattern as per Annexure II of the circular.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** June 3, 2026
- **Listing & Trading Commencement:** June 5, 2026 (Friday)
- **Trade-for-Trade Period:** 10 trading days from June 5, 2026
- **Special Pre-open Session (SPOS):** June 5, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard SME IPO listing with limited broad market impact. The Trade-for-Trade restriction for the first 10 trading days means no netting of positions is allowed, increasing settlement risk awareness for trading members. The market lot of 2,000 shares at Rs. 70/- implies a minimum trade value of Rs. 1,40,000, restricting retail participation. Market Maker is Giriraj Stock Broking Private Limited, and the Registrar is MUFG Intime India Private Limited (formerly Link Intime). Investors with lock-in obligations should refer to Annexure I for details.
