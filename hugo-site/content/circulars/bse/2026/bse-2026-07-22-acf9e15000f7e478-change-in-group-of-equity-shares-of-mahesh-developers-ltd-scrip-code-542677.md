## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the equity shares of Mahesh Developers Ltd (Scrip Code: 542677) will be moved into the 'X' trading group with effect from July 29, 2026.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code 542677 (Mahesh Developers Ltd) will be included under the 'X' group.
- The change takes effect from July 29, 2026.
- No attachments were provided with this notice.
- Queries can be directed to bse.revocation@bseindia.com.

## Regulatory Changes

This is a reclassification of the trading group for the specified equity scrip under BSE's Listing Compliance-Monitoring department, rather than a change to broader regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members dealing in this scrip should update their systems and trading practices to reflect the new group classification effective July 29, 2026. Any clarifications should be sought via the designated email address.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 22, 2026
- Effective Date of Group Change: July 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Inclusion in the 'X' group generally denotes a trade-to-trade settlement segment on BSE, which restricts intraday trading and requires actual delivery for settlement. This move typically reflects heightened surveillance or risk concerns for the stock and may reduce trading volumes and liquidity for Mahesh Developers Ltd shares, though the impact is confined to this specific counter rather than the broader market.
