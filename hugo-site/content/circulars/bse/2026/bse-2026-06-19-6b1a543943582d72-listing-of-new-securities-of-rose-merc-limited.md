## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 2,25,583 new equity shares of Rose Merc. Limited (Scrip Code: 512115, ISIN: INE649C01012) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Monday, June 22, 2026. The shares were issued at Rs. 90/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- + premium of Rs. 80/-) to Promoters and Non-Promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 2,25,583 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 80/- (issue price Rs. 90/-)
- Issued on a preferential basis to Promoters and Non-Promoters via conversion of warrants
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Listing effective: Monday, June 22, 2026
- ISIN: INE649C01012 | Scrip Code: 512115

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for newly allotted equity shares following warrant conversion.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the listing of the new securities and update their systems accordingly. Lock-in details for the newly listed shares are provided in Annexure A attached to the circular.

## Impact Assessment

The listing of 2,25,583 new shares represents a modest increase in the equity share capital of Rose Merc. Limited. The shares were issued through warrant conversion at Rs. 90/- per share, indicating prior investor commitments at that price level. Lock-in restrictions apply to certain allottees (details in Annexure A), which limits near-term selling pressure from those shareholders. The impact is primarily relevant to existing shareholders and active traders of this scrip.
