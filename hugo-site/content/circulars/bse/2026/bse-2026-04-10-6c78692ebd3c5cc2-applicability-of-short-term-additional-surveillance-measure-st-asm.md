## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260410-47 placing specified securities under the Short Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) framework under Stage I and Stage II classifications. Enhanced margin requirements will be applicable from April 15, 2026, affecting both existing open positions as of April 13, 2026, and all new positions thereafter.

## Key Points

- Securities listed in Annexure I have satisfied criteria for inclusion under ST-ASM Stage I with a 50% margin requirement (or existing margin, whichever is higher, capped at 100%).
- A separate set of securities in Annexure I have satisfied criteria for ST-ASM Stage II with a 100% margin requirement (or existing margin, whichever is higher).
- The ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the exchanges.
- Inclusion under ASM is purely a market surveillance action and should not be construed as an adverse action against the concerned company or entity.
- Securities moving out of the framework will have their price band reinstated to the pre-shortlisting level, unless covered by another surveillance measure.

## Regulatory Changes

This notice is issued further to prior exchange notices:
- 20240920-71 dated September 20, 2024
- 20181027-1 dated October 27, 2018
- 20190719-33 dated July 19, 2019
- 20201204-56 dated December 4, 2020
- 20230925-49 dated September 25, 2023
- 20240920-63 dated September 20, 2024

These notices collectively established the ASM framework and its revisions. The current notice applies the existing framework to newly shortlisted securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure applicable margin rates are collected: 50% (minimum) for ST-ASM Stage I securities and 100% for ST-ASM Stage II securities.
- Margins apply to all open positions as on April 13, 2026, and all new positions from April 15, 2026.
- Members must refer to the attached Annexure I for the complete list of securities under each stage.
- For clarifications, members may contact BSE Surveillance at bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **April 10, 2026**: Notice date; securities shortlisted under ST-ASM.
- **April 13, 2026**: Reference date for existing open positions subject to revised margin requirements.
- **April 15, 2026**: Effective date for enhanced margin requirements under both ST-ASM Stage I and Stage II.

## Impact Assessment

Traders and investors holding positions in the shortlisted securities will face significantly higher margin requirements from April 15, 2026. Stage I securities require at least 50% margin (up to 100%), while Stage II securities require 100% margin, substantially increasing the cost of carrying positions. This may trigger forced liquidations for undercapitalized accounts and reduce liquidity in the affected scrips. Market participants should review the Annexure I list immediately to identify affected holdings and ensure adequate margin coverage ahead of the April 15, 2026 deadline.
