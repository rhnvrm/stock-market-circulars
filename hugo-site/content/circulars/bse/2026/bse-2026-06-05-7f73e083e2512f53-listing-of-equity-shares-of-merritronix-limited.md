## Summary

Merritronix Limited, an SME company headquartered in Hyderabad, Telangana, will be listed on BSE under the MT Group of Securities effective Monday, June 8, 2026. The scrip (code: 544773, ID: MRTX) will be placed in the Trade-for-Trade segment for 10 trading days as per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012.

## Key Points

- **Listing Date:** Monday, June 8, 2026
- **Scrip Code:** 544773 | **Scrip ID (BOLT):** MRTX
- **ISIN:** INE1RQS01010
- **Group:** MT Group of Securities (SME segment)
- **Trade-for-Trade:** Yes, for 10 trading days from listing
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 149/- per share (Face Value Rs. 10/- + Premium Rs. 139/-)
- **Total Securities:** 17,484,854 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each, fully paid up
- **Distinctive Number Range:** 1 to 17,484,854
- **Market Lot:** 1,000 equity shares
- **Financial Year End:** March 31
- **Date of Allotment:** June 4, 2026
- **Pari Passu:** Yes
- Trading is permitted only in dematerialised form
- The scrip will participate in Special Pre-open Session (SPOS) on June 8, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. Listing is conducted under the existing SEBI framework for SME IPOs, including the Trade-for-Trade requirement per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 (January 20, 2012) and the dematerialisation requirement per SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade this scrip only in dematerialised form
- Trades must be executed in minimum market lots of 1,000 equity shares; the Exchange may modify this lot size with at least one month's prior notice
- Trading members must participate in the Special Pre-open Session (SPOS) on June 8, 2026, per Exchange notice 20120216-29
- Lock-in details as per Annexure I and shareholding pattern as per Annexure II must be observed
- Market Maker: Giriraj Stock Broking Private Limited (SEBI Reg. No. INZ000212638, Market Maker Reg. No. 6551)
- Registrar: Bigshare Services Private Limited (SEBI Reg. No. INR000001385)

## Important Dates

- **June 4, 2026:** Date of allotment in the public issue
- **June 8, 2026:** Effective listing date on BSE SME platform; SPOS participation required
- **10 trading days from June 8, 2026:** Trade-for-Trade segment restriction period

## Impact Assessment

This listing is limited in broader market impact as it involves a small-cap SME company. However, trading members dealing in SME securities should note the Trade-for-Trade restriction for the first 10 trading days, which means no netting of positions and compulsory delivery on both sides. The SPOS requirement on listing day is standard for IPO/relisted scrips. Investors should be aware of the higher market lot size (1,000 shares) compared to mainboard listings, and the absence of intraday trading during the Trade-for-Trade period.
