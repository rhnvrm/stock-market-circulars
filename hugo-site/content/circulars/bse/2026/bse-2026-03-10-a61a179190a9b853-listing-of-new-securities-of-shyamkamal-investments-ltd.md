## Summary

BSE has listed 1,29,15,000 new equity shares of Shyamkamal Investments Ltd (Scrip Code: 505515, ISIN: INE203N01015), issued at par (Rs. 10/-) to non-promoters on a preferential basis. These shares are permitted to trade on BSE with effect from Wednesday, March 11, 2026.

## Key Points

- 1,29,15,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at par to non-promoters on a preferential basis
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 13498001 to 26413000
- Date of allotment: December 12, 2025
- Issue price: Rs. 10/- per share
- ISIN: INE203N01015
- All 1,29,15,000 shares are subject to lock-in until September 14, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for newly allotted securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities and their eligibility to trade from March 11, 2026
- Lock-in restrictions apply to all 1,29,15,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 13498001 to 26413000) until September 14, 2026; these shares cannot be traded until the lock-in period expires

## Important Dates

- Date of allotment: December 12, 2025
- Trading commencement on BSE: March 11, 2026
- Lock-in expiry date: September 14, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. The listing increases the total share count of Shyamkamal Investments Ltd by 1,29,15,000 shares, representing a preferential allotment to non-promoters at face value. All newly listed shares are under lock-in until September 14, 2026, so there is no immediate free-float addition. Relevant only to existing shareholders and trading members dealing in Scrip Code 505515.
