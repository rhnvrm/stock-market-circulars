## Summary

BSE has issued a notice to all trading members updating them on the front-running matter involving Ashok Maheshwari and others, in respect of CHL Stock Concepts Pvt. Ltd and Chirag Mahendra Shah. The Securities Appellate Tribunal (SAT), vide its order dated June 22, 2026, noted that the appellants deposited the disgorgement amount of Rs. 21.74 lakhs and undertook to deposit the penalty amount of Rs. 41 lakhs, resulting in a stay of debarment. SEBI has since confirmed via email dated July 13, 2026 that the noticees have paid 100% of the penalty amount, and accordingly the debarment imposed under the SEBI Final Order dated April 27, 2026 remains stayed as per the SAT Order.

## Key Points

- This notice is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20260427-33 dated April 27, 2026 on the same matter.
- SAT order dated June 22, 2026 recorded deposit of Rs. 21.74 lakhs (disgorgement) and an undertaking to deposit Rs. 41 lakhs (penalty).
- The deposited amount is to be placed in an interest-bearing account.
- SAT disposed of Stay Application No. 486 of 2026 and granted a stay of debarment.
- SEBI confirmed on July 13, 2026 that 100% of the penalty amount has been paid by the noticees.
- The debarment imposed under the SEBI Final Order dated April 27, 2026 remains stayed per the SAT Order.
- Trading members are advised to take note and ensure compliance.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory rule changes; this is a case-specific enforcement update reflecting SAT's stay of a SEBI debarment order pending final adjudication, contingent on payment of disgorgement and penalty amounts.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to take note of the stay of debarment concerning Ashok Maheshwari and others in relation to CHL Stock Concepts Pvt. Ltd and Chirag Mahendra Shah, and ensure compliance with the updated status.

## Important Dates

- April 27, 2026: Original SEBI Final Order and Exchange Notice No. 20260427-33.
- June 22, 2026: SAT Order granting stay of debarment.
- July 13, 2026: SEBI email confirming full penalty payment; date of this notice.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to the specific individuals and entity named (Ashok Maheshwari and others, CHL Stock Concepts Pvt. Ltd, Chirag Mahendra Shah) and does not affect listed securities, trading operations, or the broader market. It serves as a compliance and awareness update for trading members regarding the current debarment status of these parties under SEBI/SAT proceedings related to front-running.
