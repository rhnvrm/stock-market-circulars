## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260310-10 informs trading members that Vertex Securities Ltd. (BSE Code: 531950) has fixed a record date of March 12, 2026 for a rights issue. The company will issue 1 new equity share of Rs.2/- each for every 1 equity share held. Trading in the equity shares must be conducted on an ex-rights basis effective from March 12, 2026 under Settlement No. DR-828/2025-2026.

## Key Points

- Company: Vertex Securities Ltd. (BSE Code: 531950)
- Rights ratio: 1 new equity share for every 1 existing equity share held (1:1)
- Face value: Rs.2/- per share, issued at par (cash at par)
- Record date: March 12, 2026
- Ex-rights date: March 12, 2026 (Settlement No. DR-828/2025-2026)
- Segment: Dematerialised Securities – Rolling Settlement Segment
- Payment structure: Rs.0.50 per share payable on application; Rs.1.50 payable in not more than two subsequent calls

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notification under BSE's existing framework for rights issues and ex-entitlement trading.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all transactions in Vertex Securities Ltd. equity shares are executed on an ex-rights basis from March 12, 2026 onwards.
- Members should update their systems and notify clients accordingly to avoid cum-rights trading errors post the ex-rights date.

## Important Dates

- **Record Date:** March 12, 2026
- **Ex-Rights Effective Date:** March 12, 2026
- **Settlement Number:** DR-828/2025-2026
- **Circular Date:** March 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The rights issue has a moderate impact on existing shareholders of Vertex Securities Ltd. as it doubles the share count on a 1:1 ratio at par value. The split payment structure (Rs.0.50 on application, Rs.1.50 in calls) provides flexibility for shareholders. Trading members must adjust trading systems to reflect ex-rights pricing from March 12, 2026. Broader market impact is limited given the company's scale.
