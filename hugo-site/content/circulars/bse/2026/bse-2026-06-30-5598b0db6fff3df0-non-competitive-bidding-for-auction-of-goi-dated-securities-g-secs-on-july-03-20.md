## Summary

BSE has announced the non-competitive bidding window for the Government of India's auction of four dated securities on July 03, 2026, as per the RBI press release dated June 29, 2026. Trading members and direct investors can participate via the NCB-GSec module on BSE's iBBS web-based platform.

## Key Points

- The GoI will auction four dated securities on July 03, 2026; one security is specifically detailed: **6.94% GS 2036** (Symbol: 694GS36J26, re-issue)
- Bidding is facilitated through BSE's NCB-GSec module at https://ibbs.bseindia.com
- Issue size for 6.94% GS 2036 is Rs. 34,000 crores
- Minimum subscription is 100 units; minimum bid amount is Rs. 10,000 with no stated maximum
- This notice follows Exchange Circular No. 20180423-42 dated April 23, 2018, which launched the non-competitive bidding facility for RBI-conducted G-sec auctions

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine operational notice under the existing non-competitive bidding framework established by BSE circular 20180423-42.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the NCB-GSec module on iBBS (https://ibbs.bseindia.com) to submit bids
- Members needing new user IDs or password resets must contact Trading Operations at user.trdops@bseindia.com or 022-2272-8005/8215
- Bidding and obligation-related queries should be directed to respective BSE/ICCL teams as listed in the circular

## Important Dates

| Event | Date/Time |
|---|---|
| Bid Collection Start | June 30, 2026, 10:00 am onwards (24-hour availability) |
| Bid Collection End (Members) | July 03, 2026, till 8:00 am |
| Bid Collection End (Direct Investors) | July 02, 2026, till 5:00 pm |
| Auction Date | July 03, 2026 |
| Settlement Date | July 06, 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

This circular is operationally relevant to debt segment trading members and direct investors wishing to participate in the GoI G-sec auction. The 6.94% GS 2036 re-issue with a large issue size of Rs. 34,000 crores indicates significant government borrowing activity. Participation is voluntary; members not intending to bid are unaffected. Settlement falls on July 06, 2026, which debt desk operations teams should account for in their liquidity planning.
