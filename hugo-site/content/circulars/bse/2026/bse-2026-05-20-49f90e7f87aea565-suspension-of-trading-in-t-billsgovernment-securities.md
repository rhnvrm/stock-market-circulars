## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (20260520-3) informing trading members that government security 798GJSDL26 (Scrip Code: 810592, ISIN: IN1520160046) is maturing on its redemption date. Trading in this instrument is suspended effective May 21, 2026 (DR-635/2026-2027).

## Key Points

- Scrip Code 810592 (798GJSDL26, ISIN: IN1520160046) will mature on its redemption date
- Trading members are advised not to deal in this security from May 21, 2026 onwards
- Reference: DR-635/2026-2027
- Notice issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President – Listing Compliance & Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational notice implementing suspension of trading upon maturity of a government security under existing BSE debt segment rules.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in Scrip Code 810592 (ISIN: IN1520160046) with effect from May 21, 2026
- Members holding or intending to trade this security must ensure no transactions are executed on or after the effective date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** May 20, 2026
- **Suspension Effective Date:** May 21, 2026 (Redemption/Maturity Date)

## Impact Assessment

This suspension affects only trading members dealing in the specific government security 798GJSDL26. The impact is limited to holders and traders of this single instrument. As this is a scheduled maturity event, the suspension is routine and expected. Members with open positions should ensure settlement is completed prior to the effective date.
