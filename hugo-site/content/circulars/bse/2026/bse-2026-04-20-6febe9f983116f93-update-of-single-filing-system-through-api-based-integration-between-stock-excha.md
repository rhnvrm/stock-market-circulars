## Summary

BSE has extended the single filing system through API-based integration between stock exchanges to include Annual Report XBRL filings, effective April 20, 2026. This builds on the earlier notice 20240930-60 dated September 30, 2024, and expands the range of disclosures that listed entities can submit once across exchanges.

## Key Points

- The single filing system via API-based integration now includes Annual Report XBRL filings (Regulation 34, SEBI LODR) effective April 20, 2026.
- Applicable to equity-listed and equity+debt listed companies; not applicable to exclusively debt-listed companies, REITs, or InvITs.
- Disclosures for Acquisition/Amalgamation/Merger/De-merger/Sale or disposal/Restructuring (Regulation 30, Schedule III Part A) are included.
- Changes in directors, KMP (MD, CEO, CFO, Company Secretary, etc.), senior management, auditors, compliance officers, and registrars are covered.
- Resignation of Statutory Auditor, Independent Director, Director, KMP, SMP, and Compliance Officer disclosures are included.
- Business Responsibility and Sustainability Report (BRSR) under Regulation 34(2)(f) was available from March 31, 2026.
- Prior Intimation for Board Meetings under Regulation 29 was available from March 7 (date truncated in source).
- The utility provides an option to submit resignation details and resignation letters as required under sub-paras 7A, 7B, and 7C of Para A Part A Schedule III of SEBI LODR.

## Regulatory Changes

- Annual Report XBRL filing added to the single filing system under Regulation 34 of SEBI LODR, effective April 20, 2026.
- All listed equity companies (equity-only and equity+debt) must use the integrated API-based system for the covered disclosures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Company Secretaries and listed entities should use the single filing system for all covered disclosures instead of filing separately on each exchange.
- Entities must ensure their systems are compatible with the API-based integration for submitting XBRL annual reports and event-based disclosures.
- Resignation-related filings must include resignation letters as required under sub-paras 7A, 7B, and 7C of Schedule III.

## Important Dates

- **April 20, 2026**: Annual Report XBRL filing added to single filing system.
- **March 31, 2026**: BRSR filing made available on the single filing system.
- **March 7, 2026** (approximate): Prior Intimation for Board Meetings under Regulation 29 made available.
- **September 30, 2024**: Original single filing system notice (20240930-60) issued.

## Impact Assessment

This update reduces the administrative burden on listed entities by enabling a single submission for key regulatory disclosures across stock exchanges. The inclusion of XBRL annual reports is significant as it streamlines a high-volume, periodic filing. The impact is primarily operational — improving efficiency for compliance teams and Company Secretaries. No new compliance obligations are introduced; existing SEBI LODR requirements remain unchanged. Exclusively debt-listed companies, REITs, and InvITs are not affected by this extension.
