## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 24,87,100 new equity shares of Blue Coast Hotels Ltd (Scrip Code: 531495, ISIN: INE472B01011) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Wednesday, June 24, 2026. These shares were issued at par (Rs. 10/- each) to Promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to the conversion of Compulsorily Convertible Preference Shares (CCPS).

## Key Points

- 24,87,100 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at par to Promoters via preferential allotment
- Shares issued pursuant to conversion of Compulsorily Convertible Preference Shares (CCPS)
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 17192458 to 19679557
- Date of allotment: May 13, 2026
- ISIN: INE472B01011
- Entire allotment of 24,87,100 shares is subject to lock-in until December 30, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification under BSE Listing Operations following completion of a preferential allotment and CCPS conversion.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed that the new securities are available for trading from June 24, 2026
- The 24,87,100 shares (dist. nos. 17192458 to 19679557) are under lock-in and cannot be traded until December 30, 2026
- Members should update their systems to reflect the expanded equity share capital of Blue Coast Hotels Ltd

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** May 13, 2026
- **Listing / Trading Effective Date:** June 24, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry:** December 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 24,87,100 equity shares to the tradeable capital of Blue Coast Hotels Ltd; however, since all newly listed shares are under promoter lock-in until December 30, 2026, there is no immediate increase in free-float. Market impact is minimal in the near term. Post lock-in expiry, the additional shares could modestly expand available supply for trading.
