## Summary

BSE has announced the launch of a New Fund Offer (NFO) for Specialized Investment Funds (SIF) with Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) facility from The Wealth Company Mutual Fund on the BSE StAR MF platform. This notice (No. 20260414-3) is issued by the Trading Operations department under the Mutual Fund segment and is accompanied by an annexure containing further details of the offering.

## Key Points

- The Wealth Company Mutual Fund is launching a New Fund Offer (NFO) for Specialized Investment Funds (SIF) on BSE StAR MF.
- The NFO includes a SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) facility, allowing investors to invest in instalments.
- The circular is categorized under Trading and the Mutual Fund segment.
- An annexure is attached with additional details of the fund(s) being offered.
- Notice issued by BSE Trading Operations department on 14 April 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This notice is an operational announcement enabling the transactional availability of The Wealth Company Mutual Fund's SIF NFO on the BSE StAR MF platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- Mutual fund distributors and registered participants on BSE StAR MF should refer to the attached annexure for fund-specific details including scheme codes, SIP parameters, and applicable dates.
- Participants must ensure transactions are processed in accordance with BSE StAR MF platform guidelines and SEBI regulations governing Specialized Investment Funds.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 14 April 2026
- NFO subscription period and SIP commencement dates to be confirmed per the attached annexure.

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a medium operational impact for BSE StAR MF participants, distributors, and investors looking to subscribe to Specialized Investment Funds from The Wealth Company Mutual Fund. SIF products are SEBI-regulated structures targeted at sophisticated investors. The availability of SIP facility broadens access and investment flexibility. No impact on equity market participants or listed securities.
