## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Dolat Capital Market Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 173) will voluntarily cease business operations in the Currency Derivative segment effective Friday, April 24, 2026.

## Key Points

- Dolat Capital Market Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 173) is voluntarily closing its business in the Currency Derivative segment.
- Cessation of business is effective from April 24, 2026.
- The closure is voluntary in nature.
- For clarifications, members may contact BSE Membership Department at Membership.ops@bseindia.com.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative notice regarding the exit of a single trading member from the Currency Derivative segment.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance action required from other trading members. Members with pending transactions or relationships with Dolat Capital Market Pvt. Ltd. in the Currency Derivative segment should take note of the effective closure date.

## Important Dates

- **April 24, 2026**: Dolat Capital Market Pvt. Ltd. ceases business in the Currency Derivative segment.

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The voluntary exit of a single member from the Currency Derivative segment is unlikely to affect broader market operations. Other trading members and investors should ensure no outstanding obligations exist with Dolat Capital Market Pvt. Ltd. in this segment prior to the effective date.
