## Summary

BSE has announced shut periods for Securities Lending and Borrowing (SLB) operations across 22 securities due to dividend corporate actions. The shut periods range from February 10-18, 2026, with corresponding record dates and ex-dates. During these shut periods, SLB transactions will be restricted for the affected securities.

## Key Points

- 22 companies have declared dividends with record dates between February 11-18, 2026
- SLB shut periods implemented one day before record date through the record date itself
- Ex-dates align with record dates for all listed securities
- No foreclosure dates specified for any of the securities
- Shut periods vary by company based on individual record dates

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine operational notification regarding SLB restrictions during corporate actions.

## Compliance Requirements

- SLB participants must cease lending/borrowing activities for affected securities during specified shut periods
- Market participants should plan their SLB strategies accounting for these restrictions
- No new positions in SLB can be initiated during shut periods for these securities

## Important Dates

**February 10-11, 2026:** Page Industries Ltd (Shut Period)
- Record/Ex Date: February 11, 2026

**February 11-12, 2026:** Procter & Gamble Health, SUN TV Network, Star Cement, Goldiam International, Gateway Distriparks (Shut Period)
- Record/Ex Date: February 12, 2026

**February 12-13, 2026:** BEML, M.R.F., Dynamatic Technologies, HBL Engineering, NRB Bearings, Sonata Software, Suprajit Engineering, K.P.R. Mill, Gulf Oil Lubricants, S H Kelkar, Mahanagar Gas, BLS International (Shut Period)
- Record/Ex Date: February 13, 2026

**February 13-16, 2026:** Aarti Pharmalabs (Shut Period)
- Record/Ex Date: February 16, 2026

**February 16-17, 2026:** Shipping Corporation Of India, International Gemmological Institute India (Shut Period)
- Record/Ex Date: February 17, 2026

**February 17-18, 2026:** Oil And Natural Gas Corporation (Shut Period)
- Record/Ex Date: February 18, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - affects liquidity in SLB segment for 22 securities across multiple sectors including oil & gas, FMCG, automobiles, media, cement, and pharmaceuticals.

**Operational Impact:** Participants in securities lending and borrowing must adjust their positions ahead of shut periods. Short sellers using SLB mechanism need to account for restricted borrowing windows.

**Sectoral Spread:** Diverse sector representation minimizes concentrated impact on any single industry segment.
