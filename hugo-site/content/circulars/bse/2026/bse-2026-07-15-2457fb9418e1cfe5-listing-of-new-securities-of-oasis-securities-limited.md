## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that new equity shares of Oasis Securities Limited (Scrip Code - 512489), issued on a rights basis, are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Thursday, July 16, 2026.

## Key Points

- 2,77,50,000 equity shares of Rs. 1/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 9/- per share on rights basis
- ISIN: INE876A01023
- Distinctive numbers: 18500001 to 46250000
- Date of allotment: 13/07/2026
- New shares rank pari-passu with the existing equity shares of the company

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing approval notice under BSE Listing Operations.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action required from trading members or market participants beyond updating trading systems to permit trading in the newly listed shares from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: July 13, 2026
- Effective Date of Listing/Trading: July 16, 2026 (Thursday)

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact, company-specific corporate action reflecting the completion of a rights issue by Oasis Securities Limited. It increases the free-float and total share count of the company but has no broader market or systemic implications.
