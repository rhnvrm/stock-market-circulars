## Summary

BSE has published the trading and settlement programme for the Securities Lending and Borrowing (SLB) segment for February 2026. The schedule covers 20 trading days from February 1 to February 28, 2026, with settlement numbers ranging from SL-210/25-26 to SL-230/25-26. The circular outlines specific dates for trading, 6A7A entry and confirmation, and pay-in/pay-out timings.

## Key Points

- Settlement programme covers February 1-28, 2026
- 20 settlement cycles scheduled (SL-210/25-26 to SL-230/25-26)
- Standard pay-in timing: 08:00 a.m.
- Members must submit pay-in instructions to Depositories/Banks by 07:50 a.m.
- Special timing arrangements for specific settlement dates (February 2 and February 20, 2026)
- Depository settlement numbers provided for each trading date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine settlement schedule notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must adhere to the published settlement schedule
- Pay-in instructions must be submitted to Depositories/Banks by specified deadlines (typically 07:50 a.m., with exceptions at 10:05 a.m. for certain settlements)
- 6A7A entry and confirmation must be completed on the trading date itself

## Important Dates

**Special Settlement Timings:**

- **February 2, 2026**: Two settlements (SL-209 and SL-210) with different timings
  - SL-209: Pay-in 08:00 a.m., Pay-out 09:30 a.m. (instruction deadline 07:50 a.m.)
  - SL-210: Pay-in 10:15 a.m., Pay-out 12:00 p.m. (instruction deadline 10:05 a.m.)

- **February 20, 2026**: Two settlements (SL-223 and SL-224) with different timings
  - SL-223: Pay-in 08:00 a.m., Pay-out 09:30 a.m. (instruction deadline 07:50 a.m.)
  - SL-224: Pay-in 10:15 a.m., Pay-out 12:00 p.m. (instruction deadline 10:05 a.m.)

**Non-Trading Days:** February 7-8, 14-15, 21-22, 2026 (weekends)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal - routine operational schedule

**Operational Impact:** Low - standard settlement procedures apply with two dates requiring dual settlement windows

**Stakeholder Impact:** 
- SLB segment participants must note the special timing arrangements for February 2 and 20
- No disruption to regular trading operations expected
- Settlement cycle follows T+1 model with specific exceptions on designated dates
