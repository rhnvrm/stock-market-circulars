## Summary

ICCL (Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd.) has published the settlement calendar for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Buyback) of Zydus Lifesciences Limited. The bidding window runs from June 4 to June 10, 2026, with the final settlement on June 17, 2026. Members and custodians must deliver demat shares exclusively through the Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism.

## Key Points

- Settlement Type: Buyback; Settlement No.: 2026063
- Bidding window: June 4, 2026 – June 10, 2026
- Obligation download date: June 16, 2026
- Settlement date: June 17, 2026
- Demat shares must be delivered only via the Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism available through both CDSL and NSDL
- Market type "(Buyback)" and Settlement No. 2026063 must be used in the depository system
- Members and custodians are NOT required to upload any EPN file for early pay-in
- CDSL and NSDL early pay-in securities will be earmarked/blocked in clients' accounts under the requisite market type and settlement number
- Members should refer to MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for client-wise Stamp Duty charges

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. Members and custodians are directed to refer to Notice No. 20200528-32 dated May 28, 2020, regarding settlement as per Revised Guidelines of Mechanism for acquisition of shares through Stock Exchange.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and custodians must use Market Type "(Buyback)" and Settlement No. 2026063 in the depository system for all early pay-in transactions
- Early pay-in of securities must be completed sufficiently in advance so that the effect is reflected in BSE/ICCL systems before prescribed timelines for bid acceptance/confirmation on the BSE OTB platform
- Custodians must confirm bids within the designated confirmation window on the BSE OTB platform
- No EPN file upload is required for early pay-in under this settlement

## Important Dates

## Impact Assessment

This circular is operationally significant for stock brokers (members) and custodians participating in the Zydus Lifesciences buyback offer via the BSE OTB platform. Participants must ensure timely early pay-in through CDSL or NSDL to avoid rejection of bids. The settlement follows the standard BSE/ICCL buyback acquisition window process and does not introduce any new procedures beyond existing guidelines.
