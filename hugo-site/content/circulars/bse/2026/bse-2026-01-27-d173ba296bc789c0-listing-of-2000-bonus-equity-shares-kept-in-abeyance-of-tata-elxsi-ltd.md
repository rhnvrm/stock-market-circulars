## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 2,000 bonus equity shares of Tata Elxsi Ltd (Scrip Code: 500408) that were previously kept in abeyance. These shares are permitted for trading with effect from Wednesday, January 28, 2025.

## Key Points

- 2,000 bonus equity shares of Rs. 10/- each kept in abeyance are now listed
- Scrip Code: 500408
- ISIN: INE670A01012
- Distinctive Numbers: 31163221 to 31165220
- Trading Members have been informed of this listing

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard administrative listing of previously abeyance shares.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to take note of the listing of these securities for trading purposes.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** January 27, 2026
- **Trading Effective Date:** Wednesday, January 28, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The impact is minimal as this involves only 2,000 bonus equity shares being released from abeyance. This is a routine administrative action that affects a very small number of shares relative to Tata Elxsi's overall equity capital. No material impact on trading volumes or stock price is expected.
