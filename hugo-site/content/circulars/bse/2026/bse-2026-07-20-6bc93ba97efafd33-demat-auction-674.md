## Summary

BSE Limited has invited offers from trading members for purchase of securities in the Auction segment for Rolling Settlement No. DR-674/2026-27, conducted through Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd. This is a routine settlement process notice covering timing and procedural requirements for auction offer entry, matching, and pay-in.

## Key Points

- Auction pertains to Rolling Settlement No. DR-674/2026-27, covering securities that were short-delivered in the settlement cycle
- Auction offers accepted on trading terminals on Monday, July 20, 2026
- Auction offer entry window: 02:00 p.m. to 02:45 p.m.
- Auction matching window: 02:45 p.m. to 03:00 p.m.
- Delivery statement and report of accepted auction offers: 03:00 p.m. to 03:30 p.m.
- Money statement and final reports: 03:35 p.m. to 05:30 p.m.
- Pay-in of the auction to be held on Tuesday, July 21, 2026 at 08:00 a.m.
- Post uniform membership structure implementation, settlement will be done through the Clearing Member of a Trading Member
- Restriction on trading members offering securities in auction for settlements where they had short-delivered those securities has been removed
- No corrections permitted for punching errors in securities code and rate; Exchange reserves right to accept or reject any offer without assigning reasons
- Trading members advised to refer to cutoff rate on Auction Offer Entry Screen and check for related Corporate Action notices (Ex-Rights/Ex-Bonus/Ex-Split)

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine operational/settlement notice rather than a regulatory change. It references the prior removal of the restriction preventing trading members from offering securities in auction settlements where they had short-delivered.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members and Custodians must check their auction pay-in obligations for securities and execute securities pay-in within depository cut-off timings. Members must exercise care while punching securities codes and rates in the Auction Offer Entry Screen, as no corrections are permitted after entry.

## Important Dates

- July 20, 2026: Auction offer entry, matching, and reporting
- July 21, 2026, 08:00 a.m.: Pay-in of the auction

## Impact Assessment

This notice has limited market-wide impact and is primarily operational in nature, affecting trading members and custodians involved in settlement of short-delivered securities under Rolling Settlement DR-674/2026-27. No impact on listed companies or broader market sentiment is expected.
