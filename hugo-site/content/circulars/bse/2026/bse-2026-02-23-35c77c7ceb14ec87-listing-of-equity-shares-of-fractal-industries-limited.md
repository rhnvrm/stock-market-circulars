## Summary

BSE Limited has notified trading members that equity shares of Fractal Industries Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange. The listing falls under the SME segment. The specific date of listing and further security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Fractal Industries Limited equity shares are approved for listing on BSE under the SME segment.
- Scrip Code: 544707
- Trading Symbol: FIL
- ISIN: INE1W3Q01011
- Exact listing date to be announced via a separate notice.
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notice informing trading members of an upcoming admission of securities to dealings on the Exchange.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should take note of the upcoming listing of Fractal Industries Limited (scrip code 544707) on the SME segment.
- Members should await the follow-up notice for the confirmed listing date and security details before initiating dealings.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: February 23, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced via a separate notice.

## Impact Assessment

This notice signals the imminent listing of Fractal Industries Limited on the BSE SME platform. The impact is contained to market participants interested in SME segment stocks. Trading members should monitor for the subsequent notice confirming the listing date. No immediate trading action is required until the listing date is officially notified.
