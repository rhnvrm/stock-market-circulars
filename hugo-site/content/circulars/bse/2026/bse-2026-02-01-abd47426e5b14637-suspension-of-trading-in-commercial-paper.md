## Summary

BSE has issued notice 20260201-18 suspending trading in commercial papers of 8 companies that have fixed record dates for redemption purposes. Trading members are advised not to deal in these commercial papers from their respective no-dealings dates.

## Key Points

- 8 commercial papers suspended from trading due to redemption
- Record dates range from February 4-6, 2026
- No dealings effective from the same dates as record dates
- Applies to debt segment under corporate actions category
- Notice reference: DR-813/2025-2026, DR-814/2025-2026, DR-815/2025-2026

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any deals in the listed commercial papers from their respective no-dealings dates
- Members must ensure clients are informed about the trading suspension
- All pending orders for these instruments should be cancelled before the no-dealings date

## Important Dates

- **February 4, 2026**: Record date and no dealings date for Standard Chartered Securities and Mirae Asset Sharekhan CPs
- **February 5, 2026**: Record date and no dealings date for Infina Finance CP
- **February 6, 2026**: Record date and no dealings date for Bajaj Housing Finance, Time Technoplast, Tata Power, NABARD, and 360 ONE WAM CPs

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action affecting the debt market segment. The suspension ensures orderly redemption of commercial papers. Impact is limited to holders of these specific debt instruments who need to be aware of the record dates for redemption eligibility. Trading members dealing in commercial papers should update their systems to prevent inadvertent trades in these securities from the specified dates.
