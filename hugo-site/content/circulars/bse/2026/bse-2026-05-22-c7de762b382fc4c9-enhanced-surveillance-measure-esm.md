## Summary

BSE has updated the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework effective May 25–26, 2026. New securities (Annexure I) will attract a minimum 100% margin and be shifted from Rolling Settlement to Trade-for-Trade segment from May 26, 2026. Certain securities are being downgraded to Stage I, while others are being removed from the ESM framework entirely.

## Key Points

- Securities in Annexure I satisfy ESM inclusion criteria and will attract **minimum 100% margin** on all open positions as on May 25, 2026 and all new positions from May 26, 2026.
- Securities moving to **Stage I** (lower stage) under ESM Framework take effect May 25, 2026 (included in Annexure I).
- All ESM-qualifying securities shift from **Rolling Settlement to Trade-for-Trade** segment w.e.f. May 26, 2026.
- Securities moving to **Stage II** will be under Trade-for-Trade with a **2% price band** and **Periodic Call Auction** w.e.f. May 25, 2026.
- Securities exiting the ESM framework w.e.f. May 25, 2026 are listed in **Annexure II**.
- A consolidated list of all securities currently under ESM is provided in **Annexure III**.
- ESM operates in conjunction with all other prevailing exchange surveillance measures.
- Shortlisting under ESM is purely for market surveillance and should not be construed as adverse action against the concerned company.

## Regulatory Changes

This notice references and builds upon prior ESM framework notices:
- Notice 20230602-44 (June 02, 2023)
- Notice 20230718-46 (July 18, 2023)
- Notice 20240809-42 (August 09, 2024)
- Notice 20240920-63 (September 20, 2024)
- Notice 20241004-65 (October 04, 2024)
- Notice 20250725-61 (July 25, 2025)

Upon exit from ESM, a security's price band reverts to the band applicable before its ESM inclusion, unless it remains subject to another surveillance measure.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading members** must ensure 100% margin is collected on all open and new positions in ESM-listed securities from May 26, 2026.
- Members should review Annexures I, II, and III to identify affected securities and update risk and margin systems accordingly.
- For clarifications, members may contact: **bse.surv@bseindia.com**

## Important Dates

| Date | Event |
|------|-------|
| May 25, 2026 | Stage I downgrade effective; Stage II Trade-for-Trade + 2% price band + Periodic Call Auction effective; ESM exits (Annexure II) effective |
| May 26, 2026 | 100% margin requirement effective on open positions (as on May 25) and new positions; shift to Trade-for-Trade settlement effective for newly included securities |

## Impact Assessment

This is a high-impact notice for market participants holding or trading in securities across the ESM annexures. The mandatory 100% margin requirement significantly increases the capital cost of holding positions in affected securities. The shift to Trade-for-Trade settlement eliminates netting benefits and increases settlement risk for members. Stage II securities face further restrictions via a tight 2% price band and Periodic Call Auction mechanism, severely limiting intraday liquidity. Members with large existing positions in affected scrips as of May 25, 2026 must ensure adequate margin availability to avoid forced liquidations.
