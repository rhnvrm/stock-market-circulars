## Summary

BSE Limited has notified trading members that equity shares of KANISHK ALUMINIUM INDIA LIMITED will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544693 and symbol KANISHK with ISIN No. INE163401016. The exact date of listing and detailed security information will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Kanishk Aluminium India Limited
- Scrip Code: 544693
- Symbol: KANISHK
- ISIN Number: INE163401016
- Segment: SME (Small and Medium Enterprises)
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Security details to be provided in subsequent notice

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the scrip code, symbol, and ISIN number for Kanishk Aluminium India Limited. Members must await further notification regarding the specific listing date and security details before commencing trading activities in this security.

## Important Dates

- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

This is a routine listing notification with minimal market-wide impact. The announcement affects only trading members who may have interest in the SME segment.
