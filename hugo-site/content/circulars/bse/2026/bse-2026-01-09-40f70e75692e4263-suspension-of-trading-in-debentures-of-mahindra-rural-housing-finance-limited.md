## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in a specific series of non-convertible debentures issued by Mahindra Rural Housing Finance Limited (MRHFL) effective January 15, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing the record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest on the same date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to MRHFL-8.20%-30-1-26-PVT debentures (ISIN: INE950O07420, Code: 974569)
- Record date set for January 15, 2026
- No dealings permitted from January 15, 2026 onwards
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Notice issued under DR-799/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational suspension for debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified MRHFL debentures from January 15, 2026
- Trading members are required to take note of the suspension and adjust their systems accordingly

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** January 9, 2026
- **Record Date:** January 15, 2026
- **No Dealings From:** January 15, 2026
- **Redemption Date:** January 30, 2026 (as indicated by debenture series name)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - affects only holders of this specific debenture series. This is a scheduled maturity event.

**Operational Impact:** Minimal - standard procedure for debenture redemption. Trading members need to update their systems to block trading in the specified ISIN.

**Investor Impact:** Debenture holders will receive their principal redemption amount plus final interest payment based on the record date eligibility.
