## Summary

BSE announced the addition of Hannah Joseph Hospital Limited (Exchange ticker: 544687) to the BSE SME IPO Index, effective at the open of trading on February 3, 2026. The stock is being listed on BSE on February 2, 2026.

## Key Points

- Hannah Joseph Hospital Limited (ticker: 544687) to be added to BSE SME IPO Index
- Stock listing date: February 2, 2026
- Index addition effective date: February 3, 2026 (at market open)
- Action included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- Reference made to previous Notice No. 20260201-4

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index maintenance activity.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index composition change is automatically reflected in corporate action files.

## Important Dates

- **February 2, 2026**: Hannah Joseph Hospital Limited lists on BSE
- **February 3, 2026**: Stock added to BSE SME IPO Index at market open

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine index addition affecting only the BSE SME IPO Index. Index funds and ETFs tracking this index will need to rebalance to include the new constituent. Queries can be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com.
