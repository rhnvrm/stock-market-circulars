## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 3,22,50,000 new equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued by CCME Global Limited (Scrip Code: 514336) on a preferential basis are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Monday, May 11, 2026. The shares were allotted to Promoters and Non-Promoters at par on March 26, 2026, and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 3,22,50,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- face value issued at par (issue price Rs. 10/-) on preferential basis
- Shares allotted to both Promoters and Non-Promoters
- ISIN: INE194N01016
- Distribution numbers: 13000001 to 45250000
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Trading permitted from May 11, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice under BSE's Listing Operations for newly issued securities following a preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their systems to recognize the new share distribution numbers (13000001–45250000) from May 11, 2026
- Lock-in restrictions must be observed: 2,40,00,000 shares are locked until November 20, 2027, and 82,50,000 shares are locked until November 20, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** March 26, 2026
- **Trading Commencement:** May 11, 2026 (Monday)
- **Partial Lock-in Expiry (82,50,000 shares):** November 20, 2026
- **Full Lock-in Expiry (2,40,00,000 shares):** November 20, 2027

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 3,22,50,000 new shares to the tradeable pool of CCME Global Limited, representing a significant increase in total share count. However, effective free float impact is limited in the near term due to lock-in restrictions: 2,40,00,000 shares (74.4% of new issue) are locked until November 2027, and 82,50,000 shares (25.6% of new issue) are locked until November 2026. Only shares outside these lock-in tranches, if any, are immediately tradeable. Existing shareholders may experience dilution effects from the preferential allotment.
