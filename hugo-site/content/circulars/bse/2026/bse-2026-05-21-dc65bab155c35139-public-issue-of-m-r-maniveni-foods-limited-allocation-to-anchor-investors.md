## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that the Anchor Investor (AI) portion of the Public Issue of M R Maniveni Foods Limited has been subscribed. A total of 14,70,000 equity shares were allotted to Anchor Investors at a price of Rs. 52 per equity share on May 21, 2026.

## Key Points

- 14,70,000 equity shares allocated to Anchor Investors in the M R Maniveni Foods Limited IPO
- Allotment price: Rs. 52 per equity share
- Allotment date: May 21, 2026
- Notice issued by Mangesh Tayde, Vice President, BSE
- Company announcement dated May 21, 2026 enclosed as attachment (Anchor_allotment_Maniveni)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard informational notice regarding anchor investor allotment in a public issue.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance actions required for trading members. The notice is informational, advising members of the anchor allotment details ahead of the broader public issue opening.

## Important Dates

- Anchor Investor allotment date: May 21, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. The anchor investor allotment of 14,70,000 shares at Rs. 52 per share signals institutional interest in M R Maniveni Foods Limited's IPO. This is a routine pre-IPO step and primarily relevant to market participants tracking the company's upcoming listing on BSE.
