## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members that the commercial paper issued by Julius Baer Capital (India) Private Limited (Scrip Code: 730733, ISIN: INE824H14SZ2, Series: JBCPL-10-3-26-CP) is approaching its maturity date. Accordingly, trading in this instrument is suspended with effect from March 09, 2026.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of Julius Baer Capital (India) Private Limited (Scrip Code: 730733) is set to mature.
- ISIN: INE824H14SZ2, Series: JBCPL-10-3-26-CP.
- Trading members are advised not to deal in this commercial paper from March 09, 2026 onwards.
- Notice reference: DR-835/2025-2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine operational notice regarding the suspension of trading in a maturing debt instrument as per standard BSE procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- All trading members must cease trading in the commercial paper of Julius Baer Capital (India) Private Limited (Scrip Code: 730733, ISIN: INE824H14SZ2) effective March 09, 2026.
- Members must take note of this suspension and ensure no transactions are executed in this instrument on or after the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** March 06, 2026
- **Suspension Effective Date:** March 09, 2026 (trading prohibited from this date)
- **Maturity Date:** On or around March 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited and routine. This notice affects only traders holding or intending to trade in this specific commercial paper (Scrip Code: 730733). The suspension is a standard pre-maturity procedure to ensure orderly wind-down of the instrument. No broader market or systemic impact is expected.
