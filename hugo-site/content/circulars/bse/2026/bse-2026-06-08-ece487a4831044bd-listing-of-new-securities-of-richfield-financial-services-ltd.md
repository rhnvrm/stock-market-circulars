## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 21,56,000 new equity shares of Richfield Financial Services Ltd. (Scrip Code: 539435, ISIN: INE201C01012) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Tuesday, June 9, 2026. The shares were issued at a premium on a preferential basis to Promoters and Non-Promoters.

## Key Points

- 21,56,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- face value issued at a premium of Rs. 15/- (Issue Price: Rs. 25/-)
- Shares issued to Promoters and Non-Promoters on a preferential basis
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 7500201 to 9656200
- Date of allotment: March 10, 2026
- Trading permitted from June 9, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine notification for the listing of newly allotted preferential shares.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the new securities and their lock-in restrictions before executing trades. Shares under lock-in cannot be traded until the respective lock-in expiry dates.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** March 10, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** June 9, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (20,14,400 shares – dist. nos. 7500201–9514600):** December 15, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (21,600 shares – dist. nos. 9514601–9536200):** December 15, 2027
- **Lock-in Expiry (1,20,000 shares – dist. nos. 9536201–9656200):** December 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine preferential allotment listing for Richfield Financial Services Ltd., a small-cap company. The addition of 21,56,000 shares increases the company's listed share capital. The majority of shares (approximately 93%) are under lock-in until December 2026, limiting immediate free-float impact. Broader market impact is negligible.
