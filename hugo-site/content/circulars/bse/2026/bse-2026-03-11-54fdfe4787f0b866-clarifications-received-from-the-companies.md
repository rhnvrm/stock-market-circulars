## Summary

BSE received clarifications from three listed companies in response to surveillance queries regarding unusual price or volume movements in their shares. All three companies — Authum Investment & Infrastructure Ltd (Scrip: 539177), Fabtech Technologies Ltd (Scrip: 544558 / NSE: FABTECH), and Hindustan Oil Exploration Company Ltd (Scrip: 500186) — confirmed that movements are purely market-driven and that no price-sensitive information remains undisclosed.

## Key Points

- Authum Investment & Infrastructure Ltd responded to BSE surveillance letter Ref. No. L/SURV/ONL/PV/APJ/2025-2026/965 dated March 11, 2026, clarifying that the significant increase in share volume is market-driven and all information is in the public domain.
- Fabtech Technologies Ltd clarified in response to a similar query that there is no pending price-sensitive announcement that could have caused the movement in share price.
- Hindustan Oil Exploration Company Ltd responded to BSE email Ref. No. L/SURV/ONL/PV/APJ/2025-2026/3419 dated March 10, 2026, confirming periodic dissemination of all material updates to stock exchanges.
- All three companies affirmed compliance with Regulation 30 of the SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. These are compliance responses to existing surveillance mechanisms under Regulation 30 of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies are required to respond promptly to BSE surveillance queries regarding unusual price or volume movements.
- Disclosures must be made in accordance with Regulation 30 of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015 as and when material information arises.
- All three companies have confirmed ongoing compliance with applicable disclosure obligations.

## Important Dates

- March 10, 2026: BSE surveillance query issued to Hindustan Oil Exploration Company Ltd.
- March 11, 2026: BSE surveillance query issued to Authum Investment & Infrastructure Ltd (Ref. No. L/SURV/ONL/PV/APJ/2025-2026/965).
- March 11, 2026: All three companies submitted their clarification responses.

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. These are standard surveillance-related clarifications with no new material disclosures. Investors should note that the companies have confirmed no undisclosed price-sensitive information exists. The volume/price movements in these scrips remain unexplained by fundamentals per company statements, suggesting speculative or market-driven activity. No trading restrictions or regulatory actions are indicated.
