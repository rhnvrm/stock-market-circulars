## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that new commercial paper securities issued on a private placement basis by five companies have been listed and admitted to trading on the BSE Debt Segment, effective July 6, 2026. The securities will trade in dematerialised form in standard denominations of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof, with a tick size of 1 paise.

## Key Points

- Five companies had commercial papers freshly listed on the BSE Debt Segment: Axis Finance Limited, Manappuram Finance Limited, SEIL Energy India Limited, Julius Baer Capital (India) Private Limited, and Bajaj Financial Securities Limited.
- Axis Finance Limited (AFL-30-04-27-CP, ISIN INE891K14QC8): 5,000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 face value, credit rating CRISIL A1+/IND A1+, issue price Rs. 4,71,609, interest rate 7.30% p.a., allotted 03/07/2026, redemption 30/04/2027.
- Manappuram Finance Limited (MFL-02-07-27-CP, ISIN INE522D14PT0): 4,500 units of Rs. 5,00,000 face value, credit rating CARE A1+/CRISIL A1+, issue price Rs. 4,62,416.50, interest rate 8.15% p.a., allotted 03/07/2026, redemption 02/07/2027.
- SEIL Energy India Limited (SEIL-22-9-26-CP, ISIN INE460M14883): 6,000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 face value, credit rating CRISIL A1+/ICRA A1+, issue price Rs. 4,93,202, interest rate 6.45% p.a., allotted 06/07/2026, redemption 22/09/2026.
- Julius Baer Capital (India) Private Limited (JBC-25-9-26-CP, ISIN INE824H14UQ7): 4,000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 face value, credit rating CRISIL A1+/ICRA A1+, issue price Rs. 4,92,567, interest rate 6.80% p.a., allotted 06/07/2026, redemption 25/09/2026.
- Bajaj Financial Securities Limited (BFSL-22-09-26-CP, ISIN INE01C314GH7): 4,000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 face value, credit rating CRISIL A1+/IND A1+, issue price Rs. 4,92,621, allotted 03/07/2026.
- All securities trade only in dematerialised form; market lot is 1 unit.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine listing notification, not a regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must ensure trades in these instruments comply with the standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof, and the tick size of 1 paise. Queries can be directed to the BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915.

## Important Dates

- Deemed date of allotment: 03/07/2026 (Axis Finance, Manappuram Finance, Bajaj Financial Securities) and 06/07/2026 (SEIL Energy India, Julius Baer Capital)
- Listing effective date: 06/07/2026
- Redemption dates: 30/04/2027 (Axis Finance), 02/07/2027 (Manappuram Finance), 22/09/2026 (SEIL Energy India), 25/09/2026 (Julius Baer Capital)

## Impact Assessment

This circular is procedural and primarily relevant to debt market trading members and institutional investors dealing in commercial paper. It has no direct effect on equity share prices or broader market operations. It may be of interest for tracking short-term credit market activity and borrowing costs for the issuing NBFCs/financial institutions, including listed entity Manappuram Finance.
