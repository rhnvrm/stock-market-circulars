## Summary

BSE has scheduled a Mock Trading session from its Disaster Recovery (DR) site for the Equity segment on Saturday, June 20, 2026. The session is intended for members to test trading systems, including third-party platforms, and verify readiness for DR live scenarios. All resulting trades carry no margin obligations, pay-in/pay-out obligations, or legal rights and liabilities.

## Key Points

- Mock Trading will be conducted from BSE's Disaster Recovery (DR) site on Saturday, June 20, 2026, for the Equity segment.
- Members may use this session to fulfil regulatory testing requirements per SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD1/DSAP/CIR/P/2020/234 dated November 24, 2020, or participate in the UAT environment instead.
- Third-party trading platform users can test functionalities including call auction sessions, risk-reduction mode, trading halt, and block deals.
- Trades arising from mock trading create no margin obligations, pay-in/pay-out obligations, rights, or liabilities.
- Members are requested to submit feedback by 01:00 pm on the day of the session.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. The notice references existing SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD1/DSAP/CIR/P/2020/234 (November 24, 2020) on software testing requirements for trading and risk management systems.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members using trading software (including third-party platforms) should participate in the Mock Trading session or the UAT environment to meet SEBI's software testing requirements.
- Members are encouraged to actively participate and submit feedback by 01:00 pm on June 20, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **June 20, 2026 (Saturday):** Mock Trading session from DR site for Equity segment.
  - Log-in: 08:15 am – 08:45 am
  - Morning Block Deal Window: 08:45 am – 09:00 am
  - Pre-Open Order Entry: 09:00 am – 09:08 am
  - Pre-Open Matching: 09:08 am – 09:15 am
  - Continuous Trading T+1: 09:15 am – 12:00 pm
  - Continuous Trading T+0: 09:15 am – 11:30 am
  - Special Pre-Open (IPO & Re-listed) Order Entry: 09:00 am – 09:45 am
  - Special Pre-Open Matching: 09:45 am – 10:00 am
  - Continuous Trading for SPOS Scrips: 10:00 am – 12:00 pm
  - Periodic Call Auction (2 sessions of 1 hour): 09:30 am – 11:30 am
  - Auction for Settlement – Offer Entry & Matching: 11:00 am – 11:45 am
  - Afternoon Block Deal Window: 11:30 am – 11:45 am
  - Closing: 12:00 pm – 12:10 pm
  - Post-Closing: 12:10 pm – 12:20 pm
  - Trade Modification T+1: up to 12:30 pm
  - Trade Modification T+0: up to 11:45 am
- **Feedback deadline:** 01:00 pm on June 20, 2026.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational exercise with no market or financial impact. Mock trades carry no obligations or legal effect. The session is primarily relevant to trading members using third-party platforms who need to verify DR readiness and meet SEBI's software testing regulatory requirements. Participation is voluntary but encouraged for compliance purposes.
