## Summary

BSE has published the schedule of Registrar & Transfer Agents (RTAs) visiting its premises during January 2026. The circular lists companies serviced by Adroit Corporate Services Pvt. Ltd., one of the RTAs scheduled to visit. Investors can interact with RTAs during these visits for share transfer, dematerialization, and other shareholder services.

## Key Points

- RTA visits scheduled at BSE Ltd. for January 2026
- Adroit Corporate Services Pvt. Ltd. services 52+ listed companies
- Companies include Antariksh Industries, IndiaNivesh, Hind Rectifiers, LKP Finance, Safari Industries, and others
- RTAs facilitate investor services including share transfers, name changes, dematerialization requests, and grievance resolution
- Investors can visit BSE during scheduled dates to meet with RTAs directly
