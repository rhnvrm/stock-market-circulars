## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the equity shares of Leapfrog Engineering Services Limited (Scrip Code: 544797) will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment with effect from Thursday, July 09, 2026. Post transfer, dealings in the equity shares will be classified under M Group.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 544797 (Leapfrog Engineering Services Limited)
- Segment: SME
- Transfer: From Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to Rolling segment (M Group)
- This notice is in continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20260623-30 dated June 23, 2026
- Contact person for queries: Mr. Anurag Jain, Tel. No. 022-2272 8822

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares of Leapfrog Engineering Services Limited are being moved out of the Trade for Trade (T+1 or T+0 compulsory delivery) mechanism under the MT Group and into the standard Rolling settlement under M Group. This change alters the settlement obligations for trades in this scrip.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should update their systems and inform clients about the change in trading segment for Scrip Code 544797. Trades executed on or after July 09, 2026 will settle under the Rolling segment (M Group) rules rather than the Trade for Trade mechanism.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: June 24, 2026
- Effective Date: Thursday, July 09, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This migration from Trade for Trade (MT Group) to Rolling segment (M Group) typically indicates improved liquidity or compliance status for the scrip. Under the Rolling segment, traders gain flexibility for netting and carry-forward positions (subject to applicable regulations), as opposed to the compulsory delivery requirement under Trade for Trade. The impact is limited to traders and investors holding or transacting in this SME scrip.
