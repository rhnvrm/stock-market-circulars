## Summary

BSE has informed members that HDFC Mutual Fund will launch the HDFC NIFTY METAL ETF New Fund Offer (NFO) on the BSE StAR MF Platform, open from July 20, 2026, to July 24, 2026. The NFO includes both a regular plan and a direct plan, with a tentative allotment date of July 27, 2026.

## Key Points

- HDFC NIFTY METAL ETF (Equity category) and HDFC NIFTY METAL ETF - DIRECT PLAN (Equity category) are being offered.
- NFO period: Jul 20, 2026 to Jul 24, 2026.
- Tentative allotment date for both schemes: Jul 27, 2026.
- Transactions must be funded by 9:00 AM on T+1 day, else they will be cancelled with an 'N' flag reported to RTA/AMC.
- Transactions with initiated payment remain pending until 3:00 PM on the last fund pay-out day or T+5 day, whichever is earlier.
- Clear funds must be available in the ICCL account by 6:00 PM on Jul 27, 2026, for allotment eligibility.
- Cheque and OTM payments accepted subject to clear funds by 6:00 PM on Jul 27, 2026.
- Switch transactions accepted during the NFO period, subject to fund receipt prior to allotment date; switch-out funds received on or after allotment date will not be considered.
- NFO period extended till 6:00 PM on Jul 24, 2026, for Lumpsum purchases on BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an operational notice regarding a new fund offer launch, not a regulatory or policy change.

## Compliance Requirements

BSE StAR MF Platform members must ensure client transactions are properly funded within specified timelines and that clear funds are available in the ICCL account by the stipulated deadline to ensure valid allotment. Members must report cancellations with 'N' flag to RTA/AMC for unfunded transactions.

## Important Dates

- NFO Open: Jul 20, 2026
- NFO Close (Lumpsum, extended): 6:00 PM, Jul 24, 2026
- Tentative Allotment Date: Jul 27, 2026
- Fund availability deadline in ICCL account: 6:00 PM, Jul 27, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine mutual fund product launch notice with operational relevance primarily for BSE StAR MF Platform members, distributors, and investors interested in the HDFC NIFTY METAL ETF. It does not have a direct market-wide or listed-equity trading impact, and no specific stock tickers are implicated.
