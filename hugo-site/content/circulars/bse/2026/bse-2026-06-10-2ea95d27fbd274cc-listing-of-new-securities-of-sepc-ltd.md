## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 2,42,15,722 new equity shares of SEPC Ltd. (Scrip Code: 532945, ISIN: INE964H01014) are admitted for trading with effect from Thursday, June 11, 2026. These shares arise from the conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up shares of Rs. 10/- each, issued on a rights basis.

## Key Points

- Trading in new securities permitted from June 11, 2026
- Total new shares: 2,42,15,722 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each
- Issued pursuant to conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up on rights basis
- ISIN: INE964H01014
- Distinctive numbers: 1,57,32,730 shares (range 1890868399 to 1906601128) and 84,82,992 shares (range 1906604380 to 1915087371)
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of SEPC Ltd.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notice pursuant to an already-completed rights issue conversion.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are permitted to trade in the newly listed securities from June 11, 2026. No additional compliance action is required beyond standard trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 10, 2026
- **Trading Commencement Date:** June 11, 2026 (Thursday)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. The listing increases the freely tradable equity base of SEPC Ltd. by 2,42,15,722 shares following the conversion of partly paid rights shares to fully paid up status. Existing shareholders who held partly paid shares will now be able to trade their fully paid up shares. The new shares are pari-passu with existing shares, so no dilution differential or preferential rights are created.
