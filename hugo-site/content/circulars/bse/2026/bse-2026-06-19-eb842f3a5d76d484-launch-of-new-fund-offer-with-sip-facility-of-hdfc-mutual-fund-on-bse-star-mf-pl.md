## Summary

BSE has notified members that HDFC Mutual Fund will offer the HDFC NIFTY AUTO INDEX FUND as a New Fund Offer (NFO) with SIP facility on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The NFO opens on June 22, 2026, and closes on July 3, 2026, with two plan variants — Regular Plan and Direct Plan — both classified as equity schemes.

## Key Points

- NFO period: June 22, 2026 to July 3, 2026
- Two schemes available: HDFC NIFTY AUTO INDEX FUND - Regular Plan - Growth (ISIN: INF179KC1KP2) and HDFC NIFTY AUTO INDEX FUND - Direct Plan - Growth (ISIN: INF179KC1KQ0)
- Tentative allotment date: July 7, 2026
- Lumpsum purchases extended till midnight on July 3, 2026 on BSE StAR MF Platform
- SIP/XSIP registrations must be authenticated by clients on or before 3:00 PM on July 3, 2026
- Transactions without client-initiated funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day will be cancelled
- Pending transactions (where payment is initiated) remain in system until 3:00 PM on the last day of funds pay-out or T+5, whichever is earlier

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard NFO launch notification for members of BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clients initiate funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day to avoid cancellation
- Clear funds must be available in the ICCL account by 6:00 PM on July 7, 2026 for allotment eligibility
- Cheque and OTM payments accepted during NFO period subject to clear funds reaching ICCL account by 6:00 PM on July 7, 2026
- Switch transactions accepted during NFO period only if funds are received prior to the allotment date; switch-out funds received on or after the allotment date will not be considered
- SIP/XSIP client authentication deadline: 3:00 PM on July 3, 2026

## Important Dates

- **June 22, 2026**: NFO opens
- **July 3, 2026**: NFO closes (midnight for lumpsum; 3:00 PM for SIP/XSIP authentication)
- **July 7, 2026**: Tentative allotment date; clear funds must be in ICCL account by 6:00 PM

## Impact Assessment

This notice is operational in nature and impacts BSE members facilitating mutual fund transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform. Distributors and investors interested in auto-sector index exposure via an NFO route are the primary audience. No systemic market impact is expected.
