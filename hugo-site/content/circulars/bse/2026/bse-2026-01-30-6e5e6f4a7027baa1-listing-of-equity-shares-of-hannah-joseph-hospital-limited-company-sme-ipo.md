## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of equity shares of Hannah Joseph Hospital Limited on the SME segment of the Exchange. The listing will be effective from Monday, February 02, 2026. This follows an intimation received from the Company on January 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: Hannah Joseph Hospital Limited
- Listing segment: BSE SME
- Listing type: IPO (Initial Public Offering)
- Notice Number: 20260130-55
- Category: Company related
- Contact for queries: equity.ipo@bseindia.com

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

- **Notice Date**: January 30, 2026 (Friday)
- **Listing Effective Date**: February 02, 2026 (Monday)

This circular has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains to the listing of a single SME company.
