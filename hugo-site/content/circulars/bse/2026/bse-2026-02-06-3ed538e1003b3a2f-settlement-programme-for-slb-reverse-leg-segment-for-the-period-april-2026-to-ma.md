## Summary

BSE has announced the settlement programme for the Securities Lending and Borrowing (SLB) Reverse Leg Segment for the financial year 2026-27. The circular provides a complete schedule of 12 monthly settlements from April 2026 to March 2027, with corresponding depository settlement numbers, obligation dates, and pay-in/pay-out dates.

## Key Points

- 12 monthly settlements scheduled from April 2026 to March 2027
- Settlement numbers range from SLR-001/26-27 to SLR-012/26-27
- Depository settlement numbers range from 2627001 to 2627012
- Pay-in/pay-out occurs one day after the obligation date for all settlements
- Pay-in timing: 09:00 a.m.
- Members must submit pay-in instructions to Depositories/banks by 08:50 a.m.
- Circular issued by Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational schedule announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members participating in SLB Reverse Leg Segment must adhere to the published settlement schedule
- Members must submit pay-in instructions to Depositories or banks latest by 08:50 a.m. on pay-in dates
- Pay-in must be completed by 09:00 a.m. on the specified pay-in/pay-out dates

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This is a routine operational circular.

**Operational Impact:** Members participating in SLB Reverse Leg Segment need to plan their settlement obligations according to this schedule. The predictable monthly schedule allows for proper operational planning and liquidity management.

**Affected Parties:** Trading members and clearing members involved in Securities Lending and Borrowing (SLB) transactions on BSE.
