## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that Rights Entitlements (REs) for renunciation of AVG Logistics Limited will be available for trading on the exchange from Monday, June 1, 2026. The rights issue runs from June 1 to June 9, 2026, with the renunciation trading window closing on June 4, 2026.

## Key Points

- Rights Entitlements (REs) trading commences on BSE effective June 1, 2026
- Scrip Code: 751119, Scrip ID: AVG-RE, ISIN: INE680Z20018
- Market Lot: 1, Group: R
- Settlement on T+1 Rolling Settlement basis; trades settled on trade-for-trade basis
- REs are tradable in dematerialized form only
- REs that are neither subscribed nor renounced by the Issue Closing Date will lapse and be extinguished

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. Trading members are referred to prior notices 20200305-21 (March 5, 2020) and 20200518-34 (May 18, 2020) for further procedural details on rights entitlement trading.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must inform all clients about the commencement of trading in REs, specifying the ISIN (INE680Z20018)
- Members must clarify that REs should not be mistaken for ordinary shares of AVG Logistics Limited
- Contract notes and bills for RE purchases must prominently include a disclaimer stating that purchasing REs only grants the right to participate in the ongoing rights issue or renounce the REs before issue closure

## Important Dates

- **June 1, 2026**: Commencement of RE trading on BSE; Rights Issue opens
- **June 1–4, 2026**: Trading window for renunciation of Rights Entitlements
- **June 9, 2026**: Rights Issue closes; REs not subscribed or renounced will lapse after this date

## Impact Assessment

This notice has a moderate and time-bound impact, relevant primarily to existing shareholders of AVG Logistics Limited and trading members handling client orders in REs. The trade-for-trade settlement and short renunciation window (4 days) require prompt action from eligible shareholders. Failure to either subscribe or renounce within the stipulated period will result in the lapse of entitlements.
