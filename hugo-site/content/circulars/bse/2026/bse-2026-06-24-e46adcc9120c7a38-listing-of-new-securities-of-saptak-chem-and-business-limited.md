## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 20,00,000 new equity shares of Saptak Chem And Business Limited (Scrip Code: 506906) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Thursday, June 25, 2026. These shares were issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to the conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 20,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 0.50/- (issue price Rs. 10.50/-)
- Shares issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis via warrant conversion
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- ISIN: INE467X01023
- Distribution numbers: 1073228 to 3073227
- Date of allotment: April 6, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for newly allotted securities pursuant to a previously approved preferential allotment and warrant conversion.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed that the new securities are now available for trading. The entire allotment of 20,00,000 shares is subject to a lock-in period and cannot be freely traded until the lock-in expiry date.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** April 6, 2026
- **Trading Commencement:** June 25, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry:** February 28, 2027 (for all 20,00,000 shares, dist. nos. 1073228 to 3073227)

## Impact Assessment

This listing increases the total share count of Saptak Chem And Business Limited by 20,00,000 shares. Since the entire allotment is under lock-in until February 28, 2027, there is no immediate free-float impact. The shares were allotted to non-promoters at Rs. 10.50/-, which may serve as a reference price point. Market impact is minimal given the small size and lock-in restriction.
