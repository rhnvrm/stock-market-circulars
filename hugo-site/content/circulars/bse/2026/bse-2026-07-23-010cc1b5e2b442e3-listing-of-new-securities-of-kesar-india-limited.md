## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 71,428 new equity shares of Kesar India Limited (Scrip Code: 543542), issued at a premium of Rs. 340 per share to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants, are listed and permitted to trade with effect from Friday, July 24, 2026.

## Key Points

- 71,428 equity shares of Rs. 10/- face value each, issued at a premium of Rs. 340/-, totaling an issue price of Rs. 350/- per share.
- Shares allotted to Non-Promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants.
- Distinctive numbers: 30028465 to 30099892.
- ISIN: INE0L1C01019.
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company.
- Entire allotment of 71,428 shares is under lock-in until 31/01/2027.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing approval notice, not a regulatory or policy change.

## Compliance Requirements

No action required from trading members beyond updating systems to reflect the newly listed shares and applicable lock-in status.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: 21/05/2026
- Effective date of listing/trading: Friday, July 24, 2026
- Lock-in expiry: 31/01/2027

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds a small tranche of shares from warrant conversion, resulting in minor dilution. Since the shares are locked in until January 31, 2027, there is no immediate free-float impact or trading pressure expected from this allotment.
