## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in debt securities of AVIOM India Housing Finance Private Limited effective January 29, 2026. The suspension is due to the maturity of the securities on January 31, 2026. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debt securities.

## Key Points

- Debt securities of AVIOM India Housing Finance Private Limited to be suspended from trading
- ISIN: INE0E2307229
- Scrip Code: 975877
- Security Details: AIHFPL-14%-31-1-26-PVT (14% coupon rate)
- Maturity Date: January 31, 2026
- Trading suspension effective: January 29, 2026
- Notice Reference: DR-808/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for debt security maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debt securities of AVIOM India Housing Finance Private Limited from January 29, 2026 onwards
- Trading members are required to take note of this suspension

## Important Dates

- **January 28, 2026**: Circular issued
- **January 29, 2026**: Trading suspension effective date
- **January 31, 2026**: Debt security maturity date

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational matter with minimal market impact. The suspension affects only a single private debt security that is reaching its scheduled maturity. Investors holding these securities will receive their principal and final interest payment on the maturity date. No broader market or systemic implications are anticipated.
