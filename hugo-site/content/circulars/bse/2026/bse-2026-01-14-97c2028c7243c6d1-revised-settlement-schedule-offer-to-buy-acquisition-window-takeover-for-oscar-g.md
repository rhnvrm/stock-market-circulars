## Summary

BSE has issued a revised settlement schedule for the Offer to Buy - Acquisition Window (Takeover) for Oscar Global Limited. The bid end date has been extended from January 15, 2026 to January 16, 2026, while other settlement dates remain adjusted accordingly. This revision updates the previous notice dated December 31, 2025.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 2025138
- Bid Start Date: January 2, 2026
- Revised Bid End Date: January 16, 2026 (previously January 15, 2026)
- Obligation Download Settlement Date: January 29, 2026
- Final Settlement Date: January 30, 2026
- Settlement Type: Takeover
- Demat shares must be delivered through Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism available in both depositories

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular revises the timeline for an existing takeover settlement process.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members/Custodians must use Market Type "(Takeover)" and Settlement No. 2025138 in the depository system
- Demat shares delivery only through Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism
- Members must refer to Notice No. 20200528-32 dated May 28, 2020 for settlement guidelines under the Revised Mechanism for acquisition of shares through Stock Exchange
- Members should consult MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for client-wise Stamp Duty charges

## Important Dates

- **Bid Start Date:** January 2, 2026
- **Revised Bid End Date:** January 16, 2026
- **Obligation Download Settlement Date:** January 29, 2026
- **Settlement Date:** January 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This revision provides one additional day for shareholders to participate in the takeover offer for Oscar Global Limited. The extension affects members, custodians, and shareholders who wish to tender their shares in the acquisition window. The operational impact is limited to entities participating in this specific takeover offer.
