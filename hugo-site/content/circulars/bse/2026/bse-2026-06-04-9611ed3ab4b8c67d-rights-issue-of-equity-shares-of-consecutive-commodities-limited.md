## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Consecutive Commodities Limited (BSE Code: 539091) has fixed 08 June 2026 as the record date for its rights issue. With effect from 08 June 2026, all transactions in the company's equity shares on the Rolling Settlement Segment must be conducted on an ex-rights basis.

## Key Points

- Rights issue ratio: 3 equity shares of Rs. 1/- each (at par) for every 1 equity share held
- Record date: 08 June 2026
- Ex-rights basis effective from: 08 June 2026
- Settlement number: DR-646/2026-2027
- Segment: Dematerialised Securities – Rolling Settlement

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification under BSE's Listing Compliance and Operations framework.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are required to:
- Ensure all transactions in Consecutive Commodities Limited (539091) equity shares are executed on an ex-rights basis from 08 June 2026 onwards.
- Take note of the record date and settlement number for operational and settlement purposes.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 04 June 2026
- **Record Date:** 08 June 2026
- **Ex-Rights Effective Date:** 08 June 2026
- **Settlement Number:** DR-646/2026-2027

## Impact Assessment

This rights issue will dilute existing shareholding by a factor of 3:1, effectively quadrupling the total number of shares outstanding for Consecutive Commodities Limited. Existing shareholders who exercise their rights will maintain their proportional ownership, while those who do not will face dilution. Trading members should inform their clients holding positions in BSE code 539091 and adjust their records accordingly from the ex-rights date. Market impact is limited to existing shareholders of this company.
