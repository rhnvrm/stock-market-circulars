## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260310-7 informs trading members that 24 companies have fixed 12 March 2026 as the record date for redemption of their respective Commercial Papers. Trading members are advised not to deal in the commercial papers of these companies from 12 March 2026 onwards under DR-838/2025-2026.

## Key Points

- Trading suspended in commercial papers of 24 companies effective 12 March 2026
- All suspensions are under circular DR-838/2025-2026
- Record date for all listed instruments is 12 March 2026
- Purpose for all: Redemption of Commercial Paper
- Segment: Debt Market

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This notice is issued under the existing framework DR-838/2025-2026 governing suspension of trading in debt instruments ahead of redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the commercial papers of the 24 listed companies from 12 March 2026
- Applicable to all trading members of BSE operating in the Debt segment

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 10 March 2026
- **Record Date / No-Dealings From:** 12 March 2026
- **Circular Reference:** DR-838/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative action with limited market impact. All 24 commercial paper instruments are being redeemed on 12 March 2026, and the suspension prevents any last-minute secondary market transactions that could complicate the redemption process. Investors holding these CPs will receive redemption proceeds on the record date. No impact on equity trading or broader market operations.
