## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 11,16,667 new equity shares of iStreet Network Limited (Scrip Code: 524622, ISIN: INE532B01020) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Thursday, June 18, 2026. These shares were issued at Rs. 6/- per share (face value Rs. 4/- + premium Rs. 2/-) to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- **Company:** iStreet Network Limited (Scrip Code: 524622)
- **New Shares:** 11,16,667 equity shares of Rs. 4/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 2/- each
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 6/- per share
- **Allotment basis:** Preferential allotment to non-promoters via warrant conversion
- **Distribution Numbers:** 68300003 to 69416669
- **ISIN:** INE532B01020
- **Pari-passu:** New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- **Trading effective:** June 18, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification under BSE's listing operations for newly allotted securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities and may trade them from June 18, 2026 onwards, subject to the lock-in restrictions applicable to all 11,16,667 shares.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** March 27, 2026
- **Trading effective from:** June 18, 2026
- **Lock-in period ends:** January 15, 2027 (all 11,16,667 shares; Dist. Nos. 68300003–69416669 are locked in)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. The addition of 11,16,667 shares represents a modest increase in the freely tradable equity base, though the entire tranche is under lock-in until January 15, 2027, meaning no immediate increase in tradable float. Market participants should note the lock-in expiry date as a future potential supply event.
