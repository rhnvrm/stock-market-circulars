## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted to trading privately placed Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of Asirvad Microfinance Limited on the BSE Debt Segment effective June 4, 2026. The securities carry a coupon rate of 9.10% p.a. with monthly interest payments and mature on June 2, 2028. They are rated CRISIL AA-/Stable.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code 977842 (Scrip ID: 910AML2028) is now live for trading on the BSE Debt Segment
- Securities trade exclusively in dematerialised form under ISIN INE516Q07481
- Tick size is 1 paise
- 15,000 units listed at a face value and issue price of Rs. 1,00,000 each
- Credit rating: CRISIL AA-/Stable
- Full placement details available on the BSE Placement Memorandum portal

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification under BSE's debt market admission process for privately placed instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form
- Members with clarifications should contact the BSE Debt Department at 22728352 / 8597 / 8995 / 5753 / 8915
- Refer to the Placement Memorandum at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for full instrument details

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** June 2, 2026
- **Listing / Trading Effective:** June 4, 2026
- **First Interest Payment:** July 2, 2026
- **Interest Payment Schedule:** Monthly from July 2, 2026 to June 2, 2028
- **Date of Redemption:** June 2, 2028

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt listing with limited broad market impact. The listing adds a short-duration (approximately 2-year) AA-rated microfinance NCD to the BSE Debt Segment, providing liquidity to existing holders of Asirvad Microfinance's privately placed paper. Impact is confined to debt market participants and institutional investors holding or tracking this ISIN.
