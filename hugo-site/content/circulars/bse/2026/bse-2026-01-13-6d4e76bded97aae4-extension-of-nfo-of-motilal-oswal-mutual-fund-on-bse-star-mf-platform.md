## Summary

Motilal Oswal Asset Management Company Limited has announced a one-day extension of the New Fund Offer (NFO) period for the Motilal Oswal Nifty Diversified Equity Flexicap Passive Fund of Funds (FOF) scheme. The NFO closing date has been extended from January 15, 2026 to January 16, 2026, with the scheme re-opening date scheduled for January 29, 2026. All other terms and conditions of the scheme remain unchanged.

## Key Points

- NFO closing date extended from January 15, 2026 to January 16, 2026
- Scheme re-opening date will be January 29, 2026
- Scheme type: Nifty Diversified Equity Flexicap Passive Fund of Funds
- Fund house: Motilal Oswal Mutual Fund
- All other scheme terms and conditions remain unchanged
- This notice cum addendum forms an integral part of the Scheme Information Document and Key Information Memorandum

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is an administrative extension of the NFO period.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Investors interested in subscribing to the NFO should note the revised closing date.

## Important Dates

- **Original NFO Closing Date**: January 15, 2026
- **Extended NFO Closing Date**: January 16, 2026
- **Scheme Re-opening Date**: January 29, 2026
- **Circular Date**: January 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact as it is purely administrative in nature. The one-day extension provides prospective investors with an additional day to subscribe to the NFO. The extension does not affect existing market operations, listed securities, or impose any new obligations on market participants. It is relevant only to investors considering investment in this specific mutual fund scheme on the BSE StAR MF platform.
