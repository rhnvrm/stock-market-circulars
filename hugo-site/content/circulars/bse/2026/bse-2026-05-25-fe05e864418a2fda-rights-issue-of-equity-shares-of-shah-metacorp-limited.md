## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that Shah Metacorp Limited (scrip code 533275) has fixed **27 May 2026** as the record date for its rights issue of equity shares. Effective from the same date, all transactions in the company's equity shares must be conducted on an **ex-rights basis**.

## Key Points

- **Company:** Shah Metacorp Limited (BSE code: 533275)
- **Record Date:** 27 May 2026
- **Ex-Rights Date:** 27 May 2026 (Settlement No. DR-639/2026-2027)
- **Rights Ratio:** 36 equity shares for every 311 equity shares held
- **Face Value:** Re. 1/- per share
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 4.86 per share (Re. 1/- face value + Rs. 3.86 premium)
- **Payment Terms:** Full issue price of Rs. 4.86 per share is payable on application
- **Segment:** Equity – Dematerialised Securities, Rolling Settlement

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification under BSE listing compliance and operations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all transactions in Shah Metacorp Limited equity shares are executed on an **ex-rights basis** with effect from 27 May 2026.
- Members should update their systems to reflect the ex-rights adjustment from the specified date and settlement number.

## Impact Assessment

The rights issue is relatively small in scale (36 shares per 311 held, at Rs. 4.86 per share). Existing shareholders of Shah Metacorp Limited should take note of the record date to determine their eligibility for the rights entitlement. Buyers of shares on or after the ex-rights date (27 May 2026) will not be entitled to participate in the rights issue. The low issue price (Rs. 4.86) may attract retail participation. Overall market impact is limited to shareholders and traders of this specific scrip.
