## Summary

BSE has notified that Brigade Enterprises Limited (Scrip Code: 532929/132929) has fixed **17 June 2026** as the record date for a bonus issue. Equity shares will trade on an ex-bonus basis from 17 June 2026. Approximately 81,540,595 bonus equity shares will be allotted on 18 June 2026.

## Key Points

- Bonus ratio: **1 bonus equity share for every 3 equity shares held** (1:3)
- Face value of bonus shares: Rs. 10/- each
- Record date: **17 June 2026**
- Ex-bonus effective date: **17 June 2026** (Settlement No. DR-653/2026-2027)
- Total bonus shares to be allotted: **81,540,595 equity shares** on 18 June 2026
- Reference: BSE Notice No. 20260611-46; Company letter dated 9 June 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification pursuant to BSE Listing Regulations requiring disclosure of record dates for bonus issues.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that Brigade Enterprises Limited shares will trade ex-bonus from **17 June 2026**
- Members should update client portfolios and settlement systems to reflect the adjusted ex-bonus price from the effective date
- Settlement No. DR-653/2026-2027 applies for ex-bonus transactions

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Notice Date | 11 June 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

The 1:3 bonus issue will increase Brigade Enterprises Limited's equity share count by approximately 81.54 million shares, proportionally reducing the share price on the ex-bonus date. Existing shareholders will receive one additional share for every three held, with no cash outflow. The share count expansion may improve liquidity in the counter. Trading members and clearing systems must adjust for the ex-bonus price adjustment effective 17 June 2026.
