## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that three new Treasury Bills (T-Bills) will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange under G GROUP - DEBT INSTRUMENTS in the Debt segment, effective April 24, 2026. The bills include 91-day, 182-day, and 364-day maturities.

## Key Points

- Three T-Bills listed effective April 24, 2026 under G GROUP - DEBT INSTRUMENTS
- Scrip codes: 805205 (91TB230726), 805206 (182T221026), 805207 (364TB22427)
- ISINs: IN002026X032, IN002026Y030, IN002026Z037
- Market lot size is 1 for all three securities
- Trading will be suspended 2 working days prior to each bill's maturity/redemption date (excluding bank holidays)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice for newly issued government Treasury Bills.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must note the trading suspension rule: the listed T-Bills will not be available for trading two (2) working days prior to the Maturity/Redemption Date (Maturity Date T minus 2 trading days, excluding bank holidays).

## Important Dates

- **Listing effective date:** April 24, 2026
- **91-day T-Bill (91TB230726) maturity:** July 23, 2026
- **182-day T-Bill (182T221026) maturity:** October 22, 2026
- **364-day T-Bill (364TB22427) maturity:** April 22, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine administrative listing of sovereign government securities. The T-Bills provide short-to-medium term risk-free investment options for institutional and retail participants in the debt segment. No disruption to existing trading activity is expected. Trading members should update their systems to reflect the new scrip codes and observe the pre-maturity trading suspension window.
