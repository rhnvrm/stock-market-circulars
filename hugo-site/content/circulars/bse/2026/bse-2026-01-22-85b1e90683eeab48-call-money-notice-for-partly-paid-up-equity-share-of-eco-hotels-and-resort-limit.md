## Summary

BSE has activated the Call Money Module on its IBBS platform for the first call on partly paid-up equity shares of Eco Hotels and Resort Limited. Shareholders holding 1,28,76,808 partly paid-up equity shares must pay Rs. 3.80 per share during the issue period from January 27, 2026 to February 10, 2026. The call money collection will be facilitated through Self-Certified Syndicate Banks (SCSBs) and Trading Members with 3-in-1 type accounts. Bigshare Services Private Limited will serve as the Registrar to the Issue.

## Key Points

- First call on partly paid-up equity shares of Eco Hotels and Resort Limited
- Total partly paid-up shares subject to call: 1,28,76,808 shares
- Call money amount: Rs. 3.80 per partly paid-up equity share
- Security symbol: ECOCM
- Market lot: 1 share
- Issue period: 15 days (January 27 to February 10, 2026)
- Bidding hours: 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM daily
- Registrar: Bigshare Services Private Limited
- Call Money Module available to SCSBs and Trading Members with 3-in-1 accounts
- Process follows SEBI circular dated December 8, 2020 and BSE notice no. 20210104-37 dated January 4, 2021

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The call money process follows existing framework established by:
- SEBI circular dated December 8, 2020
- BSE Exchange notice no. 20210104-37 dated January 4, 2021

## Compliance Requirements

**For Trading Members and SCSBs:**
- Submit details of call money notices collected through the Call Money Module on IBBS platform during the issue period
- Process call money payments from shareholders holding partly paid-up shares
- Ensure compliance with the established call money collection framework

**For Shareholders:**
- Pay Rs. 3.80 per partly paid-up equity share within the specified issue period
- Submit call money payment through SCSBs or Trading Members with 3-in-1 accounts

**Market Impact:** Medium - This call money notice affects only the shareholders of Eco Hotels and Resort Limited who hold partly paid-up equity shares. The total amount to be collected is approximately Rs. 48.93 crores (1,28,76,808 shares Ã— Rs. 3.80).
