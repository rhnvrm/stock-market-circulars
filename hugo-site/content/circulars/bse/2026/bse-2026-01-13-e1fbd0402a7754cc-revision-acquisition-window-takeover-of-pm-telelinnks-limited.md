## Summary

BSE has revised the acquisition window (takeover) period for P.M. TELELINNKS LIMITED. The window will now be extended from Tuesday, January 27, 2026, to Wednesday, January 28, 2026. This revision is necessary to account for two holidays: Monday, January 26, 2026 (SEBI Holiday) and Thursday, January 15, 2026 (Exchange Holiday due to Municipal Corporation Elections in Maharashtra).

## Key Points

- Acquisition window for P.M. TELELINNKS LIMITED extended by one day
- New window period: Tuesday, January 27, 2026 to Wednesday, January 28, 2026
- Extension accounts for SEBI holiday on January 26, 2026
- Extension accounts for exchange holiday on January 15, 2026 (Municipal Corporation Elections in Maharashtra)
- This is a revision to notice no. 20260109-14 issued on January 09, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an administrative adjustment to the previously announced acquisition window schedule to accommodate intervening holidays.

## Compliance Requirements

Market participants involved in the takeover of P.M. TELELINNKS LIMITED must note the revised acquisition window dates and plan their activities accordingly.

## Important Dates

- **January 15, 2026 (Thursday)**: Exchange Holiday (Municipal Corporation Elections in Maharashtra)
- **January 26, 2026 (Monday)**: SEBI Holiday
- **January 27, 2026 (Tuesday)**: Revised acquisition window start date
- **January 28, 2026 (Wednesday)**: Revised acquisition window end date

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to participants in the P.M. TELELINNKS LIMITED takeover process. The one-day extension ensures adequate trading time despite the intervening holidays. This is a procedural adjustment with minimal market-wide impact.
