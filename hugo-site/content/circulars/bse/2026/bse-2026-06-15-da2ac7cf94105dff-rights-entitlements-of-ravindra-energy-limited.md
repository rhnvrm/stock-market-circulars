## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Rights Entitlements (REs) for Renunciation of Ravindra Energy Limited will be available for trading on the Exchange from June 16, 2026 to June 19, 2026. The underlying Rights Issue itself remains open from June 16 to June 24, 2026.

## Key Points

- Rights Entitlements (REs) trading commences on Tuesday, June 16, 2026
- Scrip Code: 751122 | Scrip ID: RELTD-RE | ISIN: INE206N20018
- Market Lot: 1 | Group: R
- Settlement on T+1 Rolling Settlement basis, trade-for-trade
- REs are tradable in dematerialised form only
- Trading members must inform clients that REs are not ordinary shares
- REs that are neither subscribed nor renounced by the Issue Closing Date will lapse and be extinguished

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. Trading is governed by existing framework referenced in notice no. 20200305-21 (March 5, 2020) and notice no. 20200518-34 (May 18, 2020).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must inform all clients about commencement of trading in REs, including the ISIN (INE206N20018)
- Members must clarify that REs should not be mistaken for ordinary shares of Ravindra Energy Limited
- Contract notes and bills for RE purchases must prominently include a disclaimer stating that purchase of REs only confers the right to participate in the Rights Issue or renounce REs before issue closure

## Important Dates

- **June 16, 2026**: Commencement of RE trading and Rights Issue opening
- **June 19, 2026**: Last day of RE trading for renunciation
- **June 24, 2026**: Rights Issue closing date; unsubscribed and unrenounced REs lapse after this date

## Impact Assessment

This notice has a limited but direct impact on trading members and shareholders of Ravindra Energy Limited. Eligible shareholders may trade their REs during the short 4-day window (June 16–19). The trade-for-trade settlement and T+1 basis adds operational considerations for members. REs that go unacted upon will be extinguished after June 24, 2026, so timely client communication is important.
