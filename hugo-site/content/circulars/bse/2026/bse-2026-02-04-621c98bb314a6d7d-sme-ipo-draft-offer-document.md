## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document of Panchatv Bharat Limited, filed on February 02, 2026, containing all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations. The document is available on the BSE SME website for a minimum period of 21 days for public comments.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Panchatv Bharat Limited on February 02, 2026
- Document contains all disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website: www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public comments are invited within 21 days from the notice date
- Comments should be submitted to: equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard procedural notification for SME IPO draft offer document publication.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants and public can review the Draft Offer Document on the BSE SME website
- Those wishing to provide comments must submit them to equity.ipo@bseindia.com within 21 days from February 04, 2026
- The draft offer document will remain available on the website for at least 21 days as per regulatory requirements

## Important Dates

- **February 02, 2026**: Draft Offer Document filed by Panchatv Bharat Limited
- **February 04, 2026**: Notice date and start of public comment period
- **February 25, 2026**: Last date for submitting public comments (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This notification has minimal impact on existing market operations. It is a procedural announcement for a new SME IPO candidate entering the pre-listing stage. The public comment period is a standard regulatory requirement under ICDR Regulations. Impact is limited to potential investors and stakeholders interested in Panchatv Bharat Limited's IPO. No immediate action required for other market participants.
