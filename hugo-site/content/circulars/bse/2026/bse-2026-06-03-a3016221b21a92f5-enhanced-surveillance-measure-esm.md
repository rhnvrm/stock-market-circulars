## Summary

BSE has updated the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework effective early June 2026. Securities newly added under ESM (Annexure I) will attract a minimum 100% margin on all open and new positions from June 05, 2026, and will shift from Rolling Settlement to Trade-for-Trade segment. Separately, some securities are being downgraded to Stage I from June 04, 2026, and a set of securities (Annexure II) is being removed from the ESM framework effective June 03, 2026.

## Key Points

- Securities in Annexure I will attract **minimum 100% margin** on all open positions as on June 04, 2026 and new positions from June 05, 2026.
- These securities will shift from **Rolling Settlement to Trade-for-Trade segment** w.e.f. June 05, 2026.
- Securities moving to **Stage II** will have a **2% price band** and be subject to **Periodic Call Auction** w.e.f. June 04, 2026.
- Securities meeting criteria for **downgrade to Stage I** will be moved accordingly w.e.f. June 04, 2026.
- Securities listed in **Annexure II** will exit the ESM framework w.e.f. June 03, 2026.
- A consolidated list of all securities currently under ESM is provided in **Annexure III**.
- ESM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures.
- Shortlisting under ESM is purely a market surveillance action and does not imply adverse action against the company.

## Regulatory Changes

This notice updates the ESM framework established under prior notices: 20230602-44 (June 02, 2023), 20230718-46 (July 18, 2023), 20240809-42 (August 09, 2024), 20240920-63 (September 20, 2024), 20241004-65 (October 04, 2024), and 20250725-61 (July 25, 2025). Securities exiting the framework will have their price bands reinstated to pre-ESM levels, unless subject to another surveillance measure.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Members/Brokers**: Must collect minimum 100% margin on affected securities from the specified effective dates.
- **Members/Brokers**: Must comply with Trade-for-Trade settlement restrictions for ESM securities from June 05, 2026.
- **Members/Brokers**: Stage II securities must be traded under Periodic Call Auction with a 2% price band.
- Participants should review Annexures I, II, and III to identify affected securities.
- For clarifications, members may contact: **bse.surv@bseindia.com**

## Important Dates

| Date | Event |
|------|-------|
| June 03, 2026 | Securities in Annexure II exit the ESM framework |
| June 04, 2026 | Stage I movement effective; Stage II 2% price band + Periodic Call Auction effective; reference date for open positions |
| June 05, 2026 | 100% margin requirement effective on new positions; Trade-for-Trade segment shift effective |

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a **high impact** on traders and investors in the affected securities. Imposition of 100% margins drastically increases the cost of holding positions, effectively deterring leveraged trading. The shift to Trade-for-Trade settlement eliminates netting benefits and increases settlement obligations. Stage II securities face additional restrictions via 2% price bands and Periodic Call Auction, severely limiting price discovery and liquidity. Conversely, securities exiting the framework (Annexure II) will see restored trading conditions and price bands, potentially improving liquidity. Market participants should review all three annexures to assess exposure.
