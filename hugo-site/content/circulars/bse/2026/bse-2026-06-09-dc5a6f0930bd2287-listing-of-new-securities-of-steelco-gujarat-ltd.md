## Summary

BSE has listed 13,30,060 new equity shares of Steelco Gujarat Ltd. (Scrip Code 500399) issued on a rights basis. These shares are permitted for trading on the Exchange effective Wednesday, June 10, 2026.

## Key Points

- 13,30,060 equity shares of Rs. 10/- face value each, issued at a premium of Rs. 102/- (total issue price Rs. 112/- per share)
- Issued on rights basis; ISIN: INE629B01032
- Distribution numbers: 4966013 to 6296072
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Scrip Code: 500399

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice informing trading members of the listing of new securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the listing and may trade these securities from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: June 06, 2026
- Trading effective from: June 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The listing adds approximately 13.3 lakh new equity shares of Steelco Gujarat Ltd. to the tradeable float. These shares are pari-passu with existing shares and result from a previously announced rights issue, so no new information is introduced to the market.
