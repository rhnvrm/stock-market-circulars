## Summary

BSE has announced the availability of the Draft Offer Document for DHANWEL HYBRID SEEDS LIMITED's SME IPO, filed on December 31, 2025. The document is hosted on the BSE SME website for a minimum period of 21 days to invite public comments as per ICDR Regulations Schedule VI requirements.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by DHANWEL HYBRID SEEDS LIMITED on December 31, 2025
- Document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website for at least 21 days for public review
- Public comments are invited and must be submitted within 21 days from notice date
- Comments should be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural notice for SME IPO draft offer document filing.

## Compliance Requirements

- The Draft Offer Document is available at www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public comments, if any, must be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com within 21 days from January 01, 2026 (deadline: January 22, 2026)
- Document complies with Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations disclosure requirements

## Important Dates

- **Draft Offer Document Filing Date**: December 31, 2025
- **Notice Date**: January 01, 2026
- **Public Comment Period**: 21 days from January 01, 2026
- **Public Comment Deadline**: January 22, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This notice has limited market impact as it pertains to a single SME IPO filing. The public comment period is a standard regulatory requirement under ICDR Regulations for transparency and investor protection. Impact is restricted to prospective investors interested in DHANWEL HYBRID SEEDS LIMITED's upcoming SME IPO. No immediate trading or operational implications for existing market participants.
