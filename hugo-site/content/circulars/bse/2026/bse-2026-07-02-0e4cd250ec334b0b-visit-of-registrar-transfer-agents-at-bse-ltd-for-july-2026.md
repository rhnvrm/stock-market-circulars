## Summary

BSE Ltd. has issued a notice under its 'Others' category and 'DIS' department, providing the schedule of visits by Registrar & Transfer Agents (RTAs) to the BSE office for the month of July 2026. The complete list of RTAs and their scheduled visit dates is provided in the attached document (RTAList).

## Key Points

- Notice No. 20260702-7 dated 02 Jul 2026 issued by BSE.
- Pertains to the General segment under the Department of Investor Services (DIS).
- Lists Registrar & Transfer Agents scheduled to visit BSE Ltd. during July 2026.
- Intended to facilitate investor services and coordination between BSE and RTAs.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine administrative/informational notice and does not introduce any regulatory changes.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance actions are required from listed companies, brokers, or investors. RTAs named in the attached list should adhere to their scheduled visit dates.

## Important Dates

Notice dated 02 Jul 2026; specific RTA visit dates are detailed in the attached RTAList document.

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact, purely administrative notice concerning the operational schedule of RTA visits at BSE. It has no bearing on trading, listing, or market pricing of securities and is primarily relevant to RTAs and BSE's investor services department.
