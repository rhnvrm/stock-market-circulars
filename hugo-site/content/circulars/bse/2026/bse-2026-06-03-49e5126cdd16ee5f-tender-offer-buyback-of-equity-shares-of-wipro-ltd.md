## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that Wipro Ltd. (BSE Code: 507685) has fixed **June 5, 2026** as the record date for its buyback tender offer. The record date is used to determine the entitlement and identify the names of equity shareholders eligible to participate in the buyback offer under the Dematerialised Securities – Rolling Settlement Segment.

## Key Points

- Wipro Ltd. (BSE Code: 507685) has announced a buyback of equity shares via a tender offer.
- Record date is set as **05/06/2026** for determining shareholder entitlement.
- Ex-entitlement date is also **05/06/2026**.
- Settlement reference: DR-645/2026-2027.
- Applicable under the Dematerialised Securities – Rolling Settlement Segment.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notification under existing buyback regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are requested to take note of the record date and ex-entitlement date.
- Members should communicate the buyback eligibility criteria to their clients holding Wipro Ltd. equity shares.
- Shareholders must hold shares as of the record date (05/06/2026) to be eligible to participate in the buyback offer.

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Notice Date | 03 Jun 2026 |
| Record Date | 05 Jun 2026 |
| Ex-Entitlement Date | 05 Jun 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

This notification is relevant to existing shareholders of Wipro Ltd. who may be eligible to tender their shares in the buyback offer. The record date of June 5, 2026 is imminent (two days from the notice date), so shareholders and trading members should act promptly. The buyback offer may provide an exit opportunity at a potentially premium price for eligible shareholders. No broader market impact is anticipated beyond Wipro's own share price dynamics.
