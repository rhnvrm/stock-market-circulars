## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 2,87,880 new equity shares of Som Distilleries & Breweries Ltd. (Scrip Code: 507514) issued to promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion. The shares will be available for trading from January 27, 2026, and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares. All shares are subject to lock-in until July 28, 2027.

## Key Points

- 2,87,880 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 108/-
- Total issue price: Rs. 110/- per share
- Issued to promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE480C01038
- Distinctive Numbers: 203473273 to 203761152
- Date of allotment: February 22, 2025
- Scrip Code: 507514

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification following preferential allotment procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to note the new securities for trading purposes
- Lock-in restrictions must be observed for all 2,87,880 shares until the specified date
- Shares cannot be transferred or sold before lock-in expiry

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 23, 2026
- **Allotment Date**: February 22, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: January 27, 2026
- **Lock-in Period Ends**: July 28, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - The preferential allotment to promoters represents a modest increase in share capital with minimal immediate trading impact due to complete lock-in until July 2027.

**Liquidity Impact**: Negligible in near-term as all shares are locked-in and held by promoters.

**Shareholder Impact**: Minor dilution for existing shareholders. The issue price of Rs. 110/- indicates the valuation at which promoters subscribed to the warrants.

**Corporate Action**: Reflects promoter confidence through warrant conversion and capital infusion into the company.
