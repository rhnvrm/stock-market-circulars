## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 9,01,526 new equity shares of Praxis Home Retail Limited (Scrip Code: 540901) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Thursday, March 5, 2026. These shares were issued on a preferential basis to non-promoters at a premium.

## Key Points

- 9,01,526 equity shares of Rs. 5/- face value each issued at a premium of Rs. 38.26/- per share
- Issue price: Rs. 43.26/- per share
- Issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE546Y01022
- Distribution numbers: 18,49,10,685 to 18,58,12,210

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification pursuant to a preferential allotment approved by the company.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities available for trading from March 5, 2026
- Lock-in compliance: All 9,01,526 shares are subject to lock-in until September 29, 2026 and cannot be traded until that date

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** November 10, 2025
- **Trading Commencement:** March 5, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry:** September 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 9,01,526 new shares to the tradable float of Praxis Home Retail Limited, though all shares are under lock-in until September 29, 2026, meaning no immediate increase in free-float liquidity. Impact is moderate and confined to shareholders monitoring dilution from the preferential allotment. Post lock-in expiry, a potential supply of ~9 lakh shares may enter the market.
