## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that trading in Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) issued by Avanse Financial Services Limited will be suspended with effect from 11 June 2026. The suspension is in connection with the company's Record Date fixed for Early Redemption of the NCD due to a coupon reset process, along with payment of interest.

## Key Points

- Trading in the specified NCD series is prohibited from 11 June 2026.
- The Record Date is 11 June 2026 for Early Redemption under the coupon reset process and for interest payment.
- The affected security is AFSL-9.40%-27-9-27-PVT (ISIN: INE087P07402, BSE Code: 976056), issued under DR-649/2026-2027.
- No dealings in these debentures are permitted from 11 June 2026 onwards.
- The notice has been issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President – Listing Compliance and Operations.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced. This is an operational notice under existing BSE listing and trading regulations that mandates suspension of trading around record dates for NCD-related corporate actions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any buy or sell transactions in the debentures of Avanse Financial Services Limited (ISIN: INE087P07402) from 11 June 2026.
- Members are required to take note of the suspension and ensure client orders in this security are not processed on or after the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Record Date:** 11 June 2026
- **No Dealings From:** 11 June 2026
- **Purpose:** Early Redemption of NCD due to Coupon Reset Process and Payment of Interest

## Impact Assessment

The suspension is limited to a single NCD series (AFSL-9.40%-27-9-27-PVT) in the debt segment. Existing holders will be subject to the early redemption at the terms determined by the coupon reset process. Market impact is narrow, affecting only investors holding this specific instrument. Trading members need to update their systems to block transactions in ISIN INE087P07402 from 11 June 2026.
