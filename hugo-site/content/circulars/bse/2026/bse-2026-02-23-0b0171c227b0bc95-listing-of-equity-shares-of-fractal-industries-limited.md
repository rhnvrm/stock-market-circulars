## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members that the equity shares of Fractal Industries Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange. The exact date of listing and further security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Fractal Industries Limited is set to be listed on the BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544707
- Trading Symbol: FIL
- ISIN: INE1W3Q01011
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing admission notice for a new SME segment entrant.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should note the upcoming listing of Fractal Industries Limited and await the follow-up notice confirming the exact listing date and security details.
- Members should update their systems with scrip code 544707 and ISIN INE1W3Q01011 in preparation for trading commencement.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: February 23, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced via a separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a moderate market impact limited to the BSE SME segment. It signals the imminent debut of Fractal Industries Limited (FIL) on the exchange. Investors and trading members interested in SME listings should monitor for the follow-up notice that will confirm the exact listing date and additional security details. No immediate trading action is required until the listing date is formally announced.
