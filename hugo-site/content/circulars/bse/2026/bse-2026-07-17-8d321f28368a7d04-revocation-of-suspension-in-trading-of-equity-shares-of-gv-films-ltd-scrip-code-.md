## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that the suspension in trading of equity shares of GV Films Ltd (Scrip Code: 523277) will be revoked with effect from July 27, 2026. Trading in the company's securities will resume in the "XT" group, pursuant to SEBI's Master Circular No. HO/49/14/14(7)2025-CFD-POD2/I/3762/2026 dated July 11, 2023 (last updated January 30, 2026), which supersedes the erstwhile SEBI Circular No. SEBI/HO/CFD/CMD/CIR/P/2020/12 dated January 22, 2020.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension on GV Films Ltd equity shares (Scrip Code: 523277) will be revoked w.e.f. July 27, 2026
- Trading will resume in the "XT" group
- A company profile is attached as Annexure I to the notice
- The Information Memorandum for the company will be published on BSE's website under Corporates → Corporate Filings → Information Memorandum → Revocation
- Notice issued by Listing Compliance-Monitoring department, signed by Netra Sahani (Deputy Vice President) and Kunal Borkar (Manager), Listing Compliance

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; the action is taken under the existing framework of SEBI Master Circular No. HO/49/14/14(7)2025-CFD-POD2/I/3762/2026, which governs revocation of suspension of trading for listed companies.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to take note of the revocation and the resumption of trading in the "XT" group. No specific action is required from investors beyond awareness that trading will resume on the effective date.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 17, 2026
- Effective Date of Revocation: July 27, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The revocation restores tradability for GV Films Ltd shareholders and potential investors, ending a period of suspended trading. Placement in the "XT" group may carry trading restrictions typical of this segment (such as trade-for-trade settlement), so investors should verify applicable trading conditions before transacting. The impact is company-specific and does not affect the broader market.
