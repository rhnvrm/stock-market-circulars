## Summary

BSE has issued a periodic update to the Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LT-ASM) framework, first introduced via notice dated March 21, 2018 and subsequently revised through multiple notices including August 09, 2024 and September 20, 2024. This notice lists securities newly placed under LT-ASM, securities moving to higher LT-ASM stages, securities moving out of the framework, and securities being transferred to trade-for-trade groups (T/XT/MT/TS) while remaining under LT-ASM. Detailed scrip-level lists are provided in Annexures I through III attached to the notice.

## Key Points

- 100% margin shall apply on all open positions as on July 21, 2026, and on new positions created from July 22, 2026 onwards, for securities in Annexure I (Part A).
- Securities moving to higher LT-ASM stages, per Annexure I (Part B), take effect July 20, 2026.
- Lower price band shall apply w.e.f. July 20, 2026 for the securities covered under Part A/B.
- Securities under the June 04, 2021 framework (Annexure I Part D) will continue in LT-ASM but be transferred to T/XT/MT/TS trading groups w.e.f. July 22, 2026.
- Securities exiting the LT-ASM framework, per Annexure II, take effect July 20, 2026; their price bands revert to pre-shortlisting levels unless subject to another surveillance measure.
- A consolidated list of all securities currently under the framework is provided in Annexure III.
- Shortlisting under this framework is based purely on market surveillance criteria (including XBRL submissions by listed companies) and does not constitute an adverse view of the company.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced; this is a periodic (routine) application of the existing LT-ASM framework established via BSE notices dated March 21, 2018, October 27, 2018, December 04, 2020, June 04, 2021, August 09, 2024, and September 20, 2024.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must apply 100% margin on open and new positions in the identified securities from the specified effective dates.
- Members must observe the revised (lower) price bands and any group reclassification (T/XT/MT/TS) for affected scrips.
- Queries may be directed to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- July 20, 2026: New LT-ASM stage migrations, lower price band applicability, and framework exits take effect.
- July 21, 2026: Reference date for open positions subject to 100% margin.
- July 22, 2026: 100% margin applies to new positions; securities under the June 2021 framework transfer to T/XT/MT/TS trading groups.

## Impact Assessment

Securities newly added to or moved to higher stages of LT-ASM face materially higher trading costs due to 100% margin requirements and tighter price bands, which typically reduces liquidity and volatility for those scrips. Conversely, securities exiting the framework should see restored price bands and normalized margin treatment, potentially improving trading activity. Since the specific list of affected companies is contained in the PDF annexures rather than the notice text, market participants should review the attachments directly to identify impacted stocks.
