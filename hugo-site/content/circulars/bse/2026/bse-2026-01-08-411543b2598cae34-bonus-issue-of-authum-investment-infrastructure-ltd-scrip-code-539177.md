## Summary

Authum Investment & Infrastructure Limited (Scrip Code: 539177, ISIN: INE206F01022) has issued 67,93,80,400 bonus equity shares of Re. 1 each. The bonus shares will be listed on January 15, 2026, with a deemed allotment date of January 14, 2026. The new shares have distinctive numbers ranging from 169845101 to 849225500.

## Key Points

- Company: Authum Investment & Infrastructure Limited
- Scrip Code: 539177
- ISIN: INE206F01022
- Number of bonus shares: 67,93,80,400 equity shares of Re. 1 each
- Distinctive number range: 169845101 to 849225500
- Bonus ratio: 4:1 (4 bonus shares for every 1 existing share)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard corporate action notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Exchange members and investors should note the new distinctive number range for the bonus shares
- Demat accounts will be credited with bonus shares as per the bonus ratio
- Trading members must ensure proper settlement of trades post-listing

## Important Dates

- **Deemed Date of Allotment**: January 14, 2026
- **Effective Listing Date**: January 15, 2026
- **Circular Date**: January 7, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: The bonus issue will increase the total number of outstanding shares by approximately 400% (4:1 ratio), leading to proportional dilution in share price while maintaining shareholder value. This will improve liquidity and make the stock more accessible to retail investors.

**Shareholder Impact**: Existing shareholders will receive 4 bonus shares for every 1 share held, increasing their total shareholding without any additional investment. The percentage ownership remains unchanged.

**Trading Impact**: Post-listing on January 15, 2026, the stock price is expected to adjust proportionately to reflect the increased share count. Higher share count may lead to improved trading volumes and liquidity.
