## Summary

BSE has notified all market participants that effective Friday, July 10, 2026, the equity shares of Lord's Mark Industries Limited (formerly known as Kratos Energy & Infrastructure Limited), the Corporate Debtor under a Pre-Packaged Insolvency Resolution Process (PPIRP), shall be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange under the XT Group. Trading will continue in the Trade-to-Trade segment as per earlier Exchange Notice No. 20230210-55 dated February 10, 2023.

## Key Points

- Company: Lord's Mark Industries Limited (formerly Kratos Energy & Infrastructure Limited), Registered Office at 317, Maker Chamber V, 221, Nariman Point, Mumbai, Maharashtra, 400021.
- Total securities: 42,66,22,217 fully paid-up equity shares of Rs. 10/- each, comprising 25,498 shares allotted post capital reduction and 42,65,96,719 shares allotted pursuant to merger of Lords Marks India Limited (Transferor Company) with the Company.
- Distinctive numbers: 1 to 426622217.
- Scrip Code: 501261; Group: XT; Market Lot: 1; Face Value: Rs. 10/- each fully paid up.
- Scrip ID / Abbreviated name on BOLT System: LORDSMARK.
- ISIN: INE567L01025.
- Lock-in details: Not applicable.
- The Base Resolution Plan under the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code, 2016 was approved by the Hon'ble NCLT (Mumbai Bench) order dated July 28, 2025.
- Resolution plan involved extinguishment of 4,90,051 shares held by promoters, maintenance of at least 5% public shareholding per Rule 19A(5) of SCRR, 1957, and cancellation of remaining public shareholding.
- The merger of Lords Marks India Limited (unlisted Transferor Company) with the Company resulted in allotment of 42,65,96,719 shares to eligible shareholders of the Transferor Company at an exchange ratio of 1.25 shares of the Company for every 1 share held in the Transferor Company.
- Issued, subscribed and paid-up share capital changed from Rs. 1,00,00,000/- (10,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each) to Rs. 4,26,62,22,170/- (42,66,22,217 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each).
- Record date for the resolution plan was fixed as November 20, 2025, per Exchange Notice No. 20251119-9 dated November 19, 2025.

## Regulatory Changes

The listing follows the NCLT-approved Base Resolution Plan under the PPIRP framework of the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code, 2016, along with a scheme of merger involving Lords Marks India Limited. Public shareholding compliance is being maintained per Rule 19A(5) of the Securities Contracts (Regulation) Rules, 1957 (as amended January 2, 2023).

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must recognize the Company's shares as trading under the XT Group and Trade-to-Trade segment from the listing date.
- The scrip will be included in the special pre-open session for IPO and other category scrips per SEBI Circulars CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 and CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012.
- Depository participants and market participants should note the credit of dematerialized shares under the applicable temporary/interim arrangements as described in the notice.

## Important Dates

- November 20, 2025: Record date for giving effect to the resolution plan.
- July 28, 2025: NCLT (Mumbai Bench) order approving the Base Resolution Plan.
- July 10, 2026: Effective date of listing of equity shares under XT Group.

## Impact Assessment

The listing enables trading resumption/commencement for Lord's Mark Industries Limited shareholders following completion of the insolvency resolution and merger process. Trading is restricted to the Trade-to-Trade segment, limiting speculative activity and requiring delivery-based settlement. The special pre-open session requirement helps in fair price discovery for this newly listed/relisted scrip, reducing volatility risk on debut trading.
