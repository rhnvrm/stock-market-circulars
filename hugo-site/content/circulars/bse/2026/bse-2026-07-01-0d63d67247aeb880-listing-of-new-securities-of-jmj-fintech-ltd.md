## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that they are permitted to trade in new securities of JMJ Fintech Ltd (Scrip Code: 538834) with effect from Thursday, July 02, 2026. The new securities consist of 8,51,137 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each, issued pursuant to conversion of partly paid rights shares to fully paid-up shares.

## Key Points

- 8,51,137 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each are being listed following conversion of partly paid shares (issued on rights basis) to fully paid-up shares.
- ISIN: INE242Q01016
- Distinctive numbers: 36784412 - 37635548
- The new shares rank pari-passu with the existing equity shares of the company.
- No attachments were included with this notice.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine listing permission notice issued by BSE under its Listing Operations department.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must recognize these newly listed fully paid-up shares as tradable under the existing scrip code (538834) from the effective date, treating them as pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 1, 2026
- Trading effective from: Thursday, July 2, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact, routine corporate action affecting a small block of shares (8,51,137) of JMJ Fintech Ltd arising from conversion of partly paid rights shares to fully paid status. It does not affect overall market operations and is relevant primarily to existing shareholders and traders of this specific scrip.
