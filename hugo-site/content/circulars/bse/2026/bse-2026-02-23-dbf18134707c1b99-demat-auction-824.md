## Summary

BSE has published the demat auction list for Settlement No. 824 dated February 20, 2026. The auction covers 47 scrips where delivery shortfalls exist, with quantities ranging from 1 to 2,668 shares per scrip.

## Key Points

- 47 scrips listed for demat auction due to delivery shortfalls

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational auction notice under BSE's settlement and auction procedures for handling delivery shortfalls.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members with delivery obligations in the listed scrips must participate in or comply with the auction process
- Buyers of short-delivered securities will receive shares through the auction mechanism or cash compensation
- Applicable auction charges and price differences will apply per BSE bylaws

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: February 20, 2026 (Settlement No. 824)
- Circular Publication Date: February 23, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This is a routine demat auction covering delivery shortfalls in 47 scrips across sectors including energy, banking, technology, and infrastructure. The largest auction quantity is Gokul Refoils at 2,668 shares. Impact is limited to the specific parties involved in these settlement shortfalls and does not represent any systemic or policy-level change.
