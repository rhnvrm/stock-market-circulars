## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 58,36,990 new equity shares of Regal Entertainment & Consultants Ltd. (Scrip Code: 531033) have been listed and are permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Friday, April 24, 2026. These shares were issued on a rights basis at Rs. 14/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- with a premium of Rs. 4/-).

## Key Points

- 58,36,990 new equity shares listed for trading effective April 24, 2026
- Issued on rights basis at Rs. 10/- face value with Rs. 4/- premium (total issue price Rs. 14/-)
- ISIN: INE101E01010
- Distribution numbers: 3072101 to 8909090
- Date of allotment: April 21, 2026
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification pursuant to a completed rights issue.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed and may commence trading in the newly listed shares from April 24, 2026. No specific compliance action is required beyond standard trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** April 21, 2026
- **Listing / Trading Commencement Date:** April 24, 2026 (Friday)

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 58,36,990 equity shares to the tradeable float of Regal Entertainment & Consultants Ltd. (Scrip Code: 531033). Since the new shares are pari-passu with existing shares and carry the same ISIN (INE101E01010), they will trade alongside existing shares without any distinction. The rights issue price of Rs. 14/- per share may influence near-term price discovery. Impact is limited to existing shareholders and traders in this specific scrip.
