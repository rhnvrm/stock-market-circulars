## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 8,30,000 new equity shares of UVS Hospitality and Services Limited (Scrip Code: 531652, ISIN: INE528C01018) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Tuesday, June 30, 2026. The shares were issued at Rs. 17/- each (face value Rs. 10/- + premium Rs. 7/-) on a preferential basis to Promoter and Non-Promoter shareholders pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 8,30,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 7/- (issue price Rs. 17/-) via preferential allotment
- Shares issued pursuant to conversion of warrants to Promoter and Non-Promoter
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 35812601 to 36642600
- Effective date for trading: June 30, 2026
- ISIN: INE528C01018

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification following preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to update their systems to permit trading in the newly listed shares from June 30, 2026. Lock-in restrictions apply to specific tranches and must be observed.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date 1:** 09/10/2025 — 2,60,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 35812601–36072600)
- **Allotment Date 2:** 18/10/2025 — 1,50,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 36072601–36222600)
- **Allotment Date 3:** 24/10/2025 — 4,20,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 36222601–36642600)
- **Trading commences:** June 30, 2026
- **Lock-in expiry (2,60,000 shares):** March 31, 2027
- **Lock-in expiry (1,50,000 shares):** March 31, 2028
- **Lock-in expiry (4,20,000 shares):** March 31, 2027

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 8,30,000 shares to the tradeable float of UVS Hospitality and Services Limited, though a significant portion remains under lock-in until March 2027 or March 2028. The preferential issue at Rs. 17/- per share (warrant conversion) is a standard capital-raising mechanism. Market impact is expected to be minimal given the small size and lock-in constraints on most allotted shares.
