## Summary

BSE has listed 22,30,641 new equity shares of Beezaasan Explotech Limited (Scrip Code: 544369, ISIN: INE13VU01016) issued to Promoters on a preferential basis at an issue price of Rs. 236/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- plus premium of Rs. 226/-). These shares are permitted to trade on BSE with effect from Friday, June 5, 2026.

## Key Points

- **Shares Issued:** 22,30,641 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each, issued at a premium of Rs. 226/- per share
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 236/- per share
- **Allottee:** Promoters (preferential basis)
- **Distribution Numbers:** 12921134 to 15151774
- **ISIN:** INE13VU01016
- **Pari-passu:** Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- **Lock-in:** All 22,30,641 shares (Dist. Nos. 12921134 to 15151774) are locked in until December 31, 2027

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice for newly allotted securities following a preferential allotment to promoters.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed that these securities are now listed and tradeable on BSE from June 5, 2026. However, all shares are subject to a lock-in restriction until December 31, 2027, meaning the allottee (Promoters) cannot trade or transfer these shares during the lock-in period.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** March 2, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** June 5, 2026 (Friday)
- **Lock-in Expiry:** December 31, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a routine preferential allotment listing for Beezaasan Explotech Limited, a smaller-cap company. The newly listed shares are entirely under promoter lock-in until end of 2027, so there is no immediate increase in tradeable float. The preferential allotment at Rs. 236/- per share (to promoters) indicates promoter confidence in the company.
