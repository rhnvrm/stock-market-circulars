## Summary

BSE has announced the Live Activities Schedule for the Offer to Buy (Buyback) – Acquisition Window for Windlas Biotech Ltd (scrip code: 543329, symbol: WINDLAS). The buyback window is open from Thursday, April 30, 2026 to Thursday, May 7, 2026, with an offer price of Rs. 1000 per share for up to 4,70,000 equity shares.

## Key Points

- **Security:** Windlas Biotech Ltd | Symbol: WINDLAS | ISIN: INE0H5O01029 | Scrip Code: 543329
- **Offer Size:** Up to 4,70,000 equity shares
- **Offer Price:** Rs. 1000.00 per share
- **Face Value:** Rs. 5.00
- **Opening Date:** Thursday, April 30, 2026
- **Closing Date:** Thursday, May 7, 2026
- **Session Timings:** 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. (Bid Offering Period and Give-up); Take-up on last day closes at 4:00 p.m.
- Bid Modifications/Cancellations allowed from April 30 to May 6, 2026 (9:15 a.m. – 3:30 p.m.)
- Only demat shares are eligible for bidding

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice operates under existing OTB framework as per Exchange Notices 20150930-1 (September 30, 2015) and 20170203-29 (February 3, 2017).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must ensure the Investor's UCC is registered and/or updated with the correct PAN Number and Category in the Exchange UCC database.
- Members and Custodians should note that no End of Issue files will be generated on the Extranet if no securities are accepted.
- Participation is via the iBBS platform and RTRMS system; login credentials and password resets handled through Trading Operations (022-22728005 / user.trdops@bseindia.com).

## Impact Assessment

This circular is relevant to trading members and custodians who hold or manage Windlas Biotech Ltd shares on behalf of investors. The buyback at Rs. 1000 per share for up to 4,70,000 shares provides a liquidity event for eligible shareholders. The impact is stock-specific and moderate in scope; broader market impact is limited. Members must ensure operational readiness on iBBS and RTRMS platforms before April 30, 2026.
