## Summary

BSE Limited has notified trading members that the equity shares of Devson Catalyst Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange's SME segment in due course. The exact date of listing and further securities details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company: Devson Catalyst Limited
- Scrip Code: 544823
- Symbol: DEVSON
- ISIN: INE2KRP01017
- Segment: SME
- Department: DCS-Listing
- No attachment accompanies this notice

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is an administrative listing notification, not a regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should update their systems to recognize the new scrip code and symbol once trading commences. No immediate action is required until the listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 15 July 2026
- Date of listing: To be announced via a separate notice

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to trading members and investors interested in Devson Catalyst Limited. As a new SME listing, it does not affect broader market operations, but signals the addition of a new tradable security once the listing date is confirmed.
