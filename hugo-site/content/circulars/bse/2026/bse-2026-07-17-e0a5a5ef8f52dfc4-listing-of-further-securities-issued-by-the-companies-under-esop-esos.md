## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that further securities issued under Employee Stock Option Plans/Schemes (ESOP/ESOS) by nine listed companies will be listed and admitted for trading with effect from Monday, July 20, 2026. The companies involved are FSN E-Commerce Ventures (Nykaa), ICICI Bank, ICICI Lombard General Insurance, ICICI Prudential Life Insurance, Nitco, Pidilite Industries, Sona BLW Precision Forgings, Ujjivan Small Finance Bank, and Vedant Fashions (Manyavar).

## Key Points

- FSN E-Commerce Ventures Limited (Nykaa): 63,637 shares, face value ₹1
- ICICI Bank Limited: 94,547 shares, face value ₹2
- ICICI Lombard General Insurance Company Limited: 8,186 shares, face value ₹10
- ICICI Prudential Life Insurance Company Limited: 6,688 shares, face value ₹10
- Nitco Limited: 50,000 shares, face value ₹10
- Pidilite Industries Limited: 15,000 shares, face value ₹1
- Sona BLW Precision Forgings Limited: 662,301 shares, face value ₹10
- Ujjivan Small Finance Bank Limited: 572,713 shares, face value ₹10
- Vedant Fashions Limited: 1,000 shares, face value ₹1
- None of the newly listed shares are under lock-in

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine listing notice under standard BSE listing operations procedures for ESOP/ESOS-issued securities.

## Compliance Requirements

No action required from investors or trading members beyond noting the updated listed share quantities for these companies.

## Important Dates

- Effective date of listing and trading: Monday, July 20, 2026
- Notice date: Friday, July 17, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listed quantities represent incremental increases to existing share capital from employee stock option exercises, which are immaterial relative to the outstanding share base of these large-cap and mid-cap companies. No trading disruption or price impact is expected; this is a routine administrative listing update.
