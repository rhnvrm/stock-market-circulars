## Summary

BSE has informed all market participants that the equity shares of Vedanta Power Ltd (formerly known as Talwandi Sabo Power Limited), bearing BSE scrip code 544781, will be reclassified and permitted for trading under the 'B' group effective Tuesday, June 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- Equity shares of Vedanta Power Ltd (BSE scrip code: 544781) are being moved to the 'B' group.
- The change is effective from Tuesday, June 30, 2026.
- The company was formerly known as Talwandi Sabo Power Limited.
- This is a group classification change; trading in the shares will continue under the new group designation.

## Regulatory Changes

The group classification of the equity shares is being changed to 'B' group as per BSE's listing operations decision. The 'B' group on BSE typically includes companies that do not meet the criteria for higher-tier groups and may carry different trading and settlement implications.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants and brokers should update their systems to reflect the new group classification for scrip code 544781 effective June 30, 2026.
- No specific compliance action is required from investors; the change is administrative in nature.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 15, 2026
- **Effective Date:** Tuesday, June 30, 2026 (group change to 'B' group becomes applicable)

## Impact Assessment

The reclassification to 'B' group may impact the stock's liquidity and trading dynamics, as the 'B' group on BSE is generally associated with companies with lower market capitalization or reduced compliance standing. Investors and market participants should take note of the change when assessing trading strategies for Vedanta Power Ltd shares. The change does not affect the company's underlying fundamentals but may influence institutional investor participation and margin requirements.
