## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 3,544 new equity shares of Lloyds Enterprises Ltd. (Scrip Code: 512463) are available for trading effective Thursday, May 07, 2026. These shares arise from the conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid shares of Re. 1/- each, originally issued on a rights basis.

## Key Points

- 3,544 equity shares of Lloyds Enterprises Ltd. listed for trading from May 07, 2026
- Shares issued pursuant to conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid shares of Re. 1/- each on rights basis
- ISIN: INE080I01025
- Distinctive Numbers: 1404608529 to 1404612072
- New shares are pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Scrip Code: 512463

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice under BSE's Listing Operations department for newly admitted securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are permitted (and informed) to trade in these new securities from the effective date. No additional compliance action is required from members beyond standard trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** May 06, 2026
- **Trading Effective Date:** May 07, 2026 (Thursday)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The addition of 3,544 shares is a small incremental increase to the existing float of Lloyds Enterprises Ltd. The shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares, so there is no dilution of rights for current shareholders beyond what was already anticipated under the rights issue. Routine operational notice for trading members.
