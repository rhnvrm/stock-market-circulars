## Summary

BSE has notified Trading Members, Self-Certified Syndicate Banks (SCSBs), and Intermediaries that Prabha Energy Limited has requested activation of the Call Money Module on the IBBS (Internet Based Book Building Software) platform for collection of the second and final call money on its partly paid-up equity shares.

## Key Points

- Issuer: Prabha Energy Limited
- Type of Issue: Call Money Notice (Second and Final Call)
- Security Type: Partly Paid Equity Shares
- Security Symbol: PRABHAFCM
- Market Lot: 1
- Number of Partly Paid-up Equity Shares: 96,67,258
- Call Money Amount Payable: Rs. 47.52 per Partly Paid-up Equity Share
- Registrar to the Issue: MUFG Intime India Private Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this notice operationalizes the existing framework under BSE notice no. 20210104-37 dated January 4, 2021 governing the Call Money Module of the IBBS platform.

## Compliance Requirements

SCSBs and Trading Members with 3-in-1 type accounts must submit details of call money notices collected from investors during the issue period via the IBBS platform. Queries on regulatory/business rules should be directed to BSE Listing Sales; bidding-related queries to IBBS Operations; and new user ID/password reset requests to Trading Operations.

## Important Dates

- Issue Opening Date: Tuesday, July 28, 2026
- Issue Closing Date: Tuesday, August 11, 2026
- Bidding Period: 10:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. daily
- Issue Period: 15 days

## Impact Assessment

This is a procedural/operational notice affecting holders of Prabha Energy Limited's partly paid-up equity shares and the intermediaries (SCSBs, Trading Members) involved in collecting the second and final call money. It does not affect the broader market but is important for affected shareholders to fulfil their payment obligations within the stipulated window to avoid forfeiture risk.
