## Summary

BSE has listed 278,300 new equity shares of Emkay Global Financial Services Ltd (Scrip Code: 532737) issued to promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. The shares are permitted to trade with effect from February 1, 2026, and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares. All shares are subject to lock-in until July 31, 2027.

## Key Points

- 278,300 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at premium of Rs. 229.50/-
- Issue price: Rs. 239.50 per share
- Allotment date: December 12, 2025
- Issued to promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distinctive numbers: 25632464 to 25910763
- ISIN: INE296H01011
- Complete lock-in on all 278,300 shares until July 31, 2027

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification following preferential allotment procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed that these securities are now tradable on the exchange
- Lock-in restrictions must be observed until July 31, 2027
- Shares must be treated as pari-passu with existing equity shares for all corporate actions

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 12, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: February 1, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry**: July 31, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. The issuance represents a small addition to the equity base through preferential allotment to promoters following warrant conversion. The complete lock-in until July 2027 means these shares will not be available for trading, preventing any immediate supply pressure.

**Operational Impact**: None for general market participants. Trading members should note the updated share capital and lock-in restrictions in their systems.
