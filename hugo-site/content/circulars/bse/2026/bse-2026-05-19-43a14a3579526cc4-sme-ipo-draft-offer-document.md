BSE has issued Notice No. 20260519-4 dated May 19, 2026, pertaining to the filing of a Draft Offer Document (DRHP) for an SME IPO. The notice is categorised under company-related matters for the SME segment and is administered by the DCS-Listing department.

- Notice number: 20260519-4, issued on May 19, 2026
- Segment: SME (Small and Medium Enterprises)
- Department: DCS-Listing
- Subject: SME IPO – Draft Offer Document

No regulatory changes are introduced by this notice. It is a standard procedural filing acknowledgment under BSE's SME IPO listing framework.

The issuing company must ensure compliance with BSE SME IPO listing requirements and SEBI's Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements (ICDR) Regulations with respect to the Draft Offer Document. Prospective investors and market participants should review the Draft Offer Document once publicly available for complete disclosure details.
