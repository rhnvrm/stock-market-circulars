## Summary

BSE has issued a circular notifying the extension of the issue closing date and on-market renunciation date for Vineet Laboratories Limited's rights issue. This extension provides additional time for eligible shareholders to exercise their rights entitlement or renounce their rights in the market.

## Key Points

- Extension granted for issue closing date
- On-market renunciation period extended accordingly
- Affects shareholders of Vineet Laboratories Limited
- Provides additional time for subscription decisions

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a company-specific timeline adjustment approved by BSE.
