## Summary

BSE has published the settlement schedule for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Buyback) for CYIENT LIMITED (Settlement No. 2026076). The bidding window runs from June 23 to June 30, 2026, with the final settlement date of July 7, 2026.

## Key Points

- Settlement Type: Buyback under Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window
- Settlement No.: 2026076
- Bid Start Date: June 23, 2026
- Bid End Date: June 30, 2026
- Obligation Download Date: July 6, 2026
- Settlement Date: July 7, 2026
- Demat shares must be delivered exclusively via the Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism available through CDSL and NSDL
- Market Type "(Buyback)" and Settlement No. 2026076 must be used in the depository system
- Members/Custodians are NOT required to upload any EPN file for early pay-in
- Early pay-in securities will be earmarked/blocked in client accounts under the requisite market type and settlement number

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. Members are directed to refer to Notice No. 20200528-32 dated May 28, 2020, for guidelines on the Revised Mechanism for acquisition of shares through Stock Exchange.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and Custodians must ensure early pay-in of securities is reflected in BSE/ICCL systems before prescribed bid acceptance/confirmation timelines
- The process must be completed sufficiently in advance of closure of the BSE OTB Bidding window and the Custodian confirmation window
- Members must use Market Type "(Buyback)" and Settlement No. 2026076 in the depository system
- Members should refer to the MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for client-wise Stamp Duty charges

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Bid Start Date | June 23, 2026 |
| Bid End Date | June 30, 2026 |
| Obligation Download | July 6, 2026 |
| Settlement Date | July 7, 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

This circular is operationally significant for brokers, members, and custodians facilitating client participation in CYIENT LIMITED's buyback. Shareholders tendering shares must ensure their demat holdings are processed through the Early Pay-in mechanism on time. The impact is limited to participants in this specific buyback and does not affect broader market operations. ICCL Help Desk (022-69158590 / 45720490 / 45720690) is available for queries.
