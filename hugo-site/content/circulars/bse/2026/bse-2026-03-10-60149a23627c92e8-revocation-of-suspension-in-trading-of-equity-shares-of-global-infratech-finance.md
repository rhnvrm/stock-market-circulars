## Summary

BSE has revoked the suspension on trading in equity shares of Global Infratech & Finance Limited (CIN: L16299KA1995PLC214634, Scrip Code: 531463). The company is engaged in investments in shares and securities and is currently operational. This circular includes the company's profile (Annexure IV) confirming its compliance details.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension in equity shares of Global Infratech & Finance Limited (Scrip Code: 531463) has been revoked by BSE.
- The company is registered in Karnataka (CIN: L16299KA1995PLC214634) with its registered address at F-10-11-12 BSR Arcade, 198, Gandhi Bazaar Main Road, Basavangudi, Bengaluru – 560 004.
- Corporate/communication address is at 7/23 Kirti Nagar Industrial Area, Near Underpass, Delhi – 110015.
- Managing Director and Chairman: Mr. V S Amarnath.
- Company Secretary & Compliance Officer: Dhaval Bajaj (Contact: +91 88661 46195).
- Business nature: Investments in Shares & Securities.
- Operational status confirmed as 'Operational'.

## Regulatory Changes

The prior suspension of trading in the equity shares of Global Infratech & Finance Limited on BSE has been lifted. No new regulatory framework changes are introduced; this circular reinstates normal trading status for the scrip.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company has fulfilled the requisite compliance conditions that led to the revocation of the trading suspension.
- Market participants and brokers should note that the scrip (Code: 531463) is now available for normal trading on BSE.
- The company's compliance officer (Dhaval Bajaj) remains the point of contact for regulatory correspondence.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: March 10, 2026 (effective date of revocation).

## Impact Assessment

The revocation of suspension restores normal market access for Global Infratech & Finance Limited's equity shares (Scrip Code: 531463). Shareholders who were unable to trade during the suspension period can now transact freely. Given the company's small-cap nature and focus on investments in shares and securities, the broader market impact is limited, but the development is significant for existing investors and stakeholders of the company.
