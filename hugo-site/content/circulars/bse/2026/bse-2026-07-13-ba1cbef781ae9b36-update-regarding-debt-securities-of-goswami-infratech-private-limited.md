## Summary

In continuation of Exchange Notice 20260629-25 dated June 29, 2026, BSE has informed Trading Members that the debt security of Goswami Infratech Private Limited under Scrip Code 974946 will be available for trading with revised terms effective Tuesday, July 14, 2026. The ISIN and maturity date for this security have been updated. Additionally, per Exchange Notice No. 20260713-7 dated July 13, 2026, this ISIN will be suspended from trading effective Wednesday, July 15, 2026, on account of a record date fixed for the purpose of voluntary early redemption.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code 974946 (Goswami Infratech Pvt Ltd debt security) will trade with revised ISIN and maturity details from July 14, 2026.
- Old ISIN: INE219O07370 (Scrip Long Name: GIPL-ZC-30-6-26-PVT, Maturity Date: 30/06/2026).
- New ISIN: INE219O07388 (Scrip Long Name: GIPL-ZC-30-7-26-PVT, Maturity Date: 30/07/2026).
- The security will be suspended from trading effective July 15, 2026, due to a record date set for voluntary early redemption, as per Exchange Notice No. 20260713-7.
- This update follows and continues from the earlier Exchange Notice 20260629-25 dated June 29, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced; this is an operational/administrative update to ISIN and maturity details for a specific privately placed debt security on BSE's Debt segment, along with a subsequent trading suspension tied to a corporate action (voluntary early redemption).

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members dealing in this ISIN must update their systems to reflect the revised ISIN (INE219O07388) and maturity date (30/07/2026) effective July 14, 2026, and must cease trading in the security from July 15, 2026 following its suspension. Queries can be directed to Mr. Akash Shinde at BSE's Debt Listing desk (+91 22 2272 8844, debt.listing@bseindia.com).

## Important Dates

- July 14, 2026 (Tuesday): Revised ISIN (INE219O07388) and maturity date (30/07/2026) become effective for trading.
- July 15, 2026 (Wednesday): ISIN suspended from trading due to record date for voluntary early redemption.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to Trading Members and investors holding or trading this specific Goswami Infratech Pvt Ltd debt instrument (Scrip Code 974946). Failure to update ISIN references could disrupt trade processing for this security during the one-day trading window (July 14) before its suspension on July 15, 2026. There is no broader market-wide impact.
