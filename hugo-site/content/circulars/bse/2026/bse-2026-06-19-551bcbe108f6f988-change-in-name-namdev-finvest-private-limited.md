## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that NAMDEV FINVEST PRIVATE LIMITED, a debt-listed company, has informed the Exchange of a change in its corporate name to **Namdev Finvest Limited**. Accordingly, the Scrip ID and Abbreviated Name for all 10 of the company's debt-listed securities on the BOLT Plus System will be updated effective **June 25, 2026**.

## Key Points

- Company name changes from **NAMDEV FINVEST PRIVATE LIMITED** to **Namdev Finvest Limited**
- 10 debt-listed securities (scrip codes: 975808, 975879, 975987, 976058, 976184, 976212, 976922, 976964, 977177, 977365) are affected
- Scrip IDs and abbreviated names on the BOLT Plus System will be updated to reflect the new name
- A Certificate of Incorporation is attached as supporting documentation
- Notice issued by the Listing Compliance & Operations department

## Regulatory Changes

The Scrip ID and Abbreviated Name for all 10 debt securities listed under NAMDEV FINVEST PRIVATE LIMITED will be changed on the BOLT Plus System as follows:

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must note the updated Scrip IDs and abbreviated names for all 10 debt securities on the BOLT Plus System and update their internal records accordingly from June 25, 2026 onwards.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 19, 2026
- **Effective Date of Name Change on BOLT Plus System:** June 25, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative change with minimal market impact. The name change from NAMDEV FINVEST PRIVATE LIMITED to Namdev Finvest Limited reflects a corporate identity update (likely conversion from private to public limited company). Debt investors holding these securities are unaffected in terms of financial terms or trading continuity; only the display identifiers on the exchange system change. Trading members should update internal systems to reference the new scrip identifiers from the effective date.
