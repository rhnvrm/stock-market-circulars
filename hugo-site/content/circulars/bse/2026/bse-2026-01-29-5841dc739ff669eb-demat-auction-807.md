## Summary

Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE) has announced a demat auction for Settlement No. 807 dated January 29, 2026. The auction covers 143 scrips with specified quantities ranging from 1 share to 606,014 shares across various companies. This auction is conducted to settle delivery shortages from the specified settlement period.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 807
- Auction Date: January 29, 2026
- Total Scrips Covered: 143 companies
- Largest Quantity: Deep Diamond - 606,014 shares
- Major Scrips Include: IDFC First Bank (18,550), OMAX Autos (18,100), Banganga Paper (12,512), HIT KIT Global (6,996), GAIL (3,193), Moschip Technologies (3,149), India Homes (2,924)
- Covers multiple sectors including banking, energy, manufacturing, technology, and consumer goods

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced in this circular. This is a standard operational notice for demat auction proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members with delivery obligations for the listed scrips must participate in the auction process
- Settlement participants must ensure availability of securities or cash for auction settlement
- Members must comply with BSE's standard auction procedures and timelines
- Participants must adhere to the auction rules as per BSE regulations

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: January 29, 2026
- Settlement Number: 807
- Auction participants must follow BSE's prescribed auction timeline for this settlement

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - The auction affects 143 scrips with significant variation in quantities. While this is a routine settlement mechanism, it may cause temporary price volatility in affected scrips, particularly those with larger auction quantities.

**Operational Impact:** Medium - Members with delivery shortages in these scrips must arrange for securities through the auction mechanism, which may involve additional costs and operational coordination.

**Financial Impact:** The financial impact varies by scrip and quantity. Major impact on scrips with large quantities like Deep Diamond (606,014 shares), IDFC First Bank (18,550 shares), and OMAX Autos (18,100 shares) where auction prices may differ from market prices.

**Member Impact:** Affects brokers and trading members who have delivery obligations in any of the 143 listed scrips for Settlement No. 807.
