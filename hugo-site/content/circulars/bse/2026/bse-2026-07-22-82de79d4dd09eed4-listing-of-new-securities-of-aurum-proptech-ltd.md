## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that they are permitted to trade in 1,170 new equity shares of Aurum Proptech Ltd (Scrip Code: 539289) with effect from Thursday, July 23, 2026. These shares arise from the conversion of partly paid-up equity shares into fully paid-up shares of Rs. 5/- each, originally issued on a rights basis.

## Key Points

- 1,170 equity shares of Aurum Proptech Ltd approved for trading
- Shares result from conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid-up shares (Rs. 5/- each face value)
- Originally issued on a rights basis
- ISIN: INE898S01029
- Distinctive numbers: 660 shares (72130176-72130835), 10 shares (65556344-65556353), 500 shares (65616669-65617168)
- These shares rank pari-passu with the existing equity shares of the company

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing approval notice under existing rights issue terms.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific action required from investors or trading members beyond standard trading procedures; brokers/trading members should update systems to reflect the newly listed shares under the existing ISIN.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 22, 2026
- Effective Date for Trading: Thursday, July 23, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is minimal given the small quantum of shares (1,170) involved. This is a routine corporate action completing the rights issue process by converting partly paid shares to fully paid status. No material effect on stock price, liquidity, or shareholding pattern is expected.
