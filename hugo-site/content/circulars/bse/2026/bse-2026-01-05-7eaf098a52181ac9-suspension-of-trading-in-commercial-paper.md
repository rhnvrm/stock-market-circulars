## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in commercial papers of 10 instruments issued by 7 companies effective from January 7-13, 2026. The suspension is due to record dates fixed for redemption purposes. Trading members are advised not to deal in these commercial papers from the specified no-dealings dates.

## Key Points

- 10 commercial paper instruments affected across 7 issuers
- Suspension period: January 7-13, 2026
- Reason: Record date fixation for redemption of commercial papers
- Trading members must cease dealing from specified dates
- Notice issued under Corporate Actions category for Debt segment

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the listed commercial papers from the specified no-dealings dates
- All market participants must adhere to the suspension timeline for each instrument
- Members should update their systems to reflect trading restrictions on these ISINs

## Important Dates

- **January 7, 2026**: Trading suspension begins for Dhampur Sugar Mills CP
- **January 9, 2026**: Trading suspension begins for Navi Finserv, Bajaj Housing Finance, and Infina Finance CPs
- **January 12, 2026**: Trading suspension begins for Network 18 Media and Bajaj Finance CPs
- **January 13, 2026**: Trading suspension begins for Julius Baer Capital, Infina Finance (second instrument), Navi Finserv (second instrument), and Network 18 Media (second instrument) CPs

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice affecting the debt market segment. The trading suspension is procedural and relates to upcoming redemptions of commercial papers. Impact is limited to holders of these specific instruments and does not affect broader market operations. The staggered suspension dates across multiple issuers suggest normal maturity cycles in the commercial paper market.
