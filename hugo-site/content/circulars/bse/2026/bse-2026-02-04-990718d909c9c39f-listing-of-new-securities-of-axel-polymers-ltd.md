## Summary

BSE has announced the listing and commencement of trading for 24,84,444 new equity shares of Axel Polymers Ltd. (Scrip Code: 513642) effective February 5, 2026. These shares were issued on a preferential basis to non-promoters at an issue price of Rs. 45/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- plus premium of Rs. 35/-). The shares are subject to a lock-in period until August 10, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: Axel Polymers Ltd. (Scrip Code: 513642)
- Number of shares: 24,84,444 equity shares
- Face value: Rs. 10/- per share
- Premium: Rs. 35/- per share
- Issue price: Rs. 45/- per share
- Allotment date: December 13, 2025
- Trading commencement date: February 5, 2026
- ISIN: INE197C01012
- Distinctive numbers: 8516681 to 11001124
- Allotment type: Preferential basis to non-promoters
- Ranking: Pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification following SEBI guidelines for preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the new securities for trading purposes
- All 24,84,444 shares are subject to mandatory lock-in provisions
- The locked-in shares cannot be transferred or traded until the lock-in period expires

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 13, 2025
- **Trading Start Date**: February 5, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry Date**: August 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This listing has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains to a single company's capital expansion through preferential allotment. The lock-in period until August 10, 2026 means these shares will not be available for trading in the immediate term, limiting any supply-side pressure on the stock price. Trading members should update their systems to reflect the increased share capital and new distinctive number ranges for Axel Polymers Ltd.
