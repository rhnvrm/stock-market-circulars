## Summary

BSE Limited has notified trading members that the equity shares of Sampark India Logistics Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange's SME segment in due course. The scrip has been allotted code 544810, trading symbol SILL, and ISIN INE147701010. The exact date of listing will be announced through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Sampark India Logistics Limited's equity shares are to be listed and admitted to dealings on BSE SME segment.
- Scrip Code: 544810
- Symbol: SILL
- ISIN: INE147701010
- The date of listing and further security details will be communicated via a separate notice.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing notification under BSE's DCS-Listing department, Company related category.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific action required from trading members at this stage beyond noting the upcoming listing; further compliance steps will follow the separate notice on the listing date.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 6, 2026
- Listing date: To be announced separately

## Impact Assessment

This notice formalizes the SME listing of Sampark India Logistics Limited, enabling trading once the listing date is confirmed. Impact is limited to investors and market participants tracking this specific SME stock, with no broader market implications.
