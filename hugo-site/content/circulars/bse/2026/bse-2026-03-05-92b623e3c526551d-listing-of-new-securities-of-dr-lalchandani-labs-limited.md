## Summary

BSE has listed 43,04,000 new equity shares of DR LALCHANDANI LABS LIMITED (Scrip Code: 541299) issued on a rights basis at par value of Rs. 10/- each. These shares are permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Friday, March 6, 2026.

## Key Points

- 43,04,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at par on rights basis
- ISIN: INE871Z01013
- Distribution numbers: 4333069 to 8637068
- Date of Allotment: 02/03/2026
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Trading permitted from March 6, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice for newly allotted securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed to note the listing and permit trading in these new securities from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: March 2, 2026
- Effective Date for Trading: March 6, 2026 (Friday)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. The listing of 43,04,000 rights-issue shares increases the total tradable equity of DR LALCHANDANI LABS LIMITED. The shares carry equal rights as existing shares (pari-passu), so no dilution of rights for existing shareholders beyond the rights issue itself. Impact is confined to existing shareholders and traders of Scrip Code 541299.
