## Summary

BSE has issued a new ISIN number for Pashupati Cotspin Ltd (Scrip Code: 544448) in continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20260407-7 dated April 7, 2026. The new ISIN, INE124Y01028, applies to the company's sub-divided equity shares following a reduction in face value from Rs.10 per share to Re.1 per share. The new ISIN becomes effective from the ex-date of April 17, 2026.

## Key Points

- New ISIN assigned: **INE124Y01028** for Pashupati Cotspin Ltd (Scrip Code: 544448)
- Sub-division of equity shares from face value Rs.10/- to Re.1/- per share
- This notice is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20260407-7 dated April 07, 2026
- DR reference: 612/2026-2027
- Issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President – Listing Compliance and Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular operationalises the previously announced corporate action (share sub-division) by assigning the updated ISIN under BSE's listing compliance framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their systems to use the new ISIN INE124Y01028 for all trades in Pashupati Cotspin Ltd equity shares on and from the ex-date.
- The old ISIN should no longer be used for trades executed on or after April 17, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** April 13, 2026
- **Ex-Date (effective date for new ISIN):** April 17, 2026
- **Reference Notice:** Exchange Notice No. 20260407-7 dated April 07, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine post-corporate-action ISIN update. The share sub-division (10:1 ratio based on face value change from Rs.10 to Re.1) increases the number of shares in circulation proportionally, which can improve liquidity and affordability for retail investors. Trading members and depository participants must ensure their back-office and trading systems reflect the new ISIN before the ex-date of April 17, 2026 to avoid settlement issues. The impact is largely operational and limited to systems handling this specific scrip.
