## Summary

BSE has notified members that, pursuant to its earlier Notice No. 20260629-40 dated June 29, 2026, the ICICI Prudential Balanced Hybrid Fund will reopen for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP and SWP facilities on the BSE StAR MF Platform with effect from July 21, 2026.

## Key Points

- The ICICI Prudential Balanced Hybrid Fund resumes ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform from July 21, 2026.
- Facilities being enabled include subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP and SWP.
- This follows a prior notice (20260629-40) dated June 29, 2026, which had presumably suspended or restricted these transactions.
- Six scheme/plan variants are covered, spanning Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Reinvestment, and IDCW Payout options.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is an operational update reactivating existing transaction facilities for a specific mutual fund scheme on the BSE StAR MF platform.

## Compliance Requirements

Member brokers and platform participants dealing in BSE StAR MF should update their systems to reflect that the listed ICICI Prudential Balanced Hybrid Fund scheme codes/ISINs are again available for ongoing transactions from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- June 29, 2026: Original notice (20260629-40) referenced.
- July 21, 2026: Effective date for resumption of ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP and SWP transactions.

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact, routine operational notice affecting mutual fund distributors and investors using the BSE StAR MF Platform. It restores normal transaction capability for six ICICI Prudential Balanced Hybrid Fund plan/option variants (ISINs INF109K1A484, INF109K1A492, INF109K1A500, INF109K1A518, INF109K1A526, INF109K1A534) with no bearing on listed equity trading or broader market structure.
