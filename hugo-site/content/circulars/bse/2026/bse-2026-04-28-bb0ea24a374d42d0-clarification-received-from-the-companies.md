## Summary

BSE's Surveillance Department (DOSS) issued Notice No. 20260428-28 on 28 April 2026, disclosing the status of clarifications sought from companies that witnessed significant price and/or volume movement in their equity scrips. The Exchange proactively reached out to these companies to ensure trading members and investors have the latest relevant information. Replies have been received from 11 companies, while 16 companies are yet to respond.

## Key Points

- BSE observed significant price/volume movement in 27 listed equity scrips and sought clarifications from all of them.
- Replies have been received from 11 companies: Aptech Ltd, Frontline Corporation Ltd, Galada Finance Ltd, Hercules Investments Ltd, Knowledge Marine & Engineering Works Ltd, Maan Aluminium Ltd, Shaily Engineering Plastics Ltd, Sharvaya Metals Ltd, Sutlej Textiles and Industries Ltd, USG Tech Solutions Ltd, and Valplast Technologies Ltd.
- Replies are still awaited from 16 companies: Archidply Decor Ltd, Deep Polymers Ltd, Dhillon Freight Carrier Ltd, Gconnect Logitech and Supply Chain Ltd, Hindustan Appliances Ltd, Lactose India Ltd, Milgrey Finance & Investments Ltd, Nagreeka Exports Ltd, Paul Merchants Ltd, PCS Technology Ltd, Resourceful Automobile Ltd, Rudra Global Infra Products Ltd, Sampat Aluminium Ltd, Sri KPR Industries Ltd, Trigyn Technologies Ltd, and ZR2 Bioenergy Ltd.
- Attachment(s) include three sets of replies (Replies1, Replies2, Replies3) from the companies that have responded.
- Notice issued by Sanjay Jain (Deputy Vice President, Surveillance) and Shreya Ghose (Manager, Surveillance).

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This notice is a standard surveillance communication under BSE's existing framework to ensure market transparency during periods of abnormal price or volume activity in listed securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies that have already replied: No further immediate action required; their clarifications are attached for review by investors and trading members.
- Companies with pending replies (16 companies): Required to submit their clarifications to BSE at the earliest. Non-submission or delayed response may attract further surveillance or regulatory action.
- Trading members and investors: Advised to review the attached replies before taking positions in the mentioned scrips.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 28 April 2026
- **Reply Deadline for Pending Companies:** Not explicitly stated; companies are expected to respond promptly given the surveillance context.

## Impact Assessment

This notice has a moderate market impact. The 11 companies that have responded provide some transparency to the market, potentially reducing speculative uncertainty around their scrips. However, the 16 companies that have not yet replied may continue to face heightened scrutiny, and their scrips could remain under surveillance watch. Investors in these 16 stocks should exercise caution until clarifications are received. The notice itself does not impose trading restrictions but signals that BSE is actively monitoring these counters for unusual activity.
