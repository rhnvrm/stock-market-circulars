## Summary

BSE Clearing Limited (BSECL) has informed all Clearing Members that SIC Stocks & Services Pvt. Ltd. (SEBI Registration Number INZ000289933) has been declared a defaulter, effective July 08, 2026. This action was taken pursuant to SEBI Circular No. SEBI/HO/MIRSD/MIRSD-PoD/P/CIR/2025/90 dated June 17, 2025, following the member being declared a defaulter by another Exchange/Clearing Corporation.

## Key Points

- Clearing Member SIC Stocks & Services Pvt. Ltd. (SEBI Reg. No. INZ000289933) has been declared a defaulter by BSECL.
- The declaration follows the member's default status at another Exchange/Clearing Corporation, applied under the cross-default framework set out in SEBI's June 17, 2025 circular.
- The notice was issued by BSECL's Settlement/RMS department under the General segment.
- All members are advised to take note of the declaration.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced; this notice applies the existing SEBI framework (Circular No. SEBI/HO/MIRSD/MIRSD-PoD/P/CIR/2025/90 dated June 17, 2025) governing cross-exchange defaulter declarations for clearing members.

## Compliance Requirements

All Clearing Members are required to take note of the defaulter declaration and act in accordance with BSECL's default handling procedures when dealing with SIC Stocks & Services Pvt. Ltd.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 09, 2026
- Effective Date of Default Declaration: July 08, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The declaration directly impacts SIC Stocks & Services Pvt. Ltd. and any members or clients with open positions or exposures through this clearing member, who should assess counterparty risk accordingly. The broader market impact is limited but the notice underscores active risk-management coordination between exchanges under SEBI's cross-default reporting framework.
