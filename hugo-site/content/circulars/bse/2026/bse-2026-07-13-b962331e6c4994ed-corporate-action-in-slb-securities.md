## Summary

BSE Clearing (BSECL), on behalf of ICCL Ltd., has issued a notice informing trading members of a corporate action affecting one or more securities in the Securities Lending and Borrowing (SLB) segment. Specific details of the corporate action are provided in an attached annexure, which is not included in the notice text itself.

## Key Points

- Notice pertains to the SLB (Securities Lending and Borrowing) segment.
- Trading members are directed to refer to the attached annexure for full details of the corporate action.
- Issued by ICCL Ltd. (Indian Clearing Corporation Limited), signed by Tushar Nimkar, Assistant Manager.

## Regulatory Changes

None specified in the notice text; this is an operational/corporate action communication rather than a regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members with open SLB positions in the affected securities should review the annexure and take appropriate action (e.g., adjustments to lending/borrowing positions) as required by the corporate action.

## Important Dates

Notice dated 13 July 2026. Specific corporate action dates (record date, effective date, etc.) are not disclosed in the notice text and would be in the annexure.

## Impact Assessment

Impact is operational and limited to trading members holding SLB positions in the affected security or securities. Without the annexure details (specific ISIN/security and nature of the corporate action), broader market impact cannot be assessed from this notice alone.
