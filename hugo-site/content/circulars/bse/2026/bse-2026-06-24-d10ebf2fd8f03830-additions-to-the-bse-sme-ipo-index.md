## Summary

BSE Index Services Private Limited has announced the addition of three newly listed companies to the BSE SME IPO Index effective at the open of Thursday, June 25, 2026. The companies—Liotech Industries Limited, Leapfrog Engineering Services Limited, and Diksha Polymers Limited—were listed on BSE on June 24, 2026.

## Key Points

- Three SME IPO stocks will be added to the BSE SME IPO Index effective June 25, 2026 (at market open).
- Liotech Industries Limited (ticker: 544796) listed June 24, 2026 per Notice No. 20260623-33.
- Leapfrog Engineering Services Limited (ticker: 544797) listed June 24, 2026 per Notice No. 20260623-30.
- Diksha Polymers Limited (ticker: 544798) listed June 24, 2026 per Notice No. 20260623-31.
- These additions have been included in the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE) for June 24, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index rebalancing notice triggered by new SME IPO listings on BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index-tracking funds and ETFs benchmarked to the BSE SME IPO Index should update their portfolios to reflect the addition of these three securities effective June 25, 2026. Queries can be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **June 24, 2026**: Listing date for all three companies on BSE; end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE) updated.
- **June 25, 2026**: Effective date for addition to BSE SME IPO Index (at market open).

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to index-linked products and portfolios tracking the BSE SME IPO Index. Passive funds and ETFs referencing this index will need to incorporate these three new constituents. There is no broader market or compliance impact for other participants.
