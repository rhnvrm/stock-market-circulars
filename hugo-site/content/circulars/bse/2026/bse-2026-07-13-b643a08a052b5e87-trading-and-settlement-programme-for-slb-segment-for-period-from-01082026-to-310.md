## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260713-11 dated 13 July 2026, announcing the trading and settlement programme for the Securities Lending and Borrowing (SLB) segment for the period from 01.08.2026 to 31.08.2026. The detailed schedule is provided in the attachment SLB082026.

## Key Points

- Notice pertains to the Settlement/RMS category for the SLB segment, issued by BSE's Clearing (BSECL) department.
- The circular specifies the trading and settlement calendar applicable for August 2026 for SLB transactions.
- Full details, including specific trade and settlement dates, are contained in the attached file SLB082026.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced; this is a periodic operational notice detailing the settlement calendar for the SLB segment.

## Compliance Requirements

Market participants, clearing members, and trading members active in the SLB segment should refer to the attached schedule to align their trading and settlement operations with the specified programme for August 2026.

## Important Dates

The programme covers the period from 01 August 2026 to 31 August 2026, with specific trade and settlement dates detailed in the SLB082026 attachment.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative notice with low market impact. It primarily assists SLB segment participants in planning their trading and settlement activities for the stated period and does not affect any specific stock or trigger broader regulatory changes.
