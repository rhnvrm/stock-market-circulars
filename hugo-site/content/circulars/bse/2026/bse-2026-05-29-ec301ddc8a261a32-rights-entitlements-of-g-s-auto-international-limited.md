## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that dealings in Rights Entitlements (REs) for renunciation of G S Auto International Limited will commence on Tuesday, June 02, 2026. The Rights Issue is open from June 02 to June 10, 2026, with the renunciation trading window closing on June 05, 2026.

## Key Points

- Rights Entitlements (REs) will be tradable on BSE for renunciation purposes during a defined window
- Scrip Code: 751120, Scrip ID: GSAUTO-RE, ISIN: INE736H20016
- Market Lot: 1, Group: R
- Settlement is on T+1 Rolling Settlement basis, on a trade-for-trade basis
- REs are tradable in dematerialised form only
- REs that are neither subscribed nor renounced by the Issue Closing Date (June 10, 2026) shall lapse and be extinguished

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice references existing framework circulars (notice no. 20200305-21 dated March 05, 2020 and notice no. 20200518-34 dated May 18, 2020) governing Rights Entitlements trading.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must inform all clients about the commencement of trading in REs, specifying the ISIN (INE736H20016)
- Members must clarify that REs should not be mistaken for ordinary shares of G S Auto International Limited
- Contract notes/bills for RE purchases must prominently include a disclaimer stating that purchase of REs only confers the right to participate in the Rights Issue or renounce before issue closure
- Members must ensure REs are traded in dematerialised form only

## Important Dates

- **June 02, 2026**: Commencement of trading in REs for renunciation; Rights Issue opens
- **June 05, 2026**: Last day for trading/renunciation of Rights Entitlements
- **June 10, 2026**: Rights Issue closes; unsubscribed/unrenounced REs lapse after this date

## Impact Assessment

This notice is relevant primarily to existing shareholders of G S Auto International Limited and trading members facilitating RE transactions. The trade-for-trade, T+1 settlement framework limits systemic risk. Shareholders who do not act by June 05, 2026 (to renounce) or June 10, 2026 (to subscribe) will lose the value of their entitlements. Impact is limited to participants directly involved in this rights issue.
