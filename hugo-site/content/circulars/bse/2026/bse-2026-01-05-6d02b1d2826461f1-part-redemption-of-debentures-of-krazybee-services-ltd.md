## Summary

KRAZYBEE SERVICES LTD has announced the part redemption of its Non-Convertible Debentures (ISIN: INE07HK07791, Code: 976345) along with payment of interest. The record date has been set as January 8, 2026, and trading in these debentures will be conducted with a reduced face value effective from the same date.

## Key Points

- Company: KRAZYBEE SERVICES LTD
- Security Type: Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs)
- ISIN: INE07HK07791
- Scrip Code: 976345
- Record Date: January 8, 2026
- Purpose: Part Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- New Reduced Face Value: Rs. 50,000 per Debenture (from Rs. 100,000)
- Effective Date: January 8, 2026
- Settlement Number: DR-794/2025-2026
- Notice Number: 20260105-39

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action involving part redemption of debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must note that trading in the debentures will be conducted with the reduced face value of Rs. 50,000 per debenture effective January 8, 2026
- Trading Members should update their systems to reflect the new face value
- Settlement will be processed under DR-794/2025-2026

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 5, 2026
- **Record Date**: January 8, 2026
- **Effective Date for Reduced Face Value**: January 8, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The part redemption reduces the face value of the debentures by 50% (from Rs. 100,000 to Rs. 50,000 per debenture), which will affect the outstanding debt quantum and trading dynamics for these securities.

**Operational Impact**: Trading Members need to ensure their systems are updated to reflect the reduced face value before the effective date. Debenture holders will receive partial redemption proceeds along with interest payment on the record date.

**Investor Impact**: Existing debenture holders will receive partial repayment of principal along with accrued interest, reducing their exposure to KRAZYBEE SERVICES LTD's debt securities.
