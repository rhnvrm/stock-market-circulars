## Summary

BSE Surveillance issued clarification requests to two companies - Jindal Poly Films Limited (BSE: 500227) and AXISCADES Technologies Limited (BSE: 532395) - regarding unusual price and volume movements in their securities. Both companies responded confirming that the movements are market-driven and that they have disclosed all material events as required under SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015.

## Key Points

- Jindal Poly Films Limited received query regarding significant price movement (Ref. No.: L/SURV/ONL/PV/APJ/2025-2026/879)
- AXISCADES Technologies Limited received query regarding significant volume increase (Ref. No.: L/SURV/ONL/PV/APJ/2025-2026/878)
- Both companies confirmed all disclosures under Regulation 30 of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015 are up to date
- No undisclosed price sensitive information or material events pending disclosure
- Companies attributed movements to market forces
- Both companies committed to timely disclosure of future material events

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine surveillance monitoring exercise by BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies must continue to disclose all material events under Regulation 30 of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015
- Any unpublished price sensitive information (UPSI) must be disclosed promptly upon crystallization
- Disclosures must be made after due approval from competent authority
- Companies must comply with SEBI (Prohibition of Insider Trading) Regulations, 2015

## Important Dates

- January 23, 2026: BSE Surveillance queries issued
- January 23, 2026: Companies submitted clarifications

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. These are routine surveillance queries that exchanges issue when unusual trading patterns are observed. Both companies provided standard responses indicating no material undisclosed information.

**Operational Impact:** None. Companies are already in compliance with disclosure requirements.

**Investor Impact:** Neutral. The clarifications confirm that recent price/volume movements are market-driven and not based on any undisclosed corporate developments. Investors can continue trading with confidence that all material information has been disclosed.

**Regulatory Significance:** This demonstrates active surveillance by BSE to ensure market integrity and timely disclosure of material information.
