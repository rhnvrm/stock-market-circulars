## Summary

BSE's Surveillance department (DOSS) issued Notice No. 20260508-17 dated 8 May 2026, informing market participants that clarifications have been received from 11 listed companies that experienced significant price/volume movements. The Exchange had previously written to these companies to ensure trading members and investors have the latest relevant information. Replies from 4 additional companies remain pending.

## Key Points

- BSE observed significant price/volume movement in 15 scrips and sought clarifications from the respective companies
- 11 companies have responded; their replies are attached to the notice (Replies1 and Replies2)
- 4 companies have not yet submitted their replies to the Exchange
- Notice issued by Deputy Vice President (Sanjay Jain) and Manager (Shreya Ghosh) of the Surveillance department

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a surveillance disclosure action under BSE's ongoing market monitoring framework to maintain transparency and protect investor interests.

## Compliance Requirements

- The 4 companies with pending replies (Adhata Global, Gconnect Logitech, Milgrey Finance, ZR2 Bioenergy) are expected to submit their clarifications to BSE promptly
- Trading members and investors are advised to review the attached company replies before making trading decisions in the listed scrips

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 8 May 2026
- **Notice Number:** 20260508-17
- No specific deadline mentioned for pending replies; however, non-compliance may attract further regulatory action

## Impact Assessment

This notice signals that BSE's Surveillance team is actively monitoring unusual price and volume activity across these 15 scrips. Investors should treat this as a caution flag — the underlying reasons for price/volume movements may be disclosed in the attached company replies. The 4 companies still awaiting reply submission face potential follow-up regulatory action from BSE. No trading halts or sanctions are announced at this stage, making the immediate market impact moderate. Retail investors in these scrips should review the company clarifications before trading.
