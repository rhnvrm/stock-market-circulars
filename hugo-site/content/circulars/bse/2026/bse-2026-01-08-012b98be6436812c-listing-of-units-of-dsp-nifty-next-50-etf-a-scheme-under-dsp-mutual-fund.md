## Summary

BSE announces the listing of DSP Nifty Next 50 ETF units effective January 9, 2026. The ETF is an open-ended scheme tracking the Nifty Next 50 Index, managed by DSP Asset Managers Private Limited. A total of 922,004 units with scrip code 544674 will be listed in the 'B' Group with 13 registered market makers providing liquidity.

## Key Points

- ETF units listed in 'B' Group with scrip code 544674
- ISIN: INF740KA1XU7
- Total units: 922,004
- Face value: Rs. 10.00 per unit
- Issue price: Rs. 70.41 per unit
- Market lot: 1 unit
- BOLT System ID: NEXT50ADD
- Date of allotment: January 5, 2026
- Trading only in dematerialized form as per SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000
- 13 market makers registered including Kotak Securities, Motilal Oswal, CHOICE EQUITY, and others

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This circular follows existing SEBI guidelines requiring mutual fund units to be traded only in dematerialized form.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all trades in DSP Nifty Next 50 ETF are conducted in dematerialized form only
- Trades will be executed in unit market lot (1 unit minimum)
- Market makers must provide liquidity as per their obligations

## Important Dates

- **January 5, 2026**: Date of allotment
- **January 9, 2026**: Effective date for listing and commencement of trading

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This is a standard ETF listing that adds a new investment product tracking the Nifty Next 50 Index. The presence of 13 market makers ensures adequate liquidity. No impact on existing securities or market operations. Trading members are simply informed of the new listing for awareness and trading purposes.
