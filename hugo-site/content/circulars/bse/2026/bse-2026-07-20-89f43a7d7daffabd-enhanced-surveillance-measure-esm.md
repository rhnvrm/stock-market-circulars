## Summary

BSE has issued a notice under its Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework, referencing a series of prior notices dating back to June 2023. Securities listed in Annexure I have met the criteria for inclusion under ESM and will be subject to a minimum 100% margin requirement on all open and new positions effective July 22, 2026. Some securities already under ESM will be moved to a lower stage (Stage I), while others will exit the framework entirely per Annexure II. A consolidated list of all securities currently under ESM is provided in Annexure III.

## Key Points

- Securities in Annexure I have satisfied ESM inclusion criteria and will attract minimum 100% margin from July 22, 2026, applicable to open positions as of July 21, 2026 and new positions created from July 22, 2026.
- Securities continuing under ESM but being downgraded to Stage I will move w.e.f. July 21, 2026 (also listed in Annexure I).
- Securities newly qualifying under ESM will shift from Rolling Settlement to Trade-for-Trade segment w.e.f. July 22, 2026.
- Securities shifting to Stage II will be under Trade-for-Trade with a 2% price band and Periodic Call Auction w.e.f. July 21, 2026.
- Securities exiting the ESM framework w.e.f. July 21, 2026 are listed in Annexure II; their price bands will revert to pre-ESM levels unless subject to another surveillance measure.
- ESM operates in conjunction with other prevailing surveillance measures and is purely a market surveillance action, not an adverse judgment on the company.
- A consolidated list of all securities currently under the ESM framework is provided in Annexure III.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced; this notice implements periodic review outcomes under the existing ESM framework established via prior BSE notices (June 2023 through July 2025).

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must apply minimum 100% margin on open and new positions in newly included ESM securities from July 22, 2026, and ensure affected securities are handled under Trade-for-Trade settlement with applicable price bands and call auction mechanisms where relevant. Queries can be directed to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- July 21, 2026: Stage revisions (including moves to Stage I and Stage II with 2% price band and Periodic Call Auction) and exits from ESM framework take effect.
- July 22, 2026: Minimum 100% margin and shift to Trade-for-Trade segment take effect for newly included securities.

## Impact Assessment

The measure significantly increases margin requirements and reduces tradability (via Trade-for-Trade settlement and tighter price bands) for affected securities, likely reducing trading volumes and liquidity in the near term. Securities exiting ESM will see restored price bands, potentially improving trading flexibility. Overall market impact is stock-specific and depends on the securities named in the annexures, which are not detailed in the notice text itself.
