## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260429-12 informs trading members that 2,00,000 new equity shares of AMBO AGRITEC LIMITED (Scrip Code: 543678, ISIN: INE0N6V01016) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Thursday, April 30, 2026. The shares were issued at Rs. 30/- each (face value Rs. 10/- plus premium of Rs. 20/-) to the Promoter on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 2,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 20/- (issue price Rs. 30/-) to Promoter on preferential basis
- Shares issued pursuant to conversion of warrants
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 19645161 to 19845160
- Date of allotment: 12 August 2025
- ISIN: INE0N6V01016 | Scrip Code: 543678
- Trading effective from April 30, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notice under BSE's Listing Operations department.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to take note of the new securities available for trading from April 30, 2026. No specific compliance action required from members beyond awareness of the new listing.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** 12 August 2025
- **Trading Commencement:** 30 April 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry:** 01 December 2026 (applicable to all 2,00,000 shares, Dist. Nos. 19645161 to 19845160)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal broader market impact. The 2,00,000 shares are subject to a lock-in period until December 1, 2026, meaning they cannot be traded in the open market until that date. The shares were allotted to the Promoter, increasing promoter shareholding. The issuance at Rs. 30/- per share (at a premium of Rs. 20/- over face value) reflects the warrant conversion terms. Overall, this is a routine corporate action with no systemic market impact.
