## Summary

BSE has issued a revised notice (Notice No. 20260407-5) informing trading members of the exact reduced face value per debenture for Adani Airport Holdings Limited following a part redemption. This is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20260318-48 dated March 18, 2026, which had originally announced the record date for the part redemption. The company has now confirmed the precise reduced face value at Rs. 67,703 per debenture.

## Key Points

- Revised notice updating the exact actual reduced face value for Adani Airport Holdings Limited debentures
- Scrip: AAHL-9.35%-8-9-28-PVT (Scrip Code: 976065, ISIN: INE0GCN07047)
- Reduced face value set at Rs. 67,703 per debenture
- Original notice reference: Exchange Notice No. 20260318-48 dated March 18, 2026
- Debentures will trade with the new reduced face value from April 8, 2026 (DR-606/2026-2027)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework changes. This notice is a revision to an earlier corporate action notice to provide the exact reduced face value following part redemption of debentures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are required to take note of the revised reduced face value of Rs. 67,703 per debenture
- All trades in AAHL-9.35%-8-9-28-PVT debentures must reflect the new face value effective April 8, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Original Record Date Notice:** March 18, 2026 (Exchange Notice No. 20260318-48)
- **This Revised Notice Date:** April 7, 2026
- **Effective Date for Reduced Face Value Trading:** April 8, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This notice affects traders and holders of Adani Airport Holdings Limited's 9.35% private placement debentures maturing September 8, 2028 (ISIN: INE0GCN07047). The reduction in face value from the original to Rs. 67,703 per debenture reflects the partial principal repayment. Impact is limited to this specific debenture series in the debt segment; equity markets are unaffected. Holders should update their valuation models to reflect the new face value.
