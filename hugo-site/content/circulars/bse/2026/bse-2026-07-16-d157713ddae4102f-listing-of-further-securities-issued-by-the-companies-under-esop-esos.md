## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that further new securities issued by seven companies under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) / Employee Stock Option Schemes (ESOS) have been listed and admitted for trading with effect from Friday, July 17, 2026.

## Key Points

- Aditya Birla Sun Life AMC Limited (ISIN INE404A01024, Scrip Code 543374): 60,252 shares of face value ₹5, distinctive numbers 289203495–289263746
- Axis Bank Limited (ISIN INE238A01034, Scrip Code 532215): 50,993 shares of face value ₹2, distinctive numbers 3110915679–3110966671
- Clean Max Enviro Energy Solutions Limited (ISIN INE647U01026, Scrip Code 544717): 8,740 shares of face value ₹1, distinctive numbers 117217411–117226150
- Coromandel International Limited (ISIN INE169A01031, Scrip Code 506395): 1,800 shares of face value ₹1, distinctive numbers 295014640–295016439
- Manorama Industries Limited (ISIN INE00VM01036, Scrip Code 541974): 12,500 shares of face value ₹2, distinctive numbers 63109891–63122390, locked in until July 3, 2027
- Northern Arc Capital Limited (ISIN INE850M01015, Scrip Code 544260): 39,000 shares of face value ₹10, distinctive numbers 161612376–161651375
- Zodiac Energy Limited (ISIN INE761Y01019, Scrip Code 543416): 28,943 shares of face value ₹10, distinctive numbers 15123691–15152633

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine listing notice under existing ESOP/ESOS listing procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific action required from investors or trading members beyond noting the updated share counts for these companies. All market participants are requested to take note of the listing.

## Important Dates

- Notice date: July 16, 2026
- Effective listing/trading date: Friday, July 17, 2026
- Manorama Industries Limited shares: locked in until July 3, 2027

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative listing of incremental shares arising from employee stock option exercises across seven companies. The quantities involved are small relative to each company's total outstanding share capital, so the impact on float, market capitalization, or price discovery is expected to be minimal. No trading restrictions apply except for the lock-in on Manorama Industries Limited shares.
