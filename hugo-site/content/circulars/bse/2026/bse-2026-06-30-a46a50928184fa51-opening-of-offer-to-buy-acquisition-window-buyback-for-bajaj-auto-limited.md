## Summary

BSE has notified trading members and custodians that Bajaj Auto Limited proposes to buy back up to 46,94,000 fully paid-up equity shares through the Tender Offer route at INR 12,000 per share, for an aggregate amount of up to INR 5,632.80 crore. The offer window is open from July 1 to July 7, 2026, with BSE serving as the Designated Stock Exchange.

## Key Points

- Bajaj Auto proposes to buy back up to 46,94,000 equity shares (Face Value: INR 10 each), representing 1.68% of total paid-up equity share capital.
- Buyback price is INR 12,000 per share, payable in cash via the Tender Offer route.
- Total buyback size is up to INR 5,632,80,00,000 (INR 5,632 Crore and 80 Lakh), excluding transaction costs.
- Buyback constitutes 16.93% (standalone) and 15.59% (consolidated) of aggregate paid-up equity share capital and free reserves as of March 31, 2026 — within the 25% statutory limit.
- BSE is the Designated Stock Exchange for this offer.
- Letter of Offer is attached for market participant reference.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. The offer is conducted pursuant to SEBI's existing mechanism for acquisition of shares through stock exchanges under Tender Offers (SEBI Circular No. CIR/CFD/POLICYCELL/1/2015 dated April 13, 2015, and CFD/DCR2/CIR/P/2016/131 dated December 9, 2016), along with BSE's revised guidelines issued via notices dated February 10, 2017; April 24, 2019; May 28, 2020; November 2, 2020; and August 25, 2021.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and custodians must note and comply with the Revised Guidelines for the acquisition mechanism under SEBI and BSE circulars.
- Eligible shareholders (beneficial owners as on the Record Date of June 24, 2026) may tender shares through the BSE acquisition window during the offer period.
- Full offer details are available on the BSE website (www.bseindia.com).

## Important Dates

- **Record Date:** Wednesday, June 24, 2026
- **Offer Opening Date:** Wednesday, July 1, 2026
- **Offer Closing Date:** Tuesday, July 7, 2026
- **Notice Date:** June 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This buyback represents a major capital return event for Bajaj Auto shareholders. At INR 12,000 per share, the offer price signals a significant premium intent and may influence short-term market price dynamics. Trading members and custodians have a narrow 7-day window (July 1–7) to facilitate client participation. Shareholders on record as of June 24, 2026 are eligible to tender on a proportionate basis. Given the substantial outlay (INR 5,632.80 crore) and Bajaj Auto's market prominence, this circular carries high importance and broad market impact.
