## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of LENDINGKART FINANCE LIMITED (ISIN: INE090W07667, Scrip Code: 975356) effective January 15, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing the record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Company: LENDINGKART FINANCE LIMITED
- Debenture Series: LFL-30-1-26-PVT
- ISIN: INE090W07667
- Scrip Code: 975356
- Record Date: January 15, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures and Payment of interest
- No Dealings From: January 15, 2026
- Circular Reference: DR-799/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard procedural suspension aligned with debenture redemption and interest payment processes.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all trading activities in the specified debentures from January 15, 2026
- Members should ensure no fresh positions are created in these NCDs
- Existing positions should be squared off or settled before the suspension date
- Members must communicate this suspension to their clients holding or trading in these debentures

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 9, 2026
- **Record Date**: January 15, 2026
- **Suspension Start Date**: January 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders and traders of this specific NCD series. Investors holding these debentures will receive their redemption proceeds and interest payment as per the terms. The suspension ensures orderly settlement for the record date determination.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members dealing in debt securities need to update their systems to block trading in this specific ISIN. Clients with positions in these debentures should be notified about the impending suspension and redemption process.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders as of the record date will be entitled to interest payment and redemption proceeds. No trading opportunity available from January 15, 2026 onwards.
