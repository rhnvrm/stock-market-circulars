## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (No. 20260615-3) informing trading members that Cyient Limited (BSE Code: 532175/132175) has fixed a record date for its ongoing equity buyback offer. The record date will be used to determine which shareholders are entitled and eligible to participate in the buyback.

## Key Points

- Cyient Limited has announced a record date for its tender offer (buyback) of equity shares
- Record date is set for 17 June 2026
- Ex-entitlement date is also 17 June 2026
- Settlement reference: DR-653/2026-2027 (Rolling Settlement Segment)
- Applies to dematerialised securities in the rolling settlement segment
- BSE Code for Cyient Limited: 532175 / 132175

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification pursuant to SEBI buyback regulations, informing trading members of the record date fixed by the company.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are required to take note of the record date and ex-entitlement date (17 June 2026)
- Members should ensure that eligible client positions are recorded accurately as of the record date to facilitate correct entitlement determination
- No additional compliance actions are required from trading members beyond acknowledgement

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 15 June 2026
- **Record Date:** 17 June 2026
- **Ex-Entitlement Date:** 17 June 2026
- **Settlement Number:** DR-653/2026-2027

## Impact Assessment

This notification has a moderate impact limited to existing shareholders of Cyient Limited. Shareholders holding equity shares as of the record date (17 June 2026) will be eligible to participate in the buyback offer. The buyback provides an exit opportunity for shareholders at a likely premium to market price, which may influence short-term trading activity in the stock around the record date. No broader market or systemic impact is expected.
