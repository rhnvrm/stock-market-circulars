## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 4,085,000 new equity shares of Jeevan Scientific Technology Limited (Scrip Code: 538837) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Friday, February 27, 2026. These shares were issued on a preferential basis to Non-Promoters at an issue price of Rs. 40/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- plus premium of Rs. 30/-).

## Key Points

- **Company:** Jeevan Scientific Technology Limited (Scrip Code: 538837)
- **Number of Shares:** 4,085,000 equity shares
- **Face Value:** Rs. 10/- per share
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 40/- per share (Rs. 10 face value + Rs. 30 premium)
- **Allotted To:** Non-Promoters on a preferential basis
- **ISIN:** INE237B01018
- **Distinctive Numbers:** 15833966 to 19918965
- **Date of Allotment:** January 2, 2026
- **Trading Effective From:** February 27, 2026
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for newly allotted securities under existing SEBI preferential allotment norms.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities and may facilitate trading from February 27, 2026.
- Lock-in restrictions apply to all 4,085,000 shares until September 2, 2026; these shares cannot be traded until the lock-in expires.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** January 2, 2026
- **Listing / Trading Effective Date:** February 27, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry Date:** September 2, 2026 (for all 4,085,000 shares, Dist. Nos. 15833966 to 19918965)

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 4,085,000 shares to the tradeable equity base of Jeevan Scientific Technology Limited, representing a preferential allotment to Non-Promoters. However, all newly listed shares are subject to a lock-in period until September 2, 2026, meaning no immediate increase in free-float or trading liquidity. Market impact is minimal and confined to this single scrip.
