## Summary

BSE has revised the price bands for 21 scrips in the Equity Segment, effective May 29, 2026. The revised bands range from 2% to 20%, replacing the existing levels as a surveillance measure.

## Key Points

- 21 scrips have had their price bands revised effective May 29, 2026
- Price bands revised down to 2%, 5%, 10%, or 20% depending on the scrip
- Action taken by BSE Surveillance Department (DOSS)
- Members may seek clarification at bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

The price bands for the listed scrips have been tightened under BSE's surveillance framework. This restricts the maximum permissible intraday price movement for these securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must adhere to the revised price bands for the listed scrips from May 29, 2026 onwards. No further action is required from members beyond awareness and system compliance.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** May 27, 2026
- **Effective Date:** May 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The tightening of price bands limits daily price movement for these 21 scrips, reducing volatility risk for retail investors but also constraining liquidity and price discovery. Scrips moved to a 2% band face the most restrictive trading conditions, while Steelco Gujarat retains a relatively wider 20% band. This is a standard BSE surveillance action targeting securities with abnormal price or volume activity.
