## Summary

BSE has informed its members that the Edelweiss Nifty LargeMidcap250 Plus 8-13 Yr G-Sec 70:30 Index Fund will reopen for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective April 9, 2026. This follows Notice No. 20260317-43 dated March 17, 2026. The fund is an equity category scheme offering SIP, STP, and SWP facilities.

## Key Points

- The scheme reopens for ongoing transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform from **April 9, 2026**.
- Facilities available: subscriptions, redemptions, SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan).
- Scheme category: **Equity**.
- This notice is pursuant to an earlier Notice No. 20260317-43 dated March 17, 2026.
- Full details of plans and options are provided in the attached Annexure.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notice regarding the availability of a mutual fund scheme on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action required from members beyond updating their systems and processes to reflect the availability of this scheme for ongoing transactions from April 9, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **April 9, 2026**: Effective date for reopening of the Edelweiss Nifty LargeMidcap250 Plus 8-13 Yr G-Sec 70:30 Index Fund for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions on the BSE StAR MF Platform.
- **March 17, 2026**: Date of original Notice No. 20260317-43 referenced in this circular.

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a routine operational update enabling investors to transact in the Edelweiss Nifty LargeMidcap250 Plus 8-13 Yr G-Sec 70:30 Index Fund through the BSE StAR MF Platform. The fund is a hybrid equity-debt index fund tracking both the Nifty LargeMidcap250 index and 8-13 year government securities in a 70:30 ratio, catering to investors seeking blended equity and fixed income exposure.
