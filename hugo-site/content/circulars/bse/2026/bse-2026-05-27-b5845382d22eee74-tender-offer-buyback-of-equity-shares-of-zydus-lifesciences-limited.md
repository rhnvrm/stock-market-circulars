## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that Zydus Lifesciences Limited (BSE Code: 532321/132321) has fixed 29 May 2026 as the record date for its tender offer buyback. This date will be used to determine the entitlement and identify the names of equity shareholders eligible to participate in the buyback offer.

## Key Points

- Zydus Lifesciences Limited has announced a tender offer buyback of its equity shares.
- Record date fixed at 29 May 2026 for determining eligible shareholders.
- Ex-entitlement date is also 29 May 2026.
- Settlement reference: DR-640/2026-2027 (Dematerialised Securities – Rolling Settlement Segment).
- BSE codes: 532321 / 132321.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notification under BSE's listing compliance framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are required to take note of the record date and ex-entitlement date.
- Shareholders holding equity shares of Zydus Lifesciences Limited as on the record date (29 May 2026) will be eligible to participate in the buyback offer.

## Important Dates

- **Record Date:** 29 May 2026
- **Ex-Entitlement Date:** 29 May 2026
- **Settlement Number:** DR-640/2026-2027

## Impact Assessment

Shareholders of Zydus Lifesciences Limited holding shares as on 29 May 2026 will be entitled to participate in the buyback tender offer. The buyback provides an exit opportunity at the offer price, which is typically at a premium to market price. Trading members should notify their clients holding ZYDUSLIFE shares accordingly. Broader market impact is limited to the stock and its existing shareholders.
