## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document of **Anand Seamless Limited**, filed on February 27, 2026, on the SME platform. The document complies with Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and is available for public review and comments for a minimum period of 21 days from March 2, 2026.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Anand Seamless Limited on February 27, 2026
- Document is compliant with Schedule VI of the ICDR (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations
- Hosted on the BSE SME website for public access
- Public comments are invited within 21 days from the date of this notice (deadline: approximately March 23, 2026)
- Comments to be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular is issued in accordance with existing ICDR Regulations requiring public availability of draft offer documents for SME IPOs.

## Compliance Requirements

- Interested parties and the public may review the Draft Offer Document at: https://www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Any comments or objections must be submitted within 21 days from March 2, 2026 to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Draft Offer Document Filing Date:** February 27, 2026
- **Notice Date:** March 2, 2026
- **Public Comment Deadline:** On or before March 23, 2026 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a limited and localised market impact. It is relevant to prospective retail and institutional investors interested in participating in the upcoming SME IPO of Anand Seamless Limited. There is no immediate impact on broader market operations or existing listed securities. The 21-day public comment window is a standard regulatory step before the IPO proceeds further.
