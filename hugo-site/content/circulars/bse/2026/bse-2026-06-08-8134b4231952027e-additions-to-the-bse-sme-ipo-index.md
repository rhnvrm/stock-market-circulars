## Summary

BSE Index Services Private Limited has announced the addition of two newly listed SME stocks — MERRITRONIX LIMITED and SMR JEWELS LIMITED — to the BSE SME IPO Index. Both stocks are listed on BSE effective June 08, 2026, with their index inclusion effective at the open of trading on June 09, 2026.

## Key Points

- MERRITRONIX LIMITED (Exchange ticker: 544773) added to BSE SME IPO Index effective June 09, 2026
- SMR JEWELS LIMITED (Exchange ticker: 544774) added to BSE SME IPO Index effective June 09, 2026
- Both stocks listed on BSE effective Monday, June 08, 2026
- The index additions are captured in the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE) dated June 08, 2026
- Queries may be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index rebalancing action following new SME IPO listings on BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index-tracking funds and products referencing the BSE SME IPO Index should update their portfolios to reflect these additions effective at the open of June 09, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **June 08, 2026**: Listing date for MERRITRONIX LIMITED and SMR JEWELS LIMITED on BSE
- **June 09, 2026 (market open)**: Effective date for addition of both stocks to the BSE SME IPO Index

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. The addition of these two SME stocks to the BSE SME IPO Index is a standard post-listing index inclusion event. The BSE SME IPO Index tracks recently listed SME IPO companies, and these inclusions are expected following the stocks' listing. Impact is confined to index-tracking products and any funds benchmarked against the BSE SME IPO Index.
