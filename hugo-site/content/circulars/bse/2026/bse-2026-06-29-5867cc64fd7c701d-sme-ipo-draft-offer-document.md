## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document (DRHP) of Powertronix Engineering Limited, which was filed on June 26, 2026. The document is hosted on the BSE SME website in accordance with Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and is open for public comments for a minimum of 21 days from the notice date of June 29, 2026.

## Key Points

- Powertronix Engineering Limited filed its Draft Offer Document on June 26, 2026
- The document is available on the BSE SME website: https://www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- All disclosures are as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations
- Public comments are invited within 21 days from June 29, 2026 (deadline: July 20, 2026)
- Comments should be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural notice under ICDR Regulations requiring public display of draft offer documents for SME IPOs.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members of the public and stakeholders who wish to comment on the Draft Offer Document must submit their comments to equity.ipo@bseindia.com within 21 days of the notice date.
- The company and exchange must keep the document accessible on the SME platform for at least 21 days per ICDR norms.

## Important Dates

- **Document Filed:** June 26, 2026
- **Notice Date:** June 29, 2026
- **Public Comment Deadline:** July 20, 2026 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal broad market impact. It is a routine administrative step in the SME IPO process for Powertronix Engineering Limited. It is relevant primarily to prospective investors, analysts, and stakeholders interested in evaluating or commenting on the company's proposed public offering on the BSE SME platform.
