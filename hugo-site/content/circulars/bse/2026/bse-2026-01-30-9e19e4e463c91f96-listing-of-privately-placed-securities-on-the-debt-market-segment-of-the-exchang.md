## Summary

BSE has notified trading members about the listing of new commercial paper securities issued on a private placement basis by multiple companies effective January 30, 2026. The securities will trade in dematerialized form on the BSE Debt Segment with standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and tick size of 1 paise.

## Key Points

- Five commercial paper issuances listed from 360 ONE Alternates Asset Management, Bajaj Finance, Pilani Investment And Industries Corporation, Gera Developments, and Vivriti Capital
- All securities to trade only in dematerialized form under respective ISIN numbers
- Standard trading denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Total quantity across all issuances: 5,800 units
- Interest rates range from 6.95% to 8.10% per annum
- Maturity periods range from 3 months to 12 months

## Securities Listed

### 360 ONE Alternates Asset Management Limited
- **CP 1**: 100 units, ISIN INE0TQI14087, 8.00% p.a., maturing 29/04/2026
- **CP 2**: 200 units, ISIN INE0TQI14079, 8.10% p.a., maturing 29/07/2026
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Limited

### Bajaj Finance Limited
- **CP**: 3,000 units, ISIN INE296A14E61, 7.65% p.a., maturing 30/04/2026
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited

### Pilani Investment And Industries Corporation Limited
- **CP**: 2,000 units, ISIN INE417C14AK6, 8.00% p.a., maturing 29/01/2027
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Ltd

### Gera Developments Private Limited
- **CP**: 500 units, ISIN INE965R14013, 6.95% p.a., maturing 29/07/2026
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+
- Issuing and Paying Agent: AXIS BANK LIMITED

### Vivriti Capital Limited
- Details mentioned but not fully provided in the circular content

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading must adhere to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- All trades subject to 1 paise tick size
- For clarifications, trading members should contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: January 30, 2026
- **Allotment Dates**: January 29-30, 2026
- **Redemption Dates**: Range from April 29, 2026 to January 29, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard operational notification informing trading members about new debt securities available for trading. The listing adds commercial paper options for debt market participants and increases liquidity in the debt segment. All issuers carry A1+ credit ratings indicating high credit quality.
