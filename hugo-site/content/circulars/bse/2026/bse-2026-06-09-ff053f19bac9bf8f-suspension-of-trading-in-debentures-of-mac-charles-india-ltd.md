## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (Notice No. 20260609-11) informing trading members that Mac Charles India Ltd has communicated early redemption of its Non-Convertible Debenture (NCD) with ISIN INE435D07144 (Scrip Code: 974457). Consequently, trading in this NCD is suspended with effect from June 10, 2026.

## Key Points

- Mac Charles India Ltd has notified BSE of the early redemption of its NCD (MCL-IRR-24-8-26-PVT).
- ISIN: INE435D07144 | Scrip Code: 974457
- Trading members are advised not to deal in the aforesaid NCD effective June 10, 2026.
- Reference: DR-648/2026-2027

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is an operational notice pursuant to an early redemption event disclosed by the issuer.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease dealing in the NCD (ISIN: INE435D07144, Scrip Code: 974457) of Mac Charles India Ltd from June 10, 2026 onwards.
- Members are required to take note of the suspension and ensure no trades are executed in this instrument post the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 09, 2026
- **Suspension Effective Date:** June 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The suspension is limited to a single NCD issued by Mac Charles India Ltd and is a routine consequence of the company's decision to redeem the debenture early. Existing holders of this NCD will receive redemption proceeds as per the terms of the instrument. There is no broader market impact beyond holders and traders of this specific debt security.
