## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Genesis Ibrc India Ltd (Scrip Code: 514336) has officially changed its name to **CCME Global Limited**. The change will take effect on **April 22, 2026**. The SCRIP ID and abbreviated name on the BOLT Plus System will remain **CCME**, so no system-level identifier change is required for trading members.

## Key Points

- Company name changes from **Genesis Ibrc India Ltd** to **CCME Global Limited**
- Scrip Code remains **514336**
- BOLT Plus SCRIP ID remains **CCME**
- Abbreviated name on BOLT Plus System remains **CCME**
- Effective date: **April 22, 2026**
- Notice issued by Listing Compliance & Operations department

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory rule changes. This is a company-initiated name change under the listing framework. BSE is informing trading members of the updated corporate identity as required under listing obligations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should update internal records to reflect the new company name: **CCME Global Limited**
- No changes to BOLT Plus System configurations are required as the SCRIP ID and abbreviated name remain the same

## Important Dates

- **April 16, 2026** – Notice issued by BSE
- **April 22, 2026** – Effective date for the name change on the Exchange

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market or operational impact. The SCRIP ID (CCME) and abbreviated name (CCME) on the BOLT Plus System are unchanged, so trading workflows are unaffected. Trading members and investors should note the updated legal name — CCME Global Limited — for documentation and correspondence purposes from April 22, 2026 onwards.
