## Summary

BSE has revised the go-live schedule for enhancements to its Trading System APIs (ETI API v1.6.14) in Equity Derivatives, Currency Derivatives, Commodity Derivatives, and EGR segments. Based on feedback from trading members, the go-live has been pushed from June 29, 2026 to July 13, 2026, with mock trading rescheduled to July 11, 2026. The Equity (Cash) segment go-live on July 6, 2026 (mock trading July 4, 2026) remains unchanged.

## Key Points

- ETI API v1.6.14 go-live for Equity Derivatives, Currency Derivatives, Commodity Derivatives & EGR segments is extended to **Monday, July 13, 2026**.
- Mock trading for these derivative segments is rescheduled to **Saturday, July 11, 2026**.
- Equity (Cash) segment go-live remains on **Monday, July 6, 2026** with mock trading on **Saturday, July 4, 2026** — no change.
- Extension is based on feedback received from trading members.
- This is a continuation of Exchange circulars 20260515-36 (May 15, 2026) and 20260529-13 (May 29, 2026).

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory rule changes. This is an operational update revising the deployment timeline for mandatory API enhancements previously communicated.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members using ETI APIs in Equity Derivatives, Currency Derivatives, Commodity Derivatives, and EGR segments must upgrade to ETI API v1.6.14 by **July 13, 2026**.
- Members in the Equity (Cash) segment must comply by **July 6, 2026** (deadline unchanged).
- Members are encouraged to participate in mock trading sessions to validate readiness before the go-live dates.
- For technical queries, contact BSE Tech Team at **bse.tech@bseindia.com** or **022 2272 8053**.

## Important Dates

| Segment | Mock Trading | Go-Live |
|---|---|---|
| Equity (Cash) | Saturday, July 4, 2026 | Monday, July 6, 2026 |
| Equity Derivatives, Currency Derivatives, Commodity Derivatives & EGR | Saturday, July 11, 2026 | Monday, July 13, 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

Trading members and technology vendors interfacing with BSE via ETI APIs across derivative segments get additional time (approximately two weeks beyond the original June 29, 2026 date) to complete integration and testing. This reduces the risk of disruptions at go-live. The mandatory nature of the release means non-compliant systems will be unable to trade in affected segments post go-live. Members should use the mock trading window on July 11, 2026 to validate API compatibility.
