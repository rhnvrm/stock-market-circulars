## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document of AEGEUS TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED filed on December 31, 2025, containing disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations. The document is available on BSE SME website for a minimum 21-day public comment period.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by AEGEUS TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED on December 31, 2025
- Document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website at www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public comment period of at least 21 days from notice date
- Comments to be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard disclosure pursuant to existing ICDR Regulations requirements for SME IPOs.

## Compliance Requirements

- Interested parties can review the Draft Offer Document on BSE SME website
- Public comments, if any, must be submitted within 21 days from January 01, 2026
- Comments should be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 01, 2026
- **Draft Document Filed**: December 31, 2025
- **Public Comment Deadline**: January 22, 2026 (21 days from notice date)
- **Document Availability Period**: Minimum 21 days on BSE SME website

## Impact Assessment

This notice has limited market impact as it pertains to a single SME IPO. The draft offer document provides transparency for prospective investors in AEGEUS TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED. The 21-day public comment period allows stakeholders to review and provide feedback on the proposed IPO before finalization. This is part of the standard regulatory process for SME listings on BSE.
