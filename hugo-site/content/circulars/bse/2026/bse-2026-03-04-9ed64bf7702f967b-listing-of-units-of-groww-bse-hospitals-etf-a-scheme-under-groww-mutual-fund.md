## Summary

Groww Mutual Fund's Groww BSE Hospitals ETF has been listed and admitted to dealings on BSE under Group B with effect from Thursday, March 5, 2026. The ETF is an open-ended scheme tracking the BSE Hospitals Index – TRI, managed by Groww Asset Management Limited. Units will trade exclusively in dematerialized form in a market lot of one unit.

## Key Points

- **Scrip Code:** 544721 | **Scrip ID:** GROWWHOSPI | **ISIN:** INF666M01OD9
- **Number of Units Listed:** 1,786,452 units
- **Face Value:** Rs. 10.00 per unit | **Issue Price:** Rs. 48.88 per unit
- **Date of Allotment:** February 02, 2026
- **Group:** B | **Market Lot:** 1 unit
- Units must be traded only in dematerialized form as per SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000
- Four market makers appointed: Kanjalochana Finserve Pvt Ltd, East India Securities, Parwati Capital Market Pvt. Ltd, and IRAGE BROKING SERVICES LLP

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The listing complies with existing SEBI guidelines requiring mutual fund units to be traded solely in dematerialized form.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all trades in GROWWHOSPI are executed in demat form only.
- Trades must be in unit market lot (minimum 1 unit).
- Members requiring clarification may contact Mr. Parag Jain at mf.listing@bseindia.com or Tel: 022-2272-8685.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** February 2, 2026
- **Listing / Trading Effective Date:** March 5, 2026
- **Circular Date:** March 4, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This listing provides investors with exchange-traded access to the BSE Hospitals Index – TRI through the Groww BSE Hospitals ETF, expanding options for thematic exposure to the Indian hospitals/healthcare sector. The appointment of four market makers supports liquidity. Impact is limited to market participants interested in healthcare-sector ETFs; no broad systemic or compliance impact on the wider market.
