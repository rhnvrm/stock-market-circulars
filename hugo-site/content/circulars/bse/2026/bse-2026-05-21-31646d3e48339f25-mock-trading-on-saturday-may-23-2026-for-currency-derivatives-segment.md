## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Currency Derivatives segment on Saturday, May 23, 2026. The session is intended for trading members to test their third-party or in-house trading platforms via ETI APIs under simulated market conditions, including exceptional scenarios. No new BOLT TWS release is planned.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Currency Derivatives and Cross Currency Derivatives on May 23, 2026
- Open to trading members using third-party empaneled vendor platforms or in-house systems via ETI APIs
- No new BOLT TWS release associated with this session
- Trades during mock session carry no margin, pay-in/pay-out obligations, or legal rights and liabilities
- Members are encouraged to actively participate and provide feedback by 1:00 PM

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an operational notice for a scheduled mock trading exercise.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are requested to participate actively in the mock trading session
- Feedback must be submitted by 1:00 PM on the day of the session via the contact details provided
- Members with queries should contact their Relationship Manager, BSE Helpdesk, or BSE Tech Support

## Important Dates

- **Mock Trading Date:** Saturday, May 23, 2026
- **Log-in Window:** 9:15 AM – 10:00 AM
- **Continuous Trading (Currency Derivatives):** 10:00 AM – 12:00 PM
- **Continuous Trading (Cross Currency Derivatives):** 10:00 AM – 12:00 PM
- **Feedback Deadline:** 1:00 PM on May 23, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact as it pertains solely to a mock/test trading session. No actual trades, obligations, or liabilities will arise from participation. The session is operationally relevant to trading members who use Currency Derivatives platforms and wish to validate their systems before live trading.
