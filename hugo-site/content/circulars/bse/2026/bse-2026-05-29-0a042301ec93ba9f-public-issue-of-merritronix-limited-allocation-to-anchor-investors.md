## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that MERRITRONIX LIMITED has completed the Anchor Investor (AI) allocation as part of its public issue. A total of 13,36,000 equity shares were subscribed by Anchor Investors on May 29, 2026, at a price of Rs. 149 per equity share.

## Key Points

- 13,36,000 equity shares allocated to Anchor Investors under the MERRITRONIX LIMITED public issue
- Anchor Investor price fixed at Rs. 149 per equity share
- Allocation completed on May 29, 2026
- Notice issued by Mangesh Tayde, Vice President, BSE
- Company announcement dated May 29, 2026 is enclosed as attachment (Company_Anchor_Allocation)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine procedural notice informing trading members of anchor investor allocation under an ongoing public issue.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action required from trading members. The notice is informational, advising members of the anchor investor subscription details ahead of the broader public issue opening.

## Important Dates

- **Anchor Allocation Date:** May 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard pre-IPO anchor allocation disclosure with limited market impact. The anchor investor price of Rs. 149 per share sets a reference point for the public issue price. The allocation of 13,36,000 shares to anchor investors signals institutional interest in the offering. Impact is confined to prospective investors and trading members participating in the MERRITRONIX LIMITED IPO.
