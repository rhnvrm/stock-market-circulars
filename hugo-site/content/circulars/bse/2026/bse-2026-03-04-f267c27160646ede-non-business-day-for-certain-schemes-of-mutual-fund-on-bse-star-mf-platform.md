## Summary

BSE has notified non-business days for March 2026 for select schemes of Invesco India, DSP, Kotak, and Nippon India mutual funds on the BSE StAR MF platform. On the specified dates, transactions (subscriptions/redemptions) in the listed schemes will not be processed.

## Key Points

- Invesco India: Four overseas Fund of Funds (including Pan European, Global Equity Income, Global Consumer Trends, and EQQQ Nasdaq-100 ETF FoF) are closed on **March 19, 2026**.
- DSP: DSP Value Fund is closed on **March 17, 2026**. All DSP Debt Schemes (including DSP Liquidity Fund, Overnight Fund, and multiple Overseas Equity Omni FoFs) are closed on **March 19, 2026**. Notably, DSP Gold ETF FoF, Multi Asset Allocation Fund, Silver ETF FoF, and Equity Savings Fund remain open on March 19.
- Kotak: Multiple overseas FOFs (Global Emerging Market, US Specific Equity, Global Innovation, International REIT) are closed on **March 3, March 6, March 26, and March 31, 2026**.
- Nippon India: Nippon India Japan Equity Fund is closed on **March 20, 2026**.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notification listing designated non-business days for specific mutual fund schemes.

## Compliance Requirements

- Distributors and investors must refrain from placing transactions in the affected schemes on the specified non-business days via the BSE StAR MF platform.
- AMCs and platform operators must ensure the schemes are unavailable for transactions on the listed dates.

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited and routine. The non-business days primarily affect overseas Fund of Funds and debt schemes, typically aligned with market holidays in the underlying international markets (e.g., European, US, Japanese markets). Investors in these schemes should plan transactions around the listed closure dates. DSP's exception carve-out for Gold ETF FoF, Silver ETF FoF, Multi Asset Allocation Fund, and Equity Savings Fund on March 19 is noteworthy for investors in those schemes.
