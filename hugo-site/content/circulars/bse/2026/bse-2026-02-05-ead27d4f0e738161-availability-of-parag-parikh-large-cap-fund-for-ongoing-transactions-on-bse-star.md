## Summary

BSE has announced that PARAG PARIKH LARGE CAP FUND will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective February 06, 2026. The fund will also support SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities. This follows a previous notice (20260116-56) regarding the fund's status.

## Key Points

- PARAG PARIKH LARGE CAP FUND reopening for transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Effective date: February 06, 2026
- Available facilities: Subscription, Redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Six plan variants available: Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Reinvestment, and IDCW Payout options
- All plans are equity category funds

## Scheme Details

The following six plan variants are available:

1. **Regular Plan - Growth** (ISIN: INF879O01365)
2. **Regular Plan - IDCW Reinvestment** (ISIN: INF879O01381)
3. **Regular Plan - IDCW Payout** (ISIN: INF879O01373)
4. **Direct Plan - Growth** (ISIN: INF879O01332)
5. **Direct Plan - IDCW Reinvestment** (ISIN: INF879O01357)
6. **Direct Plan - IDCW Payout** (ISIN: INF879O01340)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding fund availability.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. Members and investors can proceed with transactions through the BSE StAR MF Platform as per existing procedures.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** February 05, 2026
- **Effective Date:** February 06, 2026 - Fund reopens for ongoing transactions

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal market impact and is primarily informational for investors and members of the BSE StAR MF Platform. It enables existing and new investors to resume or initiate transactions in PARAG PARIKH LARGE CAP FUND. The availability of systematic investment facilities (SIP, STP, SWP) provides flexibility for retail investors engaging in long-term wealth creation strategies through this large-cap equity fund.
