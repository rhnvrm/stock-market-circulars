## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Softbpo Global Services Ltd (Scrip Code 504375) has officially changed its name to IDream Film Infrastructure Company Limited. The SCRIP ID and abbreviated name on the BOLT Plus System will be updated accordingly effective May 07, 2026.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code 504375 undergoes a name change from Softbpo Global Services Ltd to IDream Film Infrastructure Company Limited
- New SCRIP ID on BOLT Plus System: IDREAM
- New abbreviated name on BOLT Plus System: IDREAM
- Change effective date: May 07, 2026
- A Certificate of Incorporation has been attached as supporting documentation

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative update to reflect the company's legal name change as registered with the relevant authority, evidenced by the Certificate of Incorporation.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to take note of the updated SCRIP ID (IDREAM) and abbreviated name (IDREAM) for Scrip Code 504375 when executing trades on the BOLT Plus System on or after May 07, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **April 30, 2026**: BSE notice issued
- **May 07, 2026**: Effective date for new SCRIP ID and abbreviated name on BOLT Plus System

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine administrative name change affecting only the display identifiers (SCRIP ID and abbreviated name) of a single scrip on the BOLT Plus trading system. Traders should update their records to reference IDREAM instead of the previous Softbpo Global Services Ltd identifiers to avoid confusion in order placement after the effective date.
