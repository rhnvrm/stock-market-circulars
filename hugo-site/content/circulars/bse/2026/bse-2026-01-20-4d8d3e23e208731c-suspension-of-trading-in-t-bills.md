## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in T-Bill 91TB220126 (ISIN: IN002025X307) effective January 21, 2026, as the instrument is scheduled to mature on the redemption date of January 22, 2026. Trading members are advised to cease all dealings in this T-Bill from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 805133
- ISIN: IN002025X307
- Security: 91TB220126 (91-day Treasury Bill)
- Trading suspension effective: January 21, 2026
- Maturity/Redemption date: January 22, 2026
- Notice reference: DR-803/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for instrument maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal with T-Bill 91TB220126 from January 21, 2026
- All trading activities in this instrument must cease before the suspension date
- Members should update their trading systems to reflect the suspension

## Important Dates

- **January 20, 2026**: Notice date
- **January 21, 2026**: Trading suspension effective date
- **January 22, 2026**: T-Bill maturity/redemption date

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine operational suspension affecting a single T-Bill instrument approaching maturity. Only holders and prospective traders of this specific 91-day Treasury Bill are affected. Standard redemption procedures will apply for holders on the maturity date.
