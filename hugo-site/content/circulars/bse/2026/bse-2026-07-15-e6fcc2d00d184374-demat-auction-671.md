## Summary

BSE has issued a notice inviting offers from trading members of the Exchange for purchase of securities in Auction (ARR 671 / Demat Auction 671) in respect of Rolling Settlement No. DR-671/2026-27. The auction process is scheduled for Wednesday, July 15, 2026, with pay-in on Thursday, July 16, 2026.

## Key Points

- BSE Limited invites offers from trading members for purchase of securities in Auction under Rolling Settlement No. DR-671/2026-27.
- Auction offer entry window: 02.00 p.m. to 02.45 p.m.
- Auction matching window: 02.45 p.m. to 03.00 p.m.
- Delivery statement and report of accepted auction offers: 03.00 p.m. to 03.30 p.m.
- Money statement and final reports: 03.35 p.m. to 05.30 p.m.
- Pay-in of the auction to be held on Thursday, July 16, 2026 at 08.00 a.m.
- Settlement will be done through the Clearing Member of a Trading Member, per the Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment.
- The earlier restriction preventing trading members from offering securities in auction in the settlement where they had short-delivered such securities has been removed.
- No corrections will be permitted for punching errors in securities code and rate; the Exchange reserves the right to accept or reject any offer without assigning reasons.
- Trading members should refer to the cutoff rate on the Auction Offer Entry Screen and to notices on Corporate Actions (Ex-Rights/Ex-Bonus/Ex-Split etc.) before submitting offers.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine operational notice under existing settlement and auction procedures, referencing the previously implemented Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and custodians must check their auction pay-in obligations for securities and execute securities pay-in in depositories within depository cut-off timings.
- Members must exercise care while entering securities codes and rates, as no corrections are allowed post-punching.
- Members should cross-check applicable Corporate Action notices before placing auction offers.

## Important Dates

- Auction offer entry, matching, and reporting: Wednesday, July 15, 2026 (02.00 p.m. – 05.30 p.m.)
- Auction pay-in: Thursday, July 16, 2026 at 08.00 a.m.

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard operational/settlement notice relevant only to trading members and custodians involved in the demat auction process for Rolling Settlement DR-671/2026-27. It has no direct bearing on listed companies' stock prices or broader market sentiment, and carries low severity and impact.
