## Summary

BSE has notified that 1,29,93,708 equity shares of Ambuja Cements Limited (Scrip Code: 500425, ISIN: INE079A01024) will be listed and permitted for trading effective June 30, 2026. These shares were issued pursuant to the Scheme of Arrangement between Sanghi Industries Limited (Transferor Company) and Ambuja Cements Limited (Transferee Company).

## Key Points

- 1,29,93,708 new equity shares of Rs. 2/- face value each are being listed on BSE.
- Listing is effective from Tuesday, June 30, 2026.
- Shares issued under the Scheme of Arrangement between Sanghi Industries Limited (Transferor) and Ambuja Cements Limited (Transferee).
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of Ambuja Cements Limited.
- ISIN: INE079A01024; Distribution Nos.: 2622493599 – 2635487306.
- Date of Allotment: April 10, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an operational notice confirming the admission to trading of shares already allotted under a court/NCLT-approved scheme of arrangement.

## Compliance Requirements

Market participants are informed to update their records to reflect the additional 1,29,93,708 shares of Ambuja Cements Limited (Scrip Code: 500425) available for trading from June 30, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** April 10, 2026
- **Listing / Trading Effective Date:** June 30, 2026
- **Notice Date:** June 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing increases the tradeable float of Ambuja Cements Limited by approximately 1.3 crore shares. Since the shares are pari-passu with existing equity, there is no dilution in rights for existing shareholders beyond the share count increase. This is a standard post-merger listing event following the absorption of Sanghi Industries Limited into Ambuja Cements Limited, and market impact is expected to be minimal.
