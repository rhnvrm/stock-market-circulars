## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (20260409-1) informing trading members of a revision to the ISIN associated with Aditya Birla Real Estate Ltd's debt securities under Scrip Code 975457. This update is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20250613-47 dated June 13, 2025. The revised ISIN takes effect from Wednesday, April 10, 2026.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code 975457 of Aditya Birla Real Estate Ltd will trade under a revised ISIN from April 10, 2026
- Old ISIN: INE055A08045
- New ISIN: INE055A08078
- This notice continues from Exchange Notice No. 20250613-47 dated June 13, 2025
- Trading members must update their systems to reflect the new ISIN

## Regulatory Changes

The ISIN for Scrip Code 975457 (Aditya Birla Real Estate Ltd debt security) is being revised from INE055A08045 to INE055A08078. Trading in this instrument will reflect the new terms associated with the updated ISIN effective April 10, 2026.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to:
- Take note of the ISIN change for Scrip Code 975457
- Ensure internal systems, order management, and settlement workflows are updated to reflect the new ISIN (INE055A08078) before trading commences on April 10, 2026
- Contact the designated BSE officials for any clarifications

## Important Dates

- **April 9, 2026**: Notice issued
- **April 10, 2026 (Wednesday)**: Revised ISIN becomes effective for trading
- **Reference Notice**: 20250613-47 dated June 13, 2025 (original notification)

## Impact Assessment

This change has a moderate operational impact on trading members who hold or trade Aditya Birla Real Estate Ltd's debt securities under Scrip Code 975457. Systems referencing the old ISIN (INE055A08045) must be updated to the new ISIN (INE055A08078) to avoid settlement or trade booking errors. The impact is limited in scope to this single debt instrument and does not affect equity markets.
