## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures of AXIS FINANCE LIMITED (ISIN: INE891K07945, Code: 975448) effective February 12, 2026. The suspension is imposed as the company has fixed the record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest on the same date.

## Key Points

- Trading Members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from February 12, 2026
- The debentures affected are AFL-8.35%-27-2-26-PVT series
- Record date fixed for both redemption and interest payment purposes
- Notice issued under DR-819/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notification for debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must cease all dealings in the specified debentures from February 12, 2026
- Trading Members are required to take note of this suspension and ensure compliance
- No transactions should be executed in ISIN INE891K07945 (Code: 975448) from the specified date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: February 10, 2026
- **Record Date**: February 12, 2026
- **No Dealings From**: February 12, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: High - Complete trading suspension affects liquidity for debenture holders who may need to exit positions before the suspension date.

**Investor Impact**: High - Debenture holders of AFL-8.35%-27-2-26-PVT series will receive their interest payment and principal redemption based on holdings as of the record date. Trading is prohibited from the record date onwards.

**Operational Impact**: Trading Members must update their systems to block transactions in the specified debenture ISIN from February 12, 2026 to ensure compliance with the suspension directive.
