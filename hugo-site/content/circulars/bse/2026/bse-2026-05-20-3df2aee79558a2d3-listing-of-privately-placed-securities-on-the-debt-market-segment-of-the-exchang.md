## Summary

BSE has notified the listing of newly issued Commercial Papers (CPs) on the BSE Debt Market Segment effective May 20, 2026. The securities were issued on a private placement basis and are now admitted to trading. The notice covers at least four CP issuances across three issuers: Adani Ports and Special Economic Zone Ltd, Fedbank Financial Services Ltd, and Jamnagar Utilities & Power Private Limited.

## Key Points

- All securities are traded exclusively in dematerialised form under their respective ISIN numbers
- Standard denomination for trading is Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for all listed securities is 1 paise
- Date of allotment for all issuances: May 19, 2026
- Queries to be directed to BSE Debt Department: 22728352 / 8597 / 8995 / 5753 / 8915

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice for debt market participants. The listing of these Commercial Papers expands the tradeable instruments on the BSE Debt Segment. All four CPs carry the highest short-term credit ratings (A1+ from multiple agencies), indicating low credit risk. Jamnagar Utilities & Power's combined issuance of 38,000 units (Rs. 1,900 crore face value) represents the largest single-issuer exposure in this batch. Fedbank Financial Services' higher yield of 8.10% p.a. versus Adani Ports' 7.20% reflects differing issuer credit profiles despite both carrying top ratings. Impact is limited to institutional and debt market participants; no direct equity market impact is anticipated.
