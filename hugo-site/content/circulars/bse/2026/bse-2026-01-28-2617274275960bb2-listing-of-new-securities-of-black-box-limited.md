## Summary

BSE has listed 71,944 new equity shares of BLACK BOX LIMITED (Scrip Code: 500463) effective January 29, 2026. These shares were issued at Rs. 417/- per share (face value Rs. 2/- plus premium of Rs. 415/-) to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. The shares are subject to lock-in until August 5, 2026.

## Key Points

- 71,944 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each listed
- Issue price: Rs. 417/- per share (Rs. 2/- face value + Rs. 415/- premium)
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis
- Shares issued pursuant to conversion of warrants
- Distinguished Numbers: 170281101 to 170353044
- Date of allotment: December 17, 2025
- Trading commences: January 29, 2026
- ISIN: INE676A01027
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for new securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the new securities available for trading from January 29, 2026
- Lock-in restrictions apply to all 71,944 shares until August 5, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: December 17, 2025
- **Listing Date**: January 29, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry**: August 5, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The listing involves a small number of shares (71,944) representing a minor addition to the company's equity capital. The shares are subject to lock-in until August 5, 2026, preventing immediate trading. This is a routine corporate action related to warrant conversion with no significant operational or market implications.
