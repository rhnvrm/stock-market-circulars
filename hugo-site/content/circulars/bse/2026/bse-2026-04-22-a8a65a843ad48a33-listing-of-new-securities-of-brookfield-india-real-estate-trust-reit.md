## Summary

BSE has listed 8,04,95,356 new units of Brookfield India Real Estate Trust REIT (Scrip Code: 543261, ISIN: INE0FDU25010) allotted to Qualified Institutional Buyers (QIBs) pursuant to an Institutional Placement. The units are admitted for trading on the Exchange effective Thursday, April 23, 2026.

## Key Points

- **Units Listed:** 8,04,95,356 units allotted to QIBs via Institutional Placement
- **Scrip Code:** 543261
- **ISIN:** INE0FDU25010
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 323 per unit
- **Distribution Numbers:** 749385514 to 829880869
- **Pari-passu:** New units rank equally with existing units
- **Trading Commencement:** April 23, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice following completion of an Institutional Placement by Brookfield India Real Estate Trust REIT.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are notified to update their systems to reflect the newly listed units. For clarifications, members may contact Mr. Pranav Rewale at Tel. No. 9653268851.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** April 22, 2026
- **Trading Effective Date:** April 23, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing of approximately 8.05 crore new units at Rs. 323 each represents a significant capital raise (~Rs. 2,599 crore) for Brookfield India REIT. The new units are pari-passu with existing units, meaning no dilution in rights for existing unitholders beyond the increase in unit count. Market impact is expected to be moderate as institutional investors (QIBs) are the allottees, and trading begins the following day.
