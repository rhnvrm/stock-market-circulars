## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that LGT Business Connextions Ltd (Scrip Code: 544489) has informed the Exchange of a change in the company's name to LGT Global Hospitality Limited. The Scrip ID and Abbreviated Name on the BOLT Plus System will be updated accordingly, effective July 13, 2026.

## Key Points

- Existing Name: LGT Business Connextions Ltd
- New Name: LGT Global Hospitality Limited
- Scrip Code: 544489 (unchanged)
- Scrip ID for BOLT Plus System: LGT (no change)
- Abbreviated Name on BOLT Plus System: LGT (no change)
- Notice issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President - Listing Compliance & Operations

## Regulatory Changes

The company name change has been recorded by BSE based on the Certificate of Incorporation (COI) submitted by the company. No change is being made to the Scrip ID.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the updated company name for record-keeping and reference purposes. No action is required for trading as the Scrip Code and Scrip ID remain unchanged.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 07, 2026
- Effective Date of Name Change: July 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a purely administrative name change with no impact on trading, corporate structure, or shareholder rights. Since the Scrip ID and Abbreviated Name remain unchanged, there should be minimal disruption to trading systems or market participants.
