## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 48,000 new equity shares of Naapbooks Limited (Scrip Code: 543351) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Thursday, April 16, 2026. The shares were issued on a preferential basis to non-promoters pursuant to the conversion of warrants at an issue price of Rs. 61/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- + premium Rs. 51/-).

## Key Points

- 48,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 51/- (issue price Rs. 61/-) to non-promoters on a preferential basis
- Shares issued pursuant to conversion of warrants
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 10786201 to 10834200
- ISIN: INE0GOA01018
- All 48,000 shares are subject to lock-in until November 14, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification for newly allotted shares.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to update their systems to reflect the new securities available for trading from April 16, 2026
- The lock-in restriction on all 48,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 10786201 to 10834200) must be observed until November 14, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** January 22, 2026
- **Trading Effective From:** April 16, 2026 (Thursday)
- **Lock-in Expiry:** November 14, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing of 48,000 shares represents a minor increase in the tradable float of Naapbooks Limited. Since all newly listed shares are under lock-in until November 14, 2026, there is no immediate increase in freely tradable supply. The market impact is expected to be minimal given the small size of the allotment and the lock-in restriction.
