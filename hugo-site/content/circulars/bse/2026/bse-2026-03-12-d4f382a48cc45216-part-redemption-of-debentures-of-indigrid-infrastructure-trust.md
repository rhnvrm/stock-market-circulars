## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that INDIGRID INFRASTRUCTURE TRUST has fixed 16 March 2026 as the Record Date for part redemption and interest payment on two series of Non-Convertible Debentures. Trading in these debentures will occur at a reduced face value effective from 16 March 2026 under settlement number DR-840/2025-2026.

## Key Points

- Record Date for both debenture series is **16 March 2026**
- Two NCD series are affected: IGT-7.70%-31-3-41-PVT (Code: 974781, ISIN: INE219X07371) and IGT-7.35%-31-3-38-INVIT (Code: 975036, ISIN: INE219X07389)
- IGT-7.70% series: Reduced Face Value set at **Rs. 90,000 per debenture**
- IGT-7.35% series: Reduced Face Value set at **Rs. 85,712 per debenture**
- Both series will trade at reduced face value effective 16 March 2026
- Settlement number: DR-840/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a scheduled corporate action (part redemption) under existing debt market segment rules for privately placed NCDs.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the reduced face values for both debenture series effective 16 March 2026
- All trades in these debentures from the effective date must reflect the reduced face values as specified
- Members should update their systems to reflect the new face values under settlement DR-840/2025-2026

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |

## Impact Assessment

This corporate action directly affects holders of INDIGRID INFRASTRUCTURE TRUST's two NCD series. The part redemption reduces the outstanding face value of each debenture — IGT-7.70% series to Rs. 90,000 and IGT-7.35% series to Rs. 85,712 per debenture — alongside an interest payment. Market impact is limited to existing debenture holders in the debt segment; equity markets are unaffected. Trading members must ensure correct pricing and settlement from 16 March 2026 onwards.
