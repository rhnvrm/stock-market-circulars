## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the Rights Issue of Shakti Press Limited has been extended. The issue closing date has been pushed from Friday, May 29, 2026 to Tuesday, June 02, 2026. This notice is issued in continuation of earlier notices 20260512-13 and 20260506-4.

## Key Points

- Rights Issue of Shakti Press Limited extended by four calendar days
- Original closing date: Friday, May 29, 2026
- Revised closing date: Tuesday, June 02, 2026
- Extension is in continuation to BSE notices 20260512-13 and 20260506-4

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative extension of an existing rights issue timeline.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should update their systems and inform clients about the revised closing date
- Eligible shareholders wishing to subscribe to the rights issue now have until June 02, 2026 to do so

## Important Dates

- **Revised Rights Issue Closing Date:** Tuesday, June 02, 2026
- **Original Closing Date (superseded):** Friday, May 29, 2026
- **Notice Date:** May 27, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The extension provides existing shareholders of Shakti Press Limited additional time to subscribe to the rights entitlements. No changes to the terms or pricing of the rights issue are indicated. Trading members should ensure clients are aware of the revised timeline to avoid missing the subscription window.
