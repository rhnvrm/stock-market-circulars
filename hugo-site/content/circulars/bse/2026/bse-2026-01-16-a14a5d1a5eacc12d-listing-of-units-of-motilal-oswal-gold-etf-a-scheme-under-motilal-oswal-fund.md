## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of Motilal Oswal Gold ETF units effective January 19, 2026. The scheme is an open-ended fund replicating/tracking the Gold ETF Total Return Index. The ETF will be listed in the 'E' Group with scrip code 590146 and ISIN INF247L01FY4. Three market makers have been appointed to provide liquidity.

## Key Points

- ETF Name: Motilal Oswal Gold ETF
- Listing Date: January 19, 2026
- Scrip Code: 590146
- ISIN: INF247L01FY4
- Number of Units: 600,000 units
- Face Value: Rs. 10.00 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 140.05 per unit
- Date of Allotment: July 28, 2025
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Group: E
- Trading: Only in dematerialized form
- Investment Manager: Motilal Oswal Asset Management Company Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification following SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000, requiring mutual fund units to be traded only in dematerialized form.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all trades in this ETF are executed in dematerialized form only
- Trades will be effected in unit market lot (one unit)
- Market makers must fulfill their obligations for providing liquidity

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: July 28, 2025
- **Listing Date**: January 19, 2026 (Monday)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine ETF listing with minimal market impact. The listing expands the available investment options for gold exposure through exchange-traded funds. Three market makers (East India Securities Ltd., Motilal Oswal Financial Services Limited, and Kanjalochana Finserve Private Limited) have been appointed to ensure adequate liquidity. The ETF provides investors an opportunity to gain exposure to gold prices through a regulated, transparent exchange-traded instrument.
