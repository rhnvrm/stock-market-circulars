## Summary

BSE has notified members that ABAKKUS Mutual Fund will launch the ABAKKUS Large & Mid Cap Fund as a New Fund Offer (NFO) with SIP facility on the BSE StAR MF Platform, open from July 15, 2026 to July 29, 2026. The circular outlines operational instructions for transaction processing, fund pay-in deadlines, and allotment timelines for both Direct and Regular growth plans.

## Key Points

- ABAKKUS Large & Mid Cap Fund NFO (Equity category) will be available with SIP facility on BSE StAR MF Platform.
- Two scheme variants: Direct Plan - Growth (ISIN INF2JJD01193) and Regular Plan - Growth (ISIN INF2JJD01201).
- Tentative allotment date for both schemes is August 4, 2026.
- Transactions without client fund initiation by 9:00 AM on T+1 day will be cancelled and reported to RTA/AMC with 'N' flag.
- Transactions with initiated payment remain pending until 3:00 PM on the last fund pay-out day or T+5 day, whichever is earlier.
- Cheque and OTM payments accepted during the NFO period, subject to clear funds by the cutoff.
- Switch transactions accepted during the NFO period, but switch-out funds received on/after the allotment date will not be considered for allotment.
- NFO period is extended till midnight July 29, 2026 for lumpsum purchases only; SIP/XSIP registrations must be authenticated by 3:00 PM on July 29, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is an operational/product notice enabling a new fund offer on the BSE StAR MF distribution platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure client funds are initiated by 9:00 AM on T+1 day to avoid transaction cancellation.
- Clear funds must be available in the ICCL account by 6:00 PM on August 3, 2026 for allotment eligibility.
- Cheque/OTM payments require clear funds by the same August 3, 2026, 6:00 PM cutoff.
- SIP/XSIP registrations must be authenticated by clients before 3:00 PM on July 29, 2026.

## Important Dates

- NFO Period: July 15, 2026 to July 29, 2026
- Lumpsum purchase extension: till midnight July 29, 2026
- SIP/XSIP authentication deadline: 3:00 PM, July 29, 2026
- Clear funds deadline in ICCL account: 6:00 PM, August 3, 2026
- Tentative allotment date: August 4, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice for mutual fund distributors and BSE StAR MF Platform members regarding a new fund launch. It has no direct impact on listed equity securities or broader market operations, and is relevant primarily to mutual fund distributors, RTAs, and investors participating in this specific NFO.
