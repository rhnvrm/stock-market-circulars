## Summary

BSE has notified that BERAR FINANCE LIMITED has fixed the Record Date for Payment of Interest and Part Redemption of its Non-Convertible Debentures (ISIN: INE998Y07162). Trading in these debentures will be conducted with a reduced face value of Rs. 5000 per debenture effective from January 28, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: BERAR FINANCE LIMITED
- Debenture Code: BRFL-11.50%-12-2-27-PVT
- ISIN: INE998Y07162 (Code: 976404)
- Record Date: January 28, 2026
- Purpose: Part Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- Reduced Face Value: Rs. 5000 per Debenture
- Effective Date: January 28, 2026
- Settlement Number: DR-807/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification for debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members of BSE must:
- Note that trading in the debentures will be conducted with reduced face value of Rs. 5000 per debenture
- Ensure proper settlement from the effective date January 28, 2026 under settlement number DR-807/2025-2026
- Update their systems to reflect the reduced face value for trading purposes

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 21, 2026
- **Record Date**: January 28, 2026
- **Effective Date for Reduced Face Value**: January 28, 2026
- **Settlement Number**: DR-807/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited market impact as it affects only holders of specific non-convertible debentures issued by BERAR FINANCE LIMITED. The part redemption will result in a reduced face value for the debentures, which is a routine corporate action in the debt segment. Trading members need to update their systems to reflect the new face value for accurate trading and settlement.
