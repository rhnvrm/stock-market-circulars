## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in debentures of IOT UTKAL ENERGY SERVICES LIMITED (ISIN: INE310L07878, Scrip Code: 950211) effective February 13, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for the purpose of interest payment and redemption of non-convertible debentures. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Company: IOT UTKAL ENERGY SERVICES LIMITED
- Debenture Code: 1008IOT26B
- ISIN: INE310L07878
- Scrip Code: 950211
- Record Date: February 13, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- No dealings permitted from: February 13, 2026
- Notice Number: DR-820/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard trading suspension procedure implemented when a company fixes a record date for corporate actions on debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures (1008IOT26B) from February 13, 2026
- Trading members are required to take note of the suspension and inform their clients accordingly
- All dealing activities in the mentioned debentures must cease from the specified date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: February 10, 2026
- **Record Date**: February 13, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Effective From**: February 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders and traders of the specific debenture series 1008IOT26B of IOT UTKAL ENERGY SERVICES LIMITED. The suspension is temporary and related to the processing of interest payment and redemption on the record date.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders will receive their interest payment and redemption proceeds as per the record date. No trading will be possible from February 13, 2026 onwards in this security.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to block trading in the specified debentures and communicate the suspension to clients holding or interested in trading these securities.
