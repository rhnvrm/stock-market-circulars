## Summary

BSE has informed members that the ALTIVA EQUITY EX-TOP 100 LONG-SHORT FUND will reopen for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP facility on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective June 10, 2026, following Notice No. 20260515-40 dated May 15, 2026.

## Key Points

- Six scheme variants (Regular and Direct plans) are available under Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options.
- Effective date for ongoing transactions: June 10, 2026.
- If Monday or Wednesday is a non-business day, redemption and switch-out transactions will be processed on the next working/business day.
- Redemption and switch-out transactions will follow NAV applicability rules stated in the notice, with payout TAT as per scheme master.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a reactivation of an existing fund on the BSE StAR MF Platform pursuant to a prior notice (20260515-40 dated May 15, 2026).

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must process redemption and switch-out transactions under the listed SIF schemes per the applicable NAV rules.
- Redemption payouts will be released from the AMC/RTA end as per the payout TAT in the scheme master.

## Important Dates

- **June 10, 2026**: ALTIVA EQUITY EX-TOP 100 LONG-SHORT FUND reopens for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP on BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact operational notice applicable to mutual fund distributors and members using the BSE StAR MF Platform. It restores availability of six ALTIVA fund variants (ISINs: INF754K30136, INF754K30144, INF754K30151, INF754K30094, INF754K30102, INF754K30110) for investor transactions. No broad market impact is expected.
