## Summary

BSE has listed 14,99,200 new equity shares of Hemant Surgical Industries Limited (Scrip Code: 543916) issued at Rs. 197/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- + premium Rs. 187/-) to Non-Promoters on a preferential basis, pursuant to the conversion of warrants. These shares are permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Wednesday, June 10, 2026.

## Key Points

- 14,99,200 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 187/- (issue price Rs. 197/-)
- Issued to Non-Promoters on a preferential basis via conversion of warrants
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- ISIN: INE0GYI01028
- Distribution numbers: 13038401 to 14537600
- Date of allotment: 15 May 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification pursuant to a preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed that these securities are listed and available for trading from June 10, 2026. All 14,99,200 shares are subject to a lock-in period and cannot be sold or transferred until January 15, 2027.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** 15 May 2026
- **Trading Effective From:** 10 June 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry:** 15 January 2027

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. The addition of 14,99,200 shares expands the float of Hemant Surgical Industries Limited, but the entire new tranche is under lock-in until January 15, 2027, meaning no immediate selling pressure from these shares. The preferential allotment at Rs. 197/- per share via warrant conversion is a company-level capital-raising event with no broader market or regulatory consequences.
