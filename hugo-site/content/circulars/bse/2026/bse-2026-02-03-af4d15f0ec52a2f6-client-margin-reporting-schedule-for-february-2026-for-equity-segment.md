## Summary

BSE (Indian Clearing Corporation Limited) has issued the client margin reporting schedule for the Equity segment for February 2026. This circular (Notice No 20260203-23) provides trading members with specific deadlines for margin reporting and corresponding penalty dates for trades executed during February 2026.

## Key Points

- Schedule covers equity trades from February 1, 2026 to February 27, 2026
- Margin reporting deadline is typically 5 business days after trade date
- Penalty date follows 1 business day after margin reporting deadline
- Extended timelines account for weekends and market holidays
- Applicable to all trading members in the Equity segment

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine monthly schedule for margin reporting compliance under existing Settlement/RMS framework.

## Compliance Requirements

**Trading Members Must:**
- Submit client margin reports by the specified "Last Date for Margin Reporting" for each trade date
- Ensure timely compliance to avoid penalties that become applicable from the "Penalty Date"
- Track reporting deadlines for all equity segment trades executed in February 2026

## Important Dates

**Sample Key Deadlines:**
- Trade Date: February 1, 2026 → Reporting Deadline: February 6, 2026 → Penalty Date: February 9, 2026
- Trade Date: February 10, 2026 → Reporting Deadline: February 17, 2026 → Penalty Date: February 18, 2026
- Trade Date: February 20, 2026 → Reporting Deadline: February 27, 2026 → Penalty Date: March 2, 2026
- Trade Date: February 27, 2026 → Reporting Deadline: March 9, 2026 → Penalty Date: March 10, 2026

**Note:** Complete schedule includes all trading days in February 2026 with corresponding deadlines extending into early March 2026.

## Impact Assessment

**Operational Impact:** Medium - Affects daily compliance operations for all trading members in the equity segment. Requires proper internal tracking systems to meet reporting deadlines.

**Market Impact:** Low - Routine operational schedule with no impact on trading activities or market structure.

**Compliance Impact:** Medium - Non-compliance with reporting deadlines results in penalties. Trading members must ensure robust margin reporting processes are in place for the specified dates.
