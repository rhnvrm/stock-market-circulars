## Summary

BSE announces the addition of Modern Diagnostic & Research Centre Limited (Exchange ticker: 544673) to the BSE SME IPO Index effective at the open of Thursday, January 08, 2026. The company is being listed on BSE on Wednesday, January 07, 2026.

## Key Points

- Modern Diagnostic & Research Centre Limited (544673) listed on BSE on January 07, 2026
- Stock will be added to BSE SME IPO Index on January 08, 2026
- Addition effective at market open on January 08, 2026
- Corporate action included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- Reference made to Notice No. 20260106-16

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index constituency update following a new SME listing.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index tracking funds and ETFs may need to rebalance portfolios to reflect the new index composition.

## Important Dates

- **January 07, 2026**: Modern Diagnostic & Research Centre Limited listing date on BSE
- **January 08, 2026**: Effective date for addition to BSE SME IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine index addition affecting only the BSE SME IPO Index.

**Operational Impact**: Index funds tracking the BSE SME IPO Index will need to include this stock in their portfolios. The addition has been communicated in advance to allow for proper rebalancing.

**Investor Impact**: Passive investors tracking the BSE SME IPO Index will gain exposure to Modern Diagnostic & Research Centre Limited through their index holdings.
