## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that units of PropShare Celestia (the third scheme of Property Share Investment Trust) will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange effective Friday, April 24, 2026, under the 'IF' Group of Securities. The scrip has been allotted code 544752 and ISIN INE19RO25039.

## Key Points

- 2,330 units of PropShare Celestia will be listed with effect from April 24, 2026
- Issue price is Rs. 10,50,000 per unit with a market lot of 1 unit
- Trading lot must represent a value of not less than Rs. 10,00,000
- Scrip Code: 544752; ISIN: INE19RO25039; Abbreviated Name: PSCELESTIA
- Units allotted on April 21, 2026 in the public issue
- Financial year end: March 31
- Trading is permitted in dematerialized form only, as per SEBI (Infrastructure Investment Trusts) Regulations, 2014
- Registrar to the issue: KFin Technologies Limited, Hyderabad (SEBI Reg. No. INR000000221)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The listing is governed by SEBI (Infrastructure Investment Trusts) Regulations, 2014, which mandates that securities of the trust be traded exclusively in dematerialized form.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all trades in PSCELESTIA are executed in dematerialized form only
- Units subject to lock-in (detailed in Annexure I) are not transferable until the specified dates and will not constitute good delivery in the market during the lock-in period
- The trust has executed necessary corporate actions with the depository to mark lock-in periods for demat units
- The scrip will participate in the Special Pre-open Session (SPOS) from April 24, 2026; members should refer to Exchange notice no. 20120216-29 dated February 16, 2012 for SPOS details

## Important Dates

- **April 21, 2026**: Date of allotment in the public issue
- **April 24, 2026**: Effective date of listing and commencement of trading on BSE
- **April 24, 2026**: Inclusion in Special Pre-open Session (SPOS)

## Impact Assessment

The listing provides market access to investors in PropShare Celestia, a Real Estate Investment Trust scheme. With a high per-unit issue price of Rs. 10,50,000 and minimum trading lot value of Rs. 10,00,000, this product is targeted at institutional and high-net-worth investors. Market impact is limited to REIT segment participants. Trading members should update their systems with the new scrip details and ensure compliance with demat-only trading and lock-in restrictions before the April 24 listing date.
