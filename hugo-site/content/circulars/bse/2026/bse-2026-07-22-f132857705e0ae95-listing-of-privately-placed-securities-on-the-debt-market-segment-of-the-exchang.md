## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that new Commercial Papers (CPs), issued on a private placement basis by five entities, have been listed and admitted to trading on the BSE Debt Segment effective 22 July 2026. The notice provides ISIN, credit rating, face value, issue price, interest rate, and redemption details for each instrument.

## Key Points

- Securities will trade only in dematerialised form under the respective ISINs.
- Trading will occur in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof.
- Tick size for these securities is 1 paise.
- Five fresh Commercial Paper listings covered in this notice:
  - **J.K. Cement Ltd** (Scrip Code 732124, ISIN INE823G14BI8): Qty 2000, Face/Paidup Value Rs. 5,00,000, Issue Price Rs. 4,94,059, Rate 6.65% p.a., Allotment 21/07/2026, Redemption 25/09/2026, Rating CARE A1+, IPA IDBI Bank Limited.
  - **Ashoka Buildcon Limited** (Scrip Code 732127, ISIN INE442H14550): Qty 2000, Face/Paidup Value Rs. 5,00,000, Issue Price Rs. 4,91,278, Rate 7.20% p.a., Allotment 21/07/2026, Redemption 19/10/2026, Rating ACUITE A1+, IPA HDFC Bank Limited.
  - **Julius Baer Capital (India) Private Limited** (Scrip Code 732125, ISIN INE824H14UT1): Qty 8000, Face/Paidup Value Rs. 5,00,000, Issue Price Rs. 4,90,474, Rate 7.79% p.a., Allotment 22/07/2026, Redemption 21/10/2026, Rating CRISIL A1+/ICRA A1+, IPA HDFC Bank Limited.
  - **Muthoot Fincorp Limited** (Scrip Code 732126, ISIN INE549K14CT6): Qty 2000, Face/Paidup Value Rs. 5,00,000, Issue Price Rs. 4,79,874, Rate 8.60% p.a., Allotment 21/07/2026, Redemption 15/01/2027, Rating CRISIL A1+, IPA IndusInd Bank Limited.
  - **Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited** (Scrip Code 732128, ISIN INE831R14GG1): Qty 7000, Face/Paidup Value Rs. 5,00,000, Issue Price Rs. 4,91,516.50, Rate 7% p.a., Allotment 21/07/2026, Redemption 19/10/2026, Rating CRISIL A1+/ICRA A1+, IPA HDFC Bank Limited (source content truncated for this entry).

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing notice under BSE's standard process for admitting privately placed debt securities to trading on the Debt Segment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must ensure trades in these instruments are executed in dematerialised form, in denominations of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof, with a tick size of 1 paise. Queries can be directed to the BSE Debt Department at the listed phone numbers.

## Important Dates

- Effective listing/trading date: 22 July 2026
- J.K. Cement CP: Allotment 21/07/2026, Redemption 25/09/2026
- Ashoka Buildcon CP: Allotment 21/07/2026, Redemption 19/10/2026
- Julius Baer Capital CP: Allotment 22/07/2026, Redemption 21/10/2026
- Muthoot Fincorp CP: Allotment 21/07/2026, Redemption 15/01/2027
- Aditya Birla Housing Finance CP: Allotment 21/07/2026, Redemption 19/10/2026

## Impact Assessment

This is an administrative/operational notice for the debt market segment with no direct impact on equity share trading or pricing of the listed companies. It is primarily relevant to debt market participants, institutional investors, and trading members dealing in commercial paper instruments; impact on broader market sentiment or the underlying companies' equity is negligible.
