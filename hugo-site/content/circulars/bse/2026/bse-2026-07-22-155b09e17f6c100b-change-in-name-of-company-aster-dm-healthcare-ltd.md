## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Aster DM Healthcare Ltd (Scrip Code 540975) has changed its corporate name to Quality Care Limited. The Scrip ID on the BOLT Plus System will remain unchanged as ASTERDM, and the abbreviated name will also remain ASTERDM. The change takes effect from July 28, 2026.

## Key Points

- Existing Name: Aster DM Healthcare Ltd
- New Name: Quality Care Limited
- Scrip Code: 540975 (unchanged)
- Scrip ID for BOLT Plus System: ASTERDM (no change)
- Abbreviated name on BOLT Plus System: ASTERDM (no change)
- Attachment: Certificate of Incorporation (COI_ASTER) confirming the name change

## Regulatory Changes

This is a corporate name change update communicated by BSE's Listing Operations Department. No change is being made to the Scrip ID or trading symbol; only the legal/registered name of the company is updated in Exchange records.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should update their internal records and systems to reflect the new company name, Quality Care Limited, while continuing to use the existing Scrip ID and Scrip Code for trading purposes.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 22, 2026
- Effective Date of Name Change: July 28, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact on trading and market operations is minimal since the Scrip ID, Scrip Code, and abbreviated trading symbol remain unchanged. This is primarily an administrative/compliance update requiring trading members to note the new corporate name for record-keeping and communication purposes.
