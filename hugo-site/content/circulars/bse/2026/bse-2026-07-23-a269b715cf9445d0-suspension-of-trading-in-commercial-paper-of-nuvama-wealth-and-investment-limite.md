## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited has fixed a record date for the redemption of its commercial paper. Consequently, trading members are advised not to deal in this commercial paper instrument from the specified ex-date.

## Key Points

- Instrument: Commercial Paper of Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited (Scrip ID: NWIL-27-7-26-CP)
- Scrip Code: 730980
- ISIN Number: INE523L14AX5
- Settlement Number: 679/2026-2027
- Trading members are advised not to deal in this commercial paper from the ex-date
- Notice issued by BSE's Listing Compliance and Operations Department under the Corporate Actions category, Debt segment

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a standard trading advisory tied to a corporate action (redemption) rather than a regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of the Exchange must refrain from dealing in the specified commercial paper (ISIN INE523L14AX5) of Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited from the ex-date onward, in line with the record date fixed for redemption.

## Important Dates

- Book Closure/Record Date: 24 July 2026
- Ex Date: 24 July 2026
- Settlement Number: 679/2026-2027

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to debt market participants holding or trading this specific commercial paper instrument, which is being redeemed. There is no direct impact on Nuvama's listed equity shares or broader market operations. This is a routine operational notice ensuring an orderly redemption process for the commercial paper.
