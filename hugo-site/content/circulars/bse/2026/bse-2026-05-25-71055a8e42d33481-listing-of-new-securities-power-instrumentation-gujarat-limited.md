## Summary

BSE has listed 1,73,530 new equity shares of Power & Instrumentation (Gujarat) Limited (Scrip Code: 543912, ISIN: INE557Z01018) on the Exchange. These shares were issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to the conversion of warrants, at a face value of Rs. 10/- each and a premium of Rs. 73.75/- per share (issue price Rs. 83.75/-). Trading in these securities is permitted with effect from Tuesday, May 26, 2026.

## Key Points

- 1,73,530 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 73.75/- (total issue price Rs. 83.75/-) to non-promoters on a preferential basis
- Shares issued pursuant to conversion of warrants
- Distinctive numbers: 19312901 to 19486430
- Date of allotment: March 3, 2026
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Trading permitted from May 26, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice informing trading members of the admission of new securities to trading on BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities and may trade them from May 26, 2026, subject to the lock-in restrictions applicable to these shares.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** March 3, 2026
- **Trading Commencement:** May 26, 2026 (Tuesday)
- **Lock-in Expiry:** December 1, 2026 (for all 1,73,530 shares, Dist. Nos. 19312901–19486430)

## Impact Assessment

The listing involves a relatively small number of shares (1,73,530) and the entire allotment is subject to lock-in until December 1, 2026, meaning no additional free-float enters the market immediately. The impact on the company's share price and liquidity is expected to be minimal in the short term. Post lock-in expiry, the shares may add marginally to the tradeable float.
