## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260721-1 dated 21 July 2026 informs Trading Members and Custodian Members of the schedule of activities for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Buyback) of shares of ORBIT EXPORTS LTD. The buyback window is scheduled from Tuesday, 21st July 2026 to Monday, 27th July 2026 on the Exchange.

## Key Points

- Security: ORBIT EXPORTS LTD (Symbol: ORBTEXP, ISIN: INE231G01010, Scrip Code: 512626)
- Offer size: Up to 11,04,000 equity shares
- Market lot: 1 share
- Face value: Rs. 10.00 per share
- Offer price: Rs. 250.00 per share
- Opening date: Tuesday, 21st July 2026
- Closing date: Monday, 27th July 2026
- Bidding categories include FII, FI, Mutual Fund, Insurance Companies, Other QIBs, Other NIB, Company, and Individual investors
- Access to iBBS, RTRMS, and Extranet platforms provided via both Internet and Leased Line URLs

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this notice operationalizes the previously approved buyback under existing Exchange Notices no. 20150930-1 (dated 30 September 2015) and 20170203-29 (dated 3 February 2017) governing Offer to Buy (OTB) procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must ensure the UCC of investors is registered and/or updated with correct PAN number and category in the Exchange UCC database.
- Trading Members can bid only for investors holding shares in demat form.
- Members and Custodians should note that if no securities are accepted, no End of Issue files will be generated in Extranet.
- Members and Custodians must use designated iBBS/RTRMS/Extranet platforms for login, bidding, and file access, contacting Trading Operations for new logins or password resets.

## Important Dates

- Bid Offering Period: 21st July 2026 to 27th July 2026, 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.
- Bid Modifications/Cancellation: 21st July 2026 to 27th July 2026, 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.
- Give-up/Take-up: 21st July 2026 to 24th July 2026, 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.
- Give-up (last day of offer): 27th July 2026, 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.
- Take-up (last day of offer): 27th July 2026, 9:15 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.

## Impact Assessment

This is a procedural notice enabling Trading and Custodian Members to participate in the buyback bidding process for Orbit Exports Ltd shares via the Acquisition Window mechanism. It has direct relevance to shareholders considering tendering shares in the buyback at Rs. 250 per share and to market intermediaries facilitating the transaction, but does not represent a broader market or regulatory policy change.
