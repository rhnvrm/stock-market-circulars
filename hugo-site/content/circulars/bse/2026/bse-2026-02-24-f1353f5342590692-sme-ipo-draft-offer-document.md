## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document of Sham Foam Limited, filed on February 23, 2026, on its SME platform. The document is available for public review for a minimum of 21 days as mandated under Schedule VI of the ICDR (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Sham Foam Limited on February 23, 2026
- Document hosted on BSE SME website for a minimum of 21 days
- Public invited to submit comments on the draft document
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This notice is a standard procedural disclosure under Schedule VI of the SEBI ICDR Regulations, which requires draft offer documents of SME IPOs to be made publicly available for comment prior to the issue opening.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public and market participants may review the Draft Offer Document at: https://www.bsesme.com/download/314291/SME_IPO%20InPrinciple/ShamFoamDP_20260223212947.pdf
- Comments, if any, must be submitted within 21 days from February 24, 2026 (deadline: approximately March 17, 2026)
- Comments to be sent via email to: equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Filing Date:** February 23, 2026
- **Notice Date:** February 24, 2026
- **Public Comment Deadline:** On or before March 17, 2026 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine pre-IPO disclosure with limited immediate market impact. It signals the upcoming SME IPO of Sham Foam Limited on BSE's SME platform. Investors and analysts interested in the SME segment may review the document and provide comments. No impact on existing listed securities or broader market operations.
