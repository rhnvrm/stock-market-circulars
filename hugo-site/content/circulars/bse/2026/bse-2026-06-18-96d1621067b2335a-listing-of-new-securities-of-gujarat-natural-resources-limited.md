## Summary

BSE has listed 2,50,00,000 new equity shares of Gujarat Natural Resources Limited (Scrip Code: 513536, ISIN: INE207H01018) issued to Non-Promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to the conversion of warrants. The shares are permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Friday, June 19, 2026.

## Key Points

- 2,50,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 11.70/- (issue price Rs. 21.70/-) to Non-Promoters on a preferential basis
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 128402633 to 153402632
- Date of allotment: November 1, 2025
- ISIN: INE207H01018
- All 2,50,00,000 shares are subject to lock-in until December 31, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for securities already allotted under a board/shareholder-approved preferential issue.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed that the new securities are listed and permitted to trade from June 19, 2026. The lock-in restriction on all 2,50,00,000 shares (dist. nos. 128402633 to 153402632) must be observed until December 31, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** November 1, 2025
- **Listing / Trading Effective Date:** June 19, 2026 (Friday)
- **Lock-in Expiry:** December 31, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 2.5 crore shares to the tradeable float of Gujarat Natural Resources Limited, representing equity dilution for existing shareholders. However, since these shares are fully locked-in until December 31, 2026, there is no immediate increase in freely tradeable supply. Market impact is expected to be minimal. This is a routine preferential allotment pursuant to warrant conversion and does not signal any change in business operations or regulatory status.
