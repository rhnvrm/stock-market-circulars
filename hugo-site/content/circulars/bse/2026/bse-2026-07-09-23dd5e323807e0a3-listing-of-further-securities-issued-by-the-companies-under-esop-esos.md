## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that further equity shares issued by 11 listed companies under their Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) / Employee Stock Option Schemes (ESOS) have been approved for listing and trading on the Exchange with effect from Friday, July 10, 2026.

## Key Points

- New ESOP/ESOS shares of the following companies are listed effective July 10, 2026:
  - Aarti Pharmalabs Limited (ISIN INE0LRU01027, Scrip 543748) – 22,062 shares, face value ₹5
  - Ather Energy Limited (ISIN INE0LEZ01016, Scrip 544397) – 189,032 shares, face value ₹1
  - Axis Bank Limited (ISIN INE238A01034, Scrip 532215) – 137,549 shares, face value ₹2
  - Federal Bank Limited (ISIN INE171A01029, Scrip 500469) – 474,994 shares, face value ₹2
  - Global Health Limited (ISIN INE474Q01031, Scrip 543654) – 4,000 shares, face value ₹2
  - ICICI Prudential Life Insurance Company Limited (ISIN INE726G01019, Scrip 540133) – 11,360 shares, face value ₹10
  - Kajaria Ceramics Limited (ISIN INE217B01036, Scrip 500233) – 20,760 shares, face value ₹1
  - Meesho Limited (ISIN INE0VDM01015, Scrip 544632) – 13,450,895 shares, face value ₹1
  - Niva Bupa Health Insurance Company Limited (ISIN INE995S01015, Scrip 544286) – 1,476,432 shares, face value ₹10
  - SRG Housing Finance Limited (ISIN INE559N01010, Scrip 534680) – 1,350 shares, face value ₹10
  - Yes Bank Limited (ISIN INE528G01035, Scrip 532648) – 658,070 shares, face value ₹2
- None of the newly listed shares carry a lock-in condition (marked "NA").
- The notice specifies the distinctive number ranges for the new shares for each company.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine listing approval, not a regulatory or policy change.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific action required from trading members or investors beyond noting that these additional shares become part of the tradable equity from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 9, 2026
- Effective Listing/Trading Date: Friday, July 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, low-impact administrative listing of incremental shares arising from employee stock option exercises across 11 companies. The additional share quantities are small relative to each company's total outstanding equity (with the exception of Meesho's relatively larger allotment), so no material impact on stock price, liquidity, or trading behavior is expected. The notice serves primarily to inform trading members that these new shares are now eligible for trading.
