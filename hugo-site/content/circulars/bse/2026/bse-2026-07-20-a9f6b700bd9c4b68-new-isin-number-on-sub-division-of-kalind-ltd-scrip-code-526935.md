## Summary

BSE has issued a follow-up notice to Exchange Notice No. 20260709-28 dated July 09, 2026, informing trading members of the new ISIN number assigned to KALIND LTD (Scrip Code: 526935) following the sub-division of its equity shares from a face value of Rs.10/- to Rs.2/- per share.

## Key Points

- The new ISIN for KALIND LTD's sub-divided equity shares is INE377D01026.
- The sub-division changes the face value of equity shares from Rs.10/- each to Rs.2/- each.
- This notice supersedes/continues from the original corporate action notice (20260709-28) dated July 09, 2026.
- The new ISIN will be effective for trades done on and from the ex-date.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is an operational/administrative notice implementing a previously approved corporate action (stock split) by assigning the corresponding new ISIN.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must update their systems to recognize and trade under the new ISIN (INE377D01026) for KALIND LTD shares from the effective date, ensuring order routing and settlement reflect the new face value and ISIN.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 20, 2026
- Ex-Date (effective date for new ISIN): July 24, 2026
- Reference DR Number: 679/2026-2027

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine but operationally important update for trading members and depository participants handling KALIND LTD shares. Failure to update systems with the new ISIN could result in trade or settlement errors post ex-date. Shareholders will see their holdings reflected under the new ISIN with adjusted quantity and face value post sub-division, with no change in overall investment value.
