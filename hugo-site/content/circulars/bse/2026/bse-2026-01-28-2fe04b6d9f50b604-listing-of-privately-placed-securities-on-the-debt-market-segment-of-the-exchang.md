## Summary

BSE has listed commercial papers issued by five companies on private placement basis on the Debt Market Segment effective January 28, 2026. The securities include commercial papers from Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited, Time Technoplast Limited, 360 ONE Prime Limited, Bajaj Finance Limited, and Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited with varying redemption dates and interest rates.

## Key Points

- Total of 5 commercial paper issuances listed on BSE Debt Segment
- All securities to be traded in dematerialized form only
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs.5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for securities is 1 paise
- Market lot size is 1 for all listed securities
- Interest rates range from 7.20% to 8.60% per annum
