## Summary

BSE has activated the Call Money Module on its IBBS (Internet Based Book Building Software) platform for the third and final call money notice for partly paid-up equity shares of Eco Hotels and Resort Limited (symbol: ECOCM3). The call money issue opens on June 16, 2026, and closes on June 30, 2026, with a call money amount of Rs. 3.80 per partly paid-up equity share.

## Key Points

- Eco Hotels and Resort Limited has requested BSE to activate the Call Money Module of the IBBS platform for the third and final call on its partly paid-up equity shares.
- The activation is in accordance with Exchange Notice No. 20210104-37 dated January 4, 2021.
- Security symbol for the call money notice is **ECOCM3** with a market lot of 1.
- A total of **1,28,76,808 partly paid-up equity shares** are subject to this call.
- Call money amount payable is **Rs. 3.80 per partly paid-up equity share**.
- The IBBS Call Money Module is available to SCSBs and trading members with 3-in-1 type accounts.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. The process follows the existing framework established under SEBI circular dated December 8, 2020, and BSE Exchange Notice No. 20210104-37 dated January 4, 2021, governing the Call Money Module on the IBBS platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members** and **Self-Certified Syndicate Banks (SCSBs)** must submit call money notice details collected during the issue period via the IBBS platform.
- Only SCSBs and trading members with 3-in-1 type accounts are eligible to use the Call Money Module.
- For regulatory/business rule queries: contact Listing Sales at listing.sales@bseindia.com or 022-2272 8846/5063/8179.
- For bidding-related queries: contact IBBS Operations at ibbs.ops@bseindia.com or 022-2272 8376/8436/8226.

## Important Dates

- **Issue Opening Date:** Tuesday, June 16, 2026
- **Issue Closing Date:** Tuesday, June 30, 2026
- **Bidding Period:** 10:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. (daily during issue period)
- **Issue Period:** 15 days

## Impact Assessment

This notice has a limited and targeted impact, primarily affecting existing shareholders of Eco Hotels and Resort Limited who hold partly paid-up equity shares and are required to pay the third and final call money of Rs. 3.80 per share. Trading members and SCSBs with 3-in-1 accounts must be prepared to submit call money details via IBBS during the 15-day window. The registrar, Bigshare Services Private Limited, manages investor queries. No broader market impact is expected.
