## Summary

BSE has notified trading members and custodians of an open offer under the SEBI acquisition-through-stock-exchange mechanism for Rekvina Laboratories Limited. Three acquirers — Surbhit Mukesh Shah, Amit Mukesh Shah, and Dhruvalkumar Patel — seek to acquire up to 28,90,100 equity shares (26% of expanded share capital) at Rs. 10.00 per share, payable in cash, pursuant to SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 2011.

## Key Points

- **Acquirers:** Surbhit Mukesh Shah (Acquirer-1), Amit Mukesh Shah (Acquirer-2), and Dhruvalkumar Patel (Acquirer-3)
- **Target Company:** Rekvina Laboratories Limited
- **Offer Price:** Rs. 10.00 per equity share (face value Rs. 5.00 per share)
- **Shares Offered For:** Up to 28,90,100 fully paid-up equity shares
- **Percentage of Expanded Share Capital:** 26.00%
- **Offer Period:** Wednesday, July 1, 2026 to Tuesday, July 14, 2026
- **Regulatory Basis:** SEBI (SAST) Regulations, 2011 — Regulations 3(2) and 4
- A Letter of Offer is attached to this notice for perusal

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This offer operates under the existing SEBI mechanism for acquisition of shares through stock exchanges pursuant to Tender-Offers under Takeover, as established by:
- SEBI circular CIR/CFD/POLICYCELL/1/2015 dated April 13, 2015
- SEBI circular CFD/DCR2/CIR/P/2016/131 dated December 9, 2016
- BSE revised guidelines vide notices dated February 10, 2017; April 24, 2019; May 28, 2020; November 2, 2020; and August 25, 2021

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and custodians must follow the revised guidelines under SEBI circular CFD/DCR2/CIR/P/2016/131 (Dec 9, 2016) and BSE notice 20170210-16 (Feb 10, 2017) and 20190424-35 (April 24, 2019)
- Participants should refer to the BSE website (www.bseindia.com) for full details of this Offer to Buy
- Public shareholders of Rekvina Laboratories Limited who wish to tender shares must do so within the offer window

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Notice Date | June 30, 2026 |
| Offer Opens | Wednesday, July 1, 2026 |
| Offer Closes | Tuesday, July 14, 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

This open offer directly impacts public shareholders of Rekvina Laboratories Limited, who must evaluate whether to tender their shares at Rs. 10.00 per equity share (a premium or discount relative to the prevailing market price warrants assessment). The offer targets 26% of expanded share capital, which is a significant stake and consistent with the mandatory open offer threshold under SEBI (SAST) Regulations. Trading members and custodians handling client positions in this stock must facilitate client participation within the offer window. Market participants can obtain the Letter of Offer from the BSE website for full terms and conditions.
