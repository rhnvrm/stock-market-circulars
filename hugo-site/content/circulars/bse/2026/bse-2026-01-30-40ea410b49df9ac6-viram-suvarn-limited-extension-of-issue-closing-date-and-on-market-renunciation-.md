## Summary

Viram Suvarn Limited (Script Code: 540252) has extended its Rights Issue closing date to provide shareholders additional time to exercise their rights. The Board of Directors, in a meeting held on January 29, 2026, approved extending the issue closing date by 14 days from February 06, 2026 to February 20, 2026. Correspondingly, the last date for on-market renunciation of rights entitlements has been extended from January 30, 2026 to February 13, 2026.

## Key Points

- Rights Issue opening date remains unchanged at January 23, 2026
- Issue closing date extended from February 06, 2026 to February 20, 2026
- On-market renunciation deadline extended from January 30, 2026 to February 13, 2026
- Extension approved by Board of Directors on January 29, 2026
- Board meeting commenced at 6:00 PM and concluded at 6:30 PM
- ISIN for dematerialized Rights Entitlement: INE607V20010
- Company formerly known as Veeram Securities Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a company-specific procedural extension of an ongoing rights issue timeline.

## Compliance Requirements

- Equity shareholders entitled to the Rights Issue should note the revised closing date of February 20, 2026
- Shareholders wishing to renounce rights on-market must do so by February 13, 2026
- Duly filled Common Application Forms (CAF) with payment must be submitted by February 20, 2026
- All other terms of the issue remain as per the Letter of Offer dated January 05, 2026

- **Last Date for Credit of Rights Entitlements:** January 22, 2026

- **Issue Closing Date:** February 20, 2026 (extended from February 06, 2026)
- **Finalization of Basis of Allotment:** February 24, 2026 (on or about)
- **Date of Allotment:** February 24, 2026 (on or about)
- **Date of Credit:** February 25, 2026 (on or about)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This is a company-specific corporate action affecting only Viram Suvarn Limited shareholders.

**Shareholder Impact:** Medium - The extension provides existing shareholders with an additional 14 days to decide on participating in the rights issue, potentially improving participation rates. Shareholders who intended to renounce their rights on-market gain an extra two weeks (from January 30 to February 13, 2026).

**Operational Impact:** The extension shifts all downstream dates including allotment (February 24, 2026) and listing (February 26, 2026) accordingly, providing more time for processing applications and ensuring adequate shareholder participation.
