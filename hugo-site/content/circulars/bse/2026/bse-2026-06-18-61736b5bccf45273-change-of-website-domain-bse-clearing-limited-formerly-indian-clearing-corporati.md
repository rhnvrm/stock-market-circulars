## Summary

BSE Clearing Limited (BSECL), formerly known as Indian Clearing Corporation Limited (ICCL), has changed its official website domain effective 18 June 2026. Stakeholders are advised to update all references to use the new domain within a 30-day transition window, after which the old domain will no longer be accessible.

## Key Points

- The company was renamed from Indian Clearing Corporation Limited (ICCL) to BSE Clearing Limited (BSECL).
- Official website domain has changed from `https://www.icclindia.com` to `https://www.bseclearing.com`.
- Both domains will remain operational in parallel for 30 days from 18 June 2026.
- After the transition period, only the new domain (`bseclearing.com`) will be accessible.
- Website content, services, and functionalities remain unchanged.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notification consequent to the company's renaming from ICCL to BSECL.

## Compliance Requirements

- Stakeholders must update bookmarks, automated processes, internal references, and any other dependencies currently pointing to `https://www.icclindia.com`.
- All records should be updated to reflect the new domain `https://www.bseclearing.com` within the 30-day transition period.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** 18 June 2026 (date of circular)
- **Parallel Run End Date:** 18 July 2026 (30 days from effective date; old domain access ceases)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is an administrative change affecting only how stakeholders access the BSECL website. No trading operations, settlement processes, or compliance obligations are altered. Stakeholders with automated systems or scripts referencing the old domain (`icclindia.com`) should prioritise updating those integrations before the 30-day window closes.
