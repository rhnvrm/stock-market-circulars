## Summary

BSE has notified that certain securities, as listed in the attached Annexure, will move into their respective higher stages of the Graded Surveillance Measure (GSM) framework with effect from July 07, 2026. This action follows prior Exchange notices dated February 23, 2017, March 03, 2017, July 20, 2018, November 29, 2019, and November 17, 2023 that established and refined the GSM framework. Securities under GSM Stages I to IV will face escalating trading restrictions including higher margins, reduced price bands, trade-for-trade settlement, restricted trading frequency, and additional surveillance deposits.

## Key Points

- Securities listed in the Annexure_GSM attachment will be moved to their respective higher GSM stages effective July 07, 2026.
- Stage I: 100% applicable margin and a price band of 5% or lower.
- Stage II: Trade-for-trade settlement with a 5% or lower price band, plus an Additional Surveillance Deposit (ASD) of 50% of trade value payable by buyers.
- Stage III: Trade-for-trade settlement with a 5% or lower price band, trading permitted only once a week (every Monday or the first trading day of the week), and 100% ASD payable by buyers.
- Stage IV: Same restrictions as Stage III, with the additional condition of no upward price movement permitted.
- All settlements for affected securities will be done strictly on a trade-to-trade basis with no netting off allowed.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced; this notice applies the existing GSM Framework (published February 23, 2017, and amended via subsequent notices) to move specific securities into higher surveillance stages.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure compliance with applicable margin requirements, price bands, and ASD collection for the affected securities from their buyers.
- Members must observe the trade-for-trade settlement basis with no netting off for securities in Stages II, III, and IV.
- Members should refer to the published FAQ on GSM or contact bse.surv@bseindia.com for clarifications.

## Important Dates

- Effective date of movement to higher GSM stages: July 07, 2026.
- Notice issued: July 06, 2026.

## Impact Assessment

This action significantly restricts trading liquidity and increases capital requirements for investors and traders dealing in the affected securities, as it imposes higher margins, ASD deposits, and in higher stages, limits trading to once a week with no upward price movement. Trading members must adjust their systems and client communications accordingly to ensure compliance with the stricter surveillance regime. The specific list of affected securities is contained in the Annexure attachment, which was not available in the text extracted from this notice.
