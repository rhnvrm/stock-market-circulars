## Summary

BSE has notified members that Baroda BNP Paribas ESG Best-in-Class Strategy Fund will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption, along with SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities on the BSE StAR MF Platform, effective March 06, 2026. This notice references a prior Notice No. 20260211-41.

## Key Points

- The fund reopens for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform from March 06, 2026.
- Both Regular and Direct plans are available across Growth and IDCW (Payout and Reinvestment) options.
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities are included.
- Six scheme variants are made available under this notice.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding fund availability on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of BSE dealing in mutual fund transactions on the StAR MF Platform should update their systems to reflect the availability of the six Baroda BNP Paribas ESG Best-in-Class Strategy Fund schemes for ongoing transactions from March 06, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **March 06, 2026**: Effective date for reopening of ongoing subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Impact Assessment

This notice has limited market impact and is relevant primarily to mutual fund distributors and investors using the BSE StAR MF Platform. The six scheme variants available are:
