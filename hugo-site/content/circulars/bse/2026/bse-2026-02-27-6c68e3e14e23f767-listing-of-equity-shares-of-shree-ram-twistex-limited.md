## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260227-14 informing trading members that the equity shares of Shree Ram Twistex Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange. The specific date of listing and further details of the securities will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Equity shares of Shree Ram Twistex Limited to be listed on BSE
- Scrip Code: 544716
- Symbol: SRTL
- ISIN: INE19GK01015
- Exact listing date to be announced via a separate notice
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing admission notice in the Equity segment under the Company Related category.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the upcoming listing of SRTL and await the subsequent notice from BSE confirming the exact date of listing and full security details before initiating any trades in this scrip.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** February 27, 2026
- **Listing Date:** To be announced via separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This circular signals an imminent listing of Shree Ram Twistex Limited (SRTL) on BSE. The impact is moderate and will be better assessed once the actual listing date and allotment details are released. Trading members should monitor for the follow-up notice confirming the listing date to prepare accordingly.
