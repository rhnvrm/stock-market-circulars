## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 1,94,323 new equity shares of Iykot Hitech Toolroom Ltd. (Scrip Code: 522245) are permitted for trading effective Thursday, February 26, 2026. These shares arise from the conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up equity shares of Rs. 5/- each, issued on a rights basis.

## Key Points

- 1,94,323 new equity shares of Iykot Hitech Toolroom Ltd. are admitted for trading on BSE.
- Shares are the result of conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up shares issued under a rights offering.
- Face value: Rs. 5/- per share.
- ISIN: INE079L01013.
- Distinctive numbers: 67565 (range 7383431 to 7450995) and 126758 (range 17331277 to 17458034).
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for newly admitted securities following completion of a rights issue conversion process.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are permitted and expected to trade in these newly listed securities from the effective date. No additional compliance action is required beyond standard trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** February 25, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** Thursday, February 26, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal broader market impact. The listing involves a small quantity of equity shares (1,94,323 shares) in Iykot Hitech Toolroom Ltd., a small-cap company. The new shares are pari-passu with existing shares, so no dilution of rights occurs for existing shareholders beyond what was already accounted for in the rights issue. Existing shareholders who held partly paid shares will now have fully tradeable securities.
