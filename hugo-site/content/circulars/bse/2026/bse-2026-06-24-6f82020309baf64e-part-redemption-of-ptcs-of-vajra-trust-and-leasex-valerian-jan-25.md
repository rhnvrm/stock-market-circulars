## Summary

Axis Trustee Services Limited has informed BSE of reduced face values arising from partial redemption of Pass-through Certificates (PTCs) across nine Vajra Trust series and LeaseX Valerian Jan 25. All affected PTCs will trade at their new reduced face values effective June 25, 2026.

## Key Points

- Nine Vajra Trust PTC series and one LeaseX Valerian Jan 25 PTC series undergo partial redemption
- Axis Trustee Services Limited is the trustee communicating the revised face values
- Effective date for trading at reduced face value: June 25, 2026
- Reference notification: DR-659/2026-2027
- No attachments accompany this notice

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a routine operational update reflecting scheduled partial redemptions under the existing PTC structure administered by Axis Trustee Services Limited.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members are required to note the revised reduced face values and ensure all trades in the affected PTCs from June 25, 2026 onwards reflect the updated face values as listed below.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 24, 2026
- **Effective Date for Reduced Face Value Trading:** June 25, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The partial redemptions reduce the outstanding face value of each PTC, which directly affects pricing and settlement for holders and traders of these instruments. The ten affected securities span the debt segment and carry coupons ranging from 9.25% to 14.68%. Reduced face values per PTC are as follows:
