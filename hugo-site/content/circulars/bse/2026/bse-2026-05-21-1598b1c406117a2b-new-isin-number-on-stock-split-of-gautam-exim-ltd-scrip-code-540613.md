## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260521-7 informs trading members of the new ISIN number assigned to GAUTAM EXIM LTD (Scrip Code: 540613) following a stock split (sub-division) of equity shares from a face value of Rs. 10/- per share to Rs. 5/- per share. This notice is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20260514-14 dated May 14, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: GAUTAM EXIM LTD (Scrip Code: 540613)
- New ISIN: INE721X01023
- Stock split ratio: Sub-division from Rs. 10/- face value to Rs. 5/- face value per equity share
- The new ISIN is effective for trades on and from the ex-date: May 22, 2026
- DR Reference: 636/2026-2027
- This notice continues from Exchange Notice No. 20260514-14 dated May 14, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

A new ISIN (INE721X01023) has been assigned to the sub-divided equity shares of GAUTAM EXIM LTD. The old ISIN associated with Rs. 10/- face value shares is replaced by this new identifier for all trades from the ex-date onwards.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their systems to use the new ISIN INE721X01023 for GAUTAM EXIM LTD effective May 22, 2026.
- All trades on or after the ex-date must reference the new ISIN for the sub-divided shares of Rs. 5/- each.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** May 21, 2026
- **Continuation of:** Exchange Notice No. 20260514-14 dated May 14, 2026
- **Ex-Date (effective date for new ISIN):** May 22, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard corporate action affecting all holders and traders of GAUTAM EXIM LTD shares. Investors holding the stock will see their share count double (proportional to the 2:1 split ratio implied by the face value halving from Rs. 10/- to Rs. 5/-) while the per-share price adjusts accordingly. Trading members and depository participants must ensure the new ISIN is correctly mapped in their systems before the ex-date to avoid settlement issues. The impact is limited to operational/administrative updates and does not alter the overall market capitalization of the company.
