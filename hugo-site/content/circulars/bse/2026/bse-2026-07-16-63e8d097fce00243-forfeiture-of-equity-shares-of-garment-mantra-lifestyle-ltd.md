## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Garment Mantra Lifestyle Ltd (Scrip Code: 539216) has forfeited 1,40,55,863 equity shares that were issued pursuant to a rights issue at Re. 1/- each. These shares, bearing distinctive numbers from 578210033 to 592265895, were partly paid-up and have been forfeited due to non-payment of allotment/call monies by the respective shareholders.

## Key Points

- Garment Mantra Lifestyle Ltd (Scrip Code: 539216) has forfeited 1,40,55,863 equity shares of Re. 1/- each.
- The forfeited shares were issued under a rights issue and were partly paid-up.
- Forfeiture occurred due to non-payment of allotment/call monies by shareholders.
- Distinctive numbers of the forfeited shares range from 578210033 to 592265895.
- The notice carries no attachments and was signed by Janardhan Wagle, Deputy Vice President, BSE Listing Operations.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced; this is a company-specific disclosure of share forfeiture under existing rights issue terms.

## Compliance Requirements

No action is required from trading members beyond being informed of the forfeiture for record-keeping and market awareness purposes. The company is responsible for updating its share capital records to reflect the forfeited shares.

## Important Dates

Notice issued and dated 16 July 2026 (Notice No. 20260716-16).

## Impact Assessment

The forfeiture reduces the number of partly-paid shares outstanding for Garment Mantra Lifestyle Ltd and may marginally affect the company's paid-up capital structure. The impact is limited to the company and affected shareholders whose shares were forfeited; there is no broader market-wide or trading impact expected from this notice.
