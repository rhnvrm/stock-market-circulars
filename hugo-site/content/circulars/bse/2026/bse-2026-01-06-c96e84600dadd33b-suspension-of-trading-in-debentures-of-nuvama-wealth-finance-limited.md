## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in specific non-convertible debentures of NUVAMA WEALTH FINANCE LIMITED effective January 9, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption purposes. Trading members are advised to cease dealings in the specified debenture series immediately from the no-dealings date.

## Key Points

- Debenture ISIN: INE918K07IX6 (Security Code: 974081)
- Debenture Series: NWFL-NIFTY 50 INDEX-27-1-26-PV
- Record Date: January 9, 2026
- No Dealings From: January 9, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures
- Notice Reference: DR-795/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notification for debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from January 9, 2026
- Members should update their trading systems to reflect the suspension
- Ensure client communications regarding the trading halt in this security

## Important Dates

- **January 6, 2026**: Circular issued
- **January 9, 2026**: Record date for redemption
- **January 9, 2026**: Trading suspension effective date

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders of this specific debenture series (INE918K07IX6). The trading suspension is temporary and aligned with the redemption process. Investors holding these debentures will receive redemption proceeds as per the terms of issuance. No broader market impact anticipated as this is a routine corporate action affecting a single debt instrument.
