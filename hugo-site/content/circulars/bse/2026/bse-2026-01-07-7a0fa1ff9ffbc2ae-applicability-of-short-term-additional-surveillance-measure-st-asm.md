## Summary

BSE has updated the list of securities under Short Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) framework effective January 08, 2026. Nine securities are newly shortlisted for ST-ASM, one security will move to higher stage ASM, and eleven securities are exiting the ST-ASM framework. The measure applies 5/15/30 day surveillance periods to monitor unusual price movements and excessive volatility.

## Key Points

- 9 securities newly shortlisted in Short Term 5/15/30 Days ASM Framework
- 1 security (Western Overseas Study Abroad Limited) moving to higher Stage ASM while continuing in ST-ASM
- 11 securities moving out of ST-ASM Framework
- Several securities exiting due to inclusion in other frameworks (LT-ASM, GSM)
- Framework includes both main board and SME scrips
- Effective date: January 08, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

**New Entries to ST-ASM (9 securities):**
1. Aryan Share and Stock Brokers Ltd (542176)
2. Flora Corporation Ltd (540267)
3. Godavari Drugs Ltd (530317)
4. Mirc Electronics Ltd (500279)
5. Neptune Logitek Ltd (544663) - SME
6. Orient Technologies Ltd (544235)
7. Prime Fresh Ltd (540404)
8. Shankara Building Products Ltd (540425)
9. Sofcom Systems Ltd (538923)

**Moving to Higher Stage ASM:**
- Western Overseas Study Abroad Limited (544636) - SME

**Exiting ST-ASM (11 securities):**
- Chartered Logistics Ltd - moving to LT-ASM
- Cubex Tubings Ltd
- Dr Lalchandani Labs Ltd - SME
- Hindustan Copper Ltd (2 codes: 513599, 113599)
- Landmarc Leisure Corporation Ltd
- MMTC Ltd (2 codes: 513377, 113377)
- Prodocs Solutions Ltd - SME
- Silver Touch Technologies Ltd - moving to LT-ASM
- SMC Global Securities Ltd

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure compliance with ST-ASM framework restrictions for listed securities
- Enhanced surveillance monitoring on price movements and volatility for securities under ST-ASM
- Adherence to position limits and margin requirements specific to ST-ASM securities
- Market participants should note that some securities are transitioning between different surveillance frameworks (ST-ASM, LT-ASM, GSM, ESM)

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** January 08, 2026
- Changes to ST-ASM applicability take effect from market opening on this date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:**
- Securities entering ST-ASM may experience reduced liquidity and increased margin requirements
- Higher surveillance scrutiny could lead to price stabilization for volatile securities
- Investors should be aware of additional compliance measures when trading these securities

**Operational Impact:**
- Trading restrictions apply to 9 new securities under enhanced surveillance
- Brokers need to update systems and risk management protocols for affected securities
- Securities moving between frameworks require recalibration of risk parameters
- SME securities (marked with @) require special attention as they have different trading characteristics
- T+0 scrips (marked with ~) under ST-ASM follow parent company framework rules
