## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in specific debentures of MUTHOOT MICROFIN LIMITED effective January 12, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the no-dealings date.

## Key Points

- Company: MUTHOOT MICROFIN LIMITED
- ISIN: INE046W07230
- Code: 974561
- Security: MML-11%-27-1-26-PVT (Non-Convertible Debentures)
- Record Date: January 12, 2026
- No Dealings From: January 12, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures and Payment of interest
- Notice Number: DR-796/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action notice for debenture redemption and interest payment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures of MUTHOOT MICROFIN LIMITED from January 12, 2026
- Trading members are required to take note of the suspension and inform their clients accordingly
- All trading activities in the specified debentures must cease from the no-dealings date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 1, 2026
- **Record Date**: January 12, 2026
- **No Dealings From**: January 12, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The suspension affects holders and potential traders of the specific non-convertible debentures (INE046W07230) of MUTHOOT MICROFIN LIMITED. Investors holding these debentures on the record date will be eligible for interest payment and redemption. The impact is limited to this particular debt security and does not affect the company's equity shares or other securities. Trading members must ensure compliance to avoid regulatory issues.
