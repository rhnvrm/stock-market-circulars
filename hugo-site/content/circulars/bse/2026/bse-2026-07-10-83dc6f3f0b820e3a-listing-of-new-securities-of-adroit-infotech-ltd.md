## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that they are permitted to trade in new securities of Adroit Infotech Ltd (Scrip Code: 532172) with effect from Monday, July 13, 2026. The securities comprise 4,51,312 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each, issued on a rights basis, arising from the conversion of partly paid shares into fully paid-up shares.

## Key Points

- Company: Adroit Infotech Ltd (Scrip Code: 532172)
- New securities: 4,51,312 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each
- Origin: Conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid-up shares issued on rights basis
- ISIN: INE737B01033
- Distinctive Numbers: 51077209 to 51528520
- These shares rank pari-passu with the existing equity shares of the company

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing notice under BSE Listing Operations following completion of the call money/conversion process for partly paid rights shares.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are permitted to trade in the new fully paid-up shares from the effective date. No specific action required beyond standard trading system updates to reflect the additional listed quantity under the existing ISIN.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 10, 2026
- Effective trading date: Monday, July 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is minimal and company-specific. The listing merely reflects the fully paid-up conversion of previously partly paid rights shares into the existing ISIN, adding to the tradable float of Adroit Infotech Ltd without altering the company's overall capital structure significantly.
