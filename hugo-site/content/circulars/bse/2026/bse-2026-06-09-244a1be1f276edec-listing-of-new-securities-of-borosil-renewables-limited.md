## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 94,338 new equity shares of Borosil Renewables Limited (Scrip Code: 502219, ISIN: INE666D01022) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Wednesday, June 10, 2026. These shares were issued to Non-Promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants at an issue price of Rs. 530/- per share (face value Rs. 1/- + premium Rs. 529/-).

## Key Points

- 94,338 equity shares of Rs. 1/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 529/- (total issue price Rs. 530/-)
- Allotment made to Non-Promoters on a preferential basis via conversion of warrants
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution Numbers: 145151126 to 145245463
- Date of Allotment: May 8, 2026
- All 94,338 shares are subject to lock-in until December 21, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for newly allotted shares under an existing preferential/warrant conversion arrangement.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to update their systems to reflect the new shares available for trading from June 10, 2026, subject to the lock-in restrictions applicable to the allotted shares.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** May 8, 2026
- **Trading Effective From:** June 10, 2026 (Wednesday)
- **Lock-in Expiry:** December 21, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds a minor 94,338 shares to the tradable float of Borosil Renewables Limited; however, all allotted shares are locked in until December 21, 2026, so there is no immediate impact on market liquidity or free float. The preferential allotment to Non-Promoters at Rs. 530/- per share via warrant conversion represents a modest capital raise with minimal dilution effect.
