## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in the Commercial Paper (ISIN: INE248U14RD6, Scrip Code: 729020) of 360 ONE PRIME LTD effective February 04, 2025. The suspension follows the company's decision to buyback the commercial paper instrument. Trading members are advised not to deal in this security from the effective date.

## Key Points

- Company: 360 ONE PRIME LTD
- Scrip Code: 360OPL-16-06-26-CP
- ISIN: INE248U14RD6
- Internal Code: 729020
- Action: Buyback of Commercial Paper
- Effective Date: February 04, 2025
- Reference: DR-813/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a corporate action notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified Commercial Paper (INE248U14RD6) from February 04, 2025
- All exchange members are required to take note of this suspension

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: February 03, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date**: February 04, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - affects only a single commercial paper instrument of one issuer.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - trading members need to update their systems to prevent trades in this specific ISIN. The buyback is a standard corporate action for debt instruments nearing maturity or being retired early.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to holders of this specific commercial paper who will participate in the buyback process as per the company's terms.
