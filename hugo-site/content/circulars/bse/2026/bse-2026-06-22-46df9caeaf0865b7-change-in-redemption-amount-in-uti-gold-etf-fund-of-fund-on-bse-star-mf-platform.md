## Summary

BSE has notified members of a revision in the minimum redemption amount for the UTI Gold ETF Fund of Fund on the BSE StAR MF Platform. UTI AMC communicated the change, which reduces the minimum redemption threshold from ₹25 to ₹1, effective June 23, 2026.

## Key Points

- The minimum redemption amount for UTI Gold ETF Fund of Fund is being reduced from ₹25 to ₹1.
- The change is applicable on the BSE StAR MF Platform.
- BSE has attached the Notice-cum-Addendum issued by UTI AMC for reference.
- The notice is classified under the Trading category for the Mutual Fund segment.

## Regulatory Changes

The minimum redemption amount parameter for UTI Gold ETF Fund of Fund on BSE StAR MF is revised as follows:

| Scheme Name | Existing Minimum Redemption Amount | Revised Minimum Redemption Amount |
|---|---|---|
| UTI Gold ETF Fund of Fund | ₹25 | ₹1 |

## Compliance Requirements

Members operating on the BSE StAR MF Platform should update their systems and processes to reflect the revised minimum redemption amount of ₹1 for UTI Gold ETF Fund of Fund with effect from June 23, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 22, 2026
- **Effective Date:** June 23, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a minor operational change that lowers the barrier for redemptions in the UTI Gold ETF Fund of Fund on BSE StAR MF. Reducing the minimum redemption amount from ₹25 to ₹1 increases flexibility for investors redeeming small amounts. The overall market impact is minimal, confined to mutual fund distributors and investors transacting through the BSE StAR MF platform for this specific scheme.
