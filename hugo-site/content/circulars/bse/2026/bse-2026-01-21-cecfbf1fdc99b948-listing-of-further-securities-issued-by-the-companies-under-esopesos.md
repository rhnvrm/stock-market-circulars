## Summary

BSE has listed 3,199 equity shares issued by a company under its Employee Stock Option Plan (ESOP) or Employee Stock Option Scheme (ESOS). The shares are divided into three tranches with varying lock-in conditions.

## Key Points

- Total shares listed: 3,199 equity shares
- Shares distributed across three tranches: 1,660, 947, and 592 shares respectively
- Share range: 20757847 to 20761045
- Two tranches subject to lock-in restrictions starting January 15, 2026
- First tranche (1,660 shares) has no lock-in restrictions

## Lock-in Details

**Tranche 1 (1,660 shares):**
- Range: 20757847 to 20759506
- Lock-in Status: No

**Tranche 2 (947 shares):**
- Range: 20759507 to 20760453
- Lock-in Status: Yes
- Lock-in Period: January 15, 2026 to February 2, 2026

**Tranche 3 (592 shares):**
- Range: 20760454 to 20761045
- Lock-in Status: Yes
- Lock-in Period: January 15, 2026 to February 2, 2027

## Important Dates

- **Lock-in Start Date:** January 15, 2026 (for applicable tranches)
- **Lock-in End Date (Tranche 2):** February 2, 2026
- **Lock-in End Date (Tranche 3):** February 2, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact due to small volume of shares and partial lock-in restrictions. The staggered lock-in periods ensure limited immediate trading activity for majority of the listed shares.
