## Summary

BSE has listed additional securities issued by seven companies under their Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and Employee Stock Option Schemes (ESOS). These securities will be admitted for trading on the exchange effective Wednesday, January 7, 2026. The listings include a total of 343,734 shares across the seven companies with no lock-in restrictions.

## Key Points

- Seven companies have issued new securities under ESOP/ESOS programs
- Total of 343,734 shares to be listed across all companies
- Trading commences on January 7, 2026
- No lock-in period applies to any of the listed securities
- Notice Number: 20260106-10
- Companies span various sectors including hospitality, aviation, technology, and manufacturing

**Market Impact:** Minimal. The listing of ESOP/ESOS shares is a routine corporate action with negligible impact on overall market liquidity or trading volumes. The total number of shares (343,734) represents a small fraction of the outstanding shares for these companies.
