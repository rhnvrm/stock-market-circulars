## Summary

BSE Limited has issued a notice informing trading members that the equity shares of Seemax Resources Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange's SME segment in due course. The date of listing and further security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company: Seemax Resources Limited
- Scrip Code: 544813
- Symbol: SEEMAX
- ISIN: INE1EVO01017
- Segment: SME
- Category: Company related, Department: DCS-Listing

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is an administrative listing notice under BSE's standard listing process for SME companies.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the scrip details (code, symbol, ISIN) for Seemax Resources Limited in preparation for trading once listing is confirmed. No specific action is required until the formal listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 6, 2026
- Date of listing: To be announced via a separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This notice has limited immediate market impact as it only confirms upcoming admission to dealings without specifying the listing date. Impact will increase once the actual listing date and trading details are announced, at which point investors and trading members can begin transacting in the scrip.
