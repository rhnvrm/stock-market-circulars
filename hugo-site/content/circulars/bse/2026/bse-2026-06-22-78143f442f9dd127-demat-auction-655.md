## Summary

BSE Limited, through Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd. (BSECL), invites offers from trading members for purchase of securities under Demat Auction 655, pertaining to Rolling Settlement No. DR-655/2026-27. The auction session is scheduled for June 22, 2026, with pay-in due on June 23, 2026.

## Key Points

- Auction offer entry window: 2:00 PM to 2:45 PM on June 22, 2026
- Auction matching: 2:45 PM to 3:00 PM
- Delivery statement and accepted auction offer reports: 3:00 PM to 3:30 PM
- Money statement and final reports: 3:35 PM to 5:30 PM
- Securities pay-in (auction): June 23, 2026 at 8:00 AM
- Offers accepted on trading terminals only; no corrections permitted after punching errors
- Trading members must check auction pay-in obligations and execute securities pay-in within depository cut-off timings

## Regulatory Changes

Following implementation of the Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment, settlement is processed through the Clearing Member of a Trading Member. The earlier restriction on trading members offering securities in auction for the same settlement in which they short-delivered has been removed.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and Custodians must verify their auction pay-in obligations for securities.
- Securities pay-in must be executed in depositories before the depository cut-off time on June 23, 2026.
- Trading members must exercise utmost care when entering securities codes and rates, as no corrections will be permitted after submission.
- Members should refer to the cutoff rate on the Auction Offer Entry Screen before submitting offers.
- Members should also check notices issued by respective departments regarding Corporate Actions (ex-rights, ex-bonus, ex-split, etc.) affecting auctioned securities.

## Important Dates

- **June 22, 2026**: Auction session (offer entry, matching, and report generation)
- **June 23, 2026 at 8:00 AM**: Auction pay-in of securities

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice impacting trading members with short-delivery obligations in Rolling Settlement DR-655/2026-27. The scope is limited to participants involved in this specific settlement cycle. No broad market or regulatory impact is anticipated.
