## Summary

BSE has issued a clarification on the amended Rule 8(1)(f) and 8(3)(f) of the Securities Contracts (Regulation) Rules, 1957 (SCRR), following a gazette amendment published by the Department of Economic Affairs, Ministry of Finance on May 19, 2025. The amendment inserts a new proviso clarifying that investments made by a broker member shall not be treated as "business" — and therefore not constitute a compliance violation — unless such investments involve client funds, client securities, or create a financial liability on the broker.

## Key Points

- A new proviso inserted in Rule 8(1)(f) and Rule 8(3)(f) of SCRR specifies that member investments are not construed as "business" unless they involve client funds/securities or create financial liability on the broker.
- Points 1 and 2 of the illustrative non-compliance list from Exchange Notice No. 20220107-45 (dated January 07, 2022) are retained but modified.
- Points 3 to 12 of the same illustrative list stand deleted entirely.
- FAQs addressing the revised illustrative list are provided in Annexure A.
- BSE members are advised to take note and ensure compliance.

## Regulatory Changes

The Department of Economic Affairs amended SCRR Rule 8 via Gazette of India notification dated May 19, 2025:

- **Rule 8(1)(f):** After the first proviso, a new proviso is inserted stating that member investments do not constitute "business" unless they involve client funds/securities or create a financial liability on the broker.
- **Rule 8(3)(f):** A similar proviso is inserted after clause (f) with identical wording.

Consequently, the illustrative list of activities previously treated as non-compliant (from Notice No. 20220107-45, January 07, 2022) has been revised:

- **Modified (still non-compliant):**
  1. Issuing Corporate Guarantees towards credit facilities availed by any entity, including group companies (subsidiaries, associates, etc.) of the Member.
  2. Deposits pledged with the bank for overdraft facilities availed by any entity, including group companies of the Member.
- **Deleted:** Points 3 through 12 of the original illustrative list are no longer applicable.

## Compliance Requirements

- All BSE member brokers must review the revised provisions of Rule 8(1)(f) and 8(3)(f) of SCRR.
- Members must ensure they do not engage in the two remaining non-compliant activities (corporate guarantees and pledged deposits for group entities).
- Members should refer to Annexure A (FAQs) for further guidance on the revised illustrative list.
- For clarifications, members may contact BSE's Department of Member Oversight at 022-69158540, 022-45720640, 022-45720440, or email Bse.inspection@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **May 19, 2025:** Gazette of India notification of SCRR Rule 8 amendment by Department of Economic Affairs.
- **June 23, 2026:** BSE Notice date providing clarification and revised illustrative list.
- **January 07, 2022:** Original Exchange Notice No. 20220107-45 (partially modified/deleted by this notice).

## Impact Assessment

This notice provides partial regulatory relief to broker members by narrowing the scope of what constitutes non-compliant "business" under SCRR Rule 8. The deletion of Points 3–12 from the earlier illustrative list removes several categories of previously prohibited activities, giving members greater flexibility in their investment activities. However, members must remain cautious about corporate guarantees and pledged deposits for group entities, which continue to be treated as violations. This clarification reduces compliance burden for brokers with investment holdings unrelated to client funds or liabilities.
