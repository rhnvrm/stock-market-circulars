## Summary

BSE has announced a Demat Auction for Settlement No. 832 dated March 5, 2026. This auction covers securities from 91 scrips across multiple companies where shares are required to be auctioned to fulfil short delivery or other settlement obligations in the equity segment.

## Key Points

- Settlement No. 832 auction involves 91 different scrip codes listed on BSE
- Significant quantities include: LEADING LEASING (51,001 shares), VINNY OVERSEAS (10,100 shares), RELIANCE INDUSTRIES (3,437 shares), APM INDUSTRIES (2,710 shares), BHARAT COKING COAL (4,189 shares), META INFOTECH (1,600 shares), and ECO HOTELS (1,000 shares)
- Securities span multiple sectors including banking, IT, pharma, infrastructure, FMCG, and metals
- Auction applies to both large-cap and small-cap/SME listed companies

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice under BSE's standard auction mechanism for handling settlement shortfalls in the equity segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants with short positions in the listed scrips must participate in the auction process
- Brokers and trading members must ensure their clients are informed of auction obligations for the affected securities
- Settlement of auction trades must be completed as per BSE's standard settlement timeline

## Important Dates

- Auction Date: March 5, 2026
- Settlement No.: 832

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a moderate operational impact on trading members and investors holding the 91 affected securities. The auction is a standard settlement mechanism to address short deliveries and does not signal any fundamental market disruption. Investors in high-quantity auction scrips such as LEADING LEASING (51,001 shares) and RELIANCE INDUSTRIES (3,437 shares) may see minor price impact due to forced auction buying. The circular is routine and does not have broad systemic implications.
