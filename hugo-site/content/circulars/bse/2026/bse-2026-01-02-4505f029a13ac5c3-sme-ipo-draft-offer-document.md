## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document for Craftech Paper Mills Limited's SME IPO, filed on December 31, 2025. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and is available on the stock exchange website for public review and comments for a period of 21 days.

## Key Points

- Draft offer document filed by Craftech Paper Mills Limited on December 31, 2025
- Document complies with Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on SME website for public review
- Public comments invited within 21 days from notice date
- Comments to be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard disclosure requirement for SME IPO filings.

## Compliance Requirements

- Interested parties may review the draft offer document on the BSE SME website
- Public comments, if any, must be submitted within 21 days from January 02, 2026 (deadline: January 23, 2026)
- Comments should be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **December 31, 2025**: Draft offer document filed
- **January 02, 2026**: Notice date and start of public comment period
- **January 23, 2026**: Deadline for public comments (21 days from notice)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains to a single SME company's IPO process. The disclosure is part of the standard regulatory process for SME listings on BSE. Market participants interested in the Craftech Paper Mills Limited IPO or SME segment may review the draft offer document and provide feedback during the comment period.
