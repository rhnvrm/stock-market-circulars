## Summary

BSE has announced the schedule for an Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for equity shares of SANMITRA COMMERCIAL LIMITED (Symbol: ZSANMCOM, ISIN: INE896J01014, Scrip Code: 512062). The offer is for up to 1,49,76,000 equity shares at an offer price of Rs. 15.00 per share with a face value of Rs. 10.00. The acquisition window will run from Tuesday, January 6, 2026 to Monday, January 19, 2026.

## Key Points

- **Offer Size**: Up to 1,49,76,000 equity shares
- **Offer Price**: Rs. 15.00 per share
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10.00
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Opening Date**: Tuesday, January 6, 2026
- **Closing Date**: Monday, January 19, 2026
- **Session Timings**: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM (standard), Take-up on last day extends to 4:00 PM
- Trading Members can bid for investors having shares in demat form only
- UCC of investors must be registered with correct PAN Number and Category

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notice for a company-specific takeover offer.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members and Custodian Members** must note the schedule of activities
- **UCC Registration**: Trading Members must ensure investor UCC is registered/updated with correct PAN Number and Category in Exchange UCC database
- **Demat Requirement**: Trading Members can only bid for investors having shares in demat form
- **Platform Access**: Members must use designated iBBS and RTRMS platforms (Internet or Leased Line URLs provided)
- **Bidding Categories**: Must categorize investors correctly (FII, FI, MF, IC, OTH, NOH, CO, IND)
- Members should refer to Exchange Notices 20150930-1 (September 30, 2015) and 20170203-29 (February 3, 2017) for operational instructions

## Important Dates

- **Bid Offering Period**: January 6, 2026 to January 19, 2026 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Give-up/Take-up Period**: January 6, 2026 to January 16, 2026 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Give-up (Last Day)**: January 19, 2026 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Take-up (Last Day)**: January 19, 2026 (9:15 AM - 4:00 PM)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a company-specific takeover offer affecting only shareholders of Sanmitra Commercial Limited.

**Operational Impact**: Medium for Trading Members and Custodians handling bids for this specific security. They must ensure proper access to iBBS and RTRMS platforms and comply with UCC registration requirements.

**Investor Category Impact**: The offer is open to multiple investor categories including FII, Financial Institutions, Mutual Funds, Insurance Companies, Other QIBs, Companies, and Individual investors.

**Platform Requirements**: 
- iBBS platform: https://ibbs.bseindia.com/ (Internet) or https://ibbsll.bseindia.com/ (Leased Line)
- RTRMS: https://rtrms.bseindia.com/ (Internet) or https://rtrmsll.bseindia.com/ (Leased Line)
- Extranet: https://member.bseindia.com/ (Internet) or https://memberll.bseindia.com/ (Leased Line)

**Contact Support**: Multiple contact points provided for iBBS login issues, password resets, and OTB module queries through Trading Operations and Listing Sales teams.
