## Summary

BSE has announced a trading suspension for partly paid equity shares of ECO HOTELS AND RESORTS LTD (Scrip Code 890225) effective from January 8, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date to determine shareholders eligible to receive the First Call notice for payment of Rs. 3.80 per equity share (Rs. 2.50 towards face value and Rs. 1.30 towards securities premium).

## Key Points

- Company: ECO HOTELS AND RESORTS LTD
- Scrip Code: 890225 (Partly Paid up Equity Shares)
- Record Date: January 8, 2026
- No dealings from: January 8, 2026 (Settlement No. DR-794/2025-2026)
- First Call Money amount: Rs. 3.80 per equity share
  - Face Value component: Rs. 2.50 per share
  - Securities Premium component: Rs. 1.30 per share
- Purpose: Ascertain shareholders of partly paid shares eligible to pay First Call Money

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action procedure for partly paid equity shares where the company collects the remaining amount through call notices.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in partly paid equity shares of ECO HOTELS AND RESORTS LTD from January 8, 2026
- Trading members are required to take note of this suspension
- Shareholders holding partly paid shares as of record date will receive First Call notice

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 5, 2026
- **Record Date**: January 8, 2026
- **No Dealings From**: January 8, 2026
- **Settlement Number**: DR-794/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders of partly paid equity shares of ECO HOTELS AND RESORTS LTD. Trading suspension prevents transactions in these specific shares starting from the record date.

**Shareholder Impact**: Shareholders holding partly paid shares as of January 8, 2026 will be required to pay the First Call Money of Rs. 3.80 per share when notified by the company.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must ensure compliance with the no-dealings directive to avoid regulatory issues. The suspension is temporary and relates only to the specific scrip code 890225.
