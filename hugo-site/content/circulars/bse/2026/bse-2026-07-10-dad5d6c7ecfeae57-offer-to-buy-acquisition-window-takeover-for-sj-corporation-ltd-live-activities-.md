## Summary

BSE has issued the live activities schedule for the Offer to Buy under the Acquisition Window (Takeover) mechanism for SJ Corporation Ltd (Security Symbol: SJCORP, ISIN: INE312B01027, Scrip Code: 504398). The offer covers up to 1,12,72,300 equity shares at a face value of Rs. 1.00 each, with an offer price of Rs. 12.00 per share. The offer window runs from Monday, 13th July 2026 to Friday, 24th July 2026, with Market Lot of 1 share.

## Key Points

- Offer Size: Up to 1,12,72,300 equity shares of SJ Corporation Ltd
- Offer Price: Rs. 12.00 per share
- Face Value: Rs. 1.00 per share
- Market Lot: 1
- Bid Offering Period: 13th July 2026 to 24th July 2026, 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.
- Bid Modifications/Cancellation: 13th July 2026 to 24th July 2026, 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.
- Give-up/Take-up: 13th July 2026 to 23rd July 2026, 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.
- Give-up (last day of offer): 24th July 2026, 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.
- Take-up (last day of offer): 24th July 2026, 9:15 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.
- Bidding categories include FII, FI, MF, IC, Other QIBs, Other NIB, Company, and Individual investors
- Access via iBBS (Internet/Leased Line), RTRMS, and Extranet platforms for members and custodians

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this notice operationalizes the existing OTB (Offer to Buy) framework per Exchange Notices no. 20150930-1 (September 30, 2015) and 20170203-29 (February 3, 2017) for the specific SJ Corporation Ltd takeover offer.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must ensure the UCC of investors is registered and/or updated with correct PAN number and category in the Exchange UCC database
- Trading Members can bid only for investors holding shares in demat form
- Members and Custodians should note that if no securities are accepted, no End of Issue files will be generated in Extranet
- Files on the Extranet for bid acceptance become available only after confirmation of acceptance is received from the Merchant Banker/RTA post issue closure

## Important Dates

- Opening Date: Monday, 13th July 2026
- Closing Date: Friday, 24th July 2026
- Give-up/Take-up cutoff (except last day): 23rd July 2026
- Final Give-up: 24th July 2026, until 3:30 p.m.
- Final Take-up: 24th July 2026, until 4:00 p.m.

## Impact Assessment

This is a procedural/operational circular directing Trading and Custodian Members on session timings and platform access for participating in the SJ Corporation Ltd takeover offer. It does not alter regulatory requirements but is important for members and eligible shareholders/investors wishing to tender shares within the specified window and price of Rs. 12.00 per share.
