## Summary

BSE announces the listing of 35,69,50,000 new equity shares of Pulsar International Limited (Scrip Code: 512591) issued on a rights basis. The shares were allotted on January 21, 2026, and will commence trading from January 27, 2026. These shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- Company: Pulsar International Limited
- Scrip Code: 512591
- Number of Shares: 35,69,50,000 equity shares
- Face Value: Rs. 1/- per share
- Issue Price: At Par (Rs. 1/-)
- Issue Type: Rights Issue
- ISIN: INE183U01022
- Distinctive Numbers: 71390001 to 428340000
- Allotment Date: January 21, 2026
- Trading Commencement: January 27, 2026 (Tuesday)
- Ranking: Pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should note the new securities in their systems
- Updated share capital and distinctive number range to be reflected
- New ISIN INE183U01022 to be used for these securities

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: January 21, 2026
- **Trading Commencement**: January 27, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This listing represents a significant dilution with approximately 356.95 million new shares being added to the traded float. The rights issue at par value suggests capital raising by the company. Trading members and investors should note the expanded share capital when trading from January 27, 2026. The shares carry equal rights with existing equity, ensuring fair treatment of all shareholders.
