## Summary

BSE has approved the surrender of trading membership of Rudra Shares & Stock Brokers Ltd. (Member No. 3269) in the Currency Derivative segment, effective June 11, 2026. This is a routine membership compliance notice issued to all trading members of the Exchange.

## Key Points

- Rudra Shares & Stock Brokers Ltd. (Member No. 3269) has surrendered its trading membership in the Currency Derivative segment.
- The surrender has been approved by BSE with effect from June 11, 2026.
- Trading members requiring clarification may contact BSE at membership.ops@bseindia.com.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative action recording the voluntary surrender of a trading membership in the Currency Derivative segment.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance actions required from other trading members. Members with queries or clarifications regarding this notice may contact BSE's Membership Operations team at membership.ops@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** June 11, 2026 — surrender of trading membership approved.
- **Notice Date:** June 12, 2026.

## Impact Assessment

Low impact. This notice pertains solely to the administrative exit of a single trading member (Rudra Shares & Stock Brokers Ltd.) from the Currency Derivative segment. Existing clients of the member would need to transfer or close their positions in the segment. No broader market, pricing, or regulatory impact is anticipated.
