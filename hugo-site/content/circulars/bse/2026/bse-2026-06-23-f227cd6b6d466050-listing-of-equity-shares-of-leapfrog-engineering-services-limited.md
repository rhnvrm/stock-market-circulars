## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the equity shares of Leapfrog Engineering Services Limited (Scrip Code: 544797, Scrip ID: LESL) will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange effective Wednesday, June 24, 2026, under the 'MT' Group of Securities. The scrip will be placed in the Trade-for-Trade segment for 10 trading days as per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012.

## Key Points

- **Listing Date:** June 24, 2026 (Wednesday)
- **Group:** MT Group of Securities
- **Scrip Code:** 544797 | **Scrip ID:** LESL
- **ISIN:** INE0X6O01027
- **Total Shares:** 14,17,92,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each, fully paid up
- **Distinctive Number Range:** 1 to 14,17,92,000
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 23/- per share (Face Value Rs. 1.00/-, Premium Rs. 22.00/-)
- **Market Lot:** 6,000 equity shares
- **Date of Allotment:** June 22, 2026
- **Pari Passu:** Yes
- **Financial Year End:** March 31
- **Trade-for-Trade Segment:** 10 trading days from listing date
- **Special Pre-open Session (SPOS):** June 24, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. Listing is conducted in accordance with existing SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000 (dematerialised trading only) and SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012 (Trade-for-Trade segment for new listings).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade this scrip only in dematerialised form.
- All trades must be executed in the minimum market lot of 6,000 equity shares.
- Trading members should note SPOS participation requirements per Exchange notice 20120216-29 dated February 16, 2012.
- Lock-in details are as per Annexure I; shareholding pattern is as per Annexure II of the notice.

## Important Dates

- **June 22, 2026:** Date of allotment in public issue
- **June 24, 2026:** Listing date and commencement of trading (SPOS applicable)
- **Trade-for-Trade period:** 10 trading days from June 24, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine SME IPO listing with limited broader market impact. The scrip will trade in the Trade-for-Trade segment for 10 days, meaning no netting of positions is permitted and delivery is compulsory for each trade. The market lot of 6,000 shares restricts participation to investors able to trade in larger quantities. The Market Maker is Anant Securities (Kolkata), and the Registrar is Integrated Registry Management Services Private Limited (Bengaluru). Impact is confined to investors and trading members dealing in LESL.
