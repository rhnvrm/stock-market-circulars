## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that additional securities issued under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and Employee Stock Option Schemes (ESOS) by 12 companies are listed and admitted for trading on the Exchange effective Monday, June 08, 2026. None of the newly listed shares carry any lock-in restrictions.

## Key Points

- 12 companies have received listing approval for ESOP/ESOS shares effective June 08, 2026
- No lock-in period applies to any of the newly listed securities
- Total new shares span a wide range: from 1,500 shares (InterGlobe Aviation) to 22,374,033 shares (Suzlon Energy)
- All securities are in the Equity segment under Company Related / Listing Operations category

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice under existing ESOP/ESOS listing framework.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are requested to take note of the newly listed securities and update their systems accordingly. The securities are available for trading from Monday, June 08, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 05, 2026
- **Effective Date for Trading:** Monday, June 08, 2026
