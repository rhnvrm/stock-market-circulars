## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 15,00,000 new equity shares of Baazar Style Retail Limited (Scrip Code: 544243) will be listed and permitted to trade effective Monday, July 13, 2026. These shares arose from the conversion of warrants issued to a non-promoter entity on a preferential basis.

## Key Points

- 15,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 5/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 323.25/-, i.e., an issue price of Rs. 328.25/- per share.
- Shares issued to a non-promoter on preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants.
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company.
- Distinctive numbers: 74617354 to 76117353.
- Date of allotment: 18/05/2026.
- ISIN: INE01FR01028.
- Shares are subject to lock-in until 31/01/2027.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing approval notice under BSE Listing Operations.

## Compliance Requirements

No action required from trading members other than updating systems to permit trading in the new shares from the effective date; the lock-in restricts transfer of these specific shares until 31/01/2027.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: 18 May 2026
- Listing/Trading effective date: 13 July 2026 (Monday)
- Lock-in expiry: 31 January 2027

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected given the small size of the tranche (15 lakh shares) relative to typical listed float, and the shares are locked in until January 2027, limiting immediate tradable supply. The listing simply formalizes conversion of previously issued warrants into equity.
