## Summary

BSE has listed 6,01,04,119 new equity shares of Corporate Merchant Bankers Limited (Scrip Code: 540199, ISIN: INE850R01014), issued at Rs. 16.08/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- + premium of Rs. 6.08/-) to Non-Promoters on a preferential basis. Trading in these shares is permitted effective Wednesday, May 13, 2026.

## Key Points

- **Company:** Corporate Merchant Bankers Limited (Scrip Code: 540199)
- **ISIN:** INE850R01014
- **New Shares Listed:** 6,01,04,119 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 16.08/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- + premium Rs. 6.08/-)
- **Allottees:** Non-Promoters, on a preferential basis
- **Distribution Numbers:** 3300001 to 63404119
- **Pari-passu:** New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- **Trading Effective:** Wednesday, May 13, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification pursuant to preferential allotment of equity shares.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to permit trading of the newly listed shares from May 13, 2026 onwards.
- The allotted shares are subject to a lock-in period and cannot be traded until the lock-in expires.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** November 27, 2025
- **Trading Commencement:** May 13, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry:** November 30, 2026 (for all 6,01,04,119 shares, Dist. Nos. 3300001–63404119)

## Impact Assessment

This listing introduces approximately 6.01 crore new equity shares into the market for Corporate Merchant Bankers Limited. However, all newly listed shares are under lock-in until November 30, 2026, meaning no additional supply will enter the tradeable float immediately. Market impact is minimal in the near term. Post lock-in expiry, a significant number of shares may become available for trading, which could affect the stock's liquidity and price dynamics.
