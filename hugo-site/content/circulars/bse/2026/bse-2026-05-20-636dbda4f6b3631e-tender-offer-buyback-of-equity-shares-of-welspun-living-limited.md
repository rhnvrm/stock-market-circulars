## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Welspun Living Limited (BSE Code: 514162 / 114162) has fixed **22 May 2026** as the record date for its equity share buyback offer. The record date will be used to determine the entitlement and names of equity shareholders eligible to participate in the buyback.

## Key Points

- Welspun Living Limited has announced a Tender Offer (Buyback) of its equity shares.
- Record Date fixed at **22 May 2026** under the Dematerialised Securities – Rolling Settlement Segment.
- Ex-Entitlement date is also **22 May 2026** (Settlement No. DR-636/2026-2027).
- Trading members are directed to take note of the record date for settlement and eligibility determination purposes.
- Notice issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President – Listing Compliance and Operations, BSE.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notification under existing BSE listing compliance and buyback regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the record date of 22 May 2026 for Welspun Living Limited's buyback offer.
- Members must ensure proper settlement of trades in the scrip in accordance with the ex-entitlement date under Settlement No. DR-636/2026-2027.
- Shareholders holding dematerialised equity shares as of the record date will be eligible to participate in the buyback.

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Notice Date | 20 May 2026 |
| Record Date | 22 May 2026 |
| Ex-Entitlement Date | 22 May 2026 |
| Settlement No. | DR-636/2026-2027 |

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a **medium** impact, limited to existing equity shareholders of Welspun Living Limited (BSE: 514162). Shareholders holding shares before the ex-entitlement date of 22 May 2026 will be eligible for the buyback offer, which may provide a potential exit opportunity at a premium to market price. Trading members must account for the ex-date in trade settlements. No broader market or systemic impact is expected.
