## Summary

BSE has notified that the Draft Offer Document of Shreekrishna Impex Ventures Limited, filed on June 30, 2026, has been made available on the SME (Small and Medium Enterprises) platform website. The document contains disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the SEBI ICDR (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations and is being hosted for at least 21 days to solicit public comments.

## Key Points

- The Draft Offer Document pertains to the SME IPO of Shreekrishna Impex Ventures Limited.
- The document was filed with BSE on June 30, 2026.
- It is hosted on the SME website for a minimum period of 21 days from the notice date to allow public comments.
- The document link: bsesme.com/download/364046/SME_IPO InPrinciple/Final_DRHP_ShreeKrishna_20260630220713.pdf
- Public comments can be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com.
- The notice was issued by Sachin Jain, Vice President, BSE, DCS-Listing Department.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is a procedural disclosure under Schedule VI of the SEBI (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, mandating public hosting of draft offer documents for SME IPOs.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of the public, investors, and stakeholders wishing to comment on the Draft Offer Document must submit their comments within 21 days from the date of this notice (by approximately July 23, 2026) via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- Draft Offer Document filed: June 30, 2026
- Notice date: July 02, 2026
- Public comment period: At least 21 days from notice date (up to approximately July 23, 2026)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has limited market-wide impact and is primarily relevant to prospective investors and stakeholders of Shreekrishna Impex Ventures Limited's upcoming SME IPO. It represents a standard regulatory step in the SME listing process, providing transparency and allowing public scrutiny before the IPO proceeds further.
