## Summary

SUNDARAM MUTUAL FUND is launching SUNDARAM INCOME PLUS ARBITRAGE ACTIVE FOF (Fund of Funds) as a New Fund Offer (NFO) with Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) facility on BSE StAR MF Platform. The NFO will be open from January 05, 2026 to January 08, 2026, with a tentative allotment date of January 12, 2026. Six plan variants are being offered across Regular and Direct plans with Growth and IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal) options.

## Key Points

- NFO period: January 05, 2026 to January 08, 2026
- Six scheme variants offered: Regular Plan (Growth, IDCW Reinvestment, IDCW Payout) and Direct Plan (Growth, IDCW Reinvestment, IDCW Payout)
- All schemes categorized under Equity with ISINs ranging from INF903JA1LZ7 to INF903JA1ME0
- Tentative allotment date: January 12, 2026
- Extended trading till midnight on January 08, 2026 for lumpsum purchases only
- SIP/XSIP registrations must be completed by 3:00 PM on NFO closure date
- Transactions without client-initiated funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 will be cancelled
- Switches accepted during NFO period subject to fund receipt on or before allotment date

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds are available in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on January 12, 2026
- Transactions without client-initiated funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day will be cancelled and reported to RTA/AMC with CC and N flag
- Transactions with client-initiated payment will remain pending until 3:00 PM on the last day of fund payout or T+5, whichever is earlier
- Cheques and OTM payments are acceptable during NFO period only if clear funds are received in ICCL account by 6:00 PM on January 12, 2026
- SIP/XSIP registrations must be completed before 3:00 PM on NFO closure date (January 08, 2026)
- Switch transactions must receive funds on or before allotment date to be considered for allotment

## Important Dates

- **NFO Start Date**: January 05, 2026
- **NFO End Date**: January 08, 2026 (3:00 PM for SIP/XSIP registrations)
- **Extended Period**: Till midnight on January 08, 2026 (lumpsum purchases only)
- **Tentative Allotment Date**: January 12, 2026
- **Clear Funds Deadline**: 6:00 PM on January 12, 2026 (ICCL Account)

## Impact Assessment

**Operational Impact**: This NFO launch follows standard procedures for mutual fund offerings on BSE StAR MF Platform. Members need to ensure compliance with payment timelines and transaction processing requirements.

**Market Impact**: Minimal - this is a routine NFO launch affecting only mutual fund distribution participants and investors interested in arbitrage fund-of-funds products. No impact on equity market operations or listed securities.

**Investor Impact**: Provides new investment opportunity in an actively managed arbitrage fund-of-funds structure with both Regular and Direct plan options, catering to different investor preferences for growth or income distribution.
