## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document (Draft Red Herring Prospectus) of Royal Foodstuffs Limited, filed on June 23, 2026, on its SME platform in accordance with Schedule VI of the SEBI ICDR Regulations. The document is open for public comments for a minimum period of 21 days from the date of this notice.

## Key Points

- Royal Foodstuffs Limited filed its Draft Offer Document on June 23, 2026
- The DRHP is hosted on the BSE SME website (bsesme.com)
- The document is available for at least 21 days for public review and comment
- Comments must be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Filed under the SME segment, DCS-Listing department

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural notice under Schedule VI of the SEBI ICDR Regulations governing SME IPO disclosures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members of the public and market participants may review the Draft Offer Document and submit comments within 21 days from June 24, 2026
- Comments to be sent to: equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Filing Date:** June 23, 2026
- **Notice Date:** June 24, 2026
- **Public Comment Deadline:** July 15, 2026 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This notice is limited in market impact as it pertains to a single SME IPO filing. It is a routine disclosure requirement under ICDR Regulations. Investors and analysts interested in Royal Foodstuffs Limited may review the DRHP and submit comments before the deadline. No trading or settlement changes are involved.
