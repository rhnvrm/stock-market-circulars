## Summary

BSE has revoked the suspension in trading of equity shares of Global Infratech & Finance Limited (CIN: L16299KA1995PLC214634, Scrip Code: 531463). The company is engaged in investments in shares and securities and is operationally active.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension in equity shares of Global Infratech & Finance Limited (Scrip Code: 531463) has been revoked by BSE.
- The company is incorporated under CIN L16299KA1995PLC214634.
- Registered address: F-10-11-12 BSR Arcade, 198, Gandhi Bazaar Main Road, Basavangudi, Bengaluru – 560 004.
- Corporate/communication address: 7/23 Kirti Nagar Industrial Area, Near Underpass, Delhi – 110015.
- Managing Director and Chairman: Mr. V S Amarnath.
- Company Secretary & Compliance Officer: Dhaval Bajaj.
- Business activity: Investments in Shares & Securities.
- Operational status: Operational.

## Regulatory Changes

The earlier suspension order on trading of equity shares of Global Infratech & Finance Limited stands revoked. Shares are now eligible to resume trading on BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must maintain compliance with all applicable SEBI and BSE listing regulations to avoid re-suspension.
- Investors and brokers may now trade in the equity shares of Scrip Code 531463 on BSE.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: March 10, 2026 (effective date of revocation as per this circular).

## Impact Assessment

The revocation of trading suspension is a positive development for existing shareholders of Global Infratech & Finance Limited, as it restores liquidity and allows market participants to buy or sell the scrip on BSE. Given the company's small size and niche business (investments in shares and securities), the broader market impact is limited, but the move is significant for retail investors holding the scrip.
