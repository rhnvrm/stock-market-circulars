## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that International Gemmological Institute India Limited (scrip code 533259) has officially changed its name to International Gemological Institute Ltd. The change takes effect on May 04, 2026. The scrip ID and abbreviated name on BOLT Plus System remain IGIL with no alteration.

## Key Points

- Company name changes from **International Gemmological Institute India Limited** to **International Gemological Institute Ltd**
- Scrip Code: **533259** (unchanged)
- Scrip ID on BOLT Plus System: **IGIL** (no change)
- Abbreviated name on BOLT Plus System: **IGIL** (no change)
- Effective date: **May 04, 2026**
- Notice issued by Marian D'Souza, Assistant Vice President – Listing Compliance & Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a purely administrative update reflecting the company's legal name change as registered with the ROC (Registrar of Companies). The exchange is updating its records to reflect the new corporate name.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are notified for awareness purposes only. No specific action is required from trading members as the scrip ID and abbreviated name used for trading (IGIL) remain unchanged.

## Important Dates

- **April 27, 2026**: BSE notice issued
- **May 04, 2026**: Name change effective on BOLT Plus System

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. Since the scrip ID and abbreviated name (IGIL) remain unchanged, day-to-day trading operations are unaffected. Market participants should update any internal records or documentation that reference the company's full legal name.
