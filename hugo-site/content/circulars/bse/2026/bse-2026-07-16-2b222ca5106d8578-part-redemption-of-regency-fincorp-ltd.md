## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that REGENCY FINCORP Ltd has fixed a Record Date for payment of interest and part redemption on its Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs). Consequent to the part redemption, the debentures will trade at a reduced face value effective from the ex-date.

## Key Points

- Company: REGENCY FINCORP Ltd
- Scrip Code: 977843
- Scrip ID: RFL-15%-1-1-28-PVT
- ISIN: INE964R07085
- Record Date: 17 July 2026
- Ex Date: 17 July 2026
- Settlement Number: 674/2026-2027
- Reduced Face Value (DR Value): Rs. 89,474/- per debenture
- No attachment was provided with this notice

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is a routine corporate action notification under BSE's Debt segment listing operations.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must ensure trades in these debentures are executed and settled at the reduced face value from the ex-date onward, and must account for the part redemption and interest payment due to eligible debenture holders as of the record date.

## Important Dates

- Record Date: 17 July 2026
- Ex Date: 17 July 2026
- Settlement: 674/2026-2027

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt market operational action limited to holders of REGENCY FINCORP Ltd's NCDs (ISIN INE964R07085). It does not affect equity markets or the broader investor base. Debenture holders as of the record date will receive interest payment and part redemption proceeds, and the security's face value will reduce accordingly for future trading.
