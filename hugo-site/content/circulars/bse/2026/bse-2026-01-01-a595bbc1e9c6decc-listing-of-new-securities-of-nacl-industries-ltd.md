## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 3,25,01,851 new equity shares of NACL Industries Ltd (Scrip Code: 524709) issued on a rights basis. The shares are listed with effect from January 2, 2026, and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 3,25,01,851 equity shares of Rs. 1/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 75.70 per share
- Total issue price: Rs. 76.70 per share
- Date of allotment: December 31, 2025
- Trading commences: January 2, 2026
- ISIN: INE295D01020
- Distinctive Numbers: 201511480 to 234013330
- New shares rank pari-passu with old equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities and should update their systems to reflect the increased share capital of NACL Industries Ltd.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 31, 2025
- **Listing/Trading Date**: January 2, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a company-specific event with minimal market-wide impact. The rights issue increases NACL Industries' equity base by approximately 3.25 crore shares. Trading members can execute trades in these new shares from January 2, 2026. The new shares have the same rights and privileges as existing shares.
