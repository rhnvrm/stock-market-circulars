## Summary

SHAYONA ENGINEERING LIMITED (Exchange ticker 544686) will be added to the BSE SME IPO Index effective at the open of Sunday, February 1, 2026. The stock is being listed on BSE effective Friday, January 30, 2026. This action has been included in the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE).

## Key Points

- SHAYONA ENGINEERING LIMITED (ticker 544686) listed on BSE on January 30, 2026
- Stock will be added to BSE SME IPO Index
- Addition effective at market open on February 1, 2026
- Corporate action included in end-of-day file (*.SDE)
- Notice references prior Notice No. 20260129-39

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index constituent addition following the listing of a new SME company.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements. Index tracking funds and market participants should note the index composition change for rebalancing purposes. For queries, contact bseindex@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **January 30, 2026**: SHAYONA ENGINEERING LIMITED listing date on BSE
- **February 1, 2026**: Effective date for addition to BSE SME IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine index addition affecting only the BSE SME IPO Index. Impact is limited to:
- Index tracking funds that replicate the BSE SME IPO Index will need to include this stock
- Passive investment flows into the stock from index funds
- Enhanced visibility for SHAYONA ENGINEERING LIMITED within the SME segment

No broader market operational or trading rule changes.
