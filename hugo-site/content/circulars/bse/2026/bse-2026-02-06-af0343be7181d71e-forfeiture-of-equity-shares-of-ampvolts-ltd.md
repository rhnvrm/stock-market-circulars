## Summary

BSE has published a list of equity shares to be forfeited in Ampvolts Limited (formerly known as Quest Softech (India) Limited). The forfeiture affects 76 shareholders with a total of 39,557 shares being forfeited. The shares have specific distinctive numbers ranging from 14803976 to 15880357.

## Key Points

- Total shares to be forfeited: 39,557 equity shares
- Total affected shareholders: 76 individuals and entities
- Company name: Ampvolts Limited (formerly Quest Softech (India) Limited)
- Largest forfeiture: 10,000 shares (Gaurav Pansari)
- Smallest forfeiture: 1 share (multiple shareholders)
- Distinctive number range: 14803976 to 15880357

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a corporate action notification for share forfeiture.

## Compliance Requirements

Affected shareholders listed in the notification will have their shares forfeited as per the company's forfeiture process. The shares will cease to be held by the named shareholders.

## Important Dates

- Notification Date: February 6, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Shareholder Impact:** Direct impact on 76 shareholders who will lose their shareholdings totaling 39,557 shares. This typically occurs due to non-payment of calls or other defaults.

**Company Impact:** Reduction in the number of outstanding shares and the shareholder base. The forfeited shares may be reissued by the company as per applicable regulations.

**Market Impact:** Limited broader market impact as the forfeiture affects a relatively small number of shares and specific shareholders. Trading activity in the stock remains unaffected for other shareholders.
