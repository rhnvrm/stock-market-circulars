## Summary

BSE has issued a notice to all trading members informing them of the suspension of trading in a government security — 798GUJ2026 (Scrip Code: 810587, ISIN: IN1520160038) — effective May 07, 2026. The suspension is due to the security reaching its Redemption Date, making further trading impermissible.

## Key Points

- Trading in government security 798GUJ2026 is suspended effective **May 07, 2026**
- Scrip Code: **810587** | ISIN: **IN1520160038** | Particulars: **798GUJ2026**
- The security is maturing on its Redemption Date, triggering the trading suspension
- Reference DR number: **DR-625/2026-2027**
- Notice issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President – Listing Compliance & Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard operational suspension triggered by the maturity of a listed government security under existing BSE listing and trading rules.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must **not deal** in Scrip Code 810587 (798GUJ2026 / ISIN: IN1520160038) from May 07, 2026 onwards
- Members are required to take note of the suspension and ensure no trades are executed in this security on or after the effective date

## Important Dates

- **May 06, 2026**: Date of notice issuance
- **May 07, 2026**: Effective date of trading suspension (Redemption Date)

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited and routine. This notice affects only one specific government security (798GUJ2026) which is a Gujarat state government bond nearing its maturity. Trading members holding or dealing in this security must cease trading from May 07, 2026. Investors holding this security to maturity will receive redemption proceeds as per standard settlement procedures. No broader market or systemic impact is expected.
