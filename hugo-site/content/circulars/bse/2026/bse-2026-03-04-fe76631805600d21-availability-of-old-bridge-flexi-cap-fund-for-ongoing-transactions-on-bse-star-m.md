## Summary

BSE has notified members (Notice No. 20260304-1) that OLD BRIDGE FLEXI CAP FUND has been reopened for ongoing subscription and redemption, along with SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities on the BSE StAR MF Platform, effective March 04, 2026. This follows a prior notice (No. 20260212-36) which had presumably suspended these transactions.

## Key Points

- OLD BRIDGE FLEXI CAP FUND is now available for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform from March 04, 2026.
- Both Regular and Direct plans are reopened across Growth and IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal) variants.
- Facilities available: subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP.
- Six scheme variants are included, covering Regular Plan and Direct Plan options.
- Reference circular: BSE Notice No. 20260212-36.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational reinstatement notice confirming the resumption of transaction availability for the fund on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members beyond awareness. Members/distributors using BSE StAR MF Platform may now process transactions for the listed Old Bridge Flexi Cap Fund schemes.

## Important Dates

- **March 04, 2026**: Effective date for reopening of Old Bridge Flexi Cap Fund for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This is a routine operational notice restoring transaction availability for a single AMF's flexi cap fund on the BSE StAR MF Platform. Investors and distributors who were unable to transact in the fund since the earlier suspension notice (20260212-36) can now resume normal operations. The six available schemes and their ISINs are:
