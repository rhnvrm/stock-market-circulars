## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Commodity Derivatives segment on Saturday, February 21, 2026. The session is intended for trading members using third-party trading platforms from empaneled vendors or in-house systems via ETI APIs to test their trading applications, including exceptional market condition scenarios such as risk-reduction mode and trading halt. No new BOLT TWS release will accompany this session.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for the Commodity Derivatives segment on Saturday, February 21, 2026
- Open to trading members using third-party empaneled vendor platforms or in-house systems via ETI APIs
- Covers testing of functionalities including risk-reduction mode and trading halt
- No new BOLT TWS Release will be issued
- Trades from mock session carry no margin, pay-in, or pay-out obligations and create no rights or liabilities
- Members are requested to actively participate and submit feedback by 3:00 PM

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice for a scheduled mock trading session.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are encouraged (not mandated) to participate actively in the mock trading session
- Feedback must be submitted by 3:00 PM on the day of the session via the contact numbers or email IDs provided
- For queries, members should contact their Relationship Manager, BSE Helpdesk, or BSE Tech Support

## Important Dates

- **Mock Trading Date:** Saturday, February 21, 2026
- **Log-in Session:** 10:15 AM – 11:00 AM
- **Continuous Trading:** 11:00 AM – 2:00 PM
- **Trade Modification:** 2:00 PM – 2:10 PM
- **Feedback Deadline:** 3:00 PM on February 21, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a test session with no financial obligations or legal consequences for participants. It is a standard operational exercise to ensure trading systems are functioning correctly ahead of live trading. Members using ETI APIs or third-party platforms benefit from the opportunity to validate system readiness.
