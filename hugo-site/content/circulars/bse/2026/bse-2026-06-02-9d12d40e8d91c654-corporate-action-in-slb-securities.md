## Summary

ICCL Ltd. has issued Notice No. 20260602-11 dated 2 June 2026 informing trading members of corporate actions applicable to securities in the Securities Lending and Borrowing (SLB) segment. The specific details of the affected securities and actions are provided in the attached annexure.

## Key Points

- Trading members are advised of corporate actions in the SLB segment.
- Details are provided via an annexure attached to the notice.
- The notice is issued by ICCL Ltd., the clearing corporation of BSE.
- Issued by Tushar Nimkar, Assistant Manager, ICCL.

## Regulatory Changes

No specific regulatory changes are introduced. This is an operational notification regarding corporate actions impacting existing SLB positions.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should review the attached annexure to identify which SLB securities are subject to corporate actions and take appropriate action on open positions or lending/borrowing contracts as required.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 2 June 2026
- Effective dates for individual corporate actions: refer to the annexure.

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited to participants with open positions in the SLB segment for the affected securities. Corporate actions such as dividends, bonuses, or splits can affect the terms of outstanding lending and borrowing contracts, requiring adjustments or settlement. Members should consult the annexure for the specific securities involved.
