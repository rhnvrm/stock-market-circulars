## Summary

BSE has listed 3,54,339 new equity shares of Power & Instrumentation (Gujarat) Limited (Scrip Code: 543912, ISIN: INE557Z01018) for trading effective Tuesday, May 26, 2026. The shares were issued at Rs. 83.75 per share (face value Rs. 10 + premium Rs. 73.75) to Promoter and Non-Promoter shareholders on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 3,54,339 equity shares of Rs. 10/- face value issued at a premium of Rs. 73.75/- (issue price Rs. 83.75/-)
- Issued to Promoter and Non-Promoter on a preferential basis via conversion of warrants
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 19971031 to 20325369
- Date of allotment: March 16, 2026
- Trading commences: May 26, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification enabling newly allotted shares to be traded on BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed that the new securities are listed and permitted to trade from May 26, 2026. Lock-in restrictions apply as detailed below and must be observed.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** March 16, 2026
- **Trading Commencement:** May 26, 2026
- **Lock-in expiry (54,339 shares):** December 1, 2026
- **Lock-in expiry (3,00,000 shares):** December 1, 2027

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 3,54,339 shares to the tradable float of Power & Instrumentation (Gujarat) Limited, though a substantial portion (3,00,000 shares) remains locked in until December 2027 and 54,339 shares until December 2026. The immediate increase in freely tradable shares is limited, minimising short-term dilution impact on existing shareholders. The preferential allotment at Rs. 83.75 per share reflects the conversion price of previously issued warrants.
