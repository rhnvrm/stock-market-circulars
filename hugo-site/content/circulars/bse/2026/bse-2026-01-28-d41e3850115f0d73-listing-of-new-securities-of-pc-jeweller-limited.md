## Summary

PC Jeweller Limited (Scrip Code: 534809) has listed 17,56,260 new equity shares of Re. 1/- each issued on a preferential basis to non-promoters at a premium of Rs. 4.62/-. The securities are listed and permitted for trading on BSE from Thursday, January 29, 2026. These shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares and are subject to lock-in restrictions until July 31, 2026.

## Key Points

- Total shares listed: 17,56,260 equity shares of Re. 1/- each
- Issue price: Rs. 5.62/- per share (Re. 1/- face value + Rs. 4.62/- premium)
- Allotment type: Preferential basis to non-promoters
- Date of allotment: November 15, 2025
- Trading commencement: January 29, 2026
- ISIN: INE785M01021
- Distinctive Numbers: 7326738596 to 7328494855
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities listing
- Lock-in provisions must be observed for the specified period
- All 17,56,260 shares are subject to mandatory lock-in restrictions

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: November 15, 2025
- **Trading Start Date**: January 29, 2026
- **Lock-in Period Ends**: July 31, 2026 (for all shares with Dist. Nos. 7326738596 to 7328494855)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low. The preferential allotment represents additional equity capital for PC Jeweller Limited. With all shares locked-in until July 31, 2026, there will be no immediate selling pressure from these securities. The issue to non-promoters at Rs. 5.62/- per share indicates institutional or strategic investor participation. Trading members should note the lock-in restrictions when processing orders and ensure compliance with trading regulations for these newly listed securities.
