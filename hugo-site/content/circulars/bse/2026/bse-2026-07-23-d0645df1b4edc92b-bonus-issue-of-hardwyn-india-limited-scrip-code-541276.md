## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that HARDWYN INDIA LIMITED (Scrip Code: 541276) has fixed 28 July 2026 as the Record Date for its bonus issue of equity shares. The company's shares will trade on an ex-bonus basis effective the same date.

## Key Points

- HARDWYN INDIA LIMITED (Scrip Code: 541276) will issue bonus shares in the ratio of 2 (TWO) new equity shares of Re.1/- each for every 5 (FIVE) existing equity shares of Re.1/- each held.
- Record Date for determining eligible shareholders: 28 July 2026.
- Shares will trade Ex-Bonus from 28 July 2026 (Settlement No. DR-681/2026-2027).
- As informed by the company, 19,53,73,621 equity shares are expected to be allotted as on 29 July 2026.
- Notice issued under Notice No. 20260723-47 by BSE's Listing Compliance and Operations department, signed by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is a routine corporate action notification under BSE's Listing Operations for a bonus share issue.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must ensure trades in HARDWYN INDIA LIMITED shares are settled on an ex-bonus basis from 28 July 2026 onward and reflect the adjusted share base following allotment.

## Important Dates

- Record Date: 28 July 2026
- Ex-Bonus Date: 28 July 2026
- Expected Allotment Date: 29 July 2026

## Impact Assessment

The bonus issue increases the equity share count of Hardwyn India Limited without affecting overall market capitalization, and will result in a proportional downward price adjustment on the ex-bonus date. Impact is limited to shareholders and traders of this specific stock, with negligible broader market effect.
