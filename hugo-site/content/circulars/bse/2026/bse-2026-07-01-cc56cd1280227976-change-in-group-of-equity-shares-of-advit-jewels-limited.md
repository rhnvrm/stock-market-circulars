## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the equity shares of Advit Jewels Limited (Scrip Code: 544803) will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (T Group) to the Rolling segment, effective Wednesday, July 15, 2026. As a result, dealings in the company's equity shares will be shifted to the B Group. This notice is issued in continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20260630-40 dated June 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code 544803 (Advit Jewels Limited) will move out of the Trade for Trade (T) segment.
- The shares will be transferred to the Rolling segment under B Group.
- The change takes effect from Wednesday, July 15, 2026.
- This is a continuation of an earlier notice (No. 20260630-40, dated June 30, 2026).
- Trading members can contact Mr. Parag Jain (Tel: 022 2272 8685) for further details.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced; this is an administrative reclassification of the trading segment/group for a specific scrip under existing BSE surveillance and settlement group rules.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should update their systems and records to reflect the change in trading group for Advit Jewels Limited from T Group to B Group effective the specified date, and ensure trades are settled/executed under the applicable Rolling segment norms.

## Important Dates

- June 30, 2026: Original Exchange Notice No. 20260630-40 issued.
- July 1, 2026: Current notice issued.
- July 15, 2026 (Wednesday): Effective date for transfer to Rolling segment/B Group.

## Impact Assessment

The reclassification from Trade for Trade (T Group) to Rolling segment (B Group) generally indicates improved liquidity classification and may allow for netting of trades rather than mandatory delivery-based settlement, which is typically viewed as a positive development for trading flexibility in the scrip. Impact is limited to shareholders, investors, and traders of Advit Jewels Limited, with no broader market-wide implications.
