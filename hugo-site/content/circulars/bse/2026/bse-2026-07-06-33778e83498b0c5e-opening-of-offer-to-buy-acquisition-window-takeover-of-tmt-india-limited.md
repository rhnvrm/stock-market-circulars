## Summary

BSE has informed Trading Members and Custodians about the opening of an open offer under the SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 2011, for the acquisition of shares of TMT (India) Limited through the Stock Exchange Acquisition Window mechanism. The offer is being made jointly by three acquirers — Yoga Builders Private Limited, Scaffold Properties Private Limited, and MDK Properties and Estates Private Limited — to public shareholders of the target company.

## Key Points

- Acquirers: M/s. Yoga Builders Private Limited (Acquirer-1), M/s. Scaffold Properties Private Limited (Acquirer-2), and M/s. MDK Properties and Estates Private Limited (Acquirer-3)
- Target Company: TMT (India) Limited
- Offer Price: Rs. 10.00 per fully paid-up equity share (face value Rs. 10.00), payable in cash
- Offer Size: Up to 12,87,988 equity shares, representing 26.00% of the fully paid-up equity and voting share capital of the target company
- The offer is made pursuant to Regulation 3(1) and Regulation 4 of the SEBI (SAST) Regulations, 2011, involving substantial acquisition of shares/voting rights accompanied by a change in control
- Letter of Offer has been attached for market participants' perusal

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced; this notice implements existing SEBI mechanisms for acquisition of shares through the Stock Exchange under Tender-Offers for Takeovers, Buy-Back, and De-listing, as per SEBI circular no. CIR/CFD/POLICYCELL/1/2015 dated April 13, 2015, and no. CFD/DCR2/CIR/P/2016/131 dated December 9, 2016, along with subsequent BSE notices revising these guidelines (dated Feb 10, 2017; April 24, 2019; May 28, 2020; Nov 2, 2020; and Aug 25, 2021).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members and Custodians must process the acquisition through the Stock Exchange Acquisition Window in accordance with the Revised Guidelines under SEBI circular no. CFD/DCR2/CIR/P/2016/131 and BSE notice no. 20170210-16
- Market participants should refer to the Letter of Offer and details available on the BSE website (www.bseindia.com) for full terms
- Public shareholders of TMT (India) Limited wishing to tender shares must do so within the offer window via their trading members

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 6, 2026
- Offer Opening Date: Wednesday, July 8, 2026
- Offer Closing Date: Tuesday, July 21, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine procedural notice enabling execution of a pre-announced open offer/takeover for TMT (India) Limited. The impact is largely confined to existing shareholders of the target company and trading members/custodians handling the tender process, with no broader market-wide implications. Shareholders holding TMT (India) Limited shares should evaluate the Rs. 10/- per share offer against prevailing market price and their investment objectives before the July 21, 2026 deadline.
