## Summary

BSE has issued an oversubscription notice for the Offer for Sale (OFS) of Coal India Limited, where The President of India (through the Ministry of Coal) is the seller. The Oversubscription Option has been exercised, increasing the total offer size from the base 6,16,27,283 shares (1%) to 12,32,54,566 equity shares (2% of total paid-up equity share capital). This is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20251201-40 dated December 01, 2025.

## Key Points

- Seller: The President of India, acting through the Ministry of Coal, Government of India (Promoter of Coal India Limited)
- Base Offer Size: 6,16,27,283 equity shares (1% of total issued and paid-up equity share capital, face value Rs. 10 each)
- Oversubscription Option exercised: Additional 6,16,27,283 equity shares (1%)
- **Total Offer Size: 12,32,54,566 equity shares (2% of total paid-up equity share capital)**
- 1,23,25,458 equity shares (10% of total offer) reserved for Retail Investors on T+1 Day
- Up to 25,000 equity shares additionally offered to eligible employees of Coal India Limited
- OFS conducted through a separate designated window on BSE and NSE

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This notice is an operational update exercising the Oversubscription Option as permitted under the OFS Guidelines, pursuant to the original OFS notice (Exchange Notice No. 20251201-40 dated December 01, 2025).

## Compliance Requirements

- Non-Retail Investors who bid on T Day (May 27, 2026) and have un-allotted bids may carry forward to T+1 Day (May 29, 2026)
- Retail Investor allocation window is T+1 Day (May 29, 2026), subject to receipt of valid bids
- Employee allocation (up to 25,000 shares) governed by OFS Guidelines terms and conditions
- All participants must comply with the terms of the OFS as specified in the original exchange notice

## Important Dates

- **T Day (May 27, 2026):** OFS open for Non-Retail Investors only
- **T+1 Day (May 29, 2026):** OFS open for Retail Investors; also available for Non-Retail Investors carrying forward un-allotted bids from T Day
- **Reference Notice:** BSE Notice No. 20251201-40, dated December 01, 2025 (original OFS announcement)
- **This Notice Date:** May 27, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The exercise of the Oversubscription Option indicates strong demand from Non-Retail (institutional) investors on T Day, effectively doubling the government's divestment from 1% to 2% of Coal India's equity. This is a significant liquidity event for Coal India (COALINDIA) shares. The large supply of shares (12.32 crore shares) entering the market may exert short-term downward pressure on the stock price. Retail investors should note that their participation window is May 29, 2026 (T+1 Day), with 10% of the total offer reserved for them. The employee quota of up to 25,000 shares is minor relative to total offer size. This divestment contributes to the Government of India's disinvestment proceeds target.
