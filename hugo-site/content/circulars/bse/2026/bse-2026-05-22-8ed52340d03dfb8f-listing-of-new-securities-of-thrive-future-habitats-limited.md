## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 17,58,592 new equity shares of Thrive Future Habitats Limited (Scrip Code: 523120, ISIN: INE628D01014) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Monday, May 25, 2026. These shares were issued on a preferential basis to Non Promoters at an issue price of Rs. 125.10/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- at a premium of Rs. 115.10/-).

## Key Points

- 17,58,592 equity shares (face value Rs. 10/-) issued at a premium of Rs. 115.10/- (issue price Rs. 125.10/-)
- Shares issued on a preferential basis to Non Promoters
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 9561829 to 11320420
- Allotment date: March 31, 2026
- Trading commences: May 25, 2026
- All 17,58,592 shares are under lock-in until December 13, 2026
- ISIN: INE628D01014

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice for newly allotted securities following a preferential issue.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed that these securities are now available for trading from May 25, 2026. The lock-in restriction on all 17,58,592 shares must be observed until December 13, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** March 31, 2026
- **Trading Commencement:** May 25, 2026 (Monday)
- **Lock-in Expiry:** December 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The addition of 17,58,592 new equity shares increases the company's total share capital. However, since all newly listed shares are under lock-in until December 13, 2026, there is no immediate increase in free float. Market impact is expected to be minimal in the near term. The preferential issue to Non Promoters at Rs. 125.10/- per share indicates a fundraising activity by the company.
