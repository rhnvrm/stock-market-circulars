## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures of ARKA FINCAP LIMITED (Code: AFL-5-2-26-PVT, ISIN: INE03W107165, Scrip Code: 974525) effective January 21, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing the record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to debentures with ISIN INE03W107165 (Scrip Code: 974525)
- Company has fixed record date for both redemption and interest payment purposes
- No dealings permitted from January 21, 2026
- Notice issued under reference number DR-803/2025-2026
- Circular category: Corporate Actions, Debt Segment

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification for debenture redemption and interest payment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from January 21, 2026
- All trading members are required to take note of this suspension
- Adherence to the no-dealings restriction is mandatory for compliance

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: January 16, 2026
- **Record Date**: January 21, 2026
- **No Dealings From**: January 21, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: High - Trading suspension prevents all buy/sell transactions in these debentures, affecting liquidity and investor exit options.

**Investor Impact**: Existing debenture holders will receive redemption proceeds and interest payment as per the record date. However, no new positions can be established or existing positions liquidated from the suspension date.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to block trades in these securities and inform clients holding or interested in these debentures about the suspension.
