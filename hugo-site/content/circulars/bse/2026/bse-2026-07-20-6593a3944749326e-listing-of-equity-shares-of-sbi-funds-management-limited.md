## Summary

BSE Limited has notified trading members that the equity shares of SBI Funds Management Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been allotted scrip code 544829, trading symbol SBIFUNDS, and ISIN INE640G01020. The exact date of listing and further details of the securities will be announced through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- SBI Funds Management Limited's equity shares are approved for listing and dealings on BSE
- Scrip Code: 544829
- Trading Symbol: SBIFUNDS
- ISIN: INE640G01020
- The specific listing date has not yet been announced and will follow in a separate notice

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is an administrative listing notice under BSE's DCS-Listing department, not a regulatory or policy change.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should update their systems and records to recognize the new scrip code, symbol, and ISIN for SBI Funds Management Limited ahead of the actual listing date.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 20 July 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced via separate notice

## Impact Assessment

The listing of SBI Funds Management Limited, a major asset management company, adds a new tradable security to the Exchange. Market participants, brokers, and investors should track the forthcoming notice for the confirmed listing date to prepare for trading activity, index inclusion considerations, and any related corporate actions.
