## Summary

BSE has listed 6,65,000 new equity shares of SPML Infra Limited (Scrip Code 500402, ISIN INE937A01023) on the Exchange. These shares were issued to Promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to the conversion of warrants and are permitted to trade effective Thursday, May 21, 2026.

## Key Points

- 6,65,000 equity shares of Rs. 2/- face value each issued at a premium of Rs. 213/-, resulting in an issue price of Rs. 215.00/-
- Issued to Promoters on a preferential basis via conversion of warrants
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 78156336 to 78821335
- Date of allotment: 12 March 2026
- All 6,65,000 shares are subject to lock-in until 14 December 2027

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notice for newly allotted securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed that the new securities are listed and permitted to trade from May 21, 2026
- The lock-in restriction on all 6,65,000 shares must be observed until 14 December 2027

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: 12 March 2026
- Trading Commencement: 21 May 2026
- Lock-in Expiry: 14 December 2027

## Impact Assessment

Minimal broader market impact. The listing adds 6,65,000 shares to the tradeable float of SPML Infra Limited, though these are under promoter lock-in until December 2027 and will not be freely tradeable in the near term. Existing shareholders see no dilution impact on voting rights relative to the preferential allotment already completed.
