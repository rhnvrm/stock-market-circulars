## Summary

HORIZON RECLAIM (INDIA) LIMITED equity shares are listed and admitted to dealings on the BSE effective Friday, June 19, 2026, under the 'MT' Group of Securities in the SME segment. The scrip will remain in the Trade-for-Trade segment for the first 10 trading days per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 544794; Scrip ID / Abbreviated Name on BOLT: HORIZON
- ISIN: INE1SEO01013
- Total securities listed: 19,515,400 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each, fully paid up (distinctive numbers 1 to 19,515,400)
- Issue price: Rs. 103/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- + premium Rs. 93/-)
- Market lot: 1,200 equity shares
- Date of allotment in the public issue: June 17, 2026
- Pari Passu: Yes
- Financial year end: March 31
- Registered Office: Khasra no. 9, Dehradun Road, Near Nirankari Bhawan, Village Kumar Hera, Saharanpur, Uttar Pradesh – 247001
- Market Maker: Giriraj Stock Broking Private Limited, Kolkata (SEBI Reg. No. INZ000212638)
- Registrar: KFIN TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED, Mumbai / Hyderabad (SEBI Reg. No. INR000000221)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. Existing SEBI guidelines apply:
- Per SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000, trading is permitted only in dematerialised form.
- Per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 (January 20, 2012), the scrip is placed in the Trade-for-Trade segment for 10 trading days from listing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade this scrip only in dematerialised form.
- Minimum trade size is the market lot of 1,200 equity shares; the Exchange will give at least one month's prior notice before any change.
- Trading members should refer to Exchange notice no. 20120216-29 (February 16, 2012) for Special Pre-open Session (SPOS) procedures.
- Lock-in details are as per Annexure I; shareholding pattern as per Annexure II of the notice.

## Important Dates

- June 17, 2026: Date of allotment in the public issue
- June 19, 2026 (Friday): Effective listing and commencement of trading on BSE SME; Special Pre-open Session (SPOS) also on this date
- 10 trading days from June 19, 2026: Trade-for-Trade segment restriction period

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard SME IPO listing with no unusual conditions beyond the mandatory 10-day Trade-for-Trade restriction applicable to all new listings under SEBI norms. Impact is confined to trading members active in the BSE SME segment and investors who participated in the HORIZON RECLAIM (INDIA) LIMITED IPO. The market lot of 1,200 shares means block sizes are relatively large for retail participants. The designation of Giriraj Stock Broking as market maker should support liquidity during the initial trading period.
