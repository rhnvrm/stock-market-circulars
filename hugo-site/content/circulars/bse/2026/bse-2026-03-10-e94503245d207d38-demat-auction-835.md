## Summary

BSE has published the Demat Auction Notice No. 835 for Settlement dated 10 March 2026. This circular lists 62 scrip codes and their respective quantities that are to be put up for auction due to short deliveries in the normal settlement process.

## Key Points

- Auction Settlement No. 835 dated 10/03/2026
- A total of 62 scrips are listed for demat auction
- Quantities range from 1 unit (multiple scrips) to 9,027 units (SVARAJ TRADING)
- Notable large-quantity auction entries include: SVARAJ TRADING (9027), TATA CHEMICALS (2000), DANUBE INDUSTRIES (227), GAGAN GASES (226), PREMIER SYNTHETIK (175), GARWARE MARINE (156), MERCURY EV-TECH (112)
- The auction covers scrips across diverse sectors including pharma, metals, chemicals, IT, FMCG, and financial services

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational auction notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants who have short deliveries in the listed scrips must participate or be subject to auction settlement
- Brokers and clearing members should review the list and ensure proper settlement for their clients' positions

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: 10 March 2026
- Auction Settlement No.: 835

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine demat auction notice with low market impact. The auction affects 62 scrips with varying quantities. Most quantities are small (1–50 units), indicating minor short delivery issues in normal course of settlement. The largest quantity is SVARAJ TRADING (9,027 units), followed by TATA CHEMICALS (2,000 units), which may see slight price discovery during the auction session. Overall market impact is minimal and confined to short delivery resolution for the listed securities.
