## Summary

BSE has notified members that Edelweiss Mutual Fund will launch a New Fund Offer (NFO) for the EDELWEISS BSE LARGEMID (60:40) STABLE DIVIDEND 50 ETF on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The NFO will be open from July 27, 2026 to July 29, 2026, with two scheme variants offered: the regular ETF and the Direct Plan - Growth option, both categorized under Equity with a tentative allotment date of August 5, 2026.

## Key Points

- Scheme 1: EDELWEISS BSE LARGEMID (60:40) STABLE DIVIDEND 50 ETF (Equity category)
- Scheme 2: EDELWEISS BSE LARGEMID (60:40) STABLE DIVIDEND 50 ETF - DIRECT PLAN - GROWTH (Equity category)
- Tentative allotment date for both schemes: August 5, 2026
- NFO period: July 27, 2026 to July 29, 2026
- NFO extended till midnight on July 29, 2026 for Lumpsum purchases on BSE StAR MF Platform

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an operational/product notice, not a regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

- Transactions where clients do not initiate funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day (including those during the extended NFO period) will be cancelled, with CC marked 'N' flag reported to RTA/AMC.
- Transactions with initiated payment remain pending until 3:00 PM on the last day of funds pay-out to the AMC, or T+5 day, whichever is earlier.
- Clear funds must be available in the ICCL account by 6:00 PM on August 3, 2026 for allotment eligibility.
- Cheque and OTM payments accepted during the NFO period, subject to clear funds in ICCL account by 6:00 PM on August 3, 2026.
- Switch transactions accepted during the NFO period, subject to receipt of funds prior to the allotment date; switch-out funds received on/after allotment date will not be considered for allotment.

## Important Dates

- NFO Period: July 27, 2026 - July 29, 2026
- Extended Lumpsum purchase deadline: Midnight, July 29, 2026
- Fund availability deadline in ICCL account: 6:00 PM, August 3, 2026
- Tentative allotment date: August 5, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine mutual fund product launch notice with limited market-wide impact. It primarily affects BSE StAR MF Platform members, distributors, and investors participating in the Edelweiss ETF NFO, requiring adherence to specific fund pay-in timelines and transaction cancellation rules to ensure allotment eligibility.
