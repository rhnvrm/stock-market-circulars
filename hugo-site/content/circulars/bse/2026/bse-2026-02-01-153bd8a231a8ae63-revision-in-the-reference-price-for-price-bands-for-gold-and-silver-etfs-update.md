## Summary

BSE has issued an update to Notice No 20260201-01 dated February 1, 2026, regarding changes in the reference price for price bands for Gold and Silver ETFs. This revision is applicable for today only (February 1, 2026).

## Key Points

- This is a continuation notice updating earlier Notice No 20260201-01 from the same day
- Changes apply to reference prices for price bands for both Gold and Silver ETFs
- The revision is applicable for today only (February 1, 2026)
- Trading members are notified to note these changes

## Regulatory Changes

No permanent regulatory changes. This is a temporary one-day adjustment to reference prices for price bands on Gold and Silver ETFs.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the revised reference prices for price bands
- Changes must be implemented for trading on February 1, 2026 only

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: February 1, 2026
- **Effective Date**: February 1, 2026 (today only)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The revision affects price bands for Gold and Silver ETFs, which may influence trading activity in these instruments for the day.

**Operational Impact**: Low - Trading members need to note the updated reference prices, but the impact is limited to a single trading day.

**Investor Impact**: Medium - Investors trading in Gold and Silver ETFs should be aware of the adjusted price bands for the day.
