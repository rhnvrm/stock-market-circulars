## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that MIDWEST GOLD LIMITED (Scrip Code: 526570) has undergone a change in company name. The company will now be known as MIDWEST ENERGY LIMITED, and its Scrip ID and abbreviated name on the BOLT Plus System will change to REMAGNET, effective July 10, 2026.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code 526570 remains unchanged.
- Existing Name: MIDWEST GOLD LIMITED.
- New Name: MIDWEST ENERGY LIMITED.
- New Scrip ID / Abbreviated Name on BOLT Plus System: REMAGNET.
- Change is based on the Certificate of Incorporation submitted by the company confirming the name change.
- Notice issued and signed by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President, Listing Compliance and Operations.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is an administrative update to reflect the company's change of name as evidenced by its updated Certificate of Incorporation.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must update their systems and records to reflect the new Scrip ID (REMAGNET) and company name (MIDWEST ENERGY LIMITED) for BOLT Plus System trading from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 7, 2026.
- Effective Date of Name/Scrip ID Change: July 10, 2026.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to an administrative and operational update. Trading members and investors should note the new company name and scrip ID to ensure accurate order placement and record-keeping; there is no change to the underlying business, shareholding, or financials of the company as a result of this notice.
