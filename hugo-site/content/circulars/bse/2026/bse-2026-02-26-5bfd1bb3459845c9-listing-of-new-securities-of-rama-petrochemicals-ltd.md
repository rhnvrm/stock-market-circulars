## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 15,96,450 new equity shares of Rama Petrochemicals Ltd. (Scrip Code: 500358, ISIN: INE783A01013) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Friday, February 27, 2026. These shares were issued at par (Rs. 10/- each) to promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to the conversion of warrants, and rank pari-passu with the existing equity shares of the company.

## Key Points

- **Company:** Rama Petrochemicals Ltd. (Scrip Code: 500358)
- **Security Type:** Equity shares of Rs. 10/- each, issued at par
- **Number of Shares:** 15,96,450
- **Allottees:** Promoters (preferential basis, warrant conversion)
- **Distribution Numbers:** 11724151 to 13320600
- **ISIN:** INE783A01013
- **Pari-passu:** These shares rank equally with existing equity shares
- **Trading effective from:** February 27, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notice pursuant to an existing preferential allotment approval and warrant conversion.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to update their records to reflect the new securities available for trading from February 27, 2026.
- The newly listed shares carry a lock-in restriction and cannot be freely traded until the lock-in expiry date.

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Date of Allotment | January 23, 2026 |
| Trading Commencement | February 27, 2026 |
| Lock-in Expiry (15,96,450 shares) | September 30, 2027 |

## Impact Assessment

The addition of 15,96,450 shares to Rama Petrochemicals Ltd.'s equity base represents a moderate dilution for existing shareholders. Since these shares are allotted to promoters and are locked in until September 30, 2027, there is no immediate increase in free-float or liquidity impact on the market. The event is routine in nature and has limited broader market significance.
