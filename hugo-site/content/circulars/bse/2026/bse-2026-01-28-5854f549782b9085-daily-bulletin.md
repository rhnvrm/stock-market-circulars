## Summary

BSE Daily Bulletin No. 205/2025-2026 dated January 28, 2026, announces book closure dates and record dates for various corporate actions across multiple companies. The bulletin includes dividend declarations, interest payments on debentures, NCD redemptions, and a rights issue announcement.

## Key Points

- Multiple companies have declared interim dividends with record dates in early February 2026
- Alldigi Tech Limited: Second interim dividend of Rs. 30.00 per share (300%), record date February 4, 2026
- Balkrishna Industries: Third interim dividend of Rs. 4.00 per share (200%), record date February 2, 2026
- Cochin Shipyard Limited: Second interim dividend of Rs. 3.50 per share (70%), record date February 3, 2026
- CRIZAC Limited: Interim dividend of Rs. 8.00 per share (400%), record date February 4, 2026, payment date February 26, 2026
- D & H India Limited: Rights issue of equity shares, record date January 31, 2026
- Bajaj Housing Finance Limited: Multiple NCD interest payments and redemptions scheduled for 2027-2029
- Dishman Carbogen Amcis: PUT option exercise and interest payment, record date January 29, 2026
- DOLAT ALGOTECH Limited: Interim dividend of Rs. 0.10 per share (10%), record date February 4, 2026
- EARLYSALARY Services: Multiple NCD redemptions and interest payments scheduled for 2026-2027

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard daily bulletin format.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies must ensure shareholders on record as of the specified record dates are eligible for announced corporate actions
- Trading flags indicate whether securities will continue trading (flag 1) or trading suspended (flag 0) during the book closure/record date period
- Payment and allotment dates must be adhered to as specified

## Important Dates

- January 29, 2026: Record date for Dishman Carbogen Amcis NCD interest and PUT option
- January 31, 2026: Record date for D & H India Rights Issue
- February 2, 2026: Record date for Balkrishna Industries dividend
- February 3, 2026: Record date for Cochin Shipyard dividend
- February 4, 2026: Record date for Alldigi Tech, CRIZAC, and DOLAT ALGOTECH dividends
- February 26, 2026: Payment date for CRIZAC dividend
- Multiple dates in 2026-2029: Various NCD interest payments and redemptions for Bajaj Housing Finance and EARLYSALARY Services

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact as this is a routine daily bulletin. Individual stock impacts depend on dividend amounts and rights issue terms. Shareholders of record will receive announced dividends. NCD holders should note redemption and interest payment schedules for cash flow planning. The bulletin provides necessary transparency for investors to track corporate actions and entitlements.
