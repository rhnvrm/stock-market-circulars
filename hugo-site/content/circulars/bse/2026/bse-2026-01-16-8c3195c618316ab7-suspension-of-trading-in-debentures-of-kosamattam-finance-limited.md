## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in debentures of KOSAMATTAM FINANCE LIMITED effective January 22, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing the record date for redemption of its Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) with ISIN INE403Q07EN6. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Company: KOSAMATTAM FINANCE LIMITED
- Security Code: KFL-6-2-26-NCD
- ISIN: INE403Q07EN6 (939637)
- Record Date: January 22, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures
- No Dealings From: January 22, 2026
- Notice Reference: DR-804/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notice for debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures of KOSAMATTAM FINANCE LIMITED from January 22, 2026
- Trading members are required to take note of this suspension and ensure compliance
- No new positions should be created in the affected debenture series

## Important Dates

- **January 16, 2026**: Notice issued by BSE
- **January 22, 2026**: Record date for redemption
- **January 22, 2026**: Trading suspension begins (no dealings allowed)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This suspension affects only one specific debenture series of KOSAMATTAM FINANCE LIMITED. The action is routine and related to scheduled redemption.

**Investor Impact**: Low - Only holders of this specific NCD series (INE403Q07EN6) are affected. Investors holding these debentures will have their securities redeemed as per the company's redemption terms.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Trading members need to ensure their systems prevent any dealings in this specific debenture from January 22, 2026. This is a standard operational procedure for corporate actions in debt securities.
