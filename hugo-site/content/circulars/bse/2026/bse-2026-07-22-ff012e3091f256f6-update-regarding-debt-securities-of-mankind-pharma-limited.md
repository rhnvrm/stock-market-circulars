## Summary

BSE has issued a notice in continuation of its earlier Exchange Notice No. 20241017-29 dated October 17, 2024, regarding the debt securities of Mankind Pharma Limited. Trading Members are informed that two specific scrip codes will become available for trading with revised terms, as detailed in Annexure I, following debt restructuring, effective Thursday, July 23, 2026.

## Key Points

- Debt restructuring has been carried out for certain non-convertible debt securities of Mankind Pharma Limited.
- The following scrip codes and ISINs will trade with revised terms from the effective date:
  - Scrip Code 976103, ISIN INE634S07025
  - Scrip Code 976104, ISIN INE634S07033
- The revised terms are specified in Annexure I attached to the notice.
- This update follows and is linked to the earlier Exchange Notice No. 20241017-29 dated October 17, 2024.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework changes; this is an implementation notice reflecting a debt restructuring already undertaken by the issuer, updating the trading terms of existing listed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members must update their systems to reflect the revised terms for Scrip Codes 976103 and 976104 from the effective date and refer to Annexure I for the specific revised terms. Queries can be directed to Ms. Pooja Trivedi or Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, DCS-Listing (debt.listing@bseindia.com, +91 22 2272 8844).

## Important Dates

- Effective date for revised trading terms: Thursday, July 23, 2026
- Original related notice: October 17, 2024 (Notice No. 20241017-29)
- Current notice date: July 22, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and traders of these two specific debt securities of Mankind Pharma Limited. The revised terms stem from a debt restructuring exercise, which could affect coupon rates, maturity, or other terms material to bondholders. No direct impact on Mankind Pharma's equity shares is indicated in this notice.
