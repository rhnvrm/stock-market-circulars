## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260602-21 directs trading members to refrain from dealing in Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of 13 companies that have fixed record dates for the purpose of payment of interest or redemption. The affected debentures span record dates of 4 June, 8 June, and 9 June 2026.

## Key Points

- Trading in the listed NCDs is suspended effective their respective ex-dates, which coincide with their record dates.
- The suspension applies to debentures of companies including Muthoot Fincorp Limited (3 scrips), HDFC Bank, L&T Finance, India Infradebt, Edelweiss Asset Reconstruction, Aditya Birla Capital, Kotak Mahindra Investments, MAS Financial Services, Mahindra & Mahindra Financial Services, Infopark Properties, and Hari and Company Investments Madras.
- All settlement references fall under the DR 644/2026-2027, 646/2026-2027, or 647/2026-2027 cycles.
- The notice was issued by the Listing Compliance and Operations department.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is a standard operational notice under existing BSE debt market rules requiring suspension of NCD trading ahead of record dates for corporate action processing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not buy or sell the specified NCD scrips from their respective ex-dates onward.
- Members should update their systems to block trading in the listed ISIN codes on and after the respective ex-dates.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt market notice with limited systemic impact. Trading members holding or planning to trade in these 13 NCD scrips must cease activity from the respective ex-dates. The suspension is temporary and specific to each debenture's interest payment or redemption event. Investors holding these debentures through the record date will receive their due interest or redemption proceeds as per settlement schedule DR 644–647/2026-2027.
