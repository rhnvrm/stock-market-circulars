## Summary

BSE has issued an update regarding debt securities of Setco Auto Systems Pvt. Ltd, introducing new ISINs and extending maturity dates for two scrip codes. This is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20251128-32 dated November 28, 2025. The changes will be effective from February 1, 2026.

## Key Points

- Two scrip codes (975859 and 973453) will be available for trading with revised terms
- New ISINs have been assigned to replace existing ISINs
- Maturity dates have been extended from December 1, 2025 to January 31, 2026
- Changes effective from February 1, 2026
- Trading members can contact debt listing team for clarifications

## Regulatory Changes

**Scrip Code 975859:**
- Existing ISIN: INE0I0R07078 → New ISIN: INE0I0R07102
- Available trading ISIN: INE0I0R07045
- Quantity: 975
- Previous Maturity: December 1, 2025 → New Maturity: January 31, 2026

**Scrip Code 973453:**
- Existing ISIN: INE0I0R07086 → New ISIN: INE0I0R07110
- Available trading ISIN: INE0I0R07052
- Quantity: 2,150
- Previous Maturity: December 1, 2025 → New Maturity: January 31, 2026

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the new ISINs and revised terms for both scrip codes
- Members should update their systems to reflect the new ISINs and maturity dates
- Members holding positions in these securities should be aware of the extended maturity period

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** January 30, 2026
- **Effective Date:** February 1, 2026 (Sunday)
- **New Maturity Date:** January 31, 2026 (for both securities)
- **Previous Maturity Date:** December 1, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This update has moderate impact on trading members dealing with debt securities of Setco Auto Systems Pvt. Ltd. The maturity extension by approximately two months provides additional time for debt servicing. Trading members need to update their records with new ISINs to ensure proper settlement. The change affects a limited number of securities (975 units and 2,150 units respectively).
