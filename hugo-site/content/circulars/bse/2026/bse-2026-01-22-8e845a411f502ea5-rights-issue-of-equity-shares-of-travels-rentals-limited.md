## Summary

TRAVELS & RENTALS LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544242) has fixed the record date for its rights issue of equity shares. The company will issue 1 equity share of Rs. 10/- face value for every 1 equity share held at a premium of Rs. 5/- per share, making the total issue price Rs. 15/- per share. Trading on ex-rights basis will commence from January 28, 2026.

## Key Points

- **Rights Entitlement:** 1 equity share for every 1 equity share held (1:1 ratio)
- **Face Value:** Rs. 10/- per equity share
- **Premium:** Rs. 5/- per share
- **Total Issue Price:** Rs. 15/- per share (payable in full at application)
- **Scrip Code:** 544242
- **Settlement Number:** DR-807/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification for a rights issue in compliance with BSE listing requirements.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must execute all transactions in TRAVELS & RENTALS LIMITED shares on an ex-rights basis from the specified date
- The entire issue price of Rs. 15/- per share must be paid at the time of application
- Trading members are required to note this corporate action for proper settlement and client communication

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** January 22, 2026
- **Record Date:** January 28, 2026
- **Ex-Rights Date:** January 28, 2026
- **Settlement Number:** DR-807/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - The rights issue will result in equity dilution for non-participating shareholders. Trading on ex-rights basis from January 28, 2026 will adjust the market price to reflect the rights entitlement.

**Shareholder Impact:** Existing shareholders have the opportunity to maintain their proportional ownership by subscribing to the rights issue at Rs. 15/- per share. Non-participation will result in dilution of their holdings.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members must ensure their systems and processes account for ex-rights trading from the specified date to ensure proper settlement and prevent trade failures.
