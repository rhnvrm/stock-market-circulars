## Summary

BSE has listed five new commercial papers issued on a private placement basis, now admitted to trading on the BSE Debt Segment effective May 27, 2026. The issuers are Tata Housing Development Company Limited, Muthoot MCred Limited, Bajaj Financial Securities Limited, SBICAP Securities Limited, and Poonawalla Fincorp Limited. All securities trade in dematerialised form in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof, with a tick size of 1 paise.

## Key Points

- Five commercial papers listed on BSE Debt Segment effective May 27, 2026
- All securities are traded in demat form only, in denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise for all listed CPs
- Issuing and paying agents: HDFC Bank Limited (for THDCL, MML), ICICI Bank Limited (for BFSL), State Bank of India (for SBICAPSL), and TBD for Poonawalla Fincorp
- Face value and paid-up value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit for all CPs

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification for privately placed commercial papers admitted to the BSE Debt Segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form
- Trades must be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the BSE Debt Department at 22728352 / 8597 / 8995 / 5753 / 8915

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard operational listing notice with minimal market impact. The five commercial papers are short-term debt instruments (tenors of approximately 2–6 months) available to institutional and eligible investors on the BSE Debt Segment. Key details:

- **Tata Housing Development Company (THDC26526):** ISIN INE582L14JO1; Qty 7,300; Issue Price Rs. 4,90,582; Rate 7.70% p.a.; Credit Rating CARE A1+, IND A1+; PA: HDFC Bank
- **Muthoot MCred (MML26526):** ISIN INE101Q14363; Qty 800; Issue Price Rs. 4,77,264; Rate 9.45% p.a.; Credit Rating CARE A1; PA: HDFC Bank
- **Bajaj Financial Securities (BFSL26526):** ISIN INE01C314FR8; Qty 3,500; Issue Price Rs. 4,93,131.50; Rate 7.20% p.a.; Credit Rating CRISIL A1+, IND A1+; PA: ICICI Bank
- **SBICAP Securities (SBICA26526):** ISIN INE212K14EC3; Qty 2,000; Issue Price Rs. 4,90,162.50; Rate 8.05% p.a.; Credit Rating CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+; PA: State Bank of India
- **Poonawalla Fincorp (PFL26526):** ISIN INE511C14ZO5; Qty 3,000; Issue Price Rs. 4,90,102.50; Rate 8.10% p.a.; Credit Rating CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+

All issuers carry high short-term credit ratings (A1/A1+), indicating low credit risk. Muthoot MCred carries a slightly lower CARE A1 rating compared to the A1+ ratings of the other four issuers, reflected in its higher coupon rate of 9.45% p.a.
