## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260713-16 dated 13 July 2026, releasing the Trading and Settlement Programme for the Corporate Debt segment for the period from 01.08.2026 to 31.08.2026. The programme is provided via the attached annexure (ICDM082026) and falls under the Settlement/RMS category, managed by the BSECL department.

## Key Points

- Notice pertains to the Corporate Debt segment of BSE.
- Covers the trading and settlement calendar for August 2026 (01.08.2026 to 31.08.2026).
- Detailed schedule is provided in the attachment titled ICDM082026.
- Issued under the Settlement/RMS category by the BSECL department.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced; this is a periodic administrative notice publishing the operational trading and settlement calendar for the segment.

## Compliance Requirements

Market participants and trading members active in the Corporate Debt segment should refer to the attached calendar (ICDM082026) to align trading and settlement activities with the specified dates for August 2026.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 13 July 2026
- Programme Period: 01 August 2026 to 31 August 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, low-impact operational circular. It does not affect specific stocks or introduce policy changes but ensures market participants in the Corporate Debt segment are informed of the applicable trading and settlement schedule for the upcoming month.
