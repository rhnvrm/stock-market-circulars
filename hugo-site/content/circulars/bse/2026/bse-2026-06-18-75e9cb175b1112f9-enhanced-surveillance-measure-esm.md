## Summary

BSE has updated the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework under Notice No. 20260618-30. Securities identified in the annexed list will be subject to minimum 100% margin requirements and shifted from Rolling Settlement to Trade-for-Trade settlement effective June 22, 2026. Additionally, certain securities will be downgraded to Stage I, while others will exit the ESM framework entirely, both effective June 19, 2026.

## Key Points

- Securities in Annexure I will attract a minimum **100% margin** on all open positions as of June 19, 2026, and on new positions from June 22, 2026.
- Affected securities will move from **Rolling Settlement to Trade-for-Trade (TFT) segment** effective June 22, 2026.
- Securities shifting to **Stage II** will be placed under Trade-for-Trade with a **2% price band** and **Periodic Call Auction** effective June 19, 2026.
- Some securities currently in ESM will be **moved down to Stage I** (lower stage) effective June 19, 2026 (listed in Annexure I).
- Securities exiting the ESM framework entirely are listed in **Annexure II**, effective June 19, 2026.
- A consolidated list of all securities currently under ESM is provided in **Annexure III**.
- ESM placement is a **market surveillance action** and should not be construed as adverse action against the company.
- Scrips exiting ESM will have their **prior price band reinstated**, unless covered by another surveillance measure.

## Regulatory Changes

This notice is issued under the ESM framework established by earlier Exchange notices: 20230602-44, 20230718-46, 20240809-42, 20240920-63, 20241004-65, and 20250725-61. The current update operationalizes the latest periodic review of securities under the ESM framework, resulting in new inclusions, stage changes, and exclusions.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading members** must ensure collection of minimum 100% margins on open and new positions in newly included ESM securities from the specified effective dates.
- Members must note the shift to **Trade-for-Trade settlement** and ensure client positions are handled accordingly.
- Members must comply with the **2% price band and Periodic Call Auction** mechanism for Stage II securities.
- Members may contact BSE Surveillance at **bse.surv@bseindia.com** for clarifications.

## Important Dates

## Impact Assessment

This circular has **high market impact** on the specific scrips listed in the annexures. The 100% margin requirement effectively doubles the capital requirement for holding positions, likely reducing liquidity and trading volumes in affected securities. The shift from Rolling Settlement to Trade-for-Trade eliminates netting benefits and requires full delivery of shares, further restricting speculative activity. The 2% price band on Stage II securities severely limits intraday price movement. Collectively, these measures are designed to curb excessive speculation and price manipulation in high-risk equities. Market participants holding positions in affected scrips should review the annexures immediately and prepare for the capital and settlement implications.
