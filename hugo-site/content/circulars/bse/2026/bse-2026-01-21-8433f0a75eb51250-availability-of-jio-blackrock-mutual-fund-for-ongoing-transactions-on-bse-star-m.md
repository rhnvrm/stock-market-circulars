## Summary

BSE has announced that two JIO BLACKROCK MUTUAL FUND debt schemes - Low Duration Fund and Short Duration Fund - will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective January 22, 2026. Both schemes will also support Systematic Investment Plan (SIP), Systematic Transfer Plan (STP), and Systematic Withdrawal Plan (SWP) facilities.

## Key Points

- Two JIO BLACKROCK debt schemes reopening for transactions: Low Duration Fund and Short Duration Fund
- Both schemes available in Direct Plan - Growth option
- Full transactional capabilities restored: subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Effective date: January 22, 2026
- Previous suspension referenced in Notice No. 20260107-18

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding the availability of specific mutual fund schemes on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance requirements. This is an informational notice for market participants and investors regarding scheme availability.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 21, 2026
- **Effective Date**: January 22, 2026 - Schemes reopen for ongoing transactions

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This affects only investors interested in JIO BLACKROCK debt mutual funds.

**Investor Impact**: Positive for existing and prospective investors in these schemes, as they can now resume or initiate investments, redemptions, and systematic transaction facilities.

**Operational Impact**: BSE StAR MF Platform members can process transactions for these two schemes starting January 22, 2026.
