## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 65,34,507 equity shares of Ashika Credit Capital Limited (Scrip Code: 543766) effective January 16, 2026. These shares were issued pursuant to a Scheme of Amalgamation between Yaduka Financial Services Limited (Transferor Company) and Ashika Credit Capital Limited (Transferee Company) under sections 230-232 of the Companies Act, 2013.

## Key Points

- 65,34,507 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each to be listed
- Shares issued under Scheme of Amalgamation with Yaduka Financial Services Limited
- Trading permitted from January 16, 2026
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE094B01013
- Distinctive Numbers: 38196639 to 44731145
- Allotment Date: December 1, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification following corporate restructuring.

## Compliance Requirements

Market participants should:
- Update their systems to reflect the increased share capital of Ashika Credit Capital Limited
- Note the new distinctive number range for the allotted shares
- Ensure trading systems are configured for the January 16, 2026 trading commencement

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 1, 2025
- **Notice Date**: January 14, 2026
- **Trading Commencement**: January 16, 2026 (Friday)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a routine corporate action resulting from amalgamation. The listing increases Ashika Credit Capital Limited's share capital but does not affect broader market operations or trading rules. Impact is confined to shareholders and traders of this specific security.
