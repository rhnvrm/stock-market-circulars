## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (No. 20260518-22) dated 18 May 2026 permitting the trading of units of ETFs belonging to Groww Mutual Fund under the Permitted Category on the exchange. This is an administrative listing/trading enablement notice from the DCS-Listing department.

## Key Points

- BSE Notice No. 20260518-22 dated 18 May 2026.
- ETF units of Groww Mutual Fund are permitted to be traded under the Permitted Category on BSE.
- Issued by the DCS-Listing department under the Mutual Fund segment.
- No attachments provided with this notice.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice facilitates trading access for Groww Mutual Fund ETF units within the existing Permitted Category framework on BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements imposed on market participants. Brokers and members should note the availability of Groww Mutual Fund ETF units for trading under the Permitted Category and update their systems accordingly.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 18 May 2026
- Effective trading availability as per the notice; no separate effective date mentioned.

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This is a routine operational notice that expands the tradable universe on BSE by allowing Groww Mutual Fund ETF units to be transacted under the Permitted Category. It benefits investors seeking exposure to these ETFs through BSE but does not alter existing regulations or impose new obligations on listed companies or market intermediaries.
