## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 1,00,00,000 (1 crore) equity shares of **Credent Global Finance Limited** (Scrip Code: 539598, ISIN: INE727C01024) have been listed and admitted for trading on the Exchange with effect from **Tuesday, February 24, 2026**. These shares were allotted to Qualified Institutional Buyers (QIBs) pursuant to a Qualified Institutional Placement (QIP).

## Key Points

- 1,00,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 2/- face value each are newly listed
- Shares allotted via Qualified Institutional Placement (QIP) to QIBs
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Issue price: Rs. 30/- per share
- Date of allotment: February 19, 2026
- Distinctive numbers: 51461231 to 61461230
- Trading commences: February 24, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine admission of newly allotted securities to trading on BSE following a completed QIP.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to update their records to reflect the new securities. Members requiring clarification may contact Mr. Gaurav Bajare at Tel. No. 022 2272 8291.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** February 19, 2026
- **Listing/Trading Effective Date:** February 24, 2026 (Tuesday)
- **Notice Date:** February 23, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing increases the total tradable equity share count of Credent Global Finance Limited by 1 crore shares. As these shares are pari-passu with existing equity, there is a dilutive effect on existing shareholders. The QIP at Rs. 30/- per share reflects the institutional pricing benchmark. Impact is limited to shareholders and traders of this specific scrip (Code: 539598); no broader market or systemic impact anticipated.
