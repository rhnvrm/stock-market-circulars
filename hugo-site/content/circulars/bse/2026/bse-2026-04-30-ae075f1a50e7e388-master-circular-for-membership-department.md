## Summary

BSE has issued a Master Circular for Membership Operations consolidating all previously issued circulars and guidelines applicable to Members up to March 31, 2026. The circular is intended to provide Members with a single point of reference for all membership-related regulatory requirements and facilitate ongoing compliance.

## Key Points

- The Master Circular is a compilation of all relevant Membership Operations circulars and guidelines issued up to March 31, 2026.
- It consists of two parts: Annexure A (Index and detailed description of Membership Operations requirements) and Annexure B (Quick Reference).
- In case of any inconsistency between the Master Circular and an applicable individual circular, the content of the relevant individual circular shall prevail.
- All actions taken under previously rescinded guidelines remain valid and unaffected.
- Rights, obligations, liabilities, penalties, and legal proceedings under rescinded guidelines continue to remain in force.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced by this circular. It is a consolidation of existing circulars and guidelines for ease of reference. Individual circulars, where inconsistent with the Master Circular, continue to take precedence.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must take note of the Master Circular and comply with all norms in letter and spirit on a continuous basis.
- Members should refer to Annexure A for detailed descriptions of Membership Operations requirements.
- Members should use Annexure B as a quick reference guide for compliance checks.
- For clarifications, members may contact their relationship manager, email Membership.ops@bseindia.com, or contact Ms. Bina at 022-22728348.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** April 30, 2026
- **Coverage Period:** All Membership Operations circulars and guidelines up to March 31, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a moderate operational impact on BSE Members. It simplifies compliance by providing a consolidated reference document for all membership-related obligations, reducing the need to track multiple individual circulars. No new obligations are imposed; however, Members should review the Master Circular to ensure they are aware of and adhering to all applicable membership requirements. The protection of rights and liabilities under previously rescinded guidelines ensures continuity and legal certainty for Members.
