## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Electronic Gold Receipts (EGR) segment on Saturday, June 13, 2026. The session will run from both the primary site (PR) and disaster recovery site (DR), allowing trading members to test their systems and trading applications.

## Key Points

- Mock trading covers both primary site (PR) and disaster recovery (DR) switchover testing
- Trading members using third-party ETI API platforms or in-house systems are encouraged to participate
- No new BOLT TWS release for this session
- Trades from mock sessions carry no margin obligations, pay-in/pay-out obligations, or legal rights and liabilities
- Members are requested to submit feedback by 5:00 pm on the same day

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a scheduled operational test session.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are encouraged to actively participate to test functionalities including risk-reduction mode and trading halt scenarios
- Members should submit feedback to BSE Tech Support or BSE Helpdesk by 5:00 pm on June 13, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Mock Trading Date:** Saturday, June 13, 2026
- **Login:** 09:15 am – 10:00 am
- **Pre-Open Order Entry (PR):** 10:00 am – 10:08 am
- **Pre-Open Matching (PR):** 10:08 am – 10:15 am
- **Continuous Trading (PR):** 10:15 am – 01:00 pm
- **Switchover to DR:** 01:00 pm – 01:45 pm
- **Pre-Open Order Entry (DR):** 01:45 pm – 01:53 pm
- **Pre-Open Matching (DR):** 01:53 pm – 02:00 pm
- **Continuous Trading (DR):** 02:00 pm – 03:45 pm
- **Closing:** 03:45 pm – 03:55 pm
- **Post-Closing:** 03:55 pm – 04:05 pm
- **Feedback Deadline:** 5:00 pm on June 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine mock trading exercise with no real market impact. It provides trading members an opportunity to validate their EGR trading systems, ETI API connectivity, and response to exceptional market conditions such as risk-reduction mode and trading halts. No financial obligations or liabilities arise from participation.
