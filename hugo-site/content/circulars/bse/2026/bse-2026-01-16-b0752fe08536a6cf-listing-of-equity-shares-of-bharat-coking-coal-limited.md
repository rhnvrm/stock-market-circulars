## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Bharat Coking Coal Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544678, trading symbol BHARATCOAL, and ISIN number INE05XR01022. The exact listing date and detailed security information will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Bharat Coking Coal Limited
- Scrip Code: 544678
- Trading Symbol: BHARATCOAL
- ISIN Number: INE05XR01022
- Listing status: To be listed in due course
- Notice issued to all trading members of BSE
- Separate notice will provide listing date and security details

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the security details for Bharat Coking Coal Limited in preparation for its upcoming listing on the Exchange.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: January 16, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a new equity listing that will expand the universe of tradeable securities on BSE. As a coking coal company, this listing adds to the coal and mining sector representation on the exchange. The impact is moderate as this is an advance notification with the actual listing date pending. Trading members and market participants should watch for the follow-up notice announcing the specific listing date and additional security details before trading can commence.
