## Summary

BSE has updated the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework under Notice No. 20260525-52. Securities listed in Annexure I have met the ESM inclusion criteria and will attract a minimum 100% margin requirement and be moved from Rolling Settlement to Trade-for-Trade settlement. Some securities are being downgraded to Stage I, while others are exiting the framework entirely.

## Key Points

- Securities in Annexure I will attract minimum **100% margin** on all open positions as on May 26, 2026 and new positions from May 27, 2026.
- Annexure I also includes securities being moved to a **lower Stage (Stage I)** under the ESM Framework, effective May 26, 2026.
- All ESM-qualifying securities will shift from **Rolling Settlement to Trade-for-Trade** segment w.e.f. May 27, 2026.
- Securities moving to **Stage II** will be subject to Trade-for-Trade with a **2% price band** and **Periodic Call Auction**, effective May 26, 2026.
- Securities exiting the ESM Framework (Annexure II) are effective May 26, 2026.
- A consolidated list of all securities currently under the ESM framework is provided in Annexure III.
- ESM operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures.
- Inclusion in ESM is a market surveillance action and should **not** be construed as an adverse action against the company.

## Regulatory Changes

This notice is issued in continuation of prior ESM notices: 20230602-44, 20230718-46, 20240809-42, 20240920-63, 20241004-65, and 20250725-61. The current update revises the list of securities under each ESM stage and applies updated margin and settlement conditions accordingly.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members** must ensure collection of minimum 100% margin on open and new positions in newly included ESM securities from the applicable effective dates.
- Members must note the shift to Trade-for-Trade settlement and the 2% price band with Periodic Call Auction for Stage II securities.
- Price bands for securities exiting the ESM framework will be reinstated to pre-ESM levels, unless the security remains under another surveillance measure.
- For clarifications, members may contact: **bse.surv@bseindia.com**

## Important Dates

- **May 26, 2026**: Stage I reclassification effective; Stage II price band (2%) and Periodic Call Auction effective; securities exit from ESM framework effective.
- **May 27, 2026**: 100% margin requirement effective on all open positions (as of May 26, 2026) and new positions; Trade-for-Trade settlement shift effective.

## Impact Assessment

The ESM update significantly restricts liquidity and increases trading costs for affected securities. The mandatory 100% margin eliminates leverage for traders holding or initiating positions in these scrips. The shift to Trade-for-Trade settlement removes netting benefits and increases settlement obligations. Stage II securities face additional constraints via a tight 2% price band and Periodic Call Auction mechanism, further limiting intraday price movement. Securities exiting the framework will see trading conditions normalized, improving liquidity and reducing compliance burden for members.
