## Summary

In conjunction with Exchange Circular No. 20260622-1, ICCL (Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd.) will operate its clearing and settlement functions from its Disaster Recovery (DR) Site from Monday, June 22, 2026 through Tuesday, June 23, 2026. All major segments are covered under this DR activation.

## Key Points

- ICCL DR Site will be operational from June 22, 2026 (Monday) to June 23, 2026 (Tuesday)
- Covers Equity Cash Segment, Equity Derivatives Segment, SLB, Currency Derivatives Segment, Commodity Derivatives Segment, Debt, and EGR Segment
- Members and custodians will experience no change in accessing ICCL applications or URLs
- This notice is issued in reference to Exchange Circular No. 20260622-1 dated June 22, 2026
- Schedule of operations follows the timeline mentioned in the Exchange circular

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational continuity notice for a temporary shift to DR infrastructure.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and custodians should note the DR Site activation and continue normal operations
- No action required regarding system access or URLs — existing ICCL application URLs remain unchanged
- Members should refer to Exchange Circular No. 20260622-1 for the detailed schedule of trading/settlement timings during the DR period

## Important Dates

- **June 22, 2026 (Monday):** DR Site operations begin
- **June 23, 2026 (Tuesday):** DR Site operations end

## Impact Assessment

Operational impact is minimal for members and custodians as ICCL has confirmed no changes to application access URLs. All segments — Equity Cash, Equity Derivatives, SLB, Currency Derivatives, Commodity Derivatives, Debt, and EGR — remain accessible through normal channels. The DR activation is a planned infrastructure resilience measure; clearing and settlement are expected to proceed per the schedule in the companion circular (20260622-1).
