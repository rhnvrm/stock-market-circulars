## Summary

BSE Index Services Private Ltd. announced constituent changes in two fixed income indices: the BSE 4–8 Year G-Sec Index and the BSE INDIA 10 Year Sovereign Bond Index. The changes are effective Friday, May 29, 2026. The BSE 8–13-year G-Sec Index remains unchanged.

## Key Points

- Two BSE fixed income indices are being rebalanced effective May 29, 2026
- BSE 4–8-year G-Sec Index: one security added, one dropped
- BSE INDIA 10 Year Sovereign Bond Index: one security added, one dropped
- BSE 8–13-year G-Sec Index remains unchanged
- Changes announced by BSE Index Services Private Ltd. (formerly Asia Index Pvt. Ltd.)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory rule changes. This is a routine periodic rebalancing of fixed income indices based on maturity and eligibility criteria.

## Compliance Requirements

Fund managers, ETF operators, and index-linked portfolio managers tracking the affected indices should update their portfolios to reflect the new constituents by the effective date of May 29, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Announcement Date:** May 21, 2026
- **Effective Date:** May 29, 2026 (Friday)

|--------|------|----------|---------------|

The rebalancing reflects updated on-the-run benchmark securities for each tenor bucket. Investors and fund managers benchmarking against these indices will need to adjust holdings accordingly. Impact is limited to fixed income market participants.
