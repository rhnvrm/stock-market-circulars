## Summary

PVV INFRA LIMITED (Scrip Code: 536659) has announced a rights issue with Record Date set for February 5, 2026. Existing shareholders will be entitled to subscribe to 6 new equity shares for every 7 equity shares held. The shares will be issued at par value of Rs.5/- per share. Trading in the company's equity shares will be conducted on an ex-rights basis effective February 5, 2026 (Settlement No. DR-814/2025-2026).

## Key Points

- Record Date: February 5, 2026
- Rights Ratio: 6 new equity shares for every 7 existing shares held
- Issue Price: Rs.5/- per share (at par)
- Face Value: Rs.5/- per equity share
- Payment Structure: Rs.1.25 per share on application, Rs.3.75 per share on first and final call
- Ex-Rights Trading: Commences February 5, 2026
- Settlement Number: DR-814/2025-2026
- Scrip Code: 536659

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a corporate action notification regarding a rights issue by PVV INFRA LIMITED.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all transactions in PVV INFRA LIMITED equity shares are conducted on an ex-rights basis from February 5, 2026 onwards
- Members must note the record date and settlement number for processing trades
- Shareholders eligible as on the record date will receive rights entitlements

## Important Dates

- **February 1, 2026**: Notice date
- **February 5, 2026**: Record date for rights issue eligibility
- **February 5, 2026**: Ex-rights trading commences (Settlement No. DR-814/2025-2026)

## Impact Assessment

**Shareholder Impact**: Existing shareholders of PVV INFRA LIMITED as of the record date will have the opportunity to subscribe to additional shares at par value. The rights issue offers a 6:7 ratio, representing an 85.71% increase in shareholding for those who fully subscribe.

**Market Impact**: Trading will be adjusted to ex-rights basis from February 5, 2026. The theoretical ex-rights price will be lower than the cum-rights price to reflect the dilution from the rights issue.

**Payment Terms**: The staggered payment structure (Rs.1.25 on application and Rs.3.75 on call) allows shareholders to subscribe with partial upfront payment, with the balance payable later on first and final call.
