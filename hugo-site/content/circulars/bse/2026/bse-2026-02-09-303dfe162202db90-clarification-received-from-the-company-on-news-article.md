## Summary

Kalyan Jewellers India Limited responded to BSE Surveillance's query regarding a news article published on Moneycontrol. The company confirmed it is not aware of any undisclosed price-sensitive information that could explain the news details and that there are no regulatory or legal proceedings to report.

## Key Points

- BSE Surveillance requested clarification via email ref. L/SURV/ONL/RV/SG/(2025-2026)/182 dated February 09, 2026
- Company confirmed no undisclosed information exists that has not been announced to exchanges
- No regulatory or legal proceedings initiated or concluded
- Company reaffirmed commitment to disclosure obligations under SEBI LODR Regulation 30
- Response submitted by Company Secretary & Compliance Officer Jishnu RG

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

The company reiterated its ongoing obligation to inform stock exchanges about price-sensitive information as required under Regulation 30 of SEBI (Listing Obligation and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations 2015.

## Important Dates

- **February 09, 2026**: Date of BSE query and company response

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine compliance disclosure with no material market impact. The clarification confirms business-as-usual operations with no undisclosed developments. Such surveillance queries are standard exchange procedures to ensure market transparency when news articles circulate about listed companies.
