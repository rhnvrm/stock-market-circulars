## Summary

BSE's Surveillance department (Notice No. 20260512-32) has revised price bands for 18 equity scrips effective May 13, 2026. Fourteen scrips have been moved to a 5% price band and four scrips to a stricter 2% price band, limiting the permissible daily price movement range for these securities.

## Key Points

- Price band revisions are effective from May 13, 2026
- 14 scrips revised to a 5% price band
- 4 scrips revised to a tighter 2% price band (Fundviser Capital, Genus Prime Infra, Tejassvi Aaharam, Trade Wings)
- Action initiated by BSE Surveillance department (DOSS)
- Applies to the Equity segment
- Queries to be directed to bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

Price bands have been revised from their existing levels for the listed scrips. Price bands cap the maximum permissible intraday price movement, and tightening them is a standard BSE surveillance tool to manage excessive volatility or suspected price manipulation in specific securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the revised price bands and ensure orders placed on or after May 13, 2026 comply with the new limits
- No action required from retail investors directly, but orders outside the new band will be rejected by the exchange
- Members with queries may contact BSE Surveillance at bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** May 12, 2026
- **Effective Date:** May 13, 2026
