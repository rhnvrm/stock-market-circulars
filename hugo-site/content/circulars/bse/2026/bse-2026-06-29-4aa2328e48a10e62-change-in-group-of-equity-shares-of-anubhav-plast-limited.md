## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of Anubhav Plast Limited (Scrip Code: 544800) will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment, effective Monday, July 13, 2026. The dealings in the company's shares will subsequently be placed under the M Group. This notice is in continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20260625-25 dated June 25, 2026.

## Key Points

- Equity shares of Anubhav Plast Limited (Scrip Code: 544800) are being moved out of the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group).
- Effective July 13, 2026, the scrip will trade under the Rolling segment, M Group.
- This is an SME segment listed company.
- Contact for queries: Mr. Anurag Jain, Tel. No. 022-2272 8822.

## Regulatory Changes

The scrip's trading classification is changing from MT Group (Trade for Trade) to M Group (Rolling settlement). Under Trade for Trade, each transaction must result in delivery and cannot be netted against other positions. The move to Rolling segment allows normal netting and settlement procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members should update their systems and client communications to reflect the new group classification for Anubhav Plast Limited (Scrip Code: 544800) effective July 13, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **June 25, 2026**: Original Exchange Notice No. 20260625-25 issued.
- **June 29, 2026**: Current notice date confirming the group change.
- **July 13, 2026**: Effective date for transfer from MT Group (Trade for Trade) to M Group (Rolling segment).

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative reclassification with limited market impact. The move from Trade for Trade to Rolling settlement generally eases trading restrictions, potentially increasing liquidity for the scrip. It affects only participants trading in Anubhav Plast Limited on the BSE SME platform. No broader market or systemic impact is anticipated.
