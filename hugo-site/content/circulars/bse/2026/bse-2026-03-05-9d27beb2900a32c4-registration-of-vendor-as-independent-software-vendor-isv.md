## Summary

BSE has registered Mugenesys Software Private Limited as an Independent Software Vendor (ISV) with vendor code 81, authorized to provide Trading frontend services in the Equity Segment. Members are informed of this new vendor's availability for trading frontend solutions.

## Key Points

- Mugenesys Software Private Limited has been registered as an ISV with BSE under vendor code 81
- The vendor is approved to provide Trading frontend services in the Equity Segment
- Contact person: Tushar Jain, reachable at 8879958888 or tushar@mugenesys.com
- Vendor address: Unit No1B, 9th Floor, A Wing, Times Square Building, Andheri Kurla Road, Marol Naka, Maharashtra, Mumbai-400059
- The complete list of registered ISVs is available on the BSE website

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine vendor registration announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members. Members may optionally engage Mugenesys Software Private Limited for trading frontend services in the Equity Segment.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 05 March 2026 (Notice No. 20260305-31)
- Registration is effective as of the notice date.

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This notice expands the list of BSE-approved ISVs available to members for trading frontend solutions in the Equity Segment. Members seeking trading frontend services now have an additional registered vendor option.
