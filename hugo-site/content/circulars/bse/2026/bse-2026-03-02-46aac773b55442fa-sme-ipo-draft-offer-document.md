## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document (DRHP) of **Navkkar Gold & Diamond Limited** under the SME segment. The document was filed on February 28, 2026, and contains all disclosures as required under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations. It is available on the BSE SME website for public review and comment for a minimum period of 21 days from the date of notice.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Navkkar Gold & Diamond Limited on February 28, 2026
- Document hosted on BSE SME website in compliance with Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Public comments are invited within 21 days from March 02, 2026 (deadline: approximately March 23, 2026)
- Comments to be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This notice is issued in accordance with existing ICDR (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, which mandate public availability of the Draft Offer Document for at least 21 days to solicit comments.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Public/Investors:** May review the Draft Offer Document on the BSE SME website and submit comments to equity.ipo@bseindia.com within 21 days
- **Navkkar Gold & Diamond Limited:** Must ensure the DRHP remains accessible on the exchange website for the stipulated period
- **BSE:** Responsible for hosting the document and facilitating the public comment process per ICDR norms

## Important Dates

- **Filing Date:** February 28, 2026
- **Notice Date:** March 02, 2026
- **Public Comment Deadline:** March 23, 2026 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine procedural notice with limited market-wide impact. It pertains exclusively to the proposed SME IPO of Navkkar Gold & Diamond Limited. The notice enables transparency in the IPO process by inviting public scrutiny of the draft prospectus. Investors interested in the SME segment may review the DRHP to assess the company's disclosures ahead of the formal IPO launch.
