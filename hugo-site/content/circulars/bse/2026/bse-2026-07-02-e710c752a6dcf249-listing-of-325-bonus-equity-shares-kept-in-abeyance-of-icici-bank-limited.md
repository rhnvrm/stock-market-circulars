## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 325 bonus equity shares of ICICI Bank Limited (Scrip Code: 532174), which were earlier kept in abeyance, have been listed and permitted to trade with effect from Friday, July 03, 2026.

## Key Points

- Security: 325 Bonus Equity Shares of Rs. 2/- each, previously held in abeyance
- Issuer: ICICI Bank Limited
- Scrip Code: 532174
- ISIN: INE090A01021
- Distinctive number ranges specified: 15 shares (6412592494-6412592508), 10 shares (6412333258-6412333267), and 300 shares (6412488389-6412488688)
- No attachments accompanied the notice

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing action under existing bonus issue norms for shares previously withheld pending resolution of ownership or legal issues.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should update their systems to reflect the additional 325 shares becoming tradable and ensure correct settlement processing for these newly listed shares.

## Important Dates

- Listing and trading permission effective: Friday, July 3, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is negligible given the very small quantity (325 shares) involved. This is a routine administrative listing action releasing previously abeyance-held bonus shares into tradable form, with no material effect on ICICI Bank's overall float or stock price.
