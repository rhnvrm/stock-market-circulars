## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (No. 20260312-35) informing trading members that Tata Motors Passenger Vehicles Limited has fixed a record date for the redemption and interest payment of its Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs). Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debenture series from 16 March 2026.

## Key Points

- Tata Motors Passenger Vehicles Limited has fixed 16 March 2026 as the record date for its NCD series TMPVL-6.95%-31-3-26-PVT (ISIN: INE155A08431, Code: 973330)
- The purpose is redemption of debentures along with payment of interest
- Trading members are prohibited from dealing in this debenture series effective 16 March 2026
- Reference circular: DR-840/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational notice under existing BSE debt market regulations governing suspension of trading ahead of corporate actions on debentures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in TMPVL-6.95%-31-3-26-PVT (INE155A08431 / Code 973330) from 16 March 2026
- Members are required to take note of the no-dealings date and ensure compliance

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 12 March 2026
- **Record Date:** 16 March 2026
- **No Dealings From:** 16 March 2026
- **Debenture Maturity:** 31 March 2026

## Impact Assessment

This notice affects holders and traders of the specific NCD series (TMPVL-6.95%-31-3-26-PVT) in the BSE debt market segment. The suspension is temporary and routine, triggered by the upcoming redemption on 31 March 2026. Impact is limited to participants in this particular instrument; broader market impact is minimal.
