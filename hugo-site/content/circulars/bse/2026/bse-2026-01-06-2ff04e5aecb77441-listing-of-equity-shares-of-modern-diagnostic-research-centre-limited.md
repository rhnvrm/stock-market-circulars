## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Modern Diagnostic & Research Centre Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company will trade on the SME segment with scrip code 544673, symbol MDRC, and ISIN INE1HK501016. The exact listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Modern Diagnostic & Research Centre Limited
- Scrip Code: 544673
- Trading Symbol: MDRC
- ISIN Number: INE1HK501016
- Segment: SME (Small and Medium Enterprises)
- Listing status: Approved, date to be announced

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the upcoming listing. No specific compliance actions required at this stage pending the separate notice with listing date and security details.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: January 6, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement with minimal immediate market impact. The listing will add a new equity option in the healthcare diagnostics sector on the SME platform. Trading members should await the subsequent notice for exact listing date and trading commencement details.
