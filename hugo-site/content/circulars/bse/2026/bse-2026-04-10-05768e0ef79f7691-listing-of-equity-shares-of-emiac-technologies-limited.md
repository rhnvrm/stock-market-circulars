## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260410-13 informing trading members that the equity shares of Emiac Technologies Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange. The exact listing date and further security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Emiac Technologies Limited equity shares will be listed on BSE under the SME segment.
- Scrip Code: **544747**, Symbol: **EMIAC**, ISIN: **INE1OSW01015**.
- The date of listing and detailed security information will be announced via a subsequent notice.
- Notice issued by the DCS-Listing department on 10 April 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing admission notice under BSE's DCS-Listing department for the SME segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should note the scrip details (code 544747, symbol EMIAC, ISIN INE1OSW01015) in preparation for the listing.
- Await the separate notice confirming the listing date before initiating trades.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 10 April 2026
- **Listing Date:** To be announced via a separate notice.

## Impact Assessment

Limited immediate market impact as this is a preliminary listing intimation for an SME-segment company. Trading members are advised to monitor for the follow-up notice that will confirm the listing date and full security details. The listing broadens the SME segment with the addition of Emiac Technologies Limited.
