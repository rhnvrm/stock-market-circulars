## Summary

Gayatri Sugars Limited (Scrip Code: 532183) has listed 13,26,664 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at par to non-promoters on a preferential basis. These shares are permitted to trade on BSE with effect from Wednesday, January 28, 2026. The shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares and are subject to varying lock-in periods.

## Key Points

- Total shares listed: 13,26,664 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each
- Issue price: Rs. 10/- (at par)
- Issued to: Non-promoters on preferential basis
- ISIN: INE622E01023
- Scrip Code: 532183
- Trading commencement: January 28, 2026
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Two allotment dates: August 13, 2024 and October 4, 2024

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification for preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities listing and lock-in restrictions. Lock-in periods must be observed as per the details provided:

- 11,66,666 shares (Dist. Nos. 66200302-67366967): Locked-in until July 31, 2026
- 1,33,332 shares (Dist. Nos. 67366968-67500299): Locked-in until August 15, 2026
- 26,666 shares (Dist. Nos. 67500300-67526965): Locked-in until August 14, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date 1:** August 13, 2024 (1,60,002 shares)
- **Allotment Date 2:** October 4, 2024 (1,59,998 shares)
- **Trading Commencement:** January 28, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (Majority):** July 31, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (Batch 2):** August 15, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (Batch 3):** August 14, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. The listing adds 13.27 lakh equity shares to Gayatri Sugars Limited's tradeable stock, representing a preferential allotment to non-promoters. All shares are subject to lock-in periods ranging from 6 to 18 months, preventing immediate trading. The shares are issued at par value (Rs. 10/-) and carry equal voting and dividend rights. Limited immediate liquidity impact due to lock-in restrictions.
