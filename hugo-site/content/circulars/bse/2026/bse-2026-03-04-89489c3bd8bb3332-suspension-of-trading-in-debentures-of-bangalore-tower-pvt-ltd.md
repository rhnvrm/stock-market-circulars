## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Bangalore Tower Pvt Ltd has informed the Exchange of the upcoming redemption of its Non-Convertible Debentures (NCD) — BTPL-9.80%-14-3-26-PVT (Scrip Code: 974664, ISIN: INE0LQT08018). Trading in these debentures is suspended effective March 5, 2025, in anticipation of their maturity on the redemption date of March 14, 2026.

## Key Points

- Bangalore Tower Pvt Ltd's NCD (BTPL-9.80%-14-3-26-PVT) will mature on the redemption date of March 14, 2026.
- Scrip Code: 974664; ISIN: INE0LQT08018.
- Trading in the aforesaid debentures is suspended with effect from March 5, 2025 (reference: DR-833/2025-2026).
- All trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the effective suspension date.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice under existing BSE framework for suspension of trading in NCDs approaching maturity/redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must immediately cease trading in BTPL-9.80%-14-3-26-PVT (Scrip Code: 974664) effective March 5, 2025.
- Members are required to take note of the suspension and ensure no further deals are executed in the said debenture series.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** March 4, 2026
- **Suspension Effective Date:** March 5, 2025 (as stated in circular; likely intended as March 5, 2026)
- **Redemption Date:** March 14, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to trading members dealing in the debt segment on BSE who hold or trade this specific privately placed NCD of Bangalore Tower Pvt Ltd. This is a routine pre-redemption suspension with no broader market impact. Holders of the debenture will receive redemption proceeds on the maturity date of March 14, 2026.
