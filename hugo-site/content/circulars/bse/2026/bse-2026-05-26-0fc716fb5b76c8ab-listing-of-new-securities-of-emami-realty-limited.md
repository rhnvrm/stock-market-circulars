## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 82,00,000 new equity shares of Emami Realty Limited (Scrip Code: 533218, ISIN: INE778K01012) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Wednesday, May 27, 2026. These shares were issued to promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to the conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 82,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 2/- face value each issued at a premium of Rs. 126.50/- per share (total issue price Rs. 128.50/-)
- Shares issued to promoters on a preferential basis via conversion of warrants
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 43833890 to 52033889
- Date of allotment: April 8, 2026
- Trading permitted from May 27, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice for newly allotted securities pursuant to an earlier board-approved preferential allotment and warrant conversion.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to update their records. The new shares carry the same ISIN (INE778K01012) as existing equity shares and are tradable from May 27, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** April 8, 2026
- **Trading Commencement:** May 27, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry:** December 30, 2027 (all 82,00,000 shares locked in)

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 82,00,000 new equity shares to Emami Realty's tradable capital, increasing total share count. However, all newly listed shares are subject to a lock-in until December 30, 2027, meaning they cannot be sold by promoters in the near term. This limits any immediate dilution impact on existing shareholders from open-market selling. The preferential allotment at Rs. 128.50 per share reflects promoter confidence in the company.
