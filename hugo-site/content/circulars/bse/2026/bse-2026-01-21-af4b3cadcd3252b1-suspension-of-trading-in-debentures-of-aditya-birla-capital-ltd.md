## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in specific Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of ADITYA BIRLA CAPITAL LTD effective January 28, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the no-dealings date.

## Key Points

- Company: ADITYA BIRLA CAPITAL LTD
- Security Code: 974499
- ISIN: INE860H07IH9
- Security Description: ABCL-7.88%-12-2-26-PVT
- Interest Rate: 7.88%
- Record Date: January 28, 2026
- No Dealings From: January 28, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- Notice Reference: DR-807/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notice for debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures (ISIN: INE860H07IH9) from January 28, 2026
- Trading members are required to take note of the suspension and ensure compliance
- No trading activity should be executed in these debentures on or after the no-dealings date

## Important Dates

- **January 21, 2026**: Notice date
- **January 28, 2026**: Record date for redemption and interest payment
- **January 28, 2026**: Trading suspension effective date (no dealings from this date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders of the specific debenture series (ABCL-7.88%-12-2-26-PVT). This is a routine corporate action that affects only the debt segment and not equity markets.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders with record date positions will receive redemption proceeds and final interest payment. Trading suspension ensures proper settlement of redemption process.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update their systems to block trading in this specific ISIN from January 28, 2026. Standard procedure for debenture maturity and redemption.
