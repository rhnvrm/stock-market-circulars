## Summary

BSE has announced Demat Auction 834, dated March 9, 2026, listing 78 scrips with specified quantities up for auction settlement. This is a routine demat auction conducted by the Bombay Stock Exchange to handle delivery shortfalls in the cash segment.

## Key Points

- Settlement No. 834 dated 09/03/2026
- 78 scrips listed across diverse sectors including pharma, finance, infrastructure, FMCG, and technology
- Notable quantities include DSM Fresh Foods (8,400), SOFCOM SYSTEMS (13,798), SELLWIN TRADERS (9,800), LT FOODS (6,900), DECCAN GOLD (5,000), and LEADING LEASING (18,402)
- Scrip codes range across BSE-listed equities from large-caps to SME-listed entities

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational auction notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Brokers and clearing members with delivery shortfalls in the listed scrips must participate or settle obligations as per BSE demat auction procedures
- Members should refer to their respective settlement obligations for Settlement No. 834

## Important Dates

- Auction Date: March 9, 2026
- Settlement No.: 834

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited to brokers and investors involved in the specific scrips listed. The auction covers a wide range of 78 securities with varying quantities. Largest auction quantities are in LEADING LEASING (18,402 shares), SOFCOM SYSTEMS (13,798 shares), DSM FRESH FOODS (8,400 shares), SELLWIN TRADERS (9,800 shares), and LT FOODS (6,900 shares), which may see temporary price pressure due to auction settlement dynamics. Overall market impact is low as this is a standard settlement mechanism.
