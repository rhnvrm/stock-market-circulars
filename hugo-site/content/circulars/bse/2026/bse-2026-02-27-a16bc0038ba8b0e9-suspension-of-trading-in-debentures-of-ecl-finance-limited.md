## Summary

BSE has issued Notice DR-831/2025-2026 directing trading members to cease dealing in the 9.65% Non-Convertible Debentures of ECL Finance Limited (Scrip Code: 953677, ISIN: INE804I07O32) with effect from 02 March 2026. The suspension is in connection with the company's fixed Record Date for the redemption of these debentures.

## Key Points

- ECL Finance Limited has fixed 02 March 2026 as the Record Date for redemption of its 9.65% NCDs
- Debenture series: ECL-9.65%-18-03-26-PVT (ISIN: INE804I07O32, Scrip Code: 953677)
- Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from 02 March 2026
- Notice reference: DR-831/2025-2026
- Issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President â€“ Listing Compliance and Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational circular implementing existing BSE procedures for debenture redemption and the associated trading suspension.

## Compliance Requirements

- All trading members of BSE must immediately cease dealing in ECL Finance Limited's NCDs (Scrip Code: 953677) from 02 March 2026
- Members should update their systems and inform relevant desks to block transactions on this security from the effective date
- Members holding or facilitating client positions in this debenture must take note of the no-dealings restriction

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** 27 February 2026
- **Record Date for Redemption:** 02 March 2026
- **No Dealings From:** 02 March 2026
- **Scheduled Maturity Date (implied by series name):** 18 March 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to trading members and investors specifically holding or transacting in this debenture series. The suspension is a routine pre-redemption measure ensuring clean settlement of the redemption process. Holders of ECL-9.65%-18-03-26-PVT should prepare for redemption proceeds as per the company's schedule. No impact on equity trading or other debt instruments of ECL Finance Limited.
