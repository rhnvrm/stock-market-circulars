## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 3,03,030 equity shares of Som Distilleries & Breweries Ltd. (Scrip Code: 507514) issued on preferential basis to Promoters pursuant to conversion of warrants. The shares will be available for trading from Tuesday, January 27, 2026, and will rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 3,03,030 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 108/-
- Issued to Promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Issue price: Rs. 110/- per share
- Date of allotment: December 27, 2024
- ISIN: INE480C01038
- Distinguished numbers: 196503583 to 196806612
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the listing and must update their systems to reflect the availability of these shares for trading from January 27, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 27, 2024
- **Trading Commencement**: January 27, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry**: July 28, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - The listing represents a small equity dilution of 3.03 lakh shares issued to promoters on preferential basis.

**Lock-in Period**: All 3,03,030 shares are subject to lock-in until July 28, 2027, preventing any immediate trading activity by the allottees.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Standard listing procedure for shares issued pursuant to warrant conversion. No impact on trading operations or market participants beyond standard system updates.
