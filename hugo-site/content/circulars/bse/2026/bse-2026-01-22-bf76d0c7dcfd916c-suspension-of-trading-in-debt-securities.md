## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in debt securities of IL&FS Financial Services Limited (Scrip Code: 953460, ISIN: INE121H08073) effective January 22, 2026, due to the securities maturing on January 27, 2026. Trading members are advised not to deal in these securities.

## Key Points

- Debt security: IL&FS Financial Services Limited 8.90% bonds
- Scrip Code: 953460
- ISIN: INE121H08073
- Trading suspension effective from January 22, 2026
- Reference: DR-805/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine suspension due to debt security maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealing in the specified debt securities of IL&FS Financial Services Limited
- Effective date: January 22, 2026
- Trading members must take note of this suspension

## Important Dates

- **Suspension Effective Date:** January 22, 2026
- **Maturity Date:** January 27, 2026
- **Notice Date:** January 22, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a standard procedural suspension for a maturing debt instrument. Only affects holders and traders of this specific IL&FS Financial Services Limited debt security. No broader market implications expected.
