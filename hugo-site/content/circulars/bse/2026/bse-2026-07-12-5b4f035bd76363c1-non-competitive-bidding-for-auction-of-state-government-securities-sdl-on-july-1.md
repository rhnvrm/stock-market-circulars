## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members about the non-competitive bidding facility for the auction of State Government Securities (SDL) scheduled for July 14, 2026, in reference to the RBI press release dated July 10, 2026. This is in continuation of Exchange circular no. 20191122-28 dated November 22, 2019, regarding the launch of the non-competitive bidding facility for SDL auctions conducted by RBI. Bidding will be conducted through the NCB-GSec module of BSE's iBBS web-based system.

## Key Points

- Notice references RBI's press release dated July 10, 2026 announcing the SDL Securities Auction for July 14, 2026
- Bidding is conducted via the Exchange's iBBS web-based system (https://ibbs.bseindia.com) through the NCB-GSec module
- Securities on offer span multiple states: Andhra Pradesh, Gujarat, Maharashtra, Meghalaya, Mizoram, Punjab, Rajasthan, Tamil Nadu, and Telangana, including both new issues and re-issues
- Total notified amount across all securities spans a range of maturities (2031 to 2056) with coupons ranging from approximately 7.35% to 8.07%
- Minimum subscription size is Rs. 10,000 with multiples thereof, per security

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory change; this is an operational notice implementing the existing non-competitive bidding facility framework established under Exchange circular no. 20191122-28 (November 22, 2019), triggered by RBI's latest SDL auction announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must facilitate client bids for SDL securities within the specified bid collection windows
- Bids for direct investors must be submitted by July 13, 2026, till 5:00 pm
- Bids submitted by members must be completed by July 14, 2026, till 8:00 am
- All bidding must occur through the NCB-GSec module on BSE's iBBS platform

## Important Dates

- Bid Collection Start Date: July 13, 2026, from 10:00 am onwards (24-hour availability)
- Bid Collection End Date (Direct Investors): July 13, 2026, till 5:00 pm
- Bid Collection End Date (Members): July 14, 2026, till 8:00 am
- Auction Date: July 14, 2026
- Settlement Date: July 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This notice has a low impact on equity markets and listed stocks, as it pertains solely to debt segment operations for state government securities. Its relevance is confined to trading members and investors participating in the non-competitive bidding process for SDL auctions, with no bearing on equity trading, corporate actions, or listed company disclosures.
