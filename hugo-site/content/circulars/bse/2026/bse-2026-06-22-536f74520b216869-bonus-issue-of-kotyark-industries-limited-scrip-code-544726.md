## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Kotyark Industries Limited (Scrip Code: 544726) has fixed **24 June 2026** as the record date for a bonus issue in the ratio of **10:1** — 10 bonus equity shares of Rs. 10/- each for every 1 equity share of Rs. 10/- each held. Equity shares of the company will trade on an ex-bonus basis from 24 June 2026. A total of 102,791,160 new equity shares will be allotted on 25 June 2026.

## Key Points

- **Company:** Kotyark Industries Limited (BSE Scrip Code: 544726)
- **Bonus Ratio:** 10 new shares for every 1 share held (10:1)
- **Face Value:** Rs. 10/- per share
- **Record Date:** 24 June 2026
- **Ex-Bonus Date:** 24 June 2026 (Settlement No. DR-658/2026-2027)
- **Total Shares to be Allotted:** 102,791,160 equity shares
- **Allotment Date:** 25 June 2026
- **Notice Reference:** BSE Notice No. 20260622-6

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory policy changes. This is a corporate action notification requiring trading members to apply ex-bonus price adjustments to the company's equity shares from 24 June 2026 onward.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all transactions in Kotyark Industries Limited equity shares from 24 June 2026 are executed on an ex-bonus basis.
- Members should update their systems to reflect the revised share price post-bonus adjustment.
- Refer to the company's letter dated 19 June 2026 (attached as Undertaking_KIL and Annexure_KIL) for supporting documentation.

## Impact Assessment

The 10:1 bonus ratio is unusually large and will have a significant market impact. The share price is expected to adjust downward by approximately 90% on the ex-bonus date to reflect the tenfold increase in outstanding shares. Shareholders holding shares as of the record date (24 June 2026) will receive 10 additional shares for each share held. With over 102.79 million new shares being issued, the total share capital of Kotyark Industries will increase substantially. Traders and investors should account for the ex-bonus price adjustment and the resulting change in EPS, book value per share, and other per-share metrics.
