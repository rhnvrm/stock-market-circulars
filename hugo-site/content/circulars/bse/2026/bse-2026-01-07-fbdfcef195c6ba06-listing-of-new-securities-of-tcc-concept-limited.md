## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 1,06,10,690 new equity shares of TCC Concept Limited (Scrip Code: 512038) effective from January 8, 2026. These shares were issued at a premium of Rs. 547.94 per share to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to a share swap arrangement. The newly listed shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- **Company**: TCC Concept Limited (Scrip Code: 512038)
- **Number of Shares**: 1,06,10,690 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 557.94 per share (Rs. 10 face value + Rs. 547.94 premium)
- **Allotment Basis**: Preferential allotment to non-promoters pursuant to share swap
- **Distinctive Numbers**: 35672509 to 46283198
- **Date of Allotment**: December 8, 2025
- **ISIN**: INE887D01016
- **Status**: Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- **Lock-in Period**: All 1,06,10,690 shares locked-in until July 14, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification pursuant to preferential allotment regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to note the listing of new securities
- Lock-in restrictions apply to all allotted shares until July 14, 2026
- Shares cannot be transferred or sold during the lock-in period

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: December 8, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: January 8, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry**: July 14, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a company-specific listing event with no broader market implications.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Standard listing process for preferential allotment. The share swap has increased the outstanding share capital of TCC Concept Limited by 1.06 crore shares. The seven-month lock-in period ensures these shares will not create immediate selling pressure in the market. Trading members should note the updated share capital and lock-in restrictions when processing trades for this scrip.
