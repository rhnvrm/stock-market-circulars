## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that certain companies have fixed record dates for payment of interest and/or redemption of their Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs). Trading members have been advised not to deal in these debentures around the specified dates.

## Key Points

- Two NCD instruments are covered under this notice, both privately placed.
- Muthoot Fincorp Limited (Scrip Code 975022, Scrip ID MFL-9.10%-9-8-26-PVT, ISIN INE549K07CG7) has a record date and ex date of 24/07/2026, with settlement number DR 679/2026-2027.
- Nirmal Bang Securities Private Limited (Scrip Code 976408, Scrip ID NBSPL-10.75%-13-8-26-PVT, ISIN INE681R08109) has a record date and ex date of 29/07/2026, with settlement number DR 682/2026-2027.
- These record dates relate to upcoming interest payments and/or redemption of the respective NCDs.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is a routine administrative notice under BSE's Listing Operations department for the Debt segment, issued under the Corporate Actions category.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of the Exchange are advised not to deal in the debentures of the listed companies on and around the specified record/ex dates to ensure trades settle appropriately relative to the interest payment/redemption.

## Important Dates

- Muthoot Fincorp Limited NCD (ISIN INE549K07CG7): Record Date / Ex Date - 24 July 2026 (Settlement DR 679/2026-2027)
- Nirmal Bang Securities Private Limited NCD (ISIN INE681R08109): Record Date / Ex Date - 29 July 2026 (Settlement DR 682/2026-2027)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal broader market impact as it pertains to specific privately placed NCDs held by a limited set of debenture holders. The suspension of trading around record dates is a standard operational safeguard to ensure accurate entitlement to interest/redemption payments and does not affect equity trading or the companies' listed equity shares.
