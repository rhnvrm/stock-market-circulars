## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 28,30,188 new equity shares of AGI Infra Limited (Scrip Code: 539042, ISIN: INE976R01033) are listed and admitted for trading on the Exchange effective Friday, March 13, 2026. These shares were allotted to Qualified Institutional Buyers (QIBs) pursuant to a Qualified Institutional Placement (QIP).

## Key Points

- **Company:** AGI Infra Limited (Scrip Code: 539042, ISIN: INE976R01033)
- **Number of Shares:** 28,30,188 equity shares of Re. 1/- each
- **Issue Type:** Qualified Institutional Placement (QIP) to QIBs
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 265/- per share
- **Distinctive Numbers:** 122167201 to 124997388
- **Pari-passu:** Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- **Trading Effective Date:** Friday, March 13, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice pursuant to completion of a QIP allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities and may contact Vaidehi Phatak at Tel. 022 2272 8646 for any clarifications.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** March 10, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** March 13, 2026 (Friday)

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 28,30,188 new equity shares to the tradable float of AGI Infra Limited at an issue price of Rs. 265/-. This increases the total share capital of the company and may have a dilutive effect on existing shareholders. The QIP allotment to institutional buyers signals institutional confidence in the company. Market impact is moderate and limited to AGI Infra Limited's stock (AGIINFRA).
