## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in T-Bill 759CG2026 (Scrip Code: 800242, ISIN: IN0020150093) effective from January 7, 2026, as the security will mature on its redemption date. Trading members are advised to cease dealing in this T-Bill from the specified date.

## Key Points

- T-Bill 759CG2026 to be suspended from trading
- Scrip Code: 800242
- ISIN: IN0020150093
- Suspension effective from January 7, 2026
- Reason: Security maturing on redemption date
- Notice reference: DR-793/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard procedural notice for security maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal with T-Bill 759CG2026 from January 7, 2026 onwards
- Trading members should update their systems to reflect the trading suspension
- Members are required to take note of this suspension and ensure compliance

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 6, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Date**: January 7, 2026
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: On or around January 7, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a single T-Bill reaching maturity, which is a routine market event. The suspension affects only one security in the debt segment.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Trading members need to update their systems to prevent trading in this specific T-Bill. Standard end-of-life procedure for debt securities.

**Investor Impact**: Low - Investors holding this T-Bill will receive redemption proceeds as per the maturity schedule. No trading impact for other securities.
