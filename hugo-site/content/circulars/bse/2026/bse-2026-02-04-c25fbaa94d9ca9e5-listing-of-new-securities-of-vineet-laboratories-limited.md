## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 9,987,258 new equity shares of Vineet Laboratories Limited (Scrip Code: 543298) issued on a rights basis. The shares will be listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Thursday, February 5, 2026. These shares rank pari-passu with the existing equity shares of the company.

## Key Points

- 9,987,258 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 20/- per share
- Issued on rights basis to existing shareholders
- ISIN: INE505Y01010
- Distinctive Numbers: 9219009 to 19206266
- Date of Allotment: February 2, 2026
- New shares rank equally with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed about the listing and must ensure proper trading arrangements for these new securities from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: February 2, 2026
- **Trading Commencement Date**: February 5, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement following a rights issue by Vineet Laboratories Limited. The addition of approximately 10 million new shares will increase the company's equity base. The impact is limited to this specific company and its shareholders. Trading members need to update their systems to reflect the expanded share capital. The shares being pari-passu ensures equal rights for all shareholders.
