## Summary

BSE has issued a revised live activities schedule for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for shares of SANMITRA COMMERCIAL LIMITED. The offer is scheduled from Tuesday, January 6, 2026 to Tuesday, January 20, 2026. Trading members and custodian members are requested to note the detailed schedule of activities and operational instructions for participation.

## Key Points

- **Security**: SANMITRA COMMERCIAL LIMITED (Symbol: ZSANMCOM, ISIN: INE896J01014, Scrip Code: 512062)
- **Offer Size**: Up to 1,49,76,000 equity shares
- **Offer Price**: Rs. 15.00 per share
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10.00 per share
- **Market Lot**: 1 share
- **Bid Offering Period**: January 6-20, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Give-up/Take-up Period**: January 6-19, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Last Day Give-up**: January 20, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Last Day Take-up**: January 20, 2026 (9:15 AM to 4:00 PM)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a revised schedule notification for an ongoing corporate action.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure UCC of investors is registered and/or updated with correct PAN Number and Category in Exchange UCC database
- Trading members can only bid for investors having shares in demat form
- Members must refer to Exchange Notices 20150930-1 (September 30, 2015) and 20170203-29 (February 3, 2017) for operational instructions
- Members and custodians to note that if no securities are accepted, no End of Issue files will be generated in Extranet

## Important Dates

- **Opening Date**: January 6, 2026
- **Closing Date**: January 20, 2026
- **Bid Offering**: January 6-20, 2026 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Give-up/Take-up**: January 6-19, 2026 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Last Day Give-up**: January 20, 2026 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Last Day Take-up**: January 20, 2026 (9:15 AM - 4:00 PM)
