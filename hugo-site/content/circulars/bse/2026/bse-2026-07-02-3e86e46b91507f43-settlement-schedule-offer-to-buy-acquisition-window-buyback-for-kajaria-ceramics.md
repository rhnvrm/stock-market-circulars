## Summary

BSE Clearing Corporation (BSECL)/ICCL has issued the settlement calendar for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Buyback) mechanism for Kajaria Ceramics Limited, to be conducted from July 3, 2026 to July 9, 2026. The notice specifies the bidding period, obligation download date, and final settlement date, along with instructions for early pay-in of demat shares.

## Key Points

- Settlement Type: Buyback
- Settlement Number: 2026081
- Bid Start Date: 03/07/2026
- Bid End Date: 09/07/2026
- Obligation Download Date: 15/07/2026
- Settlement Date: 16/07/2026
- Demat shares must be delivered only through the Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism, available in both depositories (CDSL & NSDL).
- Members/Custodians must use Market Type "(Buyback)" and Settlement No. 2026081 in the depository system.
- Early pay-in securities will be earmarked/blocked in the client's account under the requisite market type and settlement number; no EPN file upload is required.
- Members/Custodians are directed to refer to Notice No. 20200528-32 dated May 28, 2020 for revised guidelines on settlement mechanism for acquisition of shares through the Stock Exchange.
- Members should refer to the MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for client-wise stamp duty charges.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this notice applies existing guidelines under Notice No. 20200528-32 (May 28, 2020) governing the mechanism for acquisition of shares through the stock exchange (buyback/takeover acquisition window).

## Compliance Requirements

- Participants must ensure early pay-in of securities is reflected in BSE/ICCL systems before the prescribed timelines for bid acceptance/confirmation on the OTB platform.
- Early pay-in must be completed in the depository system well before the closure of the BSE OTB bidding window (members) and confirmation window (custodians).
- Members/Custodians must use the correct Market Type and Settlement Number when processing shares in the depository system.
- No EPN file upload is required for CDSL/NSDL early pay-in securities.

## Important Dates

- Bid Start Date: July 3, 2026
- Bid End Date: July 9, 2026
- Obligation Download: July 15, 2026
- Settlement Date: July 16, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, operational settlement notice affecting members, custodians, and shareholders participating in the Kajaria Ceramics Limited buyback via the Acquisition Window mechanism. It has limited broader market impact but is important for ensuring timely and accurate settlement for participants tendering shares in the buyback offer.
