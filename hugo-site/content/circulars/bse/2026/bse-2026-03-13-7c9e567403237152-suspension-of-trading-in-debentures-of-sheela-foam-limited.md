## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members that Sheela Foam Limited has fixed a record date of March 20, 2026 for the redemption and interest payment of its Non-Convertible Debentures (NCD) series SFL-8.45%-6-4-26-PVT (ISIN: INE916U08038, Code: 975147). Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from March 20, 2026 onwards.

## Key Points

- Sheela Foam Limited has fixed March 20, 2026 as the record date for its NCD series SFL-8.45%-6-4-26-PVT
- ISIN: INE916U08038 | BSE Code: 975147
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- No dealings are permitted in these debentures from March 20, 2026
- Notice reference: DR-844/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice under BSE's debt segment procedures for suspending trading ahead of a debenture redemption event.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must refrain from dealing in Sheela Foam Limited's NCD (INE916U08038 / Code 975147) from March 20, 2026 onwards
- Members are required to take note of the suspension and ensure no transactions are executed in this security on or after the no-dealings date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** March 13, 2026
- **Record Date:** March 20, 2026
- **No Dealings From:** March 20, 2026
- **Purpose on Record Date:** Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and traders of this specific NCD series of Sheela Foam Limited. The suspension is a routine pre-redemption measure ensuring clean settlement. Upon redemption, the debenture will cease to exist, and trading members should update their systems accordingly to prevent erroneous orders in this security post-suspension.
