## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non Convertible Debentures of JM Financial Credit Solutions Ltd (JFCSL-7.26 GS 2032-2-3-26-PVT, ISIN: INE651J07887, Scrip Code: 974577) effective February 10, 2025. The suspension follows the company's notification to the exchange regarding full redemption of these debentures through buyback.

## Key Points

- JM Financial Credit Solutions Ltd has completed full redemption of its Non Convertible Debentures via buyback
- Affected debenture: JFCSL-7.26 GS 2032-2-3-26-PVT (ISIN: INE651J07887, Scrip Code: 974577)
- Trading suspension effective from February 10, 2025
- Trading members advised not to deal in the specified debentures
- Reference number: DR-817/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard procedure following complete redemption of debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute trades in the suspended debentures from February 10, 2025 onwards
- Trading members are required to take note of this suspension
- All market participants must ensure compliance with the trading suspension directive

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** February 09, 2026
- **Effective Date of Trading Suspension:** February 10, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders of this specific debenture issue. The suspension is a procedural action following full redemption and buyback completion. Trading members should update their systems to prevent inadvertent trading in these securities. No broader market or systemic impact expected.
