## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 31,64,653 new equity shares of SEPC LIMITED (Scrip Code: 532945) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Monday, May 25, 2026. The shares were issued on a preferential basis to Non-Promoters pursuant to conversion of Compulsorily Convertible Debentures (CCDs).

## Key Points

- 31,64,653 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 3.24/- per share
- Issue price: Rs. 13.24/- per share
- Issued to Non-Promoters on a preferential basis via CCD conversion
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- ISIN: INE964H01014
- Distribution Numbers: 1940158104 to 1943322756

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification following allotment of new securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the listing and permit trading in the new securities from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: November 14, 2025
- Trading Effective From: May 25, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. The new shares are pari-passu with existing equity and represent a modest addition to the tradeable float of SEPC LIMITED. The CCD conversion was allotted to Non-Promoters on a preferential basis, which marginally increases the non-promoter holding in the company.
