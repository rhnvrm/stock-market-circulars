## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260529-6 informing members that certain mutual fund schemes will observe Non-Business Days (NBD) during June 2026 on the BSE StAR MF platform. The affected schemes have been identified by their respective AMCs, and transactions in those schemes will be suspended on the specified NBD dates.

## Key Points

- Certain mutual fund schemes will have Non-Business Days in June 2026 as communicated by the respective fund houses to BSE.
- Subscription, Redemption, Switch, SIP, Normal STP, SWP, and AMC-STP transactions will not be permitted under affected schemes on NBD dates via BSE StAR MF.
- The specific scheme-wise NBD dates are provided in the attached annexure titled **NON_BUSINESS_JUN_2026**.
- The notice is addressed to all BSE members dealing in mutual fund transactions.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is a routine operational notification in line with standing mutual fund industry practice of observing Non-Business Days for certain schemes.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and distributors must ensure that no transactions (Subscription, Redemption, Switch, SIP, Normal STP, SWP, or AMC-STP) are placed for the affected schemes on the designated NBD dates through the BSE StAR MF platform.
- Members should refer to the attached annexure **NON_BUSINESS_JUN_2026** to identify the specific schemes and their corresponding NBD dates.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 29 May 2026
- **Applicable Period:** June 2026 (specific NBD dates as per attached annexure)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has a moderate operational impact on investors, distributors, and members who transact through the BSE StAR MF platform. Investors in affected schemes will be unable to place transactions on the specified Non-Business Days, which may require advance planning for SIPs and systematic transactions. The impact is limited to the listed schemes and does not affect the broader market or non-mutual-fund segments.
