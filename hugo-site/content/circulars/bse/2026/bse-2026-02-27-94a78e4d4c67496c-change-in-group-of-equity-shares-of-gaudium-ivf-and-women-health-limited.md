## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Gaudium IVF and Women Health Limited (Scrip Code: 544709) will be transferred from the Trade for Trade (T Group) segment to the Rolling segment under B Group, effective Monday, March 16, 2026. This notice follows the Exchange's earlier Notice No. 20260226-41 dated February 26, 2026.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 544709 (Gaudium IVF and Women Health Limited)
- Transfer from Trade for Trade (T Group) to Rolling segment
- Dealings will be classified under B Group post-transfer
- Effective date: Monday, March 16, 2026
- Continuation of earlier Notice No. 20260226-41 dated February 26, 2026
- Contact person: Mr. Parag Jain, Tel. No. 022 2272 8685

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares of Gaudium IVF and Women Health Limited are being moved out of the Trade for Trade (T Group) segment into the Rolling segment. In the T Group, each trade must result in actual delivery and netting of positions is not permitted. The shift to Rolling segment (B Group) allows for standard rolling settlement with netting benefits.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must take note of the segment change effective March 16, 2026
- Members should update their systems and client communications to reflect the new B Group classification
- Members may contact Mr. Parag Jain at 022 2272 8685 for further clarifications

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: February 27, 2026
- Prior Notice Reference: 20260226-41, dated February 26, 2026
- Effective Date of Segment Transfer: Monday, March 16, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This change improves trading flexibility for Gaudium IVF and Women Health Limited shares. Moving from T Group (trade-for-trade, no netting) to B Group (rolling settlement with netting) typically signals an improvement in the compliance or surveillance standing of the company. Traders and investors will benefit from standard rolling settlement, improved liquidity, and the ability to net positions. Members with existing positions should be aware of the change in settlement mechanics starting March 16, 2026.
