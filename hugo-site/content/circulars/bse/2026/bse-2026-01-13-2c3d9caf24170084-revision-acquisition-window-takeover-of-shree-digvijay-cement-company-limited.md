## Summary

BSE has revised and extended the Acquisition Window (Takeover) period for Shree Digvijay Cement Company Limited. The acquisition window, originally scheduled to conclude on Friday, January 16, 2026, has been extended to Monday, January 19, 2026. This extension excludes January 15, 2026 (Thursday), which has been declared as an exchange holiday due to Municipal Corporation Elections in Maharashtra.

## Key Points

- Acquisition window for Shree Digvijay Cement Company Limited extended by one trading day
- Extension from January 16, 2026 to January 19, 2026
- January 15, 2026 excluded from trading due to exchange holiday
- Exchange holiday declared for Municipal Corporation Elections in Maharashtra
- Circular issued in continuation of notice no. 20251231-35 dated December 31, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a procedural adjustment to accommodate the exchange holiday during the acquisition window period.

## Compliance Requirements

- All market participants must note the revised acquisition window timeline
- Trading in the acquisition window will not occur on January 15, 2026
- Participants should adjust their takeover-related activities according to the extended timeline

## Important Dates

- **January 13, 2026**: Notice issued
- **January 15, 2026**: Exchange holiday (Municipal Corporation Elections in Maharashtra) - No trading
- **January 16, 2026**: Original acquisition window end date
- **January 19, 2026**: Extended acquisition window end date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The extension provides additional time for shareholders to participate in the acquisition window process for Shree Digvijay Cement Company Limited.

**Operational Impact**: The one-day extension (excluding holiday) ensures that market participants have adequate time to complete takeover-related transactions despite the exchange holiday interruption.

**Affected Parties**: Shareholders of Shree Digvijay Cement Company Limited, potential acquirers, brokers, and trading members involved in the takeover process.
