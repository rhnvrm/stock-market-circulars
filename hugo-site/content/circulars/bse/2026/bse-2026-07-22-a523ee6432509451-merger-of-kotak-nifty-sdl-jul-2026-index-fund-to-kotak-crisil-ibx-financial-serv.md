## Summary

BSE has notified members of the merger of KOTAK NIFTY SDL JUL 2026 INDEX FUND (Transferor Scheme) into KOTAK CRISIL-IBX FINANCIAL SERVICES 9 TO 12 MONTHS DEBT INDEX FUND (Transferee Scheme), based on communication received from Kotak Mahindra Mutual Fund. The merger becomes effective on July 31, 2026, after close of business hours, and is being implemented through the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Key Points

- KOTAK NIFTY SDL JUL 2026 INDEX FUND will merge into KOTAK CRISIL-IBX FINANCIAL SERVICES 9 TO 12 MONTHS DEBT INDEX FUND.
- Investors opposed to the merger can redeem or switch to other Kotak Mahindra Mutual Fund schemes without exit load during the specified window.
- Fresh subscriptions (lump sum, additional investments, switch-in, AMC STP-in) and SIP/XSIP registrations will continue to be accepted in the Transferor Scheme during the exit load period.
- Existing systematic registrations (SIP/XSIP, AMC STP, SWP) on BSE StAR MF in the Transferor Scheme will shift to the corresponding Plan/Option of the Transferee Scheme after close of business on July 31, 2026.
- The Transferor Scheme will cease to be available for transactions on BSE StAR MF after close of business on July 31, 2026.
- Redemptions and switch-outs will continue to be allowed in the Transferor Scheme up to the timeline permitted by the AMC.
- Members should refer to the attached Annexure and Notice-cum-Addendum for complete scheme details.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory change is introduced; this is an AMC-initiated scheme merger being operationally implemented on the BSE StAR MF Platform per SEBI mutual fund scheme merger norms.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members/distributors must inform investors of their right to exit without load and process redemption/switch requests during the exit window.
- Members must ensure fresh subscriptions and systematic transactions in the Transferor Scheme are handled correctly until the effective date.
- Members must update systematic registrations to reflect the shift to the Transferee Scheme's corresponding Plan/Option post-merger.

## Important Dates

- Exit load-free window: July 02, 2026 to July 31, 2026 (both days inclusive).
- Merger effective date: July 31, 2026, after close of business hours.
- Transferor Scheme ceases for transactions on BSE StAR MF: after close of business on July 31, 2026.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to unitholders and distributors dealing in the Transferor Scheme via BSE StAR MF; it does not affect listed equity trading or broader market operations. Investors need to act within the stipulated window if they do not wish to continue in the merged scheme, while systematic investment plans will automatically transition to the Transferee Scheme thereafter.
