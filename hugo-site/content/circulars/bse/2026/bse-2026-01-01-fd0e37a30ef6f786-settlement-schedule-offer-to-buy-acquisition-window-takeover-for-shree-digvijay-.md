## Summary

BSE has announced the settlement calendar for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for Shree Digvijay Cement Company Limited. The takeover bidding window will run from January 5, 2026 to January 16, 2026, with obligation download on January 30, 2026 and final settlement on February 2, 2026. Settlement number 2026001 has been assigned to this takeover.

## Key Points

- Takeover bidding period: January 5, 2026 to January 16, 2026
- Settlement Type: Takeover (Settlement No. 2026001)
- Obligation Download Date: January 30, 2026
- Settlement Date: February 2, 2026
- Demat shares must be delivered only through Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism available in both depositories
- Market Type "(Takeover)" and settlement number 2026001 must be used by Members/Custodians in the depository system
- CDSL & NSDL early pay-in securities will be earmarked/blocked in clients' accounts
- No EPN file upload required by Members & Custodians

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular implements the existing Revised Guidelines of Mechanism for acquisition of shares through Stock Exchange as per Notice No. 20200528-32 dated May 28, 2020.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Members/Custodians**: Must ensure early pay-in of securities is reflected in BSE/ICCL systems before prescribed timelines for accepting/confirming bids
- **Members/Custodians**: Must complete early pay-in process in Depository system sufficiently in advance before closure of BSE OTB Bidding window
- **Members**: Should refer to MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for client-wise Stamp Duty charges
- **Participants**: Must use Market Type "(Takeover)" and settlement number 2026001 in depository system
- **Members/Custodians**: Should refer to Notice No. 20200528-32 dated May 28, 2020 regarding settlement guidelines

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - affects shareholders and trading members dealing with Shree Digvijay Cement Company Limited during the takeover period.

**Operational Impact**: Members and Custodians need to ensure timely early pay-in of securities through the depository mechanism. The 12-day bidding window provides adequate time for shareholders to participate in the acquisition window. Proper coordination with CDSL/NSDL is essential to ensure securities are earmarked before bidding deadlines.
