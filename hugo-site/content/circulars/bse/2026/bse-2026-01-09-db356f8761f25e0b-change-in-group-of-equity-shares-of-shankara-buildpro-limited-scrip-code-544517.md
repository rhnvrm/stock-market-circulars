## Summary

BSE has notified that the equity shares of Shankara Buildpro Limited (Scrip Code - 544517) will be permitted to trade under 'B' group effective Friday, January 23, 2026. This represents a change in the trading group classification for the company's equity shares.

## Key Points

- Company: Shankara Buildpro Limited
- Scrip Code: 544517
- New Trading Group: 'B' Group
- Effective Date: Friday, January 23, 2026
- Notice Number: 20260109-9
- Notice Date: January 09, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares of Shankara Buildpro Limited will be reclassified to trade under the 'B' group on BSE. The B group typically includes companies with specific characteristics related to trading frequency, market capitalization, or compliance parameters as defined by BSE's grouping criteria.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must note the group change for trading purposes
- All trades in Shankara Buildpro Limited equity shares must be executed under B group parameters from the effective date
- Trading members should update their systems to reflect the new group classification

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 09, 2026
- **Effective Date**: January 23, 2026 (Friday)

## Impact Assessment

The change in trading group may affect the trading dynamics of Shankara Buildpro Limited shares, including potential changes in trading parameters, margin requirements, or settlement cycles associated with B group securities. Market participants and investors should be aware of this reclassification for their trading and investment decisions.
