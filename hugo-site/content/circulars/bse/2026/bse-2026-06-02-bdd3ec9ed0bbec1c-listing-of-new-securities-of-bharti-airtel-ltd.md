## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 4,366 new equity shares of Bharti Airtel Ltd. (Scrip Code: 532454, ISIN: INE397D01024) are permitted for trading effective Wednesday, June 03, 2026. These shares arise from the conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up equity shares of Rs. 5/- each, issued on a rights basis.

## Key Points

- **Company:** Bharti Airtel Ltd. (Scrip Code: 532454)
- **ISIN:** INE397D01024
- **New Shares:** 4,366 equity shares of Rs. 5/- each
- **Origin:** Conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up, issued on rights basis
- **Pari-passu:** These shares rank equally with existing equity shares of the company
- **Distinctive Numbers:** 4,154 shares (5748678797 to 5748682950) and 212 shares (5883201890 to 5883202101)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice following completion of partly paid share conversion under the rights issue framework.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are permitted to trade in the newly listed shares from June 03, 2026. No specific compliance actions are required beyond normal trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 02, 2026
- **Trading Effective Date:** Wednesday, June 03, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. Only 4,366 shares are being listed, which is an insignificant quantity relative to Bharti Airtel's total equity base. The shares are pari-passu with existing equity, so no dilution effect beyond what was already priced in at the rights issue stage. Relevant only to holders of the specific partly paid rights shares being converted.
