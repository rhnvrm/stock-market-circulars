## Summary

BSE's Department of Surveillance (DOSS) has revised price bands for 27 equity scrips effective June 25, 2026. The revisions range from tighter 2% bands (indicating heightened surveillance) to broader 20% bands, impacting trading activity in these securities.

## Key Points

- Price bands revised for 27 scrips across various sectors effective June 25, 2026
- Four scrips assigned the most restrictive 2% band: Aarey Drugs & Pharmaceuticals (524412), Panafic Industrials (538860), Sigachi Industries (543389), and Tulasee Bio Ethanol (524514)
- Three scrips assigned a wide 20% band: Sumeet Industries (751123), UHM Vacation (544779), and Vahh Chemicals (544778)
- Four scrips assigned a 10% band: Danube Industries (540361), Mkventures Capital (514238), Mohite Industries (532140), and Standard Engineering Technology (544333)
- Remaining 16 scrips assigned a 5% band
- Issued by Surveillance department; members may contact bse.surv@bseindia.com for clarifications

## Regulatory Changes

Price bands are revised by BSE Surveillance to manage volatility and protect investors. A reduction to a 2% band typically reflects increased scrutiny under surveillance frameworks, restricting the daily price movement to limit speculative activity. Broader bands (10%, 20%) may indicate relaxation of prior restrictions or reclassification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the revised price bands for affected scrips and ensure orders are placed within the new limits effective June 25, 2026
- No action required beyond awareness; the exchange enforces band limits at the system level
- For queries, members should write to bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 24, 2026
- **Effective Date:** June 25, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Traders and investors holding or planning to trade in any of the 27 affected scrips will face revised daily price movement limits starting June 25, 2026. The 2% band scrips (Aarey Drugs, Panafic Industrials, Sigachi Industries, Tulasee Bio Ethanol) face the most significant impact — intraday and positional trading strategies will be severely constrained. Scrips with widened bands (20%) may see increased participation as trading room expands. Overall market impact is limited given the small-cap nature of most affected companies.
