## Summary

BSE has announced the movement of GSL Securities Ltd. (Security Code: 530469, ISIN: INE721D01017) to GSM Stage III under the Graded Surveillance Measure framework. This represents an escalation in surveillance level, subjecting the security to stricter monitoring and trading controls.

## Key Points

- GSL Securities Ltd. (530469) has been moved to GSM Stage III
- ISIN: INE721D01017
- Only one security affected in this circular
- Securities marked with (#) may move to lower GSM stages if included in ESM Framework
- Securities marked with ($) may move to lower GSM stages if included in IBC Framework

## Regulatory Changes

The security will now be subject to enhanced surveillance measures applicable to GSM Stage III, which typically include:
- Higher Additional Surveillance Deposit (ASD) requirements
- Increased margin requirements
- Price bands or trade-for-trade settlement
- Periodic call auctions instead of continuous trading

## Compliance Requirements

- Investors and brokers must comply with enhanced margin and deposit requirements for GSL Securities Ltd.
- All trades in this security will be subject to GSM Stage III restrictions
- Market participants should review BSE's GSM framework guidelines for specific compliance obligations

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** January 8, 2026
- The surveillance measure is applicable immediately from the date of circular

## Impact Assessment

**Trading Impact:** Movement to GSM Stage III significantly impacts liquidity and trading activity. Investors will face higher costs due to increased margin requirements and potential trade-for-trade settlement.

**Investor Impact:** Existing holders may experience reduced liquidity and higher transaction costs. New investors must be aware of enhanced surveillance conditions before trading.

**Market Signal:** Placement in GSM Stage III indicates the exchange has identified concerns regarding price movements, volatility, or other market integrity issues requiring heightened monitoring.
