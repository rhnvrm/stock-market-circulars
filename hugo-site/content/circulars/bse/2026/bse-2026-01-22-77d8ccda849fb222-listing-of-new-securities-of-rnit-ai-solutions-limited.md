## Summary

BSE has listed 47,33,463 equity shares of Rnit Ai Solutions Limited (Scrip Code: 517286) issued to strategic investors on a preferential basis. The shares were allotted pursuant to a Resolution Plan approved by the National Company Law Tribunal (NCLT), Jaipur Bench, and will be available for trading from January 23, 2026. All shares are subject to lock-in until February 14, 2027.

## Key Points

- 47,33,463 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 34/-
- Issue price: Rs. 44/- per share
- Allotted to strategic investors (non-promoters) on preferential basis
- Allotment pursuant to NCLT Jaipur Bench order dated September 23, 2024
- Date of allotment: November 5, 2025
- Trading commencement: January 23, 2026
- ISIN: INE335Q01026
- Distinctive Numbers: 74792084 to 79525546
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification following NCLT-approved corporate restructuring.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants should note the new securities in Rnit Ai Solutions Limited
- All 47,33,463 shares are under complete lock-in until February 14, 2027
- No trading restrictions after lock-in period expires

## Important Dates

- **NCLT Order Date**: September 23, 2024
- **Date of Allotment**: November 5, 2025
- **Circular Date**: January 22, 2026
- **Trading Commencement**: January 23, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry**: February 14, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. The listing adds approximately 47.33 lakh shares to the company's equity capital, but all shares are locked-in for over one year. No immediate impact on trading volumes or liquidity is expected.

**Operational Impact**: Routine listing following NCLT-approved resolution plan. The preferential allotment to strategic investors (non-promoters) may indicate corporate restructuring or debt resolution activities.

**Investor Impact**: Existing shareholders should note the dilution from preferential allotment, though shares cannot be traded until February 2027.
