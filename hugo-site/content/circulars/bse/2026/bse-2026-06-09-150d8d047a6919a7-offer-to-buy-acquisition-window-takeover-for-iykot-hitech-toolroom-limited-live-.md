## Summary

BSE has announced the live activities schedule for the Offer to Buy (OTB) – Acquisition Window (Takeover) of equity shares of IYKOT HITECH TOOLROOM LIMITED (Scrip Code: 522245, Symbol: IYKOTHITE). The offer is open from 10th June 2026 to 23rd June 2026 at an offer price of Rs. 8.50 per share, covering up to 26,98,298 equity shares.

## Key Points

- **Security:** IYKOT HITECH TOOLROOM LIMITED | Symbol: IYKOTHITE | ISIN: INE079L01013 | Scrip Code: 522245
- **Offer Size:** Up to 26,98,298 equity shares
- **Offer Price:** Rs. 8.50 per share
- **Face Value:** Rs. 5.00
- **Market Lot:** 1
- **Opening Date:** Wednesday, 10th June 2026
- **Closing Date:** Tuesday, 23rd June 2026
- **Session Timings:** 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. (Bid Offering Period); Take-up on last day until 4:00 p.m.
- Bidding is available for investors holding shares in demat form only
- OTB module accessible via iBBS and RTRMS platforms (Internet and Leased Line)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice follows operational procedures established under Exchange Notices No. 20150930-1 (dated September 30, 2015) and No. 20170203-29 (dated February 3, 2017).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must ensure the Unique Client Code (UCC) of each investor is registered and/or updated with the correct PAN Number and Category in the Exchange UCC database.
- Trading Members can bid only for investors holding shares in demat form.
- Members and Custodians should note that if no securities are accepted, no End of Issue files will be generated on Extranet.
- For Leased Line URL access settings, refer to Exchange Circular No. 20170503-12.

## Important Dates

| Activity | Period | Timings |
|---|---|---|
| Bid Offering Period | 10th June – 23rd June 2026 | 9:15 a.m. – 3:30 p.m. |
| Give-up / Take-up | 10th June – 22nd June 2026 | 9:15 a.m. – 3:30 p.m. |
| Give-up (last day) | 23rd June 2026 | 9:15 a.m. – 3:30 p.m. |
| Take-up (last day) | 23rd June 2026 | 9:15 a.m. – 4:00 p.m. |

## Impact Assessment

This circular is of moderate operational significance, primarily affecting trading members and custodians who manage client holdings in IYKOT HITECH TOOLROOM LIMITED. Investors holding shares of the company may tender their shares through registered trading members via the iBBS platform during the offer period. The offer size of approximately 26.98 lakh shares at Rs. 8.50 per share represents a defined exit opportunity for existing shareholders under the takeover acquisition window.
