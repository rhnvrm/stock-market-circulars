## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members that Anuriti Multy Broking Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 6497) has applied to surrender its membership of the Exchange in accordance with BSE's Rules, Bye-laws, and Regulations. Trading members with objections to the surrender must submit them in writing within 15 days.

## Key Points

- Anuriti Multy Broking Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 6497) has applied for voluntary surrender of BSE membership.
- The application is being processed under BSE Rules, Bye-laws, and Regulations.
- All trading members are invited to raise objections, if any, in writing to the Membership Department.
- The objection deadline is July 7, 2026 (15 days from the notice date of June 23, 2026).
- Contact for clarifications: membership.ops@bseindia.com.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This notice is procedural, following the standard process for membership surrender as prescribed under BSE Rules, Bye-laws, and Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members who wish to object to the surrender of Anuriti Multy Broking Pvt. Ltd.'s membership must submit written objections to BSE's Membership Department.
- Objections must be received on or before **July 7, 2026**.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 23, 2026
- **Objection Deadline:** July 7, 2026 (15 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative notice with minimal market impact. It affects only trading members who may have grounds to object to the membership surrender of Anuriti Multy Broking Pvt. Ltd. There is no impact on listed securities, market operations, or broader compliance obligations for the general trading community.
