## Summary

BSE has notified that trading member SKY COMMODITIES INDIA PRIVATE LIMITED (Member No. 6745) will change its name to GOPOCKET INVEST TECH PRIVATE LIMITED effective Monday, January 5, 2026. This is an administrative change in the trading member's corporate name.

## Key Points

- Trading Member: SKY COMMODITIES INDIA PRIVATE LIMITED
- Member Number: 6745
- New Name: GOPOCKET INVEST TECH PRIVATE LIMITED
- Effective Date: Monday, January 5, 2026
- Contact for clarifications: membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is purely an administrative name change for an existing trading member.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the name change for their records and reference. No specific compliance action is required from other market participants.

## Important Dates

- **January 2, 2026**: Notice date
- **January 5, 2026**: Effective date of name change

## Impact Assessment

Minimal impact on market operations. This is an administrative change affecting only the corporate name of one trading member. The member number (6745) remains unchanged, ensuring continuity in trading operations and member identification systems.
