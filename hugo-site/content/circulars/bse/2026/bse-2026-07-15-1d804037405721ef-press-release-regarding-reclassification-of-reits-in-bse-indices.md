## Summary

BSE Index Services Private Limited issued a press release on July 15, 2026, announcing that, in reference to a SEBI circular (SEBI Circular No. HO/24/13/12(1)2025-IMD-POD-2/I/157/2025) reclassifying Real Estate Investment Trusts (REITs) as equity-related instruments, REITs will now be considered eligible for inclusion in BSE Equity Indices. The revised eligibility criteria will take effect from the upcoming scheduled reconstitution of BSE Equity Indices.

## Key Points

- SEBI has reclassified REITs as equity-related instruments via circular HO/24/13/12(1)2025-IMD-POD-2/I/157/2025.
- Following this reclassification, REITs will be eligible for inclusion in BSE Equity Indices.
- The change in eligibility criteria applies from the next scheduled reconstitution of BSE Equity Indices, not immediately.
- Queries can be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com.

## Regulatory Changes

The change stems from a SEBI circular that reclassifies REITs as equity-related instruments rather than a separate asset class. This reclassification enables index providers like BSE to consider REITs alongside other equity instruments for index eligibility and inclusion purposes.

## Compliance Requirements

No direct compliance action is required from listed companies, brokers, or investors as a result of this notice. Index methodology adjustments will be handled internally by BSE Index Services during the next scheduled reconstitution cycle.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 15, 2026
- Effective Date: Applicable from the upcoming scheduled reconstitution of BSE Equity Indices (specific date not disclosed in this notice)

## Impact Assessment

This is a structural/methodological change that broadens the eligible universe of instruments for BSE Equity Indices to include REITs. Over time, this could lead to increased passive and active fund flows into REITs as they become eligible for inclusion in benchmark and sectoral indices, potentially improving liquidity and visibility for listed REITs. The immediate market impact is limited since actual inclusion depends on individual REITs meeting index-specific eligibility criteria (float, market cap, liquidity, etc.) at the time of reconstitution.
