## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that Sikozy Realtors Limited (Scrip Code: 524642) has fixed 22 July 2026 as the Record Date for reduction of its equity share capital, pursuant to an order passed by the Hon'ble National Company Law Tribunal (NCLT), Mumbai Bench. There will be no dealings/suspension in the security with effect from 22 July 2026 for this purpose.

## Key Points

- Company: Sikozy Realtors Limited, Scrip Code 524642
- Record Date: 22 July 2026
- No Dealings/Suspension w.e.f.: 22 July 2026
- Reference: DR-677/2026-2027
- The issued, subscribed and paid-up equity share capital is being reduced from Rs. 4,45,83,000 (comprising 4,45,83,000 equity shares of Rs. 1 each) to Rs. 44,58,300 (comprising 44,58,300 equity shares of Rs. 1 each)
- This is achieved by cancelling and extinguishing 4,01,24,700 equity shares of Rs. 1 each held as on the record date
- The reduction sets off Rs. 4,01,24,700 against accumulated losses, out of total accumulated losses of Rs. 6,03,74,113

## Regulatory Changes

The capital reduction has been approved via an order passed by the Hon'ble National Company Law Tribunal, Mumbai Bench, under the applicable provisions governing reduction of share capital.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of the Exchange are required to take note of the record date and the no-dealings/suspension period for the security, and process the capital reduction in line with the Exchange's notice.

## Important Dates

- Record Date: 22 July 2026
- No Dealings/Suspension effective from: 22 July 2026

## Impact Assessment

The capital reduction will decrease the number of outstanding equity shares of Sikozy Realtors Limited from 4,45,83,000 to 44,58,300 (a reduction of roughly 90%), used to write off accumulated losses of Rs. 4,01,24,700 against paid-up capital. Trading in the scrip will be suspended around the record date to process the corporate action. Existing shareholders' shareholding count will be adjusted accordingly, though this is a book-value restructuring rather than a change in proportional ownership.
