## Summary

BSE has issued a notice to resume trading in the Commercial Paper of AAVAS FINANCIERS LIMITED (Scrip Code 729667) effective June 10, 2026. Trading was previously halted in anticipation of a redemption Record Date of 02.06.2026, which the company has since clarified was an inadvertent error. The correct Record Date is 16.06.2026, and a separate notice will be issued to confirm the revised date.

## Key Points

- Trading in AAVAS FINANCIERS LIMITED's Commercial Paper (Scrip Code 729667) will resume from **June 10, 2026** (DR-648/2026-2027).
- The company had erroneously stated 02.06.2026 as the Record Date for Redemption; the correct Record Date is **16.06.2026**.
- The original suspension was communicated via Exchange Notice No. 20260526-6 dated May 25, 2026.
- A separate notice will be issued by BSE confirming the revised Record Date.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This notice is a correction to a previously communicated Record Date for Commercial Paper redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members are requested to note the resumption of trading in Scrip Code 729667 from June 10, 2026.
- Members should await the separate BSE notice confirming the revised Record Date of 16.06.2026.

## Important Dates

- **June 10, 2026**: Trading resumes in Commercial Paper of AAVAS FINANCIERS LIMITED (Scrip Code 729667).
- **16.06.2026**: Revised Record Date for Redemption (to be confirmed via separate notice).
- **May 25, 2026**: Original notice (20260526-6) announcing the (erroneous) Record Date of 02.06.2026.

## Impact Assessment

This is a correction of limited operational scope, restoring normal trading activity in a specific debt instrument. Investors and trading members holding or transacting in this commercial paper should note the revised timeline. The correction extends the trading window by approximately two weeks relative to the originally communicated Record Date, providing additional liquidity before the redemption date.
