## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 2,76,000 new equity shares of Kaarya Facilities and Services Limited (Scrip Code: 540756) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Thursday, March 5, 2026. These shares were issued on a preferential basis to non-promoters at a price of Rs. 13.09 per share.

## Key Points

- 2,76,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 3.09/- per share to non-promoters on a preferential basis
- Shares are ranking pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 9350259 to 9626258
- ISIN: INE282Y01016
- All 2,76,000 shares are subject to lock-in until September 7, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for newly allotted securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed that these securities are now permitted to trade from March 5, 2026
- Lock-in restriction applies to all 2,76,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 9350259 to 9626258) until September 7, 2026; these shares cannot be traded during the lock-in period

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** December 13, 2025
- **Trading Effective Date:** March 5, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry Date:** September 7, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing is limited in scope, involving a small preferential allotment for Kaarya Facilities and Services Limited. The entire allotment is under lock-in until September 2026, meaning no immediate selling pressure from these shares. Market impact is minimal and confined to existing investors and traders of scrip code 540756.
