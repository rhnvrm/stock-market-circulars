## Summary

BSE announces the settlement schedule for the buyback of FAIRCHEM ORGANICS LIMITED through the Offer to Buy Acquisition Window mechanism. The bidding window will be open from January 8-14, 2026 (Settlement No. 2026003), with obligation download on January 20, 2026 and settlement on January 21, 2026. Members and custodians must deliver demat shares through early pay-in mechanism using both CDSL and NSDL depository systems.

## Key Points

- Settlement Type: Buyback
- Settlement Number: 2026003
- Bid Start Date: January 8, 2026
- Bid End Date: January 14, 2026
- Obligation Download: January 20, 2026
- Settlement Date: January 21, 2026
- Demat shares must be delivered only through Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism
- Market Type "Buyback" to be used in depository system along with settlement number
- CDSL & NSDL early pay-in securities will be earmarked/blocked in clients' accounts
- No EPN file upload required by members and custodians
- Members must refer to MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for client-wise stamp duty charges

## Regulatory Changes

This circular implements the Revised Guidelines of Mechanism for acquisition of shares through Stock Exchange as referenced in Notice No.20200528-32 dated May 28, 2020.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Members/Custodians**: Must ensure early pay-in of securities is reflected in BSE/ICCL systems before prescribed timelines for accepting/confirming bids on BSE OTB platform
- **Process Timing**: Complete early pay-in process in depository system sufficiently in advance before BSE OTB bidding window closure and custodian confirmation window deadlines
- **Depository Details**: Use Market Type "Buyback" and Settlement No. 2026003 in depository systems (both CDSL and NSDL)
- **Stamp Duty**: Members must check MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for client-wise stamp duty charges
- **No EPN Upload**: Members and custodians are NOT required to upload any EPN file

## Important Dates

- **January 8, 2026**: Bid start date
- **January 14, 2026**: Bid end date
- **January 20, 2026**: Obligation download date
- **January 21, 2026**: Settlement date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to shareholders of FAIRCHEM ORGANICS LIMITED who wish to participate in the buyback offer. No broader market impact expected.

**Operational Impact**: Members and custodians handling FAIRCHEM ORGANICS LIMITED shares must coordinate with depository systems (CDSL/NSDL) to ensure timely early pay-in of shares. Proper coordination required between trading members and custodians to meet bidding and confirmation window deadlines.
