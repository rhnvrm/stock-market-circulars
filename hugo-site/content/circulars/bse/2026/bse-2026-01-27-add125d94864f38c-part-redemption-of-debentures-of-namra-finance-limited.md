## Summary

NAMRA FINANCE LIMITED has announced part redemption of its non-convertible debentures (ISIN: INE229U07103, Code: 975050) along with interest payment. The record date is set for February 6, 2026, and trading in these debentures will commence with reduced face value from the same date.

## Key Points

- Company: NAMRA FINANCE LIMITED
- Security: Non-Convertible Debentures (NFL-11.95%-25-5-26-PVT)
- ISIN: INE229U07103
- BSE Code: 975050
- Record Date: February 6, 2026
- Purpose: Part redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Reduced Face Value: Rs. 9,090 per debenture
- Effective Date: February 6, 2026
- Settlement Number: DR-815/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that trading in the debentures will be conducted with reduced face value of Rs. 9,090 per debenture effective from February 6, 2026
- All trades on and after the effective date must reflect the reduced face value

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: February 6, 2026 - For determining eligibility for interest payment and part redemption
- **Effective Date**: February 6, 2026 - Trading commences with reduced face value (Settlement DR-815/2025-2026)
- **Notice Date**: January 27, 2026
- **Circular Issued**: January 22, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action with limited impact:

- **Debenture Holders**: Those holding debentures on record date will receive interest payment and partial redemption proceeds
- **Market Impact**: Minimal - affects only holders of this specific debt instrument
- **Trading**: Post-redemption trading will reflect reduced face value of Rs. 9,090 per debenture
- **Liquidity**: No significant impact on overall debt market liquidity
