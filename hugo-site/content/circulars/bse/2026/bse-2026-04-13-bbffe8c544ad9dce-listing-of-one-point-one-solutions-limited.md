## Summary

One Point One Solutions Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on BSE in the 'B' Group of Securities effective Wednesday, April 15, 2026. The company has 26,29,36,776 equity shares of Rs. 2 each, fully paid up, assigned scrip code 544748 and ISIN INE840Y01029.

## Key Points

- **Effective Date:** Wednesday, April 15, 2026
- **Group:** 'B' Group of Securities on BSE
- **Scrip Code:** 544748
- **Scrip ID (BOLT):** ONEPOINT
- **ISIN:** INE840Y01029
- **Total Shares:** 26,29,36,776 equity shares of Rs. 2 each, fully paid up
- **Distinctive Nos.:** 1 to 262936776
- **Market Lot:** 1 (One) share
- Trading will be in dematerialised form only, as per SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000
- All shares rank pari-passu including dividend entitlement
- Lock-in period has been marked in the depository's records for demat shares

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The listing complies with existing SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000, mandating that securities be traded exclusively in dematerialised form.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade this scrip in dematerialised form only
- Unit market lot of 1 (one) share applies to all trades
- Members requiring clarifications may contact the company's compliance officer Pritesh Sonawane (pritesh.sonawane@1point1.com, Tel: 022 6687 3800) or BSE contacts: Parag Jain (Manager) and Hardik Bhuta (Assistant Vice President), Tel: (91) 022 2272 8685

## Important Dates

- **April 13, 2026:** Notice date
- **April 15, 2026:** Effective date of listing and commencement of trading on BSE

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard new listing event for One Point One Solutions Limited on BSE's B Group. Trading members and investors interested in this scrip should note the mandatory demat-only trading requirement and the unit market lot of one share. The registrar to the issue is MUFG Intime India Private Limited (formerly Link Intime). The company's registered office is in Bandra Kurla Complex, Mumbai, and its financial year ends March 31. Unaudited financial results for Q3 FY2025-26 (quarter ended December 31, 2025) and the Annual Report for FY2024-25 are available as annexures to this notice.
