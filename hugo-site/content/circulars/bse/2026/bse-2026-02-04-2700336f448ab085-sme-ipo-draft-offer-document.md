## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document for Panchatv Bharat Limited's SME IPO, filed on February 02, 2026. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and is available on the exchange website for public comments for a period of at least 21 days.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Panchatv Bharat Limited on February 02, 2026
- Document complies with Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website at www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public comments invited within 21 days from notice date
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard procedural notice for SME IPO draft offer document filing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants and public can review the Draft Offer Document on BSE SME website
- Comments or feedback on the draft offer document must be submitted within 21 days from February 04, 2026
- Comments should be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **February 02, 2026**: Draft Offer Document filed
- **February 04, 2026**: Notice published on BSE
- **February 25, 2026**: Last date for public comments (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has medium impact on the SME segment and potential investors. The Draft Offer Document provides transparency for Panchatv Bharat Limited's proposed SME IPO, allowing market participants to review and provide feedback before the final offer document is issued. The 21-day public comment period is a standard regulatory requirement to ensure stakeholder participation in the IPO process.
