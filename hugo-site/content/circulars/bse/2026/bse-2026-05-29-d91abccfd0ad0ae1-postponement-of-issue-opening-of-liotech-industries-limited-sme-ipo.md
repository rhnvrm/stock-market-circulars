## Summary

BSE has issued a notice regarding the postponement of the issue opening date for the SME IPO of Liotech Industries Limited. The notice (No. 20260529-43) is dated 29 May 2026 and was issued by the DCS-Listing department under the SME segment.

## Key Points

- The issue opening of Liotech Industries Limited's SME IPO has been postponed.
- Notice issued by BSE's DCS-Listing department under the SME segment.
- No attachments are provided with this notice.
- A revised issue opening date has not been specified in the circular.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced. This is an operational notice informing market participants of a schedule change for the Liotech Industries Limited SME IPO.

## Compliance Requirements

- Investors who had applied or were planning to apply for the Liotech Industries Limited SME IPO should await further communication from BSE and the company regarding the revised issue opening date.
- Intermediaries (brokers, registrars, bankers to the issue) should note the postponement and halt any processing activities tied to the original opening date.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 29 May 2026
- **Revised Issue Opening Date:** Not specified in this notice; to be announced separately.

## Impact Assessment

The postponement of the SME IPO issue opening for Liotech Industries Limited primarily affects prospective retail and institutional investors who were preparing to subscribe. It also impacts the company's near-term capital raising plans. The broader market impact is limited given the SME segment scope. Investors should monitor BSE announcements for the revised opening date.
