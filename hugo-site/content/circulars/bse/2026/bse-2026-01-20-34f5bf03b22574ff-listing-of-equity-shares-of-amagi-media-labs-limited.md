## Summary

Amagi Media Labs Limited has filed its shareholding pattern under Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015. The filing is dated January 19, 2026, and represents the post-issue shareholding structure for the company's equity shares.

## Key Points

- Entity Name: Amagi Media Labs Limited
- Security Type: Equity Shares
- Filing Date: January 19, 2026 (Post Issue)
- Filed under: Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI LODR Regulations, 2015
- No partly paid-up shares issued
- No convertible securities, warrants, or outstanding ESOPs
- No depository receipts issued against shares
- No shares held by promoters are pledged or encumbered
- No locked-in shares
- No equity shares with differential voting rights
- No significant beneficial owners

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this circular. This is a standard disclosure requirement under existing SEBI regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Listed entities are required to submit shareholding pattern declarations under Regulation 31 of SEBI LODR Regulations, 2015
- The filing must specify whether the entity has issued partly paid shares, convertible securities, warrants, ESOPs, depository receipts, locked-in shares, pledged shares, or shares with differential voting rights
- Where 'NO' is declared for any category, values are considered zero by default

## Important Dates

- Shareholding Pattern Date: January 19, 2026
- Circular Date: January 20, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine regulatory disclosure with minimal market impact. The filing confirms that Amagi Media Labs Limited has a clean capital structure with no encumbrances, pledges, or complex securities outstanding. This provides transparency to investors regarding the company's shareholding composition following its listing.
