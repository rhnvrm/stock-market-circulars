## Summary

BSE's Surveillance Department (DOSS) issued Notice No. 20260617-22 on 17 June 2026, stating that it has sought clarification from two companies — Dixon Technologies (India) Ltd and FSN E-Commerce Ventures Ltd (Nykaa) — regarding news articles published across newspapers, websites, and television channels. The exchange initiated this process to verify the accuracy of the reported news and to keep members and investors informed.

## Key Points

- BSE Surveillance sought clarification from Dixon Technologies (India) Ltd (Scrip Code: 540699) regarding a news article on Moneycontrol.
- BSE Surveillance sought clarification from FSN E-Commerce Ventures Ltd (Scrip Code: 543384) regarding a news article on Moneycontrol.
- The purpose is to verify the accuracy of media reports and to inform investors accordingly.
- The notice was issued by the DOSS (Department of Surveillance and Supervision) under Sanjay Jain (Deputy Vice President) and Shreya Ghose (Manager).

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this notice. This is a standard surveillance inquiry.

## Compliance Requirements

The two named companies are required to provide clarifications to BSE regarding the news articles circulating in the media. Members and investors should await the official company responses before acting on the underlying news.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 17 June 2026
- Clarification request issued on the same date; company response timelines not specified in the notice.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine surveillance action with low immediate market impact. The clarification process is standard procedure by BSE to ensure price-sensitive information is accurately communicated to investors. Potential impact is limited to short-term attention on Dixon Technologies (DIXON) and FSN E-Commerce Ventures (NYKAA) shares until the companies issue their official clarifications.
