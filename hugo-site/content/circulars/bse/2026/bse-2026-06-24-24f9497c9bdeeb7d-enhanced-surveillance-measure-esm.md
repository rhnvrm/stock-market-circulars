## Summary

BSE has updated the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework effective June 25–29, 2026. Securities newly qualifying under ESM will attract a minimum 100% margin and be shifted from Rolling Settlement to Trade-for-Trade segment. Stage II securities will additionally be subject to a 2% price band with Periodic Call Auction. Certain securities have been moved to a lower stage (Stage I), while others have been removed from the framework entirely.

## Key Points

- Securities in Annexure I will attract a minimum 100% margin on all open positions as of June 25, 2026, and on new positions from June 29, 2026.
- Listed securities under ESM will move from Rolling Settlement to Trade-for-Trade (T2T) segment w.e.f. June 29, 2026.
- Securities moving to Stage II will be subject to Trade-for-Trade with a 2% price band and Periodic Call Auction w.e.f. June 25, 2026.
- Some securities are being downgraded to Stage I (lower stage) under the ESM framework w.e.f. June 25, 2026.
- Securities exiting the ESM framework are listed in Annexure II, effective June 25, 2026.
- A consolidated list of all securities currently under ESM is provided in Annexure III.
- ESM classification is purely for market surveillance purposes and should not be construed as an adverse finding against the concerned company.
- ESM operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by the exchanges.

## Regulatory Changes

This notice references and updates the ESM framework established under the following prior notices:
- Notice no. 20230602-44 dated June 02, 2023
- Notice no. 20230718-46 dated July 18, 2023
- Notice no. 20240809-42 dated August 09, 2024
- Notice no. 20240920-63 dated September 20, 2024
- Notice no. 20241004-65 dated October 04, 2024
- Notice no. 20250725-61 dated July 25, 2025

Securities exiting the ESM framework will have their price band reinstated to the level applicable prior to their inclusion, unless they remain under another surveillance measure.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members/brokers must ensure 100% margin collection on open and new positions in securities listed in Annexure I.
- Trading in affected securities must comply with Trade-for-Trade settlement rules as applicable from the effective dates.
- Stage II securities must be traded under Periodic Call Auction with a 2% price band.
- Members may direct clarification queries to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **June 25, 2026**: Stage I downgrade effective; Stage II Trade-for-Trade with 2% price band and Periodic Call Auction effective; securities exiting ESM framework effective.
- **June 29, 2026**: Minimum 100% margin effective on all open positions (as on June 25, 2026) and new positions; shift to Trade-for-Trade settlement effective for newly included securities.

## Impact Assessment

The ESM update significantly restricts trading activity for affected securities. The mandatory 100% margin requirement increases the cost of holding positions, while the move to Trade-for-Trade eliminates netting benefits and increases settlement risk awareness. The 2% price band for Stage II securities sharply limits intraday price movement. Collectively, these measures reduce speculative activity and improve investor protection in securities that have exhibited unusual trading patterns. Securities moving out of the framework will see their trading restrictions lifted, potentially improving liquidity.
