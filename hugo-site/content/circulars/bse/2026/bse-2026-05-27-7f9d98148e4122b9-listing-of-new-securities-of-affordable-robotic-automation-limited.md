## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 6,04,839 new equity shares of Affordable Robotic & Automation Limited (Scrip Code: 541402, ISIN: INE692Z01013) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Friday, May 29, 2026. The shares were issued on a preferential basis to non-promoters at Rs. 248/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- + premium Rs. 238/-).

## Key Points

- 6,04,839 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 238/- (issue price Rs. 248/-) to non-promoters on a preferential basis
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 11246267 to 11851105
- Date of allotment: April 14, 2026
- Trading permitted from: May 29, 2026
- All 6,04,839 shares are subject to lock-in until November 30, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for newly allotted securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed that these securities are now listed and permitted to trade. The lock-in restriction on all 6,04,839 shares must be observed until November 30, 2026.

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: April 14, 2026
- Trading Effective Date: May 29, 2026
- Lock-in Expiry: November 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a routine preferential allotment listing for Affordable Robotic & Automation Limited (Scrip Code: 541402), a small-cap company. The entire newly listed tranche is under lock-in until November 30, 2026, meaning no immediate selling pressure from these shares. The allotment represents a capital raise from non-promoter investors at Rs. 248/- per share.
