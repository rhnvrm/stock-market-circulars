## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of Sham Foam Limited, filed on February 23, 2026, on the Stock Exchange website. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and is open for public comments for a minimum period of 21 days from the date of notice.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Sham Foam Limited on February 23, 2026
- Document hosted on the BSE SME website for public review
- Public comment period: minimum 21 days from February 24, 2026
- Comments to be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Document complies with Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice is issued in compliance with existing ICDR (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, which mandate public availability of draft offer documents for SME IPOs.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public/investors may review the Draft Offer Document at: https://www.bsesme.com/download/314291/SME_IPO%20InPrinciple/ShamFoamDP_20260223212947.pdf
- Any comments or objections must be submitted within 21 days from February 24, 2026 to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Filing Date:** February 23, 2026
- **Notice Date:** February 24, 2026
- **Public Comment Deadline:** On or before March 17, 2026 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has limited broad market impact as it pertains solely to the upcoming SME IPO of Sham Foam Limited. Investors interested in participating in the IPO or providing regulatory feedback should review the draft document and submit comments before the deadline. The filing signals Sham Foam Limited's intent to list on the BSE SME platform.
