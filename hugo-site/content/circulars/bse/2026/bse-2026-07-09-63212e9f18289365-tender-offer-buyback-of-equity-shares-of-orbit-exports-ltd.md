## Summary

BSE has informed Trading Members that Orbit Exports Ltd (Scrip Code: 512626) has fixed 15 July 2026 as the Record Date for determining the entitlement and names of equity shareholders eligible to participate in the company's buyback offer.

## Key Points

- Company: Orbit Exports Ltd (Scrip Code: 512626)
- Record Date: 15 July 2026
- Purpose: To determine entitlement and names of equity shareholders eligible to participate in the buyback offer
- Applicable to dematerialised securities in the Rolling Settlement segment
- Ex-entitlement basis effective from 15 July 2026, Settlement No. DR-672/2026-2027

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a procedural notice communicating a corporate action record date, not a regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members are required to take note of the Record Date and ensure trades are processed on an ex-entitlement basis from 15 July 2026 to correctly reflect buyback eligibility.

## Important Dates

- Record Date: 15 July 2026
- Ex-entitlement effective date: 15 July 2026
- Settlement Number: DR-672/2026-2027

## Impact Assessment

The notice affects only shareholders of Orbit Exports Ltd, determining their eligibility to tender shares in the buyback offer. Trading Members must adjust settlement processing for the stock on an ex-entitlement basis from the record date, but there is no broader market-wide impact.
