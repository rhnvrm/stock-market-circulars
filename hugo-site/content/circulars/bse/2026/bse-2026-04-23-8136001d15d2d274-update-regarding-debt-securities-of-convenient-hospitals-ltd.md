## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (20260423-13) updating trading members about revised terms for the debt securities of Convenient Hospitals Limited. This is a continuation of Exchange Notice 20251006-31 dated October 6, 2025. The revised terms will take effect from Friday, April 24, 2026.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code 977185 (ISIN: INE01W207017) will be available for trading with revised terms effective April 24, 2026.
- This notice is a continuation of Exchange Notice 20251006-31 dated October 6, 2025.
- Issued by the DCS-Listing department under the Debt segment.
- Trading members are advised to refer to Annexure I for the specific revised terms.

## Regulatory Changes

The debt securities of Convenient Hospitals Limited (Scrip Code 977185, ISIN INE01W207017) will trade under revised terms as specified in Annexure I, replacing or updating the terms previously communicated in October 2025.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must take note of the revised trading terms for Scrip Code 977185 and ensure compliance when executing trades on or after April 24, 2026. Members requiring further clarification may contact:
- Mr. Akash Shinde: +91 22 2272 8597 | debt.listing@bseindia.com
- Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President

## Important Dates

- **April 23, 2026**: Notice issued.
- **April 24, 2026 (Friday)**: Revised terms become effective for trading in Scrip Code 977185.

## Impact Assessment

This update primarily affects trading members and investors holding or trading the debt securities of Convenient Hospitals Limited. The revision of terms may impact pricing, yield, or other trading parameters for ISIN INE01W207017. Market participants should review Annexure I carefully before trading this instrument from April 24, 2026 onward.
