## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the equity shares of Waterways Leisure Tourism Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange. The exact date of listing will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Waterways Leisure Tourism Limited's equity shares are approved for listing on BSE.
- Scrip Code: **544802**, Symbol: **CORDELIA**, ISIN: **INE0LZF01013**.
- The listing date and full security details will be provided in a subsequent notice.
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification under BSE's DCS-Listing department.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the scrip details (Code: 544802, Symbol: CORDELIA, ISIN: INE0LZF01013) and await the follow-up notice confirming the exact listing date before initiating any trades.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 30, 2026
- **Listing Date:** To be announced via a separate notice.

## Impact Assessment

Limited immediate market impact as the actual listing date has not yet been specified. Trading members should monitor for the follow-up BSE notice that will confirm the commencement of trading in CORDELIA (Waterways Leisure Tourism Limited) shares.
