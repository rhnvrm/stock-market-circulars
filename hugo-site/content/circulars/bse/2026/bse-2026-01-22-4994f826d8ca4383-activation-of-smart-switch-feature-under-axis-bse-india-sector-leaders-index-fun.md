## Summary

BSE has activated the SMART Switch feature for the AXIS BSE INDIA SECTOR LEADERS INDEX FUND - NFO on the BSE StAR MF platform. This feature allows investors to switch from AXIS Overnight Fund plans (both Regular and Direct Growth options) into the new fund offering.

## Key Points

- SMART Switch feature activated for AXIS BSE India Sector Leaders Index Fund NFO
- Available on BSE StAR MF platform
- Two source schemes eligible for switching:
  - AXIS OVERNIGHT FUND REGULAR PLAN - GROWTH
  - AXIS OVERNIGHT FUND DIRECT PLAN - GROWTH
- Feature enables seamless switching from overnight fund to sector leaders index fund

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational feature activation on the BSE mutual fund platform.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. This is a platform feature available for investors who wish to utilize the switch facility.

## Important Dates

- Activation Date: January 22, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact, affecting only investors interested in switching from AXIS Overnight Fund to the AXIS BSE India Sector Leaders Index Fund. This is a convenience feature for mutual fund investors on the BSE StAR MF platform and does not affect broader market operations or trading activities.
