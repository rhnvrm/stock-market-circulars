## Summary

BSE has notified Trading Members that the price bands applicable to 27 specified scrips have been revised from their existing levels, effective July 03, 2026. The revised price band percentages range from 5% to 20% depending on the security.

## Key Points

- 27 scrips across BSE's Equity segment have had their daily price band percentages revised.
- Most scrips (22 of 27) have been set to a 5% price band.
- Aegis Logistics Ltd and Galaxy Bearings Ltd have been set to a 10% price band.
- Susan Electricals India Limited has been set to a 20% price band.
- The revised bands take effect from July 03, 2026.
- Queries can be directed to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members must update their systems to reflect the revised price band limits for the above scrips from the effective date. No further action is required beyond ensuring order placement complies with the new bands.

## Important Dates

- Effective date of revised price bands: July 03, 2026
- Notice date: July 02, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The revision in price bands directly affects the permissible daily price movement (circuit limits) for the listed scrips. A narrower band (5%) restricts intraday volatility, while a wider band (10% or 20%) allows for greater price movement. Traders and investors holding or trading these specific securities should adjust their risk management and order strategies accordingly. The overall market impact is limited given the small number of scrips affected relative to the full exchange universe.
