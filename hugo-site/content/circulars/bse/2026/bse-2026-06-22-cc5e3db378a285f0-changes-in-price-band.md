## Summary

BSE's Department of Surveillance (DOSS) has revised price bands for 23 equity scrips effective June 23, 2026. The revised bands range from 2% to 20%, reflecting adjustments to trading limits as part of ongoing market surveillance activities.

## Key Points

- 23 scrips have price bands revised effective June 23, 2026
- Revised bands: 2% (5 scrips), 5% (8 scrips), 10% (6 scrips), 20% (4 scrips)
- Applicable to the Equity segment
- Issued under Notice No. 20260622-28 by DOSS
- Contact for clarifications: bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must note the revised price bands for the above scrips and ensure that orders placed from June 23, 2026 onward comply with the new limits. No special action is required beyond awareness of the updated circuit limits.

## Important Dates

- **June 22, 2026**: Notice issued
- **June 23, 2026**: Revised price bands take effect

## Impact Assessment

These revisions tighten or loosen intraday trading limits for 23 scrips across various sectors including healthcare, petrochemicals, finance, and infrastructure. Scrips moved to narrower bands (2%) — Gujarat Lease Financing, R.S. Software, RR MetalMakers, Smiths & Founders, Transchem — face the most restrictive trading limits, suggesting heightened surveillance concern. Scrips at 20% — Asian Petroproducts, Bridge Securities, Sarvottam Finvest, TPI India — have the widest permitted movement. Traders in these counters should update their risk parameters accordingly.
