## Summary

BSE has announced that SUNDARAM INCOME PLUS ARBITRAGE ACTIVE FOF (Fund of Funds) scheme will reopen for ongoing transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective January 19, 2026. The scheme will be available for subscription, redemption, and systematic facilities including SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan).

## Key Points

- SUNDARAM INCOME PLUS ARBITRAGE ACTIVE FOF reopening for ongoing transactions
- Available on BSE StAR MF Platform from January 19, 2026
- Six plan variants available: Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Reinvestment, and IDCW Payout options
- All schemes categorized under Equity category
- Full facility coverage: Subscription, Redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Follows previous notice No. 20260102-20

## Scheme Details

### Regular Plans
1. **Growth Plan** - ISIN: INF903JA1MC4
2. **IDCW Reinvestment** - ISIN: INF903JA1ME0
3. **IDCW Payout** - ISIN: INF903JA1MD2

### Direct Plans
4. **Growth Plan** - ISIN: INF903JA1LZ7
5. **IDCW Reinvestment** - ISIN: INF903JA1MB6
6. **IDCW Payout** - ISIN: INF903JA1MA8

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding scheme availability on the trading platform.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of BSE StAR MF Platform should note the availability of these schemes for client transactions starting January 19, 2026. No specific compliance actions required.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 16, 2026
- **Effective Date**: January 19, 2026 - Scheme reopens for ongoing transactions

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine operational announcement affecting a single mutual fund scheme.

**Investor Impact**: Low. Investors interested in this specific Fund of Funds scheme focusing on arbitrage strategies can now resume transactions through the BSE StAR MF Platform.

**Platform Impact**: Routine addition to available schemes on BSE StAR MF Platform with full transactional capabilities including systematic investment facilities.
