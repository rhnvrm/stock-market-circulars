## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that GROWW Mutual Fund will be launching the **GROWW NIFTY PRIVATE BANK INDEX FUND** as a New Fund Offer (NFO) with SIP/XSIP facility on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The NFO subscription window runs from **May 6, 2026 to May 20, 2026**, with six scheme variants covering Regular and Direct plans across Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options.

## Key Points

- NFO period: May 6, 2026 to May 20, 2026 (extended till midnight for Lumpsum purchases)
- Six scheme variants across Regular/Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- Tentative allotment date: May 27, 2026
- Clear funds must be in the ICCL account by 6:00 PM on May 22, 2026
- Transactions without client-initiated funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day will be cancelled
- SIP/XSIP registrations must be authenticated by clients by 3:00 PM on May 20, 2026
- Cheque and OTM payments accepted subject to clear fund receipt by May 22, 2026
- Switch-out funds received on or after the allotment date will not be considered for allotment

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for a new mutual fund NFO launch on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure client funds are initiated by 9:00 AM on T+1 day to avoid transaction cancellation
- Pending transactions (with payment initiated) will be held until 3:00 PM on the last day of funds pay-out or T+5 day, whichever is earlier
- Members must ensure SIP/XSIP client authentications are completed by 3:00 PM on May 20, 2026
- Clear funds must be available in ICCL account by 6:00 PM on May 22, 2026 for allotment eligibility
