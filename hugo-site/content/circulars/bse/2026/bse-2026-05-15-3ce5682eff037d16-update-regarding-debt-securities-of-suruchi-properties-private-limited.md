## Summary

BSE has issued an update regarding the debt securities of Suruchi Properties Private Limited, continuing from Exchange Notice No. 20260216-23 dated February 16, 2026. Scrip Code 976775 (ISIN: INE217T07027) will be available for trading with revised terms as specified in Annexure I, effective Monday, May 18, 2026.

## Key Points

- Continuation of earlier notice (Exchange Notice No. 20260216-23, dated February 16, 2026)
- Scrip Code 976775 with ISIN INE217T07027 will resume trading under revised terms
- Revised terms are detailed in Annexure I attached to the notice
- Effective date for revised trading terms: Monday, May 18, 2026
- Trading members are advised to take note and act accordingly

## Regulatory Changes

The debt securities of Suruchi Properties Private Limited will be subject to revised terms as outlined in Annexure I. The specific revised terms (such as coupon rate, maturity, or other structural changes) are contained in the annexure to the original notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure trading of Scrip Code 976775 (ISIN: INE217T07027) is conducted under the revised terms from May 18, 2026 onwards
- Members should refer to Annexure I for the full details of the revised terms
- For queries, members may contact Ms. Pooja Trivedi at +91 22 2272 8844 or debt.listing@bseindia.com, or Hardik Bhuta (Assistant Vice President)

## Important Dates

- **May 15, 2026**: Notice issued
- **May 18, 2026 (Monday)**: Effective date for revised trading terms for Scrip Code 976775

## Impact Assessment

This notice has a limited and targeted impact, affecting only the specific debt security (Scrip Code 976775, ISIN: INE217T07027) of Suruchi Properties Private Limited. Trading members dealing in this scrip must apply the revised terms from May 18, 2026. There is no broader market-wide impact anticipated.
