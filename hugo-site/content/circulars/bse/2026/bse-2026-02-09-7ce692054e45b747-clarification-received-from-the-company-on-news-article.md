## Summary

Kalyan Jewellers India Limited responded to BSE Surveillance query dated February 09, 2026 regarding a news article on Moneycontrol. The company confirmed it is not aware of any undisclosed information that could explain the news details and stated there are no regulatory or legal proceedings to report.

## Key Points

- Company reference: SEC/80/2025-2026 dated February 09, 2026
- BSE query reference: L/SURV/ONL/RV/SG/ (2025-2026)/ 182
- No undisclosed information available that has not been announced to exchanges
- No regulatory or legal proceedings initiated or concluded
- Company reaffirms commitment to disclosure under Regulation 30 of SEBI LODR 2015

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

Company continues ongoing compliance with Regulation 30 of SEBI (Listing Obligation and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations 2015 for disclosure of price-sensitive information.

## Important Dates

- **February 09, 2026**: Date of company clarification and BSE query

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard clarification response to exchange surveillance queries. The company's confirmation of no undisclosed material information provides transparency but does not indicate any new developments or material changes to business operations. No action required by investors or market participants.
