## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in debentures of BAJAJ HOUSING FINANCE LIMITED (ISIN: INE377Y07466, Code: 975391) effective February 2, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing the record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Debenture series: BHFL-8%-16-2-26-PVT (INE377Y07466)
- BSE scrip code: 975391
- Record date: February 2, 2026
- No dealings permitted from: February 2, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Notice reference: DR-811/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard trading suspension notice for corporate action processing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any deals in the specified debentures from February 2, 2026
- Trading members are required to take note of this suspension and ensure compliance
- All pending transactions must be settled before the suspension date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 22, 2026
- **Record Date**: February 2, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date**: February 2, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This suspension affects holders and potential traders of BAJAJ HOUSING FINANCE LIMITED's 8% debentures maturing on February 16, 2026. The suspension is procedural and necessary for processing the redemption and interest payment. Debenture holders on record as of February 2, 2026 will receive their redemption proceeds and final interest payment. The impact is limited to this specific debenture series in the debt market segment, with no effect on equity trading or other securities of the company.
