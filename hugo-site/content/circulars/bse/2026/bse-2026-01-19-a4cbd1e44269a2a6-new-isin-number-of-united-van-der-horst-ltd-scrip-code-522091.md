## Summary

BSE has issued a new ISIN number INE890G01047 for United Van Der Horst Ltd (Scrip Code: 522091) following the sub-division of equity shares from face value Rs.5/- to Re.1/-. This is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20260107-19 dated January 07, 2026. The new ISIN will be effective for all trades executed on or after the ex-date of January 22, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company Name: United Van Der Horst Ltd
- Scrip Code: 522091
- New ISIN Number: INE890G01047
- Corporate Action: Sub-division of equity shares from Rs.5/- to Re.1/- (5:1 split)
- Reference: Continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20260107-19 dated January 07, 2026
- DR Number: DR-804/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an implementation notice for a previously announced corporate action involving share sub-division.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the new ISIN INE890G01047 for all trades in United Van Der Horst Ltd equity shares from the ex-date onwards
- All trading and settlement systems must be updated to reflect the new ISIN and revised face value of Re.1/- per share
- Demat accounts will be credited with sub-divided shares in the ratio of 5:1

## Important Dates

- **Ex-Date: January 22, 2026** - New ISIN becomes effective for all trades
- Notice Date: January 19, 2026
- Previous Notice Reference: January 07, 2026 (Notice No. 20260107-19)

## Impact Assessment

**Trading Impact:** Trading members must ensure their systems are configured with the new ISIN from January 22, 2026. Any trades executed on or after the ex-date must use INE890G01047.

**Shareholder Impact:** Shareholders will see their holdings multiply by 5 times with the face value reducing to Re.1/- per share. The total investment value remains unchanged.

**Operational Impact:** Medium - This is a standard corporate action requiring system updates for ISIN, face value, and share quantities. Market infrastructure participants must coordinate to ensure smooth transition on the ex-date.
