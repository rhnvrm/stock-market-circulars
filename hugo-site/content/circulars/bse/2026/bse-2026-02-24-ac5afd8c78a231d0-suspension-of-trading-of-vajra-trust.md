## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in two Pass-Through Certificates (PTCs) issued by Vajra Trust effective February 25, 2025 (DR-828/2025-2026). Axis Trustee Services Limited informed the Exchange of the redemption of these PTCs, prompting the suspension notice to all trading members.

## Key Points

- Trading is suspended in two PTCs of Vajra Trust: VT-9.25%-20-10-26-PTC (ISIN: INE20NI15013, Scrip Code: 976743) and VT-9.25%-20-7-26-PTC (ISIN: INE1EJ815016, Scrip Code: 976276)
- Axis Trustee Services Limited notified BSE of the redemption of both PTCs
- Both PTCs carry a coupon rate of 9.25%
- Reference: DR-828/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational action under existing BSE debt segment trading rules for securities undergoing redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are advised not to deal in the aforesaid PTCs of Vajra Trust with effect from February 25, 2025
- All trading members are required to take note of this suspension

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** February 24, 2026
- **Suspension Effective Date:** February 25, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The suspension affects holders and traders of these two Vajra Trust PTCs in the BSE Debt Market Segment. Both securities are being redeemed, so the trading suspension is a routine procedural step. Impact is limited to participants directly holding or trading these specific instruments; no broader market disruption is expected.
