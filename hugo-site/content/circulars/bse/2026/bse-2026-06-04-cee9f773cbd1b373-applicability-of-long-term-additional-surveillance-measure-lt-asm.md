## Summary

BSE has issued a notice under the Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LT-ASM) framework imposing 100% margins on select securities from June 09, 2026. Additionally, certain securities are being moved to higher LT-ASM stages and a lower price band is being applied from June 05, 2026, while some securities are exiting the framework entirely.

## Key Points

- 100% margins will be applicable from June 09, 2026 on all open positions as of June 08, 2026, and on all new positions created from June 09, 2026 onwards (Annexure I, Part A)
- Securities are being moved to higher stages of the LT-ASM framework effective June 05, 2026 (Annexure I, Part B)
- Lower Price Band is applicable effective June 05, 2026 for securities moved to higher LT-ASM stages
- Certain securities will continue in LT-ASM but will be transferred to T / XT / MT / TS group effective June 09, 2026 (Annexure I, Part D)
- Securities moving out of the LT-ASM framework are listed in Annexure II, effective June 05, 2026
- A consolidated list of all securities under the LT-ASM framework is provided in Annexure III
- Shortlisting is based on XBRL submissions by listed companies and is purely for market surveillance purposes

## Regulatory Changes

This notice is issued in reference to the original LT-ASM framework notice (20180321-46, March 21, 2018) and subsequent revisions, including:
- Exchange notice 20181027-1 (October 27, 2018)
- Exchange notice 20201204-56 (December 04, 2020)
- Exchange notice 20240809-46 (August 09, 2024)
- Exchange notice 20240920-63 (September 20, 2024)

The revised LT-ASM framework governs the imposition of enhanced surveillance measures on securities exhibiting abnormal market behavior over an extended period.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must maintain 100% margins on open positions in affected securities from June 09, 2026
- Members must adhere to the lower price band restrictions on securities moved to higher LT-ASM stages from June 05, 2026
- For clarifications, members may contact BSE Surveillance at bse.surv@bseindia.com
- Shortlisting should not be construed as an adverse action against the concerned company/entity

## Important Dates

- **June 05, 2026**: Effective date for securities moving to higher LT-ASM stages (Annexure I, Part B); Lower Price Band applicable; securities moving out of LT-ASM framework (Annexure II) effective
- **June 08, 2026**: Reference date for open positions subject to 100% margin from June 09
- **June 09, 2026**: 100% margins applicable on all open positions and new positions in Annexure I (Part A) securities; securities transferred to T/XT/MT/TS group (Annexure I, Part D)

## Impact Assessment

The imposition of 100% margins significantly increases the cost of trading in affected securities, reducing leverage and potentially lowering liquidity. Securities placed under LT-ASM are subject to ongoing periodic review. For securities exiting the LT-ASM framework, the price band will revert to the band applicable before shortlisting, provided the security is not under any other surveillance measure. The LT-ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by exchanges.
