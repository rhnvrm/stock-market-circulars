## Summary

BSE has issued instructions for listed companies to submit their status of readiness and compliance with accessibility requirements for investor-facing digital platforms (websites, mobile apps, etc.) through the BEFS portal. Submissions must be completed by March 31, 2026.

## Key Points

- Companies must log into the BEFS portal and navigate to: Rights of Persons with Disabilities – Digital platform wise accessibility
- Details must be submitted for all relevant digital platforms separately (e.g., website, mobile app)
- After saving platform-wise details, companies must click the final SUBMIT button to complete the submission
- Once the SUBMIT button is clicked, no further changes can be made — the submission is final
- Multiple platforms can be added before final submission

## Regulatory Changes

This circular operationalizes compliance with the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act requirements as they apply to investor-facing digital platforms of listed companies and trading members.

## Compliance Requirements

- Login to BEFS portal
- Navigate to 'Rights of Persons with Disabilities – Digital platform wise accessibility'
- Fill in accessibility readiness details for each applicable digital platform
- Save details for each platform individually
- Click the SUBMIT button after all platforms are updated — this constitutes final submission to the Exchange
- Applicable to all companies/members with investor-facing digital platforms (websites, mobile apps, etc.)

## Important Dates

- **Submission Deadline: March 31, 2026** — All companies must complete their submission on the BEFS portal by this date

## Impact Assessment

This is an administrative compliance requirement affecting all BSE-listed companies and relevant trading members with investor-facing digital platforms. There is no direct market or trading impact. Non-compliance by the March 31, 2026 deadline could result in regulatory action by the Exchange. The process is straightforward and involves a one-time portal submission.
