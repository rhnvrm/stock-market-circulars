## Summary

AUREATE TRADDE LIMITED (Exchange ticker: 544772) has been listed on BSE effective June 05, 2026. Following its listing, the stock will be added to the BSE SME IPO Index effective at the open of Monday, June 08, 2026.

## Key Points

- AUREATE TRADDE LIMITED (ticker: 544772) listed on BSE on June 05, 2026
- Stock to be added to the BSE SME IPO Index effective June 08, 2026
- Action referenced from Notice No. 20260604-33
- Index addition included in the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE) for June 05, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard index composition update following the listing of a new SME IPO on BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index-linked funds and ETFs tracking the BSE SME IPO Index should note the addition of AUREATE TRADDE LIMITED effective June 08, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **June 05, 2026**: AUREATE TRADDE LIMITED listed on BSE
- **June 08, 2026**: Stock added to BSE SME IPO Index at market open

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a routine constituent addition to the BSE SME IPO Index for a newly listed SME stock. Passive funds or products benchmarked against the BSE SME IPO Index will need to incorporate this addition at the open of June 08, 2026.
