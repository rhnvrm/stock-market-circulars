## Summary

BSE has notified market participants of the opening of an Offer to Buy through the Acquisition Window (Takeover mechanism) for Bliss GVS Pharma Limited. The open offer is being made by Anupam Rasayan India Limited (Acquirer), along with Mates Visa Consultancy Private Limited (Person Acting in Concert), to public shareholders of Bliss GVS Pharma Limited at an offer price of Rs. 299.00 per fully paid-up equity share of face value Re. 1 each. The offer is made under the SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 2011, pursuant to Regulation 3(1), Regulation 4 and other applicable provisions.

## Key Points

- Acquirer: Anupam Rasayan India Limited
- Person Acting in Concert (PAC): Mates Visa Consultancy Private Limited
- Target Company: Bliss GVS Pharma Limited
- Offer Price: Rs. 299.00 per equity share, payable in cash
- Shares sought: Up to 2,77,26,848 equity shares (face value Re. 1 each), representing 26.00% of the Expanded Voting Share Capital of the Target Company
- Mechanism: Acquisition through Stock Exchange window pursuant to Tender-Offer under Takeover Regulations, as per SEBI circulars dated April 13, 2015 and Dec 09, 2016, and subsequent BSE guideline notices
- Letter of Offer has been attached for market participants' reference

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; the offer is executed under existing SEBI (SAST) Regulations, 2011 and the revised guidelines for the Mechanism for Acquisition of Shares through Stock Exchange under Tender-Offers, as set out in SEBI circular no. CFD/DCR2/CIR/P/2016/131 dated Dec 09, 2016, and BSE notices dated Feb 10, 2017; April 24, 2019; May 28, 2020; Nov 2, 2020; and Aug 25, 2021.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members and Custodians must process tenders through the Acquisition Window mechanism in accordance with the revised guidelines referenced above. Market participants should refer to the attached Letter of Offer and monitor BSE's website for further details on the offer process, including timelines and settlement procedures.

## Important Dates

- Offer Opens: Tuesday, 28 July 2026
- Offer Closes: Monday, 10 August 2026
- Notice Issued/Published: 24 July 2026

## Impact Assessment

This open offer provides an exit opportunity to public shareholders of Bliss GVS Pharma Limited for up to 26% of its expanded share capital at a fixed price of Rs. 299 per share. Trading activity and price discovery in Bliss GVS Pharma Limited shares are likely to be influenced during the offer period as shareholders evaluate tendering versus holding. Trading members and custodians need to ensure operational readiness to handle tenders via the exchange's acquisition window within the specified offer period.
