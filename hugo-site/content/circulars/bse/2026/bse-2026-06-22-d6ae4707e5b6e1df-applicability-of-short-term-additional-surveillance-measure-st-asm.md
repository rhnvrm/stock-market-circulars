## Summary

BSE has notified the applicability of the Short-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) for securities meeting the criteria for inclusion under Stage I and Stage II. Enhanced margin requirements will be effective from June 24, 2026, covering all open positions as on June 23, 2026, and new positions created from June 24, 2026.

## Key Points

- Securities satisfying ST-ASM Stage I criteria will attract a margin of 50% or existing margin, whichever is higher, capped at 100%.
- Securities satisfying ST-ASM Stage II criteria will attract a margin of 100% or existing margin, whichever is higher, capped at 100%.
- The ST-ASM framework operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by exchanges.
- Shortlisting under ASM is purely a market surveillance action and should not be construed as an adverse action against the concerned company or entity.
- Price bands for scrips exiting the framework will be reinstated to pre-ASM levels, unless the scrip remains under another surveillance measure.
- Specific securities are listed in Annexure I (attached to the notice).

## Regulatory Changes

This notice references the revised ASM framework as per prior Exchange notices: 20240920-71 (September 20, 2024), 20181027-1 (October 27, 2018), 20190719-33 (July 19, 2019), 20201204-56 (December 4, 2020), 20230925-49 (September 25, 2023), and 20240920-63 (September 20, 2024). No new regulatory framework changes are introduced; this is an application of the existing ST-ASM framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure applicable margin rates are collected from clients on open positions as on June 23, 2026, and all new positions created from June 24, 2026.
- Members should refer to Annexure I for the complete list of securities under ST-ASM Stage I and Stage II.
- For clarifications, members may write to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **June 23, 2026**: Reference date for open positions subject to revised margin requirements.
- **June 24, 2026**: Effective date for enhanced margin obligations under ST-ASM Stage I (50% or higher, max 100%) and Stage II (100%).

## Impact Assessment

Market participants holding positions in ST-ASM shortlisted securities will face substantially higher margin requirements, potentially leading to forced unwinding of under-margined positions. Stage II securities will require full (100%) upfront margin, significantly restricting leveraged trading. The measure is intended to curb excessive speculation or price volatility in identified scrips and does not reflect any fundamental adverse development at the company level. Traders and investors in affected securities should review their margin adequacy before June 24, 2026.
