## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that Rajgarh Transmission Limited has fixed 12 March 2026 as the record date for the part redemption of its Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) and concurrent payment of interest. Following the redemption, trading in these debentures will continue with a reduced face value of Rs. 96,482 per debenture.

## Key Points

- Company: Rajgarh Transmission Limited
- Debenture Series: RTL-7.03%-05-06-30-PVT (ISIN: INE0N1Z08013, Scrip Code: 976760)
- Record Date: 12 March 2026
- Purpose: Part Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- Reduced Face Value: Rs. 96,482 per debenture
- Effective Date: 12 March 2026
- Settlement Number: DR-838/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a corporate action under existing BSE debt market framework governing part redemption of privately placed NCDs.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the reduced face value of Rs. 96,482 per debenture effective 12 March 2026.
- All trades in the debenture (ISIN: INE0N1Z08013) from the effective date onward must reflect the reduced face value.
- Members should update their systems to account for settlement DR-838/2025-2026.

## Important Dates

- **Record Date:** 12 March 2026
- **Effective Date for Reduced Face Value:** 12 March 2026
- **Notice Date:** 09 March 2026

## Impact Assessment

This part redemption affects holders of Rajgarh Transmission Limited's 7.03% privately placed NCDs maturing on 05 June 2030. The face value will be reduced to Rs. 96,482 per debenture post-redemption. Impact is limited to existing NCD holders in the debt segment; broader market impact is minimal given the privately placed nature of the instrument.
