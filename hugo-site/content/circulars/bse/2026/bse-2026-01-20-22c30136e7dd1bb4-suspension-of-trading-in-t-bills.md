## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Treasury Bill 91TB220126 (Scrip Code: 805133, ISIN: IN002025X307) effective from January 21, 2026, due to its maturity on January 22, 2026. Trading members are advised not to deal with this T-Bill from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- T-Bill 91TB220126 will mature on January 22, 2026
- Trading suspension effective from January 21, 2026
- Scrip Code: 805133
- ISIN: IN002025X307
- Notice issued under reference DR-803/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational procedure for maturing debt instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal with the specified T-Bill from January 21, 2026
- Members should ensure their trading systems and operations reflect this suspension
- No new trades should be executed in this instrument after the suspension date

## Important Dates

- **January 20, 2026**: Notice date
- **January 21, 2026**: Trading suspension effective date
- **January 22, 2026**: T-Bill redemption/maturity date

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine administrative action for a single maturing T-Bill. Trading suspension one day prior to maturity is standard practice to facilitate smooth settlement and redemption. Investors holding this T-Bill will receive redemption proceeds on the maturity date. No broader implications for debt market operations or other securities.
