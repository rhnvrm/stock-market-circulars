## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of SHREE RAM TWISTEX LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544716) will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (T Group) to the Rolling segment under B Group, effective Tuesday, March 17, 2026. This is a continuation of the Exchange's earlier Notice No. 20260227-42 dated February 27, 2026.

## Key Points

- Equity shares of SHREE RAM TWISTEX LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544716) will move from T Group to B Group.
- Transfer is from Trade for Trade (T Group) segment to Rolling settlement segment.
- Effective date of transfer: Tuesday, March 17, 2026.
- This notice continues from BSE Notice No. 20260227-42 dated February 27, 2026.
- Contact person: Mr. Parag Jain, Tel. No. 022 2272 8685.

## Regulatory Changes

The scrip is being reclassified from the Trade for Trade (T Group) segment to the Rolling segment (B Group). Under Trade for Trade, each trade must result in delivery and cannot be squared off intra-day. The move to Rolling settlement (B Group) allows normal rolling settlement with netting of trades.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members are informed to update their systems accordingly ahead of March 17, 2026.
- Members should note the change in settlement mechanism for this scrip from the effective date.
- For queries, Trading Members may contact Mr. Parag Jain at Tel. No. 022 2272 8685.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** March 2, 2026
- **Reference Notice:** No. 20260227-42 dated February 27, 2026
- **Effective Date of Transfer:** Tuesday, March 17, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The transfer from T Group to B Group has a moderate market impact. Previously, trades in SHREE RAM TWISTEX LIMITED required mandatory delivery under the Trade for Trade mechanism, which limits speculative activity and intra-day squaring off. Upon transfer to the Rolling (B Group) segment from March 17, 2026, traders will be able to net off positions and normal rolling settlement rules will apply, potentially increasing liquidity and trading activity in the scrip.
