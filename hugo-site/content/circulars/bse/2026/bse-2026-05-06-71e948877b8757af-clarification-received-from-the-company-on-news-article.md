## Summary

BSE's Surveillance department (DOSS) sought clarifications from four listed companies regarding news articles published in various newspapers, websites, and television channels. The exchange proactively reaches out to companies to verify the accuracy of such reports and keep members and investors informed. Two companies have already submitted their replies, while two others are still awaited.

## Key Points

- BSE issued clarification requests to four companies under its surveillance function to verify media reports.
- GE Vernova T&D India Ltd (Scrip Code: 522275) replied regarding an article on Economic Times.
- BASF India Ltd (Scrip Code: 500042) replied regarding an article on The Hindu BusinessLine.
- Reply is still awaited from Biocon Ltd (Scrip Code: 532523) regarding an Economic Times article.
- Reply is still awaited from Bondada Engineering Ltd (Scrip Code: 543971) regarding a Business Standard article.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine surveillance and disclosure notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Biocon Ltd and Bondada Engineering Ltd are required to submit their clarifications to BSE on the respective news articles referenced in the notice.
- All listed companies are expected to respond promptly to exchange queries regarding media reports under SEBI's continuous disclosure obligations.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 06 May 2026
- No specific deadline mentioned for pending replies; responses are expected on a priority basis.

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This is a standard BSE surveillance exercise to protect investor interests by ensuring accuracy of information circulating in media. The pending replies from Biocon and Bondada Engineering should be monitored; any material clarification or denial in those responses could have stock-specific price implications.
