## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members that the equity shares of Vegorama Punjabi Angithi Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on BSE's SME segment. The scrip has been assigned code 544765, symbol VPAL, and ISIN INE1L3801017. A separate notice will be issued to communicate the exact date of listing and further security details.

## Key Points

- Equity shares of Vegorama Punjabi Angithi Limited are approved for listing on BSE SME segment.
- Scrip Code: 544765 | Symbol: VPAL | ISIN: INE1L3801017
- Actual listing date will be communicated through a subsequent separate notice.
- Notice issued by Sachin Jain, Vice President, BSE Limited.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing admission notice under BSE's DCS-Listing department for the SME segment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the new scrip details and await the follow-up notice for the confirmed listing date before initiating any dealings in the shares of Vegorama Punjabi Angithi Limited.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: May 26, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced via a separate notice

## Impact Assessment

Limited immediate market impact as this is a preliminary listing notice for an SME segment company. The actual trading commencement date is pending. Market participants and trading members should monitor BSE for the follow-up notice confirming the listing date and full security details.
