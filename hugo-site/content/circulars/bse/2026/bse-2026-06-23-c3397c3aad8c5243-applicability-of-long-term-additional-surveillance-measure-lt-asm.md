## Summary

BSE has issued its periodic update on the Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LT-ASM) framework. Effective June 29, 2026, 100% margins will be imposed on all open positions as of June 25, 2026 and on new positions created from June 29, 2026 onwards for securities listed in Annexure I (Part A). Separately, securities moving to higher LT-ASM stages and those exiting the framework are also notified, effective June 24, 2026.

## Key Points

- 100% margins applicable from June 29, 2026 on open positions as of June 25, 2026 and all new positions thereafter (Annexure I, Part A)
- Securities moving to higher stages of LT-ASM framework effective June 24, 2026 (Annexure I, Part B)
- Lower Price Band imposed effective June 24, 2026 for stage-upgraded securities
- Securities continuing in LT-ASM but transferred to T / XT / MT / TS Group effective June 29, 2026 (Annexure I, Part D)
- Securities moving out of LT-ASM framework effective June 24, 2026 listed in Annexure II
- Consolidated list of all securities under the framework provided in Annexure III
- Shortlisting is purely for market surveillance and should not be construed as adverse action against any company

## Regulatory Changes

This notice references the original LT-ASM framework established via notice no. 20180321-46 (March 21, 2018) and subsequent revisions, including the most recent framework revisions via notices dated August 9, 2024 and September 20, 2024. The June 4, 2021 notice governs the transfer of securities to T/XT/MT/TS groups.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must ensure 100% margin compliance for positions in LT-ASM securities from June 29, 2026
- Price bands for securities exiting the framework will revert to pre-LT-ASM levels, subject to no other surveillance measure being applicable
- Securities shortlisted under LT-ASM are based on XBRL submissions by listed companies
- Members with clarifications may write to bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **June 24, 2026**: Stage upgrades to higher LT-ASM levels take effect; Lower Price Band imposed; securities begin moving out of LT-ASM framework
- **June 25, 2026**: Reference date for open positions subject to 100% margin requirement
- **June 29, 2026**: 100% margins effective on open positions (as of June 25) and all new positions; transfer of specified securities to T/XT/MT/TS Group

## Impact Assessment

The circular imposes significant margin requirements (100%) on affected securities, which will increase the cost of holding or initiating positions in LT-ASM-listed stocks. Traders and investors in these securities must be prepared for enhanced capital requirements. Securities moving to higher LT-ASM stages face tighter price bands, reducing intraday trading flexibility. Securities exiting the framework will see their price bands restored to pre-LT-ASM levels, potentially improving liquidity. The LT-ASM framework is designed to protect investors from abnormal price movements in potentially manipulated or fundamentally weak securities.
