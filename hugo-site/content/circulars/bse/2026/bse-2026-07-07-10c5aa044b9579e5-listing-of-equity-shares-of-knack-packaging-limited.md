## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that effective Wednesday, July 8, 2026, the equity shares of Knack Packaging Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange under the 'B' Group of Securities, following allotment in its public issue.

## Key Points

- Company: Knack Packaging Limited, Registered Office: 330 A Third Floor Kalasagar Mall, Opp Saibaba Temple, Near Sattadhar Crossroads, Ghatlodiya 380061
- Listing group: 'B' Group of Securities
- Number of securities: 12,22,65,164 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each fully paid up
- Distinctive number range: 1 to 122365164
- Scrip ID / Abbreviated Name on BOLT System: KNACK
- Scrip Code: 544814
- ISIN: INE17QZ01016
- Market Lot: 1
- Issue Price: Rs. 170/- per share (Face Value Rs. 10/-, premium Rs. 160/-)
- Date of Allotment in the public issue: July 6, 2026
- Pari Passu status: Yes
- Financial Year: March 31
- Lock-in details as per Annexure I; Shareholding pattern as per Annexure II
- Registrar to the issue: MUFG Intime India Private Limited (formerly Link Intime India Private Limited)

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing notice pursuant to completion of the public issue and allotment process. Securities will trade only in dematerialised form as per SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the scrip will be part of the Special Pre-open Session (SPOS) on July 8, 2026, per Exchange notice no. 20120216-29 dated February 16, 2012.
- The company has executed necessary corporate action for lock-in period marking in depository records for shares held in demat form.
- Queries may be directed to the company's Compliance Officer or BSE's Listing Department contacts (Mr. Parag Jain, Manager; Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President).

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 7, 2026
- Date of Allotment: July 6, 2026
- Listing and dealings effective from: July 8, 2026 (Wednesday)
- SPOS date: July 8, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard listing notification with limited market-wide impact, though it is directly relevant for trading members and investors dealing in Knack Packaging Limited (KNACK, Scrip Code 544814). Trading members should ensure systems recognize the new scrip and account for its participation in the Special Pre-open Session on the listing date.
