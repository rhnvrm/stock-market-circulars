## Summary

Onelife Capital Advisors Limited (BSE: 533632, NSE: ONELIFECAP) has announced an extension of its ongoing Rights Issue closing date and last date for on-market renunciation, as approved by its Rights Issue Committee on March 02, 2026. The Rights Issue, which opened on February 23, 2026, will now close on March 16, 2026 (extended from March 06, 2026), providing eligible equity shareholders additional time to apply.

## Key Points

- Rights Issue Committee meeting held on March 02, 2026 (1:45 PM to 2:15 PM) approved the further extension
- Issue Opening Date remains unchanged: Monday, February 23, 2026
- Issue Closing Date extended from Friday, March 06, 2026 to Monday, March 16, 2026
- Last Date for On-Market Renunciation extended from Monday, March 02, 2026 to Tuesday, March 10, 2026
- Last Date for receiving requests for Application Form and Rights Entitlement Letter extended from Monday, March 02, 2026 to Tuesday, March 10, 2026
- No changes to terms and conditions of the Letter of Offer (LOF), Abridged Letter of Offer (ALOF), or Application Form
- Addendum to the LOF will be made available on the Company's website, Registrar's website (KFintech), BSE, and NSE

## Regulatory Changes

This circular is filed pursuant to Regulation 30 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015. No changes to the underlying regulatory framework; this is an administrative revision to the Rights Issue schedule only.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company has notified both BSE and NSE of the revised schedule as required under Regulation 30 of SEBI LODR
- An addendum to the Letter of Offer reflecting the revised closing date and updated indicative post-issue timeline will be published on the Company's website (www.onelifecapital.in), Registrar's website (www.kfintech.com), BSE (www.bseindia.com), and NSE (www.nseindia.com)
- Eligible shareholders must apply or renounce their Rights Entitlements within the revised timelines

## Important Dates

| Event | Old Date | Revised Date |
|---|---|---|
| Issue Opening Date | Monday, February 23, 2026 | Monday, February 23, 2026 (unchanged) |
| Last Date for On-Market Renunciation | Monday, March 02, 2026 | Tuesday, March 10, 2026 |
| Last Date for Application Form / Rights Entitlement Letter Requests | Monday, March 02, 2026 | Tuesday, March 10, 2026 |
| Issue Closing Date | Friday, March 06, 2026 | Monday, March 16, 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

The extension provides eligible equity shareholders of Onelife Capital Advisors Limited an additional 10 days to apply for the Rights Issue or renounce their entitlements on-market. This is a company-specific action with limited broader market impact. Shareholders who had planned around the original March 06, 2026 deadline must note the revised March 16, 2026 closing date. Post-issue activity timelines will also shift accordingly, though the indicative revised schedule will be published via the LOF addendum.
