## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260515-21 informs trading members that new privately placed securities from four companies have been listed and admitted to trading on the BSE Debt Segment effective May 15, 2026. All securities are Commercial Papers traded in dematerialised form with a standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and a tick size of 1 paise.

## Key Points

- Four companies have listed Commercial Papers under fresh listing on the BSE Debt Segment.
- All securities are traded only in dematerialised form.
- Standard denomination is Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof; tick size is 1 paise.
- All allotments were deemed on May 14, 2026.
- Contact: BSE Debt Department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915 for clarifications.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notice under existing BSE debt market framework for privately placed commercial papers.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure transactions in these securities occur only in dematerialised form.
- Trades must be in standard denominations of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof.
