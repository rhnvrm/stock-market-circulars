## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 14,00,000 equity shares of Aanchal Ispat Ltd. (Scrip Code: 538812, ISIN: INE322R01022), allotted to Qualified Institutional Buyers (QIBs) under a Qualified Institutional Placement, are listed and admitted for trading on the Exchange effective Thursday, June 11, 2026.

## Key Points

- 14,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each listed under Scrip Code 538812 (ISIN: INE322R01022)
- Shares allotted to QIBs pursuant to a Qualified Institutional Placement (QIP)
- Issue price: Rs. 50/- per share
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 2833332 to 4233331
- Date of allotment: June 05, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing admission notice for newly allotted QIP shares.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should update their systems to reflect the new share count for Aanchal Ispat Ltd. (Scrip Code: 538812) effective June 11, 2026. For clarifications, members may contact Ms. Hetika Chandni at 022 2272 8772.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** June 05, 2026
- **Effective Date for Trading:** June 11, 2026 (Thursday)

## Impact Assessment

This listing adds 14,00,000 new equity shares to the tradeable float of Aanchal Ispat Ltd., representing a fresh capital raise via QIP at Rs. 50/- per share. The shares are pari-passu with existing equity, so there is no differential rights impact. The broader market impact is minimal given the company's small-cap nature; however, existing shareholders should note the dilution effect from the QIP issuance.
