## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of Craftech Paper Mills Limited for public comments. The document was filed on December 31, 2025 and contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations. Public comments are invited within 21 days from the notice date.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Craftech Paper Mills Limited on December 31, 2025
- Document available on BSE SME website for public viewing
- Contains all disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Minimum 21-day period for hosting and public comments
- Comments to be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard procedural notification as per existing ICDR Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- The draft offer document must remain available on the stock exchange website for at least 21 days
- Public comments, if any, must be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com within 21 days from January 02, 2026
- The document must contain all disclosures specified under Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations

## Important Dates

- **December 31, 2025**: Draft Offer Document filed by Craftech Paper Mills Limited
- **January 02, 2026**: Notice issued and document made available for public comments
- **January 23, 2026**: Deadline for public comments (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains specifically to a single SME IPO. It affects:

- Prospective investors interested in Craftech Paper Mills Limited IPO
- Market participants who wish to provide feedback on the draft offer document
- The company and its merchant bankers preparing for the IPO

The notice follows standard regulatory procedure for SME IPOs and does not affect existing listed securities or trading operations.
