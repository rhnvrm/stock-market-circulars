## Summary

BSE has issued a circular regarding the extension of the issue closing date and on-market renunciation date for Vineet Laboratories Limited's rights issue. This extension provides additional time for eligible shareholders to subscribe to the rights offering or to renounce their rights entitlement through on-market trading.

## Key Points

- Extension granted for rights issue closing date
- On-market renunciation period also extended
- Affects existing shareholders of Vineet Laboratories Limited
- Provides additional time for subscription and trading of rights entitlements

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a procedural extension for a specific company's rights issue timeline.

## Compliance Requirements

- Eligible shareholders should note the revised closing date for subscription
- Shareholders wishing to renounce rights should be aware of the extended on-market renunciation period
- Trading members should update their systems with the revised dates
- Rights entitlements remain tradable until the new renunciation end date

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: January 13, 2026
- Extended issue closing date and renunciation date details would be specified in the circular document

## Impact Assessment

**Impact on Shareholders**: Medium - Existing shareholders of Vineet Laboratories Limited have additional time to decide on rights subscription or to trade their renunciation rights in the market.

**Impact on Market**: Low to Medium - Limited to shareholders and those interested in participating in the rights issue of Vineet Laboratories Limited.

**Operational Impact**: The extension allows for better price discovery in the renunciation market and provides flexibility to investors for capital allocation decisions.
