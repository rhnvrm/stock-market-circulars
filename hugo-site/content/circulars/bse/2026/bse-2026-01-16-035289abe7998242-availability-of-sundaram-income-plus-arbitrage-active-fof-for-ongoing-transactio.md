## Summary

BSE has announced that SUNDARAM INCOME PLUS ARBITRAGE ACTIVE FOF scheme will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective January 19, 2026. The scheme will also support SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities. This follows the previous notice No. 20260102-20 and covers both Regular and Direct plan variants across Growth and IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal) options.

## Key Points

- SUNDARAM INCOME PLUS ARBITRAGE ACTIVE FOF reopening for ongoing transactions
- Six plan variants available: Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Reinvestment, and IDCW Payout options
- All variants categorized as Equity schemes
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities will be enabled
- Transactions available through BSE StAR MF Platform
- Supersedes previous notice No. 20260102-20

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding fund availability on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members. This is an informational notice about fund availability for transactions.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: January 19, 2026 - SUNDARAM INCOME PLUS ARBITRAGE ACTIVE FOF available for ongoing transactions
- **Notice Date**: January 16, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine operational notice affecting a single mutual fund scheme.

**Investor Impact**: Positive for investors interested in this specific Sundaram fund scheme, as they will now be able to perform ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, and systematic transactions (SIP/STP/SWP) starting January 19, 2026.

**Operational Impact**: BSE StAR MF Platform members can facilitate transactions in all six variants of the scheme (three Regular plan options and three Direct plan options) covering Growth and IDCW payout structures.

## Scheme Details

All six ISINs are now available for trading:
1. Regular Plan - Growth (INF903JA1MC4)
2. Regular Plan - IDCW Reinvestment (INF903JA1ME0)
3. Regular Plan - IDCW Payout (INF903JA1MD2)
4. Direct Plan - Growth (INF903JA1LZ7)
5. Direct Plan - IDCW Reinvestment (INF903JA1MB6)
6. Direct Plan - IDCW Payout (INF903JA1MA8)
