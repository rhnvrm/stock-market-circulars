## Summary

BSE has published the list of securities subject to demat auction under Settlement No. 794 dated January 09, 2026. The auction covers 82 securities across various companies with quantities ranging from 1 to 11,764 shares. This auction is part of the exchange's standard settlement process to handle delivery shortfalls.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 794
- Settlement Date: January 09, 2026
- Total Securities Listed: 82 different scrips
- Quantity Range: From 1 share (multiple securities) to 11,764 shares (NEWTIME INFR)
- Notable high-volume auctions: NEWTIME INFR (11,764 shares), SWOJAS FOODS (5,496 shares), Jonjua Overs (4,286 shares)
- Includes major stocks like ITC LTD (250 shares), BHARAT HEAVY (352 shares), WIPRO LTD (6 shares)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine operational circular for demat auction settlement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members with shortages in the listed securities must participate in the demat auction
- Members must ensure settlement of their delivery obligations as per BSE settlement procedures
- Participants must comply with auction timelines and procedures set by BSE

## Important Dates

- Auction Settlement Date: January 09, 2026
- Settlement No.: 794

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - The auction affects 82 securities with varying liquidity levels. While most quantities are small, some securities like NEWTIME INFR show significant shortfall quantities.

**Operational Impact:** Medium - Trading members with delivery shortfalls in these securities must participate in the auction mechanism to close out their positions. The auction may result in higher procurement costs for members facing shortages.

**Investor Impact:** Low to Medium - Investors in affected securities may experience temporary price volatility during the auction window. However, this is part of standard market settlement operations.
