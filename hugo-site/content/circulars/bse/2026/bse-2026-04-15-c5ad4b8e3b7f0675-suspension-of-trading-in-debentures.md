## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260415-6 informs trading members that three companies have fixed record dates for interest payment or redemption of their Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs)/Bonds. Trading members are advised not to deal in the debentures/bonds of these companies from the respective ex-dates.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to specific NCD/Bond scrips of NTPC Limited, IndiGrid Infrastructure Trust, and Happiest Minds Technologies Limited
- Suspension is for the purpose of Payment of Interest and/or Redemption of Debentures/Bonds
- All three instruments have ex-dates and record dates coinciding in the third week of April 2026
- Settlements fall under the 2026-2027 settlement cycle

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice under BSE's Listing Operations framework advising trading members of upcoming record dates for debt instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must refrain from dealing in the specified debentures/bonds of the listed companies from their respective ex-dates
- Members must note the ISIN numbers and scrip codes to ensure compliance with the suspension advisory

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited to debt segment participants holding or trading in the specified NCD/Bond scrips. Equity trading in NTPC and Happiest Minds Technologies is unaffected. The suspension is temporary and instrument-specific, lasting through the respective record dates. Bondholders eligible for interest or redemption payments should ensure their holdings are reflected prior to the ex-dates. IndiGrid Infrastructure Trust's INVIT-linked NCD is also covered under this advisory.
