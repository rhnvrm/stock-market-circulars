## Summary

Hannah Joseph Hospital Limited has filed its shareholding pattern under Regulation 31(1)(a) as of January 30, 2026. The company has a total of 22,698,363 equity shares held by 628 shareholders. Promoter and Promoter Group hold 68.83% of total shares, while Public shareholders hold 31.17%. The company has declared no partly paid shares, convertible securities, warrants, ESOPs, depository receipts, locked-in shares, pledged shares, or shares under non-disposal undertaking.

## Key Points

- Total equity shares: 22,698,363 held by 628 shareholders
- Promoter & Promoter Group: 6 shareholders holding 15,624,400 shares (68.83%)
- Public shareholders: 622 shareholders holding 7,073,963 shares (31.17%)
- Dematerialized shares: 18,404,363 shares (81.08% of total)
- No partly paid shares, convertible securities, warrants, or ESOPs outstanding
- No shares locked-in, pledged, or under non-disposal undertaking
- No depository receipts issued against shares
- No shares with differential voting rights
- Filing date: January 30, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard disclosure filing.

## Compliance Requirements

This filing satisfies the requirement under Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015 for periodic disclosure of shareholding pattern.

## Important Dates

- Filing Date: January 30, 2026
- Circular Publication: February 1, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine compliance disclosure with no immediate market impact. The shareholding pattern shows stable promoter holding at 68.83% with no encumbrances. The high level of dematerialization (81.08%) indicates good market infrastructure participation. No concerns regarding share pledging, lock-ins, or other encumbrances that could affect stock liquidity or corporate governance.
