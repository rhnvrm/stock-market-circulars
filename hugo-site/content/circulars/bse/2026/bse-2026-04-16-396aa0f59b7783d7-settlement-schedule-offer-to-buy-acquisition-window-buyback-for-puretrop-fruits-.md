## Summary

BSE, through ICCL (Indian Clearing Corporation Limited), has published the settlement schedule for the Offer to Buy via the Acquisition Window under the Buyback mechanism for PURETROP FRUITS LIMITED. This notice (No. 20260416-4) provides the settlement timeline that participants must follow when tendering shares in the buyback acquisition window.

## Key Points

- Notice No.: 20260416-4, dated 16 April 2026
- Issued by the ICCL department under the Settlement/RMS category
- Applicable to the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Buyback) for PURETROP FRUITS LIMITED
- Segment: General
- No attachments accompanying this notice

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a procedural notice providing the settlement schedule for an existing buyback offer to buy through the acquisition window mechanism as per SEBI buyback regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and participants tendering shares on behalf of shareholders in the PURETROP FRUITS LIMITED buyback must adhere to the settlement schedule specified by ICCL.
- Clearing members are required to ensure timely delivery of shares and settlement of funds as per the schedule published in this notice.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 16 April 2026
- Specific settlement dates for the acquisition window are governed by this schedule (refer to the official PDF for detailed date-wise settlement timelines).

## Impact Assessment

This notice has a moderate, company-specific impact, affecting shareholders and trading members participating in the PURETROP FRUITS LIMITED buyback. The acquisition window settlement schedule ensures orderly processing of buyback transactions on BSE. Participants in the buyback must align their settlement obligations with the prescribed schedule to avoid any penalties or settlement failures.
