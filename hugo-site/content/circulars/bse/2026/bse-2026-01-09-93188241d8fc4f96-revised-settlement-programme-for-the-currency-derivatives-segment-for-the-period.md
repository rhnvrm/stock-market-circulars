## Summary

BSE has issued a revised settlement programme for the Currency Derivatives Segment covering the period from January 1, 2026 to January 31, 2026. The circular provides the daily settlement dates corresponding to each trade date and specifies the expiry and final settlement dates for January 2026 currency future contracts.

## Key Points

- Revised settlement schedule covers January 1-31, 2026
- Daily settlement dates are typically T+1 (next business day after trade date)
- Settlement holiday observed on January 15, 2026 (settlement for January 14 trades occurs on January 16)
- January 28, 2026 expiry contracts settle on January 30, 2026
- All settlement dates account for weekends and holidays

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine operational update to the settlement calendar.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must adhere to the revised settlement schedule
- Clearing members must ensure funds/securities availability as per the published settlement dates
- Back-office systems should be updated to reflect the revised settlement timeline

## Important Dates

- **Effective Period**: January 1, 2026 to January 31, 2026
- **Settlement Holiday**: January 15, 2026
- **January 2026 Contract Expiry (Last Trading Day)**: January 28, 2026
- **January 2026 Contract Final Settlement Date**: January 30, 2026
- **Month-end Settlement**: January 30 trades settle on February 2, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Operational Impact**: Medium - All participants in currency derivatives segment must align their settlement operations with the revised schedule. Clearing members need to ensure adequate liquidity planning around the settlement holiday on January 15, 2026.

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine administrative update with no impact on trading activities or market structure.

**Risk Management**: Settlement teams must update their systems to reflect the modified settlement cycle, particularly noting the gap on January 15 and the January 2026 contract expiry settlement on January 30.
