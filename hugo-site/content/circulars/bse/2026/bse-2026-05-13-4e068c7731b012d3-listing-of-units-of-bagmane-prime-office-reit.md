## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260513-29 dated 13 May 2026 announcing the listing of units of Bagmane Prime Office REIT on the Exchange. This notice is issued by the DCS-Listing department under the Equity segment and includes relevant annexures for market participants.

## Key Points

- BSE Notice No. 20260513-29 announces the listing of Bagmane Prime Office REIT units
- The listing falls under the Equity segment, Company-related category
- Issued by the DCS-Listing department on 13 May 2026
- Annexures are attached to provide additional listing details
- Bagmane Prime Office REIT is a Real Estate Investment Trust focusing on office properties

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This notice formalizes the admission of Bagmane Prime Office REIT units to trading on BSE, in compliance with SEBI REIT regulations and BSE listing requirements.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants, brokers, and trading members should take note of the new REIT listing
- Investors should review the attached annexures for specific trading parameters, lot sizes, and other listing details
- All trading in Bagmane Prime Office REIT units must comply with applicable SEBI and BSE regulations for REITs

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 13 May 2026
- Listing Date: 13 May 2026 (as indicated by the notice date and standard BSE listing procedure)

## Impact Assessment

The listing of Bagmane Prime Office REIT on BSE is a high-impact event for the real estate investment trust market in India. It provides retail and institutional investors with an opportunity to invest in commercial office real estate through an exchange-traded instrument. The listing expands the REIT universe available on BSE, increases market depth in the REIT segment, and may attract interest from income-seeking investors given REITs' mandatory distribution requirements. Brokers and trading members will need to update their systems to accommodate trading in the new REIT units.
