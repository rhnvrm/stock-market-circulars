## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures of Aadhar Housing Finance Limited (ISIN: INE538L07213, Code: 953289) effective January 14, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the no-dealings date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to debentures with ISIN INE538L07213 (9.55% debentures maturing 29-1-26)
- Record date fixed: January 14, 2026
- No dealings permitted from: January 14, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Notice reference: DR-798/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard operational suspension for corporate action processing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from January 14, 2026
- Trading members must ensure compliance with the no-dealings restriction
- All exchange participants must take note of the suspension

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 6, 2026
- **Record Date**: January 14, 2026
- **No Dealings From**: January 14, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders and traders of this specific debenture series. The suspension is temporary and procedural, facilitating the scheduled redemption and interest payment process.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders will receive redemption proceeds and final interest payment. No trading liquidity available from the suspension date.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to prevent trades in the suspended security and inform clients holding these debentures about the redemption process.
