## Summary

BSE has listed 527,500 new equity shares of Suyog Telematics Limited (Scrip Code: 537259) issued to promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. The securities will be available for trading from January 30, 2026, and are subject to lock-in until July 30, 2027.

## Key Points

- 527,500 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 560/-
- Issue price: Rs. 570/- per share
- Issued to promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distinguished numbers: 11189621 to 11717120
- ISIN: INE442P01014
- Date of allotment: October 10, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities listing and should update their systems accordingly for trading commencing January 30, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: October 10, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: January 30, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry**: July 30, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as this is a routine preferential allotment to promoters following warrant conversion. The entire allotment of 527,500 shares is subject to lock-in until July 30, 2027, preventing immediate trading by the allottees. The shares will rank pari-passu with existing equity shares once the lock-in period expires.
