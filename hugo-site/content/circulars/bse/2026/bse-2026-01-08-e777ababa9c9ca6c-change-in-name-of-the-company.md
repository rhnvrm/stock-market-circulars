## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for the company previously known as Vintron Informatics Limited. The company's name has been officially changed to Reganto Enterprises Limited with effect from December 12, 2025. The company operates under CIN L72100DL1991PLC045276 and remains a company limited by shares.

## Key Points

- Former name: Vintron Informatics Limited
- New name: Reganto Enterprises Limited
- CIN: L72100DL1991PLC045276
- Original incorporation name: Vintron Industries Limited
- Company type: Limited by shares
- Registered office: 1117, 11th Floor, Hemkunt Chambers, 89, Nehru Place, New Delhi, South Delhi- 110019, Delhi, India
- Certificate issued by: Central Processing Centre, ROC
- Approved by: Sabari Raj, Assistant/Deputy Registrar of Companies

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate name change process executed under the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014, pursuant to rule 29.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display the old name (Vintron Informatics Limited) along with the new name (Reganto Enterprises Limited) for a period of two years at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- All stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company and its directors on the MCA website at www.mca.gov.in/MCA21
- The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders

## Important Dates

- Effective date of name change: December 12, 2025
- Certificate issuance date: December 12, 2025 (digitally signed at 12:23:31 IST)
- Old name display requirement period: Two years from December 12, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a purely administrative change with minimal market impact. The name change does not affect:
- Company's legal status or corporate structure
- Rights and liabilities of shareholders, creditors, or other stakeholders
- Ongoing contracts or obligations
- CIN or incorporation details

The company must ensure compliance with the two-year dual-name display requirement. All correspondence, filings, and public documents should reflect the new name while maintaining references to the former name during the transition period.
