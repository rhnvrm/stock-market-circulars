## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that the equity shares of Vegorama Punjabi Angithi Limited (SME IPO, Scrip Code: 544765) will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment effective Thursday, June 11, 2026. Post-transfer, the stock will be classified under the M Group. This notice is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20260526-31 dated May 26, 2026.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 544765 (Vegorama Punjabi Angithi Limited – SME IPO)
- Current segment: Trade for Trade (MT Group)
- New segment: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective date: Thursday, June 11, 2026
- Contact person: Mr. Anurag Jain, Tel: 022-2272 8822

## Regulatory Changes

The stock is being moved out of the Trade for Trade (TFT) settlement mechanism under the MT Group and into the standard Rolling settlement under the M Group. In TFT, each trade results in mandatory delivery and no netting of positions is allowed. The shift to Rolling segment enables normal netting and settlement practices.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should update their systems and risk frameworks to reflect the new segment classification of Vegorama Punjabi Angithi Limited (Scrip Code: 544765) from June 11, 2026. No specific compliance filing is required from members beyond awareness of the change.

## Important Dates

- **May 26, 2026**: Original notice issued (Notice No. 20260526-31)
- **May 27, 2026**: This continuation notice issued
- **June 11, 2026**: Effective date of transfer from MT Group (Trade for Trade) to M Group (Rolling segment)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine post-IPO group migration for a small-cap SME stock. The transfer from Trade for Trade to Rolling settlement typically occurs after a stabilisation period following IPO listing and reflects improved liquidity or compliance status. Impact is limited to traders and investors holding or tracking this specific scrip. Broader market impact is negligible.
