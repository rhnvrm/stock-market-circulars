## Summary

BSE has announced the settlement calendar for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for P.M TELELINNKS LIMITED. The takeover bidding window will run from January 13, 2026 to January 27, 2026, with settlement number 2026005. Obligation download and settlement dates are scheduled for February 9 and February 10, 2026 respectively.

## Key Points

- Settlement Type: Takeover
- Settlement Number: 2026005
- Bid Start Date: January 13, 2026
- Bid End Date: January 27, 2026
- Obligation Download Date: February 9, 2026
- Settlement Date: February 10, 2026
- Demat shares must be delivered only through Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism available in both depositories
- Market Type "Takeover" and settlement number 2026005 must be used by Members/Custodians in the depository system
- CDSL & NSDL early pay-in securities will be earmarked/blocked in clients' accounts
- No EPN file upload required by Members & Custodians

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The circular follows the Revised Guidelines of Mechanism for acquisition of shares through Stock Exchange as per Notice No.20200528-32 dated May 28, 2020.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members/Custodians must deliver demat shares through Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism only
- Participants must ensure early pay-in of securities is reflected in BSE/ICCL systems before prescribed timelines for accepting/confirming bids
- Early pay-in process must be completed sufficiently in advance before closure of BSE OTB Bidding window
- Members must specify Market Type "Takeover" and Settlement No. 2026005 in depository system
- Members should refer to MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for client-wise Stamp Duty charges
- Securities will be automatically earmarked/blocked in requisite market type and settlement number

## Important Dates

- **January 13, 2026**: Bid Start Date
- **January 27, 2026**: Bid End Date
- **February 9, 2026**: Obligation Download Date
- **February 10, 2026**: Settlement Date

## Impact Assessment

This takeover offer will impact shareholders of P.M TELELINNKS LIMITED who wish to participate in the acquisition window. Members and custodians handling transactions for this security must ensure compliance with early pay-in requirements and timeline adherence. The settlement process spans approximately one month from bid opening to final settlement. Market participants must coordinate with depositories (CDSL/NSDL) for proper earmarking of securities.
