## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260223-16 informing members that HDFC NIFTY India Consumption Index Fund will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption, along with SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities on the BSE StAR MF Platform, effective February 24, 2026. This follows the earlier Notice No. 20260203-28.

## Key Points

- HDFC NIFTY India Consumption Index Fund resumes ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform from February 24, 2026.
- Both Regular Plan and Direct Plan (Growth option) are available.
- Facilities available: subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP.
- ISIN for Regular Plan – Growth: INF179KC1JZ3.
- ISIN for Direct Plan – Growth: INF179KC1KA4.
- Scheme category: Equity (Index Fund tracking NIFTY India Consumption Index).

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notice regarding the availability of a mutual fund scheme for transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of BSE StAR MF Platform should update their systems to enable transactions in the two scheme ISINs (INF179KC1JZ3 and INF179KC1KA4) from February 24, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **February 24, 2026**: Effective date for reopening of ongoing subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This is a routine operational update enabling investors to transact in HDFC NIFTY India Consumption Index Fund through the BSE StAR MF Platform. Distributors and investors who previously could not transact will now have access from February 24, 2026. No changes to fund strategy, fees, or regulatory framework.
