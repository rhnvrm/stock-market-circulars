## Summary

BSE has informed members that JIO BLACKROCK MUTUAL FUND will launch a New Fund Offer (NFO) for PRISM HYBRID LONG SHORT FUND under the Specialized Investment Funds (SIF) category on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The NFO opens on June 29, 2026 and closes on July 13, 2026, with SIP facility available. This notice follows an earlier notice (20260625-12) introducing the SIF category for JIO BLACKROCK MUTUAL FUND.

## Key Points

- NFO for PRISM HYBRID LONG SHORT FUND (SIF) available on BSE StAR MF Platform from June 29 to July 13, 2026
- Two plan variants offered: Regular Plan – Growth (ISIN: INF22M030019) and Direct Plan – Growth (ISIN: INF22M030027)
- Both schemes are classified under the EQUITY category
- SIP/XSIP facility available alongside lump sum purchases
- Tentative allotment date for both schemes is July 17, 2026
- Clear funds must be available in the ICCL account by 6:00 PM on July 17, 2026
- Cheque and OTM payments accepted during NFO period subject to fund clearance by allotment date

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice operationalises the earlier introduction of the Specialized Investment Funds (SIF) category for JIO BLACKROCK MUTUAL FUND on BSE StAR MF Platform (as per notice 20260625-12).

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clients initiate funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day; transactions without timely fund initiation will be cancelled and CC with 'N' flag will be reported to the RTA/AMC
- Transactions with initiated payments remain pending until 3:00 PM on the last day of funds pay-out to AMC for the NFO or T+5 day, whichever is earlier
- SIP/XSIP registrations must be authenticated by clients on or before 3:00 PM on July 13, 2026 (NFO closure date)
- Lump sum purchases on BSE StAR MF Platform extended till 6:00 PM on July 13, 2026

## Important Dates

- **June 29, 2026**: NFO opens on BSE StAR MF Platform
- **July 13, 2026 (3:00 PM)**: Deadline for SIP/XSIP client authentication
- **July 13, 2026 (6:00 PM)**: NFO closes for lump sum purchases
- **July 17, 2026**: Tentative allotment date for both schemes
- **July 17, 2026 (6:00 PM)**: Deadline for clear funds in ICCL account for allotment eligibility

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine NFO operational notice with limited market-wide impact. It is relevant to BSE members and distributors who facilitate mutual fund transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The PRISM HYBRID LONG SHORT FUND is a Specialized Investment Fund (SIF), a relatively new product category, which may attract attention from high-net-worth and institutional investors. Members should update their systems to support SIF transactions and communicate fund initiation deadlines to clients to avoid cancellations.
