## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260227-13 informs trading members of the suspension of trading in commercial paper issued by SBICAP Securities Limited and Mindspace Business Parks REIT. Both instruments have fixed record dates for redemption in March 2026, and trading members are advised not to deal in these commercial papers from their respective record dates.

## Key Points

- Two commercial paper instruments are subject to trading suspension ahead of redemption
- SBICAP Securities Limited (SBICAPSL-06-03-26-CP, ISIN: INE212K14CV7, BSE Code: 730717) — no dealings from 05/03/2026
- Mindspace Business Parks REIT (MBPREIT-9-3-26-CP, ISIN: INE0CCU14112, BSE Code: 730079) — no dealings from 06/03/2026
- Reference circulars: DR-833/2025-2026 and DR-834/2025-2026
- Issued under the Debt segment, Corporate Actions category

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice under existing BSE procedures for commercial paper redemptions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all transactions in SBICAP Securities Limited commercial paper (INE212K14CV7) on and from 05/03/2026
- Trading members must cease all transactions in Mindspace Business Parks REIT commercial paper (INE0CCU14112) on and from 06/03/2026
- Members should update their systems and desk instructions to block trading in these ISINs from the respective dates

## Impact Assessment

Impact is low and limited to participants holding or trading these specific commercial paper instruments in the debt segment. Both suspensions are routine actions tied to scheduled maturities/redemptions. No broader market disruption is expected. Affected counterparties should ensure settlement of any outstanding positions prior to the respective record dates.
