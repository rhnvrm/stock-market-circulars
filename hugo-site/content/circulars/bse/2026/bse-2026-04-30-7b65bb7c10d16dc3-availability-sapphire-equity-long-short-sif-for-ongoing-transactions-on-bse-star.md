## Summary

BSE has informed members that SAPPHIRE EQUITY LONG-SHORT SIF will reopen for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP facility on the BSE StAR MF Platform with effect from May 04, 2026. This follows Notice No. 20260410-2 dated April 10, 2026. Six scheme variants are available under both Regular and Direct plans.

## Key Points

- SAPPHIRE EQUITY LONG-SHORT SIF reopens for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform from May 04, 2026
- Facility covers subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP
- Six scheme variants available across Regular and Direct plans
- All schemes fall under the EQUITY category
- ISINs assigned to each scheme variant

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice operationalises the earlier Notice No. 20260410-2 dated April 10, 2026, which had announced the reopening of this SIF.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members (brokers/distributors) on BSE StAR MF Platform should update their systems to enable transactions in the listed SAPPHIRE EQUITY LONG-SHORT SIF schemes from May 04, 2026
- Ensure correct ISIN mapping for all six scheme variants before processing transactions

## Important Dates

- **April 10, 2026**: Original notice (20260410-2) issued regarding reopening of the scheme
- **April 30, 2026**: This notice issued confirming scheme details and ISINs
- **May 04, 2026**: Effective date for reopening of ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP on BSE StAR MF Platform

## Impact Assessment

This notice has a moderate operational impact on mutual fund distributors and investors using the BSE StAR MF Platform. Investors in SAPPHIRE EQUITY LONG-SHORT SIF can resume transactions from May 4, 2026. The scheme covers six variants:

| Sr. No. | Scheme Name | Category | ISIN |
|---------|-------------|----------|------|
| 1 | SAPPHIRE EQUITY LONG-SHORT SIF - REGULAR PLAN - GROWTH | EQUITY | INF090I30014 |
| 2 | SAPPHIRE EQUITY LONG-SHORT SIF - REGULAR PLAN - IDCW REINVESTMENT | EQUITY | INF090I30022 |
| 3 | SAPPHIRE EQUITY LONG-SHORT SIF - REGULAR PLAN - IDCW PAYOUT | EQUITY | INF090I30030 |
| 4 | SAPPHIRE EQUITY LONG-SHORT SIF - DIRECT PLAN - GROWTH | EQUITY | INF090I30048 |
| 5 | SAPPHIRE EQUITY LONG-SHORT SIF - DIRECT PLAN - IDCW REINVESTMENT | EQUITY | INF090I30055 |
| 6 | SAPPHIRE EQUITY LONG-SHORT SIF - DIRECT PLAN - IDCW PAYOUT | EQUITY | INF090I30063 |

No broader market impact anticipated; this is a platform-level operational enablement notice.
