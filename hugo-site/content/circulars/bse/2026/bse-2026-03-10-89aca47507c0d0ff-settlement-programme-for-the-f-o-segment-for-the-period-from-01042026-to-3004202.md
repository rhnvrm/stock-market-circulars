## Summary

BSE has issued the settlement programme for the Futures & Options (F&O) segment for the period from April 1, 2026 to April 30, 2026. The circular, issued by Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd. (ICCL), specifies the daily settlement dates corresponding to each trade date during the month.

## Key Points

- Settlement programme covers the F&O segment for the full month of April 2026 (01.04.2026 to 30.04.2026).
- Daily settlement dates are T+1 business days, adjusted for market holidays.
- Notable gaps in trading dates indicate exchange holidays (e.g., April 3-5, April 11-12, April 14, April 18-19).
- The last trade date of April 30, 2026 has a settlement date of May 4, 2026, due to intervening holidays.
- Circular issued by Tajindersingh Virdi, Assistant Vice President, Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notification providing the scheduled settlement calendar.

## Compliance Requirements

- All F&O segment participants (brokers, clearing members, trading members) must adhere to the published settlement dates.
- Clearing members must ensure funds and securities are available on the respective daily settlement dates.
- No special compliance action required beyond standard settlement obligations.

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a medium operational impact on all F&O segment participants. Clearing members, brokers, and trading members need to align their back-office operations, margin calls, and fund management with the published settlement schedule. The multiple holiday-adjusted settlement dates (e.g., April 2 settling on April 6, April 10 settling on April 13) require careful planning. The last settlement date falling on May 4, 2026 for trades done on April 30 should be noted for month-end reconciliation purposes.
