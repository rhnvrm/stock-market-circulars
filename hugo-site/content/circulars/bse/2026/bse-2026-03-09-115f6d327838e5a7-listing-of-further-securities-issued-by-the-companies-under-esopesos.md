## Summary

BSE has notified the listing and admission for trading of further securities issued by 15 companies under their respective Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP/ESOS). These new securities are effective for trading from Tuesday, March 10, 2026. None of the listed securities are subject to any lock-in period.

## Key Points

- 15 companies have listed additional shares under ESOP/ESOS schemes
- All securities are effective for trading from March 10, 2026
- No lock-in period applies to any of the newly listed securities
- Shares span face values of ₹1, ₹2, and ₹10 depending on the company
- Total new shares range from 577 (Astec Lifesciences) to 399,870 (Moschip Technologies)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice informing market participants of newly listed ESOP/ESOS shares in accordance with standard BSE listing procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action is required from market participants. The notice is informational, advising that the following securities are now available for trading:

## Impact Assessment

The impact of this circular is low. ESOP/ESOS listings represent incremental additions to existing share capital as employee stock options are exercised. The share counts are modest relative to the total issued capital of each company. No dilution concern is significant at these volumes. Market participants holding or trading in these scrips should note the updated distribution number ranges for reconciliation purposes.
