## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 7,74,946 new equity shares of Shree Hari Chemicals Export Ltd. (Scrip Code: 524336) are listed and permitted to trade with effect from Friday, July 10, 2026. These shares were issued to promoters on a preferential basis at a price of Rs. 79.00 per share (face value Rs. 10/- plus premium of Rs. 69/-), following conversion of Compulsorily Convertible Debentures (CCDs). The new shares rank pari-passu with the existing equity shares of the company.

## Key Points

- 7,74,946 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each listed, issued at a premium of Rs. 69/- per share (total issue price Rs. 79.00/-)
- Shares issued to Promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of Compulsorily Convertible Debentures (CCDs)
- Distinctive numbers: 5591635 to 6366580
- ISIN: INE065E01017
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Entire allotment of 7,74,946 shares is under lock-in until 15/01/2028

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing notification under BSE Listing Operations.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should update their systems to reflect the additional listed shares and note the lock-in status of these shares (not available for trading until lock-in expiry on 15/01/2028).

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: 03/04/2026
- Effective Listing/Trading Date: Friday, July 10, 2026
- Lock-in Expiry: 15/01/2028

## Impact Assessment

The listing increases the company's paid-up equity share capital marginally through promoter preferential allotment, which typically signals promoter confidence but also results in modest dilution for existing shareholders. Since the shares are locked in until January 2028, there is no immediate impact on free-float liquidity or trading volumes. Overall market impact is limited given the small size of the allotment relative to typical free float.
