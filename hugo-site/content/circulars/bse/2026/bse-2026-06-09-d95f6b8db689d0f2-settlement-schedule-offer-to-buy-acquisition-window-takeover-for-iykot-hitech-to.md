## Summary

ICCL (Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd.) has published the settlement schedule for the Offer to Buy via Acquisition Window (Takeover) for IYKOT HITECH TOOLROOM LIMITED. The bidding window opens on June 10, 2026 and closes on June 23, 2026, with obligations downloadable on July 7, 2026 and final settlement on July 8, 2026 (Settlement No. 2026070).

## Key Points

- Settlement Type: Takeover (Settlement No. 2026070)
- Bid Start Date: June 10, 2026
- Bid End Date: June 23, 2026
- Obligation Download Date: July 7, 2026
- Settlement Date: July 8, 2026
- Demat shares must be delivered exclusively via the Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism available in both CDSL and NSDL depositories
- Market Type to be used in depository system: "(Takeover)" with Settlement No. 2026070
- CDSL & NSDL early pay-in securities will be earmarked/blocked in client accounts under the requisite market type and settlement number
- Members and Custodians are NOT required to upload any EPN file
- Members should refer to MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for client-wise Stamp Duty charges

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. Members and Custodians are directed to refer to Notice No. 20200528-32 dated May 28, 2020, for settlement procedures under the Revised Guidelines of Mechanism for acquisition of shares through Stock Exchange.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members/Custodians must complete early pay-in of securities in the Depository system sufficiently in advance of the prescribed timelines for closure of the BSE OTB Bidding window
- Early pay-in effect must be reflected in BSE/ICCL systems before the prescribed timelines for accepting/confirmation of Bids on the BSE OTB platform
- Use Market Type "(Takeover)" and Settlement No. 2026070 in depository system entries
- No EPN file upload is required for early pay-in

## Impact Assessment

This notice is operationally relevant to brokers (Members) and Custodians participating in the takeover offer for IYKOT HITECH TOOLROOM LIMITED. The early pay-in requirement means participants must plan securities delivery ahead of the OTB platform's bid closure timelines. The impact is limited to parties involved in this specific acquisition window and does not affect the broader market.
