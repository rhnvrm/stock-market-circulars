## Summary

BSE Limited, through Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd. (ICCL), has announced Demat Auction 633 for Rolling Settlement No. DR-633/2026-27. Trading members of the Exchange are invited to submit offers for purchase of securities that were short-delivered in this settlement. The auction offer entry window opens on May 20, 2026, with pay-in scheduled for May 21, 2026.

## Key Points

- Auction is for Rolling Settlement No. DR-633/2026-27
- Offers accepted on trading terminals on Wednesday, May 20, 2026
- Auction offer entry: 2:00 PM to 2:45 PM
- Auction matching: 2:45 PM to 3:00 PM
- Delivery statement and accepted auction offer reports: 3:00 PM to 3:30 PM
- Money statement and final reports: 3:35 PM to 5:30 PM
- Auction pay-in: Thursday, May 21, 2026 at 8:00 AM
- Members and Custodians must check auction pay-in obligations and execute securities pay-in within depository cut-off timings
- No corrections permitted for punching errors in securities code or rate
- BSE reserves the right to accept or reject any offer without assigning reasons

## Regulatory Changes

Following implementation of the Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment, settlement is now done through the Clearing Member of a Trading Member. The restriction on trading members offering securities in auction for settlements in which they have short-delivered such securities has been removed.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must check auction pay-in obligations for securities
- Securities pay-in must be executed in depositories within depository cut-off timings
- Members should refer to the cutoff rate shown in the "Auction Offer Entry Screen" before submitting offers
- Trading members must refer to notices on Corporate Actions (Ex-Rights, Ex-Bonus, Ex-Split, etc.) issued by respective departments
- Utmost care required when entering securities code and rate — no corrections permitted post-submission

## Important Dates

- **May 20, 2026**: Auction offer entry (2:00 PM – 2:45 PM), Matching (2:45 PM – 3:00 PM), Reports (3:00 PM – 5:30 PM)
- **May 21, 2026**: Auction pay-in at 8:00 AM

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice affecting trading members and custodians with obligations under Rolling Settlement DR-633/2026-27. The removal of the restriction on trading members offering securities in auctions where they had short deliveries is a notable structural change under the Uniform Membership framework, potentially increasing participation and liquidity in auction sessions. Members with short-delivery obligations must act promptly to meet the May 21 pay-in deadline.
