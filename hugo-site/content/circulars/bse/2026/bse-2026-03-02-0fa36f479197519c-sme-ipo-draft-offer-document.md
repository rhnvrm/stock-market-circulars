## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document of Susan Electricals India Limited, filed on February 28, 2026, on the BSE SME website. The document is available for a minimum period of 21 days in accordance with Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations, inviting public comments.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Susan Electricals India Limited on February 28, 2026
- Document hosted on the BSE SME website for public access
- Public comments invited for 21 days from the date of the notice (March 2, 2026)
- Comments to be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This notice is a standard procedural disclosure under Schedule VI of the SEBI (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations (ICDR Regulations).

## Compliance Requirements

- Public and market participants may review the Draft Offer Document on the BSE SME website
- Any comments or objections must be submitted within 21 days from March 2, 2026 (deadline: March 23, 2026)
- Comments to be sent to: equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Document Filing Date:** February 28, 2026
- **Notice Date:** March 2, 2026
- **Public Comment Deadline:** March 23, 2026 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal broad market impact. It is a routine SME IPO disclosure process, relevant to investors and analysts interested in Susan Electricals India Limited's upcoming SME IPO. The draft offer document is available for review at the BSE SME website, enabling informed public participation before the IPO proceeds.
