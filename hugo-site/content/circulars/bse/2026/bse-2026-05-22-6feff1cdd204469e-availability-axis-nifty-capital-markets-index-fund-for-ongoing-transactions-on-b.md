## Summary

BSE has informed members that AXIS NIFTY CAPITAL MARKETS INDEX FUND will reopen for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP facility on the BSE StAR MF Platform with effect from May 25, 2026. This follows Notice No. 20260501-2 dated May 01, 2026.

## Key Points

- The fund reopens for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform from May 25, 2026
- Both Regular Plan and Direct Plan Growth options are available
- Facilities covered include subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP
- Fund category is Equity; managed under Axis AMC (INF846K series ISINs)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notice restoring transaction availability for a previously restricted mutual fund scheme on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action required for members beyond processing transactions in these schemes through BSE StAR MF Platform from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **May 22, 2026**: Notice issued
- **May 25, 2026**: AXIS NIFTY CAPITAL MARKETS INDEX FUND reopens for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, and SIP on BSE StAR MF Platform

## Impact Assessment

Low operational impact. Investors and distributors using BSE StAR MF Platform can resume transacting in the two Axis Nifty Capital Markets Index Fund schemes (Regular Plan – ISIN INF846KA1531; Direct Plan – ISIN INF846KA1549) from May 25, 2026. No impact on broader market participants.
