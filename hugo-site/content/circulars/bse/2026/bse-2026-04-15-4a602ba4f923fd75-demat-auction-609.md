## Summary

BSE Limited (via Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd.) has announced Demat Auction 609 for Rolling Settlement No. DR-609/2026-27. Trading members are invited to submit offers for the purchase of securities through their trading terminals on April 15, 2026. The auction covers short-delivered securities in the equity segment.

## Key Points

- Auction offer entry is open on **April 15, 2026** between **2:00 PM and 2:45 PM**
- Auction matching takes place from **2:45 PM to 3:00 PM**
- Delivery statements and reports of accepted auction offers are available from **3:00 PM to 3:30 PM**
- Money statements and final reports are available from **3:35 PM to 5:30 PM**
- Pay-in for the auction is scheduled on **April 16, 2026 at 8:00 AM**
- Settlement is processed through the Clearing Member of the Trading Member under the Uniform Membership structure
- Restriction on trading members offering securities in auctions for settlements where they have short-delivered has been removed
- No corrections will be permitted for punching errors in securities code or rate

## Regulatory Changes

Following the implementation of the Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment, settlement is now routed through the Clearing Member of a Trading Member. The earlier restriction on trading members offering securities in auction for settlements in which they had short-delivered such securities has been lifted.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and custodians must check their auction pay-in obligations for securities
- Securities pay-in must be executed in depositories within depository cut-off timings
- Trading members must refer to the cutoff rate shown in the "Auction Offer Entry Screen" before submitting offers
- Members should review notices issued by respective departments regarding Corporate Actions (Ex-Rights, Ex-Bonus, Ex-Split, etc.) for relevant scrips
- Utmost care must be taken while entering securities codes and rates, as no corrections will be allowed after submission

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice affecting trading members and custodians who have short-delivery obligations in Rolling Settlement DR-609/2026-27. The auction mechanism allows the exchange to source securities that were not delivered by the original sellers. Members with open short-delivery positions must participate or ensure obligations are met. The removal of the restriction on offering in the same settlement where short-delivery occurred provides slightly more flexibility to members. No broad market impact is expected.
