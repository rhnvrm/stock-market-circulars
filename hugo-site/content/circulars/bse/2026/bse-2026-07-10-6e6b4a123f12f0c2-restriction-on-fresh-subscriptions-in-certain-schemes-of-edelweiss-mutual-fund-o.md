## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (Notice No. 20260710-4) informing members that Edelweiss Mutual Fund has restricted fresh subscriptions through Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) and Systematic Transfer Plan (STP-In) in seven designated schemes, effective July 10, 2026. This follows an earlier notice (No. 20251016-2 dated October 16, 2025) on the same subject. The changes have been implemented on the BSE StAR MF Platform based on a Notice-cum-Addendum issued by Edelweiss AMC.

## Key Points

- Fresh SIP and STP-In registrations will not be accepted after the cut-off time on July 10, 2026 for the designated schemes.
- Existing SIP/XSIP and STP-In registrations made prior to the effective date will continue to be processed at the AMC/RTA end without any change.
- Redemption, Switch-out, and STP-out transactions remain permitted without any amount restriction.
- The restriction applies to the following schemes:
  - Edelweiss ASEAN Equity Off-shore Fund
  - Edelweiss Europe Dynamic Equity Off-shore Fund
  - Edelweiss Emerging Markets Opportunities Equity Off-shore Fund
  - Edelweiss Greater China Equity Off-shore Fund
  - Edelweiss US Value Equity Offshore Fund
  - Edelweiss US Technology Equity Fund of Fund
  - Edelweiss MSCI India Domestic & World Healthcare 45 Index Fund

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory rule is introduced by BSE; the exchange is operationally implementing restrictions communicated by Edelweiss Mutual Fund via a Notice-cum-Addendum, updating the BSE StAR MF Platform to block fresh SIP/STP-In registrations in the named schemes.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and mutual fund distributors using the BSE StAR MF Platform must stop accepting fresh SIP and STP-In registrations in the listed schemes after the cut-off time on July 10, 2026.
- Existing SIP/STP-In mandates should continue to be honored without alteration.
- Redemption, switch-out, and STP-out transactions should continue to be processed normally, without amount restrictions.

## Important Dates

- July 10, 2026: Effective date of restriction on fresh SIP and STP-In registrations (cut-off time applies).
- Reference to prior Notice No. 20251016-2 dated October 16, 2025.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is operational and limited to distributors and investors dealing in these specific offshore and thematic Edelweiss schemes via the BSE StAR MF platform. There is no impact on listed equities or broader market trading. Investors with existing SIPs in these schemes are unaffected, and liquidity via redemption/switch-out remains fully available, minimizing investor disruption.
