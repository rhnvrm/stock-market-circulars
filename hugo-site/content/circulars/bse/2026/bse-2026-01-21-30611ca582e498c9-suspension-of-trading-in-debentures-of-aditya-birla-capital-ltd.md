## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in non-convertible debentures of Aditya Birla Capital Ltd (ISIN: INE860H07IH9, Code: 974499) effective January 28, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing the record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Company: ADITYA BIRLA CAPITAL LTD
- Debenture Series: ABCL-7.88%-12-2-26-PVT
- ISIN: INE860H07IH9
- BSE Code: 974499
- Record Date: January 28, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- No Dealings From: January 28, 2026
- Notice Reference: DR-807/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification for debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in the specified debentures from January 28, 2026
- Trading members are required to take note of the suspension and ensure compliance
- No transactions should be executed in the affected debenture series (INE860H07IH9) from the suspension date

## Important Dates

- **January 21, 2026**: Notice date and circular issuance
- **January 28, 2026**: Record date for redemption and interest payment
- **January 28, 2026**: Trading suspension effective date (no dealings from this date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders and traders of this specific debenture series of Aditya Birla Capital Ltd. The suspension is procedural and relates to the maturity and redemption process.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders whose names appear on the register as of the record date (January 28, 2026) will be eligible for interest payment and redemption proceeds. Investors will not be able to trade these debentures from January 28, 2026 onwards.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to prevent any transactions in this debenture series from the suspension date. This is a routine corporate action with no broader market implications beyond the specific instrument.
