## Summary

BSE has issued a circular regarding the forfeiture of equity shares of National General Industries Ltd. The circular lists shareholders whose equity shares have been forfeited, including multiple entries for Amit Bharat (Folio 101), Balwant Roy (Folio 107), Ved Prakash Reheja (Folio 108), Yogesh Bagai (Folio 109), Meena Goel (Folio 115), and Virender Chauhan (Folio 118 and 119). All affected shareholders are located in Ghaziabad.

## Key Points

- Forfeiture affects equity shares of National General Industries Ltd.
- Six shareholders affected with multiple share entries per shareholder
- Folio numbers: 101, 107, 108, 109, 115, 118, and 119
- All shareholders have Ghaziabad addresses
- Shareholders include: Amit Bharat (10 entries), Balwant Roy (5 entries), Ved Prakash Reheja (10 entries), Yogesh Bagai (5 entries), Meena Goel (10 entries), and Virender Chauhan (20 entries)
