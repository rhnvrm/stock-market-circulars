## Summary

Indian Clearing Corporation Limited (ICCL) has published the client margin reporting schedule for the Equity Segment for July 2026. Members are required to report client margins within the specified deadlines to avoid penalties.

## Key Points

- Covers all 23 trading days in July 2026 (01-Jul to 31-Jul 2026)
- Each trade date has a corresponding last date for margin reporting (typically T+5 working days) and a penalty date (one day after)
- Late or non-reporting of client margins will attract penalties as per the penalty date column
- Issued by ICCL on behalf of BSE under the Settlement/RMS category

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a routine monthly schedule notice issued by ICCL for operational compliance.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must report client margins for each trade date no later than the specified "Last Date of Margin Reporting"
- Failure to report by the last date will result in penalties applied on the "Penalty Date"
- Applies to all BSE members trading in the Equity Segment
