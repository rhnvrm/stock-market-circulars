## Summary

BSE has notified trading members about special live trading timings for the Equity Derivatives segment on Sunday, February 01, 2026, due to the presentation of Union Budget 2026. The trading session will follow a modified schedule starting from 7:00 AM with login and continuing until 4:15 PM for trade modifications.

## Key Points

- Special trading session scheduled for Sunday, February 01, 2026
- Login starts at 7:00 AM
- Pre-open session from 9:00 AM to 9:07/08 AM with system-driven random stoppage
- Continuous trading from 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM
- Trade modification window extends until 4:15 PM
- Notice issued on January 16, 2026 (Notice No. 20260116-34)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a one-time operational adjustment for the Union Budget presentation.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the modified trading timings
- Members should ensure systems and personnel are ready for Sunday trading operations
- Contact BSE Helpdesk (022-45720400/600) or Relationship Manager for any clarifications

## Important Dates

- **February 01, 2026 (Sunday)**: Special trading session for Equity Derivatives segment
  - Log-in: 7:00 AM - 9:00 AM
  - Pre-Open Order Entry: 9:00 AM - 9:07/08 AM
  - Order Matching & Confirmation: 9:08 AM - 9:12 AM
  - Buffer Period: 9:12 AM - 9:15 AM
  - Continuous Trading: 9:15 AM - 3:30 PM
  - Trade Modification: 9:15 AM - 4:15 PM

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: High - All equity derivatives trading participants must prepare for Sunday operations, which is unusual for Indian markets.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to arrange for staffing, system readiness, and infrastructure support on a Sunday. This includes risk management teams, dealers, and technical support.

**Strategic Consideration**: The special session allows market participants to react to Union Budget announcements in real-time, providing liquidity and price discovery opportunities immediately following the budget presentation.
