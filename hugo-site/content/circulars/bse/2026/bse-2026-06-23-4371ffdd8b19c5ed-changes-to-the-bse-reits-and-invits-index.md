## Summary

BSE Index Services Private Ltd (formerly Asia Index Pvt. Ltd.) has announced changes to the BSE REITS AND INVITS INDEX following a fast track IPO review. Two new securities will be added to the index effective at the open of trading on Tuesday, July 7, 2026.

## Key Points

- Two securities are being added to the BSE REITS AND INVITS INDEX via fast track IPO review
- Bagmane Prime Office REIT (Exchange Ticker: 544758) is being added
- Citius TransNet Investment Trust (Exchange Ticker: 544753) is being added
- Both additions are effective at the open of July 7, 2026
- No securities are being removed from the index in this rebalancing

## Regulatory Changes

The BSE REITS AND INVITS INDEX composition is being updated to include two newly listed instruments. This change follows the standard fast track IPO review process conducted by BSE Index Services Private Ltd for eligible REITs and InvITs.

## Compliance Requirements

- Fund managers and portfolio managers tracking or benchmarking against the BSE REITS AND INVITS INDEX should update their portfolios accordingly before the open of July 7, 2026
- Index-linked products (ETFs, index funds) referencing this index will need to rebalance to include the new constituents
- Queries may be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Announcement Date:** June 23, 2026
- **Effective Date:** July 7, 2026 (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

This index change has a medium market impact, primarily affecting passive investment vehicles such as ETFs and index funds that track the BSE REITS AND INVITS INDEX. Active fund managers using the index as a benchmark will also need to review their positioning relative to the updated composition. The addition of Bagmane Prime Office REIT and Citius TransNet Investment Trust broadens exposure within the index to newly listed real estate investment trusts and infrastructure investment trusts. Market participants have approximately two weeks from the announcement date to prepare for the rebalancing.
