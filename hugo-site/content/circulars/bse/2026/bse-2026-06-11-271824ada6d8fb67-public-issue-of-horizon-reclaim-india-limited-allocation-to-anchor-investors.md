## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that under the Anchor Investor (AI) portion of the Public Issue of Horizon Reclaim (India) Limited, 15,01,200 equity shares were subscribed on June 11, 2026, at a price of Rs. 103 per equity share.

## Key Points

- **Company:** Horizon Reclaim (India) Limited
- **Anchor Investor Allocation Date:** June 11, 2026
- **Shares Allocated:** 15,01,200 equity shares
- **Issue Price (Anchor):** Rs. 103 per equity share
- **Total Anchor Allocation Value:** Approximately Rs. 15.46 crore
- Notice issued by Mangesh Tayde, Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a procedural notification informing trading members of the anchor investor subscription under the existing IPO framework.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed for reference purposes. No direct compliance action is required from trading members in response to this notice. The announcement from the company dated June 11, 2026 is enclosed as an attachment (Anchor_Allocation).

## Important Dates

- **Anchor Investor Allocation Date:** June 11, 2026
- **Notice Date:** June 11, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The anchor investor allocation is a standard pre-IPO step that signals the commencement of the public issue process for Horizon Reclaim (India) Limited. Allocation of 15,01,200 shares at Rs. 103 to anchor investors provides a price benchmark for the broader public subscription. Impact is limited to investors and market participants tracking this specific IPO.
