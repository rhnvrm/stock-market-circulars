## Summary

BSE has issued a circular regarding the forfeiture of equity shares of National General Industries Ltd. The circular contains a list of shareholders whose shares are being forfeited, identified by folio numbers and shareholder details.

## Key Points

- Shares forfeited for multiple shareholders across different folio numbers (101, 107, 108, 109, 115, 118, 119)
- Affected shareholders include: Amit Bharat, Balwant Roy, Ved Prakash Reheja, Yogesh Bagai, Meena Goel, and Virender Chauhan
- All affected shareholders are located in Ghaziabad
- Folio-wise details provided include shareholder names, joint holder information, father's/husband's name, and addresses

## Shareholders Affected

**Folio 101:** Amit Bharat (S/O Vijay Bharat) - C/O Bhagwati Industries (India), G T Road, Ghaziabad

**Folio 107:** Balwant Roy (S/O Vasudev Reheja) - 174 Prem Nagar, S D College Building, Ghaziabad

**Folio 108:** Ved Prakash Reheja (S/O Vasudev Reheja) - 174 Prem Nagar, Opp Gurdwara, Ghaziabad

**Folio 109:** Yogesh Bagai (S/O Balwant Roy) - III C/58 Nehru Nagar, Ghaziabad

**Folio 115:** Meena Goel (W/O Satish Goel) - B-61 Lohia Nagar, Ghaziabad

**Folio 118:** Virender Chauhan (S/O Kunwar Lal) - C-230 Shastri Nagar, Ghaziabad

**Folio 119:** Virender Chauhan (S/O Kunwar Lal) - C-230 Shastri Nagar, Ghaziabad

## Compliance Requirements

- The exchange has published the list of forfeited shares for information and records
- Affected shareholders' equity holdings in National General Industries Ltd. have been forfeited

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: January 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a company-specific corporate action with minimal market impact. The forfeiture affects a limited number of shareholders and does not impact the broader trading or market operations. Investors and market participants should note this for record-keeping purposes regarding National General Industries Ltd.'s shareholding pattern.
