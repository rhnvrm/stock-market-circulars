## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document of FX Multitech Limited for its proposed SME IPO. The document was filed on December 31, 2025 and contains all disclosures as required under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations. The document is available on BSE SME website for a minimum period of 21 days for public comments.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by FX Multitech Limited on December 31, 2025
- Document complies with Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website: www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public comment period: 21 days from January 1, 2026
- Comments to be submitted to: equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural filing as per existing ICDR Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Interested parties may review the Draft Offer Document on BSE SME website
- Comments on the draft document should be submitted within 21 days (by January 22, 2026)
- Comments must be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **December 31, 2025**: Draft Offer Document filed by FX Multitech Limited
- **January 1, 2026**: Notice published, public comment period begins
- **January 22, 2026**: Last date for public comments (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market-wide impact as it relates to a single SME IPO filing. The impact is limited to:
- Potential investors interested in FX Multitech Limited's SME IPO
- Market participants who wish to provide feedback on the offer document
- Standard procedural requirement for SME IPO process on BSE platform

No immediate trading or compliance implications for existing market participants.
