## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260511-33 dated 11 May 2026 announcing the listing of equity shares of United Polyfab Gujarat Limited on the Exchange under the Equity segment, managed by the DCS-Listing department.

## Key Points

- Notice No.: 20260511-33
- Date of Notice: 11 May 2026
- Company: United Polyfab Gujarat Limited
- Segment: Equity
- Category: Company related
- Department: DCS-Listing
- Annexures are attached as part of the notice

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification under BSE's DCS-Listing framework.

## Compliance Requirements

Market participants, brokers, and investors should take note of the listing of United Polyfab Gujarat Limited's equity shares on BSE. Trading members should update their systems to reflect the new listing.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 11 May 2026
- Listing effective as communicated in the attached annexures

## Impact Assessment

The listing of United Polyfab Gujarat Limited provides investors with a new equity instrument available for trading on BSE. Impact is limited to participants interested in this company; no broad market or systemic implications are expected.
