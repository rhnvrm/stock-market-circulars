## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260604-18 informs trading members that 92,57,696 equity shares of Hexagon Nutrition Limited have been subscribed by Anchor Investors (AIs) at a price of Rs. 45 per equity share under the company's public issue, effective June 04, 2026.

## Key Points

- 92,57,696 equity shares allotted to Anchor Investors in the Hexagon Nutrition Limited public issue
- Allotment price: Rs. 45 per equity share
- Anchor investor subscription date: June 04, 2026
- Notice issued by Mangesh Tayde, Vice President, BSE
- Attachment: Anchor Intimation Letter from the company

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural intimation regarding anchor investor allocation as part of the IPO process.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed for awareness purposes. No specific compliance action is required from members in response to this notice.

## Important Dates

- **June 04, 2026**: Date of anchor investor allocation and subscription

## Impact Assessment

This notice is of moderate market relevance, signalling the commencement of the Hexagon Nutrition Limited IPO process with anchor investor participation. Anchor allotment at Rs. 45 per share sets a reference price point. The allotment of approximately 92.6 lakh shares to anchor investors indicates institutional interest in the issue and provides price discovery ahead of the public subscription window.
