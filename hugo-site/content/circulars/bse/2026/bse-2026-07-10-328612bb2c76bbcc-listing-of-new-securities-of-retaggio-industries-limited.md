## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 2,70,000 new equity shares of Retaggio Industries Limited (Scrip Code - 544391) are listed and permitted to trade with effect from Monday, July 13, 2026. These shares were issued to promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants, at an issue price of Rs. 26/- per share (face value Rs. 10/-, premium Rs. 16/-).

## Key Points

- 2,70,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 16/- (total issue price Rs. 26/-) to promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants.
- Distinctive numbers: 18168161 to 18438160.
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company.
- ISIN: INE0KWJ01014.
- Date of allotment: 07/04/2026.
- Shares are subject to lock-in until 25/02/2028.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a listing/trading permission notice, not a regulatory policy change.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should update their systems to reflect the new securities as tradable from the effective date. No separate action required from investors.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: 07/04/2026
- Effective date of listing/trading: Monday, July 13, 2026
- Lock-in expiry: 25/02/2028

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds a small quantum of promoter shares (2,70,000) to the tradable pool, but these are locked in until February 2028 and therefore will not affect free-float liquidity immediately. Market impact is expected to be minimal given the limited size of the allotment relative to typical free float.
