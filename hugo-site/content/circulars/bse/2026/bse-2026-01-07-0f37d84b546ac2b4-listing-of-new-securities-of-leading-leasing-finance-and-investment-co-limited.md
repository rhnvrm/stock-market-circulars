## Summary

BSE has listed 1,00,00,000 (1 crore) new equity shares of LEADING LEASING FINANCE AND INVESTMENT CO. LIMITED (Scrip Code: 540360) issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. The shares are listed and permitted to trade with effect from Thursday, January 8, 2026. These shares were allotted on November 25, 2025 at an issue price of Rs. 7.40 per share (face value Rs. 1 with premium of Rs. 6.40) and are subject to lock-in until July 14, 2026.

## Key Points

- 1,00,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 1/- each listed on BSE
- Issue price: Rs. 7.40 per share (Rs. 1 face value + Rs. 6.40 premium)
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Distinctive numbers: 553565501 to 563565500
- Date of allotment: November 25, 2025
- Trading commences: January 8, 2026
- ISIN: INE715Q01029
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice for new securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed that the new securities are available for trading from January 8, 2026, subject to lock-in restrictions.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: November 25, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: January 8, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry**: July 14, 2026 (all 1 crore shares)

## Impact Assessment

Limited immediate market impact as all newly listed shares are subject to lock-in until July 14, 2026. The preferential allotment through warrant conversion represents a capital raising exercise for the company. No immediate trading impact expected due to lock-in restrictions on the entire issue.
