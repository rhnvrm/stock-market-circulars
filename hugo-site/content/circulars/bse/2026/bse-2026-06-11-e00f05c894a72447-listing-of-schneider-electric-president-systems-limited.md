## Summary

Schneider Electric President Systems Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on BSE effective Friday, June 12, 2026, under the 'T' Group of Securities. The company has 12,096,000 equity shares of Rs. 10 each fully paid up, with scrip code 544786 and ISIN INE155D01018.

## Key Points

- Listing effective date: Friday, June 12, 2026
- Group: 'T' Group of Securities
- Scrip Code: 544786
- Scrip ID on BOLT: SELECTRIC
- ISIN: INE155D01018
- Total shares: 12,096,000 equity shares of Rs. 10 each, fully paid up
- Market lot: 1 (One) share
- Distinctive numbers: 1 to 12,096,000
- Trading exclusively in dematerialised form as per SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000
- Physical share certificates have been enfaced as non-transferable; demat shares have lock-in period marked in depository records
- Financial year ending: March 31
- All shares rank pari-passu including dividend entitlement

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. Listing is governed by existing SEBI dematerialisation guidelines dated February 16, 2000, requiring all trades to be settled in demat form.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade this scrip exclusively in dematerialised form
- Unit market lot of 1 (One) share applies for all trades
- Members with queries may contact the company (Ms. Sapna Bhatia, Company Secretary) or the Exchange (Mr. Parag Jain / Mr. Hardik Bhuta)
- Registrar to the issue: MUFG Intime India Private Limited, Mumbai

## Important Dates

- June 11, 2026: Notice date
- June 12, 2026: Effective date of listing and commencement of trading

## Impact Assessment

The listing of Schneider Electric President Systems Limited on BSE under the 'T' Group introduces a new equity scrip for trading members. The 'T' Group designation means trade-for-trade settlement applies, limiting speculative activity. The lock-in on shares (both physical and demat) may constrain early liquidity. Impact is primarily relevant to investors and trading members dealing in this scrip.
