## Summary

BSE has listed commercial paper securities issued on private placement basis by five companies on the debt market segment, effective January 28, 2026. The listed securities include commercial papers from Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited (2,500 units), Time Technoplast Limited (200 units), 360 ONE Prime Limited (5,000 units), Bajaj Finance Limited (4,000 units), and Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited (100 units). All securities are traded in dematerialized form with standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof.

## Key Points

- Five companies' commercial paper securities admitted to trading on BSE Debt Segment
- Total quantity: 11,800+ units across all issuers
- Trading only in dematerialized form under specified ISIN numbers
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Credit ratings: All securities rated A1+ by ICRA and/or CRISIL
- Interest rates ranging from 7.20% to 8.60% per annum
- Maturity periods ranging from March 2026 to January 2027
