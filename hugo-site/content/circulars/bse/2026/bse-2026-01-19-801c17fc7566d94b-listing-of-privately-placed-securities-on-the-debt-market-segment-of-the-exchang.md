## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of commercial papers issued by multiple companies on its debt market segment effective January 19, 2026. The securities were issued on a private placement basis and are now admitted to trading in dematerialized form with a standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs.

## Key Points

- Commercial papers of 5 issuances from 4 companies listed on BSE debt segment
- All securities to be traded only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Market lot: 1 unit
- POONAWALLA FINCORP LIMITED: 2 issuances (1000 units at 7.10% p.a., 3000 units at 7.26% p.a.)
- Navi Finserv Limited: 2 issuances (200 units each at 9.85% and 9.50% p.a.)
- Infina Finance Private Limited: 1 issuance (500 units at 7.38% p.a.)
- Tata Housing Development Company Limited: Details partially provided

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification under existing debt market regulations.

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Trading members requiring clarification may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Listing Effective Date:** January 19, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notification with minimal market impact. The listing of commercial papers provides additional short-term debt instruments for institutional investors and adds liquidity to the debt market segment. The interest rates range from 7.10% to 9.85% p.a., reflecting the credit ratings and tenures. The circular is primarily informational for trading members and does not affect equity market operations.
