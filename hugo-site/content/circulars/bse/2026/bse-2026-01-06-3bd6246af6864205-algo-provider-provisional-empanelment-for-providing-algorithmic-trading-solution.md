## Summary

BSE has provisionally empanelled Bull8.AI Solutions Private Limited as an Algo provider for providing algorithmic trading solutions to trading members of the Exchange. This empanelment is effective immediately. The complete list of empanelled Algo providers is available on the BSE website.

## Key Points

- Bull8.AI Solutions Private Limited has been provisionally empanelled as an Algo provider
- The vendor is authorized to provide algorithmic trading solutions to BSE trading members
- Vendor office located at Spectrum Metro Mall, Sector-75, Gautam Buddha Noida – 201301
- Contact number: +91 7042753487
- Updated list of all Algo providers available at BSE website

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an administrative notification regarding vendor empanelment.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. Trading members may choose to engage with the newly empanelled Algo provider for algorithmic trading solutions if needed.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: January 6, 2026 (Immediate effect)
- **Notice Date**: January 6, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact as it is purely informational. Trading members now have an additional vendor option for algorithmic trading solutions. The provisional empanelment expands the choice of service providers for members interested in implementing algo trading strategies. No mandatory actions required from trading members.
