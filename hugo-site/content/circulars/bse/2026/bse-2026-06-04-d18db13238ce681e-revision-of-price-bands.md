## Summary

BSE has revised the price bands for various equity scrips as part of a periodic review of surveillance actions. The revised price bands are listed in the attached Annexure (Annexure_June_2026) and will be effective from June 05, 2026.

## Key Points

- Price bands have been revised for multiple scrips as part of BSE's surveillance action review.
- The revised price bands are detailed in the enclosed Annexure.
- Scrips in the Trade for Trade segment will continue to attract a price band of 5%, irrespective of this revision.
- Trading Members are the primary audience for this notice.

## Regulatory Changes

Price bands for the scrips listed in Annexure_June_2026 are being revised. Price bands control the permissible intraday price movement range for a security, and their revision can either expand or restrict trading limits depending on the surveillance assessment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must take note of the revised price bands for the affected scrips.
- For clarifications, members may write to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 04, 2026
- **Effective Date:** June 05, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The revision of price bands directly affects the allowable price movement range for the listed scrips during trading sessions. Depending on whether bands are widened or tightened, this can increase or restrict trading volatility for the affected securities. Traders and investors holding positions in the affected scrips should review the Annexure to understand new limits. Scrips under Trade for Trade surveillance remain at a fixed 5% band and are unaffected by this circular.
