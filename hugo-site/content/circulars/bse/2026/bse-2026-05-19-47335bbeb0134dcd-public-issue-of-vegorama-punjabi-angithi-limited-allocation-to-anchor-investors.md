## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 14,16,000 equity shares were subscribed by Anchor Investors (AIs) in the public issue of Vegorama Punjabi Angithi Limited at a price of Rs. 77 per equity share on May 19, 2026.

## Key Points

- 14,16,000 equity shares allocated to Anchor Investors in the Vegorama Punjabi Angithi Limited IPO
- Subscription price: Rs. 77 per equity share
- Allocation date: May 19, 2026
- Company intimation letter enclosed as attachment
- Notice issued by Mangesh Tayde, Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural notice regarding anchor investor allocation in an ongoing public issue.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action required from trading members. The notice is informational, advising members of the anchor investor allocation details ahead of the public issue opening.

## Important Dates

- Anchor Investor allocation date: May 19, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a routine disclosure related to the IPO of Vegorama Punjabi Angithi Limited, a small company. Anchor investor participation at Rs. 77 per share provides a price reference for the upcoming public issue. No broader market or systemic implications.
