## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members that the commercial paper of Can Fin Homes Ltd (scrip code: 731247, ISIN: INE477A14ED1) under the series CFHL-3-6-26-CP is maturing. Accordingly, trading in this commercial paper is suspended with effect from June 02, 2026.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of Can Fin Homes Ltd (ISIN: INE477A14ED1) is reaching its redemption/maturity date.
- Scrip code on BSE: 731247; Series: CFHL-3-6-26-CP.
- Trading members are instructed not to deal in this commercial paper from June 02, 2026 onwards.
- Reference number: DR-642/2026-2027.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural notice issued upon maturity of a debt instrument listed on BSE's debt segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all transactions in Can Fin Homes Ltd commercial paper (ISIN: INE477A14ED1) effective June 02, 2026.
- Members are advised to take note of the suspension and ensure no trades are executed in this instrument on or after the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 01, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date:** June 02, 2026 (Redemption/Maturity Date)

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited and routine. This notice affects only holders and traders of this specific commercial paper instrument (ISIN: INE477A14ED1) of Can Fin Homes Ltd. The suspension is a standard operational step upon maturity and does not reflect any adverse credit or regulatory event. No broader market or equity trading impact is expected.
