## Summary

BSE's Surveillance department (DOSS) sought clarification from IndusInd Bank Ltd (Scrip Code: 532187) in connection with news articles appearing in various newspapers, websites, and television channels. The company has provided its clarification in response to the Exchange's query, which was triggered by a report on Moneycontrol.

## Key Points

- BSE Surveillance initiated clarification proceedings against IndusInd Bank Ltd (Scrip Code: 532187)
- The news source referenced is Moneycontrol (https://www.moneycontrol.com)
- Clarification has been received from the company
- Notice issued by Deputy Vice President Sanjay Jain and Manager Shreya Ghose, Surveillance

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this notice.

## Compliance Requirements

No additional compliance requirements are imposed on members or investors. The clarification has already been submitted by the company. Members and investors are advised to take note of the company's response for informed decision-making.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 03 June 2026

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This is a routine disclosure mechanism used by BSE Surveillance to verify media reports and provide transparency to market participants. The clarification is informational in nature and does not impose trading restrictions or penalties on IndusInd Bank Ltd.
