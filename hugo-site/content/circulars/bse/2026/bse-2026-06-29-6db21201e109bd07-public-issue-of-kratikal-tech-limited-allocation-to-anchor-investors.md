## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that anchor investors have subscribed to 8,31,000 equity shares in the public issue of Kratikal Tech Limited at a price of Rs. 135 per equity share on June 29, 2026.

## Key Points

- 8,31,000 equity shares allocated to anchor investors in the Kratikal Tech Limited IPO
- Anchor investor subscription price: Rs. 135 per equity share
- Allocation date: June 29, 2026
- Notice issued by Mangesh Tayde, Vice President, BSE
- Anchor allocation letter attached as enclosure

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine procedural notice informing trading members of completed anchor investor allocation.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action required from trading members. The notice is for information purposes only.

## Important Dates

- Anchor Investor Allocation Date: June 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. Anchor investor allocation is a standard pre-IPO step that signals institutional interest in Kratikal Tech Limited at Rs. 135 per share. The allocation of 8,31,000 shares to anchor investors provides price discovery and may indicate demand levels ahead of the public issue opening.
