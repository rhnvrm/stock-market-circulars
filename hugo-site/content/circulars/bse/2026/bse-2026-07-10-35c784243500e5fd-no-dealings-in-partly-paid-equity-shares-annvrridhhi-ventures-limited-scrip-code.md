## Summary

BSE has notified Trading Members that a Record Date of 14 July 2026 has been fixed for Annvrridhhi Ventures Limited (Scrip Code: 890229) to determine holders of partly paid-up equity shares eligible to receive the Second and Final Call notice for payment of Rs. 4.00 per rights equity share. Trading Members are advised not to deal in the partly paid equity shares of the company from 14 July 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: ANNVRRIDHHI VENTURES LIMITED (Scrip Code: 890229)
- Reference: DR-671/2026-2027
- Record Date: 14 July 2026
- Purpose: To ascertain shareholders of partly paid shares eligible to pay the Second and Final Call of Rs. 4.00 per partly paid equity share
- No Dealings From Date: 14 July 2026
- Notice issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President, Listing Compliance and Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is an operational notice implementing existing rights issue call money procedures for partly paid equity shares.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members of BSE must not deal in the partly paid equity shares of Annvrridhhi Ventures Limited on and from the Record Date of 14 July 2026, in order to allow accurate determination of shareholders liable for the second and final call payment.

## Important Dates

- Record Date / No Dealings From Date: 14 July 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to trading in the partly paid equity shares of Annvrridhhi Ventures Limited, a narrowly held instrument. The restriction ensures accurate identification of shareholders liable for the Rs. 4.00 per share second and final call, with no broader implications for the equity market or fully paid shares of the company.
