## Summary

BSE has listed 12,50,000 new equity shares of Vintage Coffee And Beverages Ltd. (Scrip Code: 538920, ISIN: INE498Q01014) for trading effective Monday, 20 April 2026. The shares were issued at a premium of Rs. 36/- per share (face value Rs. 10/-) to a Promoter on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 12,50,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 36/- (issue price Rs. 46/-)
- Issued to Promoter on preferential basis via conversion of warrants
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 144438410 to 145688409
- Date of allotment: 04 November 2025
- Trading permitted from 20 April 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice for newly issued securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed that the new securities are listed and permitted to trade on BSE from 20 April 2026. No additional compliance action required from members beyond awareness of the new shares in circulation.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** 04 November 2025
- **Trading Effective Date:** 20 April 2026 (Monday)
- **Lock-in Expiry:** 31 October 2027 (for all 12,50,000 shares, dist. nos. 144438410–145688409)

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 12,50,000 shares to Vintage Coffee And Beverages Ltd.'s tradable equity base. However, all newly listed shares are subject to lock-in until 31 October 2027, meaning they will not be available for trading in the open market during this period. Market impact is minimal as these are promoter shares under lock-in.
