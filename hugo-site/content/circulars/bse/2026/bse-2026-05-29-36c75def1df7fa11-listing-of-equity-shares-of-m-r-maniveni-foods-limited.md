## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the equity shares of M.R. Maniveni Foods Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange effective Monday, June 1, 2026, under the 'MT' Group of Securities on the SME segment. The scrip will be in the Trade-for-Trade segment for 10 trading days as per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 544768; Scrip ID / Abbreviated Name on BOLT: MANIVENI
- ISIN: INE0YD301010
- Total securities: 1,95,72,400 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each, fully paid up (Distinctive Nos. 1 to 19572400)
- Issue price: Rs. 52/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- + premium Rs. 42/-)
- Market lot: 2,000 equity shares
- Date of allotment in public issue: May 27, 2026
- Listing effective date: June 1, 2026 (Monday)
- Scrip will participate in the Special Pre-open Session (SPOS) on June 1, 2026
- Trading only in dematerialised form as per SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000
- Pari Passu: Yes; Financial Year end: March 31

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The listing is governed by existing SEBI framework including:
- SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 (January 20, 2012): mandates Trade-for-Trade segment for 10 trading days post-listing
- SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000: securities to be traded in dematerialised form only
- Exchange notice 20120216-29 (February 16, 2012): Special Pre-open Session for IPOs and relisted scrips

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade this scrip in minimum market lot of 2,000 equity shares; the Exchange may modify the lot size with at least one month's prior notice
- All trades must be in dematerialised form only
- Lock-in details as per Annexure I and shareholding pattern as per Annexure II of the notice must be noted
- Market Maker: Capitalsquare Financial Services Private Limited (SEBI Reg. No. INZ000281836)
- Registrar to the Issue: Bigshare Services Private Limited (SEBI Reg. No. INR000001385)

## Important Dates

- May 27, 2026: Date of allotment in the public issue
- June 1, 2026: Listing and commencement of trading on BSE SME platform; SPOS participation
- June 1, 2026 onwards (10 trading days): Trade-for-Trade segment restriction applies

## Impact Assessment

The listing is limited in broader market impact, being an SME IPO. Trading members dealing in SME securities should note the mandatory Trade-for-Trade restriction for the first 10 trading days, which prevents netting of positions and requires delivery-based settlement. The Special Pre-open Session on listing day may create price discovery dynamics. The market lot of 2,000 shares at an issue price of Rs. 52/- implies a minimum trade value of approximately Rs. 1,04,000, which is standard for SME listings. Lock-in details (Annexure I) are relevant for institutional and promoter-level stakeholders monitoring free-float.
