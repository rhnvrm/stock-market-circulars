## Summary

BSE has de-registered Geojit Technologies Pvt Ltd (Vendor Code 15) from its list of approved Independent Software Vendors (ISVs). The company is no longer authorized to provide trading front-end software systems to BSE Trading Members.

## Key Points

- Geojit Technologies Pvt Ltd (Vendor Code: 15) has been removed from the BSE ISV registry
- The de-registration applies to their trading front-end software systems
- Trading members using Geojit Technologies' software should seek an alternative registered ISV
- Contact details provided for further clarification: Email Trading.app@bseindia.com, Phone 022 2272 5116/5873/8926/5376

## Regulatory Changes

Geojit Technologies Pvt Ltd is no longer a BSE-registered ISV and cannot legally provide trading front-end software to BSE Trading Members going forward.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members currently using Geojit Technologies' trading front-end software must transition to a software system provided by a currently registered ISV
- Members should contact the Exchange at Trading.app@bseindia.com or 022 2272 5116/5873/8926/5376 for guidance on registered alternatives

## Important Dates

- Effective Date: 22 April 2026 (Notice date; de-registration is effective immediately)

## Impact Assessment

Trading members relying on Geojit Technologies Pvt Ltd for their trading front-end software will need to migrate to another registered ISV. This has a moderate operational impact on affected members but no broader market or systemic implications. Members should act promptly to ensure uninterrupted trading access.
