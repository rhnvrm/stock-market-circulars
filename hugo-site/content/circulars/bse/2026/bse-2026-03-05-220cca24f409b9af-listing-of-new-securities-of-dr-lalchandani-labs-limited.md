## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 43,04,000 new equity shares of Dr Lalchandani Labs Limited (Scrip Code: 541299) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Friday, March 6, 2026. The shares were issued at par on a rights basis.

## Key Points

- 43,04,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at par on rights basis
- ISIN: INE871Z01013
- Distribution Numbers: 4333069 to 8637068
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Effective trading date: March 6, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification for newly issued securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to update their records and systems to reflect the new securities available for trading from March 6, 2026.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: February 28, 2026
- Effective Trading Date: March 6, 2026 (Friday)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. The listing adds 43,04,000 new equity shares to the existing float of Dr Lalchandani Labs Limited. As a rights issue at par, existing shareholders have already been given the opportunity to subscribe. The shares rank pari-passu with existing shares, meaning no dilution of rights for existing shareholders beyond the rights issue itself.
