## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (20260519-5) directing trading members to suspend all dealing in the 91-day Treasury Bill with scrip code 805181 (particulars: 91TB220526, ISIN: IN002025X463) with effect from May 20, 2026. The suspension is necessitated by the instrument's upcoming maturity on its Redemption Date.

## Key Points

- Trading in T-bill scrip code **805181** (91TB220526, ISIN: IN002025X463) is suspended from **May 20, 2026**.
- The suspension is triggered by the instrument's maturity on the Redemption Date.
- Reference number: DR-634/2026-2027.
- All trading members are advised not to deal in the aforesaid T-bill/Government Security from the effective date.
- No attachments were included with this notice.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework changes. This is a standard operational notice under BSE's Listing Compliance & Operations department for instruments reaching maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all transactions in scrip code 805181 (ISIN: IN002025X463) effective May 20, 2026.
- Members should update their systems to reflect the trading halt for this instrument.
- Members holding or intermediating trades in this T-bill should ensure no new orders are placed on or after the suspension date.

## Important Dates

- **May 19, 2026** – Notice issued by BSE.
- **May 20, 2026** – Effective date of trading suspension; instrument matures on its Redemption Date.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine end-of-life event for a 91-day T-bill maturing on May 22, 2026 (implied by the instrument name 91TB220526). The impact is limited to holders and active traders of this specific instrument. Trading desks dealing in short-duration government debt should update their systems to block new orders for scrip 805181 from May 20 onwards. No broader market disruption is anticipated.
