## Summary

BSE has listed 1,38,13,666 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each of Sumuka Agro Industries Limited (Scrip Code: 532070), issued pursuant to the Scheme of Merger by Absorption of Gujjubhai Foods Private Limited (Transferor Company / GFPL) with Sumuka Agro Industries Limited (Transferee Company / SAIL). These shares are permitted for trading on BSE with effect from Tuesday, May 26, 2026.

## Key Points

- 1,38,13,666 new equity shares of Rs. 10/- each are listed on BSE.
- Shares issued under the Scheme of Merger by Absorption of Gujjubhai Foods Private Limited into Sumuka Agro Industries Limited under Sections 230 to 232 of the Companies Act, 2013.
- ISIN: INE311N01016.
- Distribution numbers: 7107171 to 20920836.
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a routine listing action consequent to a court/NCLT-approved scheme of merger under Sections 230–232 of the Companies Act, 2013.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action is required from market participants. The securities are available for normal trading from May 26, 2026 onwards.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** March 20, 2026
- **Trading commences:** May 26, 2026
- **Notice Date:** May 25, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 1,38,13,666 equity shares to the tradeable float of Sumuka Agro Industries Limited (Scrip Code: 532070), increasing the total share capital following the absorption of Gujjubhai Foods Private Limited. The impact is limited to shareholders and market participants trading in this specific scrip; there is no broader market or systemic impact.
