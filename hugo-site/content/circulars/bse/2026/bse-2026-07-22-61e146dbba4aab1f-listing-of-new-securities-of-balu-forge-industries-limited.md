## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 17,10,000 new equity shares of Balu Forge Industries Limited (Scrip Code: 531112), issued on a preferential basis to promoters and non-promoters pursuant to conversion of warrants, will be listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Thursday, July 23, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company: Balu Forge Industries Limited (Scrip Code: 531112)
- Number of shares listed: 17,10,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each
- Issue price: Rs. 360/- per share (including premium of Rs. 350/-)
- Allottees: Promoters and non-promoters, on a preferential basis
- Reason for issue: Conversion of warrants
- Distinctive numbers: 119681901 to 121391900
- ISIN: INE011E01029
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company

## Regulatory Changes

Not applicable; this is a routine listing notice issued by BSE under its Listing Operations Department.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific action required from investors or trading members beyond updating trading systems to recognize the newly listed shares as fully tradable pari-passu equity, subject to the lock-in restrictions noted below.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: March 9, 2026
- Effective Date of Listing/Trading: Thursday, July 23, 2026
- Lock-in expiry (15,00,000 shares, distinctive nos. 11,96,81,901 to 12,11,81,900): January 31, 2028
- Lock-in expiry (2,10,000 shares, distinctive nos. 12,11,81,901 to 12,13,91,900): January 27, 2027

## Impact Assessment

The listing marginally increases the free-float and total outstanding equity of Balu Forge Industries Limited. Since a portion of the new shares (15,00,000 out of 17,10,000) remains under lock-in until January 2028 and the remainder until January 2027, the immediate tradable float impact is limited. The event is administrative in nature and is expected to have minimal near-term impact on share price or trading volumes.
