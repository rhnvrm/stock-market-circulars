## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the Rajasthan State Development Loan security (839RAJSDL26) with scrip code 810957 and ISIN IN2920150355 is maturing on its redemption date. Trading in this government security is suspended with effect from March 11, 2026.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 810957, ISIN: IN2920150355, Security: 839RAJSDL26 (Rajasthan SDL) is maturing
- Trading suspension is effective from March 11, 2026
- Reference: DR-837/2025-2026
- Notice issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President – Listing Compliance & Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice issued upon maturity of a government security listed on BSE's debt segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in scrip code 810957 (839RAJSDL26) on or after March 11, 2026
- Members holding or facilitating trades in this security must ensure no transactions are executed post the suspension date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** March 10, 2026
- **Suspension Effective Date:** March 11, 2026 (Redemption/Maturity Date)

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited to holders and traders of the specific Rajasthan SDL security (839RAJSDL26). This is a standard end-of-life action for a maturing government bond. No broader market disruption is anticipated. Members should ensure settlement of any pending trades prior to the suspension date.
