## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that effective Monday, July 6, 2026, the equity shares of Aastha Spintex Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange under the 'T' Group of Securities. Per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012, the scrip will trade in the Trade-for-Trade segment for the first 10 trading days.

## Key Points

- Company: AASTHA SPINTEX LIMITED, registered in Halvad, Surendra Nagar, Gujarat, India.
- No. of Securities: 44,142,190 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each, fully paid up (Distinctive Numbers 1 to 44,142,190).
- Scrip ID / Abbreviated Name on BOLT: AASTHA
- Scrip Code: 544808
- ISIN: INE2FMX01012
- Market Lot: 1
- Issue Price: Rs. 136/- per share (Face Value Rs. 10/- + premium Rs. 126/-)
- Date of Allotment in public issue: July 2, 2026
- Securities will trade only in dematerialised form per SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000.
- The scrip will be part of the Special Pre-open Session (SPOS) on July 6, 2026, per Exchange notice no. 20120216-29.
- Lock-in details as per Annexure I; Shareholding Pattern as per Annexure II.
- Registrar to the issue: Bigshare Services Private Limited, Mumbai.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is an implementation notice applying existing SEBI circulars (CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 on trade-for-trade, and the February 16, 2000 dematerialisation guideline) to a new listing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure that AASTHA SPINTEX LIMITED shares are traded only in dematerialised form.
- Trading members must account for the Trade-for-Trade segment restriction for the first 10 trading days from listing.
- The company has executed necessary corporate action with the depository to mark the lock-in period on shares as per Annexure I.

## Important Dates

- July 2, 2026: Date of allotment in the public issue.
- July 6, 2026 (Monday): Listing and commencement of dealings; Special Pre-open Session (SPOS) held.
- Trade-for-Trade segment applies for 10 trading days from listing (July 6, 2026 onward).

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine new listing notice affecting only trading members and investors dealing in AASTHA SPINTEX LIMITED shares. The trade-for-trade restriction for 10 days limits speculative/intraday trading in the scrip immediately post-listing, which is standard practice for new IPOs and does not affect broader market operations.
