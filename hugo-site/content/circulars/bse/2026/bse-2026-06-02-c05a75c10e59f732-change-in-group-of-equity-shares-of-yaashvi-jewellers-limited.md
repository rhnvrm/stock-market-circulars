## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the equity shares of YAASHVI JEWELLERS LIMITED (SME IPO, Scrip Code: 544770) will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment, where dealings will be conducted under the M Group. This change is effective from Tuesday, June 16, 2026, and is a continuation of Notice No. 20260601-25 dated June 01, 2026.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 544770 (YAASHVI JEWELLERS LIMITED — SME IPO)
- Transfer from Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective date: Tuesday, June 16, 2026
- This notice continues Exchange Notice No. 20260601-25 dated June 01, 2026
- Contact for queries: Mr. Anurag Jain, Tel. No. 022-2272 8822

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares of YAASHVI JEWELLERS LIMITED will move out of the Trade for Trade (TFT) settlement mechanism under the MT Group and will instead be settled under the Rolling segment in the M Group. Under TFT, each trade must result in delivery; the move to Rolling segment allows netting of trades in the normal settlement cycle.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members dealing in or intending to deal in YAASHVI JEWELLERS LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544770) must note the segment change and update their systems and client advisories accordingly prior to June 16, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **June 02, 2026**: Circular issuance date
- **June 16, 2026**: Effective date of transfer from MT Group (Trade for Trade) to M Group (Rolling segment)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative change with low broader market impact. The migration from Trade for Trade to Rolling settlement is generally a positive development for market participants, as it allows netting of positions and greater trading flexibility in the scrip. The impact is limited to trading members and investors holding or trading in YAASHVI JEWELLERS LIMITED shares on BSE's SME platform.
