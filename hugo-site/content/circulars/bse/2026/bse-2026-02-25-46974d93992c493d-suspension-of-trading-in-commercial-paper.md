## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260225-43 directing trading members to suspend dealings in commercial papers of 15 companies ahead of their respective record dates for redemption. The suspensions are effective from February 27, 2026 and March 4, 2026 depending on the company.

## Key Points

- Trading members are advised not to deal in the commercial papers of the listed companies from the specified 'No Dealings From' dates.
- Two companies (Manappuram Finance Limited and Balkrishna Industries Ltd.) have record dates on February 27, 2026.
- One company (360 ONE WAM LIMITED - OWL-4-3-26-CP) has a record date on March 2, 2026.
- Twelve companies have record dates on March 4, 2026, covered under DR-832/2025-2026.
- All suspensions are for the purpose of Redemption of Commercial Paper.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational notice under existing BSE framework for debt segment commercial paper redemptions.

- **March 2, 2026**: Record date and no-dealings date for 360 ONE WAM LIMITED (OWL-4-3-26-CP).
- **March 4, 2026**: Record date and no-dealings date for remaining 12 companies.
- **Notice issued**: February 25, 2026.

## Impact Assessment

This circular impacts debt segment participants and trading members active in the commercial paper market. The suspensions are temporary and routine, triggered by upcoming redemption dates. Holders of these instruments should be aware that trading will be suspended from the specified dates. No impact is expected on equity markets. The breadth of issuers — spanning NBFCs, finance companies, and large corporates — reflects normal commercial paper maturity activity for early March 2026.
