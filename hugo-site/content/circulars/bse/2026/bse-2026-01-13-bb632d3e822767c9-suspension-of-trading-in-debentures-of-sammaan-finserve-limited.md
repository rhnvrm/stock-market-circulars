## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in three series of non-convertible debentures issued by SAMMAAN FINSERVE LIMITED effective January 19, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these specified debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Three debenture series of SAMMAAN FINSERVE LIMITED suspended from trading
- Record date fixed as January 19, 2026 for redemption and interest payment
- No dealings permitted from January 19, 2026
- Notice issued under reference DR-801/2025-2026
- All three debentures have same record date and suspension date

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the suspended debentures from January 19, 2026
- Members should update their systems to reflect the trading suspension
- Ensure clients are informed about the suspension and corporate action

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 13, 2026
- **Record Date**: January 19, 2026
- **No Dealings From**: January 19, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard corporate action with limited market impact. Debenture holders on the record date will receive their interest payment and principal redemption. The trading suspension is temporary and specific to these three debenture series. Market participants holding these securities should note the suspension and plan accordingly for the redemption proceeds.
