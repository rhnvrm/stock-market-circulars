## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 97,53,750 new equity shares of Artificial Electronics Intelligent Material Limited (Scrip Code: 526443) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Wednesday, March 11, 2026. These shares were issued on a preferential basis to Non-Promoters at an issue price of Rs. 40/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- plus premium of Rs. 30/-).

## Key Points

- 97,53,750 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 30/- (total issue price Rs. 40/-) to Non-Promoters on a preferential basis
- Shares are ranking pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 16965841 to 26719590
- Date of allotment: November 17, 2025
- ISIN: INE072B01027
- All 97,53,750 shares (Dist. Nos. 16965841–26719590) are subject to lock-in until September 30, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice pursuant to a completed preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to update their systems to allow trading in these new securities from March 11, 2026
- Allottees must observe the lock-in restriction; the 97,53,750 shares cannot be traded until September 30, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** November 17, 2025
- **Trading commencement date:** March 11, 2026
- **Lock-in expiry date:** September 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing of preferential allotment shares for a small-cap company. The addition of approximately 97.54 lakh shares to the tradeable float is subject to a lock-in period until September 30, 2026, meaning no immediate increase in free-float liquidity. Market impact is expected to be minimal and confined to existing shareholders and the specific allottees of this preferential issue.
