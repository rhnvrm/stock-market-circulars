## Summary

BSE has issued an update to the Short Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) framework under the 5/15/30 Days ASM criteria. Six new securities are being added to the framework and ten securities are being removed, effective March 04, 2026. No securities are being moved to a higher or lower ASM stage in this update.

## Key Points

- 6 securities newly shortlisted under ST-ASM (5/15/30 Days) framework effective March 04, 2026
- 10 securities removed from ST-ASM framework effective March 04, 2026
- No securities moved to higher or lower ASM stages in this cycle
- 2 securities added (Sukhjit Starch & Chemicals Ltd, Xtglobal Infotech Ltd) are per NSE shortlisting
- 1 SME scrip (Zelio E Mobility Ltd) added to ST-ASM
- 2 securities (Cemantic Infra-Tech Ltd, Sikozy Realtors Ltd) moved out due to inclusion in LT-ASM Framework
- 1 SME scrip (Techknowgreen Solutions Ltd) moving out of ST-ASM

## Regulatory Changes

The ST-ASM framework applies enhanced margin requirements (5%, 15%, or 30% additional surveillance margin depending on stage) to shortlisted securities. This circular updates the list of securities subject to these requirements:

## Compliance Requirements

- **Brokers/Members:** Must apply applicable ST-ASM margin requirements for the newly added securities from March 04, 2026
- **Brokers/Members:** Must remove ST-ASM margin requirements for the 10 securities exiting the framework from March 04, 2026
- **Investors:** Trading in ST-ASM securities requires payment of additional surveillance margin; positions in newly added securities will attract these margins
- Refer to Annexure III (consolidated list) for the full current list of securities under ST-ASM and their respective stages

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** March 02, 2026
- **Effective Date:** March 04, 2026 (for all additions, removals, and stage changes)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine surveillance update with moderate impact on the specific securities affected. Securities added to ST-ASM will face increased margin requirements, which typically reduces speculative activity and may temporarily suppress trading volumes and price volatility. The 2 securities (Cemantic Infra-Tech Ltd and Sikozy Realtors Ltd) moving from ST-ASM to LT-ASM face a more restrictive regime. The 10 securities exiting ST-ASM may see improved trading liquidity as margin burdens are reduced. The inclusion of 1 SME scrip (Zelio E Mobility Ltd) in the framework reflects continued surveillance of the SME segment. Overall market impact is low; only traders and investors in the specific affected scrips need to take action.
