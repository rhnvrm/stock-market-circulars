## Summary

BSE has made the Draft Offer Document of Lorven International Limited available on the stock exchange website following its filing on February 25, 2026. The document contains all disclosures as required under Schedule VI of the ICDR (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations. The draft is open for public comments for a minimum of 21 days from the date of notice.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Lorven International Limited on February 25, 2026
- Document hosted on the BSE SME website for a minimum of 21 days
- Public comments are invited within 21 days from the date of this notice (by approximately March 19, 2026)
- Disclosures comply with Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations
- Draft Prospectus available at the BSE SME portal

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice is issued in compliance with existing ICDR Regulations, which mandate public availability of Draft Offer Documents for a minimum of 21 days to facilitate transparency and public scrutiny before an SME IPO.

## Compliance Requirements

- Interested parties and the public may review the Draft Offer Document on the BSE SME website
- Comments or observations on the draft document must be submitted within 21 days from February 26, 2026
- Comments to be sent via email to: equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Filing Date:** February 25, 2026
- **Notice Date:** February 26, 2026
- **Public Comment Deadline:** On or before March 19, 2026 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal broader market impact. It is a standard procedural disclosure related to Lorven International Limited's proposed SME IPO. The primary impact is limited to prospective investors, analysts, and stakeholders interested in the company's forthcoming public offering. Market participants wishing to comment on the draft prospectus must do so within the stipulated 21-day window.
