## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 67,708 new equity shares of Gabriel Pet Straps Limited (Scrip Code: 544108) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Thursday, March 12, 2026. These shares were issued to promoters on a preferential basis at an issue price of Rs. 256/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- + premium of Rs. 246/-).

## Key Points

- **Company:** Gabriel Pet Straps Limited (Scrip Code: 544108)
- **Number of shares:** 67,708 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each
- **Issue price:** Rs. 256/- per share (at a premium of Rs. 246/-)
- **Allottee:** Promoters (preferential basis)
- **ISIN:** INE0QZF01012
- **Distribution Numbers:** 7412337 to 7480044
- **Ranking:** Pari-passu with existing equity shares
- **Trading effective from:** March 12, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for newly allotted securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities and may trade them from March 12, 2026.
- Promoters holding the allotted shares are subject to a lock-in period and cannot sell/transfer these shares until September 30, 2027.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** December 8, 2025
- **Trading permitted from:** March 12, 2026
- **Lock-in expiry:** September 30, 2027

## Impact Assessment

The listing involves a relatively small number of shares (67,708) issued to promoters on a preferential basis. All allotted shares are under lock-in until September 30, 2027, so there is no immediate increase in tradable float. Market impact is minimal. The shares are pari-passu with existing equity, so no change in rights or entitlements for existing shareholders.
