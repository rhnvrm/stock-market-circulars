## Summary

BSE has listed additional securities issued by 14 companies under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and Employee Stock Option Schemes (ESOS). These new shares are admitted for trading on the Exchange effective Tuesday, May 19, 2026. None of the newly listed shares carry any lock-in restrictions.

## Key Points

- 14 companies have had further securities listed under ESOP/ESOS schemes
- All new securities are effective for trading from May 19, 2026
- None of the shares are under lock-in (all marked NA)
- Wipro Limited has the largest issuance: 2,669,612 shares (scrip 507685)
- A2Z Infra Engineering Limited issued 1,402,500 shares (scrip 533292)
- ICICI Bank issued 164,293 shares (scrip 532174)
- Total new shares span a wide range of sectors including IT, banking, FMCG, and financial services

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification under existing ESOP/ESOS frameworks.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are required to take note of the newly listed securities and their respective distribution number ranges for settlement and record-keeping purposes.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** May 18, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** Tuesday, May 19, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice with low market impact. The newly listed ESOP/ESOS shares represent employee compensation conversions and are standard dilutive events. No lock-in periods apply to any of the listed tranches. The largest issuances are from Wipro (2,669,612 shares at face value ₹2) and A2Z Infra Engineering (1,402,500 shares at face value ₹10), which may have minor dilutive effects. Overall market impact is minimal as these are pre-approved employee option exercises.
