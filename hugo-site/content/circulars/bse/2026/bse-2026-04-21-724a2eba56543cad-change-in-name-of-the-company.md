## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260421-39 dated April 21, 2026 informs trading members that SER Industries Ltd (scrip code 507984) has changed its corporate name to **Desi Farms India Limited**, effective April 27, 2026. The SCRIP ID and abbreviated name on the BOLT Plus System will remain **DESIFARMS**.

## Key Points

- Scrip code 507984 (SER Industries Ltd) is being renamed to Desi Farms India Limited.
- The SCRIP ID for BOLT Plus System remains unchanged: **DESIFARMS**.
- The abbreviated name on BOLT Plus System remains unchanged: **DESIFARMS**.
- Notification issued by Marian D'Souza, Assistant Vice President – Listing Compliance & Operations.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a company-initiated name change communicated to the Exchange as per listing obligations. The Exchange is updating its records to reflect the new legal name.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should note the new legal name of the company for all documentation and correspondence from April 27, 2026.
- No changes required to order entry on BOLT Plus System as the SCRIP ID and abbreviated name remain the same.

## Important Dates

- **April 21, 2026**: Date of BSE notice.
- **April 27, 2026**: Effective date of name change on BOLT Plus System.

## Impact Assessment

Minimal operational impact. Since the SCRIP ID (DESIFARMS) and abbreviated name on BOLT Plus System remain unchanged, trading operations are unaffected. Members only need to update internal records to reflect the new legal entity name — Desi Farms India Limited — for compliance and documentation purposes.
