## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that further new equity shares issued under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) / Employee Stock Option Schemes (ESOS) by nine listed companies are admitted for trading with effect from Tuesday, July 07, 2026. The companies are Aarti Surfactants Limited, Asian Energy Services Limited, Eris Lifesciences Limited, Federal Bank Limited, IIFL Capital Services Limited, Kernex Microsystems (India) Limited, Metropolis Healthcare Limited, Sona BLW Precision Forgings Limited, and Vishal Mega Mart Limited.

## Key Points

- Nine companies have new equity shares from ESOP/ESOS exercises listed and admitted for trading.
- Aarti Surfactants Limited (ISIN INE09EO01013, Scrip 543210): 5,910 shares, face value ₹10, distinctive nos. 8514996–8520905.
- Asian Energy Services Limited (ISIN INE276G01015, Scrip 530355): 18,641 shares, face value ₹10, distinctive nos. 48611001–48629641.
- Eris Lifesciences Limited (ISIN INE406M01024, Scrip 540596): 11,233 shares, face value ₹1, distinctive nos. 140306387–140317619.
- Federal Bank Limited (ISIN INE171A01029, Scrip 500469): 240,794 shares, face value ₹2, distinctive nos. 2467798218–2468039011.
- IIFL Capital Services Limited (ISIN INE489L01022, Scrip 542773): 1,780,600 shares, face value ₹2, distinctive nos. 328670359–330450958.
- Kernex Microsystems (India) Limited (ISIN INE202H01019, Scrip 532686): 1,200 shares, face value ₹10, distinctive nos. 16802423–16803622.
- Metropolis Healthcare Limited (ISIN INE112L01020, Scrip 542650): 4,200 shares, face value ₹2, distinctive nos. 207327769–207331968; a portion subject to lock-in as per Annexure I.
- Sona BLW Precision Forgings Limited (ISIN INE073K01018, Scrip 543300): 6,500 shares, face value ₹10, distinctive nos. 624621273–624627772.
- Vishal Mega Mart Limited (ISIN INE01EA01019, Scrip 544307): 7,933,333 shares, face value ₹10, distinctive nos. 4676375307–4684308639.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine operational listing notice under existing ESOP/ESOS listing procedures, not a change in regulation.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to take note of the listing of these additional shares for settlement and trading purposes. Metropolis Healthcare's newly listed shares carry a lock-in as specified in Annexure I, which trading members and depositories should account for accordingly.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 06, 2026 (Monday)
- Effective Date of Listing/Trading: July 07, 2026 (Tuesday)

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact, routine administrative listing of incremental shares arising from employee stock option exercises across nine companies. The additional share quantities are generally small relative to each company's total outstanding capital, implying minimal dilution and negligible immediate market impact. No trading restrictions apply except the lock-in noted for a portion of Metropolis Healthcare's shares.
