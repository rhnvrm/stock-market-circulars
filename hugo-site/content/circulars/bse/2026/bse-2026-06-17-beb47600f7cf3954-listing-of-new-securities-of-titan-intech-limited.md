## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 95,00,000 new equity shares of TITAN INTECH LIMITED (Scrip Code: 521005) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Thursday, June 18, 2026. These shares were issued on a preferential basis to Non Promoters pursuant to conversion of warrants at an issue price of Rs. 5.50/- per share (face value Re. 1/- at a premium of Rs. 4.50/-).

## Key Points

- 95,00,000 equity shares of Re. 1/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 4.50/- to Non Promoters on a preferential basis
- Issued pursuant to conversion of warrants
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distribution numbers: 847576883 to 857076882
- Issue price: Rs. 5.50/- per share
- ISIN: INE807M01031
- Date of allotment: April 10, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice under BSE's Listing Operations for newly allotted equity shares.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the listing and commencement of trading for the new securities. The newly listed shares are subject to a lock-in period and cannot be freely traded until the lock-in expiry date.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** April 10, 2026
- **Trading Commencement:** June 18, 2026 (Thursday)
- **Lock-in Expiry:** February 20, 2027 (for all 95,00,000 shares, dist. nos. 847576883–857076882)

## Impact Assessment

The listing of 95,00,000 new preferential shares represents a dilution of the existing equity base of TITAN INTECH LIMITED. Non-promoter allottees are subject to a lock-in until February 20, 2027, limiting immediate market supply of these shares. The issue price of Rs. 5.50/- (at a modest premium of Rs. 4.50/- over face value of Re. 1/-) indicates the shares were issued at a relatively low valuation. Market impact is moderate and limited to existing shareholders and traders in TITAN INTECH LIMITED.
