## Summary

BSE has notified trading members and custodians of an open offer by five acquirers — Mrs. Gayathri Boreddy, Mr. Jagannath Edla, Mr. Radha Krishna Avudari, Mr. Mahammad Amaan Shaik, and Mr. Ravi Kiran Veeramalla — to acquire up to 97,50,000 equity shares (26% of the expanded equity and voting share capital) of Midland Polymers Limited at Rs. 10.00 per share in cash. The offer operates through BSE's stock exchange acquisition window mechanism under SEBI (SAST) Regulations, 2011.

## Key Points

- Five acquirers collectively are making an open offer to public shareholders of Midland Polymers Limited.
- Offer price: Rs. 10.00 per fully paid-up equity share of face value Rs. 10.00, payable in cash.
- Maximum shares to be acquired: 97,50,000 shares, representing 26.00% of the expanded equity and voting share capital.
- Offer triggered under Regulation 3(1) and Regulation 4 of SEBI (SAST) Regulations, 2011, involving substantial acquisition of shares/voting rights with change in control.
- The offer will be conducted through BSE's acquisition window as per SEBI circular no. CFD/DCR2/CIR/P/2016/131 dated December 9, 2016 and Exchange notices dated February 10, 2017, April 24, 2019, May 28, 2020, November 2, 2020, and August 25, 2021.
- Letter of Offer is attached to the notice for reference.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. The offer is conducted under the existing SEBI mechanism for acquisition of shares through stock exchanges pursuant to Tender-Offers under Takeovers, as established by SEBI circular no. CIR/CFD/POLICYCELL/1/2015 dated April 13, 2015, and subsequently revised.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and custodians must facilitate tendering of shares by eligible public shareholders of Midland Polymers Limited during the offer window.
- All market participants must adhere to the revised guidelines under SEBI circular no. CFD/DCR2/CIR/P/2016/131 and BSE Exchange notice no. 20170210-16.
- Offer-to-buy details are available on the BSE website (www.bseindia.com).
- Public shareholders wishing to participate must tender shares through the BSE acquisition window within the stipulated offer period.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 1, 2026
- **Offer Opens:** Wednesday, June 3, 2026
- **Offer Closes:** Tuesday, June 16, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This open offer directly affects existing public shareholders of Midland Polymers Limited, who have the opportunity to tender their shares at Rs. 10.00 per share — a price equal to the face value of the equity share. The acquisition of 26% of the expanded capital represents a significant change-in-control event under SEBI (SAST) Regulations. Shareholders should review the attached Letter of Offer carefully to evaluate the offer price against the prevailing market price before deciding to tender. Trading members must be prepared to handle client orders through the BSE acquisition window mechanism during the June 3–16, 2026 offer period.
