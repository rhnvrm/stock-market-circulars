## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Jetmall Spices and Masala Ltd (Scrip Code: 543286) has changed its name to Artemis ADR Marketplace Limited. The Scrip ID and Abbreviated Name on the BOLT Plus System will be updated to ARTEMISADR effective July 09, 2026.

## Key Points

- Company Jetmall Spices and Masala Ltd is renamed to Artemis ADR Marketplace Limited
- Scrip Code 543286 remains unchanged
- New Scrip ID on BOLT Plus System: ARTEMISADR
- New Abbreviated Name on BOLT Plus System: ARTEMISADR
- Change is based on the Certificate of Incorporation (COI) submitted by the company

## Regulatory Changes

This is an administrative update to reflect the company's change of name, as communicated to the Exchange by the company along with supporting documentation (Certificate of Incorporation).

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must update their systems to recognize the new Scrip ID and Abbreviated Name (ARTEMISADR) for Scrip Code 543286 effective the change date. No other action is required from investors or trading members beyond system recognition of the new identifiers.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 03, 2026
- Effective Date of Name Change: July 09, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact administrative change affecting only the company's name and associated trading identifiers on the BOLT Plus System. There is no change to the scrip code, and the underlying business and shareholding structure remain unaffected. Trading members should ensure their systems reflect the updated Scrip ID and Abbreviated Name from the effective date to avoid order routing or identification errors.
