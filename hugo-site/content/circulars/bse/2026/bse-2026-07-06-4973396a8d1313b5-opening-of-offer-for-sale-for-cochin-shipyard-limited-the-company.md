## Summary

BSE has notified the opening of an Offer for Sale (OFS) by the President of India (acting through the Ministry of Ports, Shipping and Waterways), the Seller, to divest shares in Cochin Shipyard Limited through a separate, designated window on BSE and NSE. The base offer comprises up to 66,29,636 equity shares (up to 2.52% of paid-up capital), with an oversubscription option of an additional 66,29,636 shares (another 2.52%), taking the total to up to 5.04% of paid-up equity capital if fully exercised. Additionally, 26,308 equity shares (0.20% of Offer Shares) are reserved for eligible employees, subject to competent authority approval.

## Key Points

- Seller: President of India, acting through the Ministry of Ports, Shipping and Waterways
- Base Offer Size: up to 66,29,636 equity shares (face value Rs. 5 each), representing up to 2.52% of total paid-up equity share capital
- Oversubscription Option: additional 66,29,636 equity shares (up to 2.52%), taking total Offer Shares up to 5.04% of paid-up capital if exercised
- Employee Offer: 26,308 equity shares (0.20% of Offer Shares), subject to competent authority approval; eligible employees may apply for shares up to Rs. 5,00,000
- Floor Price: Rs. 1400 per equity share
- Conducted through a separate, designated OFS window of BSE and NSE
- Reference guidelines: BSE notice nos. 20190118-43, 20200701-27, 20230127-14, and 20240701-19, issued per SEBI circulars dated December 28, 2018, January 10, 2023, and June 14, 2024

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this notice implements existing SEBI OFS framework circulars (December 28, 2018; January 10, 2023; June 14, 2024) governing OFS of shares and employee participation through the stock exchange mechanism.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members and Custodians must refer to the existing OFS bidding guidelines referenced in the prior BSE notices. Market participants should monitor the BSE website for full offer details, and eligible employees must comply with OFS Guidelines and the Rs. 5,00,000 application cap.

## Important Dates

- T Day (Non-Retail Investors only): July 07, 2026
- T+1 Day (Retail Investors, Employees, and Non-Retail Investors carrying forward unallotted bids from T Day): July 08, 2026
- Notice Date: July 06, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The OFS increases free-float supply of Cochin Shipyard shares by up to 5.04% of paid-up capital if the oversubscription option is exercised, which could exert short-term downward pressure on the stock price, especially near the Rs. 1400 floor price. The two-day structure segregating non-retail and retail/employee participation is designed to manage price discovery and demand absorption across investor categories. Employees are given a dedicated allocation, which may have a marginal, positive medium-term impact on internal ownership alignment. Overall this is a market-moving disinvestment event with high relevance to traders and investors tracking government stake sales.
