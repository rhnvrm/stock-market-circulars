## Summary

BSE has announced a change in the Scrip ID for Arisinfra Solutions Limited (Scrip Code: 544419) in the BOLT Plus trading system. The Scrip ID will be updated from "ARISINFRA" to "ARIS" effective February 09, 2026, as per the company's request.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code remains unchanged: 544419
- Old Scrip ID: ARISINFRA
- New Scrip ID: ARIS
- Change applies to BOLT Plus System only
- Trading members must update their systems accordingly

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative update to the trading system identifier as requested by the company.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must take note of the new Scrip ID
- Systems and records should be updated to reflect the new identifier
- For clarifications, trading members can contact:
  - Mr. Tushar Shetty: +91 22 2272 8634 (Listing.crd@bseindia.com)
  - Ms. Asmita Chavan: +91 22 2272 8266 (Listing.crd@bseindia.com)

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** February 03, 2026
- **Effective Date:** February 09, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact administrative change affecting only the internal identifier used in BSE's BOLT Plus trading system. The scrip code (544419) remains unchanged, and there is no impact on trading operations, share ownership, or market participants beyond the need for trading members to update their system references. Investors are not affected by this change.
