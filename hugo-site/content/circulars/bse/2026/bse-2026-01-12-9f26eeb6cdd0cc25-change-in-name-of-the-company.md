## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for CIN L29220TG2012PLC082904. The company's name has been officially changed from **STANDARD GLASS LINING TECHNOLOGY LIMITED** to **STANDARD ENGINEERING TECHNOLOGY LIMITED** with effect from December 29, 2025. The certificate was issued by the Central Processing Centre, ROC.

## Key Points

- Company CIN: L29220TG2012PLC082904
- Old Name: STANDARD GLASS LINING TECHNOLOGY LIMITED
- New Name: STANDARD ENGINEERING TECHNOLOGY LIMITED
- Original Name at Incorporation: STANDARD GLASS LINING TECHNOLOGY PRIVATE LIMITED
- Company Type: Company limited by shares
- Certificate Date: December 29, 2025
- Approved by: Sunidhi Matroja, Central Processing Centre
- Registered Office: D.12, Phase I, IDA, Jeedimetla, Hyderabad-500055, Telangana, India

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action for company name change processed through the Ministry of Corporate Affairs.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company is obligated to display the old name along with the new name for a period of **two years** at all places where the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders
- All stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company and its directors on the MCA website: www.mca.gov.in/MCA21

## Important Dates

- **December 29, 2025**: Effective date of name change (certificate issuance date)
- **Two-year period from December 29, 2025**: Duration for mandatory display of old name alongside new name

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine administrative change that does not affect the company's operations, financial position, or shareholder rights.

**Operational Impact**: Low. The company will need to update all statutory documents, letterheads, signage, and communications to reflect the new name while maintaining dual display for two years.

**Stakeholder Impact**: No material impact on rights and liabilities of stakeholders. This is purely a nomenclature change from a glass lining technology focus to a broader engineering technology positioning.
