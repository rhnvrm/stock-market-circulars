## Summary

BSE has announced the discontinuation of trading and voluntary delisting of equity shares of Hindustan Fluorocarbons Ltd (Scrip Code: 524013) pursuant to SEBI (Delisting of Equity Shares) Regulations, 2021. Trading will cease on January 30, 2026, and the scrip will be delisted from exchange records on February 06, 2026. The promoter/acquirer Hindustan Organic Chemicals Limited will provide an exit option to remaining public shareholders at Rs. 17.76 per share for one year from the delisting date.

## Key Points

- Company has completed formalities for voluntary delisting under SEBI (Delisting of Equity Shares) Regulations, 2021
- Trading discontinuation effective Friday, January 30, 2026
- Delisting from exchange records effective Friday, February 06, 2026
- Exit price determined at Rs. 17.76 per equity share
- Exit option available for one year from delisting date
- Promoter/acquirer: Hindustan Organic Chemicals Limited
- Shareholders must contact RTA KFin Technologies Limited for exit option

## Regulatory Changes

The delisting is conducted in accordance with SEBI (Delisting of Equity Shares) Regulations, 2021 as amended. The company has complied with all regulatory formalities required for voluntary delisting.

**For Trading Members:**

## Important Dates

- **January 22, 2026**: Circular notice date
- **January 30, 2026**: Last trading day for Hindustan Fluorocarbons Ltd equity shares
- **February 06, 2026**: Delisting from exchange records
- **Up to February 06, 2027**: Exit option period (one year from delisting date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** High - Complete removal of the security from trading platform affecting all existing shareholders and trading members.

**Shareholder Impact:** Remaining public shareholders have a one-year window to exit at the determined price of Rs. 17.76 per share through the RTA. Shareholders who do not exercise the exit option will hold unlisted shares.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members must update their systems and client communications regarding the discontinuation of trading and delisting timeline. The acquirer Hindustan Organic Chemicals Limited must maintain the exit option facility for one year post-delisting.
