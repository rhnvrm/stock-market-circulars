## Summary

ICCL (Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd.) has published the settlement schedule for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Buyback) of Garware Technical Fibres Limited. The buyback window will be open from May 26, 2026 to June 2, 2026, under Settlement No. 2026057, with the final settlement on June 9, 2026.

## Key Points

- Settlement Type: Buyback (Settlement No. 2026057)
- Bid Start Date: May 26, 2026
- Bid End Date: June 2, 2026
- Obligation Download Date: June 8, 2026
- Settlement Date: June 9, 2026
- Demat shares must be delivered only through the Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism available on both depositories (CDSL and NSDL)
- Market Type "(Buyback)" and Settlement No. 2026057 must be used in the depository system
- Members/Custodians are NOT required to upload any EPN file for early pay-in
- CDSL & NSDL early pay-in securities will be earmarked/blocked in clients' accounts under the requisite market type and settlement number

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. Members are referred to Notice No. 20200528-32 dated May 28, 2020 regarding settlement as per Revised Guidelines of Mechanism for acquisition of shares through Stock Exchange.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and Custodians must ensure early pay-in of securities is reflected in BSE/ICCL systems before the prescribed timelines for accepting/confirming bids on the BSE OTB platform
- Early pay-in must be completed in the depository system sufficiently in advance before closure of the BSE OTB Bidding window
- Custodians must confirm bids within the designated confirmation window
- Members should refer to the MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for client-wise Stamp Duty charges

## Important Dates

|---|---|

## Impact Assessment

This notice is operationally relevant for trading members and custodians participating in the Garware Technical Fibres Limited buyback offer. Participants must coordinate early pay-in of securities through CDSL or NSDL before BSE OTB window closure to ensure bids are accepted. Failure to comply with early pay-in timelines may result in bid rejection. The settlement spans approximately two weeks from bid opening to final settlement.
