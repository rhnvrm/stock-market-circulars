## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260121-13 suspending trading in debentures of PURVA OAK PRIVATE LIMITED (ISIN: INE0SRE07015, Code: 975840) effective January 27, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for early redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the no-dealing date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to POPL-12.50%-RESET RATE-19-11-2 debentures
- ISIN: INE0SRE07015
- BSE Code: 975840
- Record date set for January 27, 2026
- No dealings permitted from January 27, 2026
- Purpose: Early redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Notice reference: DR-806/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action requiring temporary trading suspension as per existing BSE protocols for debt securities undergoing early redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from January 27, 2026
- Trading members must take note of the suspension and record date
- All trading activity in these debentures must cease by the no-dealing date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 21, 2026
- **Record Date**: January 27, 2026
- **No Dealings From**: January 27, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: High for debenture holders of PURVA OAK PRIVATE LIMITED as trading will be suspended and early redemption will occur. Holders will receive interest payment and principal redemption as per the debenture terms.

**Trading Impact**: Complete suspension of trading in these specific debentures from January 27, 2026. Trading members must ensure no transactions are executed in this security.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders as of record date will receive early redemption proceeds and accrued interest. No further trading opportunity will be available from the suspension date.
