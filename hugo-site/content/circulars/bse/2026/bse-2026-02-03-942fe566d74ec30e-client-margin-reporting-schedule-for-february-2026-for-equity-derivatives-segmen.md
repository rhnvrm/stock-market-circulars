## Summary

BSE has released the client margin reporting schedule for the Equity Derivatives segment for February 2026. The circular provides a comprehensive table mapping trade dates to their corresponding last dates for margin reporting and penalty dates. Members must report client margins within the specified timelines to avoid penalties.

## Key Points

- Margin reporting schedule covers all trading days in February 2026
- Last date for margin reporting is typically 5 business days after the trade date
- Penalty date is 1 business day after the last reporting date
- Schedule accounts for weekends and public holidays
- Issued by Indian Clearing Corporation Limited
- Notice Number: 20260203-24
- Category: Settlement/RMS

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine monthly schedule announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must submit client margin reports by the specified "Last Date for Margin Reporting" for each trade date
- Failure to report by the last date will result in penalties being applied on the "Penalty Date"
- Compliance applies to all members trading in the Equity Derivatives segment
- Reporting must follow existing BSE and Indian Clearing Corporation Limited guidelines

## Important Dates

**February 2026 Trading Schedule (Selected Examples):**
- Trade Date: 01-02-2026 → Last Reporting: 06-02-2026 → Penalty: 09-02-2026
- Trade Date: 13-02-2026 → Last Reporting: 20-02-2026 → Penalty: 23-02-2026
- Trade Date: 27-02-2026 → Last Reporting: 09-03-2026 → Penalty: 10-03-2026

**Note:** Full schedule covering all 20 trading days in February 2026 is provided in the circular.

## Impact Assessment

**Operational Impact:** Medium - Affects daily operations of all brokers and clearing members trading in equity derivatives. Requires timely submission of client margin data.

**Market Impact:** Low - This is a standard administrative process that does not affect market trading or pricing.

**Compliance Impact:** Medium - Non-compliance results in penalties. Members must ensure proper systems and processes are in place to meet reporting deadlines.

**Target Audience:** All BSE members and clearing participants active in the Equity Derivatives segment.
