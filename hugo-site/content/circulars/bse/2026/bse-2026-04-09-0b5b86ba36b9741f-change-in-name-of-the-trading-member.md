## Summary

BSE has notified all trading members that BNR CAPITAL SERVICES PVT.LTD. (Member No. 71) will officially change its name to **BNR STOCK BROKING PRIVATE LIMITED** with effect from Friday, 10th April 2026. This is an administrative update issued by the Membership Compliance department.

## Key Points

- Trading member BNR CAPITAL SERVICES PVT.LTD. (Member No. 71) is undergoing a name change.
- New name: **BNR STOCK BROKING PRIVATE LIMITED**
- Effective date: Friday, 10th April 2026
- Membership number (71) remains unchanged.
- Issued by Amit Kadam, Assistant Vice President, Membership department.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory rule changes. This is a purely administrative notice reflecting a corporate name change for a BSE trading member.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members dealing with BNR CAPITAL SERVICES PVT.LTD. should update their records to reflect the new name **BNR STOCK BROKING PRIVATE LIMITED** from 10th April 2026 onwards.
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE at: membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **10th April 2026 (Friday):** Effective date for the name change from BNR CAPITAL SERVICES PVT.LTD. to BNR STOCK BROKING PRIVATE LIMITED.

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine administrative name change for a single trading member (Member No. 71). Counterparties and clearing participants interacting with this member should update their internal records accordingly. No trading activity, settlement obligations, or regulatory requirements are affected beyond the name update.
