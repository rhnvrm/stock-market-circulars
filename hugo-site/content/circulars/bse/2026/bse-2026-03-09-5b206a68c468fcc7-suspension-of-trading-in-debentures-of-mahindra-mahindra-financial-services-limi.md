## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members that Mahindra & Mahindra Financial Services Limited (MMFSL) has fixed a record date of 11 March 2026 for the redemption and interest payment on its 8.25% Non-Convertible Debentures. Trading members are directed not to deal in these debentures from 11 March 2026.

## Key Points

- MMFSL has fixed 11 March 2026 as the record date for redemption and interest payment on its NCD series MMFSL-8.25%-26-3-26-PVT
- ISIN: INE774D07UT1 (Scrip Code: 974726)
- Trading in these debentures is suspended with no dealings permitted from 11 March 2026
- Notice reference: DR-837/2025-2026
- Notice issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President – Listing Compliance and Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational notice under existing BSE rules governing suspension of trading ahead of corporate action record dates for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in MMFSL debentures with ISIN INE774D07UT1 (Scrip Code 974726) from 11 March 2026 onwards
- Members are required to take note of the no-dealings directive and ensure compliance

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 09 March 2026
- **Record Date:** 11 March 2026
- **No Dealings From:** 11 March 2026
- **Purpose:** Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest

## Impact Assessment

This suspension affects trading in one specific NCD series of MMFSL (8.25% coupon, maturing 26 March 2026). The impact is limited to holders and prospective traders of this debenture series. Since the debenture is approaching its maturity/redemption date, liquidity impact is minimal. Debenture holders will receive redemption proceeds and final interest on or around the record date.
