## Summary

PAN HR Solution Limited has finalized the allocation of equity shares to anchor investors as part of its public issue. The Board of Directors approved the allocation of 5,37,600 equity shares at ₹78 per share (face value ₹10 plus premium of ₹68) to three anchor investors, raising a total of ₹4.19 crore. The company confirmed that no applications were received from domestic mutual funds.

## Key Points

- Total anchor allocation: 5,37,600 equity shares
- Anchor investor allocation price: ₹78 per share (face value ₹10 + premium ₹68)
- Total amount raised from anchor investors: ₹4,19,32,800 (approximately ₹4.19 crore)
- Three anchor investors participated: Rajasthan Global Securities Private Limited (26.19%), LRSD Securities Private Limited (25.89%), and INTI Capital VCC – INTI Capital I (47.92%)
- No applications received from domestic mutual funds
- Board meeting held on February 05, 2026

This is a routine IPO disclosure with limited market-wide impact. The anchor allocation represents the initial institutional participation in PAN HR Solution Limited's public issue.
