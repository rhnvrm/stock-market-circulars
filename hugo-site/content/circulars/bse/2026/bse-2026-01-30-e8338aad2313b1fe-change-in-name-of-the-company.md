## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation consequent upon the conversion of NIWAS Housing Finance Private Limited to a public limited company. The company, originally incorporated on January 1, 2016 as Indostar Home Finance Private Limited, has been renamed to NIWAS Housing Finance Limited effective December 31, 2025. The conversion was approved by the Registrar of Companies, Central Processing Centre under Section 18 of the Companies Act, 2013.

## Key Points

- Corporate Identity Number: U65990MH2016PLC271587
- Original incorporation date: January 1, 2016
- Original name: Indostar Home Finance Private Limited
- Conversion approval date: December 31, 2025 (SRN AC0878110)
- New name: NIWAS Housing Finance Limited
- Company converted from private limited to public limited company
- Approved by M. Yadubhushana Rao, Registrar of Companies, CPC
- Registered office: Unit No.305, 3rd Floor, Wing 2/E, Corporate Avenue, Andheri-Ghatkopar Link Road, Chakala, Mumbai City - 400093, Maharashtra

## Regulatory Changes

The company has undergone conversion from a private limited company to a public limited company under Section 18 of the Companies Act, 2013. This conversion requires approval from the Central Government through the Registrar of Companies and involves changes to the company's constitutional documents and regulatory obligations.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements are imposed on market participants. The company itself must comply with public company regulations under the Companies Act, 2013 and SEBI regulations if it intends to list its securities.

## Important Dates

- **January 1, 2016**: Original incorporation date as Indostar Home Finance Private Limited
- **December 31, 2025**: Conversion approval granted and name changed to NIWAS Housing Finance Limited
- **December 31, 2025**: Certificate of Incorporation issued
- **January 30, 2026**: BSE circular notification date

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action involving the conversion of a private housing finance company to a public company. The impact on market participants is minimal as this is an administrative notification. The conversion may be a precursor to potential listing activities, but no immediate market implications exist. Investors and market participants should note the name change from Indostar Home Finance Private Limited to NIWAS Housing Finance Limited for future reference.
