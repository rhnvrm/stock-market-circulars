## Summary

BSE has notified Trading Members and Custodian Members of the schedule of activities for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Buyback) of shares of TMT India Ltd. The offer runs from Wednesday, 8th July 2026 to Tuesday, 21st July 2026, covering up to 12,87,988 equity shares at an offer price of Rs. 10 per share (face value Rs. 10).

## Key Points

- Security: TMT India Ltd (Symbol: TMTINDB1, ISIN: INE182E01010, Scrip Code: 522171)
- Offer size: Up to 12,87,988 equity shares, market lot of 1
- Offer price: Rs. 10.00 per share (equal to face value)
- Offer period: 8th July 2026 to 21st July 2026
- Bidding categories include FII, FI, MF, IC, Other QIBs, Other NIB, Company, and Individual investors
- Access via iBBS, RTRMS, and Extranet platforms (internet and leased line URLs provided)
- Trading Members must ensure UCC registration with correct PAN and category before participating

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is an operational notice implementing the acquisition window mechanism per existing BSE circulars (Notice No. 20150930-1 and 20170203-29) governing OTB participation.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members and Custodian Members must ensure the Unique Client Code (UCC) of investors is registered/updated with correct PAN and investor category in the Exchange UCC database.
- Bids can only be placed for investors holding shares in demat form.
- Members and Custodians should note that no End of Issue files will be generated on the Extranet if no securities are accepted.

## Important Dates

- Bid Offering Period: 8th July 2026 to 21st July 2026, 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.
- Bid Modification/Cancellation: 8th July 2026 to 21st July 2026, 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.
- Give-up/Take-up: 8th July 2026 to 20th July 2026, 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.
- Give-up (last day of offer): 21st July 2026, 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.
- Take-up (last day of offer): 21st July 2026, 9:15 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.
- Offer opens: Wednesday, 8th July 2026
- Offer closes: Tuesday, 21st July 2026

## Impact Assessment

This notice is primarily procedural, providing trading and custodian members with the operational timeline for participating in the buyback acquisition window for TMT India Ltd. It has direct relevance for market intermediaries handling bids on behalf of investors but limited broader market impact given the small offer size and single-security scope.
