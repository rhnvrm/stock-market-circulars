## Summary

BSE observed significant price/volume movement in 10 listed scrips and sought clarifications from the respective companies as part of its surveillance process. Replies have been received from 5 companies and are attached to the notice, while replies from the remaining 5 companies are still awaited.

## Key Points

- BSE identified unusual price/volume activity in the following scrips and wrote to the companies for clarification.
- Replies received from: SC Agrotech Ltd (526081), Liotech Industries Ltd (544796), One Mobikwik Systems Ltd (544305), Unichem Laboratories Ltd (506690), and S.P. Apparels Ltd (540048).
- Replies still awaited from: CMX Holdings Ltd (532217), Mapro Industries Ltd (509762), Sujala Trading & Holdings Ltd (539117), VJTF Eduservices Ltd (509026), and BF Investment Ltd (533303).
- The intent is to ensure trading members and investors have access to the latest relevant information on these companies.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine surveillance disclosure under BSE's Department of Surveillance (DOSS), not a new regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

No direct compliance action is required from trading members or investors; companies with pending replies are expected to respond to the exchange's queries.

## Important Dates

Notice dated 13 July 2026 (Notice No. 20260713-26).

## Impact Assessment

Investors and traders holding or tracking these 10 scrips should review the attached company replies for material information that may explain recent price/volume volatility. The lack of response from 5 companies may warrant continued caution and monitoring for further surveillance action (e.g., ASM/GSM placement) if clarifications remain outstanding.
