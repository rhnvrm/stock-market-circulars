## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted for trading new equity shares issued by 7 companies under their Employee Stock Option / Employee Stock Ownership Schemes (ESOP/ESOS), effective from Wednesday, June 17, 2026. All newly issued shares are free from lock-in restrictions.

## Key Points

- 7 companies have listed additional shares arising from ESOP/ESOS exercises
- All shares are effective for trading from June 17, 2026
- No lock-in period applies to any of the newly listed shares
- Total new shares listed span a range of 466 (Can Fin Homes) to 40,843 (BlueStone Jewellery)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice pursuant to existing ESOP/ESOS scheme allotments.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are required to take note of the new securities and their respective ISIN and scrip codes for trading purposes from June 17, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 16, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** June 17, 2026 (Wednesday)
