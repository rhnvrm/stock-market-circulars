## Summary

BSE, as part of its routine surveillance process, sought clarification from Rajesh Exports Ltd (Scrip Code: 531500) regarding a news article that appeared on The Hindu Business Line website/newspaper. As of the notice date, the company's reply is still awaited.

## Key Points

- The Exchange verifies news articles appearing in newspapers, websites, and television channels to inform members and investors about their accuracy.
- Rajesh Exports Ltd (Scrip Code: 531500) was asked to clarify a news article published by The Hindu Business Line.
- The company's response to the Exchange's query is pending.
- No attachment was provided with this notice.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine surveillance clarification request under BSE's standard market surveillance practice, not a regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

Rajesh Exports Ltd is required to submit a formal clarification/response to BSE regarding the accuracy of the news article in question, in line with disclosure obligations for listed companies.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 21 July 2026
- Reply from the company: Awaited (no deadline specified in the notice)

## Impact Assessment

Limited immediate market impact, as this is a preliminary clarification request rather than a confirmed corporate development. Investors and market participants may watch for the company's forthcoming clarification, as the underlying news article could pertain to material information affecting the stock. Until the company responds, the news remains unverified by the Exchange.
