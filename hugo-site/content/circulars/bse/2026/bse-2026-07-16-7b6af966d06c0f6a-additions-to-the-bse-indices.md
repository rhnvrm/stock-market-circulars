## Summary

BSE Index Services has announced the addition of two newly listed companies to BSE indices following their respective listings on the exchange. Laser Power & Infra Limited, listed on the main board on July 16, 2026, will be added to the BSE IPO index at the open of trading on Monday, July 20, 2026. Devson Catalyst Limited, listed on the SME platform of BSE on July 16, 2026, will be added to the BSE SME IPO index at the open of trading on Friday, July 17, 2026.

## Key Points

- Laser Power & Infra Limited (Exchange ticker: 544822) listed on BSE main board effective July 16, 2026
- Laser Power & Infra Limited to be added to the BSE IPO index effective July 20, 2026
- Devson Catalyst Limited (Exchange ticker: 544823) listed on BSE SME platform effective July 16, 2026
- Devson Catalyst Limited to be added to the BSE SME IPO index effective July 17, 2026
- These index actions will be reflected in the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- Reference made to prior Notice Nos. 20260715-22 and 20260715-23 for the original listing approvals

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an operational index maintenance notice reflecting standard inclusion of newly listed IPO stocks into their respective tracking indices.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action required from market participants. Index fund managers and institutions tracking the BSE IPO index and BSE SME IPO index should note the addition dates to ensure accurate portfolio rebalancing.

## Important Dates

- July 16, 2026: Laser Power & Infra Limited and Devson Catalyst Limited listed on BSE
- July 17, 2026: Devson Catalyst Limited added to BSE SME IPO index (effective at market open)
- July 20, 2026: Laser Power & Infra Limited added to BSE IPO index (effective at market open)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, low-impact administrative notice. The primary effect is on passive index funds and ETFs that track the BSE IPO or BSE SME IPO indices, which will need to include these stocks in their portfolios on the specified effective dates. There is minimal direct impact on broader market operations or existing listed companies.
