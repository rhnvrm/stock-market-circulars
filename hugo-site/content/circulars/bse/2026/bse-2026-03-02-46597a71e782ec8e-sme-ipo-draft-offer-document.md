## Summary

BSE has made the Draft Offer Document (DRHP) of **Navkkar Gold & Diamond Limited** available on the Exchange's SME website. The document was filed on February 28, 2026, and is compliant with Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations. It is open for public comments for a minimum period of 21 days from the date of this notice (March 2, 2026).

## Key Points

- Navkkar Gold & Diamond Limited has filed its Draft Offer Document for an SME IPO on February 28, 2026.
- The document is available on BSE's SME website per ICDR Regulations (Schedule VI).
- Public and market participants are invited to submit comments within 21 days.
- Comments must be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The disclosure follows existing SEBI ICDR Regulations, Schedule VI requirements for SME IPO draft offer documents.

## Compliance Requirements

- Any member of the public or market participant wishing to comment on the Draft Offer Document must do so within **21 days** from March 2, 2026 (i.e., by approximately March 23, 2026).
- Comments should be submitted via email to **equity.ipo@bseindia.com**.

## Important Dates

- **February 28, 2026** – Draft Offer Document filed by Navkkar Gold & Diamond Limited.
- **March 2, 2026** – Notice date; 21-day public comment period begins.
- **~March 23, 2026** – Deadline for submitting public comments.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine SME IPO filing disclosure with limited market-wide impact. The circular is relevant primarily to prospective retail and institutional investors interested in the Navkkar Gold & Diamond Limited IPO, as well as analysts and advisors reviewing the company's disclosures. No trading or operational changes are required for existing market participants.
