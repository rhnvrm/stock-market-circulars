## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260701-38 dated July 01, 2026 informs trading members that the applicable price bands for 27 specified scrips have been revised from their existing levels, effective July 02, 2026. The revised bands range from 2% to 20% depending on the individual security, reflecting BSE's periodic recalibration of volatility limits under its surveillance framework.

## Key Points

- 27 scrips across BSE have had their daily price bands revised.
- Revised bands vary by scrip: 2% (Atvo Enterprises, Bafna Pharmaceuticals, Lexora Global, Super Spinning Mills, Tejassvi Aaharam), 5% (majority of scrips including Accuracy Shipping, Aion-Tech Solutions, AksharChem India, Anna Infrastructures, Archit Organosys, Bannari Amman Spinning Mills, D.P. Wires, Everest Industries, Laxmipati Engineering Works, Lyka Labs, Patel Chem Specialities, Prakash Pipes, Resonance Specialties, Shri Balaji Valve Components, Southern Latex, Standard Engineering Technology, Tamboli Industries), 10% (City Pulse Multiventures, Shrydus Industries, Veljan Denison), and 20% (Marg Techno Projects, Vikram Solar).
- Change takes effect from July 02, 2026.
- No attachment accompanies this notice; details are provided directly in the notice table.
- Queries can be directed to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Regulatory Changes

This is an operational adjustment under BSE's surveillance and risk management framework, revising the applicable daily price band percentage for the listed scrips. It is not a new regulation but an application of existing BSE surveillance policy to recalibrate volatility limits for specific securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must update their trading systems to reflect the revised price bands for the listed scrips effective July 02, 2026, and ensure order placement complies with the new band limits to avoid order rejections.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 01, 2026
- Effective Date of Revised Price Bands: July 02, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The revision affects intraday and positional trading strategies for the 27 listed scrips by altering the permissible daily price movement range. Scrips moving to tighter bands (2%) will see reduced daily volatility ceilings, while those moving to wider bands (10-20%) may experience increased potential for larger price swings. Traders and algorithmic systems referencing these securities should recalibrate risk parameters accordingly. Overall market-wide impact is limited given the narrow set of scrips involved.
