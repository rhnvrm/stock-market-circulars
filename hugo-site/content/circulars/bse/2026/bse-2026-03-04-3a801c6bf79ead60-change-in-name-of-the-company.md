## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that Arunis Abode Ltd (scrip code 526935) has changed its name to Kalind Limited. The SCRIP ID and abbreviated name on the BOLT Plus System will be updated accordingly, effective March 10, 2026.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code 526935 undergoes a name change from **Arunis Abode Ltd** to **Kalind Limited**
- New SCRIP ID on BOLT Plus System: **KALIND**
- New abbreviated name on BOLT Plus System: **KALIND**
- Notice issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President – Listing Compliance and Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative update reflecting the company's legal name change, as communicated by the company to BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should update their systems and references to reflect the new company name (Kalind Limited) and the new SCRIP ID/abbreviated name (KALIND) on BOLT Plus System from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** March 04, 2026
- **Effective Date of Name/ID Change:** March 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The change is purely administrative — the scrip code (526935) remains the same. Trading members need to update internal references and systems to use the new SCRIP ID 'KALIND' from March 10, 2026 to avoid any operational confusion.
