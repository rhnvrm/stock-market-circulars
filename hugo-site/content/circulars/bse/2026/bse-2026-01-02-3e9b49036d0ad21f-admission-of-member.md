## Summary

BSE has admitted Algoquant Global Securities Private Limited as a new Trading Member of the Exchange with Member Number 6975. The membership is granted for the Cash segment only, subject to BSE's Rules, Bye-laws and Regulations.

## Key Points

- New trading member: Algoquant Global Securities Private Limited
- Member Number: 6975
- Segment: Cash
- Membership Type: New Membership
- Trading rights granted on BSE Ltd. only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine membership admission notice.

## Compliance Requirements

The new member must comply with all Rules, Bye-laws and Regulations of the Exchange as per their membership terms.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: January 2, 2026
- Notice Number: 20260102-16

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative notice with minimal market impact. The admission of a new trading member expands the exchange's participant base but does not affect existing trading members' operations or market structure. Trading members requiring clarification can contact membership.ops@bseindia.com.
