## Summary

BSE has notified trading members about the listing of privately placed debt securities issued by InCred Financial Services Limited on the BSE Debt Segment, effective January 16, 2026. The securities comprise 11,000 units of 9% coupon bonds maturing on December 24, 2027, with quarterly interest payments.

## Key Points

- InCred Financial Services Limited has listed privately placed debt instruments on BSE Debt Segment
- Total quantity: 11,000 units with market lot of 1
- Scrip Code: 977459, Scrip ID: 9IFSL27
- ISIN: INE321N07616
- Credit Rating: CRISIL AA-/Stable
- Face Value and Issue Price: Rs. 100,000 per unit
- Coupon Rate: 9% per annum
- Trading will be in dematerialized form only
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Date of Allotment: January 13, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE321N07616
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for complete details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: January 13, 2026
- **Listing Effective Date**: January 16, 2026
- **First Interest Payment**: April 13, 2026
- **Interest Payment Frequency**: Quarterly from April 13, 2026 to December 24, 2027
- **Date of Redemption**: December 24, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine listing of privately placed debt instruments affecting only the debt market segment.

**Operational Impact**: Low impact on trading members. Standard debt trading procedures apply with dematerialized trading requirements.

**Investor Impact**: Provides additional investment opportunity in the debt segment for qualified investors. The CRISIL AA-/Stable rating indicates high credit quality with adequate safety for timely payment of financial obligations.
