## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Trent Ltd [Lakme Ltd] (BSE Code: 500251 / Demat Code: 100251) has fixed **04 June 2026** as the record date for a bonus issue. Shares will trade on an ex-bonus basis from 04 June 2026 (Settlement DR-644/2026-2027). A total of 177,744,100 equity shares will be allotted on 05 June 2026.

## Key Points

- **Bonus ratio:** 1 bonus equity share of Rs. 1/- for every 2 equity shares of Rs. 1/- held
- **Record date:** 04 June 2026
- **Ex-bonus effective date:** 04 June 2026 (Settlement DR-644/2026-2027)
- **Shares to be allotted:** 177,744,100 equity shares on 05 June 2026
- **Company letter reference:** Undertaking_Trent dated 07 May 2026
- **BSE Notice:** 20260529-45 dated 29 May 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification pursuant to the company's board decision to issue bonus shares.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all transactions in Trent Ltd equity shares from 04 June 2026 onwards are processed on an ex-bonus basis.
- Members should update client records and positions to reflect the adjusted share quantities post-allotment.
- Settlement obligations must account for the bonus-adjusted pricing from the ex-bonus date.

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| BSE Notice Date | 29 May 2026 |
| Record Date | 04 June 2026 |
| Ex-Bonus Basis Effective From | 04 June 2026 |
| Bonus Share Allotment | 05 June 2026 |

## Impact Assessment

The 1:2 bonus issue will result in a 50% increase in Trent Ltd's outstanding equity share count, with a proportional reduction in share price on the ex-bonus date to maintain market capitalisation. Existing shareholders will receive one additional share for every two held, improving liquidity and affordability of the stock. Trading members must update position limits, margin calculations, and client holdings accordingly prior to 04 June 2026. The allotment of approximately 177.7 million shares is a significant capital event and may attract increased trading activity around the record date.
