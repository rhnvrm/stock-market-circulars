## Summary

BSE has published the list of securities scheduled for demat auction under Settlement No. 829, dated 27/02/2026. The auction covers delivery shortfalls across 60+ listed companies. Participants who have failed to deliver securities in the normal settlement cycle are subject to close-out through this auction mechanism.

## Key Points

- Settlement No. 829 demat auction scheduled by Bombay Stock Exchange
- 60+ securities across diverse sectors are listed for auction
- Quantities range from 1 share (multiple scrips) to 372,400 shares (SpiceJet)
- Auction is a standard BSE mechanism to resolve delivery defaults in the settlement cycle
- Scrip codes and names are listed alongside the shortfall quantities

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice under existing BSE settlement and auction framework for handling delivery defaults.

## Compliance Requirements

- Brokers and members with delivery shortfalls in the listed securities must participate or be subject to close-out at auction prices
- Members should ensure their clients are informed of any close-out positions arising from Settlement No. 829
- Auction participants must adhere to BSE auction session timings and bidding rules

## Important Dates

- Auction Date: 27/02/2026 (Settlement No. 829)
