## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that debt listed company Vivriti Funds Private Ltd (Scrip Code 975094) will change its name to Vivriti Asset Management Private Limited. The Scrip ID and Abbreviated Name for its listed debt securities on the BOLT Plus System will be updated accordingly, effective July 15, 2026. The change follows submission of a Certificate of Incorporation reflecting the new name.

## Key Points

- Vivriti Funds Private Ltd is changing its registered name to Vivriti Asset Management Private Limited.
- Scrip Code 975094 remains unchanged.
- New Scrip ID and Abbreviated Name on BOLT Plus System: 11VAMPL27 (in both fields).
- The change is supported by a Certificate of Incorporation attachment filed with the exchange.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is an administrative update to the company's registered name as reflected in exchange records and trading systems.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must update their systems to reflect the new Scrip ID and Abbreviated Name for the company's debt securities from the effective date. No action is required from debt security holders.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 9, 2026
- Effective Date of Name Change: July 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact administrative notice affecting only the naming and identification of the debt securities on the BOLT Plus System. Scrip code continuity ensures no disruption to existing holdings or settlement processes.
