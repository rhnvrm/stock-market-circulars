## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260610-5 informs trading members that 18 companies have fixed record dates for the redemption of their Commercial Papers. Trading members are advised not to deal in these Commercial Papers from their respective ex-dates onward.

## Key Points

- 18 commercial paper instruments across multiple issuers are suspended from trading ahead of their redemption record dates
- Redemption record dates fall on June 12, 15, and 16, 2026
- Ex-dates coincide with the record dates for all instruments
- Settlement DRs referenced: 650/2026-2027, 651/2026-2027, and 652/2026-2027
- Category: Corporate Actions | Segment: Debt | Department: Listing Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice under existing debt market trading rules requiring suspension of commercial paper trading ahead of redemption record dates.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must cease dealing in the commercial papers of the listed companies effective from their respective ex-dates. Non-compliance with trading suspension advisories may attract regulatory action.

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact routine notice. Suspension of trading in commercial papers ahead of redemption is a standard procedure in the debt segment. Investors holding these CPs will receive redemption proceeds on the respective maturity dates. No equity market impact is expected. The notice affects only the specific commercial paper scrips listed; the equity shares of the issuing companies are unaffected.
