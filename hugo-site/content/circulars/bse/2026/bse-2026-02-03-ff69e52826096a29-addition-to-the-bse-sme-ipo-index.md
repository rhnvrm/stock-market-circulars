## Summary

BSE has announced the addition of KASTURI METAL COMPOSITE LIMITED (Exchange ticker: 544688) to the BSE SME IPO Index effective at the open of Wednesday, February 4, 2026. This follows the company's listing on BSE on Tuesday, February 03, 2026.

## Key Points

- KASTURI METAL COMPOSITE LIMITED is being listed on BSE effective February 03, 2026
- The stock will be added to the BSE SME IPO Index from the opening of February 4, 2026
- Exchange ticker for the company is 544688
- This action has been included in the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index rebalancing action following a new SME listing.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index constituents are automatically updated in BSE's systems.

## Important Dates

- **February 3, 2026**: Listing of KASTURI METAL COMPOSITE LIMITED on BSE
- **February 4, 2026**: Effective date for addition to BSE SME IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine index maintenance action with minimal market impact. The addition affects only the BSE SME IPO Index composition and may result in modest index fund rebalancing activity. Investors tracking or benchmarking against the BSE SME IPO Index should note the inclusion of this new constituent.
