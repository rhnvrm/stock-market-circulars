## Summary

BSE has announced revised terms for debt securities of Setco Auto Systems Pvt. Ltd. Four scrip codes will have new ISINs assigned and maturity dates extended to January 31, 2026. The updated securities will be available for trading from February 01, 2026.

## Key Points

- Continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20251128-32 dated November 28, 2025
- Two scrip codes (975859 and 973453) will continue trading with revised terms
- Two scrip codes will have ISINs changed: 975859 from INE0I0R07078 to INE0I0R07102, and 973453 from INE0I0R07086 to INE0I0R07110
- Quantities affected: 975 units (Scrip 975859) and 2150 units (Scrip 973453)
- Original maturity date was December 01, 2025, extended to January 31, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a corporate action relating to debt securities restructuring.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must note the revised terms and new ISINs for affected scrip codes
- Trading Members can contact Ms. Akshay Arolkar (+91 22 2272 8547) or email debt.listing@bseindia.com for further details

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 30, 2026
- **Effective Date**: February 01, 2026 (revised terms applicable for trading)
- **Original Maturity Date**: December 01, 2025
- **New Maturity Date**: January 31, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact as this affects specific debt securities of a private limited company. The maturity extension by two months and ISIN changes are administrative updates that facilitate continued trading of these securities. Holders of these debt instruments will see extended maturity timelines. No broader market implications expected.
