## Summary

BSE has listed 22,47,000 new equity shares of Hilltone Software and Gases Ltd. (Scrip Code: 544308) issued on a preferential basis to non-promoters. The shares are permitted to trade from January 29, 2026, and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares. All shares are subject to lock-in until August 16, 2026.

## Key Points

- 22,47,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 34/- per share
- Issue price: Rs. 44/- per share
- Allotted to non-promoters on preferential basis
- Date of allotment: November 29, 2025
- Distinctive numbers: 10930801 to 13177800
- ISIN: INE168C01013
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Trading commences: January 29, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification for preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities listing
- All 22,47,000 shares are subject to lock-in restrictions until August 16, 2026
- Shares cannot be transferred or sold before the lock-in expiry date

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: November 29, 2025
- **Trading Commencement Date**: January 29, 2026
- **Lock-in Period Expiry**: August 16, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This listing represents a capital raise through preferential allotment of approximately Rs. 98.87 lakhs (22.47 lakh shares at Rs. 44/- per share). The impact is limited to Hilltone Software and Gases Ltd. shareholders and trading members. The lock-in period until August 16, 2026 means these shares will not be available for trading in the secondary market for approximately 6.5 months, limiting immediate liquidity impact. The shares rank pari-passu with existing equity, ensuring equal rights for all equity shareholders.
