## Summary

BSE has informed its members that ICICI Prudential Mutual Fund has declared a Non-Business Day for the ICICI Prudential Global Advantage Fund scheme on July 20, 2026, owing to market closures in the underlying overseas markets. As a result, Subscription, Redemption, Switch, SIP, STP, and SWP transactions will not be permitted for this scheme through the BSE StAR MF Platform on that date.

## Key Points

- The notice pertains specifically to ICICI Prudential Global Advantage Fund.
- The Non-Business Day is due to market closures affecting the scheme's underlying investments.
- All transaction types—Subscription, Redemption, Switch, SIP, STP, and SWP—are suspended for the scheme on the specified date via the BSE StAR MF Platform.
- No attachments were provided with this notice.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an operational communication from the fund house relayed by BSE, not a regulatory rule change.

## Compliance Requirements

BSE StAR MF Platform members and distributors must ensure no transaction requests for ICICI Prudential Global Advantage Fund are processed on July 20, 2026, and should inform investors accordingly to avoid failed or rejected transaction attempts.

## Important Dates

- Non-Business Day: July 20, 2026 (transactions suspended for ICICI Prudential Global Advantage Fund).

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited and operational in nature, affecting only investors and distributors dealing in the specific ICICI Prudential Global Advantage Fund scheme. There is no effect on listed equities, broader market trading, or other mutual fund schemes.
