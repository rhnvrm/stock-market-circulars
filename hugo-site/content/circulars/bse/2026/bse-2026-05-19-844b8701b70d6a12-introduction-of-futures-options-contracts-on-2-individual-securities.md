## Summary

BSE has announced the introduction of futures and options (F&O) contracts on two individual securities — GE Vernova T&D India Ltd (Scrip Code: 522275) and Radico Khaitan Ltd (Scrip Code: 532497) — in the Equity Derivatives segment. Trading in these contracts will be available from May 29, 2026.

## Key Points

- Two new securities added to the Equity Derivatives (F&O) segment on BSE
- GE Vernova T&D India Ltd (Equity Scrip Code: 522275) to get F&O contracts
- Radico Khaitan Ltd (Equity Scrip Code: 532497) to get F&O contracts
- Trading commences effective May 29, 2026
- Contract details will be available in the derivatives contract master file generated end of day May 27, 2026
- Contract master file accessible via BSE extranet and the BSEIndia website

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework changes. This is an operational expansion of the existing Equity Derivatives segment to include two additional underlying securities under BSE's standard F&O framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are required to note the availability of F&O contracts for these two securities from May 29, 2026
- Members should download and update the derivatives contract master file (available end of day May 27, 2026) from BSE extranet or the BSEIndia website before commencing trading

## Important Dates

- **May 27, 2026**: Derivatives contract master file generated (end of day); available for download via extranet and BSEIndia website
- **May 29, 2026**: F&O contracts on GE Vernova T&D India Ltd and Radico Khaitan Ltd available for trading

## Impact Assessment

The inclusion of GE Vernova T&D India Ltd and Radico Khaitan Ltd in the F&O segment expands hedging and speculative trading opportunities for market participants holding or tracking these stocks. Investors in these securities will now be able to hedge positions or take leveraged exposures through futures and options. This is a routine but notable expansion of the derivatives universe on BSE, particularly relevant to traders focused on the power/energy equipment sector (GE Vernova T&D) and the spirits/FMCG sector (Radico Khaitan).
