## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for MEGASOFT LIMITED (CIN: L24100TN1999PLC042730). The company's name has been officially changed to SIGMA ADVANCED SYSTEMS LIMITED with effect from January 12, 2026. The certificate was issued by the Central Processing Centre, ROC and digitally signed by Assistant/Deputy/Registrar of Companies Shorya Chak.

## Key Points

- Company name changed from MEGASOFT LIMITED to SIGMA ADVANCED SYSTEMS LIMITED
- Effective date of name change: January 12, 2026
- Corporate Identification Number (CIN): L24100TN1999PLC042730
- Company type: Company limited by shares
- Certificate issued by: Central Processing Centre, ROC
- Registered address: No.85, Kutchery Road, Mylapore, Chennai-600004, Tamil Nadu, India
- The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative notification of a company name change approved by the Ministry of Corporate Affairs under the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014, Rule 29.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company is obligated to display the old name (MEGASOFT LIMITED) for a period of two years along with its new name (SIGMA ADVANCED SYSTEMS LIMITED) at all places wherever the company is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- All stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company and its directors on the Ministry's website www.mca.gov.in/MCA21
- The company must update its name on all official documents, letterheads, signage, and communications going forward

## Important Dates

- **January 12, 2026**: Effective date of name change from MEGASOFT LIMITED to SIGMA ADVANCED SYSTEMS LIMITED
- **January 12, 2026 to January 11, 2028**: Two-year period during which old name must be displayed alongside new name

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a standard corporate administrative action with no impact on the company's operations, financial position, or shareholder rights.

**Operational Impact**: The company will need to update all corporate stationery, documentation, signage, and digital presence to reflect the new name while maintaining dual display for two years.

**Stakeholder Impact**: No change to rights and liabilities of stakeholders. Shareholders, creditors, and other stakeholders retain all existing rights under the new company name. Stakeholders should note the name change for future correspondence and verification purposes.
