## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of equity shares of Bharat Coking Coal Limited on the exchange. This circular provides details regarding the admission and commencement of trading of the company's equity shares.

## Key Points

- Equity shares of Bharat Coking Coal Limited have been listed on BSE
- The company operates in the coal sector
- This appears to be a new listing (IPO) on the exchange
- Trading in the shares will commence as per the schedule mentioned in the circular

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members to note the new listing
- Compliance with standard listing requirements and trading regulations
- Members to update their systems for the new scrip

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: January 16, 2026
- Listing effective date as mentioned in the detailed circular
