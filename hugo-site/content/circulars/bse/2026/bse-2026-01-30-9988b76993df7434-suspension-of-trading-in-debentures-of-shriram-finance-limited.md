## Summary

BSE has announced suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of Shriram Finance Limited (ISIN: INE721A07RG1, Code: 974379) effective from February 6, 2026. The suspension is due to the company setting February 8, 2026 as the record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Company: Shriram Finance Limited
- ISIN: INE721A07RG1
- Debenture Code: 974379 (SFL-GSEC-23-2-26-PVT)
- Record Date: February 8, 2026
- No Dealings From: February 6, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Notice Reference: DR-815/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures of Shriram Finance Limited from February 6, 2026
- Trading members are required to take note of the suspension and record date
- Members must ensure compliance with the no-dealing restriction for the affected ISIN

## Important Dates

- **January 30, 2026**: Circular issued
- **February 6, 2026**: Trading suspension effective date (no dealings from this date)
- **February 8, 2026**: Record date for redemption and interest payment

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders of specific Shriram Finance debentures (ISIN: INE721A07RG1). The suspension is a routine corporate action process.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must ensure their systems and processes prevent trading in the affected debentures from February 6, 2026. This is a temporary suspension related to the redemption process.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders as of the record date (February 8, 2026) will receive redemption proceeds and interest payments. Secondary market liquidity is suspended from February 6, 2026 to facilitate the corporate action.
