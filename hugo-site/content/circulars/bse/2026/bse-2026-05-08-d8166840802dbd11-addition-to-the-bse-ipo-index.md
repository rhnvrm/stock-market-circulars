## Summary

BSE Index Services has announced that ONEMI Technology Solutions Limited (BSE ticker: 544754) will be added to the BSE IPO Index effective at the open of Tuesday, May 12, 2026. The company was listed on BSE on May 8, 2026, per Notice No. 20260507-36.

## Key Points

- ONEMI Technology Solutions Limited (ticker: 544754) listed on BSE on May 8, 2026.
- The stock will be added to the **BSE IPO Index** effective at market open on **May 12, 2026**.
- The corporate action is included in the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE) dated May 8, 2026.
- Queries can be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index reconstitution following a new company listing on BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

Index fund managers and ETFs tracking the BSE IPO Index should update their portfolios to include ONEMI Technology Solutions Limited (544754) prior to market open on May 12, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **May 8, 2026**: ONEMI Technology Solutions Limited listed on BSE; corporate action file updated.
- **May 12, 2026**: Effective date for addition of 544754 to the BSE IPO Index (at market open).

## Impact Assessment

This addition primarily affects passive investment vehicles and index-linked products tracking the BSE IPO Index. Market participants should review portfolio weightings accordingly. The impact on the broader market is minimal; however, funds replicating the BSE IPO Index will need to purchase shares of ONEMI Technology Solutions Limited before May 12, 2026.
