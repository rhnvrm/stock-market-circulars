## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Prabha Energy Limited has fixed a record date to identify eligible shareholders of its partly paid-up equity shares who will receive the second and final call notice for payment of the balance issue price. Trading members are advised not to deal in the partly paid equity shares of the company on the specified date.

## Key Points

- Company: Prabha Energy Limited, Scrip Code 890236 (Partly Paid Equity Shares), DR- 668/2026-2027
- Purpose: To ascertain shareholders of partly paid shares eligible to pay the second and final call
- Call amount: Rs.47.52 per Rights Equity Share, comprising Re.0.33 towards Face Value and Rs.47.19 towards Premium (33% of the Issue Price of Rs.144)
- Trading members are directed not to deal in these partly paid shares on the no-dealings date

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing operations notice under BSE's standard process for partly paid-up securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must refrain from dealing in Prabha Energy Limited's partly paid equity shares (Scrip Code 890236) on 09 July 2026, the designated no-dealings date, to ensure accurate identification of shareholders liable for the call payment.

## Important Dates

- Record Date / No Dealings Date: 09 July 2026

## Impact Assessment

The notice has minimal market-wide impact, being restricted to a single company's partly paid equity shares. It is procedurally important for trading members and shareholders of Prabha Energy Limited to ensure compliance with the no-dealings restriction and awareness of the impending second and final call obligation.
