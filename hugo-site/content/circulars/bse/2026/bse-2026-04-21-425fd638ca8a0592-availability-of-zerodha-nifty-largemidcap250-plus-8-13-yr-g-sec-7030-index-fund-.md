## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260421-24 informing members that the ZERODHA NIFTY LARGEMIDCAP250 PLUS 8-13 YR G-SEC 70:30 INDEX FUND will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions, along with SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities on the BSE StAR MF Platform, effective April 22, 2026. This follows the earlier Notice No. 20260331-1 dated March 31, 2026.

## Key Points

- The fund will be available for ongoing transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform from April 22, 2026.
- Transactions supported include subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP.
- The specific plan available is the Direct Plan - Growth option under the Equity category.
- ISIN for the Direct Plan - Growth option: INF0R8F01208.
- This reopening was previously announced via Notice No. 20260331-1 dated March 31, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice is an operational update pursuant to a prior BSE notice (20260331-1) regarding the fund's reopening for ongoing transactions.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of BSE who are mutual fund distributors or intermediaries using the BSE StAR MF Platform should update their systems and inform their clients about the availability of this fund for ongoing transactions from April 22, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **April 21, 2026**: Notice issued.
- **April 22, 2026**: ZERODHA NIFTY LARGEMIDCAP250 PLUS 8-13 YR G-SEC 70:30 INDEX FUND reopens for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP on BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Impact Assessment

This notice has a limited and targeted operational impact. It enables investors and distributors to transact in the ZERODHA NIFTY LARGEMIDCAP250 PLUS 8-13 YR G-SEC 70:30 INDEX FUND — a hybrid index fund combining 70% exposure to the Nifty LargeMidcap 250 Index (equity) and 30% to 8-13 year government securities — through the BSE StAR MF Platform. No broader market disruption or compliance burden is expected. The fund falls under the Equity category with ISIN INF0R8F01208.
