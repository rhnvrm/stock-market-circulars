## Summary

BSE has activated the SMART Switch feature on the BSE StAR MF platform for the Helios Arbitrage Fund NFO. This feature allows investors to seamlessly switch from eligible Helios Overnight Fund schemes into the new Helios Arbitrage Fund during its New Fund Offer period.

## Key Points

- The SMART Switch feature is activated specifically for the Helios Arbitrage Fund NFO on BSE StAR MF.
- Two source schemes are eligible for the switch:
  1. Helios Overnight Fund – Direct Plan – Growth
  2. Helios Overnight Fund – Regular Plan – Growth

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational enablement notice for a platform feature on BSE StAR MF.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Distributors and investors using BSE StAR MF may utilise the SMART Switch option when transacting in the Helios Arbitrage Fund NFO.

## Important Dates

No specific dates mentioned beyond the NFO period of Helios Arbitrage Fund (dates to be confirmed from the NFO offer document).

## Impact Assessment

Limited operational impact. The circular facilitates investor convenience by enabling automated switching from Helios Overnight Fund plans into the Helios Arbitrage Fund NFO. This is a routine platform-level activation with no broader market or regulatory implications.
