## Summary

BSE has listed eight series of Secured, Redeemable, Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) issued by Kosamattam Finance Limited, allotted on February 20, 2026. The NCDs carry a face value and issue price of Rs. 1,000 each, are rated IND A/Stable, and span maturities from May 2027 to February 2033. The eight series include both cumulative and monthly interest payment options with effective yields ranging from 8.00% to 10.47% per annum.

## Key Points

- Eight NCD series (Series I–VIII) listed on BSE debt market segment with scrip codes 940882, 940884, 940886, 940888, 940890, 940892, 940894, and 940896
- Face value and issue price: Rs. 1,000 per NCD; market lot: one NCD
- Total securities across all series: 282,102 (I) + 125,360 (II) + 512,952 (III) + 173,079 (IV) + 244,538 (V) + 176,058 (VI) + 300,367 (VII) + 185,544 (VIII)
- Interest options: Cumulative (Series I, IV, VI, VIII) and Monthly (Series II, III, V, VII)
- Coupon rates: 8.50% (II), 9.00% (III), 9.75% (V), 10.00% (VII); Series I, IV, VI, VIII are cumulative with no periodic coupon
- Effective yields range from 8.00% (Series I) to 10.47% (Series VII)
- Credit rating: IND A/Stable
- Date of allotment: February 20, 2026
- ISINs assigned: INE403Q07GP6, INE403Q07GW2, INE403Q07GU6, INE403Q07GT8, INE403Q07GV4, INE403Q07GR2, INE403Q07GS0, INE403Q07GQ4

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard debt securities listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Kosamattam Finance Limited must comply with BSE listing obligations for listed debt securities
- Periodic interest payments for monthly series (II, III, V, VII) commence April 1, 2026
- Redemption obligations must be met on respective maturity dates per the terms disclosed

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** February 20, 2026
- **First Interest Payment Date (Monthly Series II, III, V, VII):** April 1, 2026
- **First Interest Payment / Redemption Date (Cumulative Series I):** May 19, 2027
- **Redemption Dates by Series:**
  - Series I: May 19, 2027 (Rs. 1,101/NCD)
  - Series II: February 19, 2028 (Rs. 1,000/NCD)
  - Series III: February 19, 2029 (Rs. 1,000/NCD)
  - Series IV: February 19, 2029 (Rs. 1,304/NCD)
  - Series V: August 19, 2029 (Rs. 1,000/NCD)
  - Series VI: April 19, 2030 (Rs. 1,500/NCD)
  - Series VII: February 19, 2031 (Rs. 1,000/NCD)
  - Series VIII: February 19, 2033 (Rs. 2,000/NCD)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing of NCD securities for Kosamattam Finance Limited, an NBFC. The listing provides liquidity to NCD holders on the BSE debt market segment. The IND A/Stable credit rating indicates adequate credit quality. The broad range of maturities (1–7 years) and both cumulative and monthly payout options suggest a retail-focused issuance. No broad market or regulatory impact is anticipated.
