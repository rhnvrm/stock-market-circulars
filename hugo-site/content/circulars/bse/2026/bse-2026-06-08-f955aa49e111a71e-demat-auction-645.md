## Summary

BSE Limited (via Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd.) has invited offers from trading members for purchase of securities in Demat Auction 645, pertaining to Rolling Settlement No. DR-645/2026-27. The auction session is scheduled for June 08, 2026, with securities pay-in due on June 09, 2026.

## Key Points

- Demat Auction 645 covers Rolling Settlement No. DR-645/2026-27.
- Auction offer entry window: 02:00 p.m. to 02:45 p.m. on June 08, 2026.
- Auction matching: 02:45 p.m. to 03:00 p.m.
- Delivery statement and accepted offer reports: 03:00 p.m. to 03:30 p.m.
- Money statement and final reports: 03:35 p.m. to 05:30 p.m.
- Securities pay-in: June 09, 2026 at 08:00 a.m.
- Under the Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment, settlement is processed through the Clearing Member of the Trading Member.
- The earlier restriction on trading members from offering securities in auctions for settlements in which they had short-delivered has been removed.
- No corrections will be permitted for punching errors in securities code or rate.
- Trading members should refer to the cutoff rate on the Auction Offer Entry Screen and check Corporate Action notices (ex-rights, ex-bonus, ex-split, etc.) before submitting offers.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. The notice reiterates the previously implemented Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment, under which settlement flows through the Clearing Member of a Trading Member, and confirms that restrictions on auction participation for short-delivering members have been lifted.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must submit auction offers during the designated offer entry window (02:00–02:45 p.m.).
- Members and Custodians must verify their auction pay-in obligations and execute securities pay-in in depositories within depository cut-off timings by June 09, 2026 at 08:00 a.m.
- Trading members must exercise care when entering securities codes and rates; no corrections will be allowed post-submission.
- Members should review applicable Corporate Action notices before submitting offers.

## Important Dates

- **June 08, 2026**: Auction offer entry (02:00–02:45 p.m.), Auction matching (02:45–03:00 p.m.), Delivery statement issuance (03:00–03:30 p.m.), Money statement and final reports (03:35–05:30 p.m.).
- **June 09, 2026**: Auction pay-in at 08:00 a.m.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice applicable to trading and clearing members involved in settlements with short deliveries under DR-645/2026-27. The impact is limited to affected members who must participate in the auction to fulfill delivery obligations. No broad market or regulatory impact is anticipated.
