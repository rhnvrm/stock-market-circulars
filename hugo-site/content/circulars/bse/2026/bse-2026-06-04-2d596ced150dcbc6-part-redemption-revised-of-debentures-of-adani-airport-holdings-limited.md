## Summary

BSE has issued a revised notice (in continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20260525-10 dated May 25, 2026) updating the reduced face value for the part redemption of debentures of Adani Airport Holdings Limited. The revised face value is Rs. 64,733 per debenture, effective June 05, 2026.

## Key Points

- This notice revises Exchange Notice No. 20260525-10 dated May 25, 2026 regarding part redemption of debentures with scrip code 976065.
- The company (Adani Airport Holdings Limited) has communicated a revised reduced face value of Rs. 64,733 per debenture.
- Scrip: AAHL-9.35%-8-9-28-PVT | ISIN: INE0GCN07047 | Scrip Code: 976065.
- The debentures will trade with the revised reduced face value from June 05, 2026 onwards.
- Reference number: DR-645/2026-2027.

## Regulatory Changes

The face value per debenture has been revised downward as part of the part redemption process. The earlier notice (20260525-10) is superseded by this revised face value figure of Rs. 64,733 per debenture.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members of BSE are required to take note of the revised reduced face value and ensure that all trades in scrip code 976065 (AAHL-9.35%-8-9-28-PVT) from June 05, 2026 reflect the updated face value of Rs. 64,733 per debenture.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 04, 2026
- **Effective Date for Revised Face Value:** June 05, 2026
- **Original Notice:** Exchange Notice No. 20260525-10 dated May 25, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This revision affects trading members active in BSE's debt segment who hold or trade Adani Airport Holdings Limited debentures (ISIN: INE0GCN07047). The change in face value directly impacts valuation and settlement computations for this NCD. Members must update their systems to reflect the revised face value of Rs. 64,733 before the market opens on June 05, 2026. The impact is limited to this specific debenture series and does not affect equity markets.
