## Summary

BSE announces that JIOBLACKROCK LOW DURATION FUND and JIOBLACKROCK SHORT DURATION FUND will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective January 22, 2026. The schemes will also support SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities.

## Key Points

- Two JioBlackRock debt schemes reopening for transactions: Low Duration Fund and Short Duration Fund
- Effective date: January 22, 2026
- Available transaction types: Subscription and Redemption
- Facility availability: SIP, STP, and SWP
- Platform: BSE StAR MF Platform
- Both schemes are Direct Plan - Growth options
- Reference to previous notice: 20260107-18

## Scheme Details

**JIOBLACKROCK LOW DURATION FUND - DIRECT PLAN - GROWTH**
- Category: DEBT
- ISIN: INF22M001150

**JIOBLACKROCK SHORT DURATION FUND - DIRECT PLAN - GROWTH**
- Category: DEBT
- ISIN: INF22M001143

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding scheme availability.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements. BSE members are informed of the scheme availability for processing investor transactions through the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 21, 2026
- **Effective Date**: January 22, 2026 (schemes reopen for transactions)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine operational notice affecting only two specific JioBlackRock mutual fund schemes.

**Investor Impact**: Positive for existing and potential investors as it restores the ability to transact in these debt fund schemes with full facilities including systematic plans.

**Operational Impact**: BSE StAR MF Platform members can resume processing transactions for these two schemes from January 22, 2026.
