## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Lypsa Gems & Jewellery Ltd (Scrip Code: 534532) has informed the Exchange about a change in its corporate name. Consequently, the SCRIP ID and Abbreviated Name on the BOLT Plus System will be updated effective July 14, 2026.

## Key Points

- Existing Name: Lypsa Gems & Jewellery Ltd
- New Name: Aurus Gem Corporation Limited
- Scrip Code: 534532 (unchanged)
- New Scrip ID on BOLT Plus System: AURUS
- New Abbreviated Name on BOLT Plus System: AURUS
- Change supported by Certificate of Incorporation submitted by the company

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is an administrative update to reflect the company's change of name as evidenced by its Certificate of Incorporation.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should update their systems and records to reflect the new scrip ID and abbreviated name for the security effective the stated date. No action required from investors regarding existing holdings, as the scrip code remains unchanged.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 08, 2026
- Effective Date of Name Change on BOLT Plus System: July 14, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact administrative notification. The company's underlying scrip code (534532) remains unchanged, so there is no effect on existing shareholdings, trading continuity, or corporate structure. Trading members and systems referencing the scrip by name or abbreviated code should update to 'AURUS' / 'Aurus Gem Corporation Limited' from the effective date to avoid identification errors.
