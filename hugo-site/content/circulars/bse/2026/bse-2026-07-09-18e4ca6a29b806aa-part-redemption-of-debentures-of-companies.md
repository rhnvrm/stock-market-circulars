## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that seven companies have fixed record dates for payment of interest and/or part redemption of their outstanding Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs). Trading in these debentures will occur at a reduced face value from the respective ex-date/record date onward, reflecting the partial redemption of principal.

## Key Points

- Regency Fincorp Ltd (Scrip Code 977511, ISIN INE964R07051, Series RFL-14%-2-2-27): Record/Ex Date 13/07/2026, reduced face value Rs. 5,000 per debenture, Settlement DR 670/2026-2027.
- Belstar Microfinance Limited (Scrip Code 977020, ISIN INE443L07190, Series BML-9.1%-12-8-27): Record/Ex Date 28/07/2026, reduced face value Rs. 50,000 per debenture, Settlement DR 681/2026-2027.
- IKF Finance Limited (Scrip Code 977108, ISIN INE859C07246, Series IKFFL-9.40%-12-08-27): Record/Ex Date 28/07/2026, reduced face value Rs. 66,667 per debenture, Settlement DR 681/2026-2027.
- VR Dakshin Private Limited (Scrip Code 974772, ISIN INE084S07049, Series VRDPL-TBILL+SPREAD-31-3-35): Record/Ex Date 29/07/2026, reduced face value Rs. 92,666.67 per debenture, Settlement DR 682/2026-2027.
- VR Dakshin Private Limited (Scrip Code 974773, ISIN INE084S07031, Series VDPL-10.70%-31-3-35): Record/Ex Date 29/07/2026, reduced face value Rs. 92,666.67 per debenture, Settlement DR 682/2026-2027.
- VR Dakshin Private Limited (Scrip Code 974774, ISIN INE084S07056, Series VDPL-MIBOR+SPREAD-31-3-35): Record/Ex Date 29/07/2026, reduced face value Rs. 92,666.67 per debenture, Settlement DR 682/2026-2027.
- Fedbank Financial Services Limited (Scrip Code 975918, ISIN INE007N07058, Series FFSL-8.50%-RESET RATE-14-8-28): Record/Ex Date 30/07/2026, reduced face value Rs. 50,000 per debenture, Settlement DR 683/2026-2027.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine operational notice under BSE's Listing Operations department for the Debt segment, applying existing procedures for interest payment and part redemption of NCDs.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members dealing in these debentures must update their systems to reflect the reduced face value from the respective record/ex-date and ensure trades are settled per the specified settlement DR numbers.

## Important Dates

- 13 July 2026: Record/Ex Date for Regency Fincorp Ltd NCD (Settlement DR 670/2026-2027).
- 28 July 2026: Record/Ex Date for Belstar Microfinance Limited and IKF Finance Limited NCDs (Settlement DR 681/2026-2027).
- 29 July 2026: Record/Ex Date for all three VR Dakshin Private Limited NCD series (Settlement DR 682/2026-2027).
- 30 July 2026: Record/Ex Date for Fedbank Financial Services Limited NCD (Settlement DR 683/2026-2027).

## Impact Assessment

This notice affects only holders and traders of the specific listed NCDs mentioned, all of which are privately placed debt instruments of non-listed or debt-only issuers. There is no direct impact on equity markets or listed equity stock prices; the operational change is limited to face-value adjustment for continued trading of the debentures post part-redemption.
