## Summary

BSE Index Services Private Limited has announced the addition of VEGORAMA PUNJABI ANGITHI LIMITED (Exchange ticker: 544765) to the BSE SME IPO Index, effective at the open of Friday, May 29, 2026. The company was listed on BSE on May 27, 2026, with reference to prior Notice No. 20260526-31.

## Key Points

- VEGORAMA PUNJABI ANGITHI LIMITED (ticker: 544765) listed on BSE effective May 27, 2026.
- The stock will be added to the **BSE SME IPO Index** effective at market open on **May 29, 2026**.
- This index action has been included in the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE) dated May 27, 2026.
- Queries can be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index rebalancing action following a new SME IPO listing.

## Compliance Requirements

Index-tracking funds and ETFs benchmarked against the BSE SME IPO Index should update their portfolios to reflect the addition of VEGORAMA PUNJABI ANGITHI LIMITED (544765) effective May 29, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **May 27, 2026**: Listing of VEGORAMA PUNJABI ANGITHI LIMITED on BSE.
- **May 29, 2026**: Effective date for addition to BSE SME IPO Index (at market open).

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard index inclusion event with limited broader market impact. It affects participants who track or replicate the BSE SME IPO Index, requiring portfolio adjustments before the open of May 29, 2026. The corporate action file (*.SDE) has been updated to reflect this change.
