## Summary

BSE has issued a revised live activities schedule for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for OSCAR GLOBAL LTD (Security Symbol: OSCARGLO, Scrip Code: 530713, ISIN: INE473F01010). The offer is scheduled from Friday, January 2, 2026 to Friday, January 16, 2026. The offer targets up to 858,000 equity shares at an offer price of Rs. 12.15 per share with a face value of Rs. 10.00.

## Key Points

- **Security Name**: OSCAR GLOBAL LTD (OSCARGLO)
- **ISIN**: INE473F01010
- **Scrip Code**: 530713
- **Offer Size**: Up to 858,000 equity shares
- **Offer Price**: Rs. 12.15 per share
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10.00
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Opening Date**: Friday, January 2, 2026
- **Closing Date**: Friday, January 16, 2026
- Trading members can bid for investors having shares in demat form only
- Members must ensure UCC of the investor is registered with correct PAN Number and Category

## Regulatory Changes

This is a revised schedule notice. No new regulatory changes are introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members and Custodian Members must note the revised schedule of activities
- Trading Members must ensure UCC (Unique Client Code) of the investor is registered and/or updated with correct PAN Number and Category in the Exchange UCC database
- Trading Members can only bid for investors having shares in demat form
- Members and Custodians should note that if no securities are accepted, there will be no End of Issue files generated in Extranet

## Important Dates

**Live OTB Session Schedule:**

- **Bid Offering Period**: January 2, 2026 to January 16, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Give-up / Take-up Period**: January 2, 2026 to January 14, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Give-up (Last Day)**: January 16, 2026 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Take-up (Last Day)**: January 16, 2026 (9:15 AM to 4:00 PM)

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to shareholders of OSCAR GLOBAL LTD and trading members facilitating the acquisition window. The offer size of 858,000 shares represents a relatively small transaction. Trading members using the iBBS platform, RTRMS, and Extranet systems for OTB module will need to follow the revised schedule. The offer is accessible to various investor categories including FIIs, FIs, Mutual Funds, Insurance Companies, Other QIBs, Companies, and Individuals.
