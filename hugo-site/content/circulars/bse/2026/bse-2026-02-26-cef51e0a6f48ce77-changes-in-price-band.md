## Summary

BSE has revised the price bands for 9 equity scrips under its surveillance framework, effective February 27, 2026. The revised bands range from 2% (Transglobe Foods Ltd) to 20% (PAN HR Solution Ltd), reflecting updated risk assessments for these securities.

## Key Points

- Price bands revised for 9 scrips effective February 27, 2026
- Bands vary from 2% to 20% depending on the scrip
- Revision is effective from the next trading day (February 27, 2026)
- Issued by BSE Surveillance department under Notice No. 20260226-39
- Trading members should update their systems accordingly

## Regulatory Changes

The following price band revisions have been made:

| Scrip Code | Scrip Name | Revised Price Band |
|---|---|---|
| 533292 | A2Z Infra Engineering Ltd | 5% |
| 523467 | Jai Mata Glass Ltd | 10% |
| 544693 | Kanishk Aluminium India Ltd | 5% |
| 544437 | Marc Loire Fashions Ltd | 5% |
| 511535 | NDA Securities Ltd | 5% |
| 544698 | PAN HR Solution Ltd | 20% |
| 544456 | R M Drip and Sprinklers Systems Ltd | 10% |
| 538923 | Sofcom Systems Ltd | 10% |
| 519367 | Transglobe Foods Ltd | 2% |

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the revised price bands for the affected scrips
- Orders placed outside the new price band limits will be rejected by the exchange system
- Members may contact BSE Surveillance at bse.surv@bseindia.com for clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** February 26, 2026
- **Effective Date:** February 27, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The revisions tighten or adjust circuit limits for these securities, directly constraining intraday price movement. Notably, Transglobe Foods Ltd (519367) has a very restrictive 2% band, indicating heightened surveillance concern, while PAN HR Solution Ltd (544698) has a wider 20% band. Trading members and investors holding these scrips should be aware of the new limits to avoid order rejections and manage intraday risk accordingly.
