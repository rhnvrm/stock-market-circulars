## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Abhijit Trading Company Ltd (Scrip Code: 539560) has changed its name to Malt Land Distilleries Limited. The SCRIP ID and abbreviated name on the BOLT Plus System will be updated accordingly, effective June 12, 2026.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code 539560 undergoes a company name change from Abhijit Trading Company Ltd to Malt Land Distilleries Limited.
- The new SCRIP ID on BOLT Plus System will be **MALTLAND**.
- The new abbreviated name on BOLT Plus System will be **MALTLAND**.
- Change is effective from **June 12, 2026**.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a purely administrative update to exchange system identifiers following the company's legal name change.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are advised to update their records and systems to reflect the new company name (Malt Land Distilleries Limited) and updated BOLT Plus identifiers (MALTLAND) for Scrip Code 539560 effective June 12, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **June 8, 2026**: Notice issued by BSE.
- **June 12, 2026**: Effective date for the name change and updated SCRIP ID/abbreviated name on BOLT Plus System.

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine administrative name change affecting only the display identifiers on BSE's BOLT Plus trading system. Scrip Code 539560 remains unchanged, ensuring continuity in trade settlement and order routing. Trading members and market participants should note the updated identifiers to avoid confusion in order placement.
