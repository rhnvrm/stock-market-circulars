## Summary

BSE has informed its members that Kotak Services Fund will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption, along with SIP, STP, and SWP facility, on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective March 02, 2026. This notice follows an earlier communication (Notice No. 20260203-22) and covers six scheme variants under both Regular and Direct plans.

## Key Points

- Kotak Services Fund reopens for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform from **March 02, 2026**
- Both subscription and redemption are available
- SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities are enabled
- Six scheme variants are covered across Regular and Direct plans
- This is a follow-up to Notice No. 20260203-22

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update restoring normal transaction availability for Kotak Services Fund on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action required from members. Distributors and platform users may begin facilitating transactions in the listed Kotak Services Fund schemes from March 02, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **March 02, 2026**: Effective date for reopening of Kotak Services Fund on BSE StAR MF Platform for ongoing subscription, redemption, and SIP/STP/SWP transactions

## Impact Assessment

Low operational impact. This notice restores transactional access to Kotak Services Fund schemes on the BSE StAR MF Platform, benefiting mutual fund distributors and investors who use the platform. No market-wide or compliance implications. Relevant primarily to members operating on the BSE StAR MF Platform who wish to offer or transact in Kotak Services Fund schemes.
