## Summary

BSE's Surveillance department (DOSS) issued Notice No. 20260624-24 informing members and investors about clarifications sought from listed companies regarding news articles appearing in newspapers, websites, and television channels. Raymond Ltd has submitted its reply, while Rajesh Exports Ltd's response is still awaited.

## Key Points

- BSE sought clarifications to verify accuracy of news articles and inform members/investors accordingly
- Raymond Ltd (Scrip Code: 500330) has submitted its clarification in response to a Times of India article
- Rajesh Exports Ltd (Scrip Code: 531500) has not yet submitted its clarification in response to a MoneyControl article
- Notice issued by BSE Surveillance department (Deputy Vice President Sanjay Jain and Manager Shreya Ghose)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Rajesh Exports Ltd (Scrip Code: 531500) is expected to submit its clarification on the MoneyControl news article to BSE
- Companies listed on BSE are required to respond promptly to BSE's requests for clarification on media reports under their continuous disclosure obligations

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 24 June 2026
- No specific deadline mentioned for Rajesh Exports Ltd's pending reply

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine surveillance notice with minimal market impact. Investors in Raymond Ltd (500330) may refer to the attached reply for clarity on the news article in question. Investors in Rajesh Exports Ltd (531500) should await the company's forthcoming clarification. No trading restrictions or price band changes are associated with this notice.
