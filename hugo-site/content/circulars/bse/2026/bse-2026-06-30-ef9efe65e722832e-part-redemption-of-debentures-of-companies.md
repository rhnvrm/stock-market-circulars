## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 13 companies have fixed record dates for part redemption of their Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs). Post redemption, trading in these debentures will occur at a reduced face value effective from the respective ex-dates.

## Key Points

- 13 NCD issuances across 8 companies are undergoing part redemption in July 2026
- Trading members must note that affected debentures will trade with reduced face value from the ex-date onwards
- Record dates span 03 July 2026 to 13 July 2026
- All settlements are under DR series 664/2026-2027, 669/2026-2027, and 670/2026-2027
- No attachments accompany this notice

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework introduced. This is a standard corporate action notification under BSE Listing Operations for the Debt segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure trades in the affected debentures reflect the reduced face value from the respective ex-dates
- Members should update client records and systems to account for the revised face values post part redemption

## Impact Assessment

This notice affects NCD holders and debt segment trading members. The part redemptions reduce the outstanding principal of these instruments, and post ex-date trading must reflect the lower face values. Impact is confined to debt market participants holding or trading these specific NCDs. No equity market impact is anticipated.
