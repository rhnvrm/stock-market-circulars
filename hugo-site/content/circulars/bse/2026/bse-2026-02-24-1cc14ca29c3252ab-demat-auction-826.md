## Summary

BSE has published the Demat Auction list for Settlement No. 826, dated 24 February 2026. This notice lists securities that were subject to auction due to short delivery in the normal rolling settlement cycle. A total of 81 scrip entries across various companies are listed along with the auction quantities.

## Key Points

- Settlement No. 826, dated 24/02/2026
- 81 securities listed for demat auction
- Quantities range from 1 share (e.g., Allied Blend, KITEX GARMEN) to 25,638 shares (DEE DEVELOPM)
- Notable large auction quantities: DEE DEVELOPM (25,638), Blue Jet Hea (10,686), GUJARAT TOOL (13,598), R M DRIP AND (5,136)
- Covers a broad cross-section of BSE-listed companies across sectors

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational auction notice under existing BSE settlement and auction mechanisms.

## Compliance Requirements

- Buying brokers/members with short delivery obligations must participate in or honour the auction outcome for the listed securities.
- Settlement of auction proceeds follows standard BSE demat auction settlement procedures.
- Members should ensure timely delivery/pay-in as per the auction settlement schedule.

## Important Dates

- Auction Date: 24 February 2026
- Settlement No.: 826

## Impact Assessment

Low market-wide impact. This is a standard settlement auction notice. Affected parties are limited to the brokers and clients involved in the short deliveries for the 81 listed scrips. The largest auction quantities are in DEE DEVELOPM (25,638), GUJARAT TOOL (13,598), and Blue Jet Hea (10,686), which may see minor price impact in thin-market scrips.
