## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that several companies have fixed Record Dates for payment of interest and/or redemption of specific Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) and Bonds. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debt securities on and around the specified record/ex dates.

## Key Points

- IndusInd General Insurance Company Limited: IGICL-9.10%-17-08-26-PVT (ISIN INE124D08019), Record/Ex Date 31/07/2026, Settlement DR 684/2026-2027
- Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited: NWFL-9.85%-20-8-26-PVT (ISIN INE918K07PS1), Record/Ex Date 05/08/2026, Settlement DR 687/2026-2027
- HDFC Bank Ltd.: HDFCB-7.90%-24-08-26-PVT (ISIN INE040A08484), Record Date 08/08/2026, Ex Date 07/08/2026, Settlement DR 689/2026-2027
- Muthoot Fincorp Limited: MFLIV-9.30%-24-8-26-NCD (ISIN INE549K07FV9) and MFLIV-24-8-26-NCD (ISIN INE549K07FQ9), Record/Ex Date 10/08/2026, Settlement DR 690/2026-2027
- Power Finance Corporation Ltd: PFCL-ZC-25-8-26-PVT (ISIN INE134E08MS3) and PFCL-7.64%-25-8-26-PVT (ISIN INE134E08MT1), Record/Ex Date 10/08/2026, Settlement DR 690/2026-2027
- Power Finance Corporation Ltd: PFCL-6.09%-27-08-26-PVT (ISIN INE134E08LK2), Record/Ex Date 11/08/2026, Settlement DR 691/2026-2027
- Midland Microfin Limited: MML-10.75%-27-8-26-PVT (ISIN INE884Q07723), Record/Ex Date 12/08/2026, Settlement DR 692/2026-2027
- Muthoot Fincorp Limited: MFL-8.35%-6-9-26-NCD (ISIN INE549K07BN5) and MFL-6-9-26-NCD (ISIN INE549K07BK1), Record/Ex Date 13/08/2026, Settlement DR 693/2026-2027
- Aye Finance Limited: AFL-10.50%-28-8-26-PVT (ISIN INE501X07620), Record/Ex Date 13/08/2026, Settlement DR 693/2026-2027
- Sammaan Capital Limited: SCL-9.25%-28-8-26-PVT (ISIN INE148I07UY7), Record/Ex Date 13/08/2026, Settlement DR 693/2026-2027

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a routine administrative advisory under BSE's standard process for handling record dates on listed debt instruments; no new regulation is introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of the Exchange must not deal in the listed Debentures/Bonds of the above companies on their respective record/ex dates to ensure orderly settlement of interest payment and/or redemption proceeds.

## Important Dates

- 31 Jul 2026: Record/Ex Date for IndusInd General Insurance NCD
- 05 Aug 2026: Record/Ex Date for Nuvama Wealth Finance NCD
- 07-08 Aug 2026: Ex Date/Record Date for HDFC Bank NCD
- 10 Aug 2026: Record/Ex Date for Muthoot Fincorp and Power Finance Corporation NCDs
- 11 Aug 2026: Record/Ex Date for Power Finance Corporation NCD
- 12 Aug 2026: Record/Ex Date for Midland Microfin NCD
- 13 Aug 2026: Record/Ex Date for Muthoot Fincorp, Aye Finance and Sammaan Capital NCDs

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact, procedural notice limited to specific privately placed NCD/bond ISINs and does not affect equity trading, share prices, or broader market operations. It ensures orderly interest payment/redemption processing for debt security holders and requires no action beyond routine compliance by trading members handling these instruments.
