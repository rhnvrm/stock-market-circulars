## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (No. 20260227-4) informing trading members that the equity shares of Accord Transformer & Switchgear Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the BSE SME segment. The specific listing date and security details will be communicated through a subsequent notice.

## Key Points

- Accord Transformer & Switchgear Limited is being listed on the BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544710
- Symbol: ACCORDTS
- ISIN: INE132201018
- Exact listing date to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing admission notice under BSE's SME listing framework.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are advised to note the upcoming listing of ACCORDTS and await the follow-up notice containing the confirmed listing date and full security details before initiating any trades.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: February 27, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced via a separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This notice signals the imminent listing of Accord Transformer & Switchgear Limited on the BSE SME platform. Impact is moderate and limited to market participants tracking SME IPOs and new listings. Investors and trading members should watch for the follow-up circular confirming the listing date to plan accordingly.
