## Summary

BSE has listed 68,47,600 new equity shares of Rs. 10/- each of Kshitij Investments Ltd (Scrip Code: 503626), issued at par to Promoters and Non-Promoters on a preferential basis. These shares are permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Monday, April 20, 2026. The new shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company.

## Key Points

- 68,47,600 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at par on a preferential basis
- Shares allotted to both Promoters and Non-Promoters
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE733N01011
- Distribution numbers: 3152401 to 10000000
- Date of allotment: January 5, 2026
- Trading permitted from April 20, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice for newly allotted securities under a preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed that the new securities are listed and permitted to trade from April 20, 2026. Members should update their systems to reflect the expanded share count and applicable lock-in restrictions before commencing trades in these securities.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** January 5, 2026
- **Trading Commencement:** April 20, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (10,00,000 shares — Dist. Nos. 3152401–4152400):** October 25, 2027
- **Lock-in Expiry (13,35,000 shares — Dist. Nos. 4152401–5487400):** October 25, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (10,00,000 shares — Dist. Nos. 5487401–6487400):** October 25, 2027
- **Lock-in Expiry (35,12,600 shares — Dist. Nos. 6487401–10000000):** October 25, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 68,47,600 equity shares to the tradable float of Kshitij Investments Ltd, though a significant portion remains under lock-in until October 2026 and October 2027. The immediate freely tradable portion is limited by these lock-in restrictions, which may constrain liquidity impact in the near term. The preferential allotment at par (Rs. 10/-) signals capital raising activity. Overall market impact is minimal given the company's small-cap status.
