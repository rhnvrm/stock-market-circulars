## Summary

BSE has listed 58,00,000 equity shares of Choice International Ltd (Scrip Code: 531358) effective from Thursday, January 29, 2026. These shares were issued at a premium of Rs. 290/- on a face value of Rs. 10/- to Promoter and Non-Promoter on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. The shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares and are subject to lock-in periods.

## Key Points

- Total shares listed: 58,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each
- Issue price: Rs. 300/- (Rs. 10/- face value + Rs. 290/- premium)
- Allotment basis: Preferential allotment to Promoter and Non-Promoter
- Warrant conversion: Shares issued pursuant to conversion of warrants
- Date of allotment: November 28, 2025
- Trading commencement: January 29, 2026
- Distinctive numbers: 208988834 to 214788833
- ISIN: INE102B01014
- Ranking: Pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must note the listing of new securities and lock-in restrictions:
- 40,00,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 208988834-212988833) locked-in until August 1, 2026
- 18,00,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 212988834-214788833) locked-in until August 2, 2027

## Important Dates

- **November 28, 2025**: Date of allotment
- **January 29, 2026**: Trading commencement date
- **August 1, 2026**: Lock-in expiry for 40 lakh shares
- **August 2, 2027**: Lock-in expiry for 18 lakh shares

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notice with minimal market impact. The preferential allotment increases Choice International Ltd's equity base by 58 lakh shares. The staggered lock-in periods (until August 2026 and August 2027) will restrict immediate liquidity for these shares. Trading members should be aware of the lock-in restrictions when processing trades in this security.
