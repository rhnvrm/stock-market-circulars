## Summary

BSE has notified members that ICICI Mutual Fund will launch two Specialized Investment Fund (SIF) New Fund Offers (NFOs) on the BSE StAR MF Platform: the ISIF Active Asset Allocator Long-Short Fund and the ISIF Equity Long-Short Fund. The NFO subscription period runs from May 19, 2026, to June 2, 2026, with SIP facility available. Tentative allotment date is June 5, 2026.

## Key Points

- Two SIF NFOs are being launched: ISIF Active Asset Allocator Long-Short Fund and ISIF Equity Long-Short Fund, each available in Regular and Direct Plan – Growth options (4 schemes total).
- NFO period: May 19, 2026 to June 2, 2026; SIP/XSIP registrations must be authenticated by clients by 3:00 PM on June 2, 2026.
- All four schemes are classified as EQUITY type with a tentative allotment date of June 5, 2026.
- Lumpsum purchases on BSE StAR MF Platform will be accepted until midnight on June 2, 2026.
- Clear funds must be available in the ICCL account by 6:00 PM on June 4, 2026, to be eligible for allotment.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This notice operationalizes the launch of Specialized Investment Funds (SIFs), a newer fund category, on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clients initiate fund payments by 9:00 AM on T+1 day; transactions without timely fund initiation will be cancelled and CC with 'N' flag reported to RTA/AMC.
- Transactions with initiated payments remain pending until 3:00 PM on the last day of funds pay-out to AMC or T+5, whichever is earlier.
- Cheque and OTM payments are accepted during the NFO period only if clear funds reach ICCL by 6:00 PM on June 4, 2026.
- Switch transactions are accepted during the NFO period subject to receipt of funds prior to the allotment date; switch-out funds received on or after the allotment date will not be considered.

## Important Dates

- **May 19, 2026**: NFO subscription opens
- **June 2, 2026 (3:00 PM)**: Deadline for SIP/XSIP client authentication
- **June 2, 2026 (midnight)**: NFO closure for lumpsum purchases on BSE StAR MF Platform
- **June 4, 2026 (6:00 PM)**: Deadline for clear funds to be available in ICCL account
- **June 5, 2026**: Tentative allotment date for all four schemes

## Impact Assessment

This circular primarily impacts mutual fund distributors, brokers, and members operating on the BSE StAR MF Platform who facilitate client investments. Investors interested in long-short equity strategies through ICICI Mutual Fund have a new product avenue. The SIF category represents a relatively new fund structure, and members need to carefully follow the payment and transaction cut-off instructions to avoid cancellations. There is no broader market or regulatory impact beyond operational awareness for BSE StAR MF participants.
