## Summary

BSE has notified Trading Members and Custodians that Patel Integrated Logistics Limited is conducting a buyback of equity shares through the Stock Exchange Acquisition Window (Tender Offer) mechanism, pursuant to SEBI's revised guidelines. The buyback offer window opens Monday, July 6, 2026, and closes Friday, July 10, 2026, with BSE serving as the Designated Stock Exchange.

## Key Points

- Company: Patel Integrated Logistics Limited
- Buyback size: Up to 54,00,000 (54 lakh) fully paid-up equity shares of face value Rs. 10 each
- Buyback price: Rs. 20 per equity share, payable in cash
- Aggregate buyback amount: Not exceeding Rs. 10,80,00,000 (Rs. 10.80 crore), excluding transaction costs
- Buyback route: Proportionate basis through tender offer using the stock exchange mechanism
- Buyback size represents 8.434% (standalone) and 8.435% (consolidated) of total paid-up capital and free reserves as per FY2025-26 audited financial statements, within the statutory 10% limit
- Eligible shareholders: Equity Shareholders/Beneficial Owners as per depository records on the Record Date
- Letter of Offer attached for market participants' reference

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; the offer follows existing SEBI circulars CIR/CFD/POLICYCELL/1/2015 (April 13, 2015) and CFD/DCR2/CIR/P/2016/131 (December 9, 2016), along with BSE's revised guideline notices (20170210-16, 20190424-35, 20200528-32, 20201102-43, 20210825-62) on the mechanism for acquisition of shares through Stock Exchange pursuant to Tender-Offers under Takeovers, Buyback and Delisting.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members and Custodians must process the buyback in accordance with the Revised Guidelines under SEBI circular CFD/DCR2/CIR/P/2016/131 and related BSE notices
- Market participants should refer to the Letter of Offer and BSE website (www.bseindia.com) for full offer details

## Important Dates

- Record Date: June 30, 2026
- Offer Opens: Monday, July 6, 2026
- Offer Closes: Friday, July 10, 2026
- Notice issued: July 3, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The buyback provides an exit opportunity at a fixed price of Rs. 20 per share for eligible shareholders of Patel Integrated Logistics Limited during the specified tender window. Impact is limited to the company's shareholders and does not represent a broader market or systemic event; trading members should ensure operational readiness to process tender offer transactions through the stock exchange mechanism during the offer period.
