## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260623-27 dated June 23, 2026, announcing a change in the group classification of equity shares of Schneider Electric President Systems Ltd. This notice is issued by the DCS-Listing department under the Equity segment.

## Key Points

- Notice No.: 20260623-27
- Effective Date: June 23, 2026
- The group classification of equity shares of Schneider Electric President Systems Ltd has been changed on BSE
- Issued by the DCS-Listing department under the Equity segment
- No attachment provided with this notice

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares of Schneider Electric President Systems Ltd have been reclassified into a different group on BSE. Group changes on BSE affect how securities are categorized for trading, surveillance, and settlement purposes.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members and market participants should take note of the revised group classification for Schneider Electric President Systems Ltd and update their records accordingly. No specific action is mandated beyond awareness of the updated classification.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: June 23, 2026
- Effective Date: As per BSE Notice No. 20260623-27

## Impact Assessment

The change in group classification is an administrative action by BSE. Depending on the new group assigned, this may affect the stock's eligibility for certain trading mechanisms, margin requirements, or surveillance categories. Investors and brokers should check the updated group designation to understand any applicable trading or settlement implications.
