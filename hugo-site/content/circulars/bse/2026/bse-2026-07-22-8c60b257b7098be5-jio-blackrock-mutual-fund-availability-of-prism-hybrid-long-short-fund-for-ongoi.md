## Summary

BSE has notified members that, pursuant to its earlier Notice No. 20260625-35 dated June 25, 2026, the PRISM HYBRID LONG - SHORT FUND scheme of JIO BlackRock Mutual Fund has been reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions, along with SIP and SWP facilities, on the BSE StAR MF Platform with effect from July 23, 2026.

## Key Points

- The reopening applies to two scheme variants: PRISM HYBRID LONG - SHORT FUND - Regular Plan - Growth (ISIN INF22M030019) and PRISM HYBRID LONG - SHORT FUND - Direct Plan - Growth (ISIN INF22M030027), both classified under the Equity scheme category.
- Both schemes will be available for ongoing transactions including subscriptions, redemptions, SIP and SWP on the BSE StAR MF Platform starting July 23, 2026.
- As communicated by JIO BlackRock Mutual Fund, redemption and switch-out transactions under this SIF (Specialized Investment Fund) scheme will be accepted as per the transaction frequency allowed at the AMC/RTA end.
- If Monday or Wednesday is a non-business day, the AMC will process redemptions and switch-outs on the next working/business day.
- Redemption requests received after Wednesday 3:00 p.m. up to Monday 3:00 p.m. will be processed based on Monday's NAV; requests received after Monday 3:00 p.m. up to Wednesday 3:00 p.m. will be processed based on Wednesday's NAV.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is an operational update implementing terms of the earlier Notice No. 20260625-35 dated June 25, 2026.

## Compliance Requirements

BSE StAR MF Platform members and participating distributors should update their systems to enable ongoing transactions for the specified ISINs and ensure investor communications reflect the twice-weekly (Monday/Wednesday) NAV-based redemption cycle applicable to this scheme.

## Important Dates

- June 25, 2026: Original Notice No. 20260625-35 issued.
- July 23, 2026: PRISM HYBRID LONG - SHORT FUND reopens for ongoing subscriptions, redemptions, SIP and SWP transactions.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice affecting mutual fund distributors and investors transacting in the PRISM HYBRID LONG - SHORT FUND via BSE StAR MF. It does not impact listed equity securities or broader market trading activity, and carries low market-wide significance, being relevant mainly to participants in this specific mutual fund scheme.
