## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Adani Airport Holdings Limited has fixed a Record Date of 26 May 2026 for the part redemption of its Non-Convertible Debentures. Effective from the Ex Date (26 May 2026), trading in these debentures will occur at a reduced face value of Rs. 63,843 per debenture.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 976065
- Scrip ID: AAHL-9.35%-8-9-28-PVT
- ISIN: INE0GCN07047
- Record Date: 26 May 2026
- Ex Date: 26 May 2026
- Settlement Number: 638/2026-2027
- Reduced Face Value: Rs. 63,843 per debenture

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework introduced. This is a standard corporate action under BSE Listing Operations for debt segment instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to note that from 26 May 2026, all trades in the debentures of Adani Airport Holdings Limited (ISIN: INE0GCN07047) must reflect the reduced face value of Rs. 63,843 per debenture.

## Important Dates

- **Record Date:** 26 May 2026
- **Ex Date:** 26 May 2026 (trading with reduced face value begins)
- **Circular Date:** 25 May 2026

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited to holders and traders of Adani Airport Holdings Limited's privately placed 9.35% NCDs maturing 8 September 2028. The part redemption reduces the outstanding face value per debenture, which affects accrued interest calculations and market pricing. Members must update their systems to reflect the new face value of Rs. 63,843 per debenture from 26 May 2026.
