## Summary

BSE has listed 7,024,420 equity shares of DEFRAIL TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED following its Initial Public Offering (IPO). All shares are fully paid and held in demat form, with various lock-in periods ranging from 3 months to 3 years depending on the category of securities.

## Key Points

- Total securities listed: 7,024,420 equity shares
- All securities are fully paid and in demat form
- Securities divided into 6 categories with different lock-in periods
- Lock-in periods range from 3 months (anchor investors) to 3 years (promoter shares)
- 1,342,400 shares are freely tradable without lock-in
- Distinctive numbers allocated from 1 to 7,024,420

## Securities Breakdown

### Category 1: 1,440,000 shares
- Distinctive Numbers: 1 to 1,440,000
- Lock-in: January 7, 2026 to January 16, 2029 (3 years)
- Status: Fully Paid, Demat

### Category 2: 1,856,000 shares
- Distinctive Numbers: 1,440,001 to 3,296,000
- Lock-in: January 7, 2026 to January 16, 2028 (2 years)
- Status: Fully Paid, Demat

### Category 3: 1,867,620 shares
- Distinctive Numbers: 3,296,001 to 5,163,620
- Lock-in: January 7, 2026 to January 16, 2027 (1 year)
- Status: Fully Paid, Demat

### Category 4: 260,800 shares (Anchor)
- Distinctive Numbers: 5,163,621 to 5,424,420
- Lock-in: January 14, 2026 to April 16, 2026 (3 months)
- Status: Fully Paid, Demat

### Category 5: 257,600 shares (Anchor)
- Distinctive Numbers: 5,424,421 to 5,682,020
- Lock-in: January 14, 2026 to February 15, 2026 (1 month)
- Status: Fully Paid, Demat

### Category 6: 1,342,400 shares
- Distinctive Numbers: 5,682,021 to 7,024,420
- Lock-in: None (FREE)
- Status: Fully Paid, Demat

## Important Dates

- **Lock-in Start (Categories 1-3)**: January 7, 2026
- **Lock-in Start (Categories 4-5)**: January 14, 2026
- **Anchor Lock-in Expiry (Category 5)**: February 15, 2026
- **Anchor Lock-in Expiry (Category 4)**: April 16, 2026
- **One-year Lock-in Expiry**: January 16, 2027
- **Two-year Lock-in Expiry**: January 16, 2028
- **Three-year Lock-in Expiry**: January 16, 2029

## Impact Assessment

### Market Liquidity
- Approximately 19% (1,342,400 shares) are immediately available for trading
- Anchor investor shares (518,400 shares or 7.4%) will unlock within 1-3 months
- Remaining shares unlock gradually over 1-3 years, preventing supply shock

### Trading Implications
- Limited initial float may lead to higher volatility
- Staged unlock schedule provides predictable supply increases
- Large promoter lock-in (3.3 million shares for 1-3 years) indicates long-term commitment
