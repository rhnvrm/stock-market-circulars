## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Equity segment on Saturday, May 16, 2026. Trading members, including those using third-party trading platforms, are encouraged to use this session to test their trading applications across various functionalities such as call auction sessions, risk-reduction mode, trading halt, and block deals. There will be no new BOLT TWS Release in conjunction with this session.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session for the Equity segment on May 16, 2026 (Saturday)
- Open to trading members using third-party and in-house trading platforms
- No new BOLT TWS Release associated with this session
- Covers testing of exceptional market conditions: call auctions, risk-reduction mode, trading halt, block deals
- Members are requested to submit feedback by 3:00 pm on the day of the session

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational/testing notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- No mandatory compliance obligations; participation is voluntary
- Members are encouraged to participate and provide feedback to BSE by 3:00 pm on May 16, 2026
- Feedback to be submitted via contact numbers or email provided by BSE

## Important Dates

- **May 16, 2026 (Saturday):** Mock trading session for Equity segment
- **3:00 pm, May 16, 2026:** Deadline for submitting member feedback
