## Summary

BSE's Surveillance department has revised price bands for 40 equity scrips effective March 9, 2026. The revised bands range from 1% to 20%, and the changes apply across a diverse set of small and mid-cap companies listed on the exchange.

## Key Points

- Price bands revised for 40 scrips across the equity segment
- Effective date: March 9, 2026
- Revised bands: 1% (1 scrip), 2% (2 scrips), 5% (18 scrips), 10% (10 scrips), 20% (9 scrips)
- Action initiated by BSE Surveillance department
- Members may contact bse.surv@bseindia.com for clarifications

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the revised price bands for all 40 affected scrips
- Orders placed outside the new price band limits will be rejected by the exchange system
- Members should update their risk management and order management systems accordingly before March 9, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** March 6, 2026
- **Effective Date:** March 9, 2026 (Monday)

## Impact Assessment

Trading in all 40 affected scrips will be subject to the new circuit limits from March 9, 2026. Scrips moved to tighter bands (e.g., 1% or 2%) such as Seshachal Technologies Ltd (1%), KSR Footwear Ltd (2%), and PFL Infotech Ltd (2%) will face significantly restricted intraday price movement, indicating heightened surveillance concern. Scrips moved to wider bands (20%) such as Coastal Corporation Ltd, Jet Freight Logistics Ltd, and TVS Electronics Ltd will have more trading flexibility. These revisions are typically a surveillance tool to manage unusual price movements and protect investors.
