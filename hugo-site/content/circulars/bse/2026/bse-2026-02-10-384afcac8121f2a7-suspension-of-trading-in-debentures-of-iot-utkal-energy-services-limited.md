## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of IOT UTKAL ENERGY SERVICES LIMITED effective February 13, 2026. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Company: IOT UTKAL ENERGY SERVICES LIMITED
- Debenture Code: 1008IOT26B
- ISIN: INE310L07878 (950211)
- Record Date: February 13, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- No Dealings From: February 13, 2026
- Circular Reference: DR-820/2025-2026
- Notice Number: 20260210-17

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures of IOT UTKAL ENERGY SERVICES LIMITED from February 13, 2026
- Trading members are required to note this suspension and comply with the no-dealings directive
- All trading activity in debenture code 1008IOT26B (ISIN: INE310L07878) must cease from the specified date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: February 10, 2026
- **Record Date**: February 13, 2026
- **Trading Suspension Date**: February 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders and traders of IOT UTKAL ENERGY SERVICES LIMITED's Non-Convertible Debentures. The suspension is temporary and related to standard corporate action procedures for redemption and interest payment.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members dealing in debt securities need to update their systems to prevent trading in the specified debenture. Debenture holders with positions as of the record date will be eligible for interest payment and redemption proceeds.

**Investor Impact**: Affects specific debenture holders who will receive their redemption amount and final interest payment based on holdings as of February 13, 2026.
