## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 1,00,000 new equity shares of Manoj Ceramic Limited (Scrip Code: 544073, ISIN: INE0A6N01026) are listed and permitted to trade effective Wednesday, July 15, 2026. These shares were issued to promoters on a preferential basis at Rs. 161/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- plus premium of Rs. 151/-) pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- New securities: 1,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 151/- per share (total issue price Rs. 161/-)
- Issued to Promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Distinctive numbers: 13707001 to 13807000
- Date of allotment: 22/04/2026
- ISIN: INE0A6N01026
- Scrip Code: 544073

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing notification under BSE Listing Operations.

## Compliance Requirements

No action required from trading members beyond updating trading systems to reflect the new listed quantity; the shares are subject to a lock-in as detailed below.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: 22/04/2026
- Listing and trading effective date: Wednesday, July 15, 2026
- Lock-in period upto: 31/01/2028 (applicable to all 1,00,000 shares, Dist. Nos. 13707001 to 13807000)

## Impact Assessment

The listing marginally increases Manoj Ceramic Limited's total outstanding equity share capital. Since the shares are allotted to promoters and locked in until January 31, 2028, there is no immediate free-float dilution or trading impact. The event is administrative in nature with negligible market-wide significance.
