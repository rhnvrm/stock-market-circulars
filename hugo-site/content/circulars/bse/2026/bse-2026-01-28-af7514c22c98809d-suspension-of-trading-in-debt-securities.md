## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in debt securities of AVIOM India Housing Finance Private Limited effective January 29, 2026, as the securities are scheduled to mature on January 31, 2026. Trading members are advised not to deal in these securities from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Company: AVIOM India Housing Finance Private Limited
- ISIN: INE0E2307229
- Scrip Code: 975877
- Security: AIHFPL-14%-31-1-26-PVT (14% coupon)
- Suspension effective date: January 29, 2026
- Maturity date: January 31, 2026
- Notice number: DR-808/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action procedure for maturing debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debt securities from January 29, 2026
- Trading members should update their systems to prevent inadvertent trading in the suspended securities
- All pending orders for these securities should be cancelled

## Important Dates

- **January 28, 2026**: Notice issued
- **January 29, 2026**: Trading suspension effective date
- **January 31, 2026**: Debt securities maturity date

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders of this specific private debt security. This is a routine suspension preceding maturity, affecting only investors and trading members dealing with AVIOM India Housing Finance Private Limited's 14% debt instrument. No broader market implications expected as this is a single private company security reaching natural maturity.
