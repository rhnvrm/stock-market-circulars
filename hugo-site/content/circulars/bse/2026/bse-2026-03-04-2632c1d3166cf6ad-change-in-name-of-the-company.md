## Summary

BSE has issued a circular notifying the change in name of a company. The circular is supported by a certificate from the Ministry of Corporate Affairs, issued by M. Nagaraju of the Office of the Registrar of Companies, digitally signed on February 10, 2026.

## Key Points

- A listed company has undergone an official change in its corporate name.
- The name change has been certified by the Registrar of Companies under the Ministry of Corporate Affairs.
- The certificate was digitally signed by M. Nagaraju on February 10, 2026 (18:59:33 IST).
- BSE is notifying market participants of this change for awareness and record-keeping purposes.

## Regulatory Changes

The company's registered name has been formally changed as per the Companies Act, with the Registrar of Companies issuing the requisite certificate of incorporation on change of name.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants, brokers, and depository participants should update their records to reflect the new company name.
- The company's scrip name and related identifiers on BSE may be updated accordingly.

## Important Dates

- **Certificate Date:** February 10, 2026 — Registrar of Companies certificate issued.
- **Circular Date:** March 4, 2026 — BSE notification to market participants.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative action with minimal market impact. Investors holding shares in the company are not affected in terms of shareholding or value. The change is purely nominal and operational. Participants should note any scrip code or name updates on the exchange to avoid confusion in trading or reporting.
