## Summary

BSE has revised the trading date for Shankara Buildpro Limited (Scrip Code: 544517) equity shares under 'B' group classification. The revised trading start date is now Tuesday, January 27, 2026, instead of the previously announced date, due to a trading holiday declared for January 15, 2026 on account of Municipal Corporation Elections.

## Key Points

- This is a continuation of earlier notices (20260112-8 dated January 12, 2026, and 20260109-9 dated January 09, 2026)
- Trading in Shankara Buildpro Limited equity shares will be permitted under 'B' group
- Revised effective date: Tuesday, January 27, 2026
- Reason for revision: Trading holiday on January 15, 2026 for Municipal Corporation Elections
- Scrip Code: 544517

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares of Shankara Buildpro Limited are undergoing a group change and will be classified under 'B' group for trading purposes.

## Compliance Requirements

- All market participants must note the revised trading date of January 27, 2026
- Trading in these equity shares under the new 'B' group classification should only commence from the revised date
- Market participants should refer to previous notices (20260112-8 and 20260109-9) for complete context

## Important Dates

- **January 15, 2026**: Trading holiday (Municipal Corporation Elections)
- **January 27, 2026**: Revised date for trading commencement of Shankara Buildpro Limited equity shares under 'B' group
- **Original notice dates**: January 09, 2026 and January 12, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to market participants and investors interested in trading Shankara Buildpro Limited equity shares. The postponement ensures proper implementation of the group change while accommodating the municipal election trading holiday. This is a procedural update with minimal broader market impact, affecting only transactions related to this specific scrip.
