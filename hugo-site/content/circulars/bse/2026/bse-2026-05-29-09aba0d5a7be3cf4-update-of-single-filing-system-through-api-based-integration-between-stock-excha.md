## Summary

BSE has extended the single filing system through API-based integration between stock exchanges to cover additional XBRL disclosure categories effective May 29, 2026. This builds on the earlier framework established via Exchange Notice No. 20240930-60 dated September 30, 2024, and now includes disclosures related to analyst/institutional investor meets, regulatory orders, and related filings.

## Key Points

- The single filing system via API-based integration now covers additional XBRL filing categories effective May 29, 2026.
- Two new categories added effective May 29, 2026: Schedule of analysts/institutional investor meets (including presentations, transcripts, audio/video recordings) and Actions/orders by regulatory, statutory, enforcement, or judicial bodies (sub-paras 19 and 20 of Para A, Part A, Schedule III of SEBI LODR).
- Applicable to both equity-only and equity+debt listed companies; not applicable to exclusively debt, REITs, or InvITs for these categories.
- Several categories were already enabled at earlier dates (April 25, 2026 and May 5, 2026) including Outcome of Board Meeting for dividends/buyback/bonus/delisting, Initiation of Forensic Audit, and Prohibition of Insider Trading.
- Prohibition of Insider Trading (Regulation 7(2) of SEBI PIT) is also applicable to REITs/InvITs.

## Regulatory Changes

- Regulation 30 of SEBI LODR read with sub-para 15 of Para A, Part A, Schedule III: Analyst/institutional investor meets — effective May 29, 2026.
- Regulation 30 of SEBI LODR read with sub-para 19 of Para A, Part A, Schedule III: Actions initiated or orders passed by regulatory/statutory/enforcement/judicial bodies — effective May 29, 2026.
- Regulation 30 of SEBI LODR read with sub-para 20 of Para A, Part A, Schedule III: Actions taken or orders passed by regulatory/statutory/enforcement/judicial bodies — effective May 29, 2026.
- Regulation 30 of SEBI LODR read with sub-para 17: Initiation of Forensic Audit — effective May 5, 2026.
- Regulation 7(2) of SEBI PIT: Prohibition of Insider Trading — effective May 5, 2026.
- Regulation 30 of SEBI LODR read with sub-para 4 of Para A: Board Meeting outcomes (dividend, buyback, bonus, voluntary delisting) — effective April 25, 2026.

## Compliance Requirements

- Compliance Officers of all listed entities must use the single filing system via API-based integration for the newly covered disclosure categories from the respective effective dates.
- Filings for the newly added categories (analyst meets, regulatory/judicial orders) must be submitted through the integrated system starting May 29, 2026.
- Entities must ensure their filing workflows are updated to route the applicable XBRL disclosures through the API-based single filing system.

## Important Dates

- **April 25, 2026**: Board Meeting outcome disclosures (dividend, buyback, bonus, voluntary delisting) enabled on single filing system.
- **May 5, 2026**: Initiation of Forensic Audit and Prohibition of Insider Trading disclosures enabled.
- **May 29, 2026**: Schedule of analyst/institutional investor meets; Actions/orders initiated or passed by regulatory/statutory/enforcement/judicial bodies (sub-paras 19 and 20) enabled.

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a moderate operational impact on all listed companies. By expanding the single filing system, listed entities only need to file certain XBRL disclosures once (at one exchange), with the API integration propagating the filing to other exchanges. This reduces compliance burden and duplication for equity-listed and equity+debt-listed companies. No financial or trading-related impact is expected; the change is purely procedural and applies to the mechanics of regulatory disclosure submission.
