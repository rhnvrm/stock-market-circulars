## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (No. 20260616-29) informing trading members and custodians about the opening of an Offer to Buy under the Acquisition Window (Takeover) mechanism for Senthil Infotek Limited. The open offer is made by Kolli Murali Krishna (Acquirer 1) and Gogineni Srinivas (Acquirer 2) to public shareholders of the Target Company at Rs. 8.00 per equity share, pursuant to SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 2011.

## Key Points

- Open offer made by two acquirers — Kolli Murali Krishna and Gogineni Srinivas — to public shareholders of Senthil Infotek Limited
- Offer price: Rs. 8.00 (Rupees Eight Only) per equity share, payable in cash
- Shares targeted: Up to 13,13,000 (Thirteen Lakhs Thirteen Thousand) fully paid-up equity shares of face value Rs. 10/- each
- This represents 26.00% of the voting share capital of the Target Company
- Offer is made under Regulation 3(1) and 4 of SEBI (SAST) Regulations, 2011
- Offer governed by revised SEBI circular CFD/DCR2/CIR/P/2016/131 dated December 9, 2016 and BSE notice no. 20170210-16 dated February 10, 2017
- Letter of Offer is attached to the BSE notice

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The offer is conducted pursuant to the existing SEBI mechanism for acquisition of shares through Stock Exchange under Tender-Offers (Takeovers, Buy Back, and De-listing), originally established via SEBI circular CIR/CFD/POLICYCELL/1/2015 dated April 13, 2015, and subsequently revised.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and custodians must note the opening of the Offer to Buy under the Acquisition Window
- Market participants must process buy/sell orders in accordance with the revised guidelines under SEBI circular CFD/DCR2/CIR/P/2016/131 and BSE notice 20170210-16
- Full details of the offer are available on the BSE website
- Letter of Offer should be reviewed for complete terms and conditions before participation

## Important Dates

- **Offer Opening Date:** Thursday, June 18, 2026
- **Offer Closing Date:** Thursday, July 2, 2026
- **Excluded Date:** Friday, June 26, 2026 (SEBI Holiday — not counted within offer period)
- **Notice Date:** June 16, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This open offer has a direct and significant impact on public shareholders of Senthil Infotek Limited, who are being offered an exit opportunity at Rs. 8.00 per share. The offer covers 26% of voting share capital, which is a substantial stake. Shareholders must evaluate the offer price relative to market price and decide whether to tender their shares within the offer window. Trading members and custodians need to facilitate client orders through the BSE Acquisition Window mechanism during the offer period. The offer excludes June 26, 2026 (SEBI Holiday), so participants must plan tendering activity accordingly.
