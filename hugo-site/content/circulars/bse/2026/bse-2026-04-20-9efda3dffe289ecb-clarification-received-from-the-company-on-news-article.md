## Summary

BSE's Surveillance department (DOSS) issued Notice No. 20260420-24 dated April 20, 2026, confirming receipt of a clarification from Indian Energy Exchange Ltd (Scrip Code: 540750) in response to news articles circulating in media. The exchange proactively sought this clarification to verify accuracy of news and keep members and investors informed.

## Key Points

- BSE Surveillance sought clarification from Indian Energy Exchange Ltd (Scrip Code: 540750) regarding news articles on MoneyControl
- The company's reply has been received and is attached to the notice
- This is a routine surveillance action to verify media reports and protect investor interests
- Notice issued under the authority of Deputy Vice President Sanjay Jain and Manager Shreya Ghose of the Surveillance department

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this notice.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements for market participants. This notice is informational, conveying that the company has responded to BSE's query. Investors and members may refer to the attached reply for the company's clarification.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: April 20, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. This is a standard BSE Surveillance disclosure mechanism to maintain market transparency. The clarification from Indian Energy Exchange Ltd is available as an attachment for investor reference. No trading restrictions or penalties are indicated.
