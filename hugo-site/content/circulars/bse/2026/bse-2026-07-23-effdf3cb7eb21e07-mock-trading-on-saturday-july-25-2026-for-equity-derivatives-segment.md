## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session in the Equity Derivatives segment on Saturday, July 25, 2026, comprising two separate sessions. Session 1 will run from the Disaster Recovery (DR) site to test changes related to the newly introduced Closing Auction Session (CAS). Session 2 will run from the Primary (PR) site to test general trading functionality without CAS changes, ahead of the resumption of live trading on Monday, July 27, 2026.

## Key Points

- Two mock trading sessions scheduled for July 25, 2026 in the Equity Derivatives segment.
- Session 1 (DR site): Log-in 07:30–07:45 am; Order Entry 08:00–08:08 am; Order Matching & Buffer 08:08–08:15 am; Continuous Trading 08:15–10:45 am. Stock future price bands to be aligned with CAS in the Equity Segment starting 09:00 am (applied sequentially).
- Session 2 (PR site): Log-in 12:30–01:00 pm; Order Entry 01:00–01:08 pm; Order Matching & Buffer 01:08–01:15 pm; Continuous Trading 01:15–02:00 pm.
- Trades executed in Session 1 will not be available/carried over into Session 2.
- Members must use BOLT Pro TWS version 13.25 (currently live in production) during the mock sessions.
- Connectivity details for the DR site are provided in an annexure.
- Third-party trading application users should use this opportunity to test CAS functionality.
- Mock trades do not attract any margin, pay-in, or pay-out obligations and create no rights or liabilities.
- Members are requested to submit feedback on the mock trading session by 2:00 pm on the day of the session.

## Regulatory Changes

This notice operationalizes the Closing Auction Session (CAS) and related Standard Operating Procedures previously introduced via circulars no. 20260319-21 and 20260610-41, by scheduling a dedicated mock test from the DR site.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members, particularly those using third-party trading applications, must participate in the mock sessions to validate CAS-related functionality before live rollout.
- Members must ensure their systems are compatible with BOLT Pro TWS version 13.25.
- Members should refer to the enclosed annexure for DR site connectivity setup.
- Feedback on the mock trading session must be submitted by 2:00 pm on July 25, 2026 via the provided contact numbers/email.

## Important Dates

- July 25, 2026 (Saturday): Mock trading sessions 1 (DR site, 07:30 am–10:45 am) and 2 (PR site, 12:30 pm–02:00 pm).
- July 27, 2026 (Monday): Resumption of live trading incorporating CAS changes tested during Session 1.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine but important operational readiness exercise ahead of the go-live of Closing Auction Session functionality in the Equity Derivatives segment. Trading members, particularly those with custom or third-party trading systems, should treat participation as necessary to avoid disruptions when CAS goes live on July 27, 2026. No direct market or stock-specific impact is expected, as mock trades carry no financial obligations.
