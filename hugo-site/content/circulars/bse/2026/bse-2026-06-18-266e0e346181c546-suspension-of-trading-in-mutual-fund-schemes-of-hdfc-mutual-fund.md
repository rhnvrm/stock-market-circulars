## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that HDFC Mutual Fund has fixed 24 June 2026 as the Record Date for redemption of 12 Fixed Maturity Plan (FMP) schemes across two series (1406D August 2022 Series 46 and 1359D September 2022 Series 46). Trading members are directed not to deal in these mutual fund plans from the ex-date of 24 June 2026 onwards under Settlement No. 658/2026.

## Key Points

- 12 HDFC Mutual Fund FMP schemes are suspended from trading effective 24 June 2026
- Schemes span two series: HDFC FMP 1406D August 2022 - Series 46 (scrip codes 543584–543589) and HDFC FMP 1359D September 2022 - Series 46 (scrip codes 543629–543634)
- Each series has six plan variants: Direct/Regular options each with Growth, Normal Dividend, and Quarterly Dividend sub-options
- Record Date and Ex-Date are both set to 24 June 2026
- Settlement detail applicable: 658/2026, with DR year 2027
- Notice issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President, Listing Compliance and Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework changes. This is a standard operational notice pursuant to the fund house's notification of a redemption record date, requiring BSE to suspend secondary market trading in the affected schemes.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any buy or sell transactions in the 12 listed HDFC Mutual Fund schemes on or after the ex-date of 24 June 2026
- Members should ensure client orders in these scrip codes are blocked in their systems from the applicable date

## Important Dates

- **Ex-Date:** 24 June 2026
- **Record Date:** 24 June 2026
- **Settlement Number:** 658/2026
- **DR Year:** 2027

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited to investors and trading members dealing in these 12 specific HDFC FMP schemes on BSE. As these are fixed maturity plans nearing redemption, secondary market liquidity is typically low. The suspension is a routine administrative step ensuring orderly redemption processing and does not signal any adverse event. Investors holding these units will receive redemption proceeds as per the fund's terms after the record date.
