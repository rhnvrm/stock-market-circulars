## Summary

BSE Limited, through Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd. (ICCL), has announced Demat Auction 616 for Rolling Settlement No. DR-616/2026-27. Trading members of the Exchange are invited to submit offers for purchase of securities through auction terminals on April 24, 2026.

## Key Points

- Auction offer entry session: 2:00 PM to 2:45 PM on April 24, 2026
- Auction matching session: 2:45 PM to 3:00 PM on April 24, 2026
- Delivery statement and accepted auction offer reports: 3:00 PM to 3:30 PM
- Money statement and final reports: 3:35 PM to 5:30 PM
- Auction pay-in will be held on Monday, April 27, 2026 at 8:00 AM
- Members and custodians must check auction pay-in obligations and execute securities pay-in in depositories within depository cut-off timings

## Regulatory Changes

- Following implementation of Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment, settlement is now processed through the Clearing Member of a Trading Member
- The restriction on trading members offering securities in auction in settlements where they have short-delivered such securities has been removed

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must submit auction offers through trading terminals within the specified session timings
- Members and custodians are required to verify their auction pay-in obligations for securities
- Securities pay-in must be executed in depositories within depository cut-off timings
- Trading members must refer to the cutoff rate on the "Auction Offer Entry Screen" before submitting offers
- Trading members should refer to notices issued by respective departments regarding Corporate Actions (Ex-Rights, Ex-Bonus, Ex-Split, etc.) for relevant scrips
- No corrections permitted for punching errors; extreme care required when entering securities code and rate

## Important Dates

- **April 24, 2026**: Auction session day
  - 2:00 PM – 2:45 PM: Auction offer entry
  - 2:45 PM – 3:00 PM: Auction matching
  - 3:00 PM – 3:30 PM: Delivery statement and accepted offer reports
  - 3:35 PM – 5:30 PM: Money statement and final reports
- **April 27, 2026**: Auction pay-in at 8:00 AM

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice applicable to trading members and custodians participating in the Equity Cash Segment. The auction addresses short delivery situations in Rolling Settlement DR-616/2026-27. The removal of the restriction on trading members offering securities in settlements where they have short-delivered is a notable structural change under the Uniform Membership framework, potentially increasing liquidity in the auction process. Members must ensure timely securities delivery on April 27, 2026 to avoid further settlement complications.
