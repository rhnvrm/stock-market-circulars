## Summary

BSE/ICCL has issued Notice No. 20260623-3 dated 23 June 2026 informing trading members of corporate actions applicable to securities in the Securities Lending and Borrowing (SLB) segment. The specific securities and details of the corporate actions are provided in the attached annexure.

## Key Points

- Notice issued by ICCL Ltd (Indian Clearing Corporation Limited) on behalf of BSE
- Pertains to the SLB (Securities Lending and Borrowing) segment
- Trading members are directed to refer to the annexure for the full list of affected securities and applicable corporate action details
- Issued by Tushar Nimkar, Assistant Manager, ICCL Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is an operational notice communicating corporate action adjustments that affect open SLB positions in specific securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members participating in the SLB segment must review the attached annexure to identify securities in their SLB portfolios that are subject to corporate actions
- Members should take appropriate action to account for corporate action adjustments in their SLB positions as per existing ICCL/BSE guidelines

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 23 June 2026
- Specific ex-dates and record dates for the affected securities are listed in the annexure

## Impact Assessment

This notice has a medium operational impact on trading members active in the SLB segment. Corporate actions such as dividends, bonus issues, or stock splits can affect the terms of outstanding lending/borrowing positions and may require adjustments to collateral, quantities, or settlement values. Members with open SLB positions in the affected securities should review the annexure and liaise with ICCL for position adjustments.
