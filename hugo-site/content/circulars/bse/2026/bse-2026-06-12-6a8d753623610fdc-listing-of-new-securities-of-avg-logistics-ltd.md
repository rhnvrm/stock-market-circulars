## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 36,50,356 new equity shares of AVG Logistics Ltd (Scrip Code 543910) issued on a rights basis are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Monday, June 15, 2026. The shares were allotted on June 10, 2026, at an issue price of Rs. 145/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- plus premium of Rs. 135/-).

## Key Points

- 36,50,356 new equity shares of AVG Logistics Ltd listed on BSE
- Scrip Code: 543910 | ISIN: INE680Z01018
- Issue price: Rs. 145/- per share (Rs. 10/- face value + Rs. 135/- premium) on rights basis
- Distribution numbers: 15057721 to 18708076
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Trading effective from: Monday, June 15, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification following completion of a rights issue.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to update their systems to reflect the availability of the new shares for trading from June 15, 2026, under the existing ISIN INE680Z01018.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** June 10, 2026
- **Trading Commencement:** June 15, 2026 (Monday)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact; affects only AVG Logistics Ltd shareholders who participated in the rights issue. The addition of 36,50,356 shares to the tradeable float may cause minor dilution effects on the existing share price. The pari-passu ranking ensures no differential treatment between old and new shareholders.
