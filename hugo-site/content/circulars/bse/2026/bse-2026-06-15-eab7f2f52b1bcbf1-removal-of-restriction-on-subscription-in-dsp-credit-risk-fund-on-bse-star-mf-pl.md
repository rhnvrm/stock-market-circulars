## Summary

BSE Trading Operations has informed members that DSP Mutual Fund has removed the restriction on subscriptions in DSP Credit Risk Fund on the BSE StAR MF platform. This notice supersedes the earlier restriction referenced in Notice No. 20260101-4 dated January 1, 2026. Fresh/additional purchases, Systematic Investment Plans (SIP), and Systematic Transfer Plans (STP-In) are now permitted under this scheme.

## Key Points

- Restrictions on fresh/additional purchases in DSP Credit Risk Fund have been lifted.
- SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) subscriptions are now permitted.
- STP-In (Systematic Transfer Plan – Inward) is now permitted.
- The removal of restrictions is effective from June 15, 2026.
- A notice cum addendum issued by DSP Mutual Fund has been attached to this circular.

## Regulatory Changes

The subscription restriction that was in place since the notice dated January 1, 2026 (Notice No. 20260101-4) has been formally removed. DSP Mutual Fund communicated this change to BSE, which in turn notified its members through this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action is required from members beyond taking note of the updated subscription status. Members operating on the BSE StAR MF platform may now process fresh/additional purchases, SIP registrations, and STP-In transactions for DSP Credit Risk Fund as per normal procedures.

## Important Dates

- **June 15, 2026**: Effective date for removal of subscription restrictions on DSP Credit Risk Fund on BSE StAR MF platform.
- **January 1, 2026**: Date of earlier notice (No. 20260101-4) that originally communicated the resumption/restriction of subscriptions.

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited in scope. This is a routine operational update restoring normal subscription functionality for DSP Credit Risk Fund on the BSE StAR MF platform. Investors and distributors who were previously restricted from making fresh/additional purchases or setting up SIPs/STP-Ins can now do so. There is no broader market or regulatory impact.
