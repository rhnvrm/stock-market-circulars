## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of INDO SMC Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in the SME segment. The company has been assigned scrip code 544681, trading symbol INDOSMC, and ISIN INE0WKY01013. The exact listing date and detailed security specifications will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- INDO SMC Limited to be listed on BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544681
- Trading Symbol: INDOSMC
- ISIN: INE0WKY01013
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Security details will be provided in subsequent notice

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should note the company details for upcoming trading
- Members to await separate notice for listing date and security details before commencing trading

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: January 20, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

Minimal immediate market impact as this is a pre-listing announcement for a single SME company. Trading members are informed in advance to prepare their systems for the upcoming listing. Actual trading will commence only after the listing date is formally announced.
