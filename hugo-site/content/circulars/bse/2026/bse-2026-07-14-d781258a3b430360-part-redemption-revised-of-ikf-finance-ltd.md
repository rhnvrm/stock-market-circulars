## Summary

BSE issued a corrective notice regarding the part redemption of debentures of IKF Finance Ltd (scrip code: 976301, ISIN: INE859C07212). In continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20260610-39 dated June 10, 2026, the Exchange has informed trading members that the reduced face value is Rs. 50,000.02 per debenture, instead of Rs. 50,000/- as mentioned in the earlier notice.

## Key Points

- This notice revises the reduced face value figure from the original June 10, 2026 notice on part redemption of IKF Finance Ltd debentures.
- Scrip code: 976301; ISIN: INE859C07212.
- Corrected reduced face value: Rs. 50,000.02 per debenture (previously stated as Rs. 50,000/-).
- Notice issued under the Debt segment, Listing Operations department.
- No attachments accompanied this notice.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory change; this is a correction/clarification to a previously issued part redemption notice (20260610-39) to update the reduced face value figure.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are requested to take note of the revised reduced face value of Rs. 50,000.02 per debenture for IKF Finance Ltd (ISIN INE859C07212) and update their records/systems accordingly for any related trading, settlement, or reporting activity.

## Important Dates

- Original notice date: June 10, 2026 (Notice No. 20260610-39)
- Revised notice date: July 14, 2026 (Notice No. 20260714-7)

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and trading members dealing in IKF Finance Ltd's specific debt instrument (ISIN INE859C07212). The correction ensures accurate reflection of the post-redemption face value for accounting, settlement, and valuation purposes. No broader market or equity impact is expected.
