## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document (DRHP) of Horizon Reclaim (India) Limited, filed on March 06, 2026, on the SME Exchange website. The document contains all disclosures as required under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and is available for public comments for a minimum period of 21 days from the date of notice.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Horizon Reclaim (India) Limited on March 06, 2026
- Document complies with Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website for at least 21 days for public review
- Public comments to be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- DRHP hosted at BSE SME platform: https://www.bsesme.com/download/362363/SME_IPO%20InPrinciple/DRHP_HorizonReclaim_20260306223648.pdf

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural notice in accordance with existing ICDR Regulations requiring public disclosure of draft offer documents for SME IPOs.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants, investors, and the public may review the DRHP and submit comments within 21 days from March 09, 2026 (deadline: approximately March 30, 2026)
- Comments to be sent to: equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- Filing Date: March 06, 2026
- Notice Date: March 09, 2026
- Public Comment Deadline: 21 days from March 09, 2026 (on or before March 30, 2026)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has limited market-wide impact. It pertains solely to the upcoming SME IPO of Horizon Reclaim (India) Limited. Investors and stakeholders interested in this SME IPO are encouraged to review the DRHP and submit comments if any within the stipulated timeframe.
