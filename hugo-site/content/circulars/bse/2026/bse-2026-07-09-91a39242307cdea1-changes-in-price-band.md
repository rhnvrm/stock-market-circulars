## Summary

BSE has notified trading members of a revision in the daily price band applicable to 12 scrips, effective from July 10, 2026. The revised price bands range from 2% to 10% depending on the scrip.

## Key Points

- Price band revised to 5%: Aartech Solonics Ltd (542580), AJC Jewel Manufacturers Ltd (544425), Cargosol Logistics Ltd (543621), DB International Stock Brokers Ltd (530393), Hittco Tools Ltd (531661), Mayank Cattle Food Ltd (544106), National Standard (India) Ltd (504882)
- Price band revised to 2%: Amanta Healthcare Ltd (544502), Kartik Investments Trust Ltd (501151), Quest Flow Controls Ltd (543982), Trio Mercantile & Trading Ltd (534755)
- Price band revised to 10%: Unichem Laboratories Ltd (506690)
- Notice issued by BSE's Department of Surveillance (DOSS), Notice No. 20260709-44

## Regulatory Changes

BSE has adjusted the applicable daily price band percentage for the listed scrips under its surveillance framework. No attachment or additional supporting document was provided with this notice.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must apply the revised price bands to the specified scrips in their trading systems from the effective date. Queries can be directed to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

Revised price bands take effect from July 10, 2026.

## Impact Assessment

The revision alters the permissible intraday price movement for the 12 affected scrips, which can influence trading strategies and volatility management for market participants holding or trading these securities. The change is procedural and part of routine surveillance monitoring rather than indicative of a fundamental change in the underlying companies.
