## Summary

BSE has listed 3,10,000 new equity shares of Five-Star Business Finance Ltd. (Scrip Code: 543663, ISIN: INE128S01021) issued at Rs. 770/- per share (face value Rs. 1/- + premium Rs. 769/-) to Promoter and Non-Promoter shareholders on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. These shares are tradeable on the Exchange from Monday, May 18, 2026, and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- **Company:** Five-Star Business Finance Ltd. (Scrip Code: 543663)
- **Shares Listed:** 3,10,000 equity shares of Rs. 1/- face value each
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 770/- per share (Rs. 1 face value + Rs. 769 premium)
- **Allotment Type:** Preferential basis to Promoter and Non-Promoter via warrant conversion
- **Distribution Numbers:** 294566169 to 294876168
- **ISIN:** INE128S01021
- **Trading Effective From:** Monday, May 18, 2026
- **Ranking:** Pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a procedural listing notice under BSE's Listing Operations framework for newly allotted securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed that these securities are now permitted for trading from May 18, 2026.
- Lock-in restrictions must be observed for the specified tranches; trading in locked-in shares is not permitted until the respective lock-in expiry dates.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** March 23, 2026
- **Trading Commencement:** May 18, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (50,000 shares, Dist. 294826169–294876168):** November 24, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (2,60,000 shares, Dist. 294566169–294826168):** November 24, 2027

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 3,10,000 shares to the tradeable float of Five-Star Business Finance Ltd., though the majority (2,60,000 shares) remain under lock-in until November 2027 and 50,000 shares until November 2026. The immediate free-float increase is minimal, limiting near-term price pressure. Investors and traders in FIVESTAR should note the distribution number ranges and lock-in periods when assessing actual market supply. The preferential allotment at Rs. 770/- per share via warrant conversion signals prior capital-raising activity by the company.
