## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that dealings in Rights Entitlements (REs) for renunciation of Oasis Securities Limited (scrip code 751125, ISIN INE876A20015) will commence on Monday, June 29, 2026. The REs are tradeable in dematerialized form only and will be settled on a T+1 rolling settlement basis on a trade-for-trade basis.

## Key Points

- Rights Entitlements trading permitted on BSE from June 29, 2026 to July 6, 2026
- Scrip Code: 751125 | Scrip ID: OASISE-RE | ISIN: INE876A20015
- Market Lot: 1 | Group: R
- Settlement on T+1 Rolling Settlement basis, trade-for-trade
- REs tradeable in dematerialized form only
- Rights Issue open period: June 29, 2026 to July 10, 2026
- REs not subscribed or renounced by Issue Closing Date will lapse and be extinguished

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice operates under the framework established by BSE notices 20200305-21 (March 5, 2020) and 20200518-34 (May 18, 2020) governing Rights Entitlement trading.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must inform all clients about commencement of RE trading, specifying the ISIN (INE876A20015)
- Clients must be clearly informed that REs are not ordinary shares of Oasis Securities Limited
- Contract notes/bills for RE purchases must prominently include a disclaimer stating that purchase of REs only grants the right to participate in the ongoing Rights Issue or renounce the REs before issue close
- Trading members must ensure clients understand that unsubscribed and un-renounced REs will lapse after the Issue Closing Date

## Important Dates

- **June 29, 2026**: Commencement of RE trading on BSE; Rights Issue opens
- **July 6, 2026**: Last day of RE trading for renunciation
- **July 10, 2026**: Rights Issue closes; deadline to subscribe or renounce REs

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action notice with limited broad market impact. It affects existing shareholders of Oasis Securities Limited who may wish to renounce their rights entitlements rather than subscribe to the rights issue. Trading members facilitating transactions in Group R securities must ensure proper client disclosure and disclaimer compliance. The trade-for-trade settlement mechanism reduces settlement risk but limits netting benefits for members.
