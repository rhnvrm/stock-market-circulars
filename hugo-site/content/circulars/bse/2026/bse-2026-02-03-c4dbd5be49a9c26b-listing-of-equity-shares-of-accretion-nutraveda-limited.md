## Summary

BSE has listed 7,240,000 fully paid equity shares of Accretion Nutraveda Limited in demat form. The shares have varying lock-in periods, with distinctive number ranges from 1 to 72,40,000. The circular is signed by Managing Director Mayur Popatlal Sojitra.

## Key Points

- Total shares listed: 7,240,000 equity shares
- All securities are fully paid and in demat form
- Seven distinct tranches with different lock-in periods
- Lock-in periods range from free trading to 3 years
- Distinctive numbers allocated from 1 to 72,40,000

## Share Details

### Lock-in Structure

- **1,736,000 shares** (DN: 1 to 17,36,000): Lock-in until 02.02.2027
- **400,000 shares** (DN: 17,36,001 to 21,36,000): Lock-in until 02.02.2027
- **1,736,000 shares** (DN: 21,36,001 to 38,72,000): Free trading (no lock-in)
- **1,448,000 shares** (DN: 38,72,001 to 53,20,000): Lock-in until 02.02.2028
- **272,000 shares** (DN: 53,20,001 to 55,92,000): Lock-in until 02.02.2029
- **272,000 shares** (DN: 55,92,001 to 58,64,000): Lock-in until 03.03.2026
- **1,376,000 shares** (DN: 58,64,001 to 72,40,000): Lock-in until 02.05.2026

## Compliance Requirements

- All listed shares are in dematerialized form
- Lock-in restrictions apply to specific tranches as per the schedule above
- Shareholders must comply with applicable lock-in periods before trading

## Important Dates

- **03.03.2026**: Lock-in expiry for 272,000 shares
- **02.05.2026**: Lock-in expiry for 1,376,000 shares
- **02.02.2027**: Lock-in expiry for 2,136,000 shares
- **02.02.2028**: Lock-in expiry for 1,448,000 shares
- **02.02.2029**: Lock-in expiry for 272,000 shares

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard new listing with no immediate market impact. The staggered lock-in periods will result in gradual release of shares into the market over the next 3 years, with approximately 24% (1,736,000 shares) immediately available for free trading.
