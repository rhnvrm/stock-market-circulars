## Summary

BSE has revised the acquisition window (takeover) schedule for OSCAR GLOBAL LIMITED. The acquisition window, originally scheduled for Thursday, January 15, 2026, will be extended to Friday, January 16, 2026. This revision is necessitated by the exchange holiday on January 15, 2026, declared due to Municipal Corporation Elections in Maharashtra.

## Key Points

- Acquisition window for OSCAR GLOBAL LIMITED extended by one day
- Original date: January 15, 2026 (excluded due to exchange holiday)
- Revised date: January 16, 2026
- Reason for change: Exchange holiday for Maharashtra Municipal Corporation Elections
- This is a continuation of notice no. 20251230-32 dated December 30, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a schedule adjustment to accommodate an exchange holiday.

## Compliance Requirements

- All market participants must note the revised acquisition window date
- Participants involved in the takeover of OSCAR GLOBAL LIMITED must adjust their timelines accordingly
- Trading activities related to this acquisition window should be planned for January 16, 2026

## Important Dates

- **January 13, 2026**: Circular issued
- **January 15, 2026**: Original acquisition window date (now excluded - Exchange Holiday)
- **January 16, 2026**: Revised acquisition window date for OSCAR GLOBAL LIMITED takeover

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to stakeholders directly involved in the acquisition/takeover of OSCAR GLOBAL LIMITED. The one-day extension accommodates the exchange holiday and ensures the acquisition window process can be completed on a trading day. This administrative change maintains the integrity of the takeover process while complying with the exchange holiday schedule.
