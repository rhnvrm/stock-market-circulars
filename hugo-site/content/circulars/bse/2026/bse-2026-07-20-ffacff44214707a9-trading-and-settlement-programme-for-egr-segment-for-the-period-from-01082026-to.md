## Summary

BSE Clearing Limited (BSECL) has published the trading and settlement programme for the EGR (Electronic Gold Receipts) segment for the period from 01.08.2026 to 31.08.2026. The notice details settlement numbers, trading dates, pay-in/pay-out dates, and auction settlement schedules for the month.

## Key Points

- Covers settlement cycles DR-090/2026-2027 through DR-110/2026-2027 for trading dates between 03-08-2026 and 31-08-2026
- Each trading date has a corresponding T+1 pay-in/pay-out date
- Auction settlement numbers (RA series) are also specified, with auction offer submission and auction pay-in/pay-out dates
- Pay-in must be submitted to Depositories/banks by 3:00 p.m., with pay-out of funds and securities effected by 4:00 p.m.
- Auction pay-in occurs at 9:00 a.m., with instructions to be submitted to Depositories/banks by 8:50 a.m.
- Notice issued by Deputy Managers Omkar Hatkar and Mayurkumar Prajapati for BSE Clearing Limited

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine periodic publication of the settlement calendar, not a change to regulations or trading rules.

## Compliance Requirements

Clearing members and depository participants dealing in the EGR segment must adhere to the specified pay-in deadlines (3:00 p.m. for regular settlement, 8:50 a.m. for auction settlement) to avoid settlement shortfalls or penalties.

## Important Dates

- Settlement period: 01-08-2026 to 31-08-2026
- Trading dates range from 03-08-2026 to 31-08-2026, each followed by next-business-day pay-in/pay-out
- Auction offer submissions and auction pay-in/pay-out follow one to two business days after the corresponding regular settlement pay-out

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard operational/administrative circular providing the monthly settlement calendar for the EGR segment. It has no direct impact on listed stocks or broader market sentiment, but is necessary reference material for clearing members, custodians, and depository participants to ensure timely settlement compliance.
