## Summary

BSE has announced the addition of two newly listed companies to their respective indices. SHADOWFAX TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED (ticker: 544685) will be added to the BSE IPO index effective January 30, 2026, following its listing on January 28, 2026. DIGILOGIC SYSTEMS LIMITED (ticker: 544684) will be added to the BSE SME IPO index effective January 29, 2026, following its SME platform listing on January 28, 2026.

## Key Points

- SHADOWFAX TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED (544685) listed on BSE on January 28, 2026
- SHADOWFAX to be added to BSE IPO index effective at open of January 30, 2026
- DIGILOGIC SYSTEMS LIMITED (544684) listed on BSE SME platform on January 28, 2026
- DIGILOGIC to be added to BSE SME IPO index effective at open of January 29, 2026
- Actions included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- References Notice No: 20260127-50 for SHADOWFAX and Notice No: 20260127-49 for DIGILOGIC

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index maintenance activity following new listings.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements. Index tracking funds and market participants tracking these indices should note the additions for portfolio rebalancing purposes.

## Important Dates

- **January 28, 2026**: Both SHADOWFAX TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED and DIGILOGIC SYSTEMS LIMITED listed on BSE
- **January 29, 2026**: DIGILOGIC SYSTEMS LIMITED added to BSE SME IPO index (effective at open)
- **January 30, 2026**: SHADOWFAX TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED added to BSE IPO index (effective at open)

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This is a routine administrative action for index maintenance following new listings. Impact is primarily on index-tracking funds and passive investment products that replicate these indices. The additions are automatically included in the end-of-day corporate action file for seamless integration into market systems. For queries, market participants can contact bseindex@bseindia.com.
