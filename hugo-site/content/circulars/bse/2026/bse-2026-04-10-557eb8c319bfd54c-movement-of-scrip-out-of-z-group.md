## Summary

BSE has announced the movement of BF Utilities Ltd (Scrip Code: 532430) out of the 'Z' group and into the 'B' group, effective April 21, 2026. This reclassification is made pursuant to SEBI's Master Circular on penal action for non-compliance with listing regulations.

## Key Points

- BF Utilities Ltd (Scrip Code: 532430) will be transferred from 'Z' group to 'B' group effective April 21, 2026.
- The action is taken under SEBI Master Circular No. HO/49/14/14(7)2025-CFD-POD2/I/3762/2026 (originally issued July 11, 2023, last updated January 30, 2026), specifically Chapter VII (A) — Penal Action for Non-Compliance.
- The notice is addressed to all market participants.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. The reclassification is executed under the existing SEBI Master Circular framework governing penal action and group classification for listed securities on BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

- All market participants and brokers are required to take note of the group change for BF Utilities Ltd effective April 21, 2026.
- Trading systems and surveillance processes should reflect the updated group classification from that date.
- For queries or clarifications, market participants may contact BSE at bse.soplodr@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** April 10, 2026
- **Effective Date of Group Transfer:** April 21, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The transfer of BF Utilities Ltd from 'Z' group to 'B' group is a positive development. The 'Z' group on BSE typically designates companies that have failed to comply with listing requirements or have pending investor grievances. Reclassification to 'B' group signals that the company has resolved its compliance issues. This may improve market sentiment toward the stock, enhance its liquidity, and reduce trading restrictions associated with the 'Z' group designation. Impact is limited to shareholders and traders of BF Utilities Ltd (BSE: 532430).
