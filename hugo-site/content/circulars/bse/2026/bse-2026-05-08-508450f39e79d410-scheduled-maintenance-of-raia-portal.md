## Summary

BSE has notified Research Analysts and Investment Advisers that the RA/IA Portal will be unavailable due to scheduled maintenance starting Friday, May 8, 2026 at 5:30 PM through Sunday, May 10, 2026. Portal services are expected to resume on Monday, May 11, 2026.

## Key Points

- The RA/IA Portal will be offline for scheduled maintenance from **May 8, 2026 at 5:30 PM** to **May 10, 2026** (end of day Sunday)
- Services are expected to resume from **Monday, May 11, 2026**
- All Research Analysts are advised to plan their activities around the downtime
- Notice issued by the Membership Compliance Department

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational maintenance notice only.

## Compliance Requirements

- Research Analysts should complete any pending portal activities before Friday, May 8, 2026 at 5:30 PM
- Plan submissions, renewals, or other portal-dependent activities accordingly to avoid disruption

## Important Dates

- **May 8, 2026 (5:30 PM)**: Portal goes offline for maintenance
- **May 10, 2026**: End of maintenance window (Sunday)
- **May 11, 2026 (Monday)**: Portal services expected to resume

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The downtime is confined to a weekend period (Friday evening through Sunday), reducing disruption to business operations. Research Analysts will be unable to access the RA/IA Portal during this window and should plan any portal-dependent activities in advance. No trading systems or market operations are affected.
