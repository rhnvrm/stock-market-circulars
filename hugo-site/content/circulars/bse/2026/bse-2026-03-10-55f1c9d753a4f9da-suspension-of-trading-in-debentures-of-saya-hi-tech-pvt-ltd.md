## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members that SAYA HI-TECH PVT LTD has fixed a record date of 11 March 2026 for the redemption of its Non-Convertible Debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from 11 March 2026 onwards.

## Key Points

- Trading in SAYA HI-TECH PVT LTD's NCD (code: SHTPL-19%-30-9-28-PVT, ISIN: INE23VL07011, Scrip Code: 977106) is suspended effective 11 March 2026.
- Record date is set as 11 March 2026 for the purpose of redemption and interest payment.
- Notice reference: DR-837/2025-2026.
- Issued under BSE Notice No. 20260310-20 dated 10 March 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice under BSE's debt segment corporate actions framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in SAYA HI-TECH PVT LTD debentures (ISIN: INE23VL07011, Scrip Code: 977106) from 11 March 2026.
- Members are required to take note of and comply with the trading suspension immediately.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** 10 March 2026
- **Record Date:** 11 March 2026
- **No Dealings From:** 11 March 2026
- **Purpose:** Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest

## Impact Assessment

The suspension affects trading in one specific NCD series of SAYA HI-TECH PVT LTD on BSE's debt segment. Debenture holders as of the record date (11 March 2026) will receive redemption proceeds and interest payment. The impact is limited to holders and traders of this specific instrument; broader market impact is minimal.
