## Summary

BSE has activated the Call Money Module of its IBBS (Internet Based Book Building Software) platform for TIL LIMITED's First and Final Call on its partly paid-up equity shares. Trading Members, Self-Certified Syndicate Banks (SCSBs), and Intermediaries are directed to note the issue details and facilitate the call money collection during the specified period.

## Key Points

- TIL LIMITED has requested activation of BSE's Call Money Module under IBBS for a First and Final Call on partly paid equity shares
- Security symbol for the partly paid shares is **TILCM** with a market lot of 1
- Call money amount payable is **Rs. 41.25 per partly paid-up equity share**
- Total partly paid-up equity shares subject to the call: **1,20,91,760 shares**
- Bidding window: 10:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. during the issue period
- Registrar to the Issue: MUFG Intime India Private Limited (formerly Link Intime India Private Limited)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. The call money process is being conducted in accordance with SEBI circular dated December 8, 2020 and BSE Exchange Notice No. 20210104-37 dated January 4, 2021.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members** and **SCSBs** with 3-in-1 type accounts must submit call money notice details collected during the issue period via the IBBS Call Money Module
- Members must adhere to the bidding window of 10:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. on each day of the issue period
- For regulatory/business rule queries: contact Listing Sales team at listing.sales@bseindia.com or 022-2272 8846/5063/8179
- For bidding-related queries: contact IBBS Operations at ibbs.ops@bseindia.com or 022-2272 8376/8436/8226

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Issue Opening Date | Friday, 19 June 2026 |
| Issue Closing Date | Friday, 3 July 2026 |
| Issue Period | 15 days |

## Impact Assessment

This notice is operationally relevant to Trading Members and SCSBs facilitating the call money payment for TIL LIMITED's partly paid-up shareholders. Existing holders of TILCM shares must pay Rs. 41.25 per share during the 15-day window (June 19 – July 3, 2026) to convert their partly paid shares. The impact is limited to existing partly paid shareholders of TIL LIMITED and the intermediaries processing the call money; broader market impact is low.
