## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 24,37,650 new equity shares of LAST MILE ENTERPRISES LIMITED (Scrip Code: 526961) effective January 5, 2026. These shares were issued at a premium of Rs. 59/- (total issue price Rs. 60/-) to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. The shares are subject to lock-in until July 15, 2026.

## Key Points

- Total shares listed: 24,37,650 equity shares of Re. 1/- each
- Issue price: Rs. 60/- per share (face value Re. 1/- + premium Rs. 59/-)
- Issued to: Non-Promoters on preferential basis
- Basis: Conversion of warrants
- Scrip Code: 526961
- ISIN: INE404B01022
- Listing date: January 5, 2026 (Monday)
- Ranking: Pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must note the listing of new securities and updated share capital of LAST MILE ENTERPRISES LIMITED for trading operations from January 5, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **October 28, 2025**: First allotment of 14,76,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 353331351-354807350)
- **October 30, 2025**: Second allotment of 9,61,650 shares (Dist. Nos. 354807351-355769000)
- **January 5, 2026**: Listing and trading commencement date
- **July 15, 2026**: Lock-in expiry date for all 24,37,650 shares

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notice with minimal market impact. The preferential issue increases the paid-up capital of LAST MILE ENTERPRISES LIMITED by 24,37,650 shares. The lock-in period until July 15, 2026 prevents immediate sale of these shares. The allotment to non-promoters may marginally dilute promoter holding but the shares rank equally with existing equity. Trading members should update their records to reflect the new share capital and distinctive number ranges.
