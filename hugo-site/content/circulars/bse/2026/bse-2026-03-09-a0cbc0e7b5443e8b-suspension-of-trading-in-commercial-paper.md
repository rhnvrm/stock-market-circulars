## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260309-11 informs trading members that 11 companies have fixed record dates for redemption of their commercial papers. Trading members are advised not to deal in these commercial papers from the respective no-dealings dates of March 10–11, 2026.

## Key Points

- 11 companies have fixed record dates for commercial paper redemption on March 10–11, 2026
- Trading in the listed commercial papers is suspended from the record date onwards
- Affected notices are under DR-836/2025-2026 (one company) and DR-837/2025-2026 (ten companies)
- Notice issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President – Listing Compliance and Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice under existing BSE procedures for commercial paper redemption.

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited to debt segment participants holding or intending to trade these specific commercial paper instruments. This is a routine pre-redemption suspension. Investors holding these CPs will receive redemption proceeds on the respective record dates. No broader equity market impact is expected.
