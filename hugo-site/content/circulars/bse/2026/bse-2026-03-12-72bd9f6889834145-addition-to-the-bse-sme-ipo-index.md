## Summary

BSE has announced the addition of Elfin Agro India Limited (Exchange ticker: 544724) to the BSE SME IPO Index, effective at the open of Friday, March 13, 2026. The company was listed on BSE on March 12, 2026, pursuant to Notice No. 20260311-44.

## Key Points

- Elfin Agro India Limited (ticker: 544724) listed on BSE on March 12, 2026
- Stock will be added to the BSE SME IPO Index effective at market open on March 13, 2026
- The corporate action is included in the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE) for March 12, 2026
- Queries can be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard index composition update following a new SME IPO listing.

## Compliance Requirements

No direct compliance requirements for market participants. Index-tracking funds and ETFs benchmarked to the BSE SME IPO Index should update their portfolios to reflect the addition of Elfin Agro India Limited (544724) effective March 13, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **March 12, 2026**: Elfin Agro India Limited listed on BSE; corporate action included in end-of-day SDE file
- **March 13, 2026**: Effective date for addition of 544724 to the BSE SME IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine index rebalancing action with low market-wide impact. The addition affects only the BSE SME IPO Index and is relevant to fund managers, ETFs, and portfolios tracking this index. Market participants should ensure their systems reflect the updated index composition before the open of trading on March 13, 2026.
