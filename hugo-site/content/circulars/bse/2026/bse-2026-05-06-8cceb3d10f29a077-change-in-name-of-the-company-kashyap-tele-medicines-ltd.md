## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Kashyap Tele-Medicines Ltd (Scrip Code 531960) has informed the Exchange of a change in its company name to **June Industries Limited**. The Scrip ID and Abbreviated Name on the BOLT Plus System will be updated accordingly effective May 12, 2026.

## Key Points

- Existing company name: **Kashyap Tele-Medicines Ltd**
- New company name: **June Industries Limited**
- Scrip Code: **531960** (unchanged)
- New Scrip ID on BOLT Plus System: **JUNE**
- New Abbreviated Name on BOLT Plus System: **JUNE**
- Effective date of change: **May 12, 2026**
- Notice issued by: Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President - Listing Compliance & Operations

## Regulatory Changes

The Scrip ID and Abbreviated Name for Scrip Code 531960 on the BOLT Plus System will change from those associated with Kashyap Tele-Medicines Ltd to **JUNE** (both Scrip ID and Abbreviated Name) as per the company's intimation to the Exchange.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their records and systems to reflect the new company name, Scrip ID, and Abbreviated Name effective May 12, 2026.
- Any orders, references, or internal systems referencing the old scrip identification should be updated prior to the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **May 06, 2026**: Notice issued by BSE
- **May 12, 2026**: Effective date for change in Scrip ID and Abbreviated Name on BOLT Plus System

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative update limited to a single listed company. Traders and investors holding or monitoring Scrip Code 531960 should note the new identifier (JUNE) to avoid confusion in order placement or portfolio tracking on the BOLT Plus System. No change in scrip code or trading segment is involved.
