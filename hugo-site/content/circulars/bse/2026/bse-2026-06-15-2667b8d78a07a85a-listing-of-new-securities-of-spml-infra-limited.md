## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 8,50,000 new equity shares of SPML Infra Limited (Scrip Code: 500402) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange effective Tuesday, June 16, 2026. These shares were issued to the Promoter on a preferential basis at Rs. 215/- per share (face value Rs. 2/- + premium Rs. 213/-) pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 8,50,000 equity shares of Rs. 2/- face value issued at a premium of Rs. 213/- per share (total issue price Rs. 215/-)
- Issued to Promoter on preferential basis via conversion of warrants
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- ISIN: INE937A01023
- Distribution numbers: 78821336 to 79671335
- Effective trading date: June 16, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice for newly allotted securities following a preferential warrant conversion.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed that these new securities are listed and available to trade from June 16, 2026. All 8,50,000 shares are subject to a lock-in period and cannot be freely traded by the Promoter until the lock-in expiry.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** April 10, 2026
- **Listing / Trading Effective From:** June 16, 2026 (Tuesday)
- **Lock-in Expiry:** December 30, 2027 (applicable to all 8,50,000 shares, Dist. Nos. 78821336–79671335)

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds 8,50,000 shares to the tradeable pool of SPML Infra Limited equity, representing a modest dilution of existing shareholders. However, since all newly listed shares are held by the Promoter under a lock-in until December 30, 2027, there is no immediate increase in free-float or market supply. The effective market impact is low in the near term. Investors should note the increased promoter holding and the eventual unlock date in late 2027 as a potential supply event.
