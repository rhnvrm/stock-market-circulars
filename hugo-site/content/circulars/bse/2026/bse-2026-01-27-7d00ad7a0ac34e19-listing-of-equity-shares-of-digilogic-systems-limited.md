## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that the equity shares of DIGILOGIC SYSTEMS LIMITED will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company will trade on the SME segment with scrip code 544684, symbol DIGILOGIC, and ISIN INE1OOT01028. The exact listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Digilogic Systems Limited
- Scrip Code: 544684
- Trading Symbol: DIGILOGIC
- ISIN: INE1OOT01028
- Segment: SME
- Notice Number: 20260127-3
- Listing date to be announced separately
