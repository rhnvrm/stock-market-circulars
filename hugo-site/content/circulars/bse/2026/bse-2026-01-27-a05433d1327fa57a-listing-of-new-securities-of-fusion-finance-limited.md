## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 566,562 new equity shares of Fusion Finance Limited (Scrip Code: 543652) resulting from the conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up shares of Rs. 10/- each issued on rights basis. Trading in these new securities will commence on Wednesday, January 28, 2026. The shares are pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company.

## Key Points

- Company: Fusion Finance Limited
- Scrip Code: 543652
- ISIN: INE139R01012
- Number of new shares: 566,562 equity shares
- Face value: Rs. 10/- per share
- Nature: Conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up shares issued on rights basis
- Distinctive Numbers: 423,502 shares (107380110 to 107803611) and 143,060 shares (161207971 to 161351030)
- Status: Pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification for new securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are permitted to trade in the newly listed securities effective from the trading day specified. No additional compliance actions required from market participants.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: January 27, 2026
- Trading Commencement Date: Wednesday, January 28, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. This is a routine corporate action involving conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid shares from a rights issue. The 566,562 new shares represent additional paid-up capital from existing shareholders who exercised their rights. The shares being pari-passu means they have equal rights and rank equally with existing equity shares. This typically results from shareholders completing payment on partly paid shares subscribed during the rights issue.
