## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that units of Bajaj Finserv BSE Top 10 Banks ETF, an open-ended scheme managed by Bajaj Asset Management Limited tracking the BSE Top 10 Banks Total Return Index, will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange under the "B" Group with effect from Tuesday, July 21, 2026.

## Key Points

- Scheme: Bajaj Finserv BSE Top 10 Banks ETF, an open-ended ETF tracking the BSE Top 10 Banks Total Return Index
- Fund House: Bajaj Finserv Mutual Fund; Investment Manager: Bajaj Asset Management Limited
- Number of units: 11,735,489
- Scrip Code: 544827; Scrip ID/Abbreviated name on BOLT: BANK10BETF
- ISIN: INF0QA701CI0
- Face value: Rs. 10.00 per unit; Issue Price: Rs. 10.00 per unit
- Date of Allotment: 16/07/2026
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Market Makers: East India Securities Ltd, Parwati Capital Market Pvt. Ltd., Kanjalochana Finserve Private Limited
- Registrar and Transfer Agent: KFin Technologies Limited, Hyderabad

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing notice under existing SEBI guidelines dated 16th February 2000 requiring mutual fund units to trade only in dematerialized form.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure trades in this scrip are executed only in dematerialized form and in unit market lots (i.e., one unit).
- Market makers are designated to provide liquidity for the ETF.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: 16 July 2026
- Listing effective from: Tuesday, 21 July 2026
- Notice Date: 20 July 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, low-impact listing notification for a new sectoral banking ETF. It does not affect existing listed securities or broader market operations; impact is limited to trading members and investors dealing in this newly listed ETF.
