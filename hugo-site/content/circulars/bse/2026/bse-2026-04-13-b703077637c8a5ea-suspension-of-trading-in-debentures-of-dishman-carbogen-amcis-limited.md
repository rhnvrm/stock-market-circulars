## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Dishman Carbogen Amcis Limited has fixed a record date of April 15, 2026 for part redemption on exercise of option on its Non-Convertible Debentures (NCD). Trading in the specified debenture is suspended with effect from April 15, 2026.

## Key Points

- Trading members are directed not to deal in the specified NCD of Dishman Carbogen Amcis Limited from April 15, 2026
- The suspension is triggered by a part redemption on exercise of option corporate action
- The affected debenture is listed under BSE scrip code 975834 with ISIN INE385W07034
- Notice issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President – Listing Compliance and Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework changes. This is an operational notice directing trading members to comply with the suspension of dealings in the specified debt security in accordance with standard BSE listing and trading regulations for corporate action record dates.

## Compliance Requirements

- All trading members of BSE must refrain from executing any buy or sell transactions in the debenture DCAL-10.04%-15-7-26-PVT (ISIN: INE385W07034, Scrip Code: 975834) from April 15, 2026 onwards
- Members must take note of the no-dealings restriction and ensure their systems and clients are informed accordingly

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** April 13, 2026
- **Record Date:** April 15, 2026
- **No Dealings From:** April 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Holders of Dishman Carbogen Amcis Limited NCD (DCAL-10.04%-15-7-26-PVT) will be unable to trade this instrument on BSE from April 15, 2026. The suspension is standard procedure ahead of a part redemption event. Investors holding this debenture should note the liquidity restriction and plan accordingly. The debenture details are:
- **Company Code:** DR-610/2026-2027
- **Debenture Series:** DCAL-10.04%-15-7-26-PVT
- **ISIN:** INE385W07034
- **BSE Scrip Code:** 975834
- **Purpose:** Redemption on Exercise of Option
