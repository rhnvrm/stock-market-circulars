## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that three Treasury Bills (T-bills) will mature on their redemption dates and trading in these instruments must cease effective June 02, 2026. This is a routine operational notice under DR-642/2026-2027.

## Key Points

- Trading in three T-bills suspended from June 02, 2026 due to maturity/redemption.
- Instruments include a 91-day, a 182-day, and a 364-day T-bill.
- Trading members are advised not to deal in these securities from the effective date.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard notice issued upon maturity of government short-term debt instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the three listed T-bill scrip codes (805152, 805075, 805187) on or after June 02, 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 01, 2026
- **Suspension Effective Date:** June 02, 2026 (Redemption date)

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited to holders and traders of these three specific T-bill instruments. The suspension is a routine end-of-lifecycle action triggered by maturity; no broader market or operational disruption is expected. Holders will receive redemption proceeds as per standard settlement processes.
