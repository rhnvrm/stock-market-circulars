## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the Anchor Investor (AI) portion of the Public Issue of Susan Electricals India Limited has been subscribed. A total of 15,23,000 equity shares were allotted to Anchor Investors at Rs. 127 per equity share on June 10, 2026.

## Key Points

- 15,23,000 equity shares allocated to Anchor Investors under the public issue
- Issue price for Anchor Investors: Rs. 127 per equity share
- Allocation completed on June 10, 2026
- Company announcement dated June 10, 2026 enclosed as attachment (Company_Anchor_Letter)
- Notice issued by Mangesh Tayde, Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard informational notice regarding anchor investor allocation as part of the IPO process.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for trading members. This notice is for information purposes only.

## Important Dates

- Anchor Investor allocation date: June 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. The anchor investor allocation is a routine pre-IPO step indicating that institutional investors have subscribed to the issue at Rs. 127 per share. This sets a reference price point for the broader public issue of Susan Electricals India Limited and signals institutional confidence in the offering.
