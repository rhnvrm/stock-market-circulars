## Summary

BSE has issued an update regarding the debt securities of Varthana Finance Private Limited, continuing from Notice No. 20250623-22 dated June 23, 2025. Trading members are informed that the scrip bearing ISIN INE125T07345 (Scrip Code 976812) will be available for trading with revised terms effective June 10, 2026.

## Key Points

- Continuation of earlier Exchange Notice No. 20250623-22 dated June 23, 2025
- Scrip Code 976812 with ISIN INE125T07345 will trade under revised terms
- Revised terms take effect from Wednesday, June 10, 2026
- Detailed revised terms are provided in Annexure I of the notice

## Regulatory Changes

The debt securities of Varthana Finance Private Limited will be subject to revised terms as specified in Annexure I, superseding or updating the terms communicated in the original notice dated June 23, 2025.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to take note of the revised terms for Scrip Code 976812 (ISIN INE125T07345) and update their systems accordingly before trading commences on June 10, 2026. Members with queries are directed to contact DCS-Listing department officials.

## Important Dates

- **June 9, 2026**: Notice issued
- **June 10, 2026 (Wednesday)**: Revised terms become effective for trading
- **Reference Notice**: 20250623-22 dated June 23, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This update impacts traders and investors holding or transacting in Varthana Finance Private Limited debt securities (ISIN INE125T07345). The revised terms may affect pricing, coupon, maturity, or other contractual parameters of the security. Trading members should review Annexure I for the specific revisions before executing trades from June 10, 2026 onwards. The impact is contained to participants in this specific debt instrument.
