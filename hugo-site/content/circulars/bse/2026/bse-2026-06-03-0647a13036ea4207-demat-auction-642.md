## Summary

BSE Limited, through Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd. (ICCL), has invited offers from trading members for purchase of securities in Demat Auction 642, pertaining to Rolling Settlement No. DR-642/2026-27. The auction session is scheduled for June 3, 2026, with pay-in on June 4, 2026.

## Key Points

- Auction offer entry window: 2:00 PM to 2:45 PM on June 3, 2026
- Auction matching session: 2:45 PM to 3:00 PM on June 3, 2026
- Delivery statement and accepted auction offer reports: 3:00 PM to 3:30 PM on June 3, 2026
- Money statement and final reports: 3:35 PM to 5:30 PM on June 3, 2026
- Auction pay-in (securities): June 4, 2026 at 8:00 AM
- Members and custodians must check auction pay-in obligations and execute securities pay-in within depository cut-off timings
- No corrections will be permitted for punching errors in securities code or rate

## Regulatory Changes

Following implementation of the Uniform Membership structure in the Equity Cash Segment, settlement is now processed through the Clearing Member of a Trading Member. The previous restriction on trading members offering securities in auction for settlements in which they had short-delivered has been removed.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must submit auction offers via trading terminals during the designated session window
- Members and custodians are required to verify auction pay-in obligations for securities and complete securities pay-in in depositories within depository cut-off timings
- Trading members must refer to the cutoff rate in the "Auction Offer Entry Screen" before submitting offers
- Trading members should review notices from respective departments regarding Corporate Actions (Ex-Rights, Ex-Bonus, Ex-Split, etc.) for relevant scrips
- No corrections will be accepted after offer submission; trading members must exercise utmost care in entering securities codes and rates

## Important Dates

- **June 3, 2026**: Auction session (offer entry, matching, delivery statements)
- **June 4, 2026 (8:00 AM)**: Securities pay-in deadline for auction

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational circular applicable to trading members of BSE with short delivery positions in Rolling Settlement DR-642/2026-27. The notice clarifies the auction schedule and operational guidelines. The removal of the restriction on trading members offering securities in settlements where they had short deliveries (post Uniform Membership structure) provides additional flexibility. Impact is limited to participants in this specific settlement cycle.
