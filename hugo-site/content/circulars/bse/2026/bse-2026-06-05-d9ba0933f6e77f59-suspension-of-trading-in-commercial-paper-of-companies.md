## Summary

BSE Notice No. 20260605-49 informs trading members that 10 companies have fixed 10 June 2026 as the record date for redemption of their respective commercial paper instruments. Trading members are directed not to deal in these commercial papers effective from the ex-date of 10 June 2026.

## Key Points

- 10 companies have set 10 June 2026 as the record date and ex-date for commercial paper redemption
- All affected instruments fall under Settlement No. 648/2026-2027
- Trading members are advised to cease dealing in these commercial papers immediately
- The suspension covers the Debt segment under the Listing Operations department
- Issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President, Listing Compliance and Operations

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice pursuant to the companies fixing redemption record dates for their commercial paper instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the listed commercial paper instruments of the 10 companies on or after the ex-date (10 June 2026)
- Members should update their systems to reflect the suspension of these scrip codes in the debt segment

## Impact Assessment

Impact is limited to debt segment participants holding or trading these specific commercial paper instruments. The suspension is a routine pre-redemption measure with no broader market implications. All 10 instruments share the same record/ex-date of 10 June 2026 and settlement number 648/2026-2027, suggesting a batch processing of near-maturity commercial papers. No equity trading is affected.
