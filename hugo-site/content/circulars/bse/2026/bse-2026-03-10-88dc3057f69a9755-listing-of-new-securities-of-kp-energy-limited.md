## Summary

BSE has notified the listing and permission to trade for 6,88,800 new equity shares of K.P. Energy Limited (Scrip Code: 539686) issued to the promoter on a preferential basis. These shares will be available for trading effective Wednesday, March 11, 2026, though they are subject to a lock-in period until September 30, 2027.

## Key Points

- 6,88,800 equity shares of Rs. 5/- face value each have been listed on BSE
- Shares issued at a premium of Rs. 407/-, making the issue price Rs. 412/- per share
- Allotment was made to the promoter on a preferential basis
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- ISIN: INE127T01021
- Distribution numbers: 66907042 to 67595841

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification pursuant to a preferential allotment already completed.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed that the new securities are listed and permitted to trade from March 11, 2026
- The entire allotment of 6,88,800 shares is subject to lock-in and cannot be traded until October 1, 2027

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** January 21, 2026
- **Trading Effective From:** March 11, 2026 (Wednesday)
- **Lock-in Expiry:** September 30, 2027

## Impact Assessment

The market impact of this listing is minimal. The 6,88,800 shares allotted to the promoter are fully locked in until September 30, 2027, meaning no additional floating stock enters the market immediately. The preferential allotment at Rs. 412/- per share (face value Rs. 5/- with a premium of Rs. 407/-) reflects the company's capital-raising activity. Existing shareholders are unaffected in terms of rank, as the new shares are pari-passu with old equity shares.
