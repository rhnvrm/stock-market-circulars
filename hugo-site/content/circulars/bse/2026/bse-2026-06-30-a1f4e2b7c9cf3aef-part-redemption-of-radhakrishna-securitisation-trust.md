## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that RADHAKRISHNA SECURITISATION TRUST has informed the exchange of a partial redemption of its Pass-Through Certificates (PTCs). As a result, the face value per PTC is reduced to Rs. 9,740,334.33, effective July 1, 2026.

## Key Points

- RADHAKRISHNA SECURITISATION TRUST has carried out a partial redemption of its PTCs listed on the Debt segment of BSE.
- The reduced face value per PTC following partial redemption is **Rs. 9,740,334.33**.
- The affected scrip is **RKST-7.66%-28-9-28-PTC** (Scrip Code: 977124, ISIN: INE2I7H15018).
- The reduced face value will be effective for trading from **July 1, 2026** (Reference: DR-662/2026-2027).

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework introduced. This notice implements an operational update to the traded face value of the PTC in accordance with the partial redemption event notified by the issuer trust.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are advised to update their systems to reflect the reduced face value of Rs. 9,740,334.33 per PTC for scrip code 977124 (RKST-7.66%-28-9-28-PTC).
- Members must ensure all trades in this instrument from July 1, 2026 onward are executed at the revised face value.

## Important Dates

- **June 30, 2026**: BSE notice issued.
- **July 1, 2026**: Effective date for trading at the reduced face value of Rs. 9,740,334.33 per PTC.

## Impact Assessment

The partial redemption reduces the outstanding principal (face value) of each PTC unit, which is a standard occurrence in securitisation structures as underlying loan collections are passed through to investors. The impact is limited to existing holders and active traders of RKST PTCs on the BSE Debt segment. Market participants holding this instrument should note the revised face value for valuation, margin, and settlement purposes from July 1, 2026.
