## Summary

BSE Index Services Private Limited has announced the addition of SEDEMAC MECHATRONICS LIMITED (Exchange ticker: 544723) to the BSE IPO Index. The stock was listed on BSE effective March 11, 2026, and will be included in the index at the open of March 13, 2026.

## Key Points

- SEDEMAC MECHATRONICS LIMITED (ticker: 544723) listed on BSE on March 11, 2026
- The stock will be added to the BSE IPO Index effective at market open on Friday, March 13, 2026
- This addition is referenced against Notice No. 20260310-24
- The corporate action is included in the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE) dated March 11, 2026
- Queries can be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index rebalancing action pursuant to the listing of a new security on BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants and index fund/ETF managers tracking the BSE IPO Index must update their portfolios to include SEDEMAC MECHATRONICS LIMITED (544723) effective at the open of March 13, 2026
- Clients should refer to the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE) for processing

## Important Dates

- **March 11, 2026**: SEDEMAC MECHATRONICS LIMITED listed on BSE; corporate action file published
- **March 13, 2026 (market open)**: Effective date for addition to BSE IPO Index

## Impact Assessment

This addition impacts investors and fund managers tracking or replicating the BSE IPO Index. Passive funds and ETFs benchmarked to this index will need to incorporate SEDEMAC MECHATRONICS LIMITED into their holdings before the market opens on March 13, 2026. The impact is moderate for index-linked products but routine in nature.
