## Summary

BSE has issued its periodic update on the Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LT-ASM) framework, referencing prior notices dating back to March 2018. The circular identifies securities newly placed under LT-ASM, securities moving to higher LT-ASM stages, securities continuing in the framework but shifting trading groups, and securities exiting the framework, along with a consolidated list of all securities currently under LT-ASM.

## Key Points

- Securities listed in Annexure I (Part A) will be subject to 100% margin requirements.
- Securities moving to higher LT-ASM stages are listed in Annexure I (Part B).
- Securities under the framework per the June 04, 2021 notice are listed in Annexure I (Part D) and will continue in LT-ASM but be transferred to T/XT/MT/TS trading group.
- Securities exiting the LT-ASM framework are listed in Annexure II.
- A consolidated list of all securities currently under the LT-ASM framework is provided in Annexure III.
- Shortlisting under this framework is based on XBRL submissions by listed companies and is purely a surveillance measure, not an adverse action against any company.
- The LT-ASM framework operates in conjunction with other prevailing surveillance measures.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced; this is a periodic review and update under the existing LT-ASM framework established via notices dated March 21, 2018, October 27, 2018, December 04, 2020, June 04, 2021, August 09, 2024, and September 20, 2024.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and market participants must apply 100% margins on open positions (as on July 15, 2026) and new positions in securities listed in Annexure I (Part A) from July 16, 2026 onwards.
- Members must apply the revised lower price band effective July 14, 2026 for relevant securities.
- Securities transferring to T/XT/MT/TS trading groups must be reclassified accordingly from July 16, 2026.
- Price bands for securities exiting the framework will revert to pre-shortlisting levels, unless the security remains subject to another surveillance measure.
- Queries can be directed to bse.surv@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- July 14, 2026: Lower price band applicable; securities moving to higher LT-ASM stages take effect; securities exiting LT-ASM framework take effect.
- July 15, 2026: Reference date for open positions subject to 100% margin.
- July 16, 2026: 100% margin applicable on open and new positions; securities transferred to T/XT/MT/TS trading group.

## Impact Assessment

This update affects trading conditions for specific securities named in the annexures, including increased margin obligations, adjusted price bands, and potential trading group reclassification. While routine in nature, these changes can materially affect liquidity, trading costs, and investor sentiment for the affected scrips. Market participants trading in these securities should review the annexures to confirm impact and adjust positions and margin funding accordingly.
