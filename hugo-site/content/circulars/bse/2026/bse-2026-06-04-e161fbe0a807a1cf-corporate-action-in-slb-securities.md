## Summary

ICCL Ltd. has issued Notice No. 20260604-37 dated 04 June 2026 informing trading members about corporate actions affecting securities in the Securities Lending and Borrowing (SLB) segment. The detailed list of affected securities is provided in the annexure attached to the notice.

## Key Points

- Trading members are required to take note of corporate actions impacting SLB segment securities.
- The specifics of the affected securities and the nature of the corporate actions are contained in the attached annexure.
- Notice issued by ICCL Ltd. (Indian Clearing Corporation Limited), the clearing arm of BSE.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is an operational notice communicating existing corporate action adjustments applicable to the SLB segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members participating in the SLB segment must review the annexure to identify affected securities.
- Members should ensure their SLB positions and obligations are managed in accordance with the corporate action adjustments disclosed in the annexure.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 04 June 2026
- Effective dates for individual corporate actions are specified in the annexure.

## Impact Assessment

This notice has a moderate operational impact on trading members active in the SLB segment. Corporate actions (such as dividends, splits, or bonuses) on SLB securities typically require adjustments to open lending/borrowing positions and collateral values. Members with open SLB positions in the affected securities should act promptly upon reviewing the annexure to avoid settlement discrepancies.
