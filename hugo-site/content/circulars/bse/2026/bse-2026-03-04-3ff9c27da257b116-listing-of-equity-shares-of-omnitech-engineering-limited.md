## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the equity shares of Omnitech Engineering Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on BSE. The exact listing date and further security details will be communicated through a subsequent notice.

## Key Points

- Omnitech Engineering Limited equity shares are to be listed on BSE under scrip code 544720 and symbol OMNI
- ISIN assigned: INE0UH301010
- Listing date is yet to be announced and will be notified separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing admission notice under BSE's listing framework.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should await the subsequent notice from BSE confirming the exact listing date and trading details before initiating any dealings in the shares of Omnitech Engineering Limited.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** March 4, 2026
- **Listing Date:** To be announced via a separate notice

## Impact Assessment

The listing of Omnitech Engineering Limited (OMNI, 544720) will provide investors access to trade the company's equity shares on BSE. The immediate market impact is limited as the listing date has not yet been specified. Trading members should monitor for the follow-up notice confirming the listing date and associated security details.
