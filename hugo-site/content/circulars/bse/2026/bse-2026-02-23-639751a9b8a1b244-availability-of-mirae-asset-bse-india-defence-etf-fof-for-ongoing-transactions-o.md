## Summary

BSE has informed members that the MIRAE ASSET BSE INDIA DEFENCE ETF FOF, which was earlier available as an NFO, has been reopened for ongoing subscription and redemption along with SIP, STP & SWP facilities on the BSE StAR MF Platform with effect from February 24, 2026. This follows the earlier Notice No. 20260130-26.

## Key Points

- MIRAE ASSET BSE INDIA DEFENCE ETF FOF is now available for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform from February 24, 2026.
- Both Regular Plan and Direct Plan variants are available across Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options â€” six schemes in total.
- SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities are also enabled.
- This notice supersedes/follows up on BSE Notice No. 20260130-26.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational platform availability notice.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action required from members beyond ensuring their systems recognize the newly available schemes and ISINs on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Important Dates

- **February 24, 2026**: Effective date for ongoing subscription, redemption, SIP, STP & SWP availability on BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact. This notice is of operational relevance to mutual fund distributors and members using the BSE StAR MF Platform who wish to transact in MIRAE ASSET BSE INDIA DEFENCE ETF FOF schemes. The six available schemes with their ISINs are:
