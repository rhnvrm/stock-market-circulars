## Summary

BSE has announced the addition of SEDEMAC MECHATRONICS LIMITED (Exchange ticker: 544723) to the BSE IPO Index. The stock was listed on BSE on March 11, 2026, and will be included in the BSE IPO Index effective at the open of Friday, March 13, 2026.

## Key Points

- SEDEMAC MECHATRONICS LIMITED (ticker: 544723) listed on BSE on March 11, 2026
- The stock will be added to the BSE IPO Index effective at market open on March 13, 2026
- This notice references prior Notice No. 20260310-24
- The corporate action has been included in the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- Queries can be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index composition update following a new listing on BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

- Index fund managers and ETF providers tracking the BSE IPO Index must rebalance their portfolios to include SEDEMAC MECHATRONICS LIMITED effective March 13, 2026
- Clients requiring additional information should contact bseindex@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **March 11, 2026**: SEDEMAC MECHATRONICS LIMITED listed on BSE
- **March 13, 2026**: Effective date for addition to BSE IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

The addition is limited in scope, affecting only funds and portfolios benchmarked against the BSE IPO Index. There is no broad market impact. Passive investors tracking the BSE IPO Index will need to add exposure to SEDEMAC MECHATRONICS LIMITED (544723) before the effective date of March 13, 2026.
