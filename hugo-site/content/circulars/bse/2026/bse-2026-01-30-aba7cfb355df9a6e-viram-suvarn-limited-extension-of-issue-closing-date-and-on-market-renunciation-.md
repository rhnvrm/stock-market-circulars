## Summary

Viram Suvarn Limited (formerly Veeram Securities Limited, Script Code: 540252) announced on January 29, 2026, that its Board of Directors has extended the closing date of its ongoing Rights Issue from February 6, 2026 to February 20, 2026. The extension provides shareholders additional time to exercise their rights entitlements. Correspondingly, the on-market renunciation deadline has been extended from January 30, 2026 to February 13, 2026.

## Key Points

- Rights Issue originally opened on January 23, 2026
- Issue closing date extended by 14 days from February 6 to February 20, 2026
- On-market renunciation deadline extended from January 30 to February 13, 2026
- Extension decided in Board meeting held on January 29, 2026 (6:00 PM to 6:30 PM)
- ISIN for dematerialized Rights Entitlement: INE607V20010
- Letter of Offer dated January 5, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a company-specific corporate action timeline modification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Shareholders entitled to participate must submit Common Application Form (CAF) with applicable payment by February 20, 2026
- On-market renunciation of rights entitlements must be completed by February 13, 2026
- Rights entitlements were credited by January 22, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - affects only shareholders of Viram Suvarn Limited (Script Code: 540252)

**Shareholder Impact:** Positive - provides additional 14-day window for shareholders to decide on rights participation and complete on-market renunciation
