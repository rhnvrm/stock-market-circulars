## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that equity shares of Kasturi Metal Composite Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544688, symbol KASTURI, and ISIN INE0T1301023. The specific listing date and detailed securities information will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Kasturi Metal Composite Limited
- Scrip Code: 544688
- Symbol: KASTURI
- ISIN: INE0T1301023
- Segment: SME
- Listing date to be announced separately

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the company details and await further notice regarding the exact listing date and security details before commencing trading activities.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: February 2, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement for the SME segment with minimal market impact. Trading members are informed in advance to prepare for the new security. The listing will add one more equity to the SME platform, providing investors with an additional investment opportunity in the metal composite sector.
