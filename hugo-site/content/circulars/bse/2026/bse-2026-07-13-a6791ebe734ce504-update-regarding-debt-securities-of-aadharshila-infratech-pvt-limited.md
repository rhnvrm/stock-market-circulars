## Summary

BSE has issued a notice in continuation of its earlier communication (Notice No. 20240228-61 dated 28th February 2024) regarding the debt securities of Aadharshila Infratech Pvt Limited. Trading Members are informed that the debt security bearing Scrip Code 975430 and ISIN INE0REQ08017 will be available with revised terms effective Tuesday, July 14, 2026, following amendments to the Debenture Trust Deed as part of a debt restructuring exercise.

## Key Points

- The notice pertains to debt securities of Aadharshila Infratech Pvt Limited under Scrip Code 975430 (ISIN: INE0REQ08017).
- Revised terms take effect from Tuesday, July 14, 2026.
- The External Debt to Distribution Amount ratio covenant has been relaxed: previously required to stay below 3x at all times until Final Redemption Date; now allowed up to below 4x until 31st March 2027, reverting to below 3x from 1st April 2027 until Final Redemption Date.
- The Permitted Indebtedness clause has been amended: the Issuer previously required Debenture Holder permission for further indebtedness; the Debenture Holder has now issued a No Objection Certificate (NOC) permitting additional indebtedness of Rs. 300 crores to meet the Issuer's sponsor obligation.

## Regulatory Changes

The amendments relate to two covenants in the Debenture Trust Deed:

1. **External Debt to Distribution Amount Ratio**: Relaxed from a flat 3x ceiling to a phased approach — below 4x until 31st March 2027, then below 3x from 1st April 2027 onward until the Final Redemption Date.
2. **Permitted Indebtedness**: The Debenture Holder has granted an NOC allowing the Issuer to take on further indebtedness of Rs. 300 crores specifically to meet its sponsor obligation.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members are required to note the revised terms applicable to the specified ISIN and update their records accordingly. Queries can be directed to Ms. Rutuja Mhasade or Hardik Bhuta (Assistant Vice President) at debt.listing@bseindia.com or +91 22 2272 8597.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: 13 July 2026
- **Effective Date of Revised Terms**: Tuesday, 14 July 2026
- **Covenant Transition Date**: 31 March 2027 (ratio tightens from below 4x to below 3x from 1 April 2027)

## Impact Assessment

This update has limited market-wide impact as it concerns a single, privately-held debt instrument (NCD) rather than a publicly traded equity security. The relaxation of the debt ratio covenant and the NOC for additional indebtedness suggest a restructuring aimed at providing the Issuer financial flexibility, likely to meet sponsor-related obligations. The impact is primarily relevant to debenture holders and trading members dealing in this specific debt segment ISIN, with no direct effect on equity markets or broader investor classes.
