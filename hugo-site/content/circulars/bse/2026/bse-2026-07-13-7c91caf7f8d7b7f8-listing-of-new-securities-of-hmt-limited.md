## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 28,96,79,400 new equity shares of HMT Limited (Scrip Code: 500191), each of face value Rs. 10, issued at par to the promoter on a preferential basis, are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Tuesday, July 14, 2026. These shares rank pari-passu with the existing equity shares of the company.

## Key Points

- 28,96,79,400 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each allotted to the promoter on a preferential basis are now listed.
- ISIN of the securities: INE262A01018.
- The new shares rank pari-passu with the company's existing equity shares.
- Shares were allotted across multiple dates ranging from 04/02/2005 to 31/10/2007, with corresponding distinctive number ranges.
- Trading in the new securities commences from July 14, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing approval under BSE's Listing Operations department for securities issued on a preferential basis.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific action required from trading members beyond recognizing the new shares as tradable and pari-passu with existing equity from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- Effective date of listing and trading: Tuesday, July 14, 2026
- Underlying allotment dates range from 04/02/2005 to 31/10/2007

## Impact Assessment

The listing formalizes previously allotted preferential shares to the promoter, increasing the company's tradable equity base and promoter shareholding. Since the shares rank pari-passu with existing shares, there is no differential rights impact, but the increase in floating/paid-up capital may have a marginal dilutive effect on per-share metrics. Overall market impact is limited to HMT Limited shareholders and traders in the scrip.
