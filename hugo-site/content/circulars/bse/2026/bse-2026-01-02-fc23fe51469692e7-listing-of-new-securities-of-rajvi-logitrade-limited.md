## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 22,51,425 new equity shares of RAJVI LOGITRADE LIMITED (Scrip Code: 511185) effective from Monday, January 5, 2026. These shares were issued at par value of Rs. 10/- each to promoters and non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. The shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares and are subject to lock-in requirements.

## Key Points

- Total shares listed: 22,51,425 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each
- Scrip Code: 511185
- Issue Price: Rs. 10/- (at par)
- Allotment Date: September 18, 2025
- Trading commencement: January 5, 2026
- ISIN: INE381N01019
- Distinctive Numbers: 4074101 to 6325525
- Issued to promoters and non-promoters on preferential basis
- Shares issued pursuant to conversion of warrants

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must note:
- New shares are available for trading from January 5, 2026
- Shares are subject to lock-in restrictions as specified below
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: September 18, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: January 5, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (630,000 shares)**: July 9, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (1,621,425 shares)**: July 9, 2027
