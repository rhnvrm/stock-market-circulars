## Summary

BSE has issued a notice to Trading Members informing them that government security GS23JUN26C (Scrip Code: 800356, ISIN: IN000626C068) will mature on its redemption date. Accordingly, trading in this security has been suspended with effect from June 19, 2026.

## Key Points

- Government security GS23JUN26C (Scrip Code 800356, ISIN: IN000626C068) is scheduled for redemption.
- Trading Members are advised not to deal in this security from June 19, 2026 onwards.
- The suspension is referenced under DR-655/2026-2027.
- The notice was issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President – Listing Compliance & Operations.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice under BSE's debt segment procedures for securities reaching maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must cease all dealings in GS23JUN26C (Scrip Code 800356, ISIN: IN000626C068) with effect from June 19, 2026.
- Members should ensure no trades are executed in this security on or after the effective suspension date.

## Important Dates

- **June 18, 2026**: Date of notice.
- **June 19, 2026**: Effective date of trading suspension.
- **June 23, 2026**: Redemption/maturity date of the security (implied by the security name GS23JUN26C).

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard end-of-lifecycle action for a maturing government security on BSE's Debt Market Segment. The impact is limited to Trading Members holding or transacting in this specific security. Holders will receive redemption proceeds on the maturity date. No broader market disruption is anticipated.
