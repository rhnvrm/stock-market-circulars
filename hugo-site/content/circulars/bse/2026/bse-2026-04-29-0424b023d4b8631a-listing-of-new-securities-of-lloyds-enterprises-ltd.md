## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260429-24 dated April 29, 2026, announcing the listing of new securities of Lloyds Enterprises Ltd. on the Equity segment. The notice is issued by the Listing Operations department under the Company Related category.

## Key Points

- Notice No.: 20260429-24
- Date: April 29, 2026
- Category: Company Related
- Segment: Equity
- Department: Listing Operations
- Subject: Listing of New Securities of Lloyds Enterprises Ltd.
- No attachments provided with this notice

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes indicated. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Market participants and traders should note the admission of new securities of Lloyds Enterprises Ltd. for trading on the BSE Equity segment. No specific compliance actions are required from the general market.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: April 29, 2026
- Listing effective as per the notice date unless otherwise specified in accompanying documentation.

## Impact Assessment

This notice has a limited and company-specific impact. It enables trading in newly listed securities of Lloyds Enterprises Ltd. on BSE's Equity segment. Investors interested in this company should take note of the new securities becoming available for trading. Broader market impact is negligible.
