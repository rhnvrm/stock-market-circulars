## Summary

BSE has admitted Secured Redeemable Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of Muthoot Mercantile Limited to dealings on the Exchange effective Tuesday, June 16, 2026, under the F Group segment.

## Key Points

- NCDs of Muthoot Mercantile Limited listed under **F Group** on BSE Debt segment.
- Trading commences on **June 16, 2026**.
- Trading permitted only in **dematerialized form** as per SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000.
- Market lot is **one bond** (unit market lot).
- Financial year of Muthoot Mercantile Limited ends on **31st March**.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. Listing is governed by existing SEBI guidelines (February 16, 2000) requiring bond trading exclusively in dematerialized form.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must ensure all trades in these NCDs are settled in dematerialized form only.
- Trades must be executed in unit market lot (one bond per lot).

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 15, 2026
- **Trading Commencement:** June 16, 2026 (Tuesday)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt listing with limited broader market impact. The listing expands the available debt instruments on BSE's debt market segment. Investors interested in Muthoot Mercantile Limited's debt offerings can now trade these NCDs on the Exchange. The registrar is KFIN Technologies Limited (Mumbai), and the compliance officer is Rupesh V H at Muthoot Mercantile Limited (Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala).
