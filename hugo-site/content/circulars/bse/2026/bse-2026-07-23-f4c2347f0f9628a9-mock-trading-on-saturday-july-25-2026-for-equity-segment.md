## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session in the Equity segment on Saturday, July 25, 2026. Per earlier circulars on the Closing Auction Session (CAS) and Standard Operating Procedures, the exercise comprises two separate mock sessions: one from the Disaster Recovery (DR) site to test CAS-related changes, and one from the Primary (PR) site to test trading functionality without CAS changes, in preparation for live trading resumption on Monday, July 27, 2026.

## Key Points

- Two independent mock trading sessions will run on Saturday, July 25, 2026.
- Mock Trading Session 1 (DR site): tests changes related to the Closing Auction Session (CAS) introduced via circulars 20260319-21 and 20260610-41.
- Mock Trading Session 2 (PR site): tests general trading functionality without CAS changes, to facilitate live trading from Monday, July 27, 2026.
- Session 1 (DR site) timeline: Log-in 07:30–07:45 am; Block Deal Window 07:45–08:00 am; Pre-Open order entry 08:00–08:08 am with matching/buffer till 08:15 am; Continuous Trading (T+1) 08:15 am–10:35 am; Continuous Trading (T+0) 08:15–08:45 am; Special Pre-Open for IPO/re-listed scrips 08:00–08:35 am with continuous trading till 10:35 am; Closing Auction Session transition begins 09:00 am with order entry through 10:35 am, matching/confirmation to 10:37 am; Closing 10:35/10:37–10:50 am; Post-closing 10:50–11:00 am; Trade Modification T+1 by 11:00 am, T+0 by 08:45 am.
- Session 2 (PR site) timeline: Log-in 12:30–12:45 pm; Morning Block Deal 12:45–01:00 pm; Pre-Open order entry 01:00–01:08 pm with matching/buffer till 01:15 pm; Continuous Trading (T+1) 01:15–02:00 pm; Continuous Trading (T+0) 01:15–01:45 pm; Special Pre-Open for IPO/re-listed scrips 01:00–01:23 pm with continuous trading till 02:00 pm; a Periodic Call Auction session of 30 minutes starting 01:15 pm is also referenced (details truncated in source).
- Random stoppage windows apply to pre-open and special pre-open order entry periods, as well as final order entry before closing, per standard exchange practice.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced; this notice implements testing procedures referenced in earlier BSE circulars 20260319-21 (Closing Auction Session) and 20260610-41 (Standard Operating Procedures).

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members and market participants intending to test system readiness should log in and participate during the specified windows for both DR-site and PR-site mock sessions. No mandatory participation is stated beyond standard operational readiness.

## Important Dates

- Mock trading date: Saturday, July 25, 2026.
- Session 1 (DR site): 07:30 am to 11:00 am.
- Session 2 (PR site): 12:30 pm onward (through at least 02:00 pm based on available schedule).
- Live trading resumes: Monday, July 27, 2026.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, low-impact operational notice confined to system and infrastructure testing. It does not affect any listed security, corporate action, or market pricing, and mainly concerns trading members' technical preparedness ahead of resumed live trading.
