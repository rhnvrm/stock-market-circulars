## Summary

BSE has published the list of Registrar & Transfer Agents (RTAs) scheduled to visit BSE Ltd. during January 2026. The circular provides details of companies whose registry work is handled by Adroit Corporate Services Pvt. Ltd., which will be visiting the exchange premises during this period.

## Key Points

- RTA visiting BSE: Adroit Corporate Services Pvt. Ltd.
- Visit period: January 2026
- Total of 52+ listed companies serviced by this RTA are included in the list
- Companies span multiple sectors including finance, manufacturing, real estate, textiles, and infrastructure
- The list includes scrip codes and full company names for reference

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced through this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. This is an informational circular regarding RTA visit schedules.

## Important Dates

- **Visit Period**: January 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact and serves purely informational purposes. It allows investors and market participants to know which RTA will be present at BSE premises during January 2026 for any investor grievances, share transfer matters, or registry-related queries concerning the listed companies. The visit facilitates coordination between the exchange, RTAs, and listed companies for better investor service delivery.
