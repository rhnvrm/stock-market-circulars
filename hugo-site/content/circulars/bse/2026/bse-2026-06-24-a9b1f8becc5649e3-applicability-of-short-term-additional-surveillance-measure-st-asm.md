## Summary

BSE has issued a notice identifying securities that have satisfied the criteria for inclusion under Short Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) Stage I and Stage II. Enhanced margin requirements will apply from June 29, 2026, on open positions as of June 25, 2026, and on all new positions created thereafter.

## Key Points

- Securities in ST-ASM Stage I will attract a margin of 50% or existing margin, whichever is higher, capped at 100%
- Securities in ST-ASM Stage II will attract a margin of 100% or existing margin, whichever is higher, capped at 100%
- The measure applies to all open positions as on June 25, 2026, and new positions from June 29, 2026
- The specific list of securities is provided in Annexure I attached to the notice
- ST-ASM operates in conjunction with all other prevailing surveillance measures imposed by Exchanges
- Shortlisting under ASM is purely for market surveillance purposes and should not be construed as adverse action against the concerned company

## Regulatory Changes

This notice is issued further to the revised ASM framework established under Exchange notices: 20240920-71 (September 20, 2024), 20181027-1 (October 27, 2018), 20190719-33 (July 19, 2019), 20201204-56 (December 4, 2020), 20230925-49 (September 25, 2023), and 20240920-63 (September 20, 2024). No new framework changes are introduced; this notice implements existing ASM provisions for newly qualifying securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must apply enhanced margin requirements on the shortlisted securities as specified for Stage I and Stage II
- Members must ensure compliance with margin collection on all open positions as of June 25, 2026
- Members may contact bse.surv@bseindia.com for clarifications
- When a scrip is removed from the ASM framework, its price band shall be reinstated to the pre-ASM level, unless covered under another surveillance measure

## Important Dates

- **June 24, 2026**: Notice date
- **June 25, 2026**: Reference date for open positions subject to new margin requirements
- **June 29, 2026**: Effective date for enhanced margin requirements (ST-ASM Stage I and Stage II)

## Impact Assessment

Market participants holding or planning to take positions in the shortlisted securities will face significantly higher margin obligations. ST-ASM Stage I raises margins to a minimum of 50% (capped at 100%), while Stage II mandates 100% margin, effectively requiring traders to fund their full position value as collateral. This substantially increases the cost of carrying or initiating positions in affected scrips and may lead to forced position closures if participants cannot meet the enhanced margin call by June 29, 2026. The measure is intended to curb excessive speculation or volatility in the flagged securities.
