## Summary

BSE has issued a notice (No. 20260610-9) announcing the listing of equity shares of UHM Vacation Limited on the SME segment. The listing is administered by the DCS-Listing department and is categorized as a company-related action.

## Key Points

- Equity shares of UHM Vacation Limited are being listed on BSE's SME segment.
- Notice issued by the DCS-Listing department on June 10, 2026.
- Categorized as a company-related action under the SME segment.
- No attachments accompany this notice.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this notice. It is a standard listing announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

Market participants and brokers should update their systems to recognize UHM Vacation Limited as a newly listed SME security on BSE. Trading members may begin facilitating trades in the shares upon commencement of listing.

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date:** June 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to UHM Vacation Limited and its investors. As an SME segment listing, the broader market effect is minimal. Investors and trading members dealing in SME securities should note the availability of this scrip for trading on BSE.
