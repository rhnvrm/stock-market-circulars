## Summary

BSE has published the Demat Auction list for Settlement No. 836 dated March 11, 2026. This auction covers securities that could not be delivered during normal settlement and will be auctioned to fulfil outstanding delivery obligations. Approximately 130 scrips are listed with their respective auction quantities.

## Key Points

- Settlement No. 836 dated March 11, 2026
- Approximately 130 scrips listed for demat auction
- Quantities range from 1 share (e.g., Antony Waste, Bodhi Tree, Godrej Agrovet) to 50,000 shares (Rajnish Wellness) and 245,520 shares (Deep Diamonds)
- Large quantity auctions include: Deep Diamonds (245,520), Rajnish Wellness (50,000), DSP Mutual Fund (40,000), Prismx Global (14,158), Svaraj Trading (14,584), Viji Finance (13,671)
- Sectors represented include pharma, FMCG, infrastructure, finance, technology, and metals

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice under BSE's settlement and auction mechanism.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members/brokers with short delivery positions in the listed scrips must participate in or be aware of the auction process
- Buyers in auction are required to settle at the auction price determined by BSE
- Short sellers failing delivery will be obligated to pay the auction price differential

## Important Dates

- Auction Date: March 11, 2026
- Settlement No.: 836

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine demat auction notice with moderate market impact limited to the specific scrips listed. Stocks with unusually high auction quantities — particularly Deep Diamonds (245,520 shares), Rajnish Wellness (50,000 shares), and DSP Mutual Fund units (40,000) — may see short-term price pressure. Members with outstanding short positions in the listed securities face settlement cost implications. No broader market or systemic impact is anticipated.
