## Summary

BSE issued an update on surveillance measures related to unsolicited videos circulating on social media platforms, effective January 28, 2026. The update shows Silverline Technologies Ltd (Scrip Code: 500389, ISIN: INE368A01021) has been removed from the surveillance list. No new scrips were added to the list, and the consolidated list of affected securities is now empty.

## Key Points

- No new scrips added to surveillance list for unsolicited social media videos
- One scrip removed from surveillance: Silverline Technologies Ltd (500389)
- Consolidated list of affected securities is now empty (Nil)
- Changes effective from January 28, 2026
- Part of BSE's ongoing monitoring of social media activities affecting market integrity

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine update to the existing surveillance framework for dealing with unsolicited videos and messages circulated on social media platforms.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance actions required from market participants. This is an informational update reflecting the current status of surveillance measures.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: January 28, 2026 - Changes to surveillance list take effect

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. The removal of Silverline Technologies from the surveillance list is positive for that specific scrip, potentially restoring normal trading conditions. With no new additions and an empty consolidated list, there is no negative impact on any securities.

**Operational Impact**: Nil. No operational changes required from brokers, trading members, or listed companies.

**Investor Impact**: Positive for Silverline Technologies shareholders as surveillance restrictions are lifted. No impact on other investors as no new securities are affected.
