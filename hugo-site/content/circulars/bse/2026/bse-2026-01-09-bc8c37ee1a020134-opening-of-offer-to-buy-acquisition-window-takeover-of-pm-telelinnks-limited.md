## Summary

BSE has announced the opening of an Offer to Buy (Acquisition Window) for P.M. Telelinnks Limited as part of a takeover process. A Letter of Offer will be sent to all public shareholders of the company. This is an important document requiring immediate attention from shareholders.

## Key Points

- Letter of Offer will be sent to all public shareholders of P.M. Telelinnks Limited
- This pertains to an acquisition window under takeover regulations
- Shareholders requiring clarifications should consult their stockbroker, investment consultant, or the Manager/Registrar to the Offer
- Shareholders who have recently sold their equity shares should hand over the Letter of Offer and accompanying forms to the new owners
- Document is marked as important and requires immediate attention

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a disclosure regarding a corporate action (takeover offer) under existing SEBI takeover regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public shareholders will receive the Letter of Offer document
- Shareholders who sold shares recently must forward the Letter of Offer to new shareholders
- Shareholders seeking clarifications should contact appropriate advisors or the Offer Manager/Registrar

## Important Dates

Specific dates for the offer period are not provided in the available excerpt. These details would be contained in the full Letter of Offer document.

**High Impact on P.M. Telelinnks Limited Shareholders:**
- Direct impact on all public shareholders who will have the opportunity to tender their shares
- Shareholders need to evaluate the offer terms and make informed decisions
- Recent sellers of shares need to ensure proper communication with buyers
- May affect trading activity and price of the stock during the offer period
