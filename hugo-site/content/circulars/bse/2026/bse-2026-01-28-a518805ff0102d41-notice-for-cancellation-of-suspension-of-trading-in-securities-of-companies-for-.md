## Summary

BSE has issued a notice canceling the suspension of trading in securities of Benara Bearings and Pistons Ltd (scrip code 541178). This notice is issued in continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20251230-27 dated December 30, 2025. The company will not be suspended and normal trading will resume from January 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension for Benara Bearings and Pistons Ltd (scrip code 541178) is being canceled
- The company has addressed non-compliances with SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015
- This is a follow-up to previous Exchange Notice No. 20251230-27 dated December 30, 2025
- All market participants are notified of this change

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice represents the reversal of a previously imposed trading suspension due to the company's return to compliance with existing SEBI LODR Regulations 2015.

## Compliance Requirements

The company (Benara Bearings and Pistons Ltd) has successfully met the compliance requirements under SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015 that were previously not fulfilled, leading to the cancellation of the suspension.

Market participants should note this change in the trading status of the security.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 28, 2026
- **Effective Date**: January 30, 2026 - Trading suspension will be canceled and normal trading will resume
- **Reference Date**: December 30, 2025 - Date of original suspension notice (Notice No. 20251230-27)

## Impact Assessment

**Positive Impact**: This is a favorable development for Benara Bearings and Pistons Ltd and its shareholders. The cancellation of suspension indicates:

- The company has rectified its non-compliance issues
- Shareholders will regain ability to trade the stock on the exchange
- Normal price discovery and liquidity will resume
- Enhanced investor confidence due to regulatory compliance

**Market Impact**: Limited to the specific company and its stakeholders. Investors holding this stock can now execute trades starting January 30, 2026.
