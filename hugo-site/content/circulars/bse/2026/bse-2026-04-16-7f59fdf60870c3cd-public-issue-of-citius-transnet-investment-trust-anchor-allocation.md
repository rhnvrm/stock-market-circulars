## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that under the Anchor Investors (AIs) portion of the Public Issue of CITIUS TRANSNET INVESTMENT TRUST, a total of 4,97,24,850 units were subscribed on April 16, 2026, at a price of Rs. 100 per unit. This notice was issued by the Listing Business Relationship Department.

## Key Points

- 4,97,24,850 units subscribed by Anchor Investors in the Public Issue of CITIUS TRANSNET INVESTMENT TRUST
- Subscription price: Rs. 100 per unit
- Anchor investor allocation completed on April 16, 2026
- Notice issued under Notice No. 20260416-43 by Vice President Mangesh Tayde
- An announcement dated April 16, 2026, issued by the company is enclosed as attachment

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an informational notice regarding anchor investor subscription in a public issue.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance action is required from trading members. The notice serves as an information update regarding the anchor investor allocation in the CITIUS TRANSNET INVESTMENT TRUST public issue.

## Important Dates

- **April 16, 2026**: Anchor investor subscription date and notice issuance date

## Impact Assessment

This notice has limited direct market impact as it is an informational update on anchor investor participation in the CITIUS TRANSNET INVESTMENT TRUST IPO. The anchor allocation of approximately 4.97 crore units at Rs. 100 each indicates significant institutional interest, which may signal positive sentiment for the broader public issue subscription period. Trading members are advised to note this for client advisory purposes.
