## Summary

BSE's surveillance department (DOSS) issued this notice after observing significant price and volume movement in six listed equity scrips. The exchange wrote to all six companies seeking relevant information for the benefit of trading members and investors. As of this notice, three companies have submitted replies, which are attached to the circular, while replies from three other companies remain awaited.

## Key Points

- BSE identified unusual price/volume activity in six equity scrips and sought clarifications from the respective companies.
- Replies received from: Noida Toll Bridge Company Ltd (BSE: 532481), Genesys International Corporation Ltd (BSE: 506109), and Containerway International Ltd (BSE: 540597).
- Replies still awaited from: ZR2 Bioenergy Ltd (BSE: 506640), Elango Industries Ltd (BSE: 513452), and Accord Transformer & Switchgear Ltd (BSE: 544710).
- The notice is issued under the Surveillance (DOSS) department, categorised as Company Related, Equity segment.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this notice. It is an informational surveillance communication disclosing the status of clarification requests.

## Compliance Requirements

- ZR2 Bioenergy Ltd, Elango Industries Ltd, and Accord Transformer & Switchgear Ltd are expected to submit their clarifications to BSE promptly.
- Trading members and investors are advised to review the attached replies from the three responding companies before making trading decisions in these scrips.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 22 May 2026
- No specific deadline mentioned for pending replies.

## Impact Assessment

The immediate market impact is informational. Companies that have replied allow investors to make more informed decisions regarding those scrips. The three companies with pending replies (ZR2 Bioenergy, Elango Industries, Accord Transformer & Switchgear) remain under active surveillance attention, which may signal continued regulatory scrutiny and potential for further action if replies are not forthcoming or if price/volume anomalies persist.
