## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260610-2 dated June 10, 2026, informing trading members about the availability of penalty reports for modification of client codes in non-institutional trades across all segments.

## Key Points

- Penalty reports for client code modifications in non-institutional trades are available for download.
- The notice covers all segments of BSE.
- Issued by the Trading Operations department under the Compliance category.
- No attachments accompany this notice.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine operational notice for periodic penalty report dissemination.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to download and review the penalty report for client code modifications in non-institutional trades. Members who have incurred penalties for client code modifications should act accordingly based on the report details.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: June 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This notice has limited market impact and is directed at trading members who have modified client codes in non-institutional trades. It is a standard compliance communication to ensure members are aware of penalties levied for such modifications, promoting discipline in client code management across all trading segments.
