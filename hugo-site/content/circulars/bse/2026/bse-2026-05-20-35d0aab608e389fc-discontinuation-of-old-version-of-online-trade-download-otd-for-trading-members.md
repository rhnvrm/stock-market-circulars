## Summary

BSE has announced the discontinuation of all old versions (up to version 1.9) of the Online Trade Download (OTD) application effective June 1, 2026. The new version 3.01 is already live in production across all segments and will become mandatory. Trading members must upgrade before May 29, 2026, after which old versions will cease to function.

## Key Points

- New OTD version 3.01 is live in production for all segments: Equity, Equity Derivatives, Currency Derivatives, Commodity Derivatives, and EGR
- All old OTD versions up to version 1.9 will be discontinued from Monday, June 1, 2026
- Old versions will continue to function only until Friday, May 29, 2026
- Trading members who have not yet upgraded must contact BSE helpdesk immediately
- This notice follows earlier circulars: 20260206-37 (Feb 6), 20260213-36 (Feb 13), and 20260505-4 (May 5)

## Regulatory Changes

The Exchange is making OTD version 3.01 mandatory for all trading members. All prior versions (up to v1.9) will be formally discontinued and rendered non-functional from June 1, 2026.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members still using old OTD versions must contact BSE helpdesk to download and enable the new version 3.01
- Members should obtain updated connectivity details from the helpdesk
- Upgrade must be completed before May 29, 2026 to avoid disruption
- Contact helpdesk: 022-45720400/600 or 022-69158500 or email bsehelp@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **May 29, 2026 (Friday):** Last day old OTD versions (up to v1.9) will function
- **June 1, 2026 (Monday):** Old OTD versions officially discontinued; new version 3.01 becomes mandatory

## Impact Assessment

Trading members who fail to upgrade before May 29, 2026 will lose the ability to download online trade files, potentially disrupting trade reconciliation and back-office operations. The impact is operational and affects all trading member firms across all segments. No market-wide trading rules or investor-facing regulations are changed. Members should treat this as an urgent IT action item.
