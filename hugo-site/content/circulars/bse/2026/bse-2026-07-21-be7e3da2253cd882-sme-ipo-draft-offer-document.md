## Summary

BSE has notified that the Draft Offer Document of Apar Advertisers Limited, filed on July 17, 2026, containing disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations, has been made available on the SME website of the Stock Exchange for a period of at least 21 days to seek public comments.

## Key Points

- The Draft Offer Document pertains to the proposed SME IPO of Apar Advertisers Limited.
- The document was filed with BSE on July 17, 2026.
- It is hosted on the SME public issues portal for public review: https://www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public comments on the draft offer document are invited within 21 days from the date of this notice.
- Comments can be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com.
- The notice was issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, DCS-Listing department, SME segment.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a procedural disclosure under Schedule VI of the SEBI ICDR Regulations for public review of a draft SME IPO offer document, not a change in regulation.

## Compliance Requirements

- Interested parties/public must submit comments, if any, on the Draft Offer Document within 21 days of the notice date (by approximately August 11, 2026) to equity.ipo@bseindia.com.
- No compliance action required from listed companies or trading members.

## Important Dates

- Draft Offer Document filed: July 17, 2026
- Notice date: July 21, 2026
- Public comment period: 21 days from notice date (approximately until August 11, 2026)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine, procedural notice related to the SME IPO process for Apar Advertisers Limited and does not affect existing listed securities or trading activity. Impact is limited to prospective investors and stakeholders tracking the upcoming SME IPO pipeline.
