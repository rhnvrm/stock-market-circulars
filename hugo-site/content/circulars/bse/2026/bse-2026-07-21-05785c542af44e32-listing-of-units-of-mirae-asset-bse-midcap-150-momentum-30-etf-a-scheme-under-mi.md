## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that units of Mirae Asset BSE Midcap 150 Momentum 30 ETF, an open-ended scheme of Mirae Asset Mutual Fund that replicates/tracks the BSE Midcap 150 Momentum 30 Total Return Index, will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange under Group "B" with effect from Wednesday, July 22, 2026.

## Key Points

- Security: Units of Mirae Asset BSE Midcap 150 Momentum 30 ETF
- Number of units: 57,47,986
- Scrip Code: 544830
- Group: B
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Face Value: Rs. 10.00 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 12.62 per unit
- Date of Allotment: 16/07/2026
- Scrip ID / Abbreviated name on BOLT System: MOMMIDCAP
- ISIN: INF769K01RC0
- Fund House: Mirae Asset Mutual Fund; Investment Manager: Mirae Asset Investment Managers (India) Private Limited
- Registrar and Transfer Agent: KFin Technologies Limited
- Market Makers include Mirae Asset Capital Market (India) Private Limited, Kanjalochana Finserve Private Limited, East India Securities Limited, Parwati Capital Markets Private Limited, Vaibhav Stock & Derivatives Broking Private Limited, IRage Broking Services LLP, Junomoneta Finsol Pvt Ltd, Motilal Oswal Financial Services Limited, and Obsidian Investment Pvt Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is an implementation of SEBI guidelines dated 16th February, 2000 mandating that mutual fund units be traded only in dematerialized form.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must ensure trades in this scrip are executed in dematerialized form and in the specified unit market lot (i.e., one unit).

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: 16 July 2026
- Effective listing and trading date: 22 July 2026 (Wednesday)
- Notice date: 21 July 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notice enabling trading of a new ETF product on BSE. It provides trading members and investors access to a new passive investment vehicle tracking the BSE Midcap 150 Momentum 30 Total Return Index, with limited direct market-wide impact beyond expanding the exchange's product offerings.
