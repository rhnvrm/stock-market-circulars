## Summary

BSE has issued a notice informing trading members that ROLEX RINGS LIMITED has fixed 3 July 2026 as the Record Date for determining shareholder entitlement and eligibility to participate in the company's tender offer (buyback) of equity shares.

## Key Points

- Notice pertains to dematerialised securities in the Rolling Settlement Segment.
- Company: ROLEX RINGS LIMITED (BSE Code: 543325).
- Record Date: 03/07/2026.
- Purpose: To determine the entitlement and names of equity shareholders eligible to participate in the buyback offer.
- Ex-entitlement basis from date and settlement number: 03/07/2026, DR-664/2026-2027.
- No attachments were included with this notice.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine corporate action notification issued under BSE's Listing Operations department, not a regulatory or policy change.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are required to take note of the record date and ensure appropriate handling of trades and settlements involving Rolex Rings Limited shares around the ex-entitlement date to ensure buyers and sellers are correctly reflected as eligible or ineligible participants in the buyback.

## Important Dates

- Record Date: 3 July 2026
- Ex-entitlement basis effective from: 3 July 2026 (Settlement No. DR-664/2026-2027)

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to shareholders and traders of Rolex Rings Limited, who need to ensure their trades settle appropriately to be recognized as eligible participants in the buyback. Trading members must ensure back-office and settlement systems correctly apply the ex-entitlement basis from the record date. No broader market-wide impact is expected.
