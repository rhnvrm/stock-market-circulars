## Summary

BSE has announced the availability of ARUDHA HYBRID LONG - SHORT FUND for ongoing transactions on the BSE StAR MF (Mutual Fund) Platform. The fund offers 34 different plan options across Regular and Direct plans with various dividend (IDCW) frequency options including Growth, Daily, Weekly, Fortnightly, Monthly, Quarterly, Half Yearly, Annual, and Periodic options. The tentative allotment date for the schemes is January 28, 2026.

## Key Points

- Fund Category: Hybrid (C) - Long Short Fund
- Total Plans Available: 34 (17 Regular Plans + 17 Direct Plans)
- Dividend Options: Growth, Daily IDCW, Weekly IDCW, Fortnightly IDCW, Monthly IDCW, Quarterly IDCW, Half Yearly IDCW, Annual IDCW, and Periodic IDCW
- Each dividend option available in both Payout and Reinvestment modes
- All plans have unique ISIN codes ranging from INF194K30010 to INF194K30341
- Tentative allotment date: January 28, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements. Market participants and investors can now transact in these schemes through the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Important Dates

- **Tentative Allotment Date**: January 28, 2026
- **Availability Date**: February 3, 2026 (circular issuance date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine mutual fund listing that expands investment options for investors on the BSE platform.

**Investor Impact**: Provides investors access to a hybrid long-short fund strategy through BSE's StAR MF Platform with multiple plan options to suit different investment preferences and dividend payout requirements.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Standard addition to the mutual fund trading platform with no changes to existing processes or systems.
