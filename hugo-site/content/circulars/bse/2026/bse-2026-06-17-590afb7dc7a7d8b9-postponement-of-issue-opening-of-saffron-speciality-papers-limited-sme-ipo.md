## Summary

BSE has issued Notice No. 20260617-18 dated June 17, 2026, announcing the postponement of the issue opening for Saffron Speciality Papers Limited's SME IPO. The notice was issued by the DCS-Listing department under the SME segment.

## Key Points

- The issue opening for Saffron Speciality Papers Limited's SME IPO has been postponed.
- Notice issued by BSE's DCS-Listing department under the SME segment.
- Notice Number: 20260617-18, dated June 17, 2026.
- No attachments were included with this notice.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes indicated. This is an operational notice informing market participants of a scheduling change to the IPO issue opening.

## Compliance Requirements

Investors and intermediaries involved in the Saffron Speciality Papers Limited SME IPO should note the postponement and await a revised issue opening date from BSE and the company.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: June 17, 2026
- Revised issue opening date: To be announced (original opening date postponed as of this notice)

## Impact Assessment

The postponement affects prospective retail and institutional investors looking to subscribe to the Saffron Speciality Papers Limited SME IPO. Capital raising plans for the company are delayed. Impact is limited in scope to this single SME issuer and its stakeholders; broader market impact is minimal.
