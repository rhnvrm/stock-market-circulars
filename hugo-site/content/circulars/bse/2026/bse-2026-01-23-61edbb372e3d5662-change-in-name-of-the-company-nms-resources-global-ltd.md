## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs, through the Registrar of Companies (ROC Delhi), has issued a Certificate of Incorporation for the name change of NMS RESOURCES GLOBAL LIMITED to NMS GLOBAL LIMITED. The certificate was issued on October 31, 2023, and the name change is effective from that date. The company, originally incorporated as IFM IMPEX GLOBAL LIMITED, holds CIN L74110DL1986PLC025457 and is a company limited by shares.

## Key Points

- Company name changed from NMS RESOURCES GLOBAL LIMITED to NMS GLOBAL LIMITED
- Certificate issued by Registrar of Companies, ROC Delhi on October 31, 2023
- Corporate Identification Number (CIN): L74110DL1986PLC025457
- Company originally incorporated as IFM IMPEX GLOBAL LIMITED
- Certificate approved and digitally signed by Banwari Sharma, Registrar of Companies
- Registered address: H.No. 48, Hasanpur, I.P. Extension, East Delhi-110092, Delhi, India

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action pursuant to the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014, Rule 29.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display both old and new names for a period of two years at all places where it is required to display its name as per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- The name change does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders
- All stakeholders are advised to verify the latest company status and view public documents on www.mca.gov.in/MCA21

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date of Name Change**: October 31, 2023
- **Certificate Issuance Date**: October 31, 2023
- **Dual Name Display Period**: Two years from October 31, 2023

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative corporate action with minimal market impact. The name change does not affect the company's operations, financial position, or shareholder rights and liabilities. Stakeholders should note the new name for future correspondence and documentation purposes. The requirement to display both names for two years ensures continuity and prevents confusion during the transition period.
