## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document of Lorven International Limited, filed on February 25, 2026, on its SME platform. The document contains all disclosures as required under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and will remain available for at least 21 days to solicit public comments.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Lorven International Limited on February 25, 2026
- Document complies with Schedule VI of the ICDR (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations
- Hosted on BSE SME website for a minimum period of 21 days
- Public comments are invited within 21 days from the date of notice (by March 19, 2026)
- Comments to be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This notice is procedural, fulfilling the mandatory public disclosure requirement under Schedule VI of the SEBI ICDR Regulations prior to an SME IPO.

## Compliance Requirements

- Interested parties and the public may review the Draft Offer Document on the BSE SME website
- Any comments or objections must be submitted within 21 days from February 26, 2026
- Comments to be sent to: equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Filing Date:** February 25, 2026
- **Notice Date:** February 26, 2026
- **Public Comment Deadline:** March 19, 2026 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited broader market impact. It is a standard procedural disclosure for Lorven International Limited's upcoming SME IPO on BSE. Investors and analysts interested in the SME segment may review the draft prospectus. No immediate trading or compliance obligations are imposed on existing market participants.
