## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that 2,57,28,500 new equity shares of Rs. 2/- each of Sterlite Technologies Limited (Scrip Code: 532374, ISIN: INE089C01029), allotted to Qualified Institutional Buyers (QIBs) pursuant to a Qualified Institutional Placement (QIP), are listed and admitted for trading on the Exchange with effect from Friday, July 3, 2026.

## Key Points

- 2,57,28,500 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each allotted via QIP
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company
- Date of allotment: July 1, 2026
- Issue price: Rs. 583.01/- per share
- Distinctive numbers: 497034636 to 522763135
- Listing and trading effective from Friday, July 3, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is a routine listing notice under BSE Listing Operations for securities issued via QIP.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific action required from trading members; the notice is informational. Queries may be directed to Ms. Sahana Shetty at BSE (Tel: 022 2272 8600).

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: July 1, 2026
- Listing/Trading Effective Date: July 3, 2026 (Friday)
- Notice Date: July 2, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds approximately 2.57 crore new equity shares to Sterlite Technologies' tradable float, issued at Rs. 583.01/- per share via QIP. This increases the company's share capital and may have a modest dilutive effect on existing shareholders, while also indicating fresh capital raised from institutional investors, which could be used for growth, debt reduction, or working capital purposes.
