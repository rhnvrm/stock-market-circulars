## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in 7.25% Non-Convertible Debentures (ISIN: INE191B07162, Code: 960491) of Welspun Corp Limited effective February 2, 2026. The suspension is due to the company setting a record date for redemption of debentures and interest payment. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension effective from February 2, 2026
- Applies to WCL-7.25%-16-2-26-PVT debentures (Code: 960491)
- ISIN: INE191B07162
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Notice issued under DR-811/2025-2026
- Trading members must cease all dealings in these securities

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard suspension procedure for debenture redemption as per existing corporate action guidelines.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must immediately stop dealing in the specified debentures from February 2, 2026
- No buy or sell transactions permitted in WCL-7.25%-16-2-26-PVT debentures
- Trading members must inform their clients holding these debentures about the suspension
- Members should ensure compliance with the no-dealings directive

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: January 22, 2026
- **Record Date**: February 2, 2026
- **No Dealings From**: February 2, 2026
- **Purpose Date**: Redemption and interest payment on February 2, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: High - Complete trading halt in these specific debentures affects liquidity and trading activities. Debenture holders cannot exit positions through secondary market from the suspension date.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to block trading in this security (Code: 960491). Clients holding these debentures will receive redemption proceeds and final interest payment directly from the company as per record date eligibility.

**Investor Impact**: Investors holding these debentures as of record date will receive redemption amount plus accrued interest. No trading opportunity available from February 2, 2026 onwards.
