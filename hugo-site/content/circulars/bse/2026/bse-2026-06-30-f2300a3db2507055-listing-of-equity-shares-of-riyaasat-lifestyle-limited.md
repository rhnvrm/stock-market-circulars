## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that the equity shares of Riyaasat Lifestyle Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course under the SME segment. The scrip details have been assigned, but the exact listing date will be communicated via a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Riyaasat Lifestyle Limited equity shares approved for listing on BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544804, Trading Symbol: RIYAASAT, ISIN: INE0KYI01012
- Exact listing date to be announced through a subsequent notice
- Notice issued by DCS-Listing department under the Company Related category

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing admission notice under BSE's existing listing framework for SME companies.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to take note of the upcoming listing. No immediate compliance action is required pending the separate notice confirming the listing date and security details.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: June 30, 2026
- Listing Date: To be announced via a separate notice

## Impact Assessment

Low market-wide impact. This notice pertains solely to the upcoming listing of a single SME company (Riyaasat Lifestyle Limited). Investors and trading members interested in this scrip should await the follow-up notice confirming the listing date and any additional security details before initiating trades.
