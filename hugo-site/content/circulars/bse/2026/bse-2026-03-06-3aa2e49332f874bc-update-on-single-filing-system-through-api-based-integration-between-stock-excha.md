## Summary

BSE has extended its single filing system (via API-based integration between stock exchanges) to include XBRL filings for two additional events: **Prior Intimation for Board Meeting** (Regulation 29 of SEBI LODR) and **Closure of Trading Window** (SEBI PIT Regulations), both effective **March 07, 2026**. This builds on the framework established in Exchange Notice No. 20240930-60 dated September 30, 2024.

## Key Points

- Single filing system extended to XBRL filings for Prior Intimation for Board Meeting (Reg. 29) and Closure of Trading Window effective March 07, 2026.
- PDF filings for Regulation 29, Regulation 30 (SEBI LODR), and Trading Window disclosures must still be submitted separately to both exchanges until further notice.
- For all XBRL events now under single filing, listed entities should avoid duplicate submissions on both exchanges.
- If an exchange seeks clarification post-submission, the listed entity must respond only to the exchange that raised the query.
- The single filing system currently covers equity and equity+debt listed companies; availability for exclusively debt, REITs, and InvITs for some events will be communicated later.

## Regulatory Changes

- **Effective March 07, 2026:** Prior Intimation for Board Meeting (Reg. 29 SEBI LODR) and Closure of Trading Window (SEBI PIT Regulations) added to the XBRL single filing framework for equity and equity+debt listed companies.
- The full list of events now covered under single filing includes:
  - Integrated Filing – Governance (Reg. 13(3), 27(2) & 30): March 01, 2025
  - Meetings of Shareholders and Voting Results (Reg. 44(3)): December 28, 2024
  - Reconciliation of Share Capital Audit Report (Reg. 76): November 15, 2024
  - Annual Secretarial Compliance Report (Reg. 24A(2)): September 15, 2025
  - Integrated Filing – Financials (Reg. 23(9), 32(1), 33(3)): January 03, 2026
  - Fraud/Default/Arrest (Reg. 30, Schedule III Para A): February 21, 2026
  - Corporate Debt Restructuring; Resolution Plan/OTS (Reg. 30): February 21, 2026
  - Issue Summary Document (ISD) – Buyback: February 21, 2026
  - Prior Intimation for Board Meeting (Reg. 29): **March 07, 2026** *(new)*
  - Closure of Trading Window (SEBI PIT): **March 07, 2026** *(new)*
  - Corporate Governance Report (Reg. 27(2)): Merged with Integrated Filing – Governance per SEBI Circular dated December 31, 2024.
  - Investor Grievance Report (Reg. 13(3)): October 01, 2024

## Compliance Requirements

- Listed entities must file XBRL disclosures for the above-listed events through the single filing system (i.e., file once; it applies to both exchanges).
- **PDF filings** for Regulation 29 (Board Meeting), Regulation 30 (SEBI LODR), and Trading Window disclosures must continue to be submitted separately on both exchanges.
- Entities must not make duplicate XBRL filings on both exchanges for events covered under the single filing system.
- For any exchange-specific clarification requests, respond only to the exchange that raised the query.
- For queries, contact the Listing Compliance team via NEAPS (NEAPS > Help > Contact Us > Listing Compliance) or email takeover@nse.co.in.

## Impact Assessment

- **Operational Efficiency:** Listed companies (equity and equity+debt) benefit from reduced administrative burden — a single XBRL submission now suffices for an expanding set of regulatory disclosures across BSE and NSE.
- **PDF Filing Continuity:** No relief on PDF filing duplication yet for Regulation 29, Regulation 30, and Trading Window disclosures; these remain dual-exchange obligations.
- **Scope Limitation:** Exclusively debt-listed entities, REITs, and InvITs are not yet included for most events; timelines for their inclusion will be announced separately.
- **Risk of Non-Compliance:** Entities must be careful not to duplicate XBRL filings on both exchanges, and must remain responsive to exchange-specific clarification requests.
