## Summary

BSE has informed members that UTI Mutual Fund has revised the minimum application amounts for the **UTI Gold ETF Fund of Fund** scheme on the BSE StAR MF platform, effective **May 29, 2026**. The key change is a significant reduction in the minimum additional purchase, redemption, and SIP amounts.

## Key Points

- The revision applies exclusively to the **UTI Gold ETF Fund of Fund** scheme.
- Effective date: **May 29, 2026**.
- Minimum Additional Purchase Amount reduced from **Rs. 1,000** (in multiples of Re. 1) to **Rs. 25** (in multiples of Re. 1).
- Minimum Redemption Amount revised to **Rs. 25** and in multiples of Re. 1/- thereafter.
- Minimum SIP (Monthly Frequency) amount was **Rs. 500** and in multiples of Re. 1/-; the revised amount is as per the addendum attached.
- A Notice-cum-Addendum issued by UTI AMC is attached to the circular for complete details.

## Regulatory Changes

UTI AMC has issued a Notice-cum-Addendum revising the minimum application amounts for UTI Gold ETF Fund of Fund. The revised minimum additional purchase and redemption threshold is substantially lowered to Rs. 25, making the scheme more accessible to retail investors on the BSE StAR MF platform.

| Type of Purchase | Existing Minimum Amount | Revised Minimum Amount |
|---|---|---|
| Minimum Application Amount | Rs. 5,000 and in multiples of Rs. 1/- | As per addendum |
| Minimum Additional Purchase Amount | Rs. 1,000 and in multiples of Rs. 1/- | Rs. 25 and in multiples of Re. 1/- |
| Redemption Minimum Amount | Rs. 1,000 and in multiples of Rs. 1/- | Rs. 25 and in multiples of Re. 1/- |
| SIP (Monthly Frequency) | Rs. 500 and in multiples of Rs. 1/- | As per addendum |

## Compliance Requirements

- BSE members dealing in mutual fund transactions on StAR MF must note the updated minimum thresholds for order placement in UTI Gold ETF Fund of Fund.
- Members should refer to the attached UTI AMC Notice-cum-Addendum for the complete and authoritative revised schedule of minimum amounts.

## Important Dates

- **May 29, 2026**: Effective date of the revised minimum application amounts for UTI Gold ETF Fund of Fund.
- **May 30, 2026**: Date of BSE notice to members.

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact operational change. The reduction in minimum additional purchase and redemption amounts to Rs. 25 lowers the barrier for retail investors transacting in UTI Gold ETF Fund of Fund on BSE StAR MF. No system-level changes or member-side compliance actions are required beyond updating internal reference data for minimum order validation.
