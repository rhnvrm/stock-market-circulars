## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that additional securities issued by seven companies under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP/ESOS) are listed and admitted for trading on the Exchange effective Tuesday, May 5, 2026. All newly listed shares carry no lock-in restrictions (NA).

## Key Points

- Seven companies have listed new ESOP/ESOS shares effective May 5, 2026
- All newly issued shares are free from lock-in restrictions
- Total new shares range from 2,352 (Jaro Institute) to 1,815,944 (WeWork India)
- Companies span banking, finance, technology, and logistics sectors
- Notice issued by BSE Listing Operations department under Company Related / Equity segment

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice informing trading members of newly listed ESOP/ESOS securities.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for trading members beyond awareness of the new securities available for trading. The listing is administrative in nature.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** May 4, 2026
- **Effective Trading Date:** May 5, 2026 (Tuesday)
