## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Aarnav Fashions Limited (Scrip Code: 539562) will undergo a group change from Group X to Group B with effect from February 25, 2026. This reclassification indicates an improvement in the company's standing under BSE's group classification framework.

## Key Points

- Aarnav Fashions Limited (Scrip Code: 539562) is being moved from Group X to Group B
- The group change is effective from February 25, 2026
- Notice issued under Notice No. 20260224-16 dated February 24, 2026
- Issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President â€“ Listing Compliance & Operations, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

The company's equity shares will be reclassified from Group X to Group B on BSE's equity segment. Group X typically includes securities under enhanced surveillance or with specific compliance concerns, while Group B is a standard trading group. This change reflects an upgrade in the company's classification status.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are required to take note of the group change effective February 25, 2026
- No specific action is required from traders beyond updating their records to reflect the new group classification for Scrip Code 539562

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** February 24, 2026
- **Effective Date of Group Change:** February 25, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The reclassification from Group X to Group B is generally a positive development for Aarnav Fashions Limited and its investors. Group X securities often face additional surveillance measures, higher margin requirements, or trading restrictions. Moving to Group B may result in improved liquidity, reduced trading restrictions, and a more favourable perception among market participants. Traders and investors holding or tracking this scrip should update their systems accordingly before market open on February 25, 2026.
