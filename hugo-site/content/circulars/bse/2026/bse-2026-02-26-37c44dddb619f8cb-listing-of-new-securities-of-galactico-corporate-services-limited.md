## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that 3,42,72,605 new equity shares of Galactico Corporate Services Limited (Scrip Code: 542802) are listed and permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Friday, February 27, 2026. The shares were issued on a Rights Basis at a face value of Re. 1/- each at a premium of Rs. 0.75/- per share.

## Key Points

- **Company:** Galactico Corporate Services Limited
- **Scrip Code:** 542802
- **ISIN:** INE906Y01028
- **Number of New Shares:** 3,42,72,605 equity shares
- **Face Value:** Re. 1/- per share
- **Issue Premium:** Rs. 0.75/- per share (total issue price Rs. 1.75/- per share)
- **Issue Type:** Rights Basis
- **Distribution Numbers:** 149011331 to 183283935
- **Date of Allotment:** February 24, 2026
- **Trading Commencement:** February 27, 2026
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares of the company

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notice for securities issued under a rights issue.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed that the new securities are available for trading from February 27, 2026. Members should update their systems to reflect the new share listing under ISIN INE906Y01028.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** February 24, 2026
- **Listing/Trading Effective Date:** February 27, 2026 (Friday)

## Impact Assessment

The listing increases the tradable equity share count of Galactico Corporate Services Limited. The impact is limited to existing shareholders and participants in the rights issue. The issue price of Rs. 1.75/- per share (Re. 1/- face value + Rs. 0.75/- premium) reflects a modest premium. Market impact is expected to be minimal given the company-specific and routine nature of the rights issue listing.
