## Summary

BSE Limited has notified trading members that the equity shares of Crazy Snacks Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange (SME segment) in due course. The date of listing and further details of the securities will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company: CRAZY SNACKS LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544807
- Symbol: CRAZY
- ISIN: INE0RBX01014
- Segment: SME
- Department: DCS-Listing
- No attachments accompany this notice

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an administrative listing notice issued under BSE's standard listing process for SME companies.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should update their systems to recognize the new scrip code, symbol, and ISIN once the security becomes tradable. No specific action is required until the formal listing date notice is issued.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 2, 2026
- Actual listing date: To be announced via a separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This notice marks the formal admission of Crazy Snacks Limited's equity shares for trading on BSE's SME platform. Impact is limited to market participants tracking new SME listings and IPO allottees; no impact on existing listed securities or broader market operations.
