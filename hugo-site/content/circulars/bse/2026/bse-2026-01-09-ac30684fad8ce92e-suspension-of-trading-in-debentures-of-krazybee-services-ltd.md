## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in non-convertible debentures of KRAZYBEE SERVICES LTD effective January 15, 2026. The suspension is due to the company setting a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest on the same date. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the no-dealings date.

## Key Points

- Debenture series affected: KSL-11%-30-1-26-PVT (ISIN: INE07HK07767, Code: 975869)
- Record date: January 15, 2026
- No dealings from: January 15, 2026
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Circular reference: DR-799/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational circular for debt market participants.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any deals in the specified debentures of KRAZYBEE SERVICES LTD from January 15, 2026
- Members must take note of the suspension and ensure compliance with the no-dealings directive
- All debt market participants should update their systems to reflect the trading suspension

## Important Dates

- **January 9, 2026**: Circular issued
- **January 15, 2026**: Record date for redemption and interest payment
- **January 15, 2026**: No dealings/trading suspension commences

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and traders of this specific debenture series (INE07HK07767). This is a routine corporate action where trading is suspended to facilitate the redemption process and ensure proper identification of debenture holders entitled to interest payment and redemption proceeds. The suspension affects only the debt segment and has no broader market implications. Investors holding these debentures will receive their redemption proceeds and final interest payment as per the terms of issuance.
