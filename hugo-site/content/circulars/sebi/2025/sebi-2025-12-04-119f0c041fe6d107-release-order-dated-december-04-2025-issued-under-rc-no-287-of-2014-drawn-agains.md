## Summary

SEBI issued a release order dated December 04, 2025 under Recovery Certificate No. 287 of 2014 drawn against M/s Albeli Trading Private Limited in connection with the matter of Kolar Biotech Limited. This order relates to the recovery proceedings initiated by SEBI for enforcement of previous orders.

## Key Points

- Release order issued under RC No. 287 of 2014
- Entity involved: M/s Albeli Trading Private Limited
- Related matter: Kolar Biotech Limited
- Order date: December 04, 2025
- This is part of SEBI's recovery and enforcement proceedings

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an administrative order related to existing recovery proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

The release order indicates completion or partial satisfaction of recovery obligations under the specified recovery certificate. Entities involved must comply with any remaining conditions specified in the order.

## Important Dates

- **Order Date**: December 04, 2025
- **Recovery Certificate**: RC No. 287 of 2014

## Impact Assessment

This release order has limited market impact as it pertains to specific recovery proceedings against M/s Albeli Trading Private Limited. The order indicates progress in SEBI's enforcement and recovery mechanisms. Impact is primarily on the entities directly involved in the recovery proceedings related to Kolar Biotech Limited matter.
