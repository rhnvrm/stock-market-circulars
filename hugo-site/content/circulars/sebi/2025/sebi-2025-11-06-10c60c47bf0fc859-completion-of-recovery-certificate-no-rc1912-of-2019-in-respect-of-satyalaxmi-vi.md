## Summary

SEBI has issued a notice regarding the completion of recovery proceedings under Recovery Certificate No. RC1912 of 2019. The recovery certificate was issued against Satyalaxmi Vinimay Pvt. Ltd. (PAN: AATCS8589E) in connection with the matter of Svaraj Trading & Agencies Ltd.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate Number: RC1912 of 2019
- Entity: Satyalaxmi Vinimay Pvt. Ltd.
- PAN: AATCS8589E
- Related Matter: Svaraj Trading & Agencies Ltd.
- Status: Recovery proceedings completed
- Notice Date: November 6, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an enforcement completion notice.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. This notice indicates the conclusion of recovery proceedings against the named entity.

## Important Dates

- Notice Publication Date: November 6, 2025
- Recovery Certificate Issue Year: 2019

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine enforcement completion notice with minimal market impact. It serves as a public record that SEBI has concluded recovery proceedings under the specified recovery certificate. The notice is entity-specific and does not introduce new regulatory requirements or affect broader market participants.
