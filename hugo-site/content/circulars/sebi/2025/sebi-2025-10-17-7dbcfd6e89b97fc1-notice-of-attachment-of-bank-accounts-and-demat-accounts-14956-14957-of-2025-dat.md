## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Attachment of Bank Accounts and Demat Accounts (Notice Nos. 14956 & 14957 of 2025) dated October 17, 2025 against Ancient Education Pvt Ltd (PAN: AALCA2837R) under Recovery Certificate No. 8339 of 2024 in the matter of Superior Finlease Ltd. This enforcement action is part of SEBI's recovery proceedings to recover dues from the defaulter entity.

## Key Points

- Attachment notices issued: 14956 & 14957 of 2025
- Date of notice: October 17, 2025
- Defaulter entity: Ancient Education Pvt Ltd
- PAN of defaulter: AALCA2837R
- Related matter: Superior Finlease Ltd
- Recovery Certificate No.: 8339 of 2024
- Action: Attachment of bank accounts and demat accounts
- Authority: Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI recovery procedures and powers.

## Compliance Requirements

- Banks and depository participants holding accounts of Ancient Education Pvt Ltd (PAN: AALCA2837R) must comply with the attachment orders
- Affected accounts must be frozen and no debits or withdrawals permitted without SEBI authorization
- Financial institutions must cooperate with SEBI's recovery proceedings
- Ancient Education Pvt Ltd must settle outstanding dues as per Recovery Certificate No. 8339 of 2024

## Important Dates

- October 17, 2025: Date of attachment notice
- Recovery Certificate issued: 2024 (RC No. 8339/2024)

## Impact Assessment

**Entity-Specific Impact:**
- Ancient Education Pvt Ltd faces severe restrictions with bank and demat accounts frozen
- The entity's ability to conduct financial transactions is significantly curtailed
- This enforcement action signals SEBI's commitment to recovering dues in the Superior Finlease Ltd matter

**Market Impact:**
- Limited direct market impact as this targets a specific defaulter entity
- Demonstrates SEBI's enforcement capabilities and commitment to recovery proceedings
- Serves as a deterrent for other entities against non-compliance with SEBI orders

**Operational Impact:**
- Banks and depositories must implement account freezing procedures
- Enhanced due diligence required for transactions involving the defaulter entity
- Recovery proceedings will continue until outstanding amounts are settled
