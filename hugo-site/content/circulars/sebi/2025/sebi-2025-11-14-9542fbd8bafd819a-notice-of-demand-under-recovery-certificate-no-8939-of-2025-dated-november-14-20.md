## Summary

SEBI has issued Notice of Demand under Recovery Certificate No. 8939 of 2025 dated November 14, 2025 against Ashish P Shah (PAN: AJRPS7737C) in the matter of Svarnim Trade Udyog Limited. This recovery certificate represents enforcement action to recover outstanding dues owed to SEBI as a result of regulatory violations or non-compliance.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8939 of 2025 issued on November 14, 2025
- Notice issued against: Ashish P Shah (PAN: AJRPS7737C)
- Related entity: Svarnim Trade Udyog Limited
- Represents formal demand for recovery of dues through legal enforcement mechanisms

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI recovery procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Ashish P Shah is required to comply with the recovery certificate and pay outstanding dues as demanded
- Failure to comply may result in further enforcement actions including attachment of assets or other recovery proceedings
- The concerned party should immediately engage with SEBI recovery officials to settle outstanding amounts

## Important Dates

- **November 14, 2025**: Date of issuance of Recovery Certificate No. 8939 of 2025
- Payment deadline: As specified in the recovery certificate (refer to PDF for specific timeline)

**Individual Impact**: High - Recovery certificate against Ashish P Shah indicates significant financial liability and potential asset attachment if dues remain unpaid.

**Entity Impact**: High - Svarnim Trade Udyog Limited's association with this recovery action may affect its regulatory standing and reputation.

**Market Impact**: Medium - Recovery certificates signal regulatory enforcement and may affect investor confidence in the related entity.
