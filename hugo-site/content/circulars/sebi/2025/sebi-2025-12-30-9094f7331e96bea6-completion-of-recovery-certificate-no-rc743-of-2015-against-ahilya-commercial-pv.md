## Summary

SEBI has announced the completion of Recovery Certificate No. RC743 of 2015 issued against Ahilya Commercial Pvt. Ltd. (PAN: AACCA2226R) in the matter of Sarang Viniyog Ltd. This represents the closure of recovery proceedings that were initiated in 2015.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate Number: RC743 of 2015
- Defaulter Entity: Ahilya Commercial Pvt. Ltd.
- PAN of Defaulter: AACCA2226R
- Related Matter: Sarang Viniyog Ltd.
- Status: Completed
- Year of Initiation: 2015

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an administrative notification regarding the completion of existing recovery proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. This circular serves as a notification of completed recovery action against the specified entity.

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate Issue Year: 2015
- Completion Notification Date: December 2025

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains to the completion of specific recovery proceedings against Ahilya Commercial Pvt. Ltd. The completion indicates that SEBI has successfully concluded its recovery actions in this matter, which was part of enforcement proceedings related to Sarang Viniyog Ltd. This is primarily an administrative update with no broader regulatory or market implications.
