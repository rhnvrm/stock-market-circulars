## Summary

SEBI's Local Office at Indore is organizing an Investor Service Camp on December 16, 2025, to provide a single-window facility for various investor needs. The camp will be held in collaboration with major Market Infrastructure Institutions (BSE, NSE, CDSL, NSDL) and Registrar and Transfer Agents (KFIN Technologies Ltd., Alankit Assignments Ltd., Bigshare Services Pvt. Ltd., and MUFG Intime India Pvt Ltd.). The day-long event will offer dedicated service desks under one roof for investor-related services.

## Key Points

- Investor Service Camp on December 16, 2025, from 10 AM to 5 PM
- Location: Pravasa Hotels, Plot No.9, PU-3, Opposite C-21 Mall, AB Road Indore, Madhya Pradesh - 452010
- Organized by SEBI's Local Office at Indore
- Collaboration with BSE, NSE, CDSL, NSDL, and four RTAs (KFIN Technologies, Alankit Assignments, Bigshare Services, MUFG Intime India)
- Single-window facility for multiple investor services
- End-to-end assistance available for various investor needs

## Services Available

The camp will provide dedicated service desks offering assistance on:

- RTA related service requests for Shares and Mutual Funds
- KYC and Nominee updation
- Dematerialization of physical shares
- Transmission of shares
- Name correction/updation
- SCORES and SMART ODR portals related queries

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an informational announcement about an investor outreach initiative.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance requirements. This is a voluntary service camp for investors to resolve their grievances and service requests.

## Important Dates

- **December 16, 2025**: Investor Service Camp in Indore (10 AM to 5 PM)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: None. This is a local investor service initiative.

**Operational Impact**: Provides convenient access to investor services for residents of Indore and surrounding areas. Investors can address multiple service needs related to shares, mutual funds, demat accounts, and grievance redressal portals at a single location. This initiative aims to improve investor education and facilitate resolution of investor grievances through direct interaction with market infrastructure institutions and RTAs.
