## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand under Recovery Certificate No. 8945 of 2025 dated November 21, 2025 against Pankaj Rameshchandra Vyas (PAN: ADGPV4521L) and Vaishali Pankaj Vyas (PAN: AAPPV4036J) in connection with manipulation in the scrip of SecUR Credentials Limited. This notice is part of SEBI's enforcement proceedings to recover outstanding dues from the parties involved in market manipulation activities.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8945 of 2025 issued on November 21, 2025
- Notice issued against two individuals: Pankaj Rameshchandra Vyas and Vaishali Pankaj Vyas
- Matter relates to manipulation in SecUR Credentials Limited scrip
- Enforcement action for recovery of dues following adjudication or settlement proceedings

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI recovery framework.

## Compliance Requirements

The noticees (Pankaj Rameshchandra Vyas and Vaishali Pankaj Vyas) are required to:
- Comply with the demand notice within the specified timeline
- Pay the outstanding amount as per the recovery certificate
- Failure to comply may result in further recovery proceedings including attachment of assets

## Important Dates

- **November 21, 2025**: Date of Notice of Demand and Recovery Certificate issuance

## Impact Assessment

**For Noticees**: High impact as recovery proceedings can lead to asset attachment and further legal consequences if the demand is not met.

**For Market**: Low direct impact. This is an enforcement action against specific individuals and does not affect trading or operations of SecUR Credentials Limited or broader market participants.

**For SecUR Credentials Limited**: Minimal operational impact, though the company name is associated with the manipulation case which may have historical reputational considerations.
