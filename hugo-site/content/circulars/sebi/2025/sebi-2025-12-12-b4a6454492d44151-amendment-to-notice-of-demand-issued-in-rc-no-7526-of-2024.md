- Part of SEBI's enforcement and recovery actions
- Document published on December 12, 2025

This is an amendment to an existing enforcement action rather than a new regulatory change. It updates the terms of the demand notice in ongoing recovery proceedings.

The impact is limited to the specific entities involved in Recovery Certificate No. 7526 of 2024. This is a procedural amendment to ongoing enforcement proceedings and does not have broader market implications. The amendment likely corrects or updates details in the original demand notice.
