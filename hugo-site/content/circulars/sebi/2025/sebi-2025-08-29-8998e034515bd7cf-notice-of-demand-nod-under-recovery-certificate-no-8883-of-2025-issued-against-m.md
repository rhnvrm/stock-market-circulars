## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand under Recovery Certificate No. 8883 of 2025 against Mr. Jitendra Bharbhuja (PAN: EZSPB9777C), who operates as the proprietor of Maxx Innovation. The action is taken for providing unregistered Investment Advisory Services and Portfolio Manager Services through Maxx Innovation Growth and Maxx Innovation.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8883 of 2025 issued against Mr. Jitendra Bharbhuja
- PAN: EZSPB9777C
- Proprietor of Maxx Innovation
- Violation: Providing unregistered Investment Advisory Services and Portfolio Manager Services
- Entities involved: Maxx Innovation Growth and Maxx Innovation

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI regulations requiring registration for investment advisory and portfolio management services.

## Compliance Requirements

- Investment advisors must obtain proper registration from SEBI before providing services
- Portfolio managers must be registered with SEBI
- Entities providing such services without registration face recovery proceedings
- Payment of demanded amount as per the recovery certificate

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate issued: 2025
- Notice published: August 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This enforcement action reinforces SEBI's commitment to ensuring only registered entities provide investment advisory and portfolio management services. It serves as a deterrent for unregistered operators and protects investor interests. The action may impact other unregistered service providers operating in similar capacity.
