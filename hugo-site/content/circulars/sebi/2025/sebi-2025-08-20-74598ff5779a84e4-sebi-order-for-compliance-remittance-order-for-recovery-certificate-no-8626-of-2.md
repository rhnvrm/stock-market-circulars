## Summary

SEBI has issued a remittance order for Recovery Certificate No. 8626 of 2025 against Vaibhav Dhadda in connection with front running activities involving various funds of Fidelity Group. This represents an enforcement action to recover amounts determined in the front running case.

## Key Points

- Recovery certificate issued against Vaibhav Dhadda
- Case relates to front running activities involving Fidelity Group funds
- Recovery Certificate No. 8626 of 2025
- Remittance order for compliance with recovery proceedings

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is an enforcement action under existing regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Vaibhav Dhadda must comply with the remittance order
- Payment of amounts as determined in the recovery certificate
- Compliance with recovery proceedings as per SEBI enforcement mechanisms

## Important Dates

- Order Date: August 20, 2025
- Recovery Certificate No: 8626 of 2025

## Impact Assessment

This enforcement action demonstrates SEBI's continued focus on front running violations and recovery of amounts from violators. While specific to an individual case, it reinforces regulatory vigilance in fund management practices and market integrity measures.
