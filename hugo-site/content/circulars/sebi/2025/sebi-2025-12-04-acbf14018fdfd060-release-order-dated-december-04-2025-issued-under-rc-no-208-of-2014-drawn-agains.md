## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order dated December 04, 2025 under Recovery Certificate No. 208 of 2014 drawn against Mastermind Mercantile Co-Op Limited in connection with the matter of Adam Comsof Limited. This order relates to the release or compliance regarding recovery proceedings initiated by SEBI.

## Key Points

- Release Order issued under RC No. 208 of 2014
- Entity involved: Mastermind Mercantile Co-Op Limited
- Related to the matter of Adam Comsof Limited
- Order date: December 04, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a case-specific release order related to ongoing recovery proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

The release order pertains to compliance with recovery proceedings under the specific Recovery Certificate. Entities subject to the order must comply with the terms specified in the release order.

## Important Dates

- Order Date: December 04, 2025
- Recovery Certificate: No. 208 of 2014

## Impact Assessment

This release order has limited market impact as it relates to specific recovery proceedings against Mastermind Mercantile Co-Op Limited in connection with the Adam Comsof Limited matter. The order is administrative in nature and affects only the parties directly involved in the recovery proceedings.
