## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order dated December 18, 2025, in the matter of RC No.5481 of 2022 against Adam Comsof Ltd. The order pertains to non-obtaining of SCORES (SEBI Complaints Redress System) authentication, releasing the company from previously imposed directions.

## Key Points

- Release Order issued in RC No.5481 of 2022
- Matter concerns non-obtaining of SCORES authentication by Adam Comsof Ltd
- Order indicates resolution of the compliance issue
- Company has been released from the directions previously imposed

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a release order indicating compliance with existing requirements.

## Compliance Requirements

The release order suggests that Adam Comsof Ltd has now:
- Obtained the required SCORES authentication
- Met the compliance requirements that were subject to the original proceedings
- Fulfilled conditions for release from directions

## Important Dates

- **Release Order Date**: December 18, 2025
- **Original RC Number**: RC No.5481 of 2022

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a positive development for Adam Comsof Ltd as it indicates resolution of a compliance matter.

**Operational Impact**: The company is now released from any restrictions imposed under the original proceedings, allowing normal operations to continue without impediments related to this matter.

**Compliance Significance**: Demonstrates the company's correction of the SCORES authentication issue and return to compliance with SEBI requirements.
