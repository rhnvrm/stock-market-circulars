## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC8989 of 2025 containing a Notice of Demand against Nightangle Marcom Pvt Ltd, which has been amalgamated with Smile Suppliers Private Ltd (PAN: AAMCS2306N). This enforcement action is related to the matter of Thirani Projects Ltd and represents SEBI's efforts to recover dues from entities involved in regulatory violations.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate Number: RC8989 of 2025
- Entity: Nightangle Marcom Pvt Ltd (now amalgamated with Smile Suppliers Private Ltd)
- PAN of Successor Entity: AAMCS2306N
- Related Matter: Thirani Projects Ltd
- Document Type: Notice of Demand under recovery proceedings
- Enforcement action initiated through recovery certificate mechanism

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework changes. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI recovery and enforcement provisions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Smile Suppliers Private Ltd (as successor entity post-amalgamation) is required to comply with the Notice of Demand
- The entity must respond to SEBI's recovery proceedings
- Failure to comply may result in further enforcement action including attachment of assets or other recovery measures under SEBI Act
- The demand amount and payment timeline would be specified in the detailed certificate

## Important Dates

- Certificate Issue Date: December 31, 2025
- Specific payment deadlines and compliance dates would be detailed in the full recovery certificate document

## Impact Assessment

**For Smile Suppliers Private Ltd:** High impact as the successor entity bears the liabilities of Nightangle Marcom Pvt Ltd post-amalgamation. The company faces financial obligations and potential enforcement action.

**For Thirani Projects Ltd:** This recovery action is part of the broader enforcement case involving Thirani Projects Ltd, indicating ongoing regulatory scrutiny.

**Market Impact:** Highlights SEBI's continued enforcement efforts and ability to pursue liabilities even through corporate restructuring like amalgamations, reinforcing regulatory accountability.
