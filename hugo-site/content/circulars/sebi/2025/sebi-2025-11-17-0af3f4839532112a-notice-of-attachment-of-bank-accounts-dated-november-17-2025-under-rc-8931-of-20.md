## Summary

SEBI has issued a notice of attachment of bank accounts dated November 17, 2025, against Anup Karwa (PAN - BFNPK5368C) under Recovery Certificate No. 8931 of 2025. This action is related to recovery proceedings in the matter of Prakash Constrowell Limited, which has been renamed to Setubandhan Infrastructure Limited. The attachment order is part of SEBI's enforcement and recovery mechanism to collect outstanding dues.

## Key Points

- Notice issued against: Anup Karwa (PAN - BFNPK5368C)
- Recovery Certificate Number: RC 8931 of 2025
- Date of Notice: November 17, 2025
- Related Company: Prakash Constrowell Limited (now Setubandhan Infrastructure Limited)
- Action Type: Attachment of bank accounts
- Authority: Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI recovery provisions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Banks are required to attach the specified bank accounts of Anup Karwa as per the notice
- The individual is prohibited from operating the attached bank accounts
- Banks must remit the attached amounts to SEBI as per recovery certificate directions

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 17, 2025
