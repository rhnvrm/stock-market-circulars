## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC8983 of 2025 along with a Notice of Demand against Northstar Dealcomm Private Limited, which has been amalgamated with Moreplus Merchants Pvt Ltd (PAN: AAFCM6761L). This enforcement action is in connection with the matter of Thirani Projects Ltd and represents recovery proceedings initiated by the securities regulator.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate Number: RC8983 of 2025
- Entity Subject to Demand: Northstar Dealcomm Private Limited (now amalgamated with Moreplus Merchants Pvt Ltd)
- PAN of Successor Entity: AAFCM6761L
- Related Matter: Thirani Projects Ltd
- Document Type: Recovery Certificate and Notice of Demand
- Issuance Date: December 31, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI recovery and enforcement frameworks.

## Compliance Requirements

- The successor entity (Moreplus Merchants Pvt Ltd) must comply with the demand notice
- Outstanding amounts specified in the recovery certificate must be paid within stipulated timelines
- The entity is required to respond to SEBI's recovery proceedings
- Failure to comply may result in further enforcement actions including asset attachment and other recovery measures

## Important Dates

- **Certificate Issue Date**: December 31, 2025
- **Recovery Certificate Number**: RC8983 of 2025
- Payment deadlines and compliance timelines as specified in the detailed notice

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: High - This recovery action against an amalgamated entity demonstrates SEBI's continued enforcement efforts in the Thirani Projects Ltd matter. The action affects both the original entity (Northstar Dealcomm) and its successor entity (Moreplus Merchants Pvt Ltd).

**Operational Impact**: High - The successor entity faces significant financial obligations and potential asset recovery actions. The amalgamation does not relieve the combined entity of liabilities from the original entity.

**Enforcement Significance**: This action is part of broader enforcement proceedings related to Thirani Projects Ltd, indicating systemic issues that required regulatory intervention and recovery measures against multiple entities.
