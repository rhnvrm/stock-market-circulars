## Summary

SEBI has issued a completion notice for Recovery Certificate No. RC741 of 2015 against Ahilya Commercial Pvt. Ltd. (PAN: AACCA2226R) in relation to the matter of M. P. Investment and Consultancy Services Ltd. This document marks the conclusion of recovery proceedings initiated under the relevant recovery certificate.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate Number: RC741 of 2015
- Defaulter Entity: Ahilya Commercial Pvt. Ltd.
- PAN of Defaulter: AACCA2226R
- Related Matter: M. P. Investment and Consultancy Services Ltd.
- Status: Completion of recovery proceedings

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a completion notice for existing recovery proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. This document confirms the completion of recovery proceedings against the specified entity.

## Important Dates

- Completion Notice Date: December 30, 2025
- Original Recovery Certificate: RC741 of 2015

## Impact Assessment

This completion notice has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains to the conclusion of specific recovery proceedings against Ahilya Commercial Pvt. Ltd. The notice serves as a formal closure of the recovery certificate issued in 2015, indicating that SEBI's recovery actions in this matter have been completed.
