## Summary

SEBI has issued a release order for Recovery Certificate No. 4758 of 2022 against Mr. Ankur J. Chaturvedi in the matter of Brijlaxmi Leasing and Finance Limited. A release order typically indicates that the recovery proceedings have been satisfied or compliance has been achieved, allowing for the release of attached assets or closure of enforcement actions.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 4758 of 2022 issued against Mr. Ankur J. Chaturvedi
- Matter relates to Brijlaxmi Leasing and Finance Limited
- Release order issued, indicating compliance or satisfaction of recovery requirements
- Order published on October 27, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement-related release order.

## Compliance Requirements

This order pertains to specific recovery proceedings against Mr. Ankur J. Chaturvedi and does not impose general compliance requirements on market participants.

## Important Dates

- **Order Date**: October 27, 2025
- **Recovery Certificate**: No. 4758 of 2022

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is an individual enforcement matter with no direct market-wide implications.

**Operational Impact**: None for general market participants. The release order affects only the specific individual and entity mentioned in the recovery proceedings.

**Stakeholders Affected**: Mr. Ankur J. Chaturvedi and potentially Brijlaxmi Leasing and Finance Limited, depending on the nature of the recovery proceedings.
