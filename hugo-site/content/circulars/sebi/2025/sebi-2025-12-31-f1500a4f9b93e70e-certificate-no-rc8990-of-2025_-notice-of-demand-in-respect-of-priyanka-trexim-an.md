## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC8990 of 2025 as a Notice of Demand against Priyanka Trexim and Commerce Pvt Ltd, which has been amalgamated with Sutlej Sales Private Ltd (PAN: AADCS9410Q). This certificate pertains to recovery proceedings in the matter of Thirani Projects Ltd.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate Number: RC8990 of 2025
- Entity: Priyanka Trexim and Commerce Pvt Ltd (now amalgamated with Sutlej Sales Private Ltd)
- PAN of Successor Entity: AADCS9410Q
- Related Matter: Thirani Projects Ltd
- Action Type: Notice of Demand for recovery of outstanding dues
- Legal Status: Formal recovery certificate issued under SEBI enforcement provisions

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI recovery provisions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Sutlej Sales Private Ltd (as successor entity to Priyanka Trexim and Commerce Pvt Ltd) must comply with the demand notice
- Payment of outstanding dues as specified in the recovery certificate
- Cooperation with SEBI's recovery proceedings
- Failure to comply may result in further enforcement actions including asset attachment and recovery through designated authorities

## Important Dates

- Certificate Issue Date: December 31, 2025
- Compliance deadline and specific payment terms would be detailed in the certificate document

## Impact Assessment

**Financial Impact:** High - Recovery certificate indicates significant outstanding dues owed to SEBI or affected parties in the Thirani Projects Ltd matter.

**Legal Impact:** High - Formal recovery proceedings initiated. Non-compliance can lead to coercive recovery measures including attachment of assets, bank accounts, and other properties.

**Corporate Impact:** The amalgamation of Priyanka Trexim and Commerce Pvt Ltd with Sutlej Sales Private Ltd means the successor entity inherits the liabilities and obligations under this recovery certificate.

**Market Impact:** Enforcement action demonstrates SEBI's continued focus on recovery of dues in cases involving market violations and investor protection matters related to listed entities like Thirani Projects Ltd.
