## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand dated November 21, 2025, under Recovery Certificate No. 8950 of 2025 against Deepika Jain (PAN: ACEPJ7192A) in connection with the matter of Rajasthan Tube Manufacturing Company Limited. This notice is part of SEBI's recovery proceedings for enforcement of regulatory orders.

## Key Points

- Notice issued against Deepika Jain (PAN: ACEPJ7192A)
- Recovery Certificate No. 8950 of 2025
- Related to Rajasthan Tube Manufacturing Company Limited
- Date of Notice: November 21, 2025
- Enforcement action under SEBI recovery proceedings

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI recovery framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Deepika Jain is required to comply with the demand specified in the Recovery Certificate
- Payment of outstanding amounts as determined by SEBI
- Failure to comply may result in further recovery proceedings including attachment of assets

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 21, 2025
- **Payment Deadline**: As specified in the Recovery Certificate

## Impact Assessment

**Individual Impact**: High - The named individual faces recovery proceedings which may include penalties, attachment of assets, and potential legal consequences for non-compliance.

**Company Impact**: Moderate - This action against an individual associated with Rajasthan Tube Manufacturing Company Limited may indicate broader compliance issues with the company.

**Market Impact**: Low to Moderate - Recovery proceedings against individuals associated with listed companies may affect investor confidence in the concerned entity.
