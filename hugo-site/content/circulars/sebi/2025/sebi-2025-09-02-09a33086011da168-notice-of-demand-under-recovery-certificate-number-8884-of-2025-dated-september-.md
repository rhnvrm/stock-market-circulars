## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand under Recovery Certificate number 8884 of 2025 dated September 02, 2025, to Mrinal Verma (PAN: ADDPV0274F) in the matter of dealing in illiquid stock options at BSE. This is an enforcement action for recovery of penalties.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8884 of 2025 issued on September 02, 2025
- Defaulter: Mrinal Verma (PAN: ADDPV0274F)
- Matter relates to dealing in illiquid stock options at BSE
- Document represents an enforcement recovery action by SEBI

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes indicated. This is an individual enforcement action.

## Compliance Requirements

The named individual (Mrinal Verma) is required to comply with the recovery certificate demands as per SEBI enforcement procedures.

## Important Dates

- Certificate Issue Date: September 02, 2025
- Further compliance deadlines would be specified in the recovery certificate details

## Impact Assessment

This is an individual enforcement action with limited market impact. It serves as a reminder of SEBI's enforcement actions against violations related to trading in illiquid stock options. Market participants should ensure compliance with regulations governing options trading, particularly in illiquid securities.
