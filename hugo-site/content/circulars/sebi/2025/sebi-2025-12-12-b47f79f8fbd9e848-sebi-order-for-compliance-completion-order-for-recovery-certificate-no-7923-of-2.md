## Summary

SEBI has issued a completion order for Recovery Certificate No. 7923 of 2024 against Sanjay Soni in the matter of Timbor Home Limited. This order marks the conclusion of recovery proceedings initiated under SEBI's enforcement powers.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 7923 of 2024 proceedings completed
- Order pertains to Sanjay Soni
- Related to Timbor Home Limited matter
- Completion order signifies closure of recovery process

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action completion order.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. This order documents the completion of existing recovery proceedings.

## Important Dates

- Order Date: December 12, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This completion order affects only the specific individual named in the recovery proceedings. It indicates that SEBI has completed its recovery process for the amounts due under Recovery Certificate No. 7923 of 2024. No broader market or regulatory implications.
