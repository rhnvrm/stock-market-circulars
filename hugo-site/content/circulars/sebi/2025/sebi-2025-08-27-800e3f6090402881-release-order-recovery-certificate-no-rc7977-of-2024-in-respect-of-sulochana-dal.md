## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order for Recovery Certificate No. RC7977 of 2024 concerning Sulochana Dalmia (PAN: AAXPD9770F) in relation to illiquid stock options violations. This order concludes the recovery proceedings against the individual.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC7977 of 2024 has been released
- Pertains to Sulochana Dalmia with PAN: AAXPD9770F
- Related to illiquid stock options matter
- Concludes enforcement proceedings against the individual

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action completion.

## Compliance Requirements

No additional compliance requirements for market participants. This relates to completion of individual enforcement proceedings.

## Important Dates

- Release Order Date: August 27, 2025
- Original Recovery Certificate: RC7977 of 2024

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this relates to individual enforcement completion. No systemic implications for market operations or other participants.
