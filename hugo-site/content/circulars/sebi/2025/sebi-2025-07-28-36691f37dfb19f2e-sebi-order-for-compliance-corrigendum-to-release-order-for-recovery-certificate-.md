## Summary

SEBI has issued a corrigendum to correct details in the release order for Recovery Certificate No. 8728 of 2025 against Bhogilal Kubadia (legal heirs and husband of Vimlaben Kubadia) in the matter of illiquid stock options at BSE.

## Key Points

- Corrigendum issued for Recovery Certificate No. 8728 of 2025
- Related to Bhogilal Kubadia and legal heirs of Vimlaben Kubadia
- Matter concerns illiquid stock options at BSE
- Administrative correction to existing enforcement proceedings

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - administrative correction only.

No new compliance requirements introduced.
