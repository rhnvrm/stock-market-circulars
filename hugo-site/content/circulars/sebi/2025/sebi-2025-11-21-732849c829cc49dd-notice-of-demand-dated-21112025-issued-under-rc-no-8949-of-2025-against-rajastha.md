## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand dated November 21, 2025, under Recovery Certificate No. 8949 of 2025 against Rajasthan Tube Manufacturing Company Limited (PAN: AABCR1642P). This notice represents formal recovery proceedings initiated by SEBI to recover outstanding dues from the company.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8949 of 2025 issued against Rajasthan Tube Manufacturing Company Limited
- PAN of the entity: AABCR1642P
- Notice issued on November 21, 2025
- Formal recovery proceedings under SEBI enforcement framework

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI recovery provisions.

## Compliance Requirements

- The entity must respond to the notice of demand immediately
- Payment of outstanding dues as specified in the recovery certificate
- Failure to comply may result in further enforcement actions including asset attachment
- The entity should engage with SEBI's Recovery Cell to resolve outstanding obligations

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: November 21, 2025
- Recovery Certificate No.: 8949 of 2025

## Impact Assessment

This notice has high impact on Rajasthan Tube Manufacturing Company Limited as it represents formal recovery proceedings by SEBI. Non-compliance with the notice of demand can lead to severe consequences including:

- Attachment and sale of movable and immovable properties
- Arrest and detention in civil prison
- Prohibition from accessing capital markets
- Adverse impact on the company's creditworthiness and business operations

The company must take immediate action to settle outstanding dues or approach appropriate legal forums for relief.
