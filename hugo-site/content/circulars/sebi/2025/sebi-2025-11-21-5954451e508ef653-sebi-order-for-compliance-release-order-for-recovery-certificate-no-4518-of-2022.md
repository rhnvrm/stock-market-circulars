## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order for Recovery Certificate No. 4518 of 2022 against Kaliyaben Himanshu Shah (PAN: BFQPS1462A) in connection with the matter of SMS Techsoft (India) Ltd. This order pertains to the conclusion or modification of recovery proceedings initiated against the individual.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 4518 of 2022 issued against Kaliyaben Himanshu Shah
- Individual's PAN: BFQPS1462A
- Matter relates to SMS Techsoft (India) Ltd.
- Release order indicates resolution or modification of recovery proceedings
- Order issued on November 21, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement/recovery matter specific to an individual case.

## Compliance Requirements

This order pertains to the resolution of recovery proceedings against the named individual. No new compliance requirements for market participants.

## Important Dates

- **Order Date**: November 21, 2025
- **Recovery Certificate Date**: 2022 (RC No. 4518 of 2022)

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to the individual named in the recovery certificate. This is an administrative enforcement matter with no direct market-wide implications. The release order suggests that recovery proceedings have been satisfied or modified for Kaliyaben Himanshu Shah in relation to SMS Techsoft (India) Ltd.
