## Summary

- SEBI has issued a remittance order for Recovery Certificate No. 4806 of 2022
- The order is against Imtiyazhusain Siayed in connection with Imtsons Investments Limited
- This represents an enforcement action for compliance violations

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 4806 of 2022 is being enforced
- Individual named: Imtiyazhusain Siayed
- Entity involved: Imtsons Investments Limited
- Document classification: Remittance Order for compliance

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced - this is an enforcement action under existing regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Payment of dues as specified in the recovery certificate
- Compliance with SEBI regulations and orders
- Cooperation with enforcement proceedings

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate date: 2022
- Current remittance order date: August 2025

## Impact Assessment

This enforcement action demonstrates SEBI's continued efforts to recover dues from non-compliant entities and individuals. The remittance order mechanism ensures systematic recovery of regulatory dues and penalties.
