## Summary

SEBI has issued a notice confirming the completion of recovery proceedings under Recovery Certificate No. 8044 of 2024 dated September 20, 2024, against V K Aggarwal HUF (Defaulter) with PAN: AAGHV0343D. The recovery action was related to the matter of illiquid stock options at the Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE). This notice confirms that the recovery proceedings have been successfully concluded.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8044 of 2024 issued on September 20, 2024
- Defaulter: V K Aggarwal HUF (PAN: AAGHV0343D)
- Matter pertains to illiquid stock options at BSE
- Recovery proceedings have been completed
- Notice published on October 9, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a completion notice for an existing recovery proceeding.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. This notice confirms the conclusion of enforcement action against the defaulter.

## Important Dates

- **September 20, 2024**: Recovery Certificate No. 8044 of 2024 issued
- **October 9, 2025**: Completion notice published

**Operational Impact**: None for market participants. The recovery proceedings against V K Aggarwal HUF have been concluded.

**Investor Impact**: Low. This relates to a specific defaulter case that has been resolved. No action required from other market participants or investors.
