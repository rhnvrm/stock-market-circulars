## Summary

SEBI organized a Municipal Bond and REIT/InvIT Outreach Programme in Raipur, Chhattisgarh on October 30, 2025, aimed at educating Urban Local Bodies (ULBs) and state government departments about municipal bonds, REIT/InvITs and capital market fundraising mechanisms for infrastructure development. The programme saw participation from senior officials of 14 ULBs including Raipur, Bilaspur, Bhilai, and Raigarh, along with officials from the Urban Administration and Development and Finance Department of Chhattisgarh government. A follow-up meeting was held on October 31, 2025, between SEBI officials led by Whole Time Member Amarjeet Singh and Chhattisgarh government officials to address specific queries and concerns of municipal corporations.

## Key Points

- Outreach programme held on October 30, 2025, in Raipur for Urban Local Bodies and state government officials
- Participation from 14 ULBs including Raipur, Bilaspur, Bhilai, and Raigarh
- Programme featured sessions on accessing securities markets through municipal bonds and REIT/InvIT
- Municipal Corporation of Raipur announced raising INR 100 Crore through municipal bonds on October 24, 2025
- Amarjeet Singh, SEBI Whole Time Member, highlighted the transformative role of municipal bonds and asset monetization through REIT/InvIT
- Follow-up meeting held on October 31, 2025, to address specific queries and concerns of municipal corporations
- Focus on transparency, market readiness, and sustainable urban finance

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is an outreach and awareness initiative to promote existing financing mechanisms.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. The programme aimed at educating ULBs on existing frameworks for:
- Accessing securities markets through municipal bonds
- Utilizing REIT/InvIT structures for public asset monetization
- Meeting investor expectations and market readiness standards
- Improving financial management and revenue enhancement projects

## Important Dates

- **October 24, 2025**: Municipal Corporation of Raipur announced INR 100 Crore municipal bond issuance
- **October 30, 2025**: Municipal Bond and REIT/InvIT Outreach Programme held in Raipur
- **October 31, 2025**: Follow-up meeting between SEBI and Chhattisgarh government officials
- **November 06, 2025**: Press release issued

## Impact Assessment

This outreach initiative has medium impact on capital markets development, particularly for alternative financing channels. The programme supports SEBI's broader objective of deepening debt markets and promoting sustainable urban infrastructure financing. Key impacts include:

- **Market Development**: Encourages ULBs to access capital markets, potentially expanding the municipal bond market
- **Infrastructure Financing**: Promotes alternative financing mechanisms for urban development projects beyond traditional government funding
- **Investor Base**: Educating ULBs on investor expectations may improve quality of municipal bond issuances
- **Asset Monetization**: Highlights REIT/InvIT as viable structures for monetizing public infrastructure assets
- **Regional Impact**: Specific to Chhattisgarh and participating ULBs, with potential replication in other states

No immediate impact on listed companies or trading activities. This is a capacity-building initiative aimed at long-term market development.
