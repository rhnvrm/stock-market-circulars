## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. 8845/2025 dated 01.08.2025 against Onelife Capital Advisors Limited (PAN: AAACO9540L). This is an enforcement action by the regulator to recover dues from the entity.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate Number: 8845/2025
- Entity: Onelife Capital Advisors Limited
- PAN: AAACO9540L
- Issue Date: 01.08.2025
- Regulatory Authority: SEBI

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes - this is an enforcement action under existing recovery provisions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Onelife Capital Advisors Limited must comply with the recovery certificate
- Payment of dues as specified in the certificate
- Failure to comply may result in further enforcement measures

## Important Dates

- Certificate Issue Date: 01.08.2025
- Recovery proceedings are effective immediately

## Impact Assessment

High impact enforcement action that may affect the operations of Onelife Capital Advisors Limited. Recovery certificates typically indicate serious compliance failures and outstanding dues to SEBI.
