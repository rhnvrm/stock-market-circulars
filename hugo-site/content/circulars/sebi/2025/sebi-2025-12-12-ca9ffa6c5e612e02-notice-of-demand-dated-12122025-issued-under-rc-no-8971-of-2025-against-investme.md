## Summary

SEBI has issued Certificate No. 8971 of 2025 under Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992 read with Section 222 of the Income Tax Act, 1961, demanding payment of Rs. 50,53,872.09 from Investment Research Advisor (Proprietor: Amit Gangrade) and Algo System (Proprietor: Sudeep Gangrade). The demand arises from a Quasi-Judicial Authority (QJA) refund order dated April 26, 2023 (Order No. QJA/GG/QJC/QJC-2/25931/2023-24) in the matter of Investment Research Advisor. The entities have 15 days from receipt to pay, failing which SEBI will initiate recovery proceedings including asset attachment, bank account freezing, property sale, and potential arrest.

## Key Points

- **Total Demand Amount**: Rs. 50,53,872.09 (Fifty Lakh Fifty-Three Thousand Eight Hundred Seventy-Two and Nine Paisa Only)
- **Demand Breakdown**: Original refund order amount of Rs. 37,99,151.95; Interest from April 2023 to December 2025 @ 1% per month totaling Rs. 12,53,720.14; Recovery costs of Rs. 1,000
- **Entities**: Investment Research Advisor (Proprietor: Amit Gangrade, PAN: AVRPG8196P) and Algo System (Proprietor: Sudeep Gangrade, PAN: GDTPS4158F)
- **Locations**: Multiple addresses in Indore and Bhikangaon, Madhya Pradesh
- **Original Order**: QJA refund order dated April 26, 2023 in matter of Investment Research Advisor
- **Payment Deadline**: 15 days from receipt of notice
- **Recovery Powers**: Asset attachment/sale, bank account attachment, property attachment/sale, arrest/detention, receiver appointment

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action under existing provisions of Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992 read with Sections 220-227, 228A, 229 and 232 of the Income Tax Act, 1961.

## Compliance Requirements

**Immediate Actions Required by Named Entities:**

1. **Payment Within 15 Days**: Pay Rs. 50,53,872.09 through:
   - Direct credit via EFT/NEFT/RTGS to Account No. SEBIRNCIS8971, Bank of India, IFSC: BKID00VAN04, OR
   - Online payment at https://siportal.sebi.gov.in (Recovery Payment module), OR
   - Payment link: SEBI Website → Enforcement → Recovery Proceedings → Pay Now

2. **Property Transfer Restrictions**: As per Rule 16 of Second Schedule to Income Tax Act, entities cannot mortgage, charge, lease or deal with any property without Recovery Officer permission; such transfers are void

3. **Deemed Property Provision**: Any direct/indirect property or money transfer to spouse, minor child, son's wife, or son's minor child without adequate consideration on or after April 26, 2023 is deemed entity property for recovery purposes

**Failure to Comply Results In:**
- Attachment and sale of movable property
- Attachment of bank accounts
- Attachment and sale of immovable property
- Arrest and detention in prison
- Appointment of receiver for property management
