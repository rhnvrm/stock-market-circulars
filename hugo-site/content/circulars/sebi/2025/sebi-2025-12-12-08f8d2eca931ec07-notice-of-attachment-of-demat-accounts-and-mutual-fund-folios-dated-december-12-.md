## Summary

SEBI has issued a notice of attachment of demat accounts and mutual fund folios against Ashish P Shah (PAN: AJRPS7737C) dated December 12, 2025, under Recovery Certificate No. 8939 of 2025. This enforcement action is taken in the matter of Svarnim Trade Udyog Limited as part of SEBI's recovery proceedings.

## Key Points

- Notice issued under Recovery Certificate No. 8939 of 2025
- Target: Ashish P Shah (PAN: AJRPS7737C)
- Attachment covers demat accounts and mutual fund folios
- Related entity: Svarnim Trade Udyog Limited
- Notice date: December 12, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an enforcement action under existing recovery provisions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Depositories and mutual fund houses must freeze the specified demat accounts and mutual fund folios
- No transactions to be permitted in the attached accounts until further orders
- Entities holding the attached accounts must comply with SEBI's recovery directions

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: December 12, 2025
- **Effective Date**: December 12, 2025 (date of attachment notice)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to specific individual and entity. This enforcement action demonstrates SEBI's continued efforts to recover dues through attachment of financial assets.

**Operational Impact**: High impact on Ashish P Shah - all demat accounts and mutual fund folios are frozen, preventing any transactions until recovery proceedings are completed or further orders are issued.

**Compliance Impact**: Depositories and mutual fund registrars must ensure strict compliance with the attachment order to avoid regulatory consequences.
