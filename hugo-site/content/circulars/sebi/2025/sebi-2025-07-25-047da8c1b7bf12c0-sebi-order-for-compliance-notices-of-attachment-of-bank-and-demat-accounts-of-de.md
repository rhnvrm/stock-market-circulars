## Summary

SEBI has issued a compliance order regarding notices of attachment of bank and demat accounts of In House Productions Limited LLP (PAN: AAACV1685K) under Recovery Certificate No. 4833 of 2022. This enforcement action is part of SEBI's recovery proceedings against the defaulting entity.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 4833 of 2022 issued against In House Productions Limited LLP
- PAN of defaulter: AAACV1685K
- Notices of attachment served for bank and demat accounts
- Compliance order issued for enforcement of recovery proceedings

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action under existing recovery provisions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Banks and depositories must comply with attachment notices
- Defaulter entity must settle outstanding dues to SEBI
- Financial institutions must freeze relevant accounts as per attachment orders

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate issued: 2022 (RC No. 4833)
- Current order date: July 25, 2025

## Impact Assessment

High impact enforcement action demonstrating SEBI's commitment to recovery of dues from defaulting entities. This affects the specific defaulter's access to banking and demat facilities until compliance is achieved.
