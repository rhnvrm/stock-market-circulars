## Summary

SEBI has issued clarifications on digital accessibility requirements for all regulated entities (REs) operating digital platforms. The circular extends the compliance deadline for accessibility auditor appointment, introduces a new reporting framework, and establishes an accessibility complaint mechanism on SCORES. The "Investors' Right to have digital accessibility" will be included in respective Investor Charters.

## Key Points

- Investors' right to digital accessibility will be included in respective Investor Charters published by SEBI
- Compliance deadline extended from December 14, 2025 to March 31, 2026 for submitting readiness status
- New accessibility complaint category available on SCORES for investors
- REs must conduct periodic accessibility audits through certified accessibility professionals
- Different reporting authorities designated based on RE category (Stock Exchanges/Depositories, BSE Ltd., or SEBI directly)

## Regulatory Changes

**Modified Compliance Timeline:**
- Previous requirement: Appoint accessibility auditor by December 14, 2025
- New requirement: Submit status of readiness and compliance for each digital platform by March 31, 2026

**New Complaint Mechanism:**
- Accessibility-related complaints can now be lodged on SCORES under "Accessibility" category
- REs must remediate accessibility issues for complaint closure

**Updated Investor Rights:**
- Digital accessibility rights to be formally included in Investor Charters for all regulated entities

## Compliance Requirements

**For All Regulated Entities:**

1. **Status Submission (by March 31, 2026):**
   - Submit readiness and compliance status for each digital platform
   - Submit to specified reporting authorities (see reporting structure below)
   - REs reporting to SEBI: email to digital_acc@sebi.gov.in
   - Use format provided in Annexure B

2. **Accessibility Audits:**
   - Conduct periodic accessibility audits of all digital platforms (websites, mobile apps, portals)
   - Use certified accessibility professionals

3. **Complaint Resolution:**
   - Respond to accessibility complaints on SCORES
   - Remediate accessibility issues for complaint closure

**Reporting Structure:**

- **Stock Brokers/Depository Participants:** Report to Stock Exchanges/Depositories
- **Investment Advisors and Research Analysts:** Report to BSE Ltd.
- **15 Categories of REs report directly to SEBI:** AIFs, Bankers to Issue, SCSBs, Clearing Corporations, CIS, Credit Rating Agencies, Custodians, Debenture Trustees, Depositories, KRAs, Merchant Bankers, Mutual Funds/AMCs, Portfolio Managers, RTAs, Stock Exchanges, Venture Capital Funds

## Important Dates

- **December 8, 2025:** Circular issue date
- **December 14, 2025:** Original deadline for accessibility auditor appointment (now modified)
- **March 31, 2026:** New deadline for submitting status of readiness and compliance to accessibility requirements

## Impact Assessment

**Affected Entities:**
- All SEBI-regulated entities operating investor-facing digital platforms
- Stock brokers, depositories, mutual funds, portfolio managers, investment advisors, research analysts, and 15+ other RE categories

**Operational Impact:**
- **High:** All REs must assess and report digital platform accessibility compliance
- REs need to establish processes for periodic accessibility audits
- New complaint handling workflow for accessibility issues on SCORES
- Resource allocation required for certified accessibility professionals

**Investor Benefits:**
- Enhanced accessibility rights formally recognized in Investor Charters
- Structured complaint mechanism for accessibility issues
- Improved digital platform accessibility for investors with disabilities

**Timeline Relief:**
- Extended deadline provides additional 3.5 months for compliance preparation
- Shift from auditor appointment to readiness status submission offers more flexibility
