## Summary

SEBI has completed Recovery Certificate No. 2255 of 2019 dated June 25, 2019 issued against Rani Goel (PAN: AFOPG8596A) in the matter of Vandana Knitwear Limited. This represents the conclusion of enforcement proceedings against the defaulter.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 2255 of 2019 has been completed
- Original certificate dated June 25, 2019
- Defaulter: Rani Goel (PAN: AFOPG8596A)
- Related entity: Vandana Knitwear Limited
- Enforcement proceeding concluded

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a procedural completion notice for existing enforcement action.

## Compliance Requirements

No additional compliance requirements. This notice confirms completion of recovery proceedings.

## Important Dates

- Original Recovery Certificate Date: June 25, 2019
- Completion Notice Date: August 8, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a completion notice for enforcement proceedings against an individual defaulter. The action concludes existing recovery efforts rather than introducing new restrictions or requirements.
