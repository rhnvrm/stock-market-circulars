## Summary

SEBI, in association with BSE, NSE, CDSL, NSDL, AMFI, NISM, NCDEX, MCX, CAMS, and KFin Technologies, has set up a pavilion titled "Bharat Ka Share Bazaar" at the 44th India International Trade Fair (IITF) 2025, held from November 14-27, 2025, at Bharat Mandapam, Pragati Maidan, New Delhi. The pavilion was inaugurated on November 14, 2025, by Shri Tuhin Kanta Pandey, Chairman, SEBI, to showcase India's well-regulated securities market and promote financial literacy and investor awareness.

## Key Points

- Pavilion theme: "Viksit Bharat Ka Adhaar, Bharat Ka Share Bazaar" aligns with IITF theme "Ek Bharat, Shreshtha Bharat"
- Participating organizations: BSE, NSE, CDSL, NSDL, AMFI, NISM, NCDEX, MCX, CAMS, and KFin Technologies
- Features Live Demo Zones and Investor Service Centre for real-time investor services
- Visitors can verify intermediaries, understand SEBI's digital initiatives, and learn about safe investment practices
- Activities include expert talk shows, interactive quizzes, skits, muppet shows, and live demonstrations
- Focus on cautioning public against illegal money mobilization schemes and fraudulent investment offerings
- Targets diverse audiences: students, first-time investors, institutions, and senior citizens

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is an informational press release about SEBI's public outreach initiative.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance requirements. This is a public awareness and financial literacy initiative.

## Important Dates

- **November 14, 2025**: Pavilion inauguration by SEBI Chairman
- **November 14-27, 2025**: IITF 2025 exhibition period with SEBI pavilion operational

## Impact Assessment

This is a public outreach initiative with no direct regulatory or market impact. The pavilion serves to:

- Enhance financial literacy and investor awareness among the general public
- Showcase India's well-regulated securities market infrastructure
- Promote safe investment practices and warn against fraudulent schemes
- Foster financial inclusion aligned with the "Viksit Bharat" vision
- Provide interactive educational experiences on securities market participation

The initiative is part of SEBI's ongoing investor protection and financial education efforts, targeting broad public engagement during a major national trade fair.
