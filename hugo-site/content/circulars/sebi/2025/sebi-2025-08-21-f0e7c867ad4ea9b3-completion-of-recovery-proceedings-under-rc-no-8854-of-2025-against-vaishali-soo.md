## Summary

SEBI has completed recovery proceedings under Recovery Certificate No. 8854 of 2025 against Vaishali Sood Sharma in connection with the Family Care Hospitals Limited matter. This notice confirms the successful collection of penalties previously imposed through enforcement action.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8854 of 2025 proceedings against Vaishali Sood Sharma have been completed
- Action relates to the Family Care Hospitals Limited matter
- SEBI has successfully recovered the imposed penalties
- Administrative closure of enforcement proceedings

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. This is a completion notice for existing recovery proceedings.

## Important Dates

- August 21, 2025: Date of completion notice

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is an administrative notice confirming completion of penalty recovery. Demonstrates SEBI's continued enforcement efforts and ability to collect imposed penalties from violators.
