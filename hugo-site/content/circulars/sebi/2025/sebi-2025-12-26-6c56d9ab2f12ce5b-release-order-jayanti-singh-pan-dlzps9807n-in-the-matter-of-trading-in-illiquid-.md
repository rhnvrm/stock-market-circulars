## Summary

SEBI has issued a release order for Ms. Jayanti Singh (PAN: DLZPS9807N) in connection with recovery proceedings related to trading in illiquid stock options on the Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE). This order relates to Certificate No. 7255 of 2023 and indicates the conclusion of recovery proceedings against the individual.

## Key Points

- Release order issued for Jayanti Singh (PAN: DLZPS9807N)
- Matter relates to trading in illiquid stock options on BSE
- Associated with Recovery Certificate No. 7255 of 2023
- Enforcement action has been concluded
- Order published in December 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is an individual enforcement action.

## Compliance Requirements

The release order indicates completion of recovery proceedings. No ongoing compliance requirements are specified in this release notification.

## Important Dates

- **Order Date**: December 26, 2025
- **Recovery Certificate Reference**: No. 7255 of 2023 (dated October 20, 2023)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is an individual enforcement action with no direct market-wide implications.

**Operational Impact**: Low - The release order concludes proceedings for the specific individual. It serves as a record of SEBI's enforcement action regarding illiquid stock options trading violations on BSE.

**Significance**: This order demonstrates SEBI's continued enforcement activities in cases involving manipulation or irregularities in illiquid stock options trading, reinforcing market integrity standards.
