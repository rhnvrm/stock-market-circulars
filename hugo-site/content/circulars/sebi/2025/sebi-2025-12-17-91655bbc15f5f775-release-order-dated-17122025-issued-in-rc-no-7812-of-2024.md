## Summary

SEBI has issued a release order dated December 17, 2025 in relation to Recovery Certificate No. 7812 of 2024. This order releases certain assets or restrictions that were previously attached or frozen as part of recovery proceedings.

## Key Points

- Release order issued on December 17, 2025
- Relates to Recovery Certificate No. 7812 of 2024
- Part of SEBI's enforcement and recovery proceedings
- Releases previously attached assets or restrictions

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is an entity-specific enforcement action.

## Compliance Requirements

The affected entity or persons must comply with any conditions specified in the release order. Banks, depositories, and mutual funds holding the previously attached assets should process the release as per the order.

## Important Dates

- **Release Order Date:** December 17, 2025
- **Original Recovery Certificate:** RC No. 7812 of 2024

## Impact Assessment

This release order has limited market impact as it pertains to specific recovery proceedings against particular entities. It represents the conclusion or partial conclusion of enforcement action where SEBI is releasing previously attached assets, indicating either completion of recovery, settlement, or compliance with regulatory requirements.
