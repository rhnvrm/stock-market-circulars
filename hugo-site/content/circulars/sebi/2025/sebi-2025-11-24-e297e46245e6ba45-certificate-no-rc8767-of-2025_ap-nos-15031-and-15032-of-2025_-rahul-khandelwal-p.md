## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC8767 of 2025 (A.P Nos 15031 and 15032 of 2025) against Rahul Khandelwal (PAN: AWPPK7085E) in the matter of Getrise Infotech Pvt Ltd. This certificate authorizes recovery of amounts due from the noticee.

## Key Points

- Recovery certificate issued against Rahul Khandelwal
- Case relates to Getrise Infotech Pvt Ltd
- Certificate Number: RC8767 of 2025
- Appeal Numbers: A.P Nos 15031 and 15032 of 2025
- Individual PAN: AWPPK7085E

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action.

## Compliance Requirements

Rahul Khandelwal is required to pay the amounts specified in the recovery certificate. Non-compliance may lead to further recovery proceedings including attachment of assets.

## Important Dates

- Certificate Issue Date: November 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is an individual enforcement action with no direct market impact. It demonstrates SEBI's continued efforts in recovery proceedings against entities and individuals involved in securities violations related to Getrise Infotech Pvt Ltd.
