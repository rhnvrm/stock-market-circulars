- Matter relates to Brijlaxmi Leasing and Finance Limited

This is a specific enforcement order with minimal broader market impact. The completion order indicates SEBI has concluded its recovery proceedings in this particular case involving Brijlaxmi Leasing and Finance Limited and Siddharth J Chaturvedi.
