## Summary

SEBI has announced the completion of Recovery Certificate No. 284 of 2014 that was issued against Mahesh Mistry in connection with the Kolar Biotech Limited matter. This notice marks the conclusion of recovery proceedings that were initiated over a decade ago.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 284 of 2014 has been completed
- Proceedings were against Mahesh Mistry
- Related to Kolar Biotech Limited matter
- Represents closure of enforcement action from 2014

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a completion notice for existing recovery proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

No ongoing compliance requirements as the recovery certificate has been completed.

## Important Dates

- Original Recovery Certificate: 2014
- Completion Notice: December 17, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The completion of this recovery certificate has minimal market impact as it relates to enforcement proceedings from 2014. This notice provides closure to a historical matter involving Kolar Biotech Limited and serves as a record of completed recovery action against the individual named in the certificate.
