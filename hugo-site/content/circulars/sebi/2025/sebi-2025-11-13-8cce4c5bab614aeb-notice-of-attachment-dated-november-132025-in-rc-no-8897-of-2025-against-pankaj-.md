## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Attachment dated November 13, 2025 in Recovery Certificate No. 8897 of 2025 against Pankaj Kumar Shukla, Proprietor of M/s. Market Anand, for carrying out unregistered investment advisory activities. This enforcement action is part of SEBI's ongoing efforts to protect investors from unregistered and unauthorized investment advisors.

## Key Points

- Enforcement action under Recovery Certificate No. 8897 of 2025
- Target: Pankaj Kumar Shukla, Proprietor of M/s. Market Anand
- Violation: Unregistered Investment Advisory activities
- Action: Notice of Attachment issued
- Notice Date: November 13, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI regulations regarding registration requirements for investment advisors.

## Compliance Requirements

- All investment advisors must be registered with SEBI under the SEBI (Investment Advisers) Regulations, 2013
- Entities and individuals providing investment advice must ensure proper registration before commencing operations
- Failure to comply may result in enforcement actions including attachment of assets and recovery proceedings

## Important Dates

- **November 13, 2025**: Notice of Attachment issued

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is an enforcement action against a specific individual/entity and does not affect broader market operations.

**Investor Impact**: Medium - Serves as a warning to investors to verify registration status of investment advisors before engaging their services.

**Regulatory Significance**: Medium - Demonstrates SEBI's active enforcement against unregistered investment advisory activities, reinforcing the importance of regulatory compliance in the investment advisory sector.
