## Summary

SEBI has issued a Completion Order for Recovery Certificate No. 5556 of 2022 dated November 17, 2025, confirming the successful completion of recovery proceedings against Himanshu Prafulchandra Shah (PAN: ANEPS9445A) in the matter of SMS Techsoft (India) Limited. This order marks the closure of enforcement action.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 5556 of 2022 has been marked as completed
- Noticee: Himanshu Prafulchandra Shah (PAN: ANEPS9445A)
- Related entity: SMS Techsoft (India) Limited
- Order issued on November 17, 2025
- Completion order signifies full recovery or resolution of dues

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an administrative order confirming completion of existing recovery proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

No ongoing compliance requirements as the recovery proceedings have been completed. The certificate has been satisfied and the matter is now closed.

## Important Dates

- **November 17, 2025**: Completion Order issued
- **Original RC Date**: 2022 (Recovery Certificate No. 5556 of 2022)

**Operational Impact**: No operational impact on markets or trading activities.
