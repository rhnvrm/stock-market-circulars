## Summary

SEBI has announced the completion of recovery proceedings under Recovery Certificate No. RC736 of 2015 against Ahilya Commercial Pvt. Ltd. (PAN: AACCA2226R) in the matter of Limtex Investments Ltd. This marks the conclusion of enforcement action initiated in 2015.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC736 of 2015 has been completed
- Defaulter: Ahilya Commercial Pvt. Ltd. (PAN: AACCA2226R)
- Related matter: Limtex Investments Ltd.
- Recovery proceedings initiated in 2015 have now been concluded
- Completion notice issued on December 30, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a completion notice for existing recovery proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. This notice confirms the completion of recovery proceedings against the specified entity.

## Important Dates

- **Recovery Certificate Issue Year**: 2015
- **Completion Notice Date**: December 30, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative notice with minimal market impact. The completion of recovery proceedings affects only the specific entity (Ahilya Commercial Pvt. Ltd.) and related parties in the Limtex Investments Ltd. matter. No broader implications for market participants or trading activities.
