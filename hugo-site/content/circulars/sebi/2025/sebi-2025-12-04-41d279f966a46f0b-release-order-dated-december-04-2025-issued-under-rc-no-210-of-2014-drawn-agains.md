## Summary

SEBI has issued a release order dated December 04, 2025 under Recovery Certificate No. 210 of 2014 that was drawn against M/s Million Investrade Limited in the matter of Adam Comsof Limited. This order addresses the recovery proceedings initiated against the entity.

## Key Points

- Release order issued under Recovery Certificate No. 210 of 2014
- Order pertains to M/s Million Investrade Limited
- Related to the matter of Adam Comsof Limited
- Order date: December 04, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an administrative order related to existing recovery proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

Parties involved should note the release order and comply with any remaining obligations as specified in the order document.

## Important Dates

- Order Date: December 04, 2025
- Original Recovery Certificate: RC No. 210 of 2014

## Impact Assessment

This release order has limited market impact as it is specific to the parties involved in the recovery proceedings. The order addresses administrative matters related to the recovery certificate drawn against M/s Million Investrade Limited.
