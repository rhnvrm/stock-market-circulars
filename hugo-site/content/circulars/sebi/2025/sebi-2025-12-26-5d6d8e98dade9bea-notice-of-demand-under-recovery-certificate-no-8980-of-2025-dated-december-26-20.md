## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand under Recovery Certificate No. 8980 of 2025 dated December 26, 2025 against Cerebra Integrated Technologies Limited (PAN: AAACC5941K). This notice is part of SEBI's enforcement and recovery proceedings to collect outstanding dues from entities that have failed to comply with regulatory orders or pay penalties.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8980 of 2025 issued on December 26, 2025
- Entity: Cerebra Integrated Technologies Limited
- PAN: AAACC5941K
- Notice issued under SEBI's recovery and enforcement mechanism
- Indicates outstanding dues owed to SEBI by the company

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI recovery procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Cerebra Integrated Technologies Limited is required to pay the outstanding amount specified in the recovery certificate
- Failure to comply may result in further enforcement actions including attachment of assets, bank accounts, or other recovery measures
- The company must respond to the notice within the stipulated timeframe

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate Date: December 26, 2025
- Notice Publication Date: December 26, 2025
- Payment deadline and response timeframe would be specified in the detailed notice

## Impact Assessment

**Impact on Entity:** High - Recovery certificate proceedings can lead to attachment of assets, freezing of bank accounts, and restrictions on business operations until the outstanding amount is recovered.

**Market Impact:** Low to Medium - Impact is specific to Cerebra Integrated Technologies Limited. May affect investor confidence in the company if it is publicly listed or has public shareholders.

**Compliance Precedent:** This notice reinforces SEBI's commitment to enforcing regulatory compliance and recovering penalties from non-compliant entities through formal recovery certificate mechanisms.
