## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order dated December 04, 2025 under Recovery Certificate No. 300 of 2014 drawn against Adsul Trading Private Limited in the matter of Kolar Biotech Limited. This order pertains to enforcement proceedings and recovery actions.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate Number: RC No. 300 of 2014
- Noticee: Adsul Trading Private Limited
- Related Matter: Kolar Biotech Limited
- Order Date: December 04, 2025
- Order Type: Release Order

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement order specific to the proceedings against the noticee.

## Compliance Requirements

Compliance requirements are specific to Adsul Trading Private Limited as per the terms of the release order and the underlying recovery certificate.

## Important Dates

- **Order Date**: December 04, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This release order has limited market impact as it is specific to enforcement proceedings against Adsul Trading Private Limited in relation to Kolar Biotech Limited. The order affects only the parties involved in the recovery certificate proceedings and does not have broader market or operational implications for other market participants.
