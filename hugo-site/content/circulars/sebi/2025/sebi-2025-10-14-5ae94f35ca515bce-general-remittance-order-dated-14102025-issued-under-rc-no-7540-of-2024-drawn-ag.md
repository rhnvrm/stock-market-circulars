## Summary

SEBI has issued a General Remittance Order dated 14.10.2025 under Recovery Certificate No. 7540 of 2024 drawn against Core Investment (Proprietor: Deepak Bamne) and Core Group (Proprietor: Matwar Mehra) in the matter of Core Investment. This order is part of SEBI's enforcement and recovery proceedings.

## Key Points

- Order issued under Recovery Certificate No. 7540 of 2024
- Entities involved: Core Investment (Prop. Deepak Bamne) and Core Group (Prop. Matwar Mehra)
- General Remittance Order for recovery of dues
- Date of order: October 14, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI recovery procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Core Investment (Prop. Deepak Bamne) must comply with the remittance order
- Core Group (Prop. Matwar Mehra) must comply with the remittance order
- Payment of dues as specified in the recovery certificate
- Entities must respond to SEBI's recovery proceedings

## Important Dates

- Order Date: October 14, 2025
- Recovery Certificate No.: 7540 of 2024

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - affects specific proprietary firms only

**Operational Impact:** High for the named entities - remittance orders typically involve financial recovery and potential restrictions on the entities' operations until compliance is achieved

**Investor Impact:** Minimal direct impact on general investors; relevant for those who may have dealings with Core Investment or Core Group
