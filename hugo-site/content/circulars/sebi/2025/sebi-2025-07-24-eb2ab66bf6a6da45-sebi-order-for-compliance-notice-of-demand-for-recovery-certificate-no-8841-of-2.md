SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand under Recovery Certificate No. 8841 of 2025 against Purav Bharatbhai Patel and Hansaben Bharatkumar Patel in matters relating to Pressure Sensitive Systems (India) Limited. This enforcement action represents SEBI's formal demand for compliance with regulatory requirements and likely involves monetary recovery.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate Number: 8841 of 2025
- Enforcement action against two named individuals: Purav Bharatbhai Patel and Hansaben Bharatkumar Patel
- Matter relates to Pressure Sensitive Systems (India) Limited
- Document type: Notice of Demand for compliance
- Part of SEBI's enforcement and recovery proceedings

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI regulations and recovery procedures.

- Non-compliance may result in further enforcement measures
