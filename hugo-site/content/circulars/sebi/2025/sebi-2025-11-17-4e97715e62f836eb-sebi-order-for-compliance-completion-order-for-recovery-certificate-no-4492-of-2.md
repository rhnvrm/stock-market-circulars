## Summary

SEBI has issued a Completion Order for Recovery Certificate No. 4492 of 2022 dated November 17, 2025, against Minaben Prafulbhai Shah (PAN: COYPS9153J) in the matter of SMS Techsoft (India) Limited. This order marks the completion of recovery proceedings under the SEBI Act.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 4492 of 2022 has been completed
- Noticee: Minaben Prafulbhai Shah (PAN: COYPS9153J)
- Related entity: SMS Techsoft (India) Limited
- Order date: November 17, 2025
- Order type: Completion Order indicating successful recovery

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- The completion order signifies that the recovery proceedings have been concluded
- No further compliance actions required from the noticee in this matter
- The recovery certificate stands satisfied/completed

## Important Dates

- **November 17, 2025**: Completion Order issued
- **Original RC Date**: 2022 (Recovery Certificate No. 4492 of 2022)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is an administrative order marking the completion of recovery proceedings against an individual in relation to SMS Techsoft (India) Limited.

**Investor Impact**: No direct impact on investors. The completion order indicates that SEBI's recovery efforts in this case have concluded.

**Operational Impact**: No operational changes required. This is a closure notification for a recovery proceeding that has been satisfied.
