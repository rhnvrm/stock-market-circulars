## Summary

SEBI has issued Notice of Attachment of Bank Accounts and Demat Accounts (Notices 14990 and 14991 of 2025) dated November 03, 2025, against Pawan Kumar Verma (PAN: AEKPV3608M) as a defaulter in the matter of Illegal Screen-based Order (ISO) trading at BSE under Recovery Certificate No. 8013 of 2024.

## Key Points

- **Defaulter**: Pawan Kumar Verma (PAN: AEKPV3608M)
- **Notice Numbers**: 14990 and 14991 of 2025
- **Notice Date**: November 03, 2025
- **Recovery Certificate**: No. 8013 of 2024
- **Matter**: Illegal Screen-based Order (ISO) trading at BSE
- **Action**: Attachment of bank accounts and demat accounts

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is an enforcement action under existing recovery mechanisms.

## Compliance Requirements

- Banks and depositories are required to comply with the attachment notices
- The defaulter's accounts are subject to attachment until recovery is completed
- Financial institutions must freeze the specified accounts as per SEBI directions

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: November 03, 2025
- Recovery proceedings under RC No. 8013 of 2024

## Impact Assessment

**Direct Impact**: High impact on the defaulter (Pawan Kumar Verma) whose bank and demat accounts are attached for recovery purposes.

**Market Impact**: Minimal broader market impact as this is an individual enforcement action. However, it demonstrates SEBI's continued vigilance against illegal screen-based order trading and reinforces the regulatory framework for market discipline.

**Precedential Value**: Serves as a deterrent for market participants engaging in ISO violations or other market irregularities at stock exchanges.
