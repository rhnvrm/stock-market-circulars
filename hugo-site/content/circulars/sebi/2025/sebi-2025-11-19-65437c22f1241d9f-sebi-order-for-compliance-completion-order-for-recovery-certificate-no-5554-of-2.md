- Recovery Certificate No. 5554 of 2022 issued against Keval Kirtikumr Shah
- PAN of defaulter: DFNPS6796L
- Matter relates to SMS Techsoft (India) Limited
- Compliance completion order indicates conclusion of recovery proceedings
- Order published on November 19, 2025

No regulatory framework changes. This is an individual enforcement action.

- Compliance completion suggests recovery action has been concluded
- Other market participants are not directly affected by this order

## Important Dates

- Compliance completion order date: November 19, 2025

**Market Impact:** Low - This is an individual recovery action with no broader market implications.
