## Summary

SEBI has announced the completion of Recovery Certificate No. RC742 of 2015 against Ahilya Commercial Pvt. Ltd. (PAN: AACCA2226R) in the matter of Minolta Finance Ltd. This represents the conclusion of recovery proceedings initiated in 2015.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC742 of 2015 has been completed
- Defaulter: Ahilya Commercial Pvt. Ltd. (PAN: AACCA2226R)
- Related matter: Minolta Finance Ltd.
- This is part of SEBI's enforcement and recovery actions

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this circular. This is a completion notice for existing recovery proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements are imposed. This circular serves as a notification of completion of recovery proceedings.

## Important Dates

- Original Recovery Certificate: 2015
- Completion Notice: December 30, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact as it relates to the administrative completion of a specific recovery certificate against a private limited company. It demonstrates SEBI's continued efforts in enforcement and debt recovery but does not affect broader market operations or listed entities.
