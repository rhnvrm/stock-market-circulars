## Summary

SEBI has issued an order under Sections 11(1), 11(4), 11B(1) and 11B(2) read with Section 15G of the SEBI Act, 1992 in the matter of alleged insider trading in the scrip of Adani Green Energy Limited (AGEL). The order concerns three noticees: Pranav Vinod Adani (Director in various Adani Group companies), Kunal Dhanpalbhai Shah, and Nrupal Dhanpalbhai Shah (brothers married to relatives of Pranav Adani). The investigation relates to trading activity during January 28, 2021 to August 20, 2021, surrounding AGEL's announcement on May 19, 2021 regarding share purchase agreements with Softbank Group Capital Limited and Bharti Global Limited to acquire stakes in SB Energy Holdings Limited.

## Key Points

- AGEL announced acquisition of SB Energy stakes on May 19, 2021 at 08:20:21 hours, leading to 3.75% price increase (from ₹1198.75 to ₹1243.65)
- The acquisition increased AGEL's operational capacity by 46% (from 3.7 GW to 5.4 GW) and overall capacity by 33% (from 14.8 GW to 19.8 GW)
- SEBI determined the acquisition announcement was price sensitive information
- Investigation period: January 28, 2021 to August 20, 2021
- Investigating Officer appointed on April 20, 2023; Investigation Report dated November 10, 2023
- Pranav Adani (Noticee No. 1) is Director in Adani Enterprises Limited, Adani Wilmar Limited, Adani Total Gas Limited and handles brand custodian and reputation management for Adani Group
- Kunal Shah (Noticee No. 2) is married to cousin of Pranav Adani; Nrupal Shah (Noticee No. 3) is married to sister of Pranav Adani
- Show Cause Notice issued on November 10, 2023

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action for alleged violations of existing regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

**Allegations Against Noticees:**

- **Pranav Adani (Noticee No. 1):** Alleged violation of Section 12A(e) of SEBI Act read with Regulation 3(1) of SEBI (Prohibition of Insider Trading) Regulations, 2015
- **Kunal Shah and Nrupal Shah (Noticees No. 2 and 3):** Alleged violation of Section 12A(d) and (e) of SEBI Act read with Regulation 4(1) of PIT Regulations, 2015

**Proceedings Initiated:**

- Against Pranav Adani: Under Section 11(1), 11(4) read with 11B(1) and under Section 11B(2) read with Section 15G of SEBI Act
- Against Kunal Shah and Nrupal Shah: For directions including disgorgement of unlawful profits under Section 11(1), 11(4) read with Section 11B(1) and penalty under Section 11B(2) read with Section 15G of SEBI Act

## Important Dates

- **May 18, 2021:** AGEL closing price ₹1198.75
- **May 19, 2021, 08:20:21 hours:** AGEL announced share purchase agreements for SB Energy acquisition on BSE and NSE
- **May 19, 2021:** AGEL closing price ₹1243.65 (3.75% increase)
- **Investigation Period:** January 28, 2021 to August 20, 2021
- **April 20, 2023:** Investigating Officer appointed
- **November 10, 2023:** Investigation Report completed and Show Cause Notice issued

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:**
- The announcement resulted in immediate positive market reaction with 3.75% price increase in AGEL scrip
- The acquisition was materially significant, increasing AGEL's operational capacity by 46% and total capacity by 33%
- SB Energy portfolio consisted of 5 GW total capacity with 1.7 GW operational

**Enforcement Impact:**
- High-profile case involving senior leadership of Adani Group
- Potential disgorgement of unlawful profits and penalties if violations are established
- Reinforces SEBI's focus on insider trading enforcement in large corporate transactions
- Family relationships and connections examined as part of insider trading investigation framework

**Precedential Value:**
- Case demonstrates SEBI's scrutiny of trading patterns around major acquisition announcements
- Highlights examination of connected persons and their trading activities in context of price sensitive information
