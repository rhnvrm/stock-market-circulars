## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order dated December 04, 2025 under Recovery Certificate No. 302 of 2014, which was drawn against M/s Structure Housing and Finance Private Limited in connection with the matter of Kolar Biotech Limited. This order signifies the completion or resolution of recovery proceedings initiated under the recovery certificate.

## Key Points

- Release Order issued on December 04, 2025
- Recovery Certificate No. 302 of 2014
- Entity: M/s Structure Housing and Finance Private Limited
- Related matter: Kolar Biotech Limited
- Indicates completion of recovery proceedings

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an administrative order for releasing a recovery certificate.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. The release order indicates that the obligations under Recovery Certificate No. 302 of 2014 have been satisfied or resolved.

## Important Dates

- **Release Order Date:** December 04, 2025
- **Original Recovery Certificate:** 2014 (RC No. 302)

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to the specific entity M/s Structure Housing and Finance Private Limited and stakeholders in Kolar Biotech Limited matter. The release order suggests resolution of past enforcement proceedings with no broader market impact. This is a routine administrative action concluding recovery proceedings from 2014.
