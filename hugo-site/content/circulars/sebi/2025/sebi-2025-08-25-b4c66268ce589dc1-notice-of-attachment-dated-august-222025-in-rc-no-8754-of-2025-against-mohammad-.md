## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Attachment dated August 22, 2025, in RC no 8754 of 2025 against Mohammad Nasiruddin Ansari (PAN: BYSPA0419E) in connection with unregistered investment advisory activities conducted through 'Baap of Chart'.

## Key Points

- Notice of attachment issued against Mohammad Nasiruddin Ansari
- PAN: BYSPA0419E
- Case reference: RC no 8754 of 2025
- Allegation involves unregistered investment advisory activities
- Entity name: 'Baap of Chart'
- Date of notice: August 22, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

This action reinforces SEBI's stance on unregistered investment advisory services and the regulatory requirement for proper registration before providing such services.

## Compliance Requirements

- Investment advisors must be registered with SEBI before providing advisory services
- Unregistered entities conducting investment advisory activities face enforcement action including asset attachment
- Investors should verify registration status of investment advisors before taking services

## Important Dates

- Notice date: August 22, 2025
- Case filing: RC no 8754 of 2025

## Impact Assessment

This enforcement action serves as a deterrent for unregistered investment advisory activities and protects investors from potentially fraudulent or unqualified advisory services. The attachment proceedings indicate serious regulatory violation warranting asset freezing measures.
