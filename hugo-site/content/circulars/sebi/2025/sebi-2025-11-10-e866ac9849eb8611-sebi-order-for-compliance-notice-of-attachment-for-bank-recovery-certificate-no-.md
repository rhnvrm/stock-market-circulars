## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Attachment under Recovery Certificate No. 8491 of 2025 against INSIGHT RESEARCH and its proprietor Anil Gurjar for operating as an unregistered investment adviser. This notice directs banks and financial institutions to attach and freeze the bank accounts and assets of the defaulters to facilitate recovery of outstanding dues.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8491 of 2025 issued against INSIGHT RESEARCH and proprietor Anil Gurjar
- Entity operated as an unregistered investment adviser in violation of SEBI regulations
- Notice of attachment issued for bank accounts and assets
- Banks and financial institutions directed to comply with the attachment order
- Part of SEBI's enforcement and recovery proceedings

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI regulations prohibiting unregistered investment advisory activities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Banks and financial institutions must immediately freeze and attach all accounts and assets belonging to INSIGHT RESEARCH and Anil Gurjar
- No withdrawals, transfers, or transactions to be permitted in the attached accounts
- Banks must report compliance with the attachment order to SEBI
- Any person or entity holding funds or assets of the defaulters must preserve and report them

## Important Dates

- **Order Date**: November 10, 2025
- **Recovery Certificate No.**: 8491 of 2025
- **Effective Date**: Immediate upon issuance

## Impact Assessment

**Impact Level**: High

**Affected Parties**:
- INSIGHT RESEARCH and its proprietor Anil Gurjar (direct impact)
- Banks and financial institutions holding accounts/assets (compliance obligation)
- Clients who may have engaged with the unregistered adviser

**Market Impact**: Limited direct market impact, but reinforces SEBI's strict enforcement against unregistered investment advisers and serves as a deterrent.

**Investor Protection**: This action protects investors by taking strong action against entities providing unregistered investment advisory services and demonstrates SEBI's commitment to enforcing registration requirements.
