## Summary

SEBI has completed recovery proceedings under Recovery Certificate No. RC737 of 2015 issued against Ahilya Commercial Pvt. Ltd. (PAN: AACCA2226R) in the matter of Blue Print Securities Ltd.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC737 of 2015 has been completed
- Proceedings were against Ahilya Commercial Pvt. Ltd. (PAN: AACCA2226R)
- Related to the matter of Blue Print Securities Ltd.
- Completion indicates full recovery or closure of outstanding dues

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative completion order.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. This order marks the completion of existing recovery proceedings.

## Important Dates

- **Order Date**: December 30, 2025
- **Original Recovery Certificate**: RC No. 737 of 2015

## Impact Assessment

This is a completion order for a specific recovery case from 2015. The impact is limited to the parties involved (Ahilya Commercial Pvt. Ltd. and Blue Print Securities Ltd.) with no broader market implications. This represents the conclusion of enforcement proceedings.
