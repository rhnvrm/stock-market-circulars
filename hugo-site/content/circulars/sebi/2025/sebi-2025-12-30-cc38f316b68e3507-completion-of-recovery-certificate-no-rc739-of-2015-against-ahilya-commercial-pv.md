## Summary

SEBI has announced the completion of recovery proceedings under Recovery Certificate No. RC739 of 2015 against Ahilya Commercial Pvt. Ltd. (PAN: AACCA2226R) in the matter of Oasis Cine Communications Ltd. This is an administrative closure notification indicating that the recovery process has been concluded.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC739 of 2015 has been completed
- Proceedings against: Ahilya Commercial Pvt. Ltd. (PAN: AACCA2226R)
- Related matter: Oasis Cine Communications Ltd.
- Document issued: December 30, 2025
- This represents closure of enforcement proceedings initiated in 2015

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this notice.

## Compliance Requirements

No ongoing compliance requirements. This is a completion notice for concluded recovery proceedings.

## Important Dates

- **Recovery Certificate Issuance**: 2015 (RC739 of 2015)
- **Completion Notice**: December 30, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is an administrative notice with minimal market impact. The completion of recovery proceedings indicates that SEBI has concluded its enforcement action against Ahilya Commercial Pvt. Ltd. in relation to the Oasis Cine Communications Ltd. matter. The notice serves as a formal record that the recovery process initiated through RC739 of 2015 has been finalized. No broader market implications or compliance requirements arise from this notice.
