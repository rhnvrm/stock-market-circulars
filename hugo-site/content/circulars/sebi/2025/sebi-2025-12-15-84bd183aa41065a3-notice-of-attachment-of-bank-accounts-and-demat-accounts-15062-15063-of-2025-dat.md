## Summary

SEBI has issued Notices of Attachment of Bank Accounts and Demat Accounts numbered 15062 and 15063 of 2025, dated December 15, 2025, against Dharmvir Singh (PAN: DSVPS1071K), who has been identified as a defaulter. This enforcement action is taken under Recovery Certificate No. 8619 of 2025 in connection with violations related to illiquid stock options at the Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE).

## Key Points

- Notices of Attachment issued: 15062 & 15063 of 2025
- Date of notices: December 15, 2025
- Defaulter: Dharmvir Singh
- PAN of defaulter: DSVPS1071K
- Recovery Certificate No.: 8619 of 2025
- Matter relates to: Illiquid Stock Options at BSE
- Action taken: Attachment of bank accounts and demat accounts

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework changes. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI recovery and enforcement provisions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Banks and depository participants must comply with the attachment notices and freeze the specified accounts of Dharmvir Singh
- The defaulter is prohibited from operating the attached bank accounts and demat accounts until recovery is complete or the attachment is lifted
- Financial institutions must cooperate with SEBI's recovery proceedings

## Important Dates

- **December 15, 2025**: Date of issuance of attachment notices 15062 & 15063
- Attachment effective immediately upon receipt by banks and depositories

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is an individual enforcement action with no broader market implications.

**Regulatory Impact**: Demonstrates SEBI's continued commitment to enforcing compliance and recovering dues from defaulters involved in illiquid stock options violations.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to the individual defaulter. Reinforces investor protection measures through active recovery proceedings against violators.

**Operational Impact**: Banks and depository participants holding accounts of Dharmvir Singh must process and implement the attachment orders promptly.
