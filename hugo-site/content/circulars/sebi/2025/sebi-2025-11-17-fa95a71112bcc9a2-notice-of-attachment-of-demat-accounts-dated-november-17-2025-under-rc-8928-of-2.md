## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Attachment of Demat Accounts dated November 17, 2025 under Recovery Certificate No. 8928 of 2025 against Prafulla Subhash Chandra Bhat (PAN: AMEPB0155F) in connection with the matter of Prakash Constrowell Limited, which has been renamed as Setubandhan Infrastructure Limited. This action is part of SEBI's enforcement and recovery proceedings.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate Number: RC 8928 of 2025
- Person against whom action is taken: Prafulla Subhash Chandra Bhat
- PAN of the person: AMEPB0155F
- Type of attachment: Demat Accounts
- Related company: Prakash Constrowell Limited (now Setubandhan Infrastructure Limited)
- Notice date: November 17, 2025
- Category: Enforcement - Recovery Proceedings

## Regulatory Changes

This notice represents an enforcement action under SEBI's recovery proceedings framework. The attachment of demat accounts is a regulatory measure to secure potential dues or penalties arising from violations or non-compliance related to securities market regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- The demat accounts of Prafulla Subhash Chandra Bhat are subject to attachment orders
- Depositories and depository participants must comply with the attachment notice
- No transactions should be permitted in the attached demat accounts without SEBI's approval
- The person concerned must respond to the recovery proceedings as per SEBI regulations

## Important Dates

- Notice Issue Date: November 17, 2025
- Recovery Certificate: RC 8928 of 2025
