## Summary

SEBI has issued a General Remittance Order dated 29.09.2025 in Recovery Certificate No.4523 of 2022 against Nimbus Industries Limited (PAN: AAACN2708B) for non-redressal of investor grievances.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No: 4523 of 2022
- Entity: Nimbus Industries Limited
- PAN: AAACN2708B
- Date of Order: 29 September 2025
- Subject: Non-redressal of investor grievances

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action under existing investor protection framework.

## Compliance Requirements

Nimbus Industries Limited is subject to recovery proceedings for non-compliance with investor grievance redressal requirements.

## Important Dates

- Order Date: 29 September 2025

## Impact Assessment

This enforcement action specifically targets Nimbus Industries Limited for failure to redress investor grievances. The general remittance order indicates SEBI is pursuing recovery of amounts owed to investors. Limited broader market impact, but demonstrates SEBI's continued focus on investor protection and enforcement against non-compliant entities.
