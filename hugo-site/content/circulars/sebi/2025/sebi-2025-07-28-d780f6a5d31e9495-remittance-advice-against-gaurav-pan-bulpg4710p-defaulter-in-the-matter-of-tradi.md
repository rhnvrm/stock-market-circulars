## Summary

SEBI has issued a remittance advice against Gaurav (PAN: BULPG4710P) as part of recovery proceedings under Recovery Certificate No. 8475 of 2024. This action relates to trading activities in the scrip of Vikas Proppant and Granite Limited.

## Key Points

- Individual defaulter: Gaurav (PAN: BULPG4710P)
- Recovery Certificate No. 8475 of 2024
- Related to trading activities in Vikas Proppant and Granite Limited
- Part of SEBI's enforcement and recovery proceedings

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an enforcement action against a specific individual.

## Compliance Requirements

- Subject individual must comply with recovery proceedings
- Payment of dues as per the recovery certificate
- Compliance with any restrictions imposed during proceedings

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate issued: 2024
- Remittance advice date: July 28, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact as this is an individual enforcement action. The action demonstrates SEBI's continued efforts to recover dues from defaulters and maintain market integrity through enforcement proceedings.
