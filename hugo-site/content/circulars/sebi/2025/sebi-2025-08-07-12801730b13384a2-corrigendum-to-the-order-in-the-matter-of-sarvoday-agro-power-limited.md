## Summary

SEBI has issued a corrigendum to correct PAN numbers mentioned in its order dated July 31, 2025 (Reference: QJA/MN/ERO/ERO/31577/2025-26) in the matter of Sarvoday Agro Power Limited. The correction affects PAN numbers of two noticees in the original order.

## Key Points

- Corrigendum issued by N. Murugan, Quasi-Judicial Authority, SEBI
- Corrects PAN numbers in order QJA/MN/ERO/ERO/31577/2025-26 dated July 31, 2025
- Noticee No. 3 PAN corrected from 'AIDPV1683E' to 'AIDPV1638E'
- Noticee No. 10 PAN corrected from 'BREPM799E' to 'BREPM7999E'
- Original order must be read with this corrigendum

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is purely an administrative correction to ensure accuracy of PAN numbers in the enforcement order.

## Compliance Requirements

All parties must refer to the corrected PAN numbers when dealing with matters related to the original order dated July 31, 2025.

## Important Dates

- **August 07, 2025**: Date of corrigendum issuance
- **July 31, 2025**: Date of original order being corrected

## Impact Assessment

Minimal impact as this is an administrative correction. The corrigendum ensures proper identification of the concerned noticees in the enforcement proceedings against Sarvoday Agro Power Limited.
