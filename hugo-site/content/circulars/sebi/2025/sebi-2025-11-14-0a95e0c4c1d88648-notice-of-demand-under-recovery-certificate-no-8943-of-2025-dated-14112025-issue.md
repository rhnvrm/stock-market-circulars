## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand under Recovery Certificate No. 8943 of 2025 dated November 14, 2025, against B. Suryanarayana Raju (PAN: ACEPB2811N) in connection with the matter of Satyam Computer Services Ltd. This notice is part of SEBI's enforcement and recovery proceedings.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8943 of 2025 issued on November 14, 2025
- Notice issued against B. Suryanarayana Raju (PAN: ACEPB2811N)
- Related to Satyam Computer Services Ltd matter
- Part of SEBI's enforcement and recovery proceedings

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI recovery procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

B. Suryanarayana Raju is required to comply with the demand notice as per the recovery certificate issued by SEBI.

## Important Dates

- **November 14, 2025**: Date of Recovery Certificate No. 8943 of 2025

## Impact Assessment

This notice is specific to enforcement action against an individual in relation to the Satyam Computer Services Ltd case. The impact is limited to the concerned party and does not affect broader market operations. This continues SEBI's ongoing enforcement efforts in the Satyam matter.
