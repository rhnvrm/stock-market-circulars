## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order for Recovery Certificate No. RC8541 of 2025 dated January 14, 2025 in respect of Sumit Bhoot (PAN: BLPPB9770D) in the matter of Eros International Media Limited. This release order signifies the conclusion or modification of recovery proceedings initiated against the individual.

## Key Points

- Release Order issued for Recovery Certificate No. RC8541 of 2025
- Original Recovery Certificate dated January 14, 2025
- Person concerned: Sumit Bhoot (PAN: BLPPB9770D)
- Related entity: Eros International Media Limited
- Order type: Release Order indicating resolution of recovery matter

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action resolution document.

## Compliance Requirements

No ongoing compliance requirements. The release order concludes the recovery proceedings for the concerned party.

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate Date: January 14, 2025
- Release Order Date: November 20, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The release order has limited market impact as it pertains to resolution of recovery proceedings against a specific individual in relation to Eros International Media Limited. The order indicates that the recovery matter has been resolved, either through payment, settlement, or other resolution mechanism. This is a standard enforcement action conclusion and does not affect broader market operations or regulatory framework.
