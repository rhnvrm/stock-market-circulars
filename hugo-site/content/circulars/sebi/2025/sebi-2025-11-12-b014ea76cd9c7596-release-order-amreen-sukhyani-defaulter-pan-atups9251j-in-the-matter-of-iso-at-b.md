## Summary

SEBI has issued a release order regarding Recovery Certificate No. 8360 of 2024 against AMREEN SUKHYANI (PAN: ATUPS9251J) in connection with an Initial Public Offering (ISO) matter at the Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE). This order pertains to recovery proceedings initiated against the defaulter.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8360 of 2024 issued against AMREEN SUKHYANI
- Matter relates to ISO (Initial Public Offering) at BSE
- Defaulter identified with PAN: ATUPS9251J
- Release order issued by SEBI under enforcement proceedings

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI recovery regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

This release order is specific to enforcement proceedings against the named defaulter AMREEN SUKHYANI. No broader compliance requirements for market participants.

## Important Dates

- Certificate Issue Date: 2024
- Release Order Date: November 12, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is an individual enforcement action with limited market-wide impact. The release order pertains to recovery proceedings against a specific defaulter in an ISO matter at BSE. Market participants are not directly affected, though it demonstrates SEBI's continued enforcement of securities regulations and recovery of dues from defaulters.
