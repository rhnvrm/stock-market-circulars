## Summary

SEBI has issued a remittance order for Recovery Certificate No. 8760 of 2025 against Manish Mishra in connection with enforcement proceedings related to Pressure Sensitive (India) Limited. This order pertains to the recovery of dues from the defaulter.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8760 of 2025 issued against Manish Mishra
- Matter relates to Pressure Sensitive (India) Limited
- Remittance order for compliance with recovery proceedings
- Enforcement action for recovery of outstanding dues

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI recovery framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Manish Mishra must comply with the recovery certificate terms
- Payment of outstanding dues as specified in the recovery proceedings
- Adherence to remittance order conditions

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate No. 8760 of 2025 (reference number)
- Order date: July 28, 2025 (implied from filing)

## Impact Assessment

This enforcement action specifically targets an individual defaulter in connection with Pressure Sensitive (India) Limited. The impact is primarily on the named individual and the recovery of dues owed to SEBI or affected parties.
