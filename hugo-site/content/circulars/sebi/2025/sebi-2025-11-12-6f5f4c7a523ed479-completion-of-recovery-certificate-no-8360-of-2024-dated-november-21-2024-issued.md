## Summary

SEBI has completed Recovery Certificate No. 8360 of 2024 dated November 21, 2024 against AMREEN SUKHYANI (PAN: ATUPS9251J) in relation to an ISO matter at BSE. This represents the conclusion of recovery proceedings against the defaulter.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8360 of 2024 has been completed
- Certificate was issued on November 21, 2024
- Defaulter: AMREEN SUKHYANI (PAN: ATUPS9251J)
- Matter relates to ISO (Initial Service Offering) at BSE
- Recovery proceedings have been concluded

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a completion notice for an enforcement action.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. This circular is informational regarding the completion of recovery proceedings.

## Important Dates

- November 21, 2024: Recovery Certificate issued
- November 12, 2025: Completion notice published

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact as it pertains to completion of recovery proceedings against an individual defaulter. It demonstrates SEBI's ongoing enforcement activities and successful recovery actions.
