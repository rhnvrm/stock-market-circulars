## Summary

SEBI has issued a completion order for Recovery Certificate No. 8934 of 2025 against Pinal Rajeshbhai Mehta in the matter of Jay Energy & S. Energies Limited. This order marks the completion of recovery proceedings initiated by SEBI.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8934 of 2025 issued against Pinal Rajeshbhai Mehta
- Related to Jay Energy & S. Energies Limited matter
- Completion order indicating conclusion of recovery proceedings
- Enforcement action by SEBI

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action order.

## Compliance Requirements

Pinal Rajeshbhai Mehta is subject to the recovery proceedings as per Recovery Certificate No. 8934 of 2025.

## Important Dates

- Order Date: November 28, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This completion order indicates that SEBI has concluded recovery proceedings against the individual in relation to Jay Energy & S. Energies Limited. The impact is specific to the parties involved in the enforcement action.
