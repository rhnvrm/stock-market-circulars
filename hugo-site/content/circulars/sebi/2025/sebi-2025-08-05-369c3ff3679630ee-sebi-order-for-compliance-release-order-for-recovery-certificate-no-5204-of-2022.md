## Summary

SEBI has issued an order for compliance regarding the release of Recovery Certificate No. 5204 of 2022 against Blink Share and Comtrade Pvt. Ltd. in the matter of Excel Castronics Limited. This represents a completion of enforcement proceedings related to recovery of dues.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 5204 of 2022 has been released
- Action taken against Blink Share and Comtrade Pvt. Ltd.
- Related to Excel Castronics Limited matter
- Compliance order indicates completion of recovery proceedings

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement completion order.

## Compliance Requirements

- Affected entities must comply with the recovery certificate terms
- No additional compliance requirements for other market participants

## Important Dates

- Order Date: August 5, 2025
- Recovery Certificate: No. 5204 of 2022

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact on broader market operations. Specific to the named entities and completion of existing enforcement proceedings. Demonstrates SEBI's continued enforcement of recovery mechanisms.
