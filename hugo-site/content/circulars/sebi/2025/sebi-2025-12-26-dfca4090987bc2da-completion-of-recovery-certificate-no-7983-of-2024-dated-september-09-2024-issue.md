## Summary

SEBI has issued a notification regarding the completion of recovery proceedings under Recovery Certificate No. 7983 of 2024 dated September 09, 2024. The recovery certificate was issued against Anil Kumar Sharma (PAN: CGUPS0090N) in connection with the matter of Sanco Industries Limited.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 7983 of 2024 has been completed
- Original certificate dated September 09, 2024
- Issued against: Anil Kumar Sharma (PAN: CGUPS0090N)
- Related entity: Sanco Industries Limited
- Administrative closure of recovery proceedings

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a status update on existing recovery proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. This circular serves as a notification of the completion of recovery proceedings.

## Important Dates

- **Recovery Certificate Date**: September 09, 2024
- **Completion Notification Date**: December 26, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact as it pertains to the administrative completion of recovery proceedings against a specific individual in relation to Sanco Industries Limited. The notification serves as a formal closure of the recovery certificate proceedings and does not introduce new regulatory requirements or affect broader market operations.
