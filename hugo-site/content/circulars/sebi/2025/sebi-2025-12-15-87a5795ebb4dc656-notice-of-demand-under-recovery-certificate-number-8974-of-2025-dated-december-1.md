## Summary

SEBI has issued Notice of Demand under Recovery Certificate Number 8974 of 2025 dated December 15, 2025, to multiple defaulters in the matter of CIG Realty Fund-Scheme I, II and IV. The defaulters include Shri Hitender Malhotra (PAN: CDUPM8087L), Unitech Advisors India Private Limited (now known as Auram Asset Management Pvt. Ltd.) (PAN: AACCC5880D), Sanjay Chandra (PAN: AACPC5804C), Ramesh Chandra (PAN: AACPC5805D), and Ajay Chandra (PAN: AACPC5803F). All entities are held jointly and severally liable for the recovery amount.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate Number: 8974 of 2025
- Date of Issue: December 15, 2025
- Five defaulters named with joint and several liability
- Related to CIG Realty Fund-Scheme I, II and IV
- Unitech Advisors India has been renamed to Auram Asset Management Pvt. Ltd.
- Formal enforcement action initiated for recovery of dues

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI recovery procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- All named defaulters must comply with the demand notice
- Payment of the recovery amount as specified in the certificate
- Joint and several liability means SEBI can recover the full amount from any one or combination of the defaulters
- Non-compliance may lead to further enforcement actions including attachment of assets

## Important Dates

- **December 15, 2025**: Date of issuance of Recovery Certificate No. 8974/2025
- Payment deadline and other compliance timelines would be specified in the detailed notice

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited direct market impact as this is an enforcement action against specific entities and individuals. However, it demonstrates SEBI's continued focus on recovering dues from defaulters in investment fund matters.

**Entities Affected**: 
- Hitender Malhotra (individual)
- Auram Asset Management Pvt. Ltd. (formerly Unitech Advisors India Private Limited)
- Sanjay Chandra (individual)
- Ramesh Chandra (individual)
- Ajay Chandra (individual)

**Investors**: Investors in CIG Realty Fund schemes may have potential recovery of their dues if SEBI successfully recovers the amounts from the defaulters.

**Industry Impact**: Reinforces regulatory accountability for fund managers and promoters. Sets precedent for joint and several liability in fund management violations.
