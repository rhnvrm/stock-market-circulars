## Summary

SEBI has issued Notice of Demand (NoD) under Recovery Certificate No. 8953 of 2025 against Mr. Himanshu Mahendrabhai Patel, Mr. Raj Mahendrabhai Patel, Mr. Mahendrabhai Bechardas Patel, Mr. Kokilaben Mahendrabhai Patel, Mr. Avaniben Kirankumar Patel, and Mr. Jaydev Zala for providing unauthorized stock recommendations through social media channels. This enforcement action follows violations of securities regulations regarding unregistered investment advice activities.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8953 of 2025 issued against six individuals
- Violation relates to stock recommendations provided via social media platforms
- Individuals named: Himanshu Mahendrabhai Patel, Raj Mahendrabhai Patel, Mahendrabhai Bechardas Patel, Kokilaben Mahendrabhai Patel, Avaniben Kirankumar Patel, and Jaydev Zala
- Enforcement action for unauthorized investment advisory activities
- Notice of Demand issued for recovery of dues/penalties

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This document represents enforcement of existing regulations prohibiting unauthorized investment advice and stock recommendations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Entities and individuals must not provide stock recommendations or investment advice without proper SEBI registration as Investment Advisers
- Social media platforms cannot be used for unregistered investment advisory services
- Parties named in the Recovery Certificate must comply with payment demands as specified
- Failure to comply may result in further recovery proceedings and enforcement actions

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate issuance: 2025
- Document publication: November 28, 2025
- Payment deadline: As specified in the Recovery Certificate (refer to PDF for specific dates)

## Impact Assessment

**Regulatory Impact:** This enforcement action reinforces SEBI's stance against unauthorized investment advice and stock recommendations on social media platforms. It serves as a deterrent for unregistered entities and individuals providing such services.

**Market Impact:** Limited direct market impact as this affects specific individuals. However, it signals continued regulatory scrutiny of social media-based stock recommendation activities.

**Investor Protection:** Strengthens investor protection by taking action against unauthorized advisors who may mislead retail investors through social media channels.

**Compliance Implications:** Market participants must ensure proper registration and licensing before providing any form of investment advice or stock recommendations, regardless of the medium used.
