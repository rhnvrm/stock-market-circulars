## Summary

SEBI has issued a release order for Recovery Certificate No. 8639 of 2025 against Parul Sahu in connection with matters related to Pinnacle Market Investment Advisory Pvt. Ltd. This represents an enforcement action by SEBI for compliance violations.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8639 of 2025 issued against Parul Sahu
- Related to Pinnacle Market Investment Advisory Pvt. Ltd.
- SEBI compliance enforcement action
- Release order indicates progression in recovery proceedings

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes announced. This is an enforcement action under existing regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Investment advisors must ensure compliance with SEBI regulations
- Non-compliance may result in enforcement actions including recovery proceedings
- Market participants should review their compliance frameworks

## Important Dates

- Order Date: August 13, 2025
- Recovery Certificate No.: 8639 of 2025

## Impact Assessment

This enforcement action serves as a reminder to investment advisors and market participants about the importance of regulatory compliance. While specific to Pinnacle Market Investment Advisory and Parul Sahu, it highlights SEBI's ongoing enforcement activities in the investment advisory space.
