## Summary

SEBI has issued Certificate No. 8922 of 2025 along with Attachment Proceedings (A.P. Nos. 14996 and 14998 of 2025) against Smart Infraproperties Private Limited (PAN: AAQCS6937F) in connection with the matter of Cressanda Solutions Limited. This represents enforcement action involving attachment of assets as part of recovery proceedings.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8922 of 2025 issued
- Attachment Proceedings: A.P. Nos. 14996 and 14998 of 2025
- Target entity: Smart Infraproperties Private Limited (PAN: AAQCS6937F)
- Related matter: Cressanda Solutions Limited
- Enforcement action involves asset attachment procedures
- Certificate issued on 06.11.2025

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action under existing recovery and attachment proceedings framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Smart Infraproperties Private Limited must comply with attachment proceedings requirements
- Entities subject to attachment must respond as per certificate terms
- Compliance with recovery certificate procedures and timelines
- Assets identified under attachment proceedings subject to SEBI control

## Important Dates

- Certificate Date: 06.11.2025
- Recovery Certificate: No. 8922 of 2025
- Attachment Proceedings: A.P. Nos. 14996 and 14998 of 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - Enforcement action specifically targets Smart Infraproperties Private Limited in connection with Cressanda Solutions Limited matter. Impact limited to named entities and their associated stakeholders.

**Operational Impact**: High for Smart Infraproperties Private Limited - Assets subject to attachment proceedings, restricting operational flexibility and financial transactions pending resolution of recovery matters.

**Regulatory Precedent**: Demonstrates SEBI's continued enforcement of recovery proceedings against entities involved in securities market violations, using attachment proceedings as a mechanism to secure recovery amounts.
