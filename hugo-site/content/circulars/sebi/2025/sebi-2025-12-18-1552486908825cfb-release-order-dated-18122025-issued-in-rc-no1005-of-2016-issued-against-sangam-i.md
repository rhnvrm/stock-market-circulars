## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order dated December 18, 2025, in Recovery Certificate No. 1005 of 2016 issued against Sangam Infotech Pvt. Ltd. in the matter of Sangam Infotech Ltd. This order pertains to recovery proceedings initiated by SEBI.

## Key Points

- Release Order issued on December 18, 2025
- Recovery Certificate Number: RC No. 1005 of 2016
- Entity: Sangam Infotech Pvt. Ltd.
- Matter relates to: Sangam Infotech Ltd.
- Type: Recovery proceedings

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is an entity-specific enforcement action.

## Compliance Requirements

The release order concludes recovery proceedings against the named entity. Specific compliance requirements would be detailed in the full order document.

## Important Dates

- **Order Date**: December 18, 2025
- **Original RC Date**: 2016 (RC No. 1005 of 2016)

## Impact Assessment

This is an entity-specific release order with no broader market impact. The order pertains to the conclusion of recovery proceedings that were initiated in 2016 against Sangam Infotech Pvt. Ltd. The limited scope affects only the parties named in the recovery certificate.
