SEBI has issued a release order dated December 19, 2025, confirming the completion of Recovery Certificate No. 8884 of 2025. The recovery certificate was issued against Abhinesh Kumar (PAN: AGWPK6944M) in the matter of Initial Selling Obligation (ISO) at BSE. The completion of the recovery certificate indicates that the enforcement proceedings have been concluded.
