## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC8832 of 2025 under A.P. Nos. 14818 and 14819 of 2025 against Sumit Popli (PAN: ANVPP2968A) in the matter of Le Waterina Resorts and Hotels Ltd. This certificate indicates enforcement action for recovery of dues/penalties.

## Key Points

- Recovery certificate issued against Sumit Popli (PAN: ANVPP2968A)
- Related to Le Waterina Resorts and Hotels Ltd. matter
- Certificate number: RC8832 of 2025
- Associated with adjudication proceedings A.P. Nos. 14818 and 14819 of 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No specific regulatory changes introduced through this recovery certificate.

## Compliance Requirements

- Sumit Popli is required to pay the dues as specified in the recovery certificate
- Failure to comply may result in further enforcement measures including attachment of assets
- Certificate enables SEBI to recover amounts through tax recovery mechanisms

## Important Dates

- Certificate issued: August 5, 2025
- Recovery proceedings initiated under the mentioned A.P. numbers

## Impact Assessment

This recovery certificate has limited market impact as it targets a specific individual. However, it demonstrates SEBI's continued enforcement efforts against non-compliance. Investors in Le Waterina Resorts and Hotels Ltd. should be aware of the regulatory issues involving associated persons.
