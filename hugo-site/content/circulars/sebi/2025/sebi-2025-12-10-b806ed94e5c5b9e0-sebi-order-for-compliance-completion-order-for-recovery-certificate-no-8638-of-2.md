## Summary

SEBI has issued a completion order for Recovery Certificate No. 8638 of 2025 against Pinnacle Market Investment Advisory Pvt. Ltd. This order pertains to recovery proceedings initiated by SEBI for compliance with regulatory requirements and recovery of dues.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8638 of 2025 issued against Pinnacle Market Investment Advisory Pvt. Ltd.
- Completion order signifies conclusion of recovery proceedings
- Enforcement action by SEBI against investment advisory firm
- Order pertains to regulatory compliance and recovery of outstanding amounts

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement order specific to Pinnacle Market Investment Advisory Pvt. Ltd.

## Compliance Requirements

- Pinnacle Market Investment Advisory Pvt. Ltd. must comply with the recovery certificate terms
- Payment of outstanding dues as specified in the recovery certificate
- Adherence to SEBI's enforcement directions

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate No.: 8638 of 2025
- Order Date: December 2025 (specific date to be confirmed from PDF)

## Impact Assessment

This order has limited market-wide impact as it is specific to Pinnacle Market Investment Advisory Pvt. Ltd. However, it demonstrates SEBI's continued enforcement activities against entities failing to comply with regulatory requirements. Investment advisory clients of the firm may need to review their relationship and service continuity.
