## Summary

Shri Sandip Pradhan took charge as Whole Time Member of the Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) on December 04, 2025. Prior to this appointment, he served as Director General of Income Tax (Investigation) in Pune. In his new role, he will oversee Market Intermediaries Regulation and Supervision Department, Alternative Investment Fund and Foreign Portfolio Investors Department, Information Technology Department, Office of Investor Assistance and Education, and National Institute of Securities Market.

## Key Points

- Shri Sandip Pradhan assumed office as SEBI Whole Time Member on December 04, 2025
- Previously served as Director General of Income Tax (Investigation) in Pune
- Educational background: B.Tech in Computer Science from IIT Kanpur
- Over three and half decades of experience in Income Tax Department across investigation, assessment, administration, judicial matters, and systems
- Worked in Ahmedabad, Surat, Mumbai, and Pune during his career
- Previously served as Director General, Sports Authority of Gujarat and Sports Authority of India
- Spearheaded Khelo India Scheme and Target Olympic Podium Scheme (TOPS)
- Served as Member Convener of Olympic Task Force formed by Government of India

## Departments Under Supervision

Shri Pradhan will handle the following departments:
- Market Intermediaries Regulation and Supervision Department
- Alternative Investment Fund and Foreign Portfolio Investors Department
- Information Technology Department
- Office of Investor Assistance and Education
- National Institute of Securities Market
