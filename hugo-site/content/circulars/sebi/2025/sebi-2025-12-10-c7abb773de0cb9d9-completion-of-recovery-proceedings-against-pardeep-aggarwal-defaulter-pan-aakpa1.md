## Summary

SEBI has issued a completion order for recovery proceedings against Pardeep Aggarwal (PAN: AAKPA1255B), who was identified as a defaulter in the matter of Priyadarshini Spinning Mills Ltd. The proceedings were initiated under Recovery Certificate No. 385 of 2014 and have now been concluded.

## Key Points

- Recovery proceedings initiated under Recovery Certificate No. 385 of 2014
- Defaulter: Pardeep Aggarwal (PAN: AAKPA1255B)
- Matter relates to Priyadarshini Spinning Mills Ltd
- Recovery proceedings have been completed
- Order issued on December 10, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a completion notice for existing recovery proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. This document marks the completion of recovery proceedings against the defaulter.

## Important Dates

- **Recovery Certificate Date**: 2014 (Certificate No. 385)
- **Completion Order Date**: December 10, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a case-specific enforcement action with minimal market-wide impact. The completion of recovery proceedings indicates closure of a long-standing matter from 2014. The impact is limited to the individual defaulter and does not affect broader market operations or trading activities.
