## Summary

SEBI has issued a Completion Order for Recovery Certificate No. 4512 of 2022 against Minaben Prafulbhai Shah (PAN: COYPS9153J) in the matter of SMS Techsoft (India) Ltd. This order confirms the completion of recovery proceedings that were initiated against the individual.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 4512 of 2022 proceedings have been completed
- Individual concerned: Minaben Prafulbhai Shah (PAN: COYPS9153J)
- Related entity: SMS Techsoft (India) Ltd.
- Order type: Completion Order indicating closure of recovery proceedings
- Order date: November 21, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement completion order.

## Compliance Requirements

This completion order indicates that the recovery proceedings have been concluded. No further compliance actions are required from the concerned individual in relation to Recovery Certificate No. 4512 of 2022.

## Important Dates

- **Order Date:** November 21, 2025
- **Recovery Certificate:** RC No. 4512 of 2022

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This is a completion order that closes existing recovery proceedings rather than initiating new enforcement action.

**Operational Impact:** Low - Affects only the specific individual (Minaben Prafulbhai Shah) in relation to SMS Techsoft (India) Ltd. The completion order suggests the recovery process has been satisfied or concluded.

**Entity Impact:** Limited to the individual named in the recovery certificate. No broader market or sectoral implications.
