## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order dated December 04, 2025 under Recovery Certificate No. 307 of 2014, which was drawn against M/s Kalbadevi Finance Limited in relation to the matter of Kolar Biotech Limited. This order pertains to enforcement and recovery proceedings initiated by SEBI.

## Key Points

- Release Order issued under Recovery Certificate No. 307 of 2014
- Entity involved: M/s Kalbadevi Finance Limited
- Related matter: Kolar Biotech Limited
- Order date: December 04, 2025
- Pertains to SEBI enforcement and recovery proceedings

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action related to existing regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

Compliance requirements specific to M/s Kalbadevi Finance Limited as per the recovery certificate and release order terms.

## Important Dates

- **Release Order Date:** December 04, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This release order has limited market-wide impact as it pertains to specific recovery proceedings against M/s Kalbadevi Finance Limited. The order is part of SEBI's enforcement mechanism for recovery of dues in the matter of Kolar Biotech Limited.
