## Summary

SEBI has issued a General Remittance Order dated December 02, 2025, in Recovery Certificate No. 8919 of 2025 against Arun Ramachandra Gole (PAN: AMYPG6141Q). This order is issued in the matter of Generic Engineering Construction and Projects Limited as part of SEBI's debt recovery proceedings.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate Number: 8919 of 2025
- Individual against whom order is issued: Arun Ramachandra Gole
- PAN of individual: AMYPG6141Q
- Related entity: Generic Engineering Construction and Projects Limited
- Order date: December 02, 2025
- Order type: General Remittance Order

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced through this order. This is an enforcement action related to existing recovery proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

Arun Ramachandra Gole is required to comply with the terms of the General Remittance Order as specified in Recovery Certificate No. 8919 of 2025. The specific payment or compliance obligations would be detailed in the full order document.

## Important Dates

- Order Date: December 02, 2025
- Recovery Certificate Issue Date: 2025

## Impact Assessment

This order has limited market-wide impact as it is specific to an individual enforcement action. The impact is confined to Arun Ramachandra Gole and parties related to Generic Engineering Construction and Projects Limited. General Remittance Orders are part of SEBI's debt recovery mechanism under its enforcement framework.
