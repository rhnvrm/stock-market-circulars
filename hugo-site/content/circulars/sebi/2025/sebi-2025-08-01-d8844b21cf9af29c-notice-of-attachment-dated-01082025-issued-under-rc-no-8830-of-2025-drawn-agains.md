## Summary

SEBI has issued a notice of attachment dated 01.08.2025 under Recovery Certificate No. 8830 of 2025 against Sudhir Agarwal HUF in connection with dealing in illiquid stock options at the Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE).

## Key Points

- Attachment notice issued against Sudhir Agarwal HUF
- Related to illiquid stock options trading violations at BSE
- Recovery Certificate No. 8830 of 2025
- Notice dated August 1, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action under existing regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Affected party must comply with attachment orders
- Banks and depositories must freeze specified accounts
- No transactions permitted in attached accounts without SEBI approval

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: August 1, 2025
- Recovery Certificate: RC No. 8830 of 2025

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact to specific entity (Sudhir Agarwal HUF). Demonstrates SEBI's continued enforcement against illiquid stock options manipulation. Banks and depositories handling the entity's accounts must implement freezing measures.
