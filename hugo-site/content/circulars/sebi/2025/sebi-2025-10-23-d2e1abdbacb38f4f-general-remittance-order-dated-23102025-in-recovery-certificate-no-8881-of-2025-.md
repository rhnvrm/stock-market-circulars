## Summary

SEBI has issued a General Remittance Order dated October 23, 2025, under Recovery Certificate No. 8881 of 2025 against Thousand Keys and its proprietor Tapas Das (PAN: ANKPD3883D). The order relates to enforcement action taken for operating unregistered investment advisory services in violation of SEBI regulations.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8881 of 2025 issued against Thousand Keys
- Proprietor identified as Tapas Das (PAN: ANKPD3883D)
- Violation: Operating unregistered investment advisory services
- Order type: General Remittance Order enabling recovery proceedings
- Order date: October 23, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This order enforces existing requirement that investment advisors must be registered with SEBI under the SEBI (Investment Advisers) Regulations, 2013.

## Compliance Requirements

- All persons providing investment advisory services must obtain SEBI registration
- Operating without registration is a violation subject to enforcement action and recovery proceedings
- Entities must cease unregistered advisory operations immediately

## Important Dates

- **October 23, 2025**: General Remittance Order issued
- Recovery proceedings active under Recovery Certificate No. 8881 of 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Entity Impact**: High - Recovery proceedings initiated against Thousand Keys and Tapas Das for operating without registration.

**Market Impact**: Low - Action against specific entity, no broader market implications.

**Compliance Impact**: Reinforces mandatory registration requirements for investment advisors. Serves as deterrent against operating unregistered advisory services.
