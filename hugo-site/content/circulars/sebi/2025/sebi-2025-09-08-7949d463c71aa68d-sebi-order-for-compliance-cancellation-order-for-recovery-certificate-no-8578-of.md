## Summary

SEBI has issued a cancellation order for Recovery Certificate No. 8578 of 2025 that was issued against Mohd Faisal in connection with proceedings related to Biocon Limited. This order formally cancels the recovery certificate, indicating resolution of the compliance matter.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8578 of 2025 against Mohd Faisal has been cancelled
- The certificate was related to Biocon Limited proceedings
- Order issued on September 8, 2025
- Formal compliance action resolution

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework changes introduced. This is an administrative order cancelling a previously issued recovery certificate.

## Compliance Requirements

- No new compliance requirements for market participants
- Resolution of individual enforcement matter
- Standard administrative closure procedures apply

## Important Dates

- Order Date: September 8, 2025
- Recovery Certificate Cancellation: Effective immediately

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this represents the resolution of an individual compliance matter. The cancellation of the recovery certificate indicates that the enforcement action against the individual has been resolved, with no broader implications for Biocon Limited or the securities market.
