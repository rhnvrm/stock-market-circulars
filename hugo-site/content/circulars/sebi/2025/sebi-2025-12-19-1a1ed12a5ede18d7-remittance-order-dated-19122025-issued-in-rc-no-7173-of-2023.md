## Summary

SEBI has issued a remittance order dated December 19, 2025 in relation to Recovery Certificate No. 7173 of 2023. This order pertains to the remittance and collection of outstanding monetary dues from entities/noticees as determined through SEBI's enforcement and recovery proceedings.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate issued under RC No. 7173 of 2023
- Remittance order dated December 19, 2025
- Part of SEBI's enforcement and recovery mechanism for outstanding dues
- Order facilitates collection of penalties/dues through prescribed recovery procedures

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an enforcement and recovery action under existing regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Entities named in the recovery certificate must comply with remittance demands
- Payment of outstanding dues as per the recovery certificate
- Failure to comply may lead to further recovery action including attachment of assets
- Recovery proceedings under SEBI Act provisions

## Important Dates

- **Order Date**: December 19, 2025
- **RC Number**: 7173 of 2023
- Payment deadlines as specified in the order document

## Impact Assessment

**For Affected Entities**: High impact - entities subject to this recovery certificate face mandatory payment obligations and potential asset attachment if non-compliant.

**For Market**: Low impact - this is a specific enforcement action against particular entities and does not affect broader market operations or regulations.

**Compliance Burden**: High for named entities who must arrange for payment of outstanding dues to avoid further recovery action including potential asset seizure or bank account attachments.
