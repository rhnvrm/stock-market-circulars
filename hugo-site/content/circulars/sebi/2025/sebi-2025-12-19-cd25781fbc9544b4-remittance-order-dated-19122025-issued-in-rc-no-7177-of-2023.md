## Summary

SEBI has issued a Remittance Order dated December 19, 2025 in the matter of Recovery Certificate No. 7177 of 2023. This order pertains to the remittance of funds recovered as part of enforcement proceedings.

## Key Points

- Order issued on December 19, 2025
- Related to Recovery Certificate No. 7177 of 2023
- Part of SEBI's enforcement and recovery mechanism
- Specific to individual case proceedings

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a case-specific enforcement order.

## Compliance Requirements

This order applies to the specific parties involved in RC No. 7177 of 2023. The concerned parties must comply with the remittance directions specified in the order.

## Important Dates

- **Order Date**: December 19, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This remittance order has limited market-wide impact as it pertains to specific recovery proceedings under RC No. 7177 of 2023. The order is relevant primarily to the parties directly involved in the enforcement case.
