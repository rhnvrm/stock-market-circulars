## Summary

SEBI has issued a notice of attachment (Order Nos. 14940 & 14941 of 2025 dated October 17, 2025) for bank accounts and demat accounts of Spectrum Technoprojects Pvt Ltd (PAN: AAOCS7016F) under Recovery Certificate No. 8899 of 2025. The action is taken in connection with the matter of dealing in illiquid stocks options at BSE.

## Key Points

- Entity: Spectrum Technoprojects Pvt Ltd
- PAN: AAOCS7016F
- Recovery Certificate No.: 8899 of 2025
- Order Numbers: 14940 & 14941 of 2025
- Notice Date: October 17, 2025
- Matter: Dealing in Illiquid Stocks Options at BSE
- Action: Attachment of bank accounts and demat accounts

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI recovery proceedings framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Banks and depository participants must comply with the attachment orders
- The defaulter entity's accounts are frozen pending recovery proceedings
- Third parties holding funds or securities of the defaulter must not release them without SEBI authorization

## Important Dates

- **October 17, 2025**: Notice of attachment issued (Order Nos. 14940 & 14941 of 2025)

## Impact Assessment

**Entity-Specific Impact**: Severe impact on Spectrum Technoprojects Pvt Ltd as all bank and demat accounts are attached, effectively freezing the entity's financial operations.

**Market Impact**: Limited direct market impact. This is a targeted enforcement action against a specific defaulter entity involved in illiquid stock options violations.

**Precedent Value**: Demonstrates SEBI's continued commitment to recovering dues from entities involved in market manipulation and illiquid stock schemes, serving as a deterrent for similar violations.
