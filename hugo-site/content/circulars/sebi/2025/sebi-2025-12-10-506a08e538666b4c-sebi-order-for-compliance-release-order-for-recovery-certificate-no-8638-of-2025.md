## Summary

SEBI has issued a release order for Recovery Certificate No. 8638 of 2025 against Pinnacle Market Investment Advisory Pvt. Ltd. This order indicates the completion or settlement of recovery proceedings initiated by SEBI against the entity.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8638 of 2025 issued against Pinnacle Market Investment Advisory Pvt. Ltd.
- Release order signifies completion of recovery proceedings
- Entity-specific enforcement action by SEBI
- Part of SEBI's regulatory enforcement mechanism

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an administrative order relating to specific recovery proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

This order is specific to Pinnacle Market Investment Advisory Pvt. Ltd. No new compliance requirements for other market participants.

## Important Dates

- Order Date: December 10, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to the specific entity named in the recovery proceedings. This is a routine enforcement action with no broader market implications. Investment advisory firms should note SEBI's active enforcement of compliance obligations and recovery proceedings against defaulting entities.
