## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Attachment of Bank Account pursuant to Recovery Certificate No. 4286 of 2021. The notice is directed against Mr. Sanjay Jethalal Soni, Ms. Krupa Sanjay Soni and M/s. J M Soni Consultancy in the matter of Oregon Commercial Limited (now known as Saianand Commercial Limited). This is part of SEBI's enforcement and recovery proceedings.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 4286 of 2021 is being enforced
- Notice of attachment issued against bank accounts of the named parties
- Parties involved: Mr. Sanjay Jethalal Soni, Ms. Krupa Sanjay Soni, and M/s. J M Soni Consultancy
- Related to Oregon Commercial Limited (now Saianand Commercial Limited)
- This is a compliance order under SEBI's recovery proceedings

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI recovery framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- The named parties must comply with the bank account attachment notice
- Banks holding accounts of the named parties must freeze/attach the specified accounts
- Non-compliance may attract further enforcement action

## Important Dates

- Order Date: December 24, 2025
- Recovery Certificate: No. 4286 of 2021

## Impact Assessment

**Impact on Parties:** High - Bank accounts of Mr. Sanjay Jethalal Soni, Ms. Krupa Sanjay Soni and M/s. J M Soni Consultancy will be attached, restricting their access to funds.

**Market Impact:** Low to Medium - Limited direct market impact, though signals SEBI's active enforcement of recovery proceedings related to Oregon Commercial Limited/Saianand Commercial Limited.

**Compliance Impact:** Demonstrates SEBI's continued efforts in recovery proceedings and enforcement actions against defaulting parties.
