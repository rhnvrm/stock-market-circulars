## Summary

SEBI and the Investor Education and Protection Fund Authority (IEPFA) are jointly organizing the third Niveshak Shivir in Amritsar, Punjab on November 01, 2025. The event will be held in collaboration with Market Infrastructure Institutions (NSE, BSE, NSDL, CDSL) and multiple Registrar and Transfer Agents. The camp aims to assist shareholders with unpaid dividend transfers, KYC and nomination updates, and resolution of pending IEPFA claims.

## Key Points

- Third Niveshak Shivir to be held on November 01, 2025 in Amritsar
- Joint initiative by SEBI and IEPFA, Ministry of Corporate Affairs
- Participating MIIs: NSE, BSE, NSDL, and CDSL
- Participating RTAs: KFIN Technologies Limited, MUFG Intime India Private Limited, Bigshare Services Pvt. Ltd., Datamatics Business Solutions Ltd., Purva Sharegistry India Pvt
- Venue: Jashn E Mahal, Dr. Surat Singh Rd, near Railway crossing, Guru Arjun Nagar, Kot Khalsa, Amritsar - 143002
- Timing: 10 AM to 4 PM
- Shareholders must bring official government identification documents (Voter ID, PAN card, Driver's licence, etc.)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is an investor service initiative.

## Compliance Requirements

Not applicable. This is a voluntary investor assistance program with no compliance obligations.

## Important Dates

- **November 01, 2025**: Niveshak Shivir event date at Amritsar (10 AM to 4 PM)

## Impact Assessment

This is a localized investor service camp with no market-wide impact. The event provides direct assistance to shareholders in the Amritsar region for:

- **Unpaid Dividends**: Facilitation of transfer of unpaid dividends up to 7 years old
- **KYC Updates**: Assistance with KYC and nomination updates with companies
- **IEPFA Claims**: Resolution of pending claims for unclaimed and unpaid shares and dividends

The initiative is part of SEBI's ongoing investor education and protection efforts but does not affect trading operations, listing requirements, or broader market regulations.
