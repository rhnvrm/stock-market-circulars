## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order dated December 17, 2025, in Recovery Certificate No. 7881 of 2024 concerning Arun Panchariya. This order relates to the matter of Global Depositary Receipts (GDR) issue of Winsome Yarns Limited. A release order typically signifies the lifting of attachment or other recovery measures previously imposed on the individual.

## Key Points

- Release order issued in RC No. 7881 of 2024
- Concerns recovery proceedings against Arun Panchariya
- Related to GDR issue of Winsome Yarns Limited
- Order dated December 17, 2025
- Indicates resolution or modification of enforcement action

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement-related release order specific to an individual case.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements for market participants. This order is specific to the named individual in the recovery proceedings.

## Important Dates

- **Release Order Date**: December 17, 2025
- Recovery Certificate reference: RC No. 7881 of 2024

## Impact Assessment

The release order has limited market-wide impact as it pertains to individual enforcement proceedings. It may indicate:

- Conclusion of recovery proceedings against Arun Panchariya
- Partial or full satisfaction of recovery demands
- Lifting of attachment orders on assets
- Resolution of the specific matter related to Winsome Yarns Limited's GDR issue

The order is relevant primarily to the individual concerned and has minimal direct impact on current market operations or other participants.
