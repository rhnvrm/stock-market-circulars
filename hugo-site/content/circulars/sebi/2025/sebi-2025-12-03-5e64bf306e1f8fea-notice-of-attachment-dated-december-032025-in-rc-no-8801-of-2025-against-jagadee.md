## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Attachment dated December 3, 2025 in Recovery Certificate No. 8801 of 2025 against Jagadeesan S (PAN: BDDPJ0903L) in connection with the matter of unregistered investment advisory services/activities of YM Forecast managed by P Krishnakumar and Jagadeesan S. This notice is part of SEBI's enforcement action for recovery proceedings against entities operating investment advisory services without proper registration.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8801 of 2025 issued against Jagadeesan S
- Individual PAN: BDDPJ0903L
- Related to unregistered investment advisory services/activities
- Entity involved: YM Forecast
- Also managed by P Krishnakumar (co-manager)
- Notice of attachment issued as part of recovery proceedings

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes announced. This notice represents enforcement of existing regulations requiring registration of investment advisory services under SEBI regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- All entities providing investment advisory services must be registered with SEBI
- Operating unregistered investment advisory services is a violation of SEBI regulations
- Non-compliance can lead to recovery proceedings and attachment of assets
- Investment advisors must ensure proper registration before commencing operations

## Important Dates

- **December 3, 2025**: Notice of Attachment issued
- Recovery Certificate No.: 8801 of 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Regulatory Impact**: This enforcement action demonstrates SEBI's continued focus on curbing unregistered investment advisory activities. It serves as a warning to entities operating without proper registration.

**Market Impact**: Limited direct market impact as this action is specific to named individuals and entities. However, it reinforces regulatory compliance requirements for the investment advisory industry.

**Investor Protection**: Protects investors from unregistered and potentially fraudulent investment advisory services by taking action against non-compliant operators.

**Industry Impact**: Sends a clear message to the investment advisory industry about the consequences of operating without registration, encouraging compliance with SEBI regulations.
