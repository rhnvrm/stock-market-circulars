## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC8985 of 2025 serving a notice of demand against Moonclub Merchants Private Limited (PAN: AAMCS2508Q), which has been amalgamated with Superior Commodeal Pvt Ltd. This demand is issued in connection with the matter of Thirani Projects Ltd.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate Number: RC8985 of 2025
- Entity: Moonclub Merchants Private Limited (PAN: AAMCS2508Q)
- Current Status: Amalgamated with Superior Commodeal Pvt Ltd
- Related Matter: Thirani Projects Ltd
- Type: Notice of Demand under SEBI recovery proceedings

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI recovery procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Moonclub Merchants Private Limited (now Superior Commodeal Pvt Ltd) must comply with the demand notice
- Payment obligations as specified in the recovery certificate must be fulfilled
- The successor entity (Superior Commodeal Pvt Ltd) is responsible for obligations following amalgamation

## Important Dates

- Certificate Issue Date: December 31, 2025
- Publication Date: January 2026
- Payment deadline and other specific dates would be mentioned in the detailed certificate

## Impact Assessment

**Financial Impact**: High impact on the named entities - Moonclub Merchants Private Limited and its successor Superior Commodeal Pvt Ltd - who are liable for payment under this recovery certificate.

**Legal Impact**: Enforcement action may lead to further recovery proceedings if demand is not met, including attachment of assets and bank accounts.

**Market Impact**: Limited direct market impact, but demonstrates SEBI's continued enforcement activities in the Thirani Projects Ltd matter against associated entities.

**Corporate Impact**: Amalgamation does not absolve liability; successor entity inherits obligations of amalgamated company.
