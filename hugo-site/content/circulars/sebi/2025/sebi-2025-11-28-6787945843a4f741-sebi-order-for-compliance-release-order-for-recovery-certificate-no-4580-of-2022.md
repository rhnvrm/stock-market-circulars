## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order for Recovery Certificate No. 4580 of 2022 against Karan Kirtibhai Shah, Keval Kirtikumar Shah, Alkaben Kirtibhai Shah, and Kirtibhai Rasiklal Shah in the matter of SMS Techsoft (India) Ltd. This order releases certain obligations or attachments related to the recovery proceedings.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 4580 of 2022

- Release Order issued by SEBI
- Published on November 28, 2025
