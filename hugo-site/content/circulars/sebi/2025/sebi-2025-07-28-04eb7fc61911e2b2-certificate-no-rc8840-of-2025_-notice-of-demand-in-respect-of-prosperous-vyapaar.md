- Certificate issued: RC8840 of 2025
- Target entity: Prosperous Vyapaar Pvt. Ltd. (PAN: AAHCP7740A)

No general regulatory changes. This is an entity-specific enforcement action.

Prosperous Vyapaar Pvt. Ltd. must comply with the demand notice requirements as specified in the certificate. Specific compliance details require review of the full document content.

- Compliance deadlines: As specified in the notice (document review required)
