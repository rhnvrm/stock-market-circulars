## Summary

SEBI, in collaboration with NISM, has launched the 'SEBI Arth Yatra Contest 2025', a nationwide creative expression contest aimed at promoting investor awareness and education. The contest is open to the public with two categories: Short Video and Static Post.

## Key Points

- Contest launched by SEBI Chairman Shri Tuhin Kanta Pandey at NISM Campus, Patalganga
- Two competition categories: Short Video and Static Post
- Open to public participation with entries accepted in multiple languages
- Attractive prizes to be awarded to best entries
- Part of SEBI's broader investor awareness campaign

## Contest Themes

Participants must create content based on these themes:
- Invest Responsibly, Prosper Wisely
- Investor-Friendly Initiatives to Empower Investors
- Bust the Scam

## Important Information

- Contest details available on SEBI Investor Website
- Follow hashtag #SEBIArthYatra2025 on social media for updates
- Aims to promote responsible investment habits and raise awareness about financial frauds

## Impact Assessment

This initiative is purely educational and promotional, designed to enhance investor awareness without any regulatory or compliance implications for market participants.
