## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order for Recovery Certificate No. RC1912 of 2019 in respect of Satyalaxmi Vinimay Pvt. Ltd. (PAN: AATCS8589E) in the matter of Svaraj Trading & Agencies Ltd. This order signifies the completion of recovery proceedings initiated under the recovery certificate.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC1912 of 2019 pertains to Satyalaxmi Vinimay Pvt. Ltd.
- Entity PAN: AATCS8589E
- Related to the matter of Svaraj Trading & Agencies Ltd.
- Release order indicates successful completion or conclusion of recovery proceedings
- Order issued in November 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action completion notice.

## Compliance Requirements

No ongoing compliance requirements. The release order indicates the completion of recovery proceedings against the specified entity.

## Important Dates

- Order Date: November 6, 2025
- Original Recovery Certificate: RC1912 of 2019

## Impact Assessment

This release order has minimal market impact as it pertains to the completion of specific recovery proceedings against Satyalaxmi Vinimay Pvt. Ltd. The order primarily affects the named entity and signifies the conclusion of enforcement actions under the specified recovery certificate. There is no broader regulatory or operational impact on market participants.
