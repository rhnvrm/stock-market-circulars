## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate Number: RC738 of 2015
- Entity: Ahilya Commercial Pvt. Ltd.
- PAN: AACCA2226R
- Related Matter: M/s. Brahmanand Himghar Ltd.
- Status: Completed

No regulatory changes. This is a completion notice for existing recovery proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. The recovery certificate has been completed.

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate Issue Year: 2015
- Completion Date: December 2025

## Impact Assessment

This completion notice has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains to specific recovery proceedings against Ahilya Commercial Pvt. Ltd. The completion indicates that SEBI has concluded its recovery efforts in this particular case related to M/s. Brahmanand Himghar Ltd.
