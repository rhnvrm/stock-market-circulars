## Summary

SEBI has issued a notice of attachment dated December 16, 2025, under Recovery Certificate No. 8945 of 2025 against Pankaj Rameshchandra Vyas (PAN: ADGPV4521L) and Vaishali Pankaj Vyas (PAN: AAPPV4036J). The attachment pertains to demat accounts and mutual fund folios held by these individuals in connection with alleged manipulation in the scrip of SecUR Credentials Limited.

## Key Points

- Attachment notice issued under Recovery Certificate No. 8945 of 2025
- Targets demat accounts and mutual fund folios of two individuals
- Related to market manipulation case involving SecUR Credentials Limited
- Enforcement action demonstrates SEBI's powers to freeze financial assets during recovery proceedings
- Notice dated December 16, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI recovery and attachment provisions.

## Compliance Requirements

Depositories, depository participants, and mutual fund houses must comply with the attachment notice and freeze the specified accounts/folios. The attached assets cannot be transferred, sold, or pledged until further orders from SEBI.

## Important Dates

- **December 16, 2025**: Date of attachment notice

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited direct market impact, but serves as a deterrent against market manipulation activities. Demonstrates SEBI's willingness to use asset attachment as an enforcement tool.

**Operational Impact**: Affected individuals face restrictions on their demat accounts and mutual fund folios. Financial institutions holding these accounts must implement the attachment and maintain frozen status until SEBI directs otherwise.

**Investor Impact**: Reinforces investor confidence in market integrity by showing active enforcement against alleged manipulators. Highlights the consequences of engaging in manipulative trading practices.
