## Summary

SEBI has issued a remittance advice against Usha Maurya (PAN: AIFPM3626B), who has been identified as a defaulter in the matter of Initial Service Offering (ISO) at BSE. This notice pertains to recovery proceedings under Recovery Certificate No. 8250 of 2024.

## Key Points

- Remittance advice issued against Usha Maurya (PAN: AIFPM3626B)
- Matter relates to ISO (Initial Service Offering) at BSE
- Recovery Certificate No. 8250 of 2024
- Entity classified as defaulter
- Part of SEBI's enforcement and recovery proceedings

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action under existing recovery framework.

## Compliance Requirements

This notice is specific to Usha Maurya and relates to recovery of outstanding dues. No compliance requirements for other market participants.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: November 19, 2025
- Recovery Certificate No.: 8250 of 2024

## Impact Assessment

This is an entity-specific enforcement action with minimal impact on broader market operations. The remittance advice is part of SEBI's ongoing recovery proceedings against defaulters and does not affect general market participants or trading activities.
