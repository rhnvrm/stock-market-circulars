## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand under Recovery Certificate No. 8979 of 2025 dated December 26, 2025 against Kishan S Rao (PAN: ARXPK0976D) in connection with the matter of Cerebra Integrated Technologies Limited. This represents enforcement action for recovery of dues from the individual.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8979 of 2025 issued on December 26, 2025
- Action against: Kishan S Rao (PAN: ARXPK0976D)
- Related to: Cerebra Integrated Technologies Limited matter
- Notice type: Demand notice under recovery certificate
- Enforcement action by SEBI for dues recovery

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an enforcement action under existing recovery proceedings framework.

## Compliance Requirements

Kishan S Rao is required to comply with the demand notice and settle the outstanding dues as specified in the recovery certificate. Failure to comply may result in further enforcement actions including attachment of assets.

## Important Dates

- December 26, 2025: Recovery Certificate No. 8979 of 2025 issued
- Payment deadline: As specified in the notice (refer to PDF for specific dates)

## Impact Assessment

This action has limited market-wide impact as it targets a specific individual in relation to Cerebra Integrated Technologies Limited. The enforcement demonstrates SEBI's continued efforts to recover dues from non-compliant entities and individuals. Market participants should note SEBI's active pursuit of recovery proceedings against defaulters.
