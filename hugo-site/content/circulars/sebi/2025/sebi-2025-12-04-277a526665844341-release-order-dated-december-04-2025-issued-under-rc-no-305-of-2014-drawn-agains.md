## Summary

SEBI has issued a release order dated December 04, 2025 under Recovery Certificate No. 305 of 2014 drawn against M/s Jugnu Securities Private Limited in the matter of Kolar Biotech Limited. This release order indicates compliance with recovery obligations or settlement of dues in the specified matter.

## Key Points

- Release order issued under Recovery Certificate No. 305 of 2014
- Order drawn against M/s Jugnu Securities Private Limited
- Related to the matter of Kolar Biotech Limited
- Order dated December 04, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this order. This is a release order indicating completion or settlement of recovery proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. This order releases the entity from the recovery certificate obligations upon fulfillment of the specified conditions.

## Important Dates

- **Order Date:** December 04, 2025
- **Recovery Certificate:** RC No. 305 of 2014

## Impact Assessment

This release order has limited market impact as it is specific to M/s Jugnu Securities Private Limited and relates to settlement of recovery proceedings in the matter of Kolar Biotech Limited. The order signifies closure or resolution of the recovery action initiated under RC No. 305 of 2014.
