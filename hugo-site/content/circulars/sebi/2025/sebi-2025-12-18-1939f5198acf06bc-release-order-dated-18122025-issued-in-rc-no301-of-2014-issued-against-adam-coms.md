## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order dated December 18, 2025 in adjudication proceedings RC No.301 of 2014 against Adam Comsof Ltd. in the matter of Kolar Biotech Ltd.

## Key Points

- Release order issued in adjudication case RC No.301 of 2014
- Proceedings against Adam Comsof Ltd.
- Related to matter involving Kolar Biotech Ltd.
- Order date: December 18, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a case-specific order.

## Compliance Requirements

Compliance requirements specific to the parties involved in the adjudication proceedings will be detailed in the complete order document.

## Important Dates

- **Release Order Date:** December 18, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a release order in a specific adjudication case. The impact is primarily limited to the parties directly involved (Adam Comsof Ltd. and matters relating to Kolar Biotech Ltd.). Broader market impact is expected to be minimal.
