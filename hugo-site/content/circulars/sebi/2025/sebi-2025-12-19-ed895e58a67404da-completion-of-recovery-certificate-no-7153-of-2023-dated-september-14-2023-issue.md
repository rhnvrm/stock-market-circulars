## Summary

SEBI has completed recovery proceedings under Recovery Certificate No. 7153 of 2023, dated September 14, 2023, issued against Anuj Garg (PAN: AHEPG5644F) in the matter of CPR Capital Services Ltd. This notice confirms the conclusion of the debt recovery process initiated by the securities regulator.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 7153 of 2023 issued on September 14, 2023
- Defaulter: Anuj Garg (PAN: AHEPG5644F)
- Related entity: CPR Capital Services Ltd.
- Recovery proceedings now completed
- Enforcement action concluded

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a completion notice for an existing recovery proceeding.

## Compliance Requirements

No ongoing compliance requirements. The recovery certificate proceedings have been completed.

## Important Dates

- **Original Certificate Date**: September 14, 2023
- **Completion Notice Date**: December 19, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a specific enforcement action completion notice relating to an individual defaulter and does not affect broader market operations.

**Operational Impact**: Limited to the parties involved in the recovery proceedings. The completion of the recovery certificate indicates successful conclusion of SEBI's debt recovery efforts against the defaulter.

**Investor Impact**: No direct impact on investors. This is an administrative notice confirming the closure of recovery proceedings.
