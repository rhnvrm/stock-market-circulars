## Summary

SEBI has issued a Remittance Order in Recovery Certificate No. 8849 of 2025 drawn against Emed.com Technologies Limited (PAN: AAACC8289N). This order is part of SEBI's enforcement proceedings to recover outstanding monetary dues from the entity through the recovery certificate mechanism under applicable regulations.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8849 of 2025 has been issued against Emed.com Technologies Limited
- The entity's PAN is AAACC8289N
- This is a remittance order as part of recovery proceedings
- The order represents SEBI's enforcement action to recover outstanding amounts
- The recovery certificate mechanism is used when entities fail to comply with payment obligations

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI recovery regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Emed.com Technologies Limited must comply with the remittance order
- The entity is required to pay the outstanding amounts as per the recovery certificate
- Failure to comply may result in further enforcement actions including attachment of assets and bank accounts
- The recovery process follows the legal framework for debt recovery

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate issued: 2025 (RC No. 8849 of 2025)
- Order date: October 9, 2025 (based on URL pattern)
