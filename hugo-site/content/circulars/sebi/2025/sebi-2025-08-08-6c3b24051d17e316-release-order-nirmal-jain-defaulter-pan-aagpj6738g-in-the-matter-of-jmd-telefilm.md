- Release order issued for defaulter Nirmal Jain
- PAN: AAGPJ6738G
- Related to JMD Telefilms Industries Ltd. matter
- Recovery Certificate No. 7737 of 2024
- Order dated August 2025

Release of defaulter status for Nirmal Jain in the specified matter.

The release order indicates completion of recovery proceedings or settlement of dues related to the defaulter case.

## Important Dates

- Release Order Date: August 2025
- Recovery Certificate No. 7737 of 2024

This release order resolves the defaulter status for the individual in relation to JMD Telefilms Industries Ltd., potentially affecting the company's regulatory standing and the individual's ability to participate in securities market activities.
