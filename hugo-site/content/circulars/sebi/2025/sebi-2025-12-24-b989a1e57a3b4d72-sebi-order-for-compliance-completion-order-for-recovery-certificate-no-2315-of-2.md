## Summary

SEBI has issued a completion order for Recovery Certificate No. 2315 of 2019 in the matter against J M Soni related to Ushdev International Limited. This order signifies the conclusion of recovery proceedings initiated under the recovery certificate.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 2315 of 2019 has been completed
- Proceedings were against J M Soni
- Matter relates to Ushdev International Limited
- Completion order issued on December 24, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action completion notice.

## Compliance Requirements

The completion order indicates that the recovery proceedings have been concluded. No further compliance actions are required from market participants unless directly involved in the matter.

## Important Dates

- **Order Date:** December 24, 2025
- **Recovery Certificate:** No. 2315 of 2019

## Impact Assessment

This completion order has minimal impact on the broader market. It represents the closure of specific recovery proceedings against an individual (J M Soni) in a matter involving Ushdev International Limited. The order is primarily of administrative significance, indicating that SEBI has successfully completed its recovery actions in this particular case.
