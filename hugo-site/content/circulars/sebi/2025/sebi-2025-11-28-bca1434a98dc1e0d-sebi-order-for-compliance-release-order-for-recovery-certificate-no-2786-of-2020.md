## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order for Recovery Certificate No. 2786 of 2020 against SMS Techsoft (India) Ltd. and 32 other entities. This order indicates that the recovery proceedings initiated against these parties have been successfully completed or complied with, leading to the release from the recovery certificate obligations.

## Key Points

- Release Order issued for Recovery Certificate No. 2786 of 2020
- Involves SMS Techsoft (India) Ltd. and 32 other individual and HUF entities
- Order relates to compliance with recovery proceedings in the SMS Techsoft matter
- Release indicates successful resolution of outstanding recovery obligations
