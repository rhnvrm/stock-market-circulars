## Summary

SEBI has completed recovery proceedings under Recovery Certificate No. 8967 of 2025 dated December 12, 2025 against Mr. Vijay Tulshyan (PAN: ACFPT5009H). This action relates to the inspection of CIG Realty Fund Scheme I, II and IV, and marks the conclusion of recovery efforts in this matter.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8967 of 2025 has been completed
- Certificate dated December 12, 2025
- Defaulter: Mr. Vijay Tulshyan (PAN: ACFPT5009H)
- Related to inspection of CIG Realty Fund Scheme I, II and IV
- Represents concluded enforcement proceedings

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a completion notice for existing recovery proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. This notice confirms the completion of recovery actions against the specified individual.

## Important Dates

- **December 12, 2025**: Date of Recovery Certificate No. 8967 of 2025
- **December 30, 2025**: Completion notice published

## Impact Assessment

This completion notice has minimal market impact as it relates to concluded recovery proceedings against a specific individual in connection with CIG Realty Fund schemes. The completion of the recovery certificate indicates that SEBI has successfully concluded its enforcement actions in this matter. No direct impact on listed securities or broader market operations.
