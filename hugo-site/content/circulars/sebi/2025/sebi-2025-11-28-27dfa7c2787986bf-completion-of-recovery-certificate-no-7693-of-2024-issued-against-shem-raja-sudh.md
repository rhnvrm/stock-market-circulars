- Related matter: Arvind Remedies Ltd.
- Order type: Completion order indicating conclusion of recovery proceedings

No new compliance requirements. The completion order indicates that the recovery proceedings have been concluded.

- Recovery Certificate: RC No. 7693 of 2024
