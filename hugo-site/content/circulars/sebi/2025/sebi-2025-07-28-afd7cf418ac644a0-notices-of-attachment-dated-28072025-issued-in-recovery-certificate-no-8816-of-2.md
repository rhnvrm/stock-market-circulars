## Summary

SEBI has issued notices of attachment dated 28.07.2025 against Krishnamurthy G. (PAN: BSFPK6683P) under Recovery Certificate No. 8816 of 2025. This enforcement action is related to unregistered investment advisor activities, indicating SEBI's continued crackdown on individuals providing investment advisory services without proper registration.

## Key Points

- Attachment notices issued against Krishnamurthy G. (PAN: BSFPK6683P)
- Recovery Certificate No. 8816 of 2025
- Notice date: 28.07.2025
- Related to unregistered investment advisor activities
- Part of SEBI's enforcement mechanism for recovery of dues

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This represents enforcement of existing regulations requiring proper registration for investment advisory services.

## Compliance Requirements

- Investment advisors must obtain proper SEBI registration before providing advisory services
- Unregistered advisory activities can result in recovery proceedings and asset attachment
- Market participants should ensure compliance with investment advisor registration requirements

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 28.07.2025
- Recovery Certificate: No. 8816 of 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is an individual enforcement action with limited direct market impact. However, it demonstrates SEBI's active enforcement against unregistered investment advisors, serving as a deterrent for similar violations. Market participants providing investment advisory services should ensure proper registration and compliance to avoid similar enforcement actions.
