## Summary

SEBI has issued a General Remittance Order dated 07.10.2025 under Recovery Certificate No.8506 of 2025 against Anil Kumar Sajjankumar Sharma (PAN: CBWPS6212F) in the matter of Eros International Media Limited. This order pertains to the recovery of dues as part of SEBI's enforcement proceedings.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No.: 8506 of 2025
- Person Against Whom Order Issued: Anil Kumar Sajjankumar Sharma
- PAN: CBWPS6212F
- Related Company: Eros International Media Limited
- Order Type: General Remittance Order
- Date of Order: 07.10.2025
- This is part of SEBI's enforcement and recovery proceedings

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this order. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI regulations for recovery of outstanding dues.

## Compliance Requirements

The General Remittance Order directs the recovery of specified amounts from Anil Kumar Sajjankumar Sharma. Compliance with the recovery order is mandatory, and failure to comply may result in further enforcement actions including attachment of assets, bank accounts, or other recovery measures as per SEBI's recovery provisions.

## Important Dates

- **Order Date**: 07.10.2025
- **Recovery Certificate Number**: 8506 of 2025
- Specific payment deadlines and compliance timelines would be mentioned in the detailed order

**Market Impact**: Medium - This order targets a specific individual in relation to Eros International Media Limited. While it represents enforcement action, the direct market impact is limited to the specific case.

**Severity**: High - Recovery certificates and remittance orders are serious enforcement mechanisms indicating non-compliance with SEBI orders or payment of penalties/dues.
