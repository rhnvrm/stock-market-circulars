## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order for Recovery Certificate No. 286 of 2014 against Cavalier Securities in the matter of Kolar Biotech Limited. This order pertains to enforcement and recovery proceedings.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 286 of 2014
- Entity: Cavalier Securities
- Matter relates to Kolar Biotech Limited case
- Release order issued on December 4, 2025
- Part of SEBI's enforcement and recovery mechanism

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action related to existing proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

The release order indicates completion or settlement of recovery obligations under Recovery Certificate No. 286 of 2014. Cavalier Securities has fulfilled the requirements leading to this release.

## Important Dates

- **Order Date**: December 4, 2025
- **Recovery Certificate**: No. 286 of 2014

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a specific enforcement action against one entity relating to a historical case.

**Operational Impact**: Medium for Cavalier Securities - Release from recovery proceedings may restore normal operational status.

**Investor Impact**: Low - Limited impact on broader market participants. Relevant primarily to parties involved in the Kolar Biotech Limited matter.
