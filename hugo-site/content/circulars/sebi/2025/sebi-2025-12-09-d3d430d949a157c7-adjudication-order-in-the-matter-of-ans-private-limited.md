## Summary

SEBI issued Adjudication Order No. Order/AK/DS/2025-26/31827 against ANS Private Limited (PAN: AACCA4841J, SEBI Registration No. INZ000196431), a registered stock broker. The order follows a thematic inspection conducted on July 30, 2024, at the office of the broker's Authorized Person - M/s Soham Fincare India LLP. The inspection covered the period from January 01, 2024 to June 30, 2024 and found prima facie violations of SEBI (Stock Brokers) Regulations, 1992 and applicable SEBI and Stock Exchange Circulars.

## Key Points

- Adjudication proceedings initiated on June 02, 2025 under Section 15-I(1) of SEBI Act, 1992
- Adjudicating Officer appointed on June 16, 2025 to inquire into violations under Section 15HB of SEBI Act
- Show Cause Notice (SCN) issued on July 16, 2025 (No. SEBI/HO/EAD/EAD1/P/OW/2025/19049/1)
- Noticee requested and was granted inspection of documents on August 14, 2025
- Multiple extensions granted for response submission
- Noticee submitted reply on September 24, 2025 with annexures on September 25, 2025
- Personal hearing scheduled for October 16, 2025
- Inspection conducted at Authorized Person's office: M/s Soham Fincare India LLP

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This order enforces existing provisions under:
- SEBI (Stock Brokers) Regulations, 1992
- SEBI (Procedure for Holding Inquiry and Imposing Penalties) Rules, 1995
- Applicable SEBI and Stock Exchange Circulars

## Compliance Requirements

- Stock brokers must ensure full compliance with SEBI (Stock Brokers) Regulations, 1992
- Authorized Persons (APs) of stock brokers must maintain compliance with all applicable SEBI and Stock Exchange Circulars
- Brokers are subject to thematic inspections by SEBI to verify regulatory compliance
- Violations may result in adjudication proceedings and penalties under Section 15HB of SEBI Act

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited direct market impact as this is an enforcement action against a single stock broker intermediary.

**Regulatory Impact**: Reinforces SEBI's oversight of stock brokers and their Authorized Persons through thematic inspections. Sets compliance expectations for all registered brokers.

**Operational Impact**: ANS Private Limited faces potential penalties and must address identified violations. The order demonstrates SEBI's continued focus on broker compliance and client protection.

**Precedent Value**: Serves as a reminder to all stock brokers and Authorized Persons regarding the importance of strict adherence to SEBI regulations and circulars, with enforcement through adjudication proceedings for non-compliance.
