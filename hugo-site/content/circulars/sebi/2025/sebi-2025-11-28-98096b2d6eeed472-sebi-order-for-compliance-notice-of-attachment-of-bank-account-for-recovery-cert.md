## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Attachment of Bank Account under Recovery Certificate No. 4514 of 2022 against Mitesh Kaniyalal Thakkar in relation to recovery proceedings in the matter of SMS Techsoft (India) Limited. This enforcement action involves attachment of bank accounts as part of SEBI's debt recovery process.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 4514 of 2022 issued against Mitesh Kaniyalal Thakkar
- Matter relates to SMS Techsoft (India) Limited
- Notice of Attachment of Bank Account issued as part of recovery proceedings
- Order dated November 28, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI recovery regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Banks and financial institutions must comply with the attachment order
- Attached bank accounts cannot be operated without SEBI's permission
- Mitesh Kaniyalal Thakkar must comply with recovery proceedings

## Important Dates

- **Order Date:** November 28, 2025
- **Recovery Certificate:** No. 4514 of 2022

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This is an individual enforcement action with no direct market-wide implications.

**Operational Impact:** High for the individual involved - bank account attachment restricts financial operations and is part of debt recovery enforcement by SEBI in the SMS Techsoft (India) Limited matter.
