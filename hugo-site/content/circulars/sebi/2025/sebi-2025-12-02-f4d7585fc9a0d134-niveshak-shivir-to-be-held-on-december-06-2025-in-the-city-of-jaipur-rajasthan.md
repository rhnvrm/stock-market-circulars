## Summary

SEBI and the Investor Education and Protection Fund Authority (IEPFA) are jointly organizing the fourth "Niveshak Shivir" on December 06, 2025 in Jaipur, Rajasthan. The event will be conducted in collaboration with Market Infrastructure Institutions (NSE, BSE, NSDL, CDSL) and Registrar and Transfer Agents (KFIN Technologies Limited, MUFG Intime India Private Limited, Bigshare Services Pvt. Ltd., Datamatics Business Solutions Ltd., Purva Sharegistry India Pvt, etc.). The camp aims to help shareholders resolve dividend and share-related issues.

## Key Points

- Fourth edition of Niveshak Shivir organized by SEBI and IEPFA
- Event date: December 06, 2025, Time: 10 AM to 4 PM
- Venue: Swarn Mahal â€“ The Banquet Hall, Khatipura Road, near Gandhi Path, Marudhar Vihar, Vaishali Nagar, Jaipur, Rajasthan - 302012

- Participating RTAs: KFIN Technologies Limited, MUFG Intime India Private Limited, Bigshare Services Pvt. Ltd., Datamatics Business Solutions Ltd., Purva Sharegistry India Pvt
- Shareholders must bring official government identification documents (Voter ID, PAN card, Driver's licence, etc.) for entry

## Objectives

- Facilitate transfer of unpaid dividends (up to 7 years)
- Assist shareholders with KYC and nomination updates with companies
- Resolve pending IEPFA claims of unclaimed and unpaid shares and dividends

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an investor outreach and service event.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance requirements. This is a voluntary service event for shareholders who wish to resolve pending issues related to dividends, shares, and KYC updates.

## Important Dates

- **Event Date**: December 06, 2025
- **Event Time**: 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM
- **Press Release Date**: December 02, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a shareholder service initiative with no market or regulatory impact. The event provides a platform for individual shareholders to:

- Claim unpaid dividends from the past 7 years
- Update their KYC details with companies
- Resolve claims for shares and dividends transferred to IEPFA

The initiative benefits retail investors by providing direct access to market infrastructure institutions and RTAs to resolve long-pending issues. No impact on market operations, trading activities, or listed entities' compliance obligations.
