## Summary

SEBI has issued a Remittance Order dated December 19, 2025, in Recovery Certificate (RC) No. 7167 of 2023. This order pertains to the recovery of outstanding dues from entities/individuals as determined in enforcement proceedings. The remittance order authorizes recovery officers to take necessary steps for collection of amounts due.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate Number: RC No. 7167 of 2023
- Order Date: December 19, 2025
- Type: Remittance Order for recovery of dues
- Issued under SEBI's recovery and enforcement powers
- Pertains to outstanding penalties/disgorgement amounts from previous enforcement actions

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI recovery framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Entities/individuals named in the recovery certificate must comply with payment demands
- Recovery officers are authorized to take necessary steps including attachment of assets if required
- Non-compliance may lead to further enforcement actions including arrest warrants in civil matters

## Important Dates

- **Order Date**: December 19, 2025
- **Recovery Certificate**: RC No. 7167 of 2023

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - Limited to specific entities under recovery proceedings

**Regulatory Impact**: Medium - Demonstrates SEBI's continued enforcement of recovery mechanisms for unpaid penalties and disgorgement amounts. Reinforces the regulatory framework for collection of dues from violators of securities laws.

**Operational Impact**: High for named entities - Those subject to this recovery order must make arrangements for payment or face potential asset attachment and other recovery measures by SEBI's recovery officers.
