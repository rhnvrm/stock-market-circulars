## Summary

SEBI has issued a release order for Recovery Certificate No. 7720 of 2024 against N R Doshi in the matter of Safal Herbs Limited (formerly known as Parikh Herbals Limited). This order signifies the completion of recovery proceedings or compliance with the recovery certificate requirements.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 7720 of 2024 issued against N R Doshi
- Related to the matter of Safal Herbs Limited (formerly Parikh Herbals Limited)
- Release order indicates completion of recovery or compliance obligations
- Published on December 24, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action completion notice.

## Compliance Requirements

The release order indicates that the obligations under Recovery Certificate No. 7720 of 2024 have been satisfied or completed as per SEBI's recovery proceedings.

## Important Dates

- **Order Publication Date**: December 24, 2025
- **Recovery Certificate Number**: 7720 of 2024

## Impact Assessment

This release order has limited market impact as it relates to the completion of recovery proceedings against a specific individual (N R Doshi) in connection with Safal Herbs Limited. The order indicates resolution of the recovery matter and does not impose new obligations on market participants or other entities.
