## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Attachment of Bank Accounts (AP No. 15400 of 2026) dated June 03, 2026, against Upendra Tarachand Mahadule, a defaulter identified by PAN ACAPM4821B. The attachment is ordered in connection with the matter of Illiquid Stock Options under Recovery Certificate No. 9110 of 2026, as part of SEBI's recovery proceedings.

## Key Points

- Attachment order issued: AP No. 15400 of 2026, dated June 03, 2026
- Defaulter: Upendra Tarachand Mahadule, PAN: ACAPM4821B
- Matter: Illiquid Stock Options
- Recovery Certificate No.: 9110 of 2026
- Action: Attachment of bank accounts as a recovery measure

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement/recovery action pursuant to existing SEBI recovery and attachment powers.

## Compliance Requirements

- Banks holding accounts of Upendra Tarachand Mahadule (PAN: ACAPM4821B) are required to comply with the attachment order and freeze the specified accounts.
- Relevant financial institutions must not allow withdrawal or transfer of funds from the attached accounts without SEBI's permission.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: June 03, 2026
- Recovery Certificate No. 9110 of 2026 (underlying order)

## Impact Assessment

This action is limited in scope, targeting a single individual defaulter in the Illiquid Stock Options matter. There is no broad market impact. It reflects SEBI's continued enforcement of recovery proceedings against defaulters in the illiquid stock options segment, which has been a focus area for regulatory action.
