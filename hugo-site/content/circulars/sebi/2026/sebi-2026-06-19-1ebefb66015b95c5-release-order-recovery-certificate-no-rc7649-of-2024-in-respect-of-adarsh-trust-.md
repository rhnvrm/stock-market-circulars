## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order in connection with Recovery Certificate No. RC7649 of 2024 against Adarsh Trust (PAN: AABTA7344L). The order relates to enforcement proceedings in the matter of JMD Telefilms Industries Limited.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC7649 of 2024 issued against Adarsh Trust (PAN: AABTA7344L).
- The matter concerns JMD Telefilms Industries Limited, which has been subject to SEBI enforcement action.
- A Release Order has been issued, indicating that attached or frozen assets of Adarsh Trust are being released, likely following satisfaction of the recovery demand or pursuant to a legal/regulatory direction.
- The circular is issued by SEBI's enforcement/recovery wing.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is an operational enforcement order specific to Adarsh Trust in the JMD Telefilms matter.

## Compliance Requirements

- Adarsh Trust and relevant financial intermediaries (banks, depositories) are required to act on the Release Order and lift any attachment or freeze placed on the assets of Adarsh Trust pursuant to RC7649 of 2024.
- Entities holding attached assets must process the release promptly upon receipt of this order.

## Important Dates

- Order Date: June 19, 2026
- Recovery Certificate Reference: RC7649 of 2024

## Impact Assessment

This order has a limited and specific market impact. It pertains to the resolution of a recovery proceeding against a single entity (Adarsh Trust) related to JMD Telefilms Industries Limited. The release of attachment suggests the noticee has fulfilled the recovery demand or obtained appropriate legal relief. No broad investor or market impact is anticipated beyond the parties directly involved in the JMD Telefilms enforcement matter.
