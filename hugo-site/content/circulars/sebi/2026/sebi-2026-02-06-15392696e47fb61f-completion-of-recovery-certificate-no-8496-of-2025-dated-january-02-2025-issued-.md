## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8496 of 2025 has been completed
- Defaulter: Kamaljeet Singh (PAN: ANIPK8791P)
- Original certificate date: January 02, 2025
- Case relates to trading in illiquid stock options at BSE
- Enforcement action has been concluded

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an enforcement completion notice.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. This document serves as a notice of completion of recovery proceedings.

## Important Dates

- **January 02, 2025**: Recovery Certificate No. 8496 of 2025 issued
- **February 06, 2026**: Completion of recovery certificate announced

This is a case-specific enforcement action completion with minimal market impact.
