## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order in respect of Fancos Trademart Private Ltd. (PAN: AACCF8546P), designated as a defaulter in the matter of trading by certain entities in Mentha Oil futures contracts at Multi Commodity Exchange of India Limited (MCX). The release order pertains to Recovery Certificate No. 7210 of 2023, indicating that the conditions for release have been satisfied.

## Key Points

- **Defaulter:** Fancos Trademart Private Ltd. (PAN: AACCF8546P)
- **Matter:** Trading by certain entities in Mentha Oil futures contracts at MCX
- **Recovery Certificate No.:** 7210 of 2023
- SEBI has issued a formal release order, signifying the discharge or satisfaction of the recovery certificate
- The release order is specific to the named entity and the cited certificate number

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is an enforcement/administrative order giving effect to the release of a previously issued recovery certificate.

## Compliance Requirements

- No new compliance obligations are imposed on market participants generally
- The defaulter, Fancos Trademart Private Ltd., is released from the encumbrances associated with Recovery Certificate No. 7210 of 2023
- Relevant authorities (e.g., recovery officers, depositories, exchanges) are expected to give effect to this release order

## Important Dates

- **Order Date:** May 2026
- **Recovery Certificate Reference:** Certificate No. 7210 of 2023

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine enforcement closure notice with minimal direct market impact. The release order indicates that SEBI's recovery proceedings against Fancos Trademart Private Ltd. in the Mentha Oil futures matter have reached a conclusion. No broader implications for market structure, trading rules, or other participants are expected.
