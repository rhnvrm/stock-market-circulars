## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand (Recovery Certificate No. 9170 of 2026) against Girish Kantilal Parmar (PAN: AUDPP6127A) of Ahmedabad, Gujarat, demanding payment of Rs. 6,37,000 (including penalty, interest, and recovery costs) arising from a quasi-judicial order dated 31.12.2025 related to trading activities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited). The notice directs payment within 15 days or enforcement action will be initiated.

## Key Points

- Penalty of Rs. 6,00,000 imposed vide Order No. QJA/MN/IVD/ID7/31947/2025-26 dated 31.12.2025 for trading irregularities in DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited)
- Interest of Rs. 36,000 accrued from January 2026 to June 2026 at 1% per month
- Recovery cost of Rs. 1,000 added, bringing the total demand to Rs. 6,37,000
- Payment must be made within 15 days of receipt of this notice via EFT/NEFT/RTGS to SEBI's designated ICICI Bank account (A/c No. SEBIRRDPEN9170, IFSC: ICIC0000106) or via the SEBI SI Portal online recovery payment module
- Non-payment will trigger enforcement including attachment and sale of movable/immovable property, bank account attachment, arrest and detention, or appointment of a receiver

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This notice is an enforcement action under Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992 read with Sections 220–227, 228A, 229, and 232 of the Income Tax Act, 1961, and the Second Schedule thereto.

## Compliance Requirements

- Girish Kantilal Parmar must pay Rs. 6,37,000 within 15 days of receiving the notice
- No transfer of property or bank account monies to spouse, minor child, son's wife, or son's minor child without adequate consideration is permissible, as such transfers will be deemed to belong to the defaulter (per Explanation 1, Section 28A, SEBI Act)
- The noticee cannot mortgage, charge, lease, or otherwise deal with any property without prior permission from the Recovery Officer; any unauthorised transfer will be deemed void (Rule 16, Second Schedule, Income Tax Act, 1961)
- Confirmation of e-payment must be forwarded to the Recovery Officer at SEBI Bhavan-II, Mumbai, or emailed to akashb@sebi.gov.in and recoveryho1@sebi.gov.in

## Important Dates

- Original penalty order date: 31.12.2025
- Interest accrual period: January 2026 to June 2026
- Notice date: 24.06.2026
- Payment deadline: Within 15 days of receipt of this notice

## Impact Assessment

This is an individual enforcement action with limited broader market impact. The penalty relates to trading irregularities in DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited). Failure to comply will result in coercive recovery measures including property attachment, bank account freezing, and potential arrest. Investors and market participants should note SEBI's active enforcement posture against trading violations in this scrip.
