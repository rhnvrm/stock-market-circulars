## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC9115 of 2026 pursuant to Adjudication Proceeding No. 15440 of 2026 against Rudramukhi Infrastructure Private Limited (PAN: AAGCR0172D) in the matter of Illiquid Stock Options. This certificate authorises the recovery of penalty amounts determined by SEBI's adjudicating officer.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9115 of 2026 issued against Rudramukhi Infrastructure Private Limited
- PAN of the entity: AAGCR0172D
- Arising from Adjudication Proceeding (A.P.) No. 15440 of 2026
- Related to SEBI's enforcement actions in the matter of Illiquid Stock Options
- Recovery certificate empowers SEBI to recover dues as arrears of land revenue

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an enforcement/recovery action under existing SEBI regulations and the SEBI Act, 1992.

## Compliance Requirements

- Rudramukhi Infrastructure Private Limited is required to pay the penalty amount specified in the adjudication order
- Failure to comply with the recovery certificate may result in further enforcement action including attachment of assets
- The entity must satisfy the dues as directed under the recovery certificate

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate issued: 2026 (specific date in document)
- Adjudication Proceeding No. 15440 of 2026 forms the basis of this certificate

## Impact Assessment

This recovery certificate has limited broader market impact and is specific to Rudramukhi Infrastructure Private Limited. It is part of SEBI's ongoing enforcement actions against entities involved in manipulative trading in illiquid stock options, a segment that SEBI has been actively pursuing since its investigations into suspected price manipulation and tax evasion through illiquid stock option contracts on exchanges.
