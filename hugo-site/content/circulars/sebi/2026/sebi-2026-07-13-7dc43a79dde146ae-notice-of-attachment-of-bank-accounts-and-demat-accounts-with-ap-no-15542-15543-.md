## Summary

SEBI's Recovery Officer has issued a Notice of Attachment (AP No. 15542 & 15543 of 2026, dated July 13, 2026) directing the attachment of the bank accounts and demat accounts of Jigneshkumar Purshottamdas Patel, described as a "Defaulter," for failure to pay dues arising from proceedings connected to trading activities of certain entities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited). The attachment is issued pursuant to Recovery Certificate No. 9169 of 2026 under the recovery mechanism available to SEBI.

## Key Points

- The notice is issued against Jigneshkumar Purshottamdas Patel, who has been categorized as a Defaulter for non-payment of amounts due to SEBI.
- The underlying matter relates to trading activities of certain entities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited).
- Recovery is being pursued under Recovery Certificate No. 9169 of 2026 issued by SEBI's Recovery Officer.
- The notice covers two attachment proceedings, AP No. 15542 and AP No. 15543 of 2026.
- Banks and depository participants holding accounts of the defaulter are directed to attach the relevant bank accounts and demat accounts and restrict further operations pending recovery of dues.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced; this is an enforcement action exercising SEBI's existing statutory powers of recovery (typically under Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992, read with the Second and Third Schedules to the Income-tax Act, 1961 and the Income Tax Certificate Proceedings Rules, 1962) against a specific defaulter.

## Compliance Requirements

- Banks and depository participants holding accounts in the name of the defaulter must comply with the attachment order and not permit withdrawal, transfer, or operation of the attached bank and demat accounts without the written permission of SEBI's Recovery Officer.
- The defaulter is required to clear outstanding dues under Recovery Certificate No. 9169 of 2026 to seek release of the attached accounts.

## Important Dates

- Notice date: July 13, 2026 (AP No. 15542 & 15543 of 2026).

## Impact Assessment

The impact of this notice is confined to the individual defaulter, Jigneshkumar Purshottamdas Patel, whose bank and demat accounts stand attached, restricting his ability to operate these accounts or trade in securities markets until dues are settled. There is no direct impact on DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited) as a listed entity, its shareholders, or the broader market, since the action targets an individual's assets in connection with past trading conduct rather than the company's operations or securities.
