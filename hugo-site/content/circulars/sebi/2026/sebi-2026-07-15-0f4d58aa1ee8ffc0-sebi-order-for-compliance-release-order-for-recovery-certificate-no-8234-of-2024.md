## Summary

SEBI has issued a compliance order releasing Recovery Certificate No. 8234 of 2024, which had been issued against Rocket Tips, proprietor Mehul Pravinbhai Jiyani (PAN: AGPPJ1611H), in connection with a case involving unregistered investment advisory services. The release order indicates that the recovery action associated with this certificate has been closed by SEBI's Recovery Officer.

## Key Points

- The order relates to Recovery Certificate No. 8234 of 2024.
- The noticee is Rocket Tips, proprietor Mehul Pravinbhai Jiyani (PAN: AGPPJ1611H).
- The underlying matter concerns provision of unregistered investment advisory services, i.e., operating as an investment adviser without SEBI registration.
- SEBI has released/closed the recovery certificate, signaling resolution of the associated recovery proceeding.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced; this is an individual enforcement action closing a specific recovery proceeding under SEBI's recovery of dues framework.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance obligations are placed on market participants generally. The order pertains specifically to closure of recovery proceedings against the named individual/entity.

## Important Dates

Order dated 2026-07-15 (as per circular publication); specific recovery certificate reference is No. 8234 of 2024.

## Impact Assessment

This order has limited market-wide impact as it concerns an individual unregistered investment adviser rather than a listed company or systemic regulatory change. It reinforces SEBI's ongoing enforcement against unregistered investment advisory operations and serves as a closure notice in the recovery process.
