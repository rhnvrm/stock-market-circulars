## Summary

Appeal No. 6682 of 2026 has been filed by Chhaya before the Securities Appellate Tribunal (SAT) against an order issued by the Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI). This document represents the formal appeal filing in the matter.

## Key Points

- Appeal filed before the Securities Appellate Tribunal
- Appellant: Chhaya
- Appeal Number: 6682 of 2026
- Filed against a SEBI order
- Date of filing: February 2, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an appeal against an existing SEBI order.

## Compliance Requirements

This appeal filing does not create new compliance requirements for market participants. It represents an individual's challenge to a SEBI order through the established appellate process.

## Important Dates

- **Appeal Filing Date**: February 2, 2026
- Further hearing dates and timelines to be determined by SAT

## Impact Assessment

This is an individual appeal case filed before SAT. The impact is limited to the specific appellant and the underlying matter. The outcome may have precedential value depending on the issues raised and SAT's decision, but does not have immediate market-wide implications. The appeal represents the statutory right of aggrieved parties to challenge SEBI orders before the appellate tribunal.
