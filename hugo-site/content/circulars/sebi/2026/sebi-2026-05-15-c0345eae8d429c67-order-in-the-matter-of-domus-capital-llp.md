SEBI has issued an enforcement order in the matter of Domus Capital LLP. - SEBI has passed a formal order against Domus Capital LLP
- The order is categorised under SEBI's enforcement/adjudication proceedings
- The order was issued on or around May 15, 2026
- Domus Capital LLP is the respondent entity in these proceedings

No broad regulatory changes are introduced by this order. It is an enforcement action specific to Domus Capital LLP based on findings of violation(s) of SEBI regulations.

## Important Dates

- Appeal window: Typically 45 days from the date of receipt of the order before SAT

This enforcement order directly impacts Domus Capital LLP, potentially affecting its registration, operations, or imposing a monetary penalty.
