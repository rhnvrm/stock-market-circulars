## Summary

SEBI has constituted an Expert Working Group to undertake a comprehensive review of the extant regulatory framework governing Debenture Trusteeship activities, primarily the SEBI (Debenture Trustees) Regulations, 1993. The review is prompted by significant changes in the financial sector since 1993, including the introduction of the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code, 2016, and growing investor participation in the corporate bond market. The Group is chaired by Mr. Ananta Barua, Former Whole Time Member, SEBI, with Shri Rajnish Kumar, Former Chairman, State Bank of India, as Co-Chairperson. SEBI has also invited public and market participant suggestions on the existing framework, to be submitted by July 15, 2026.

## Key Points

- SEBI has formed an Expert Working Group to review the regulatory framework applicable to Debenture Trustees and related matters.
- The review is necessitated by the evolving debt market landscape and the increasing role of Debenture Trustees in protecting debenture holders' interests.
- Chairperson: Mr. Ananta Barua (Former Whole Time Member, SEBI); Co-Chairperson: Shri Rajnish Kumar (Former Chairman, State Bank of India).
- Other members include representatives from CCIL, Axis Trustee Services, Beacon Trusteeship, DSK Legal, Vinod Kothari & Co., FIDC, SIDBI, The CFO Board, and CareEdge Ratings, with a SEBI Chief General Manager as Member Secretary.
- Terms of reference include reviewing the SEBI (Debenture Trustees) Regulations, 1993; examining market participant representations; strengthening the role of Debenture Trustees; reviewing net-worth requirements; and examining other relevant matters.
- SEBI has opened a public consultation inviting suggestions on simplification, rationalisation, and strengthening of the regulatory framework, to be submitted in a prescribed format by July 15, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes have been notified yet; this is the constitution of an advisory/expert body tasked with reviewing and recommending changes to the SEBI (Debenture Trustees) Regulations, 1993. Any actual regulatory amendments would follow as a subsequent consultation paper and board approval process based on the Group's recommendations.

## Compliance Requirements

No immediate compliance requirements are imposed on Debenture Trustees or other market participants by this announcement. Market participants and stakeholders wishing to contribute may submit suggestions, covering streamlining of provisions, removal of redundancies, updates aligned with market developments, enhancement of Debenture Trustee roles, and ease of compliance, in the prescribed format to rohitd@sebi.gov.in and rinkig@sebi.gov.in.

## Important Dates

- June 30, 2026: Constitution of the Expert Working Group announced by SEBI (Mumbai).
- July 15, 2026: Deadline for public and stakeholder suggestions on the existing Debenture Trustee regulatory framework.

## Impact Assessment

This is a procedural/governance development with no immediate market or trading impact. It signals SEBI's intent to modernize the Debenture Trustee regulatory framework, which could eventually affect net-worth norms, compliance obligations, and operational responsibilities for registered Debenture Trustees (e.g., Axis Trustee Services, Beacon Trusteeship) and indirectly influence corporate bond market practices. Stakeholders in the debt market, including issuers, trustees, and institutional investors, should monitor subsequent consultation papers for potential medium-term regulatory changes.
