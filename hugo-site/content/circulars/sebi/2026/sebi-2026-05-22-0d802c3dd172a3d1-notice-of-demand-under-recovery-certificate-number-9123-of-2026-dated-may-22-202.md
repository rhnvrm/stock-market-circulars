## Summary

SEBI issued a Notice of Demand under Recovery Certificate number 9123 of 2026 dated May 22, 2026, directed at Mr. J Murali Manohar (PAN: ATTPM0621R), identified as a defaulter in the matter of Mediaone Global Entertainment Limited. The notice demands recovery of dues as determined by SEBI's enforcement proceedings.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate number 9123 of 2026 issued by SEBI on May 22, 2026
- Defaulter: Mr. J Murali Manohar (PAN: ATTPM0621R)
- Related matter: Mediaone Global Entertainment Limited
- Notice of Demand issued under SEBI's recovery proceedings framework
- The defaulter is required to pay outstanding dues as specified in the recovery certificate

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement and recovery action under existing SEBI powers to recover penalties, disgorgement amounts, or other dues from defaulters.

## Compliance Requirements

- Mr. J Murali Manohar is required to satisfy the dues specified in Recovery Certificate number 9123 of 2026
- Payment must be made within the timeline stipulated in the notice
- Failure to comply may result in further enforcement action including attachment of assets

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate Date: May 22, 2026
- Notice of Demand issued: May 22, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This order has limited broader market impact and is confined to the individual defaulter, Mr. J Murali Manohar, and the matter of Mediaone Global Entertainment Limited. It reflects SEBI's ongoing enforcement and recovery efforts to ensure compliance with its earlier orders and collection of outstanding dues from parties found in violation.
