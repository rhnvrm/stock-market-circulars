## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC9114 of 2026 (Application/Petition No. 15421 of 2026) against Shanti Bhutra (PAN: AHRPB8152A) for recovery of a monetary penalty imposed in the matter of Illiquid Stock Options. This certificate authorises recovery proceedings against the individual for non-payment of dues arising from a prior SEBI adjudication order.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9114 of 2026 issued against Shanti Bhutra (PAN: AHRPB8152A)
- Application/Petition No. 15421 of 2026 filed under recovery proceedings
- Relates to SEBI's enforcement action in the matter of Illiquid Stock Options segment manipulation
- Certificate enables SEBI to recover unpaid monetary penalty through due process
- Part of a series of recovery certificates issued by SEBI against individuals penalised in the Illiquid Stock Options case

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an enforcement/recovery action under existing SEBI (Recovery of Amount) Rules.

## Compliance Requirements

- The named individual (Shanti Bhutra, PAN: AHRPB8152A) is required to pay the outstanding dues as specified in the recovery certificate
- Failure to comply may result in attachment and sale of movable/immovable properties by the Recovery Officer

## Important Dates

- Certificate Date: June 2026
- Underlying enforcement action: Prior adjudication order in the Illiquid Stock Options matter

## Impact Assessment

This recovery certificate has no direct impact on broader markets or investors. It is an individual-level enforcement action targeting one person found to have violated SEBI regulations in the Illiquid Stock Options segment. SEBI has issued a large number of such certificates as part of its ongoing enforcement sweep against entities that engaged in non-genuine, synchronized trades in the illiquid stock options segment to create artificial volumes.
