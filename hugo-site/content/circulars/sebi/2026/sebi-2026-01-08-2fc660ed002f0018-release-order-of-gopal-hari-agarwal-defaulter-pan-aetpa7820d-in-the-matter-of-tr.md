## Summary

SEBI has issued a release order for Gopal Hari Agarwal (PAN: AETPA7820D), a defaulter in the matter of trading in illiquid stock options on the Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE). This order relates to Recovery Certificate No. 7315 of 2023.

## Key Points

- Release order issued for defaulter Gopal Hari Agarwal
- PAN: AETPA7820D
- Matter relates to trading in illiquid stock options on BSE
- Associated with Recovery Certificate No. 7315 of 2023
- Order issued in January 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action related to existing regulations on trading in illiquid stock options.

## Compliance Requirements

This is a release order indicating resolution of recovery proceedings. No additional compliance requirements for market participants.

## Important Dates

- Order Date: January 8, 2026
- Original Recovery Certificate: No. 7315 of 2023

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low to Medium - This is an individual enforcement case related to illiquid stock options trading practices.

**Regulatory Impact:** Medium - Demonstrates SEBI's continued enforcement of trading regulations and recovery proceedings for defaults in options trading.

**Operational Impact:** Low - Limited impact on broader market operations. Relevant primarily for enforcement tracking and regulatory compliance records.
