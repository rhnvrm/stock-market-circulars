## Summary

SEBI has issued a prohibitory order against M Jyotsana Lakshmi pursuant to Recovery Certificate Nos. 2831, 2832, and 2833 of 2020 in the matter of Ranklin Solutions Ltd and Vaishnavi Gold Ltd. The order directs banks, depositories, and other financial institutions holding assets belonging to the named individual to freeze those assets to facilitate recovery of dues owed to SEBI.

## Key Points

- Three recovery certificates (Nos. 2831, 2832, and 2833 of 2020) have been invoked against M Jyotsana Lakshmi.
- The proceedings relate to two matters: Ranklin Solutions Ltd and Vaishnavi Gold Ltd.
- SEBI exercises powers under Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992 to recover amounts as arrears of land revenue.
- Banks, depositories, and other asset-holding institutions are directed not to allow debit or transfer of the named individual's assets.
- The order applies to all accounts, demat holdings, and other assets held in the name of M Jyotsana Lakshmi.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI recovery and enforcement powers conferred by Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Banks**: Must freeze all accounts held in the name of M Jyotsana Lakshmi and not permit any debit transactions until further orders.
- **Depositories/DPs**: Must freeze all demat accounts and securities held in the name of M Jyotsana Lakshmi.
- **Other financial institutions**: Must not allow alienation, transfer, or disposal of any assets belonging to the named individual.
- Institutions must acknowledge receipt and compliance with this prohibitory order to SEBI's Recovery Officer.

## Important Dates

- **Recovery Certificates issued**: 2020 (Nos. 2831, 2832, 2833)
- **Prohibitory Order date**: March 6, 2026
- Compliance with the freeze is effective immediately upon receipt of this order.

## Impact Assessment

This order has no broader market or systemic impact. It is a targeted enforcement action affecting only M Jyotsana Lakshmi and the financial institutions holding her assets. The proceedings against Ranklin Solutions Ltd and Vaishnavi Gold Ltd are part of SEBI's ongoing recovery efforts against individuals and entities that have defaulted on regulatory dues. Market participants and investors are not directly affected.
