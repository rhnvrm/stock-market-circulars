## Summary

SEBI has issued a release order for Recovery Certificate No. 8491 of 2025 that was issued against Anil Gurjar, proprietor of Insight Research, in connection with operating as an unregistered investment adviser. The release order indicates that the recovery proceedings have been completed or compliance has been achieved, resulting in the cancellation of the recovery certificate.

## Key Points

- Release order issued for Recovery Certificate No. 8491 of 2025
- Entity involved: Insight Research (Proprietor: Anil Gurjar)
- Original matter: Operating as unregistered investment adviser
- Recovery certificate is being cancelled/released
- Document created on February 3, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action resolution document.

## Compliance Requirements

- The release order indicates that the compliance obligations related to this recovery certificate have been satisfied
- No ongoing compliance requirements for other entities as this pertains to a specific case resolution

## Important Dates

- **February 3, 2026**: Release order issued for Recovery Certificate No. 8491 of 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal - This is an individual case resolution with no broader market implications.

**Operational Impact**: Limited to the specific entity (Insight Research/Anil Gurjar). The release order suggests that the matter has been resolved, either through payment of dues or other compliance measures.

**Regulatory Significance**: This demonstrates SEBI's enforcement mechanism for unregistered investment advisers and the completion of recovery proceedings. It serves as a reminder of the requirement for investment advisers to be registered with SEBI.
