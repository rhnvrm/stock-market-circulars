## Summary

SEBI has issued a remittance advice in connection with recovery proceedings initiated against Capacious Farming Private Limited, along with Gaurav Yadav (PAN: ACQPY5145E) and Gurbakhsh Singh (PAN: BCLPS1117G), who are held jointly and severally liable. The action arises from Recovery Certificate No. 7543 of 2024 issued in the matter of Capacious Farming Private Limited, and directs the remittance of recovered amounts pursuant to enforcement action under SEBI's recovery proceedings framework.

## Key Points

- The remittance advice pertains to Recovery Certificate No. 7543 of 2024.
- Parties involved: Capacious Farming Private Limited (PAN: AAECC3138N), Gaurav Yadav (PAN: ACQPY5145E), and Gurbakhsh Singh (PAN: BCLPS1117G).
- Liability is joint and several among the named entity and individuals.
- The advice directs remittance of amounts recovered pursuant to the recovery certificate to the appropriate authority/account.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced; this is an enforcement action executing a pre-existing recovery certificate under SEBI's recovery proceedings mechanism.

## Compliance Requirements

The named entities are required to comply with the recovery certificate and any directions for remittance of dues. Recipients of the remittance advice (such as banks or intermediaries holding attached funds/assets) are required to act on the instructions to transfer recovered sums as directed by the Recovery Officer.

## Important Dates

The remittance advice is dated July 13, 2026, referencing Recovery Certificate No. 7543 of 2024.

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited direct market impact as it concerns a private limited company and individual defaulters rather than a publicly listed entity. It is primarily relevant for tracking SEBI's ongoing enforcement and recovery actions against defaulters of regulatory dues, penalties, or disgorgement amounts.
