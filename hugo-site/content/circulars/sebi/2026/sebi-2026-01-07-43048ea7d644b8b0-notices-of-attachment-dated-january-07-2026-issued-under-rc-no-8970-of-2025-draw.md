## Summary

SEBI has issued Notice(s) of Attachment dated January 07, 2026 under Recovery Certificate No. 8970 of 2025 drawn against M/s Highlight Investment Research (Proprietor: Ms. Shilpa Garg). This represents enforcement action to recover outstanding dues through attachment of properties or assets.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC 8970 of 2025 issued against M/s Highlight Investment Research
- Proprietor identified as Ms. Shilpa Garg
- Notice of Attachment issued on January 07, 2026
- Action taken under SEBI recovery proceedings
- Represents escalated enforcement measure for non-compliance or non-payment

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI recovery procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- M/s Highlight Investment Research and Ms. Shilpa Garg must comply with the attachment notice
- Outstanding dues must be settled to prevent further enforcement action
- All attached properties/assets will be subject to SEBI recovery proceedings
- Failure to comply may result in sale of attached assets for recovery

## Important Dates

- **January 07, 2026**: Date of Notice(s) of Attachment issuance
- Recovery Certificate issued in 2025 (RC No. 8970 of 2025)

## Impact Assessment

**Direct Impact**: High severity for M/s Highlight Investment Research and its proprietor Ms. Shilpa Garg. Attachment of properties/assets can significantly impact business operations and personal finances.

**Market Impact**: Limited broader market impact as this affects a single investment research firm. However, it demonstrates SEBI's continued enforcement of recovery proceedings against non-compliant entities.

**Investor Impact**: Investors who have used services of Highlight Investment Research should exercise caution and verify the firm's regulatory standing before relying on their research or recommendations.

**Precedent**: Reinforces SEBI's commitment to recovering outstanding dues through asset attachment when other recovery methods fail.
