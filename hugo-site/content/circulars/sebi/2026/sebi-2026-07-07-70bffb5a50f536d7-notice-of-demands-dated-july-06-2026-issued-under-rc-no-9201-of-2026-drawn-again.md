## Summary

SEBI's Recovery Officer has issued a Notice of Demand dated July 06, 2026 under Recovery Certificate (RC) No. 9201 of 2026, drawn against Dhaval Vinodbhai Gadani. The notice arises from enforcement proceedings concerning the trading activities of certain entities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now renamed DU Digital Global Limited). The document was published as a scanned notice (image-based PDF), so granular figures such as the exact demand amount and underlying SEBI order reference could not be extracted from the source file; the summary below reflects the standard structure and legal basis of such recovery notices.

## Key Points

- The notice is issued under Recovery Certificate No. 9201 of 2026, a formal instrument used by SEBI's Recovery Officer to enforce collection of monies (typically penalties, disgorgement amounts, or other dues) confirmed by an earlier SEBI adjudication or order.
- The noticee, Dhaval Vinodbhai Gadani, is stated to be connected to "trading activities of certain entities" in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited).
- This notice is part of a batch of related enforcement actions issued around the same date concerning the same underlying matter, including at least one other Notice of Demand (RC No. 9204 of 2026) and a separate order for attachment of a demat account and mutual fund folios.
- Recovery proceedings of this type are typically conducted under Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992, read with the Second and Third Schedules to the Income Tax Act, 1961, and the Income Tax (Certificate Proceedings) Rules, 1962.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced by this notice. It is an enforcement/recovery action under SEBI's existing statutory recovery framework, not a policy or rule change.

## Compliance Requirements

- The noticee named in the RC is required to pay the demanded amount to SEBI within the period specified in the notice (typically 15 days from service), failing which coercive recovery measures may follow — such as attachment and sale of movable/immovable property, attachment of bank and demat accounts, or arrest and detention under the applicable recovery rules.
- No compliance obligations arise for listed companies, intermediaries, or the general investing public from this specific notice.

## Important Dates

- Notice of Demand dated: July 06, 2026
- Publication date: July 07, 2026
- Payment/response deadline: Not specified in extractable text; typically 15 days from service of notice under standard recovery procedure.

## Impact Assessment

This notice has no direct bearing on trading, listing, or disclosure obligations of DU Digital Technologies Limited (DU Digital Global Limited) itself, nor on other market participants. Its effect is confined to the individual noticee against whom SEBI is enforcing collection of confirmed dues. Investors and market participants are not required to take any action in response to this circular. The broader matter — alleged irregular trading activity in the scrip — may be of background interest to shareholders tracking governance/enforcement history of the company, but this particular filing is a routine recovery-enforcement step rather than a new finding or market-moving event.
