## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order in respect of Recovery Certificate No. RC9082 of 2026, issued against Tavarya Trade-Link Private Limited (PAN: AACCT5851H), in the matter concerning illiquid stock options. The order relates to recovery proceedings initiated by SEBI's Recovery Officer to recover outstanding dues (such as penalties or disgorgement amounts) from the entity, arising out of SEBI's enforcement action against entities involved in non-genuine/reversal trades in the illiquid stock options segment of the exchanges.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9082 of 2026 was issued against Tavarya Trade-Link Private Limited (PAN: AACCT5851H).
- The matter pertains to SEBI's enforcement proceedings on illiquid stock options trading.
- SEBI has now issued a release order in respect of this recovery certificate, which typically follows realization/settlement of dues, release of attached assets, or closure/withdrawal of recovery proceedings against the entity.
- The specific grounds, recovered amount, and effective date are set out in the original order document.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is an administrative/enforcement order under SEBI's existing recovery framework (Section 28A of the SEBI Act read with the Second and Third Schedules to the Income Tax Act, 1961), applicable to the specific entity named.

## Compliance Requirements

This order is specific to Tavarya Trade-Link Private Limited and does not impose new compliance obligations on other market participants. The entity itself and any parties holding attached assets (e.g., banks, depositories) on its behalf should note the release and act per the order's directions.

## Important Dates

Specific dates (order date, recovery certificate issuance date) are contained in the source document and are not independently verifiable from the available metadata.

## Impact Assessment

The order has a narrow, entity-specific impact limited to Tavarya Trade-Link Private Limited and does not directly affect listed securities, exchange trading, or the broader investor community. It is primarily relevant for tracking SEBI's enforcement and recovery actions related to the illiquid stock options matter.
