## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC9090 of 2026 in Adjudication Proceedings No. 15469 of 2026 against Sandeep Agarwal HUF (PAN: AAVHS6180G) in the matter of trading in illiquid stock options on BSE. This certificate is issued under Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992, enabling SEBI to recover outstanding monetary penalties as arrears of land revenue.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9090 of 2026 issued against Sandeep Agarwal HUF
- PAN of the entity: AAVHS6180G
- Related Adjudication Proceedings (A.P.) No.: 15469 of 2026
- Matter pertains to trading in illiquid stock options on BSE
- Certificate issued under Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992
- Recovery to be effected as arrears of land revenue through the Collector/District Magistrate

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This document enforces an existing adjudication order by converting the outstanding penalty into a recoverable certificate.

## Compliance Requirements

- Sandeep Agarwal HUF is required to pay the penalty amount specified in the adjudication order
- Failure to comply will result in recovery through the Collector/District Magistrate as arrears of land revenue
- No action required from other market participants or intermediaries

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate issued: June 15, 2026
- Original adjudication proceedings: A.P. No. 15469 of 2026

## Impact Assessment

This enforcement action has minimal broader market impact. It is directed solely at Sandeep Agarwal HUF for alleged participation in illiquid stock options trading on BSE — a category of trades that SEBI has been systematically investigating for use in tax evasion and circular trading schemes. The issuance of a recovery certificate signals that the entity did not voluntarily pay the adjudicated penalty, prompting SEBI to invoke its coercive recovery mechanism under Section 28A of the SEBI Act.
