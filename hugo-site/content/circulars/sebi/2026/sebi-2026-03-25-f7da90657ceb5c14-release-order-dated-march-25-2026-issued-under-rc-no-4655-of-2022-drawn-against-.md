## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order dated March 25, 2026 under Recovery Certificate (RC) No. 4655 of 2022 drawn against Mahesh Somabhai Desai in the matter of Timbor Home Limited. This release order signifies that the recovery proceedings initiated against the respondent have been concluded, and any attachments or liens placed on assets pursuant to the recovery certificate are hereby released.

## Key Points

- Release Order issued on March 25, 2026 under RC No. 4655 of 2022
- Order drawn against Mahesh Somabhai Desai in the matter of Timbor Home Limited
- Indicates completion or settlement of recovery proceedings
- Any asset attachments or encumbrances arising from RC No. 4655 of 2022 are released
- Issued by SEBI under its enforcement and recovery proceedings framework

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an administrative order concluding existing enforcement action under the recovery proceedings mechanism.

## Compliance Requirements

- No new compliance obligations for market participants
- Relevant financial institutions and banks holding attachments under RC No. 4655 of 2022 should give effect to the release order
- The respondent (Mahesh Somabhai Desai) is released from the obligations under the said recovery certificate

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate No. 4655 issued: 2022
- Release Order Date: March 25, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This order has minimal broader market impact. It is specific to the individual (Mahesh Somabhai Desai) and the company (Timbor Home Limited) involved in the original enforcement action. The release order closes out the recovery proceedings, freeing any attached assets. No systemic or sector-wide implications are anticipated.
