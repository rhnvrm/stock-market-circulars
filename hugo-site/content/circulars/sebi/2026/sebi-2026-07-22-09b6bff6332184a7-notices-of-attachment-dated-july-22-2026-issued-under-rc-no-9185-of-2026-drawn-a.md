## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Attachment dated July 22, 2026, under Recovery Certificate (RC) No. 9185 of 2026, drawn against Usha Devi. The notice arises from recovery proceedings connected to the trading activities of certain entities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now renamed DU Digital Global Limited). The attachment notice is part of SEBI's enforcement mechanism to recover dues (such as penalties, disgorgement amounts, or other regulatory dues) from the named individual through attachment of property or assets.

## Key Points

- Notice of attachment issued against Usha Devi under RC No. 9185 of 2026.
- The matter pertains to trading activities of certain entities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited).
- The action is part of SEBI's recovery proceedings mechanism used to enforce outstanding regulatory dues.
- The notice was issued on July 22, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory or policy changes are introduced by this notice. It is an individual enforcement action under SEBI's recovery proceedings framework, typically invoked under provisions analogous to the Income Tax Act recovery rules as applied by SEBI for recovery of penalties, fees, or disgorgement amounts from non-compliant entities.

## Compliance Requirements

The named individual (Usha Devi) is subject to attachment of property/assets as per the recovery certificate. Entities connected to this matter and market intermediaries should note the enforcement action, particularly if the named entity holds any accounts, securities, or assets that may be subject to attachment. No broader compliance obligations are imposed on other market participants.

## Important Dates

- Notice date: July 22, 2026
- Recovery Certificate No.: 9185 of 2026

## Impact Assessment

This notice has limited direct market impact as it targets an individual's assets under a recovery proceeding rather than imposing obligations on listed companies or the broader market. It is relevant primarily for regulatory compliance tracking and reflects SEBI's ongoing enforcement action related to past irregular trading activities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited).
