## Summary

SEBI, in collaboration with leading market infrastructure institutions and industry bodies, has launched the Securities Market TechSprint as part of the Global Fintech Fest 2026 (GFF'26). Operating under the theme "Innovation in Action: Shaping Securities Market for Tomorrow", the initiative invites innovators to design digital solutions that empower retail investors and enhance transparency, efficiency, compliance, and accessibility in India's capital markets.

## Key Points

- TechSprint is a collaborative initiative by SEBI and market infrastructure institutions at GFF'26
- Participants must address one of four defined problem statements
- Solutions will be showcased at the Global Fintech Fest with cash prizes awarded
- Shortlisted innovations may receive mentorship through the SEBI Innovation Sandbox
- Registration details, timelines, eligibility, and evaluation criteria are available via the linked registration portal

## Problem Statements

- **AI-Driven Detection of Synthetic Media and Phishing Attacks in Securities Markets** — solutions to identify and counter AI-generated misinformation and phishing threats
- **Agentic Compliance — From Regulatory Text to Operational Action** — automating compliance workflows from regulatory documents
- **Super App for Unified Multi-Asset Investing and Awareness for Retail Investors** — a single platform enabling retail investors to manage multi-asset portfolios
- **Simplifying IPO Offer Document Preparation for SMEs** — tools to reduce complexity and cost of IPO documentation for small and mid-sized enterprises

## Prize Structure

| Prize | Amount (INR) |
|---|---|
| 1st Prize | 2,50,000 |
| 2nd Prize | 2,00,000 |
| 3rd Prize | 1,50,000 |
| Jury Recommendation | 50,000 each |

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a press release announcing a fintech innovation competition.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance requirements imposed on market participants. Participation in the TechSprint is voluntary and open to eligible innovators as per the terms available on the registration portal.

## Important Dates

- Announcement Date: June 22, 2026
- Further timelines (registration deadlines, submission dates, event date) are available on the official registration link referenced in the circular.

## Impact Assessment

This announcement has no direct market or operational impact on listed companies, brokers, or investors. It is primarily relevant to fintech startups, technology developers, and innovators interested in building solutions for the Indian securities market. Shortlisted participants gain visibility at GFF'26 and potential access to SEBI's Innovation Sandbox for mentorship and further development.
