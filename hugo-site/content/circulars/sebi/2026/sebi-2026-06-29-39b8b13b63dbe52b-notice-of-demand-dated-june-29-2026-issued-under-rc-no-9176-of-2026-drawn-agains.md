## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand dated June 29, 2026 under Recovery Certificate (RC) No. 9176 of 2026 against Nikunj Sureshchandra Shah. The demand arises from trading activities of certain entities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited, now known as DU Digital Global Limited. This notice is part of SEBI's enforcement and recovery proceedings against entities found to have violated securities laws.

## Key Points

- Notice of Demand issued on June 29, 2026 under RC No. 9176 of 2026
- Demand drawn against Nikunj Sureshchandra Shah
- Related to trading activities of certain entities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited)
- Issued by SEBI as part of its enforcement and recovery proceedings
- Recovery Certificate indicates prior adjudication order or penalty has been passed and remains unpaid

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced by this notice. It represents enforcement of existing SEBI orders and recovery of dues under the SEBI (Recovery of Amounts) Procedure, 2013.

## Compliance Requirements

- Nikunj Sureshchandra Shah is required to pay the demanded amount as specified in the Recovery Certificate No. 9176 of 2026
- Non-compliance with the Notice of Demand may result in further recovery actions including attachment of assets, bank accounts, and demat accounts
- The noticee should respond to SEBI within the timeline specified in the notice

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 29, 2026
- **RC No.:** 9176 of 2026
- Payment/response deadline: As specified in the notice document

## Impact Assessment

This is an individual enforcement action against Nikunj Sureshchandra Shah related to alleged trading irregularities in DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited). The market impact is limited to the named individual. However, it signals SEBI's continued active enforcement against entities involved in suspected manipulation or irregular trading in listed scrips. Investors in DU Digital Global Limited should note the ongoing regulatory scrutiny of trading activities in this scrip.
