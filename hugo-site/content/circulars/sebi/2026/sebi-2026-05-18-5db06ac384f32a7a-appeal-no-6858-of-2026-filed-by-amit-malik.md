## Summary

The SEBI Appellate Authority adjudicated Appeal No. 6858 of 2026 filed by Amit Malik against CPIO, SEBI, Mumbai. The appellant challenged SEBI's responses to three RTI queries seeking information about whether Indraprastha Gas Limited (IGL) had disclosed court case W.P.(C) 6759/2007 as a material litigation under Regulation 30 of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015.

## Key Points

- Appellant Amit Malik filed an RTI application on March 26, 2026 seeking information about IGL's disclosure obligations
- Three queries related to: (1) whether IGL disclosed W.P.(C) 6759/2007 as material litigation to BSE/NSE, (2) whether the case is mentioned in IGL's Annual Report 2024-25 under Material Litigation or Contingent Liabilities, and (3) whether any complaint or inquiry is pending against IGL regarding non-disclosure
- SEBI (respondent) replied that query 1 information is not maintained by SEBI in the normal course of regulation
- Query 2 was deemed a request for clarification, not "information" under Section 2(f) of the RTI Act
- Query 3 was addressed by noting investigations are confidential, and any resultant regulatory action would be publicly available on SEBI's website
- Appellant filed the appeal on grounds of incomplete, misleading or false information

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an appellate order on an individual RTI matter and does not introduce any new rules or amendments.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements for market participants. The order pertains solely to the handling of an RTI application and SEBI's information-maintenance obligations.

## Important Dates

- March 26, 2026: Appellant's original RTI application filed
- April 20, 2026: SEBI (respondent) replied to the RTI application
- April 21, 2026: Appellant filed the first appeal (Reg. No. SEBIH/A/E/26/00146)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a procedural RTI appellate order concerning one individual's queries about IGL's disclosure practices. No penalties, directions, or regulatory actions against IGL or any other entity have been issued as part of this order. The order does not alter any disclosure norms under SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015.
