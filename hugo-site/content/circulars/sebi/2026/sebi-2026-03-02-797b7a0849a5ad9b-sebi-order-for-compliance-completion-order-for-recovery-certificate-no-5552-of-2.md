## Summary

SEBI has issued a Completion Order for Recovery Certificate No. 5552 of 2022 against Mr. S Rajaganesh in connection with the matter of SMS Techsoft (India) Ltd. This order marks the formal conclusion of the recovery proceedings initiated under the said Recovery Certificate, confirming that the recovery process has been completed. A companion Release Order (for the same Recovery Certificate) has also been issued on the same date.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 5552 of 2022 has been fully executed against Mr. S Rajaganesh
- The completion order formally closes the recovery proceedings under this certificate
- The order is issued under SEBI's enforcement and recovery proceedings framework
- A companion Release Order for the same Recovery Certificate No. 5552 of 2022 was issued simultaneously
- The underlying matter involves SMS Techsoft (India) Ltd.
- Published under SEBI's enforcement/recovery proceedings section dated March 2, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is an administrative order that concludes existing enforcement proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

This completion order does not impose new compliance obligations on the broader market. The recovery proceedings against Mr. S Rajaganesh in the SMS Techsoft (India) Ltd. matter have been concluded. No further action is required from other market participants.

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate Issued: 2022 (No. 5552 of 2022)
- Completion Order Date: March 2, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This order has minimal impact on the broader market. It is an individual enforcement action closure specific to Mr. S Rajaganesh and the SMS Techsoft (India) Ltd. matter. The issuance of both a Completion Order and a Release Order on the same date indicates that the recovery proceedings have been fully resolved, likely through payment of dues or successful asset recovery. There is no systemic or sector-wide significance to this order.
