## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Attachment of Bank Accounts and Demat Accounts vide AP No. 15504 & 15505 of 2026, dated June 29, 2026, against Arpit PiyushBhai Shah (PAN: BECPS1043E) as a defaulter. The attachment is executed under Recovery Certificate No. 9150 of 2026 arising from proceedings related to price and volume manipulation in the scrip of Quasar India Limited.

## Key Points

- Attachment Petition Numbers: AP No. 15504 & 15505 of 2026
- Defaulter: Arpit PiyushBhai Shah (PAN: BECPS1043E)
- Recovery Certificate: No. 9150 of 2026
- Underlying matter: Price and volume manipulation in the scrip of Quasar India Limited
- Scope of attachment: Both bank accounts and demat accounts of the defaulter
- Notice date: June 29, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement and recovery action under SEBI's existing statutory powers for attachment of assets of defaulters.

## Compliance Requirements

- Banks holding accounts of Arpit PiyushBhai Shah are required to freeze and attach the identified accounts as directed
- Depositories and depository participants are required to freeze the identified demat accounts
- The defaulter is prohibited from operating or transacting through the attached accounts until SEBI issues further directions
- Any entity served with the notice must comply immediately and confirm compliance with SEBI

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: June 29, 2026
- Recovery Certificate No. 9150 of 2026 (issuance date not specified in title)
- Attachment Petition Nos. 15504 & 15505 of 2026

## Impact Assessment

This action has no broader market or systemic impact. It is an individual-level enforcement measure targeting a specific defaulter found to have engaged in price and volume manipulation in Quasar India Limited. The attachment restricts the defaulter's ability to access or transfer financial assets pending recovery of dues owed to SEBI. The action reinforces SEBI's ongoing use of recovery certificate proceedings to enforce monetary penalties and disgorgement orders against market manipulators.
