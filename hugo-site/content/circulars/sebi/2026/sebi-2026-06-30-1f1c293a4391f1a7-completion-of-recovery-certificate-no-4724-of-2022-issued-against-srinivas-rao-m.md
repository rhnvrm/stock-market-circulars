## Summary

SEBI has issued a notice confirming the completion of recovery proceedings under Recovery Certificate No. 4724 of 2022, which was issued against Srinivas Rao Marupudi in connection with the matter of Venus Power Ventures Ltd. The recovery certificate, originally issued to recover outstanding dues (such as penalties, disgorgement amounts, or other monetary liabilities imposed by SEBI), has now been marked as completed/closed by the Recovery Officer.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 4724 of 2022 was issued against Srinivas Rao Marupudi.
- The certificate relates to enforcement action in the matter of Venus Power Ventures Ltd.
- SEBI has confirmed that the recovery proceedings under this certificate stand completed.
- This closure indicates that the amount recoverable under the certificate has been realized or the proceeding has otherwise been concluded as per applicable recovery rules.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is an administrative/enforcement update closing out a specific recovery proceeding under the SEBI (Procedure for Holding Inquiry and Imposing Penalties) Rules and the applicable recovery of dues provisions referencing the Income Tax Act, 1961 recovery mechanism (as adopted by SEBI recovery proceedings).

## Compliance Requirements

No further compliance action is required from market participants generally. For the individual named in the recovery certificate, obligations under the certificate are now considered discharged/completed. No action is required from listed companies, brokers, or investors as a result of this notice.

## Important Dates

- Notice of completion dated: June 30, 2026.

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal market impact. It pertains to the closure of an individual recovery certificate related to a past SEBI enforcement action and does not affect trading, listing status, or disclosure obligations of any publicly traded company. It is primarily relevant for regulatory record-keeping and enforcement tracking purposes.
