## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC9138 of 2026 in Adjudication Proceeding (A.P.) No. 15486 of 2026 against Amanat Developers Private Limited (PAN: AAGCA9440D) in connection with the matter of trading in illiquid stock options. This certificate authorises recovery of the penalty amount imposed on the entity through adjudication proceedings.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9138 of 2026 issued against Amanat Developers Private Limited
- PAN of the defaulter: AAGCA9440D
- Adjudication Proceeding reference: A.P. No. 15486 of 2026
- The matter pertains to alleged fraudulent or manipulative trading in illiquid stock options
- Recovery certificate enables SEBI to recover the penalty amount as arrears of land revenue

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is an enforcement/recovery action under SEBI's adjudication powers pursuant to the SEBI Act, 1992 and the SEBI (Procedure for Holding Inquiry and Imposing Penalties) Rules, 2002.

## Compliance Requirements

- Amanat Developers Private Limited is required to pay the penalty amount specified in the adjudication order
- Non-payment will result in recovery through attachment and sale of movable/immovable property under the relevant recovery provisions
- No compliance obligations arise for third parties or the broader market from this certificate

## Important Dates

- Certificate issued: June 2026
- Payment of penalty should have been made pursuant to the underlying adjudication order; recovery certificate is issued upon default of such payment

## Impact Assessment

This recovery certificate has minimal market impact as it is directed at a single private entity (Amanat Developers Private Limited) for penalty recovery in the illiquid stock options enforcement matter. It reflects SEBI's ongoing enforcement actions against entities found to have engaged in manipulative trading in illiquid stock options. Broader market participants are not affected.
