## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC9085 of 2026 under Application No. 15444 of 2026 against Arrowlink Chemical Private Limited (PAN: AAKCA1933D) in connection with the matter of illiquid stock options trading. This certificate enables SEBI to recover dues as determined by prior adjudication or settlement orders.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9085 of 2026 issued against Arrowlink Chemical Private Limited
- PAN of the entity: AAKCA1933D
- Application No. (A.P.): 15444 of 2026
- The matter pertains to illiquid stock options, a long-running SEBI enforcement area
- Recovery certificate empowers SEBI to recover outstanding dues from the noticee

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement/recovery action pursuant to an existing SEBI order in the illiquid stock options matter.

## Compliance Requirements

- Arrowlink Chemical Private Limited is required to pay the outstanding dues as specified in the recovery certificate
- Non-payment may result in attachment of assets or other coercive recovery measures by SEBI
- The entity must comply with directions in the underlying adjudication/settlement order

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9085 issued in 2026
- A.P. No. 15444 of 2026 filed in 2026
- Document published: June 2026

## Impact Assessment

This recovery certificate has limited broader market impact as it targets a single entity — Arrowlink Chemical Private Limited — for dues arising from illiquid stock options trading violations. SEBI has issued a large number of such recovery certificates as part of its ongoing crackdown on manipulative trading in illiquid stock options on BSE. The action reinforces SEBI's continued enforcement posture in this segment but does not affect ongoing market operations or other participants.
