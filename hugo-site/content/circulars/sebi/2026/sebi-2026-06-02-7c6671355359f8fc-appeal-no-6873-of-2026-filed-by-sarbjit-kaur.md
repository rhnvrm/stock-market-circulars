## Summary

The SEBI Appellate Authority (Ruchi Chojer) dismissed RTI Appeal No. 6873 of 2026 filed by Sarbjit Kaur against the CPIO, SEBI, Mumbai. The appellant had sought detailed information about a PACL land allotment related to her refund claim, but SEBI's CPIO responded that such information is not available with SEBI. The Appellate Authority found no deficiency in the respondent's reply and upheld the dismissal.

## Key Points

- Appellant filed RTI application (received by SEBI on April 02, 2026) seeking information about a PACL land allotment relevant to her refund claim
- Queries included: land allotment records, plot number, location, date of allotment, possession documents, SEBI's verification procedure, applicable rules, and reconsideration process
- CPIO responded on April 08, 2026, stating the requested information is not available with SEBI
- Appellant filed appeal (Reg. No. SEBIH/A/P/26/00026, received May 08, 2026)
- Appellate Authority cited CIC precedent (Sh. Pattipati Rama Murthy vs. CPIO, SEBI, July 8, 2013): CPIO cannot invent information it does not possess
- Appeal dismissed on June 02, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine administrative RTI appeal decision with no broader regulatory implications.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. SEBI reiterated that PACL-related information—including status reports, FAQs, press releases, and public notices—is available on the SEBI website. Investors with PACL-related queries are directed to those public resources.

## Important Dates

- April 02, 2026: RTI application received by SEBI
- April 08, 2026: CPIO response issued
- May 08, 2026: Appeal received by Appellate Authority
- June 02, 2026: Appeal dismissed by Appellate Authority

## Impact Assessment

No market or operational impact. This decision affects only the individual appellant and confirms that SEBI does not hold PACL land allotment data at the investor level. Responsibility for PACL investor repayment remains with the Justice (Retd.) R. M. Lodha Committee, constituted pursuant to the Supreme Court of India order dated February 2, 2016.
