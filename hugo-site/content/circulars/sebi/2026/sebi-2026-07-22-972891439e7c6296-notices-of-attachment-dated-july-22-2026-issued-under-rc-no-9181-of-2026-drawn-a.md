## Summary

The Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) has issued a Notice of Attachment dated July 22, 2026, under Recovery Certificate (RC) No. 9181 of 2026. The notice is drawn against Rathod Mahendrkumar in connection with proceedings relating to the trading activities of certain entities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now known as DU Digital Global Limited). This action forms part of SEBI's recovery proceedings to enforce outstanding dues/penalties arising from an earlier regulatory order concerning alleged irregular or manipulative trading in the said scrip.

## Key Points

- SEBI has issued a formal Notice of Attachment against Rathod Mahendrkumar.
- The notice is issued under Recovery Certificate No. 9181 of 2026.
- The matter pertains to trading activities of certain entities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited, now renamed DU Digital Global Limited.
- The notice is dated July 22, 2026 and is published as part of SEBI's ongoing enforcement/recovery action.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory policy is introduced. This is an enforcement action under SEBI's existing recovery mechanism, typically invoked under the Securities and Exchange Board of India Act, 1992, read with the Income Tax Act (Second Schedule) recovery provisions applied by SEBI Recovery Officers, to recover dues confirmed via a Recovery Certificate against a defaulter.

## Compliance Requirements

- The named individual, Rathod Mahendrkumar, is subject to attachment proceedings for recovery of dues under RC No. 9181 of 2026.
- Entities or persons holding assets, bank accounts, or dues on behalf of Rathod Mahendrkumar may be required to comply with attachment directions issued by the SEBI Recovery Officer and refrain from releasing attached assets without authorization.
- Market intermediaries and depositories dealing with the named individual should verify compliance with any related freeze/attachment directives referenced in the notice.

## Important Dates

- Notice of Attachment date: July 22, 2026.

## Impact Assessment

This notice represents a targeted enforcement/recovery action against a specific individual rather than a market-wide or company-wide directive, and it does not directly affect trading, listing, or disclosure obligations of DU Digital Global Limited itself. Its primary relevance is to regulatory compliance and enforcement tracking, and to any parties holding assets or conducting transactions on behalf of the named defaulter, who should take note of the attachment restrictions.
