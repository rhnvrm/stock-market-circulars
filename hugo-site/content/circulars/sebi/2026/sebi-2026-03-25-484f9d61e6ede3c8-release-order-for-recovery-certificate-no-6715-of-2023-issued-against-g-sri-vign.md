SEBI has issued a release order for Recovery Certificate No. 6715 of 2023 against G Sri Vignesh (PAN: GOAPS3552D) in the matter of Securekloud Technologies Limited. The release order signifies the conclusion or resolution of the recovery proceedings initiated under this certificate.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 6715 of 2023
- Individual: G Sri Vignesh (PAN: GOAPS3552D)
- Related matter: Securekloud Technologies Limited
- Order type: Release Order (concluding recovery proceedings)
- A companion Completion of Recovery Certificate order was also issued on the same date

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a case-specific enforcement action conclusion releasing the defaulter from recovery proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

No ongoing compliance requirements for the broader market. This order pertains solely to G Sri Vignesh in connection with the Securekloud Technologies Limited matter.

## Important Dates

- Release Order Date: March 25, 2026
- Recovery Certificate No. 6715 issued: 2023

Minimal market impact. This is an individual-specific release order concluding SEBI's recovery proceedings against G Sri Vignesh in connection with Securekloud Technologies Limited. The release indicates the recovery matter has been resolved or otherwise closed by SEBI.
