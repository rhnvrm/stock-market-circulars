## Summary

The SEBI Appellate Authority under the RTI Act dismissed Appeal No. 6851 of 2026 filed by Amit Kumar. The appellant sought a Yes/No clarification on whether an investor can file a dispute against Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE) on the Smart ODR portal. The authority upheld the CPIO's response that this constitutes a request for opinion/clarification rather than 'information' as defined under Section 2(f) of the RTI Act.

## Key Points

- Appellant Amit Kumar filed an RTI application on March 18, 2026 asking whether an investor can file a dispute against BSE on the Smart ODR portal
- CPIO responded on April 15, 2026 stating the query seeks clarification/opinion, not 'information' under RTI Act
- CPIO voluntarily informed that investors can file disputes against regulated entities listed in Schedule A of SEBI Master Circular dated July 31, 2023 (updated December 28, 2023)
- Appellant appealed on April 28, 2026 (Reg. No. SEBIH/A/E/26/00153) alleging incomplete, misleading or false information
- Appellate Authority dismissed the appeal, finding no deficiency in the respondent's response

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative appellate order with no new rules or amendments.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. Investors seeking information about ODR portal dispute eligibility are directed to Schedule A of the SEBI Master Circular dated July 31, 2023 (updated December 28, 2023), available on the SEBI website.

## Important Dates

- RTI Application filed: March 18, 2026
- CPIO Response: April 15, 2026
- Appeal filed: April 28, 2026
- Appeal Order Date: May 25, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This order has no market or operational impact. It is a routine RTI appellate decision clarifying that queries seeking opinions or clarifications do not qualify as 'information' under Section 2(f) of the RTI Act. The decision reaffirms established CIC precedent (Azad Singh vs. CPIO, Oriental Insurance Company Limited, March 23, 2021) that CPIOs are communicators of existing records, not creators or interpreters of information.
