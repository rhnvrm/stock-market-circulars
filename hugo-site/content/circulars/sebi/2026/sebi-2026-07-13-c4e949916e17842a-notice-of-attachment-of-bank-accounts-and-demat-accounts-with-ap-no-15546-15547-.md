## Summary

SEBI has issued a notice of attachment (AP No. 15546 & 15547 of 2026, dated July 13, 2026) directing the attachment of bank accounts and demat accounts belonging to Sagarkumar Pravinchandra Dataniya, who has been declared a defaulter. The action arises from recovery proceedings under Recovery Certificate No. 9171 of 2026, issued in connection with the trading activities of certain entities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited).

## Key Points

- Defaulter named: Sagarkumar Pravinchandra Dataniya.
- Attachment Proceeding (AP) Nos.: 15546 and 15547 of 2026.
- Notice dated: July 13, 2026.
- Underlying Recovery Certificate No.: 9171 of 2026.
- Matter relates to trading activities of certain entities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now renamed DU Digital Global Limited).
- The attachment covers both bank accounts and demat (securities) accounts held by the defaulter.
- This notice is part of SEBI's broader enforcement action against multiple individuals connected to the same matter, evidenced by parallel recovery certificates issued around the same date.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory policy is introduced. This is an enforcement/recovery action taken under SEBI's existing powers to recover dues (such as disgorgement amounts, penalties, or other sums due) as arrears of land revenue, pursuant to Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992 read with the Second and Third Schedules to the Income-tax Act, 1961 and the Income-tax (Certificate Proceedings) Rules, 1962, as applicable to recovery proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

- Banks and depository participants holding accounts in the name of Sagarkumar Pravinchandra Dataniya are required to comply with the attachment notice and restrict/freeze the specified accounts as directed.
- The defaulter is barred from operating the attached bank and demat accounts until the recovery dues are cleared or the attachment is lifted/varied by the Recovery Officer.
- Any person or entity holding assets on behalf of the defaulter must report compliance with the attachment order to SEBI's Recovery Officer.

## Important Dates

- Notice date: July 13, 2026.
- Underlying Recovery Certificate No. 9171 of 2026 (date as specified in the certificate; attachment follows from this certificate).

## Impact Assessment

The direct market impact is minimal since this action targets an individual defaulter's personal bank and demat accounts rather than the listed company or its shares. It does not affect trading in DU Digital Global Limited shares directly. The notice is significant from a compliance and enforcement-tracking perspective, reflecting SEBI's continued efforts to recover dues from individuals implicated in trading-related violations connected to this scrip, as part of a larger set of coordinated recovery actions against multiple defaulters in the same matter.
