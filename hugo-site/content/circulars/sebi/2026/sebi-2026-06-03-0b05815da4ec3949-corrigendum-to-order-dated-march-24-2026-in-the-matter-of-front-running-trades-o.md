## Summary

SEBI has issued a corrigendum to its Order dated March 24, 2026 (ref: QJA/MN/IVD-1/ID3/32250/2025-26) in the matter of front-running trades of Big Client - Sarvottam Securities Private Limited by multiple entities. The corrigendum corrects a typographical error in the name of a fund referenced in the original order.

## Key Points

- The original order was passed on March 24, 2026 and dealt with front-running trades involving Sarvottam Securities Private Limited as the Big Client.
- At page 40, sub-paragraph (a) of paragraph 130, the fund name was incorrectly stated as "Investor Education and Protection Fund (IEPF)".
- The correct name is "Investor Protection and Education Fund (IPEF)".
- The original order shall always be read together with this corrigendum.

## Regulatory Changes

No substantive regulatory changes. This corrigendum solely corrects a typographical error — the fund acronym and full name were transposed in the original order.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance obligations arise from this corrigendum. Parties subject to the original March 24, 2026 order should read that order in conjunction with this correction wherever paragraph 130(a) is referenced.

## Important Dates

- **March 24, 2026**: Date of the original order (ref: QJA/MN/IVD-1/ID3/32250/2025-26)
- **June 3, 2026**: Date of this corrigendum

## Impact Assessment

This corrigendum has no market or operational impact. It is a purely administrative correction to a fund name acronym within an existing enforcement order on front-running. The substantive findings and directions of the original order remain unchanged.
