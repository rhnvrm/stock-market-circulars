## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Attachment dated July 08, 2026 under Recovery Certificate (RC) No. 9136 of 2026, drawn against Mediaone Global Entertainment Limited (PAN: AAACR0405M), in the matter of recovery proceedings against the entity. The notice is issued by SEBI's Recovery Officer in exercise of powers under Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992, read with the Second and Third Schedules to the Income Tax Act, 1961 and the Income Tax (Certificate Proceedings) Rules, 1962, as applicable to recovery of amounts due to SEBI.

## Key Points

- Notice of attachment issued against Mediaone Global Entertainment Limited (PAN: AAACR0405M).
- Action taken pursuant to Recovery Certificate No. 9136 of 2026 issued by SEBI's Recovery Officer.
- The notice pertains to attachment of property/assets/bank accounts of the defaulting entity to recover outstanding dues.
- Forms part of SEBI's ongoing enforcement and recovery proceedings against entities that have failed to comply with earlier SEBI orders or payment obligations.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced; this is an enforcement action under existing recovery provisions of the SEBI Act, 1992 and the Income Tax Act, 1961 (as applied to SEBI recovery proceedings).

## Compliance Requirements

- Mediaone Global Entertainment Limited and any custodians/holders of its attached property or funds must comply with the attachment order and refrain from dealing with the attached assets.
- Any bank, intermediary, or third party holding assets/funds on behalf of the entity must act in accordance with the attachment notice.

## Important Dates

- Notice of Attachment dated: July 08, 2026.

## Impact Assessment

This action has limited direct impact on broader securities markets or listed stocks, as it targets a specific entity's assets for recovery of dues owed to SEBI. It is primarily relevant for compliance and enforcement tracking, and for stakeholders connected to Mediaone Global Entertainment Limited, including its creditors, bankers, and any related parties.
