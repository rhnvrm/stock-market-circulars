## Summary

SEBI has issued a completion order for Recovery Certificate No. 8219 of 2024 against Riya Jain (Kanungo) in connection with front running activities involving various funds of Fidelity Group. This order marks the completion of recovery proceedings initiated against the individual.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8219 of 2024 proceedings have been completed
- Individual involved: Riya Jain (Kanungo)
- Matter relates to front running involving various funds of Fidelity Group
- This is a compliance completion order indicating conclusion of recovery proceedings

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an enforcement completion order.

## Compliance Requirements

- Recovery proceedings under the specified certificate have been completed
- The order documents the conclusion of enforcement actions against the individual

## Important Dates

- Order Date: January 16, 2026
- Recovery Certificate No: 8219 of 2024

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This is an individual enforcement completion order with no direct market-wide implications.

**Operational Impact:** Low - The completion order closes recovery proceedings against a specific individual and does not impose new compliance requirements on market participants.

**Stakeholder Impact:** Limited to the individual named in the order (Riya Jain Kanungo) and relates to concluded front running proceedings involving Fidelity Group funds.
