## Summary

SEBI issued a Notice of Demand (Certificate No. 9192 of 2026) under Rule 2 of the Second Schedule to the Income-tax Act, 1961 read with Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992, against Sagarkumar Pravinchandra Dataniya (PAN: BJIPD7414F) of Ahmedabad, Gujarat. The notice demands recovery of Rs. 3,91,725.66, arising from a disgorgement order issued in connection with the trading activities of certain entities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited).

## Key Points

- The demand stems from a Quasi-Judicial Authority Order No. QJA/MN/IVD/ID7/31947/2025-26 dated 31.12.2025, which imposed a disgorgement amount of Rs. 3,65,164.17 on the entity.
- Interest of Rs. 25,561.49 has been added, calculated at 12% p.a. from January 2026 to July 2026.
- A recovery cost of Rs. 1,000 has also been levied.
- Total amount demanded: Rs. 3,91,725.66.
- The entity is directed to pay within 15 days of receipt of the notice via EFT/NEFT/RTGS to SEBI's designated recovery account, or through the SEBI online payment portal.
- Failure to pay may result in SEBI's Recovery Officer proceeding under Sections 220 to 232 of the Income-tax Act, 1961, and the Second Schedule thereto.

## Regulatory Changes

This is an enforcement action rather than a regulatory or policy change. It applies existing recovery provisions under Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992, read with the Income-tax Act, 1961.

## Compliance Requirements

- The noticee must pay the total demanded sum within 15 days of receipt of the notice.
- Confirmation of e-payment must be forwarded to SEBI's Recovery Officer at SEBI Bhavan-II, Mumbai, or emailed to the designated recovery officer addresses.
- Post-service of the notice, the noticee is barred from mortgaging, charging, leasing, or otherwise dealing with any property without permission from the Recovery Officer; any such unauthorized transfer will be deemed void.
- Any transfer of property or funds to a spouse, minor child, son's wife, or son's minor child without adequate consideration on or after December 31, 2025, will be deemed the noticee's property for recovery purposes.

## Important Dates

- Order date: 31.12.2025 (original disgorgement order)
- Notice date: 02.07.2026
- Payment deadline: within 15 days of receipt of this notice
- Interest accrual period referenced: January 2026 to July 2026

## Impact Assessment

This notice affects only the individual noticee and does not have direct implications for the broader market, listed company operations, or shareholders of DU Digital Global Limited. In case of non-payment, SEBI may pursue coercive recovery measures including attachment of movable/immovable property, bank account attachment, or arrest and detention. The action underscores SEBI's ongoing enforcement of disgorgement orders related to irregular trading activities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited).
