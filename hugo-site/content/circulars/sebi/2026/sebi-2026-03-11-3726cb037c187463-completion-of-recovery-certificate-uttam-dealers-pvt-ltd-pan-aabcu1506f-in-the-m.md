## Summary

SEBI has issued a completion order for Recovery Certificate proceedings against Uttam Dealers Pvt. Ltd. (PAN: AABCU1506F) in the matter of Illiquid Stock Options. The completion of the recovery certificate indicates that the outstanding dues payable by Uttam Dealers Pvt. Ltd. have been fully recovered by SEBI.

## Key Points

- SEBI has completed the recovery certificate issued against Uttam Dealers Pvt. Ltd. (PAN: AABCU1506F).
- The recovery pertains to the matter of Illiquid Stock Options, which was an enforcement action taken by SEBI against manipulative trading in illiquid stock option contracts.
- The completion of the recovery certificate signifies that all outstanding dues owed to SEBI have been recovered from the entity.
- This order concludes the recovery proceedings against Uttam Dealers Pvt. Ltd. in this matter.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced by this order. This is an enforcement closure notice specific to Uttam Dealers Pvt. Ltd.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements are imposed by this order. The completion of the recovery certificate closes the enforcement action against Uttam Dealers Pvt. Ltd. for this matter.

## Important Dates

- **Order Date:** March 11, 2026
- The recovery certificate proceedings are now completed as of this date.

## Impact Assessment

This order has minimal market impact as it is a routine enforcement closure specific to one entity (Uttam Dealers Pvt. Ltd.) in connection with past illiquid stock options trading violations. It does not affect broader market operations, trading rules, or compliance obligations for other market participants.
