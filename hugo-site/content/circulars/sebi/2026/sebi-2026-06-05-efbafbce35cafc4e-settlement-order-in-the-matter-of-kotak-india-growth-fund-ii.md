## Summary

SEBI issued a settlement order (SO/KV/PSD/2026-27/8628) in respect of Kotak India Growth Fund II (PAN: AABTK3261A), settling enforcement proceedings for violation of the SEBI (Venture Capital Funds) Regulations, 1996. The applicant paid ₹10,87,500 as settlement amount, resolving the matter without admitting or denying the findings.

## Key Points

- Kotak India Growth Fund II filed a suo-motu settlement application under SEBI (Settlement Proceedings) Regulations, 2018
- The fund was registered with SEBI as a venture capital fund, launched on March 27, 2008 with an initial tenure of nine years (extendable by 36 months)
- The scheme's tenure ended on March 27, 2020, but winding up and distribution of proceeds was completed only on October 16, 2025 — a delay of over five years
- Violations relate to Regulation 23(1)(a) read with Regulations 23(3) and 24(2) of the SEBI (Venture Capital Funds) Regulations, 1996
- Settlement amount of ₹10,87,500 (Rupees Ten Lakhs Eighty-Seven Thousand Five Hundred) was proposed and accepted
- The High Powered Advisory Committee (HPAC) recommended settlement on March 25, 2026
- Panel of Whole Time Members approved settlement terms on May 11, 2026
- Payment was confirmed remitted by the applicant on May 21, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This order applies existing provisions of the SEBI (Settlement Proceedings) Regulations, 2018 and SEBI Act, 1992 (Section 15JB read with Section 19).

## Compliance Requirements

- SEBI will not initiate enforcement action against Kotak India Growth Fund II for the specified violations
- The settlement is without prejudice to SEBI's rights under Regulations 28 and 31 of the Settlement Regulations if any representation made by the applicant is found to be untrue or the applicant fails to comply with the settlement terms

## Important Dates

- March 27, 2008: Scheme commencement date
- March 27, 2020: Scheme tenure end date
- October 16, 2025: Actual completion of winding up and distribution of proceeds
- February 10, 2026: Internal Committee meeting with applicant's representatives
- March 02, 2026: Applicant filed revised settlement terms
- March 25, 2026: HPAC recommended settlement
- May 11, 2026: Panel of Whole Time Members approved settlement
- May 21, 2026: Applicant confirmed remittance; SEBI confirmed credit

## Impact Assessment

This settlement has minimal broader market impact. It is a closed administrative matter concerning a single venture capital fund that has already completed its wind-up. The relatively modest settlement amount (₹10.87 lakh) and the suo-motu nature of the application indicate cooperative compliance. The order sets a precedent that venture capital funds face regulatory scrutiny for delays in winding up beyond their extended tenure, but does not impose new obligations on other market participants.
