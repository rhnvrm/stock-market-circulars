## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Attachment of Bank Accounts (No. 15384 of 2026, dated May 26, 2026) against Money Mishra Financial Services, identified as the defaulter with PAN AAZFM1357R. This action is taken under Recovery Certificate No. 9073 of 2026, in the matter of unregistered portfolio management services provided by Allied Financial Services Private Limited.

## Key Points

- Notice of Attachment issued: No. 15384 of 2026, dated May 26, 2026
- Defaulter: Money Mishra Financial Services (PAN: AAZFM1357R)
- Underlying matter: Unregistered portfolio management services by Allied Financial Services Private Limited
- Recovery Certificate No.: 9073 of 2026
- Enforcement action: Attachment of bank accounts of the defaulter

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement/recovery action under existing SEBI recovery and attachment powers.

## Compliance Requirements

- Banks and financial institutions holding accounts of Money Mishra Financial Services (PAN: AAZFM1357R) are required to comply with the attachment notice and restrict debit transactions from the attached accounts as directed.
- The defaulter is prohibited from operating or disposing of funds in the attached bank accounts.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: May 26, 2026
- Recovery Certificate No. 9073 of 2026 (date of original certificate not specified in title)

## Impact Assessment

This enforcement action has limited broader market impact as it targets a specific defaulter entity. It reinforces SEBI's ongoing efforts to enforce recovery against entities involved in unregistered portfolio management services. Investors who may have engaged with Money Mishra Financial Services or Allied Financial Services Private Limited for portfolio management should be aware of the regulatory action and associated risks of dealing with unregistered intermediaries.
