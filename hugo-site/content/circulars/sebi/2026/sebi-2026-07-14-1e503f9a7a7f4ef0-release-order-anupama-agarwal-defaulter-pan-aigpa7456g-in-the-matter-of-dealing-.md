## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order under Certificate No. 9121 of 2026 concerning Anupama Agarwal (PAN: AIGPA7456G), who had been declared a defaulter in connection with a matter involving dealing in illiquid stock options on BSE. The order formally releases the individual from defaulter status, typically issued after settlement of dues, expiry of a debarment period, or fulfillment of conditions previously imposed by the regulator/exchange.

## Key Points

- The order pertains to Anupama Agarwal, identified by PAN AIGPA7456G.
- The underlying matter relates to dealing in illiquid stock options on the BSE platform.
- The release is recorded under Certificate No. 9121 of 2026.
- This is an individual-level regulatory/enforcement action rather than a market-wide directive.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced by this order. It is a case-specific release action closing out defaulter status for the named individual.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance obligations arise for market participants, listed companies, or intermediaries as a result of this order. The action is limited to the individual named in the certificate.

## Important Dates

Order dated 2026-07-14 (based on document issuance date); specific effective or hearing dates are not detailed in the available circular metadata.

## Impact Assessment

This release order has minimal market-wide impact. It is relevant primarily for regulatory record-keeping and for market surveillance teams tracking the status of individuals previously flagged for irregular trading practices in illiquid stock options.
