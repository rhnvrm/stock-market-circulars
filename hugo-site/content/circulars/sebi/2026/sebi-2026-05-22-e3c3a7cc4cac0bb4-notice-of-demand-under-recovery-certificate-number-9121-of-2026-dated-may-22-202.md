## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand under Recovery Certificate No. 9121 of 2026, dated May 22, 2026, against Anupama Agarwal (PAN: AIGPA7456G), who has been identified as a defaulter in connection with dealing in illiquid stock options on BSE. The notice demands recovery of outstanding dues as determined by SEBI's enforcement proceedings.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 9121 of 2026 issued by SEBI dated May 22, 2026
- Demand notice served on Anupama Agarwal (PAN: AIGPA7456G) as a defaulter
- Matter relates to dealing in illiquid stock options on BSE
- Notice initiates formal recovery proceedings against the named individual

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement and recovery action under existing SEBI regulations and powers to recover dues from defaulters via recovery certificates.

## Compliance Requirements

- Anupama Agarwal is required to pay the demanded amount as specified in the recovery certificate
- Failure to comply may result in further coercive recovery measures by SEBI's Recovery Officer
- The defaulter must respond to the notice within the stipulated timeframe mentioned in the demand

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate Date: May 22, 2026
- Notice of Demand Date: May 22, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This action has limited broader market impact as it pertains to an individual defaulter. It reinforces SEBI's ongoing crackdown on manipulation and unauthorized trading in illiquid stock options on BSE, signaling continued regulatory vigilance in this segment. No systemic or market-wide implications are expected.
