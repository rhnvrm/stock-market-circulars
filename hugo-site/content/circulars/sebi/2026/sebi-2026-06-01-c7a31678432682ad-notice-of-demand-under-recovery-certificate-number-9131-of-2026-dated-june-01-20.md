## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand under Recovery Certificate No. 9131 of 2026, dated June 01, 2026, against Mr. Amit Kumar Singhl (PAN: GJOPS0881L) in connection with the matter of Blue Coast Hotels Limited. The notice demands recovery of dues as determined by SEBI through the applicable legal process.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 9131 of 2026 issued against Mr. Amit Kumar Singhl (PAN: GJOPS0881L)
- Mr. Amit Kumar Singhl is identified as a defaulter in the matter of Blue Coast Hotels Limited
- Notice of Demand dated June 01, 2026
- Recovery proceedings initiated by SEBI under its enforcement powers

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an enforcement action under SEBI's existing recovery and demand notice framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Mr. Amit Kumar Singhl is required to satisfy the demand raised under Recovery Certificate No. 9131 of 2026
- Failure to comply may result in further enforcement measures including attachment of assets

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate Date: June 01, 2026
- Notice of Demand Date: June 01, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a targeted enforcement action with no direct impact on broader market participants or trading operations. The matter concerns an individual defaulter (Mr. Amit Kumar Singhl) in the context of Blue Coast Hotels Limited. Investors in Blue Coast Hotels Limited should note this as a regulatory enforcement proceeding related to the company.
