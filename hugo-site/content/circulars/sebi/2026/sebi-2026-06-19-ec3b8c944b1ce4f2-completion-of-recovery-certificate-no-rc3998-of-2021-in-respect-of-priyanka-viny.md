## Summary

SEBI has announced the completion of Recovery Certificate no. RC3998 of 2021 issued against Priyanka Vinyas Trust (PAN: AABTP1562B) in the matter of Unisys Software and Holding Industries Limited. The completion indicates that the recovery proceedings under this certificate have been concluded, either through realisation of dues or other resolution.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC3998 of 2021 has been marked as completed by SEBI.
- The certificate was issued against Priyanka Vinyas Trust (PAN: AABTP1562B).
- The underlying enforcement matter pertains to Unisys Software and Holding Industries Limited.
- Completion of a recovery certificate typically signifies full or agreed recovery of SEBI-imposed dues, penalties, or disgorgement amounts.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This notice pertains solely to the closure of an existing enforcement/recovery proceeding.

## Compliance Requirements

No ongoing compliance requirements are imposed on market participants by this notice. The recovery obligation on Priyanka Vinyas Trust under RC3998/2021 is deemed fulfilled upon completion.

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate issued: 2021 (RC3998)
- Completion notified: June 19, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This notification has minimal market impact. It is an administrative closure of a legacy enforcement matter. Investors and market participants in Unisys Software and Holding Industries Limited should note that SEBI's recovery proceedings against the named trust in this matter are now concluded. There is no broader trading or operational impact on the market.
