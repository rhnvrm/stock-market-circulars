## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC9045 of 2026 in Adjudication Proceedings No. 15398 of 2026 against Subhranshu Roy HUF (PAN: AAXHS8157D) in connection with the matter of trading in illiquid stock options. The recovery certificate authorizes enforcement of the penalty amount determined through adjudication proceedings.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9045 of 2026 issued against Subhranshu Roy HUF
- PAN of the defaulter: AAXHS8157D
- Adjudication Proceedings (A.P.) No. 15398 of 2026
- Matter relates to trading in illiquid stock options
- Certificate enables SEBI to recover dues as arrears of land revenue

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This document enforces an existing adjudication order under the SEBI Act, 1992 and the SEBI (Procedure for Holding Inquiry and Imposing Penalties) Rules, 1995.

## Compliance Requirements

- Subhranshu Roy HUF is required to pay the penalty amount as specified in the adjudication order
- Non-payment of dues subjects the defaulter to recovery proceedings as arrears of land revenue under Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992
- The designated authority is empowered to attach and sell the defaulter's movable and immovable properties to recover the dues

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate issued: June 2026
- Payment of dues was required prior to issuance of this certificate

## Impact Assessment

This recovery certificate has limited broader market impact and is specific to the named party, Subhranshu Roy HUF. It is part of SEBI's ongoing enforcement actions against entities found to have engaged in manipulative trading in illiquid stock options — a segment that has seen sustained regulatory scrutiny. The action reinforces SEBI's commitment to deterring manipulation in the options market segment.
