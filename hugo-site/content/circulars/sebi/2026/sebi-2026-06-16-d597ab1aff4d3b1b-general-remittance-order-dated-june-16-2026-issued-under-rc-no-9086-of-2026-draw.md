## Summary

SEBI's Recovery and Refund Department (Recovery Division I) has issued a General Remittance Order dated June 16, 2026 under Recovery Certificate No. 9086 of 2026 against Ravi Ludhiyani HUF (PAN: AAQHR9636F). The order directs all banks and mutual funds in India to remit attached funds held in the defaulter's accounts to SEBI, in connection with violations related to dealing in illiquid stock options at BSE.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 9086 of 2026 was originally issued on April 16, 2026 against Ravi Ludhiyani HUF for total dues of Rs. 5,16,000 (Five Lakh Sixteen Thousand)
- Outstanding dues as on date of this order have increased to Rs. 5,26,000 (Five Lakh Twenty-Six Thousand) due to accrued interest and charges
- Attachment proceedings (Nos. 15348 and 15349 of 2026) cover bank/demat accounts and mutual fund folios of the defaulter
- Notice of Demand was sent on April 16, 2026; Notices of Attachment of Bank Accounts were issued on May 7, 2026
- Banks and mutual funds are directed to remit funds via EFT/NEFT/RTGS to SEBI's designated ICICI Bank account (A/c No. SEBIRRDPEN9086, IFS Code: ICIC0000106)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an enforcement action under existing powers granted by Sections 28A and 28B of the SEBI Act, 1992 (as amended by the Securities Laws (Amendment) Act, 2014), read with Sections 220–227, 228A, 229, 232 and the Second and Third Schedules to the Income Tax Act, 1961, and the Income Tax (Certificate Proceedings) Rules, 1962.

## Compliance Requirements

- All banks and mutual funds holding accounts or folios in the name of Ravi Ludhiyani HUF (PAN: AAQHR9636F) must remit attached amounts forthwith
- Remittance must be made via EFT/NEFT/RTGS to A/c No. SEBIRRDPEN9086 at ICICI Bank (IFS Code: ICIC0000106)
- Post-remittance, institutions must intimate details (case name, payee name, date, amount, transaction number, bank details) to ashokkj@sebi.gov.in and surendrak@sebi.gov.in
- If the defaulter holds no account or folio with a receiving institution, no response to SEBI is required

## Important Dates

- April 16, 2026: Recovery Certificate No. 9086 of 2026 issued; Notice of Demand sent to defaulter
- May 7, 2026: Notices of Attachment of Bank Accounts issued to financial institutions
- June 16, 2026: General Remittance Order issued directing immediate remittance of Rs. 5,26,000

## Impact Assessment

This order has no broad market impact. It is a targeted enforcement and recovery action against a single HUF entity (Ravi Ludhiyani HUF, Indore) for illicit dealings in illiquid stock options at BSE. The financial obligation is limited to Rs. 5,26,000. Banks and mutual funds with no exposure to this entity are unaffected. The action reaffirms SEBI's ongoing enforcement posture against manipulation in illiquid derivatives segments.
