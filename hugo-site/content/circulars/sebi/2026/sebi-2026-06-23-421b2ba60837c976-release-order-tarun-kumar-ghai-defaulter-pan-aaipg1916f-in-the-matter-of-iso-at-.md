## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order (Certificate No. 9100 of 2026) in the matter of Tarun Kumar Ghai (PAN: AAIPG1916F), who was declared a defaulter in connection with Individual Stock Options (ISO) trading at BSE. The release order formally discharges the individual from defaulter obligations under applicable SEBI/exchange regulations.

## Key Points

- Release Order Certificate No. 9100 of 2026 issued by SEBI.
- Pertains to Tarun Kumar Ghai, PAN: AAIPG1916F, classified as a defaulter.
- Matter relates to Individual Stock Options (ISO) at BSE.
- The release order signifies formal closure or discharge of the defaulter's outstanding obligations.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is an administrative order issuing a release certificate to a previously declared defaulter.

## Compliance Requirements

Upon issuance of the release order, the defaulter (Tarun Kumar Ghai) is discharged from obligations arising from the defaulter declaration. BSE and relevant counterparties are informed of the release status.

## Important Dates

- Date of Release Order: June 23, 2026
- Certificate No.: 9100 of 2026

## Impact Assessment

This order has no material impact on the broader market or other market participants. It is a routine administrative action concluding the defaulter proceedings against an individual in the ISO segment at BSE.
