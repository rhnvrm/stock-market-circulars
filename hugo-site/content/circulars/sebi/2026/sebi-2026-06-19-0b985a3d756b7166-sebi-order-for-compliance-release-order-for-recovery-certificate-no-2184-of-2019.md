## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order for Recovery Certificate No. 2184 of 2019 against Ajitbhai P. Vasa in the matter of Exelon Infrastructure Ltd. The release order signifies that the outstanding dues under the recovery certificate have been paid or otherwise satisfied, and the certificate is accordingly being released.

## Key Points

- Release Order issued for Recovery Certificate No. 2184 of 2019
- The order pertains to Ajitbhai P. Vasa as the noticee/defaulter
- Underlying matter relates to Exelon Infrastructure Ltd.
- Release indicates compliance with or satisfaction of the recovery demand
- Recovery certificates are issued by SEBI to recover penalties and dues from defaulters

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced by this order. This is an administrative/enforcement action indicating closure of a specific recovery proceeding.

## Compliance Requirements

- The noticee, Ajitbhai P. Vasa, is required to have fulfilled the payment obligations under Recovery Certificate No. 2184 of 2019
- Upon satisfaction of dues, the recovery certificate is released and enforcement proceedings under it are closed

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate No. 2184 originally issued in 2019
- Release Order issued: June 2026

## Impact Assessment

This order has minimal market impact. It is an administrative enforcement closure order specific to an individual (Ajitbhai P. Vasa) in the matter of Exelon Infrastructure Ltd. It signals successful recovery of dues by SEBI and conclusion of the enforcement action under this particular recovery certificate.
