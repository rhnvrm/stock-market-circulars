## Summary

SEBI's Recovery Officer has issued a Release Order concerning Recovery Certificate No. RC9164 of 2026, which was issued against Adhunik Ventures Private Limited (PAN: AAHCA7165L) in connection with SEBI's enforcement action in the Illiquid Stock Options matter. A release order of this nature is typically issued when recovery proceedings under a Recovery Certificate come to a close, such as upon satisfaction of the outstanding dues or resolution of the recovery action, resulting in release of any attachment or other recovery measures against the entity.

## Key Points

- The order pertains to Recovery Certificate No. RC9164 of 2026, issued against Adhunik Ventures Private Limited.
- The entity's PAN is AAHCA7165L.
- The underlying matter relates to SEBI's enforcement proceedings on illiquid stock options trading.
- A release order signals closure or withdrawal of the specific recovery action associated with this certificate.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced; this is an individual enforcement/recovery action order under SEBI's existing framework for recovery of dues (typically under the Second Schedule to the Income-tax Act, 1961, as applied to SEBI recovery proceedings).

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance obligations arise for the broader market. Adhunik Ventures Private Limited's specific recovery matter under RC9164 of 2026 is addressed by this order; no other entities are directly affected.
