## Summary

SEBI's Recovery Officer has issued a Notice of Demand (Certificate No. 9191 of 2026) under Rule 2 of the Second Schedule to the Income-tax Act, 1961 read with Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992, against Dhaval Girishbhai Parmar (PAN: EPZPP2397F). The notice demands payment of Rs. 3,61,684.70, arising from a disgorgement order passed against the entity in connection with trading activities of certain entities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited).

## Key Points

- Certificate No. 9191 of 2026 issued against Dhaval Girishbhai Parmar (EPZPP2397F), address: 70 Sindhvainagar Society, Near CTM Cross Road, Amraiwadi, Ahmedabad, Gujarat - 380026.
- Total demand of Rs. 3,61,684.70, comprising:
  - Disgorgement amount: Rs. 3,37,088.50 (per Order No. QJA/MN/IVD/ID7/31947/2025-26 dated 31.12.2025)
  - Interest (January 2026 to July 2026 @ 12% p.a.): Rs. 23,596.20
  - Recovery cost: Rs. 1,000
- Underlying order relates to trading activities of certain entities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited).
- Payment must be made within 15 days of receipt via EFT/NEFT/RTGS to A/c No. SEBIRRDDSG9191, Bank of India, IFSC BKID00VAN04, or through SEBI's online recovery payment portal.
- Non-payment empowers SEBI's Recovery Officer to pursue recovery via attachment/sale of movable or immovable property, attachment of bank accounts, arrest and detention, or appointment of a receiver.
- Any transfer of property or funds to spouse, minor child, son's wife, or son's minor child on or after December 31, 2025 (other than for adequate consideration) is deemed to be the entity's property for recovery purposes.
- Upon service of the notice, the entity is barred from mortgaging, charging, leasing, or otherwise dealing with property without permission of the Recovery Officer; such transfers are void.
- Payment confirmations must be sent to the Recovery Officer at SEBI Bhavan-II or emailed to akashb@sebi.gov.in and recoveryho1@sebi.gov.in.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced; this is an enforcement/recovery action implementing a pre-existing disgorgement order under Section 28A of the SEBI Act read with the Income-tax Act recovery provisions.

## Compliance Requirements

The named entity, Dhaval Girishbhai Parmar, must remit the full demanded amount of Rs. 3,61,684.70 within 15 days of receiving the notice and must refrain from transferring or encumbering property without the Recovery Officer's permission.

## Important Dates

- Underlying disgorgement order dated: 31.12.2025
- Interest computed from: January 2026 to July 2026
- Notice of Demand dated: 02.07.2026
- Payment deadline: 15 days from receipt of the notice

## Impact Assessment

This notice has no direct market-wide impact and does not affect trading, listing, or operations of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited) itself. It is a routine enforcement/recovery action against an individual found liable for disgorgement in a past trading-manipulation matter, relevant primarily for compliance and enforcement tracking purposes.
