## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order pertaining to Recovery Certificate No. RC3783 of 2021 in the matter of Blue Haven Agro Industries Limited. This order relates to recovery proceedings initiated by SEBI against the company.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate Number: RC3783 of 2021
- Entity involved: Blue Haven Agro Industries Limited
- Order type: Release Order
- Issuing authority: Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced through this order. This is an enforcement/recovery action specific to Blue Haven Agro Industries Limited.

## Compliance Requirements

This release order indicates satisfaction or partial satisfaction of recovery proceedings. Specific compliance requirements would be detailed in the original recovery certificate and associated orders.

## Important Dates

- Release Order Date: January 14, 2026
- Recovery Certificate Date: 2021 (RC3783 of 2021)

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to Blue Haven Agro Industries Limited and parties involved in the recovery proceedings. This release order suggests resolution or progress in the recovery matter. Market-wide impact is minimal as this relates to specific enforcement action against one entity.
