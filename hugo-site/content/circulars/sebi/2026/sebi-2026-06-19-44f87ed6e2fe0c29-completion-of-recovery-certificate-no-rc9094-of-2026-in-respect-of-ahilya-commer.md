## Summary

SEBI has announced the completion of Recovery Certificate No. RC9094 of 2026 issued against Ahilya Commercial Pvt. Ltd. (PAN: AACCA2226R) in connection with the matter of Parbati Holdings Ltd. This notice signifies that the recovery proceedings initiated by SEBI have been concluded.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9094 of 2026 has been marked as completed.
- The certificate was issued against Ahilya Commercial Pvt. Ltd. (PAN: AACCA2226R).
- The recovery pertains to the matter of Parbati Holdings Ltd.
- Completion indicates that dues/penalties owed to SEBI have been recovered or the proceedings have otherwise concluded.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is a closure notice for an existing enforcement/recovery proceeding.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance obligations arise from this notice. It records the conclusion of recovery action against Ahilya Commercial Pvt. Ltd. in connection with Parbati Holdings Ltd.

## Important Dates

- Date of notice: 19 June 2026
- Recovery Certificate: RC9094 of 2026

## Impact Assessment

This notice has no direct impact on listed securities or broader market operations. It is an administrative enforcement update specific to Ahilya Commercial Pvt. Ltd. and the Parbati Holdings Ltd. matter, with no implications for general market participants.
