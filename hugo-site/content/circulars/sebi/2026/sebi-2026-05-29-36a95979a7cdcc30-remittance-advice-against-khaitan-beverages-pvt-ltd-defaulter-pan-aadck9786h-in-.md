## Summary

SEBI has issued a Remittance Advice against Khaitan Beverages Pvt Ltd (PAN: AADCK9786H), identified as a defaulter in the matter of ISO at BSE. The remittance advice pertains to recovery of dues under Recovery Certificate No. 8070 of 2024.

## Key Points

- Defaulter: Khaitan Beverages Pvt Ltd
- PAN: AADCK9786H
- Recovery Certificate No.: 8070 of 2024
- Exchange/Matter: ISO at BSE
- Document type: Remittance Advice issued by SEBI for enforcement of recovery

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement/recovery action under existing SEBI recovery mechanisms.

## Compliance Requirements

The defaulter, Khaitan Beverages Pvt Ltd, is required to comply with the remittance directives outlined in this notice. Banks, depositories, and other intermediaries holding assets of the defaulter may be directed to remit funds in accordance with the recovery certificate.

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate No. 8070 issued: 2024
- Remittance Advice issued: May 2026

## Impact Assessment

This action is targeted solely at Khaitan Beverages Pvt Ltd and has no broader market impact. It reflects SEBI's ongoing enforcement of recovery proceedings against entities that have defaulted on dues related to securities market obligations at BSE. General investors and market participants are not directly affected.
