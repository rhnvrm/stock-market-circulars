## Summary

SEBI has initiated Attachment Proceedings (A.P. No. 15369 of 2026) under Recovery Certificate No. RC8054 of 2024 against Vedant Commodeal Pvt Ltd (PAN: AABCL9683L) in connection with the matter of Worth Investment and Trading Private Limited. This is part of SEBI's ongoing enforcement actions to recover dues from defaulters.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No.: RC8054 of 2024
- Attachment Proceedings No.: A.P. No. 15369 of 2026
- Defaulter: Vedant Commodeal Pvt Ltd
- PAN: AABCL9683L
- Matter: Worth Investment and Trading Private Limited
- Action Type: Attachment Proceedings
- Issuing Authority: Securities and Exchange Board of India

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an enforcement/recovery action under SEBI's existing recovery proceedings framework.

## Compliance Requirements

Vedant Commodeal Pvt Ltd is required to comply with the attachment order. Banks, depositories, or other entities holding assets of the defaulter may be directed to freeze or transfer such assets toward satisfaction of the recovery certificate dues.

## Important Dates

- Notice/Order Date: May 19, 2026
- Recovery Certificate No. RC8054 issued: 2024
- Attachment Proceedings initiated: 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a targeted enforcement action against a specific corporate entity with no direct market-wide impact. It reflects SEBI's continued pursuit of dues recovery in the Worth Investment and Trading Private Limited matter. A companion attachment proceedings order (A.P. No. 15368 of 2026) under the same recovery certificate has also been issued simultaneously.
