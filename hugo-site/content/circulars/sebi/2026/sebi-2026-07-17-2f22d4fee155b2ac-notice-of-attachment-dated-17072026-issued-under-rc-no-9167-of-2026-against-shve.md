## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Attachment dated 17.07.2026, issued under Recovery Certificate (RC) No. 9167 of 2026, against Shvetalben Sagarbhai Dataniya (PAN: CQGPD5278K). The notice arises in connection with proceedings related to the trading activities of certain entities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now renamed DU Digital Global Limited).

## Key Points

- Notice of Attachment issued under RC No. 9167 of 2026.
- Notice dated 17.07.2026.
- Named party: Shvetalben Sagarbhai Dataniya, PAN: CQGPD5278K.
- Matter relates to trading activities of certain entities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited).
- Full text and specifics of the attached property/dues were not available for extraction from the source PDF (scanned/image-based document).

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an individual recovery/enforcement notice, not a policy or regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

The named individual (Shvetalben Sagarbhai Dataniya) is subject to attachment proceedings for recovery of dues pursuant to the underlying SEBI order/recovery certificate. Recipients dealing with this party should note the attachment status.

## Important Dates

- Notice date: 17.07.2026 (17 July 2026)

## Impact Assessment

This notice is part of SEBI's recovery enforcement process against an individual found liable in connection with irregular trading activity in the DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited) scrip. It does not have a direct operational or trading impact on the listed company or the broader market, but reflects continued enforcement action stemming from a prior SEBI order on trading activities in this scrip.
