## Summary

Dr. Sadanand S. Date took charge as Executive Director at the Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI), Mumbai on March 4, 2026. He will oversee the Investigations Department at SEBI.

## Key Points

- Dr. Sadanand Date assumed charge as Executive Director, SEBI on March 4, 2026
- He will head the Investigations Department at SEBI
- He is an IPS officer of the 2007 batch from Uttarakhand Cadre
- Prior to this appointment, he was on Central Deputation to the Central Bureau of Investigation (CBI)
- He served in various capacities at CBI Mumbai including SP ACB/BSFC and Head of Branch roles in Economic Offences, Special Crime, Special Task, and Anti-Corruption Branches

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a personnel/administrative announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance requirements arising from this circular.

## Important Dates

- **March 4, 2026**: Dr. Sadanand Date took charge as Executive Director, SEBI
- **March 13, 2026**: Date of press release (PR No. 17/2026)

## Impact Assessment

This announcement has no direct market or operational impact. The appointment of a seasoned IPS officer with extensive experience in economic offences and anti-corruption investigations to head SEBI's Investigations Department may signal continued focus on enforcement and investigation activities. Stakeholders interacting with SEBI's Investigations Department should be aware of the new leadership.
