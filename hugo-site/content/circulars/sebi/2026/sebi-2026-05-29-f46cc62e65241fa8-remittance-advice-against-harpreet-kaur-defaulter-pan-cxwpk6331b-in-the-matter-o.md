## Summary

SEBI has issued a Remittance Advice against Harpreet Kaur (PAN: CXWPK6331B) designated as a defaulter in the matter of dealings in Illiquid Stock Options at BSE. This action is taken under Recovery Certificate No. 8034 of 2024, directing recovery of outstanding dues from the defaulter.

## Key Points

- Defaulter: Harpreet Kaur, PAN: CXWPK6331B
- Recovery Certificate No.: 8034 of 2024
- Matter: Dealings in Illiquid Stock Options at BSE
- Document type: Remittance Advice — part of SEBI's recovery proceedings against the defaulter
- SEBI issues remittance advices to direct attachment and recovery of funds from defaulters

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an enforcement/recovery document under existing SEBI recovery mechanisms.

## Compliance Requirements

- The defaulter (Harpreet Kaur) is required to remit the dues as specified in the recovery certificate.
- Banks, depositories, or other entities served with this remittance advice must comply with the recovery directions as per SEBI's recovery proceedings framework.

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate No. 8034 issued in 2024
- Remittance Advice issued in May 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is an individual-level enforcement action with no broad market impact. It forms part of SEBI's ongoing efforts to recover dues from entities found to have engaged in manipulative dealings in the illiquid stock options segment at BSE. Impact is limited to the named defaulter and connected financial institutions handling the recovery.
