## Summary

SEBI has issued a notice confirming the completion of recovery proceedings under Recovery Certificate No. RC744 of 2015 against Ahilya Commercial Pvt. Ltd. (PAN: AACCA2226R) in the matter related to Sangotri Constructions Ltd. This represents the closure of enforcement recovery action initiated in 2015.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate Number: RC744 of 2015
- Defaulter Entity: Ahilya Commercial Pvt. Ltd.
- PAN of Defaulter: AACCA2226R
- Related Matter: Sangotri Constructions Ltd.
- Status: Recovery proceedings completed
- Notice Date: January 27, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an administrative notice regarding the completion of existing recovery proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

No ongoing compliance requirements. The completion notice indicates that recovery actions under this certificate have been concluded.

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate Issued: 2015
- Completion Notice Date: January 27, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal market impact as it relates to the administrative closure of a decade-old recovery proceeding against a specific private limited company. The completion indicates successful resolution or conclusion of the recovery process initiated under the original certificate. Market participants are not required to take any action in response to this notice.
