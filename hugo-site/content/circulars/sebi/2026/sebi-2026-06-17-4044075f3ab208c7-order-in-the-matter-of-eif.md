## Summary

SEBI has issued an order against EIF (ESG Impact Fund), registered as an Alternative Investment Fund (AIF) with registration number IN/AIF1/17-18/0326, for failing to file Quarterly Activity Reports (QARs) for all four quarters of FY2025-26 (March 2025, June 2025, September 2025, and December 2025). Summary proceedings have been initiated under Regulation 30A of SEBI (Intermediaries) Regulations, 2008 read with Section 12(3) of the SEBI Act, 1992, which may result in cancellation of the fund's AIF registration.

## Key Points

- EIF (ESG Impact Fund) failed to file QARs for four consecutive quarters: March 2025, June 2025, September 2025, and December 2025
- QARs are mandatory under Regulation 28 of AIF Regulations and SEBI Master Circular dated May 07, 2024, requiring submission within 15 calendar days from quarter-end via the SI Portal
- A Show Cause Notice was issued on April 27, 2026 via RPAD to the fund's Varanasi address and email (missioneif@gmail.com)
- The noticee responded via email dated May 15, 2026 and letter received May 21, 2026, citing persistent technical and system-related issues with the SI Portal as the reason for non-filing
- The fund claimed ongoing correspondence since December 2022 with SEBI officials and SI Portal Helpdesk regarding XML validation and upload errors

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This order enforces existing obligations under:
- Regulation 28 of SEBI (Alternative Investment Fund) Regulations, 2012
- Clause 15.1.1 of SEBI Master Circular dated May 07, 2024
- SEBI Circular SEBI/HO/IMD/DF1/CIR/P/2017/87 dated July 31, 2017 (mandating QAR filing on SI Portal)
- Section 12(3) of the SEBI Act, 1992
- Regulation 30A of SEBI (Intermediaries) Regulations, 2008

## Compliance Requirements

- Registered AIFs must file QARs on a quarterly basis within 15 calendar days from the end of each quarter
- QARs must be filed using the reporting format hosted by IVCA and Equalifi on the SI Portal
- Non-compliance exposes AIFs to summary proceedings and potential cancellation of registration
- AIFs experiencing technical issues with the SI Portal should proactively resolve them to avoid compliance lapses

## Important Dates

- **Missed QAR deadlines**: March 2025, June 2025, September 2025, and December 2025 quarters
- **Show Cause Notice issued**: April 27, 2026
- **Noticee's email response**: May 15, 2026
- **Noticee's letter received by SEBI**: May 21, 2026
- **Order date**: June 2026

## Impact Assessment

This order has limited broader market impact as it concerns a single AIF, EIF (ESG Impact Fund), based in Varanasi. However, it serves as a regulatory signal to all AIFs about the strict enforcement of QAR filing obligations. The fund's registration is at risk of cancellation under Section 12(3) of the SEBI Act. The noticee's defense of technical portal issues—if substantiated—may mitigate the penalty, but SEBI's initiation of summary proceedings indicates a low tolerance for prolonged non-compliance regardless of the reason. Other AIFs experiencing portal difficulties should ensure they document their issues and seek alternative resolution channels to avoid similar enforcement action.
