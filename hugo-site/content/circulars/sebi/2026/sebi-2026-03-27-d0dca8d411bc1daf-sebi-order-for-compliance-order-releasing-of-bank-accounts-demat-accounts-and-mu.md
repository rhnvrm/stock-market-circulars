## Summary

SEBI has issued a compliance order directing the release of bank accounts, demat accounts, and mutual fund folios belonging to Mr. Mahesh Somabhai Desai (PAN: AMGPD0183P) that were frozen as part of Recovery Certificate (RC) No. 6811 of 2023. This order follows the completion or satisfaction of the recovery proceedings initiated against the individual.

## Key Points

- SEBI has ordered the release of all frozen bank accounts, demat accounts, and mutual fund folios of Mr. Mahesh Somabhai Desai (PAN: AMGPD0183P)
- The order pertains to Recovery Certificate (RC) No. 6811 of 2023
- The release is consequent upon compliance with or satisfaction of the outstanding dues under the recovery proceedings
- All banks, depositories, and mutual funds holding frozen assets are directed to act on this order

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an individual-specific compliance order giving effect to the closure of recovery proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

- Banks holding frozen accounts of Mr. Mahesh Somabhai Desai are directed to unfreeze and release the accounts immediately upon receipt of this order
- Depositories (NSDL/CDSL) are directed to release the frozen demat accounts
- Mutual fund houses / registrars are directed to release the frozen mutual fund folios
- Concerned entities must act promptly and confirm compliance

## Important Dates

- Order Date: March 27, 2026
- RC No. 6811 of 2023 — original recovery proceedings
- Release effective from the date of this order

## Impact Assessment

This order has minimal broader market impact as it concerns a single individual's assets. It signals the conclusion of recovery proceedings against Mr. Mahesh Somabhai Desai and restores his access to financial accounts and investments. No impact on listed securities, market operations, or general investors.
