## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order for Recovery Certificate No. 8995 of 2026 against Sumit Kanungo and Riya Jain (Kanungo) in connection with front running activities involving various funds of Fidelity Group. This order indicates compliance or resolution of the recovery proceedings initiated against the named individuals.

## Key Points

- Release Order issued for Recovery Certificate No. 8995 of 2026
- Noticees: Sumit Kanungo and Riya Jain (Kanungo)
- Matter relates to front running involving Fidelity Group funds
- Order represents compliance or settlement in enforcement proceedings

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is an enforcement compliance order.

## Compliance Requirements

This is a release order indicating that recovery obligations have been satisfied or compliance has been achieved by the noticees.

## Important Dates

- Order Date: January 19, 2026
- Recovery Certificate No.: 8995 of 2026

Limited market impact. This order pertains to specific individuals involved in a front running case and represents the resolution or compliance stage of enforcement proceedings.
