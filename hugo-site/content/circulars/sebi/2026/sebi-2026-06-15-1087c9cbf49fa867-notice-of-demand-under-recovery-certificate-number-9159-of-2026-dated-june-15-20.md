## Summary

SEBI issued a Notice of Demand under Recovery Certificate No. 9159 of 2026 (dated June 15, 2026) to Sapna, the Proprietor of Stallion Trading Co. (PAN: DRUPS8079D), designated as the defaulter. The notice pertains to trading activities of certain entities in the scrip of Delta Leasing and Finance Ltd.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 9159 of 2026 issued by SEBI on June 15, 2026.
- Defaulter: Sapna, Proprietor of Stallion Trading Co. (PAN: DRUPS8079D).
- The matter relates to alleged irregular or manipulative trading activities by certain entities in the scrip of Delta Leasing and Finance Ltd.
- SEBI has initiated recovery proceedings against the defaulter under applicable provisions.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This document is an enforcement/recovery action under existing SEBI regulations governing recovery of dues from defaulters.

## Compliance Requirements

- The defaulter (Sapna / Stallion Trading Co.) is required to comply with the demand raised under the recovery certificate.
- Payment of the demanded amount or response to the notice must be made within the timeline specified in the certificate/notice.
- Non-compliance may result in further enforcement actions including attachment of assets.

## Important Dates

- **Recovery Certificate Date:** June 15, 2026
- **Recovery Certificate No.:** 9159 of 2026

## Impact Assessment

This notice has limited broad market impact as it is directed at a specific individual and proprietorship firm. It reflects SEBI's ongoing enforcement efforts against entities found to have engaged in manipulative or irregular trading in the scrip of Delta Leasing and Finance Ltd. Market participants are not directly affected unless they are the named defaulter or associated entities.
