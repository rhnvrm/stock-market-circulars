## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Attachment dated July 22, 2026, under Recovery Certificate (RC) No. 9180 of 2026, against Mr. Sanjay Savjibhai Parmar. The notice arises from recovery proceedings connected to an earlier SEBI action concerning trading activities of certain entities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now renamed DU Digital Global Limited). The Recovery Officer has invoked attachment proceedings to recover outstanding dues from the defaulter, who has apparently failed to pay amounts due under a prior SEBI order (such as a penalty, disgorgement, or other monetary direction) despite the issuance of a Recovery Certificate.

## Key Points

- Notice of Attachment issued on July 22, 2026, against Sanjay Savjibhai Parmar.
- Action is taken under Recovery Certificate No. 9180 of 2026.
- Underlying matter relates to trading activities of certain entities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited).
- The notice is a recovery/enforcement mechanism used when a defaulter fails to pay amounts due under a SEBI order within the stipulated time.
- Such notices are typically issued under the procedure prescribed in the Second Schedule to the Income Tax Act, 1961, as applied to recovery of SEBI dues under Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992.

## Regulatory Changes

This is not a policy or regulatory change; it is an individual enforcement action (recovery proceeding) taken against a specific defaulter pursuant to a Recovery Certificate issued earlier by SEBI's Recovery Officer.

## Compliance Requirements

- The named defaulter, Sanjay Savjibhai Parmar, is required to clear outstanding dues under the underlying SEBI order to avoid further coercive recovery action, which may include attachment and sale of movable/immovable property, bank account attachment, or other measures available under the recovery process.
- Market intermediaries and banks that may hold assets or accounts belonging to the named defaulter should take note of the attachment notice for compliance with any restraint on dealing with such assets.

## Important Dates

- Notice of Attachment date: July 22, 2026.
- Underlying Recovery Certificate: RC No. 9180 of 2026 (date of original certificate not specified in available extract).

## Impact Assessment

The direct market impact is minimal, as this action pertains to an individual defaulter rather than the listed company itself. However, it signals continued SEBI enforcement of recovery proceedings connected to past irregular trading activity in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited), reinforcing SEBI's ongoing scrutiny of trading conduct in this scrip and underscoring compliance risk for individuals found to have violated market regulations.
