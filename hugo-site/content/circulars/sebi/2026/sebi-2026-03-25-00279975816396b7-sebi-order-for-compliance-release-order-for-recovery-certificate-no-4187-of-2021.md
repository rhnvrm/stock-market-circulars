## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order for Recovery Certificate (RC) No. 4187 of 2021 against Ramilaben S Patel in the matter of Illiquid Stock Options at BSE. This Release Order signifies that Ramilaben S Patel has complied with the dues/penalty amount specified in the recovery certificate, and accordingly, any attachment or freeze placed on the noticee's assets or accounts is being released. This order is issued in conjunction with a Completion Order for the same recovery certificate, marking the full closure of the recovery proceedings against this individual.

## Key Points

- Release Order issued for Recovery Certificate No. 4187 of 2021 against Ramilaben S Patel
- The matter pertains to SEBI's enforcement action against illiquid stock options trading at BSE
- The release order indicates the noticee has paid the penalty/dues stipulated under the recovery certificate
- Any attachment of assets, bank accounts, or securities imposed under the recovery proceedings is hereby released
- A companion Completion Order (circular ID: c456da4e261c01a4) has also been issued for the same recovery certificate, confirming full closure
- The recovery certificate was originally issued in 2021 as part of SEBI's broader crackdown on manipulation of illiquid stock options at BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced by this order. This is an administrative closure order under SEBI's recovery proceedings framework, confirming satisfaction of an existing monetary liability.

## Compliance Requirements

- No further compliance obligations remain for Ramilaben S Patel with respect to RC No. 4187 of 2021
- All attached assets or frozen accounts of the noticee are to be released by the concerned banks, depositories, or other entities that had received attachment orders
- Entities holding attachments under this recovery certificate must act on the release order promptly

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate No. 4187 issued: 2021
- Release Order issued: March 25, 2026
- Compliance completion confirmed: March 25, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This order has minimal market impact as it is specific to an individual noticee (Ramilaben S Patel) in an already concluded enforcement matter. The broader SEBI action on illiquid stock options at BSE involved numerous entities and has been an ongoing enforcement exercise since 2018–2019. The issuance of this release order, along with a companion completion order, confirms that this particular respondent has settled their dues with SEBI. There is no systemic risk, no new trading restriction, and no impact on listed securities or broader market participants.
