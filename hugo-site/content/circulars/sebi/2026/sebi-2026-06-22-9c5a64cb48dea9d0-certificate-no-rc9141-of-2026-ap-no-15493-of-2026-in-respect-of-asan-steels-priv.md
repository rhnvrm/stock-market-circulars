## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC9141 of 2026 under Attachment Proceedings (A.P.) No. 15493 of 2026 against Asan Steels Private Limited (PAN: AAHCA8637P) for recovery of monetary penalty imposed in the matter of illiquid stock options on BSE.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9141 of 2026 issued by SEBI
- Attachment Proceedings No. A.P. 15493 of 2026
- Entity: Asan Steels Private Limited (PAN: AAHCA8637P)
- Matter relates to trading in illiquid stock options on BSE
- Certificate authorises recovery of outstanding penalty dues

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an enforcement/recovery action under existing SEBI penalty recovery mechanisms.

## Compliance Requirements

- Asan Steels Private Limited is required to pay the outstanding penalty amount as specified in the Recovery Certificate
- Non-compliance may result in attachment of assets and further enforcement action by SEBI

## Important Dates

- Date of issuance: June 2026
- Payment/compliance deadline: As specified in the Recovery Certificate (refer to the original PDF for exact dates)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal broader market impact as it is a targeted enforcement and penalty recovery action against a single entity, Asan Steels Private Limited, for past violations related to illiquid stock options trading on BSE. It reinforces SEBI's continued enforcement drive against entities involved in manipulative or non-compliant trading in illiquid derivatives segments.
