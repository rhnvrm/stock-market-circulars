## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand dated June 29, 2026, under Recovery Certificate (RC) No. 9174 of 2026, drawn against Amit Kumar Agarwal HUF. The notice pertains to violations arising from dealings in illiquid stock options on BSE, which SEBI has been actively penalizing as part of its enforcement actions against manipulative and fraudulent trading in illiquid options segments.

## Key Points

- Notice of Demand issued by SEBI under RC No. 9174 of 2026
- Drawn against: Amit Kumar Agarwal HUF
- Violation: Dealing in illiquid stock options on BSE
- Date of issuance: June 29, 2026
- This is an enforcement/recovery action requiring payment of dues determined by SEBI

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This circular represents an enforcement action under existing SEBI regulations governing securities market conduct and recovery of penalties from entities found to have engaged in illiquid stock options trading.

## Compliance Requirements

- The named entity (Amit Kumar Agarwal HUF) is required to comply with the demand and pay the specified amount as per the notice
- Non-compliance may result in further enforcement action including attachment of bank accounts and demat accounts
- The entity must respond to the notice within the stipulated timeframe mentioned in the demand

## Important Dates

- Notice of Demand Date: June 29, 2026
- RC No. 9174 of 2026 issued under applicable recovery proceedings
- Compliance deadline: As specified in the demand notice issued to Amit Kumar Agarwal HUF

## Impact Assessment

This notice has a limited and targeted impact, affecting only the named entity (Amit Kumar Agarwal HUF). It is part of SEBI's broader crackdown on illiquid stock options trading on BSE, which has seen numerous such recovery certificates issued against various individuals and entities. There is no systemic market impact; however, it signals continued regulatory vigilance in enforcing penalties for past violations in the illiquid options segment.
