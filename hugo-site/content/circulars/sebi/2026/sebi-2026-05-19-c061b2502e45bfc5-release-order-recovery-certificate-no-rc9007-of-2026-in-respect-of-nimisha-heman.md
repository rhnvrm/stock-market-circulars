## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order for Recovery Certificate No. RC9007 of 2026 in respect of Nimisha Hemani (PAN: ABFPH7961K) in the matter of illiquid stock options. A release order under SEBI's recovery proceedings indicates that the attached property or securities of the noticee are being released, typically after satisfaction of the dues or as directed by a competent authority.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9007 of 2026 issued against Nimisha Hemani (PAN: ABFPH7961K)
- The release order pertains to the illiquid stock options matter — an enforcement action where entities were found to have engaged in manipulative trading in illiquid stock options on BSE
- Release orders are issued when the recovery dues have been satisfied or when directed by an appellate/court order
- The order is part of SEBI's ongoing enforcement and recovery proceedings against entities found guilty in the illiquid stock options manipulation cases

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an administrative enforcement order concluding or partially resolving the recovery proceedings against a specific individual.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements for market participants at large. This order applies solely to the named individual, Nimisha Hemani (PAN: ABFPH7961K).

## Important Dates

- Order Date: May 19, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to the named individual. This order has no broader market impact. It is part of SEBI's wider enforcement drive against participants who traded in illiquid stock options on BSE, which involved widespread alleged manipulation. The release of attached property signals resolution of the recovery dues in this specific case.
