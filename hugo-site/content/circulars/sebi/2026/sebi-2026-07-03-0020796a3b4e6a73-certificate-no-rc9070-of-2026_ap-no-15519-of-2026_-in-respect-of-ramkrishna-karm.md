## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC9070 of 2026 (A.P. No. 15519 of 2026) against Ramkrishna Karmakar (PAN: BYMPK3820M) for failure to pay a penalty previously imposed by SEBI in an adjudication order relating to dealing in illiquid stock options on the BSE. As the penalty amount remains unpaid despite the demand, SEBI has referred the matter to the Recovery Officer to initiate recovery proceedings under the provisions of the Income Tax Act, 1961, as made applicable to the recovery of amounts due under Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992.

## Key Points

- The noticee, Ramkrishna Karmakar (PAN: BYMPK3820M), was found to have contravened SEBI regulations in connection with trading in illiquid stock options on the BSE.
- A monetary penalty was imposed on the noticee pursuant to an earlier SEBI adjudication/enforcement order.
- The penalty amount, along with applicable interest, has not been paid within the stipulated period.
- SEBI has therefore issued a formal notice of demand/recovery certificate (RC9070 of 2026) to initiate recovery proceedings against the noticee.

## Regulatory Changes

This is an individual enforcement action and does not introduce any new regulatory change, rule, or framework. It represents the exercise of SEBI's existing recovery powers under Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992, read with the Income Tax Act, 1961 (as applicable to recovery of sums due to SEBI).

## Compliance Requirements

- The noticee is required to pay the outstanding penalty amount, together with interest accrued, to SEBI within the period specified in the notice of demand.
- Failure to comply may result in coercive recovery measures being taken by the Recovery Officer, including attachment and sale of movable and immovable property, and other measures available under the Income Tax Act recovery mechanism.
- This circular does not impose any compliance obligation on market intermediaries, listed companies, or the general investing public.

## Important Dates

- Certificate/notice date: 2026-07-03
- Specific payment deadline: As specified in the notice of demand (not detailed in the extracted content); recipients should refer to the source PDF for the exact date.

## Impact Assessment

This circular pertains solely to an individual recovery proceeding initiated by SEBI against a specific noticee for non-payment of a penalty related to illiquid stock options trading. It has no direct market-wide, trading, or listing impact, and does not concern any specific listed stock or security. Its significance is limited to enforcement and compliance tracking, serving as a public record of SEBI's ongoing efforts to recover unpaid penalties from violators of securities market regulations.
