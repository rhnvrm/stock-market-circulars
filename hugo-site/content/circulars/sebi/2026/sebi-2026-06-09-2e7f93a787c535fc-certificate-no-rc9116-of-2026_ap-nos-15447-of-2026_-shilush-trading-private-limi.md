## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC9116 of 2026 under Application/Petition No. 15447 of 2026 against Shilush Trading Private Limited (PAN: AALCS0075M) in connection with the matter of illiquid stock options. This document formalises SEBI's recovery proceedings to collect dues owed by the entity pursuant to prior enforcement orders.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9116 of 2026 issued against Shilush Trading Private Limited (PAN: AALCS0075M)
- Application/Petition No. 15447 of 2026
- Proceedings relate to the illiquid stock options matter — a long-running SEBI enforcement action targeting entities that misused BSE illiquid stock options to generate artificial tax losses
- The recovery certificate is a formal instrument enabling SEBI to recover penalty/disgorgement amounts as arrears of land revenue

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This document is an enforcement/recovery instrument issued under existing SEBI (Recovery of Amount) Rules.

## Compliance Requirements

- Shilush Trading Private Limited is required to pay the dues specified in the recovery certificate
- Failure to comply may result in further coercive recovery action including attachment of assets and bank accounts
- The named entity must settle outstanding dues to avoid escalated enforcement measures

## Important Dates

- Certificate issued: June 2026 (exact date per document)
- A.P. No. 15447 of 2026 filed in 2026

## Impact Assessment

This order is entity-specific and directly impacts Shilush Trading Private Limited. It has no direct broader market impact. It is part of SEBI's sustained enforcement campaign against entities implicated in the illiquid stock options manipulation scheme on BSE, which involved hundreds of entities using artificial options trades to generate fictitious book losses for tax evasion purposes. The recovery proceedings demonstrate SEBI's continued follow-through on penalty collection from adjudication orders in this matter.
