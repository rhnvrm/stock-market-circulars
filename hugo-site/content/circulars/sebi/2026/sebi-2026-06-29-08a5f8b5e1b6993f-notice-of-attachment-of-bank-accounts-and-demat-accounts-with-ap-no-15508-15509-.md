## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Attachment of Bank Accounts and Demat Accounts under Attachment Proceedings (AP) No. 15508 & 15509 of 2026, dated June 29, 2026. The action is taken against 18 entities — including M/s Chandrima Mercantiles Limited and 17 individuals — in the matter of price and volume manipulation in the scrip of **Quasar India Limited**. The attachment is being enforced pursuant to Recovery Certificate No. 9155 of 2026.

## Key Points

- SEBI has ordered attachment of bank accounts and demat accounts of 18 defaulting entities under AP No. 15508 & 15509 of 2026.
- The action arises from price and volume manipulation in the scrip of Quasar India Limited.
- Recovery Certificate No. 9155 of 2026 is the legal basis for the attachment proceedings.
- The defaulters include one corporate entity (M/s Chandrima Mercantiles Limited, PAN: AABCC1666J) and 17 individuals.
- All 18 entities are named with their respective PAN numbers in the notice.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework changes are introduced. This is an enforcement action under SEBI's existing powers to attach and recover dues from defaulters through attachment proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

- Banks and depositories holding accounts of the named defaulters are directed to freeze/attach the specified bank accounts and demat accounts.
- The attached entities are prohibited from operating or withdrawing from the attached accounts.
- Relevant financial institutions must comply with SEBI's attachment orders immediately upon receipt of the notice.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 29, 2026
- **AP No.:** 15508 & 15509 of 2026
- **Recovery Certificate No.:** 9155 of 2026

## Impact Assessment

This enforcement action directly restricts the financial assets of 18 entities involved in manipulating the scrip of Quasar India Limited. The attachment of both bank and demat accounts effectively prevents these defaulters from transferring or liquidating assets pending recovery of dues owed to SEBI. The action reinforces SEBI's commitment to deterring market manipulation and enforcing penalties through asset recovery mechanisms. Market participants and investors in Quasar India Limited should note that manipulation-related proceedings are actively being pursued.
