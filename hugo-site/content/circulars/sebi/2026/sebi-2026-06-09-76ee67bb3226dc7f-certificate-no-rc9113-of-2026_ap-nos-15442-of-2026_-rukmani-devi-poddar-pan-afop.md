## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC9113 of 2026 in connection with Attachment Proceedings No. 15442 of 2026 against Rukmani Devi Poddar (PAN: AFOPP5613B). This certificate pertains to the recovery of dues from the named individual in the matter of illiquid stock options, which is part of SEBI's broader enforcement action against manipulation in the illiquid stock options segment.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9113 of 2026 issued by SEBI
- Attachment Proceedings No. (A.P.) 15442 of 2026 initiated against Rukmani Devi Poddar
- PAN of the defaulter: AFOPP5613B
- Matter pertains to illiquid stock options trading violations
- Certificate authorises recovery of outstanding dues/penalties imposed by SEBI

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an enforcement/recovery action under existing SEBI recovery and attachment proceedings powers.

## Compliance Requirements

- Rukmani Devi Poddar is required to comply with the recovery certificate and settle outstanding dues as directed
- Relevant banks/depositories and other entities may be required to give effect to attachment orders as specified in the proceedings

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate issued: June 2026
- Attachment Proceedings No. 15442 of 2026 initiated in 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is an individual-level enforcement action with no direct broader market impact. The illiquid stock options matter is a long-running SEBI enforcement initiative targeting entities that allegedly manipulated the illiquid stock options segment on exchanges. Recovery certificates such as this one represent the final stage of enforcement, aimed at realising penalties and dues from non-compliant entities.
