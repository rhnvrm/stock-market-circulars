## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Attachment dated July 22, 2026, under Recovery Certificate (RC) No. 9184 of 2026, drawn against Dhaval Vinodbhai Gadani. The notice arises from recovery proceedings connected to a matter involving the trading activities of certain entities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now known as DU Digital Global Limited). Such notices are issued by SEBI's Recovery Officer to enforce outstanding dues (such as penalties, disgorgement amounts, or other monetary directions) confirmed against an individual who has not complied with an earlier SEBI order.

## Key Points

- Notice type: Notice of Attachment, typically used to attach movable or immovable property, bank accounts, or other assets of a defaulter to recover unpaid dues.
- Recovery Certificate Number: RC No. 9184 of 2026.
- Person against whom the notice is drawn: Dhaval Vinodbhai Gadani.
- Underlying matter: Investigation/order concerning trading activities of certain entities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited).
- Notice date: July 22, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

This is an enforcement/recovery action rather than a policy or regulatory change. It reflects SEBI's exercise of powers under the SEBI Act (recovery of amounts under Section 28A, read with the Second and Third Schedules to the Income-tax Act, 1961, as applicable to recovery proceedings) to enforce compliance with a prior directive against the named individual.

## Compliance Requirements

- The named individual (Dhaval Vinodbhai Gadani) is required to settle the outstanding dues referenced under RC No. 9184 of 2026 to avoid further attachment or recovery action against his assets.
- Entities or intermediaries holding assets, accounts, or property belonging to the named individual may be required to comply with directions under the attachment notice, such as restricting withdrawal or transfer of attached assets.

## Important Dates

- Notice of Attachment issued: July 22, 2026.
- Specific compliance/response deadlines, if any, are set out in the full text of the notice and were not independently verifiable from the available circular metadata.

## Impact Assessment

This notice pertains to individual recovery proceedings against Dhaval Vinodbhai Gadani and does not directly impact the listing status, trading, or securities of DU Digital Global Limited. Market impact is expected to be minimal, as the action targets an individual in connection with a past trading-related matter rather than the company itself or its shareholders broadly. The notice is primarily relevant for compliance and enforcement tracking purposes.
