## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand dated June 29, 2026 under Recovery Certificate (RC) No. 9183 of 2026 against Nayan Mahendrabhai Thakkar. The notice arises from proceedings related to trading activities of certain entities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited, now renamed DU Digital Global Limited. A Recovery Certificate is issued by SEBI when a person against whom a monetary penalty or disgorgement order has been passed fails to comply voluntarily, triggering formal recovery proceedings.

## Key Points

- SEBI has initiated recovery proceedings via RC No. 9183 of 2026 against Nayan Mahendrabhai Thakkar.
- The underlying matter concerns alleged irregular trading activities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited).
- The Notice of Demand is dated June 29, 2026, requiring the named individual to fulfil outstanding monetary obligations to SEBI.
- Failure to comply with a Notice of Demand can lead to attachment of bank accounts, demat accounts, or other assets.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced by this notice. It is an enforcement action under existing SEBI recovery and penalty provisions (SEBI Act, 1992 and SEBI (Recovery of Amounts) Rules).

## Compliance Requirements

- Nayan Mahendrabhai Thakkar is required to pay the demanded amount specified in the Recovery Certificate within the stipulated timeframe.
- Non-compliance may result in coercive recovery measures including attachment and sale of properties, bank accounts, and demat holdings.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 29, 2026
- **RC No.:** 9183 of 2026
- Payment deadline: As specified within the notice document served on the individual.

## Impact Assessment

This notice has no direct impact on broader market participants or trading in DU Digital Global Limited. The action is limited to an individual respondent and represents SEBI's ongoing enforcement of penalty/disgorgement orders. Investors in DU Digital Global Limited are not directly affected by this recovery proceeding.
