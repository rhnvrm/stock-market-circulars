## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand under Recovery Certificate number 9219 of 2026, dated July 15, 2026, to Jitendra Surendra Gupta (PAN: AEJPG1171R) in connection with recovery proceedings arising from the matter of Oriental Trimex Limited. The notice directs the recipient to pay the outstanding demand amount specified in the recovery certificate, failing which further coercive recovery action may be initiated under applicable law.

## Key Points

- Notice of Demand issued under Recovery Certificate No. 9219 of 2026.
- Recovery Certificate dated July 15, 2026.
- Notice issued to Jitendra Surendra Gupta, PAN: AEJPG1171R.
- Recovery proceedings relate to the matter of Oriental Trimex Limited.
- Non-compliance with the demand may result in further recovery action, including attachment of bank accounts, movable/immovable property, and other coercive measures under the Income Tax Act (Second Schedule) provisions as applied to SEBI recovery proceedings.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced; this is an enforcement action under SEBI's existing recovery mechanism for realizing outstanding dues, penalties, or disgorgement amounts confirmed against the named individual in relation to Oriental Trimex Limited.

## Compliance Requirements

The noticee, Jitendra Surendra Gupta, is required to pay the amount demanded under Recovery Certificate No. 9219 of 2026 within the timeline specified in the notice. Failure to comply may lead to SEBI/Recovery Officer initiating further recovery proceedings, including attachment and sale of assets, prohibition on dealing in securities, and other measures permitted under law.

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate/Notice date: July 15, 2026.
- Specific payment deadline: As stated in the notice (not detailed in available metadata).

## Impact Assessment

This action is targeted at a specific individual, Jitendra Surendra Gupta, and does not directly affect listed securities, trading, or broader market operations. Its impact is confined to enforcement/recovery proceedings tied to the Oriental Trimex Limited matter, with high severity for the noticee but negligible impact on the wider market.
