## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand dated June 29, 2026 under Recovery Certificate (RC) No. 9180 of 2026 against Sanjay Savjibhai Parmar. This notice pertains to the investigation into trading activities of certain entities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited, now known as DU Digital Global Limited.

## Key Points

- Notice of Demand issued under RC No. 9180 of 2026
- Issued against: Sanjay Savjibhai Parmar
- Date of notice: June 29, 2026
- Related to trading activities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited)
- Part of SEBI's enforcement and recovery proceedings against entities involved in alleged irregular trading

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement/recovery action under existing SEBI regulations and the Securities and Exchange Board of India Act.

## Compliance Requirements

- The named individual (Sanjay Savjibhai Parmar) is required to comply with the demand specified in the Notice of Demand issued under RC No. 9180 of 2026.
- Payment of the demanded amount or other compliance as directed by SEBI is expected within the stipulated timeframe mentioned in the notice.
- Non-compliance may result in attachment of bank accounts and/or demat accounts as per SEBI's recovery powers.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 29, 2026
- **RC No.:** 9180 of 2026

## Impact Assessment

This enforcement action is targeted at a specific individual and relates to alleged irregular trading in DU Digital Global Limited scrip. The broader market impact is limited; however, it signals continued SEBI vigilance in monitoring and penalising entities involved in manipulative or non-compliant trading activities. Investors in DU Digital Global Limited (formerly DU Digital Technologies Limited) should be aware of this ongoing regulatory scrutiny.
