## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC9099 of 2026 under Adjudication Proceeding No. 15410 of 2026 against Anita Jhajharia (PAN: ACQPJ4887N) in the matter of Illiquid Stock Options. This certificate is issued to facilitate recovery of the penalty amount imposed through the adjudication order, as part of SEBI's enforcement action against illegal trading in illiquid stock options on the derivatives segment.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9099 of 2026 issued against Anita Jhajharia
- PAN of the noticee: ACQPJ4887N
- Adjudication Proceeding (A.P.) No. 15410 of 2026
- Matter relates to trading in illiquid stock options
- Certificate enables SEBI to recover the adjudicated penalty amount
- Part of SEBI's broader enforcement drive against manipulative trading in illiquid stock options on NSE

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is an enforcement/recovery document issued under the SEBI Act, 1992 and SEBI (Recovery of Amount) Regulations, 2013.

## Compliance Requirements

- Anita Jhajharia is required to pay the penalty amount as specified in the adjudication order under A.P. No. 15410 of 2026
- Non-payment may result in further coercive recovery action by SEBI
- The recovery certificate authorises the Recovery Officer to proceed with recovery of dues

## Important Dates

- **June 4, 2026**: Recovery Certificate No. RC9099 of 2026 issued

## Impact Assessment

This recovery certificate has minimal direct market impact. It represents an administrative enforcement step by SEBI to recover penalty amounts from an individual found to have engaged in trading in illiquid stock options. SEBI has issued multiple such certificates (A.P. Nos. 15410, 15411, and others) under RC9099 of 2026, indicating consolidated recovery proceedings against Anita Jhajharia. The action is part of SEBI's sustained crackdown on manipulative practices in the illiquid stock options segment.
