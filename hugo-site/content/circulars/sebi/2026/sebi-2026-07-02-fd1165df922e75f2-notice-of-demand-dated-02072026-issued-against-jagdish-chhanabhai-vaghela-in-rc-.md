## Summary

SEBI's Recovery and Refund Department has issued a Notice of Demand (Certificate No. 9188 of 2026) against Jagdish Chhanabhai Vaghela under Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992 read with Section 222 of the Income Tax Act, 1961. The notice demands payment of Rs. 8,57,000 (comprising penalty, interest, and recovery cost) arising from a penalty order dated 31.12.2025 related to trading activities of certain entities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited).

## Key Points

- Certificate No. 9188 of 2026 issued against Jagdish Chhanabhai Vaghela (PAN: AFPPV2562Q), Ahmedabad.
- Total demand: Rs. 8,57,000 (Penalty: Rs. 8,00,000; Interest: Rs. 56,000; Recovery cost: Rs. 1,000).
- Underlying penalty imposed via Order No. QJA/MN/IVD/ID7/31947/2025-26 dated 31.12.2025 concerning trading activities in DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited).
- Payment must be made within 15 days of receipt via EFT/NEFT/RTGS or SEBI's online Recovery Payment portal (siportal.sebi.gov.in).
- Non-payment empowers the Recovery Officer to enforce recovery via attachment/sale of movable or immovable property, attachment of bank accounts, arrest and detention, or appointment of a receiver.
- Any property transfers to spouse, minor child, son's wife, or son's minor child without adequate consideration on or after 31.12.2025 will be deemed the defaulter's property for recovery purposes.
- Upon service of notice, the individual is barred from mortgaging, charging, leasing, or otherwise dealing with property without permission of the Recovery Officer; violations are void.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is an enforcement action applying existing recovery provisions under Section 28A of the SEBI Act and the Second Schedule to the Income Tax Act, 1961.

## Compliance Requirements

The noticee (Jagdish Chhanabhai Vaghela) is required to pay the full outstanding amount of Rs. 8,57,000 within 15 days of receiving the notice, failing which coercive recovery measures will be initiated. Confirmation of any e-payment must be forwarded to the Recovery Officer.

## Important Dates

- Penalty Order Date: 31.12.2025
- Notice of Demand Date: 02.07.2026
- Payment Deadline: Within 15 days of receipt of notice
- Interest accrual period noted: January 2026 to July 2026 @ 1% per month

## Impact Assessment

This circular is an individual enforcement and recovery action with no direct bearing on market operations, listed companies, or broader investor community. Its relevance is limited to the concerned individual and stands as a reference case regarding SEBI's recovery mechanism for unpaid penalties tied to unauthorized trading activity in DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited).
