## Summary

SEBI has issued Notice of Demand under Recovery Certificate No. 9212 of 2026 dated 07.07.2026 against Rohit Bairwa (PAN: ELYPB0179Q), residing in Jaipur, Rajasthan. The notice demands payment of Rs. 1,68,257.46 towards disgorgement dues arising from trading activities of certain entities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited). The demand is issued under Rule 2 of the Second Schedule to the Income-tax Act, 1961 read with Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 9212 of 2026 issued against Rohit Bairwa (PAN: ELYPB0179Q).
- Underlying dues stem from a disgorgement order (Order No. QJA/MN/IVD/ID7/31947/2025-26 dated 31.12.2025) passed by the Quasi-Judicial Authority in connection with trading activities of certain entities in DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited).
- Total amount demanded: Rs. 1,68,257.46, comprising principal disgorgement of Rs. 1,56,315.39, interest of Rs. 10,942.07 (January 2026 to July 2026 @ 1% p.a.), and recovery cost of Rs. 1,000.
- Payment due within 15 days of receipt of the notice via EFT/NEFT/RTGS to a designated ICICI Bank account or through SEBI's online recovery payment portal (siportal.sebi.gov.in).
- Non-payment empowers SEBI's Recovery Officer to enforce recovery via attachment/sale of movable or immovable property, attachment of bank accounts, arrest and detention, or appointment of a receiver.
- Restrictions apply on the individual's ability to transfer property or dispose of assets once the notice is served, per Section 28A of the SEBI Act and Rule 16 of the Second Schedule to the Income-tax Act.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is an enforcement action executing a prior disgorgement order under existing SEBI Act and Income-tax Act recovery provisions.

## Compliance Requirements

Rohit Bairwa must remit the total due amount (Rs. 1,68,257.46) within 15 days of receiving the notice and forward payment confirmation to SEBI's Recovery Officer. He is barred from mortgaging, charging, leasing, or otherwise transferring his property without permission of the Recovery Officer once the notice is served; any such transfer would be void.

## Important Dates

- Order for disgorgement: 31.12.2025 (Order No. QJA/MN/IVD/ID7/31947/2025-26)
- Recovery Certificate date: 07.07.2026
- Payment deadline: within 15 days of receipt of the notice
- Interest accrual period noted: January 2026 to July 2026 @ 1% p.a.

## Impact Assessment

This notice has no direct market-wide impact; it is a targeted enforcement/recovery action against a single individual for a relatively small monetary amount. It reflects SEBI's ongoing enforcement of disgorgement orders related to irregular trading activities in DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited) and does not affect the listed company's operations or trading status directly, though it may be part of a broader set of enforcement actions tied to that scrip's trading investigation.
