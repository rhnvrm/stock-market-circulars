## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order in connection with Recovery Certificate No. RC7668 of 2024, which was raised against Renuka Trust (PAN: AABTR0465M) in the matter of JMD Telefilms Industries Ltd. A release order typically indicates that the outstanding dues under the recovery certificate have been satisfied or the certificate is otherwise discharged.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC7668 of 2024 issued against Renuka Trust (PAN: AABTR0465M) is being released.
- The matter arises from SEBI enforcement proceedings related to JMD Telefilms Industries Ltd.
- A release order signifies closure or satisfaction of the outstanding recovery demand against the named entity.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is an administrative/enforcement closure order under SEBI's recovery mechanism.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements for market participants. The order is specific to Renuka Trust and the recovery proceedings in the JMD Telefilms matter.

## Important Dates

- Order Date: June 2026
- Recovery Certificate Reference: RC7668 of 2024

## Impact Assessment

The release order has no broader market or operational impact. It marks the conclusion of a specific enforcement recovery action against Renuka Trust related to JMD Telefilms Industries Ltd. Investors and market participants not party to these proceedings are unaffected.
