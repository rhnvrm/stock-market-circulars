## Summary

SEBI has issued a Remittance Advice against Mr. MD Sarfraz Ansari (Defaulter, PAN: AVSPA8138L), jointly and severally with 11 others, in the matter of Unique Multi Foods Industries Limited. The action is taken under Recovery Certificate No. 5435 of 2022, indicating that a recoverable amount has been remitted or attached as part of SEBI's enforcement and recovery proceedings.

## Key Points

- Remittance advice issued against Mr. MD Sarfraz Ansari (PAN: AVSPA8138L) as the primary defaulter
- Liability is joint and several with 11 other named parties
- Relates to the matter of Unique Multi Foods Industries Limited
- Proceeds under Recovery Certificate No. 5435 of 2022
- Issued by SEBI's enforcement division as part of ongoing recovery proceedings

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced by this circular. It is an operational enforcement action notifying the remittance of recovered funds under an existing recovery certificate.

## Compliance Requirements

- The named defaulters are required to comply with the terms of Recovery Certificate No. 5435 of 2022
- Any banks, depositories, or intermediaries holding assets of the defaulters may be required to act on SEBI's recovery instructions
- Other market participants are not directly affected

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate No.: 5435 of 2022
- Circular Date: 24 June 2026

## Impact Assessment

This remittance advice has minimal impact on the broader market. It is an enforcement-specific document directed at named individuals and entities in connection with Unique Multi Foods Industries Limited. General investors and market intermediaries are not affected by this circular.
