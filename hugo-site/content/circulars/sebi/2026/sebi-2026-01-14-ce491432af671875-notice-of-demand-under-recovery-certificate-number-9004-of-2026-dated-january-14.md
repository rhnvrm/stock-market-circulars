## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand under Recovery Certificate number 9004 of 2026 dated January 14, 2026, to Sattu Yadav (PAN: AHXPY0089P) in connection with violations related to dealing in illiquid stock options at the Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE). This enforcement action seeks recovery of dues arising from regulatory violations.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 9004 of 2026 issued on January 14, 2026
- Target entity: Sattu Yadav (PAN: AHXPY0089P)
- Violation pertains to dealing in illiquid stock options at BSE
- Formal demand notice issued for recovery of outstanding amounts
- Part of SEBI's ongoing enforcement against market manipulation and illiquid securities trading

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI regulations governing trading in illiquid securities and stock options.

## Compliance Requirements

- Sattu Yadav is required to pay the demanded amount as specified in the recovery certificate
- Failure to comply may result in further enforcement proceedings including attachment of assets
- The entity must respond to SEBI's demand within the stipulated timeframe

## Important Dates

- **January 14, 2026**: Date of issuance of Recovery Certificate No. 9004 of 2026
- Payment deadline would be specified in the detailed recovery notice

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is an individual enforcement action with no direct impact on broader market operations or trading systems.

**Entity Impact**: High - Sattu Yadav faces financial liability and potential asset attachment if recovery demands are not met.

**Regulatory Significance**: This notice demonstrates SEBI's continued focus on enforcement against illiquid stock option trading violations and market manipulation. It serves as a deterrent to similar violations by other market participants.
