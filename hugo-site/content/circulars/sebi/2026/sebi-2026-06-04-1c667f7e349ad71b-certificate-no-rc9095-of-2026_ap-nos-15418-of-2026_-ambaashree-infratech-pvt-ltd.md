## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC9095 of 2026 against Ambaashree Infratech Pvt. Ltd. (PAN: AAKCA4144N) pursuant to Adjudication Proceedings No. 15418 of 2026. The certificate relates to violations committed in the illiquid stock options segment and enables SEBI to recover the adjudicated penalty amount through coercive means if the entity fails to pay voluntarily.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9095 of 2026 issued against Ambaashree Infratech Pvt. Ltd.
- Entity PAN: AAKCA4144N
- Adjudication Proceedings reference: A.P. No. 15418 of 2026
- Matter pertains to violations in the illiquid stock options segment on Indian stock exchanges
- Recovery certificate enables SEBI to pursue coercive recovery of outstanding penalty amounts
- Part of SEBI's ongoing enforcement action against entities involved in illiquid stock options manipulation

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement/recovery action under existing SEBI adjudication and recovery framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Ambaashree Infratech Pvt. Ltd. is required to pay the penalty amount as adjudicated in A.P. No. 15418 of 2026
- Failure to comply with the recovery certificate may result in coercive recovery proceedings including attachment of assets
- Other market participants involved in illiquid stock options trading should ensure all prior SEBI orders and penalties are settled promptly to avoid similar recovery proceedings

## Important Dates

- Certificate Date: June 4, 2026
- Recovery Certificate No.: RC9095 of 2026
- Adjudication Proceedings No.: 15418 of 2026

## Impact Assessment

This recovery certificate has limited broader market impact and is specific to Ambaashree Infratech Pvt. Ltd. It is part of SEBI's continued and systematic enforcement against entities found to have participated in manipulation of the illiquid stock options segment. The illiquid stock options matter has seen SEBI issue numerous adjudication orders and recovery certificates, signalling sustained regulatory scrutiny of past manipulative trading practices in this segment. Market participants with pending SEBI adjudication orders should treat this as a reminder to settle outstanding dues proactively.
