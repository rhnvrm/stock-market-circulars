## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand under Recovery Certificate No. 9169 of 2026 against Jigneshkumar Purshottamdas Patel (PAN: AQEPP8019J) of Ahmedabad, Gujarat, for a total amount of Rs. 8,49,000. The demand arises from a penalty of Rs. 8,00,000 imposed by SEBI's Quasi-Judicial Authority (Order No. QJA/MN/IVD/ID7/31947/2025-26 dated 31 December 2025) for trading irregularities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited), along with accrued interest and recovery costs.

## Key Points

- **Demand amount:** Rs. 8,49,000 comprising penalty (Rs. 8,00,000), interest from January–June 2026 at 1% per month (Rs. 48,000), and recovery cost (Rs. 1,000)
- **Underlying order:** QJA/MN/IVD/ID7/31947/2025-26 dated 31.12.2025 by SEBI's Quasi-Judicial Authority
- **Scrip involved:** DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited)
- **Payment window:** 15 days from receipt of this notice
- **Payment modes:** EFT/NEFT/RTGS to ICICI Bank A/c No. SEBIRRDPEN9169 (IFSC: ICIC0000106), or via SEBI's online Recovery Payment portal at https://siportal.sebi.gov.in
- **Recovery Officer contact:** akashb@sebi.gov.in and recoveryho1@sebi.gov.in

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This notice invokes existing provisions under Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992 read with Sections 220–227, 228A, 229, and 232 of the Income Tax Act, 1961, and the Second Schedule thereto.

## Compliance Requirements

- The named entity must pay Rs. 8,49,000 within 15 days of receiving this notice.
- Any property transfer to spouse, minor child, son's wife, or son's minor child without adequate consideration on or after 31 December 2025 will be treated as the defaulter's own property for recovery purposes.
- The named entity is prohibited from mortgaging, charging, leasing, or otherwise dealing with any property without prior permission of the Recovery Officer; any such transfer will be void.
- Confirmation of payment (including case name, RC number, payee name, date, amount, transaction number, and bank details) must be forwarded to SEBI's Recovery Officer at the Mumbai address or the designated email addresses.

## Important Dates

- **31 December 2025:** Date of original penalty order (QJA/MN/IVD/ID7/31947/2025-26)
- **January 2026 – June 2026:** Period for which interest at 1% per month has accrued
- **24 June 2026:** Date of this Notice of Demand
- **Within 15 days of receipt:** Deadline for full payment to avoid coercive recovery action

## Impact Assessment

This is an individual enforcement action with limited broader market impact. The penalty relates to trading irregularities in DU Digital Global Limited (formerly DU Digital Technologies Limited). Failure to pay within the stipulated period will trigger coercive recovery measures, including attachment and sale of movable or immovable property, bank account attachment, and potential arrest and detention. Investors in DU Digital Global Limited should be aware of the ongoing regulatory scrutiny of trading activities in this scrip.
