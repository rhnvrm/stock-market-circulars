## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Attachment dated July 08, 2026, under Recovery Certificate (RC) No. 9161 of 2026, drawn against Rupee Services Private Limited. The notice arises from recovery proceedings initiated by SEBI to recover monetary dues (such as penalties, disgorgement amounts, or other sums adjudicated) from the entity in connection with its involvement in dealing in illiquid stock options at BSE. Notices of attachment are issued when an entity fails to pay amounts due under a SEBI order within the stipulated period, prompting SEBI's Recovery Officer to attach the entity's movable or immovable properties, bank accounts, or other assets to enforce recovery.

## Key Points

- Notice of Attachment issued on July 08, 2026 under RC No. 9161 of 2026.
- The recovery certificate is drawn against Rupee Services Private Limited.
- The underlying matter relates to dealing in illiquid stock options at BSE, an area SEBI has actively pursued for enforcement due to concerns around non-genuine trading and market manipulation.
- The notice is part of SEBI's coercive recovery process, typically invoked after non-compliance with a prior payment demand or adjudication/recovery order.
- Attachment notices are published by SEBI as part of its enforcement and recovery-proceedings disclosures.

## Regulatory Changes

This is an enforcement action rather than a rule or regulatory amendment; no changes to SEBI regulations, byelaws, or market-wide compliance frameworks are introduced by this notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Rupee Services Private Limited is required to settle the outstanding dues covered under RC No. 9161 of 2026 to prevent further coercive recovery action (such as sale of attached assets).
- Banks, depositories, or other custodians holding assets of the named entity may be required to comply with the attachment by restricting withdrawal, transfer, or disposal of the attached assets pending resolution.
- No compliance obligations are created for other market participants, listed companies, or intermediaries not named in the notice.

## Important Dates

- Notice of Attachment date: July 08, 2026.

## Impact Assessment

The direct impact of this notice is confined to Rupee Services Private Limited and any custodians/banks holding its attached assets; it does not affect listed securities, trading operations, or the broader market. The action reinforces SEBI's ongoing enforcement stance against entities involved in illiquid stock options dealings at BSE and serves as a continued deterrent signal to market participants engaging in similar non-genuine trading practices.
