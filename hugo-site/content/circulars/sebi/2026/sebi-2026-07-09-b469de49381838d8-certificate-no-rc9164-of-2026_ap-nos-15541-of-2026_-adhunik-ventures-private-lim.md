## Summary

SEBI's Recovery Officer has issued Certificate No. RC9164 of 2026 under A.P. No. 15541 of 2026 against Adhunik Ventures Private Limited (PAN: AAHCA7165L), authorizing recovery of outstanding dues arising from SEBI's enforcement action in the illiquid stock options matter. This is a companion recovery certificate to a related A.P. No. 15540 of 2026 issued against the same entity under the same certificate number.
