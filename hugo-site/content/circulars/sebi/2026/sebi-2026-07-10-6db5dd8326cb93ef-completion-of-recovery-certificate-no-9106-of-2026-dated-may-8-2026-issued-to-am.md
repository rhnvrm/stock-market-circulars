## Summary

SEBI has issued a notice confirming completion of recovery proceedings under Recovery Certificate No. 9106 of 2026, dated May 8, 2026, which was issued against Amit Agarwal (PAN: AEEPA5456L), a defaulter in a matter concerning dealing in illiquid stock options on BSE. The recovery certificate, originally issued to recover dues from the defaulter, has now been marked as completed.

## Key Points

- Defaulter: Amit Agarwal (PAN: AEEPA5456L)
- Recovery Certificate No.: 9106 of 2026, dated May 8, 2026
- Underlying violation: Dealing in Illiquid Stock Options on BSE
- Status: Recovery proceedings under this certificate have been completed

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced; this is a case-specific enforcement update closing out a previously initiated recovery action.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance action is required from market participants, listed companies, or intermediaries. The notice pertains solely to closure of recovery proceedings against the named individual defaulter.

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate issued: May 8, 2026
- Completion notice date: July 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market-wide impact as it concerns the conclusion of an individual enforcement/recovery matter. It does not affect trading, listing status, or obligations of any listed company or the broader investor community, but reflects SEBI's ongoing enforcement of recovery certificates against individuals found in default relating to illiquid derivatives trading violations.
