## Summary

SEBI has announced the completion of Recovery Certificate no. RC7647 of 2024 issued against Saurabh Family Trust (PAN: ABFFS3296Q) in the matter of JMD Telefilms Industries Limited. The completion of the recovery certificate signifies that the recovery proceedings initiated by SEBI have been concluded, either through payment of dues or through other means of recovery under applicable law.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC7647 of 2024 has been marked as completed.
- The certificate was issued against Saurabh Family Trust, identified by PAN ABFFS3296Q.
- The underlying matter pertains to JMD Telefilms Industries Limited.
- Completion indicates the enforcement/recovery action by SEBI against the named entity has concluded.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This notice represents an administrative closure of a specific enforcement action under SEBI's recovery proceedings framework.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance obligations arise from this notice for market participants. The notice is specific to the named entity (Saurabh Family Trust) and the underlying case relating to JMD Telefilms Industries Limited.

## Important Dates

- Date of Notice: June 19, 2026
- Recovery Certificate: RC7647 of 2024 (issued in 2024, now completed as of June 2026)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal broader market impact. It is an administrative update confirming the closure of a SEBI recovery action against a specific trust entity in the JMD Telefilms Industries Limited matter. Investors and market participants in JMD Telefilms may note this as a resolution of a pending enforcement matter, which could be marginally positive for the company's regulatory standing.
