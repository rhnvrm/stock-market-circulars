## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand dated June 29, 2026 under Recovery Certificate (RC) No. 9184 of 2026 against Dhaval Vinodbhai Gadani. The notice pertains to trading activities of certain entities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now known as DU Digital Global Limited), directing recovery of outstanding dues.

## Key Points

- Notice of Demand issued under RC No. 9184 of 2026
- Directed against: Dhaval Vinodbhai Gadani
- Relates to trading activities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited)
- SEBI is exercising recovery powers under the SEBI Act to enforce payment of outstanding penalties or disgorgement amounts
- This notice is part of a series of recovery actions issued by SEBI on June 29, 2026 in this matter

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement/recovery action under existing SEBI regulations and the SEBI Act, 1992.

## Compliance Requirements

- The noticee (Dhaval Vinodbhai Gadani) is required to pay the demanded amount as specified in the Recovery Certificate
- Failure to comply may lead to further recovery proceedings including attachment of bank accounts, demat accounts, and other assets
- The noticee must respond to the notice within the timeframe stipulated in the demand

## Important Dates

- Notice of Demand Date: June 29, 2026
- RC No. 9184 of 2026 issued by SEBI

## Impact Assessment

This enforcement action has limited broader market impact. It is targeted at a specific individual (Dhaval Vinodbhai Gadani) and relates to past trading irregularities in DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited). The action signals SEBI's continued pursuit of recovery from entities involved in alleged manipulative or irregular trading activities. Investors in DU Digital Global Limited should note that SEBI is actively enforcing penalties arising from the investigation into trading activities in this scrip.
