## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order for Recovery Certificate No. 4120 of 2021, which was drawn against Ankit Jagadishbhai Pithava (PAN: BXVPP0489B) in connection with matters related to Vani Commercials Ltd. A release order typically indicates that the outstanding dues have been recovered or compliance has been achieved, resulting in the closure of recovery proceedings.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 4120 of 2021 has been released
- The certificate was issued against Ankit Jagadishbhai Pithava (PAN: BXVPP0489B)
- Related to the matter of Vani Commercials Ltd.
- Release order signifies completion of recovery proceedings
- Order issued on January 30, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this order. This is a routine enforcement compliance order related to specific recovery proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements are imposed. This order concludes existing recovery proceedings against the named individual, indicating that compliance obligations have been fulfilled.

## Important Dates

- **Order Date:** January 30, 2026
- **Recovery Certificate No.:** 4120 of 2021

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This is a specific enforcement action against an individual related to recovery proceedings.

**Operational Impact:** Limited to the parties directly involved in the recovery proceedings. The release order indicates that the recovery matter has been resolved, either through payment of dues or other compliance measures.

**Investor Impact:** No direct impact on general investors. This order pertains to enforcement proceedings against a specific individual in connection with Vani Commercials Ltd.
