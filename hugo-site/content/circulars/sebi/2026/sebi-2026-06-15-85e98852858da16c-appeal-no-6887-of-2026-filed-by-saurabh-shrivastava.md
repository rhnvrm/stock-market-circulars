## Summary

SEBI's Appellate Authority dismissed RTI Appeal No. 6887 of 2026 filed by Saurabh Shrivastava against CPIO, SEBI, Mumbai. The appellant, a buyer who had invested in PACL's 'Pearls Royal Garden Bhopal (Sehore)' property, sought details on its sale/auction status. SEBI's CPIO responded that the information was not available with SEBI, and the Appellate Authority upheld this response.

## Key Points

- Appellant Saurabh Shrivastava filed an RTI application dated May 03, 2026 seeking sale/auction status of PACL property 'Pearls Royal Garden Bhopal (Sehore)'
- Information sought included whether the property was sold/auctioned, sale date, sale amount, transfer status, buyer name, and current stage if unsold
- CPIO, SEBI responded on May 05, 2026 that the requested information is not available with SEBI
- Appellant filed appeal dated May 17, 2026 (Reg. No. SEBIH/A/E/26/00184) alleging incomplete, misleading, or false information
- Appellate Authority found no deficiency in the CPIO's response, citing CIC precedent that a CPIO cannot invent information it does not possess
- Responsibility for PACL property disposal and investor repayment is with the Justice (Retd.) R. M. Lodha Committee, constituted per the Supreme Court order dated February 2, 2016

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative RTI appellate order with no new rules or directives.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. The order reaffirms that SEBI's CPIO is not obligated to provide information it does not possess, consistent with established CIC jurisprudence.

## Important Dates

- May 03, 2026: Original RTI application filed by appellant
- May 05, 2026: CPIO, SEBI responded to the application
- May 17, 2026: Appellant filed appeal (Reg. No. SEBIH/A/E/26/00184)
- June 15, 2026: Appellate Authority order issued dismissing the appeal

## Impact Assessment

This order has no market or operational impact. It is a routine RTI appellate dismissal affecting only the individual appellant. Investors with queries about PACL property disposal are directed to consult Status Reports, FAQs, Press Releases, and Public Notices published on the SEBI website, which relate to the ongoing work of the Justice Lodha Committee.
