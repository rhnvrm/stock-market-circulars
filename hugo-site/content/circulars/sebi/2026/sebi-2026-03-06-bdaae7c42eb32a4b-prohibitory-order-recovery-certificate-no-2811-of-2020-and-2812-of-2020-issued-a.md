## Summary

SEBI has issued a prohibitory order against JVV Raghava Kumar pursuant to Recovery Certificate Nos. 2811 and 2812 of 2020 arising from enforcement proceedings in the matter of Ranklin Solutions Ltd. The order restrains the noticee from alienating, transferring, or disposing of assets until the outstanding dues are recovered.

## Key Points

- Prohibitory order issued against JVV Raghava Kumar under Recovery Certificate Nos. 2811 and 2812 of 2020
- Proceedings relate to the matter of Ranklin Solutions Ltd.
- Order prohibits disposal, transfer, or encumbrance of assets of the noticee
- Action taken under SEBI's recovery proceedings framework to enforce outstanding dues
- Recovery Certificates were originally issued in 2020

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an enforcement/recovery action under existing SEBI recovery proceedings provisions.

## Compliance Requirements

- JVV Raghava Kumar is prohibited from alienating, transferring, or disposing of any assets until the recovery of dues under Recovery Certificate Nos. 2811 and 2812 of 2020
- Banks and financial institutions holding assets of the noticee may be directed to freeze accounts as applicable
- The noticee must cooperate with SEBI's recovery proceedings

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificates issued: 2020
- Prohibitory order date: March 6, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is an individual enforcement action with no direct impact on broader markets or listed companies. It affects only JVV Raghava Kumar personally, restricting asset disposal until SEBI recovers outstanding dues related to Ranklin Solutions Ltd. proceedings. Ranklin Solutions Ltd. shareholders and the general investing public are not directly impacted by this order.
