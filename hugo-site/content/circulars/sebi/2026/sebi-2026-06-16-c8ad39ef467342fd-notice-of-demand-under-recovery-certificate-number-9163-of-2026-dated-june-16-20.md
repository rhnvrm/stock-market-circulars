## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand under Recovery Certificate No. 9163 of 2026, dated June 16, 2026, against Mr. Praveen Goyal, Proprietor of Shivam Trading Company, PAN: AQMPG2650C. This notice arises in the matter of Delta Leasing and Finance Ltd. and is part of SEBI's enforcement and recovery proceedings against the named defaulter.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 9163 of 2026 issued on June 16, 2026
- Defaulter: Mr. Praveen Goyal, Proprietor of Shivam Trading Company
- PAN of defaulter: AQMPG2650C
- Matter relates to Delta Leasing and Finance Ltd.
- Notice of Demand formally served as part of SEBI's recovery proceedings

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI recovery and demand notice provisions.

## Compliance Requirements

The defaulter, Mr. Praveen Goyal (Proprietor of Shivam Trading Company), is required to comply with the terms of the Notice of Demand issued under Recovery Certificate No. 9163 of 2026, which typically entails payment of the outstanding dues specified therein within the stipulated timeframe to avoid further enforcement action.

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate Date: June 16, 2026
- Notice of Demand issuance date: June 16, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This notice has no direct market-wide impact. It is a targeted enforcement measure by SEBI against a specific individual defaulter in connection with an older matter involving Delta Leasing and Finance Ltd. The action reflects SEBI's continued pursuit of outstanding recovery dues from defaulters. No listed securities or broader investor community are affected.
