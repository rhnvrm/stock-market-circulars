## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order for Recovery Certificate No. RC7663 of 2024, which was issued against Madhav Trust (PAN: AABTM6615Q) in connection with the matter of JMD Telefilms Industries Ltd. A release order typically signifies that the outstanding dues or penalties have been paid in full, and SEBI is formally discharging the recovery proceedings against the entity.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC7663 of 2024 has been formally released by SEBI
- The certificate was issued against Madhav Trust (PAN: AABTM6615Q)
- The underlying matter pertains to JMD Telefilms Industries Ltd.
- The release order indicates settlement or full payment of dues previously demanded under the recovery certificate
- SEBI's enforcement action against this entity is now concluded

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced by this order. This is an administrative closure of an existing enforcement proceeding.

## Compliance Requirements

No ongoing compliance requirements arise from this release order. The issuance of a release order by SEBI discharges Madhav Trust from the obligations under Recovery Certificate No. RC7663 of 2024. Concerned authorities (e.g., the Tax Recovery Officer) should treat the recovery proceedings as concluded.

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate No.: RC7663 of 2024
- Release Order issued: June 19, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This order has minimal market impact. It is an administrative closure of an enforcement action against Madhav Trust relating to the JMD Telefilms Industries Ltd. matter. Investors and market participants are not directly affected. The order confirms resolution of a prior SEBI enforcement action and has no bearing on current trading or regulatory obligations for other market participants.
