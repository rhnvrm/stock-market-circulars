## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand under Recovery Certificate No. RC9165 of 2026 against Grand Dealtrade Private Limited (PAN: AAECG1630P) in the matter of illiquid stock options. The notice demands payment of outstanding penalty and/or disgorgement amounts that remain unpaid by the entity following earlier SEBI enforcement orders related to artificial/circular trading in illiquid stock options.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9165 of 2026 issued against Grand Dealtrade Private Limited (PAN: AAECG1630P)
- The matter pertains to illiquid stock options, a segment subject to widespread SEBI enforcement for artificial and circular trading
- This Notice of Demand signifies the entity has not yet paid outstanding dues under the recovery certificate
- SEBI is formally demanding payment of penalty and/or disgorgement amounts from Grand Dealtrade Private Limited
- Part of SEBI's ongoing series of recovery proceedings in the illiquid stock options enforcement action

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is an administrative enforcement notice directed at a specific entity to compel payment of outstanding dues under an existing recovery certificate.

## Compliance Requirements

- Grand Dealtrade Private Limited (PAN: AAECG1630P) is required to pay the outstanding dues demanded under Recovery Certificate No. RC9165 of 2026
- Failure to comply may result in further coercive recovery action by SEBI including attachment of assets
- No new compliance obligations arise for other market participants from this notice

## Important Dates

- Date of Notice of Demand: 2026-06-22
- Recovery Certificate: RC9165 of 2026

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal broader market impact. It is an administrative enforcement action against a single private entity (Grand Dealtrade Private Limited) as part of SEBI's ongoing recovery proceedings in the illiquid stock options matter. The illiquid stock options enforcement series has resulted in numerous recovery certificates and demand notices against entities found to have engaged in artificial/circular trading. No market-wide implications arise from this specific notice.
