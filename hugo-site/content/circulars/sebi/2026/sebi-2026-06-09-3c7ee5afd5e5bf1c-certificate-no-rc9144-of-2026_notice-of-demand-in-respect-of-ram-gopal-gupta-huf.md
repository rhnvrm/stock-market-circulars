## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand against Ram Gopal Gupta HUF (PAN: AAPHR5560P) under Recovery Certificate No. RC9144 of 2026 in connection with the matter of illiquid stock options. This notice is part of SEBI's ongoing enforcement and recovery proceedings against entities found to have engaged in fraudulent or manipulative trading in illiquid stock options segments on Indian exchanges.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9144 of 2026 has been issued against Ram Gopal Gupta HUF (PAN: AAPHR5560P).
- The notice pertains to the illiquid stock options matter, which involves alleged manipulation and fraudulent trading in the illiquid stock options segment.
- The Notice of Demand requires the named entity to pay the outstanding dues as determined by SEBI's earlier order(s).
- Non-compliance with the demand may result in further recovery actions including attachment of assets.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced by this notice. It is an enforcement instrument under the existing SEBI recovery and demand framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Ram Gopal Gupta HUF is required to comply with the Notice of Demand and remit the outstanding penalty or disgorgement amount specified under Recovery Certificate No. RC9144 of 2026.
- Failure to comply may lead to coercive recovery measures such as attachment of bank accounts, properties, or other assets.

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate No.: RC9144 of 2026
- Notice issued: June 2026
- Compliance deadline: As specified in the notice document.

## Impact Assessment

This is an individual enforcement action and has no direct impact on broader market operations or participants. It reflects SEBI's continued enforcement efforts against entities implicated in the illiquid stock options manipulation matter, which has been an area of sustained regulatory scrutiny. Market participants not involved in the proceedings are unaffected.
