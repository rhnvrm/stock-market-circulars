## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order for Recovery Certificate No. 8711 of 2025, which was issued against Securekloud Technologies Limited (formerly known as 8Kmiles Software Services Ltd), bearing PAN AABCP6266D, in the matter of Securekloud Technologies Limited. A release order signifies that the recovery proceedings initiated under the certificate have been completed, either through full payment of the certified amount or by other lawful means.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8711 of 2025 stands released as of May 11, 2026.
- The certificate was issued against Securekloud Technologies Limited (formerly 8Kmiles Software Services Ltd), PAN: AABCP6266D.
- A companion Completion Order (circular ID: f3432932286b184a) was issued on the same date, confirming the recovery is fully satisfied.
- The release order is issued in the matter of Securekloud Technologies Limited under SEBI enforcement/recovery proceedings.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced by this order. It is a closure document within existing SEBI enforcement machinery under the SEBI Act, 1992 and the SEBI (Recovery of Amounts) Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

No fresh compliance obligations arise for market participants from this release order. The affected entity — Securekloud Technologies Limited — is discharged from the specific liability covered under RC No. 8711 of 2025.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Release Order:** May 11, 2026
- **Recovery Certificate Number:** 8711 of 2025

## Impact Assessment

The release of a recovery certificate has no broader market or operational impact. It is an administrative action confirming closure of a specific enforcement recovery proceeding against Securekloud Technologies Limited. Investors and market intermediaries are not affected. The stock may see minor sentiment impact given the formal closure of a SEBI enforcement matter against the company.
