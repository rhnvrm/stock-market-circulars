## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC9096 of 2026 under Appeal/Adjudication Proceeding No. 15407 of 2026 against Swift Dealmark Private Limited (PAN: AAPCS1110D) in connection with the matter of illiquid stock options. This certificate is issued to facilitate recovery of dues/penalties imposed on the entity through prior adjudication proceedings.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9096 of 2026 issued against Swift Dealmark Private Limited (PAN: AAPCS1110D)
- Pertains to Appeal/Adjudication Proceeding No. 15407 of 2026
- Matter relates to illiquid stock options trading on stock exchanges
- Certificate issued by SEBI to enforce recovery of outstanding penalty amounts
- Part of a broader SEBI enforcement action involving multiple entities in the illiquid stock options matter

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement/recovery action under existing SEBI adjudication and recovery provisions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Swift Dealmark Private Limited is required to pay the outstanding penalty amount as specified in the Recovery Certificate
- Non-compliance with the Recovery Certificate may lead to further enforcement action including attachment of assets
- The entity must settle dues within the timeframe specified in the certificate

## Important Dates

- Certificate Date: June 4, 2026
- Adjudication Proceeding No.: A.P. 15407 of 2026

## Impact Assessment

This Recovery Certificate is part of SEBI's ongoing enforcement actions in the illiquid stock options matter, where multiple entities were found to have engaged in non-genuine or fictitious trades in the illiquid stock options segment. The impact is primarily on Swift Dealmark Private Limited, which faces financial liability for the penalty. The broader market impact is low as this is a targeted enforcement action against a specific entity rather than a policy or regulatory change.
