## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Attachment of Demat Accounts (No. 15385 of 2026) dated May 26, 2026, against Money Mishra Financial Services (Defaulter, PAN: AAZFM1357R). The attachment is ordered in the matter of unregistered portfolio management services rendered by Allied Financial Services Private Limited, and is executed under Recovery Certificate No. 9073 of 2026.

## Key Points

- Notice of Attachment of Demat Accounts No. 15385 of 2026 issued on May 26, 2026.
- Defaulter: Money Mishra Financial Services, PAN: AAZFM1357R.
- Recovery Certificate No. 9073 of 2026 is the basis for the attachment action.
- The underlying matter involves unregistered portfolio management services by Allied Financial Services Private Limited.
- SEBI is exercising its recovery powers to enforce dues against the defaulter.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is an enforcement/recovery action under existing SEBI recovery mechanisms.

## Compliance Requirements

- Depositories and depository participants are directed to attach the demat accounts held by or on behalf of Money Mishra Financial Services (PAN: AAZFM1357R) and prevent any debit transactions from the attached accounts.
- No transfers, pledges, or encumbrances are to be permitted on the attached demat accounts until further orders.

## Important Dates

- Notice date: May 26, 2026
- Recovery Certificate No. 9073 of 2026
- Attachment Notice No. 15385 of 2026

## Impact Assessment

This action is targeted at a specific defaulter, Money Mishra Financial Services, and has limited systemic market impact. It signals SEBI's continued enforcement against entities involved in unregistered portfolio management services. Investors who may have engaged with Money Mishra Financial Services or Allied Financial Services Private Limited should be aware of SEBI's recovery proceedings in this matter.
