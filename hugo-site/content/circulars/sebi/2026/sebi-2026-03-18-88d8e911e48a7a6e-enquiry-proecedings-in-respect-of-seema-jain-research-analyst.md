## Summary

SEBI conducted an inspection of Ms. Seema Jain (SEBI Registration No. INH100006667), a registered Research Analyst operating under the name 'StockPro', covering the period April 1, 2021 to October 31, 2022. The inspection revealed multiple regulatory violations. A Designated Authority (DA) was appointed, issued a show-cause notice on October 8, 2024, held a personal hearing on March 17, 2025, and submitted an Enquiry Report dated May 23, 2025 recommending suspension of the certificate of registration for one month.

## Key Points

- Seema Jain (INH100006667) found in violation of SEBI Act, 1992 and SEBI (Research Analysts) Regulations, 2014
- Inspection covered April 1, 2021 to October 31, 2022
- DA confirmed violations across all seven allegations raised in the show-cause notice
- Recommended penalty: suspension of certificate of registration for one month under Regulation 26(1)(iii) of Intermediaries Regulations, 2008
- Noticee was operating research analyst services in the name of 'StockPro', a partnership firm, despite being registered as an individual

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement order under existing provisions:
- Section 12(3) of the SEBI Act, 1992
- Regulation 27 of SEBI (Intermediaries) Regulations, 2008
- SEBI (Research Analysts) Regulations, 2014

## Compliance Requirements

Violations identified serve as compliance reminders for all registered Research Analysts:
- Must hold requisite certifications as mandated under RA Regulations
- Must maintain proper records as prescribed
- Must not trade during restricted periods
- Must publish research reports with adequate documentary basis
- Must provide all necessary disclosures in research reports
- Must obtain SCORES login credentials promptly and redress investor complaints in a timely manner
- Must not provide research analyst services through a partnership firm or entity if registered as an individual

## Important Dates

- Inspection period: April 1, 2021 – October 31, 2022
- Show-cause notice issued: October 8, 2024
- Noticee's reply: October 30, 2024
- Personal hearing: March 17, 2025
- DA Enquiry Report: May 23, 2025
- Order date: March 18, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The direct market impact is minimal as this action is limited to a single individual research analyst. However, the order reinforces SEBI's scrutiny of Research Analysts for certification compliance, record-keeping, trading restrictions, disclosure standards, and the prohibition on operating through entities other than the registered form. Other individual RAs operating informally through firms or partnerships should review their registration structure and compliance posture to avoid similar enforcement action.
