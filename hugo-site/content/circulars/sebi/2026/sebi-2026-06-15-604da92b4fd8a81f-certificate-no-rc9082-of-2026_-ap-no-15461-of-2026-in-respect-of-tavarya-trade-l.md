## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC9082 of 2026 under A.P. No. 15461 of 2026 against Tavarya Trade-Link Private Limited (PAN: AACCT5851H) for recovery of outstanding dues arising from enforcement proceedings in the matter of trading in Illiquid Stock Options.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9082 of 2026 issued by SEBI against Tavarya Trade-Link Private Limited
- PAN of the entity: AACCT5851H
- Application/Appeal reference: A.P. No. 15461 of 2026
- Matter pertains to the Illiquid Stock Options segment, where SEBI has historically taken action against entities involved in non-genuine/artificial trading
- Recovery certificate is a mechanism under Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992 enabling SEBI to recover dues as arrears of land revenue

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is an enforcement/recovery action pursuant to existing SEBI orders and penalty proceedings related to Illiquid Stock Options.

## Compliance Requirements

- Tavarya Trade-Link Private Limited is required to pay the amount specified in the recovery certificate
- Non-compliance may result in recovery through land revenue machinery as per Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992
- The entity or its authorised representatives must engage with SEBI and/or the designated recovery officer to settle the dues

## Important Dates

- Certificate Date: June 15, 2026
- Payment/compliance deadline: As specified in the recovery certificate (refer to the original document for exact deadline)

## Impact Assessment

This recovery certificate has a targeted impact on Tavarya Trade-Link Private Limited and does not directly affect broader market participants or operations. It forms part of SEBI's ongoing enforcement actions against entities found involved in creating artificial volumes or non-genuine trades in the Illiquid Stock Options segment of Indian exchanges. Market-wide impact is low; impact on the named entity is high given potential asset attachment risk under the recovery proceedings.
