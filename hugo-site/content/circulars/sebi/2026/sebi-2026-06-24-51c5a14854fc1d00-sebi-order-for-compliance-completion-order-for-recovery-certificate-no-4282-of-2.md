## Summary

SEBI has issued a Completion Order with respect to Recovery Certificate No. 4282 of 2021 against Naimish K. Shah in the matter of Tripex Overseas Ltd. This order signifies the conclusion of recovery proceedings, confirming that the dues under the recovery certificate have been satisfied or that the recovery process has been formally concluded.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 4282 of 2021 issued against Naimish K. Shah in the matter of Tripex Overseas Ltd. has been completed
- SEBI's recovery proceedings under this certificate are now formally closed
- The completion order is an administrative closure action with no prospective obligations for general market participants

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced by this order. It is an administrative completion/closure of an existing enforcement recovery proceeding.

## Compliance Requirements

This order is specific to Naimish K. Shah in the matter of Tripex Overseas Ltd. and does not impose any new compliance obligations on other market participants or intermediaries.

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate No. 4282 originally issued: 2021
- Completion Order date: June 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is an administrative order with no broader market impact. It marks the closure of a historical SEBI enforcement action related to Tripex Overseas Ltd. and has no implications for current trading, listing, or compliance obligations of other market participants.
