## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC9120 of 2026 under A.P. No. 15466 against Aashish Kumar Goenka (PAN: BOCPG5241L) for recovery of outstanding dues in the matter of Illiquid Stock Options. This certificate authorises SEBI to recover the amounts as arrears of land revenue.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9120 of 2026 issued by SEBI
- Appeal/Application No.: 15466
- Entity: Aashish Kumar Goenka (Individual)
- PAN: BOCPG5241L
- Matter: Illiquid Stock Options enforcement proceedings
- Certificate enables SEBI to recover dues as arrears of land revenue under Section 28A of the SEBI Act

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an enforcement/recovery action under existing SEBI powers.

## Compliance Requirements

- Aashish Kumar Goenka is required to pay the outstanding dues as directed by SEBI
- Failure to comply enables recovery through the district collector mechanism as arrears of land revenue

## Important Dates

- Certificate issued: June 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is an individual-specific enforcement action with no broader market impact. It forms part of SEBI's ongoing enforcement against participants found to have traded in illiquid stock options in a non-genuine manner. Impact is confined to the named individual.
