- Entity: Lalita Devi Sureka
- PAN: AJAPS4841C
- Matter: Illiquid Stock Options
- Document Type: Notice of Demand under recovery proceedings

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI recovery and enforcement regulations.

- Compliance with any additional directions or requirements specified in the notice

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This is an individual enforcement action with no direct impact on market operations or trading.
