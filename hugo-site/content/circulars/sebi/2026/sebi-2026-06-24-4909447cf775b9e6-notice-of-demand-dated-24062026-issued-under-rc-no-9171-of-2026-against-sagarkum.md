## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand under Recovery Certificate No. 9171 of 2026 against Sagarkumar Pravinchandra Dataniya (PAN: BJIPD7414F) of Ahmedabad, Gujarat, directing payment of Rs. 8,49,000 within 15 days. The demand arises from a penalty imposed by SEBI's Quasi-Judicial Authority vide Order No. QJA/MN/IVD/ID7/31947/2025-26 dated 31.12.2025 in connection with trading activities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited).

## Key Points

- Total amount due: Rs. 8,49,000/- (Rupees Eight Lakh Forty-Nine Thousand Only)
- Principal penalty: Rs. 8,00,000/- imposed by Quasi-Judicial Authority Order No. QJA/MN/IVD/ID7/31947/2025-26 dated 31.12.2025
- Interest accrued (January 2026 to June 2026 @ 1% per month): Rs. 48,000/-
- Recovery cost: Rs. 1,000/-
- Payment required within 15 days of receipt of notice
- Payment to be made to A/c No. SEBIRRDPEN9171, ICICI Bank, IFSC: ICIC0000106 via EFT/NEFT/RTGS or via SEBI's online Recovery Payment portal at https://siportal.sebi.gov.in

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an enforcement/recovery action pursuant to:
- Section 28A of the Securities and Exchange Board of India Act, 1992
- Section 222 of the Income Tax Act, 1961
- Rule 2 of the Second Schedule to the Income-tax Act, 1961

## Compliance Requirements

- Sagarkumar Pravinchandra Dataniya must pay Rs. 8,49,000/- within 15 days of receipt of this notice.
- Payment confirmation (including case name, RC number, payee name, date, amount, transaction number, and bank details) must be forwarded to the Recovery Officer at SEBI Bhavan-II, Mumbai, or emailed to akashb@sebi.gov.in and recoveryho1@sebi.gov.in.
- The noticee is prohibited from mortgaging, charging, leasing, or otherwise dealing with any property without prior permission of the Recovery Officer upon service of this notice; any such transfer shall be deemed void under Rule 16 of the Second Schedule to the Income-tax Act, 1961.
- Any transfer of property or bank funds to spouse, minor child, son's wife, or son's minor child without adequate consideration on or after 31.12.2025 shall be deemed the noticee's property for recovery purposes.

## Important Dates

- Original penalty order date: 31.12.2025 (Order No. QJA/MN/IVD/ID7/31947/2025-26)
- Interest calculation period: January 2026 to June 2026
- Notice of Demand date: 24.06.2026
- Payment deadline: Within 15 days of receipt of this notice

## Impact Assessment

This is an individual enforcement action with limited broader market impact. The penalty relates to alleged trading violations in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited). Failure to pay within the stipulated period may result in coercive recovery measures including attachment and sale of movable and immovable property, attachment of bank accounts, and arrest and detention in prison. Market participants involved in similar trading patterns in the referenced scrip should take note of SEBI's continued enforcement activity in this matter.
