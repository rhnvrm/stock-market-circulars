## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC9051 of 2026 under Application No. 15397 of 2026 against Vineeta Kabra (PAN: ABNPL7384C) for recovery of outstanding dues in connection with the Illiquid Stock Options matter. This certificate authorises recovery proceedings to enforce payment of penalties or disgorgement amounts previously ordered by SEBI.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9051 of 2026 issued against Vineeta Kabra (PAN: ABNPL7384C)
- Application (A.P.) No. 15397 of 2026 filed before the Recovery Officer
- Matter relates to trading in Illiquid Stock Options — a large-scale SEBI enforcement action involving artificial creation of losses/gains via illiquid stock option contracts
- Recovery proceedings authorised to enforce payment of penalties, disgorgement, or interest as directed in prior SEBI orders

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an enforcement/recovery action implementing an existing SEBI order.

## Compliance Requirements

- Vineeta Kabra is required to pay the outstanding dues specified in the Recovery Certificate
- Banks, depositories, and other entities holding assets of Vineeta Kabra may be directed to attach or remit funds pursuant to this certificate
- Non-compliance with recovery proceedings may result in further enforcement action

## Important Dates

- Certificate Date: June 2026
- Recovery Certificate No.: RC9051 of 2026
- Application No.: A.P. 15397 of 2026

## Impact Assessment

This order has limited broader market impact. It is directed solely at the named individual, Vineeta Kabra, as part of SEBI's ongoing enforcement sweep in the Illiquid Stock Options matter. The Illiquid Stock Options case is one of SEBI's largest enforcement actions, involving hundreds of entities that allegedly used illiquid stock option contracts on exchanges to generate fictitious long-term capital gains or losses for tax evasion purposes. Recovery certificates of this nature are routine enforcement tools used to collect dues from adjudicated parties who have not voluntarily complied with SEBI's monetary orders.
