## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand dated June 29, 2026 under Recovery Certificate (RC) No. 9178 of 2026 against Madhu Kumari Bairwa. The notice pertains to trading activities of certain entities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited, now known as DU Digital Global Limited. This is a recovery proceeding initiated by SEBI to enforce payment of dues established through prior regulatory orders.

## Key Points

- Notice of Demand issued on June 29, 2026 under RC No. 9178 of 2026
- Action is directed against Madhu Kumari Bairwa
- Related to trading activities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited)
- Represents a recovery/enforcement action following SEBI's earlier adjudication proceedings
- SEBI is exercising its powers to recover outstanding dues from the named entity

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced by this notice. It is an enforcement action under existing SEBI recovery and adjudication framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Madhu Kumari Bairwa is required to pay the demanded amount as specified in the Recovery Certificate No. 9178 of 2026
- Failure to comply may result in attachment of bank accounts and/or demat accounts
- The noticee must respond to SEBI within the stipulated timeframe as specified in the notice

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** June 29, 2026
- **RC No.:** 9178 of 2026
- Response/payment deadline: As specified in the underlying Recovery Certificate (refer to the original order for exact deadline)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has a limited broader market impact as it is directed at a specific individual (Madhu Kumari Bairwa) for trading violations in DU Digital Global Limited. The scrip involved (DU Digital Global Limited, formerly DU Digital Technologies Limited) may see some attention from market participants monitoring SEBI enforcement actions. The notice signals continued SEBI vigilance over trading activities in this particular scrip, which may deter similar violations by other market participants.
