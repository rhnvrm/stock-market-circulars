## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC9142 of 2026, constituting a Notice of Demand against Agomoni Commercial Private Limited (PAN: AAHCA2663H), requiring recovery of dues established in SEBI's enforcement proceedings related to manipulation in the Illiquid Stock Options segment.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9142 of 2026 issued against Agomoni Commercial Private Limited.
- PAN of the noticee: AAHCA2663H.
- The notice arises out of SEBI's enforcement action in the matter of Illiquid Stock Options.
- SEBI is proceeding to recover outstanding dues (penalty/disgorgement) from the entity as directed by earlier order(s).
- The certificate is issued under Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992, which empowers SEBI to recover amounts as arrears of land revenue.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced by this document. It is an enforcement/recovery instrument implementing a prior SEBI order.

## Compliance Requirements

- Agomoni Commercial Private Limited is required to pay the demanded amount specified in the recovery certificate within the prescribed time.
- Failure to comply may result in coercive recovery proceedings including attachment of assets, bank accounts, and other properties.
- The entity or its authorised representatives should refer to the original SEBI order for the exact quantum of dues and respond to the Recovering Officer accordingly.

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate issued: June 2026.
- Deadline for payment: As specified in the recovery certificate (refer to the original PDF for the exact date).

## Impact Assessment

This notice of demand has direct financial impact on Agomoni Commercial Private Limited, which faces mandatory recovery of SEBI-imposed dues. The broader market impact is negligible as this is an entity-specific enforcement action in the ongoing crackdown on illiquid stock options manipulation. Investors and market participants are not directly affected, though the action reinforces SEBI's continued enforcement posture against manipulation in the illiquid options segment.
