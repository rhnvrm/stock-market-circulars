## Summary

SEBI has issued a Remittance Advice against Pankaj Kumar Aggarwal HUF (PAN: AAOHP0665E), designated as a defaulter, in the matter of ISO (Inspection of Stock Brokers) at BSE. The action is taken under Recovery Certificate No. 8199 of 2024, directing remittance of outstanding dues owed to SEBI.

## Key Points

- Defaulter: Pankaj Kumar Aggarwal HUF
- PAN: AAOHP0665E
- Matter: ISO at BSE (Pankaj Kumar Aggarwal HUF)
- Recovery Certificate No.: 8199 of 2024
- Document type: Remittance Advice — a formal direction to remit/pay outstanding dues
- Issued by SEBI as part of its enforcement and recovery proceedings

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an enforcement document directing payment of dues under an existing recovery certificate.

## Compliance Requirements

- The defaulter, Pankaj Kumar Aggarwal HUF, is required to remit the outstanding amount as specified in the remittance advice.
- Non-compliance may result in further enforcement actions including attachment of assets.

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate No. 8199 issued: 2024
- Remittance Advice issued: May 2026

## Impact Assessment

This action is limited in market impact as it targets a single HUF entity. It reflects SEBI's ongoing enforcement of recovery proceedings against defaulters identified through stock broker inspections at BSE. No broader market, trading, or systemic implications are anticipated.
