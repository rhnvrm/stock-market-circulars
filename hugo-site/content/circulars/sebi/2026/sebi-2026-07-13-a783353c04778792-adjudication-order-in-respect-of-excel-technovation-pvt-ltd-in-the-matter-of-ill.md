## Summary

SEBI's Adjudicating Officer issued an order against Excel Technovation Pvt Ltd (PAN: AAACE3703B) for allegedly executing non-genuine reversal trades in illiquid stock options on BSE. The investigation covered the period April 1, 2014 to September 30, 2015, during which SEBI found that 2,91,744 trades (81.40% of all trades in BSE's stock options segment) were allegedly non-genuine, creating artificial trading volumes. The Noticee was found to have executed 46 such trades across 23 stock options contracts, resulting in artificial volume of 12,92,000 units, allegedly in violation of the SEBI (Prohibition of Fraudulent and Unfair Trade Practices Relating to Securities Market) Regulations, 2003 (PFUTP Regulations).

## Key Points

- SEBI investigated large-scale reversal trading in BSE's stock options segment during the Investigation Period (IP) of April 1, 2014 to September 30, 2015.
- 2,91,744 trades (81.40% of total trades in the segment) were identified as allegedly non-genuine, creating artificial volume.
- Excel Technovation Pvt Ltd was identified as one of several entities involved in such reversal trades.
- The Noticee allegedly executed 46 non-genuine trades in 23 stock options contracts, generating artificial volume of 12,92,000 units.
- Alleged violations: Regulations 3(a), (b), (c), (d), 4(1) and 4(2)(a) of the PFUTP Regulations, 2003.
- Ms. Soma Majumder was appointed as the Adjudicating Officer following a transfer of the case (order dated April 3, 2025).
- The Noticee is under liquidation; a liquidator was appointed by the Hon'ble NCLT, Jaipur Bench, via order dated October 31, 2023.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes were introduced; this is an enforcement action applying existing PFUTP Regulations, 2003 to conduct that occurred during 2014-2015.

## Compliance Requirements

- The liquidator of Excel Technovation Pvt Ltd was required to respond to the Show Cause Notice (SCN) and engage with the adjudication proceedings on behalf of the company under liquidation.
- Entities under liquidation remain subject to SEBI adjudication proceedings for historical conduct despite insolvency proceedings before the NCLT.

## Important Dates

- Investigation Period: April 1, 2014 to September 30, 2015
- Show Cause Notice issued: August 11, 2022 (served August 18, 2022)
- NCLT order appointing liquidator: October 31, 2023
- Post SCN Intimation issued: March 22, 2024
- Liquidator's response: April 15, 2024 (letter) and April 20, 2024 (email)
- AO appointment (transfer of case): April 3, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This order is part of SEBI's broader enforcement drive against non-genuine reversal trading in illiquid stock options on BSE during 2014-2015, a pattern involving numerous entities. Since Excel Technovation Pvt Ltd is undergoing liquidation, the practical impact of any penalty imposed will depend on the outcome of insolvency proceedings and available assets. The order does not directly affect any currently listed securities or ongoing market operations, but reinforces SEBI's continued scrutiny of historical market manipulation in derivatives segments and serves as a precedent for adjudicating entities that have since entered insolvency.
