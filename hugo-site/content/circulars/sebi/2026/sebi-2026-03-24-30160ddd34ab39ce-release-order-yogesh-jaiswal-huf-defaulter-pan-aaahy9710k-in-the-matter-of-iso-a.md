## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order for YOGESH JAISWAL HUF (PAN: AAAHY9710K), a named defaulter in the matter of ISO (Investor Service Operations) at BSE, under Recovery Certificate No. 9033 of 2026. The release order formally discharges the defaulter from enforcement/attachment measures that were in place under the recovery certificate, signifying that the outstanding dues have been recovered or the proceedings have otherwise been concluded.

## Key Points

- Release Order issued against YOGESH JAISWAL HUF, a Hindu Undivided Family (HUF) entity, with PAN AAAHY9710K.
- The release pertains to Recovery Certificate No. 9033 of 2026 in the matter of ISO at BSE.
- The order formally releases the defaulter from recovery/attachment proceedings initiated by SEBI.
- Issued by SEBI's enforcement/recovery division on March 24, 2026.
- Related to the completion of Recovery Certificate No. 9033 of 2026 (originally dated February 12, 2026).

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a routine administrative enforcement action issuing a formal release from recovery proceedings for a specific named defaulter.

## Compliance Requirements

- No new compliance requirements for general market participants.
- The order is specific to YOGESH JAISWAL HUF (PAN: AAAHY9710K).
- The release order signifies the formal closure of attachment/recovery enforcement measures against the named defaulter.

## Important Dates

- **February 12, 2026**: Recovery Certificate No. 9033 of 2026 originally issued against YOGESH JAISWAL HUF.
- **March 24, 2026**: Release Order issued, formally releasing the defaulter from recovery proceedings.

## Impact Assessment

This order has minimal market impact. It concerns a specific individual/HUF entity (YOGESH JAISWAL HUF) in the context of ISO proceedings at BSE and represents the administrative conclusion of a recovery action. The release order confirms that SEBI's enforcement objective under RC No. 9033 of 2026 has been achieved. No implications for broader market operations, trading, or listed companies.
