## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC9076 of 2026 under A.P. No. 15462 of 2026 against Allnew Distributors Private Limited (PAN: AAKCA2105M) in connection with the matter of Illiquid Stock Options. This certificate authorises recovery of outstanding dues — penalties, disgorgement, or both — that the entity has failed to pay following adjudication proceedings.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9076 of 2026 issued by SEBI against Allnew Distributors Private Limited.
- PAN of the noticee: AAKCA2105M.
- Proceedings reference: A.P. No. 15462 of 2026.
- Matter pertains to violations in the Illiquid Stock Options segment.
- A recovery certificate is issued when an entity defaults on payment of SEBI-imposed monetary penalties or disgorgement orders.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This document is an enforcement instrument issued under SEBI's existing powers to recover dues from defaulting entities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Allnew Distributors Private Limited is required to pay the outstanding amount specified in the recovery certificate immediately.
- Failure to comply may result in attachment of assets and further enforcement action by SEBI or authorised recovery officers.
- Other market participants engaged in illiquid stock options trading should ensure their activities comply with SEBI regulations to avoid similar enforcement.

## Important Dates

- Certificate issued: June 2026.
- Payment obligation: Immediate upon receipt of the recovery certificate.

## Impact Assessment

This action has limited broader market impact as it targets a single entity — Allnew Distributors Private Limited — for past violations in the Illiquid Stock Options segment. However, it reflects SEBI's continued enforcement drive against entities that engaged in manipulative or non-compliant trading in illiquid stock options on exchanges such as BSE. Distributors and intermediaries involved in similar practices should treat this as a regulatory signal of ongoing scrutiny.
