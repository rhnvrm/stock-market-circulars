## Summary

SEBI issued a Notice of Demand under Recovery Certificate number 9124 of 2026, dated May 25, 2026, to Mr. Timothy Alfred Joseph Moses (PAN: AAXPM5649J) in the matter of Mediaone Global Entertainment Limited. Mr. Moses has been identified as a defaulter and is required to pay the outstanding dues as specified under the recovery certificate.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate number 9124 of 2026 issued against Mr. Timothy Alfred Joseph Moses (PAN: AAXPM5649J)
- Mr. Moses is designated as a Defaulter in this matter
- The notice pertains to the matter of Mediaone Global Entertainment Limited
- Notice of Demand issued on May 25, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI recovery mechanisms.

## Compliance Requirements

- Mr. Timothy Alfred Joseph Moses is required to pay the outstanding dues specified under Recovery Certificate number 9124 of 2026
- Payment must be made as directed in the notice of demand
- Failure to comply may result in further enforcement action by SEBI

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate date: May 25, 2026
- Notice of Demand issued: May 25, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is an individual-level enforcement action with limited broader market impact. The action is directed specifically at Mr. Timothy Alfred Joseph Moses in connection with the matter of Mediaone Global Entertainment Limited. It reflects SEBI's ongoing enforcement of recovery of dues from defaulters identified in prior proceedings.
