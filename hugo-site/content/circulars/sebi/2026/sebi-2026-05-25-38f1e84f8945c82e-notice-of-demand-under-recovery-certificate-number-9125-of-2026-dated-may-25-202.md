## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand under Recovery Certificate number 9125 of 2026, dated May 25, 2026, to Mr. M Srinivas Kumar (PAN: AAIPK6962H), designated as a defaulter in the matter of Mediaone Global Entertainment Limited. The notice directs the defaulter to pay the outstanding dues recoverable under the certificate within the stipulated period.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate number 9125 of 2026 was issued by SEBI on May 25, 2026.
- The notice is directed at Mr. M Srinivas Kumar (PAN: AAIPK6962H), identified as a defaulter.
- The matter pertains to Mediaone Global Entertainment Limited.
- SEBI is exercising its recovery powers under applicable regulations to enforce payment of outstanding dues.
- Non-compliance with the notice may result in further enforcement action, including attachment of assets.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI recovery and demand provisions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Mr. M Srinivas Kumar is required to pay the outstanding amount specified under Recovery Certificate number 9125 of 2026 within the deadline prescribed in the notice.
- Failure to comply may lead to coercive recovery measures including attachment and sale of movable and immovable property.

## Important Dates

- **Recovery Certificate Date:** May 25, 2026
- **Notice of Demand Date:** May 25, 2026
- Payment deadline: As specified in the notice served to the defaulter.

## Impact Assessment

This is an individual enforcement action with no direct broad market impact. It signals SEBI's continued pursuit of outstanding dues from defaulters in historical enforcement matters. Market participants and intermediaries associated with Mediaone Global Entertainment Limited should note SEBI's active recovery proceedings.
