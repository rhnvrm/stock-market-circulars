## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order dated June 19, 2026 under Recovery Certificate (RC) No. 8446 of 2024 in the matter of Citrus Check Inns Limited. A Release Order in SEBI's recovery proceedings typically indicates that previously attached properties, bank accounts, or securities of the concerned party are being released, generally following the satisfaction of outstanding dues, penalties, or disgorgement amounts imposed by SEBI.

## Key Points

- SEBI issued this Release Order on June 19, 2026 under RC No. 8446 of 2024
- The matter concerns Citrus Check Inns Limited
- A Recovery Certificate (RC) is issued by SEBI when monetary dues (penalties/disgorgement) are not paid voluntarily, enabling SEBI to recover amounts as arrears of land revenue
- A Release Order follows satisfaction of the recovery dues, resulting in de-attachment of frozen assets
- This order marks a resolution stage in SEBI's enforcement action against Citrus Check Inns Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced by this order. It is an administrative/enforcement action specific to Citrus Check Inns Limited under SEBI's existing recovery framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- The attached assets, bank accounts, or securities of Citrus Check Inns Limited (and/or associated persons) are to be released by the concerned authorities upon receipt of this order
- Banks, depositories, or other entities holding attached assets are required to process the release as directed

## Important Dates

- **Order Date:** June 19, 2026
- **RC No.:** 8446 of 2024 (Recovery Certificate issued in 2024)

## Impact Assessment

This order has limited broader market impact. It is company-specific and signals the resolution or partial resolution of an enforcement action against Citrus Check Inns Limited. Investors and market participants in Citrus Check Inns Limited should note that SEBI's recovery proceedings in this matter are progressing toward closure. No trading restrictions or market-wide implications are expected from this release order.
