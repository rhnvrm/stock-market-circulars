## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand (Certificate No. 9172 of 2026) against Dhaval Girishbhai Parmar (PAN: EPZPP2397F) under Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992 read with Section 222 of the Income Tax Act, 1961. The demand is for recovery of Rs. 6,37,000 comprising a penalty, accrued interest, and recovery costs related to trading activities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited).

## Key Points

- Notice issued under RC No. 9172 of 2026 against Dhaval Girishbhai Parmar, Ahmedabad.
- Penalty of Rs. 6,00,000 imposed vide Quasi-Judicial Order No. QJA/MN/IVD/ID7/31947/2025-26 dated 31.12.2025.
- Additional interest of Rs. 36,000 levied at 1% per month for January–June 2026.
- Recovery cost of Rs. 1,000 included; total demand is Rs. 6,37,000.
- Payment must be made within 15 days of receipt of the notice via EFT/NEFT/RTGS or SEBI's online recovery portal.
- In case of non-payment, SEBI may proceed with attachment and sale of movable/immovable property, attachment of bank accounts, or arrest and detention.
- Any transfer of property to spouse, minor child, son's wife, or son's minor child without adequate consideration on or after 31.12.2025 will be deemed the defaulter's property for recovery purposes.
- Subject is prohibited from mortgaging, charging, or dealing with any property without prior permission of the Recovery Officer from the date of service of this notice.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This notice enforces an existing quasi-judicial order dated 31.12.2025 through the recovery mechanism under Section 28A of the SEBI Act.

## Compliance Requirements

- Dhaval Girishbhai Parmar must pay Rs. 6,37,000 within 15 days of receipt of this notice.
- Payment via NEFT/RTGS to A/c No. SEBIRRDPEN9172, ICICI Bank, IFSC: ICIC0000106, or through SEBI's online portal at https://siportal.sebi.gov.in.
- Confirmation of e-payment must be forwarded to the Recovery Officer at SEBI Bhavan-II, Mumbai, or emailed to akashb@sebi.gov.in and recoveryho1@sebi.gov.in.
- No transfer or encumbrance of any property is permitted without the Recovery Officer's consent.

## Important Dates

- Underlying penalty order date: 31.12.2025
- Interest accrual period: January 2026 to June 2026
- Notice date: 24.06.2026
- Payment deadline: Within 15 days of receipt of this notice

## Impact Assessment

This is an individual enforcement action with limited direct market impact. It reflects SEBI's continued pursuit of recovery against entities penalised for irregular trading activities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited). Investors in or around this scrip should note the ongoing regulatory scrutiny. The use of Section 28A enforcement powers, including potential arrest and property attachment, underscores SEBI's intent to enforce compliance with its quasi-judicial orders.
