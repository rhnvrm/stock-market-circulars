## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand under Recovery Certificate No. 9160 of 2026 dated June 15, 2026 against Parmanand Yadav, proprietor of Roxtech Securities Services (PAN: AIMPY3580D), designated as the defaulter. The notice pertains to trading activities of certain entities in the scrip of Delta Leasing and Finance Ltd. and demands recovery of dues pursuant to SEBI's enforcement proceedings.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 9160 of 2026 issued by SEBI on June 15, 2026
- Defaulter: Parmanand Yadav, Proprietor of Roxtech Securities Services (PAN: AIMPY3580D)
- Matter relates to trading activities of certain entities in the scrip of Delta Leasing and Finance Ltd.
- Notice of Demand formally directs the defaulter to pay outstanding dues to SEBI
- Non-compliance with the recovery certificate may invite further enforcement action including attachment of assets

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is an enforcement/recovery action under existing SEBI recovery mechanisms pursuant to prior adjudication or settlement proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

- The defaulter (Parmanand Yadav / Roxtech Securities Services) is required to pay the demanded amount as specified in Recovery Certificate No. 9160 of 2026
- Payment must be made to SEBI within the timeline stipulated in the notice
- Failure to comply may result in attachment and sale of movable and immovable properties of the defaulter

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate Date: June 15, 2026
- Notice of Demand Date: June 15, 2026
- Payment deadline: As specified in the notice (refer to the original document for the exact deadline)

## Impact Assessment

This enforcement action has minimal broader market impact and is directed at a specific individual and proprietary firm. It signals SEBI's continued pursuit of recovery from entities found to have engaged in irregular trading activities in the scrip of Delta Leasing and Finance Ltd. Investors and market participants in that scrip should be aware of the regulatory scrutiny surrounding historical trading in the stock.
