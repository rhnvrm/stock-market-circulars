## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order in the matter of Hindustan Breweries and Bottling Ltd. (PAN: AAACH0104M), a defaulter, concerning non-redressal of investor grievances. The release order pertains to Recovery Certificate No. 2535 of 2019, originally issued on September 12, 2019. This order signifies the conclusion of recovery proceedings initiated against the company.

## Key Points

- SEBI issued a Release Order for Hindustan Breweries and Bottling Ltd. (Defaulter), PAN: AAACH0104M
- The release order relates to Recovery Certificate No. 2535 of 2019 (dated September 12, 2019)
- The matter pertains to non-redressal of investor grievances by the company
- The release order indicates that recovery proceedings under this certificate have been addressed or concluded
- The order was published under SEBI's enforcement/recovery proceedings dated March 20, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced by this order. It is an administrative enforcement action concluding prior recovery proceedings against a specific defaulter.

## Compliance Requirements

- No new compliance requirements for the broader market or listed entities
- Hindustan Breweries and Bottling Ltd. must comply with the terms of the release order
- The company is expected to have fulfilled the obligations that triggered the original recovery certificate

## Important Dates

- **Recovery Certificate Date:** September 12, 2019 (Certificate No. 2535 of 2019)
- **Release Order Date:** March 20, 2026
- **Publication Date:** March 20, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This release order has minimal broader market impact. It is specific to Hindustan Breweries and Bottling Ltd. and its investors who had pending grievances. The issuance of a release order typically indicates that the outstanding dues or obligations under the recovery certificate have been satisfied, providing relief to the defaulter entity. Investors who had filed grievances against the company may benefit from the resolution of this enforcement action.
