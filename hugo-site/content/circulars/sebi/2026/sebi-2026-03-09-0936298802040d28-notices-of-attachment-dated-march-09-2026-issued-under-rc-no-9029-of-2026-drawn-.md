## Summary

SEBI issued a Notice of Attachment dated March 09, 2026 under Recovery Certificate (RC) No. 9029 of 2026 against Rajnikant Nathalal Kananni (HUF). The notice relates to the entity's involvement in dealings in illiquid stock options at BSE. This is an enforcement action to recover dues pursuant to an adjudication order or settlement previously issued by SEBI.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 9029 of 2026 drawn against Rajnikant Nathalal Kananni (HUF)
- Attachment notice dated March 09, 2026
- Matter pertains to dealings in illiquid stock options at BSE
- Action taken under SEBI's recovery proceedings to enforce payment of dues
- The HUF is the named respondent/noticee

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is an enforcement/recovery action under existing SEBI regulations and the Securities and Exchange Board of India Act.

## Compliance Requirements

- The noticee (Rajnikant Nathalal Kananni HUF) is required to comply with the attachment order
- Banks, depositories, or other financial intermediaries holding assets of the noticee may be directed to freeze or attach such assets
- Non-compliance with attachment orders may lead to further enforcement action by SEBI

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** March 09, 2026
- **RC Number:** 9029 of 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a targeted enforcement action against a single HUF entity and has no direct impact on broader markets, listed companies, or market participants. It reflects SEBI's continued enforcement actions against entities found to have engaged in manipulative or non-compliant trading in illiquid stock options at BSE. Market impact is negligible.
