## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC9097 of 2026 (A.P. No. 15408 of 2026) against Basundhara Marcom Pvt. Ltd. (PAN: AAECB7768B) for recovery of dues arising from enforcement proceedings in the matter of illiquid stock options.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9097 of 2026 issued by SEBI against Basundhara Marcom Pvt. Ltd.
- PAN of the entity: AAECB7768B
- Application/Appeal No. 15408 of 2026 under which the certificate is issued
- Relates to SEBI's enforcement action in the matter of illiquid stock options segment
- Recovery Certificate enables SEBI to recover penalty/dues as arrears of land revenue

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement/recovery action under existing SEBI regulations and the SEBI Act, which empowers SEBI to issue Recovery Certificates for collection of dues.

## Compliance Requirements

- Basundhara Marcom Pvt. Ltd. is required to pay the outstanding dues as specified in the Recovery Certificate
- The certificate is enforceable as arrears of land revenue under Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992
- Non-payment may result in coercive recovery proceedings through the concerned district authority

## Important Dates

- Certificate Issue Date: June 4, 2026
- Recovery Certificate No.: RC9097 of 2026
- A.P. No.: 15408 of 2026

## Impact Assessment

This Recovery Certificate is targeted solely at Basundhara Marcom Pvt. Ltd. and has no broader market impact. It is part of SEBI's ongoing enforcement drive against entities that engaged in irregular/manipulative trading in the illiquid stock options segment on Indian exchanges. The issuance of recovery certificates in bulk (multiple RCs issued on the same date) signals continued regulatory action in this segment.
