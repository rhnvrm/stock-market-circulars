## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC9142 of 2026 under Appeal Proceeding No. 15495 of 2026 against Agomoni Commercial Private Limited (PAN: AAHCA2663H). The matter pertains to the entity's involvement in Illiquid Stock Options, an area where SEBI has undertaken significant enforcement action against fraudulent and manipulative trading practices.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9142 of 2026 issued by SEBI
- Appeal Proceeding (A.P.) No. 15495 of 2026
- Entity: Agomoni Commercial Private Limited (PAN: AAHCA2663H)
- Matter: Illiquid Stock Options — a key enforcement area for SEBI involving alleged manipulation in thinly traded options
- This certificate enables SEBI to recover penalty/dues from the named entity as per its enforcement powers

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This document represents an enforcement and recovery action under existing SEBI regulations governing market manipulation and fraudulent trading in illiquid stock options.

## Compliance Requirements

- Agomoni Commercial Private Limited is required to comply with the recovery certificate and settle dues as directed by SEBI
- Non-compliance with a recovery certificate may result in further enforcement action, including asset attachment and prosecution
- Other market participants involved in illiquid stock options should ensure their trading activity complies with SEBI regulations to avoid similar enforcement

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate issued: June 2026
- A.P. No. 15495 of 2026 refers to the appeal proceeding filed in 2026

## Impact Assessment

This action is part of SEBI's ongoing enforcement drive against entities found to have engaged in fraudulent trading in illiquid stock options on Indian exchanges. The direct market impact is limited to the named entity; however, it signals continued regulatory vigilance in the illiquid options segment. Other entities that participated in similar trading patterns in illiquid stock options remain at risk of comparable enforcement actions.
