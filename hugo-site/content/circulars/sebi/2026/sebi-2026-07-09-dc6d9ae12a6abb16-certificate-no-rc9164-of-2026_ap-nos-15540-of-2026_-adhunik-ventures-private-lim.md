## Summary

SEBI's Recovery Officer has issued Certificate No. RC9164 of 2026 (Attachment Proceedings No. 15540 of 2026) against Adhunik Ventures Private Limited (PAN: AAHCA7165L). The certificate initiates formal recovery proceedings to recover outstanding dues owed by the entity, arising from an earlier SEBI enforcement order relating to trading activity in the illiquid stock options segment.

## Key Points

- Recovery certificate issued against Adhunik Ventures Private Limited (PAN: AAHCA7165L).
- Certificate No.: RC9164 of 2026; Attachment Proceedings No.: 15540 of 2026.
- Matter pertains to prior SEBI enforcement findings on trading in illiquid stock options.
- Certificate empowers the Recovery Officer to proceed with recovery of unpaid dues (e.g., penalty and/or disgorgement amounts) under applicable recovery rules.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is an enforcement/recovery action under existing SEBI recovery proceedings framework (Section 28A of the SEBI Act read with the Second and Third Schedules to the Income Tax Act, 1961, and Income Tax Certificate Proceedings Rules, 1962, as applied by SEBI), not a new rule or policy change.

## Compliance Requirements

The named entity, Adhunik Ventures Private Limited, is required to settle the outstanding dues specified in the underlying enforcement/demand order. Failure to pay may lead to coercive recovery measures available to the Recovery Officer, such as attachment and sale of movable/immovable property, attachment of bank accounts, or other measures permitted under the recovery rules.

## Important Dates

Certificate dated 2026 (specific day not extractable from the scanned document); exact payment deadline and underlying order date are not discernible from the available document text.

## Impact Assessment

This is a targeted enforcement/recovery action against a single private entity and does not have direct implications for listed securities, market participants generally, or trading operations. Its relevance is limited to the named entity and any counterparties involved in the underlying illiquid stock options case.
