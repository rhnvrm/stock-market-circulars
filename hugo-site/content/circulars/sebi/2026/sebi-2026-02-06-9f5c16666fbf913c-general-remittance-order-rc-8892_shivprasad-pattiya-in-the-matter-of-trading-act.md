## Summary

SEBI has issued General Remittance Order RC-8892 against Shivprasad Pattiya in connection with trading activities of a certain group of clients in stock options based out of Betul and Indore. This order is part of SEBI's enforcement mechanism for recovery of dues from entities found in violation of securities regulations.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No: RC-8892
- Entity: Shivprasad Pattiya
- Type: General Remittance Order
- Location: Betul, Indore
- Subject Matter: Trading activities of certain group of clients in stock options
- This is an enforcement action document for recovery proceedings

## Regulatory Changes

This is an enforcement order rather than a regulatory change. It represents SEBI's action to recover dues from entities involved in violations related to trading activities in stock options.

## Compliance Requirements

The entity named in the order (Shivprasad Pattiya) is required to:
- Comply with the remittance order
- Pay the amounts specified in the recovery certificate
- Cooperate with SEBI's recovery proceedings

## Important Dates

- Order Date: February 6, 2026
- Document Reference: RC-8892

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** High - This order is part of SEBI's ongoing enforcement actions against irregular trading activities in derivatives segment, particularly stock options. Such actions signal regulatory scrutiny on coordinated trading patterns.

**Operational Impact:** High - The issuance of a recovery certificate indicates that previous orders or penalties have led to recovery proceedings, suggesting non-compliance or inability to pay.

**Regulatory Significance:** This order is part of a series of enforcement actions by SEBI against entities involved in manipulative or fraudulent trading activities in the options segment, demonstrating the regulator's commitment to maintaining market integrity.
