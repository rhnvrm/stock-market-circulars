## Summary

SEBI has issued a compliance order releasing Recovery Certificate No. 6284 of 2025, which had been issued against Ashi Texfab Pvt Ltd in connection with recovery proceedings arising out of the matter of Excel Castronics Limited. The release order indicates that the recovery proceedings against this entity under the referenced certificate have been concluded, typically following satisfaction of the outstanding dues, penalty, or disgorgement amount for which the certificate was originally issued.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 6284 of 2025 was issued against Ashi Texfab Pvt Ltd as part of SEBI's enforcement action in the matter of Excel Castronics Limited.
- SEBI's Recovery Officer has now ordered the release of this recovery certificate, effectively closing the recovery proceedings against the named entity.
- The order is administrative/enforcement in nature and pertains to a single named entity rather than the listed company or its securities generally.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced by this order. It is a case-specific enforcement action closing out a recovery certificate issued under SEBI's recovery proceedings framework (typically invoked under the Income Tax Act's recovery provisions as applied via SEBI Recovery of Dues Rules).

## Compliance Requirements

There are no new compliance requirements arising for listed companies, intermediaries, or market participants from this order. The order only affects the named entity, Ashi Texfab Pvt Ltd, whose recovery certificate stands released.

## Important Dates

The release order was issued on July 2, 2026. No further compliance deadlines are prescribed for market participants.

## Impact Assessment

This order has negligible impact on the broader market, on Excel Castronics Limited as a listed entity, or on its shareholders. It reflects the closure of an individual recovery proceeding and is primarily of record-keeping and enforcement-tracking relevance.
