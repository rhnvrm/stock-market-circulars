## Summary

SEBI issued an order under Section 12(3) of the SEBI Act, 1992 read with Regulation 30A of the SEBI (Intermediaries) Regulations, 2008 against 15 Noticees registered as Research Analysts under the SEBI (Research Analysts) Regulations, 2014. The order finds that each Noticee failed to pay the mandatory renewal fee due every five years from the date of grant of registration, as required under Clause 3 of the Second Schedule to the SEBI (Research Analysts) Regulations, 2014, despite the fee being required to be paid within three months before expiry of the period for which it was last paid.

## Key Points

- 15 Research Analysts (individuals and entities) named as Noticees, including Bipin Manilal Savla, Comfort Securities Ltd., Sudip Bandyopadhyay, Shefali Sahita, Prasenjit Kumar Paul, Arya Fin-Trade Services (India) Pvt. Ltd., Ideal Associates, Kalpesh Rathi, Deepa Gupta, Abhijeet Ramachandrran, Rashi, Rahul Mukesh Randeria, Shivansh Kariwala, Krishnamurthy Sethuraman, and K Karthik Raja.
- Each Noticee's SEBI registration number, PAN, and original date of registration are listed, ranging from 2015 to 2017.
- All Noticees failed to pay the renewal fee due five years after their respective registration dates (e.g., renewal due from 16-Jun-2020 for Noticee 1, 12-Aug-2020 for Noticee 2, and so on).
- The order is passed under Section 12(3) of the SEBI Act, 1992 read with Regulation 30A of the SEBI (Intermediaries) Regulations, 2008, which governs action for non-compliance by registered intermediaries.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced; this is an enforcement order applying existing provisions of Clause 3 of the Second Schedule to the SEBI (Research Analysts) Regulations, 2014, which mandates renewal fee payment every five years from the date of grant of registration.

## Compliance Requirements

- Registered Research Analysts must pay the prescribed renewal fee within three months before expiry of the five-year period for which fee was last paid, to keep their certificate of registration in force.
- Failure to comply exposes Research Analysts to regulatory action under Section 12(3) of the SEBI Act read with Regulation 30A of the SEBI (Intermediaries) Regulations, 2008, which can include suspension or cancellation of registration.

## Important Dates

- Registration dates of Noticees range from 16-Jun-2015 to 03-Jul-2017 (and later, per the truncated list).
- Renewal fee default dates range from 16-Jun-2020 onward, five years after each respective registration date.

## Impact Assessment

The order directly affects the 15 named Research Analysts/entities, whose ability to operate as SEBI-registered Research Analysts may be curtailed pending compliance or further SEBI action. The broader market impact is limited, as these are individual intermediaries rather than listed companies, but the order underscores SEBI's continued enforcement of renewal fee compliance among registered intermediaries and serves as a reminder for other Research Analysts to ensure timely fee payments.
