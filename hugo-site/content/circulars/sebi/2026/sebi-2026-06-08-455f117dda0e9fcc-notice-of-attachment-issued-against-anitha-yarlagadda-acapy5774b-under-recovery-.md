## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Attachment against Anitha Yarlagadda (PAN: ACAPY5774B) under Recovery Certificate No. 4687 of 2022 in the matter of Venus Power Ventures (India) Limited. The notice directs banks, depositories, and other financial institutions to attach the assets and funds held in the name of the defaulter to recover dues owed to SEBI.

## Key Points

- Notice issued under Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992, empowering SEBI to recover amounts as arrears of land revenue.
- Recovery Certificate No. 4687 of 2022 forms the basis of this attachment action.
- The notice is directed against Anitha Yarlagadda (PAN: ACAPY5774B) as a defaulter in the Venus Power Ventures (India) Limited matter.
- Banks, depositories, and financial entities are directed to freeze and attach assets/funds held by the named individual.
- The attached assets will be applied toward recovery of the outstanding amount specified in the recovery certificate.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI recovery provisions (Section 28A, SEBI Act, 1992).

## Compliance Requirements

- Banks and financial institutions holding accounts or assets in the name of Anitha Yarlagadda (PAN: ACAPY5774B) must attach the same upon receipt of this notice.
- Depositories and depository participants must freeze demat holdings linked to the named PAN.
- Entities must report compliance and the quantum of attached assets to SEBI within the stipulated timeframe specified in the notice.
- No transfer, withdrawal, or encumbrance of attached assets is permitted after receipt of notice.

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate No.: 4687 of 2022
- Notice issued: 2026-06-08

## Impact Assessment

This notice has no direct impact on broader markets or listed securities. It is a targeted enforcement action against an individual in connection with the Venus Power Ventures (India) Limited matter. The attachment is limited to the assets and funds of the named defaulter (Anitha Yarlagadda, PAN: ACAPY5774B) and does not affect general investors or market operations.
