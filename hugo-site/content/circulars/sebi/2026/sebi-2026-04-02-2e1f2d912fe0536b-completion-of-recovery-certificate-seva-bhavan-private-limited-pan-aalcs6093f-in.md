## Summary

SEBI has issued a notice confirming the completion of Recovery Certificate proceedings against Seva Bhavan Private Limited (PAN: AALCS6093F) arising from the matter of Illiquid Stock Options. The completion of a recovery certificate signifies that the monetary amounts directed to be recovered under the enforcement order have been fully recovered, bringing the recovery proceedings to a close.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate issued against Seva Bhavan Private Limited (PAN: AALCS6093F) has been completed.
- The matter pertains to SEBI's enforcement actions in the Illiquid Stock Options segment.
- Completion indicates that the dues payable under the underlying adjudication or settlement order have been recovered in full.
- This notice is issued by SEBI's Recovery Officer under the SEBI Act, 1992.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced by this order. This is an administrative closure document confirming the completion of an existing recovery enforcement action.

## Compliance Requirements

- No new compliance obligations are imposed on market participants by this notice.
- The concerned entity (Seva Bhavan Private Limited) has fulfilled its financial obligations under the recovery certificate.
- Other entities involved in the Illiquid Stock Options matter should ensure their own recovery certificate obligations (if any) are satisfied.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Completion Notice:** April 2, 2026
- The underlying recovery certificate proceedings are now closed as of this date.

## Impact Assessment

This order has minimal market impact as it represents the administrative closure of enforcement proceedings against a single private entity. It is part of SEBI's broader and ongoing enforcement drive against manipulative trading in the Illiquid Stock Options segment on exchanges. No trading restrictions, new obligations, or systemic changes result from this notice. Market participants in the derivatives segment should note SEBI's continued active pursuit of recovery in Illiquid Stock Options cases.
