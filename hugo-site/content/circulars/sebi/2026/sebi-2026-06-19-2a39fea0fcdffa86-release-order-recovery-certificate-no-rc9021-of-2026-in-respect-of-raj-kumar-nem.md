## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order for Recovery Certificate No. RC9021 of 2026 pertaining to Raj Kumar Nemani and Sons HUF (PAN: AAHHR0158K). The release order indicates that the recovery proceedings initiated under the recovery certificate in the Illiquid Stock Options (ISO) enforcement matter have been concluded, typically upon payment of the outstanding dues by the defaulter.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9021 of 2026 has been released by SEBI.
- The certificate was issued against Raj Kumar Nemani and Sons HUF (PAN: AAHHR0158K).
- The matter relates to SEBI's enforcement action in the Illiquid Stock Options (ISO) segment.
- A Release Order signifies that the recovery of the penalty/dues has been completed or the proceedings are otherwise concluded.
- This is part of SEBI's ongoing enforcement drive against entities involved in manipulative trading in illiquid stock options on stock exchanges.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced by this order. It is an administrative enforcement document completing a prior recovery action.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance obligations arise from this release order for market participants at large. The order is specific to the named entity — Raj Kumar Nemani and Sons HUF — and pertains solely to the closure of its outstanding recovery proceedings.

## Important Dates

- **Order Date:** June 19, 2026
- Recovery Certificate No. RC9021 of 2026 is released as of the date of this order.

## Impact Assessment

This order has no direct impact on broader market operations, trading participants, or listed securities. It is an enforcement closure document specific to one HUF entity. The Illiquid Stock Options (ISO) enforcement series by SEBI has historically involved a large number of entities found to have engaged in reversal trades and price manipulation in the options segment; this release order represents the conclusion of recovery proceedings for one such entity.
