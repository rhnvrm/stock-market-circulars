## Summary

SEBI has announced the completion of Recovery Certificate no. 1618 of 2018, originally dated 03.08.2018, which was issued to Lyra Housing and Finance Pvt. Ltd. (PAN: AAACL3708P) in the matter of Linkson International Limited. This notice marks the formal conclusion of the recovery proceedings initiated against the entity.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate no. 1618 of 2018 dated 03.08.2018 has been completed
- Certificate was issued to Lyra Housing and Finance Pvt. Ltd. (PAN: AAACL3708P)
- Proceedings relate to the matter of Linkson International Limited
- Completion indicates that recovery proceedings have been successfully concluded

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this circular. It is an enforcement closure notice specific to the named entity.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements for market participants. This notice pertains solely to the completion of enforcement action against Lyra Housing and Finance Pvt. Ltd. in the context of the Linkson International Limited matter.

## Important Dates

- Original Recovery Certificate Date: 03.08.2018
- Recovery Certificate Number: 1618 of 2018
- Completion Notice Date: 30 April 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact as it is an administrative enforcement closure. The completion of recovery proceedings against Lyra Housing and Finance Pvt. Ltd. is specific to that entity and the Linkson International Limited matter. No broader trading, listing, or compliance implications exist for other market participants.
