## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand (Certificate No. 9193 of 2026) under Rule 2 of the Second Schedule to the Income-tax Act, 1961 read with Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992, against Jigneshkumar Purshottamdas Patel (PAN: AQEPP8019J), Ahmedabad, Gujarat. The notice demands payment of Rs. 5,42,222.72 towards a disgorgement order arising from trading activities of certain entities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited).

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 9193 of 2026 issued against Jigneshkumar Purshottamdas Patel (AQEPP8019J), resident of B-9 Bela Appartment, Nehru Nagar, Ambawadi, Anand Vatika Society, Ahmedabad, Gujarat – 380015.
- Total demand of Rs. 5,42,222.72, comprising: disgorgement of Rs. 5,05,815.63 (per Order No. QJA/MN/IVD/ID7/31947/2025-26 dated 31.12.2025), interest of Rs. 35,407.09 (January 2026 to July 2026 @ 12% p.a.), and recovery cost of Rs. 1,000.
- The underlying disgorgement order relates to trading activities of certain entities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited).
- Payment must be made within 15 days of receipt of the notice via EFT/NEFT/RTGS to SEBI's designated account (A/c No. SEBIRRDDSG9193, Bank of India, IFSC BKID00VAN04) or through SEBI's online Recovery Payment portal.
- Non-payment empowers SEBI's Recovery Officer to enforce recovery through attachment/sale of movable and immovable property, attachment of bank accounts, arrest and detention, or appointment of a receiver.
- Under Explanation 1 to Section 28A of the SEBI Act, any transfer of the noticee's property or funds to a spouse, minor child, son's wife, or son's minor child on or after 31 December 2025 without adequate consideration is deemed to be the noticee's property for recovery purposes.
- Upon service of this notice, the noticee is barred from mortgaging, charging, leasing, or otherwise dealing with any property without permission of the Recovery Officer; such transfers are void under Rule 16 of the Second Schedule.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is an individual enforcement/recovery notice, not a new regulation or policy change.

## Compliance Requirements

- The noticee, Jigneshkumar Purshottamdas Patel, must remit Rs. 5,42,222.72 to SEBI within 15 days of receipt of the notice.
- Payment confirmation (case name, recovery certificate number, payee name, date and amount paid, transaction number, and bank details) must be forwarded to SEBI's Recovery Officer at SEBI Bhavan-II, Mumbai, or emailed to akashb@sebi.gov.in and recoveryho1@sebi.gov.in.
- No action is required from DU Digital Global Limited or other market participants; this notice pertains solely to the named individual.

## Important Dates

- 31 December 2025: Date of the underlying disgorgement order (QJA/MN/IVD/ID7/31947/2025-26).
- 02 July 2026: Date of issuance of the Notice of Demand.
- Payment due within 15 days from receipt of this notice (on or around mid-July 2026).

## Impact Assessment

This notice is a routine individual recovery/enforcement action under SEBI's Section 28A powers and has no direct impact on DU Digital Global Limited's listing, operations, or shareholders. It carries reputational and legal significance for the named individual but is not expected to affect the company's stock price or trading activity.
