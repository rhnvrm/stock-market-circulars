## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC9045 of 2026 along with Attachment Proceeding (A.P.) No. 15399 of 2026 against Subhranshu Roy HUF (PAN: AAXHS8157D) in the matter of Illiquid Stock Options. This is part of SEBI's ongoing enforcement and recovery actions related to manipulation in the illiquid stock options segment.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9045 of 2026 issued against Subhranshu Roy HUF
- Attachment Proceeding No. 15399 of 2026 initiated under this certificate
- Entity identified by PAN: AAXHS8157D
- Action relates to the Illiquid Stock Options matter, a longstanding SEBI enforcement focus
- SEBI is pursuing recovery of dues/penalties through attachment of assets

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an individual enforcement and recovery action under existing SEBI recovery mechanisms.

## Compliance Requirements

- The named entity (Subhranshu Roy HUF) is required to comply with the terms of the Recovery Certificate
- Attachment of bank accounts, demat accounts, or other assets may follow under A.P. No. 15399 of 2026
- Any third parties holding assets of the named entity may be obligated to respond to attachment notices

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate issued: June 2026
- A.P. No. 15399 of 2026 initiated: June 2026

## Impact Assessment

This action is limited in scope and targets a single Hindu Undivided Family (HUF) entity. There is no broad market impact. It is part of SEBI's systematic recovery proceedings against entities penalised in the Illiquid Stock Options investigation, which involved widespread fictitious trading in the options segment. No impact on general investors or market operations is expected.
