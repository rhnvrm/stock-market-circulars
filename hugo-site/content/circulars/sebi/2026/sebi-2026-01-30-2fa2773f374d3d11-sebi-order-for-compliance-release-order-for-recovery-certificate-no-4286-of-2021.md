## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order for Recovery Certificate No. 4286 of 2021 against Sanjay Jethalal Soni (PAN: BVSPD9741N), Krupa Sanjay Soni (PAN: BVSPB9740P), and M/s. J M Soni Consultancy (PAN: AAGFJ2939P) in the matter related to Timbor Home Limited. A release order typically indicates that the recovery proceedings have been concluded, either through compliance, settlement, or other resolution.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 4286 of 2021 has been released
- Parties involved: Sanjay Jethalal Soni, Krupa Sanjay Soni, and J M Soni Consultancy
- Matter relates to Timbor Home Limited
- Release order suggests conclusion of recovery proceedings
- Order dated January 30, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement/compliance order related to specific recovery proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

The release order indicates that the recovery proceedings against the named parties have been concluded. No further compliance actions are required from these specific entities under this recovery certificate.

## Important Dates

- **Order Date:** January 30, 2026
- **Recovery Certificate Number:** 4286 of 2021
