## Summary

SEBI has issued a notice of attachment of bank accounts and demat accounts against Investment Research Advisor (Proprietor: Amit Gangrade, PAN: AVRPG8196P) under Attachment Proceedings No. 15129 & 15130 of 2026 dated January 01, 2026. This action is being taken in the matter of Investment Research Advisor under Recovery Certificate No. 8973 of 2025.

## Key Points

- Entity: Investment Research Advisor
- Proprietor: Amit Gangrade (PAN: AVRPG8196P)
- Recovery Certificate No.: RC 8973 of 2025
- Attachment Proceedings No.: AP 15129 & 15130 of 2026
- Date of Notice: January 01, 2026
- Action: Attachment of bank accounts and demat accounts
- Status: Defaulter

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI recovery proceedings framework.

## Compliance Requirements

The defaulter (Investment Research Advisor and its proprietor Amit Gangrade) is subject to:
- Attachment of bank accounts
- Attachment of demat accounts
- Recovery proceedings under RC No. 8973 of 2025

Banks and depositories are required to comply with the attachment order and freeze the specified accounts.

## Important Dates

- **January 01, 2026**: Date of attachment notice (AP No. 15129 & 15130 of 2026)
- Recovery Certificate issued: 2025 (RC No. 8973 of 2025)

## Impact Assessment

**Direct Impact:**
- The proprietor Amit Gangrade and Investment Research Advisor entity will have their bank accounts and demat accounts frozen
- Inability to access funds or securities in the attached accounts

**Market Impact:**
- Limited direct market impact as this affects a specific defaulter entity
- Reinforces SEBI's enforcement capabilities in recovery proceedings

**Compliance Impact:**
- Demonstrates consequences of non-compliance with SEBI regulations
- Banks and depositories must implement the attachment orders promptly
- Serves as a deterrent for other investment advisors regarding regulatory compliance
