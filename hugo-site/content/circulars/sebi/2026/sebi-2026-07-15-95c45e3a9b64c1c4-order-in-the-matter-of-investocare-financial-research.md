## Summary

SEBI passed an order in the matter of Investocare Financial Research & Others, following up on an earlier order dated May 25, 2023 that directed Mr. Ramesh Babu (Noticee No. 3) to refund monies collected from investors for unregistered investment advisory activities, and debarred Investocare Financial Research and its proprietor Mr. Ravish Kandhari from accessing the securities market for one year. This order addresses compliance status and further directions under Sections 11(1), 11(4), 11(4A), 11B(1) and 11B(2) read with Section 15HA and 15EB of the SEBI Act, 1992.

## Key Points

- The original May 25, 2023 order required Mr. Ramesh Babu to refund all money collected from complainants/investors related to unregistered investment advisory services within three months.
- Mr. Ramesh Babu was directed to issue public notice in two National Dailies (English and Hindi) and one local daily detailing refund modalities within 15 days.
- Repayments must be made only through traceable banking channels (Demand Draft, Pay Order, or electronic transfer) to ensure audit trails.
- Mr. Ramesh Babu was restrained from selling assets, properties, and mutual fund/share/securities holdings except for making the directed refunds; banks were directed to allow debits only for such refund purposes.
- A completion report, certified by an independent Chartered Accountant, was required to be filed with SEBI's Division of Post-Inspection Enforcement Action within 15 days after the three-month refund period.
- Any remaining balance was to be deposited with SEBI in an escrow account for one year for distribution to affected investors, with leftover funds going to the Investor Protection and Education Fund.
- Investocare Financial Research and its proprietor Mr. Ravish Kandhari (Noticee Nos. 1 and 2) were debarred from accessing the securities market for one year from the date of the original order.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework changes; this is an enforcement/adjudication order applying existing SEBI Act provisions to unregistered investment advisory conduct.

## Compliance Requirements

Noticees are required to complete investor refunds via traceable banking channels, issue public notices detailing refund modalities, refrain from disposing of assets except for refund purposes, and file a Chartered Accountant-certified completion report with SEBI's enforcement division.

## Important Dates

- May 25, 2023: Original SEBI order issued with directions to Noticees.
- Refunds to be completed within three months of the directions coming into force, with a completion report due within 15 days thereafter.
- One-year market debarment period for Investocare Financial Research and Mr. Ravish Kandhari from the date of the original order.

## Impact Assessment

The order primarily affects the individual noticees involved in unregistered investment advisory activities and does not pertain to listed companies or broader market operations. It reinforces SEBI's continued enforcement against unregistered investment advisers and unauthorized market practices, serving as a compliance signal to similar entities but carrying limited direct impact on trading or listed securities.
