## Summary

The Appellate Authority under the RTI Act, SEBI, disposed of Appeal No. 6808 of 2026 filed by Rakesh Hosmani against CPIO, SEBI, Mumbai. The appellant had complained that the respondent's RTI reply was not accessible on the RTI MIS portal. Upon review, it was found that a technical issue affected the portal during March 17-18, 2026, making uploaded responses inaccessible to applicants. The appeal was declared infructuous as the reply became viewable subsequently.

## Key Points

- Appellant Rakesh Hosmani filed an RTI application on March 05, 2026 via the RTI MIS portal
- CPIO, SEBI, Mumbai responded by letter dated March 18, 2026
- Appellant filed first appeal on March 19, 2026 (Reg. No. SEBIH/A/E/26/00114), citing inability to open the reply on the portal
- A technical outage in the RTI MIS portal during March 17-18, 2026 caused RTI responses to be inaccessible to applicants
- The Appellate Authority confirmed the reply is currently viewable and enclosed a copy with the order
- Appeal disposed of as infructuous
