## Summary

SEBI has issued a Completion Order for Recovery Certificate No. 2184 of 2019 against Ajitbhai P Vasa in the matter of Exelon Infrastructure Ltd. This order signifies that the recovery proceedings initiated under the recovery certificate have been concluded, either through successful recovery of the dues or through other legal resolution.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 2184 of 2019 was originally issued by SEBI against Ajitbhai P Vasa
- The certificate pertains to the matter of Exelon Infrastructure Ltd.
- SEBI has now issued a Completion Order, marking the closure of recovery proceedings
- Recovery certificates are issued under Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992, enabling SEBI to recover dues as arrears of land revenue
- The completion of this order indicates resolution of the outstanding enforcement action

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced by this order. This is an administrative closure of an existing enforcement action.

## Compliance Requirements

This completion order does not impose new compliance obligations on market participants. It is specific to Ajitbhai P Vasa and the matter of Exelon Infrastructure Ltd. The closure of the recovery certificate indicates that the dues owed to SEBI have been settled or the proceedings have otherwise been concluded.

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate issued: 2019 (Certificate No. 2184 of 2019)
- Completion Order date: June 2026

## Impact Assessment

This order has minimal market or operational impact. It represents the administrative closure of a specific enforcement action against an individual respondent in a historical matter. Investors and market participants are not directly affected by this completion order. The order demonstrates SEBI's continued follow-through on enforcement actions to ensure recovery of dues from errant entities and individuals.
