## Summary

The Appellate Authority under SEBI dismissed RTI Appeal No. 6893 of 2026 filed by Sunil Kumar against the CPIO, SEBI, Mumbai. The appellant sought information regarding discrepancies in PACL refund claim applications, including reasons for partial payments, calculation of paid amounts, and status of pending balances. SEBI's response directed the appellant to the Justice (Retd.) R.M. Lodha Committee, which is the body responsible for PACL refund processing, and the appeal was found to lack merit.

## Key Points

- Appellant Sunil Kumar filed an RTI application on May 17, 2026 seeking details on PACL refund claim discrepancies such as partial payments, incorrect/illegible certificate remarks, and wrong bank details.
- CPIO, SEBI responded on May 20, 2026 stating that all PACL refund communications are handled by the Justice (Retd.) R.M. Lodha Committee and directed the appellant accordingly.
- The appellant filed an appeal on May 21, 2026 (Reg. No. SEBIH/A/E/26/00192) alleging the response was incomplete, misleading, or false.
- The Appellate Authority found no deficiency in SEBI's response, noting that SEBI does not hold the requested information.
- The CIC precedent (Sh. Pattipati Rama Murthy vs. CPIO, SEBI, July 8, 2013) was cited: a CPIO cannot invent information it does not possess.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an appellate order resolving an individual RTI dispute.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements for market participants. The order reaffirms that queries related to PACL refund claims must be directed to the Justice (Retd.) R.M. Lodha Committee, constituted pursuant to the Supreme Court order dated February 2, 2016.

## Important Dates

- May 17, 2026: Appellant's RTI application filed.
- May 20, 2026: CPIO, SEBI's response issued.
- May 21, 2026: Appeal filed (Reg. No. SEBIH/A/E/26/00192).

## Impact Assessment

This order has no market or operational impact. It is an administrative RTI appellate decision affecting only the individual appellant. Investors with PACL refund queries are reminded to approach the Justice (Retd.) R.M. Lodha Committee rather than SEBI directly. Public information including Status Reports, FAQs, Press Releases, and Public Notices on PACL Ltd. remains accessible on the SEBI website.
