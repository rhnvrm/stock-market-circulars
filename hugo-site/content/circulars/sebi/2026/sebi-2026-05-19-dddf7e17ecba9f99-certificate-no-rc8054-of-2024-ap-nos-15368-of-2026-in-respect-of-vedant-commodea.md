## Summary

SEBI has issued an attachment proceeding (A.P No. 15368 of 2026) under Recovery Certificate No. RC8054 of 2024 against Vedant Commodeal Pvt Ltd (PAN: AABCL9683L). This proceeding arises in the matter of Worth Investment and Trading Private Limited and directs attachment of bank accounts and/or demat accounts of the defaulting entity to recover dues owed to SEBI.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC8054 of 2024 forms the basis for this attachment action.
- Attachment Proceeding No. A.P 15368 of 2026 is issued against Vedant Commodeal Pvt Ltd (PAN: AABCL9683L).
- The underlying matter involves Worth Investment and Trading Private Limited.
- SEBI's Recovery Officer has directed banks and depositories to freeze and attach the accounts of the named entity.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI recovery and attachment powers pursuant to the SEBI Act and the SEBI (Recovery of Amounts) Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Banks holding accounts of Vedant Commodeal Pvt Ltd are required to freeze/attach the specified accounts and report compliance to SEBI.
- Depositories are required to freeze the demat accounts of the named entity as directed.
- The defaulter (Vedant Commodeal Pvt Ltd) must not alienate, transfer, or dispose of any assets subject to attachment.

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate No. RC8054 issued: 2024
- Attachment Proceeding No. 15368 of 2026 issued: May 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has no broad market impact. It is a targeted enforcement action against a single private entity (Vedant Commodeal Pvt Ltd) to recover SEBI-ordered dues. Market participants and investors not associated with this entity are unaffected. The action reflects SEBI's ongoing enforcement of outstanding recovery certificates.
