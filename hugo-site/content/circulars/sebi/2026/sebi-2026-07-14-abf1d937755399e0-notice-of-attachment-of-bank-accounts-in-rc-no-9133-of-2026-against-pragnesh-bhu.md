## Summary

SEBI's Recovery Officer has issued a notice of attachment of bank accounts held by Pragnesh Bhupendrabhai Thakkar (PAN: AEVPT7357L) pursuant to Recovery Certificate No. 9133 of 2026. The proceedings stem from a SEBI order relating to trading in illiquid stock options at BSE, where monetary dues (such as penalty and/or disgorgement amounts) remain unpaid by the noticee. Under powers available to the Recovery Officer, the bank(s) maintaining accounts of the defaulter have been directed to attach the accounts and remit the available balance towards recovery of the outstanding dues.

## Key Points

- Notice issued against Pragnesh Bhupendrabhai Thakkar (PAN: AEVPT7357L).
- Action taken under Recovery Certificate No. 9133 of 2026.
- Underlying matter relates to trading in illiquid stock options at BSE.
- Banks holding accounts in the name of the noticee are directed to attach the account(s) and remit funds to SEBI to the extent of the outstanding recovery amount.
- This is part of SEBI's enforcement mechanism for recovering unpaid penalties/dues from market participants found in violation of securities regulations.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is an enforcement action carried out under existing recovery provisions (Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992, read with the Second and Third Schedules to the Income Tax Act, 1961, and the Income Tax (Certificate Proceedings) Rules, 1962, as applicable to recovery of SEBI dues).

## Compliance Requirements

- The bank(s) receiving this notice must attach the account(s) held by the noticee and are barred from permitting any withdrawals without permission of the Recovery Officer, except as provided under the applicable rules.
- The bank must remit the attached amount (up to the outstanding recovery amount) to SEBI.
- The noticee remains liable for the balance dues, if any, after adjustment of the attached amount.

## Important Dates

Specific compliance timelines and payment deadlines are set out in the original notice; no separate market-wide effective date applies since this is an individual enforcement action.

## Impact Assessment

This notice has no direct impact on listed securities, trading systems, or market participants at large. It is a targeted enforcement/recovery action against an individual found liable in a prior SEBI order concerning illiquid stock options trading at BSE, relevant primarily for compliance and enforcement tracking purposes.
