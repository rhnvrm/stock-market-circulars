## Summary

SEBI has amended paragraph 21.10 of the SEBI Master Circular for Mutual Funds (dated March 20, 2026) to revise the certification requirements applicable to persons engaged in the sale and/or distribution of Specialized Investment Funds (SIFs). A new dedicated certification, "NISM Series-V-D – Mutual Fund - Specialized Investment Fund Distributors Certification," has been introduced, replacing the earlier requirement of holding both NISM Series V-A and NISM Series XIII certifications for SIF distribution.

## Key Points

- Persons engaged or to be engaged in sale/distribution of SIF products must hold a valid "NISM Series-V-D - Mutual Fund - Specialized Investment Fund Distributors Certification".
- Entities holding NISM Series-V-D certification can distribute both Mutual Fund and SIF products without separately holding NISM Series V-A certification.
- Entities distributing only Mutual Fund products (not SIF) must continue to comply with the existing NISM Series V-A certification requirement.
- The requirement to hold "NISM Series XIII – Common Derivatives Certification" for SIF distribution will cease to apply after September 21, 2026.
- Distributors already holding a valid NISM Series XIII certification obtained on or before September 21, 2026 are exempted from obtaining NISM Series-V-D until their existing NISM Series XIII certification expires, provided they continue to hold NISM Series V-A certification during this transition period.
- AMFI and AMCs are responsible for ensuring distributor/agent compliance with these requirements.

## Regulatory Changes

Paragraph 21.10 of the SEBI Master Circular for Mutual Funds dated March 20, 2026 has been modified to reflect the new NISM Series-V-D certification framework for SIF distributors, replacing reliance on the combination of NISM Series V-A and NISM Series XIII certifications. All other provisions of the MF Master Circular remain unchanged.

## Compliance Requirements

- Mutual Funds, AMCs, Trustee Companies, RTAs, and AMFI must ensure that all persons engaged in SIF distribution obtain the NISM Series-V-D certification.
- Existing NISM Series XIII certificate holders (obtained on or before September 21, 2026) may continue distributing SIFs under transitional provisions but must maintain valid NISM Series V-A certification in the interim.
- AMCs and AMFI must monitor and enforce certification compliance among distributors and agents.

## Important Dates

- July 21, 2026: Circular issued; provisions effective immediately.
- September 21, 2026: Last date for obtaining/relying on NISM Series XIII certification for SIF distribution; transitional relief applies only to certifications obtained on or before this date.

## Impact Assessment

The change streamlines certification requirements for SIF distributors by consolidating them into a single dedicated NISM Series-V-D certification, reducing duplicative certification burden (previously requiring both V-A and XIII). This is an operational/compliance-focused change affecting mutual fund distributors, AMCs, RTAs, and AMFI members, with a transition period to ensure continuity of SIF distribution activities. No direct impact on listed securities or trading is expected.
