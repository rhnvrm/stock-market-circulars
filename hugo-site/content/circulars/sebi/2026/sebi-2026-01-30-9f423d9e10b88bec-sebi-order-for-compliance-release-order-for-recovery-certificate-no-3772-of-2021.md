## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order for Recovery Certificate No. 3772 of 2021 against Rainbow Housing Development & Finance Co. Ltd. in the matter of non-obtaining of SCORES (SEBI Complaints Redress System) authentication. This order indicates that the recovery proceedings under the said certificate have been concluded or the outstanding amount has been satisfied.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 3772 of 2021 issued against Rainbow Housing Development & Finance Co. Ltd.
- Matter relates to non-obtaining of SCORES authentication
- Release order issued on January 30, 2026
- Indicates completion or satisfaction of recovery proceedings

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced by this order.

## Compliance Requirements

- The release order concludes the recovery proceedings against Rainbow Housing Development & Finance Co. Ltd. under Recovery Certificate No. 3772 of 2021
- Market participants must ensure compliance with SCORES authentication requirements to avoid similar enforcement actions

## Important Dates

- **January 30, 2026**: Release Order issued

## Impact Assessment

This release order has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains specifically to Rainbow Housing Development & Finance Co. Ltd. and the conclusion of recovery proceedings. The order serves as a reminder to market participants about the importance of maintaining compliance with SEBI's SCORES authentication requirements.
