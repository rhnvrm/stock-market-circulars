## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC9090 of 2026 dated June 15, 2026, in relation to A.P. No. 15468 of 2026, against Sandeep Agarwal HUF (PAN: AAVHS6180G). This certificate relates to recovery of dues in the matter of illiquid stock options, an area where SEBI has conducted extensive enforcement proceedings against entities found to have engaged in manipulative or fraudulent trading.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9090 of 2026 issued by SEBI against Sandeep Agarwal HUF
- PAN of the respondent entity: AAVHS6180G
- Associated application/proceeding: A.P. No. 15468 of 2026
- Pertains to the matter of illiquid stock options — a longstanding SEBI enforcement focus area
- The certificate enables SEBI to recover outstanding dues as per applicable law

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This circular is an enforcement/recovery action under existing SEBI regulations and the Securities and Exchange Board of India Act.

## Compliance Requirements

- Sandeep Agarwal HUF is required to comply with the terms of the recovery certificate and settle the outstanding dues as specified
- The designated authority and relevant parties must act upon the recovery certificate in accordance with SEBI's recovery proceedings framework

## Important Dates

- Certificate Date: June 15, 2026
- Recovery Certificate No.: RC9090 of 2026
- A.P. No.: 15468 of 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a targeted enforcement action against a single HUF entity (Sandeep Agarwal HUF) and has no broader market or systemic impact. It forms part of SEBI's ongoing campaign to recover dues from parties found to have participated in illiquid stock options manipulation. Market participants and investors not involved in the specific proceedings are unaffected.
