## Summary

The Appellate Authority under the Right to Information Act, 2005 at SEBI dismissed Appeal No. 6931 of 2026 filed by J. Monisha against the CPIO, SEBI, Mumbai. The appellant had sought her Phase II examination marks (paper-wise and total), category-wise cutoff marks for the Phase II examination, and details of any SEBI policy on disclosure of marks/cutoffs for recruitment exams, in connection with the SEBI Grade A Officer Recruitment Exercise 2025. The CPIO had responded that this recruitment exercise was still under process and that the requested information would be published on SEBI's website under the "Career" section only after the recruitment process is completed. The Appellate Authority, Ruchi Chojer, found the CPIO's response adequate and dismissed the appeal on July 7, 2026.

## Key Points

- Appellant J. Monisha filed an RTI application dated April 27, 2026 seeking Phase II exam marks, category-wise cutoffs, and SEBI's disclosure policy for recruitment exams.
- The CPIO responded on May 15, 2026 stating the SEBI Grade A Officer Recruitment Exercise 2025 is still under process.
- Marks and cutoff information will be published on SEBI's website under the "Career" section after the recruitment exercise concludes.
- The appellant filed an appeal dated June 14, 2026 (Reg. No. SEBIH/A/E/26/00220) on the ground that access to information was refused.
- The Appellate Authority found the CPIO's response adequate and dismissed the appeal, ruling no interference was warranted.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this order. It is a procedural RTI appeal disposal reaffirming SEBI's existing practice of publishing recruitment exam marks and cutoffs only after the recruitment exercise is complete.

## Compliance Requirements

Not applicable to listed entities, market participants, or intermediaries. This order pertains solely to SEBI's internal RTI compliance and its recruitment process disclosure timeline.

## Important Dates

- April 27, 2026: RTI application filed by appellant
- May 15, 2026: CPIO's response to the application
- June 14, 2026: Appeal filed by appellant (Reg. No. SEBIH/A/E/26/00220)

## Impact Assessment

This order has no market, trading, or listed-company impact. It is relevant only to SEBI's internal HR/recruitment transparency practices and RTI compliance procedures, and is of interest primarily to candidates involved in the SEBI Grade A Officer Recruitment 2025.
