## Summary

SEBI has issued a release order for Jatin Ahuja (PAN: AGDPA4644F), who was classified as a defaulter in a matter concerning trading in illiquid stock options on the Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE). This order relates to Recovery Certificate No. 7320 of 2023.

## Key Points

- Subject: Jatin Ahuja, PAN: AGDPA4644F
- Classification: Defaulter
- Matter: Trading in illiquid stock options on BSE
- Recovery Certificate No.: 7320 of 2023
- Action: Release order issued

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action regarding an existing matter.

## Compliance Requirements

This is a release order indicating resolution or completion of the recovery proceedings against the named defaulter.

## Important Dates

- Release Order Date: January 8, 2026
- Original Recovery Certificate: 2023 (Certificate No. 7320)

## Impact Assessment

This release order has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains to an individual defaulter case involving illiquid stock options. The order indicates conclusion of recovery proceedings, which may allow the individual to resume certain market activities subject to SEBI regulations.
