## Summary

SEBI has issued a notice confirming the completion of Recovery Certificate No. 8592 of 2025 dated January 30, 2025, against DEE KARTAVYA FINANCE LIMITED (Defaulter, PAN: AAACD1157A) in the matter of trading in illiquid stock options on the Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE). This notice indicates that the recovery proceedings initiated against the defaulter have been successfully completed.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8592 of 2025 has been completed
- Recovery Certificate was originally issued on January 30, 2025
- Defaulter: DEE KARTAVYA FINANCE LIMITED
- PAN of Defaulter: AAACD1157A
- Matter relates to trading in illiquid stock options on BSE
- Notice confirms successful conclusion of recovery proceedings

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a completion notice for existing enforcement proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

No ongoing compliance requirements as the recovery proceedings have been completed. The notice serves as formal confirmation that:

- All dues/penalties have been recovered from the defaulter
- The recovery certificate has been satisfied
- The enforcement action has been concluded

## Important Dates

- **January 30, 2025**: Original issuance date of Recovery Certificate No. 8592 of 2025
- **January 27, 2026**: Date of completion notice issuance

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a completion notice indicating closure of enforcement proceedings rather than initiation of new action.

**Entity Impact**: The completion of recovery proceedings against DEE KARTAVYA FINANCE LIMITED indicates successful enforcement action by SEBI. The entity was previously identified as a defaulter for trading in illiquid stock options on BSE.

**Investor Impact**: Minimal direct impact on investors. The notice demonstrates SEBI's active enforcement against entities engaging in illiquid stock options trading violations.

**Precedent Value**: Serves as a reminder of SEBI's continued focus on enforcement actions related to manipulation through illiquid stock options trading.
