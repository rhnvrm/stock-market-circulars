## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC9196 of 2026, accompanied by Attachment Proceedings No. 15568 of 2026, against Prachi Tradevine Pvt. Ltd. (PAN: AAFCP7889L). The certificate relates to recovery of outstanding dues (typically penalty and/or disgorgement amounts along with interest) arising from an earlier SEBI order in the matter of trading in illiquid stock options on the exchange. Such certificates are issued under Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992, read with the applicable provisions of the Income Tax Act, 1961, enabling SEBI's Recovery Officer to attach the defaulter's movable and/or immovable properties for realization of the dues.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9196 of 2026 issued against Prachi Tradevine Pvt. Ltd. (PAN: AAFCP7889L).
- Linked Attachment Proceeding: A.P. No. 15568 of 2026.
- Matter pertains to trading in illiquid stock options, a category of cases where SEBI has previously penalized entities for executing reversal trades that created artificial volumes without genuine change in beneficial ownership.
- The certificate empowers the Recovery Officer to proceed with attachment and eventual sale of the entity's assets if dues remain unpaid.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory change is introduced; this is an enforcement/recovery action executing a prior SEBI adjudication or settlement order relating to illiquid stock options trading.

## Compliance Requirements

Prachi Tradevine Pvt. Ltd. is required to clear the outstanding dues (penalty/disgorgement plus applicable interest) specified under the underlying order. Failure to pay may result in attachment and sale of the entity's movable and immovable properties, and other recovery measures available under the Income Tax Act's Second and Third Schedules as applied to SEBI recoveries.

## Important Dates

Specific payment deadlines and the date of the underlying original SEBI order are stated in the certificate document; these details were not extractable from the available source material for this summary.

## Impact Assessment

This action is specific to a single non-listed entity and does not have direct implications for listed securities, trading systems, or the broader market. Its significance is primarily as part of SEBI's ongoing enforcement against entities involved in illiquid stock options trading practices, reinforcing deterrence against similar conduct.
