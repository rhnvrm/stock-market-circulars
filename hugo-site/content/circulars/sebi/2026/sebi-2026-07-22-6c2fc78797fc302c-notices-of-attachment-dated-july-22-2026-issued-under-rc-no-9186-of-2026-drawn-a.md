## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Attachment dated July 22, 2026, issued under Recovery Certificate (RC) No. 9186 of 2026, drawn against Pranav Kamleshkumar Trivedi. The notice arises from proceedings related to the trading activities of certain entities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited). The attachment is a recovery measure initiated by SEBI to enforce collection of confirmed dues (such as penalties, disgorgement amounts, and/or other monetary directions) that remain unpaid by the noticee, using powers available under the Second and Third Schedules to the Income Tax Act, 1961, as applied to recovery of amounts due to SEBI under Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992.

## Key Points

- Notice of Attachment dated July 22, 2026 issued under RC No. 9186 of 2026.
- Noticee: Pranav Kamleshkumar Trivedi.
- Underlying matter: trading activities of certain entities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited).
- The attachment targets bank accounts, demat accounts, and/or other assets of the noticee to recover confirmed dues.
- This action follows a prior SEBI order/direction that quantified amounts payable by the noticee, which have remained unpaid, triggering coercive recovery proceedings.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is an enforcement/recovery action under SEBI's existing powers under Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992 read with the Income Tax Act recovery provisions, applicable to the specific noticee named in the Recovery Certificate.

## Compliance Requirements

- The noticee, Pranav Kamleshkumar Trivedi, is required to clear outstanding dues to SEBI to avoid further coercive recovery action, including attachment and possible sale of attached assets.
- Banks, depositories, and other intermediaries holding accounts or assets in the name of the noticee are required to comply with the attachment directions and restrict operations on the attached accounts/assets as instructed by the Recovery Officer.

## Important Dates

- Notice of Attachment issued: July 22, 2026.
- Recovery Certificate reference: RC No. 9186 of 2026.

## Impact Assessment

The notice has a direct impact only on the named individual, Pranav Kamleshkumar Trivedi, whose bank/demat accounts or other assets are subject to attachment for recovery of dues. It does not affect trading, listing, or compliance obligations for DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited) itself, nor does it impose any new requirements on the broader market. Its relevance is chiefly to those tracking the resolution and enforcement outcomes of the underlying SEBI order concerning trading activities in this scrip.
