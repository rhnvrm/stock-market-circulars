## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC9013 of 2026 along with a Notice of Demand against Pramod Kumar Jain (PAN: ACLPJ7469D) in connection with illiquid stock options matter. This document initiates recovery proceedings to collect outstanding dues determined by SEBI in the underlying enforcement case.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9013 of 2026 issued against Pramod Kumar Jain
- Individual PAN: ACLPJ7469D
- Case relates to illiquid stock options violations
- Notice of Demand issued for recovery of dues
- Part of SEBI's enforcement and recovery proceedings

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI recovery proceedings framework pursuant to Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992.

## Compliance Requirements

Pramod Kumar Jain is required to:
- Pay the demanded amount as specified in the recovery certificate
- Comply with the notice of demand within the stipulated timeframe
- Failure to comply may result in further recovery actions including attachment of assets, bank accounts, and other coercive measures

## Important Dates

- Certificate Issue Date: February 6, 2026
- Payment deadline and other specific dates would be mentioned in the detailed certificate document

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This is an individual enforcement action with no direct market-wide implications.

**Regulatory Significance:** Medium - Demonstrates SEBI's continued focus on recovering dues from defaulters in illiquid stock options cases and its commitment to enforcement actions.

**Affected Parties:** Pramod Kumar Jain specifically. Other entities involved in similar illiquid stock options violations should note SEBI's active pursuit of recovery proceedings.
