## Summary

SEBI has issued a Completion Order for Recovery Certificate No. 4286 of 2021 dated January 30, 2026, in the matter involving Sanjay Jethalal Soni (PAN: BVSPD9741N), Krupa Sanjay Soni (PAN: BVSPB9740P), and M/s. J M Soni Consultancy (PAN: AAGFJ2939P) related to Timbor Home Limited. This order signifies the completion of recovery proceedings against the named entities.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 4286 of 2021 has been marked as completed
- Order issued on January 30, 2026
- Entities involved: Sanjay Jethalal Soni, Krupa Sanjay Soni, and M/s. J M Soni Consultancy
- Matter relates to Timbor Home Limited
- Completion order indicates successful recovery or closure of proceedings

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a compliance completion order for existing recovery proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

No ongoing compliance requirements as this is a completion order. The recovery proceedings have been concluded.
