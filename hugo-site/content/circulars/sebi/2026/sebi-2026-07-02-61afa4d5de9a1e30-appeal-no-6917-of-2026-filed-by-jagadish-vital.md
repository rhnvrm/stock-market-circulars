## Summary

The Appellate Authority under the Right to Information Act, 2005 disposed of Appeal No. 6917 of 2026 filed by appellant Jagadish V against the CPIO, SEBI, Mumbai. The appellant had filed an RTI application dated May 09, 2026 seeking records related to enforcement proceedings against Monarch Networth Capital Limited (MNCL), its officials Anish Shah and Rajesh Ranka, in connection with the SMS Techsoft (India) Ltd. adjudication matter, along with SEBI inspection visit reports for premises inspections conducted in September and November 2013, and correspondence between SEBI and Kotak Mahindra Bank regarding disputed savings accounts. The respondent CPIO responded via letter dated June 05, 2026, following which the appellant filed the present appeal on the same date.

## Key Points

- Appellant sought certified copies of Show Cause Notices issued to MNCL, Anish Shah, and/or Rajesh Ranka regarding SMS Techsoft (India) Ltd. adjudication proceedings (RC-2786/2020 and RC-4469/2022).
- Appellant sought certified copies of any Adjudication/Penalty Orders passed against MNCL, Anish Shah, and/or Rajesh Ranka, including case number, date, authority, and penalty amount.
- Appellant sought file-closure notes or termination memos if proceedings against MNCL/Anish Shah/Rajesh Ranka were closed or dropped.
- Appellant sought Inspection Visit Reports for SEBI's surprise inspections of SMS Techsoft (India) Ltd. premises on/around September 13, 2013 and November 5, 2013, including details of inspecting officers and report references.
- Appellant requested a certified 'Nil' reply if no formal Inspection Visit Report exists for either 2013 visit.
- Appellant sought copies of SEBI's written correspondence with Kotak Mahindra Bank concerning disputed savings account(s) in the applicant's name.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is an individual RTI appellate order and does not introduce or amend any regulatory framework, circular, or market rule.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements are imposed on market participants. The order pertains solely to the CPIO's obligations under the RTI Act to provide or deny specific information to the named appellant.

## Important Dates

- May 09, 2026: RTI application filed by appellant.
- June 05, 2026: CPIO's response letter issued.
- June 05, 2026: Appeal filed by appellant (Reg. No. SEBIH/A/E/26/00207).

## Impact Assessment

This order has no direct market, trading, or listing impact. It is an administrative RTI disclosure matter concerning historical enforcement records related to Monarch Networth Capital Limited and SMS Techsoft (India) Ltd. Any relevance is limited to transparency around SEBI's past enforcement and inspection processes rather than any new obligation or market-moving disclosure.
