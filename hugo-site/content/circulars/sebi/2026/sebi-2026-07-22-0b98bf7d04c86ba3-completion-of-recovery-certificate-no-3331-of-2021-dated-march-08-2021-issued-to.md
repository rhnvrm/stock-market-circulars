## Summary

SEBI's Recovery Officer has issued a notice confirming the completion of Recovery Certificate No. 3331 of 2021, dated March 08, 2021, which was issued against defaulter Ravindra Nath Mishra (PAN: AMCPM1105C) in connection with recovery proceedings related to Synergy Bizcon Limited. The notice formally closes out the recovery certificate issued under SEBI's powers to recover dues, penalties, or disgorgement amounts from persons found in default.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 3331 of 2021 was originally issued on March 08, 2021 against the named defaulter.
- The defaulter is identified as Ravindra Nath Mishra, PAN: AMCPM1105C.
- The matter relates to Synergy Bizcon Limited.
- SEBI has now issued a completion notice for this recovery certificate, indicating that the recovery proceedings initiated under it have concluded.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced by this notice. It documents the closure of an existing recovery proceeding initiated under Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992, read with the Second and Third Schedules to the Income Tax Act, 1961, as applicable to recovery of amounts due to SEBI.

## Compliance Requirements

No further compliance action is required from market participants as a result of this specific notice. It serves as an official record that recovery action against this defaulter under the cited certificate has been completed.

## Important Dates

- Original Recovery Certificate issued: March 08, 2021
- Completion notice published: July 2026

## Impact Assessment

This notice has limited market-wide impact, as it pertains to the closure of an individual enforcement/recovery matter rather than a listed company's securities or broader regulatory policy. It is primarily relevant for record-keeping and transparency around SEBI's enforcement and recovery actions.
