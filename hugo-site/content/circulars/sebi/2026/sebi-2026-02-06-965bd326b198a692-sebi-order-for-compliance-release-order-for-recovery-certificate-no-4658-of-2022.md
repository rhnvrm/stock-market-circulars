## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order for Recovery Certificate No. 4658 of 2022 against Amul Gagabhai Desai in the matter of Timbor Home Limited. This order indicates the completion or resolution of recovery proceedings initiated under SEBI's enforcement actions.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 4658 of 2022 against Amul Gagabhai Desai
- Related to Timbor Home Limited matter
- Release order signifies completion of recovery process
- Part of SEBI's compliance and enforcement framework

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an order for compliance related to existing recovery proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

This release order concludes the recovery proceedings. No new compliance requirements for market participants.

## Important Dates

- Order Date: February 6, 2026
- Recovery Certificate issued: 2022 (RC No. 4658)

## Impact Assessment

This order has minimal market impact as it relates to the conclusion of specific recovery proceedings against an individual in relation to Timbor Home Limited. It demonstrates SEBI's ongoing enforcement and recovery mechanism but does not affect broader market operations or introduce new compliance obligations for other market participants.
