## Summary

SEBI has issued a Remittance Advice against Awanish Kumar Mishra (Defaulter, PAN: AGOPM7538H) in connection with the transfer of mutual fund units by Money Mishra Financial Services and others. This action is taken under Recovery Certificate No. 8599 of 2025.

## Key Points

- Remittance advice issued by SEBI against defaulter Awanish Kumar Mishra (PAN: AGOPM7538H)
- Pertains to transfer of mutual fund units by Money Mishra Financial Services and others
- Issued under Recovery Certificate No. 8599 of 2025
- Document dated June 1, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement/recovery action under existing SEBI recovery certificate framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Entities in receipt of this remittance advice are required to comply with the directions specified therein.
- Any custodians, depositories, or registrars holding assets of the defaulter may be directed to act in accordance with SEBI's recovery instructions.

## Important Dates

- Remittance Advice Date: June 1, 2026
- Recovery Certificate No. 8599 of 2025

## Impact Assessment

This action is limited in scope to the specific defaulter (Awanish Kumar Mishra) and entities associated with Money Mishra Financial Services. There is no broader market or systemic impact. Investors in mutual fund schemes affected by the alleged transfer of units may see resolution through this recovery process.
