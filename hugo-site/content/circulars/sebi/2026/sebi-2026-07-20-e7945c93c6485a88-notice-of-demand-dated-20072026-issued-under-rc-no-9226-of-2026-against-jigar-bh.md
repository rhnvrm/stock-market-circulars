## Summary

SEBI's Recovery Officer has issued a Notice of Demand (Certificate No. 9226 of 2026) under Rule 2 of the Second Schedule to the Income-tax Act, 1961, read with Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992, against Jigar Bhailalbhai Khalash (PAN: ESZPK4415E) of Ahmedabad, Gujarat. The notice demands payment of Rs. 2,13,000, comprising an unpaid penalty, accrued interest, and recovery costs, arising from an Adjudicating Officer's order related to trading activity in the scrip of ANI Integrated Services Limited.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 9226 of 2026 has been issued against Jigar Bhailalbhai Khalash (PAN: ESZPK4415E), Ahmedabad, Gujarat.
- Total amount due: Rs. 2,13,000, consisting of:
  - Penalty: Rs. 2,00,000 (imposed vide Order No. Order/AK/DS/2025-26/32082-32109 dated 12.02.2026, related to trading activity in ANI Integrated Services Limited)
  - Interest (February 2026 to July 2026 @ 1% p.m.): Rs. 12,000
  - Recovery cost: Rs. 1,000
- Payment must be made within 15 days of receipt of the notice.
- Payment channels include direct EFT/NEFT/RTGS to SEBI's designated ICICI Bank account, the SEBI Recovery Payment portal, or the SEBI website's Enforcement > Recovery Proceedings > Pay Now link.
- Non-payment empowers SEBI's Recovery Officer to pursue recovery via attachment/sale of movable and immovable property, attachment of bank accounts, arrest and detention, or appointment of a receiver.
- Post-notice, the entity is barred from mortgaging, charging, leasing, or transferring property without the Recovery Officer's permission; any such transfer is deemed void.
- Transfers of property or funds to a spouse, minor child, son's wife, or son's minor child without adequate consideration on or after 12.02.2026 are deemed the entity's property for recovery purposes.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is an individual enforcement/recovery action, not a regulatory or policy change.

## Compliance Requirements

- The noticee must pay the total demand of Rs. 2,13,000 within 15 days of receipt via the specified payment channels.
- Confirmation of any e-payment must be forwarded in the prescribed format to SEBI's Recovery Officer at SEBI Bhavan-II, Mumbai, or emailed to akashb@sebi.gov.in and recoveryho1@sebi.gov.in.
- The noticee must refrain from transferring, mortgaging, or otherwise dealing with property without the Recovery Officer's permission.

## Important Dates

- 12 February 2026: Original penalty order (Order/AK/DS/2025-26/32082-32109) issued by the Adjudicating Officer.
- 20 July 2026: Notice of Demand (Recovery Certificate No. 9226 of 2026) issued.
- Payment due within 15 days of receipt of this notice (i.e., on or around early August 2026).

## Impact Assessment

This notice is a routine enforcement/recovery action targeting an individual trader and does not affect ANI Integrated Services Limited as a listed entity, its shareholders, or broader market operations. Its relevance is limited to SEBI's ongoing enforcement of penalties for market misconduct and serves as a reminder of consequences for non-compliance with adjudication orders.
