## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order concerning Ravindra Nath Mishra (PAN: AMCPM1105C), who was proceeded against as a defaulter under Recovery Certificate No. 3331 of 2021 in the matter of Synergy Bizcon Limited. The order releases the individual from the recovery proceedings initiated under the Recovery Certificate, indicating that the outstanding dues or obligations underlying the certificate have been addressed to SEBI's satisfaction, or that the basis for continued recovery action no longer subsists.

## Key Points

- The order concerns Ravindra Nath Mishra, identified by PAN AMCPM1105C, in his capacity as a defaulter named in Recovery Certificate No. 3331 of 2021.
- The underlying matter relates to Synergy Bizcon Limited.
- SEBI's Recovery Officer has passed a Release Order in respect of this individual, effectively closing or lifting the coercive recovery measures initiated against him under the certificate.
- The order forms part of SEBI's ongoing recovery proceedings process used to enforce monetary demands, penalties, or disgorgement directions arising from earlier SEBI orders.

## Regulatory Changes

This is an individual enforcement/recovery action and does not introduce any new regulatory requirement, rule, or market-wide compliance change.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance obligations arise for market participants, listed entities, or intermediaries from this order. It applies specifically to the named individual defaulter in relation to the cited Recovery Certificate.

## Important Dates

The release order was issued in July 2026; specific procedural dates are detailed in the full order available at the linked PDF.

## Impact Assessment

The order has no direct impact on listed securities, trading activity, or the broader market. Its significance is confined to the enforcement record of the named defaulter and the closure status of recovery proceedings under Recovery Certificate No. 3331 of 2021 in the Synergy Bizcon Limited matter.
