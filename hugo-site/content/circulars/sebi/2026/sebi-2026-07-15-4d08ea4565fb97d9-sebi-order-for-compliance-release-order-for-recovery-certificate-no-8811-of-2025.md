## Summary

SEBI's Recovery Officer has issued a Release Order in connection with Recovery Certificate No. 8811 of 2025 against BIR Finance Private Limited. The underlying recovery certificate was issued in connection with the entity's involvement in trading in illiquid stock options at BSE, a practice SEBI has historically treated as an unauthorized/manipulative market practice warranting monetary penalties and disgorgement directions. The Release Order typically records that specific attachment/recovery action taken against the entity's bank or demat accounts under this certificate is being released, either following satisfaction of the demand, part-payment, or another change in the recovery status, without necessarily closing the entire recovery proceeding.

## Key Points

- The order pertains to Recovery Certificate No. 8811 of 2025 issued against BIR Finance Private Limited.
- The underlying matter relates to trading in illiquid stock options at BSE.
- SEBI's Recovery Officer has issued a Release Order releasing the attachment/recovery action previously initiated under this certificate.
- This is a procedural/administrative order within an ongoing enforcement matter rather than a new finding of violation.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced by this order. It is an enforcement-related administrative action concerning a previously issued recovery certificate against the named entity.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance action is required from market participants generally. The order relates specifically to BIR Finance Private Limited's status under Recovery Certificate No. 8811 of 2025.

## Important Dates

Order dated: 2026-07-15 (published on SEBI's website in July 2026).

## Impact Assessment

The order has no direct impact on listed securities, trading activity, or the broader market, as it concerns a release action within recovery proceedings against a single unlisted private entity. Its significance is limited to SEBI's enforcement record-keeping regarding illiquid stock options trading violations.
