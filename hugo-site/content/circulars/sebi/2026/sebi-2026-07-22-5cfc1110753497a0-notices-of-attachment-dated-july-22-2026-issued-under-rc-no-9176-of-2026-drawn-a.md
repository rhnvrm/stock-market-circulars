## Summary

SEBI has issued Notice(s) of Attachment dated July 22, 2026, under Recovery Certificate (RC) No. 9176 of 2026, drawn against Nikunj Sureshchandra Shah. The recovery proceedings stem from an earlier SEBI order relating to trading activities of certain entities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now renamed DU Digital Global Limited). The notice is a recovery-enforcement step to attach the defaulter's movable and/or immovable property, and/or bank accounts, for recovery of monies (penalty/disgorgement dues) due to SEBI.

## Key Points

- SEBI's Recovery Officer has issued a Notice of Attachment under RC No. 9176 of 2026 against Nikunj Sureshchandra Shah.
- The underlying matter concerns alleged irregular/manipulative trading activities by certain entities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited).
- The notice enables attachment of the defaulter's assets (bank accounts, movable/immovable property) as part of SEBI's recovery process for unpaid dues arising from a prior SEBI adjudication/order.
- This action falls under SEBI's recovery mechanism pursuant to Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992, read with the relevant Income Tax Act (Second Schedule) recovery provisions applied by SEBI Recovery Officers.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced; this is an enforcement/recovery action executing a pre-existing SEBI order against the named individual.

## Compliance Requirements

- The named entity (Nikunj Sureshchandra Shah) is required to clear outstanding dues to avoid further attachment or sale of attached assets.
- Banks, depositories, and other intermediaries holding assets of the named entity may be required to comply with the attachment notice and restrict operations on the attached assets/accounts.

## Important Dates

- Notice of Attachment dated: July 22, 2026
- Recovery Certificate reference: RC No. 9176 of 2026

## Impact Assessment

The notice targets an individual defaulter and does not impose any direct trading restriction, suspension, or surveillance measure on DU Digital Global Limited's listed shares. Impact is limited to the named individual's assets being subject to attachment for recovery of SEBI dues. The action reinforces SEBI's ongoing enforcement against market manipulation cases and may be of interest to investors tracking regulatory history related to DU Digital Global Limited, but it carries low direct market impact for the company's trading or shareholders.
