## Summary

SEBI has issued a release order for Recovery Certificate No. 9078 of 2026 against I Zone Academy Private Limited (PAN: AACCI0592E). The release order is issued in connection with the matter of trading in illiquid stock options on BSE, indicating that the entity has complied with the recovery demands and the certificate is being discharged.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 9078 of 2026 has been issued against I Zone Academy Private Limited (PAN: AACCI0592E)
- The matter pertains to trading in illiquid stock options on BSE
- SEBI has issued a release/compliance order, indicating the entity has satisfied the recovery demand
- This is an enforcement follow-up action confirming resolution of dues

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This order relates to the enforcement and resolution of an existing recovery certificate against a specific entity.

## Compliance Requirements

- I Zone Academy Private Limited has fulfilled its obligations under the recovery certificate
- The recovery certificate stands released upon satisfaction of the dues specified therein
- No further compliance action is required from the entity with respect to this certificate

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate No. 9078 of 2026 issued and subsequently released as of June 2026

## Impact Assessment

This order has minimal market impact. It represents the conclusion of an enforcement action against a single entity (I Zone Academy Private Limited) for trading in illiquid stock options on BSE. The release of the recovery certificate indicates that the financial penalty or dues have been paid, closing out this particular enforcement matter. Broader market participants are not affected.
