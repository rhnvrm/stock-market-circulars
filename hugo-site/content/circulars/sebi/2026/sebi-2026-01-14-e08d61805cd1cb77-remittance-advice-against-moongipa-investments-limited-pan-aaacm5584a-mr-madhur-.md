## Summary

SEBI has issued a remittance advice under Recovery Certificate No. 8624 of 2025 against Moongipa Investments Limited (PAN: AAACM5584A) and its directors - Mr. Madhur Agarwal (PAN: AAFPA1251Q), Mr. Sudhir Agarwal (PAN: AAFPA1250R), and Mr. Shiv Kumar Agarwal (PAN: AAFPA1252P), holding them jointly and severally liable as defaulters. This document pertains to SEBI's enforcement and recovery actions.

## Key Points

- Recovery proceedings initiated against Moongipa Investments Limited and three of its directors
- All parties named as defaulters and held jointly and severally liable
- Recovery Certificate No. 8624 of 2025 issued
- Remittance advice indicates active enforcement and collection efforts

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI recovery procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Moongipa Investments Limited and the named directors must comply with the recovery certificate terms
- Payment of outstanding amounts as determined by SEBI
- Failure to comply may result in further enforcement measures including attachment of assets

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate Date: 2025
- Remittance Advice Date: January 14, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**For Named Entities:** High impact - the company and directors face enforcement action with joint and several liability, potentially including asset attachment and market access restrictions.

**Market Impact:** Low - limited to the specific entities involved. No broader market implications unless part of a larger enforcement pattern.

**Investor Impact:** High for any investors or creditors of Moongipa Investments Limited, as recovery proceedings indicate financial/regulatory distress.
