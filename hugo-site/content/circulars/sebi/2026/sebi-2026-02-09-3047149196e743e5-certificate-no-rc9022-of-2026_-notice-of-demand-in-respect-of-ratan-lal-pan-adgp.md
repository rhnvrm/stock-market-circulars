## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC9022 of 2026 as a Notice of Demand against Ratan Lal (PAN: ADGPL6812N) in connection with an illiquid stock options matter. This is part of SEBI's enforcement and recovery proceedings.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate Number: RC9022 of 2026
- Individual: Ratan Lal
- PAN: ADGPL6812N
- Matter: Illiquid Stock Options
- Document Type: Notice of Demand

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an individual enforcement action.

## Compliance Requirements

Ratan Lal is required to comply with the demand notice and recovery proceedings as specified in the certificate.

## Important Dates

- Certificate Issue Date: February 9, 2026
- Specific payment deadlines and compliance dates are detailed in the recovery certificate document.

## Impact Assessment

This is an individual enforcement action targeting a specific person in relation to illiquid stock options. The impact is limited to the individual named in the recovery certificate. No broader market impact is expected. This action demonstrates SEBI's continued enforcement efforts in matters related to market manipulation and illiquid securities.
