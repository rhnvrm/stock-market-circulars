## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC9165 of 2026 together with Attachment Proceeding (A.P.) No. 15556 of 2026 against Grand Dealtrade Private Limited (PAN: AAECG1630P) in relation to enforcement proceedings arising from the entity's dealings in illiquid stock options. The certificate forms part of SEBI's ongoing recovery action to enforce dues determined against the defaulter.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate Number: RC9165 of 2026
- Attachment Proceeding (A.P.) Number: 15556 of 2026
- Defaulter: Grand Dealtrade Private Limited
- PAN: AAECG1630P
- Matter: Illiquid Stock Options
- Issuing Authority: Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI)
- Action Type: Recovery certificate / attachment proceeding against bank and/or demat accounts of the defaulter

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is an enforcement action taken under SEBI's existing recovery framework for realization of dues from parties found to have engaged in irregular trading in illiquid stock options.

## Compliance Requirements

Grand Dealtrade Private Limited is required to settle the outstanding dues specified under Recovery Certificate No. RC9165 of 2026. Non-compliance may result in attachment of bank accounts and demat accounts under A.P. No. 15556 of 2026 and further recovery proceedings, including restraint on dealing in securities until dues are cleared.

## Important Dates

- Certificate/Order Date: July 15, 2026
- Specific compliance deadlines, if any, would be detailed in the full order text

## Impact Assessment

This is a targeted enforcement action against a single defaulting entity and does not have direct market-wide impact. It reflects SEBI's continued enforcement of recovery proceedings against entities implicated in illiquid stock options trading, reinforcing deterrence against similar market misconduct.
