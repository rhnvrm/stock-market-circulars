## Summary

SEBI's Recovery Officer has issued a Notice of Attachment of Bank Accounts and Demat Accounts (AP No. 15544 & 15545 of 2026), dated July 13, 2026, against Girish Kantilal Parmar, who has been declared a defaulter. The action arises from Recovery Certificate No. 9170 of 2026, issued in connection with proceedings related to trading activities of certain entities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now known as DU Digital Global Limited). As the defaulter has failed to pay dues confirmed against him, SEBI has directed attachment of his bank and demat accounts to recover the outstanding amount.

## Key Points

- Notice of attachment issued under AP No. 15544 & 15545 of 2026, dated July 13, 2026.
- Defaulter named: Girish Kantilal Parmar.
- Attachment is based on Recovery Certificate No. 9170 of 2026 issued by SEBI's Recovery Officer.
- Underlying matter relates to trading activities of certain entities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited).
- Both bank accounts and demat accounts of the defaulter are covered under the attachment order.

## Regulatory Changes

This is an individual enforcement/recovery action and does not introduce any new regulatory requirement, rule, or amendment applicable to market participants generally.

## Compliance Requirements

- Banks and depository participants holding accounts of the named defaulter are required to comply with the attachment order and restrict operations in the identified accounts pursuant to the recovery certificate.
- The defaulter is expected to settle outstanding dues under the recovery certificate to seek release of the attached accounts.

## Important Dates

- Notice date: July 13, 2026
- Underlying Recovery Certificate: No. 9170 of 2026

## Impact Assessment

The direct impact is confined to the named defaulter, whose bank and demat accounts stand attached pending recovery of dues. There is no indication of any impact on trading, listing status, or shareholders of DU Digital Global Limited itself, as the notice pertains to enforcement action against an individual in connection with past trading conduct rather than the company's current operations or disclosures.
