## Summary

SEBI has issued a completion order for Recovery Certificate (RC) No. 6715 of 2023, which was issued against G. Sri Vignesh (PAN: GOAPS3552D) in the matter of Securekloud Technologies Limited. The completion order confirms that the outstanding dues under the recovery certificate have been fully recovered or the recovery process has been concluded.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 6715 of 2023 issued against G. Sri Vignesh (PAN: GOAPS3552D) has been completed.
- The matter pertains to Securekloud Technologies Limited.
- A companion release order (for the same RC No. 6715 of 2023) has also been issued simultaneously, indicating attached assets or bank accounts have been released.
- The completion signifies that SEBI's enforcement action under this specific recovery certificate has concluded.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an administrative enforcement closure order.

## Compliance Requirements

No ongoing compliance requirements for market participants. This order is specific to the named individual respondent (G. Sri Vignesh) and concludes SEBI's recovery proceedings under RC No. 6715 of 2023.

## Important Dates

- Date of Completion Order: March 25, 2026
- Recovery Certificate No. 6715 originally issued in 2023

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine enforcement closure order specific to an individual respondent in the matter of Securekloud Technologies Limited. No broader market or operational implications for investors or other market participants.
