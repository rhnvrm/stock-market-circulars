## Summary

SEBI has announced the completion of Recovery Certificate No. 9028 of 2026, originally issued on February 11, 2026, against Shashi Jain (PAN: ACBPJ2127L), a designated defaulter in the matter of ISO (Investor Securities Obligation) at BSE. This notice confirms that the recovery proceedings initiated under the certificate have been concluded.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 9028 of 2026, dated February 11, 2026, has been marked as completed.
- The defaulter is Shashi Jain with PAN: ACBPJ2127L.
- The matter pertains to ISO (Investor Securities Obligation) at BSE under the name BSE-SHASHI JAIN.
- SEBI has formally closed the recovery proceedings against this individual.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This order is an administrative closure of an existing enforcement action.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements arise from this notice. It is a closure order confirming the completion of an earlier recovery action.

## Important Dates

- **Recovery Certificate Issued:** February 11, 2026
- **Completion Order Date:** April 28, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact as it pertains solely to the closure of a specific individual enforcement/recovery action. It does not affect trading, listing, or broader market operations. Relevant only as a record of SEBI's enforcement activity against the named defaulter.
