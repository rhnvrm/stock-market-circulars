## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC9070 of 2026 under A.P. No. 15518 of 2026 against Ramkrishna Karmakar (PAN: BYMPK3820M) in the matter of Illiquid Stock Options. This represents enforcement action for recovery of dues arising from the illiquid stock options proceedings.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9070 of 2026 issued
- Adjudication Proceeding No. 15518 of 2026
- Individual: Ramkrishna Karmakar (PAN: BYMPK3820M)
- Matter: Illiquid Stock Options
- Enforcement action for dues recovery under SEBI recovery proceedings

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this certificate.

## Compliance Requirements

- Subject individual must comply with recovery proceedings
- Payment of dues as determined in the adjudication proceeding
- Cooperation with recovery authorities

## Important Dates

- Certificate issued: July 2026
- Related to A.P. No. 15518 of 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is an individual enforcement action. The certificate facilitates recovery of dues from the specified individual in connection with the illiquid stock options matter. This is part of SEBI's ongoing enforcement activities to ensure compliance and recover outstanding amounts in the illiquid stock options case.
