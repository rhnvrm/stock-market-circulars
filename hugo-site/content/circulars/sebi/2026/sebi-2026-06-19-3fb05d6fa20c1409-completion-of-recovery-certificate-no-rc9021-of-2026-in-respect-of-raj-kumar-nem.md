## Summary

SEBI has announced the completion of Recovery Certificate no. RC9021 of 2026 issued against Raj Kumar Nemani and Sons HUF (PAN: AAHHR0158K) in the matter of Illiquid Stock Options (ISO). This circular marks the closure of the recovery proceedings, indicating that the dues or penalty amount outstanding under the recovery certificate have been fully satisfied.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate RC9021 of 2026 has been fully executed and completed.
- The subject entity is Raj Kumar Nemani and Sons HUF, identified by PAN AAHHR0158K.
- The underlying matter pertains to SEBI's enforcement action in the Illiquid Stock Options (ISO) segment, where widespread manipulative and fraudulent trading practices were detected.
- Completion of the RC signifies that SEBI has successfully recovered the penalty or disgorgement amount from the defaulter.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This circular is an administrative notification confirming the closure of a specific enforcement recovery proceeding.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance obligations arise for market participants from this circular. It is relevant solely to the named entity (Raj Kumar Nemani and Sons HUF) and records the conclusion of its liability under RC9021 of 2026.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** June 19, 2026
- **Recovery Certificate:** RC9021 of 2026 (now completed as of this circular)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has no market-wide or operational impact. It is an administrative closure notice confirming that SEBI has completed its recovery action against a specific defaulting entity in the long-running Illiquid Stock Options enforcement matter. The ISO matter involved large-scale manipulation in the illiquid stock options segment on stock exchanges and led to penalties and disgorgement orders against numerous entities. Completion of individual recovery certificates like this one represents the final enforcement step — successful collection of dues — and has no bearing on other market participants or ongoing trading activities.
