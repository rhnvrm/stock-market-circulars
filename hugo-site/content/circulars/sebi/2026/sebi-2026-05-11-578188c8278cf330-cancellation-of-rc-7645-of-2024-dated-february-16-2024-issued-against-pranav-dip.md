## Summary

SEBI has cancelled Recovery Certificate No. 7645 of 2024 dated February 16, 2024, which was issued against Pranav Dipak Desai and Kokila Dipak Desai in the matter of a complaint filed by Finsec Law Advisors Ltd on behalf of Aeqitas.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 7645 of 2024 has been cancelled
- Original RC was issued on February 16, 2024
- RC was issued against Pranav Dipak Desai and Kokila Dipak Desai
- Complaint was filed by Finsec Law Advisors Ltd on behalf of Aeqitas
- Cancellation order issued on May 11, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

The cancellation of Recovery Certificate No. 7645 of 2024 removes the enforcement action previously initiated against Pranav Dipak Desai and Kokila Dipak Desai, indicating resolution of the underlying recovery proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific ongoing compliance requirements arise from this cancellation order. The cancellation concludes the recovery proceedings against the named individuals.

## Important Dates

- Original Recovery Certificate Date: February 16, 2024
- RC Number: 7645 of 2024
- Cancellation Order Date: May 11, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is an administrative cancellation of a recovery certificate with minimal direct market impact. The order primarily affects Pranav Dipak Desai and Kokila Dipak Desai, concluding the enforcement action initiated on behalf of Aeqitas through its legal representative Finsec Law Advisors Ltd. The cancellation likely indicates that the recovery amount has been realized or the matter otherwise resolved.
