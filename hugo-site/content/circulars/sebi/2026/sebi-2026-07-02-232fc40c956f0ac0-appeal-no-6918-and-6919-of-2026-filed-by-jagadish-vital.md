## Summary

The SEBI Appellate Authority under the Right to Information Act, 2005 issued an order disposing of two identical appeals (Appeal Nos. 6918 & 6919 of 2026) filed by appellant Jagadish Vital against the CPIO, SEBI, Mumbai. The appeals arose from RTI applications dated May 09 & 10, 2026, seeking information related to SMS Techsoft (India) Ltd, including directorship records held by the Ministry of Corporate Affairs, a Kotak Mahindra Bank account, and details of SEBI's internal handling of enforcement proceedings (RC 2786/2020) following a National Faceless Appeal Centre (NFAC) order that allegedly characterized a preferential allotment as a bogus transaction.

## Key Points

- The appellant filed identical RTI applications on May 09 & 10, 2026, and the CPIO responded via a common letter dated June 05, 2026.
- The appellant then filed two identical appeals dated June 05, 2026 (Reg. Nos. SEBIH/A/E/26/00208 & SEBIH/A/E/26/00209), alleging incomplete, misleading, or false information was provided.
- Queries 1-16 sought Ministry of Corporate Affairs records on directorship pertaining to SMS Techsoft (India) Ltd; the Appellate Authority upheld the CPIO's response that this information does not pertain to SEBI.
- Queries 31-34 sought information on a Kotak Mahindra Bank account, addressed to RBI; the Appellate Authority similarly held this does not pertain to SEBI.
- Queries 17, 18, and 25(bbb) sought details on whether SEBI received and acted upon an NFAC order dated 31/05/2023 that reportedly found the SMS Techsoft preferential allotment to be a bogus transaction, and questioned the legal basis for continued recovery proceedings, property attachment, and auction against the appellant under enforcement matter RC 2786/2020.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is an adjudicatory order under the RTI Act concerning a specific information access dispute, not a new regulation or policy change.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance obligations are created for market participants. The order confirms SEBI's position that it cannot provide information outside its custody (i.e., MCA or RBI records) and addresses procedural handling of the appellant's RTI queries regarding SEBI's own enforcement file.

## Important Dates

- May 09 & 10, 2026: RTI applications filed.
- June 05, 2026: CPIO response and appeals filed.
- May 31, 2023: Referenced NFAC order date cited by appellant.

## Impact Assessment

This order has no direct impact on listed companies, trading, or the broader market. It is relevant primarily to the appellant and to SEBI's RTI compliance record, and to observers tracking the enforcement matter RC 2786/2020 concerning SMS Techsoft (India) Ltd.
