## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand under Recovery Certificate No. RC9072 of 2026 against Alternative Dealers Private Limited (PAN: AAICA2613G). This notice is issued in connection with the matter of Illiquid Stock Options, directing the entity to pay outstanding dues as determined by prior SEBI orders.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9072 of 2026 issued by SEBI against Alternative Dealers Private Limited
- PAN of the defaulter entity: AAICA2613G
- Notice relates to the Illiquid Stock Options enforcement matter
- Demand is for recovery of penalty/disgorgement amounts previously ordered by SEBI
- Issued under Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992, which empowers SEBI to recover dues as arrears of land revenue

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is an enforcement/recovery action pursuant to existing SEBI orders in the Illiquid Stock Options matter.

## Compliance Requirements

- Alternative Dealers Private Limited is required to pay the demanded amount within the stipulated time period as mentioned in the notice
- Non-compliance may lead to attachment and sale of properties, recovery through certificate proceedings, or other coercive recovery measures under applicable law
- The entity must respond to the designated SEBI recovery officer within the deadline specified in the notice

## Important Dates

- Notice of Demand date: March 24, 2026
- Recovery Certificate No.: RC9072 of 2026
- Payment deadline: As specified in the notice (typically 15–30 days from date of demand)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has a direct and significant financial impact on Alternative Dealers Private Limited, which faces recovery proceedings for dues arising from the Illiquid Stock Options enforcement action. The Illiquid Stock Options matter is a large-scale market manipulation case investigated by SEBI involving numerous entities. The broader market impact is limited, as this is a targeted enforcement action against a specific defaulting entity. Investors in or counterparties of Alternative Dealers Private Limited should be aware of the ongoing enforcement proceedings.
