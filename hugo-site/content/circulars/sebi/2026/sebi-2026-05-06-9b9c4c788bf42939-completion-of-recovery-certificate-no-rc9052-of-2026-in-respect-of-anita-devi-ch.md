## Summary

SEBI has announced the completion of Recovery Certificate No. RC9052 of 2026 issued against Anita Devi Chokhani (PAN: ACWPC0520B) in connection with the matter of Illiquid Stock Options. Completion indicates that the outstanding dues under the recovery certificate have been fully recovered.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9052 of 2026 has been marked as completed.
- The noticee is Anita Devi Chokhani (PAN: ACWPC0520B).
- The matter pertains to SEBI's enforcement action in the Illiquid Stock Options segment, which involved alleged manipulation of illiquid stock options contracts on BSE.
- Completion of the recovery certificate signifies that all outstanding penalty or disgorgement amounts have been recovered from the noticee.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an administrative closure notice confirming completion of an existing enforcement/recovery action.

## Compliance Requirements

No further compliance obligations arise from this notice. The recovery proceedings against Anita Devi Chokhani under RC9052 of 2026 are concluded.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Completion Notice:** May 6, 2026
- **Recovery Certificate:** RC9052 of 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This notice is an administrative closure of a single individual's recovery proceedings under SEBI's broader Illiquid Stock Options enforcement drive. It has no bearing on ongoing market operations, trading rules, or listed entities.
