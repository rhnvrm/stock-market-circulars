## Summary

SEBI has issued a Remittance Advice against Deepak Goel HUF (PAN: AAHHD4142B), identified as a defaulter, in connection with the matter of ISO at BSE. The remittance advice is issued under Recovery Certificate No. 8358 of 2024, directing recovery of dues from the defaulter.

## Key Points

- Defaulter: Deepak Goel HUF (Hindu Undivided Family)
- PAN of Defaulter: AAHHD4142B
- Matter: ISO at BSE
- Recovery Certificate No.: 8358 of 2024
- Document Type: Remittance Advice (enforcement/recovery instrument)
- Issuing Authority: Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI recovery mechanisms.

## Compliance Requirements

- The defaulter (Deepak Goel HUF) is required to remit the dues as specified under Recovery Certificate No. 8358 of 2024.
- Relevant parties and intermediaries (such as banks, depositories, or exchanges) may be directed to assist in recovery as per SEBI's recovery procedures.

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate issued: 2024 (specific date in original certificate)
- Remittance Advice issued: May 2026

## Impact Assessment

This action has a limited and targeted impact, affecting only the named defaulter (Deepak Goel HUF). There is no broad market or investor impact. The action reflects SEBI's ongoing enforcement of recovery proceedings against individuals and entities that have outstanding dues arising from regulatory orders.
