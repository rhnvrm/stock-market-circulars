## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Attachment of Bank Accounts (No. 15389 of 2026) dated June 01, 2026, against AD Advertising Private Limited (PAN: AAJCA3551C), a defaulter in the matter of Fortis Healthcare Limited. The attachment is being effected under Recovery Certificate No. 8808 of 2025, directing banks to freeze and remit funds held in the defaulter's accounts towards recovery of dues owed to SEBI.

## Key Points

- Notice number: 15389 of 2026, dated June 01, 2026
- Defaulter: AD Advertising Private Limited (PAN: AAJCA3551C)
- Matter: Fortis Healthcare Limited
- Recovery Certificate No.: 8808 of 2025
- Action: Attachment of bank accounts held by the defaulter
- Banks are directed to freeze accounts and remit available balances to SEBI towards recovery

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an enforcement and recovery action under existing SEBI recovery provisions (Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992 read with the SEBI (Recovery of Amounts) Regulations).

## Compliance Requirements

- Banks holding accounts of AD Advertising Private Limited (PAN: AAJCA3551C) must immediately attach/freeze the accounts upon receipt of this notice.
- Banks are required to remit the available balance (up to the outstanding recovery amount) to SEBI.
- Banks must not allow any debit transactions from the attached accounts until further orders.
- Banks must acknowledge receipt of this notice and report the action taken to SEBI.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: June 01, 2026
- Recovery Certificate: No. 8808 of 2025
- Attachment Notice: No. 15389 of 2026

## Impact Assessment

This notice has no broad market impact. It is a targeted enforcement action against a specific defaulter entity (AD Advertising Private Limited) in connection with SEBI's recovery proceedings in the Fortis Healthcare Limited matter. The attachment of bank accounts is a standard recovery mechanism used by SEBI to enforce payment of penalty or disgorgement amounts from non-compliant entities. Market participants and investors in Fortis Healthcare Limited are not directly affected by this administrative action.
