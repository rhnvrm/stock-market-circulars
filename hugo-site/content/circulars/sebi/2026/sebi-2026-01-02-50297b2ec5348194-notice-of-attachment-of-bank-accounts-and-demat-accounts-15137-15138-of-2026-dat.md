## Summary

SEBI has issued Notice of Attachment (Nos. 15137 & 15138 of 2026 dated January 02, 2026) against Mr. Mahesh Kumar Sharma (PAN: AYYPS1602E) under Recovery Certificate No. 8969 of 2025. The attachment relates to the matter of inspection of CIG Realty Fund Scheme I, II and IV. This is an enforcement action for recovery of dues from the defaulter.

## Key Points

- Defaulter Name: Mr. Mahesh Kumar Sharma
- PAN: AYYPS1602E
- Notice Numbers: 15137 & 15138 of 2026
- Recovery Certificate No.: 8969 of 2025
- Matter: Inspection of CIG Realty Fund Scheme I, II and IV
- Date of Notice: January 02, 2026
- Action: Attachment of bank accounts and demat accounts

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI recovery procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

Banks and depository participants are required to comply with the attachment notices and freeze the specified accounts of the defaulter until further orders from SEBI.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: January 02, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a targeted enforcement action against a specific individual defaulter in relation to CIG Realty Fund schemes. The impact is limited to the defaulter and does not affect general market participants or operations. The attachment of accounts is a standard recovery mechanism used by SEBI to recover dues in enforcement matters.
