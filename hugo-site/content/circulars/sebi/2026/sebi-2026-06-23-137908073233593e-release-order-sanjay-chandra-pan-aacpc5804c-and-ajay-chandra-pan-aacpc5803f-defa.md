## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order against Sanjay Chandra (PAN: AACPC5804C) and Ajay Chandra (PAN: AACPC5803F), categorised as defaulters, in the matter of CIG Realty Fund Scheme I, II and IV. The release pertains to Recovery Certificate No. 8974 of 2025, signifying that the recovery proceedings under that certificate have been addressed to the extent warranted.

## Key Points

- Release Order issued against Sanjay Chandra (PAN: AACPC5804C) and Ajay Chandra (PAN: AACPC5803F).
- Both individuals are designated as defaulters in SEBI's records.
- The matter concerns CIG Realty Fund Scheme I, II and IV.
- Recovery Certificate No. 8974 of 2025 is the basis for this release action.
- The document was published in June 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is an administrative order concluding or partially concluding enforcement action under an existing recovery certificate.

## Compliance Requirements

- No new compliance obligations are imposed on market participants.
- The release order affects only the named defaulters (Sanjay Chandra and Ajay Chandra) with respect to Certificate No. 8974 of 2025.

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate No.: 8974 of 2025
- Release Order published: June 2026

## Impact Assessment

This order has limited market impact as it is an administrative enforcement action specific to named individuals in the context of CIG Realty Fund. Investors in CIG Realty Fund Scheme I, II and IV may note the progression of recovery proceedings. There is no direct impact on listed securities or broader market operations.
