## Summary

SEBI's Adjudicating Officer issued a corrigendum to Adjudication Order Ref. No. Order/MS/KS/2026-27/32482, dated July 14, 2026, which was passed in the matter of dealings in Illiquid Stock Options (ISO) at BSE concerning the late Ms. Anju Rani. The corrigendum corrects a clerical error in paragraph 12, page 4 of the original order, where the name was inadvertently mentioned as "Ms. Anju Rani" instead of the intended "Mr. Nitin Agarwal".

## Key Points

- The original Adjudication Order (Ref. No. Order/MS/KS/2026-27/32482) was passed on July 14, 2026 in the matter of dealings in Illiquid Stock Options at BSE involving the late Ms. Anju Rani.
- Paragraph 12, page 4 of the order incorrectly named "Ms. Anju Rani" where it should have read "Mr. Nitin Agarwal".
- The corrigendum directs that the name in paragraph 12 be read as "Mr. Nitin Agarwal".
- The original order must always be read together with this corrigendum; all other contents of the order remain unchanged.
- The corrigendum is to be served on the last known addresses of the deceased noticee and Mr. Nitin Agarwal, as well as to SEBI.

## Regulatory Changes

No substantive regulatory changes; this is a clerical correction to an existing adjudication order under SEBI's enforcement action related to illiquid stock options trading at BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

Parties associated with the original order, including the estate/representatives of the deceased noticee and Mr. Nitin Agarwal, should note the corrected name reference. No new compliance obligations are created beyond acknowledging the corrected order.

## Important Dates

- Original Order Date: July 14, 2026
- Corrigendum Date: July 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This corrigendum has minimal market or operational impact, as it is limited to correcting a clerical name error in an existing adjudication order rather than altering any substantive findings, penalties, or directions issued therein.
