## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand under Recovery Certificate No. RC9094 of 2026 against Ahilya Commercial Pvt. Ltd. (PAN: AACCA2226R) in connection with the matter of Parbati Holdings Limited. This notice demands payment of outstanding dues owed to SEBI, failing which recovery proceedings including attachment and sale of property may be initiated.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9094 of 2026 issued against Ahilya Commercial Pvt. Ltd.
- PAN of the defaulter entity: AACCA2226R
- Proceedings arise in the matter of Parbati Holdings Limited
- Notice of Demand requires payment of dues within the stipulated timeframe
- Non-compliance may result in attachment and sale of movable/immovable property under SEBI recovery rules

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an enforcement/recovery action under existing provisions of the SEBI Act, 1992 and the SEBI (Recovery of Dues) Rules, 1994.

## Compliance Requirements

- Ahilya Commercial Pvt. Ltd. must pay the demanded dues within the period specified in the notice
- Failure to comply will expose the entity to coercive recovery measures including property attachment
- The entity may file objections or representations as permitted under applicable rules

## Important Dates

- Notice issued: April 30, 2026
- Payment deadline: As specified in the notice (typically 7–15 days from date of service)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has no direct impact on broader markets or investors. It is a targeted enforcement action against a specific private company (Ahilya Commercial Pvt. Ltd.) for recovery of dues in the Parbati Holdings Limited matter. The action is routine in nature and part of SEBI's ongoing recovery proceedings against entities that have failed to pay monetary penalties or settlement amounts.
