## Summary

SEBI has issued a notice regarding the completion of Recovery Certificate No. 9100 of 2026, originally dated May 04, 2026, against Tarun Kumar Ghai (PAN: AAIPG1916F) in the matter of ISO (Investor Services Order) at BSE. The completion of this recovery certificate signifies the conclusion of recovery proceedings initiated against the defaulter.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 9100 of 2026 has been marked as completed.
- The certificate was originally issued on May 04, 2026 against Tarun Kumar Ghai.
- Tarun Kumar Ghai (PAN: AAIPG1916F) is the named defaulter in the matter.
- The matter pertains to ISO (Investor Services Order) proceedings at BSE.
- Completion indicates that the recovery process against the defaulter has concluded.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced by this notice. It is an administrative communication confirming the closure of an existing enforcement/recovery action.

## Compliance Requirements

This notice is specific to the named defaulter (Tarun Kumar Ghai) and does not impose new compliance obligations on other market participants. The completion of the recovery certificate typically implies the outstanding dues have been recovered or the matter has otherwise been resolved per SEBI's recovery procedures.

## Important Dates

- **Recovery Certificate Issue Date:** May 04, 2026 (RC No. 9100 of 2026)
- **Completion Notice Date:** June 23, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact of this circular is limited to the specific enforcement case against Tarun Kumar Ghai. It has no direct bearing on broader market operations, trading, or other participants. It signals the conclusion of SEBI's recovery proceedings in this matter at BSE.
