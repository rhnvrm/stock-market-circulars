## Summary

SEBI has issued notices of attachment (AP No. 15562 and AP No. 15563 of 2026, dated July 17, 2026) directing the attachment of bank accounts and demat accounts held by Amit Kumar Agarwal HUF. The notices have been issued under Recovery Certificate (RC) No. 9174 of 2026, in relation to a matter concerning dealing in illiquid stock options at BSE.

## Key Points

- Notices of attachment issued against Amit Kumar Agarwal HUF.
- Two attachment proceeding (AP) numbers involved: AP No. 15562 of 2026 and AP No. 15563 of 2026.
- Notices dated July 17, 2026.
- Issued under Recovery Certificate No. 9174 of 2026.
- Underlying matter relates to dealing in illiquid stock options at BSE.
- Attachment covers both bank accounts and demat accounts held by the entity.

## Regulatory Changes

This is an individual enforcement action rather than a rule or policy change. It represents SEBI's use of recovery proceedings to enforce an existing recovery certificate against a specific defaulting entity found to have dealt in illiquid stock options.

## Compliance Requirements

- Banks and depository participants holding accounts of Amit Kumar Agarwal HUF are required to comply with the attachment order and restrict operations on the specified accounts as directed.
- The entity (Amit Kumar Agarwal HUF) is subject to recovery proceedings under RC No. 9174 of 2026 and should address outstanding dues/compliance per the recovery certificate.

## Important Dates

- Notice date: July 17, 2026 (AP No. 15562 & 15563 of 2026).
- Recovery Certificate reference: RC No. 9174 of 2026.

## Impact Assessment

The notice has no direct impact on listed securities, market trading, or investors at large. Its effect is confined to the named entity, Amit Kumar Agarwal HUF, whose bank and demat accounts are being attached as part of SEBI's recovery enforcement process related to prior illiquid stock options trading violations at BSE. It serves as a continuing signal of SEBI's enforcement activity against illiquid derivatives trading practices.
