## Summary

SEBI's Recovery Officer has issued a Release Order under Recovery Certificate No. 8708 of 2025 against Gurumurthi Jayaraman in the matter of Securekloud Technologies Ltd. (formerly 8K Miles Software Services Ltd.). The release order directs that any assets or bank accounts attached during the recovery proceedings be released, as the outstanding dues recoverable under the certificate have been fully satisfied.

## Key Points

- Release Order issued under Recovery Certificate No. 8708 of 2025 against Gurumurthi Jayaraman.
- The matter pertains to Securekloud Technologies Ltd. (formerly 8K Miles Software Services Ltd.).
- A companion Completion Notice (circular ID: 53b08013bc6c3963) was also issued on the same date, confirming conclusion of recovery proceedings.
- The release order is issued by SEBI's Recovery Officer under the SEBI Act, 1992, authorising the release of any attached properties or bank balances.
- No further recovery action remains pending under RC No. 8708 of 2025.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is an administrative enforcement closure document confirming the release of attachments following full satisfaction of dues under Recovery Certificate No. 8708 of 2025.

## Compliance Requirements

- No new compliance obligations arise for market participants from this order.
- Attaching authorities (banks, depositories, or other entities) are directed to release any hold or attachment placed pursuant to RC No. 8708 of 2025.
- Gurumurthi Jayaraman and associated entities are no longer subject to asset attachment or recovery actions under this certificate.

## Important Dates

- **Recovery Certificate No.:** 8708 of 2025
- **Release Order Date:** June 8, 2026
- **Related Completion Notice Date:** June 8, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This order has minimal market impact as it represents the administrative closure of an individual enforcement action against Gurumurthi Jayaraman. Investors in Securekloud Technologies Ltd. (NSE: SECUREKLOUD) may note this as a resolution of a prior SEBI enforcement matter involving an individual associated with the company. No trading restrictions, financial obligations for the listed company, or changes to its listed status arise from this release order.
