## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Attachment dated July 08, 2026, under Recovery Certificate (RC) No. 9139 of 2026, drawn against Mr. K Sai Prasad (PAN: AAJPS6517M) in connection with recovery proceedings related to the matter of Mediaone Global Entertainment Limited. The notice, issued by SEBI's Recovery Officer, directs the attachment of the named individual's assets/accounts to recover amounts due pursuant to the underlying recovery certificate.

## Key Points

- Notice issued under RC No. 9139 of 2026, dated July 08, 2026.
- The notice is drawn against Mr. K Sai Prasad (PAN: AAJPS6517M).
- The proceeding arises in connection with the matter of Mediaone Global Entertainment Limited.
- This action is part of SEBI's enforcement/recovery mechanism used to recover unpaid dues (such as penalties, fees, or disgorgement amounts) from defaulting entities or associated individuals.
- The notice of attachment typically covers movable and/or immovable property, bank accounts, and other assets belonging to the noticee, restricting their transfer or disposal pending recovery.

## Regulatory Changes

This is not a rule-making or policy circular; it is an individual enforcement action taken under SEBI's recovery proceedings framework (recovery of dues as arrears of land revenue, in line with the Income Tax Act recovery provisions applied via SEBI Recovery Rules).

## Compliance Requirements

- Mr. K Sai Prasad, as the noticee, is required to comply with the terms of the attachment notice, which generally restricts dealing with, transferring, or alienating the attached property/assets without permission from the Recovery Officer.
- Any banks, depositories, or other institutions holding assets/accounts of the noticee may be required to act in accordance with the attachment order.
- Entities connected to Mediaone Global Entertainment Limited should be aware of ongoing recovery proceedings that may affect associated individuals.

## Important Dates

- Notice date: July 08, 2026.

## Impact Assessment

The direct impact is limited to the named individual, Mr. K Sai Prasad, whose assets are now subject to attachment restrictions under the recovery proceedings. There is no direct or immediate impact on trading, listing, or disclosure obligations for the broader market. Stakeholders connected to Mediaone Global Entertainment Limited may want to monitor further developments in this recovery matter, as it may be indicative of unresolved regulatory dues connected to the company or its associated persons.
