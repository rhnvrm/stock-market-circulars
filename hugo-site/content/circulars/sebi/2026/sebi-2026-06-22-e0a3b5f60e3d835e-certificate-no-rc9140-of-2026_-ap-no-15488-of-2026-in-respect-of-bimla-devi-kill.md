## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC9140 of 2026 under Attachment Proceedings (A.P.) No. 15488 of 2026 against Bimla Devi Killa (PAN: AESPK3270J) in the matter of illiquid stock options. The recovery certificate is a formal instrument authorising recovery of outstanding penalty or disgorgement amounts from the named individual who has failed to comply with earlier SEBI orders related to illegal/circular trading in illiquid stock options.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9140 of 2026 issued against Bimla Devi Killa (PAN: AESPK3270J)
- Proceedings initiated under A.P. No. 15488 of 2026
- Matter pertains to the illiquid stock options segment, subject to widespread SEBI enforcement for artificial/circular trading
- The certificate enables SEBI to recover dues directly from the individual's assets or accounts
- Part of SEBI's ongoing enforcement series against entities involved in illiquid stock options manipulation

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an administrative enforcement order specific to an individual recovery proceeding.

## Compliance Requirements

- Bimla Devi Killa (PAN: AESPK3270J) is required to discharge the dues specified in Recovery Certificate No. RC9140 of 2026
- Non-compliance may result in attachment of assets and further enforcement action under the SEBI Act
- No new compliance obligations arise for other market participants

## Important Dates

- Date of Recovery Certificate: 2026-06-22
- Recovery Certificate No.: RC9140 of 2026
- A.P. No.: 15488 of 2026

## Impact Assessment

This order has minimal broader market impact. It is an individual enforcement action against Bimla Devi Killa as part of SEBI's extensive enforcement proceedings in the illiquid stock options matter. The illiquid stock options enforcement series has resulted in numerous recovery certificates against individuals and entities found to have engaged in artificial/circular trading. No market-wide operational or trading implications arise from this specific certificate.
