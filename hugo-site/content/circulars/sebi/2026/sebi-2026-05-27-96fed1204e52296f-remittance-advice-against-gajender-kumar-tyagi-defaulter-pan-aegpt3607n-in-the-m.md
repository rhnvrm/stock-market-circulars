## Summary

SEBI has issued a Remittance Advice against Gajender Kumar Tyagi (PAN: AEGPT3607N), identified as a defaulter, in the matter of Mahadushi International Trade Limited (currently known as Negotium International Trade Limited). The remittance advice is issued under Recovery Certificate No. 7141 of 2023, indicating that SEBI has recovered dues and is directing their remittance to the appropriate parties.

## Key Points

- Defaulter: Gajender Kumar Tyagi (PAN: AEGPT3607N)
- Associated entity: Mahadushi International Trade Limited (currently Negotium International Trade Limited)
- Recovery Certificate No.: 7141 of 2023
- SEBI has proceeded with recovery action and is now issuing remittance advice for recovered amounts
- The company has undergone a name change from Mahadushi International Trade Limited to Negotium International Trade Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This document is an operational enforcement action — a remittance advice — within SEBI's existing recovery proceedings framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- The defaulter (Gajender Kumar Tyagi) and associated entities must comply with the remittance directions specified in the recovery proceedings.
- Relevant banks and financial intermediaries may be required to facilitate the remittance as directed under the Recovery Certificate.

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate No. 7141 issued: 2023
- Remittance Advice issued: 2026-05-27

## Impact Assessment

This remittance advice has no broad market or investor impact. It is a targeted enforcement action limited to the named defaulter and the company (Mahadushi International Trade Limited / Negotium International Trade Limited). The action reflects SEBI's ongoing enforcement of recovery certificates against individuals who have defaulted on dues in securities market matters.
