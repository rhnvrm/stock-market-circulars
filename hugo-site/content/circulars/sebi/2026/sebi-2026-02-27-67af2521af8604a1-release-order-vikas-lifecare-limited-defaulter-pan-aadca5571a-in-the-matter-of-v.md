## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order for Vikas Lifecare Limited (designated as a defaulter, PAN: AADCA5571A) in connection with Recovery Certificate No. 8713 of 2025 (originally dated April 25, 2025). This order releases any assets or properties that were attached or under enforcement action pursuant to the recovery proceedings. The release order is a companion document to the completion certificate issued on the same date, confirming that recovery obligations have been discharged and attached property is now free from encumbrance.

## Key Points

- SEBI issues a release order in favour of Vikas Lifecare Limited against Recovery Certificate No. 8713 of 2025
- The defaulter entity: Vikas Lifecare Limited, PAN: AADCA5571A
- Release order dated February 27, 2026, issued alongside the completion certificate for the same recovery certificate
- Any assets or securities attached under enforcement proceedings pursuant to RC No. 8713 of 2025 are hereby released
- The matter pertains to recovery proceedings against Vikas Lifecare Limited under the SEBI Act

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an administrative enforcement closure document directing the release of attached assets upon satisfaction of recovery dues under Recovery Certificate No. 8713 of 2025.

## Compliance Requirements

- No further compliance obligations for Vikas Lifecare Limited with respect to Recovery Certificate No. 8713 of 2025 following this release order
- Relevant parties (e.g., banks, depositories, or other custodians holding attached assets) are required to act upon the release order and lift any encumbrances placed under the recovery proceedings

## Important Dates

- April 25, 2025: Original Recovery Certificate No. 8713 of 2025 issued against Vikas Lifecare Limited
- February 27, 2026: Release Order issued by SEBI, releasing attached assets following satisfaction of dues

## Impact Assessment

This order has minimal broader market impact. It is an administrative enforcement closure specific to Vikas Lifecare Limited, releasing assets that were attached during recovery proceedings. The issuance of this release order, together with the companion completion certificate, confirms that Vikas Lifecare Limited has fully discharged its recovery obligations under RC No. 8713 of 2025. Investors in VIKASLIFE may view the resolution of this enforcement matter as a positive development for the company's regulatory standing, as it removes an outstanding SEBI enforcement encumbrance.
