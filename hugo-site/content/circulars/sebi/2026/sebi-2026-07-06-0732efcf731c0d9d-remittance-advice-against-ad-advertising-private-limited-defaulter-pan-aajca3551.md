## Summary

SEBI has published a remittance advice against AD Advertising Private Limited, which has been categorized as a "Defaulter" (PAN: AAJCA3551C), in connection with enforcement recovery proceedings arising in the matter of Fortis Healthcare Limited. The remittance advice pertains to Recovery Certificate No. 8808 of 2025, issued by SEBI's Recovery Officer to enforce recovery of outstanding dues from the defaulter.

## Key Points

- Entity involved: AD Advertising Private Limited (Defaulter), PAN: AAJCA3551C
- Underlying matter: Fortis Healthcare Limited
- Recovery certificate reference: No. 8808 of 2025
- Document type: Remittance advice issued as part of SEBI's recovery proceedings process
- Published under SEBI's enforcement/recovery proceedings section

## Regulatory Changes

This is not a regulatory or policy change; it is an enforcement action document. It reflects the operational execution of a previously issued recovery certificate against a defaulter entity, under SEBI's powers to recover outstanding statutory dues, penalties, or disgorgement amounts.

## Compliance Requirements

There are no new compliance obligations imposed on listed companies, market intermediaries, or investors through this notice. The remittance advice is an internal enforcement/administrative communication confirming the status of funds recovered or remitted pursuant to the recovery certificate against the named defaulter.

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate No. 8808 of 2025 (year of issuance: 2025)
- Remittance advice published: July 2026

## Impact Assessment

This notice has negligible direct impact on listed securities, trading activity, or market participants at large, as it concerns the recovery of dues from a specific defaulter entity (AD Advertising Private Limited) rather than any action against a publicly traded company. It is relevant primarily for tracking SEBI's enforcement and recovery proceedings record-keeping and for parties associated with the underlying Fortis Healthcare Limited matter.
