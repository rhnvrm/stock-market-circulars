## Summary

SEBI's Recovery Officer has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC9088 of 2026, arising out of Attachment Proceedings (A.P.) No. 15517 of 2026, against Mr. Ramesh Kumar Deosaria (PAN: AIBPD5871D) in the matter of illiquid stock options. The certificate formalises recovery proceedings for dues outstanding against the noticee, who was found liable in SEBI's long-running enforcement sweep against entities involved in non-genuine/reversal trades in illiquid stock options on the BSE derivatives segment.

## Key Points

- SEBI has initiated coercive recovery action against an individual noticee, Ramesh Kumar Deosaria, in connection with the illiquid stock options matter.
- Recovery Certificate No. RC9088 of 2026 and Attachment Proceedings No. 15517 of 2026 have been issued by the SEBI Recovery Officer.
- The certificate is directed at recovering monetary dues (such as an unpaid penalty or disgorgement amount) previously imposed on the noticee in earlier adjudication or enforcement proceedings.
- The matter forms part of SEBI's continued follow-up on enforcement actions taken against numerous entities identified in the illiquid stock options investigations.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This document does not introduce any new rule, regulation, or policy; it is an individual recovery action against a specific noticee.

## Compliance Requirements

The noticee, Ramesh Kumar Deosaria, is required to clear the outstanding dues specified in the recovery certificate. Such certificates are typically issued and enforced under Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992, read with the Second and Third Schedules to the Income Tax Act, 1961, and the Income Tax (Certificate Proceedings) Rules, 1962. Non-compliance can result in further coercive measures, including attachment and sale of movable or immovable property, arrest and detention, or appointment of a receiver, as provided under the recovery framework.

## Important Dates

The certificate and attachment proceedings are numbered for 2026; the precise issuance and compliance deadline dates are not discernible from the available source metadata (the underlying document is a scanned image without extractable text).

## Impact Assessment

This is a narrowly scoped enforcement/recovery action concerning a single individual and does not affect any listed company, security, or market segment. It carries no direct market impact but reflects SEBI's ongoing efforts to enforce recovery of penalties levied in the illiquid stock options cases.
