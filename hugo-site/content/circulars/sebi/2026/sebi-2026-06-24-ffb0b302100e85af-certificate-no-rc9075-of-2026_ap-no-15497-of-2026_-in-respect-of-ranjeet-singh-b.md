## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC9075 of 2026 under Application (A.P.) No. 15497 of 2026 against Ranjeet Singh Baid HUF (PAN: AAPHR3293F) for recovery of penalty amount that remained unpaid following adjudication proceedings in the matter of trading in illiquid stock options on BSE.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9075 of 2026 issued by SEBI
- Application No. A.P. 15497 of 2026
- Party: Ranjeet Singh Baid HUF (PAN: AAPHR3293F)
- Matter relates to trading in illiquid stock options on BSE
- Certificate authorises recovery of outstanding penalty dues as arrears of land revenue
- Part of SEBI's broader enforcement action against entities involved in illiquid stock options manipulation

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This document is an enforcement instrument — a Recovery Certificate — enabling SEBI to recover unpaid penalty amounts from the defaulting entity under Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992.

## Compliance Requirements

- Ranjeet Singh Baid HUF is required to pay the outstanding penalty amount as specified in the original adjudication order
- The Recovery Certificate enables SEBI to recover dues through the Collector/District Magistrate as arrears of land revenue
- The entity must settle all outstanding dues to avoid further recovery proceedings

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate issued: June 2026
- Original matter: Illiquid Stock Options on BSE (historical enforcement action)

## Impact Assessment

This recovery certificate has limited broader market impact and is specific to the named entity — Ranjeet Singh Baid HUF. It is part of SEBI's ongoing enforcement sweep against participants who traded in illiquid stock options on BSE to create artificial volumes or manipulate prices. The issuance of such certificates demonstrates SEBI's continued follow-through on collecting unpaid penalties from entities penalised in the illiquid stock options matter. No impact on listed securities or market operations.
