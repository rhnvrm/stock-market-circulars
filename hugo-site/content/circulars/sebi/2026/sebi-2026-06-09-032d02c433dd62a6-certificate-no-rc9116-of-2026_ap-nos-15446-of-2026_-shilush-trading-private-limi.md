## Summary

SEBI has issued attachment proceedings under Recovery Certificate No. RC9116 of 2026 (A.P. No. 15446 of 2026) against Shilush Trading Private Limited (PAN: AALCS0075M) in connection with the matter of Illiquid Stock Options. This document represents a recovery action to enforce an outstanding monetary penalty or disgorgement order against the entity.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9116 of 2026 issued against Shilush Trading Private Limited
- Attachment Proceedings No. 15446 of 2026 initiated under SEBI's recovery mechanism
- Entity PAN: AALCS0075M (Shilush Trading Private Limited)
- Matter relates to violations in the Illiquid Stock Options segment
- SEBI is pursuing recovery of dues through attachment of assets

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement/recovery action under existing SEBI regulations and recovery certificate provisions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Shilush Trading Private Limited is required to comply with the attachment order and satisfy the outstanding recovery certificate amount
- Third parties holding assets of the entity may be required to respond to the attachment notice
- Any banks or depositories holding assets of the entity are directed to comply with the attachment directions

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate: RC9116 of 2026
- Attachment Proceedings: A.P. No. 15446 of 2026
- Document date: June 2026

## Impact Assessment

This enforcement action has limited broader market impact and is specific to Shilush Trading Private Limited. The illiquid stock options segment has been a focus of SEBI enforcement for several years, with numerous entities penalized for engaging in circular/synchronized trading to generate fictitious profits or facilitate tax evasion. This recovery action signals continued enforcement of prior penalty orders against such entities.
