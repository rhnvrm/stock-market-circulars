## Summary

SEBI has notified the completion of Recovery Certificate No. RC8981 of 2025 issued against Piccadily Sales and Services Pvt. Ltd. (since amalgamated into Plenty Niryat Pvt. Ltd., PAN: AABCP6776C) in the matter of Thirani Projects Ltd. This circular marks the closure of the recovery proceedings initiated under the said certificate.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC8981 of 2025 has been completed.
- The defaulter entity is Piccadily Sales and Services Pvt. Ltd., which has since been amalgamated into Plenty Niryat Pvt. Ltd. (PAN: AABCP6776C).
- The recovery pertains to proceedings in the matter of Thirani Projects Ltd.
- SEBI's recovery mechanism has been successfully enforced, resulting in the closure of this certificate.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced by this circular. It is an administrative closure notice confirming that the outstanding dues under Recovery Certificate No. RC8981 of 2025 have been satisfied.

## Compliance Requirements

No further compliance action is required from market participants. The concerned entity (Plenty Niryat Pvt. Ltd., as successor to Piccadily Sales and Services Pvt. Ltd.) has fulfilled its obligations under the recovery certificate.

## Important Dates

- **Recovery Certificate No.:** RC8981 of 2025
- **Completion notified:** May 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal broader market impact. It represents the successful conclusion of SEBI's enforcement and recovery action against a private entity involved in the Thirani Projects Ltd. matter. The amalgamation of the defaulter entity into Plenty Niryat Pvt. Ltd. did not impede SEBI's recovery process, signalling the regulator's ability to pursue dues through corporate restructuring events.
