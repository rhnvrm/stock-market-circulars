## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand dated June 29, 2026 under Recovery Certificate (RC) No. 9181 of 2026 against Rathod Mahendrkumar. The notice pertains to an investigation into the trading activities of certain entities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited, now renamed DU Digital Global Limited. This is a recovery action to enforce an earlier SEBI order requiring payment of dues.

## Key Points

- Notice of Demand issued under RC No. 9181 of 2026 dated June 29, 2026
- Demand raised against: Rathod Mahendrkumar
- Matter relates to trading activities of certain entities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited)
- SEBI is exercising its recovery powers under the SEBI Act to enforce collection of dues arising from a prior order
- The RC mechanism is used by SEBI when an entity fails to voluntarily comply with a penalty or disgorgement order

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This circular represents enforcement of an existing SEBI order through the recovery certificate mechanism.

## Compliance Requirements

- Rathod Mahendrkumar is required to pay the demanded amount as specified in the Recovery Certificate No. 9181 of 2026
- Non-compliance may result in further coercive recovery actions including attachment of bank accounts and demat accounts
- The named individual should contact the relevant SEBI Recovery Officer to resolve the demand

## Important Dates

- Date of Notice of Demand: June 29, 2026
- RC No. 9181 of 2026 issued as of June 29, 2026
- Payment deadline: As specified in the recovery certificate (refer to the original document)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has a limited broader market impact as it is directed at a specific individual (Rathod Mahendrkumar) in connection with a prior enforcement proceeding related to trading in DU Digital Global Limited (formerly DU Digital Technologies Limited). The action signals continued SEBI vigilance over market manipulation and irregular trading activities. Investors in DU Digital Global Limited should note this as part of ongoing regulatory scrutiny of trading in the scrip, but no immediate trading restrictions or operational changes are expected for the stock.
