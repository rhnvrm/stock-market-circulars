## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand dated 02.07.2026 under Recovery Certificate No. 9190 of 2026 against Girish Kantilal Parmar (PAN: AUDPP6127A), directing payment of Rs. 10,58,558.98 (including principal disgorgement amount, interest, and recovery cost). The demand arises from a disgorgement order passed by the Quasi-Judicial Authority on 31.12.2025 in connection with trading activities of certain entities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited).

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 9190 of 2026 issued under section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992 read with section 222 of the Income Tax Act, 1961.
- Total amount due: Rs. 10,58,558.98, comprising disgorgement amount of Rs. 9,88,372.88, interest of Rs. 69,186.10 (Jan 2026 to Jul 2026 @ 12% p.a.), and recovery cost of Rs. 1,000.
- Underlying order: Order No. QJA/MN/IVD/ID7/31947/2025-26 dated 31.12.2025, relating to trading activities of certain entities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited).
- Entity must pay within 15 days of receipt via EFT/NEFT/RTGS or SEBI's online "Recovery Payment" portal, failing which SEBI's Recovery Officer will initiate recovery under sections 220-232 of the Income-tax Act, 1961.
- Non-payment may lead to attachment/sale of movable and immovable property, attachment of bank accounts, arrest and detention, or appointment of a receiver.
- Transfers of property to spouse, minor child, son's wife, or son's minor child on or after 31.12.2025 (except for adequate consideration) are deemed to be the defaulter's property for recovery purposes.
- The entity is barred from mortgaging, charging, leasing, or otherwise dealing with property without the Recovery Officer's permission after service of the notice.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an enforcement action implementing a prior disgorgement order, not a new regulatory change.

## Compliance Requirements

Girish Kantilal Parmar must pay the demanded amount within 15 days of receiving the notice and forward payment confirmation to SEBI's Recovery Officer. No compliance obligations apply to other market participants.

## Important Dates

- 31.12.2025: Underlying disgorgement order (QJA/MN/IVD/ID7/31947/2025-26) passed.
- 02.07.2026: Notice of Demand issued under RC No. 9190 of 2026.
- Payment due within 15 days of receipt of the notice.

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine individual enforcement/recovery notice targeting a specific person found liable for disgorgement in a past trading matter. It carries no direct impact on DU Digital Technologies/DU Digital Global Limited's listed securities, market operations, or other investors, and is relevant primarily as a compliance and enforcement tracking item.
