## Summary

SEBI has issued a prohibitory order under Recovery Certificate No. 4693 of 2022 against Inaganti Vishnu Sekhar (PAN: AAMPI3761J) in the matter of Venus Power Ventures (India) Ltd. This order pertains to recovery proceedings for outstanding dues owed to SEBI.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 4693 of 2022 issued against Inaganti Vishnu Sekhar
- PAN of the individual: AAMPI3761J
- Matter relates to Venus Power Ventures (India) Ltd.
- Prohibitory order issued to enforce recovery of dues
- Order published February 25, 2026 under SEBI's enforcement/recovery proceedings

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action under existing recovery mechanisms established by SEBI.

## Compliance Requirements

- Inaganti Vishnu Sekhar must comply with the recovery certificate and prohibitory order
- Outstanding dues to SEBI must be settled as per Recovery Certificate No. 4693 of 2022
- Non-compliance may result in further enforcement actions including attachment of assets
- Banks and financial institutions are directed not to allow disposal of assets belonging to the defaulter

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate No.: 4693 of 2022
- Prohibitory Order Date: February 25, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is an individual enforcement action with limited broader market implications.

**Entity Impact**: High for Inaganti Vishnu Sekhar - Prohibitory measures restrict financial activities until recovery is completed.

**Compliance Significance**: This order demonstrates SEBI's continued enforcement of recovery proceedings against individuals with outstanding dues from previous regulatory actions or penalties related to Venus Power Ventures (India) Ltd.
