## Summary

SEBI has issued Notice of Attachment of Bank Accounts (No. 15386 of 2026) dated May 26, 2026, against Sattu Yadav (Defaulter), PAN: AHXPY0089P, under Recovery Certificate No. 9004 of 2026. The action arises from proceedings related to the defaulter's dealings in illiquid stock options at BSE.

## Key Points

- Notice number: 15386 of 2026, dated May 26, 2026
- Defaulter: Sattu Yadav, PAN: AHXPY0089P
- Recovery Certificate No.: 9004 of 2026
- Matter: Dealing in illiquid stock options at BSE
- Action: Attachment of bank accounts of the defaulter

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement/recovery measure under existing SEBI recovery proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

- Banks and financial institutions holding accounts of Sattu Yadav (PAN: AHXPY0089P) are directed to comply with the attachment order and freeze the specified accounts.
- Any funds held in the attached accounts must not be released without SEBI's prior approval.

## Important Dates

- Notice date: May 26, 2026
- Recovery Certificate No. 9004 of 2026 (date of certificate not specified in title)

## Impact Assessment

This action is limited in scope, targeting a single named individual (Sattu Yadav) in connection with illiquid stock option dealings at BSE. There is no broader market impact. The attachment restricts the defaulter's access to bank funds until dues under the recovery certificate are satisfied. Banks servicing the defaulter must comply immediately upon receipt of the notice.
