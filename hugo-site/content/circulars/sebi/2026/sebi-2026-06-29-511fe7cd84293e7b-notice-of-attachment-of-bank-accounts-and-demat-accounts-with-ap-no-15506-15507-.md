## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Attachment of Bank Accounts and Demat Accounts vide Attachment Proceedings No. 15506 & 15507 of 2026 dated June 29, 2026, against Mr. Mrugesh Natwarlal Ruparel (PAN: AITPR2718M) as a defaulter. This action arises from Recovery Certificate No. 9151 of 2026 in the matter of price and volume manipulation in the scrip of Quasar India Limited.

## Key Points

- Attachment Proceedings: AP No. 15506 & 15507 of 2026 dated June 29, 2026
- Defaulter: Mr. Mrugesh Natwarlal Ruparel (PAN: AITPR2718M)
- Recovery Certificate: No. 9151 of 2026
- Underlying matter: Price and volume manipulation in the scrip of Quasar India Limited
- Action: Attachment of bank accounts and demat accounts held by the defaulter
- Banks and depositories are directed to freeze/attach accounts as specified in the attachment order

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement/recovery action under existing SEBI (Recovery of Money) Regulations and the SEBI Act, 1992.

## Compliance Requirements

- Banks holding accounts of Mr. Mrugesh Natwarlal Ruparel must comply with the attachment order and freeze the specified accounts
- Depositories and depository participants must attach the demat accounts of the defaulter as directed
- Any funds or securities held in the attached accounts cannot be released without SEBI's permission
- Entities receiving this notice must acknowledge and act upon the attachment order promptly

## Important Dates

- Attachment Notice Date: June 29, 2026
- Recovery Certificate No. 9151 of 2026 (date of issuance as per underlying proceedings)

## Impact Assessment

The market-wide impact of this enforcement action is limited, as it targets a single named individual defaulter. However, it signals SEBI's continued enforcement of market manipulation cases involving the scrip of Quasar India Limited. Investors and market participants in Quasar India Limited should be aware of the ongoing regulatory scrutiny. Banks and depositories named in the attachment order face immediate compliance obligations.
