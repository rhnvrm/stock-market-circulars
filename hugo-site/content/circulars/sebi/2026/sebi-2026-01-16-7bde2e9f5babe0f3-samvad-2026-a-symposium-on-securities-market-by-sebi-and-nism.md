## Summary

SEBI in collaboration with NISM, and supported by NSE, BSE, NSDL and CDSL, organized the annual flagship Symposium 'Samvad 2026' on January 16, 2026 at Jio World Convention Centre, Mumbai. The event, themed 'Sustained Capital Formation', brought together regulators, policy makers, academicians, industry leaders, market infrastructure institutions and investors to discuss market efficiency, innovation, investor trust and broader participation across asset classes. The Symposium featured keynote addresses from Shri Arvind Virmani (Member, NITI Aayog), Ms. Samara Cohen (Global Head of Market Development, BlackRock), and Shri Tuhin Kanta Pandey (Chairman, SEBI). SEBI also unveiled the full report of the 'Investor Survey 2025' during the event.

## Key Points

- Annual flagship Symposium 'Samvad 2026' held on January 16, 2026 in Mumbai with theme 'Sustained Capital Formation'
- Organized by SEBI in collaboration with NISM and supported by NSE, BSE, NSDL and CDSL
- Chief Guest: Shri Arvind Virmani, Member, NITI Aayog
- Keynote Address: Ms. Samara Cohen, Global Head of Market Development, BlackRock
- Chairman SEBI Shri Tuhin Kanta Pandey outlined vision for future of India's capital markets
- Full report of 'Investor Survey 2025' unveiled (undertaken by SEBI with AMFI, NSE, BSE, NSDL and CDSL)
- Fireside chat on 'India as a Magnet for Long-Term Global Capital' with Chairman SEBI
- Panel discussions covered: Reimagining the Securities Market, Going Public challenges for Startups and SMEs, and Investor Education in the Era of Technological Transformation
- Event broadcasted live and archived on www.sebi.gov.in
