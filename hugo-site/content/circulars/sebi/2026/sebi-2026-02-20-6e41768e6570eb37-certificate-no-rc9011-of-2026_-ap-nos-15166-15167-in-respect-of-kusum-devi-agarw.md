## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC9011 of 2026 under Adjudication Proceedings (A.P.) Nos. 15166 & 15167 against Kusum Devi Agarwal (PAN: AFBPA5176P) in connection with the Illiquid Stock Options matter. This notice of demand requires the named individual to pay outstanding dues as determined by SEBI's adjudication process.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9011 of 2026 has been issued by SEBI
- Covers two adjudication proceedings: A.P. Nos. 15166 and 15167
- Named defaulter: Kusum Devi Agarwal (PAN: AFBPA5176P)
- Relates to the Illiquid Stock Options segment enforcement action
- This is a formal Notice of Demand for recovery of adjudicated dues

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This document is an enforcement/recovery action under existing SEBI (Recovery of Amounts) Rules.

## Compliance Requirements

- Kusum Devi Agarwal is required to pay the outstanding dues as specified in the recovery certificate
- Failure to comply may result in further enforcement actions including attachment of assets

## Important Dates

- Certificate Issue Date: February 20, 2026
- Demand due as per the terms specified in the recovery certificate

## Impact Assessment

This circular has no broader market or systemic impact. It is an individual enforcement action targeting a specific person found to have participated in the Illiquid Stock Options segment irregularities. The Illiquid Stock Options matter has been one of SEBI's long-running enforcement campaigns against manipulative trading in the BSE options segment.
