## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC9051 of 2026 under A.P No. 15395 of 2026 against Vineeta Kabra (PAN: ABNPL7384C) for recovery of outstanding dues arising from the Illiquid Stock Options matter. This certificate authorizes SEBI to recover the specified penalty amount as arrears of land revenue.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9051 of 2026 issued against Vineeta Kabra (PAN: ABNPL7384C)
- Attachment/Penalty Proceeding No.: A.P No. 15395 of 2026
- Action pertains to the Illiquid Stock Options enforcement matter
- SEBI is authorized to recover dues as arrears of land revenue under applicable law

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI recovery provisions.

## Compliance Requirements

- The named defaulter (Vineeta Kabra) is required to pay the outstanding dues specified in the Recovery Certificate
- Failure to pay may result in attachment of assets and bank accounts
- The certificate empowers the designated authority to recover the dues coercively if voluntary payment is not made

## Important Dates

- Certificate issued: June 2026
- Recovery Certificate No.: RC9051 of 2026
- A.P No.: 15395 of 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is an individual-level enforcement action with no direct broad market impact. It is part of SEBI's ongoing effort to recover penalties imposed on entities found to have engaged in manipulative trading in illiquid stock options on the BSE. The illiquid stock options enforcement drive has targeted numerous individuals and entities over the years; this action reflects continued follow-through on pending recovery proceedings.
