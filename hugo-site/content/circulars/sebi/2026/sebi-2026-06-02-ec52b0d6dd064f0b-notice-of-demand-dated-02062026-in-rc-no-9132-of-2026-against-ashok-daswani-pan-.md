## Summary

SEBI's Recovery and Refund Department has issued a Notice of Demand under RC No. 9132 of 2026 against four individuals — Ashok Daswani (PAN: AAEPD1306N), Kanchan Mansinghani (PAN: AAQPM1032Q), Sunil Mansinghani (PAN: AARPM7113P), and Surendra Gama Yadav (PAN: AAIPY0498C) — for recovery of Rs. 24,11,28,108.15 (approximately Rs. 24.11 crore) in the matter of Sunchan Securities Limited. The demand arises from a disgorgement order passed by the Whole Time Member (WTM) in April 2014.

## Key Points

- Total amount demanded: Rs. 24,11,28,108.15 (Rupees Twenty-Four Crore Eleven Lakh Twenty-Eight Thousand One Hundred Eight and Fifteen Paisa)
- Principal disgorgement amount: Rs. 9,76,22,310.99 (ordered via WTM Order No. WTM/PS/03/MIRSD-2/APR/2014 dated 23.04.2014)
- Accrued interest (April 2014 to June 2026 @ 1% per month): Rs. 14,35,04,797.16
- Recovery cost: Rs. 1,000.00
- Issued under Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992 read with Section 222 of the Income Tax Act, 1961
- Respondents are based in Mumbai (Mahim and Prabhadevi)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an enforcement action invoking existing provisions of Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992 and the Second Schedule to the Income Tax Act, 1961 to recover outstanding dues from a 2014 order.

## Compliance Requirements

- The four named individuals must pay the total demanded amount of Rs. 24,11,28,108.15 within **15 days** of receipt of this notice.
- Payment must be made via EFT/NEFT/RTGS to A/c No. SEBIRNCIS9132, Bank of India, IFS Code: BKID00VKN04, or through the online payment facility at https://siportal.sebi.gov.in, or via the SEBI website under Enforcement > Recovery Proceedings > Pay Now.
- Upon service of this notice, the respondents are **not permitted** to mortgage, charge, lease, or otherwise deal with any of their properties without prior permission from the Recovery Officer.
- Any transfer of property or bank account monies to a spouse, minor child, son's wife, or son's minor child (other than for adequate consideration) on or after 23.04.2014 shall be deemed the respondent's property for recovery purposes.

## Important Dates

- Original WTM Disgorgement Order: 23 April 2014 (WTM/PS/03/MIRSD-2/APR/2014)
- Interest accrual period: April 2014 to June 2026
- Notice date: 02 June 2026
- Payment deadline: Within 15 days of receipt of notice

## Impact Assessment

This notice has limited broader market impact as it is directed at specific individuals in connection with a decade-old enforcement matter involving Sunchan Securities Limited. However, it signals SEBI's continued pursuit of outstanding recovery certificates, including those with significant interest accumulation. Failure to comply may result in attachment and sale of movable/immovable property, attachment of bank accounts, arrest and detention, or appointment of a receiver — escalating consequences for the named respondents.
