## Summary

SEBI has issued a Remittance Advice against Pankaj Goel (PAN: ACTPG7828D), designated as a defaulter, in the matter of ISO at BSE – Pankaj Goel. This remittance advice is issued pursuant to Recovery Certificate No. 8390 of 2024, directing the relevant parties to remit outstanding dues owed by the defaulter to SEBI.

## Key Points

- Defaulter: Pankaj Goel, PAN: ACTPG7828D
- Matter: ISO at BSE – Pankaj Goel
- Recovery Certificate No.: 8390 of 2024
- Document type: Remittance Advice issued by SEBI under its recovery proceedings
- The remittance advice directs recovery of dues from the defaulter's assets or accounts

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This document is part of SEBI's ongoing enforcement and recovery mechanism under existing powers.

## Compliance Requirements

- Entities in receipt of this remittance advice (exchanges, depositories, banks, or custodians) are required to remit the specified amount to SEBI as directed.
- Non-compliance with remittance advice can attract further enforcement action.

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate No. 8390 issued: 2024
- Remittance Advice issued: May 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is an individual enforcement action targeting a single defaulter (Pankaj Goel) at BSE. The market-wide and systemic impact is negligible. The action reflects SEBI's continued enforcement of recovery proceedings against members or participants who have defaulted on their obligations.
