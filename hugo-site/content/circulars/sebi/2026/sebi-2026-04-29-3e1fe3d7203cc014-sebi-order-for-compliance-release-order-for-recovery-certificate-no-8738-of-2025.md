## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order for Recovery Certificate No. 8738 of 2025 against 3M Team Research Private Limited, Rakesh Sethi, and Pramod Jain. This order is issued in the matter of unregistered portfolio management services provided by 3M Team Research Private Limited. The release order indicates that the concerned parties have fulfilled their financial obligations as mandated under the recovery certificate, and SEBI is formally releasing the certificate.

## Key Points

- SEBI issued a Release Order for Recovery Certificate No. 8738 of 2025
- The order is against three parties: (1) 3M Team Research Private Limited, (2) Rakesh Sethi, and (3) Pramod Jain
- The underlying matter relates to the provision of unregistered portfolio management services by 3M Team Research Private Limited
- The release order signifies that the recovery proceedings have been concluded following compliance by the noticees
- This is an enforcement/recovery action under SEBI's jurisdiction over unregistered intermediaries

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced by this order. It is a closure document in existing enforcement proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

- The named parties (3M Team Research Private Limited, Rakesh Sethi, and Pramod Jain) are required to have discharged all dues and penalties as stipulated under Recovery Certificate No. 8738 of 2025
- No further compliance obligations arise from this release order itself, as it marks the conclusion of the recovery proceedings

## Important Dates

- **Order Date:** April 29, 2026
- **Recovery Certificate No.:** 8738 of 2025

## Impact Assessment

This order has minimal direct market impact as it pertains to the administrative closure of a recovery certificate against specific individuals and a private limited company involved in unregistered portfolio management services. It does not affect listed securities, trading operations, or broader market participants. The order reinforces SEBI's ongoing enforcement stance against unregistered intermediaries offering portfolio management services without requisite SEBI registration.
