## Summary

SEBI has introduced a lighter certification pathway for Persons Associated with Investment Advice (PAIA) who perform only sales and other non-core services. Such staff — including sales staff and relationship managers who interact with clients but are not directly involved in investment advice — will now be required to pass the new NISM Series-XXV-B examination instead of the full NISM Series-X-A and X-B examinations. This change is effective immediately.

## Key Points

- A new NISM certification — "NISM Series-XXV-B: Persons Associated with Investment Advice (Sales and Other Non-Core Services) Certification Examination" — is introduced for PAIA in sales and non-core roles.
- PAIA involved in core investment advice activities must continue to hold both NISM Series-X-A (Level 1) and NISM Series-X-B (Level 2) certifications.
- PAIA who already hold valid NISM Series-X-A and X-B certifications are not required to obtain Series-XXV-B immediately; they must do so only before the expiry of their existing certifications.
- The Investment Adviser Administration and Supervisory Body (IAASB) is directed to amend its bye-laws and rules and disseminate this circular to registered Investment Advisers.

## Regulatory Changes

Under Regulation 7 of the SEBI (Investment Advisers) Regulations, 2013, all PAIA were previously required to obtain NISM Series-X-A and X-B certifications. This circular carves out a sub-category of PAIA — those in sales and non-core roles — and mandates a new, lighter NISM Series-XXV-B certification for them. The change is grounded in feedback from market participants and is part of SEBI's ongoing ease-of-doing-business initiative.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Investment Advisers** must identify PAIA who perform only sales and non-core services and ensure they obtain the NISM Series-XXV-B certification.
- **PAIA in sales/non-core roles** with existing valid Series-X-A and X-B certifications need not immediately switch; transition to Series-XXV-B is required upon expiry of current certifications.
- **PAIA in core investment advice roles** must continue obtaining and renewing NISM Series-X-A and X-B certifications.
- **IAASB** must amend relevant bye-laws/rules and notify all registered Investment Advisers.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** June 24, 2026 (immediate effect).
- **NISM Series-XXV-B Communique Reference:** NISM/Investment Certification/NISM-Series-XXV-B/2026/01 dated May 18, 2026.
- **Transition deadline for existing certificate holders:** Before expiry of their current NISM Series-X-A / X-B certifications.

## Impact Assessment

This circular provides regulatory relief to investment adviser firms by reducing the certification burden on sales staff, relationship managers, and other non-core personnel. It lowers barriers to staffing client-facing roles without compromising the standards required for those directly providing investment advice. The impact is primarily operational for Investment Adviser entities and their PAIA workforce, with no direct effect on securities markets, trading, or listed companies.
