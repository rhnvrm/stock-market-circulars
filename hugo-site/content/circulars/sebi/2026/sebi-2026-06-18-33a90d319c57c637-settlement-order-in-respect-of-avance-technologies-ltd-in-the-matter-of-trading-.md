## Summary

SEBI issued a settlement order (No. SO/MS/BK/2026-27/8672) against Avance Technologies Ltd. (PAN: AAECA5763B) in connection with off-market transactions in shares of Cressanda Solutions Limited. The company filed a settlement application (No. 8672/2025) to resolve adjudication proceedings without admission of liability or guilt. SEBI's Internal Committee recommended a settlement amount of ₹9,75,000.

## Key Points

- Avance Technologies Ltd. entered into off-market transactions in shares of Cressanda Solutions Ltd. without complying with Spot Delivery Contract provisions under Section 2(i) of SCRA, 1956
- Alleged violations: Section 16 of SCRA read with SEBI Notification No. LAD-NRO/GN/2013-14/26/6667 dated October 03, 2013; Sections 13 and 18 read with Section 2(i) of SCRA, 1956
- Show Cause Notice No. SEBI/EAD-3/AN/RG/18054/7/2025 was issued on July 09, 2025
- Settlement application filed under SEBI (Settlement Proceedings) Regulations, 2018
- SEBI's Internal Committee met on March 05, 2026 and recommended Indicative Settlement Amount of ₹9,75,000
- Settlement is without admission of liability or guilt

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This order applies existing provisions of SCRA, 1956 and SEBI (Settlement Proceedings) Regulations, 2018.

## Compliance Requirements

- Avance Technologies Ltd. is required to submit Revised Settlement Terms (RST) within 15 working days from the date of the Internal Committee meeting
- Settlement amount of ₹9,75,000 must be paid as computed under Schedule II read with Regulation 10 of SEBI (Settlement Proceedings) Regulations, 2018
- Entities conducting off-market transactions must comply with Spot Delivery Contract provisions under Section 2(i) of SCRA, 1956

## Important Dates

- July 09, 2025: Show Cause Notice issued
- March 05, 2026: SEBI Internal Committee meeting; Indicative Settlement Amount recommended
- June 09, 2026: Current Adjudicating Officer appointed
- June 18, 2026: Settlement order date

## Impact Assessment

This order has limited broader market impact as it concerns a single entity (Avance Technologies Ltd.) and involves a modest settlement amount of ₹9,75,000. The matter relates to off-market transactions in Cressanda Solutions Ltd. shares without following Spot Delivery Contract norms. The settlement, without admission of guilt, closes the adjudication proceeding and serves as a reminder to market participants about compliance with SCRA off-market transaction requirements.
