## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand dated June 29, 2026 under Recovery Certificate (RC) No. 9177 of 2026 against Vidhi Nikunj Shah. The notice arises from SEBI's investigation into trading activities of certain entities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited, now renamed DU Digital Global Limited. This is a formal demand for payment of dues pursuant to a prior SEBI order imposing monetary penalty or disgorgement.

## Key Points

- Notice of Demand issued under RC No. 9177 of 2026 dated June 29, 2026
- Respondent: Vidhi Nikunj Shah
- Matter relates to trading activities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited)
- Issued under SEBI's recovery proceedings mechanism for enforcement of monetary orders
- Part of a broader SEBI enforcement action involving multiple entities in the same scrip

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular is an enforcement/recovery action under existing SEBI recovery and penalty frameworks.

## Compliance Requirements

- Vidhi Nikunj Shah is required to pay the demanded amount as specified in the Recovery Certificate within the stipulated time period
- Failure to comply may result in further recovery measures including attachment of bank accounts, demat accounts, and other assets
- The respondent may approach SEBI or the appropriate appellate authority if contesting the demand

## Important Dates

- Notice of Demand date: June 29, 2026
- RC No. 9177 of 2026 issued: June 29, 2026
- Payment deadline: As specified in the demand notice (typically 15–30 days from date of notice)

## Impact Assessment

This enforcement action is directed at a specific individual (Vidhi Nikunj Shah) and has no direct broader market impact. It signals continued SEBI vigilance over trading irregularities in mid/small-cap scrips such as DU Digital Global Limited. Multiple RCs have been issued in this matter (as evidenced by related circulars with nearby RC numbers), indicating a coordinated enforcement sweep against several entities involved in alleged trading misconduct in this scrip.
