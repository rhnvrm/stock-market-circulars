## Summary

SEBI's Recovery Officer has issued Certificate No. RC9196 of 2026 along with a Notice of Demand against Prachi Tradevine Pvt. Ltd. (PAN: AAFCP7889L) in connection with enforcement proceedings related to dealing in illiquid stock options. The certificate initiates formal recovery proceedings for outstanding dues owed by the noticee, following an underlying SEBI order or penalty determination in the illiquid stock options matter. Note: the source document is a scanned image file without an extractable text layer, so granular details such as the exact demand amount, recovery officer's name, and specific notice date could not be retrieved; this summary is based on the available title and metadata.

## Key Points

- SEBI has issued a formal Certificate of Recovery (Certificate No. RC9196 of 2026) against Prachi Tradevine Pvt. Ltd.
- The noticee is identified by PAN: AAFCP7889L.
- The underlying matter relates to dealing in illiquid stock options, a category SEBI has historically penalized under its surveillance of the options segment.
- A Notice of Demand accompanies the certificate, formally requiring payment of outstanding dues.
- This is an enforcement/recovery proceeding, not a new regulatory policy or market-wide directive.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced by this notice. It is an enforcement action pursuant to SEBI's existing framework for recovery of penalties/dues under Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992, read with the Second Schedule and relevant rules of the Income Tax Act, 1961 (as applied to SEBI recovery proceedings).

## Compliance Requirements

- Prachi Tradevine Pvt. Ltd. is required to settle the outstanding demand specified in the certificate within the timeline stipulated in the Notice of Demand.
- Failure to comply may result in coercive recovery measures, which can include attachment and sale of movable/immovable property, appointment of a receiver, or other measures available to a Recovery Officer under the applicable recovery rules.

## Important Dates

- Notice published/listed on SEBI's enforcement recovery proceedings page: 2026-07-03.
- Specific compliance deadline for payment is stated within the notice itself but could not be extracted from the scanned source document.

## Impact Assessment

This notice has a narrow, entity-specific impact limited to Prachi Tradevine Pvt. Ltd. and does not directly affect listed companies, exchanges, or broader market participants. It reflects SEBI's ongoing enforcement of penalties related to illiquid stock options trading and serves as a reminder to market participants of the regulator's continued follow-through on recovery of dues from entities found in violation of trading norms in the options segment.
