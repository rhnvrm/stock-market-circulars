## Summary

SEBI has cancelled recovery certificate no. 7569 of 2024 that was issued against Prince Securities (PAN: ACXPB9917G) in the matter of Silicon Valley Infotech Ltd. Recovery certificates are issued under Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992 to recover outstanding monetary penalties or disgorgement amounts from defaulters. Cancellation typically indicates that the dues have been paid in full or that the underlying enforcement order has been set aside.

## Key Points

- Recovery certificate no. 7569 of 2024 issued against Prince Securities (PAN: ACXPB9917G) stands cancelled
- The recovery action was in connection with the matter of Silicon Valley Infotech Ltd
- Cancellation closes the recovery proceedings against this entity for this matter

## Regulatory Changes

No broader regulatory changes. This order is specific to Prince Securities and the Silicon Valley Infotech Ltd enforcement matter.

## Compliance Requirements

No ongoing compliance obligations for market participants arising from this cancellation. The action closes the outstanding recovery proceeding against the named entity.

## Important Dates

- Date of cancellation order: 22 June 2026
- Original recovery certificate: No. 7569 of 2024

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a case-specific enforcement closure affecting only Prince Securities in relation to the Silicon Valley Infotech Ltd matter. No systemic or sector-wide implications.
