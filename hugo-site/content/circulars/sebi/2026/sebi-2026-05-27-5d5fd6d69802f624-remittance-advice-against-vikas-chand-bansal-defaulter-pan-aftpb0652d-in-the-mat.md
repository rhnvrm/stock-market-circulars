## Summary

SEBI has issued a Remittance Advice against Vikas Chand Bansal (PAN: AFTPB0652D), identified as a defaulter, in the matter of ISO (Inspection/Settlement Order) at BSE. This action is taken under Recovery Certificate No. 8359 of 2024, directing the recovery of outstanding dues owed by the defaulter to SEBI.

## Key Points

- Defaulter: Vikas Chand Bansal, PAN: AFTPB0652D
- Recovery Certificate No.: 8359 of 2024
- Matter: ISO at BSE – Vikas Chand Bansal
- Action: Remittance Advice issued by SEBI for recovery of dues
- This follows the issuance of a recovery certificate under SEBI's enforcement powers

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This document is an enforcement instrument issued under existing SEBI recovery and enforcement provisions.

## Compliance Requirements

- The defaulter (Vikas Chand Bansal) is required to remit the outstanding amount as specified in Recovery Certificate No. 8359 of 2024.
- Relevant banks, depositories, or other intermediaries may be directed to facilitate the recovery pursuant to this remittance advice.

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate No. 8359 issued in 2024
- Remittance Advice issued: May 2026

## Impact Assessment

This action is narrowly scoped to the individual defaulter and has no broad market impact. It reflects SEBI's ongoing enforcement of recovery certificates against individuals who have defaulted on regulatory dues or penalties. Market participants and investors are not directly affected unless they have outstanding claims or dealings with the named defaulter.
