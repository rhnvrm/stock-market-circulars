## Summary

SEBI has issued a corrigendum to the exemption order dated February 26, 2026 (Reference No. WTM/KCV/CFD/23/2025-26). The correction clarifies that the name of the Target Company should be read as 'Hardcastle and Waud Manufacturing Company Limited' instead of 'Hardcastle and Waud Manufacturing Limited' as originally stated.

## Key Points

- Corrigendum reference: WTM/KCV/CFD/23A/2025-26
- Original order reference: WTM/KCV/CFD/23/2025-26, dated February 26, 2026
- Correction: Target Company name amended to include the word 'Company' — 'Hardcastle and Waud Manufacturing Company Limited'
- Issued by Whole Time Member Kamlesh Chandra Varshney

## Regulatory Changes

No substantive regulatory changes. This corrigendum solely corrects a typographical omission of the word 'Company' in the Target Company's name within the original exemption order.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. All references to the Target Company in the original exemption order dated February 26, 2026 should be read with the corrected name: 'Hardcastle and Waud Manufacturing Company Limited'.

## Important Dates

- Original exemption order date: February 26, 2026
- Corrigendum date: March 9, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This corrigendum has negligible market or operational impact. It is a purely administrative correction to ensure the accurate legal name of the Target Company is reflected in the official exemption order. No changes to the substance of the exemption or any obligations of involved parties.
