## Summary

SEBI has issued a Completion Certificate for Recovery Certificate No. 7673 of 2024 against Biglife Capital Solutions and its proprietor Prateek Vyas (PAN: AKIPV4554A). This document confirms the completion of recovery proceedings initiated against the entity.

- Recovery Certificate No. 7673 of 2024 proceedings have been completed
- Entity: Biglife Capital Solutions
- Proprietor: Prateek Vyas (PAN: AKIPV4554A)
- Document type: Completion Certificate indicating closure of recovery proceedings
- Order issued on January 7, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an enforcement-related completion certificate.

## Compliance Requirements

No ongoing compliance requirements as the recovery proceedings have been completed through this certificate.

## Important Dates

- **Order Date**: January 7, 2026
- **Recovery Certificate No.**: 7673 of 2024

**Market Impact**: Minimal to none. This is a completion certificate for recovery proceedings against a specific entity.

**Operational Impact**: The completion certificate indicates that recovery proceedings under RC No. 7673 of 2024 have been concluded. This affects only Biglife Capital Solutions and its proprietor, with no broader market implications.

**Entity-Specific Impact**: The completion certificate formally closes the recovery proceedings for Biglife Capital Solutions and Prateek Vyas.
