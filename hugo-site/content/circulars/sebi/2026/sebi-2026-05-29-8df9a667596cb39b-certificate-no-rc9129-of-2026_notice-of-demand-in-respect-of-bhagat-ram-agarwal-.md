## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC9129 of 2026, constituting a Notice of Demand against Bhagat Ram Agarwal HUF (PAN: AAGHB3924E) in the matter of Illiquid Stock Options. This notice requires the defaulter to pay the demanded amount as determined in SEBI's earlier adjudication/recovery proceedings related to manipulative trading in illiquid stock options.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9129 of 2026 issued by SEBI
- Directed against Bhagat Ram Agarwal HUF (PAN: AAGHB3924E)
- Relates to proceedings in the matter of Illiquid Stock Options
- Constitutes a formal Notice of Demand for recovery of dues
- Issued under SEBI's enforcement and recovery powers

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This document is an enforcement action implementing existing SEBI orders and recovery provisions under the SEBI Act and relevant regulations governing illiquid stock options.

## Compliance Requirements

- Bhagat Ram Agarwal HUF is required to comply with the demand specified in Recovery Certificate RC9129 of 2026
- Payment of the demanded amount must be made within the stipulated timeframe as mentioned in the notice
- Failure to comply may lead to further enforcement action including attachment of assets

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate RC9129 of 2026 issued: May 2026
- Compliance deadline: As specified in the demand notice (refer to the original document)

## Impact Assessment

This enforcement action is specific to Bhagat Ram Agarwal HUF and has no direct market-wide impact. It is part of SEBI's broader crackdown on entities involved in manipulation of illiquid stock options on stock exchanges. The action signals continued enforcement activity against participants found to have engaged in non-genuine trades in illiquid stock options segments.
