## Summary

This is a SEBI Order for Compliance issued by the Recovery Officer directing actions related to the sale of property belonging to Nina Ajay Maloo. The order is part of SEBI's enforcement and recovery process to recover dues owed pursuant to prior SEBI directions or adjudication orders against the individual.

## Key Points

- Recovery Officer order issued under SEBI's enforcement powers for recovery of dues
- Concerns the sale of immovable/movable property of Nina Ajay Maloo
- Issued as part of compliance with an earlier substantive SEBI order
- Directs relevant parties to facilitate or recognise the property sale process
- Part of SEBI's standard recovery mechanism under the SEBI Act and Recovery of Debts Due to Banks and Financial Institutions Act

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an individual enforcement/compliance order issued under existing SEBI recovery framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Relevant parties (banks, registrars, sub-registrars, or other entities) directed to cooperate with the Recovery Officer in effecting the sale
- Nina Ajay Maloo or connected parties required to comply with the sale process and not obstruct proceedings
- Any proceeds from the sale to be applied toward outstanding SEBI-mandated dues

## Important Dates

- Order Date: April 2026
- Compliance timeline: As specified in the order by the Recovery Officer

## Impact Assessment

This order has limited broader market impact as it pertains to an individual enforcement matter. It reflects SEBI's continued use of its recovery machinery to enforce compliance with its orders. No systemic or sector-wide implications are anticipated.
