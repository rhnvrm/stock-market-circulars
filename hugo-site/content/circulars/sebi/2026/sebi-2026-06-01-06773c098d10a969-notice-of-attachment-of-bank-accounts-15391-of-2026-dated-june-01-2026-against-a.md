## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Attachment of Bank Accounts (No. 15391 of 2026, dated June 01, 2026) against Amit Agarwal, a declared defaulter with PAN AEEPA5456L. The attachment is being enforced under Recovery Certificate No. 9106 of 2026, arising from proceedings related to dealing in illiquid stock options at BSE.

## Key Points

- Notice number: 15391 of 2026, dated June 01, 2026
- Defaulter: Amit Agarwal, PAN: AEEPA5456L
- Recovery Certificate No.: 9106 of 2026
- Basis of action: Dealing in illiquid stock options at BSE
- Action taken: Attachment of bank accounts to recover dues

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an enforcement/recovery action under existing SEBI recovery powers.

## Compliance Requirements

- Banks and financial institutions holding accounts of Amit Agarwal (PAN: AEEPA5456L) are required to comply with the attachment notice and freeze the specified accounts.
- No debits are to be permitted from the attached accounts without SEBI's authorization.

## Important Dates

- Notice date: June 01, 2026
- Recovery Certificate No. 9106 of 2026 underpins the enforcement action.

## Impact Assessment

This notice has no broad market impact. It is a targeted enforcement action against an individual found to have dealt in illiquid stock options at BSE. The attachment of bank accounts is a standard recovery mechanism used by SEBI to collect dues from defaulters. Market participants not involved in illiquid stock option manipulation are unaffected.
