## Summary

SEBI has issued a notice confirming the completion of Recovery Certificate No. 8708 of 2025 against Gurumurthi Jayaraman in the matter of Securekloud Technologies Ltd. This notice signifies that the recovery proceedings initiated under the certificate have been successfully concluded and the dues recoverable have been collected.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8708 of 2025 issued against Gurumurthi Jayaraman has been fully executed.
- The recovery proceedings pertain to the matter of Securekloud Technologies Ltd. (formerly known as 8K Miles Software Services Ltd.).
- A corresponding Release Order (circular ID: 7c1e41fcb2ff9193) has also been issued on the same date, indicating the release of any attached assets following completion of recovery.
- This completion notice is issued by SEBI's Recovery Officer under the SEBI Act, 1992.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is an administrative closure document confirming the end of enforcement/recovery action under Recovery Certificate No. 8708 of 2025.

## Compliance Requirements

- No new compliance obligations arise from this notice for market participants.
- The notice serves as a formal record that the outstanding dues under the recovery certificate have been satisfied.
- Relevant parties (Gurumurthi Jayaraman and associated entities) are no longer subject to the attachment or recovery actions under RC No. 8708 of 2025.

## Important Dates

- **Recovery Certificate No.:** 8708 of 2025
- **Completion Notice Date:** June 8, 2026
- **Related Release Order Date:** June 8, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal market impact as it represents the administrative conclusion of an individual enforcement action. Investors in Securekloud Technologies Ltd. (NSE: SECUREKLOUD) should note this as a resolution of a prior SEBI enforcement matter involving a named individual associated with the company. No trading restrictions, financial obligations for the company, or changes to its listed status arise from this completion notice.
