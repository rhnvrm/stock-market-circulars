## Summary

SEBI has issued a prohibitory order against P Venkateswara Rao in connection with Recovery Certificate Nos. 2813 of 2020 and 2814 of 2020, arising out of enforcement proceedings in the matter of Ranklin Solutions Ltd. The order restrains the noticee from alienating, transferring, or disposing of assets to facilitate recovery of outstanding dues owed to SEBI.

## Key Points

- Prohibitory order issued against P Venkateswara Rao under Recovery Certificate Nos. 2813 and 2814 of 2020.
- Proceedings relate to the matter of Ranklin Solutions Ltd.
- The order directs attachment/restraint on assets of the noticee to prevent dissipation before recovery.
- Issued under SEBI's recovery powers pursuant to the SEBI Act and relevant regulations.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an enforcement/recovery action under existing SEBI recovery mechanism provisions.

## Compliance Requirements

- P Venkateswara Rao is prohibited from alienating, transferring, or creating any charge on assets as specified in the order.
- Banks, depositories, and other entities holding assets of the noticee may be directed to freeze/attach such assets.
- Any third party notified of the order must comply with its directions.

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificates issued: 2020 (Nos. 2813 and 2814 of 2020)
- Prohibitory order date: March 6, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This order has no broad market impact. It is a targeted enforcement action against a specific individual (P Venkateswara Rao) to recover dues related to Ranklin Solutions Ltd. The impact is limited to the noticee and any financial institutions or intermediaries holding their assets.
