## Summary

SEBI has issued an order permitting the release of a portion of salary from the account of Mr. Jyotiranjan Panda (PAN: BEAPP0928K), Director for Magnox Infraprojects Limited, whose assets/accounts are subject to attachment under recovery proceedings. This is a limited relief measure allowing the individual to access funds for basic living expenses while the broader recovery action continues.

## Key Points

- SEBI grants partial release of salary from the attached/frozen account of Mr. Jyotiranjan Panda (PAN: BEAPP0928K).
- Mr. Panda serves as a Director for Magnox Infraprojects Limited.
- The release is limited to a specified portion of salary for subsistence purposes.
- The underlying recovery proceedings against Mr. Panda and/or Magnox Infraprojects Limited remain in force.
- All other attachments and restrictions on the account continue to apply.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This order is an operational action within existing recovery and enforcement powers under SEBI regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- The employer/bank is directed to release only the specified portion of salary as indicated in the order.
- The remaining balance in the attached account must continue to be held subject to the recovery certificate/attachment order.
- Any disbursement beyond the permitted amount would be in violation of SEBI's recovery directions.

## Important Dates

- **Order Date:** March 13, 2026
- Effective immediately upon issuance.

## Impact Assessment

This order has a very limited market impact as it relates to an individual enforcement matter. It provides humanitarian relief to the named individual while preserving SEBI's recovery rights. There is no systemic or broader market significance. Investors and market participants are not directly affected.
