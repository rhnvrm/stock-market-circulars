## Summary

SEBI has established a Settlement Helpdesk Facility to assist applicants and prospective applicants in understanding and complying with the procedural requirements for filing settlement applications under the SEBI (Settlement Proceedings) Regulations, 2018. The initiative is aimed at easing the settlement process and providing procedural guidance to applicants.

## Key Points

- The Settlement Helpdesk will address queries related to the settlement process and guide applicants through procedural steps.
- The facility assists with three key aspects: filing settlement applications, computing the Indicative Amount as per the SEBI (Settlement Proceedings) Regulations, 2018, and inquiring about the status of pending Settlement Applications.
- SEBI has issued a "User Manual for Settlement Helpdesk Facility," available on the SEBI website at the specified link.
- Interested applicants may write to the Helpdesk with their queries, following the process outlined in the User Manual.
- This initiative is part of SEBI's broader efforts to ease the filing and availing of settlement of specified proceedings.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is an operational/procedural facility introduced under the existing framework of the SEBI (Settlement Proceedings) Regulations, 2018.

## Compliance Requirements

Applicants and prospective applicants seeking to file settlement applications should refer to the User Manual for Settlement Helpdesk Facility available on the SEBI website and follow the specified process to raise queries with the Helpdesk.

## Important Dates

Announcement dated July 01, 2026 (Mumbai), issued as PR No. 38/2026.

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact administrative/procedural update that facilitates ease of doing business for entities involved in SEBI settlement proceedings. It does not affect trading, listing, or specific stocks, but improves accessibility and transparency of the settlement application process for regulated entities and intermediaries.
