## Summary

SEBI has initiated recovery proceedings under Recovery Certificate No. RC8242 of 2024 against Podium Market Research (Proprietor: Suman Kumar, PAN: AMCPK0541Q). The matter is registered as Application/Petition No. 15365 of 2026 before the competent authority for enforcement of outstanding dues payable to SEBI.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC8242 of 2024 issued against Podium Market Research
- Proprietor: Suman Kumar (PAN: AMCPK0541Q)
- Application/Petition No. 15365 of 2026 filed for enforcement of recovery
- Proceedings relate to non-payment of penalty/dues imposed by SEBI
- Action is part of SEBI's ongoing enforcement against non-compliant entities

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This document pertains to enforcement of an existing SEBI order through recovery proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

- Podium Market Research and its proprietor Suman Kumar are required to satisfy the outstanding dues under Recovery Certificate No. RC8242 of 2024
- Failure to comply may result in further enforcement action including attachment of assets

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate issued: 2024 (RC8242 of 2024)
- Application/Petition filed: 2026 (A.P. No. 15365 of 2026)
- Document date: May 2026

## Impact Assessment

This action is limited in scope to a single proprietorship entity (Podium Market Research). There is no broad market impact. The action signals SEBI's continued enforcement against investment advisors and market intermediaries that fail to pay imposed penalties. Investors dealing with Podium Market Research should be aware of the ongoing regulatory action against the entity.
