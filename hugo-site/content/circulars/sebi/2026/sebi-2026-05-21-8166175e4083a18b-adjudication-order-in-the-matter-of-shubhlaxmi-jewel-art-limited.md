## Summary

SEBI's Adjudicating Officer issued Order No. Order/AK/DS/2026-27/32412-32419 under Section 15-I of the SEBI Act, 1992, against eight noticees — the promoter and persons acting in concert (PACs) of Shubhlaxmi Jewel Art Limited — for alleged violations of the SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 2011 (SAST Regulations). The violations relate to a preferential allotment of convertible warrants and subsequent equity share allotment without fulfilling requisite disclosure and compliance obligations.

## Key Points

- Eight noticees named: Narendrasinh J Chauhan (Noticee 1, promoter) and Noticees 2–8 (promoter group / PACs).
- On November 02, 2021, the Board of Shubhlaxmi Jewel Art Limited approved allotment of 23,00,000 convertible warrants on a preferential basis — 18,00,000 to Noticee 1 and 5,00,000 to non-promoter allottees.
- On May 08, 2023, the Board approved conversion and allotment of 18,00,000 equity shares to Noticee 1 upon exercise of warrants.
- Noticees 1–8 were deemed PACs under Regulation 2(1)(q) of SAST Regulations based on the Target Company's shareholding pattern filed with exchanges.
- AO was appointed vide Order dated December 18, 2025; Show Cause Notice (SCN) was issued on January 02, 2026.
- Proceedings initiated under Section 15-I(1) of the SEBI Act read with Rule 3 of SEBI (Procedure for Holding Inquiry and Imposing Penalties) Rules, 1995, and Section 19 of the SEBI Act.
- Penalties, if imposed, fall under Section 15H of the SEBI Act.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement order applying existing SAST Regulations, 2011, specifically obligations triggered by preferential allotment and warrant conversion that alter promoter shareholding thresholds.

## Compliance Requirements

- Promoters and PACs must comply with disclosure and open-offer obligations under SAST Regulations whenever acquisitions cross prescribed thresholds.
- Convertible instruments such as warrants must be factored into shareholding calculations for SAST compliance purposes.
- Entities acting in concert are jointly responsible for SAST obligations; promoter-group members classified as PACs cannot claim independent status to avoid compliance.

## Important Dates

- **November 02, 2021**: Board of Directors approved allotment of 23,00,000 convertible warrants (preferential basis).
- **May 08, 2023**: Board approved allotment of 18,00,000 equity shares to Noticee 1 upon warrant conversion.
- **December 18, 2025**: SEBI appointed Adjudicating Officer.
- **January 02, 2026**: Show Cause Notice issued to all eight noticees.
- **May 2026**: Adjudication order passed (Order/AK/DS/2026-27/32412-32419).

## Impact Assessment

This order has limited direct market impact as it concerns enforcement against the promoter and promoter group of a small listed company. However, it reinforces SEBI's continued scrutiny of preferential allotments and warrant conversions that result in significant promoter shareholding changes without corresponding SAST compliance. Market participants — particularly promoters of SME and small-cap companies undertaking preferential issues — should ensure timely SAST disclosures and assess open-offer obligations upon warrant conversion events.
