## Summary

SEBI has issued a Remittance Advice against Sattu Yadav, named as a defaulter, in connection with a matter involving dealing in illiquid stock options at BSE. The advice has been issued under Recovery Certificate No. 9004 of 2026, as part of SEBI's recovery proceedings to enforce collection of dues arising from the underlying enforcement action against the defaulter.

## Key Points

- Defaulter named: Sattu Yadav
- PAN of defaulter: AHXPY0089P
- Underlying violation: Dealing in illiquid stock options at BSE
- Recovery Certificate No.: 9004 of 2026
- Document type: Remittance Advice issued in recovery proceedings

## Regulatory Changes

This document does not introduce any new regulatory changes. It is an enforcement/recovery communication issued pursuant to an already-existing recovery certificate against the named defaulter.

## Compliance Requirements

The named defaulter, Sattu Yadav, is required to remit the outstanding dues specified under Recovery Certificate No. 9004 of 2026. Non-compliance may result in further recovery action by SEBI's Recovery Officer under applicable provisions, including attachment of assets or bank accounts.

## Important Dates

The remittance advice was generated/published on July 2, 2026, based on the source document metadata.

## Impact Assessment

This notice has limited and narrow impact, being an individual enforcement/recovery action tied to a historical case of dealing in illiquid stock options at BSE. It does not affect listed companies, exchange operations, or the broader investor base, and mainly serves as a record of SEBI's continued enforcement of recovery certificates related to illiquid derivatives trading violations.
