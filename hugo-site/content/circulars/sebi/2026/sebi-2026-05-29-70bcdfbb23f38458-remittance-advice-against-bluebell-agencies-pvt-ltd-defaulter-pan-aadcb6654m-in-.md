## Summary

SEBI has issued a remittance advice against Bluebell Agencies Pvt Ltd (PAN: AADCB6654M), identified as a defaulter in the matter of Multipurpose Trading and Agencies Limited. This action is taken under Recovery Certificate No. 8488 of 2025, directing the recovery of dues owed to SEBI.

## Key Points

- Defaulter: Bluebell Agencies Pvt Ltd (PAN: AADCB6654M)
- Matter: Multipurpose Trading and Agencies Limited
- Recovery Certificate No.: 8488 of 2025
- SEBI has initiated remittance/recovery proceedings against the defaulter
- The recovery certificate was originally issued in 2025; remittance advice follows as part of enforcement

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an enforcement/recovery action under existing SEBI recovery mechanisms.

## Compliance Requirements

- Bluebell Agencies Pvt Ltd is required to remit the dues as specified in the remittance advice
- Banks and depositories involved may be directed to freeze or remit funds held in accounts of the defaulter
- Non-compliance with the remittance advice may lead to further enforcement action by SEBI

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate No. 8488 issued: 2025
- Remittance Advice issued: May 2026

## Impact Assessment

This action is limited in scope, targeting a specific defaulter (Bluebell Agencies Pvt Ltd) in connection with the matter of Multipurpose Trading and Agencies Limited. There is no broader market or systemic impact. The action reflects SEBI's ongoing enforcement of recovery proceedings against entities that have failed to comply with earlier orders or payment obligations.
