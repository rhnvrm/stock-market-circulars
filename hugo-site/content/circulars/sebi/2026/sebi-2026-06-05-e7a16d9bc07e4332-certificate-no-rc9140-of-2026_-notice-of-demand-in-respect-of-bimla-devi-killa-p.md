## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC9140 of 2026 along with a Notice of Demand against Bimla Devi Killa (PAN: AESPK3270J) in connection with the matter of Illiquid Stock Options. This certificate authorises recovery of outstanding dues/penalties imposed on the named individual pursuant to SEBI enforcement proceedings.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9140 of 2026 issued against Bimla Devi Killa (PAN: AESPK3270J)
- Pertains to SEBI's ongoing enforcement action in the Illiquid Stock Options matter
- Notice of Demand formally communicates the outstanding amount to be recovered
- Recovery action is being pursued under SEBI's statutory powers

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an enforcement/recovery action under existing SEBI regulations and orders passed in the Illiquid Stock Options matter.

## Compliance Requirements

- Bimla Devi Killa is required to pay the demanded amount as specified in the Recovery Certificate within the stipulated time period
- Failure to comply may result in further enforcement measures including attachment of assets

## Important Dates

- Certificate Date: 2026 (specific date in the PDF)
- Payment deadline: As specified in the Notice of Demand (refer to PDF for exact date)

## Impact Assessment

This action has no broad market impact. It is targeted enforcement against a single named individual (Bimla Devi Killa) as part of SEBI's sustained recovery drive in the Illiquid Stock Options manipulation matter. The illiquid stock options segment enforcement series has resulted in numerous recovery certificates being issued against various entities found to have engaged in manipulative trading practices.
