## Summary

SEBI has issued a Certificate of Completion dated January 30, 2026 under Recovery Certificate No. 8959 of 2025 drawn against Ranjeet S Yadav. This certificate pertains to recovery proceedings in the matter of dealing in illiquid stock options at the Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE). The completion certificate indicates that the recovery proceedings have been concluded.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate Number: 8959 of 2025
- Entity: Ranjeet S Yadav
- Matter: Dealing in illiquid stock options at BSE
- Status: Certificate of Completion issued
- Date of Certificate: January 30, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an enforcement outcome document.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. The completion certificate indicates that recovery proceedings have been concluded.

## Important Dates

- **January 30, 2026**: Certificate of Completion issued

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine enforcement completion document with no direct market impact. The certificate indicates that SEBI's recovery proceedings against Ranjeet S Yadav in relation to illiquid stock options dealings at BSE have been completed. This does not affect current market operations or require any action from market participants.
