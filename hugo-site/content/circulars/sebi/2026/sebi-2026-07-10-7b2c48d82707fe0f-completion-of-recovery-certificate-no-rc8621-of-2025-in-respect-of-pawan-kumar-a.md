## Summary

SEBI has issued a notice confirming completion of Recovery Certificate No. RC8621 of 2025, which was issued against Pawan Kumar Agarwal (PAN: AANPA5607N) in connection with dues arising from the illiquid stock options matter. The certificate, issued to SEBI's Recovery Officer for enforcement under Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992 read with the Second and Third Schedules to the Income Tax Act, 1961 and the Income Tax (Certificate Proceedings) Rules, 1962, has now been closed following completion of the recovery process.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC8621 of 2025 was issued against Pawan Kumar Agarwal (PAN: AANPA5607N).
- The matter relates to the long-running SEBI enforcement action concerning illiquid stock options traded on the equity derivatives segment.
- The Recovery Officer's proceedings under this certificate have now been completed/closed.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced; this is a case-specific enforcement/recovery action update under SEBI's existing recovery mechanism for dues (penalties, disgorgement amounts, or other sums) confirmed by SEBI orders.

## Compliance Requirements

No action is required from listed companies, market intermediaries, or the investing public. The notice pertains solely to closure of recovery proceedings against the named individual.

## Important Dates

Circular published: July 10, 2026 (as per PDF publication path). Specific dates of the underlying order and recovery completion are per SEBI's internal recovery officer records referenced in the certificate.

## Impact Assessment

This circular has narrow, individual-specific impact and does not affect trading, listing, or market operations. It documents closure of an enforcement recovery case tied to the broader illiquid stock options matter and is primarily of record-keeping and regulatory-transparency significance.
