## Summary

SEBI's Appellate Authority dismissed RTI Appeal No. 6903 of 2026 filed by Sureshchand Pawar against CPIO, SEBI, Mumbai. The appellant sought information relating to Nawan Salt Traders Association, but SEBI determined the matter falls outside its purview as the entity is an unlisted company. The appeal was decided on June 23, 2026 by Appellate Authority Ruchi Chojer.

## Key Points

- Appellant filed an RTI application received by SEBI on April 15, 2026 seeking information about Nawan Salt Traders Association.
- CPIO responded on April 23, 2026 referring appellant to previous letter no. CPIO/BS/550 & 55/2024-2025, stating the information concerns an unlisted company outside SEBI's purview.
- Two physical applications from the appellant dated June 26, 2024, forwarded by Ministry of Finance (Department of Economic Affairs), were transferred by SEBI to Ministry of Corporate Affairs.
- Appellant filed the appeal on May 21, 2026 (Reg. No. SEBIH/A/P/26/0029), received by the Appellate Authority on May 26, 2026.
- The Appellate Authority found no deficiency in the respondent's response and dismissed the appeal.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a procedural order under the Right to Information Act, 2005.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. The order reaffirms that SEBI is not obligated to provide information it does not possess, citing CIC precedent (Sh. Pattipati Rama Murthy vs. CPIO, SEBI, July 8, 2013): if SEBI does not have information in its possession, the CPIO cannot invent one for the benefit of the appellant.

## Important Dates

- April 15, 2026: RTI application received by SEBI
- April 23, 2026: CPIO response issued
- May 21, 2026: Appeal filed by appellant
- May 26, 2026: Appeal received by Office of Appellate Authority
- June 23, 2026: Appeal dismissed

## Impact Assessment

No market or operational impact. This is a routine administrative RTI appeal dismissal with no bearing on listed securities, trading operations, or market participants. The matter solely concerns an unlisted entity (Nawan Salt Traders Association) that falls under the jurisdiction of the Ministry of Corporate Affairs, not SEBI.
