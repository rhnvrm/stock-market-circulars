## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC9076 of 2026 in the matter of Appeal No. 15463 of 2026 against Allnew Distributors Private Limited (PAN: AAKCA2105M). This certificate is issued in connection with SEBI's enforcement proceedings relating to Illiquid Stock Options, directing recovery of outstanding dues from the named entity.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9076 of 2026 issued by SEBI.
- Pertains to Appeal No. 15463 of 2026.
- Entity named: Allnew Distributors Private Limited (PAN: AAKCA2105M).
- Arises from SEBI's enforcement actions in the matter of Illiquid Stock Options.
- The certificate authorises recovery of dues as determined in SEBI's order.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework is introduced. This document is an enforcement instrument issued under existing SEBI (Recovery of Amounts) Regulations, giving SEBI authority to recover dues from the defaulting entity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Allnew Distributors Private Limited is required to pay the outstanding dues as specified in the recovery certificate.
- Non-compliance may result in attachment and sale of assets, arrest, or other recovery measures as permissible under SEBI regulations.
- Other market participants and intermediaries are not directly subject to compliance obligations under this circular.

## Important Dates

- Certificate issued: June 15, 2026.
- Specific payment deadline: As indicated in the recovery certificate (refer to the PDF for the exact amount and due date).

## Impact Assessment

This action is part of SEBI's broader crackdown on entities involved in manipulation of Illiquid Stock Options on Indian stock exchanges. The impact is confined to the named entity. Broader market participants are not directly affected. The enforcement reinforces SEBI's continued scrutiny of illiquid derivatives segment participants and sends a deterrent signal to market intermediaries engaged in unauthorised trading practices.
