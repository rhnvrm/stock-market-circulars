## Summary

This order is issued by the Appellate Authority under the Right to Information Act, 2005, in Appeal No. 6914 of 2026 filed by Peeyush Nigam against the CPIO, SEBI, Mumbai. The appellant had sought detailed information regarding a complaint he filed against M/s G Surgiwear Limited alleging misleading disclosures and incorrect information in its IPO-related filings. The appellant sought details of the vertical handling his complaint, actions taken by SEBI, communications with the company, internal examination records, and the rationale for SEBI issuing observations allowing the company's public issue to proceed despite his complaint.

## Key Points

- Appellant Peeyush Nigam filed an RTI application dated May 9, 2026, referencing his earlier complaint (No. SEBIE/UP26/KANP/016667/1) filed on April 11, 2026 against M/s G Surgiwear Limited.
- The complaint alleged misleading disclosures and submission of incorrect information in connection with G Surgiwear Limited's IPO-related filings.
- The complaint was closed on May 8, 2026 with a note that it was forwarded to the relevant vertical for appropriate action.
- The appellant noted that SEBI subsequently issued observations permitting the company to proceed with its public issue, and sought clarity on whether his complaint was considered in that decision.
- The respondent (CPIO) directed the appellant to the SCORES website for complaint status tracking and stated that SEBI's investigations/examinations are conducted confidentially.
- The appellant filed this appeal (Reg. No. SEBIH/A/E/26/00205) after the CPIO's response dated June 4, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is an adjudicatory order on an individual RTI appeal, not a regulatory or policy change.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements are imposed on market participants. The order pertains to information disclosure obligations under the RTI Act as applicable to SEBI as a public authority.

## Important Dates

- April 11, 2026: Original complaint filed by appellant against G Surgiwear Limited.
- May 8, 2026: Complaint closed and forwarded to relevant vertical.
- May 9, 2026: RTI application filed by appellant.
- June 4, 2026: CPIO response to RTI application and appeal filed by appellant (Reg. No. SEBIH/A/E/26/00205).

## Impact Assessment

This order has minimal direct market impact as it concerns an individual RTI appeal regarding procedural disclosure of SEBI's internal complaint-handling records. It does not alter regulatory requirements or affect trading, listing, or compliance obligations for market participants. However, it provides indirect visibility into investor grievance handling related to IPO due diligence, specifically concerning G Surgiwear Limited, which may be of interest to investors tracking that company's IPO process.
