## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC9134 of 2026 in Adjudication Proceeding No. 15491 of 2026 against Pawan Kumar Agarwal HUF (PAN: AAJHP1925N) in connection with violations relating to illiquid stock options. The certificate authorises recovery of the adjudicated penalty amount from the entity.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9134 of 2026 issued against Pawan Kumar Agarwal HUF (PAN: AAJHP1925N).
- Adjudication Proceeding No. (A.P. No.) 15491 of 2026 underlies this certificate.
- Matter pertains to illiquid stock options — a category of securities that SEBI has identified as a vehicle for price manipulation and fraudulent trading.
- The certificate empowers SEBI to recover the penalty amount as arrears of land revenue.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This document is an enforcement instrument (recovery certificate) issued under the SEBI Act and relevant adjudication regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Pawan Kumar Agarwal HUF is required to pay the penalty amount specified in the underlying adjudication order.
- Non-payment may result in coercive recovery action by SEBI as authorised under the SEBI Act.

## Important Dates

- Certificate issued: June 2026
- Specific payment deadline: as specified in the underlying adjudication order (not separately stated in this certificate).

## Impact Assessment

This is an individual enforcement action with no direct market-wide impact. It forms part of SEBI's ongoing campaign against manipulation through illiquid stock options on exchanges. Market participants and brokers should note SEBI's continued enforcement activity in this segment.
