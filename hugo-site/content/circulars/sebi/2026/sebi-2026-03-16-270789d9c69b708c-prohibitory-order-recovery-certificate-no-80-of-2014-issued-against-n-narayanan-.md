## Summary

SEBI has issued a prohibitory order under Recovery Certificate No. 80 of 2014 against N Narayanan in the matter of Pyramid Saimira Theatre Ltd. The order restrains the noticee from alienating, transferring, or disposing of any assets in furtherance of SEBI's recovery proceedings.

## Key Points

- Prohibitory order issued against N Narayanan pursuant to Recovery Certificate No. 80 of 2014
- The order is in connection with the matter of Pyramid Saimira Theatre Ltd.
- The noticee is restrained from alienating, disposing of, encumbering, or transferring any assets, properties, or securities held in their name
- The order is issued by SEBI's Recovery Officer under the SEBI Act, 1992 read with the Second Schedule to the Income Tax Act, 1961
- Banks, depositories, and financial institutions are directed to freeze the accounts/holdings of the noticee

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an individual enforcement/recovery action under existing SEBI recovery procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- N Narayanan must not alienate, transfer, or dispose of any movable or immovable property without prior permission from SEBI's Recovery Officer
- Banks, depositories, and registrar & transfer agents are required to note the prohibitory order and not permit any debit transactions in accounts/demat accounts held by the noticee
- Any third party holding assets on behalf of the noticee is required to comply with the directions in the order

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate No. 80 of 2014: Original certificate issued in 2014
- Order Date: March 16, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This order has a narrow impact limited to the individual named (N Narayanan) and institutions holding assets on their behalf. There is no broader market impact. Pyramid Saimira Theatre Ltd. was a listed company that was subject to SEBI enforcement actions; this order represents continued recovery proceedings in a long-standing enforcement case. Market participants are not affected unless they hold assets as custodians or trustees for the named individual.
