## Summary

SEBI's Recovery Officer has issued a release order in respect of Recovery Certificate No. RC9075 of 2026, issued against Ranjeet Singh Baid HUF (PAN: AAPHR3293F). The certificate was originally issued in connection with enforcement action relating to trading in illiquid stock options, a practice SEBI has actively targeted for creating artificial volumes and non-genuine trades in the options segment. Following confirmation that the recovery proceedings under this certificate have been completed (dues recovered or otherwise satisfied), this order directs the release of the bank accounts and/or demat accounts of the noticee that had earlier been attached to enforce recovery.

## Key Points

- The release order pertains to Recovery Certificate No. RC9075 of 2026 issued against Ranjeet Singh Baid HUF (PAN: AAPHR3293F).
- The underlying matter relates to trading in illiquid stock options, an area of continued SEBI enforcement scrutiny due to concerns over artificial liquidity and non-genuine trading patterns.
- The order follows completion of the recovery process under this certificate and formally releases the attachment placed on the noticee's bank and/or demat accounts.
- This is an individual, entity-specific enforcement action and does not create obligations for market participants or listed entities generally.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is a case-specific enforcement/recovery closure action rather than a new rule, circular, or policy change affecting market participants generally.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance obligations arise for market participants, listed companies, or intermediaries from this order. It concerns release of attachment on accounts of a specific noticee (Ranjeet Singh Baid HUF) following completion of recovery, exercised by SEBI's Recovery Officer under the recovery mechanism provided by Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992, read with the Second and Third Schedules to the Income Tax Act, 1961 as applicable to such proceedings. Banks and depositories holding the previously attached accounts would be expected to give effect to the release as directed.

## Important Dates

The release order was published on the SEBI website on 2026-07-15. No further deadlines or compliance dates apply to other parties.

## Impact Assessment

The impact of this order is limited to the specific noticee named in the recovery certificate. It does not affect any listed company's securities, trading activity, or broader market operations. Such release orders are administrative in nature, confirming that attachment measures used to enforce recovery are no longer required once dues are recovered, and carry low significance for the broader investing public or market participants.
