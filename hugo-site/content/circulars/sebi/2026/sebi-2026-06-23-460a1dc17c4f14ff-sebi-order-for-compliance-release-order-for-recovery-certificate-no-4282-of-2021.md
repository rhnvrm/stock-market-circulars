## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order for Recovery Certificate No. 4282 of 2021 against Naimish K. Shah in the matter of Tripex Overseas Ltd. The order indicates that Naimish K. Shah has complied with the outstanding recovery dues, and SEBI has accordingly released the recovery certificate, closing the enforcement proceedings.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 4282 of 2021 issued against Naimish K. Shah is hereby released
- The matter concerns Tripex Overseas Ltd., an earlier SEBI enforcement action
- Release order issued June 23, 2026, signifying full compliance by the respondent
- Proceedings under this recovery certificate are now concluded

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an individual enforcement matter. The release order has no effect on broader SEBI regulations or market participants.

## Compliance Requirements

Naimish K. Shah has satisfied the compliance requirements under Recovery Certificate No. 4282 of 2021. No further action is required from any party in connection with this recovery certificate.

## Important Dates

- Release Order Date: June 23, 2026
- Recovery Certificate No. 4282 of 2021 (originally issued in 2021)

## Impact Assessment

This order has minimal market impact. It represents the closure of an individual recovery proceeding against Naimish K. Shah arising from the Tripex Overseas Ltd. enforcement matter. The release of the Recovery Certificate signals that SEBI's dues have been recovered and the enforcement action is concluded. There is no systemic regulatory change or market-wide significance.
