## Summary

SEBI has issued a General Remittance Order dated June 18, 2026, in connection with Recovery Certificate No. 3517 of 2021 against Gujarat Meditech Ltd. The order directs banks, depositories, and other financial institutions to remit funds held in the accounts of the defaulter towards recovery of outstanding dues owed to SEBI.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 3517 of 2021 was issued against Gujarat Meditech Ltd in the matter of Gujarat Meditech Ltd.
- SEBI has issued this General Remittance Order to enforce recovery of dues pursuant to the certificate.
- Banks and depositories are directed to remit funds from the defaulter's accounts to SEBI.
- The order is part of SEBI's ongoing enforcement and recovery proceedings against Gujarat Meditech Ltd.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI recovery mechanisms.

## Compliance Requirements

- Banks, depositories, and financial institutions holding assets of Gujarat Meditech Ltd are required to remit the specified amount to SEBI upon receipt of this order.
- Non-compliance with a SEBI remittance order may attract further enforcement action.

## Important Dates

- **Order Date:** June 18, 2026
- **Recovery Certificate:** No. 3517 of 2021 (original certificate issued in 2021)

## Impact Assessment

This order has limited broader market impact and is restricted to Gujarat Meditech Ltd and financial institutions holding its assets. It reflects SEBI's continued enforcement of recovery proceedings against non-compliant entities. Market participants are not directly affected.
