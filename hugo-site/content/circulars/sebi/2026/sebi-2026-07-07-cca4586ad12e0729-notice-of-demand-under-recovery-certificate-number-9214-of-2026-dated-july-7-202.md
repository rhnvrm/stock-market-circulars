## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand dated July 07, 2026 under Recovery Certificate (RC) No. 9214 of 2026, drawn against Dheeraj Wadhawan (PAN: AAOPW4517G), who has been classified as a defaulter. The notice arises from recovery proceedings initiated pursuant to a SEBI order in the matter of Dewan Housing Finance Corporation Limited (DHFL). The document directs the noticee to clear outstanding dues raised against him within the stipulated period, failing which further coercive recovery measures under the applicable recovery provisions may follow.

## Key Points

- Notice of Demand issued on July 07, 2026 under RC No. 9214 of 2026.
- Addressed to Dheeraj Wadhawan (PAN: AAOPW4517G) as the noticee/defaulter.
- Linked to a SEBI enforcement order relating to the matter of Dewan Housing Finance Corporation Limited.
- Forms part of SEBI's recovery proceedings process for enforcing monetary directions (such as disgorgement, penalty, or other dues) arising from a prior adjudication/order.
- Part of a batch of similar demand notices issued around the same date under different RC numbers against multiple individuals connected to the DHFL matter.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is an enforcement/recovery action taken under SEBI's existing powers (read with the Income-tax Act recovery mechanism as applied to SEBI recovery proceedings) to recover amounts due from the noticee pursuant to an earlier SEBI order.

## Compliance Requirements

The noticee, Dheeraj Wadhawan, is required to pay the outstanding demand amount specified in the notice within the timeframe prescribed therein. Non-compliance may result in further recovery action, including attachment of assets, bank accounts, or other coercive measures available to the Recovery Officer under the applicable recovery rules.

## Important Dates

- Notice of Demand date: July 07, 2026
- Publication/circular date: July 07, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact of this notice is confined to the named individual noticee, Dheeraj Wadhawan, and does not have a direct or broad market-wide effect. It reflects continued enforcement of a prior SEBI order concerning the Dewan Housing Finance Corporation Limited matter, reinforcing SEBI's ongoing efforts to recover dues from entities found involved in the underlying matter. No fresh trading restriction or company-level regulatory action is indicated in this specific notice.
