## Summary

SEBI has issued a release order for Recovery Certificate No. 7700 of 2024, which was issued against M. Hemasairam (PAN: ANLPM1562B) in the matter of Arvind Remedies Limited. A release order in the context of SEBI recovery certificates typically signifies that the recovery proceedings have been concluded — either through full payment of dues, settlement, or other resolution of the outstanding demand.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 7700 of 2024 issued against M. Hemasairam (PAN: ANLPM1562B) is being released
- The matter pertains to Arvind Remedies Limited
- A companion circular (ID: 4aadf9e1f5524d31) addresses the "Completion" of the same recovery certificate, confirming conclusion of proceedings
- Release orders are issued when SEBI's recovery demands have been satisfied or proceedings formally concluded
- The individual named is M. Hemasairam with PAN ANLPM1562B

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this order. This is an administrative enforcement document specific to the named individual and the Arvind Remedies Limited matter.

## Compliance Requirements

- No general compliance requirements for market participants arise from this order
- The release of the recovery certificate concludes SEBI's outstanding enforcement action against M. Hemasairam in this specific matter
- Entities involved in the Arvind Remedies Limited proceedings should note the closure of this recovery action

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate No. 7700 originally issued: 2024
- Release Order issued: May 11, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This order has minimal market impact. It is a routine administrative closure of an enforcement/recovery proceeding against a named individual in connection with Arvind Remedies Limited. No trading restrictions, listing changes, or broader compliance obligations result from this release order. The impact is limited solely to the named respondent, M. Hemasairam.
