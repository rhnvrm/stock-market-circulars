## Summary

SEBI has issued proceedings under Recovery Certificate No. RC9113 of 2026 (Attachment Proceeding No. 15443 of 2026) against Rukmani Devi Poddar (PAN: AFOPP5613B) in the matter of illiquid stock options. This order is part of SEBI's ongoing enforcement actions related to unauthorized trading in illiquid stock options.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9113 of 2026 issued against Rukmani Devi Poddar (PAN: AFOPP5613B)
- Attachment Proceeding No. 15443 of 2026 initiated
- Matter relates to illiquid stock options trading violations
- Action is part of SEBI's broader enforcement drive against illiquid stock options manipulation

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement/recovery action under existing SEBI recovery and attachment provisions.

## Compliance Requirements

- The named party (Rukmani Devi Poddar) is subject to the terms of the recovery certificate and attachment proceedings
- Banks, depositories, and other financial intermediaries may be directed to freeze or attach assets as specified in the order
- Any entities holding assets of the named party should comply with attachment directives issued by SEBI

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9113 of 2026 — issued in 2026
- Attachment Proceeding No. 15443 of 2026 — filed in 2026
- Document date: June 2026

## Impact Assessment

This order has limited broader market impact and is directed at a specific individual. It is part of SEBI's sustained enforcement campaign against participants found guilty of fraudulent trading in illiquid stock options. The action reinforces SEBI's commitment to pursuing recovery of dues from penalized entities through attachment proceedings.
