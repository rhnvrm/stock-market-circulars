## Summary

This order, issued by SEBI's Recovery Officer, releases the attachment on the salary account of Gauranga Roy. The account had earlier been attached as part of recovery proceedings initiated under Recovery Certificate (RC) 3547 of 2021, issued in the matter of Vasundhara Realcon Ltd. Recovery proceedings of this nature are typically initiated by SEBI to recover monetary dues (such as penalties, disgorgement amounts, or other regulatory dues) confirmed against an entity, using powers under Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992 read with the Second and Third Schedules to the Income Tax Act, 1961 and the Income Tax (Certificate Proceedings) Rules, 1962. As part of such proceedings, bank accounts connected to the defaulting entity or associated persons/garnishees may be provisionally attached to secure recovery. This order formally lifts that attachment on Gauranga Roy's salary account.

## Key Points

- The order pertains to Recovery Certificate RC3547 of 2021 in the matter of Vasundhara Realcon Ltd.
- The salary account of Gauranga Roy, which had been attached in connection with the recovery proceedings, is being released by this order.
- The order is issued by SEBI's Recovery Officer under its enforcement/recovery proceedings framework.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory or policy changes are introduced by this order. It is an individual enforcement action limited to the recovery proceedings against Vasundhara Realcon Ltd. and does not alter any SEBI regulations, circulars, or market-wide requirements.

## Compliance Requirements

There are no new compliance obligations arising from this order for market participants generally. Any obligations are specific to Vasundhara Realcon Ltd., its officers, and associated persons named in the underlying recovery certificate (RC3547 of 2021), and to the bank holding the salary account in question, which must now act in accordance with the release of attachment.
