## Summary

SEBI's Recovery Officer has issued a Notice of Demand dated 07.07.2026 under Recovery Certificate No. 9203 of 2026 against Sanjay Arunkumar Choksi (PAN: ABIPC0957H) in connection with the matter of Retro Green Revolution Limited. The notice formally demands payment of outstanding dues arising from an earlier SEBI order/direction and initiates the recovery process against the noticee under the recovery provisions applicable to SEBI proceedings (Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992 read with the Second and Third Schedules to the Income-tax Act, 1961, and the Income-tax (Certificate Proceedings) Rules, 1962, as made applicable to recovery of sums due to SEBI).

## Key Points

- A Recovery Certificate (No. 9203 of 2026) has been drawn up by SEBI's Recovery Officer against Sanjay Arunkumar Choksi (PAN: ABIPC0957H) for non-payment of amounts due pursuant to a prior SEBI order/direction in the matter of Retro Green Revolution Limited.
- The Notice of Demand, issued on 07.07.2026, formally calls upon the noticee to pay the outstanding amount within the statutory period specified in the notice.
- This notice is part of a series of parallel recovery certificates issued against multiple individuals/entities connected to the same underlying matter involving Retro Green Revolution Limited.
- Failure to comply within the stipulated period can expose the noticee to coercive recovery measures available to SEBI's Recovery Officer, including attachment and sale of movable/immovable property, arrest and detention, and appointment of a receiver, as provided under the applicable recovery rules.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced by this notice. It is an enforcement/recovery action giving effect to a pre-existing SEBI order or direction against the noticee in the Retro Green Revolution Limited matter.

## Compliance Requirements

- The noticee (Sanjay Arunkumar Choksi) is required to pay the demanded sum specified in Recovery Certificate No. 9203 of 2026 within the time period stated in the notice.
- Non-compliance may result in SEBI's Recovery Officer proceeding with further recovery action under the Income-tax (Certificate Proceedings) Rules, 1962, as applicable to SEBI recovery proceedings.

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate No. 9203 of 2026 dated 07.07.2026 (date of the underlying certificate).
- Notice of Demand issued on 07.07.2026, triggering the statutory payment window for the noticee to remit outstanding dues before further recovery steps are initiated.

## Impact Assessment

This notice has a narrow, individual-specific impact limited to the noticee named in the recovery certificate and does not affect listed securities, market trading, or investors at large. Its significance lies in SEBI's continued enforcement of recovery against parties connected to the Retro Green Revolution Limited matter, reinforcing the regulator's follow-through on collection of dues arising from its orders.
