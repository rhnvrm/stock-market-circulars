## Summary

SEBI has announced the completion of Recovery Certificate no. RC7668 of 2024 issued against Renuka Trust (PAN: AABTR0465M) in connection with enforcement proceedings in the matter of JMD Telefilms Industries Ltd. The completion signifies that the recovery process initiated under the certificate has been concluded.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate RC7668 of 2024 has been completed against Renuka Trust (PAN: AABTR0465M).
- The recovery pertains to enforcement action in the matter of JMD Telefilms Industries Ltd.
- Completion of the certificate indicates the dues/penalties directed to be recovered have been settled or the recovery process is otherwise concluded.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular is an administrative closure notice confirming the completion of an existing recovery certificate.

## Compliance Requirements

- No ongoing compliance obligations arise from this circular for market participants generally.
- Renuka Trust and related parties in the JMD Telefilms matter should retain records of the completed recovery certificate for their files.

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate: RC7668 of 2024
- Circular Date: 19 June 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market-wide impact. It is a procedural enforcement closure notice specific to Renuka Trust and the JMD Telefilms Industries Ltd. matter. Investors and market participants not connected to this matter are unaffected. The completion of the recovery certificate signals the conclusion of SEBI's recovery action against this entity.
