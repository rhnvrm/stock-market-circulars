## Summary

SEBI has issued a compliance/release order — Release Order No. 7773 of 2024 — in an ongoing recovery proceeding against Ramesh Babu (PAN: BGNPB5216K), who is alleged to have carried out unregistered investment advisory activities under the trade name "Investocare" without obtaining the requisite registration as an Investment Adviser under the SEBI (Investment Advisers) Regulations, 2013. The order relates to the recovery of dues/penalties previously levied against the respondent and reflects a compliance step (such as release of an attachment on bank accounts or other property) in the recovery process administered under SEBI's enforcement mechanism (Section 28A of the SEBI Act, read with the Second Schedule to the Income Tax Act, 1961, and Income Tax (Certificate Proceedings) Rules, 1962).

## Key Points

- Respondent: Ramesh Babu (PAN: BGNPB5216K)
- Entity/trade name involved: Investocare
- Nature of violation: Unregistered investment advisory activities, in contravention of SEBI (Investment Advisers) Regulations, 2013
- Order type: Compliance/Release Order No. 7773 of 2024, issued in recovery proceedings
- The order is administrative in nature and pertains to enforcement/recovery action against an individual, not a listed entity

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced by this order. It is an individual enforcement action arising from SEBI's ongoing recovery proceedings against persons found to have engaged in unregistered investment advisory activities.

## Compliance Requirements

- The order pertains specifically to the named respondent and does not impose new compliance obligations on market participants generally
- Investors and the public are reminded to verify the SEBI registration status of any person offering investment advisory services before engaging with them, via SEBI's official intermediary database

## Important Dates

- Order/Release Order No.: 7773 of 2024
- Date of publication: July 14, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This order has no direct impact on listed securities, trading activity, or market operations, as it concerns recovery/compliance proceedings against an individual for unregistered investment advisory activity. Its significance is primarily as an investor-protection and enforcement signal, reinforcing SEBI's continued action against unregistered advisory operators.
