## Summary

SEBI's Recovery Officer has issued a release order in respect of Recovery Certificate No. 7774 of 2024 issued against Ramesh Babu (PAN: BGNPB5216K), who was proceeded against for carrying out unregistered investment advisory activities under the name "Investocare." The release order indicates that the outstanding dues/demand under this recovery certificate have been satisfied or otherwise resolved, resulting in the recovery proceedings against the noticee being closed or the attachment/restraint previously imposed being lifted.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 7774 of 2024 was issued against Ramesh Babu (PAN: BGNPB5216K) for recovery of dues arising from unregistered investment advisory activities conducted under the name Investocare.
- SEBI's Recovery Officer has now issued a compliance/release order in this matter, indicating that the demand under the recovery certificate stands satisfied.
- This order is part of SEBI's ongoing enforcement action against unregistered investment advisers offering advisory services without the requisite SEBI registration.
- A related recovery certificate (No. 7773 of 2024) covering the same PAN and matter was also released around the same time, suggesting these are companion proceedings against the same entity.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is an individual enforcement/recovery order and does not introduce any new regulatory requirement, amendment, or policy change.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements are imposed on market participants generally. The noticee, Ramesh Babu, is released from further recovery action specific to Recovery Certificate No. 7774 of 2024 upon the Recovery Officer's satisfaction that dues have been paid/complied with.

## Important Dates

- Order date: July 14, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This order has minimal market-wide impact. It pertains solely to the closure of recovery proceedings against a single unregistered investment adviser and does not affect listed securities, trading operations, or the broader investor community. Its significance lies in demonstrating SEBI's continued enforcement against unregistered investment advisory activities and the eventual resolution of recovery actions once demands are met.
