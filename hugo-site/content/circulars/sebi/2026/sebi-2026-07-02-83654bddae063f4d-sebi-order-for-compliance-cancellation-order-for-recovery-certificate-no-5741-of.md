## Summary

SEBI's Recovery Officer has passed a Cancellation Order withdrawing Recovery Certificate No. 5741 of 2022, which had previously been issued against Prabhaben Gordhandas Savjani as part of recovery proceedings connected to the matter of Excel Castronics Limited. The order formally closes out this specific recovery instrument.

## Key Points

- The order relates to Recovery Certificate No. 5741 of 2022, originally issued against Prabhaben Gordhandas Savjani.
- The certificate was issued in connection with recovery proceedings in the matter of Excel Castronics Limited.
- SEBI has now cancelled this recovery certificate through a compliance order issued by the Recovery Officer.
- Recovery certificates of this nature are typically issued under the SEBI Act/Recovery of Debts framework to enforce monetary demands (such as penalties or disgorgement amounts) against defaulting individuals.

## Regulatory Changes

No broader regulatory changes are introduced by this order. It is an administrative/enforcement action specific to one recovery certificate and one named individual.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance obligations arise for market participants generally. Any prior obligations tied specifically to Recovery Certificate No. 5741 of 2022 stand cancelled pursuant to this order; the named individual's status under this particular certificate is resolved.

## Important Dates

Order dated July 2, 2026 (per document metadata). No further compliance deadlines are indicated.

## Impact Assessment

This order has a narrow, individual-specific impact limited to Prabhaben Gordhandas Savjani and the underlying recovery proceedings tied to Excel Castronics Limited. It does not affect trading, listing status, or the general investing public, and carries no direct market-wide implications.
