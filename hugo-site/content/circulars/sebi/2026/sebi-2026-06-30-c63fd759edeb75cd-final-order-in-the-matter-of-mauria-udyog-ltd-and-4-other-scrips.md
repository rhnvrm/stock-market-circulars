## Summary

SEBI has issued a Final Order (Ref: WTM/AS/IVD-2/ID16/32460/2026-27) under Sections 11(1), 11(4), 11(4A), 11B(1) and 11B(2) of the SEBI Act, 1992 in the matter of Mauria Udyog Ltd. and 4 other scrips. The order names a large group of entities and individuals — including Mauria Udyog Ltd., Vee Em Infocenter Ltd., Robert Resources Ltd., Econo Trade India Ltd., Econo Broking Pvt. Ltd. (erstwhile Bansal Finstock), Sai Metaltech LLP, and numerous individual noticees — running to 394 pages, indicating a wide-ranging investigation into trading activity across the five scrips covered by the proceedings.

## Key Points

- Final order issued against Mauria Udyog Ltd. and 4 other listed scrips along with a network of more than 49 connected individuals and corporate/LLP entities.
- Key corporate entities named include Robert Resources Ltd., Econo Trade India Ltd., Econo Broking Pvt. Ltd. (formerly Bansal Finstock), Vee Em Infocenter Ltd., and Sai Metaltech LLP.
- The order is issued under SEBI's powers to protect investors and regulate the securities market (Sections 11(1), 11(4), 11(4A)) and its powers of investigation/interim measures (Sections 11B(1), 11B(2)).
- The extensive list of individual noticees (with PAN details) suggests the case involves a coordinated trading network across the implicated scrips.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced; this is an enforcement/adjudicatory action applying existing SEBI Act provisions to specific entities found in violation.

## Compliance Requirements

Named entities and individuals are required to comply with any directions, restrictions, or penalties specified in the final order (full directions are detailed in the complete 394-page order, not fully captured in the extracted content). Market participants dealing with the named entities should review the order for any trading restrictions or debarment directions.

## Important Dates

Order dated June 2026 (PDF published under SEBI's June 2026 attachments); specific effective dates and compliance deadlines are detailed within the full order document.

## Impact Assessment

This is a high-impact enforcement action given the scale of entities involved and the listed status of Mauria Udyog Ltd. Investors and market participants should review the full order for details on any trading bans, monetary penalties, or other directions affecting the named scrips and entities, as such orders can materially affect trading in the implicated securities and the business operations of the named broking/financial entities.
