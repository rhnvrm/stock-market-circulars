## Summary

SEBI has cancelled Recovery Certificate No. 7315 of 2023 dated November 02, 2023 that was issued against Gopal Hari Agarwal (PAN: AETPA7820D), who was designated as a defaulter in connection with trading in illiquid stock options on the Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE).

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 7315 of 2023 dated November 02, 2023 has been cancelled
- The certificate was issued against Gopal Hari Agarwal (PAN: AETPA7820D)
- The matter relates to trading in illiquid stock options on BSE
- This is an administrative action reversing a previous enforcement measure

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a cancellation of a previously issued recovery certificate.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. This is an administrative action specific to an individual case.

## Important Dates

- Original Recovery Certificate Date: November 02, 2023
- Cancellation Order Date: January 08, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This cancellation has minimal market impact as it pertains to a specific enforcement action against an individual. The reversal of the recovery certificate suggests either completion of recovery proceedings, settlement of dues, or other resolution of the matter related to illiquid stock options trading on BSE.
