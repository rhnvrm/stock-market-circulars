## Summary

SEBI has issued a General Remittance Order dated June 09, 2026 under Recovery Certificate (RC) No. 9077 of 2026 against Amit S Yadav (PAN: ADZPY6811D). The order is issued in the matter of dealing in illiquid stock options at BSE and directs the remittance of outstanding dues payable to SEBI by the defaulter.

## Key Points

- General Remittance Order issued on June 09, 2026 by SEBI's Recovery Officer
- Recovery Certificate No. 9077 of 2026 drawn against Amit S Yadav (PAN: ADZPY6811D)
- The underlying matter involves dealing in illiquid stock options at BSE
- The order directs banks/depositories to remit funds from the defaulter's accounts to SEBI
- Part of SEBI's ongoing enforcement and recovery proceedings against market participants who violated regulations on illiquid stock options trading

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action under SEBI's existing recovery and penalty mechanism pursuant to earlier adjudication orders related to illiquid stock options manipulation at BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

- Banks, depositories, and financial institutions holding accounts or assets of Amit S Yadav (PAN: ADZPY6811D) are directed to remit the specified outstanding amount to SEBI upon receipt of this order
- The defaulter is required to comply with the remittance directions and clear outstanding dues under RC No. 9077 of 2026
- Non-compliance with a SEBI remittance order may attract further enforcement action

## Important Dates

- Order Date: June 09, 2026
- Recovery Certificate: RC No. 9077 of 2026

## Impact Assessment

This order has negligible market-wide impact. It is an individual enforcement and recovery action targeting a specific person (Amit S Yadav) found to have participated in dealings involving illiquid stock options at BSE. SEBI has been systematically pursuing recovery proceedings against individuals and entities penalised in the BSE illiquid stock options matter, and this order is part of that ongoing enforcement sweep. No broader market operations or listed securities are directly affected.
