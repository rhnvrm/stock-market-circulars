## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand dated July 14, 2026 under Recovery Certificate No. 9217 of 2026 to Mr. Vivek Seth (PAN: AACPS3800Q), who has been declared a defaulter in connection with dues arising in the matter of Oriental Trimex Limited. The notice formally demands payment of the outstanding amount specified in the Recovery Certificate, as part of SEBI's recovery proceedings against parties who have failed to comply with earlier regulatory or monetary orders.

## Key Points

- Notice of Demand issued under Recovery Certificate No. 9217 of 2026, dated July 14, 2026.
- Addressed to Mr. Vivek Seth (PAN: AACPS3800Q), identified as a defaulter.
- Recovery relates to the matter of Oriental Trimex Limited.
- The notice is part of SEBI's statutory recovery mechanism used to enforce unpaid penalties, disgorgement amounts, or other monetary directions.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced; this is an enforcement action under SEBI's existing recovery framework (recovery of sums due under the SEBI Act/regulations, akin to recovery of arrears of land revenue provisions).

## Compliance Requirements

The named defaulter, Mr. Vivek Seth, is required to pay the outstanding dues specified in the Recovery Certificate within the timeframe stipulated in the notice, failing which further coercive recovery measures (such as attachment of bank accounts, movable/immovable property, or other assets) may be initiated by the Recovery Officer.

## Important Dates

- Notice date: July 14, 2026
- Recovery Certificate No.: 9217 of 2026

## Impact Assessment

This notice pertains to an individual recovery proceeding against a named defaulter and does not directly impact trading, listing, or market operations of any listed company. Its significance is limited to the individual against whom recovery action is being enforced and serves as a continuation of SEBI's efforts to recover outstanding statutory dues in the Oriental Trimex Limited matter.
