## Summary

The SEBI Appellate Authority disposed of RTI Appeal No. 6925 of 2026 filed by S. Meenakshi Sundaram, who had sought information regarding refunds to investors in the PACL matter. The CPIO's original response directed the appellant to the Justice (Retd.) R.M. Lodha Committee, which handles PACL refund communications, while noting that public notices, press releases, status reports, and FAQs on the PACL matter are available on the SEBI website. The Appellate Authority found this response inadequate, as it did not clarify whether the requested information was actually available with SEBI, and remitted the matter back to the CPIO for fresh (de novo) consideration.

## Key Points

- Appellant S. Meenakshi Sundaram filed an RTI application dated May 12, 2026, seeking information on investor refunds in the PACL matter.
- The CPIO responded on June 02, 2026, stating that refund status communications are handled by the Justice (Retd.) R.M. Lodha Committee, and pointed to publicly available PACL-related materials on the SEBI website.
- The appellant filed an appeal (Reg. No. SEBIH/A/P/26/00033) dated June 10, 2026, dissatisfied with the response.
- The Appellate Authority, Ruchi Chojer, found the CPIO's reply did not clarify whether the requested information was available with SEBI, and directed de novo consideration of the application.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is an individual RTI appeal order under the Right to Information Act, 2005.

## Compliance Requirements

The CPIO, SEBI Mumbai, must reconsider the original RTI application afresh and send an appropriate response to the appellant in accordance with the RTI Act.

## Important Dates

- May 12, 2026: RTI application filed by appellant (received by SEBI May 15, 2026).
- June 02, 2026: CPIO's original response issued.
- June 10, 2026: Appeal filed by appellant.
- July 07, 2026: Appellate Authority's order issued; CPIO must respond within 15 working days from receipt of this order.

## Impact Assessment

This order has no direct impact on listed securities or market operations. It is a procedural RTI matter affecting the individual appellant and SEBI's internal handling of information requests related to the PACL refund process.
