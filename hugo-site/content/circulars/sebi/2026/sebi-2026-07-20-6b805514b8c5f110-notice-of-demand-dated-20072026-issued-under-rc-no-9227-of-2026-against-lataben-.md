## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand (Certificate No. 9227 of 2026) under Rule 2 of the Second Schedule to the Income-tax Act, 1961, read with Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992, against Lataben Narotambhai Rangi (PAN: AYRPR7467F), a resident of Ahmedabad, Gujarat. The notice demands recovery of Rs. 2,13,000, comprising an unpaid penalty of Rs. 2,00,000 imposed by SEBI's Adjudicating Officer for trading activity in the scrip of ANI Integrated Services Limited, along with accrued interest and recovery costs.

## Key Points

- The penalty of Rs. 2,00,000 was originally imposed via Adjudication Order No. Order/AK/DS/2025-26/32082-32109 dated 12.02.2026, in connection with trading activity in ANI Integrated Services Limited.
- Interest of Rs. 12,000 (calculated at 1% per month from February 2026 to July 2026) and a recovery cost of Rs. 1,000 have been added, bringing the total demand to Rs. 2,13,000.
- The notice directs the entity to pay the full amount within 15 days of receipt, via EFT/NEFT/RTGS to a designated SEBI recovery account, or through SEBI's online "Recovery Payment" portal.
- Non-payment empowers SEBI's Recovery Officer to pursue recovery through attachment/sale of movable and immovable property, attachment of bank accounts, arrest and detention, or appointment of a receiver.
- Any transfer of the entity's property or funds to a spouse, minor child, or son's family made without adequate consideration on or after 12.02.2026 will be deemed the entity's property for recovery purposes.
- The entity is barred from mortgaging, charging, leasing, or otherwise dealing with property without the Recovery Officer's permission; any such transaction is deemed void.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes; this is an enforcement action (recovery notice) under existing provisions of the SEBI Act, 1992 and the Income-tax Act, 1961.

## Compliance Requirements

The named individual, Lataben Narotambhai Rangi, must pay the total outstanding amount of Rs. 2,13,000 within 15 days of receiving the notice, and forward payment confirmation to SEBI's Recovery Officer via email or post using the prescribed format.

## Important Dates

- 12.02.2026: Original Adjudication Order imposing the penalty.
- 20.07.2026: Date of issuance of the Notice of Demand.
- Payment due within 15 days from receipt of this notice.

## Impact Assessment

This notice pertains to an individual enforcement/recovery action and does not impact ANI Integrated Services Limited's listed operations, share price, or broader market activity. It serves as a reminder of SEBI's ongoing enforcement of penalties related to irregular trading practices, but the market-wide and company-specific impact is minimal.
