## Summary

SEBI has issued a completion order for Recovery Certificate No. 6712 of 2023, which was issued against Dinesh Raja Punniamurthy (PAN: ALIPD6880G) in the matter of Securekloud Technologies Limited. The completion signifies that the recovery proceedings under the said certificate have been fulfilled and the matter is concluded.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 6712 of 2023 has been marked as completed
- The RC was issued against Dinesh Raja Punniamurthy (PAN: ALIPD6880G)
- The underlying enforcement matter relates to Securekloud Technologies Limited
- A corresponding Release Order (RC No. 6712 of 2023) was also issued on March 25, 2026
- This order concludes the recovery proceedings initiated under the said certificate

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an administrative order concluding existing enforcement/recovery proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements for market participants. This order pertains to the conclusion of a specific individual recovery action and does not impose obligations on other entities.

## Important Dates

- **March 25, 2026**: Date of completion order for Recovery Certificate No. 6712 of 2023
- **2023**: Year of original issuance of Recovery Certificate No. 6712

## Impact Assessment

This order has minimal market impact. It represents the administrative closure of an individual enforcement case related to Securekloud Technologies Limited. The completion of this recovery certificate, alongside the simultaneous release order, indicates that the dues under RC No. 6712 of 2023 have been recovered or otherwise settled, bringing the enforcement action to a close.
