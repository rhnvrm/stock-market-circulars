## Summary

SEBI has issued an order confirming the completion of recovery proceedings under Recovery Certificate No. RC3783 of 2021 against Bimla Devi Mundra (PAN: CBZPM6743E) in connection with the matter of Illiquid Stock Options. This represents the conclusion of enforcement and recovery action taken against the defaulter.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate Number: RC3783 of 2021
- Defaulter: Bimla Devi Mundra
- PAN: CBZPM6743E
- Matter: Illiquid Stock Options
- Status: Recovery proceedings completed
- Order Date: January 15, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement completion order.

## Compliance Requirements

This order confirms completion of recovery proceedings. No new compliance requirements are imposed by this order.

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate Issue Date: 2021 (RC3783 of 2021)
- Completion Order Date: January 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is an administrative order confirming the completion of recovery proceedings against a specific individual in relation to illiquid stock options. The order indicates successful conclusion of SEBI's enforcement action in this particular case. No broader implications for market participants or regulatory requirements.
