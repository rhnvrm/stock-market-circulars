## Summary

SEBI has issued a release order in respect of Recovery Certificate No. RC8621 of 2025, which was issued against Pawan Kumar Agarwal (PAN: AANPA5607N), a defaulter in a matter concerning dealing in illiquid stock options. The release order indicates that the recovery proceedings initiated under this certificate have been concluded or otherwise disposed of, and any attachment or coercive recovery action taken pursuant to the certificate is accordingly released.

## Key Points

- Defaulter: Pawan Kumar Agarwal (PAN: AANPA5607N)
- Recovery Certificate No.: RC8621 of 2025
- Underlying violation: Dealing in Illiquid Stock Options
- Action: Release order issued in respect of the recovery certificate, closing out the associated recovery action

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced; this is a case-specific enforcement update releasing a previously initiated recovery action against an individual defaulter.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance action is required from market participants, listed companies, or intermediaries. The notice pertains solely to the release of recovery proceedings against the named individual defaulter.

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate No.: RC8621 of 2025
- Release order notice date: July 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market-wide impact as it concerns the conclusion of an individual enforcement/recovery matter. It does not affect trading, listing status, or obligations of any listed company or the broader investor community, but reflects SEBI's ongoing enforcement and closure of recovery certificates issued against individuals found in default relating to illiquid stock options trading violations.
