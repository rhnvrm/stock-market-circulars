## Summary

SEBI has completed Recovery Certificate No. RC6856 of 2023 issued against Gitesh Tikamani HUF (PAN: AAFHG0088M) in connection with the matter of Illiquid Stock Options. This order signifies the conclusion of recovery proceedings against this entity, indicating that the outstanding dues have been recovered or the recovery process has otherwise been brought to a close.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC6856 of 2023 has been marked as completed
- The defaulter entity is Gitesh Tikamani HUF (Hindu Undivided Family), PAN: AAFHG0088M
- The matter pertains to SEBI's enforcement action in the Illiquid Stock Options case on BSE
- Completion of the RC indicates recovery of dues owed to SEBI or conclusion of the enforcement action against this specific entity
- This is part of SEBI's broader enforcement drive against entities involved in illiquid stock options trading on BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This order represents the administrative closure of a specific recovery certificate under SEBI's enforcement and recovery framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- No new compliance requirements for market participants
- The specific entity (Gitesh Tikamani HUF) has discharged its obligations under Recovery Certificate RC6856 of 2023
- Any attachments or restrictions placed on the entity's assets or accounts under this RC stand released upon completion

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate RC6856 originally issued: 2023
- Completion order date: May 7, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This order has minimal broader market impact. It is an administrative closure specific to one entity (Gitesh Tikamani HUF) in the long-running Illiquid Stock Options enforcement matter. The completion of this RC is a routine step in SEBI's recovery proceedings and does not affect other market participants. Investors and market intermediaries need not take any action in response to this order.
