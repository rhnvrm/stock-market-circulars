## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order under Recovery Certificate No. RC9013 of 2026 in respect of Pramod Kumar Jain (PAN: ACLPJ7469D) in the matter of illiquid stock options. The release order signifies that the dues or penalties imposed under the recovery certificate have been satisfied or that attached assets/accounts are being released.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9013 of 2026 issued against Pramod Kumar Jain (PAN: ACLPJ7469D)
- The release order pertains to the illiquid stock options matter, which involved manipulation in the illiquid stock options segment
- SEBI's recovery mechanism under Section 28A of the SEBI Act enables attachment and recovery of amounts from defaulters
- The release order indicates partial or full satisfaction of the outstanding recovery amount

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is an administrative/enforcement order under the existing recovery framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- The release order is directed at the concerned individual (Pramod Kumar Jain) and relevant custodians (banks/depositories)
- Custodians holding attached assets are instructed to release the attachment upon receipt of this order
- No new compliance obligations are imposed on market participants generally

## Important Dates

- Release Order Date: June 2026
- Recovery Certificate No.: RC9013 of 2026

## Impact Assessment

This order has minimal broader market impact. It is an individual enforcement/recovery matter related to the illiquid stock options segment. The release of attachment suggests recovery proceedings against Pramod Kumar Jain have reached a stage of satisfaction or partial resolution. The illiquid stock options matter has been a long-standing enforcement focus for SEBI involving multiple entities.
