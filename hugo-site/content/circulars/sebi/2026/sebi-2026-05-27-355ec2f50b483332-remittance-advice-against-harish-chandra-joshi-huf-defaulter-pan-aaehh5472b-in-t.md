## Summary

SEBI has issued a Remittance Advice against Harish Chandra Joshi HUF, designated as a defaulter with PAN AAEHH5472B, in connection with the matter of ISO (Inspection/Settlement Operations) at BSE. This action is taken under Recovery Certificate No. 8198 of 2024, directing recovery of outstanding dues from the defaulting entity.

## Key Points

- Defaulter: Harish Chandra Joshi HUF (PAN: AAEHH5472B)
- Recovery Certificate No.: 8198 of 2024
- Matter: ISO at BSE — Harish Chandra Joshi HUF
- Action type: Remittance Advice issued as part of SEBI's recovery proceedings
- The remittance advice directs deposit/remittance of dues owed to SEBI by the defaulter

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is an enforcement and recovery action under SEBI's existing recovery proceedings framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- The defaulter (Harish Chandra Joshi HUF) is required to remit the amounts specified under Recovery Certificate No. 8198 of 2024.
- Non-compliance may result in further coercive recovery measures including attachment of assets, bank accounts, or securities.

## Important Dates

- Document Date: May 27, 2026
- Recovery Certificate: No. 8198 of 2024 (issued in 2024)

## Impact Assessment

This is a targeted enforcement action against a single defaulting entity (HUF) and has no broad market-wide impact. It reflects SEBI's continued exercise of its recovery powers to enforce outstanding dues from defaulters in the securities market. Market participants and investors are not directly affected unless they have dealings with the named defaulter.
