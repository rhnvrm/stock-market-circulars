## Summary

SEBI has issued a notice confirming completion of Recovery Certificate No. RC8992 of 2025, which was issued for recovery of dues in the matter of Thirani Projects Ltd. The certificate was issued against Pugalia Vyapaar Pvt. Ltd. (PAN: AABCP6776C), an entity that has since been amalgamated into Plenty Niryat Pvt. Ltd. The recovery proceedings under this certificate now stand completed/closed.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC8992 of 2025 relates to outstanding dues connected to the matter of Thirani Projects Ltd.
- The certificate was originally issued against Pugalia Vyapaar Pvt. Ltd. (PAN: AABCP6776C).
- Pugalia Vyapaar Pvt. Ltd. has since been amalgamated into Plenty Niryat Pvt. Ltd.
- SEBI/the Recovery Officer has confirmed that recovery action under this certificate has been completed.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced; this is an administrative/enforcement update confirming closure of a specific recovery proceeding under the SEBI Act recovery mechanism (typically initiated under Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992).

## Compliance Requirements

No fresh compliance obligations arise from this notice for market participants generally. Plenty Niryat Pvt. Ltd. (as successor to Pugalia Vyapaar Pvt. Ltd.) and any relevant parties in the Thirani Projects Ltd. matter should retain this confirmation for their records as evidence of closure of the recovery certificate.

## Important Dates

Notice dated 2026-07-01. Specific dates of the amalgamation and underlying recovery proceedings are not detailed in the available circular text.

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact administrative closure notice concerning a private, non-listed entity and does not directly affect trading, listing, or the securities of any publicly listed company. It is primarily relevant for regulatory record-keeping and parties directly involved in the Thirani Projects Ltd. recovery matter.
