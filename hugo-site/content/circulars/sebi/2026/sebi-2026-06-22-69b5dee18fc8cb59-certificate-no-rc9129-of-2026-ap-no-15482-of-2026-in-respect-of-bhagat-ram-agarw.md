## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC9129 of 2026 under Application No. 15482 of 2026 against Bhagat Ram Agarwal HUF (PAN: AAGHB3924E) in the matter of Illiquid Stock Options. The certificate authorises recovery of outstanding dues as determined in prior regulatory proceedings against the entity.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9129 of 2026 issued against Bhagat Ram Agarwal HUF (PAN: AAGHB3924E)
- Application No. 15482 of 2026 filed for recovery of SEBI-determined dues
- The matter pertains to illiquid stock options, an area where SEBI has taken extensive enforcement action against fraudulent trading practices
- The recovery certificate enables SEBI to recover the outstanding penalty/disgorgement amount through the certificate mechanism

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is an enforcement/recovery action under existing SEBI recovery mechanisms.

## Compliance Requirements

- Bhagat Ram Agarwal HUF is required to satisfy the dues specified in the recovery certificate
- Non-payment may result in further coercive recovery proceedings by SEBI

## Important Dates

- Date of issuance: 2026-06-22
- Recovery Certificate No.: RC9129 of 2026
- Application No.: 15482 of 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a limited market impact as it is directed solely at a specific entity (Bhagat Ram Agarwal HUF) in connection with SEBI's ongoing enforcement actions in the illiquid stock options segment. The illiquid stock options enforcement program has broadly targeted entities that engaged in manipulative and fraudulent trading in BSE's illiquid stock options segment. This recovery certificate represents a late-stage enforcement step to collect dues from the named party.
