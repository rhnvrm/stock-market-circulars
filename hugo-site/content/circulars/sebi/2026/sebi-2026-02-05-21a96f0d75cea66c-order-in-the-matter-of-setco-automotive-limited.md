## Summary

SEBI has issued an order under sections 11(1), 11(4), 11(4A), 11B(1), 11B(2) read with sections 15HA and 15HB of the SEBI Act, 1992 in the matter of Setco Automotive Limited. The order involves 10 noticees including Setco Automotive Ltd., Setco Auto Systems Pvt. Ltd., and 8 individuals (Harish Sheth, Udit Harish Sheth, Arun Arora, Ashok Kumar Jha, Suhasini Somesh Sathe, Rovinder Kumar Singla, Urja Harshal Shah, and Jatinder Bir Singh Gujral) for alleged violations relating to fraudulent and unfair trade practices in securities markets.

## Key Points

- Order issued against Setco Automotive Limited and 9 other entities/individuals
- Proceedings initiated under multiple sections of SEBI Act including enforcement and investigation provisions
- Case involves voluminous records including a 40-page show cause notice with 26 annexures and 3 CDs of documents
- SEBI exercising its regulatory powers to prohibit fraudulent and unfair trade practices as mandated under section 11(2)(e) of SEBI Act
- Order references Supreme Court observation on SEBI's wide-ranging legislative, executive and judicial powers
- The proceedings examine scope of 'fraud', 'fraudulent', 'unfair' and 'manipulative' practices under section 12A of SEBI Act

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI Act provisions.
