## Summary

The SEBI Appellate Authority disposed of RTI Appeal No. 6912 of 2026 filed by Peeyush Nigam against the CPIO, SEBI, Mumbai. The appeal challenged the respondent's reply to query nos. 2 to 6 of an RTI application dated May 07, 2026, which sought information regarding SEBI's handling of Complaint No. SEBIE/UP26/KANP/016667/1 filed against G Surgiwear Limited concerning alleged misleading disclosures in its Draft Red Herring Prospectus (DRHP).

## Key Points

- The appellant sought information on: (i) whether SEBI issued any notice/show cause/deficiency letter/query to G Surgiwear Limited regarding the complaint; (ii) copies of correspondence/notices issued to the company; (iii) whether the company submitted any reply/explanation/documents; (iv) whether the matter is under examination/investigation by SEBI; and (v) whether the allegations were considered during processing/review of the company's DRHP/RHP.
- The respondent held that query nos. 2, 4, 5 and 6 sought clarification rather than "information" as defined under Section 2(f) of the RTI Act, and were therefore not required to be answered.
- For query no. 3 (copies of correspondence/notices), the respondent invoked the exemption under Section 8(1)(e) of the RTI Act, stating the information was held by SEBI in a fiduciary relationship.
- The Appellate Authority upheld the respondent's stance on query nos. 2, 4 to 6, relying on the CIC ruling in Azad Singh vs. CPIO, Oriental Insurance Company Limited, which held that queries seeking explanation, opinion, confirmation, or clarification from a CPIO do not constitute "information" under the RTI Act.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is an individual adjudicatory order under the RTI Act and does not introduce any new regulatory requirement.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance obligations arise for market participants or listed/proposed-to-be-listed companies from this order.

## Important Dates

- May 07, 2026: RTI application filed by the appellant.
- June 01, 2026: Respondent's reply to the RTI application.
- June 02, 2026: Appeal filed by the appellant (Reg. No. SEBIH/A/E/26/00204).
- July 01, 2026: Date of the Appellate Authority's order disposing of the appeal.

## Impact Assessment

The order has minimal direct market impact as it pertains to an individual RTI request rather than a substantive regulatory or enforcement action against G Surgiwear Limited. It confirms SEBI's established practice of declining to disclose internal correspondence and investigation-related details under RTI exemptions, and does not indicate any conclusion regarding the underlying complaint against the company's DRHP disclosures.
