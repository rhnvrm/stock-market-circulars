## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC9099 of 2026 against Anita Jhajharia (PAN: ACQPJ4887N) in connection with Adjudication Proceedings A.P. No. 15411 of 2026 in the matter of Illiquid Stock Options. This certificate authorises recovery of outstanding dues/penalties imposed on the named individual pursuant to SEBI enforcement proceedings in the illiquid stock options manipulation matter.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9099 of 2026 issued against Anita Jhajharia (PAN: ACQPJ4887N)
- Relates to Adjudication Proceedings A.P. No. 15411 of 2026
- Pertains to SEBI's ongoing enforcement action in the Illiquid Stock Options matter
- Recovery action is being pursued under SEBI's statutory powers
- Part of a series of recovery certificates issued by SEBI in the illiquid stock options enforcement drive

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an enforcement/recovery action under existing SEBI regulations and orders passed in the Illiquid Stock Options matter.

## Compliance Requirements

- Anita Jhajharia is required to pay the demanded amount as specified in the Recovery Certificate within the stipulated time period
- Failure to comply may result in further enforcement measures including attachment of assets

## Important Dates

- Certificate Date: June 2026 (specific date in the PDF)
- Payment deadline: As specified in the Recovery Certificate (refer to PDF for exact date)

## Impact Assessment

This action has no broad market impact. It is targeted enforcement against a single named individual (Anita Jhajharia) as part of SEBI's sustained recovery drive in the Illiquid Stock Options manipulation matter. The illiquid stock options segment enforcement series has resulted in numerous recovery certificates being issued against various entities found to have engaged in manipulative trading practices.
