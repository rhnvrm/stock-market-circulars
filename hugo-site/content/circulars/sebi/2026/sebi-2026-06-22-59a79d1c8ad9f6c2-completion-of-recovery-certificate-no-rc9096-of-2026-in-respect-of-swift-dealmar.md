## Summary

SEBI has completed Recovery Certificate No. RC9096 of 2026 issued against Swift Dealmark Private Limited (PAN: AAPCS1110D) in the matter of illiquid stock options. This completion notice, read alongside the related Release Order for the same RC, indicates that the full recovery amount has been realised and the enforcement proceedings under this certificate are now closed.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9096 of 2026 has been formally completed by SEBI.
- The noticee entity is Swift Dealmark Private Limited (PAN: AAPCS1110D).
- The matter pertains to SEBI's enforcement action in the illiquid stock options segment.
- A companion Release Order (separate circular) was issued simultaneously, confirming discharge of the recovery obligation.
- No further recovery action is pending under this certificate.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced by this notice. It is an administrative closure document under SEBI's enforcement/recovery proceedings framework.

## Compliance Requirements

No ongoing compliance obligations arise from this completion notice for market participants broadly. The recovery obligation for Swift Dealmark Private Limited has been discharged.

## Important Dates

- **Publication Date:** 22 June 2026
- **Recovery Certificate:** RC9096 of 2026 (now completed)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has no direct market impact. It represents the administrative conclusion of an individual enforcement matter related to illiquid stock options trading. The illiquid stock options enforcement drive by SEBI has resulted in numerous such recovery certificates being issued and completed over the years, and this is one such routine closure.
