## Summary

SEBI has issued a notice regarding the completion of Recovery Certificate No. RC9007 of 2026 against Nimisha Hemani (PAN: ABFPH7961K). The recovery proceedings relate to the matter of Illiquid Stock Options, and this notice confirms that SEBI has successfully completed the recovery process against the defaulting individual.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate Number: RC9007 of 2026
- Defaulter: Nimisha Hemani
- PAN of Defaulter: ABFPH7961K
- Subject Matter: Illiquid Stock Options
- Status: Recovery proceedings completed
- Issuing Authority: SEBI
- A companion Release Order (removing the defaulter from the list) was also issued on the same date

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a completion notice for existing recovery proceedings initiated against Nimisha Hemani in the matter of Illiquid Stock Options.

## Compliance Requirements

This notice serves as public information regarding the completion of recovery proceedings. No new compliance requirements are imposed through this circular. The completion indicates that the outstanding dues under Recovery Certificate No. RC9007 of 2026 have been recovered by SEBI.

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate Number: RC9007 of 2026
- Completion Notice Date: May 19, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a completion notice for recovery proceedings against a specific individual defaulter (Nimisha Hemani) in the matter of Illiquid Stock Options. The impact is limited to the defaulter and has minimal market-wide implications. The notice confirms SEBI's enforcement action and successful completion of recovery proceedings, demonstrating the regulator's continued pursuit of outstanding dues from defaulters in the illiquid stock options matter. A corresponding Release Order was issued simultaneously, indicating the defaulter has been discharged from the recovery proceedings upon payment of dues.
