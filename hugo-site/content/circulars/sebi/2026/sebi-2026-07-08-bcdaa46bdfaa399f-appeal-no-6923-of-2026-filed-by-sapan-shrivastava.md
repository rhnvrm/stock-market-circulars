## Summary

The Appellate Authority under the Right to Information Act, 2005 disposed of Appeal No. 6923 of 2026 filed by Sapan Shrivastava against SEBI's CPIO, Mumbai. The appellant had sought details on the usage of the Delhi Guest House and an official car attached to the Delhi office by a named Executive Director (Babita Rayudu) and/or her son over the period 2019-2025, as well as copies of any policy governing such usage. The CPIO had denied the information citing privacy exemptions, and the Appellate Authority concurred with this denial.

## Key Points

- The RTI application, originally filed with the Department of Economic Affairs, was transferred to SEBI on May 26, 2026.
- The appellant sought year-wise data (2019-2025) on Delhi Guest House usage and official car kilometres/days of use by a named SEBI Executive Director and/or her son.
- The appellant also sought copies of any circulars/office notes/policy prescribing permissible guest house and car usage for Executive Directors.
- The CPIO denied the request under Section 8(1)(j) (personal information with no public interest, unwarranted invasion of privacy) and Section 8(1)(g) (information that could endanger life or physical safety) of the RTI Act.
- The Appellate Authority reviewed the application, the CPIO's response, and the appeal, and concurred with the CPIO's reasoning to deny the information.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is an adjudicatory order under the RTI Act disposing of a specific appeal; it does not introduce any new regulation, circular, or policy change.

## Compliance Requirements

Not applicable to listed companies, market intermediaries, or stock exchanges. This order pertains solely to SEBI's internal RTI grievance redressal process.

## Important Dates

- May 26, 2026: Application transferred to SEBI.
- June 08, 2026: CPIO's response to the RTI application.
- June 10, 2026: Appeal filed by the appellant (Reg. No. SEBIH/A/E/26/00214).

## Impact Assessment

This order has no market, trading, or listing impact. It is an internal administrative/governance matter concerning SEBI's handling of an RTI request about personal facility usage by an Executive Director, decided on privacy-exemption grounds under the RTI Act.
