## Summary

SEBI has issued a completion notice for Recovery Certificate No. 8711 of 2025 against Securekloud Technologies Limited (formerly known as 8Kmiles Software Services Ltd), bearing PAN AABCP6266D. The completion signifies that the outstanding dues covered under this recovery certificate have been fully recovered by SEBI's Recovery Officer.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8711 of 2025 has been marked as completed against Securekloud Technologies Limited
- The company was formerly known as 8Kmiles Software Services Ltd (PAN: AABCP6266D)
- Completion indicates full recovery of dues/penalties imposed by SEBI in the matter of Securekloud Technologies Limited
- The recovery proceedings under this certificate are now concluded

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This notice represents the administrative closure of an existing enforcement/recovery action.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements for market participants. This notice is specific to Securekloud Technologies Limited and confirms the conclusion of recovery proceedings against the entity.

## Important Dates

- **Publication Date:** May 11, 2026
- **Recovery Certificate:** No. 8711 of 2025 (issued in 2025, completed May 2026)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal market impact as it pertains to the administrative closure of enforcement proceedings against a specific entity. Investors in Securekloud Technologies Limited (formerly 8Kmiles Software Services Ltd) should note that SEBI's recovery action against the company has been concluded. No broader market or operational impact is anticipated.
