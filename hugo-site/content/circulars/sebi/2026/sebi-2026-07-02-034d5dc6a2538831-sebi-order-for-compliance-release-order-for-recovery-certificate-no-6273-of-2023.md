## Summary

SEBI's Recovery Officer has issued a compliance order releasing Recovery Certificate No. 6273 of 2023, which had been issued against Kartik Clothing And Fabrics Pvt. Ltd. in connection with recovery proceedings arising in the matter of Excel Castronics Limited. Recovery Certificates are issued by SEBI under its recovery proceedings mechanism (in terms of the Securities and Exchange Board of India Act, 1992 read with the Second Schedule to the Income Tax Act, 1961) to recover dues, penalties, or disgorgement amounts from parties connected with a SEBI enforcement matter. A release order signifies that the recovery certificate against the named party is being discharged or withdrawn.

## Key Points

- The order pertains to Recovery Certificate No. 6273 of 2023 issued against Kartik Clothing And Fabrics Pvt. Ltd.
- The underlying recovery proceedings arise in the matter of Excel Castronics Limited.
- This release order is issued for compliance purposes, effectively closing or discharging the recovery action against this specific party.
- This filing is part of a batch of similar release orders issued the same day against multiple parties connected to the Excel Castronics Limited matter, suggesting a coordinated closure of related recovery certificates.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is an administrative/enforcement action closing out a specific recovery certificate under SEBI's existing recovery proceedings framework.

## Compliance Requirements

No further compliance action is required from market participants generally. Kartik Clothing And Fabrics Pvt. Ltd., as the named party, is released from the specific recovery certificate referenced in this order.

## Important Dates

Order dated: 2026-07-02 (as per publication date on SEBI's website).

## Impact Assessment

This order has narrow, party-specific impact and does not directly affect listed securities, trading, or broader market participants. It is relevant primarily to those tracking enforcement and recovery proceedings connected to Excel Castronics Limited.
