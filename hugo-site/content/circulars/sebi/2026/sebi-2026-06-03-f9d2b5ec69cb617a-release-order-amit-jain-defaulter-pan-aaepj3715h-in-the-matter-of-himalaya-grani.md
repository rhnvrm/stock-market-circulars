## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order against Amit Jain (Defaulter, PAN: AAEPJ3715H) in the matter of Himalaya Granites Ltd. under Recovery Certificate No. 9105 of 2026. This order directs the release of previously attached bank accounts and/or demat accounts held by or on behalf of the defaulter, indicating that the recovery proceedings have reached a stage warranting release — either through payment of dues, compliance with the recovery order, or other resolution.

## Key Points

- Release Order issued by SEBI against Amit Jain, designated as a defaulter
- PAN of defaulter: AAEPJ3715H
- Recovery Certificate No. 9105 of 2026 is the underlying instrument for this action
- The order pertains to the matter of Himalaya Granites Ltd.
- Previously attached assets (bank accounts and/or demat accounts) are directed to be released
- Banks and depositories holding attached assets are required to act on this release instruction

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an enforcement and recovery action specific to a named individual. It reflects the conclusion or partial resolution of recovery proceedings initiated by SEBI under the Recovery Certificate mechanism.

## Compliance Requirements

- Banks holding attached accounts of Amit Jain (PAN: AAEPJ3715H) must process the release as directed
- Depositories and depository participants holding attached demat accounts must similarly comply with the release instruction
- No compliance obligations apply to the general market or other entities

## Important Dates

- Release Order date: June 2026
- Recovery Certificate No. 9105 of 2026 (issued earlier in 2026)

## Impact Assessment

This order has minimal market impact and is limited to the individual defaulter Amit Jain in the context of the Himalaya Granites Ltd. matter. The release of attached assets is a routine enforcement action indicating resolution of the underlying recovery demand. No systemic or broad investor impact is anticipated.
