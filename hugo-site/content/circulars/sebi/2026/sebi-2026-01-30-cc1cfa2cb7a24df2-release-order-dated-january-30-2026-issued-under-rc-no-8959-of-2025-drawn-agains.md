## Summary

SEBI has issued a release order dated January 30, 2026 under Recovery Certificate No. 8959 of 2025 against Ranjeet S Yadav in connection with dealings in illiquid stock options at the Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE). This is an enforcement action related to compliance with recovery proceedings.

## Key Points

- Release order issued under RC No. 8959 of 2025
- Subject: Ranjeet S Yadav
- Matter relates to dealing in illiquid stock options at BSE
- Order dated January 30, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement/recovery action.

## Compliance Requirements

The order pertains to compliance with recovery certificate proceedings against the named individual.

## Important Dates

- Order Date: January 30, 2026
- Recovery Certificate No.: 8959 of 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is an individual enforcement case with no broad market impact. The release order is specific to recovery proceedings against Ranjeet S Yadav for past dealings in illiquid stock options at BSE.
