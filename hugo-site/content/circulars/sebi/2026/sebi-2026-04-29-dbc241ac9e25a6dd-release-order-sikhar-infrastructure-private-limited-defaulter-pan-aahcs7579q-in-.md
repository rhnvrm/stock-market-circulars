## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order in respect of Recovery Certificate No. 7223 of 2023 against Sikhar Infrastructure Private Limited (PAN: AAHCS7579Q). The release order formally discharges the defaulter from further recovery proceedings in the matter of trading in illiquid stock options at BSE, confirming that the outstanding obligations under the recovery certificate have been satisfied.

## Key Points

- Release Order issued for Recovery Certificate No. 7223 of 2023
- Defaulter: Sikhar Infrastructure Private Limited (PAN: AAHCS7579Q)
- Matter pertains to trading in illiquid stock options at BSE
- The release order follows the completion of recovery proceedings against the defaulter
- This is a procedural enforcement closure; no new regulatory directives are issued

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced by this order. It represents the administrative release and formal closure of an existing enforcement and recovery action initiated by SEBI.

## Compliance Requirements

- No new compliance obligations arise for market participants from this release order
- Sikhar Infrastructure Private Limited has discharged its financial obligations under Recovery Certificate No. 7223 of 2023
- SEBI recovery proceedings against this entity in this matter are formally closed

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate No.: 7223 of 2023
- Release Order Date: April 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This release order has no broader market impact. It is a procedural administrative order formally releasing Sikhar Infrastructure Private Limited from recovery obligations under Certificate No. 7223 of 2023 related to trading in illiquid stock options at BSE. The illiquid stock options segment at BSE has been subject to extensive SEBI enforcement for artificial trading and price manipulation; this closure indicates the individual enforcement action against this entity has concluded successfully.
