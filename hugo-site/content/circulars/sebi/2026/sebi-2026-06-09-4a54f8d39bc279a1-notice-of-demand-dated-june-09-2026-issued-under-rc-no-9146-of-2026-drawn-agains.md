## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand dated June 09, 2026 under Recovery Certificate (RC) No. 9146 of 2026 against Sanjay V Mohare HUF (PAN: AAGHS4356D). The notice pertains to the entity's involvement in dealing in illiquid stock options at BSE, for which SEBI has directed recovery of dues as specified in the recovery certificate.

## Key Points

- Notice of Demand issued on June 09, 2026 under RC No. 9146 of 2026
- Issued against Sanjay V Mohare HUF (PAN: AAGHS4356D)
- Matter relates to dealing in illiquid stock options at BSE
- Action is part of SEBI's enforcement and recovery proceedings against entities involved in illiquid options trading
- The Recovery Certificate authorises SEBI to recover amounts as if they were arrears of land revenue

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is an enforcement/recovery action under existing SEBI regulations and recovery mechanisms.

## Compliance Requirements

- The respondent (Sanjay V Mohare HUF) is required to pay the demanded amount as specified under RC No. 9146 of 2026
- Non-compliance with the demand may result in further recovery proceedings including attachment of assets
- The entity must respond to the notice within the stipulated timeframe mentioned in the demand

## Important Dates

- Notice of Demand Date: June 09, 2026
- Recovery Certificate No.: 9146 of 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is an individual enforcement action with no direct broad market impact. It reflects SEBI's continued focus on recovering dues from entities found to have engaged in manipulative trading in illiquid stock options at BSE. The illiquid stock options segment at BSE has been the subject of extensive SEBI enforcement action over the years. The impact is limited to the respondent entity (Sanjay V Mohare HUF) and does not affect other market participants.
