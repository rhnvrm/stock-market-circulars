## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC9140 of 2026 (A.P. No. 15489 of 2026) against Bimla Devi Killa (PAN: AESPK3270J) for recovery of outstanding dues in connection with the Illiquid Stock Options enforcement matter. This certificate is issued under the SEBI Act empowering the Recovery Officer to recover the specified amount as arrears of land revenue.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9140 of 2026 issued against Bimla Devi Killa (PAN: AESPK3270J)
- Application/Appeal No.: 15489 of 2026
- Relates to the Illiquid Stock Options matter — SEBI's enforcement action against manipulation in NSE's illiquid options segment
- Issued under SEBI's recovery proceedings mechanism to enforce collection of penalty/dues
- Certificate enables the Recovery Officer to recover amounts as arrears of land revenue

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an enforcement/recovery action implementing an existing order.

## Compliance Requirements

The named individual (Bimla Devi Killa) is required to pay the outstanding dues as specified in the recovery certificate. Non-payment enables SEBI's Recovery Officer to attach and sell assets to recover the amount.

## Important Dates

- Certificate issued: 22 June 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is an individual-level enforcement action with no broader market or systemic impact. It is part of SEBI's ongoing series of recovery proceedings in the Illiquid Stock Options case, where numerous traders and entities were found to have engaged in manipulative trading in the illiquid options segment of NSE. The impact is confined to the named individual.
