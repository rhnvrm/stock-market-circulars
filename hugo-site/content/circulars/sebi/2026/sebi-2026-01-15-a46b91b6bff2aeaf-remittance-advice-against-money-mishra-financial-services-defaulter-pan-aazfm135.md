## Summary

SEBI has issued a remittance advice against Money Mishra Financial Services (PAN: AAZFM1357R) in connection with the transfer of mutual fund units under Recovery Certificate No. 8596 of 2025. This is part of SEBI's enforcement and recovery proceedings.

## Key Points

- Entity: Money Mishra Financial Services
- PAN: AAZFM1357R
- Recovery Certificate No. 8596 of 2025
- Matter relates to transfer of mutual fund units
- Status: Defaulter under SEBI proceedings

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an enforcement action specific to the named entity.

## Compliance Requirements

This notice serves as formal documentation of recovery proceedings against Money Mishra Financial Services. Relevant parties and intermediaries should take note of the defaulter status of this entity.

## Important Dates

- Document Date: January 15, 2026
- Recovery Certificate No. 8596 of 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is an entity-specific enforcement action with no direct market-wide impact. The remittance advice is part of SEBI's standard recovery procedures for defaulting entities in matters related to mutual fund unit transfers.
