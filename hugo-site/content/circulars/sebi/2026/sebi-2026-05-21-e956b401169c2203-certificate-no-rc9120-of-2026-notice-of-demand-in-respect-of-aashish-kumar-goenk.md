## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand under Recovery Certificate No. RC9120 of 2026 against Aashish Kumar Goenka (PAN: BOCPG5241L) in connection with the Illiquid Stock Options enforcement matter. The notice calls on the named individual to discharge outstanding dues owed to SEBI arising from earlier adjudication or disgorgement orders.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9120 of 2026 has been issued against Aashish Kumar Goenka (PAN: BOCPG5241L).
- The notice relates to the Illiquid Stock Options matter, a major SEBI enforcement case involving manipulation of illiquid stock options contracts.
- A formal Notice of Demand has been served requiring payment of outstanding dues.
- Non-compliance with a recovery certificate notice can lead to attachment of assets and other coercive recovery measures under SEBI law.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an enforcement/recovery action under existing SEBI recovery and adjudication powers.

## Compliance Requirements

- Aashish Kumar Goenka is required to satisfy the outstanding demand specified in Recovery Certificate No. RC9120 of 2026 within the period stipulated in the notice.
- Failure to pay may result in further coercive recovery proceedings including attachment and sale of assets.

## Important Dates

- Notice issued: May 2026
- Payment deadline: As specified in the notice served on the named individual.

## Impact Assessment

This notice has no direct impact on broader markets or other market participants. It is a targeted enforcement action against a single individual (Aashish Kumar Goenka) for non-payment of dues in the Illiquid Stock Options matter. Market participants not involved in the Illiquid Stock Options case are unaffected.
