- Recovery Certificate No. 8445 of 2024 has been marked as completed
- Pertains to Vishnubhai Ishwarbhai Brahmabhatt (PAN: ANAPB1917A)
- Related to ISO (Initial Service Offering) matters at BSE
- Represents closure of enforcement proceedings

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an entity-specific enforcement completion order.

This is a completion order indicating the fulfillment of recovery obligations. No new compliance requirements are imposed on market participants.

## Important Dates

This completion order has minimal market-wide impact as it is specific to an individual case.
