## Summary

SEBI has issued a notice regarding the completion of Recovery Certificate No. 4720 of 2022, issued against Srinivas Rao Marupudi in the matter of Venus Power Ventures Ltd. The completion of this recovery certificate signifies the conclusion of recovery proceedings initiated against the defaulter in this matter.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 4720 of 2022 has been marked as completed.
- The certificate was issued against Srinivas Rao Marupudi as the named defaulter.
- The matter pertains to Venus Power Ventures Ltd.
- A related Recovery Certificate No. 4724 of 2022 was issued against the same defaulter in the same matter.
- Completion indicates that the recovery process against the defaulter under this certificate has concluded.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced by this notice. It is an administrative communication confirming the closure of an existing enforcement/recovery action.

## Compliance Requirements

This notice is specific to the named defaulter (Srinivas Rao Marupudi) and does not impose new compliance obligations on other market participants. The completion of the recovery certificate typically implies the outstanding dues have been recovered or the matter has otherwise been resolved per SEBI's recovery procedures.

## Important Dates

- **Recovery Certificate No.:** 4720 of 2022
- **Completion Notice Date:** June 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact of this circular is limited to the specific enforcement case against Srinivas Rao Marupudi in the matter of Venus Power Ventures Ltd. It has no direct bearing on broader market operations, trading, or other participants. It signals the conclusion of SEBI's recovery proceedings in this matter.
