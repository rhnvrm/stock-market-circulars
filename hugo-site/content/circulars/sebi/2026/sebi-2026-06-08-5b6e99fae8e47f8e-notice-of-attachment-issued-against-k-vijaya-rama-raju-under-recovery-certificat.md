## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Attachment against K. Vijaya Rama Raju under Recovery Certificate No. 4876 of 2022, in connection with proceedings in the matter of Ranklin Solutions Limited. The attachment is a recovery enforcement measure to collect dues ordered against the defaulter.

## Key Points

- Defaulter: K. Vijaya Rama Raju
- Recovery Certificate No.: 4876 of 2022
- Underlying matter: Ranklin Solutions Limited
- Action: Attachment of assets/accounts to enforce recovery of dues
- Issuing authority: SEBI (Securities and Exchange Board of India)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a targeted enforcement action under SEBI's existing recovery powers, exercised pursuant to Recovery Certificate No. 4876 of 2022.

## Compliance Requirements

- Banks, depositories, and other financial institutions holding assets or accounts of K. Vijaya Rama Raju are directed to comply with the attachment notice.
- No debits or withdrawals are to be permitted from attached accounts without SEBI's prior authorization.
- Institutions receiving this notice must acknowledge receipt and act in accordance with the directions issued therein.

## Important Dates

- Notice date: June 08, 2026
- Recovery Certificate No. 4876 of 2022 (originally issued in 2022) underpins this enforcement action.

## Impact Assessment

This notice has no broad market impact. It is a targeted enforcement action against an individual (K. Vijaya Rama Raju) connected to proceedings against Ranklin Solutions Limited. The attachment of accounts is a standard recovery mechanism employed by SEBI to collect dues from defaulters who have not complied with earlier orders. Market participants not involved in the Ranklin Solutions Limited matter are unaffected.
