## Summary

SEBI has issued a General Remittance Order dated April 20, 2026, under Recovery Certificate No. 9029 of 2026 against Rajnikant Nathalal Kananni (HUF). This order is part of ongoing recovery proceedings related to dealings in illiquid stock options at the Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE).

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 9029 of 2026 issued against Rajnikant Nathalal Kananni (HUF)
- Order date: April 20, 2026
- Case pertains to dealing in illiquid stock options at BSE
- Enforcement action under SEBI's recovery and restitution framework
- Applies to a Hindu Undivided Family (HUF) entity

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement and recovery action under existing SEBI regulations and the Securities and Exchange Board of India Act.

## Compliance Requirements

This order is specific to Rajnikant Nathalal Kananni (HUF) and pertains to recovery of dues arising from violations related to illiquid stock options trading at BSE. The entity is required to comply with the remittance directions specified in the order.

## Important Dates

- Order Date: April 20, 2026
- Recovery Certificate No.: 9029 of 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is an entity-specific enforcement action targeting Rajnikant Nathalal Kananni (HUF) for illiquid stock options violations at BSE. It has no direct market-wide implications and is part of SEBI's broader crackdown on manipulative trading in illiquid stock options. Impact is limited to the specific HUF entity named in the recovery proceedings.
