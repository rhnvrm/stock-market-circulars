## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order for Recovery Certificate No. RC9096 of 2026 in respect of Swift Dealmark Private Limited (PAN: AAPCS1110D) in the matter of illiquid stock options. The release order indicates that the entity has paid the outstanding dues or otherwise fulfilled the conditions required to discharge the recovery certificate.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9096 of 2026 issued against Swift Dealmark Private Limited (PAN: AAPCS1110D) stands released
- The matter pertains to illiquid stock options, a segment that was subject to widespread SEBI enforcement action for artificial/circular trading
- Release order signifies settlement or full payment of penalty/disgorgement amounts by the entity
- No further recovery proceedings remain outstanding under this specific certificate

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an administrative order concluding enforcement proceedings for a specific recovery certificate.

## Compliance Requirements

- No new compliance obligations arise for market participants from this order
- Swift Dealmark Private Limited has fulfilled its obligations under Recovery Certificate No. RC9096 of 2026

## Important Dates

- Date of Release Order: 2026-06-22
- Recovery Certificate: RC9096 of 2026

## Impact Assessment

This order has minimal market impact. It is an administrative closure of an enforcement matter against a single private entity (Swift Dealmark Private Limited) related to the illiquid stock options matter. No broader market-wide implications. The illiquid stock options enforcement series has seen numerous such release orders as penalised entities discharge their liabilities.
