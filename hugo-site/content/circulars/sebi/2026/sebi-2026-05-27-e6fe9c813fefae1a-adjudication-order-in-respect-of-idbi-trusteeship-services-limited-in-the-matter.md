## Summary

SEBI's Adjudicating Officer issued an adjudication order (Order No. Order/JS/VC/2026-27/32424) against IDBI Trusteeship Services Limited (ITSL), a registered Debenture Trustee (Registration No. IND000000460), for multiple violations discovered during a thematic inspection. The inspection covered the period July 31, 2022 to July 31, 2024 and examined ITSL's compliance with SEBI (Debenture Trustees) Regulations, 1993 and related circulars in the context of actions taken upon issuer defaults.

## Key Points

- ITSL was subjected to thematic inspection focused on actions taken by debenture trustees when issuers default.
- SEBI initiated adjudication proceedings under Section 15HB of the SEBI Act for violations of multiple regulations and circulars.
- A Show Cause Notice (Ref. No. DIS/1997-1998/2026) was issued on January 5, 2026.
- ITSL failed to issue timely notices to debenture holders upon default events and failed to maintain proof of delivery of notices sent.
- Violations span five distinct regulatory provisions including Regulation 15(1)(d), 15(1)(e), and 15(2)(b) of DT Regulations.
- Non-compliance observed with SEBI circulars dated October 13, 2020 and April 13, 2022, as well as Master Circulars for DTs dated July 06, 2023 and May 16, 2024.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This order enforces existing provisions under:
- SEBI (Debenture Trustees) Regulations, 1993 — Regulations 15(1)(d), 15(1)(e), and 15(2)(b).
- SEBI Circular No. SEBI/HO/MIRSD/CRADT/CIR/P/2020/203 dated October 13, 2020 — Clauses 6.1, 6.2, and 6.3.
- SEBI Circular No. SEBI/HO/DDHS/P/CIR/2021/613 dated April 13, 2022 — Clause 2.5 of Chapter XIV.
- Master Circular for Debenture Trustees dated July 06, 2023 and May 16, 2024.

## Compliance Requirements

- Debenture trustees must issue notices to debenture holders within prescribed timelines upon issuer default.
- Proof of delivery of all such notices must be maintained as per regulatory requirements.
- Trustees are required to take specific actions upon default as outlined in clauses 6.1, 6.2, and 6.3 of the October 13, 2020 circular.
- Trustees must comply with disclosure and reporting obligations under Chapter XII of the Master Circular and Chapter XIV of the April 13, 2022 circular.
- Registered debenture trustees should review their default-management processes and documentation practices to ensure full compliance with DT Regulations and applicable master circulars.

## Important Dates

- Inspection Period: July 31, 2022 to July 31, 2024.
- Adjudicating Officer appointed: December 12, 2025.
- Show Cause Notice issued: January 5, 2026.
- Order Number: Order/JS/VC/2026-27/32424.

## Impact Assessment

This order signals continued SEBI enforcement focus on debenture trustees' conduct during issuer defaults. IDBI Trusteeship Services Limited faces financial penalties under Section 15HB of the SEBI Act. The action underscores systemic gaps in ITSL's default-notification and documentation processes. Other registered debenture trustees should treat this as a compliance benchmark and audit their own default-response frameworks. The broader debenture market may see heightened regulatory scrutiny of trustee practices, which could improve investor protection for debenture holders in future default scenarios.
