## Summary

The Appellate Authority under the Right to Information Act, 2005 issued an order in Appeal No. 6895 of 2026 filed by Sumit Saurabh against the Central Public Information Officer (CPIO), SEBI, Mumbai. The appeal relates to an RTI application filed on April 02, 2026 seeking information about Shree Hanuman Sugar and Industries Ltd (SHSIL) and SMS Techsoft, specifically regarding compulsory delisting by NSE in August 2018 and investor complaints filed on the SCORES portal.

## Key Points

- Appellant Sumit Saurabh challenged SEBI's response to query nos. 10 and 12 of his RTI application dated April 02, 2026.
- Query 10 sought details of SEBI directives issued to promoters for an exit route following NSE's compulsory delisting/suspension of SHSIL in August 2018, and whether an Escrow Account was established for share buyback.
- Query 12 sought information on total investor complaints against SHSIL and SMS Techsoft on the SCORES portal since 2014, and whether SEBI has a plan to compensate investor losses using the Investor Protection Fund (IPF) or by liquidating promoter assets.
- SEBI's respondent informed for query 10 that the information is not maintained by SEBI in the normal course of regulation of the securities market, and directed the appellant to SEBI (Delisting of Equity Shares) Regulations 2021 (Chapter V) and the NSE website.
- For query 12, SEBI stated that the information sought is in the nature of seeking clarification/opinion and cannot be construed as "information" under Section 2(f) of the RTI Act; investor complaint data is also not maintained in the normal course of regulation.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The order interprets the scope of SEBI's information obligations under the RTI Act, 2005.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements for market participants. The order is specific to this RTI appeal and affects only the parties involved.

## Important Dates

- RTI Application filed: April 02, 2026
- SEBI Response: April 30, 2026
- Appeal filed: May 21, 2026 (Reg. No. SEBIH/A/E/26/00189)
- Order date: June 18, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This order has no direct market or operational impact. It is an administrative ruling on the scope of SEBI's disclosure obligations under the RTI Act concerning a specific delisted company (Shree Hanuman Sugar and Industries Ltd). Investors in delisted companies seeking similar information should note that SEBI may not maintain compulsory delisting directives or SCORES complaint counts as part of its normal regulatory records, and may need to approach the relevant stock exchange directly.
