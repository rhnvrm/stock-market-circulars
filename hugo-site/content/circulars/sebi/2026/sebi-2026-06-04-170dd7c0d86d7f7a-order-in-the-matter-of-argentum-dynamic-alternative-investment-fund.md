## Summary

SEBI has issued an enforcement order in the matter of Argentum Dynamic Alternative Investment Fund, addressing alleged violations of the SEBI (Alternative Investment Funds) Regulations, 2012. The order sets out findings by the adjudicating authority and issues directions and/or penalties against the fund.

## Key Points

- Order issued by SEBI against Argentum Dynamic Alternative Investment Fund.
- Pertains to alleged non-compliance with SEBI AIF Regulations, 2012.
- Directions or penalties have been imposed as part of the enforcement action.
- The fund is a category under SEBI's regulatory framework for pooled investment vehicles.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This order applies existing SEBI (Alternative Investment Funds) Regulations, 2012 provisions to enforce compliance.

## Compliance Requirements

- Argentum Dynamic Alternative Investment Fund is required to comply with all directions specified in the order.
- Other AIFs are advised to review their compliance with SEBI AIF Regulations to avoid similar enforcement action.

## Important Dates

- Order Date: June 4, 2026
- Compliance timelines as specified within the order document.

## Impact Assessment

The order primarily affects Argentum Dynamic Alternative Investment Fund and its investors. It signals SEBI's continued focus on enforcing compliance among AIFs. Broader market impact is limited, but the order serves as a regulatory precedent for the AIF industry.
