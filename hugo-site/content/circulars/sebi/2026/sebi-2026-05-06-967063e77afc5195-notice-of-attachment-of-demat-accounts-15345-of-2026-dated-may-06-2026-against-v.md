## Summary

SEBI has issued Notice of Attachment of Demat Accounts No. 15345 of 2026 dated May 06, 2026 against Vijay Prakash Gupta (PAN: ANCPV2725J), identified as a defaulter in the matter of investigation into trading activities of certain entities in the scrip of Continental Seeds and Chemicals Limited. The attachment is being executed under Recovery Certificate No. 8873 of 2025.

## Key Points

- **Notice Number:** 15345 of 2026
- **Date of Notice:** May 06, 2026
- **Defaulter:** Vijay Prakash Gupta (PAN: ANCPV2725J)
- **Recovery Certificate:** No. 8873 of 2025
- **Action:** Attachment of Demat Accounts
- **Matter:** Investigation into trading activities of certain entities in the scrip of Continental Seeds and Chemicals Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an enforcement/recovery action under existing SEBI (Recovery of Amounts) Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Depositories and depository participants holding demat accounts of Vijay Prakash Gupta (PAN: ANCPV2725J) are required to freeze/attach the specified demat accounts as directed.
- No transfers, withdrawals, or dealings are to be permitted in the attached demat accounts until further orders from SEBI.
- Relevant financial institutions must acknowledge and comply with the attachment notice promptly.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** May 06, 2026
- **Recovery Certificate Date:** 2025 (RC No. 8873 of 2025)

## Impact Assessment

This enforcement action directly restricts the securities holdings of the named defaulter, Vijay Prakash Gupta. The attachment of demat accounts prevents disposal or transfer of securities, aiding SEBI's recovery of dues. The broader market impact is limited, but the action underscores SEBI's continued enforcement against entities implicated in the Continental Seeds and Chemicals Limited trading investigation. Investors and market participants in this scrip should be aware of ongoing regulatory scrutiny.
