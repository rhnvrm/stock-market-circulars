## Summary

SEBI has issued a notice of attachment of bank accounts and demat accounts against Makwana Bharat S. HUF, designated as a defaulter in the matter of dealings in illiquid stock options on BSE. The attachment is executed under Recovery Certificate No. 9093 of 2026, with Attachment Proceedings (AP) Nos. 15366 and 15367 of 2026, dated May 18, 2026.

## Key Points

- Defaulter: Makwana Bharat S. HUF
- Recovery Certificate No.: 9093 of 2026
- Attachment Proceedings Nos.: 15366 & 15367 of 2026
- Date of notice: May 18, 2026
- Accounts attached: bank accounts and demat accounts
- Matter: dealing in illiquid stock options on BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI recovery and attachment provisions.

## Compliance Requirements

Banks and depository participants holding accounts of Makwana Bharat S. HUF are directed to freeze/attach the specified accounts and not release any funds or securities without SEBI's prior approval.

## Important Dates

- Notice date: May 18, 2026
- Recovery Certificate No. 9093 of 2026 (date of issuance not specified in title)

## Impact Assessment

This is a targeted enforcement action against a single entity (Makwana Bharat S. HUF) and has no broader market-wide impact. The attachment restricts the defaulter's ability to access bank and demat account assets until SEBI's recovery proceedings are concluded. General market participants and investors are unaffected.
