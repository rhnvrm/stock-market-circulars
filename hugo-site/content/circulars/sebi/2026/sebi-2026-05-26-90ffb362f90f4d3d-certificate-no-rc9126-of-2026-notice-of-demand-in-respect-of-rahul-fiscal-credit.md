## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand under Recovery Certificate No. RC9126 of 2026 against Rahul Fiscal & Credit Co. Pvt. Ltd. (PAN: AACCR4625F), demanding recovery of outstanding dues arising from the matter of fraudulent trading in illiquid stock options. This is a formal enforcement step by SEBI's Recovery Officer directing the defaulter to pay the specified amount within the stipulated period.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9126 of 2026 issued against Rahul Fiscal & Credit Co. Pvt. Ltd.
- PAN of the defaulter: AACCR4625F
- Relates to SEBI's enforcement action in the matter of illiquid stock options traded on stock exchanges
- Notice demands payment of dues within a specified deadline
- Non-compliance may lead to attachment and sale of assets under the SEBI Act

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement/recovery action under existing SEBI (Recovery of Money) Rules, 2014 and relevant provisions of the SEBI Act, 1992.

## Compliance Requirements

- Rahul Fiscal & Credit Co. Pvt. Ltd. is required to pay the demanded amount as specified in Recovery Certificate No. RC9126 of 2026 within the time period stated in the notice
- Failure to comply may result in coercive recovery measures including attachment of bank accounts, properties, or other assets

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate date: 2026 (specific date as per the certificate)
- Notice of Demand issuance: May 2026
- Payment deadline: As specified in the notice

## Impact Assessment

This notice has limited broader market impact. It is a targeted enforcement action against a single entity (Rahul Fiscal & Credit Co. Pvt. Ltd.) as part of SEBI's ongoing crackdown on entities involved in fraudulent illiquid stock options trading. It reinforces SEBI's continued use of recovery proceedings to enforce penalties and disgorgement orders arising from the illiquid stock options matter, which involved widespread manipulation on BSE.
