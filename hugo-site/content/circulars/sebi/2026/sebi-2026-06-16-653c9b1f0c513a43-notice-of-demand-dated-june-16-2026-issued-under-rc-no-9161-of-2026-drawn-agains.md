## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand dated June 16, 2026 under Recovery Certificate (RC) No. 9161 of 2026 against Rupee Services Private Limited. The notice pertains to the entity's involvement in dealing in illiquid stock options at BSE, a segment that SEBI has been actively monitoring for manipulative trading practices.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 9161 of 2026 issued against Rupee Services Private Limited
- Notice of Demand dated June 16, 2026
- Matter relates to dealing in illiquid stock options at BSE
- SEBI is seeking recovery of dues/penalties previously ordered
- Part of SEBI's broader enforcement drive against manipulation in BSE's illiquid stock options segment

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement/recovery action under existing SEBI recovery mechanisms.

## Compliance Requirements

- Rupee Services Private Limited is required to pay the amounts specified in the Recovery Certificate
- Failure to comply with the Notice of Demand may result in further recovery proceedings including attachment of assets
- The entity must respond to or comply with the demand within the timeframe specified in the notice

## Important Dates

- Notice of Demand Date: June 16, 2026
- Recovery Certificate No. 9161 of 2026

## Impact Assessment

This notice directly impacts Rupee Services Private Limited, which is required to satisfy the outstanding dues under the recovery certificate. The broader market impact is limited, as this is an individual enforcement action. However, it reinforces SEBI's continued crackdown on entities involved in the illiquid stock options segment at BSE, which has been a focus area for market surveillance and enforcement. Market participants engaged in options trading on BSE should note SEBI's active pursuit of recovery proceedings in this segment.
