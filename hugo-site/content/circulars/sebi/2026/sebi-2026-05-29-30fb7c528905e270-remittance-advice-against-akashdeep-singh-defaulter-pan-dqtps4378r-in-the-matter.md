## Summary

SEBI has issued a Remittance Advice against Akashdeep Singh, identified as a defaulter with PAN DQTPS4378R, in connection with the matter of ISO at BSE. The remittance advice is issued under Recovery Certificate No. 8072 of 2024, directing recovery of dues from the defaulter.

## Key Points

- Defaulter: Akashdeep Singh
- PAN: DQTPS4378R
- Matter: ISO at BSE
- Recovery Certificate No.: 8072 of 2024
- Document Type: Remittance Advice (enforcement/recovery action)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is an enforcement document directing remittance/recovery of dues owed by a designated defaulter.

## Compliance Requirements

The designated defaulter, Akashdeep Singh (PAN: DQTPS4378R), is required to remit the outstanding dues as specified under Recovery Certificate No. 8072 of 2024. Banks, depositories, and other intermediaries named in the remittance advice are expected to comply with the directions for attachment or remittance of funds.

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate No.: 8072 of 2024 (issued in 2024)
- Remittance Advice Date: May 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is an individual enforcement action with limited broader market impact. It reflects SEBI's ongoing recovery proceedings against defaulters in securities market matters. No systemic or sector-wide implications are anticipated.
