## Summary

SEBI has issued a Remittance Advice against Mr. Prem Chand (PAN: AXNPC3713G) as a defaulter in the matter of Delta Leasing & Finance Ltd. under Recovery Certificate No. 7418 of 2023. This document confirms remittance of recovered dues to SEBI pursuant to enforcement proceedings.

## Key Points

- Defaulter: Mr. Prem Chand (PAN: AXNPC3713G)
- Matter: Delta Leasing & Finance Ltd.
- Recovery Certificate No.: 7418 of 2023
- Document Type: Remittance Advice (confirmation of amount remitted under recovery proceedings)
- Issuing Authority: Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an enforcement/recovery action under existing SEBI recovery mechanisms.

## Compliance Requirements

This remittance advice is directed at the defaulter and relevant parties involved in the recovery proceedings. No new compliance obligations are imposed on the broader market or other participants.

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate No. 7418 issued in 2023
- Remittance Advice dated: 2026-06-24

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine enforcement document with no broader market impact. It pertains solely to the recovery of dues from an individual defaulter (Mr. Prem Chand) in connection with the matter of Delta Leasing & Finance Ltd. Market participants and listed entities are not affected.
