## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. 8996 of 2026 dated January 02, 2026 against Steady Capital Advisory Services Private Limited (PAN: AATCS2130B) as a defaulter in the matter of Jolly Plastic Industries Ltd. This notice of demand is part of SEBI's enforcement and recovery proceedings.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate Number: RC 8996 of 2026
- Date of Issue: January 02, 2026
- Defaulter: Steady Capital Advisory Services Private Limited
- PAN of Defaulter: AATCS2130B
- Related Matter: Jolly Plastic Industries Ltd.
- Action Type: Notice of Demand for recovery

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI recovery provisions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Steady Capital Advisory Services Private Limited is required to comply with the recovery demand issued by SEBI
- Failure to comply may result in further enforcement actions including asset attachment and other recovery proceedings
- The entity must settle outstanding dues as determined by SEBI in relation to the Jolly Plastic Industries Ltd. matter

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate Issue Date: January 02, 2026
