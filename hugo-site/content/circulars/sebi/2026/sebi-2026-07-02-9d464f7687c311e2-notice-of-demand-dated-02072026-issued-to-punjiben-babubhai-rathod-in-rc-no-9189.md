## Summary

SEBI's Recovery and Refund Department has issued Certificate No. 9189 of 2026 and an accompanying Notice of Demand against Punjiben Babubhai Rathod (PAN: CABPR8593L), directing recovery of Rs. 6,43,000/- comprising a penalty of Rs. 6,00,000/-, interest of Rs. 42,000/-, and recovery cost of Rs. 1,000/-. The dues arise from a penalty order (QJA/MN/IVD/ID7/31947/2025-26 dated 31.12.2025) passed in connection with trading activities of certain entities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited). The notice is issued under Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992 read with Section 222 of the Income Tax Act, 1961.

## Key Points

- Total demand of Rs. 6,43,000/- payable within 15 days of receipt of the notice.
- Amount includes principal penalty (Rs. 6,00,000), interest at 1% per month from January 2026 to July 2026 (Rs. 42,000), and recovery cost (Rs. 1,000).
- Payment can be made via EFT/NEFT/RTGS to a designated ICICI Bank account or through SEBI's online "Recovery Payment" portal.
- Non-payment will trigger recovery under Sections 220 to 232 of the Income Tax Act, 1961 and the Second Schedule thereto.
- Recovery mechanisms available to SEBI include attachment/sale of movable and immovable property, bank account attachment, arrest and detention, and appointment of a receiver.
- Any transfer of property or funds to spouse, minor child, son's wife, or son's minor child without adequate consideration on or after 31.12.2025 is deemed the defaultor's property for recovery purposes.
- The individual is barred from mortgaging, charging, leasing, or otherwise dealing with property without the Recovery Officer's permission; violating transfers are void.

## Regulatory Changes

None; this is an individual enforcement/recovery notice, not a regulatory or policy change.

## Compliance Requirements

Punjiben Babubhai Rathod must pay the total outstanding amount of Rs. 6,43,000/- within 15 days of receiving the notice and forward confirmation of e-payment to the Recovery Officer at SEBI.

## Important Dates

- Penalty order dated: 31.12.2025
- Notice of demand dated: 02.07.2026
- Interest accrual period: January 2026 to July 2026
- Payment deadline: within 15 days of receipt of the notice

## Impact Assessment

This notice has no bearing on listed companies, market operations, or broader compliance requirements for market participants. Its impact is confined to the named individual, who faces recovery action including possible attachment of assets and restrictions on property dealings if the dues remain unpaid.
