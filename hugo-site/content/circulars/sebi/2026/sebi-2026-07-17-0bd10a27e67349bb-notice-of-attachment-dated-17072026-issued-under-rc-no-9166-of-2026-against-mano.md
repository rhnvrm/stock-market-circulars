## Summary

SEBI's Recovery Officer has issued a Notice of Attachment dated 17.07.2026, issued under Recovery Certificate (RC) No. 9166 of 2026, against Mr. Manoj Rameshbhai Solanki (PAN: GEMPS6325N). The proceeding arises out of a matter concerning the trading activities of certain entities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now renamed DU Digital Global Limited). The notice directs attachment of the defaulter's assets/bank accounts to recover outstanding dues confirmed under the recovery certificate.

## Key Points

- Notice of Attachment dated 17.07.2026 issued against Manoj Rameshbhai Solanki (PAN: GEMPS6325N).
- Issued under Recovery Certificate No. 9166 of 2026, indicating a prior SEBI order/demand that remained unpaid, prompting recovery proceedings.
- The underlying matter relates to trading activities of certain entities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited, now known as DU Digital Global Limited following a name change.
- Recovery proceedings are typically initiated under Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992, read with the Second and Third Schedules to the Income Tax Act, 1961, and the Income Tax (Certificate Proceedings) Rules, 1962, as applicable to SEBI recovery actions.
- Attachment notices are a coercive recovery mechanism used when a noticee fails to pay penalties, disgorgement amounts, or other dues confirmed by SEBI or appellate authorities within the stipulated period.

## Regulatory Changes

This is an individual enforcement/recovery action and does not introduce any new regulatory rule, framework, or market-wide policy change. It is a procedural step in SEBI's recovery mechanism against a specific defaulter.

## Compliance Requirements

- The noticee, Manoj Rameshbhai Solanki, is required to clear the outstanding dues under RC No. 9166 of 2026 to avoid further coercive recovery action, including sale of attached assets.
- Banks, depositories, and other intermediaries holding assets or accounts in the name of the noticee may be required to comply with the attachment directive and refrain from permitting withdrawal, transfer, or disposal of the attached property without SEBI's Recovery Officer's consent.
- No direct compliance obligation arises for other listed entities or market participants from this notice.

## Important Dates

- Notice of Attachment date: 17.07.2026
- Recovery Certificate reference: RC No. 9166 of 2026

## Impact Assessment

The notice has limited market-wide impact as it pertains to an individual recovery action tied to a specific past SEBI order concerning trading activity in one scrip (DU Digital Technologies Limited, now DU Digital Global Limited). It does not affect the listed status, trading, or corporate actions of the company itself. Its significance lies primarily in SEBI's continued enforcement of recovery against individuals found liable in market misconduct matters, reinforcing regulatory deterrence.
