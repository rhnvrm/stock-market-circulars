## Summary

SEBI has issued a notice of attachment against the bank accounts and demat accounts of Anagha C. Bambardekar (PAN: ANCPB5855B), who has been declared a defaulter in a matter concerning dealings in illiquid stock options on the BSE. The attachment has been carried out under AP Nos. 15522 and 15523 of 2026, in pursuance of Recovery Certificate No. 9148 of 2026, drawn to recover outstanding dues/penalty amounts owed to SEBI.

## Key Points

- Defaulter identified: Anagha C. Bambardekar (PAN: ANCPB5855B)
- Underlying matter: Dealings in Illiquid Stock Options on BSE
- Recovery Certificate No. 9148 of 2026 forms the basis for recovery proceedings
- Attachment Proceeding (AP) Nos. 15522 and 15523 of 2026 issued dated July 07, 2026
- Action covers both bank accounts and demat accounts held by the defaulter

## Regulatory Changes

This is not a policy or regulatory change; it is an enforcement action taken under SEBI's recovery mechanism (akin to provisions under the Income Tax Act's Second Schedule, as applied by SEBI recovery officers) to recover dues arising from a confirmed default.

## Compliance Requirements

- The defaulter's bank(s) and depository participant(s) are required to freeze/restrict operations on the attached accounts and comply with the attachment order.
- No further action is required from listed companies or other market participants; this notice is specific to the named defaulter and their bankers/depositories.

## Important Dates

- Notice date: July 07, 2026
- AP Nos.: 15522 & 15523 of 2026
- Recovery Certificate No.: 9148 of 2026

## Impact Assessment

The impact is confined to the individual defaulter, whose bank and demat accounts are now attached, restricting access to funds and securities held therein. There is no direct impact on listed securities, market operations, or other investors, as this is a targeted recovery action arising from a prior enforcement order related to illiquid stock options trading on BSE.
