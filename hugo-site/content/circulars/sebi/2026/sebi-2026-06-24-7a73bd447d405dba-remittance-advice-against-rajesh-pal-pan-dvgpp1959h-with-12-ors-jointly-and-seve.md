## Summary

SEBI has issued a Remittance Advice against Rajesh Pal (PAN: DVGPP1959H) along with 12 other persons, jointly and severally, in the matter of Continental Seeds and Chemicals Limited. The notice is issued under Recovery Certificate No. 8858 of 2025, directing the named parties to remit outstanding dues to SEBI.

## Key Points

- Remittance advice issued against Rajesh Pal (PAN: DVGPP1959H) and 12 other persons (13 parties total)
- All named parties are jointly and severally liable for the recovery amount
- Action relates to the matter of Continental Seeds and Chemicals Limited
- Issued under Recovery Certificate No. 8858 of 2025
- SEBI is exercising its recovery powers under the SEBI Act/SEBI (Recovery of Amounts) Rules

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is an enforcement action pursuant to an existing SEBI order and recovery certificate against named individuals.

## Compliance Requirements

- The named parties — Rajesh Pal (PAN: DVGPP1959H) and the 12 other co-defaulters — are required to remit the outstanding amount as specified in Recovery Certificate No. 8858 of 2025
- Payment must be made to SEBI as directed in the remittance advice
- Failure to comply may result in further enforcement actions including attachment of assets

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate No.: 8858 of 2025
- Remittance Advice issued: June 2026
- Specific payment deadline: As specified in the recovery certificate and remittance advice document

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market-wide impact. It is an enforcement and recovery action targeting specific individuals in connection with the matter of Continental Seeds and Chemicals Limited. The action signals SEBI's continued enforcement of recovery certificates issued against defaulters. Broader market participants are not affected.
