## Summary

This is an order by the Appellate Authority under the Right to Information Act, 2005 (SEBI) on Appeal No. 6894 of 2026 filed by appellant Poonam against the CPIO, SEBI, Mumbai. The appellant originally filed an RTI application on April 22, 2026, received a response on May 19, 2026, and subsequently filed this appeal on May 23, 2026 (Reg. No. SEBIH/A/E/26/00193).

## Key Points

- Appellant sought 14 categories of information regarding SEBI's internal procedures, departmental responsibilities, and official provisions
- Queries relate to SEBI officials' authority to handle investor applications, departmental assignments for market intermediary supervision, and CPGRAMS grievance handling
- Specific information sought on Benpos (Beneficiary Position) photocopies and Excel files related to Bajaj Hindustan shares requested via CPGRAMS references DOEAF/E/2025/0001195, DOEAF/E/2026/0000215, and DOEAF/E/2026/0000415
- Appellant questioned which SEBI officials and departments handle registration, supervision, compliance monitoring, and inspection of market intermediaries including stock brokers and depository participants
- Information sought on officials Mr. Surendra Gangavarapu, Mr. Krishnanad Raghawan, Mr. Vijayakant K. Verma, and Mr. Tunhin Kanta Pandey
- Queries about integration of NSE, BSE, NSDL, CDSL into the RTI online portal

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a procedural RTI appellate order concerning access to information about SEBI's internal workings.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements for market participants. The order pertains solely to SEBI's obligation to respond to RTI queries under the Right to Information Act, 2005.

## Important Dates

- RTI Application filed: April 22, 2026
- CPIO Response: May 19, 2026
- Appeal filed: May 23, 2026 (Reg. No. SEBIH/A/E/26/00193)

## Impact Assessment

This order has no direct market impact. It is an individual RTI appeal concerning procedural and administrative information about SEBI's internal departments. The only specific stock reference is Bajaj Hindustan (BAJAJHIND) in the context of the appellant's request for Benpos records, with no regulatory action indicated against the company.
