## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand dated June 29, 2026 under Recovery Certificate (RC) No. 9182 of 2026 against Ankit Ajitbhai Panchal. The demand arises from trading activities conducted by certain entities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited, now known as DU Digital Global Limited. This notice is issued under SEBI's recovery proceedings to enforce payment of dues ordered through prior regulatory action.

## Key Points

- Notice of Demand issued under RC No. 9182 of 2026
- Demand issued against Ankit Ajitbhai Panchal
- Related to trading activities of certain entities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited)
- Issued by SEBI as part of recovery/enforcement proceedings
- Dated June 29, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This notice represents enforcement of an existing SEBI order through the issuance of a Recovery Certificate and corresponding demand notice under the SEBI Act and/or SEBI (Recovery of Amounts) Rules.

## Compliance Requirements

- The named individual (Ankit Ajitbhai Panchal) is required to comply with the demand and pay the amount specified in the Recovery Certificate No. 9182 of 2026 within the stipulated time frame
- Non-compliance may result in further enforcement actions including attachment of bank accounts, demat accounts, or other assets
- The noticee should respond to SEBI's demand as per the directions contained in the notice

## Important Dates

- **Date of Notice:** June 29, 2026
- **RC No.:** 9182 of 2026
- Payment/compliance deadline: As specified in the demand notice (refer to original PDF for exact date)

## Impact Assessment

The market impact of this notice is limited to the individual named (Ankit Ajitbhai Panchal) and potentially other associated entities involved in the trading activity in DU Digital Global Limited. The issuance of a Recovery Certificate indicates that a prior SEBI order imposing a penalty or disgorgement has not been complied with, and SEBI is now pursuing formal recovery. Investors in DU Digital Global Limited should be aware of ongoing regulatory scrutiny related to the company's scrip. There is no direct operational impact on the company itself from this demand notice.
