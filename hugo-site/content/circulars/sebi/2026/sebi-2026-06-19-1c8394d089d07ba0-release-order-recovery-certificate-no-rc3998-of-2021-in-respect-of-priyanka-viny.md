## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order in respect of Recovery Certificate No. RC3998 of 2021, which was issued against Priyanka Vinyas Trust (PAN: AABTP1562B) in the matter of Unisys Software and Holding Industries Limited. A release order typically signifies that the recovery proceedings have been concluded, either due to payment of dues or withdrawal of the certificate by SEBI.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC3998 of 2021 is the subject of this release order.
- The defaulter entity is Priyanka Vinyas Trust with PAN AABTP1562B.
- The underlying enforcement action relates to Unisys Software and Holding Industries Limited.
- A release order indicates closure or partial resolution of the outstanding recovery action.

## Regulatory Changes

No broader regulatory changes are introduced. This is an entity-specific enforcement order issued by SEBI under its recovery and enforcement powers.

## Compliance Requirements

- The concerned entity (Priyanka Vinyas Trust) and any attached/encumbered assets referenced in RC3998 of 2021 are subject to the terms of this release order.
- Relevant authorities (e.g., banks, registrars) that were previously notified of the recovery certificate may need to act on the release order as directed.

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate originally issued: 2021 (RC3998 of 2021)
- Release Order date: June 19, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This order has minimal broader market impact. It is an administrative/enforcement action confined to Priyanka Vinyas Trust and its obligations arising from the Unisys Software and Holding Industries Limited matter. Market participants and investors in general are not directly affected. Entities previously notified under RC3998 of 2021 should update their records in accordance with this release.
