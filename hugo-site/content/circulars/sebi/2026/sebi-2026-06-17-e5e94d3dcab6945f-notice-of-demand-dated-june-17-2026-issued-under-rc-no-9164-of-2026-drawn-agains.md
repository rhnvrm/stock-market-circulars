## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand dated June 17, 2026 under Recovery Certificate (RC) No. 9164 of 2026 against Adhunik Ventures Private Limited (PAN: AAHCA7165L) in connection with violations related to trading in illiquid stock options. This notice directs the entity to pay the outstanding dues as determined by SEBI.

## Key Points

- Notice of Demand issued under Recovery Certificate No. 9164 of 2026
- Directed against Adhunik Ventures Private Limited (PAN: AAHCA7165L)
- Matter pertains to illiquid stock options trading violations
- Notice dated June 17, 2026
- Issued by SEBI's enforcement/recovery division

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement/recovery action under existing SEBI regulations governing illiquid stock options trading.

## Compliance Requirements

- Adhunik Ventures Private Limited is required to pay the amount specified in the Recovery Certificate No. 9164 of 2026
- Failure to comply with the Notice of Demand may result in further recovery proceedings by SEBI
- The entity must respond to the notice within the stipulated timeframe as mentioned in the demand notice

## Important Dates

- Notice of Demand date: June 17, 2026
- Recovery Certificate No. 9164 of 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a company-specific enforcement action with no direct broad market impact. It reflects SEBI's continued enforcement efforts against entities involved in non-genuine or manipulative trading in the illiquid stock options segment. Adhunik Ventures Private Limited is a private limited company and not a publicly listed entity, limiting any secondary market impact.
