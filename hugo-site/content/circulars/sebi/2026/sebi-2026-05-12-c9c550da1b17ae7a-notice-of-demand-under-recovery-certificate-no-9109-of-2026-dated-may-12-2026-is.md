## Summary

SEBI issued a Notice of Demand under Recovery Certificate No. 9109 of 2026 dated May 12, 2026, against Suryaraj Kumar (PAN: AAFPK9296D) in the matter of Mediaone Global Entertainment Limited. This notice requires the recovery of dues as specified in the recovery certificate from the named individual.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 9109 of 2026 was issued on May 12, 2026
- Notice is directed against Suryaraj Kumar (PAN: AAFPK9296D)
- The matter pertains to Mediaone Global Entertainment Limited
- SEBI is exercising its statutory recovery powers to enforce collection of dues
- The notice is part of SEBI's enforcement mechanism to recover penalties/dues from defaulters

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI recovery provisions, utilizing the statutory authority to issue Recovery Certificates and Notices of Demand against individuals who have not complied with SEBI orders.

## Compliance Requirements

- Suryaraj Kumar (PAN: AAFPK9296D) is required to pay the dues specified under Recovery Certificate No. 9109 of 2026
- Failure to comply with the Notice of Demand may result in further enforcement actions including attachment of assets
- The concerned individual must respond to the notice within the stipulated time frame as specified in the demand notice

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate issued: May 12, 2026
- Notice of Demand issued: May 12, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This enforcement action has minimal broader market impact as it is directed against a specific individual (Suryaraj Kumar) in relation to the Mediaone Global Entertainment Limited matter. The action demonstrates SEBI's continued enforcement of recovery proceedings against individuals who have not complied with regulatory orders. Market participants in the Mediaone Global Entertainment Limited scrip should note this as part of ongoing regulatory scrutiny of the company and its associated persons.
