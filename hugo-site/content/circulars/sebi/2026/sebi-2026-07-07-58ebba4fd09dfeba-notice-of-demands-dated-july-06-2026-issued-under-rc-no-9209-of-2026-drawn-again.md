## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand dated July 06, 2026, drawn under Recovery Certificate (RC) No. 9209 of 2026, against Pranav Kamleshkumar Trivedi. The notice arises from SEBI's investigation into trading activities of certain entities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now renamed DU Digital Global Limited). This action forms part of SEBI's recovery proceedings against individuals found liable for dues arising from the investigation.

## Key Points

- The notice of demand is dated July 06, 2026 and is issued against Pranav Kamleshkumar Trivedi.
- It is drawn under Recovery Certificate No. 9209 of 2026, indicating an underlying recovery proceeding for outstanding dues or penalties.
- The matter relates to trading activities of certain entities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited, now known as DU Digital Global Limited.
- This is part of SEBI's broader enforcement action against multiple individuals/entities connected to the same investigation, evidenced by a series of similarly numbered RC notices issued around the same date.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced by this notice. It is an enforcement/recovery action under SEBI's existing powers to recover dues, penalties, or disgorgement amounts determined in prior proceedings related to the DU Digital Technologies trading investigation.

## Compliance Requirements

- The noticee, Pranav Kamleshkumar Trivedi, is required to respond to or satisfy the demand raised under RC No. 9209 of 2026 within the timeframe specified in the notice.
- Failure to comply may result in further recovery proceedings, including attachment of assets or bank accounts, as per SEBI's recovery mechanisms under the SEBI Act and applicable recovery rules.

## Important Dates

- Notice date: July 06, 2026
- Publication/announcement date: July 07, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This notice has limited direct impact on the broader securities market, as it targets a specific individual in connection with a past investigation into DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited). It does not affect trading in the scrip itself or impose any new market-wide obligations. However, it underscores SEBI's continued enforcement of recovery actions against individuals linked to market manipulation or irregular trading investigations, reinforcing regulatory deterrence.
