## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand under Recovery Certificate No. 9137 of 2026 dated June 5, 2026 to Mediaone Global Entertainment Ltd (PAN: AAACR0405M) designating the entity as a defaulter in the matter of Mediaone Global Entertainment Limited. This notice is part of SEBI's enforcement and recovery mechanism to collect outstanding dues, penalties, or disgorgement amounts from entities that have failed to comply with prior SEBI orders.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 9137 of 2026 issued on June 5, 2026
- Defaulter: Mediaone Global Entertainment Ltd (PAN: AAACR0405M)
- Notice issued under SEBI's statutory recovery powers
- The entity is formally designated as a 'Defaulter' in this matter
- SEBI may proceed with coercive recovery measures if dues are not paid

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI recovery provisions, wherein SEBI acts as a certificate holder and can direct recovery of outstanding amounts as arrears of land revenue.

## Compliance Requirements

- Mediaone Global Entertainment Ltd is required to pay the outstanding dues specified in the Recovery Certificate
- Failure to comply with the Notice of Demand may result in further coercive recovery actions including attachment of assets
- The defaulter should respond to the notice within the stipulated timeframe indicated in the demand notice

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate Date: June 5, 2026
- Notice of Demand issued: June 5, 2026 (or shortly thereafter)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal broader market impact as it is directed at a specific entity, Mediaone Global Entertainment Ltd, for recovery of dues owed to SEBI. The action signals SEBI's continued enforcement of its orders and use of statutory recovery mechanisms. Investors in Mediaone Global Entertainment Limited should be aware that the company is subject to active enforcement proceedings by SEBI, which could affect the company's financial position and regulatory standing.
