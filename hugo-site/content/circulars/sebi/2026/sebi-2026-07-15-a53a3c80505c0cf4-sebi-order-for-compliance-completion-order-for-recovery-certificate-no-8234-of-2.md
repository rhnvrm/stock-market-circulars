## Summary

SEBI has passed a completion order closing recovery proceedings initiated under Recovery Certificate No. 8234 of 2024 against Rocket Tips, a proprietorship of Mehul Pravinbhai Jiyani (PAN: AGPPJ1611H). The underlying matter relates to the entity providing unregistered investment advisory services in violation of SEBI's regulatory framework governing investment advisers.

## Key Points

- The order relates to Recovery Certificate No. 8234 of 2024 issued against Rocket Tips, Prop. Mehul Pravinbhai Jiyani.
- The entity was found to have engaged in unregistered investment advisory services, i.e., providing investment advice without obtaining registration as an Investment Adviser under SEBI regulations.
- This order marks the completion/disposal of recovery proceedings that were pursued under the recovery certificate mechanism, which allows SEBI's Recovery Officer to recover monetary dues (such as penalties or disgorgement amounts) using procedures analogous to those under the Income Tax Act.
- The order is administrative/enforcement in nature and does not pertain to any listed company or traded security.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced by this order. It is a case-specific enforcement action closing out recovery proceedings against a previously identified unregistered investment advisory operator.

## Compliance Requirements

- Entities and individuals offering investment advice to the public must be registered with SEBI as Investment Advisers under the SEBI (Investment Advisers) Regulations, 2013.
- This order serves as a reminder that SEBI actively pursues recovery of dues from defaulting/unregistered entities and pursues such proceedings to completion.

## Important Dates

- Order date: July 15, 2026 (as per publication)
- Underlying Recovery Certificate: No. 8234 of 2024

## Impact Assessment

The direct impact is limited to the named individual/proprietorship and does not affect listed securities or broader market operations. It is primarily relevant for tracking SEBI's ongoing enforcement activity against unregistered investment advisers and reinforces the regulatory message around investor protection and the risks of engaging unregistered advisory services.
