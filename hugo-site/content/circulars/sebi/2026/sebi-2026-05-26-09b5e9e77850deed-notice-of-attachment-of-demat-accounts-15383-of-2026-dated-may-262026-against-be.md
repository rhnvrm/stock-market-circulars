## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Attachment of Demat Accounts (Case No. 15383 of 2026, dated May 26, 2026) against Best Healthcare Private Limited (PAN: AADCB1811A) as a defaulter in the matter of Fortis Healthcare Limited. This action is taken under Recovery Certificate No. 8725 of 2025, directing the attachment of the defaulter's demat accounts to recover dues.

## Key Points

- Notice of attachment issued against Best Healthcare Private Limited (PAN: AADCB1811A)
- Case No. 15383 of 2026, dated May 26, 2026
- Action taken under Recovery Certificate No. 8725 of 2025
- Relates to the matter of Fortis Healthcare Limited
- Demat accounts of the defaulter are subject to attachment

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement/recovery action under existing SEBI recovery mechanisms.

## Compliance Requirements

- Depositories and depository participants are required to freeze and attach the demat accounts of Best Healthcare Private Limited as directed
- No securities shall be transferred, pledged, or otherwise dealt with in the attached demat accounts without SEBI's prior permission
- Relevant parties must comply with the attachment order immediately upon receipt

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: May 26, 2026
- Recovery Certificate No.: 8725 of 2025

## Impact Assessment

This enforcement action directly impacts Best Healthcare Private Limited, whose demat accounts are subject to attachment. The action is limited in broader market impact but signals continued SEBI enforcement in the Fortis Healthcare matter. Holders or counterparties of the defaulter's securities should note the restriction on dealing in the attached accounts.
