## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC9096 of 2026 under A.P. No. 15406 of 2026 against Swift Dealmark Private Limited (PAN: AAPCS1110D) in the matter of Illiquid Stock Options. The certificate enables SEBI to recover outstanding dues from the entity as determined in the adjudication proceedings.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9096 of 2026 issued against Swift Dealmark Private Limited (PAN: AAPCS1110D)
- Pertains to Adjudication Proceedings No. 15406 of 2026
- Relates to the Illiquid Stock Options matter, in which SEBI found entities engaged in manipulative and fraudulent trading to generate fictitious profits or losses
- Certificate authorises recovery of the adjudicated penalty/disgorgement amount from the entity
- Recovery Certificates are a statutory mechanism under the SEBI Act enabling enforcement of unpaid orders

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This certificate is an enforcement instrument issued pursuant to an earlier adjudication order under the SEBI Act, 1992 and SEBI (Intermediaries) Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Swift Dealmark Private Limited is required to pay the dues specified in the Recovery Certificate immediately
- Failure to comply may result in further coercive recovery action by SEBI, including attachment of assets
- Other entities against whom similar RC certificates have been issued in this matter should ensure prompt payment to avoid enforcement proceedings

## Important Dates

- Certificate Date: 4 June 2026
- Payment of dues is expected promptly upon receipt of the certificate

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to the specific entity — Swift Dealmark Private Limited. This is part of a broader SEBI enforcement wave in the Illiquid Stock Options matter, where numerous entities were found to have created artificial volume and fictitious profits/losses in the BSE F&O segment. Individual recovery certificates of this nature do not affect market operations or other participants.
