## Summary

The Appellate Authority under the Right to Information Act, 2005 disposed of Appeal No. 6720 of 2026 filed by Amisha Baghel against CPIO, SEBI, Mumbai. The appellant had sought her Phase 2 marks (Paper 1 and Paper 2) for the SEBI Officer Grade A Recruitment 2024. The respondent directed the appellant to the SEBI website's careers section where the information is publicly available. The Appellate Authority found no deficiency in the respondent's response and dismissed the appeal.

## Key Points

- Appellant Amisha Baghel (Registration No. 47603xxxxx, Roll No. 129100xxx) filed an RTI application on January 11, 2026 seeking Phase 2 marks for SEBI Officer Grade A Recruitment 2024
- The CPIO responded on February 03, 2026, informing that the information is available on the SEBI website under the 'Careers' section along with a direct link
- The appellant filed an appeal (Reg. No. SEBIH/A/E/26/00041) on February 03, 2026, claiming incomplete, misleading, or false information was provided
- The appellant contended that the web link provided by the respondent was not opening
- The Appellate Authority verified that the web link was correct and operational
- The Appellate Authority relied on the Central Information Commission's decision dated December 22, 2022 (Kul Bhushan Jain vs. CPIO, SEBI), affirming that information available on a website is in the public domain and no further action is warranted
