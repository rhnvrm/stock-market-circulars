## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order for Recovery Certificate No. RC7647 of 2024 in respect of Saurabh Family Trust (PAN: ABFFS3296Q) in the enforcement matter concerning JMD Telefilms Industries Ltd. A release order typically indicates that the dues underlying the recovery certificate have been satisfied or that the certificate is being withdrawn for legal or procedural reasons.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC7647 of 2024 has been released by SEBI
- The certificate was issued against Saurabh Family Trust (PAN: ABFFS3296Q)
- The matter pertains to enforcement proceedings related to JMD Telefilms Industries Ltd.
- A release order discharges the recovery certificate, ending the active recovery proceedings against the named entity

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is an enforcement action update — the release of a previously issued recovery certificate.

## Compliance Requirements

- No new compliance obligations are imposed on market participants generally
- The release order closes the recovery proceedings against Saurabh Family Trust in this matter
- Saurabh Family Trust and related parties are discharged from the obligations under RC7647 of 2024

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate: RC7647 of 2024
- Release Order Date: June 2026

## Impact Assessment

This order has limited broader market impact. It affects only the specific parties named — Saurabh Family Trust — in connection with the JMD Telefilms Industries Ltd. enforcement matter. The release of the recovery certificate signals conclusion of recovery proceedings against this entity, which is a routine enforcement lifecycle event. Investors in JMD Telefilms Industries Ltd. should be aware of ongoing SEBI enforcement actions related to the company.
