## Summary

SEBI has issued a notice regarding the completion of Recovery Certificate No. RC9005 of 2026 against Middleton Goods Pvt. Ltd. (PAN: AACCM0757H). The recovery proceedings relate to the matter of Illiquid Stock Options and indicate that SEBI has successfully completed the recovery process against the defaulting entity.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate Number: RC9005 of 2026
- Defaulter Entity: Middleton Goods Pvt. Ltd.
- PAN of Defaulter: AACCM0757H
- Subject Matter: Illiquid Stock Options
- Status: Recovery proceedings completed
- Issuing Authority: SEBI

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a completion notice for existing recovery proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

This notice serves as information regarding the completion of recovery proceedings. No new compliance requirements are imposed through this circular.

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate Date: 2026
- Completion Notice Date: February 5, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a completion notice for recovery proceedings against a specific defaulting entity (Middleton Goods Pvt. Ltd.) in the matter of Illiquid Stock Options. The impact is limited to the defaulter entity and has minimal market-wide implications. The notice confirms SEBI's enforcement action and successful completion of recovery proceedings, demonstrating the regulator's commitment to pursuing defaulters and recovering dues.
