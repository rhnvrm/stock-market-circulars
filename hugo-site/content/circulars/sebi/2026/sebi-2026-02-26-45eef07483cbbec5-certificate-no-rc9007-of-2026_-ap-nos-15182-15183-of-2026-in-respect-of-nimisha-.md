## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC9007 of 2026 in connection with Attachment Proceedings (A.P. Nos. 15182 & 15183 of 2026) against Nimisha Hemani (PAN: ABFPH7961K). This action is part of SEBI's ongoing enforcement efforts in the matter of trading in Illiquid Stock Options on Indian exchanges.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9007 of 2026 issued against Nimisha Hemani (PAN: ABFPH7961K)
- Attachment Proceedings initiated under A.P. Nos. 15182 & 15183 of 2026
- The matter pertains to alleged violations in connection with trading in Illiquid Stock Options
- This is part of SEBI's broader enforcement drive against manipulative trading in illiquid stock options

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This order is an enforcement action under existing SEBI recovery and attachment proceedings regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- The named individual (Nimisha Hemani) is required to comply with the recovery certificate and settlement of dues as directed
- Any banks, financial institutions, or third parties holding assets of the defaulter may be bound by the attachment proceedings

## Important Dates

- Certificate Date: February 26, 2026
- A.P. Nos. 15182 & 15183 of 2026 filed in 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is an individual-level enforcement order with no direct broader market impact. It reflects SEBI's continued enforcement of penalties arising from the Illiquid Stock Options manipulation cases, which have resulted in a large number of recovery proceedings against individuals and entities found to have engaged in non-genuine trades in illiquid options segments.
