## Summary

SEBI has issued a prohibitory order under Recovery Certificate No. 4704 of 2022 against Vadlamudi Srikrishna (PAN: ADNPV5504C) in the matter of Venus Power Ventures (India) Ltd. This order pertains to recovery proceedings for outstanding dues to SEBI.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 4704 of 2022 issued against Vadlamudi Srikrishna
- PAN of the individual: ADNPV5504C
- Matter relates to Venus Power Ventures (India) Ltd
- Prohibitory order issued to enforce recovery of dues

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action under existing recovery mechanisms.

## Compliance Requirements

- Vadlamudi Srikrishna must comply with the recovery certificate and prohibitory order
- Outstanding dues to SEBI must be settled as per the recovery certificate
- Non-compliance may result in further enforcement actions including attachment of assets

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate Date: 2022
- Prohibitory Order Date: February 2, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is an individual enforcement action with limited broader market implications.

**Entity Impact**: High for Vadlamudi Srikrishna - Prohibitory measures restrict financial activities until recovery is completed.

**Compliance Significance**: This order demonstrates SEBI's continued enforcement of recovery proceedings against individuals who have outstanding dues from previous regulatory actions or penalties.
