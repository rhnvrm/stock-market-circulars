## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC9103 of 2026 in the matter of Adjudication Proceeding (A.P.) No. 15412 of 2026 against Ravi Himmatramka and Sons HUF (PAN: AAOHR1118J) in connection with violations related to Illiquid Stock Options trading. The recovery certificate is a formal instrument enabling SEBI to recover dues from the entity as per the adjudication order.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9103 of 2026 issued against Ravi Himmatramka and Sons HUF
- PAN of the entity: AAOHR1118J
- Related Adjudication Proceeding: A.P. No. 15412 of 2026
- Matter pertains to Illiquid Stock Options segment violations
- Certificate enables recovery of penalty/dues as determined in the adjudication order

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This document is an enforcement instrument arising from a previously concluded adjudication proceeding.

## Compliance Requirements

- Ravi Himmatramka and Sons HUF is required to pay the penalty/dues specified in the underlying adjudication order.
- Non-payment may result in further enforcement action including attachment of assets under SEBI Act provisions.

## Important Dates

- Certificate Date: June 4, 2026
- A.P. No. 15412 of 2026 (adjudication proceeding reference)

## Impact Assessment

This recovery certificate has a direct financial impact on Ravi Himmatramka and Sons HUF. The broader market impact is minimal as this is an individual enforcement action. It is part of SEBI's ongoing enforcement actions in the Illiquid Stock Options segment, which has been under regulatory scrutiny for alleged manipulative trading practices.
