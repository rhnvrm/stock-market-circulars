## Summary

SEBI has issued a Remittance Advice against Amita Bhalla, designated as a defaulter (PAN: AAAPB2585M), in the matter of ISO at BSE under Recovery Certificate No. 9068 of 2026. The document directs remittance of recovered dues to SEBI pursuant to enforcement proceedings.

## Key Points

- Defaulter: Amita Bhalla (PAN: AAAPB2585M)
- Recovery Certificate No.: 9068 of 2026
- Matter: ISO at BSE – AMITA BHALLA
- SEBI has directed remittance of funds recovered from the defaulter
- The action is part of SEBI's enforcement and recovery mechanism under applicable securities law

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This document is an operational enforcement instrument under existing SEBI recovery powers.

## Compliance Requirements

- The concerned bank or authority receiving this remittance advice is required to remit the specified recovered amount to SEBI as directed.
- No action is required from general market participants or intermediaries.

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate Date: 2026 (specific date in the certificate)
- Remittance Advice Issued: June 2026

## Impact Assessment

This remittance advice is specific to the named defaulter and has no broader market impact. It reflects SEBI's ongoing enforcement against individuals who have failed to comply with regulatory obligations or pay dues under recovery certificates. Market participants and intermediaries are not affected.
