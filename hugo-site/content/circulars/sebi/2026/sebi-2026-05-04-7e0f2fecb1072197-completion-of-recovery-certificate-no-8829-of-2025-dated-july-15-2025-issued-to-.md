## Summary

SEBI has notified the completion of Recovery Certificate No. 8829 of 2025 dated July 15, 2025, issued against SBL Infratech Limited (PAN: AAWCS1619N) as the defaulter in the matter of SBL Infratech Limited. The completion indicates that the recovery proceedings initiated under the certificate have concluded.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8829 of 2025, originally dated July 15, 2025, has been completed.
- Defaulter entity: SBL Infratech Limited (PAN: AAWCS1619N).
- The matter pertains to SBL Infratech Limited.
- SEBI's recovery mechanism under applicable regulations has been exercised and concluded.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This circular is an administrative communication confirming the closure of an existing enforcement action.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance obligations are imposed on market participants by this circular. It is specific to the defaulter SBL Infratech Limited and the recovery proceedings against it.

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate issued: July 15, 2025
- Completion notified: May 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal broader market impact. It is an administrative notice confirming the conclusion of a recovery proceeding against a specific defaulter. Investors and market participants are not directly affected beyond the resolution of the enforcement action against SBL Infratech Limited.
