## Summary

SEBI's Recovery Officer has issued a Notice of Demand dated July 06, 2026, under Recovery Certificate (RC) No. 9207 of 2026, drawn against Rathod Mahendrkumar. The notice arises out of enforcement action related to trading activities of certain entities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now renamed DU Digital Global Limited). The notice forms part of SEBI's recovery proceedings to enforce monetary dues (such as penalties, disgorgement amounts, and/or fees) previously ordered against the noticee following an investigation into trading activity in this scrip.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 9207 of 2026 has been drawn against Rathod Mahendrkumar.
- The underlying matter relates to alleged irregular/manipulative trading activities by certain entities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited).
- The Notice of Demand is dated July 06, 2026, and initiates the formal recovery process for outstanding dues under the relevant recovery certificate.
- This action is part of SEBI's broader enforcement and recovery proceedings against multiple individuals/entities linked to the same scrip investigation (parallel notices have been issued under other RC numbers in the same matter).

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced by this notice. It is an enforcement/recovery action executing a pre-existing SEBI order against the noticee under applicable recovery provisions (typically invoked under the SEBI Act read with Income Tax Act recovery procedures applied to securities market recoveries).

## Compliance Requirements

- The noticee (Rathod Mahendrkumar) is required to clear the outstanding demand specified in the notice within the stipulated period, failing which further coercive recovery measures (such as attachment of assets/bank accounts, demat account freeze, or other recovery steps under the Recovery Certificate) may be initiated by SEBI's Recovery Officer.
- Market intermediaries, depositories, and banks may be required to comply with any subsequent attachment or garnishee orders issued in connection with this Recovery Certificate.

## Important Dates

- Notice date: July 06, 2026
- Publication/upload date: July 07, 2026

## Impact Assessment

The notice has a direct financial and legal impact on the individual noticee, Rathod Mahendrkumar, who faces recovery action for dues arising from the underlying SEBI order. There is no direct impact on the trading of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited) shares or on other market participants, as this is an individual enforcement/recovery matter rather than a market-wide regulatory or trading directive. It does, however, reflect SEBI's continued follow-through on enforcement orders relating to alleged manipulative trading in this scrip.
