## Summary

SEBI issued a Notice of Demand under Recovery Certificate No. 9053 of 2026 dated February 27, 2026, against Prerna Sharma, a Research Analyst (PAN: BJEPS7547A), who has been designated as a defaulter. The notice demands payment of outstanding dues as adjudicated by SEBI.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 9053 of 2026 issued on February 27, 2026
- Directed against Prerna Sharma, Research Analyst (PAN: BJEPS7547A)
- Individual designated as a defaulter by SEBI
- Formal Notice of Demand issued for recovery of outstanding dues
- Action taken under SEBI's enforcement and recovery mechanisms

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an enforcement/recovery action specific to an individual.

## Compliance Requirements

- Prerna Sharma (Research Analyst, PAN: BJEPS7547A) is required to pay the outstanding dues as specified in Recovery Certificate No. 9053 of 2026
- Non-compliance may result in further enforcement actions including attachment of assets

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate Date: February 27, 2026
- Notice of Demand issued: February 27, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This order has no broader market or sector impact. It is an individual enforcement action targeting a single Research Analyst for recovery of dues determined in prior SEBI proceedings. Market participants and investors are not directly affected.
