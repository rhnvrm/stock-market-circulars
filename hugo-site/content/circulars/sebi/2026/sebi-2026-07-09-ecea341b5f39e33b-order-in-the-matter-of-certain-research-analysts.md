## Summary

SEBI issued an order under Section 12(3) of the SEBI Act, 1992 read with Regulation 30A of the SEBI (Intermediaries) Regulations, 2008 against 12 Research Analysts registered under the SEBI (Research Analysts) Regulations, 2014. The Noticees — Arjun Lenin, Anita Patnaik, CNI Research Ltd, East Bridge Advisors Private Limited, Kushank Kamal Poddar, Manish Kumar, Praful Nath Purohit (Proprietor, Market Future India), R K Global Shares & Securities Limited, Raghavendra Rajendra, Raghuveer Singh Rathore, Rajesh Jain (Proprietor, Jinanand Research Analyst), and S Venkateshwar Rao — failed to pay the mandatory renewal registration fee required every five years under Clause 3 of the Second Schedule to the SEBI (Research Analysts) Regulations, 2014.

## Key Points

- 12 Research Analysts named as Noticees for non-payment of five-year renewal fees required to keep their SEBI registration in force.
- Noticees include individual research analysts as well as corporate/proprietorship entities: CNI Research Ltd, East Bridge Advisors Private Limited, R K Global Shares & Securities Limited, Market Future India (proprietor Praful Nath Purohit), and Jinanand Research Analyst (proprietor Rajesh Jain).
- Each Noticee's SEBI registration number, PAN, original registration date, and the date from which renewal fees remain unpaid are listed in the order.
- Order issued under Section 12(3) of the SEBI Act, 1992 read with Regulation 30A of the SEBI (Intermediaries) Regulations, 2008, which govern action against intermediaries for regulatory non-compliance.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced; the order enforces existing requirements under Clause 3 of the Second Schedule to the SEBI (Research Analysts) Regulations, 2014, which mandates renewal fee payment every five years from the date of grant of registration, to be made within three months before expiry of the period for which fee was last paid.

## Compliance Requirements

Affected Research Analysts are required to pay outstanding renewal registration fees to keep their SEBI registration in force. Failure to comply exposes the Noticees to regulatory action under Section 12(3) of the SEBI Act read with Regulation 30A of the SEBI (Intermediaries) Regulations, 2008, which can include suspension or cancellation of certificate of registration.

## Important Dates

- Registration dates and the specific dates from which renewal fees have remained unpaid vary by Noticee (e.g., Arjun Lenin: registered 22 Jul 2020, fees unpaid from 22 Jul 2025); full Noticee-wise schedule is detailed in the order (document truncated in source extract).

## Impact Assessment

The order primarily affects the 12 named Research Analysts and their clients, who may face disruption in advisory services if registrations are suspended or cancelled. None of the Noticees are listed companies, so direct stock market impact is minimal. The action reinforces SEBI's ongoing enforcement of registration fee compliance among market intermediaries, serving as a compliance reminder to other registered Research Analysts.
