## Summary

The Appellate Authority under the Right to Information Act, 2005 at SEBI issued an order in Appeal No. 6949 of 2026, filed by appellant Anand Mishra against the CPIO, SEBI, Mumbai. The appellant had sought data on the number of SEBI investigations conducted over the last 10 years, how many were settled via the settlement mechanism, rejection rates of settlement offers, punishment outcomes, repeat offenders in settlements, names of settling entities/persons, and the quantum of settlements as a percentage of alleged misappropriated gains. The Appellate Authority found the appeal time-barred but proceeded to examine it on merits as well.

## Key Points

- Appellant Anand Mishra filed an RTI application dated April 21, 2026, seeking detailed statistics on SEBI investigations and settlements over the preceding 10 years.
- The CPIO responded on May 19, 2026, stating that SEBI conducts investigations confidentially and holistically, and that enforcement action outcomes are published as Orders on the SEBI website.
- The first appeal was filed on June 26, 2026, beyond the 30-day limit prescribed under Section 19(1) of the RTI Act, with no request for condonation of delay.
- The Appellate Authority held the appeal time-barred and liable to be dismissed on that ground, but nonetheless reviewed the matter on merits.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is an individual RTI appeal order and does not introduce or amend any regulatory requirement.

## Compliance Requirements

Not applicable to market participants; this order pertains solely to the disposal of an RTI request between the appellant and SEBI's CPIO.

## Important Dates

- April 21, 2026: RTI application filed by appellant.
- May 19, 2026: CPIO's response issued.
- June 26, 2026: First appeal filed (beyond the 30-day statutory window).

## Impact Assessment

This order has no bearing on listed companies, trading, or market operations. It is a routine administrative/procedural RTI matter concerning transparency requests about SEBI's internal investigation and settlement statistics, relevant only to RTI process observers and not to investors or issuers.
