## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order for Recovery Certificate No. 8456 of 2024 against Dollar Advisory and Financial Services, a sole proprietorship concern owned by Mr. Ravi Prakash Mohta. A release order is issued by SEBI when the outstanding amount specified in the recovery certificate has been duly paid or otherwise settled, resulting in the discharge of the recovery certificate.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8456 of 2024 has been released by SEBI.
- The recovery certificate was issued against Dollar Advisory and Financial Services, proprietorship of Mr. Ravi Prakash Mohta.
- The release order signifies that the outstanding dues under the recovery certificate have been satisfied.
- SEBI's recovery mechanism under the SEBI Act allows it to recover penalties and other amounts as arrears of land revenue through recovery certificates.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced by this order. It is an administrative closure action concluding the enforcement recovery proceeding against the named entity.

## Compliance Requirements

- No ongoing compliance requirements arise from this release order for third parties.
- Dollar Advisory and Financial Services / Mr. Ravi Prakash Mohta are required to retain proof of payment and the release order for their records.
- The District Collector / Recovery Officer, as applicable, is directed to take note of the release and update records accordingly.

## Important Dates

- **Recovery Certificate No.:** 8456 of 2024
- **Release Order Date:** June 5, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This release order has minimal market impact. It represents the closure of an individual enforcement action against a small financial services proprietor. Market participants, investors, and registered intermediaries are not directly affected. The order confirms that SEBI's recovery process functioned as intended, with the outstanding dues having been collected or settled.
