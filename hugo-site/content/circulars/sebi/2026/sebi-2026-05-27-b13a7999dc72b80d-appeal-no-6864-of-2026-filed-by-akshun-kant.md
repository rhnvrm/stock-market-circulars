## Summary

This is an RTI appeal (No. 6864 of 2026) filed by Akshun Kant before SEBI's Appellate Authority under the Right to Information Act, 2005. The appellant challenged the response given by the CPIO, SEBI, Mumbai to an application dated April 01, 2026, seeking detailed information about DRHP filing requirements, account freezing during IPO processes, and name variation implications for IPO approvals, with a specific interest in Kisaan Parivar Limited.

## Key Points

- Appellant filed RTI application on April 01, 2026; CPIO responded on April 24, 2026; appeal filed May 01, 2026 (Reg. No. SEBIH/A/E/26/00162)
- Queries focus on whether DRHP filing mandates auditing by SEBI or any authority
- Appellant seeks to know if DRHP filing can trigger freezing of bank or operational accounts of an issuer company
- Questions raised about whether minor name variations (e.g., 'Kisaan Parivar Industries Ltd' vs 'Kisaan Parivar Limited') can delay or reject IPO processing
- Appellant seeks information on SEBI's public database or portal for checking DRHP/RHP/IPO filings
- Appellant appears to be an investor in Kisaan Parivar Limited and suspects the company is citing DRHP formalities and account freezing to withhold investor payments

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this order. This is an administrative/appellate proceeding under the RTI Act, 2005.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements arise from this appeal. The matter is procedural and involves adjudication of an information request under the RTI framework.

## Important Dates

- April 01, 2026: RTI application filed by appellant
- April 24, 2026: Response provided by CPIO, SEBI
- May 01, 2026: Appeal filed by Akshun Kant (Reg. No. SEBIH/A/E/26/00162)

## Impact Assessment

This RTI appeal has no direct market or regulatory impact. It is an individual investor's effort to obtain publicly available procedural information about DRHP/IPO processes and to investigate whether a specific company (Kisaan Parivar Limited) has any regulatory filings with SEBI. The queries, if addressed, could provide general investor education on SEBI's IPO oversight framework, but no binding regulatory guidance or market-moving information is expected from this proceeding.
