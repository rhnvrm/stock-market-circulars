## Summary

SEBI has issued a cancellation order for Recovery Certificate No. 8491 of 2025 that was issued against Insight Research (Proprietor: Anil Gurjar) in connection with operating as an unregistered investment adviser. This order signifies the completion or resolution of the recovery proceedings initiated against the entity.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8491 of 2025 has been cancelled
- The certificate was issued against Insight Research and its proprietor Anil Gurjar
- The matter relates to violations under unregistered investment adviser provisions
- Cancellation typically indicates compliance, settlement, or completion of recovery proceedings

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement compliance order.

## Compliance Requirements

This order concludes the recovery proceedings against the specified entity. The cancellation indicates:
- Completion of recovery process
- Compliance with SEBI's recovery demands, or
- Settlement of the matter

## Important Dates

- **Order Date**: February 3, 2026
- **Recovery Certificate No.**: 8491 of 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal - This is a specific enforcement compliance order affecting a single unregistered investment adviser.

**Operational Impact**: Limited to Insight Research and its proprietor Anil Gurjar. The cancellation order closes the recovery proceedings, indicating resolution of the matter.

**Investor Impact**: No direct impact on investors or market operations. This order serves as a reminder of SEBI's enforcement actions against entities operating without proper registration.
