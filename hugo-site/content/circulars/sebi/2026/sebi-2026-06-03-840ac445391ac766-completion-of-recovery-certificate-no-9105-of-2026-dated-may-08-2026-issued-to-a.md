## Summary

SEBI has issued a notice confirming the completion of Recovery Certificate No. 9105 of 2026, originally dated May 08, 2026, against Amit Jain (PAN: AAEPJ3715H), a defaulter in the matter of Himalaya Granites Ltd. The completion of the recovery certificate indicates that the dues/penalties outstanding against the defaulter have been successfully recovered by the relevant recovery officer.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 9105 of 2026 dated May 08, 2026 has been completed
- Defaulter: Amit Jain, PAN: AAEPJ3715H
- Matter relates to Himalaya Granites Ltd.
- Completion signifies successful recovery of outstanding dues owed to SEBI
- No further recovery action is required under this certificate

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this circular. It is a closure/completion notice for an existing enforcement action.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements arise from this notice. The matter is concluded upon completion of the recovery certificate. Relevant parties (banks, depositories, or other entities that may have been served with attachment or enforcement orders) may treat their obligations under this recovery certificate as discharged.

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate issued: May 08, 2026
- Completion notice date: June 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact. It is an administrative closure of an enforcement action against an individual defaulter in the matter of Himalaya Granites Ltd. The completion of the recovery certificate is a positive enforcement outcome for SEBI, confirming that monetary dues have been recovered. No broader market, trading, or operational impact is anticipated.
