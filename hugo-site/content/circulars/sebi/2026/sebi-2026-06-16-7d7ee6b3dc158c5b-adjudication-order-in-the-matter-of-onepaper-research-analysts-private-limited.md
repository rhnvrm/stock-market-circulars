## Summary

SEBI issued an adjudication order (Order/AK/DS/2026-27/32446) against OnePaper Research Analysts Private Limited (PAN: AADCO2642Q, SEBI Regn No.: INH000008093), a SEBI-registered Research Analyst based in Bengaluru. Following an inspection conducted on March 14, 2024 covering the period April 1, 2022 to March 13, 2024, multiple violations of the SEBI (Research Analysts) Regulations, 2014, PFUTP Regulations, 2003, and applicable SEBI Circulars were found and adjudicated.

## Key Points

- SEBI conducted an on-site inspection of OnePaper Research Analysts Pvt. Ltd. at its Bengaluru office on March 14, 2024.
- The inspection period covered April 1, 2022 to March 13, 2024.
- Violations were found under Regulations 3(a), (b), (c), (d), 4(1), and 4(2)(k), (o), and (s) of the PFUTP Regulations read with Section 12A(a), (b), and (c) of the SEBI Act.
- Violations also found under Clauses 1, 2, 7, and 8 of the Code of Conduct (Third Schedule) read with Regulation 24(2) of the RA Regulations.
- Breach of Clause 1(c)(x) of SEBI Circular SEBI/HO/MIRSD/MIRSD-PoD-2/P/CIR/2023/51 dated April 5, 2023 and corresponding Master Circular provisions.
- Mr. N Hariharan was initially appointed Adjudicating Officer; subsequently reassigned to the undersigned AO on November 22, 2024.
- Show Cause Notice (SCN No. SEBI/EAD-2/NH/YK/35518/2024) issued November 14, 2024; Noticee replied December 13, 2024.
- Adjudication proceedings initiated under Sections 15EB and 15HA of the SEBI Act, 1992.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This order applies existing provisions of the SEBI Act, 1992, RA Regulations, 2014, and PFUTP Regulations, 2003 to adjudicate specific violations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Registered Research Analysts must comply with all clauses of the Code of Conduct under the Third Schedule of the RA Regulations, particularly Clauses 1, 2, 7, and 8.
- Research Analysts must not engage in fraudulent or unfair trade practices as prohibited under PFUTP Regulations 3 and 4.
- Compliance with SEBI Master Circular for Research Analysts (SEBI/HO/MIRSD/MIRSD-PoD-1/P/CIR/2024/49 dated May 21, 2024) is mandatory.
- Research Analysts are subject to periodic SEBI inspections and must maintain documentation for inspection periods.

## Important Dates

- April 1, 2022 – March 13, 2024: Inspection period covered by SEBI.
- March 14, 2024: SEBI on-site inspection conducted at Bengaluru office.
- May 16, 2024: Inspection findings communicated to Noticee.
- May 23, 2024: Noticee submitted reply to inspection findings.
- September 9, 2024: SEBI approved initiation of adjudication proceedings.
- October 31, 2024: Mr. N Hariharan appointed as initial Adjudicating Officer.
- November 14, 2024: Show Cause Notice issued.
- November 22, 2024: Undersigned AO appointed upon transfer.
- December 13, 2024: Noticee submitted reply to SCN.
- January 8, 2025: SEBI responded to Noticee's document inspection request.

## Impact Assessment

This order directly impacts OnePaper Research Analysts Private Limited, which faces penalties under Sections 15EB and 15HA of the SEBI Act for violations spanning two years. The order serves as a regulatory signal to other registered research analysts regarding SEBI's active enforcement of RA Regulations and PFUTP compliance. Market participants relying on research analyst services should note SEBI's scrutiny of compliance with the Code of Conduct and prohibitions on fraudulent practices. No broader market-wide operational impact is anticipated.
