## Summary

SEBI has announced the completion of recovery proceedings under Recovery Certificate No. 8433 of 2024 dated December 10, 2024 against Navneet Agarwal and Sons HUF (PAN: AAGHN4487C). The recovery action relates to violations concerning trading in illiquid stock options on the Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE).

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8433 of 2024 issued on December 10, 2024
- Defaulter: Navneet Agarwal and Sons HUF (PAN: AAGHN4487C)
- Matter relates to trading in illiquid stock options on BSE
- Recovery proceedings have been completed
- Enforcement action concluded

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a completion notice for existing enforcement proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. This circular serves as a public notice of completed recovery proceedings against the defaulter entity.

## Important Dates

- **December 10, 2024**: Recovery Certificate No. 8433 of 2024 issued
- **January 9, 2026**: Completion of recovery proceedings announced
