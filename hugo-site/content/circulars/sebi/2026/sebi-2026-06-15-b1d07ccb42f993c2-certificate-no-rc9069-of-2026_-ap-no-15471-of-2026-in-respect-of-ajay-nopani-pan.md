## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC9069 of 2026 (A.P. No. 15471 of 2026) against Ajay Nopani (PAN: ADGPN4214N) for recovery of outstanding dues in connection with the Illiquid Stock Options enforcement matter. This is a recovery proceeding initiated by SEBI to collect penalties or disgorgement amounts previously ordered against the individual.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9069 of 2026 issued against Ajay Nopani (PAN: ADGPN4214N)
- Associated with Application/Adjudication Proceeding No. 15471 of 2026
- Pertains to the Illiquid Stock Options matter — a large-scale SEBI enforcement action targeting manipulative trading in the illiquid stock options segment on exchanges
- The certificate authorises SEBI to recover dues as arrears of land revenue under applicable law

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement/recovery action under existing SEBI (Recovery of Dues) Procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Ajay Nopani is required to pay outstanding dues as specified in the recovery certificate
- Failure to comply may result in attachment and sale of assets by the Recovery Officer
- No compliance obligations for market participants other than the named individual

## Important Dates

- Certificate Date: June 15, 2026
- Recovery Certificate No.: RC9069 of 2026
- A.P. No.: 15471 of 2026

## Impact Assessment

This order has no direct impact on the broader market or general investors. It is a targeted enforcement/recovery action against a named individual arising from SEBI's ongoing proceedings related to manipulation of the illiquid stock options segment. The Illiquid Stock Options matter has been a long-running enforcement initiative by SEBI against entities found to have engaged in non-genuine trades to create artificial volumes.
