## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC9019 of 2026 containing a Notice of Demand against Namrata Choradia (PAN: AVLPC7083R) in connection with illiquid stock options violations. This document is part of SEBI's enforcement and recovery proceedings for securities market violations.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate Number: RC9019 of 2026
- Party: Namrata Choradia (PAN: AVLPC7083R)
- Matter: Illiquid Stock Options violations
- Document Type: Notice of Demand under recovery proceedings
- Enforcement action initiated by SEBI

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI regulations governing illiquid stock options and market manipulation.

## Compliance Requirements

- Namrata Choradia is required to comply with the demand notice
- Payment of amounts demanded within specified timeline
- Failure to comply may result in further recovery proceedings including attachment of assets
- Party must respond to recovery officer as per the notice requirements

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate Date: 2026
- Notice Issue Date: February 9, 2026
- Payment deadline: As specified in the notice (refer to PDF document)

## Impact Assessment

**Individual Impact:** High - Direct enforcement action against Namrata Choradia with financial implications and potential asset recovery proceedings.

**Market Impact:** Low - This is an individual enforcement case with no broader market implications. It demonstrates SEBI's continued vigilance in pursuing violations related to illiquid stock options.

**Compliance Impact:** Serves as a reminder to market participants about SEBI's enforcement capabilities and the consequences of engaging in illiquid stock options violations or market manipulation activities.
