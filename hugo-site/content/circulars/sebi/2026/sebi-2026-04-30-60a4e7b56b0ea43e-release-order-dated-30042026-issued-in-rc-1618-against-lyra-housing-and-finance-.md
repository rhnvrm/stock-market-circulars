## Summary

SEBI's Recovery Officer has issued a release order dated 30 April 2026 under Recovery Certificate No. RC 1618 against Lyra Housing and Finance Pvt. Ltd. (PAN: AAACL3708P) in the matter of Linkson International Limited. The order directs that attachments or freezes previously placed on the assets or bank accounts of Lyra Housing and Finance Pvt. Ltd., in furtherance of RC 1618, be lifted following satisfaction of the recovery dues outstanding under the certificate.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC 1618 was issued against Lyra Housing and Finance Pvt. Ltd. (PAN: AAACL3708P)
- The release order dated 30 April 2026 confirms that the recovery amount specified in RC 1618 has been paid or otherwise recovered
- Attachments or freezes placed on the company's assets/bank accounts in connection with RC 1618 are directed to be released
- The underlying matter concerns Linkson International Limited, in which SEBI had initiated enforcement and recovery proceedings
- This order effectively closes the recovery proceedings against Lyra Housing and Finance Pvt. Ltd. under RC 1618

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced by this order. It is a closure/release order under SEBI's recovery enforcement framework (SEBI Act, 1992 and SEBI (Recovery of Amounts) Rules), confirming resolution of the outstanding recovery dues for Lyra Housing and Finance Pvt. Ltd. under RC 1618.

## Compliance Requirements

- No new compliance obligations arise for the broader market from this order
- Lyra Housing and Finance Pvt. Ltd. is discharged from its recovery obligations under RC 1618 upon release
- Entities involved in securities markets must ensure compliance with SEBI orders; non-compliance leading to recovery certificates and asset attachments remains an active enforcement tool for SEBI

## Important Dates

- **30 April 2026**: Release order issued by SEBI's Recovery Officer under RC 1618

## Impact Assessment

This order has no direct market impact. It is a retrospective enforcement closure confirming that Lyra Housing and Finance Pvt. Ltd. (PAN: AAACL3708P) has settled its recovery obligations under RC 1618 in the matter of Linkson International Limited. Release orders of this nature are issued routinely as respondents pay their dues under recovery certificates; they signal compliance resolution for the named entity rather than any new market concern or regulatory change.
