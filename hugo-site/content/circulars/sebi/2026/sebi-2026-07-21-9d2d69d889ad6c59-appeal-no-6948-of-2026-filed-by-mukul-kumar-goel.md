## Summary

SEBI's Appellate Authority under the Right to Information Act, 2005, has disposed of Appeal No. 6948 of 2026 filed by Mukul Kumar Goel by remitting the matter back to the CPIO, SEBI, Mumbai for de novo consideration. The appellant had sought information relating to refund status in the PACL matter via an RTI application dated June 19, 2026. The CPIO's original response directed the appellant to the Justice (Retd.) R.M. Lodha Committee and SEBI's website for PACL-related public notices, press releases, status reports, and FAQs. The Appellate Authority found this response did not clarify whether the requested information was actually available with SEBI, and has ordered the CPIO to issue a fresh, adequate response within 15 working days.

## Key Points

- Appellant Mukul Kumar Goel filed an RTI application dated June 19, 2026 seeking information on refund status in the PACL matter.
- CPIO, SEBI, Mumbai responded on June 23, 2026, directing the appellant to the Justice (Retd.) R.M. Lodha Committee and to publicly available PACL materials on SEBI's website.
- Appellant filed an appeal (Reg. No. SEBIH/A/E/26/00234) dated June 26, 2026, dissatisfied with the response.
- Appellate Authority Ruchi Chojer found the CPIO's reply did not clarify whether the requested information is held by SEBI.
- The application has been remitted to the CPIO for fresh (de novo) consideration and response.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this order. It is an individual case disposal under the RTI Act, 2005, directing procedural correction in SEBI's RTI response process.

## Compliance Requirements

The CPIO, SEBI, Mumbai must reconsider the appellant's RTI application afresh and issue a clear, adequate response in terms of the RTI Act, specifically clarifying whether the requested PACL refund status information is available with SEBI.

## Important Dates

- June 19, 2026: RTI application filed by appellant.
- June 23, 2026: CPIO's original response.
- June 26, 2026: Appeal filed by appellant.
- July 21, 2026: Appellate Authority's order issued.
- Compliance deadline: Fresh response due within 15 working days from receipt of this order.

## Impact Assessment

This order has no bearing on markets, listed securities, or trading activity. Its impact is confined to SEBI's internal RTI grievance redressal process and the individual appellant seeking clarity on PACL refund status, a matter overseen separately by the Justice (Retd.) R.M. Lodha Committee.
