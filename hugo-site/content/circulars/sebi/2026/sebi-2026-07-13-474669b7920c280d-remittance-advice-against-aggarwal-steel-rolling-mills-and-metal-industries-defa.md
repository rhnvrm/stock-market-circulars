## Summary

SEBI has issued a remittance advice relating to Recovery Certificate No. 8964 of 2025, directing the remittance of funds recovered from the defaulter, Aggarwal Steel Rolling Mills and Metal Industries (PAN: AABFA9058R), in connection with recovery proceedings arising out of the matter involving PG Electroplast Limited.

## Key Points

- The notice pertains to enforcement action against Aggarwal Steel Rolling Mills and Metal Industries, identified as a defaulter under SEBI recovery proceedings.
- The defaulter's PAN is AABFA9058R.
- Recovery Certificate No. 8964 of 2025 forms the basis for this remittance advice.
- The underlying matter relates to PG Electroplast Limited.
- This is part of SEBI's ongoing recovery proceedings against defaulters who have failed to comply with earlier orders, such as disgorgement, penalty, or other monetary directions.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is an administrative/enforcement communication executing an existing recovery certificate under SEBI's recovery proceedings framework (per the Second Schedule to the Income Tax Act, 1961, as applied by SEBI recovery rules).

## Compliance Requirements

This notice is procedural and primarily concerns internal SEBI recovery administration and the recovery officer's actions; no new compliance obligations are imposed on listed entities, market intermediaries, or the general investing public.

## Important Dates

Recovery Certificate No. 8964 of 2025 dated 2025; remittance advice published on 2026-07-13.

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal direct market impact. It documents the remittance of recovered funds pursuant to enforcement proceedings against a specific defaulter and does not affect trading, listing, or the operations of PG Electroplast Limited itself, which is referenced only as the originating matter for the recovery certificate.
