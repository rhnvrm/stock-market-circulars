## Summary

The Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) has issued a Notice of Demand dated July 06, 2026 under Recovery Certificate (RC) No. 9198 of 2026, drawn against Madhu Kumari Bairwa. The notice arises from an underlying SEBI order concerning trading activities of certain entities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited). The notice directs the recipient to pay the outstanding demand amount specified in the recovery certificate, failing which recovery proceedings may be initiated against their assets.

## Key Points

- Notice issued by SEBI's Recovery Officer under Recovery Certificate No. 9198 of 2026, dated July 06, 2026.
- The notice is addressed to Madhu Kumari Bairwa, identified as liable under the underlying enforcement order.
- The matter relates to trading activities of certain entities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited).
- This notice is part of a batch of similar recovery demand notices issued to multiple individuals/entities connected to the same case.
- Non-payment within the stipulated period can lead to recovery action, including attachment of assets, under the applicable recovery rules.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced by this notice. It is an enforcement action executing recovery of amounts (such as penalty, disgorgement, or other dues) already determined in a prior SEBI order against the named individual.

## Compliance Requirements

The noticee, Madhu Kumari Bairwa, is required to pay the amount specified under RC No. 9198 of 2026 within the timeframe stipulated in the notice. Failure to comply may result in coercive recovery measures, including but not limited to attachment and sale of movable/immovable property, as permitted under the recovery provisions applicable to SEBI recovery proceedings.

## Important Dates

- Notice date: July 06, 2026
- Publication date: July 07, 2026
- Specific payment deadline: Not specified in available metadata; typically a limited period is granted from the date of service of the notice.

## Impact Assessment

This notice has a direct impact primarily on the named individual, Madhu Kumari Bairwa, rather than on the broader market or on DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited) shareholders generally. It reflects continued enforcement follow-through by SEBI on a previously adjudicated matter involving alleged irregular trading activity in the scrip. Investors and market participants should note this as part of SEBI's ongoing enforcement against entities involved in the underlying case, which may be relevant context for assessing regulatory risk associated with the scrip.
