## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC9016 of 2026 along with a Notice of Demand against Lalita Chowdhary (PAN: ACOPC0249F) in connection with violations related to illiquid stock options. This enforcement action is part of SEBI's ongoing efforts to recover dues from entities found to have violated securities regulations.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate Number: RC9016 of 2026
- Individual: Lalita Chowdhary
- PAN: ACOPC0249F
- Case Matter: Illiquid Stock Options
- Action Type: Notice of Demand for recovery of dues
- Date of Notice: February 9, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI regulations related to illiquid stock options and market manipulation.

## Compliance Requirements

- Lalita Chowdhary is required to comply with the demand notice and pay the specified amount
- Failure to comply may result in further recovery proceedings including attachment of assets
- The recovery certificate enables SEBI to initiate recovery proceedings as per the SEBI Act

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate Issue Date: February 9, 2026
- Payment deadline: As specified in the demand notice (refer to PDF for specific date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is an individual enforcement action with no direct impact on market operations or trading activities.

**Regulatory Impact**: Medium - Demonstrates SEBI's continued focus on enforcement and recovery in cases involving illiquid stock options and market manipulation.

**Compliance Impact**: High for the individual concerned - Non-compliance can lead to asset attachment and further legal proceedings.
