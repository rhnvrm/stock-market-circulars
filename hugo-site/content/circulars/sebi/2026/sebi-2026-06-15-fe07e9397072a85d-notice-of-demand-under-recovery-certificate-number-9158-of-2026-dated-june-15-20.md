## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand to Sanjay Kumar (PAN: AVBPK4726F) under Recovery Certificate No. 9158 of 2026 dated June 15, 2026. The notice relates to the defaulter's dealings in illiquid stock options on BSE, for which SEBI has initiated recovery proceedings to enforce payment of dues determined in prior adjudication or settlement orders.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 9158 of 2026 issued on June 15, 2026
- Defaulter: Sanjay Kumar (PAN: AVBPK4726F)
- Matter: Dealings in illiquid stock options on BSE
- SEBI's Recovery Officer has issued this Notice of Demand to enforce the outstanding dues
- Non-compliance may lead to attachment of assets and further coercive recovery action under the SEBI Act

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an enforcement/recovery action under existing SEBI (Recovery of Amount) Rules, 2006 pursuant to Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992.

## Compliance Requirements

- Sanjay Kumar is required to pay the demanded amount as specified in Recovery Certificate No. 9158 of 2026 within the prescribed timeframe
- Failure to comply may result in attachment of movable and immovable properties, bank accounts, and other assets
- The defaulter may file an objection before the Recovery Officer if grounds exist under applicable rules

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate issued: June 15, 2026
- Notice of Demand served: June 15, 2026
- Payment deadline: As specified in the notice (refer to the attached PDF for the exact deadline)

## Impact Assessment

This notice affects only the named individual defaulter, Sanjay Kumar. There is no direct impact on broader market participants, listed companies, or trading operations. The action is part of SEBI's ongoing enforcement drive against participants found guilty of manipulative dealings in illiquid stock options on BSE, a segment that has historically been misused for tax evasion and fund diversion.
