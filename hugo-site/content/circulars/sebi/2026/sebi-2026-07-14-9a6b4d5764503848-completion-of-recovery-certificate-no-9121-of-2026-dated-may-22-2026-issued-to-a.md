## Summary

SEBI has issued a notice confirming the completion of Recovery Certificate No. 9121 of 2026, dated May 22, 2026, which was issued to defaulter Anupama Agarwal (PAN: AIGPA7456G) in the matter of dealing in illiquid stock options on the BSE. The completion indicates that the recovery proceedings initiated against the defaulter under this certificate have been concluded.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 9121 of 2026 was originally issued on May 22, 2026 against defaulter Anupama Agarwal.
- The matter pertains to dealing in illiquid stock options on the BSE, a category of cases SEBI has pursued as part of its ongoing enforcement action against non-genuine options trading.
- SEBI has now formally notified completion of the recovery certificate, closing out this specific recovery action.
- The defaulter is identified by PAN AIGPA7456G.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced by this notice. It documents the closure of an individual recovery proceeding initiated under Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992, read with the Income-tax (Certificate Proceedings) Rules, applied mutatis mutandis for recovery of sums due to SEBI.

## Compliance Requirements

This notice does not impose new compliance obligations on market participants, listed companies, or intermediaries. It is an administrative closure record concerning a specific individual defaulter's recovery certificate.

## Important Dates

- May 22, 2026: Date of issuance of Recovery Certificate No. 9121 of 2026.
- July 14, 2026: Date of the notice confirming completion of the recovery certificate.

## Impact Assessment

The notice has no direct impact on listed securities, trading operations, or broader market participants. It reflects closure of an individual enforcement/recovery matter tied to historical illiquid stock options dealing on the BSE and is relevant primarily for tracking SEBI's enforcement and recovery activity.
