## Summary

SEBI's Appellate Authority under the Right to Information Act, 2005 has passed an order disposing of Appeal No. 6922 of 2026 filed by Balkishan Baldawa against the CPIO, SEBI, Mumbai. The appellant had sought detailed records relating to complaints he filed against Techindia Nirman Limited (BSE: 526576, NSE: TECHIN) and Agri-Tech (India) Limited between January 4, 2024 and the date of his RTI application, alleging he was provided incomplete, misleading or false information by the CPIO in the original response dated May 27, 2026.

## Key Points

- Appellant Balkishan Baldawa filed an RTI application dated April 28, 2026, received via the SEBI RTI MIS portal.
- The CPIO, SEBI Mumbai responded on May 27, 2026; the appellant filed the appeal on June 9, 2026 (Reg. No. SEBIH/A/E/26/00212).
- Ground of appeal: the appellant claims he was provided incomplete, misleading, or false information.
- Queries 1, 2, 5, 6, 9 and 10 sought: a chronological list of all complaints/RTI applications filed by the appellant against Techindia Nirman Limited and Agri-Tech (India) Limited across multiple SEBI channels (Chairperson's office, SCORES, MIPORTAL, CFID, EFD, CVO, Vigilance, Regional Office Mumbai); details of each complaint (receipt date, mode, reference number, assigned officer, status, grounds of closure); a complete list of SCORES complaints filed by the appellant with registration numbers and closure details; copies of Action Taken Reports (ATRs) filed by the two companies on SCORES; and file notings/action taken by SEBI in response to the NCLAT's Para 83 referral in its Judgment dated December 15, 2025 in CA(AT)(Ins.) No. 970 of 2025 concerning the two companies.
- The order records that the Appellate Authority reviewed the RTI application, the CPIO's response, and the appeal, and found the matter could be decided on the material on record.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is an individual adjudicatory order under the RTI Act relating to information disclosure, not a regulatory or policy change.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance obligations are created for market participants or listed entities by this order. It concerns SEBI's internal RTI disclosure process and its handling of a specific appellant's grievance and enforcement-related information requests.

## Important Dates

- January 4, 2024 to April 28, 2026: Period covered by the appellant's information request.
- April 28, 2026: RTI application filed.
- May 27, 2026: CPIO response issued.
- June 9, 2026: Appeal filed (Reg. No. SEBIH/A/E/26/00212).
- December 15, 2025: NCLAT Judgment in CA(AT)(Ins.) No. 970 of 2025 containing the Para 83 referral against Techindia Nirman Limited and Agri-Tech (India) Limited.

## Impact Assessment

This order has no direct market or trading impact and does not alter any regulatory framework. Its relevance lies in indicating ongoing SEBI enforcement-related engagement concerning Techindia Nirman Limited (TECHIN) and Agri-Tech (India) Limited, referenced via an NCLAT referral, which investors tracking these entities may wish to monitor, though the order itself is limited to an RTI disclosure dispute rather than a substantive enforcement action.
