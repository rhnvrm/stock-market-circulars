## Summary

SEBI's Adjudicating Officer issued Adjudication Order No. Order/JS/YK/2026-27/32423 against Rajiv Sharma, Proprietor of M/s. Capital Life Market Research (IA Registration No. INA000001365, PAN: BVYPS5256C), following a thematic inspection conducted jointly by SEBI and BSE Ltd. on February 07, 2025. The inspection covered the period April 1, 2023 to January 31, 2025, focusing on fees charged, NISM qualification compliance, periodic and annual report submissions, and redressal of investor complaints.

## Key Points

- Thematic inspection of Rajiv Sharma's investment advisory firm was conducted on February 07, 2025 by SEBI and BSE Ltd.
- Inspection period covered April 01, 2023 to January 31, 2025.
- SEBI communicated inspection findings to the Noticee via letter dated June 19, 2025; replies were submitted August 31 and September 01, 2025.
- Show Cause Notice (SCN Ref. No. DIS/7582-83/2026) was issued on March 10, 2026.
- Adjudicating Officer was appointed on January 23, 2026 under Section 15-I of the SEBI Act.
- Penalty proceedings initiated under Section 15EB of the SEBI Act.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This order enforces existing SEBI Investment Advisers Regulations, 2013 and relevant Master Circulars.

## Compliance Requirements

The following violations were alleged against the Noticee:

- **Regulation 15(3) of IA Regulations**: Non-compliance related to fees charged by the investment adviser.
- **Regulations 7(2) and 15(13) read with Regulation 13(a)**: Violations concerning NISM qualification requirements and associated compliance.
- **Regulation 19(1)(a to h), (2), (3)**: Non-compliance with periodic and annual report submissions and redressal of investor complaints.
- **Regulation 15(12) read with Clause 27 of Master Circular SEBI/HO/MIRSD/MIRSD-PoD/P/CIR/2025/94 dated June 27, 2025**: Failure to meet compliance obligations under the Master Circular.
- **Regulation 15(8) read with Clause 84 of Master Circular SEBI/HO/MIRSD/SECFATF/P/CIR/2023/169 dated October 12, 2023**: Non-compliance with KYC/AML-related obligations.

## Important Dates

- **April 01, 2023 – January 31, 2025**: Inspection period.
- **February 07, 2025**: Onsite thematic inspection conducted.
- **June 19, 2025**: SEBI communicated inspection findings to Noticee.
- **August 31 – September 01, 2025**: Noticee submitted replies to inspection findings.
- **January 23, 2026**: Adjudicating Officer appointed.
- **March 10, 2026**: Show Cause Notice issued.

## Impact Assessment

This order is directed at a single small investment adviser and does not indicate systemic market risk. The action reinforces SEBI's ongoing thematic inspection program targeting investment advisers for compliance with fee regulations, NISM qualifications, reporting obligations, and KYC/AML norms. Other registered investment advisers should review their compliance with the cited regulations and Master Circulars to avoid similar enforcement actions.
