## Summary

SEBI has issued a Remittance Advice addressed to the Defaulter, Bets Healthcare Private Limited (PAN: AADCB1811A), under Recovery Certificate No. 8725 of 2025. The recovery proceedings arise in connection with the matter of Fortis Healthcare Limited, under which SEBI's Recovery Officer had earlier issued a Recovery Certificate to recover dues from the defaulter entity. This advice directs remittance of the recovered/recoverable amount in accordance with the certificate.

## Key Points

- Defaulter entity: Bets Healthcare Private Limited (PAN: AADCB1811A)
- Underlying matter: Fortis Healthcare Limited
- Instrument: Recovery Certificate No. 8725 of 2025
- Document type: Remittance Advice issued by SEBI's Recovery Officer

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is an enforcement/recovery action under SEBI's existing powers to recover sums due (such as disgorgement amounts, penalties, or other dues) as arrears of land revenue, pursuant to a previously issued Recovery Certificate.

## Compliance Requirements

The named defaulter, Bets Healthcare Private Limited, and any intermediary or authority handling the remittance are required to act in accordance with the directions in the Remittance Advice to ensure the recovered amount is properly transferred/credited as per the Recovery Certificate.

## Important Dates

Specific remittance deadlines and amounts are contained in the source PDF, which is an image-based scanned document and could not be extracted in text form for this summary. Refer to the original PDF for precise figures and dates.

## Impact Assessment

This notice has limited market-wide impact as it pertains to a specific enforcement/recovery action against a named defaulter entity in connection with the Fortis Healthcare Limited matter. It does not directly affect trading, listing status, or disclosure obligations of Fortis Healthcare Limited or other listed entities, though it reflects ongoing SEBI enforcement activity related to the case.
