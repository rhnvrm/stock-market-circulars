## Summary

SEBI has issued a notice of attachment of bank accounts and demat accounts under Attachment Proceedings No. 15125 & 15126 of 2026 dated January 01, 2026, against Investment Research Advisor (Proprietor – Amit Gangrade, PAN: AVRPG8196P) and Algo System (Proprietor – Sudeep Gangrade, PAN: GDTPS4158F) in the matter of Investment Research Advisor, under Recovery Certificate No. 8971 of 2025. This action is part of SEBI's debt recovery mechanism for defaulters.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate Number: RC 8971 of 2025
- Attachment Proceedings Numbers: AP No. 15125 & 15126 of 2026
- Notice Date: January 01, 2026
- Defaulting Entities:
  - Investment Research Advisor (Proprietor: Amit Gangrade, PAN: AVRPG8196P)
  - Algo System (Proprietor: Sudeep Gangrade, PAN: GDTPS4158F)
- Action Taken: Attachment of bank accounts and demat accounts

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI recovery mechanisms.

## Compliance Requirements

- The attached bank and demat accounts of the defaulters are subject to SEBI's recovery proceedings
- Banks and depositories are required to comply with the attachment orders
- The defaulters must settle outstanding dues as per the recovery certificate
- Third parties dealing with these entities should be aware of the attachment proceedings

## Important Dates

- **January 01, 2026**: Notice of attachment issued
- Recovery Certificate No. 8971 issued in 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is an enforcement action against specific entities and does not affect broader market operations.

**Regulatory Impact**: High - Demonstrates SEBI's active enforcement of recovery proceedings against defaulters. The attachment of both bank and demat accounts represents a strong enforcement measure.

**Entity Impact**: High - The named entities (Investment Research Advisor and Algo System) face severe restrictions with their accounts attached, preventing them from accessing or transferring funds until the recovery proceedings are resolved.

**Investor Impact**: Medium - Investors or clients of these entities may face difficulties in transactions or fund recovery. This notice serves as a public warning about dealing with these defaulting entities.
