## Summary

SEBI has issued a notice of attachment against Ravi Swarajya Bahrathi (PAN: AJXPR4790C) under Recovery Certificate No. 4690 of 2022 in the matter of Venus Power Ventures (India) Limited. This action is part of SEBI's enforcement and recovery proceedings to recover dues owed by the named individual.

## Key Points

- Notice of attachment issued against Ravi Swarajya Bahrathi (PAN: AJXPR4790C)
- Issued under Recovery Certificate No. 4690 of 2022
- Related to the matter of Venus Power Ventures (India) Limited
- Published by SEBI on 8 June 2026
- Part of SEBI's ongoing enforcement and recovery proceedings

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This document is an enforcement action under existing SEBI recovery certificate powers.

## Compliance Requirements

- The named individual (Ravi Swarajya Bahrathi) is subject to attachment of assets as directed by SEBI
- Banks, depositories, and other institutions holding assets of the named individual may be required to comply with attachment directions
- Any entity served with this notice must not allow transfer or encumbrance of the attached assets

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate No. 4690 issued: 2022
- Notice of Attachment issued: 8 June 2026

## Impact Assessment

This notice has no broad market or investor impact. It is a targeted enforcement action affecting only the named individual (Ravi Swarajya Bahrathi) and parties holding their assets. The action reflects SEBI's continued enforcement of recovery proceedings related to Venus Power Ventures (India) Limited.
