## Summary

SEBI has issued Notice of Attachment of Bank Accounts No. 15344 of 2026 dated May 06, 2026 against Vijay Prakash Gupta, identified as a defaulter with PAN ANCPV2725J. The notice is issued under Recovery Certificate No. 8873 of 2025, arising from SEBI's investigation into the trading activities of certain entities in the scrip of Continental Seeds and Chemicals Limited.

## Key Points

- Notice of Attachment of Bank Accounts No. 15344 of 2026 issued on May 06, 2026
- Directed against defaulter Vijay Prakash Gupta (PAN: ANCPV2725J)
- Issued under Recovery Certificate No. 8873 of 2025
- Connected to investigation in trading activities in scrip of Continental Seeds and Chemicals Limited
- SEBI exercising recovery powers under Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This notice is an enforcement action under existing SEBI recovery and attachment powers.

## Compliance Requirements

- Banks maintaining accounts of Vijay Prakash Gupta (PAN: ANCPV2725J) are required to freeze/attach the specified bank accounts upon receipt of this notice
- Banks must not allow withdrawals or debits from attached accounts
- Banks must report the balance and status of attached accounts to SEBI as directed
- Non-compliance with attachment notice by banks may attract penalties under SEBI Act

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate No. 8873 issued: 2025 (exact date in certificate)
- Notice of Attachment issued: May 06, 2026
- Effective date of attachment: Immediately upon receipt by banks

## Impact Assessment

This enforcement action is limited in market impact as it targets a single individual defaulter. It reflects SEBI's active pursuit of recovery proceedings stemming from its investigation into alleged market manipulation or irregular trading activities in the scrip of Continental Seeds and Chemicals Limited. The attachment of bank accounts is a coercive recovery measure to enforce payment of dues under the recovery certificate. Broader market participants and investors in Continental Seeds and Chemicals Ltd should be aware that SEBI investigations related to this scrip are progressing through the enforcement stage.
