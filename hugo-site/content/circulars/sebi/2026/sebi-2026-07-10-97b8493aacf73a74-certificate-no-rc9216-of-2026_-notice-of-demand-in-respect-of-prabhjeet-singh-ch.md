## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand under Recovery Certificate No. RC9216 of 2026 against Prabhjeet Singh Chawla (PAN: ACAPC4870A), a defaulter in a matter concerning dealing in illiquid stock options. The notice demands payment of the outstanding dues specified in the recovery certificate, failing which coercive recovery proceedings may be initiated against the defaulter under applicable recovery of debts laws.

## Key Points

- Defaulter: Prabhjeet Singh Chawla (PAN: ACAPC4870A)
- Recovery Certificate No.: RC9216 of 2026
- Underlying violation: Dealing in Illiquid Stock Options
- Action: Notice of demand issued requiring payment of dues under the recovery certificate

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced; this is a case-specific enforcement update demanding payment of dues from an individual defaulter pursuant to a previously issued recovery certificate.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance action is required from market participants, listed companies, or intermediaries. The notice pertains solely to the recovery of dues from the named individual defaulter, who is required to satisfy the demand or face further recovery action.

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate No.: RC9216 of 2026
- Notice of demand date: July 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market-wide impact as it concerns an individual enforcement/recovery matter. It does not affect trading, listing status, or obligations of any listed company or the broader investor community, but reflects SEBI's ongoing enforcement action to recover dues from individuals found in default relating to illiquid stock options trading violations.
