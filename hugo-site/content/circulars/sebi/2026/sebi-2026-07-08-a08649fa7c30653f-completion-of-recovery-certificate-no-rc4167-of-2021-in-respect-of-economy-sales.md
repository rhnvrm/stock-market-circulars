## Summary

SEBI has issued a notification confirming the completion of Recovery Certificate No. RC4167 of 2021, which was issued against Economy Sales Private Limited (PAN: AACCE1151B) in connection with recovery proceedings related to the matter of PS IT Infrastructure and Services Limited. The notice formally records that the recovery action under this certificate has been concluded.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC4167 of 2021 has been completed/closed by SEBI's recovery officer.
- The certificate was issued against Economy Sales Private Limited, identified by PAN AACCE1151B.
- The proceeding relates to the broader matter involving PS IT Infrastructure and Services Limited.
- Completion indicates the recovery process under this specific certificate has reached finality, typically following full recovery of the outstanding dues or closure as per due process.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is an administrative/enforcement update closing out a specific recovery certificate issued under SEBI's recovery proceedings framework (Income Tax Certificate Proceedings Rules, as applied by SEBI recovery officers).

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance obligations arise for market participants generally. Economy Sales Private Limited and any related parties to the underlying matter should note the formal closure of this recovery certificate for their records.

## Important Dates

Notice issued: July 2026 (specific completion date as per SEBI recovery officer's order; refer to source PDF for exact date).

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal direct market impact as it concerns closure of a recovery certificate against a private (non-listed) entity, Economy Sales Private Limited. It is primarily relevant to parties involved in the underlying PS IT Infrastructure and Services Limited matter and to SEBI's enforcement/recovery record-keeping. No listed securities are directly affected.
