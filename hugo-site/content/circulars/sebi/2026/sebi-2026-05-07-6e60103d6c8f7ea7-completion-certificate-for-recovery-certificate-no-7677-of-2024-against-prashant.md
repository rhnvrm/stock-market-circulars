## Summary

SEBI has issued a Completion Certificate for Recovery Certificate No. 7677 of 2024 against Prashant Talwalkar (PAN: AAAPT3528N) in the matter of Talwalkars Better Value Fitness Limited. This certificate signifies the formal closure of recovery proceedings that had been initiated under the said recovery certificate.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 7677 of 2024 has been completed/closed
- Proceedings were against Prashant Talwalkar (PAN: AAAPT3528N)
- Matter pertains to Talwalkars Better Value Fitness Limited
- Issuance of a completion certificate indicates the recovery action has been concluded
- Prashant Talwalkar is associated with Talwalkars Better Value Fitness Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This document represents the conclusion of an existing enforcement/recovery action by SEBI.

## Compliance Requirements

- No new compliance obligations arise from this completion certificate
- The underlying recovery dues under RC No. 7677 of 2024 have been addressed
- Relevant parties (banks, depositories, or intermediaries previously notified of the recovery certificate) should take note of the completion and release any attached assets if applicable

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate No. 7677 of 2024: Original certificate issued in 2024
- Completion Certificate issued: May 2026

## Impact Assessment

This completion certificate has minimal direct market impact. It represents the closure of a legacy enforcement matter against Prashant Talwalkar in relation to Talwalkars Better Value Fitness Limited. Stakeholders holding any freeze or attachment orders linked to RC No. 7677 of 2024 should treat them as discharged. The stock TALWALKARS may see negligible impact as this resolves a historical enforcement action rather than initiating a new one.
