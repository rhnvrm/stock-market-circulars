## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate Number: 4365 of 2021
- Defaulter: Neha Bimal Shah (PAN: ABCPS4435Q)
- Matter relates to: Mapro Industries Limited
- Order Date: January 6, 2026
- SEBI Reference: HO/50/11/11(10)2026-RRD-TPD-RD3, I/1936/2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an enforcement action under existing recovery framework.

## Compliance Requirements

The remittance order is directed at the defaulter Neha Bimal Shah for compliance with recovery proceedings initiated by SEBI.

## Important Dates

- Order Date: January 6, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a specific enforcement action against an individual in recovery proceedings. The impact is limited to the parties directly involved in the recovery process related to Mapro Industries Limited. There is no broader market or regulatory impact expected from this order.
