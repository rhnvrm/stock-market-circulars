## Summary

SEBI has notified the completion of Recovery Certificate no. RC7649 of 2024 issued against Adarsh Trust (PAN: AABTA7344L) in connection with the enforcement matter of JMD Telefilms Industries Limited. This circular marks the conclusion of the recovery proceedings under this certificate.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate no. RC7649 of 2024 has been marked as completed.
- The certificate was issued against Adarsh Trust, identified by PAN AABTA7344L.
- The recovery proceedings relate to the matter of JMD Telefilms Industries Limited.
- SEBI issues such notices to formally close recovery actions once dues are satisfied or proceedings are otherwise concluded.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced by this circular. It is an administrative notice confirming the completion of an existing enforcement recovery action.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements are imposed on market participants by this circular. The action is specific to Adarsh Trust in the context of the JMD Telefilms Industries Limited matter.

## Important Dates

- Date of circular: June 19, 2026
- Recovery Certificate: RC7649 of 2024 (issued in 2024, now completed)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal broader market impact. It is an enforcement closure notice specific to Adarsh Trust and the JMD Telefilms Industries Limited matter. General investors and market participants are not directly affected. The completion indicates SEBI has concluded its recovery action under this certificate.
