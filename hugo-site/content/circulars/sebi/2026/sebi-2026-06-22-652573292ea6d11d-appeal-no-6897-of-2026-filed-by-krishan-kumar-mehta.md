## Summary

This order pertains to Appeal No. 6897 of 2026 filed by Krishan Kumar Mehta before the Appellate Authority under the Right to Information Act, 2005 at SEBI. The appellant sought information about PACL certificates and associated land/property details. SEBI's Central Public Information Officer (CPIO) responded that the requested information is not available with SEBI, while directing the appellant to SEBI's website for publicly available PACL-related notices and FAQs.

## Key Points

- Appellant Krishan Kumar Mehta filed an RTI application on March 16, 2026 (received by SEBI on March 27, 2026).
- The CPIO responded by letter dated April 8, 2026, stating the information is not available with SEBI.
- The appellant filed an appeal dated May 13, 2026 (Reg. No. SEBIH/A/P/26/00074), received on May 25, 2026.
- Information sought covered PACL certificate numbers U119009xxx and U038106xxx, property possession details (Khasra No. 919 Plot No. 51 and Khasra No. 971 Plot No. 67, Village Vanndu, Tehsil Thuraiyur, District Trichy, Tamil Nadu), rights to possess or sell the properties, and sale deed details from 2000 to 2026.
- SEBI directed the appellant to its website for available PACL-related public notices, press releases, status reports, and FAQs.
- The CIC precedent (Sh. Pattipati Rama Murthy vs. CPIO, SEBI, July 8, 2013) was cited in support of SEBI's position that it cannot provide information it does not hold.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an individual appellate order under the RTI Act and does not alter any SEBI regulation or market rule.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements for market participants. The order is specific to the appellant's RTI request and SEBI's obligation to provide only information it possesses.

## Important Dates

- March 16, 2026: Original RTI application filed by appellant.
- March 27, 2026: RTI application received by SEBI.
- April 8, 2026: CPIO response issued.
- May 13, 2026: First appeal filed by appellant.
- May 25, 2026: Appeal received by Office of Appellate Authority.

## Impact Assessment

This order has no market-wide impact. It is an individual RTI appellate matter involving a PACL investor seeking property and certificate information. SEBI confirmed it does not hold the specific records requested, consistent with its established position in similar PACL-related RTI matters. No trading, listing, or operational implications for market participants.
