## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order for Recovery Certificate No. 4498 of 2022 against Vaishali Natvarlal Thakkar in the matter of SMS Techsoft (India) Ltd. This release order signifies that the dues or penalties specified under the recovery certificate have been satisfied, and the enforcement/recovery proceedings initiated against the noticee are being formally closed.

## Key Points

- SEBI issued Recovery Certificate No. 4498 of 2022 against Vaishali Natvarlal Thakkar in connection with the SMS Techsoft (India) Ltd. matter.
- The present order is a Release Order, indicating that the recovery proceedings have been completed — the outstanding dues have been paid or the liability has otherwise been discharged.
- This order formally releases the attachment or recovery action initiated under the original certificate.
- The order is issued under SEBI's enforcement and recovery proceedings framework.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an administrative closure order under existing SEBI recovery proceedings provisions.

## Compliance Requirements

- No further compliance obligations arise from this order for Vaishali Natvarlal Thakkar with respect to Recovery Certificate No. 4498 of 2022.
- The noticee's dues or penalties under the said certificate are treated as fully discharged.

## Important Dates

- **Recovery Certificate No. 4498 issued:** 2022
- **Release Order date:** March 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative order with no broader market or investor impact. It closes out a specific enforcement action against an individual in the SMS Techsoft (India) Ltd. matter. No trading restrictions, market operations, or systemic compliance changes are involved.
