## Summary

The SEBI Appellate Authority disposed of Appeal No. 6927 of 2026 filed by Harsh Shah under the Right to Information Act, 2005. The appellant had sought confirmation of whether investigations were pending against three named individuals (Harsh Rajesh Shah, Foram Harsh Shah, and Asha Rajesh Shah) in connection with summonses issued by SEBI, along with copies of any related reports or orders. The CPIO had disclosed that the individuals were issued summons as part of an ongoing investigation into alleged front-running of trades of Axis Mutual Fund, involving multiple suspected entities, but declined to share further details citing the confidentiality exemption under Section 8(1)(h) of the RTI Act. The Appellate Authority concurred with the CPIO's reasoning and upheld the denial of information, noting that disclosure could impede the ongoing investigation.

## Key Points

- Appellant sought details on pending SEBI investigations against three individuals connected to an alleged Axis Mutual Fund front-running case.
- SEBI confirmed the individuals were summoned on January 26, 2025 under reference numbers SEBI/HO/IVD/ID04/2025/648/1, /644/1, and /636/1.
- The investigation covers alleged front-running of trades of Axis Mutual Fund involving multiple suspected entities.
- CPIO invoked Section 8(1)(h) of the RTI Act to withhold investigation-related documents, citing potential to impede the ongoing probe.
- Appellate Authority upheld the CPIO's decision, rejecting the appellant's claim of incomplete or misleading information.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is an adjudicatory order under the RTI Act affirming existing disclosure exemptions for ongoing investigations.

## Compliance Requirements

Not applicable to market participants generally; relevant only to the RTI appellant and SEBI's internal RTI processing.

## Important Dates

- Original RTI application: May 04, 2026
- CPIO response: June 01, 2026
- Appeal filed: June 11, 2026
- Summons to individuals: January 26, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The order has no direct market impact but confirms that SEBI's investigation into alleged front-running of Axis Mutual Fund trades remains active and confidential. Market participants and investors tracking the Axis Mutual Fund front-running matter should note that no new substantive details were disclosed, and further developments will likely emerge only through formal enforcement actions or orders.
