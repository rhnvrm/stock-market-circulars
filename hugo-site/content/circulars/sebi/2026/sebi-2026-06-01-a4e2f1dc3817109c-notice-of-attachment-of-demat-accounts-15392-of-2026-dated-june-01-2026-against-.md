## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Attachment of Demat Accounts (Case No. 15392 of 2026, dated June 01, 2026) against Amit Agarwal, a defaulter identified by PAN AEEPA5456L. The attachment is being effected under Recovery Certificate No. 9106 of 2026, arising from proceedings related to dealing in illiquid stock options at BSE.

## Key Points

- Defaulter: Amit Agarwal, PAN: AEEPA5456L
- Notice Number: 15392 of 2026
- Recovery Certificate Number: 9106 of 2026
- Date of Notice: June 01, 2026
- Matter: Dealing in illiquid stock options at BSE
- Action: Attachment of demat accounts held by or on behalf of the defaulter
- Issuing authority: Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This notice is an enforcement/recovery action executed under SEBI's existing powers to attach assets of defaulters pursuant to recovery certificates issued against adjudicated violators.

## Compliance Requirements

- Depositories (NSDL/CDSL) and depository participants are directed to freeze/attach the demat accounts of Amit Agarwal (PAN: AEEPA5456L) as specified in the notice.
- No debit transactions should be permitted in the attached demat accounts until further orders.
- Relevant parties must acknowledge receipt and comply with the attachment order promptly.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: June 01, 2026
- Recovery Certificate No. 9106 of 2026 (date of issuance of underlying certificate as per SEBI records)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has no broad market or systemic impact. It is an individual enforcement action targeting the demat accounts of a single named defaulter. The matter pertains to past misconduct in illiquid stock options at BSE, a segment SEBI has been actively policing. The attachment restricts the defaulter's ability to transfer or liquidate securities held in the attached accounts, serving as a recovery mechanism for dues owed to SEBI.
