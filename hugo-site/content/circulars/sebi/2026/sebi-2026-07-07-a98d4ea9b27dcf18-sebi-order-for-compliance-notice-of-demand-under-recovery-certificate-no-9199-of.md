## Summary

SEBI's Recovery Officer has issued a Notice of Demand under Recovery Certificate No. 9199 of 2026, dated 08.07.2026, against Rajesh Pandey (PAN No. BFKPP2415Q) in the matter of Retro Green Revolution Limited. The notice directs the noticee to pay the outstanding amount specified in the recovery certificate within the stipulated period, failing which SEBI may initiate coercive recovery proceedings.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 9199 of 2026 dated 08.07.2026 has been issued by SEBI's Recovery Officer.
- The notice is addressed to Rajesh Pandey, holder of PAN BFKPP2415Q.
- The underlying matter relates to Retro Green Revolution Limited.
- The notice demands payment of the outstanding dues specified in the recovery certificate.
- Non-payment within the prescribed timeline can lead to recovery action under the modes available to SEBI's Recovery Officer.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is an individual enforcement and recovery action, not an amendment to SEBI regulations or a new regulatory framework.

## Compliance Requirements

The noticee, Rajesh Pandey, is required to pay the outstanding demand amount stated in Recovery Certificate No. 9199 of 2026 within the period specified in the notice. Failure to comply may result in recovery proceedings being initiated under the modes of recovery available to SEBI (such as attachment and sale of movable/immovable property, appointment of a receiver, or arrest and detention), as applicable under the Second Schedule to the Income-tax Act, 1961, read with Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992.

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate No. 9199 of 2026: dated 08.07.2026 (date of issue of the Notice of Demand).

## Impact Assessment

This notice has limited market-wide impact as it pertains to recovery action against a named individual in connection with Retro Green Revolution Limited, rather than a broad regulatory, trading, or listing change. It is primarily relevant to the noticee and to stakeholders tracking enforcement and recovery actions connected with Retro Green Revolution Limited.
