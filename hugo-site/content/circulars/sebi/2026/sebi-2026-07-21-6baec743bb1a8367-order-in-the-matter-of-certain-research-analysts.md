## Summary

SEBI has issued an order under Section 12(3) of the SEBI Act, 1992 read with Regulation 30A of the SEBI (Intermediaries) Regulations, 2008 against 11 Research Analysts registered under the SEBI (Research Analysts) Regulations, 2014. The order finds that each of these Noticees failed to pay the mandatory five-year renewal registration fee within the prescribed timeline, resulting in their fee remaining unpaid since dates ranging from 2020 to 2025.

## Key Points

- 11 Research Analysts (individuals and entities) were found to have not paid their renewal registration fees as required under Clause 3 of the Second Schedule to the SEBI (Research Analysts) Regulations, 2014.
- The Noticees include: 24 Carat Financial Services, Pankaj Mahasukhlal Shah, Sridhar Reddy Eragamreddy, Amar Kumar Srivastava, Lakshmishree Investment & Securities Pvt Ltd, M/s. PCS Securities Ltd., Manoj Asok Kumar, Sagar Shah, Yash Utmani, Bansal Finstock Private Limited, and Pinank.
- Renewal fee is required every five years from the date of grant of registration, to be paid within three months before expiry of the period for which fee was previously paid.
- Non-payment periods range from as early as 16 August 2021 (24 Carat Financial Services) to as recent as 6 October 2025 (Sridhar Reddy Eragamreddy).

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes; this is an enforcement order applying existing provisions of the SEBI (Research Analysts) Regulations, 2014 (Second Schedule, Clause 3) regarding mandatory five-year renewal fee payment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Registered Research Analysts must pay renewal fees every five years from the date of grant of certificate of registration, within three months before expiry of the fee-paid period.
- Failure to comply exposes Research Analysts to action under Section 12(3) of the SEBI Act read with Regulation 30A of the SEBI (Intermediaries) Regulations, 2008, which can include suspension or cancellation of registration.

## Important Dates

- Renewal fee due dates (by original registration date) ranged from 14 April 2015 to 6 October 2020 for the Noticees.
- Non-payment periods commenced between 14 April 2020 and 6 October 2025, depending on the Noticee.

## Impact Assessment

The order has a narrow, direct impact limited to the 11 named Research Analyst entities/individuals, who face potential regulatory action including suspension or cancellation of SEBI registration for non-compliance with renewal fee requirements. There is no direct impact on listed securities, exchanges, or the broader market; the matter is primarily relevant to compliance monitoring within the research analyst intermediary segment.
