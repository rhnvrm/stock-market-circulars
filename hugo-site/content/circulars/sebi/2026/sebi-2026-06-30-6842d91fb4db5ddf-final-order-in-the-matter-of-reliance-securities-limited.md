## Summary

SEBI has passed a Final Order under Section 12(3) of the SEBI Act, 1992 read with Regulation 27 of the SEBI (Intermediaries) Regulations, 2008, against Reliance Securities Limited (now IndusInd Securities Limited), a registered Stock Broker (SEBI Registration No. INZ000172433). The order follows a joint inspection conducted with stock exchanges from November 29, 2022 to December 8, 2022, covering the inspection period April 1, 2021 to November 30, 2022, concerning compliance with the SEBI (Stock Brokers) Regulations, 1992 and applicable SEBI/exchange circulars. Pursuant to enquiry proceedings under Chapter V of the Intermediaries Regulations, the Designated Authority (DA) recommended that regulatory censure be issued to the Noticee.

## Key Points

- Noticee: Reliance Securities Limited, now IndusInd Securities Limited (SEBI Reg. No. INZ000172433, PAN AADCR0206P).
- Inspection findings were communicated to the Noticee via letter dated April 22, 2024; the Noticee responded on June 25, 2024.
- A Designated Authority was appointed on September 22, 2023 to conduct enquiry proceedings under Chapter V of the Intermediaries Regulations.
- A pre-Enquiry show cause notice was issued April 22, 2024 under Regulation 25(1) of the Intermediaries Regulations.
- Enquiry proceedings were temporarily kept in abeyance due to a settlement application filed by the Noticee, which was later withdrawn.
- The Noticee filed replies dated November 4, 2024 and November 19, 2024, and availed a personal hearing on November 13, 2024.
- The DA submitted an Enquiry Report dated April 3, 2025 recommending regulatory censure under Regulation 26 of the Intermediaries Regulations.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced; this is an enforcement action applying existing provisions of the SEBI (Intermediaries) Regulations, 2008 and SEBI (Stock Brokers) Regulations, 1992 to a specific entity based on inspection findings.

## Compliance Requirements

The order pertains specifically to Reliance Securities Limited (now IndusInd Securities Limited) and does not impose new compliance obligations on other market participants. The Noticee is subject to the regulatory censure recommended by the DA following the enquiry process.

## Important Dates

- Inspection period: April 1, 2021 – November 30, 2022
- Joint inspection conducted: November 29, 2022 – December 8, 2022
- DA appointed: September 22, 2023
- Pre-Enquiry SCN issued: April 22, 2024
- Noticee response to inspection findings: June 25, 2024
- Personal hearing: November 13, 2024
- Additional submissions filed: November 19, 2024
- Enquiry Report submitted: April 3, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The order results in a regulatory censure for IndusInd Securities Limited (formerly Reliance Securities Limited) rather than a monetary penalty or suspension/cancellation of registration, indicating a relatively limited direct market impact. The action is primarily relevant for compliance and governance tracking of the broker entity and underscores SEBI's continued scrutiny of stock broker adherence to inspection and regulatory requirements.
