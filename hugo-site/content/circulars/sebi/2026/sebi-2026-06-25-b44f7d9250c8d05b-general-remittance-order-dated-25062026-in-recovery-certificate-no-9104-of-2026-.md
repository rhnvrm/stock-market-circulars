## Summary

SEBI has issued a General Remittance Order dated 25 June 2026 under Recovery Certificate No. 9104 of 2026 against Rajendra Madanlal HUF (PAN: AALHR7398H). The order relates to the entity's dealing in illiquid stock options on BSE and is directed at recovering outstanding dues imposed through prior SEBI enforcement proceedings.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 9104 of 2026 issued against Rajendra Madanlal HUF (PAN: AALHR7398H)
- The General Remittance Order is dated 25 June 2026
- The matter pertains to dealing in illiquid stock options on BSE
- SEBI is invoking its recovery powers under the SEBI Act to enforce collection of outstanding dues
- The order is directed to the concerned bank/authority to remit funds held in the entity's accounts toward recovery of dues

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is an enforcement and recovery action under existing SEBI powers to recover penalties, disgorgement amounts, or other dues from entities found in violation of securities laws relating to illiquid options trading.

## Compliance Requirements

- The named entity (Rajendra Madanlal HUF) is required to comply with the remittance/recovery directed under the order
- Banks or financial institutions holding funds of the named entity may be directed to remit specified amounts to SEBI
- No compliance obligations arise for the broader market or other participants

## Important Dates

- **Order Date:** 25 June 2026
- **Recovery Certificate:** No. 9104 of 2026

## Impact Assessment

This order has no direct impact on the broader market or other market participants. It is a targeted enforcement action against a specific HUF entity (Rajendra Madanlal HUF) for alleged dealings in illiquid stock options on BSE. Such orders are part of SEBI's ongoing efforts to curb manipulation and price rigging in the illiquid options segment. The broader significance lies in SEBI's continued enforcement posture against entities implicated in illiquid options trading, which has been a focus area of regulatory action in recent years.
