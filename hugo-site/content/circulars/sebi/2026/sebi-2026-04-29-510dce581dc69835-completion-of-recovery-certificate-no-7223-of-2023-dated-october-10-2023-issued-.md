## Summary

SEBI has issued a completion order for Recovery Certificate No. 7223 of 2023, originally dated October 10, 2023, against Sikhar Infrastructure Private Limited (PAN: AAHCS7579Q). This order signifies that the recovery proceedings initiated against the defaulter in the matter of trading in illiquid stock options at BSE have been successfully concluded.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 7223 of 2023 dated October 10, 2023 has been completed
- Defaulter: Sikhar Infrastructure Private Limited (PAN: AAHCS7579Q)
- Matter pertains to trading in illiquid stock options at BSE
- SEBI has confirmed completion of the recovery process against the defaulter
- This is an enforcement/recovery closure order, not a new regulatory directive

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This order represents the administrative closure of an existing enforcement action.

## Compliance Requirements

- No new compliance obligations for market participants arise from this order
- The defaulter (Sikhar Infrastructure Private Limited) has satisfied the outstanding recovery amount under Certificate No. 7223 of 2023
- Relevant SEBI recovery proceedings against this entity in this matter are now closed

## Important Dates

- Original Recovery Certificate Date: October 10, 2023
- Recovery Certificate Number: 7223 of 2023
- Completion Order Date: April 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This order has minimal broader market impact. It is a procedural closure confirming that SEBI's recovery action against Sikhar Infrastructure Private Limited for its role in trading illiquid stock options at BSE has been completed. The illiquid stock options segment at BSE has historically been subject to significant enforcement actions by SEBI for price manipulation and artificial trading. This completion order indicates the defaulter has discharged their financial obligations arising from such enforcement.
