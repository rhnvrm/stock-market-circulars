## Summary

The Appellate Authority under the Right to Information Act, 2005 adjudicated Appeal No. 6867 of 2026 filed by Mahesh Nagar against the CPIO, SEBI, Mumbai. The appellant sought detailed examination-related information for SEBI's Grade A Officer Recruitment 2025 Phase-II examination. The appeal was considered and the respondent's position was upheld, as the recruitment process remains ongoing and premature disclosure could disrupt it.

## Key Points

- Appellant Mahesh Nagar filed an RTI application on March 31, 2026 seeking Phase-II examination marks, section-wise breakdowns, cut-off marks, normalization methodology, evaluation scheme details, evaluated answer sheets, and rank/position information
- CPIO responded on April 17, 2026, stating that the SEBI Grade A Officer Recruitment 2025 exercise is still in progress and relevant information will be published on the SEBI website under the "career" section upon completion
- Appellant filed the appeal on May 05, 2026 (Reg. No. SEBIH/A/E/26/00165), alleging incomplete, misleading, or false information was provided
- The Appellate Authority upheld the respondent's reply, noting that premature disclosure during an ongoing recruitment process could hinder its integrity
- Cited precedent: CIC/AT/A/2008/01463 (Shri U.R.M. Raju vs. Visakhapatnam Port Trust, March 09, 2009), where the Central Information Commission held that disclosing recruitment process information before completion could disrupt the process and serve no public interest

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This order is a case-specific adjudication under the RTI Act, 2005 and does not alter any existing regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance requirements for market participants. This matter pertains solely to SEBI's internal recruitment process and the handling of RTI requests related to ongoing recruitment exercises.

## Important Dates

- March 31, 2026: RTI application filed by appellant
- April 17, 2026: CPIO, SEBI responded to the RTI application
- May 05, 2026: Appellant filed the first appeal (Reg. No. SEBIH/A/E/26/00165)

## Impact Assessment

This order has no impact on market participants, investors, or trading operations. It is confined to an internal administrative matter regarding SEBI's Grade A Officer recruitment for 2025. The decision reinforces SEBI's position that examination-related data will be disclosed only after the recruitment process concludes, consistent with established CIC jurisprudence on protecting ongoing recruitment integrity.
