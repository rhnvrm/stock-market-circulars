## Summary

SEBI issued a Notice of Demand under Recovery Certificate No. 9139 of 2026 (dated June 5, 2026) against Mr. K. Sai Prasad (PAN: AAJPS6517M), identified as a defaulter in the matter of Mediaone Global Entertainment Limited. The notice demands payment of outstanding dues as determined by SEBI's recovery proceedings.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 9139 of 2026 was issued on June 5, 2026
- Defaulter: Mr. K. Sai Prasad (PAN: AAJPS6517M)
- Matter pertains to Mediaone Global Entertainment Limited
- SEBI has initiated demand proceedings to recover dues from the defaulter
- The notice is part of SEBI's enforcement and recovery mechanism under applicable securities laws

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This notice is an enforcement action under SEBI's existing recovery powers under the SEBI Act and the SEBI (Recovery of Amounts) Rules.

## Compliance Requirements

- Mr. K. Sai Prasad is required to pay the demanded amount as specified in the Recovery Certificate within the stipulated timeframe
- Non-compliance may result in further coercive recovery measures including attachment of assets

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate Date: June 5, 2026
- Notice of Demand Issue Date: June 5, 2026 (as indicated by the document date in the URL)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has limited broader market impact. It is a targeted enforcement action against an individual defaulter in connection with Mediaone Global Entertainment Limited. The action reflects SEBI's ongoing efforts to recover dues from persons found in violation of securities regulations. Investors in Mediaone Global Entertainment Limited or the general market are not directly affected by this individual recovery proceeding.
