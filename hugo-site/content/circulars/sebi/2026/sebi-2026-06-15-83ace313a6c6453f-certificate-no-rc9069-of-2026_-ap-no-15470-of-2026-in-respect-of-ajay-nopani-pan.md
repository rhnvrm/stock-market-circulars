## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC9069 of 2026 corresponding to Adjudication Proceeding (A.P.) No. 15470 of 2026 against Ajay Nopani (PAN: ADGPN4214N) for recovery of outstanding penalty amounts arising from the Illiquid Stock Options enforcement matter.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9069 of 2026 has been issued against Ajay Nopani (PAN: ADGPN4214N).
- The certificate is linked to Adjudication Proceeding No. 15470 of 2026.
- The matter relates to the Illiquid Stock Options case, in which SEBI imposed penalties on entities found to have engaged in non-genuine trades.
- The recovery certificate enables SEBI to recover unpaid penalty dues as arrears of land revenue.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This document is an enforcement instrument under existing SEBI recovery proceedings provisions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Ajay Nopani is required to pay the outstanding dues specified in the recovery certificate.
- Failure to comply allows SEBI to pursue recovery through statutory mechanisms available under the SEBI Act.

## Important Dates

- Certificate issued: June 15, 2026
- A.P. No. 15470 of 2026 forms the basis of the recovery action.

## Impact Assessment

This is an individual enforcement action with no direct market-wide impact. It is part of SEBI's ongoing effort to enforce penalties levied in the Illiquid Stock Options manipulation case, which involved a large number of entities executing non-genuine trades in the stock options segment.
