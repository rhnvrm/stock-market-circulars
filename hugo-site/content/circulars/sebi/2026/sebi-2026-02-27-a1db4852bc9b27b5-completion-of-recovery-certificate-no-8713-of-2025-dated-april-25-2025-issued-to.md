## Summary

SEBI has issued a Completion Certificate for Recovery Certificate No. 8713 of 2025 (dated April 25, 2025) issued to Vikas Lifecare Limited, designated as a defaulter with PAN AADCA5571A. This order signifies that the recovery dues specified in the original recovery certificate have been fully satisfied or otherwise resolved.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8713 of 2025 was originally issued on April 25, 2025 against Vikas Lifecare Limited
- The defaulter entity: Vikas Lifecare Limited, PAN: AADCA5571A
- SEBI has now certified the completion/satisfaction of this recovery certificate
- The completion order is dated February 27, 2026
- The matter pertains to dues recoverable from Vikas Lifecare Limited in proceedings under the SEBI Act

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an administrative closure document confirming the satisfaction of an existing enforcement action (recovery certificate).

## Compliance Requirements

- No further compliance action is required from Vikas Lifecare Limited with respect to Recovery Certificate No. 8713 of 2025, as it has been marked as completed
- Other entities under similar recovery proceedings should ensure timely payment to avoid continued enforcement action

## Important Dates

- April 25, 2025: Original Recovery Certificate No. 8713 of 2025 issued to Vikas Lifecare Limited
- February 27, 2026: Completion/satisfaction certificate issued by SEBI

## Impact Assessment

This order has minimal broader market impact. It is an administrative closure of an enforcement action specific to Vikas Lifecare Limited. The completion indicates the company has discharged its recovery obligations under the cited certificate. Investors in VIKASLIFE should note the resolution of this particular SEBI enforcement matter, which may be viewed as a positive development for the company's regulatory standing.
