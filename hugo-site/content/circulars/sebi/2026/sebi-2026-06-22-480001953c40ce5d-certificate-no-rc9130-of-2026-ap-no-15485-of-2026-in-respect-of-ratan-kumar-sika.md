## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC9130 of 2026 under A.P. No. 15485 of 2026 against Ratan Kumar Sikaria HUF (PAN: AAOHR3968L) for recovery of dues arising from proceedings in the matter of Illiquid Stock Options. This certificate authorizes recovery proceedings against the entity as per SEBI's enforcement powers.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9130 of 2026 issued by SEBI
- Arising from A.P. No. 15485 of 2026
- Issued against Ratan Kumar Sikaria HUF (PAN: AAOHR3968L)
- Relates to the Illiquid Stock Options matter, which has been a focus of SEBI enforcement action
- Recovery certificate enables SEBI to recover penalties/dues as arrears of land revenue

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an enforcement/recovery action under existing SEBI powers to issue recovery certificates for unpaid penalties or dues.

## Compliance Requirements

- Ratan Kumar Sikaria HUF is required to pay the outstanding dues specified in the recovery certificate
- Non-compliance may result in recovery proceedings as arrears of land revenue under the SEBI Act

## Important Dates

- Certificate issued: 2026 (RC9130 of 2026)
- A.P. No. 15485 of 2026 — adjudication proceedings reference year: 2026

## Impact Assessment

This action is targeted at a single named entity (Ratan Kumar Sikaria HUF) and does not have broad market impact. It is part of SEBI's ongoing enforcement actions in the Illiquid Stock Options segment, where SEBI has been actively pursuing recovery of penalties from entities found to have engaged in non-genuine trades. The broader Illiquid Stock Options enforcement wave has affected numerous market participants.
