## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand under Recovery Certificate No. 9068 of 2026 dated March 18, 2026 against Amita Bhalla (PAN: AAAPB2585M) in the matter of ISO at BSE. This notice requires the defaulter to pay the outstanding dues as specified in the recovery certificate.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 9068 of 2026 issued on March 18, 2026
- Defaulter: Amita Bhalla (PAN: AAAPB2585M)
- Matter: ISO (Investor Securities Obligations) at BSE
- SEBI has initiated recovery proceedings to collect outstanding dues from the named defaulter
- The notice demands payment of the amount specified in the recovery certificate

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI recovery procedures pursuant to the SEBI Act and applicable regulations governing recovery of amounts from defaulters.

## Compliance Requirements

- Amita Bhalla (PAN: AAAPB2585M) is required to pay the outstanding amount as specified in Recovery Certificate No. 9068 of 2026
- The defaulter must comply with the demand within the stipulated time period mentioned in the notice
- Failure to comply may result in further coercive recovery measures including attachment of assets

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate Date: March 18, 2026
- Notice of Demand Issued: March 18, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This order has no broad market impact. It is a targeted enforcement/recovery action against a specific individual defaulter. The proceeding is administrative in nature and concerns recovery of dues in the matter of ISO at BSE. Market participants and investors are not directly affected by this notice.
