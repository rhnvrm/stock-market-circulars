## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order for Recovery Certificate No. 5552 of 2022 against Mr. S Rajaganesh in connection with the matter of SMS Techsoft (India) Ltd. This Release Order formally discharges the recovery certificate and releases any attachments or liens placed on the respondent's assets under the recovery proceedings. A companion Completion Order for the same Recovery Certificate was issued simultaneously on the same date, together marking the full conclusion of enforcement recovery proceedings against Mr. S Rajaganesh.

## Key Points

- SEBI issues Release Order for Recovery Certificate No. 5552 of 2022 against Mr. S Rajaganesh
- The Release Order discharges the recovery certificate and releases attached assets
- Issued in the matter of SMS Techsoft (India) Ltd.
- A companion Completion Order (circular ID: 797b7a0849a5ad9b) was issued on the same date for the same recovery certificate
- The simultaneous issuance of both orders indicates full resolution of the recovery proceedings, likely through payment of dues or successful asset recovery
- Published under SEBI's enforcement/recovery proceedings section dated March 2, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is a purely administrative order concluding existing enforcement proceedings under Recovery Certificate No. 5552 of 2022.

## Compliance Requirements

This Release Order does not impose any new compliance obligations on market participants. The recovery proceedings against Mr. S Rajaganesh in the SMS Techsoft (India) Ltd. matter have been fully resolved. No further action is required from other market participants or entities.

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate Issued: 2022 (No. 5552 of 2022)
- Release Order Date: March 2, 2026
- Companion Completion Order Date: March 2, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This order has minimal impact on the broader market. It is an individual enforcement action closure specific to Mr. S Rajaganesh in the SMS Techsoft (India) Ltd. matter. The concurrent issuance of both a Release Order and a Completion Order confirms that the recovery proceedings have been fully and finally resolved. There is no systemic, sector-wide, or broader investor significance to this administrative closure order.
