## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order under Recovery Certificate No. RC6856 of 2023 in respect of Gitesh Tikamani HUF (PAN: AAFHG0088M) in the matter of Illiquid Stock Options. This order indicates that the recovery proceedings against the named entity have been completed or that attached assets are being released following satisfaction of dues.

## Key Points

- Release Order issued under Recovery Certificate No. RC6856 of 2023
- Entity involved: Gitesh Tikamani HUF (PAN: AAFHG0088M)
- Matter pertains to SEBI's enforcement action in Illiquid Stock Options cases
- Release of previously attached bank or demat accounts of the entity
- Signals completion of recovery proceedings for this specific certificate

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an administrative enforcement order specific to the named entity and does not alter existing regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Banks, depositories, or financial institutions holding attachments under RC6856 of 2023 against Gitesh Tikamani HUF are directed to release the attached assets upon receipt of this order.
- Relevant parties must comply promptly with the release instructions as directed by SEBI's Recovery Officer.

## Important Dates

- Release Order Date: May 2026
- Recovery Certificate Reference: RC6856 of 2023

## Impact Assessment

This order has minimal broader market impact as it pertains solely to the enforcement proceedings against one HUF entity (Gitesh Tikamani HUF) in the Illiquid Stock Options matter. It represents the conclusion of recovery action under this specific certificate, releasing the entity's assets from attachment. No systemic or sector-wide implications are expected.
