## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand dated 07.07.2026 under Recovery Certificate No. 9210 of 2026 against Amesh Jaiswal (PAN: AFZPJ0067Q) in connection with the matter of Retro Green Revolution Limited. The notice directs the recovery of outstanding dues from the noticee as part of SEBI's ongoing recovery proceedings.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 9210 of 2026 was issued on 07.07.2026 against Amesh Jaiswal (PAN: AFZPJ0067Q).
- The action arises out of the matter concerning Retro Green Revolution Limited.
- The notice of demand formally calls upon the noticee to pay the amounts specified in the underlying recovery certificate.
- Non-compliance with the notice may lead to further coercive recovery measures under the applicable recovery proceedings (such as attachment of bank accounts, movable/immovable property, or other assets).

## Regulatory Changes

This is an individual enforcement action rather than a change to SEBI regulations. It reflects the exercise of SEBI's existing powers to recover dues (such as penalties, disgorgement amounts, or other statutory dues) from persons found liable in connection with violations related to Retro Green Revolution Limited.

## Compliance Requirements

- Amesh Jaiswal is required to pay the demanded amount as specified in Recovery Certificate No. 9210 of 2026 within the timeframe stipulated in the notice.
- Failure to comply may result in SEBI/Recovery Officer initiating further recovery action, including attachment and sale of assets, as provided under the recovery proceedings framework.

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate No. 9210 of 2026: dated 07.07.2026
- Notice of Demand: issued pursuant to the above certificate

## Impact Assessment

This notice primarily affects the individual named (Amesh Jaiswal) and is part of SEBI's enforcement and recovery mechanism related to the Retro Green Revolution Limited matter. It does not directly impact listed securities trading, market operations, or broader market participants, but it is relevant for tracking SEBI's enforcement actions and compliance history associated with the parties and entity involved.
