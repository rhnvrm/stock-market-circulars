## Summary

SEBI has issued a corrigendum order dated January 08, 2026 (Order No. Order/AK/DS/2025-26/31949) to correct an error in Adjudication Order No. Order/SM/AE/2022-23/19751-19761 dated September 28, 2022. The PAN of Birla Pacific Medspa Limited was incorrectly mentioned as AAAFK1583C in the original order and has been corrected to AADCB5167F. All other contents of the original adjudication order remain unchanged.

## Key Points

- Corrigendum order issued under Section 15-I of SEBI Act, 1992 and Section 23-I of Securities Contracts (Regulation) Act, 1956
- Original adjudication order dated September 28, 2022 covered 11 entities in the matter of Birla Pacific Medspa Limited
- Incorrect PAN: AAAFK1583C
- Correct PAN: AADCB5167F
- Adjudicating Officer Amit Kapoor appointed on December 30, 2025 to issue the corrigendum
- No changes to any other content, findings, or penalties in the original adjudication order

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is purely an administrative correction to rectify a typographical error in the PAN number.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. The corrigendum order is to be read as part of the original adjudication order dated September 28, 2022. Copy of this order has been sent to Birla Pacific Medspa Limited and SEBI.

## Important Dates

- **Original Adjudication Order Date**: September 28, 2022
- **AO Appointment Date**: December 30, 2025
- **Corrigendum Order Date**: January 08, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a purely administrative correction with minimal market or operational impact. The corrigendum does not alter any substantive findings, penalties, or obligations from the original adjudication order. It only corrects a clerical error in the PAN number of Birla Pacific Medspa Limited for accurate record-keeping purposes.
