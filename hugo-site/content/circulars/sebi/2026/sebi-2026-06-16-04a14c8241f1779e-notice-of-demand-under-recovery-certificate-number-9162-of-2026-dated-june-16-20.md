## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand under Recovery Certificate number 9162 of 2026, dated June 16, 2026, to Rashmi Chandak (PAN: AEKPC4067M), identified as a defaulter in the matter of ISO at BSE – Rashmi Chandak. The notice demands payment of outstanding dues as directed under the recovery proceedings initiated by SEBI.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate number: 9162 of 2026
- Date of issuance: June 16, 2026
- Defaulter: Rashmi Chandak (PAN: AEKPC4067M)
- Matter: ISO at BSE – Rashmi Chandak
- SEBI has formally demanded recovery of dues from the named individual

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This circular represents enforcement of an existing SEBI order through the recovery certificate mechanism under the SEBI Act.

## Compliance Requirements

- Rashmi Chandak is required to satisfy the demand raised under Recovery Certificate 9162 of 2026 within the stipulated timeframe stated in the notice.
- Non-compliance may result in further enforcement action including attachment of assets.

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate date: June 16, 2026
- Payment deadline: As specified in the demand notice served to the defaulter

## Impact Assessment

This is an individual enforcement action with no direct impact on broader markets or other market participants. The notice is directed solely at the named defaulter, Rashmi Chandak, and pertains to recovery of dues arising from the ISO at BSE matter. Market impact is negligible.
