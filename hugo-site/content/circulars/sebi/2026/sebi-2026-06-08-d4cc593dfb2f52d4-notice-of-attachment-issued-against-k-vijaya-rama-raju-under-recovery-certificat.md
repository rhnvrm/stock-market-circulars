## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Attachment against K. Vijaya Rama Raju under Recovery Certificate No. 4877 of 2022 in connection with enforcement proceedings in the matter of Ranklin Solutions Limited. This action is part of SEBI's recovery proceedings to enforce outstanding dues or penalties imposed on the respondent.

## Key Points

- Notice of Attachment issued against K. Vijaya Rama Raju (individual respondent)
- Recovery Certificate No. 4877 of 2022 is the basis for this attachment action
- Proceedings relate to the matter of Ranklin Solutions Limited
- SEBI's Recovery Officer is empowered to attach property/assets of the defaulter to recover outstanding dues
- A companion Recovery Certificate No. 4876 of 2022 has also been issued against the same respondent in the same matter

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an enforcement/recovery action under existing SEBI (Recovery of Amounts) Rules and the SEBI Act, which empower SEBI to issue recovery certificates and attach assets of defaulters.

## Compliance Requirements

- K. Vijaya Rama Raju is required to comply with the terms of the attachment notice
- Any third parties holding attached assets must not deal with them in contravention of the notice
- The respondent may approach SEBI or the appropriate forum to contest the attachment if grounds exist

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate No. 4877 issued: 2022
- Notice of Attachment issued: June 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a targeted enforcement action against a specific individual and has no direct impact on broader market participants or listed securities. It reflects SEBI's ongoing use of recovery proceedings to enforce penalty orders and is part of a series of attachment notices issued in the Ranklin Solutions Limited matter.
