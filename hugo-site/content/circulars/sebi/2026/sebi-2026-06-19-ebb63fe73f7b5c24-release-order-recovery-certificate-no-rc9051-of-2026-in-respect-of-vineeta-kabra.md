## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order for Recovery Certificate No. RC9051 of 2026 pertaining to Vineeta Kabra (PAN: ABNPL7384C) in the matter of Illiquid Stock Options. A release order in this context typically signifies that the recovery proceedings initiated against the individual have been concluded, either due to full payment of the penalty/dues or other regulatory settlement.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9051 of 2026 has been released by SEBI.
- The order pertains to Vineeta Kabra (PAN: ABNPL7384C).
- The matter arises from SEBI's enforcement action in the Illiquid Stock Options segment.
- A release order indicates the recovery process against this individual has been completed or settled.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced by this order. It is an enforcement/recovery closure action under existing SEBI recovery mechanisms.

## Compliance Requirements

- No new compliance obligations are imposed on market participants by this order.
- The release is specific to Vineeta Kabra and concludes her outstanding recovery proceedings.

## Important Dates

- Date of Release Order: 2026-06-19
- Recovery Certificate: RC9051 of 2026

## Impact Assessment

This order has no direct impact on broader market participants or trading operations. It is an individual enforcement closure in SEBI's ongoing crackdown on illiquid stock options manipulation. The illiquid stock options matter has historically involved numerous entities found to have engaged in non-genuine trades, and this release order represents one such case being resolved.
