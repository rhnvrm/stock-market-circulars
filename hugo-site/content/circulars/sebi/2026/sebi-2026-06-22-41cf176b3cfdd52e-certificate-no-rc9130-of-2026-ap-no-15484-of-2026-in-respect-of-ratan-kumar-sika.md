## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC9130 of 2026 under Adjudication Proceeding No. 15484 of 2026 against Ratan Kumar Sikaria HUF (PAN: AAOHR3968L) in connection with the matter of trading in illiquid stock options. This certificate authorises recovery of the penalty/dues ordered against the entity.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9130 of 2026 issued by SEBI
- Adjudication Proceeding (AP) No. 15484 of 2026
- Entity: Ratan Kumar Sikaria HUF (Hindu Undivided Family)
- PAN: AAOHR3968L
- Matter relates to trading in illiquid stock options
- Recovery certificate enables SEBI to recover outstanding dues as arrears of land revenue

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an enforcement/recovery action under existing SEBI adjudication powers pursuant to the SEBI Act, 1992 and the Securities and Exchange Board of India (Procedure for Holding Inquiry and Imposing Penalties) Rules, 1995.

## Compliance Requirements

- Ratan Kumar Sikaria HUF is required to pay the penalty/dues as specified in the underlying adjudication order
- Failure to comply with the recovery certificate may result in recovery of dues through the Collector/District Magistrate as arrears of land revenue under Section 28A of the SEBI Act

## Important Dates

- Certificate issued: June 2026
- The underlying adjudication order (AP No. 15484 of 2026) would have specified the payment deadline

## Impact Assessment

This recovery certificate has no direct broad market impact. It is targeted enforcement against a single HUF entity found to have participated in illiquid stock options trading — a segment SEBI has been actively investigating for potential tax evasion and artificial loss/gain generation schemes. The action reinforces SEBI's continued enforcement posture in the illiquid stock options space and serves as a deterrent to similar market participants.
