## Summary

SEBI has issued a Remittance Advice against Money Mishra Overseas Pvt. Ltd. (PAN: AAJCM8612N), designated as a defaulter, in connection with the transfer of mutual fund units by Money Mishra Financial Services and others. This action is taken under Recovery Certificate No. 8597 of 2025, directing remittance of dues owed to SEBI.

## Key Points

- Defaulter entity: Money Mishra Overseas Pvt. Ltd. (PAN: AAJCM8612N)
- Recovery Certificate No.: 8597 of 2025
- The action pertains to the transfer of mutual fund units by Money Mishra Financial Services and associated parties
- SEBI has issued this remittance advice as part of its recovery proceedings against the defaulter
- The circular instructs relevant parties (banks, depositories, RTAs) to remit funds as directed under the recovery certificate

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is an enforcement/recovery action under SEBI's existing powers to recover dues from defaulters via Recovery Certificates issued under the SEBI Act.

## Compliance Requirements

- Banks, depositories, and other intermediaries holding assets of Money Mishra Overseas Pvt. Ltd. are directed to comply with the remittance advice
- Relevant RTAs and fund houses holding mutual fund units in the name of the defaulter or associated entities should act on SEBI's instructions promptly
- Non-compliance with the remittance advice may attract further regulatory action

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate No. 8597 issued: 2025
- Remittance Advice issued: June 1, 2026
- Compliance expected promptly upon receipt of the advice

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited broad market impact as it targets a specific defaulter entity (Money Mishra Overseas Pvt. Ltd.) and the mutual fund units associated with Money Mishra Financial Services and related parties. It reflects SEBI's ongoing enforcement of recovery proceedings and demonstrates the regulator's use of recovery certificates to recover dues. Mutual fund investors and the general market are not directly affected.
