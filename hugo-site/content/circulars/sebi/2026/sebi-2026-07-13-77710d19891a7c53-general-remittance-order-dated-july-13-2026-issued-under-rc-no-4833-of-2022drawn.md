## Summary

SEBI has issued a General Remittance Order dated July 13, 2026, under Recovery Certificate (RC) No. 4833 of 2022, drawn against In House Productions Ltd. The order relates to recovery proceedings arising from the entity's failure to obtain SCORES (SEBI Complaints Redress System) authentication, a mandatory compliance requirement for entities registered with or regulated by SEBI.

## Key Points

- The order is a General Remittance Order issued under Recovery Certificate No. 4833 of 2022.
- The entity involved is In House Productions Ltd.
- The underlying violation pertains to failure to obtain SCORES authentication, a SEBI-mandated grievance redressal registration requirement.
- This order forms part of SEBI's recovery/enforcement process for previously imposed penalties or dues.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This is an enforcement action remitting an existing recovery certificate for continued recovery proceedings against the named entity.

## Compliance Requirements

Entities regulated by SEBI must register and maintain active authentication on the SCORES platform to enable investor grievance redressal. Failure to comply can result in penalties and subsequent recovery action, as seen in this order against In House Productions Ltd.

## Important Dates

- Order date: July 13, 2026
- Original Recovery Certificate: RC No. 4833 of 2022

## Impact Assessment

This order is specific to a single non-compliant entity and does not have broader market-wide implications. It serves as a reminder to SEBI-regulated entities of the importance of maintaining SCORES authentication to avoid penalty and recovery proceedings.
