## Summary

SEBI has completed Recovery Certificate no. RC7663 of 2024 in respect of Madhav Trust (PAN: AABTM6615Q) in the matter of JMD Telefilms Industries Ltd. This notification signifies the conclusion of the recovery proceedings initiated against Madhav Trust under SEBI's enforcement mechanism.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate no. RC7663 of 2024 has been formally completed.
- The certificate was issued against Madhav Trust (PAN: AABTM6615Q).
- The underlying enforcement matter relates to JMD Telefilms Industries Ltd.
- Completion of a recovery certificate typically indicates that dues payable to SEBI have been recovered or the proceeding has otherwise been concluded.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this circular. This is an administrative closure notice for a specific recovery proceeding.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance obligations are imposed on market participants by this circular. The completion of the recovery certificate closes out the enforcement obligation on Madhav Trust with respect to RC7663 of 2024.

## Important Dates

- Date of completion notice: 2026-06-19
- Recovery Certificate reference: RC7663 of 2024

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact. It is an administrative enforcement closure notice specific to Madhav Trust and the JMD Telefilms Industries Ltd. matter. Investors and market participants are not directly affected. The completion of the recovery certificate may be relevant to parties monitoring SEBI enforcement actions against entities associated with JMD Telefilms Industries Ltd.
