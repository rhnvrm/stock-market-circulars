## Summary

SEBI has cancelled Recovery Certificate No. 8887 of 2025 dated September 02, 2025, which was issued against Mr. Madhav Tiwari (PAN: AGZPT6444J), the proprietor of Divinecommodity.co., in connection with the matter of unregistered investment advisory activities. The cancellation order was issued on May 20, 2026.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8887 of 2025 (dated September 02, 2025) issued to Mr. Madhav Tiwari stands cancelled.
- Mr. Madhav Tiwari was the proprietor of Divinecommodity.co. and was identified as a defaulter (PAN: AGZPT6444J).
- The original enforcement action pertained to the operation of unregistered investment advisory services by Mr. Madhav Tiwari through Divinecommodity.co.
- SEBI's Recovery Certificates are instruments used to recover outstanding dues/penalties from defaulters; cancellation typically indicates dues have been settled or the certificate is no longer enforceable.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This order is an administrative act concluding the recovery proceedings under the earlier enforcement action for unregistered investment advisory.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements for market participants arise from this order. The order is specific to Mr. Madhav Tiwari and the recovery proceedings against him.

## Important Dates

- **Original Recovery Certificate Date**: September 02, 2025 (RC No. 8887 of 2025)
- **Cancellation Order Date**: May 20, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This order has no broader market or operational impact. It is an individual enforcement matter where SEBI cancels a recovery certificate issued against an unregistered investment adviser. Market participants, brokers, and investors are not directly affected. The cancellation closes the recovery proceedings against Mr. Madhav Tiwari under this particular certificate.
