## Summary

SEBI's Recovery Officer has passed a Release Order in respect of Recovery Certificate No. RC4167 of 2021, which had been issued against Economy Sales Private Limited (PAN: AACCE1151B) as part of recovery proceedings connected to the matter of PS IT Infrastructure and Services Limited. The release order effectively discharges or closes the recovery action against this specific entity under the referenced certificate.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC4167 of 2021 was issued against Economy Sales Private Limited (PAN: AACCE1151B).
- The certificate was issued in connection with dues/liabilities arising in the matter of PS IT Infrastructure and Services Limited.
- SEBI has now passed an order releasing the recovery certificate against this entity, indicating closure of the recovery action for the stated amount/party.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced; this is an administrative/enforcement order under SEBI's recovery proceedings framework (Section 28A of the SEBI Act read with the Income Tax Act recovery provisions applied by SEBI Recovery Officers).

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance obligations are imposed on market participants generally. Economy Sales Private Limited and any associated parties should note the closure of this specific recovery certificate for their records.

## Important Dates

Specific dates of the underlying recovery certificate (2021) and the release order are referenced in the source document; no further compliance deadlines are indicated.

## Impact Assessment

This order is narrow in scope, applying to a specific private, unlisted entity involved in a recovery matter tied to PS IT Infrastructure and Services Limited. It has no direct bearing on listed securities, trading activity, or the broader market, and is primarily relevant for enforcement record-keeping purposes.
