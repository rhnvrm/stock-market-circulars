## Summary

The SEBI Appellate Authority under the RTI Act dismissed Appeal No. 6754 of 2026 filed by Peeyush Nigam. The appellant had sought information about the validity and status of environmental consents (under the Water Act and Air Act) disclosed in the Draft Red Herring Prospectus (DRHP) of Renny Strips Limited, filed on December 19, 2025. SEBI's CPIO responded that the DRHP was still under examination and no observation had been issued, making the requested information unavailable. The Appellate Authority found the response adequate and dismissed the appeal.

## Key Points

- Appellant Peeyush Nigam filed an RTI application on January 26, 2026 seeking details on environmental approvals in Renny Strips Limited's DRHP
- Information sought included validity periods of Water Act and Air Act consents for manufacturing units and whether any consents had expired as of the DRHP filing date (December 19, 2025)
- CPIO responded on February 13, 2026 stating the DRHP is still under examination and SEBI is yet to issue its observation
- Appellant filed appeal (Reg. No. SEBIH/A/E/26/00069) on February 13, 2026 contending the response did not clarify whether SEBI had issued any observation or notice
- Appellate Authority found no deficiency in the CPIO's response and dismissed the appeal

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine RTI appellate order with no amendments to existing regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements for market participants. The order is specific to the RTI appeal and does not impose obligations on any entity other than the parties involved.

## Important Dates

- January 26, 2026: RTI application filed by appellant
- December 19, 2025: Date of DRHP filing by Renny Strips Limited with SEBI
- February 13, 2026: CPIO response and appeal filing date
- March 05, 2026: Appellate Authority order date (appeal dismissed)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This order concerns an individual RTI appeal and does not affect trading, listing requirements, or broader compliance obligations. The DRHP of Renny Strips Limited remains under SEBI examination, and no observation letter has been issued as of the order date.
