## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Attachment dated July 22, 2026, drawn against Nayan Mahendrabhai Thakkar under Recovery Certificate (RC) No. 9183 of 2026. The notice arises from SEBI's recovery proceedings in the matter concerning trading activities of certain entities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now renamed DU Digital Global Limited). The Recovery Officer has invoked attachment proceedings to recover outstanding dues (such as penalties, disgorgement amounts, or other regulatory dues) confirmed against the noticee.

## Key Points

- The notice is issued under Recovery Certificate No. 9183 of 2026, indicating a formal recovery certificate has been drawn up against the defaulter for unpaid regulatory dues.
- The action targets Nayan Mahendrabhai Thakkar in connection with an underlying SEBI order relating to trading activities of certain entities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited).
- Notices of Attachment are typically issued by SEBI's Recovery Officer under powers analogous to those in the Second Schedule to the Income Tax Act, 1961, read with Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992, to attach movable/immovable property, bank accounts, or other assets of a defaulter.
- This action is part of SEBI's enforcement mechanism to recover confirmed dues where the noticee has failed to pay amounts due under an earlier SEBI order (e.g., penalty, disgorgement, or other monetary directions).

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced; this is an enforcement/recovery action under existing SEBI recovery proceedings provisions (Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992).

## Compliance Requirements

- The noticee (Nayan Mahendrabhai Thakkar) is required to comply with the attachment notice, which may restrict dealing with the attached assets/accounts pending recovery of dues.
- Banks, depositories, or other intermediaries holding assets of the noticee may be required to act in accordance with the attachment order and refrain from permitting withdrawal or transfer of attached assets.

## Important Dates

- Notice of Attachment date: July 22, 2026
- Underlying Recovery Certificate: RC No. 9183 of 2026

## Impact Assessment

The direct market impact is limited, as this is an individual enforcement/recovery action rather than a market-wide regulatory change. However, it underscores SEBI's continued enforcement of penalties/disgorgement orders related to irregular trading activities in DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited), which may be of interest to investors and compliance teams tracking enforcement outcomes tied to the scrip.
