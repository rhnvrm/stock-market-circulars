## Summary

SEBI has passed Adjudication Order No. ORDER/AK/RK/2026-27/32437-32445 against Prime Focus Limited (PFL) and nine of its officials for alleged violations of the SEBI Act, 1992, PFUTP Regulations, 2003, and LODR Regulations, 2015. The violations relate to the alleged failure to prepare financial statements in accordance with applicable Accounting Standards during the Investigation Period of FY 2019-20 to FY 2021-22.

## Key Points

- Nine noticees named including Prime Focus Limited, two promoter-directors (Naresh Malhotra and Namit Malhotra), Non-Executive Director, CFO, and four Independent Directors/Audit Committee members
- Investigation covered FY 2019-20 to FY 2021-22 for alleged non-compliance with applicable Accounting Standards
- Violations alleged under SEBI Act, 1992; PFUTP Regulations, 2003; and LODR Regulations, 2015
- Show Cause Notice issued December 11, 2023 to all noticees
- Penalties adjudicated under Sections 15HA and 15HB of the SEBI Act
- PFL is listed on both BSE and NSE
- Multiple adjudicating officers were appointed over the course of proceedings; undersigned AO appointed via order dated November 22, 2024

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This order applies existing provisions of:
- Section 15-I of the SEBI Act, 1992
- Rule 5 of SEBI (Procedure for Holding Inquiry and Imposing Penalties) Rules, 1995
- SEBI (PFUTP) Regulations, 2003
- SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015

## Compliance Requirements

- Listed companies must ensure financial statements are prepared strictly in accordance with applicable Accounting Standards
- Directors, promoters, CFOs, and Audit Committee members bear responsibility for financial disclosure integrity under LODR Regulations
- Independent directors and audit committee members must exercise due diligence in overseeing financial reporting
- Non-compliance with disclosure obligations under LODR can attract penalties under Sections 15HA and 15HB of the SEBI Act

## Important Dates

- Investigation Period: FY 2019-20 to FY 2021-22
- Show Cause Notice issued: December 11, 2023
- Current AO appointed: November 22, 2024
- Order Number: ORDER/AK/RK/2026-27/32437-32445 (FY 2026-27)

## Impact Assessment

This order has significant implications for Prime Focus Limited and its officials. The involvement of promoters (Naresh Malhotra and Namit Malhotra), the CFO (Nishant Avinash Fadia), and independent directors/audit committee members signals broad accountability across the corporate governance structure. Penalties under Sections 15HA (fraud and unfair trade practices) and 15HB (failure to comply with provisions) can be substantial. The order reinforces SEBI's scrutiny of financial reporting quality and the responsibilities of audit committees at listed entities. Investors in PFL (listed on BSE and NSE) should note the regulatory risk associated with the company's past financial disclosures.
