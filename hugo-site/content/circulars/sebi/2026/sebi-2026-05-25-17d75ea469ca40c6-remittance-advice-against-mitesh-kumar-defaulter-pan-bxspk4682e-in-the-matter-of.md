## Summary

SEBI has issued a Remittance Advice against Mitesh Kumar (PAN: BXSPK4682E), identified as a defaulter in the matter of dealings in illiquid stock options on BSE. This notice is issued under Recovery Certificate No. 7305 of 2023, directing recovery of dues owed to SEBI.

## Key Points

- Defaulter: Mitesh Kumar (PAN: BXSPK4682E)
- Recovery Certificate No. 7305 of 2023
- Matter relates to dealings in illiquid stock options on BSE
- SEBI has issued a formal remittance advice to recover outstanding dues
- The action is part of SEBI's enforcement proceedings against manipulation in illiquid stock options

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement/recovery action under existing SEBI recovery mechanisms.

## Compliance Requirements

- The defaulter (Mitesh Kumar) is directed to remit the outstanding dues as specified in the recovery certificate.
- Relevant banks/depositories/registrars may be directed to facilitate recovery as per SEBI's recovery powers under the SEBI Act.

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate Date: 2023 (Certificate No. 7305 of 2023)
- Remittance Advice issued: May 2026

## Impact Assessment

This remittance advice is targeted at a specific individual defaulter and has no direct broader market impact. It reflects SEBI's continued enforcement actions in the illiquid stock options segment on BSE, where manipulative trading practices have been a focus of regulatory scrutiny. The action reinforces SEBI's resolve to recover dues from entities found to have engaged in unauthorized or manipulative market practices.
