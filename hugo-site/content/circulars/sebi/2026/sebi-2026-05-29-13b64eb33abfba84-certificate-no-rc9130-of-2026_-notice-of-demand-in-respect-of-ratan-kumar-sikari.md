## Summary

SEBI has issued Certificate No. RC9130 of 2026 as a Notice of Demand against Ratan Kumar Sikaria HUF (PAN: AAOHR3968L) in connection with the Illiquid Stock Options matter. This certificate authorises the recovery of dues determined through prior adjudication or order proceedings against the entity.

## Key Points

- Certificate No. RC9130 of 2026 issued by SEBI under recovery proceedings
- Demand raised against Ratan Kumar Sikaria HUF (PAN: AAOHR3968L)
- Matter pertains to trading in Illiquid Stock Options
- This is a recovery/demand notice following an earlier SEBI adjudication or order
- The HUF is required to pay the demanded amount to SEBI

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This document represents an enforcement action under existing SEBI (Recovery of Money) Regulations, giving effect to a previously determined penalty or disgorgement amount.

## Compliance Requirements

- Ratan Kumar Sikaria HUF must satisfy the demand specified in Certificate No. RC9130 of 2026
- Payment must be made to SEBI within the stipulated time period as indicated in the notice
- Failure to comply may result in further recovery proceedings including attachment of assets

## Important Dates

- Certificate issued in 2026 (May)
- Compliance deadline: As specified in the notice of demand (refer to the PDF for the exact date)

## Impact Assessment

This enforcement action is limited to a single entity — Ratan Kumar Sikaria HUF — and has no direct impact on the broader market. It is part of SEBI's ongoing enforcement drive against entities involved in manipulation of illiquid stock options on Indian exchanges. Market participants and retail investors are not directly affected. The action reinforces SEBI's commitment to pursuing recovery of dues from entities found guilty of market misconduct in the illiquid stock options segment.
