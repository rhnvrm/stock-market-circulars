## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order in favour of Ravinder Kumar Jain (PAN: ABUPJ8211B), who was designated as a defaulter in the matter of ISO at BSE. The release order formally discharges the defaulter from obligations under Recovery Certificate No. 8977 of 2025, which was originally issued on December 19, 2025. This order is issued in conjunction with the completion of recovery proceedings under the said certificate.

## Key Points

- Release Order issued to Ravinder Kumar Jain (PAN: ABUPJ8211B)
- Relates to Recovery Certificate No. 8977 of 2025 dated December 19, 2025
- Matter pertains to ISO at BSE (Bombay Stock Exchange)
- Formally releases the defaulter from recovery certificate obligations
- Issued by SEBI's Recovery Officer following completion of proceedings
- Companion document to the Completion Notice for the same recovery certificate

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a concluding administrative order under SEBI's enforcement and recovery framework, formally closing the recovery action against the named individual.

## Compliance Requirements

No fresh compliance requirements for market participants. This release order is relevant only to the named defaulter (Ravinder Kumar Jain, PAN: ABUPJ8211B) and confirms that the recovery obligation has been discharged.

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate originally issued: December 19, 2025
- Recovery Certificate No.: 8977 of 2025
- Release Order issued: May 5, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This release order has minimal market impact. It represents the administrative closure of enforcement action against a specific individual defaulter in the ISO at BSE matter. The issuance of a release order indicates that SEBI has fully enforced its earlier recovery certificate and the defaulter has satisfied the outstanding obligations. There is no impact on trading, listing requirements, or compliance obligations for broader market participants.
