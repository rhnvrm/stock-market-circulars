## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Attachment of Bank Accounts (Case No. 15382 of 2026) dated May 26, 2026, against Best Healthcare Private Limited (PAN: AADCB1811A) as defaulter, in the matter of Fortis Healthcare Limited. The action is taken under Recovery Certificate No. 8725 of 2025, directing the attachment of the defaulter's bank accounts to recover dues owed to SEBI.

## Key Points

- **Defaulter:** Best Healthcare Private Limited (PAN: AADCB1811A)
- **Case Number:** 15382 of 2026
- **Recovery Certificate:** No. 8725 of 2025
- **Matter:** Fortis Healthcare Limited
- **Action:** Attachment of bank accounts of the defaulter
- **Issuing Authority:** Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI)
- **Date of Notice:** May 26, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework introduced. This is an enforcement action under SEBI's existing recovery and attachment powers pursuant to the SEBI Act and the SEBI (Recovery of Penalties) Rules.

## Compliance Requirements

- Banks holding accounts of Best Healthcare Private Limited are directed not to allow any debit or withdrawal from the attached accounts.
- The defaulter (Best Healthcare Private Limited) is prohibited from operating the attached bank accounts.
- Banks and the defaulter must comply immediately upon receipt of this notice and inform SEBI of the account balances.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** May 26, 2026
- **Recovery Certificate Date:** 2025 (No. 8725 of 2025)

## Impact Assessment

The impact is confined to Best Healthcare Private Limited and the banks holding its accounts. The action is a standard enforcement measure by SEBI to recover dues in the Fortis Healthcare Limited matter. Shareholders of Fortis Healthcare Limited (NSE/BSE: FORTIS) are not directly impacted by this recovery proceeding against the defaulter entity. Broader market impact is negligible.
