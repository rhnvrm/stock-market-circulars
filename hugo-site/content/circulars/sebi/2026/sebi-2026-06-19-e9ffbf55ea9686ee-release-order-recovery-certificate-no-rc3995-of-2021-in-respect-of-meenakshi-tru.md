## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order for Recovery Certificate No. RC3995 of 2021 in respect of Meenakshi Trust (PAN: AABTM6613J). This order pertains to the matter of Unisys Software and Holding Industries Limited, signalling that the outstanding dues under the recovery certificate have been satisfied and the certificate is being formally released.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC3995 of 2021 has been released by SEBI.
- The certificate was issued against Meenakshi Trust (PAN: AABTM6613J).
- The underlying matter relates to Unisys Software and Holding Industries Limited.
- A Release Order indicates that recovery dues have been paid or otherwise settled to SEBI's satisfaction.
- This is an enforcement and recovery action closure by SEBI.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This order concludes an existing enforcement recovery proceeding by releasing the recovery certificate previously issued against Meenakshi Trust.

## Compliance Requirements

- No new compliance obligations are imposed on market participants at large.
- The relevant parties (Meenakshi Trust and associated persons) should retain records of the release order for their compliance files.
- The matter of Unisys Software and Holding Industries Limited enforcement action is formally closed to the extent covered by this certificate.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Release Order:** 19 June 2026
- **Original Recovery Certificate:** RC3995 of 2021

## Impact Assessment

The impact of this order is limited and specific to the parties named. It signals closure of a SEBI enforcement recovery action that originated in 2021 against Meenakshi Trust in connection with Unisys Software and Holding Industries Limited. Broader market participants are not affected. Investors in Unisys Software and Holding Industries Limited should note this as a resolution of a regulatory matter involving an associated entity.
