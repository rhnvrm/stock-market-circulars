## Summary

The SEBI Appellate Authority issued an order disposing of Appeal No. 6944 of 2026, filed by Madhu K Lenin under the Right to Information Act, 2005 (RTI Act). The appellant had sought information from SEBI's Central Public Information Officer (CPIO) regarding facilities for Persons with Benchmark Disabilities (PwBD), a copy of SEBI's HR Manual/Employee Handbook, reservation provisions in promotions for SC/ST/OBC/EWS/PwBD categories, and region-wise/department-wise staffing and vacancy data. The CPIO had earlier responded citing SEBI's published Equal Opportunity Policy for PwBDs, recruitment advertisement details, physical accessibility facilities at SEBI offices, and referred the appellant to publicly available information on pay scales, benefits, and service conditions. The appellant, dissatisfied with the response, escalated the matter to the Appellate Authority.

## Key Points

- Appellant Madhu K Lenin filed an RTI application dated May 12, 2026 seeking four categories of information: (1) PwBD facilities/policies, (2) HR Manual/Employee Handbook, (3) reservation in promotions for SC/ST/OBC/EWS/PwBD, and (4) office-wise and department-wise vacancy/staffing data.
- CPIO responded on June 09, 2026, directing the appellant to SEBI's published Equal Opportunity Policy for PwBDs and the Recruitment Exercise 2025 advertisement, both available on the SEBI website.
- CPIO noted physical accessibility measures at SEBI offices, including ramps, Braille-equipped lifts, floor announcers, handrails, and PwD-accessible toilets.
- Appellant filed an appeal dated June 23, 2026 (Registration No. SEBIH/A/E/26/00230) contesting the adequacy of the CPIO's response.
- The Appellate Authority reviewed the application, CPIO's response, and the appeal to decide the matter based on record.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is an individual RTI appellate order and does not introduce new regulations, policies, or market rules.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance obligations are created for listed entities, market intermediaries, or the public. The order pertains solely to SEBI's internal RTI disclosure obligations and information-sharing practices with the appellant.

## Important Dates

- May 12, 2026: RTI application filed by appellant.
- June 09, 2026: CPIO's response letter issued.
- June 23, 2026: Appeal filed by appellant (Reg. No. SEBIH/A/E/26/00230).

## Impact Assessment

This order has no impact on securities markets, listed companies, or trading activity. It is purely an internal administrative and transparency matter concerning SEBI's RTI compliance and HR-related disclosure practices, relevant primarily to governance and transparency observers rather than market participants.
