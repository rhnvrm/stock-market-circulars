## Summary

SEBI has issued Notice of Attachment of Bank Accounts No. 15342 of 2026 dated May 06, 2026, against Hari Om Singh (designated as Defaulter, PAN: BGBPS4181A). The attachment is being effected under Recovery Certificate No. 8872 of 2025, which itself arose from SEBI's investigation into the trading activities of certain entities in the scrip of Continental Seeds and Chemicals Limited.

## Key Points

- Notice Number: 15342 of 2026, dated May 06, 2026
- Defaulter: Hari Om Singh, PAN: BGBPS4181A
- Instrument: Notice of Attachment of Bank Accounts
- Underlying authority: Recovery Certificate No. 8872 of 2025
- Origin: Investigation into trading activities in Continental Seeds and Chemicals Limited
- Issuing authority: Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This notice is an enforcement action under SEBI's existing recovery and attachment powers conferred by the SEBI Act and the Securities and Exchange Board of India (Recovery of Amounts) Rules.

## Compliance Requirements

- The bank(s) holding accounts in the name of Hari Om Singh are directed to attach the specified accounts and not to allow any debit transactions from those accounts.
- The defaulter (Hari Om Singh) must satisfy the outstanding dues under Recovery Certificate No. 8872 of 2025 to secure release of the attached accounts.
- Any third parties holding funds on behalf of the defaulter may also be subject to the attachment order.

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate No. 8872 issued: 2025 (exact date not specified in notice title)
- Notice of Attachment issued: May 06, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This action has a narrow, targeted impact confined to the individual defaulter Hari Om Singh. There is no direct market-wide or systemic impact. The underlying investigation into Continental Seeds and Chemicals Limited trading activities may have broader implications depending on the number of entities involved, but this specific notice pertains only to one individual's bank accounts. Market participants in Continental Seeds and Chemicals Limited scrip should monitor any related SEBI orders for wider enforcement actions stemming from the same investigation.
