## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC9138 of 2026 along with a Notice of Demand against Amanat Developers Private Limited (PAN: AAGCA9440D) in connection with the illiquid stock options matter. This certificate empowers SEBI to recover outstanding dues from the entity as a result of penalties or disgorgement orders passed in the illiquid stock options case.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9138 of 2026 issued against Amanat Developers Private Limited
- PAN of the entity: AAGCA9440D
- Action relates to the illiquid stock options matter, in which SEBI found widespread manipulation in the illiquid stock options segment
- Notice of Demand issued requiring the entity to pay outstanding dues
- Recovery certificate enables SEBI to recover amounts as arrears of land revenue if not paid

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced. This document represents enforcement of existing SEBI orders and penalties under the SEBI (Recovery of Amounts) Regulations or applicable provisions of the SEBI Act.

## Compliance Requirements

- Amanat Developers Private Limited is required to pay the demanded amount within the stipulated timeframe specified in the Notice of Demand
- Failure to comply may result in coercive recovery proceedings, including attachment and sale of assets
- The entity should engage with SEBI's recovery officer and settle outstanding dues promptly

## Important Dates

- Certificate issued: June 2026
- Payment deadline: As specified in the accompanying Notice of Demand (refer to the original PDF for exact dates)

## Impact Assessment

This action is targeted at a specific entity (Amanat Developers Private Limited) and does not have broad market impact. It is part of SEBI's ongoing enforcement drive against participants found to have engaged in manipulative trading in the illiquid stock options segment. The illiquid stock options matter has seen SEBI issue numerous recovery certificates against various entities. Market-wide impact is low, but the named entity faces significant financial liability and potential asset recovery proceedings.
