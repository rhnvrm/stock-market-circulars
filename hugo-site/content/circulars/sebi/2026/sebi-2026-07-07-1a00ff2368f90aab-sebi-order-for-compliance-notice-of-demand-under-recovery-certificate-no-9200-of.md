## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand under Recovery Certificate No. 9200 of 2026, dated 08.07.2026, against M/s Western Agrotech Innovative Limited (PAN: AAACW9940Q). The recovery proceedings are connected to the matter of Retro Green Revolution Limited, indicating enforcement action to recover outstanding dues or penalties determined in a prior SEBI order or adjudication.

## Key Points

- SEBI has initiated recovery proceedings against Western Agrotech Innovative Limited via Recovery Certificate No. 9200 of 2026.
- The notice is dated 08 July 2026.
- The matter arises in connection with Retro Green Revolution Limited.
- The entity has been identified by its PAN (AAACW9940Q) for recovery action.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced; this is an enforcement/recovery action under existing SEBI recovery mechanisms (typically invoked under the Income Tax Act recovery provisions as applied to SEBI recovery proceedings for unpaid penalties, fees, or disgorgement amounts).

## Compliance Requirements

M/s Western Agrotech Innovative Limited is required to respond to and comply with the Notice of Demand, which may involve payment of outstanding dues, penalties, or amounts ordered for recovery. Failure to comply may result in further coercive recovery measures such as attachment of bank accounts, property, or other assets.

## Important Dates

- Notice of Demand dated: 08 July 2026 (Recovery Certificate No. 9200 of 2026).

## Impact Assessment

This notice primarily impacts M/s Western Agrotech Innovative Limited and parties connected to Retro Green Revolution Limited. It signals continued SEBI enforcement of recovery proceedings against non-compliant entities. Broader market impact is limited, as this is a targeted enforcement action rather than a market-wide regulatory change, though it underscores SEBI's ongoing efforts to enforce recovery of dues from entities found non-compliant with prior orders.
