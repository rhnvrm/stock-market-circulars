## Summary

SEBI has announced the completion of Recovery Certificate No. 2752 of 2020, dated January 13, 2020, which was issued against Mamta Jindal (Defaulter, PAN: AEEPJ6955K) in the matter of Pawansut Holdings Limited. The completion indicates that the recovery proceedings initiated under this certificate have been concluded.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 2752 of 2020 originally issued on January 13, 2020
- Defaulter: Mamta Jindal (PAN: AEEPJ6955K)
- Related matter: Pawansut Holdings Limited
- SEBI has formally declared the recovery proceedings under this certificate as complete

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an administrative order confirming the closure of existing recovery proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

No ongoing compliance obligations arise from this circular. It marks the end of enforcement action under the referenced recovery certificate.

## Important Dates

- Original Recovery Certificate date: January 13, 2020
- Completion order date: May 20, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has no market-wide impact. It is an administrative enforcement closure notice affecting only the named individual defaulter. Investors and market participants in Pawansut Holdings Limited or related securities are not subject to any new requirements as a result of this order.
