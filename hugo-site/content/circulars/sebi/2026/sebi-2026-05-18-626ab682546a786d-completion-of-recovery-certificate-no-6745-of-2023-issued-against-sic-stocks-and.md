## Summary

SEBI has issued a notice regarding the completion of Recovery Certificate No. 6745 of 2023, which was issued against SIC Stocks and Services Private Ltd (PAN: AAHCS8176P). The completion indicates that the recovery proceedings initiated under the said certificate have been concluded.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 6745 of 2023 issued against SIC Stocks and Services Private Ltd has been marked as completed.
- The entity's PAN is AAHCS8176P.
- This is an enforcement/recovery action by SEBI to recover dues from the defaulter.
- Completion may indicate full recovery of dues, settlement, or closure of proceedings.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this circular. It is an administrative order notifying the closure of a specific recovery proceeding.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements are imposed on market participants. This circular pertains solely to the enforcement action against SIC Stocks and Services Private Ltd.

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate No. 6745 originally issued: 2023
- Completion notice date: May 18, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has no direct impact on the broader market or other market participants. It is an administrative closure of a recovery proceeding against a single entity (SIC Stocks and Services Private Ltd). Investors and market participants are not affected by this action.
