## Summary

SEBI's Recovery Officer has issued a Notice of Demand under Recovery Certificate No. 9223 of 2026, dated July 15, 2026, to Mr. Rajesh Punia (PAN: AVNPP5342G) and Mrs. Savita Punia (PAN: AALPP0729L), who have been named as defaulters, jointly and severally. The recovery proceedings stem from SEBI's investigation into the financial statements of Oriental Trimex Limited. The notice formally demands payment of the outstanding dues specified in the recovery certificate and initiates the recovery process against the defaulters' assets.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 9223 of 2026 has been issued against Mr. Rajesh Punia and Mrs. Savita Punia.
- Both individuals are held jointly and severally liable as defaulters.
- The underlying matter relates to SEBI's investigation into the financial statements of Oriental Trimex Limited.
- The notice is issued by SEBI's Recovery Officer under statutory recovery provisions (Section 28A of the SEBI Act read with the Income Tax Act recovery machinery) to recover amounts due.

## Regulatory Changes

This is an enforcement action rather than a policy or regulatory change. No new rules or amendments are introduced; the notice applies existing recovery provisions under the SEBI Act to enforce collection of dues from the named defaulters.

## Compliance Requirements

- The defaulters, Mr. Rajesh Punia and Mrs. Savita Punia, are required to pay the outstanding demand amount specified in Recovery Certificate No. 9223 of 2026 within the timeframe stipulated in the notice.
- Failure to comply may result in further coercive recovery measures, including attachment and sale of movable/immovable property, garnishee proceedings, or other actions available to the Recovery Officer under the Second and Third Schedules to the Income Tax Act, 1961, as applied to SEBI recovery proceedings.

## Important Dates

- Notice date: July 15, 2026 (Recovery Certificate No. 9223 of 2026).

## Impact Assessment

The notice directly affects the named defaulters, Mr. Rajesh Punia and Mrs. Savita Punia, who face recovery action for outstanding dues linked to the Oriental Trimex Limited investigation. There is no direct trading, listing, or disclosure impact on any listed security or the broader market; the action is confined to enforcement against the specified individuals.
