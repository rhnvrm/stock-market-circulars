## Summary

SEBI has issued a release order pertaining to Recovery Certificate No. 8433 of 2024 for Navneet Agarwal and Sons HUF (PAN: AAGHN4487C) in the matter of trading in illiquid stock options on the Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE). This order marks the completion or resolution of recovery proceedings against the defaulter.

- Entity: Navneet Agarwal and Sons HUF
- PAN: AAGHN4487C
- Certificate No.: 8433 of 2024
- Matter: Trading in Illiquid Stock Options on BSE
- Order Type: Release Order
- Date: January 9, 2026

No regulatory framework changes. This is an enforcement action against a specific defaulter.

This order pertains to recovery proceedings against a specific defaulter entity. No compliance requirements for other market participants.

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate No.: 8433 of 2024

Limited impact on the broader market. This is an enforcement action specific to one HUF entity involved in trading illiquid stock options. The release order indicates completion of recovery proceedings. No systemic implications for other market participants or trading operations on BSE.
