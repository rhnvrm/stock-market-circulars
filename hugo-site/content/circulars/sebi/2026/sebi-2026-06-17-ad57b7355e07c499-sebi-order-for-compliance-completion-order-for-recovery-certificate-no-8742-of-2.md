## Summary

SEBI has issued a Compliance Completion Order for Recovery Certificate No. 8742 of 2025 against Hansaben Bharatbhai Patel in the matter of Pressure Sensitive Systems (India) Limited. This order signifies that the recovery proceedings initiated under the said recovery certificate have been duly completed.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8742 of 2025 has been acted upon and the compliance completion order has been issued.
- The order is against Hansaben Bharatbhai Patel in connection with the matter of Pressure Sensitive Systems (India) Limited.
- A compliance completion order indicates that SEBI's recovery mechanism has been fulfilled, either through payment of dues or completion of the enforcement process.
- The order was issued on June 17, 2026.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced by this order. It is an administrative enforcement action concluding previously initiated recovery proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

- No new compliance requirements are imposed on market participants by this order.
- The order is specific to the named respondent, Hansaben Bharatbhai Patel, and marks the closure of recovery proceedings under Recovery Certificate No. 8742 of 2025.

## Important Dates

- **Order Date:** June 17, 2026
- **Recovery Certificate:** No. 8742 of 2025

## Impact Assessment

This order has no broader market impact. It is an administrative closure of enforcement/recovery proceedings against a specific individual in connection with Pressure Sensitive Systems (India) Limited. Market participants and investors are not directly affected by this order.
