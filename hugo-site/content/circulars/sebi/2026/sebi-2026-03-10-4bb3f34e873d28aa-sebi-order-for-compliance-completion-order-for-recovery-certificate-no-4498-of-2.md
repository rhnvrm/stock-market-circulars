## Summary

SEBI has issued a completion order for Recovery Certificate No. 4498 of 2022 against Vaishali Natvarlal Thakkar in connection with the matter of SMS Techsoft (India) Ltd. This order signifies the conclusion of recovery proceedings initiated under the said certificate.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 4498 of 2022 proceedings have been completed
- Individual involved: Vaishali Natvarlal Thakkar
- Matter relates to SMS Techsoft (India) Ltd.
- This is a compliance completion order indicating the conclusion of SEBI's recovery enforcement action

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine enforcement completion order concluding recovery proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

- Recovery proceedings under Recovery Certificate No. 4498 of 2022 have been concluded
- The order documents the closure of enforcement actions against Vaishali Natvarlal Thakkar
- No new compliance requirements imposed on broader market participants

## Important Dates

- Order Date: March 10, 2026
- Recovery Certificate No: 4498 of 2022

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This is an individual enforcement completion order with no direct market-wide implications.

**Operational Impact:** Low - The completion order closes recovery proceedings against a specific individual and does not impose new compliance requirements on market participants generally.

**Stakeholder Impact:** Limited to Vaishali Natvarlal Thakkar named in the order and relates to concluded proceedings in the matter of SMS Techsoft (India) Ltd.
