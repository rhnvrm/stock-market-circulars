## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand under Recovery Certificate No. 9071 of 2026, dated March 24, 2026, against Allo Roy (PAN: ANOPR5262Q), who has been identified as a defaulter in the matter of ISO (Investor Securities Obligations) at BSE. This notice demands recovery of outstanding dues as determined by SEBI's enforcement proceedings.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 9071 of 2026 issued on March 24, 2026
- Defaulter: Allo Roy (PAN: ANOPR5262Q)
- Relates to the matter of ISO (Investor Securities Obligations) at BSE
- Notice of Demand issued for recovery of outstanding dues
- Action taken under SEBI's recovery and enforcement powers

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an enforcement/recovery action under existing SEBI recovery certificate provisions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Allo Roy is required to satisfy the demand raised under Recovery Certificate No. 9071 of 2026
- Payment of the demanded amount must be made to SEBI within the stipulated timeframe as specified in the notice
- Failure to comply may result in further enforcement action including attachment of assets

## Important Dates

- **March 24, 2026**: Date of issuance of Recovery Certificate No. 9071 of 2026 and Notice of Demand

## Impact Assessment

This order has a limited market impact as it is directed at a specific individual (Allo Roy) identified as a defaulter in the ISO matter at BSE. It does not affect broader market operations or regulatory frameworks. The action reflects SEBI's ongoing enforcement of recovery certificates against defaulters to protect investor interests and maintain market integrity.
