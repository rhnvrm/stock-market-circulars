## Summary

The SEBI Appellate Authority passed an order disposing of RTI Appeal No. 6913 of 2026 filed by Dhieraj Pal against the CPIO, SEBI, Mumbai. The appellant had sought detailed information regarding Godrej Consumer Products Limited (CIN: L24246MH2000PLC129806), including material event disclosures, complaints of financial irregularities, investigations, corporate correspondence, corporate governance reports, forensic audit reports, and enforcement actions for the period February 2010 to March 2014. The respondent replied that certain information (queries 1 and 5) is not maintained by SEBI in the normal course of regulation, noting that listed entity disclosures are disseminated on stock exchange websites. For other queries (2, 3, 4, 6, and 9), the respondent invoked Section 7(9) of the RTI Act, stating that furnishing the information would disproportionately divert the resources of the public authority.

## Key Points

- Appellant Dhieraj Pal filed an RTI application dated April 15, 2026 (received by SEBI on April 20, 2026) seeking a decade-old disclosure history for Godrej Consumer Products Limited.
- The respondent (CPIO, SEBI, Mumbai) responded on May 14, 2026.
- The appellant filed an appeal dated May 29, 2026 (Reg. No. SEBIH/A/P/26/00032), received by the Office of Appellate Authority on June 02, 2026.
- For queries on material event disclosures and Corporate Governance Reports (query nos. 1 and 5), SEBI stated the information is not maintained by it, and that such disclosures are available on stock exchange websites.
- For queries on complaints, investigations, correspondence, forensic audits, and stock exchange communications (query nos. 2, 3, 4, 6, and 9), SEBI cited Section 7(9) of the RTI Act, stating that compiling the information would disproportionately divert the resources of the public authority.
- The appeal pertains to a decade-old period (February 2010 - March 2014) of alleged disclosure lapses concerning Godrej Consumer Products Limited.
