## Summary

SEBI's Recovery Officer has passed a General Remittance Order dated July 13, 2026 under Recovery Certificate (RC) No. 4687 of 2022, issued against Anitha Yarlagadda in connection with recovery proceedings arising out of the matter of Venus Power Ventures Ltd. A remittance order of this nature is issued under the recovery mechanism provided in the Second Schedule to the Income Tax Act, 1961 (as made applicable to SEBI recovery proceedings), and typically transfers or forwards the recovery certificate to another Recovery Officer having territorial jurisdiction over the place where the defaulter resides, carries on business, or holds property/assets, so that further coercive recovery steps (such as attachment and sale of property, garnishee proceedings, or arrest and detention) can be pursued in that jurisdiction.

## Key Points

- Order type: General Remittance Order in recovery proceedings.
- Recovery Certificate reference: RC No. 4687 of 2022.
- Noticee/defaulter: Anitha Yarlagadda.
- Underlying matter: Venus Power Ventures Ltd.
- Order date: July 13, 2026.
- Issuing authority: SEBI Recovery Officer, acting under powers conferred for recovery of sums due under SEBI orders/directions.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced by this order. It is an administrative/enforcement step within an existing recovery proceeding (RC No. 4687 of 2022), consistent with SEBI's established recovery framework under Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992 read with the Second and Third Schedules to the Income Tax Act, 1961.

## Compliance Requirements

- The noticee, Anitha Yarlagadda, remains liable to satisfy the outstanding recovery amount under RC No. 4687 of 2022.
- The Recovery Officer to whom the certificate is remitted is required to take further recovery action in accordance with law within their jurisdiction.
- Any banks, depositories, or authorities holding assets/accounts of the noticee may be required to cooperate with subsequent attachment or recovery steps once initiated by the transferee Recovery Officer.

## Important Dates

- Order date: July 13, 2026.
- Circular published/processed: July 14, 2026.

## Impact Assessment

This order is a procedural enforcement step in an individual recovery proceeding and does not directly affect listed securities, market trading, or investor obligations at large. Its relevance is limited to the specific noticee and the recovery of dues connected to Venus Power Ventures Ltd; there is no broader market or compliance impact for other listed entities or market participants.
