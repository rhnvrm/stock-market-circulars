## Summary

SEBI's Recovery Officer has issued a cancellation order in respect of Recovery Certificate No. 6273 of 2023, which had earlier been issued against Kartik Clothing And Fabrics Pvt. Ltd. in connection with recovery proceedings arising out of the matter of Excel Castronics Limited. The order formally cancels the previously issued recovery certificate, typically following satisfaction of the underlying demand, correction of the certificate, or a related administrative development in the recovery proceedings.

## Key Points

- The order relates to recovery proceedings initiated by SEBI against Kartik Clothing And Fabrics Pvt. Ltd. in the matter of Excel Castronics Limited.
- Recovery Certificate No. 6273 of 2023, previously issued to enforce recovery of dues, has now been cancelled by the Recovery Officer.
- The cancellation is issued alongside related recovery-proceeding orders concerning other noticees in the same matter published around the same date.
- This is an administrative/enforcement action under SEBI's recovery proceedings framework rather than a market-wide regulatory or trading directive.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory requirements, rules, or policy changes are introduced by this order. It is a case-specific administrative action taken under SEBI's powers of recovery (Section 28A of the SEBI Act, read with the Second and Third Schedules to the Income Tax Act, 1961, and the Income Tax (Certificate Proceedings) Rules, 1962) in relation to a specific noticee.

## Compliance Requirements

There are no new compliance obligations placed on listed companies, market intermediaries, or investors generally as a result of this order. The order pertains solely to the status of recovery proceedings against the named entity, Kartik Clothing And Fabrics Pvt. Ltd.

## Important Dates

The cancellation order was issued and published on July 2, 2026. No further compliance deadlines are prescribed for market participants.

## Impact Assessment

The order has no direct bearing on trading, listing, or disclosure obligations for any listed security and is not expected to have any market-wide impact. Its relevance is limited to the specific recovery proceeding against the named entity in connection with Excel Castronics Limited.
