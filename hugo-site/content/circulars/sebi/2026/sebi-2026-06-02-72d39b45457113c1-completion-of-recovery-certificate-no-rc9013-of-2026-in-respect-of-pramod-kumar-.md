## Summary

SEBI has notified the completion of Recovery Certificate No. RC9013 of 2026 issued against Pramod Kumar Jain (PAN: ACLPJ7469D) in the matter of Illiquid Stock Options. The completion of a recovery certificate indicates that the outstanding dues pursuant to SEBI's enforcement order have been fully recovered or otherwise settled.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9013 of 2026 against Pramod Kumar Jain (PAN: ACLPJ7469D) has been completed.
- The matter pertains to Illiquid Stock Options, which have been under SEBI's surveillance and enforcement for alleged manipulative trading practices.
- Completion of the recovery certificate signifies that the financial penalties or disgorgement amounts imposed on the individual have been recovered by SEBI.
- The action is part of SEBI's broader enforcement drive against manipulation in the illiquid stock options segment.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced by this notice. This is an administrative closure communication confirming the fulfillment of an existing enforcement order.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements arise from this notice. It is informational in nature, confirming the closure of recovery proceedings against the named individual.

## Important Dates

- Date of notice: June 2026
- Recovery Certificate No. RC9013 of 2026 is now marked as completed.

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market or operational impact. It is an individual enforcement matter confined to Pramod Kumar Jain. The Illiquid Stock Options enforcement program has been an ongoing SEBI initiative; this notice represents successful recovery in one case within that broader program. No listed companies, broader market participants, or trading operations are affected.
