## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order in respect of Uttam Dealers Pvt. Ltd. (PAN: AABCU1506F) in connection with recovery proceedings arising from the Illiquid Stock Options matter. The order releases the attachment or freeze previously imposed on the assets/accounts of the entity, indicating satisfaction of dues or compliance with recovery conditions.

## Key Points

- Release Order issued by SEBI under recovery proceedings against Uttam Dealers Pvt. Ltd.
- PAN of the entity: AABCU1506F
- Matter pertains to the Illiquid Stock Options case, a long-running SEBI enforcement action against entities involved in manipulative trading in illiquid stock options on BSE
- The release indicates that the entity has settled dues or fulfilled conditions imposed under the Recovery Certificate

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an administrative/enforcement order specific to the named entity.

## Compliance Requirements

- No new compliance obligations for market participants generally
- Relevant banks, depositories, and third parties holding attached assets of Uttam Dealers Pvt. Ltd. are directed to release the freeze/attachment in accordance with this order

## Important Dates

- Order Date: 2026-03-11

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is an entity-specific enforcement closure step in the Illiquid Stock Options matter. It signals partial resolution of recovery proceedings against one of the named entities but has no broader market or trading implications.
