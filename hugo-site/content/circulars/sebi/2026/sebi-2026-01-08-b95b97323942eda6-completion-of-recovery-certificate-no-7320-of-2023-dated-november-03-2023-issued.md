## Summary

SEBI has announced the completion of Recovery Certificate No. 7320 of 2023 dated November 03, 2023, which was issued against Jatin Ahuja (Defaulter, PAN: AGDPA4644F). The recovery certificate was issued in connection with trading in illiquid stock options on the Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE). This notice confirms that the recovery process has been successfully completed.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 7320 of 2023 has been completed
- Original certificate was dated November 03, 2023
- Defaulter: Jatin Ahuja (PAN: AGDPA4644F)
- Matter relates to trading in illiquid stock options on BSE
- Recovery proceedings have been concluded

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a completion notice for an existing enforcement action.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. This notice is informational, confirming the conclusion of recovery proceedings against the named defaulter.

## Important Dates

- **November 03, 2023**: Original Recovery Certificate issued
- **January 08, 2026**: Completion of recovery proceedings announced

## Impact Assessment

This is an administrative notice with minimal market impact. It confirms the successful completion of debt recovery proceedings against a specific individual involved in illiquid stock options trading violations on BSE. The completion indicates SEBI's enforcement actions have been fulfilled. No broader regulatory changes or market-wide implications are expected from this notice.
