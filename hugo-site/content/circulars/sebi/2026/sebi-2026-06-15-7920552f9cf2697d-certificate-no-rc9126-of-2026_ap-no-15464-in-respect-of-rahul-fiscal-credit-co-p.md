## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC9126 of 2026 under Appeal/Adjudication Proceeding No. 15464 against Rahul Fiscal & Credit Co. Pvt. Ltd. (PAN: AACCR4625F). This certificate relates to the matter of Illiquid Stock Options and directs recovery of outstanding penalty or dues owed to SEBI by the named entity.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No.: RC9126 of 2026
- A.P. No.: 15464
- Entity: Rahul Fiscal & Credit Co. Pvt. Ltd.
- PAN: AACCR4625F
- Matter: Illiquid Stock Options
- Issued by SEBI's Recovery Officer under the SEBI Act for non-payment of penalty/dues

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an enforcement action enforcing an existing adjudication order.

## Compliance Requirements

Rahul Fiscal & Credit Co. Pvt. Ltd. is required to pay the outstanding dues as specified in the recovery certificate. Failure to comply may result in further enforcement action including attachment of assets.

## Important Dates

- Certificate issued: June 2026
- Payment due: As specified in the recovery certificate notice

## Impact Assessment

This recovery certificate has a limited market impact and is specific to the named entity. It is part of SEBI's broader enforcement campaign against entities involved in illiquid stock options manipulation. No impact on broader markets or other market participants is expected.
