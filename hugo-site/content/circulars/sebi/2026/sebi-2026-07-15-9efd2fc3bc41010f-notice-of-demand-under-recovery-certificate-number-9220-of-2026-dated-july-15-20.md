## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand under Recovery Certificate No. 9220 of 2026, dated July 15, 2026, to Mr. Om Prakash Sharma (PAN: ATEPS0439A), who has been declared a defaulter in the matter concerning Oriental Trimex Limited. The notice initiates recovery proceedings for outstanding dues payable to SEBI, pursuant to the recovery certificate issued against the defaulter.

## Key Points

- SEBI's Recovery Officer has issued a formal Notice of Demand against Mr. Om Prakash Sharma under Recovery Certificate No. 9220 of 2026.
- The defaulter is identified by PAN ATEPS0439A.
- The recovery action arises out of proceedings related to Oriental Trimex Limited.
- The notice is issued under SEBI's powers of recovery of amounts due, exercised in a manner similar to recovery of tax arrears under the Income Tax Act, 1961.
- Non-payment within the stipulated period may lead to further coercive recovery measures, including attachment and sale of movable/immovable property, arrest and detention, and appointment of a receiver.

## Regulatory Changes

This is not a regulatory/policy change but an individual enforcement action. It reflects SEBI's continued use of its recovery powers under Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992, to enforce monetary demands (such as penalties, disgorgement amounts, or fees) against defaulters who have failed to comply with earlier orders or demands.

## Compliance Requirements

- The defaulter, Mr. Om Prakash Sharma, is required to pay the outstanding amount specified in the underlying recovery certificate within the timeframe stipulated in the notice.
- Failure to comply may result in coercive recovery action by the Recovery Officer, including attachment of bank accounts, immovable property, and other assets.

## Important Dates

- Notice of Demand date: July 15, 2026
- Recovery Certificate No.: 9220 of 2026

## Impact Assessment

This notice has no direct impact on listed securities, trading, or market operations, as it pertains to an individual recovery proceeding rather than a company-wide or market-wide regulatory action. Its significance is confined to enforcement of SEBI's recovery mechanism against the named defaulter in connection with the Oriental Trimex Limited matter.
