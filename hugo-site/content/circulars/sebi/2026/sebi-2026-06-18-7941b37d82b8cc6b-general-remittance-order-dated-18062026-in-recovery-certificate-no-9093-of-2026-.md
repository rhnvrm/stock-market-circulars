## Summary

SEBI issued a General Remittance Order dated 18 June 2026 under Recovery Certificate No. 9093 of 2026 against Makwana Bharat S. HUF (PAN: AALHM4561P). The order pertains to dealings in illiquid stock options on BSE and is part of SEBI's ongoing enforcement efforts to recover amounts due from entities found to have engaged in unauthorized or manipulative trading in illiquid options segments.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 9093 of 2026 issued against Makwana Bharat S. HUF (PAN: AALHM4561P)
- Order dated 18 June 2026 directing remittance of dues to SEBI
- Entity involved in dealing in illiquid stock options on BSE
- Enforcement proceeding initiated to recover outstanding penalty or disgorgement amounts

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This order is an enforcement/recovery instrument issued under existing SEBI recovery powers.

## Compliance Requirements

- Makwana Bharat S. HUF is required to remit the amount specified in the Recovery Certificate No. 9093 of 2026 to SEBI
- Non-compliance may result in further enforcement action including attachment of assets

## Important Dates

- Order date: 18 June 2026
- Recovery Certificate No.: 9093 of 2026

## Impact Assessment

This order is limited in scope, targeting a single HUF entity (Makwana Bharat S. HUF) for illiquid stock options dealing on BSE. It has no direct market-wide impact. It reflects SEBI's continued enforcement against participants involved in illiquid options segment irregularities, which has been a sustained regulatory focus area.
