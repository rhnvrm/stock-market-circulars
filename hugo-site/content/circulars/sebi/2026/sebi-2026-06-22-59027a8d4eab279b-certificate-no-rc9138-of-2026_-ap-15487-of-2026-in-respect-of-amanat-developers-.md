## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC9138 of 2026 under Attachment Petition No. 15487 of 2026 against Amanat Developers Private Limited (PAN: AAGCA9440D) for recovery of outstanding dues in the matter of Illiquid Stock Options. This certificate authorises recovery proceedings against the entity.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9138 of 2026 issued by SEBI
- Attachment Petition (A.P.) No. 15487 of 2026
- Entity: Amanat Developers Private Limited (PAN: AAGCA9440D)
- Matter relates to Illiquid Stock Options violations
- Certificate enables SEBI to recover dues through statutory enforcement mechanisms

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement/recovery action under existing SEBI regulations governing illiquid stock options.

## Compliance Requirements

- Amanat Developers Private Limited is directed to comply with the recovery certificate and settle outstanding dues with SEBI
- Failure to comply may result in further attachment and coercive recovery proceedings

## Important Dates

- Certificate issuance date: June 2026
- Recovery proceedings effective upon issuance of certificate

## Impact Assessment

This circular has a limited direct market impact as it pertains to enforcement action against a single private entity. It reinforces SEBI's continued crackdown on illiquid stock options manipulation and signals ongoing recovery proceedings against entities penalised in earlier orders. Market participants in the illiquid derivatives space should note the continued enforcement activity.
