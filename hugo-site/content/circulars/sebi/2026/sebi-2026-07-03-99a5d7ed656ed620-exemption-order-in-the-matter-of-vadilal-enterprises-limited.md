## Summary

SEBI's Whole Time Member passed an order under Sections 11(1) and 11(2)(h) of the SEBI Act, 1992, read with Regulation 11(5) of the SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 2011, on an application filed by IVG Trust (the Acquirer Trust) seeking exemption from the applicability of Regulation 3 and Regulation 5 read with Regulation 4 of the SAST Regulations, 2011. The exemption was sought in connection with the proposed direct and indirect acquisition of shares and voting rights in Vadilal Enterprises Limited (Target Company), a BSE-listed company belonging to the Gandhi family group, by the Acquirer Trust as part of an internal restructuring of promoter/family holdings.

## Key Points

- The original application was filed on August 07, 2025, and subsequently updated on November 9, 2025 after the Acquirer Trust decided not to undertake a previously contemplated HUF partition.
- SEBI identified discrepancies in the names of the settlor, trustees, and beneficiaries across the KYC documents, Trust Deed, and shareholding pattern filed with the exchange; the Acquirer Trust confirmed and rectified these discrepancies.
- A revised Trust Deed and related undertakings were submitted by the Acquirer Trust on May 08, 2026.
- Final clarifications sought by SEBI on May 19, 2026 were answered by the Acquirer Trust on June 03, 2026.
- The Target Company's paid-up equity capital is INR 86,26,680 divided into 8,62,668 equity shares of INR 10 face value each.
- As per the shareholding pattern as on March 31, 2026, key promoter/family shareholders include Devanshu Laxmanbhai Gandhi (13.99%), Rajesh Ramchandra Gandhi (9.13%), Mamta Rajesh Gandhi (2.47%), Virendra Ramchandra Gandhi HUF (1.35%), Janmajay Virendrabhai Gandhi (1.23%), Rajesh R. Gandhi HUF (0.25%), and Kalpit Rajesh Gandhi (0.01%).

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes were introduced; this is a case-specific exemption order applying existing provisions of the SAST Regulations, 2011 (Regulations 3, 4, 5, and 11(5)) to a particular intra-family/trust restructuring transaction.

## Compliance Requirements

The Acquirer Trust was required to rectify discrepancies between KYC records, the Trust Deed, and the shareholding pattern disclosed to the exchange, and to submit an amended Trust Deed along with undertakings before SEBI could complete its examination of the exemption application.

## Important Dates

- August 07, 2025: Original exemption application filed.
- November 9, 2025: Updated application received reflecting revised transaction structure.
- May 08, 2026: Amended Trust Deed and undertakings submitted.
- May 19, 2026: SEBI sought final clarifications.
- June 03, 2026: Acquirer Trust submitted final reply.

## Impact Assessment

The order primarily affects the internal ownership structuring of the promoter/family group of Vadilal Enterprises Limited and grants relief from triggering open offer obligations under the SAST Regulations for the specific trust-based transfer. Market impact is limited to shareholders and analysts tracking promoter shareholding changes and takeover-regulation compliance at Vadilal Enterprises Limited, with no broader market-wide implications.
