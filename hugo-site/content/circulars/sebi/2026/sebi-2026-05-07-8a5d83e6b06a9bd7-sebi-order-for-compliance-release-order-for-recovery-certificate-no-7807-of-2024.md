## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order dated May 2026 under Recovery Certificate No. 7807 of 2024 against Siddhant Suresh Chandan. The release order pertains to the matter of unregistered investment advisory services. Such release orders are typically issued once the outstanding dues under the recovery certificate have been recovered or the relevant attachment orders are no longer required.

## Key Points

- Release Order issued under Recovery Certificate No. 7807 of 2024
- Action against: Siddhant Suresh Chandan
- Matter: Unregistered Investment Advisory Services
- The release order indicates that attached assets/accounts are being released, signalling recovery of dues or satisfaction of the recovery certificate
- Issued by SEBI's Recovery Officer as part of enforcement proceedings

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement/recovery action under existing SEBI regulations governing investment advisory services and recovery of penalties.

## Compliance Requirements

- No new compliance obligations imposed on the broader market
- Entities operating as investment advisors must ensure registration with SEBI under the SEBI (Investment Advisers) Regulations, 2013
- Unregistered investment advisory activity remains prohibited under SEBI regulations

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate No.: 7807 of 2024 (original certificate issued in 2024)
- Release Order Date: May 2026

## Impact Assessment

This release order has minimal market impact. It is an individual enforcement action concluding the recovery proceedings against Siddhant Suresh Chandan for operating as an unregistered investment advisor. The order signals SEBI's continued enforcement against unregistered intermediaries but does not affect listed companies, trading operations, or broader market participants.
