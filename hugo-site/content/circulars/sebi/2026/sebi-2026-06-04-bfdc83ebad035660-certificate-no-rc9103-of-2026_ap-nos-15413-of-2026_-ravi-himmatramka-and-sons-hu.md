## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC9103 of 2026 against Ravi Himmatramka and Sons HUF (PAN: AAOHR1118J) in connection with Appellate Proceedings (AP) No. 15413 of 2026 in the matter of Illiquid Stock Options. The certificate authorises recovery of dues determined against the entity following SEBI's enforcement action in the illiquid stock options segment.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9103 of 2026 issued by SEBI
- Pertains to Appellate Proceedings No. 15413 of 2026
- Entity: Ravi Himmatramka and Sons HUF, PAN: AAOHR1118J
- Matter relates to illiquid stock options segment irregularities
- Certificate enables SEBI to recover outstanding dues from the named entity

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This certificate is an enforcement instrument issued under existing SEBI recovery powers pursuant to prior adjudication/settlement orders in the illiquid stock options matter.

## Compliance Requirements

- The named entity (Ravi Himmatramka and Sons HUF) is required to discharge the dues specified in the recovery certificate
- Non-compliance may result in further enforcement actions including attachment of assets
- No compliance obligations arise for third parties or market participants generally

## Important Dates

- Certificate issued: June 2026
- AP proceedings reference: AP No. 15413 of 2026

## Impact Assessment

This recovery certificate has a narrow, entity-specific impact. It affects only Ravi Himmatramka and Sons HUF and does not alter trading rules, listing requirements, or obligations for other market participants. It is part of SEBI's broader enforcement sweep against entities involved in the illiquid stock options manipulation matter.
