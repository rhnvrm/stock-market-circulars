## Summary

SEBI's Recovery Officer has issued a Notice of Attachment dated July 22, 2026, under Recovery Certificate (RC) No. 9182 of 2026, drawn against Ankit Ajitbhai Panchal. The notice arises from proceedings connected to the trading activities of certain entities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited). It forms part of a batch of similar attachment notices issued the same day under different RC numbers against other individuals/entities linked to the same matter, indicating a coordinated SEBI recovery action following an underlying adjudication or disgorgement order.

## Key Points

- Notice issued under RC No. 9182 of 2026, dated July 22, 2026.
- Noticee: Ankit Ajitbhai Panchal.
- Underlying matter: trading activities of certain entities in the scrip of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited).
- Action is a recovery/attachment proceeding, typically initiated under the Second Schedule to the Income Tax Act, 1961, as applied to recovery of sums due under the SEBI Act, 1992.
- Part of a series of related attachment notices issued concurrently under separate RC numbers against other noticees in the same matter.

## Regulatory Changes

None. This is an enforcement/recovery action rather than a policy or regulatory change; it operationalizes recovery of dues already adjudicated or confirmed in an earlier SEBI order against the noticee.

## Compliance Requirements

- The noticee, Ankit Ajitbhai Panchal, is required to clear the outstanding dues specified in the Recovery Certificate to avoid attachment/sale of movable or immovable property.
- Banks, depositories, and other intermediaries dealing with the noticee's assets may be required to comply with any attachment directions issued in connection with RC No. 9182 of 2026.

## Important Dates

- Notice date: July 22, 2026.

## Impact Assessment

The notice has a direct financial and legal impact on the named individual (Ankit Ajitbhai Panchal), whose assets are subject to attachment for recovery of dues. It does not directly affect the listed status, trading, or disclosures of DU Digital Technologies Limited (now DU Digital Global Limited) itself, and has no broader market-wide implications beyond signaling continued SEBI enforcement follow-through on the underlying trading-manipulation matter involving the scrip.
