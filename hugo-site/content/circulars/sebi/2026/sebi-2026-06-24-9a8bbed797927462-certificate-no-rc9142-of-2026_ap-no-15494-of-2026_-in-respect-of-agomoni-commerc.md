## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC9142 of 2026 arising from Adjudication Proceeding No. 15494 of 2026 against Agomoni Commercial Private Limited (PAN: AAHCA2663H). The matter pertains to the entity's involvement in illiquid stock options trading, which SEBI has been pursuing enforcement action against as part of its broader crackdown on manipulative and fraudulent practices in the illiquid stock options segment.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC9142 of 2026 issued against Agomoni Commercial Private Limited
- PAN of the entity: AAHCA2663H
- Adjudication Proceeding (A.P.) No. 15494 of 2026
- Matter relates to illiquid stock options trading violations
- Recovery certificate signifies that a penalty was levied via adjudication order and remains unpaid

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced by this order. This is an enforcement/recovery action under existing SEBI regulations, specifically under the SEBI (Procedure for Holding Inquiry and Imposing Penalties) Rules, 1995 and the Securities and Exchange Board of India Act, 1992.

## Compliance Requirements

- Agomoni Commercial Private Limited is required to pay the penalty amount as specified in the underlying adjudication order
- Failure to comply with the recovery certificate may result in further coercive recovery action by SEBI under Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992
- The entity must settle the outstanding dues to avoid additional legal consequences

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate issued: 2026
- Adjudication Proceeding No. 15494 of 2026 (year of proceeding: 2026)
- Specific payment deadlines are as per the terms of the recovery certificate

## Impact Assessment

This recovery certificate has a limited direct impact on the broader market. It is specific to Agomoni Commercial Private Limited and relates to past violations in the illiquid stock options segment. The illiquid stock options enforcement campaign by SEBI has involved hundreds of entities, and this certificate is part of that ongoing enforcement effort. Market participants should note SEBI's continued vigilance in pursuing penalties and recovery from entities found to have participated in manipulative trading in illiquid stock options.
