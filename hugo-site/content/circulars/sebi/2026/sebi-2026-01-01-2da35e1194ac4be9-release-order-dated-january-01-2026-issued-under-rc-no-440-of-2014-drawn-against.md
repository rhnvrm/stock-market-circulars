## Summary

SEBI issued a Release Order dated January 01, 2026 under Recovery Certificate No. 440 of 2014 drawn against Kolar Sharex Pvt. Ltd. in the matter of Kolar Biotech Ltd. This order pertains to ongoing recovery proceedings under SEBI's enforcement mechanism.

## Key Points

- Release order issued under RC No. 440 of 2014
- Order date: January 01, 2026
- Entity: Kolar Sharex Pvt. Ltd.
- Related matter: Kolar Biotech Ltd.
- Part of SEBI's recovery and enforcement proceedings

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement/recovery order related to existing proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

Compliance requirements are specific to Kolar Sharex Pvt. Ltd. as detailed in the recovery certificate proceedings. The release order modifies or updates previous directions under RC No. 440 of 2014.

## Important Dates

- **Order Date:** January 01, 2026
- **Recovery Certificate:** RC No. 440 of 2014

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This order is specific to Kolar Sharex Pvt. Ltd. and does not have broad market implications.

**Entity Impact:** Medium - The release order affects the specific entity (Kolar Sharex Pvt. Ltd.) involved in recovery proceedings related to Kolar Biotech Ltd.

**Investor Impact:** Limited to stakeholders of Kolar Biotech Ltd. and Kolar Sharex Pvt. Ltd.
