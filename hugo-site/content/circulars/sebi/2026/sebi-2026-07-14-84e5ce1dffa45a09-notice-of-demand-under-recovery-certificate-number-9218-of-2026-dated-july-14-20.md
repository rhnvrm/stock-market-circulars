## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand dated July 14, 2026 under Recovery Certificate No. 9218 of 2026, directed jointly and severally against three defaulters — Mirage Marble Private Limited (PAN: AAFCM1894A), Nirmal Marble Limited (PAN: AAACN6944R), and Mr. Abhishek Jain (PAN: AEJPJ9045B) — in connection with recovery proceedings arising out of the matter of Oriental Trimex Limited. The notice is issued pursuant to a Recovery Certificate drawn up by SEBI's Recovery Officer for enforcing an outstanding demand against the named defaulters, invoking SEBI's powers to recover sums due under applicable recovery and certificate proceedings.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 9218 of 2026 has been issued against the defaulters, dated July 14, 2026.
- The defaulters named jointly and severally are: Mirage Marble Private Limited (PAN AAFCM1894A), Nirmal Marble Limited (PAN AAACN6944R), and Mr. Abhishek Jain (PAN AEJPJ9045B).
- The proceedings arise in the matter of Oriental Trimex Limited, indicating a connection to prior SEBI orders, penalties, or dues linked to that entity.
- "Jointly and severally" liability means SEBI can recover the full outstanding amount from any one or more of the named defaulters.
- The Notice of Demand formally calls upon the defaulters to pay the amount specified in the Recovery Certificate within the stipulated period, failing which further recovery action (such as attachment of assets/bank accounts) may follow under recovery proceedings.

## Regulatory Changes

This is an individual enforcement action rather than a policy or regulatory change. No new rule, circular, or amendment is introduced; the notice operationalizes recovery of dues already adjudicated or certified against the named defaulters.

## Compliance Requirements

- The named defaulters (Mirage Marble Private Limited, Nirmal Marble Limited, and Mr. Abhishek Jain) are required to satisfy the demand raised under Recovery Certificate No. 9218 of 2026 within the timeframe specified in the notice.
- Failure to comply may result in further coercive recovery measures available to the Recovery Officer under the applicable recovery framework (e.g., attachment and sale of movable/immovable property, garnishee proceedings, or other modes of recovery).
- Entities and intermediaries dealing with the named defaulters should note the outstanding liability when assessing counterparty risk.

## Important Dates

- Notice/Recovery Certificate date: July 14, 2026.
- Specific payment deadline and further procedural dates are set out in the full notice (refer to the source PDF for exact timelines, as they could not be extracted from the scanned document).

## Impact Assessment

The direct impact is confined to the named defaulters — Mirage Marble Private Limited, Nirmal Marble Limited, and Mr. Abhishek Jain — who now face formal recovery action for outstanding dues linked to the Oriental Trimex Limited matter. There is no indication that Oriental Trimex Limited itself, its listed securities (if any), or the broader market are directly affected by this notice. Market participants with commercial or financial exposure to the named defaulters should take note of the recovery proceedings as a credit and counterparty risk consideration.
