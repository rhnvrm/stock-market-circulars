## Summary

SEBI's Recovery Officer has issued a Release Order in connection with Recovery Certificate No. RC9084 of 2026, issued against Ratan Lal Rathi (PAN: AJSPR3863P) in the matter of Illiquid Stock Options. The order records that the dues covered under the Recovery Certificate have been satisfied or the recovery proceedings against the noticee are otherwise being closed, and directs release of the certificate/any attachment made pursuant to it.

## Key Points

- The order pertains to Recovery Certificate No. RC9084 of 2026 issued against Ratan Lal Rathi (PAN: AJSPR3863P).
- The underlying matter relates to SEBI's enforcement action concerning trading in illiquid stock options, where monetary penalties/dues were levied and referred for recovery.
- The release order indicates closure or withdrawal of the recovery proceedings/attachment initiated under the Recovery Certificate.
- Issued by SEBI's Recovery Officer under powers exercised pursuant to Section 28A of the SEBI Act, 1992, read with the Second Schedule to the Income Tax Act, 1961, as applicable to recovery of amounts due to SEBI.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced; this is an administrative/enforcement action closing out a specific recovery proceeding.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance obligations arise for market participants generally. Any bank, depository, or other authority holding an attachment order pursuant to the referenced Recovery Certificate should note the release and lift the corresponding attachment/restraint in respect of the noticee, as directed in the order.

## Important Dates

Order dated July 1, 2026 (as per publication on SEBI's website under Recovery Proceedings, July 2026).

## Impact Assessment

The order has a narrow, individual-specific impact limited to the noticee named in the Recovery Certificate and does not have implications for the broader securities market, listed companies, or trading activity.
