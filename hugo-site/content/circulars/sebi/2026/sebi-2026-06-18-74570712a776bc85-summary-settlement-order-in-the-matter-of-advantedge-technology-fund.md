## Summary

SEBI issued a Summary Settlement Order (SO/2026-27/PSD/14-18) settling enforcement proceedings against Advantedge Technology Fund (AIF), its Manager Advantedge Investment Advisors LLP, and three Key Managerial Personnel — Kunal Khattar, Ridhish Talwar, and Nitin Garg. The violations relate to the failure of the Manager and Sponsor to contribute pro-rata to AIF Scheme III alongside other investors during two periods in 2024. The applicants filed for settlement and remitted ₹12,75,000 jointly and severally, which SEBI confirmed and accepted.

## Key Points

- Settlement applications SS-14/2026 through SS-18/2026 were filed by five applicants covering the AIF, its Manager, and three KMPs
- The Manager and Sponsor failed to maintain pro-rata contributions to Advantedge Technology Fund Scheme III for the period January 30, 2024 to July 02, 2024 and August 16, 2024 to December 17, 2024
- SEBI issued Notices of Summary Settlement on March 04, 2026 under regulation 16 of the Settlement Regulations
- Applicants filed their settlement application on April 02, 2026 and remitted the settlement amount on April 01, 2026
- Settlement amount of ₹12,75,000 (Rupees Twelve Lakh Seventy-Five Thousand) paid jointly and severally
- SEBI confirmed receipt of the settlement amount and closed the proceedings

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This order applies existing AIF Regulations and Settlement Regulations to conclude enforcement proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

- AIF Managers and Sponsors must contribute to fund schemes on a pro-rata basis with other investors as required under regulation 10(d) of the SEBI (Alternative Investment Funds) Regulations, 2012
- Compliance with clause 11.1.2 of the Master Circular for Alternative Investment Funds dated May 07, 2024 is mandatory
- Managers must adhere to the Code of Conduct under the Fourth Schedule of the AIF Regulations, including clauses 2(a) and 2(c)
- Key Managerial Personnel bear individual responsibility for ensuring fund compliance with regulation 20(1) read with regulation 10(d)

## Important Dates

- January 30, 2024 – July 02, 2024: First period of alleged pro-rata contribution violation
- August 16, 2024 – December 17, 2024: Second period of alleged pro-rata contribution violation
- March 04, 2026: SEBI issued Notices of Summary Settlement to all applicants
- April 01, 2026: Settlement amount of ₹12,75,000 remitted by applicants
- April 02, 2026: Settlement application filed by applicants
- June 18, 2026: Summary Settlement Order passed

## Impact Assessment

This order has limited broader market impact as it concerns a single AIF (Advantedge Technology Fund Scheme III) and its internal governance. The settlement amount of ₹12,75,000 is modest relative to typical enforcement penalties. The case reinforces SEBI's expectations that AIF Managers and Sponsors must maintain skin-in-the-game by contributing pro-rata alongside investors. This serves as a reminder to AIF managers and their KMPs of individual accountability under the AIF Regulations and the Code of Conduct. No publicly listed securities or retail investors are directly affected.
