## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Attachment of Demat Accounts (Order No. 15387 of 2026, dated May 26, 2026) against Sattu Yadav, designated as a defaulter with PAN AHXPY0089P. The attachment is being executed under Recovery Certificate No. 9004 of 2026, arising from the defaulter's dealings in illiquid stock options at BSE.

## Key Points

- Defaulter: Sattu Yadav (PAN: AHXPY0089P)
- Nature of action: Attachment of demat accounts
- Recovery Certificate No.: 9004 of 2026
- Order No.: 15387 of 2026
- Date of Notice: May 26, 2026
- Underlying matter: Dealing in illiquid stock options at BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an enforcement/recovery action under existing SEBI recovery mechanisms.

## Compliance Requirements

- Depositories and depository participants holding demat accounts of Sattu Yadav (PAN: AHXPY0089P) are directed to attach those accounts as per the notice.
- No transfers or debits from the attached demat accounts are permitted until further orders from SEBI.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: May 26, 2026
- Recovery Certificate Date: 2026 (No. 9004)

## Impact Assessment

This action is limited in market impact, targeting a single individual defaulter. It has no systemic effect on broader markets or other market participants. The attachment of demat accounts is a standard recovery measure used by SEBI to enforce dues from defaulters involved in manipulative or illegal trading practices in illiquid stock options at BSE.
