## Summary

SEBI has issued a Completion Order marking the closure of recovery proceedings initiated under Recovery Certificate No. 8811 of 2025 against BIR Finance Private Limited. The underlying recovery certificate was issued in connection with the entity's involvement in trading in illiquid stock options at BSE, a practice SEBI has historically treated as an unauthorized/manipulative market practice warranting monetary penalties and disgorgement directions. The completion order signifies that the Recovery Officer has recorded satisfaction of the outstanding demand or otherwise concluded the recovery process initiated against the entity.

## Key Points

- The order pertains to Recovery Certificate No. 8811 of 2025 issued against BIR Finance Private Limited.
- The underlying matter relates to trading in illiquid stock options at BSE.
- SEBI's Recovery Officer has issued a Completion Order, indicating the recovery proceedings under this certificate now stand concluded.
- This is a closure/administrative order rather than a new finding of violation.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced by this order. It is an enforcement action closing out a specific recovery certificate issued earlier against the named entity.

## Compliance Requirements

No further compliance action is required from market participants generally. BIR Finance Private Limited's obligations under Recovery Certificate No. 8811 of 2025 are treated as discharged/concluded pursuant to this completion order.

## Important Dates

Order dated: 2026-07-15 (published on SEBI's website in July 2026).

## Impact Assessment

The order has no direct impact on listed securities, trading activity, or the broader market, as it concerns closure of recovery proceedings against a single unlisted private entity. Its significance is limited to SEBI's enforcement record-keeping regarding illiquid stock options trading violations.
