## Summary

SEBI has issued a Completion Certificate for Recovery Certificate No. 8736 of 2025 against Ravindra Bharti Education Institute Private Limited (PAN: AAHCR6075L) and its associated individual Ravindra Balu Bharti (PAN: AVDPB1473A). The completion certificate indicates that the recovery proceedings initiated under the said recovery certificate have been concluded.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8736 of 2025 has been marked as completed by SEBI.
- The proceedings were against Ravindra Bharti Education Institute Private Limited (PAN: AAHCR6075L) and Ravindra Balu Bharti (PAN: AVDPB1473A).
- The matter pertains to Ravindra Bharti Education Institute Private Limited.
- A completion certificate signifies that the outstanding dues/penalties under the recovery certificate have been satisfied or the recovery process has otherwise concluded.

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes are introduced by this order. It is an enforcement closure document specific to the named parties.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements arise for the broader market from this order. The named entities — Ravindra Bharti Education Institute Private Limited and Ravindra Balu Bharti — are subject to the terms of the concluded recovery proceedings.

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate No.: 8736 of 2025
- Completion Certificate issued: April 2026

## Impact Assessment

This order has a narrow impact, confined to the specific entities named. There is no systemic market impact. It signals the closure of enforcement/recovery action by SEBI against Ravindra Bharti Education Institute Private Limited and its principal Ravindra Balu Bharti.
