## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. RC9012 of 2026 containing a Notice of Demand against Shiw Mohan Jaiswal (PAN: ADSPJ7624G) in connection with the matter of Illiquid Stock Options. This certificate initiates formal recovery proceedings for outstanding dues owed to SEBI.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate Number: RC9012 of 2026
- Defaulter: Shiw Mohan Jaiswal
- PAN: ADSPJ7624G
- Matter: Illiquid Stock Options
- Document Type: Notice of Demand for recovery of dues
- Recovery proceedings initiated under SEBI Act

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an enforcement action under existing recovery provisions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Shiw Mohan Jaiswal is required to pay the demanded amount as specified in the recovery certificate
- Failure to comply may result in further enforcement actions including attachment of assets and bank accounts
- The recovery certificate empowers SEBI to take coercive measures for debt recovery

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate Issue Date: 2026
- Payment deadline and specific dates would be mentioned in the detailed notice

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Negligible - This is an individual enforcement action with no direct market-wide implications.

**Regulatory Impact:** Part of SEBI's ongoing enforcement efforts to recover dues from defaulters in illiquid stock options cases. Demonstrates SEBI's commitment to pursuing recovery proceedings against violators.

**Operational Impact:** Specific to the individual named in the certificate. No impact on market participants or intermediaries.
