## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Attachment of Bank Accounts and Demat Accounts against Rajendra Madanlal HUF, identified as a defaulter, under Attachment Proceedings (AP) No. 15380 & 15381 of 2026 dated May 25, 2026. The action is taken under Recovery Certificate No. 9104 of 2026 in connection with illegal dealings in illiquid stock options on BSE.

## Key Points

- Defaulter: Rajendra Madanlal HUF
- Attachment Proceedings: AP No. 15380 & 15381 of 2026
- Recovery Certificate No.: 9104 of 2026
- Date of Notice: May 25, 2026
- Nature of default: Dealing in illiquid stock options on BSE
- Assets attached: Bank accounts and demat accounts

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice is an enforcement and recovery action executed under existing SEBI recovery mechanisms and applicable securities laws.

## Compliance Requirements

- Banks and depositories holding accounts of Rajendra Madanlal HUF are required to freeze and not permit any debits from the attached accounts.
- Any entity holding assets of the defaulter must comply with the attachment notice and not facilitate transactions that would defeat the recovery proceedings.
- Non-compliance with the attachment notice may attract further legal consequences under SEBI's recovery framework.

## Important Dates

- May 25, 2026: Date of Attachment Proceedings (AP No. 15380 & 15381 of 2026) and issuance of this notice.
- Recovery Certificate No. 9104 of 2026 provides the underlying authority for the attachment action.

## Impact Assessment

This enforcement action directly impacts Rajendra Madanlal HUF by freezing their financial assets — both bank accounts and demat accounts — as part of SEBI's recovery proceedings for violations related to illiquid stock options trading on BSE. The action has no direct impact on broader market participants or market operations. It reinforces SEBI's continued efforts to enforce recovery against entities found guilty of manipulative or unauthorized trading in illiquid derivatives segments on BSE.
