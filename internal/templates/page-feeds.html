RSS Feed Format

All feeds follow the RSS 2.0 specification and include custom namespace elements for circular-specific metadata:

  • circular:source - Exchange (NSE, BSE, SEBI)
  • circular:category - Regulatory category
  • circular:impact - Impact level
  • circular:severity - Severity level
  • circular:id - Unique circular ID
  • circular:pdfUrl - Link to PDF document
  • circular:stock - Related stock symbols
